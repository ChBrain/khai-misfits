Two coinage misattributions in existing warrants, found by a corpus-wide citation audit (601 sources fact-checked, both confirmed by an independent verifier):

- The Anticommons: the term "anticommons" was coined by Michelman (1982), not Heller; Heller coined "the tragedy of the anticommons" (1998) and popularised it. Reassign "the name" to Michelman and credit Heller with the tragedy framing, resolving an internal inconsistency (the Michelman row already read "the concept coined").
- The Extra Road: Koutsoupias & Papadimitriou's "Worst-case Equilibria" (STACS 1999) introduced the measure as the "coordination ratio"; "the price of anarchy" was coined by Papadimitriou (STOC 2001). The row no longer credits the 1999 paper with the name.

No staging changed; corrections are confined to the Origin tables and intros.
