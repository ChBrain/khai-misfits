A stressful vigil is not a restorative environment (Kaplan's attention restoration comes through being away and soft fascination, not an anxious wait on a wet floor), and Hsee, Yang, and Wang's finding is the need for a justification to be busy, not a permission to rest. Corrected in the Arc, the Vigil trigger, plot_the_vigil, piece_the_refill, and the warrant's Line of Work and Encoding, so the crisis grants the time but never the restoration, and the refill, owed only to the unlicensed hour and refused, never comes.

- Tightens the Kaplan grounding so it does not overclaim: attention restoration was established for natural settings, so the warrant and piece_the_refill now credit only that and read the bath and book as an undemanding hour that can offer being away and soft fascination, rather than asserting a bath is what Kaplan measured. Also fixes a residual drift in piece_the_refill's Taxonomy ("unlocked only when the permit allows it") to match the correction that the permit never buys the refill.
