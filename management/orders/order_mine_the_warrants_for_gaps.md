# Order: Mine the Warrants for Gaps

## Direction

The season is refilled from evidence, not whim, and the richest evidence is the
house's own warrants: every misfit's `REFERENCE.md` names, in its Line of Work and
its Origin table, the neighbours it is cut against and the scholars it rests on, so
the collection carries a map of the concepts adjacent to it but not yet staged. A
corpus-wide pass read that map. Fourteen scouts swept all one hundred and three
warrants, surfaced one hundred and fifty-one adjacent candidates, and a synthesis
deduped them against the staged set and ranked them by the house standard. In the
same run a companion pass fact-checked all six hundred and one Origin-table
citations against the literature: it confirmed two coinage misattributions,
corrected under separate cover, and recorded the rest as sound. This order records
the mine and refills the slate from it.

## Orders

Slate the distinct new gaps the mine surfaced, in priority order, each with its
source and the cut that clears every staged misfit, and add them to the
Playwright's standing slate, [Fill the Season](../plan_fill_the_season.md), worked
from the top as every batch is. Confirm the held: several strong candidates the
mine re-surfaced are already triaged on the slate as held off or folded, and their
reasons stand, so they are noted as reaffirmed and not restacked. Strike the stale:
the slate still holds risk compensation off against Moral Hazard, but it has since
been staged as The Spent Margin, so that held-off note is struck and retired. The
synthesis's own dedup missed a handful that a staged misfit already carries by a
canonical name the id list did not show; those are struck before slating and named
below, so the slate stays clean.

## Implementation

The refill batch is appended to [Fill the Season](../plan_fill_the_season.md) at
the one-hundred-and-three reconcile, in the plan's established form: a triage note
of method and dedup, then the slated targets tiered P1 and P2, each carrying the
concept, its source, and its distinct-from cut. The strongest, cleanest survivors
lead: the Keynesian beauty contest, statistical discrimination, publication bias,
and legibility and the loss of métis as P1; Berkson's paradox, the dollar auction,
bootleggers and Baptists, diminishing returns on complexity, the Samaritan's
dilemma, Schelling's tipping, the optimizer's curse, the tyranny of
structurelessness, and the cobweb cycle as P2.

Struck before slating, the mine's synthesis having proposed each while a staged
misfit already carries it by canonical name: risk compensation (The Spent Margin),
the race to the bottom (The Lowest Bar), Brooks's law (The Ninth Woman),
institutional isomorphism (The Done Thing), Rose's prevention paradox (Not Worth My
While), pluralistic ignorance (The Trip to Abilene), the tragedy of the horizon
(The Horizon Problem), the Icarus paradox (The Success Trap), and the family
scapegoat and triangulation (The Identified Patient).

Reaffirmed held, the mine re-surfacing candidates the slate already triaged and the
reasons standing: preference falsification, held as the society-scale sibling of
The Trip to Abilene until its threshold-cascade sliver can be cut narrowly; the
security dilemma, held against Accidental Adversaries; the iron law of oligarchy,
held against Pournelle's Iron Law; normalization of deviance and the shifting
baseline, held against The New Normal and The Brittle Optimum; the Red Queen, held
against The Arms Race; and Muller's ratchet, held against Model Collapse until the
irreversible-click vector is cut clear.
