# Order: Adopt Cut to Fit

## Direction

The house adopts **cut to fit** as its authoring shape. The uniform mould the
first misfits were built to (the same seven personas, seven positions, eight
pieces in every play) is retired as the default: it was copied, not read from the
source. From here the play is the one fixed element and every element below it is
cast to the concept's own vectors.

## Orders

Stand up the standard and point the house at it. This order rides the governance
change that establishes it and closes when that change lands. The restaging of
the existing misfits and the authoring of new ones proceed under the standing
plan the order stands up, not under this order: an order completes, the plan runs
on.

## Implementation

The standing plan [Cut to Fit](../plan_cut_to_fit.md), owned by the Playwright,
and the coding contract in `CLAUDE.md` that points authors to it. No misfit
content moves in this order: it establishes the shape, and the recut lands in its
own `misfit/` lane afterward.

## Targets

- [x] the standing plan Cut to Fit is written and owned by the Playwright
- [x] CLAUDE.md points authors at the standard
- [x] the recut of existing misfits is deferred to its own misfit lane, not this order
