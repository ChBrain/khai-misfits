# Position: Wheel

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The seat that holds the target of accepted risk, feels the margin as slack, and spends it back until the balance is restored.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A target of accepted risk and the freedom to act on it: a felt balance of danger against reward, and the room to close a distance or carry more speed when the road allows.
A clear case: keeping a steady, comfortable level of risk is what every competent driver does, and trading danger against haste is the ordinary work of getting there.
No way to feel a margin as anything but slack, and every reason to draw it down when the road feels safer than the target it holds.

## Orders

Spend the margin.
Read the safer road, close the following distance, carry the extra speed, lend the road a little less attention, and restore the felt danger to the level that feels right.
Keep the balance, and take the room the new device seems to offer.

## Loses

The engineered gain, exactly by keeping the balance a good driver keeps.
Because the device lowers the felt danger below the target the Wheel defends, the risk feels like slack going spare, so it is re-taken to restore the balance, drawing the built margin off as speed and attention until the crash rate is back where it began, and the safety meant to be banked is enjoyed as performance.
It gains the room to press on, and loses the protection that room was, the margin kept becoming the margin spent.

## Drives

Toward a balance restored and a road driven to its felt edge,
bought with the very margin the measure installed,
so the seat that only keeps its ordinary target of risk spends the safety built for it and leaves the toll unmoved.
