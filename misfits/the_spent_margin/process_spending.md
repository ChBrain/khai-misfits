# Process: Spending

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The driver re-taking the risk to restore the balance, so the margin is drawn off as behaviour and part of it lands outside the car.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Driver, with a margin sensed as slack and a balance to restore.
At [the road](place_the_road.md), the felt danger below target, the driver closing the following distance, carrying more speed, lending the road less attention, each move restoring the felt danger toward the level they hold.

## Direction

Toward a balance restored at the old risk, and a share of it borne outside.
Because the driver re-takes the felt slack until the danger is back at target, spending points the margin out of the account as behaviour, and because the road is shared, it points part of that re-taken risk past the car onto the exposed, the balance restored inside and the cost divided beyond.

## Lever

The soundness of keeping a steady target.
Because holding a constant, comfortable level of accepted risk is exactly what a competent driver does, spending runs on good driving, not bad: the margin is drawn down by the ordinary defence of a balance, the gain consumed from no recklessness, only from a target kept as any careful driver keeps it.

## Echo

A margin spent, a toll unmoved, a cost partly moved.
The driver has restored the balance and the felt danger sits back at target, so the built margin is gone, drawn off as speed and inattention while the device works exactly as made, the crash rate holding where it began and part of the re-taken risk carried by the exposed who never held the margin at all.
