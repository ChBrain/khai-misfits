# Position: Verge

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The seat outside the device, handed no margin, that bears the share of the re-taken risk the harder-driven road transfers onto it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A path and no protection: the same crossing, the same verge, the same care as before, and no device, no buffer, and no margin fitted to them.
A clear case: the one outside the car asked for nothing, chose nothing, and changed nothing, and is owed the road as it was.
No way to opt out of a road driven harder by others, and no seat at the table where the margin was fitted and spent.

## Orders

Hold the path.
Keep to the crossing and the verge at the same care as before, expecting the road to be no more dangerous than it was, having been given no reason to think otherwise.
Carry on unchanged, because nothing about the Verge's own conduct has changed.

## Loses

Its share of the safety, taken not by anything it did but by the margin someone else spent.
Because the driver's re-taken risk does not all fall back on the driver, a road driven harder for the car's new buffer is more dangerous for the one at the verge, so part of the danger the device removed from inside the car is transferred to the uncovered outside it, who lose margin they never held.
It keeps to its path and loses ground it never gave up, the covered made safer partly out of the exposure of the rest.

## Drives

Toward a path unchanged on a road grown more dangerous,
charged for a margin it neither held nor spent,
so the seat that took no part in the trade pays a share of its cost, the safety of the car drawn in part from the exposure of the verge.
