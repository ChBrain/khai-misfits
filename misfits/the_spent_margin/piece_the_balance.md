# Piece: The Balance

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The target of accepted risk the driver defends, the felt danger set against reward that converts a margin into re-taken risk.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Held at [the road](place_the_road.md) in the driver, and reset from the felt side at [the shared space](place_the_shared_space.md).
Flows nowhere and moves nothing: it sits in the agent as a level to be kept, the setting the whole loop returns to.

## Load Bearing

Without it a margin would simply be a gain, kept because nothing pulls it down: no target, no defence, no reason to spend.
With it every buffer becomes something to be drawn off: because the driver holds a roughly constant level of accepted risk and defends it, a felt danger below that level reads as slack and a felt danger above it reads as too much, so the Balance is the set point the behaviour restores. The Balance is load bearing because it is the mechanism that turns a safety into a spend (Wilde; Adams): the homeostatic target that makes a margin unstable, so the piece carries the reason a real protection does not stay, the defended level that draws the gain back to where it began.

## Apparent

Good, careful driving, the plain sense of a competent agent keeping a steady, comfortable level of risk.
Seen at the surface, the Balance looks like exactly the prudence we want in a driver, and the truth that this same prudence is what spends the safety, that a defended target will draw down any margin it can feel, that the careful keeping of a balance is the engine of the offset, stays hidden behind how sound and how ordinary the keeping of a steady risk appears.

## Yearbook

Held by [the Driver](persona_the_driver.md), sensed through [reading](process_reading.md) and defended through [spending](process_spending.md).
It is the pivot of the trap: the Balance reads [the margin](piece_the_margin.md) as slack and draws it down, and it is the one thing [redesigning](process_redesigning.md) works upon, raising the felt danger to meet the target from the other side so the [thermostat](piece_the_thermostat.md) holds without a spend.
