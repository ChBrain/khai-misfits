# Plot: The Spend

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Road](place_the_road.md), the margin sensed as slack and the balance under target.
A driver with room they can feel and a felt danger below the level they hold acceptable.

## Action

[Spending](process_spending.md): the driver re-takes the risk to restore the balance, closing the distance, carrying more speed, lending the road less attention.

## Stage

[The Driver](persona_the_driver.md), on [Spend the Margin](plan_spend_the_margin.md), draws [the margin](piece_the_margin.md) down until the felt danger is back at [the balance](piece_the_balance.md), each adjustment small and sensible for one who feels a buffer beneath them.
The device works exactly as built, and the crash rate does not move, because the gain was drawn as speed and not banked as safety.

## Tension

Every move is sound and the sum of the sound moves is the safety gone.
There is nothing reckless: closing a distance and carrying more speed on a road that feels safer is what a competent driver does, keeping the steady target they always keep. But the target is met by re-taking the very risk the device removed, so the plot closes with the margin spent and the toll unmoved, the built gain consumed by the ordinary defence of a balance, no bad driving required for a real safety to disappear.
