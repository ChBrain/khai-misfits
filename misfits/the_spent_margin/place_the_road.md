# Place: The Road

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The open traffic where the margin is actually spent, the driver at their target and the exposed at the verge.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The live road, mixed traffic in real weather, no baseline held and no run repeated.
The Driver with the new device beneath them, closing the distance and carrying more speed, and at the kerb and the verge the Exposed on foot and on the bicycle, crossing the same road at the same care as ever.

## Holds

The reading, the spending, and the transfer.
Here the defended target the Track lacked is present, so the Road holds the place where the margin becomes behaviour: the Driver feels the buffer as slack and re-takes the risk to restore the balance, and because the road is shared, it holds the point where that re-taken risk spills past the car, the harder driving met not only by the Driver who chose it but by the Exposed who did not, the spend and its transfer in one place.

## Offers

Room to the Driver, and the same road to everyone on it.
Because the Road carries the buffer the device installed, it offers the Driver the felt room to press on and restore the balance, handing out the sense that there is slack to spend, and in the same motion it offers that spend to the whole of the traffic, the pedestrian and the cyclist included, so the room enjoyed inside the car is a danger raised for those outside it, the margin drawn down on a road no one else agreed to drive harder.

## Withheld

Any margin for the exposed, and any sign the gain has been drawn off.
The Road carries the device only for those it was fitted to, so it withholds any buffer from the one at the verge, and, the car working exactly as built and the toll simply failing to fall, it withholds any visible sign that the safety has been spent, the crash rate holding steady while the margin quietly empties, no wreck to point to and no moment where the gain is seen to go.
