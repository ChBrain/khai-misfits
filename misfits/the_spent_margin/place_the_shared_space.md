# Place: The Shared Space

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The redesigned street where the felt danger is raised on purpose, so the target is met without spending the margin.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The street with its certainties removed, the kerbs and centre lines and signals taken out, foot traffic and vehicles left to negotiate a space that reads as uncertain.
The Redesigner's ground, where the Driver, given nothing that says "you are safe here," slows and watches of their own accord.

## Holds

The redesigning, and the felt danger held up at target.
Here the comfort that the road usually offers has been stripped away by design, so the Shared Space holds the one arrangement in which the margin is not read as slack: the Driver feels the space as risky, the felt danger sits back at the target without any real risk being re-taken, and the care that the device would otherwise let them spend is kept, the balance met from the felt side rather than restored by pressing on.

## Offers

A kept gain, and a road that feels worse for it.
Because the Shared Space raises the felt danger to the target by design, it offers the Redesigner the one thing that holds the margin, a driver who keeps the care without being handed a comfort to spend, and it hands out, in the same motion, the price of that keeping, a street that feels less safe in order to be more so, the reassurance the measure promised withheld as the very means of keeping its gain.

## Withheld

The comfort, and the protection that real crashes still need.
The Shared Space can raise the felt danger, but it withholds the reassurance the measure was sold as, so it can be demanded back, and it cannot go past the point where a crash that still happens needs the real device, so it withholds any claim to replace protection with feeling, holding the margin only up to where the true guard is what the moment requires.
