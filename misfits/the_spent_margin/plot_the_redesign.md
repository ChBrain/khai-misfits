# Plot: The Redesign

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Shared Space](place_the_shared_space.md), someone who would keep the gain instead of spending it.
A street with its comforts removed, the kerbs and lines taken out, and a driver given nothing that says they are safe.

## Action

[Redesigning](process_redesigning.md): the felt danger is raised by design, so the target is met without re-taking real risk and the margin stays banked.

## Stage

[The Redesigner](persona_the_redesigner.md), on [Change the Feel](plan_change_the_feel.md), sharpens the sense of risk and strips the false comfort, resetting [the balance](piece_the_balance.md) from the felt side so [the margin](piece_the_margin.md) is not read as slack and [the thermostat](piece_the_thermostat.md) holds at target without a spend.
The driver, feeling less safe, slows of their own accord, and the gain, for once, is kept.

## Tension

The one move that saves the gain is the one the measure was sold to prevent.
The remedy works: make the road feel as risky as it is and the driver keeps the care of their own accord. But it keeps the gain only by refusing the driver the comfort, asking people to feel less safe in order to be safer, and it cannot be pushed past the protection real crashes still need, so the plot closes with the gain kept and the comfort refused ... or the reassurance demanded back, the felt danger smoothed again, and the margin spent once more.
