# Persona: The Driver

## Taxonomy

[Wheel](position_wheel.md). Holds a target of accepted risk, reads the new margin as slack, and re-spends it as speed until the felt danger is back at target.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ordinary. Comfortable. In control.
Is the protected agent, the one handed the margin, who keeps, as everyone does, a rough sense of how much danger is worth the reward of getting there sooner.
No recklessness in it: only a driver trading felt danger against haste, at the same balance they have always kept.

## Action

Feels the road as safer under the new device, and adjusts: closes the following distance, carries more speed on the bend, lets the eyes rest a moment longer.
Each move small, each move sensible for someone who feels a buffer beneath them, restoring the felt danger to the level they find acceptable.
Does what any driver does: keeps their own balance of risk and reward, and spends slack where they feel it.

## Shadow

Not a reckless driver, an ordinary one, and that is the whole difficulty: keeping a steady target of accepted risk is the sound thing to do, and doing it spends the margin the measure built.
Because a working device lowers the felt danger below the target the Driver defends, the risk feels, to them, as if it is going spare, so they re-take it to restore the balance, drawing the engineered gain off as speed and attention until the crash rate is back where it began, and the safety that was built to be banked is enjoyed as performance instead.
It keeps the balance every good driver keeps, and the balance kept is the margin spent.

## Tell

Says "I can push it a little, the car is safer now, there is room."
Feels the room truly, and cannot see that the room is the safety, and that spending it puts the danger back exactly where the device took it from.
