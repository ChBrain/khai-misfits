# Piece: The Margin

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The real safety gain the measure opens, the felt buffer proven on the track and handed to the driver to spend.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Proven at [the track](place_the_track.md) with behaviour held fixed, and carried onto [the road](place_the_road.md) beneath the driver.
Flows from the device that makes it to the agent who feels it, a buffer between the danger they meet and the danger they can bear.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to spend and no offset: no buffer, no felt slack, and the measure's gain simply stays.
With it the device becomes a real protection that is also a real thing to draw down: the shorter stopping distance, the belt's hold, the helmet's absorption, a margin between the risk that was and the risk that is. The Margin is load bearing because it is the quantity the whole trap turns on (Peltzman; Aschenbrenner and Biehl): the gain the engineer proves and the driver spends, so the piece carries the safety that is genuinely delivered and genuinely available to consume, real on the track and, once felt, slack on the road.

## Apparent

Delivered safety, the plain good of a device that works and a distance that is shorter.
Seen at the surface, the Margin looks like a gain banked, the lives the test promised counted as saved, and the truth that a buffer handed to a defended target of risk is not banked but offered, that a margin one can feel is a margin one will spend, that the proven gain and the kept gain are two different numbers, stays hidden behind how real and how measured the protection is.

## Yearbook

Opened by [the Fitter](persona_the_fitter.md) through [fitting](process_fitting.md), the buffer the device installs.
It is the first move of the trap: the Margin is read against [the balance](piece_the_balance.md) as slack, spent through the driver's re-taking of risk, and its spending drives [the transfer](piece_the_transfer.md) that lands on the exposed, the quantity the whole [thermostat](piece_the_thermostat.md) draws back down to target.
