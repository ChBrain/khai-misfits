# Process: Reading

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The driver sensing the safer road, so the felt danger falls below the target and the margin registers as slack.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Driver, with a target of accepted risk and a road that now feels safer.
At [the road](place_the_road.md), the new device beneath them, the felt danger dropping under the level they hold acceptable, risk registering as room now going spare.

## Direction

Toward a margin sensed as slack.
Because the driver measures the road not by its true danger but by how dangerous it feels, reading points the margin at the gap between the felt danger and the target: the buffer arrives as a sense of safety, and the sense of safety reads, against a defended target, as risk available to take.

## Lever

The felt, not the actual.
Because the target is defended against a felt danger and not a measured one, reading runs on perception, so a real gain in safety enters the driver as a feeling of slack, the margin becoming spendable the moment it is felt, its truth on the track irrelevant to how it registers on the road.

## Echo

A buffer felt as room to spend.
The driver has sensed the safer road and found the felt danger below target, so the margin, real and proven, now sits inside them as slack against the balance they keep, read as risk they may take back, the reading that turns a delivered safety into a thing about to be spent.
