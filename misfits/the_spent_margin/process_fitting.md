# Process: Fitting

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The device built and proven against a fixed baseline, opening a real margin the road will be handed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Fitter, with a working guard and a clean test.
At [the track](place_the_track.md), the device fitted and proven with behaviour held constant, the stopping distance measured shorter, the gain read off real and repeatable.

## Direction

Toward a margin opened and booked as delivered.
Because the test holds the operator fixed, fitting proves the whole gain the device can give and counts it as the gain the road will keep, so the process points from a real protection to a real margin, handed to the road as safety already scored.

## Lever

The honesty of the controlled test.
Because the device does stop the car shorter and the baseline does not move, the margin fitting measures is true, so the process runs on sound engineering, the gain assembled from no error, only from a real protection proven exactly as a protection should be proven.

## Echo

A true margin, handed off before it is read.
The device works and the gain is real, so what leaves the Track is a genuine buffer counted as delivered safety, the margin proven complete and passed to a road that will decide, by a target the test never held, whether it is kept or spent.
