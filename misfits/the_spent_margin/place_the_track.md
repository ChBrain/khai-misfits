# Place: The Track

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The controlled test where the device is proven with behaviour held fixed, and the margin shows at its full measured size.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The proving ground, a closed course of measured runs where the guard is tested against a fixed baseline.
The Fitter braking the car on the wet surface, the stopping distance shorter with the device than without, the gain read off clean and repeatable while the operator does exactly as instructed.

## Holds

The fitting, and the margin at its full size.
Here behaviour is held constant by design, so the Track holds the one condition under which the whole margin is real and kept: the device changes and the driving does not, the anti-lock brake stops the car shorter and nothing about how it is driven varies, so the gain measured here is the gain before anyone has read it as slack, the gain the road will be handed and has yet to draw down.

## Offers

A true number, and the confidence to book it.
Because the Track measures the device against a baseline that does not move, it offers the Fitter a real and honest margin and the ground to count it as delivered safety, handing out a proven gain and, with it, the mistaken assurance that the proven gain is the kept one, the clean test standing in for the road it does not contain.

## Withheld

The driver's target, and every behaviour that will spend the margin.
The Track can hold the device and the fixed baseline, but it cannot hold the defended target of accepted risk that the road brings, so it withholds the one thing that decides whether the margin stays: the driver free to feel the buffer as slack and re-take the risk, so the proving ground shows a gain complete and withholds the conduct that will draw it off, the margin true here and spent elsewhere.
