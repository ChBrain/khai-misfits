# Plan: Change the Feel

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Redesigner](position_redesigner.md), inside [The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Redesigner](position_redesigner.md)

## Direction

Change what the driver feels rather than what the device does. Strip the false comfort, sharpen the sense of risk, take out the kerbs and lines so the space reads as uncertain, and hold the felt danger up at the target from the other side, so the balance is met without re-taking real risk. Keep the gain by refusing the driver the sense that it is slack to spend.

## Orders

([The Redesigner](persona_the_redesigner.md) raises the felt danger back to target by design, the unwelcome remedy that keeps the margin only by giving up the comfort the measure promised.)

## Implementation

([The Shared Space](place_the_shared_space.md); the [redesigning](process_redesigning.md) process; the Redesigner resets [the balance](piece_the_balance.md) from the felt side so [the margin](piece_the_margin.md) is not read as slack, holding [the thermostat](piece_the_thermostat.md) at target without a spend.)

## Targets

- [ ] the false comfort is stripped and the sense of risk sharpened
- [ ] the felt danger is held at the target without re-taking real risk
- [ ] the driver keeps the care of their own accord and the gain is kept
- [ ] the cost is named: a road that feels worse to be a road that is better
