# Plot: The Fitting

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Track](place_the_track.md), the device fitted and the baseline held.
A real guard, a wet surface, an operator doing exactly as instructed, and a stopping distance to be measured.

## Action

[Fitting](process_fitting.md): the device is proven with behaviour held fixed, and a real margin appears exactly as large as the test measures.

## Stage

[The Fitter](persona_the_fitter.md), on [Prove the Guard](plan_prove_the_guard.md), builds the device and measures it against a baseline that does not move, opening [the margin](piece_the_margin.md) and booking it as the gain the road will keep.
The number is honest, the device works, and the gain is scored complete before any driver has touched it.

## Tension

The margin is real and the test is clean, and that is exactly why it will be trusted too far.
There is nothing to fault: the device does stop the car shorter and the baseline is held fair. But the gain is measured with behaviour fixed and handed to a road where behaviour is not, so the plot closes with a true margin booked as delivered safety, the proven gain mistaken for the kept one, the buffer real and, as yet, unspent.
