# Position: Redesigner

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The seat that would keep the gain by changing the felt danger rather than the real one, so the target is met without a spend.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An understanding and an unwelcome remedy: the knowledge that a margin the driver can feel will be spent, and the means to change what the driver feels rather than what the device does.
A hard case: the fix works only by making the road feel as risky as it is, so the reward it offers is a safety kept, and the price it asks is a comfort refused.
No way to keep the gain and the comfort both, and every reason to expect the comfort to be demanded back.

## Orders

Change the feel.
Strip the false comfort, sharpen the sense of risk, take out the kerbs and lines so the space reads as uncertain, hold the felt danger up at the target from the other side, and let the driver keep the care of their own accord.
Keep the gain by refusing the driver the sense that it is slack to spend.

## Loses

The reassurance the measure was sold as, exactly by keeping the gain it promised.
Because a target of accepted risk draws down any margin it can feel, the Redesigner holds the gain only by denying the driver the comfort, so the price of the kept margin is a road that feels worse to be a road that is better, and the remedy cannot be pushed past the protection that real crashes still need.
It gains the safety the measure built and loses the feeling of safety the measure promised, and is thanked for neither.

## Drives

Toward a gain kept and a comfort refused,
the felt danger raised so the target is met without a spend,
so the seat that alone can hold the margin must ask people to feel less safe in order to be safer, against the whole reason the measure was wanted.
