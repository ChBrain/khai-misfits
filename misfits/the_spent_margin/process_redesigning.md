# Process: Redesigning

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The felt danger raised on purpose, so the target is met without re-taking risk and the margin stays banked.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Redesigner, with the knowledge that a felt margin will be spent.
At [the shared space](place_the_shared_space.md), the false comfort stripped, the kerbs and lines removed, the sense of risk sharpened so the road reads as uncertain and the felt danger climbs back toward the target from the other side.

## Direction

Toward a target met without a spend.
Because the driver defends a felt danger, redesigning raises that felt danger by changing the road's feel rather than its device, so the process points the balance back to target without any real risk being re-taken, the margin held because it is never read as slack.

## Lever

The same perception the spend ran on, turned the other way.
Because the target answers the felt and not the actual, redesigning runs on the very lever that lost the gain: it works the driver's sense of danger, raising it by design so the margin is kept, the feeling that spent the safety made, deliberately, the feeling that saves it.

## Echo

A gain kept, and a comfort refused.
The felt danger sits back at target with no real risk taken, so the margin stays banked and the device's gain is finally kept, bought with the reassurance the measure promised, a road made to feel as risky as it is so the driver holds the care, the safety kept only by giving up the feeling of safety.
