# Position: Bench

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The seat that builds and proves the safety measure, and books the measured gain as the gain delivered.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The device and the test: the standing to design the guard and to prove it against a fixed baseline, and a real, measured margin to show for it.
A clear case: building a protection that works and proving it honestly is exactly the engineer's proper work.
No way to test behaviour from the bench, and every reason to book the margin the controlled test measures as the margin the road will keep.

## Orders

Prove the guard.
Build the device, hold the operator constant, measure the gain, and carry it into the case for the measure and the count of lives saved.
Deliver a real protection, and trust the number the clean test returns.

## Loses

The difference between the proven margin and the kept one, exactly by measuring against a baseline that does not move.
Because the test holds behaviour fixed and the road does not, the Bench books the whole margin as delivered, not seeing that a real buffer handed to a defended target of risk is a buffer that will be spent, so the gain proven on the bench is counted before the driver draws it off.
It gains a true and honest number, and loses the road's answer to it, the margin measured mistaken for the margin kept.

## Drives

Toward a working device and a proven gain,
booked against a behaviour that will not hold still,
so the seat that builds a real protection hands the road a margin it has already counted as saved and the driver has yet to spend.
