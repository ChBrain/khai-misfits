# Piece: The Thermostat

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md). The feedback loop entire, the target that reads a margin, spends it, and holds the felt danger at its setting so the gain is consumed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across [the road](place_the_road.md) as the whole loop, and is reset only at [the shared space](place_the_shared_space.md).
Flows through every corner of the mechanism: the margin sensed, the risk re-taken, the danger held at target, round again with the next gain.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no loop, only a margin and a driver and no rule that binds them: a gain that might stay or go with nothing to say which.
With it the pieces become a governor: the felt danger read against the target, the margin spent to restore it, the setting held so any new buffer is drawn down in turn, a self-correcting loop that returns the risk to where the agent keeps it. The Thermostat is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole (Wilde; Adams): the homeostatic feedback that makes the offset a law and not an accident, so the piece carries the trap entire, the reason a safety measure is met by the behaviour that consumes it, again and again, as reliably as a thermostat holds a room.

## Apparent

A safer world, each measure adding protection and the record of care growing longer.
Seen at the surface, the Thermostat is invisible and only its inputs are seen, a steady stream of real safety devices, and the truth that a loop sits beneath them holding the felt danger constant, that each new margin is drawn down to the same setting, that the sum of many genuine protections can be a toll that barely moves, stays hidden behind how plainly each single measure works.

## Yearbook

Kept at the driver's target and reset only by [the Redesigner](persona_the_redesigner.md), the loop that governs the whole.
It is the trap entire: the Thermostat reads [the margin](piece_the_margin.md) against [the balance](piece_the_balance.md), spends it through the driver's re-taking of risk, settles [the transfer](piece_the_transfer.md) onto the exposed, and holds until [redesigning](process_redesigning.md) raises the felt danger and lets the gain, at last, be kept.
