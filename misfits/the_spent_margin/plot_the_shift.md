# Plot: The Shift

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Road](place_the_road.md), the risk re-taken and needing somewhere to land.
A road driven harder for a margin fitted to the car, and at the kerb and the verge the exposed, fitted with none.

## Action

[Spending](process_spending.md), seen from outside the car: the harder-driven road carries part of the re-taken risk past the driver and onto those beside it.

## Stage

[The Exposed](persona_the_exposed.md), on [Hold the Path](plan_hold_the_path.md), keeps to the crossing at the same care as before and meets a road grown more dangerous, [the transfer](piece_the_transfer.md) landing on them a share of a margin they never held.
The driver restored their balance inside the car; the cost of restoring it did not stay inside the car.

## Tension

The spend was the driver's to make and the cost is not the driver's alone to bear.
There is no bargain the exposed agreed to: they changed nothing and chose nothing. But the re-taken risk is a harder-driven road, and a harder-driven road is more dangerous for the one at the verge, so the plot closes with the gain not only shrunk but moved, part of the danger the device removed from inside the car transferred to the uncovered outside it, the safety of the covered drawn in part from the exposure of the rest.
