# Persona: The Exposed

## Taxonomy

[Verge](position_verge.md). Stands outside the device, is handed no margin, and bears the share of the re-taken risk that the road driven harder transfers onto them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Unchanged. Uncovered. Beside the road, not in the car.
Is the pedestrian at the kerb, the cyclist at the verge, the one the safety measure never fitted and never protected.
No part in it: keeps to the same path at the same care as before, having asked for nothing and been given nothing.

## Action

Crosses the road, rides the verge, walks the kerb, exactly as always, with no device, no buffer, and no margin of their own.
Meets a road that is now driven a little harder, closer, faster, for a margin fitted to the car and not to them.
Does what they always did, and finds the danger around them risen for a safety that was never theirs.

## Shadow

Not a party to the trade, and that is the whole difficulty: the Exposed changed nothing and chose nothing, yet bears a share of a margin spent by someone else.
Because the driver's re-taken risk does not all fall back on the driver, a road driven harder for the car's new buffer is a more dangerous road for the one at the verge, so the danger the measure removed from inside the car is transferred, in part, to the uncovered outside it, who receive the cost of a protection they were never given.
It carries the bill for a margin it never held, the safety of the covered paid partly out of the exposure of the rest.

## Tell

Says nothing, and is asked nothing, because there is no seat at the verge for the one the device did not fit.
Keeps to the path as before, and cannot see, until it is met, that the road has grown more dangerous for a safety installed in someone else's hands.
