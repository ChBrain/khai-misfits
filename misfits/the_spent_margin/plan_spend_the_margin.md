# Plan: Spend the Margin

## Taxonomy

## Direction

Read the safer road and restore the balance. Close the following distance, carry more speed, lend the road less attention, each move sensible for a driver who feels a buffer beneath them, until the felt danger is back at the target of accepted risk. Keep the balance, and take the room the new device seems to offer.
