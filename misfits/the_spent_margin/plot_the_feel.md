# Plot: The Feel

## Taxonomy

[The Spent Margin](play_the_spent_margin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Road](place_the_road.md), the margin arrived beneath the driver.
A real buffer under them and a target of accepted risk to measure it against, the felt danger of the road now lower than it was.

## Action

[Reading](process_reading.md): the driver senses the road as safer and the felt danger falls below the target, the margin registering as risk now going spare.

## Stage

[The Driver](persona_the_driver.md), holding [the balance](piece_the_balance.md), reads the safer road and feels [the margin](piece_the_margin.md) as slack, the felt danger dropped under the level they keep and room opened they did not have before.
Nothing is done yet, only sensed: the buffer felt, the balance under target, the slack waiting.

## Tension

The reading is accurate and the feeling is true, and that is what makes the slack look free to take.
There is nothing false: the road genuinely is safer, and the driver genuinely feels it. But a target defended against a felt danger reads any margin it can feel as risk available, so the plot closes with the margin sensed as slack, the balance under target and the driver holding room the measure never meant them to spend.
