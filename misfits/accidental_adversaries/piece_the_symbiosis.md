# Piece: The Symbiosis

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The mutual-benefit bond, each party's success lifting the other, the strength and the exposure in one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the handshake](place_the_handshake.md), where the bond is formed.
In the flow of benefit both ways, each rising with the other.

## Load Bearing

Without it there are no allies to turn adversary, and no misfit.
With it the two are bound so that each other's success is their own, so the alliance genuinely lifts them both and, in the same tie, makes each depend on the other. The Symbiosis is load bearing because it is both the good and the exposure (Kemeny's archetype): the mutual benefit is real and worth having, and the dependence it builds is exactly what a partner's later defensive move will threaten, so the strength of the bond is the measure of the wound a single move can deal.

## Apparent

Simply a good partnership.
A bond where each helps the other looks like an unqualified win, so the symbiosis reads as pure advantage, and the truth that the dependence it builds is an exposure, that the closer the tie the more a defensive move can hurt, stays hidden behind how plainly both parties gain.

## Yearbook

Formed by [the partner](persona_the_partner.md) through [allying](process_allying.md), tabled as [Keep Faith](plan_keep_faith.md).
It is the bond the misfit turns adversarial: the Symbiosis ties the fortunes that [the local move](piece_the_local_move.md) will cross the boundary to harm, the strength [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md) will drag down.
