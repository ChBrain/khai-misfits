# Pitch: I'm Only Defending Myself

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Reasonable, measured, and quietly aggrieved.
The voice of a partner sure it is only protecting itself against the other's bad faith.
Every move it makes is defence; every move it receives is attack.

## Undertow

Under the reasonableness, a harm dealt without seeing it.
Each defensive move lands across the boundary, where the mover cannot see and the partner can, so both parties are dealing injury and reading only the injury they take.
The bad faith each is sure of is the other's mirror of its own blindness.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching two allies wreck an alliance that lifted them both,
each certain it is the victim, each move a defence and a wound at once,
and neither ever meaning the harm it does.

## Echo

After the run: a partnership turned to a feud no one chose,
two parties dragging each other down while each defends itself,
and the whole loop visible to no one standing inside it.
