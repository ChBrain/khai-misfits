# Position: Ally

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The seat of the cooperator in the bond, its fortunes tied to the other's.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A partnership that pays both sides, each other's success its own.
A dependence on the bond, and the strength that dependence brings.
No sight of the far side of the seam between the two.

## Orders

Keep faith with the alliance.
Invest in the mutual good, and let your fortunes ride with the other's.
Build on the shared benefit.

## Loses

Its security, in the exposure that the bond creates.
Its dependence is the source of its strength and of its wound, so the closer the tie the more a partner's defensive move can hurt it, and it cannot read that harm because its cause lives where it cannot look.
The success of the alliance is exactly what a defensive move will threaten.

## Drives

Toward the shared benefit,
against a blindness to the other's side,
so the very closeness that lifts both parties is what a single defensive move can turn to a wound.
