# Piece: The Misattribution

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The harmed party reading the accidental injury as intended hostility.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the feud](place_the_feud.md), where the injury is read.
In the wounded party's account of a harm whose cause it cannot see.

## Load Bearing

Without it the injury would be an accident, and no retaliation would follow.
With it the accident becomes an attack: the misattribution is the wounded party reading a harm it cannot explain as a thing done to it on purpose, because the cause lives across the blindspot where it cannot look. The Misattribution is load bearing because it is the trigger of the spiral (the fundamental attribution error): it converts an unseen side effect into a hostile act, and so licenses a retaliation that feels like justice, turning the wounded party into the next to deal unseen harm.

## Apparent

A fair reading of the evidence to hand.
An unexplained injury with no visible innocent cause looks like it must have been meant, so the misattribution reads as reasonable inference, and the truth that the harm was an accident whose logic lives out of sight, that the inference is honest and wrong, stays hidden behind the vividness of the wound and the blankness where its cause should be.

## Yearbook

Made by [the wounded](persona_the_wounded.md) through [spiralling](process_spiralling.md), tabled as [Answer the Blow](plan_answer_the_blow.md).
It is the accident read as attack: the Misattribution takes the harm dealt through [the blindspot](piece_the_blindspot.md) for intent, and lights [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md) with a grievance that feels like justice.
