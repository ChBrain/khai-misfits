# Process: Allying

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The partnership forming and delivering mutual benefit, building the dependence the misfit turns on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Two parties who each do better with the other than alone.
At [the handshake](place_the_handshake.md), the bond forming and the benefit beginning to flow both ways.

## Direction

Toward a symbiosis that lifts both, and a dependence that exposes both.
Each other's success becomes its own, so the alliance compounds their fortunes, and, in the same motion, each comes to rely on the other, so that what helps the bond helps them and what threatens it threatens them.

## Lever

A mutual benefit real enough to be depended on.
Because the alliance genuinely pays both sides, each invests in it and leans on it, so the dependence is not naive but earned, and the very realness of the benefit is what will make a partner's later defensive move land as a wound.

## Echo

A strong bond, and a shared exposure.
Two parties lifted by each other and bound to each other, each now holding a stake in the other that a single self-protective move can put at risk.
