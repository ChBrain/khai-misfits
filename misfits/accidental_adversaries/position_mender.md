# Position: Mender

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The seat that sees the whole loop and de-escalates first.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Sight of the whole loop, and of its own half in it.
The one move that breaks the spiral: stop undermining and step down first.
No guarantee the other will meet the step.

## Orders

See the whole, and own your part.
Stop your own defensive move and de-escalate before the other does.
Offer a first step, and ask to be answered in kind.

## Loses

Its guard, in the very act that could end the feud.
Stopping first means dropping its defence while the other may still be striking, and it cannot tell an accidental adversary who will meet its step from a true one who will take it.
The one seat that can break the spiral must make itself vulnerable to the one case where the spiral was not an accident.

## Drives

Toward a restored alliance,
through a step that must be taken undefended,
so the only move that ends the feud is the move a real predator is built to exploit.
