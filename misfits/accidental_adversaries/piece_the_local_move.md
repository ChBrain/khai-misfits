# Piece: The Local Move

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). Each party's inward optimisation, locally rational, with an unseen cost across the boundary.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the boundary](place_the_boundary.md), where it is made and its harm crosses.
The self-protective choice, sensible on the mover's own side.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no harm, and the alliance holds.
With it the trouble begins from something reasonable: the local move is a party's optimisation of its own results, entirely rational where it stands, that degrades the partner across the seam. The Local Move is load bearing because it is the engine of the whole misfit (the archetype's crossing loop): it is not an attack but a self-defence, and it produces injury precisely because each party optimises its own side while the cost lands on the other, so the more sensible the move, the more surely it wounds the partner it never aimed at.

## Apparent

Ordinary good management of one's own side.
A party improving its own results looks like simple prudence, so the local move reads as sound self-interest, and the truth that it imposes a cost across the boundary, that its whole harm lands where the mover cannot see, stays hidden behind how reasonable it is where it is made.

## Yearbook

Made by [the defender](persona_the_defender.md) through [undermining](process_undermining.md), tabled as [Protect My Own](plan_protect_my_own.md).
It is the reasonable act that wounds: the Local Move deals its harm through [the blindspot](piece_the_blindspot.md), and that harm, misread, becomes [the misattribution](piece_the_misattribution.md) that starts the spiral.
