# Place: The Feud

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). Where the spiral runs, blow answering blow, allies now adversaries.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Two parties trading defensive moves, each larger than the last, each sure it is the wronged one.
The alliance forgotten, the exchanges compounding, both dragged down and both certain the other began it.

## Holds

The misattribution and the spiral.
Here each injury is read as intended and each answer is felt as just, and here the loop compounds them, so the feud holds both the misreading that turns accident into attack and the escalation that turns attack into war, the wrong story and the runaway it feeds.

## Offers

The clarity of a righteous grievance.
Each party feels, vividly and sincerely, the harm done to it, so the feud offers the certainty of being the victim, a clean sense of who is at fault that both sides hold at once.

## Withheld

The other half of the loop.
The feud shows each party the harm it takes and never the harm it deals, so it withholds the shape of the whole, and both fight on in the honest conviction that they are only answering, never starting.
