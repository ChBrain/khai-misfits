# Persona: The Defender

## Taxonomy

[Defender](position_defender.md). The partner in acting mode, making a locally sensible move to protect its own results.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Prudent, self-reliant, minding its own side of the ledger.
Makes the choice that improves its own results, as any careful party would.
Sees nothing hostile in it: it is only looking after itself.

## Action

Takes a move that is rational on its own side of the boundary.
Improves its own position, and, across the seam, degrades the partner's without seeing it.
Deals harm it does not aim at and cannot watch land.

## Shadow

Its move is genuinely reasonable and its harm is genuinely unseen, so it is guilty of injury and innocent of intent at once.
It cannot see the far side of the boundary, so the cost it imposes is invisible to it and vivid to the one who bears it.
The most sensible self-protection is the one that most surely wounds the partner it never meant to touch.

## Tell

Says "this just makes sense for us."
Of a move that makes exactly as much sense as it quietly costs the other.
