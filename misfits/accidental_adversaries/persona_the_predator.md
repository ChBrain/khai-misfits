# Persona: The Predator

## Taxonomy

[Predator](position_predator.md). The genuine adversary, indistinguishable from the accidental one until the guard is down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Reasonable, patient, and wearing the partner's face.
Looks exactly like an accidental adversary defending itself, and is not.
Waits, in the spiral, for the other to drop its guard.

## Action

Deals harm on purpose, and lets it read as another sad accident of the loop.
Meets a mender's first step not with a step but with an opening seized.
Takes de-escalation as weakness, and the dropped guard as the moment to strike.

## Shadow

It is rare, and it is the reason the remedy is never safe: it exists just often enough that stopping first cannot be trusted.
It cannot be told from an accidental adversary from inside the spiral, because both defend, both aggrieve, both look the same until the test.
The one case that would exploit the cure is the case no one can rule out before paying to find out.

## Tell

Says all the same reasonable things the accidental adversary says.
And means them, until the guard drops, when it means something else.
