# Persona: The Wounded

## Taxonomy

[Retaliator](position_retaliator.md). The partner in receiving mode, its success degraded, reading the harm as attack.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Injured, wary, sure it has been wronged.
Feels its results slipping and cannot find the reason on its own side.
Reads the harm as something done to it, on purpose.

## Action

Receives the unseen cost of the other's local move as an unexplained injury.
Attributes it to hostility, because it cannot see the local logic that produced it.
Answers in kind, with a move sensible to itself and harmful across the seam.

## Shadow

Its injury is real and its reading of it is wrong: it takes an accident for an attack, because the accident's cause lives where it cannot look.
It becomes, in answering, a defender in its turn, dealing the same unseen harm it just suffered.
The sincerity of its grievance is exactly what makes its retaliation feel like justice.

## Tell

Says "they started this."
Of a harm that started as a move the other never knew would touch it.
