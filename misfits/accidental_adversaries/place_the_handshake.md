# Place: The Handshake

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). Where the partnership is formed and the mutual benefit flows.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Two parties joining, each other's success becoming its own, the alliance lifting them together.
The good years of the bond: benefit flowing both ways, and each coming to rely on the other.

## Holds

The symbiosis, whole.
Here the two are tied so that each rises with the other, so the handshake holds both the mutual benefit that pays them and the dependence that benefit builds, the strength of the bond and the exposure it creates.

## Offers

A partnership that lifts both sides.
Each other's success is a gain to share, so the handshake offers real mutual advantage, the genuine good of an alliance in which both do better than either would alone.

## Withheld

Sight of the far side of the seam.
The bond joins the parties but does not let either see the other's inner side, so the handshake withholds the view each would need to know when its own move harms the other, and the closeness it builds is the very thing a defensive move will later wound.
