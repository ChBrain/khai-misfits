# Position: Retaliator

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The seat of the wounded party, reading the harm as attack and answering it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An injury it cannot explain from its own side.
A grievance that feels like justice.
An answering move, sensible to itself and harmful across the seam.

## Orders

Answer the blow.
Read the harm as hostility, since its cause cannot be seen.
Repay it in kind, to protect yourself.

## Loses

The truth of the injury, and its own innocence with it.
It takes an accident for an attack, because the accident's cause lives where it cannot look, and in answering it becomes a defender in its turn, dealing the same unseen harm it just suffered.
The sincerity of its grievance is exactly what makes its retaliation feel like justice.

## Drives

Toward repayment,
of a harm it misreads as intended,
so the honest sense of being wronged is what turns the wounded party into the next one to wound.
