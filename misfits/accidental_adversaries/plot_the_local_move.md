# Plot: The Local Move

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The boundary](place_the_boundary.md), one party moving to protect its own results.
The choice is sensible on its own side of the seam, and its cost will land on the other, where the mover cannot look.

## Action

[Undermining](process_undermining.md): [The Defender](persona_the_defender.md), holding [the defender's seat](position_defender.md), makes the locally rational move, while [The Wounded](persona_the_wounded.md), holding [the retaliator's seat](position_retaliator.md), feels its success degrade with no cause it can find.

## Stage

[The Defender](persona_the_defender.md) tables [Protect My Own](plan_protect_my_own.md) and makes [the local move](piece_the_local_move.md), improving its position and, through [the blindspot](piece_the_blindspot.md), harming the partner.
[The Wounded](persona_the_wounded.md) takes the injury in full and can see none of the logic that produced it.

## Tension

Harm is dealt in full view of its cause and received with no view of it at all.
The defender's move is entirely reasonable where it stands, and its harm is entirely invisible to it, so it is guilty of injury and innocent of intent at once. And the wounded, feeling its results slip with no explanation on its own side, has been handed an injury whose only apparent source is the partner, an accident already wearing the shape of an attack.
