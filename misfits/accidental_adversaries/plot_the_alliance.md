# Plot: The Alliance

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The handshake](place_the_handshake.md), two parties joining to mutual benefit.
Each other's success becomes its own, the alliance lifts them together, and each begins to rely on the other.

## Action

[Allying](process_allying.md): [The Partner](persona_the_partner.md), holding [the ally's seat](position_ally.md), keeps faith with the bond, while [The Defender](persona_the_defender.md), holding [the defender's seat](position_defender.md), prospers and begins to mind its own results.

## Stage

[The Partner](persona_the_partner.md) tables [Keep Faith](plan_keep_faith.md) and builds on [the symbiosis](piece_the_symbiosis.md), fortunes tied to the other's.
[The Defender](persona_the_defender.md) tables [Protect My Own](plan_protect_my_own.md) in its first, mildest form, tending its side of a bond it still shares.

## Tension

The bond that lifts both parties is quietly building the exposure that will wound them.
Nothing here is wrong: the alliance genuinely pays both sides, the dependence is earned, and minding one's own results is only prudent. But each party now holds a stake in the other that a single self-protective move could threaten, and neither can see the far side of the seam, so the very success of the partnership is laying the ground for a harm no one yet intends.
