# Plot: The Table

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The table](place_the_table.md), someone seeing the whole loop at last.
The move is to own one's own half, stop the undermining, and de-escalate first, before the other reciprocates.

## Action

[Mending](process_mending.md): [The Mender](persona_the_mender.md), holding [the mender's seat](position_mender.md), stops first and offers a step, while [The Predator](persona_the_predator.md), holding [the predator's seat](position_predator.md), waits behind an identical face for the guard to drop.

## Stage

[The Mender](persona_the_mender.md) tables [See the Whole](plan_see_the_whole.md) and answers [accidental adversaries](piece_accidental_adversaries.md) by stepping down undefended.
[The Predator](persona_the_predator.md) tables [Take the Opening](plan_take_the_opening.md), and whether the step meets a partner or [the true adversary](piece_the_true_adversary.md) cannot be known until it is taken.

## Tension

The one move that ends the feud must be made blind to the one case that punishes it.
Stopping first is the only way to drain the spiral, and it works the moment the other meets it, and most of the time the other is an accident who will. But the mender cannot tell, before it steps, an accidental adversary who will answer in kind from a true one who will seize the dropped guard, so the cure is a real gamble across a seam thick with felt betrayals. It closes with the alliance restored, or the spiral holding, or an undefended step meeting a predator who was no accident.
