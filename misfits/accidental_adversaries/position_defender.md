# Position: Defender

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The seat that makes a locally sensible move to protect its own results.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A choice that improves its own position, rational on its own side of the boundary.
A clear view of its own results and none of the other's.
A move that helps here and, unseen, harms there.

## Orders

Protect your own results.
Make the move that is sensible on your side of the seam.
See nothing hostile in it: you are only looking after yourself.

## Loses

Its innocence, though never its intent.
Its move is genuinely reasonable and its harm genuinely unseen, so it deals injury without aiming at it, and the cost it imposes is invisible to it and vivid to the one who bears it.
The most sensible self-protection is the one that most surely wounds the partner it never meant to touch.

## Drives

Toward its own results,
across a boundary it cannot see past,
so the reasonable move to defend itself is the move that lands, unseen, as an attack.
