# Plan: See the Whole

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the mender's seat](position_mender.md), inside [Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md).

## Owner

## Direction

See the whole loop, and your own half in it. Recognise that the harm you receive is a response to harm you dealt unseen, and that the other is not a villain but a mirror. Stop your own undermining first, de-escalate before the other does, and offer a step that asks to be met in kind.

## Orders

([The Mender](persona_the_mender.md) stops first and de-escalates, breaking the spiral by dropping its guard while the other may still be striking.)

## Implementation

([The Table](place_the_table.md); the [mending](process_mending.md) process; [accidental adversaries](piece_accidental_adversaries.md) is answered by seeing the whole loop and stepping down first, at the risk that the other is [the true adversary](piece_the_true_adversary.md).)

- [ ] the whole loop, and one's own half in it, is seen
- [ ] the harm received is recognised as a response to harm dealt unseen
- [ ] one's own undermining is stopped first, de-escalating before the other
- [ ] the step is taken undefended, at the risk of a partner who is no accident
