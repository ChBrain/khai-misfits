# Plot: The Spiral

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The feud](place_the_feud.md), the first party now harmed in turn.
The retaliation lands as unprovoked, since its cause too lives across the seam, and the loop has both parties dealing and receiving.

## Action

[Spiralling](process_spiralling.md) compounding: [The Defender](persona_the_defender.md), holding [the defender's seat](position_defender.md), receives the answer as an attack and strikes back, while [The Spiral](persona_the_spiral.md), holding [the spiral's seat](position_spiral.md), makes each round larger than the last.

## Stage

[The Defender](persona_the_defender.md) tables [Protect My Own](plan_protect_my_own.md) again, now as a wounded party too, answering a blow it reads as unprovoked.
[The Spiral](persona_the_spiral.md) tables [Escalate](plan_escalate.md) and compounds the exchanges into [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md), the alliance tipping fully into rivalry.

## Tension

Two allies become adversaries with no aggressor anywhere in the room.
Each party now occupies both seats, dealing unseen harm and receiving it, and each sees only its own half, so both are sincerely the victim and both keep answering. The loop needs no malice to run, only two reasonable parties each certain it is defending itself, and it drags the symbiosis that lifted them down, one justified move at a time.
