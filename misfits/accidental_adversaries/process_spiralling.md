# Process: Spiralling

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The harm misread as attack, repaid in kind, and compounded round on round.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An unexplained injury, and a party that cannot see its cause.
At [the feud](place_the_feud.md), the wounded reading the harm as hostility and answering it.

## Direction

Toward a partnership turned rivalry.
The injury is misattributed to intent, answered with a move harmful across the seam, received by the first party as unprovoked, and answered again, each round larger, so the alliance tips into a feud that both sides sustain and neither chose.

## Lever

A wound each party can see and a harm each party cannot.
Because each sees only the injury it takes, never the injury it deals, both read themselves as answering aggression, so the loop runs on sincere grievance alone and compounds without any malice to power it.

## Echo

Two allies made adversaries by reasonable moves.
The bond forgotten, the exchanges escalating, and both parties dragged down, each certain to the last that it was only ever defending itself.
