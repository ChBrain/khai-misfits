# Piece: The True Adversary

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The genuine exploiter, indistinguishable from the accidental adversary until the guard is down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the table](place_the_table.md), where the remedy must risk it.
Behind a face and a set of reasons identical to the accidental adversary's.

## Load Bearing

Without it the cure would be simple: see the whole, stop first, restore the bond.
With it the cure is a gamble: the true adversary is the party that really is dealing harm on purpose, that would meet a mender's step not with a step but with an opening seized. The True Adversary is load bearing because it is what makes the remedy unsafe and the misfit humane: it is rare, but it exists just often enough that stopping first cannot be trusted, and it cannot be told from an accidental adversary inside the spiral, since both defend and both aggrieve, so the one move that ends the feud must be made blind to the one case that punishes it.

## Apparent

Either a paranoid excuse to keep fighting, or a danger too real to disarm before.
To the mender it can look like a fantasy that justifies never stopping; to the wary, a certainty that forbids the first step; and both are wrong, because it is genuinely possible and genuinely rare, so the true adversary reads as myth or as menace, and the truth that it is a chance that cannot be ruled out, not a knowable fact, stays hidden behind whichever fear is speaking.

## Yearbook

Played by [the predator](persona_the_predator.md), tabled as [Take the Opening](plan_take_the_opening.md), the peril [See the Whole](plan_see_the_whole.md) must risk.
It is the rare real exploiter tangled in the accident: the True Adversary is why de-escalation cannot be safe, the reason answering [accidental adversaries](piece_accidental_adversaries.md) is a step taken undefended.
