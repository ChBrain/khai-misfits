# Persona: The Spiral

## Taxonomy

[Spiral](position_spiral.md). The reinforcing loop of move and counter-move that turns allies into adversaries.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Self-feeding, accelerating, needing no new cause.
Takes each defensive move as fuel for the next, and each grievance as the ground for another.
Grows on retaliation, and asks only that both parties keep answering.

## Action

Turns a wound into a blow and a blow into a wound, round after round.
Compounds each exchange, so every answer is larger than the harm it answers.
Converts a partnership into a rivalry, one justified move at a time.

## Shadow

It needs no villain, only two parties each defending itself, so it runs on reasonable moves and cannot be stopped by finding fault.
It hides its own shape, because each party sees only its own half, the harm it takes and not the harm it deals.
The loop that drags both down is built entirely from acts each party is sure were right.

## Tell

Says "and after what they just did, we have no choice."
Each round, to both parties, in the same words.
