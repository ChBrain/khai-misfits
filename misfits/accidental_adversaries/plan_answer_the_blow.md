# Plan: Answer the Blow

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the retaliator's seat](position_retaliator.md), inside [Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Retaliator](position_retaliator.md)

## Direction

When your results slip for no reason you can find on your own side, read the harm as an attack, because its cause is nowhere you can see. Answer it in kind, protect yourself, and repay the injury you are sure was meant.

## Orders

([The Wounded](persona_the_wounded.md) misreads the accidental harm as hostility and repays it, becoming, in answering, a defender dealing the same unseen harm it just suffered.)

## Implementation

([The Feud](place_the_feud.md); the [spiralling](process_spiralling.md) process; [the misattribution](piece_the_misattribution.md) reads the injury as intended, so the answering blow closes the loop and the partnership tips toward rivalry.)

## Targets

- [ ] the unexplained injury is read as a deliberate attack
- [ ] its cause, living across the seam, cannot be seen and so is assumed hostile
- [ ] the blow is answered in kind, felt as justice
- [ ] the wounded party becomes, in answering, the next to deal unseen harm
