# Process: Mending

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The whole loop seen, the undermining stopped first, and the risk that carries.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A recognition that the harm received answers a harm dealt unseen, and a will to break the loop.
At [the table](place_the_table.md), the mender owning its half and the predator waiting for a dropped guard.

## Direction

Toward a restored alliance, through an undefended step.
The whole loop is seen, one's own part in it owned, the defensive move stopped, and de-escalation offered before the other reciprocates, so the spiral loses its fuel the moment one side stops feeding it and the other meets the step.

## Lever

A unilateral stop that only works if the other is an accident.
Because the loop is powered by both sides answering, one side stopping first can drain it, but stopping first means dropping the guard while the other may still strike, and no one can tell the accidental adversary who will meet the step from the true one who will take it.

## Echo

The alliance recovered, or the guard exploited.
The bond restored when the other meets the step, or the dropped guard seized when the other was no accident, so the mending ends either in a partnership renewed or in the one wound the cure itself opened.
