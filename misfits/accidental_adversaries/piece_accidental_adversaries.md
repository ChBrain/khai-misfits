# Piece: Accidental Adversaries

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The phenomenon whole: allies turned adversaries by defensive moves, and its remedy in seeing the loop at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the table](place_the_table.md), where the loop is seen or the spiral holds.
And across the whole seam, in every move dealt as defence and received as attack.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of moves and grievances with no shape.
With it the moves become one law: the symbiosis, the local move, the blindspot, the misattribution, the spiral, and the true adversary are facets of a single mechanism, in which two allies each make a locally rational move that harms the other across a boundary neither can see past, so each reads the harm it deals as prudence and the harm it takes as attack, and a partnership spirals into a rivalry no one chose. Accidental Adversaries is load bearing because it is the whole finding with its whole remedy attached (Kemeny; Senge): the only answer is to see the loop, own your half, and stop first, a de-escalation that must be offered undefended and cannot be told, in advance, from surrender to a real predator.

## Apparent

Either a betrayal with a clear culprit, or two stubborn parties who should just get along.
Seen from inside, one side is plainly the aggressor; seen from outside, both are simply being difficult; and both miss the structure, that each move is defensive and each harm unseen and each grievance sincere, so neither blame nor a scolding to cooperate answers it, only seeing the whole loop and stepping down first, which is the one thing no party inside the spiral can safely do.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the mender](persona_the_mender.md) through [mending](process_mending.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: Accidental Adversaries is what [the symbiosis](piece_the_symbiosis.md) becomes once [the local move](piece_the_local_move.md) crosses the boundary unseen, undone only by seeing the loop and stopping first, at the risk that the other is [the true adversary](piece_the_true_adversary.md).
