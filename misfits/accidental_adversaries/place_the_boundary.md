# Place: The Boundary

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The seam between the two parties, where a local move's harm crosses unseen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The line between the two, each side visible only to the party on it.
A move made on one side for good local reasons, and its cost landing on the other, where the mover cannot look and the partner cannot miss.

## Holds

The local move and the blindspot.
Here the sensible self-protection is chosen and here its harm crosses to the far side, so the boundary holds both the reasonable move and the blind seam it crosses, the act and the reason no one connects the act to the injury.

## Offers

A clear view of one's own side.
Each party sees its own results and its own logic perfectly, so the boundary offers the confidence of a move that is entirely sensible where the mover stands, a self-protection with no visible cost.

## Withheld

The far side, where the cost lands.
The seam shows each party only its own half, so the boundary withholds the sight of the harm each deals, and the injury is dealt in full view of its cause and received with no view of it at all.
