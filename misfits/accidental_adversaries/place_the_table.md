# Place: The Table

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). Where the remedy is weighed: see the whole and step down first, or the spiral holds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A move to see the whole loop and break it: the mender owning its half, proposing to stop its undermining and de-escalate first.
The doubt across the table, that a dropped guard may meet a partner who is no accident but a predator waiting for exactly this.

## Holds

The remedy and its risk.
Here the spiral can be broken by one party stepping down undefended, and here that step may meet a true adversary who takes it, so the table holds both the one move that restores the alliance and the one case that punishes it.

## Offers

A way back to the alliance.
By seeing the whole and stopping first, the table offers the restoration of the bond, a partnership recoverable the moment one side stops feeding the loop and the other meets it.

## Withheld

A way to tell the accident from the predator.
The table cannot distinguish, in advance, an accidental adversary who will meet the step from a true one who will seize it, so it withholds the certainty the undefended step would need, and offers the cure only as a gamble taken across a seam thick with felt betrayals.
