# Persona: The Partner

## Taxonomy

[Ally](position_ally.md). The cooperator in the bond, whose success and the other's rise and fall together.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Trusting, invested, glad of the alliance.
Sees the other's success as its own and builds on the shared benefit.
Depends on the bond, and is stronger for it.

## Action

Keeps faith with the partnership and invests in the mutual good.
Lets its fortunes ride with the other's, each lifting the other.
Comes to rely on the alliance, and to be exposed by that reliance.

## Shadow

Its dependence is the source of the strength and the source of the wound: the closer the tie, the more a partner's defensive move can hurt it.
It cannot see the far side of the seam, so when harm comes it cannot read the local logic that made it.
The very success of the bond is what a defensive move will threaten, and what turns a slip into a betrayal.

## Tell

Says "we do better together."
And is right, until the first move each makes to protect itself alone.
