# Position: Spiral

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The seat of the reinforcing loop, compounding move and counter-move.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Two parties each defending itself, and a wound to feed on every round.
The power to compound, each answer larger than the harm it answers.
No need of a villain, only of both sides continuing to respond.

## Orders

Turn each wound into a blow and each blow into a wound.
Compound the exchanges, and make every round justify the next.
Convert the partnership into a rivalry, one reasonable move at a time.

## Loses

Nothing of its own, and it drags both parties down.
It runs on reasonable moves, so it cannot be stopped by finding fault, and it hides its own shape, since each party sees only its own half, so the loop that ruins both is built from acts each is sure were right.
The rivalry it makes is chosen by no one and sustained by everyone in it.

## Drives

Toward escalation,
on fuel that both sides keep supplying,
so the self-feeding loop needs no malice, only two parties each certain it is the one defending itself.
