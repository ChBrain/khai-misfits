# Persona: The Mender

## Taxonomy

[Mender](position_mender.md). The one who sees the whole loop and moves to de-escalate first.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Clear-eyed about the loop, willing to go first.
Sees that the harm it receives is a response to harm it dealt unseen, and that the other is not a villain but a mirror.
Ready to stop its own undermining before the other stops theirs.

## Action

Names the whole loop and its own half in it.
Stops its defensive move and de-escalates, unilaterally, without waiting for the other.
Offers a restored alliance, and a first step that asks to be answered in kind.

## Shadow

Its move is the only one that breaks the spiral, and it means dropping its guard while the other may still be striking.
It cannot tell, in advance, an accidental adversary who will meet its step from a true one who will take it.
The one who could end the feud must make itself vulnerable to the one case where the feud was not an accident.

## Tell

Says "I'll stop first, and hope you meet me."
Knowing the hope is exactly what a real predator would exploit.
