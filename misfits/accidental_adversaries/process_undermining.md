# Process: Undermining

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). A local defensive move degrading the partner across the boundary, unseen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A party minding its own results, and a seam it cannot see past.
At [the boundary](place_the_boundary.md), the defender making a move sensible on its own side.

## Direction

Toward harm dealt without intent.
The move improves the mover's position and, across the boundary, degrades the partner's success, so injury is done in a place the mover cannot watch and did not aim at, landing in full on the far side of the seam.

## Lever

A boundary that shows each party only its own side.
Because each can see its own logic and results and not the other's, the cost of a local move is invisible to the one who imposes it and vivid to the one who bears it, so the undermining needs no ill will, only a move rational here that happens to land there.

## Echo

An injury dealt and unregistered by its author.
The mover's position improved, the partner's quietly worsened, and a harm now loose in the alliance that the one who dealt it does not know it dealt.
