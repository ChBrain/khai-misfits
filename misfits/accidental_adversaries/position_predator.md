# Position: Predator

## Taxonomy

[Accidental Adversaries](play_accidental_adversaries.md). The seat of the genuine adversary, wearing the accidental one's face.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A harm dealt on purpose, dressed as another accident of the loop.
The patience to wait, in the spiral, for the other to drop its guard.
A face and a set of reasons identical to the accidental adversary's.

## Orders

Deal harm, and let it read as the loop's doing.
Meet a mender's first step with an opening seized, not a step returned.
Take de-escalation as weakness.

## Loses

Nothing, and it costs the remedy its safety.
It exists just often enough that stopping first cannot be trusted, and it cannot be told from an accidental adversary from inside the spiral, since both defend and both aggrieve and both look the same until the test.
The one case that would exploit the cure is the case no one can rule out before paying to find out.

## Drives

Toward the opening,
behind a face no one can distinguish in time,
so the rare true adversary is what makes the honest cure a gamble every accidental pair must also take.
