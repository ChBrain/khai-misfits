# Persona: The Beneficiary

## Taxonomy

[Beneficiary](position_beneficiary.md). The one the work was for, who is served the paper instead of the outcome.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Waiting, dependent, in need of the actual result.
The patient who needs to be well, the customer who needs the thing to work, the mission that needs to be met.
Cares nothing for the file, and everything for the aim it was supposed to serve.

## Action

Asks for the outcome, and receives the cover.
Comes with a real need and is given the shielding test, the documented referral, the process run to completion, all of it aimed at the record and not at it.
Gets a service optimised to be defensible about it, rather than good for it.

## Shadow

Its need is the aim that was displaced, and it is the one who never sees the file that replaced it.
The whole apparatus is nominally for it, yet the effort meant to serve it went to covering the servers, so it bears the missed outcome while everyone around it is blameless, and it cannot even point to a culprit, only to a spotless process that failed it.
Not ungrateful: the one for whom the work was meant and to whom the least of it arrived.

## Tell

"I didn't need the paperwork. I needed it to work."
The plain statement of the aim that all the covering quietly set aside.
