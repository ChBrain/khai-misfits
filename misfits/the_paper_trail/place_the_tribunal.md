# Place: The Tribunal

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). Where failure is judged, and blame lands or scatters.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The review after a bad outcome: the question of who is at fault, the files produced in defence.
The blamer asking who deviated, and a row of clean records answering that everyone followed the process.

## Holds

The blame, and the diffusion that defeats it.
Here failure is met with who broke procedure, which sets the whole incentive, and here, when it fails, the blame finds a hundred covered hands and no author, so the tribunal holds both the source of the covering and its blameless end.

## Offers

An account of who did what.
The tribunal produces a full reckoning of steps taken and rules followed, so it offers the appearance of accountability, a thorough answer to the question of fault.

## Withheld

A hand to hold responsible for the aim.
The tribunal can see who deviated from the process and not who failed the outcome, and with every file spotless it finds no one, so it withholds the author it came to find, and the failure comes to rest on no one at all.
