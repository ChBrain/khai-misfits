# Pitch: I Followed the Process

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Diligent, thorough, above reproach.
Every step documented, every box ticked, every sign-off in place, so no one can ever say you did not follow the process.
The voice of conscientious compliance, spoken by people who are right that a clean file protects them and cannot see that the file was never the point.

## Undertow

Under the diligence, work bent away from the outcome and toward the record.
Because failure is judged by whether the process was followed, the safe act is the documented one, so effort flows to being covered rather than to being right, and the aim recedes behind the paper raised to shield it.
No one is lazy: the coverer documents because an undocumented judgement is punished and a documented folly is not, the auditor checks the boxes it was told to check, and the sum of these prudent moves is a mountain of file and a missed result.

## Nerve

The feeling of careful, blameless professionalism,
every action justified, every record complete,
the real work thinning under the effort to leave nothing that could be blamed.

## Echo

After the run: a spotless file and a failed aim,
a failure with a hundred covered hands and no author,
and a paper trail that proved everyone did their part and nobody did the thing.
