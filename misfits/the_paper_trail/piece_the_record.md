# Piece: The Record

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The accreted file: every cover stacked and stamped into a spotless account of the process followed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the desk](place_the_desk.md), where the paper is made and certified.
Where the covers pile clean and thick, and the auditor stamps each box as done.

## Load Bearing

Without it the covering would leave no trace to point to, and defensibility would have nowhere to stand.
With it the worker holds proof: the record is the sum of the covers, the file that shows every step was taken and every rule followed, and it is what the tribunal will accept as a job done right. The Record is load bearing because it is where the effort goes: it grows exactly as fast as the aim is neglected, a full account of the process that says everything except whether the process worked, so a met outcome and a missed one leave the same clean page.

## Apparent

Proof of care.
A thick, complete, certified file looks like an organisation that documented everything and left nothing to chance, so the record reads as rigour made visible, and the truth that it measures steps taken and never outcomes reached, that its cleanliness is silent on success, stays hidden behind its sheer weight.
