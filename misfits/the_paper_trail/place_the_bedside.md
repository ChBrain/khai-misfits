# Place: The Bedside

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The frontline, where the real aim is served or displaced.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place where the work actually meets its purpose: the patient, the customer, the case at hand.
A real need present, and a choice between the act that serves it and the act that shields the file.

## Holds

The aim, and the displacement of it.
Here the outcome is either reached or crowded out, so the bedside holds both the genuine judgement that serves the need and the defensive service that serves the record instead.

## Offers

The chance to do the thing that matters.
The bedside is where effort can be spent on the outcome, where a real need can actually be met, so it offers the aim its one true point of contact with the work.

## Withheld

A reward for meeting the aim over covering the file.
The bedside cannot make the outcome protect the worker as the record does, so it withholds the incentive that would keep the effort here, and the need it presents loses, quietly, to the paper raised elsewhere.
