# The Paper Trail: Reference

The research warrant for [The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The title names the artefact;
the play runs on the study of defensive practice under blame, the sociology of goal displacement in
bureaucracy, and the just-culture answer to the blame cycle, all cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging the paper trail as a **systemic system**: where a bad outcome is judged by whether the process
was followed rather than by what went wrong, each worker is rational to make its acts defensible
rather than effective, so effort flows to documentation, sign-off, and box-ticking, the aim recedes
behind the effort to be blameless, responsibility diffuses across a chain in which everyone owns a box
and no one owns the result, and when the thing fails the failure has many covered hands and no author.
The play models the documented mechanics, the blame that punishes deviation over failure, the cover
that is the defensible act, the record that accretes, the displacement of the aim, the diffusion of
ownership, the covered failure, and the reform of the incentive that alone turns the work back at a
cost, as the load-bearing vectors of the structure, not as a tale of idleness. The governing finding
of the source literature is the play's governing law: accountability for the justifiability of a
process, rather than for the quality of the outcome, drives people to defensive, self-protective
action that displaces the goal, and the remedy is to change what is judged, not to exhort harder. It
is the sibling of the house's Plausible Deniability, the other face of blame-avoidance: there a
principal engineers ignorance so no knowledge can attach, here everyone engineers process so no fault
can, and its cure runs straight into the house's Chesterton's Fence, since some of the defensive paper
is a real safeguard that the stripping hand may tear out.

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast the
  coverer as lazy or the auditor as a fool, because the source finding is precisely that the behaviour
  is rational: an undocumented judgement is punished when it fails and a documented folly is not, so
  covering yourself is the sound move; the auditor checks the boxes it was told to check; and the
  manager spreads the sign-off because an owned decision is a blamed decision. The misfit is that a
  crowd of prudent, self-protective acts leaves a spotless file and a missed aim.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into simply banning paperwork or exhorting people
  to care about the outcome. The behaviour follows the incentive, so the cure is to change what is
  judged, punishing the dishonest judgement and not the honest failure and giving people the safety to
  own a result, which the blame culture's keepers resist, and which cannot strip the defensive file
  without risking the genuine safeguard hidden in it. The reckoning is a changed incentive bought
  against resistance and at the peril of a Chesterton's fence.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here,
  never claimed as the house's. The staging is the house's; the defensive-medicine evidence, the
  goal-displacement account, the accountability research, and the just-culture answer are theirs, and
  the honesty that not all process is defensive ritual, that much of it is real and load-bearing, is
  carried as carefully as the finding itself.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **[The Blame](piece_the_blame.md)**: carries accountability aimed at process (Tetlock), failure judged by
  whether the rules were followed, which makes defensibility the goal.
- **[The Cover](piece_the_cover.md)**: carries the defensive act (Studdert et al.), the test, email, or sign-off
  done to protect the doer rather than to reach the aim.
- **[The Record](piece_the_record.md)**: carries the accreting documentation (Merton), the file raised to prove
  the process was followed.
- **[The Displacement](piece_the_displacement.md)**: carries goal displacement (Merton; Studdert et al.), the aim
  crowded out as effort flows to being covered.
- **[The Diffusion](piece_the_diffusion.md)**: carries dispersed ownership (Graeber), the sign-off chain in which
  everyone owns a box and no one owns the outcome.
- **[The Covered Failure](piece_the_covered_failure.md)**: carries the blameless failure, the aim missed and every
  hand pointing, truthfully, to its spotless file.
- **[The Paper Trail](piece_the_paper_trail.md)**: carries the phenomenon whole (Tetlock; Dekker), work bent from
  outcome to record, and its remedy in a changed incentive at the peril of a real safeguard.
