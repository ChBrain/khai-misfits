# Plot: The Covered Failure

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The tribunal](place_the_tribunal.md), the thing having failed anyway.
The aim was missed, the review is convened to find who is at fault, and the room is lined with the spotless files that every hand built along the way.

## Action

[Blaming](process_blaming.md) come full circle: [The Blamer](persona_the_blamer.md), holding [the blamer's seat](position_blamer.md), looks for the author of the failure, while [The Coverer](persona_the_coverer.md), holding [the coverer's seat](position_coverer.md), points, truthfully, to a record in which every step was taken and every rule followed.

## Stage

[The Blamer](persona_the_blamer.md) tables [Punish the Deviation](plan_punish_the_deviation.md) and finds no deviation to punish, only [the diffusion](piece_the_diffusion.md), a hundred covered hands and no author.
[The Coverer](persona_the_coverer.md) rests on [the record](piece_the_record.md), and the failure settles as [the covered failure](piece_the_covered_failure.md), owned by no one.

## Tension

The blame that started the trail cannot land at its end.
Everyone is telling the truth: each hand did follow the process, each file is genuinely clean, and the sign-offs gathered to share the decision have shared the blame for it too, so the search for an author runs into a wall of honest records and stops. The failure is real, the covering caused it, and it comes to rest on no one, which is exactly what a chain optimised for blamelessness was built to deliver.
