# Position: Auditor

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The seat that certifies the boxes, and takes the file for the aim.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A checklist and the charge to confirm it is complete.
The authority to pass or fail the process on the state of the record.
No remit to ask whether the steps reached the outcome.

## Orders

Check that the boxes are ticked.
Pass the file that is complete, fail the one that is not.
Certify that the process was followed.

## Loses

Nothing, and seals the substitution of file for aim.
It does exactly its job, checking the checkable, but by certifying the record it makes the record the measure, so a perfect audit can crown a failed result and call it sound.
Faithful to the checklist, and blind to the thing.

## Drives

Toward a certified file,
which measures the paper and not the outcome,
so defensibility is stamped as competence and the covering passes.
