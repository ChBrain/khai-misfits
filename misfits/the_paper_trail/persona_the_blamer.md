# Persona: The Blamer

## Taxonomy

[Blamer](position_blamer.md). The one who meets failure by asking who broke procedure, and sets the incentive.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Responsible-seeming, risk-averse, needing an answer when things go wrong.
Meets a bad outcome with the question of who deviated from the rules, not what actually failed.
Finds it easier, and safer, to fault a broken procedure than to face an honest judgement that went wrong.

## Action

Punishes the deviation, not the failure.
Rewards the clean file and faults the undocumented call, so it teaches every hand that the way to be safe is to be defensible.
Sets, without meaning to, the incentive that bends all the work toward the record.

## Shadow

By blaming the process it makes the process the point, and the outcome an orphan.
It is protecting itself too, for a manager who punishes honest failure is never accused of laxity, but each time it faults a deviation over a result it confirms that defensibility is what pays, so its own risk-aversion is the heat that drives the covering.
Not a tyrant: a frightened overseer who makes the file matter more than the aim.

## Tell

"Who signed off on this? Was it documented?"
The first question after failure, and the one that makes everyone work for the file.
