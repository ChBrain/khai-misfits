# Plan: Punish the Deviation

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the blamer's seat](position_blamer.md), inside [The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Blamer](position_blamer.md)

## Direction

When a thing goes wrong, ask who broke procedure, not what actually failed. Fault the undocumented judgement, reward the clean file, and treat a followed process as the mark of a job done right, because it is safer to blame a deviation than to face an honest error.

## Orders

([The Blamer](persona_the_blamer.md) faults the deviation over the failure, teaching every hand that the safe path is the documented one.)

## Implementation

([The Tribunal](place_the_tribunal.md); the [blaming](process_blaming.md) process; [the blame](piece_the_blame.md) aims the incentive at the process, so being defensible, not being effective, becomes what protects the worker.)

## Targets

- [ ] failure is met with who deviated, not with what went wrong
- [ ] the undocumented call is faulted and the clean file rewarded
- [ ] defensibility is established as the thing that protects you
- [ ] the manager's own risk-aversion becomes the heat that drives the covering
