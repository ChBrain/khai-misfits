# Persona: The Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Reformer](position_reformer.md). The one who changes what is punished, and must not strip the safeguard with the ritual.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Clear-eyed, patient, aiming at the incentive rather than the paper.
Sees that the covering follows the blame, so the file cannot be cut until the blame is changed.
Means to judge the honesty of the judgement, not the thickness of the record, and to give people the safety to own an outcome.

## Action

Changes what is judged, and prunes with care.
Rewards the honest failure over the documented folly, protects the one who owns a result, and strips the defensive paper that served only blamelessness.
Turns the incentive back toward the aim, so the work can follow.

## Shadow

The hand that strips the defensive file cannot always tell it from the safeguard.
Some of the paper was real, a check that was actually holding something up, and it looks exactly like the covering ritual, so the reformer's pruning risks tearing out a genuine protection, and its change to the incentive is resisted by a blame culture that protects those who run it.
Not a clean liberator: a careful pruner who cannot be sure which paper was load-bearing.

## Tell

"Judge the judgement, not the file, and cut the paper gently."
The whole cure and its whole danger in one careful line.
