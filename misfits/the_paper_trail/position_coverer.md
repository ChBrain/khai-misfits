# Position: Coverer

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The seat that makes each act defensible, and works for the record.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Work that could be done for the outcome or for the file.
The knowledge that failure will be judged by whether the process was followed.
The choice, act by act, between being right and being covered.

## Orders

Leave a clean file.
Do the documented thing, get the sign-off, raise the paper.
When the outcome and the record diverge, protect the record.

## Loses

Nothing when it fails, and the aim it worked around.
Its clean file shields it from the blame that an honest, undocumented judgement would draw, so it is right to cover, but every cover is effort spent on blamelessness instead of the result.
Safe, and pointed at the wrong target.

## Drives

Toward the defensible act,
which protects the doer and not the aim,
so the work bends toward the record and away from the outcome.
