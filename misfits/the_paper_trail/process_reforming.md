# Process: Reforming

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The move to judge the judgement and not the file, and the two dangers it runs into.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A recognition that the incentive is aimed at the wrong target, and a hand willing to turn it.
The reformer, at [the reform](place_the_reform.md), moving to reward the honest judgement over the thick file and to give people the safety to own an outcome.

## Direction

Toward an incentive pointed back at the aim.
What is punished is changed, so the honest failure is spared and the dishonest judgement is not, the safety to own a result is given, and the defensive paper is pruned, so that effort can flow to the outcome once more instead of to the record.

## Lever

A just culture that separates the honest error from the reckless act (Dekker, Reason).
Only when covering stops paying does effort return to the aim, so the reform must change what the tribunal rewards, not merely ask people to try harder, against a blame culture entrenched and often serving those who run it.

## Echo

Work turned back toward the aim, or a safeguard torn out with the ritual.
The incentive re-pointed and the paper thinned, but the pruning hand cannot tell the defensive file from the genuine check, so the reform ends either in effort restored to the outcome or in a real safeguard cut at the peril of a Chesterton's fence.
