# Process: Blaming

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). A bad outcome met with who broke procedure rather than what went wrong, and the lesson every hand takes from it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A failure, and a review that asks the wrong question of it.
The blamer, at [the tribunal](place_the_tribunal.md), meeting a bad result not with what failed but with who deviated from the rules.

## Direction

Toward an incentive pointed at the process, not the aim.
The undocumented judgement is faulted and the clean file is spared, so each hand watching learns that the safe work is the documented work, and the reward moves from reaching the outcome to being able to show the steps.

## Lever

Punishment that lands on the deviation and never on the covered failure.
Because a followed process is treated as a job done right, defensibility becomes what protects the worker, and the whole company bends its effort toward the record, since that, and not the result, is what the tribunal will judge.

## Echo

A room full of hands who now work to be blameless.
The deviation punished, the clean file rewarded, and an incentive set that will, act by act, crowd the aim out behind the effort to be covered.
