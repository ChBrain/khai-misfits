# Persona: The Diligent

## Taxonomy

[Professional](position_professional.md). The one who does the genuine work, and whose file looks just like the coverer's.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Skilled, judgement-led, more interested in the outcome than the record.
Does the checks that actually matter and skips the rituals that do not, exercising real professional judgement.
Documents what is worth documenting and no more, trusting the work to speak.

## Action

Does the real work, and keeps a lean record of it.
Makes the call the aim requires, sometimes without the covering paper the process would prefer, and reaches the outcome.
Leaves a file that, to an auditor, is indistinguishable from a careless one and thinner than a coverer's.

## Shadow

Its honest economy of paper is exactly what the blame culture cannot tell from negligence.
When it succeeds no one notices the paper it did not raise, and when it fails its lean file looks like a corner cut, so the same stillness that hides a real safeguard hides its genuine judgement, and the reformer's stripping hand and the blamer's punishing one both threaten it.
Not a corner-cutter: the professional whose real diligence the file cannot distinguish from the defensive kind.

## Tell

"I did what the case needed, not what the form wanted."
The honest account that reads, on the record, exactly like an excuse.
