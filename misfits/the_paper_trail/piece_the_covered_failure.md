# Piece: The Covered Failure

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The outcome missed and disowned: the thing fails anyway, and every covered hand truthfully points to its spotless file.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the tribunal](place_the_tribunal.md), where the failure is reviewed and no author is found.
Where the aim, missed, meets a row of clean records that each answer for their own box and none for the result.

## Load Bearing

Without it the trail would be a cost with a culprit, and someone would still answer for the aim.
With it the loop is complete and empty: the covered failure is the outcome missed behind a full file, the folly no undocumented judgement produced and no clean record prevented, so it happens and then belongs to no one. The Covered Failure is load bearing because it is the payoff the whole structure delivers: not a folly hidden but a folly owned by nobody, the proof that a chain optimised for blamelessness will reach its bad end and disperse the blame for it, and the reason effort spent on the file buys survival and not success.

## Apparent

Nobody's fault.
A bad outcome after which every hand is shown to have followed the process looks like an honest failure that no one could have prevented, so the covered failure reads as bad luck, and the truth that the covering caused it and the process it followed was the wrong target, that a hundred right-for-me acts summed to a wrong result, stays hidden behind the spotlessness of every file.

## Yearbook

Made through [blaming](process_blaming.md) come full circle, the end [Cover Yourself](plan_cover_yourself.md) reaches and [Seek the Outcome](plan_seek_the_outcome.md) bears.
It is where the trail arrives: the Covered Failure is [the displacement](piece_the_displacement.md) paid out, dissolved by [the diffusion](piece_the_diffusion.md), the missed aim at the heart of [the paper trail](piece_the_paper_trail.md).
