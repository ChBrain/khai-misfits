# Process: Displacing

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). Finite effort flowing to what is rewarded, so the aim recedes behind the work of being blameless.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A covering that pays, and a need that does not.
At [the bedside](place_the_bedside.md), a real outcome asked for, and an effort that the incentive has already turned toward the file.

## Direction

Toward the record and away from the result.
The beneficiary presents the genuine need, but the hand serving it is rewarded for the defensive act, so effort spent on the file is effort not spent on the aim, and the outcome is crowded out by the ritual raised to shield it.

## Lever

The scarcity of effort under an incentive aimed at the wrong target.
Because no one can both cover the file and serve the aim with the same hour, and only one of the two protects them, the goal displaces (Merton): the measure becomes the target, the file becomes the work, and the diligent judgement that skips the ritual looks, on paper, like a corner cut.

## Echo

A need met with a process instead of an outcome.
The beneficiary served the cover, the diligent hand indistinguishable from the careless one, and the aim quietly lost to the paper raised in its place.
