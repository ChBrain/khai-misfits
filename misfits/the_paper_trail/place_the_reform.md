# Place: The Reform

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). Where the incentive is changed, or the blame culture holds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A move to judge the judgement and not the file, to protect the one who owns an outcome, to prune the defensive paper.
The reformer at work, the blame culture's keepers resisting, and a pile of paper in which the ritual and the safeguard look the same.

## Holds

The cure, and its danger together.
Here the incentive can be turned from the process to the honesty of the judgement, and here the pruning hand may tear out a real check with the defensive file, so the reform holds both the remedy and the safeguard it risks.

## Offers

A way to turn the work back toward the aim.
The reform can change what is punished, so that covering stops paying and effort flows to the outcome again, offering the one real route out of the trail.

## Withheld

A safe way to tell the ritual from the safeguard.
The reform cannot mark which paper was load-bearing, since the defensive file and the genuine check look identical, so it withholds the certainty its pruning needs and cuts only at the peril of a Chesterton's fence.
