# Plot: The Displacement

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The bedside](place_the_bedside.md), a real need present and asking for the outcome.
Effort is finite, and the incentive has already turned it toward the file, so the need arrives at a hand rewarded for shielding it rather than serving it.

## Action

[Displacing](process_displacing.md): [The Beneficiary](persona_the_beneficiary.md), holding [the beneficiary's seat](position_beneficiary.md), asks for the result and is handed the defensive service, while [The Diligent](persona_the_diligent.md), holding [the professional's seat](position_professional.md), does the real work on judgement and leaves a file no one can tell from a careless one.

## Stage

[The Beneficiary](persona_the_beneficiary.md) tables [Seek the Outcome](plan_seek_the_outcome.md) and meets [the displacement](piece_the_displacement.md), served the cover in place of the aim.
[The Diligent](persona_the_diligent.md) tables [Use My Judgement](plan_use_my_judgement.md) and its lean, honest record reads, against the thick files around it, like a corner cut.

## Tension

The need is met with a process, and the one hand serving it truly cannot prove it did.
No hour covers the file and serves the aim at once, and only the file protects the worker, so the aim loses the effort quietly and by the arithmetic of it: the beneficiary gets the ritual instead of the result, the diligent judgement that skipped the ritual looks negligent on paper, and the goal displaces without anyone deciding to abandon it.
