# Position: Professional

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The seat that does the genuine work, and leaves a file like the coverer's.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The judgement to do what the aim requires.
A lean record, of what is worth recording and no more.
No easy way to prove, on paper, that its economy of file was competence and not neglect.

## Orders

Do the real work.
Make the call the case needs, document what matters, skip the ritual that does not.
Reach the outcome, and trust the work to show.

## Loses

The benefit of the doubt, when the file is read instead of the work.
Its thin, honest record looks to an auditor like a careless one, so when it succeeds the paper it wisely did not raise goes unnoticed, and when it fails its leanness reads as a corner cut.
Right in its judgement, and defenceless on the record.

## Drives

Toward the outcome and a lean file,
which the blame culture cannot tell from negligence,
so the real diligence is at risk from the punishing hand and the pruning one alike.
