# Plan: Change the Incentive

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), inside [The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Reformer](position_reformer.md)

## Direction

Aim at the incentive, not the paper. Judge the honesty of the judgement rather than the thickness of the file, reward the honest failure over the documented folly, and give people the safety to own an outcome. Then, and only then, strip the defensive paper, and strip it gently, for some of it is a real safeguard.

## Orders

([The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) changes what is punished and prunes with care, unable to be sure which paper was load-bearing.)

## Implementation

([The Reform](place_the_reform.md); the [reforming](process_reforming.md) process; [the paper trail](piece_the_paper_trail.md) is answered by turning [the blame](piece_the_blame.md) from the process to the honesty of the judgement, at the peril of a Chesterton's fence.)

## Targets

- [ ] the judgement, not the file, is made the thing that is judged
- [ ] the honest failure is rewarded over the documented folly
- [ ] people are given the safety to own a result
- [ ] the defensive paper is pruned without tearing out the safeguard hidden in it
