# Persona: The Auditor

## Taxonomy

[Auditor](position_auditor.md). The one who certifies the boxes, and mistakes the file for the aim.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Methodical, literal, satisfied by a completed checklist.
Was told to check that the boxes are ticked, and checks that the boxes are ticked.
Reads a full file as a job done, and an empty box as the only kind of failure it can see.

## Action

Certifies the process as followed.
Passes the audit when the record is complete, signs that the steps were taken, and asks nothing about whether the steps reached the outcome.
Turns a spotless file into an official verdict that all is well.

## Shadow

It validates defensibility as if it were effectiveness, and so seals the substitution.
It is doing exactly its job, checking the checkable, but by certifying the record it makes the record the measure, so a perfect audit can sit atop a failed aim and call it sound, and its diligence is what lets the covering pass for competence.
Not corrupt: a faithful checker of boxes that were never the point.

## Tell

"All the boxes are ticked. It passes."
The true verdict on the file that says nothing about the thing.
