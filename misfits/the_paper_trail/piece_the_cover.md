# Piece: The Cover

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The single defensive act: the deed done for the record it leaves, not the outcome it reaches.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the desk](place_the_desk.md), where each act is chosen for how it will read.
Where the shielding test is ordered, the covering email sent, the extra sign-off gathered.

## Load Bearing

Without it the incentive would be a threat with no answer, and the paper would never accrete.
With it the worker has a move that protects them whatever happens: the cover is the act picked for its defensibility, the one that leaves a clean file whether or not it served the aim, and it is rational every time because the documented folly is spared what the undocumented judgement is not. The Cover is load bearing because it is the atom of the trail (Studdert et al.: the defensive act done to protect the doer, not the patient): every one of them is right for the one who makes it, and every one of them spends effort on the record that the outcome will never get back.

## Apparent

Diligence.
An act fully documented, signed off, and filed looks like care taken and duty done, so the cover reads as thoroughness, and the fact that it was chosen for the doer and not the aim, that its point is the file and not the result, stays out of sight behind the look of a careful hand.
