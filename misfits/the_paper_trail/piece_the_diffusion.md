# Piece: The Diffusion

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). Responsibility spread across a chain where everyone owns their box and no one owns the result.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the tribunal](place_the_tribunal.md), where the blame comes looking for an author.
Where a hundred clean files answer, each showing its own step taken, its own rule followed.

## Load Bearing

Without it the covering would still leave one hand to hold, and the failure would find its author.
With it the failure finds no one: the diffusion is the spread of ownership across every covered hand, so that each can point to a spotless file and truthfully say the process was followed, and the sign-off gathered to share a decision has shared the blame for it too. The Diffusion is load bearing because it closes the loop: the same paper that made each hand defensible makes the whole chain authorless, so accountability, chased to its end, finds a wall of clean records and no one behind them.

## Apparent

Shared responsibility.
A decision signed off by many looks like an organisation that spread its judgement wisely and took no reckless step alone, so the diffusion reads as prudence, and the truth that a blame shared by all is a blame borne by none, that the spreading was defensive and not deliberative, stays hidden behind the look of collective care.

## Yearbook

Made through [blaming](process_blaming.md), the end [the blame](piece_the_blame.md) reaches when the failure it aimed to prevent arrives.
It is what defeats the search for an author: the Diffusion turns [the record](piece_the_record.md) into a wall of clean hands, and is the mechanism by which [the covered failure](piece_the_covered_failure.md) comes to rest on no one.
