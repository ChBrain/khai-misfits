# Persona: The Coverer

## Taxonomy

[Coverer](position_coverer.md). The one who does the defensible act, and works for the record.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful, conscientious, watchful of its own exposure.
Knows that when a thing fails, the question will be whether the process was followed, not whether the aim was reached.
Chooses the act that leaves a clean file over the act that would serve the outcome, when they differ.

## Action

Covers itself, act by act.
Orders the test that shields the chart, sends the email that proves the concern was raised, gets the extra sign-off that spreads the risk, and documents everything.
Builds a spotless record, whether or not the record helped the thing succeed.

## Shadow

Its prudence points at the wrong target, and it hits it perfectly.
It is right that a documented folly will be forgiven and an undocumented judgement punished, so working for the file is sound self-protection, but the file is not the aim, so every careful cover is effort taken from the outcome and given to blamelessness.
Not lazy: a diligent worker aiming, rationally, at the wrong thing.

## Tell

"Make sure it's documented, whatever happens."
The prudent instruction that shifts the work from the outcome to the record.
