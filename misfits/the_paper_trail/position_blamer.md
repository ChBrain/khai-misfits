# Position: Blamer

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The seat that judges failure by the process, and sets the incentive.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The power to assign fault when a thing goes wrong.
A choice of question: what failed, or who deviated.
The safety, for itself, of faulting a broken rule over a bad judgement.

## Orders

Meet failure with who broke procedure.
Fault the undocumented call, reward the clean file.
Ask whether the process was followed, not whether the aim was reached.

## Loses

Nothing, and makes the process the point.
It protects itself by never being lax, but each time it faults a deviation over a result it teaches that defensibility is what pays, so its own risk-aversion is the heat that drives the covering.
Safe from the charge of laxity, and the author of the whole trail.

## Drives

Toward fault assigned to the deviation,
which is safer to punish than the honest error,
so the incentive it sets bends every hand toward the file.
