# Piece: The Paper Trail

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The phenomenon whole: work bent from the outcome to the record by a blame aimed at the process, and its remedy in a changed incentive at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the reform](place_the_reform.md), where the incentive is turned or the blame culture holds.
And across the whole chain, in every act done for the file and every box ticked instead of aimed at the outcome.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of covers with no shape.
With it the moves become one law: the blame, the cover, the record, the displacement, the diffusion, and the covered failure are facets of a single machine, in which a failure judged by whether the process was followed teaches every hand to work for the record instead of the result, so effort flows to the paper, the aim recedes, responsibility diffuses, and the failure at the end belongs to no one. The Paper Trail is load bearing because it is the whole finding with its whole remedy attached (Merton on goal displacement; Tetlock on process accountability): the only answer is to judge the honesty of the judgement and not the thickness of the file, a change dear to make, resisted by those the blame culture serves, and unable to tell the defensive paper from the safeguard hidden in it.

## Apparent

Either a diligent organisation undone by bad luck, or a swamp of pointless bureaucracy to be slashed.
Seen from one side the paper is care and the failure is misfortune no one could have prevented; from the other, every form is waste and every check is red tape to cut; and both miss the structure, that the paper is a rational answer to a misaimed blame and some of it is load-bearing, so neither praise nor a blanket cull answers it, only a change to what is punished, which is the very thing the culture's keepers resist and which cannot mark the ritual from the real check. The trail is not laziness and not honest bureaucracy: it is effort, real and diligent, pointed by the incentive at the wrong target.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) through [reforming](process_reforming.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: the Paper Trail is what [the blame](piece_the_blame.md) makes of the work when the process becomes the target, undone only by turning the blame from the file to the honesty of the judgement, at the peril of a Chesterton's fence.
