# Plot: The Blame

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The tribunal](place_the_tribunal.md), a bad outcome brought up for review.
The question on the table is who broke procedure, not what actually went wrong, and every hand in the room is watching to see what gets punished.

## Action

[Blaming](process_blaming.md): [The Blamer](persona_the_blamer.md), holding [the blamer's seat](position_blamer.md), faults the deviation and spares the clean file, while [The Coverer](persona_the_coverer.md), holding [the coverer's seat](position_coverer.md), watches and reads the lesson exactly.

## Stage

[The Blamer](persona_the_blamer.md) tables [Punish the Deviation](plan_punish_the_deviation.md) and lays down [the blame](piece_the_blame.md), aiming the incentive at the process and not the aim.
[The Coverer](persona_the_coverer.md) sees which way it points and readies [Cover Yourself](plan_cover_yourself.md), having learned that the safe path is the documented one.

## Tension

The room is taught the wrong lesson, and taught it correctly.
Nothing here is a mistake: the blamer really is safer faulting a deviation than facing an honest error, and the coverer really is safer documenting than judging, so the tribunal sets, cleanly and rationally, an incentive pointed at the record instead of the result, and every hand leaves knowing that to be defensible is to be safe, whether or not to be defensible is to be right.
