# Piece: The Displacement

## Taxonomy

[The Paper Trail](play_the_paper_trail.md). The aim crowded out: finite effort drawn off the outcome and onto the record that pays.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the bedside](place_the_bedside.md), where the real need is either served or shielded.
Where the outcome asked for meets the effort the incentive has already turned toward the file.

## Load Bearing

Without it the covering would cost nothing, and the trail would be harmless paper beside a job still done.
With it the covering has a price, and the aim pays it: because no hour spent on the file is an hour spent on the outcome, and only the file protects the worker, the goal is displaced (Merton: the measure becomes the target). The Displacement is load bearing because it is where the harm lands: it turns the covering from waste into damage, hands the beneficiary a process instead of a result, and makes the diligent judgement that skips the ritual look, on the file, identical to a corner cut.

## Apparent

Thoroughness.
Effort visibly poured into documentation, checks, and sign-offs looks like a job being done with great care, so the displacement reads as diligence, and the truth that this effort is subtracted from the aim, that the care is spent on the record and not the result, stays hidden behind the industry of it.

## Yearbook

Made through [displacing](process_displacing.md), the cost that [Use My Judgement](plan_use_my_judgement.md) resists and [Seek the Outcome](plan_seek_the_outcome.md) bears.
It is what [the cover](piece_the_cover.md) does to the aim: the Displacement spends what [the record](piece_the_record.md) accretes, and delivers the beneficiary to [the covered failure](piece_the_covered_failure.md).
