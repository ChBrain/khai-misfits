# Persona: The Reckoner

## Taxonomy

[Accountant](position_accountant.md). The one who does the private cost-benefit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Precise, honest, no thumb on the scale.
He totals every figure.
He totals them all from one seat.

## Action

He sets the whole gain of an animal against his own fractional share of its cost.
He carries the columns down.
He finds the answer is add.

## Shadow

The arithmetic is exact and blind to every other seat.
He never adds the seats he cannot see.
So an honest sum yields a ruinous rule.

## Tell

"The numbers say take."
They do, from where he sits.
