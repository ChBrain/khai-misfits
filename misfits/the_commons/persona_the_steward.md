# Persona: The Steward

## Taxonomy

[Conserver](position_conserver.md). Grazes less to spare the field, and loses by it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful, far-sighted, a patient man.
He keeps fewer animals than he could.
He thinks of the field next year.

## Action

He pulls beasts off the grass.
He watches the spared grass eaten by the others.
He falls behind them.

## Shadow

He is right that the field needs sparing.
He is wrong that he can spare it alone.
So his restraint is a gift to the takers and a loss to himself.

## Tell

"If we all held back..."
But we do not.
