# Persona: The Bylaw

## Taxonomy

[Rule-Maker](position_rule_maker.md). Writes the cap all agree is wise.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fair-minded, careful, a good pen.
He drafts the sensible rule.
He believes a good rule is enough.

## Action

He proposes the number the green can bear.
All at the table nod.
None is bound to it.

## Shadow

A rule no one is held to is only a suggestion.
Agreement is not compliance.
So the cap is written and the grass still goes.

## Tell

"Everyone agreed to the cap."
Everyone also grazed past it.
