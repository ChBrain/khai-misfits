# Piece: The Private Gain

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The whole benefit of one more, kept by the one who takes it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), on the line where the herder totals what one more animal earns him.
The full value of the extra beast: the extra deploy squeezed onto the shared pipeline, the extra ask dropped on the platform team, the extra branch left on the common trunk. All of it lands with the one who took it.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to pull against restraint, and the field is easy to spare.
With it the pull is one-sided, because the Private Gain is whole and undivided: the herder who adds an animal keeps every ounce of what it earns, and pays only his thin slice of what it costs the grass. The good of the act is concentrated on one seat and the harm is smeared across all seats, so from that one seat the act is simply worth doing, again and again.

## Apparent

Just getting your due.
It looks like ordinary self-interest, the same reasonable wish to do a little better that runs any honest trade. Nothing about it feels like plunder, because it is not: the herder is only keeping what his own animal makes, which is his by every fair rule.

## Yearbook

Banked by [the grazer](persona_the_grazer.md), reckoned by [the reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md), refused by [the steward](persona_the_steward.md) at his own expense.
It is one half of the split that drives the play: set whole against [the shared cost](piece_the_shared_cost.md), it makes [the one more](piece_the_one_more.md) rational every time, no matter what it does to the field.
