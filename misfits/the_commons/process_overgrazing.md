# Process: Overgrazing

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The aggregate.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Every herder grazing at once.
No one signalled, and no one needed to.
The same sound reasoning, at every seat.

## Direction

The herd driven past what the grass can renew.
More mouths than the field can grow back in a season.
The stock crossing its own ceiling.

## Lever

The sum of individually-rational draws.
Each one sound, each one defensible, taken alone.
Added together, more than the green can bear.

## Echo

The overshoot, the taking gone beyond return.
Losses that compound instead of healing.
Each round poorer for the last round's plenty.
