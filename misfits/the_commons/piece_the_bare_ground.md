# Piece: The Bare Ground

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The field eaten down to dirt, and restraint punished.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the bare patch](place_the_bare_patch.md), spreading outward from where the grass first failed.
The depleted resource made plain: the platform team burnt out, the pipeline jammed solid, the codebase a swamp of half-mended debt. What everyone drew on and no one could spare, now unable to serve anyone.

## Load Bearing

Without it the tragedy is only feared, an argument on paper about a field still green.
With it the tragedy is realised, and its cruellest term shows: on the Bare Ground the one who grazed less is worst off, because the grass he spared was eaten by the others and he took less of a field now equally dead. Restraint did not save the field and did cost the restrained, so the Bare Ground proves the giver a fool and the taker merely early, which is the lesson that keeps any next commons from being spared.

## Apparent

A ruin no one chose.
It looks like bad luck or hard times, a field that simply failed, because no single act on it was the ruinous one. There is no culprit standing on the Bare Ground, only a crowd of neighbours who each took a fair share and a field that could not survive their fairness.

## Yearbook

Reached by [depletion](process_depletion.md), inherited alike by [the steward](persona_the_steward.md) who spared it and [the taker](persona_the_taker.md) who did not.
It is where the play would end without a fix: past [the overshoot](piece_the_overshoot.md), the field arrives here by arithmetic, and only [enclosure](process_enclosure.md) from outside the open commons can turn the ground back to grass.
