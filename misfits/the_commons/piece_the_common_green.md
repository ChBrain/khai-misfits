# Piece: The Common Green

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The shared field open to all and owned by none.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the green](place_the_green.md), the ground everyone may use and no one may close.
A field held in common: the shared codebase, the platform team's hours, the on-call rotation, the CI queue, the brand's goodwill. Any pooled thing each may draw on freely and none can be shut out of.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no tragedy: a field with an owner is grazed to what the owner can bear, because the owner keeps both the gain and the harm.
With it the gain and the harm come apart. The Common Green is rivalrous, one animal's mouthful is not another's, and non-excludable, no neighbour can be kept off it, and those two facts together are the whole engine: what is everyone's to use is no one's to spare, because sparing it only leaves more for the next to take.

## Apparent

A generous arrangement.
It looks like abundance shared: a field free to all, a resource no one is denied, a commons in the warm old sense. The trap is invisible while the grass is thick, because plenty hides the arithmetic, and the arithmetic only shows when the field starts to fail.

## Yearbook

Grazed by [the grazer](persona_the_grazer.md) and drawn on by [the taker](persona_the_taker.md), spared in vain by [the steward](persona_the_steward.md).
It is the ground the whole play stands on: because the green is shared, [the shared cost](piece_the_shared_cost.md) of using it is split while [the private gain](piece_the_private_gain.md) is kept, and that split is the wedge that drives the field to [the bare ground](piece_the_bare_ground.md).
