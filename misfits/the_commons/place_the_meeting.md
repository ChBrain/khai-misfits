# Place: The Meeting

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The assembly.

## Owner

## Shown

The neighbours gathered over a failing field.
Chairs in a rough ring, hats in hand.
Faces that all know the bare ground is coming.

## Holds

The proposed cap, written on a slip.
The wise rule spoken aloud, nodded to.
A sense of what ought to be done.

## Offers

A place to agree a limit together.
Voices that reach agreement by dusk.
The shape of a promise.

## Withheld

Any power to bind the promise made.
Talk is free, breaking it is freer.
No teeth here, until a watch is set on it.
