# Process: Depletion

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The field failing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The draw crossing the renewal rate.
More taken each round than the grass grows back.
The tipping quiet, unmarked when it happens.

## Direction

The resource falling below where it recovers.
Past the point the field could climb from on its own.
Downward now, and the slope steepens.

## Lever

Each round starting poorer than the last.
Less grass to grow the next season's grass.
The deficit feeding on itself.

## Echo

The bare ground, brown and spreading.
Restraint proven a private loss, never repaid.
The field a ruin that every fair share built.
