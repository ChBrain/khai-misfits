# Piece: The Tragedy

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The whole shape at once, and its costly cure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Over [the green](place_the_green.md) entire, from the full field to the fenced one.
The phenomenon named: a shared resource, freely drawn, ruined not by malice but by the honest arithmetic of everyone taking a fair share, and mended, if at all, only by a rule that closes what was open.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a heap of grazings with no shape.
With it the play has its thesis: the Tragedy is that ruin needs no villain, only a commons, a private gain, a shared cost, and neighbours rational enough to add one more. It is also, and this is the second half the name usually drops, not inevitable: a commons can be governed, by neighbours who bind themselves with rules they make, watch, and enforce, so the Tragedy carries its own remedy, which is not the free market and not the distant state but the hard, unglamorous work of the commoners governing their own green.

## Apparent

An old cautionary tale.
It looks like a settled verdict, the commons must fail, privatise it or police it, the moral everyone half-remembers. The fuller finding is quieter and less tidy: many commons endure for centuries under home-grown rule, and the tragedy is not the commons but the ungoverned commons, which is a different and more hopeful thing.

## Yearbook

Averted, when it is averted, by [the bylaw](persona_the_bylaw.md) and [the warden](persona_the_warden.md) through [enclosure](process_enclosure.md), at the price of the open freedom the green began with.
It is the piece all the others resolve into: [the common green](piece_the_common_green.md) and its split of [private gain](piece_the_private_gain.md) from [shared cost](piece_the_shared_cost.md) make the Tragedy, and only self-governance, costly and imperfect, unmakes it.
