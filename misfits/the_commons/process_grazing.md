# Process: Grazing

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The individual draw.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A herder with an animal to feed.
The green in view, the gate standing open.
No reason found to hold back.

## Direction

One beast led onto the common grass.
Head down, and the feeding begins.
The count goes up by one.

## Lever

Private gain against a fractional cost.
The whole of the animal is the herder's, the wear on the field is everyone's.
So the draw always pays the one who makes it.

## Echo

A field a shade thinner than before.
The blade this beast took, gone from the common store.
And the next herder's turn, reading the same ledger.
