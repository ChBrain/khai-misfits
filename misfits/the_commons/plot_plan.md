# Plot: Plan

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The green](place_the_green.md) stands full and open, and every neighbour is free to graze it.
At [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), a herder weighs whether to turn out one more animal. The grass is thick, the field is shared, and the only question is his own.

## Action

[Grazing](process_grazing.md): [The Grazer](persona_the_grazer.md), holding [the commoner's seat](position_commoner.md), considers adding one more animal to the common green.
Alongside, [The Reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md) at [the accountant's seat](position_accountant.md) works the private math that will decide it.

## Stage

[The Reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md) tables [Count My Share](plan_count_my_share.md): total the gain and the cost from one seat, and find that [the private gain](piece_the_private_gain.md) of the animal is whole to its owner while [the shared cost](piece_the_shared_cost.md) is split among all.
[The Grazer](persona_the_grazer.md) tables [Add One More](plan_add_one_more.md): turn out the beast, because on [the common green](piece_the_common_green.md) one more is a clear yes for the one who owns it.

## Tension

Nothing here is greedy.
The herder is only doing the sum any sensible person would do, counting what an animal earns him against what it costs him, and the honest answer is that it earns him a whole share and costs him a sliver. But the sum is done from one seat, and every seat on the green will do the same sum and reach the same yes, so the reasonable private answer, multiplied by all who share the field, is the first step toward a field that cannot feed them.
