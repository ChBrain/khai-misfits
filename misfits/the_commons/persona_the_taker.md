# Persona: The Taker

## Taxonomy

[Free Rider](position_free_rider.md). Draws on the field's upkeep and adds none.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Busy, always busy, and grateful for it.
Not malicious, only pressed for time.
Means to pitch in sometime.

## Action

He crosses the mended fence.
He uses the fixed thing and thinks well of whoever fixed it.
Then he moves on to his own work.

## Shadow

The upkeep helps everyone and can be billed to no one.
His reasonable thrift is only with his own time.
So the mending is left to rot.

## Tell

"Someone's got it."
No one has it.
