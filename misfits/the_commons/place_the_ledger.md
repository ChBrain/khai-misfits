# Place: The Ledger

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The private reckoning.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A herder's own account of gain and cost.
One page, one hand, kept close to the chest.
Numbers a neighbour never sees.

## Holds

The whole gain on one line, the herder's alone.
The fractional cost on another, shared out thin.
A margin that always favours the writer.

## Offers

A clear verdict, printed plain: add.
The arithmetic never argues back.
One more pays, so one more it is.

## Withheld

Every other seat's ledger, closed to this one.
The common total none of them keep.
The tally that would say enough, kept by no one.
