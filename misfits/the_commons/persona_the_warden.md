# Persona: The Warden

## Taxonomy

[Governor](position_governor.md). Binds each neighbour to the cap with a watch and a penalty.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Firm, trusted, plain about the cost.
He is willing to close the green.
He is the reversal the field was waiting for.

## Action

He sets the watch and names the penalty.
The overgrazer is caught and fined.
The grass comes back.

## Shadow

The field is saved only by ending the openness that made it a commons.
The fence costs the freedom.
It holds only while it is enforced.

## Tell

"The gate is shut now, and the grass is back."
Both are true.
