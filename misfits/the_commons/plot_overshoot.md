# Plot: Overshoot

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The herd](place_the_herd.md), growing, because every neighbour ran the same sum and reached the same yes.
The private math that told one herder to add one more told all of them, so the animals go onto the grass together, and the upkeep the field needs is another matter entirely.

## Action

[Overgrazing](process_overgrazing.md): [The Grazer](persona_the_grazer.md), holding [the commoner's seat](position_commoner.md), adds his animal, and so does every commoner beside him, and the herd swells.
[Free Riding](process_free_riding.md): [The Taker](persona_the_taker.md) at [the free rider's seat](position_free_rider.md) draws on the field and leaves its keeping to whoever will do it.

## Stage

[The Grazer](persona_the_grazer.md) tables [Add One More](plan_add_one_more.md): put another beast on the green, and [the one more](piece_the_one_more.md), summed over every herder, becomes a herd past what the grass can grow back.
[The Taker](persona_the_taker.md) tables [Let Them Mend It](plan_let_them_mend_it.md): enjoy the field's upkeep without adding to it, because [the free ride](piece_the_free_ride.md) cannot be denied to anyone, so the green is over-taken and under-kept in the same season.

## Tension

Everyone is being reasonable, and the herd outgrows the field.
Each herder adds an animal that pays, and each declines an upkeep that is nobody's job in particular, and both choices are sound from where each stands. But the sound choices sum into [the overshoot](piece_the_overshoot.md): the draw crosses what the grass renews just as the mending falls to no one, so the field is eaten faster than it grows and tended slower than it fails, and the record herd is grazing on borrowed grass.
