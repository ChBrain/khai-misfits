# Pitch: A Fair Share

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Neighbourly, reasonable, fair.
Everyone takes only their share, no one grabs, no one cheats, and each is a good neighbour keeping to what is theirs.
The voice of a shared field worked in common, spoken by people who would never take more than their due and cannot see that their due, all added up, is more than the field can bear.

## Undertow

Under the fairness, a field being eaten faster than it grows.
Every fair share is a whole gain to the one who takes it and a small harm split among all, so every neighbour takes, and the sum of the fair shares is a herd the grass cannot feed.
No one is greedy: they only each take what is fair, and what is fair for each, summed over all, is a bare field by autumn.

## Nerve

The feeling of a good neighbourhood on shared ground,
each person modest, each share reasonable,
watching the grass thin under the honest arithmetic of everyone taking only their fair part.

## Echo

After the run: a green worn down to dirt,
a herd too big for a field that can no longer feed it,
and a neighbourhood where every share was fair and the field fed no one.
