# Piece: The Shared Cost

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The harm of one more, split so many ways it disappears.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the green](place_the_green.md), spread thin across every neighbour who grazes it.
The damage the extra animal does to the grass: the pipeline a minute slower for all, the platform team an hour more buried, the trunk a degree more tangled. Real, and divided until each one's portion is too small to feel.

## Load Bearing

Without it every herder pays his own damage and stops when it stops paying, and the field self-limits.
With it the brake is gone, because the Shared Cost is an externality: the harm of the extra animal falls on the commons, so the herder who caused it carries only one over many of it, and that fraction is far smaller than his whole Private Gain. The cost is real and total on the field and near-zero on the one who made it, so no single herder ever meets a reason to stop.

## Apparent

A cost too small to notice.
It looks like nothing, one animal on a wide green, one deploy on a busy pipeline, and at the level of the single act it truly is almost nothing. The catastrophe is only in the sum, which no single herder pays and so no single herder sees.

## Yearbook

Split across [the grazer](persona_the_grazer.md), [the taker](persona_the_taker.md), and every seat on the green, and named too late by [the tally](persona_the_tally.md).
It is the other half of the split: whole [private gain](piece_the_private_gain.md) against fractional Shared Cost is the arithmetic that feeds [the overshoot](piece_the_overshoot.md), and the reason [restraint](process_restraint.md) never pays the one who practises it.
