# Piece: The Free Ride

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The upkeep you enjoy whether or not you pay in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the green](place_the_green.md), on the side of keeping it, not just taking from it.
The mending no one can be billed for: the flaky test someone else fixes, the shared doc someone else updates, the on-call shift someone else covers, the fence someone else mends. The benefit lands on all, the labour on whoever volunteers.

## Load Bearing

Without it the commons is only overused, and a good field might still be well kept.
With it the commons is also under-kept, because the Free Ride is non-excludability turned on provision: the upkeep of the green helps everyone, and no one can be shut out of that help, so each neighbour does best to let another do the mending and enjoy it for nothing. The same fact that lets all draw the green down, lets all decline to keep it up, and a field over-taken and under-mended fails from both ends at once.

## Apparent

Reasonable thrift with your own effort.
It looks like sensible prioritising, not shirking: everyone is busy, the upkeep is nobody's job in particular, and surely someone else will get to it. Each declines for good reasons, and the sum of the good reasons is a fence no one mends.

## Yearbook

Taken by [the taker](persona_the_taker.md) under [let them mend it](plan_let_them_mend_it.md), and quietly resented by [the steward](persona_the_steward.md), who mends and is not repaid.
It is the play's second engine, running alongside the draw: while [the one more](piece_the_one_more.md) over-grazes the field, the Free Ride under-keeps it, so [depletion](process_depletion.md) comes faster than grazing alone would bring it.
