# Persona: The Grazer

## Taxonomy

[Commoner](position_commoner.md). The herder who turns out one more animal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Practical, thrifty, a plain man.
He keeps his own count, close and honest.
A good husband of his own herd.

## Action

He turns out one more beast onto the green.
The field goes a shade thinner.
Next round he does it again.

## Shadow

Each animal is his to add, whole and to his gain.
The harm is spread thin across all the others.
So his good husbandry is exactly what bares the field.

## Tell

"One more won't break it."
Then he turns out another.
