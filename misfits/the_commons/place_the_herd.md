# Place: The Herd

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The growing stock.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The animals on the grass, heads down, feeding.
More of them each round the season turns.
A field that looks fuller every count.

## Holds

The sum of every herder's one-mores.
Each addition small, each addition sound.
Together, a number climbing.

## Offers

A full field, a record count.
A good year to the eye, plenty on the hoof.
The look of thriving.

## Withheld

The line where the count outruns the grass.
Held back, unseen, until it is crossed.
By then the crossing is already behind.
