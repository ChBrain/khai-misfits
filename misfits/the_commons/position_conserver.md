# Position: Conserver

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The seat that restrains itself and loses.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Foresight: you can see next year from here.
And the will to hold back, the rare will to graze below your share.

## Orders

Graze less than you could.
Spare the grass, leave the roots to hold.
Keep something for the season that comes.

## Loses

The spared grass to the others' herds, who take what you left.
You fall behind them, poorer for your care.

## Drives

Toward sparing the field.
Away from a field that rewards the sparer.
Restraint punished, so the far-sighted starve first.
