# Process: Enclosure

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The reversal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The neighbours binding themselves together.
The talk at the meeting given teeth at last.
A promise they agree to hold each other to.

## Direction

The open green closed under a cap.
Watched, and enforced, and counted at the gate.
The field made a governed thing.

## Lever

A rule the users make, monitor, and sanction.
Not handed down: raised by the hands it binds.
Each seat watching the next, the penalty real.

## Echo

The field holds, the grass stands to next year.
The commons is a governed thing now, no longer free.
The freedom traded for a field that lasts.
