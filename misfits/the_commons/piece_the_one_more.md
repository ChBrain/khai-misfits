# Piece: The One More

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The extra animal, always worth adding, one at a time.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the herd](place_the_herd.md), at the margin, the next beast about to be turned onto the grass.
The one more: the extra service on the shared cluster, the extra meeting on the common calendar, the extra dependency on the shared build. Each considered alone, each an easy yes.

## Load Bearing

Without it the herd is a fixed thing and the field can be planned around it.
With it the herd has no ceiling, because the One More is decided at the margin and the margin always favours adding: this animal's Private Gain is whole and its Shared Cost is a fraction, so this animal pays, and so does the one after it, and the one after that. No single step is the wrong step. The wrong is only in the total, which is reached one right step at a time.

## Apparent

A small, sensible increment.
It looks like prudence, not excess: just one more, hardly a change, well within what the field has always carried. That is exactly why it is added, and exactly why the next is too, until the sum of all the sensible one-mores is a herd the grass cannot hold.

## Yearbook

Turned out by [the grazer](persona_the_grazer.md) under [add one more](plan_add_one_more.md), justified each time by [the reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md).
It is the unit the tragedy is built from: repeated across every seat, the One More becomes [the overshoot](piece_the_overshoot.md), and the field crosses from thick grass to [bare ground](piece_the_bare_ground.md) without any single animal being the one too many.
