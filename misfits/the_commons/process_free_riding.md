# Process: Free Riding

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). Underprovision of upkeep.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A neighbour who benefits from the field's keeping.
The mended fence, the cleared water, the rested grass.
All of it his, whether he lifts a hand or not.

## Direction

Use the upkeep, add none to it.
Take the keeping's fruit, leave the keeping to another.
The work becomes someone else's, always.

## Lever

The benefit cannot be withheld, non-excludable by its nature.
So mending is left for someone else to do.
Each waits, and the waiting is rational at every seat.

## Echo

A fence no one mends, a task no one claims.
The field failing from the upkeep side too.
Not only over-taken now, but under-tended.
