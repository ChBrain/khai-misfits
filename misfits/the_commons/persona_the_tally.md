# Persona: The Tally

## Taxonomy

[Monitor](position_monitor.md). Counts the herd, warns, can bind no one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Watchful, accurate, never wrong on a number.
He sees the green failing.
He has no hand to stop it.

## Action

He counts the animals.
He measures the failing grass.
He sounds the alarm.

## Shadow

Seeing the tragedy is not the same as stopping it.
His count is true and toothless.
So the number changes nothing.

## Tell

"We are past the line."
The herd keeps growing.
