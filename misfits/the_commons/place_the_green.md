# Place: The Green

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The shared field.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A wide open field, level to the far hedge.
Free to all, no gate at any corner.
The grass stands high the morning the story starts.

## Holds

The common resource: rivalrous, non-excludable.
What one beast eats, another cannot.
Everyone's field, and so no one's.

## Offers

Grass to any who bring animals.
No question asked, no ticket taken.
Come, and let them feed.

## Withheld

Any owner who might keep it whole.
Any limit on the taking, any last blade spoken for.
The green does not say when to stop.
