# Position: Commoner

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The seat that always says add.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A share of the open green, held in common with every neighbour.
A herd of its own to feed, and mouths that want more grass.

## Orders

Graze the common field.
Grow your herd, one head at a time.
Keep your count up with the rest.

## Loses

Nothing at the margin: the next animal costs you almost nothing.
So you never meet a reason to stop.

## Drives

Toward one more animal, always the next one.
Away from any limit you might set yourself.
The field thinned by honest husbandry, the tragedy fed.
