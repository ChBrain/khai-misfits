# Process: Restraint

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The conserver's self-limit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A herder who grazes less to spare the field.
Far-sighted, kind, wanting grass next year.
Holding a beast back at his own gate.

## Direction

Fewer animals led out, spared grass left standing.
A margin given back to the common store.
The right thing done, quietly, alone.

## Lever

One seat's sacrifice against all seats' taking.
The spared blade is offered, not fenced.
Nothing binds the others to spare with him.

## Echo

The spared grass eaten by others before dusk.
The gift absorbed, the giver no richer.
The restrained left behind, poorer for being good.
