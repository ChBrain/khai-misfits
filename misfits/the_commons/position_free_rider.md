# Position: Free Rider

## Taxonomy

[The Commons](play_the_commons.md). The seat that uses upkeep and adds none.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The right to the field's keeping without paying for it.
The mended fence, the cleared ditch, all of it yours by simply being here.

## Orders

Use what is kept.
Spare your own effort for your own work.
Let the others tend it.

## Loses

Nothing, because the upkeep cannot be withheld from anyone.
The good of the field arrives at your gate whether you helped or not.

## Drives

Toward using the mended field.
Away from mending it.
The fence left unrepaired, because no one who pays is repaid.
