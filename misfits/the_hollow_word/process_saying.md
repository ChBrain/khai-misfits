# Process: Saying

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). The Speaker using the word as if full, spending the term in the sense inherited and taking that sense for the whole content.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A living word to hand and a room that uses it.
In [the currency](place_the_currency.md), each speaker taking the term and using it in the sense nearest to hand, the sense the field supplied.

## Direction

Toward the word spent and the inherited sense taken for the content.
Because a word carries not its referent but the sense its speakers supply, saying uses the term in the sense received, and because that sense comes with the fluent ease of a word everyone says, it feels like the word's whole content, so each speaker takes their inherited sense for the plain meaning and assumes the field means the same, the thinner inheritance wearing the face of the full coin.

## Lever

The ease of the inherited word.
Because a word everyone says goes down without effort and what goes down without effort feels like what is simply there, saying runs not on any misuse but on the plain sincerity of using a living word in the sense one received, the drift assembled from no error, only from each spending the coin in the sense nearest to their own hand.

## Echo

A word spent, and the spending mistaken for the referent.
The speaker has used the term sincerely, so what reads as meaning is, in truth, an inherited sense worn thinner than the practice the word once named, taken for the content because it came easily, each speaker certain the field spends the same coin.
