# Piece: The Currency

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). The word's continued acceptance as coin, its face value outrunning its content, the term taken and passed on trust while the referent it once bought departs.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Spent in [the currency](place_the_currency.md), the place named for it, where the word passes at face value.
Refused in [the anchorage](place_the_anchorage.md), where the coin is held until it buys something specific.

## Load Bearing

Without it the emptied word falls out of use and the drift stops, and a hollow term no one accepts cannot circulate.
With it the trap holds against the check: the word's acceptance as coin, taken at face value and spent on trust, so the term keeps passing exactly because it is smooth and old and everyone accepts it (Bloomfield, on the persistence of a word's form through change of sense; the acceptance of a term outrunning its content). The Currency is load bearing because it is what carries the hollow word across the field: the face value that outruns the content, the trust in the coin surviving the departure of what it bought, so the piece carries the acceptance that keeps the emptied term in use, the reason the drift never stops and the referent is never assayed in the spending.

## Apparent

Sound money, on the surface: a coin everyone takes, the word accepted and passing, its worth felt too obvious to check.
Seen at the surface, the Currency looks like content, because a coin everyone accepts feels like a coin that buys, so the acceptance looks like meaning and the drift looks not to exist, and the truth that the term passes on trust and not on referent, that the face value has outrun the content, that a smooth old coin is accepted precisely because it goes down easy, stays hidden behind the ease of a word the whole field spends.
