# Position: Speaker

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). The seat that uses the word in good faith, where the inherited sense is taken for the whole content and the term is spent as common coin.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A living word to use and a room that uses it: the term inherited and the sense nearest to hand that fills it.
A sound use: the word is said as the field says it, a sincere use of the sense received.
No way to use the word without supplying its sense, and no sign that the inherited sense is thinner than the practice the word once named.

## Orders

Say the word.
Take the term, mean by it about what the field means, and spend it as common coin, sure the sense is plain and shared.
Use it the easy way, because the easy way is the way the room uses it.

## Loses

The chance of catching that the coin has thinned, exactly because the word goes down easy and everyone says it.
Because the inherited sense feels like the word's whole content, the seat cannot see it as one worn thinner with every mouth it passed through, so takes it for the full meaning and spends it again, one more sincere use keeping the coin passing over a referent that has changed or gone, and loses the drift to its own fluency until someone stops to ask.
It gains a usable word to speak with, and loses the sight that the practice it names has departed.

## Drives

Toward the word said and the inherited sense taken for the meaning,
the easy usage felt as the full content and believed shared,
so the seat that says the word keeps the currency alive, sure to the last that the term still points where it always did.
