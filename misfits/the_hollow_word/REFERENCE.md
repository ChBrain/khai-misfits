# The Hollow Word: Reference

The research warrant for [The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md).
The dynamic is semantic drift by bleaching: a word's form persisting in a field while its sense generalises,
thins, and departs its referent through repeated use, the regular semantic change of Traugott and Dasher, the
semantic bleaching of Hopper and Traugott, and the standing mutability of a living word's meaning in Bloomfield,
all cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging semantic drift as a **bleached-coin structure**: a word carries not its referent but the sense its
speakers supply, and through the collective, repeated use of many speakers over time the sense thins and the
referent departs, so the term keeps circulating as accepted coin while the practice it named changes, goes, or
inverts, the field speaking the old language and doing the new thing and marking nowhere the place the two parted.
The play models the documented mechanics, the living word whose sense is transmitted by use and not stapled to the
form, the sincere saying that spends the term in the sense inherited, the collective and undeliberate bleaching
that drains content through the very traffic that keeps the word alive, the currency by which a face value
outruns its content and a coin passes on acceptance, the ghost of residual sense each speaker assumes and none
shares, the honest inheritance by which a newcomer learns the word already hollow and seals the drift, and the
costly re-anchoring that alone ties the term back to a concrete referent, as the load-bearing vectors of the
structure, not as a tale of jargon or dishonesty. The governing finding of the sources is the play's governing
law: semantic change is a regular, non-deliberate property of living language, meaning shifting and bleaching
through use with no speaker intending it, so a field can go on saying a word in perfect good faith long after the
practice it named has departed, each speaker sincere and the shared meaning quietly gone.

It is distinct from the house's **The Cargo Cult**, and the two are close enough that the cut must be exact: there
the engine is the performance of a ritual FORM to summon a result, copying the visible appearance of a success in
the expectation of its outcome; here the engine is a LABEL bleached of content through use, a word outliving or
inverting its referent, not a ritual performed for a payoff. It is distinct from **The Leadership Script**, where a
whole naturalised REGISTER is selected in promotion and mistaken for competence, a manner rewarded until it is
taken for the thing; here it is a single word's content emptying over time through collective use, not a register
mistaken for substance. It is distinct from **Prevalence-Induced Concept Change**, where a category's boundary
EXPANDS as its instances grow rarer, the concept creeping to fill the scarcity; here the drift is USE-driven
bleaching that decouples a word from its referent, not a boundary widening with prevalence. And it is distinct from
the house's **The Abstraction Ladder**, whose loss is the checkable particular shed as a single report climbs the
levels of abstraction in the moment; the Hollow Word is a word losing its content across time through the repeated
use of a whole field, a diachronic bleaching and not a vertical ascent.

## Origin

The phenomenon in language, and the research spine beneath it.

| Source                     | Key Work / Event                                                          | Scope                                                                                                                                                                          |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Traugott & Dasher**      | _Regularity in Semantic Change_ (Cambridge University Press, 2002)        | **The spine**: the regular, patterned processes of semantic change, including the bleaching of content through use. The Word, The Bleach.                                      |
| **Bloomfield**             | _Language_ (1933), the chapters on semantic change                        | **The living word**: meaning as a standing, mutable property of language, a word's form persisting while its sense departs. The Referent, The Currency.                        |
| **Hopper & Traugott**      | _Grammaticalization_ (Cambridge University Press, 1993; 2nd edn 2003)     | **Semantic bleaching**: the loss of semantic content through repeated use, sense generalising and thinning as a form is used. The Bleach, The Word.                            |
| **Practitioner instance**  | the observed hollowing of organisational buzzwords ("agile", "ownership") | **The field-level case**: a discipline's terms bleached of content through use, the word surviving while the practice departs or inverts. The Currency, The Ghost, The Hollow. |
| **Boundary of the effect** | semantic change is a normal, non-deliberate property of living language   | The honest note: no one empties the word on purpose, so it is drift and not a lie, and the re-anchoring is the costed remedy.                                                  |

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast the speakers as
  liars or the field as cynical, because the source finding is precisely that no one empties the word: each
  speaker uses the term in good faith, meaning roughly what they inherited, the practice changes for its own
  reasons, and the newcomer learns honestly what there is to learn. The drift is authored by no one and emerges
  from the collective, repeated use of a living word, so the misfit is that a word smooth enough to share is worn
  enough to drift, and a term that feels full to its speaker feels full to every speaker who inherited a thinner
  sense.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into a costless correction. The one move that re-anchors the
  word is to insist each use cash out in a concrete referent, name the specific practice the word stands for, and
  check the term against the thing, and it means stopping a fluent conversation, sounding like a pedant over a
  word everyone is happy to say, breaking a shared language the field is comfortable speaking, and treating a
  smooth old coin as suspect precisely because it goes down easy. The reckoning is a referent recovered only by
  paying, continuously, the social cost of doubting a currency the field still accepts, not a free correction.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here, never claimed as
  the house's. The staging is the house's; the regularity of semantic change is Traugott and Dasher's, semantic
  bleaching and the loss of content through use are Hopper and Traugott's, and semantic change as a standing
  property of living language is Bloomfield's. The organisational instance, the hollowing of buzzwords like
  "agile" and "ownership", is carried as the field-level case beneath the linguistic spine, and the honest
  boundary, that no one empties the word on purpose and each use is sincere, is carried as carefully as the
  finding itself.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **[The Word](piece_the_word.md)**: carries the living term whose sense is transmitted by use and not stapled to
  its form (Traugott & Dasher; Hopper & Traugott), the coin the field spends.
- **[The Referent](piece_the_referent.md)**: carries the departed sense (Bloomfield), the concrete practice the
  word once named, now changed, gone, or inverted.
- **[The Bleach](piece_the_bleach.md)**: carries semantic bleaching (Hopper & Traugott; Traugott & Dasher), the
  loss of content through the collective repeated use, authored by no single speaker.

  word's acceptance as coin surviving the departure of what it bought.
- **[The Ghost](piece_the_ghost.md)**: carries the residual sense assumed and unshared, the felt content that
  keeps the emptied coin looking good.
- **[The Hollow](piece_the_hollow.md)**: carries the whole, a field coordinating on an emptied word, speaking the
  old language and doing the new thing, the regular non-deliberate outcome of semantic drift.
