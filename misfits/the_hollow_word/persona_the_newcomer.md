# Persona: The Newcomer

## Taxonomy

[Newcomer](position_newcomer.md). The one who learns the word already emptied, without ever seeing the referent it named, and passes on the hollow, sealing the drift. The archetype of the arriving speaker who inherits the token and not the thing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

New. Diligent. Learning the language.
Is the graduate, the joiner, the reader coming to the field for the first time, taking in its words the way one takes in any working vocabulary, honestly and without suspicion.
No error in it: the Newcomer learns the word as the field uses it, which is the only way there is to learn it, and means by it what the field now means.

## Action

Learns the word already hollow, without the practice it once named, and takes the current use for the whole of the word.
Hears "ownership" or "agile" as the field says them now, never having seen the referent that has gone, so inherits the token and means by it about nothing definite, the current vagueness the only content on offer.
Does what a newcomer must: it learns the living usage and not a departed referent, and passes the word on as it received it.

## Shadow

Not a poor student, a faithful learner of a word that arrived already emptied, and that is the whole difficulty: there is no fuller sense to learn, because the practice is gone and only the word remains.
Because the Newcomer never saw the referent, it cannot know the word is hollow, cannot miss a content it never met, so takes the token for a full coin and uses it in perfect good faith, then hands it to the next arrival, one more sincere speaker keeping the currency alive over an absent practice, sealing the drift precisely by learning honestly what there was to learn.
It inherits the token and not the thing, and the honesty of the inheritance is exactly what carries the hollow forward.

## Tell

Says the word as confidently as anyone, and could not say what practice it points at, because it never saw one.
Says it having learned it from the field's current use, and cannot tell the emptied word from a full one, so passes on the hollow as coin, certain it received and gives good money.
