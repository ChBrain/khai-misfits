# Piece: The Bleach

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). The draining of content through repeated use, the collective and undeliberate wearing of the word's sense as the term passes across many mouths over time.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs in [the currency](place_the_currency.md), where the traffic of the word wears its sense.
Undone in [the anchorage](place_the_anchorage.md), where the term is re-tied to the referent the bleaching loosed.

## Load Bearing

Without it the word keeps its referent and never comes loose, and there is no drift at all.
With it the trap has its engine: the loss of semantic content through use, the sense generalising and thinning as the word passes, no single speaker draining it and no one intending the loss (Hopper and Traugott, on semantic bleaching; Traugott and Dasher, on the regularity of the change). The Bleach is load bearing because it is what empties the word without anyone emptying it: the collective, cumulative wearing that lives between the speakers and not in any of them, so the piece carries the undeliberate draining that is the difference between a lie and a drift, the coin worn smooth by the very handling that keeps it in use, the loss belonging to the language and authored by no mouth.

## Apparent

Nothing happening, on the surface: the word in steady use, the field speaking as it always did, no event to point at and no moment of loss.
Seen at the surface, the Bleach is invisible, because it runs along the whole length of the passing and never at a point, so nothing looks to change and the draining looks not to occur, and the truth that the sense is thinning with every mouth, that the referent is coming loose, that a word is going hollow inside its own currency, stays hidden behind the steadiness of a term everyone keeps saying.

## Yearbook

Worked by [the field](persona_the_field.md) through [bleaching](process_bleaching.md), the wearing that thins [the word](piece_the_word.md) and looses [the referent](piece_the_referent.md).
