# Position: Field

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). The seat of the collective, where the repeated use of many speakers over time bleaches the word's content, present in no single mouth and authored by none.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A word in heavy circulation and a population of speakers to circulate it: the term everyone says and the traffic that carries it, mouth to mouth and year to year.
A standing property: the language changes under use, the sense generalising and thinning as the word passes down the line.
No mind to intend the drift and no mouth to author it, only the many sincere uses whose sum wears the coin none of them means to wear.

## Orders

Keep the word in use.
Pass the term down the line, each speaker meaning about what they inherited, and let the traffic do what traffic does to a word.
Circulate it, because a useful word is used, and the use is the only thing the seat can do.

## Loses

The referent, drained along the whole length of the passing, exactly because no single speaker drains it.
Because the loss lives between the speakers and not in any of them, the seat cannot notice the draining any more than a river notices the bank it wears, so the sense thins cumulatively and undeliberately, the word going hollow inside the very currency that keeps it good, and loses the content to a drift authored by no one.
It gains a word kept alive by constant use, and loses the practice the word was alive to name.

## Drives

Toward the word circulated and the content bleached,
the sense worn thin by traffic no single mouth controls,
so the seat of the collective keeps the coin passing at face value while the referent departs unremarked, the loss real and cumulative and intended by none.
