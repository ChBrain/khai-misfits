# Piece: The Referent

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). The practice the word once named, the concrete thing on the ground, now departed or changed or inverted, the content the coin once bought and no longer does.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Lived on [the ground](place_the_ground.md), the floor where the practice was done.
Sought again in [the anchorage](place_the_anchorage.md), where the word is brought down to see what it now points at.

## Load Bearing

Without it the word never named anything and there is nothing to depart, and the drift has no distance to run.
With it the trap has its absence: the concrete practice the term once pointed at, real and checkable, now changed or gone while the word stays (Bloomfield, on semantic change as a standing property of living language; the sense of a word departing while the form persists). The Referent is load bearing because it is what leaves while the coin keeps passing: the practice the word cashed out in, departed unmarked because words are not checked against the ground in the ordinary course of speaking, so the piece carries the thing the term has come loose from, the floor the word still points at and the practice no longer occupies, at the far end the contrary of what stands there now.

## Apparent

Still there, on the surface: the word points and the field assumes the practice stands where it always did, the coin taken to buy what it once bought.
Seen at the surface, the Referent looks present, because the word keeps pointing and no one comes down to the ground to look, so the practice looks to stand and the departure looks not to have happened, and the truth that the thing has changed or gone, that the word points at an empty floor, that the field says "ownership" while control has centralised, stays hidden behind a coin no one thinks to assay.
