# Place: The Currency

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). Where the word circulates as accepted coin, taken at face value, spent and passed and never assayed, the field speaking the term as if it still buys what it once bought.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the word is used and accepted, the meeting, the review, the job post, the hallway, where the term is spent as common coin and taken at face value.
The Speaker saying the word, the Field passing it hand to hand, the Newcomer learning it as coin, and everyone accepting the term without asking what it now buys.

## Holds

The face value, and the assay it suppresses.
Here the word is spent and accepted, so the Currency holds the point where the term passes on its acceptance alone: a coin taken at face value by all, so it is where the currency of the word is trusted for the content of the word, and where asking what the term now points at comes to read as pedantry over a coin no one else questions, the field fluent and the fluency untested.

## Offers

A ready word and a field that accepts it.
Because the Currency is where the word is spent and taken, it offers a term always to hand and a meaning felt to be shared, the coin passing and the field speaking on, so it hands out the ease of a common language, and hands it out on the face value and not the content, the departed referent left unremarked beneath a smooth accepted coin.

## Withheld

Any sight of the content behind the face value.
The Currency shows the word accepted but cannot show what it now buys, because the coin passes on acceptance and the referent is never assayed in the spending, so it withholds the drift beneath the fluency, letting the currency of the word stand for its content, and, all accepted coin and no cashing out, it withholds the gap between the word and the referent until someone stops the fluent room to check the term against the thing.
