# Plan: Say the Word

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the speaker's seat](position_speaker.md), inside [The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Speaker](position_speaker.md)

## Direction

Take the living word and use it in the sense you inherited. Say the term the easy way, the way the room says it, mean by it about what the field means, and spend it as common coin, assuming the sense is plain and shared, because the word goes down easy and everyone says it the same.

## Orders

([The Speaker](persona_the_speaker.md) uses the word in good faith, takes the inherited sense for the whole content, and spends the term as common coin, one more sincere use keeping the currency alive over a referent that has changed or gone.)

## Implementation

([The Currency](place_the_currency.md) is where the word is spent; the [saying](process_saying.md) process; [the word](piece_the_word.md) is used in the sense nearest to hand, taken for full and passed toward [the hollow](piece_the_hollow.md) while [the referent](piece_the_referent.md) departs unremarked.)

## Targets

- [ ] the word is used in the sense the speaker inherited from the field
- [ ] the inherited sense is taken for the word's whole content
- [ ] the field is assumed to mean the same thing by the word
- [ ] the term is spent as common coin, one more use keeping the currency alive
