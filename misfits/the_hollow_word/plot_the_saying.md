# Plot: The Saying

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Currency](place_the_currency.md), a living word to hand and a field that uses it.
The Speaker reaching for the term as common coin, meaning about what they inherited.

## Action

[Saying](process_saying.md): [The Speaker](persona_the_speaker.md), holding [the speaker's seat](position_speaker.md), uses the word in the sense received and takes it for the whole content.

## Stage

The [speaker](persona_the_speaker.md), on [Say the Word](plan_say_the_word.md), spends [the word](piece_the_word.md) as [the currency](piece_the_currency.md) accepts it, saying "ownership" or "agile" the way the room says them, sure the sense is plain.
Each takes the inherited sense not for a thinner inheritance but for the meaning, and assumes the field means the same.

## Tension

Using a living word in the sense one received is sincere, and the sincerity is what keeps the emptied coin passing.
There is nothing to fault: each use is good faith and the word goes down easy. But the sense that comes without effort feels like the full content, so the plot closes with the word spent as if full, every speaker certain it points where it always did and that the field spends the same coin.
