# Process: Anchoring

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). The remedy: re-tying the word to its referent, making each use cash out in a concrete practice, checking the term against the thing rather than the room's comfort.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone who would trust the word only if it cashes out.
In [the anchorage](place_the_anchorage.md), the Restorer stopping the fluent use and bringing the term down from [the currency](place_the_currency.md) to [the ground](place_the_ground.md) to see what it now buys.

## Direction

Toward the word re-anchored and the coin made to buy.
Because a word trusted without a referent is a currency without content, anchoring insists each use name a concrete practice, refuses the coin until it buys something specific, and checks the term against the ground, so the drift is caught in the speaking and the referent recovered, the word tied back to a thing a field actually does.

## Lever

The demand that the coin buy something.
Because the remedy works only by assaying the term against the referent, anchoring runs on the continuous cost of doubting a coin the field still accepts, the fluent room slowed and the asker made to sound like a pedant, so the referent is recovered not for free but by paying, each time, to break the ease of a shared language everyone is comfortable speaking.

## Echo

A word that cashes out, and a bill for the cashing.
The restorer has tied the term back to the ground, so what reads as pedantry is, in truth, a coin made to buy again, the referent recovered at the price of the fluent room, the remedy real and the payment never once and done.
