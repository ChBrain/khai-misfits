# Plan: Learn the Word

## Taxonomy

## Direction

Take in the word as the field now uses it. Learn the term the current way, the only way on offer, mean by it what the field means, and spend it as you received it, never having seen the referent that has gone, so the current use is the whole of the word you can learn.

## Orders

([The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) learns the word already hollow, without the practice it once named, takes the current use for the whole content, and passes the token on, sealing the drift by learning honestly what there was to learn.)
