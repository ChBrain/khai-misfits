# Plot: The Inversion

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Currency](place_the_currency.md), a token worn loose enough to cover anything.
The field saying the old word while [the ground](place_the_ground.md) shows the practice changed to its contrary.

## Action

The field, on [Say the Word](plan_say_the_word.md) and [Pass It On](plan_pass_it_on.md), speaks the old word over the new thing, [the word](piece_the_word.md) worn smooth enough to cover the opposite of what it named.

## Stage

The [speaker](persona_the_speaker.md) and [the field](persona_the_field.md) say "ownership" while control centralises and "agile" while the plan is fixed, [the currency](piece_the_currency.md) still good and [the ghost](piece_the_ghost.md) still assumed, [the referent](piece_the_referent.md) now the contrary of the term.
Each speaker is sincere and the coordination holds, because everyone shares the vagueness and no one brings the word and the ground together.

## Tension

A token loose enough to cover its opposite is the far end of an ordinary drift, and the coverage holds because the coin is still accepted.
There is nothing to fault: no one says the word to deceive and each means about what they inherited. But a bleached term covers more and more loosely, so the plot closes with the field speaking the old language and doing the new thing, the word full of nothing and the two never marked as parted, which is [the hollow](piece_the_hollow.md) whole.
