# Plan: Pass It On

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the field's seat](position_field.md), inside [The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Field](position_field.md)

## Direction

Keep the useful word in circulation. Pass the term down the line, mouth to mouth and year to year, each speaker meaning about what they inherited, and let the sheer traffic do what traffic does to a word, the sense generalising and thinning as the coin passes, no single mouth intending the wear.

## Orders

([The Field](persona_the_field.md) circulates the word across many speakers over time, and by the collective repeated use bleaches its content, the referent draining along the whole length of the passing, authored by no one and noticed by none.)

## Implementation

([The Currency](place_the_currency.md) as the word passes and [the ground](place_the_ground.md) as the referent departs; the [bleaching](process_bleaching.md) process; [the word](piece_the_word.md) is worn by traffic into [the bleach](piece_the_bleach.md), the content draining toward [the hollow](piece_the_hollow.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the word is circulated across many speakers over time
- [ ] the sense is thinned and the referent loosened by the collective use
- [ ] no single speaker drains the content or intends the drift
- [ ] the coin keeps passing at face value while the referent departs
