# Position: Newcomer

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). The seat that learns the word already emptied, where the current use is the only content on offer and the token is inherited and passed on, sealing the drift.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A word to learn and a field that uses it: the term as it is now said and the current sense that is all there is to receive.
A sound learning: the word is taken in as the field uses it, the only way there is to learn a living word.
No access to the departed referent and no sign that the word is hollow, because the practice is gone and only the term remains.

## Orders

Learn the word.
Take the term as the field now uses it, mean by it what the field means, and spend it as you received it.
Learn it the current way, because the current way is the only way on offer.

## Loses

The chance of ever missing the departed content, exactly because the seat never met it.
Because the referent is gone before the seat arrives, it cannot know the word is hollow, cannot miss a practice it never saw, so takes the token for a full coin and passes it to the next arrival, sealing the drift by learning honestly what there was to learn, and loses nothing it can feel while the field loses the last speaker who knew the word full.
It gains the working vocabulary of the field, and loses, without knowing it, the referent the vocabulary once carried.

## Drives

Toward the word learned hollow and the token passed on,
the current use taken for the whole content and handed to the next,
so the seat that learns the word seals the drift and keeps the currency alive, certain it received and gives good money.
