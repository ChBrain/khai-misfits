# Process: Inheriting

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). The Newcomer learning the word already emptied, taking in the term as the field now uses it, without the referent it once named.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A newcomer arriving after the practice has gone.
In [the currency](place_the_currency.md), the arriving speaker learning the word as coin, [the ground](place_the_ground.md) standing empty where the referent was.

## Direction

Toward the token received and the drift sealed.
Because the newcomer learns a living word from its current use and the practice it named is already gone, inheriting takes in the term without its referent, the current sense the only content on offer, so the newcomer means by the word what the field now means and passes it on, one more sincere speaker keeping the coin passing, the drift sealed not by error but by honest learning of what there was to learn.

## Lever

The absence of the referent to miss.
Because the practice is gone before the newcomer arrives, there is no fuller sense to learn and no departed content to miss, so inheriting runs not on any failure of learning but on the plain fact that a hollow word is all there is to inherit, the token taken for a full coin because the newcomer never met the thing it once bought.

## Echo

A token inherited, and the hollow carried forward.
The newcomer has learned the word faithfully, so what reads as fluency is, in truth, an emptied term received as coin and handed to the next arrival, the currency kept alive over an absent referent, the drift sealed by the very honesty of the inheritance.
