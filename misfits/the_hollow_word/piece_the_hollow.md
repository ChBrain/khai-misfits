Seen at the surface, the Hollow looks like understanding, because everyone says the words the same and the coordination holds, so the shared vagueness looks like shared meaning and the emptiness looks not to exist, and the truth that the field speaks the old language while doing the new thing, that the currency carries no referent, that the coordination rests on a coin worn to a token, stays hidden behind how well a hollow language works.
