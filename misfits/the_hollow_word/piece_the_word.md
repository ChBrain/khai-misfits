# Piece: The Word

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). The term still in circulation, the token the field spends, carrying not its referent but the sense its speakers supply, worn smooth by the use that keeps it alive.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Spent in [the currency](place_the_currency.md), where the word passes as accepted coin.
Assayed in [the anchorage](place_the_anchorage.md), where the word is checked against the referent and made to cash out.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no coin to spend and no drift to run, and the field has nothing to say the old language with.
With it the trap has its token: the term that carries only the sense its speakers supply, passed hand to hand and worn by the traffic, none of its speakers wrong and each meaning about what they inherited (Traugott and Dasher, on the regularity of semantic change; a word's sense transmitted by use, not stapled to the word). The Word is load bearing because it is what circulates while the referent departs: the term that stays in currency after the practice it named has gone, so the piece carries the standing coin the field trusts, spent at face value and drained of content by its own passing, the old language the field keeps speaking over the new thing.

## Apparent

Nothing amiss, on the surface: a good word everyone knows, its meaning plain and shared, the term to hand and the field agreed.
Seen at the surface, the Word looks full, because each speaker fills it with the sense nearest to hand and hears its own use as the plain meaning, so the coin looks good and the drift looks not to exist, and the truth that the referent has departed, that the sense is worn thinner with every mouth, that the fullness each feels is their own supply and not the word's, stays hidden behind how full a word in fluent use feels.
