# Plot: The Anchorage

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Anchorage](place_the_anchorage.md), someone who would trust the word only if it cashes out.
The Restorer stopping the fluent use to ask what practice the term stands for now.

## Action

[Anchoring](process_anchoring.md): [The Restorer](persona_the_restorer.md), holding [the restorer's seat](position_restorer.md), re-ties the word to the ground, insisting each use name a concrete referent, against a field happy to spend the coin.

## Stage

The [restorer](persona_the_restorer.md), on [Re-anchor the Word](plan_re_anchor_the_word.md), brings [the word](piece_the_word.md) down from [the currency](piece_the_currency.md) to [the ground](place_the_ground.md), asks what "ownership" points at now, and refuses the coin until it buys something specific, dispelling [the ghost](piece_the_ghost.md).
The check is real and the price is continuous: the fluent room slows and the asker sounds like a pedant over a term no one else questions.

## Tension

Re-anchoring the word recovers [the referent](piece_the_referent.md), and the recovery is dear.
There is nothing free in it: to check the term against the thing is to doubt a coin the field still accepts and to break the ease of a shared language. So the plot closes with the word re-tied to the ground and the drift caught in the speaking ... or the check waived as pedantry, the currency taken for meaning, and [the hollow](piece_the_hollow.md) left standing, the remedy real and forgone at the same cost.
