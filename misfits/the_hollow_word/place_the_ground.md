## Holds

The referent, and its departure.
Here the practice was real, so the Ground holds the point where the word once cashed out: the concrete thing the term named, now changed or gone, so it is where the referent lived and where it left, the floor the word still points at and the practice no longer occupies, the departure unmarked because words are not checked against the ground in the ordinary course of speaking.

Because the Ground is where the practice was done, it offers the referent against which the term could be checked, the concrete thing that would say whether the coin still buys, so it holds out the possibility of an assay, and holds it out unvisited, the field spending the word in the Currency and never coming down to the Ground to see what stands there now.

The Ground shows what the word once named but cannot announce that the practice has gone, because the departure leaves no mark and the field does not come down to look, so it withholds the emptiness beneath the fluent term, letting the word keep pointing at a floor the practice has left, and, all departed referent and no one to see it, it withholds the gap until the Restorer comes down to the Ground and finds the practice changed or gone.
