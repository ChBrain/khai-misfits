# Piece: The Ghost

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md). The residual sense everyone assumes the word still carries but no one actually shares, the felt content of the emptied term, present as an assumption and absent as a referent.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Haunts [the currency](place_the_currency.md), where each speaker assumes the coin carries a content the field no longer holds.
Dispelled in [the anchorage](place_the_anchorage.md), where the word is asked to cash out and the residual sense finds nothing to buy.

## Load Bearing

Without it the field would feel the word go hollow and stop to check, and the drift could not run unremarked.
With it the trap keeps its cover: the felt sense each speaker assumes the word still carries, the content taken for shared while no one holds the same, so the coin feels full to each precisely because each supplies its own faint sense and hears its own use as the plain meaning. The Ghost is load bearing because it is what the field mistakes for the referent: the residual meaning that everyone assumes and no one shares, so the piece carries the assumption that keeps the coin looking good, the felt fullness of an emptied word, the reason the field feels it shares a meaning while it shares only a currency.

## Apparent

A shared meaning, on the surface: the word felt to carry a plain content everyone holds, the sense obvious and common, no need to spell it out.
Seen at the surface, the Ghost looks like the referent, because each speaker's own faint sense feels like the word's whole content and feels like everyone's, so the assumed meaning looks shared and the absence looks not to exist, and the truth that no one holds the same content, that each supplies a private residue, that the shared meaning is a shared assumption and not a shared thing, stays hidden behind how full a haunted word feels.
