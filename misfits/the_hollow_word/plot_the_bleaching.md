# Plot: The Bleaching

## Taxonomy

[The Hollow Word](play_the_hollow_word.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Currency](place_the_currency.md), a word in heavy use across many mouths over time.
The Field passing the term hand to hand, the referent slipping a little further with each passing.

## Action

[Bleaching](process_bleaching.md): [The Field](persona_the_field.md), holding [the field's seat](position_field.md), thins the sense and looses the referent by the sheer traffic of the word, no single mouth draining it.

## Stage

The [field](persona_the_field.md), on [Pass It On](plan_pass_it_on.md), wears [the word](piece_the_word.md) into [the bleach](piece_the_bleach.md), the content draining along the whole length of the passing while [the referent](piece_the_referent.md) departs the ground.
No one empties the coin and no one intends the loss, yet the term goes hollow inside the very currency that keeps it good.

## Tension

Keeping a useful word in use is what any field does, and the use itself is what drains the content.
There is nothing to fault: the loss lives between the speakers and not in any of them, authored by no one (Traugott and Dasher; Hopper and Traugott, on semantic bleaching). But a living word is worn by its own traffic, so the plot closes with the coin bleached to a token, still passing at face value, the referent departed and no speaker having worn it.
