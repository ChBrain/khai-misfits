# Plot: The Pool

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The pool](place_the_pool.md), where someone with the means moves to end the fragmentation.
The gridlock is priced and proven, the deal impossible owner by owner, and a mover arrives who will not do it owner by owner.

## Action

[Consolidating](process_consolidating.md): [The Consolidator](persona_the_consolidator.md), holding [the unifier's seat](position_unifier.md), gathers the pieces at once, by pool, by hidden buyout, or by a taking that overrides the veto no fair price would move, and pays not the holdout's figure but a court's.

## Stage

[The Consolidator](persona_the_consolidator.md) tables [Unify the Title](plan_unify_the_title.md): draw the many titles into one so the last owner cannot hold out for last, and [the anticommons](piece_the_anticommons.md) is answered by making the many owners one.
The resource moves again under a single hand, and the veto that was perfectly good is overridden or bought out to do it.

## Tension

The only fix frees the resource by unmaking the ownership that froze it.
Every route out breaks or buys a lawful right: the taking compels an owner who did no wrong, the pool strips each holder of the veto the law gave him, the hidden buyout wins only by concealing the assembly the owners would otherwise have priced. So the resource is freed at the cost of the property that defined it, and the fix holds ... or no holder will sell and no taking will come, and the shop stays dark for good.
