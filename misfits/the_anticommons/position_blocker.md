# Position: Blocker

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The seat whose one piece is essential, and priced as the whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A fragment no larger than any other, and a veto as large as all of them.
The knowledge that the deal dies without it, and the patience to wait for that to be worth the most.
The lawful right to name any price for its yes.

## Orders

Price your piece at what the deal cannot do without.
Say yes last, when the need is greatest.
Take the difference between a fair share and a desperate one.

## Loses

Nothing, and neither does it gain, when every owner plays the same.
Its correct price, summed with every other correct price, kills the deal it was pricing, so the toll is never paid.
The essential piece is worth everything to a deal that, so priced, never happens.

## Drives

Toward the highest yes,
away from the first,
so the tolls stack past the value and the resource no one would sell cheap, no one buys at all.
