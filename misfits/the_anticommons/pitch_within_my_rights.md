# Pitch: Within My Rights

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Proper, lawful, sure of its title.
Every owner within his rights, every no a plain exercise of clear ownership, no one stealing and no one cheating.
The voice of people who hold what is theirs and refuse what is theirs to refuse, and cannot see that a hundred lawful noes over one thing add up to a resource no one can use.

## Undertow

Under the propriety, a shop that should be open standing dark.
A thing that needs every yes to move is stopped by any single no, and there are too many hands each holding one, so each owner, priced as if his piece were the whole, asks a toll that with all the others stacks past what the resource is worth.
No one is grasping: they only each hold what is theirs, and what is theirs, all held at once over one resource, is a deal that can never close.

## Nerve

The feeling of an orderly world of clear titles and honest owners,
each within his rights, each no lawful,
watching the resource sit idle under the honest arithmetic of everyone refusing only what is his to refuse.

## Echo

After the run: a storefront empty while kiosks crowd the pavement,
a drug undeveloped behind a thicket of patents each holder can enforce,
and a resource that too many people owned, wanted by all and moved by none.
