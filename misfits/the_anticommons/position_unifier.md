# Position: Unifier

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The seat that gathers the fragments into one, and pays the price of doing it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The funds, the patience, or the public power to end the fragmentation.
A way to gather every piece at once, so no owner can hold out for last.
The authority, in the end, to override a veto that no fair price would move.

## Orders

Gather the pieces into one hand.
Pool the rights, buy the block unseen, or take it and pay what a court decides.
Turn the many titles into one so the resource can move.

## Loses

The openness of the ownership, and something of the right it overrides.
It frees the resource only by breaking or buying a veto that was lawful, compelling an owner who did no wrong, so the cure spends the very property it clears, and holds only until the title splits again.
Bought back at the price of the ownership itself.

## Drives

Toward one owner for one resource,
away from the many the law allowed,
so the thing too many held is used again only once too many no longer hold it.
