# Persona: The Holdout

## Taxonomy

[Blocker](position_blocker.md). The essential owner who knows the deal dies without his one piece, and prices it so.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Shrewd, patient, entirely lawful.
Holds a piece no larger than any other, but knows that every piece is essential, so his is worth the whole.
Sees the asking not as extortion but as the market value of a thing without which nothing moves.

## Action

Names a price to match his power, which is the power to stop everything.
Waits for the assembler to need him last and most, and holds out for the difference.
Refuses what is his to refuse, at whatever figure the desperation of the deal will bear.

## Shadow

Every essential owner reasons the same, so every price is the whole price.
He is not asking more than his piece is worth to the deal, he is asking exactly that, and his piece is worth the deal because without it there is none, and so is the next holder's, and the next, until the tolls stack past the value and the deal dies.
The holdout does not break the deal by greed: he prices it correctly, and correct pricing, summed, is gridlock.

## Tell

"Without my piece there is no project, so my piece is worth the project."
The true valuation that, held by every owner at once, is worth to all of them nothing.
