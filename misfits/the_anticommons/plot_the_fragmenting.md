# Plot: The Fragmenting

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The registry](place_the_registry.md), where one valuable resource passes into many hands at once.
An estate divided, a technology layered in patents, a block parcelled out, each new owner recorded with a clear and lawful title.

## Action

[Fragmenting](process_fragmenting.md): [The Holder](persona_the_holder.md), holding [the owner's seat](position_owner.md), takes his piece and means no harm by it, while [The Reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md), holding [the appraiser's seat](position_appraiser.md), notes that every piece just recorded is now a veto over the whole.

## Stage

[The Holder](persona_the_holder.md) tables [Keep My Piece](plan_keep_my_piece.md): hold what is his and trouble himself with nothing further.
[The Reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md) tables [Add Up the Tolls](plan_add_up_the_tolls.md) and marks the count of owners rising, so [the divided title](piece_the_divided_title.md) hands each holder [the veto](piece_the_veto.md), and the resource now needs every one of them to move.

## Tension

Every division is legal, and the whole is already frozen.
Nothing here looks wrong: each title is clear, each owner legitimate, the fragmentation orderly and defensible. That is the trap loading, a resource that a moment ago one hand could use, split into pieces no hand can, with a veto laid quietly in each and no one yet counting how many yeses the thing will now require.
