# Position: Developer

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The seat that would use the resource, and must gather every owner to do it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The will and the means to put the resource to work.
The need for every owner's yes, and so a dependence on each and a hostage to any.
A budget that shrinks with every toll it pays and every owner it must still reach.

## Orders

Gather the consents, one owner at a time.
Pay each asking price to keep the deal alive.
Reach the last yes before the budget runs out.

## Loses

The deal, to the last no.
Every owner it needs is an owner who can stop it, so its effort compounds into fragility, and the sum of the prices it must pay outruns the worth of the thing it would build.
Beaten not by a competitor but by the count of owners it cannot get under one roof.

## Drives

Toward assembling every yes,
which the number of owners forbids,
so the use it can see is the use it can never reach.
