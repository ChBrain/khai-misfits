# Piece: The Anticommons

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The phenomenon whole: the overowned resource sitting idle, and its remedy in consolidation at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the pool](place_the_pool.md), where the fragmentation is undone.
And across the whole resource, in the sum of every lawful no that froze it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of mechanisms with no shape.
With it the mechanisms become one thing: the divided title, the veto, the holdout premium, the stacked toll, and the assembly cost are facets of a single inversion, a resource wasted by too many owners exactly as a commons is wasted by none, and the only cure is to gather the pieces into one hand, which must break or buy a veto that was perfectly good. The Anticommons is load bearing because it is the whole finding (Heller) with its symmetry attached (Buchanan & Yoon) and its whole price named: it holds the misfit together, and it holds that the fix is ownership unmade, not greed corrected.

## Apparent

Either a market that will clear itself, or a resource simply not in demand.
Seen from outside it looks like a deal that will happen once the price is right, or an empty shop no one wants, when in truth no price clears it and the want is real, which is precisely what makes the gridlock so hard to see and so easy to leave standing.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the consolidator](persona_the_consolidator.md) through [consolidating](process_consolidating.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: the Anticommons is what [the divided title](piece_the_divided_title.md) becomes when a resource is owned by too many to move, undone only when the pieces are gathered into one, at the price of the ownership that froze them.
