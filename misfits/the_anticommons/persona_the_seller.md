# Persona: The Seller

## Taxonomy

[Cooperator](position_cooperator.md). The owner who names a fair price to let the deal live, and is undercut for it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Reasonable, willing, glad to see the resource used.
Holds a piece and would take a fair share to release it, wanting the shop open and the street served.
Believes that if each asks a fair price the deal will close, and cannot make the others believe it too.

## Action

Names a modest price and says yes early.
Releases his piece for a fair share, hoping to move the deal along.
Watches the holdout beside him ask for more, and get it, and end with a larger cut than his own.

## Shadow

His fairness does not close the deal, it only funds the holdout's price.
The share he gives up is money the assembler can now offer the holdout, so his restraint is a gift to the one who refuses to restrain, and the deal still dies on the last no.
Punished for cooperating exactly as the commons' steward was punished for sparing the grass: whatever he gives back, the holdout takes.

## Tell

"If we each ask what is fair, this will get built."
The reasonable hope the anticommons is built to disappoint, because the fair seller only makes room for the holdout's toll.
