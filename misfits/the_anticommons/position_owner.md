# Position: Owner

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The seat that holds one piece, and with it a right to exclude.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A clear title to one fragment of the resource.
The lawful power to refuse its use, held by simply doing nothing.
No power at all to use the whole, which needs every other owner too.

## Orders

Hold what is yours.
Refuse what is yours to refuse.
Trouble yourself with nothing, for the keeping asks nothing of you.

## Loses

Nothing it can see, and the use of the whole it cannot.
Its own piece stays safe and idle, and the resource it might have shared in stays frozen with it.
Costless to hold, and worthless while held with all the rest.

## Drives

Toward keeping the piece,
away from any act at all,
so the resource waits on a yes this seat is never moved to give.
