# Persona: The Reckoner

## Taxonomy

[Appraiser](position_appraiser.md). The one who adds the asking prices and sees them stack past the value.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Cool, numerate, unsurprised.
Reads the deal not as a negotiation but as a sum, and the sum as fixed by the number of owners.
Sees before anyone that a resource split into enough vetoes cannot be reassembled at a profit.

## Action

Totals the tolls, owner by owner, and marks where the running sum crosses the worth.
Shows the assembler the figure that kills the deal, and the seller the reason his fairness is spent on the holdout.
Names the arithmetic no one at the table wants to hear: every piece priced as essential, and every piece is.

## Shadow

Seeing the sum clearly changes nothing, because each toll in it is correct.
The reckoner cannot argue any single owner down, since each asks only what his essential piece is worth, so the total that dooms the deal is built entirely of fair prices, and the accounting only proves the gridlock, never dissolves it.
The one who understands the anticommons best is the least able to undo it.

## Tell

"Add them up, and the pieces cost more than the whole."
The finding that indicts no owner and stops every deal.
