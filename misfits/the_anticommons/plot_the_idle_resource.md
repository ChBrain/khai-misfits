# Plot: The Idle Resource

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The storefront](place_the_storefront.md), the deal failed and the resource left where it stood.
The shop dark, the kiosks crowding the pavement outside, the need met poorly at the margins of the thing that could have met it well.

## Action

[Idling](process_idling.md): [The Seller](persona_the_seller.md), holding [the cooperator's seat](position_cooperator.md), named a fair price and said yes early, and watches the holdout beside him end with the larger cut, while [The Holder](persona_the_holder.md), holding [the owner's seat](position_owner.md), keeps his piece and loses nothing he can see.

## Stage

[The Seller](persona_the_seller.md) tables [Sell My Share](plan_sell_my_share.md): release his piece for a fair share to move the deal, and finds his fairness only funded [the holdout premium](piece_the_holdout_premium.md), so he ends with less than the owner who cooperated least.
[The Holder](persona_the_holder.md) tables [Keep My Piece](plan_keep_my_piece.md), and the resource settles into [the idle resource](piece_the_idle_resource.md), its worth bled off by an unpaid [assembly cost](piece_the_assembly_cost.md) and an unclosable deal.

## Tension

The one who acted fairest is the one left worst off, and the shop stays dark.
The seller did the reasonable, cooperative thing and was harvested for it, the holder did nothing and lost nothing, and the resource that everyone wanted is used by no one. It is the commons' cruelty mirrored: there the steward who spared the field fed the takers, here the seller who priced fairly fed the holdout, and the value sits idle while every owner remains, on paper, whole.
