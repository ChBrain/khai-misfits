# Process: Fragmenting

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). One resource split into many separate rights, each a veto over the whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A single resource passing into many hands at once.
An estate divided among heirs, a technology layered in patents, a block parcelled among owners, each holding lawfully.

## Direction

Toward a title so scattered that no one holds enough to use it.
Every division is orderly and legal, and the sum of the divisions is a resource that needs everyone and answers to no one.

## Lever

Clear ownership, granted piece by piece.
Each grant is a defensible right, so the fragmentation is not a failure of law but a success of it, and the firmer each title the harder the whole is to reassemble.

## Echo

A resource owned by too many to move.
Every owner certain of his piece, no owner able to use the whole, and a veto in every hand where before there was one thing.
