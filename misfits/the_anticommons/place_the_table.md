# Place: The Table

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). Where consent must be gathered, and comes apart.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A negotiation, chairs for every owner, the assembler working down the row.
Offers made and countered, prices named, the running total climbing in the corner.

## Holds

The holdout, staged as a bargain.
Here each owner learns his piece is essential and prices it so, and the sum of the essential prices is added in the open and found larger than the whole.

## Offers

A deal, in principle, that would serve everyone.
The resource is worth more in use than idle, so there is money enough to make every owner glad, and the table exists to find the split, which is exactly the split it cannot find.

## Withheld

A close.
Every owner must say yes at once and each says yes only after the others, so the last consent is always outstanding and the deal never quite lands, no matter how reasonable the room.
