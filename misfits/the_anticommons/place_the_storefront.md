# Place: The Storefront

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The resource that should be in use, standing empty.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A shop with a clean floor and a dark window, and kiosks crowding the pavement outside it.
A drug on no shelf, a block with no building, a thing plainly wanted and plainly unused.

## Holds

The waste, in full view.
Here the resource sits idle while the need for it is met poorly and expensively at the margins, the kiosks doing badly outside what the shop could do well within.

## Offers

Everything, to whoever could open it.
The use is real and the value is there, so the emptiness is not a lack of demand but a lack of a single yes, a resource ready for a tenant it cannot legally acquire.

## Withheld

A door that opens.
The shop stays dark not because no one wants it but because too many own it, so the most wanted resource on the street is the one no one can put to work.
