# Piece: The Veto

## Load Bearing

Without it a fragment would be a share to be outvoted, and the majority could use the resource.
With it every fragment can stop the whole, so use needs unanimity and any single owner can withhold it, by act or by rest. The Veto is load bearing because it is the defining right of the anticommons (Michelman), the mirror of the commons' missing power to exclude: there no one could keep anyone out and the field was used to death, here everyone can keep everyone out and the resource is used not at all, and the whole inversion turns on this one right multiplied.

## Apparent

The most basic and legitimate thing an owner has.
The right to say what is done with what is yours is property itself, so the veto reads as nothing more than ownership, and its power to freeze a resource hides inside its obvious rightness.
