# Process: Holding Out

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). Each essential owner pricing his one piece as the whole, until the tolls stack past the value.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An assembler who needs every yes, and an owner who knows it.
The deal that dies without any single piece, met by a piece that names its price accordingly.

## Direction

Toward asking prices that each are fair and together are ruinous.
Every essential owner prices his veto at what the deal cannot do without, and the sum of those correct prices climbs above the worth of the whole.

## Lever

The power to stop everything, held by each.
Because any owner can block the deal, each is worth the deal, and each prices for it, so the holdout is not the greed of one but the geometry of many essential pieces priced at once.

## Echo

A stack of fair tolls larger than the whole.
The deal priced correctly out of existence, the cooperator's fair share spent funding the holdout, and a resource no one would sell cheap and no one can buy dear.
