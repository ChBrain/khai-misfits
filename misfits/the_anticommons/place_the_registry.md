# Place: The Registry

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). Where the one resource is written down as many owners.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A ledger of deeds, a wall of patents, a cap table, a list of heirs.
One resource entered under many names, each with a clear line of title and a lawful right to refuse.

## Holds

The fragmentation, made official.
Here the single thing becomes a set of separate ownerships, and every entry carries a veto over the use of all the rest, recorded and enforceable.

## Offers

Clear title to each owner.
Everyone knows exactly what is his, down to the line, so the ownership is orderly, certain, and defensible, which is precisely what makes it impossible to override.

## Withheld

Any single right to use the whole.
The registry records who may say no and never who may say yes, so the resource has as many owners as entries and no owner at all in the sense that would let it move.
