# Process: Consolidating

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The fragments gathered into one hand, freeing the resource by ending the ownership that froze it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A resource plainly worth more in use than idle, and someone with the means to unify it.
The gridlock priced, the deal proven impossible owner by owner, and a mover who will not do it owner by owner.

## Direction

Toward a single title where there were many.
The pieces gathered at once, by pool, by hidden buyout, or by a taking, so no owner can hold out for last and the resource passes to one who can use it.

## Lever

Overriding or buying every veto together.
Gathering the pieces at once defeats the holdout, who had power only as the last needed yes, and a taking defeats him outright, paying not his price but a court's.

## Echo

A resource freed, and a lawful ownership unmade.
The shop open under one owner, the veto that was perfectly good overridden or bought out, and a fix that lasts exactly until the title fragments again.
