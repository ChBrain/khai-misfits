# Piece: The Divided Title

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The setup: one valuable resource split into many separate ownership rights.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the registry](place_the_registry.md), one resource written under many names.
Where the single thing becomes a set of clear, separate titles.

## Load Bearing

Without it there would be one owner who could simply decide to use the thing.
With it the resource has too many owners and no single one who holds enough to move it, so use requires gathering them all, which is where every later trouble begins. The Divided Title is load bearing because it is the founding condition of the anticommons (Michelman; Heller), the exact inverse of the commons' unowned field: not a resource with no one to exclude, but a resource with too many who can, and the fragmentation is the whole structure in seed.

## Apparent

Ordinary, orderly ownership.
Every title is clear and every owner legitimate, so the arrangement looks like property working exactly as it should, and nothing in any single deed hints that the sum of the deeds is a resource no one can use.

## Yearbook

Made by [fragmenting](process_fragmenting.md), held by [the holder](persona_the_holder.md), the first piece laid down.
It is where the play begins: the Divided Title is what hands each owner [the veto](piece_the_veto.md), and a resource split into enough of them is the anticommons already forming.
