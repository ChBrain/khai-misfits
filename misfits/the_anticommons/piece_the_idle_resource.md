# Piece: The Idle Resource

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The outcome: the resource unused, its value never made, while every owner holds a piece worth nothing idle.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the storefront](place_the_storefront.md), the shop dark and the kiosks outside.
Where the most wanted resource on the street is the one no one can open.

## Load Bearing

Without it the anticommons would be a bad negotiation with no lasting mark.
With it the failure to close becomes a standing waste: the shop stays empty, the drug stays shelved, the block stays vacant, and the value use would have created is simply never created, year after year. The Idle Resource is load bearing because it is the anticommons' harm made real (Heller), the exact mirror of the commons' bare ground: there the shared thing was destroyed by overuse, here the overowned thing is wasted by underuse, and it carries the same cruel term, that the owner who priced fairest is left worst off.

## Apparent

Simply a resource no one happens to be using.
It looks like slack, a shop between tenants, a patent not yet licensed, a lull that the market will surely fill, when in truth nothing will fill it, because the emptiness is not idleness but gridlock.

## Yearbook

Suffered by [the seller](persona_the_seller.md) and [the holder](persona_the_holder.md) through [idling](process_idling.md), left by [the stacked toll](piece_the_stacked_toll.md).
It is the waste that stands: the Idle Resource is what [the anticommons](piece_the_anticommons.md) produces, a value wanted by all, owned by many, and reached by none.
