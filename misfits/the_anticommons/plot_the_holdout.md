# Plot: The Holdout

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The table](place_the_table.md), where someone wants to put the resource to use and must gather every owner's yes.
The assembler works down the row, and each owner learns in turn that the deal cannot close without him.

## Action

[Holding Out](process_holding_out.md): [The Assembler](persona_the_assembler.md), holding [the developer's seat](position_developer.md), pays each asking price and finds the next rise to meet him, while [The Holdout](persona_the_holdout.md), holding [the blocker's seat](position_blocker.md), waits to be needed last and names a price to match, and [The Reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md), holding [the appraiser's seat](position_appraiser.md), totals the asks.

## Stage

[The Holdout](persona_the_holdout.md) tables [Name My Price](plan_name_my_price.md): price the one essential piece as the whole, because without it there is no whole, so [the holdout premium](piece_the_holdout_premium.md) is charged, correctly, at every essential seat.
[The Reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md) tables [Add Up the Tolls](plan_add_up_the_tolls.md) and shows [the stacked toll](piece_the_stacked_toll.md) climbing past the value, while [The Assembler](persona_the_assembler.md) tables [Gather Every Yes](plan_gather_every_yes.md) and cannot reach the last.

## Tension

Every price is fair, and the sum is impossible.
No owner asks more than his essential piece is worth to the deal, and each is right that his is essential, so the tolls that kill the deal are built entirely of correct valuations. There is no one to argue down, only an arithmetic to lose to: complementary owners pricing independently overshoot the whole, and the resource is priced, fairly and fatally, out of use.
