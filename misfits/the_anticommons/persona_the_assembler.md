# Persona: The Assembler

## Taxonomy

[Developer](position_developer.md). The one who would put the resource to use, and must gather every owner's yes to do it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Willing, capable, ready to build.
Sees the empty shop, the shelved drug, the vacant block, and sees the use no one else can reach.
Believes the deal is there to be made, if only every owner will come to the table.

## Action

Goes owner to owner, gathering consent one signature at a time.
Pays each asking price and finds the next asking rises to meet him.
Assembles and assembles, and never quite closes, because one yes is missing until the last, and the last knows it.

## Shadow

Needing every yes, it can be stopped by any no, so its whole effort hangs on its weakest link and its greediest holder.
The more owners it must gather, the surer the stack of tolls climbs past the value, so the resource it could put to use is the resource it cannot afford to free.
Not defeated by a rival: defeated by arithmetic, one no at a time.

## Tell

"I only need everyone to agree."
The whole of the tragedy in a sentence, because everyone is exactly the number that will not.
