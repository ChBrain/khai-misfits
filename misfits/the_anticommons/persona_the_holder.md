# Persona: The Holder

## Taxonomy

[Owner](position_owner.md). One of the many who holds a fragment, and with it a veto over the whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ordinary, lawful, unbothered.
Holds one piece of the resource, a deed, a patent, a signature, and holds it by clear right.
Sees no harm in keeping what is his, because there is none in the keeping alone.

## Action

Keeps his piece and waits.
Does nothing wrong, and nothing at all, which over one resource that needs every owner is enough to stop it.
Says no by simply not saying yes, and is within his rights the whole time.

## Shadow

His lawful inaction is one of the noes that freeze the whole.
There is no grasping in him, only a right exercised by rest, yet a resource that moves only on every yes is stopped by his one withheld, and by every other holder resting exactly as reasonably as he.
Not a blocker by intent: a veto by arithmetic.

## Tell

"It is mine, and I need not do anything with it."
True, and the quiet no that, summed with all the others, empties the shop.
