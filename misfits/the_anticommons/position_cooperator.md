# Position: Cooperator

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The seat that names a fair price to let the deal live.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A piece it is willing to release, and a fair figure for it.
A hope that if each asks what is fair the resource will be used.
No way to make the other owners ask fairly too.

## Orders

Name a modest price and say yes early.
Give up your piece to move the deal along.
Trust the others to be as reasonable as you.

## Loses

Its share, and the deal anyway.
The fairness it offers becomes budget the assembler spends on the holdout, so it funds the very toll that kills the deal, and ends with less than the owner who cooperated least.
Punished for the yes, exactly as the commons' steward was punished for the restraint.

## Drives

Toward a fair, early yes,
which does not close the deal,
only feeds the holdout who will not give one.
