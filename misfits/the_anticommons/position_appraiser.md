# Position: Appraiser

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). The seat that adds the tolls and reads the sum.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The whole set of asking prices, and the arithmetic to total them.
The clear sight that the sum tracks the number of owners, not their greed.
No lever to move any single price, since each is fairly set.

## Orders

Add the tolls, owner by owner.
Mark where the running total crosses the worth of the whole.
Say the number aloud, and let it fall where it will.

## Loses

Its use, though never its accuracy.
The sum it computes is correct and damning and inert, because every toll inside it is a fair price no argument can lower, so the reckoning proves the gridlock and dissolves none of it.
The clearest eye at the table is the one that can change the least.

## Drives

Toward the true total,
which indicts no owner,
and shows only that the pieces, each fairly priced, cost more than the whole.
