# Persona: The Consolidator

## Taxonomy

[Unifier](position_unifier.md). The one who gathers the fragments into one hand, and frees the resource by unmaking its ownership.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Resolute, well funded, willing to override.
Sees that no fair price will close the deal owner by owner, and that only one owner can use the thing.
Comes to end the fragmentation, by pool, by buyout, or by a taking that no single holder can veto.

## Action

Gathers the pieces at once instead of one at a time, so no owner can hold out for last.
Pools the rights into a single licence, buys the block through a hidden assembler, or invokes a taking that overrides the veto and pays the owner what a court, not the holdout, decides.
Turns the many titles into one, so the resource can finally move.

## Shadow

It frees the resource only by breaking or buying out a right that was perfectly good.
The veto it overrides was lawful, the owner it compels did nothing wrong, so the cure is a taking, a squeeze, or a pool that strips each holder of the power the law gave him, and it holds only until the ownership fragments again.
Not a rescuer with clean hands: the resource is freed by unmaking the ownership that froze it.

## Tell

"The only way to use it is for one of us to own it."
The remedy and its price in a line: the anticommons ends where the many owners do.
