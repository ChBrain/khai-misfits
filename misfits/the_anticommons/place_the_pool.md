# Place: The Pool

## Taxonomy

[The Anticommons](play_the_anticommons.md). Where the scattered pieces are gathered into one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A single licence that bundles every patent, a buyer quietly holding every deed, a taking that vests the block in one name.
The many titles drawn together, so one hand can finally move the resource.

## Holds

The consolidation, and its price.
Here the fragmentation is undone by buying out or overriding every veto at once, so no owner can hold out for last, and the resource passes from too-many-owned to one-owned.

## Offers

A resource freed to be used again.
With the pieces in one hand the deal need no longer close with every owner, only be decided by the one, so the shop opens, the drug ships, the block is built.

## Withheld

The ownership it clears.
The pool frees the resource only by ending the many titles that froze it, breaking or buying a veto that was perfectly lawful, and it holds only until the ownership fragments once more.
