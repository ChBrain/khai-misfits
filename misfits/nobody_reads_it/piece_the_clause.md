# Piece: The Clause

## Taxonomy

[Nobody Reads It](play_nobody_reads_it.md). The non-salient term itself, the buried provision in the standard form that no buyer prices and the drafter can move, the object the drift acts on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Lives in [the Fine Print](place_the_fine_print.md), carried to every buyer at [the Counter](place_the_counter.md) in the one form.
Flows as a single dictated term, the warranty limit or the arbitration line or the fee, offered take-it-or-leave-it and set by the seller rather than bargained.

## Load Bearing

Without the Clause there is nothing to drift: it is the non-salient term, unpriced by the buyer and dictated by the seller, that carries the whole decline in one movable provision.
The Clause is load bearing because it is where the market's discipline is meant to reach and does not, the exact place a competitive rivalry on the price fails to police (Kessler). It is non-salient by nature: the buyer reads the price and not the term, so the Clause bears the drift precisely because it is the part of the deal attention does not cover, a provision the seller can worsen without losing a sale because the losing would require a reading no buyer does. The whole trap narrows to this one term: policed where enough read it, degraded where they do not, and dictated either way.

## Apparent

Just standard wording, the same boilerplate every form carries.
Seen at the surface, the Clause looks settled and uniform, a bit of legal furniture common to all sellers and therefore nothing to compare, and the truth that it is movable, that its uniformity is the drift's cover, that a term looking fixed and standard is exactly the term a seller can quietly worsen, stays hidden behind how ordinary and boilerplate the wording seems.

## Yearbook

Skipped by [the Signer](persona_the_signer.md) who takes it on trust, moved by [the Drafter](persona_the_drafter.md) who writes it toward itself, and read by [the Shopper](persona_the_shopper.md) who cannot move it alone.
It is the term at the centre: the Clause is what [drifting](process_drifting.md) moves and [policing](process_policing.md) would hold, the provision [clearing](process_clearing.md) lays on all, dictated in [the fine print](place_the_fine_print.md) and degraded wherever [the discipline](piece_the_discipline.md) has thinned, each notch of [the ratchet](piece_the_ratchet.md) a movement of this one term.
