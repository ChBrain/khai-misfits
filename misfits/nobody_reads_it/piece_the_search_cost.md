# Piece: The Search Cost

## Taxonomy

[Nobody Reads It](play_nobody_reads_it.md). The cost of reading and comparing the terms, the price in time and effort that makes non-reading rational and keeps the informed margin thin.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Sits at [the Counter](place_the_counter.md) and reaches into [the Fine Print](place_the_fine_print.md), the price of the reading no buyer pays.
Flows as the effort a buyer would spend to parse the clauses and compare them across sellers, borne wholly by the one who reads and rising with the queue and the length of the print.

## Load Bearing

Without the Search Cost the buyers would read, the margin would be thick, and the discipline would hold: it is the cost of reading that keeps the informed minority too thin to police the terms.
The Search Cost is load bearing because it sets the size of the reading margin, the quantity every other part depends on (Stigler). It is what makes skipping rational and thus what thins the discipline: because the cost of reading falls on the one buyer and the benefit of a policed clause spreads across the market, no buyer captures enough of the discipline they supply to pay for the reading it takes, so the correct private choice is to skip, and the aggregate of correct skips is a margin below the threshold. Every distortion downstream, the lifted discipline and the drifting term, follows from this one cost, which no buyer can lower and each buyer is right to refuse to pay.

## Apparent

Just a bit of effort, a page one could read if one wished.
Seen at the surface, the Search Cost looks trivial and optional, a small reading any diligent buyer could do, and the truth that it is decisive, that it sets the margin of readers and so the fate of the terms, that a cost each buyer rightly skips is the thing that removes the discipline for all, stays hidden behind how little the reading seems to ask of any single buyer.

## Yearbook

Paid by no one and priced by everyone: skipped by [the Signer](persona_the_signer.md) whose skip it justifies, exploited by [the Drafter](persona_the_drafter.md) whose drift it permits, and borne by [the Shopper](persona_the_shopper.md) whose reading it renders futile.
It is the root quantity: the Search Cost is what [skipping](process_skipping.md) answers to and what keeps [the discipline](piece_the_discipline.md) thin, the reason [the clause](piece_the_clause.md) can drift and [the ratchet](piece_the_ratchet.md) turns, the one cost that, refused rationally by each, thins the margin for all.
