# Plan: Read and Compare

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Margin](position_the_margin.md), inside [Nobody Reads It](play_nobody_reads_it.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Margin](position_the_margin.md)

## Direction

Open the form, parse the clauses, compare them across sellers, and walk on a bad term, being the informed custom the seller must keep (Schwartz and Wilde). Hold the print in line by making the reading buyers worth more than the terms concede, so that worsening a clause loses custom the seller cannot afford, and discipline through mass rather than through any single reading, since the seller's fear scales with the number of readers it stands to lose. Accept that the plan works only above a critical margin: the reading of one buyer disciplines nothing, because a seller who cannot separate the reader from the many absorbs a lone walker without a flinch, so the plan's whole power is in being numerous enough to be missed, and where the search cost keeps the readers thin it holds the terms not at all (Salop and Stiglitz).

- [ ] the clauses are read and compared across sellers, not within one
