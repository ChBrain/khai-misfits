# Plot: The Lone Reader

## Taxonomy

[Nobody Reads It](play_nobody_reads_it.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Aisle](place_the_aisle.md), one buyer who read and a margin too thin to matter.
The clauses compared across sellers, a bad term found, and a threat to walk that the seller can absorb without a flinch.

## Action

[Policing](process_policing.md): the Shopper reads the terms, finds them poor, and would take custom elsewhere, applying a discipline that bites only in bulk.

## Stage

[The Shopper](persona_the_shopper.md) acts from [the Margin](position_the_margin.md) on [Read and Compare](plan_read_and_compare.md), reading [the clause](piece_the_clause.md) and reaching for [the discipline](piece_the_discipline.md), but below the threshold [the ratchet](piece_the_ratchet.md) turns on regardless.
The reading is right and the walk is real, and the term does not move.

## Tension

The one buyer who did the work disciplines nothing, and being right buys no leverage.
There is no failure of diligence: the Shopper read correctly, compared honestly, and would walk on the bad clause, doing exactly what the discipline is supposed to run on. But the discipline is a threshold, and a single reader is below it, so the seller who cannot tell them from the many loses a rounding error by ignoring them. The plot closes with the informed reader right and unheeded, the term unmoved, the discipline idle for want of numbers no lone reader can supply, and no private care enough to hold a floor that engages only in company.
