# Plan: Sign and Go

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Sign-Off](position_the_sign_off.md), inside [Nobody Reads It](play_nobody_reads_it.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Sign-Off](position_the_sign_off.md)

## Direction

Read the price, skip the terms, and close the sale, spending scarce attention on the axis the market prices and none on the buried clause. Weigh the number that varies by seller and bites today, take the print as a fixed backdrop a competitive market is presumed to have set, and complete the purchase, because the cost of reading and comparing a page of clauses exceeds the expected weight of the rare bad term on any single buy (Stigler). Treat the skipping as sound procedure and not neglect, since the benefit of policing a clause is spread across a market and the cost of reading it falls on the one buyer, so the rational allocation of a buyer's attention is exactly to buy well on the price and leave the terms unread.
