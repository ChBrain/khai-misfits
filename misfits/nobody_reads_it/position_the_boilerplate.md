# Position: The Boilerplate

## Taxonomy

[Nobody Reads It](play_nobody_reads_it.md). The drafting seat, which writes the one form offered to all and sets its non-salient terms, able to move a clause wherever no reader resists and unable to be rewarded for holding one that no reader sees.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The form, the pen that writes the clauses, and the single contract every buyer takes or leaves.
The seat holds the drafting of the non-salient terms and the power to set them take-it-or-leave-it, so it commands the print that no buyer negotiates and few buyers read.
Authority over the clause and dependence on the reader, since the seat can write the term to any point it likes but is disciplined only where enough buyers compare it, so its freedom over the print is bounded not by the buyer's consent but by the buyer's attention.

## Orders

Write the terms to where the market rewards them.
Set each clause at the point that wins the sale, holding it generous only where the comparing buyers are numerous enough to punish a worse one, and moving it toward the seat wherever they are too few to notice, then pass the saving to the price where it will be seen.
Compete on the axis buyers read, since the seat's mandate is to win the purchase, and the purchase turns on the posted price, so the boilerplate recovers in the unread clause what it surrenders in the read number, drafting toward itself as the sound answer to a rival who will.

## Loses

The capacity to be paid for a generous term, which the market strips from the seat structurally.
The Boilerplate can offer a better clause but captures no buyer by it, because the buyer choosing on price does not read the clause, so a generous term is a cost the seat bears and a benefit no buyer sees, and competition therefore removes the seat's ability to compete on the print at all. What the position loses is any profitable path to holding the term: a seat that keeps the warranty whole and prices it in is undercut by one that thins the warranty and cuts the price, so the market punishes the decent clause and rewards the mean one, and the loss is not of will but of instrument, since no drafting the seat can do makes an unread generosity pay, the one form clearing to reader and non-reader alike so that the concession to the reader is dispensed to everyone and recovered from no one, which is why the seat that would hold the term cannot afford the discipline it is supposed to answer to.

## Drives

Toward a form written to the price and thinned in the print,
and a drafter that competes hardest exactly where competition is worst observed,
so the seat most responsive to its market is the seat that degrades its own terms fastest, its very discipline on the visible number driving the drift in the unseen clause, each rational form one notch harsher than the last.
