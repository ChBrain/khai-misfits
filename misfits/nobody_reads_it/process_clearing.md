# Process: Clearing

## Taxonomy

[Nobody Reads It](play_nobody_reads_it.md). The settling of the market on one form for all, the single contract the seller posts because it cannot separate the reader from the many, so the term the thin margin permits is the term everyone takes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Drafter](persona_the_drafter.md), at [the Counter](place_the_counter.md), posting one form to every buyer.
Begins whenever the seller cannot tell at the point of sale which buyer read and which did not, and so must offer a single contract to all.

## Direction

Turns the margin's thinness into everyone's clause.
Because one form clears to reader and non-reader alike, the terms the informed few could hold and the terms the uninformed many will accept collapse into a single posted contract, so clearing converts a discipline that runs on a minority into an outcome dispensed to all, the free ride the many take and the floor the few would hold decided together in the one clause the market settles on (Schwartz and Wilde).

## Lever

The seller's blindness to who read.
Because the drafter cannot price-discriminate between the reader and the rest at the counter, clearing works through the pooling of all buyers into one contract: the seller sets the term to the whole demand and not to its parts, so the lever is the single form itself, which makes the reader's concession a gift to everyone and the non-reader's acceptance a cost to everyone, one clause carrying both.

## Echo

A market where reader and non-reader are bound by the identical drifted term.
The echo of clearing is a single clause laid on the diligent and the careless together, so the thinning of the margin does not merely fail the buyers who skipped but removes the floor from under the ones who read, the careful custom bound by the same print as the careless, the discipline gone for both because both were handed one form.
