# Piece: The Ratchet

## Taxonomy

[Nobody Reads It](play_nobody_reads_it.md). The standard form as a one-way mechanism, its terms tightening toward the drafter each cycle the margin stays thin and never loosening, the whole phenomenon of which every other piece is a part: a contract that competition on the price turns one way only.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Turns at [the Counter](place_the_counter.md) where one form clears to all, its teeth cut in [the Fine Print](place_the_fine_print.md), held only where [the Aisle](place_the_aisle.md) sends enough readers to catch it.
Flows as the movable body of terms every buyer signs, competitive where the reading margin is thick and drifting where it is thin, the shape of the whole trap in one form.

## Load Bearing

Without the Ratchet there is no misfit, only a fair market and a bad one: it is the Ratchet that ties the search cost to the clause to the discipline, the one mechanism in which a rational skip becomes a thinned margin and a thinned margin becomes a drifting term.
The Ratchet is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole, the standard form as a device that moves toward the drafter each cycle too few read to police it and never back, so its terms degrade regardless of any seller's intent (Schwartz and Wilde; Kessler). It carries the entire misfit: the search cost's economy is the Ratchet's pawl, letting the margin thin and never thicken, the clause is the tooth it moves, the discipline is the catch that would stop it and is lifted below the threshold, so the Ratchet is not one more piece beside the others but the shape they all compose, the form whose every property is another of the play's pieces at work. It is built of sound parts, rational buyers, competing sellers, a market that clears on one form, which is why it cannot simply be reversed: every notch is a correct response to a market that reads only the price, and the flaw is not in any turn but in the direction the whole mechanism is free to move, so holding the terms is not undoing a turn but manufacturing the reading margin the Ratchet's own pawl keeps too thin.

## Apparent

Just a standard form, the neutral document everyone signs without a thought.
Seen at the surface, the Ratchet looks like settled paperwork, a fair contract a competitive market surely keeps in line, and the truth that it is a mechanism with a direction, that it turns one way and locks, that a form looking neutral and standard is exactly a device whose terms only tighten, stays hidden behind how ordinary and boilerplate the whole form appears.
