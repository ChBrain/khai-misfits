# Position: The Margin

## Taxonomy

[Nobody Reads It](play_nobody_reads_it.md). The disciplining seat, the informed edge of buyers who read and compare, whose custom holds the terms in line above a critical mass and holds nothing below it, a floor that exists only at scale.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The reading, the comparison, and the threat to switch on a term.
The seat holds the informed demand, the buyers who parse the clauses and would move their custom over a bad one, and through them the only pressure that disciplines the print.
Power that is a threshold and not a lever, since the seat disciplines the seller only when its readers are numerous enough to be worth keeping, so what it holds is not a steady force but a floor that engages above a critical mass and disengages below it.

## Orders

Read, compare, and walk on a bad term, in numbers enough to be missed.
Hold the seller to decent clauses by making the informed custom worth more than the terms concede, so that worsening a clause loses buyers the seller cannot afford to lose, and keep the print in line by being the demand the drafter must retain.
Discipline through mass, since the seat's whole power is the seller's fear of losing readers, and that fear scales with the readers' number, so the margin polices the terms only while it is thick and forfeits the print the moment it thins.

## Loses

The threshold, which the search cost holds the seat below.
The Margin disciplines only above a critical mass of readers, and reading the terms costs each buyer more than it saves, so the informed demand is priced too thin to reach the line, and the seat sits structurally under the threshold where its power switches on. What the position loses is the discipline itself, not by weakness of will but by a step in the mechanism: below the critical mass the seller gains more by exploiting the uninformed many than by holding terms to keep the reading few, so the margin's threat does not bite, and no diligence within the seat closes the gap, because the missing force is a count of readers the search cost will not supply, the seat holding a floor that engages only at a scale the market's own economy keeps it from reaching, its policing power a switch stuck off for want of numbers no single reader can add.

## Drives

Toward a discipline that would hold the terms and a margin too thin to apply it,
and a floor that is real above the line and absent below it, with the market resting below,
so the seat that carries the market's whole protection carries it as a threshold it cannot cross, its policing power intact in principle and idle in fact, the terms unheld not because the discipline is weak but because too few read to reach it.
