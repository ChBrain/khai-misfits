# Position: Patron

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). The seat the fix serves, that rewards the relief and calls for more.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A symptom it needs gone now, and a clock that ends at the near term.
The power to reward, fund, and mandate whatever makes the symptom drop.
No line of sight past the delay to the backfire it never pays.

## Orders

Reward what relieves the symptom.
Fund the fix, make it policy, expect it repeated.
Judge by the result you can feel, on the horizon you live by.

## Loses

Nothing on its horizon, and the long problem beyond it.
It buys the relief and never the harm, because the harm lands past its clock and on another seat, so its demand for the visible result keeps the failing fix standing.
Rational about the near term, and blind to the loop by the shape of its own horizon.

## Drives

Toward the felt relief,
away from a cost that never reaches it,
so the pressure for a visible result turns a fixer's reflex into a standing policy.
