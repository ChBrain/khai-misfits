# Piece: The Relief

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). The short-term success: the visible result that earns the fix its trust.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the emergency](place_the_emergency.md), in the symptom's immediate drop.
Where the fix proves itself the instant it lands.

## Load Bearing

Without it the fix would not be trusted, and the loop would never get its grip.
With it the fix is confirmed by the plainest evidence, a symptom that visibly goes, so the relief is what makes the fix the natural reach next time and the reason the worse return reads as a call for more. The Relief is load bearing because it is the balancing loop's reward that reinforces the fix (Kim), the fast, felt success that outweighs a slow, diffuse harm in every judgement, and the very thing that keeps a failing remedy in good standing.

## Apparent

Proof the fix is working.
The symptom drops, so the fix must be right, and the relief is read as the whole result rather than the fast half of a two-part effect, which is precisely how it earns the trust the loop feeds on.

## Yearbook

Felt by [the relieved](persona_the_relieved.md) through [fixing](process_fixing.md), grown from [the fix](piece_the_fix.md) it rewards.
It is the trust the loop needs: the Relief is what makes [the escalation](piece_the_escalation.md) feel sensible, more of a thing that so visibly helps.
