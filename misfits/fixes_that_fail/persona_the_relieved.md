# Persona: The Relieved

## Taxonomy

[Patron](position_patron.md). The one the fix serves, who feels the relief and demands more of it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Pressured, grateful, watching the near-term number.
Wants the symptom gone now, and gets it, and rewards whoever made it go.
Judges the fix by the relief it can feel, on the clock it lives by.

## Action

Rewards the relief and asks for it again.
Praises the fix when the symptom drops, funds it, makes it policy, and expects it repeated whenever the trouble returns.
Presses for more of the fix precisely as it begins to fail, because the relief is the only result it can see.

## Shadow

Its demand for the visible relief is what keeps the fix in place.
The near-term result is all that reaches it and the delayed backfire never does, so it pays for the relief and never the harm, and its pressure turns a fixer's reflex into an institution's policy.
Not greedy: rationally buying the only outcome it can perceive, and funding the loop by doing so.

## Tell

"Whatever you did last time, do it again."
The reasonable demand that makes the failing fix the standing order.
