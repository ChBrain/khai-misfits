# Process: Forgoing

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). The fix let go, the symptom borne, the cause reached at the cost of a worse-before-better.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A loop seen for what it is, and someone willing to stop feeding it.
The recognition that the fix is the cause, and the nerve to let the symptom return unanswered.

## Direction

Toward a quieted loop and a reachable cause.
The fix stops, the compounding unwinds, the symptom flares and is not met, and in the space that opens the underlying problem can finally be worked.

## Lever

A certain pain now against a late and unpromised gain.
Only stopping the fix lets the loop fall quiet, and stopping means bearing the flare with no relief, so the cure is paid for up front in the very symptom the fix used to hide.

## Echo

A broken loop, or a fix reached for again.
The cause reached at last and the problem falling, if the flare was borne long enough, or a flare too sharp to bear, the relief taken once more, and the cycle resumed.
