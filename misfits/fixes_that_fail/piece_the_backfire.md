# Piece: The Backfire

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). The unintended consequence: the delayed effect by which the fix feeds the very problem it relieved.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the morning after](place_the_morning_after.md), in the symptom returned worse.
Where the fix's second effect comes due and deepens the trouble.

## Load Bearing

Without it the fix would be a mere shortcut, imperfect but harmless.
With it the fix is self-defeating: the same act that eased the symptom feeds it through a longer loop, so the remedy is the cause and its success is what hides that, and the problem does not merely persist but worsens by being treated. The Backfire is load bearing because it is the reinforcing consequence that names the archetype (Senge; Forrester), the turn that makes the fix fail rather than just fall short, and the reason the cure and the disease are one act.

## Apparent

Fresh trouble, or bad luck.
Arriving late and unlinked, the worsening looks like a new problem or an unlucky turn, never the return of the fix, so the backfire wears any face but its own and points, if anywhere, at too little fix.

## Yearbook

Seen by [the diagnostician](persona_the_diagnostician.md) through [backfiring](process_backfiring.md), loosed by [the fix](piece_the_fix.md) and hidden by [the delay](piece_the_delay.md).
It is the cure turned cause: the Backfire is what drives [the escalation](piece_the_escalation.md), each worse return calling for the more fix that deepens it.
