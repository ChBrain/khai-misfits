# Position: Heir

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). The seat downstream of the delay, that inherits the compounded problem.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A problem deeper than the one the fix first met.
The bill the delay deferred, arriving after the relief has faded.
No memory, often, that the fix is what deepened it.

## Orders

Receive the trouble the earlier relief bought.
Deal with a symptom now larger and a cause now buried.
Reach, most likely, for the same fix, having inherited the reflex with the debt.

## Loses

The whole of the deferred cost, having had none of the relief.
The fix booked its benefit early and addressed its harm to the future, so the seat pays a bill it did not run up, and its paying is what lets the earlier cycle look worth it.
Structurally downstream, holding the compounding that the relief left behind.

## Drives

Toward a problem it did not cause and cannot easily read,
away from the memory of the fix that made it,
so the true cost of the loop lands where it can neither be felt in time nor charged to its author.
