# Plot: The Letting Go

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The reckoning](place_the_reckoning.md), where someone moves to stop the fix and face the cause it hid.
The loop unwinds only if the fix stops, and stopping means the symptom flaring unrelieved.

## Action

[Forgoing](process_forgoing.md): [The Breaker](persona_the_breaker.md), holding [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), sets the fix down and holds through the flare, while [The Heir](persona_the_heir.md), holding [the heir's seat](position_heir.md), carries the compounded trouble into the space the stopping opens.

## Stage

[The Breaker](persona_the_breaker.md) tables [Let the Fix Go](plan_let_the_fix_go.md): stop the fix, bear [the symptom](piece_the_symptom.md) while the loop falls quiet, and work the cause, so [the fix that fails](piece_the_fix_that_fails.md) is answered at last.
The relief is refused on purpose, the worse-before-better borne, and the cause the fix was hiding finally reachable in the unrelieved space.

## Tension

The one move that works looks like negligence the entire time it is working.
While the loop unwinds the symptom is loud and unhandled, so the Breaker is the one who took away the thing that helped, judged by a pain deliberately borne, and shows no result until, late, the cause gives way. It holds ... or the flare proves too sharp, the proven fix is right there, the hand reaches for it once more, and the loop resumes at the speed it left off.
