# Place: The Morning After

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). Where the delayed effect arrives and the problem returns worse.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The symptom back, and deeper than before, some time after the relief has faded.
No obvious culprit in the room, only a trouble that has quietly grown while attention was elsewhere.

## Holds

The backfire, landed at last.
Here the fix's second effect comes due, far enough from the fix in time that no one connects them, so the place holds the harm and hides its author behind the delay.

## Offers

A clear view of the worsening, to anyone who arrives now.
The trouble is plainly deeper than it was, and the evidence of harm is real, so the morning after shows exactly how bad it has become.

## Withheld

The line back to the fix.
The delay has cut the effect from its cause, so the worsening looks like bad luck or fresh trouble rather than the remedy's own return, and the natural reading is that there was too little fix, not too much.
