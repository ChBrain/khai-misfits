# Piece: The Fix that Fails

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). The phenomenon whole: the cure that is the disease, and its remedy in letting the fix go.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the reckoning](place_the_reckoning.md), where the fix is set down.
And through the whole loop, in every relief that fed the trouble it eased.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of effects with no shape.
With it the effects become one thing: the symptom, the fix, the relief, the delay, the backfire, and the escalation are facets of a single loop, in which a remedy relieves at the surface and feeds the problem beneath, and the only escape is to stop the fix and bear the symptom while the loop unwinds and the cause is faced. The Fix that Fails is load bearing because it is the whole finding (Senge) with its whole price attached: it holds the misfit together, and it holds that the remedy is a worse-before-better, a certain pain taken now against a late and unpromised gain, not a tweak that keeps the relief and loses the harm.

## Apparent

Either a good fix undone by bad luck, or a call to never intervene.
Seen from one side the trouble looks like a sound remedy spoiled by unrelated misfortune; from the other, like a warning that all fixes are traps; and both miss the point, that this fix worked and was the cause, and the cure is not paralysis but letting this one go to reach what it hid.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the breaker](persona_the_breaker.md) through [forgoing](process_forgoing.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: the Fix that Fails is what [the fix](piece_the_fix.md) becomes once its loop has turned, undone only by bearing [the symptom](piece_the_symptom.md) long enough to reach the cause beneath it.
