# Position: Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). The seat that stops the fix, bears the flare, and reaches the cause.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The sight that the loop unwinds only if the fix stops.
The nerve to let the symptom return unrelieved while it does.
The freed attention to work the cause the fix kept hidden.

## Orders

Let the fix go.
Bear the symptom through the unwinding.
Turn to the cause the relief was covering, and fix that.

## Loses

Its standing, for the whole time the cure is working.
The symptom is loud and unhandled while the loop falls quiet, so the seat looks like the one who took away the thing that helped, and shows no result until, late, the cause gives way.
Bears the fix's true cost on purpose, and is the easiest to blame for the flare the fix used to hide.

## Drives

Toward the cause at last,
away from the relief that was feeding the problem,
so the loop breaks only by choosing a certain pain now for a late and unpromised gain.
