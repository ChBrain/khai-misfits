# Pitch: It Works, Doesn't It

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Practical, decisive, results-first.
The problem is here, the fix works, so apply it and get on with the day.
The voice of sensible action, spoken by people who can see the symptom go the moment the fix lands and quite reasonably conclude that a thing that works this well is the answer.

## Undertow

Under the results, a fix quietly feeding the problem it relieves.
The same act has a second effect, slow and hard to trace, that deepens the trouble, so the relief now and the harm later are the one gesture, and when the trouble returns worse it looks like too little fix, never too much.
No one is reckless: they only reach for what works, and what works now is exactly what is making it worse.

## Nerve

The feeling of a problem competently handled,
the symptom gone, the metric green, the fix proven again,
the loop turning underneath so slowly that the cure is never once suspected of being the cause.

## Echo

After the run: a trouble compounded under a remedy no one will question,
a reach for the fix worn into reflex, each dose buying less and costing more,
and a cause left untouched behind the very thing that keeps relieving its symptom.
