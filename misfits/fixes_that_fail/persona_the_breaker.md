# Persona: The Breaker

## Taxonomy

[Reformer](position_reformer.md). The one who lets the fix go, bears the symptom, and reaches the cause it was hiding.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady, unpopular, willing to look like the problem.
Sees that the loop unwinds only if the fix stops, and that stopping means the symptom flaring unrelieved.
Prepared to hold through the worse-before-better for a cause that can only be reached with the fix out of the way.

## Action

Forgoes the relief and holds the line.
Stops the fix, lets the symptom return and not be answered, and turns the freed attention to the cause the fix kept hidden.
Bears the pain of the unwinding long enough for the loop to fall quiet and the real problem to be worked.

## Shadow

The one move that works looks like negligence the entire time it is working.
While the loop unwinds the symptom is loud and unhandled, so the Breaker is the one who took away the thing that helped, judged by a pain deliberately borne, and shows no result until, late, the cause gives way.
Not careless: the only one bearing the fix's true cost on purpose, and the easiest to blame for the flare the fix used to hide.

## Tell

"Stop fixing it. Let it hurt, and let's find out why."
The negligent-sounding sentence that is the only thing that breaks the loop.
