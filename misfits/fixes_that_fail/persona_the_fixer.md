# Persona: The Fixer

## Taxonomy

[Fixer](position_fixer.md). The one who applies the fix, sees it work, and reaches for it again.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable, decisive, trusted for results.
Meets the problem with a fix that eases it fast, and is right there the moment it flares.
Reads the immediate relief as proof the fix is the answer, because visibly it is.

## Action

Applies the fix, and applies it again when the trouble returns.
Sees the symptom go each time, credits the fix each time, and when it comes back worse, brings more of it.
Turns the reach for the fix into reflex, faster with each cycle, never once suspecting his own hand.

## Shadow

The fix he trusts is the loop he is feeding.
The relief is real and the backfire is delayed and diffuse, so nothing ever points back to him, and the worse return reads as too little fix, so he escalates the very thing making it worse, in perfect good faith.
Not a bungler: a competent hand whose proven remedy is the cause, hidden by how well it works.

## Tell

"It works, doesn't it? Just do it again."
The true report of the relief, and the sentence that spins the loop one turn faster.
