# Persona: The Heir

## Taxonomy

[Heir](position_heir.md). The one who inherits the worsened problem after the delay, and pays the fix's real bill.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Later, downstream, holding the account when it comes due.
The next quarter, the next shift, the next generation, arriving after the relief has faded.
Receives a problem deeper than the one the fix was first aimed at, and no memory that the fix caused it.

## Action

Foots the bill the delay deferred.
Meets the compounded trouble the earlier relief bought, and must deal with a symptom now larger and a cause now buried under cycles of fix.
Often reaches, in turn, for the same fix, having inherited the reflex along with the debt.

## Shadow

The one who pays is not the one who chose, so the fix never meets its true cost.
The relief was booked early and the backfire lands late on someone else, so each cycle looks worth it to whoever is fixing and ruinous only to whoever inherits, and the separation of the two is what lets the loop run.
Not unlucky: structurally downstream, holding a bill the delay addressed to the future.

## Tell

"How did it get this bad? We were handling it."
The true bewilderment of the one who received the compounding and not the relief.
