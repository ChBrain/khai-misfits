# Plan: Let the Fix Go

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), inside [Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Reformer](position_reformer.md)

## Direction

Stop the fix, though it works. Let the symptom return and go unanswered while the loop unwinds, and turn the freed attention to the cause the relief was hiding. Bear the flare long enough for the trouble to fall quiet, knowing you will look, the whole time, like the one who took away what helped.

## Orders

([The Breaker](persona_the_breaker.md) forgoes the relief, holds through the worse-before-better, and works the cause.)

## Implementation

([The Reckoning](place_the_reckoning.md); the [forgoing](process_forgoing.md) process; [the fix that fails](piece_the_fix_that_fails.md) is answered by stopping the fix and bearing [the symptom](piece_the_symptom.md) until the cause can be reached.)

## Targets

- [ ] the fix is stopped, and the symptom left unrelieved while the loop unwinds
- [ ] the flare is borne long enough for the trouble to fall quiet
- [ ] the freed attention is turned to the cause the fix was hiding
- [ ] the loop breaks and the cause gives way, or the flare proves too sharp and the fix returns
