# Persona: The Diagnostician

## Taxonomy

[Diagnostician](position_diagnostician.md). The one who traces the worsening to the fix, and is disbelieved.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Patient, systemic, unimpressed by the obvious.
Follows the loop past the relief to the delayed effect, and sees the fix feeding the problem.
Holds the unwelcome finding that the cure and the disease are the same act.

## Action

Names the cause, and points it at the fix.
Traces the worse return through the delay back to the remedy everyone trusts, and says the thing no one wants to hear.
Warns that more fix will mean more trouble, and watches the warning waved off as absurd.

## Shadow

Being right is no help, because the fix so plainly works.
The relief is immediate and the backfire is buried in delay, so the evidence everyone can see says the fix is good and the evidence it takes patience to trace says it is the cause, and patience loses to a symptom that just went away.
The one who understands the loop is the least able to stop it, indicting a remedy by its slow harm against its fast relief.

## Tell

"The fix is what's making it worse. Look at the delay."
The true diagnosis that sounds like nonsense next to a symptom the fix just cured.
