# Piece: The Symptom

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). The pressure: the problem that flares and demands action now.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the emergency](place_the_emergency.md), loud and on the clock.
Where the trouble presents and something must be done at once.

## Load Bearing

Without it there would be no reason to reach for the fix, and no loop to start.
With it there is urgency: a problem that hurts now and will not wait, so the fastest answer wins by simply being able to arrive in time, and the fix is reached for because the symptom cannot be left alone. The Symptom is load bearing because it is the pressure the whole archetype runs on (Meadows), the demand for relief that makes a fast fix irresistible and a slow cause easy to defer, and it is the thing the fix relieves at the surface while the cause goes untouched beneath.

## Apparent

The problem itself, simply.
It looks like the whole of what is wrong, so relieving it looks like solving it, and the distinction between the symptom on the surface and the cause underneath is exactly what the urgency erases.

## Yearbook

Borne at [the emergency](place_the_emergency.md), the pressure the play opens on.
It is the flare that starts everything: the Symptom is what [the fix](piece_the_fix.md) is reached for to relieve, and what returns, worse, once the loop has turned.
