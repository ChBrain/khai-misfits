# Position: Diagnostician

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). The seat that traces the worsening to the fix, and cannot be heard.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The patience to follow the loop past the relief to its delayed effect.
The finding that the fix is feeding the problem.
No result to show for it, because the harm it names is slow and the relief it doubts is fast.

## Orders

Trace the worse return through the delay.
Name the fix as the cause.
Warn that more of it will mean more trouble.

## Loses

The argument, though never the analysis.
Its diagnosis is correct and slow-proving, set against a symptom the fix just visibly cured, so the evidence anyone can see beats the evidence it takes patience to trace, and the warning is waved off.
The clearest reader of the loop is the least able to interrupt it.

## Drives

Toward the cause under the delay,
against a relief too immediate to argue with,
so the true diagnosis is dismissed as absurd beside a fix that so plainly works.
