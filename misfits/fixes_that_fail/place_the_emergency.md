## Shown

The symptom loud, the clock running, and a remedy that makes it stop the moment it is applied.

## Holds

The relief, and the trust it buys.
Here the fix earns its reputation, because the symptom really does go, at once and visibly, so the place shows the fix at its most convincing and its second effect not at all.

## Offers

Fast, dependable relief.
Whatever the flare, the fix quiets it, and it works every time it is tried here, which is exactly why it will be tried every time.

## Withheld

Any sight of the loop.
The emergency shows only the relief and never the delayed effect that follows it, so the fix looks like pure remedy, and nothing in the room hints that the same hand is feeding the trouble it just calmed.
