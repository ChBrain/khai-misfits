# Piece: The Fix

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md). The intervention: the act that eases the symptom quickly and reliably.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the emergency](place_the_emergency.md), within reach and quick to work.
Where the remedy is applied and the symptom stops.

## Load Bearing

Without it the symptom would simply have to be endured or its cause addressed.
With it there is a fast, dependable answer that works at the surface and sets a second effect running beneath, so the fix is both the relief and, through a longer loop, the cause of the worsening. The Fix is load bearing because it is the symptomatic intervention at the centre of the archetype (Senge), the single act with two effects on two clocks, and the whole misfit is what happens when a remedy this reliable is also, slowly, the disease.

## Apparent

The solution, plainly.
Because it eases the symptom every time, it looks like exactly the right response, and nothing about the act on its face reveals the delayed effect that will follow, so the fix reads as pure remedy.

## Yearbook

Applied by [the fixer](persona_the_fixer.md) through [fixing](process_fixing.md), the act the play turns on.
It is the two-effect gesture: the Fix earns its trust through [the relief](piece_the_relief.md) and sets loose [the backfire](piece_the_backfire.md), relief and harm the one hand.
