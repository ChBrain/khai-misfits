# Plot: The Fix

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The emergency](place_the_emergency.md), a problem flaring and demanding an answer now.
A fix is within reach that can make the symptom stop the moment it is applied.

## Action

[Fixing](process_fixing.md): [The Fixer](persona_the_fixer.md), holding [the fixer's seat](position_fixer.md), applies the fix and the symptom goes at once, while [The Relieved](persona_the_relieved.md), holding [the patron's seat](position_patron.md), sees the trouble drop and credits the fix.

## Stage

[The Fixer](persona_the_fixer.md) tables [Reach for the Fix](plan_reach_for_the_fix.md), meeting [the symptom](piece_the_symptom.md) with [the fix](piece_the_fix.md), so it eases at once.
[The Relieved](persona_the_relieved.md) tables [Reward the Relief](plan_reward_the_relief.md), and [the relief](piece_the_relief.md) earns the fix its trust, the visible result standing in for the whole.

## Tension

The fix works, and its working is the whole of the trap.
Nothing here is wrong: the symptom is real, the fix relieves it, the relief is earned and rewarded. That is the trap loading, a remedy proving itself by the plainest evidence there is, its second effect set silently running underneath, and no reason yet, none at all, to suspect that the thing that just helped will be the thing that hurts.
