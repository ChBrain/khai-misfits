# Plot: The Escalation

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The treadmill](place_the_treadmill.md), the problem back and worse, and a fix that worked before.
The reading is natural and wrong: a deeper trouble must mean too little of the remedy.

## Action

[Escalating](process_escalating.md): [The Fixer](persona_the_fixer.md), holding [the fixer's seat](position_fixer.md), applies more of the fix and faster, while [The Relieved](persona_the_relieved.md), holding [the patron's seat](position_patron.md), presses for it, and [The Diagnostician](persona_the_diagnostician.md), holding [the diagnostician's seat](position_diagnostician.md), warns that more fix means more trouble and is waved off.

## Stage

[The Fixer](persona_the_fixer.md) tables [Reach for the Fix](plan_reach_for_the_fix.md) again, harder, so [the escalation](piece_the_escalation.md) tightens the loop and deepens [the backfire](piece_the_backfire.md).
[The Relieved](persona_the_relieved.md) tables [Reward the Relief](plan_reward_the_relief.md), rewarding the brief drop each larger dose still buys, while [The Diagnostician](persona_the_diagnostician.md) tables [Name the Cause](plan_name_the_cause.md) and cannot be heard.

## Tension

Everyone works harder, and the harder they work the worse it gets.
The response looks like diligence: the trouble is worse, so more fix, applied with more urgency, and the sheer effort reads as control. That is the loop at speed, the reach for the remedy worn into reflex, each dose buying less and costing more, the one true warning dismissed as absurd beside a fix that keeps, however briefly, appearing to help.
