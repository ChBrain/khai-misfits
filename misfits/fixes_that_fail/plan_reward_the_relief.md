# Plan: Reward the Relief

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the patron's seat](position_patron.md), inside [Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md).

## Owner

## Direction

Get the symptom gone now, and reward whoever makes it go. Fund the fix, praise it, make it policy, and call for it again whenever the trouble returns. Judge by the relief you can feel, on the horizon you live by, and trust that a result this visible is a result worth buying.

## Orders

([The Relieved](persona_the_relieved.md) rewards the visible relief and demands the fix repeated, turning a reflex into a policy.)

## Implementation

([The Emergency](place_the_emergency.md); the [fixing](process_fixing.md) it funds; [the symptom](piece_the_symptom.md) drops on the near-term clock, so [the relief](piece_the_relief.md) is all the seat can see and all it pays for.)

## Targets

- [ ] the symptom's drop on the near-term clock is what is measured
- [ ] the fix is funded, praised, and made the standing answer
- [ ] the relief is bought and the delayed harm never reaches this seat
- [ ] the pressure for a visible result keeps the failing fix in place
