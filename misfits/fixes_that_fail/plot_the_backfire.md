# Plot: The Backfire

## Taxonomy

[Fixes that Fail](play_fixes_that_fail.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The morning after](place_the_morning_after.md), the relief faded and the symptom back, deeper than before.
Some time has passed, no culprit is in the room, and the trouble has quietly grown.

## Action

[Backfiring](process_backfiring.md): [The Heir](persona_the_heir.md), holding [the heir's seat](position_heir.md), inherits a problem worse than the one the fix first met, while [The Diagnostician](persona_the_diagnostician.md), holding [the diagnostician's seat](position_diagnostician.md), traces the worsening back through the gap to the fix.

## Stage

[The Heir](persona_the_heir.md) tables [Foot the Bill](plan_foot_the_bill.md), receiving the compounded trouble the earlier relief bought, so [the delay](piece_the_delay.md) has cut the harm from its cause.
[The Diagnostician](persona_the_diagnostician.md) tables [Name the Cause](plan_name_the_cause.md), showing that [the backfire](piece_the_backfire.md) is the fix's own return, not fresh trouble or bad luck.

## Tension

The proof arrives, and arrives unreadable.
The trouble is plainly worse, and the one who traces it says the fix is the cause, but the delay has severed the two, so the evidence anyone can see is a symptom that once dropped under the fix, and the evidence it takes patience to follow is a loop no one wants to believe. The natural reading is waiting: not too much fix, but too little.
