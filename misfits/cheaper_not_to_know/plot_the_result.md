# Plot: The Result

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Tally](place_the_tally.md), where the cast ballots come in.
Every ballot marked at [the Booth](place_the_booth.md), on cues by the many and on study by the few, arrives here to be summed.

## Action

[Tallying](process_tallying.md): [The Electorate](persona_the_electorate.md), holding [the electorate's seat](position_electorate.md), carries out [Count the Vote](plan_count_the_vote.md), aggregating [the Vote](piece_the_vote.md) cast by [The Voter](persona_the_voter.md) and [The Diligent](persona_the_diligent.md) alike into [the Verdict](piece_the_verdict.md).

## Stage

The count weighs each ballot equally and asks nothing about how it was arrived at, so a vote cast on a party label counts exactly as much as one cast after a season of homework. A hundred million individual choices, most of them made on [the Cue](piece_the_cue.md) that [The Shortcut](plot_the_shortcut.md) supplied and a few of them made on the knowledge [The Homework](plot_the_homework.md) paid for, sum into a single collective answer. The Tally does its job faithfully: it reads the votes, and only the votes.

## Tension

The choice is settled, legitimately and on the majority of what was cast, and what was cast was mostly cues rather than knowledge, the sum of a hundred million rational refusals to study. The verdict is sovereign and it is half-blind at once, and the beat closes with the count finished and the competence it implies not quite there.
