# Place: The Trail

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md). Where the Candidate competes for a public that decides on cues, the site of [The Slogan](plot_the_slogan.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Rallies, ads, yard signs, a slogan repeated until it sticks.
The party and the name pushed hard and pushed often, and the deep record filed somewhere behind them, a document the studious can find and almost no one is paid to read.

## Holds

The cue, supplied and repeated.
Here [the Candidate](persona_the_candidate.md) meets a public that decides on cues, so the Trail holds the point where [the Cue](piece_the_cue.md) is made and handed out: party, name, endorsement, slogan, offered clearly because clearly is what a cue-deciding electorate can use.

## Offers

A contest a cue-deciding public can actually judge.
Because the Trail supplies what the vote turns on, it offers the electorate a fast, legible choice, party against party, name against name, so it hands out a contest that can be read in a glance, at the cost of the fine print no glance was ever going to catch.

## Withheld

Any reward for leading with the record.
The Trail cannot pay a candidate for the twelve-point policy no one studies, because the vote turns on cues and not on the studying, so it withholds the return on substance that would make campaigning on it worthwhile, and a candidate who tried would spend the hour on a case the public was never going to weigh.
