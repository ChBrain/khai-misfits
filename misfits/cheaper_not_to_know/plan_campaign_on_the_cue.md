# Plan: Campaign on the Cue

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the candidate's seat](position_candidate.md), inside [Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md).

## Owner

## Direction

Give the public the party, the name, and the slogan it decides on, supplying the cue clearly and often, because that is what wins a vote cast on cues. Place the full record where the studious can find it, but do not lead with what no one is paid to hear, since the contest is fought on the ground the electorate actually stands on.

## Orders

([The Candidate](persona_the_candidate.md) supplies [the Cue](piece_the_cue.md) through [Campaigning](process_campaigning.md) on [the Trail](place_the_trail.md), because [the Verdict](piece_the_verdict.md) turns on cues and the unstudied substance wins nothing.)
