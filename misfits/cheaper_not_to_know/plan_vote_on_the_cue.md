# Plan: Vote on the Cue

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the voter's seat](position_voter.md), inside [Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Voter](position_voter.md)

## Direction

Price the cost of mastering the records and the measures against the benefit of a better-informed vote, find the benefit gated behind a near-zero chance that one vote decides, and decline a real cost that buys a near-zero return. Then cast the ballot anyway on the cheap proxy at hand, the party, the name, the endorsement, because the vote must be marked and the deep knowledge was correctly judged too dear.

## Orders

([The Voter](persona_the_voter.md) weighs [the Cost](piece_the_cost.md) against [the Return](piece_the_return.md) gated by [the Pivot](piece_the_pivot.md), declines to inform, and marks [the Vote](piece_the_vote.md) on [the Cue](piece_the_cue.md).)

## Implementation

([The Booth](place_the_booth.md) is where the decision resolves; the [weighing](process_weighing.md) process prices the costly knowledge against a near-zero return; the deep knowledge is declined and [the Vote](piece_the_vote.md) is cast on [the Cue](piece_the_cue.md), a sound shortcut, one of the millions that sum to [the Verdict](piece_the_verdict.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the costly knowledge is declined, a real cost for a near-zero return
