# Place: The Tally

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md). Where the cast ballots aggregate into the collective verdict, the site of [The Result](plot_the_result.md) and [No One's Fault](plot_no_ones_fault.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The count coming in, precinct by precinct, a majority settling out of the returns, a mandate declared once the number is final.
Nothing here looks unusual: a tally like any other, numbers rising against each other until one side is ahead for good.

## Holds

The verdict, and nothing behind it.
Here [the Electorate](persona_the_electorate.md) closes [Count the Vote](plan_count_the_vote.md), so the Tally holds the point where [the Verdict](piece_the_verdict.md) is fixed: the exact sum of the members' choices, no more and no less than what was cast.

## Offers

A clean, sovereign number the whole public can stand behind.
Because the Tally counts every ballot the same, it offers a verdict no one can call rigged on its own terms, one member, one vote, summed without favor, so it hands out legitimacy in the counting even where it cannot hand out the knowledge the counting was owed.

## Withheld

The knowledge behind the votes.
The Tally weighs each ballot equally and never asks what it was cast on, cue or homework alike counting the same, so it cannot show, from inside its own count, that the sovereign verdict it declares was rendered on cues, a half-blindness the number itself has no way to register.
