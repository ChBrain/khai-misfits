# Plan: Count the Vote

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the electorate's seat](position_electorate.md), inside [Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Electorate](position_electorate.md)

## Direction

Weigh each ballot equally, take each as cast, and decide the election by the majority of them, since the count reads votes and never the knowledge behind them. Render the verdict as the exact aggregate of a hundred million individual choices, and inherit whatever those choices were made on, cue or knowledge alike.

## Orders

([The Electorate](persona_the_electorate.md) aggregates the cast ballots through [Tallying](process_tallying.md) at [the Tally](place_the_tally.md) into [the Verdict](piece_the_verdict.md), each [Cue](piece_the_cue.md)-marked vote taken as given.)

## Implementation

([The Tally](place_the_tally.md) sums the ballots; the [tallying](process_tallying.md) process weighs each vote and never the knowledge behind it; the verdict is the exact sum of the members' choices, uninformed because each was rationally cast on [the Cue](piece_the_cue.md), and binding all the same.)

## Targets
