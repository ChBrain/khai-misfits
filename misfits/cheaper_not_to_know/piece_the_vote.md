# Piece: The Vote

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md). The single ballot, one among millions, the unit whose weight in deciding the outcome is the whole of the calculus.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Cast at [the Booth](place_the_booth.md), by the Voter who has already weighed and by the Diligent who has already learned alike.
Not a fraction of a decision and not a symbol of one: one mark, counted once, equal in weight to every other mark cast anywhere in the same election, however it was arrived at.

## Load Bearing

The Vote is load bearing because it is the unit the whole calculus is run over: every other piece in this misfit, the pivot, the cost, the return, exists only to answer the question of what one vote is worth to the citizen who casts it (Downs). It is precise to say the vote is real and equal, never worthless: it counts exactly as much as any other ballot in the box. The misfit is not that the vote does not matter, it is that it is one of millions, so its own weight in deciding anything is set by how many others share the count with it, not by how it was cast. Guard against reading this as a claim that voting is pointless: the Vote here is the fixed, honest unit the rest of the arithmetic is done against.

## Apparent

A voice. A say. The instrument of the citizen's will.
It looks like the whole of civic power concentrated in one act, a mark that speaks for the citizen and moves the outcome by however much conviction went into it. That reading mistakes weight for volume: the Vote carries exactly one share of the count, and no amount of conviction behind it changes the share.
