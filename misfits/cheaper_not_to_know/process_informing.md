# Process: Informing

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md). The homework paid for in full, run by [the Diligent](persona_the_diligent.md) on [Do the Homework](plan_do_the_homework.md), at [the Booth](place_the_booth.md), in [The Homework](plot_the_homework.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

One citizen who decides to know it all anyway, the arithmetic notwithstanding.
Evenings given over to the records and the measures, a real cost paid in full while everyone around them, correctly, declines to pay it.

## Direction

Toward a vote cast genuinely informed.
Every measure is read, every record checked, the offices and their actual reach followed to the end, and [the Vote](piece_the_vote.md) that finally gets cast carries the whole of that homework, marked on knowledge rather than on [the Cue](piece_the_cue.md) the Voter beside them reached for.

## Lever

One informed vote no more pivotal than one ignorant one.
Because the chance that any single vote decides the election stays vanishingly small whether or not that vote is informed, the diligence changes the voter and not the outcome: the homework buys the Diligent the knowing, and the near-zero pivot it was always up against buys the result nothing (Downs; Somin).

## Echo

The private good of knowing bought, the public good left unsecured.
The Diligent leaves the Booth changed, genuinely better informed than they arrived, and the Verdict leaves unchanged behind them, exactly as it would have if this one ballot had been cast on a cue: the no-hero of the piece, real effort spent and no outcome moved.
