# Process: Campaigning

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md). The contest fought on the ground the electorate actually stands on, run by [the Candidate](persona_the_candidate.md) on [Campaign on the Cue](plan_campaign_on_the_cue.md), at [the Trail](place_the_trail.md), in [The Slogan](plot_the_slogan.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A public that decides on cues, and a candidate who must win it as it actually is.
The records prepared, the twelve points drafted, and a plain read of the electorate that will not be paying to hear most of them.

## Direction

Toward party, name, and slogan, supplied clearly and often.
[The Cue](piece_the_cue.md) is what a cue-deciding public can use, so the campaign leads with it: the record is filed where the studious can find it, and the trail is spent on what the electorate will actually carry into [the Booth](place_the_booth.md).

## Lever

A vote that turns on cues rewarding the candidate who supplies them.
Because the electorate rationally decides on party and name rather than on the deep record, the Candidate is rewarded for campaigning on cues and penalized for the substance no one is paid to weigh, meeting the public exactly where the arithmetic on the other side has already left it, no cynicism required to explain the choice (Downs).

## Echo

A contest fought on cues, because the vote it wins is fought on cues.
Nothing in the campaign hides the record; it simply spends its breath where the breath pays, so what is left after the trail is a public handed the party and the name clearly, and the fine case handed to almost no one at all.
