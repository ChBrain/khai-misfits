# Position: Diligent

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md). The seat of the citizen who pays the full cost to master the issues and casts one vote as unpivotal as any other, the diligence real and the outcome unmoved.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The will to do the homework and the time to spend on it: the standing to learn the records and measures in full, at a real cost willingly borne.
A conscientious case: the knowledge acquired is genuine and complete, exactly the knowledge the electorate as a whole was short of, honestly earned.
No power to make its informed ballot weigh more than one vote, so no way to convert its diligence into any share of the outcome larger than the ignorant hold.

## Orders

Do the homework, and cast the one vote it earns.
Read every measure, check every record, come to the booth genuinely informed, at whatever cost to the evenings it takes.
Then mark a single ballot, as decisive as the most careless one, and accept that the knowing changed the voter and not the verdict.

## Loses

The cost of the homework, against a return to the outcome of near zero.
Because one informed vote is as unlikely to decide the election as one ignorant vote, the seat pays in real time for knowledge that moves the result by the weight of a single ballot, so what it loses is the price of a cure that secures no public good, borne in full and returned, to the outcome, almost nothing.
It gains the private good of knowing and surrenders the hope that knowing would be enough, because being informed and being pivotal were never the same thing.

## Drives

Toward diligence that changes only the diligent,
the homework done in full and the verdict unmoved,
so the seat that tries the obvious cure proves it costs the trier real effort and buys the outcome nothing, the one honest fix that the arithmetic leaves powerless.
