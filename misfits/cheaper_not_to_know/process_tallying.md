# Process: Tallying

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md). The aggregation of the cast ballots into the collective verdict, run by [the Electorate](persona_the_electorate.md) on [Count the Vote](plan_count_the_vote.md), at [the Tally](place_the_tally.md), in [The Result](plot_the_result.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The polls closing, and every ballot cast at [the Booth](place_the_booth.md) arriving to be counted.
Cue-marked ballots and homework-marked ballots alike, indistinguishable once they reach the count, each one simply a vote.

## Direction

Toward one verdict, summed from all of them equally.
Each ballot is weighed the same as every other, taken exactly as cast, and the tally moves toward [the Verdict](piece_the_verdict.md), the majority settling out of a hundred million individual choices with nothing behind any one of them consulted.

## Lever

A count that reads votes and never the knowledge behind them.
Because the tally weighs each ballot equally and never asks what it was cast on, it sums cue-cast ballots into an uninformed collective choice all the same, half-blind and binding regardless, a competent count producing an incompetent verdict without any flaw in the counting itself (Somin).

## Echo

A sovereign verdict that is the sum of a hundred million rational refusals.
The mandate is declared, real and binding, and behind it stands not a failure of counting but the arithmetic every voter ran alone: the verdict is half-blind, and the count that produced it, faithful to every ballot handed to it, is at no member's fault.
