# Persona: The Candidate

## Taxonomy

[Candidate](position_candidate.md). The one who competes for a public that decides on cues, and so campaigns on party, name, and slogan rather than the records no one is paid to study, meeting the electorate where the arithmetic has left it, no cynic in it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Competitive. Fluent. On message.
Is the seeker of votes who must win the public as it actually is, and reads that public correctly: it decides on cues, so cues are what a campaign must supply.
No deceit required: the party is real, the name is real, the slogan sorts a true side from another, and campaigning on them is meeting voters on the ground they actually stand on.

## Action

Campaigns on the cue, on [Campaign on the Cue](plan_campaign_on_the_cue.md), through [Campaigning](process_campaigning.md) on [the Trail](place_the_trail.md), supplying [the Cue](piece_the_cue.md) the public decides by.
Speaks to party, name, and slogan rather than to the records, because [the Verdict](piece_the_verdict.md) turns on cues and the substance no one studies wins no votes for the studying.
Does what a candidate before a cue-deciding electorate does: gives the public the cue it decides on, and leaves unspoken the detail it will not be paid to hear.

## Shadow

Not a demagogue hiding the truth, a competitor meeting the electorate where it is, and that is the difficulty: the campaign runs on cues because the vote does, and a candidate who campaigned on the fine record would spend breath on what wins nothing.
Because the public rationally decides on cues, the Candidate is rewarded for supplying them and penalized for the deep case no one is paid to weigh, so campaigning on party and slogan is not a choice to cheapen the contest but the shape the contest already has, set by the arithmetic on the other side.
It meets the voters exactly where they are, and meeting them exactly where they are is a campaign of cues.

## Tell

Says "here is who I am and whose side I am on," clearly and often, and says the twelve-point record once, quietly, where the studious can find it.
Does not pretend the substance is hidden, only knows it wins nothing to lead with, because the electorate that would reward it in principle has, in fact and for good reason, declined to come learn it.
