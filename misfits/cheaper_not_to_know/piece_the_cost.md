# Piece: The Cost

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md). The real price of becoming informed: the time and effort to learn the records, the measures, what the offices do.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Weighed at [the Booth](place_the_booth.md) through [Weighing](process_weighing.md), and paid in full by [the Diligent](persona_the_diligent.md) through [Informing](process_informing.md).
Not a fee charged by anyone: the hours an evening actually takes to read a ballot measure closely, check a record, follow what an office does, spent by a citizen who has a job and a life besides.

## Load Bearing

The Cost is load bearing because it is the one side of the ledger that is real and positive and ordinary, the price of acquiring and understanding political information (Downs), set against a Return that the Pivot keeps near zero. It is precise to say the knowledge itself is freely available: no one hides the records, no one prices the measures out of reach, the Cost is simply the ordinary work of learning them, no different from the effort any subject takes to master. Guard against reading the Cost as a barrier erected by someone: it is erected by nothing but the plain fact that understanding takes time, the same cost a diligent citizen pays in full and an ordinary one rationally does not.

## Apparent

Laziness. A citizen who could not be bothered.
It looks like the natural reading of a cost left unpaid, a person who found the reading too tedious or the records too dull to sit with. That reading mistakes a priced decision for an unwillingness: the Cost is paid or unpaid on the same sound arithmetic either way, and declining it is not a failure to bother, it is a correct judgment about what the paying would buy.
