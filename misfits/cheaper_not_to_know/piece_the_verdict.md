# Piece: The Verdict

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md). The collective outcome: an electorate deciding on cues rather than knowledge, an uninformed democratic choice that is the sum of many rational refusals and the fault of none.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Rendered at [the Tally](place_the_tally.md), the settlement every ballot cast at [the Booth](place_the_booth.md) and every campaign fought on [the Trail](place_the_trail.md) resolves into.
Not a judgment handed down from outside: the exact sum of the votes as they were actually cast, weighed equally and counted once each, whatever each was decided on.

## Load Bearing

The Verdict is load bearing because it is where the individually rational choices of a hundred million citizens become one collective fact: the measured depth of political ignorance across the electorate carries a real cost to the competence of democratic choice (Somin), and that ignorance is the aggregate of individually rational refusals to bear a real cost for a near-zero return (Downs). It is precise to hold both readings at once and not collapse them: the Verdict is legitimate, the exact and binding sum of votes lawfully cast, and it is half-blind, decided predominantly on cues rather than the knowledge that would have made it sound, and neither fact cancels the other. Guard against moralizing the electorate here: the aggregate is uninformed not because its members were foolish but precisely because every member reasoned correctly, so the Verdict's competence is lost to arithmetic, not to any failing that a wiser or more diligent citizenry would have avoided.

## Apparent

The will of the people. A considered mandate.
It looks like the deliberate judgment of an informed public, weighed and meant, the kind of verdict that speaks for what the electorate actually understood and chose. That reading claims a competence the count never purchased: the Verdict says only what was voted, and what was voted was, for the most part, decided on party and name rather than on the records behind them.
