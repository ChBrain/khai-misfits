# Plot: No One's Fault

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Tally](place_the_tally.md), where [The Result](plot_the_result.md) stands and nothing further will change it.
The verdict is in, half-blind, and every actor who might have made it otherwise has already played the hand the arithmetic dealt.

## Action

The reckoning that closes the arc: [The Electorate](persona_the_electorate.md) has governed itself on [the Cue](piece_the_cue.md), [The Diligent](persona_the_diligent.md)'s full [Cost](piece_the_cost.md) bought [the Verdict](piece_the_verdict.md) nothing, and the good that would have made the choice sound, an informed public, went unbought because no single citizen's purchase of it, priced against [the Pivot](piece_the_pivot.md), was ever going to move [the Return](piece_the_return.md) off zero.

## Stage

Look for someone to blame and the search comes up empty at every seat. [The Voter](persona_the_voter.md) ran the sum in [The Long Odds](plot_the_long_odds.md) and declined a real cost for a near-zero return, correctly. [The Diligent](persona_the_diligent.md) paid the cost in full in [The Homework](plot_the_homework.md) and moved nothing. [The Candidate](persona_the_candidate.md) campaigned on the cue in [The Slogan](plot_the_slogan.md) because that is what the vote turns on. Exhorting the electorate to be informed asks the same real cost for the same near-zero return that already priced the knowledge out, so the call changes nothing the arithmetic did not already settle.

## Tension

What stands at [the Tally](place_the_tally.md) is not folly and not apathy: it is the sum of everyone, separately and reasonably, declining a knowledge that was never worth its price to them, and a democracy choosing on cues because that is what a hundred million sound individual choices add up to. Nothing in the mechanism is broken, and nothing in it is fixed, either. The arc closes here, half-blind and at no one's fault.
