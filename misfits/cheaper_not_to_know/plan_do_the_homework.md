# Plan: Do the Homework

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the diligent's seat](position_diligent.md), inside [Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Diligent](position_diligent.md)

## Direction

Read every measure, check every record, follow what the offices do, and come to the booth genuinely informed, at whatever real cost to the evenings it takes. Then cast the single ballot that homework earns, as decisive as the most careless one, and accept that the knowing changed the voter and not the verdict.

## Orders

([The Diligent](persona_the_diligent.md) pays [the Cost](piece_the_cost.md) in full through [Informing](process_informing.md), and casts [the Vote](piece_the_vote.md) as unpivotal as any other, so [the Return](piece_the_return.md) to the outcome lands near zero.)

## Implementation

([The Booth](place_the_booth.md) receives the one informed ballot; the [informing](process_informing.md) process masters the records at real cost; the vote is as unpivotal as an ignorant one, so the diligence buys the private good of knowing and changes nothing in [the Verdict](piece_the_verdict.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] every measure is read and every record checked, at real cost to the evenings
- [ ] the citizen comes to the booth genuinely informed, the homework done in full
- [ ] a single ballot is cast, as decisive as the most careless one
- [ ] the knowing changes the voter and not the verdict, the cure real and the outcome unmoved
