# Position: Electorate

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md). The seat of the whole body of citizens, which sums the cast ballots into a verdict and inherits whatever they were cast on, every member sound and the aggregate half-blind.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The franchise and the decision: the standing to render the collective choice, and a count that weighs each vote equally and the knowledge behind it not at all.
A sovereign case: the verdict it renders is legitimate and its own, the exact sum of its members' choices, owed to no one and binding on all.
No power to sum anything but what its members give it, so no way to render an informed verdict out of ballots each rationally cast on cues.

## Orders

Count the vote, and render what it sums to.
Weigh each ballot equally, take each as cast, and decide the election by the majority of them, since the count reads votes and never the knowledge behind them.
Render the verdict as the exact aggregate of a hundred million individual choices, and inherit whatever those choices were made on, cue or knowledge alike.

## Loses

The competence its verdict claims, and loses it to no member's failing.
Because each citizen rationally declines the costly knowledge and votes on a cue, the seat can only sum cue-cast ballots, so what it surrenders is the informed collective judgment its mandate implies, lost not to folly anywhere in it but to the arithmetic every member correctly obeyed.
It keeps its sovereignty and its legitimacy and loses the knowledge that would have made the mandate mean what it says, because that knowledge was the one good no member had reason to buy.

## Drives

Toward a sovereign verdict rendered half-blind,
the count settled and the choice made on party and name,
so the seat composed entirely of good sense governs itself on cues, its uninformed mandate the sum of everyone's rational refusal and the fault of none.
