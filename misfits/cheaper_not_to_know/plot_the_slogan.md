# Plot: The Slogan

## Taxonomy

[Cheaper Not to Know](play_cheaper_not_to_know.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Trail](place_the_trail.md), where a campaign must be run for a public that decides the way [The Shortcut](plot_the_shortcut.md) showed.
A seat to win, and an electorate that has already, and rationally, declined to study for it.

## Action

[Campaigning](process_campaigning.md): [The Candidate](persona_the_candidate.md), holding [the candidate's seat](position_candidate.md), runs [Campaign on the Cue](plan_campaign_on_the_cue.md), supplying [the Cue](piece_the_cue.md), party, name, slogan, rather than the record no one is paid to study.

## Stage

The Candidate has a full record to run on, a real account of positions and history, and a public that will not read it, so the campaign leads instead with what the public will take: a party label, a recognizable name, a slogan carrying the position in a phrase. The record is not hidden, it is filed where the studious can still find it, but it is not what wins the vote, because the vote turns on cues and a rival candidate will supply them if this one does not. The Trail runs on the same cheap currency the Booth decides on.

## Tension

Nothing here is cynicism: the Candidate is meeting the electorate exactly where [The Long Odds](plot_the_long_odds.md) and [The Shortcut](plot_the_shortcut.md) left it, and leading with the unstudied record would spend effort on what wins nothing. The substance goes unspoken because the arithmetic on the other side of the podium makes it unspeakable, and the beat closes on a campaign fought, honestly, on the only ground the vote is actually cast on.
