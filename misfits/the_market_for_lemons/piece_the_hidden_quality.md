# Piece: The Hidden Quality

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). What only the seller knows and the buyer cannot see.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the listing](place_the_listing.md), in the gap between what is written and what is true.
The condition of the thing, known cold to the one selling it and invisible to the one buying: the engine's real history, the code's real quality, the candidate's real record.

## Load Bearing

Without it the buyer prices each unit for what it is and the market clears cleanly.
With it the buyer cannot price a unit at all, only the pool it comes from, because the Hidden Quality is the information the seller has and the buyer does not, and the whole play is what a market does when one side can see and the other cannot. It is not deceit: the good seller is not hiding anything and the bad one need not lie, the quality is simply unobservable from the buyer's side of the table, and unobservable is enough.

## Apparent

An ordinary sale.
It looks like any market: a thing for sale, a price to agree, both parties free to deal or walk. The asymmetry is invisible, because the buyer does not know what they cannot see, only that they cannot pay top price for a thing they cannot check.

## Yearbook

Held by [the honest](persona_the_honest.md) as surely as by [the passer](persona_the_passer.md), and read by neither buyer.
It is the root of the play: because quality is hidden, price cannot track it, so [the pooling price](piece_the_pooling_price.md) forms for the whole pool at once, and a single price for unlike things is the wedge that splits the good from the market.
