# Persona: The Honest

## Taxonomy

[Good Seller](position_good_seller.md). Holds a real thing and knows its worth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Straightforward. Plain-spoken.
Knows the worth of a good thing to the coin.
Nothing hidden, nothing dressed up.

## Action

Lists a genuinely good unit. Asks a fair price.
The floor comes back at the average.
It thanks them, and withdraws.

## Shadow

It has quality and no way to prove it.
So its reasonable refusal to sell below worth is what bleeds the good from the market.
Right to leave. And its leaving is the injury.

## Tell

"It's worth more than that, and I can't make you see it."
Then it takes the thing home.
