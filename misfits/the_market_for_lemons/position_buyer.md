# Position: Buyer

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). The seat with money and no eyes, unable to tell good from bad.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Money, ready to spend.
No way to verify quality before the sale.

## Orders

Do not overpay an unknown.
Price for the pool, not the promise.
Assume the worst the fog allows, and offer that.

## Loses

The good, discounted out of reach.
A safe offer meets only the units glad to take it.
The prudent price selects for the units it fears.

## Drives

Toward a safe price, which is a low one.
The caution is rational and the result is ruin.
By guarding against the lemon, it drives the good away.
