# Persona: The Squint

## Taxonomy

[Appraiser](position_appraiser.md). Kicks the tyres and means to know.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent, thorough.
Kicks the tyres, lifts the hood.
Takes the time nobody else will.

## Action

Inspects hard.
Reads every surface it can reach.
Still cannot reliably read the hidden quality.

## Shadow

Its diligence cannot see what is unobservable.
So the honest inspection changes nothing, and the buyer discounts anyway.
Effort against a thing effort cannot reach.

## Tell

"Looks fine to me, but who knows."
And no one does.
