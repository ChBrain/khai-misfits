# Process: Withdrawal

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). The cherry out of the market.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A good unit offered the average.
An honest seller handed a price built for a worse thing.

## Direction

The cherry out of the market.
The best stock quietly leaves rather than sell at the mean.

## Lever

Worth against the offer, leave rather than sell low.
When the pooling price sits below true value, the rational move is to walk, and the good always walk first.

## Echo

A pool poorer for the leaving.
With the cherries gone, the average drops, and the next round starts lower.
