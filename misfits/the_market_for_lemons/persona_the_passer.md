# Persona: The Passer

## Taxonomy

[Lemon Seller](position_lemon_seller.md). Holds a poor thing and no shame about it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Agreeable, unbothered, glad to deal.
Easy in the room while the others go stiff.
No reason to leave and no reason to bluff.

## Action

Lists a poor unit at the market rate.
Takes the average without a blink.
Happy to close at the going number.

## Shadow

Not lying. Not leaving.
The average overpays it, so it stays where the good flee.
Its contentment is the counterweight that makes the exit one-way.

## Tell

"Sounds fair to me."
For a lemon, the average always is.
