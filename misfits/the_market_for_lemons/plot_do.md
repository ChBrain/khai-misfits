# Plot: Do

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The haggle](place_the_haggle.md), the buyer weighing an offer against the risk of a dud.
The pooling price is on the table, and the buyer, knowing some units are lemons and unable to tell which, will not pay even that in full.

## Action

[Discounting](process_discounting.md): [The Discounter](persona_the_discounter.md), holding [the buyer's seat](position_buyer.md), offers below the average, to cover the chance of a lemon.
[Withdrawal](process_withdrawal.md): [The Passer](persona_the_passer.md) at [the lemon seller's seat](position_lemon_seller.md) accepts the offer gladly, while the good seller, offered too little, leaves by [the exit](place_the_exit.md).

## Stage

[The Discounter](persona_the_discounter.md) tables [Price the Doubt](plan_price_the_doubt.md): offer least for what cannot be checked, and [the discount](piece_the_discount.md) falls hardest on the units that were worth full price.
[The Passer](persona_the_passer.md) tables [Take the Average](plan_take_the_average.md): pocket a premium for a poor thing and stay, as [the cherry](piece_the_cherry.md) withdraws rather than sell below its worth.

## Tension

Both sides are being sensible.
The buyer is right to discount an unknown, and the good seller is right to refuse a price below worth, and the two right acts are one event: a fair caution and a fair refusal meeting in the middle, where the deal does not happen. So the good exit and the lemon stays, and the pool the market priced from is already worse than the price it quoted.
