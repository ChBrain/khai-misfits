# Position: Lemon Seller

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). The seat that the average pays too much, worth less than the pool.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A poor unit, worth less than the average.
The going rate, priced for a quality it lacks.

## Orders

Sell it for the going rate, no less.
Take the average, which overpays the truth.
Say nothing the fog would not already hide.

## Loses

Nothing. The average overpays it, sale after sale.
The pool subsidizes the poor unit at the good one's cost.
Every honest exit raises this seat's share of the whole.

## Drives

Toward the sale, cheap to make and dear to price.
The market rewards it for staying, so it stays.
As the good leave, the average falls to meet it.
