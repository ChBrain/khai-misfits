# Piece: The Unraveling

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). The travelling spiral: price and quality dragging each other down, round by round.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Through [unraveling](process_unraveling.md), one round of the market after another.
The good leave, the average drops, the price drops, the next-best leave, the average drops again: a staircase going down, each step the reasonable response to the last.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a single round and a slightly worse pool.
With it it is a collapse in slow motion, because the Unraveling is the loop that will not stop on its own: each departure of the good lowers the average, each lower average deepens the discount, each deeper discount drives out the new best remaining, so the market does not settle at a bad price, it descends through every price, shedding a tier of quality at each. It runs one way, down, because nothing in the loop ever raises the average, only lowers it, and a market that can only fall does not find a floor until it hits the lemons.

## Apparent

A market softening.
Read round by round it looks like ordinary decline, prices easing, a category going out of fashion. Each single step is unremarkable. The staircase is only visible from the top, and by then most of the good are already gone.

## Yearbook

Driven by [the discount](piece_the_discount.md) and [the cherry](piece_the_cherry.md) in turn, stopped only by [the stamp](plot_stamp.md).
It carries the market from a fair average down to a lemon price, quality and price spiralling together, until the pool is all [the lemon](piece_the_lemon.md) and even that will not clear, because a buyer who assumes the worst will not pay the average of a pool they no longer believe in.
