# Process: Certification

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). Quality made visible from outside.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A market unable to trust itself.
A floor so hollowed by exits that no honest seller expects a fair price.

## Direction

Quality made visible from outside.
An outside party puts a mark on the good that the pool could not read.

## Lever

A costly check against a cheap claim, a stamp a lemon cannot buy.
The check is priced so the good can pass it and the bad cannot afford to fake it, so the signal holds.

## Echo

A market restored at a permanent toll.
Trade returns, but every honest unit now pays the standing fee that dishonesty made necessary.
