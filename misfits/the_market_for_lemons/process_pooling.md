# Process: Pooling

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). One price for the whole pool.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Units that cannot be told apart.
A floor of look-alikes, no signal to separate them.

## Direction

One price for the whole pool.
The market collapses the range into a single quoted number.

## Lever

The average against the individual, fair to the mean and unfair to the rest.
The mean is exactly right for the middle and exactly wrong for everything above and below it.

## Echo

A rate that suits nothing on the lot.
The good are underpaid, the bad are overpaid, and the price fits no real thing.
