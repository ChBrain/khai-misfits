# Persona: The Voucher

## Taxonomy

[Signaller](position_signaller.md). Stands behind the goods and says so.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sincere, confident.
Stands behind the goods.
Means every word of the promise.

## Action

Offers a costly warranty.
Puts a guarantee on the unit to prove it good.
Backs the claim with its own coin.

## Shadow

A promise a lemon could also make proves nothing.
So the sincere signal is only half believed, unless it costs more than a liar would pay.
The good seller's honesty, indistinguishable from a bluff.

## Tell

"I'll guarantee it."
And the buyer thinks: so would a crook.
