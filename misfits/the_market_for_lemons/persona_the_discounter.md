# Persona: The Discounter

## Taxonomy

[Buyer](position_buyer.md). Careful with the coin, careful with the risk.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful, rational, will not overpay.
Reads the room, counts the odds.
Keeps a hand on the wallet.

## Action

Offers below the average.
Prices in the chance of a dud.
Pays for what the pool is, not what it claims.

## Shadow

Its caution is correct, and it falls hardest on the good.
A discount on a pool robs the units that were worth full price.
Fair distrust of the pool: exactly what drives out the cherries in it.

## Tell

"I can't pay top money for something I can't check."
And it is exactly right.
