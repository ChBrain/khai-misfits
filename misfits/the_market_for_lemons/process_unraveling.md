# Process: Unraveling

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). Price and quality spiralling down together.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The good having gone.
One tier of cherries has already left, and the next best now sit at the top.

## Direction

Price and quality spiralling down.
Each round strips another layer of good and drops the average again.

## Lever

Each round against the last, lower pool and deeper discount and more exits.
Falling prices push out the newly best, whose leaving falls the price further, and the loop tightens.

## Echo

A market descending to lemons.
Round by round the floor sinks until only the worst are left to trade.
