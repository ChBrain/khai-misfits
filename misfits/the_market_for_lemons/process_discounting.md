# Process: Discounting

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). The offer marked down for what cannot be checked.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A buyer facing an unverifiable unit.
Someone who cannot tell cherry from lemon, and must guard against being wrong.

## Direction

The offer marked down for risk.
Every unknown is priced as a loss and shaved off the bid.

## Lever

Caution against worth, pay least for the uncheckable.
The rational buyer bids the average, which is a bargain for a lemon and an insult to a cherry.

## Echo

A price that punishes the good most.
The better the unit, the further the offer falls below it.
