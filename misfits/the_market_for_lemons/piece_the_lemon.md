# Piece: The Lemon

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). The below-average unit, worth less than the price it happily takes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Staying on [the lot](place_the_lot.md), listed and never withdrawn.
The genuinely poor thing dressed as an ordinary one: the tired car, the brittle system, the weak hire, held by someone glad to be paid the average for it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the pooling price would be fair and no one would need to flee it.
With it the pool always contains units the average overpays, because the Lemon is worth less than the pooling price and its owner knows it, so where the cherry withdraws in protest the Lemon stays in delight: the same average that robs the good rewards the bad, and the bad, being rewarded, never leaves. It is the counterweight that makes the exit one-directional, the good going and the bad remaining, so every departure enriches the lemon's share of the pool.

## Apparent

An ordinary unit at the market rate.
It looks like everything else on the lot, priced the same, described the same, indistinguishable at a glance, which is the whole point: if the Lemon looked like a lemon it would be priced like one, and it does not, so it is not.

## Yearbook

Held by [the passer](persona_the_passer.md), who is not lying and is not leaving.
It is why the market fills with what it least wants: as [the cherry](piece_the_cherry.md) exits and the Lemon stays, the pool tilts a little more toward lemons each round, and the average, faithfully tracking the pool, sinks to meet them.
