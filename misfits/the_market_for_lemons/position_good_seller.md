# Position: Good Seller

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). The seat that is right and powerless, holding quality it cannot prove.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A genuinely good unit, sound to the core.
Knowledge of its worth, exact and honest.

## Orders

Sell it for what it is worth.
Ask the true price, the one the unit has earned.
Refuse the discount that treats the good as ordinary.

## Loses

The sale. Offered only the average, the good withdraws.
The market cannot pay the true price, so the true price leaves.
Each honest exit lowers the average for whoever stays.

## Drives

Toward a fair price, the number the unit deserves.
Away from a market that cannot pay one.
The good quietly leaving, because the average is a robbery.
