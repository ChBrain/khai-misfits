# Persona: The Stamp

## Taxonomy

[Certifier](position_certifier.md). Neutral, trusted, third-party.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Neutral, trusted, third-party.
Owes nothing to buyer or seller.
Its word travels where theirs cannot.

## Action

Imposes a check a lemon cannot pass and a cherry can.
Makes the hidden quality visible at last.
Draws the line the eye could never draw.

## Shadow

Not a hero. A toll.
The certification works, and it costs: it restores the market by taxing every trade for the trust the market could not keep.
A fix that is a permanent fee on the fog.

## Tell

"Certified, at a price."
And the price is the deadweight the asymmetry left behind.
