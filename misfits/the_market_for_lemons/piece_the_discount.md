# Piece: The Discount

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). The buyer's rational markdown for what they cannot check.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the haggle](place_the_haggle.md), in the gap between the asking price and the offer.
The buyer's protection: knowing some units are lemons and being unable to tell which, they offer less than the average asked, to cover the chance of buying a dud.

## Load Bearing

Without it the buyer overpays for lemons and the market never punishes the good.
With it the buyer offers below the average, and offering below the average is what starts the exit, because the Discount is the buyer being sensible about risk, and sensible about risk means paying least for what cannot be verified. It is not distrust of any seller in particular, it is distrust of the pool, and the pool contains cherries, so the cherries feel a discount they did not earn, and go. The buyer's fair caution and the good seller's fair refusal are the same event seen from two sides.

## Apparent

Prudence.
It looks like a careful buyer not throwing money at unknowns, which is exactly what a careful buyer should be. The caution is right. It is also the pressure that pushes the good out, because a discount applied to a pool falls hardest on the units that were worth full price.

## Yearbook

Offered by [the discounter](persona_the_discounter.md), who will not pay top money for a coin flip.
It is the buyer's half of the spiral: the Discount drives out [the cherry](piece_the_cherry.md), the cherry's leaving lowers the pool, the lower pool justifies a deeper Discount, and the two feed each other down toward [the lemons market](piece_the_lemons_market.md).
