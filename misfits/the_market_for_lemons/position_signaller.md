# Position: Signaller

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). The seat that promises the quality, staking a word a liar could copy.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A good unit, sound and ready.
A promise to back it, offered in good faith.

## Orders

Prove the quality.
Say what the unit is and stand behind the saying.
Make the buyer believe the truth is true.

## Loses

Belief, because a liar could promise the same.
A word costs nothing, so a word convinces no one.
The cheap signal cannot separate the good from the rest.

## Drives

Toward a trusted signal, one that carries weight.
Which only counts if it costs the liar more than the truth.
A warranty, a stake, a burden the lemon cannot bear.
