# Persona: The Averager

## Taxonomy

[Price-Setter](position_price_setter.md). Quotes the going rate and means it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Even-handed, market-fair.
Quotes the going rate without a flinch.
One number for one kind of thing.

## Action

Prices the whole pool at a single figure.
A unit that cannot be judged cannot be priced alone.
So the pool gets one tag.

## Shadow

One price for unlike things is a discount to the good and a premium to the bad at once.
The fairness of the average is precisely what is unfair.
The market rate that suits nothing actually on the lot.

## Tell

"This is what these go for."
And these are not alike.
