# Pitch: A Fair Price

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Brisk, fair, market-rate.
Everyone pays a reasonable price for what they can see, and the deal is square on both sides.
The voice of an honest marketplace, spoken by buyers who will not overpay a coin flip and sellers who will not undersell what they know they hold.

## Undertow

Under the fairness, an average that punishes the good.
One price for good and bad alike is a bargain for the lemon and a robbery of the cherry, so the cherry leaves, and the average it leaves behind is a little lower.
The market never cheats: it only pays fairly for what it cannot see, and what it cannot see is quality, so quality quietly goes.

## Nerve

The feeling of a fair deal in a fair market,
buyer and seller both acting sensibly,
watching the best goods vanish one by one because sensible is all anyone can be.

## Echo

After the run: a lot full of lemons,
a price no honest seller will take,
and a marketplace where every trade was fair and the good all went somewhere else.
