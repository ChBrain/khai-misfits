# Position: Price-Setter

## Taxonomy

[The Market for Lemons](play_the_market_for_lemons.md). The seat that quotes the pool, one number for everything unlike.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The going rate for the pool, an honest average.
One number to cover every unit at once.

## Orders

Quote the market price.
Set the average and let each unit meet it.
Hold the rate steady against a quality you cannot see.

## Loses

The ability to price a unit on its worth.
One number cannot separate the sound from the spoiled.
As the good depart, the average slides down to chase them.

## Drives

Toward a fair average, computed and clean.
Which is unfair to everything unlike the mean.
The good underpaid, the poor overpaid, the pool draining.
