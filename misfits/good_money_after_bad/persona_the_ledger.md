# Persona: The Ledger

## Taxonomy

[Controller](position_controller.md). The seat that keeps the total honest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Precise. Dutiful. Keeps the numbers.
Every figure reconciles.
The books do not lie.

## Action

Totals what has been spent.
Reads it out at every gate.
Puts the sum where the room can see it.

## Shadow

Its figure is true and points exactly the wrong way.
Feeds the room the one number that should be ignored.
Accuracy in service of the fallacy.

## Tell

Opens every review with "to date we've invested."
The sentence does the escalation for everyone.
