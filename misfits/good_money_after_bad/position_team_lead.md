# Position: Team Lead

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The seat that knows best, and can least stop it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The work, and the truth about it. Hands on the thing, close enough to feel it not working.

## Orders

Build what is funded. Ship the phase. Turn the plan into deliverables and hit the dates the board released.

## Loses

Any standing to halt what it knows is dead. Truth without authority is just a private grief. The seat closest to the failure is the one furthest from the switch.

## Drives

Toward delivering a thing it does not believe in. Craft continues, quality holds, the team performs. And the better it performs, the longer the corpse walks.
