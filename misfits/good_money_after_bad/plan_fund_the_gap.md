# Plan: Fund the Gap

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the funder's seat](position_funder.md), inside [Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Funder](position_funder.md)

## Direction

Send one more increment to protect the last. The gap is small. The last cheque is large. So a small cheque goes out to defend a large one, and good money follows bad.

## Orders

[The Underwriter](persona_the_underwriter.md) approves the top-up, frames it as rescue, and defends the prior funding by adding to it.

## Implementation

[The Gate Review](place_the_gate_review.md); the [Escalation](process_escalation.md) process; a clause of friction: every rescue is priced against the loss it means to save, never against the value it might make.

## Targets

- [ ] the top-up is approved
- [ ] the last funding is defended
- [ ] good money follows bad
- [ ] the rescue enlarges the loss
