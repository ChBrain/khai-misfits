# Position: Funder

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The seat that writes the cheque, to protect the last cheque.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The cheque. The capital that moves the phases, the yes that keeps the lights on.

## Orders

Fund the phases. Release the tranche. Keep the project alive across the gate the board just passed.

## Loses

The good money, sent to guard the bad. Each new dollar goes not to a return but to defending the dollars already gone. The fresh capital becomes a hostage.

## Drives

Toward protecting the last investment, with the next. The instinct is preservation, and preservation reads as continuation. So the drive that would cut losses instead compounds them.
