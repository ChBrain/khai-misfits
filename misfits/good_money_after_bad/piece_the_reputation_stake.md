# Piece: The Reputation Stake

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The champion's name, welded to the project's survival.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

In [the war room](place_the_war_room.md), where the project and the person became one thing.
The moment the champion said this will work and staked their standing on it, so that the project failing and the champion failing are now the same event.

## Load Bearing

Without it the champion could read the falling prospect plainly and call the stop.
With it the champion cannot see the prospect at all, because to see it clearly is to see their own misjudgement, and the mind that made the call is the last mind that can afford to. The Reputation Stake is the self-justification engine: it is not that the champion hides the failure from others, it is that they are structurally the person least able to admit it to themselves, having the most tied to its not being true.

## Apparent

Conviction, leadership, belief in the vision.
It looks like the very quality the organisation hired for: a leader who backs their judgement and does not flinch. The flinch it prevents is the one that would save the money.

## Yearbook

Carried by [the believer](persona_the_believer.md), who is not lying, and cannot look.
It is why the kill can only come from outside: the seat with the authority to stop it earliest is the seat that made the promise, and no promise kills itself. Only [the axe](persona_the_axe.md), who staked nothing, can weigh it clean.
