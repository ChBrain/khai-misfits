# Persona: The Underwriter

## Taxonomy

[Funder](position_funder.md). Backs the team, tranche by tranche.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Supportive. Responsible. Backs the team.
Would not strand a crew mid-build.
Sees the increment through.

## Action

Releases one more increment.
Protects the last funding with the next.
Keeps the line open.

## Shadow

Draws good money after bad.
Each tranche a rational defence of the one before.
The chain of defences, the irrational whole.

## Tell

Says "let's not strand what we've built."
Strands more.
