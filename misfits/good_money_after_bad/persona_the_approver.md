# Persona: The Approver

## Taxonomy

[Review Board](position_review_board.md). The gate that was built to stop things.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent. Procedural. Thorough.
Reads every pack.
Follows the process to the letter.

## Action

Reviews each phase.
Votes proceed, because stopping wastes the last.
Records the actions, expects them met.

## Shadow

Turns the control built to kill failures into a turnstile.
Diligent in the wrong direction.
The stop is the one verdict no board wants recorded holding.

## Tell

Says "proceed, with actions," gate after gate.
Means the actions sincerely.
