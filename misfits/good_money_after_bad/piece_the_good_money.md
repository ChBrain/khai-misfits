# Piece: The Good Money

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The sound next increment, sent after the unsound last one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Crossing [the gate review](place_the_gate_review.md), a fresh budget approved for a dying line.
The new money, itself perfectly good, committed to the same course that consumed the old money to no return.

## Load Bearing

Without it the project starves at its first shortfall and ends.
With it the project is fed exactly when it is failing, because the Good Money is drawn to the Bad by the Backward Rule: the more the last increment failed, the more the next is needed to protect it, and each sound tranche makes the pile it joins larger and therefore harder to abandon. It is the mechanism by which a bad decision recruits good resources, one defensible top-up at a time, until the good money spent chasing the bad dwarfs the bad itself.

## Apparent

Support, rescue, the responsible top-up.
It looks like backing the team, not pulling the rug, giving it what it needs to land. Each tranche is justified, approved, and clean. It is good money, doing exactly what throwing good money after bad has always looked like from inside.

## Yearbook

Released by [the underwriter](persona_the_underwriter.md), who funds the gap to protect the last funding.
It is the escalation made liquid: every increment is a rational defence of the increment before it, and the chain of rational defences is the irrational whole, a fortune spent to avoid admitting a smaller one was lost.
