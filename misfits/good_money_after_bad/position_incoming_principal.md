# Position: Incoming Principal

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The seat that arrived after the spend, and owes the pile nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Fresh authority, and no stake in the pile. New to the room, unbound by the votes already cast.

## Orders

Review the whole. Open the file cold. Ask what the project is worth from here, not from the pitch.

## Loses

Nothing. It arrived after the spend. The sunk cost is somebody else's grief, so the number that traps every other seat has no grip on this one.

## Drives

Toward the forward question no invested seat could ask. Not what was spent, but what remains. Not vindication, but value. The seat that can finally say stop.
