# Position: Review Board

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The gate that cannot close, because it opened the last one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The gate, and the vote. The formal power to pass or to halt, phase by phase.

## Orders

Approve or stop. Sit the gate. Weigh the case, hear the champion, cast the vote that releases the next phase or ends it.

## Loses

Its ability to say stop. It spent the last phase, and stopping now would indict the vote that funded it. So the gate that guards the spend is compromised by the spend.

## Drives

Toward due diligence. The diligence is real, the questions sharp. But it runs in the wrong direction, hunting reasons to continue when the honest question was whether to.
