# Position: Controller

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The seat that keeps the total, and mistakes the total for a case.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The running total. Every dollar in, tallied, reconciled, carried forward without loss.

## Orders

Account for every dollar. Tie out the spend. Show the sum already committed, precise to the cent.

## Loses

The distinction between what is spent and what is worth spending. The ledger grows, and the size of it becomes the argument. Sunk reads as owed.

## Drives

Toward a complete ledger. The ledger is honest. But read the wrong way, a bigger number pulls harder toward continuing, and the completeness serves the wrong master.
