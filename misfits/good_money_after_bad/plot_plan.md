# Plot: Plan

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The war room](place_the_war_room.md), at the launch, everyone certain.
The project is championed and a reputation is staked on it. In [the books](place_the_books.md) the first over-run has just landed, and a pile of spend begins to gather.

## Action

[Sponsorship](process_sponsorship.md): [The Believer](persona_the_believer.md), holding [the champion's seat](position_champion.md), welds its standing to the project, so that the project failing and the champion failing become one event.
[Sunk accounting](process_sunk_accounting.md): [The Ledger](persona_the_ledger.md) at [the controller's seat](position_controller.md) begins totalling what is in.

## Stage

[The Believer](persona_the_believer.md) tables [Prove it Right](plan_prove_it_right.md): vindicate the original call, and sets [the reputation stake](piece_the_reputation_stake.md).
[The Ledger](persona_the_ledger.md) tables [Count What's In](plan_count_whats_in.md): keep the running total in view, and [the sunk cost](piece_the_sunk_cost.md) takes its place at the top of every deck.

## Tension

Nothing here is reckless.
Backing a launched project is right, tracking its spend is right, and a leader standing behind their call is exactly what was asked for. But the reputation is now staked and the pile has started, and the first gate that could stop the thing cheaply turns to the pile and re-commits, because calling it waste this early is the one thing certainty will not do.
