# Position: Champion

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The seat that authored the bet, now living inside it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The vision, and its authorship. The name on the pitch, the promise made in the room.

## Orders

Deliver the project. Defend the call in every review. Hold the line the pitch drew, phase after phase.

## Loses

The ability to see it failing. Sight goes when the self is staked on its not failing. The signal arrives, and the seat reads it as noise.

## Drives

Toward vindication. Vindication was the point from the first slide. But vindication now requires the project never ending, so the drive turns into a refusal to arrive.
