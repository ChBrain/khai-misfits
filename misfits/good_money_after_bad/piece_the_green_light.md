# Piece: The Green Light

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The gate approval that should have been a stop.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the gate review](place_the_gate_review.md), the decision the whole apparatus exists to make.
The stage-gate built precisely to catch a failing project and end it, returning, review after review, the one verdict it was designed to be able to refuse: proceed.

## Load Bearing

Without it the governance works, and a gate somewhere says no.
With it the governance is theatre, because the gate that can only ever say proceed is not a gate, it is a turnstile: the Green Light is the failure of the exact control that was supposed to prevent this, and it fails not by being skipped but by being followed, each board applying the Backward Rule with full diligence and voting to continue. A stop at any gate would have worked. No gate stops, because stopping is the one seat no one on the board wants to be recorded holding.

## Apparent

Due diligence, passed.
It looks like oversight functioning: a formal review, a considered vote, a documented decision. Every box is ticked. The box that is never ticked is the one marked halt.

## Yearbook

Given by [the approver](persona_the_approver.md), gate after gate, with a clear conscience.
It is the institutional heart of the play: the escalation is not one person's weakness but a committee's procedure, and a procedure that cannot say no funds the failure with the full authority of having looked hard at it first.
