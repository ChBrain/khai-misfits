# Plot: Receding Finish

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The timeline](place_the_timeline.md), where the finish line is being re-drawn just ahead, again.
The prospect has fallen since launch, and the payoff sits smaller and further off. The next tranche of funding is on the table.

## Action

[Re-forecasting](process_reforecasting.md): [The Almost](persona_the_almost.md), holding [the forecaster's seat](position_forecaster.md), moves completion to just beyond reach, believing each new date as it is set.
[Escalation](process_escalation.md): [The Underwriter](persona_the_underwriter.md) at [the funder's seat](position_funder.md) releases one more increment to protect the last.

## Stage

[The Almost](persona_the_almost.md) tables [Move the Finish Line](plan_move_the_finish_line.md): re-draw the milestone one phase out, and [the receding finish](piece_the_receding_finish.md) keeps the payoff always almost in hand.
[The Underwriter](persona_the_underwriter.md) tables [Fund the Gap](plan_fund_the_gap.md): send good money to defend the last funding, and [the good money](piece_the_good_money.md) is drawn after the bad.

## Tension

Every increment is defensible.
The re-forecast has reasons, the top-up rescues a team mid-stride, and pulling funding now would strand everything already built. So continuing is justified by the spend behind and not the value ahead, and each rational defence of the last dollar is another dollar the whole cannot get back.
