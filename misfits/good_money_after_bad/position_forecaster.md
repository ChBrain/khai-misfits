# Position: Forecaster

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The seat that names the date, and moves it as it nears.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The plan, and the estimate. The curve to completion, the confidence band, the next milestone marked.

## Orders

Forecast completion. Give the room a date it can hold. Re-plan when the date slips, and slip it forward, credibly.

## Loses

The finish line. It re-draws each time it nears. Ninety percent done for the third quarter running, the last ten always a phase away.

## Drives

Toward a credible date. Credibility is the whole job. But a credible date, perpetually one phase out, is what keeps the money flowing, so the estimate funds the trap.
