## Cue

## Action

[Gating](process_gating.md): [The Approver](persona_the_approver.md), holding [the review board's seat](position_review_board.md), applies its diligence and votes to continue, because stopping now would waste the last phase.

## Stage

[The Approver](persona_the_approver.md) tables [Approve the Next Phase](plan_approve_the_next_phase.md): pass the gate on the strength of what is already spent, and [the green light](piece_the_green_light.md) turns the control into a turnstile.

## Tension

The governance is working perfectly, and it cannot say no.
A formal review, a considered vote, a documented proceed: every box ticked but halt. The board is not negligent, it is diligent in the wrong direction, and the team that knows best has the least standing to stop it. So the project is officially on track, funded entirely by its own history, and dead on every merit no gate is asked to weigh.
