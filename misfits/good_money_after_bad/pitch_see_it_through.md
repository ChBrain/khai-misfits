# Pitch: See It Through

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Resolute, disciplined, committed.
Every phase is delivered, every review is passed, every dollar is accounted for.
The voice of a team that does not quit, that honours what it started, spoken by people who each have too much invested to be the one who stops.

## Undertow

Under the resolve, a pile that only grows.
Each decision looks back at what is spent and calls it a reason to spend more.
The commitment never wavers: it only funds the failing thing in proportion to how far it has failed.

## Nerve

The feeling of a project marching on, on time and on budget for its latest budget,
approved at every gate by people doing their diligence,
toward a finish that has moved every time they looked.

## Echo

After the run: a write-off booked all at once,
a payoff that was always one more phase away,
and a ledger in which every single approval was correct.
