# Piece: The Zombie

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md). The accumulated whole: a project dead on its merits, alive on its history.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

The gap between the two lines, walking.
The prospect died phases ago and the funding never noticed, so the project goes on: sustained not by any future it can reach but by the past it cannot abandon. The Zombie is the standing distance between what it is worth and what it has cost.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a run of careful approvals and one unlucky bet.
With it those are one thing: a project that should have ended at its first honest gate and instead outlives every reason for it, held whole by nobody, because the seat that owns the pile is never the seat that weighs the future, and no seat is asked to do both. It is a corpse kept upright by the cost of its own funeral.

## Apparent

A going concern.
From inside it is a live project, staffed, funded, on this quarter's revised plan. The trap is invisible from within because nothing is failing right now: this phase is on track, this budget is approved, this review is passed. Only the whole is dead, and no gate sees the whole.

## Yearbook

The gates built the body: [the sunk cost](piece_the_sunk_cost.md), [the green light](piece_the_green_light.md), [the good money](piece_the_good_money.md), each defensible in its hour.
[The Axe](persona_the_axe.md), who staked nothing on it, was the first to weigh the future against the future and find nothing there, and killed in one review what six years of careful reviews had kept alive. The loss was the same on the last day as the first. It had only grown more expensive to admit.
