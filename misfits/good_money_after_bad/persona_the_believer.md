# Persona: The Believer

## Taxonomy

[Champion](position_champion.md). The one who called it, and cannot un-call it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Certain. Driven. Staked on the vision.
The original call was right.
It will be right when it lands.

## Action

Champions the project, room after room.
Vindicates the first decision at every turn.
Backs it because backing it is who they are.

## Shadow

Cannot see the falling prospect: to see it clearly is to see its own misjudgement.
Holds the earliest authority to stop, and the last standing that can afford to.
Not blind. Cornered by belief.

## Tell

Says "we're closer than ever."
Means the finish it cannot admit has moved.
