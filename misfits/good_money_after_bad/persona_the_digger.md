# Persona: The Digger

## Taxonomy

[Team Lead](position_team_lead.md). Heads-down on a thing it doubts.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Loyal. Heads-down.
Delivers what the plan asks.
Does not make noise.

## Action

Keeps building because it is funded.
Privately certain it is dead.
Turns the doubt into throughput.

## Shadow

The seat that knows best, and has the least standing to stop it.
Its own effort sunk into the pile it distrusts.
Every clean sprint deepens the hole.

## Tell

Says "we'll deliver what's on the plan."
Does not say the plan is a grave.
