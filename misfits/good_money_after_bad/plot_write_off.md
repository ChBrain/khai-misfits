# Plot: Write-Off

## Taxonomy

[Good Money After Bad](play_good_money_after_bad.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The write-off](place_the_write_off.md), the day someone with no stake in the pile sits down.
A new principal arrives, or the money simply runs out. For the first time the question in the room is not how much is in, but how much is left to gain.

## Action

[Write-off](process_write_off.md): [The Axe](persona_the_axe.md), holding [the incoming principal's seat](position_incoming_principal.md), weighs the future against the future, finds nothing there, and books the loss whole.

## Stage

[The Axe](persona_the_axe.md) tables [Kill It](plan_kill_it.md): stop the project on its forward merits and take the write-down in one hit.
[The ratchet](piece_the_ratchet.md) is broken from outside, the only way a ratchet breaks, and [the zombie](piece_the_zombie.md) falls: dead on its merits for years, upright only on its history, and now finally down.

## Tension

Nobody was reckless.
Every gate was diligent, every increment defensible, every re-forecast made in good faith. The kill is not courage or insight arriving in the room, it is a seat that staked nothing doing the plain arithmetic the staked seats could not bear. The loss booked today was the same loss available on the first day ... only larger now, by everything spent proving it was not there. Or the next check clears, the newcomer is overruled, and the zombie staggers on.
