# Process: Complexifying

## Taxonomy

[The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md). The move that meets a problem by adding a layer of structure, the strategy that solves and, in solving, accumulates.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Administrator](persona_the_administrator.md), at [the Frontier](place_the_frontier.md), meeting a problem with the structure that solves it.
Begins whenever a difficulty arrives and is answered by raising complexity against it, a new office, road, tier, or army, rather than by any other means.

## Direction

Turns a problem into a solution and a standing structure at once.
The difficulty is met by building the apparatus that resolves it, and because the apparatus works it is kept, so complexifying does not merely solve the problem in front of it but adds a permanent layer to the whole, proving the method and committing the society to it for the next problem too.

## Lever

The cheapness of the early solutions.
Because the most rewarding complexity is available first, the layer added at the start returns far more than it costs, so complexifying looks like unmixed gain and the strategy is adopted wholesale, its lever the enormous margin on solutions taken before the hard ones remain.

## Echo

Every problem thereafter is met the same way, and the apparatus only grows.
The echo of complexifying is a society that has no method but addition: each new difficulty calls up another layer, the structure ratchets upward, and the strategy that paid so well at the Frontier runs on unchanged into the range where each layer will pay less than the last.
