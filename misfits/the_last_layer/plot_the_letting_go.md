# Plot: The Letting-Go

## Taxonomy

[The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Retreat](place_the_retreat.md), the return to the base fallen toward nothing and the levy climbing still.
A producing edge that has weighed the apparatus and found it takes more than it gives.

## Action

[Shedding](process_shedding.md): support is withdrawn, the upkeep goes unfunded, and the society sheds the complexity it can no longer pay for.

## Stage

[The Provincial](persona_the_provincial.md), on [Fall Away](plan_fall_away.md), stops yielding the surplus once [the return](piece_the_return.md) to them has gone negative, so [the upkeep](piece_the_upkeep.md) is unmet and the apparatus sheds its [layers](piece_the_layer.md), the whole dropping to a level the base will support.
The withdrawal is a sound private economy, and the sum of many such economies is the fall of the whole.

## Tension

Declining to pay for what no longer pays you is the rational move, and the rational move is what brings the apparatus down.
There is no treachery: the Provincial owes nothing to a structure that takes more than it returns, and letting go is the cheaper, saner choice from where they stand, an economy and not an act of ruin. But the surplus they withhold is the surplus the whole runs on, so the plot closes with the complexity shed and the society simplified, a descent that from the apex is catastrophe and from the margin is relief, the same drop that a deliberate hand could have chosen earlier and at less cost arriving instead as the aggregate of people rationally ceasing to pay.
