# Process: Accruing

## Taxonomy

[The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md). The standing upkeep piling up as every layer laid down must be fed and maintained forever after.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Successor](persona_the_successor.md), at [the Capital](place_the_capital.md), funding the maintenance of everything already built.
Begins the moment a layer is kept rather than dismantled, since a structure retained is a cost retained, and every solution that stays becomes a bill that recurs.

## Direction

Turns each past solution into a permanent charge on the present.
The layer that solved a problem does not stop costing when it stops earning: its staff, its stores, its repair go on being owed, so the maintenance of the whole apparatus climbs with every layer added and never falls, converting the sum of all past solutions into a standing upkeep that the surplus must cover before anything new is bought.

## Lever

The permanence of complexity, which is easy to raise and seldom retired.
Because the society has a method for adding structure and none for shedding it, the upkeep can only accumulate, so accruing works by ratchet, each layer joining a total that grows and does not shrink, its lever the absence of any mechanism for subtraction.

## Echo

The surplus is consumed by maintenance, and the society runs to stand still.
The echo of accruing is a revenue swallowed whole by upkeep: nearly everything the base produces goes to holding the existing apparatus in place, so there is nothing left to invest and no reserve to absorb a shock, the whole strength of the society spent merely to keep from falling behind.
