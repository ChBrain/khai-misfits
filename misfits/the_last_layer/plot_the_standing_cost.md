# Plot: The Standing Cost

## Taxonomy

[The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Capital](place_the_capital.md), the whole apparatus standing and its maintenance falling due at once.
An inherited complexity of offices, armies, and works, every layer of it owed whether or not it still earns.

## Action

[Accruing](process_accruing.md): the upkeep of everything already built climbs with each layer and must be paid before anything new can be bought.

## Stage

[The Successor](persona_the_successor.md), on [Carry the Whole](plan_carry_the_whole.md), funds [the upkeep](piece_the_upkeep.md) of the standing apparatus, drawing on [the margin](position_the_margin.md) to cover a bill that only grows.
The duty is real and the maintenance is prudent, and the surplus that once built is now spent merely to hold.

## Tension

Keeping the apparatus in repair is the responsible thing, and the responsible thing consumes everything the society makes.
There is no waste: the Successor maintains what earlier hands built because a layer let fall is a capacity lost, and prudence says keep what works. But the upkeep climbs with every layer and falls with none, so the plot closes with the surplus swallowed by maintenance and the society running to stand still, its whole revenue spent to keep the machine turning and nothing left to invest or to hold in reserve, the strength on display a standing cost with almost nothing behind it.
