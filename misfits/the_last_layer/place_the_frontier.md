# Place: The Frontier

## Taxonomy

[The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md). The expanding edge, where a problem is met by new complexity at a large return and a small cost, the scene of the strategy while it still pays.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An open edge with cheap problems and rich solutions: land to organise, trade to route, order to impose where there was none.
Shown as growth that pays for itself, the place where a little structure buys a great deal, so the apparatus raised here returns many times what it costs and the method looks like pure gain.

## Holds

Holds the easy, high-return complexity, the first and best solutions a society reaches for.
The Frontier is where the cheapest effective layers are laid: the new road that opens a province, the office that orders a fresh domain, the conquest that funds the next, each solving a real problem at a return so large the cost is scarcely felt.

## Offers

Offers proof that the method works, and the surplus to extend it.
Here the return on complexity is at its peak, so the layer added pays for itself and more, funding the next layer and confirming that answering a problem with structure is simply what a capable society does.

## Withheld

Withholds the curve, which is invisible while the returns are large.
What the Frontier hides is that the best solutions are being spent first, so every layer raised here is cheaper than any that can follow, and the enormous early return is not the shape of the whole strategy but only its beginning, the part before the margin starts to fall.
