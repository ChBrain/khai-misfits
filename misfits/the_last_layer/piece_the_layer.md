# Piece: The Layer

## Taxonomy

[The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md). The unit of complexity added to solve a problem, the office, road, tier, or army raised against a difficulty and kept standing after.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Raised at [the frontier](place_the_frontier.md), where the problem is met, and carried into [the capital](place_the_capital.md), where its upkeep falls due.
Flows from a difficulty to its solution, the structure built to answer a problem and retained because a solved problem stays solved only while its layer remains.

## Load Bearing

Without the Layer there is no strategy: a problem met by adding structure is the whole method, and the Layer is the increment that method lays down.
With it the society has a way to solve almost anything, and a thing that only accumulates: the Layer is the unit of complexity a problem calls forth (Tainter; Service), a piece of standing organisation that resolves the difficulty in front of it and joins the permanent apparatus thereafter. The Layer is load bearing because it is at once the solution and the cost, the answer to today's problem and a charge on every year after, so the piece carries the double nature of complexity, useful when raised and owed forever, the brick that builds the society up and weighs it down in the same act.

## Apparent

Just a solution, the sensible structure a real problem needs.
Seen at the surface, the Layer looks like nothing but competence answered, a difficulty met by the apparatus that resolves it, and the truth that every layer is also a permanent cost, that the solution kept is a bill that recurs, that the method which adds them has no way to take them off, stays hidden behind how plainly the problem in front of it needed solving.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the Administrator](persona_the_administrator.md), inherited by [the Successor](persona_the_successor.md), and paid for by [the Provincial](persona_the_provincial.md) who yields the surplus that feeds it, the unit the whole strategy is built of.
It is the brick of the trap: the Layer is what [complexifying](process_complexifying.md) lays down and [accruing](process_accruing.md) turns into standing cost, the solution whose [return](piece_the_return.md) falls as more are added and whose [upkeep](piece_the_upkeep.md) climbs, the increment that sums, layer on layer, into the [descent](piece_the_descent.md).
