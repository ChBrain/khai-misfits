# Persona: The Administrator

## Taxonomy

[The Office](position_the_office.md). Meets a problem by raising a new layer of complexity, and is rewarded, because the early returns on organisation are large.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Building. Solving the problem in front of them.
Is the one who answers a difficulty by adding structure: a new office for a new need, a road for a supply line, a tier of administration for a growing realm, and sees it work, the problem solved and the realm the stronger for it.
No folly wished on them: they meet a real problem with a real solution, and at the point they act the return on the layer is large and the cost is small, so adding it is plainly the right thing to do.

## Action

Raises a layer of complexity against a problem, and solves it, so the method proves itself and becomes the way the next problem is met too.
Answers the difficulty at hand with the cheapest effective solution available, and because the cheapest effective solutions are taken first, the early return is enormous, a little organisation buying a great deal of order.
Does what a capable administrator does: sees a problem, builds the apparatus that solves it, and moves on to the next, no thought of a curve required, because on the day they act the layer pays for itself many times over.

## Shadow

Not a blunderer, but the first mover on a strategy that pays less every time it is used, and that is the quiet of it: the layer that solves the problem also joins the standing weight, and the method that works today is the method that cannot be stopped tomorrow.
Because the Administrator meets each problem by adding complexity and each solution is kept and fed thereafter, the apparatus only grows, and the returns that are huge at the start fall as the easy problems are used up, so the very success of the early layers is what commits the society to a strategy whose margin bends toward zero.
The solution is sound and it is permanent, so the administrator who solves the problem well is the one who lays the first stone of a weight no later hand can lift.

## Tell

Says "there is a problem, and here is what will fix it."
Is right about the fix and cannot see that fixing is itself the trap, that each sound solution is a standing cost forever after, so the competence that solves the problem is exactly what builds the apparatus toward the point where solving costs more than it saves.
