# Place: The Capital

## Taxonomy

[The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md). The accumulated centre, where the whole apparatus stands and its upkeep falls due, the scene of running to stand still.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A great standing complexity: the offices, the armies, the works, the tiers, all of it built and all of it running, an apparatus that looks like the sum of the society's strength.
Shown as grandeur and permanence, the seat of everything the society has solved, so the weight of it reads as power rather than as cost.

## Holds

Holds the upkeep of every layer ever raised, the standing bill of the whole.
The Capital is where the maintenance of the entire apparatus comes due at once: each office to be staffed, each army to be paid, each work to be kept, whether or not any of it still earns, so the centre carries the total of all past solutions as a cost that recurs forever.

## Offers

Offers the appearance of a functioning whole, held together by the surplus poured into it.
Here the apparatus is maintained and the society persists, so long as the base can be drawn on hard enough to cover the upkeep, the centre buying continuation at the price of nearly everything produced.

## Withheld

Withholds the margin, spent long before it reaches here.
What the Capital hides is that its grandeur is a standing cost with almost nothing behind it: the surplus arrives and is consumed by maintenance, so the whole revenue runs through the apparatus merely to keep it turning, and the reserve that would let the society absorb any shock has already been eaten by the upkeep of holding the whole in place.
