# Piece: The Return

## Taxonomy

[The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md). The benefit complexity yields, large at first and falling at the margin as the cheapest solutions are used up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Largest at [the frontier](place_the_frontier.md), where the easy solutions are taken, and fallen at [the capital](place_the_capital.md), where the dear ones remain.
Flows downward across the strategy's life, high on the first layers and lower on each after, the yield that draws the society into the method and then thins beneath it.

## Load Bearing

Without the Return there is no trap, only cost: it is the benefit that makes complexity worth adding, and its fall is the whole shape of the misfit.
With it the strategy is rational at the start and ruinous at the end by the same measure: the Return is the marginal benefit of a layer (Turgot and Ricardo; Tainter), enormous while the cheap solutions are available and shrinking as they are spent, so the same act of adding complexity that paid many times over at first comes to cost more than it yields. The Return is load bearing because its decline is the engine, the classical law of diminishing returns transposed from land onto problem-solving, the falling curve that turns a sound method into a losing one without the method changing at all, so the piece carries the reason the trap is a trap and not merely an expense.

## Apparent

Just the payoff, the benefit a good solution brings.
Seen at the surface, the Return looks steady, the reward of solving problems well, and the truth that it falls with every layer, that the best solutions were spent first and the rest yield less, that the method is being run down a curve toward the point where it pays nothing, stays hidden behind how well the early layers paid and how unchanged the solving looks.

## Yearbook

Banked large by [the Administrator](persona_the_administrator.md), found thin by [the Successor](persona_the_successor.md), and weighed by [the Provincial](persona_the_provincial.md) against the levy they pay, the yield whose fall is the misfit's engine.
It is the bending curve of the trap: the Return is what [complexifying](process_complexifying.md) reaps and [diminishing](process_diminishing.md) draws down, the benefit of each [layer](piece_the_layer.md) set against its rising [upkeep](piece_the_upkeep.md), the falling yield that carries the strategy into the [descent](piece_the_descent.md).
