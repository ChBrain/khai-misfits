# Piece: The Upkeep

## Taxonomy

[The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md). The standing cost of the complexity already built, the maintenance paid in energy and surplus that climbs with every layer and never leaves.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Falls due at [the capital](place_the_capital.md), where the whole apparatus is maintained, and drawn from [the margin](position_the_margin.md), whose surplus covers it.
Flows from every layer ever raised into a single recurring bill, the sum of all past solutions presented anew each year whether or not any of them still earns.

## Load Bearing

Without the Upkeep complexity would be a one-time cost, paid and done, and no society would be ruined by it.
With it every solution is a permanent charge: the Upkeep is the standing maintenance complexity demands (White; Tainter), paid in the energy and surplus the producing base yields, and it accumulates with every layer because a structure kept is a cost kept. The Upkeep is load bearing because it is what makes the return matter, the fixed and rising floor the yield must clear, so when the marginal return falls below the upkeep the apparatus consumes more than it produces, the piece carrying the weight of the trap, the cost that stands whether the layer earns or not and that the surplus must cover before anything new is bought.

## Apparent

Just maintenance, the ordinary cost of keeping useful things running.
Seen at the surface, the Upkeep looks like prudence, the price of not letting the apparatus fall into disrepair, and the truth that it climbs with every layer and falls with none, that it claims the surplus before any new benefit can be had, that a society can be spending everything merely to stand still, stays hidden behind how reasonable it is to maintain what one has built.

## Yearbook

Owed on every layer, funded by [the Successor](persona_the_successor.md) from [the Treasury](position_the_treasury.md), and paid at last by [the Provincial](persona_the_provincial.md) whose surplus feeds it, the standing weight the whole strategy leaves behind.
It is the weight of the trap: the Upkeep is what [accruing](process_accruing.md) piles up, the standing cost of each [layer](piece_the_layer.md) that the falling [return](piece_the_return.md) can no longer clear, the maintenance that, once it outruns the surplus, forces the [descent](piece_the_descent.md).
