# Plan: Carry the Whole

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Treasury](position_the_treasury.md), inside [The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Treasury](position_the_treasury.md)

## Direction

Fund the upkeep of everything already built, first and without fail, because a layer left unfunded is a capacity lost and a promise broken. Meet each new problem the proven way, by adding another layer, since the difficulties keep coming and the method for them is more structure. Draw harder on the base to balance the account, because the standing costs are fixed and rising and the only variable the seat controls is how much it extracts.

## Orders

([The Successor](persona_the_successor.md) funds the standing apparatus and adds another layer to each new problem, past the peak of the curve where the method costs more than it yields.)

## Implementation

([The Capital](place_the_capital.md); the [accruing](process_accruing.md) process; the Successor pays the [upkeep](piece_the_upkeep.md) of the whole and adds each new [layer](piece_the_layer.md) though [the return](piece_the_return.md) on it has fallen, the surplus consumed to hold the apparatus up.)

## Targets

- [ ] the upkeep of the standing apparatus is funded first and in full
- [ ] each new problem is met by adding another layer, the only method the society knows
- [ ] the base is drawn on harder as the standing costs climb and the surplus thins
- [ ] the apparatus is held together though the marginal return on more of it has turned
