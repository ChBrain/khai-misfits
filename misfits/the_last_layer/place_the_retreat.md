# Place: The Retreat

## Taxonomy

[The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md). The edge of withdrawal, where support falls away and the complexity that can no longer be paid for is shed, the scene of collapse as simplification.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A base pulling back from the whole: levies unpaid, provinces slipping to nearer powers, the apparatus thinning as the surplus that fed it is kept at home.
Shown from the apex as catastrophe, the loss of everything built, and from the margin as relief, a weight set down, the same event read as ruin or as economy depending where one stands.

## Holds

Holds the shedding, the rapid drop from a complexity that cannot be afforded to a level that can.
The Retreat is where the layers come off: the offices go unstaffed, the frontier is let go, the tiers fall away, so the society simplifies not by plan but by ceasing to pay for what no longer pays it, and the descent is quick because the upkeep was the only thing holding the whole in place.

## Offers

Offers the base a return to a bargain that pays, and the survivors a level they can sustain.
Here the producer who stops funding the apparatus keeps their surplus, and the smaller, cheaper order that follows returns more per unit of cost than the great one it replaced, so for those who let go the simplification is a sounder arrangement, not merely a loss.

## Withheld

Withholds the choice, which was available only earlier and only to those who would not take it.
What the Retreat hides is that this same simplification could have been chosen deliberately, before the return went negative, at less cost and less suffering, but only by forgoing capacities the society still used, so the descent that arrives here is the reckoning refused at the Capital, the necessary economy taken late and all at once instead of early and by design.
