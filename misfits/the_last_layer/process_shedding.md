# Process: Shedding

## Taxonomy

[The Last Layer](play_the_last_layer.md). The rapid drop from a complexity that cannot be afforded to a level that can, collapse as simplification.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Provincial](persona_the_provincial.md), at [the Retreat](place_the_retreat.md), withdrawing support from an apparatus that costs more than it returns.
Begins when the return to the base has gone negative and enough producers reach the same arithmetic, ceasing to fund a whole that no longer pays them.

## Direction

Turns an unfunded upkeep into a lost layer, quickly and across the whole.
Once the surplus is withheld, the maintenance the apparatus depends on is not met, so the offices empty, the frontiers are let go, and the tiers fall away, the complexity coming off as fast as the upkeep that held it up is withdrawn, until the society settles at a level the base is willing to support.

## Lever

The arithmetic of the base, for whom letting go costs less than holding on.
Because the producer at the margin owes nothing to a structure that takes more than it gives, the rational move once the return turns is to stop paying, so shedding works not by revolt but by economy, its lever the plain sum in which withdrawal is cheaper than support.

## Echo

A simpler order that pays, reached by fall rather than by choice.
The echo of shedding is a society dropped to a complexity it can afford, more economical per unit of cost than the great one it replaced, so the collapse is for those who stop paying a relief and not a ruin, the same simplification a deliberate hand could have chosen earlier, arriving instead late and all at once.
