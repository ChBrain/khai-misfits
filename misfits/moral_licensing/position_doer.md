# Position: Doer

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The seat that does the genuine good deed and earns the credit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A real good deed, done for its own sake.
The honest proof that it is a good person.
No sense that the proof is a deposit, because the deed felt like an end.

## Orders

Do the good, and do it truly.
Establish, to yourself, that you are good.
Hand the proof to the ledger, and feel the warmth of having earned it.

## Loses

Nothing it can see, and the good it thought it had added.
Its deed is real and becomes the very thing that funds the lapse, so the virtue it is proud of is the source of the licence, and the more genuinely it did good the more slack it has to spend.
The seat that did the real good is the seat whose good is spent to buy the pass.

## Drives

Toward the good deed,
which it takes for an end and the ledger takes for a deposit,
so the true virtue it means as a peak becomes the surplus the balance is built to spend.
