# Persona: The License

## Taxonomy

[Warrant](position_warrant.md). The permission the credit issues, waving the later lapse through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Generous, reassuring, quick with a pass.
Draws on the balance the ledger holds and issues permission against it.
Speaks in the voice of desert: you've earned this, you're allowed this, just this once.

## Action

Grants the lapse on the strength of the credit.
Reaches across domains, so a good deed here pays for a slip anywhere.
Makes the fall feel like a reward, permission rather than transgression.

## Shadow

It issues its passes against a real balance, so the permission feels earned and true.
It does not check that the lapse has nothing to do with the good deed, because the credit spends anywhere.
The warmer and more deserved the pass feels, the more surely it turns a virtue into a permit.

## Tell

Says "go on, you deserve it."
Of a lapse the good deed never actually paid for, in a currency that spends everywhere.
