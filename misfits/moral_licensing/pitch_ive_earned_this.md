# Pitch: I've Earned This

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Warm, assured, at ease with itself.
The voice of someone who knows they are good and has just proved it, and feels the pleasant weight of a credit in hand.
Not defensive: entitled, gently, to a little slack.

## Undertow

Under the assurance, a credit being spent.
The good deed was real and is now a balance, and the balance is not saved but drawn on, so a later lapse is waved through on the strength of it.
The virtue does not raise the conduct: it buys permission to lower it.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching someone do a genuine good thing,
and then, on the warmth of it, let themselves off something else,
sure they have earned the slack, never seeing the two cancel.

## Echo

After the run: a good deed and a lapse that net to nothing,
a balance sitting back at the level it started,
and a virtue that was true, spent entirely on the licence to do less of it.
