# Piece: The Earned Rest

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The legitimate slack after real good, indistinguishable from the licensed lapse.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the reckoning](place_the_reckoning.md), where the remedy tries to tell them apart.
Between the fall that was a lapse and the rest that was truly earned.

## Load Bearing

Without it the cure would be simple: refuse every pass and make the good add.
With it the cure is a hazard: the earned rest is the slack that a person genuinely deserves after real good, a rest and not a lapse, and it looks exactly like the licensed fall because both follow a good deed and both feel deserved. The Earned Rest is load bearing because it is what makes the remedy costly and the misfit humane: it gives the motivator a true case, since virtue that is never allowed to count and never allowed a rest will not be sustained, so the standard that refuses every pass also refuses a rest that was owed, and no ledger can cleanly cut between them.

## Apparent

Either self-indulgence to police or a due to defend, depending who is asked.
To the consistent it can look like all licensing to refuse; to the motivator, all honest rest to protect; and both are wrong, because it is genuinely a mix with no clean seam, so the earned rest reads as one thing or the other, and the truth that the legitimate rest and the licensed lapse are the same shape stays hidden behind whichever case is being pressed.

## Yearbook

Defended by [the motivator](persona_the_motivator.md) within [holding](process_holding.md), the peril [Hold the Line](plan_hold_the_line.md) must cut around.
It is the true rest tangled in the licence: the Earned Rest is why refusing [the license](piece_the_license.md) risks denying a due, the reason answering [moral licensing](piece_moral_licensing.md) cannot be done with a clean knife.
