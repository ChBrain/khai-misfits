# Process: Cashing

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The pass spent on a lapse, and the good deed netted against it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A pass in hand, and a lesser thing it will cover.
At [the indulgence](place_the_indulgence.md), the licence granting the lapse and the lapse ready to take it.

## Direction

Toward a balance brought back to level.
The lapse is taken on the credit, often far from the good deed's domain, and it draws the balance back down to the set point the good deed lifted it above, so the surplus is spent and the account returns to where it began.

## Lever

A withdrawal that feels like a reward.
Because the pass was issued in the voice of desert, the lapse arrives feeling earned, so its cost is hidden in the warmth of a treat, and the fall is never set beside the good deed it cancels.

## Echo

A good deed and a lapse, netted to nothing.
The lesser thing done and felt as owed, the balance back at level, and a real virtue spent, in full, on the permission to do less of it.
