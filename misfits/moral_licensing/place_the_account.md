# Place: The Account

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). Where the moral balance is kept and the pass is drawn against it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The inner books of a self-image: credits and debits, a set level held, a surplus noted when the good deed lands.
The ledger banking the deed and the licence reaching for the surplus, permission drawn in the voice of desert.

## Holds

The balance and the licence.
Here the self is kept to a level and here the surplus is turned into a pass, so the account holds both the bookkeeping that credits a true good deed and the permission it issues, the balance maintained and the licence drawn.

## Offers

A fair record, and a warrant it feels entitled to spend.
The account keeps honest books and reads a rise above the line as slack, so it offers both a just accounting of the good and a permission that feels earned, the credit and the pass in one place.

## Withheld

Any reason to save the surplus instead of spending it.
The account regulates toward a level, not a maximum, so it withholds the impulse to bank the good and add to it, and treats every surplus as slack to be drawn down rather than a gain to be kept.
