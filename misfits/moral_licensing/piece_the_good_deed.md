# Piece: The Good Deed

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The genuine moral act, real and earned, that the self banks as credit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the good turn](place_the_good_turn.md), where it is done.
The real act, helping where it lands and proving the doer good.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no credit to spend, and no licensing at all.
With it the trap has its deposit: the good deed is a true moral act, done for its own sake, and precisely because it is genuine the credit it mints is deserved, so the self can spend it in good conscience. The Good Deed is load bearing because its reality is the whole engine's fuel (Monin and Miller): it is not a fake or a pose to be exposed but an honest virtue, and the misfit is that this honest virtue becomes the surplus a later lapse is charged against, so the better the deed, the larger the pass it funds.

## Apparent

Simply a good thing done.
A real act of virtue looks like exactly what it is, an end in itself, so the good deed reads as pure good, and the truth that it is also a deposit, a credential the account will hold and the licence will draw on, stays hidden behind how genuinely good it was.
