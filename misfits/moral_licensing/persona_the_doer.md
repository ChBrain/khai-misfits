# Persona: The Doer

## Taxonomy

[Doer](position_doer.md). The one who does the genuine good deed and earns the credit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sincere, capable, glad to have done the right thing.
Does the good deed for its own sake, and it is real, no pose.
Feels, afterward, the honest warmth of having been good.

## Action

Performs the genuine good act and earns a true credit by it.
Establishes, to itself, that it is a good person.
Hands that proof to the ledger, which will bank it.

## Shadow

Its good deed is real and becomes the thing that funds the lapse, so its virtue is the very source of the licence.
It cannot see that proving itself good has bought it slack, because the deed felt like an end and not a deposit.
The more genuinely it did good, the more credit it has to spend on doing less.

## Tell

Says "I'm the kind of person who does this."
Just before letting itself off something else, on the strength of exactly that.
