# Piece: The License

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The permission the credit issues, waving a later lapse through as earned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the account](place_the_account.md), where the pass is drawn.
Against the surplus the balance holds, in the voice of desert.

## Load Bearing

Without it the credit would sit unspent and the good deed would just be good.
With it the surplus becomes a warrant: the license is the permission drawn on the balance, the pass that turns a rise above the line into leave to fall back to it, and it spends across any domain, so a good deed here pays for a slip anywhere. The License is load bearing because it is the hinge of the misfit (Monin and Miller's moral credential): it converts a true virtue into a permit, and because it is drawn on a real balance the permission feels earned, which is exactly what lets a good person do the lesser thing with a clear conscience.

## Apparent

A fair sense of having earned a little slack.
Permission drawn on a genuine credit looks like simple desert, so the license reads as a reasonable treat, and the truth that it converts virtue into a permit and spends across domains the good deed never touched stays hidden behind how honestly the credit was earned.
