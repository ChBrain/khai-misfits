# Position: Ledger

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The seat of the moral self-image, banking the good deed as a balance.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A running account of one's own goodness, credits and debits both.
A set point to hold the self at, neither saint nor sinner.
A surplus, when the good deed lands, that reads as slack.

## Orders

Keep the books.
Bank the good deed as a credit, and hold the self to a level, not a peak.
Ready the surplus to be spent, and call the spending earned.

## Loses

The good, by regulating toward a level instead of a maximum.
Its bookkeeping is the ordinary machinery of a self-image and is exactly what makes a good deed spendable, so it treats virtue as a balance rather than a direction, and the honest credit it grants is the credit the licence draws on.
The seat that fairly records the good is the seat that makes it available to be undone.

## Drives

Toward a balance kept,
against a good that was never being maximised,
so the fair accounting of a true good deed becomes the account the lapse is charged to.
