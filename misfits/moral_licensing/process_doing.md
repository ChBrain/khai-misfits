# Process: Doing

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The genuine good deed performed and its credit earned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A chance to do good, and a hand that takes it sincerely.
At [the good turn](place_the_good_turn.md), the doer performing the real act, for its own sake.

## Direction

Toward a true good, and a proof of goodness.
The deed helps where it lands and establishes, to the doer, that it is a good person, so the act is genuinely virtuous and, in the same motion, mints the credential that the account will hold.

## Lever

A good deed that is real, and so a credit that is deserved.
Because the virtue is genuine, the proof it gives is honest, so the credit the ledger banks is not a fiction but a truly earned surplus, which is exactly what makes it feel fair to spend.

## Echo

A real good done, and a deposit made without knowing it.
The deed helping where it fell, the doer warmed by having been good, and a credential handed to the account with no sign that it is about to be drawn down.
