# Process: Holding

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The standard held flat against the credit, and the two costs of holding it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A recognition that the good deed is buying a pass, and a will to stop it.
At [the reckoning](place_the_reckoning.md), the consistent holding the line and the motivator defending the earned rest.

## Direction

Toward a good that adds instead of nets.
The standard is held whether or not a good deed was just done, no slack is granted for it, and the line met is treated as the line met, so virtue is never spent as a pass and can accumulate rather than balance out.

## Lever

A refusal to read a rise above the line as a surplus.
Because the good adds only when it is never credited as licence, the remedy must hold the standard flat, which drains the motive that counting virtue would feed and denies a rest it cannot cleanly tell from a lapse.

## Echo

Virtue made to add, at the price of the will and the rest.
The line held and the good accumulating, or the motive sapped and a true rest refused, so the holding ends either in a self that climbs or in a joyless vigilance that tired the will it needed.
