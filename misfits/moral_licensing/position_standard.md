# Position: Standard

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The seat that holds the line regardless of the credit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

One standard, held flat whether the good deed was just done or not.
The refusal to treat a rise above the line as a surplus to spend.
The view of the self as a work in progress, not an account.

## Orders

Hold the line.
Grant no slack for the good deed, and treat the line met as the line met, not a credit earned.
Make the good add, by never letting it net.

## Loses

The motive and the rest, in the price of holding the line.
Its consistency is right and cannot always tell a licensed lapse from an earned rest, and held too hard it curdles into joyless self-watching, and a bar that credits virtue with nothing can sap the will to be virtuous.
The seat that would make the good add pays in the will and the rest it must also refuse.

## Drives

Toward the standard held flat,
against the motive and the rest that holding it costs,
so the refusal that makes virtue add is the refusal that risks draining the will to do it.
