# Plot: The Wash

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The indulgence](place_the_indulgence.md), the account come to be totalled.
The good deed and the lapse it funded are set, at last, in the same column, and the sum is taken.

## Action

[Cashing](process_cashing.md) come to its close: [The Lapse](persona_the_lapse.md), holding [the lapse's seat](position_lapse.md), stands as the withdrawal, while [The Doer](persona_the_doer.md), holding [the doer's seat](position_doer.md), sees its real virtue was the deposit the withdrawal drew on.

## Stage

[The Lapse](persona_the_lapse.md) tables [Cash the Credit](plan_cash_the_credit.md) once more, and the balance sits back at the level it started.
[The Doer](persona_the_doer.md) faces [the wash](piece_the_wash.md): [the good deed](piece_the_good_deed.md) and the lapse net to nothing, the virtue spent in full on the leave to do less.

## Tension

The good deed added no good, because it paid for the fall.
The two acts looked unrelated and were causally one: the virtue was the credit and the lapse was its spending, so the total comes out level, or below, and the doer, who really did good, has ended no better than before. That is the sting the whole misfit turns on, that being good freed the doer to be worse, and the honest virtue was the very thing that bought the permission to undo it.
