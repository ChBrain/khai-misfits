# Plot: The Good Turn

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The good turn](place_the_good_turn.md), a genuine good deed being done.
The act is real and helps where it lands, and it proves, to the one doing it, that they are a good person.

## Action

[Doing](process_doing.md): [The Doer](persona_the_doer.md), holding [the doer's seat](position_doer.md), performs the good deed for its own sake, while [The Ledger](persona_the_ledger.md), holding [the ledger's seat](position_ledger.md), records it as a credit against the doer's name.

## Stage

[The Doer](persona_the_doer.md) tables [Do the Good](plan_do_the_good.md) and makes [the good deed](piece_the_good_deed.md), true and earned.
[The Ledger](persona_the_ledger.md) tables [Bank the Credit](plan_bank_the_credit.md), entering the deed into [the balance](piece_the_balance.md) as a surplus above the line.

## Tension

A real good is done, and, unnoticed, deposited.
Nothing here is false: the deed genuinely helps and the doer is genuinely good, and the warmth afterward is deserved. But the same act that is an end to the doer is a credit to the ledger, so the virtue is banked in the instant it is felt, and the good turn that means to be a peak becomes, quietly, a surplus the balance is already preparing to spend.
