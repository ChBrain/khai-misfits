# Position: Lapse

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The seat of the permitted worse act, charged to the credit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A pass already granted, so the act arrives allowed.
A small fall framed as a treat, a withdrawal dressed as a reward.
A landing point often far from the good deed's domain.

## Orders

Take the pass and do the lesser thing.
Draw the balance back down to the level the good deed lifted it above.
Feel it as owed, not as a fall.

## Loses

The good, exactly, and hides the loss in the warmth of desert.
It brings the moral balance back to level, so it is the good deed undone, and because it feels earned its cost is never counted against the virtue that funded it.
The seat that takes the earned treat is the seat where the good deed is spent to nothing.

## Drives

Toward the lesser act,
felt as a reward and functioning as a withdrawal,
so the treat waved through as earned is the point at which the virtue nets to zero.
