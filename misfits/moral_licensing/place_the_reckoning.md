# Place: The Reckoning

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). Where the remedy is weighed: hold the line, or keep virtue worth doing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A move to stop the good deed buying a pass: the consistent holding the standard flat, refusing to credit virtue with slack.
The motivator answering that virtue uncounted goes undone, that the earned rest is real, and that vigilance curdles.

## Holds

The remedy and its two costs.
Here the good can be made to add by never letting it net, and here that holding drains the motive and denies the rest, so the reckoning holds both the one move that makes virtue add and the will and the rest it spends to do it.

## Offers

A way to make the good add instead of net.
By holding the standard regardless of the credit, the reckoning offers a self that climbs rather than balances, virtue that accumulates because it is never spent as a pass.

## Withheld

A clean line between the licensed lapse and the earned rest.
The reckoning cannot tell the fall dressed as a rest from the rest that was truly earned, so it withholds the certainty its holding needs, and refuses the pass only at the risk of refusing a rest, and of tiring the will it depends on.
