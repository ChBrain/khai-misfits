# Persona: The Consistent

## Taxonomy

[Standard](position_standard.md). The one who holds the standard regardless of the credit, refusing to let a good deed buy a pass.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady, unsentimental about its own virtue, uninterested in a balance.
Holds the same line whether it has just done good or not, and grants itself no slack for it.
Treats the self as a work in progress, not an account to spend.

## Action

Refuses to credit the good deed as licence for a later lapse.
Holds the standard flat, so a rise above the line is not a surplus but simply the line met.
Makes virtue add, by never letting it net.

## Shadow

Its consistency is right and dear, and it cannot always tell a licensed lapse from a rest that was earned.
Held too hard, its refusal of all slack curdles into a joyless self-watching, and its bar that never credits virtue can sap the will to be virtuous.
The one who would make the good add pays for it in the motive and the rest it must also deny.

## Tell

Says "the good deed doesn't buy the slip."
And cannot always say whether the slip was a lapse or a rest that was truly earned.
