# Persona: The Motivator

## Taxonomy

[Motive](position_motive.md). The one who warns that virtue uncounted goes undone, and the earned rest is real.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Humane, wary of severity, protective of the will to do good.
Points out that if virtue never counts, fewer will trouble to be virtuous.
Insists that some slack is a rest honestly earned, not a lapse to be policed.

## Action

Argues against a standard that credits the good deed with nothing.
Reminds the room that a joyless self-watching does not make better people, only tireder ones.
Defends the earned rest, and the warmth that keeps the good deed worth doing at all.

## Shadow

Its case is often right and is exactly the door the licence walks through.
It cannot tell, any better than the consistent can, the rest that was earned from the lapse dressed as one, so its defence of desert shelters the very licensing it does not mean to.
The voice that keeps virtue worth doing is the voice that keeps the pass available.

## Tell

Says "let them enjoy having been good, or they'll stop."
Holding open the same door the licence needs, for the best of reasons.
