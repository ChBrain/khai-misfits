# Plan: Keep It Worth Doing

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the motive's seat](position_motive.md), inside [Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Motive](position_motive.md)

## Direction

Keep virtue worth doing. Let the good deed count for something, defend the earned rest as a rest, and guard the will to be good against a severity that would only tire it out. Remember that virtue uncounted goes undone, and that a joyless self-watching makes not better people but wearier ones.

## Orders

([The Motivator](persona_the_motivator.md) defends the earned rest and the counting of virtue, and cannot tell it, any better than the standard can, from the licence it thereby shelters.)

## Implementation

([The Reckoning](place_the_reckoning.md); the [holding](process_holding.md) it tempers; defending [the earned rest](piece_the_earned_rest.md) keeps the will alive but holds open the door [the license](piece_the_license.md) needs, the two indistinguishable.)

## Targets

- [ ] virtue is allowed to count, so it stays worth doing
- [ ] the earned rest is defended as a rest, not policed as a lapse
- [ ] the will to be good is guarded against a tiring severity
- [ ] the door kept open for the earned rest is the door the licence walks through
