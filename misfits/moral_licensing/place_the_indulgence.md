# Place: The Indulgence

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). Where the lapse is taken on the credit, and the good washes out.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The pass being spent: a small fall taken as a treat, permission already in hand.
The lapse landing, as often as not, far from the good deed's domain, and the balance easing back down to level.

## Holds

The lapse and the wash.
Here the lesser thing is done on the credit and here the good deed nets against it, so the indulgence holds both the permitted act and the cancelling, the withdrawal and the zero it brings the balance to.

## Offers

A treat that feels owed.
The pass is in hand and the fall is framed as a reward, so the indulgence offers the warmth of a slack honestly earned, a lapse that arrives feeling less like a fall than a due.

## Withheld

The bill, charged to the virtue that funded it.
The lapse feels like a reward and functions like a withdrawal, so the indulgence withholds the line between the treat and its cost, and never sets the fall beside the good deed it just cancelled.
