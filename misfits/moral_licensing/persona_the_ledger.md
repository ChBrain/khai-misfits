# Persona: The Ledger

## Taxonomy

[Ledger](position_ledger.md). The moral self-image that keeps books, banking the good deed as a balance to spend.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Orderly, fair-minded, keeping a running account of one's own goodness.
Records the good and the bad and holds the self to a level, neither saint nor sinner.
Feels a surplus as slack, and a deficit as a debt to make up.

## Action

Banks the good deed as a credit against the doer's name.
Keeps the moral self at a set point, not a peak, so a rise above it becomes spendable.
Readies the surplus to be drawn on, and calls it earned.

## Shadow

Its bookkeeping is the ordinary machinery of a self-image, and it is exactly what makes a good deed spendable.
It regulates toward a level and not a maximum, so it treats virtue as a balance rather than a direction.
The honest accounting that credits a true good deed is the accounting that licenses the lapse.

## Tell

Says "you're due a little slack, you've been good."
Keeping books on a self that was never meant to be audited.
