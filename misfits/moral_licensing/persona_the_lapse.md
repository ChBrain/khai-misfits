# Persona: The Lapse

## Taxonomy

[Lapse](position_lapse.md). The permitted worse act, taken on the credit, often far from the good deed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Small, forgivable, framed as a treat rather than a fall.
Arrives with permission already granted, so it feels allowed, even owed.
Wears the face of a reward, not a transgression.

## Action

Takes the pass the licence grants and does the lesser thing.
Draws the balance back down to the level the good deed lifted it above.
Lands, as often as not, in a domain the good deed never touched.

## Shadow

It feels like a reward and functions like a withdrawal, so its cost is hidden in the warmth of desert.
It brings the moral balance back to level, so it is exactly the good deed undone.
The lapse waved through as earned is the point where the virtue is spent to nothing.

## Tell

Says "just this once, I've earned it."
Cashing a credit whose spending cancels the very good that filled it.
