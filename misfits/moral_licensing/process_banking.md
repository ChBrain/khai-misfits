# Process: Banking

## Taxonomy

[Moral Licensing](play_moral_licensing.md). The good deed credited to the self-image and the pass drawn against the surplus.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A credential handed over, and books ready to receive it.
At [the account](place_the_account.md), the ledger recording the good deed and the licence eyeing the surplus.

## Direction

Toward a credit that becomes a permission.
The good deed is banked against the doer's name, the self is held to a set level rather than a peak, so the rise above the line reads as slack, and the licence draws on it, turning a surplus of virtue into a pass.

## Lever

A self regulated toward a level, not a maximum.
Because the moral self-image keeps a balance instead of chasing a peak, a rise above the line is a surplus to spend, not a gain to keep, so the banking does not save the good deed but readies it, and the drawing feels not like theft but like desert.

## Echo

A virtue turned into a warrant.
The good deed on the books, the self held at its level, and a pass issued against the surplus, permission drawn in the honest voice of a credit that was really earned.
