# Plot: The Finding

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The help desk](place_the_help_desk.md), turned the other way: for the first time the chain faces back toward itself.
A ticket has been filed. Something is going to be written down and sent upstream.

## Action

[Integration](process_integration.md): [The Witness](persona_the_witness.md), holding [the integrator's seat](position_integrator.md), reads the ticket, hears the story behind it, and writes what the format can carry back.

## Stage

[The Witness](persona_the_witness.md) raises [the finding](piece_the_finding.md): a fragment of the whole, formatted for a chain that files one symptom at a time.
Behind it, unsent, is [the traverse](piece_the_traverse.md): the accumulated whole that only the Body holds, the thing no finding has a field for.

## Tension

The finding goes back. The whole does not.
The single loop corrects the symptom and calls it done, so [the traverse](piece_the_traverse.md) reaches the place where the loop could finally close and closes only the smallest part of it. Read the fragments together and the chain would see the room. Nobody in the chain has ever read them together. So the loop looks closed ... and, as it always has, the decision runs anyway.
