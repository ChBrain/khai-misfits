# Process: Manufacturing

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The third station of the chain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An awarded design entering production.
Once the decision is locked and funded, building it is the only remaining move.

## Direction

From award to part.
It moves toward a physical object that matches the drawing within the tolerance band.

## Lever

The line meeting the tolerance.
The force is precise, repeatable production; the resistance is that the band was set before the room, so building it true builds the gap in too.

## Echo

A conformant product carrying the gap in its material.
It hands forward a part that passes every check the drawing defined, and the check the room would have written was never on the sheet.
