# Plot: Act

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Help Desk](place_the_help_desk.md), turned the other way: for the first time the chain faces back toward itself.
A ticket has been filed. Something is going to be written down and sent upstream.

## Action

[Optimisation](process_optimisation.md): [The Witness](persona_the_witness.md), holding [the service manager's desk](position_service_manager.md), reads the ticket, hears the story behind it, and writes what can be carried back.

## Stage

[The Witness](persona_the_witness.md) raises [the finding misfit](piece_the_finding_misfit.md): a fragment of the whole, formatted for a chain that files one symptom at a time.
Behind it, unsent, is [the misfits](piece_the_misfits.md): the accumulated whole that only the Body holds, the thing no finding has a field for.

## Tension

The finding goes back. The whole does not.
[The decision](piece_the_decision_travels.md) reaches the place where the loop could finally close: read the fragments together and the chain would see the room. Nobody in the chain has ever read them together. So the decision closes the loop ... or, as it always has, runs anyway.
