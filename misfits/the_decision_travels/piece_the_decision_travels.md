# Piece: The Decision Travels

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The thing that moves through every process without being named.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

It has no fixed place. It travels.
Every place in the chain, at once.

## Load Bearing

Without it the processes are independent events, each complete in itself.
With it they are one chain, and every decision made at a desk arrives, eventually, in a body.

## Apparent

Invisible.
It looks like normal operations. It is normal operations. That is the problem.

## Yearbook

It began at [R&D](process_rd.md) as a category.
It was funded at [procurement](process_procurement.md) as a budget.
It was built at [manufacturing](process_manufacturing.md) into a tolerance.
It was installed at [commissioning](process_commissioning.md) in a room.
It is running at [operation](process_operation.md) in a body.
It was named at [service management](process_service_management.md) as a ticket.
It was carried at [optimisation](process_optimisation.md) as a finding.
It has been running the whole time. Nobody commissioned it.
It runs until the loop closes. Or it runs anyway.
