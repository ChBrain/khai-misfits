## Place

## Load Bearing

Without it the room stops working and the other gaps become unliveable.
With it the room functions, which is exactly why no one upstream ever learns the room did not: the Body is handed the residue the design did not cover, absorbs it, and the deviation is quietly redefined as normal operation.

## Apparent

A person doing their job.
It looks like competence, because it is competence, a thousand small compensations performed so smoothly they have stopped feeling like compensation.
