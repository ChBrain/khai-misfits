# Position: Integrator

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The reversal seat: the one desk turned to face the whole chain, and given a channel to send it back.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The one view that reads the fragments together, and a route upstream for what it sees.
Where every other seat holds one station, this one holds the loop: the authority to make the chain run backward before the decision locks.

## Orders

Read the tickets as one whole, not one symptom at a time.
Carry the room's reality back to the desk that drew for a category, and measure the site before the tolerance is set.
Touch the governing variable upstream, not only the symptom downstream: close the loop, do not just log it.

## Loses

The clean, forward-only throughput of a chain that never reopens.
A seat that turns the chain around gives up the settled basis every downstream station inherited, and trades a fast, defensible order for a slow, contested one that keeps having to go back.

## Drives

Toward a chain that validates against the body, and away from one that only verifies against the spec.
Toward double-loop learning, at the cost of the ease the single loop bought, and toward the room, finally, having a desk.
