# Piece: The Traverse

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The phenomenon whole: the decision that crosses the chain, the room that never follows, and the loop that could close it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

[The room where it lives](place_the_room_where_it_lives.md), the one place the whole is ever assembled, and the only vantage the chain has never been inside.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is seven separate gaps, each defensible, and no account of why they are one thing.
With it there is a named whole: a correct decision travelling the entire chain while the Body who must live with it never travels back, the product wearing the shape of the communication structure that built it, so the gap is not any station's error but the sum of the validation no station owned. The Traverse is load bearing because it holds both the law and its one remedy: read the fragments together, run the chain in reverse and measure the room before the decision locks, and the accumulation stops accumulating. The order is the story.

## Apparent

A run of unrelated tickets.
It looks like ordinary friction, a scatter of small issues each closed on its own, because nothing in the chain has ever held them in one place long enough to see the shape they make.

## Yearbook

Assembled from [the specification](piece_the_specification.md) through [the finding](piece_the_finding.md), and carried alone by [the bearer](persona_the_bearer.md); named, at last, when [the witness](persona_the_witness.md) holds [the integrator's seat](position_integrator.md) and routes it back through [integration](process_integration.md).
It is the whole the chain files one fragment of at a time, and the loop the chain could close only by paying for the return the forward-only order was built to skip.
