# Piece: The Finding Misfit

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The gap carried part way back.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Between [the help desk](place_the_help_desk.md) and the rest of the chain, in transit upstream.

## Load Bearing

Without it nothing returns and the chain never learns anything.
With it a single fragment travels back, which lets the chain believe it has a feedback loop while the whole stays behind in the room.

## Apparent

A diligent service finding.
It looks like the loop closing, because part of the loop is closing, the part that fits the form that travels.

## Yearbook

Raised at the seventh station, formatted for a chain that files one symptom at a time.
It is the most hopeful misfit and the most deceptive: it carries enough back to look like learning, and not enough to change the first station.
