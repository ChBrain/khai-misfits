# Pitch: Procedural Calm

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Even, competent, unbothered.
Every desk is doing its job and doing it well.
The voice of a chain that has no reason to raise its voice.

## Undertow

Under the calm, a meter running.
Each correct decision adds a load nobody booked.
The register never breaks; the cost only accumulates.

## Nerve

The feeling of being told that everything is fine
by people who are not wrong,
while something you cannot name keeps not fitting.

## Echo

After the run: the quiet of a room
that holds the whole chain
and was never on anyone's agenda.
