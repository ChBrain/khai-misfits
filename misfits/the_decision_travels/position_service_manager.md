# Position: Service Manager

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The seat held by both the Advocate and the Witness.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The intake form, the queue, and the catalogue of categories.
Whoever holds this desk is the first in the chain whose work is to receive rather than to send.

## Orders

Capture the issue, classify it, route it.

## Loses

Whatever does not fit a field.
The seat can only forward what it can encode, so what it cannot keep is the part of the story with no category, the remainder the form drops on its way to becoming a case.

## Drives

Toward a filed, routable case.
The desk is satisfied when the symptom is logged and moving, and a single symptom can be logged and moving while the whole it was torn from stays in the room.
