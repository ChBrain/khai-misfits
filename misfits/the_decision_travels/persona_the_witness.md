# Persona: The Witness

## Taxonomy

[Service Manager](position_service_manager.md). The same desk as the Advocate, turned toward the chain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Unhurried. Listens longer than the call requires.
Files the symptom, and then keeps the line open for the story the symptom came wrapped in.

## Action

Hears what the Body actually carries and writes more of it down than the field asked for.
Holds the gap between the one symptom the system will accept and the whole the Body brought.

## Shadow

Can hold the gap but cannot send it.
The chain files one symptom at a time, so the Witness becomes the place the whole accumulates and stops, a custodian of everything that did not fit the ticket.

## Tell

Adds a note the form did not request.
Says "there is more to this" into a system with no field for more.
