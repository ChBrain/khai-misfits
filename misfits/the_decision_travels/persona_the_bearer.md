# Persona: The Bearer

## Taxonomy

[End User](position_end_user.md). The room the whole chain designed toward and never entered.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Someone doing their job.
The room is arranged in a way that works.
Don't ask why.

## Action

Absorb. Adapt. Continue.
When the threshold finally arrives, name the one thing that can be named, and file it.

## Shadow

Manages it. Survives. Finds the tolerance inside herself. Somehow.
The Bearer is doing six jobs. The chain commissioned one. She stopped being able to tell them apart the day the workarounds stopped feeling like workarounds.

## Tell

Reaches for the workaround before the problem finishes arriving.
Calls a daily act of compensation "just how it is" and means it.
