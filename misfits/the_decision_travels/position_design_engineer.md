# Position: Design Engineer

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The opening seat of the chain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The brief, the tools, and the authority to turn a category into geometry.
Whoever holds this desk decides what the thing will be.

## Orders

Design to the category. Trace every requirement, justify every tolerance, deliver a drawing that is complete.

## Loses

Contact with the person.
The seat only passes specifications downstream; it never receives a room back. What it cannot keep is the difference between a class and a body.

## Drives

Toward a correct, complete, buildable drawing.
The desk is satisfied when the geometry closes, and the geometry can close without anyone who lives with it ever being in the file.
