# Persona: The Courier

## Taxonomy

[Field Service Engineer](position_field_service_engineer.md). The last desk inside the chain to touch the room.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Efficient and finished.
Delivered, installed, commissioned, signed.
The ticket is green and the van is already moving to the next site.

## Action

Brings it to the room, sets it, proves it powers on, and closes the door.
Transfers custody to whoever is standing there and counts the job complete.

## Shadow

Completion means a closed door, not a working room.
The Courier is the only station that stands where the Body will stand, and stands there for an hour, with a checklist that has no line for fit.

## Tell

The click of the door is the deliverable.
"It is up and running" ends the visit, whether or not it is running for the person who has to live with it.
