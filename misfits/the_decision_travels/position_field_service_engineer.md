# Position: Field Service Engineer

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The last seat inside the chain to stand in the room.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The van, the checklist, and the authority to sign the job complete.
Whoever holds this desk is the chain's one hour of physical presence at the destination.

## Orders

Deliver, install, commission, prove power, and close the job.

## Loses

The room, the moment the door clicks.
The seat counts presence in minutes and completion in signatures; what it cannot keep is the room after the sign-off, because the chain only flows forward.

## Drives

Toward a completed, green ticket.
The desk is satisfied when the job is closed, and a job can be cleanly closed in a room where nothing yet fits the person who stays behind in it.
