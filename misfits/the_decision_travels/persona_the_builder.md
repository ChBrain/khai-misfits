# Persona: The Builder

## Taxonomy

[Production Manager](position_production_manager.md). The desk that turns the decision into a thing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Craft and pride.
Builds what they are given to build, and builds it well.
Hold the part to the light: it is exactly the part that was drawn.

## Action

Manufactures to the drawing, inside the tolerance, on time.
Measures the output against the specification and passes it down the line.

## Shadow

Nobody visits to see what it became.
The Builder measures against the drawing, never against the room, because the room is downstream of the loading dock and the chain only flows one way.

## Tell

Defends the work by pointing at the spec it met.
The question "does it fit" lands as the question "is it in tolerance", and the answer is always yes.
