# Position: End User

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The terminal seat: the room the chain designed toward and never entered.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The room, the product, and the whole day to live with both.
Whoever holds this desk holds the only place where the decision stops being a document and becomes a life.

## Orders

Keep going.

## Loses

The line between the job and the workaround.
The seat absorbs every gap the chain shipped, so what it cannot keep is the knowledge of how much of the day is the task and how much is compensating for the task.

## Drives

Toward a room that functions, by whatever compensation the room demands.
The desk is satisfied when the day is survived, and a day can be survived for years while the chain that produced it counts the deployment a success.
