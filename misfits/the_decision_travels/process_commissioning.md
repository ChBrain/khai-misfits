# Process: Commissioning

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The fourth station of the chain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A finished product ready to deploy.
Once it is built and shipped, installing it is the next inevitable step.

## Direction

From product to installed and signed.
It moves toward a working power-on and a closed job.

## Lever

An hour of field presence meeting a checklist with no line for fit.
The force is competent installation; the resistance is that the one station physically present in the room has no field in which fit could be recorded.

## Echo

Custody transferred and the door closed.
It hands the thing to the Body and counts itself complete, and from here the chain does not reopen.
