# Plot: Check

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Room Where It Lives](place_the_room_where_it_lives.md), and later [the help desk](place_the_help_desk.md).
The room the chain designed toward and has never been inside. Here the decision is not a document. It is a Tuesday.

## Action

[Operation](process_operation.md): [The Bearer](persona_the_bearer.md) at [the end user's desk](position_end_user.md) absorbs, adapts, compensates, and calls it normal.
Then [service management](process_service_management.md): [The Advocate](persona_the_advocate.md), holding [the service manager's desk](position_service_manager.md), is the first person in the chain with a form open to what the Body holds.

## Stage

[The Bearer](persona_the_bearer.md) has already built [the adaptation misfit](piece_the_adaptation_misfit.md): the workarounds that made the others liveable, now invisible even to her.
At the desk, [The Advocate](persona_the_advocate.md) and [The Witness](persona_the_witness.md) open [the ticket misfit](piece_the_ticket_misfit.md): one symptom, named small enough to file.
Two plans collide here: [Keep the Room Running](plan_keep_the_room_running.md), which the Body has been executing alone for years, and [Route the Case](plan_route_the_case.md), which can carry only what fits a field.

## Tension

The accumulated whole arrives, and only a fragment of it fits the form.
The Body can name one thing today. The chain receives one thing. Everything that did not fit the field stays in the room, still being carried, still uncounted.
