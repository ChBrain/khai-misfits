# Piece: The Misfits

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The accumulated whole that only the Body holds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

The Body.
The chain holds only fragments: [the specification misfit](piece_the_specification_misfit.md), [the budget misfit](piece_the_budget_misfit.md), [the tolerance misfit](piece_the_tolerance_misfit.md), [the installation misfit](piece_the_installation_misfit.md), [the adaptation misfit](piece_the_adaptation_misfit.md), [the ticket misfit](piece_the_ticket_misfit.md), [the finding misfit](piece_the_finding_misfit.md). None of those is The Misfits. The Misfits is what the Body holds that no single desk can see.

## Load Bearing

Without it the chain is a sequence of correct decisions that produced a successful deployment.
With it the chain is a sequence of correct decisions that produced a person who absorbs, adapts, compensates, endures, and calls it Tuesday.

## Apparent

Invisible to the chain.
It looks like a person doing their job. It is a person doing their job, and six other jobs the chain never commissioned and will never pay for.

## Yearbook

The chain held the fragments, one per station, filed separately, never read together.
The Body held all of it: before the ticket, through the ticket, after the ticket.
The Misfits is what you get when you read the fragments together. Nobody in the chain has ever done this. The Body always knew. It just never had a word for it.
