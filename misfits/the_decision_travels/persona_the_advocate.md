# Persona: The Advocate

## Taxonomy

[Service Manager](position_service_manager.md). The first desk in the chain with a form open to the Body.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Helpful, and genuinely so.
The form is open, the tone is warm, the intake is ready.
Tell me what is wrong and I will get it into the system.

## Action

Opens a record for what the Body brings.
Is the first person in the whole chain whose job is to receive, not to send.

## Shadow

The form has fields, and the Body holds more than fields.
The Advocate can capture what fits a category and must let the rest fall off the edge of the intake, not from indifference but from schema.

## Tell

Reaches, kindly, for the right thing to file it under.
Turns a life into a case so the case can move, and does not see what the turning leaves behind.
