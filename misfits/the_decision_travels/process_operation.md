# Process: Operation

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The fifth station of the chain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A deployed product meeting a body.
The moment custody passes, living with the thing begins and does not stop.

## Direction

From installed to lived with.
It moves toward a daily routine that makes the product usable in the room as it actually is.

## Lever

Human adaptability meeting the accumulated gaps.
The force is the Body's capacity to absorb and compensate; the resistance is that every prior station has left a gap for that capacity to close.

## Echo

A functioning room built on workarounds.
It hands forward nothing visible, because adaptation looks like normal operation, and that is exactly why it is never counted.
