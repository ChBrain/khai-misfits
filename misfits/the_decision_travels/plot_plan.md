# Plot: Plan

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Design Office](place_the_design_office.md), then [the meeting room](place_the_meeting_room.md).
Clean light, clean drawings. The room where the decision is made and the room where it is funded. The Body is in neither.

## Action

[R&D](process_rd.md) names a category and designs to it: [The Architect](persona_the_architect.md), holding [the design engineer's desk](position_design_engineer.md), draws for the category as though the category were a person.
Then [procurement](process_procurement.md): [The Gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md) at [the procurement manager's desk](position_procurement_manager.md) applies the criteria correctly and locks the tolerance before the room is measured.

## Stage

[The Architect](persona_the_architect.md) hands down [the specification misfit](piece_the_specification_misfit.md): correct, complete, addressed to no one who lives here.
[The Gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md) signs [the budget misfit](piece_the_budget_misfit.md): the decision, now official.
Each works a standing plan, [Close the Drawing](plan_close_the_drawing.md) and [Award and Lock](plan_award_and_lock.md): correct intents, executed in full, addressed to a room neither seat has seen.

## Tension

Nothing here is a mistake.
[The decision](piece_the_decision_travels.md) is already moving, and there is no step at which it could be stopped, because no step is wrong. The category was named as though it were a person, and the only person it will ever meet is not in the room.
