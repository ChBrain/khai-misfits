## Taxonomy

## Owner

## Place

[The room where it lives](place_the_room_where_it_lives.md), woven invisibly through the daily routine.

## Load Bearing

Without it the room stops working and the other six misfits become unliveable.
With it the room functions, which is exactly why no one upstream ever learns the room did not.

## Apparent

A person doing their job.
It looks like competence, because it is competence, a thousand small compensations performed so smoothly they have stopped feeling like compensation.

## Yearbook

Built at the fifth station by the Body, not by the chain.
It is the only misfit the chain did not make; it is the Body making the chain's product liveable at the Body's own unpaid expense.
