# Place: The Room Where It Lives

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The room the chain designed toward and has never been inside.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An ordinary room that works.
Everything in reach, everything arranged just so. Nothing about it announces that it is the end of a chain.

## Holds

The accumulated whole.
Every workaround the Body built, every compensation that became routine, the seven gaps now living together as one daily life.

## Offers

The only view of the chain from the inside.
Here, and nowhere upstream, the whole decision can be seen as the thing it became rather than the thing it was correctly designed to be.

## Withheld

Itself, from the chain.
No desk has ever been in this room, so the room cannot send its view upstream; it can only be entered, and the chain that built it never comes.
