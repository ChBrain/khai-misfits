# Process: Integration

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The sixth station, where the chain receives the Body and could finally turn back on itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A threshold crossed, a symptom finally nameable, arriving at a desk with a form open to it.
When the accumulated load produces one thing small enough to say, a case can begin, and behind the case, a chance to close the loop.

## Direction

From lived gap to filed case, and then, if anyone reads the cases together, from case back toward the chain.
It is the one station that can point upstream: toward a finding the earlier desks might act on, or a whole they might finally see.

## Lever

An intake that records one symptom at a time meeting a will to send the whole back.
The single loop files a fragment and closes; the double loop reads the fragments together and reaches the governing variable, and only the second turns the chain around.

## Echo

A fragment returned and the rest left in the room, or, rarely, the whole carried back and the decision remade.
It hands upstream either a closed ticket that changes nothing, or a room that finally has a desk, and the difference is whether the loop closes or the chain runs on as it always has.
