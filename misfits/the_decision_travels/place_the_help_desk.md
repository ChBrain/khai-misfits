# Place: The Help Desk

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). Where the Body arrived with something small enough to file.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An open form and a waiting queue.
The first door in the chain that opens inward, that exists to receive rather than to send.

## Holds

The categories, and the rule of one symptom at a time.
What arrives here is sorted into fields and turned into a case that the system can move.

## Offers

A record and a route.
Here a problem can finally be named to the chain, logged, classified, and sent somewhere.

## Withheld

A field for the whole.
The desk can accept one symptom but has no schema for the accumulated life behind it, so it offers a ticket and keeps, unasked, everything that did not fit the ticket.
