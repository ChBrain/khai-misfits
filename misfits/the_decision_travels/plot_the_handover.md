# Plot: The Handover

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The factory floor](place_the_factory_floor.md), then [the installation site](place_the_installation_site.md).
The locked decision arrives as a work order. Nobody on the floor met the desk that wrote it, and nobody here will meet the room it is going to.

## Action

[Manufacturing](process_manufacturing.md): [The Builder](persona_the_builder.md), holding [the production manager's desk](position_production_manager.md), builds it true to the drawing and builds it well.
Then [commissioning](process_commissioning.md): [The Courier](persona_the_courier.md) at [the field service engineer's desk](position_field_service_engineer.md) delivers, installs, signs off, and closes the door.

## Stage

[The Builder](persona_the_builder.md) sets [the stack-up](piece_the_stack_up.md) into the material: the gap is now physical, machined to spec.
[The Courier](persona_the_courier.md) leaves [the handover](piece_the_handover.md) in the room: custody transferred, job complete, verification passed and validation never run.
Both run their plans to the last target, [Build to Spec](plan_build_to_spec.md) and [Close the Job](plan_close_the_job.md): every box ticked, the gap shipped inside the success.

## Tension

The build is faultless and the handover is clean.
That is the problem: the chain considers itself finished at the closed door, and on the far side of that door [the traverse](piece_the_traverse.md) keeps running, now inside a room the chain will never reopen.
