# Process: Procurement

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The second station of the chain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A specification that needs sourcing and funding.
Once a design exists, the decision to buy and to budget it cannot be deferred.

## Direction

From specification to award.
It moves toward a single locked choice with a price attached.

## Lever

The criteria matrix meeting an unmeasured site.
The force is disciplined evaluation; the resistance is that fit was never a criterion and the room was never surveyed before the lock.

## Echo

A decision made official and a tolerance fixed early.
It hands forward an award the whole chain will treat as settled, and the moment to measure the room has already passed.
