# Position: Production Manager

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The seat that turns the decision into a thing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The drawing, the line, and the tolerance band.
Whoever holds this desk commands the means to make the part exactly as specified.

## Orders

Build to the drawing, inside tolerance, on schedule. Pass conformance and ship.

## Loses

Sight of the room downstream.
The seat receives a specification and emits a product; it never receives the room back, so what it cannot keep is any measure other than conformance.

## Drives

Toward a part that matches the drawing.
The desk is satisfied when the output is in tolerance, and a part can be perfectly in tolerance and still not fit the only room it was ever for.
