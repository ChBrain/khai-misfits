# Plan: Award and Lock

## Taxonomy

## Owner

## Direction

Select the compliant option at the best price and lock the commitment early, so the rest of the chain can proceed on a settled basis.
A defensible award, signed before anyone can reopen it.

## Orders

[The Gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md) scores every option against the matrix, awards the winner, and locks the tolerance and the budget at the moment of award.

[The meeting room](place_the_meeting_room.md); the [procurement](process_procurement.md) process; the criteria matrix, which has no column for fit.
