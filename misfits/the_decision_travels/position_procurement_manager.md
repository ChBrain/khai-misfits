# Position: Procurement Manager

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The seat where the decision becomes official.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The criteria matrix, the budget envelope, and the power to award.
Whoever holds this desk turns a recommendation into a commitment.

## Orders

Score every option against the criteria and award the compliant winner at the best price. Lock it.

## Loses

Any criterion that was never written into the matrix.
The seat cannot weigh what it cannot see, and fit was never a column, so fit is what it forfeits.

## Drives

Toward a defensible award, locked early.
The desk is satisfied when the decision is signed and can be justified to anyone, which it can, because every step it took was correct.
