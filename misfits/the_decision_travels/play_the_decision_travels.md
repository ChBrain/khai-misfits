# Play: The Decision Travels

## Estate

[khai-misfits](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-misfits): the house that holds this production and answers for the whole run.

## Name

The Decision Travels.
One decision, made correctly at a desk, crossing every station of a product chain ... and the Body at the end of it, who never gets to follow.

## Arc

The bend is this: the chain is never wrong, and the gap arrives anyway.
A category is named in the design office as though it were a person. The award locks the tolerance before the room is measured. The line builds it true. The door closes on the install. The Body absorbs what no station shipped, files the one symptom small enough to name, and a finding carries a fragment back while the whole stays in the room.
Every station verifies against the spec it was handed, correctly; validating against the actual body is nobody's station, so a chain of locally correct steps yields a globally wrong result, and the gap is the sum of the validation no one owned.
The one move that closes it is a reversal: read the fragments together, run the chain backward, and measure the room before the decision locks. It works, and it costs the clean forward-only throughput that made the chain feel finished. Reorder the stations and the accumulation stops accumulating: the order is the story.

## Triggers

**[The Specification](plot_the_specification.md)**
The decision is made: the category specified, the tolerance awarded and locked. Closes when the spec and the budget are fixed and handed down, correct and addressed to no one who lives here.

**[The Handover](plot_the_handover.md)**
Cue: the locked decision reaches the floor. It is built true, commissioned into the room, and signed off. Closes when the door shuts and custody passes to the Body, verification done and validation never run.

**[The Adaptation](plot_the_adaptation.md)**
Cue: the Body is living with it. The accumulated gap becomes daily routine, and meets the first form open to it. Closes when one symptom is filed as a ticket and the rest stays in the room.

**[The Finding](plot_the_finding.md)**
Cue: the ticket reaches the desk that faces back up the chain. A finding carries a fragment upstream. Closes when the single loop corrects the symptom ... and the whole runs on anyway.

**[The Return Path](plot_the_return_path.md)**
Cue: someone reads the fragments together instead of one at a time. The room's reality is carried back to a station that can still change the drawing. Closes when the loop closes and the chain runs in reverse ... or the return path is judged too slow, and the forward-only chain resumes.

## Stakes

Whether the Body ever receives a correct chain, or only ever a correct decision.
Every plot but the last moves the same direction: not toward repair, toward visibility, each station verifying and none validating. The production cannot make a forward-only chain catch a gap that is no station's job to catch. It can only make the chain visible from the room it never entered, and show that the one thing that would have closed it, a desk that reads the whole and carries it back, is the throughput the chain was built to skip.
