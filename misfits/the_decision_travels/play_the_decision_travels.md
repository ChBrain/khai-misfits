# Play: The Decision Travels

## Estate

[khai-misfits](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-misfits): the house that holds this production and answers for the whole run.

## Name

The Decision Travels.
One decision, made correctly at a desk, crossing every station of a product chain ... and the Body at the end of it, who never gets to follow.

## Arc

The bend is this: the chain is never wrong, and the gap arrives anyway.
A category is named in the design office as though it were a person. The budget locks the tolerance before the room is measured. The line builds it true. The door closes on the install. The Body absorbs what no station shipped, files the one symptom small enough to name, and a finding carries a fragment back while the whole stays in the room.
Reorder the stations and the accumulation stops accumulating: the order is the story.

## Triggers

**[Plan](plot_plan.md)**
The decision is made: the category specified, the budget set. Closes when the spec and the budget are locked and handed down.

**[Do](plot_do.md)**
Cue: the locked decision reaches the floor. It is built to tolerance and commissioned into the room. Closes when the door shuts and custody passes to the Body.

**[Check](plot_check.md)**
Cue: the Body is living with it. The accumulated gap meets the first open form. Closes when one symptom is filed as a ticket.

**[Act](plot_act.md)**
Cue: the ticket reaches service management. A finding carries a fragment back toward the chain. Closes when the loop closes ... or runs anyway.

## Stakes

Whether the Body ever receives a correct chain.
Every plot moves it the same direction: not toward repair, toward visibility. The production cannot close the gap. It can only make the chain visible from the room it never entered.
