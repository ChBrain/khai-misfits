# Process: Service Management

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The sixth station of the chain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A threshold crossed, a symptom finally nameable.
When the accumulated load produces one thing small enough to say, a case can begin.

## Direction

From lived gap to filed case.
It moves toward an intake record the system can classify and route.

## Lever

An open form meeting a whole that exceeds its fields.
The force is a desk that finally listens; the resistance is that the form has fields and the Body has more than fields.

## Echo

A ticket: one symptom captured, the rest retained.
It hands forward a single nameable issue and keeps, unsent, the whole it was torn from.
