# Persona: The Architect

## Taxonomy

[Design Engineer](position_design_engineer.md). The first desk the decision crosses.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Rigorous. The drawing is complete and the drawing is correct.
Every requirement traced, every tolerance justified.
A clean sheet for a clean problem.

## Action

Designs to the category.
Takes the named class of user and renders it into geometry, as though the category were a person who could stand in the room and be measured.

## Shadow

The category is not a person.
It is an average with the edges removed, and the edges are where people live. The Architect has never been told this, because from the design office it is not visible.

## Tell

Says "the user" and means a line in a specification.
When pressed on who that is, reaches for the requirement, not the room.
