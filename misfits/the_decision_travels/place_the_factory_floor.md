# Place: The Factory Floor

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). Where it was built to the specification.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The line, the jig, the part coming off true.
Measured, gauged, conformant. A place that knows exactly whether it did its job.

## Holds

The tolerance, now in the material.
The decision stops being a number and becomes a physical fact here, machined into the thing where it can no longer be argued with.

## Offers

A faultless build.
Here the drawing becomes a product that passes every check the drawing defined.

## Withheld

The room downstream.
Nobody on this floor visits what the part becomes once it leaves the dock, so the floor cannot tell you whether a perfect part is the right part.
