# Persona: The Gatekeeper

## Taxonomy

[Procurement Manager](position_procurement_manager.md). The desk that makes the decision official.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fair, consistent, defensible.
Applies the same criteria to every option and can show its work to anyone who asks.
Nothing personal; only the matrix.

## Action

Scores against the criteria and selects the winner.
Locks the tolerance and the budget at the moment of award, before the installation site has been measured.

## Shadow

The criteria did not include fit.
They could not, because fit lives in a room no column on the matrix can see. A correct selection against an incomplete matrix is still a correct selection.

## Tell

"It meets the requirements" closes every question.
Treats the absence of a criterion as the absence of a problem.
