# The Decision Travels: Reference

The research warrant for [The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The
chain the play stages is an engineering and systems vocabulary; the mechanics the
play runs on are grounded in the verification-and-validation, human-factors, and
organisational-learning literature cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging the travelling decision as a **systemic system**: a chain in which every
station builds correctly to the specification handed to it, and the gap arrives
anyway because no station is the one that checks the product against the body that
must live with it. The play models the documented mechanics, the verification and
validation split, designing to an averaged category, the human left to absorb what
automation did not cover, the normalisation of a workaround, the intake that only
records what its form can hold, and single-loop learning, as the load-bearing
vectors of the structure, not as a tale of incompetence. The governing finding of
the source literature is the play's governing law: every station verifies, no
station validates, so a chain of locally correct steps yields a globally wrong
result and the gap is the sum of the validation nobody owned.

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses
  to cast any station as wrong, because the source finding is precisely that the chain
  is competent: each seat verifies against the spec it was handed, correctly. The
  misfit is that validating against the actual body is nobody's station.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into rescue. No seat heroically
  catches the gap, because catching it is no seat's job; the finding carries a fragment
  back and the whole stays in the room. The production cannot close the gap, only make
  the chain visible from the room it never entered.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and
  attributed here, never claimed as the house's. The staging is the house's; the
  findings are theirs.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **[The Specification Misfit](piece_the_specification_misfit.md)**: carries design to
  the mean (Daniels and Rose) and the idealised user (Norman), the category named as
  though it were a person so the design fits an average nobody occupies.
- **[The Budget Misfit](piece_the_budget_misfit.md)**: carries the cost-of-change curve
  (Boehm), the tolerance locked before the room is measured, so the correction that
  would be cheap now is the one deferred until it is dear, which is to say never.
- **[The Tolerance Misfit](piece_the_tolerance_misfit.md)**: carries tolerance stack-up,
  the gap machined into the material as an accumulation of individually allowable
  deviations, each defensible on its own.
- **[The Installation Misfit](piece_the_installation_misfit.md)**: carries the
  verification and validation split (Boehm), the door closing on a commissioning that
  verifies against the spec and passes, while validation against the room never runs
  and custody flows forward only.
- **[The Adaptation Misfit](piece_the_adaptation_misfit.md)**: carries the ironies of
  automation (Bainbridge) and normalisation of deviance (Vaughan), the Body absorbing
  the residue until the workaround becomes routine and the deviation reads as normal
  operation.
- **[The Ticket Misfit](piece_the_ticket_misfit.md)**: carries legibility (Scott), the
  intake recording the one symptom small enough to fit its fields while the rest falls
  off the edge of the form, illegible rather than denied.
- **[The Finding Misfit](piece_the_finding_misfit.md)**: carries single-loop learning
  (Argyris and Schön), the fragment returned as a correction to the symptom and never
  to the governing variable at the first station, so it looks like a closed loop and
  changes nothing upstream.
- **[The Decision Travels](piece_the_decision_travels.md)** and
  **[The Misfits](piece_the_misfits.md)**: carry Conway's Law, the product wearing the
  shape of the chain that built it, the whole held by nobody because it lives only in
  the room at the end.
