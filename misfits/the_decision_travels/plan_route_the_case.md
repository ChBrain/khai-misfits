# Plan: Route the Case

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the service manager's seat](position_service_manager.md), inside [The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Service Manager](position_service_manager.md)

## Direction

Receive what the Body brings, classify it into a category, and route a case the system can move.
A logged, moving case is the seat's whole intent: capture the symptom, send it on.

## Orders

[The Advocate](persona_the_advocate.md) opens the intake and files the one symptom that fits a field; [The Witness](persona_the_witness.md) keeps the line open and holds the part of the story the form cannot carry.

## Implementation

[The help desk](place_the_help_desk.md); the [integration](process_integration.md) process; an intake form with fields, and a whole that exceeds them.

## Targets

- [ ] the Body's symptom is received
- [ ] the symptom is classified into a category
- [ ] the case is routed and moving
- [ ] the remainder, which has no field, is held off the record
