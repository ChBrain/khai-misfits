# Process: Optimisation

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The seventh station, where the chain could finally turn back on itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A filed ticket reaching review.
Once a case is logged, the work of learning from it can begin, if anyone reads it as more than a closure metric.

## Direction

From case back toward the chain.
It is the one station that points upstream: toward a finding the earlier desks might act on.

## Lever

A finding format meeting a whole with no field.
The force is the will to feed the lesson back; the resistance is that the format carries one fragment and the room is an accumulation.

## Echo

A fragment returned and the rest left in the room.
It hands a finding upstream and inherits the open question of whether the loop closes, or whether, as it always has, the decision runs anyway.
