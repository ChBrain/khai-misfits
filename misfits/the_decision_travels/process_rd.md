# Process: R&D

## Taxonomy

[The Decision Travels](play_the_decision_travels.md). The first station of the chain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A category identified as a need.
The moment a class of user is named, the work of designing for it becomes inevitable.

## Direction

From category to specification.
It moves one way: toward a complete, traceable definition of the thing to be built.

## Lever

Design competence meeting an absent room.
The force is real engineering skill; the resistance is that the room it designs for is never present to push back.

## Echo

A specification that names a class as though it named a person.
It hands the specification forward and inherits nothing back, because nothing flows upstream to correct it.
