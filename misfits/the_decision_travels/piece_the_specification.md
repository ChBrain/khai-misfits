## Load Bearing

Without it the category would have had to become a person before anything was built.
With it the chain can proceed treating a class as a body, and every later decision rests on that one substitution: the average named as though it were someone who could stand in the room and be measured.

## Apparent

It looks like good engineering, because it is good engineering, aimed at someone who is not there.
