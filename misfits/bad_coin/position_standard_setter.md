# Position: Standard-Setter

## Taxonomy

[Bad Coin](play_bad_coin.md). The seat that holds the bar and stamps every coin the same.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The bar, the height a coin must clear.
The stamp that says it passed.

## Orders

Pass or fail, nothing between.
Apply the line evenly to every coin.
Stamp the good and the barely good alike.

## Loses

The quality difference, ungraded above the line.
Two coins clear the bar: one true, one thin.
Both leave with the same mark.

## Drives

Toward an even standard, one line for all.
Away from paying for care, since care buys no better stamp.
The good coin priced at zero, and the misfit fed.
