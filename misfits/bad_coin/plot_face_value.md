# Plot: Face Value

## Taxonomy

[Bad Coin](play_bad_coin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The gate](place_the_gate.md), where every piece of work is passed or failed and never graded.
Two pieces come up together, a careful one and a quick one, and the bar has exactly one question for both: does it clear.

## Action

[Acceptance](process_acceptance.md): [The Mint](persona_the_mint.md), holding [the standard-setter's seat](position_standard_setter.md), stamps both pieces passed, at the same worth, because the bar reads pass or fail and cannot read how well.

## Stage

[The Mint](persona_the_mint.md) tables [Accept at Par](plan_accept_at_par.md): grade the work yes or no, so [the face value](piece_the_face_value.md) is equal for all that pass, whatever they cost to make.
[The good coin](piece_the_good_coin.md) and [the bad coin](piece_the_bad_coin.md) leave the gate worth exactly the same, one having cost a day and the other an hour.

## Tension

Nothing here is unfair, and it sets the whole slide in motion.
An even bar, applied to everyone alike, is the picture of a fair standard: no favourites, one line, pass or fail. But evenness is the trap, because a gate that treats all passing work as equal has quietly declared the careful maker's extra worth to be worth nothing, and priced quality at zero without ever deciding to, so from this stamp on, exceeding the bar is a gift no one will be thanked for.
