# Piece: The Gresham

## Taxonomy

[Bad Coin](play_bad_coin.md). The whole shape at once, and its costly cure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across [the gate](place_the_gate.md) and [the assay](place_the_assay.md), the bar that accepts at par and the price that grades again.
The phenomenon named: when unequal quality is accepted at equal value, the cheaper drives the dearer out of circulation, so a standard debases not through fraud but through the simple refusal to pay for the difference it can see.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a run of shortcuts with no thesis.
With it the play has its law: the decline needs no cheat and no slacker, only a bar that grades pass or fail, work that costs differently to pass it, and makers rational enough to spend the cheap and keep the dear. It is also, and this is the half the lament leaves out, curable, but only one way: put a price back on the quality the bar ignored, grade the work instead of passing it, pay a premium for care, and the good coin becomes worth spending again. So the Gresham carries its own remedy, which is not a higher bar but a graded one, and not more inspection but a reward for exceeding.

## Apparent

The market working, or the market failing, but nothing to do with a rule.
It looks like either healthy efficiency, everyone getting lean, or a mysterious rot in quality, when it is neither: it is the exact and predictable result of a specific rule, equal acceptance of unequal worth. That the cause is a grading rule and not a moral failing is the deepest cover, because everyone looks for a lazy maker to blame when the fault is in the stamp that valued them all the same.

## Yearbook

Broken, when it is broken, by [the assayer](persona_the_assayer.md) through [grading](process_grading.md), at the price of the speed and ease that passing at par supplied.
It is the piece all the others resolve into: [the face value](piece_the_face_value.md) equalising [the good coin](piece_the_good_coin.md) and [the bad coin](piece_the_bad_coin.md), the good sent to [the hoard](piece_the_hoard.md) and the careful lost to [the race](piece_the_race.md), make the Gresham, and only a price on quality unmakes it.
