# Piece: The Race

## Taxonomy

[Bad Coin](play_bad_coin.md). The competition under equal acceptance, where fast beats careful.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the queue](place_the_queue.md), in the count of pieces cleared per day and who cleared most.
The contest the bar sets up: two makers judged on throughput, both passing the same gate, one shipping three pieces to the other's one. Same acceptance, different speed, and speed is the only axis left that the gate can see.

## Load Bearing

Without it care and quickness could coexist, each valued in its lane.
With it they cannot, because the Race is what equal acceptance turns quality into: once the gate grades pass or fail, the remaining margin of competition is speed, and on speed the careful maker loses to the quick every time. So exceeding the bar is not merely unrewarded, it is a handicap, a self-imposed slowness in a race scored on volume, and the pressure that starts as the good makers being unpaid ends as the good makers falling behind.

## Apparent

Healthy productivity.
It looks like a shop getting faster, makers pushed to be lean and prolific, throughput climbing quarter on quarter. And rising output is a real thing, which is the cover, because the Race measures pieces cleared and not worth carried, so it reads a shop shedding its quality as a shop hitting its stride.

## Yearbook

Set by [the foreman](persona_the_foreman.md) at pace, and won by [the spender](persona_the_spender.md) over anyone who keeps to care.
It is what turns a private loss into a public rout: the Race makes [the good coin](piece_the_good_coin.md) not just unpaid but uncompetitive, so [the debasement](piece_the_debasement.md) spreads from choice to necessity, and the careful convert to survive.
