# Process: Hoarding

## Taxonomy

[Bad Coin](play_bad_coin.md). The good coin held back where care earns nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Care that earns no premium.
The extra given, the extra unpaid.

## Direction

The good withheld, or taken elsewhere.
Kept for a shelf where worth is seen, spent where it counts.

## Lever

A surplus worth keeping rather than giving away.
Value the maker will not surrender for nothing.

## Echo

The good coin drained from a place that will not pay for it.
What is left circulating is only the cheap.
