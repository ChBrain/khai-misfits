# Plot: Spend Bad

## Taxonomy

[Bad Coin](play_bad_coin.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The floor](place_the_floor.md), the work going out, the two coins worth the same to spend and costing differently to make.
Passing is all that is asked, so the only live question for a maker is how cheaply they can pass, and the count of pieces cleared is going up on the board.

## Action

[Circulation](process_circulation.md): [The Spender](persona_the_spender.md), holding [the spender's seat](position_spender.md), ships the quick version and clears the gate, while [The Foreman](persona_the_foreman.md) at [the pacer's seat](position_pacer.md) rewards the throughput.
[Hoarding](process_hoarding.md): [The Craftsman](persona_the_craftsman.md) at [the keeper's seat](position_keeper.md) does the piece right, earns no more for it, and keeps his best back.

## Stage

[The Spender](persona_the_spender.md) tables [Ship the Quick](plan_ship_the_quick.md): make exactly enough to pass and no more, so [the bad coin](piece_the_bad_coin.md) circulates cheaply and the board reads a fast, productive shop.
[The Craftsman](persona_the_craftsman.md) tables [Keep the Care](plan_keep_the_care.md): give the gate what it pays for and save the rest, so [the good coin](piece_the_good_coin.md) becomes [the hoard](piece_the_hoard.md), pulled from this circulation and kept for where it counts.

## Tension

Both makers are being rational, and between them the good leaves the shop.
The spender is lean and prolific, wasting nothing the standard did not ask, and the craftsman is not lazy but exact, spending only the care that is bought and holding the surplus a gate priced at zero. Each is responding correctly to the same true signal, that quality earns nothing extra here, and the sum of their two correct responses is [the race](piece_the_race.md) tilting to the quick and the careful quietly taking their best somewhere it is weighed.
