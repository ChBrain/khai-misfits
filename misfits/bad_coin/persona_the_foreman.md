# Persona: The Foreman

## Taxonomy

[Pacer](position_pacer.md). Pushes throughput, watches the count.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Driven. Practical. Eyes on the board.
Loves a line that moves and a number that climbs.
Delivery is the job, and delivery is happening.

## Action

Rewards the count of pieces cleared.
Pushes the pace, praises the volume.
Every cleared piece scores one, and one is one.

## Shadow

Scoring volume on an equal bar turns care into a handicap.
The careful fall behind, so the good become the losers on the board.
He is only reading the board... the board only counts.

## Tell

"Numbers are up, keep it moving."
The numbers are pieces, not worth.
