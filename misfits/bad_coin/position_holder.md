# Position: Holder

## Taxonomy

## Owner

## Has

The accumulated corpus, every coin that passed the gate.
No one to blame: each coin cleared the bar it was given.

## Orders

Make the body of work bear weight.
Build on what shipped, trust the stamps.
Lean the next thing against the last.

## Loses

The corpus was hollowed one passing at a time.

## Drives

Away from a corpus that carries the next load.
The bill in one lump, arriving after the counter is gone.
