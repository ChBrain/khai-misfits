# Position: Keeper

## Taxonomy

[Bad Coin](play_bad_coin.md). The seat that mints good coin and watches it earn nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The care, the extra hour past the line.
The surplus worth, real metal the gate never weighs.

## Orders

Do it right.
Mint the coin true, above what passing asks.
Give more than the bar demands.

## Loses

The reward for care, priced at zero at the gate.
The true coin and the thin one spend the same.
So he withholds the good, and keeps it out of trade.

## Drives

Toward quality, the coin worth more than its face.
Away from a gate that will not pay for it.
The good hoarded, and the bad left free to run.
