# Persona: The Craftsman

## Taxonomy

[Keeper](position_keeper.md). Does careful work, and hoards it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Exact. Proud of the work. Quietly stubborn about quality.
Knows the difference between passing and good.
Not loud about it, just certain.

## Action

Does the piece right when he does it.
Earns no more for right than for barely.
So he keeps his best back, and spends the rest.

## Shadow

Paying for care that earns nothing is a plain loss.
So he rationally withholds it, and the good leaves the room with him.
The gate never sees what walked out.

## Tell

"I will do it properly, but not for this."
Not for a gate that cannot see it.
