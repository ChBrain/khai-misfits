# Persona: The Heir

## Taxonomy

[Holder](position_holder.md). Inherits the debased corpus, whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Earnest. Unlucky. Handed a body of work that has to bear weight.
Trusts it, because all of it passed.
Ready to build on the pile he was given.

## Action

Opens the corpus and leans on it.
Finds it brittle through, part after part.
Looks for the one bad actor, and there is none.

## Shadow

Blamed for a ruin spread thin across years of passed work.
Every piece cleared the bar, so no piece is the crime... and the corpus still cannot hold.
Tempted to lower the bar again, just to match what he inherited.

## Tell

"How is all of this this bad?"
Because all of it merely passed.
