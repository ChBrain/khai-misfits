# Persona: The Spender

## Taxonomy

[Spender](position_spender.md). Ships quick, passes, moves on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Lean. Prolific. Wastes nothing the spec did not ask for.
Every hour goes where the gate can see it.
Reads the bar, hits the bar, done.

## Action

Makes exactly enough to clear the gate.
Then ships, then the next one.
The margin above the line is spend with no return, so there is none.

## Shadow

Meeting the bar at lowest cost is the rational play.
So he spends the cheap coin and holds the dear... and the cheap coin circulates.
Nobody chose to debase anything: it was just cheaper.

## Tell

"Good enough, next."
Good enough is the whole strategy.
