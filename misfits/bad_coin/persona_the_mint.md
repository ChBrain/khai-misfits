# Persona: The Mint

## Taxonomy

[Standard-Setter](position_standard_setter.md). Sets the pass/fail bar, one line, for everyone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fair-minded. Even-handed. One clean standard for all.
No favourites, no special cases, no argument about degrees.
Proud that the bar is the bar.

## Action

Grades each piece pass or fail.
Everything that clears the line gets the same stamp.
The stamp does not vary, so the work does not need to.

## Shadow

An even bar prices quality at zero without ever deciding to.
The careful earn exactly what the barely-adequate earn... nothing extra for the care.
No malice, just one honest line that cannot see above itself.

## Tell

"It either meets the bar or it does not."
And meeting it is all that counts.
