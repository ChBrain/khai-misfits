# Persona: The Assayer

## Taxonomy

[Grader](position_grader.md). The reversal: grades by degree, and pays for it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Discerning. Patient. Willing to judge degrees, not just sides.
Believes passing was never the interesting question.
Reads the work close enough to rank it.

## Action

Tests by degree, not by line.
Puts a price on care above the bar, so care earns.
Rewards the dear coin, so the dear coin comes back out.

## Shadow

Grading is slow, subjective, and dear.
It trades crisp throughput for a judgement anyone can contest... the clean pass/fail is gone.
The honest cost of paying for good is that good is now argued over.

## Tell

"Passing is not the question. How good is it?"
A slower, harder question.
