# Process: Grading

## Taxonomy

[Bad Coin](play_bad_coin.md). The reversal: weighing the work instead of waving it through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone weighing the work instead of passing it.
A hand that asks how much, not only whether.

## Direction

A price put back on quality above the bar.
The care that cleared the line now paid for the height it cleared it by.

## Lever

A graded standard that rewards care.
Degree measured, and the measure paid.

## Echo

The good coin back in circulation.
Bought at the cost of speed and ease, the even throughput given up.
