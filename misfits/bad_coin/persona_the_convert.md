# Persona: The Convert

## Taxonomy

[Converter](position_converter.md). Drops to quick to keep up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Once careful. Now tired of losing. Pragmatic about it.
Watched the good work score the same and take longer.
Made the sensible peace with the arithmetic.

## Action

Watches care lose the race, week after week.
Converts to the quick, like everyone ahead of him.
Meets the bar, moves on, stops reaching past it.

## Shadow

Surrendering to the arithmetic is rational, and hard to fault.
Each conversion sinks the standard one more step... his own good coin now spent as cheap.
He did not lower the bar. He just stopped standing above it.

## Tell

"You cannot hold the line on your own."
So he stops holding it.
