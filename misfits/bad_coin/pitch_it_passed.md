# Pitch: It Passed

## Taxonomy

[Bad Coin](play_bad_coin.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Brisk, practical, unbothered.
It cleared the bar, it shipped, it counts: done is done, and the next one is waiting, so why would anyone spend a day on what an hour will pass.
The voice of a shop that keeps things moving, that trusts its gate and its deadline, and treats every piece that gets through as exactly as good as every other.

## Undertow

Under the throughput, a standard quietly sinking.
Careful work and quick work count the same at the gate, but only one costs a day, so the day is the loss, and the maker who keeps paying it is a maker being punished for care.
No one is lazy: they ship what passes, because passing is all that is asked, and what passes gets cheaper every cycle as the good makers stop paying to exceed a bar that cannot see them exceed it.

## Nerve

The feeling of a shop that hums,
everything green, everything on time, everything through the gate,
and the careful hand at the back bench slowly deciding to stop being careful, or to take the care somewhere it counts.

## Echo

After the run: a body of work all of a cheap and even quality,
a bar still passing everything and a standard nobody remembers being higher,
and the good coin long gone, melted down and spent in a market that bothered to weigh it.
