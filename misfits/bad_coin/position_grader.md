# Position: Grader

## Taxonomy

[Bad Coin](play_bad_coin.md). The reversal seat: the one that weighs the coin instead of waving it through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The will to weigh, not just to pass.
A scale where the gate had only a bar.

## Orders

Grade the work, do not merely admit it.
Price the care, put metal back on the good coin.
Let the true coin buy more than the thin one.

## Loses

The crisp, even throughput of passing at par.
Grading is slower than stamping, and it sorts.
The smooth line breaks where the scale begins to read.

## Drives

Toward a graded standard, quality with a price again.
Away from equal acceptance, the flat bar that fed the bad.
The good coin back in trade, at a cost, and worth it.
