# Place: The Crosswalk

## Taxonomy

[The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md). The remedy place where the several readings are made explicit and walked across the boundary and reconciled, at the cost of the collaboration's momentum.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the readings are surfaced and reconciled, the workshop, the joint review, the boundary-spanning session where each community says what the object means in its own terms and the meanings are carried across.
The Translator having each Community make its reading explicit, the local senses laid side by side, and the difference between them walked across the boundary before the separate work is joined.

## Holds

The reconciliation, and the cost it exacts.
Here the readings are made explicit and walked across, so the Crosswalk holds the point where the divergence can be caught while the work is still separable: each community's reading surfaced and set beside the others, the meanings translated across the boundary and reconciled, so it is where the object's several senses are made one by labour, and where that labour is paid against a collaboration that is visibly moving and a shared thing everyone can point to, the remedy real and never free.

## Offers

The divergence caught early and the meanings reconciled.
Because the Crosswalk is where the readings are surfaced and walked across, it offers the gap closed while the work is separable, the meanings made one before the seam, so it hands out the safety of a reconciliation done in time, and hands it out only for the price of translation, the momentum of the shared object spent to surface what it hid, so what it offers it offers dearly.

## Withheld

Any way to make the reconciliation free, or to ask for it without doubting the object.
The Crosswalk can reconcile the meanings but cannot spare the cost, because surfacing each reading slows a collaboration that is plainly moving and doubts a thing everyone can point to, so it withholds the costless correction, letting the meanings be made one only by continuous translation across the boundary, and, all real remedy and no free one, it withholds the momentum the object seemed to grant, the labour paid or the gap left for the seam.
