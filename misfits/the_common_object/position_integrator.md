# Position: Integrator

## Taxonomy

[The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md). The seat that brings the communities' separate work together, where the divergence hidden under the shared object surfaces at the join and does not fit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Several bodies of separate work and a join to make: the pieces each community built from the object, and the expectation that they mesh because they came from one shared thing.
A reasonable expectation: the object coordinated every community, so the pieces should fit, and the join should be an assembly and not a reconciliation.
No way to see the divergence before the work is joined, and no sign, until the pieces meet, that the one object meant several things.

## Orders

Bring it together.
Take the separate bodies of work, fit the pieces, and assemble the coordinated whole the shared object was meant to produce.
Expect the mesh, because everything was built from the same object.

## Loses

The cheapness of the reconciliation, exactly because the divergence shows only at the join.
Because the object looked shared and every community worked from it, the seat meets the divergence only at the seam, where the finished pieces do not fit and reconciling the meanings now means unpicking finished work, so the seat that joins the work is the first to see the gap and the last who could have caught it cheap.
It gains the assembled whole it went to make, and loses the chance to have caught the divergence while the work was still separable.

## Drives

Toward the work joined and the divergence surfaced,
the pieces brought together and found not to mesh, the one object revealed to have meant several things,
so the seat that brings it together meets at the seam the divergence the shared object hid, no one having misused it.
