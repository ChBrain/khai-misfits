# Piece: The Reading

## Taxonomy

[The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md). Each community's own meaning of the object, the competent local sense supplied by its practice and taken for what the object means to all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Made on each [home ground](place_the_home_ground.md), where the object is completed into the local practice.
Surfaced, if ever, at [the crosswalk](place_the_crosswalk.md), where the several readings are laid side by side.

## Load Bearing

Without it the object is a token no one uses and there is nothing to diverge; the object is only usable once a community reads it into its own terms.
With it the trap has its several meanings: the reading each community makes is competent and specialised, the object transformed into the sense its own knowledge and practice supply, correct by its own lights and different from the reading across the boundary (Carlile, on knowledge across boundaries, meaning transformed as it crosses the pragmatic boundary between communities of practice). The Reading is load bearing because it is where the divergence lives: the local meaning each community takes the object to have, felt as the object's own and assumed shared, so the piece carries the private sense wearing the common face, one per community and no two the same, the several readings that look like one until the work is joined.

## Apparent

The obvious meaning of the object, plainly: what the artefact says, as any competent reader in the community can see.
Seen at the surface, the Reading is invisible as a reading, because each community has already made it and sees only the object's meaning, so the sense looks like the object's own and not the community's supply, and the truth that the object left its meaning open, that each read it into its own practice, that the meaning each feels shared is its own, stays hidden behind how plainly a competent reading reads.

## Yearbook

Made by each [community](persona_the_community.md) through [homing](process_homing.md), out of [the plasticity](piece_the_plasticity.md) of [the object](piece_the_object.md).
It is the several meanings of the trap: the Reading is the local sense each community supplies, hidden under [the semblance](piece_the_semblance.md) of coordination, and its difference from the other readings becomes [the seam](piece_the_seam.md) and the whole of [the divide](piece_the_divide.md).
