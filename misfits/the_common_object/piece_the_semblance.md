# Piece: The Semblance

## Taxonomy

[The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md). The apparent coordination the circulating object gives, the look of joined work that the shared thing produces while the meanings diverge under it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Felt across [the boundary](place_the_boundary.md), where the object passes cleanly and the groups look joined.
Dispelled, if ever, at [the crosswalk](place_the_crosswalk.md), where the readings surface and the coordination is found to have been on the object.

## Load Bearing

Without it the circulating object is just a shared token and no one mistakes it for agreement; the object could be shared without being trusted to have joined the meaning.
With it the trap has its substitution: the presence of the common object, passing cleanly between communities each working from it, taken for a shared understanding, the shared thing standing in for the conversation it was meant to open (Bowker and Star, on standards and classifications, the arrangements that let communities cooperate without agreeing and make the cooperation durable and invisible). The Semblance is load bearing because it is what stops the check: the look of coordination so real that to ask what the object means to each community reads as doubting a collaboration that is plainly moving, so the piece carries the appearance that lets the object substitute for the meaning, the coordination believed because the object is visibly common.

## Apparent

Coordination, plainly: a shared object, groups working from it, the collaboration moving as one.
Seen at the surface, the Semblance looks like exactly what it feels like, real joined work, and the truth that the coordination is on the object and never the meaning, that the shared thing has stood in for the shared understanding, that the collaboration everyone feels moving rests on a face each community reads its own way, stays hidden behind how real a working collaboration feels, right up until the seam.

## Yearbook

Given by the circulating [object](piece_the_object.md) through [circulating](process_circulating.md), felt by every community and the [maker](persona_the_maker.md).
It is the substitution of the trap: the Semblance is the presence of the common Object taken for agreement on [the reading](piece_the_reading.md), the coordination believed over several meanings, false the moment [the seam](piece_the_seam.md) shows [the divide](piece_the_divide.md).
