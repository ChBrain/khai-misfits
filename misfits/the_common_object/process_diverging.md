# Process: Diverging

## Taxonomy

[The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md). The readings parting while the object still looks shared, the several bodies of work growing apart under one artefact until the seam brings them together.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Several competent readings of one object, each built faithfully on its own ground.
At [the home ground](place_the_home_ground.md), each community working its own reading forward, apart from the others and out of their sight.

## Direction

Toward the readings parted and the object still looking shared.
Because each community builds its own reading faithfully and apart, diverging carries the several bodies of work in different directions, and because the object still circulates and looks common, nothing compares the readings, so the gap widens beneath a shared face, the divergence real and invisible until the separate work is carried to the join.

## Lever

The shared face of the one object.
Because the artefact stays recognisably one object in every set of hands, diverging runs not on any visible split but on the object's own robustness, the gap hidden by exactly the common identity that lets the object be shared, the divergence growing under a face that looks the same to all.

## Echo

The readings parted, and the parting hidden under the object.
The communities have built their several readings faithfully, so what looks like coordinated work is, in truth, divergent work under a common artefact, the gap unseen because the object looks shared, waiting for the seam to bring the pieces together and show they do not fit.
