# Position: Translator

## Taxonomy

[The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md). The seat that would make each community's reading explicit and walk the meanings across the boundary, where the remedy is real and paid for against a collaboration that is visibly moving.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A view of the divergence coming and a move that would catch it: the sight that the object means differently in each community, and the labour of surfacing the readings and reconciling them at the boundary.
A real remedy: making each community's reading explicit and walking the meanings across genuinely catches the divergence while the work is still separable.
No way to make the remedy free, and no way to ask for it that does not read as doubting a shared thing everyone can point to and slowing a collaboration that is plainly moving.

## Orders

Walk it across.
Have each community say what the object means in its own concrete terms, surface the local reading and not just the shared form, and reconcile the meanings at the boundary before the work is joined.
Pay the cost of translation, because the object spared the conversation and not the divergence.

## Loses

The collaboration's momentum, exactly because the remedy slows a process that is visibly working.
Because the object is visibly coordinating, the seat's question, what does this mean to each of you, reads as doubting a real shared thing, so the seat spends its standing to ask for a reconciliation that is cheapest now and looks least necessary now, and pays continuously or watches the gap go to the seam.
It gains the divergence caught while the work is separable, and loses the momentum the object seemed to grant.

## Drives

Toward the readings made explicit and the meanings walked across,
the local sense surfaced and reconciled at the boundary before the seam,
so the seat that would translate pays, continuously, the cost of the conversation the object was reached for to avoid.
