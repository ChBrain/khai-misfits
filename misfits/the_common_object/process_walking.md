# Process: Walking

## Taxonomy

[The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md). The remedy: the Translator surfacing each community's reading and walking the meanings across the boundary, the reconciliation that is real and paid for.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone who would reconcile the meanings before the work is joined.
At [the crosswalk](place_the_crosswalk.md), the Translator having each community make its reading of the object explicit and setting the readings side by side.

## Direction

Toward the readings surfaced and the meanings reconciled across the boundary.
Because the object hides its several senses under one face, walking makes each community's reading explicit and carries the difference across the boundary, so the meanings are set beside each other and made one while the work is still separable, the reconciliation done at the boundary rather than left for the seam, and done by labour rather than by the object alone.

## Lever

The explicit reading laid across the boundary.
Because the divergence lives in the local readings that the object leaves tacit, walking runs on making those readings explicit and translating them across, the reconciliation achieved not by a better object but by the conversation the object spared, the meanings walked across only by paying the cost of the translation the shared artefact was reached for to avoid.

## Echo

The readings surfaced, and the meanings made one at a price.
The Translator has walked the local senses across the boundary, so what is reconciled is reconciled while the work is separable, but only for the cost of slowing a collaboration that is visibly moving and doubting a thing everyone can point to, the remedy real and never free, paid continuously or the gap left for the seam.
