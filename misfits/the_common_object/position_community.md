# Position: Community

## Taxonomy

[The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md). The seat that reads the shared object into its own practice, where the competent local reading is taken for the common meaning and believed shared.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A shared object to work from and a practice to read it in: the artefact received and the knowledge nearest its own work that completes it.
A competent reading: the object read into the community's own terms, a specialised and correct resolution of what the artefact means here.
No way to use the object without reading it into its own practice, and no sign that the competent local reading is one of several rather than the object's meaning.

## Orders

Read it home.
Take the shared artefact, complete it from the practice closest to your work, and work from that reading as what the object plainly means.
Read it the way your world reads, because that is the reading nearest to hand and the one your practice supplies.

## Loses

The chance of catching that its reading is only a reading, exactly because the reading feels like the object.
Because the meaning its practice supplies feels like what the object simply is, the seat cannot see it as one of several, so takes it for the common meaning and builds it faithfully, one of several communities each working a different reading, and loses the divergence to its own competence until the work is joined.
It gains a usable meaning to build on, and loses the sight that the community across the boundary built another.

## Drives

Toward the object read home and the reading taken for the meaning,
the local reading felt as the object's plain sense and believed shared,
so the seat that reads the object into its own practice builds its own reading faithfully, sure to the seam that the other communities built the same.
