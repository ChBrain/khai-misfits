# Piece: The Seam

## Taxonomy

[The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md). Where the divergence surfaces, the join at which the communities' separate work is brought together and does not fit, revealing the object meant several things.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Reached at the join, where the several [home ground](place_the_home_ground.md) bodies of work are brought together.
Forestalled, if ever, at [the crosswalk](place_the_crosswalk.md), where the readings are reconciled before the work is joined.

## Load Bearing

Without it the divergence never shows and the several readings stay comfortably apart, each community sure the work is joined; it is the join that makes the gap visible.
With it the trap has its reckoning: the point where the separate bodies of work, each built faithfully from one shared object, are fitted together and do not mesh, the interfaces mismatched and the assumptions collided, the object revealed to have named a different thing in each community (the divergence surfacing only at integration, when the joined work shows the meanings were never one). The Seam is load bearing because it is where the hidden divergence finally becomes real and dear: not a difference noticed early but a misfit found in finished work, so the piece carries the moment the shared object's several meanings meet, and carries it too late to be cheap.

## Apparent

A surprise, plainly: pieces that should mesh and do not, an integration that should be assembly and turns out to be reconciliation.
Seen at the surface, the Seam looks like a sudden fault at the join, a mismatch come from nowhere, and the truth that the divergence was there all along, grown quietly on the home grounds under one shared face, invisible only because the object looked common and the work was done apart, stays hidden behind how sudden the misfit at the join appears.

## Yearbook

Reached by the [integrator](persona_the_integrator.md) through [diverging](process_diverging.md), where the several [readings](piece_the_reading.md) meet.
It is the reckoning of the trap: the Seam is where [the semblance](piece_the_semblance.md) breaks and the divergence under [the object](piece_the_object.md) shows, the misfit that reveals [the divide](piece_the_divide.md), forestalled only if [walking](process_walking.md) reconciled the meanings first.
