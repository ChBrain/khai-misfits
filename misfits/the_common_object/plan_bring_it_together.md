# Plan: Bring It Together

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the integrator's seat](position_integrator.md), inside [The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Integrator](position_integrator.md)

## Direction

Take the communities' separate work and fit the pieces into the coordinated whole the shared object was meant to produce. Expect the mesh, because everything was built from the same object, and assemble rather than reconcile, until the pieces meet and do not fit.

## Orders

([The Integrator](persona_the_integrator.md) brings the separate bodies of work together and fits them, and is the first to see, at the join, the divergence the shared object hid.)

## Implementation

([The Home Ground](place_the_home_ground.md) grounds converging at the join; the [diverging](process_diverging.md) process surfacing; the several [readings](piece_the_reading.md) meet at [the seam](piece_the_seam.md), revealing [the divide](piece_the_divide.md) beneath [the semblance](piece_the_semblance.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the communities' separate bodies of work are brought together at the join
- [ ] the pieces are expected to mesh because they came from one shared object
- [ ] the pieces are found not to fit, the interfaces and assumptions colliding
- [ ] the divergence surfaces at the seam, the one object revealed to have meant several things
