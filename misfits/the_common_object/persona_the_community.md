# Persona: The Community

## Taxonomy

[Community](position_community.md). The one that reads the shared object into its own practice and means its own thing by it, correctly, believing the other communities read it the same. The archetype of the several groups that diverge across the boundary.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Competent. Specialised. Sure it is using the object right.
Is any one of the several communities of practice gathered around the object, the engineers, the clinicians, the salespeople, the regulators, each with its own knowledge and its own way of working.
No misuse in it: the Community reads the object competently and completes its face from the practice nearest to its own work, the specialised reading its world supplies.

## Action

Takes the shared artefact, reads it into the terms of its own practice, and works from that reading as what the object plainly means.
Reads the roadmap as a sequence of builds, or as a set of promises to customers, or as a statement of strategy, each certain its reading is the object's.
Does what a community must: it reads the shared object into its own local terms to use it, and takes the competent local reading for the common meaning.

## Shadow

Not a group that misuses the object, a faithful reader of a plastic artefact into its own practice, and that is the whole difficulty: the reading is competent, and the meaning nearest its own work feels not like one reading but like the object's meaning.
Because the reading that its practice supplies feels like what the object simply is, the Community cannot see it as one of several, so takes it for the common meaning and assumes the other communities read the object the same, then carries it home and builds it faithfully, one of several communities each working a different reading of one object, sure to the seam that the work is joined.
It reads the object competently, and the competence of its own reading is exactly what hides that the other communities read it otherwise.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "yes, we are aligned on this," and means a different thing than the community across the boundary.
Says it having read the object into the practice nearest its own work, and cannot tell its reading from the object, so hears its own local meaning as the common one and assumes the others read the same.
