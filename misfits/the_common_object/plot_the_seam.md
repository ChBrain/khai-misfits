# Plot: The Seam

## Taxonomy

[The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

The join, each community having carried the object home and built its own reading faithfully.
The Integrator bringing the separate bodies of work together and expecting them to mesh, because they came from one shared object.

## Action

[Diverging](process_diverging.md): [The Integrator](persona_the_integrator.md), holding [the integrator's seat](position_integrator.md), fits the pieces and finds they do not mesh, meeting the divergence the object hid.

## Stage

At [the seam](piece_the_seam.md), the [integrator](persona_the_integrator.md), on [Bring It Together](plan_bring_it_together.md), fits the several bodies of work built from one [object](piece_the_object.md) and finds the interfaces mismatched and the assumptions collided.
The several [readings](piece_the_reading.md) meet, [the semblance](piece_the_semblance.md) breaks, and the one object is revealed to have meant a different thing in every community.

## Tension

The divergence was invisible until the work was joined, and the join is what reveals it, too late to be cheap.
There is nothing to fault: the join was competent and the pieces were built faithfully. But the object looked shared and the work was done apart, so the plot closes with the misfit surfacing at the seam, the shared object's several meanings meeting in finished work, no one having misused it.
