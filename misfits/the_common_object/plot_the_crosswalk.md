# Plot: The Crosswalk

## Taxonomy

[The Common Object](play_the_common_object.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

Someone would reconcile the readings before the work is joined.
The Translator seeing the divergence coming and moving to catch it while the work is still separable, against a collaboration that is visibly moving.

## Action

[Walking](process_walking.md): [The Translator](persona_the_translator.md), holding [the translator's seat](position_translator.md), has each community make its reading explicit and walks the meanings across the boundary.

## Stage

At [the crosswalk](place_the_crosswalk.md), the [translator](persona_the_translator.md), on [Walk It Across](plan_walk_it_across.md), surfaces each community's [reading](piece_the_reading.md) of [the object](piece_the_object.md) and sets the local senses side by side, reconciling the meanings before [the seam](piece_the_seam.md).
The divergence is caught while the work is separable, and the reconciliation is paid for by slowing the collaboration and doubting a thing everyone can point to.

## Tension

Reconciling the meanings early is real and never free, and the cost is what keeps the check from being made.
There is nothing costless here: the remedy genuinely catches the divergence, but only by spending the momentum the object seemed to grant. So the plot closes either with the meanings walked across and the gap caught in time ... or the translation waived as slowing a working collaboration, the circulation taken for coordination, and the divide left for the seam.
