# Piece: The Dampening

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). The travelling mechanism: both signals losing amplitude through the same layer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across the middle, in both directions at once.
The change coming down and the truth going up, each entering the layer at full strength and leaving it a little weaker, level by level.

## Load Bearing

Without it the middle is just a relay and the play is a clean transmission.
With it the middle is a filter: the Dampening is the single mechanism seen from both sides, [the dilution](piece_the_dilution.md) going down and [the smoothing](piece_the_smoothing.md) coming up, the same reflex, protect delivery, applied to whichever signal is passing. It is a low-pass layer in an organisation, and what it passes are the low-amplitude signals, the small and safe and agreeable, while the large ones, the bold change and the hard truth, are exactly the ones it attenuates to nothing. The middle transmits perfectly, as long as nothing important is being said.

## Apparent

A functioning chain of command.
It looks like information flowing as it should: directives down, reports up, the hierarchy doing its work. And it is, for everything that does not matter. The failure is invisible because it is selective: only the signals worth sending are the ones that do not arrive.

## Yearbook

Run by every layer of the middle, in good faith, in both directions.
It is why [the mandate](piece_the_mandate.md) becomes [the husk](piece_the_husk.md) and the floor's alarm becomes [the on-track report](piece_the_on_track_report.md): one reflex, faithfully applied at every level, that keeps the two ends of the organisation in perfect agreement about a change that only one of them can see and neither of them feels.
