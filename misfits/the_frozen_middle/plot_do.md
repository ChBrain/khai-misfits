# Plot: Do

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The cascade](place_the_cascade.md), the change on its way down.
The safe plan is handed layer to layer, and at [the operating review](place_the_operating_review.md) each manager is answering for delivery in the same week they are asked to disrupt it.

## Action

[Cascade](process_cascade.md): the change passes down, softened a notch at each hand-off to protect the numbers.
[Buffering](process_buffering.md): [The Buffer](persona_the_buffer.md), holding [the manager's seat](position_manager.md), shields its team from the thrash and its delivery from the disruption, and [The Hand](persona_the_hand.md) at [the frontline's seat](position_frontline.md) waits on [the shop floor](place_the_shop_floor.md) to see what actually arrives.

## Stage

[The Buffer](persona_the_buffer.md) tables [Protect Delivery](plan_protect_delivery.md): hold the quarter, delay and dilute the rest, and [the dilution](piece_the_dilution.md) files the change down another notch.
[The Hand](persona_the_hand.md) tables [Do the Work](plan_do_the_work.md): take on whatever comes, and what comes is [the husk](piece_the_husk.md), the change with its disruption and its point removed.

## Tension

Nothing is refused.
Each layer passes the change on, agreeing, and each takes one more notch off to survive its own review. The frontline is not resisting: there is nothing left to resist, so it complies fully with a version that asks nothing of it, and its faithful adoption of an empty shape is entered, upward, as proof the change is landing.
