# Pitch: Embracing Change

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Aligned, positive, on message.
Every layer says the right words about the change and means them.
The voice of an organisation that is transforming, spoken by people who are each, genuinely, on board.

## Undertow

Under the alignment, a layer that softens.
Each hand-off makes the bold thing a little safer and the hard thing a little finer, and no single softening is wrong.
The message never breaks: the change goes down and the truth comes up, and both arrive as agreement.

## Nerve

The feeling of a transformation everyone supports,
announced with conviction, reported as landing,
that the people doing the actual work have somehow never felt.

## Echo

After the run: a strategy declared embedded and visible nowhere,
a frontline still doing last year's job with a new name on it,
and a reorg to fix the middle that becomes the next middle.
