# Persona: The Successor

## Taxonomy

[Incoming Executive](position_incoming_executive.md). Fresh eyes, same chart, new middle.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fresh. Unsentimental.
Sees the whole at once.
No attachment to how it got here.

## Action

Stands at top and bottom together, sees they never touched.
Names the frozen layer out loud.
Orders a reorg.

## Shadow

Not a fixer, a relocator: it names the frozen layer and answers with a new chart over the same incentive, so the ice melts here and forms there. The reckoning that mistakes the layer for the cause. The diagnosis is sharp and honest; the remedy redraws the box and keeps the pressure that filled it.

## Tell

Says "we need to flatten this."
Draws the next middle.
