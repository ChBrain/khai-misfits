# Plot: Reorg

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The reorg](place_the_reorg.md), months on, the change plainly nowhere, or a new executive in the chair.
The gap is finally visible from the top: a transformation declared embedded and a frontline that never felt it. The instinct is to fix the layer that swallowed it.

## Action

[Restructure](process_restructure.md): [The Successor](persona_the_successor.md), holding [the incoming executive's seat](position_incoming_executive.md), stands at the top and the bottom at once, sees they never touched, and bypasses the middle with a new structure.

## Stage

[The Successor](persona_the_successor.md) tables [Reorganise](plan_reorganise.md): dissolve the frozen layer and draw a flatter chart.
[The dampening](piece_the_dampening.md) is named at last, the one reflex that turned [the mandate](piece_the_mandate.md) into a husk and the floor's alarm into good news, and [the frozen middle](piece_the_frozen_middle.md) stands revealed: a layer of ice made of nobody's malice and everybody's delivery.

## Tension

Nobody froze it.
Each manager only protected the numbers it was measured on, and the sum was a layer that let nothing large through. The reorg is not insight fixing the cause, it is a new chart drawn over the same incentive: the boxes move, the reporting lines change, and the next set of managers is handed the same continuity trap ... so the ice melts here and begins, quietly, to form there.
