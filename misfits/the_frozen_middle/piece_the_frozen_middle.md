# Piece: The Frozen Middle

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). The accumulated whole: the layer where change and truth both go to be made safe.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

The gap between the two ends.
At the top, a change believed in and reported as done. At the bottom, a job unchanged and a truth unheard. The Frozen Middle is the layer standing between them, agreeing with both.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a run of sensible hand-offs and one disappointing outcome.
With it those are one object: an organisation whose top and bottom never reach each other, because the layer built to connect them is measured on keeping things running, and keeping things running means letting nothing large through. Held whole by nobody, because no manager froze the middle, each only protected its own delivery, and the sum of every local protection is a global layer of ice, thick exactly where the important signals try to cross.

## Apparent

A middle doing its job.
From every management seat the layer is working: directives received and actioned, reports compiled and sent, delivery held steady through a period of change. The trap is invisible from within because the buffering that dampens the change is the same buffering that keeps the company alive, and no seat can see where the one becomes the other.

## Yearbook

The layer built the ice in good faith: [the dilution](piece_the_dilution.md) going down, [the smoothing](piece_the_smoothing.md) coming up, [the continuity trap](piece_the_continuity_trap.md) making both rational.
[The successor](persona_the_successor.md) was the first to stand at the top and the bottom at once and see that they had never touched, and answered it with a reorg, which dissolved this middle and set the next one freezing. Nobody had a name for the layer, because from inside it was only everyone doing their jobs.
