# Piece: The Smoothing

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). The frontline's hard truth softened a notch at each layer on the way up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Along [the roll-up](place_the_roll_up.md), each report a little calmer than the one beneath it.
The floor's this is not working becoming a manageable teething issue, becoming on track with minor risks, becoming green.

## Load Bearing

Without it the top would hear the change had not landed and could correct.
With it the top hears the opposite, because each layer, passing the news up, rounds off the alarm that would reflect on its own delivery, and every rounding is small and defensible: it is early, the team is adjusting, we are on it. The Smoothing is the upward twin of the dilution, attenuation by reassurance, and it runs one way, because a manager will file down bad news that indicts them and never file down good news that flatters them.

## Apparent

Composure, and command.
It looks like managers on top of their patch: no drama, issues handled, a steady hand. Composure is a virtue. This composure is manufactured by subtraction, one worrying detail removed at each level.

## Yearbook

Performed by [the filter](persona_the_filter.md), and by every layer above it.
It is the upward half of [the dampening](piece_the_dampening.md): the floor's reality loses amplitude in steps just as the change did, so the signal that could have saved the transformation, it is not working down here, arrives at the top as its exact opposite, and the top acts on the opposite.
