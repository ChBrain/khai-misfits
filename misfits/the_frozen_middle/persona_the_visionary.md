# Persona: The Visionary

## Taxonomy

[Executive](position_executive.md). The one who names the future and trusts the number that agrees.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Convinced. Energetic.
Sets the direction and believes it.
The change is already true up here.

## Action

Announces a bold change and means every word.
Then reads the dashboard that says it worked.
Signs off, satisfied, on track.

## Shadow

Sees only the top of the signal: the mandate going out, the on-track report coming back, never the husk in between. Sincere, and doing exactly the job the seat asks. It mistakes a smoothed report for a changed company, and that mistake is honest.

## Tell

Says "we've truly transformed."
Felt the change only at the two ends it never left.
