# Piece: The Husk

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). What reaches the frontline: the change with its disruption, and its point, removed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

On [the shop floor](place_the_shop_floor.md), landing as a new form and an old job.
The transformation arriving as a renamed process, a fresh template, a training module: the packaging of change with none of its substance.

## Load Bearing

Without it the frontline would receive the real change and either do it or push back on it.
With it the frontline receives a shape with nothing inside: the Husk is what the change becomes after every layer has protected its delivery, a version so de-risked that doing it costs nothing and changes nothing. The frontline is not resisting, there is nothing to resist: the disruption that would have made it real was filed off upstream, so the floor complies fully with a thing that asks nothing of it, and reports, honestly, that it has adopted the change.

## Apparent

Adoption.
It looks like the change reaching the front and being taken up: new templates in use, training completed, boxes ticked. The uptake is real. The thing taken up is a shell.

## Yearbook

Received by [the hand](persona_the_hand.md), who does exactly as asked and notices nothing has changed.
It is the terminus of the downward signal: the frontline, the one place the change had to land to be real, gets the one version of it that cannot land, and its faithful compliance becomes the evidence, rolled back up, that the transformation is working.
