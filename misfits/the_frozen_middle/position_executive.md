# Position: Executive

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). The top of the path, where the mandate goes out and the dashboard comes back.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The strategy, whole and confident.
The conviction that the company will move.

## Orders

Set the change. Name it, fund it, brand it.
Hold the org to the mandate.
Ask for the report, and read the report.

## Loses

Sight of the middle of the signal. Only two ends reach the top: the mandate going out, the report coming back.
The layer between them is dark. Nothing measures it, so it does not exist.
The frozen band stays frozen because no one at the top can see it is there.

## Drives

Toward a transformed company.
Shown a transformed dashboard.
Believes the second is the first.
