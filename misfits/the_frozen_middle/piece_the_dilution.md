# Piece: The Dilution

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). The change softened a notch at each layer on the way down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Along [the cascade](place_the_cascade.md), one hand-off at a time.
The bold thing translated into a pilot, the pilot into a workstream, the workstream into a slide in an all-hands nobody actions.

## Load Bearing

Without it the change would arrive at the floor intact and the play would be a success story.
With it the change loses amplitude in steps, because each layer, passing it on, files off the part that threatens its own delivery, and every filing is small and defensible: scope it down, sequence it later, run it as a trial first. The Dilution is attenuation by reasonableness: no layer kills the change, each merely makes it safe enough to survive its own review, and safe enough times enough is gone.

## Apparent

Prudent implementation.
It looks like sensible change management: de-risking, phasing, not betting the quarter on an untested idea. Each step is textbook. The sum is a strategy that has been managed into harmlessness.

## Yearbook

Applied by [the translator](persona_the_translator.md) first, and by every layer after.
It is the downward half of [the dampening](piece_the_dampening.md): each softening hands the next layer a slightly smaller change to soften, so the reductions compound, and what began as [the mandate](piece_the_mandate.md) reaches the floor as [the husk](piece_the_husk.md), all of it agreed to, none of it felt.
