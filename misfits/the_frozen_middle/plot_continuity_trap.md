# Plot: Continuity Trap

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The off-site](place_the_off_site.md), the strategy day, energy high.
A bold change is set at the top with real conviction. It is about to be handed to a layer that will agree with it and begin, at once, to make it safe.

## Action

[Translation](process_translation.md): [The Visionary](persona_the_visionary.md), holding [the executive's seat](position_executive.md), sets the mandate, and [The Evangelist](persona_the_evangelist.md) at [the transformation lead's seat](position_transformation_lead.md) takes it up with conviction and no line authority to pull.
[The Translator](persona_the_translator.md) at [the director's seat](position_director.md) begins turning the bold thing into a plan that will not threaten the quarter.

## Stage

[The Visionary](persona_the_visionary.md) tables [Set the Change](plan_set_the_change.md): announce it and mean it, and issues [the mandate](piece_the_mandate.md) at full strength.
[The Evangelist](persona_the_evangelist.md) tables [Carry the Torch](plan_carry_the_torch.md): drive the change with a mandate and no line.
[The Translator](persona_the_translator.md) tables [Make it Safe](plan_make_it_safe.md): scope it to fit delivery, and [the continuity trap](piece_the_continuity_trap.md) closes, the middle measured on the quarter the change disturbs.

## Tension

Everyone backs the change.
The executive is sincere, the evangelist is committed, the director genuinely agrees. But the director is held to this period's numbers, and the change threatens them, so making it safe is not sabotage, it is the rational first move of a seat asked to transform and to deliver and paid for only one.
