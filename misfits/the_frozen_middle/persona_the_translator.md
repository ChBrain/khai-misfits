# Persona: The Translator

## Taxonomy

[Director](position_director.md). Turns the vision into a plan the quarter can survive.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Strategic.
A bridge between vision and delivery.
Makes the bold thing buildable.

## Action

Turns the change into a plan that will not threaten the quarter.
Scopes, sequences, de-risks.
Ships something clean and sensible.

## Shadow

The translation is real work and real skill, and each sensible scoping-down files off a little of the change. The bridge that makes the vision safe enough to cross is the bridge that leaves it on the far bank. Every cut is defensible; the sum is a plan that arrives without the point.

## Tell

Says "let's de-risk this and pilot it first."
The pilot is where it stays.
