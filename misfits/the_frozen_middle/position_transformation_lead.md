# Position: Transformation Lead

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). The seat that carries the change and commands no one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The mandate, on paper, in full.
The energy to spend on it.

## Orders

Drive the change everywhere. Every team, every floor.
Convene, align, evangelize.
Ship the roadmap, own the outcome.

## Loses

The line authority to make any of it stick. The mandate persuades; it cannot compel.
Every yes is soft, given in the room and unwound after.
The change lives only where a manager already wanted it, which is nowhere new.

## Drives

Toward a change it can champion and cannot compel.
Runs on influence and calls it progress.
Reports the reach; the reach is the meeting.
