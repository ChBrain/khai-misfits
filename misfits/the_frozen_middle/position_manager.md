# Position: Manager

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). The seat where the change meets the number and the number wins.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The team, the delivery, and the disruption.
The one who holds all three and is measured on only one.

## Orders

Transform and hit the number.
Adopt the change, keep the throughput.
Do both, report both green.

## Loses

The change, damped to protect the number it is measured on. The incentive is the number, so the number wins.
Every disruption is smoothed before it can help.
The transformation becomes whatever fits inside this quarter's delivery, which is almost nothing.

## Drives

Toward steady delivery through a period of change.
Which means change that does not disturb delivery.
Which means, quietly, no change at all.
