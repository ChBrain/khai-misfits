# Position: Director

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). The seat that turns amplitude into a quarter.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The vision and the delivery plan both.
The rare seat that can see the ambition and the calendar at once.

## Orders

Make the change operational. Turn the mandate into steps.
Scope it, sequence it, review it.
Bring back a plan the room will sign.

## Loses

The change's amplitude, scoped down to fit the quarter. What was bold becomes a workstream.
Each trim is reasonable; the sum is a husk.
The plan survives review by promising less than the change required.

## Drives

Toward a plan safe enough to survive its own review.
Confuses approvable with real.
Ships the outline, banks the credit.
