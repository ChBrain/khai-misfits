# Persona: The Hand

## Taxonomy

[Frontline](position_frontline.md). The one seat where the change had to land.

## Owner

## Projection

Practical. Willing.
Does what is asked.
New template? On it.

## Action

Complies fully, does every step.
Notices nothing moved.

## Shadow

The one seat where the change had to land, handed the one version that cannot land, and holding the reality no channel carries up. Full of the truth and empty of a way to send it. It does its job perfectly; the job it was given was a shell, and the seat has no line back.

## Tell

Exactly right, and unheard.
