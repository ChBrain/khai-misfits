# Position: Team Lead

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). The seat between the floor's truth and the report that hides it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The floor's reality, in full.
The report to write, due up.

## Orders

Report up. Roll the week into a status.
Keep it green where you can.
Flag only what you must, and soften what you flag.

## Loses

The alarm, rounded off before it climbs. Each level rounds a little; the sum reads calm.
The truth reaches the report and the report reaches no one who could act.
The floor's real state never leaves the floor.

## Drives

Toward a calm patch, and a top that hears calm.
Protects the team by muting the team.
Sends the smooth number, keeps the rough one.
