# Piece: The Mandate

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md). The bold change as it leaves the top, at full strength.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the off-site](place_the_off_site.md), on the last slide, in the largest font.
The transformation announced with conviction: we will change how we work, and it starts now.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to dampen and the play has no charge.
With it there is a signal of real amplitude entering the top of the organisation, and the whole drama is what happens to that amplitude on the way down. The Mandate is genuine: the executive means it, the strategy is sound, the intent is not theatre. That is what makes its fate a misfit and not a farce: a real change, fully backed, that arrives at the floor as nothing, not because anyone rejected it, but because everyone it passed through had a reason to turn it down a notch.

## Apparent

The start of something.
It looks like the moment the organisation turns: leadership committed, direction set, energy high. All of that is true at the top. None of it has met the middle yet.

## Yearbook

Issued by [the visionary](persona_the_visionary.md), and carried off the stage by [the evangelist](persona_the_evangelist.md) with no line to pull.
It is the loudest the change will ever be, and it is loudest here because here is the only place it has not yet been made safe. Every layer below will receive it, agree with it, and quietly lower its volume.
