# Plot: Check

## Taxonomy

[The Frozen Middle](play_the_frozen_middle.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The roll-up](place_the_roll_up.md), where the reports climb back toward the top.
The floor's reality starts its ascent, and each layer is about to round off the part of it that would reflect on its own delivery.

## Action

[Filtering](process_filtering.md): [The Filter](persona_the_filter.md), holding [the team lead's seat](position_team_lead.md), turns the floor's this is not working into a manageable issue.
[Roll-up](process_roll_up.md): each layer above softens it again, until the summary that lands at the top says the change is being adopted well.

## Stage

[The Filter](persona_the_filter.md) tables [Smooth the Report](plan_smooth_the_report.md): send up the calm version, and [the smoothing](piece_the_smoothing.md) rounds the alarm off a notch at every level.
The rounded truth arrives as [the on-track report](piece_the_on_track_report.md): adoption high, milestones met, the programme green and ready to be declared embedded.

## Tension

No line in it is false.
Every report is real and every softening is small and defensible, it is early, the team is adjusting, we are on it. But the signal that could have saved the change, it did not land down here, loses amplitude at each level exactly as the change did coming down, so the top reads success in the one place there was none, and declares the transformation embedded on the strength of a floor that never felt it.
