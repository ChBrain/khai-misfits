# Persona: The Evangelist

## Taxonomy

[Transformation Lead](position_transformation_lead.md). All the change's heat, none of its handle.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Passionate. Tireless.
All-in on the change.
Carries the fire to every floor.

## Action

Takes the mandate through the org with conviction.
Rooms fill, heads nod, the deck lands.
No line authority behind a word of it.

## Shadow

Has the change's energy and none of its power, so it can inspire the middle and never compel it. A mandate without a lever, worn thin on a layer that agrees warmly and moves not at all. The seat gives everything it holds; it just holds no leverage.

## Tell

Says "who's with me?"
The room is entirely with it, and entirely unchanged.
