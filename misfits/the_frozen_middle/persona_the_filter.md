# Persona: The Filter

## Taxonomy

[Team Lead](position_team_lead.md). Composure manufactured by subtraction.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Composed. Reliable.
On top of the patch.
Nothing rattles it.

## Action

Turns the floor's alarm into a manageable issue on the way up.
Rounds the edges, softens the wording.
Reports handled.

## Shadow

Rounds off the bad news that would reflect on its own delivery, one worrying detail at a time. The composure is real, and it is made by subtraction. Reassurance that starves the top of the truth, each cut small enough to feel like judgment rather than concealment.

## Tell

Says "nothing we can't handle."
Sends up green.
