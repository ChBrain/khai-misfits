# Persona: The Keystone

## Taxonomy

[Keeper](position_keeper.md). The one head that holds the whole system, and holds it well.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Generous. Always there when you need it.
Ask anything, any hour, and the answer comes back clean.
Nothing waits on a queue when it is the queue.

## Action

Answers every question directly, on the spot.
Teaching is slower than telling, so it tells.
The help is real, and it is gladly given.

## Shadow

The generosity is the fast path, and the fast path is the trap.
Every quick answer keeps the knowledge in one head... the more it gives, the more it is the only place to ask.
It could not write it all down in a year, and it is never given a week.

## Tell

Says "just ask me, it's quicker," and it always is.
And nothing is ever written.
