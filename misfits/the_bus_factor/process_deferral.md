# Process: Deferral

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). The writing pushed one sprint further.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A deadline against a doc ticket.
A ship date that will not move.

## Direction

Writing pushed to next sprint.
The capture slides right, again and again.

## Lever

Delivery against capture, always a fire first.
Something urgent burns, the page can wait.

## Echo

The sharing task perpetually about to happen.
A promise renewed each planning, never kept.
