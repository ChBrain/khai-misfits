# Piece: The Tacit Knowledge

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). The know-how that lives in a head and resists being written down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the keyboard](place_the_keyboard.md), in the hands and the habits of the one who built it.
The why behind the config, the gotcha that is not in any comment, the reason it is done this odd way and must be: the working knowledge that never made it to a page.

## Load Bearing

Without it the system could be run from a manual and anyone could hold it.
With it the system can be run only by the person who carries the unwritten half, because the Tacit Knowledge is the part of knowing that does not survive being written: we know more than we can tell, and what cannot be told stays where it was learned, in one head. It is not withheld, it is simply not extractable at the speed anyone has, so it accretes in the builder as a by-product of building, and every fix adds a little more that only they now hold.

## Apparent

Expertise.
It looks like deep skill, hard-won mastery, the mark of someone who really understands the system. It is all of that. It is also a single uncopied volume, and the library has one shelf.

## Yearbook

Carried by [the keystone](persona_the_keystone.md), who could not write it all down in a year and is never given a week.
It is the root of the play: [the fast path](piece_the_fast_path.md) forms because asking this head is the only way to reach what only this head holds, and so the knowledge that resists writing is the knowledge that most needs it, and least gets it.
