# Piece: The Fast Path

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). Asking the one who knows, which is quicker than any way that would share.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Through [the queue](place_the_queue.md), a message to the expert and an answer in minutes.
The route every stuck person takes because it works: not the search, not the doc, not the dig, just ask the head that holds it and be unblocked.

## Load Bearing

Without it the team would be forced to the slow ways that leave a trail: the write-up, the shared debugging, the doc that outlives the moment.
With it the team takes the quick way every time, and the quick way is the trap, because the Fast Path resolves the problem and distributes nothing: a direct answer is a transfer from one head to one head, gone when the second forgets, while the slow ways would have made a copy anyone could read. It is not that the team chooses concentration over sharing. It chooses fast over slow, and fast happens to be the one that does not share.

## Apparent

Efficiency.
It looks like a well-oiled team removing blockers quickly, exactly what a good team does. Speed is a virtue. This speed is bought by never leaving anything behind.

## Yearbook

Taken by [the asker](persona_the_asker.md), and offered gladly by the one asked.
It is the engine of [the concentration](piece_the_concentration.md): every trip down the Fast Path is a lesson given and not recorded, so the knowledge is used constantly and copied never, and the more the team relies on it the deeper it sinks into the single head it came from.
