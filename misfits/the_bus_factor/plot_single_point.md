# Plot: Single Point

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The onboarding](place_the_onboarding.md), a new hire at a fresh desk with nothing to read.
The documentation that would bring them up to speed does not exist, so the one way in is to ask the expert, and the risk of that is plainly on the register.

## Action

[Reliance](process_reliance.md): [The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md), holding [the new hire's seat](position_new_hire.md), learns by asking the one who knows, deepening the very concentration that left them nothing to read.
[The Bettor](persona_the_bettor.md) at [the risk owner's seat](position_risk_owner.md) reads the single point of failure and accepts it, because closing it costs now and the failure is only ever later.

## Stage

[The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) tables [Onboard by Asking](plan_onboard_by_asking.md): learn the only way open, and each question adds again to the one head.
[The Bettor](persona_the_bettor.md) tables [Bet They Stay](plan_bet_they_stay.md): log it, name [the paper backup](piece_the_paper_backup.md), and carry [the single point of failure](piece_the_single_point.md) another quarter, because the expert is here and delivering.

## Tension

The arithmetic is sound every time.
A newcomer must learn from someone, and the someone is the expert, and paying real hours now to prevent a possible loss later never wins against this quarter's delivery. So the risk is seen, rated, and rationally accepted, mitigated on paper and never in fact, and the single point of failure sits on the register, acknowledged, growing, one sane deferral at a time.
