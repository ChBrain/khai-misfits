# Persona: The Bettor

## Taxonomy

[Risk Owner](position_risk_owner.md). Prudent, numerate, keeper of the register.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Prudent. Numerate. Manages the register line by line.
Sees the single point of failure clearly, names it, logs it.
Nothing hidden, everything counted.

## Action

Reads the single point of failure, names a paper backup.
Weighs the cost of fixing now against the odds of failing later.
Accepts the risk, one more quarter.

## Shadow

The arithmetic is genuinely sound: a certain cost now against a possible cost later.
So the rational owner waits, and rational waiting is how a seen risk is carried... growing, quarter by quarter, until the quarter it is too late.
No recklessness here, only a wager that pays out until it does not.

## Tell

Says "it's on the register, we're managing it."
And managing means watching it grow.
