# Position: Nominal Backup

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). The seat named second, that could be real redundancy and never gets the hours.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The title of second, on the org chart, in the plan.
And none of the knowledge, because none of it was ever handed over.

## Orders

Be the backup.
Be ready to step in the day the one who knows is gone.
Stand behind the seat you cannot yet fill.

## Loses

The hours to become one, always to delivery.
Shadowing loses to shipping in every planning: the backup is real only on paper.
The seat that could be redundancy is never resourced to be.

## Drives

Toward real redundancy, wanted by all and funded by none.
From a seat never given the time to build it.
The title stays; the coverage never arrives.
