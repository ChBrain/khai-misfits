# Piece: The Single Point of Failure

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). The whole system resting on one seat that could empty without notice.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

On [the onboarding](place_the_onboarding.md) and the risk register both, where the dependence is plainly visible and calmly accepted.
The known fact that if this one person is unavailable, the system cannot be changed, fixed, or explained by anyone else.

## Load Bearing

Without it the concentration is a habit and not yet a hazard.
With it the habit has a name and a place on a list, and is accepted anyway, because the Single Point of Failure is a risk everyone can see and no one is moved to pay to remove: mitigating it costs real time now, the failure it guards against is only possible and only later, and the expert is here today and delivering. So it is logged, rated, given a paper backup, and left, a known exposure carried in the open, growing, because the cost of closing it is certain and the cost of leaving it is merely probable.

## Apparent

A managed risk.
It looks handled: identified, assessed, on the register with an owner and a mitigation. Being on the register reads as being controlled. It is being watched, which is not the same, and what is watched here only ever gets larger.

## Yearbook

Accepted by [the bettor](persona_the_bettor.md), who weighs a certain cost against a possible one and rationally waits.
It is the risk that [the empty desk](plot_the_empty_desk.md) collects: seen for years, mitigated on paper, never actually reduced, because every quarter the same sane arithmetic says not yet, until the quarter it says too late.
