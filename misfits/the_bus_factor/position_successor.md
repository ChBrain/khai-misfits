# Position: Successor

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). An empty desk, and an undocumented system to keep alive.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An empty desk, where the one who knew used to sit.
An undocumented system, running, that no one can now explain.

## Orders

Keep it alive.
Learn it from the outside, by reading the code that has no notes.
Rebuild what left with the person.

## Loses

Nothing it was given: it inherited a gap.
The knowledge was never written, so nothing was there to hand down.
The bill for years of unwritten answers comes due here, all at once.

## Drives

Toward rebuilding the knowledge from scratch.
At a cost the writing-down would have spared many times over.
The fast path arrives, at last, as the slowest one.
