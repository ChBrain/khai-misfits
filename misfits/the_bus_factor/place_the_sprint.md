# Place: The Sprint

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). Two weeks against a clock.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Delivery measured against the clock.
Points burned down, velocity climbing.

## Holds

The deferral, docs below every ship.
The backup untrained, one more cycle out.

## Offers

Dates hit.
A board of green, a demo that lands.

## Withheld

The hours to write it down or to train the backup.
The slack that would spread the load.
