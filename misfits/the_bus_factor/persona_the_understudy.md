# Persona: The Understudy

## Taxonomy

[Nominal Backup](position_nominal_backup.md). Named as the second, and quietly aware it is not.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Willing. Named on the chart as the backup.
Present in the plan, the box that says: covered.
Ready, on paper.

## Action

Stands as the backup, where the org chart says to stand.
Never given the hours to actually learn the thing.
The label holds; the knowledge does not.

## Shadow

This seat could be real redundancy, and it knows the redundancy is fiction.
The time to shadow always loses to delivery, so it stays a label on an empty box... and it cannot fix that from where it sits.
Sensible people filled the box, and the box is still empty.

## Tell

Says "I'm the backup, but honestly I couldn't run it."
And no one hears the second half.
