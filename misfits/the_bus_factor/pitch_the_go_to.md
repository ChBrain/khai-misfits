# Pitch: The Go-To

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Fast, capable, unblocked.
Whenever anyone is stuck, they know exactly who to ask, and the answer comes back in minutes.
The voice of a team that never waits long, spoken by people who each, sensibly, ask the one who already knows.

## Undertow

Under the speed, a single copy.
Each quick answer solves the problem and skips the writing down, so the knowledge deepens in one head and spreads to none.
The team never slows: it only leans a little harder on the one person, and stands a little nearer the day they are gone.

## Nerve

The feeling of a team that always knows who to ask,
moving fast because it never has to look anything up,
resting its whole weight on a chair that could be empty on Monday.

## Echo

After the run: a system no one left can explain,
a wiki page that was always next sprint,
and a week when the fastest team in the building could not answer its own questions.
