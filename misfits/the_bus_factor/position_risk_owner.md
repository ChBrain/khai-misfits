# Position: Risk Owner

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). The register, and the choice to spend now or wait.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The register, with the concentration logged on it in red.
The choice to spend now or wait, quarter after quarter.

## Orders

Manage the risk.
Weigh the cost of acting against the odds of the day it lands.
Keep the register current and let the numbers decide.

## Loses

Nothing this quarter, which is why the risk is never closed.
The bet pays every quarter it does not fail, so waiting always looks cheapest.
The loss is deferred until it arrives all at once.

## Drives

Toward a sound bet, that the expert stays.
Renewed each quarter it holds, until the quarter it does not.
Every renewal makes the eventual loss larger.
