# Process: Departure

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). The one head out the door.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A notice, an offer, a burnout, a bus.
Any reason a chair goes empty.

## Direction

The one head out the door.
The knowledge walking with the person.

## Lever

Any exit against no copy, the chair empties and the knowledge with it.
No backup means the loss is total.

## Echo

A system no one left can explain.
A machine still running, its logic now a rumor.
