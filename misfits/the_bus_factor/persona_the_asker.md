# Persona: The Asker

## Taxonomy

[Team Member](position_team_member.md). Unblocked, always, because there is always someone who knows.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Pragmatic. Unblocked. Gets on with it.
No time lost hunting through pages that do not exist.
One message, one answer, back to work.

## Action

Sends every question straight to the expert.
It is the route that works, so it is the route taken.
Fast in, fast out, moving again.

## Shadow

Taking the quick answer is sensible, and it leaves no copy.
Each ask deepens the dependence it relies on... a good team member doing the efficient thing, again, onto the single point.
Nothing malicious, just the shortest line between blocked and shipping.

## Tell

Says "I'll just ask them, they'll know," and they do.
And no one else learns it.
