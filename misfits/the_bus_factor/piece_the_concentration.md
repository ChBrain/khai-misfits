# Piece: The Concentration

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). The travelling mechanism: dependence rising as redundancy falls, question by question.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across every interaction, in one direction.
Each answer given, each fire fought, each fix applied by the one who knows, all of it deepening the knowledge in the same single head instead of spreading it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is one clever person and a bit of key-person risk.
With it it is a current: the Concentration is why the two lines diverge rather than drift, because the very act of using the knowledge, taking the fast path, is what deepens it, so reliance does not merely coexist with fragility, it manufactures it. Every question routed to the expert makes the expert a little more the only one, so the team's growing skill at getting answers is the same motion as its growing inability to survive without the answerer. It runs one way, because a direct answer adds to the head and subtracts from no one, and there is no interaction that spreads the knowledge as a by-product the way asking concentrates it.

## Apparent

A team getting better.
It looks like expertise deepening, a go-to person becoming ever more capable, the team ever quicker. All true. The same graph, read the other way, is the redundancy going to zero.

## Yearbook

Driven by every ask, and reversed by nothing, until [the empty desk](plot_empty_desk.md).
It runs the dependence up while the redundancy falls, [the fast path](piece_the_fast_path.md) feeding [the tacit knowledge](piece_the_tacit_knowledge.md) back into the one head it came from, the two lines drawing apart the whole way, until the distance between what the team relies on and what it could survive losing is the whole system.
