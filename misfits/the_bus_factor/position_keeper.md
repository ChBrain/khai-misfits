# Position: Keeper

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). The one head that holds the whole system, and the unwritten half of it.

## Owner

The whole system, load-bearing and live.
And the unwritten half of it, the part that lives nowhere but here.

## Orders

Help whoever asks, fast, because asking beats the page that was never written.

By staying the only one who can.
Each help renews the trap it means to relieve.
