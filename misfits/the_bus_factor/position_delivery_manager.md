# Position: Delivery Manager

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md). The deadlines, the sprint, and the one who makes them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The deadlines, dated and public.
The sprint, planned around who can move fastest.

## Orders

Ship.
Route the hard work to the one who knows: it lands soonest there.
Fill the board and burn it down.

## Loses

The documentation, deferred every planning.
Writing it down is always slower this sprint than asking, so it never wins the slot.
The velocity that ships is the velocity that hides the risk.

## Drives

Toward delivery, sprint over sprint.
On the back of the one who knows.
Each on-time ship makes the next one lean harder on the same head.
