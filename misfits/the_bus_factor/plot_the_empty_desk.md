# Plot: The Empty Desk

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The empty desk](place_the_empty_desk.md), the Monday after the one head is gone.
A notice worked, or a better offer, or a burnout, or the bus itself: the chair is empty, and everything it held is not there.

## Action

[Departure](process_departure.md): the expert leaves, and the tacit half of the system leaves with them, uncopied and unrecoverable.
[Excavation](process_excavation.md): [The Excavator](persona_the_excavator.md), holding [the successor's seat](position_successor.md), digs through the undocumented system to rebuild what was only ever spoken.

## Stage

[The Excavator](persona_the_excavator.md) tables [Dig It Out](plan_dig_it_out.md): reconstruct the knowledge from the code and the scraps, and [the concentration](piece_the_concentration.md) is read at last, all of it that ran into one head and none that ran out.
[The bus](piece_the_bus.md) has taken what it always could: a partial, wrong copy is rebuilt at a cost that dwarfs the writing-down never done.

## Tension

Nobody hoarded, and nobody was unlucky.
Every fast answer was generous, every deferral was reasonable, every accepted risk was sound arithmetic. The reckoning is not a betrayal or an accident, it is the fast path arriving where it always led: the knowledge was used constantly and copied never, so the day its one holder left, it left. Write it down once would have cost a week ... or the desk is refilled, the new expert starts answering, and the next single point begins to form.
