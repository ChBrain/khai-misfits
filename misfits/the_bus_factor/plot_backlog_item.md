# Plot: Backlog Item

## Taxonomy

[The Bus Factor](play_the_bus_factor.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The sprint](place_the_sprint.md), where delivery is the measure and the clock is always running.
The team ships on the expert, fast, and on [the backlog](place_the_backlog.md) the ticket to write it all down sinks a little lower each planning.

## Action

[Deferral](process_deferral.md): [The Leaner](persona_the_leaner.md), holding [the delivery manager's seat](position_delivery_manager.md), pulls delivery every sprint and lets the documentation wait.
[Indispensability](process_indispensability.md): the expert, asked for everything and rewarded for being asked, becomes the one seat nothing can route around.

## Stage

[The Leaner](persona_the_leaner.md) tables [Ship It](plan_ship_it.md): hit the delivery, defer the writing, and [the backlog item](piece_the_backlog_item.md) is endorsed by all and pulled by none.
[The Understudy](persona_the_understudy.md) at [the nominal backup's seat](position_nominal_backup.md) tables [Cover on Paper](plan_cover_on_paper.md): stand as the named second, never given the hours to become real, while [the indispensable](piece_the_indispensable.md) makes the single point pay.

## Tension

Every call is the right call this sprint.
Shipping beats documenting when there is a deadline, and there is always a deadline, and the backup is always needed on delivery too. But the sum of every reasonable this-sprint is a team at its fastest and its most fragile, the expert now the only one who can, the backup real only on paper, and the redundancy quietly at zero.
