# Persona: The Newcomer

## Taxonomy

[New Hire](position_new_hire.md). Keen, arriving, looking for the door marked docs.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Keen. Ready to learn. Looking for something to read.
First week, open to everything, notebook out.
Wants the map before the territory.

## Action

Finds nothing to read, and learns the only way open.
Asks the expert, because the expert is the documentation.
Onboards by conversation, one question at a time.

## Shadow

Onboarding by asking is the sole route, and it deepens the concentration that left nothing to read.
A new person, blameless, adding again to the one head... because the org gave them no other door.
The newest arrival becomes the newest dependency, before they even choose to.

## Tell

Says "where are the docs?" and is pointed at a person.
Not a page.
