# Persona: The Excavator

## Taxonomy

[Successor](position_successor.md). Plain, arriving after, holding a shovel.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Plain. Arrived after the one who knew was gone.
No handover waiting, no page to open.
Just a running system and a shovel.

## Action

Digs through the undocumented system, layer by layer.
Rebuilds what was only ever spoken, from traces and guesses.
Reconstructs the map by walking the ground.

## Shadow

Not a hero, just the one who came next.
It rebuilds a partial, wrong copy at a cost that dwarfs the writing-down never done... and proves by the labour that one page would have saved it.
This is the reckoning arriving as excavation, where foresight would have cost almost nothing.

## Tell

Says "there's nothing written down anywhere."
And there is not.
