# Place: The Trial

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). Where the explanation is put before a real novice and its gap found, the usability test or reader trial that checks felt clarity against evidence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the account is tried on someone who does not yet know, a usability session, a reader test, a fresh pair of hands given the instructions and watched.
The tester observing where the novice stumbles, the expert's felt clarity meeting the learner's real confusion, the gap showing at last in what the novice cannot do.

## Holds

The testing, and the evidence it gathers.
Here the explanation meets a novice on purpose, so the Trial holds the point where the gap becomes visible: the account put before a mind that lacks the knowledge and watched, so it is where felt clarity is checked against what actually lands, the expert's sense of the obvious weighed against the learner's stumble and found wanting.

## Offers

A way to see the gap from outside, and the evidence to rebuild.
Because the Trial seats a real novice and watches them, it offers the tester the one thing the knowing withheld, the novice's actual response, so it hands out the sight of the gap the knower could not feel and the record of where to rebuild, and hands it out at the price of watching the clear account fail before a stranger.

## Withheld

The novice's ignorance to inhabit, and a finished cure.
The Trial can show where a novice stumbles, but it cannot let the expert become the novice or supply an ignorance to reason from directly, so it withholds the inside view the knower lost, leaving only the outside evidence of the stumble, and, all test and no re-entry, it withholds any final closing of the gap, since each remade account must be tried again on a mind the maker still cannot inhabit.
