# Persona: The Knower

## Taxonomy

[Knower](position_knower.md). Explains the thing that has become second nature, from inside the knowing and over the head of the learner, in good faith and unable to see the gap.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Expert. Fluent. Glad to explain.
Is the one who knows the subject cold, for whom the steps are reflex and the shorthand is plain, and who explains it willingly and clearly, as they see it.
No condescension in it: only someone who understands the thing well trying, in good faith, to hand it on.

## Action

Explains from inside the knowing, giving the account that is clear to them, skipping the steps now automatic and speaking the shorthand now transparent.
Judges the explanation by how plain it feels, and it feels plain, because they know.
Does what a helpful expert does: sets out the thing they understand in the way that makes sense to them.

## Shadow

Not a talker-down, a genuine helper, and that is the whole difficulty: the explanation is honest and clear to the one giving it, and it goes over the head of the one who needs it.
Because the knowing has overwritten the ignorance and cannot be set aside, the Knower cannot simulate the mind without the knowledge, so they explain to a version of the learner who already half-knows, and cannot even perceive the miss, since perceiving it would mean un-knowing the thing, and the more expert they grow the wider and less visible the gap becomes.
It explains as clearly as it can see, and what it can see is set by the very knowledge the learner lacks.

## Tell

Says "it's obvious: you just do this, then this, and there you are."
Says it in good faith, and cannot feel that "just" is carrying every step that took them years to make reflex.
