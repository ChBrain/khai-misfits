# Position: Peer

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The seat that reviews the explanation from inside the same knowing, finds it clear, and ratifies the opacity no knower can see.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The same subject mastered and the charge to check the account: the standing of the second expert asked to review it and pass it on.
A clear case: the explanation reads clearly and correctly to a qualified eye, so approving it is honest and right.
No more sight of the gap than the writer had, because it shares the knowledge that hides it.

## Orders

Wave it through.
Read the explanation against your own understanding, confirm it is sound and clear, and approve it as ready.
Check the account by whether it holds for you, and pass it on.

## Loses

The catch the review was meant to make, exactly by being qualified to make it.
Because the Peer shares the knowledge, it shares the blind spot, so the account reads clearly to it for the same reason it was written unclearly, and the check meant to find the miss ratifies it instead, the very qualification that makes the seat a fit reviewer making it the one least able to feel the gap a novice would fall into.
It gains an honest approval and loses the one thing a review is for, the sight of the reader who does not already know.

## Drives

Toward the account read clear and approved,
bought against the novice it cannot stand in for,
so the seat asked to catch the gap is the seat that certifies it as clarity.
