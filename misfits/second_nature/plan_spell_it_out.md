# Plan: Spell It Out

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the knower's seat](position_knower.md), inside [Second Nature](play_second_nature.md).

## Direction

Explain the thing as you understand it. Set out the steps and the shorthand that make it plain to you, judge the account by how clear it feels, and hand the knowledge on in the way that makes sense. Help, plainly and in good faith, sure the thing is obvious.

## Orders

([The Knower](persona_the_knower.md) explains from inside the knowing, skipping the reflex steps and speaking the transparent shorthand, honestly and unable to see the gap.)

## Targets

- [ ] the thing is explained as the knower understands it, in good faith
- [ ] the reflex steps are skipped and the transparent shorthand spoken
- [ ] the account is judged clear by a feeling the knower's own knowledge sets
- [ ] the explanation is pitched over the head of the learner, the gap unseen
