# Process: Testing

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The putting of the account before a real novice and rebuilding from the stumble, the move that finds the gap, at the cost of the obvious and never quite finished.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A distrust of felt clarity and a real novice to try.
At [the trial](place_the_trial.md), the tester giving the explanation to someone who does not yet know and watching where they fall.

## Direction

Toward the gap made visible, if the felt clarity can be given up.
Because obviousness is felt from inside and the gap is invisible to the knower, testing checks the account from outside, taking the novice's confusion as the evidence, so it drives toward the gap found and the account remade: but it means admitting the thing is not obvious, submitting the clear account to a stranger who finds it unclear, and rebuilding against the expert's certainty, so it closes the gap only by spending the comfort of the obvious.

## Lever

The truth that only a novice can show what a novice will miss.
Because the knower cannot inhabit the ignorance and the novice's stumble is the one honest signal of it, testing is the single move that reaches the gap, so it runs on a sound method: yet the same move that finds the gap forgoes the felt clarity every knower trusts, and can only approximate the ignorance it cannot re-enter, so it works, and is never quite done.

## Echo

A gap found and an account rebuilt, or a test waived as needless.
Either the explanation is tried on a real novice and remade from where they stumbled, bought against the ease of the obvious, or the test is waved off with "but it's obvious," the account left as it was, and the miss runs on, ratified and unseen.
