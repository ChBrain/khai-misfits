# Plot: The Compounding

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Lectern](place_the_lectern.md), mastery deepening and more of the subject going to reflex.
The knower growing more expert, the steps once conscious now automatic, the gap widening and the sense of it fading.

## Action

[Explaining](process_explaining.md): [The Knower](persona_the_knower.md), holding [the knower's seat](position_knower.md), grows more expert and explains from ever deeper inside the knowing, the gap wider and less felt.

## Stage

[The Knower](persona_the_knower.md), on [Spell It Out](plan_spell_it_out.md), masters the subject further, and each thing that goes to reflex is a thing they no longer think to say and can no longer feel themselves assuming.
So the more they know, the more the account compresses and the wider the gap it must cross, and the less of that gap they can perceive, until the ablest at the thing is the least able to hand it on.

## Tension

Deepening mastery is nothing but gain in the subject, and it widens and hides the very gap the teaching must cross.
There is nothing to fault: growing more expert is the point of study. But expertise and the power to teach it pull apart, so the plot closes with the two divorced, knowledge failing to cross exactly where there is most of it to give, the person who knows the most speaking furthest over the head of the one who knows the least.
