# Persona: The Tester

## Taxonomy

[Tester](position_tester.md). Would put the explanation before a real novice and rebuild from where they stumble, at the cost of admitting the obvious is not and submitting the clear account to a mind that finds it unclear.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Humble. Empirical. Watching the novice.
Is the one who does not trust their own sense of what is clear, who insists on trying the explanation on someone who does not yet know and watching where they actually fall.
No pedantry in it: only the discipline of knowing that obviousness is felt from inside and must be checked from outside.

## Action

Puts the explanation before a real novice, watches where they stumble, and rebuilds the account from there.
Treats the felt clarity of the expert as unreliable and the confusion of the learner as the data, and closes the gap by evidence rather than by feel.
Does what usability and reader-testing do: replaces the knower's guess at the novice with the novice's actual response.

## Shadow

Not a doubter of expertise, a doubter of felt clarity, and that is the whole difficulty: testing against a real novice is the one move that finds the gap, and it forgoes the comfort of the obvious.
Because the account feels clear to every knower and the test will show it is not, the Tester must admit the thing is not obvious, submit the clear explanation to a stranger who will find it unclear, and rebuild it against the expert's certainty and the peer's nod, and even then can only approximate an ignorance that cannot be truly re-entered, so the move that lets knowledge cross is slow, humbling, and never quite finished.
It would close the gap by evidence, and closing it means spending the ease of the obvious for the discomfort of being shown unclear.

## Tell

Says "let's watch someone who doesn't know try to use it, and see where they get stuck."
Says it, and is met with "but it's obvious," because the test that would prove the gap is the one the knowing says is needless.
