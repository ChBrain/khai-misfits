# Place: The Lectern

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). Where the explanation is given and reviewed, the lecture or document or design from which the knower speaks and the peer signs off.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the knowledge is set out, a lecture, a manual, a diagram, an interface, from which the expert explains the thing they understand.
The knower speaking from inside the knowing, the peer reading it over and approving, the account judged by how plain it feels to those who give and check it.

## Holds

The explaining and the ratifying, and the felt clarity they share.
Here the knowledge meets its expression, so the Lectern holds the point where the account is set and approved: an explanation given from inside the knowing and a review made from inside it too, so it is where the thing is declared clear by everyone who already understands it, the felt plainness never questioned by anyone who lacks the knowledge.

## Offers

A stage for the clear account, and a qualified eye to bless it.
Because the Lectern is where the expert explains and the peer reviews, it offers the knower a place to hand the knowledge on and the peer a place to certify it, so it hands out the honest, confident, ratified explanation, and hands it out judged only by minds that share the knowledge that makes it feel plain.

## Withheld

The novice's ear, and any sign of the gap.
The Lectern gathers those who know to give and to check the account, but it cannot seat among them the one who does not know, so it withholds the only reader who could feel the gap, letting the explanation be judged clear by everyone unable to fall into it, and, all expertise and no ignorance, it withholds any sign that the account approved as plain sails clean over the head it was written for.
