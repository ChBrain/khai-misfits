# Plot: The Blank

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Back Row](place_the_back_row.md), the account landing opaque on a mind that lacks the knowledge.
The plain-sounding words assuming what the learner does not have, the skipped step where the thread breaks, the room apparently nodding.

## Action

[Losing](process_losing.md): [The Learner](persona_the_learner.md), holding [the learner's seat](position_learner.md), cannot follow, and takes the miss for their own dullness.

## Stage

[The Learner](persona_the_learner.md), on [Try to Follow](plan_try_to_follow.md), strains to make the plain-sounding words connect and loses the thread where a reflex step was skipped.
Glancing round to find everyone senior apparently following, they conclude the fault is their own, resolve to look it up later, and stay quiet.

## Tension

Reading one's own confusion as one's own failing is the natural inference, and it silences the one voice that could report the gap.
There is nothing to fault: the account sounds obvious and the seniors seem to follow, so shame is reasonable. But the miss is the explanation's, not the learner's, so the plot closes with the learner lost and silent, blaming themselves, the gap the knower cannot see now the gap the learner will not name.
