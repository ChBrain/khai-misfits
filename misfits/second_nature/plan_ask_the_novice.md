# Plan: Ask the Novice

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the tester's seat](position_tester.md), inside [Second Nature](play_second_nature.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Tester](position_tester.md)

## Direction

Put the explanation before a real learner and watch where they stumble. Take their confusion as the evidence and the expert's felt clarity as unreliable, rebuild the account from where they fell, and replace the knower's guess at the novice with the novice's actual response. Check from outside what feels obvious from inside.

## Orders

([The Tester](persona_the_tester.md) tests the account on a real novice and rebuilds from the stumble, at the cost of the obvious and against the knower's certainty and the peer's nod.)

## Implementation

## Targets

- [ ] the obvious is admitted not obvious, or the test is waived and the miss runs on
