# Position: Tester

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The seat that would test the explanation on a real novice and rebuild from where they stumble, at the cost of the obvious and against the knower's certainty.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A distrust of felt clarity and a way to check it: the standing to put the account before someone who does not know and watch where they fall.
A clear case: obviousness is felt from inside and must be checked from outside, so the novice's confusion is the data and the expert's ease is not.
No way to inhabit the novice's ignorance fully, and no cover from the charge of doubting a thing everyone qualified finds plain.

## Orders

Ask the novice.
Put the explanation before a real learner, watch where they stumble, take their confusion as the evidence, and rebuild the account from there.
Replace the knower's guess at the novice with the novice's actual response.

## Loses

The comfort of the obvious and the speed of trusting felt clarity, exactly by checking the account from outside.
Because the explanation feels clear to every knower and the test will show it is not, the seat must admit the thing is not obvious, submit the clear account to a stranger who finds it unclear, and rebuild against the expert's certainty and the peer's nod, and even then only approximate an ignorance it cannot truly re-enter, so the one move that lets knowledge cross is slow, humbling, and never quite done.
It buys a gap found and an account remade against the ease it forgoes, and is taken, for a while, as the one who doubted what everyone qualified could see.

## Drives

Toward the explanation tested and rebuilt from the stumble,
bought against the comfort of the obvious and the knower's certainty,
so the one seat that would carry the knowledge across must spend the felt clarity that hid the gap.
