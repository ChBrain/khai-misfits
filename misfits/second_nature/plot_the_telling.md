# Plot: The Telling

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Lectern](place_the_lectern.md), a thing that has become second nature and a knower glad to explain it.
The subject mastered, the steps gone to reflex, the shorthand gone transparent, the account there to be given as it feels plain.

## Action

[Explaining](process_explaining.md): [The Knower](persona_the_knower.md), holding [the knower's seat](position_knower.md), gives the account from inside the knowing, skipping the reflex steps and speaking the transparent shorthand.

## Stage

[The Knower](persona_the_knower.md), on [Spell It Out](plan_spell_it_out.md), sets out the thing as they understand it, judging the account clear by how plain it feels, and it feels plain, because they know.
The steps now reflex are skipped and the terms now transparent are spoken, honestly and in good faith, and the account is pitched to a mind that already half-knows.

## Tension

Explaining a thing you understand, in good faith, is only helpful, and the helping is pitched over the head of the one who needs it.
There is nothing to fault: the account is honest and the thing really is obvious to the knower. But the knowing sets what feels plain and cannot be set aside, so the plot closes with the explanation given, clear to the one giving it and blank to the one it is for, the tune ringing for the tapper and bare taps for the listener.
