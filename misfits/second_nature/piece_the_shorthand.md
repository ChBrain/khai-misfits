# Piece: The Shorthand

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The compressed jargon and the skipped steps, transparent to the knower and opaque to the novice, the medium through which the account misses.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Spoken at [the lectern](place_the_lectern.md), where it reads as plain to those who know.
Falls blank in [the back row](place_the_back_row.md), where the learner meets it and loses the thread.

## Load Bearing

Without it the account would spell out every step, and the gap would have nothing to slip through.
With it the miss has its medium: the words the knower uses that carry, for them, whole chains of meaning, and the steps they skip because those steps are now reflex, all transparent to the one who knows and blank to the one who does not. The Shorthand is load bearing because it is where the knowledge speaks past the learner (Newton's tappers; Pinker): the compression the expert cannot feel is compression, so the piece carries the "just" that hides ten steps and the term that hides a year, the plain-sounding surface through which the account sails over the head it is for.

## Apparent

Precision, the efficient, expert phrasing that gets straight to the point.
Seen at the surface, the Shorthand looks like fluency, an economical account that wastes no words, and the truth that its economy is bought by assuming the reader shares the knowledge it compresses, that each skipped step is a place the learner will fall, that the plain-sounding term is transparent only to those who already know, stays hidden behind how crisp and expert the compressed account sounds.

## Yearbook

Spoken by [the knower](persona_the_knower.md) through [explaining](process_explaining.md), the compression that reads plain and lands blank.
It is the medium of the trap: the Shorthand carries [the knowledge](piece_the_knowledge.md) in a form transparent to the knower and opaque across [the gap](piece_the_gap.md), the surface [ratifying](process_ratifying.md) reads as clear and [the miss](piece_the_miss.md) slips through, the plain-sounding face of [second nature](piece_second_nature.md).
