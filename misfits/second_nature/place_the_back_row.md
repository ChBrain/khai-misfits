# Place: The Back Row

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). Where the learner sits with the explanation and loses it, the seat of the one who does not yet know and will not say so.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the account is received, a back row, a support ticket, a first day, where the one who does not yet know meets the explanation given for them.
The learner straining to follow, losing the thread at a skipped step, glancing round to find everyone else apparently nodding, and staying quiet.

## Holds

The losing, and the silence that keeps it.
Here the explanation meets the mind that lacks the knowledge, so the Back Row holds the point where the account falls short: the learner unable to follow and reading the miss as their own, so it is where the gap is felt and not named, the one place the opacity is actually experienced and the one voice that could report it stilled by shame.

## Offers

The only true sight of the gap, and no way to voice it.
Because the Back Row is where the account is met by someone who does not already know, it offers the one honest test of whether it lands, the learner's real confusion, so it hands out the very evidence the Lectern lacked, and hands it out to someone with no standing to speak it, sure the fault is their own and unwilling to hold up the room.

## Withheld

The confidence to name the miss, and any relay of it to the knower.
The Back Row holds the one who felt the gap, but it cannot lend them the certainty that the fault was the explanation's, so it withholds the voice that would report the opacity, letting the learner take it inward, and, all felt confusion and no standing, it withholds any path by which the miss reaches the knower who could not see it and the peer who ratified it.
