# Persona: The Peer

## Taxonomy

[Peer](position_peer.md). Another knower who reads the explanation over, finds it perfectly clear because they too know, and waves it through, ratifying the opacity no knower can see.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Qualified. Diligent. A fair reviewer.
Is the second expert asked to check the explanation, who reads it carefully and finds it sound and clear, and approves it in good conscience.
No rubber-stamping in it: only a competent reviewer honestly reporting that the account reads plainly to them.

## Action

Reads the explanation over and finds it clear, because the same knowledge that made it plain to the writer makes it plain to the reader.
Approves it as sound, sees no gap, and passes it on as ready.
Does what a good reviewer does: checks the account against their own understanding and finds it holds.

## Shadow

Not a careless checker, a competent one, and that is the whole difficulty: the review is honest and the explanation really does read clearly to the Peer, and that is exactly why the opacity survives it.
Because the Peer shares the knowledge, they share the blind spot, so the very qualification that makes them a fit reviewer makes them unable to feel the gap the novice would fall into, and the check meant to catch the miss ratifies it instead, a second knower confirming as clarity what only a novice could have shown to be opaque.
It reviews in good faith, and reviewing in good faith from inside the knowing is what certifies the miss as clear.

## Tell

Says "reads well to me: clear and correct, ship it."
Reads well, truly, because they already know, so the one asked to catch the gap is the one who cannot.
