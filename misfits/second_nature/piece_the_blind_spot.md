# Piece: The Blind Spot

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The knower's inability to perceive the gap, the missing instrument, so the miss cannot be caught from inside the knowing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Sits in the knower at [the lectern](place_the_lectern.md), and in the peer who shares it there.
Corrected only from [the trial](place_the_trial.md), where an outside test supplies the sight the inside lacks.

## Load Bearing

Without it the knower would feel the gap and adjust, and the curse would fix itself.
With it the trap is sealed from inside: to perceive the gap the knower would have to simulate a mind without the knowledge, and they cannot, because their own knowledge intrudes and will not be set aside, so the gap is not merely unbridged but unperceived. The Blind Spot is load bearing because it is what makes the curse uncorrectable from within (Fischhoff's hindsight cousin; Nickerson): the absence of any instrument to feel the gap, so the piece carries the reason the knower cannot self-correct, the second knower cannot catch it, and only an outside test can, the sightlessness that shares itself with everyone who knows.

## Apparent

Confidence, the plain assurance of someone who understands the thing and knows they have explained it well.
Seen at the surface, the Blind Spot looks like justified certainty, an expert sure of a clear account, and the truth that the certainty is exactly the symptom, that the knower cannot feel the gap because feeling it would require un-knowing, that the surest sign the miss is unseen is how obvious it all feels, stays hidden behind how reasonable it is for someone who knows to trust that they were clear.

## Yearbook

Shared by [the knower](persona_the_knower.md) and [the peer](persona_the_peer.md) across [ratifying](process_ratifying.md), the missing sight of the gap.
It is the seal of the trap: the Blind Spot hides [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) from everyone who knows, so [the miss](piece_the_miss.md) passes uncaught and only [testing](process_testing.md) from outside can reveal it, the sightlessness that makes [second nature](piece_second_nature.md) invisible to the very minds it traps.
