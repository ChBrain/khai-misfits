# Process: Ratifying

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The peer reading the account from inside the same knowing, finding it clear, and certifying the opacity a novice would fall into.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An account to be checked and a second knower to check it.
At [the lectern](place_the_lectern.md), the peer reading the explanation over against their own understanding and finding it sound and clear.

## Direction

Toward the miss confirmed as clarity and passed on.
Because the peer shares the knowledge, they share the blind spot, so ratifying reads the account as plain for the same reason it was written unclear, and the review meant to catch the gap approves it, a second qualified mind confirming as clear what only a novice could have shown to be opaque, and passing it on as ready.

## Lever

The competence of the reviewer and the honesty of the check.
Because the peer is genuinely qualified and the account genuinely reads clearly to them, approving it is an honest, competent judgement, so ratifying runs on real diligence, the certified miss assembled from no rubber-stamping, only from a reviewer whose very qualification is what makes them unable to feel the gap.

## Echo

An opaque account approved as clear by someone who also cannot see it.
The explanation has passed a qualified review and been found plain, so what reads as a checked and certified lesson is, in truth, a miss ratified by a second knower, the one asked to catch the gap the one least able to, and the opacity now stamped as clarity.
