# Persona: The Learner

## Taxonomy

[Learner](position_learner.md). Receives the explanation given from inside the knowing, cannot follow it, and takes the miss for their own dullness, leaving the gap unspoken.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Willing. Attentive. Trying to keep up.
Is the one who does not yet know, listening hard to the expert, wanting to understand, following as far as they can.
No slowness in it: only someone who lacks the knowledge the explanation quietly assumes, doing their honest best to catch what is being said.

## Action

Tries to follow the account and loses the thread where a reflex step was skipped or a transparent shorthand used.
Hears the taps and not the tune, and reaches for the sense that the explanation withheld without meaning to.
Does what a learner does: attends, strains, and tries to make the plain-sounding words connect to something they can hold.

## Shadow

Not a dull student, a person who simply does not yet know, and that is the whole difficulty: the miss is the explanation's and the Learner reads it as their own.
Because the account sounds obvious and everyone senior seems to follow it, the Learner takes their failure to keep up for a failure in themselves, concludes they are the slow one, and stays quiet rather than expose it, so the gap that the knower cannot see the learner will not name, and the one voice that could report the miss is the one most sure the fault is its own.
It cannot follow, and cannot see that not-following was the explanation's doing, not its own.

## Tell

Says nothing, nods, and resolves to look it up later, alone.
Says nothing, and by saying nothing leaves the knower sure the account was clear and the gap unmarked.
