# Pitch: It's Obvious

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Clear, helpful, plain.
The plain sense of a thing that seems obvious and an expert glad to explain it, a lesson that could hardly be simpler, given in good faith to help.
Nothing false in it: the explanation is honest, the expert is trying to help, and the thing really is, to them, obvious.

## Undertow

Under the clarity, a knowledge grown so deep it has become second nature, so the knower cannot picture the mind that lacks it and explains from inside the knowing.
Because obviousness is set by what one knows and cannot be unlearned, the expert skips the reflex steps and speaks the transparent shorthand, over the head of the learner, who takes the miss for their own dullness, while the gap stays invisible to the one who made it.
The clarity that is meant to hand knowledge on speaks past the very person it is for.

## Nerve

The feeling of explaining a thing so plain you cannot imagine anyone missing it, sure you are being clear,
because it is obvious, to you, and honestly given,
while the one who does not know hears only dull taps for a tune ringing in your head, and blames themselves, and stays quiet.

## Echo

After the run: knowledge that failed to cross, worst where the expert knew the most, an account clear to everyone who already understood it,
no one careless and no one unkind, only an honest explanation from inside a knowing that overwrote the ignorance it needed to reach,
and the one move that would carry it, testing against a real novice and rebuilding from where they stumble, forgone because the thing felt obvious and the gap could not be seen.
