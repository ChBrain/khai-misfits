# Piece: Second Nature

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The phenomenon whole, a knowing grown so deep it hides the ignorance it would need to reach, so the account misses and the miss cannot be seen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across all three: spoken and ratified at [the lectern](place_the_lectern.md), lost in [the back row](place_the_back_row.md), recovered only at [the trial](place_the_trial.md).
It is the whole circuit, the knowledge that fails to cross because the knowing hides the gap.

## Load Bearing

Without it the pieces are separate: a mastery, a shorthand, a gap, a blind spot, a failed transfer, with nothing to bind them.
With it they are one mechanism: the knowledge resets what feels obvious, the shorthand speaks it past the learner, the gap opens, the blind spot hides it, and the miss lands and is misblamed, each part sealing the next. Second Nature is load bearing because it is the structure entire (Camerer, Loewenstein, and Weber; Nickerson; Pinker): the curse of knowledge, the knowing that overwrites the ignorance it must reach, so the piece carries the trap whole, the reason the better one knows a thing the worse one hands it on, and no one need be careless for knowledge to fail to cross.

## Apparent

Good teaching, the plain competence of an expert explaining clearly and a qualified reviewer approving it.
Seen at the surface, the whole trap looks like knowledge well handed on, an honest account from someone who understands the thing, and the truth that it sailed over the head it was for, that the felt clarity was the symptom, that the transfer failed worst where the expertise was deepest, stays hidden behind how exactly like a clear, helpful, well-reviewed explanation each step in turn appears.

## Yearbook

Assembled from every element: [the knowledge](piece_the_knowledge.md) that resets the obvious, [the shorthand](piece_the_shorthand.md) that speaks past, [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) it opens, [the blind spot](piece_the_blind_spot.md) that hides it, and [the miss](piece_the_miss.md) it lands.
It is the trap named: Second Nature binds [explaining](process_explaining.md), [losing](process_losing.md), and [ratifying](process_ratifying.md) into one failed transfer, crossed only where [testing](process_testing.md) recovers the view the knowing lost.
