# Position: Knower

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The seat that explains the thing it knows, from inside a knowing that sets what feels obvious and hides the gap.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The subject mastered and the wish to hand it on: the standing of the one who understands the thing cold and is glad to explain it.
A clear case: the thing is plain, the explanation honest, and helping someone learn it is only good.
No way to un-know the subject, and no instrument to feel the gap between what it knows and what the learner does.

## Orders

Spell it out.
Explain the thing as you understand it, set out the steps and the shorthand that make it plain, and judge the account by how clear it feels.
Hand the knowledge on in the way that makes sense to you.

## Loses

The reach to the mind that lacks the knowledge, exactly by explaining from inside the knowing.
Because the knowing has overwritten the ignorance and cannot be set aside, the seat judges clarity by a feeling its own knowledge sets, so it skips the reflex steps and speaks the transparent shorthand and cannot perceive that it has, and the more it masters the subject the wider and less visible the gap grows, the ablest at the thing the least able to hand it on.
It gains a clear account and the ease of the obvious, and loses any sight of the learner its clarity sails past.

## Drives

Toward the thing explained and felt plain,
bought against the gap it cannot see,
so the seat that knows the most explains furthest over the head of the one who needs it.
