# Piece: The Miss

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The failed transfer and its misplaced blame, the knowledge that did not cross and the learner who is faulted for it, the cost of the curse.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Falls in [the back row](place_the_back_row.md), where the account does not land and the learner takes the blame.
Undone only at [the trial](place_the_trial.md), where the gap is found and the account remade.

## Load Bearing

Without it the curse is a private quirk of feeling, and no knowledge is lost and no one wronged.
With it the trap does its damage: the knowledge fails to cross, the account lands opaque, the learner is blamed or blames themselves, and the transfer the explanation was for does not happen. The Miss is load bearing because it is where the curse costs (Newton; Pinker): the failed transfer and the fault laid on the wrong party, so the piece carries the outcome the whole structure builds to, the knowledge stalled where there was most of it to give and the learner marked slow for standing where the account could not reach.

## Apparent

A slow learner, the plain appearance of someone who could not grasp a clear explanation.
Seen at the surface, the Miss looks like the failure of the one who did not follow, and the truth that the account was written past them, that the transfer failed on the explanation and not the mind, that the blame sits on the only party who could not have caused it, stays hidden behind how much a learner who did not keep up looks like the reason the learning did not happen.

## Yearbook

Suffered by [the learner](persona_the_learner.md) through [losing](process_losing.md), the transfer failed and misblamed.
It is the cost of the trap: the Miss is [the shorthand](piece_the_shorthand.md) fallen into [the gap](piece_the_gap.md), ratified past [the blind spot](piece_the_blind_spot.md), the knowledge stalled that only [testing](process_testing.md) recovers, the price [second nature](piece_second_nature.md) exacts on the one it was meant to teach.
