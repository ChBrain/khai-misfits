# Process: Losing

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The learner falling off the account at the skipped step, taking the miss for their own dullness and leaving the gap unspoken.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An account pitched over the head and a mind that lacks the knowledge.
At [the back row](place_the_back_row.md), the learner straining to follow and losing the thread where a reflex step was skipped or a transparent shorthand used.

## Direction

Toward the thread lost and the fault taken inward.
Because the account sounds obvious and the seniors seem to follow, losing leaves the learner reading their failure to keep up as their own dullness rather than the explanation's opacity, so they take the miss inward, conclude they are the slow one, and stay quiet, and the gap the knower cannot see becomes the gap the learner will not name.

## Lever

The felt obviousness of the account and the nods of the room.
Because the explanation is pitched as plain and everyone qualified appears to follow, reading one's own confusion as one's own failing is the natural inference, so losing runs on a reasonable shame, the silence assembled from no stubbornness, only from a learner who takes an opaque account at its confident word and blames the mirror.

## Echo

A learner lost and sure the fault is theirs.
The thread has been lost and the miss taken for dullness, so what reads as a slow student who could not keep up is, in truth, a mind that met an account written past it, silenced by the very shame the gap produced and leaving the knower certain the lesson was clear.
