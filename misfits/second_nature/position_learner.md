# Position: Learner

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The seat that receives the explanation and cannot follow it, taking the miss for its own dullness and leaving the gap unspoken.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Attention and goodwill and the very ignorance the explanation assumes away: the standing of the one who does not yet know and is trying to learn.
A clear case: the account sounds obvious and everyone senior seems to follow, so the failure to keep up looks like its own.
No knowledge to fill the skipped steps, and no standing to name a gap that the knower cannot see and the seniors do not report.

## Orders

Try to follow.
Attend to the explanation, strain to make its plain-sounding words connect, and reach for the sense the account withheld without meaning to.
Keep up as far as you can, and do not hold up the room.

## Loses

Its own account of the failure, exactly by trying in good faith to follow.
Because the explanation sounds obvious and the seniors nod, the seat reads its inability to follow as its own dullness rather than the account's opacity, so it takes the miss inward, concludes it is the slow one, and stays quiet, and the one voice that could report the gap is silenced by the very shame the gap produces.
It gains a reputation for keeping up and loses the knowledge it came for, and with it any claim that the fault was not its own.

## Drives

Toward the thread lost and the fault taken inward,
bought against its own account of the miss,
so the seat the explanation failed is the seat that blames itself for failing.
