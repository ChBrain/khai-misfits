# Process: Explaining

## Taxonomy

[Second Nature](play_second_nature.md). The giving of the account from inside the knowing, the knower setting out the thing as it feels plain to them and pitching it over the head of the learner.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A thing mastered and a wish to hand it on.
At [the lectern](place_the_lectern.md), the knower explaining the subject they understand cold, setting out the steps and the shorthand that make it plain to them.

## Direction

Toward an account clear to the knower and opaque to the learner.
Because the knowing sets what feels obvious and cannot be set aside, explaining judges clarity by the knower's own sense of it, so it skips the steps now reflex and speaks the shorthand now transparent, and the account is pitched to a mind that already half-knows, landing plain on the expert and blank on the one who does not.

## Lever

The honest wish to help and the genuine plainness of the thing.
Because the explanation is given in good faith and the thing really is obvious to the knower, each choice of what to include and what to assume is a sound, well-meant judgement, so explaining runs on real helpfulness, the miss assembled from no condescension, only from a knowing that overwrote the ignorance the account would need to reach.

## Echo

An account that feels clear and lands opaque.
The thing has been explained honestly and set out as plainly as the knower can feel, so what reads as a clear and helpful lesson is, in truth, an account pitched over the head of the one it is for, the tapper's tune ringing for the tapper and bare taps for the listener.
