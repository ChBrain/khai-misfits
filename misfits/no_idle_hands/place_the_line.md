# Place: The Line

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md). Where the queue forms behind the server and the wait grows, the place the variability the idle time no longer holds waits, and the backlog diverges as the busy fraction nears one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the jobs wait to be served, a queue before the machine, a backlog of cases, the lead time downstream, where every arrival takes its place and waits its turn.
The variability the buffer used to hold now waiting here, the Queue lengthening as the busy fraction rises, and the wait each job takes on growing behind the server.

## Holds

The wait, and the variability that has nowhere else to sit.
Here the arrivals wait behind the loaded server, so the Line holds the point where the cost of the loading is carried: the variability the idle time used to absorb now held in the queue, the wait growing as the utilisation over one minus itself, so it is where the backlog and the lead time diverge together, and where the whole cost of the last increments is borne out of the metric's sight.

## Offers

The wait made real and the cost of the leanness carried.
Because the Line is where the variability waits once the idle time is spent, it offers the delay every arrival now takes on and the backlog that grows with the fraction, so it hands out the true price of the busy resource, and hands it out behind the server where the utilisation metric does not look, the swelling line and the high fraction the same full resource from two ends.

## Withheld

Any voice on the floor, or any warning before the wall.
The Line can grow but cannot be seen from where the metric is read, because it forms behind the work and the utilisation reads only the server, so it withholds itself from the loading that drives it, letting the fraction be raised while the wait diverges unseen, and, all growing backlog and no place in the metric, it withholds the choke until the wait goes to infinity at full utilisation.
