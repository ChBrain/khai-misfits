# Persona: The Queue

## Taxonomy

[Queue](position_queue.md). The accumulating backlog and the wait behind the server, growing as the utilisation over one minus itself, without bound as the slack runs out, bearing the whole cost the leanness never shows. The archetype of the diverging line.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Patient. Growing. Behind the work and out of the metric's sight.
Is the backlog of jobs waiting, the lead time downstream, the inventory before the server, the delay every arrival now takes on.
No fault in it: the Queue forms because a loaded resource can only absorb its variability in the time it stands free, and that time has been squeezed away.

## Action

Grows as the busy fraction rises, not in step but as the utilisation over one minus itself, gently while slack remains and steeply as it thins.
Holds the variability the idle time used to hold, so every arrival waits longer, and the wait and the backlog diverge together toward the wall at full utilisation.
Does what a queue must: it takes up the variability that no longer has idle time to sit in, and carries the cost the busy metric on the wall cannot show.

## Shadow

Not a sign of a failed server, a sign of a full one, and that is the whole difficulty: the line grows precisely because the resource is reliable and busy, not because anything broke.
Because the wait rises as the utilisation over one minus itself, the Queue is bounded and modest while there is slack and diverges without bound as the fraction nears one, so it carries the whole cost of the last efficient-looking increments while the floor still reads lean, growing behind the server where the utilisation metric does not look, the leanness on the floor and the swelling line the same fact seen from two ends.
It absorbs the variability the buffer used to absorb, and the cost it carries is exactly the cost the busy fraction was raised to hide.

## Tell

Says, in effect, nothing the floor can hear, and lengthens.
Says it in lead time that stretches and due dates that slip downstream while the utilisation metric reads high, so the one who watches the server sees leanness and the one who waits in line sees the wait, the same full resource read as well run at the front and as a jam at the back.
