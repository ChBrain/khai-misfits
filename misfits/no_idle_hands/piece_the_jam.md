# Piece: The Jam

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md). The phenomenon whole, a system run hot and choked by its own load, the leanness on the floor and the diverging line behind it the same fact seen from two ends.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Grown across [the floor](place_the_floor.md) and [the line](place_the_line.md), where the loading and the wait are the same resource from two ends.
Spared, if ever, from [the reserve](place_the_reserve.md), where the slack held keeps it from ever forming.

## Load Bearing

Without it the pieces are just a busy server and a growing line, and no one names the thing itself.
With it the trap is complete and authorless: a system driven to full and choked by its own load, the wait diverging on ordinary variability alone in a resource that never once failed (Kingman, on the single server queue in heavy traffic, the congestion at the limit; Hopp and Spearman, Factory Physics, on the flow choked as utilisation nears one). The Jam is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole: not a breakdown and not an accident but a flow run hot until its slack is gone, so the piece carries the busiest resource revealed as the one whose line grows without bound, the leanness the manager was right to chase and the choke behind it named at last as one thing.

## Apparent

A resource fully used at the front, a mystery of slow delivery at the back, two facts that seem unrelated.
Seen from the floor, the Jam does not look like one thing at all: the server reads lean and the lead time reads long and nothing connects them, and the truth that the leanness and the choke are the same full resource, that the busy fraction the manager raised is the wait the flow now carries, that no failure caused it, stays hidden behind how unrelated a busy server and a slow flow appear until the law joins them.
