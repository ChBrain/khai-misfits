# Position: Queue

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md). The seat behind the server that holds the variability the idle time no longer holds, where the wait and the backlog grow as the utilisation over one minus itself and carry the cost the leanness never shows.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The variability that no longer has idle time to sit in, and a length that grows with the busy fraction: the arrivals waiting, the lead time downstream, the delay each job now takes on.
A real load: the wait is not imaginary, it is the whole cost of the last increments, carried where the utilisation metric does not look.
No standing of its own, and no way to be seen from the floor, forming behind the work and out of the metric's sight.

## Orders

Wait in line.
Hold the variability the buffer used to hold, and grow as the busy fraction rises, gently while slack remains and steeply as it thins.
Lengthen, because the idle time that would have absorbed the variation has been squeezed away.

## Loses

Any chance of being seen while it can still be spared, exactly because it grows behind the server and not at it.
Because the wait rises as the utilisation over one minus itself, the seat is modest while there is slack and diverges without bound as the fraction nears one, so it carries the whole cost of the efficient-looking increments while the floor still reads lean, the swelling line and the busy metric the same full resource seen from two ends.
It takes up the variability the buffer used to take up, and loses any voice on the floor that priced the idle time as waste.

## Drives

Toward the line lengthened and the wait diverging,
the variability held where idle time used to hold it,
so the seat behind the busiest hands grows without bound, carrying the cost the leanness on the floor was raised to hide.
