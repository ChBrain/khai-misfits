# Plot: The Choke

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Line](place_the_line.md), the wait diverged and the backlog with it.
The whole flow feeling the delay the loaded server has pushed behind it, lead time and its variability blowing up together.

## Action

[Queuing](process_queuing.md): [The Queue](persona_the_queue.md), holding [the queue's seat](position_queue.md), diverges without bound at fixed throughput, and the cost reaches everything downstream.

## Stage

The Queue, on [Wait in Line](plan_wait_in_line.md), carries [the wait](piece_the_wait.md) and the backlog past the knee of the curve, and [the jam](piece_the_jam.md) forms, the whole flow choked on [the variability](piece_the_variability.md) the spent [slack](piece_the_slack.md) no longer holds.
The busiest resource stands revealed as the one whose line grows without bound, the leanness on the floor and the jam behind it the same full resource from two ends.

## Tension

The flow choking on its own load is no accident, and the absence of any accident is what makes it hard to name.
There is nothing to fault: throughput held steady, the server never failed, and the variability was ordinary. But the wait diverged all the same, so the plot closes with lead time and its variability blown up together, the choke authored by no failure and carried by everything downstream of a resource that reads, on the floor, as lean.
