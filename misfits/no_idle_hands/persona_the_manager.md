# Persona: The Manager

## Taxonomy

[Manager](position_manager.md). The one who sees idle capacity as waste and drives the server to full utilisation, feeding it the next job the moment it frees, right that idleness costs and right to drive it out. The archetype of the local, rational squeeze.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent. Cost-aware. Sure a busy resource is a resource well run.
Is any owner of the resource who answers for its use, the plant lead, the ward sister, the scheduler, the one whose metric reads utilisation.
No greed in it: the Manager loads the server because idle capacity genuinely costs and closing the gaps genuinely wrings more output from the same fixed price.

## Action

Watches the busy fraction, sees the idle time as waste, and drives it out, feeding the server the next job the moment it comes free and closing every gap.
Reads a high utilisation as good and a low one as slack to be spent, raising the fraction point by point toward one.
Does what a manager must: it prices the idle hour as a cost and erases it, and each erased hour reads as leanness gained.

## Shadow

Not a waster of effort, a diligent driver-out of idleness, and that is the whole difficulty: the loading is rational, and the idle time driven out was the buffer the whole flow needed.
Because the metric reads utilisation and the line forms behind the server and not at it, the Manager sees the resource busy and cannot see the queue swelling, so takes the high fraction for a flow run well, drives the last increments hardest because they look the most virtuous, and spends the slack that absorbed the variability without ever seeing it was a buffer, sure to the choke that a fully occupied resource is the leanest one.
It squeezes out the idle time sensibly, and the sense of the squeeze is exactly what hides that the idle time was the buffer.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "look how busy it is, nothing wasted," and means the resource is run well.
Says it watching a metric that reads high and cannot show the line, so hears a full busy fraction as leanness and the idle hour as waste, and cannot see that the hour was the slack the flow needed.
