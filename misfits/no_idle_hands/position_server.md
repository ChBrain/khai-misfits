# Position: Server

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md). The seat that does the work, run ever nearer saturation and still perfectly reliable, where each job is taken the moment it frees and the only thing that shrinks is the time it stands free.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A steady rate of work and a busy fraction the Manager drives higher: the reliable capacity to complete each job, and less and less idle time between them.
A blameless record: the seat never fails and never slows, serving exactly as well loaded near saturation as it did with slack to spare.
No control of its own load, and no way to signal that its shrinking idle time was the buffer the line behind it needs.

## Orders

Take the next job.
Complete the work at your reliable rate and take the next the moment you come free, whatever the busy fraction.
Serve, and stand free less, because the gaps are for closing.

## Loses

The idle time that was the buffer, exactly because the seat is reliable enough to be loaded to the edge without faltering.
Because it never fails, nothing about the seat warns of the wall: it serves each job as always while its free time shrinks toward nothing, so the variability that used to sit in its idle minutes moves to the line behind it, and the seat carries the load to saturation without a fault to point to.
It keeps its steady rate and its clean record, and loses the free time in which the flow's variability used to wait.

## Drives

Toward the next job taken and the idle time shrunk,
the same reliable work done at an ever higher fraction,
so the seat that never fails is driven to the edge of saturation, serving perfectly while the line behind it grows without bound.
