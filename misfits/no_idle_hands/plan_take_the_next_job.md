# Plan: Take the Next Job

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the server's seat](position_server.md), inside [No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Server](position_server.md)

## Direction

Complete the work at a reliable rate and take the next job the moment you come free, whatever the busy fraction. Serve exactly as well loaded near saturation as with slack to spare, never failing and never refusing, standing free less as the gaps are closed.

## Orders

([The Server](persona_the_server.md) takes each job at its steady rate and stands free less as it is loaded, so the variability that used to sit in its idle minutes moves to the line behind it, no fault to point to.)

## Implementation

([The Floor](place_the_floor.md), where the server works; the [serving](process_serving.md) process; the steady rate is held to [saturation](piece_the_saturation.md) while [the slack](piece_the_slack.md) that was the server's idle time shrinks toward nothing, the resource reliable to the last.)

## Targets

- [ ] each job is completed at the server's reliable rate
- [ ] the next job is taken the moment the server comes free
- [ ] the work holds steady as the busy fraction is driven higher
- [ ] the idle time between jobs shrinks toward nothing without any failure
