# Piece: The Wait

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md). The delay behind the server that diverges as utilisation approaches one, rising as the utilisation over one minus itself, the cost of the leanness carried out of the metric's sight.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Grown behind the server at [the line](place_the_line.md), where the variability waits.
Kept bounded, if ever, from [the reserve](place_the_reserve.md), where the slack that would hold it down is kept.

## Load Bearing

Without it the loading has no cost and the leanness is simply gained, and there is no trap.
With it the flow has its price: the delay every arrival takes on behind a loaded server, rising as the utilisation over one minus itself times the variability, bounded while there is slack and without bound as the fraction nears one (Kingman, on the single server queue in heavy traffic, the mean wait proportional to the utilisation over one minus the utilisation). The Wait is load bearing because it is the divergence itself: not a linear cost a manager could weigh against the efficiency but a hyperbolic one that hides low down and blows up near the top, so the piece carries the delay that makes the last increment of utilisation buy an unbounded loss.

## Apparent

Nothing much, on the floor: a small delay, a modest line, a cost easily traded for the leanness.
Seen while there is slack, the Wait looks bounded and cheap, a delay that rose gently with the fraction and could rise gently again, and the truth that it grows as one over one minus the fraction, that it doubles and doubles again as the last points are spent, that it diverges at the wall, stays hidden behind how modest a bounded wait looks right up to the knee of the curve.

## Yearbook

Grown by [the queue](persona_the_queue.md) through [queuing](process_queuing.md), held bounded only by [the reserver](persona_the_reserver.md) through [reserving](process_reserving.md).
It is the divergence of the trap: the Wait is [the variability](piece_the_variability.md) multiplied by [the utilisation](piece_the_utilisation.md) over one minus itself, rising toward [the saturation](piece_the_saturation.md) where it goes to infinity, the delay whose blow-up is [the jam](piece_the_jam.md).
