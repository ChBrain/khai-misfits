# Persona: The Server

## Taxonomy

[Server](position_server.md). The resource that does the work, run ever nearer saturation and still perfectly reliable, taking the next job the moment it frees and never once failing. The archetype of the loaded, blameless resource.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady. Reliable. Never broken.
Is the machine, the nurse, the desk, the processor, the docket, any single resource that jobs must pass through to be done.
No fault in it: the Server does not fail and does not slow, it simply takes the next job and completes it, run harder than before and holding.

## Action

Takes the next job the moment it comes free, completes it, and takes the next, run at a busy fraction the Manager drives ever higher.
Does its work at the same reliable rate whether the fraction is two parts in three or nineteen in twenty, never failing and never refusing.
Does what a resource does: it serves the jobs that arrive, and the harder it is loaded the less time it stands free between them.

## Shadow

Not a broken thing, a perfectly reliable one, and that is the whole difficulty: the divergence needs no failure, the wait grows on the server's blameless, steady work alone.
Because the Server never breaks, nothing about it warns of the wall: it takes each job as it always has, and only the time it stands free shrinks as it is loaded, so the variability that used to wait in its idle minutes now waits in the line behind it, and the flow chokes not because the server faltered but because it was fed to the edge of saturation while working exactly as well as ever.
It serves reliably to the last, and its reliability is exactly what lets the fraction be driven to where the wait diverges without a single fault to point to.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "still running, still on time, nothing wrong here," and means it, job by job.
Says it completing every job at its steady rate, so shows no strain even as the fraction nears one, and cannot signal that its shrinking idle time was the buffer the line behind it has lost.
