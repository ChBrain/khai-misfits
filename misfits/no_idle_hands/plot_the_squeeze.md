# Plot: The Squeeze

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Floor](place_the_floor.md), idle time standing between jobs and priced as waste.
The Manager seeing the gaps and reaching to close them, feeding the server the next job the moment it frees.

## Action

[Loading](process_loading.md): [The Manager](persona_the_manager.md), holding [the manager's seat](position_manager.md), drives out the idle time and raises the busy fraction point by point.

## Stage

The Manager, on [Fill Every Hour](plan_fill_every_hour.md), squeezes out [the slack](piece_the_slack.md) and drives [the utilisation](piece_the_utilisation.md) up, closing the gaps and reading each closed gap as leanness gained.
The idle time driven out is priced as waste, and cannot be seen as the buffer the flow needed to absorb its variability.

## Tension

Driving out idle time that genuinely costs is rational, and the rationality is what hides that the idle time was the buffer.
There is nothing to fault: the fixed cost runs whether the resource works or not, and closing the gaps wrings more output from the same price. But the slack spent was the variability's only place to sit, so the plot closes with the busy fraction raised and read as lean, the buffer gone and the variability bound for the line behind the server.
