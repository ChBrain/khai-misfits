# Position: Reserver

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md). The seat that would hold utilisation below saturation and keep planned slack, leaving the resource idle on purpose so the buffer stays, at the cost of looking like it tolerates waste.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A target busy fraction below one and the standing to defend it: the will to keep planned slack and the authority to leave capacity free on purpose.
A real corrective: held slack is the buffer the variability needs, and the one thing that keeps the wait bounded and the flow fast.
No cheap way to do it, and no welcome for the one who leaves a good server visibly idle against the pressure to load it.

## Orders

Hold the slack.
Keep the busy fraction below saturation, leave idle time on purpose so the variability has somewhere to sit, and refuse the last increment that reads most efficient and buys the choke.
Defend the free capacity, and treat a fully loaded resource as suspect for being fully loaded.

## Loses

The look of a resource well run, and the standing of one who wastes nothing.
Because the utilisation metric rewards a high fraction and the queue the slack spares leaves no trace, the seat must defend visibly idle capacity against every signal that a busy resource is lean, be answerable for an idle hour whose worth is a jam that never happens, so the choke it prevents leaves nothing to point to while the idleness it holds is plain for all to see.
It gains the buffer kept and the flow held fast, and loses the leanness and the standing that loading the resource would have kept.

## Drives

Toward the slack held and the busy fraction kept below saturation,
at the cost of tolerating visible idleness that reads as waste,
so the seat that leaves hands idle on purpose keeps the line bounded, and is read as the one who will not let a good server be fully used.
