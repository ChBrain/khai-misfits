# Piece: The Utilisation

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md). The busy fraction of the resource, the metric watched on the floor and driven toward one, read as leanness gained with every point.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Watched on [the floor](place_the_floor.md), where the busy fraction is read and raised.
Held down, if ever, at [the reserve](place_the_reserve.md), where it is kept below saturation on purpose.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to drive and no metric to reward, and the loading has no lever.
With it the trap has its dial: the fraction of capacity in use, the number a manager can raise and be right to raise, since idle capacity genuinely costs (Hopp and Spearman, Factory Physics, on utilisation as the fraction of a resource's time it is busy, the standing target of operations). The Utilisation is load bearing because it is the term that sets the wait: the queue grows as the utilisation over one minus the utilisation, so the same dial that reads leanness as it climbs is the dial that sends the wait toward infinity near one, the piece carrying the busy fraction that is both the manager's virtue and the flow's undoing.

## Apparent

Leanness, a resource well used, output wrung from a fixed cost.
Seen on the floor, the Utilisation looks like exactly the good it is priced as, a high fraction reading as a flow run lean, and the truth that the same fraction over one minus itself sets a wait that diverges near one, that the last points read best and cost most, that the metric improves right up to the choke, stays hidden behind how good a busy resource looks.

## Yearbook

Driven up by [the manager](persona_the_manager.md) through [loading](process_loading.md), held below saturation only by [the reserver](persona_the_reserver.md) through [reserving](process_reserving.md).
It is the dial of the trap: the Utilisation is raised by spending [the slack](piece_the_slack.md), and as it nears one it multiplies [the variability](piece_the_variability.md) into [the wait](piece_the_wait.md), toward [the saturation](piece_the_saturation.md) where the line becomes [the jam](piece_the_jam.md).
