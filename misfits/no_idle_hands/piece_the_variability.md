# Piece: The Variability

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md). The variation in arrivals and service that, with the utilisation, sets the wait, Kingman's other term, the blameless weather the buffer was for.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Present at [the floor](place_the_floor.md), in arrivals that cluster and service that varies.
Held, while slack lasts, in [the slack](piece_the_slack.md); flooding, once it is spent, into [the line](place_the_line.md).

## Load Bearing

Without it a resource could run at full with no queue at all, and there is no trap, only a metronome.
With it the flow has its weather: the ordinary, blameless variation in when jobs arrive and how long they take, the second term of the law that multiplies the utilisation factor into the wait (Kingman, on the heavy-traffic approximation, the wait scaling with the variability of arrivals and service; Hopp and Spearman, the VUT relation, variability times a utilisation factor times the process time). The Variability is load bearing because it is why the slack was never spare: it never stops, so a resource run near full will queue on ordinary variation alone, the piece carrying the reason full utilisation cannot be free, no burst and no failure needed.

## Apparent

Noise, a thing that averages out, nothing a good schedule cannot smooth.
Seen on the floor, the Variability looks like it should cancel over time, arrivals and service evening out to a steady mean, and the truth that it never has idle time to average against once the slack is spent, that it and the utilisation set the wait together, that it is the weather the buffer existed for, stays hidden behind how harmless ordinary variation looks against an average.

## Yearbook

Held by [the slack](piece_the_slack.md) while the idle time lasts, flooding [the queue](persona_the_queue.md) through [queuing](process_queuing.md) once it is spent.
It is the weather of the trap: the Variability multiplied by [the utilisation](piece_the_utilisation.md) over one minus itself is [the wait](piece_the_wait.md), blameless and unending, the reason [the saturation](piece_the_saturation.md) is a wall and the line becomes [the jam](piece_the_jam.md).
