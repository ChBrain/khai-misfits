# Process: Queuing

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md). The wait growing behind the server as the busy fraction nears one, the non-linear rise that carries the variability the idle time no longer holds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Variability with no idle time left to sit in.
At [the line](place_the_line.md), the arrivals and the service varying against a server whose free minutes have been squeezed away.

## Direction

Toward the wait diverging as the fraction nears one.
Because a loaded resource can only absorb its variability in the time it stands free, queuing holds that variability in the line once the idle time is spent, so the wait rises not in step with the loading but as the utilisation over one minus itself, bounded while slack remains and without bound as the busy fraction nears one, the backlog and the lead time diverging together at fixed throughput.

## Lever

The arithmetic of the utilisation factor.
Because the wait scales as the busy fraction over one minus the busy fraction times the variability, queuing runs not on any burst or failure but on the plain multiplication of ordinary variation by a factor that blows up near one, so the same one point of efficiency costs almost nothing low down and an unbounded delay near the top, the divergence built from the numbers alone.

## Echo

The line grown, and the cost of the leanness carried.
The queue has taken up the variability the buffer used to take up, so what reads on the floor as a lean resource is, in truth, a diverging wait behind it, the whole cost of the last increments borne out of the metric's sight, the swelling line and the high fraction the one full resource from two ends.
