# Process: Serving

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md). The Server processing the jobs at its reliable rate, the blameless work that holds steady to saturation while its idle time shrinks toward nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A job at the head of the line and a server come free.
At [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the server taking the next job the moment it can and completing it at its steady rate.

## Direction

Toward each job served and the idle time shrunk.
Because the server completes the work at the same reliable rate however it is loaded, serving turns the next job around and reaches for the next, so the only thing that changes as the busy fraction rises is the time it stands free between jobs, the free minutes that held the variability shrinking toward nothing as the load nears saturation.

## Lever

The perfect reliability of the resource.
Because the server never fails and never slows, serving runs to the edge of saturation without a fault to warn of it, so the load can be driven to where the wait diverges on the server's steady, blameless work alone, no breakdown and no burst required, only a reliable resource fed until its idle time is gone.

## Echo

Each job served, and the buffer gone with the idle time.
The server has done its work exactly as well as ever, so what reads as a resource holding up is, in truth, the free time between jobs vanishing, the variability that used to wait in those minutes moved to the line, the reliability that lets the load reach the wall leaving nothing to blame.
