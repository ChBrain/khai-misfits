# Process: Reserving

## Taxonomy

[No Idle Hands](play_no_idle_hands.md). The Reserver holding the busy fraction below saturation and keeping planned slack, the costly restraint that leaves the buffer in place so the wait stays bounded.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A busy fraction driven toward one and someone unwilling to spend the last of the slack.
At [the reserve](place_the_reserve.md), the reserver setting a target below saturation and defending the idle time against the pressure to load it.

## Direction

Toward the slack held and the wait kept bounded.
Because the idle time is the buffer the variability needs, reserving leaves capacity free on purpose, so the busy fraction is held below the wall and the variability keeps somewhere to sit, the wait staying bounded because the slack was never squeezed away, the flow kept fast rather than lean.

## Lever

The willingness to keep visible idleness that reads as waste.
Because holding the slack means leaving a good server below full and refusing the increment that reads best on the metric, reserving runs only on someone choosing to pay that cost, so the buffer is defended continuously and thanked rarely, the choke it prevents leaving no trace of the jam it spared and the idle hour it keeps plain for all to see.

## Echo

The slack kept, and the flow held fast.
The reserver has left the buffer in place, so what reads as tolerating waste is, in truth, the one act that keeps the wait bounded, the idle time held so the variability never floods the line, the jam that now never forms leaving nothing to thank the held capacity for.
