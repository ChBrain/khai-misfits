# Plot: The Closing

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The vacancy](place_the_vacancy.md), the option dropped below the level it needs.
The custom has thinned past the threshold, and the alternative can no longer keep on.

## Action

[Losing](process_losing.md): [The Option](persona_the_option.md), holding [the option's seat](position_option.md), closes, for everyone and for good, while [The Bereft](persona_the_bereft.md), holding [the bereft's seat](position_bereft.md), loses it with no say and no warning.

## Stage

[The Option](persona_the_option.md) tables [Stay Available](plan_stay_available.md) one last time and drops past saving, taken by [the irreversibility](piece_the_irreversibility.md).
[The Bereft](persona_the_bereft.md) tables [Miss It Too Late](plan_miss_it_too_late.md) and finds the thing it valued simply gone.

## Tension

A thing everyone half-relied on ends, and no one decided to end it.
The closing is not a choice but a threshold crossed: the option needed a level of custom the free small choices did not supply, so it drops, all at once, for the users and the non-users alike. And because it cannot be cheaply revived, the loss is permanent the instant it lands, so the bereft meet not a decision they can argue but a vacancy already sealed, a thing gone that no one ever chose to lose.
