# Piece: The Sum

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The large outcome that assembles from the small choices, chosen by no one and made by all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the tally](place_the_tally.md), where it assembles.
The residue of a thousand small picks, settling the large question.

## Load Bearing

Without it the small choices would stay small, and no large thing would ride on them.
With it the picks become an outcome: the sum is the aggregate the small choices add to, a large result no chooser intended or was asked, wearing the shape of a decision and holding none of the deciding. The Sum is load bearing because it is where the harm arrives with no author (Schelling on the gap between micromotives and macrobehavior): it is produced by the structure and by no hand in it, so it can be laid at no one's door, and the outcome that looks most chosen is the one that was most purely a sum.

## Apparent

The will of the people, expressed.
A large outcome that emerged from everyone's free choices looks like a collective decision, what all of them wanted, so the sum reads as democracy in action, and the truth that no one was ever asked the whole, that many would have chosen otherwise had it been put to them directly, stays hidden behind the look of a verdict everyone helped reach.
