# Plot: The Tally

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The tally](place_the_tally.md), the small choices accumulating where no one is watching.
A thousand fine picks, each about itself, adding toward a large outcome, and no forum anywhere posing the whole.

## Action

[Summing](process_summing.md): [The Sum](persona_the_sum.md), holding [the aggregate's seat](position_aggregate.md), assembles the choices into a result no one chose, while [The Chooser](persona_the_chooser.md), holding [the chooser's seat](position_chooser.md), keeps choosing well and is never once asked the whole.

## Stage

[The Sum](persona_the_sum.md) tables [Add Up](plan_add_up.md) and settles the large question as [the sum](piece_the_sum.md), authored by no hand in it.
[The Chooser](persona_the_chooser.md) tables [Take the Convenient One](plan_take_the_convenient_one.md) again, and [the missing forum](piece_the_missing_forum.md) means no one ever puts the aggregate to it.

## Tension

A large outcome is being decided, and there is nowhere it is being decided.
The choices are right and the count is real, but no ballot exists for the whole, only for the small picks, so the aggregate falls out as their residue, settled by default because no forum settles it. The result will wear the face of a collective decision, and yet no one, at any point, was asked it, so the thing takes shape unchosen, in a place where it could not have been chosen otherwise.
