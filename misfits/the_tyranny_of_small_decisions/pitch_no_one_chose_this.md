# Pitch: No One Chose This

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Easy, sensible, unremarkable.
The tone of a small choice made well: the convenient one, the obvious one, the one anyone would make.
Nothing here feels like a decision at all, only a shrug and a preference.

## Undertow

Under the small shrugs, a large outcome assembling.
Each choice is right and free and about nothing but itself, and their sum is settling a question no one was ever asked.
The whole is being decided by default, as the residue of a thousand picks that were never about it.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching a thing quietly die that no one chose to end,
each defection reasonable, each chooser blameless,
and the vote on whether to keep it never once held.

## Echo

After the run: an option gone for good, killed by no one's decision,
a loss authored by nobody and assembled by everybody,
and the question of the whole raised at last, over the space where the thing used to be.
