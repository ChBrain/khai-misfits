# Position: Chooser

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The seat that makes each small choice, freely and well.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A small choice at each turn, cheap, reversible, and its own.
The convenient option, genuinely the better one for the one choosing.
No sight of the whole its choices sum to.

## Orders

Take the convenient option.
Choose freely and well, one small choice at a time.
Leave the large question alone: it is not on your ballot.

## Loses

Nothing on any single choice, and a share in an outcome it never chose.
Each pick is genuinely the better one, so its blamelessness is total and its part in the loss is real, and it is never once asked the whole it is helping to settle.
The most sensible small choice is the one that most surely settles the aggregate without anyone choosing it.

## Drives

Toward the convenient,
correctly, one small choice at a time,
so a thousand right small choices settle a large outcome no one was ever asked.
