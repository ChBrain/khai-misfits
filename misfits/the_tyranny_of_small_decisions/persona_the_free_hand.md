# Persona: The Free Hand

## Taxonomy

[Liberty](position_liberty.md). The defender of the small free choice, wary of any hand that would convene the whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Liberal, plainspoken, protective of the ordinary shrug.
Holds that most small decisions should stay small, made by the person making them, not gathered up and voted on.
Suspicious of the convener's forum as a tyranny of its own.

## Action

Defends the free small choice against being bundled into an aggregate someone else decides.
Warns that convening every sum smothers the liberty that most small choices rightly are.
Points out that the convener cannot know which aggregates matter, so its cure over-reaches by default.

## Shadow

Its case is true and is exactly what keeps the missing forum missing, so the option keeps dying on its watch.
It cannot tell, any better than the convener, the sum that will cost something irreplaceable from the sum that is harmless.
The honest guardian of free choice is the honest guardian of the tyranny free choices sum to.

## Tell

Says "you can't put every little choice to a committee."
Right about the danger of the cure, and holding the door for the disease.
