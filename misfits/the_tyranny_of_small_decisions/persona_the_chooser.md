# Persona: The Chooser

## Taxonomy

[Chooser](position_chooser.md). The one who makes each small choice, freely and well.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sensible, unhurried, choosing the obvious.
Takes the convenient option each time, as anyone would, and is right to.
Feels no weight in it: it is a small choice, cheap and reversible and its own.

## Action

Picks the convenient option at each turn, correctly and freely.
Withdraws, without meaning to, one more unit of the patronage the alternative needs.
Makes many small right choices, and is never once asked the large one they sum to.

## Shadow

Each of its choices is genuinely the better one, so its blamelessness is total and its contribution to the loss is real.
It is never presented with the whole, so it cannot weigh a thing it is helping to end.
The freer and more sensible each small pick, the more surely the aggregate is settled without anyone choosing it.

## Tell

Says "it's just easier this way, this once."
Of a this-once repeated by everyone until the alternative is gone.
