# Position: Aggregate

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The seat of the large outcome that assembles from the small choices, chosen by no one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A thousand small choices, each about itself and none about the whole.
The shape of a decision, and none of the deciding.
An author in the structure, and none among the hands.

## Orders

Add the small choices up.
Settle the large question by default, as their residue.
Present, at the end, an outcome everyone made and no one chose.

## Loses

Nothing of its own, and it can be laid at no one's door.
It has the look of a decision without the deciding, so the loss it carries is authored by the missing forum and by no hand in it, and cannot be charged to anyone.
The outcome that looks most chosen is the one that was most purely a sum.

## Drives

Toward the settled whole,
assembled from choices that were never about it,
so the large result wears the face of a decision that nobody ever made.
