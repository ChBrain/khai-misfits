# Position: Convener

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The seat that would put the whole to a real decision before it settles by default.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The reading that the large outcome is being decided by choices never about it.
The move to convene the aggregate: a vote on the whole, a price on the option, a bundle of the small into the big.
No way to spare the free small choices while overriding them.

## Orders

Convene the whole.
Put the large question actually to a vote, price the option so its value counts, bundle the small choices into the big one.
Make the outcome decided rather than merely summed.

## Loses

The free choices it overrides, and the certainty of which sums to override.
Its forum is the one move that could keep a thing worth keeping, and it works by taking the small decisions from the people making them, and it cannot know in advance which sums will cost something irreplaceable, so it must convene everything or guess.
The hand that would save the option by deciding the whole is the hand that takes the small liberties away.

## Drives

Toward the aggregate decided,
by overriding the free small choices it is made of,
so the cure for a tyranny of small decisions arrives as a tyranny of large ones.
