# Position: Bereft

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The seat of the one who valued the option and loses it with no say.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A real value placed on the option's being there.
No vote on the whole, only the small choices it made like everyone.
A loss it would have paid to prevent, discovered too late.

## Orders

Value the option, and assume it will keep on.
Take the convenient small choice yourself, like the rest.
Find, when it closes, that you would have kept it, and were never asked.

## Loses

The option, and its own innocence with it.
It is often a chooser too, so it is victim and author at once, having withdrawn its own small share of the custom that would have kept the thing it wanted kept, because the whole was never on the table.
The one who most wanted the option to stay is complicit in the sum that closed it.

## Drives

Toward the option kept,
which it never once chose to keep at any fork,
so the seat that would have paid for the whole is a seat that helped sum it away.
