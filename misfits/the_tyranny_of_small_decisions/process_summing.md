# Process: Summing

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The small choices accumulating into the aggregate, with no forum deciding the whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A stream of small choices, and no place where their sum is weighed.
At [the tally](place_the_tally.md), the picks accumulating while the large question is put to no one.

## Direction

Toward a large outcome settled by default.
The small choices add up, each about itself and none about the whole, so the aggregate is decided as their residue, and the big question, never posed at any forum, is answered by the sum without anyone choosing the answer.

## Lever

A missing forum: no moment where the whole is decided.
Because there is no ballot for the aggregate, only for the small choices, the outcome must fall out as their sum, so the settling needs no decision, only the absence of one at the level that matters.

## Echo

A large result no one chose and everyone made.
The whole settled by residue, the option kept or lost by a count no one kept, and an outcome wearing the face of a decision that was never taken.
