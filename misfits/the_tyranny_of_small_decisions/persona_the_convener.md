# Persona: The Convener

## Taxonomy

[Convener](position_convener.md). The one who would put the whole to a real decision before it is settled by default.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Farsighted, deliberate, uneasy with outcomes no one chose.
Wants the large question actually asked: should the option exist, and is it worth what keeping it costs.
Would build the forum the small choices never pass through.

## Action

Convenes the aggregate: puts the whole to a vote, prices the option so its value counts, bundles the small choices into the big one.
Makes the large outcome a thing decided rather than a thing that happened.
Overrides, to do it, the free small choices the aggregate is made of.

## Shadow

Its forum is the one move that could keep a thing worth keeping, and it works by overriding choices that were each free and correct.
It cannot know in advance which sums of small picks will cost something irreplaceable, so it must either convene everything or guess.
The hand that would save the option by deciding the whole is the hand that takes the small decisions away from the people making them.

## Tell

Says "let's actually decide this, together, before it decides itself."
Which is right, and is also a large decision imposed where a thousand small free ones were.
