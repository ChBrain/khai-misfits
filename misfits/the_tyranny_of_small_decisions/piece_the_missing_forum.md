# Piece: The Missing Forum

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The absent decision point: the large question that is never put to anyone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the tally](place_the_tally.md), where the whole is never posed.
In the gap where a forum for the aggregate should be, and is not.

## Load Bearing

Without it the whole would be decided directly, and the small choices could not tyrannise it.
With it the aggregate has nowhere to be chosen: the missing forum is the absence of any moment, vote, or place where the large outcome is weighed, so it falls out as the residue of small choices instead. The Missing Forum is load bearing because it is the distinctive engine of the misfit (Kahn's whole point): the trap is not that people choose badly but that the big question is never a question, so a thing no one would have chosen is settled by default, and there is nowhere it could have been decided otherwise.

## Apparent

Just the normal way of things.
No one misses a forum that was never there, so the absence of a decision point looks like the ordinary background of free life, and the truth that this exact absence is what lets small choices settle a large outcome, that the tyranny lives in a missing question and not a wrong answer, stays hidden behind how natural it is for most things to have no vote.

## Yearbook

Working through [summing](process_summing.md), the gap [Put the Whole to a Vote](plan_put_the_whole_to_a_vote.md) would fill and [Leave It Free](plan_leave_it_free.md) would keep.
It is the absence the misfit turns on: the Missing Forum lets [the small choice](piece_the_small_choice.md) settle the whole as [the sum](piece_the_sum.md), and is the very thing convening the aggregate must supply.
