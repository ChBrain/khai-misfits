# Piece: The Option Value

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The worth of the alternative's mere availability, held even by those who do not use it each time.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the vacancy](place_the_vacancy.md), where its worth shows only once it is gone.
In the being-there of the alternative, valued apart from any single use.

## Load Bearing

Without it the option's loss would cost only its users, and no more than they withdrew.
With it the loss is larger than the sum of the choices that caused it: the option value is the worth of the alternative being available, held even by those who chose the convenient thing this time, so more is destroyed than any chooser meant to withdraw. The Option Value is load bearing because it is what no small choice protects (Weisbrod on option value): it is not paid for by any single trip and cannot be defended trip by trip, so the thing valued for being there is precisely the thing that trip-by-trip choosing does not keep there.

## Apparent

Something for nothing, that no one will miss.
An alternative that many keep available but seldom use looks like idle capacity, worth only what its users pay, so the option value reads as negligible, and the truth that its availability is worth more than its use, and to more people than use it, stays hidden until the day it is gone and the worth of having had it shows all at once.

## Yearbook

Held by [the option](persona_the_option.md), tabled as [Stay Available](plan_stay_available.md).
It is the worth no small choice keeps: the Option Value is thinned by every [small choice](piece_the_small_choice.md) and, once the threshold is crossed, lost to [the irreversibility](piece_the_irreversibility.md), the value the whole misfit destroys without anyone spending it.
