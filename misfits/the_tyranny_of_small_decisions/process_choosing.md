# Process: Choosing

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The small rational choices being made, each correct, each about itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A fork at each turn, and a chooser free to take the better way.
At [the junction](place_the_junction.md), the convenient option offered against the one that keeps the alternative alive.

## Direction

Toward the convenient, freely and correctly.
Each chooser picks the option that is genuinely better for it, cheap and reversible and low in stakes, so the choices are made well, one at a time, and each withdraws a little of the custom the alternative lives on.

## Lever

A choice that is right on its own and blind to its sum.
Because every small pick is genuinely the better one and is never presented with the whole, the choosing needs no error and no greed, only free people each sensibly preferring the convenient thing in front of them.

## Echo

A stream of right small choices, and an option one patron lighter each time.
The convenient taken, the alternative thinned, and no chooser aware of, or asked about, the aggregate the choices are quietly building.
