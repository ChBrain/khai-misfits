# Plan: Put the Whole to a Vote

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the convener's seat](position_convener.md), inside [The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Convener](position_convener.md)

## Direction

Put the large question actually to a decision before it settles by default. Price the option so its value counts, bundle the small choices into the big one they add up to, and make the outcome a thing decided rather than a residue. Do it knowing you override the free choices the aggregate is made of.

## Orders

([The Convener](persona_the_convener.md) convenes the aggregate and overrides the free small choices, unable to know in advance which sums will cost something irreplaceable.)

## Implementation

([The Assembly](place_the_assembly.md); the [convening](process_convening.md) process; answering [the tyranny of small decisions](piece_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md) means putting the whole to a vote, at the cost of overriding [the free choice](piece_the_free_choice.md) it is built from.)

## Targets

- [ ] the large question is put to a real decision before it settles by default
- [ ] the option is priced so its value counts, the small choices bundled into the big
- [ ] the outcome is made decided rather than merely summed
- [ ] the cost is borne of overriding free choices, and of guessing which sums matter
