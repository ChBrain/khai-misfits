# Place: The Assembly

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). Where the remedy is weighed: convene the whole in time, or leave the small choices free.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A move to build the forum the small choices never pass through: the convener proposing to put the whole to a vote and price the option.
The free hand across the room, warning that gathering up the small choices is a tyranny of its own, and that most of them should stay small.

## Holds

The remedy and its cost.
Here the aggregate can be decided in time to keep a thing worth keeping, and here that means overriding free, sensible choices for a sum no one is sure of, so the assembly holds both the one forum that could save the option and the liberty it must take to do it.

## Offers

A way to decide the whole before it settles by default.
By convening the aggregate and pricing the option, the assembly offers the outcomes that matter a real decision, the chance to keep an alternative on purpose rather than lose it by residue.

## Withheld

A rule for which sums to convene, and a way to convene without overriding.
The assembly cannot tell in advance which aggregates will cost something irreplaceable, so it withholds the certainty its forum needs, and cannot gather the small choices into a decision without taking them from the people who were making them freely.
