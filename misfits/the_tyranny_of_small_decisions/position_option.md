# Position: Option

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The seat of the alternative, worth more than its use and kept alive only by enough choosers.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An availability that is worth having, used or not.
A threshold of custom it needs to keep existing.
No claim on any single choice, and no defender but the sum of them.

## Orders

Stay available.
Persist while enough small choices fall your way.
Offer the value of being there, to all, whether or not they come.

## Loses

Itself, all at once, when the patronage falls past its threshold.
Its worth is largely in its availability, which no single trip pays for and no small choice protects, so it needs a level of custom that free choices preferring the convenient will not supply.
The thing valued for being there is the thing that trip-by-trip choosing does not keep there.

## Drives

Toward remaining,
on a custom that free small choices withdraw,
so the alternative worth having available is the one that availability alone will not save.
