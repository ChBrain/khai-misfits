# Piece: The Tyranny of Small Decisions

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The phenomenon whole: a large outcome no one chose settled by small choices each of which was right, and its remedy in a convened decision at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the assembly](place_the_assembly.md), where the whole is decided or left to settle.
And across every fork, in each small choice made freely and never about the whole.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of choices and closings with no shape.
With it the moves become one law: the small choice, the missing forum, the sum, the option value, the irreversibility, and the free choice are facets of a single mechanism, in which a large question that is put to no one is settled by the residue of small choices that were never about it, so an option no one chose to end is thinned past saving and lost for all. The Tyranny of Small Decisions is load bearing because it is the whole finding with its whole remedy attached (Kahn; Schelling): the only answer is to convene the aggregate, price the option, put the whole to a real decision, and that overrides the free choices it is made of, cannot know which sums matter until the option is gone, and is a tyranny of large decisions raised against the small.

## Apparent

Either the free market speaking or a failure to be fixed by force.
Seen from one side the outcome is just what everyone freely chose; from the other, it is a market failure to be planned away; and both miss the structure, that each small choice was free and correct and the loss authored by no one, so neither celebrating the choices nor overriding them all answers it, only convening the aggregates that matter, which cannot be told from the ones that do not until the option they would have saved is already gone.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the convener](persona_the_convener.md) through [convening](process_convening.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: the Tyranny of Small Decisions is what [the small choice](piece_the_small_choice.md) becomes once [the missing forum](piece_the_missing_forum.md) leaves the whole undecided, undone only by convening the aggregate, at the cost of overriding [the free choice](piece_the_free_choice.md) and guessing which sums are worth it.
