# Persona: The Bereft

## Taxonomy

[Bereft](position_bereft.md). The one who valued the option and loses it with no say, the whole never put to them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Attached, quietly, to the thing being there.
Valued the option for its availability, used it seldom or often, and assumed it would keep on.
Never noticed the level dropping until it dropped past saving.

## Action

Loses the option when it closes, having had no vote on the whole.
Discovers, too late, that it would have paid to keep the thing it never chose to end.
Mourns an alternative gone for good, and a decision it was never offered.

## Shadow

It is, often, one of the choosers too, having taken the convenient option itself, so it is victim and author at once.
It would have kept the whole and did not keep any single small choice for it, because the whole was never on the table.
The one who most wanted the option to stay is complicit in the sum that closed it.

## Tell

Says "I'd have paid to keep it, if anyone had asked."
Of a thing no one was ever given the chance to keep.
