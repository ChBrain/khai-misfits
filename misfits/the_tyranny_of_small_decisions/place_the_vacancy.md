# Place: The Vacancy

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). Where the option used to be, gone for good, and the bereft find it too late.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The space where the thing was: the line closed, the shop shuttered, the meadow built on, the alternative simply no longer there.
The bereft arriving to find it gone, realising they would have kept it, and that no one ever asked them whether to.

## Holds

The irreversibility and the loss.
Here the option has dropped past saving and cannot be cheaply brought back, and here those who valued it discover the price they would have paid, so the vacancy holds both the permanence of the closing and the bereavement of the ones it was for.

## Offers

An honest reckoning of what the option was worth.
Only once it is gone does its value show plainly, so the vacancy offers, too late, the clearest account of what the alternative meant to the ones who assumed it would keep on.

## Withheld

The option, and the vote that might have kept it.
The vacancy is a closing already permanent, so it withholds any cheap return, and offers the large question only over the empty space, when deciding it can no longer save the thing it concerned.
