# Place: The Tally

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). Where the small choices sum, unwatched, into the aggregate no one chose.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A running count no one is keeping: a thousand small picks, each about itself, accumulating into a large outcome.
No ballot, no forum, no moment where the whole is weighed, only the residue of choices that were never about it, quietly settling the big question.

## Holds

The missing forum and the sum.
Here there is no place where the aggregate is decided, and here it assembles anyway, so the tally holds both the absence of any decision on the whole and the whole deciding itself, the forum that never convenes and the outcome that needs none.

## Offers

An outcome with the look of a verdict.
The tally presents a settled result, the option kept or lost, as though it had been chosen, so it offers a decision's face to a thing that was only ever a sum.

## Withheld

Any hand that decided it, and any moment it could have been decided.
The tally has no forum, so it withholds the point at which the whole might have been weighed, and the outcome can be laid at no one's door because no one, at any point, was ever asked it.
