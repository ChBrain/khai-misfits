# Place: The Junction

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). Where each small choice is made, the convenient against the one that keeps the alternative alive.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The fork at each turn: take the car or the train, the big store or the corner one, and each time the convenient way is the sensible way.
The chooser choosing well, and the option losing, quietly, one more unit of the custom it lives on.

## Holds

The small choice and the option it draws from.
Here the convenient is picked, correctly and freely, and here the alternative is thinned by the picking, so the junction holds both the right small choice and the slow withdrawal of the custom the option needs, the free pick and its unmeant cost.

## Offers

A choice that is easy, cheap, and one's own.
Each fork offers the convenient way, genuinely the better one for the one choosing, reversible and low in stakes, a small free decision with no weight to it.

## Withheld

The whole the small choice is part of.
The junction presents only this fork, never the aggregate it sums into, so it withholds the large question, and lets the chooser withdraw its share of the option's life without ever being shown what is at stake.
