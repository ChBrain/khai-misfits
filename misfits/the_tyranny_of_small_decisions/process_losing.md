# Process: Losing

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The option dropping past its threshold and closing, for everyone and for good.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A custom fallen below the level the option needs, and a threshold quietly crossed.
At [the vacancy](place_the_vacancy.md), the alternative closing and the bereft arriving too late.

## Direction

Toward an option gone irreversibly.
The patronage drops past the point the alternative can survive, so it closes, for everyone at once, including those who valued having it, and the loss cannot be cheaply undone, so the whole becomes a question only over the empty space.

## Lever

A threshold and an irreversibility.
Because the option needs a level of custom to exist and cannot be cheaply revived once shut, the losing turns a gradual thinning into a sudden and permanent absence, so the small defections cross, unnoticed, into a loss that no later choice can reverse.

## Echo

An alternative closed, and a vote arriving too late to save it.
The option gone for good, the bereft discovering what they would have paid, and the large question raised, if at all, only once it is moot.
