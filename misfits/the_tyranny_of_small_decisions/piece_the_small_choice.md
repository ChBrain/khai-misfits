# Piece: The Small Choice

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The individually rational, correct, low-stakes decision, made freely and about itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the junction](place_the_junction.md), where it is made.
The convenient option, taken at each fork, cheap and reversible.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no aggregate to sum, and no misfit.
With it the trouble is built from something genuinely good: the small choice is the convenient option, correct for the one choosing, low in stakes and its own, and it is made well every time. The Small Choice is load bearing because its rightness is the whole difficulty (Kahn): it is not an error or a greed to be corrected, so nothing about any single one is wrong, and the loss is assembled entirely from choices that were each exactly what they should have been.

## Apparent

Just a sensible preference.
A person taking the convenient way looks like nothing at all, a shrug rather than a decision, so the small choice reads as trivial, and the truth that it withdraws a share of an option's life and helps settle a large question it is never about stays hidden behind how obviously right and light it is.

## Yearbook

Made by [the chooser](persona_the_chooser.md) through [choosing](process_choosing.md), tabled as [Take the Convenient One](plan_take_the_convenient_one.md).
It is the right pick the misfit is built from: the Small Choice withdraws a little of [the option value](piece_the_option_value.md) and, multiplied where [the missing forum](piece_the_missing_forum.md) leaves it unwatched, becomes [the sum](piece_the_sum.md).
