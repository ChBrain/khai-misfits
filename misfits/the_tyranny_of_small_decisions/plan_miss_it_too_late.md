# Plan: Miss It Too Late

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the bereft's seat](position_bereft.md), inside [The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Bereft](position_bereft.md)

## Direction

Value the option, and assume it will keep on. Take the convenient small choice yourself, like the rest, and notice nothing until it closes. Then find that you would have paid to keep the thing you never chose to end, and were never asked, and mourn an alternative gone for good.

## Orders

([The Bereft](persona_the_bereft.md) loses the option with no vote on the whole, victim and author at once, having withdrawn its own small share of the custom that would have kept it.)

## Implementation

([The Vacancy](place_the_vacancy.md); the [losing](process_losing.md) process; [the irreversibility](piece_the_irreversibility.md) makes the loss permanent, so the vote to keep [the option value](piece_the_option_value.md) comes, if at all, only once it is past saving.)

## Targets

- [ ] the option is valued, and assumed to keep on
- [ ] the convenient small choice is taken by the one who valued it too
- [ ] the loss is discovered too late, the whole never having been asked
- [ ] the alternative, once gone, cannot be cheaply brought back
