# Plan: Leave It Free

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the liberty seat](position_liberty.md), inside [The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Liberty](position_liberty.md)

## Direction

Leave the small choice with the person making it. Defend it against being bundled and voted on, warn that convening every aggregate is a tyranny of its own, and hold that most small decisions rightly stay small. Point out that the convener cannot know which sums matter, so its cure over-reaches by default.

## Orders

([The Free Hand](persona_the_free_hand.md) defends the small free choice and, in doing so, holds the door for the sum that quietly closes the option.)
