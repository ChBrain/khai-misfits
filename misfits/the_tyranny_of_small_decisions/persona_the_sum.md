# Persona: The Sum

## Taxonomy

[Aggregate](position_aggregate.md). The large outcome that assembles from the small choices, chosen by no one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Faceless, cumulative, arriving unannounced.
Built from a thousand small picks, each about itself and none about the whole.
Wears the look of a decision, though no one made it.

## Action

Adds the small choices into an outcome no chooser intended or was asked.
Settles the large question by default, as the residue of choices that were never about it.
Presents, at the end, a result that everybody helped make and nobody chose.

## Shadow

It has the shape of a decision and none of the deciding, so it can be laid at no one's door.
It is authored by the structure, the missing forum, and not by any hand in it.
The outcome that looks most chosen is the one that was most purely a sum.

## Tell

Says "well, this is what everyone wanted, apparently."
Of a thing no one was ever actually asked whether they wanted.
