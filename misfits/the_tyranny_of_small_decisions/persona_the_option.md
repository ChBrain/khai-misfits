# Persona: The Option

## Taxonomy

[Option](position_option.md). The alternative kept alive only by enough choosers, and worth more than its use.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Standing by, available, quietly load-bearing.
The train that still runs, the shop still open, the meadow not yet built on: there when wanted, and worth having there even when not.
Asks only for enough patronage to clear the bar it needs to survive.

## Action

Persists as long as enough small choices fall its way.
Thins as each defection withdraws a little of the custom that sustains it.
Drops, when the patronage falls past its threshold, out of existence, for everyone at once.

## Shadow

Its worth is largely in its availability, which no single trip pays for and no small choice protects.
It needs a level of custom that free small choices, each preferring the convenient, will not reliably supply.
The thing valued for being there is the thing that no one, choosing trip by trip, keeps there.

## Tell

Says "I'm still here, for now, if you want me."
To a stream of choosers each reasonably going the other way.
