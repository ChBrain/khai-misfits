# Plot: The Too-Late

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The vacancy](place_the_vacancy.md), the whole surfacing as a question once it is moot.
Over the empty space, the large decision no one was ever offered finally comes up, and the answer, now, would have been to keep it.

## Action

[Losing](process_losing.md) come to its reckoning: [The Bereft](persona_the_bereft.md), holding [the bereft's seat](position_bereft.md), realises it would have paid to keep the option, while [The Sum](persona_the_sum.md), holding [the aggregate's seat](position_aggregate.md), stands as a thing nobody chose and everybody made.

## Stage

[The Bereft](persona_the_bereft.md) tables [Miss It Too Late](plan_miss_it_too_late.md) and sees it was a chooser too, its own small picks part of [the sum](piece_the_sum.md).
[The Sum](persona_the_sum.md) tables [Add Up](plan_add_up.md), the settled outcome now plain, and no one to charge it to.

## Tension

The people who would have kept the option are the people who summed it away.
Put the whole to them directly and they would have voted to keep it; put it to them as a thousand small forks and each, freely and rightly, chose the convenient thing. So victim and author are the same, and the grievance has no target: not a villain, not a bad choice, only a large decision that was never a decision, faced at last over the space where its subject used to be, too late to answer.
