# Process: Convening

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of Small Decisions](play_the_tyranny_of_small_decisions.md). The move to decide the whole in time, and the liberty it costs.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A recognition that the aggregate is being settled by choices never about it, and a will to decide it on purpose.
At [the assembly](place_the_assembly.md), the convener proposing the forum and the free hand counting its cost.

## Direction

Toward the whole decided rather than summed.
The large question is put to a real vote, the option is priced so its value counts, and the small choices are bundled into the big one, so an alternative worth keeping can be kept on purpose instead of lost by residue.

## Lever

A forum that overrides the free small choices it gathers.
Because the outcome falls out as a sum only for want of a decision, convening one can catch it, but only by taking the small choices from the people making them, and only by guessing which sums are worth the taking, so the cure works by the very overriding that makes it a cost.

## Echo

An option kept on purpose, or a small liberty smothered to keep it.
The whole decided in time and the alternative saved, or free choices overridden for a sum that did not need it, so the convening ends either in a thing kept worth keeping or in a tyranny of large decisions raised against the small.
