# Piece: The Averted Fire

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The counterfactual, the crisis prevention stopped from ever starting, the non-event no one can point to or cheer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Made in [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), where the prevention that spares it is done.
Absent from [the podium](place_the_podium.md), where it can never be named or honoured.

## Load Bearing

Without it prevention is just unrewarded work, and the trap loses the reason the reward cannot find it.
With it the asymmetry has its cause: the success of prevention is a fire that never lit, a non-event, so there is nothing to measure, point to, or celebrate, and the reward, needing evidence, passes it by. The Averted Fire is load bearing because it is the invisible pole of the asymmetry (Repenning & Sterman): the achievement that consists in nothing happening, so the piece carries the counterfactual whose very success is its invisibility, the greater work that can never be shown against the lesser one that always can.

## Apparent

A quiet quarter, an ordinary stretch where nothing much happened, nothing to write home about.
Seen from outside, the averted fire looks like an absence of achievement, a period where the preventer simply had little to do, and the truth that the nothing that happened was the whole achievement, that a fire kept from lighting is worth more than one heroically put out, stays hidden behind how much like idleness a crisis successfully prevented appears.

## Yearbook

Produced by [the preventer](persona_the_preventer.md), the non-event that earns no [credit](piece_the_credit.md).
It is the invisible half of the trap: the Averted Fire is the greater achievement that cannot be seen beside [the fire](piece_the_fire.md) fought, so it wins nothing and its work starves through [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md), credited at last only when [the firebreak](piece_the_firebreak.md) chooses to honour the crisis that never came.
