# Piece: The Spiral

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The reinforcing loop and its tipping point, starved prevention breeding more fires and more fires starving prevention.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Turns through [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), where prevention is drained, and onto [the floor](place_the_floor.md), where the extra fires burn.
The motion of the whole system between the two.

## Load Bearing

Without it the reward asymmetry is a one-time unfairness, and the place stays where it started.
With it the trap becomes a trap: less prevention means fires start faster, and faster fires leave still less time for prevention, so the loss compounds and the system crosses from a self-sustaining improvement regime into a self-sustaining firefighting one. The Spiral is load bearing because it is the bistable dynamic that makes the misfit permanent (Repenning & Sterman, 2002): the tipping point past which firefighting sustains itself by consuming the capacity to escape, so the piece carries the loop whose crossing turns a bad allocation into a burning equilibrium.

## Apparent

A busy patch, a run of bad luck, more crises than usual that surely will pass.
Seen from inside, the spiral looks like a temporary spike in emergencies, a hard season that hard work will see through, and the truth that the extra fires are made by the prevention the last fires starved, that hard work at firefighting is what deepens it, that the system has tipped into a regime it will not drift back out of, stays hidden behind how much like a passing rough patch a tipping loop first appears.

## Yearbook

Driven by [the manager](persona_the_manager.md) and the whole floor through [starving](process_starving.md), the loop that compounds the loss.
It is the engine that locks the trap: the Spiral turns starved prevention into more [fire](piece_the_fire.md) and more fire into more starving, tipping the system past the point [the firebreak](piece_the_firebreak.md) alone, fencing capacity, can bring it back from.
