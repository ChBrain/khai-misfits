# Place: The Workshop

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). Where prevention would be done, the quiet room the capability is built in and the fires headed off, and where the marshal would fence the time.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The room away from the blaze where the dull unglamorous work is done, the process tightened, the failing thing caught, the capability built.
The preventer at it while it can, its hours thinning as the fires pull them away, and, at the last, the marshal trying to fence a share of them back.

## Holds

The starving and the fencing, the prevention drained and the capacity held.
Here the work that ends the fires meets the pull that empties it, so the Workshop holds the point where the loop is fed or broken: prevention starves as its hours drain to the blaze and the fires come faster, or a share of capacity is fenced against them and the fires begin to thin, so it is where the system tips one way or the other.

## Offers

The capability that would end the fires.
Because the Workshop is where prevention is done and the gaps are closed, it offers the place its one escape from the blaze, the capacity built that keeps the fires from lighting, so it hands out the work that would turn the loop back, and hands it out unrewarded and first to be raided when a fire flares.

## Withheld

The hours it needs, unless they are fenced.
The Workshop cannot hold its own capacity against a floor that raids it for every fire, so it withholds nothing willingly and loses everything by default, its time drained to the blaze, and only a deliberate fence, a marshal spending present drama to hold the hours, keeps it the room where the fires are ended rather than an empty one.
