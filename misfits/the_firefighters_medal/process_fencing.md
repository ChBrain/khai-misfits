# Process: Fencing

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The holding of capacity for prevention and the crediting of the crisis that never came, the remedy that turns the loop back, at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A place ablaze and decorating itself for it, and someone who sees the way out is to build the capability the fires eat.
At [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), the marshal fencing a share of capacity against the blaze and crediting the quiet work.

## Direction

Toward prevention protected and the fires thinning.
The marshal holds hours for prevention that the fires may not raid and honours the crisis that never came, so fencing drives capacity back into the work that ends the blaze, and the fires, headed off before they light, begin to come less often, the loop turning back the way it tipped.

## Lever

The plain truth that only the capability the blaze eats can end it.
Because a system that keeps fighting fires stays ablaze and a system that builds prevention stops, the case to fence is sound, so fencing runs on the same sense the firefighting denies, turned to the hard discipline of protecting the invisible work.

## Echo

Fires thinning, at the price of a present fire and the glory withheld.
The prevention has its fenced hours and the quiet quarter its credit, and a current fire was let burn a little and a medal went to the person nothing happened to, so what reads as letting a crisis smoulder and snubbing a hero is, in truth, the one move that stops the fires the reward system keeps lighting.
