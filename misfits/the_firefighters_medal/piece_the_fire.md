# Piece: The Fire

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The visible crisis, the vivid emergency that demands an answer now.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Flares on [the floor](place_the_floor.md), where the work runs and the crises erupt.
Fought in full view, the save it draws retold at [the podium](place_the_podium.md).

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to fight and nothing to reward, and prevention is simply diligence with no drama beside it.
With it the whole trap has its occasion: a vivid emergency that must be answered now, provable and urgent, so that the effort it pulls and the glory it earns are both undeniable. The Fire is load bearing because it is the visible pole of the asymmetry (Repenning & Sterman; Bohn): the crisis you can point to, fight, and honour, so the piece carries the emergency whose vividness is exactly what the prevented one lacks, the seen thing against which prevention is invisible.

## Apparent

A test of the team, a chance to show what it is made of, the very stuff of a good day's heroism.
Seen at the surface, the fire looks like an occasion for excellence, a crisis met and beaten, and the truth that each one fought is one prevention might have spared, that the floor's talent for fighting fires is what keeps its fires burning, stays hidden behind how bracing and how honourable a crisis well met appears.

## Yearbook

Fought by [the firefighter](persona_the_firefighter.md) through [firefighting](process_firefighting.md), the visible crisis that pulls the effort.
It is the occasion of the trap: the Fire draws the hours from prevention and wins [the credit](piece_the_credit.md) for its save, the vivid twin of [the averted fire](piece_the_averted_fire.md) no one can see, multiplying through [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md) until only [the firebreak](piece_the_firebreak.md) thins it.
