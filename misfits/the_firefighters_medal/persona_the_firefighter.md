# Persona: The Firefighter

## Taxonomy

[Firefighter](position_firefighter.md). Charges into the crisis and makes the visible save, genuinely good, and is the hero the medal goes to.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Tireless. Brilliant in the emergency.
Is the one who runs toward the fire when it flares, works the night, and pulls the launch or the account back from the edge.
No grandstanding in it: only a genuinely good hand doing hard, real, visible work under pressure.

## Action

Puts out the fire that is burning, dramatically and well, and is seen doing it.
Saves the day when the day needs saving, and earns the medal that the save deserves.
Does what a competent firefighter does: answers the crisis and wins.

## Shadow

Not a glory-hound, a genuine hero, and that is the whole difficulty: honouring the save is right, and honouring it is what draws everyone toward the fire.
Because the rescue is vivid and provable where prevention is a non-event, the Firefighter is the visibly best worker in the place, so the reward that rightly finds it is the reward that teaches all the others to seek the blaze rather than prevent it, and its brilliance in the emergency is what makes firefighting the path to standing.
It deserves the medal, and the medal deserved is the signal that lights the next fire.

## Tell

Says "someone had to put it out, and I did."
Did, and did it so well that everyone now wants a fire of their own to put out.
