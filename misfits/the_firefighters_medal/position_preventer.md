# Position: Preventer

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The seat of the invisible prevention, most valuable and least rewarded, whose starving feeds the blaze.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The quiet work that stops fires from starting, and, for all of it, a nothing to show: an absence of crisis.
The capability that keeps the place from burning, built where no one is looking.
No event to point to, no save to be seen making, and no medal coming.

## Orders

Head it off.
Tighten the process, check the thing that would have failed, close the gaps, and keep the fires from ever lighting.
Do the dull unglamorous work that means the day never needs saving.

## Loses

Its reward and then its hours, exactly because its success is a non-event.
Because the fire it spared cannot be seen or cheered, the seat earns no medal, and watching the glory go to the save it feels the pull toward the fire, so it cannot both do the unrewarded prevention and hold its effort against the reward flowing elsewhere, and its calendar drains into the blaze.
It is the most valuable worker in the place and the least honoured, and starved of its time the fires it held back begin to come.

## Drives

Toward prevention done and unrewarded,
most valuable and least seen,
so the work that would end the fires is the work whose starving lights them.
