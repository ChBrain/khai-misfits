# Place: The Floor

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). Where the work runs and the fires break out, the operations floor the crises flare on and the saves are made.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the work is done and the crises erupt, a floor under constant deadline where a fire can flare at any hour.
The firefighter charging in when one does, working the night in full view, and the manager pulling whatever effort the blaze demands.

## Holds

The firefighting, and the effort it pulls.
Here the crisis meets the response, so the Floor holds the point where the urgent claims the hours: a fire flares and must be answered now, so it is where effort is triaged off prevention and onto the blaze, the present emergency always louder than the distant one.

## Offers

A stage for the visible save.
Because the Floor is where the fires burn and are fought in full view, it offers the firefighter its rescue in plain sight, so it hands out the vivid, provable win that the medal will find, and hands it out at the cost of the hours it pulled from the prevention no one was watching.

## Withheld

The time and the calm the work needs to stop burning.
The Floor cannot both answer every present fire and hold capacity for the prevention that would end them, so it withholds the fenced hours prevention needs, letting the urgent devour them, and, all drama and no quiet, it withholds any sign that the fires it fights so well are the ones its own triage keeps lighting.
