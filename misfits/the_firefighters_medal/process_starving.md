# Process: Starving

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The draining of prevention as effort follows the reward, the reinforcing loop that tips the system into a self-sustaining blaze.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A reward that flows to the fire and hours that follow the reward.
At [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), the preventer's time thinning as it drains toward the firefighting that pays, prevention losing the capacity it was built on.

## Direction

Toward prevention emptied and the fires multiplying.
Effort follows the glory and the urgency, so starving drives the hours from prevention into firefighting, and prevention, being where capability is built, takes the loss twice: less of it lights the fires faster, and faster fires leave still less time for it, the loop tightening until the system tips into a self-sustaining blaze.

## Lever

The rationality of going where the reward and the safety are.
Because people follow the glory and the pressed hour favours the present fire, each drain of time from prevention to firefighting is a sensible individual move, so starving runs on reasonable choices, the loop assembled from no one starving prevention on purpose.

## Echo

A place ablaze and heroic and improving nothing.
Prevention has emptied into firefighting and the fires come faster and the medals multiply with them, and the system has crossed from improving to burning, so what reads as a floor rising ever more heroically to ever more crises is, in truth, a capability trap consuming the very capacity that would let it escape.
