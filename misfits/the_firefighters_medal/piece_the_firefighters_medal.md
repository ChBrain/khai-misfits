# Piece: The Firefighter's Medal

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The phenomenon whole, a system that decorates the save, starves the prevention, and burns.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Everywhere the production runs: from [the floor](place_the_floor.md) to [the podium](place_the_podium.md) to [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), the three places of the loop.
It is the whole arc, the visible save honoured, the invisible prevention starved, the fires multiplying under the medals.

## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without a name: a fire, a credit, a non-event, a loop, and no account of how one feeds the next.
With it the misfit is whole: the firefighter's medal is the trap entire, a reward that can find only the visible save and so starves the prevention that would end the fires, the heroism and the blaze shown to be one thing. It is load bearing because it is the structure the whole production exists to stage (Repenning & Sterman): the frame that binds the visible fire, the asymmetric credit, the invisible averted fire, the tipping spiral, and the costly firebreak into a single bend, so the piece carries the phenomenon as a whole, a system that rewards its way from improving into burning.

## Apparent

A place of heroes, an organisation that meets its crises magnificently and honours those who do.
Seen at the surface, the firefighter's medal looks like an excellent, resilient team, decorated for its saves and rising to every emergency, and the truth that the emergencies are made by the prevention the reward starves, that the busiest and most decorated floor is the one improving least, that a place of heroes can be a place perpetually on fire, stays hidden behind how admirable a team of firefighters appears.

## Yearbook

The whole of it: the Firefighter's Medal is [the credit](piece_the_credit.md) that finds [the fire](piece_the_fire.md) fought and never [the averted fire](piece_the_averted_fire.md) prevented, draining prevention through [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md) into a permanent blaze, turned back only by [the firebreak](piece_the_firebreak.md) that credits the calm at a cost.
It is the misfit named: a system that decorates the save and starves the prevention and burns, no cynic in it and no free hero, the work that would end the fires the work no one is thanked for.
