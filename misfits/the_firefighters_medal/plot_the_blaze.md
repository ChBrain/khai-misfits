## Taxonomy

## Owner

## Cue

[The Floor](place_the_floor.md), a crisis flaring and demanding an answer now.
The deadline pressing, the fire real, the effort meant for prevention there to be pulled.

## Action

[Firefighting](process_firefighting.md): [The Manager](persona_the_manager.md), holding [the manager's seat](position_manager.md), diverts effort to the current fire, and [The Firefighter](persona_the_firefighter.md), holding [the firefighter's seat](position_firefighter.md), charges in to put it out.

## Stage

[The Manager](persona_the_manager.md), on [Fight the Fire](plan_fight_the_fire.md), answers the crisis in front of it and pulls whatever hours the blaze demands, the urgent always louder than the distant.
[The Firefighter](persona_the_firefighter.md), on [Save the Day](plan_save_the_day.md), runs toward the fire and begins the work of putting it out, in full view.

## Tension

Answering the fire that burns is right, and the answering empties the work that would end the fires.
There is nothing to fault: the crisis is real and urgent, and a pressed manager must triage to it. But the hours it takes come from the prevention that had no deadline, so the plot closes with the fire being fought and the quiet work quietly emptied, the response to this blaze feeding the next.
