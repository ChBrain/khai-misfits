# Place: The Podium

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). Where the credit is given, the stage the medal is pinned on and the save is celebrated.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place of recognition, the all-hands and the award, where the save is retold and the hero is named and the medal is pinned.
The firefighter honoured before everyone, and the preventer in the audience with nothing to be called up for, its quarter recorded as quiet.

## Holds

The rewarding, and the asymmetry it enacts.
Here the visible save meets the recognition, so the Podium holds the point where credit goes only to what can be seen: the fought fire is retold and honoured while the spared one cannot even be named, so it is where the reward system, doing the only thing it can, teaches the whole place that glory lives in the blaze.

## Offers

Honour, to whatever can be pointed to.
Because the Podium can only celebrate what it can name, it offers its medal to the vivid rescue, so it hands out the recognition that rightly finds the hero, and hands it out where the prevented crisis, being a non-event, can never stand, so the honour it gives is honour the invisible can never win.

## Withheld

A medal for the fire that never lit.
The Podium cannot celebrate a disaster that did not happen, so it withholds all recognition from the preventer whose success is that nothing occurred, and, able to name only the save, it withholds from the place the one lesson that would save it, that the empty calendar was the greater achievement.
