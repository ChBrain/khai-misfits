# Persona: The Manager

## Taxonomy

[Manager](position_manager.md). Triages effort to the current fire and pins the medal on the visible save, reasonably, and by both starves prevention.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Pressed. Fair-minded. Set on rewarding the best.
Runs the floor under constant deadline, answers the crisis in front of it, and honours the one who charges in and saves the day.
No cynicism in it: only a manager doing the urgent thing and crediting the visible good.

## Action

Diverts effort from prevention to fight the current fire, and pins the medal on the save that everyone can see.
Triages the urgent over the distant, and rewards the measurable rescue over the unmeasurable prevention.
Does what a pressed, fair manager does: answers the emergency and honours the hero.

## Shadow

Not cynical, fair: it cannot reward a fire that never lit, and it cannot ignore the one burning now, so each choice is the only reasonable one.
Because it credits the save it can see and starves the prevention it cannot, and pulls hours to the urgent fire from the distant one, the Manager's fairness is what bends effort toward firefighting and its triage is what feeds the blaze, and it reads the worsening not as its own reward system but as a people problem.
It rewards competence and, rewarding what it can see, rewards its way into a permanent fire.

## Tell

Says "I reward results, and the save was a real result."
Pins the medal, and teaches everyone that the way to be valued is to be seen putting out a fire.
