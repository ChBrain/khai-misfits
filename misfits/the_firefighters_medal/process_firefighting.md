# Process: Firefighting

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The answering of the current crisis, effort pulled to the blaze, necessary in the moment and the draw that starves prevention.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A fire flaring on a floor under deadline.
At [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the crisis erupting and demanding an answer now, the firefighter charging in and the manager pulling the effort it needs.

## Direction

Toward the current fire put out, and the hours drawn onto it.
The crisis is urgent and vivid, so firefighting drives effort to the blaze in front of it, the launch saved and the day won, and the hours it takes are pulled from the prevention that had no such deadline.

## Lever

The urgency and the reality of the fire.
Because the crisis is genuinely burning and the save genuinely needed, answering it is the only responsible thing, so firefighting runs on real necessity, the draw of effort to the blaze made of emergencies no one can ignore.

## Echo

A fire out, a day saved, and prevention a little emptier.
The crisis has been answered and the save is there for all to see, and the hours it took came from the quiet work that would have kept the next fire from lighting, so what reads as a floor rising heroically to its emergencies is, unseen, the draw that feeds the blaze.
