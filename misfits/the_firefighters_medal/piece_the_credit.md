# Piece: The Credit

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The reward asymmetry, the recognition that flows to the measurable save and never to the unobservable prevention.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Given at [the podium](place_the_podium.md), pinned on the visible save.
Never reaches [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), where the prevention that earns none is done.

## Load Bearing

Without it the fire is just fought and the prevention just done, and effort has no reason to prefer the one.
With it the whole trap has its fulcrum: recognition that can find the save and not the prevention, so effort follows it to the blaze. The Credit is load bearing because it is the asymmetry that bends the effort (Repenning & Sterman): the glory goes where the evidence is, and the evidence is the fought fire, so the piece carries the reward that, doing the only thing a reward can, teaches every worker that the way to be valued is to be seen fighting a fire.

## Apparent

Simple fairness, rewarding results, honouring the person who plainly did the good.
Seen alone, the credit looks like merit properly recognised, the save honoured because the save was real, and the truth that it can only ever find the visible, that the greater achievement of the prevented crisis can never be credited, that the fair reward is the one that starves prevention, stays hidden behind how just it is to honour a real and visible result.

## Yearbook

Pinned by [the manager](persona_the_manager.md) through [rewarding](process_rewarding.md), the recognition that finds only the seen.
It is the fulcrum of the trap: the Credit goes to [the fire](piece_the_fire.md) fought and never to [the averted fire](piece_the_averted_fire.md), so effort drains through [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md) toward the blaze, until [the firebreak](piece_the_firebreak.md) chooses to credit the quiet at a cost.
