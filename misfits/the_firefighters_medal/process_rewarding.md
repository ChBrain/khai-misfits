# Process: Rewarding

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The pinning of credit on the visible save, fair against a prevention that cannot be seen, and the signal that draws effort to the blaze.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A save made in full view and a prevention that produced nothing to show.
At [the podium](place_the_podium.md), the rescue retold and the hero named, and the preventer's quiet quarter with nothing to be called up for.

## Direction

Toward glory pinned on the seen and passed over the unseen.
The save is vivid and provable and the prevention is a non-event, so rewarding drives the medal to the firefighter and nothing to the preventer, and, doing the only thing a reward can, teaches the whole place that recognition lives in the fire.

## Lever

The observability of the save against the invisibility of the spared fire.
Because you can measure the fire fought and never the fire that never lit, crediting the save and not the prevention is not spite but the plain difficulty of rewarding a counterfactual, so rewarding runs on a genuine asymmetry, the glory going where the evidence is.

## Echo

A hero honoured, and a lesson learned by all.
The firefighter has its medal and deserves it, and everyone has seen where the glory goes, so what reads as a place rightly celebrating its best is, unseen, the signal bending every worker's effort toward the fires and away from the prevention that earns nothing.
