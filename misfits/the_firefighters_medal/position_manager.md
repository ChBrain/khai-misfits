# Position: Manager

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The seat that triages to the fire and rewards the save, fair for each call and inflaming for all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A floor under deadline, a fire to answer now, and a save to honour when it comes.
A sound basis for each: the urgent crisis is real and the visible rescue is a real result.
No way to measure the fire that never lit, only the one fought.

## Orders

Fight the fire.
Answer the crisis in front of you, pull the effort it needs, and pin the medal on the save that everyone can see.
Reward the results you can point to, the way any fair manager would.

## Loses

The prevention it cannot see, exactly by rewarding the save it can.
Because it credits the measurable rescue and starves the unmeasurable prevention, and triages hours from the distant fire to the present one, the seat cannot both reward what it can point to and keep the effort flowing to the work that ends the fires, so its fairness bends the place toward firefighting.
It rewards competence and, crediting only the visible, rewards its way into a permanent blaze it misreads as a people problem.

## Drives

Toward effort triaged to the fire and glory pinned on the save,
fair for each call and inflaming in the sum,
so the reward meant to honour the best is what starves the work that would end the fires.
