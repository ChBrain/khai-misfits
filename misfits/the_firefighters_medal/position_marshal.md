# Position: Marshal

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The seat that would fence prevention and credit the crisis that never came, at the cost of a present fire and the visible glory.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A lever over capacity and reward: the power to fence prevention time and to credit the quiet work.
A clear case: the way out of the blaze is to build the capability the fires keep eating.
No way to make prevention visible, and no cover from the charge of letting a fire burn.

## Orders

Fence the time.
Hold a share of capacity for prevention that fires may not touch, credit the crisis that never came, and honour the empty calendar as much as the heroic night.
Pay the visible for the invisible, and turn the loop back.

## Loses

The present fire's quick answer and the glory of the save, exactly by ending the blaze.
Because fencing prevention means letting a current fire burn a little, and crediting a non-event means rewarding what cannot be proven, and pinning the medal on the preventer means withholding it from the visible hero, the seat cannot break the loop without spending real capacity and real glory on fires that will now never light.
It buys the end of the blaze against every pull toward the drama, and is taken, for a while, as the one who let a fire burn and snubbed a hero.

## Drives

Toward capacity fenced and the calm credited,
bought against a present fire and the medal the drama demands,
so the one seat that would end the fires must reward the work no one can see.
