# Persona: The Marshal

## Taxonomy

[Marshal](position_marshal.md). Would fence capacity for prevention even while fires burn and credit the crisis that never came, against every pull toward the save.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady. Unsentimental about drama. Willing to reward the calm.
Sees the place burning and decorating itself for it, and moves to protect a share of prevention and to honour the fire that never lit.
No indifference to the crisis in it: only someone who knows the way out is to build the capability the blaze keeps eating.

## Action

Fences a share of capacity for prevention that fires may not touch, and credits the quiet work and the crisis that never came.
Honours the empty calendar as much as the heroic night, and pins the medal, sometimes, on the person nothing happened to.
Does the one thing that turns the loop back: pays the visible for the invisible.

## Shadow

Not a saviour: fencing prevention means letting a present fire burn a little, and crediting a non-event means rewarding what cannot be proven.
Because holding capacity back while a crisis rages looks like negligence, and honouring the preventer over the visible hero looks like snubbing real merit, and a threat, a fenced hour spent, is one it must truly hold against the pull of the blaze, the Marshal cannot break the loop without spending real capacity and real glory on fires that will now never light and can never be shown.
It can end the blaze, only by rewarding the calm no one can see and withholding the medal the drama demands.

## Tell

Says "credit the quarter where nothing caught fire, and hold the hours to keep it that way."
Is right, and is heard, at first, as the one who would let a fire burn and snub a hero.
