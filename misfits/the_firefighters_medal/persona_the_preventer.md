# Persona: The Preventer

## Taxonomy

[Preventer](position_preventer.md). Does the quiet work that stops fires from starting, so nothing happens, so no one notices, and no medal comes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent. Unseen. The reason for a calm no one remarks on.
Tightens the process, checks the thing that would have failed, does the dull unglamorous work of prevention, and the crises that would have come do not.
No showiness in it: only someone quietly keeping the fires from ever lighting.

## Action

Heads off the crisis before it starts, so the day never needs saving.
Builds the capability and closes the gaps, and produces, for all its labour, a nothing: an absence of fire.
Does the one kind of work that would end the blaze, and has nothing to show for it.

## Shadow

Not idle, invisible: its whole success is that nothing happens, and nothing happening is exactly what cannot be seen or cheered.
Because the fire it spared is a non-event no one can point to, the Preventer earns no medal and, being human, watches the glory go to the save and feels the pull of it, so its hours drain toward the firefighting that pays and its prevention starves, and starved, the fires it held back begin to come.
It is the most valuable worker in the place and the least rewarded, and the gap between the two is what empties its calendar into the blaze.

## Tell

Says "nothing went wrong on my watch," and means it as an achievement.
Is heard as having had a quiet quarter, while the firefighter down the hall is decorated.
