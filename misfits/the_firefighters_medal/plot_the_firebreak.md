## Action

## Stage

[The Marshal](persona_the_marshal.md), having tabled [Fence the Time](plan_fence_the_time.md), holds a share of capacity for prevention that the fires may not raid and honours the empty calendar as much as the heroic night.
[The Manager](persona_the_manager.md), still on [Fight the Fire](plan_fight_the_fire.md), feels every reason to raid the fenced hours for the fire burning now and to give the medal to the visible hero.

## Tension

The one move that ends the fires is the one that looks like letting one burn and snubbing a hero.
There is no easy remedy: fencing prevention plainly turns the loop back, yet it means letting a present fire burn a little, rewarding a non-event that cannot be proven, and withholding the medal from the visible save, against every pull toward the drama. So the play closes where it must, the prevention fenced and the fires thinning ... or the current fire is fought once more, the medal pinned once more, and the blaze runs on.
