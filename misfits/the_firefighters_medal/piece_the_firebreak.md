# Piece: The Firebreak

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The remedy, the fenced capacity and counterfactual credit that reverse the loop, at the cost of a present fire and the visible glory.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Held at [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), where the fenced hours protect the prevention.
Weighed against [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the blaze that would raid those hours if the fence did not hold.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play has no answer, and the spiral runs to a floor forever ablaze.
With it the misfit has its costly cure: a share of capacity fenced for prevention that the fires may not touch, and credit given to the crisis that never came, so the loop turns back and the fires begin to thin. The Firebreak is load bearing because it is the remedy that carries its own price (Bohn; Repenning & Sterman): it works only by letting a present fire burn a little and by honouring the person nothing happened to, so the piece carries both the cure for the blaze and the drama and glory it must spend to hold.

## Apparent

Negligence, an idle reserve, a manager withholding hands from a crisis and a medal from a hero.
Seen with a fire burning, the firebreak looks like dereliction, capacity kept back while the floor needs everyone and honour given to someone who did nothing visible, and the truth that the fenced hours are the only thing that ends the fires, that the calm credited is the greater achievement, stays hidden behind how much like negligence holding capacity from a blaze appears.

## Yearbook

Held by [the marshal](persona_the_marshal.md) through [fencing](process_fencing.md), the capacity fenced and the calm credited.
It is the cure the play cannot make painless: the Firebreak protects the prevention that [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md) would drain and credits [the averted fire](piece_the_averted_fire.md) that [the credit](piece_the_credit.md) never finds, and cannot do so without letting a [fire](piece_the_fire.md) burn a little and withholding the medal the drama demands.
