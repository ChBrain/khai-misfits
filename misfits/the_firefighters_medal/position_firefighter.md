# Position: Firefighter

## Taxonomy

[The Firefighter's Medal](play_the_firefighters_medal.md). The seat of the visible save, genuinely good, that the medal rightly finds and wrongly teaches.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A crisis to answer, the skill to answer it, and a rescue everyone can see.
A real result to show: the fire fought, the day saved, the win provable and plain.
No way to be less visible than the save makes it, and no wish to be.

## Orders

Save the day.
Charge into the fire, work the night, and pull the launch or the account back from the edge.
Win the emergency, visibly and well, and earn the medal the save deserves.

## Loses

Nothing of its own, and it is the reward's undoing all the same.
Because the rescue is vivid where prevention is a non-event, the seat is the visibly best worker in the place, so the medal that rightly finds it is the signal that teaches all the others to seek the blaze, and it cannot both deserve the honour and keep the honour from lighting the next fire.
It does real good and wins real glory, and the glory it wins is the beacon that draws the effort toward the fires.

## Drives

Toward a save honoured and imitated,
deserving for the one and inflaming for all,
so the hero the medal rightly finds is the signal that lights the next crisis.
