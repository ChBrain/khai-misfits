# Piece: The Made Number

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The target hit by the nearest means, truthfully, goal aside.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

On [the floor](place_the_floor.md), where the number is actually produced.
Where the desk that is measured meets the target with whatever is closest to hand: the reclassified case, the deferred hard one, the test taught to, the call closed fast.

## Load Bearing

Without it the target is an aspiration and the goal survives untouched.
With it the target is met and the goal is not, and the crucial thing is that no line is false: the number really was hit, by real work, honestly recorded. The means were the nearest ones, not the truest ones, because the nearest ones are what a target selects for. The Made Number is the honest hit, which is why it never triggers an alarm.

## Apparent

Performance.
It looks like a desk delivering exactly what was asked, on time, to spec. It did. The number is genuine and the effort behind it is real.

## Yearbook

Produced by [the hitter](persona_the_hitter.md), who is complying, not cheating.
To hit the number, it spends [the unmeasured](piece_the_unmeasured.md): the part of the goal no column counts. The spend is invisible because nothing measures it, and the hit is visible because everything does. The desk is thanked for the second and never charged for the first.
