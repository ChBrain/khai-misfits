# Plot: Mind Metric

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The scorecard](place_the_scorecard.md), rows of names against a single line, reward and sanction hung on the number.
The target is no longer a reading anyone takes: it is a bar that pays those above it and punishes those below, posted where the whole team can see exactly where they stand.

## Action

[Optimisation](process_optimisation.md): [The Surrogate](persona_the_surrogate.md), holding [the unit manager's seat](position_unit_manager.md), points the team at the gauge and manages to it, because the gauge is what pays.

## Stage

[The Surrogate](persona_the_surrogate.md) tables [Mind the Metric](plan_mind_the_metric.md): defend the number as the mission and let the dashboard stand for the thing it once measured, so [the surrogate goal](piece_the_surrogate_goal.md) is not adopted by anyone's decision but grown by everyone's incentive.
The measure has become a target, and on the day it does it stops being a measure: the team now moves the number, which is a different act from moving the thing the number was watching.

## Tension

Managing to the dashboard is just taking the target seriously.
The Surrogate is doing exactly what the scorecard asks, pointing the team at the one line the firm chose to reward, and the team responds to the incentive it is actually given rather than the one someone meant to give. Nobody games in bad faith: they optimise the number because the number is what is paid and punished, and the goal it stood for goes unserved not because anyone abandoned it but because nothing on the scorecard rewards it any more.
