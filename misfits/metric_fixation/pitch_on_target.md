# Pitch: On Target

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Exact, confident, evidenced.
Every claim has a number behind it and every number is real.
The voice of an organisation that can prove it is winning, because it defined winning as the thing it can prove.

## Undertow

Under the number, a goal going quiet.
The measure is true and rising, and the thing it once stood for is spent to keep it rising.
Nothing is falsified. The proof is sound. The proof is of the wrong thing.

## Nerve

The feeling of being shown a target hit,
by people who are not gaming so much as complying,
while the reason the target existed is nowhere on the page.

## Echo

After the run: a goal quietly abandoned,
a dashboard that was green and honest the whole time,
and a number that measured everything except the point.
