# Position: Mission Owner

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The seat that holds the real goal, and holds no number.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The purpose, whole and true.
No column, no dashboard, no way to count what it holds.

## Orders

Keep the purpose in view.
Ask, each quarter, whether the work still serves the thing it was for.

## Loses

Standing. The house rewards the proxy, and the proxy is not the purpose.
The seat that is right is the seat with nothing to show.
Every review, a little more of the room's attention.

## Drives

Toward the goal actually being served.
It sees clearly and moves nothing: it holds the purpose and no lever.
Right, and without the leverage to compel it.
