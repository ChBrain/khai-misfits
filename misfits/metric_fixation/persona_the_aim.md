# Persona: The Aim

## Taxonomy

[Mission Owner](position_mission_owner.md). The goal itself, still in the room, growing quiet.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Holds the real goal.
Speaks in outcomes, not numbers.
Heard less each quarter.

## Action

Names the purpose behind the metric.
Asks whether the number still means it.

## Shadow

Right about the goal, and powerless: the house rewards the proxy.
The one seat serving the goal is the one seat with no number to show.
Serving the aim is doing right; the house just cannot see it.

## Tell

Says "but are we actually doing the thing?"
The room answers with the metric.
