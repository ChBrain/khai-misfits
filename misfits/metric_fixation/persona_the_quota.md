# Persona: The Quota

## Taxonomy

[Target Setter](position_target_setter.md). The clear bar, fairly drawn.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Decisive, fair.
Sets a number every desk can see.
No favourites, no fog.

## Action

Turns the measure into a number every desk must hit.
Reward and sanction attached.

## Shadow

The moment it makes the gauge a target, the gauge stops telling the truth.
The corruption is in the targeting, not the desk.
Setting a fair bar is doing right; the bar is what bends the gauge.

## Tell

Says "what gets measured gets managed."
Does not hear that what gets targeted gets gamed.
