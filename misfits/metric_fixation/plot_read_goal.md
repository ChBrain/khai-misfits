# Plot: Read Goal

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

The day the goal is measured directly, not through its proxy.
Someone arrives with no stake in the number, or the goal's collapse finally shows where no metric was looking: the students who passed and cannot read, the targets hit and the mission gone.

## Action

[Reckoning](process_reckoning.md): [The Reader](persona_the_reader.md), holding [the incoming reviewer's seat](position_incoming_reviewer.md), measures the goal itself and lays its line over the number's, so the two can be seen at once for the first time.

## Stage

[The Reader](persona_the_reader.md) tables [Read the Goal](plan_read_the_goal.md): put the outcome on the page beside its proxy.
[The divergence](piece_the_divergence.md) is drawn at last, the rising number and the falling goal in one frame, and [the measure](piece_the_measure.md) stands revealed: true the whole way, and hollow the whole way.

## Tension

Nobody was wrong.
The number was honest, the audit was clean, every desk complied. The reckoning is not a fraud uncovered, because there was no fraud: it is a substitution noticed, far too late, by someone who thought to measure the goal instead of the gauge ... or nobody does, and the number runs on, green and true and pointing at nothing.
