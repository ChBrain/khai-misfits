# Position: Unit Manager

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The seat with the dashboard, and the team pointed at it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The dashboard, green where it should be green.
The team, aimed at the same figures.

## Orders

Keep the number green.
Steer the team by the panel, and keep the panel steady.

## Loses

The goal, quietly, out of its own attention.
Not by choice, and not all at once: the metric fills the field of view.
By the time the number is the mission, the substitution is invisible from inside.

## Drives

Toward the metric, believed as the mission.
It manages the proxy with real care, real skill.
The care is genuine; the target is a stand-in it has stopped seeing as one.
