## Action

## Stage

To make it, the desk spends [the unmeasured](piece_the_unmeasured.md): the care, the teaching, the maintenance no column counts, drawn down to fund the column that is counted.

## Tension

No line is false.
The number really was hit, by real work, filed correctly. The means were the nearest and not the truest, because a target rewards the nearest, and the cost was paid out of an account nothing measures. The desk is complying, not cheating, and it is thanked for the hit and never charged for the spend.
