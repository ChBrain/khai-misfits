# Position: Metric Author

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The seat that reduces the goal to a measure, and calls it the goal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The method. The instrument.
The goal, translated into a figure the house can read.

## Orders

Make the goal measurable.
Find the proxy that stands in for the purpose, and ship it.

## Loses

The proxy's honesty, the instant it becomes a target.
A good measure describes; a targeted measure is gamed.
What was a faithful reading turns, quietly, into a thing to beat.

## Drives

Toward a manageable measure.
That is the achievement, and the first step away from the goal.
The clearer the number, the further from the thing it stood for.
