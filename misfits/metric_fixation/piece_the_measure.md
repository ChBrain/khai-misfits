# Piece: The Measure

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The accumulated whole: a number true and green, and the goal it stood for gone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

The gap between the two lines, held as one object.
The number that rose and the goal that fell were always the same measure, before and after it stopped meaning what it measured.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a sensible metric that drifted and some work that went wide.
With it those are one thing: a measure that is entirely true and entirely beside the point, green at every rung and hollow at the centre, held whole by nobody, because the seat that certifies the number is never the seat that lives the goal, and no seat is asked to hold both.

## Apparent

A number, on target.
From every desk that reports it, the organisation is succeeding, and by the measure it is. The trap is invisible from within because there is no error to find: no false figure, no failed audit, no broken rule, only a true measure of a goal that quietly left.

## Yearbook

The proxy became [the target](piece_the_target.md), the target became [the made number](piece_the_made_number.md), the metric became [the surrogate goal](piece_the_surrogate_goal.md), and [the clean audit](piece_the_clean_audit.md) certified all of it, correctly.
[The Reader](persona_the_reader.md) was the first to measure the goal again instead of its proxy, and lay the two lines together. The number had been true the whole time. That was always the problem. Nobody had a name for the measure until it was all that was left.
