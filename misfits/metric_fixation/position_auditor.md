# Position: Auditor

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The seat that certifies the figure, and never the goal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The definition, precise.
The stamp that says the figure meets it.

## Orders

Verify the figure.
Trace it to the definition, confirm the arithmetic, certify.

## Loses

The goal, which is never in scope.
It can attest that the number is true and say nothing of whether the number matters.
A perfectly certified proxy is still a proxy; that gap is outside its charter.

## Drives

Toward a true number, correctly certified.
It does its work faultlessly, and the work does not ask the one question that counts.
The stamp is honest; it just lands on the wrong thing.
