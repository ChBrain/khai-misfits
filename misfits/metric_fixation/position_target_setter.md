# Position: Target Setter

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The seat that turns a reading into a bar, with a reward and a sanction behind it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The bar. The reward for clearing it.
The sanction for falling short.

## Orders

Set a target and hold people to it.
Name the number, attach the stakes, keep the line firm.

## Loses

The measure's truth, spent to make it a target.
Pressure applied to a proxy deforms it; the figure bends toward the reward.
It buys motion with the honesty of the number, and the honesty does not come back.

## Drives

Toward performance against the number.
People clear the bar, which is what it asked for.
Whether the goal moved is not a question it was built to hold.
