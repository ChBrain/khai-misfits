# Persona: The Proxy

## Taxonomy

[Metric Author](position_metric_author.md). The one who made the goal manageable.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Rigorous, reasonable.
Turned a vague aim into something you could hold.
The measure everyone now trusts.

## Action

Reduces the goal to a measure that tracks it today.
You cannot manage what you cannot measure.

## Shadow

The measure tracks the goal only until it is chased.
No measure it builds survives being targeted, and it cannot build one that does.
The first domino, sensibly placed: it did the honest thing, once.

## Tell

Says "this is the best available proxy," true when said.
Never revisits it once it is load-bearing.
