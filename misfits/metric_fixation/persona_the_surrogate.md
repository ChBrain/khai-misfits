# Persona: The Surrogate

## Taxonomy

[Unit Manager](position_unit_manager.md). The one who manages to the dashboard.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Aligned, focused, on-message.
Defends the number.
Runs a tight unit.

## Action

Manages to the dashboard.
Defends the number as if it were the mission.

## Shadow

Not fooled: focused. The metric has become the goal in its head.
The metric is what it is asked about, and what it is promoted on.
It manages exactly what the house measures: the substitution was rewarded into it.

## Tell

Stops saying the goal and only says the measure.
Does not notice the substitution.
