# Piece: The Divergence

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The travelling gap: the number rising as the goal falls.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Between the two lines, widening.
The measure climbs through [targeting](process_targeting.md), [optimisation](process_optimisation.md), and [assurance](process_assurance.md), certified higher at every rung; the goal it stood for sinks the whole way, unmeasured and so unseen.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a good metric and a run of hard work.
With it those are one motion pulling apart: the very act of chasing the proxy is what drives the proxy away from the goal, so the harder the number is worked the wider the gap it opens. The Divergence is the play's slow separation. Early on the two lines are close enough to call the same, which is why no one calls it, and by the time they are plainly two it is plainly late.

## Apparent

Progress.
Read on its own the number is a rising line, and a rising line is success. The goal has no line to read, because measuring it was the thing everyone agreed was too hard.

## Yearbook

Driven by every seat doing its job: [the target](piece_the_target.md) chased, [the made number](piece_the_made_number.md) produced, [the unmeasured](piece_the_unmeasured.md) spent.
It crosses no one's desk as a single figure, because the number lives on the scorecard and the goal lives nowhere, until [reading the goal](plot_read_goal.md) draws the second line at last and lays it over the first.
