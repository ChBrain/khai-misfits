# Piece: The Surrogate Goal

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The metric mistaken for the mission it was meant to track.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the review](place_the_review.md), where the number is discussed as though it were the thing itself.
The moment nobody says "the goal" any more, only the measure: not "are we teaching" but "is the pass rate up," not "are they well" but "are the wait times down."

## Load Bearing

Without it the metric stays a tool, and someone still holds the goal behind it.
With it the tool becomes the end: the proxy is not merely optimised, it is believed, and the seat that once served the goal now serves the number in full sincerity, because the number is what it is asked about, rewarded on, and promoted for. The Surrogate Goal is the swap completing, quietly, in people's heads, so that defending the number feels like defending the mission.

## Apparent

Alignment.
It looks like an organisation that finally agrees on what matters, everyone rowing to the same number. The agreement is real. It is agreement on the proxy.

## Yearbook

Held by [the surrogate](persona_the_surrogate.md), who is not fooled so much as focused.
By now [the goal](persona_the_aim.md) has no seat that speaks for it in the room, because every seat has been given the measure to speak for instead. The mission did not lose an argument. It stopped being on the agenda.
