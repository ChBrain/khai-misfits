# Piece: The Target

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The proxy turned into a number that must be hit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the scorecard](place_the_scorecard.md), where the measure stops describing and starts demanding.
The threshold a desk is rewarded for clearing and punished for missing: the quota, the SLA, the OKR, the pass rate.

## Load Bearing

Without it the proxy is only a gauge, watched but not chased, and it stays roughly honest.
With it the proxy becomes a pressure, and the pressure is the whole mechanism: the moment a measure is used to decide who is doing well, every desk is moved to serve the measure ahead of the goal. The Target is where a description becomes an incentive, and an incentive bends whatever it is aimed at.

## Apparent

Accountability.
It looks like the organisation setting a clear bar and holding itself to it. The bar is specific, the bar is fair, and everyone knows exactly what is being asked.

## Yearbook

Set by [the quota](persona_the_quota.md), from [the proxy](piece_the_proxy.md) the definition handed up.
It is the turn the whole play pivots on: a number watched can still tell the truth, a number chased cannot, because chasing it is precisely what corrupts it. Nobody at the scorecard intends the corruption. They intend the bar.
