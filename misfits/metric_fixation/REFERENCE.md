# Metric Fixation: Reference

The research warrant for [Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The vocabulary is
performance-management practice; the mechanics the play runs on are grounded in the
measurement-distortion, principal-agent, and audit literature cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging metric fixation as a **systemic system**: an organisation in which every seat
does exactly what a true, audited measure asks, and the goal that measure stood for is
lost anyway because targeting the proxy is what drives the proxy away from the goal. The
play models the documented mechanics, the target corrupting its own measure, effort
flowing to the measured dimension, the metric surrogating for the construct, and the
audit that verifies the number and not its meaning, as the load-bearing vectors of the
structure, not as a tale of cheats. The governing finding of the source literature is
the play's governing law: the distortion is a structural property of measurement under
incentive, one-directional and predictable, so the failure is a property of honest
measurement, not a lapse from it. This is not its neighbour, misreporting: here the
number is not a false account of reality, it is a true account of the wrong thing.

## Origin

The phenomenon in practice, and the research spine beneath it.

| Source                   | Key Work / Event                                                                                                           | Scope                                                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Ridgway**              | "Dysfunctional Consequences of Performance Measurements", _Administrative Science Quarterly_ (1956)                        | The earliest naming: single, composite, and ratio measures each distort behaviour toward the measure and away from the purpose. The play's oldest root.                              |
| **Goodhart / Strathern** | Goodhart's Law (1975); Marilyn Strathern's formulation, "Improving ratings: audit in the British University system" (1997) | **When a measure becomes a target, it ceases to be a good measure**: the act of targeting the proxy is what breaks its link to the goal. The play's central law.                     |
| **Campbell**             | "Assessing the Impact of Planned Social Change" (1979)                                                                     | **Campbell's Law**: the more a quantitative indicator is used for decisions, the more it corrupts the process it was meant to monitor. Teaching to the test, and every cousin of it. |
| **Holmström & Milgrom**  | "Multitask Principal-Agent Analyses: Incentive Contracts, Asset Ownership, and Job Design", _JLEO_ (1991)                  | The **multitasking distortion**: rewarding a measured dimension pulls effort off the unmeasured ones, which is where the rest of the goal lived. The Unmeasured, formalised.         |
| **Choi, Hecht & Tayler** | "Lost in Translation: The Effects of Incentive Compensation on Strategy Surrogation", _The Accounting Review_ (2012)       | **Surrogation**: under incentives, decision-makers come to treat the metric as the construct it proxies, not merely to optimise it. The metric becoming the mission, measured.       |
| **Power**                | "The Audit Society: Rituals of Verification" (1997)                                                                        | Assurance verifies form against its own definition and certifies it, sound on the figure and silent on whether the figure still means anything. The Clean Audit's deaf spot.         |
| **Muller**               | "The Tyranny of Metrics" (2018)                                                                                            | Metric fixation as a cross-sector pattern, from schools to hospitals to policing to business: the same substitution, everywhere it is measured. The synthesis in practice.           |
| **Practitioner**         | KPIs, OKRs, SLAs, quotas, and their gaming (performance-management practice)                                               | The naming: a target hit to the letter by means that hollow out its point, with no line falsified.                                                                                   |

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to
  cast any seat as a cheat, because the source finding is precisely that no one need cheat:
  the number is true, the audit is clean, and each desk is complying with a measure it was
  told to serve. The misfit is that serving the measure and serving the goal came apart.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into integrity arriving. The reckoning
  is a substitution noticed late by someone who thought to measure the goal instead of its
  proxy, not a whistle blown on a lie, because there was no lie to blow it on.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed
  here, never claimed as the house's. The staging is the house's; the findings are theirs.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **[The Proxy](piece_the_proxy.md)**: carries the operationalisation that precedes Goodhart's
  Law, a measurable stand-in that tracks the goal only until it is targeted.
- **[The Target](piece_the_target.md)**: carries Campbell's Law and Goodhart's Law, the measure
  used for decisions becoming the measure that corrupts.
- **[The Made Number](piece_the_made_number.md)**: carries Ridgway's dysfunction and the practice
  of gaming to target, the honest hit by the nearest means.
- **[The Unmeasured](piece_the_unmeasured.md)**: carries the multitasking distortion (Holmström &
  Milgrom), the goal's unmeasured dimensions spent to fund the measured one.
- **[The Surrogate Goal](piece_the_surrogate_goal.md)**: carries surrogation (Choi, Hecht & Tayler),
  the metric mistaken for the mission it was meant to track.
- **[The Clean Audit](piece_the_clean_audit.md)**: carries the audit society (Power), assurance that
  verifies the proxy against its definition and never against the goal.
- **[The Divergence](piece_the_divergence.md)**: carries the Goodhart divergence itself, the widening
  gap that chasing the proxy is what opens.
- **[The Measure](piece_the_measure.md)**: carries the tyranny of metrics (Muller) and Goodhart's Law
  made whole, a number true and green and hollow, held whole by nobody.
