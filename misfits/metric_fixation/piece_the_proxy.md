# Piece: The Proxy

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The measurable stand-in for the goal that cannot be counted.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the definition](place_the_definition.md), where a goal too large to measure is given something to measure instead.
The reasonable substitution: learning becomes test scores, care becomes wait times, health becomes weight, safety becomes incidents filed.

## Load Bearing

Without it the goal cannot be managed at all, and the work runs on faith.
With it the goal becomes a number, and everything follows from the swap: the proxy is chosen because it correlates with the goal today, and nothing holds that correlation once the proxy becomes the thing everyone optimises. The Proxy is not a lie. It is a true measure of the goal, on the day before it is targeted.

## Apparent

Rigour, at last.
It looks like the organisation getting serious: naming the goal, quantifying it, making it accountable. The measure really does track the goal, at the moment it is chosen.

## Yearbook

Authored by [the proxy](persona_the_proxy.md), because you cannot manage what you cannot measure.
It is the most sensible move in the play and the first domino: the goal was real and unmeasurable, the proxy is measurable and almost the goal, and almost is where [the divergence](piece_the_divergence.md) will open.
