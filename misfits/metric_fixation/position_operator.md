# Position: Operator

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The seat at the desk, the target above it, the nearest route to it in hand.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The desk. The target.
The nearest means to reach it.

## Orders

Hit the number.
Take the closest path to the bar and clear it.

## Loses

Nothing it is measured on.
It spends only what nothing counts: the goal, which no column watches.
What is not on the dashboard is free to give away, so it goes.

## Drives

Toward clearing the bar, by the shortest route available.
The route is chosen for the number, not the purpose.
It is doing exactly what it was told, and that is the problem.
