# Piece: The Clean Audit

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). The number certified true at every rung, because it is.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

In [the audit room](place_the_audit_room.md), where the number is checked against its definition and passes.
The control that confirms the figures are real, the method sound, the evidence filed: assurance doing its job correctly.

## Load Bearing

Without it the number could be doubted, and a false figure would eventually be caught.
With it the number is guaranteed, and the guarantee is the trap: the audit verifies the proxy against its definition, never the proxy against the goal, because the goal is not in its scope and could not be measured if it were. The Clean Audit certifies that the measure is true and is silent on whether the measure still means anything. It is the deaf spot the whole structure leans on: everything is checked except the one substitution that matters.

## Apparent

Assurance.
It looks like the final guarantee of integrity: an independent check, passed. The check is genuine and the pass is earned. Nothing in the figures is wrong.

## Yearbook

Signed by [the certifier](persona_the_certifier.md), who validates the number and was never asked to validate the goal.
It is the most reassuring piece and the most dangerous, because a clean audit of a surrogate goal is a stamp of truth on the wrong question, and it travels upward as proof.
