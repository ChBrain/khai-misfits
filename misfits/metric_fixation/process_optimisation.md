# Process: Optimisation

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md). Toward the number, by the nearest route.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A target landing on a desk.
A required number arriving with a name beside it.

## Direction

Toward the number, by the nearest route.
Effort bending to the shortest path that clears it.

## Lever

The measured against the unmeasured.
Effort flowing where it is counted, away from where it is not.

## Echo

A hit that is true and a goal that is spent.
The number lands; the purpose it stood for is drained.
