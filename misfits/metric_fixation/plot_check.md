# Plot: Check

## Taxonomy

[Metric Fixation](play_metric_fixation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The review](place_the_review.md), where the number is discussed as though it were the thing itself.
The target is met and rising. Nobody in the room says the goal any more, only the measure, and in [the audit room](place_the_audit_room.md) the figure is about to be confirmed.

## Action

[Surrogation](process_surrogation.md): [The Surrogate](persona_the_surrogate.md), holding [the unit manager's seat](position_unit_manager.md), manages to the dashboard in full sincerity, because the dashboard is what it is asked about and rewarded on.
[Assurance](process_assurance.md): [The Certifier](persona_the_certifier.md) at [the auditor's seat](position_auditor.md) checks the number against its definition, and it passes.

## Stage

[The Surrogate](persona_the_surrogate.md) tables [Mind the Metric](plan_mind_the_metric.md): defend the number as if it were the mission, and [the surrogate goal](piece_the_surrogate_goal.md) completes the swap.
[The Certifier](persona_the_certifier.md) tables [Confirm the Green](plan_confirm_the_green.md): verify the figure is real, and [the clean audit](piece_the_clean_audit.md) stamps it true.
The goal has no seat left in the room to speak for it.

## Tension

Everything checks.
The number is real, the audit is honest, the manager is aligned, and keeping the green is exactly what earns the next promotion. The dashboard is all green and the goal is nobody's job, and none of that required a single person to do anything but their job well.
