# Pitch: Safety in Numbers

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Sound, thriving, safe in the crowd.
The plain good sense of a population doing what a throng rewards, each member finding its mate, diluting the predator, mobbing the intruder, foraging in company, the numbers high and the count reading as a population that will go on.
Nothing false in it on its face: the safety is genuine, the crowd truly confers the benefit, and every behaviour is the one the numbers made correct.

## Undertow

Under the thriving, a benefit that is per-capita and rises with density, so the safety the crowd confers is the crowd's to lose.
Because a benefit that rose with the numbers falls with them, a population thinned below a critical line does not merely grow slower but turns to negative growth, and the decline feeds the decline, faster the fewer remain, drawn down by the very density dependence that had held it up.
What makes a crowd safe, that fitness rises with numbers, is what makes fewness fatal once the numbers fall.

## Nerve

The feeling of a population sound and secure, the throng dense and the safety real,
each member doing exactly what works, because in a crowd it does work and the numbers are there,
while below a line no count reveals the same behaviours cannot sustain the few, and the safety in numbers becomes a peril in fewness.

## Echo

After the run: a population drawn to extinction not by any member's fault but by the loss of the numbers that were its safety,
no one having over-bred and no behaviour having changed, only a per-capita benefit falling with a density thinned past the line where growth turns negative and the spiral runs,
and the one move that would have kept it, holding the numbers above the line before the crossing, forgone because it means spending on a population that reads ample and restraining a harvest that reads sustainable.
