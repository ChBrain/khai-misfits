# Piece: The Vortex

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The phenomenon whole, the extinction vortex, a population below the line drawn to extinction by the very density dependence that had held it up, the safety in numbers become a peril in fewness.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Turns across [the edge](place_the_edge.md), where the population past the line is pulled down by its own thinning.
Averted, if ever, at [the refuge](place_the_refuge.md), where the numbers are held above the line and the vortex never opens.

## Load Bearing

Without it the decline is a loss and no more, and nothing draws the population all the way down.
With it the trap is complete and authorless: a population below the critical number pulled toward extinction by mutually reinforcing feedbacks, each small step lowering the density and each lower density deepening the descent, the benefit that had made the crowd safe now the engine of its loss (Gilpin and Soulé, on the extinction vortex, small populations drawn to extinction by the reinforcing loops their own smallness sets off; Courchamp, Berec and Gascoigne, on the Allee effect and extinction). The Vortex is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole: not one bad season but a self-deepening spiral no member started and none can halt by behaving well, so the piece carries the extinction the loss of the numbers finally becomes, the safety that was real drawn down by its own dependence on the density.

## Apparent

A tragic collapse, a population that failed against the odds.
Seen at the surface, the Vortex looks like bad luck or a hostile world, a species that simply could not make it, and the truth that the descent is the density dependence in reverse, that every member behaved as the crowd rewarded, that the safety was genuine and lost only with the numbers that held it, stays hidden behind how much an extinction looks like a fate rather than a benefit running backward.
