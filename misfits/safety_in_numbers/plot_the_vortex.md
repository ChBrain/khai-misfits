# Plot: The Vortex

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Edge](place_the_edge.md), a population below the line with its per-capita growth turned negative.
Each member fending for one as it always did, the crowd the one thing that is going.

## Action

[Spiralling](process_spiralling.md): [The Member](persona_the_member.md), holding [the member's seat](position_member.md), keeps to the behaviour the throng rewarded while the density dependence runs in reverse.

## Stage

Each [member](persona_the_member.md), on [Fend for One](plan_fend_for_one.md), draws the thinning [benefit](piece_the_benefit.md) from a falling [density](piece_the_density.md), and each loss lowers the density and the density lowers the benefit and the benefit lowers the growth.
The decline feeds the decline, [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md) running faster the fewer remain, until it is [the vortex](piece_the_vortex.md), a population drawn to extinction by the very benefit that had held it up.

## Tension

Every member behaves exactly as the crowd rewarded, and the faithfulness is exactly why the collapse has no author.
There is no fault at the edge: each does what worked and the crowd is what fails. But the density dependence runs both ways, so the plot closes with the spiral running to extinction, no member having erred, the safety in numbers become a peril in fewness and the population drawn down by its own thinning.
