# Place: The Edge

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). Near the critical density, where the thinning has carried the numbers close to the line, and where the crossing turns the per-capita growth negative and the spiral begins, unmarked to those within it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the population comes to near the line, a thinned range, a remnant, a sparse patch, where the throng is smaller and the benefit each member draws has fallen with it.
The Members still fending for one, the density near the critical number, and the count a figure that a year ago was plenty, giving no sign the line is close.

## Holds

The crossing, and the spiral that waits below it.
Here the density is near the critical number, so the Edge holds the point where the safety fails: a per-capita growth about to turn negative and a benefit too thin to carry it, so it is where the same behaviours that thrived can no longer sustain the few, and where, once the line is behind, each loss lowers the density and the density lowers the benefit and the decline feeds itself.

## Offers

A population that still reads survivable, and a line about to be crossed.
Because the Edge is where the numbers are near the threshold but not yet marked by it, it offers a headcount that still looks like plenty and a growth about to turn, so it hands out a last stretch of apparent safety, and hands it out over a line no count reveals, the crossing due and the count not showing it.

## Withheld

Any warning that the line is here.
The Edge holds the population at the brink but cannot mark the brink, because the threshold is invisible from above and the count reads survivable to the last figure before the turn, so it withholds the crossing until it is past, letting the number stand for safety at the very density where safety fails, and, all apparent plenty and no marker, it withholds the spiral until the growth has already gone negative and the fewer remain the faster they fall.
