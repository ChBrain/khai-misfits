# Plot: The Thinning

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Range](place_the_range.md), and attrition no member authored, a harvest, a drought, a road, a hard winter.
The numbers beginning to fall for reasons outside anyone in the throng, the density lowering.

## Action

[Thinning](process_thinning.md): [The Threshold](persona_the_threshold.md), holding [the threshold's seat](position_threshold.md), lowers the density by the losses any population takes and carries the numbers toward the line.

## Stage

[The Threshold](persona_the_threshold.md), on [Thin the Ranks](plan_thin_the_ranks.md), lowers [the density](piece_the_density.md) and with it [the benefit](piece_the_benefit.md) each member draws, the mate harder to find, the dilution weaker, the mob too small.
The members carry on unchanged, each still fending for one, and the [count](piece_the_count.md) still reads survivable while [the critical number](piece_the_critical_number.md) beneath it draws nearer.

## Tension

The attrition is ordinary and no member's fault, and the same behaviours carry on because nothing about the member has changed, only the density.
There is nothing careless: the losses are the losses any population takes. But the benefit falls with the numbers and the line gives no warning, so the plot closes with the throng thinned and the benefit reduced, the count reassuring at the very density where the safety is failing and the line still unseen.
