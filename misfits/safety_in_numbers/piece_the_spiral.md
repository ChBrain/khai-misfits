# Piece: The Spiral

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The self-reinforcing acceleration of the decline below the line, each loss lowering the density and each lower density lowering the benefit, the population falling faster the fewer of it remain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs at [the edge](place_the_edge.md), where the population past the line is drawn down by its own thinning.
Kept from starting, if ever, at [the refuge](place_the_refuge.md), where the density is held above the line.

## Load Bearing

Without it a population below the line simply declines and the loss is a subtraction, not an acceleration.
With it the trap has its reckoning: below the critical number each loss lowers the density, lower density lowers the per-capita benefit, and lower benefit lowers the growth further, so the decline compounds and the population falls faster the fewer remain, the density dependence running in reverse (Dennis, on the accelerating extinction risk below the critical density; the demographic Allee effect turning a thinned population toward collapse). The Spiral is load bearing because it is what the loss of the numbers finally costs: not a slower population but a self-feeding descent, so the piece carries the acceleration that no member started and none can halt by behaving well, the safety in numbers become a peril in fewness.

## Apparent

A sudden crash, a collapse that seems to come from nowhere.
Seen at the surface, the Spiral looks like a shock from outside, a population fine one season and failing the next, and the truth that the descent is the population's own density dependence in reverse, that each member is behaving exactly as the crowd rewarded, that the acceleration was latent in the benefit all along, stays hidden behind how much a compounding decline looks like a blow that struck from without.

## Yearbook

Runs from [the benefit](piece_the_benefit.md) lost below [the critical number](piece_the_critical_number.md), kept from starting only by [holding](process_holding.md), the descent each member's faithful behaviour feeds.
It is the reckoning of the trap: the Spiral is [the density](piece_the_density.md) falling faster the fewer remain, the true cost of the safety that was the numbers', the engine of [the vortex](piece_the_vortex.md) that [the count](piece_the_count.md) gave no warning of.
