# Plot: The Hold

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Refuge](place_the_refuge.md), and someone unwilling to trust a count that reads ample.
The Keeper setting out to hold the density above the line before the attrition can carry it across.

## Action

[Holding](process_holding.md): [The Keeper](persona_the_keeper.md), holding [the keeper's seat](position_keeper.md), protects the range, restrains the harvest, and supplements the throng to keep the numbers over the critical point.

## Stage

[The Keeper](persona_the_keeper.md), on [Hold the Number](plan_hold_the_number.md), keeps [the density](piece_the_density.md) above [the critical number](piece_the_critical_number.md), holding [the benefit](piece_the_benefit.md) and keeping the population off [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md).
Against the holding stand a [count](piece_the_count.md) that reads ample and a harvest that reads sustainable, so the guarding must be spent before the numbers give any reason for it.

## Tension

Holding the numbers above the line is the one move that keeps the safety, and it asks the outlay the ample count makes hard to justify.
It spends on a population that looks fine, restrains a harvest that reads sustainable, and guards against a threshold no count reveals. So the plot closes one of two ways: the density held above the line and the spiral never entered ... or the guarding waived as spending on nothing, the headcount taken for safety, and the line left for the thinning to cross.
