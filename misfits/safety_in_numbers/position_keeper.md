# Position: Keeper

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The seat that would hold the density above the line, spending on the numbers before they thin to the critical point, at the cost of guarding a population that reads ample.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A doubt about a healthy count and the means to hold the numbers up: the will to keep the density above the line and the levers to protect the range, restrain the harvest, and supplement the throng.
A real corrective: holding the density above the critical number is the one thing that keeps the safety the numbers confer.
No cheap way to do it, and no welcome for the one who spends on a population that looks fine.

## Orders

Hold the number.
Keep the density above the critical line, protect the range, restrain the attrition, and supplement the throng before it thins to the threshold, and treat an ample headcount as suspect for showing the danger only once it is past.
Spend early, because the line gives no warning.

## Loses

The standing of one who spends only when the count demands it.
Because the numbers read survivable right up to the line, holding them above it means spending on a population that looks fine, restraining a harvest that reads sustainable, and guarding against a threshold no count reveals, so the density is held only against every signal that it need not be, and the spiral it prevents leaves no trace while the cost of holding is plain, the averted extinction looking, after, like one that was never going to happen.
It gains the numbers held and the spiral never entered, and loses the ease and the standing that spending late would have kept.

## Drives

Toward the density held above the line and the safety kept,
at the continuous cost of spending on a population that reads ample,
so the seat that holds the number keeps the population off the spiral, and is read as guarding against nothing.
