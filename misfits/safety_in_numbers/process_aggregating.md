# Process: Aggregating

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The Throng drawing together and conferring on each member the benefit of its density, the gathering that makes the safety real and makes it the numbers'.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A population and a habitat that rewards being many.
At [the range](place_the_range.md), the throng gathering, each member keeping to the crowd because the crowd finds the mate, dilutes the predator, and mobs the intruder.

## Direction

Toward the density high and the benefit conferred.
Because a crowd dilutes the predator, mobs the intruder, and holds the mates within reach, aggregating gathers the numbers and confers on each member a per-capita gain that rises with them, so the more of the throng there is the more each within it draws, the safety made of bodies and genuinely the density's.

## Lever

The rise of the benefit with the numbers.
Because each component gain, the mate found, the dilution, the mob, the forage, improves with how many are present, aggregating runs on a real positive return to density and not on any comfort, so the safety is assembled from no illusion, only from a crowd whose size is the thing that protects and provides for each within it.

## Echo

A density gathered, and a safety made of it.
The throng has drawn together and conferred its benefit, so what reads as a settled protection is, in truth, a safety that is the numbers and only the numbers, real while the crowd is dense and the density's to lose the moment the crowd thins.
