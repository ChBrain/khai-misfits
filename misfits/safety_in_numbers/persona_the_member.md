# Persona: The Member

## Taxonomy

[Member](position_member.md). The one that lives the way the crowd rewards, trusting the throng to find the mate and dilute the predator and raise the alarm, doing exactly what works in a crowd. The archetype of the many whose fitness rises with the numbers around them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sound. Thriving. Doing what works.
Is any one of the many in the throng, the bird in the flock, the fish in the shoal, the individual that must find a mate, avoid the predator, and feed, and does each of these by being among the rest.
No fault in it: the Member behaves exactly as the crowd rewards, its reliance on the throng not a habit to drop but the behaviour the numbers made correct.

## Action

Keeps to the many, finds its mate in the throng, is diluted among the crowd against the predator, mobs the intruder with the rest, and forages the ground in company.
Does the same at low density as at high, because nothing about the member has changed, only the number around it.
Does what a member must: it lives the way the crowd rewards, and the way the crowd rewards is the way that thrives while the crowd is there.

## Shadow

Not over-breeding, not erring, a member behaving faithfully as it always did, and that is the whole difficulty: the behaviour is sound, and its soundness depends on a density the member cannot hold up alone.
Because the safety was in the numbers and not in the member, when the numbers fall the same reliance draws a thinner benefit, the mate harder to find, the dilution weaker, the mob too small, so the member below the line is drawn down not by any change in itself but by the loss of the crowd, doing to the last exactly what worked while the crowd was there.
It behaves as the numbers rewarded, and the reward was the numbers, so behaving well is no defence once the numbers go.

## Tell

Does, in effect, "what I have always done, because it works."
Does it while the density falls around it, and cannot behave its way out, because there is no better behaviour than the one the crowd rewarded, and the one thing failing is the crowd.
