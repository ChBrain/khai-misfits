# Piece: The Benefit

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The per-capita gain that rises with density, the mate found, the predator diluted, the intruder mobbed, the ground foraged in company, the safety each member draws from the numbers around it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Conferred across [the range](place_the_range.md), where the density is high and the gain each member draws is full.
Reduced toward [the edge](place_the_edge.md), where the thinning lowers the density and the benefit with it.

## Load Bearing

Without it density is just a number and there is nothing the crowd gives, and no safety to rise and fall.
With it the trap turns: the per-capita gain each member draws from the density, the mate-finding, the predator dilution, the cooperative defence and foraging, each a component effect that improves with how many are present (Courchamp, Berec and Gascoigne, on the mechanisms of the Allee effect; Allee, on the survival value of aggregation). The Benefit is load bearing because it is what makes the safety the numbers' and not the member's: a gain that rises with density and falls with it, so the piece carries the return to crowding that is real at high numbers and thin at low, the same reliance paying less as the throng thins though the member has not changed.

## Apparent

A trait of the member, a fitness it seems to carry in itself.
Seen at the surface, the Benefit looks like the member's own soundness, a bird that finds mates and evades hawks because it is a capable bird, and the truth that the gain is per-capita and density-dependent, that it is drawn from the crowd and not held by the member, that it falls when the numbers fall for no change in the member at all, stays hidden behind how much a thriving member looks like the author of its own thriving.

## Yearbook

Conferred by [the throng](persona_the_throng.md) through [aggregating](process_aggregating.md), drawn by [the member](persona_the_member.md) through fending for one, the gain the numbers give.
It is the turn of the trap: the Benefit is drawn from [the density](piece_the_density.md) and rises with it, falls as [thinning](process_thinning.md) lowers the numbers past [the critical number](piece_the_critical_number.md), and its loss below the line drives [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md) within [the vortex](piece_the_vortex.md).
