# Position: Threshold

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The seat of the critical density, a line without a marker and a downward pressure, where per-capita growth turns negative and the attrition carries the numbers across.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A fixed line in the numbers and the ordinary force that lowers them to it: the critical density where growth crosses zero, and the attrition, harvest, drought, and hard seasons that thin the ranks toward it.
A real edge: the density where the benefit can no longer carry the growth is genuine and fixed, not a mood or a slope.
No marker above it and no return below it, because the line gives no sign of itself until it is passed.

## Orders

Thin the ranks.
Lower the density by the losses any population takes, carry the numbers toward the critical line, and past it turn the per-capita growth negative, because that is what attrition and a fitness curve do, neither authored by any member.
Give no warning, since the line is invisible from above.

## Loses

Nothing of its own, and takes the safety with it: the Threshold is a property and a pressure, not a seat that can be harmed, and what it removes is the population's margin above the line.
Because the attrition thins the density unremarked and the critical line carries no marker, the numbers cross it reading survivable, and below it the population is not slowed but reversed, drawn toward extinction by a growth rate gone negative, the line that gave no warning giving no way back.
It gains no benefit and bears no malice, and what is lost across it is the safety the numbers had conferred.

## Drives

Toward the density lowered and the line crossed,
a fixed critical number and an attrition that reaches it unremarked,
so the seat that thins the ranks carries the numbers past a line no count reveals, and below it the same numbers cannot hold.
