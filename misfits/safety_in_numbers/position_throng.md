# Position: Throng

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The seat of the aggregate, where the density is the benefit and the crowd is the safety no single member carries.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A size and the benefit that size confers: the many bodies and the dilution, the mobbing, and the mate-finding that rise with how many it holds.
A real protection: the safety is genuine and the density's, so gathering is not a comfort but the thing that works.
No safety apart from its numbers, and no floor it keeps once thinned, because the benefit is made of size and falls with it.

## Orders

Draw together.
Gather, keep the density high, and confer on each member the dilution and the mobbing and the mates that only numbers give, because the more of the crowd there is, the more each within it draws.
Be the numbers, since the numbers are the safety.

## Loses

The safety it is, exactly by being made of a density it cannot keep.
Because the benefit it confers rises with its own density, a thinning lowers what each member draws and there is no protection it holds once thinned below the line, so the crowd that was the safety becomes too small to confer it, the very thing that held the population up now too thin to hold it.
It gains a real safety while it is dense, and loses it precisely by losing the numbers that were it.

## Drives

Toward the density gathered and the benefit conferred,
the safety that is the numbers and only the numbers,
so the seat that draws together is the safety while it is dense, and cannot keep any one once the density falls below the line.
