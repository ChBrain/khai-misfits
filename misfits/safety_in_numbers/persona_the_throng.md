# Persona: The Throng

## Taxonomy

[Throng](position_throng.md). The aggregate whose density confers the benefit, the crowd that dilutes the predator, mobs the intruder, and holds the mates within reach, the many that are the safety no single member carries.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Dense. Many. The safety itself.
Is the crowd as a body, the flock, the shoal, the herd, the colony, the aggregate whose size is the thing that protects and provides for each within it.
No illusion in it: the Throng genuinely confers the benefit, the dilution and the mobbing and the mate-finding real gains that scale with how many it holds.

## Action

Gathers, and by gathering makes each member safer and better fed than it would be alone, the predator diluted across many, the intruder driven off by the mob, the ground foraged in company.
Confers a benefit that rises with its own density, so the more of it there is, the more each within it draws.
Does what a crowd does: it is the numbers, and the numbers are the safety, so the Throng is the benefit made of bodies.

## Shadow

Not a permanent shelter, a safety made entirely of its own size, and that is the whole difficulty: the protection is real and it is the density's, so it can only be as large as the numbers are.
Because the benefit it confers is per-capita and rises with density, a thinning of the Throng lowers what each member draws, and there is no floor of safety it keeps once thinned, the dilution weaker, the mob smaller, the mates sparser, so the crowd that was the safety becomes, below the line, too small to confer it, the very thing that held the population up now too thin to hold it.
It is the safety while it is dense, and being the safety is exactly why losing its density loses the safety.

## Tell

Is, in effect, "the many that keep each one."
Is it only while the many are there, and cannot keep any one once thinned below the line, because the keeping was the number, and the number is what has gone.
