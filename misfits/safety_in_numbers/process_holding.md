# Process: Holding

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The Keeper keeping the density above the line, the costly guarding that spends on the numbers before they thin to the critical point and keeps the population off the spiral.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A count that reads ample and someone unwilling to trust it.
At [the refuge](place_the_refuge.md), the keeper protecting the range, restraining the harvest, and supplementing the throng before the attrition can carry the density to the line.

## Direction

Toward the density held above the line and the safety kept.
Because the safety fails below the critical number and the line gives no warning, holding keeps the density well over the threshold before the thinning can reach it, so the benefit is held and the growth kept positive, the population kept off the edge where it would turn, the safety preserved while it can still be preserved rather than restored once lost.

## Lever

The willingness to spend against an ample count.
Because holding the numbers means spending on a population that reads survivable and restraining a harvest that reads sustainable, it runs only on someone choosing to guard against a line no count reveals, so the corrective is exacted continuously and thanked rarely, the spiral it prevents leaving no trace and the cost of holding plain for all to see.

## Echo

A density held, and a spiral never entered.
The keeper has kept the numbers above the line, so what reads as spending on a population that was doing fine is, in truth, the one act that keeps the safety the numbers confer, the extinction it averts leaving nothing to thank it for and the outlay it took felt at once.
