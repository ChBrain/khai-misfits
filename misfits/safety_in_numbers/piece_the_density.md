# Piece: The Density

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The numbers per area of the throng, the thing the safety is made of, high enough to confer the benefit and thinnable enough to lose it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Gathered across [the range](place_the_range.md), where the throng draws together and the numbers are high.
Lowered toward [the edge](place_the_edge.md), where the thinning carries the density toward the line.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no crowd and no benefit, and no numbers to thin below the line.
With it the trap has its substance: the numbers per area on which the whole safety rests, so that a member's fitness is not fixed but rises with the density around it, positive density dependence and not a constant (Allee, on animal aggregations, the finding that survival and growth improve with numbers and that under-crowding harms as over-crowding does). The Density is load bearing because it is the variable the safety hangs on: the quantity that confers the benefit when high and withdraws it when low, so the piece carries the numbers that are the safety itself, real while they are many and the safety's to lose the moment they thin.

## Apparent

A count, a population size, a figure that is high and reads as secure.
Seen at the surface, the Density looks like a stock that is either present or not, a healthy number that means a healthy population, and the truth that the safety is a rising function of it and not a constant, that a benefit high at high density is low at low density, that the numbers are the protection and not merely its measure, stays hidden behind how much a large number simply looks like enough.
