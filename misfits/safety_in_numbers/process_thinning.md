# Process: Thinning

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The attrition lowering the density toward the line, the ordinary losses no member authored that carry the numbers toward the critical point and, past it, into negative growth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Losses any population takes and no member can prevent.
At [the range](place_the_range.md) and toward [the edge](place_the_edge.md), the harvest, the drought, the road, and the hard winter lowering the numbers, the density falling without anyone in the throng having caused it.

## Direction

Toward the density lowered and the line neared.
Because attrition removes members and the benefit is per-capita, thinning lowers both the numbers and the gain each draws from them, so the same reliance pays less as the density drops, the mate harder to find and the dilution weaker, and the population is carried toward the critical line unremarked, the count still reading survivable.

## Lever

The invisibility of the line from above.
Because the threshold carries no marker and a number just over it reads as a number far above it, thinning lowers the density without any signal that a line is near, so the crossing is assembled from no alarm, only from ordinary losses that reach a critical point the headcount does not show.

## Echo

A throng thinned, and a line drawn nearer.
The attrition has lowered the density, so what reads as a smaller but surviving population is, in truth, a benefit reduced with the numbers and a line approached unseen, the count reassuring at the very density where the safety is failing.
