# Position: Member

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The seat of the one in the many, where a behaviour that thrives is the crowd's reward and its fitness is the density's to confer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A behaviour that works and a crowd that makes it work: the reliance on the throng to find the mate and dilute the predator, and the numbers that make that reliance pay.
A real fitness: the behaviour is sound and rewarded, so keeping to it is not an error.
No safety of its own and no way to hold the density up alone, because the safety was in the numbers and not in the member.

## Orders

Fend for one.
Live the way the crowd rewards, keep to the many, trust the throng to find the mate and dilute the hawk and raise the alarm, because that is what works while the numbers are there.
Do the same at low density as at high, since nothing about the member has changed.

## Loses

The safety, exactly by its being the numbers' and not its own.
Because the benefit is per-capita and rises with density, when the throng thins the same reliance draws a thinner benefit, so the member below the line is not slowed by any change in itself but drawn down by the loss of the crowd, doing to the last what worked while the crowd was there.
It keeps a sound behaviour and a faithful life, and loses the density that was the only thing making them safe.

## Drives

Toward the behaviour the crowd rewarded, held to whatever the numbers do,
its fitness rising with the density and falling with it,
so the seat that fends for one behaves faithfully still as the crowd that made it safe goes, and cannot behave its way out of a fewness the behaviour did not cause.
