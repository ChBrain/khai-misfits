# Piece: The Critical Number

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The density below which the per-capita growth rate turns negative, the line without a marker, invisible from above and irreversible from below, the mark of a strong Allee effect.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Approached at [the edge](place_the_edge.md), where the thinning carries the density toward it.
Held off at [the refuge](place_the_refuge.md), where the numbers are kept well above it before the crossing.

## Load Bearing

Without it the benefit only slows the growth and there is no line to cross, and a thinned population merely grows more slowly.
With it the trap has its threshold: the density beneath which per-capita growth falls below zero, so a population thinned past it shrinks rather than slows, the mark that makes the effect strong rather than weak (Stephens, Sutherland and Freckleton, on the definition of the Allee effect and the critical density; Dennis, on the critical population size and the threshold below which growth cannot sustain the population). The Critical Number is load bearing because it is the line that turns decline into collapse: the point at which the safety fails outright, so the piece carries the threshold no headcount marks, above which the numbers hold and below which they cannot.

## Apparent

Nothing, on the surface: a number like any other, no different from the counts above it.
Seen at the surface, the Critical Number is invisible, because a density just over it reads as a density far above it and the count gives no sign of the line, so the population reads survivable to the last figure before the turn, and the truth that there is a real threshold, that it is fixed and unmarked, that the same numbers cannot hold below it, stays hidden behind how ordinary the number looks right up to the crossing.

## Yearbook

Approached as [thinning](process_thinning.md) lowers [the density](piece_the_density.md), held off only by [the keeper](persona_the_keeper.md) through [holding](process_holding.md), the line the safety sits above.
It is the threshold of the trap: the Critical Number is where [the benefit](piece_the_benefit.md) can no longer carry the growth, unseen beneath [the count](piece_the_count.md), and once crossed it opens [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md) that is [the vortex](piece_the_vortex.md).
