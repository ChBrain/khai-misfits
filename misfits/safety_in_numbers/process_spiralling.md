# Process: Spiralling

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md). The self-reinforcing decline below the line, each member fending for one as it always did while the density dependence runs in reverse, the loss feeding the loss faster the fewer remain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A population below the critical line, its per-capita growth turned negative.
At [the edge](place_the_edge.md), past the crossing, each member still keeping to the behaviour the throng rewarded while the numbers can no longer sustain it.

## Direction

Toward the decline feeding the decline.
Because each loss lowers the density and lower density lowers the benefit and lower benefit lowers the growth, spiralling turns each reduction into a further one, so the population below the line does not merely shrink but shrinks faster the fewer of it remain, the engine that lifted it now lowering it, no member having changed and the crowd the thing that goes.

## Lever

The density dependence running both ways.
Because the same positive return that made a dense crowd safe makes a thin one perilous, spiralling runs on no new force but the benefit falling with the numbers, so the acceleration is assembled from no error and no member's fault, only from each fending for one below a line where the behaviour that thrived can no longer pay.

## Echo

A decline that gathers speed, and no member to blame for it.
Each has done exactly what the crowd rewarded, so what reads as a sudden collapse is, in truth, the density dependence in reverse, the safety in numbers become a peril in fewness, the population drawn down by the very benefit that had held it up.
