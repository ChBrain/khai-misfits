# Plot: The Turn

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Edge](place_the_edge.md), the density come close to the critical number.
The throng thinned near the line, the headcount a figure that a year ago was plenty, the crossing due.

## Action

[Thinning](process_thinning.md) past the line: [The Threshold](persona_the_threshold.md), holding [the threshold's seat](position_threshold.md), carries the density across [the critical number](piece_the_critical_number.md), where the per-capita growth turns negative.

## Stage

The density crosses [the critical number](piece_the_critical_number.md), and the per-capita growth passes from positive to negative, so the population no longer merely grows slower but shrinks, [the benefit](piece_the_benefit.md) now too thin to carry it.
The members still fend for one exactly as before, and [the count](piece_the_count.md) is still a number that reads survivable, marking the crossing with nothing at all.

## Tension

The line is real and fixed, and its being invisible from above is what lets the numbers cross it unremarked.
There is nothing sudden the members did: each behaves as the crowd rewarded and the density passed a mark no count shows. But below the line the growth is negative and the same numbers cannot hold, so the plot closes with the line behind the population, the count still reassuring and the descent about to feed itself.
