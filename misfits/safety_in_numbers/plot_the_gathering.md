# Plot: The Gathering

## Taxonomy

[Safety in Numbers](play_safety_in_numbers.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Range](place_the_range.md), a habitat that rewards being many and a population to fill it.
The Throng drawing together, the density high, each member keeping to the crowd because the crowd is the thing that works.

## Action

[Aggregating](process_aggregating.md): [The Throng](persona_the_throng.md), holding [the throng's seat](position_throng.md), gathers and confers on each member the benefit of its density.

## Stage

[The Throng](persona_the_throng.md), on [Draw Together](plan_draw_together.md), makes [the density](piece_the_density.md) high and confers [the benefit](piece_the_benefit.md) on each member, the mate found, the predator diluted, the intruder mobbed, the ground foraged in company.
Each member draws the gain the crowd gives, and every [count](piece_the_count.md) taken over the range reads as a population that will go on.

## Tension

The safety the crowd confers is genuine, and its being genuine is what makes the numbers the safety.
There is nothing false: the density really does dilute the predator and hold the mates within reach. But the benefit is per-capita and rises with the numbers, so the plot closes with the throng dense and the safety real, the whole of it resting on a density that can be thinned and a line beneath it no count reveals.
