# Process: Amplifying

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The raising of a fear by repetition alone, the vivid case sounded until its ease of recall is read as how often it happens.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A dramatic case and a channel that will carry it.
At [the airwaves](place_the_airwaves.md), the amplifier telling and retelling the vivid instance, the danger kept at the top of mind while the cold number stays off the air.

## Direction

Toward a risk that feels common because it is easy to bring to mind.
Because the mind reads the ease of recall as a sign of frequency, and each retelling makes the case easier to recall, amplifying raises the fear by repetition alone, without any new fact about the risk, so the vivid instance rises to the top of mind and the danger comes to feel as common as it is available.

## Lever

The honest wish to warn of a real danger.
Because the case is true and a real danger deserves a loud warning, each retelling is a sensible act of advocacy, so amplifying runs on sincere warnings as readily as on self-serving ones, the two doing the same work and the fear it inflates assembled from no one lying about the risk.

## Echo

A danger felt as common because it was told so often.
The vivid case has been repeated until it is the first thing anyone recalls, and its availability is read by everyone as frequency, so what began as a true story of a rare event has become, through telling alone, a public sense that the rare event is everywhere.
