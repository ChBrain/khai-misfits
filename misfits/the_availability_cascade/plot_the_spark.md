# Plot: The Spark

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Airwaves](place_the_airwaves.md), a vivid case breaking and there to be carried.
The instance dramatic and true, the channel ready to repeat it, the public within reach of the story.

## Action

[Amplifying](process_amplifying.md): [The Amplifier](persona_the_amplifier.md), holding [the amplifier's seat](position_amplifier.md), sounds the vivid case louder, and [The Public](persona_the_public.md), holding [the public's seat](position_public.md), begins to take it up.

## Stage

[The Amplifier](persona_the_amplifier.md), on [Sound It Louder](plan_sound_it_louder.md), tells and retells the dramatic instance, keeping it at the top of mind while the cold number stays off the air.
[The Public](persona_the_public.md), on [Take It Up](plan_take_it_up.md), finds the case easy to picture and easy to recall, and begins to fear the danger as a common one.

## Tension

Warning of a real danger is right, and the warning is what makes the danger feel larger than it is.
There is nothing to fault: the case is true and worth telling, and heeding a vivid danger is only careful. But the ease of recall the telling builds is read as frequency, so the plot closes with the vivid instance loose and rising, felt as common because it is easy to bring to mind, the fear kindled by the telling and not by any new fact.
