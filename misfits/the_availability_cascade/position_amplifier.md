# Position: Amplifier

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The seat that sounds the vivid story louder and keeps it available, where repetition alone raises the fear.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A reach and a platform: the standing to carry the dramatic case to everyone and to keep it in front of them.
A clear case: a real danger deserves a loud warning, and the loudest warning serves the public best.
No cost for the vividness it adds, and no measure that would tell its sincere warning from a self-serving one, since both do the same work.

## Orders

Sound it louder.
Repeat the vivid case, keep it at the top of mind, and let the story carry where the cold number cannot.
Warn and warn again, and do not let a danger be forgotten.

## Loses

Any check on the fear it raises, exactly by raising it as loudly as it can.
Because the more the story is told the more available and so the more probable it feels, the seat moves the fear by repetition alone, so it cannot press the warning without inflating the risk, and cannot tell whether it is correcting an oversight or manufacturing an alarm, the sincere motive and the self-serving one running through the same act.
It gains the whole public's ear, and loses any way to warn of a danger without making it feel larger than it is.

## Drives

Toward the story sounded louder and the fear raised,
bought against any check on how large the fear grows,
so the seat that would warn of a real danger warns it into feeling common.
