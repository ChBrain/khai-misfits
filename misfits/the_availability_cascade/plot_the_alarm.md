# Plot: The Alarm

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Chamber](place_the_chamber.md), a fear loud enough to reach the ones who must answer it.
The consensus arriving from the forum, the public frightened and watching, the regulator bound to meet what its people dread.

## Action

[Answering](process_answering.md): [The Regulator](persona_the_regulator.md), holding [the regulator's seat](position_regulator.md), moves to meet the alarm the forum has raised.

## Stage

[The Regulator](persona_the_regulator.md), on [Answer the Alarm](plan_answer_the_alarm.md), takes the swelling consensus as its mandate and prepares a visible response, because a system that answers its people cannot sit still while they are frightened.
The public watches for the act it demands, the loud fear now a thing the seat is bound to answer.

## Tension

Answering a loud public fear is what a responsive government must do, and the answering binds the guard to the fear rather than the harm.
There is nothing to fault: to sit still before a frightened public is to lose the trust the system runs on. But the mandate is the fear and the fear tracks availability, so the plot closes with the alarm on the table as a thing that must be met, the response about to be set to how loud the danger is rather than how large.
