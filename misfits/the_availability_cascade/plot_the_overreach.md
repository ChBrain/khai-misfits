# Plot: The Overreach

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Chamber](place_the_chamber.md), the response about to be set by how vivid the risk is, not how large.
The mandate loud, the salient danger on everyone's lips, the dull great one unstoried and unfeared beside it.

## Action

[Answering](process_answering.md): [The Regulator](persona_the_regulator.md), holding [the regulator's seat](position_regulator.md), allocates the recall, the ban, the budget to the danger the public fears, and passes over the risk no one names.

## Stage

[The Regulator](persona_the_regulator.md), on [Answer the Alarm](plan_answer_the_alarm.md), meets the loud fear with a visible response and fences the salient small risk round, faithfully spending the guard where the alarm is.
The great dull danger, drawing no headline and no fear, waits unattended, its true magnitude the cold number no one in the room can hear.

## Tension

Meeting the fear the public raised is faithful service, and the faithful service puts the guard on the wrong risk.
There is nothing to fault: the regulator answers the mandate a democracy hands it. But the mandate tracks salience, so the plot closes with the protection spent on the vivid danger and the vast one left unmet, a public guarded against the risk that tells best and exposed to the one that harms most.
