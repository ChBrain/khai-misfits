# Piece: The Availability Cascade

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The phenomenon whole, a vivid risk repeated into a self-feeding alarm that outruns the evidence and sets the response to salience rather than size.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across all three: kindled on [the airwaves](place_the_airwaves.md), swelled in [the forum](place_the_forum.md), answered in [the chamber](place_the_chamber.md).
It is the whole circuit, the story becoming a fear becoming a rule.

## Load Bearing

Without it the pieces are separate: a vivid case, a felt fear, a crowd, a misjudged rule, with nothing to bind them.
With it they are one mechanism: repetition raises availability, availability becomes felt probability, the felt fear cascades into a shared consensus, and the consensus sets a response by salience not size, each step feeding the next. The Availability Cascade is load bearing because it is the structure entire (Kuran and Sunstein): the self-reinforcing process by which an expressed perception triggers a chain that makes the perception ever more available and so ever more believed, until it drives risk regulation, so the piece carries the loop whole, the reason no single actor need err for the alarm to outrun the truth.

## Apparent

An informed public and a responsive government, a danger recognised and met, democracy working as it should.
Seen at the surface, the whole cascade looks like a system learning of a risk and acting on it, and the truth that the fear was built by its own repetition, that the consensus measured its volume and not the danger, that the response guarded the vivid risk and left the vast one, stays hidden behind how exactly like due concern and due care each step in turn appears.
