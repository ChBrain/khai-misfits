# Piece: The Story

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The vivid dramatic case, the terrible instance easy to picture and easy to recall that the whole cascade is told from.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Breaks on [the airwaves](place_the_airwaves.md), where the vivid case is carried and repeated.
Taken up in [the forum](place_the_forum.md), where the crowd retells it and the fear becomes shared.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to make available and nothing to fear, and the risk is only a number no one pictures.
With it the whole cascade has its occasion: a real, dramatic, memorable case, true in itself, that the mind can call up at once, so the fear it feeds and the availability it lends are both undeniable. The Story is load bearing because it is the vivid pole of the asymmetry (Kuran and Sunstein; Tversky and Kahneman): the instance you can picture, retell, and dread, so the piece carries the drama whose ease of recall the dull statistic lacks, the seen case against which the true magnitude is invisible.

## Apparent

A warning worth heeding, a real danger brought vividly to light, the very stuff of a public rightly informed.
Seen at the surface, the Story looks like knowledge, a danger made known and understood, and the truth that its vividness is not its frequency, that a case so easy to picture is felt as common precisely because it is easy to picture and not because it is common, stays hidden behind how true and how terrible the instance plainly is.

## Yearbook

Sounded by [the amplifier](persona_the_amplifier.md) through [amplifying](process_amplifying.md), the vivid case that lends the fear its availability.
It is the occasion of the trap: the Story feeds [the salience](piece_the_salience.md) and swells [the consensus](piece_the_consensus.md), the vivid twin of [the cold number](piece_the_cold_number.md) no one pictures, climbing through [the availability cascade](piece_the_availability_cascade.md) into [the overreach](piece_the_overreach.md).
