# Place: The Forum

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). Where the public sees each other's alarm and the cascade runs, the square of shared opinion in which the fear becomes a crowd's.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the public meets its own opinion, a square of talk and poll and comment where everyone can see what everyone else appears to fear.
The public taking up the shared alarm, each reading the concern around them as evidence and adding a voice, the doubters keeping quiet where the doubt would cost.

## Holds

The cascading, and the consensus it builds.
Here each person meets the visible belief of all the others, so the Forum holds the point where a fear becomes shared: the alarm of others read as evidence and echoed at less cost than it is doubted, so the concern swells with belief and with silence alike, and the two cannot be told apart.

## Offers

A mirror in which the alarm proves itself.
Because the Forum shows each person how alarmed everyone else appears, it offers the public the consensus it will update on, so it hands the fear its own reflection, the shared concern whose size is read as proof of its truth, and hands it out while the private hint that might have checked it is swallowed, unspoken, into the crowd.

## Withheld

Any way to tell the believed consensus from the silent one.
The Forum cannot both show the swelling agreement and reveal how much of it is echo rather than belief, so it withholds the count of who is convinced and who is merely quiet, letting the silence read as assent, and, all shared voice and no private doubt, it withholds any sign that the alarm it reflects is loud because it is reflected, not because it is sized.
