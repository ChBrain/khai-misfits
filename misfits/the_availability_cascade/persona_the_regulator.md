# Persona: The Regulator

## Taxonomy

[Regulator](position_regulator.md). Answers the alarm the public raises, allocating the response to the fear that is loudest, the responsive official a democracy asks to meet what its people fear.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Responsive. Accountable. Bound to answer.
Is the one who must act when the public is frightened, the official who cannot tell a fearful people their fear does not merit a response and keep their trust.
No opportunism needed: often the Regulator is simply doing the job a responsive system defines, meeting the concern its public has raised.

## Action

Answers the alarm: allocates the recall, the ban, the inquiry, the budget to the danger the public fears.
Meets the loud fear with a visible response, because a system that answers its people must be seen to act on what they dread.
Does what a responsive official does: takes the public's fear as its mandate, and moves against the risk that is on everyone's lips.

## Shadow

Not a panderer, a public servant, and that is the whole difficulty: answering a loud public fear is what a responsive government must do, and doing it sets the guard by salience rather than size.
Because the mandate is the fear and the fear tracks availability, the Regulator allocates protection to the vivid small risk and passes over the dull great one no one is frightened of, so the response is spent where the alarm is and not where the harm is, and to do otherwise, to meet the statistic and ignore the story, is to defy the very public it answers to.
It serves the people's fear faithfully, and the faithful service is what puts the guard on the wrong risk.

## Tell

Says "the public is frightened and demands action: I cannot sit on my hands."
Acts, and by acting on the fear that is loudest sets the protection to the danger that tells best, not the one that harms most.
