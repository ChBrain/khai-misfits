# Piece: The Overreach

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The response set to salience rather than size, the guard spent on the vivid small risk while the dull great one waits.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Falls in [the chamber](place_the_chamber.md), where the alarm is answered with a recall, a ban, a budget.
Its cost lands back on [the forum](place_the_forum.md), where the guarded danger was never the one that harms most.

## Load Bearing

Without it the cascade is only a fright that passes, a fear with no mark left on the world.
With it the trap does its damage: the fear becomes a rule, and the protection is allocated by how vivid a danger is rather than how large, so the small dramatic risk is fenced round tenfold while the great dull one goes unattended. The Overreach is load bearing because it is where the misfit costs (Kuran and Sunstein; Sunstein): the response set to the loudest fear, the guard spent where the alarm is and not where the harm is, so the piece carries the outcome the whole cascade was building toward, a public protected against the risk that tells best and exposed to the one that harms most.

## Apparent

Prudence, a responsive system meeting its people's fear, better safe than sorry with a real danger.
Seen at the surface, the Overreach looks like care, a government acting on a public concern, and the truth that the guard has gone to the salient risk and left the vast one unmet, that spending protection on the vivid danger is spending it away from the deadly dull one, that the response tracks the story and not the harm, stays hidden behind how responsible answering a loud fear plainly looks.

## Yearbook

Set by [the regulator](persona_the_regulator.md) through [answering](process_answering.md), the guard allocated to the loudest fear.
It is the cost of the trap: the Overreach spends the protection [the consensus](piece_the_consensus.md) demanded on the vivid danger of [the story](piece_the_story.md), and leaves the vast risk of [the cold number](piece_the_cold_number.md) unmet, the mark [the availability cascade](piece_the_availability_cascade.md) leaves on the world, undone only if [the weighing](process_weighing.md) is heard.
