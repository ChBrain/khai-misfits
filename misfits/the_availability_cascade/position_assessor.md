# Position: Assessor

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The seat that would weigh the magnitude against the salience and insulate the assessment from the story, at the cost of standing and against the public's fear.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A cold number and a comparison: the base rate that sizes this danger and the great dull risk beside it, and the case that protection should track the harm.
A clear case: the way to guard the public well is to meet each risk by its magnitude, not by how vividly it frightens.
No way to make the statistic vivid, and no cover from the charge of dismissing a danger everyone can picture.

## Orders

Weigh the risk.
Hold the cold number up against the vivid case, insulate the assessment from the alarm, and ask that the response go where the harm is, not where the fear is loudest.
Size the danger before it is answered, and credit the great dull risk no story is told about.

## Loses

The public's trust and the warmth of answering its fear, exactly by sizing the danger honestly.
Because the cold number is bloodless beside the vivid case and the fear is real, telling a frightened public its alarm is out of proportion reads as waving the danger away, and crediting the statistic over the story means answering less to the loudest voice, so the seat cannot bring the response back to the evidence without spending its standing and being taken for the one who did not care.
It buys a guard set to the magnitude against the fear it must deny, and is taken, for a while, as the one who shrugged at a danger everyone could see.

## Drives

Toward the risk weighed by its size and the assessment held clear of the story,
bought against the public's fear and the standing of the one who denies it,
so the one seat that would guard the right danger must tell frightened people their fear is larger than the risk.
