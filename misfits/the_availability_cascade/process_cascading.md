# Process: Cascading

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The swelling of a shared alarm, each person updating on everyone else's concern and echoing it, the reinforcing loop that carries the fear past the evidence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A visible alarm and a crowd that can see it.
At [the forum](place_the_forum.md), each of the public reading the concern around them as evidence and adding a voice, the doubters keeping quiet where the doubt would cost.

## Direction

Toward a consensus whose size is read as its proof.
Because each person takes the shared alarm as evidence and updates toward it, and each has reason to echo the concern rather than pay the cost of lone dissent, cascading swells the consensus with belief and with silence alike, so the fear feeds on its own visibility, the more it is voiced the truer it feels and the truer it feels the more it is voiced, until the alarm has climbed far past what the facts support.

## Lever

The reason in following the crowd and holding one's tongue.
Because a shared alarm really is some evidence and echoing it really costs less than doubting a public danger, each update toward the consensus and each silence is a sensible individual move, so cascading runs on reasonable choices, the runaway fear assembled from no one being foolish or dishonest.

## Echo

A belief held because it is held, and no way to see the silence inside it.
The consensus has swelled until its size is taken as proof, and the voices that added belief cannot be told from the voices that only withheld doubt, so what reads as a public that has weighed the danger and agreed is, in truth, a crowd that has updated on its own reflection.
