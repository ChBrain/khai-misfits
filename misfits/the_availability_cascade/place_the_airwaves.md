# Place: The Airwaves

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). Where the vivid story is carried and repeated, the channel of broadcast and feed on which the dramatic case is kept at the top of mind.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the story travels, a channel of headline and broadcast and feed where a dramatic case can be told to everyone at once.
The amplifier carrying the vivid instance out and telling it again, the danger kept in front of the public while the cold number stays off the air.

## Holds

The amplifying, and the repetition it runs on.
Here the story meets its reach, so the Airwaves hold the point where a case is made available: told and retold to everyone at once, so the danger rises to the top of mind, the vivid instance always louder than the dull statistic that draws no headline.

## Offers

A carrier for the vivid case.
Because the Airwaves reward what is dramatic and repeatable, they offer the amplifier a reach the cold number cannot buy, so they hand the story its availability, the top-of-mind presence that will read as frequency, and hand it out while the statistic that would size the risk stays unaired and unheard.

## Withheld

The room for the number that would weigh the danger.
The Airwaves cannot both carry the vivid case that travels and give equal air to the dull statistic that does not, so they withhold the reach the cold number needs, letting the story crowd it out, and, all headline and no base rate, they withhold any sign that the danger they carry so well is felt as common only because they carry it so often.
