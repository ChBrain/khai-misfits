# Process: Weighing

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The contested attempt to size a danger by its magnitude against its salience, the cold number held up to turn the cascade back, at the cost of the one who holds it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A cold number and a seat that would insist on it.
At [the chamber](place_the_chamber.md), the assessor holding the base rate up against the vivid case, asking that the response go where the harm is and not where the fear is loudest.

## Direction

Toward a response set to the size of the danger, if the number can be heard.
Because the cold number sizes the risk the story cannot, weighing would insulate the assessment from the vividness and set the guard to the magnitude, so it drives toward the danger met by its harm: but the number is bloodless beside the case, and to say the fear is out of proportion reads as dismissing it, so the process turns the cascade back only by spending the standing of the one who runs it.

## Lever

The discipline of sizing a danger by its harm.
Because the base rate is the truest measure of the risk and protection should track the harm, holding the cold number up is the careful analyst's plain duty, so weighing runs on sound method: yet the same move that corrects the alarm is heard as callousness, so the process costs its owner exactly by being right.

## Echo

An alarm settled, or a number waved off as cold.
Either the risk is weighed by its size and the response comes back to the evidence, bought against the public's fear and the assessor's standing, or the cold number is taken for the shrug of someone who did not care, and the cascade climbs on, the truest measure of the danger left the least welcome voice in the room.
