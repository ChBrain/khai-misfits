# Plan: Weigh the Risk

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the assessor's seat](position_assessor.md), inside [The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Assessor](position_assessor.md)

## Direction

Hold the cold number up against the vivid case and set the response to the magnitude. Insulate the assessment from the story, ask that protection go where the harm is, and credit the great dull risk no one fears. Size the danger before it is answered, even when no one wants to hear it.

## Orders

([The Assessor](persona_the_assessor.md) weighs the risk by its size against the salience, and would bring the response back to the evidence, at the cost of its standing and against the public's fear.)

## Implementation

([The Chamber](place_the_chamber.md); the [weighing](process_weighing.md) process; the cold number would size the danger against [the salience](piece_the_salience.md), turning [the availability cascade](piece_the_availability_cascade.md) back ... if the number could be heard over the story.)

## Targets

- [ ] the cold number is held up against the vivid case
- [ ] the assessment is insulated from the vividness of the story
- [ ] the response is asked to track the magnitude, not the salience
- [ ] the weighing is taken for callousness and waved off, or heard and the alarm settles
