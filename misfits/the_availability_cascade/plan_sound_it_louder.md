# Plan: Sound It Louder

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the amplifier's seat](position_amplifier.md), inside [The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Amplifier](position_amplifier.md)

## Direction

Carry the vivid case to everyone and keep it at the top of mind. Tell and retell the dramatic instance, let the story carry where the cold number cannot, and warn again before the danger is forgotten. Press the case that matters, loudly, and serve the public with the loudest warning.

## Orders

([The Amplifier](persona_the_amplifier.md) finds the dramatic case and sounds it louder, sincerely or for its own ends, keeping the danger vivid and available while the cold number stays dull and unspoken.)

## Implementation

([The Airwaves](place_the_airwaves.md); the [amplifying](process_amplifying.md) process; the repetition feeds [the salience](piece_the_salience.md), so [the story](piece_the_story.md) rises to the top of mind and [the cold number](piece_the_cold_number.md) stays unheard beside it.)

## Targets

- [ ] the vivid case is repeated until it is the first thing anyone recalls
- [ ] the availability of the danger is raised by repetition alone
- [ ] the cold number is left dull and unspoken beside the story
- [ ] the sincere warning and the self-serving one do the same work and cannot be told apart
