# Piece: The Salience

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The ease of recall read as frequency, the availability heuristic's product by which a vivid danger feels more probable than a common dull one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Rises on [the airwaves](place_the_airwaves.md), where repetition makes the case ever easier to recall.
Carried into [the forum](place_the_forum.md), where the felt probability becomes a shared alarm.

## Load Bearing

Without it repetition would be only noise, and a story told often would be no more feared than a story told once.
With it the telling becomes the fear: because the mind reads the ease of bringing a case to mind as a sign of how often it happens, the Salience turns availability into felt probability, so a rare vivid danger comes to feel likelier than a common dull one. The Salience is load bearing because it is the mechanism itself (Tversky and Kahneman): the availability heuristic that converts recall into estimated frequency, so the piece carries the very step by which a repeated story inflates a risk, the bridge from vividness to fear.

## Apparent

A sensible instinct, the reasonable sense that a danger one keeps hearing about must be one worth worrying over.
Seen at the surface, the Salience looks like ordinary prudence, taking familiarity for evidence, and the truth that ease of recall tracks how often a thing is told and not how often it happens, so that the instinct reads repetition as frequency and hands the loudest story the highest fear, stays hidden behind how naturally a well-known danger feels like a common one.

## Yearbook

Raised by [amplifying](process_amplifying.md) and fed by [the story](piece_the_story.md), the felt probability that vividness lends a risk.
It is the engine of the trap: the Salience turns the availability of [the story](piece_the_story.md) into the fear the crowd updates on in [the consensus](piece_the_consensus.md), the reason [the cold number](piece_the_cold_number.md) cannot compete, driving [the availability cascade](piece_the_availability_cascade.md) upward.
