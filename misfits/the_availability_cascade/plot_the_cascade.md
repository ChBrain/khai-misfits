# Plot: The Cascade

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Forum](place_the_forum.md), everyone able to see that everyone is alarmed.
The vivid fear already loose, the crowd reading the concern around it, the cost of lone doubt plain to anyone who would voice it.

## Action

[Cascading](process_cascading.md): [The Public](persona_the_public.md), holding [the public's seat](position_public.md), updates on the shared alarm and adds a voice, while those who privately doubt keep silent.

## Stage

[The Public](persona_the_public.md), on [Take It Up](plan_take_it_up.md), reads the visible concern of everyone else as evidence and echoes it, and the ones who doubt hold their tongue rather than pay the cost of dismissing a public danger.
The consensus swells with belief and with silence alike, its growing size taken by each new voice as proof it is right.

## Tension

Following a shared alarm is reasonable, and the following is what makes the alarm.
There is nothing to fault: a broad concern really is some evidence, and holding one's tongue really costs less than lone dissent. But each update and each silence swells the consensus the next will update on, so the plot closes with the alarm self-feeding, its size read as its truth, a crowd converged on a fear built from its own reflection.
