# Persona: The Assessor

## Taxonomy

[Assessor](position_assessor.md). Holds the cold number up against the vivid story and asks that the risk be met by its size, the analyst who would weigh magnitude over salience and loses standing by it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Measured. Numerate. Unmoved by the story.
Is the one with the base rate and the comparison, who knows this vivid risk is small and that dull one is enormous, and would set the response to the magnitude.
No coldness of heart in it: only the discipline of sizing a danger by how much it harms rather than how well it frightens.

## Action

Weighs the risk by its magnitude, holding the cold number up against the vivid case.
Asks that protection go where the harm is, that the small dramatic danger not crowd out the great dull one, and that the fear be sized before it is answered.
Does what a careful analyst does: refuses to read vividness as frequency, and insists the statistic be met even when no story is told about it.

## Shadow

Not a heartless contrarian, a careful analyst, and that is the whole difficulty: sizing a danger by its magnitude is right, and doing it against a public alarm reads as waving the danger away.
Because the cold number is bloodless beside the vivid case and the fear is genuinely felt, the Assessor who says the alarm is out of proportion is heard not as correcting an error but as dismissing a danger, so the one move that would bring the response back to the evidence costs the one who makes it their standing, and the more disproportionate the fear the more callous the correction sounds.
It holds the truest measure of the risk, and holding it up is what makes it look like the person who did not care.

## Tell

Says "the number says this is rare and that is common: we are guarding the wrong one."
Says it, and is taken for the one who shrugged at a danger everyone can picture, so the truest measure is the least welcome voice.
