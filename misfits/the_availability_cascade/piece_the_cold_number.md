# Piece: The Cold Number

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The base rate and the true magnitude, unvivid and unstoried, the measure that would size the danger and the great dull risk no one fears.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Kept off [the airwaves](place_the_airwaves.md), where the vivid story crowds it out.
Held up in [the chamber](place_the_chamber.md), where the assessor would weigh it against the alarm and lose.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing the fear is out of proportion to, and the alarm is just concern with no measure beside it.
With it the trap has its wronged pole: a true magnitude that sizes the vivid danger small and the dull one vast, so the misallocation the cascade drives can be seen for what it is. The Cold Number is load bearing because it is the invisible pole of the asymmetry (Kuran and Sunstein): the base rate you cannot picture, retell, or dread, so the piece carries the measure whose bloodlessness is exactly what the vivid story exploits, the unseen truth against which the salient fear is out of scale, and the great unstoried risk the guard leaves unmet.

## Apparent

A cold abstraction, a bloodless statistic that seems to shrug at a danger everyone can feel.
Seen at the surface, the Cold Number looks like indifference, a figure that waves off a real fear, and the truth that it is the truest measure of the risk, that its coldness is only the absence of a story and not the absence of care, that the danger it names as vast is the one the alarm has left unguarded, stays hidden behind how heartless a number sounds beside a vivid case.

## Yearbook

Held up by [the assessor](persona_the_assessor.md) through [weighing](process_weighing.md), the base rate that would size the danger by its harm.
