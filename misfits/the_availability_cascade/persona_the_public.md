# Persona: The Public

## Taxonomy

[Public](position_public.md). Takes up the vivid danger and passes it on, updating on the shared alarm and echoing it, genuinely careful, and the crowd whose swelling concern the response must answer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Reasonable. Alert to a real danger.
Is the one who hears the vivid case, sees that everyone is alarmed, and quite sensibly takes the shared concern as a sign the risk is real, adding a voice to the warning.
No hysteria in it: only ordinary people reasoning from what everyone around them seems to know.

## Action

Takes up the alarm and passes it on, believing what everyone believes and voicing it.
Reads the ease of picturing the danger as a sign of how often it happens, and the alarm of others as evidence it is right to be alarmed.
Does what a careful person does: heeds a danger the whole world is naming, and does not stay silent while a risk goes unwarned.

## Shadow

Not a mob, a crowd of reasonable people, and that is the whole difficulty: each move is sound, and the sum of the sound moves is a fear past the evidence.
Because a shared alarm really is some evidence, and echoing it really is cheaper than paying the cost of lone dissent, and a vivid case really is easier to recall, the Public converges on a belief whose size is read as its proof, so the concern that swells is the reasonable response to a consensus that its own reasonableness is building.
Each one is right to take the alarm up, and the taking up is what makes the alarm.

## Tell

Says "everyone knows this is dangerous, you would have to be careless to doubt it."
Knows it because everyone says it, and says it because everyone knows, and cannot tell the belief in the consensus from the silence inside it.
