# Persona: The Amplifier

## Taxonomy

[Amplifier](position_amplifier.md). Sounds the vivid story louder and keeps it available, the availability entrepreneur who repeats the dramatic case until it is the first thing anyone recalls.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Impassioned. Tireless. On the side of the warning.
Is the one who finds the dramatic case and carries it to everyone, the reporter, the campaigner, the official who will not let a danger be forgotten.
No cynicism required: often the Amplifier sincerely fears the risk and believes the loudest warning is the truest service.

## Action

Repeats the vivid story and keeps it in front of everyone, telling and retelling the dramatic instance.
Makes the danger available: easy to picture, easy to recall, always at the top of mind, and lets the cold number stay dull and unspoken beside it.
Does what an advocate does: presses the case that matters to it, and will not be quiet while a danger goes unwarned.

## Shadow

Not a fraud, an advocate, and that is the whole difficulty: raising a risk one sincerely fears is honest, and raising it raises the availability that inflates it.
Because the more a story is told the more available it becomes, and the more available the more probable it feels, the Amplifier moves the fear simply by repeating the case, without any new fact about the risk, so the sincere warning and the self-serving one do the same work and cannot be told apart, and the vividness they add is read by everyone as frequency.
It warns of a real danger, and the warning is what makes the danger feel larger than it is.

## Tell

Says "I am only making sure people know: how can telling the truth about a danger be wrong?"
Tells a true story, and by telling it and telling it makes a true story feel like a common one.
