# Position: Public

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The seat that takes up the shared alarm and passes it on, believing the consensus and adding to it, where the fear becomes a crowd's.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A voice in the consensus and a mind that reads recall as frequency: the standing to believe the shared alarm and to swell it.
A clear case: a danger everyone is naming, easy to picture, is a danger it would be careless to ignore.
No way to tell the belief in the consensus from the silence in it, and no reward for being the lone doubter of a public fear.

## Orders

Take the alarm up.
Believe the danger the whole world is naming, add a voice to the warning, and read the shared concern as the evidence it looks like.
Heed what everyone heeds, and do not stay silent while a risk goes unwarned.

## Loses

The chance to weigh the danger on its own, exactly by heeding the crowd that seems to have weighed it already.
Because the shared alarm is taken as evidence and echoed at less cost than it is doubted, the seat updates toward the consensus and adds to it, so its own private hint is swallowed and the belief it holds is the belief it helped build, and to hold out against a public fear is to pay a cost no careful person is repaid for.
It gains the safety of believing what everyone believes, and loses any measure of the risk that is not the crowd's.

## Drives

Toward the alarm taken up and swelled,
bought against any private weighing of the danger,
so the seat that would judge the risk carefully judges it by the size of the crowd that fears it.
