# Process: Answering

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). The allocation of the response to the loudest fear, the guard set by how vivid a danger is rather than how large.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A public fear loud enough to demand an answer.
At [the chamber](place_the_chamber.md), the consensus arriving from the forum and the regulator bound, in a system that answers its people, to meet it with a visible act.

## Direction

Toward protection spent where the alarm is, not where the harm is.
Because the mandate is the public's fear and the fear tracks availability, answering allocates the recall, the ban, the budget to the salient small risk and passes over the dull great one no one dreads, so the guard is set by the vividness of the danger rather than its size, and the risk with the better story takes the protection the larger risk needed.

## Lever

The duty of a responsive system to answer its people's fear.
Because a government that answers its public must meet a fear this loud, and to sit still is to lose the trust it runs on, each move to answer the alarm is a sound democratic act, so answering runs on responsiveness itself, the misallocation assembled from no one pandering and no one shirking.

## Echo

A guard set to the vivid and not the vast.
The response has gone to the danger on everyone's lips and the great dull risk waits unattended, so what reads as a system protecting its people from what they fear is, in truth, a guard allocated by salience, faithfully answering the loudest alarm and leaving the largest harm unmet.
