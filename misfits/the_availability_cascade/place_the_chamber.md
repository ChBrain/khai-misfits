# Place: The Chamber

## Taxonomy

[The Availability Cascade](play_the_availability_cascade.md). Where the alarm is answered and the cold number would be weighed, the room of policy in which the response is set and the assessment contested.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The room where the public's fear becomes a response, a chamber of hearing and rule and budget where the alarm raised outside is met with an act.
The regulator moving to answer the loud fear, and the assessor holding the cold number up against it, the story on the table beside the statistic no one wants to hear.

## Holds

The answering, and the weighing that contests it.
Here the alarm meets the lever, so the Chamber holds the point where the response is allocated: a fear this loud demanding an answer, so the guard is set to the salient danger, the vivid risk on everyone's lips always more pressing than the dull one no one names.

## Offers

A lever for the loud fear, and a bench for the number that would check it.
Because the Chamber is where the public's fear is bound to be answered, it offers the regulator the power to meet the alarm and offers the assessor the floor to weigh it, so it hands out both the response set to salience and the contested case for size, and hands them out knowing the vivid story will be heard over the cold number nearly every time.

## Withheld

The room to answer the fear and still meet the risk by its size.
The Chamber cannot both respond to the loudest alarm and hold the guard for the danger that is largest, so it withholds the standing the assessor needs to be heard over the story, letting the salient fear claim the lever, and, all mandate and no base rate, it withholds any sign that the danger it answers so faithfully is the one that tells best, not the one that harms most.
