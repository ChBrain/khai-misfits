## Direction

Hold a complete and consistent ordering of the options and report it exactly, preferring each to the next for reasons you could give and letting no loop into your own ranking, since the seat's whole part is to supply one true preference to the count. Cast it honestly, with no eye to how others will vote and no attempt to game the tally, because the honest move is to say what you actually prefer and trust the vote to weigh it fairly. Ask only to be counted alike, and accept the majority as the group's own choice, since the plan's business is a sincere input and not a decided outcome, and it does its whole duty when the ranking it casts is true.
