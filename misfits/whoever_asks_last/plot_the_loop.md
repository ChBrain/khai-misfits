# Plot: The Loop

## Taxonomy

[Whoever Asks Last](play_whoever_asks_last.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Division](place_the_division.md), the pairings compared.
The honest ballots weighed two options at a time, and the pairwise verdicts set side by side.

## Action

[Cycling](process_cycling.md): a majority prefers A to B, and a majority B to C, and a majority C to A, so the group's preference runs in a ring, and on [the Grid](place_the_grid.md), where the choice spans more than one issue, no centre holds at all.

## Stage

[The Voter](persona_the_voter.md) collectively composes [the cycle](piece_the_cycle.md) without any member intending it, each pairwise majority sound and the loop of them appearing only in the whole.
Every contest has a clear winner, and the group has none.

## Tension

The majorities are all sound, and together they are senseless.
There is no fault: the verdict in each pair is honest and clear, so the loop is not a miscount or a trick but the plain result of summing rankings that were each in order, a property the aggregate has and no ballot does. But a preference that circles has no top, so the plot closes with a room that has voted decisively in every pair and prefers nothing to everything, the choice the group might call its own shown not to exist, and the decision that must still be reached now waiting on something other than the votes, since the votes have said all they can and named no winner.
