# Process: Ranking

## Taxonomy

[Whoever Asks Last](play_whoever_asks_last.md). The forming and casting of each member's sincere, complete, consistent ordering, the honest input the vote is built on and the one part of the machine that never fails.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Voter](persona_the_voter.md), at [the Division](place_the_division.md), ordering the options by their true lights and reporting them straight.
Begins whenever the room takes up a choice and each member settles what they prefer to what.

## Direction

Turns a member's preferences into a sound ballot.
Because each voter holds a consistent ordering and reports it honestly, ranking converts private preference into a complete, loop-free ballot cast into the count, so the process supplies the vote its raw material in exactly the rational, sincere form a fair decision assumes, nothing miscast and nothing gamed.

## Lever

The soundness of an honest, consistent preference.
Because a voter can rank the options without contradiction and has every reason to report them truly, ranking works through a rule that is simply right: prefer what you prefer and say so, so the lever is individual rationality itself, faultless in every ballot, which is why the trouble that follows can be laid at no voter's feet, the inputs clean and the fault, if any, only in their sum.

## Echo

A full set of honest ballots, and no defect anywhere in them.
The echo of ranking is a count built entirely of sincere, consistent orderings, each defensible and each fairly weighed, so the room stands with its true preferences fully expressed and nothing yet gone wrong, the material of the decision as sound as material can be and the incoherence still hidden in how it will add.
