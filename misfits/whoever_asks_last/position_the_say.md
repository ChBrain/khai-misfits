# Position: The Say

## Taxonomy

[Whoever Asks Last](play_whoever_asks_last.md). The seat of the individual vote, which holds one complete and consistent ranking and an equal weight in the count, and can shape the outcome only through the pairs it votes and never through the whole it helps compose.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

One honest ranking, and an equal weight with every other.
The seat holds a complete and consistent ordering of the options, reported straight, and the entitlement to have it counted alike, so what it has is a full and sincere preference and a fair share of the tally.
It holds a vote in each pair the ordering implies, its say in A against B and B against C fixed by the ranking it carries.

## Orders

Rank by your lights, report straight, accept the count.
Hold a consistent ordering, cast it honestly with no eye to how others will vote, and take the majority's verdict as the group's, since the seat's whole part is to supply one true preference to the sum.
Ask only to be weighed equally, because the seat's claim is not to decide the outcome but to be counted in deciding it.

## Loses

The power to make the sum cohere, which no single ranking holds.
The Say can supply an honest preference and cannot supply the group a coherent one, because coherence is a property of the aggregate and no member controls the aggregate: the seat casts its votes in each pair and has no lever on how those pair-votes combine, so a sincere ranking is fully spent in the casting and reaches the outcome only as one input among many. What the position forfeits is authorship of the result: it cannot make its first choice the group's, cannot break a cycle its own votes are consistent with, and cannot tell, from inside its honest ballot, that the sum will have no head. It loses, too, any purchase on the order the votes are taken in, which is where the outcome is really settled, so the seat that is counted fairly in every pair has no say at all in the sequence that turns those pairs into a winner.

## Drives

Toward a vote sincere in every part and a sum answerable to no member,
and a seat fully counted and never decisive, its honesty complete and its reach ending at its own pair-votes,
so the position that gives the group its truest input is the position that cannot give the group a coherent choice, its say real and its authorship none.
