# Piece: The Cycle

## Taxonomy

[Whoever Asks Last](play_whoever_asks_last.md). The intransitive majority, the ring in which a majority prefers A to B and B to C and C to A, the headless preference a room of consistent voters can compose.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Forms at [the Division](place_the_division.md), where the pairs are counted, and is the rule not the exception on [the Grid](place_the_grid.md).
Flows as a loop of honest majorities, each pairwise verdict sound and the chain of them closing on itself, a group preference that comes back to where it started.

## Load Bearing

Without the Cycle there is a winner: it is the ring in the majority that leaves the group with no option it prefers to all others, the one thing that makes the outcome the order's to decide.
The Cycle is load bearing because it is the paradox at the root, the finding that sincere, transitive individual rankings can aggregate into an intransitive majority (Condorcet). It is what removes the group's own choice, since a preference that loops has no top to crown, and it is exactly what hands the decision to the sequence, because a winner must still be found and only the order can find one in a ring. So the Cycle carries the trap's first turn: the room votes honestly and fully and still prefers nothing to everything, so the coherence the vote was meant to yield is absent from the counts and must come from outside them. Every arbitrariness downstream begins in this loop, which is made of nothing but fair majorities and belongs to no voter who cast one.

## Apparent

Just a close and complicated vote, a hard three-way call.
Seen at the surface, the Cycle looks like an ordinary contest that happens to be tight, a room split several ways with no clear favourite, and the truth that there is not merely no clear winner but no possible one, that the majorities do not just disagree but loop, that the tightness is a ring and not a near-miss, stays hidden behind how much a headless vote resembles a close one.

## Yearbook

Composed by [the Voter](persona_the_voter.md) without intending it, inherited by [the Convenor](persona_the_convenor.md) as the thing an order must resolve, and untouched by [the Framer](persona_the_framer.md), whose fair rule cannot unbend it.
It is the root of the misfit: the Cycle is what [cycling](process_cycling.md) produces and [ranking](process_ranking.md) feeds, the loop [the sequence](piece_the_sequence.md) turns into a winner and [the impossibility](piece_the_impossibility.md) forbids any fair rule from straightening, the headless preference [the made majority](piece_the_made_majority.md) is built on.
