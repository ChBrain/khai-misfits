# Place: The Grid

## Taxonomy

[Whoever Asks Last](play_whoever_asks_last.md). The space of the choice when it spans more than one issue, a map of options across several dimensions where no centre holds, the condition under which the ring is the rule and the escape is shut.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A richer choice, options that differ on more than one thing at once.
Shown at the surface is a fuller kind of decision: not a single line from less to more but a field, options ranged across several issues together, budget and pace and place all at once, the sort of many-sided question real choices actually are.

## Holds

Holds the dimensions, and the missing centre.
The Grid is where the options sit apart on more than one axis, so it holds a choice that cannot be laid on a single line, and in that spread it holds the absence of a middle: with two or more issues in play there is generically no point that a majority will not walk away from toward some other, no centre that holds, so the place that makes the choice rich is the place that removes its core.

## Offers

Offers the full shape of a real choice, and the loss of a stable winner with it.
Here the decision keeps all its dimensions rather than collapsing to one, so the Grid offers honesty to how many-sided the question is; and offers, through that same fullness, the general failure of a Condorcet winner, since once the options spread across issues the majority relation covers the whole field and any point can be beaten, so the space that honours the real choice is the space where no choice is stably the group's.

## Withheld

Withholds the single line that would save it, and the median that lives there.
What the Grid cannot offer is one axis on which preferences line up single-peaked, because the choice genuinely spans several issues and cannot be honestly flattened to one, so the median voter that would hold a one-dimensional vote has no line to stand on. The place that keeps the choice whole withholds the very restriction that dissolves the ring, so the escape that works on a single line is shut here, and the cycling that is a special case in one dimension is the ordinary case in this field.
