# Plan: Build a Fair Rule

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Method](position_the_method.md), inside [Whoever Asks Last](play_whoever_asks_last.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Method](position_the_method.md)

## Direction

Design a rule that turns the members' honest rankings into a single group choice and keeps every fairness the vote was meant to hold: defined for all preferences, faithful to what all prefer, judging each pair on that pair alone, and ruled by no one voter. Test each rule against the conditions and, when it breaks one, take that as a draft to improve and write the next, since the plan believes method can settle what the raw vote cannot. Turn, if the straight rules fail, to the escapes, narrowing what voters may prefer or admitting intensity or seating a decider, and reckon the cost each time, because the plan's business is coherence with fairness and it must see, rule by rule, that the coherence returns only as a fairness is spent.

## Orders

([The Framer](persona_the_framer.md) drafts rule after rule toward all four conditions, watches each keep three and break one, and turns to the priced escapes, seeking in good faith a fair and decisive method.)
