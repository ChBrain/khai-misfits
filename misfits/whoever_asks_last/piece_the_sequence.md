# Piece: The Sequence

## Taxonomy

[Whoever Asks Last](play_whoever_asks_last.md). The order in which the pairings are put, the running order of the contests, the thing that turns a headless cycle into a definite winner and fixes which one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set on [the Order Paper](place_the_order_paper.md), enacted through [the Division](place_the_division.md).
Flows as the schedule of contests, this pair first and that one next, a plain list that reads as procedure and decides the result.

## Load Bearing

Without the Sequence the cycle stays a cycle: it is the order of the pairings that resolves the loop into a survivor, the mechanism by which an incoherent group preference is made to yield a coherent decision.
The Sequence is load bearing because it is where the outcome is actually chosen, the agenda control a cyclic majority opens: in a ring every option can be beaten by some other, so the one that survives is the one the order shields until last, and whoever sets the order sets the winner, steering sincere voters to any option by the schedule alone (McKelvey and Schofield). It carries the trap's centre, the point at which a fair procedure becomes a decisive act: the votes are honest and the counts are clean, and still the result is the sequence's, so the legitimacy of every step coexists with the arbitrariness of the whole. This piece is what makes the decision real and authorless at once, a winner that no preference chose and the order did.

## Apparent

Just the agenda, the housekeeping order of business.
Seen at the surface, the Sequence looks like a neutral schedule, the dull matter of which motion comes first, and the truth that this schedule is the decision, that changing the order changes the winner with no vote altered, that the housekeeping is the whole outcome wearing the clothes of procedure, stays hidden behind how completely an order of business reads as mere order of business.

## Yearbook

Set by [the Convenor](persona_the_convenor.md) as a duty and enacted as a verdict, borne by [the Voter](persona_the_voter.md) as a result their honest ballot did not choose, and unreachable by [the Framer](persona_the_framer.md), whose fair rule cannot make the order not matter.
It is the trap's centre: the Sequence is what [sequencing](process_sequencing.md) sets, the resolution [the cycle](piece_the_cycle.md) demands and cannot supply itself, the hand [the made majority](piece_the_made_majority.md) is dealt by, the thing no [method](position_the_method.md) can render harmless without a fairness spent.
