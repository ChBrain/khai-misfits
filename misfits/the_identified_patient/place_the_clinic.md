# Place: The Clinic

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). Where the one is treated as the problem, the consulting room the patient is brought to and the symptom met alone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the suffering member is brought to be healed, a consulting room where the symptom is taken seriously and worked on with skill.
The clinician meeting the presented trouble, the patient seen singly, the family that brought them waiting outside the frame.

## Holds

The treating, and the frame it ratifies.
Here the referral meets the skill, so the Clinic holds the point where the one is confirmed as the unit: a patient presented as the problem, met and relieved, so it is where good care is spent on the member alone and the family that sent them stays off the table, the presenting complaint always the whole of what is seen.

## Offers

A cure for the symptom, and a name for the patient.
Because the Clinic treats the one who is brought, it offers the family relief for its suffering member and offers the patient care and a diagnosis, so it hands out real help for a real symptom, and hands it out framed on the one, ratifying that the presented member is the trouble and leaving the whole that made them ill untreated.

## Withheld

Any reach to the system that sent the patient.
The Clinic cannot both treat the one presented and treat the whole that framed them, so it withholds the question of who sent the patient and what the symptom was for, taking the referral as it comes, and, all care for the member and no eye on the family, it withholds any sign that the trouble it heals so well in the one is the trouble of a whole that is not in the room.
