# Process: Stabilising

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The symptom doing the family's work, the trouble in the one detouring the conflict and holding the whole together, resisting the patient's recovery.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A symptom surfaced in the one and a whole in need of steadying.
At [the hearth](place_the_hearth.md), the family closing round its patient, the shared worry detouring the fracture, the balance the trouble buys taking hold.

## Direction

Toward a whole held steady by a member kept unwell.
Because the symptom gives the family a common concern and draws every eye off the rift, stabilising turns the trouble in the one into the balance of the whole: it detours the conflict, unites the members, and holds the system together, so when the patient begins to improve the whole grows anxious and pulls them back, or recruits another to carry it, because a system that needs a patient keeps one.

## Lever

The steadiness a shared trouble brings a frightened whole.
Because a family under an unfaced strain is genuinely held together by having one visible thing to worry over, the symptom really does steady it, so stabilising runs on a real and welcome calm, the resistance to the patient's recovery assembled from no one wishing them ill, only from a whole that cannot yet stand without them.

## Echo

A stability built on a member who must not get well.
The symptom has done the family's work and the recovery has been resisted, so what reads as a family at last at peace around its patient is, in truth, a balance held up by a trouble in one, steady only so long as the one stays unwell.
