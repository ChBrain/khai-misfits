# Process: Surfacing

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The naming of the whole as the unit and the bringing of the fracture into the open, the systemic treatment that turns the trap back, at the cost of the one who runs it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A systemic eye and a whole brought into the room.
At [the session](place_the_session.md), the systemist reading the trouble across the family, the fracture drawn toward the light, the conflict the symptom hid asked to be faced.

## Direction

Toward the trouble met where it lives, if the family can bear it.
Because the symptom's whole function is to hide the fracture, surfacing names the whole as the unit and brings the rift into the open, so it drives toward the conflict faced and the trouble treated at its source: but the fracture surfaced is the very thing the family formed the symptom to avoid, so the process meets the trouble only by risking the whole and spending the peace the symptom bought.

## Lever

The truth that the trouble lives in the system, not the member.
Because the fracture is where the trouble actually is and the symptom only where it shows, naming the whole is the truest reading of the trap, so surfacing runs on a sound and caring insight: yet the same move that would heal the whole is felt as blaming the well and unsettling the peace, so the process costs its owner exactly by being right.

## Echo

A whole facing its fracture, or a patient sent back to be healed alone.
Either the system is named as the unit and the trouble is met where it lives, bought against the family's stabiliser and the patient's role, or the surfaced conflict proves unbearable and the family retreats to its symptom, sending the patient back to the clinic, and the system keeps its patient.
