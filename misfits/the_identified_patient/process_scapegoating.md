# Process: Scapegoating

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The settling of a whole's strain onto one member, the anxiety of the system landing on the one available to carry it, by no one's design.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A strain the whole cannot face and an anxiety that must land somewhere.
At [the hearth](place_the_hearth.md), the family under a fracture it dares not name, its tension seeking a place to go, and the member most available to receive it.

## Direction

Toward the trouble of all becoming the trouble of one.
Because the strain of the whole is unbearable to face and must go somewhere, scapegoating settles it on the available member, who becomes the symptom-bearer through whom the system discharges its tension, so the anxiety of all surfaces in the one, and the more the family treats the one as the trouble, the more the one becomes it.

## Lever

The relief of a trouble named and a whole kept together.
Because facing the fracture might break the family apart and a suffering member can be worried over and helped, settling the strain on the one is the loving and bearable move, so scapegoating runs on love and fear and not on malice, the patient selected by an anxiety that must land, not by a family that means them harm.

## Echo

A member carrying the strain of the whole, and a family that dares not see it.
The trouble of all has surfaced in the one and been taken for the one's own, so what reads as a single suffering member the family gathers round is, in truth, a system that has settled its fracture on a bearer and called the bearer the problem.
