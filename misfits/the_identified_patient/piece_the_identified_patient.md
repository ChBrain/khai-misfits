# Piece: The Identified Patient

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The phenomenon whole, a system settling its strain on one member who turns symptomatic, so treating the patient preserves the fracture that made them ill.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across all three: settled at [the hearth](place_the_hearth.md), treated at [the clinic](place_the_clinic.md), named only in [the session](place_the_session.md).
It is the whole circuit, the strain of the many surfacing and healed in the one.

## Load Bearing

Without it the pieces are separate: a hidden strain, a suffering member, a diagnosis, a fragile calm, with nothing to bind them.
With it they are one mechanism: the fracture cannot be faced, the strain settles on one, the symptom surfaces and steadies the whole, the frame names the one as the trouble, and treating the one preserves the system, each part holding the next. The Identified Patient is load bearing because it is the structure entire (Bowen; Minuchin; Vogel and Bell; Jackson): the member in whom a whole locates its dysfunction, so the piece carries the trap whole, the reason no one need scapegoat for the strain of the many to become the illness of one.

## Apparent

A family caring for its unwell member, a clinician healing a real symptom, love and treatment doing what they should.
Seen at the surface, the whole trap looks like care meeting suffering, a troubled one helped by those around them, and the truth that the trouble is the whole's and surfaces in the one, that the treatment aimed at the patient keeps the system that made them ill, that a family which needs a patient keeps one, stays hidden behind how exactly like love and good care each step in turn appears.
