# Position: Patient

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The seat the strain settles on and the symptom surfaces in, held in the role by the care and place it gives, where the trouble of the whole becomes the trouble of the one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A symptom that is real and a role that is given: the genuine trouble it carries and the care and place the role affords.
A clear case: it is truly unwell, and its suffering is its own, felt and not feigned.
No say in being chosen to carry the strain, and no way to recover without losing the place the symptom gave.

## Orders

Bear the trouble.
Carry the symptom the whole has settled on you, hold the place the family gathers round, and take the care and the concern the role draws.
Be the one who is unwell, and let the trouble of the whole surface in you.

## Loses

Its own account of the trouble, exactly by carrying the whole's.
Because the symptom surfaces the strain of the system and draws the care the role affords, the seat is held from both sides: the family needs the trouble that steadies it, and the patient is given an identity and a care that recovery would take, so it takes into itself the strain of the whole and comes to hold that the strain is its own, and cannot get well without giving up the place being ill had made for it.
It gains a care, a name, and a place in the family's worry, and loses any trouble of its own that is not the whole's.

## Drives

Toward the symptom borne and the strain taken in,
bought against its own recovery and its own account of the trouble,
so the seat the system settled its strain on comes to hold the strain as its own.
