# Persona: The Systemist

## Taxonomy

[Systemist](position_systemist.md). Would treat the whole as the unit that is unwell and surface the fracture the symptom hides, at the cost of the family's stabiliser and the patient's role.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Perceptive. Unsettling. Looking past the one.
Is the one who sees the family in the symptom, the therapist or observer who asks not what is wrong with the patient but what the patient is for.
No coldness toward the sufferer in it: only the refusal to take the identified one as the whole of the trouble.

## Action

Names the whole as the unit that is unwell, and holds the fracture the symptom hides up to the light.
Asks the family to face the conflict it formed the symptom to avoid, and would treat the system rather than the member the system settled its strain on.
Does what a systemic eye does: reads the trouble of the one as the trouble of the whole, and treats it there.

## Shadow

Not a cold contrarian, a clear-eyed helper, and that is the whole difficulty: naming the system is right, and doing it surfaces the very fracture the family cannot bear.
Because the symptom's whole function is to hide the rift and steady the whole, the Systemist who would treat the system must let the hidden conflict into the open where it might break the thing apart, and must take from the patient the role that gave them care and from the family the trouble that held it together, so the one move that meets the trouble where it lives is felt by the family as the one who blames the well and unsettles the peace, and is resisted exactly because it works.
It would heal the whole, and healing the whole is what the whole was built to prevent.

## Tell

Says "the patient is not the problem: the patient is where the family's problem shows."
Says it, and is heard as the one who will not just help the poor sufferer and let the family be, so the truest reading of the trouble is the least welcome in the room.
