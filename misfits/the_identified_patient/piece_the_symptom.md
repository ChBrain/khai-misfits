# Piece: The Symptom

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The visible trouble the one carries, the real and nameable suffering the whole gathers round, the decoy for the fracture.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Surfaces at [the hearth](place_the_hearth.md), where the strain settles on the one.
Taken to [the clinic](place_the_clinic.md), where it is treated in the patient alone.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to rally to and nothing to treat, and the fracture would sit in the open with nowhere to hide.
With it the trap has its occasion: a genuine trouble in one member, visible and nameable, that the family can gather round and the clinician can treat, so the relief it brings and the eyes it draws are both real. The Symptom is load bearing because it is the visible pole of the displacement (Vogel and Bell; Minuchin): the trouble you can see, name, and treat, standing in for the rift you cannot, so the piece carries the decoy whose very reality is what makes it work, the loud thing that keeps the quiet fracture out of view.

## Apparent

The problem itself, the plain trouble that a family and a clinician rightly move to heal.
Seen at the surface, the Symptom looks like the whole of what is wrong, a suffering member who needs help, and the truth that it is the strain of the system surfacing in the one, that its function is to hide the fracture and steady the whole, that healing it in the patient alone leaves the real trouble untouched, stays hidden behind how real and how treatable the suffering plainly is.
