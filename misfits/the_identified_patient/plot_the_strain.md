# Plot: The Strain

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Hearth](place_the_hearth.md), a fracture opening that no member can face.
The strain real and unspoken, the whole it might break too dear to risk, the tension it raises with nowhere yet to go.

## Action

[Scapegoating](process_scapegoating.md): [The Kin](persona_the_kin.md), holding [the kin's seat](position_kin.md), cannot face the fracture, and the anxiety it raises begins to seek a member to land on.

## Stage

[The Kin](persona_the_kin.md), on [Hold It Together](plan_hold_it_together.md), keeps the family whole by not looking at the rift that runs through it, because to name it might break the thing apart.
The strain finds no place in the open and no words at the hearth, so the tension gathers, unspoken and rising, and starts to seek somewhere to go.

## Tension

Sparing the whole the conflict that might break it is a kind of love, and the sparing is what leaves the strain to land elsewhere.
There is nothing to fault: the fracture is real and facing it is genuinely dangerous to the family. But a strain unfaced does not dissolve, it seeks a bearer, so the plot closes with the rift kept shut and the tension loose in the whole, looking for the member who will carry it.
