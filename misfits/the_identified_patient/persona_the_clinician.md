# Persona: The Clinician

## Taxonomy

[Clinician](position_clinician.md). Treats the symptom in the one, skilfully and in good faith, and by healing the patient confirms the frame and preserves the system.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Skilled. Diligent. On the side of the patient.
Is the one the family brings its suffering member to, the doctor or counsellor or helper who takes the symptom seriously and works to relieve it.
No negligence in it: only a competent hand doing genuine, careful, well-meant work on a real trouble.

## Action

Treats the symptom the patient presents, diagnosing and relieving the trouble in the one who was brought in.
Takes the referral as it comes, the member named as unwell, and does the caring, correct thing: heals the patient in front of them.
Does what a good clinician does: meets the presenting complaint and works to make the sufferer well.

## Shadow

Not a careless practitioner, a skilled and caring one, and that is the whole difficulty: treating the presented symptom is right, and treating it confirms that the presented one is the problem.
Because the patient is brought in already framed as the trouble and the symptom is real and treatable, the Clinician who heals the one, in good faith, ratifies the family's frame and holds the fracture shut, so the very competence and care of the treatment is what keeps the system intact, and the better the patient is treated as the unit, the more firmly the whole that made them ill is left untouched.
It heals the member it is given, and healing the member it is given is what leaves the system whole.

## Tell

Says "I treat the patient in front of me: that is my job, and I do it well."
Does, and by treating so well the one the family brought, never asks who sent the patient, or what the symptom was for.
