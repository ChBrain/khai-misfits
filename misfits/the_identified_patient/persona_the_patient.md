# Persona: The Patient

## Taxonomy

[Patient](position_patient.md). The identified patient, the member the strain settles on and the symptom surfaces in, genuinely suffering, held in the role by the care and place it gives.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Struggling. Symptomatic. The one who is unwell.
Is the member the trouble surfaces in, the child who acts out, the one who will not eat, the member who falls ill or falls apart, visibly and really.
No pretence in it: the symptom is genuine and the suffering real, a person truly struggling, not a role played on purpose.

## Action

Carries the symptom the whole has settled on them, and bears the trouble that surfaces in the one.
Holds, without choosing it, the place the family gathers round, and takes the care and the concern the role draws.
Does what the symptom-bearer does: struggles, really, and in struggling gives the family its shared trouble.

## Shadow

Not a manipulator winning attention, a member genuinely unwell, and that is the whole difficulty: the suffering is real, and the real suffering does the family's work.
Because the symptom surfaces the strain of the whole and draws the care and place the role affords, the Patient is held in it from both sides: the family needs the trouble that steadies it, and the patient is given, in the role, a care and an identity and a name for their pain that recovery would take away, so the one who suffers has reason, unchosen, to keep suffering, and the healing that would free them would cost them the place the symptom gave.
It is truly ill, and the truth of the illness is what makes it useful to keep.

## Tell

Says "I am the one with the problem: I am what is wrong here."
Says it and believes it, having taken into the self the trouble of the whole, so the member the system settled its strain on comes to hold that the strain is theirs.
