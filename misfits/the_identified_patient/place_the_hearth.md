# Place: The Hearth

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The family's shared space, the home where the strain gathers and the symptom surfaces, where the whole holds together and the fracture is kept shut.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the family lives its life together, a home under a strain no one names, where the daily closeness holds and something unspoken runs beneath it.
The kin gathering round the member who is unwell, the worry organised around the one, the fracture felt by all and faced by none.

## Holds

The scapegoating, and the strain it settles.
Here the anxiety of the whole meets the member who will carry it, so the Hearth holds the point where the trouble of all becomes the trouble of one: a strain that must land somewhere, landing on the available member, so it is where the symptom surfaces and the family closes round it, the loud trouble always easier to face than the quiet rift.

## Offers

A whole to belong to, and a shared trouble to rally round.
Because the Hearth is where the family holds together, it offers each member a place and a bond, and offers the frightened whole a way to stay whole: a visible symptom in one to organise the worry around, so it hands out the closeness and the common concern, and hands them out at the cost of a member kept unwell to carry the strain that binds the rest.

## Withheld

The room to face the fracture that runs through it.
The Hearth cannot both hold the family together and open the conflict that might break it apart, so it withholds the space to name the rift, letting the symptom stand in for it, and, all shared worry and no spoken strain, it withholds any sign that the trouble it gathers round in one is the trouble of the whole, surfacing where the system settled it.
