# Pitch: Treat the Patient

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Caring, focused, responsible.
The plain rightness of a family gathering around its suffering member and a clinician treating a real symptom, everyone doing what care asks: find the one who is unwell and help them.
Nothing false in it: the symptom is real, the suffering genuine, and treating it is the caring and correct thing to do.

## Undertow

Under the care, the strain of the whole settled on one member, whose symptom holds the family together by hiding the fracture no one can face.
Because the trouble in the one is visible where the rift in the whole is not, and because the symptom does the family's work of detouring the conflict and uniting the members, treating the patient confirms the one is the problem and holds the fracture shut, so the whole stays whole on the strength of a member kept unwell, and a family that needs a patient keeps one.
The treatment meant to heal the one preserves the system that needs them ill.

## Nerve

The feeling of rallying around the suffering member, sure you are helping the one who needs it,
because the symptom is real and treating it is only kind,
while the fracture that made them ill stays shut behind them, and the healing that works on the one leaves the whole untouched.

## Echo

After the run: a family steadied around its patient, whole on the strength of a member kept unwell,
no one cruel and no one scheming, only a real strain and a real symptom and real love and a treatment genuinely called for,
and a fracture that would meet the trouble where it lives, left unfaced because surfacing it might break the whole apart, and because it would take from the patient the role that gave them care and from the family the trouble that held it together.
