# Position: Kin

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The seat of the family under strain, gathering round the suffering member and settling the trouble on the one, where the fracture is kept shut.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A whole to hold together and a strain it cannot face: the standing to gather the family and the anxiety that must land somewhere.
A clear case: a suffering member must be worried over and helped, and a family must stay whole.
No way to face the fracture without risking the whole, and no reward for naming a trouble that belongs to everyone.

## Orders

Hold it together.
Gather round the member who is unwell, worry over them and seek their cure, and keep the family whole by locating its trouble in the one who bears it.
Face the symptom you can name, and do not face the fracture you cannot.

## Loses

The chance to meet its own fracture, exactly by gathering round the one who carries it.
Because the strain of the whole is unbearable to face and the symptom of the one is a relief to name, the seat settles its anxiety on the available member and organises its love around them, so it cannot rally to the patient without confirming the patient is the problem, and cannot keep the whole steady except on the strength of a member kept unwell.
It gains a family held together and a shared trouble to rally to, and loses any way to meet the rift that runs through it.

## Drives

Toward the family held whole and the trouble settled on the one,
bought against the fracture it cannot face,
so the seat that would heal its suffering member keeps the member the whole needs ill.
