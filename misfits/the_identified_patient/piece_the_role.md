# Piece: The Role

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). What bearing the symptom gives the one, the care and place and name for their pain that hold the patient in the position, from their own side.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Given at [the hearth](place_the_hearth.md), where the family's worry gathers round the one.
Confirmed at [the clinic](place_the_clinic.md), where the patient is named and cared for.

## Load Bearing

Without it the patient would simply recover and leave the position, and only the family's need would hold the trap.
With it the one is held from their own side too: the role gives care, attention, an identity, and a name for a pain that was the whole's, so recovery would cost the patient the place being ill had made for them. The Role is load bearing because it is the second hold of the trap (Bowen; Vogel and Bell): the patient's own stake in the position, so the piece carries the care and place that make the symptom worth keeping for the one who bears it, the reason the trouble is held from below as well as pressed from above.

## Apparent

Love, the plain care and concern a family rightly gives a suffering member.
Seen at the surface, the Role looks like nothing but kindness, a family looking after its own, and the truth that the care is bound to the illness, that it gives the patient a place recovery would take, that it holds the one in the position from their own side, stays hidden behind how right and how loving it is to gather round the member who is unwell.

## Yearbook

Held by [the patient](persona_the_patient.md) through [stabilising](process_stabilising.md), the care and place bearing the symptom affords.
It is the patient's stake in the trap: the Role binds care to [the symptom](piece_the_symptom.md) so that recovery threatens [the balance](piece_the_balance.md), the hold from below that answers the family's need from above, the reason [surfacing](process_surfacing.md) must take something real from the one it would free.
