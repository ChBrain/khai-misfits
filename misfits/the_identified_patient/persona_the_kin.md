# Persona: The Kin

## Taxonomy

[Kin](position_kin.md). The family under a strain it cannot face, gathering round the suffering member, genuinely worried and loving, whose anxiety settles the trouble on the one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Worried. Devoted. Rallying to the one who is unwell.
Is the family that gathers around its troubled member, the parents and siblings and partners doing what love asks, finding the one who is suffering and closing round them.
No coldness in it: only people trying to hold their whole together and help the member who is struggling.

## Action

Locates the trouble in the suffering one and gathers round it, taking the patient to be treated and organising the family's worry around them.
Faces the loud symptom it can see and name, and does not face the quiet fracture it cannot.
Does what a loving family does: rallies to the member who is unwell and seeks help for them.

## Shadow

Not a cabal of scapegoaters, a frightened and loving family, and that is the whole difficulty: rallying to the suffering one is right, and the rallying is what holds the fracture shut.
Because the strain of the whole is unbearable to face and the symptom of the one is a relief to name, the Kin settles its anxiety on the member most available to carry it, so the love that gathers round the patient is the same love that keeps them the patient, and the worry that seeks to heal the one confirms the one is the problem.
It gathers round the suffering member out of love, and the gathering is what the system needs the member ill for.

## Tell

Says "we are so worried about them: if only they were well, we would all be fine."
Worries truly, and cannot see that the family's being fine is built on the one not being, that the trouble it would cure is the trouble that holds it together.
