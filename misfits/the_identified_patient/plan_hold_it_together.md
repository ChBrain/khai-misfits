# Plan: Hold It Together

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the kin's seat](position_kin.md), inside [The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md).

## Direction

Keep the family whole. Gather round the member who is unwell, worry over them and seek their cure, and locate the family's trouble in the one who bears it. Face the symptom you can name and rally to it, and do not look at the fracture that might break the whole apart if it were named.

([The Kin](persona_the_kin.md) gathers round the suffering member, worried and loving, and settles the anxiety of the whole on the one available to carry it, the loving move that holds the fracture shut.)
