# Piece: The Fracture

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The real conflict running through the whole, the unfaced strain the symptom hides, the trouble where it actually lives.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs beneath [the hearth](place_the_hearth.md), unnamed, the strain the family cannot face.
Could be brought into the open only in [the session](place_the_session.md), where the whole is treated.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no trouble to displace and no reason for a symptom, and the patient is simply a person who fell ill.
With it the whole trap has its source: a real conflict in the system, a marriage gone quiet, a loss unspoken, a strain that might break the thing apart if it were named, so unbearable to face that the family forms a symptom to hide it. The Fracture is load bearing because it is the invisible pole of the displacement (Jackson; Bowen): the trouble where it actually lives, unnamed and unfaceable, so the piece carries the strain the symptom stands in for, the rift in the whole against which the trouble in the one is a decoy.

## Apparent

Nothing, an absence, a family with no visible trouble but the one member who is unwell.
Seen at the surface, the Fracture does not appear at all: the eye goes to the symptom, and the strain beneath stays out of view, and the truth that the real trouble is here in the whole and not there in the one, that the symptom exists precisely to keep this from being seen, stays hidden behind how plainly the patient, and not the family, is the one who is unwell.

## Yearbook

Hidden by [scapegoating](process_scapegoating.md) and stood in for by [the symptom](piece_the_symptom.md), the strain the whole cannot face.
It is the source of the trap: the Fracture is what [the frame](piece_the_frame.md) points away from and [the balance](piece_the_balance.md) keeps shut, the trouble that [surfacing](process_surfacing.md) alone would meet, the unfaced twin of the visible symptom, the thing the whole [identified patient](piece_the_identified_patient.md) is built to keep from view.
