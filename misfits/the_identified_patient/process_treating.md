# Process: Treating

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The healing of the symptom in the one, good care of the presented patient that ratifies the frame and holds the fracture shut.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A patient presented as the problem and a skill ready to relieve them.
At [the clinic](place_the_clinic.md), the family bringing its suffering member framed as the trouble, the clinician taking the referral and meeting the symptom.

## Direction

Toward the one healed and the whole left untouched.
Because the patient arrives already named as the problem and the symptom is real and treatable, treating meets the trouble in the one presented, so it relieves the member and, in relieving them, confirms the family's frame that the one is the unit, holding the fracture shut behind the patient healed alone, the better the treatment the firmer the system left intact.

## Lever

The rightness of relieving a real symptom in a suffering member.
Because the symptom is genuine and treating it is the caring, correct thing, each move to heal the presented patient is sound clinical care, so treating runs on good faith and real skill, the confirmation of the frame assembled from no one meaning to hold the fracture shut, only from a clinician doing right by the patient in the room.

## Echo

A symptom relieved and a system preserved.
The presented one has been healed and the frame that the one is the trouble has been ratified by the cure, so what reads as good care making a suffering member well is, in truth, a treatment aimed at the one that leaves the whole that made them ill exactly as it was.
