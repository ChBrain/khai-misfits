# Plan: Treat the System

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the systemist's seat](position_systemist.md), inside [The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Systemist](position_systemist.md)

## Direction

Name the whole as the unit that is unwell. Surface the fracture the symptom hides, hold the system rather than the member as the thing to treat, and ask the family to face the conflict it formed the symptom to avoid. Read the trouble of the one as the trouble of the whole, and meet it where it lives, even when no one wants it named.

## Orders

([The Systemist](persona_the_systemist.md) names the system as the unit and surfaces the fracture, at the cost of the family's stabiliser and the patient's role, against the peace the symptom bought.)

## Implementation

([The Session](place_the_session.md); the [surfacing](process_surfacing.md) process; the whole is held as [the identified patient](piece_the_identified_patient.md) and [the fracture](piece_the_fracture.md) is brought to light, turning the trap back ... if the family can bear the conflict surfaced.)

## Targets

- [ ] the whole is named as the unit that is unwell, not the member
- [ ] the fracture the symptom hides is surfaced and faced
- [ ] the role that gave the patient care and place is given up
- [ ] the trouble is met where it lives, or the conflict proves unbearable and the patient is sent back to be healed alone
