# Place: The Session

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). Where the whole is brought into the room and the fracture could be named, the family-therapy space in which the system is treated and the conflict surfaced, or is not.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The room where the whole family sits, not the patient alone, a space set for the system to be seen together and the trouble read across it.
The systemist looking past the one to the fracture, the kin gathered and uneasy, the conflict the symptom hid drawn toward the open.

## Holds

The surfacing, and the fracture it would name.
Here the whole meets the eye that reads it, so the Session holds the point where the system could be treated as the unit: the fracture brought toward the light, the symptom read as the whole's, so it is where the trouble might be met where it lives, the named conflict always harder to bear than the symptom that stood in for it.

## Offers

A room for the whole to be treated, and a bench for the fracture to be faced.
Because the Session sits the whole family and reads the trouble across it, it offers the systemist the ground to name the system and offers the family the chance to meet its rift, so it hands out both the treatment aimed at the whole and the conflict it surfaces, and hands them out knowing the family formed the symptom precisely to keep this room empty.

## Withheld

The comfort of leaving the fracture shut.
The Session cannot both treat the system and spare the family the conflict, so it withholds the relief of sending the patient off to be healed alone, forcing the rift toward the open, and, all whole in the room and no symptom to hide behind, it withholds any way to meet the trouble that does not cost the family its stabiliser and the patient the role that gave them care.
