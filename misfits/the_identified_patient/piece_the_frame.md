# Piece: The Frame

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The shared diagnosis that the one is the problem, the reading that locates the trouble in the member and away from the whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set at [the hearth](place_the_hearth.md), where the family names the one as the trouble.
Ratified at [the clinic](place_the_clinic.md), where the treatment confirms the reading.

## Load Bearing

Without it the symptom is just a member's trouble among the whole's, and the fracture is as visible as the one.
With it the trouble is located: the shared reading that the one is unwell and the rest are well, so the family knows where to look and the clinician knows whom to treat. The Frame is load bearing because it is the misdiagnosis the trap requires (Bowen; Minuchin): the account that the member is the unit of trouble, so the piece carries the reading that points every eye at the one and away from the whole, the shared story that must hold for the symptom to do its work.

## Apparent

A diagnosis, the plain and helpful truth that here is the one who is unwell.
Seen at the surface, the Frame looks like clarity, a family and a clinician correctly identifying the patient, and the truth that it locates in one member a trouble that belongs to the whole, that its function is to point away from the fracture, that being confirmed by the cure makes it only more firmly wrong, stays hidden behind how obviously the patient, and not the family, presents as the problem.
