# Position: Clinician

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The seat that treats the symptom in the one who is brought in, where good care of the patient ratifies the frame and holds the fracture shut.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A skill and a referral: the standing to treat the presented symptom and the patient the family brings already named as unwell.
A clear case: a real symptom in a suffering member must be met and relieved.
No mandate to ask who sent the patient, and no cover for treating the family when only the member was brought in.

## Orders

Treat the patient.
Take the referral as it comes, diagnose and relieve the symptom in the one presented, and do the caring, correct thing for the member who is suffering.
Meet the presenting complaint, and make the sufferer well.

## Loses

The chance to treat the trouble where it lives, exactly by treating the one who presents it.
Because the patient arrives already framed as the problem and the symptom is real and treatable, the seat that heals the one, in good faith, ratifies the family's frame and holds the fracture shut, so its skill and care are spent confirming that the presented member is the unit, and the better it treats the patient the more firmly the system that made them ill is left untouched.
It gains a symptom relieved and a patient served, and loses any reach to the fracture the symptom was hiding.

## Drives

Toward the symptom treated and the patient healed alone,
bought against the whole that goes untreated,
so the seat that would relieve the suffering one confirms the one and keeps the system whole.
