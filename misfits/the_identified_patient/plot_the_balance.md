# Plot: The Balance

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Hearth](place_the_hearth.md), the symptom doing the family's work.
The trouble in the one steadying the whole, the conflict detoured, the members united around a shared concern, the patient beginning, perhaps, to improve.

## Action

[Stabilising](process_stabilising.md): [The Kin](persona_the_kin.md), holding [the kin's seat](position_kin.md), settles into a calm held up by the patient's symptom, and moves to keep it when the trouble threatens to lift.

## Stage

[The Kin](persona_the_kin.md), on [Hold It Together](plan_hold_it_together.md), finds the family steadied around its patient, the shared worry holding the whole and the fracture out of view.
[The Patient](persona_the_patient.md), on [Bear the Trouble](plan_bear_the_trouble.md), holds the role and its care, and when recovery begins to draw them out of it the whole grows anxious and pulls them back, or the strain finds another to carry.

## Tension

The calm the symptom brings is real and welcome, and the calm depends on a member who must not get well.
There is nothing to fault: a frightened family is genuinely steadied by a shared trouble to gather round. But the steadiness is built on the patient's staying ill, so the plot closes with the family settled into a balance held up by the one, its peace resisting the very recovery it says it wants.
