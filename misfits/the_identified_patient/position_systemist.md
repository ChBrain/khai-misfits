# Position: Systemist

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md). The seat that would treat the whole as the unit and surface the fracture the symptom hides, at the cost of the family's stabiliser and the patient's role, and against the peace the symptom bought.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A systemic eye and a hard reading: the standing to name the whole as the unit that is unwell and the case that the trouble lives in the system, not the member.
A clear case: the way to meet the trouble is to treat it where it is, in the fracture the symptom hides.
No way to surface the fracture without risking the whole, and no cover from the charge of blaming the well and troubling the peace.

## Orders

Treat the system.
Name the fracture the symptom hides, hold the whole as the unit that is unwell, and ask the family to face the conflict it formed the symptom to avoid.
Read the trouble of the one as the trouble of the whole, and meet it there.

## Loses

The family's welcome and the patient's given place, exactly by treating the trouble where it lives.
Because the symptom's whole function is to hide the rift and steady the whole, surfacing the fracture means letting the hidden conflict into the open where it might break the thing apart, and treating the system means taking from the patient the role that gave them care and from the family the trouble that held it together, so the seat cannot meet the trouble without spending the peace the symptom bought and being taken for the one who blames the well.
It buys the trouble met where it lives against the whole it unsettles, and is taken, for a while, as the one who would not just help the poor sufferer and let the family be.

## Drives

Toward the whole named as the unit and the fracture surfaced,
bought against the peace the symptom bought and the role the patient held,
so the one seat that would meet the trouble must unsettle the whole that was built to keep it hidden.
