# Plot: The Naming

## Taxonomy

[The Identified Patient](play_the_identified_patient.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Session](place_the_session.md), someone who would treat the whole and not the one.
The family in the room together, the fracture drawn toward the light, the patient's role and the family's balance both there to be spent.

## Action

[Surfacing](process_surfacing.md): [The Systemist](persona_the_systemist.md), holding [the systemist's seat](position_systemist.md), names the whole as the unit and brings the fracture up, against [The Kin](persona_the_kin.md) on [Hold It Together](plan_hold_it_together.md).

## Stage

[The Systemist](persona_the_systemist.md), on [Treat the System](plan_treat_the_system.md), reads the symptom as the whole's and asks the family to face the conflict it formed the symptom to avoid.
[The Kin](persona_the_kin.md), on [Hold It Together](plan_hold_it_together.md), feels the stabiliser threatened and the peace unsettled, and the patient, holding [the role](piece_the_role.md), stands to lose the care and place that being ill had given them.

## Tension

Naming the whole as the unit is the truest reading, and it costs the family its balance and the patient their role.
There is nothing to fault: the trouble does live in the system, and the systemist only says so. But surfacing the fracture is the very thing the symptom was formed to prevent, so the plot closes one of two ways: the whole is named and the trouble met where it lives, bought against the stabiliser and the role ... or the conflict proves unbearable and the patient is sent back to be healed alone, and the system keeps its patient.
