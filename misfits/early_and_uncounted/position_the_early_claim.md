# Position: The Early Claim

## Taxonomy

[Early and Uncounted](play_early_and_uncounted.md). The seat that expresses a trait's early effect into a cohort still nearly whole, able to earn a full weight in the tally there and unable to do anything about the late effect the same trait carries to a cohort it will not be whole for.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Command of its early expression, and the whole width of the cohort to earn its weight from.
The seat holds whatever effect a trait shows at an early age and the nearly complete cohort present to feel it, so its gain, if real, is felt widely and counted in full, the seat's early face meeting the tally at its heaviest.
Command of timing and none of consequence, since the seat can be the age at which an effect shows and cannot set how that effect will be weighed once shown, only offer it to whatever cohort happens to be present then.

## Orders

Show the early face fully, and let the tally weigh it.
Express whatever gain the age calls for, since a real gain felt by nearly the whole cohort is a gain the tally must register in full, so the seat brings its early effect forward without reservation and counts the bringing as the whole of what it can do.
Judge its own worth by the width of the cohort it reaches, since the seat's only measure is how many carriers felt the gain, so an effect that reaches nearly everyone is a better-favoured effect, and the seat has no other register to judge itself by.

## Loses

Any hold over its own late face, which the seat cannot separate from the early one it is praised for.
The Early Claim can express its early effect in full and cannot prevent the same trait from carrying a late effect into a cohort it will, by then, be a remnant of, because the two effects belong to one trait and the seat has no instrument to keep one without the other. What the position forfeits is any control over when its bill comes due: the late effect arrives at whatever age it was always going to, met by whatever cohort survives to that age, and the seat that earned its place on the early face has no say in how thin that later cohort will be.

## Drives

Toward a trait praised in full for what reaches nearly everyone, and unaccountable for what reaches almost no one,
so the seat that wins its place earliest and widest is the seat least reachable later, its every early success measured exactly by the cohort it meets, never by the cohort its own late face will have to answer to.
