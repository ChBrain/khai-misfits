# Piece: The Thin Count

## Taxonomy

[Early and Uncounted](play_early_and_uncounted.md). The nearly nonexistent weight a late effect earns because it is felt by only a thinned remnant, the same rule that made the early face strong now returning almost nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Falls in [the Thin Years](place_the_thin_years.md), returned by [the Tally](position_the_tally.md) the moment a trait's late face is shown to whatever remains.
Flows as a cost measured against a remnant, the discount as steep as the cohort is thin.

## Load Bearing

Without the Thin Count there is no misfit, only a trait whose late effect is duly weighed and duly selected against. It is load bearing because it is the half of the mechanism that lets a real, sometimes severe cost pass for almost nothing: the same procedure that rewarded the early face in full returns next to no penalty here, not by softening the rule but by applying it to a cohort too thinned to move it (Hamilton; Medawar). It carries the harmful side of the trade: without a late cost that is real to the remnant and near-invisible to the tally, there would be no asymmetry at all, only a trait that pays in full for everything it does.

## Apparent

Just a rare cost, barely worth noticing at population scale.
Seen at the surface, the Thin Count looks like nothing more than a minor, population-level footnote, a harm too small to matter because so few carry it, and the truth that this apparent smallness is exactly what lets a trait escape selection against a cost that can be severe to every one of the few who bear it stays hidden behind how sensible it looks to weigh a rare thing lightly.

## Yearbook

Suffered by whatever remains of [the Cohort](persona_the_cohort.md), returned by [the Count](persona_the_count.md) applying the same rule it always has, and met at [the Thin Years](place_the_thin_years.md) where so little of the cohort is left to answer for it.
It is the unfavourable half of the shape: the Thin Count is what [thinning](process_thinning.md) sets up and [weighing](process_weighing.md) returns, standing opposite [the Early Weight](piece_the_early_weight.md) across the same trait's two ages, the two together the raw material [the Accrual](piece_the_accrual.md) sums and [the Toll](piece_the_toll.md) names whole.
