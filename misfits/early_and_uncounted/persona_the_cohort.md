# Persona: The Cohort

## Taxonomy

[The Remnant](position_the_remnant.md). Begins whole and thins with age from causes no gene set, predation, accident, disease, so that by any later age only a fraction of it remains to carry whatever a trait's late face brings.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Undirected and steady. Losing members for reasons of its own. Sure that its thinning has nothing to do with any one trait.
Is the one that starts a generation whole and, purely by the hazards any life is exposed to, stands smaller at every age that follows, its numbers falling for reasons that predate and outlast whichever gene happens to be carried inside it.
No blame belongs to it: the thinning is not a judgement on any trait and not a punishment for any effect, since accident and predation and disease would thin it exactly the same whether the trait existed or not.

## Action

Loses individuals steadily across its span, to causes entirely apart from what any one gene does, so that its later ages are attended by a remnant and its earliest ages by nearly the whole.
Carries, at whatever age it is asked to, whichever effects the traits inside it happen to be expressing then, in whatever numbers are left to carry them.
Does what a cohort does across a lifespan: starts full, thins by attrition, and hands the Count, at every age, exactly as many carriers as are still alive to be counted, no more.

## Shadow

From the inside, its thinning is not a decision and not a defence: it does not thin in order to protect any trait from being weighed, and it carries no sense that its own numbers, falling year on year, are what keeps a late cost from ever being billed in full.
What it cannot register is the shelter its own attrition provides: it loses members for reasons that have nothing to do with any gene's late face, and never registers that this very loss is what leaves that late face too few carriers to be weighed against.
The bind is passive and unaware: it thins because life thins it, and in thinning it does, without meaning to, exactly what would have to happen for a late cost to go uncounted, carrying that shelter as an accident of its own unrelated wearing down.

## Tell

Its record says "we were always going to be fewer by then, trait or no trait."
Is the whole shelter in one plain fact, since a cohort's ordinary attrition, owed to nothing a gene does, is exactly what leaves so few behind to answer for whatever that gene does late, the thinning entirely its own and the cover it gives entirely incidental.
