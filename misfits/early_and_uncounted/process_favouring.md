# Process: Favouring

## Taxonomy

[Early and Uncounted](play_early_and_uncounted.md). The retention of a trait because its early effect, weighed against a nearly whole cohort, earns a strong reward the same trait's uncounted late effect cannot undo.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Trait](persona_the_trait.md), in [the Thick Years](place_the_thick_years.md), showing an early effect to a cohort still nearly whole.
Begins whenever a gene's early face reaches a generation that has not yet thinned.

## Direction

Turns a widely felt early gain into a trait retained in the genome.
Because an effect felt by nearly everyone is an effect the tally must reward in full, favouring converts a real early benefit into a trait that spreads, its retention earned by reach and untouched by whatever the same trait will later cost (Williams).

## Lever

The width of the cohort present when the early effect shows.
Because a gain's weight is set by how many carriers feel it, favouring works through sheer reach: the wider the cohort at the age the effect arrives, the stronger the favour, so an early effect is favoured exactly to the degree the Thick Years are still thick.

## Echo

A trait retained for what it does young, its later face carried along unexamined.
The echo of favouring is a gene fixed in the population by its early record alone, so the trait persists, and whatever it will do once the cohort has thinned rides forward inside it, retained by the same act that never had to weigh it.
