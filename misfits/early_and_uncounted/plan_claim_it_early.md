# Plan: Claim It Early

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [The Early Claim](position_the_early_claim.md), inside [Early and Uncounted](play_early_and_uncounted.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Early Claim](position_the_early_claim.md)

## Direction

Show the early face in full and let the width of the cohort do the rest, since a gain felt by nearly everyone is a gain the tally must register in full (Hamilton). Bring the early effect forward at whatever age it was always going to arrive, without reservation, and judge success only by how widely it reaches, because the seat's one measure is the cohort it meets. Carry the late face along unaltered, since the seat has no instrument to separate the two, and let whatever cohort survives to that later age be the one the late face meets, the plan claiming everything the early age offers and answering for nothing the late age will bring.

## Orders

([The Trait](persona_the_trait.md) expresses its early effect into a cohort still nearly whole, earning the full weight the tally has to give, and carries its late effect forward unexamined, since it has no means to weigh the two against each other.)

## Implementation

([The Thick Years](place_the_thick_years.md); through the [favouring](process_favouring.md) process, which rewards the early face in full; The Trait brings [the early weight](piece_the_early_weight.md) the tally can give and, in the same act, [the thin count](piece_the_thin_count.md) its late face will later receive, driving [the toll](piece_the_toll.md) it has no way to see.)

## Targets

- [ ] the early effect is expressed into a cohort still nearly whole
- [ ] the tally weighs the early effect in full, by its true reach
- [ ] the late effect is carried forward unaltered and unexamined
- [ ] the claim is judged only by the cohort it reaches, never by what it will cost later
