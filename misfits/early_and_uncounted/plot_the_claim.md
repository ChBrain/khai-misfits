# Plot: The Claim

## Taxonomy

[Early and Uncounted](play_early_and_uncounted.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Thick Years](place_the_thick_years.md), a trait's early face showing itself.
An effect arrives in a cohort still nearly whole, and a tally that must weigh it exactly as widely as it reaches.

## Action

[Favouring](process_favouring.md): the Count weighs the Trait's early effect against the nearly whole cohort present, and returns the full reward a wide reach commands.

## Stage

[The Count](persona_the_count.md) acts from [the Tally](position_the_tally.md) on [Weigh What's Left](plan_weigh_whats_left.md), returning [the early weight](piece_the_early_weight.md) the Trait's reach warrants, the reward correct and its later cost unweighed.
The favouring begins exactly as it should, sized to the cohort and earned by a real early gain.

## Tension

The reward starts right, and the rightness is the first step toward the asymmetry.
There is no error: the gain is real, the cohort is wide, and the tally rewards exactly what it should, so the favouring is the Count doing precisely its job. But the same rule that rewards this gain in full is the rule that will, later, discount whatever the same trait costs, so the plot closes with an early effect correctly rewarded and already carrying, in that correctness, the shape of the undercount to come, the trait retained in the tissue it will one day cost and sound in every part of the retaining.
