# Piece: The Accrual

## Taxonomy

[Early and Uncounted](play_early_and_uncounted.md). The summing of many separate genes' early weight and thin count, each struck on the identical asymmetry, into a single compounding pattern across a genome and across generations.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Falls across [the Long Run](place_the_long_run.md), gathered there from every trait that has ever made the trade.
Flows as many small, individually correct countings layered one atop another, no single one large enough to be the whole story.

## Load Bearing

Without the Accrual there is no pattern, only a scattered set of individually minor asymmetries with nothing tying them together. It is load bearing because it is the piece that turns one trait's early-favoured, late-uncounted trade into an entire organism's decline: no single gene senesces a body, but many genes, each independently favoured for its own early face and each independently uncounted for its own late one, sum, across evolutionary time, into a visible pattern no one trait could produce alone (Williams; demonstrated directly by Rose and Charlesworth's selection experiments). It carries the scale of the misfit: without the accrual across many genes, an early weight and a thin count would describe one trait's quirk, not a body's whole senescence.

## Apparent

Just an organism ageing, the way organisms do.
Seen at the surface, the Accrual looks like nothing more than the ordinary fact of getting older, a body simply running down, and the truth that this running down is the summed residue of many separate, individually correct selective trades, each one reasoned and each one uncounted on its own terms, stays hidden behind how natural and inevitable ordinary ageing looks.

## Yearbook

Built from every [Trait](persona_the_trait.md) that has struck the trade, across every [Cohort](persona_the_cohort.md) it was struck in, tallied each time by [the Count](persona_the_count.md) with the same unbending rule.
It is the summing shape: the Accrual is what [accruing](process_accruing.md) performs across [the Long Run](place_the_long_run.md), gathering every [Early Weight](piece_the_early_weight.md) and every [Thin Count](piece_the_thin_count.md) any gene has ever earned, and it is what [the Toll](piece_the_toll.md) names once the gathering is complete.
