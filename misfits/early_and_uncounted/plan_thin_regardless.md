# Plan: Thin Regardless

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [The Remnant](position_the_remnant.md), inside [Early and Uncounted](play_early_and_uncounted.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Remnant](position_the_remnant.md)

## Direction

Lose members steadily across the span, to predation, accident, and disease, regardless of any trait any of them carries, since the cohort was always going to thin by hazards that have nothing to do with a gene's late face (Medawar). Offer the tally, at every age, exactly as many carriers as remain, no more and no wider, since the seat has no capacity to present itself as anything other than its true, thinned size. Carry whatever a trait's late effect brings in whatever numbers are left to carry it, and let the thinness of those numbers be exactly what it is, an accident of unrelated wearing down and not a judgement on the trait.

## Orders

([The Cohort](persona_the_cohort.md) thins by its own hazards across every age, arriving at each later age as a remnant, and carries whatever the Trait's late face shows in only the numbers still present to carry it.)

## Implementation

([The Thin Years](place_the_thin_years.md); through the [thinning](process_thinning.md) process, which narrows the cohort for reasons entirely its own; The Cohort offers the remnant it has actually become, driving [the thin count](piece_the_thin_count.md) that follows from having so few left.)

## Targets

- [ ] the cohort thins across the span from causes unrelated to any one trait
- [ ] each later age is met by a remnant, honestly reported at its true size
- [ ] the late effect is carried in only the numbers still present to carry it
- [ ] the thinning is never mistaken for a judgement on the trait it happens to carry
