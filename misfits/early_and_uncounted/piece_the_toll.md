# Piece: The Toll

## Taxonomy

[Early and Uncounted](play_early_and_uncounted.md). The visible pattern of decline with age itself, the whole phenomenon of which the other pieces are parts, built entirely of early effects rewarded in full and late effects counted almost not at all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Falls across [the Thick Years](place_the_thick_years.md) and [the Thin Years](place_the_thin_years.md) both, summed at last across [the Long Run](place_the_long_run.md).
Flows as senescence itself, a body weaker, slower, more brittle, and less fertile with age, the outcome of nothing but many correct countings, none of them wrong on their own terms.

## Load Bearing

Without the Toll there is no misfit, only a set of traits each individually favoured and each individually uncounted, with no name for what they add up to. The Toll is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole, the correlated cost of pleiotropic genes retained for their early benefit and never billed for their later one, senescence itself as the by-product of a selective force that weakens with age (Williams). It carries the entire misfit: the Early Weight is the reward that keeps such a trait in the genome, the Thin Count is the discount that lets its cost pass, the Accrual is the summing across many such traits, so the Toll is not one more piece beside the others but the shape they all compose, the point at which many correct countings become one visible decline. It is built of nothing but honest tallies, each one applying the same rule at every age it is given, which is why it cannot simply be corrected: no single weighing was wrong, the decline is only their sum, so undoing it would mean reaching every gene's late face with a cohort that never actually survived to answer for it.

## Apparent

Just a body growing old, the ordinary course of a life.
Seen at the surface, the Toll looks like nothing more than time doing what time does, and the truth that it is instead the accumulated residue of many genes each favoured for a real early gain and each excused from a real late cost by nothing more than how few carriers survived to pay it, stays hidden behind how completely ordinary ageing appears from the outside.

## Yearbook

Claimed early by [the Trait](persona_the_trait.md), carried through a thinning [Cohort](persona_the_cohort.md), and returned in two unequal weights by [the Count](persona_the_count.md) applying one unbending rule.
It is the whole shape of the pattern: the Toll is what [favouring](process_favouring.md) begins, [thinning](process_thinning.md) sets the terms for, [weighing](process_weighing.md) prices unevenly, and [accruing](process_accruing.md) sums across a genome and a lineage, the trait built of [the Early Weight](piece_the_early_weight.md) and [the Thin Count](piece_the_thin_count.md), correct in every part and total in the sum, changed only were the cohort itself to survive further into years it currently loses to hazards none of the cast commands.
