# Piece: The Early Weight

## Taxonomy

[Early and Uncounted](play_early_and_uncounted.md). The heavy reward an early effect earns because it is felt by nearly the whole cohort, the rule that makes a real early gain a strongly favoured one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Falls in [the Thick Years](place_the_thick_years.md), returned by [the Tally](position_the_tally.md) the moment a trait's early face is shown.
Flows as a gain measured against a nearly whole cohort, the reward as wide as the cohort that feels it.

## Load Bearing

Without the Early Weight there is no asymmetry, only a trait weighed the same at every age it acts. It is load bearing because it is the half of the mechanism that makes an early effect worth having at all: the reason a pleiotropic gene is retained in the first place is that its early face, met by nearly everyone, earns the full reward a wide reach commands (Hamilton). It carries the favourable side of the trade: without a real, heavily weighted early gain, there would be nothing for the Thin Count to be measured against, and no reason selection would ever have kept the trait around to carry its late face forward at all.

## Apparent

Just a trait doing well, rewarded for a real benefit.
Seen at the surface, the Early Weight looks like nothing more than ordinary selection working correctly, a gene helping its carriers and being kept for it, and the truth that this same correct rewarding is the first half of an asymmetry that will leave a later cost almost unweighed stays hidden behind how unremarkable a strong early gain looks on its own.

## Yearbook

Earned by [the Trait](persona_the_trait.md) doing what it does early, returned by [the Count](persona_the_count.md) applying its one rule, and met at [the Thick Years](place_the_thick_years.md) where the cohort is still wide enough to earn it.
It is the favourable half of the shape: the Early Weight is what [favouring](process_favouring.md) rewards and [weighing](process_weighing.md) returns, standing opposite [the Thin Count](piece_the_thin_count.md) across the same trait's two ages, the two together the raw material [the Accrual](piece_the_accrual.md) sums and [the Toll](piece_the_toll.md) names whole.
