# Place: The Thick Years

## Taxonomy

[Early and Uncounted](play_early_and_uncounted.md). The early span of a cohort's life, when nearly the whole generation is still present, where a trait's early effect is felt by nearly everyone who carries it and weighed by the tally at its fullest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A generation at its widest, still nearly whole.
Shown at the surface is a cohort barely thinned: almost everyone born into it still alive, still able to be helped or harmed by whatever a trait does now, the age at which a real gain reaches the most carriers it will ever reach.

## Holds

Holds nearly the whole cohort, and whatever a trait's early face brings to it.
The Thick Years is where the widest population and the earliest effects meet, so it holds the maximum reach any trait's early face can have, the gain felt by as many carriers as the cohort will ever again contain.

## Offers

Offers a trait its widest possible audience, and the tally its heaviest possible weight.
Here an early effect meets nearly the whole cohort at once, so the Thick Years offers a real gain the fullest count it could ever receive, the reward matched exactly to the reach, because there is no wider cohort left to reach.

## Withheld

Withholds any preview of what the same trait will do later, and any cohort thin enough to discount it.
What the Thick Years cannot offer is a look ahead: it holds only the early face and the wide cohort present for it, with no sign yet of the remnant that will one day meet the trait's other face, so nothing here anticipates or excuses the thin count still to come, the width of this age telling the tally nothing about the thinness of the next.
