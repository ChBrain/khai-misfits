# Place: The Thin Years

## Taxonomy

[Early and Uncounted](play_early_and_uncounted.md). The later span of a cohort's life, when predation, accident, and disease have thinned it to a remnant, where a trait's late effect is felt by only the few who survive to carry it and weighed by the tally at its thinnest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A generation worn down to what is left of it.
Shown at the surface is a cohort visibly smaller: most of the original carriers gone to causes that have nothing to do with any one gene, only a remnant still present to meet whatever a trait does at this age.

## Holds

Holds a remnant, and whatever a trait's late face brings to it.
The Thin Years is where the narrowest population and the latest effects meet, so it holds the minimum reach any trait's late face can have, the cost felt by as few carriers as the cohort will ever again contain.

## Offers

Offers a trait its narrowest possible audience, and the tally its thinnest possible weight.
Here a late effect meets only a remnant, so the Thin Years offers a real cost the smallest count it could ever receive, the discount matched exactly to the remnant, because there is no thinner cohort left to reach it with.

## Withheld

Withholds any memory of how wide the cohort once was, and any way to weigh the cost by what it would have meant to the whole.
What the Thin Years cannot offer is restitution: it holds only the remnant present now, with no means to summon back the individuals already lost to unrelated hazards, so the tally cannot be shown what the cost would have registered as against a cohort still whole, the thinness of this age final and uncorrectable.
