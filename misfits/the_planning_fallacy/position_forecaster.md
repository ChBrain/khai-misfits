# Position: Forecaster

## Taxonomy

[The Planning Fallacy](play_the_planning_fallacy.md). The seat that estimates the task from the class of tasks it belongs to, and pays for the number.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A reference class: the record of similar tasks and how they really landed.
The outside view, and the discipline to prefer it to the plan.
An answer that prices the task right, and that no one was hoping to hear.

## Orders

Take the outside view.
Find the class, read its distribution, and set the estimate there instead of on the plan.
Deliver the date the task will keep, and the discomfort of it.

## Loses

The particulars, and any promise of comfort, exactly by pricing from the class.
Because the reference class is an average, the seat discards what is genuinely special about this case, and pressed too hard it pads the estimate until the work expands to fill the buffer, the symmetric waste on the far side of the fault.
Its right number is the unwelcome one, every time.

## Drives

Toward an estimate set by the record,
bought with the case's own detail and the ease of a hopeful date,
so the cure for a promise too soon is the standing risk of one too slack.
