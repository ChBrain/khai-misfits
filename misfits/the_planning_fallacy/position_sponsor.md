# Position: Sponsor

## Taxonomy

[The Planning Fallacy](play_the_planning_fallacy.md). The seat that approves the work on the number, and wants the number low.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The go-ahead and the budget, and a gate the estimate must clear.
An incentive that rewards the low bid with the funding.
The bill for the slip, later, when the low bid is missed.

## Orders

Ask for the estimate and back the one that comes in low.
Press the date toward the number that wins the approval.
Commit, and hold the work to what was promised.

## Loses

The estimate's last honesty, exactly by rewarding the low one.
Because the low bid clears the gate, the seat bends the optimism a further inch, so the number that goes out is lower than even the inside view alone would set, and the seat cannot want the truthful figure without wanting to lose the approval it needs.
It buys the overrun it will later carry.

## Drives

Toward the date that gets approved,
shaded below what is already too low,
so the promise is worst exactly where the money and the stakes are largest.
