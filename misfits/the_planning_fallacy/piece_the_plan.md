# Piece: The Plan

## Taxonomy

[The Planning Fallacy](play_the_planning_fallacy.md). The task's own steps laid out as a path, the inside view made into an object, the run where the work goes well.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the drawing board](place_the_drawing_board.md), where the task is broken into steps and each given its time.
Carried onto [the calendar](place_the_calendar.md), where the path drawn clean meets the week that will not keep to it.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no inside view, only a guess with nothing under it.
With it the estimate has a method and a warrant: the plan is the task reasoned through, step by step, and the date is the sum of its parts. The Plan is load bearing because it is the very thing that makes the estimate too low (Kahneman and Tversky): a plan can only trace a route through the work, and the route it draws is the one where each step goes about as intended, so the number built from it is the best case by construction, optimistic not despite the care but because of it.

## Apparent

Diligence, and good reason to trust the date.
Seen from the board, the plan looks like exactly what a careful estimate should rest on, the task genuinely thought through, and the truth that a path through the work is always the smooth path, and so always a floor and never a mean, stays hidden behind how much real thinking the plan contains.

## Yearbook

Drawn by [the planner](persona_the_planner.md) through [estimating](process_estimating.md), the inside view made visible.
It is the case against the class: the Plan is what [the drawing board](place_the_drawing_board.md) shows in place of [the base rate](piece_the_base_rate.md), the ground of [the estimate](piece_the_estimate.md) built along its smooth run, and the very route [the unforeseen](piece_the_unforeseen.md) departs from when the work begins.
