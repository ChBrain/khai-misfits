# Persona: The Record

## Taxonomy

[Record](position_record.md). Holds how long tasks like this one really took, and is set aside as not this task.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Plain. Unwelcome. Kept, and unread.
Carries the outcomes of the tasks that came before, and every one of them ran long.
Owes no story to the task at hand: only the dull count of what happened last time, and the time before.

## Action

Offers the base rate, the distribution the new estimate belongs to.
Would price the task by its class and land the date near the truth.
Sits ready, and is passed over.

## Shadow

It is right and it is dismissed, because it knows nothing of what is special here.
The plan is vivid and particular and the record is a grey average, so the average loses to the detail exactly where the detail misleads.
The correction is present the whole time: it is only ever set aside, never absent.

## Tell

Says "tasks like this take twice as long, they always have."
And is answered: "this one is different."
