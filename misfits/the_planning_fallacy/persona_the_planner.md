# Persona: The Planner

## Taxonomy

[Planner](position_planner.md). Reads the task from its own plan, and estimates the run where it goes well.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent. Particular. Sure of the task in front of it.
Lays the work out step by step and sees each part clearly.
No haste in it: the estimate is thought through, and thought through is felt as right.

## Action

Builds the date from the plan, summing the parts at about the time each should take.
Reasons from what is special about this task, the detail it truly knows.
Promises the number, and means it.

## Shadow

Not careless, thorough: it estimates from the one scenario a plan can draw, the run where nothing in particular goes wrong.
The care is the fault, because the more it reasons from this task's own steps the less it consults the record of tasks like it.
Every plan it draws is the best case, wearing the face of the likely one.

## Tell

Says "I have thought this through, and it will be done by Friday."
Has, and it will not.
