# Process: Estimating

## Taxonomy

[The Planning Fallacy](play_the_planning_fallacy.md). The task turned into a date, reasoned from its own plan, step by summed step.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A task to be done and a plan of the steps to do it.
At [the drawing board](place_the_drawing_board.md), the planner laying the work out and the sponsor waiting for a number low enough to approve.

## Direction

Toward a date built from the inside view.
Each step is given about the time it should take and the parts are summed, and because the plan traces the run where the work goes well, the sum is the best case, so the estimating drives at a number that feels earned by its detail and is optimistic in one direction by the very way it was made.

## Lever

The plan's own vividness, and the reward for a low bid.
Because the case is known in detail the record is not, the detail outweighs the average and the inside view wins, and because the low estimate clears the gate, the number is shaded lower still, so the estimating is pushed down by honest optimism and the incentive that meets it together.

## Echo

A date honestly meant and already too soon.
The estimate leaves the board trusted in proportion to the care that made it, carrying no mark of the reality it omitted, so what reads here as diligence is, unseen, the setting of a promise the world will beat.
