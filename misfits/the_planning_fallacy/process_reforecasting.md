# Process: Reforecasting

## Taxonomy

[The Planning Fallacy](play_the_planning_fallacy.md). The estimate set again from the reference class instead of the plan, the remedy applied at its cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An overrun read and a move to price the next task differently.
At [the reforecast](place_the_reforecast.md), the forecaster leaving the plan for the record, and the planner holding that the record buries what is truly special here.

## Direction

Toward a date grounded in the class, not the case.
The reference class is found, its distribution read, and the estimate placed on it, so the reforecasting drives the number up off the inside view and onto what actually happened to like tasks, delivering the date the world will keep, and delivering with it the discomfort of a figure no one wanted.

## Lever

The record's weight put ahead of the plan's.
Because the outside view prices from outcomes and not intentions, it corrects the one-directional error the inside view cannot see in itself, so the reforecasting works by the deliberate refusal to reason from this task's own story, trading the particulars away for the accuracy the average carries.

## Echo

A truer date, bought and not free.
The estimate now prices the reality, at the cost of the case's detail and the ease of a hopeful number, and pressed too far it pads the work until the task expands to fill the buffer, so the cure for a date too soon stands over the standing risk of one too slack.
