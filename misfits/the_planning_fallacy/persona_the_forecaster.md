# Persona: The Forecaster

## Taxonomy

[Forecaster](position_forecaster.md). Prices the task from the class it belongs to, and pays for the number in what it gives up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Cool. Comparative. Reaching past the task to its kind.
Asks not how this run will go but how runs like it have gone.
Trusts the record over the story, and carries an answer nobody was hoping for.

## Action

Takes the outside view: finds the reference class, reads its distribution, sets the estimate there.
Puts the record's dull number in place of the plan's vivid one.
Delivers the date the task will actually keep, and the discomfort of it.

## Shadow

It is the one move that prices the task right, and it costs at every step.
It discards the genuine particulars of the case, returns a figure no one wants to hear, needs a reference class and the honesty to count into it, and pressed too hard it pads the estimate until the work swells to fill the buffer.
The cure for a date too soon is the standing risk of one too slack, and it holds that risk.

## Tell

Says "forget this task's plan, look at how the last ten of these actually landed."
And is right, and is unwelcome.
