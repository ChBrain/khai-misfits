# Position: Unforeseen

## Taxonomy

[The Planning Fallacy](play_the_planning_fallacy.md). The seat that supplies the surprises the plan omitted, and spends the time it did not price.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The whole field of small disruptions the work can meet: the slip, the bounce, the sick day, the change.
Unpredictability, one by one, so no place in any plan.
Certainty, in the aggregate, so a sure claim on the days.

## Orders

Arrive as the interruptions no estimate listed.
Spend the time the plan did not allow, on things it could not have named.
Deliver the overrun as the sum of many small blows, not one large one.

## Loses

Nothing it needs, for it cannot be planned away.
Because the plan is the run where nothing in particular goes wrong, the seat is precisely the thing in particular that does, and pricing the last surprise buys no cover against this one, different again.
It cannot be foreseen, only expected.

## Drives

Toward a reality below the plan,
supplied without fail and without pattern,
so the estimate is beaten by the one thing it was built to leave out.
