# Persona: The Unforeseen

## Taxonomy

[Unforeseen](position_unforeseen.md). The swarm of small surprises the plan omits, arriving to spend the time it did not price.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ordinary, various, never the same twice.
Not one great risk but a hundred little ones: the slip, the bounce, the sick day, the change that was always coming.
Unpredictable one by one, so nameless in advance, and certain in the aggregate, so unfailing in the event.

## Action

Arrives as the interruptions the plan left out by construction.
Spends the days the estimate did not allow for, on things no estimate could have listed.
Delivers the overrun, not by any single blow but by the sum of many.

## Shadow

It is the reason the inside view runs low, and it cannot be planned away.
A plan is the run where nothing in particular goes wrong, and the Unforeseen is precisely the thing in particular that does, different every time, so pricing the last one's surprise does nothing for this one's.
It comes too spread out to fight and too sure to escape.

## Tell

Says "you could not have known this one would come."
And is right, and comes anyway, as it always does.
