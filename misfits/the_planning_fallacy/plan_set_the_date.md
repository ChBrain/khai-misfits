# Plan: Set the Date

## Direction

Get the work approved on a number, and get the number low. Reward the estimate that clears the gate, press the date toward what wins the funding, and commit. Where a low bid buys the go-ahead, let it, for the project cannot start until the estimate is small enough to say yes to.
