# Persona: The Sponsor

## Taxonomy

[Sponsor](position_sponsor.md). Wants the low number that wins the approval, and lives the overrun it buys.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Decisive. Pressed. Holding the go-ahead and the bill.
Needs a date and a cost to approve, and needs them low enough to say yes.
Not a cynic: a backer who wants the project, and wants it to look as good as it can on the page.

## Action

Asks for the estimate and rewards the one that comes in low.
Bends the optimism the extra inch toward the number that clears the gate.
Commits to the date, and later carries the slip when the date is missed.

## Shadow

It joins an honest optimism to an incentive, and the two pull the same way.
Because the low bid wins the funding, the estimate is shaded lower than even the inside view alone would set it, believed and pitched at once, so the promise is worst exactly where the stakes are highest.
It suffers the overrun it helped price, having chosen the number that could not hold.

## Tell

Says "can we not do it sooner, that is the number that gets approved."
And gets it approved, and waits past it.
