# Place: The Drawing Board

## Taxonomy

[The Planning Fallacy](play_the_planning_fallacy.md). Where the task is laid out and the estimate built from its plan, before any of the work is done.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The task broken into steps, each with a time that seems about right, and the sum of them a date.
The plan drawn clean along the run where the work goes well, and the estimate reading off it, confident and particular, with the record of like tasks nowhere on the board.

## Holds

The whole case for the inside view, laid out in detail.
Here sits the task known better than any other, its steps visible and its plan vivid, so the estimate built here feels earned by the thinking that went into it, and the more detail the board carries the surer the number looks and the smaller the pull of any grey average from outside.

## Offers

A date honestly arrived at, and the confidence to promise it.
Because the board shows the plan and not the past, it offers an estimate that is the best case wearing the face of the likely one, and offers it with the full weight of the work that produced it, so the number leaves the board trusted exactly in proportion to how carefully it was drawn.

## Withheld

The reality the plan omits.
The board cannot show the swarm of small surprises, because none of them can be named in advance, so it withholds the one thing that will decide the date, and being all inside view it withholds the record too, present elsewhere and never brought onto the board where the estimate is set.
