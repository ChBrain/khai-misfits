# Process: Overrunning

## Taxonomy

[The Planning Fallacy](play_the_planning_fallacy.md). The work meeting the days it did not price, and the promised date passing with it unfinished.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An estimate laid down as dates and the work begun against them.
At [the calendar](place_the_calendar.md), the unforeseen arriving as the week's real texture, each small surprise taking its time from a schedule that allowed for none.

## Direction

Toward a finish later than the promise.
The plan's smooth run meets the slip, the bounce, the sick day, the change, and each spends days the estimate did not hold, so the overrunning drives the actual past the estimate, not by one large blow but by the sum of many small ones, until the date arrives and the work is not done.

## Lever

The certainty of the aggregate against the blindness of the parts.
Because no single surprise could be foreseen, none was priced, and because their sum is as sure as anything in the work, the total lands without fail, so the overrunning turns a hundred unpredictable little delays into one predictable large one, arriving on the dates the estimate set too soon.

## Echo

A task delivered late and over, like its kind before it.
The finish lands past the promise, the gap now real in spent days, and the reality the plan omitted is fully supplied at last, read on the calendar as the same overrun as last time, waiting to be called bad luck once more.
