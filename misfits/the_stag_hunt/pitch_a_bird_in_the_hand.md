# Pitch: A Bird in the Hand

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Level-headed, cautious, unwilling to be the fool.
The plain wisdom of taking the sure smaller thing over the grand one that hangs on everyone else holding their nerve.
Nothing timid in it, quite: only a hunter who will not stake the whole day on trusting the rest.

## Undertow

Under the caution, a prize everyone wants and no one will risk.
Because the stag is worth most but needs all, and the hare is worth less but needs none, any doubt makes the hare the guarded move, so each guards, and the guarding is the scattering, and the stag they all preferred walks off untaken.
The safe choice is not against the others: it is a shield against them, and the shield is what defeats the hunt.

## Nerve

The feeling of eyeing the great shared prize and reaching, instead, for the sure small one,
not because you want it more, but because you cannot be certain the others will hold,
while every one of them, wanting the stag as much as you, reaches for their hare for the very same reason.

## Echo

After the run: a field of hunters each with a hare, and the stag they all wanted gone,
no one greedy and no one betrayed, only each guarding against a doubt the rest were guarding against too,
and a trust that would have fed them all, real and reachable and never quite dared.
