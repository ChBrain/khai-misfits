# Place: The Muster

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). Where the party gathers before the hunt and the assurance is exchanged, the trust the stag needs built or left unbuilt.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The hunters drawn together before they take the field, giving their word, recalling the hunts they have held before, watching one to go first.
The pledges made and weighed, each hunter judging whether the others mean it, the trust for the stag either taking hold here or failing to.

## Holds

The remedy, and the whole fragility of it.
Here the assurance is made, so the muster holds the one thing that lets all take the stag, a confidence in the others strong enough to hold the line, and holds with it everything that undoes that confidence: the word that must be believed, the pull of the sure hare, the single defection that will strand the rest.

## Offers

A party assured enough to hold, at the price of a trust that must be earned.
Because the muster is where the word is given and the lead is taken first, it offers the hunters the confidence the payoffs never could, so it is where the better outcome becomes reachable, the stag made safe by a trust the party builds in itself before it sets out.

## Withheld

Any assurance that keeps itself.
The muster can build the trust and cannot guarantee it, because a word holds only while it is believed and falls the moment one hunter breaks it, so it withholds security, and raising the confidence fresh each hunt it withholds permanence, handing the party a trust it must make again every time and can lose in an instant.
