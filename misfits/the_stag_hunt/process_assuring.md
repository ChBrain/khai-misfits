# Process: Assuring

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The trust built so the party can hold for the stag, the remedy raised against the pull of the hare.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A party losing the stag to doubt and one who moves to settle it.
At [the muster](place_the_muster.md), the voucher giving a word, recalling a hunt held before, going first so the others can see the line will hold.

## Direction

Toward a confidence strong enough to take the stag.
Because the failure is doubt and not greed, assuring works on the doubt directly, building in each hunter enough trust in the others to hold, so it drives the party back toward the payoff-dominant outcome the payoffs alone could not secure, letting all commit where each alone would have hedged.

## Lever

A believed commitment, shown and not merely spoken.
Because assurance changes nothing unless it is genuinely trusted, the lever is a word made credible, a lead taken first, a pledge that binds, a history recalled, so assuring works only as far as the hunters believe it, and must be renewed each hunt against the standing safety of the hare.

## Echo

A stag takeable, on a trust that can still break.
The party holds, the great prize is within reach again, and the whole of it rests on a confidence one defection would shatter, so what assuring restores is real and provisional at once, the better outcome reachable for exactly as long as the word is kept.
