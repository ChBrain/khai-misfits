# Position: Stag

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The seat of the payoff-dominant prize, greatest of the day and won only by all together.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The largest reward on the field, enough to feed the whole party.
A single condition: that every hunter holds the line until it is taken.
No means of its own to keep them holding, only the worth that would if trust allowed.

## Orders

Hold out for all.
Pay the whole party that keeps together, and pay nothing the moment one peels away.
Reward the trust the hunters have, and withhold everything the instant it fails.

## Loses

Itself, exactly because its worth depends on everyone.
Because it pays only on all holding, the seat cannot be secured by any single hunter, so it can be wanted with a whole heart and go untaken, forfeit not to anyone preferring less but to no one being sure of the rest.
It is the best prize and the most fragile, lost to a doubt rather than a wish.

## Drives

Toward a reward that hangs on every hand at once,
preferred by all and safe for none,
so the greatest catch is the one a single flinch lets go.
