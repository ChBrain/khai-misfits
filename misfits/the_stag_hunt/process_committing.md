# Process: Committing

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The hunters holding their places for the stag, the payoff-dominant outcome taken by a party that stays whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A stag on offer and a party that would rather have it.
At [the thicket](place_the_thicket.md), the hunters holding the line around the prize, each post essential and the catch secure only while none steps away.

## Direction

Toward the best outcome of the day, held by everyone at once.
Each hunter that stays keeps the party whole, and because the stag pays most and pays all, committing drives toward the reward every hunter prefers, delivering it in full to a line that does not break, so the process is the trust made good, the shared prize taken together.

## Lever

The stag's worth to a party that holds.
Because holding for the stag is the best reply to the others holding, each hunter is rewarded for staying when the rest stay, so committing runs on the plain fact that the great catch, unlike the hare, pays only to a party that keeps faith, and pays it richly.

## Echo

A stag taken, or a line one flinch from breaking.
While all hold, the party has the best of the day, the trust cashed into the great prize, but the whole of it hangs on every hand, so the same commitment that delivers the stag is the exposure that a single peel turns into the empty hand.
