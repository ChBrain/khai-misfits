# Piece: The Assurance

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The believed commitment that makes the stag safe, built fresh each hunt and lost to one broken promise.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the muster](place_the_muster.md), where the word is given and the lead is taken first.
Set against [the hedgerow](place_the_hedgerow.md), whose sure hare it must outweigh each time.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play has no remedy, only a doubt that scatters the party every hunt.
With it the misfit is answered, and answered conditionally: a trust strong enough that each holds for the stag, made by a word believed, a lead shown, a pledge that binds. The Assurance is load bearing because it is the one correction the structure allows and the hardest to keep (Sen; Skyrms): it settles the doubt the payoffs cannot, but only while it is genuinely believed, so the piece carries both the exit and its fragility, a confidence that one defection shatters and every new hunt must rebuild.

## Apparent

An easy fix, a matter of the hunters just agreeing to hold.
Seen from outside, the assurance looks like a simple promise anyone could make, and the truth that it must be truly believed and not merely spoken, that it falls to a single broken nerve and must be raised anew each time against the pull of the sure hare, stays hidden behind how obvious agreeing to cooperate appears.

## Yearbook

Given by [the voucher](persona_the_voucher.md) through [assuring](process_assuring.md), the trust that settles the doubt.
It is the exit and its cost: the Assurance is what steadies [the fork](piece_the_fork.md) toward the stag, overcomes the pull of [the hedge](piece_the_hedge.md), and makes [the empty hand](piece_the_empty_hand.md) unthinkable enough for all to hold, for exactly as long as the word is kept.
