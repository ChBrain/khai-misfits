# Piece: The Hedge

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The risk-dominant choice, the safe hare taken under any doubt because it does not depend on the others.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the hedgerow](place_the_hedgerow.md), where the sure catch is taken alone.
Spreading across [the hunt](place_the_hunt.md), where one hunter's hedge makes the next hunter's the wiser move.

## Load Bearing

Without it the doubt would have nowhere safe to go, and the party might hold for the stag regardless.
With it the doubt has an answer: a payoff that comes whatever the others do, so no hunter taking it is ever the fool. The Hedge is load bearing because it is the move risk selects (Harsanyi and Selten): when one equilibrium needs trust and the other does not, the trust-free one is the guarded choice under uncertainty, so the piece carries the plain prudence by which each hunter, shielding against the rest, scatters the party from the stag.

## Apparent

Sensible caution, a hunter simply taking the sure thing.
Seen in one hunter, the hedge looks like ordinary good judgement, and the truth that every hunter making the same safe choice is exactly what forfeits the prize they all preferred, that the shield each raises is the scattering, stays hidden behind how reasonable it is not to be left holding alone.

## Yearbook

Taken by [the partner](persona_the_partner.md) through [hedging](process_hedging.md), the safe move that spreads.
It is the guard that undoes the hunt: the Hedge is what [the doubt](piece_the_doubt.md) drives each hunter to at [the fork](piece_the_fork.md), the choice that leaves whoever held for the stag with [the empty hand](piece_the_empty_hand.md), and the pull [the assurance](piece_the_assurance.md) must overcome each time.
