# Plot: The Empty Hand

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Thicket](place_the_thicket.md), the stag needing all and one place now empty.
Whoever held for it left in the brush with nothing, the prize gone, while the harers have their smaller sure catch.

## Action

[Hedging](process_hedging.md): [The Hunter](persona_the_hunter.md), holding [the hunter's seat](position_hunter.md), finds itself the one who stayed as another peeled, while [The Stag](persona_the_stag.md), holding [the stag's seat](position_stag.md), slips free the instant the line broke.

## Stage

[The Hunter](persona_the_hunter.md), still on [Go for the Stag](plan_go_for_the_stag.md), holds a moment too long and takes neither stag nor hare, the empty hand that every other hunter guarded against.
[The Stag](persona_the_stag.md), on [Hold Out for All](plan_hold_out_for_all.md), pays nothing to the one who stayed, and the party settles for the worse outcome that all of them, in the end, would rather not have.

## Tension

The one who trusted most comes away with the least.
There is no justice to read into it: the hunter who held did nothing wrong, it simply believed the line would hold. But the stag pays only on all staying, so a single peel leaves the loyal one with nothing and hands the day to the cautious, and the plot closes on the lesson every hunter now carries, that holding for the stag can cost you everything, which is exactly the fear that will scatter the next hunt too.
