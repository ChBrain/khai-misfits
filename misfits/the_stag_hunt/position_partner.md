# Position: Partner

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The seat whose staying the stag depends on, weighing the same doubt from the other side.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The same choice as the hunter, and the same preference for the stag.
A place in the line that the hunter is counting on, and a hare of its own within reach.
The same blindness to the others' intent, and the same fear of holding alone.

## Orders

Hold for the stag if the hunter holds, and peel to the hare if the doubt grows too great.
Watch the hunter for the flinch, and do not be the one left in the thicket.
Mirror the caution back, move for move.

## Loses

The stag, exactly by mirroring the hunter's guard with its own.
Because each reads the other's doubt as a warning and hedges against it, the seat's peel is the defection the hunter feared, so the two guard each other into the safe outcome, neither betraying and both breaking.
It loses the prize it shared by protecting against the partner it would have trusted.

## Drives

Toward the same safe hare the hunter reaches for,
doubting the hunter as the hunter doubts it,
so two hunters who both wanted the stag mirror one another off it.
