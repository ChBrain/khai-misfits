# Plot: The Hedge

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Hedgerow](place_the_hedgerow.md), the doubt loose and a sure hare within reach.
One hunter deciding not to be the fool left holding, and turning to the catch that pays whatever the rest do.

## Action

[Hedging](process_hedging.md): [The Partner](persona_the_partner.md), holding [the partner's seat](position_partner.md), peels off to the safe hare, while [The Hare](persona_the_hare.md), holding [the hare's seat](position_hare.md), pays it in full regardless of the others.

## Stage

[The Partner](persona_the_partner.md), having tabled [Take the Sure Thing](plan_take_the_sure_thing.md), takes the hare to shield against holding alone, and becomes the defection the hunter feared.
[The Hare](persona_the_hare.md) tables [Pay Regardless](plan_pay_regardless.md), rewarding the peel whatever the party does, so once one may break, breaking first is each hunter's safe move and the hedging spreads down the line.

## Tension

Each shields itself, and the shields together lose the stag.
There is no treachery in the peel: the partner takes the hare not to cheat the hunter but to guard against being stranded, the same guard every hunter is weighing. But once one turns aside, holding for the stag is only riskier for the rest, so the safe move ripples outward, and the plot closes with the party scattering to hares, the line too thin for the stag, undone by prudence and not by betrayal.
