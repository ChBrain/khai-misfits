# Position: Hare

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The seat of the risk-dominant payoff, smaller than the stag and sure whatever the party does.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A modest reward, there in the grass for whoever turns to it.
Independence from the others: it pays the same however many hold or peel.
No need of trust, and no grandeur to offer.

## Orders

Pay regardless.
Reward each hunter who takes it, and ask nothing of what the rest choose.
Stand always available, the safe answer to any doubt about the stag.

## Loses

Nothing, and that is how it wins the field it does not deserve.
Because it pays whatever happens, no hunter is ever the fool for taking it, so under any uncertainty it is the guarded move, and the seat draws the party off the stag not by being better but by being sure.
It is the lesser prize, and it takes the day whenever trust is short.

## Drives

Toward a sure smaller catch that costs no trust,
claiming each hunter the moment the stag is in doubt,
so the safe prize defeats the better one by asking nothing of anyone.
