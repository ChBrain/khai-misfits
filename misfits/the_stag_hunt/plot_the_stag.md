# Plot: The Stag

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Thicket](place_the_thicket.md), the stag cornered and takeable if the whole line holds.
The best prize of the day on offer, worth more to each hunter than any hare, needing only that all stay.

## Action

[Committing](process_committing.md): [The Hunter](persona_the_hunter.md), holding [the hunter's seat](position_hunter.md), takes its place for the stag, while [The Stag](persona_the_stag.md), holding [the stag's seat](position_stag.md), offers its whole worth to a party that holds together.

## Stage

[The Hunter](persona_the_hunter.md), having tabled [Go for the Stag](plan_go_for_the_stag.md), holds the line for the prize it prefers above any hare, ready to take the great catch with the rest.
[The Stag](persona_the_stag.md) tables [Hold Out for All](plan_hold_out_for_all.md), promising the largest reward to the whole party and nothing the instant one peels away.

## Tension

The best outcome is right there, wanted by everyone, and hanging on every hand at once.
There is no conflict of aims to find: each hunter genuinely prefers the stag, and holding for it is the best reply to the others holding. But the prize pays only if all stay, so its whole worth rests on a trust not yet tested, and the plot closes with the great catch on offer and its fate already resting not on what anyone wants but on what each will come to believe of the rest.
