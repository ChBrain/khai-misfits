# Position: Hunter

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The seat that would take the stag with the party, and hedges to the hare under doubt.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A place in the line the stag needs held, and a hare within reach if it breaks.
A clear preference for the stag, the better prize by far.
No sight into the others' minds, only a guess at whether they will stay.

## Orders

Go for the stag while the party holds, and take the hare if it will not.
Prefer the great shared prize, and guard against being the last one holding for it.
Read any doubt about the others as reason to reach for the sure thing.

## Loses

The stag, exactly by guarding against losing it alone.
Because the prize hangs on all and the seat cannot be sure of all, its hedge to the hare is prudent and, made by every seat at once, is the scattering that forfeits the stag, so it cannot protect itself without helping to lose the thing it wanted.
It ends with a hare it preferred less, and the stag gone for want of trust.

## Drives

Toward the safe hare it is never the fool for taking,
preferring the stag and unable to risk it alone,
so the caution that shields one hunter, shared by all, is what walks the stag off the field.
