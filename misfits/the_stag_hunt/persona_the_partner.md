# Persona: The Partner

## Taxonomy

[Partner](position_partner.md). The other hunter, whose staying the stag depends on, and who doubts in turn.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Equal. Uncertain. Weighing the same choice from the other side.
Wants the stag as much as the hunter does, and is just as unsure the hunter will hold.
No treachery in it: only a mirror of the same caution, looking back.

## Action

Watches the hunter for a sign of flinching, and finds only a hunter watching back.
Peels off to the safe hare the moment its own doubt outweighs its want of the stag.
Becomes, by peeling, the very defection the hunter feared, and confirms the fear it shared.

## Shadow

It is the hunter seen from across the field, and its doubt is the hunter's doubt answered.
Because each reads the other's caution as a warning and hedges against it, the two mirror one another into the safe outcome, neither betraying and both breaking, so its peel is not a stab in the back but a shield raised at the same moment as the hunter's.
It would have held, had it trusted the hunter to.

## Tell

Says "what if the others do not stay, and I am left holding alone?"
Peels to the hare, and is, to the hunter, the proof the fear was right.
