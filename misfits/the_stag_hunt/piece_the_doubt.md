# Piece: The Doubt

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The strategic uncertainty about the others, the unconfirmable trust on which the stag's whole worth depends.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the hunt](place_the_hunt.md), where each hunter weighs a resolve in the others it cannot see.
Answered only at [the muster](place_the_muster.md), where trust is built to settle what the field cannot.

## Load Bearing

Without it the party would hold for the stag, since all prefer it, and there would be no trap.
With it the better prize becomes unsafe: the stag hangs on everyone, so its worth to each depends on a trust in the rest that no hunter can confirm. The Doubt is load bearing because it is the wedge the whole failure turns on (Skyrms): not a wish to defect but an uncertainty about the others, so the trap is authored by strategic uncertainty alone, the prize lost to what cannot be known rather than to what anyone wants.

## Apparent

A vague unease, a hunter simply not sure of the party.
Seen in the moment, the doubt looks like a minor hesitation, and the truth that it is the entire mechanism, that an unconfirmable trust is enough on its own to forfeit the best prize while no one prefers less, stays hidden behind how small a thing mere uncertainty seems next to greed or betrayal.

## Yearbook

Spread through the party by [doubting](process_doubting.md), the uncertainty that loads onto the stag.
It is the wedge that needs no villain: the Doubt is what makes [the hedge](piece_the_hedge.md) rational at [the fork](piece_the_fork.md), the fear that delivers [the empty hand](piece_the_empty_hand.md) to whoever holds, and the exact thing [the assurance](piece_the_assurance.md) exists to settle.
