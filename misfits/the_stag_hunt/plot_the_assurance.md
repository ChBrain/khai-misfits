# Plot: The Assurance

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Muster](place_the_muster.md), the party gathered before the next hunt with the last one's scattering still fresh.
Someone moving to settle the doubt directly, since the stag was lost to it and not to any hunter's greed.

## Action

[Assuring](process_assuring.md): [The Voucher](persona_the_voucher.md), holding [the voucher's seat](position_voucher.md), gives a word each hunter believes and goes first, while [The Hunter](persona_the_hunter.md), holding [the hunter's seat](position_hunter.md), finds it can hold now that it trusts the rest to.

## Stage

[The Voucher](persona_the_voucher.md), having tabled [Give the Word](plan_give_the_word.md), builds the confidence the payoffs could not, a pledge shown by taking the line first and in sight of all.
[The Hunter](persona_the_hunter.md), back on [Go for the Stag](plan_go_for_the_stag.md), holds its place because it believes the others will, and the stag comes within reach of a party assured enough to keep faith.

## Tension

The one move that works is the one that must be made again each time and can break in an instant.
There is no easy peace here: the assurance is plainly what lets all take the stag, yet it counts for nothing unless each hunter truly believes it, one broken promise strands the rest, and the sure hare pulls at the party still. So the play closes where it must, the trust built and the stag taken together ... or the doubt creeps back, the word goes unkept, and the hunters scatter to their hares once more.
