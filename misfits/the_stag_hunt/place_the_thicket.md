# Place: The Thicket

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). Where the stag is cornered and can be taken, but only by a party that holds every place at once.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The stag ringed in the brush, the great catch within reach, needing the whole line to close on it together.
Hunters holding their places around it, each one's post essential, the prize secure only while none steps away and gone the instant one does.

## Holds

The payoff-dominant outcome and its single condition.
Here the best prize of the day is takeable, so the thicket holds the reward every hunter prefers, and holds it under the one term that governs the stag: all or none, so it is the place where trust is cashed into the great catch, or where a lone holder is left with the empty hand.

## Offers

The largest reward, to a party whole.
Because the stag can be taken only by everyone holding, the thicket offers the whole party its best outcome the moment all stay, so it is where the trust that survived the doubt is paid out in full, the shared prize delivered to a line that did not break.

## Withheld

Anything at all, to a party that breaks.
The thicket cannot give the stag to those who stayed if one peeled away, because the prize hangs on all, so it withholds everything from the loyal the instant the line fails, leaving whoever held for the stag with the empty hand and the harers with their smaller catch elsewhere.
