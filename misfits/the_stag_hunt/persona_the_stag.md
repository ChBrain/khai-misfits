# Persona: The Stag

## Taxonomy

[Stag](position_stag.md). The payoff-dominant prize, worth most to all and won only if none breaks away.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Great. Shared. Conditional on everyone.
Is the best catch on the field, feeding the whole party, the prize each hunter would choose above any hare.
Cares for one thing only: that no one leaves the line before it is taken.

## Action

Offers the largest reward, and offers it to all or to none.
Rewards a party that holds together, and slips away the instant a single hunter peels off.
Hangs its whole worth on the trust the hunters have, or lack, in one another.

## Shadow

It is the outcome everyone prefers and no one can secure alone, because it pays only on the others' holding.
Since its value to each depends on every other staying, it can be wanted with a whole heart and still go untaken, undone not by anyone preferring less but by no one able to be sure of the rest, so the best prize is the most fragile.
It is there for the taking, and takeable only together.

## Tell

Says "I am the best of the day, and I come only if none of you leaves."
And walks free the moment one does.
