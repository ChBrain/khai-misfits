# Position: Voucher

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The seat that builds the trust the stag needs, and answers for how easily it breaks.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The insight that the stag is lost to doubt and not to greed, and the standing to address it.
The means of assurance: a word, a shared history, a lead taken first and in sight, a binding pledge.
The burden of raising that trust anew each hunt, and guarding it once raised.

## Orders

Give the word.
Make each hunter confident enough of the others to hold for the stag, and go first if that is what it takes.
Build the assurance the payoffs cannot supply, and keep it against the pull of the sure hare.

## Loses

Ease and certainty, exactly because trust cannot be commanded.
Because assurance works only when it is genuinely believed, and one hunter breaking it strands every other, the seat cannot decree the party's confidence, only build it and defend it, so its remedy stands on nerve and falls to a single defection.
It answers a doubt no payoff can settle with a trust no promise can guarantee.

## Drives

Toward a party assured enough to take the stag,
holding a trust that is never owned, only kept,
so the one move that works is the one that must be made again each time and lost to a single broken nerve.
