# Persona: The Hunter

## Taxonomy

[Hunter](position_hunter.md). Would take the stag with the others, and hedges to the hare when unsure they will hold.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Willing. Clear about the prize. Watching the others.
Wants the stag, and knows it is the best prize on the field, and knows it needs the whole party to hold.
No greed in it: only the wish for the great catch, tempered by an eye on whether the rest will stay.

## Action

Weighs the stag against the doubt, and takes the hare when the doubt wins.
Would hold its place gladly if it could be sure of the party.
Hedges, in the end, against being the one left in the thicket with nothing.

## Shadow

Not greedy, guarded: it prefers the stag and takes the hare only to shield against the others flinching.
Because the stag's worth hangs on everyone and the hare's on no one, its own caution is rational and, shared by all, is exactly what scatters the hunt, so it defeats the prize it wanted by protecting itself from the chance the others would.
It would hold for the stag in a heartbeat, if only it were sure.

## Tell

Says "I want the stag, but I cannot be the only one still holding for it."
Takes the hare, and so does everyone who said the same.
