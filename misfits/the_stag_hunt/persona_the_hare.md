# Persona: The Hare

## Taxonomy

[Hare](position_hare.md). The risk-dominant payoff, worth less than the stag and sure whatever the others do.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Modest. Reliable. Indifferent to the party.
Is the smaller catch in the grass, taken alone, needing no one else to hold.
Asks for no trust and offers no grandeur: only a sure thing, there for whoever turns to it.

## Action

Pays each hunter who takes it, whatever the rest choose.
Rewards no coordination and punishes no defection, standing entirely apart from the others' nerve.
Waits, always available, as the safe answer to any doubt about the stag.

## Shadow

It is worth less and wins more often, because it is the one prize that trust cannot cost.
Since it pays regardless, no hunter is ever the fool for taking it, so under any uncertainty it is the guarded move, and its very safety is what draws the party off the stag, the lesser prize claiming the field by asking nothing of anyone.
It never fails a hunter, and in never failing, it defeats the better hunt.

## Tell

Says "I am smaller, but I am sure, and I do not care what the others do."
And is taken, every time the stag is in doubt.
