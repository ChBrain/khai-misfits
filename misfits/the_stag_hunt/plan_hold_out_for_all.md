# Plan: Hold Out for All

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the stag's seat](position_stag.md), inside [The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md).

## Owner

## Direction

Pay the whole party that holds together, and pay nothing the moment one peels away. Be the greatest catch of the day, worth more to each than any hare, and grant that worth only to a party whose every hand stays in. Reward the trust, and withhold on the flinch.

## Orders

([The Stag](persona_the_stag.md) offers the largest reward to all or to none, and slips free the instant a single hunter breaks the line.)

([The Thicket](place_the_thicket.md); the [committing](process_committing.md) process; at [the fork](piece_the_fork.md) the stag pays only the party that holds, so one flinch delivers [the empty hand](piece_the_empty_hand.md) to whoever stayed.)
