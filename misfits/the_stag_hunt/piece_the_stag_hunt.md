# Piece: The Stag Hunt

## Taxonomy

[The Stag Hunt](play_the_stag_hunt.md). The phenomenon whole: a better outcome all prefer and none dares alone, and its remedy in a trust that is only ever kept, never owned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set on [the hunt](place_the_hunt.md), where the fork opens, and decided at [the thicket](place_the_thicket.md) or [the hedgerow](place_the_hedgerow.md).
Answered at [the muster](place_the_muster.md), where the trust it needs is built.

## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without the mechanism that binds them.
With it the whole trap stands in one object: two equilibria, a prize that needs all and a payoff that needs none, a doubt that loads onto the better outcome, a hedge that scatters the party, and an assurance that alone can hold them together. The Stag Hunt is load bearing because it is the structure entire (Rousseau; Skyrms): not a conflict of interest but a failure of trust, every hunter preferring the stag and each driven to the hare by uncertainty about the rest, correctable only by a confidence that must be built and can break.

## Apparent

A cooperation problem like any other, solved by everyone simply choosing to work together.
Seen from outside, the stag hunt looks like a matter of will, the hunters needing only to commit, and the truth that commitment is a best reply here and still not reached, that the obstacle is an unconfirmable trust and not a clash of aims, that its remedy is fragile by nature, stays hidden behind how easily cooperation seems it should come when everyone wants it.

## Yearbook

The trap entire, staged across the run.
The Stag Hunt is [the fork](piece_the_fork.md) between stag and hare on which [the doubt](piece_the_doubt.md) drives each hunter to [the hedge](piece_the_hedge.md), the [empty hand](piece_the_empty_hand.md) that makes the hedging wise, and the better outcome only [the assurance](piece_the_assurance.md) can hold, by a trust kept fresh and never owned.
