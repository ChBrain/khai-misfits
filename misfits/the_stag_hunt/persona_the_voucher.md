# Persona: The Voucher

## Taxonomy

[Voucher](position_voucher.md). Builds the trust the stag needs, giving a word each hunter believes, and answers for its fragility.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steadying. Credible. Working on the doubt itself.
Sees that the stag is lost to no one's greed, only to each hunter's uncertainty about the rest, and reaches to settle it.
No naivety in it: knows the word must be believed and not merely said, and that it holds only as long as it is kept.

## Action

Gives the assurance the hunt lacks: a pledge, a shared history recalled, a lead taken first and in plain sight.
Makes each hunter confident enough of the others to hold for the stag.
Raises the trust anew each time, against the standing pull of the sure hare.

## Shadow

It offers the one thing that lets all take the stag, and the one thing that cannot be spoken into being.
Because assurance works only if it is genuinely believed, and one hunter breaking it strands every other, the voucher cannot command trust, only build it and guard it, so its remedy stands on nerve and falls to a single defection, and must be won again with every new hunt.
It answers a doubt no payoff can settle with a trust no promise can guarantee.

## Tell

Says "hold with me, all of you, and I will go first so you can see it done."
Goes first, and the stag is takeable ... for exactly as long as the word is kept.
