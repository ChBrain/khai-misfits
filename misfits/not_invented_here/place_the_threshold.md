# Place: The Threshold

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The boundary where the outside idea arrives and is judged.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The good idea at the door, offering its worth, and the group reading its origin first.
The import turned back, sometimes on reflex and sometimes on real risk, the two rejections wearing one face.

## Holds

The boundary, the reflex, and the better way.
Here the line between inside and outside does its marking, and here the good idea is met and refused, so the threshold holds both the edge that binds the group and the reflex that fires at whatever it did not build, with the merit of the import standing unheard between them.

## Offers

A trusted inside and a filtered door.
The threshold keeps out what the group cannot see within and admits what it made, so it offers the safety of the known and the coherence of a guarded boundary, a real protection against real outside risk.

## Withheld

A hearing on the merit.
The origin is read before the worth, so the threshold withholds the judgement the import asks for, and turns the good idea away on the one fact that has nothing to do with whether it works.
