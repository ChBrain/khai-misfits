# Persona: The Gatekeeper

## Taxonomy

[Gatekeeper](position_gatekeeper.md). The rare bridge who reads the outside and translates it inward.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Bilingual across the boundary, at home both inside and out.
Reads the outside world's work and carries it in, in the group's own terms, so it can be trusted.
Rewards a good idea adopted as much as one invented.

## Action

Brings the outside idea across and translates it into something the group can own.
Makes the import legible from the inside, so its merit can be weighed over its origin.
Turns adoption into a thing the group can take pride in, not a defeat.

## Shadow

Its bridging is the one move that lets the best idea win, and it is rare, hard, and easily lost.
It cannot open the boundary without importing the risk the guardian feared and thinning the cohesion the boundary gave.
The one who could let the world in is the one whose success erodes the very walls that made the group worth joining.

## Tell

Says "let me bring this in, in our own language."
Knowing every gate it opens is also a wall it lowers.
