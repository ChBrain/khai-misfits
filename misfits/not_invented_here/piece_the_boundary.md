# Piece: The Boundary

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The line between inside and outside that trusts the made and suspects the imported.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the threshold](place_the_threshold.md), where it does its marking.
The edge that makes an inside and an outside, and reads every idea's origin first.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no group, and no reflex to reject the outside.
With it the group has both its strength and its flaw in one edge: the boundary is the line that binds a team by marking what it made as trusted and what it did not as suspect, so it earns cohesion inward at the exact price of suspicion outward. The Boundary is load bearing because it is the engine of the whole misfit (Tajfel and Turner on in-group and out-group): it is not a wall built out of pride but the very thing that makes a close team effective, and the same edge that trusts inward is the edge that turns the good idea away.

## Apparent

Just a healthy team identity.
A group that knows itself, trusts its own, and coheres looks like exactly the strong team any org wants, so the boundary reads as culture and belonging, and the truth that the same edge that binds it reflexively suspects the outside, that its cohesion and its insularity are one line, stays hidden behind how plainly good a close team is.

## Yearbook

Held by [the insider](persona_the_insider.md), enacted through [rejecting](process_rejecting.md), tabled as [Keep It Ours](plan_keep_it_ours.md).
It is the edge the misfit turns on: the Boundary trusts inward and fires [the reflex](piece_the_reflex.md) outward, the strength [Bridge the Boundary](plan_bridge_the_boundary.md) must open and, in opening, lower.
