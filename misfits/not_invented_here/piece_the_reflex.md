# Piece: The Reflex

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The automatic devaluing of the external because it is external, not because it is worse.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the threshold](place_the_threshold.md), where it fires.
In the instant between an outside idea appearing and the group turning it away.

## Load Bearing

Without it the boundary would merely mark, and each import could still be weighed on its merit.
With it the marking becomes a verdict: the reflex is the automatic devaluing of an idea for its origin, fired before the worth is heard, so the outside is refused for being outside and the judgement never reaches the merit. The Reflex is load bearing because it is where the boundary turns from strength to flaw (Antons and Piller on the attitude-based bias): it is not reasoned rejection but the origin standing in for the merit, and it borrows the guardian's real risks as its cover, so it passes, every time, as prudence.

## Apparent

Sensible caution about outside things.
An instinct to be wary of what the group did not build looks like reasonable prudence, so the reflex reads as good judgement, and the truth that it fires on the origin before the merit, refusing the good idea for the one fact that has nothing to do with whether it works, stays hidden behind how much it resembles the guardian's genuine care.

## Yearbook

Fired by [the insider](persona_the_insider.md) through [rejecting](process_rejecting.md), the verdict [Keep It Ours](plan_keep_it_ours.md) delivers.
It is origin standing in for merit: the Reflex is [the boundary](piece_the_boundary.md) turned to a refusal, indistinguishable from [the real risk](piece_the_real_risk.md) it hides behind.
