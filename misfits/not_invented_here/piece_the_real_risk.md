# Piece: The Real Risk

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The genuine danger an outside thing carries, indistinguishable from the reflex's excuse.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the bridge](place_the_bridge.md), where the remedy must weigh it.
In every import's unknowns, lock-in, unverified fit, and burden the group cannot maintain.

## Load Bearing

Without it the cure would be simple: open the boundary and take every outside idea.
With it the cure is a hazard: the real risk is the true danger an external thing carries, so some rejections are sound and opening the boundary genuinely imports what the group cannot see or maintain. The Real Risk is load bearing because it is what makes the reflex uncatchable and the remedy costly: it gives the guardian a true case, and because a reflex rejection and a reasoned one wear the same face, no one can cleanly tell the boundary defended on merit from the boundary defended on habit, so the hand that opens the gate to end the reflex also lets the real danger in.

## Apparent

Either pure excuse or pure prudence, depending who is asked.
To the gatekeeper it can look like all reflex dressed as reason; to the guardian, all sound caution; and both are wrong, because it is genuinely a mix with no clean seam, so the real risk reads as one or the other, and the truth that a true danger and a mere reflex are refused in the same words stays hidden behind whichever case is being made.

## Yearbook

Named by [the guardian](persona_the_guardian.md), weighed within [bridging](process_bridging.md), the peril [Guard the Boundary](plan_guard_the_boundary.md) presses and [Bridge the Boundary](plan_bridge_the_boundary.md) must admit.
It is the true danger tangled in the reflex: the Real Risk is why refusing every import is wrong and opening to all of them is dangerous, the reason answering [not-invented-here](piece_not_invented_here.md) cannot be done with a clean knife.
