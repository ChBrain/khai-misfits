# Position: Guardian

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The seat that rejects the outside for the real risk it carries.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A true account of what an outside thing can hide: unknowns, lock-in, an unverified fit, a burden the group cannot maintain.
The real goods of cohesion and ownership to defend.
Grounds for rejection that have nothing to do with pride.

## Orders

Weigh the import for its genuine dangers.
Turn it away when the risk is real, and defend what the group can understand and run.
Reject on reason, not reflex.

## Loses

The clean line between its reason and the reflex it shelters.
Its dangers are true and are exactly the cover the reflex hides behind, so the sound rejection and the reflexive one wear one face, and it cannot always tell, itself, which it is doing.
The honest case against the outside is the case that makes the reflex impossible to catch.

## Drives

Toward a guarded boundary,
on grounds that are real and also perfect camouflage,
so the true risk it names is the very thing that lets the reflex pass as reason.
