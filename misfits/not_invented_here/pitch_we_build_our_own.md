# Pitch: We Build Our Own

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Proud, self-reliant, sure of the team.
The voice of a close group that builds its own, understands what it made, and trusts what it owns.
Confident, capable, and quietly wary of anything it did not build.

## Undertow

Under the self-reliance, a good idea turned away at the door.
The boundary that binds the team suspects whatever comes from outside, so the import is judged by its origin before its merit, and a worse home-grown thing is raised to keep the making in-house.
The pride is real, and it is spending the better idea to keep the origin ours.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching a good team refuse a good idea for the one reason that has nothing to do with whether it works,
build a costlier version of it themselves,
and love the copy because they made it.

## Echo

After the run: a better idea left at the door, a worse one owned with pride,
effort poured into reinventing what was offered,
and a group that, letting less of the world in each year, has quietly stopped learning.
