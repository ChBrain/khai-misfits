# Plot: The Arrival

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The threshold](place_the_threshold.md), a good idea arriving from outside.
Better or cheaper than anything in-house, it offers its worth at the door, and the group reads, first, where it came from.

## Action

[Rejecting](process_rejecting.md): [The Import](persona_the_import.md), holding [the source's seat](position_source.md), offers the better way on its merit, while [The Insider](persona_the_insider.md), holding [the insider's seat](position_insider.md), marks it theirs and not ours and turns it away.

## Stage

[The Import](persona_the_import.md) tables [Offer the Better Way](plan_offer_the_better_way.md) and presents [the better way](piece_the_better_way.md), proven and cheaper.
[The Insider](persona_the_insider.md) tables [Keep It Ours](plan_keep_it_ours.md), and [the boundary](piece_the_boundary.md) fires [the reflex](piece_the_reflex.md), refusing the idea before its worth is weighed.

## Tension

A good idea is turned away for the one fact that has nothing to do with whether it works.
Nothing here is malice: the insider's trust in the inside is the cohesion that makes the team strong, and that same trust reads the import's origin before its merit. So the boundary answers first, and the answer is theirs, and a genuinely better solution is refused at the door, its worth unheard, on the ground of where it was made.
