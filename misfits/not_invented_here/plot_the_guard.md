# Plot: The Guard

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The threshold](place_the_threshold.md), an outside idea that really might carry risk.
This import is not plainly good: it brings unknowns, a lock-in, a fit no one can verify, a burden the group may not be able to maintain.

## Action

[Rejecting](process_rejecting.md) on reason: [The Guardian](persona_the_guardian.md), holding [the guardian's seat](position_guardian.md), weighs the genuine dangers and turns the import away, while [The Import](persona_the_import.md), holding [the source's seat](position_source.md), cannot prove its fit from outside the boundary.

## Stage

[The Guardian](persona_the_guardian.md) tables [Guard the Boundary](plan_guard_the_boundary.md) and names [the real risk](piece_the_real_risk.md), rejecting on grounds that have nothing to do with pride.
[The Import](persona_the_import.md) tables [Offer the Better Way](plan_offer_the_better_way.md) and cannot answer what the group cannot see within it.

## Tension

A sound rejection and a reflex rejection wear exactly the same face.
The guardian is often right: some outside things genuinely cannot be verified or maintained, and refusing them is prudence, not habit. But the reflex speaks in the same words, so a boundary held on real risk and a boundary held on mere origin are indistinguishable from the door, and no one, not even the guardian, can always tell which one just turned the idea away.
