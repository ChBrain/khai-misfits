# Persona: The Guardian

## Taxonomy

[Guardian](position_guardian.md). The reasoned boundary-keeper, rejecting the outside for the real risk it carries.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful, responsible, alert to what an outside thing can hide.
Names the unknowns, the lock-in, the fit that cannot be verified, the burden of a thing the group cannot maintain.
Not reflexive: reasoned, and often right.

## Action

Weighs the import for its genuine dangers and, when they are real, turns it away.
Defends the cohesion and ownership that let the group understand and run what it uses.
Rejects, sometimes soundly, on grounds that have nothing to do with mere pride.

## Shadow

Its reasons are true and are exactly the cover the reflex hides behind, so the sound rejection and the reflexive one wear one face.
It cannot always tell, itself, whether it is guarding against real risk or dressing a reflex in real reasons.
The honest case against the outside is the case that makes the reflex impossible to catch.

## Tell

Says "we can't verify it, we can't maintain it, we don't own it."
And is right just often enough that no one can say when it is only the reflex talking.
