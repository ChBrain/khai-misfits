# Position: Insider

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The seat inside the boundary, trusting the made and suspecting the imported.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The cohesion of a group that builds and trusts its own.
An understanding of the inside, and a wariness of what it cannot see within.
The reflex to read origin before merit.

## Orders

Trust what the group made.
Mark the outside idea suspect, and turn it away for being theirs.
Set the group to build its own, where the making can be owned.

## Loses

The better idea, to the boundary that binds the team.
Its trust in the inside is the cohesion that makes the group strong, and the same trust reflexively suspects the outside, so it turns away the import before merit is weighed, and reinvents what was already offered.
The loyalty that makes the team work is the loyalty that spurns the good idea.

## Drives

Toward the inside,
which it trusts because it understands it,
so the very cohesion that makes the group effective is the edge that turns the best idea away.
