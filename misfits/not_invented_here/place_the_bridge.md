# Place: The Bridge

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). Where the remedy is weighed: open the boundary and read the world in, or the walls hold.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A move to let the outside in: the gatekeeper reading the world's work and translating it into the group's own terms, so its merit can beat its origin.
The guardian counting what an open boundary admits, the real risk and the thinning of the cohesion the walls once gave.

## Holds

The remedy and its cost.
Here the boundary can be opened so the best idea wins regardless of whose it is, and here that opening imports the danger the reflex feared and erodes the ownership that made the group strong, so the bridge holds both the one move that lets the world in and the walls it lowers to do it.

## Offers

The best idea, on its merit, whatever its origin.
By translating the outside inward and rewarding adoption, the bridge offers a group that learns from the world and lets the better way win, the cure for a door that only ever refused.

## Withheld

A way to open the gate without lowering the wall.
The bridge cannot admit the world's ideas without admitting its risk, nor reward adoption without thinning the pride of invention, so it withholds a costless opening, and cannot tell the reflex it would cure from the sound judgement it would override.
