# Process: Bridging

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The move to read the outside and translate it inward, and the walls it lowers.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A recognition that the boundary is turning good ideas away, and a hand able to cross it.
At [the bridge](place_the_bridge.md), the gatekeeper reading the world's work and the guardian counting the risk of letting it in.

## Direction

Toward the best idea winning on its merit.
The outside is read and translated into the group's own terms, adoption is rewarded as much as invention, and the boundary becomes a gate that opens, so a good idea can be trusted and taken up regardless of whose it is.

## Lever

A translation that makes the outside legible from within.
Because an import is trusted only when the group can understand it in its own language, the remedy runs through the rare hand that can carry it across, and that same opening admits the real risk and thins the cohesion, so the cure cannot be had without the cost.

## Echo

The world let in, and the walls lowered with the gate.
The better idea adopted on its merit, and, in the same motion, the risk imported and the pride of pure invention thinned, so the group that learns from the world is the group whose boundary no longer wholly binds it.
