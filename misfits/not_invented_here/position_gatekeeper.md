# Position: Gatekeeper

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The seat that reads the outside and translates it inward.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A fluency across the boundary, at home both inside and out.
The skill to make an outside idea legible from the inside, so it can be trusted.
The rare standing to make adoption a thing the group takes pride in.

## Orders

Read the outside world's work and carry it in.
Translate the import into the group's own terms, so its merit can beat its origin.
Reward adoption as well as invention.

## Loses

The walls, in the act of opening the gates.
Its bridging is the one move that lets the best idea win, and it cannot open the boundary without importing the risk the guardian feared and thinning the cohesion the boundary gave.
The one seat that could let the world in is the seat whose success erodes what made the group worth joining.

## Drives

Toward an open boundary,
which admits the best idea and the risk together,
so the bridge that lets the world in is the same bridge that lowers the walls the group was built behind.
