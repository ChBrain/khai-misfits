# Not-Invented-Here: Reference

The research warrant for [Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The title is Katz and Allen's
NIH syndrome; the play runs on the account of group boundaries and knowledge flow, from the R&D tenure
study through the technological gatekeeper and social identity, all cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging Not-Invented-Here as a **social system**: a group bound by a boundary that trusts what it made
and suspects what it did not, so a good idea from outside is devalued for its origin before its merit,
rebuilt worse in-house to keep the making internal, and the group, letting less of the world in, slowly
stales. The play models the documented mechanics, the better way that is genuinely superior, the
boundary that binds inward and suspects outward, the reflex that devalues an idea for being external,
the home-grown version whose ownership hides its shortfall, the staling by which a shut boundary dulls
the group's edge, and the real risk that an outside thing truly carries and that the reflex hides
behind, as the load-bearing vectors of the structure, not as a tale of mere pride. The governing
finding of the source literature is the play's governing law: as a group's tenure rises and its
communication with outside sources falls, it comes to believe it holds a monopoly on knowledge in its
area, rejects external ideas by reflex, and its performance declines. It is distinct from the house's
Cargo Cult, which over-copies an external form rather than refusing it, and from the house's Closing
Ranks, which defends the group against outside blame rather than outside ideas.

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast the
  group as merely proud, because the source finding is precisely that the reflex is the shadow of real
  goods: the boundary's cohesion is what makes a close team effective, ownership means the group
  understands and can maintain what it runs, and an outside thing genuinely carries unknowns, lock-in,
  and unverified fit, so some rejections are sound. The misfit is that the same edge that binds the
  group inward suspects the good idea outward, and refuses it for the one fact that has nothing to do
  with whether it works.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into a costless opening. The one move that works
  is to bridge the boundary, reading the outside in and rewarding adoption as well as invention, and it
  runs through a rare gatekeeper, imports the real risk the reflex feared, and erodes the cohesion and
  ownership the boundary gave, against the plain fact that a reflex rejection and a sound one wear the
  same face and cannot be cleanly told apart. The reckoning is a boundary opened at the price of the
  strength it gave, not a free correction.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here,
  never claimed as the house's. The staging is the house's; the NIH syndrome, the tenure-and-
  communication study, the technological gatekeeper, and the in-group substrate are theirs, and the
  condition the syndrome actually requires, a real cohesion whose boundary suspects outward and a good
  idea whose only flaw is its origin, is carried as carefully as the finding itself.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **[The Better Way](piece_the_better_way.md)**: carries the superior external idea (Chesbrough), the good solution from
  beyond the boundary, refused on its origin.
- **[The Boundary](piece_the_boundary.md)**: carries the group identity (Tajfel & Turner), the edge that trusts the inside
  and suspects the outside, cohesion and insularity in one line.
- **[The Reflex](piece_the_reflex.md)**: carries the attitude-based bias (Antons & Piller), the automatic devaluing of an
  idea for being external before its merit is weighed.
- **[The Home-Grown](piece_the_home_grown.md)**: carries the internal rebuild (Katz & Allen), the worse or costlier version
  whose ownership hides its shortfall.
- **[The Staling](piece_the_staling.md)**: carries the tenure-and-performance decline (Katz & Allen), the group's edge
  dulling as a shut boundary starves it of outside knowledge.
- **[The Real Risk](piece_the_real_risk.md)**: carries the genuine hazard of external things, indistinguishable from the
  reflex's excuse, the reason the cure cannot cut cleanly.
- **[Not-Invented-Here](piece_not_invented_here.md)**: carries the phenomenon whole (Katz & Allen; Allen), the good idea
  refused for its origin, and its remedy in an opened boundary at a cost.
