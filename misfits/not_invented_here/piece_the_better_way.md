# Piece: The Better Way

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The good outside idea, better or cheaper than the in-house, offered and refused.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the threshold](place_the_threshold.md), where it is offered and turned back.
The proven solution from beyond the boundary, waiting to be judged on its merit.

## Load Bearing

Without it the rejection would cost nothing, and the misfit would be mere caution.
With it the refusal has a price: the better way is the outside idea that genuinely works, cheaper or stronger than anything in-house, so turning it away is not prudence but loss, and the need it would have met is left open for the group to rebuild. The Better Way is load bearing because its real worth is what makes the reflex a mistake and not a defence (Katz and Allen): it is not a trojan to be feared but a gift to be judged, and it is refused on the one fact, its origin, that has nothing to do with whether it works.

## Apparent

Just an outside vendor's pitch.
A solution offered from beyond the group looks like someone else's product to be wary of, so the better way reads as a sales approach, and the truth that it is genuinely superior, that adopting it would serve the aim better than anything the group will build, stays hidden behind the suspicion its origin draws.

## Yearbook

Offered by [the import](persona_the_import.md) through [rejecting](process_rejecting.md), tabled as [Offer the Better Way](plan_offer_the_better_way.md).
It is the good idea the misfit spurns: the Better Way is turned back by [the boundary](piece_the_boundary.md), and the thing [the home-grown](piece_the_home_grown.md) is built, at a premium, to replace.
