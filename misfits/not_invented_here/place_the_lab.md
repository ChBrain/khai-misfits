# Place: The Lab

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The group's own ground, where it builds and trusts what it makes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The team at its bench, building its own version of what was turned away: understood line by line, maintained with care, owned.
The pride of a thing made here, and the effort going, unremarked, into reinventing what was already offered.

## Holds

The home-grown and the staling.
Here the internal version is built and loved, and here, cut off from fresh outside knowledge, the group's edge quietly dulls, so the lab holds both the owned thing the team is proud of and the slow going-stale that ownership conceals.

## Offers

Understanding, ownership, and the pride of the made.
The group knows every line of what it built and can maintain and change it at will, so the lab offers real mastery of its own work, the genuine goods that make a close team strong.

## Withheld

The world's fresh knowledge, and the measure of what was refused.
The lab builds from within and lets little in, so it withholds the outside learning that would keep it current, and the pride in the home-grown hides how far the better way, and the world, have moved ahead.
