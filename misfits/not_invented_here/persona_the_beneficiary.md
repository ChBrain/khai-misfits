# Persona: The Beneficiary

## Taxonomy

[Beneficiary](position_beneficiary.md). The one the group's work is for, handed the worse home-grown and denied the better import.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Waiting, out where the work is used, for the thing to be good.
Cares nothing for whose idea it was, only that it works and keeps up with the world.
Sees the product slip behind what others already have, and cannot say why.

## Action

Receives the home-grown version instead of the better import.
Bears the group's insularity as a product that ages and falls behind.
Wants the outcome, and gets the one the boundary allowed.

## Shadow

It is who the work is for, and has no vote at the boundary that decides what it gets.
Its need is the whole point and counts for nothing against the reflex, so its decline is the last thing weighed.
The party the group serves is the party the group's pride quietly shortchanges.

## Tell

Says "why are we behind, when the better way was right there?"
Never told that the better way was turned away for being someone else's.
