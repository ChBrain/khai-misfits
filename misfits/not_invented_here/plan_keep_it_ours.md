# Plan: Keep It Ours

## Taxonomy

## Direction

Trust what the group made and suspect what it did not. Read an idea's origin before its merit, mark the outside one theirs and not ours, and turn it away, then set the group to build its own, where the making can be understood and owned. Keep the origin in-house.
