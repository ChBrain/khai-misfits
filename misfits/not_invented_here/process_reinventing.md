# Process: Reinventing

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The group building its own version of what it turned away, to keep the origin in-house.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A need still open, and a refusal to meet it from outside.
At [the lab](place_the_lab.md), the group setting to build its own after the import is turned back.

## Direction

Toward a home-grown thing, owned and worse.
The effort that could have adopted the import goes instead to rebuilding it, so the group raises its own version, costlier or weaker than the one refused, and takes it up with pride because it was made here.

## Lever

An ownership that pays in understanding and hides the gap.
Because building it in-house means the group knows every line and can maintain and change it, the reinvention delivers a real good, and that real good is exactly what covers the fact that the home-grown does less and cost more than the thing at the door.

## Echo

A worse version, loved for being ours.
The need met at a premium, the effort spent reinventing what was offered, and a home-grown thing whose ownership conceals how far short of the import it falls.
