# Persona: The Insider

## Taxonomy

[Insider](position_insider.md). The cohesive group member, trusting what the group made and suspecting what it did not.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Loyal, capable, invested in the team and what it builds.
Trusts the inside because it understands it, and eyes the outside because it cannot see within it.
Takes pride in a thing made here, by us.

## Action

Reads an idea's origin before its merit, and marks the outside one suspect.
Turns the import away for being theirs, and sets the group to build its own.
Keeps the making in-house, where it can be understood and owned.

## Shadow

Its trust in the inside is the cohesion that makes the team strong, and the same trust reflexively suspects the outside.
It cannot weigh the import on its merit, because the boundary answers first, and the boundary says theirs.
The loyalty that binds the group is the loyalty that turns the good idea away.

## Tell

Says "we'll build our own."
Of a thing already built, better, just outside the door.
