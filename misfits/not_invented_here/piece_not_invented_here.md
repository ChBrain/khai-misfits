# Piece: Not-Invented-Here

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The phenomenon whole: the good idea refused for its origin, and its remedy in an opened boundary at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the bridge](place_the_bridge.md), where the boundary is opened or the walls hold.
And across the whole group, in every door where origin is read before merit.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of refusals and rebuilds with no shape.
With it the moves become one law: the better way, the boundary, the reflex, the home-grown, the staling, and the real risk are facets of a single mechanism, in which the edge that binds a group by trusting the inside reflexively suspects the outside, so a good idea is refused for its origin, rebuilt worse in-house, and the group, letting less of the world in, slowly stales. Not-Invented-Here is load bearing because it is the whole finding with its whole remedy attached (Katz and Allen; Allen's gatekeeper): the only answer is to open the boundary and read the world in, an opening that imports the real risk the reflex feared and erodes the cohesion and ownership the boundary gave.

## Apparent

Either a proud self-reliant team or a stubborn insular one.
Seen from one side the group simply builds its own and owns its work; from the other, it is closed-minded and behind; and both miss the structure, that the cohesion is a real strength and the outside risk is real and the reflex is their shadow, so neither praise nor scolding answers it, only opening the boundary while keeping what made it worth having, which is the one thing an edge that trusts inward cannot cleanly do.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md) through [bridging](process_bridging.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: Not-Invented-Here is what [the boundary](piece_the_boundary.md) makes of [the better way](piece_the_better_way.md) once origin outranks merit, undone only by reading the world in across the bridge, at the cost of admitting [the real risk](piece_the_real_risk.md) and lowering the walls.
