# Process: Rejecting

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The outside idea devalued for its origin before its merit is weighed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A good idea at the door, and a boundary that reads origin first.
At [the threshold](place_the_threshold.md), the insider marking the import theirs and not ours, the guardian weighing its real risk.

## Direction

Toward an idea turned away for where it came from.
The boundary reads the origin before the worth, so the import is devalued for being external, the reflex fires, and the good idea is refused, sometimes on reflex and sometimes on a real danger, the two indistinguishable at the door.

## Lever

A boundary that must suspect outward to trust inward.
Because the same edge that makes the group cohere marks the outside as unseen and unverified, rejecting the external feels like prudence, so the reflex needs no malice, only the boundary doing the trusting and suspecting that hold the group together.

## Echo

A good idea refused at the door.
The import turned back, the merit unheard, and the need it would have met still open, waiting now for the group to build its own.
