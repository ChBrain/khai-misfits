# Process: Staling

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The group's knowledge dulling as it lets less of the world in, and its output falling behind.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A boundary held shut over time, and a world that keeps moving.
At [the lab](place_the_lab.md) and out in [the field](place_the_field.md), the group building from within while outside knowledge it never admits accumulates elsewhere.

## Direction

Toward a dulling edge and a product left behind.
Cut off from fresh outside knowledge, the group learns less and repeats itself more, so its home-grown work ages against a world that has moved on, and the ones it serves receive a thing slipping steadily behind what others already have.

## Lever

A boundary that stops importing what it needs to stay current.
Because a group learns fastest from outside its own walls, the long-shut boundary starves it of the very knowledge that would keep it sharp, so the staling is not neglect but the slow price of insularity, paid in an edge that dulls without anyone deciding to lose it.

## Echo

A group quietly falling behind.
Its knowledge going stale, its output aging, and the cost reaching the ones it serves, who feel the falling-behind and never see the shut door that caused it.
