# Plan: Guard the Boundary

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the guardian's seat](position_guardian.md), inside [Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md).

## Owner

## Direction

Weigh the import for the real risk it carries: the unknowns, the lock-in, the fit you cannot verify, the burden of a thing the group cannot maintain. Turn it away when the danger is genuine, and defend the cohesion and ownership that let the group run what it uses. Reject on reason, not reflex.

## Orders

([The Guardian](persona_the_guardian.md) rejects the import for its true dangers and is sometimes right, its real reasons the very cover the reflex hides behind.)

- [ ] the cohesion and ownership of the group are defended on true grounds
