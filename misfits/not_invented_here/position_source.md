# Position: Source

## Taxonomy

[Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md). The seat of the outside origin, offering the good idea across the boundary.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A solution that works, better or cheaper than what is in-house.
A merit that would win on its own, if merit were what was read.
An origin outside the group, plain and unchangeable.

## Orders

Offer the idea across the boundary.
Present its worth, and ask to be judged on it.
Wait to be weighed on the merit.

## Loses

The judgement, to the origin that is read first.
Its worth is real and the boundary reads only whose it is, so it is turned away for the one fact that has nothing to do with whether it works, and cannot make itself in-house, which is the price of admission.
The better the idea, the more its rejection costs the group that refused it.

## Drives

Toward being adopted on its merit,
against a boundary that answers by origin,
so the good idea is spurned for the one thing it can never change, being from outside.
