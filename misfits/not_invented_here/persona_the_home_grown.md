# Persona: The Home-Grown

## Taxonomy

[Reinvention](position_reinvention.md). The internal version built in the import's place, worse or costlier, and owned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ours, understood, made with care and by hand.
Stands in for the thing turned away, and is loved for having been built here.
Wears its origin as a virtue: we made this.

## Action

Rises to meet the need the import would have met, at greater cost or lesser worth.
Absorbs the effort that reinvents what was already offered.
Is maintained and improved and defended, because it belongs to the group.

## Shadow

It is genuinely owned and understood, and that ownership is exactly what justifies having spurned the better thing.
It is weaker or dearer than the import, and its being ours hides the gap.
The pride in what the group built is what keeps it from seeing what the group could have had.

## Tell

Says "yes, but it's ours, and we understand every line of it."
Of a thing that cost more to build and does less than the one refused.
