# Plan: Bridge the Boundary

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the gatekeeper's seat](position_gatekeeper.md), inside [Not-Invented-Here](play_not_invented_here.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Gatekeeper](position_gatekeeper.md)

## Direction

Read the outside world's work and carry it in. Translate the import into the group's own terms so its merit can beat its origin, reward a good idea adopted as much as one invented, and make the boundary a gate that opens rather than a wall that only refuses. Let the world in, in a language the group can trust.

## Orders

([The Gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md) reads the outside and translates it inward, and cannot open the boundary without importing the risk and thinning the cohesion it gave.)

## Implementation

([The Bridge](place_the_bridge.md); the [bridging](process_bridging.md) process; [not-invented-here](piece_not_invented_here.md) is answered by translating the outside across [the boundary](piece_the_boundary.md), at the cost of admitting [the real risk](piece_the_real_risk.md) and lowering the walls.)

## Targets

- [ ] the outside world's work is read and carried in
- [ ] the import is translated into the group's terms, so merit can beat origin
- [ ] adoption is rewarded as much as invention
- [ ] the gate opened admits the real risk and thins the cohesion together with the walls
