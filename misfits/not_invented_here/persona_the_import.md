# Persona: The Import

## Taxonomy

[Source](position_source.md). The good idea from outside, and the one who carries it to the boundary.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Useful, ready, better or cheaper than what is in-house.
Comes offering a solution the group needs, asking only to be judged on its merit.
Wears its origin plainly: made elsewhere, by someone else.

## Action

Brings the outside idea across to the boundary and offers it.
Presents its worth: it works, it costs less, it is already proven.
Waits to be weighed, and is weighed instead by whose it is.

## Shadow

Its merit is real and its origin is the only thing the boundary reads, so it is turned away for the one fact that has nothing to do with whether it works.
It cannot make itself in-house, and in-house is the price of admission.
The better it is, the more its rejection costs, and the more surely the group reinvents it.

## Tell

Says "this already works, and it is not yours."
And is refused on the second half, before the first is even heard.
