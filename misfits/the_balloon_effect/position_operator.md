# Position: Operator

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The seat that carries the trade to the least resistance, moving it off the squeezed patch to the next softest spot, an agent of a driver no enforcer touched.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A trade to run and a demand that will not quit: the standing to move the trade and a driver that keeps pulling whatever any patch does.
A clear case: the demand is still there and the money is still there, and where a patch is closed another is open.
No stake in any one patch, and no reason to stop while the pressure that feeds the trade stands.

## Orders

Move to the soft spot.
When a patch is squeezed, take the trade to the valley, the street, the route where the cost is now lowest, and run it there.
Follow the least resistance, because the reason for the trade has not left.

## Loses

Nothing it needed to keep, and that is the point: the seat loses only the squeezed patch, and keeps the trade.
Because the driver persists and only the local cost has risen, the seat is not stopped by the squeeze but redirected by it, so what it gives up is one patch and what it holds is the whole trade, moved, and stamping it out here only clears the way for the next to run the trade there.
It loses a closed patch and keeps a conserved trade, carrying the pressure to wherever the resistance is least.

## Drives

Toward the trade moved to the soft spot and the demand still answered,
bought against nothing but a single closed patch,
so the seat the squeeze would stop is merely the seat the squeeze redirects.
