# Persona: The Coordinator

## Taxonomy

[Coordinator](position_coordinator.md). Would drain the pressure that feeds the trade or hold the patches at once so there is no softer spot, at the cost of the visible clearance and against the border where each mandate stops.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Wide-eyed. Patient. Looking past the patch.
Is the one who sees the whole map, not the corner, and asks not where the trade is now but what keeps filling it.
No disdain for the local win in it: only the refusal to take a cleared patch for a cut trade.

## Action

Drains the pressure that feeds the trade, or holds every patch at once so the trade has nowhere softer to go.
Aims at the driver rather than the corner, and coordinates across the borders each enforcer's mandate stops at.
Does what a whole-map eye does: attacks the conserved cause, not the place it happens to show.

## Shadow

Not a theorist scorning real work, a clear-eyed strategist, and that is the whole difficulty: draining the driver is what actually cuts the trade, and it forgoes every win that can be seen and proved.
Because the clearance is local and countable where the reduction is diffuse and slow, the Coordinator who spends on the invisible cause and reaches past the border gives up the near, provable victory for one no single patch can show, and asks a hundred enforcers to hold their grounds at once for a result none of them can claim, so the one move that cuts the trade is the one with no clearance to point to and no patch to reward it.
It would let the air out of the balloon, and letting the air out shows nowhere as a quiet corner anyone can count.

## Tell

Says "a cleared patch is not a cut trade: until the driver is drained, we are only moving it."
Says it, and is heard as the one who belittles real raids and real results, so the move that would cut the trade is the least creditable in the room.
