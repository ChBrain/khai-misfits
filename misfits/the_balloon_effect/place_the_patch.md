# Place: The Patch

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The enforcer's own ground, the jurisdiction where the trade runs and is cleared, the valley or corner or route held and made quiet.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The ground an enforcer holds and answers for, a bounded piece of the map where the trade runs and can be moved against.
The enforcer raiding the crop, sweeping the corner, closing the route, and the patch going quiet under the squeeze.

## Holds

The squeezing, and the local win it makes.
Here the trade meets the response, so the Patch holds the point where the pressure is pressed: a bounded ground where the enforcer can raise the cost of the trade and clear it, so it is where the squeeze lands and the clearance is made, the corner made quiet always inside the border, never beyond it.

## Offers

A stage for the visible win.
Because the Patch is bounded ground the enforcer holds and can clear, it offers a real and measurable victory: a corner swept, numbers fallen, a result that can be attributed and counted, so it hands out the provable clearance the reward will find, and hands it out at the cost of the driver it cannot touch and the bulge it pushes past its own edge.

## Withheld

Any reach to the driver or the ground the trade moves to.
The Patch cannot both clear the trade on its ground and lower the demand that feeds it or hold the soft spot beyond its border, so it withholds the lever on the driver and the sight past the edge, letting the trade bulge away unseen, and, all local win and no whole map, it withholds any sign that the corner it made quiet is loud again three streets over on someone else's ground.
