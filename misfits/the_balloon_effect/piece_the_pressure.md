# Piece: The Pressure

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The driver that feeds the trade, the demand or price or want that suppression does not touch and that fills the balloon.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Fills the trade beneath [the patch](place_the_patch.md), untouched by the squeeze there.
Could be drained only from [the map](place_the_map.md), where the driver is aimed at rather than the corner.

## Load Bearing

Without it a cleared patch would stay clear, and the squeeze would be a cut and not a shove.
With it the whole trap has its engine: a driver that persists whatever any patch does, a demand still pulling and money still there, so a pressure pressed in one place does not vanish but moves. The Pressure is load bearing because it is the conserved quantity (Reppetto; Cornish and Clarke): the force that fills the balloon, untouched by the squeeze, so the piece carries the driver whose persistence is exactly why clearing a patch relocates the trade rather than ending it, the cause the corner only shows.

## Apparent

Nothing on the patch, an invisible thing behind a visible trade, easy to leave out of the count.
Seen at the surface, the Pressure does not appear: the eye goes to the crop, the corner, the route, and the driver behind them stays out of view, and the truth that the trade is only where the pressure shows, that draining the driver is the one move that cuts it, that a squeeze leaving the pressure whole can only move the trade, stays hidden behind how solid and clearable the trade on the ground plainly looks.

## Yearbook

Untouched by [squeezing](process_squeezing.md) and carried by [displacing](process_displacing.md), the driver that fills the trade.
It is the engine of the trap: the Pressure is what [the squeeze](piece_the_squeeze.md) presses without lowering and what [the bulge](piece_the_bulge.md) is filled by, the conserved force behind [the conservation](piece_the_conservation.md), the cause that [draining](process_draining.md) alone would cut, powering the whole [balloon effect](piece_the_balloon_effect.md).
