# Position: Coordinator

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The seat that would drain the driver or hold every patch at once so there is no softer spot, at the cost of the visible clearance and against the border where each mandate stops.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A whole-map view and a hard case: the sight of the trade rolling across the patches and the argument that only the driver, or every patch held at once, cuts it.
A clear case: a cleared patch is not a cut trade, and until the pressure is drained the trade is only being moved.
No clearance to point to, and no cover from the charge of belittling real raids and real results.

## Orders

Drain the pressure.
Attack the driver that feeds the trade, or hold every patch at once so it has nowhere softer to go, and reach across the borders each mandate stops at.
Aim at the conserved cause, not the corner it shows on, even when no patch can claim the result.

## Loses

The near, provable win and the reward that follows it, exactly by cutting the trade rather than moving it.
Because the clearance is local and countable where the reduction is diffuse and slow, the seat that spends on the invisible driver and reaches past the border forgoes every victory it could show, and asks a hundred enforcers to hold their grounds at once for a result none can claim, so it cannot cut the trade without giving up the one thing the system counts and rewards.
It buys a trade cut rather than moved against every clearance it could have shown, and is taken, for a while, as the one who scorned the raids that worked.

## Drives

Toward the driver drained and the trade cut at its source,
bought against every visible clearance and the reward that finds it,
so the one seat that would cut the trade must forgo the wins the whole system counts.
