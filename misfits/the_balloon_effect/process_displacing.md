# Process: Displacing

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The moving of the trade to the least resistance, the conserved pressure bulging off the squeezed patch into the next softest spot.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A squeezed patch and a driver that has not gone.
At [the soft spot](place_the_soft_spot.md), the operator taking the trade off the closed ground and running it where the cost is now lowest, the demand still pulling.

## Direction

Toward the trade swollen up again on softer ground.
Because the driver persists and only the local cost has risen, displacing carries the conserved pressure to whichever patch, route, or method is now the softest, so the trade does not stop but moves, bulging up undiminished on ground the squeeze has not reached, the pressure finding the slack lobe as surely as air does.

## Lever

The persistence of a demand no patch touched.
Because the reason for the trade still stands and where one patch is closed another is open, moving to the soft spot is the plain rational response of an agent of a conserved demand, so displacing runs on a driver left standing, the relocation assembled from no mastermind, only from a pressure that must go somewhere finding where the resistance is least.

## Echo

A trade as large as before, somewhere else.
The squeeze has moved the trade and not ended it, and it runs on softer ground undiminished, so what reads as an operator's cunning is, in truth, a conserved pressure doing what a pressure does, and sometimes worse, driven into harder hands where the softest spot is the most dangerous.
