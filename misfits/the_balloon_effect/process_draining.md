# Process: Draining

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The attacking of the driver or the holding of every patch at once, the move that cuts the trade rather than moves it, at the cost of the one who makes it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A whole-map view and a seat that would aim at the cause.
At [the map](place_the_map.md), the coordinator tracing the trade from lobe to lobe and moving against the driver that feeds it, or against every patch at once.

## Direction

Toward the trade cut at its source, if the invisible result can be borne.
Because the trade is fed by a driver no squeeze touches, draining aims at the pressure itself, lowering the demand or holding every patch so there is no softer spot, so it drives toward a trade cut rather than moved: but the driver is diffuse and slow where the clearance is local and countable, so the process cuts the trade only by forgoing every visible win and reaching past the border where mandate and reward both stop.

## Lever

The truth that only a drained driver cuts a conserved trade.
Because the pressure is what fills the balloon and the corner is only where it shows, aiming at the driver is the one move that reduces rather than relocates, so draining runs on a sound and whole-map reading: yet the same move that cuts the trade shows no quiet corner to count, so the process costs its owner exactly by aiming where no clearance can be claimed.

## Echo

A trade cut and nothing to show for it, or a driver left standing and the balloon bulging on.
Either the pressure is drained and the trade falls across the whole, bought against every clearance and the reward that finds it, or the visible corner is cleared once more and the invisible driver left untouched, and the trade rolls on around the map, conserved.
