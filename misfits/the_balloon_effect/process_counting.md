# Process: Counting

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The tallying of the local clearances, the overseer crediting each cleared patch and seeing no further than its border, the measure that hides the bulge.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A board of patches and a lever of reward.
At [the map](place_the_map.md), the overseer recording each cleared corner and each fallen number, holding every enforcer to the ground they hold.

## Direction

Toward the squeeze rewarded and the displacement unseen.
Because the clearance falls inside the patch and the bulge falls outside it, counting credits the ground each officer holds and never the trade it moved, so it rewards every real local win and, by measuring each patch on its own, keeps the conserved total off the board, the fair attribution of results and the blindness to displacement one and the same.

## Lever

The fairness of measuring people on what they answer for.
Because each enforcer is responsible for their patch and can be credited only for it, holding them to their ground is the sound and just way to measure, so counting runs on fair accounting, the hidden conservation assembled from no one gaming the figures, only from crediting each patch the win it truly made.

## Echo

A hundred wins on the board and no total of the trade.
Every clearance has been counted and rewarded and every one is real, so what reads as a war being won patch by patch is, in truth, a moved trade read as a hundred victories, the sum never taken because no patch is answerable for it.
