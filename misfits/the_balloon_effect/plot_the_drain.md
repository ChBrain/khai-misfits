# Plot: The Drain

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Map](place_the_map.md), someone who would let the air out rather than move it.
The trade traced from lobe to lobe, the driver there to be aimed at, the clearances there to be forgone.

## Action

[Draining](process_draining.md): [The Coordinator](persona_the_coordinator.md), holding [the coordinator's seat](position_coordinator.md), would drain the driver or hold every patch at once, against [The Overseer](persona_the_overseer.md) on [Count the Clearance](plan_count_the_clearance.md).

## Stage

[The Coordinator](persona_the_coordinator.md), on [Drain the Pressure](plan_drain_the_pressure.md), aims at the driver that feeds the trade and reaches across the borders each mandate stops at, so the trade has nowhere softer to go.
[The Overseer](persona_the_overseer.md), on [Count the Clearance](plan_count_the_clearance.md), holds to the board of countable wins, and the enforcers to the near, provable clearances the reward finds.

## Tension

Draining the driver is the one move that cuts the trade, and it forgoes every win that can be seen and proved.
There is nothing to fault: a cleared patch is not a cut trade, and the coordinator only says so. But the clearance is local and countable where the reduction is diffuse and slow, so the plot closes one of two ways: the driver is drained and the trade cut across the whole, bought against every clearance and the reward that finds it ... or the visible corner is cleared once more, the win counted once more, and the balloon bulges on.
