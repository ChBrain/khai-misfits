# Plot: The Squeeze

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Patch](place_the_patch.md), a banned trade running on the ground and an enforcer to move against it.
The trade real and visible, the ground the officer's to clear, the driver that feeds it out of reach.

## Action

[Squeezing](process_squeezing.md): [The Enforcer](persona_the_enforcer.md), holding [the enforcer's seat](position_enforcer.md), raids the crop, sweeps the corner, closes the route, and clears the trade off the patch.

## Stage

[The Enforcer](persona_the_enforcer.md), on [Clear the Patch](plan_clear_the_patch.md), moves against the trade on their ground and raises its cost until the patch goes quiet, a real and measurable win.
The trade cannot run where the cost has risen, and the corner falls silent, the driver that fed it standing untouched behind the clearance.

## Tension

Clearing a real harm off your own ground is right, and the clearing raises the cost only on the patch, never on the driver.
There is nothing to fault: the trade is a genuine harm and the raid genuinely works. But the pressure that fed it is conserved and only pressed, so the plot closes with the patch cleared and the trade pressed toward the softest spot beyond the border, the real win and the coming bulge the two faces of one act.
