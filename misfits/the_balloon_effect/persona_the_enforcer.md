# Persona: The Enforcer

## Taxonomy

[Enforcer](position_enforcer.md). Moves against the trade on their patch and clears it, genuinely and measurably, the real local win the credit rightly finds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Determined. Effective on the ground.
Is the one who moves against the banned trade where it runs, raids the crop, sweeps the corner, closes the route, and makes the patch go quiet.
No showboating in it: only a competent hand doing hard, real, measurable work against a trade on the ground they hold.

## Action

Clears the trade off the patch, dramatically and well, and makes the numbers fall.
Wins the local victory when the patch needs winning, and earns the credit the clearance deserves.
Does what a good enforcer does: moves against the trade in front of them and clears their ground.

## Shadow

Not a fraud running up figures, a genuinely effective officer, and that is the whole difficulty: clearing the patch is right, and clearing it is what shoves the trade next door.
Because the driver is untouched and the pressure conserved, the very success of the squeeze is what displaces the trade to the softest spot beyond the border, so the Enforcer is really winning and the win is really moving the trade, and the competence that clears the patch is the competence that keeps the balloon full and rolling.
It clears its ground and clears it well, and clearing it well is what bulges the trade onto the next.

## Tell

Says "I cleared my patch: the corner is quiet and the numbers are down."
Cleared it, really, and never sees the corner three streets over that got loud, because that ground is not theirs to count.
