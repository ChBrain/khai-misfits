# Pitch: Clear the Patch

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Decisive, effective, accountable.
The plain worth of an enforcer who moves against the banned trade on their patch and clears it, raids the crop, sweeps the corner, closes the route, and makes the numbers fall.
Nothing false in it: the clearance is real and hard and measurable, and clearing your own patch is genuinely good for the patch.

## Undertow

Under the clearance, the pressure that fed the trade left untouched, so the squeeze here bulges the trade into the next softest spot.
Because the driver persists and a conserved pressure pressed in one place moves to the least resistance, the cleared patch pushes the trade three streets over, across the border, onto the lighter-patrolled route, undiminished, and the whole trade rolls around the map roughly conserved, sometimes shoved into harder hands.
The clearance meant to reduce the trade relocates it.

## Nerve

The feeling of sweeping the corner clean and watching the numbers fall, sure you have cut the trade,
because the patch is quiet and the win is real and yours to prove,
while the corner three streets over gets louder on someone else's ground, and no one counts the bulge against the clearance.

## Echo

After the run: a trade rolled around the map undiminished, a hundred quiet patches and no reduction at all,
no one idle and no one corrupt, only real raids that worked and enforcers rewarded on the ground they cleared,
and a driver that would have cut the trade if drained, left untouched because draining it means spending on the invisible cause and reaching past the only patch anyone is answerable for.
