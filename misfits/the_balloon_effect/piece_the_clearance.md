# Piece: The Clearance

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The measurable local win the enforcer is credited for, the quiet patch and the fallen numbers the reward finds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Counted at [the map](place_the_map.md), where the cleared patches are tallied.
Made on [the patch](place_the_patch.md), where the corner goes quiet and the numbers fall.

## Load Bearing

Without it the squeeze would earn nothing, and there would be no reward pulling effort onto the patch.
With it the trap has its lure: a real, provable, attributable win, a corner swept and numbers down, that the overseer can count and the enforcer can be rewarded for. The Clearance is load bearing because it is the measurable result the system rewards (Friesendorf): the local win that can be owned, so the piece carries the countable victory that makes squeezing rational and draining thankless, the visible pole of the measure against which the reduction is unshowable.

## Apparent

Progress, the plain proof that the trade is being beaten, patch by cleared patch.
Seen at the surface, the Clearance looks like the war being won, a result you can point to and count, and the truth that it measures the patch cleared and never the trade moved, that a hundred of them can sum to no reduction, that its very countability is what hides the bulge no patch will own, stays hidden behind how real and how provable a swept corner plainly is.
