# Persona: The Operator

## Taxonomy

[Operator](position_operator.md). The agent of the trade who, squeezed off one patch, moves it to the next softest spot, carrying the pressure to the least resistance.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Adaptive. Undeterred. Answering a demand that has not gone.
Is the one who runs the trade where it can run, and when a patch is closed, finds the valley, the street, the route where it can run instead.
No mastermind in it: only an agent of a persistent demand moving to wherever the cost is now lowest.

## Action

Moves the trade off the squeezed patch and onto the softest spot, relocating the crop, the corner, the route.
Follows the least resistance, because the pressure that feeds the trade is still there and still pulling, and only the local cost has risen.
Does what an agent of a conserved demand does: goes where the trade can still be run.

## Shadow

Not the author of the trap, an agent of a driver no one on the patches touched, and that is the difficulty: the Operator does not create the trade, the untouched pressure does, and the Operator only carries it to the soft spot.
Because the demand persists whatever any enforcer does, squeezing one patch does not stop the Operator, it only redirects them, so the relocation reads as cunning when it is merely a conserved pressure finding its lowest resistance, and stamping out the Operator here simply clears the way for the next to run the trade there.
It moves the trade to the soft spot, and moving it is what a demand left standing makes rational to do.

## Tell

Says "the trade did not stop: it moved, because the reason for it never left."
Moves it, and by moving it shows that the squeeze pressed the balloon without letting any air out.
