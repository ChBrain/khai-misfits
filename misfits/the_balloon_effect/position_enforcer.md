# Position: Enforcer

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The seat that moves against the trade on its patch and clears it, where the local win is real and the displacement is someone else's ground.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A patch to hold and the power to clear it: the standing to move against the trade where it runs and the means to make it quiet.
A clear case: the trade on this ground is a real harm, and clearing it is a real and measurable good for the patch.
No reach past the border and no lever on the driver, only on the trade where it shows.

## Orders

Clear the patch.
Move against the trade on your ground, raid the crop, sweep the corner, close the route, and make the numbers fall.
Win the local victory the patch needs, and earn the credit the clearance deserves.

## Loses

Any cut to the trade itself, exactly by clearing it off the patch.
Because the driver is untouched and the pressure conserved, the seat's success raises the local cost and moves the trade to the softest spot beyond its border, so it cannot clear its ground without displacing the trade rather than reducing it, and the better it clears the patch the more surely the balloon bulges next door.
It gains a quiet corner and a countable win, and loses any reduction of the trade that does not simply relocate it.

## Drives

Toward the patch cleared and the trade moved on,
bought against the driver it cannot touch,
so the seat that would cut the trade shoves it onto the next patch instead.
