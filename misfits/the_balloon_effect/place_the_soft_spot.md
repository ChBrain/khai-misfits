# Place: The Soft Spot

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The next lowest-resistance ground the trade bulges into, the valley across the border, the street three over, the route with the lighter patrols.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The ground where the resistance is now least, a patch not yet squeezed, a border loosely watched, a method not yet closed.
The operator moving the trade in, the crop replanted across the line, the corner reopened three streets over, the trade swelling up again undiminished.

## Holds

The displacing, and the bulge it takes.
Here the conserved pressure meets the least resistance, so the Soft Spot holds the point where the trade lands: the lowest-cost ground the pressure finds when a patch is squeezed, so it is where the balloon bulges, the trade always going to whatever spot is now softest, never staying where the cost has risen.

## Offers

A place for the trade to run again.
Because the Soft Spot is ground not yet squeezed, it offers the trade a new home the moment its old one is cleared, so it hands the displaced trade its next patch, the room to swell up undiminished, and hands it out because the driver still pulls and only the last patch's cost has risen, so the pressure finds it as surely as air finds the slack lobe.

## Withheld

Any respite from the trade the squeeze moved on.
The Soft Spot cannot both be the untouched ground it was and hold the trade that has now bulged into it, so it withholds the quiet it had before the squeeze next door, taking the displaced trade as its own new problem, and, all fresh ground and no memory of the clearance that sent the trade here, it withholds any sign that its new trouble is another patch's counted win.
