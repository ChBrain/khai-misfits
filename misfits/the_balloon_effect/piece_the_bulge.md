# Piece: The Bulge

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The trade swollen up on the soft spot, the displacement made visible, the lobe that fills when another is squeezed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Swells on [the soft spot](place_the_soft_spot.md), the ground the trade bulges into.
Traceable only from [the map](place_the_map.md), where the move from patch to patch can be seen.

## Load Bearing

Without it the squeeze would simply reduce the trade, and the clearance would be a cut.
With it the trap shows its shape: the trade rising up again on softer ground, as large as before, the pressure conserved and relocated, so the win on one patch is undone on another. The Bulge is load bearing because it is the displacement itself (Reppetto; Guerette and Bowers): the trade swelling where the resistance is least, so the piece carries the relocation that makes a clearance a shove, the loud corner three streets over that is the quiet one's true price, and, where the softest spot is the most dangerous, the trade pushed into harder hands.

## Apparent

Someone else's problem, a fresh outbreak on new ground, unconnected to the patch just cleared.
Seen at the surface, the Bulge looks like a separate trouble, a trade that has sprung up where it happens to spring up, and the truth that it is the cleared patch's trade merely moved, that the new outbreak and the old clearance are one pressure conserved, that the loud corner is the quiet corner's cost, stays hidden behind how much the bulge looks like a new problem on unrelated ground.
