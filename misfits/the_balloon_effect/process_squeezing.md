# Process: Squeezing

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The pressing of the trade on one patch, the enforcer raising the local cost and clearing the ground, the genuine win that moves the trade.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A trade running on a patch and an enforcer with the ground to clear it.
At [the patch](place_the_patch.md), the officer moving against the crop, the corner, the route, raising the cost of the trade where it shows.

## Direction

Toward a patch made quiet and a trade pressed elsewhere.
Because the enforcer can raise the cost of the trade on their ground but cannot lower the driver that feeds it, squeezing clears the patch and conserves the pressure, so it makes a real and measurable local win and, in the same move, presses the trade toward the softest spot beyond the border, the cleared corner and the displaced trade the two faces of one act.

## Lever

The rightness of clearing a real harm off your own ground.
Because the trade on the patch is a genuine harm and clearing it is a real and creditable good, each squeeze is a sound and rewarded move, so squeezing runs on competent, well-meant enforcement, the displacement assembled from no one moving the trade on purpose, only from clearing the ground each is answerable for.

## Echo

A quiet patch and a trade pressed toward the next.
The corner has been cleared and its numbers have fallen and the win is real, so what reads as the trade reduced is, in truth, the pressure conserved and pushed past the border, the balloon squeezed here and bulging there.
