# Persona: The Overseer

## Taxonomy

[Overseer](position_overseer.md). Measures each enforcer on their own patch and rewards the local clearance, the accountability that makes the squeeze rational and hides the bulge past the border.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fair. Accountable. Rewarding results.
Is the one who holds each enforcer to their ground and credits what they achieve on it, counting the cleared patch and the fallen numbers.
No laxity in it: only the sound discipline of measuring people on the work they are responsible for.

## Action

Counts the clearance on each patch and rewards the enforcer who made it, recording the quiet corner as the win it is.
Holds each to the ground they hold, because that is the ground they can be answerable for.
Does what a fair overseer does: measures results where they can be attributed, on the patch, to the officer.

## Shadow

Not a blinkered bureaucrat, a fair accountant of real results, and that is the whole difficulty: measuring each enforcer on their own patch is right, and it is exactly what hides the displacement.
Because the clearance falls inside the patch and the bulge falls outside it, the Overseer who counts the ground each officer holds credits the squeeze and never the trade it moved, so the measure that fairly rewards local success is the measure that cannot see the trade rolling around the map, and the fairness of attributing results to patches is what makes a conserved trade read as a hundred wins.
It counts what each can be answerable for, and what each can be answerable for is precisely not the bulge.

## Tell

Says "every patch on my board is down: look how much we have cleared."
Counts every clearance truly, and never totals the trade across the whole, because no patch is answerable for that.
