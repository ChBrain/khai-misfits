# Place: The Map

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The whole picture across the patches, the overview where the clearances are counted and the conservation and the driver could be seen, or are not.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The room above the patches where the whole trade could be seen at once, a board of every ground and every clearance, the overview held over the bounded corners below.
The overseer counting each cleared patch, the coordinator tracing the trade from lobe to lobe, the total there to be summed or left unsummed.

## Holds

The counting, and the coordinating that contests it.
Here the patches meet the overview, so the Map holds the point where the whole could be read: every clearance recorded and every bulge traceable, so it is where the trade might be totalled across the grounds or credited patch by patch, the sum of the whole always harder to own than the win on the corner.

## Offers

A board for the clearances, and a view of the whole trade for any eye that would take it.
Because the Map holds every patch at once, it offers the overseer a tally of local wins and offers the coordinator the sight of the trade rolling around the grounds, so it hands out both the patch-by-patch count and the whole-map view, and hands them out knowing the reward follows the counted clearance and not the summed trade.

## Withheld

The mandate to act on what the whole map shows.
The Map can show the trade conserved across the patches, but it cannot hand any patch the reach to drain the driver or the standing to hold every ground at once, so it withholds the power to act on the total it reveals, letting the whole be seen and not cut, and, all overview and no lever, it withholds any reward for the reduction it alone could show and no patch can claim.
