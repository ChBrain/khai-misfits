# Position: Overseer

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The seat that measures each enforcer on their own patch and rewards the local clearance, where the fair measure makes the squeeze rational and hides the bulge.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A board of patches and a lever of reward: the standing to hold each enforcer to their ground and to credit what they clear on it.
A clear case: people should be measured on the work they are responsible for, and each enforcer is responsible for their patch.
No measure that crosses the border, and no way to attribute to any patch the trade that moved off it.

## Orders

Count the clearance.
Hold each enforcer to their ground, record the cleared patch and the fallen numbers, and reward the officer who made them fall.
Measure results where they can be attributed, on the patch, to the one who holds it.

## Loses

Any sight of the trade across the whole, exactly by measuring each patch on its own.
Because the clearance falls inside the patch and the bulge falls outside it, the seat that fairly credits the ground each officer holds counts the squeeze and never the trade it displaced, so the fairness of attributing results to patches is what blinds it to the conserved total, and the measure that rewards real local success is the measure that reads a moved trade as a hundred wins.
It gains a fair account of each patch, and loses any total of the trade the patches sum to.

## Drives

Toward the clearance counted and the enforcer rewarded,
bought against the whole map it cannot total,
so the seat that would measure the win fairly is the seat that cannot see the bulge.
