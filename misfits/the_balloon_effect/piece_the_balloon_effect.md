# Piece: The Balloon Effect

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The phenomenon whole, a conserved trade pressed from patch to patch, each squeeze a real win and the sum a trade merely moved.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across all three: squeezed on [the patch](place_the_patch.md), bulging into [the soft spot](place_the_soft_spot.md), totalled only from [the map](place_the_map.md).
It is the whole circuit, the trade shoved lobe to lobe with the air never let out.

## Load Bearing

Without it the pieces are separate: a driver, a squeeze, a bulge, a count, a steady total, with nothing to bind them.
With it they are one mechanism: the pressure persists, the squeeze presses it, the trade bulges to the soft spot, the clearance is counted, and the total conserves, each part driving the next. The Balloon Effect is load bearing because it is the structure entire (Reppetto; Friesendorf; Windle and Farrell): the conserved trade relocated by local suppression, so the piece carries the trap whole, the reason a hundred genuine victories sum to no reduction, and no enforcer need fail for the trade to roll on undiminished.

## Apparent

A war being won, patch by cleared patch, a trade beaten wherever it is found.
Seen at the surface, the whole trap looks like steady progress, real raids and real clearances adding to victory, and the truth that the trade is only moved and not cut, that the driver was never drained, that the sum of the wins is a trade conserved and sometimes worsened, stays hidden behind how exactly like winning each real, countable clearance in turn appears.
