# The Balloon Effect: Reference

The research warrant for [The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md).
The dynamic is the displacement account of enforcement: Reppetto's crime displacement, Cornish and Clarke's
rational-choice mechanism, Friesendorf's drug-war displacement, and Windle and Farrell's critical assessment, all
cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging the balloon effect as a **displacement structure**: a banned trade fed by a driver that suppression does
not touch, in which pressing on the trade in one jurisdiction raises the local cost and clears the patch, a real
and measurable win, while the conserved pressure relocates the trade to the next softest spot, and each enforcer,
measured on their own patch, is credited for the clearance and never charged with the displacement, so a hundred
genuine local victories sum across the whole to a trade merely moved, and sometimes to one pushed into harder
hands. The play models the documented mechanics, the persistent driver that suppression leaves untouched, the
local suppression that genuinely clears the patch, the relocation of the trade to the lowest resistance, spatial,
tactical, or functional, the patch-bounded measure that rewards the clearance and hides the bulge, the roughly
conserved total across the whole, and the driver-directed or cross-jurisdiction response that alone cuts the
trade rather than moving it, at a cost, as the load-bearing vectors of the structure, not as a tale of lazy police
or a criminal mastermind. The governing finding of the source is the play's governing law: where a driver of an
illicit activity persists, localised suppression tends to displace the activity, in space, time, target, tactic,
or method, rather than to reduce it, so that clearing a patch relocates the trade and lasting reduction requires
addressing the driver or removing the softer spots.

The house is careful with the strength of the claim: the research also shows displacement is often incomplete, and
that suppression can produce a diffusion of benefits rather than pure relocation (Guerette and Bowers), and the
"containment hypothesis" that a trade can be held or reduced by sustained pressure is genuinely contested (Windle
and Farrell). The misfit stages the displacement case, where the driver is untouched and the softest spot is
always open, and credits, in the origin and restrictions, that displacement is a tendency and not a law.

It is distinct from the house's The Cobra Effect, where a reward set to reduce a problem is farmed and so breeds
the very thing it pays to remove: there the engine is a perverse incentive that creates new supply, here it is
displacement of an existing trade, the same pressure relocated rather than more of it called into being. It is
distinct from the house's goal-displacement misfits, Metric Fixation and The Paper Trail, where effort shifts
from the goal to a proxy or a defence: there the substitution is of one activity for another within an actor, here
the trade itself is physically moved from patch to patch while the actors keep doing exactly the thing they were
doing. And it is distinct from Shifting the Burden, where reliance on a symptomatic fix atrophies the fundamental
solution: here nothing atrophies, the local fix genuinely works on its patch, and the trap is that its success is
conserved and relocated, not that a capacity withers.

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast the enforcer as
  lazy or the operator as a criminal mastermind, because the source finding is precisely that each move is sound:
  the trade is a real harm, the raid genuinely works, clearing your own patch is good for the patch, measuring
  people on the ground they answer for is fair, and moving to the soft spot is the plain response of a demand no
  patch touched. The displacement is authored by no one and emerges from the structure, a conserved pressure and
  a patch-bounded reward, so the misfit is that competent enforcement, fairly measured, relocates a trade it
  cannot reduce, and reads a moved trade as a hundred wins.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into a costless correction. The one move that cuts the
  trade is to drain the driver that feeds it or hold every patch at once so there is no softer spot, and it means
  spending on the invisible cause instead of the visible corner, forgoing the measurable clearance for a
  reduction no single patch can show, and reaching past the border where each enforcer's mandate and reward both
  stop. The reckoning is a trade cut with nothing to point to, bought against every countable win, not a free
  correction, so the cure for a trade merely moved is the victory no patch is credited for.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here, never claimed
  as the house's, and its strength is not overstated. The staging is the house's; crime displacement is
  Reppetto's, the rational-choice mechanism and the forms of displacement are Cornish and Clarke's, the drug-war
  balloon is Friesendorf's, and the honest limits, that displacement is often partial, that benefits can diffuse,
  and that containment is contested, are Windle and Farrell's and Guerette and Bowers's, carried as carefully as
  the finding itself: the misfit stages the displacement case and credits it as a tendency, not a certainty.
