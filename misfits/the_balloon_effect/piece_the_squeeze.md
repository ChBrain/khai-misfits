# Piece: The Squeeze

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md). The local suppression, the genuine cost the enforcer raises on the patch, the press that clears the ground and moves the trade.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Lands on [the patch](place_the_patch.md), where the enforcer raises the cost of the trade.
Its effect leaves for [the soft spot](place_the_soft_spot.md), where the pressed trade bulges up.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no intervention and no trap, only a trade running where it will.
With it the trap has its act: a real press on the trade in one place, a genuine rise in the local cost that clears the patch, so the win it makes and the trade it moves are both real. The Squeeze is load bearing because it is the intervention that displaces (Cornish and Clarke): the local press that raises the cost on the patch without lowering the driver, so the piece carries the act whose very effectiveness relocates the trade, the real clearance that is, in the same motion, the shove past the border.

## Apparent

The cut itself, the plain and forceful reduction of the trade, the raid that worked.
Seen at the surface, the Squeeze looks like the trade defeated, a cost raised so high the trade cannot run, and the truth that it raises the cost only on the patch and not on the driver, that a conserved pressure pressed in one place moves rather than falls, that the forceful clearance is exactly the shove that fills the next lobe, stays hidden behind how plainly and how hard the trade on this ground has been pressed.
