# Plot: The Bulge

## Taxonomy

[The Balloon Effect](play_the_balloon_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Soft Spot](place_the_soft_spot.md), a patch not yet squeezed and a trade pressed off its old ground.
The driver still pulling, the cost fallen everywhere but the last patch, the least resistance there to be found.

## Action

[Displacing](process_displacing.md): [The Operator](persona_the_operator.md), holding [the operator's seat](position_operator.md), takes the trade to the next softest spot and runs it there.

## Stage

[The Operator](persona_the_operator.md), on [Move to the Soft Spot](plan_move_to_the_soft_spot.md), follows the least resistance to the valley across the border, the street three over, the route with the lighter patrols, and the trade swells up again.
The demand is still there and the money is still there, so the trade runs on softer ground undiminished, the closed patch the only thing the squeeze took.

## Tension

Moving to the soft spot is the plain response of a demand no patch touched, and the moving conserves the trade.
There is nothing masterful in it: the driver still pulls and where one patch is closed another is open. But the pressure simply finds its lowest resistance, so the plot closes with the trade as large as before, somewhere the squeeze has not reached, and sometimes in harder hands where the softest spot is the most dangerous.
