# Persona: The Pressed

## Taxonomy

[Problem Owner](position_problem_owner.md). The hand under the symptom, needing it gone now.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Responsible, harried, doing the reasonable thing.
Has a live problem and a clock, and no room to wait for a slow cure.
Wants the pain to stop so the work can go on.

## Action

Reaches for the relief that works today.
Takes the fast answer gratefully and gets back to it.
Means to start the real fix soon, once this one fire is out.

## Shadow

Its soon never comes, because the next symptom always does.
Each reasonable reach for relief is a cycle the cure goes unbuilt, and the reaching feels like coping, not like a trap closing.
Would swear it is handling things, and it is, one symptom at a time, forever.

## Tell

"Let us just get through today."
A sentence that is true every day, and is how the cause is never touched.
