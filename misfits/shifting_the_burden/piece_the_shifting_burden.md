# Piece: The Shifting Burden

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The phenomenon whole: the load moving from curing the cause to sustaining the relief, held whole by nobody.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across the whole run, from [the alarm](place_the_alarm.md) to [the weaning](place_the_weaning.md).
And wherever a fast relief is available for a slow problem, which is nearly everywhere.

## Load Bearing

Without it the parts are a scatter: a symptom, two fixes, a delay, an atrophy, a dependence.
With it they are one law: relieve a symptom by a means that wastes the capacity to cure it, and the burden shifts from solving the problem to feeding the relief, so the fix becomes permanent and the cause becomes unreachable. The Shifting Burden is load bearing because it is the archetype read at once (Senge; Meadows), and it names the only exit: not a better relief but a weaning, funding the wasted capacity and enduring the symptom while it rebuilds, protection given up so prevention can return.

## Apparent

A system coping, sensibly, indefinitely.
It looks like ordinary competent management: problems handled as they arise, reliefs kept in stock, operations steady. Nothing on the surface is a failure, and that is the whole of it, because the burden shifts not through any bad decision but through a run of good ones.

## Yearbook

Held whole by nobody: assembled from [the quick fix](piece_the_quick_fix.md), [the atrophy](piece_the_atrophy.md), and [the dependence](piece_the_dependence.md), and answered only, and painfully, by [the weaner](persona_the_weaner.md).
It is the misfit named: each easy relief is a loan against the power to cure, and the loan comes due as a problem that can no longer be solved, only fed.
