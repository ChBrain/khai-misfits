# Position: Dependent

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The seat that has lost the power to do without the fix.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A total reliance on the relief, budgeted and normalised.
A wasted capacity it no longer remembers having.
A structure built around the fix, so that removing it would bring the structure down.

## Orders

Keep the relief flowing, because nothing else now works.
Treat the fix as permanent infrastructure and plan around it.
Do not question a dependence the whole system rests on.

## Loses

The very possibility of self-correction.
The reliance has gone so deep that the cure is not merely deferred but unreachable, defended by everything now built on the fix.
Weakened past the point where choosing otherwise is even an option.

## Drives

Toward more of the fix, forever,
not because it is chosen
but because it is the only thing the wasted system can still do.
