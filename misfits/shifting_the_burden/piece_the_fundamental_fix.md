# Piece: The Fundamental Fix

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The fundamental solution and the capacity to apply it: the slow cure that addresses the cause.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), slow, quiet, made over years and kept in use.
The cure that lives in a capacity, not on a shelf.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit is only a shortcut, and a shortcut is not a trap.
With it there is a real cure that the quick fix is competing against and, worse, corroding: the fundamental fix is not a thing you buy once but a capacity you must keep exercised, so it can be lost. The Fundamental Fix is load bearing because it is what the archetype is truly about (Senge; Repenning & Sterman), the fragile, slow-built power to end the problem whose neglect is the whole cost, and whose existence is why the burden has somewhere worse to shift from.

## Apparent

An expense that can always wait.
It looks like the responsible-but-not-urgent line, the investment for a calmer day, the thing to fund once the fires are out. It is always sound and never pressing, and so it is always the item deferred.

## Yearbook

Tended by [the builder](persona_the_builder.md), and wasted by [the atrophy](piece_the_atrophy.md).
It is the cure at stake: the Fundamental Fix is what [the delay](piece_the_delay.md) makes lose every race, and what disuse quietly takes off the table one cycle at a time.
