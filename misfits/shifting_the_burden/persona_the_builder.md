# Persona: The Builder

## Taxonomy

[Capability Owner](position_capability_owner.md). The one who could cure the cause, if the cure were ever funded.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Patient, deep, a little sidelined.
Holds the slow skill that would end the problem for good, and knows it takes time to build and keep.
Watches the fires get fought while the fire-proofing goes unbought.

## Action

Works the fundamental fix, underfunded and unhurried, when allowed.
Asks for the time and the investment the cause actually needs.
Loses the argument to the alarm, again, because the alarm is louder and now.

## Shadow

Its capacity wastes precisely because the relief keeps working.
Every symptom the quick fix handles is a reason the slow cure can wait, so the muscle it tends goes unused, and unused it withers, until even the Builder is not sure it could deliver the cure it once could.
Starved not by malice but by the relief always being more urgent.

## Tell

"If we fixed the cause, this would stop happening."
True, and never quite as pressing as the thing happening right now.
