# Place: The Weaning

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). Where the fix is rationed and the capacity rebuilt, through the withdrawal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A schedule for less of the fix, a budget moved to the workshop, a flare met without the usual relief.
The hard middle where the cure is not yet strong and the fix is being taken away.

## Holds

The withdrawal, and the wager underneath it.
Here the symptom returns unrelieved while the capacity slowly comes back, and the whole bet is that the muscle rebuilds before the pain forces a relapse.

## Offers

The one path back to the cure.
Only by rationing the fix and enduring the flare does the fundamental capacity get used again, and only by being used does it recover.

## Withheld

Comfort, and proof, for a long time.
The weaning shows no result while it hurts most, so it looks like negligence right up until it works, and one unbearable flare can send the whole thing back to the dispensary.
