# Place: The Alarm

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). Where the symptom flares and demands an answer now.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A crisis, a red light, a phone that will not stop.
The symptom loud and present, and everyone turned toward it.

## Holds

The urgency that decides the answer.
Here the fast fix is the only feasible one, because the slow cure cannot act inside the time the alarm allows.

## Offers

A clear, answerable problem.
Something that can be ended today, visibly, so the day can be called a win.

## Withheld

Any room for the cure.
The alarm rewards whatever stops it soonest, and the fundamental fix, being slow, is never that, so the place that most needs the cure is the place that can least choose it.
