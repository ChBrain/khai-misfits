# Process: Relieving

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The quick fix applied, the symptom eased, the cause untouched.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A symptom flaring and a fast answer within reach.
The alarm sounding, and a hand that knows how to make it stop.

## Direction

The pain going down and the crisis ending, now, on the deadline.
Nothing changed underneath, only the surface calmed.

## Lever

Speed against a clock the cure cannot beat.
The relief acts inside the time available, and the fundamental fix does not, so relief wins by simply being able to arrive.

## Echo

A handled symptom and an untouched cause.
The problem calmed for now, primed to return, and one more cycle in which the cure was not built.
