# Piece: The Symptom

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The visible pain that demands an answer now, and does not care which one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the alarm](place_the_alarm.md), loud and present and turned toward.
The flare that has to be met today, whatever else waits.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no pressure, and no reason to reach for anything at all.
With it there is a clock, and the clock is what tilts every choice toward the fast answer: the symptom is what has to be dealt with now, so it is the symptom, not the cause, that sets the terms. The Symptom is load bearing because it is the demand the whole structure answers, the recurring pressure that makes the quick fix feel not just easier but necessary, and that keeps coming back precisely because it is the thing relieved rather than the thing cured.

## Apparent

The problem itself.
It looks like the whole of what is wrong, the thing to be solved, so solving it, ending the flare, feels like solving the problem. That mistake, symptom for cause, is the door the misfit walks in through.

## Yearbook

Felt by [the pressed](persona_the_pressed.md), answered at [the alarm](place_the_alarm.md).
It is the recurring demand: the Symptom is what [the quick fix](piece_the_quick_fix.md) is reached for to relieve, and its return, cycle after cycle, is the sign the cause was never touched.
