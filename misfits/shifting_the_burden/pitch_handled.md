# Pitch: Handled

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Competent, relieved, on top of it.
The crisis met and put down, the symptom gone by end of day, the team free to move on.
The voice of a group that handles things, spoken by people who really did handle the thing in front of them.

## Undertow

Under the relief, a muscle going soft from never being used.
Each fast answer is one more cycle the slow capacity sits idle, and idle capacity wastes, so the power to end the problem quietly leaves while the problem stays.
Nobody neglects the cure on purpose: it is only ever less urgent than the symptom, every single time.

## Nerve

The feeling of coping, and coping well,
each quick fix a small competent win,
the real cure always sensible to start tomorrow, when this fire is out and the next has not yet caught.

## Echo

After the run: a problem no longer solvable, only manageable,
a capacity too wasted to trust,
and a fix that started as a choice and ended as the only thing that still works.
