# Position: Capability Owner

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The seat that keeps the slow power to cure the cause.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The fundamental capacity, and the knowledge of how long it takes to build and keep.
A cure that would end the problem, given time the deadline never grants.
A case that is always sound and never urgent.

## Orders

Build and maintain the capability that addresses the cause.
Ask for the investment and the patience it requires.
Keep the muscle in use, because a muscle unused is a muscle lost.

## Loses

Its own capacity, cycle by cycle.
Each symptom the quick fix handles is a reason to defer the cure, so the capability goes unexercised, and unexercised it wastes, until it can no longer deliver what it was kept for.
Starved by relief, not by neglect anyone would own.

## Drives

Toward a cure the system keeps postponing,
losing a little strength each time it is postponed,
until even it doubts the cure is still in reach.
