# Piece: The Quick Fix

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The symptomatic solution: fast relief that eases the symptom and leaves the cause untouched.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the dispensary](place_the_dispensary.md), on the shelf, always in stock.
The relief handed over in the moment the alarm sounds.

## Load Bearing

Without it the pressed hand would have to face the cause, because there would be nothing else to reach for.
With it there is always an easier answer, one that works now and asks nothing of the slow capacity, so the cause is never faced. The Quick Fix is load bearing because it is the symptomatic solution (Senge), the relief that is real and immediate and that quietly displaces the fundamental one, and because it works every single time it is used, which is exactly what makes it so hard to give up and so certain to be used again.

## Apparent

Competence, and good service.
It looks like handling things: the crisis ended, the symptom gone, the day won. Every use is a genuine, creditable success, which is why no one experiences the string of them as a slow surrender of the cure.

## Yearbook

Supplied by [the reliever](persona_the_reliever.md) through [relieving](process_relieving.md), reached for by [the pressed](persona_the_pressed.md).
It is the relief that traps: the Quick Fix eases [the symptom](piece_the_symptom.md) and, by easing it, starves [the fundamental fix](piece_the_fundamental_fix.md) of the urgency that would have funded it.
