# Process: Weaning

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The fix rationed and the capacity rebuilt, through the withdrawal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone seeing that the relief is the reason the problem is permanent.
The decision to fund the cure and ration the fix, against the pull of the next alarm.

## Direction

The quick fix drawn down and the fundamental capacity put back to use, so that using it, it recovers.
The symptom endured unrelieved while the muscle slowly returns.

## Lever

Deliberate discomfort held long enough to matter.
Only a rationed fix forces the capacity back into use, and only use rebuilds it, so the withdrawal is not a side effect of the cure but its method.

## Echo

A burden shifting back toward the cause, if the flare is survived.
A capacity coming back and a dependence receding ... or a symptom too sharp to bear, the fix reached for again, and the wean undone.
