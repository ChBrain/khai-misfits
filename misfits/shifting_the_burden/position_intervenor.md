# Position: Intervenor

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The seat that supplies the relief and is thanked for it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A fix that works fast and the standing to apply it.
The visible credit that comes with ending a crisis.
No charge for what the relief does to the capacity underneath.

## Orders

Answer the alarm and make the symptom go.
Be ready, be quick, be reliable at relief.
Take the thanks and stand by for the next flare.

## Loses

Nothing it can see, and that is the problem.
The reliance its help creates never lands on its ledger, so the better it relieves, the more indispensable and the more damaging it becomes, invisibly.
It is rewarded exactly for deepening the dependence.

## Drives

Toward more relief, delivered faster,
because relief is what is asked and thanked,
and the erosion it feeds is nobody's line item.
