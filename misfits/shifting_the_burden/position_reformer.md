# Position: Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The seat that funds the cure and holds the line through the withdrawal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The authority to fund the fundamental fix and ration the quick one.
The nerve to let a symptom flare while the capacity rebuilds.
The unpopularity of the one who took the relief away.

## Orders

Invest in the slow capacity and protect it from the next alarm.
Wean the system off the fix on a schedule the flares will fight.
Bear the symptom long enough for the cure to come back.

## Loses

The comfort of the relief, up front and for a long time.
Rebuilding a wasted capacity shows no result while the symptom returns unhandled, so the fix must be given up well before the cure can replace it.
Buys the cure only by paying the whole withdrawal first.

## Drives

Toward a burden shifted back to the cause,
through a withdrawal that hurts and convinces no one until it is done,
one hard flare away from being abandoned.
