# Persona: The Reliever

## Taxonomy

[Intervenor](position_intervenor.md). The one who makes the symptom go, fast, and is thanked for it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable, quick, genuinely helpful.
Has a fix that works and the readiness to apply it the moment the alarm sounds.
Believes it is solving the problem, and it is solving the symptom.

## Action

Applies the quick fix and takes the pain away.
Arrives at the crisis, ends it, and is visibly, gratefully credited.
Answers the next flare the same way, and the next.

## Shadow

Every relief it gives makes the next one more needed.
The help is real and the thanks are earned, and both quietly deepen the reliance and starve the cure, so the better it is at relieving, the more permanent the problem becomes.
Rewarded for the rescue, never charged for the dependence.

## Tell

"Do not worry, I have got this."
Said at every flare, which is why every flare keeps coming back.
