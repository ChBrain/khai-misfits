# Position: Problem Owner

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The seat that holds the symptom and must answer it now.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A live problem, a clock, and the accountability for both.
Two answers within reach: a fast relief and a slow cure.
No slack to endure the symptom while the slow one is built.

## Orders

Stop the pain and keep the work moving.
Take the answer that fits inside the deadline.
Mean to start the fundamental fix, later, once there is room.

## Loses

The room that would let it choose the cure.
Every deadline makes the quick fix the only feasible answer, so the fundamental one is forever deferred to a calm that never comes.
The choice is real in theory and gone in practice.

## Drives

Toward relief now, sensibly, every cycle,
which is the reasonable move each time
and the trap taken as a whole.
