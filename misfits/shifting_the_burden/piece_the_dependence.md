# Piece: The Dependence

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The addiction structure: reliance on the fix deepening as the capacity shrinks, until the relief is no longer a choice.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the dispensary](place_the_dispensary.md), in the reach that has become reflex.
And through the whole system, reorganised around the fix it now cannot do without.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would end when someone simply decided to stop.
With it stopping is no longer available: the capacity is too wasted to cure and the structure is now built on the relief, so the fix defends itself, and the more it is relied on the more indispensable it makes itself. The Dependence is load bearing because it is the reinforcing loop closing (Meadows), the point where a habit becomes a need and a shortcut becomes infrastructure, so that removing the fix would not restore the cure but collapse the thing propped on it.

## Apparent

Just how the system works now.
It looks like a settled fact of operations, a permanent line in the budget, the normal cost of running the thing. The reliance has gone so deep it has stopped registering as reliance, which is precisely the state that makes it unbreakable.

## Yearbook

Lived by [the habitué](persona_the_habitue.md) through [depending](process_depending.md), grown out of [the atrophy](piece_the_atrophy.md).
It is the trap sprung: the Dependence is what [the shifting burden](piece_the_shifting_burden.md) leaves behind, a system that cannot self-correct and no longer remembers that it could.
