# Persona: The Weaner

## Taxonomy

[Reformer](position_reformer.md). The one who funds the cure and endures the withdrawal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady, unpopular, willing to hurt now to heal later.
Sees that the relief is the reason the problem is permanent, and moves to rebuild the capacity it wasted.
Braces for the symptom to flare while the cure is still too weak to hold.

## Action

Funds the fundamental fix and rations the quick one.
Protects the slow capacity from the pull of the next alarm, and holds the line through the flare.
Endures the withdrawal long enough for the muscle to come back.

## Shadow

Its cure looks like negligence for a long time.
While the capacity rebuilds the symptom returns unrelieved, so the Weaner is the one who let the fire burn, judged by a pain it chose to bear, and one hard flare can undo the whole wean.
Right, slow, and blamed the entire way.

## Tell

"We take the hit now so we stop taking it forever."
The one sentence the alarm makes hardest to keep.
