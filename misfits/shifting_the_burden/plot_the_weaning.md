# Plot: The Weaning

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The weaning](place_the_weaning.md), where someone has seen that the relief is the reason the problem is permanent.
The fix is to be rationed and the wasted capacity rebuilt, through a withdrawal that will hurt before it helps.

## Action

[Weaning](process_weaning.md): [The Weaner](persona_the_weaner.md), holding [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), funds the fundamental capacity and rations the quick fix, and holds the line while the symptom flares unrelieved.

## Stage

[The Weaner](persona_the_weaner.md) tables [Wean and Invest](plan_wean_and_invest.md): put the budget on the workshop, draw the fix down, and bear the flare long enough for the muscle to return, so [the shifting burden](piece_the_shifting_burden.md) moves back toward the cause.
The withdrawal is not a side effect of the cure but its method: only a rationed fix forces the capacity back into use, and only use rebuilds it.

## Tension

The one move that works looks like negligence the entire time it is working.
While the capacity rebuilds the symptom returns unhandled, so the Weaner is the one who let the fire burn, judged by a pain deliberately borne, and shows no result until, suddenly, the cause is cured. It holds ... or one flare proves too sharp to bear, the fix is reached for again, the muscle wastes another notch, and the burden shifts back to the relief.
