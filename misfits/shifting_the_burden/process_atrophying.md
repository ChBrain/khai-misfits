# Process: Atrophying

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The fundamental capacity wasting from disuse, cycle by cycle.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A cure deferred because the symptom is already handled.
Each flare met by relief, so the capacity that would have cured it goes unused again.

## Direction

The slow power to fix the cause weakening the longer it sits idle.
A skill unpracticed fading, a workshop unfunded rusting, the muscle thinning between uses.

## Lever

Disuse, fed by the relief that makes use unnecessary.
The better the quick fix works, the less the capacity is called on, and the less it is called on, the less of it remains.

## Echo

A cure that is harder to reach than it was last cycle.
The fundamental fix still named, still possible on paper, and quietly less deliverable each time it is skipped.
