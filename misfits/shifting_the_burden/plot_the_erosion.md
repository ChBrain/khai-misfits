# Plot: The Erosion

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The workshop](place_the_workshop.md), quiet, while the alarm rings somewhere else.
The symptom is handled, so the slow work of the cure has no urgency, and no urgency means no funding and no use.

## Action

[Atrophying](process_atrophying.md): [The Builder](persona_the_builder.md), holding [the capability owner's seat](position_capability_owner.md), makes the case for the cure and loses it to the alarm, while the fundamental capacity goes another cycle unused.

## Stage

[The Builder](persona_the_builder.md) tables [Build the Capacity](plan_build_the_capacity.md): fund the slow power that would end the cause, and keep the muscle in use, so [the fundamental fix](piece_the_fundamental_fix.md) stays deliverable.
But the request is sound and never urgent, so it is deferred again, and [the atrophy](piece_the_atrophy.md) takes another notch off a capacity that is only kept by being used.

## Tension

The cure is not rejected, only postponed, and postponement is how it dies.
No one votes to lose the capability: they simply, reasonably, fund the fire over the fire-proofing, every cycle. The Builder's case gets better and the Builder's muscle gets weaker at the same time, until the argument for the cure is strongest exactly when the power to deliver it is nearly gone.
