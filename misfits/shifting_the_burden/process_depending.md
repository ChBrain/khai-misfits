# Process: Depending

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The reliance on the fix deepening as the capacity shrinks.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A capacity too wasted to cure, and a symptom that still returns.
The moment the fix stops being one option and becomes the only one that works.

## Direction

The relief moving from choice to habit to necessity.
The system reorganising around the fix, budgeting for it, building on it, until it cannot stand without it.

## Lever

The compounding of atrophy: the weaker the cure, the more the fix is needed, and the more the fix is used, the weaker the cure gets.
A reinforcing loop that tightens each turn.

## Echo

A dependence so complete it stops feeling like one.
The relief taken as infrastructure, the cure forgotten rather than missed, and the whole structure now resting on the thing that caused the problem to stay.
