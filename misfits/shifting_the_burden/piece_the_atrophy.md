# Piece: The Atrophy

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). The side effect: the fundamental capacity wasting from disuse each cycle the quick fix is chosen instead.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), in the tools going cold and the skill going quiet.
The slow rusting of a capacity that is never called on.

## Load Bearing

Without it the quick fix would be a harmless shortcut you could stop taking any time.
With it every relief has a hidden cost paid by the cure: the capacity to fix the cause is a muscle, and a muscle unused wastes, so each cycle the fix is chosen the power to do without it shrinks. The Atrophy is load bearing because it is the turn that makes this an addiction and not a mere habit (Senge; Repenning & Sterman): the symptomatic solution does not just fail to cure, it actively erodes the ability to cure, so the two paths are not independent and the easy one eats the hard one.

## Apparent

Nothing at all.
It looks like nothing is happening, because a capacity fading from disuse makes no noise and shows no event: there is no day the cure is lost, only a slow slope no one stands on long enough to notice. The absence of a signal is exactly what lets it run to completion.

## Yearbook

Suffered by [the fundamental fix](piece_the_fundamental_fix.md) through [atrophying](process_atrophying.md), fed by every use of [the quick fix](piece_the_quick_fix.md).
It is the mechanism of the trap: the Atrophy turns a string of reasonable reliefs into [the dependence](piece_the_dependence.md), each fix quietly disabling the alternative to the next.
