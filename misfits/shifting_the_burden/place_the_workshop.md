# Place: The Workshop

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). Where the fundamental capacity is built and kept, or left to rust.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Tools, half-finished work, a skill that takes years to grow.
The slow place where the cure is made, quiet while the alarm rings elsewhere.

## Holds

The capacity that would end the problem, and its slow decay.
Here the fundamental fix lives, and here it wastes, because a workshop unused gathers dust and a skill unpracticed fades.

## Offers

The only real cure.
Given time and investment, this is the place that addresses the cause and makes the dispensary unnecessary.

## Withheld

Speed, and so attention.
The workshop cannot answer an alarm, so it is always the thing that can wait, and waiting is exactly what wastes it, until the cure it held is no longer sure to be there.
