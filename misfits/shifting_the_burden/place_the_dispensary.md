# Place: The Dispensary

## Taxonomy

[Shifting the Burden](play_shifting_the_burden.md). Where the quick fix is kept, ready and always in stock.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A shelf of relief, a counter, a hand always there to dispense it.
The fix on tap, fast to reach and easy to take.

## Holds

The reliance, deepening with every visit.
Here the relief is handed over and taken, and each transaction makes the next one likelier, until the visits are a habit and then a need.

## Offers

Instant, dependable relief.
Whatever the flare, the same answer is on the shelf, and it works, every time, which is exactly why the shelf is never empty.

## Withheld

Any cure.
The dispensary treats the symptom and stocks nothing that touches the cause, so the more it is used the more it is needed, and it is used a great deal.
