# Piece: The Mindguard

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The self-appointed protector who keeps doubt away from the group.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the quiet word](place_the_quiet_word.md), in the corridor outside the room, out of the group's hearing.
The gentle interception: the doubter taken aside and reassured before they can speak, the outside expert's warning softened before it lands, the awkward report held back so as not to worry everyone. Loyalty, doing security work.

## Load Bearing

Without it dissent and bad news reach the group and the plan meets its objections.
With it the group is shielded from both, because the Mindguard appoints itself to keep the room's confidence intact: it does not argue the doubt down in the open, it keeps the doubt from arriving, having a quiet word here and shelving an inconvenient fact there, all to spare a happy team a disturbance it is sure it does not need. It is protection, not censorship as the guard understands it, which is why it is done so willingly and so invisibly, and why the group never learns what it was protected from.

## Apparent

Looking after the team.
It looks like care and good sense: someone sparing a busy, confident group from noise and negativity, managing a fragile visitor, keeping morale up before a big push. The service is real, and gratefully received, and that is exactly why no one asks what it cost.

## Yearbook

Worked by [the gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md) under [shield the room](plan_shield_the_room.md), against [the odd one](persona_the_odd_one.md) at the door.
It is the play's active defence: where [the swallowed doubt](piece_the_swallowed_doubt.md) keeps dissent in by choice, the Mindguard keeps it out by hand, and together they leave [the frozen-out dissent](piece_the_frozen_out_dissent.md) with nowhere to land.
