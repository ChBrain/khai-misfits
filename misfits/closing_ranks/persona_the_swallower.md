# Persona: The Swallower

## Taxonomy

[Self-Censor](position_self_censor.md). Swallows the private doubt.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Thoughtful, considerate, always reading the room.
He notices the crack in the plan and he notices the warmth in the room, and he weighs them.
He would rather keep the peace than be the one who broke it.

## Action

He holds the worry up against the warmth and does the sum.
He decides the moment is wrong, the cost too high, the point too small to spoil the mood.
He keeps it in, and smiles, and lets the room move on.

## Shadow

Courtesy plus arithmetic.
The conscientious swallow exactly the doubts worth hearing, because those are the ones that would cost the most to say.
So the very care that makes him kind is what buries the warning.

## Tell

"It is probably fine, and now is not the moment."
The moment never comes.
