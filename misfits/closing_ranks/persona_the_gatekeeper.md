# Persona: The Gatekeeper

## Taxonomy

[Mindguard](position_mindguard.md). Shields the group.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Protective, loyal, tidy.
He sees himself as the one who keeps the team calm and clear and moving.
He handles the friction so the others do not have to.

## Action

He has a quiet word with the doubter after the meeting.
He softens the outsider's warning before it reaches the room, holds the bad news back for a better day.
He keeps the group's air clean, on purpose.

## Shadow

Protection is censorship the guard cannot see.
Every worry he intercepts to spare the team is a fact the team now decides without.
So the group never learns what it was spared, and thanks him for the quiet.

## Tell

"No need to worry everyone with this."
Everyone needed worrying.
