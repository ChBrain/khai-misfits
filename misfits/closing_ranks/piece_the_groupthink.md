# Piece: The Groupthink

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The whole shape at once, and its costly cure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Over [the huddle](place_the_huddle.md) entire, from the warm room to the assigned doubter.
The phenomenon named: a cohesive group's drive for agreement overriding its appraisal of the facts, so a team gets a decision badly wrong not despite being close and confident but because it is, and mends it, if at all, only by building dissent back in on purpose.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a heap of meetings with no thesis.
With it the play has its law: the failure needs no coward and no fool, only a cohesive group, a leader's early steer, and members considerate enough to keep the peace. It is also, and this is the half the scorn leaves out, curable: assign a devil's advocate, invite the outside voice, let the leader withhold a preference and the group meet twice, and the plan is tested before the world tests it. So the Groupthink carries its own remedy, which is not less cohesion but cohesion made to tolerate the one voice it least wants to hear.

## Apparent

A team that simply blundered.
It looks, after the fact, like a lapse anyone could have caught, which makes it easy to promise it will not happen here, to us, who are sharper than that. That confidence is the cult's best cover: naming groupthink is easy and seeing it inside your own happy, high-performing room is nearly impossible, because from within it never looks like groupthink, only like a good team that agrees.

## Yearbook

Broken, when it is broken, by [the advocate](persona_the_advocate.md) through [dissent](process_dissent.md), at the price of the warmth the room enjoyed.
It is the piece all the others resolve into: [the cohesion](piece_the_cohesion.md) pricing dissent, [the steer](piece_the_steer.md) starting the drift, [the swallowed doubt](piece_the_swallowed_doubt.md) and [the mindguard](piece_the_mindguard.md) clearing the room of objection, and [the invulnerability](piece_the_invulnerability.md) dismissing what remains, make the Groupthink, and only deliberate dissent unmakes it.
