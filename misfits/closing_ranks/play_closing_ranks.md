# Play: Closing Ranks

## Estate

[khai-misfits](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-misfits): the house that holds this production and answers for the whole run.

## Name

Closing Ranks.
A close team, proud of how well it gets on, moving fast toward a decision it all believes in ... and closing gently around that belief, so the one member with a doubt keeps it in to keep the warmth, and the news that would have stopped them is kept the far side of the door.

## Arc

The bend is this: the better the team gets on, the less able it is to hear the one thing it needs to.
It runs on cohesion, which is a virtue, turned into a filter.
A tight group is a good group: it trusts, it moves fast, it backs its own, and its morale is real. But the same bond that makes it strong makes dissent expensive, because to doubt the plan now is to break ranks with people you like and who like you, and that cost is paid by the doubter alone.
So the leader lets a preference show, and the group, wanting to agree, agrees. The member with a worry reads the room, sees warmth and confidence on every face, and swallows it, and every swallowed worry makes the next face look surer still. Someone appoints themselves to keep bad news and outside voices away, out of loyalty, and the group mistakes the quiet for consensus and the confidence for competence.
Put a doubter in the room on purpose and the plan is tested before the world tests it: but the team is happy, and united, and sure, and testing itself would cost it exactly that.

## Triggers

**[Set Tone](plot_set_tone.md)**
A close, capable team takes up a decision it is minded to like, and the leader lets a preference show early. The Steer names the way, and the Loyalist orients to it, glad of a room that agrees. Closes when the group has a direction to rally to before it has weighed an alternative.

**[Close Ranks](plot_close_ranks.md)**
Cue: the room converges. The Swallower reads warmth and confidence on every face and holds the worry in, and the Cheerleader talks the plan up and the warnings down, so every quiet seat makes the next look surer. Closes when silence is read as agreement and confidence as competence.

**[Mindguard](plot_mindguard.md)**
Cue: the Odd One raises the flag, and the outside view is at the door. The Gatekeeper has a quiet word, keeps the bad news and the visitor away, and the group is spared the disturbance, out of loyalty. Closes when the dissent is frozen out and the door stays shut.

**[Fiasco](plot_fiasco.md)**
Cue: the decision meets the world, and the swallowed doubt was right. The united team is intact and its judgement was wrong, and someone asks how a room this good got it this wrong. Closes when the answer is named, they agreed too well ... or the next huddle is just as warm.

**[Devil's Advocate](plot_devils_advocate.md)**
Cue: someone puts a doubter in the room on purpose, and holds the door open. The Advocate argues the other side by assignment, the leader keeps a preference back, and the plan is tested before the world tests it. Closes when the decision is stronger and the room is colder ... or the advocate is thanked, ignored, and quietly not invited again.

## Stakes

Whether the team ever tests the plan before the world does.
Every plot moves the same way: toward agreement, which is warm, fast, and freely given, and away from the doubt, which is lonely, slow, and costly to the one who carries it. The production cannot make a happy team want to fight with itself. It can only show that no one was a coward and no one was a fool, that the cohesion was real and the loyalty was sincere, and that the one voice that would have saved the decision was the one voice the room was too close to bear.
