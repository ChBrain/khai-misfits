# Position: Mindguard

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The seat that stands between the room and the news.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Access to the doubter, a quiet word before he speaks up.
Access to the door, and a hand on what comes through it.

## Orders

Protect the group's confidence, keep the mood intact.
Keep disturbance out, let no unsettling word reach the table.

## Loses

The outside view, intercepted at the threshold.
The correction the room could not hear because it never arrived.

## Drives

Toward a spared room, away from the news it needed.
Loyalty doing censorship: the misfit fed at the door.
