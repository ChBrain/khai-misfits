# Persona: The Advocate

## Taxonomy

[Devil's Advocate](position_devils_advocate.md). The reversal: argues the other side by assignment.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Deliberate, thick-skinned, willing to be the unpopular voice on purpose.
He plays the role because the role is needed, not because he feels it.
He can argue against his own friends and still buy the next round.

## Action

He makes the strongest case against the favoured plan.
He hunts the weak joint, names the failure nobody wanted named, presses it until it holds or breaks.
He does it by job, not by disloyalty, and the room knows the difference.

## Shadow

The fix costs the warmth.
Arguing on purpose with people you like cuts against the constant pull to agree, and that pull is exactly what the play runs on.
So the cure is a discipline, deliberate and a little cold, held against the room's warm current on purpose.

## Tell

"My job today is to try to talk you out of it."
And the room lets him, because it is a role.
