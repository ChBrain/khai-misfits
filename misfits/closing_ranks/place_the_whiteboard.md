# Place: The Whiteboard

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). One plan drawn, the rest erased.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

One plan, drawn clean and whole.
No counter-plan beside it.
The board fills, then it is done.

## Holds

The illusion of one mind agreeing.
The invulnerability of the sketch.
A certainty that reads as fact.

## Offers

A clear direction to rally to.
A line the room can follow.
Something to point at and nod.

## Withheld

The alternatives no one drew.
The branch wiped before it was named.
The margin where another plan might sit.
