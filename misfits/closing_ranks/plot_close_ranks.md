# Plot: Close Ranks

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The whiteboard](place_the_whiteboard.md), one plan drawn on it and no counter-plan beside it.
The room is converging, warmly and fast, and the seat with a worry is reading every face for a sign it is not alone.

## Action

[Convergence](process_convergence.md): [The Swallower](persona_the_swallower.md), holding [the self-censor's seat](position_self_censor.md), weighs the worry against the warmth and keeps it in.
[Rationalisation](process_rationalisation.md): [The Cheerleader](persona_the_cheerleader.md) at [the booster's seat](position_booster.md) talks the plan up and the warnings down, and the room's confidence rises to meet its own reflection.

## Stage

[The Swallower](persona_the_swallower.md) tables [Hold My Peace](plan_hold_my_peace.md): swallow the doubt rather than break a working room over a maybe, so [the swallowed doubt](piece_the_swallowed_doubt.md) becomes one more calm face and [the illusion of unanimity](piece_the_illusion_of_unanimity.md) widens by a seat.
[The Cheerleader](persona_the_cheerleader.md) tables [Talk It Up](plan_talk_it_up.md): meet every risk with a reason it does not apply here, so [the invulnerability](piece_the_invulnerability.md) hardens and the plan sails on shared certainty.

## Tension

Every seat is being considerate and confident, and the two together seal the room.
The swallower is tactful, declining to derail a happy team over an unformed worry they might be wrong about, and the cheerleader is only keeping morale up on a team that has won before, and both are doing what good members do. But considerate silence read across a warm room becomes a consensus no one actually holds, and shared confidence becomes a filter that bounces the very warnings it most needs, so the better the room feels the tighter it closes, and it closes on the doubt that would have saved it.
