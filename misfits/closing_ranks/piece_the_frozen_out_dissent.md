# Piece: The Frozen-Out Dissent

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The one who does speak, and is gently pushed to the edge.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the open door](place_the_open_door.md), in the cooling that meets the member who says the unwelcome thing.
The soft penalty for breaking ranks: the raised objection heard politely and set aside, the doubter teased as the pessimist, the outside expert thanked and not asked back, a degree of warmth withdrawn from whoever strained the room. No shouting, just a chill.

## Load Bearing

Without it dissent that is voiced stays in play and does its work.
With it dissent that is voiced is neutralised without ever being answered, because the Frozen-Out Dissent is direct pressure applied by cohesion itself: the group does not refute the doubter, it repositions them, from valued member to difficult one, and the mild social cost of that shift teaches everyone watching to keep their own doubts in. It is not exile, it is a lowering of the temperature, and it works precisely because it is mild, deniable, and administered by people the dissenter likes.

## Apparent

Keeping things constructive.
It looks like a team managing a negative voice, staying positive, not letting one worrier drag down a room that needs to move. The framing is sincere and almost persuasive, which is why the dissenter half-accepts it and edits themselves down, and why the watchers read the lesson correctly and stay quiet.

## Yearbook

Suffered by [the odd one](persona_the_odd_one.md) after [raise the flag](plan_raise_the_flag.md), enforced by the warmth of every seat that looks away.
It is the play's visible edge of a quiet mechanism: where [the swallowed doubt](piece_the_swallowed_doubt.md) and [the mindguard](piece_the_mindguard.md) work in silence, the Frozen-Out Dissent is the one moment the cost of doubting is shown, and shown just enough to keep it from being paid again.
