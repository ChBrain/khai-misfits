# Position: Convener

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The seat that opens the room and speaks first.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The room's ear, held before anyone else has spoken.
The first word, and the weight that a first word carries.

## Orders

Give the team a steer so it does not sit rudderless.
Lead decisively: name the plan, show the lean, set the course.

## Loses

The group's fresh view, foreclosed the moment the lean lands.
Every later voice measured against the steer, not the problem.

## Drives

Toward a room that rallies to him, away from one that weighs the problem.
Direction curdled into foreclosure: the misfit fed at its source.
