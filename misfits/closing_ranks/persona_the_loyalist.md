# Persona: The Loyalist

## Taxonomy

[Insider](position_insider.md). The cohesive member who converges.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Devoted, upbeat, all in on the team.
He values the bond above the argument, the room's good feeling above the fine print.
Being of one mind with his people is the best part of his day.

## Action

He backs the direction the moment it is named.
He enjoys the agreement, adds his weight to it, closes ranks around the plan.
He makes the aligned choice feel like the generous one.

## Shadow

Loyalty prices dissent out.
When warmth is the reward for agreeing, the doubter pays the toll, and the toll is his welcome.
So the Loyalist's kindness quietly buys the silence that sinks the plan.

## Tell

"Great to have a team that is actually aligned."
Aligned is not the same as right.
