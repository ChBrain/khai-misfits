# Persona: The Odd One

## Taxonomy

[Dissenter](position_dissenter.md). Raises the doubt and is frozen out.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Plain, a little apart from the warm middle of the room.
He says the unwelcome thing without much art, because it seems to him it needs saying.
He is not against the team, only against the silence.

## Action

He raises the flag and asks the question the mood was avoiding.
He is heard politely, thanked, and set gently aside, marked the pessimist, the one who cannot get on board.
The plan rolls on around him.

## Shadow

Right and unwelcome at once.
The mild chill that lands on him is watched by everyone else, and everyone else draws the lesson.
So the polite freezing out teaches the room, better than any rule, to stay quiet next time.

## Tell

"Has anyone thought about what happens if this fails?"
The room's smile tightens.
