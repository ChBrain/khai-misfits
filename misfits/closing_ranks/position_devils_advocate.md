# Position: Devil's Advocate

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The reversal seat: dissent made a duty, not a risk.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The assigned licence to disagree, granted by the room itself.
A mandate that makes objection the job, not the offence.

## Orders

Argue the other side, whether or not you hold it.
Test the plan: find the crack, press the weak seam.

## Loses

The warmth, spent on friction the seat is built to make.
The ease of agreement, traded for the abrasion of challenge.

## Drives

Toward a decision that survives challenge, away from easy agreement.
Dissent bought at the cost of the bond: the fix that undoes the misfit.
