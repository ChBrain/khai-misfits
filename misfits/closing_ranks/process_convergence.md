# Process: Convergence

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). Seat by seat, toward one view.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A warm room minded to agree.
Faces already half turned the same way.
The wish to keep the peace.

## Direction

Toward one view, seat by seat.
Each nod pulling the next along.
The circle closing on a single line.

## Lever

Each swallowed doubt makes the next face surer.
Silence read as assent, then as proof.
The many mistaken for the wise.

## Echo

A consensus no single member holds.
Everyone agreeing to what none quite believes.
A vote that speaks for no one in the room.
