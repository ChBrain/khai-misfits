# Piece: The Cohesion

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The bond that makes the team strong and makes dissent a betrayal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the huddle](place_the_huddle.md), in the ease with which the team gets on.
The thing every good team is praised for: the trust, the shorthand, the loyalty, the pleasure of a room that moves as one. Real, valuable, and the ground the whole misfit grows from.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no groupthink, only a set of people who happen to disagree freely.
With it there is a price on disagreement, because the Cohesion is what makes doubting costly: to break ranks is to break with people you like and who like you, in a group whose warmth is worth keeping, so the cost of dissent is paid in the one currency the team most values. It is not a flaw bolted onto a good team, it is the good team's best quality turned into a filter, and the tighter the bond the higher the toll on the voice that would strain it.

## Apparent

The mark of a healthy team.
It looks like exactly what you want and are told to build: a close, trusting, high-morale group that has each other's backs. Nothing about it reads as a hazard, because in almost every respect it is not one, which is why the hazard it does carry is so hard to see from inside the warmth.

## Yearbook

Prized by [the loyalist](persona_the_loyalist.md), guarded by [the gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md), and spent, at last, by [the advocate](persona_the_advocate.md).
It is the root the play grows from: the Cohesion is what turns [the swallowed doubt](piece_the_swallowed_doubt.md) into the rational choice and [the illusion of unanimity](piece_the_illusion_of_unanimity.md) into the room's honest reading of itself.
