# Position: Dissenter

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The seat that says the thing no one wants said.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The awkward truth, seen plainly and held onto.
The nerve to say it out loud in a warm room.

## Orders

Raise the doubt, put the objection on the table.
Name the risk the plan is walking toward.

## Loses

The standing, chilled to the difficult one, the odd voice out.
The place in the circle, spent on being right.

## Drives

Toward the plan's test, away from the room's comfort.
Right and pushed to the edge: the misfit fed by the cold shoulder.
