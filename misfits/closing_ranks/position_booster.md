# Position: Booster

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The seat that keeps the room sure of itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The team's record, a run of wins to point to.
Its morale, a shared confidence worth protecting.

## Orders

Keep confidence high, do not let the mood slip.
Answer every warning, meet each doubt with the record.

## Loses

The warning, rationalised away before it can bite.
The inconvenient fact, explained until it stops mattering.

## Drives

Toward shared certainty, away from the inconvenient fact.
Invulnerability hardening: the misfit fed by nerve.
