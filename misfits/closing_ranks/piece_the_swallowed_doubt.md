# Piece: The Swallowed Doubt

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The worry held back to keep the peace.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the huddle](place_the_huddle.md), behind the teeth of the member who reads the room and decides not now.
The concern that occurs and is not voiced: the flaw half-seen, the question that would slow things, the objection weighed against the warmth and found too expensive to raise. Present in the head, absent from the table.

## Load Bearing

Without it the doubt reaches the room and the plan is tested against it.
With it the doubt stays private and the plan sails, because the Swallowed Doubt is self-censorship: the member does the sum, the plan is probably fine, the room is happy, the objection is only a maybe, and raising it means being the one who broke the mood over a maybe, so the rational, considerate choice is to keep it in. It is not cowardice, it is courtesy plus arithmetic, and the arithmetic is real, which is why the doubt is swallowed by exactly the conscientious people whose doubts were worth hearing.

## Apparent

Being a good team player.
It looks like tact and humility, not suppression: a member choosing not to derail a working room over an unformed worry they might well be wrong about. That reading is sincere and often correct, which is what makes the pattern so safe to repeat and so hard to catch.

## Yearbook

Held in by [the swallower](persona_the_swallower.md) under [hold my peace](plan_hold_my_peace.md), and drawn out, if at all, only when [the advocate](persona_the_advocate.md) is paid to raise it.
It is the play's quietest engine: each Swallowed Doubt is a brick in [the illusion of unanimity](piece_the_illusion_of_unanimity.md), and the sum of them is a consensus no single member actually holds.
