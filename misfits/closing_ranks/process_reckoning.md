# Process: Reckoning

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The world proves the doubt right.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The decision meeting the world.
The plan leaving the warm room.
The moment the outside gets its say.

## Direction

The failure surfacing, plainly.
The doubt, at last, proven right.
The certainty coming apart in the open.

## Lever

An outcome the cohesion could not talk down.
A result immune to reassurance.
The world, deaf to the room's confidence.

## Echo

A united team, wrong together.
And unable to name a culprit.
The bond intact, the plan in pieces.
