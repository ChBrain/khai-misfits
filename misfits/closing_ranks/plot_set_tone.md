# Plot: Set Tone

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The off-site](place_the_off_site.md), a close and capable team gathered around a decision it is already minded to like.
The options are not on the board yet, and the person the room most wants to please is about to speak first.

## Action

[Steering](process_steering.md): [The Steer](persona_the_steer.md), holding [the convener's seat](position_convener.md), lets a preference show early, and [The Loyalist](persona_the_loyalist.md) at [the insider's seat](position_insider.md) takes it up gladly, orienting the room before any alternative is weighed.

## Stage

[The Steer](persona_the_steer.md) tables [Set the Tone](plan_set_the_tone.md): name the favoured way at the top of the room, so [the steer](piece_the_steer.md) sets the answer before the question is opened.
[The Loyalist](persona_the_loyalist.md) tables [Close Ranks](plan_close_ranks.md): back the direction and enjoy [the cohesion](piece_the_cohesion.md), because agreeing with the chief in a room this warm is the easy and loyal move.

## Tension

Nothing here is heavy-handed.
The leader is only giving a decisive steer, which is good leadership, and the team is only backing it, which is good teamwork, and no order was given and no arm was twisted. But a preference shown before the options are opened is a current the cohesion turns into a drift, so the room begins to measure every later thought against the chief's lean rather than against the problem, and it does so believing, sincerely, that it is making up its own mind.
