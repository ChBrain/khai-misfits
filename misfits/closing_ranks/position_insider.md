# Position: Insider

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The seat that belongs, and wants to keep belonging.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Belonging: a place inside the circle, earned and held.
The warmth of the group, the ease of being one of them.

## Orders

Back the team, add your weight to the shared line.
Keep the ranks tight, let no gap show between you.

## Loses

The doubt, priced out by loyalty before it reaches the mouth.
The reservation that belonging cannot afford to voice.

## Drives

Toward agreement that keeps the bond, away from dissent that spends it.
Cohesion turned into a filter: the misfit fed by warmth.
