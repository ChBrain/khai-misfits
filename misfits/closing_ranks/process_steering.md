# Process: Steering

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The chief's lean becomes the room's.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A leader with a preference.
The first word, spoken before the rest.
A lean the table reads at once.

## Direction

The group toward the chief's lean.
Every later voice bending that way.
The room settling where it was pointed.

## Lever

An early signal the cohesion turns to a current.
One preference, amplified by the wish to agree.
The pull of the strongest chair.

## Echo

A room that forms the leader's view.
And believes, warmly, that it chose.
The steering forgotten, the destination kept.
