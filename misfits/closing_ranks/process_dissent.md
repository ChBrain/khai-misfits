# Process: Dissent

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The reversal: doubt built in on purpose.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone building doubt into the room on purpose.
A chair kept for the awkward question.
A discipline chosen over comfort.

## Direction

The plan tested before the world tests it.
The weak seam found while it can still be sewn.
The certainty made to earn itself.

## Lever

An assigned advocate, an open door, a leader's held tongue.
Structure standing where instinct would agree.
Friction invited in, on purpose.

## Echo

A stronger decision, and a colder room.
Held only while the discipline is renewed.
A warmth given up for a truth kept.
