# Piece: The Steer

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The leader's preference, shown early, that the group forms around.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the off-site](place_the_off_site.md), in the first thing the leader says before the options are laid out.
The direction let slip at the top of the room: a favoured plan named, a lean made plain, a hope voiced by the person the group most wants to please. Small, and decisive.

## Load Bearing

Without it the group forms its own view and the leader hears it fresh.
With it the group forms the leader's view, because the Steer sets the answer before the question is opened: once the person in charge has shown a preference, agreeing with it is the safe, warm, loyal move, and every later contribution is measured against it rather than against the problem. It is not an order and needs no force, it is a lean that the cohesion turns into a current, and the room drifts with it while believing it chose.

## Apparent

Clarity from the top.
It looks like good leadership, a decisive chief giving the team a steer instead of leaving it rudderless. And it often is, which is the trap: the same early signal that focuses a team also forecloses it, and the difference between direction and foreclosure is invisible in the moment it is given.

## Yearbook

Given by [the steer](persona_the_steer.md), taken up gladly by [the loyalist](persona_the_loyalist.md).
It is the play's first move: the Steer starts [convergence](process_convergence.md) before any alternative is weighed, and only a leader who holds a preference back, in [dissent](process_dissent.md) staged on purpose, undoes it.
