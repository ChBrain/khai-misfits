# Process: Mindguarding

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The disturbance caught before it lands.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Dissent or bad news approaching the group.
A memo that would trouble the table.
A colleague with a second thought.

## Direction

The disturbance intercepted before it lands.
The message turned back at the door.
The confidence kept unbroken within.

## Lever

Loyalty keeping the room's confidence intact.
A guard who believes he is being kind.
Protection standing in for truth.

## Echo

A group spared what it needed most.
Never knowing what it missed.
The warning that arrived, and was sent home.
