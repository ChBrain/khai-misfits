# Persona: The Cheerleader

## Taxonomy

[Booster](position_booster.md). Rationalises, keeps confidence high.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sunny, certain, high on the team's record.
He believes in these people and their run of wins, and he wants everyone to feel it too.
Doubt reads to him like a failure of nerve.

## Action

He meets every warning with a reason it does not apply here.
He talks the plan up, reframes the risk, points back at the string of victories.
He keeps the morale needle pinned to the top.

## Shadow

Shared confidence bounces the warnings it most needs.
Each caution hits the high spirits and skips off, unheard, dressed as a small worry among people who do not lose.
So morale becomes deafness, and the team's best story becomes its blind spot.

## Tell

"We have pulled off harder than this."
Past wins are not a warranty.
