# Plot: Mindguard

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The open door](place_the_open_door.md), an outside view waiting on the far side of it and a member inside with a flag to raise.
The doubt is about to arrive from two directions at once, from the seat that will speak and the expert who was invited, and the warm room does not want the disturbance.

## Action

[Mindguarding](process_mindguarding.md): [The Gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md), holding [the mindguard's seat](position_mindguard.md), steps between the group and the trouble, meeting [The Odd One](persona_the_odd_one.md) at [the quiet word](place_the_quiet_word.md) and softening the visitor's warning before it can land.

## Stage

[The Odd One](persona_the_odd_one.md) tables [Raise the Flag](plan_raise_the_flag.md): say the unwelcome thing plainly, and meet [the frozen-out dissent](piece_the_frozen_out_dissent.md), heard politely, set aside, and marked as the pessimist.
[The Gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md) tables [Shield the Room](plan_shield_the_room.md): keep the bad news and the outsider away so a happy team need not be knocked off its stride, and [the mindguard](piece_the_mindguard.md) does its quiet security work, out of loyalty and out of sight.

## Tension

The gatekeeper is protecting the team, and that is the whole problem.
Nobody refutes the doubt, because refuting it would mean hearing it: instead the doubter is repositioned from valued colleague to difficult one, gently and deniably, and the outside voice is thanked and shown out, and the room is spared. It is done out of care, by someone the dissenter likes, which is exactly why it works: the cost of breaking ranks is shown just clearly enough to teach every watching seat to keep its own flag furled, and the door swings shut on the one thing that could have stopped the plan.
