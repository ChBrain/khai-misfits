# Pitch: All Agreed

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Warm, confident, together.
A team that likes each other and shows it, quick to agree, high on morale, sure of the plan and glad to be in the room.
The voice of a group at its best, trusting and fast and united, finishing each other's sentences and moving as one.

## Undertow

Under the warmth, a doubt no one will pay to raise.
Agreeing is free and pleasant and keeps the bond; doubting is lonely and slow and spends it, so the worries are swallowed one by one, and each swallowed worry makes the consensus look wider than it is.
No one is a coward: they hold back out of respect for a room that is working, and the room, seeing only held tongues, reads its own silence as agreement.

## Nerve

The feeling of a great team in full flow,
everyone aligned, everyone confident, the decision landing easily,
and one person deciding, kindly, that this is not the moment to be the difficult one.

## Echo

After the run: a united team and a decision that failed,
a doubt that was right and never spoken,
and a room so good at agreeing that it agreed its way off the edge.
