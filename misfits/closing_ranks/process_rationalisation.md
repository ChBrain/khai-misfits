# Process: Rationalisation

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The warning explained away.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A warning raised against a confident team.
A fact that does not fit the plan.
A number no one wanted.

## Direction

The warning explained away.
The awkward fact folded flat.
The doubt dressed as a detail.

## Lever

A shared certainty that filters facts before it weighs them.
Belief deciding what counts as evidence.
The plan judging its own jury.

## Echo

Invulnerability, worn like armour.
A plan sailing on its own reflection.
The last alarm, muffled and forgotten.
