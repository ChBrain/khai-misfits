# Persona: The Steer

## Taxonomy

[Convener](position_convener.md). The leader who signals a preference early.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Warm, decisive, the kind of chief a room likes to follow.
He likes a meeting that moves, an answer that lands, a team that feels sure.
He carries a preference and he does not hide it.

## Action

He names the favoured way at the top, before the options are laid out.
He calls it a lean, a hunch, a first instinct, and invites correction.
Then he watches the room settle, pleased, toward the thing he said.

## Shadow

A decisive steer forecloses the room.
Once the person in charge has shown a preference, agreeing with it is the warm, loyal, safe move.
So the group forms his view believing it chose, and he hears his own voice come back as consensus.

## Tell

"I am leaning this way, but tell me if I am wrong."
Nobody tells him.
