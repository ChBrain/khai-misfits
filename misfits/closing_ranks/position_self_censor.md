# Position: Self-Censor

## Taxonomy

[Closing Ranks](play_closing_ranks.md). The seat that holds a doubt and holds it in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A private doubt, real and unspoken, weighed and kept.
A read of the room that says now is not the moment.

## Orders

Keep the peace, do not sour a good mood.
Do not derail a working team over a maybe.

## Loses

The doubt, unspoken, swallowed with the rest.
The one contribution that might have changed the answer.

## Drives

Toward a calm face, away from the awkward question.
Self-censorship compounding the illusion: the misfit fed by silence.
