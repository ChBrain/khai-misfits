# Process: Filling

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The Receiver resolving the blank with the meaning nearest to hand, the necessary reading that feels like the plain sense and is taken for shared.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A form of words that must be filled to be used.
At [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md), each receiver taking the underdetermined message and resolving it from the meaning closest to their own context.

## Direction

Toward the blank filled and the filling taken for the meaning.
Because a form of words does not carry its own sense, filling resolves it with the reading nearest to hand, and because that reading comes effortlessly it feels like what the words simply say, so each receiver takes their own resolution for the plain sense and assumes the room resolved it the same, the private gloss wearing the face of the obvious meaning.

## Lever

The effortlessness of the nearest reading.
Because the meaning closest to a receiver's context arrives without effort and what arrives without effort feels like what is simply there, filling runs not on any misreading but on the plain necessity of resolving an underdetermined message, the divergence assembled from no error, only from each filling the blank with the sense nearest to their own hand.

## Echo

A blank filled, and the filling mistaken for the words.
The receiver has resolved the message sensibly, so what reads as understanding is, in truth, one reading among several, taken for the meaning because it came easily, each receiver certain the room filled the blank the same.
