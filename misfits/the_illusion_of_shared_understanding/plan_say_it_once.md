# Plan: Say It Once

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the sender's seat](position_sender.md), inside [The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Sender](position_sender.md)

## Direction

Name the meaning in a clear form of words and send it to the room. Say the sentence once, plainly, and trust the words to carry the vivid meaning behind them, taking the room's nod for understanding, because to the sender the meaning needs no spelling out.

## Orders

([The Sender](persona_the_sender.md) utters the form of words feeling its meaning transparent, and does not ground it, so the nod is taken for agreement on the meaning when it is only agreement on the words.)

## Implementation

([The Meeting](place_the_meeting.md); the [sending](process_sending.md) process; [the form of words](piece_the_form_of_words.md) is sent as if it carried itself, the [blank](piece_the_blank.md) it leaves unseen and [the accord](piece_the_accord.md) presumed on the meaning.)

## Targets

- [ ] the meaning is named in a clear form of words and sent to the room
- [ ] the sentence is said once, the vivid meaning felt too plain to spell out
- [ ] the room's nod is taken for agreement on the meaning, not just the words
- [ ] the blank the words leave goes unseen, the sender sure the meaning landed as sent
