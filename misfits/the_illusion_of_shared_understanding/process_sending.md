# Process: Sending

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The Sender uttering the form of words as if it carried its meaning, the good-faith transmission that leaves a blank and does not see it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A vivid meaning and a form of words to carry it.
At [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md), the sender naming their meaning in a clear sentence and sending it to the room.

## Direction

Toward the words out and the meaning assumed carried.
Because the meaning is vivid in the sender's own head, sending names it in a sentence and trusts the sentence to carry it, so the words go out plainly and the blank they leave goes unseen, the sender feeling their meaning too obvious to arrive as anything else and taking the room's nod for its landing.

## Lever

The vividness of the sender's own meaning.
Because a meaning definite and particular to its holder feels transparent to all, sending runs on real clarity and not on vagueness, so the ungrounded message is assembled from no carelessness, only from a sender whose meaning is so plain to them they cannot picture the words filled another way.

## Echo

A sentence sent, and a blank left open.
The sender has said their meaning as clearly as they feel it, so what reads as a message delivered is, in truth, a form of words with its meaning still in the sender's head, the blank open and each receiver about to fill it their own way.
