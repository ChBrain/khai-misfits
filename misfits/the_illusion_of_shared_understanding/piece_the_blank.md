# Piece: The Blank

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The underdetermination in the message, the space the words leave that each receiver must fill, invisible to those who fill it because they fill it at once.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Opened at [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md), where the words are heard and the gap left.
Exposed at [the read-back](place_the_read_back.md), where the several fillings surface and the gap shows.

## Load Bearing

Without it the message determines its own meaning and nothing needs filling, and the readings cannot diverge.
With it the trap has its room: the space a form of words leaves, which every receiver must fill to use the message and fills so immediately they never see it as a gap (Keysar, on the underdetermination of intention, the hearer resolving what the words leave open without noticing they resolved anything). The Blank is load bearing because it is where the divergence lives: the unstated part each supplies from their own context, so the piece carries the opening the words cannot close, filled differently by each and seen by none, the gap that is invisible precisely because it is filled the instant it appears.

## Apparent

Nothing, on the surface: a clear message with no gap in it, everyone knowing what it means.
Seen at the surface, the Blank is invisible, because each receiver has already filled it and sees only their completed meaning, so the words look fully determined and the gap looks not to exist, and the truth that the message left a space, that each filled it their own way, that the completeness each feels is their own supply and not the words', stays hidden behind how filled a filled-in blank feels.

## Yearbook

Left by [the form of words](piece_the_form_of_words.md), filled by [the receiver](persona_the_receiver.md) through [filling](process_filling.md), the space the message cannot close.
It is the room of the trap: the Blank is filled with [the meaning](piece_the_meaning.md) nearest each hand, hidden under [the nod](piece_the_nod.md) and [the accord](piece_the_accord.md), and its several fillings become [the divergence](piece_the_divergence.md) the merge reveals.
