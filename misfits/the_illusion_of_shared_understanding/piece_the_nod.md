# Piece: The Nod

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The visible assent, the sign of agreement that ratifies the words and, by making consensus look real, raises the cost of the check that would test it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Given at [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md), where the room shows it agrees.
Set aside at [the read-back](place_the_read_back.md), where the assent is replaced by the readings said back.

## Load Bearing

Without it the agreement is unshown and the check stays cheap, and someone may still ask what is meant.
With it the trap seals: the visible sign that the room heard and agrees, which grounds the words and not the meaning, so once it is given, asking what exactly is meant reads as confessing one did not follow (Clark and Brennan, on grounding, the confirming step the nod stands in for without performing). The Nod is load bearing because it is what suppresses the cure: a genuine assent to the words that, by looking like consensus, makes the one act that would surface the divergence expensive, so the piece carries the ratification that seals agreement on the sentence and prices out the check on the sense.

## Apparent

Understanding confirmed, a room signalling it has grasped and agreed.
Seen at the surface, the Nod looks like grounding done, the confirming sign that the meaning landed, and the truth that it confirms only that the words were heard and accepted, that it stands in for the grounding it does not perform, that a real assent to the words is not agreement on the meaning, stays hidden behind how much a nod looks like understanding.
