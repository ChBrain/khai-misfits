# Persona: The Chorus

## Taxonomy

[Chorus](position_chorus.md). The room's visible assent, the nods that ratify the agreement and, by making consensus look real, raise the cost of the one question that would test it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Agreeable. In accord. Moving the meeting along.
Is the room as a body, the nodding table, the thread of yeses and thumbs-up, the collective that signals it has heard and agreed.
No pretence in it: each nod is a genuine sign of a genuine reading, the room really does feel it has understood and agreed.

## Action

Nods, signals accord, and moves the meeting on, each member ratifying that they heard and agree.
Makes the consensus visible, so the instruction reads as settled and the room as aligned.
Does what a room does when it feels it agrees: it shows the agreement, and the shown agreement becomes the fact the room proceeds on.

## Shadow

Not a false front, a room that genuinely feels agreed, and that is the whole difficulty: the assent is real and it is assent to the words, so showing it seals a consensus that was never on the meaning.
Because the visible agreement makes the meeting look settled, the Chorus raises the social cost of asking what exactly is meant, so that to check now is to confess one did not follow and to slow a room that has plainly agreed, and the one act that would surface the divergence is suppressed exactly when the consensus looks most real, the nod that means we are agreed making sure no one finds out they are not.
It shows a real agreement, and the showing is what keeps the agreement from being tested.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "great, we're all aligned, let's move on."
Says it truly feeling aligned, and cannot tell that the room agreed on the words and not the meaning, so takes the nod for understanding and makes checking it look like holding everyone up.
