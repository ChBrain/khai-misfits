# Process: Restating

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The Clarifier having each say the instruction back in concrete terms, the costly grounding that surfaces the readings and catches the divergence before the benches.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A consensus that came easily and someone unwilling to trust it.
At [the read-back](place_the_read_back.md), the clarifier asking each receiver to restate the instruction in their own words before anyone acts.

## Direction

Toward the readings surfaced and the meaning grounded.
Because the agreement was on the words and not the sense, restating asks each to say back what they will build in concrete terms, so the several private fillings come into the open where a single nod had stood, and the divergence is caught in the room rather than at the merge, the meaning tested instead of the assent.

## Lever

The willingness to spend the cost of doubting an easy agreement.
Because grounding the meaning means slowing a settled meeting and asking the question that confesses one did not follow, restating runs only on someone choosing to pay that social cost, so the corrective is exacted continuously and thanked rarely, the caught divergence leaving no trace of the merge it spared and the delay it took plain for all to feel.

## Echo

The readings said back, and the divergence caught in the room.
The clarifier has surfaced what each took the words to mean, so what reads as labouring the obvious is, in truth, the one act that tests the meaning and not the assent, the several readings made public while they can still be reconciled, the merge that now never breaks leaving nothing to thank it for.
