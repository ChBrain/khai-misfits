# Pitch: On the Same Page

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Agreeable, aligned, settled.
The plain good sense of a room that has heard the instruction, nodded, and left certain everyone is on the same page, the meaning felt plain and the consensus real.
Nothing false in it on its face: the sentence was said in good faith, each reading is reasonable, and the agreement everyone feels is genuinely felt.

## Undertow

Under the alignment, a form of words underdetermined enough that each receiver has filled it with their own meaning and taken that filling for the shared one.
Because a reading that feels obvious feels shared, the room agrees on the words and never on the meaning, so one instruction goes to the benches as several, each built faithfully in a different direction, the gap invisible until the merge.
What makes a message agreeable, that it is short enough to nod to, is what leaves it open enough to fill differently.

## Nerve

The feeling of leaving the room on the same page, the instruction plain and the room agreed,
sure the meaning is shared, because the reading felt so obvious it could not be just yours,
while each other head left with a different word written on the same blank page, and every bench builds a faithful execution of a sentence that meant three things.

## Echo

After the run: a single clear instruction revealed at the merge as several instructions wearing one set of words, each build faithful and none of them fitting,
no one having misheard and no one careless, only an underdetermined message filled from the meaning nearest each hand and a consensus that was only ever on the words,
and the one move that would have grounded the meaning, saying it back in concrete terms before acting, forgone because it slows a settled meeting and confesses you did not follow.
