# Plan: Fill the Blank

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the receiver's seat](position_receiver.md), inside [The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md).

## Owner

## Direction

Take the form of words and resolve it with the meaning nearest to hand. Read the instruction the obvious way, the way closest to your own context, act on that reading as the plain sense, and assume the room resolved it the same, because the effortless reading feels like what the words simply say.

## Orders

([The Receiver](persona_the_receiver.md) fills the blank with the meaning closest to its context and takes the filling for the shared sense, then builds it faithfully, one of several executing a different reading of one sentence.)
