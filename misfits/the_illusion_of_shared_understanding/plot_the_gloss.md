# Plot: The Gloss

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Meeting](place_the_meeting.md), a form of words that must be filled to be used.
Each Receiver hearing the sentence and reaching for the meaning nearest to their own context.

## Action

[Filling](process_filling.md): [The Receiver](persona_the_receiver.md), holding [the receiver's seat](position_receiver.md), fills the blank with the meaning nearest to hand and takes it for the plain sense.

## Stage

Each [receiver](persona_the_receiver.md), on [Fill the Blank](plan_fill_the_blank.md), glosses [the form of words](piece_the_form_of_words.md) with [the meaning](piece_the_meaning.md) closest to their work, one reading it as fewer features, another as a cleaner interface, another as less code.
Each takes their gloss not for a reading but for what the words simply say, and assumes the room resolved it the same.

## Tension

Filling an underdetermined message with the nearest sense is necessary, and the necessity is what hides that others filled it otherwise.
There is nothing to fault: each reading is reasonable and the filling unavoidable. But the meaning that comes effortlessly feels like the plain sense, so the plot closes with each receiver holding a different gloss, every one certain it is the meaning and that the room holds it too.
