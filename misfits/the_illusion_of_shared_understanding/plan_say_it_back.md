# Plan: Say It Back

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the clarifier's seat](position_clarifier.md), inside [The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Clarifier](position_clarifier.md)

## Direction

Have each receiver restate the instruction in their own concrete terms before anyone acts. Surface the several readings, ground the meaning rather than the assent, and treat an easy consensus as suspect for coming easy, so the divergence is caught in the room and not at the merge.

## Orders

([The Clarifier](persona_the_clarifier.md) calls for a read-back and makes the private readings public, at the cost of slowing a settled meeting and asking the question that confesses one did not follow.)

## Implementation

([The Read-Back](place_the_read_back.md); the [restating](process_restating.md) process; each [meaning](piece_the_meaning.md) is said back over [the nod](piece_the_nod.md), surfacing [the divergence](piece_the_divergence.md) before the benches and grounding the accord on the sense.)

## Targets

- [ ] each receiver restates the instruction in their own concrete terms
- [ ] the several private readings are surfaced and made public
- [ ] the meaning is grounded rather than the assent, an easy consensus treated as suspect
- [ ] the divergence is caught in the room at the cost of slowing a meeting that had agreed
