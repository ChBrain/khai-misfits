# Position: Chorus

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The seat of the room's visible assent, where a real agreement on the words is shown, and the showing raises the cost of the question that would test it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A room that feels it agrees and the means to show it: the genuine sense of accord and the nods that make it visible.
A real feeling: each member truly feels they heard and agreed, so the assent is not feigned.
No test of whether the agreement is on the meaning, and no room, once the consensus is shown, for the question that would check it.

## Orders

Nod along.
Signal that you heard and agree, ratify the consensus, and move the meeting on, because the room feels aligned and showing it keeps things moving.
Show the agreement, since the agreement is genuinely felt.

## Loses

The chance to test the agreement, exactly by showing it.
Because the visible consensus makes the meeting look settled, the seat raises the cost of asking what is meant, so that checking now reads as confessing one did not follow and slowing a room that has agreed, and the one act that would surface the divergence is suppressed just as the consensus looks most real, the shown agreement sealing itself against the test.
It gains a room that looks aligned and moves, and loses the check that would show the alignment was only on the words.

## Drives

Toward the assent shown and the check suppressed,
a real agreement on the words made to look like agreement on the meaning,
so the seat that nods along seals the consensus, and makes the question that would test it read as holding the room up.
