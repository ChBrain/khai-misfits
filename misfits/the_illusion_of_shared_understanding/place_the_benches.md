# Place: The Benches

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). Where each receiver goes apart to build their reading, and where the divergent executions grow unseen until, brought together, they do not fit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the work is done after the meeting, separate benches, desks, workstreams, where each receiver builds the instruction as they understood it.
The Receivers apart, each executing their own reading faithfully, and later the pieces carried back to be joined.

## Holds

The divergent building, and the collision waiting at the join.
Here each reading is built apart from the others, so the Benches hold the point where one instruction becomes several: faithful executions of a single sentence, each diligent and correct to its own reading, so it is where the divergence grows in the open and unseen at once, every bench sure it builds the shared thing until the pieces are brought together and the gap between them shows.

## Offers

Faithful work, each reading well and diligently built.
Because the Benches are where each receiver executes the instruction as they understood it, they offer real, careful work, the reading built true to itself, so they hand out diligent execution on every side, and hand it out in several directions at once, each bench building a different meaning of the one sentence with no sight of the others.

## Withheld

Any sign, until the join, that the builds diverge.
The Benches hold each faithful execution but cannot show it against the others, because each is built apart and nothing compares the readings, so they withhold the divergence until the pieces meet, letting every bench proceed sure it builds the shared thing, and, all diligent work and no comparison, they withhold the collision until the merge, when the single instruction is revealed to have run several ways.
