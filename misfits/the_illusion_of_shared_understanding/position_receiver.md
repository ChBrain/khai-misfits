# Position: Receiver

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The seat that fills the blank the words leave, where the meaning nearest to hand is taken for the plain sense and believed shared.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A form of words to act on and a context to read it in: the message received and the meaning nearest to hand that fills it.
A sound reading: the filling is sensible, a reasonable resolution of what the words could mean.
No way to use the message without filling it, and no sign that the effortless reading is one of several rather than the meaning.

## Orders

Fill the blank.
Take the form of words, resolve it with the meaning closest to your context, and act on that reading as the plain sense of the instruction.
Read it the obvious way, because the obvious way is the one nearest to hand.

## Loses

The chance of catching that its reading is only a reading, exactly because the reading feels like the meaning.
Because the resolution that came effortlessly feels like what the words simply say, the seat cannot see it as one of several, so takes it for the shared sense and builds it faithfully, one of several builders each executing a different reading, and loses the divergence to its own certainty until the pieces meet.
It gains a usable meaning to build on, and loses the sight that the builder beside it built another.

## Drives

Toward the blank filled and the filling taken for the meaning,
the nearest reading felt as the plain sense and believed shared,
so the seat that fills the blank builds its own reading faithfully, sure to the merge that the room built the same.
