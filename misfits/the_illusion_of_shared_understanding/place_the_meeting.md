# Place: The Meeting

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). Where the message is uttered, filled, and nodded to, the room in which the accord is felt and sealed and the check goes unasked.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the instruction is given and received, a meeting room, a standup, a thread, where the form of words is said and the room shows it has heard.
The Sender saying the sentence, the Receivers filling it each their own way, the Chorus nodding, and everyone leaving certain they are on the same page.

## Holds

The presumed accord, and the check it suppresses.
Here the words are said and the room agrees, so the Meeting holds the point where consensus is felt and sealed: a form of words filled privately by each and ratified visibly by all, so it is where the agreement on the words is taken for agreement on the meaning, and where asking what exactly is meant comes to read as confessing one did not follow, the room aligned and the alignment untested.

## Offers

A settled instruction and a room that agrees.
Because the Meeting is where the words are said and nodded to, it offers a decision reached and a consensus felt, the instruction passed and the room moving on aligned, so it hands out the momentum of agreement, and hands it out on the words and not the meaning, the several private readings left unsurfaced beneath a single visible nod.

## Withheld

Any sight of the several meanings under the one agreement.
The Meeting shows the room agreeing but cannot show what each took the words to mean, because the readings are private and the nod is public, so it withholds the divergence beneath the consensus, letting the agreement on the words stand for agreement on the sense, and, all visible accord and no grounded meaning, it withholds the difference between the readings until the benches build them and the merge brings them together.
