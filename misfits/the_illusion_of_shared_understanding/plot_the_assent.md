# Plot: The Assent

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Meeting](place_the_meeting.md), the readings each felt as obvious and therefore shared.
The room ready to show it agrees, the instruction feeling settled.

## Action

[Sealing](process_sealing.md): [The Chorus](persona_the_chorus.md), holding [the chorus's seat](position_chorus.md), nods and makes the consensus visible.

## Stage

[The Chorus](persona_the_chorus.md), on [Nod Along](plan_nod_along.md), gives [the nod](piece_the_nod.md) and ratifies [the accord](piece_the_accord.md) on [the form of words](piece_the_form_of_words.md), the room shown to agree.
The visible consensus makes the meeting look settled, so asking what exactly is meant now reads as confessing one did not follow, and the check is suppressed just as the agreement looks most real.

## Tension

Showing a genuinely felt agreement is not pretence, and the showing is what seals the room against the test.
There is nothing feigned: each nod is a real sign of a real reading. But the visible consensus prices out the one question that would surface the divergence, so the plot closes with the accord sealed on the words, the meaning untested, and the check made to look like holding up a room that had plainly agreed.
