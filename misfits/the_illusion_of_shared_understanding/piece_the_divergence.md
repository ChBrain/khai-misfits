# Piece: The Divergence

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The several directions the one instruction executes in, the faithful builds that do not fit, the cost that stays invisible until the pieces are brought together.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Grows at [the benches](place_the_benches.md), where each builds their reading apart.
Caught early, if ever, at [the read-back](place_the_read_back.md), before the builds begin.

## Load Bearing

Without it the readings differ but nothing is built on them, and the illusion costs nothing.
With it the trap has its reckoning: one instruction built faithfully in several directions, each execution correct to its own reading and none fitting the others, the gap surfacing only when the pieces are joined (the divergent execution revealed at integration). The Divergence is load bearing because it is what the illusion finally costs: not a mishearing but several diligent, incompatible builds of one sentence, so the piece carries the collision at the merge, the single clear instruction shown to have been several instructions wearing one set of words, every builder faithful and the work not fitting.

## Apparent

A sudden, surprising misalignment, work that unaccountably does not come together.
Seen at the surface, the Divergence looks like a late failure of execution, diligent people whose pieces mysteriously fail to fit, and the truth that the misalignment was set in the meeting, that each build is a faithful reading of one underdetermined sentence, that the surprise is only the first comparison of readings that were always different, stays hidden behind how much a collision at the merge looks like a fault at the benches.

## Yearbook

Grows from [the meaning](piece_the_meaning.md) each built through [filling](process_filling.md), caught before the benches only by [restating](process_restating.md).
It is the reckoning of the trap: the Divergence is the several readings of [the form of words](piece_the_form_of_words.md) built faithfully apart, the true cost of [the accord](piece_the_accord.md) that was only on the words, surfacing at the merge that [the nod](piece_the_nod.md) sealed the room against seeing.
