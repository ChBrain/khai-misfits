# Plot: The Check

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Read-Back](place_the_read_back.md), and someone unwilling to trust a consensus that came easily.
The Clarifier setting out to have each say the instruction back before anyone goes to the benches.

## Action

[Restating](process_restating.md): [The Clarifier](persona_the_clarifier.md), holding [the clarifier's seat](position_clarifier.md), asks each receiver to say back what they will build in concrete terms.

## Stage

[The Clarifier](persona_the_clarifier.md), on [Say It Back](plan_say_it_back.md), surfaces each [meaning](piece_the_meaning.md) over [the nod](piece_the_nod.md), and the several readings of [the form of words](piece_the_form_of_words.md) come into the open where a single assent had stood.
Against the read-back stand a room that has plainly agreed, and a question that confesses one did not follow.

## Tension

Grounding the meaning is the one move that catches the divergence in the room, and it asks the cost the visible consensus made dear.
It slows a settled meeting, asks each to spell out what feels obvious, and treats an easy agreement as suspect. So the plot closes one of two ways: the readings surfaced and the divergence caught before the benches ... or the read-back waived as labouring the obvious, the assent taken for understanding, and the gap left to surface at the merge.
