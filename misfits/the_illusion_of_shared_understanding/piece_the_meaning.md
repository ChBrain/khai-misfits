# Piece: The Meaning

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The private reading each receiver supplies to fill the blank, the meaning nearest to hand that feels like the plain sense and is believed shared.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Supplied at [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md), where each fills the blank from their own context.
Surfaced at [the read-back](place_the_read_back.md), where the several meanings are said back and compared.

## Load Bearing

Without it the blank stays empty and nothing is built, and there are no divergent readings to collide.
With it the trap turns: the reading each receiver supplies from the meaning nearest to hand, which feels not like an interpretation but like what the words simply say, so each takes their private meaning for the shared one (Keysar, on the egocentric anchoring of comprehension; Nickerson, on imputing one's own reading to others). The Meaning is load bearing because it is what diverges while feeling shared: the effortless private resolution each mistakes for the plain sense, so the piece carries the several readings of one sentence, each certain it is the meaning and that the room holds it too.

## Apparent

The plain sense of the words, what the instruction obviously means.
Seen at the surface, the Meaning looks like the message itself, the obvious reading any sensible person would take, and the truth that it is one resolution among several, supplied by the receiver and not the words, felt as plain only because it came easily, held identically by no one else in the room, stays hidden behind how much one's own effortless reading feels like the meaning simply there.
