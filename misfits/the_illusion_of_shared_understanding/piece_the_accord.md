# Piece: The Accord

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The phenomenon whole, the shared understanding the room believes it has and does not, a consensus felt as real over a meaning that was never held in common.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Felt across [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md), where the room agrees on the words.
Undone, if ever, at [the read-back](place_the_read_back.md), where the shared meaning is found never to have been there.

## Load Bearing

Without it the divergent readings are just difference, and no one is misled about sharing a meaning.
With it the trap is complete and authorless: a whole room believing it has understood the same thing, the belief genuine and the meaning never common, so the consensus felt as real is a consensus on the words alone (Gilovich, Savitsky and Medvec, on the illusion of transparency, the felt obviousness of one's own meaning taken for its being shared). The Accord is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole: not one misread word but a room's sincere conviction that it is on the same page when each has written a different word on the same blank, so the piece carries the shared understanding that everyone feels and no one has, the one thing the room left with that it did not possess.

## Apparent

Alignment, a team on the same page, understanding reached and the room agreed.
Seen at the surface, the Accord looks like exactly what it feels like, a genuine shared understanding, and the truth that the agreement was on the words and never the meaning, that each felt reading was private and only felt shared, that the room's sincere consensus rested on a blank each filled alone, stays hidden behind how real a felt agreement feels, right up until the merge.

## Yearbook

Felt by the whole room through [sealing](process_sealing.md), undone only by [the clarifier](persona_the_clarifier.md) through [restating](process_restating.md).
It is the phenomenon whole: the Accord is [the nod](piece_the_nod.md) on [the form of words](piece_the_form_of_words.md) taken for agreement on [the meaning](piece_the_meaning.md), the shared understanding believed over a filled [blank](piece_the_blank.md), false the moment [the divergence](piece_the_divergence.md) shows at the centre of the illusion of shared understanding.
