# Persona: The Clarifier

## Taxonomy

[Clarifier](position_clarifier.md). The one who would ground the meaning before acting, having each say the instruction back in their own concrete terms to surface the reading and not just the assent, at the cost of looking like the one who did not follow.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Willing to slow the room. Suspicious of easy agreement.
Is the one who asks what exactly is meant, the facilitator who calls for a read-back, the lead who makes each restate the plan in their own words.
No pedantry in it: the Clarifier does not doubt the words were heard, only doubts that hearing the words is sharing the meaning.

## Action

Asks each receiver to say the instruction back in concrete terms, surfaces the several readings, and catches the divergence in the room before it goes to the benches.
Treats a smooth consensus as suspect precisely because it came easily, and grounds the meaning rather than the assent.
Does what catches the trap: it makes the private readings public, and public readings are the one thing that shows the agreement was only on the words.

## Shadow

Not a free corrective, a costly and unwelcome slowing, and that is the whole difficulty: the read-back that would surface the divergence is exactly the act the visible consensus made expensive.
Because the room has plainly agreed, the Clarifier must slow a settled meeting, ask the question that confesses one did not follow, and spell out what everyone feels is already obvious, so grounding the meaning is bought against every signal that the room is on the same page, the divergence it prevents leaving no trace and the delay it costs plain for all to feel, the caught misunderstanding looking, afterward, like a thing that was never going to happen.
It grounds the meaning to spare the merge, and grounding it is read as holding up a room that had already agreed.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "before we go, say back what you're each going to build."
Says it into a room that has plainly agreed, and is heard, often, as slowing things down, labouring the obvious, the one who somehow did not get what everyone else so clearly did.
