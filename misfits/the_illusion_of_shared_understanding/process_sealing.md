# Process: Sealing

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md). The Chorus's visible assent making the consensus look real, the ratification that raises the cost of the check and seals the agreement on the words.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Readings that feel obvious and therefore shared, and a room ready to agree.
At [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md), the room nodding, each member showing they heard and agree.

## Direction

Toward the consensus shown and the check suppressed.
Because each reading feels plain and the room feels aligned, sealing shows the agreement, and the shown agreement makes the meeting look settled, so asking what exactly is meant comes to read as confessing one did not follow and slowing a room that has agreed, and the one act that would surface the divergence is priced out just as the consensus looks most real.

## Lever

The reality of the felt agreement.
Because the room genuinely feels it agrees and each nod is a real sign of a real reading, sealing runs on true assent and not on pretence, so the suppressed check is assembled from no bad faith, only from a genuine agreement on the words shown so plainly that testing it would look like doubting what everyone can feel.

## Echo

A consensus sealed, and the meaning left untested.
The room has shown the agreement it truly feels, so what reads as alignment reached is, in truth, agreement on the words sealed against the question that would reveal it was never on the meaning, the nod that means we are agreed making sure no one finds out they are not.
