# Plot: The Merge

## Taxonomy

[The Illusion of Shared Understanding](play_the_illusion_of_shared_understanding.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Benches](place_the_benches.md), each receiver gone apart to build their reading.
The faithful executions growing in different directions, unseen by each other, and the pieces due to be joined.

## Action

The divergence come to its reckoning: the several builds of one instruction are brought together and do not fit, [the divergence](piece_the_divergence.md) surfacing at last.

## Stage

Each [receiver](persona_the_receiver.md), having built [the meaning](piece_the_meaning.md) they filled into [the form of words](piece_the_form_of_words.md), carries their piece to the join, and the pieces meet and clash.
Only here does it come out that the single clear instruction everyone agreed to was several instructions wearing one set of words, [the accord](piece_the_accord.md) shown false and no one having misheard.

## Tension

Every build is a faithful execution of the words, and the faithfulness is exactly why the collision is a surprise.
There is no fault at the benches: each read the sentence sensibly and built it true. But nothing compared the readings until the join, so the plot closes with the divergence surfacing at the merge, the instruction revealed as several, the work not fitting, and the shared understanding found, too late, to have been the one thing the room never had.
