# Plot: The Cut

## Taxonomy

[The Peace It Keeps](play_the_peace_it_keeps.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Bedside](place_the_bedside.md), someone who would stop the line.
A body failing of its own overgrown blood, and a set of weapons that all strike dividing cells.

## Action

Through [Cut It Out](plan_cut_it_out.md): the Physician strikes the dividing line, but the renewal is dividing cells too, so the same strike lowers the blood the body needs, spending [renewing](process_renewing.md) to spend the clone.

## Stage

[The Physician](persona_the_physician.md) acts from [the Knife](position_the_knife.md), lowering [the line](persona_the_line.md) and the [renewing](process_renewing.md) together, unable to spare the marrow from the weapon that reaches the clone, holding [the coup](piece_the_coup.md) back only by spending the body's own blood.
The blade reaches the disease, and it reaches the renewal in the same stroke.

## Tension

The one move that lowers the line lowers the blood, and there is no dose that does the first without the second.
There is no clean save: the clone and the renewal are the same kind of thing, cells that divide, so every measure that spends the disease spends the marrow, and to cut the sensitive line too well can free the resistant remnant to return. So the plot closes with the harm fought only by spending the renewal or replacing the system whole, the Physician weighing two failures with a weapon that cannot select, the patient spent to save the patient. And yet it does not close in despair: sometimes the trade is simply won, a leukaemia broken and a life carried out whole, so the cut is costly and not futile, the price of the cure and not the absence of one, paid always in the body's own blood.
