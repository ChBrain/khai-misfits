# Plan: Hold Them Down

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Whole](position_the_whole.md), inside [The Peace It Keeps](play_the_peace_it_keeps.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Whole](position_the_whole.md)

## Direction

Keep every cell specialised and mortal and in its place, spending differentiation and programmed death and senescence and surveillance to hold the competition down, since a body is not held once but held continuously and lapses only if the holding does. Renew what must be renewed, most of all the blood, because the tissue cannot last without remaking, and accept that the renewing keeps the ground open and the contest armed, since there is no way to hold the body and also switch the competition off. Guard against the other and trust the self, because the peace is built to tell foreign from own and the plan has no move against a cell that is the body's own, so it holds the line inward and keeps no wall against its own kind.

## Orders

([The Body](persona_the_body.md) spends its suppression hour on hour to keep each cell in place, renews its blood for a lifetime, and trusts every cell that is its own, holding the peace with no defence built against a member of it.)

## Implementation

([The Marrow](place_the_marrow.md); through the [renewing](process_renewing.md) it must run and the [slipping](process_slipping.md) it cannot wholly prevent; the Body keeps [the truce](piece_the_truce.md) that holds the cells down, and pays for the upkeep in the ground it leaves open, unable to raise a wall against [the coup](piece_the_coup.md) that comes from within.)

## Targets

- [ ] every cell is held specialised, mortal, and in its place
- [ ] the blood is renewed for the life of the body
- [ ] the peace is kept inward, with no wall against the body's own kind
- [ ] the upkeep is paid in a ground left open and a contest kept armed
