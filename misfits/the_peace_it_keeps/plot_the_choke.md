# Plot: The Choke

## Taxonomy

[The Peace It Keeps](play_the_peace_it_keeps.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Niche](place_the_niche.md), the line filling the marrow.
An overgrown clone reworking the ground the normal cells grow in, not shoving them out but turning the soil against them.

## Action

[Jamming](process_jamming.md): the line poisons the niche and blocks the maturing of the normal cells, so the counts fall while the marrow crowds.

## Stage

[The Line](persona_the_line.md) jams the blood while [the Body](persona_the_body.md) bears it from [the Whole](position_the_whole.md), the normal stem cells persisting and unable to finish, [the reach](piece_the_reach.md) of it carrying past the marrow into the frame.
The marrow is full, and the body is starving of blood.

## Tension

The blood fails, and not one cell was evicted.
There is no clearing here: the line does not drive the normal cells out but jams their finishing and turns their ground, so the stem cells are present and blocked, the marrow crowded and the counts low together, the harm dealt by a poisoned soil and not by a shove. But the tissue that is overgrowing is the body's own, so the plot closes with a body failing of its own marrow, starved of the blood it cannot finish while the marrow brims with the line that will not let it, the trouble already past the marrow and told through the whole frame, and still no enemy in it but the body's own cell grown too far.
