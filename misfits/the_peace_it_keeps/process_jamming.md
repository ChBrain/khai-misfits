# Process: Jamming

## Taxonomy

[The Peace It Keeps](play_the_peace_it_keeps.md). The failing of the blood by an overgrown line, not by eviction but by a jammed maturation and a poisoned ground, the marrow full and the counts falling all the same.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Line](persona_the_line.md), at [the Niche](place_the_niche.md), remodelling the soil the normal cells grow in.
Begins as the clone fills the marrow, since its growth reworks the ground and jams the finishing of the cells around it.

## Direction

Turns a growing clone into a failing blood.
Because the line does not evict the normal stem cells but blocks their maturing and turns their niche against them, jamming converts an overtaken marrow into a body starved of finished blood, the stem cells persisting and unable to complete, so the process fails the blood by suppression and remodelling and not by any clearing of the ground, the marrow crowded and the counts low together.

## Lever

The remodelling of the ground the normal cells need.
Because maturation depends on signals the niche supplies, and an overgrowing line can rework those signals and consume the room, jamming works through the subversion of support rather than the eviction of cells: the lever is the poisoned soil, which leaves the normal cells present but unfinished, so the harm is dealt by a ground turned against its own tissue and not by a line shoving that tissue out.

## Echo

A marrow full and a blood failing, the reach spreading past it.
The echo of jamming is a body low on the blood it needs though the marrow is crowded, the normal cells held short of finishing in a soil that no longer nurses them, and the trouble not staying local: the overgrown line and its signals reach through the body as a blood-borne state, so the failing that began in the marrow tells on the whole frame.
