## Direction

Divide, hold the edge, and fill the space the marrow keeps open, leaving more copies than the cells around you and passing the advantage to every daughter, since the seat's measure is copies left and a line that divides less is a line overtaken. Answer to nothing above, because the edge carries no reference to the whole and no term for the good of the body, so the plan reads its own growth as its only success and any restraint as ground given up. Grow without a stopping-point and read the growing as fitness, since there is no ceiling in the rule and the room the renewal makes is the room to take, so the plan takes the ground the body renews and lets the harm fall where the growth does.
