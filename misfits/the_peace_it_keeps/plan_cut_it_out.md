# Plan: Cut It Out

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Knife](position_the_knife.md), inside [The Peace It Keeps](play_the_peace_it_keeps.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Knife](position_the_knife.md)

## Direction

Lower the line by striking what divides, or by ablating and replacing the whole blood system, and stop short of losing the body to the emptied marrow or the infection, titrating between two failures because the right dose cannot be known in advance and the line can return. Spend the renewal to spend the clone, since the weapon reaches all division and cannot spare the marrow, and read success as a trade won and not a harm removed, since there is no strike that lifts the line out and leaves the blood. Cut hard enough to win but not so thoroughly as to clear the room around the resistant remnant, since the cleanest strike can free what survives it, so the plan holds the line down as far as the body can bear and calls that, where it holds, the cure.
