# Piece: The Edge

## Taxonomy

[The Peace It Keeps](play_the_peace_it_keeps.md). The heritable advantage one line carries, a small gain in dividing or in not dying that selection rewards, the object the resumed competition sorts on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Held in [the Marrow](place_the_marrow.md), wherever the line divides.
Flows as the property that makes one cell's descendants a larger share each generation, passed intact to every daughter, a difference that need not be large to compound.

## Load Bearing

Without the Edge nothing sorts: it is the heritable advantage that turns one slipped cell into a winning line, the object without which the competition has nothing to select on.
The Edge is load bearing because it is the engine's fuel, the acquired difference in fitness on which somatic selection acts, the variation that Darwinian sorting requires and that a lifetime of divisions supplies (Nowell; Greaves and Maley). It is what makes the line win, since a cell that divides a little faster or dies a little less leaves more copies and its share climbs; and it carries no ceiling, because an advantage is a rule that rewards more without a term for enough, so the Edge is not only the reason the line grows but the reason it does not stop. Every gain the clone makes runs through this piece, the small heritable difference that, given the room the renewal keeps open, compounds into the whole harm.

## Apparent

Just a hardier cell, a little better at surviving.
Seen at the surface, the Edge looks like nothing sinister, only a cell that holds on where others let go, and the truth that this small hardiness is the whole engine, that an advantage rewarded without limit is a growth without a stopping-point, that the difference that reads as mere vigour is the difference that undoes the body, stays hidden behind how ordinary a slightly fitter cell appears.

## Yearbook

Carried by [the Line](persona_the_line.md), held from [the Advantage](position_the_advantage.md), and rewarded by [winning](process_winning.md).
It is the fuel of the trap: the Edge is what [the advantage](position_the_advantage.md) seats and [slipping](process_slipping.md) lets loose, the difference [the coup](piece_the_coup.md) is built on and [the truce](piece_the_truce.md) exists to deny a foothold, the property no [knife](position_the_knife.md) can cut without cutting the healthy division that shares it.
