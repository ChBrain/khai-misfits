# Process: Renewing

## Taxonomy

[The Peace It Keeps](play_the_peace_it_keeps.md). The remaking of the blood for a lifetime, each stem-cell division replacing what is spent, the upkeep the body cannot live without and the ground the competition runs in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Body](persona_the_body.md), at [the Marrow](place_the_marrow.md), dividing its stem cells to replace the blood it spends.
Begins with the first blood and never finishes, since the tissue must be remade for as long as the body lives.

## Direction

Turns a spent blood supply into a fresh one, and arms the contest in the same act.
Because the blood must be remade and remaking is dividing, renewing converts the body's need for fresh cells into a stream of divisions, each one a fresh cell and a fresh chance for a variant, so the process supplies the body and keeps the competition loaded together, the upkeep and the exposure a single motion.

## Lever

The necessity of remaking.
Because a tissue that is spent must be replaced or the body fails, renewing works through a need that cannot be waived: the divisions are not optional and each carries its small chance of a change, so the lever is the unavoidability of the upkeep itself, which keeps the ground open precisely because it cannot stop, no version of the maintenance closing the door it holds.

## Echo

A body supplied, and a ground kept open under it.
The echo of renewing is fresh blood delivered wave on wave and, in the same wave, room held clear that a line can grow into, so the tissue stands maintained and exposed at once, the very currency of its health, division, being the currency the competition spends, and the exposure lasting exactly as long as the life.
