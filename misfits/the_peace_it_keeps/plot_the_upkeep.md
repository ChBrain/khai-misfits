# Plot: The Upkeep

## Taxonomy

[The Peace It Keeps](play_the_peace_it_keeps.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Marrow](place_the_marrow.md), the body holding itself together.
A peace kept cell by cell and a blood remade wave on wave, the whole maintained exactly as being alive requires.

## Action

[Renewing](process_renewing.md): the Body remakes its blood and holds each cell in its place, and in the very act of renewing keeps the ground open that a line could grow into.

## Stage

[The Body](persona_the_body.md) acts from [the Whole](position_the_whole.md) on [Hold Them Down](plan_hold_them_down.md), keeping [the truce](piece_the_truce.md) that suppresses the cells and renewing the blood it cannot do without, carrying in the same upkeep the exposure it cannot close.
The body is well, and the wellness is the ground the harm will grow in.

## Tension

The upkeep is health itself, and the health is the opening.
There is no fault here: the peace held cell by cell is the body being a body, and the blood remade is the tissue doing exactly what it must, so the maintaining is not a risk taken but life itself. But the renewal that keeps the body alive keeps the competition armed, since every division that supplies the blood is a division that could throw a variant, so the plot closes with a body soundly kept and, in the keeping, the door held open, the truce standing whole and the one thing it cannot do, rest, being the one thing the exposure needs.
