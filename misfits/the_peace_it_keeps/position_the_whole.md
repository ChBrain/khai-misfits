# Position: The Whole

## Taxonomy

[The Peace It Keeps](play_the_peace_it_keeps.md). The seat of the higher-level individual, which holds its cells' competition down and is nothing but that suppression, existing as a body only so long as the peace is kept.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The machinery that keeps the peace, and no more than that.
The seat holds the suppression that makes a body: the signals that tell a cell to specialise, to stop dividing, to die on cue, and the surveillance that clears the ones that miss the signal, layered and redundant so a single lapse need not carry.
It holds standing over every cell in its place, but only as their cooperation, since the seat is not a ruler above the cells but the shape they take when held down, present exactly as long as the holding lasts.

## Orders

Hold the peace, and never rest from it.
Keep each cell specialised and mortal and in its place, suppress the competition without cease, and renew what must be renewed, since a body is not held once but held continuously and the blood most of all.
Treat the whole as the charge, because the seat's measure is the standing of the body and any cell let off its check is the peace beginning to give.

## Loses

The border against its own kind, which the seat was never built to hold.
The Whole can suppress a cell and cannot expel one, because every power it has was made to hold self in place and none to cast self out: there is no operation in the seat that treats one of the body's own cells as foreign, so once a line wins past the suppression there is nothing in the repertoire to eject it, only more of the holding it has already slipped. What the position forfeits is a defence against itself: it has a border against the other and none against its own, so a threat that is kin finds no wall anywhere in the seat. And it forfeits rest, since the renewal it must run keeps the competition armed, so the seat cannot both maintain the body and disarm the contest, and pays for the upkeep in exposure it cannot close.

## Drives

Toward a peace that a member can break and the seat cannot re-impose,
and a body that has no wall against its own kind, undone from a place it was never built to guard,
so the seat that makes a whole by holding its parts down is the seat that cannot cast a part out once it wins, its every power turned inward to suppress and none turned outward to expel what is already inside.
