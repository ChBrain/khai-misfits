# Place: The Marrow

## Taxonomy

[The Peace It Keeps](play_the_peace_it_keeps.md). The renewing tissue where the blood is remade for a lifetime, each stem-cell division a fresh cell and a fresh chance, so the upkeep that keeps the body alive is the ground the competition runs in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A factory of blood, health at its most tireless.
Shown at the surface is renewal working: the marrow remaking the body's blood wave on wave, dividing its stem cells to replace what is spent, a tissue whose whole business is to keep the body supplied and never stop.

## Holds

Holds the renewal and the contest in one place.
The Marrow is where the stem cells divide and where every division is a selective round, so it holds not only the making of fresh blood but the standing chance that a daughter carries a change, keeping the peace and arming the competition in the same act, the room for new cells and the room a line can grow into being one room.

## Offers

Offers fresh blood to the whole body, and the ground to a line with it.
Here the tissue renews the blood for a lifetime, so the Marrow offers the upkeep no body can live without, and offers, through the same renewing, the open ground a changed cell can take: the space kept clear for new cells is the space the line fills, so the site that supplies the body is the site that hosts its undoing.

## Withheld

Withholds a renewal that does not arm the contest, and any rest from the exposure.
What the Marrow cannot offer is upkeep without risk: the divisions that remake the blood are the divisions that can throw a variant, so there is no way to run the renewal and switch the competition off. The place that keeps the body alive keeps, in the same motion, the door the line comes through, and holds no setting in which the tissue is maintained and the ground is closed, so the exposure lasts exactly as long as the life it serves.
