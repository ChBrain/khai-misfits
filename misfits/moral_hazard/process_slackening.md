# Process: Slackening

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The quiet fall of care once the loss belongs to another.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A downside that has left the hand that acts.
The first small choice where caution costs the actor and pays the insurer.

## Direction

Precaution eased a notch, then another, toward the level a covered hand will pay for.
Never a decision to be careless, only a string of economies that each make sense.

## Lever

The private cost of care, now paid for a benefit the insurer keeps.
Once the reward for guarding leaves, the guarding is a pure expense, and expenses get trimmed.

## Echo

A hand less careful than it was, and sure it is as careful as ever.
The danger unchanged, the watch on it lowered, and no rule anywhere broken.
