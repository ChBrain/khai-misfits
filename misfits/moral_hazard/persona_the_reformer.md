# Persona: The Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Risk Manager](position_risk_manager.md). The one who cuts a hole in the cover on purpose.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Clear-eyed, a little unpopular, willing to give back some protection.
Sees that the care follows the loss, and moves a share of the loss back to bring the care with it.
Trades comfort for prudence and says so plainly.

## Action

Sets a deductible, a co-share, a stake the hand must keep.
Leaves just enough of the downside on the actor that caution pays for itself again.
Restores the incentive the full cover dissolved.

## Shadow

Its fix is a wound in the thing people bought.
The stake that revives the care is exactly the risk the cover was meant to remove, so a good deductible feels like a broken promise.
Undoes the protection to save the prevention, and is thanked for neither.

## Tell

"You keep the first share of any loss."
The sentence that puts the fear, and the care, back where it acts.
