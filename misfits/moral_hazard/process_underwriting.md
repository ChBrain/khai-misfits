# Process: Underwriting

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The transfer of the downside from the insured to the pool.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A party who wants a risk carried, and one who will carry it for a price.
The premium agreed, the cover signed.

## Direction

The loss moving off the hand that acts and onto the insurer.
Priced on the class and the history, everything that can be seen before the ink.

## Lever

What the risk is on the average, across many, before any one hand chooses its care.
The rate that can be set, because it does not depend on the effort to come.

## Echo

A protected insured and a downside now held by someone who cannot watch it.
The risk carried away, and the reason to guard it carried away with it.
