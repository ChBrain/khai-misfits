# Process: Monitoring

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The attempt to see the care the cover cannot price.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An insurer that knows the care will drift, and sends an eye to catch it.
The audit scheduled, the inspector dispatched.

## Direction

Toward turning the hidden action into a visible one the contract can hold.
Logs read, hands watched, outcomes checked against the file.

## Lever

The hope that effort leaves a trace.
That enough watching can price what the premium could not, and put the care back into the deal.

## Echo

A clean record, an expensive watch, and the effort still unseen.
The cost of looking added to the premium, and the eased notch of care no closer to proof.
