# Place: The Renewal

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). Where the terms are rewritten and a share of the loss is handed back.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

New terms on the table, a deductible added, a co-share written in, a premium reset.
The cover reopened and its shape reconsidered.

## Holds

The place the stake is put back.
Here the risk manager can leave part of the downside on the insured, so the care that left with the loss has a reason to return.

## Offers

The one working fix.
Partial cover, skin retained, an incentive restored: the caution revives the moment the actor holds a slice of the loss again.

## Withheld

The comfort of full protection.
What the renewal gives back in prudence it takes away in cover, so the fix always feels like the policy getting worse.
