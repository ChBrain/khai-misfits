# Position: Insurer

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The seat that agrees to hold the loss for a price.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A pool of premiums and the base rates to price it.
The power to set the terms of the cover.
No sight of the care taken once the cover is in force.

## Orders

Price the risk on what can be seen and pool it across the many.
Honour the claim when the loss lands.
Raise the premium when the average drifts, on everyone.

## Loses

The care it is buying against.
The moment it carries the downside, the downside stops being watched by the hand that could.
It can price the class and never the conduct.

## Drives

Toward a cover that sells and a pool that clears.
Toward protecting the insured, which is the service, and is the thing that dulls their guard.
