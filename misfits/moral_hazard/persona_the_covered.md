# Persona: The Covered

## Taxonomy

[Policyholder](position_policyholder.md). The hand that acts, now that the loss is someone else's.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sensible, unafraid, getting on with it.
Bought the cover to stop lying awake, and it worked.
Not careless: only relieved.

## Action

Eases the precaution a notch, then another.
Skips the check that only ever paid off in a loss that is no longer theirs.
Does the arithmetic without ever naming it: caution costs me, and its reward went out with the policy.

## Shadow

Cannot feel the danger it stopped guarding.
The fear that held the care in place was the fear of the bill, and the bill belongs to the pool now.
Would swear it is as careful as ever, and be honestly wrong.

## Tell

"That is what the insurance is for."
True, and the exact sentence under which the care goes.
