# Position: Policyholder

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The seat that acts, holding the wheel but no longer the crash.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The hands on the risk and the choice of how carefully to use them.
A cover that has carried away the loss.
A private cost for every act of care it still pays in full.

## Orders

Get on with the work, unafraid, as the policy intends.
Spend on caution only up to what caution now returns.
Skip nothing on purpose, ease only what stopped paying.

## Loses

The reward that once made the care worth its cost.
With the loss gone to the insurer, precaution buys a benefit it no longer keeps.
The reason to guard leaves with the risk.

## Drives

Toward the rational level of care for a hand that keeps the cost and not the loss,
which is lower than before,
and reached without a single careless decision.
