# Persona: The Underwriter

## Taxonomy

[Insurer](position_insurer.md). The one who takes the risk on and cannot watch it carried.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Prudent, actuarial, fair.
Prices the risk on the average of many and holds it for a premium.
Believes the policy is a service, and it is.

## Action

Writes the cover and takes the downside on.
Prices what it can see: the class, the history, the base rate.
Cannot price the one thing that will move, the care after the ink dries.

## Shadow

Sells the very safety that loosens the hand.
Knows the average will drift once the fear is gone, and can only raise the premium on all for the slack of a few.
Insures the risk and, by insuring it, changes it.

## Tell

"You are covered."
Said in good faith, to a hand it will never see ease off.
