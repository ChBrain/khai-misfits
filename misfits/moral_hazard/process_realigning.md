# Process: Realigning

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The return of a share of the loss to the hand that acts.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone willing to make the cover partial.
A deductible drawn up, a co-share written, a stake the insured must keep.

## Direction

Part of the downside moving back onto the actor, until caution pays for itself again.
Protection deliberately holed so prevention can revive.

## Lever

The same fear that first held the care in place.
Give the actor back a slice of the loss, and the reason to guard returns with it.

## Echo

Care restored and a cover worth less than it was.
The prudence bought at the price of the protection sold back, and a policy that works only because it no longer covers everything.
