# Position: Premium Payer

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The seat in the pool that funds the loss it did not cause.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A paid-up premium and a careful record of its own.
A share in a pool that spreads every claim across all of it.
No say over the care of the hands it is pooled with.

## Orders

Pay in, keep your own precautions high, trust the pool to be fair.
Carry your slice of every loss the pool meets.
Absorb the rise when the average drifts.

## Loses

The value of its own prudence.
Careful and careless pay the same pooled price, so the careful subsidise the eased, round after round.
The reward for being careful is a bill for someone else's ease.

## Drives

Toward a fair share of a socialised loss,
which is a tax on its own care,
until the prudent begin to wonder why they stay prudent.
