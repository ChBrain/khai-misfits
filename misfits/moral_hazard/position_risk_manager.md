# Position: Risk Manager

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The seat that leaves a share of the loss on the hand that acts.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The design of the cover, and the nerve to make it partial.
The deductible, the co-share, the retained stake as instruments.
The unpopularity of the one who gives protection back.

## Orders

Cut a measured hole in the cover so the actor keeps some downside.
Set the stake where care pays for itself again.
Trade a slice of protection for the prevention it revives.

## Loses

The clean promise of full cover.
The stake that restores the care is the very risk the insured wanted gone, so the fix reads as a broken pledge.
Buys prevention only by selling back some protection.

## Drives

Toward an incentive-restoring cover that no one will love,
where the hand that acts fears the loss again,
and the price of that prudence is a protection deliberately left incomplete.
