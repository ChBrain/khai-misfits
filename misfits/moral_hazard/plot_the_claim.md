# Plot: The Claim

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The claims table](place_the_claims_table.md), the covered risk come true at last.
The bad event that the eased care no longer held off, meeting a policy that promised to pay.

## Action

[Claiming](process_claiming.md): the loss lands and is made good from the pool, while [The Bearer](persona_the_bearer.md), holding [the premium payer's seat](position_premium_payer.md), funds the payout and meets the rise that follows.

## Stage

[The Bearer](persona_the_bearer.md) tables [Carry the Pool](plan_carry_the_pool.md): pay in, keep your own precautions high, and trust the pool to be fair, so [the pooled loss](piece_the_pooled_loss.md) spreads the cost of one eased hand across every careful one.
The seat closest to the danger is nowhere at the table: the actor who could have prevented it is charged nothing, and the invoice goes to the many.

## Tension

The promise is kept, and keeping it is the problem.
The insured is made whole, the claim is honoured, the system does exactly what it said, so nothing here reads as a failure. But the loss is met by the prudent and dodged by the one who let it fall, the premium rises on the careful, and the danger, having cost its author nothing, is left free to arrive again.
