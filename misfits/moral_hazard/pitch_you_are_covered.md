# Pitch: You Are Covered

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Calm, protected, taken care of.
The relief of a risk handed to someone who has agreed to hold it: sign here, and the loss is no longer yours to lie awake over.
The voice of a good policy doing exactly its job, spoken by a party who can now get on with the work, unafraid.

## Undertow

Under the relief, a hand loosening on the wheel.
The fear that kept the care high was the fear of the loss, and the loss has been carried away, so the care it paid for quietly follows it out the door.
Nobody decides to be careless: the caution simply stops being worth its cost, one reasonable notch at a time.

## Nerve

The feeling of being safe, and acting a little freer for it,
each small ease of care a sensible economy,
the danger unchanged and only the one who would have watched it looking away.

## Echo

After the run: a loss come home to the pool,
a premium that rises on everyone who was careful,
and a hand that walks clear of the bill for the thing it stopped guarding against.
