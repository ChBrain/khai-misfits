# Place: The Claims Table

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). Where the loss lands and the pool pays it out.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A claim, a loss adjuster, a payout drawn from the pool.
The bad event, arrived at last, being made good on the policy's promise.

## Holds

The socialised bill.
Here the cost of one eased hand is met by every premium, and the actor who could have prevented it is nowhere at the table.

## Offers

The promise kept.
The cover does exactly what it said: the loss is carried, the insured is made whole, the paper honoured.

## Withheld

The bill to the hand that acted.
The one seat that is never charged here is the seat closest to the danger, so the loss is paid by all except the one who let it fall.
