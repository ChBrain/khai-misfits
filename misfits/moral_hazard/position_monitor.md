# Position: Monitor

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The seat sent to see the care and close the gap in the cover.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Audits, inspections, logs, and the right to ask.
A mandate to observe what the premium could not price.
Every tool for seeing the outcome, and none for seeing the effort.

## Orders

Watch the insured and verify the care.
Turn the hidden action into something the contract can hold.
Report what is found, and price what is watched into the cover.

## Loses

The thing it was sent for.
Effort resists the audit: the file is clean, the outcome is all that shows, and the eased notch leaves no trace.
Its own cost is added to the premium it was meant to protect.

## Drives

Toward a proof of care that cannot be gathered,
so the watch is real, expensive, and half-blind,
and a clean record is mistaken for a careful hand.
