# Persona: The Bearer

## Taxonomy

[Premium Payer](position_premium_payer.md). The pool that pays the loss the careless hand let fall.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful, dutiful, paid up.
Keeps its own precautions high, files no claim, asks nothing back.
Trusts the pool to be fair, because it is being fair.

## Action

Carries the loss it did not cause.
Pays the premium that funds the claim, and pays it again when the premium rises.
Subsidises the eased hand without ever meeting it.

## Shadow

Its own care is the thing the system quietly taxes.
The more careful it is, the more it pays for the slack of others, until the careful begin to wonder why they bother.
A fairness that punishes prudence by the round.

## Tell

"Why did my premium go up?"
Because someone it will never see stopped watching a danger it still fears.
