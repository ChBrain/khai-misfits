# Piece: The Skin

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The deductible or share that restores care by leaving part of the loss on the insured.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the renewal](place_the_renewal.md), where the cover is made partial on purpose.
The first slice of any loss written back onto the actor, the stake retained.

## Load Bearing

Without it the care has no reason to return and the pool bleeds every cycle.
With it a share of the downside is the actor's again, so caution pays for itself once more and the hand tightens. The Skin is load bearing because it is the only working remedy the sources allow (Shavell; Holmström): full insurance and full prevention cannot both be had, so the optimal cover is deliberately incomplete, trading a little risk-spreading for the incentive it buys back. It is the fix that works by not fully fixing.

## Apparent

A meaner policy.
It looks like the insurer clawing back, the cover getting worse, the customer asked to carry what they paid to be rid of. That reading is not wrong, and that is the point: the stake that revives the care is felt as the protection failing, so the remedy is resented exactly where it works.

## Yearbook

Set by [the reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) through [realigning](process_realigning.md), against the pull of [the free downside](piece_the_free_downside.md).
It is the reckoning with a price: the Skin restores what [the cover](piece_the_cover.md) dissolved, and can do it only by handing back the very risk the cover was bought to take away.
