# Piece: The Slipped Care

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). Precaution eased to the level that is optimal once its reward belongs to the insurer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

In [the field](place_the_field.md), in the notch of caution quietly let go.
The check not run, the margin trimmed, the guard stood down, each one small and each one sensible.

## Load Bearing

Without it the cover would change who pays for a loss and not how often it comes.
With it the frequency itself moves: as the care falls, the risk the premium was priced on rises, so the insurer is now carrying a bigger danger than it sold. The Slipped Care is load bearing because it is the behavioural response (Arrow; Shavell), the actual increase in risk that makes moral hazard a real cost rather than a mere transfer, the reason a pool priced on yesterday's caution runs short on today's.

## Apparent

Getting on with the work.
It looks like efficiency, even maturity: no more fretting, no wasted motion, a hand that trusts its cover and gets more done. Every eased notch is defensible on its own, and the sum of them is a danger no one chose and no one can point to.

## Yearbook

Taken by [the covered](persona_the_covered.md) through [slackening](process_slackening.md), hidden by [the hidden hand](piece_the_hidden_hand.md).
It is the misfit in motion: the Slipped Care raises the very loss [the pooled loss](piece_the_pooled_loss.md) will spread, and it does so with no rule broken and no fear felt.
