# Persona: The Watcher

## Taxonomy

[Monitor](position_monitor.md). The eye sent to see the care, that mostly cannot.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent, suspicious in the right way, sent to close the gap.
Audits, inspects, reads the logs, asks the questions.
Believes what cannot be measured can at least be watched.

## Action

Watches the hands and catches the outcome, never the care.
Sees the loss when it lands and the paperwork that is always in order.
Proves nothing about the notch of precaution that was quietly let go.

## Shadow

Its watching is the cost the policy was meant to save.
Every hour spent trying to see the unseeable is priced into the premium, and still the care stays hidden.
Confuses a clean file for a careful hand.

## Tell

"Everything checks out."
Which is not the same as everything being done.
