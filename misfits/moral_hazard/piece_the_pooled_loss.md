# Piece: The Pooled Loss

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The bad outcome paid from the pool and spread across the careful, not billed to the hand that eased.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the claims table](place_the_claims_table.md), where the loss lands and the payout is drawn.
And in every premium after, where the cost of it is quietly refilled.

## Load Bearing

Without it the actor would meet the loss its own eased care let through, and the lesson would close the loop.
With it the loop stays open: the loss is met by the many, the actor is charged nothing extra, and the danger is free to recur. The Pooled Loss is load bearing because it is where the cost actually settles, the socialisation that lets the eased hand walk clear and taxes the prudent instead, and, at scale, the guaranteed downside that a public purse absorbs so that the largest, least-watched risks are the ones most surely caught.

## Apparent

The promise kept.
It looks like the system working: a loss carried, an insured made whole, a claim paid without quibble. And it is, which is why the bill to the careful and the free walk of the careless pass unremarked, folded into a premium that simply, reasonably, went up.

## Yearbook

Carried by [the bearer](persona_the_bearer.md) through [claiming](process_claiming.md), never charged to the hand that let it fall.
It is the reckoning that does not reckon: the Pooled Loss pays for [the slipped care](piece_the_slipped_care.md) and sends the invoice to everyone but its author.
