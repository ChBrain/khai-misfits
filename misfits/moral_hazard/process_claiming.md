# Process: Claiming

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md). The loss arriving and being paid from the pool.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The covered risk coming true.
A bad event that the eased care no longer held off, meeting a policy that promised to pay.

## Direction

The loss flowing to the pool and out again as a payout, and the premium rising to refill it.
The bill spread thin across every payer, and thick across none.

## Lever

The promise of the cover, honoured exactly.
The insured is made whole, and the cost is met by the many rather than the one who let it fall.

## Echo

A socialised loss and a free-walking actor.
The careful paying a little more, the careless paying nothing extra, and the danger primed to arrive again.
