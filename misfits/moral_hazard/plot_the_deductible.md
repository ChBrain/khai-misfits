# Plot: The Deductible

## Taxonomy

[Moral Hazard](play_moral_hazard.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The renewal](place_the_renewal.md), the terms reopened and the loss still coming home to the pool.
Someone has seen that the care follows the downside, and reaches to move a share of the downside back.

## Action

[Realigning](process_realigning.md): [The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md), holding [the risk manager's seat](position_risk_manager.md), writes a deductible into the cover and leaves the first slice of any loss on the insured.

## Stage

[The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) tables [Set the Deductible](plan_set_the_deductible.md): return part of the loss to the hand that acts, set where caution pays for itself again, so [the skin](piece_the_skin.md) revives the care that [the cover](piece_the_cover.md) dissolved.
The stake that restores the guard is the very risk the insured wanted gone, so the fix arrives wearing the face of a broken promise.

## Tension

The one move that works is the one that gives protection back.
Leave a share of the loss on the actor and the fear returns, and with it the care, but full cover and full prevention were never both available, so the prudence is bought only by selling back some of the safety. The reckoning is a hole cut in the policy on purpose ... or the deductible is judged too harsh, the cover is made whole again, and the care slips back out with the risk.
