# Persona: The Scout

## Taxonomy

[Scout](position_scout.md). The reformer who would look past the edge and make the beyond visible as a cost, at cost. The archetype of the remedy that carries the future the instrument was built to bracket off.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Far-looking. Insistent. Willing to cost the beyond.
Is any planner who would extend the horizon: the analyst who runs the model past its span, the office that stress-tests against the years the roadmap does not plot, the voice asking what happens in year six.
No prophecy in it: the Scout does not see the future, only insists the plan carry the years past its edge as a cost, and pays to make them visible.

## Action

Looks past the edge, extends the horizon, stresses the plan against the years past its reach, and enters the beyond into the plan as a constraint.
Names what the instrument excludes and carries it as a line item, so the future the model could not reach appears before it lands.
Does what a scout must: it goes past the edge the instrument draws and reports back the costs waiting there, so the plan can weigh them while they can still be weighed.

## Shadow

Not a seer, a reformer who costs a future the instrument was built to bracket off, and that is the whole difficulty: the remedy is real, the beyond can be made visible, but it must be paid for and it reads like planning for years the tool was not made to hold.
Because the beyond carries no shadow to justify the line item and the plan reads complete without it, the Scout must defend a cost for a future nothing in the plan flags, spending present effort and present numbers to hold open an edge the instrument gives no reason to doubt, and treating a sound plan as incomplete precisely where it looks soundest.
It makes the beyond visible as a cost, and the beyond having no shadow of its own is exactly what makes the cost of looking hard to justify.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "and past the edge, this," and means enter the beyond as a constraint before it arrives.
Says it against a plan that reads whole and an instrument built to stop where it stops, so the insistence sounds like reaching for years no one asked to plan, the cost of looking past the edge falling due now and the future it would catch still casting no shadow to warrant it.
