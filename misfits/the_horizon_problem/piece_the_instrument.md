# Piece: The Instrument

## Taxonomy

[The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md). The planning tool with a finite reach, the model or projection or roadmap that covers a span and stops, its edge a property of the tool and not the mind that holds it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Taken up at [the model](place_the_model.md), where every cost inside its reach is counted.
Reaches its limit at [the horizon](place_the_horizon.md), where the tool stops and the edge falls.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no plan and no reach, and nothing to exclude the beyond.
With it the trap has its cause: a tool that covers a finite span, because a plan that reached to infinity would not be usable and a model must have a terminal horizon to be worked at all (the finite-horizon literature; Simon on the bounded premises a decision is actually taken within). The Instrument is load bearing because it is where the exclusion originates: not in the planner's sight but in the tool's reach, so the edge is a property of the instrument and not a fault in the mind, and the trap turns on this, that a perfectly attentive, far-sighted planner using a finite instrument still produces a plan with no costs past the edge, because the horizon belongs to the tool.

## Apparent

A sound tool, on the surface: a model that covers the span it was built for, doing its work.
Seen at the surface, the Instrument looks complete, because it reckons every cost inside its reach and produces a plan that balances, so the tool looks like it holds the whole picture, and the truth that it holds only what is inside its edge, that its reach is finite and its boundary excludes the beyond, stays hidden behind how full a full account to the edge feels.

## Yearbook

Worked by [the planner](persona_the_planner.md) at [the model](place_the_model.md), the tool that reckons [the costed](piece_the_costed.md) and draws [the edge](piece_the_edge.md).
It is the cause of the trap: the Instrument's finite reach excludes [the beyond](piece_the_beyond.md) undeliberately, and its silence about its own boundary becomes [the silence](piece_the_silence.md) the plan carries and [the landing](piece_the_landing.md) the successor meets.
