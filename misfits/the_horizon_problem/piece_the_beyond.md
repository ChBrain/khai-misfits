# Piece: The Beyond

## Taxonomy

[The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md). What lies past the edge, carrying no cost and casting no shadow, the future the instrument could not reach, not weighed and dismissed but structurally absent from the plan.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Excluded at [the horizon](place_the_horizon.md), where the instrument stops and the beyond falls past.
Made visible at [the overlook](place_the_overlook.md), where someone looks past the edge and enters it as a cost.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing excluded and the plan carries the whole future, and the trap has no loss.
With it the trap has its stakes: the future past the edge, what the boundary sweeps outside the system's account (Churchman, on what falls beyond the line the system draws, present in the world and absent from the plan). The Beyond is load bearing because it is what the exclusion excludes: not a cost weighed and set aside, which would leave a trace, but a cost never entered, structurally absent, so it carries no number, constrains nothing, and moves nothing in the model, and a cost that is absent this way leaves no gap where it was, so the piece carries the future that exists in the world and not in the plan, exerting no pull until it arrives.

## Apparent

Nothing, on the surface: no cost, no line item, no shadow on the plan.
Seen at the surface, the Beyond is invisible, because it carries no number and the plan reads whole without it, so the years past the edge look like nothing to account for, and the truth that a real future waits there, uncosted and untraded and bound to arrive, stays hidden behind how complete a plan with no line for the beyond feels, the absence reading not as a lack but as there being nothing to lack.

## Yearbook

Excluded by [the edge](piece_the_edge.md) that [the instrument](piece_the_instrument.md) draws, the future past the reach that [the costed](piece_the_costed.md) does not hold.
It is the stakes of the trap: the Beyond carries no shadow under [the silence](piece_the_silence.md) that would flag it, so it waits, uncosted, until it arrives as [the landing](piece_the_landing.md), unless [the scout](persona_the_scout.md) looks past the edge and enters it as a cost.
