# Position: Planner

## Taxonomy

[The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md). The seat that takes up the instrument and builds the plan to its edge, where every cost inside the reach is counted and the beyond is never met.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An instrument to plan with and a horizon to plan to: the model, the projection, the roadmap, and the edge it reaches.
A sound method: every cost inside the reach counted and weighed, the plan built attentively and in good faith to its horizon.
No way to plan without an instrument, and no instrument without an edge, so no reach past the finite span the tool can hold.

## Orders

Build the plan to the edge.
Take the instrument, reckon every cost inside its reach, and produce a plan complete to its horizon, sound and balancing.
Plan as far as the instrument reaches, because a plan is built with a tool and the tool has a finite edge.

## Loses

The chance of carrying the beyond, exactly because the beyond carries no cost inside the instrument.
Because what is past the edge has no number in the model, the seat cannot weigh it and set it aside but never meets it as a cost at all, so builds a plan sound to its horizon and silent past it, and loses the future the instrument could not reach to a plan that reads whole because a structurally absent cost leaves no gap where it was.
It gains a plan complete to its edge, and loses the years past the edge to the reach of the tool that built it.

## Drives

Toward the plan complete to its horizon and the beyond never entered,
every reachable cost counted and the years past the edge carrying no number,
so the seat that builds to the edge produces a plan sound to its horizon and silent past it, the future it could not reach left, uncosted, for whoever arrives.
