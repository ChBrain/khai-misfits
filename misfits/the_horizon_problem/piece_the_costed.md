Without it the plan has no content and there is nothing sound to set against the beyond.
With it the trap has its foil: the whole account inside the edge, every cost the instrument can reach weighed and balanced and constrained, what the system draws inside its boundary and keeps within its account (Churchman, on the system and its environment, what the line holds in against what it sweeps out). The Costed is load bearing because it is what makes the plan read whole: a full and genuine reckoning of everything within the reach, so sound to its horizon that its completeness inside the edge stands in for completeness, the beyond past it absent and the account full where the instrument could reach, the very soundness of the costed hiding that it is sound only to the edge.
