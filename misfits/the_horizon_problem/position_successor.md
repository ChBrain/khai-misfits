# Position: Successor

## Taxonomy

[The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md). The seat that inherits the plan and arrives in the beyond, where the uncosted future the instrument excluded lands with no one to charge.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An inherited plan and a place past its edge: the plan sound to its horizon and the years the instrument never reached.
A real bill: the maintenance the build never carried, the cliff past the projection, the debt due in the year the plan did not cover.
No decision to point to that spent the future, because none did, and no reckoning that could have caught it, because it was outside the account.

## Orders

Live in the span the plan excluded.
Take up the inherited plan, arrive in the years past its horizon, and meet the future the instrument could not reach.
Stand where the plan ran out, because the years past the edge arrive whether or not the tool reached them.

## Loses

The chance of charging anyone for the beyond, exactly because no one spent it.
Because the future that lands was structurally absent and not traded, the seat inherits a bill no one chose to run up, uncosted and unassigned and now present, and cannot find the choice that caused it because there was none, so loses to a cost that fell outside the plan and came due all the same.
It inherits a plan sound to its horizon, and the soundness of the plan to its horizon is exactly what leaves the beyond uncosted when the seat arrives in it.

## Drives

Toward the beyond arrived and the bill unassigned,
the excluded future landing on whoever is standing past the edge,
so the seat that inherits the plan meets the years the instrument could not reach, uncosted and untraded, with no one to charge because no one spent them.
