# Plot: The Bounding

## Taxonomy

[The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Horizon](place_the_horizon.md), an instrument with a finite reach and an ask that fixes it.
The edge falling where the instrument stops, everything past it dropping out of the account.

## Action

[Bounding](process_bounding.md): the instrument draws [the edge](piece_the_edge.md) where it stops, and [the beyond](piece_the_beyond.md) falls outside the plan, excluded undeliberately.

## Stage

Through [the sponsor's](persona_the_sponsor.md) [Ask for the Plan](plan_ask_for_the_plan.md), the span is named and [the edge](piece_the_edge.md) is fixed where [the instrument](piece_the_instrument.md) reaches its limit.
Everything inside the line stays in [the costed](piece_the_costed.md); everything past it becomes [the beyond](piece_the_beyond.md), not weighed and dismissed but structurally absent, the boundary judgement that placed the edge never examined as a judgement at all.

## Tension

Drawing an edge is necessary, and the necessity is what excludes the beyond without anyone deciding it.
There is nothing to fault: the ask is reasonable and the instrument covers a finite span, as any instrument must. But the boundary falls where the tool stops, so the plot closes with the beyond swept outside the account undeliberately, a line no one placed on purpose taken for the natural end of the plan.
