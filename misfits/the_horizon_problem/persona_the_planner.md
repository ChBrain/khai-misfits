# Persona: The Planner

## Taxonomy

[Planner](position_planner.md). The one who takes up the instrument and builds the plan to its edge, attentively and in good faith, every cost inside the reach counted. The archetype of competent, far-sighted planning that still stops at the horizon.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Attentive. Far-sighted. Thorough to the edge.
Is any planner working an instrument to build a plan: the analyst with the model, the team with the roadmap, the office with the projection.
No short-sightedness in it: the Planner reaches as far as the instrument reaches and counts every cost it can hold, working in good faith to the horizon that was set.

## Action

Takes up the instrument, builds the plan to its edge, and reckons every cost inside the reach, weighing and balancing until the plan is complete to its horizon.
Runs the model to year five, the projection to the end of its span, the roadmap to the last quarter it plots, and counts everything the instrument can hold.
Does what a planner must: it plans within the reach of the tool it has, because a plan is built with an instrument and an instrument has an edge.

## Shadow

Not short-sighted, a faithful builder to an edge that the instrument draws and not the Planner, and that is the whole difficulty: the reach is finite, and everything past it is not weighed and set aside but simply outside what the instrument can hold.
Because the beyond carries no number in the model, it exerts no pull on the plan, so the Planner does not weigh it and dismiss it but never meets it as a cost at all, and builds a plan that is sound to its horizon and silent past it, complete because a structurally absent cost leaves no gap where it was, so the plan reads whole and the beyond waits, uncosted, for whoever arrives past the edge.
It counts every cost the instrument can reach, and the reach of the instrument is exactly what hides the costs past its edge.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "the plan is complete," and means complete to the horizon the instrument reached.
Says it having counted every cost inside the edge, and cannot see the beyond as a lack, because a cost that is structurally absent leaves no slot it failed to fill, so the plan looks whole and the years past the edge look like nothing to say.
