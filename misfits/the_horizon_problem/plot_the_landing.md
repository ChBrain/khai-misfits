# Plot: The Landing

## Taxonomy

[The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Horizon](place_the_horizon.md) crossed, the years inside the edge running out.
A successor arriving in the beyond, where the instrument never reached and the plan has nothing to say.

## Action

[Arriving](process_arriving.md): [The Successor](persona_the_successor.md), holding [the successor's seat](position_successor.md), meets [the beyond](piece_the_beyond.md) come due as [the landing](piece_the_landing.md), uncosted and untraded and now present.

## Stage

The [successor](persona_the_successor.md), on [Inherit the Plan](plan_inherit_the_plan.md), takes up a plan sound to its horizon and arrives past [the edge](piece_the_edge.md), meeting the maintenance the build never carried, the cliff past the projection, the debt due in the year the plan did not cover.
It lands uncosted, because it was never weighed against anything, and unassigned, because no decision spent it, so there is no one to charge and no reckoning that could have caught it.

## Tension

The beyond arrives whether or not the instrument reached it, and its never having been traded is what leaves it uncosted when it lands.
There is nothing to fault: no one was short-sighted and no one spent the future to buy the present. But the future past the edge was structurally absent, not traded, so the plot closes with the excluded years landing on whoever is standing past the edge, the bill due and no account to charge it to.
