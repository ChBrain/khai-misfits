# Persona: The Successor

## Taxonomy

[Successor](position_successor.md). The one who inherits the plan and arrives in the unexamined beyond, meeting the uncosted future the instrument excluded. The archetype of whoever is standing past the edge when the excluded future lands.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Arriving. Inheriting. Present past the edge.
Is any successor who takes up a plan after its horizon: the next tenure, the team the year after the model stopped, whoever runs the sixth year of a five-year plan.
No fault in it: the Successor inherits a plan that was sound to its horizon and simply arrives in the span past it, where the plan has nothing to say and the future has already arrived.

## Action

Inherits the plan complete to its edge and arrives in the beyond, where the instrument never reached and the years run uncosted.
Meets the maintenance the build never carried, the cliff past the end of the projection, the debt that came due in the year the plan did not cover.
Does what a successor must: it lives in the span the plan excluded, because the years past the horizon arrive whether or not the instrument reached them.

## Shadow

Not the one who erred, the one who is standing where the plan ran out, and that is the whole difficulty: the future that lands was never traded away, so there is no one to charge and no reckoning that could have caught it, only a cost the instrument could not hold arriving on whoever arrives.
Because the beyond was structurally absent from the plan and not weighed and spent, the Successor inherits a bill no one chose to run up, uncosted and unassigned and now present, and cannot point to a decision that spent it because none did, so meets a future that fell outside the account and came due all the same.
It inherits a plan that was sound to its horizon, and the soundness of the plan to its horizon is exactly what leaves the beyond uncosted when the Successor arrives in it.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "this was never planned for," and means the plan was complete to its edge and this is past it.
Says it holding a bill no one entered and no one spent, and cannot find the choice that caused it, because there was no choice, only an instrument that could not reach these years and a plan that said nothing about them because it had nothing, in its own account, to say.
