# Piece: The Edge

## Taxonomy

[The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md). The horizon, the boundary of what is costed, the line where the instrument stops and past which nothing is entered, drawn by the reach of the tool and unexamined as a judgement.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Fixed at [the horizon](place_the_horizon.md), the line where the instrument reaches its limit.
Crossed at [the overlook](place_the_overlook.md), where someone looks past it and the boundary shows.

## Load Bearing

Without it the instrument reaches everywhere and nothing is excluded, and there is no beyond.
With it the trap has its boundary: the line where the plan's account stops, drawn where the instrument stops and dividing the costed from the beyond, the boundary judgement that every planning definition makes and that is usually never examined as a judgement at all (Ulrich, on critical systems heuristics and boundary critique, every framing drawing a line that sweeps stakeholders and consequences out of view). The Edge is load bearing because it is where the exclusion is made: not weighed and dismissed but placed by the reach of the tool, so the piece carries the boundary the plan is built to and the beyond falls past, a line that looks like the natural end of the plan and is only the edge of the instrument.

## Apparent

A natural stopping point, on the surface: the end of the plan, where the account properly closes.
Seen at the surface, the Edge looks like a conclusion, because the plan reaches it and stops and reads complete, so the line looks like where the plan naturally ends, and the truth that it is a boundary and not an end, that the instrument drew it and the beyond lies past it, stays hidden behind how finished a plan run to its edge feels.

## Yearbook

Drawn by [the instrument](piece_the_instrument.md) at [the horizon](place_the_horizon.md), the line where [the costed](piece_the_costed.md) stops and [the beyond](piece_the_beyond.md) begins.
It is the boundary of the trap: the Edge divides what is counted from what is not, its placement unexamined under [the silence](piece_the_silence.md), the beyond it excludes arriving at last as [the landing](piece_the_landing.md).
