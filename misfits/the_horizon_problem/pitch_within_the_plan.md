# Pitch: Within the Plan

## Taxonomy

[The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Sound, complete, far-sighted.
The plain competence of a plan built attentively to its horizon, every cost inside its reach counted and weighed, the model balancing and the edge set where the ask fixed it.
Nothing false in it on its face: the ask was reasonable, the instrument is sound, and the plan is genuinely complete to the horizon it was built to cover.

## Undertow

Under the completeness, an instrument with a finite edge, and past that edge a future that does not exist within the plan as a cost, a consequence, or a constraint.
Because the beyond is not weighed and dismissed but structurally absent, it carries no number and casts no shadow and moves nothing in the model, and nothing in the plan flags that it was excluded, so the plan reads whole precisely where it stops.
What makes an instrument usable, that it has a finite reach, is what makes it exclude what is past its reach.

## Nerve

The feeling of approving a plan that is complete to its horizon, every reachable cost reckoned and the model sound,
sure the plan is whole, because there is no gap in it and no line that names anything left out,
while past the edge a future the instrument could not reach carries no cost, casts no shadow, and waits, uncosted, for whoever arrives.

## Echo

After the run: a successor arriving past the horizon and meeting the future the instrument excluded, uncosted and untraded and now present, with no one to charge because no one spent it,
no one having been short-sighted and no one having spent the future to buy the present, only a finite instrument that excluded the beyond structurally and a plan whose silence about it read as there being nothing to say,
and the one move that would have carried the future, looking past the edge and entering the beyond as a cost, forgone because it costs years the instrument was built to bracket off and doubts a plan that reads complete.
