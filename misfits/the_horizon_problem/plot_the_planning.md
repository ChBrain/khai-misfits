# Plot: The Planning

## Taxonomy

[The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Model](place_the_model.md), an instrument taken up and a horizon to plan to.
The Planner reckoning every cost inside the reach, weighing and balancing toward a plan complete to its edge.

## Action

[Planning](process_planning.md): [The Planner](persona_the_planner.md), holding [the planner's seat](position_planner.md), builds the plan to the edge and counts every cost the instrument can reach.

## Stage

The [planner](persona_the_planner.md), on [Plan to the Edge](plan_plan_to_the_edge.md), works [the instrument](piece_the_instrument.md) to reckon [the costed](piece_the_costed.md) inside [the edge](piece_the_edge.md), the model run to its span, the projection to its end, the roadmap to its last quarter.
Every reachable cost is weighed and the plan balances, complete to its horizon and never meeting [the beyond](piece_the_beyond.md), which carries no number to weigh.

## Tension

Building within the reach of the instrument is necessary, and the necessity is what leaves the beyond unentered.
There is nothing to fault: the Planner is attentive and far-sighted and counts every cost the tool can hold. But the instrument has a finite reach, so the plot closes with a plan sound to its horizon and silent past it, the beyond outside the account not by any judgement but by the boundary of the tool.
