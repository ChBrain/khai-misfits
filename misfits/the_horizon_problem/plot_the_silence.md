# Plot: The Silence

## Taxonomy

[The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Model](place_the_model.md), a plan complete to its edge.
Nothing in the plan naming what the horizon excluded, the account full to the edge and quiet past it.

## Action

The plan carries [the silence](piece_the_silence.md): nothing flags that [the beyond](piece_the_beyond.md) was excluded, so the plan reads whole and is approved as complete.

## Stage

The plan built on [Plan to the Edge](plan_plan_to_the_edge.md) reckons [the costed](piece_the_costed.md) and says nothing of [the edge](piece_the_edge.md), because [the instrument](piece_the_instrument.md) does not represent its own boundary as a cost.
So the plan reads sound and candid, its quiet past the horizon taken for there being nothing to say, the exclusion unflagged and the account full to the edge standing for the whole.

## Tension

A plan cannot flag a cost it never held, and the not-flagging is what lets the plan read complete.
There is nothing to fault: the plan names every cost it holds and hides nothing it has. But the instrument cannot mark its own boundary, so the plot closes with the exclusion unflagged and the plan approved as whole, the silence about the beyond reading exactly like there being nothing to say.
