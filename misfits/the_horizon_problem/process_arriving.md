# Process: Arriving

## Taxonomy

[The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md). The excluded future arriving as an uncosted surprise on whoever is there, the years past the edge coming due on the successor who inherits the plan and stands in the beyond.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The years inside the edge running out.
At [the horizon](place_the_horizon.md) crossed into the beyond, the Successor arriving in the span the instrument never reached and meeting the future it could not hold.

## Direction

Toward the excluded future landed and the bill unassigned.
Because the beyond was structurally absent from the plan and not traded, arriving brings it due on whoever is standing past the edge, uncosted and untraded and now present, and because no decision spent it, there is no one to charge and no reckoning that could have caught it, so the future the instrument excluded lands with its bill and no account to charge it to.

## Lever

The beyond having been absent, not spent.
Because the future that arrives was never in the plan's account and never traded away, arriving runs not on any past error but on the plain fact that the excluded years come whether or not the tool reached them, so the cost lands where no one chose to put it, on the successor who is simply there when the beyond arrives, the bill due and unassigned because the choice that would have owned it was never made.

## Echo

A future arrived, and no one to charge for it.
The Successor has come into the span the instrument excluded, so what reads as a surprise is, in truth, the beyond the plan never carried arriving on schedule, uncosted because it was outside the account and unassigned because no one spent it, the years past the horizon landing on whoever inherited the plan and stood where it ran out.
