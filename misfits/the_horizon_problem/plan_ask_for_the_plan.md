# Plan: Ask for the Plan

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the sponsor's seat](position_sponsor.md), inside [The Horizon Problem](play_the_horizon_problem.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Sponsor](position_sponsor.md)

## Direction

Name the horizon the decision needs and ask for a plan that reaches it. Size the plan to the choice at hand, commission a plan complete to that edge, and take it back sufficient for the decision, asking for the span that fits and no more, because a plan without a horizon does not aid a choice and the fitting horizon is the one the decision requires.

## Orders

([The Sponsor](persona_the_sponsor.md) names the span that fits the decision and asks for a plan to that edge, drawing the boundary without deciding it, so the future past the horizon falls outside the plan by a reasonable request that never met it as something to leave in.)

## Implementation

([The Model](place_the_model.md) commissioned to [the horizon](place_the_horizon.md); the [bounding](process_bounding.md) process the ask sets in motion; [the edge](piece_the_edge.md) is fixed by the ask, and [the beyond](piece_the_beyond.md) falls past it, excluded without deliberation.)

## Targets

- [ ] the horizon that fits the decision is named
- [ ] a plan complete to that edge is commissioned
- [ ] the edge is fixed by the ask, the span sized to the choice
- [ ] the beyond falls outside the plan by a reasonable request that never considered it
