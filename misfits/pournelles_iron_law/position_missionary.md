# Position: Missionary

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). The seat devoted to the goal, spending its effort where the goal is served.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The mission, and the skill to serve it.
Outcomes in the field, real and earned.
No standing inside, because the field pays in a currency the house cannot spend.

## Orders

Serve the goal.
Spend the effort outward, where the mission actually meets the world.
Leave the committee to those who care for it, and win the work.

## Loses

Every internal contest, by not being in it.
Its outcomes are real and buy nothing inside, so its influence thins as the levers pass to the ones who tended the body, and its scorn for that tending gives them a true reason to hold the wheel.
The seat most devoted to the mission ends with the least say over it.

## Drives

Toward the goal,
against a house where the goal earns no standing,
so the work is done by the hand that will not hold, and cannot keep, the levers.
