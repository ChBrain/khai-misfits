# Piece: Pournelle's Iron Law

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). The phenomenon whole: control captured by devotion to the body over devotion to the goal, and its remedy in re-aimed levers at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the charter review](place_the_charter_review.md), where the levers are re-aimed or the law holds.
And across the whole house, in every place the body is served over the goal.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of promotions and departures with no shape.
With it the moves become one law: the divide, the internal currency, the capture, the drain, the turn inward, and the maintenance are facets of a single mechanism, in which control is made of the things org-devotion produces, so those devoted to the organisation win it from those devoted to its mission, whatever the organisation is for. Pournelle's Iron Law is load bearing because it is the whole finding with its whole remedy attached (Pournelle; Michels; Downs): the only answer is to re-point the internal currency at the mission and shield the goal-devoted from the contest, a reform that must be enacted by the captured and cannot tell the self-serving upkeep from the maintenance the mission needs.

## Apparent

Either a well-run institution or a nest of self-servers to purge.
Seen from one side the body is simply strong and professionally managed; from the other, it is careerists to be cleared out; and both miss the structure, that the capture needs no cynics and the tending is partly real, so neither admiration nor a purge answers it, only re-aiming what advancement rewards, which is the one thing the org-devoted who hold the levers are placed to prevent.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) through [re-aiming](process_re_aiming.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: Pournelle's Iron Law is what [the divide](piece_the_divide.md) becomes once [the internal currency](piece_the_internal_currency.md) decides control, undone only by re-pointing that currency at the field, at the cost of asking the captured to disarm and the peril of cutting [the maintenance](piece_the_maintenance.md) with the capture.
