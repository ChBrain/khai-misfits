# Plot: The Emptying

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The field](place_the_field.md), the mission losing its voice inside.
The levers are held by the org-devoted now, the missionary's say is spent, and out where the work is done the service has begun to thin.

## Action

[Draining](process_draining.md): [The Missionary](persona_the_missionary.md), holding [the missionary's seat](position_missionary.md), finds its influence gone and drifts toward the door, while [The Served](persona_the_served.md), holding [the public's seat](position_public.md), receives less of the thing the organisation was built to give.

## Stage

[The Missionary](persona_the_missionary.md) tables [Serve the Goal](plan_serve_the_goal.md) once more and meets [the drain](piece_the_drain.md), its outcomes unpriced and its ground lost.
[The Served](persona_the_served.md) tables [Come for the Service](plan_come_for_the_service.md) and gets the diminished share [the turn inward](piece_the_turn_inward.md) leaves it, with no line drawn to the cause.

## Tension

The mission bleeds out quietly, at both ends, and the body has never looked healthier.
No one is purged and no service is cancelled: the goal-devoted simply ebb from a house that pays them nothing, and the served's portion simply thins as the inward turn takes its share. The same purse funds the body's strength and the mission's loss, but the two are never set side by side, so the emptying reads as ordinary turnover and thrift, and the organisation grows stronger as the reason for it recedes.
