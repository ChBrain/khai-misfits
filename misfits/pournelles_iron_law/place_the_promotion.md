The careerist's built currency read as merit, the missionary's field outcomes finding no column, and the control passing up to the one who tended the body.

## Holds

Here the internal currency is converted into control, and here the conversion looks entirely fair, so the promotion holds both the handing of the wheel to the org-devoted and the fairness that makes it look earned, the takeover and its clean face.

## Offers

The record it weighs is real, the standing is really held, the process really owned, so the promotion offers a defensible account of why this hand and not that one rose, an advancement no one can call unjust on its own terms.

## Withheld
