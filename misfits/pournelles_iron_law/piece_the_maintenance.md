# Piece: The Maintenance

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). The real institutional upkeep the mission needs, indistinguishable from the self-serving tending.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the committee room](place_the_committee_room.md), woven through the tending.
Where the upkeep the body genuinely requires runs in the same file as the capture.

## Load Bearing

Without it the cure would be simple: cut the org-tending and free the mission.
With it the cure is a hazard: the maintenance is the part of the tending that is real, the budget, process, and continuity the mission actually cannot live without, and it looks exactly like the self-serving kind because it is done by the same hands in the same room. The Maintenance is load bearing because it is what makes the law no one's fault and the remedy unsafe: it gives the custodian a true reason to tend and the reformer no clean line to cut, so the hand that strips the capture can tear out the scaffolding, and the misfit's danger, like a Chesterton's fence, is that some of the paper was holding something up.

## Apparent

Either pure overhead or pure necessity, depending who is asked.
To the reformer it can look like all self-service to slash; to the custodian, all essential to defend; and both are wrong, because it is genuinely a mix with no visible seam, so the maintenance reads as one thing or the other, and the truth that the real and the self-serving are the same acts by the same hands stays hidden behind whichever case is being made.

## Yearbook

Done by [the custodian](persona_the_custodian.md) within [tending](process_tending.md), the peril [Re-aim the Levers](plan_re_aim_the_levers.md) must cut around.
It is the safeguard hidden in the capture: the Maintenance is why [the turn inward](piece_the_turn_inward.md) cannot simply be reversed, the real care tangled in the self-service that answering [Pournelle's Iron Law](piece_pournelles_iron_law.md) risks tearing out.
