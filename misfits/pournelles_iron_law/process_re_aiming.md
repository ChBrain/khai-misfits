# Process: Re-aiming

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). The move to re-point the internal currency at the mission, and the two things that break it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A recognition that the levers reward the body and not the goal, and a hand that would turn them.
At [the charter review](place_the_charter_review.md), the reformer proposing that outcomes in the field decide who rises.

## Direction

Toward an organisation pointed outward again.
Advancement is re-aimed to reward the mission served, the goal-devoted are shielded from the internal contest, and the self-serving upkeep is pruned, so that serving the goal buys standing and the outward work stops losing.

## Lever

A change to what the promotion rewards, made by the ones the promotion rewarded.
Because only re-pointing the currency turns the body outward, the reform must pass through the org-devoted who hold the levers and be aimed at a tending whose real maintenance cannot be told from its capture, so it is right, resisted, and unable to cut cleanly.

## Echo

The organisation turned back to its goal, or a care torn out with the capture.
The currency re-aimed and the mission buying standing again, or the reform blocked by its keepers, or the real upkeep cut with the self-serving, the remedy ending in a mission restored or a scaffolding lost.
