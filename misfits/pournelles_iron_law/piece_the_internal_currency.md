# Piece: The Internal Currency

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). The coin of control: budget, headcount, process, turf, earned by tending the body and not by serving the goal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the committee room](place_the_committee_room.md), where it is accrued.
In the budget lines and the headcount and the owned process, the stuff the body is made of.

## Load Bearing

Without it the two devotions would compete on level ground and the mission might win.
With it the ground is tilted before the contest starts: the internal currency is what power in the house is made of, and it is earned by tending the body, so the org-devoted accrue it simply by doing what they do, and the mission-devoted cannot, because the field pays in outcomes the house cannot bank. The Internal Currency is load bearing because it is the mechanism of the whole law (Niskanen on budget; Parkinson on headcount): control is made of exactly the things org-devotion produces, so the capture is not a betrayal but an exchange rate, the mission served in a coin that does not spend where control is bought.

## Apparent

The neutral resources any organisation runs on.
Budget, headcount, and process look like the plumbing of getting things done, so the internal currency reads as mere capacity, and the truth that it is the currency of control, accrued by one devotion and not the other, that holding it is holding the house, stays hidden behind its look of ordinary infrastructure.
