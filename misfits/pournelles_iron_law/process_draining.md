# Process: Draining

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). The mission losing its voice inside, and the served receiving less outside.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An org-devoted hand on the levers, and a mission with no standing to answer it.
In [the field](place_the_field.md), the missionary finding its influence gone, and the served waiting on a service that thins.

## Direction

Toward an organisation run by whoever is left.
The mission-devoted lose the internal contest and drift or are let go, their outcomes unpriced and their say spent, so the goal runs on the remnant, and the ones it is for get the diminished share the inward turn leaves them.

## Lever

An outcome that buys nothing where power is decided.
Because serving the goal earns no internal currency, the ones who serve it cannot hold their ground inside, so the drain is not a purge but an ebb, the steady loss of those whose devotion the house does not pay.

## Echo

A mission thinning at both ends.
The goal-devoted gone from the table, the served shortchanged in the field, and a body stronger than ever running a mission that is now mostly its name.
