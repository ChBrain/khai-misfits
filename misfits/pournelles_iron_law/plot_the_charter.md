# Plot: The Charter

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The charter review](place_the_charter_review.md), someone moving to re-aim the levers at the mission.
The proposal is that outcomes in the field, not standing in the house, decide who rises, and that the goal-devoted be shielded from the internal contest.

## Action

[Re-aiming](process_re_aiming.md): [The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md), holding [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), moves to re-point the internal currency at the field, while [The Custodian](persona_the_custodian.md), holding [the custodian's seat](position_custodian.md), holds the levers the reform would bind and the maintenance it would risk.

## Stage

[The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) tables [Re-aim the Levers](plan_re_aim_the_levers.md) and answers [Pournelle's Iron Law](piece_pournelles_iron_law.md) by making advancement reward the mission served.
[The Custodian](persona_the_custodian.md) tables [Protect the Institution](plan_protect_the_institution.md), and its sincere defence of [the maintenance](piece_the_maintenance.md) is also the defence of the capture the maintenance hides.

## Tension

The one move that turns the organisation outward must be made by the ones the law put in charge, and cannot cut cleanly.
Re-pointing the currency is the only real cure, but it asks the org-devoted to disarm themselves, and their resistance is not villainy but the law still running. And even granted the will, the reformer cannot find the seam: the tending it would prune is part genuine upkeep the mission needs, so the knife that strips the capture risks the care. It closes with the organisation pointed back at its goal, or the law holding, or a real safeguard torn out with the self-serving, a Chesterton's fence pulled down in the dark.
