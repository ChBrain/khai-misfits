# Persona: The Served

## Taxonomy

[Public](position_public.md). The one out in the field the mission exists for, receiving less as the body turns inward.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Waiting, in the field, for the thing the organisation was built to give.
Sees the letterhead grow and the service thin, and cannot say why the two move apart.
Owes nothing to the body, only wants the outcome.

## Action

Comes for the mission and meets the organisation instead.
Receives what is left after the inward turn has taken its share.
Feels the drain as a slow decline in the thing it was promised, with no line to the cause.

## Shadow

It is who the mission is for, and the one with no vote in the contest that decides the mission.
Its need is the whole point and buys nothing inside, so its decline is the last thing the internal currency prices.
The party the organisation exists to serve is the party its selection cannot see.

## Tell

Asks "where did the service go," of a body that is only getting stronger.
Never told that the strength and the service were drawn from the same purse.
