# Piece: The Divide

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). The two devotions: those who serve the goal, and those who serve the organisation itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the field](place_the_field.md), the outward pole, against which the inward one is defined.
And across the whole house, wherever a hand chooses the mission or the body.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no law, only an organisation with people in it.
With it the whole structure has its fault line: the divide is the split between devotion to the goal and devotion to the body, and everything follows from which side a hand spends its effort on, because the two are served in different places and paid in different currencies. The Divide is load bearing because it is the misfit's first premise (Downs on zealots and conservers): it is not a split between good and bad people but between two honest loyalties, and the law is simply that one of them is the loyalty control is made of.

## Apparent

Just a mix of temperaments.
People caring about different things looks like the ordinary variety of any workplace, so the divide reads as personality, and the truth that one devotion earns the currency of control and the other does not, that the split decides who will run the place, stays hidden behind the look of a team with a healthy range of interests.

## Yearbook

Split at the mission by [the missionary](persona_the_missionary.md) tabling [Serve the Goal](plan_serve_the_goal.md), and at the body by those who tend it.
It is the fault line the law runs along: the Divide sends one devotion to earn [the internal currency](piece_the_internal_currency.md) and the other to suffer [the drain](piece_the_drain.md), and sets the whole of Pournelle's law in motion.
