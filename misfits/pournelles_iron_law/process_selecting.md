# Process: Selecting

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). The ladder converting internal currency into control, cycle by cycle.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A store of internal currency, and a promotion built to read it.
At [the promotion](place_the_promotion.md), the standing weighed and the levers ready to change hands.

## Direction

Toward control in the hands of the org-devoted.
The record of budget and headcount and process is read as merit, the outcomes in the field find no column, so the ones who tended the body are advanced and the ones who served the goal are passed over, and the wheel goes to the inward-turned.

## Lever

A fair reading of the one record the ladder can see.
Because the promotion can price standing in the house and not outcomes in the field, its honest measure rewards the currency of tending, so the capture arrives looking exactly like merit, and is the harder to fight for being fair.

## Echo

The org-devoted holding the levers, by the book.
The mission-devoted answering to them, the advancement defensible on its own terms, and the organisation handed, cleanly, to the ones who serve it rather than its goal.
