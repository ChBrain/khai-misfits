# Persona: The Careerist

## Taxonomy

[Careerist](position_careerist.md). Devoted to the organisation instrumentally, and playing its inner game on purpose.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fluent in the committee, alert to the budget, attentive to whoever decides.
Makes no secret of it: reads the organisation as a game of internal currency and plays to win it.
Where the custodian believes, this one calculates.

## Action

Spends its effort exactly where control is won.
Builds headcount, captures process, banks the standing that promotions read.
Rises for the same reason the custodian does, only knowingly.

## Shadow

It is the cynic the reform would love to blame, and blaming it changes nothing.
Removing it leaves the custodian rising by the same currency, so the law selects its devotion whether the motive is sincere or gamed.
The visible schemer is not the cause, only the honest face of a selection that would run without it.

## Tell

Asks "how does this play upstairs," of every move.
And is right to, in a house where upstairs is won by exactly that question.
