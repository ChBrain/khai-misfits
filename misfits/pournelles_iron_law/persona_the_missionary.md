# Persona: The Missionary

## Taxonomy

[Missionary](position_missionary.md). Devoted to the goal the organisation exists to serve, and spending its effort where the goal is.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Focused outward, on the work and the ones it is for.
Impatient with the committee, indifferent to the budget line, caring for the mission and not the body that carries it.
Measures itself by the outcome, not the standing.

## Action

Spends its effort in the field, where the goal is actually served.
Wins outcomes the organisation's inside cannot spend.
Neglects the internal contest as beneath the work, and loses it by not showing up.

## Shadow

Its whole devotion is aimed where control is not won, so it does the mission and holds none of the levers.
And in scorning the body's upkeep it hands the custodian a true grievance, a real maintenance left undone.
The one most devoted to what the organisation is for has the least say in what the organisation does.

## Tell

Asks "does it serve the mission," of every choice.
In a house where the choices that matter are decided by who tended the body.
