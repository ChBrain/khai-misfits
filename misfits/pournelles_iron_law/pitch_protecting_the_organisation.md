# Pitch: Protecting the Organisation

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Responsible, institutional, sound.
The voice of people minding the body: guarding the budget, holding the process, keeping the organisation strong enough to do its work.
Every inward move is spoken as stewardship, and stewardship is what rises.

## Undertow

Under the stewardship, a mission draining.
The effort that keeps the body also buys the body, so the ones who tend it inward take the levers, and the ones who serve the goal outward answer to them.
The organisation grows more devoted to itself, quarter by quarter, and calls it health.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching the people who serve the mission lose, every time, to the people who serve the organisation,
with no villain in the room,
only a currency that pays inward and a goal that is served outward.

## Echo

After the run: a body robust and a mission thin,
budgets defended and headcount grown while the thing it was all for quietly recedes,
and a takeover that no one committed and everyone, protecting the organisation, carried out.
