# Persona: The Custodian

## Taxonomy

[Custodian](position_custodian.md). Devoted to the organisation itself, and sincerely, tending the body that carries the mission.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady, dutiful, protective of the institution.
Believes, truly, that the body must be kept strong or the mission dies with it, so it guards the budget, holds the process, defends the turf.
Not a schemer: a steward, and sure of it.

## Action

Tends the organisation's inner life and keeps it running.
Accrues, by that tending, the budget and headcount and standing that the body is made of.
Rises, without ever meaning to climb, because tending the body is how the body is won.

## Shadow

Its devotion is sincere and its rise is structural, so it cannot be purged as a cynic and cannot be told from one.
The upkeep it does is partly real and partly self-serving, and it cannot see the seam, so it defends the whole as necessary.
The most honest keeper of the body is, by the same act, its most legitimate captor.

## Tell

Says "we must protect the organisation."
And means it, while the protecting quietly hands it the wheel.
