# Plot: The Inward Turn

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The committee room](place_the_committee_room.md), the body now working for itself.
The organisation is run by those devoted to it, and its effort turns to its own survival: the budget defended, the headcount grown, the mission kept on the letterhead.

## Action

[Tending](process_tending.md) at scale: [The Custodian](persona_the_custodian.md), holding [the custodian's seat](position_custodian.md), protects the institution as duty, while [The Careerist](persona_the_careerist.md), holding [the careerist's seat](position_careerist.md), grows it as strategy, the two producing one result.

## Stage

[The Custodian](persona_the_custodian.md) tables [Protect the Institution](plan_protect_the_institution.md) and [The Careerist](persona_the_careerist.md) tables [Play the Game](plan_play_the_game.md), and sincere and instrumental together make [the turn inward](piece_the_turn_inward.md).
The mission becomes what is said, not done, and [the maintenance](piece_the_maintenance.md) that is real is folded, inseparably, into the self-service that is not.

## Tension

The organisation abandons its goal without anyone deciding to, and calls the result health.
The custodian means only to protect and the careerist only to climb, and both serve the body, so the aim shifts to the body's own perpetuation with no vote taken and no villain to name. Every sign reads as thriving: the budget holds, the headcount rises, the internal life is strong. But the strength is spent on itself, the mission it names is not the mission it serves, and the real upkeep is now so woven into the self-service that no one could pull them apart.
