# Plan: Re-aim the Levers

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), inside [Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Reformer](position_reformer.md)

## Direction

Re-point the internal currency at outcomes in the field. Make advancement reward the mission served and not the body tended, shield the goal-devoted from the internal contest, and prune the self-serving upkeep. Then prune gently, for some of the tending is the maintenance the mission cannot live without.

## Orders

([The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) asks the org-devoted who hold the levers to make those levers reward the mission, and cannot find where the capture ends and the care begins.)

- [ ] advancement is re-aimed to reward the mission served over the body tended
- [ ] the mission-devoted are shielded from the internal contest

- [ ] the self-serving upkeep is pruned without tearing out the real maintenance
