# Position: Custodian

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). The seat devoted to the body, tending it sincerely and rising by the tending.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The care of the institution, and a true belief that it must be kept.
The budget, the headcount, the process, the standing, all the currency of the body.
A rise it never schemed for, earned by minding the thing control is made of.

## Orders

Protect the organisation.
Guard the budget, hold the process, keep the body strong.
Mind the institution, and let the minding be its own advancement.

## Loses

Nothing it can see, and the very distinction that would clear it.
Its devotion is sincere and its rise is structural, so it cannot be told from a cynic and cannot tell its own real upkeep from its self-service, and defends the whole as necessary.
The most honest keeper of the body is, by the same act, its most legitimate captor.

## Drives

Toward the body's health,
against no resistance at all,
so the sincere protection of the organisation is exactly how the organisation is taken.
