# Plot: The Split

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The committee room](place_the_committee_room.md), the work dividing between two devotions.
One hand turns outward to the mission and one turns inward to the body, and only the inward turn touches the budget, the headcount, and the standing that the house runs on.

## Action

[Tending](process_tending.md): [The Custodian](persona_the_custodian.md), holding [the custodian's seat](position_custodian.md), minds the body and accrues its currency, while [The Missionary](persona_the_missionary.md), holding [the missionary's seat](position_missionary.md), spends its effort in the field for outcomes the house cannot bank.

## Stage

[The Custodian](persona_the_custodian.md) tables [Protect the Institution](plan_protect_the_institution.md) and builds [the internal currency](piece_the_internal_currency.md), sincerely and without scheming.
[The Missionary](persona_the_missionary.md) tables [Serve the Goal](plan_serve_the_goal.md) and lands on the far side of [the divide](piece_the_divide.md), winning the mission and earning nothing inside.

## Tension

Two honest devotions, and only one of them buys the organisation.
Nothing here is a betrayal: the custodian truly protects the body and the missionary truly serves the goal, and both are needed. But the body is made of the things the custodian tends and the mission is paid in a coin the house cannot spend, so the split that looks like a healthy range of interests is already the whole law in miniature, deciding, before any contest, which devotion will hold the wheel.
