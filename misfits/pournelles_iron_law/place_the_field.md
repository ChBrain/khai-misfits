# Place: The Field

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). Where the mission is served and nothing internal is earned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place where the organisation meets the world: the work done, the need met, the outcome the whole thing exists to reach.
The missionary spending its effort here, and the served waiting for a service that is quietly thinning.

## Holds

The mission, and the drain of it.
Here the goal is actually served, and here the influence of the ones who serve it runs out as the levers pass inward, so the field holds both the real work of the organisation and the emptying of those who do it, the outcome and the loss of its authors.

## Offers

The one place the mission is real.
Outcomes are won here, needs are met here, the goal touches the world here, so the field offers the organisation its only true contact with what it is for, the work that all the standing inside is nominally about.

## Withheld

Any currency the house can spend.
The field pays in outcomes, and the committee cannot bank them, so it withholds the standing that would keep its servers in power, and the hands that win the mission here lose the levers there.
