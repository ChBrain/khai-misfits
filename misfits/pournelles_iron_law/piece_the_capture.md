# Piece: The Capture

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). Control handed to the org-devoted, as the ladder reads the internal currency as merit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the promotion](place_the_promotion.md), where the levers change hands.
Where the standing is weighed and the wheel goes up to the one who tended the body.

## Load Bearing

Without it the org-devoted would merely hold resources, not the organisation.
With it the currency becomes command: the capture is the conversion of internal standing into control, the moment the ladder advances the tender of the body over the server of the goal and calls it merit. The Capture is load bearing because it is where the law completes (Michels on oligarchy): it is not a coup but a promotion, defensible on its own terms, so the takeover wears the face of fair advancement, and the fairness is exactly what makes it stick, since no single decision in it was wrong.

## Apparent

Merit rewarded.
An advancement that reads real standing off a real record looks simply like the right person rising, so the capture reads as meritocracy, and the truth that the record is the wrong record, that the measure rewards the body over the mission, stays hidden behind the honesty of each individual call.
