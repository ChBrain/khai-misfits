# Persona: The Ladder

## Taxonomy

[Ladder](position_ladder.md). The internal selection, handing control up to whoever holds the internal currency.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fair by its own lights, reading the record it is built to read.
Promotes the standing it can see: the budget held, the headcount led, the process owned, the alliances kept.
Neutral to motive, blind to the field.

## Action

Converts internal currency into control.
Advances the ones who tended the body, passes over the ones who served the goal.
Hands the levers, cycle by cycle, to the org-devoted, and calls it merit.

## Shadow

The record it reads is real, and it is the wrong record, so its fairness is exactly how the capture looks legitimate.
It cannot price an outcome in the field, only a standing in the house, so it rewards the currency it can count.
An honest selection, run on the one measure that guarantees the mission loses.

## Tell

Asks "what have they built here," and means inside.
Of a contest whose only fair-looking answer is the one that turns the organisation in.
