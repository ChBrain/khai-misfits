# Place: The Committee Room

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). Where the body is tended and the currency of control is accrued.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The inner life of the organisation: the budget argued, the headcount planned, the process owned, the standing kept.
The custodian minding it as duty and the careerist working it as game, both banking the same coin.

## Holds

The internal currency, and the maintenance it hides in.
Here the things control is made of are tended and accrued, and here the real upkeep the mission needs runs alongside the self-serving kind, indistinguishable, so the room holds both the stuff of power and the care the body genuinely requires, the capture and the maintenance in one file.

## Offers

The look of responsible stewardship.
Everyone here is minding the institution, guarding its budget, keeping it strong, so the committee room offers the language of duty and health for what is also the accrual of control, and reads every inward move as protection.

## Withheld

Any price for an outcome in the field.
The room can value a budget defended and a process held, and it cannot value the mission served, so it withholds the one measure that would reward the outward work, and the currency it does count is the currency that turns the body in.
