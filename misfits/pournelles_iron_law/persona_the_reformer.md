# Persona: The Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Reformer](position_reformer.md). The one who would re-aim the levers at the mission, and must ask the body's keepers to do it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Clear about the fix and clear about its trap.
Wants advancement to reward the goal served, not the body tended, and the mission-devoted shielded from the internal contest.
Knows the reform must pass through the very hands it would disarm.

## Action

Moves to re-point the internal currency at outcomes in the field.
Asks the org-devoted, who hold the levers, to make those levers reward what they do not do.
Reaches to prune the self-serving upkeep, and cannot find where it ends and the real upkeep begins.

## Shadow

Its remedy is correct and must be enacted by the ones the law has already put in charge, who are asked to give up the wheel.
And the tending it would cut is part genuine maintenance the mission needs, so its pruning hand risks the scaffolding it means to save.
The one move that turns the organisation outward depends on the inward-turned, and cannot tell the capture from the care.

## Tell

Says "reward the mission, not the minding."
To a room that was promoted for minding, about a minding it cannot cleanly separate from the mission.
