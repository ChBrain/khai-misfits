# Plot: The Rise

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The promotion](place_the_promotion.md), the levers about to change hands.
The record is laid out, the standing weighed, and the outcomes won in the field find no column on the form.

## Action

[Selecting](process_selecting.md): [The Ladder](persona_the_ladder.md), holding [the ladder's seat](position_ladder.md), reads the internal currency as merit, while [The Careerist](persona_the_careerist.md), holding [the careerist's seat](position_careerist.md), presents exactly the standing the ladder is built to reward.

## Stage

[The Ladder](persona_the_ladder.md) tables [Reward the Insider](plan_reward_the_insider.md) and hands up [the capture](piece_the_capture.md), advancing the tender of the body over the server of the goal.
[The Careerist](persona_the_careerist.md) tables [Play the Game](plan_play_the_game.md) and rises, its banked currency read, on its own terms, as the clearest merit in the room.

## Tension

The fairest promotion in the house is the one that hands the house to itself.
The ladder's record is real and its reading is honest, and both can price only the body tended, never the mission served, so the advancement is defensible in every particular and wrong in sum. And blaming the careerist changes nothing: the custodian behind it would rise by the same currency, so the rise is not a scheme that succeeded but a measure doing exactly what it was built to do.
