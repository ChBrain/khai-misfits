# Piece: The Turn Inward

## Taxonomy

[Pournelle's Iron Law](play_pournelles_iron_law.md). The organisation optimising for its own perpetuation, the goal become an afterthought.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the committee room](place_the_committee_room.md), where the body now works for itself.
Where the budget is defended and the headcount climbs and the mission is what is said, not done.

## Load Bearing

Without it the capture would be a change of management, not of purpose.
With it the organisation's aim itself shifts: the turn inward is the body, now run by those devoted to it, spending its effort on its own survival and comfort, so growth, budget, and continuity become the operative goals and the mission is retained as the story. The Turn Inward is load bearing because it is the law's end state (Michels; Downs): it is produced by sincere custodian and gamer alike, since both serve the body, so it needs no decision to abandon the mission, only a house full of hands that were selected for serving the organisation.

## Apparent

A healthy, growing institution.
A body with a defended budget, a rising headcount, and a strong internal life looks like an organisation thriving, so the turn inward reads as success, and the truth that the strength is spent on itself, that the mission it names is no longer the mission it serves, stays hidden behind every metric of institutional health.

## Yearbook

Produced by [the custodian](persona_the_custodian.md) through [tending](process_tending.md), the end [Protect the Institution](plan_protect_the_institution.md) reaches at scale.
It is the goal displaced by the body: the Turn Inward is where [the capture](piece_the_capture.md) leads and what [the drain](piece_the_drain.md) leaves, the inward face whose outward cost the served pays.
