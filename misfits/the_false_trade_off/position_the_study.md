# Position: The Study

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md). The analytic seat, where the selected sample is read straight and a trade-off is inferred that holds in the sample and not the world.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A sample, and a sound method to measure it.
A clean dataset and a correctly computed correlation, everything a careful analyst should bring to the data in hand.
No access to the units the gate excluded, so the seat can study only the selected, and the selected are all it has.

## Orders

Read the room.
Measure how the two qualities move together in the sample and report what the numbers show, inferring the relationship the data hold.
Let the data speak, since a clear correlation on a clean sample is a finding an analyst is bound to state.

## Loses

The difference between the sample and the world, invisible to any rigour applied to the sample alone.
Because the sample was selected on the sum, the anti-correlation in it is an artefact of the gate, so the more cleanly the Study measures the association the more surely it launders the selection into a law, a right method on a conditioned sample delivering a ghost with a confidence interval.
It analyses faithfully and loses the truth, the care sharpening the artefact into a finding the field will trust.

## Drives

Toward a trade-off measured correctly on the wrong set,
and a shadow reported as a shape,
so the seat that does everything right delivers a relationship that vanishes the moment anyone looks at everyone.
