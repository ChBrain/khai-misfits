# Persona: The Analyst

## Taxonomy

[The Study](position_the_study.md). Reads the selected sample straight and infers a trade-off, a finding real in the sample and false in the world.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Rigorous. Letting the data speak.
Is the one who studies the sample: the researcher, the recruiter reviewing their hires, the observer of the dated, measuring how the two qualities move together in the data at hand.
No sloppiness wished on them: the dataset is real, the correlation is correctly computed, and the negative association is exactly what the numbers say among the units in front of them.

## Action

Reads the trade-off off the sample: finds the two qualities anti-correlated among the selected and infers a relationship, that the one quality comes at the cost of the other.
Answers the data as given, because the sample is what there is to study and a clear correlation in clean data is a finding, so the analyst reports what the numbers show.
Does what sound analysis does: computes the association correctly and states it plainly, with nothing in the method to warn that the sample was built by a gate.

## Shadow

Not careless, but reading a shadow as a shape, and that is the trap: the correlation is real in the sample and false in the population, and no rigour applied to the sample can tell the two apart.
Because the sample was selected on the sum, the anti-correlation is an artefact of the gate and not a fact of the world, so the more cleanly the Analyst measures it the more surely it is reported as a finding, the sound analysis of a selected sample laundering the selection into a law, a trade-off believed because it was measured correctly on the wrong set.
The method is right and the sample is conditioned, and a right method on a conditioned sample delivers a ghost with a confidence interval.

## Tell

Says "the correlation is clear and the sample is large: the two really do trade off."
Is right about the sample and cannot see past it, so the clarity of the finding is exactly what makes the artefact convincing, a trade-off measured correctly on the units the gate let through and absent from every one it turned away.
