# Plan: Take the Best

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Bar](position_the_bar.md), inside [The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Bar](position_the_bar.md)

## Direction

Select on the combination. Set a threshold on the two qualities together and admit whoever clears it, letting a surplus in one buy past a shortfall in the other, because ranking on more than one thing at once means weighing them combined. Take the best on the sum, since demanding both at their peak is neither fair nor sensible, and the gate should not care which quality carried a unit through.

## Orders

([The Selector](persona_the_selector.md) admits on the sum of two independent qualities, making the gate a common effect both feed, and conditions the sample on it in the very act of choosing.)

## Implementation

([The Intake](place_the_intake.md); the [gating](process_gating.md) process; the Selector sets [the cutoff](piece_the_cutoff.md) on [the traits](piece_the_traits.md), so the gate is the common effect [the collider](piece_the_collider.md) rests on.)

## Targets

- [ ] a threshold is set on the two qualities together and whoever clears it is admitted
- [ ] a shortfall in one quality is bought by a surplus in the other, the gate blind to which
- [ ] the gate is a common effect of both qualities, and selecting on it conditions on it
- [ ] the admitted low on one quality are exactly those high on the other, the two tied together
