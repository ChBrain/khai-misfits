# Place: The Sample

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md). The selected pool, where the induced anti-correlation appears and is read as a finding.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The dataset of the admitted, the cohort of hires, the shortlist, the people dated, the papers published, the clean table of whoever cleared the bar.
The Analyst measuring how the two qualities move together across the selected, the scatter of the chosen sloping the wrong way.

## Holds

The trade-off, real here and nowhere else.
Here only the admitted are present, so the Sample holds the artefact whole: because a unit low on one quality is in the pool only if it was high on the other, the two slope apart across the selected, an anti-correlation that is a fact of the sample and a fiction of the world, complete and correctly measurable and false.
The Sample is where the shadow looks exactly like a shape, a clean dataset carrying a relationship the selection put there.

## Offers

A clean dataset, and a clear correlation to read off it.
Because the Sample is the selected pool made legible, it offers the analyst real, well-measured data and a plain association, handing out everything a sound analysis needs, and in the same motion it offers a correlation conditioned on the gate, the clean data and the manufactured trade-off delivered together, the more convincing for how correctly it can be measured.

## Withheld

The population it was drawn from, and any sign the correlation is an artefact.
The Sample can show the admitted in full detail, but it withholds everyone the gate excluded, and, the correlation reading so cleanly, it withholds any mark that would tell the analyst the association is a shadow of the selection, so it hands over a trade-off that looks like a law and can be undone only by picturing the units the Sample does not contain.
