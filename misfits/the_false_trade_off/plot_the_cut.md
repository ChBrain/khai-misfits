# Plot: The Cut

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Intake](place_the_intake.md), a field of applicants and two qualities to weigh.
A selector who must rank on more than one thing, and a bar to be set on the two together.

## Action

[Gating](process_gating.md): whoever clears the threshold on the sum is admitted, a surplus in one quality buying past a shortfall in the other.

## Stage

[The Selector](persona_the_selector.md), on [Take the Best](plan_take_the_best.md), sets [the cutoff](piece_the_cutoff.md) on [the traits](piece_the_traits.md) and keeps whoever passes, so the gate is the common effect [the collider](piece_the_collider.md) rests on, the admitted low on one quality being exactly those high on the other.
It is the only fair way to select on two things at once, and there is no bad judgement in it.

## Tension

Selecting on the combination is the fair rule, and the fair rule is what conditions the sample on a common effect.
There is nothing careless: ranking on two qualities means weighing them together, and letting one make up for the other is exactly right. But a gate both qualities feed is a collider, so the plot closes with the sample selected on the sum and the two qualities already bound within it, a fair cut that has quietly tied two unrelated things together among the ones it kept.
