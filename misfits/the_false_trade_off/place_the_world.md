# Place: The World

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md). The population and the outcome, where the two qualities are unrelated and the acted-on trade-off spends its cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The whole population beyond the gate, where the two qualities vary freely and independently, and the results of the decision built on the sample, the narrowed intake and the outcome it returns.
The Decider's new rule running against reality, the compensating units no longer admitted and the yield of the policy coming in flat or worse.

## Holds

The true independence, and the loss when the artefact is acted on.
Here the two qualities have nothing to do with each other, so the World holds what the sample hid: no trade-off at all across everyone, and the reckoning when a rule built on the false one is set loose, the range the wide gate had admitted now screened away for a relationship that was never here.
The World is where the ghost is either dispelled, by looking at everyone, or paid for, by acting as if the sample were the world.

## Offers

The ground truth, and the bill for ignoring it.
Because the World holds the whole population, it offers the one view that dissolves the trade-off, the qualities seen unrelated across everyone, handing out the correction free to anyone who looks past the gate, and, unlooked at, it offers instead the cost of the acted-on artefact, the disappointing outcome that is the only way the missing units make themselves felt.

## Withheld

Its own visibility, until the loss arrives.
The World can dissolve the trade-off at a glance, but it withholds that glance from anyone who studies only the sample, the excluded units being invisible by construction, so it withholds every warning but the last, the flat result of the narrowed rule, and lets the ghost hold until the decision built on it comes back empty.
