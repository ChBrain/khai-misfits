# Piece: The Traits

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md). The two qualities that are independent in the population and share nothing but the gate they both feed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Vary freely across [the world](place_the_world.md), are weighed together at [the intake](place_the_intake.md), and slope apart in [the sample](place_the_sample.md).
Flow through every unit as its two measured qualities, unrelated at large and bound only among those the gate selected.

## Load Bearing

Without two of them, and two that are truly unrelated, there is no common effect to select on and no association to manufacture, and the whole trap has nothing to bend.
With them the collider has its causes: two qualities that a fair gate weighs together, so that selecting on the sum ties them where nothing ties them in the world. The Traits are load bearing because they are the two independent causes the paradox needs (Berkson): unrelated everywhere and forced apart among the selected, so the piece carries the raw material of the artefact, the pair whose real independence the sample hides and whose spurious opposition the analyst reads.

## Apparent

Just two rival qualities, the one bought at the cost of the other, a trade any observer of the sample would take for real.
Seen at the surface, the Traits look like competitors, beauty against wit, talent against diligence, and the truth that they are unrelated across everyone, that their opposition is a shadow of the selection and not a fact of the world, stays hidden behind how naturally two anti-correlated qualities read as opposed.

## Yearbook

Borne by every unit, weighed by [the Selector](persona_the_selector.md), measured by [the Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md), and screened by [the Decider](persona_the_decider.md), the pair the whole trap turns on.
They are the causes of the trap: the Traits are what [the cutoff](piece_the_cutoff.md) is set on, what [inducing](process_inducing.md) forces apart, and what [the trade-off](piece_the_trade_off.md) falsely opposes, their real [independence](piece_the_independence.md) the thing the whole [collider](piece_the_collider.md) hides.
