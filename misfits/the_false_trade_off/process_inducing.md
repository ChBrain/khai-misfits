# Process: Inducing

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md). The conditioning that forces the two independent qualities apart among the selected, the anti-correlation the collider opens.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The gate, once the sample is kept, and the arithmetic of clearing a bar on the sum.
At [the sample](place_the_sample.md), a unit low on one quality is present only if it was high on the other, so across the admitted the two slope apart, the association induced by the selection and not by any tie in the world.

## Direction

Toward an anti-correlation among the selected.
Because entry required the pair to clear the threshold, inducing points the two qualities against each other within the sample: where one is low the other must have been high to compensate, so the selected trace a trade-off, an association that is a mathematical consequence of conditioning on the common effect and has no counterpart in the population.

## Lever

The compensation the bar demands.
Because clearing a threshold on the sum lets one quality make up for the other, inducing runs on that substitution alone, so no hidden cause and no real relationship is needed to bend the two apart, only the fact that among those who passed, a shortfall in one was paid for by a surplus in the other, the collider opening a path that closes the instant the excluded are counted.

## Echo

A trade-off written into the sample, real and false at once.
What leaves this process is a selected pool in which the two qualities are anti-correlated, a pattern correctly there in the data and absent from everyone the gate turned away, so the artefact sits complete and measurable in the Sample, waiting for a reader who will take a shadow for a shape.
