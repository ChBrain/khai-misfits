# Position: The Bar

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md). The selecting seat, where units are admitted on the sum of two independent qualities, making the gate a common effect.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Two criteria and one threshold across them.
A fair rule: take whatever unit is strong enough on the two qualities combined, so a shortfall in one can be bought by a surplus in the other.
No way to rank on two things at once without weighing them together, and no reason to demand both at their peak, so the seat admits on the sum.

## Orders

Take the best.
Set a bar on the two qualities together and admit whoever clears it, letting one strength carry a unit past a weakness in the other.
Choose on the combination, since that is the only fair way to select on more than one thing at a time.

## Loses

The independence of the two qualities, tied together the moment the gate is set on their sum.
Because entry requires the pair to clear the bar, the admitted who are low on one are exactly those high on the other, so the Bar, choosing fairly on the combination, is a common effect fed by both qualities, and selecting on it conditions on it.
It admits sensibly and loses the very unrelatedness the population held, handing on a sample in which an association has been manufactured.

## Drives

Toward a sample selected on a common effect,
and two independent qualities forced apart within it,
so the seat that fairly takes the best is the seat that builds the trade-off the analyst will find.
