# Plot: The Reading

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Sample](place_the_sample.md), the anti-correlation sitting clean in the data.
An analyst with a sound method, and a correlation that reads straight off the selected.

## Action

[Inferring](process_inferring.md): the trade-off is measured correctly and read as a relationship in the world.

## Stage

[The Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md), on [Read the Room](plan_read_the_room.md), measures [the trade-off](piece_the_trade_off.md) on [the traits](piece_the_traits.md) and reports that the one quality costs the other, missing [the independence](piece_the_independence.md) that holds across everyone the sample does not contain.
The measurement is correct and the sample is conditioned, and nothing in the method can tell the two apart.

## Tension

Reading a clear correlation off clean data is exactly what sound analysis does, and doing it right is what launders the selection into a law.
There is no error: the dataset is real, the association correctly computed, the finding precisely what the numbers show. But the numbers are the selected, so the plot closes with a shadow reported as a shape, a trade-off stated as a fact of the world and carried out of a sample that was built to contain it, the rigour of the reading making the artefact only more convincing.
