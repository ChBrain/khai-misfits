# Process: Inferring

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md). The reading of the sample straight, the induced trade-off inferred as a fact of the world.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Analyst, with a clean sample and a sound method.
At [the sample](place_the_sample.md), the anti-correlation is measured correctly and read as a relationship, the two qualities reported to trade off because that is what the numbers in hand show.

## Direction

Toward a false finding believed as a law.
Because the correlation is real in the sample, inferring points the analysis at a conclusion about the world: the two qualities are reported to move against each other, and nothing in a correct measurement of a conditioned sample marks the result an artefact, so a shadow is stated as a shape and the trade-off is carried out of the data as a fact.

## Lever

A right method on the wrong set.
Because the sample is clean and the correlation correctly computed, inferring runs on sound analysis, so no error and no sloppiness is needed to reach the false finding, only the trust that a clear pattern in good data is a fact of the world, and the more rigorously the sample is read the more convincingly the selection is laundered into a law.

## Echo

A trade-off reported as real, with a confidence interval around it.
What leaves this process is a finding the field will cite, a relationship measured correctly on the selected and false across everyone else, so the artefact of the gate has become a stated law of the world, sound-looking and believed and waiting to be acted on.
