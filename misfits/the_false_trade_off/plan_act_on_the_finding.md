# Plan: Act on the Finding

## Taxonomy

## Direction

Turn the finding into a rule. Screen on the one quality because the analysis found it costs the other, drop the second criterion as redundant, and retune the next selection to the measured trade-off, because a clear result is a reason to stop paying for both. Act on the evidence, since a responsible decider builds the policy a peer-reviewed finding implies.
