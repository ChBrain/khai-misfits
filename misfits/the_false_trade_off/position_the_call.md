# Position: The Call

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md). The deciding seat, where the trade-off is acted on as a fact of the world and the compensations the gate admitted are spent.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A finding, and the power to build a rule on it.
A clear, measured trade-off and the mandate to act on evidence, the means to retune the next selection to what the analysis found.
No sign in the finding that it is an artefact, so the seat has only a sound-looking result and the duty to use it.

## Orders

Act on the finding.
Screen on the one quality because it seems to cost the other, drop the second criterion as redundant, and set the gate to the law the study reported.
Let the evidence guide the rule, since a measured trade-off is a reason to stop paying for both.

## Loses

The compensations the wide gate had quietly admitted, screened away by the narrowed rule.
Because the qualities were independent all along and the gate's only trick was to let in the ones who made up for a shortfall, acting on the trade-off stops admitting them, so the Call destroys the artefact by narrowing the gate and takes real value with it, a policy built on a relationship the world never held.
It acts responsibly and loses the range, the sound decision on a false finding turned into a lasting loss.

## Drives

Toward a rule built on a shadow,
and a range spent to chase it,
so the seat that acts on the evidence is the one that converts a harmless artefact into a real cost.
