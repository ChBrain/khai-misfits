# Plan: Read the Room

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Study](position_the_study.md), inside [The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Study](position_the_study.md)

## Direction

Measure the sample and report what it holds. Compute how the two qualities move together among the selected and state the relationship the data show, because a clear correlation on a clean dataset is a finding an analyst is bound to report. Read the room in hand, since the excluded units are not in the data and the sample is all there is to study.

## Orders

([The Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md) reads the anti-correlation off the selected sample and infers a trade-off, a finding real in the sample and false in the world, with nothing in the method to warn of the gate.)

## Implementation

([The Sample](place_the_sample.md); the [inducing](process_inducing.md) process met by the [inferring](process_inferring.md) that reads it; the Analyst measures [the trade-off](piece_the_trade_off.md) on [the traits](piece_the_traits.md) and misses [the independence](piece_the_independence.md) the population holds.)

## Targets

- [ ] the anti-correlation among the selected is measured correctly on a clean sample
- [ ] the trade-off is inferred as a relationship in the world, not an artefact of the gate
- [ ] no rigour applied to the sample alone can tell the shadow from the shape
