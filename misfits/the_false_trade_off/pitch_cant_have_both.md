# Pitch: Can't Have Both

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Analytic, even-handed, evidence-led.
The plain sense that the data show a trade-off: across the whole sample the two qualities move against each other, the correlation is clear and the method is clean, so beauty really does seem to cost wit and talent to spend diligence, a pattern any careful analyst would report.
Nothing sloppy in it: the selection was sensible, the dataset is real, the correlation is correctly computed, and the finding is exactly what the numbers say.

## Undertow

Under the clean analysis, a pattern the selection put there.
Because the sample was chosen on the sum of two qualities that are unrelated in the world, a unit short on one is present only if it was long on the other, so among the chosen the two are forced apart and the trade-off appears, an association conditioned into being by the gate and absent from everyone the gate turned away.
The correlation that reads as a fact of the world is a shadow cast by the selection, real in the sample and false in the population, and it dissolves the instant the analyst pictures the units that never made it in.

## Nerve

The feeling of a trade-off so plainly in the data it must be real, a pattern the numbers simply hand you,
because the selection was reasonable and the correlation is correctly measured and nothing in the sample looks wrong,
while underneath the gate that took the best on the sum has quietly tied two unrelated qualities together, and the field reads a law of the world off the shape of its own selection.

## Echo

After the run: a field sure that the one quality costs the other, spending real value on a trade-off that was never in the world, the compensations the gate had admitted screened away by the belief the gate created,
no fraud in the story and no fallacy, only a sensible selection and a sound reading of it,
and the one move that would break it, reasoning about the sample the selection built rather than the sample in hand, refused because the excluded are invisible and a collider reads exactly like a confounder one is trained to control, so the ghost holds and the trade-off is believed without anyone ever getting a number wrong.
