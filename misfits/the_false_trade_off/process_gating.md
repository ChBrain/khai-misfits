# Process: Gating

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md). The selection on the sum of two qualities, making the gate a common effect and conditioning the sample on it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Selector, with two criteria and one threshold across them.
At [the intake](place_the_intake.md), whoever clears a bar on the two qualities together is admitted, a surplus in one buying past a shortfall in the other, the gate blind to which quality carried each unit through.

## Direction

Toward a sample conditioned on a common effect.
Because ranking on two things means weighing them combined, gating points the selection at the sum: the admitted are those whose two qualities together cleared the bar, so the gate is an effect both qualities feed, and keeping only the admitted conditions the sample on that effect, the fair combination becoming the collider the whole trap rests on.

## Lever

The fairness of selecting on the combination.
Because it would be neither fair nor sensible to demand both qualities at their peak, gating runs on the plainly correct rule to weigh them together, so no bad judgement is needed to build the collider, only the sound instinct to take the best overall, and the gate ties two unrelated qualities together in the very act of being fair to both.

## Echo

A sample selected on the sum, its qualities already forced together.
What leaves this process is a pool of the admitted conditioned on the gate, so the two qualities that vary freely in the world are, among the kept, selected together on the effect they both feed, and the sample is handed to the next process conditioned on the gate, the association it will show not yet drawn out or measured and no one having put it there on purpose.
