# Place: The Intake

## Taxonomy

[The False Trade-Off](play_the_false_trade_off.md). The gate, where units are admitted on the sum of two independent qualities and the common effect is conditioned on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The admissions desk, the hiring shortlist, the editor's acceptance, the date said yes to, wherever a bar is set on two qualities together and applicants are kept or turned away.
The Selector weighing the pair in combination, a plain candidate carried by charm, a modest scorer by a stellar essay, each let through because the sum cleared the bar.

## Holds

The selection on the sum, and the collider it makes.
Here the gate is fed by two arrows, so the Intake holds the first move of the trap: whoever is admitted cleared a threshold on the two qualities together, so the admitted low on one are the ones high on the other, and choosing on the combination conditions the sample on a common effect, the fair rule quietly tying two unrelated qualities together among those it keeps.
The Intake is where the ghost is conceived, in the sensible act of taking the best on the pair.

## Offers

The best on the combination, and a sample selected on it.
Because the Intake ranks fairly on two qualities at once, it offers the selector a defensible cut, the strong-overall kept and the weak-overall turned away, handing out exactly the fairness selecting on more than one thing requires, and in the same motion it hands on a sample conditioned on the gate, the fair selection and the manufactured association delivered together.

## Withheld

The units it turned away, and the independence they would have shown.
The Intake can admit whoever clears the bar, but it withholds everyone below it, the very units whose presence would show the two qualities unrelated, so it withholds the one thing that would break the trade-off, a view of the excluded, and hands on a sample that reads a relationship the turned-away would have dissolved.
