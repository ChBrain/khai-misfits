# Persona: The Selector

## Taxonomy

[The Bar](position_the_bar.md). Admits whatever clears a bar on two independent qualities together, making the gate a common effect both qualities feed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Discerning. Fair. Taking the best.
Is the one who sets the gate: the admissions office, the hiring panel, the editor, the one dating, choosing whoever is strong enough on the two qualities combined.
No caprice wished on them: selecting on the sum is the sensible rule, a plain-looking candidate admitted on charm and a modest scorer on a stellar essay, each let in because the combination clears the bar.

## Action

Admits on the sum: takes whatever unit is high enough on the two qualities together, letting one strength buy past a shortfall in the other.
Answers the only fair way to rank on more than one thing, weighing the qualities in combination rather than demanding both at their peak, so the chosen are those who cleared the bar however they cleared it.
Does what any selector must with two criteria: sets a threshold on the pair and keeps whoever passes, the gate blind to which quality carried each one through.

## Shadow

Not unfair, but the maker of a common effect, and that is the quiet of it: a gate fed by two arrows is a collider, and selecting on it is conditioning on it.
Because entry required the sum to clear the bar, the admitted who are low on one quality are exactly those who were high on the other, so the Selector, choosing sensibly on the combination, ties two qualities together among the chosen that share nothing in the world, and hands the analyst a sample in which an association has been manufactured by the very act of selection.
Every admission is defensible, and the sum of defensible admissions is a sample that reads a trade-off the population never held.

## Tell

Says "we take the best overall: a weakness in one thing is fine if the other more than makes up for it."
Is right that selecting on the combination is fair and cannot see that it is exactly what forces the two apart among the chosen, so the sensible rule is the one that builds the ghost the analyst will find.
