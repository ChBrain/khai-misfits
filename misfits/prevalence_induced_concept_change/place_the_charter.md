# Place: The Charter

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md). Where the mark would be fixed, the definition set in advance and held though the company shifts.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The document that could pin the thing down, the standard written before the cases are seen and meant to be held against them all.
The anchor drafting it and the pull against it: the eye that would rather judge by the room, and the watch that would rather never write down that the danger might one day be small enough to pass.

## Holds

The anchoring, and the resistance to it.
Here the move to fix the standard meets the instinct to keep it live, so the Charter holds the point where the remedy meets its price: a definition set in advance would let progress be seen, and it means calling the mild case fine and the count's fall a win, so it is where holding the line still is bought against the reflex never to sound the all-clear.

## Offers

A standard that does not drift.
Because the Charter is where the mark can be fixed before the cases come, it offers the one defence against the creep, so it hands out a yardstick the widening company cannot bend, and hands it out at the cost of the vigilance it looks like abandoning, so the fixed mark is exact and the exactness reads, to the watch, as letting go.

## Withheld

A fixed mark that feels like vigilance, or that can never be wrong.
The Charter cannot make holding the line still feel like anything but standing down, so it withholds the cover the anchor would want, and, a standard set once, it withholds any guarantee the mark was set right or will not itself go stale, so the remedy is real and costly and never quite safe.
