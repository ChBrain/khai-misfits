## Load Bearing

Without it the creep is a curiosity, a definition quietly widening with no one the worse for it.
With it the misfit has its harm: because the recategorised cases fill the gap the vanished ones leave, the count never falls, so the win is invisible and the effort, reading a flat tally as an unbeaten problem, drives on into milder and milder cases. The Mirage is load bearing because it is the outcome that makes the drift a trap and not a footnote (Levari et al.; the overdiagnosis case): it is progress made unreadable and effort made misdirected, so the piece carries the receding target that keeps the watch chasing what it has already largely won.

## Apparent

An unsolved problem, a danger the numbers show is as bad as ever.
Seen at the ledger, the mirage looks like hard evidence that the fight is not won, a level count demanding the effort press on, and the truth that the count is flat only because the concept crept, that the real cases have fallen behind the unchanging number, stays hidden behind how solid a steady tally looks to those who steer by it.
