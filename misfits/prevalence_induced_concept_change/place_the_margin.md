# Place: The Margin

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md). The edge of the concept, where the line drifts outward and the borderline case is swept in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The frontier of the category, the band where a case is neither plainly the thing nor plainly fine.
The line sitting here today and a little further out tomorrow, and the borderline case, once safely outside, now standing within as the company around it mildens.

## Holds

The creeping, and the case it catches.
Here the concept meets the cases it is unsure of, so the Margin holds the point where a shift in company becomes a shift in kind: as the severe cases thin, the edge slides out to where the mild ones are, so it is where a case that changed not at all is recategorised as the thing, moved across the line by the line moving.

## Offers

A verdict for the doubtful case.
Because the Margin is where the unsure cases are settled, it offers the concept a way to resolve its edge, so it hands out a ruling on the borderline, and hands it out by a boundary set from the current company, so the same case gets one verdict when the true cases are common and the opposite when they are rare.

## Withheld

An edge that stays where it was.
The Margin cannot fix its own frontier while the company that sets it shifts, so it withholds the stable boundary that would keep the borderline case where it was last year, and, being only ever the current edge, it withholds any record that the edge has moved, so the creep leaves no mark and the newly-caught case looks as though it was always the thing.
