# Piece: The Borderline

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md). The recategorised case, the formerly-fine instance swept in as the thing in good faith.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Caught at [the margin](place_the_margin.md), where the drifting line reaches out to it.
Judged from [the bench](place_the_bench.md), where the milder company makes it read as the thing.

## Load Bearing

Without it the creep is an abstraction, a boundary moving over nothing, with no case to show the harm.
With it the drift is made concrete: a specific case that changed not at all is called fine one year and the thing the next, because the company around it mildened and the line reached out. The Borderline is load bearing because it is the creep made visible in a single instance (Levari et al.): it is the case whose verdict flips though the case did not, so the piece carries the innocent example the widened concept sweeps in, the proof that the standard, not the case, is what moved.

## Apparent

A genuine instance of the thing, correctly flagged like any other.
Seen in the moment, the borderline case looks like a fair catch, the assessor doing its job, and the truth that the very same case would have passed without comment when the true cases were common, that it was recategorised by a shift in company and not in itself, stays hidden behind how sincerely the widened eye sees it as the thing.

## Yearbook

Swept in by [the assessor](persona_the_assessor.md) through [creeping](process_creeping.md), the fine case caught by the drifting [line](piece_the_line.md).
It is the creep made flesh: the Borderline is what the widened concept takes in as [the ebb](persona_the_ebb.md) thins the true cases, and each one caught holds the count level as [the mirage](piece_the_mirage.md), the harm that only [the fixed mark](piece_the_fixed_mark.md) would have spared it.
