# Persona: The Anchor

## Taxonomy

[Anchor](position_anchor.md). Would fix the mark, an absolute standard set in advance, against the relative eye and the reflex never to stand down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady. Exact. Willing to say the unwelcome number.
Sees the concept creeping and moves to pin the thing to a definition set before the cases are seen, held though the company shifts.
No complacency in it: only someone who holds that a problem you cannot see shrink is a problem you cannot know you are winning.

## Action

Fixes the mark: defines the thing absolutely, counts against a standard set in advance, and holds the line though the recent company mildens.
Refuses to let the boundary drift with context, so a case fine last year is fine this year.
Does the one thing that lets progress be seen: keeps the yardstick still.

## Shadow

Not a saviour: the fixed mark fights the way the eye works, and holding it costs exactly the vigilance it looks like betraying.
Because pinning the standard means calling the mild case fine when that feels like complacency, and reading the falling count as victory when declaring victory looks like calling off the watch too soon and can defund the very effort that won, the Anchor cannot make progress visible without seeming to stop caring, and a mark fixed once can itself go stale or be set wrong.
It buys a win that can be seen, at the price of looking like the one who said the danger had passed.

## Tell

Says "define it in advance and hold the line, or you will chase it forever."
Is right, and being right means being called complacent by everyone whose eye has quietly moved.
