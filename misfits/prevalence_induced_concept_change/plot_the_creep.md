# Plot: The Creep

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Margin](place_the_margin.md), the same eye meeting a milder company.
The severe cases thinned, and the assessor, judging relatively, reaching its line further out.

## Action

[Creeping](process_creeping.md): [The Assessor](persona_the_assessor.md), holding [the assessor's seat](position_assessor.md), sets its line outward to fill the space the true cases left, while [The Ebb](persona_the_ebb.md), holding [the ebb's seat](position_ebb.md), has drawn that space open.

## Stage

[The Assessor](persona_the_assessor.md), still on [Judge by the Light](plan_judge_by_the_light.md), judges the milder company as strictly as ever and so calls cases the thing it once would have passed.
[The Borderline](piece_the_borderline.md), a case plainly fine last year, now sits within the drifted line and is swept in, in perfect good faith.

## Tension

The standard widens by just what the danger fell, and no one moved it.
There is still no bias: the assessor feels exactly as strict, judging honestly against the run before it, and the run is simply milder now. But relative judgement against a softened company reaches the line outward, so the plot closes with the concept crept wide enough to take in the plainly fine, the eye as sure as ever it has not shifted.
