# Place: The Ledger

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md). Where the count is kept and progress is read, and where the flat tally hides the win.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The tally of cases found, the running count the effort watches to know whether it is winning.
The true cases falling in reality, and the number on the page holding level, because every case the ebb removes the creeping line quietly replaces with a milder one.

## Holds

The ebbing and the mirage, the real fall and the flat count that conceals it.
Here the genuine progress meets the widened concept, so the Ledger holds the point where a win becomes invisible: the true cases drop and the recategorised ones fill the gap, so the tally never moves, and it is where a solved problem is read, in good faith, as an unsolved one.

## Offers

A number to steer by.
Because the Ledger is where the effort reads its progress, it offers the seeker the count it needs to judge the fight, so it hands out the measure everyone trusts, and hands out a measure held flat by the moving line, so the harder the problem is beaten the more the number insists it is not.

## Withheld

The truth of the trend beneath the count.
The Ledger cannot show the fall in true cases while the widened concept holds the total level, so it withholds the real progress from the number that is supposed to carry it, and, counting only what the drifting line lets through, it withholds the very success it was kept to record, so the win is real on the ground and absent on the page.
