## Action

## Stage

## Tension

The good news is the seed of the trap.
There is nothing to regret: the danger really is receding, exactly the win the work was for, and no one would undo it. But the assessor judges by recent company and that company has softened, so the plot closes with the thing genuinely rarer and the eye's reference quietly milder, the progress real and the ground for the creep laid.
