# Persona: The Assessor

## Taxonomy

[Assessor](position_assessor.md). Judges each case fairly against recent company, so its line drifts out as the true cases fall.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Fair. Sure of its own consistency.
Sits to find the thing and judge each case honestly, reading every one in the light of the ones it has lately seen.
No looseness in it: only an eye that feels, from the inside, to hold one unchanging standard.

## Action

Calls each case as it comes, weighing it against the recent run rather than an absolute rule.
Flags the thing where it sees it and lets the fine case pass, by a line that feels fixed.
Does what a fair comparative judge does: reads the new case in the light of the old.

## Shadow

Not biased, relative: judging by context is how perception works and mostly serves it well, and each call feels as strict as the last.
Because it reads each case against recent company and the company grows milder as the true cases fall, the same honest eye sets its line further out, so its fairness is exactly what widens the concept, and it cannot feel the drift because from the inside the standard never moves.
It is as strict as ever and catching ever milder cases, and the two are the same fact.

## Tell

Says "I judge them all by the same standard I always have."
Does, sincerely, and the standard has crept outward by just the amount the danger receded.
