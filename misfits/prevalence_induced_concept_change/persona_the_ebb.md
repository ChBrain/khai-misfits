# Persona: The Ebb

## Taxonomy

[Ebb](position_ebb.md). The falling prevalence of true cases, genuine progress, which by receding mildens the company the eye judges by.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Quiet. Benign. Going out like a tide.
Is the real reduction of the thing as the work succeeds, the true cases growing fewer, the danger genuinely answered.
No agent behind it and no ill in it: only good news, the problem actually shrinking.

## Action

Draws the true cases down, thinning the severe from the run the assessor sees.
Leaves behind a company grown milder, the reference against which every new case is now weighed.
Does the one thing everyone wanted: makes the thing rarer.

## Shadow

Not a harm, a good, and that is the whole cruelty of it: the creep rides on progress, not on failure.
Because the assessor judges by recent company, the Ebb's genuine success is what mildens that company and pulls the line outward, so the very receding of the danger is what widens the concept of it, and the better the Ebb does its benign work the less the win can be seen.
It is progress that blinds, the good news that erases itself in the reading.

## Tell

Says "the danger is fading, the real cases are fewer every year."
Is right, and its rightness is exactly what moves the line to swallow the cases that were never the danger.
