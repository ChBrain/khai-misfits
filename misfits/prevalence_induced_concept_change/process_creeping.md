# Process: Creeping

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md). The widening of the concept as the true cases fall, the line drifting out to fill the space they left.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A comparative eye meeting a milder company.
At [the margin](place_the_margin.md), the recent run softened by the ebb, and the same standard, judged relatively, reaching further out.

## Direction

Toward a concept widened by just what the prevalence fell.
Because the eye judges by recent company and the company has mildened, creeping drives the line of the thing outward to where the mild cases now sit, so the category grows to fill the space the true cases left, and the tally at [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md) holds level as the recategorised replace the vanished.

## Lever

The invisibility of the drift to the eye that makes it.
Because the assessor feels one fixed standard while its reference moves, the widening happens with no sense of widening, so creeping runs on an honest eye's blind spot, the concept ballooning with every judgement and no judgement ever registering the change.

## Echo

A concept grown wider, and no one who moved it.
The line now takes in cases that were plainly fine and the assessor is as sure as ever it has not shifted, so what reads as the same standard steadily applied is, unseen, a boundary that has crept out to swallow milder and milder ground.
