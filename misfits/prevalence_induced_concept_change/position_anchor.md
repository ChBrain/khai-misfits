# Position: Anchor

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md). The seat that would fix the mark in advance, against the relative eye and the reflex never to stand down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A means to fix the standard: a definition set before the cases are seen, a count held though the company shifts.
A clear case: a problem you cannot see shrink is one you cannot know you are winning.
No way to make the fixed mark feel natural to the eye, and no cover from the charge of complacency.

## Orders

Fix the mark.
Define the thing absolutely, count against a standard set in advance, and hold the line though the recent company mildens.
Keep the yardstick still, so a case fine last year is fine this year.

## Loses

The look of vigilance, exactly by making progress visible.
Because holding the mark means calling the mild case fine when that reads as complacency, and reading the falling count as victory when declaring victory looks like calling off the watch too soon and can defund the effort that won, the seat cannot make the win seeable without seeming to stop caring, and a mark set once can itself be wrong or go stale.
It buys a progress that can be seen, at the price of being called the one who lowered the guard.

## Drives

Toward a mark fixed in advance,
holding the line against the eye's drift and the watch's reflex,
so the one seat that would let the win be seen must look, to the vigilant, like surrender.
