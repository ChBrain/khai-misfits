# Process: Anchoring

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md). The fixing of the mark against the drift, the standard set in advance and held though the company shifts, at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A concept seen to be creeping, and someone unwilling to chase a receding line.
At [the charter](place_the_charter.md), the anchor moving to define the thing before the cases are seen and to count against that fixed standard.

## Direction

Toward a line held still while the company moves.
The anchor pins the definition in advance and refuses to let the boundary travel with context, so anchoring drives the standard to stay put though the recent cases milden, letting the fall at [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md) show as the win it is.

## Lever

The plain truth that a win you cannot see is a win you cannot bank.
Because a problem never seen to shrink is one you can neither know you are beating nor rightly stand down from, the case to fix the mark is sound, so anchoring runs on the same care that drives the watch, turned to the harder discipline of holding the yardstick still.

## Echo

A standard that holds, and a vigilance that feels betrayed.
The line is pinned and the falling count can be read at last, and holding it has meant calling the mild case fine and the danger's retreat a victory, so what reads as letting the guard down is, in truth, the one move that lets the guard see it has already won.
