# Piece: The Fixed Mark

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md). The remedy, the absolute standard set in advance that stops the creep, at the cost of calling the mild case fine.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set at [the charter](place_the_charter.md), where the definition is pinned before the cases are seen.
Held against [the margin](place_the_margin.md), the drifting edge it exists to keep still.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play has no answer, and the line creeps on to swallow ever milder ground.
With it the misfit has its costly cure: a standard defined in advance and counted against though the company mildens is the one thing that holds the boundary still and lets the falling count read as a win. The Fixed Mark is load bearing because it is the remedy that carries its own price (implied by the finding): it stops the creep only by forcing the mild case to be called fine and the danger's retreat to be named a victory, so the piece carries both the cure for the drift and the vigilance the cure looks like betraying.

## Apparent

Complacency, a lowering of the guard, a rule that would let the mild case slip.
Seen by the watch, the fixed mark looks like standing down, a refusal to stay strict as the eye has grown, and the truth that it is the only defence against a concept that widens forever, that holding the line still is what lets progress be seen at all, stays hidden behind how much like giving up an unmoving standard feels to an eye that has quietly moved.

## Yearbook

Set by [the anchor](persona_the_anchor.md) through [anchoring](process_anchoring.md), the standard pinned against the drift of [the line](piece_the_line.md).
It is the cure the play cannot make painless: the Fixed Mark stops the creep that sweeps in [the borderline](piece_the_borderline.md) and lifts [the mirage](piece_the_mirage.md) so the win can be seen, and cannot do so without calling the mild case fine and looking, to the vigilant, like the one who stood the watch down.
