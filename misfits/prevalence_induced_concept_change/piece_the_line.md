# Piece: The Line

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md). The concept boundary, set by recent context and drifting outward as the true cases fall.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Drawn at [the margin](place_the_margin.md), the edge where a case is called the thing or let pass.
Set from [the bench](place_the_bench.md), where the recent company the assessor judges by decides where the edge falls.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no concept to widen, and a falling prevalence is just good news plainly seen.
With it the whole trap has its fulcrum: a boundary the assessor believes fixed but which is in truth set from recent company, so when the company mildens the line travels out, and the category grows by exactly the amount the true cases fell. The Line is load bearing because it is the thing that moves while feeling still (Levari et al.): it is the relative standard mistaken for an absolute one, so the piece carries the boundary whose drift, unfelt by the eye that draws it, converts genuine progress into a wider net.

## Apparent

A fixed standard, the same rule steadily applied, a line the assessor is sure has not moved.
Seen from the bench, the line looks constant, an even criterion held across every case, and the truth that it is pinned to the recent company and travels with it, that it has widened by just what the danger receded, stays hidden behind how fixed a standard feels to the eye applying it.
