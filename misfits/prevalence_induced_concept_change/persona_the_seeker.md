# Persona: The Seeker

## Taxonomy

[Seeker](position_seeker.md). Tasked to root out the thing, reads the flat count as a problem undefeated and drives on into milder cases.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Conscientious. Tireless. Set against the danger.
Is the one charged with finding and fixing the thing, watching the count to know whether the fight is won.
No zeal for its own sake: only a duty to root out the harm and a resolve not to let up while it stands.

## Action

Reads the tally, sees it has not fallen, and presses the work harder into ever milder ground.
Acts on every case the assessor flags, treating the widened concept as the same old enemy.
Does what a conscientious warden does: keeps rooting out the thing wherever it is still being found.

## Shadow

Not overzealous, misled: the count it trusts is flat because the concept crept, not because the danger held, and it cannot see the difference from where it stands.
Because the tally holds steady while the true cases fall, the Seeker reads a solved problem as an unsolved one and drives its effort onto cases that were never the thing, the definition ballooning under its diligence, its mandate widening as the reality shrinks.
It chases a boundary that recedes as it is approached, sure the work is as needed as ever.

## Tell

Says "the numbers are not falling, so the problem is as bad as ever, and we must not let up."
Trusts the count, and the count is flat only because the eye that feeds it keeps moving the line.
