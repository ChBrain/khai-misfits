# Pitch: The Same Careful Eye

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Vigilant, fair, consistent.
The plain virtue of judging each case by the same careful eye, finding the thing wherever it truly is and acting where it is warranted.
Nothing loose in it: only an honest assessor holding, so it feels, to one unchanging standard.

## Undertow

Under the fairness, an eye that judges by recent company, so its line moves as the company does.
Because the assessor reads each case against the ones lately seen, and doing the work well makes the recent cases milder, the concept of the thing creeps outward to fill the space the true cases left, and a case once plainly fine is now flagged as the thing, in perfect good faith.
The better the problem is answered, the wider the net grows, so it is never seen to be answered at all.

## Nerve

The feeling of being as strict as ever, catching the thing by the same standard you always used,
sure the standard has not moved, because from the inside it never does,
while the real cases quietly vanish and your line drifts out to meet ever milder ones, the count holding flat as the danger recedes.

## Echo

After the run: a watch that never stands down, a problem never read as solved however much it shrank,
no one biased and no one lying, only a fair eye judging by context and a danger genuinely receding and a reformer driving conscientiously on,
and a fixed mark that would have let the win be seen, refused because holding it means calling the mild case fine, and calling the mild case fine feels like calling off the watch too soon.
