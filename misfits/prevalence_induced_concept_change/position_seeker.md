# Position: Seeker

## Taxonomy

[Prevalence-Induced Concept Change](play_prevalence_induced_concept_change.md). The seat that acts on the count, reads a flat tally as a problem undefeated, and drives on into milder cases.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A duty to root out the thing, and a tally to tell it whether the fight is being won.
A conscientious resolve: do not let up while the count says the danger stands.
No sight of the drifting line behind the count, only the count itself.

## Orders

Root it out.
Watch the tally, press the work wherever the thing is still being found, and do not stand down while the numbers hold.
Meet every flagged case as the same old danger.

## Loses

Its aim, exactly by trusting a count the creep holds flat.
Because the tally stays level while the true cases fall, the seat reads a solved problem as unsolved and drives its effort onto cases that were never the thing, so it cannot both trust the count and aim true, and its diligence is what balloons the definition and widens the mandate as the reality shrinks.
It chases a boundary that recedes as it is approached, spending most on the mildest ground.

## Drives

Toward an effort that never stands down,
conscientious and misled,
so the count it trusts to end the fight is the count the moving line keeps alive.
