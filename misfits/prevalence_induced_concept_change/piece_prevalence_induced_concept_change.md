## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without a name: a drifting line, a swept-in case, a flat count, and no account of how one becomes the next.
With it the misfit is whole: prevalence-induced concept change is the trap entire, a fair eye judging by context and a danger genuinely receding summing to a concept that balloons as the reality shrinks, the success and the blindness shown to be one thing. It is load bearing because it is the structure the whole production exists to stage (Levari et al.; Haslam): the frame that binds the comparative eye, the ebbing prevalence, the creeping line, and the receding target into a single bend, so the piece carries the phenomenon as a whole, a problem made permanent by the very progress against it.

## Apparent

A problem that stubbornly refuses to improve, proof that the danger is as grave as ever.
Seen at the surface, the phenomenon looks like sober evidence of an unbeaten harm, a definition holding firm and a count that will not fall, and the truth that the definition quietly widened as the harm receded, that no amount of vigilance can reveal the win because the eye's own drift conceals it, stays hidden behind how much like an unsolved problem a well-solved one is made to look.
