# Piece: The Two Equilibria

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The fork: a good state and a run state, both consistent with the same sound bank.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the street](place_the_street.md), where the crowd settles on one belief or the other.
Where nothing in the books decides which way the reckoning falls.

## Load Bearing

Without it a run would need a real weakness, and a sound bank would be safe.
With it the same solvent bank has two possible outcomes: one where only those who truly need cash withdraw and all is well, and one where everyone withdraws because they expect everyone to, and both are self-consistent. The Two Equilibria is load bearing because it is the multiple-equilibrium core of the model (Diamond & Dybvig): the fundamentals do not pick, belief does, so a run is not a verdict on the loans but a choice of the worse of two states the loans left open.

## Apparent

Just confidence, or the lack of it.
It looks like mood, a bank that is fine when people feel fine and troubled when they feel troubled, when in truth it is a hard structural fact that two stable outcomes exist and the crowd selects between them.
