# Plan: Honour the Promise

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the banker's seat](position_banker.md), inside [The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Banker](position_banker.md)

## Direction

Do the useful work: take short savings, lend them long, earn the spread, and stand ready to pay the window on demand. When the line comes, honour the promise, first come first served, raising cash by selling whatever must be sold, and trust that good loans will speak for themselves.

## Orders

([The Banker](persona_the_banker.md) meets the queue with cash, selling good loans at a loss, and fails by paying.)

## Implementation

([The Vault](place_the_vault.md); the [transforming](process_transforming.md) process; [the maturity mismatch](piece_the_maturity_mismatch.md) that is his whole purpose forces the fire sale that becomes [the collapse](piece_the_collapse.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] short savings are lent long, and the window is kept ready
- [ ] the queue is met with cash raised by selling good loans at a loss
- [ ] each sale makes the next saver's fear the more reasonable
- [ ] a solvent bank is made insolvent by the sole act of honouring its promise
