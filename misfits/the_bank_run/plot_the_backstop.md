# Plot: The Backstop

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The guarantee](place_the_guarantee.md), where someone stands a promise behind the deposits bigger than the panic.
The fear was of being last, and a guarantee to pay whoever comes removes the reason to be first.

## Action

[Backstopping](process_backstopping.md): [The Backstop](persona_the_backstop.md), holding [the guarantor's seat](position_guarantor.md), guarantees the deposits and lends freely against good collateral, while [The Banker](persona_the_banker.md), holding [the banker's seat](position_banker.md), keeps his good loans instead of dumping them.

## Stage

[The Backstop](persona_the_backstop.md) tables [Back the Promise](plan_back_the_promise.md): promise full payment to whoever comes, so the line dissolves and [the backstop](piece_the_backstop.md) shuts the run without a coin paid.
[The self-fulfilling prophecy](piece_the_self_fulfilling_prophecy.md) is answered, the good state held, and [The Banker](persona_the_banker.md) tables [Honour the Promise](plan_honour_the_promise.md) with the fear removed from under it.

## Tension

The one move that stops the run also signs for the risk that made it possible.
The guarantee works precisely by not being called, and in removing the fear of being last it removes the watchfulness the run once enforced: the saver stops minding the bank, the banker stops minding the saver. The panic is over ... and the same promise now stands behind every gamble taken behind it, the calm bought with the house's moral hazard, or the guarantee is judged too costly and withdrawn, and the line is free to form again.
