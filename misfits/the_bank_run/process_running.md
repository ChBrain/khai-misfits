# Process: Running

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The withdrawals cascading, the line forming, the solvent bank emptied by the paying.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A shared expectation that the others will withdraw, and a window that pays first come first served.
The first savers to the counter, whose presence is itself the signal for the next.

## Direction

Toward an empty till at a solvent bank.
Each saver runs because the others are running, the banker sells good loans at a loss to pay the line, and the selling turns soundness into insolvency.

## Lever

First come first served, which makes being first the only safe place.
The finite till paid in order means the last gets nothing, so running is each saver's best reply to a run, and the queue is the sum of those replies, self-reinforcing with every arrival.

## Echo

A sound bank broken by the act of honouring its promise.
The trusting wiped out, the runner paid, the loans dumped into a falling market, and a failure that the fear of it produced.
