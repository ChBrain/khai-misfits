# Piece: The Self-Fulfilling Prophecy

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The crux: the belief that the bank will fail is what makes it fail.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the street](place_the_street.md), where the belief circulates and confirms itself.
Where a doubt, once shared widely enough, produces the very thing it feared.

## Load Bearing

Without it the run would need a true weakness to justify it, and could be argued down with facts.
With it no fact can argue it down, because the belief creates its own evidence: enough savers expecting a failure act in ways that produce the failure, and the run then stands as proof the fear was right. The Self-Fulfilling Prophecy is load bearing because it is Merton's mechanism made literal (Merton), the false or unfounded belief that becomes true by being acted upon, so the misfit cannot be dispelled by showing the bank is sound, only by removing the reason to act on the fear.

## Apparent

A fear that turned out to be justified.
After the fact the run looks like vindication, the doubters proved right that the bank would fail, when the truth is the reverse: the bank failed because they doubted, and the prophecy manufactured the outcome it claimed only to foresee.
