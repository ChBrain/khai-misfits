# Persona: The Neighbour

## Taxonomy

[Neighbour](position_neighbour.md). The sound bank next door, run because the fear did not stop to check its books.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Solid, untroubled, entirely apart from the trouble.
Well capitalised, well run, with no rumour attached and no weakness to hide.
Sure that the panic down the street has nothing to do with it, because on the merits it does not.

## Action

Insists it is different, and is run all the same.
Points to its reserves, its ratios, its clean name, and finds the queue forming anyway.
Falls to a fear it did nothing to earn, as savers reason that if runs are happening, better to be first here too.

## Shadow

Its soundness is no defence, because the run does not read balance sheets.
The panic spreads by the same logic that started it, each saver fearing the others, and that logic needs no true weakness in the next bank, only the sight of a line, so contagion proves the run was about belief and never about loans.
Not collateral damage from a real failure: a sound bank felled directly by fear itself.

## Tell

"We are not that bank. Look at our books."
The true and useless protest, because the crowd is not running from bad books but from the crowd.
