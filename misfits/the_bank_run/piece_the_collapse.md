# Piece: The Collapse

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The outcome: a solvent bank made insolvent by selling good loans at a loss to pay the queue.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the vault](place_the_vault.md), the good loans dumped and the value gone with them.
Where honouring the window empties the bank that honoured it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the run would be a scare that passed, and the bank would reopen unharmed.
With it the fear becomes fact: to pay the line the banker sells illiquid loans into a falling market, crystallising losses that turn a bank sound on paper into one broken in cash, so the withdrawal does not reveal insolvency but creates it. The Collapse is load bearing because it is the fire-sale insolvency the model and the record both describe (Diamond & Dybvig; Bernanke), the point where the run stops being a belief about failure and becomes the failure, the loss that was not there until the paying made it.

## Apparent

Proof the bank was weak all along.
The wreckage looks like a hidden rot finally exposed, so the collapse is read backward as justification for the run, when in fact the run manufactured the insolvency it is taken to have uncovered.

## Yearbook

Suffered by [the banker](persona_the_banker.md) through [running](process_running.md), forced by [the queue](piece_the_queue.md) it must feed.
It is the fear made real: the Collapse is [the self-fulfilling prophecy](piece_the_self_fulfilling_prophecy.md) come true, a sound bank insolvent by the act of proving it was not.
