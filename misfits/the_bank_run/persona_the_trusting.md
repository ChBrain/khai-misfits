# Persona: The Trusting

## Taxonomy

[Believer](position_believer.md). The one who trusts the bank, holds his place, and is wiped out for it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Loyal, steady, sure the bank is sound.
Has read the same books as everyone and believes, correctly, that the loans are good.
Thinks a run is a foolish stampede he is too sensible to join.

## Action

Keeps his money in and lets the line pass him by.
Trusts the fundamentals over the crowd, and waits, certain the panic will exhaust itself.
Arrives at the window last, on principle, and finds the till already empty.

## Shadow

He is right about the bank and ruined by being right.
His trust would have been vindicated if the others had shared it, but they did not, and a queue paid first come first served does not reward the one who understood the loans, only the one who understood the queue.
Punished not for an error but for holding, alone, to a truth the crowd stopped honouring.

## Tell

"I'm not going to join a senseless panic over a sound bank."
The correct judgement that costs him everything, because soundness is not what the line pays out on.
