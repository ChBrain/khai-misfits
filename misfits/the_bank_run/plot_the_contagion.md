# Plot: The Contagion

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The street](place_the_street.md), the panic looking for the next bank and not checking its books first.
A queue at one bank is a signal read instantly by the savers at the next.

## Action

[Running](process_running.md): [The Neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md), holding [the neighbour's seat](position_neighbour.md), insists it is sound and different, while [The Depositor](persona_the_depositor.md), holding [the depositor's seat](position_depositor.md), reasons that if runs are happening he had better be first here too.

## Stage

[The Neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md) tables [Stand Apart](plan_stand_apart.md): show the reserves, the ratios, the clean name, and be run all the same, because [the contagion](piece_the_contagion.md) reads queues, not balance sheets.
[The Depositor](persona_the_depositor.md) tables [Get in Line](plan_get_in_line.md) at a bank with nothing wrong, so [the self-fulfilling prophecy](piece_the_self_fulfilling_prophecy.md) copies itself down the street.

## Tension

A bank with nothing wrong falls exactly as fast as the one that had a rumour.
The neighbour's soundness is real and useless, because the run was never reading the loans, only the line, so the same fear that emptied the first bank empties the second on no evidence at all. That is the proof laid bare: the run is about belief and never about books, and belief, once moving, needs no fault to find its next victim.
