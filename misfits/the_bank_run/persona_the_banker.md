# Persona: The Banker

## Taxonomy

[Banker](position_banker.md). The one who runs a solvent bank, and cannot pay everyone at once.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Competent, solvent, honestly bewildered.
Turns short savings into long loans, which is the useful work a bank is for, and does it well.
Knows the loans are good and cannot make them good today, in cash, for everyone in the line.

## Action

Honours the promise at the window, first come first served, until he cannot.
Sells good loans at a loss to raise cash for the queue, and each sale makes the next saver's fear the more reasonable.
Watches a solvent bank become an insolvent one by the sole act of paying out on demand.

## Shadow

Nothing in his books can save him, because the run was never about his books.
He is undone by the mismatch that is his whole purpose: money lent long cannot answer a promise called short, and the sounder his loans the crueller it is that he must dump them into a falling market to prove it.
Not a failed banker: a solvent one, felled by a promise no solvent bank can keep.

## Tell

"The bank is sound. I simply can't pay all of you this afternoon."
Both halves true, and the second is the only one the line can hear.
