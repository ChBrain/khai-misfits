# Place: The Guarantee

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). Where a promise bigger than the panic is stood behind the deposits.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A seal on the deposits, a lender of last resort at the discount window, a pledge to pay in full whoever comes.
The backstop that answers the fear of being last with a promise that outsizes the whole line.

## Holds

The calm, and the cost of the calm.
Here the reason to run first is removed, so the queue never forms and the good state holds, and here too the discipline is removed, because a guaranteed saver stops watching and a backed banker stops fearing him.

## Offers

An end to the run without a coin paid out.
Because the guarantee is credible, no one needs to test it, so it works precisely by not being called, dissolving the line by making the line pointless.

## Withheld

The saver's watchfulness, and the banker's restraint.
The promise that stops the run also stands behind whatever risk is taken behind it, so what the guarantee withholds is the very discipline the run once enforced, and the calm is paid for in moral hazard.
