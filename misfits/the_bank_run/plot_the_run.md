# Plot: The Run

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The window](place_the_window.md), the line forming and the cash paid first come first served.
The till is finite, the order is everything, and everyone can see which end of the line they would rather be.

## Action

[Running](process_running.md): [The Depositor](persona_the_depositor.md), holding [the depositor's seat](position_depositor.md), runs because the others are running, while [The Banker](persona_the_banker.md), holding [the banker's seat](position_banker.md), sells good loans at a loss to pay the queue, and [The Trusting](persona_the_trusting.md), holding [the believer's seat](position_believer.md), holds his place on principle.

## Stage

[The Depositor](persona_the_depositor.md) tables [Get in Line](plan_get_in_line.md): be near the front, because [the queue](piece_the_queue.md) pays the fast and ruins the patient.
[The Banker](persona_the_banker.md) tables [Honour the Promise](plan_honour_the_promise.md) and dumps loans into a falling market, so [the collapse](piece_the_collapse.md) turns a sound bank insolvent, while [The Trusting](persona_the_trusting.md) tables [Keep the Faith](plan_keep_the_faith.md) and arrives last at an empty till.

## Tension

Every saver reasons soundly, and the sound reasoning empties the bank.
The runner is right that in a line paid in order he must be early; the banker is right to honour his promise; the trusting is right that the loans are good. No one is foolish and no one is a villain, and the sum of the correct moves is a solvent bank broken by the act of proving it was solvent, the fear made fact at the window.
