# Piece: The Queue

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The incentive: first come first served, so running first is each saver's rational move.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the window](place_the_window.md), lengthening down the street.
Where the till is finite and paid in the order people arrive.

## Load Bearing

Without it a saver could wait for the panic to pass with nothing lost by waiting.
With it the last in line gets nothing, so if a run may happen, being at the front is the only safe place, and every saver's best reply to an expected run is to run. The Queue is load bearing because it is the sequential-service constraint that makes the run an equilibrium (Diamond & Dybvig): it converts a private fear into a race, rewards the fastest and ruins the patient, and turns each individually sound decision into the collective act of emptying the bank.

## Apparent

An orderly, fair way to pay.
Serving people in the order they arrive looks like simple fairness, so the rule reads as neutral procedure, when it is the mechanism that makes trusting the bank a losing move and running it a winning one.

## Yearbook

Formed at the window through [running](process_running.md), driven by the finitude of [the promise](piece_the_promise.md).
It is the race that pays the front: the Queue punishes [the trusting](persona_the_trusting.md) for holding and forces [the collapse](piece_the_collapse.md) as the banker sells to feed it.
