# Persona: The Whisperer

## Taxonomy

[Doubter](position_doubter.md). The one who carries the doubt that the bank may not be able to pay.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Well meaning, or idle, or merely early.
Passes along a worry, a rumour, a headline, a queue seen at another branch.
Need not be right, or malicious, only heard by enough people who might act on it.

## Action

Spreads the belief that the bank might fail.
Turns a private doubt into a shared expectation, so that each saver begins to wonder not about the loans but about what the others now think.
Sets the crowd reckoning, which is all the run requires.

## Shadow

The doubt need not be true to come true.
A run does not wait on a real weakness, only on a shared belief in one, so the whisper manufactures its own confirmation: enough people expecting a failure produce the failure, and the whisper is proved right by the very act of being believed.
Not a liar, necessarily: the author of a prophecy that writes itself.

## Tell

"I heard they might not be good for it. Just passing it on."
The rumour that becomes the truth the moment enough ears take it seriously.
