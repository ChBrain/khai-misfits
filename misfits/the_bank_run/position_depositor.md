# Position: Depositor

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The seat that holds a claim payable on demand, and can withdraw it first.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Savings payable in full, on demand, whenever asked.
A place in a line that pays first come first served, and the freedom to take it early.
No stake in the loans, and every stake in the length of the queue.

## Orders

Watch the others, not the books.
If the line may form, be near its front.
Withdraw first when being last means being paid nothing.

## Loses

Nothing by running, and everything by waiting, once a run is on.
Its best move against a run it expects is to run, so it is driven to the very act that makes the run, and is safe only because it moved before the till was dry.
Rewarded for reading the crowd, never for reading the loans.

## Drives

Toward the front of the line,
away from any bank a queue might form at,
so each sound reply to an expected run is one more saver in the run itself.
