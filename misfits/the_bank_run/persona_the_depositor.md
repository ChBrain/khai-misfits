# Persona: The Depositor

## Taxonomy

[Depositor](position_depositor.md). The saver who runs, not because he doubts the bank, but because he doubts the others.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ordinary, prudent, quick to do the sums.
Holds his savings in a bank he has no particular reason to distrust.
Cares less whether the loans are good than whether he will be paid before the cash runs out.

## Action

Reckons with the crowd, not the books, and joins the line.
Weighs the chance the others run against the cost of being last, and finds that if they run he must run first.
Withdraws early and in full, and by withdrawing helps make true the shortfall he withdrew to escape.

## Shadow

His soundest reasoning is what breaks the sound bank.
He is right that in a queue paid first come first served the last gets nothing, so his run is a correct reply to a run he expects, and every saver making the same correct reply is the run itself.
Not a panicker: a rational man in a structure where rationality forms the line.

## Tell

"I'm sure the bank is fine. I'd just rather not be last."
The reasonable sentence that, said by everyone at once, empties the till.
