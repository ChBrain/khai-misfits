# Position: Guarantor

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The seat that stands behind the promise, and makes being first not matter.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A purse and a name larger than any panic.
The power to guarantee the deposits and to lend freely against good collateral.
The one credible answer to a fear of being last.

## Orders

Promise to pay in full whoever comes.
Remove the reason to be first, so the line need not form.
Stand behind the deposits until no one troubles to withdraw them.

## Loses

Its leverage over risk, in the very act of removing the fear.
Once it guarantees the deposits, the saver stops watching and the banker stops fearing, so the discipline the run enforced is gone and the seat is left underwriting the recklessness it made safe.
It buys the calm, and pays for it in moral hazard.

## Drives

Toward a guarantee that dissolves the queue,
away from the panic and the discipline alike,
so the run is stopped by a promise that quietly signs for every risk taken behind it.
