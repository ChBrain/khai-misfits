# Position: Believer

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The seat that trusts the loans, holds its place, and is paid nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A correct belief that the bank is sound.
A claim it declines to press, on principle, while the line forms.
Trust in the fundamentals, and no defence against the crowd that has stopped sharing it.

## Orders

Keep the money in.
Trust the loans over the rumour.
Let the panic pass, and do not join a stampede over a solvent bank.

## Loses

Everything, for being right at the wrong moment.
It holds a truth about the loans that the queue does not pay out on, so its trust is vindicated in theory and wiped out in fact, arriving last at a window already dry.
Punished not for misjudging the bank but for misjudging the crowd.

## Drives

Toward loyalty to a sound bank,
away from the line it deems beneath it,
so the seat that understood the loans is ruined by the seat that understood the queue.
