# Piece: The Contagion

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The spread: the run jumping to sound banks it has no reason to doubt.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the street](place_the_street.md), the panic looking for the next queue.
Where a run on one bank becomes a run on the next without a fact passing between them.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be one bank's misfortune, and soundness elsewhere would be safety.
With it the fear generalises: savers at a healthy bank reason that if runs are happening they too had better be first, so the run needs no weakness in the bank it moves to, only the same logic and the sight of a line. The Contagion is load bearing because it is the systemic spread the record turns on (Bernanke), the proof that the run was never about any one bank's loans, since it fells banks with nothing wrong, and the reason a private panic becomes a public collapse of credit.

## Apparent

A sign the rot was widespread.
When bank after bank falls it looks like the whole system was unsound, so the contagion reads as mass exposure of real weakness, when it is the same self-fulfilling fear copying itself down the street, indifferent to which books it empties.

## Yearbook

Suffered by [the neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md) through [running](process_running.md), generalised from [the self-fulfilling prophecy](piece_the_self_fulfilling_prophecy.md).
It is the fear jumping the fence: the Contagion proves belief and not the books drives the run, and turns one [collapse](piece_the_collapse.md) into many.
