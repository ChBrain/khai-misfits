# Position: Banker

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The seat that turns short savings into long loans, and owes everyone on demand.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Good loans that cannot be called in a day.
Deposits that can be, all of them, all at once.
A promise to pay in full on demand, and no way to keep it if demand is total.

## Orders

Lend the savings out and earn the spread.
Honour the window, first come first served.
Meet the queue with cash, selling whatever must be sold to raise it.

## Loses

Its solvency, by proving it.
To pay the line it dumps good loans at a loss, and the dumping turns a sound bank into a broken one, so the act of honouring the promise is the act of failing.
Undone by the mismatch that is its whole reason to exist.

## Drives

Toward maturity transformation, the useful work,
which is the same work that cannot answer a run,
so the seat that turns short into long is the seat a shared fear can always empty.
