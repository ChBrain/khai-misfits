# Process: Transforming

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). Short savings turned into long loans, the useful work that is also the weakness.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Savers wanting their money safe and to hand, and borrowers wanting it for years.
A bank willing to stand between, taking deposits on demand and lending them out long.

## Direction

Toward maturity transformation, the service a bank exists to give.
Idle short savings become productive long loans, the saver keeps a claim payable any time, and the spread between is the bank's living.

## Lever

A promise to pay on demand, backed by assets that cannot be recalled on demand.
The mismatch is the engine of the service and the seam of its fragility, the same fact that lets the bank be useful and cannot let it pay everyone at once.

## Echo

A solvent bank on any ordinary day.
The loans performing, the window answering the few who withdraw, and a promise standing that no one has yet had reason to call all at once.
