# Plot: The Whisper

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The street](place_the_street.md), a doubt in the air, a rumour or a headline that the bank may not be able to pay.
True or not, it is heard by enough savers to matter.

## Action

[Doubting](process_doubting.md): [The Whisperer](persona_the_whisperer.md), holding [the doubter's seat](position_doubter.md), passes the worry along, while [The Depositor](persona_the_depositor.md), holding [the depositor's seat](position_depositor.md), begins to reckon not with the loans but with what the others now believe.

## Stage

[The Whisperer](persona_the_whisperer.md) tables [Pass the Word](plan_pass_the_word.md): turn a private doubt into a shared expectation, so [the self-fulfilling prophecy](piece_the_self_fulfilling_prophecy.md) is set loose.
[The Depositor](persona_the_depositor.md) weighs the crowd and edges toward [Get in Line](plan_get_in_line.md), and the belief tips [the two equilibria](piece_the_two_equilibria.md) toward the run.

## Tension

Nothing about the bank has changed, and everything about its odds has.
The loans are as good as yesterday, but the question has quietly moved from whether the bank is sound to whether the crowd thinks the crowd will run. The doubt need not be true to matter, and cannot be answered by facts about the bank, because it is a fact about the savers, and it is beginning to be one.
