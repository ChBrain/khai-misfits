# Place: The Street

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). Where the belief travels, and jumps from bank to bank.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Word passing mouth to mouth, a headline, a photograph of a queue, another queue forming to match it.
The space between the banks, where fear moves faster than any teller can pay.

## Holds

The self-fulfilling belief, and its power to spread.
Here a doubt becomes a shared expectation and a shared expectation becomes a run, and the same logic that emptied one bank looks down the street and finds no reason it should not empty the next.

## Offers

A signal everyone can read at once.
A visible line is information, and the street carries it instantly, so savers coordinate on fear without a word of fundamentals passing between them.

## Withheld

Any check of the books.
The street reads queues, not balance sheets, so it cannot tell a weak bank from a sound one and does not try, and the contagion it carries needs no true weakness in the bank it moves to next.
