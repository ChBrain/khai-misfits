# Process: Backstopping

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). A credible guarantee removing the reason to run, at the price of the discipline it also removes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A run under way or feared, and a purse large enough to outsize the panic.
A guarantor willing to promise full payment to whoever comes, and to lend freely against good collateral.

## Direction

Toward the good state, held without a coin paid out.
Being first stops mattering once every saver is promised full payment, so the line does not form, and the bank keeps its good loans instead of dumping them.

## Lever

A promise credible enough that no one needs to test it.
The run fed on the fear of being last, and a guarantee bigger than the till removes that fear, so the backstop works precisely by not being called, dissolving the queue by making it pointless.

## Echo

A panic stopped, and a discipline gone.
The saver no longer watching, the banker no longer fearing the saver, and the same guarantee now standing behind every risk taken behind it, the calm bought with moral hazard.
