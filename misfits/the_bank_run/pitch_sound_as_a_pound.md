# Pitch: Sound as a Pound

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Calm, solvent, reassuring.
The books balance, the loans are good, the bank is sound, and there is no reason on earth to worry.
The voice of a well-run institution stating the plain truth about itself, spoken by people who are right about every number and cannot see that the numbers are not what is about to matter.

## Undertow

Under the soundness, a promise no sound bank can keep.
The money is payable to everyone on demand and lent out where it cannot be recalled in a day, so the bank can pay whoever comes as long as they do not all come at once, and the moment enough savers believe the others will come, coming first is the only safe move for each of them.
No one is foolish: they only reckon, correctly, that in a line paid first come first served it is better to be first than to be right.

## Nerve

The feeling of a safe, solid bank,
every loan performing, every figure in order,
a queue forming outside it not because the bank is weak but because everyone fears everyone else has begun to think it is.

## Echo

After the run: a solvent bank with an empty till,
a saver wiped out for trusting the loans while the crowd trusted only the crowd,
and a fear that proved itself true by the simple act of being believed.
