# Position: Neighbour

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The seat of the sound bank next door, run by a fear it did not earn.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Clean books, ample reserves, and no rumour of its own.
A run happening down the street, and savers who can see the queue.
Every reason on the merits to be spared, and no protection from a panic that reads no merits.

## Orders

Stand apart from the failing bank.
Show the reserves, the ratios, the clean name.
Insist, truthfully, that this is different.

## Loses

Its standing, to a fear that never examined it.
Its soundness is real and irrelevant, because the run spreads by the sight of a line and the logic of being first, not by any weakness of its own.
Felled directly by contagion, which proves the panic was always about belief.

## Drives

Toward a soundness that should have saved it,
away from a queue it did nothing to summon,
so the run generalises to a bank with no fault, and belief is revealed as the only thing that ever mattered.
