# Plot: The Mismatch

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The vault](place_the_vault.md), an ordinary day, the loans performing and the till ample for whoever comes.
A bank taking savings on demand and lending them out long, doing the useful work a bank is for.

## Action

[Transforming](process_transforming.md): [The Banker](persona_the_banker.md), holding [the banker's seat](position_banker.md), turns short savings into long loans and stands ready at the window, while [The Trusting](persona_the_trusting.md), holding [the believer's seat](position_believer.md), leaves his money in, paid on demand whenever he asks.

## Stage

[The Banker](persona_the_banker.md) tables [Honour the Promise](plan_honour_the_promise.md): lend the savings out, earn the spread, and pay the window on demand, so [the maturity mismatch](piece_the_maturity_mismatch.md) makes [the promise](piece_the_promise.md) real for the few and unkeepable for the all.
[The Trusting](persona_the_trusting.md) tables [Keep the Faith](plan_keep_the_faith.md), and the bank rests in the good half of [the two equilibria](piece_the_two_equilibria.md), sound and calm.

## Tension

Everything is exactly as safe as it looks, and exactly as fragile.
The bank is solvent, the loans are good, the promise is honoured, and nothing is wrong. That is the trap laid, not sprung: a promise to pay everyone on demand backed by money that cannot be recalled in a day, resting on the one condition that not everyone asks at once, a condition no one has yet had any reason to break.
