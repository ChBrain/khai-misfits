# Process: Doubting

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). A doubt spreading into a shared expectation that the bank will fail.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A rumour, a headline, a queue glimpsed elsewhere, true or not.
A saver who hears it, and wonders what the other savers now believe.

## Direction

Toward a belief held widely enough to act on.
The doubt passes from one head to many, and the question shifts from whether the loans are good to whether the crowd thinks they are.

## Lever

An expectation about others, not a fact about the bank.
What matters is not whether the bank is weak but whether enough savers expect enough savers to run, so the doubt works on the crowd's reading of itself and needs no truth to spread.

## Echo

A crowd reckoning with itself instead of the books.
The belief in a failure now shared, the savers watching each other, and a prophecy loose that will make itself true if enough act on it.
