# Persona: The Backstop

## Taxonomy

[Guarantor](position_guarantor.md). The one who stands a credible promise behind the deposits, and stops the run.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Large, credible, able to pay whatever comes.
The insurer of deposits, the lender of last resort, the promise bigger than the panic.
Understands that the run is a fear of being last, and that a guarantee removes the reason to be first.

## Action

Guarantees the deposits and lets the guarantee do the work.
Promises to pay in full whoever comes, so that no one needs to come, and the line dissolves without the till being touched.
Stops the run not by arguing the bank is sound but by making it not matter whether it is.

## Shadow

The promise that ends the run also stands behind the risk that made it possible.
Once every deposit is guaranteed, the saver stops watching the bank and the banker stops fearing the saver, so the same backstop that removes the panic removes the discipline, and underwrites the recklessness it makes safe.
Not a costless rescue: the run is bought off with the house's moral hazard.

## Tell

"Every deposit is guaranteed. There is no need to line up."
The sentence that dissolves the queue, and quietly signs for every risk taken behind it.
