# Piece: The Maturity Mismatch

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). The setup: short deposits payable on demand funding long loans that cannot be recalled.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the vault](place_the_vault.md), the demand deposits on one side and the illiquid loans on the other.
Where the useful work is done and the fault line runs.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no bank worth running on, only a warehouse of cash no one need fear for.
With it the bank is both useful and fragile in a single fact: it turns idle short savings into productive long loans, and the very loans that make it sound are the ones it cannot sell for full value in a day. The Maturity Mismatch is load bearing because it is the maturity transformation at the heart of the model (Diamond & Dybvig), the service that defines a bank and the seam that lets a run empty it, so the fragility is not a flaw bolted on but the function itself seen from the side.

## Apparent

Simply what a bank is and does.
Borrowing short and lending long looks like ordinary, prudent banking, because it is, so nothing about the mismatch announces itself as danger until the day the short side is called all at once.

## Yearbook

Run by [the banker](persona_the_banker.md) through [transforming](process_transforming.md), the ground the play stands on.
It is the useful fragility: the Maturity Mismatch is what makes [the promise](piece_the_promise.md) impossible to keep if everyone asks at once, the fault the whole run runs along.
