# Place: The Vault

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). Where the short money and the long loans are held together.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A ledger of good loans that mature in years, and a drawer of cash that must answer today.
The bank's whole business in one room: savings taken in on demand, lent out where they cannot be recalled.

## Holds

The maturity mismatch, sitting quiet until it is called.
Here the useful work is done, short turned into long, and here too the fault line runs, because the loans that make the bank sound are the loans it cannot sell for face value in a hurry.

## Offers

A sound bank, on any ordinary day.
The loans perform, the spread is earned, and the cash on hand is ample for the few who withdraw, so the vault looks and is solvent, provided not everyone asks at once.

## Withheld

Cash enough for everyone at once.
The vault can pay any normal day's demand and no day's total demand, so its solvency is real and its liquidity is conditional, and the condition is exactly what a run removes.
