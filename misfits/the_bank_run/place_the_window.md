# Place: The Window

## Taxonomy

[The Bank Run](play_the_bank_run.md). Where the promise is paid, first come first served.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A teller, a counter, a line that lengthens down the street.
The promise made good one saver at a time, cash handed over in the order people arrive.

## Holds

The queue, and the logic that forms it.
Here first come first served turns a private fear into a public race, because the till is finite and paid in order, so the front is safe and the back is not, and everyone can see which they would rather be.

## Offers

Full payment, on demand, to whoever is early enough.
The window honours the promise exactly as written, and that is precisely what makes the run rational: the promise is real for the front of the line and empty for the end of it.

## Withheld

Any payment once the till is dry.
The same counter that pays the first in full pays the last nothing, so the window rewards the runner and ruins the one who trusted, and the order of the line, not the state of the loans, decides who is made whole.
