# Place: The Market

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). Where the cheaper service meets demand and the rebound happens.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Buyers responding to a lower price, and builders finding new things to build.
The cheaper service being used more, and used for more.

## Holds

The rebound and the extensive margin together.
Here the fixed demand of the drawing board comes unfixed: more of the old use at the lower price, and new uses that the old price forbade, both drawing on the resource.

## Offers

Real value and real thrift.
Cheaper mobility, cheaper light, cheaper compute, and inventions that could never have paid before, all genuine goods.

## Withheld

Any restraint on the total.
The market answers price with quantity, so a cheaper service is simply a more-used one, and nothing here holds the whole down while every unit falls.
