# Position: Analyst

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). The seat that projects the saving the efficiency will bring.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The numbers, the model, and the credibility of a clean projection.
A per-unit gain to multiply into a total saved.
The convention of holding all else equal to isolate the effect.

## Orders

Book the saving: service times resource-per-unit, before and after.
Report the reduction the efficiency is expected to deliver.
Keep demand fixed, so the effect of the efficiency can be seen alone.

## Loses

The one variable the whole result turns on.
Fixing demand is what makes the model clean and false, because the cheaper service is exactly what moves demand, so the saving it books is spent by the rebound it left out.
Exact in the spreadsheet, wrong at the meter.

## Drives

Toward a saving that is real on the page,
projected on a demand that will not hold still,
and undone by the very price change it did not price in.
