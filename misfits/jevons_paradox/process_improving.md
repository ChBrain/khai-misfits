# Process: Improving

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). The resource made to go further per unit of service.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A brief to conserve, and the skill to cut the resource each unit costs.
An engine, a bulb, a chip made to deliver the same service for less.

## Direction

The resource per unit of service falling, cleanly and measurably.
More miles per gallon, more lumens per watt, more compute per joule.

## Lever

Genuine engineering, aimed squarely at the unit.
The gain is real and the intent is thrift, and both are entirely about the single unit of service.

## Echo

A leaner unit, and a cheaper service.
The saving delivered per unit, and, unbidden, the price of the service cut, which is where the trouble starts.
