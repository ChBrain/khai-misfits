# Persona: The User

## Taxonomy

[Consumer](position_consumer.md). The one who answers the cheaper service with more of it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ordinary, rational, price-aware.
Notices the service costs less now, and does more of it, as anyone would.
Feels thrifty, because each unit is cheaper than it used to be.

## Action

Uses more of the now-cheaper service.
Drives the further mile, heats the larger room, runs the leaner thing longer, because the price fell.
Spends the saving on more service rather than banking it as less resource.

## Shadow

Its thrift per unit is its excess in total.
Every extra use is individually cheaper and rational, and their sum is more resource burned than before the efficiency, and the User feels frugal the entire time.
Not greedy: simply answering a lower price the way prices are answered.

## Tell

"It barely costs anything to run now."
Said just before running it twice as much.
