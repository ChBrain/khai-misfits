# Process: Cheapening

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). The efficiency lowering the effective price of the service.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A unit that now costs less resource to run.
The moment a leaner unit becomes, to whoever buys the service, a cheaper one.

## Direction

The price of the service falling as its resource cost falls.
The mile, the lit hour, the computation, each dearer yesterday and cheaper today.

## Lever

The pass-through from resource saved to price paid.
What was framed as using less becomes, in the buyer's hand, spending less, and spending less on a thing invites spending on more of it.

## Echo

A service quietly repriced downward.
The saving turned into a discount, and a discount is the signal every buyer and builder reads as: use more.
