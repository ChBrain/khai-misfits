# Plan: Cap the Resource

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the regulator's seat](position_regulator.md), inside [Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Regulator](position_regulator.md)

## Direction

Move the lever from the unit to the whole. Cap or price the resource itself, above the efficiency, so a leaner unit means less resource used and not more service bought. Make the whole deliberately scarce, and hold the ceiling the rebound is pushing against.

## Orders

([The Capper](persona_the_capper.md) sets a cap or a price on the total resource and holds it.)

## Implementation

([The Cap](place_the_cap.md); the [capping](process_capping.md) process; [the jevons paradox](piece_the_jevons_paradox.md) is answered only by pricing the whole, so [the backfire](piece_the_backfire.md) has no room to form.)

## Targets

- [ ] a cap or price is set on the resource itself
- [ ] the whole is made deliberately scarce, above any unit
- [ ] the efficiency now saves the total, not feeds the demand
- [ ] the saving holds, unless the cap is lifted as too costly
