# Process: Rebounding

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). Demand rising toward the lower price, in old uses and new.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A cheaper service and a demand free to answer it.
The lower price meeting buyers who will do more and builders who will make more.

## Direction

Consumption climbing: more of the old use, and new uses that the old price forbade.
The saving being spent on service rather than kept as resource, unit by unit.

## Lever

Price elasticity, the plain fact that a cheaper thing is a more-used thing.
Direct rebound in the existing uses, and the extensive margin opening as fresh uses become viable.

## Echo

A demand that has grown to meet the lower price.
The projected saving eaten in part or in whole, and, where the rebound runs past the gain, exceeded, so the total climbs.
