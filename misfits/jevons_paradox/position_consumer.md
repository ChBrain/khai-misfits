# Position: Consumer

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). The seat that answers a cheaper service with more of it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A service that costs less than it did.
The ordinary freedom to use more of a thing when its price falls.
A sense of thrift, because each unit is leaner than before.

## Orders

Respond to the price: do more of the service now that it is cheaper.
Drive the further mile, heat the larger room, run the leaner thing longer.
Spend the saving on more service, as a rational buyer does.

## Loses

The reduction the saving was supposed to be.
Every extra use is cheaper and sensible, and their sum is more resource than before, so the seat is frugal per unit and profligate in total without ever feeling the switch.
Banks no resource; buys more service.

## Drives

Toward more of the now-cheaper service,
each added unit rational and thrifty,
their total the rebound that eats the saving whole.
