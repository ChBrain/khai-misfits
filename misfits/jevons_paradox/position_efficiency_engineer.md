# Position: Efficiency Engineer

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). The seat that makes the resource go further per unit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The skill to cut the resource each unit of service costs.
A real, measurable per-unit saving to deliver.
A brief to conserve, and the belief that a leaner unit conserves.

## Orders

Improve the efficiency and prove the per-unit gain.
Deliver more service for less resource, cleanly and honestly.
Hand the saving forward as a reduction the whole will inherit.

## Loses

Control of what the saving does once released.
A leaner unit is a cheaper service, and a cheaper service is more demanded, so the gain the seat delivers is the very thing that lifts total use.
It owns the unit and none of the whole.

## Drives

Toward a leaner unit, sincerely,
which is a genuine good and a lower price at once,
and a lower price is an invitation the seat never meant to send.
