# Place: The Drawing Board

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). Where the efficiency is designed and the saving is projected.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Blueprints, a leaner design, a spreadsheet of resource-per-unit before and after.
The gain worked out and the saving multiplied from it.

## Holds

The per-unit truth, and the fixed-demand assumption beside it.
Here the efficiency is real and the projection is clean, both resting on a world where the number of units does not change.

## Offers

A credible reduction.
A genuine saving per unit, and a total saved that follows straight from it, exactly as intended.

## Withheld

The demand curve.
The board models the unit and holds the count of units still, so the one thing it does not draw is the rebound the cheaper unit will cause, which is where the saving goes.
