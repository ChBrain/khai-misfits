# Pitch: Twice As Far

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Thrifty, green, pleased with the saving.
The satisfaction of a resource made to go twice as far: leaner per unit, cleaner per mile, obviously less used overall.
The voice of good engineering doing an evident good, spoken by people who really did halve the fuel.

## Undertow

Under the thrift, a price quietly falling and a demand quietly rising to meet it.
Twice as far per unit is half as dear per service, and half as dear is more wanted, so the very saving becomes the reason to use more, and uses that never paid before begin to pay.
Nobody wastes: the driver only answers a cheaper mile, the new use is real value, and the meter climbs anyway.

## Nerve

The feeling of doing the responsible thing,
each efficiency a genuine saving per unit,
the total quietly rising as the cheaper service pulls demand up behind it.

## Echo

After the run: a meter reading higher than before the saving,
a fleet of new uses that would never have existed at the old price,
and an efficiency that spent more of the resource than it spared, one leaner unit at a time.
