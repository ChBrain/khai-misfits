# Process: Capping

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). A ceiling or price set on the resource itself, above the unit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone who sees the efficiency feeds the demand it means to starve.
The decision to act on the whole, with a quota, a price, or a hard limit on the resource.

## Direction

The total resource made scarce or dear on purpose, over and above any efficiency of use.
A ceiling placed where the rebound was pushing through.

## Lever

Scarcity of the whole, which the unit alone can never supply.
Cap the resource and a leaner unit becomes less used rather than more, because there is only so much to use.

## Echo

A saving secured at the level it was always spent.
The efficiency finally conserving ... held only while the cap is held, and undone the moment it is lifted as too costly.
