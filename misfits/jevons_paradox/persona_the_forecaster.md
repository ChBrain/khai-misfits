# Persona: The Forecaster

## Taxonomy

[Analyst](position_analyst.md). The one who projects the saving the efficiency will bring.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful, quantitative, credible.
Takes the per-unit gain and multiplies it out into a total saved.
Believes the arithmetic, because the arithmetic is right.

## Action

Books the saving: units of service times resource-per-unit, before and after.
Holds demand fixed to isolate the effect of the efficiency.
Reports a clean reduction that the whole is expected to deliver.

## Shadow

Its fixed demand is the one thing that will not hold.
The projection is exact and its assumption is false, because the cheaper service is precisely what moves demand, so the saving it books is spent by the rebound it left out.
Right in the model, wrong at the meter.

## Tell

"All else being equal, consumption falls."
And all else is never equal, because the efficiency itself changes it.
