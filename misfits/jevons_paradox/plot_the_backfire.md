# Plot: The Backfire

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The meter](place_the_meter.md), the running total read at last against the projection.
Every unit leaner than ever, and the sum still to be counted.

## Action

[Rebounding](process_rebounding.md) totted up: [The Forecaster](persona_the_forecaster.md), holding [the analyst's seat](position_analyst.md), reads the meter and finds the resource burned has not fallen but risen above where it began.

## Stage

[The Forecaster](persona_the_forecaster.md) returns to [Book the Saving](plan_book_the_saving.md) and finds it inverted: the fixed demand held in the model came unfixed in the world, so [the backfire](piece_the_backfire.md) shows [the expected saving](piece_the_expected_saving.md) not merely eaten but overshot.
The per-unit gain is real on every line, and the total at the bottom is larger than before the efficiency, with no waste anywhere to point at.

## Tension

The saving is gone, and nothing was wasted.
The numbers are honest and the meter is plain: more resource used after the efficiency than before it, though each unit is leaner. Because no glutton and no waster appears, the rise is easy to blame on growth or bad luck, so the one reading that would name it, that the efficiency spent the resource by cheapening it, is the reading the absence of a culprit makes easiest to avoid.
