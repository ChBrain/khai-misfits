# Position: Regulator

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). The seat that caps or prices the resource itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The power to put a ceiling or a price on the total resource.
The lever that acts on the whole, above any single unit.
The unpopularity of making a cheap thing dear again.

## Orders

Cap or price the resource itself, not the efficiency of its use.
Make the whole deliberately scarce, so a leaner unit means less resource, not more service.
Hold the ceiling the rebound is pushing against.

## Loses

The cheap abundance the efficiency produced.
The cap makes dear again the very thing the saving made cheap, so the fix reads as taxing a success, and a single repeal lets the rebound flood back.
Keeps the saving only by giving back the cheapness.

## Drives

Toward a saving secured at the level of the whole,
bought by making the resource scarce on purpose,
and never more than one lifted cap from being spent again.
