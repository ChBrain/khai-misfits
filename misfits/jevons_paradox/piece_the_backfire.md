# Piece: The Backfire

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). The total resource consumed climbing above where it began, though every unit is leaner.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the meter](place_the_meter.md), in the running total that was meant to fall and rose.
The number the whole intervention was for, read in sum.

## Load Bearing

Without it the paradox would be merely a saving smaller than hoped, a rebound that ate some of the gain.
With it the rebound runs past the gain, so more resource is burned after the efficiency than before it, and the intervention bought to conserve has spent. The Backfire is load bearing because it is the strong form of the paradox, the Khazzoom-Brookes result (Khazzoom; Brookes): not just that efficiency saves less than promised, but that at the level of the whole it can increase the very consumption it was meant to reduce, so the sign of the effect flips.

## Apparent

Growth, or bad luck.
It looks like something else must have caused the rise: the economy grew, demand was always going to climb, the efficiency surely helped at the margin. That deflection is easy precisely because no waste is visible, so the backfire is read as anything but the efficiency that produced it.
