# Place: The Meter

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). Where the total resource consumed is read, against the projection.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A running total of the resource used, and the forecast that said it would fall.
The number the whole thing was for, read at last.

## Holds

The backfire, in plain figures.
Here the per-unit gain and the total consumed sit side by side, and the total has not fallen but risen, though every unit is leaner than ever.

## Offers

The honest whole.
The one measure that counts what was actually spent, not per unit but in sum, undeceived by how efficient each unit became.

## Withheld

A culprit.
The meter shows the saving gone and no waste anywhere to blame it on, because the extra use was all rational and all cheap, so it can name the backfire and never a villain.
