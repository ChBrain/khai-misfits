# Persona: The Engineer

## Taxonomy

[Efficiency Engineer](position_efficiency_engineer.md). The one who makes the resource go further per unit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Clever, thrifty, genuinely green.
Sets out to save the resource, and does: twice the service for the same fuel.
Believes a leaner unit is an obvious, unambiguous good.

## Action

Improves the efficiency and books a real per-unit gain.
Cuts the resource each unit of service costs, cleanly and honestly.
Hands the saving forward as though the total will follow the unit down.

## Shadow

Its saving is also a price cut on the thing it saved.
Making the service leaner makes it cheaper, and cheaper is wanted more, so the very gain it delivers is the invitation to use more of what it spared.
Saves the unit and, without meaning to, unleashes the whole.

## Tell

"Now it does the same for half."
True per unit, and the reason the half becomes twice.
