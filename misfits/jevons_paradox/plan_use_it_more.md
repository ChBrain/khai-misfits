# Plan: Use It More

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the consumer's seat](position_consumer.md), inside [Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Consumer](position_consumer.md)

## Direction

Answer the price. The service costs less now, so do more of it: drive the further mile, heat the larger room, run the leaner thing longer. Spend the saving on more service, as any rational buyer does, and feel thrifty per unit while doing it.

## Orders

([The User](persona_the_user.md) uses more of the now-cheaper service, each added unit rational.)

## Implementation

([The Market](place_the_market.md); the [rebounding](process_rebounding.md) process; [the cheaper service](piece_the_cheaper_service.md) pulls demand up, and [the rebound](piece_the_rebound.md) is the sum of the extra use.)

## Targets

- [ ] the lower price is noticed and answered
- [ ] more of the service is used, each unit cheaper
- [ ] the saving is spent on service, not banked as resource
- [ ] frugal per unit and profligate in total, without feeling the switch
