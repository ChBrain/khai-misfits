# Place: The Cap

## Taxonomy

[Jevons Paradox](play_jevons_paradox.md). Where a ceiling or a price is set on the resource itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A quota, a carbon price, a hard limit on the total resource, above any single unit.
The lever moved from the efficiency of use to the amount there is to use.

## Holds

The saving, finally secured.
Here the whole is made scarce on purpose, so a leaner unit becomes less resource spent rather than more service bought, and the rebound meets a ceiling it cannot push through.

## Offers

The one thing that keeps the gain.
A limit on the resource that lets the efficiency save the total, doing at the level of the whole what the unit alone could not.

## Withheld

The cheap abundance.
The cap makes dear again exactly what the efficiency made cheap, so it feels like taxing a success, and it holds only while it is held, one repeal from letting the rebound flood back.
