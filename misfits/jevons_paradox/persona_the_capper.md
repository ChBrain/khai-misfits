# Persona: The Capper

## Taxonomy

[Regulator](position_regulator.md). The one who puts the ceiling on the resource, not the unit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Systemic, unsentimental, willing to make a thing dear.
Sees that efficiency alone feeds the demand it means to starve, and moves the lever to the whole.
Prices or caps the resource itself, so the saving has nowhere to leak.

## Action

Sets a cap or a price on the total resource, above the efficiency.
Makes the whole deliberately scarce, so a leaner unit becomes less resource used and not more service bought.
Lets the efficiency finally save, by holding the ceiling the rebound was pushing through.

## Shadow

Its fix spends the cheap abundance the efficiency created.
Capping the resource makes dear again the thing the saving made cheap, so it feels like taxing a success, and one repeal lets the rebound flood straight back.
Right, resented, and always a lifted cap away from undone.

## Tell

"Cap the fuel, not the engine."
The one move that keeps the saving, and the one that gives the cheapness back.
