# Pitch: Passes Every Check

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Sound, certified, all clear.
The plain confidence of a part that passes every test it is given: the stress measured well below yield, the load safely within limits, the component cleared for service and running uneventfully under the ordinary working load.
Nothing false in it: every check is correctly passed, every load genuinely is within limits, and the part really is sound on every instant it is tested.

## Undertow

Under the all-clear, the invisible increment spent each cycle, the irreversible fraction of life no test reads, summing in the dark toward a crack.
Because the damage lives in the count and not in the moment, the part reads sound on every check while its remaining life falls, and the tally climbs unseen until a crack nucleates and runs to a fracture under the load it always carried.
The soundness certified on every instant is spent by the number of instants.

## Nerve

The quiet confidence of the part cleared for service, sure it is safe because every check said so,
because the load was measured and it was within limits and the reserve was intact on every test,
while the sum of a thousand safe cycles climbs toward the number at which a crack will start, and nothing on any instrument shows it.

## Echo

After the run: a part that passed every test and failed anyway, under the load it always carried, no overload and no warning,
no cycle too large and no check missed, only the count reaching a sum no instantaneous test could see,
and the reserve that would have saved it, spared as needless, because it could never be justified against a load that was safe every single time.
