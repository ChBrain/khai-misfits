# Piece: The Increment

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The irreversible per-cycle micro-damage, the fraction of life spent that no check reads and no cycle undoes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Deposited at [the Service](place_the_service.md), each time the part carries the load and sets it down again, a dislocation moved, a step taken that stays taken.

## Load Bearing

Without it a cycle would leave the part exactly as it found it, and a thousand cycles would be no different from one.
With it the part changes a little every time it is used, in a direction that never reverses: no rest restores what a cycle spent, no later cycle undoes what an earlier one cost. The Increment is load bearing because it is the fraction of fatigue life consumed by a single cycle (Palmgren and Miner): a quantity no instantaneous strength check measures, since it is not a matter of the present stress but of what the metal has already been through, so the piece carries the damage that exists nowhere a test can look.

## Apparent

Nothing, on any single cycle: the part looks exactly as sound after as before, the same dimensions, the same surface, the same passing mark on the same check.
Seen at the surface, the Increment does not appear at all, and the truth that something real and permanent was spent in that one cycle, a step toward failure taken and never taken back, stays hidden behind the part looking, and largely being, unchanged.

## Yearbook

Spent by every turn of [Cycling](process_cycling.md), one [Cycle](piece_the_cycle.md) at a time, at [the Service](place_the_service.md).
It is the currency of the trap: the Increment feeds [the Tally](piece_the_tally.md) through [Accumulating](process_accumulating.md), a fraction added and never subtracted, cleared by [the Passed Check](piece_the_passed_check.md) every time because the check was never built to read it, until the sum it composes reaches the number at which [the Crack](piece_the_crack.md) begins.
