# Plan: Stay Within Limits

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the load's seat](position_load.md), inside [The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Load](position_load.md)

## Direction

Apply the working stress below yield, the load the part was built to bear, and come again as often as the service demands. Never exceed the limit, and reach the part not by any surge but by the count of safe applications.

## Orders

([The Load](persona_the_load.md) applies its stress below yield to [the Part](persona_the_part.md) and repeats, each application within limits and each spending an increment.)

## Implementation

([The Service](place_the_service.md); the [cycling](process_cycling.md) process; each [cycle](piece_the_cycle.md) stays below yield and passes [the passed check](piece_the_passed_check.md), yet leaves [the increment](piece_the_increment.md) that [accumulating](process_accumulating.md) sums.)

## Targets

- [ ] each application stays below yield, within limits
- [ ] the load is no larger than the part was designed for
- [ ] the stress is applied again as often as the service demands
- [ ] the part is reached by the count of safe cycles, not by any overload
