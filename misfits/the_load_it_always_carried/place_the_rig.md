# Place: The Rig

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The test bench where the part is loaded and inspected, the surface where [The Check](plot_the_check.md) is run and, in part, where [The Derating](plot_the_derating.md) is argued.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A load frame, a dial, a part clamped and cycled, and a pass.
The Load applies its stress against the frame, the dial reads it back, and the part is certified, the whole visible scene one of measurement and clearance, nothing on the bench but the instant being tested.

## Holds

The strength check, the measured stress, the certificate.
Here the Inspector holds the load against the yield strength and reads the margin between them, so the Rig holds the point where a single application is proven safe, the comparison it is built to make and the only comparison it can make.

## Offers

A pass, freely, to anything that sits below yield.
Because the Rig tests only the instant it is given, it offers a certificate to every load that clears the strength, cheaply and honestly, so it hands out soundness readings that are true, every one of them, for the cycle they were taken on.

## Withheld

Any reading of the tally, any sight of the life already spent.
The Rig has no instrument for a history of stresses, only for the stress in front of it, so it withholds the accumulation entirely, unable to tell a part on its first cycle from a part on its ten-thousandth, the certificate reading the same for both and the count nowhere on the dial.
