# Persona: The Inspector

## Taxonomy

[Inspector](position_inspector.md). The one who tests the part against its strength each time and correctly passes it, certifying every cycle sound and blind to the sum across them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent. Rigorous. Right every time.
Is the one who measures the load against the strength, checks the part for any crack a test can find, and clears it for service on a verdict that is always correct.
No laxity in it: only a careful inspector passing a part that genuinely passes, the reserve intact on every measurement.

## Action

Tests the part and certifies it, on [Pass the Check](plan_pass_the_check.md), comparing the stress to the strength and clearing what sits safely below.
Passes each inspection honestly, finding no fault because at that instant there is none to find.
Does what a good inspector does: measures what can be measured, and certifies sound what is, at that instant, sound.

## Shadow

Not a careless inspector missing a flaw, a rigorous one seeing all there is to see, and that is the whole difficulty: every verdict is correct, and the correctness is what certifies the part all the way to fracture.
Because the check reads the present stress and the present part and the damage lives in the count of past cycles, the Inspector can measure only the instant and never the sum, so a test that is right every single time still clears a part whose life is nearly spent, the rigour of the check blind by its very nature to the tally it cannot reach.
It passes only what truly passes, and passing only what truly passes is exactly what signs off the part that the count has already condemned.

## Tell

Says "it checks out: within limits, no cracks, cleared for service."
Is right, truly, and never sees that being right about the instant says nothing about the sum, so the clean bill of health is honest and the part it clears is dying.
