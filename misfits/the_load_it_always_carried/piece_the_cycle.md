# Piece: The Cycle

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The single load application, below yield and passing every check, the unit repeated a thousand times before anything gives.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Applied at [the Service](place_the_service.md), where the part does its ordinary work, the shaft turning, the axle bearing, the wing spar flexing, one cycle at a time.
Proved first at [the Rig](place_the_rig.md), where the same cycle is measured against the strength and certified sound before the part is ever put to work.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no repetition to sum, only a single load and a single verdict, and the trap has nothing to count.
With it the whole structure has its unit: a load applied and removed, applied and removed, each one on its own entirely safe. The Cycle is load bearing because it is the below-yield application that a strength check correctly passes every time (Wöhler): the stress measured against the yield strength and found short of it, so the piece carries the single instance, sound in isolation, that the rest of the trap will multiply past soundness.

## Apparent

One load, taken once, exactly like every load the part was built to take.
Seen at the surface, the Cycle looks like nothing at all, an unremarkable repetition of a stress the part was designed to carry, and the truth that this same unremarkable stress, repeated, is the entire mechanism of the failure to come, stays hidden behind how identical the thousandth cycle looks to the first.

## Yearbook

Applied through [Cycling](process_cycling.md), the load taken up and set down, over and over, at [the Service](place_the_service.md).
It is the unit of the trap: the Cycle deposits [the Increment](piece_the_increment.md) each time it is borne, spent unread past [the Passed Check](piece_the_passed_check.md) that clears it every time, until the running count is what [the Fatigue Limit](piece_the_fatigue_limit.md) was set to hold below.
