# Position: Load

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The seat of the repeated stress, each application within every limit, where a safe load destroys by the number of times it is applied.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A stress below yield and a cadence of repetition: the standing to be applied safely, and the certainty of being applied again.
A clear case: the load is the one the part was designed for, within its limits on every application and no larger than before.
No excess to answer for, and no need of any, because it destroys not by size but by the count, the safe repetition enough.

## Orders

Stay within limits.
Apply your stress below yield, the same load the part was built to bear, and come again, uneventfully, as often as the service demands.
Give every check a stress to certify and every certification a pass.

## Loses

Nothing, and that is the point: the seat need never exceed the limit to reach the part, only be applied enough times.
Because a stress below yield spends an irreversible increment all the same, the Load takes the part on the count and not on any surge, so it wins the fracture on repetition alone and the part the largest overload could not break falls to the load it always carried.
It enters no contest of magnitude, and by never entering one it reaches by the sum what no single excess could.

## Drives

Toward the increments spent and the count climbing,
each application safe and the sum of them fatal,
so the seat that never once exceeds the limit destroys the part that no overload could.
