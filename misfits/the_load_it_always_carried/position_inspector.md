# Position: Inspector

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The seat of the check, where the part is measured against its strength and correctly passed, certifying every instant and blind to the sum.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An instantaneous test and a correct verdict: the standing to measure the present stress against the present strength and to certify what sits safely below.
A clear case: the test is rigorous and the verdict right, the part genuinely sound at the instant it is checked.
No reach beyond the moment, only the moment measured true, so the seat can read the present part perfectly and the spent life not at all.

## Orders

Pass the check.
Measure the load against the strength, look for any crack a test can find, and clear what is, at this instant, sound.
Certify honestly, and return a pass wherever a pass is truly earned.

## Loses

Any sight of the count, and with it the power to see the failure coming.
Because the seat reads the instant and the damage lives in the history, it gives up all view of the accumulated life, so it can certify only the present and never the sum, losing the one quantity that would show the part is nearly spent.
It surrenders the tally to keep the rigour, and the rigour, being of the instant, is exactly what cannot see the count.

## Drives

Toward a verdict correct every time and a part cleared to its fracture,
the check right on every instant and blind to the sum of them,
so the seat that never once errs signs off the part the count has already condemned.
