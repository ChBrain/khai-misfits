# Position: Engineer

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The seat of the one who reads the count, holding every cycle below the fatigue limit and retiring the part on its cycles, at a cost the checks call needless.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A reserve to spend and a reason no test confirms: the standing to derate the load, thicken the section, or retire a part on its count.
A clear case: the fatigue limit is real and the sum is real, so a margin held against the count is not waste but the only thing that answers it.
No instant to point to that justifies the reserve, only the tally, so the seat must spend against a failure it cannot show.

## Orders

Derate to the limit.
Hold every cycle below the fatigue limit, derate the load or overbuild the section, and retire the sound-looking part on its cycle count.
Carry the weight and the cost, and discard what still passes every test, trusting the count over the check.

## Loses

The cover of a passing test, and the weight and cost the reserve consumes.
Because the seat spends margin against a damage no instant reveals, it gives up the ease of the clean bill of health and the lightness of the minimal section, so it holds its reserve against every check that passes and every part that still looks sound, keeping the margin and paying for it.
It surrenders the comfort of the certified-sound part to answer the count, and pays, in weight and cost and discarded good parts, the price the instant never charges.

## Drives

Toward a part kept whole and a reserve spent unseen,
bought with weight, cost, and good parts retired early,
so the seat that reads the count pays for the margin every passed check calls waste.
