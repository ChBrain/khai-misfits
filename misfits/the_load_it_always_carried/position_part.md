# Position: Part

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The seat of the component that bears the load, passes every check, and is destroyed by the count of sound cycles it carries.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A yield strength and a finite fatigue life: the standing to carry any load below the strength, and a stock of cycles that each application spends and none restores.
A clear case: the part is genuinely sound, well within its limits, and passes every test it is given, the reserve intact on every instant.
No way to show the life it has spent, only the strength it still has, so the seat reads whole on every check while its stock of cycles falls.

## Orders

Bear the load.
Carry each application within your limits, pass every inspection, and give no instrument a fault to find.
Take the working load again and again, as designed, until the count arrives.

## Loses

The invisible stock of cycles, spent one per load and never restored, and in the end the section itself.
Because every application below yield spends an irreversible increment the seat cannot show, it gives up its fatigue life in the dark while every check says it is whole, so it loses its remaining life to the very loads it is built to bear, the soundness on every test hiding the stock run down.
It surrenders its cycles to the count, keeping its measured strength to the last and losing the life no measurement reads.

## Drives

Toward a part certified sound to the end and fractured by the sum,
its stock of cycles spent by the loads it always carried,
so the seat that passes every check is destroyed by the number of times it passes.
