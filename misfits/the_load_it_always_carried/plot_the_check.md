# Plot: The Check

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Rig](place_the_rig.md), the part fresh and untried, its strength known, the load about to be set against it, nothing yet measured and nothing yet wrong.

## Action

[Cycling](process_cycling.md): [The Load](persona_the_load.md), holding [the load's seat](position_load.md), applies its stress below yield, and the part takes it cleanly, within every limit.

## Stage

[The Inspector](persona_the_inspector.md), on [Pass the Check](plan_pass_the_check.md), measures the stress against the strength and certifies it sound, finding, honestly, nothing to fault.
[The Part](persona_the_part.md), on [Bear the Load](plan_bear_the_load.md), carries the application within its limits and gives the check nothing to find, [the passed check](piece_the_passed_check.md) entered and the reserve intact.

## Tension

Nothing here is wrong, nor will anything here ever be found wrong: the load is safe, the check is right, the part is sound. [The Cycle](piece_the_cycle.md) closes clean, filed as one, and closes carrying, unread, the first of an increment no test on this rig was built to see, the part standing certified and the count already begun.
