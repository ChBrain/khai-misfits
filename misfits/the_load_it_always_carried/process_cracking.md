# Process: Cracking

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). What [Accumulating](process_accumulating.md) hands off once its tally reaches one, the crack that begins where the sum arrives and runs, faster with every cycle, toward the fracture read at [The Fracture Surface](place_the_fracture_surface.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The tally arriving at its sum, nucleation reached.
Where accumulating's increments finally add to one, a crack forms, still too small for any inspection to catch, and it is this arrival, not any single cycle, that cracking takes as its start.

## Direction

Toward fracture, each cycle's growth larger than the one before.
Because a crack advances a determinate amount every cycle and a longer crack meets a higher stress at its tip, cracking runs toward its own acceleration, the growth this cycle always exceeding the growth last cycle, so the direction is not merely forward but forward and steepening, the same load doing more damage each time only because the crack it acts on is now longer.

## Lever

The stress concentrated at the tip, met by the next ordinary cycle.
Because a crack of a given length concentrates the working stress at its tip regardless of how safe that stress reads at a distance, cracking runs on a lever that strengthens itself with every turn, the crack lengthening the lever and the lever lengthening the crack, so growth compounds on growth with no new load ever applied.

## Echo

A crack real and growing, faster than it grew before, and no sign anywhere in the service it runs in.
The part carries a fracture no dial has reported, longer today than yesterday and growing faster tomorrow than today, so what still reads as ordinary service is a section thinning toward the load it cannot hold, the crack's whole history legible only later, at [The Fracture Surface](place_the_fracture_surface.md).
