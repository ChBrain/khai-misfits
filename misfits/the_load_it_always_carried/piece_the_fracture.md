# Piece: The Fracture

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The sudden final failure, the part parting under the exact load it carried a thousand times before, no overload and no warning.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Occurs at [the Service](place_the_service.md), under the ordinary working load, on an ordinary day; recorded afterward at [the Fracture Surface](place_the_fracture_surface.md).

## Load Bearing

Without it the crack would be a growing flaw and nothing more, a defect that could in principle be lived with, found, or outrun.
With it the growth resolves into an ending: the crack, run by [Cracking](process_cracking.md) past the section that can still hold, meets a moment where the remaining metal is too thin for the same load it has borne all along, and the part parts, completely and at once. The Fracture is load bearing because it is the sudden run to failure the crack's own accelerating advance makes inevitable (Paris and Erdogan): not a new load and not a new event, but the same routine load meeting a section finally too thin, so the piece carries the catastrophe that wears the face of the ordinary.

## Apparent

An accident, or a fluke, a part that simply gave way without cause, on a day like every other day, under a load like every other load.
Seen at the surface, the Fracture looks causeless, since nothing changed about the load or the operation in the moment it happened, and the truth that the moment was set up cycle by cycle, long before, by an increment no check read and a crack no inspection caught, stays hidden behind how sudden and how singular the break appears, as if it arrived from nowhere rather than from everywhere that came before it.

## Yearbook

Reached when [the Crack](piece_the_crack.md) has run past holding, through [Cracking](process_cracking.md), at [the Service](place_the_service.md).
It is the ending of the trap: the Fracture is what [the Tally](piece_the_tally.md) was always counting toward, the failure [the Passed Check](piece_the_passed_check.md) never once forecast, averted only where [the Fatigue Limit](piece_the_fatigue_limit.md) was actually held, its surface, once broken, the only place the whole history is finally legible.
