# Persona: The Load

## Taxonomy

[Load](position_load.md). The repeated stress the part bears, each application below yield and within every limit, the honest working force that destroys by the number of times it is applied.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ordinary. Within limits. The same as ever.
Is the working stress the part was built to carry, the turn of the shaft, the flex of the spar, applied at a level well below the strength and no larger today than yesterday.
No excess in it: only the load the part is meant to bear, honestly within its limits every single time.

## Action

Applies its stress cycle after cycle, on [Stay Within Limits](plan_stay_within_limits.md), always below yield and always the load the part was designed for.
Repeats, uneventfully, the same safe application, giving every check a stress to certify and every check a pass.
Does what a working load does: comes again, within limits, and is borne, again, within limits.

## Shadow

Not an overload in disguise, a genuinely safe stress repeated, and that is the whole difficulty: the load is within limits on every application, and its staying within limits is what lets it kill by repetition.
Because a stress below yield does no instantaneous harm but spends an irreversible increment all the same, the Load destroys not by any one application but by the count of them, so the more faithfully it stays within limits the more surely it accumulates, the safe repetition and not any excess the thing that fractures the part.
It never once exceeds the limit, and never exceeding the limit is exactly how it reaches the part that no overload could.

## Tell

Comes again, no larger than before, the load it always was.
Is within limits, truly, and by truly staying within limits gives every check a pass and the part one more increment, the danger hidden in the sameness rather than in any surge.
