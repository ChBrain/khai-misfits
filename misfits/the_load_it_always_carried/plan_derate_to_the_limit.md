# Plan: Derate to the Limit

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the engineer's seat](position_engineer.md), inside [The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Engineer](position_engineer.md)

## Direction

Hold every cycle below the fatigue limit so the sum never reaches failure: derate the load, overbuild the section, or retire the part on its cycle count rather than its inspection. Trust the tally over the instant, and carry the weight and cost the checks call needless.

## Orders

([The Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) holds every cycle below the fatigue limit and retires the sound-looking part on its count, against [the Inspector](persona_the_inspector.md)'s passing checks, at a cost in weight and discarded good parts.)

## Implementation

([The Rig](place_the_rig.md) and [the Service](place_the_service.md); the [derating](process_derating.md) process; the reserve is set by [the fatigue limit](piece_the_fatigue_limit.md) against [the tally](piece_the_tally.md), keeping the part clear of [the crack](piece_the_crack.md) and [the fracture](piece_the_fracture.md) ... if weight, cost, and good parts retired early can be spent against a damage no check will show.)

## Targets

- [ ] every cycle is held below the fatigue limit
- [ ] the part is retired on its cycle count rather than its inspection
- [ ] the weight and cost of the reserve are carried, and good-looking parts discarded
- [ ] the part endures, or the passing checks win and the reserve is spared as needless
