# Piece: The Fatigue Limit

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The stress below which the sum never reaches failure, the escape from the count and its price.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set at [the Rig](place_the_rig.md), where it is measured and proved, then carried into [the Service](place_the_service.md) as the ceiling the working load is derated to sit beneath.

## Load Bearing

Without it there would be no floor beneath the count, every cycle would deposit its increment regardless of size, and the sum would always eventually arrive.
With it a load small enough leaves the part able to bear it indefinitely: held under this stress, the increment a cycle deposits does not accumulate toward failure at all, and the tally that would otherwise climb toward one simply never gets there. The Fatigue Limit is load bearing because it is the threshold beneath which the S-N curve goes flat and cycling no longer costs life at any count (Wöhler): the one number that converts an inevitable sum into no sum at all, so the piece carries the escape, and it carries it only at a cost every single-cycle check calls needless, the weight, the size, or the early retirement spent against a failure that check will never itself predict.

## Apparent

Overbuilt, over-cautious, a margin nobody asked for on a part that keeps passing every test it is given.
Seen at the surface, the Fatigue Limit looks like waste, an oversized section or a component retired while it still reads perfectly sound, and the truth that the size and the retirement are the only things standing between the part and a tally no check can see climbing toward one, stays hidden behind the plain fact that a check comparing this load to this strength finds the extra reserve, every time, unnecessary.

## Yearbook

Proved at [the Rig](place_the_rig.md) against [the Passed Check](piece_the_passed_check.md) that would clear a slimmer, cheaper section just as readily.
It is the escape of the trap: the Fatigue Limit is what [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) holds the load beneath through [Derating](process_derating.md), the one move that keeps [the Tally](piece_the_tally.md) from ever reaching [the Crack](piece_the_crack.md) or [the Fracture](piece_the_fracture.md), bought by carrying, cycle after cycle, exactly the reserve every instantaneous check says the part does not need.
