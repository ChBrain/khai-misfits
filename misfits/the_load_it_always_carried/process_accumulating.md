# Process: Accumulating

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The invisible summing of the increments that [Cycling](process_cycling.md) deposits, unfolding mostly in [The Service](place_the_service.md), the process that turns a thousand safe cycles into a debt no single one of them owed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The increment cycling leaves behind, cycle by cycle.
Each application below yield spends a fraction of the part's fatigue life, a fraction too small for any strength check to register, and it is this fraction, left over and unrecovered, that accumulating takes up and carries forward.

## Direction

Toward the tally reaching its sum, the count arriving at one.
Because each increment adds to the last and none subtracts, accumulating moves only one way, toward the total the increments were always going to reach, so it runs toward nucleation the way a debt runs toward its due date, closer with every cycle and no cycle paying it down.

## Lever

An increment that cannot be undone, met by a check that cannot see it.
Because the damage is irreversible and no single-cycle test reads history, accumulating runs on the gap between what happens and what is measured, so the tally climbs precisely because nothing is built to climb it, the part's remaining life spent in a ledger no instrument opens.

## Echo

A life fallen further in the dark, and every dial still reading full.
The part has one fewer cycle of life left in it than before and nothing anywhere records the loss, so what looks like an unchanged part passing an unchanged check is, underneath, a tally one increment nearer its sum, the falling life invisible until the moment it is gone.
