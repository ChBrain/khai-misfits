# Persona: The Part

## Taxonomy

[Part](position_part.md). The component that bears the load, passes every check, and is destroyed by the sum, the axle or shaft or spar the trap is spent through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sound. Certified. Well within its limits.
Is the component that passes every test it is given, carries the working load uneventfully, and reads as strong as the day it was made.
No flaw in it that any check can find: only a part used exactly as designed, bearing a load it has borne a thousand times.

## Action

Bears the load cycle after cycle, on [Bear the Load](plan_bear_the_load.md), taking each application within its limits and reading sound on every test.
Carries the working load in service and passes each inspection, giving no instrument a fault to find.
Does what a good part does: holds the load it was built to hold, again and again, until the count reaches it.

## Shadow

Not a weak part hiding a defect, a genuinely sound one spending an invisible life, and that is the whole difficulty: the soundness is real on every instant, and the reality of it is what hides the sum.
Because each cycle spends an irreversible increment no check can read, the Part's remaining life falls in the dark while every test it passes says it is whole, so the longer it serves soundly the nearer it is to a crack, and the very record of passed checks is the record of a life quietly spent.
It passes every test it can be given, and passing every test is exactly what leaves the tally unseen until it is too late.

## Tell

Bears the load with no sign of strain, right up until it parts.
Shows nothing, truly, and shows nothing because there is nothing any instant can show: the damage is in the count, and the count keeps no visible face, so the first outward sign is the fracture itself.
