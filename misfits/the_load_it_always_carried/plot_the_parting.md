# Plot: The Parting

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Service](place_the_service.md), the ordinary working load once more, the load it always carried, the crack advanced past the section's last capacity to hold.

## Action

[Cracking](process_cracking.md): [The Load](persona_the_load.md), holding [the load's seat](position_load.md), applies the same safe stress it has applied a thousand times, and [The Part](persona_the_part.md), holding [the part's seat](position_part.md), parts, suddenly and completely, under it.

## Stage

[The Part](persona_the_part.md), on [Bear the Load](plan_bear_the_load.md), carries this cycle exactly as every one before it, giving no warning because there is none left to give.
[The Load](persona_the_load.md), on [Stay Within Limits](plan_stay_within_limits.md), applies no more than it ever did, the ordinary load reaching, at last, a part with nothing left to carry it.

## Tension

Nothing overloaded and nothing missed: the load was the load it always carried, safe as ever, and the part had passed every check it was ever given. At [The Fracture Surface](place_the_fracture_surface.md), [the fracture](piece_the_fracture.md) alone records what no service ever read, the slow growth of [the crack](piece_the_crack.md) and the final fast break, the catastrophe wearing the face of the routine, no cycle at fault and the count the only cause.
