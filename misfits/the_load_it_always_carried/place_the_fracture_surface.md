# Place: The Fracture Surface

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The broken face read after [The Parting](plot_the_parting.md), where the whole hidden history the part carried through [The Service](place_the_service.md) is finally legible.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A smooth, beach-marked zone of slow growth, and a rough zone of final fracture.
The surface lays the crack's whole life open at once, the fine ridges of a thousand advancing cycles giving way to the coarse tear of the last instant, the entire process visible now that it can no longer be stopped.

## Holds

The record of every cycle, the striations, the origin.
Here, and nowhere else in the play, the count itself is legible: each striation a cycle, their spacing the crack's changing speed, their beginning the point nucleation occurred, so the Fracture Surface holds the one ledger complete enough to answer for the whole tally.

## Offers

A reading of the history, in full.
Because the surface preserves every stage of the crack's growth, it offers the Inspector or Engineer an account exact enough to reconstruct the origin and the rate, the one place in the play where the invisible sum is made entirely visible.

## Withheld

This sight from anyone before the fracture.
The record only exists once the part has already parted, so the Fracture Surface withholds its whole legibility from precisely the audience that could have used it, the history read perfectly and read only when reading it can no longer help.
