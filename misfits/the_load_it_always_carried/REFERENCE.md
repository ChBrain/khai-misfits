# The Load It Always Carried: Reference

The research warrant for **The Load It Always Carried** (play and company not yet staged;
this warrant is drafted first, to prove the science and the distinction before authoring).
The dynamic is the metal-fatigue account of failure: Wöhler's below-yield fatigue and the S-N curve,
Palmgren and Miner's linear cumulative damage, and Paris and Erdogan's crack-growth law, all cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging metal fatigue as an **accumulation structure**: a part cyclically loaded, every single cycle
sitting safely below the yield strength and passing every instantaneous strength check, yet each cycle
depositing an invisible, irreversible increment of micro-damage that no check reads, so the increments
sum until a crack nucleates and then runs, accelerating cycle by cycle, to a sudden fracture under the
exact load the part carried a thousand times before. The governing law is not that a margin was cut too
fine or an overload arrived, but that **a load each instant certifies as safe destroys the part by the
sum of its repetitions**, no cycle at fault and the failure authored by the count. The play models the
documented mechanics: the failure at stresses well below static yield and the fatigue limit beneath
which the sum never reaches failure (Wöhler), the irreversible per-cycle increment and its linear
accumulation to unity across loads of varying size (Palmgren and Miner), the crack that, once nucleated,
grows a determinate amount each cycle and accelerates as it lengthens (Paris and Erdogan), and the
sudden final fracture with no overload and no warning, as the load-bearing vectors of the structure, not
as a tale of an overstressed part or a careless operator.

It is distinct from the house's The Brittle Optimum, where slack trimmed from a system leaves it
without the reserve to absorb a shock, so a disturbance that a slacker system would have survived brings
it down: there a margin is removed and a shock arrives, here no margin is removed and no shock is needed,
the part used strictly within its limits and failing from repetition alone. It is distinct from The New
Normal, where a risky act is reclassified as acceptable each time it passes without harm, a drift of
judgment: here nothing is reclassified and no judgment drifts, the loads are genuinely within limits and
the damage is physical and unseen, not a perception that has moved. It is distinct from Seed Corn and the
present-for-future trades, where an agent deliberately spends a future stock for a present gain: here no
one trades anything and nothing is deliberated, the accumulation a fact of the material under load, not a
choice to draw down. And it is distinct from the "each choice sound, the sum fatal" family the house
already stages in the domain of decision (The Tyranny of the Urgent, The Permission of the Emergency,
The Tyranny of Small Decisions): those sum locally rational choices, and here there is no choice inside
the mechanism at all, only a load each check correctly passes and a damage the checks cannot see, so the
one human vector is the instantaneous verification that certifies every cycle and is blind to the tally
across them.

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast an
  overstressed part or a careless operator, because the source finding is precisely that each cycle is
  sound: every load sits below the yield strength, every instantaneous strength check is correctly
  passed, and the part is used strictly as designed (Wöhler). The failure is authored by no one and
  emerges from the structure, an irreversible per-cycle increment that no check reads summing to a crack
  and a fracture (Palmgren and Miner; Paris and Erdogan), so the misfit is that a part correct on every
  single-instant test, loaded only within its limits, is destroyed by the mere number of times it bore a
  load it always carried.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into a costless correction. The one move that
  averts the failure is to hold every cycle below the fatigue limit, by derating the load or overbuilding
  the section or retiring the part on a cycle count rather than an inspection, and it is not free: it
  means carrying weight and cost the single-cycle checks all say is unnecessary, and discarding parts that
  pass every test they can be given, trusting a tally no instant reveals over a strength check that never
  fails. The reckoning is reserve bought against a damage that cannot be seen, not a free correction.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here, never
  claimed as the house's. The staging is the house's; the below-yield fatigue and the endurance limit are
  Wöhler's, the linear cumulative-damage rule is Palmgren's and Miner's, the crack-growth law is Paris's
  and Erdogan's, and the condition the trap actually requires, a load safe at every instant and an
  irreversible damage that only the count reveals, is carried as carefully as the findings themselves.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element. The play and its company are not yet authored; these are the
proposed load-bearing pieces the warrant commits the staging to carry, one per vector, no filler.

- **The Cycle**: carries the single load application (Wöhler), each one below the yield strength and
  passing every instantaneous strength check.
- **The Increment**: carries the irreversible per-cycle micro-damage (Palmgren and Miner), the fraction
  of life spent that no check reads and no cycle undoes.
- **The Tally**: carries the linear accumulation to unity (Palmgren and Miner), the running sum across
  loads of varying size that reaches failure with no single cycle to blame.
- **The Crack**: carries the propagation (Paris and Erdogan), the flaw that once nucleated grows a
  determinate amount each cycle and accelerates as it lengthens.
- **The Fracture**: carries the sudden final failure, the part parting under the exact load it carried a
  thousand times before, no overload and no warning.
- **The Passed Check**: carries the instantaneous strength verification, correctly certifying every cycle
  and structurally blind to the sum, the one human vector and the reason no one is at fault.
- **The Fatigue Limit**: carries the escape and its price (Wöhler), the stress below which the sum never
  reaches failure, bought by weight, cost, or early retirement the single-cycle checks all call needless.
