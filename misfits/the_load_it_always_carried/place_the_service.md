# Place: The Service

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The part in ordinary operation, turning or flexing under the working load it was built for, where [The Accrual](plot_the_accrual.md) and [The Nucleation](plot_the_nucleation.md) run unseen and [The Parting](plot_the_parting.md) finally shows.

## Owner

## Shown

A routine running, the load applied again and again, and nothing amiss.
The shaft turns, the spar flexes, the axle bears, the same working load met the same way every time, and to any eye watching, the Service shows only years of uneventful use.

## Holds

The working load, the cycles, the years.
Here the ordinary application repeats without variation, so the Service holds the whole run of cycles the part will ever see, the entire count accumulating inside a place that looks, throughout, exactly like nothing happening.

## Offers

Uneventful carrying of the load, freely, as designed.
Because the part is sound and the load is within limits, the Service offers exactly what it promises, cycle after cycle of ordinary service with no visible cost, the part doing precisely the job it was built to do.

## Withheld

Any warning before the fracture.
The Service has no way to surface a tally it cannot read, so it withholds every sign of the approaching crack, the part sound in the morning and parted by afternoon, the first outward evidence of anything wrong being the break itself.
