# Plot: The Accrual

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Service](place_the_service.md), the certified part at its ordinary work, the shaft turning, the axle bearing, cleared and carrying on exactly as designed.

## Action

[Accumulating](process_accumulating.md): [The Load](persona_the_load.md), holding [the load's seat](position_load.md), comes again, no larger than before, and [The Part](persona_the_part.md), holding [the part's seat](position_part.md), takes it within its limits, spending [the increment](piece_the_increment.md) it cannot show.

## Stage

[The Part](persona_the_part.md), on [Bear the Load](plan_bear_the_load.md), carries cycle after cycle, reading sound on every test it meets and giving no instrument a fault to find.
[The Load](persona_the_load.md), on [Stay Within Limits](plan_stay_within_limits.md), repeats, uneventfully, the same safe application, each one below yield and each one certified.

## Tension

No cycle here does anything a check could catch: every one is safe, every one passes, and that is exactly how [the tally](piece_the_tally.md) climbs, unread, toward the number that ends it. The plot closes with the part still sound on every test it is given and its remaining life falling in the dark, nearer the sum than the day before and nothing anywhere to show it.
