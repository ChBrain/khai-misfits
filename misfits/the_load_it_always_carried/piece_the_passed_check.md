# Piece: The Passed Check

## Taxonomy

[The Load It Always Carried](play_the_load_it_always_carried.md). The instantaneous strength verification, correct every time it is taken and structurally blind to the sum it is taken against.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Administered at [the Rig](place_the_rig.md), where the load is measured against the yield strength before and during service, and passed, every time, at [the Service](place_the_service.md) too.

## Load Bearing

Without it the part would have no certification at all, and the trap would lose its cover: a part loaded past its limits would fail its checks honestly, and the mystery would dissolve into ordinary overstress.
With it the part is cleared, correctly, on every occasion it is examined: the stress compared to the yield strength and found short of it, cycle after cycle, the verdict never once wrong on its own terms. The Passed Check is load bearing because it is the below-yield criterion a strength test correctly applies every time (Wöhler): a verification built to read one instant against one strength, and for that very reason built with no way to read a history of instants, so the piece carries the one human vector in the whole mechanism and the reason no person, in applying it correctly, is at fault for what it cannot see.

## Apparent

Proof, plain and repeated, that the part is safe, a green mark stamped again and again on a component doing exactly what it was designed to do.
Seen at the surface, the Passed Check looks like the whole truth about the part's condition, since it is administered honestly and answered honestly every time, and the truth that a check comparing load to strength has nothing to say about a tally of prior cycles, that passing it a thousand times proves nothing about the thousand-and-first, stays hidden behind the sheer, accumulating weight of a record with no failed mark on it anywhere.

## Yearbook

Applied at [the Rig](place_the_rig.md) before every [Cycle](piece_the_cycle.md) reaches [the Service](place_the_service.md).
It is the cover of the trap: the Passed Check clears every load that [Accumulating](process_accumulating.md) is quietly turning into [the Tally](piece_the_tally.md), certifying right up to the cycle before [the Crack](piece_the_crack.md) nucleates, and it is the check [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) must set against, spending [the Fatigue Limit](piece_the_fatigue_limit.md) as a cost this piece will never itself demand.
