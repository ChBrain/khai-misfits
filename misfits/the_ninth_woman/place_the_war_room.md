# Place: The War Room

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). Where the slip becomes a crisis and the decision to add people is made.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A tense meeting, a red status, a date under threat.
The room where a slipping project is escalated and something must be decided, now, in front of everyone who owns the outcome.

## Holds

The pressure, and the reach for the fastest visible lever.
The moment a slip turns into a call for capacity, because capacity is the thing the room can authorise on the spot.

## Offers

A decisive response.
Here a project in trouble can be seen to act: heads approved, budget released, a plan that looks like it is being saved.

## Withheld

The weeks between the decision and its cost.
The war room delivers a fast answer and cannot show the ramp-up and cross-talk it sets in motion, so the move that feels like rescue is the move that deepens the slip.
