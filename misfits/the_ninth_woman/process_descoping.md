# Process: Descoping

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The reversal: shortening the work instead of lengthening the team.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone deciding to change the promise rather than the headcount.
When the slip is faced honestly, the work of cutting scope can begin.

## Direction

From the whole promise toward one the team can keep.
It moves toward a smaller release, re-planned against the held team and the real date.

## Lever

Authority over scope meeting the pull of the original commitment.
The force is the will to promise less; the resistance is that cutting is visible and swallowing the slip is not.

## Echo

A date made real, and a promise made smaller.
It hands forward a plan the team can actually meet, bought with the features cut and the concession owned.
