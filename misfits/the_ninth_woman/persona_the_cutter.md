# Persona: The Cutter

## Taxonomy

[Product Owner](position_product_owner.md). The reversal: removes work instead of adding people.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Clear-eyed, unsentimental about scope.
Willing to say the promise was too big before the team was too small.
Reaches for the backlog, not the org chart, because the backlog is the lever that pulls toward the date.

## Action

Cuts the scope to what a held team can truly ship, and re-plans against it.
Protects the people who know the work from being diluted, and staffs early or not at all.
Makes the date real by shrinking the promise, not by swelling the crowd.

## Shadow

The cure looks like defeat and the disease looks like effort.
The Cutter must ship less, visibly, while the alternative of adding people looks like fighting harder, so the honest move is the one hardest to defend in a room that wants the whole promise kept.

## Tell

"We can't do all of it by the date. We can do the half that matters."
Trades the whole promise for a real one, and wears the look of giving up.
