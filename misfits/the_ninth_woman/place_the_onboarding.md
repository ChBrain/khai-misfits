# Place: The Onboarding

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). Where the newcomers learn the system and the veterans teach instead of build.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Pairing sessions, setup guides, a queue of questions.
The place a new hand becomes a working hand: context transferred, conventions absorbed, the map slowly drawn.

## Holds

The ramp-up, and the drain it takes from the people who knew the work.
The weeks where a newcomer produces little and a veteran produces less, because teaching is where their time now goes.

## Offers

A more capable team, later.
Here capability is genuinely built, an investment that will pay back once the ramp is climbed.

## Withheld

Now.
The onboarding gives the project value on the far side of the ramp and withholds it in the exact window the deadline needed it, so its payback lands after the milestone it was meant to save.
