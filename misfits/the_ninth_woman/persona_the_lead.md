# Persona: The Lead

## Taxonomy

[Tech Lead](position_tech_lead.md). The one who must keep the growing team in sync, and drowns doing it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Coordinating, conscientious, increasingly underwater.
Holds the technical picture and keeps everyone aligned to it, a job that was manageable when the team was small.
Watches the channels multiply and the day fill with keeping people in step.

## Action

Runs the standups, reviews the work, answers the cross-team questions the larger team now generates.
Spends more of each day coordinating and less building, as the paths between people grow.
Keeps the team aligned at the cost of the throughput alignment was meant to protect.

## Shadow

The coordination that holds the team together is the coordination eating its output.
The Lead cannot cut the meetings without losing alignment, and cannot keep them without losing the day, so the team's size costs it speed in a way no one is doing wrong.

## Tell

"I spent the whole day in sync meetings and shipped nothing."
Names the quadratic tax, from the seat that pays most of it.
