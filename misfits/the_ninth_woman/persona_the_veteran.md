# Persona: The Veteran

## Taxonomy

[Senior Engineer](position_senior_engineer.md). The one who knew the work, now teaching instead of doing it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Skilled, generous, quietly stretched.
Knows the system deeply, which is exactly why the newcomers are routed to them.
Wants the project to ship and is now the bottleneck for everyone learning how.

## Action

Stops delivering to onboard the reinforcements: answers questions, pairs, explains the context that lives in their head.
Transfers knowledge one conversation at a time, because that is the only speed it moves.
Becomes the project's teacher at the cost of being its fastest builder.

## Shadow

The person best able to move the work is the person taken off it to grow the team.
The Veteran's time is the scarce resource the additions consume, so adding hands subtracts the one pair that was already productive.

## Tell

"I'd be done already if I weren't answering questions all day."
Names the drain precisely, from the seat the drain runs through.
