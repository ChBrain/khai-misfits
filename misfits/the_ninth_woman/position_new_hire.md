# Position: New Hire

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The seat added late: a real capability, deferred.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Genuine skill, and a system to learn before the skill can land.
A willingness to help, and a ramp that must be climbed before the help arrives.

## Orders

Get up to speed: learn the codebase, the conventions, the context.
Take small tasks, ask the questions, ship what you safely can.
Become productive, on the ramp's timetable, not the deadline's.

## Loses

The present, which is when the project needed the value.
A seat whose worth is real and deferred cannot serve a milestone that has already passed by the time the ramp ends.

## Drives

Toward a capable contributor later, and away from the help the date could spend now.
Toward attention consumed before work returned, through no fault of the seat.
