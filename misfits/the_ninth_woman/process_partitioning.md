# Process: Partitioning

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). Trying to divide the work across more hands, and hitting the sequence that will not.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

More hands than the task can use, looking for a way to split it.
Once capacity exceeds the parallel work, the search for divisible pieces begins.

## Direction

From one task toward many, until the sequence refuses.
It moves toward parallelism up to the point the dependencies cap, and no further.

## Lever

Available hands meeting a critical path.
The force is the wish to parallelise; the resistance is a chain of dependencies that no number of people can shorten.

## Echo

A hard floor under the schedule.
It hands forward the truth that beyond the partition, another person is another channel, not another day saved, the ninth woman on a nine-month task.
