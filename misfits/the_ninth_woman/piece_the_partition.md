# Piece: The Partition

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The indivisible task: the work on the critical path that no crowd can split.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the gantt](place_the_gantt.md), on the critical path, in the task with a dependency chain and no parallel seam.
The integration that must follow the build, the design that must precede them both, the sequence that cannot be run at once: the bearing that takes nine months no matter how many are assigned.

## Load Bearing

Without it every task could be split across more hands and headcount would always buy time.
With it there is work whose sequence caps the number of useful hands, so past that cap another person is not another day saved but only another channel to keep in sync. The Partition is load bearing because it is why the man-month is a myth: months and people trade evenly only where the work divides, and the work on the critical path is exactly where it does not.

## Apparent

A task like any other, waiting for someone free.
It looks assignable, a card on the board that another pair of hands could pick up, so adding people looks like it must help. That is the trap, because the task's constraint is not availability but sequence, and no amount of availability shortens a chain.

## Yearbook

Met by every added hand through [partitioning](process_partitioning.md), and unmoved by any of them.
It is the hard floor under [the cross-talk](piece_the_cross_talk.md): the Partition is why more people cannot finish the critical work sooner, the reason [the man-month](piece_the_man_month.md) holds even when ramp-up and coordination are paid.
