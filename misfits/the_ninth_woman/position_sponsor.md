# Position: Sponsor

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The seat that owns the date and can authorise capacity.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The commitment to the customer, and the budget to add people to keep it.
The authority to pull the fastest visible lever the moment the project slips.

## Orders

Save the date. Add whatever capacity it takes and expect the curve to bend.
Read a slip as a resourcing problem, and resource it.
Do not be the one who moves the commitment.

## Loses

The distinction between headcount and progress.
A seat that can approve heads in a day and cannot approve progress in a day cannot see why the first does not buy the second.

## Drives

Toward more people on a late project, counted as more speed, and away from the smaller promise that would actually fit.
Toward the lever that is fastest to pull and pulls the wrong way.
