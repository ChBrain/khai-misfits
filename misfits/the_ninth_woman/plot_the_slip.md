# Plot: The Slip

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The gantt](place_the_gantt.md), where the bars have drifted past the line.
The project is behind, the date will not move on its own, and the pressure in the room is for someone to do something about it.

## Action

[Slipping](process_slipping.md): [The Sponsor](persona_the_sponsor.md), holding [the sponsor's seat](position_sponsor.md), reads the slip as a shortage of capacity and calls for more people.

## Stage

[The Sponsor](persona_the_sponsor.md) tables [Save the Date](plan_save_the_date.md): hold the commitment and resource the gap, so [the deadline](piece_the_deadline.md) stands and [the slip](piece_the_slip.md) is answered with headcount.
The order is given for hands, because hands are the lever the seat can pull today.

## Tension

Nothing here is unreasonable, and it sets the whole trap.
A slipping date and a call for capacity is the picture of responsible ownership: act, resource, save the commitment. But the lever that is fastest to pull is the one that pulls the wrong way, because a head can be added in a day and progress cannot, and from this order on, the project will get later by the very move meant to speed it.
