# Position: Product Owner

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The reversal seat: the one that can remove work instead of adding people.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Authority over scope, and the standing to shrink the promise.
The one lever in the play that pulls toward the date: the backlog, not the org chart.

## Orders

Cut the scope to what a held team can truly ship, and re-plan against it.
Protect the people who know the work from dilution, and staff early or not at all.
Make the date real by promising less, not by crowding more.

## Loses

The whole promise, and the look of fighting hard.
A seat that ships less on purpose must defend a visible concession against the invisible cost of the slip it avoids.

## Drives

Toward a smaller promise the team can keep, and away from the crowd that makes the date recede.
Toward the one move that shortens the work instead of lengthening the team, at the cost of the scope given up.
