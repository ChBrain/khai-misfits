# Piece: The Slip

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The schedule extending despite the additions: the date moving the wrong way.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the burndown](place_the_burndown.md), in the line that should fall and instead flattens, then climbs.
The forecast that keeps moving out, the velocity that dropped the sprint after the team grew, the completion date further away in the review than it was before the reinforcements came.

## Load Bearing

Without it adding people is at worst neutral and the law is only caution.
With it the outcome is perverse and measured: the project is not merely un-helped by the additions, it is later because of them, ramp-up and cross-talk together outrunning the capacity added. The Slip is load bearing because it is the harm the play exists to show, the date going backward while everyone works harder.

## Apparent

Just a project that was always going to be late.
It looks like the lateness that prompted the hiring, ongoing, so the additions escape blame and even seem to have softened it. That reading is the trap's defence, because it credits the reinforcements with slowing the slide they widened, and calls for yet more of them.
