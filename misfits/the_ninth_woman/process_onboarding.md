# Process: Onboarding

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The ramp-up, where a new hand costs before it pays.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A newcomer arriving into a system they do not yet know.
The moment they are seated, the transfer of context must begin.

## Direction

From a net drain toward an eventual gain.
It moves toward a productive engineer, slowly, on the ramp's own timetable.

## Lever

The veterans' knowledge meeting the newcomers' need for it.
The force is real assimilation; the resistance is that it moves one conversation at a time and spends the scarce hands to do it.

## Echo

Capability built, and delivery drained meanwhile.
It hands forward a team that will be faster later, at the cost of the output the deadline needed now.
