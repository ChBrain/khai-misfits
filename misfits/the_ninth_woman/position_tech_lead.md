# Position: Tech Lead

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The seat that keeps the growing team in sync, at the cost of building.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The technical picture, and the duty to keep everyone aligned to it.
The coordination surface of the whole team, which grows as the team does.

## Orders

Keep everyone aligned: run the standups, review the work, answer the questions the larger team generates.
Hold the shared picture as the paths between people multiply.
Coordinate first, because misalignment is worse than a lost hour.

## Loses

The hours coordination takes from building.
A seat whose alignment work grows as the square of the team spends more of each day in sync and less in delivery, and cannot cut either without cost.

## Drives

Toward a team held together and a day eaten by holding it, and away from the throughput coordination was meant to protect.
Toward the quadratic tax that turns a bigger team into a slower one per head.
