# Process: Slipping

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The project falling behind, and staying behind.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Work taking longer than the plan assumed.
The moment actuals diverge from the schedule, the date begins to move.

## Direction

From a plan on track toward a date that keeps receding.
It moves toward pressure, and pressure toward the reach for a lever.

## Lever

Reality meeting an optimistic estimate.
The force is the work's true difficulty; the resistance is a schedule that will not admit it and a room that wants it hidden.

## Echo

A crisis, and a demand for capacity.
It hands forward a slipping date and the instinct to save it with headcount, the instinct the law turns against.
