# Position: Senior Engineer

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The seat that knows the work, and is spent teaching it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Deep knowledge of the system, and therefore every newcomer's questions.
The scarce context the project runs on, held in one head and transferable only slowly.

## Orders

Onboard the reinforcements: pair, explain, answer, transfer what you know.
Bring the new people up, because you are the one who can.
Grow the team's capability out of your own delivery time.

## Loses

The hours that were the project's fastest output.
A seat routed every question cannot also be the fastest builder, so the additions subtract the one pair of hands that was already producing.

## Drives

Toward a team that will be capable later, and away from the delivery the deadline needed now.
Toward knowledge transferred one conversation at a time, at the price of the work only this seat could do.
