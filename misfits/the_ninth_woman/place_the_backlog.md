# Place: The Backlog

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). Where scope is removed rather than capacity added: the reversal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A list of what was promised, and a hand deciding what will not be done.
The features ranked, the release narrowed, the cut line drawn through the middle of the plan.

## Holds

The one lever that pulls toward the date, and the concession it costs.
The place work can be shortened instead of the team lengthened, where the promise is made to fit the time and the team that already knows it.

## Offers

A date that can actually be met.
Here the schedule stops being a wish: cut to what the held team can ship, and the line becomes real for the first time.

## Withheld

The whole promise.
The backlog buys a real date with a smaller one, and takes, in return, the features cut and the look of having given up, which is why it is the last place the crisis is willing to go.
