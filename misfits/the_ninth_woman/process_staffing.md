# Process: Staffing

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). Adding people to the late project.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An authorised demand for more capacity.
Once the crisis calls for hands, the work of finding and seating them begins.

## Direction

From a small late team toward a larger late team.
It moves toward more headcount on the schedule, booked as productive on arrival.

## Lever

Available capacity meeting a project that cannot yet absorb it.
The force is real hiring power; the resistance is that a late project is the worst place to put a new hand.

## Echo

A bigger team and a heavier ramp.
It hands forward capacity the schedule counts now and the work returns later, triggering onboarding and cross-talk.
