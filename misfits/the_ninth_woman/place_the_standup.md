# Place: The Standup

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). Where the growing team coordinates, and the coordination grows faster than the team.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A circle that used to be quick, now long.
The daily sync, the review queue, the threads: the ceremonies that keep a team aligned, swelling as the team does.

## Holds

The cross-talk, and the channels that multiply as the square of the head count.
The place the communication tax is paid, where each added person adds paths faster than they add output.

## Offers

Alignment.
Here everyone stays in step, decisions are shared, and no one is left out of the loop that a large team needs to stay coherent.

## Withheld

The hours it takes.
The standup keeps the team aligned and takes from the day the time that alignment costs, so the coordination that holds the team together is the coordination eating its throughput.
