# Plot: The Reinforcements

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The war room](place_the_war_room.md), then [the onboarding](place_the_onboarding.md).
The heads are approved and arriving: capable people, seated into a project they do not yet know, and the veterans turning to teach them.

## Action

[Staffing](process_staffing.md): [The Manager](persona_the_manager.md), holding [the project manager's seat](position_project_manager.md), brings the people on and books them into the plan.
Then [onboarding](process_onboarding.md): [The Veteran](persona_the_veteran.md) at [the senior engineer's seat](position_senior_engineer.md) stops delivering to bring them up.

## Stage

[The Manager](persona_the_manager.md) works [Add the Heads](plan_add_the_heads.md) and [The Veteran](persona_the_veteran.md) works [Onboard the New](plan_onboard_the_new.md): [the reinforcements](piece_the_reinforcements.md) land as capacity on the schedule, and [the ramp-up](piece_the_ramp_up.md) begins to drain the project through the people who knew it best.
The team is bigger the day they start, and slower for weeks after.

## Tension

Everyone acts in good faith, and the project loses ground.
The new hires are capable and the manager is doing exactly as asked, so nothing looks wrong; but the schedule counts the heads on arrival while the work returns only later, and the veterans who were the project's speed are now its teachers. The plan improves on paper precisely as it worsens in fact.
