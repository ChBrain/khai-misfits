# Plot: The Cross-Talk

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The standup](place_the_standup.md), grown long, and a task that will not divide.
The team is larger now, and the day is filling with keeping it aligned, while the work on the critical path still waits on a single chain no crowd can split.

## Action

[Communicating](process_communicating.md): [The Lead](persona_the_lead.md), holding [the tech lead's seat](position_tech_lead.md), spends the day coordinating multiplying channels.
Meanwhile [partitioning](process_partitioning.md) fails at the critical task, and [The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) at [the new hire's seat](position_new_hire.md) is still getting up to speed.

## Stage

[The Lead](persona_the_lead.md) works [Keep Everyone Aligned](plan_keep_everyone_aligned.md) and [The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) works [Get Up to Speed](plan_get_up_to_speed.md): [the cross-talk](piece_the_cross_talk.md) grows as the square of the team, and [the partition](piece_the_partition.md) caps the hands the critical work can use.
The added capacity is eaten by coordination and ramp before it reaches the line.

## Tension

The team is bigger and the work is not faster.
No one is idle: the lead is coordinating, the newcomers are learning, the veterans are teaching, and every hour is honestly spent. But coordination scales quadratically while output scales linearly at best, and the critical task cannot be parallelised at all, so the crowd's effort lands everywhere except where the schedule needed it.
