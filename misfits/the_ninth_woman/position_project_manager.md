# Position: Project Manager

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The seat that carries out the order and brings the people on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The mandate to staff the project and the schedule to book them into.
The levers of delivery: assignments, seats, a plan that counts capacity as it arrives.

## Orders

Add the heads the sponsor authorised and get them working.
Book the new capacity into the schedule as productive, and expect the next sprint to show it.
Turn the order for speed into a larger team.

## Loses

The lag between a head starting and a hand producing.
A seat that counts capacity on arrival cannot see the weeks of ramp-up before it returns, so the plan improves exactly as the project worsens.

## Drives

Toward a bigger team booked as faster, and away from the truth that the work returns later than the headcount.
Toward a schedule that reads full and a project that runs slow.
