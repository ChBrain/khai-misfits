# Process: Communicating

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). Keeping the growing team in sync, at a cost that grows faster than the team.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A team large enough that alignment can no longer be assumed.
The moment the paths between people multiply, coordination becomes work.

## Direction

From easy alignment toward expensive alignment.
It moves toward more meetings, more reviews, more threads, as the channels grow.

## Lever

The need to stay coherent meeting the arithmetic of n(n-1)/2.
The force is the honest need to align; the resistance is that channels grow as the square while output grows at best linearly.

## Echo

A team aligned and a day consumed aligning it.
It hands forward coherence bought with the very hours that would have been delivery, slower per head the larger it gets.
