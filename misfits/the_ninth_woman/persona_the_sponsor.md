# Persona: The Sponsor

## Taxonomy

[Sponsor](position_sponsor.md). The one who owns the date and demands it be saved.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Decisive, accountable, out of patience.
Owns the commitment to the customer and the board, and will not be the one to move it.
Reads a slip as a resourcing problem, because resourcing is what a sponsor can authorise.

## Action

Sees the project behind and calls for capacity: more people, now.
Approves the headcount, signs the budget, and expects the curve to bend.
Treats hands as the fastest lever, because it is the fastest one to pull.

## Shadow

The lever that is fastest to pull is the one that pulls the wrong way.
The Sponsor can add people in a day and cannot add progress in a day, and the difference between the two is the whole misfit, invisible from a seat that reads headcount as speed.

## Tell

"Whatever it takes. Get more people on it."
The most natural order to give, and the one that makes the date recede.
