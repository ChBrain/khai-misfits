# Plot: The Man-Month

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The burndown](place_the_burndown.md), where the line that should fall has risen.
The reinforcements have been on the project for weeks, and the forecast completion date is further out than it was before they came.

## Action

[Slipping](process_slipping.md): [The Estimator](persona_the_estimator.md), holding [the planner's seat](position_planner.md), re-forecasts and traces the movement to ramp-up and cross-talk, not slack.

## Stage

[The Estimator](persona_the_estimator.md) works [Re-Forecast](plan_re_forecast.md): report the date honestly, so [the slip](piece_the_slip.md) is shown to have widened under the additions and [the man-month](piece_the_man_month.md) is named as the reason.
Adding people made the project later, though everyone worked hard, exactly as the law says.

## Tension

The one number that would open the cure is the one the crisis least wants to hear.
To name the man-month is to say the additions slowed the project, which contradicts the visible response everyone approved; the easy move is to blame the new hires for not ramping faster and to demand still more of them. So the plot turns on a choice that is not about staffing at all: whether the slip is diagnosed ... or answered with another round of the thing that caused it.
