# Piece: The Reinforcements

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The people added to the late project: capable, willing, and added at the worst time.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the war room](place_the_war_room.md), named as the answer to the slip and moved onto the project.
The extra engineers, the borrowed team, the new headcount approved in a hurry: real capacity, honestly meant to help, arriving into a system they do not yet know.

## Load Bearing

Without it the late project stays its own size and the schedule finds no false lever to pull.
With it there is capacity added exactly where capacity cannot be absorbed cheaply, so the help arrives as a cost first: the Reinforcements are load bearing because they are the intervention the law turns against, not a worse class of worker but the right people at the wrong moment.

## Apparent

Help arriving.
It looks like relief: more hands, more capacity, a visible response to a slipping date that everyone can point to. That reading is the trap, because the hands are counted as speed the moment they are hired, and paid for as drag for weeks after.

## Yearbook

Brought on by [the manager](persona_the_manager.md) through [staffing](process_staffing.md), into a project already behind.
It is the move the play turns on: the Reinforcements trigger [the ramp-up](piece_the_ramp_up.md) and [the cross-talk](piece_the_cross_talk.md), and their arrival is what [the man-month](piece_the_man_month.md) counts as capacity and the work counts as cost.
