# Position: Planner

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The seat that keeps the forecast, and must report which way the date moved.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The schedule, the velocity, and the duty to report them truly.
The one view that can compare the date before the hiring to the date after.

## Orders

Re-forecast honestly: run the numbers after the reinforcements and say what they show.
Trace the slip to its causes, ramp-up and cross-talk, not slack.
Name the direction the date actually moved.

## Loses

The welcome of the room, when the true number contradicts the visible fix.
A seat that reports the additions slowed the project speaks against the response everyone approved, so honesty reads as pessimism.

## Drives

Toward the slip named plainly, or toward the easy story that blames the new hires and buys more of them.
Toward the diagnosis, from the one seat that can see the date go the wrong way.
