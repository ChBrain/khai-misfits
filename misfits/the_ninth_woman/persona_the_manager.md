# Persona: The Manager

## Taxonomy

[Project Manager](position_project_manager.md). The one who carries out the order and brings the people on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Responsive, organised, doing as asked.
Given a mandate to add capacity, finds the people, clears the seats, and gets them started.
Believes a bigger team is a faster team, because that is what the plan says.

## Action

Staffs the late project: onboards the reinforcements and assigns them work.
Books the new capacity into the schedule as though it were productive on arrival.
Turns an order for speed into a larger team, in good faith.

## Shadow

The team is bigger the day they start and slower for weeks after.
The Manager adds capacity the schedule counts immediately and the work returns only later, so the plan improves on paper exactly as it worsens in fact.

## Tell

"We've doubled the team. We should see it in the next sprint."
Counts the heads on arrival and waits for a speed that is still ramping.
