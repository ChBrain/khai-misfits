# Persona: The Estimator

## Taxonomy

[Planner](position_planner.md). The one who re-forecasts, and reads the date moving the wrong way.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Precise, honest, unwelcome.
Keeps the schedule and the velocity, and reports what they actually show.
Wants the forecast to improve after the hiring, and finds it worse.

## Action

Re-runs the numbers after the reinforcements land and sees completion move further out.
Traces the slip to ramp-up and cross-talk, not to slack.
Names, out loud, that adding people made the project later.

## Shadow

The true forecast is the one the crisis least wants to hear.
To report that the additions slowed the project is to contradict the visible response everyone approved, so the honest number reads as pessimism and the easy story blames the new hires.

## Tell

"We added six people and the date went out two weeks."
The sentence that is either the diagnosis or the reason to hire six more.
