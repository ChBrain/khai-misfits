# Pitch: Throw People At It

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Urgent, capable, sure the answer is more hands.
The date is slipping, so bring in help: pull in the extra engineers, borrow the other team, all hands on deck until it ships.
The voice of a project that trusts effort and reads a shortfall of time as a shortage of people.

## Undertow

Under the urgency, a month that will not hold more people.
The new hands must be taught, and the teaching is paid by the ones who were fastest; the channels multiply as the square of the team; and the task on the critical path cannot be split at all.
No one is idle and no one unskilled: the help is real and arrives at the one moment the project cannot spend it, so the date it was meant to save moves further out.

## Nerve

The feeling of a team that keeps growing
and keeps falling behind,
every added hand raising the effort and lowering the pace, and no one able to say why working harder is working slower.

## Echo

After the run: a bigger team, a later date,
a forecast that went backward the sprint the reinforcements landed,
and the one move that would have made the date real, promising less or staffing early, still unmade, because a project in crisis would rather add people than admit it will do less.
