# Persona: The Newcomer

## Taxonomy

[New Hire](position_new_hire.md). The added hand: capable, willing, and a net drain until assimilated.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Eager, competent, keen to contribute.
Arrives ready to work and finds a system they must learn before they can move in it.
Will be genuinely productive, later, and knows it is not yet.

## Action

Learns the codebase, the conventions, the context, asking the questions only the veterans can answer.
Takes small tasks, checks often, ships little while the map is still forming.
Consumes more attention than they return, through no fault, for as long as ramp-up lasts.

## Shadow

Being added late is not the same as being useful late.
The Newcomer is a good engineer whose value is real and deferred, and the project needed value now, so their arrival is help the deadline cannot yet spend.

## Tell

"Once I'm up to speed I'll be able to take real load off you."
True, and dated after the milestone the hiring was meant to hit.
