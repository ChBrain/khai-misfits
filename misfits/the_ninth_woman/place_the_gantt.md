# Place: The Gantt

## Taxonomy

[The Ninth Woman](play_the_ninth_woman.md). Where headcount and time are laid on one axis and the critical path is drawn.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Bars across a calendar, dependencies as arrows, a hard line for the date.
The plan as management sees it: tasks, durations, and the people assigned, all in one picture that reads capacity as time.

## Holds

The deadline, and the critical path that no crowd can shorten.
The place the man-month lives, where ten people for a month and one for ten look like the same bar.

## Offers

A plan that looks resourceable.
Here a slip appears to have an obvious fix: a task waiting, a bar to fill, another pair of hands to assign.

## Withheld

The sequence that caps the hands.
The Gantt shows a task as assignable and hides that its constraint is order, not availability, so the one bar that cannot be parallelised looks exactly like the ones that can.
