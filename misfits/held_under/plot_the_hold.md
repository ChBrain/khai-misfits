# Plot: The Hold

## Taxonomy

[Held Under](play_held_under.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

At [The Queue](place_the_queue.md), the trigger has been gone for minutes now, and the system is still failing. No spike remains to blame, and the failing has not eased on its own...

## Action

[Sustaining](process_sustaining.md) holds the floor: [the Service](persona_the_service.md), still on [Serve What Comes](plan_serve_what_comes.md) from [Under](position_under.md), sits inside [the Metastable State](piece_the_metastable_state.md), answering what arrives and failing the excess exactly as it did the moment the Surge tipped it over, because the seat has no standing to do otherwise.

## Stage

It cannot climb back to [the Steady State](place_the_steady_state.md), and not for lack of trying: it serves every request offered, correctly, and what is offered is [the Sustaining Load](piece_the_sustaining_load.md), the sum of every Caller's rightful reattempt. The more faithfully the Service serves, the more completely it stays fed the load that keeps it under, and the more faithfully the Caller retries, the more surely the Service stays down. Neither seat can see the loop it is one half of.

## Tension

This is the turn that makes it a misfit and not a bad afternoon. The failing state proves, beat by beat, that it will hold: stable, self-feeding, indifferent to how long it runs, clearing for no one and needing nothing from outside to keep going. It is held down by no fault anywhere in it, only by every correct thing everyone in it keeps doing, and it will still be doing this an hour from now unless something outside the loop intervenes.
