# Position: Onset

## Taxonomy

[Held Under](play_held_under.md). The seat of the transient trigger, whose whole role is to push the system past the line once and leave, so the cause is gone the instant the failing state it opened begins to sustain itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A single push and no more: the standing to knock the system over the threshold once, a brief spike, a pause, a stall, and then nothing.
A minor case: the perturbation is small and ordinary, the kind a healthy system meets constantly, so the seat carries no severity and needs none, only the timing to cross the line at the moment headroom is thin.
No power to hold the failure open, only to start it, and so the seat's whole contribution is spent in the moment it acts, everything after held up by something else.

## Orders

Pass through.
Cross the threshold once, tip the system out of the good equilibrium, and be gone, because a transient's whole part is to perturb and depart.
Leave the failing state behind, open and self-sustaining, and take no further part, since nothing the seat does after the first push matters to a loop now feeding on its own output.

## Loses

Its standing as the cause, the moment it departs.
Because the failure outlives it and is held up by the retries, not by the trigger, the seat loses any claim to be what is wrong: it is gone before the outage peaks, too small to blame and too early to find, so the search for a cause equal to the damage passes it over.
It surrenders all of the blame and keeps all of the origin, the thing that started what it cannot be held responsible for sustaining.

## Drives

Toward a failure with no cause left to point at,
the trigger over in a second and the state it opened going on for hours,
so the seat that did almost nothing is the origin of all of it and answerable for none of it, gone long before anyone asks what holds the system down.
