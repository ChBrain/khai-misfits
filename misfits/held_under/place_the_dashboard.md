# Place: The Dashboard

## Taxonomy

[Held Under](play_held_under.md). The reading surface where goodput and offered load are set side by side, host to [The Brownout](plot_the_brownout.md) and [The Trip](plot_the_trip.md), where [the Operator](persona_the_operator.md) reads the failing state and answers it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Two lines that used to track together, now apart: load holding high, goodput falling toward the floor. The board reads "overloaded," plainly, in whatever vocabulary its alerts use, and it reads that way whether the cause is present or long departed.

## Holds

The numbers as they are counted, not as they are caused: total requests in, requests completed, nothing in the count that marks a request as first attempt or fourth. It holds the moment [the Operator](persona_the_operator.md) chooses to trip [the Breaker](piece_the_breaker.md), and the moments after, where the same two lines begin, slowly, to separate the other way.

## Offers

A place to see [the Collapse](piece_the_collapse.md) named, and a lever to answer it: the Dashboard is where the failing state stops being felt only downstream, in complaints and timeouts, and becomes a legible shape someone can act on. It offers the one control that reaches the loop from outside it.

## Withheld

The composition of the load it displays. The Dashboard cannot show, from its own counters, that most of what is arriving is not new demand but [the Sustaining Load](piece_the_sustaining_load.md), retries of requests the system already failed: the board shows a system besieged and cannot say by what.
