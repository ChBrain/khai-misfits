# Position: Under

## Taxonomy

[Held Under](play_held_under.md). The seat of the sound system that took a transient hit and cannot climb back to health while its clients keep correctly retrying, held in the failing state by the load of their recovery.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Capacity, headroom, and a correct design: the standing to serve every request that comes, up to a limit no ordinary day approaches.
A clean case: the system is well built and overloaded by no one, so serving what comes is honest work and the trigger that knocked it over was minor and is gone.
No power of its own to refuse the load or stop the retries, only the duty to answer what arrives, and so the seat cannot lift itself once the load it must answer is the retries of the work it just failed.

## Orders

Serve what comes.
Accept every request, answer as many as capacity allows, and fail the overflow, because that is what a service is built to do and there is nothing else it may correctly do.
Then serve the retries of the failed overflow too, and fail the new overflow they create, and hold in the failing state, because the seat has no standing to turn any of it away.

## Loses

The path back to health, and with it the good equilibrium it fell out of.
Because the seat must answer what arrives and what arrives is now the sum of correct retries above its capacity, it cannot drop below capacity on its own correct behaviour: serving what comes keeps the queue full, and a full queue keeps the failures coming, so it loses the ability to recover precisely by continuing to do its job.
It surrenders the return to health to whatever outside it will break the loop, and its own correct service will not.

## Drives

Toward a system held under indefinitely,
the goodput at the floor and the load at the ceiling and the trigger long gone,
so the seat that cannot refuse the load or stop the retries stays failing until something reaches in and forces the loop open.
