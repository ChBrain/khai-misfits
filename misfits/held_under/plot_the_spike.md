# Plot: The Spike

## Taxonomy

[Held Under](play_held_under.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Steady State](place_the_steady_state.md), latency low, queues short, headroom sitting unspent. Nothing here asks to be watched. Then, for a moment, it is asked for more than it usually gives...

## Action

[Triggering](process_triggering.md): [the Surge](persona_the_surge.md), on [Pass Through](plan_pass_through.md) from [Onset](position_onset.md), lays down [the Trigger](piece_the_trigger.md), a brief spike, a pause, a stall, nothing that should matter on its own. It tips [the Service](persona_the_service.md) past the line the Steady State never usually crosses.

## Stage

The Surge does not linger to see what it started. It crosses the threshold once and is already leaving before the first request even fails, its whole contribution spent in the instant it took to pass. [The Service](persona_the_service.md), sound and correctly built, answers what it can and fails the overflow, because that is what a service does when asked for more than its limit, and for one second that overflow is real and small and would, on any ordinary day, simply clear.

## Tension

It does not clear. The Trigger has passed and the Surge is gone, and the first requests have failed anyway, opening a state the Steady State cannot see the size of yet. The cause is already gone. What it opened is still open, and nothing downstream of this beat will need the Surge again to keep it that way.
