# Place: The Queue

## Taxonomy

[Held Under](play_held_under.md). Where requests wait their turn and retries wait behind them, host to [The Storm](plot_the_storm.md) and [The Hold](plot_the_hold.md), the backlog where the metastable state physically lives.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A line of work, longer than it should be, not moving the way a line should move. What is visible is depth: more sitting here than [the Service](persona_the_service.md) can clear in the time it takes new work, and old work resubmitted, to arrive behind it.

## Holds

Fresh requests from [the Caller](persona_the_caller.md) and, mixed in beside them, [the Retry](piece_the_retry.md) of every request that already failed: two kinds of demand that from inside the Queue are indistinguishable and additive. It holds the sum, and the sum is what will not fit.

## Offers

A place in line: any request that arrives, first attempt or third, is accepted and queued in its turn, because that is what a queue does and it does it correctly regardless of what stands behind each item. The offer is order, first come first served, no request refused at the door.

## Withheld

Any mechanism, from within, for telling a request that will help drain the Queue apart from one that will not. The Queue cannot see that a fraction of what it holds is [the Sustaining Load](piece_the_sustaining_load.md): retries of work already failed, arriving to help and, by arriving, keeping the total pinned above what the Service can clear.
