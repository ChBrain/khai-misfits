# Plan: Pass Through

## Taxonomy

## Direction

Cross the threshold once and be gone: push the system out of the good equilibrium at a moment its headroom is thin, and then depart, taking no further part. Do not hold the failure open, because nothing after the first push is needed: the state, once entered, sustains itself on the retries, so the whole of the plan is spent in the instant it takes to perturb and leave.
