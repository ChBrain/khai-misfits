# Place: The Steady State

## Taxonomy

[Held Under](play_held_under.md). The healthy equilibrium the system runs in before anything tips it: where headroom sits unspent and every request clears in its turn, host to [The Spike](plot_the_spike.md), the single crossing out of it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Latency low and flat, queues short, capacity visibly in excess of demand. The graphs sit quiet; nothing here asks to be watched closely. [The Service](persona_the_service.md) answers what arrives with room left over, and the room itself is the point: this is a state with slack in it.

## Holds

The margin between what the system can do and what it is being asked to do, the margin a transient event can spend. It holds [the Caller](persona_the_caller.md)'s ordinary traffic, served promptly enough that nothing accumulates behind it, and it holds the threshold, unmarked, that [the Surge](persona_the_surge.md) will cross once.

## Offers

A working system, on its face, and a plausible return address for it: everything about the Steady State suggests that whatever knocks it down, the system should be able to climb back to it. The offer is stability that looks self-restoring, because within this state, restoring is exactly what the system does.

## Withheld

The way back, once the Surge has passed and the failing state has taken hold. The Steady State cannot be re-entered by the system's own correct service while [the Sustaining Load](piece_the_sustaining_load.md) holds the total above capacity: the door looks open from here but is closed from the other side, not by damage, only by arithmetic.
