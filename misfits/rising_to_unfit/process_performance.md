# Process: Performance

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md). Mastery, visible and rising.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A person doing their role.
Doing it better than anyone near them.

## Direction

Mastery, visible and rising.
Skill that shows, and keeps showing.

## Lever

Excellence against the role it is in.
Best at this, and measured here.

## Echo

A signal read as a reason to move up.
Good work, mistaken for readiness elsewhere.
