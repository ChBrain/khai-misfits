# Piece: The Level of Incompetence

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md). The rung where the rising person's fit finally runs out.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

On [the plateau](place_the_plateau.md), the highest rung the person reached and the first they could not climb.
The role where the promotions stop, because performance stops, because for the first time the job needs what this person does not have.

## Load Bearing

Without it people would rise forever and the ladder would never lodge anyone.
With it the rise has a natural stopping point, and the stopping point is the disaster: the Level of Incompetence is the rung a person reaches by succeeding at every rung below it, so they arrive at their worst role by way of their best ones, promoted out of competence and into the exact seat they cannot fill. It is not that they got worse. It is that the ladder kept moving them until it found the role that did not suit them, and then, by stopping, held them there.

## Apparent

A career that has found its ceiling.
It looks like someone who rose as far as their talent took them and settled, which is how careers are supposed to end. The settling is real. The place it settles is the one role, of all the ones they held, that they are not good at, and it is where they will stay.

## Yearbook

Reached by [the stuck](persona_the_stuck.md), who was excellent one rung ago and is failing now.
It is Peter's own law made local: a person rises to the level of their incompetence and there they remain, because the same success that lifted them is the thing that has just run out, and nothing in the ladder moves a person back down toward the rung they were good at.
