# Position: Manager

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md). The new title, worn over the wrong fit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The bigger title, and the raise that ratified it.
A job that does not fit the hands that earned it.

## Orders

Lead now, do not make.
Set direction, hold the room, own the plan.
The bench is behind you, so face forward.

## Loses

The rung they were good at, left the moment they rose.
The way back down, closed by title and by pride.
The old fit, unreachable from the new seat.

## Drives

Toward performing, in a role never rehearsed.
The test is on skills they were never selected for.
The selection measured the job below, not this one.
