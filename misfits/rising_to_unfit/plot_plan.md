# Plot: Plan

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The calibration](place_the_calibration.md), where performance is rated and promotions are decided.
Someone at [the workbench](place_the_workbench.md) is plainly the best at the craft, and their record is on the table as the reason to move them up.

## Action

[Performance](process_performance.md): [The Natural](persona_the_natural.md), holding [the individual contributor's seat](position_individual_contributor.md), masters the role, visibly and deservedly.
[Promotion](process_promotion.md): [The Rewarder](persona_the_rewarder.md) at [the promoting manager's seat](position_promoting_manager.md) moves to reward that mastery with a bigger role.

## Stage

[The Natural](persona_the_natural.md) tables [Master the Craft](plan_master_the_craft.md): be the best in the room at the thing, and shows [the mastery](piece_the_mastery.md), genuine and earned.
[The Rewarder](persona_the_rewarder.md) tables [Reward the Best](plan_reward_the_best.md): promote on the record, and reads [the wrong signal](piece_the_wrong_signal.md), performance in this role taken as evidence for the next.

## Tension

Nothing here is unfair.
The mastery is real, promoting the best performer is right, and demonstrated performance is the fairest evidence there is. But the evidence is about the job being left, and the job being entered is a different one, so the fairest possible promotion is aimed at the wrong question, and the best is moved up on the strength of the thing they are about to stop doing.
