# Persona: The Rewarder

## Taxonomy

[Promoting Manager](position_promoting_manager.md). The hand that lifts on merit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fair. Appreciative. Wants to reward good work.
Sees the record and honors it.
Believes, sincerely, that the ladder is justice.

## Action

Promotes the top performer on the strength of the record.
Names the wins, cites the numbers, moves them up.
A clean decision, well documented.

## Shadow

Promoting the best is right, and it reads the wrong signal.
Past performance at this role is taken as evidence for a different one.
A fair reward, aimed at the wrong question.

## Tell

Says "you've earned this."
Means the old job, and hands over a new one.
