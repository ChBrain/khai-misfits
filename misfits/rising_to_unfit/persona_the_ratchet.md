# Persona: The Ratchet

## Taxonomy

[HR](position_hr.md). Turns one way only: up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Procedural. Humane. Protects people.
Softens every landing, spares every fall.
Kindness, made into policy.

## Action

Promotes readily.
Demotes never.
Every step forward locks behind them.

## Shadow

Not demoting is decent, and it welds the misfit in place.
Sparing the fall becomes a life sentence at the ceiling.
Kindness about the drop that makes the wrong fit permanent.

## Tell

Says "we don't demote people here."
And does not, ever.
