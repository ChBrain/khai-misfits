# Pitch: Onward and Upward

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Aspirational, meritocratic, deserved.
Every promotion is earned, every rise is a reward for real excellence, and the ladder is fair.
The voice of a career going the right way, spoken by people who each did their last job brilliantly.

## Undertow

Under the merit, a signal read wrong.
Each promotion rewards mastery of the last role with a role that needs something else, and no rung asks whether the gift transfers.
The rise never slows: it only carries the best performer further from the thing they were best at.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching someone brilliant be rewarded,
promoted for excellence into a job that excellence does not fit,
and left there, high and stuck, because the ladder has no way down.

## Echo

After the run: a great engineer running a team badly,
a post that cannot be filled well because filling it well is what empties it,
and an org chart of people who each rose exactly one rung too far.
