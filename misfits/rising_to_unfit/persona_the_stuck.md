# Persona: The Stuck

## Taxonomy

[Manager](position_manager.md). Once the best, now the bottleneck.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Hard-working. Willing. Out of their depth.
Never stops trying, never quite lands it.
The effort is real, the fit is not.

## Action

Takes the bigger role and holds it.
Works as hard as ever, longer than ever.
Performs worse, and cannot see the gap closing.

## Shadow

Not incompetent as a person, stranded in the one seat their competence does not reach.
Excellent one rung ago, failing now.
And the door back is welded shut.

## Tell

Says "I'll figure it out."
Cannot, because the new job needs what they were never measured on.
