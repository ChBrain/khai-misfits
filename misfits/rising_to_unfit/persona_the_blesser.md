# Persona: The Blesser

## Taxonomy

[Executive](position_executive.md). Signs where the numbers point.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Measured. Evidence-led. Signs off on the numbers.
Trusts the record, distrusts the hunch.
Governs by what can be shown.

## Action

Approves the promotion on the record.
Then watches it land badly.
Has nothing on paper to explain the fall.

## Shadow

Blessed a sound-looking move on real evidence, and holds no evidence that would take it back.
The approval is easy, the reversal impossible.
Authority that can promote on a number, and cannot unpromote without one.

## Tell

Says "the numbers supported it."
And they did, for the wrong role.
