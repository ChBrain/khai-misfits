# Piece: The New Job

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md). The role above, which needs different gifts than the one below.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

In [the corner office](place_the_corner_office.md), a desk that faces the other way.
The manager's role above the maker's, the leader's above the manager's: each a real job, and each asking for what the rung below it did not, people instead of the craft, judgement instead of output.

## Load Bearing

Without it a promotion would be more of the same and mastery would carry straight up.
With it a promotion is a change of trade, because the New Job is not a larger version of the old one, it is a different one wearing the same career: the best maker is asked to stop making and start managing makers, which uses none of what made them the best, and tests gifts the promotion never checked for. The gap between the two jobs is where the whole misfit opens, and it is invisible from below, because both roles share a title ladder and neither shares a skill.

## Apparent

The reward for doing well.
It looks like the natural next step, the deserved bigger role, up and to the right. It is bigger and it is up. It is also sideways in every way that matters, a move into a different craft dressed as advancement in the same one.

## Yearbook

Handed to [the natural](persona_the_natural.md), who was promised a reward and given a career change.
It is the trap's quiet centre: nobody says the new job is a different job, because the ladder pretends it is the same one taller, so the master steps up expecting more of what they were good at and finds [the level of incompetence](piece_the_level_of_incompetence.md) instead.
