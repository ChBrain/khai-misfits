# Position: HR

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md). The keeper of the ladder, and the one rule it cannot break.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The promotion machinery, the forms and the ceremony.
The norm that says people move up, never down.

## Orders

Move people up, on schedule, with care.
Never demote, the ladder only climbs.
Protect dignity, and dignity means no step back.

## Loses

The one correction that would help, the step back to fit.
The demotion, humane in truth, forbidden as cruelty.
A path home, closed in the name of kindness.

## Drives

Toward a humane ladder, gentle and one directional.
The gentleness traps the wrong people in place.
No fall means no return, so the misfit stays.
