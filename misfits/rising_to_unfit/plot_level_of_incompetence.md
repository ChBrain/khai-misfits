# Plot: Level of Incompetence

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The corner office](place_the_corner_office.md), the new role taken up, facing the other way.
The job needs people-judgement where the last needed craft, and the gifts that earned the promotion are not the gifts it asks for.

## Action

[Regression](process_regression.md): [The Stuck](persona_the_stuck.md), holding [the manager's seat](position_manager.md), works as hard as ever and performs worse, because the new role tests what they were never measured on.
[Promotion](process_promotion.md): [The Blesser](persona_the_blesser.md) at [the executive's seat](position_executive.md), who signed the move off on the numbers, watches it land badly and cannot unsign it.

## Stage

[The Stuck](persona_the_stuck.md) tables [Rise and Stick](plan_rise_and_stick.md): take the bigger role and hold it, and finds [the new job](piece_the_new_job.md) is a different trade wearing the same career.
[The Blesser](persona_the_blesser.md) tables [Bless the Numbers](plan_bless_the_numbers.md): approve the promotion the record supported, and the record supported [the level of incompetence](piece_the_level_of_incompetence.md), the rung the master cannot climb.

## Tension

No one did wrong, and the master is failing.
They are working hard, the promotion was earned, the executive approved it on sound evidence, and none of it helps, because the new job needs what the old one did not, and nothing checked for that before the move. The rise stops here, at the first rung that does not fit, and the executive who blessed it on the numbers has no number that would let them take it back.
