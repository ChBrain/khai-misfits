# Piece: The Rise

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md). The travelling motion: competence climbing until it exceeds its fit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Up the rungs, one promotion at a time, until the last one that fits.
The career moving through [performance](process_performance.md) into [promotion](process_promotion.md) and again, each rung suiting the person a little less surely than the one before.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a single bad promotion and one unlucky manager.
With it it is a trajectory: the Rise is the mechanism that carries a person past their fit rather than to it, because each promotion is earned by the rung below and gambles on the rung above, and the gamble pays until it does not. It travels one way, upward, because the ladder only lifts: mastery keeps buying the next rung until a rung arrives that mastery cannot do, and there the travel ends, not at the person's best role but at their first bad one. The distance between where they were excellent and where they stopped is the whole climb.

## Apparent

A career on the way up.
Each rung looks like progress, merit rewarded, someone going places. It is progress, right up to the rung that is not, and nothing in the ascent signals which rung that will be until the person is standing on it.

## Yearbook

Driven by [the mastery](piece_the_mastery.md) read as [the wrong signal](piece_the_wrong_signal.md), stopped only by [the level of incompetence](piece_the_level_of_incompetence.md).
It carries the best performer steadily away from their best performance, rung after well-earned rung, until [the backfill](plot_backfill.md) sends the next one up the same slope, and the ladder does again, to someone new, exactly what it did.
