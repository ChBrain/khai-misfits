# Persona: The Natural

## Taxonomy

[Individual Contributor](position_individual_contributor.md). Best-in-room, and everyone knows it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Gifted. Admired. The one you route the hard thing to.
The best in the room at the craft, without trying to be.
People point at the work and say a name.

## Action

Masters the role in the open, deservedly.
Ships the thing others could not.
Gets noticed, exactly as merit says they should.

## Shadow

The excellence is real, and it is the signal that moves them off it.
Being the best at the thing is what gets them taken off the thing.
The reward for mastery is a seat that no longer needs it.

## Tell

Says "I love this work."
Says it right before being promoted out of it.
