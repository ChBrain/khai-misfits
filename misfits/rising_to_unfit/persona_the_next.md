# Persona: The Next

## Taxonomy

[Successor](position_successor.md). The vacated seat, filled and rising.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fresh. Brilliant. On the way up.
The best in the room, again.
Everyone points at a new name.

## Action

Does the craft superbly, is noticed.
Steps into the seat the last one left.
Is set on the very track that stranded them.

## Shadow

Not a fix, a repeat: the seat fills and the same climb begins.
Its promise is the principle recurring, not resolving.
The system does not learn; it reloads.

## Tell

Says "I'm ready for more."
And is, for the wrong more.
