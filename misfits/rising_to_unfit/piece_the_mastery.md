# Piece: The Mastery

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md). Excellence at the current role, the signal the promotion reads.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the workbench](place_the_workbench.md), plainly, in the quality of the work.
The person who is simply the best at the craft: the cleanest code, the surest sale, the sharpest diagnosis, visible and real and rightly admired.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no reason to promote and the ladder has nothing to lift.
With it there is a clear, deserved signal, and the whole play turns on what that signal is taken to mean: the Mastery is genuine excellence at the job being done, and the organisation reads it, reasonably, as a reason to give the person a bigger job. It is not a mistake to see it, or to reward it. It is only a mistake to assume that being excellent at this role predicts being excellent at the next, and the reward is built on exactly that assumption.

## Apparent

Someone who deserves to rise.
It looks like talent finding its level, merit being recognised, the system working. The talent is real and the recognition is fair. What is being recognised is fitness for the role they are in, and that is the one thing the promotion moves them out of.

## Yearbook

Shown by [the natural](persona_the_natural.md), who is genuinely the best in the room at the thing.
It is the honest root of the play: [the wrong signal](piece_the_wrong_signal.md) is not a lie about the person, it is a truth about the wrong role, so the better the mastery, the surer the promotion, and the surer the promotion, the more certainly the master is taken off what they had mastered.
