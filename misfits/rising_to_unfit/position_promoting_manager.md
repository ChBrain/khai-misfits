# Position: Promoting Manager

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md). The hand on the lever, reading the record.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The promotion to give, one slot, one name.
The record of who performed, clean and legible.

## Orders

Reward the best performer, by the book.
Read the numbers, name the top, move them up.
Merit gets the seat, that is the whole rule.

## Loses

The maker, promoted off the work they were good at.
The signal too, accurate about a job no longer theirs.
A record of the old rung, read as fitness for the next.

## Drives

Toward rewarding merit, honestly, every cycle.
The reward points backward, at proven work.
The seat it opens faces a job never proven.
