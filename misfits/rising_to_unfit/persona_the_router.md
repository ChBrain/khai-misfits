# Persona: The Router

## Taxonomy

[Report](position_report.md). The one who quietly keeps it moving.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Loyal. Gets things done.
Steady where the seat above is not.
The work still lands, somehow.

## Action

Quietly delivers the work around the stuck manager above.
Fills the gap without naming it.
Carries the role the role-holder cannot.

## Shadow

The seat that sees the misfit clearly, and holds up the corner office anyway.
Does the job of the rung above, with no standing to change the structure that put them there.
Carries the fit, owns none of the power.

## Tell

Says "I'll just handle it directly."
Routes around the corner office.
