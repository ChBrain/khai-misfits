# Plot: Backfill

## Taxonomy

[Rising to Unfit](play_rising_to_unfit.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The workbench](place_the_workbench.md), where the craft still has to be done and the best hand just left it.
The role the stranded master vacated needs filling, and the surest candidate is whoever is now the best at the bench: the next Natural, already rising.

## Action

[Recurrence](process_recurrence.md): [The Next](persona_the_next.md), holding [the successor's seat](position_successor.md), does the craft brilliantly, is noticed for it, and is set on the very track that stranded the last one.

## Stage

[The Next](persona_the_next.md) tables [Backfill the Star](plan_backfill_the_star.md): step up to replace the risen master, and begins [the rise](piece_the_rise.md), earning each rung by the one below and gambling on the one above.
[The Peter Principle](piece_the_peter_principle.md) stands revealed, not as one bad promotion but as the standing shape of the ladder: every seat filled by someone risen just past their fit, and a fresh master already climbing toward their own.

## Tension

Nobody learns, because there is nothing to catch.
Each promotion was earned, each rise deserved, and the pattern only shows across the whole chart and the whole span of time, never in the single fair decision in front of anyone. So the principle does not get fixed, it gets repeated: the next best performer is rewarded for the job below with the job above ... or, this once, someone rewards the gift the next role needs instead of the one the last role showed, and the ladder, for one promotion, places a person right.
