# Pitch: Rewarding Results

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Purposeful, fair, results-driven.
There is a real problem, and a clear reward for anyone who helps solve it, paid on proof.
The voice of a programme that pays for outcomes, spoken by people who mean well and pay honestly.

## Undertow

Under the fairness, a price on a token.
The reward is paid per unit removed, and a unit removed can be a unit first made, so the price quietly becomes an offer to produce.
The payouts never stop climbing: they only climb because the thing being paid for is now being farmed.

## Nerve

The feeling of a scheme working perfectly,
the numbers up, the bounties flowing, the proof in hand,
while the streets fill with more of exactly what it was meant to clear.

## Echo

After the run: a bounty cancelled and a surplus loosed,
a problem worse than the one that started it,
and a ledger in which every payment was honest and every snake was real.
