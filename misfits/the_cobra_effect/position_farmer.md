# Position: Farmer

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md). The seat that reads the price and supplies exactly what it names.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The means to produce the unit, and a price to sell it at.
A reward posted per unit removed reads as a price per unit made.

## Orders

Earn the reward.
Breed the unit, kill it, hand it in.
Answer the price rationally, since the price is the whole instruction.

## Loses

Nothing; it supplies exactly what is priced.
No rule is broken; the market is only obeyed.
The seat that never cheats is the seat that does the harm.

## Drives

Toward income.
By making what the bounty buys.
The reward set to end the thing becomes the reason to grow it.
