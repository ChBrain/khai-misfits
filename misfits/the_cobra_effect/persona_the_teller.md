# Persona: The Teller

## Taxonomy

[Paymaster](position_paymaster.md). Honest to the last coin, and that is the door.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Honest. Procedural. Pays exactly what is owed.
No favor, no skim, no delay.
The proof comes in, the money goes out.

## Action

Pays on proof it cannot question.
A dead cobra is a dead cobra.
The till sees a token, matches the rate, settles the claim.

## Shadow

The till cannot tell a wild pest from a farmed one.
Its honesty is the farm's cash register.
Paying straight on real proof is exactly what funds the manufactured supply.

## Tell

Says "proof in, payment out, no questions."
No question is the whole problem.
