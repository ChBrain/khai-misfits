# Piece: The Booming Metric

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md). Bounties paid, climbing, read as the problem being solved.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

On [the dashboard](place_the_dashboard.md), a line going up and to the right.
The count of units removed and rewards paid: the programme's headline number, higher every month, exactly the shape success is supposed to have.

## Load Bearing

Without it the farming would show as a cost with no story, and someone might ask sooner.
With it the farming shows as triumph, because the Booming Metric measures the token, not the goal, and the token is booming precisely because it is being manufactured: the same breeding that worsens the real problem is what drives the payout line up, so the graph that looks most like victory is the direct readout of the failure. It is not falsified. Every unit was real and every payment earned. It simply counts the wrong thing, and counts it beautifully.

## Apparent

Success, measured.
It looks like proof the programme works: more removed each month, the reward clearly motivating action. The motivation is real. What it is motivating is production, and the metric cannot tell the difference between a pest cleared and a pest created and cleared.

## Yearbook

Reported by [the scoreboard](persona_the_scoreboard.md), who shows the payouts and calls them progress.
It is the false witness of the play: it rises with [the farmed unit](piece_the_farmed_unit.md) and swears the problem is shrinking, so the louder the success it reports, the deeper the farm it is really measuring, and it drowns out [the noticer](persona_the_noticer.md) with its own good news.
