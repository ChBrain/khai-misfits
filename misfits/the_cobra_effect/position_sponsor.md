# Position: Sponsor

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md). The seat that names the harm and puts a price on removing it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The mandate and the budget to fix a real problem.
The good faith to spend both on it.

## Orders

Reduce the harm.
Reward the result, per unit removed.
Pay for outcomes, plainly, so the plain incentive can work.

## Loses

The goal to its proxy, priced because it could not be counted.
What was rewarded was the token, not the thing.
The design that would have worked was the one never chosen.

## Drives

Toward fewer of the thing.
Through a reward that buys more of it.
Every unit paid for is a unit worth making.
