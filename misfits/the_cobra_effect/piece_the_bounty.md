# Piece: The Bounty

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md). The reward paid per unit removed, set in good faith.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Announced from [the podium](place_the_podium.md), a fixed price per dead cobra.
The clean, fair instrument: bring in the proof, collect the reward, no questions about where it came from.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is only a problem and no lever, and the play has no engine.
With it there is a price on a token, and a price on a token is an offer to supply it: the Bounty pays for a unit removed, and never asks whether that unit was in the city yesterday or bred this morning, because it cannot tell and was not built to. It is not a bad idea, it is a precise one, aimed at the countable proxy for the goal, and precision aimed at a proxy is exactly what a farm needs to sell into.

## Apparent

A fair reward for real work.
It looks like results-based policy at its cleanest: pay for the outcome, let anyone earn it, no bureaucracy. Paying for outcomes is sound. Paying for the token that stands in for the outcome is where the door opens.

## Yearbook

Set by [the well-meaner](persona_the_well_meaner.md), who priced the token because the goal could not be counted.
It is the instrument the whole play turns on: a reward pointed at [the real problem](piece_the_real_problem.md) through a proxy anyone can produce, so the better it pays, the more worth making the proxy becomes, and the price meant to draw the supply down is the price that calls it into being.
