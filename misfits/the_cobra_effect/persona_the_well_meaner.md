# Persona: The Well-Meaner

## Taxonomy

[Sponsor](position_sponsor.md). Means well, prices the problem, opens the market.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Earnest. Public-spirited. Sees a real problem and moves to fix it.
Wants the streets safer, the count lower, the harm drawn down.
Good faith, all the way through.

## Action

Sets a fair bounty: so much per unit removed.
A price meant to pull the supply toward zero.
Pay for the removal, and the removals will come.

## Shadow

It priced the token because the goal itself could not be counted.
A price on a producible token is an offer to produce it.
The good intent aimed at the countable proxy is what opens the farm.

## Tell

Says "we'll pay for results," and means outcomes.
Buys tokens.
