# Process: Farming

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md). The unit made, not hunted.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A price worth supplying, posted where anyone can read it.
The plain sum where breeding beats searching.

## Direction

The unit made, not hunted, on schedule and at scale.
Production pointed straight at the counter's open door.

## Lever

Making against hunting, breed cheaper than track.
The market picks the cheaper path every single time.

## Echo

A supply that exists only because the reward does.
Stock with no reason to be, save the coin that calls for it.
