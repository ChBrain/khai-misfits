# Piece: The Manufactured Supply

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md). The reward calling into being the very thing it pays to remove.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Between [the farm](place_the_farm.md) and [the bounty office](place_the_bounty_office.md), a stock that exists only because the reward does.
The population of cobras that would not be alive but for the price on their deaths: a supply summoned by its own demand.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a reward that merely fails to work.
With it the reward does worse than fail, it reverses, because the Manufactured Supply is the mechanism that makes the cure a cause: a bounty on removal is, to anyone who can produce the thing, a standing offer to produce it, so the reward does not find the problem and shrink it, it prices the problem and grows it. This is the line that separates the cobra effect from a merely gamed metric: the target is not just miscounted, it is manufactured, brought into the world by the incentive meant to clear it.

## Apparent

Demand being met.
It looks like the market responding to a price, which is what a good incentive is supposed to cause. The response is real and rational. The thing it is supplying is the very harm the price was meant to end.

## Yearbook

Grown by [the breeder](persona_the_breeder.md), priced by [the bounty](piece_the_bounty.md), unseen by [the booming metric](piece_the_booming_metric.md).
It is the engine of [the backfire](piece_the_backfire.md): every reward paid funds the next unit made, so the programme buys its own worsening, and the stock keeps growing for exactly as long as the price stays up, waiting for the day it comes down.
