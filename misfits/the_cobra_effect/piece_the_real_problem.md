# Piece: The Real Problem

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md). The actual harm the bounty was meant to reduce.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Out on [the street](place_the_street.md), the thing everyone can see and no one can count exactly.
The cobras in the city, the harm to be lessened: real, urgent, and impossible to measure directly, which is why a countable proxy was rewarded in its place.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no goal, and the bounty is a reward for nothing.
With it there is a goal that cannot be seen whole: the Real Problem is what everyone actually wants reduced, and the very reason a proxy was needed, because you cannot pay per cobra-not-in-the-city, only per cobra-handed-in. The gap between the goal and the token is where the whole play lives: the token can be manufactured and the goal cannot be faked, so the reward flows to the token and the goal is left to move on its own, which, under a breeding incentive, is the wrong way.

## Apparent

The thing being solved.
From the podium it looks like the target of the programme, and it is, in intent. The trouble is that it is never what the money touches: the money touches the token, and the goal only benefits if the token and the goal happen to move together, which the bounty ensures they do not.

## Yearbook

Named by [the well-meaner](persona_the_well_meaner.md), and lived with by [the snakebit](persona_the_snakebit.md).
It is the quiet centre of the play: everyone is working on it, talking about it, funding its reduction, and nobody's reward is actually tied to it, because it could not be counted, so [the booming metric](piece_the_booming_metric.md) rises in its name while it, unmeasured, gets worse.
