# Persona: The Snakebit

## Taxonomy

[Resident](position_resident.md). Set no price, bred no unit, holds the whole sum.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Plain. Unlucky. Lives where the scheme ran.
Never sponsored it, never farmed it, never called the numbers good.
Just here when the sheds opened.

## Action

Inherits a city with more cobras than before any of it began.
Walks the same street, now thick with the surplus.
Carries what the reward left behind.

## Shadow

Not a hero, just the one left standing in the result.
It never set a price or bred a unit and carries the whole perverse sum.
A surplus that outlives the scheme that made it.

## Tell

Says "there are more of them now than when they started."
There are.
