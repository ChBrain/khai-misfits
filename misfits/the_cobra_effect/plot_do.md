# Plot: Do

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The farm](place_the_farm.md), where the reward meets someone who can supply it.
The bounty is live, and breeding a cobra to kill for the price turns out to cost less than hunting a wild one. At [the bounty office](place_the_bounty_office.md) the till is open and asks no questions.

## Action

[Farming](process_farming.md): [The Breeder](persona_the_breeder.md), holding [the farmer's seat](position_farmer.md), rears cobras as a crop, each one a route from the reward to a pocket.
[Payout](process_payout.md): [The Teller](persona_the_teller.md) at [the paymaster's seat](position_paymaster.md) pays on proof it cannot question, a dead cobra being a dead cobra at the counter.

## Stage

[The Breeder](persona_the_breeder.md) tables [Farm the Bounty](plan_farm_the_bounty.md): make the unit rather than hunt it, and [the farmed unit](piece_the_farmed_unit.md) is born to be a bounty.
[The Teller](persona_the_teller.md) tables [Pay on Proof](plan_pay_on_proof.md): reward every proof brought in, and [the manufactured supply](piece_the_manufactured_supply.md) grows in the exact shape of the price.

## Tension

Nobody is cheating.
The breeder is farming, responding to the price as offered, and the till is paying honestly on real proof. But rewarding the removal of a thing is, to anyone who can produce it, a reward for producing it, so the supply is manufactured instead of drawn down, and every payout funds the next unit bred. The reward is working perfectly, and it is making more of what it pays to remove.
