# Plot: The Release

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The street](place_the_street.md), the farming undeniable and the bounty about to be pulled.
The scheme is exposed as a farm, the sensible correction is obvious, and the correction is the trigger: end the reward, and the stock it created is suddenly worth nothing.

## Action

[Release](process_release.md): [The Repealer](persona_the_repealer.md), holding [the comptroller's seat](position_comptroller.md), pulls the bounty to stop the waste, and the breeding sheds, holding cobras now worth nothing, are emptied into the city.

## Stage

[The Repealer](persona_the_repealer.md) tables [Pull the Bounty](plan_pull_the_bounty.md): end the reward at last, and [the dumped stock](piece_the_dumped_stock.md) is loosed, the whole manufactured supply set free the hour its price dies.
[The Snakebit](persona_the_snakebit.md) at [the resident's seat](position_resident.md) tables [Live With the Surplus](plan_live_with_the_surplus.md): inherit a city with more cobras than before any of it began, and [the cobra effect](piece_the_cobra_effect.md) stands revealed, a reward that bred the problem it paid to remove.

## Tension

Nobody cheated, and the fix made it worse.
The sponsor meant well, the farmer was rational, the till was honest, and pulling the bounty was the right call, and pulling it strands the supply the bounty created, which has to go somewhere, and goes into the street. The reckoning is not a swindle exposed, it is a price correcting after it has already summoned the harm ... or a new bounty is set on the new surplus, and the farm reopens under a fresh name.
