# Process: Reporting

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md). The token shown as the goal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A rising payout line, ready to be read aloud.
The urge to show motion as if motion were the aim.

## Direction

The token shown as the goal, the proxy dressed as the prize.
The chart carried upward to the room that funds it.

## Lever

Metric against reality, count what pays not what matters.
What is easy to count becomes what is said to count.

## Echo

Failure reported as success, in confident ink.
The louder the boom, the harder the loss is to hear.
