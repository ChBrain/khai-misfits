# Piece: The Cobra Effect

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md). The accumulated whole: a cure that becomes the engine of the disease.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

The gap between the reward and its goal.
On one side, a bounty paid honestly for every unit removed. On the other, a city with more of the unit than before the bounty existed. The Cobra Effect is the standing distance between what was rewarded and what resulted, and the certainty that the reward widened it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a run of honest payments and one policy that did not work.
With it those are one object: an incentive that produced the exact thing it paid to remove, held whole by nobody, because the seat that set the price never met the farm, the seat that ran the farm never set the price, and no seat was ever answerable for the distance between the token and the goal. It is the perverse incentive complete: not a reward that failed, a reward that reversed, funding its own defeat one honest payout at a time.

## Apparent

A well-run programme.
From every seat inside it, the scheme is working: the sponsor set a fair price, the office paid on real proof, the farmer earned honestly, the metric rose. The trap is invisible from within because there is no cheat to find, only a price on a token that could be made, and everyone doing exactly what the price invited.

## Yearbook

The programme built it in good faith: [the bounty](piece_the_bounty.md) set, [the farmed unit](piece_the_farmed_unit.md) raised, [the booming metric](piece_the_booming_metric.md) reported, [the manufactured supply](piece_the_manufactured_supply.md) grown.
[The repealer](persona_the_repealer.md) ended it and, ending it, loosed the surplus and made it worse, proving the whole shape at once: the reward had been breeding the problem all along. Nobody had a name for the scheme while the payouts climbed ... until the day the streets were fuller than when it began.
