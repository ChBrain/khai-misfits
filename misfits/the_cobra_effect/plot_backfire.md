# Plot: Backfire

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The dashboard](place_the_dashboard.md), the payout line climbing month on month.
The programme reports success, more units removed than ever, while out on the street the cobras are visibly more, not fewer. At [the audit](place_the_audit.md), someone starts to trace where the proof is coming from.

## Action

[Reporting](process_reporting.md): [The Scoreboard](persona_the_scoreboard.md), holding [the reporter's seat](position_reporter.md), shows the rising bounties and calls the rise progress.
[Detection](process_detection.md): [The Noticer](persona_the_noticer.md) at [the auditor's seat](position_auditor.md) follows the supply, and finds the farm behind the proof.

## Stage

[The Scoreboard](persona_the_scoreboard.md) tables [Report the Payout](plan_report_the_payout.md): present the metric as the problem shrinking, and [the booming metric](piece_the_booming_metric.md) swears success in the language of the token.
[The Noticer](persona_the_noticer.md) tables [Follow the Supply](plan_follow_the_supply.md): ask where the cobras come from, and reads [the backfire](piece_the_backfire.md), payments up and the problem up with them.

## Tension

The metric is not lying.
Every unit it counts was real and every payment was earned, so the graph looks exactly like victory, and the seat that reads the token drowns out the seat that walked the street. The noticer sees the two lines diverging early and is answered by the boom, so the farming grows undeniable slowly, one honest record at a time, while the dashboard reports the failure as a win.
