# Persona: The Breeder

## Taxonomy

[Farmer](position_farmer.md). Reads the price, works the price, honestly.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Enterprising. Rational. Works the price on offer.
Sees a rate posted and does the arithmetic.
No malice in it: only supply meeting demand.

## Action

Rears the unit as a crop.
Making beats hunting: steadier yield, lower cost.
Breeds to the bounty and delivers the proof.

## Shadow

Not a cheat, a farmer: it responds to the incentive exactly as designed.
Every unit it breeds is one that would not exist but for the reward.
Rational supply, meeting an offered price.

## Tell

Says "the price is good, so I'll make them."
Does, honestly.
