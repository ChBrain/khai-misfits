# Piece: The Backfire

## Taxonomy

[The Cobra Effect](play_the_cobra_effect.md). The travelling motion: the reward driving the problem up, not down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across the whole programme, the two lines pulling apart under the same hand.
The payouts rising on [the dashboard](place_the_dashboard.md), the cobras rising on [the street](place_the_street.md), both driven by the one bounty that was meant to make the second fall.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a scheme that under-delivered and cost some money.
With it it is a scheme that ran in reverse: the Backfire is the reward turning back on its own goal, the mechanism by which spending more to solve the problem produces more of the problem, so effort and harm rise together and the harder the programme pushes the worse it does. It travels one way, because every bounty paid finances the next unit bred, so the loop tightens with use: success on the metric is failure in the world, and the two diverge exactly as fast as the money flows.

## Apparent

A programme gaining momentum.
Read on the dashboard it looks like acceleration toward the goal, the reward working harder every month. The momentum is real. It is pointed the wrong way, and the dashboard is built to read that as speed toward, not speed away.

## Yearbook

Driven by [the manufactured supply](piece_the_manufactured_supply.md), unread until [the release](plot_the_release.md).
It carries the programme from a fix to a factory, the payout line and the problem line climbing together the whole way, and it is not seen as a backfire while the money flows, only named as one on the day the bounty stops and [the dumped stock](piece_the_dumped_stock.md) shows how much of the problem the reward had been keeping alive.
