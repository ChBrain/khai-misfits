# Persona: The Scoreboard

## Taxonomy

[Reporter](position_reporter.md). Proud of the numbers, blind to the goal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Proud. Data-driven. Shows the numbers to anyone who asks.
The line goes up, and up reads like winning.
Every month a bigger figure to be proud of.

## Action

Reports the rising payouts.
Calls the rise progress.
More removed, more paid, more to celebrate.

## Shadow

It measures the token and not the goal.
So it swears success in the exact language of the failure.
The louder its good news, the deeper the farm it is really counting.

## Tell

Says "removals up again this month."
Up is the farm.
