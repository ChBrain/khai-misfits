# Persona: The Noticer

## Taxonomy

[Auditor](position_auditor.md). Right first, heard last.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Sceptical. Walks the street instead of the dashboard.
Trusts what the ground shows over what the ledger claims.
Asks where the supply is actually coming from.

## Action

Follows the supply.
Finds the farm behind the proof.
Traces the token back to the shed that grew it.

## Shadow

The seat that sees the two lines diverging early: payments up and cobras up.
It is answered by the boom, the very numbers it doubts.
Being right first is not enough to be heard until the farming is undeniable.

## Tell

Says "where are these coming from?"
To a room reading the dashboard.
