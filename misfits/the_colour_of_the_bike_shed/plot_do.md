# Plot: Do

## Taxonomy

[The Colour of the Bike Shed](play_the_colour_of_the_bike_shed.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The floor](place_the_floor.md), opened for discussion.
The shed is up first and the room comes alive. The reactor waits below it, and the minutes are already beginning to run.

## Action

[Deliberation](process_deliberation.md) and [expansion](process_expansion.md): [The Everyman](persona_the_everyman.md), holding [the contributor's seat](position_contributor.md), has a real view on the colour, and so does the next seat, and the next, and the shed debate swells to fill the hour.
[Deferral](process_deferral.md): [The Deferrer](persona_the_deferrer.md) at [the abstainer's seat](position_abstainer.md) says nothing on the reactor, leaving it to the experts.

## Stage

[The Everyman](persona_the_everyman.md) tables [Weigh In](plan_weigh_in.md): contribute where there is something to say, and [the expanding item](piece_the_expanding_item.md) grows with every voice.
[The Deferrer](persona_the_deferrer.md) tables [Defer to the Experts](plan_defer_to_the_experts.md): hold back where there is not, and [the comprehension gap](piece_the_comprehension_gap.md) routes every honest contribution to the shed and every honest silence past the reactor.

## Tension

Every seat is behaving well.
Speaking up where you can help and holding back where you cannot is exactly what a good member does. But the two together pour all the room's energy onto the item it grasps and none onto the item that matters, so the more sincerely the room engages, the more completely it engages with the paint, and the reactor sits untouched while the hour drains.
