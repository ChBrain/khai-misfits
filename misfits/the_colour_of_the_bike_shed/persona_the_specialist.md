# Persona: The Specialist

## Taxonomy

[Reactor Owner](position_reactor_owner.md). The one who brought the hard thing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Expert, prepared, a little anxious for a real review.
Wants the room to dig in. Comes ready to be argued with.

## Action

Tables the reactor, hoping to be challenged.
Lays out the risk, the cost, the failure modes... and gets a nod.

## Shadow

The one seat that wanted scrutiny cannot get it. The room's silence is not
agreement, it is incomprehension. It brings the vital thing and is met by a
wall of politeness, which is worse than an argument, because it cannot be answered.

## Tell

Says "please, push back on this."
The room has no purchase to push, so it does not.
