# Persona: The Auditor

## Taxonomy

[Reckoner](position_reckoner.md). Plain, late, holding the file.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Plain, after-the-fact, holding the file. Speaks once the day is over.
Not the sharpest voice in the room, just the last one.

## Action

Lays the minutes beside the outcome and asks how it passed.
Reads the record back. Names what the record does not hold.

## Shadow

Not sharp, just later. It sees the triviality only once the reactor has
failed, when the answer is free to say. The reckoning names the pattern but
does not un-approve the reactor: hindsight arrives clean, and too late to spend.

## Tell

Says "there's nothing in the minutes about this."
There is not.
