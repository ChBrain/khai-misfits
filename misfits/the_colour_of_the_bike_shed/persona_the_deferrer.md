# Persona: The Deferrer

## Taxonomy

[Abstainer](position_abstainer.md). Modest, sensible, knows the edge of its knowing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Modest, sensible, aware of its limits. Will not speak past its competence.
Trusts the experts to hold the expert thing.

## Action

Stays quiet on the reactor, leaving it to those who understand it.
Says nothing, and counts saying nothing as respect.

## Shadow

The humility is correct and fatal. Deferring where you lack expertise is
right. But a room of deferrers leaves the vital item with no one to question
it. The reasonable silence becomes the rubber stamp, one polite abstention at a time.

## Tell

Says "that's above my pay grade."
The reactor passes on a table full of pay grades below it.
