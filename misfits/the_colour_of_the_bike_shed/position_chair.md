# Position: Chair

## Taxonomy

[The Colour of the Bike Shed](play_the_colour_of_the_bike_shed.md). The seat that runs the room, and levels it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The agenda and the gavel.
The right to open, to close, to call the next item.

## Orders

Run an orderly meeting.
Cover every item on the page.
Keep the room moving, keep the room polite.

## Loses

The stakes, flattened by the order itself.
Every item weighted the same, so the largest reads no larger than the smallest.
The room stays orderly. The order is the loss.

## Drives

Toward a tidy meeting.
A tidy meeting is a meeting spent by appetite.
The gavel falls on time, on nothing.
