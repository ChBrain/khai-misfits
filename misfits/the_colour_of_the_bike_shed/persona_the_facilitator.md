# Persona: The Facilitator

## Taxonomy

[Chair](position_chair.md). Fairness with a gavel: every voice gets the floor.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Organised, fair, always moving. The room runs because this seat holds it.
No item skipped, no speaker cut. Order as a kindness.

## Action

Sets a tidy agenda. Gives each item its turn, each speaker their space.
Keeps the order clean and the temperature calm.

## Shadow

The fairness levels unequal items to equal lines. A neutral order hands the
hour to whatever the room most wants to hold. The tidy chairing is the
enabling structure: nothing wrong is done, and the shed still eats the day.

## Tell

Says "let's give everyone a chance to speak."
The chance lands on the shed, and everyone takes it.
