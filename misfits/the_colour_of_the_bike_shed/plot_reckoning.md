## Cue

## Action

[Reckoning](process_reckoning.md): [The Auditor](persona_the_auditor.md), holding [the reckoner's seat](position_reckoner.md), lays the minutes beside the outcome and finds the hour of care spent on the paint and the sentence spent on the reactor.

## Stage

[The Auditor](persona_the_auditor.md) tables [Ask How It Passed](plan_ask_how_it_passed.md): ask how a decision that large cleared with no discussion, and [the triviality](piece_the_triviality.md) stands revealed: a room fully engaged, and engaged with nothing that mattered.

## Tension

Nobody was negligent.
Every seat contributed in good faith, the chair ran a tidy meeting, the experts were deferred to as experts should be. The reckoning is not a lazy room exposed, it is a diligent one: the scrutiny was real and spent by comprehension instead of consequence, so the answer to how it passed is simply that everyone was busy with the shed ... or no one asks, the file is closed, and the next agenda is laid out just as flat.
