# Plot: Check

## Taxonomy

## Owner

## Cue

The hour nearly gone, and the reactor still on the page.
There are five minutes left and one large decision untouched. To finish on time, it must be disposed of, not discussed.

## Action

[Consent](process_consent.md): [The Clock](persona_the_clock.md), holding [the timekeeper's seat](position_timekeeper.md), moves the reactor to [the consent agenda](place_the_consent_agenda.md) and puts it to the room as a block, approved without objection, so the meeting can close on the hour.

## Stage

[The Clock](persona_the_clock.md) tables [Beat the Clock](plan_beat_the_clock.md): end on time by waving the undiscussed item through, and [the rubber stamp](piece_the_rubber_stamp.md) records incomprehension as approval.
In [the minutes](place_the_minutes.md), [the drift](piece_the_drift.md) is set down in ink: the paint debated at length, the reactor passed in a line.

## Tension

The silence looks exactly like assent.
No one objects, because no one has a question they can phrase, and a room with no questions reads as a room with no concerns. So the vital decision clears with zero scrutiny under the same nod that clears the genuinely trivial items beside it, and the minutes, faithfully, show an hour of care spent on the shed and a sentence spent on the thing that mattered.
