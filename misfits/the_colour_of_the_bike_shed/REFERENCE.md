# The Colour of the Bike Shed: Reference

The research warrant for [The Colour of the Bike Shed](play_the_colour_of_the_bike_shed.md).
The name is Parkinson's parable of the finance committee; the mechanics the play runs on
are grounded in the literature on triviality, bounded rationality, and group deliberation
cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging the law of triviality as a **systemic system**: a deliberating body in which every
seat contributes sincerely and the vital decision is waved through anyway, because scrutiny
flows to what the room can comprehend rather than to what carries the stakes. The play
models the documented mechanics, time spent inversely to consequence, discussion expanding
to fill its slot, deliberation pooling on commonly graspable content, and the bounded
rationality that caps what any seat can weigh, as the load-bearing vectors of the structure,
not as a tale of a lazy or unserious room. The governing finding of the source literature is
the play's governing law: attention is allocated by comprehensibility, not by consequence, so
a body spends its scrutiny in inverse proportion to the stakes, and it runs one direction,
because engagement breeds engagement on the graspable item while silence breeds silence on
the opaque one.

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast
  any seat as lazy or unserious, because the source finding is precisely that the room is
  diligent: every contribution is sincere, and speaking where competent and deferring where not
  are exactly what good members do. The misfit is that both, together, spend the hour on the
  paint.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into clarity arriving in the room. The
  reckoning is the un-scrutinised decision failing out in the world and an auditor naming it
  after the fact, not a seat cutting through the drift in time to stop it.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed
  here, never claimed as the house's. The staging is the house's; the findings are theirs.
