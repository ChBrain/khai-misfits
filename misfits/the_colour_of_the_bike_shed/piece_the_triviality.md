# Piece: The Triviality

## Taxonomy

[The Colour of the Bike Shed](play_the_colour_of_the_bike_shed.md). The accumulated whole: effort spent in inverse proportion to what it was worth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

The gap between the two lines, standing.
The stakes ran high on the reactor and low on the shed; the attention ran low on the reactor and high on the shed. The Triviality is the distance between where the weight was and where the work went.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a long agenda item and a short one.
With it those are one object: a room that got the small thing exactly right and the large thing entirely unexamined, held whole by nobody, because the seat that could weigh the reactor was never the seat with the floor, and the seats with the floor could only weigh the shed. It is diligence and disaster in the same hour, and neither cancels the other, because the diligence was real and aimed at nothing, and the disaster was quiet and aimed at everything.

## Apparent

A productive meeting.
From every seat the hour felt useful: opinions heard, a decision reached, an agenda cleared. The trap is invisible from within because the failure has no moment, no row, no missed step, only an item that got the nod it should have got a grilling.

## Yearbook

The room built it in good faith: [the flat agenda](piece_the_flat_agenda.md), [the expanding item](piece_the_expanding_item.md), [the rubber stamp](piece_the_rubber_stamp.md), each a reasonable act.
[The auditor](persona_the_auditor.md) was the first to lay the minutes beside the outcome and see it: an hour of genuine care spent on the paint, a sentence spent on the thing that broke. Nobody had a name for the meeting until the reactor it waved through came due.
