# Piece: The Expanding Item

## Taxonomy

[The Colour of the Bike Shed](play_the_colour_of_the_bike_shed.md). The trivial discussion swelling to fill the time it is given.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across [the floor](place_the_floor.md), one contribution making room for the next.
The shed debate that was down for five minutes and takes fifty, each seat's view opening a door for another's.

## Load Bearing

Without it the trivial item is dealt with and the meeting moves on.
With it the trivial item is never quite finished, because a discussion everyone can join is a discussion that always has one more voice to add: the Expanding Item is Parkinson's law made local, the debate growing to consume exactly the time available, and it consumes that time precisely because nothing bars entry. Every seat is qualified, so every seat contributes, so the item swells with the room's full participation until the clock, not the question, ends it.

## Apparent

Thoroughness.
It looks like a room leaving no stone unturned, hearing everyone out, building consensus properly. It is a room turning the one small stone it can lift, again and again, while the boulder sits unmoved.

## Yearbook

Driven by [the everyman](persona_the_everyman.md), and by all, because all can.
It is the meeting's clock-thief: every minute it takes is a minute [the reactor](piece_the_reactor.md) does not get, so the trivial item does not merely waste time, it spends the exact time the vital item needed, and hands the shortfall to the clock to resolve.
