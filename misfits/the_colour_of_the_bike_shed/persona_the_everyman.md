# Persona: The Everyman

## Taxonomy

[Contributor](position_contributor.md). Keen, warm, glad to be useful.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Engaged, opinionated, keen to help. Leans in. Wants to add value.
Reads the room and wants to earn its seat.

## Action

Has a genuine view on the shed's colour and offers it, warmly.
A small idea, well meant, easy to say.

## Shadow

Contributes exactly where it can, which is the trivial. Its sincerity is fuel
for the drift. A good member doing good-member things onto the wrong item:
no cynicism anywhere, and the hour still drains into the paint.

## Tell

Says "if I could just add one thing on the paint."
So could everyone, and everyone does.
