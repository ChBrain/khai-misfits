# Piece: The Reactor

## Taxonomy

[The Colour of the Bike Shed](play_the_colour_of_the_bike_shed.md). The vital item almost no one can question.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

On [the table](place_the_table.md) too, one line above the shed, with three more zeroes.
The consequential decision: the system, the contract, the architecture that the whole thing will run on for a decade.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no stake in the room, and the meeting is only a meeting.
With it there is everything to weigh and no one able to weigh it: the Reactor is where the consequence lives and where the comprehension does not, so it presents to the room as a wall, opaque and specialist, that a seat can only nod at or expose itself against. It is the item that most needs scrutiny and least invites it, because scrutiny requires understanding, and understanding is the one thing the room mostly lacks here and cannot admit lacking.

## Apparent

Handled.
It looks settled, professional, above the room's pay grade and safely in expert hands. The confidence is a guess. No one checked, because checking would mean revealing they could not.

## Yearbook

Tabled by [the specialist](persona_the_specialist.md), who brought it hoping to be challenged.
It is the cruel centre of the play: the one seat that wanted a hard look gets a rubber stamp, because the room's silence is not agreement but incomprehension wearing agreement's face, and a wall no one climbs is passed as a wall no one objected to.
