# Piece: The Rubber Stamp

## Taxonomy

[The Colour of the Bike Shed](play_the_colour_of_the_bike_shed.md). The vital decision approved for lack of anyone to question it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the consent agenda](place_the_consent_agenda.md), and then in [the minutes](place_the_minutes.md).
The reactor moved into the block of items passed together without discussion, so the meeting can close on the hour: approved, unanimously, in silence.

## Load Bearing

Without it the reactor waits for a meeting with time, and might yet be examined.
With it the reactor is disposed of, because a decision no one debated is not held over, it is waved through, and the waving looks identical to assent. The Rubber Stamp is where incomprehension is recorded as approval: the room did not decide the reactor was sound, it decided the meeting should end, and the reactor rode out on the second decision as if it were the first.

## Apparent

A clean approval.
It looks like an item so uncontroversial it needed no debate, sailing through on the nod. Sometimes that is what a consent item is. This time it is the opposite: not too small to discuss, too large to grasp.

## Yearbook

Applied by [the clock](persona_the_clock.md), to end the meeting on time.
It is the play's quiet catastrophe: the same silence that means yes for a genuinely minor item means only unfollowed for a major one, and the consent agenda cannot tell the two apart, so it stamps the reactor with the authority meant for the paperclips.
