# Piece: The Bike Shed

## Taxonomy

[The Colour of the Bike Shed](play_the_colour_of_the_bike_shed.md). The trivial item everyone can hold an opinion on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

On [the table](place_the_table.md), a small line item with a small budget.
The shed to be built by the car park: what colour, what roofing, whether a rack for six bikes or eight.

## Load Bearing

Without it the meeting has nothing it can chew, and the hour might go to the reactor by default.
With it the room has an item perfectly sized for a committee: cheap enough that a mistake costs little, and simple enough that every seat can picture it and therefore judge it. The Bike Shed is not important, and that is exactly its power: because it is understood by all, it is the one thing on the agenda the whole room is qualified to argue, so it draws the argument the whole room wants to have.

## Apparent

A decision being taken seriously.
It looks like diligence: careful people weighing options, canvassing views, not rushing a choice. The care is real. The choice is the paint.

## Yearbook

Tabled by [the handyman](persona_the_handyman.md), who expected it to take five minutes.
It is the friendliest item on the page and the greediest: it asks for nothing and takes everything, because a room starved of things it can grasp will feed on the first one it finds, and feed until the clock stops it.
