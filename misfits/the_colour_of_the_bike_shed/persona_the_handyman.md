# Persona: The Handyman

## Taxonomy

[Shed Owner](position_shed_owner.md). Small ask, quick yes, or so it thinks.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Practical, easy, unhurried. Expects a fast decision and a clean exit.
Brought the smallest item in the room.

## Action

Tables the bike shed for a five-minute sign-off.
Colour, placement, done. A courtesy, really.

## Shadow

Tables the one item the whole room can grip, and unwittingly hands it the
meeting. The small thing that eats the hour is not its fault but its item:
everyone has a paint opinion, and every opinion is free.

## Tell

Says "just need a quick decision on the shed."
Loses the afternoon to it, warmly, blamelessly.
