# Pitch: Fully Engaged

## Taxonomy

[The Colour of the Bike Shed](play_the_colour_of_the_bike_shed.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Lively, involved, productive.
Every seat has something to say and says it well, and the discussion is genuinely good.
The voice of a room doing its job with energy, spoken by people who each, honestly, are contributing.

## Undertow

Under the engagement, a target chosen for the wrong reason.
The room debates hardest what it grasps easiest, and grasps easiest what matters least.
The energy never flags: it only pools around the shed while the reactor waits, and passes.

## Nerve

The feeling of a great discussion,
sharp and full and well chaired,
about the one item on the page that did not need it.

## Echo

After the run: a bike shed painted by committee,
a reactor approved in a sentence,
and minutes that show an hour of diligence spent exactly where it could do no good.
