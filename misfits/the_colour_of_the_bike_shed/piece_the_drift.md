# Piece: The Drift

## Taxonomy

[The Colour of the Bike Shed](play_the_colour_of_the_bike_shed.md). The travelling axis: attention flowing to the shed, away from the reactor.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across the whole meeting, minute by minute, in one direction.
The room's focus sliding steadily toward the item it can hold and away from the item it cannot, gathering pace as each contribution invites the next.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit is one badly chaired hour and a bit of bad luck.
With it it is a current: the Drift is why attention does not merely land on the trivial but keeps landing there, because every seat that speaks on the shed lowers the bar for the next seat to speak on the shed, while the reactor grows more untouchable with every minute it goes unspoken. It runs one way only, because engagement breeds engagement on the graspable side and silence breeds silence on the other, so the two items pull apart the whole hour and never converge.

## Apparent

A discussion finding its level.
It looks natural, organic, the room settling on what it cares about. What it is settling on is what it can follow, and the naturalness is the trap: nothing forces the drift, which is exactly why nothing stops it.

## Yearbook

Carried by every seat and reversed by none, until [the reckoning](plot_the_reckoning.md).
It runs the attention down toward [the bike shed](piece_the_bike_shed.md) while the stakes stay up on [the reactor](piece_the_reactor.md), the two lines drawing apart, and by the time the clock calls the hour the room has spent all of its scrutiny exactly where it was worth the least.
