# Piece: The Comprehension Gap

## Taxonomy

[The Colour of the Bike Shed](play_the_colour_of_the_bike_shed.md). Attention allocated by what can be understood, not by what matters.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Between the two items on [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the distance the room cannot cross.
On one side the shed, wholly graspable; on the other the reactor, wholly not; and the room's scrutiny pooling entirely on the near side.

## Load Bearing

Without it the room might spend its hour by importance and the play never happens.
With it the room spends its hour by legibility, because a person can only weigh what they understand, and so attention flows not to the decision that matters but to the decision that can be followed. The Comprehension Gap is the single mechanism the whole play turns on: it is not that the room is lazy or unserious, it is that engagement requires comprehension, and comprehension and consequence sit on opposite sides of the table.

## Apparent

Reasonable participation.
Contributing where you have something useful to say, and holding back where you do not, is exactly what a good member does. Each seat is behaving well. The sum is a room that scrutinises in inverse proportion to stakes.

## Yearbook

Felt by every seat and closed by none, named most clearly by [the deferrer](persona_the_deferrer.md).
It is why [the bike shed](piece_the_bike_shed.md) swells and [the reactor](piece_the_reactor.md) passes: the gap routes every honest contribution toward the trivial, so the more sincerely the room engages, the more completely it engages with the wrong thing.
