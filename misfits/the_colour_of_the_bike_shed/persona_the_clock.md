# Persona: The Clock

## Taxonomy

[Timekeeper](position_timekeeper.md). Brisk, watchful, guards the hour.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Brisk, responsible, watching the time. Keeps the room to its slot.
Closing on schedule is the job, and the job is done well.

## Action

Sees the hour nearly gone. Moves the reactor to consent, closes on time.
Efficient, decisive, on the minute.

## Shadow

Ends the meeting punctually by disposing of the one item that needed it most.
Punctuality becomes the instrument of the rubber stamp: the discipline that
should protect the work is the very thing that waves it through unread.

## Tell

Says "we're almost out of time, can we take the reactor as read."
The room, relieved, does.
