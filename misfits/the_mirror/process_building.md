# Process: Building

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). Each team building the module it owns, densely inside and thinly across.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A design split into parts and handed to the teams that exist.
Once ownership is assigned, each team builds its piece to its own reading.

## Direction

From assigned module to shipped half.
It moves toward a clean part, coordinated tightly within the team and loosely with the team across the boundary.

## Lever

Dense internal communication meeting a thin cross-team one.
The force is each team's local care; the resistance is that the shared interface is agreed cheaply within a team and expensively, if at all, between two.

## Echo

Two correct halves built to two readings.
It hands forward well-made parts whose only weak contract is the one that spans a boundary, waiting to meet at integration.
