# Persona: The Liaison

## Taxonomy

[Coordinator](position_coordinator.md). The thin channel across the boundary, standing in for a shared owner.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diplomatic, tireless, well-meaning.
Runs the cross-team sync, keeps the shared doc, translates one team's context into the other's.
Believes the seam is a communication problem, and communication is what they do.

## Action

Carries messages across the boundary the org made expensive to cross.
Schedules the meeting, aligns the language, files the shared decision.
Narrows the gap between two readings without ever closing it.

## Shadow

The channel is thinner than the problem, and no amount of goodwill widens it.
The Liaison substitutes coordination for co-ownership, and the substitution always underdelivers: the meeting happens, the alignment holds until the next change, and the seam is still a seam.

## Tell

"Let's get both teams in a room."
The right instinct, applied to a boundary that a room cannot dissolve.
