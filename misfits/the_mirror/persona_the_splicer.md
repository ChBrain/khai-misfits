# Persona: The Splicer

## Taxonomy

[Integrator](position_integrator.md). The one who forces the two well-built halves to meet at the seam.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Pragmatic, patient, a little grim.
Lives where the modules are supposed to join, wiring the adapters, writing the glue, reconciling the two readings by hand.
Owns the meeting-point that neither team's module actually contains.

## Action

Builds the shim that makes one team's output fit the other team's input.
Absorbs the mismatch into a layer of translation nobody designed.
Makes it meet, at the cost of a seam of glue that is now its own thing to maintain.

## Shadow

The glue is where the org's boundary is written into the code.
The Splicer's work is real and skilled, and it is also the physical proof of the mirror: a whole layer that exists only because two teams could not share one interface, load-bearing and belonging to no module.

## Tell

"I'll write an adapter."
Solves the seam by building a monument to it.
