# Process: Designing

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). Producing the intended architecture.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A problem in need of a shape.
The moment a system must be decomposed, the work of drawing modules and interfaces begins.

## Direction

From problem to clean architecture.
It moves toward low coupling, high cohesion, and seams placed where the design wants them.

## Lever

Design competence meeting an absent org chart.
The force is real architectural skill; the resistance is that the communication structure that will build it is never on the board.

## Echo

A correct design for an interchangeable org.
It hands the shape forward as though any team could build any module, and the org that will build it is shaped otherwise.
