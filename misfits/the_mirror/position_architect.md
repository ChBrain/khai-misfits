# Position: Architect

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The seat that draws the intended design: modules, interfaces, the shape the system should take.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The authority to specify the architecture, and a clean sheet to draw it on.
A view of the problem, and the freedom to decompose it as the problem, not the org, suggests.

## Orders

Draw the low-coupling, high-cohesion design the problem deserves.
Place the seams where the architecture wants them, and trust the teams to honour the interfaces.
Specify as though any team could build any module.

## Loses

The communication structure, which the seat treats as someone else's diagram.
An architect who plans on the whiteboard cannot see which of their clean edges runs between two teams that never talk.

## Drives

Toward the design that is correct for an interchangeable org, and away from the one the real org can ship.
Toward interfaces drawn where the problem wants them, onto boundaries the org placed elsewhere.
