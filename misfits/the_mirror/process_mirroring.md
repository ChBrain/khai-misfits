# Process: Mirroring

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The system taking the shape of the org that built it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A design built through a communication structure it did not account for.
The moment work flows easily inside teams and jams between them, the shape begins to copy the org.

## Direction

From org chart to system structure.
It moves the software's real boundaries onto the org's real boundaries, seam by seam.

## Lever

Cheap communication inside a team meeting dear communication across one.
The force is the org's own shape; the resistance is a design that assumed a different one.

## Echo

A system that is a portrait of its org.
It hands forward a shipped product whose seams sit on the team lines, and a pattern only the on-call seat sees whole.
