# Position: Reorganizer

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The reversal seat: the one with authority over team boundaries, able to reshape the org to the architecture.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The authority the others lack: to move reporting lines, not just code.
A view of the seams and the org chart together, and the standing to redraw the second.

## Orders

Reshape the teams to match the intended architecture.
Put the dense communication inside each module and the thin communication on the edges the design meant to be thin.
Change the thing the mirror reflects, so the reflection changes.

## Loses

The settled org every team's habits were built around.
A seat that redraws boundaries spends real morale and months of churn, with no feature to show, against a payoff that is only the seams that never form.

## Drives

Toward an org shaped like the architecture, and away from a design that fights the chart forever.
Toward the mirror reflecting the drawing, at the price of the reorganisation the org was too settled to make.
