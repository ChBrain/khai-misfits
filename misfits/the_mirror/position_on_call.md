# Position: On-Call

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The seat that inherits the seams: it holds the integration failures together after everyone else has gone home.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The pager, the runbooks, and a map of where the system breaks.
The one view of every seam at once, because every seam eventually routes here.

## Orders

Keep it running: patch the seam, restart the service, file the incident.
Debug the failures that fall between owners and belong to no team.
Hold the whole together on the strength of knowing where the org's lines are.

## Loses

The power to change the shape it can see so clearly.
A seat that answers for the failures cannot redraw the boundaries that cause them, so it carries a structural problem with operational effort.

## Drives

Toward a system kept alive across its seams, and away from any authority to remove them.
Toward naming the mirror, outage by outage, from the seat least able to turn it.
