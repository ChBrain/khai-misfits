# Place: The Two Rooms

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The two teams' separate spaces: the boundary made physical.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Two teams, two rooms, two backlogs, a wall between.
Dense talk inside each, a scheduled meeting between: the communication structure as furniture.

## Holds

The org chart, lived rather than drawn.
The real graph of who talks to whom, and the boundary where the talking thins to a trickle.

## Offers

Clear ownership and clean scopes.
Here each team can move fast within its walls, coordinated tightly with the people it can see.

## Withheld

A shared room for the interface between them.
The two rooms give each team its own context and withhold the common one, so the contract that spans the wall is the contract no single room holds.
