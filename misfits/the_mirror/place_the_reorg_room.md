# Place: The Reorg Room

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). Where team boundaries are redrawn instead of code.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An org chart on the wall, and someone moving the boxes.
Reporting lines redrawn, teams merged and split, ownership reassigned: the rare room where the shape itself is on the table.

## Holds

The one lever that moves the seams, and the cost of pulling it.
The authority to reshape the communication structure so the system it produces is the one that was drawn.

## Offers

A mirror that reflects the architecture.
Here the boundaries can be aligned to the design, so the seams have nowhere to fall and the clean shape can finally ship.

## Withheld

A free move.
The reorg room gives the design its shape and takes, in return, the settled org: months of churn, redrawn careers, and no feature to show for the seams that now never form.
