# Persona: The Neighbor

## Taxonomy

[Sibling](position_sibling.md). The team on the other side of the boundary, building their part well, on their own schedule.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Competent, busy, reasonable.
Owns the next module, has their own roadmap, their own standup, their own reading of the shared interface.
Nothing against the other team; just rarely in the same room.

## Action

Ships their half correctly, to their own understanding of the contract.
Versions their side when it suits their schedule, not the caller's.
Meets the boundary from the far side, with a different set of assumptions held just as honestly.

## Shadow

Two correct halves, built to two readings, do not make one working whole.
The Neighbor is no less careful than the Lead and no better connected across the line, so the interface carries two truths that meet, and fail, only at integration.

## Tell

"That's not how we understood the interface."
Both teams are right, which is exactly why the seam is not either team's bug.
