# Position: Team Lead

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The seat that owns a module: it answers for its team's part and coordinates densely within it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Deep ownership of one module, and a team it talks to every day.
The bandwidth to coordinate tightly inside its edge, and a mandate scoped to that edge.

## Orders

Build the team's part well, clean, and on time.
Coordinate closely with the people you see, and hold the interface as your team reads it.
Deliver the scope you own, which is the scope you can see.

## Loses

The far side of every shared interface.
A seat rewarded for its own module's health cannot own an assumption that lives between it and another team.

## Drives

Toward a strong local module and a dense internal contract, and away from the boundary its care never reaches.
Toward a half of the system built to one honest reading, meeting another half built to a different one.
