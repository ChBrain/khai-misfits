# Persona: The Maintainer

## Taxonomy

[On-Call](position_on_call.md). The one who inherits the seams and keeps them from failing at 2 a.m.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Weary, sharp, holding a map of where it breaks.
Carries the pager, knows every seam by heart, can name which two teams a given outage lives between before the logs load.
Sees the pattern no single team sees, because the pattern is what pages them.

## Action

Holds the seam together with patches, runbooks, and retries.
Debugs the failures that fall between owners and route to whoever is on call.
Keeps the whole running on the strength of knowing exactly where the org's lines are.

## Shadow

Knowing the pattern is not the power to change it.
The Maintainer can see the system is a copy of the org chart, outage by outage, and can do nothing about the org: they hold a structural problem together with operational effort, forever.

## Tell

"It's always the boundary between those two teams."
Names the mirror precisely, from the one seat that cannot reshape it.
