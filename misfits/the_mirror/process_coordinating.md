# Process: Coordinating

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The thin communication carried across a boundary the org made expensive.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Two teams who must agree on an interface they do not share.
When a contract spans a boundary, someone starts scheduling the crossing.

## Direction

From divergent readings toward a temporary alignment.
It moves the two sides closer without ever making them one.

## Lever

Goodwill and meetings meeting the bandwidth of a single shared owner.
The force is honest effort to align; the resistance is that a channel across a boundary carries a fraction of what a shared room does.

## Echo

A gap narrowed and not closed.
It hands forward an alignment that holds until the next change, and a seam the coordination could never move.
