# Persona: The Reorganizer

## Taxonomy

[Reorganizer](position_reorganizer.md). The reversal: reshapes the teams to the architecture instead of asking the teams to fight it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Willing to pay the cost the others avoid.
Reads the seams, reads the org chart beside them, and sees they are the same shape.
Reaches for the boundaries, not the code, because the boundaries are where the design actually lives.

## Action

Redraws team ownership to match the intended architecture.
Puts the dense communication inside each module and the thin communication on the edges the design meant to be thin.
Moves the org until the mirror reflects the drawing.

## Shadow

The remedy is disruption with no feature to show for it.
The Reorganizer buys the clean design with reporting lines redrawn, teams broken up, and months of churn, paid now against a payoff that is only ever the seams that never form.

## Tell

"We're not changing the code. We're changing who owns it."
Turns the mirror by moving the thing it reflects, and wears the cost of doing so.
