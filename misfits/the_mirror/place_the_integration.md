# Place: The Integration

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). Where the parts are forced together and the mismatches surface.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The merge, the staging environment, the failing end-to-end test.
Where two well-built halves are supposed to meet: the branch that won't combine, the field one team versioned and the other didn't, the outage that pages at 2 a.m.

## Holds

The seam, and the whole system held against the org chart at last.
The one place the mirroring shows as defects, exactly on the boundaries between teams.

## Offers

The only honest view of which shape the system actually took.
Here, and nowhere on the whiteboard, the design can be seen as the thing it became rather than the thing it was drawn to be.

## Withheld

A way to move the seam from here.
The integration reveals the fault line and cannot relocate it, because the seam follows the boundary, and the boundary lives in the org, not the code.
