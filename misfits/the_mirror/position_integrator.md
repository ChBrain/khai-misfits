# Position: Integrator

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The seat at the join: it owns making two modules meet that neither team's module contains.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Responsibility for the meeting-point, and the glue to build it.
The one vantage that sees both readings of the interface at once, because both land on its desk.

## Orders

Make the halves meet: write the adapter, reconcile the assumptions, ship the join.
Absorb the mismatch into a layer of translation, and keep the system composing.
Own the seam, since no module does.

## Loses

Any way to remove the seam it is asked to bridge.
A seat that can only build glue turns the boundary into code rather than dissolving it, and the glue becomes load-bearing and its own to maintain.

## Drives

Toward a working system held together at a seam of translation, and away from the shared interface that would need no glue.
Toward a layer that is the org's boundary written into the code.
