# Position: Sibling

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The seat across the boundary: it owns the neighbouring module, on its own roadmap and its own reading.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The next module, a separate backlog, and a standup the other team is not in.
Full competence over its own scope, and a reading of the shared interface held just as honestly as the other side's.

## Orders

Ship the neighbouring part correctly, to your own understanding of the contract.
Set your own schedule and version on it, not on the caller's.
Meet the boundary from your side, with your assumptions.

## Loses

The other team's reading of the same interface.
A seat that owns the far half and rarely shares a room cannot reconcile two truths that only touch at integration.

## Drives

Toward a correct neighbouring module built to a divergent contract, and away from the shared understanding a single owner would have had.
Toward two right halves that fail to compose, on a boundary neither seat was asked to hold.
