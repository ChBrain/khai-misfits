# Pitch: Just Coordinate Better

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Reasonable, collaborative, sure the fix is more talking.
The integration keeps failing at the boundary, so add a sync, align the interface, get both teams in a room: a communication problem has a communication answer.
The voice of an org that trusts its structure and believes any seam can be closed with enough goodwill.

## Undertow

Under the goodwill, a seam set by the reporting lines.
The teams coordinate and the boundary stays exactly where the org put it, so the meetings narrow the gap and never close it, and the software keeps its shape.
No one is sloppy: each team builds its half well, and the half-to-half contract is thin because the talk across the wall is thin, and the wall is the org's, not the code's.

## Nerve

The feeling of an org that syncs and syncs
and ships, again, a system shaped like itself,
the same two teams' boundary failing in a new place every release.

## Echo

After the run: a product that is a portrait of the org chart,
a backlog of integration bugs all on the same lines,
and the one move that would have closed them, reshaping the teams to the design, still unmade, because the org was the thing no one was willing to redraw.
