# Persona: The Lead

## Taxonomy

[Team Lead](position_team_lead.md). The one who owns a module and builds it well, to their team's understanding.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Focused, accountable, proud of the team's part.
Knows their module deeply, ships it clean, coordinates tightly with the people they see every day.
Optimises for the scope they own, which is the scope they can see.

## Action

Builds the part nearest the team, densely and correctly.
Reads the interface across the boundary the way their own team would, and assumes the other team reads it the same.
Delivers a strong half of a contract with a weak far side.

## Shadow

The strength stops at the boundary, and so does the shared understanding.
The Lead's care is real and local: everything inside the team's edge is solid, and the one assumption that spans the edge to another team is the one no one owned.

## Tell

"Our side works. Check with the other team."
Draws the line of responsibility exactly where the seam will open.
