# Place: The Sync

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The thin cross-team meeting, where context is translated rather than shared.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Two teams on a call, a shared agenda, an interface doc on screen.
The scheduled crossing of the boundary: updates given, terms defined, decisions recorded for later.

## Holds

The handoff, and the belief that a meeting can do what a shared owner does.
The thin, expensive channel that the interface across the boundary leans on.

## Offers

Alignment, for now.
Here two teams can bring their readings close enough to keep moving, until the next change pulls them apart.

## Withheld

The bandwidth of one team.
The sync carries a fraction of what a shared room carries, at several times the cost, so it narrows the gap and never closes it, and the seam survives the meeting.
