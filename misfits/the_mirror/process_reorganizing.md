# Process: Reorganizing

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). The reversal: reshaping the teams to the architecture so the shape follows the design.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone reading the seams against the org chart and seeing they are one shape.
When the boundaries, not the code, are named as the cause, the work of moving them can begin.

## Direction

From an org shaped by history toward an org shaped by the architecture.
It moves the boundaries so the dense communication falls inside each intended module.

## Lever

Authority over reporting lines meeting the settled weight of the org.
The force is the will to change the shape itself; the resistance is the churn, the morale, and the absence of any feature to show.

## Echo

A mirror that reflects the design.
It hands forward an org whose communication structure produces the architecture, bought with a reorganisation paid now against seams that never form.
