# Place: The Standup

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). Where a team coordinates with itself, densely and for free.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A team around a board, aligning in five minutes.
Shared context, shared shorthand, a hundred small decisions made without a meeting because everyone already knows.

## Holds

The dense internal communication that makes a module cohesive.
The high-bandwidth agreement a team has with itself, the thing an interface within one team's ownership can lean on.

## Offers

Cheap, constant alignment inside the boundary.
Here a module can be built coherent, because the people building it are in the same conversation all day.

## Withheld

Anyone from the other side of the boundary.
The standup aligns the team and no one beyond it, so the strength it builds stops exactly at the wall, and the cross-team assumption is never in the room.
