# Process: Integrating

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). Forcing the parts to meet, where the mismatches surface.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Two halves arriving that were built to two readings.
When the modules must compose, the assumptions that never met are made to.

## Direction

From divergent parts toward a working whole, by hand.
It moves toward a shipped system, through a layer of glue that reconciles what the interface did not.

## Lever

An adapter meeting a mismatch.
The force is the integrator's skill at making things fit; the resistance is that the fit is a translation, not an agreement, and it becomes load-bearing.

## Echo

A composed system with a seam of glue at every boundary.
It hands forward a working whole and, written into it, the org's boundaries as code no module owns.
