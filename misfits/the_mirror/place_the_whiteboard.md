# Place: The Whiteboard

## Taxonomy

[The Mirror](play_the_mirror.md). Where the intended architecture is drawn.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Boxes and arrows, clean and low-coupling.
The design as it should be: modules that hide their internals, interfaces narrow and explicit, seams placed where the problem wants them.

## Holds

The architecture, and the assumption that any team could build any box.
The one shape the play keeps promising, drawn as though the org chart were not in the room.

## Offers

A correct decomposition of the problem.
Here a system can be designed for low coupling and high cohesion, answerable to the problem and to nothing else.

## Withheld

The communication structure that will actually build it.
The whiteboard shows the design and hides the org, so the one edge that runs between two teams who never talk looks exactly as clean as all the others.
