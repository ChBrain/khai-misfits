# Persona: The Drafter

## Taxonomy

[Architect](position_architect.md). The one who draws the clean design, as though the teams were interchangeable.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Rigorous, abstract, right.
Draws modules with low coupling and high cohesion, interfaces narrow and explicit, a decomposition chosen for the problem.
Reads the system as a diagram, and the diagram is good.

## Action

Specifies the architecture as if any team could build any module.
Places the seams where the design wants them, not where the org already put its walls.
Hands down a shape that is correct for a team that does not exist.

## Shadow

The diagram assumes a communication structure the org does not have.
The Drafter draws the interface clean and never sees that the two teams who must honour it barely talk, so the cleanest edge on the board is the one that will fray first.

## Tell

"The design is sound. The teams just need to follow it."
Believes the architecture is the shape that will ship, when the org chart is.
