# Plot: The Reform

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Open Floor](place_the_open_floor.md), someone outside who would break the ring.
The closed domain in view, the three corners each defending it, each holding a resource the reform needs, the outside pressing to get in.

## Action

[Opening](process_opening.md): [The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md), holding [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), would subject the domain to the president, the budget office, the rivals, and the press, against all three corners at once.

## Stage

[The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md), on [Open the Ring](plan_open_the_ring.md), cuts across the whole circuit and drags the domain into daylight, forcing the corners to answer beyond each other.
The three, each on its plan, close ranks: the committee, the agency, and the interest defend the ring together, each protesting, truthfully, that it is only doing its legitimate job.

## Tension

Opening the domain is the one move that answers the outside, and it means beating three braced corners at once and risking the real service tangled in the ring.
There is nothing to fault: a subsystem that answers only its insiders owes an answer to the public, and the reformer only presses it. But each corner holds a resource the reform needs and all three defend the circuit, so the plot closes one of two ways: the three are beaten together and the domain is opened, bought against real capital and the expertise the ring also carried ... or the corners hold, the reformer is spent, and the triangle stands, iron as before.
