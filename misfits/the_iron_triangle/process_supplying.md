# Process: Supplying

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). The three-way exchange, each corner giving the other two what only it commands, the trades that lock into a closed self-reinforcing circuit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Three corners, each holding a resource the other two need.
At [the hearing room](place_the_hearing_room.md), the lobby with its support, the committee with its law and budget, the agency with its favour, each ready to give what the others cannot make for themselves.

## Direction

Toward a closed circuit in which every corner both gives and receives.
Because each corner supplies the two it needs to keep supplying it, supplying locks the three trades into a ring: the lobby funds the committee that funds the agency that favours the lobby, so every corner's payoff depends on the other two staying in, and the giving is what keeps the receiving coming, the exchange self-reinforcing because no corner can leave without losing what the others provide.

## Lever

The plain gain of a trade each side wants.
Because each corner genuinely wants what the others offer and genuinely offers what they want, every leg of the exchange is a willing, mutually beneficial trade, so supplying runs on ordinary reciprocity, the closed circuit assembled from no coercion, only from three parties each better off for the giving and bound tighter by it.

## Echo

A ring in which each corner is held by the other two.
The three trades have locked into a circuit and every corner both gives and receives, so what reads as three separate beneficial exchanges is, in truth, a closed self-reinforcing ring, each corner committed to its standing because each needs what only the others supply.
