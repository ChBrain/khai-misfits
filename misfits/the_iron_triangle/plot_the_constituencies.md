# Plot: The Constituencies

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Hearing Room](place_the_hearing_room.md), three corners each set to a legitimate goal.
The committee wanting re-election, the agency wanting its budget and mission, the interest wanting favourable policy, and each holding what the other two lack.

## Action

[Serving](process_serving.md): [The Chairman](persona_the_chairman.md), [The Administrator](persona_the_administrator.md), and [The Lobbyist](persona_the_lobbyist.md), each holding its seat, pursue their proper mandates, each needing what the others command.

## Stage

[The Chairman](persona_the_chairman.md), on [Deliver for the District](plan_deliver_for_the_district.md), seeks the seat and the district's benefit; [The Administrator](persona_the_administrator.md), on [Guard the Program](plan_guard_the_program.md), defends the mission and its budget; [The Lobbyist](persona_the_lobbyist.md), on [Work the Inside](plan_work_the_inside.md), presses the members' case.
Each pursues a legitimate goal it cannot reach alone, and each holds a resource the other two need, so the three are already leaning toward one another before any trade is struck.

## Tension

Each corner is only doing its proper job, and each proper job points it toward the two corners that hold what it lacks.
There is nothing to fault: seeking re-election, defending a mission, and advocating for members are each legitimate. But none can reach its goal from within itself, so the plot closes with the three in place, each rightly served and each dependent on the others, the mandates that answer three constituencies already pointing toward the trade.
