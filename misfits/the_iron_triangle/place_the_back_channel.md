# Place: The Back Channel

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). The informal circuit where the three corners coordinate off the record, the fundraiser, the revolving door, the quiet call that seals the ring.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the three corners meet away from the hearing, the fundraiser and the club and the private call, the revolving door through which staff move between the corners.
The chairman, the administrator, and the lobbyist as long acquaintances who understand each other, coordinating quietly what the record will later show as three separate proper acts.

## Holds

The sealing, and the closure it keeps.
Here the three corners align out of sight, so the Back Channel holds the point where the ring is sealed: the shared understanding, the anticipated need, the favour done before it is asked, so it is where the circuit closes against outsiders, the three coordinated tightly enough to move as one and to keep any fourth party from getting between them.

## Offers

A place to align and a way to shut the outside out.
Because the Back Channel lets the three corners coordinate off the record, it offers them the trust and the timing that make the ring move as one, and the private means to close ranks the moment the domain is threatened, so it hands out the cohesion that turns three parties into a single subsystem, and hands it out where no outsider can see it form or get inside it.

## Withheld

The daylight that would let the outside contest it.
The Back Channel lets the three align out of sight, but it cannot bring that alignment into the open where the president or the press could challenge it, so it withholds the visibility the outsider would need to break in, keeping the closure informal and deniable, and, all quiet understanding and no record, it withholds any handle by which the ring, sealed off the books, can be reached and opened.
