# Position: Bureau

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). The corner that commands programmes, rulings, and implementation, and supplies the committee and the interest with what only it can give.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The programme and the power to implement it, and a budget that must be defended: the standing to write the rulings and steer the contracts, and the need for funding and autonomy.
A clear case: defending a mandated programme and the budget it needs is the public servant's proper duty.
No way to secure its budget from within itself, and every reason to take the committee's appropriation and the interest's testimony.

## Orders

Guard the program.
Run the programme, write the favourable rulings, steer contracts and benefits, and cultivate the friends who protect the budget.
Defend the mission, and serve the clientele that defends it back.

## Loses

Its answer to the executive above it, exactly by defending the mission and the budget.
Because the agency commands the favour the interest wants and the implementation the committee needs, and needs from them the budget and the testimony it cannot make itself, the seat trades rulings and benefits for appropriations and clientele support, so its legitimate defence of the programme supplies one corner of the ring, and the stewardship meant to serve the public and answer the executive comes to answer the two corners that fund and defend it.
It gains the budget and the backing that keep the programme, and loses its account to the executive and the public above the ring.

## Drives

Toward the mission defended and the budget kept,
bought against the executive it ceases to answer,
so the corner that would steward the public supplies one wall of the ring that governs it.
