# Place: The Open Floor

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). The wider arena of the full chamber, the executive, the rivals, and the press, where the reformer would drag the domain into daylight and outside contest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the domain could be brought before everyone outside it, the full chamber, the president's desk, the budget review, the front page, where the closed subsystem meets the wider public.
The reformer pressing the domain into the open, the three corners dragged out of their circuit to answer the president and the rival and the press, the arrangement exposed to contest.

## Holds

The opening, and the outside answer it forces.
Here the closed domain meets the wider public, so the Open Floor holds the point where the ring could be broken: the subsystem subjected to the president, the budget office, the rivals, and the press, so it is where the three corners must answer beyond each other, the daylight in which a circuit that ran on private alignment can be contested by everyone it shut out.

## Offers

A forum for the outside, and the contest the ring escaped.
Because the Open Floor brings the domain before those with no corner, it offers the reformer the president's leverage, the budget office's knife, the rival's challenge, and the press's light, so it hands out the outside answer the closed circuit was built to avoid, and hands it out only to whoever can hold the three corners in it long enough to beat them all at once.

## Withheld

An easy or lasting victory, and a costless break.
The Open Floor can drag the domain into daylight, but it cannot make the three corners answer without their consent for longer than the reformer can force them, nor break the ring without spending the capital and risking the service tangled in it, so it withholds the durable opening, letting the corners close again the moment the pressure lifts, and, all daylight and no permanence, it withholds any break that does not cost the reformer more than any single corner can be made to pay.
