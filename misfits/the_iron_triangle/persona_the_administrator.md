# Persona: The Administrator

## Taxonomy

[Bureau](position_bureau.md). Runs the agency and its programme, wants the budget kept and the mission defended, and supplies the other two corners with what only the agency commands.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Committed. Expert. Defending the mission.
Is the agency head who runs the programme, believes in it, and wants the funding and autonomy it needs to do its work.
No self-dealing in it: only a public servant protecting a mandate they were charged with and think matters.

## Action

Runs the programme, writes the rulings, and steers contracts and benefits, giving the lobby favourable implementation and the committee benefits to place in its district.
Takes the budget and friendly oversight the committee provides and the organised testimony the lobby mobilises, and defends the agency with both.
Does what a mission-defending administrator does: keeps the programme funded, serves its clientele, and cultivates the friends who protect its budget.

## Shadow

Not an empire-builder for its own sake, a mission-defender, and that is the whole difficulty: protecting a programme and its budget is the legitimate job, and doing it supplies one corner of the ring.
Because the agency commands the rulings and contracts the lobby wants and the implementation the committee needs, the Administrator's ordinary defence of the mission trades favour and benefits for the committee's budget and the lobby's testimony, so the stewardship that is meant to serve the public becomes a corner of a circuit that serves the other two corners, and the more ably the mission is defended the more the agency answers to its ring rather than to the executive above it.
It does the administrator's job, and the administrator's job, done well, is one wall of the triangle.

## Tell

Says "I am protecting a programme that people depend on: that is my duty."
Protects it truly, and cannot see that the protection, traded for budget and testimony, binds the agency to the ring and away from the public it serves.
