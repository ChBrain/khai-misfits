# Persona: The Chairman

## Taxonomy

[Committee](position_committee.md). Holds the committee's gate to law and budget, wants re-election above all, and supplies the other two corners with what only the committee commands.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Responsive. Constituent-minded. Delivering for the district.
Is the legislator who chairs the committee, who writes the authorising law and the appropriation, and who answers to the voters who return them.
No graft in it: only an elected representative doing the elected representative's job, bringing home benefits and keeping the seat.

## Action

Writes the favourable legislation and the budget, steers benefits into the district, and holds friendly oversight of the agency.
Takes the support the lobby offers and the implementation the agency delivers, and gives each in turn what the committee alone can give.
Does what a re-election-seeking legislator does: serves the constituency, funds the programme, and keeps the friends who keep the seat.

## Shadow

Not a bribe-taker, an elected representative, and that is the whole difficulty: seeking re-election and serving constituents is the legitimate job, and doing it supplies one corner of the ring.
Because the committee commands law and budget that the agency and the lobby both need, the Chairman's ordinary, lawful pursuit of the seat trades appropriations and legislation for the lobby's support and the agency's benefits, so the representation that is meant to answer the public becomes a corner of a circuit that answers only the other two corners, and the more faithfully the district is served the tighter the ring is drawn.
It does the representative's job, and the representative's job, done well, is one wall of the triangle.

## Tell

Says "I am just fighting for my district: that is what I was sent here to do."
Fights for it truly, and cannot see that the fight, traded for support and benefits, is the leg of a ring that shuts the wider public out.
