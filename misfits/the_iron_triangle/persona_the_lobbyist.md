# Persona: The Lobbyist

## Taxonomy

[Lobby](position_lobby.md). Speaks for the organised interest, wants the policy to go its way, and supplies the other two corners with what only the interest commands.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Organised. Persuasive. Speaking for the members.
Is the representative of the industry or the cause, who advocates for the members who pay them and presses their case where policy is made.
No bribery in it: only the lawful advocacy of an organised interest, the petition and the testimony a free system allows.

## Action

Supplies the committee with campaign support and organised votes and the agency with a loyal clientele that testifies for its budget.
Presses the members' case in the hearing and the back channel, and takes, in return, the favourable law and the favourable ruling.
Does what an interest advocate does: mobilises support, funds friends, and wins for the members the policy they are paid to seek.

## Shadow

Not a corrupter, a lawful advocate, and that is the whole difficulty: advocating for paying members is the legitimate job, and doing it supplies one corner of the ring.
Because the interest commands the support the committee needs and the clientele the agency needs, the Lobbyist's ordinary, lawful advocacy trades money and testimony for the committee's legislation and the agency's favour, so the representation of a legitimate interest becomes a corner of a circuit that governs the domain for the three, and the more effectively the members are served the more the policy is closed to everyone the interest does not speak for.
It does the advocate's job, and the advocate's job, done well, is one wall of the triangle.

## Tell

Says "I am only representing my members: that is my right and my work."
Represents them truly, and cannot see that the representation, traded for law and favour, is the leg of a ring that runs the domain for the members and against the public they are part of.
