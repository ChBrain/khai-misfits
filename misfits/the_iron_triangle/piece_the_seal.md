# Piece: The Seal

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). The closure that holds the ring shut against outsiders, the three corners braced together so the domain answers only itself, the stability that makes the triangle iron.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Held at [the back channel](place_the_back_channel.md), where the three align to move as one.
Tested at [the open floor](place_the_open_floor.md), where the reformer would break it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the three exchanges are just a trade, and any outsider could get between the corners and undo it.
With it the trade becomes a fortress: the three braced together astride the domain's gates, each defending the ring that feeds it, so no president, rival, or public can get in, and the circuit is held against elections and administrations. The Seal is load bearing because it is what makes the triangle iron rather than a passing deal (Gordon Adams; Lowi): the three-cornered closure that a single captured agency would lack, so the piece carries the stability of mutual defence, the reason the ring survives what any one corner alone could not, and the reason breaking it means beating all three at once.

## Apparent

Stability, the plain virtue of a settled, expert, well-defended policy area that knows its own business.
Seen at the surface, the Seal looks like good order, a mature domain run by those who understand it and not blown about by every outside wind, and the truth that the stability is exclusion, that a domain answering only its insiders is closed to the public it governs, that what looks like expert self-government is a captured subsystem holding its gates, stays hidden behind how much a settled, defended policy area resembles a well-run one.

## Yearbook

Kept by all three corners through [sealing](process_sealing.md), the closure that holds the ring.
It is the iron of the trap: the Seal braces the three exchanges of [the support](piece_the_support.md), [the budget](piece_the_budget.md), and [the favour](piece_the_favour.md) into a fortress shut to [the outside](piece_the_outside.md), the stability only [opening](process_opening.md) can break, the three-cornered strength that makes the [iron triangle](piece_the_iron_triangle.md) iron.
