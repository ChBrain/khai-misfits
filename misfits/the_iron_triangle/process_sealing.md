# Process: Sealing

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). The closing of the ring against outsiders, the three corners braced together to govern the domain and hold the president, the rival, and the public beyond it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A circuit formed and a domain to hold.
At [the back channel](place_the_back_channel.md), the three corners aligning off the record, coordinating tightly enough to move as one and to keep any fourth party from getting between them.

## Direction

Toward a domain governed from the inside and shut to the outside.
Because the three sit astride the committee's gate to legislation, the agency's control of implementation, and the lobby's command of the affected public, sealing lets them govern their policy slice among themselves and close it to outsiders, so the president who would cut the programme, the rival interest, and the general public find every entrance held, the ring braced to resist what any one corner alone could not.

## Lever

The strength of three braced against each other.
Because each corner defends the ring that feeds it and each holds a resource an outsider would need, the closure is held not by conspiracy but by three parties each with its own reason to keep the domain shut, so sealing runs on mutual defence, the exclusion assembled from no single gatekeeper, only from three corners whose separate interests all point at keeping the outside out.

## Echo

A domain run by its insiders and closed to everyone else.
The three have braced together and held every entrance, so what reads as a well-defended, stable policy area is, in truth, a subsystem that has captured its own domain, iron because it is three-cornered, standing against elections and administrations because to break it one must beat all three at once.
