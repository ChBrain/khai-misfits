# Position: Lobby

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). The corner that commands electoral support and organised clientele, and supplies the committee and the agency with what only it can give.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The support of an organised interest, campaign money and votes and testimony, and a case to press: the standing to mobilise the members and to petition where policy is made.
A clear case: advocating for paying members is the interest's lawful right and work.
No way to make policy from within itself, and every reason to trade its support for the committee's law and the agency's favour.

## Orders

Work the inside.
Supply the committee with support and organised votes, back the agency with a loyal clientele, and press the members' case in the hearing and the back channel.
Win the favourable law and the favourable ruling, and fund the friends who grant them.

## Loses

Nothing it holds, and it need lose nothing, for the trade only ever adds: the seat gives support and gains policy, and the giving buys the receiving.
Because the interest commands the support the committee needs and the clientele the agency needs, its lawful advocacy trades money and testimony for law and favour, so it supplies one corner of the ring at no cost to itself, and the more effectively it serves its members the more the domain is closed to everyone it does not speak for.
It gains the policy its members pay for and loses only the claim that the domain it captured was ever open to the public it is part of.

## Drives

Toward the members served and the policy won,
bought against the public it is drawn from and shuts out,
so the corner that would advocate for some supplies one wall of the ring that governs all.
