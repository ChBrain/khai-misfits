# Piece: The Outside

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). The general interest with no corner, the president, the rivals, and the public who fund the domain and pay for the ring, the excluded that bears the cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Shut out at [the back channel](place_the_back_channel.md) and unseated at [the hearing room](place_the_hearing_room.md).
Given a voice only at [the open floor](place_the_open_floor.md), where the reformer would seat it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the ring is a closed exchange that costs no one, and there is no injury and no trap.
With it the closure bites: the general public who fund the domain, the president who would govern it, the rival interests it excludes, all bearing the cost of a ring they have no corner in. The Outside is load bearing because it is who the trap harms (Lowi): the excluded majority that pays for the subsystem and holds no seat at any corner, so the piece carries the party the closed circuit is closed against, the funder of the favour and the budget and the support who never voted the bill, the cost that makes three legitimate roles into a public wrong.

## Apparent

Nobody in particular, a diffuse public with no stake sharp enough to notice, easy to leave out of the room.
Seen at the surface, the Outside does not appear: the corners are present and served and the general interest is nowhere at the table, and the truth that it funds the whole domain, that it pays for every favour and budget and support, that its exclusion is the point of the seal and the cost of the ring, stays hidden behind how naturally a diffuse public with no organised corner goes unrepresented and unmissed.
