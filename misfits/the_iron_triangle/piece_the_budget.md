# Piece: The Budget

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). What the committee supplies the other two corners, appropriations and oversight to the agency and favourable law to the interest, one leg of the closed exchange.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Written at [the hearing room](place_the_hearing_room.md) as authorisation, appropriation, and markup.
Flows from the committee to the agency that needs funding and the interest that needs law.

## Load Bearing

Without it the agency has no funding and the interest no favourable statute, and one corner has nothing the others can give.
With it the committee becomes a corner the others cannot do without: the appropriations and friendly oversight the agency needs to keep its programme, and the favourable legislation the interest needs to win its policy. The Budget is load bearing because it is one of the three currencies the ring runs on (Freeman; Ripley and Franklin): the resource only the committee commands, so the piece carries the leg by which the committee supplies the agency and the interest, the giving that buys the support and the benefits it receives in return.

## Apparent

Representation and oversight, the plain constitutional work of a committee funding programmes and making law.
Seen at the surface, the Budget looks like democracy functioning, the power of the purse and the writing of statute exercised by the people's representatives, and the truth that appropriation and legislation are here the currency that pays a corner of a closed ring, that oversight friendly by design is capture wearing procedure, that the power meant to check the agency is spent binding it into the circuit, stays hidden behind how plainly proper the committee's constitutional powers are.

## Yearbook

Supplied by [the chairman](persona_the_chairman.md) through [supplying](process_supplying.md), the committee's currency in the ring.
It is one leg of the trap: the Budget buys [the support](piece_the_support.md) from the interest and [the favour](piece_the_favour.md)'s benefits from the agency, closing the circuit that [the seal](piece_the_seal.md) holds shut, the second of the three exchanges that make the [iron triangle](piece_the_iron_triangle.md).
