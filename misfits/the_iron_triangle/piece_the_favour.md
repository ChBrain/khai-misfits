# Piece: The Favour

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). What the agency supplies the other two corners, favourable rulings and contracts to the interest and steered benefits to the committee, one leg of the closed exchange.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Written at [the hearing room](place_the_hearing_room.md) as rulings and awards, and delivered through the agency's own implementation.
Flows from the agency to the interest that needs favourable policy and the committee that needs benefits to place.

## Load Bearing

Without it the interest has no favourable rulings and the committee no benefits to steer, and one corner has nothing the others can give.
With it the agency becomes a corner the others cannot do without: the rulings, contracts, and programmes the interest needs, and the benefits the committee needs to place in its district. The Favour is load bearing because it is one of the three currencies the ring runs on (Lowi; Gordon Adams): the resource only the agency commands through its control of implementation, so the piece carries the leg by which the agency supplies the interest and the committee, the giving that buys the budget and the support it receives in return.

## Apparent

Administration, the plain expert work of an agency implementing its programme and issuing its rulings.
Seen at the surface, the Favour looks like competent public service, an agency carrying out its mandate and applying its expertise, and the truth that its rulings and contracts are here the currency that pays a corner of a closed ring, that implementation steered toward the interest and the district is capture wearing expertise, that discretion meant to serve the public is spent buying the corners that fund and defend the agency, stays hidden behind how plainly proper administering a programme is.
