# Plot: The Delivery

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Hearing Room](place_the_hearing_room.md), the ring closed and ready to produce its output.
A favourable programme, a protected budget, a friendly rule moving through the circuit, and an outside that funds it and holds no seat.

## Action

[Supplying](process_supplying.md): the three corners deliver, a favourable outcome passing through the ring to serve the insiders and charged to the outside.

## Stage

[The Chairman](persona_the_chairman.md), [The Administrator](persona_the_administrator.md), and [The Lobbyist](persona_the_lobbyist.md), each on its plan, move the favourable programme through the circuit, and it passes: authorised, funded, ruled, and delivered.
The three corners are served, and the bill for it falls on the general public, who funded the domain, voted none of this, and hold no corner to object.

## Tension

The ring delivering for its corners looks exactly like government producing policy, and the policy is charged to the party with no seat.
There is nothing on the face of it to fault: a programme is authorised, funded, and implemented through proper channels. But it serves the three and is paid by the outside, so the plot closes with the ring delivering for its insiders, the general interest paying a bill it never voted, three legitimate roles summing to a public cost.
