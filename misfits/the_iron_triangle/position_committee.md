# Position: Committee

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). The corner that commands law and budget, holds the gate to legislation, and supplies the agency and the interest with what only it can give.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The power of authorisation, appropriation, and oversight, and a seat that must be won again: the standing to make the law and the budget, and the need to be re-elected.
A clear case: serving the district and keeping the seat is the elected representative's proper work.
No way to get re-election from within itself, and every reason to take the support the interest offers and the benefits the agency delivers.

## Orders

Deliver for the district.
Write the favourable law and the appropriation, steer benefits home, hold friendly oversight of the agency, and keep the friends who keep the seat.
Serve the constituency, and fund the programme that serves it.

## Loses

Its answer to the wider public, exactly by serving the district and the friends who return it.
Because the committee commands what the agency and the interest both need and needs from them the support and benefits it cannot make itself, the seat trades law and budget for votes and implementation, so its legitimate pursuit of re-election supplies one corner of the ring, and the representation meant to answer the public comes to answer the two corners that keep the seat.
It gains the support and the benefits that hold the seat, and loses the account it owed to everyone outside the ring.

## Drives

Toward the district delivered and the seat held,
bought against the wider public it ceases to answer,
so the corner that would represent the people supplies one wall of the ring that shuts them out.
