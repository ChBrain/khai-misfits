# Plot: The Closing

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Back Channel](place_the_back_channel.md), the ring formed and a domain to hold.
The three corners astride the committee's gate, the agency's implementation, and the lobby's clientele, aligning off the record to move as one.

## Action

[Sealing](process_sealing.md): the three corners brace together and close the domain, holding the president, the rival, and the public beyond it.

## Stage

The three, coordinating quietly at [the back channel](place_the_back_channel.md), align tightly enough to move as one and to keep any fourth party from getting between them.
Each defends the ring that feeds it, so the domain is governed from the inside and every entrance held, the closure braced by three separate interests all pointing at keeping the outside out.

## Tension

Each corner defends the ring for its own legitimate reason, and the three defences together seal the domain shut.
There is nothing to fault in any single defence: each corner is entitled to protect what serves it. But braced together they hold every gate, so the plot closes with the subsystem in control of its domain, iron because it is three-cornered, standing against elections and administrations because to break it one must beat all three at once.
