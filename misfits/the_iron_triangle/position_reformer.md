# Position: Reformer

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). The seat outside the ring that would open the domain to broader answer, at the cost of beating three braced corners at once and the service tangled in the circuit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A mandate to the general interest and the levers of the outside: the standing of the president, the budget office, the rival, or the press, and the case that a closed domain must be opened.
A clear case: a subsystem that answers only to its insiders should be subjected to the wider public it governs.
No corner of its own inside the ring, and no way to break the circuit but by overpowering all three corners together.

## Orders

Open the ring.
Subject the domain to the president, the budget office, the rival interests, and the press, and force the three corners to answer beyond each other.
Cut across the whole circuit, not into a single corner, and press it into daylight.

## Loses

Its capital and its standing, exactly by trying to open a ring three braced corners defend.
Because each corner holds a resource the reform needs and each defends the ring that feeds it, the seat cannot break the circuit by taking one corner but only by beating all three at once, spending real political capital against an entrenched, information-rich, mutually supporting alliance, and risking, in the breaking, the genuine expertise and constituency service tangled in the ring, so the one move that opens the domain is costly, rarely held for long, and easily read as an attack on three parties only doing their jobs.
It buys an opened domain against three corners at once and the service the ring also carried, and is taken, for a while, as the one who wrecked a working arrangement.

## Drives

Toward the ring opened and the domain answering the outside,
bought against three braced corners and the service tangled in the circuit,
so the one seat that would open the domain must defeat at once three corners each with every reason to hold.
