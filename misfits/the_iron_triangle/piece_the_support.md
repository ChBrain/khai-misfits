# Piece: The Support

## Taxonomy

[The Iron Triangle](play_the_iron_triangle.md). What the interest supplies the other two corners, electoral support to the committee and a mobilised clientele to the agency, one leg of the closed exchange.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Given at [the hearing room](place_the_hearing_room.md) as testimony and on the record, and at [the back channel](place_the_back_channel.md) as money and organised votes.
Flows from the lobby to the committee that needs re-election and the agency that needs a defended budget.

## Load Bearing

Without it the committee has no re-election help and the agency no clientele, and two corners have nothing the interest can give.
With it the interest becomes a corner the others cannot do without: campaign money and organised votes the committee needs to keep its seat, and a loyal clientele the agency needs to defend its budget. The Support is load bearing because it is one of the three currencies the ring runs on (Cater; Gordon Adams): the resource only the organised interest commands, so the piece carries the leg by which the lobby supplies the committee and the agency, the giving that buys the law and the favour it receives in return.

## Apparent

Democratic participation, the plain right of an organised interest to advocate, fund, and testify.
Seen at the surface, the Support looks like citizenship in action, a group lawfully making its case and backing its friends, and the truth that the same money and testimony are the currency that buys a corner of a closed ring, that lawful advocacy is here the first leg of a self-dealing circuit, that participation by one interest is the exclusion of everyone it does not speak for, stays hidden behind how plainly legitimate advocacy is.
