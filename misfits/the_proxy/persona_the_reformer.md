# Persona: The Reformer

## Taxonomy

[The Reformer](position_the_reformer.md). Would break the self-confirming prior by mandating the group's hiring and lowering the bar it is held to, and meets a remedy that can dampen the very investment it needs.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Corrective. Well-meant. Fighting the equilibrium.
Is the one who sees the loop and would break it, mandating that the low-rated group be hired and lowering the bar it is held to so the door is opened whatever the rating.
No naivety wished on them: they understand the prior is self-confirming and mean to interrupt the confirmation.

## Action

Mandates the hiring and lowers the bar the group is held to, so the door is opened whatever the rating.
Aims to raise the group's average and let the evidence break the prior, forcing hiring enough that acquiring the signal comes to pay, so the group invests and the able are seen.
Does what a corrective must: pushes against the equilibrium from outside, since nothing inside it will move on its own.

## Shadow

Not naive, but caught in a bifurcation, and that is the whole difficulty: the same mandate can break the prior or feed it, turning on how hard it bites.
Because the mandate lowers the bar the group is held to, it can, short of biting hard enough to make the signal worth acquiring, dampen the very incentive to invest, the group taken without the signal, so fewer acquire it and the average stays low and the prior is confirmed by the investment the remedy discouraged, the reform settling into a patronising equilibrium in which the group is held to a lower bar and kept below it.
It opens the door by lowering the bar, and a lowered bar can lower the reason to clear it.

## Tell

Says "lower the bar and the group will invest, and the evidence will speak for itself."
Is right only if the mandate bites hard enough to make the signal pay: lower the bar too gently and the group is hired without the signal, so the reason to acquire it falls and the shortfall the remedy meant to lift is the shortfall it deepens.
