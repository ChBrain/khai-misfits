# Persona: The Reformer

## Taxonomy

[The Reformer](position_the_reformer.md). Would break the self-confirming prior by subsidising the group's signal or mandating its hiring, and meets the same rational updating that set the rating.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Corrective. Well-meant. Fighting the equilibrium.
Is the one who sees the loop and would break it, lowering the cost of the signal for the low-rated group or requiring that they be hired.
No naivety wished on them: they understand the prior is self-confirming and mean to interrupt the confirmation.

## Action

Subsidises the group's investment so the signal is cheaper to acquire, or mandates the hiring so the door is opened whatever the rating.
Aims to raise the group's average and let the evidence break the prior, changing the return so the group invests and the able are seen.
Does what a corrective must: pushes against the equilibrium from outside, since nothing inside it will move on its own.

## Shadow

Not naive, but out-reasoned by the same reason, and that is the whole difficulty: the market that priced by the group prices the intervention too.
Because the Employer updates rationally, it discounts a signal known to be subsidised and reads a mandated hire as placed rather than earned, so the boosted credential is trusted less and the required hire is doubted, and the prior survives the intervention, settling into a patronising equilibrium in which the able of the group are doubted the more for the help, the correction turned by the very updating it meant to break.
It opens the door, and the reason that shut it discounts what comes through.

## Tell

Says "lower the barrier and the group will invest, and the evidence will speak for itself."
Is right that the group will invest, and cannot ensure the evidence is read, because a signal the market knows was helped is a signal the market marks down.
