# Plot: The Withholding

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ground](place_the_ground.md), the return on the signal discounted for the low-rated group.
A member weighing a costly investment against a payoff their group is offered less of.

## Action

[Withholding](process_withholding.md): the group, offered a lower return on the signal, rationally invests less in it.

## Stage

[The Applicant](persona_the_applicant.md), on [Weigh the Return](plan_weigh_the_return.md), reckons [the discount](piece_the_discount.md) on [the signal](piece_the_signal.md) and acquires less of it, answering the return the market actually gives them, so the group's real average quality falls.
Each choice is prudent, the sound allocation of effort to the payoff it earns.

## Tension

Investing less in a discounted signal is the sound response, and the sound response is what makes the rating true.
There is no failing: a rational person puts less into a thing that pays them less, and the market does pay them less. But the group's lower investment lowers its average, so the plot closes with the average fallen toward the rating that discounted it, the prudent answer of everyone the discount touched summing to the shortfall that will confirm the prior.
