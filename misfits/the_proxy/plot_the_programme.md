# Plot: The Programme

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Programme](place_the_programme.md), someone who would break the self-confirming prior.
A reformer with a subsidy or a mandate, and a market whose reason priced by the group and will price the intervention too.

## Action

[Subsidising](process_subsidising.md): the reform lowers the cost of the signal or mandates the hire from outside, and the market discounts the help.

## Stage

[The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md), on [Break the Prior](plan_break_the_prior.md), lowers [the discount](piece_the_discount.md) on [the signal](piece_the_signal.md) from outside, against an Employer whose same rational updating reads the subsidised signal as less reliable and the mandated hire as placed, holding [the prior](piece_the_prior.md) that [the proxy](piece_the_proxy.md) rests on.
The door is opened, and the reason that shut it marks down what comes through.

## Tension

The one move that could break the prior is met by the same reason that built it.
The intervention is sound and the group does invest: but the market, updating rationally, discounts a signal it knows was helped and doubts a hire it reads as placed, so the plot closes with the prior broken and the rating undone ... or the correction discounted, the stereotype surviving into a patronising equilibrium, the able of the group doubted the more for the help, and the cure turned by the very updating it meant to end.
