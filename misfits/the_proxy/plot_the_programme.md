# Plot: The Programme

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Programme](place_the_programme.md), someone who would break the self-confirming prior.
A reformer with a mandate that lowers the bar, and a group whose reason invested to the return it was offered and will answer a lowered bar too.

## Action

[Mandating](process_mandating.md): the reform mandates the hire and lowers the bar from outside, and short of biting hard the lowered bar dampens the group's reason to invest.

## Stage

[The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md), on [Break the Prior](plan_break_the_prior.md), lowers the bar the group is held to from outside, and short of biting hard the lowered bar re-drives [the discount](piece_the_discount.md) on [the signal](piece_the_signal.md), the group taken without it investing less, holding [the prior](piece_the_prior.md) that [the proxy](piece_the_proxy.md) rests on.
The door is opened by lowering the bar, and a lowered bar lowers the reason to clear it.

## Tension

The one move that could break the prior is met by the same reason that built it.
Whether it works turns on how hard the mandate bites: pushed far enough the forced hiring makes the signal worth acquiring and the group invests, so the plot closes with the prior broken and the rating undone ... or the lowered bar dampens the reason to invest, the group taken without the signal, the stereotype surviving into a patronising equilibrium, the group held to a lower bar and kept below it, and the cure feeding the loop it meant to end.
