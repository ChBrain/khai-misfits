# Plan: Screen by the Group

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Gate](position_the_gate.md), inside [The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Gate](position_the_gate.md)

## Direction

Price the unknown by the known. Read the group average where the individual cannot be read, set the rating each applicant is offered by the group they belong to, and discount the return on any signal a low-rated applicant holds. Use the best cheap information there is, the base rate, and judge on merit only what merit can be seen.

## Orders

([The Employer](persona_the_employer.md) reads the group average and sets the rating by it, the rational actuarial screening that discounts a low-rated group's return.)
