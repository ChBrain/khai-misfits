# Piece: The Signal

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). The costly investment in quality, the credential or skill whose return decides whether the group acquires it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Acquired on [the ground](place_the_ground.md) and presented at [the desk](place_the_desk.md).
Flows from the individual who invests in it to the agent who reads it, or is not acquired at all.

## Load Bearing

Without it quality could not be shown or withheld, and there would be no return to discount and no investment to lower.
With it there is a thing the group can invest in and the agent can read: the degree, the training, the proof, whose return decides whether acquiring it is worth its cost. The Signal is load bearing because it is where the rating meets behaviour (Arrow): the investment the discount makes less worthwhile, so the piece carries both the able member's proof, marked down for the group it comes in, and the marginal member's investment, withheld because its return was cut, the one object the whole loop is fought over.

## Apparent

Merit made visible, the credential the market says it rewards, the fair way to be judged on what you can do.
Seen at the surface, the Signal looks like the answer to the proxy, the way to be priced as yourself and not your group, and the truth that its return is discounted for a low-rated group so the group invests less in it, that the able member's full signal is marked down for the company it comes in, that the very thing meant to distinguish the individual is cut for the group, stays hidden behind how plainly a credential ought to speak for itself.

## Yearbook

Invested in by [the Applicant](persona_the_applicant.md) and held in full by [the Standout](persona_the_standout.md), the proof the market underpays.
It is the object of the trap: the Signal's return is cut by [the discount](piece_the_discount.md), so it is underacquired in the withholding and marked down for the Standout, the investment whose shortfall makes [the confirmation](piece_the_confirmation.md) that upholds [the prior](piece_the_prior.md).
