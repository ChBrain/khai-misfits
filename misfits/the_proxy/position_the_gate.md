# Position: The Gate

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). The seat that screens the unknown individual by the known group average and sets the rating that discounts a group's return.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The post and the base rate: the one opening to fill, and the group averages it can read where it cannot read the individual.
A clear case: pricing the unknown by the known is the rational move under uncertainty, the same actuarial sense that sets a premium by a pool.
No way to see each applicant's true quality cheaply, and every reason to price them by the group they belong to.

## Orders

Screen by the group.
Read the group average where the individual cannot be read, set the rating each applicant is offered by their group, and discount the return on any signal a low-rated applicant holds.
Price the unknown by the known, and judge on merit only what merit can be seen.

## Loses

The tie between the person and their price, exactly by pricing rationally.
Because the Gate prices by the group average and that price discounts the low-rated group's return on the signal, the group invests less and its average falls to meet the rating, so the seat's sound actuarial screening lowers the very average it screens by, and its prior is confirmed by the effort its rating discouraged.
It gains a rational rule and loses the individual to it, its own base rate kept low by the pricing it justifies.

## Drives

Toward the unknown priced by the known,
and the rating made true by the response it provokes,
so the seat that screens most rationally is the one that makes the group become what it was rated.
