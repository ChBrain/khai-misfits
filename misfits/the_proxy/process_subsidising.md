# Process: Subsidising

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). The reform lowering the cost of the signal or mandating the hire from outside, and the market discounting the help.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Reformer, with an intervention and a self-confirming prior to break.
At [the programme](place_the_programme.md), the signal subsidised or the hire mandated, the outside push meant to raise the group's average and let the evidence break the rating.

## Direction

Toward the barrier lowered and the help discounted.
Because nothing inside the equilibrium will move it, subsidising points the correction in from outside, lowering the cost of the signal or opening the door, but the same rational updating that set the rating reads the subsidised signal as less reliable and the mandated hire as placed, so the process points the boost toward a market that marks it down.

## Lever

The same rationality, turned on the intervention.
Because the Employer updates soundly, it discounts a signal it knows was helped exactly as it priced by the group, so subsidising runs into the very reason it meant to break, the market pricing the correction as it prices everything it cannot cheaply verify, no new prejudice needed to turn the help aside.

## Echo

A door opened, and a doubt deepened.
The intervention has lowered the barrier and the market has discounted what comes through, so what leaves this process is a prior that survived the help, settled into a patronising equilibrium in which the able of the group are doubted the more for the subsidy, the correction turned by the same updating that built the rating.
