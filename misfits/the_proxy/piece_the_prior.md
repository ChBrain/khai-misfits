# Piece: The Prior

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). The group average the agent screens by, the belief that stands in for the unknown individual and sets the rating.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Held at [the desk](place_the_desk.md), read where the individual cannot be.
Flows from the group to the individual, the average priced onto the person as their rating.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to price the unknown by, no proxy and no discount, only an individual who cannot be read.
With it the agent has a rule: because the group average is the cheapest reliable signal of an unobservable quality, the Prior is what the agent screens by, the rating each applicant is offered set by the group they belong to. The Prior is load bearing because it is the belief the whole loop turns on (Phelps): the group average priced onto the person, so the piece carries the proxy at its root, the rating that will discount a group's return and, in time, be confirmed by the response that discount provokes.

## Apparent

A neutral statistic, a base rate, the sound use of real information under uncertainty.
Seen at the surface, the Prior looks like actuarial good sense, the same an insurer uses on a pool, and the truth that the belief will shape the evidence that proves it, that the rating it sets is what keeps the group's average where the rating assumed, that a base rate acted on is not a neutral fact but a self-confirming one, stays hidden behind how plainly a group average is a real number.

## Yearbook

Held by [the Employer](persona_the_employer.md) through [screening](process_screening.md), the belief the individual is priced by.
It is the root of the trap: the Prior sets [the discount](piece_the_discount.md) on [the signal](piece_the_signal.md), the discount that drives the withholding, and it is what [the confirmation](piece_the_confirmation.md) proves true, the rating the whole [proxy](piece_the_proxy.md) rests on.
