# Process: Confirming

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). The lowered average meeting the rating that caused it, so the prior is made true and the able member priced by the group.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The group's fallen average, and the Standout it cannot distinguish.
At [the gate](place_the_gate.md), the group's real average now as low as the rating assumed, and the able member presenting a full signal the rating discounts.

## Direction

Toward the prior confirmed and the exception overlooked.
Because the group's average has fallen to meet the rating, confirming points the evidence back at the belief that caused it, so the assumed average becomes the produced average and the prior is proven by the investment its rating discouraged, while the able member who did invest is priced by the group, their signal marked down, the counter-evidence the loop refuses to read.

## Lever

The circularity of a belief that shapes its own evidence.
Because the rating discounted the return, the group withheld, and the withholding lowered the average, confirming runs on the loop closing on itself, the belief manufacturing the proof, so the process turns on no new fact, only on the rating meeting the shortfall it made and calling it confirmation.

## Echo

A prior made true, and a casualty it cannot see.
The average has met the rating and the belief is confirmed by its own effect, so what leaves this process is a self-confirming equilibrium, the discrimination proven by the response it provoked, and the able member priced by the group, the exception the equilibrium is built to overlook.
