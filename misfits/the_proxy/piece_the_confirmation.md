# Piece: The Confirmation

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). The lowered average meeting the rating that caused it, the assumed average become the produced average, the prior made true.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Read at [the desk](place_the_desk.md), where the group's fallen average meets the rating that set it.
Flows back from the group's response to the agent's belief, the shortfall returned as proof.

## Load Bearing

Without it the rating would be a mere opinion, and the group's under-investment a separate fact that never touched it.
With it the two close into a loop: because the discount lowered the return, the group withheld, and the withholding lowered the average to exactly where the rating assumed, so the Confirmation is the moment the belief meets the evidence it manufactured. The Confirmation is load bearing because it is what makes the discrimination self-sustaining (Arrow; Coate & Loury): the produced average proving the assumed one, so the piece carries the self-fulfilment, the reason the prior needs no bigot and no new fact to hold, only its own effect returned as its proof.

## Apparent

Vindication, the base rate borne out, the rating shown to have been right all along.
Seen at the surface, the Confirmation is the data agreeing with the belief, and the truth that the belief produced the data, that the group's low average is the response to the rating and not its warrant, that a prior which lowers a group's investment and then reads the lowered average as proof is arguing in a circle, stays hidden behind how convincingly a confirmed prediction reads as a correct one.

## Yearbook

Produced by [the Applicant](persona_the_applicant.md)'s sound response through [confirming](process_confirming.md), the loop closing on itself.
It is the trap made whole: the Confirmation is [the discount](piece_the_discount.md)'s withholding returned as proof of [the prior](piece_the_prior.md), the produced average that upholds the rating and prices [the Standout](persona_the_standout.md) by the group, the self-fulfilment the whole [proxy](piece_the_proxy.md) rests on.
