# Piece: The Discount

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). The lowered return the rated-low member gets on the same signal, the wedge that drives the under-investment.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set at [the gate](place_the_gate.md) by the rating, and felt on [the ground](place_the_ground.md) as the payoff on the signal.
Flows from the prior to the return, the group's rating cut into the reward its members get for the same effort.

## Load Bearing

Without it the signal would pay the same to everyone and no group would have reason to invest less, and the loop would never close.
With it the same investment pays differently by group: because a low-rated applicant is still, in part, priced by their group even with the signal in hand, the return on acquiring it is cut for them, so the Discount is the wedge between the effort and the reward. The Discount is load bearing because it is what turns a belief into a behaviour (Phelps; Arrow): the cut return that makes under-investment the rational answer, so the piece carries the mechanism by which the prior discourages the very investment whose absence will confirm it.

## Apparent

Just caution, the sensible marking-down of a less certain signal, prudence and not penalty.
Seen at the surface, the Discount looks like reasonable risk-adjustment, a lower price for a less reliable proof, and the truth that the lower price is what makes the proof less reliable, that cutting the return on a group's signal is what leads the group to invest less in it, that the caution manufactures the risk it guards against, stays hidden behind how prudent it is to pay less for what you trust less.

## Yearbook

Set by [the Employer](persona_the_employer.md) and borne by [the Applicant](persona_the_applicant.md) and [the Standout](persona_the_standout.md), the cut return at the heart of the loop.
It is the engine of the trap: the Discount is set by [the prior](piece_the_prior.md) onto [the signal](piece_the_signal.md), it drives the withholding that lowers the average, and it makes [the confirmation](piece_the_confirmation.md) that proves the rating, the wedge the whole [proxy](piece_the_proxy.md) turns on.
