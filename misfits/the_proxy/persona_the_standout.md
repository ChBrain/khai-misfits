# Persona: The Standout

## Taxonomy

[The Overlooked](position_the_overlooked.md). The genuinely able member of the low-rated group, who did invest in the signal and is priced by the group anyway, the casualty the loop cannot distinguish.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Able. Credentialled. Priced by the group regardless.
Is the member of the low-rated group who is genuinely good, who acquired the signal at full cost, and who is still rated by the average they exceed.
No fault in it: they did exactly what the market says it wants, and are counted with the group they rose above.

## Action

Acquires the signal in full, the degree, the training, the proof of quality, expecting it to mark them out.
Presents it at the gate and finds it discounted, because a signal from a low-rated group is read as less reliable and the individual is still priced, in part, by the group.
Does what the market claims to reward: invests in quality and shows it, and is priced by the group they cannot signal their way free of.

## Shadow

Not a failure but the loop's casualty, and that is the whole difficulty: the Standout is the evidence that would break the prior, and the prior is exactly what keeps that evidence from being read.
Because the Employer cannot cheaply tell the able member from the group and discounts the signal that would distinguish them, the Standout's real quality goes unpriced, so the one who could disprove the rating is the one the rating refuses to see, and their investment, which the market says it rewards, is discounted for the company it comes in.
It does everything right and is priced by the group, the exception the equilibrium cannot afford to notice.

## Tell

Says "I have the same degree, the same record: what more can I show you."
Has shown everything, and cannot make the signal count, because the rating that discounts their group discounts the proof that would lift them out of it.
