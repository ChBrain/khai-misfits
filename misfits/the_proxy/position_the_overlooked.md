# Position: The Overlooked

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). The seat of the able member who did acquire the signal and is priced by the group anyway, the evidence the prior refuses to read.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A real quality and a signal to prove it: genuine ability, and the credential acquired at full cost to show it.
A clear case: they did exactly what the market says it rewards, invested in quality and can demonstrate it.
No way to be read apart from the group cheaply, and no signal the rating does not discount for the company it comes in.

## Orders

Signal past the discount.
Acquire the signal in full, present it at the gate, and try to be read as the individual the credential proves rather than the group the rating assumes.
Show the quality the market claims to want, and ask to be priced by it.

## Loses

The value of being right, exactly because the rating discounts the proof.
Because the Gate cannot cheaply tell the able member from the group and marks down a signal from a low-rated group, the Overlooked's real quality goes unpriced, so the one who could disprove the rating is the one the rating refuses to see, and the investment the market says it rewards is discounted for the group it comes in.
It holds the evidence that would break the prior and loses it to the prior, the exception the equilibrium cannot afford to notice.

## Drives

Toward a quality real and unread,
a signal held in full and discounted,
so the seat that could disprove the rating is the one the rating is built to overlook.
