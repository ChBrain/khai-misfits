# Process: Mandating

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). The reform mandating the group's hiring and lowering the bar it is held to, and the incentive to invest that the lowered bar can dampen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Reformer, with a mandate and a self-confirming prior to break.
At [the programme](place_the_programme.md), the hiring mandated and the bar lowered, the outside push meant to raise the group's average and let the evidence break the rating.

## Direction

Toward the door opened and, short of a hard enough push, the incentive to clear it lowered.
Because nothing inside the equilibrium will move it, mandating points the correction in from outside, forcing the hire and lowering the bar the group is held to: pushed far enough the forced hiring makes the signal worth acquiring and the group invests, but short of that the lowered bar dampens the very incentive to invest, the group taken without the signal, so the process can point the remedy toward the shortfall it meant to lift.

## Lever

The same rationality, turned on the mandate.
Because the group invests only to the return it is offered, a bar lowered too gently lowers that return, taken without the signal there is less reason to acquire it, so mandating runs into the very response it meant to break, the group answering a lowered bar with lowered investment, no new prejudice needed to turn the remedy aside.

## Echo

A door opened, and a shortfall deepened.
The mandate has lowered the bar and, short of biting hard enough, the group has answered with less investment, so what leaves this process is a prior that survived the help, settled into a patronising equilibrium in which the group is held to a lower bar and kept below it, the remedy feeding the loop it meant to end.
