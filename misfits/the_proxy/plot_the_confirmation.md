# Plot: The Confirmation

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Desk](place_the_desk.md), the group's average fallen to meet the rating.
A produced average now as low as the assumed one, and an able member presenting a full signal the rating discounts.

## Action

[Confirming](process_confirming.md): the lowered average meets the rating that caused it, and the prior is proven by the investment it discouraged.

## Stage

The loop closes as [the confirmation](piece_the_confirmation.md) returns the group's shortfall as proof of [the prior](piece_the_prior.md); and [the Standout](persona_the_standout.md), on [Signal Past the Discount](plan_signal_past_the_discount.md), presents [the signal](piece_the_signal.md) in full and is priced by the group anyway, the counter-evidence the loop cannot tell from the average.
The rating was right on the numbers, and the numbers were made by the rating.

## Tension

The belief is confirmed, and it is confirmed by its own effect.
There is nothing false in the data: the group's average really did fall, and the rating really did predict it. But the fall was the response to the rating, not its warrant, so the plot closes with the prior made true in a circle, the discrimination self-confirming and needing no bigot, and the able member priced by the group they cannot signal free of, the exception the equilibrium is built to overlook.
