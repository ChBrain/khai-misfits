# Plan: Break the Prior

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Reformer](position_the_reformer.md), inside [The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Reformer](position_the_reformer.md)

## Direction

Push the self-confirming prior from outside. Mandate the group's hiring so the door is opened whatever the rating, lowering the bar it is held to, and try to force hiring hard enough that acquiring the signal comes to pay and the group's average rises until the evidence breaks the prior. Change the return the equilibrium sets, since nothing inside it will move on its own.

## Orders

([The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) mandates the hire and lowers the bar the group is held to, and meets a remedy that, short of biting hard, dampens the very investment it needs, the correction that can feed the loop.)

## Implementation

([The Programme](place_the_programme.md); the [mandating](process_mandating.md) process; the Reformer lowers the bar the group is held to from outside, and short of biting hard the lowered bar re-drives [the discount](piece_the_discount.md) on [the signal](piece_the_signal.md), the group taken without it, holding [the prior](piece_the_prior.md) that [the proxy](piece_the_proxy.md) rests on.)

## Targets

- [ ] the hire is mandated and the bar the group is held to is lowered, to raise its average
- [ ] short of biting hard, the lowered bar dampens the incentive to invest, the group taken without the signal
- [ ] the prior survives into a patronising equilibrium, the group held to a lower bar and kept below it
