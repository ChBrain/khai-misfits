# Process: Withholding

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). The low-rated group investing less in the signal because its return is discounted, so the group's average falls.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Applicant, with a discounted return on the signal to answer.
At [the ground](place_the_ground.md), the payoff on the credential lower because the group is rated low, the member weighing the investment against the return it actually pays them.

## Direction

Toward the signal underacquired and the average fallen.
Because the return on the signal is discounted for the group, withholding points each member to invest less in it, so the group acquires less of the credential the market underpays, and the group's real average quality falls, the discount at the gate turned into the shortfall on the ground.

## Lever

The soundness of investing to the return you are offered.
Because a rational person puts less into a thing that pays them less, withholding runs on prudent allocation, not on any failing, so the process turns on no fault of the group, only on each member answering the discounted return exactly as reason recommends.

## Echo

A group's average lowered, by a sound response.
Each member has invested to the return they were offered and the group's average has fallen to meet the rating, so what leaves this process is the shortfall that will confirm the prior, produced by no failing but by the prudent answer of everyone the discount touched, the loop's middle turn.
