# Position: The Queue

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). The seat of the low-rated group deciding whether to invest in the signal, offered a discounted return, and answering it by investing less.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An investment to weigh and a discounted return to weigh it against: the costly signal of quality it might acquire, and the lower payoff its group is offered for holding it.
A clear case: weighing an investment by the return it actually pays is exactly what any rational person does.
No way to earn the full return a higher-rated group earns on the same signal, and every reason to invest less in a thing that pays less.

## Orders

Weigh the return.
Reckon the payoff on the signal, find it discounted because the group is rated low, and invest to that lower return, acquiring less of the credential the market underpays.
Answer the return the market sets, not the return it might have set.

## Loses

Its average, exactly by answering the return soundly.
Because the return on the signal is discounted for the low-rated group, the seat invests less, and the group's real average quality falls, so the sound response to a lowered return is the very move that confirms the rating that lowered it, each prudent choice summing to the assumed average.
It gains a rational allocation and loses the standing that would lift the rating, its own sound answer the thing that makes the rating true.

## Drives

Toward the signal underinvested and the return answered,
the group's average falling to meet the rating,
so the seat that spends its effort rationally is the one that confirms the prior that discounts it.
