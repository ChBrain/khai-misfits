# Place: The Ground

## Taxonomy

[The Proxy](play_the_proxy.md). Where the group weighs the signal against its discounted return and, investing to it, lowers its own average.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The schoolroom, the training floor, the years before the gate, where the signal is acquired or not and the cost is weighed against the return.
The Applicant reckoning whether the credential is worth its price given what their group is paid for it, and investing to that answer.

## Holds

The withholding, and the average bending to the rating.
Here the return the Gate set is felt as the payoff on the signal, so the Ground holds the point where the discount becomes behaviour: the low-rated group, offered less for the same investment, rationally acquires less of it, and the group's real average quality falls to meet the rating, the wedge set at the gate turned into the shortfall that confirms it.

## Offers

A rational allocation of effort, and the shortfall it makes.
Because the Ground is where the group answers the return it is offered, it offers each member the sound choice to invest to the payoff they actually get, handing out prudent allocation, and in the same motion it hands out the fall in the group's average that prudence produces, the sound answer to a discounted return summing to the lowered standing that proves the rating right.

## Withheld

The full return that would call forth the investment, and any sign the shortfall was made.
The Ground can hold the group's response, but it withholds the higher return that would make the signal worth acquiring, that return discounted at the gate, so the investment that would raise the average is never called forth, and, each choice on it so plainly rational, it withholds any sign that the group's shortfall is a response and not a nature, the lowered average reading as what the group is rather than what the rating made it.
