# Position: Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). The seat that clears away what it cannot see a use for.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A clear eye for cost and clutter, and the will to remove them.
A fair question, what is this for, and the standing to act on the silence that answers it.
No sight of the harm the fence prevents, because a prevented harm shows nothing to see.

## Orders

Question every barrier.
Remove what cannot justify itself.
Clear the road of the obstruction no one can explain.

## Loses

Nothing it can measure, and the protection it cannot.
Its ledger books the fence's cost as saved and the fence's benefit as nothing, because the benefit was only ever a disaster that did not occur.
Right on every figure it can see, and wrong about the one it cannot.

## Drives

Toward the cleared road,
away from the unexplained fence,
so the safeguard falls precisely because its success left no evidence to weigh against its cost.
