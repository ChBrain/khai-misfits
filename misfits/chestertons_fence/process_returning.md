# Process: Returning

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). The danger the fence held back coming through the gap, and the reason made plain too late.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An open road and a threat that never left.
The fence gone, the danger that only ever waited on the far side of it, and nothing now between the two.

## Direction

Toward the harm the fence prevented, arriving in full.
The bull through the gap, the flood over the line, the disaster the barrier had made unimaginable, and with it the sudden legibility of what the fence was for.

## Lever

A latent danger, released rather than created.
Removing the fence adds no threat and only uncovers one, so the harm returns at whatever scale it always could have, proving the fence's worth by the exact measure of the damage.

## Echo

The reason understood at the price of the harm.
The purpose recovered from the wreckage, the beneficiary struck by a danger he never knew was near, and a lesson that cost precisely what the fence existed to save.
