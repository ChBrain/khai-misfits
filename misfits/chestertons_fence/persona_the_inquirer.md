# Persona: The Inquirer

## Taxonomy

[Inquirer](position_inquirer.md). The intelligent reformer, who will not let the fence fall until its reason is found.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Patient, sceptical of his own sight, willing to be slow.
Grants the reformer's cost and still refuses the removal, on the ground that not seeing a use is not the same as there being none.
Sends the question back: go and learn why the fence was built, then we will talk of taking it down.

## Action

Holds the fence in place while its reason is hunted through the record and the memory.
Recovers the purpose and weighs it honestly, keeping the fence if it lives and removing it knowingly if it is truly dead.
Buys the road's safety with delay, and sometimes with the labour of justifying a fence that had, in fact, outlived its danger.

## Shadow

His discipline saves the working fence and taxes every other.
It is the one move that answers the misfit, and it is slow, often defeated by a reason lost past recovery, and easy to mistake for mere obstruction, so held too hard it becomes the veneration of every wall no one dares to question.
The cure for the amputation is the same caution that can leave a system fenced in by its own dead rules.

## Tell

"When you can tell me why it was built, I may let you tear it down."
The rule that keeps the road whole, at the price of the time it takes to obey.
