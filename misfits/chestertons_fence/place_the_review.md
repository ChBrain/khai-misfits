# Place: The Review

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). Where the fence is argued over and its fate decided.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A table, an agenda, a line item for the fence and the question of whether it stays.
The reformer with a clear case, the keeper with none, and a decision waiting to be made on the balance of what can be said aloud.

## Holds

The verdict, weighed on visible evidence only.
Here the fence's cost is entered and its benefit cannot be, so the scale tips before the argument starts, and the room decides soundly on everything it is able to see.

## Offers

A fair hearing.
Anyone may state the case for the fence, and the process is honest, which is exactly why it fails: an honest weighing of a plain cost against an unprovable benefit can end only one way.

## Withheld

Any evidence for the defence.
The harm the fence prevents cannot be entered into the record, because it has not happened, so the fence goes to its hearing with everything against it admissible and everything for it a non-event.
