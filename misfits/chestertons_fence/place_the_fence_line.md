# Place: The Fence Line

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). Where the safeguard stands across the road.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A fence set across a way, mended and upright, with no plaque and no note to say why.
A barrier that has always been here, kept by hands that inherited the keeping and not the reason.

## Holds

The safeguard doing its silent work.
Here the fence turns back a danger no one watches for, day after uneventful day, and the very quiet it produces is what empties it of any visible point.

## Offers

A plain, standing cost.
The fence blocks the road, wants mending, and offends the tidy eye, so its price is always in view, legible to anyone who passes and asks what it is doing there.

## Withheld

Its own reason.
The purpose is not written on the fence and not held by the keeper in words, so the barrier stands fully visible and fully unexplained, a cost with its justification missing.
