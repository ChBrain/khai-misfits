# Position: Custodian

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). The seat that keeps the fence, and holds no reason for it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A duty to maintain the fence, inherited whole.
A conviction that it matters, carried in the practice and not in any record.
No argument that will survive the question of why.

## Orders

Keep the fence standing.
Mend it as it has always been mended.
Do it because it is done, for that is all the reason that came down with the task.

## Loses

The argument, and then the fence.
It holds the truth that the fence matters and lacks the words to prove it, so its conviction is dismissed as habit and its caution as inertia.
The last keeper of the reason is the one least able to state it.

## Drives

Toward maintaining the fence,
away from explaining it,
so a real purpose dies in the meeting for want of anyone who can put it into words.
