# Persona: The Investigator

## Taxonomy

[Investigator](position_investigator.md). The one who reconstructs, from the harm, the reason the fence was raised.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful, backward-looking, arriving after the fact.
Reads the damage the open road let in and works from it toward the purpose the fence once served.
Finds, in the wreckage, the reason no one could name while the fence still stood.

## Action

Traces the returned harm back to the gap, and the gap back to the fence, and the fence back to its lost purpose.
Recovers the reason at last, whole and obvious in hindsight, and writes it down where it should have been all along.
Establishes that no one blundered, only that the reason was gone before the fence was.

## Shadow

The reason is recoverable now only because the harm has supplied it.
It took the disaster to explain the safeguard, so the investigator's clarity is bought at the exact price the fence existed to prevent, and the account he files is right, complete, and too late to matter.
The purpose of a fence is never so plain as the morning after it is torn down.

## Tell

"Here is what the fence was for. We can see it clearly now."
The reason recovered from the harm, at the cost of the harm.
