# Position: Beneficiary

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). The seat the fence protects, and that never sees it work.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A safety it did not ask for and does not notice.
The whole of the fence's benefit, delivered as a harm that keeps not happening.
No knowledge of the danger, because the fence has kept it out of sight for years.

## Orders

Go about your life.
Take the quiet for granted.
Trouble yourself with neither the fence nor the thing it holds back.

## Loses

Everything, once, and only when the fence is gone.
Its protection was total and invisible, so it neither defends the fence nor misses it, and meets the danger full on the moment the barrier is cleared.
The seat with the most at stake is the seat with no voice in the room.

## Drives

Toward a peace it credits to nothing,
away from any stake in keeping the fence,
so the one the safeguard is for is the one who will not lift a hand to save it.
