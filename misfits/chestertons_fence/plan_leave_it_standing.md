# Plan: Leave It Standing

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the custodian's seat](position_custodian.md), inside [Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Custodian](position_custodian.md)

## Direction

Keep the fence up as it has always been kept up. Mend it, hold it, and trust the conviction that it matters, even without the words to prove it. When asked why, say the truest thing you have, that it has always been there, and watch it fail to persuade.

## Implementation

## Targets

- [ ] the fence is maintained as inherited, without its reason
- [ ] the conviction that it matters is held and cannot be argued
- [ ] "it has always been there" is offered, and does not persuade
- [ ] the reason dies in the meeting for want of anyone who can state it
