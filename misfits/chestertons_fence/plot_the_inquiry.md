# Plot: The Inquiry

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The archive](place_the_archive.md), another fence and another reformer, but this time a hand raised to stay the removal.
The demand is not to keep the fence but to find its reason first, and the search goes into the record and the memory.

## Action

[Recovering](process_recovering.md): [The Inquirer](persona_the_inquirer.md), holding [the inquirer's seat](position_inquirer.md), grants the cost and refuses the clearing until the purpose is known, while [The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md), holding [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), presses his sound case and calls the delay obstruction.

## Stage

[The Inquirer](persona_the_inquirer.md) tables [Learn Before Tearing Down](plan_learn_before_tearing_down.md): hunt the reason before a plank comes down, so [chesterton's fence](piece_chestertons_fence.md) is answered by [the recovered reason](piece_the_recovered_reason.md) found in time, the live fence kept and the dead one cleared knowingly.
[The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) tables [Clear It Away](plan_clear_it_away.md) still, and the inquiry holds only if its slowness is borne.

## Tension

The one discipline that saves the fence is slow, sometimes futile, and easy to call pedantry.
Sent to learn the reason, the reformer may find it and be glad, or find it gone past recovery, or find the fence truly dead and resent the errand. The inquiry holds the road whole ... or the demand to understand first is judged mere obstruction and overruled, the fence comes down unexamined, and whether it was live or dead is learned, if at all, only from what comes through the gap.
