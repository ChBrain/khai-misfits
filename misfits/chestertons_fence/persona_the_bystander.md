# Persona: The Bystander

## Taxonomy

[Beneficiary](position_beneficiary.md). The one the fence protects, who never knew it was there.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ordinary, untroubled, safe without noticing it.
Passes the fence every day and thinks nothing of it, because nothing has ever happened at it.
Enjoys a peace he credits to no one, having never seen the danger the fence turns back.

## Action

Lives behind the fence and takes the quiet for granted.
Has no stake in the argument to remove it, because he cannot feel a protection he has never seen fail.
Notices the fence only once it is gone, and the danger it held is upon him.

## Shadow

The one with most to lose is the one with least to say.
The benefit is entirely his and entirely invisible to him, so he does not defend the fence, does not miss it, and is struck by a harm he never knew was near, exactly because the fence had kept it far.
Not indifferent: simply unable to value a safety that has only ever shown up as an absence.

## Tell

"That old fence? It has never done anything."
The true report of a beneficiary who mistakes a prevented disaster for no disaster at all.
