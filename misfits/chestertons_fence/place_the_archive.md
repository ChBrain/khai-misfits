# Place: The Archive

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). Where the reason is sought, reconstructed, or found gone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Records, minutes, old memories, the thin trail of why the fence was raised.
Someone bent over the trail, either after the harm to explain it, or before the removal to prevent it.

## Holds

The reason, if it survived, and the labour of recovering it.
Here the forgotten purpose is hunted through what was written and what was only ever practised, and the search is slow, uncertain, and as often as not comes up empty.

## Offers

The chance to know before acting.
What the fence line withheld, the archive may yet supply, so the reason can in principle be recovered and the fence weighed against its true harm rather than its visible cost.

## Withheld

Any guarantee, and any speed.
The reason may be unwritten, half-remembered, or gone with the last keeper, and even when it is there it takes time to find, so the archive answers the question at a cost the quick, clean clearing never pays.
