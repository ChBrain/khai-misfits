# Process: Forgetting

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). The reason fading from memory while the fence still stands.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A purpose carried in a practice, and the passing of the ones who knew it.
The fence built against a danger, the danger kept away, the builders gone, and the reason never written down.

## Direction

Toward a safeguard that stands with its justification stripped away.
The keeping is handed on and the knowing is not, so the fence outlives the memory of why it was raised.

## Lever

Success that erases its own cause.
The better the fence works, the longer the danger stays out of sight, and the longer it is out of sight the more certainly its memory fades, so the fence's own effectiveness is what forgets it.

## Echo

A fence maintained by habit and defended by none.
The purpose gone from every head, the barrier standing on inertia, and a reason that will have to be reconstructed because it was never kept.
