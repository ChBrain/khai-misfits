# Process: Recovering

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). The reason hunted down before the fence is touched, at the cost of the time it takes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A fence questioned, and someone who will not remove it unquestioned.
The reformer's case granted, and the demand that its reason be found before a plank comes down.

## Direction

Toward the fence weighed against its true harm, not its visible cost.
The purpose sought through record and memory, recovered and kept if it lives, and the fence removed knowingly if it is genuinely dead.

## Lever

Doubt held in front of sight.
Not seeing a use is treated as ignorance to be cured rather than proof of uselessness, so the removal waits on an inquiry, and the inquiry buys the road's safety with delay.

## Echo

A safeguard kept for a reason or dropped with eyes open.
The working fence saved, the dead one cleared on purpose, and a discipline paid for in time and in the walls it makes everyone justify before touching.
