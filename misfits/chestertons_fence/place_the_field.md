# Place: The Field

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). What the fence protected, and what the harm returns to.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The ground on the safe side of the fence, quiet and unremarkable, the bystander crossing it without a thought.
After the clearing: the same ground, open now, and the thing the fence held back moving onto it.

## Holds

The benefit and then the harm, in the same place.
Here the fence's work shows as nothing at all, an ordinary field where nothing goes wrong, until the barrier is gone and the field becomes the scene of exactly the disaster the fence had made unimaginable.

## Offers

Peace, for as long as the fence stands.
The field is safe, pleasant, and taken for granted, and its safety looks like a property of the field itself rather than a gift of the fence at its edge.

## Withheld

Any warning.
The field gives no sign of the danger the fence turns back, so the calm reads as proof there was never a threat, right up until the threat arrives and the calm is revealed to have been the fence's doing.
