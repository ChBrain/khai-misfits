# Position: Investigator

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). The seat that recovers the reason after the harm has supplied it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The wreckage the open road let in, and the method to read it.
The reason at last, made legible by the very disaster the fence prevented.
No power to act on it, because the fence it explains is already gone.

## Orders

Trace the harm to the gap.
Trace the gap to the fence, and the fence to its lost purpose.
Write down the reason where it should have been kept.

## Loses

Nothing but the timing, which is everything.
Its account is correct and complete and arrives the morning after, so the clarity it wins changes nothing about the fence and only names what the harm already proved.
The reason is cheapest to recover exactly when it is too late to use.

## Drives

which the harm alone made plain,
so understanding arrives on the far side of the disaster the fence existed to prevent.
