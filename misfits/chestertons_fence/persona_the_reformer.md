# Persona: The Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Reformer](position_reformer.md). The one who sees a fence, sees no use in it, and moves to clear it away.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Rational, tidy, confident he is improving things.
Asks of every rule and every barrier what it is for, and takes the absence of an answer as an answer.
Sees the fence's cost plainly and its purpose nowhere, and concludes, soundly, that it has none.

## Action

Clears away what no one can justify.
Lays out the price of the fence and the silence where its reason should be, and wins the argument on both.
Tears out the obstruction and opens the road, having reasoned every step correctly.

## Shadow

He is most sure the fence is useless exactly when it is doing its work.
A safeguard that prevents a harm produces a non-event, so the better it works the emptier its ledger looks, and his honest question, what is this for, is one a prevented disaster can never answer.
Not a wrecker: a good reformer, tearing out the fence because it succeeded too quietly to defend itself.

## Tell

"I don't see the use of this, so let us clear it away."
The reasonable sentence under which the bull comes back through the gap.
