# Persona: The Keeper

## Taxonomy

[Custodian](position_custodian.md). The one who maintains the fence, and cannot say why.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Dutiful, habitual, uneasy under questioning.
Mends the fence and keeps it standing because it has always been kept standing.
Feels in his bones that it matters, and has no words for the feeling that will survive a meeting.

## Action

Defends the fence with the only reason he has, which is that it has always been there.
Maintains it faithfully and argues for it feebly, because the purpose lived in a practice, not a record, and the practice is all he inherited.
Loses the case, every time, to the one who asks for a reason he cannot produce.

## Shadow

His inability to explain the fence is taken as proof there is nothing to explain.
He is right that it matters and wrong-footed that he cannot say how, so his conviction reads as mere habit and his caution as mere inertia, and the truth he guards dies for want of an argument.
The last man who knows the fence should stand is the man least able to keep it standing.

## Tell

"We have always kept it up. That is how it is done here."
True, and worthless in the room, because a custom is not a reason anyone will accept for a cost.
