# Pitch: Clear the Road

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Rational, reforming, impatient with clutter.
Clear out what serves no purpose, cut the cost, take down the obstruction that no one can explain and no one will miss.
The voice of sensible improvement, spoken by people who ask what a thing is for and, hearing no answer, quite reasonably conclude it is for nothing.

## Undertow

Under the good sense, a fence that works by making sure nothing happens.
Its cost is plain, it blocks the road and wants mending, and its benefit is a harm no one alive has seen, because the fence has held that harm back for as long as anyone remembers, so the ledger shows all price and no gain.
No one is careless: they only ask a fair question, and the true answer, that the fence prevents a disaster precisely by never letting it occur, is the one answer a non-event can never provide.

## Nerve

The feeling of clean, confident reform,
every removal justified, every cost named, every obstruction questioned,
the road cleared of a fence whose only fault was that it had done its job so well that no one remembered the job.

## Echo

After the run: an open road and a returning danger,
a reason reconstructed too late from the harm it failed to prevent,
and a reformer who did everything right and tore out the one thing holding the bull.
