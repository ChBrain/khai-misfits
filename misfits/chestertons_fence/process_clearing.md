# Process: Clearing

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). The fence removed on the sound argument that no one can say what it is for.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A reformer's fair question and the silence that answers it.
The cost of the fence plain, its reason absent, and no one able to exhibit the harm it prevents.

## Direction

Toward an open road and a removed safeguard.
The barrier weighed on what can be seen, found wanting, and taken down cleanly and with good arguments.

## Lever

A cost that shows against a benefit that cannot.
The fence's price is admissible and its use is a non-event, so an honest weighing tips against it every time, and the removal is not a blunder but the correct call on the visible evidence.

## Echo

A cleared road and a danger no longer held.
The obstruction gone, the ledger apparently improved, and the thing the fence stood against now free to return through a gap that looks like pure gain.
