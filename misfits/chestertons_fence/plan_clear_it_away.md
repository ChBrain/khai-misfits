# Plan: Clear It Away

## Direction

Ask what the fence is for, and when no one can answer, take the silence as your warrant. Lay out its cost, note the absence of any use, and clear the road of an obstruction that cannot justify itself. Reason every step correctly, and remove it.

## Orders
