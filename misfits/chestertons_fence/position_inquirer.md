# Position: Inquirer

## Taxonomy

[Chesterton's Fence](play_chestertons_fence.md). The seat that demands the reason before it will allow the removal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The authority to stay the reformer's hand.
The rule that not seeing a use is not the same as there being none.
The patience to send the question back and wait for a real answer.

## Orders

Hold the fence until its reason is found.
Send the reformer to learn why it was built.
Keep it if the reason lives, remove it knowingly if the reason is dead.

## Loses

Time, and sometimes the argument for a fence worth losing.
It buys the road's safety with delay, and pays again whenever the reason is lost past recovery or the fence had truly outlived its danger and still had to be justified before anyone could touch it.
The discipline that saves the working fence taxes every fence alike.

## Drives

Toward the reason learned before the fence is touched,
away from the clean, quick clearing,
so the safeguard is weighed against its harm at last, at the price of the time it takes to weigh it.
