# Plan: Make It Legible

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Centre](position_the_centre.md), inside [The Straight Rows](play_the_straight_rows.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Centre](position_the_centre.md)

## Direction

See the domain so it can be governed. Reduce the tangle to a legible grid, replant the forest in countable rows, flatten the customary tenure into owned squares, straighten the quarter into a diagrammable plan, and standardise the domain until it can be read and run from the centre. Impose the map, since a centre cannot act on what it cannot hold in a table, and what cannot be recorded cannot be managed at all.
