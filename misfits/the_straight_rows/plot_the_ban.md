# Plot: The Ban

## Taxonomy

[The Straight Rows](play_the_straight_rows.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Village](place_the_village.md), the plan come down where a customary order held the place.
A practitioner whose mētis kept the street and the shared rotation, and a legible scheme that has no line for it.

## Action

[Erasing](process_erasing.md): the custom is overruled by the plan and the unplanned order razed as disorder, the working knowledge cleared.

## Stage

[The Practitioner](persona_the_practitioner.md), on [Work by Feel](plan_work_by_feel.md), holds [the mētis](piece_the_metis.md) against [the map](piece_the_map.md) that cannot record it, and is cleared with the tangle the survey read as disorder, the knowing that ran the place banned because it could not be written.
The mētis is not refuted, only unread, and what the survey cannot see it treats as not there.

## Tension

The working knowledge is real and carries the community, and it is cleared not for failing but for being unwritten.
There is no malice: a legible order can keep only what it can record, and a knowing that lives in the doing is exactly what a survey cannot hold. But clearing the plan's disorder clears the mētis with it, so the plot closes with the customary order banned or built over, the place stripped of the hand that ran it and no one having meant it any harm.
