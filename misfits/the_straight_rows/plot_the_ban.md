# Plot: The Ban

## Taxonomy

[The Straight Rows](play_the_straight_rows.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Forest](place_the_forest.md), the grid come down where a working knowledge held the place.
A practitioner whose métis ran the mixed wood, and a legible order that has no line for it.

## Action

[Erasing](process_erasing.md): the mixed stand is felled for the rows and the custom overruled by the plan, the working knowledge cleared as disorder.

## Stage

[The Practitioner](persona_the_practitioner.md), on [Work by Feel](plan_work_by_feel.md), holds [the métis](piece_the_metis.md) against [the map](piece_the_map.md) that cannot record it, and is cleared with the underbrush, the knowing that ran the place banned because it could not be written.
The métis is not refuted, only unread, and what the survey cannot see it treats as not there.

## Tension

The working knowledge is real and carries the domain, and it is cleared not for failing but for being unwritten.
There is no malice: a legible order can keep only what it can record, and a knowing that lives in the doing is exactly what a survey cannot hold. But clearing the grid's disorder clears the métis with it, so the plot closes with the working knowledge banned or left to die, the domain stripped of the hand that ran it and no one having meant it any harm.
