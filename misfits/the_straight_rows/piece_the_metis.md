# Piece: The Mētis

## Taxonomy

[The Straight Rows](play_the_straight_rows.md). The tacit, situated knowledge that ran the working system, learned in the doing and beyond any survey to record.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Lives at [the forest](place_the_forest.md) and [the village](place_the_village.md), in the hands that work them, and nowhere on [the survey](place_the_survey.md).
Flows through the practice and not apart from it, the knowledge of the particular place that exists only in the doing of the work.

## Load Bearing

Without it the domain would be no more than the grid says it is, and clearing it would cost nothing and the collapse would never come.
With it the domain has the knowledge that actually carried it: the mixed stand and the rotation and the unplanned order, the thousand tacit adjustments that held the system and no rule specified. The Mētis is load bearing because it is what the grid cannot record and the system relied on (Detienne and Vernant; Hayek): the working knowledge cleared to make the place legible, so the piece carries the missing function of the trap, the intelligence that ran the domain and whose absence is the ruin, real and unwritten and gone the moment the map presses down.

## Apparent

Just disorder, or backwardness, the tangle and superstition a rational plan improves away.
Seen at the surface, the Mētis looks like the mess a survey tidies, the mixed wood and the odd custom that a proper method replaces, and the truth that it is a working knowledge the grid cannot see, that its untidiness is the shape of a real intelligence, that clearing it clears the thing that made the place run, stays hidden behind how much unwritten knowledge looks like no knowledge at all.

## Yearbook

Held by [the Practitioner](persona_the_practitioner.md), unread by [the Planner](persona_the_planner.md), and missed too late by [the Successor](persona_the_successor.md), the knowledge the whole order is missing.
It is the absence at the heart of the trap: the Mētis is what [erasing](process_erasing.md) clears, its missing function is what [the blight](piece_the_blight.md) is made of, and it is what [the map](piece_the_map.md) had no line for, the working intelligence the whole [legible order](piece_the_legible_order.md) razed to be read.
