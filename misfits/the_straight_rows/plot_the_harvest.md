# Plot: The Harvest

## Taxonomy

[The Straight Rows](play_the_straight_rows.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Forest](place_the_forest.md), the first rotation coming in heavy.
A legible order newly imposed, and a harvest that will be read as its proof.

## Action

[Vindicating](process_vindicating.md): the first yield delivers on schedule, and the method is trusted, doubled down on, and spread.

## Stage

[The Planner](persona_the_planner.md) counts [the yield](piece_the_yield.md) at [the survey](place_the_survey.md) and carries the method to the next forest, the heavy first rotation proving the grid, though the harvest was drawn on [the métis](piece_the_metis.md) the plan had already cleared.
The success is real, and it is borrowed, a proof grown on a reserve the clearing spent.

## Tension

The first yield genuinely delivers, and its delivery is what entrenches the order the collapse is already inside.
There is nothing false: the harvest is heavy and on schedule, and the plan looks vindicated because it is, for this one rotation. But the yield drew on the century of soil and the web the grid cleared, so the plot closes with the method proven and spread on the strength of a success it cannot repeat, the very harvest that answers every doubt hiding the blight it was borrowed from.
