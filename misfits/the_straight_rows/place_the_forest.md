# Place: The Forest

## Taxonomy

[The Straight Rows](play_the_straight_rows.md). The flagship ground, the wild wood remade as legible monoculture rows, where the grid delivers one rotation and then dies.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The scientific forest, the wild mixed wood cleared and replanted as one species, even-aged, in straight countable rows, the underbrush gone and the yield tabled.
The Planner reading a stand it can at last measure, the Practitioner who knew the mixed wood overruled, and, a generation on, the Successor among trees that are beginning to sicken.

## Holds

The grid pressed onto living ground, and the collapse it is growing toward.
Here the domain is a system that ran on what the survey could not see, so the Forest holds the whole arc in one place: the wild wood remade into rows for a legible yield, the mixed species and the soil web and the century of fallen wood cleared away with the disorder, and the slow death that follows when a plantation is run on only what the ledger records.
The Forest is where the map is pressed down hardest and where the ground answers, a legible order thriving over a reserve it is spending.

## Offers

A countable yield, and a domain the centre can finally manage.
Because the Forest is remade to be read, it offers the Planner a stand it can measure and harvest on schedule, handing out real revenue and real control, and in the same motion it offers a plantation with no reserve, the ecology that fed the wild wood cleared to make the rows, so the yield and the coming blight are handed out together.

## Withheld

The living web that fed the wood, and any yield past the first that the grid could keep.
The Forest can be replanted in rows and read from a table, but it withholds the mixed stand and the soil and the fungal and insect webs that no survey recorded and the plantation did not keep, so it withholds the one thing that would let the second rotation stand, and delivers a legible order that thrives once and dies on the ground the map cleared.
