# Piece: The Blight

## Taxonomy

[The Straight Rows](play_the_straight_rows.md). The slow collapse of the legible system run on the grid alone, the second rotation sick and the third dead.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Sets in at [the forest](place_the_forest.md), the second rotation and the third, long after the Yield was counted at [the survey](place_the_survey.md).
Flows from the cleared ground, not from any single failure, the collapse made of the function the métis carried and the grid did not keep.

## Load Bearing

Without it the clearing would cost nothing and the legible order would stand, and the loss of the métis would be no loss at all.
With it the reckoning arrives: because the domain now runs on only what the grid can see, the Blight is the function of the cleared métis failing, the soil unfed and the web gone, the second rotation sick and the third dead. The Blight is load bearing because it is where the missing knowledge is felt as ruin (Scott; Lowood): invisible in the heavy first yield and manufactured by the clearing that preceded it, so the piece carries the collapse the trap produces, a slow death with no single cause and no villain, only a legible order run over a knowledge it razed to be read.

## Apparent

Just bad luck, or a fixable problem, a pest or a poor season the plan can be adjusted around.
Seen at the surface, the Blight looks like a setback the method can correct, a stand that failed for reasons a better plan will address, and the truth that the cause is the clearing itself, that no adjustment restores a knowledge never recorded, that the legible order is failing on exactly the ground it made legible, stays hidden behind how much a slow collapse looks like a run of ordinary trouble.

## Yearbook

Met by [the Successor](persona_the_successor.md), unforeseen by [the Planner](persona_the_planner.md), and foretold only by the cleared [Practitioner](persona_the_practitioner.md), the ruin the order grew toward.
It is the reckoning of the trap: the Blight is what [hollowing](process_hollowing.md) delivers, it is [the métis](piece_the_metis.md) felt as a missing function, and it is the debt hidden in [the yield](piece_the_yield.md), the collapse the whole [legible order](piece_the_legible_order.md) was growing toward from the first straight row.
