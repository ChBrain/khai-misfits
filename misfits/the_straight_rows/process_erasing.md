# Process: Erasing

## Taxonomy

[The Straight Rows](play_the_straight_rows.md). The grid clearing the mētis it cannot record, the working knowledge displaced as disorder.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The grid coming down on the working domain, and the Practitioner whose knowledge it cannot hold.
At [the forest](place_the_forest.md) and [the village](place_the_village.md), the mixed stand is felled for the rows and the custom overruled by the plan, the mētis that ran the place cleared as the legible order arrives.

## Direction

Toward the working knowledge displaced.
Because the grid keeps only what it can measure, erasing points at the mētis: the tacit, particular knowledge that carried the system is unwritten and so unread, and what the survey cannot see it treats as not there, so the mixed stand and the custom and the unplanned order are banned, cleared, or left to die when the plan is imposed.

## Lever

The unrecordable met by a format that keeps only the recorded.
Because mētis lives in the doing and cannot be rendered into a rule or a table, erasing runs on no hostility to it, only the plain fact that a legible order has no line for a knowing it cannot write, so the working knowledge is cleared not because it failed but because it could not be seen, no malice needed to raze what the map cannot hold.

## Echo

A domain thinned to what the grid can read.
What leaves this process is a place stripped of the mētis that ran it, the tacit knowledge gone with the underbrush and the custom, so the legible order stands where the working knowledge was, and the domain is left to run on only what the map records, its reserve already spent before the yield is even counted.
