# Piece: The Map

## Taxonomy

[The Straight Rows](play_the_straight_rows.md). The legible grid the centre imposes, the survey and plan and standard that reduce the domain to what can be read from a distance.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Drawn at [the survey](place_the_survey.md) and pressed onto [the forest](place_the_forest.md) and [the village](place_the_village.md), the representation made real on the ground.
Flows from the centre to the domain, the grid that lets the place be read and, imposed, remakes the place to match it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the centre could not govern the domain at all, and there would be no order pressed onto the ground to clear what it cannot hold.
With it the centre has its reach and its blindness in one tool: the Map keeps only what it can measure, so it is both the instrument that lets a distant centre tax and plan and manage, and the format that drops the tacit and particular. The Map is load bearing because it is the grid the whole trap is drawn in (Scott): a representation thinner than the domain, taken for the domain and pressed down onto it, so the piece carries the reach that governs and the reduction that hollows, the same lines doing both.

## Apparent

Just the domain itself, seen clearly at last, the place made knowable and manageable.
Seen at the surface, the Map looks like knowledge of the territory, an order finally legible, and the truth that it is a reduction that keeps the countable and drops the rest, that reading is not knowing, that the lines leave out the very thing that made the place work, stays hidden behind how complete a good map looks.

## Yearbook

Drawn by [the Planner](persona_the_planner.md), imposed on the domain, and inherited by [the Successor](persona_the_successor.md), the grid the whole order is read in.
It is the instrument of the trap: the Map is what [simplifying](process_simplifying.md) produces, it is pressed down to clear [the mētis](piece_the_metis.md) it cannot record, and it is the thin frame that becomes [the legible order](piece_the_legible_order.md), the reach that governs the domain and the reduction that cannot keep it alive.
