# Position: Peer

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md). The seat of the like organisations already shaped, copied under uncertainty and copying in turn, whose numbers make the recognised form the obvious answer and whose sameness confers mutual legitimacy.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A shape already taken and a field watching it: the recognised form the seat has adopted and the standing of being one of many that have it.
A real pull: under uncertainty, copying what the credible others do is the sound guess, so the seat's shape is a reasonable model for the next organisation.
No reason it can give for the shape beyond that the others have it, the form its own warrant.

## Orders

Match the field.
Adopt what the credible others have adopted, copy the recognised shape under uncertainty, and be one more organisation that has the form.
Do what is done, because doing what is done is the safe guess when no one knows what will work.

## Loses

The independent guess, and with it any test of whether the shape works, exactly by copying the field.
Because uncertainty makes the peers' form the safest bet and each adoption makes it more the norm, the seat copies and is copied, so the field converges on a shape no one tested and each took because the others had it, the sameness emerging from a thousand sound decisions to match the field and standing on no ground but its own spread.
It gains the safety of the shape everyone has, and loses the chance that a shape fitted to the work was ever tried.

## Drives

Toward the field matched and the shape copied,
the recognised form taken because the others took it,
so the seat that matches the field converges it, and the form spreads by being the form the field already has.
