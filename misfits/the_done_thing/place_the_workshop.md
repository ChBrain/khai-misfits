## Shown

The place a shape is built from the work outward, a room where the structure is designed to the labour, the odd lean form made to suit the problem it must serve.
The Outlier fitting the organisation to its actual task, dropping the recognised parts that do not fit and building the ones the work calls for.

## Holds

The fitting, and the fit it alone produces.
Here the work sets the shape, so the Workshop holds the point where the form answers the labour: an organisation built to its own problem rather than to the field's reading, so it is where the fitting shape is made, the one arrangement in which the structure serves the work it was meant to organise and not the audience that grants it standing.

## Offers

A form that fits the work, and the labour served that the recognised shape was built above.
Because the Workshop builds from the problem outward, it offers the one thing the recognised form cannot, a structure suited to the organisation's actual task, so it hands out the fit the trap prices out, and hands it out at the cost of the standing the odd shape spends, a form the field cannot read bought against the legitimacy the done thing was the way to keep.

## Withheld

Any legibility to the field, or any daily proof the fit was worth it.
The Workshop can build the fitting form but cannot make the field read it as proper or make its value show on an ordinary day, because the shape is unfamiliar and the standing it costs is charged at once while the fit repays slowly, so it withholds the recognition the recognised form had, exacting the legitimacy continuously and returning the fit only in the work, and, all suited shape and no welcome, it withholds the field's backing until the odd form is proven or abandoned for the done thing.
