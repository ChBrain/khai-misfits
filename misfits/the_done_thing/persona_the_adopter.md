# Persona: The Adopter

## Taxonomy

[Adopter](position_adopter.md). The organisation that takes on the legitimate form to be legible to the field it depends on, installing the recognised shape for the standing it confers rather than the work it does.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Reasonable. Respectable. Legible to the field.
Is the organisation adopting the recognised form, the firm standing up the strategy office, the charity writing the framework, the agency installing the compliance function every credible peer has.
No foolishness in it: the Adopter reads the field correctly, the form really is what funders and regulators and recruits recognise as proper, and being recognised is survival.

## Action

Installs the recognised structure, the office, the function, the framework, and looks, to the field it depends on, like a real organisation.
Adopts the form that is legible to the audience that grants legitimacy, whether or not it suits the work the organisation actually does.
Does what an organisation under a legitimacy-granting field does: it takes the recognised shape, because the recognised shape is what the field funds and trusts.

## Shadow

Not fooled and not a fraud, an organisation reading its field correctly, and that is the whole difficulty: the legitimacy is real, and adopting the form for real legitimacy is exactly what takes the shape chosen for standing over the shape that would fit the work.
Because the field grants standing to what it recognises and standing is funding and survival, the Adopter installs the recognised form, and by installing it becomes one more organisation that has it, so the form is more the mark of a proper organisation and the fitting shape more the deviant one, the Adopter's rational move adding to a convergence it did not choose and cannot see from inside.
It adopts the recognised form for real reasons, and adopting it is what carries the shape built for the field's recognition and not its own work.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "this is what a serious organisation looks like."
Says it having read the field correctly, and cannot easily tell, from inside, the form that fits its work from the form that fits its standing, only which one the field will fund.
