# Pitch: In Good Standing

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Reasonable, respectable, proper.
The plain good sense of adopting the recognised form, the structure every credible organisation has, so the field reads you as a real organisation and the funder and the regulator and the recruit know what they are looking at.
Nothing false in it: the legitimacy the form confers is real, and being legible to the field you depend on is funding, credibility, and survival.

## Undertow

Under the respectability, a field of organisations each adopting the legitimate form for its legitimacy, converging on one shape chosen for standing and not for fit.
Because every organisation reasons the same way, the form spreads until it is the mark of a proper organisation and the fitting shape is the deviant one, so structures answer to the audience that grants legitimacy rather than the work they were meant to organise, and the field locks alike.
What makes the form worth adopting, that the field grants it standing, is exactly what cages the field in it.

## Nerve

The feeling of installing the recognised form and being read, at last, as proper,
sure it is only sense, because the funder does look for it and looking proper is survival,
while the shape you took fits the field's recognition and not your own work, and the form that would fit your work is now the one that looks like you do not belong.

## Echo

After the run: a field converged on one shape, each organisation carrying the overhead of looking proper,
no one having authored the sameness and no one having faked, only each adopting the recognised form for real reasons and all confirming it,
and the one move that would keep the fit, building the form the work needs and asking the field to read the unfamiliar as proper, forgone because it spends the very legitimacy the done thing was the way to keep.
