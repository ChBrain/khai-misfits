# Piece: The Mandate

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md). The coercive pressure, the requirement or dependency that forces the recognised form, the regulation, funder condition, or standard an organisation must meet to operate.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Enforced through [the grant](place_the_grant.md), where the form is a condition of the resources.
Felt across [the field](place_the_field.md), where the requirement shapes every dependent organisation alike.

## Load Bearing

Without it one of the three pressures is missing, and the form spreads only by copying and expectation.
With it the trap has its hard edge: the recognised form required outright, by the regulator that mandates the function, the funder that conditions the grant on the structure, the larger body an organisation depends on, so adoption is not merely prudent but compelled (DiMaggio and Powell, on coercive isomorphism, the pressure exerted by those an organisation depends upon). The Mandate is load bearing because it is the pressure that leaves no choice: where dependence runs, the form is a condition of operating, so the piece carries the requirement that shapes dependent organisations to a common form whether or not it fits, the hard root of the convergence.

## Apparent

A reasonable requirement, a standard any responsible body should meet, the rules of operating properly.
Seen at the surface, the Mandate looks like sound regulation, the conditions a real organisation naturally satisfies, and the truth that it forces a common form on organisations of every kind of work, that the required shape answers to the mandating body and not the labour, that dependence and not fit is doing the shaping, stays hidden behind how reasonable a requirement to be properly set up sounds.

## Yearbook

Imposed on [the adopter](persona_the_adopter.md) by [the patron](persona_the_patron.md) and the bodies it depends on, through [backing](process_backing.md), the pressure that requires the form.
It is one of the trap's three pressures: the Mandate compels [the form](piece_the_form.md) where [the example](piece_the_example.md) and [the credential](piece_the_credential.md) only draw, forcing [the fit](piece_the_fit.md) aside and welding [the cage](piece_the_cage.md) where dependence runs.
