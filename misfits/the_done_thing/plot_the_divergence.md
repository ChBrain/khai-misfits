# Plot: The Divergence

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Workshop](place_the_workshop.md), and an organisation unwilling to carry a form that does not fit its work.
The Outlier setting out to build the shape its labour calls for and to ask the field to read the unfamiliar as proper.

## Action

[Fitting](process_fitting.md): [The Outlier](persona_the_outlier.md), holding [the outlier's seat](position_outlier.md), builds the form the work needs and drops the recognised structure that does not serve it.

## Stage

[The Outlier](persona_the_outlier.md), on [Fit the Problem](plan_fit_the_problem.md), cuts [the fit](piece_the_fit.md) to the labour over [the form](piece_the_form.md), and asks [the patron](persona_the_patron.md) to grant legitimacy to the unfamiliar shape.
Against the divergence stand [the mandate](piece_the_mandate.md), [the example](piece_the_example.md), and [the credential](piece_the_credential.md), and an ear that reads the odd shape as amateur.

## Tension

Building the fit is the one move that opens the cage, and it asks the standing the recognised form was the way to keep.
It spends the legitimacy the field grants the recognised form, asks the patron to back the unfamiliar, and carries the reading of unserious the odd shape earns. So the plot closes one of two ways: the fit built at the cost of standing and the field brought, slowly, to read it as proper ... or the odd shape read as amateur, the grant lost, and the done thing taken up after all.
