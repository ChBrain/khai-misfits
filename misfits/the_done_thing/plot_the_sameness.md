# Plot: The Sameness

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

The field seen whole, and every organisation running the recognised form.
The convergence complete, the shape everywhere alike, and no one able to point to who chose it.

## Action

[Converging](process_converging.md) come to its sum: the field is locked into one shape, the recognised form now the condition of belonging and the fit the deviant option.

## Stage

Every [adopter](persona_the_adopter.md), [patron](persona_the_patron.md), and [peer](persona_the_peer.md) has done its rational part, so [the form](piece_the_form.md) is the field's whole shape and [the cage](piece_the_cage.md) is closed.
No organisation chose the sameness and none enforces it, yet each is held to it, the fitting form now the one that looks like it does not belong and the overhead of looking proper carried by all.

## Tension

Each organisation adopted the recognised form for real reasons, and the sum of every real reason is a cage no one built.
There is no author and no villain: the mandate, the example, and the credential each drew a sound adoption. But sound adoptions sum to a field locked into one shape selected for standing and not for fit, so the plot closes with the sameness complete, the fitting form priced out of reach, and a whole field running the shape it took to be proper and none of it tested against the work.
