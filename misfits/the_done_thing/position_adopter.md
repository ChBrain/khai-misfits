# Position: Adopter

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md). The seat that takes on the legitimate form for the standing it confers, where adopting the recognised shape for real legitimacy takes it over the shape that would fit the work.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A field to be legible to and a form it recognises: the standing to install the recognised structure and the dependence that makes standing survival.
A real basis: the legitimacy the form confers is genuine, so adopting it is prudent, not vain.
No way, from inside, to tell the form that fits its work from the form that fits its standing, only which the field will fund.

## Orders

Take the form.
Install the recognised structure the field reads as proper, the office, the function, the framework every credible peer has, and be legible to the funders, regulators, and recruits the organisation depends on.
Adopt the recognised shape, because the recognised shape is what the field funds and trusts.

## Loses

The fit, exactly by taking the form that earns the standing.
Because the field grants legitimacy to what it recognises and the recognised form is chosen for that recognition, the seat installs a shape built for the audience and not the work, so what the arrangement loses is the organisation's own fit: a structure that answers to the field's reading rather than to the labour it was meant to organise, and, by being adopted, one more push on the convergence the seat cannot see from inside.
It gains the standing the recognised form confers, and loses the shape its work actually needed.

## Drives

Toward the recognised form taken and the fit given up,
the shape chosen for the field's reading and not the work,
so the seat that adopts to be proper adds to the mould, and the form it took fits its standing and not its labour.
