# Piece: The Credential

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md). The normative pressure, the professionalisation that carries the recognised form, the trained expectation of consultants, schools, and professions that a proper organisation is shaped this way.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Carried into [the field](place_the_field.md) by the professionals and consultants trained to the form.
Expected at [the grant](place_the_grant.md), where the credentialed reviewer looks for the shape they were taught is proper.

## Load Bearing

Without it the form spreads only by force and imitation, and the shared shape has no teachers.
With it the trap has its self-renewing pressure: the professions, the business schools, the consultancies all trained to the recognised form carry it into every organisation they staff and advise, so the shape is expected as a matter of what a competent professional builds (DiMaggio and Powell, on normative isomorphism, professionalisation as a source of common structure). The Credential is load bearing because it is the pressure that reproduces itself through people: the trained expectation that a proper organisation is shaped this way, so the piece carries the professionalised norm that installs the form wherever its holders go and expects it wherever they review, teaching the convergence into being.

## Apparent

Professional standards, expertise, the shape competent people naturally build.
Seen at the surface, the Credential looks like knowledge applied, trained professionals building organisations properly, and the truth that the professions were themselves trained to one recognised form, that the expertise reproduces a shape rather than tests it, that credentialed expectation installs the form regardless of the work, stays hidden behind how much professional expectation reads as competence rather than convergence.
