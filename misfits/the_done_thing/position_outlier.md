# Position: Outlier

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md). The seat that would build the form its work needs and ask the field to grant legitimacy to the unfamiliar, at the cost of the standing the recognised form was the way to keep.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A problem the recognised form does not fit and the will to build for it: the standing to shape the organisation to its work and the readiness to look unlike the field.
A real corrective: fitting the form to the work is the one thing that serves the labour the recognised shape was built above.
No legibility to the field in the fitting shape, and no cheapness to standing on it.

## Orders

Fit the problem.
Build the form the work needs, drop the recognised structure that does not fit, and ask the field to read the unfamiliar shape as proper.
Shape the organisation to its labour, not to the field's recognition, and pay the standing the fit costs.

## Loses

The standing the recognised form confers, and the goodwill of the field it depends on.
Because the recognised form is what the field funds and the fitting form is not, the seat must spend the legitimacy the done thing was the way to keep, ask the patron to back the unfamiliar, and carry the reading of amateur and unserious the odd shape earns, so the fit that alone serves the work is bought against every pressure that made the recognised form safe and lost the moment the grant is.
It gains the form its work actually needed, and loses the standing that fitting shape costs.

## Drives

Toward the fit built and the standing spent,
the form shaped to the work and illegible to the field,
so the seat that fits the problem pays for it, carrying the shape the field reads as not belonging.
