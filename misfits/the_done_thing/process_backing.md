# Process: Backing

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md). The Patron placing resources on the recognised form as the warrant of a real organisation, the reward that makes the form pay and prices the fitting shape out.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Resources to place and a need to place them on what can be read.
At [the grant](place_the_grant.md), the patron reviewing an organisation for the recognised form and granting the funding, licence, or deal to the one that has it.

## Direction

Toward the recognised form rewarded and the fit passed.
Because the granter must place its resources on a legible warrant and the recognised form is that warrant, backing grants to the shape it can read over the shape it would have to investigate, so the resources flow to the recognised form, and by flowing make it pay, rewarding its adoption across the field and starving the fitting shape that carries no legible sign.

## Lever

The need to place resources on what can be judged.
Because a granter is accountable and must back a legible warrant, backing the recognised form is prudent under uncertainty, so the process runs not on caprice but on the plain need for a sign it can defend, the sameness funded from no favouritism, only from resources placed, reasonably, on the one shape the granter could read as proper.

## Echo

Resources placed, and the recognised form made the price of standing.
The patron has backed the legible warrant in good faith, so what reads as prudent funding is, in truth, the reward that pays every organisation to adopt the recognised form, the granter's prudence converging a field it never meant to and reads, when it sees it, as the field simply being proper.
