# Process: Fitting

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md). The building of a form to the actual problem, the shaping of the organisation to its work rather than the field's recognition, the costly corrective that opens the cage.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A problem the recognised form does not fit and someone unwilling to carry it anyway.
At [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), the outlier building the shape the labour calls for and dropping the recognised parts that do not serve it.

## Direction

Toward the form fitted to the work and the standing spent.
Because the recognised shape answers to the field and not the labour, fitting builds from the problem outward, cutting the structure to the organisation's actual task and asking the field to read the unfamiliar as proper, so the fit is served and the recognised form dropped, the shape suited to the work bought against the legitimacy the done thing was the way to keep.

## Lever

The willingness to spend the standing the fit costs.
Because the recognised form is what the field funds and the fitting shape is not, fitting runs only on someone choosing to pay the legitimacy the divergence costs, so the process is exacted at once and repaid slowly, the corrective assembled from no cheap trick, only from an organisation willing to look, to the field it depends on, like it does not belong, for the sake of the work.

## Echo

A form fitted, and the standing paid for it.
The outlier has built the shape the work needed, so what reads as an amateur or unserious organisation is, in truth, the one form suited to the labour, the fit real and costly and charged in the standing it spends, lost the moment the grant is lost and the done thing taken up after all.
