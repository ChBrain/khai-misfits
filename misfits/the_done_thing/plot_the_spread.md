# Plot: The Spread

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Field](place_the_field.md), every organisation reasoning the same way and the form on every side.
The peers copying the recognised shape and being copied, the mandate requiring it and the professions expecting it.

## Action

[Converging](process_converging.md): [The Peer](persona_the_peer.md), holding [the peer's seat](position_peer.md), copies the recognised form under uncertainty and is copied in turn, org after org.

## Stage

[The Peer](persona_the_peer.md), on [Match the Field](plan_match_the_field.md), takes [the form](piece_the_form.md) because the credible others have it, drawn by [the example](piece_the_example.md), pressed by [the mandate](piece_the_mandate.md), and taught by [the credential](piece_the_credential.md).
The more that have the form the stranger the ones without it look, so the pressure to adopt climbs with each adoption and the field homogenises with no one enforcing it.

## Tension

Copying the credible field under uncertainty is the safe guess, and every safe guess adds to a sameness no one chose.
There is nothing foolish in the peers: matching the field is reasonable when no one knows what works. But each adoption makes the recognised form more the norm and the fit more deviant, so the plot closes with the shape spreading of its own momentum, the field converging through a thousand sound decisions, the mould building and no author to it.
