# Piece: The Cage

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md). The field-wide convergence whole, the single shape every organisation runs, the cage built by everyone's rational adoption and no one's design, the phenomenon at full strength.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Closed across [the field](place_the_field.md), where every organisation has come to run the recognised form.
Opened, if ever, at [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), where one organisation builds the fit and spends its standing to leave.

## Load Bearing

Without it the recognised form is a shape some adopt, and an organisation can still build the fit at ordinary cost.
With it the trap is complete and authorless: the whole field converged on one shape, each having adopted it for real reasons and none having chosen the sameness, so the recognised form is now the field's condition and the fitting shape the deviant one (DiMaggio and Powell, on the iron cage revisited, the homogenisation of the organisational field; Weber, on the iron cage of rationalised order). The Cage is load bearing because it is the convergence whole: a field locked into one form by the sum of every rational adoption, so the piece carries the phenomenon at full strength, a sameness no one authored and none enforces, holding each organisation to a shape selected for standing and pricing the fitting one out of reach.

## Apparent

An industry standard, the mature shape of a well-ordered field, the way things are properly done everywhere.
Seen at the surface, the Cage looks like a field that has found the right form, every organisation converged on best practice, and the truth that the sameness was authored by no one and tested against no result, that it locks each body to a shape chosen for recognition, that the fitting form is now unreachable for the standing it would cost, stays hidden behind how settled and proper a whole field of the same shape looks.
