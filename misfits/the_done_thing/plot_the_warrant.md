# Plot: The Warrant

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Grant](place_the_grant.md), an organisation reviewed and resources to be placed.
The Patron looking for the recognised form and weighing whether to fund what it can read.

## Action

[Backing](process_backing.md): [The Patron](persona_the_patron.md), holding [the patron's seat](position_patron.md), grants the funding, licence, or deal to the organisation that has the recognised form.

## Stage

[The Patron](persona_the_patron.md), on [Back the Recognised](plan_back_the_recognised.md), places resources on [the form](piece_the_form.md) as the warrant of a real organisation, and passes over [the fit](piece_the_fit.md) that carries no legible sign.
The recognised shape is rewarded and confirmed as the price of standing, so adopting it was the safe move and diverging the costly one.

## Tension

Backing the legible warrant is prudent, and backing it is what pays every organisation to adopt the recognised form.
There is nothing capricious: the patron must place its resources on a sign it can defend. But the recognised form is that sign and the fitting shape is not, so the plot closes with resources flowed to the recognised form, the fit starved, and the patron's prudence funding a convergence it never intended and will read, when it sees it, as the field simply being proper.
