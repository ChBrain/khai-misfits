# Persona: The Peer

## Taxonomy

[Peer](position_peer.md). The other organisations in the field, copied under uncertainty and copying in turn, whose convergence is the model each new adopter reads and whose sameness confers the mutual legitimacy of the recognised form.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Familiar. Numerous. Already shaped.
Is the field of like organisations, the competitors and counterparts who have the recognised form, the ones a new organisation looks to when it does not know what to build.
No conspiracy in it: the Peers each adopted the form for their own real reasons, and together they simply are what a proper organisation in the field looks like.

## Action

Models the recognised form for every organisation deciding what to become, and, being many and alike, makes that form the obvious answer under uncertainty.
Copies and is copied, each Peer a reason for the next to adopt the same shape.
Does what a field of organisations does: it presents the recognised form as what everyone does, so that adopting it is the safe move and diverging the risky one.

## Shadow

Not a cabal, a field of organisations each reasoning soundly, and that is the whole difficulty: when no one knows what will work, copying what the credible others do is rational, and rational copying of the field is exactly what converges it.
Because uncertainty makes the peers' shape the safest guess and each adoption makes the shape more the norm, the Peers model a form that spreads by being modelled, so the field homogenises with no one directing it, each organisation copying the others copying the others, the sameness emerging from a thousand sound decisions to do what is done and answering to no author.
It offers the recognised form as the obvious shape, and being the obvious shape is what makes the field converge on it.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "this is how everyone in our field is set up."
Says it truthfully, the form really is what everyone has, and cannot say why beyond that everyone has it, the shape its own reason for being the shape.
