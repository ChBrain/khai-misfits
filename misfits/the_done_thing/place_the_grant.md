# Place: The Grant

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md). Where the field's legitimacy is converted into resources, the funding round, the accreditation, the procurement, the review at which the recognised form is backed and standing pays.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place resources are placed on an organisation, a grant panel, an accreditation review, a due-diligence room, a hiring decision, where standing becomes funding.
The Patron reviewing the organisation for the recognised form and backing the one that has it, legitimacy turning, here, into the resources that are survival.

## Holds

The reward, and the moment legitimacy becomes money.
Here the recognised form meets the resources it earns, so the Grant holds the point where standing pays: an organisation read for the legible shape and a granter that must place its resources on what it can judge, so it is where adopting the form is rewarded and diverging is not, the legitimacy the field confers converted into the funding that makes the recognised shape the safe one to have.

## Offers

Resources placed on a warrant that can be defended.
Because the Grant must put its funding on something legible, it offers the Patron a defensible placement, the recognised form as the sign of a real organisation, so it hands out the resources that reward the proper shape, and hands them out to the form and not the fit, backing what can be read over what would have to be investigated, the standing paid and the sameness funded.

## Withheld

Any resource for the shape it cannot read.
The Grant can back the recognised form but cannot easily back the fitting one, because the odd shape carries no legible warrant and there is no time to investigate it, so it withholds its resources from the organisation built to its work, exacting instead the standing an organisation must have to be funded, and, all defensible placement and no time to look closer, it withholds the grant from the Outlier until the fit is dropped and the done thing taken up, the funding flowing to the form and away from the work.
