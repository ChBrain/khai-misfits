# Position: Patron

## Taxonomy

[The Done Thing](play_the_done_thing.md). The seat that converts the field's legitimacy into resources, reading the recognised form as the warrant of a real organisation because it must place its resources on what it can judge.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Resources to place and a need to place them on what it can read: the standing to grant the funding, licence, or deal and the accountability that demands a legible sign.
A sound basis: the recognised form is a real signal that an organisation is proper, so backing it is prudent.
No time to investigate the odd shape, and no legible warrant in it to back.

## Orders

Back the recognised.
Review the organisation, look for the form that reads as proper, and grant the funding, the licence, the recruit to the one that has it.
Place resources on the legible shape, because the legible shape is the sign the seat was given to read.

## Loses

The fitting organisation it would have preferred, exactly by having to back what it can read.
Because the seat must place resources on a legible warrant and the recognised form is that warrant, it backs the form, and by backing it makes the form pay, so every organisation is rewarded for adopting the recognised shape and the field converges, the seat's prudence funding a sameness it never intended and reads, when it sees it, as the field simply being proper.
It gains a placement it can defend on a legible sign, and loses the fit the sign was meant to stand for.

## Drives

Toward the recognised form backed and the fitting one passed,
resources placed on the legible warrant,
so the seat that funds the proper funds the recognised, and by funding it makes the recognised the price of standing.
