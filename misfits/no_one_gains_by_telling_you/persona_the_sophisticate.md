# Persona: The Sophisticate

## Taxonomy

[Sophisticate](position_sophisticate.md). The buyer who sees the add-on coming and dodges it, taking the same cheap base and avoiding the dear extra, cross-subsidized by the myopic and with no reason to end the arrangement.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Savvy. Prepared. Getting the best of the deal.
Is the buyer who knows the printer is cheap because the ink is dear, and so buys the cheap printer and refills it elsewhere, takes the free account and never overdraws, joins the gym and actually goes.
No cunning against anyone in it: only a buyer anticipating the add-on and using the close substitute, taking the low base honestly on offer and declining the extra.

## Action

Avoids the add-on, on [Avoid the Add-On](plan_avoid_the_add_on.md), taking [the Base](piece_the_base.md) and dodging [the Add-On](piece_the_add_on.md) at [the Bill](place_the_bill.md) through the substitute.
Is cross-subsidized through [Subsidizing](process_subsidizing.md), riding the cheap base that [the Tax](piece_the_tax.md) on the Myope pays for.
Does what a buyer who sees the add-on does: takes the discounted base every firm offers and slips the extra, and comes out ahead of an honest single price.

## Shadow

Not a party to the trap, a beneficiary of it, and that is the difficulty: the Sophisticate needs no honest firm and rewards none, because the cheap base is everywhere and the add-on is dodged wherever it is.
Because a buyer who avoids the add-on is subsidized by the buyers who do not, the Sophisticate profits from the very arrangement the Myope loses to, and so has no interest in ending it, and is exactly what a debiased Myope becomes: not a customer for honesty but one more rider on the discount.
It comes out ahead by dodging the extra, and coming out ahead by dodging the extra is why the truth-teller wins no one.

## Tell

Says "never buy the ink, get it third-party," passing on the trick with a shrug.
Does not see, or does not mind, that the discount it enjoys is funded by the buyers who do not know the trick, that its cleverness rides on their bias, and that being warned would only turn a Myope into another of itself, dodging the tax rather than rewarding the warning.
