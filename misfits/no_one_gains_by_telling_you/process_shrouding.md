# Process: Shrouding

## Taxonomy

[No One Gains by Telling You](play_no_one_gains_by_telling_you.md). Run by [the Firm](persona_the_firm.md) on [Shroud the Add-On](plan_shroud_the_add_on.md), at [the Shelf](place_the_shelf.md), in [the Headline](plot_the_headline.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A base priced to be posted and an add-on priced to fund it, the moment the sticker is set, before any buyer arrives to read it.

## Direction

The add-on held off the compared number, so the low base is the only figure a buyer weighs at the shelf.

## Lever

The lowest visible price wins the sale, so a firm that reveals the total loses custom to one that posts a low base. Shrouding is competition, not deception (Gabaix and Laibson).

## Echo

A market where every firm shrouds, because none can afford to be the one that does not.
