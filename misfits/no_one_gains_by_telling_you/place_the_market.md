# Place: The Market

## Taxonomy

[No One Gains by Telling You](play_no_one_gains_by_telling_you.md). Where firms compete and an unshrouding deviation meets its fate, hosting [Telling the Truth](plot_telling_the_truth.md) and [No One Moves](plot_no_one_moves.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Many firms, each posting a low base beside every other firm's low base. No firm posts the true total, and no shelf looks any worse for it.

## Holds

The equilibrium itself: a shroud held by every seller at once, and [the Unshrouding](piece_the_unshrouding.md) that one rival attempts and that fails to pay.

## Offers

The same choice to every firm, every period: shroud and keep the sale, or unshroud and lose it. The offer never changes, so the answer never does either.

## Withheld

Any reward for honesty. A warned buyer dodges the add-on rather than moving their custom to the firm that warned them, so the one place that could discipline the shroud pays nothing to the firm that lifts it.
