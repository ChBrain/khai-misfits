# Plot: The Free Ride

## Taxonomy

[No One Gains by Telling You](play_no_one_gains_by_telling_you.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Bill](place_the_bill.md), where a second kind of buyer arrives, one who saw the add-on coming and arranged, in advance, to decline it.

## Action

[Subsidizing](process_subsidizing.md): [The Sophisticate](persona_the_sophisticate.md), holding [the sophisticate's seat](position_sophisticate.md), tables [Avoid the Add-On](plan_avoid_the_add_on.md) and takes the same cheap [Base](piece_the_base.md), dodging [the Add-On](piece_the_add_on.md) at [the Bill](place_the_bill.md) through the close substitute it always sits beside: refilling the printer elsewhere, never overdrawing the account, attending the gym paid for.

## Stage

The Sophisticate buys exactly what the Myope bought, the same low base at the same shelf, and simply declines the extra where the Myope meets it, using the substitute the add-on can never fully suppress. Nothing about this move requires deception or even effort beyond ordinary foresight: the base was honestly cheap and the substitute honestly available, so the seat takes a real discount and owes no one an explanation for taking it. The discount the Sophisticate rides is not free; it is funded, quietly and without either party negotiating it, by the tax the Myope pays in full at the same bill.

## Tension

The cheap base is now serving two buyers at once, one subsidizing the other, and only the Myope has any reason to want the arrangement to end. The Sophisticate needs no honest seller and rewards none, because the discount is everywhere the base is posted and the substitute is everywhere the add-on appears. The beat closes on a free rider who is exactly what a warned Myope would become: not a mark corrected, only one more rider on a discount someone else is funding.
