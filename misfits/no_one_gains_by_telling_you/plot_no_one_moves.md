# Plot: No One Moves

## Taxonomy

[No One Gains by Telling You](play_no_one_gains_by_telling_you.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Market](place_the_market.md), the Rival's telling already spent and answered, the arrangement simply left to stand another period.

## Action

No firm defects to [Tell the Truth](plan_tell_the_truth.md); every seller holds [Shroud the Add-On](plan_shroud_the_add_on.md) through [Shrouding](process_shrouding.md), and [the Tax](piece_the_tax.md) keeps moving through [Subsidizing](process_subsidizing.md), from [the Myope](persona_the_myope.md) at [the Bill](place_the_bill.md) to [the Sophisticate](persona_the_sophisticate.md) riding [the Base](piece_the_base.md).

## Stage

The Market offers every firm the same choice every period: shroud and keep the sale, or unshroud and lose it, and having watched the Rival unshroud and win no one, no firm has cause to answer differently. The shroud holds not because any seller is defending it but because the reward for lifting it is, plainly, nothing. Week over week the Myope pays the add-on in full and the Sophisticate takes the discount it funds, and the transfer continues without either party negotiating it or anyone declaring it a policy.

## Tension

The equilibrium proves itself stable by simply persisting: no cheat to catch, since nothing printed anywhere is false, and no dupe to blame, since the Myope's choice was reasonable on everything it could feel. The Sophisticate stays content, cross-subsidized and needing no honest firm; the Myope stays taxed, merely present-biased and correctable by nothing the market is willing to pay for. Information, the one cure, sits cheap to tell and worth nothing to any firm that tells it, and the arc closes here, on a shroud that holds not by force or by fraud but because unshrouding it has already been shown, once, to lose.
