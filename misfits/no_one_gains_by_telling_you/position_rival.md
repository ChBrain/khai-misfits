# Position: Rival

## Taxonomy

[No One Gains by Telling You](play_no_one_gains_by_telling_you.md). The seat of the firm that would break the shroud by telling the truth, and finds the cure does not pay, buying the education of a market that answers by dodging the add-on rather than rewarding the teller.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The truth to tell and the standing to tell it: the real total the buyer is not shown, and the freedom to advertise it, warn the market, and compete on honesty.
A correct reading: the diagnosis is right, the buyer is genuinely being taxed, and honesty would genuinely serve them, so the seat holds a true thing worth telling.
No way to capture the value of the telling, because a warned buyer takes the lesson and the cheap base anywhere and dodges the add-on, so the seat can pay for the truth but cannot be paid for it.

## Orders

Tell the truth, and expect it to lose.
Advertise the real total, unshroud the add-on, warn the buyers that the base is cheap because the extra is dear, and compete on the honest number.
Watch the buyers thank you and buy the cheap base elsewhere and dodge the add-on, and learn that the warning made a sophisticate and not a customer, so the honest move gained you nothing.

## Loses

The cost of the education, and the whole of its reward.
Because a debiased buyer avoids the add-on rather than switching to the honest firm, the seat bears the expense of unshrouding and captures none of the custom it creates, so what it loses is the price of a cure that flows to everyone and returns to no one, least of all the one who paid for it.
It gives up real money to tell a true thing and gets a market that dodges the tax at every firm, honesty made the losing move by the plain arithmetic of who gains from being warned.

## Drives

Toward a cure that cannot be captured and so is never tried,
the truth cheap to tell and worth nothing to the teller,
so the seat that would clear the trap is the one the market punishes for it, and the shroud stays down not because anyone defends it but because no one, trying the seat's move, is paid to lift it.
