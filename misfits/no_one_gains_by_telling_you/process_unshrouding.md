# Process: Unshrouding

## Taxonomy

[No One Gains by Telling You](play_no_one_gains_by_telling_you.md). Run by [the Honest Rival](persona_the_honest_rival.md) on [Tell the Truth](plan_tell_the_truth.md), at [the Market](place_the_market.md), in [Telling the Truth](plot_telling_the_truth.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A firm willing to post the true total and say plainly what the base costs once the add-on is counted in.

## Direction

The shroud lifted, the total advertised, the buyer warned of the add-on before they choose rather than after.

## Lever

The curse of debiasing: a debiased buyer with a close substitute for the add-on avoids the add-on rather than rewarding the honest firm, so the education makes sophisticates, not customers, and the truth-teller bears the cost of it and captures none of the reward (Gabaix and Laibson).

## Echo

Honesty made the losing move. The shroud holds everywhere else, because no one gains by lifting it.
