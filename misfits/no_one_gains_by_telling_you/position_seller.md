# Position: Seller

## Taxonomy

[No One Gains by Telling You](play_no_one_gains_by_telling_you.md). The seat of the competing firm that posts a low base and shrouds the add-on, held to the shroud not by malice but by the plain fact that a firm which unshrouds loses the sale.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A price to post and a rival always ready to undercut it: the standing to set the base low where the buyer compares and the add-on high where they do not.
A clean case: the base is honestly cheap and the add-on honestly disclosed somewhere, so the seat competes on real numbers and tells no lie, only chooses which one the buyer weighs.
No freedom to post the honest total and keep the sale, because a competitor will always place a lower base where attention falls, so the seat cannot unshroud without losing the custom it lives on.

## Orders

Shroud the add-on.
Put the low base where the buyer looks, fund it with the add-on they meet later, and keep the total off the number they compare, because the lowest visible price is what wins the sale.
Do not unshroud, because a firm that posts the honest total loses to one that posts a low base, and the seat that loses the sale is no seat at all.

## Loses

Its ability to compete on honesty, and with it any way out of the shroud.
Because the seat can hold the sale only by posting the lowest visible price, it gives up the option of telling the buyer the true total, since the rival who does not will take the custom, so what it surrenders is not honesty by choice but honesty made unaffordable by competition.
It keeps the sale exactly by shrouding, and keeps it only so long as it shrouds, the one move that would free the buyer being the one that would end the seat.

## Drives

Toward a market where every seller shrouds because no seller can afford not to,
the low base posted and the add-on hidden at every firm at once,
so the seat that competes exactly as it must is the seat that holds the shroud down, not by choosing to, but by being unable to choose otherwise.
