# Persona: The Firm

## Taxonomy

[Seller](position_seller.md). The competing seller that posts a low base price and shrouds the high add-on, doing exactly what the market rewards, no swindler and no conspirator, only a firm that would lose the sale by doing otherwise.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Competitive. Sensible. Offering the best price in the window.
Is one seller among many, posting the low base the buyer compares on and making the margin back on the add-on the buyer does not, exactly as every rival does and exactly as custom rewards.
No villainy in it: only a firm competing on the number the buyer looks at, because a firm that put a higher number there would simply lose the sale to one that did not.

## Action

Shrouds the add-on, on [Shroud the Add-On](plan_shroud_the_add_on.md), setting [the Base](piece_the_base.md) low on [the Shelf](place_the_shelf.md) and keeping [the Add-On](piece_the_add_on.md) off the sticker through [Shrouding](process_shrouding.md).
Prices the base down, sometimes below its cost, and funds it with the shrouded add-on the buyer meets later, because the lowest visible price is what wins the sale.
Does what a firm in a competitive market does: puts the low number where the buyer is looking and makes the money back where the buyer is not.

## Shadow

Not a cheat suppressing the truth, a competitor doing the only thing that wins, and that is the difficulty: the shrouding is not a lie but the ordinary placement of a low price where attention falls, and any firm that stopped would lose.
Because the buyer chooses on the salient base and a rival will always post a low one, the Firm cannot unshroud without losing the sale, so it shrouds not out of malice but out of survival, and the exploitation it takes part in is authored by the market's reward, not by any decision to deceive.
It competes exactly as it must, and competing exactly as it must is the shroud.

## Tell

Advertises the low base in the biggest type and lists the add-on, if at all, where no one compares.
Cannot be caught lying, because nothing it prints is false: the base really is that cheap, the add-on really is disclosed somewhere, and the truth that the one is cheap because the other is dear is not hidden so much as never made the number the buyer weighs.
