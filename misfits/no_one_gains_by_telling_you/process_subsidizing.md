## Owner

## Initiated by

Two buyers taking the same cheap base: one who will meet the add-on in full, one who has already arranged to dodge it.

## Direction

The add-on payments of the myopic funding the low base that the sophisticated ride, without either party negotiating it or naming it a transfer.

## Lever

The base is priced low because the shrouded add-on, paid by the myopic, subsidizes it, so the sophisticated, who take the base and dodge the add-on, are cross-subsidized by the myopic, who do not (Gabaix and Laibson).

## Echo
