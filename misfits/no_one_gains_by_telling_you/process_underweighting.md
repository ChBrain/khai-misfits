# Process: Underweighting

## Taxonomy

[No One Gains by Telling You](play_no_one_gains_by_telling_you.md). Run by [the Myope](persona_the_myope.md) on [Buy the Base](plan_buy_the_base.md), at [the Shelf](place_the_shelf.md), in [the Choice](plot_the_choice.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A base in front of the buyer and an add-on somewhere ahead of them, the comparison made on whichever one can be felt now.

## Direction

The salient base weighed heavily, the deferred add-on weighed too little, so the choice lands on the cheap sticker before the later cost is felt at all.

## Lever

A real, documented present bias makes the far cost genuinely harder to feel than the near price (DellaVigna and Malmendier). Not stupidity: an ordinary disposition, the same one that discounts any cost not yet due.

## Echo

A choice sound on everything the buyer looked at, and short on everything they did not.
