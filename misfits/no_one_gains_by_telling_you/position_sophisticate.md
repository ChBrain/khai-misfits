# Position: Sophisticate

## Taxonomy

[No One Gains by Telling You](play_no_one_gains_by_telling_you.md). The seat of the buyer who anticipates the add-on and dodges it, riding the cheap base every firm offers and cross-subsidized by the myopic, with no reason to end the arrangement.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Foresight of the add-on and a substitute to dodge it with: the standing to take the low base on offer and decline the dear extra, buying the printer and refilling it elsewhere.
A clean case: the base is honestly cheap and the substitute honestly available, so the seat takes a real discount and declines a real extra, wronging no one directly.
No need of an honest seller, because the cheap base is everywhere and the add-on is dodgeable wherever it appears, so the seat rewards no truth-teller and requires none.

## Orders

Avoid the add-on.
Take the low base every firm posts, and dodge the dear extra with the substitute, refilling elsewhere, never overdrawing, using what you paid for.
Ride the discount, and do not trouble to end an arrangement that pays you, because the cheap base you enjoy is funded by the buyers who meet the add-on you slip.

## Loses

Nothing, and its coming out ahead is part of the trap.
Because the seat takes the cheap base and dodges the add-on, it gains rather than loses, and what it quietly costs is borne elsewhere: the discount it rides is funded by the Myope's tax, so the seat profits from the very structure the Myope loses to, and has every reason to leave it standing.
It keeps the whole of its advantage and gives up only the impulse to fix a market that is paying it, so its gain is one more weight holding the arrangement in place.

## Drives

Toward a beneficiary who is also the reason the cure fails,
riding the discount the myopic fund and needing no honest firm to do it,
so the seat that comes out ahead is exactly what a warned Myope becomes, a dodger of the tax and not a customer for the truth, and every debiasing only makes one more of it.
