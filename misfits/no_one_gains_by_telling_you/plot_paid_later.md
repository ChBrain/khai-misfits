# Plot: Paid Later

## Taxonomy

[No One Gains by Telling You](play_no_one_gains_by_telling_you.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Bill](place_the_bill.md), where the deferred part of the arrangement comes due: the ink cartridge, the overdraft line, the minibar charge, the months paid for and never used.

## Action

[The Myope](persona_the_myope.md), still on [Buy the Base](plan_buy_the_base.md), meets [the Add-On](piece_the_add_on.md) in full at [the Bill](place_the_bill.md) and pays [the Tax](piece_the_tax.md), more across the whole than an honest single price would have cost.

## Stage

The add-on lands exactly where it was always going to land, itemized now, presented after the choice at the shelf is long since made. Nothing about the reckoning is new information: the same figure that sat off the compared number at the shelf simply becomes the number in front of the Myope, and it is paid, because there is no way back to the shelf and the choice it would have changed. The base stays as cheap as it was advertised; the whole, only now, is dear.

## Tension

The tax is paid with no lie having been told anywhere in the arrangement: a low number where the buyer looked, a high one where they did not, and the gap between them collected in full. There is no clause to point to, no swindle to name, only a bill that itemizes what the shelf never had to. The beat closes with the transfer complete and nothing on either receipt that reads as anything but ordinary commerce.
