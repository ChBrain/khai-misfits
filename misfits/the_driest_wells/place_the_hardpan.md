# Place: The Hardpan

## Taxonomy

[The Driest Wells](play_the_driest_wells.md). The ground of deepest thirst and fewest wells, poor and hard and grim, where the need is greatest and the reward that would draw a well is least.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A parched ground, a heavy need, and few wells to answer it.
Shown as hardship: the thirst is plain and the provision thin, the sort of place anyone can see is short of care, its need written in every unmet want.

## Holds

Holds the deepest thirst on the least rewarding ground.
The Hardpan is where need and inhospitality coincide: the same poverty and hardness that make the thirst deep make the ground offer nothing a well can be kept with, no coin, no firm earth, no conditions to sustain a practice, so the place that most needs a well is the place least able to hold one.

## Offers

Offers the greatest need, and the fewest reasons for a well to come.
Here the thirst is heaviest, so the Hardpan offers the strongest case in the currency of need, and by offering nothing in the currency of reward it offers the trade no reason to sink there, the very depth of the thirst bound to the very hardness that repels the well.

## Withheld

Withholds that its emptiness is read as low demand.
What the Hardpan hides, from the comfortable distance of the well-watered, is that the wells absent from it are absent for want of reward and not for want of need, so its thin use of care reads as little wanted rather than much unmet, the parched ground counted undemanding precisely because its deep thirst could never reach a well to voice itself at.
