# Process: Baring

## Taxonomy

[The Driest Wells](play_the_driest_wells.md). The sharpening of the inversion as care is exposed to the market, the slope of reward steepening where provision is bought and sold and gentling where it is shielded.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Open Market](piece_the_open_market.md), across the whole field, wherever care is left to be priced rather than held to need.
Begins as provision is exposed to market forces, the pricing of care steepening the slope that draws it toward reward.

## Direction

Turns a mild inversion into a complete one.
Because the slope of reward is steepest where care is bought and sold, exposing provision to the market sharpens how completely it follows reward, so baring converts a tendency into a law, the inverse match running to completion where care is most exposed and only partway where it is shielded (Tudor Hart).

## Lever

The exposure of care to price.
Because a priced good flows to where it is paid and a shielded one can be held to need, baring works through how much of care is left to the market: the more it is priced, the more perfectly it follows reward, so the lever is the degree of exposure itself, the same care running the inversion to completion or holding over the thirst depending on how far it is bared to the slope.

## Echo

The law made complete where the market runs, and gentled where it does not.
The echo of baring is an inversion whose depth tracks the exposure, sharpest where care is a commodity and softest where it is held to need, so the market does not author the backward map but perfects it, and shelter from the market is what keeps a well over the thirst.
