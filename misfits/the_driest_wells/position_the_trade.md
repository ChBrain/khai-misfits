# Position: The Trade

## Taxonomy

[The Driest Wells](play_the_driest_wells.md). The supplying seat, which settles where the work can be sustained, so its provision gathers along the slope of reward, away from the need that offers no ground to keep a well.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The care to give, and the mobility to place it where it lasts.
The seat holds the provision, the well and the hands to work it, and the freedom to sink it where the ground is firm and the work is viable.
A viability test as its whole compass, since the seat can only keep a well where coin, conditions, and firm earth allow, so it reads every ground by whether a practice there would last.

## Orders

Sink where it can be kept.
Settle where the ground is good and the work is viable, since a well that cannot be maintained serves no one, and choose the place where the practice will hold.
Weigh reward, not need, not from indifference but because reward is what keeps a well alive, so the seat must answer the slope that sustains it rather than the thirst that cannot.

## Loses

The power to match need, which the seat gives up by the very test that keeps it alive.
The Trade can keep a well only where the ground rewards it, and the ground that rewards is the ground already watered, so following the condition of its own survival is following away from the deepest thirst, its viability and its misdirection the same choice. What the seat forfeits is any correspondence between where it places care and where care is needed, given up not by malice but by the plain requirement of lasting, since to sink where the thirst is deepest is to sink where the well will fail, so the seat that holds the care is structurally barred from placing it by need, able to answer only the slope that will sustain a well and never the thirst that will not, its every sound siting composing, across the whole, a provision laid backwards over the map of need.

## Drives

Toward care placed where it can last and away from where it is wanted,
and a viability that keeps each well by misdirecting all of them,
so the seat that holds the provision is the seat that cannot aim it at need, its survival test the exact reason the wells gather where the thirst is least.
