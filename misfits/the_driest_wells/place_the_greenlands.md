# Place: The Greenlands

## Taxonomy

[The Driest Wells](play_the_driest_wells.md). The well-watered ground of shallow thirst and firm earth, where coin and ease and good conditions gather the wells, so care clusters where it is least needed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Good ground, ready coin, and wells in plenty.
Shown as thriving: the earth is firm, the conditions are pleasant, the means are to hand, and the wells cluster here as sensibly as any provision gathers where it can be kept and rewarded.

## Holds

Holds the shallow thirst on the most rewarding ground.
The Greenlands are where reward and low need coincide: the same wealth and health that make the thirst shallow make the ground firm and the practice viable, so the place that least needs a well is the place best able to keep one, the wells gathering where they can last and be paid.

## Offers

Offers the trade every reason to sink, and the least need to answer.
Here the ground rewards the well and the work will hold, so the Greenlands offer the strongest case in the currency of reward, and by holding only a shallow thirst they offer the least need for the care that gathers there, the very ease that draws the well bound to the very sufficiency that scarcely requires it.

## Withheld

Withholds that its plenty is the hardpan's want.
What the Greenlands hide is that the wells clustered here are wells not sunk elsewhere, so the plenty of the well-watered is not a separate abundance but the same care that the hardpan lacks, drawn to the paying ground, the fullness here and the emptiness there two ends of one slope rather than two unrelated fortunes.
