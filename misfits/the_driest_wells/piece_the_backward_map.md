# Piece: The Backward Map

## Taxonomy

[The Driest Wells](play_the_driest_wells.md). The map of provision laid backwards over the map of need, the whole phenomenon of which every other piece is a part: care most available where it is least required.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Prints itself across [the Greenlands](place_the_greenlands.md) and [the Hardpan](place_the_hardpan.md), readable only from [the Census](place_the_census.md) where the two maps are laid together.
Flows as the finished inversion, the pattern of wells running opposite to the pattern of thirst, the shape of the whole trap.

## Load Bearing

Without the Backward Map there is no misfit, only scattered sound choices: it is the Map that ties the draining to the crowding to the baring, the one pattern in which each provider's viable siting and each advantaged reach and each exposure to price compose a provision run opposite to need.
The Backward Map is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole, the availability of good care varying inversely with the need for it, most complete where care is most exposed to the market (Tudor Hart). It carries the entire misfit: the wells gathered at reward are the Map drawn, the shared source drained by reach is the Map deepened, the slope steepened by price is the Map perfected, so the Map is not one more piece beside the others but the shape they all compose, the pattern whose every stroke is another of the play's pieces at work. It is drawn by sound acts, which is why it cannot simply be redrawn: each well is sunk where it can be kept and each draw is made by whoever can reach, so setting the Map right is not correcting an error but holding care to need against a slope that composes the inversion out of nothing but reasonable choices.

## Apparent

Just where the care is, the ordinary distribution of provision.
Seen at the surface, the Backward Map looks like the plain pattern of where care has settled, unremarkable as any map of a resource, and the truth that it runs opposite to need, that the fullest provision sits on the shallowest thirst, that the whole is an inversion no one drew, stays hidden behind how natural a distribution looks when each part of it was sensibly placed.

## Yearbook

Suffered by [the Parched](persona_the_parched.md) at its empty end, drawn by [the Welldigger](persona_the_welldigger.md) siting by siting, and set right, section by section, by [the Diviner](persona_the_diviner.md) alone.
It is the whole shape of the inversion: the Backward Map is what [draining](process_draining.md) prints and [sinking](process_sinking.md) redraws, the pattern [the gradient](piece_the_gradient.md) tilts and [the open market](piece_the_open_market.md) perfects, the provision laid backwards over the thirst that only a standing effort uphill can hold to need.
