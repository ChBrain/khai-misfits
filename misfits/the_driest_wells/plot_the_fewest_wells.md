# Plot: The Fewest Wells

## Taxonomy

[The Driest Wells](play_the_driest_wells.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Census](place_the_census.md), the map of care laid over the map of need.
Two maps set together, the pattern of wells and the pattern of thirst, and the plain question of whether they match.

## Action

[Draining](process_draining.md) revealed: the wells are found clustered where the thirst is shallow and thinned to nothing where it runs deep, the provision run opposite to the need.

## Stage

[The Parched](persona_the_parched.md) stand on [the Hardpan](place_the_hardpan.md) with the deepest thirst and the fewest wells, while [the gradient](piece_the_gradient.md) has gathered the provision onto the greenlands, so [the backward map](piece_the_backward_map.md) shows care most available exactly where it is least required.
Nothing here was aimed; the pattern is only the sum of where wells could be kept.

## Tension

The inversion is plain and no one drew it, so there is a wrong to see and no wrongdoer to name.
The map runs backwards, care pooled at the shallow thirst and absent from the deep, and yet every well in it was sunk where it could be sustained, so the pattern is perverse and each part of it sound. The plot closes with the inversion seen from the one vantage that shows it whole, the deepest need matched to the thinnest provision, a backward map composed of nothing but reasonable placements and answerable to no one who placed them.
