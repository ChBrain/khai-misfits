# Plan: Sink Where It Pays

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Trade](position_the_trade.md), inside [The Driest Wells](play_the_driest_wells.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Trade](position_the_trade.md)

## Direction

Sink the well where the ground is firm and the work is viable, since a well that cannot be maintained serves no one and a practice that destroys the provider soon ends (Tudor Hart). Weigh coin and conditions and the chance of lasting, not the depth of the thirst, because reward is what keeps a well alive and need is not, so settle on the slope that will sustain the work. Do not read the choice as a turning from need but as the plain requirement of practising at all, and accept that following viability follows away from the hardpan, so the same care that would answer the deepest thirst gathers instead where it can be kept, each sound siting composing a provision laid backwards over the map of need.

## Orders

([The Welldigger](persona_the_welldigger.md) sinks the well where the ground is good, weighing viability rather than thirst, and leaves the drylands undug not from refusal but because the place of deepest need is the place least able to keep a well.)

## Implementation

([The Greenlands](place_the_greenlands.md); through the [draining](process_draining.md) process, sharpened by [baring](process_baring.md); the Welldigger follows [the gradient](piece_the_gradient.md) to the paying ground, most completely where [the open market](piece_the_open_market.md) steepens the slope, sinking away from the thirst.)
