# Plan: Take What Reaches

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Thirst](position_the_thirst.md), inside [The Driest Wells](play_the_driest_wells.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Thirst](position_the_thirst.md)

## Direction

Make do with the care that reaches the hardpan, since the provision that answers reward does not come to the deepest thirst and the far well is the only well there is (Tudor Hart). Stretch the thin water across a need far larger than it, and do not expect to summon a well by needing one, because the seat's thirst, spoken in a currency the slope does not hear, makes no pull on the supply that would answer it. Accept that the under-use forced by distance and cost and thin knowledge will read, in the ledger of demand, as a low call, so the deepest need is counted as small precisely because it cannot reach the well that would meet it (the Black Report; Marmot).

## Orders

([The Parched](persona_the_parched.md) takes what little care reaches the hardpan, uses less than their thirst would warrant for want of a well within reach, and bears a need that goes unregistered because it is voiced in a currency the market does not price.)

## Implementation

([The Hardpan](place_the_hardpan.md); under the [draining](process_draining.md) that carries care away; the Parched receive the thin edge of [the gradient](piece_the_gradient.md), their need unread on [the backward map](piece_the_backward_map.md) because it makes no pull the slope can hear.)

## Targets

- [ ] the care within reach is taken and stretched across a larger need
- [ ] no well is expected to arrive in answer to need alone
- [ ] the under-use forced by distance and cost is borne, not chosen
- [ ] the reading of deep need as low demand is recognised as the mismatch it is
