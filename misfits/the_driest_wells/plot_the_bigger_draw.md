# Plot: The Bigger Draw

## Taxonomy

[The Driest Wells](play_the_driest_wells.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

A well held in common, drawn by the well-watered and the parched alike.
A single shared source open to all, and around it the long reach of the advantaged and the short reach of those in deepest need.

## Action

[Crowding](process_crowding.md): the ones with means and voice and knowledge draw the most from the common well, so the source meant for all is emptied first by the least thirsty.

## Stage

The well-watered work [the louder claim](piece_the_louder_claim.md) at the shared well, drawing a fuller share though their thirst is shallow, while [the Parched](persona_the_parched.md), on [Take What Reaches](plan_take_what_reaches.md), take less than their need would warrant for want of the reach to draw more.
No one is turned away, and the share still runs to the reach and away from the need.

## Tension

The shared well was meant to close the gap, and the reach that draws it reproduces the gap inside it.
Nothing here is barred: the source is open to all, and the advantaged only draw on what is theirs to draw. But means and voice and knowledge are a reach the parched do not have, so the common well is emptied first by those who need it least, and the plot closes with even care held for all running the backward way, the well-watered drawing a fuller share and the deepest thirst a thinner one, an inequality made not by any rule but by the plain difference in who can reach.
