# Piece: The Open Market

## Taxonomy

[The Driest Wells](play_the_driest_wells.md). The exposure of care to price, the force that steepens the slope of reward, so the inversion runs to completion where care is bought and sold and gentles where it is shielded.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across the whole field, steepest over [the Greenlands](place_the_greenlands.md) and [the Hardpan](place_the_hardpan.md) alike wherever care is priced, and held back only where provision is shielded from it.
Flows as the pricing of care, the degree to which provision is left to be paid for rather than held to need.

## Load Bearing

Without the Open Market the inversion is a tendency; with it, a law run to completion: it is exposure to price that sets how steeply care follows reward, so the same slope that is mild under shelter is sheer under the market.
The Open Market is load bearing because it is the dial on the inversion's depth, the finding that the law operates more completely the more care is exposed to market forces and less completely where that exposure is reduced (Tudor Hart). It does not author the backward map, since the slope of reward exists wherever provision must be sustained, but it perfects it, converting a gentle inclination into a sheer drop, so the piece carries the one lever that changes how far the wells depart from the thirst, the market the amplifier and shelter from it the only thing that holds a well over the need.

## Apparent

Just care priced like any good, allocated as anything is allocated.
Seen at the surface, the Open Market looks like ordinary efficiency, a resource going to where it is paid for, and the truth that a good priced flows to reward and a good priced is care flowing from need, that the same pricing which allocates bread perversely allocates care, that exposure is the dial that runs the inversion to completion, stays hidden behind how normal it is to let a resource be bought and sold.

## Yearbook

Steepened by every exposure of care to price, resisted by [the Diviner](persona_the_diviner.md) who shields the well, and felt hardest by [the Parched](persona_the_parched.md) on whom the sheer slope bears.
It is the amplifier of the backward map: the Open Market is what [baring](process_baring.md) works through, the exposure that steepens [the gradient](piece_the_gradient.md) and runs the inversion to completion, the dial that shelter from the market alone can turn back toward the thirst.
