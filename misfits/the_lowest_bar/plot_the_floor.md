# Plot: The Floor

## Taxonomy

[The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Floor](place_the_floor.md), and a party who bears the descent and cannot follow the base out.
The Bound setting out to have the jurisdictions agree a common minimum below which none may cut.

## Action

[Harmonising](process_harmonising.md): [The Bound](persona_the_bound.md), holding [the bound's seat](position_bound.md), calls for a floor and would bind the jurisdictions to a minimum the base cannot slip beneath.

## Stage

[The Bound](persona_the_bound.md), on [Hold the Floor](plan_hold_the_floor.md), would ground [the standard](piece_the_standard.md) in a common minimum against [the mobility](piece_the_mobility.md), lifting [the externality](piece_the_externality.md) off those who cannot move and halting [the descent](piece_the_descent.md) short of [the bottom](piece_the_bottom.md).
Against the floor stands each jurisdiction's standing power to win the base back by quietly cutting below the line.

## Tension

A common floor is the one move that stops the descent, and it asks each jurisdiction to forgo the base it could win by defecting.
It binds every jurisdiction to a minimum any of them could profit by breaking. So the plot closes one of two ways: the floor agreed and held and the base no longer drawable by going lower ... or a jurisdiction defects to draw the base back, the harmonisation unravels, and the descent resumes toward the lowest bar.
