# Position: Bound

## Taxonomy

[The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md). The seat that cannot move, where the standard was the protection and the descent removes it, and where the call for a common floor is made at the cost the competition will not pay.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A stake in the standard and no way to relocate: the protection the standard gave and no jurisdiction to move it to.
A real claim: the standard existed for the immobile party, so its lowering is a protection removed and not a cost the party chose.
No standing in the competition, and no floor unless every jurisdiction keeps one, because any of them can win the base back by cutting below it.

## Orders

Hold the floor.
Call for a common minimum below which no jurisdiction may cut, because you cannot follow the base out and a floor is the only thing that holds the bar the base moved to escape.
Ask for the harmonisation, since you alone bear the descent and cannot lower your own exposure to it.

## Loses

The protection the standard gave, taken by a descent it has no part in and cannot leave.
Because the seat cannot move, the standard the base carried away was its shield and not the base's cost, so the divergence of interest between the mobile chooser and the immobile protected is where the descent lands, and the floor the seat calls for asks every jurisdiction to forgo the base it could win by cutting below, against each one's standing incentive to defect, so the remedy the seat needs is costly and defection-prone and never free.
It bears the lowered bar it did not choose, and can hold the floor only if every jurisdiction pays the price of not undercutting.

## Drives

Toward the floor called for and the bar held,
against a competition that will not keep a minimum any jurisdiction can profit by breaking,
so the seat that holds the floor bears the descent and asks the one remedy the mobility of the base and the competition for it will not let the jurisdictions keep.
