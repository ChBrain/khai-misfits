# Process: Attracting

## Taxonomy

[The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md). The Jurisdiction lowering the standard to win the mobile base, the lawful pursuit of a franchise revenue that cuts the bar the base dislikes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A mobile base worth a franchise revenue and a standard the base would rather not meet.
At [the market of laws](place_the_market_of_laws.md), the jurisdiction lowering the bar to draw a base that would otherwise sit elsewhere.

## Direction

Toward the base won and the standard given away.
Because the base is mobile and its revenue real, attracting lowers the standard the base dislikes and reads the registrations that follow as competitiveness, so the bar is cut to win the base and the win is booked while the cost of the lowered standard falls off the ledger onto the immobile party the standard protected.
Each cut is a rational move toward a base that would otherwise register with a laxer rival.

## Lever

The franchise revenue the mobile base carries.
Because the base pays fees wherever it sits and can sit wherever it likes, attracting runs on a lawful revenue the base controls, so the lowering is assembled from no corruption, only from a jurisdiction pursuing a real revenue by the one instrument the mobile base leaves it, a lower bar.

## Echo

A base drawn, and a standard lowered under it.
The jurisdiction has won the base and its revenue, so what reads as competitiveness is, in truth, a protection removed from someone who cannot move, the cut booked as a gain and the cost carried off the ledger by the immobile third party the standard was for.
