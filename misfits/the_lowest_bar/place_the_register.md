# Place: The Register

## Taxonomy

[The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md). Where the base chooses its jurisdiction and sits, the book in which a charter is granted or a flag flown, where the choice of jurisdiction is made and the revenue follows it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the base picks where to sit, a company registry, a ship's flag, a chartering office, where the mobile base enters its jurisdiction and the fees begin.
The Mobile comparing the standards on offer and registering where the bar is lowest, the Jurisdiction receiving the base it lowered the standard to win.

## Holds

The choice of jurisdiction, and the standard the choice selects.
Here the base decides where to sit, so the Register holds the point where a choice of jurisdiction is a choice of standard: the base entering the book of the jurisdiction whose terms cost it least, so it is where the mobility of the base becomes the pressure on the standard, and where the franchise revenue attaches to whoever asked the base for the least.
The choice is lawful and the revenue real, and the immobile party the standard protected has no entry in the book.

## Offers

A base won and a revenue attached.
Because the Register is where the base picks its jurisdiction, it offers the jurisdiction that lowered furthest the base and its fees, the registration entered and the revenue begun, so it hands out the prize of the competition, and hands it to whoever asked the least of the base, the standard selected being the lowest on offer.
The gain is entered in the book, and the cost is entered nowhere.

## Withheld

Any place in the book for the party who cannot register.
The Register shows the base choosing its jurisdiction but cannot show the immobile third party the standard protected, because the party cannot move and has no charter to file, so it withholds the cost of the chosen standard from the book that records the choice, letting the registration read as a clean win, and, all base won and revenue attached, it withholds the shareholder and the crew and the public who keep the lowered bar and never entered the Register at all.
