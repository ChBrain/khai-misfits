# Persona: The Bound

## Taxonomy

[Bound](position_bound.md). The immobile third party the standard protected, who cannot follow the base out and keeps the lowered standard, and who alone would call for a common floor. The archetype who bears the descent and would hold the bar.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Exposed. Unable to move. Wanting the bar held.
Is the one the standard was for and who cannot leave, the shareholder holding the shares, the crew aboard the ship, the worker, the depositor, the public that lives in the country the base registered away from.
No standing in the competition: the Bound does not choose the jurisdiction and cannot carry itself elsewhere, so the descent is done over its head and lands on it.

## Action

Bears the lowered standard the base preferred, and, having nowhere to move, would have the jurisdictions agree a common floor below which none may cut.
Keeps the shares or the ship or the citizenship while the base and its revenue go where the standard is lowest, holding the cost of a choice it did not make.
Does what the immobile party can do: it calls for a floor, because it cannot follow the base and cannot lower its own exposure, and a floor is the only thing that would hold the bar the base moved to escape.

## Shadow

Not a party to the descent, the one it is done to, and that is the whole difficulty: the Bound's interest in the standard is exactly what the mobile base's choice overrides, and the floor it needs is the thing the competition will not keep.
Because the Bound cannot move, the standard the base carried away was its protection and not the base's cost to bear, so the divergence of interest between the mobile chooser and the immobile protected is where the descent bites, and the Bound's call for a floor asks every jurisdiction to forgo the base it could win by cutting below, against each one's standing incentive to defect, so the remedy the Bound needs is real and costly and never held for free.
It would hold the bar it cannot raise alone, and holding it asks the jurisdictions to keep a floor any of them can profit by breaking.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "the standard was for me, and I cannot follow it out the door."
Says it holding a lowered bar it never chose, and is heard, when heard at all, as asking the jurisdictions to give up a competitiveness they can feel for a floor whose worth shows only when it breaks.
