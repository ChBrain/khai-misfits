# Piece: The Mobility

## Taxonomy

[The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md). The base's freedom to relocate, the engine of the descent, the mobility that lets the base sit where the standard costs it least and so makes the standard a thing jurisdictions must compete over.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Exercised at [the register](place_the_register.md), where the base enters the jurisdiction of its choice.
Neutralised, if ever, at [the floor](place_the_floor.md), where a common minimum removes the gain from moving to a lower bar.

## Load Bearing

Without it the base is bound to the standard where it sits and the jurisdiction can set the bar without losing anything, and no descent runs.
With it the trap has its engine: the freedom of the base to register wherever the standard costs it least, which turns the standard from a rule a jurisdiction sets into a bid it must win, so each jurisdiction competes the bar down to hold a base that would otherwise move (Tiebout, on mobility and jurisdictional competition, the mobile party sorting to the jurisdiction whose terms suit it; Cary, on the interstate competition mobility drives). The Mobility is load bearing because it is what makes the competition possible: without a base that can move there is no race, so the piece carries the freedom that is no one's fault and everyone's pressure, the lawful capacity to relocate that ratchets the standard down.

## Apparent

An ordinary freedom, a base entitled to register where it likes, a market working as a market should.
Seen at the surface, the Mobility looks like plain efficiency, capital and charters flowing to their best use, and the truth that the same freedom forces each jurisdiction to lower the bar to hold the base, that the base's best terms are the immobile party's lost protection, that the engine of the descent is a legitimate liberty and not a wrong, stays hidden behind how right it seems that a base may sit where it chooses.

## Yearbook

Exercised by [the mobile](persona_the_mobile.md) through [choosing](process_choosing.md), the freedom that drives [attracting](process_attracting.md) and [undercutting](process_undercutting.md) alike.
It is the engine of the trap: the Mobility carries [the charter](piece_the_charter.md) to the lowest [standard](piece_the_standard.md), forces the competition that becomes [the descent](piece_the_descent.md), and is answered only by a [floor](place_the_floor.md) that removes the gain from moving, without which the standard falls to [the bottom](piece_the_bottom.md).
