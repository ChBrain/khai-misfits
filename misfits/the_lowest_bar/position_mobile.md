# Position: Mobile

## Taxonomy

[The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md). The seat that can move, where the freedom to sit where the standard costs least is the engine that makes each jurisdiction compete the standard down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A freedom to relocate and a revenue to carry: the right to register where it likes and the fees the base pays wherever it sits.
A legitimate choice: preferring the terms that cost it least is lawful and ordinary, so moving to a laxer standard is not wrongdoing.
No exposure to the cost of the standard it prefers, because that cost falls on the immobile third party and not on the base.

## Orders

Fly the flag.
Compare the standards on offer, register where the standard asks the least, and carry your revenue there, because the lower standard costs you less to meet.
Sit where the terms are best, since the choice of jurisdiction is a choice of standard and the best terms are the lowest bar.

## Loses

Nothing it holds, and that is the point: the seat gains by moving and the loss lands elsewhere.
Because the base will sit where the standard costs it least, every jurisdiction that wants it must lower the bar to hold it, so the seat's lawful choice is the pressure that ratchets the standard down, and the cost of the lower standard the seat prefers is borne by the immobile party who cannot move with it, whose interest in the standard diverges from the base's.
It gains the best terms on offer, and loses none of the cost, which the party that cannot follow it keeps.

## Drives

Toward the laxest terms and the revenue carried to them,
the base's ordinary freedom made into the engine of the descent,
so the seat that flies the flag sits where the standard costs least, and the standard it moved to escape becomes the loss of those who cannot move.
