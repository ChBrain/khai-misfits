# Process: Choosing

## Taxonomy

[The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md). The Mobile picking the jurisdiction whose standard costs it least, the lawful choice of terms that is the engine making each jurisdiction lower the bar.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A base free to move and a field of standards set at different bars.
At [the register](place_the_register.md), the base comparing the terms on offer and entering the jurisdiction that asks the least of it.

## Direction

Toward the laxest terms and the revenue carried to them.
Because the base can register where it likes and the lower standard costs it less, choosing sits the base where the bar is lowest, so the choice of jurisdiction is a choice of standard, and the base's lawful preference becomes the pressure that makes each jurisdiction lower the bar to hold it, the cost of the chosen standard falling on those who cannot move.
The choice is ordinary and legitimate, and it is what forces the competition on the standard.

## Lever

The base's freedom to sit where its terms are best.
Because the base is entitled to register where it likes and reasonable to prefer the terms that cost it least, choosing runs on a legitimate freedom and not on any wrong, so the descent is assembled from no fault of the base, only from a mobile party doing what a mobile party may, sitting where the standard asks the least.

## Echo

A jurisdiction chosen, and a standard selected by the choosing.
The base has sat where its terms are best, so what reads as an ordinary choice is, in truth, the engine of the descent, each jurisdiction lowering to be the one the base chooses, the cost of the terms the base prefers landing on the immobile party who cannot follow it.
