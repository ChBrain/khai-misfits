# Plot: The Flight

## Taxonomy

[The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Register](place_the_register.md), a base free to move and a jurisdiction that asks less of it than its current one.
The Mobile comparing the terms on offer and about to register where the standard costs it least.

## Action

[Choosing](process_choosing.md): [The Mobile](persona_the_mobile.md), holding [the mobile's seat](position_mobile.md), registers where the standard asks the least and carries its revenue there.

## Stage

[The Mobile](persona_the_mobile.md), on [Fly the Flag](plan_fly_the_flag.md), exercises [the mobility](piece_the_mobility.md) over [the standard](piece_the_standard.md), carrying [the charter](piece_the_charter.md) to the laxest terms on offer.
The choice is lawful and ordinary, and it is the pressure that makes each jurisdiction lower the bar to hold the base.

## Tension

Sitting where the terms are best is a base's ordinary freedom, and the ordinariness is what makes the descent no one's fault.
There is nothing wrong: the base may register where it likes. But the choice of jurisdiction is a choice of standard, so the plot closes with the base moved to the lowest bar, the jurisdiction it left short its revenue, and the cost of the terms the base preferred landing on the party who cannot follow it.
