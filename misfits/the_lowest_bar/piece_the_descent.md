# Piece: The Descent

## Taxonomy

[The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md). The downward ratchet as rivals match each cut, each step read as competitiveness, the standard falling notch by notch though no jurisdiction wants it low.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across [the market of laws](place_the_market_of_laws.md), where each cut answers the last.
Halted, if ever, at [the floor](place_the_floor.md), where a common minimum stops the ratchet short.

## Load Bearing

Without it the lowerings do not compound and a standard cut once stays where it is, and there is no race, only a choice.
With it the trap has its motion: each cut to win the base answered by a rival's cut to keep it and a further cut to stay ahead, so the standard ratchets down not by any jurisdiction's wish but by none being able to hold it high while the base can find it low elsewhere (Cary, on the race to the bottom, the descent no state can arrest alone). And each step reads as a win: the jurisdiction that cuts sees registrations and revenue rise, so the downward motion wears the face of competitiveness, the metric that would show the fall not the one anyone holds. The Descent is load bearing because it is the deceptive motion of the trap: a fall measured as a series of victories, so the piece carries the ratchet in which every step is locally a gain and the sum is a bar no one chose.

## Apparent

A healthy competition, a market of jurisdictions each getting leaner and more attractive.
Seen at the surface, the Descent looks like progress, each jurisdiction improving its offer and winning business for it, and the truth that the improving offer is a falling standard, that each competitive step is a protection removed, that the sum of the local wins is a floor everyone would rather was higher, stays hidden behind how much a race down looks, step by step, like a race to get ahead.

## Yearbook

Driven by [attracting](process_attracting.md) and [undercutting](process_undercutting.md) answering each other, powered by [the mobility](piece_the_mobility.md), halted only by [harmonising](process_harmonising.md).
