# Plot: The Lure

## Taxonomy

[The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Market of Laws](place_the_market_of_laws.md), a mobile base worth a franchise revenue and a standard the base would rather not meet.
The Jurisdiction weighing the base it could win by asking less of it.

## Action

[Attracting](process_attracting.md): [The Jurisdiction](persona_the_jurisdiction.md), holding [the jurisdiction's seat](position_jurisdiction.md), lowers the standard to draw the base and its revenue.

## Stage

[The Jurisdiction](persona_the_jurisdiction.md), on [Draw Them In](plan_draw_them_in.md), lowers [the standard](piece_the_standard.md) the base dislikes and wins [the charter](piece_the_charter.md) and its franchise revenue, the registrations coming and the numbers rising.
The cut reads as competitiveness, and the cost of the lowered standard falls off the ledger onto the immobile party the standard protected.

## Tension

Lowering the bar to win a mobile base is lawful and rational, and the rationality is what keeps the cost off the ledger.
There is nothing corrupt: the base is mobile and the revenue real. But a standard is a protection for someone, so the plot closes with the base drawn and the standard lowered under it, the jurisdiction right to cut and the harm resting on those who cannot follow the base out.
