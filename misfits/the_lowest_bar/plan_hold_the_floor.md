# Plan: Hold the Floor

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the bound's seat](position_bound.md), inside [The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Bound](position_bound.md)

## Direction

Have the jurisdictions agree a common minimum below which none may cut, so the base cannot be drawn by going lower and the immobile third party keeps the protection. Ground the standard in a shared floor rather than in the terms the base prefers, and treat each jurisdiction's power to defect as the standing threat it is.

## Orders

([The Bound](persona_the_bound.md) calls for a common floor and would bind the jurisdictions to it, at the cost of asking every one to forgo the base it could win by cutting below, against each one's standing incentive to defect.)

## Implementation

([The Floor](place_the_floor.md); the [harmonising](process_harmonising.md) process; a common minimum holds [the standard](piece_the_standard.md) against [the mobility](piece_the_mobility.md), lifting [the externality](piece_the_externality.md) off the immobile party and halting [the descent](piece_the_descent.md) short of [the bottom](piece_the_bottom.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] a common minimum below which no jurisdiction may cut is agreed across the field
- [ ] the base can no longer be drawn by offering a lower standard
- [ ] the immobile third party keeps the protection the descent had removed
- [ ] the floor is held only while every jurisdiction forgoes winning the base back by defecting
