# Persona: The Jurisdiction

## Taxonomy

[Jurisdiction](position_jurisdiction.md). The one who sets the standard and, wanting a mobile base and the franchise revenue it brings, lowers the standard to attract it, and reads the rising registrations as a win.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Competitive. Open for business. Pleased to have won the base.
Is the standard-setter, the state, the flag registry, the chartering office, the one who decides how much the base must meet and can decide to ask less.
No corruption in it: the Jurisdiction pursues a lawful revenue the base controls, and lowering the standard to attract that base is an ordinary and legal instrument of competition.

## Action

Lowers the standard the mobile base would rather not meet, watches the registrations and the fees come, and books the result as competitiveness.
Cuts what the base dislikes, disclosure it must file, capital it must hold, duties it must owe, because a base that can move will sit where the standard costs it least.
Does what a jurisdiction competing for a mobile base does: it lowers the bar to win the base, and reads the revenue that follows as proof the lowering was right.

## Shadow

Not a villain, a jurisdiction pursuing a real revenue by a lawful means, and that is the whole difficulty: the lowering is rational and the gain is genuine, and the cost is booked to no account it holds.
Because the base is mobile and the franchise revenue is real, the Jurisdiction lowers to attract it, and the harm of the lowered standard falls not on the Jurisdiction and not on the base, both of whom gain, but on the immobile third party the standard protected, who cannot follow the base out, so the win the numbers show is real and the loss they do not show is real too, borne by someone off the ledger.
It lowers in good faith to compete, and the good faith is exactly what keeps the externalised cost out of the figures it watches.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "we made ourselves competitive, and the base came."
Says it watching registrations and revenue rise, and cannot see that competitiveness here means a protection removed from someone who cannot move, the win measured on the base won and never on the standard given away.
