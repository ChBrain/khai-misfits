# Plan: Undercut

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the rival's seat](position_rival.md), inside [The Lowest Bar](play_the_lowest_bar.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Rival](position_rival.md)

## Direction

Lower your own standard to match or beat the laxer neighbour, and keep the base and its revenue. Answer the lowering with your own, because a standard no base will sit under protects no one, and holding the bar higher only loses the base to whoever cut first.

## Orders

([The Rival](persona_the_rival.md) lowers to match or undercut the neighbour, and its match forces the first jurisdiction to cut again to stay ahead, so the rival both answers the descent and deepens it, the bar a notch lower with no one having chosen it low.)

## Implementation

([The Market of Laws](place_the_market_of_laws.md); the [undercutting](process_undercutting.md) process; [the standard](piece_the_standard.md) is matched down to keep [the charter](piece_the_charter.md), each cut answering the last and turning [the descent](piece_the_descent.md) toward [the bottom](piece_the_bottom.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the laxer neighbour's standard is matched or undercut to keep the base
- [ ] the higher bar the rival had held is surrendered because it loses the base
- [ ] the match forces the first jurisdiction to cut again to stay ahead
- [ ] the standard ratchets a notch lower with no jurisdiction having chosen it low
