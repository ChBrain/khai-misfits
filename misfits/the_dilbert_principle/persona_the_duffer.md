# Persona: The Duffer

## Taxonomy

[Duffer](position_duffer.md). Overmatched by the job and honest about trying, so the output suffers and the manager must act.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Earnest. Willing. Out of its depth.
Turns up, means well, and cannot do the work the seat demands, not for want of effort but because the job overmatches it.
No idleness in it: only a person miscast, giving what it has to a role that asks for more than it holds.

## Action

Muddles honestly through work it cannot do well, and the output pays for it.
Tries, falls short, tries again, its best insufficient to the seat it fills.
Does what a willing but overmatched worker does: everything asked, and not enough of it well.

## Shadow

Not lazy, misplaced: the shortfall is no vice, only a job pitched past the hand that holds it, and the effort is real.
Because it cannot be left in the seat and cannot be cheaply removed, it becomes the problem the manager solves the soft way, lifted up and out to spare the work and the confrontation both, so its honest failure at the bottom is what earns it a place above.
It is not to blame, and its blamelessness is exactly why the kind exit, not the sack, is reached for.

## Tell

Says "I am doing my best," and is.
Finds its best rewarded not with help to fit or an honest word, but with a title further up, where the work is understood still less.
