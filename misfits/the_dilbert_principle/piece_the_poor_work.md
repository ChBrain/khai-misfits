# Piece: The Poor Work

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md). The output shortfall, the honest damage a misplaced worker does in a seat the job overmatches them in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Done at [the line](place_the_line.md), where the work tells and the shortfall is plain.
Left behind when the worker is lifted to [the heights](place_the_heights.md), the damage moved off the seat but the incapacity carried up.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing for the manager to answer, and no worker to move: the seat is filled and the output holds.
With it the trap has its trigger: a willing hand falls short, the output suffers, and the manager must do something, so the shortfall is the pressure the whole mechanism releases through the soft exit. The Poor Work is load bearing because it is the honest, blameless failure the trap turns on (Adams): it is real damage and no one's vice, so it demands action while making the harsh action feel cruel, and the piece carries the shortfall that must be answered and is answered by lifting rather than removing.

## Apparent

A performance problem, an underperformer to be coached or corrected in the ordinary way.
Seen alone, the poor work looks like a routine management matter, a gap to close with training or, failing that, a parting, and the truth that its very blamelessness will steer the manager away from the honest exit and toward the kind promotion, that this shortfall is the seed of an upside-down leadership, stays hidden behind how ordinary an underperforming worker appears.

## Yearbook

Produced by [the duffer](persona_the_duffer.md) through [failing](process_failing.md), the honest shortfall of a hand the seat overmatches.
It is the trigger of the trap: the Poor Work is what makes [the sack](piece_the_sack.md) loom and, being blameless, what makes [the kick upstairs](piece_the_kick_upstairs.md) the kinder road, so the damage that should have met [the hard word](piece_the_hard_word.md) instead sends the worker up.
