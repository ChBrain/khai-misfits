# Piece: The Sack

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md). The cost of removal, the dear, cruel, fraught dismissal the soft exit is taken to avoid.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Weighed at [the line](place_the_line.md), where the manager faces it and flinches, and would be carried through at [the appraisal](place_the_appraisal.md), where the honest exit is managed.
Set against [the kick upstairs](piece_the_kick_upstairs.md), the cheap alternative whose cheapness is measured entirely by the Sack's cost.

## Load Bearing

Without it removal is easy, the overmatched are simply let go, and the leadership never fills with them.
With it the whole trap has its asymmetry: because the honest exit is dear, slow, legally fraught, cruel to inflict and an admission of a bad hire, the cheap upward move becomes the rational choice, so the Sack's cost is the pressure that bends every manager toward the promotion. The Sack is load bearing because it is the price that makes the soft exit rational (labour economics; the schools practice): remove its cost and the trap unwinds, so the piece carries the dearness of honest removal that turns a mercy into a mechanism.

## Apparent

An unpleasant duty, the hard part of management everyone would rather not do.
Seen alone, the sack looks like mere unpleasantness, a distasteful task to be handled with care, and the truth that its cost is the fulcrum the whole inversion turns on, that because removal is dear the incapable are moved up instead of out, stays hidden behind how much like ordinary managerial discomfort the cost of a dismissal appears.

## Yearbook

Faced and avoided by [the boss](persona_the_boss.md), the dear removal that [the poor work](piece_the_poor_work.md) would otherwise call for.
It is the asymmetry of the trap: the Sack's cost is why [the kick upstairs](piece_the_kick_upstairs.md) is reached for and why [the hard word](piece_the_hard_word.md) is so hard to speak, the price the soft exit defers and [the reckoning](process_facing.md) alone agrees to pay.
