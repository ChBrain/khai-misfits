# Plan: Muddle Through

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the duffer's seat](position_duffer.md), inside [The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Duffer](position_duffer.md)

## Direction

Do the work as best you can. Turn up, try, and give what you hold, though the seat asks more than you have. You mean well and the job overmatches you, so muddle honestly through it, falling short and trying again, the willing hand a role was pitched past.

## Orders

([The Duffer](persona_the_duffer.md) muddles honestly through work it cannot do well, giving its best to a seat the job overmatches, and the output pays for it.)

## Implementation

([The Line](place_the_line.md); the [failing](process_failing.md) process; the honest effort falls short as [the poor work](piece_the_poor_work.md), the shortfall that makes the manager act.)

## Targets

- [ ] the work is attempted honestly and in good faith
- [ ] the seat overmatches the hand, and the output falls short
- [ ] the shortfall is no vice, only a role pitched too high
- [ ] the manager is left with a problem that must be solved somehow
