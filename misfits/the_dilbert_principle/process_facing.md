# Process: Facing

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md). The honest reckoning that keeps competence and rank together, the remedy bought against every cost the soft exit spared.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A leadership filling upside down, and someone unwilling to lift the next shortfall out of sight.
At [the appraisal](place_the_appraisal.md), the Reckoner ready to name the shortfall plainly and answer it honestly.

## Direction

Toward competence kept where it is read.
The Reckoner coaches the misplaced to a fit or manages them out and rebuilds the rise to reward the work, so facing drives the honest word in where the soft exit would have lifted the problem away, holding the overmatched off the ranks that lead.

## Lever

The plain truth that a kindness which fills the leadership with the overmatched is no kindness.
Because lifting the incapable only defers the reckoning and sends the bill upstairs, the case to face it is sound, so facing runs on the same care that reached for the soft exit, turned now to the harder mercy of the honest word.

## Echo

Competence held in place, at the price the promotion escaped.
The shortfall has been named and answered and the ranks kept straight, and it has cost the confrontation, the grievance, and the admitted mistake, and been fought against a leadership that would not back it, so what reads as plain good management is, in truth, the dear reckoning the whole trap was built to avoid.
