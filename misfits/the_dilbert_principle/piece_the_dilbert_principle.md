# Piece: The Dilbert Principle

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md). The phenomenon whole, a kind exit for each that fills the leadership with those the work defeated.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Everywhere the production runs: from [the line](place_the_line.md) up into [the heights](place_the_heights.md), and against [the appraisal](place_the_appraisal.md) where it is resisted.
It is the whole arc, the shortfall answered by a lift, the lifts accreting into a leadership, the leadership renewing itself upside down.

## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without a name: a shortfall, a costly sack, a soft promotion, an inversion, and no account of how one becomes the next.
With it the misfit is whole: the Dilbert Principle is the trap entire, a mercy to each overmatched worker and a relief to each manager summing to a leadership least able where it leads, the kindness and the inversion shown to be one thing. It is load bearing because it is the structure the whole production exists to stage (Adams): the frame that binds the blameless failure, the dear sack, the cheap kick upstairs, and the locked inversion into a single bend, so the piece carries the phenomenon as a whole, competence sorted out of power one humane promotion at a time.

## Apparent

A cynical joke about bad bosses, a comic-strip law not to be taken seriously.
Seen at the surface, the Dilbert Principle looks like mere satire, a laugh at incompetent management, and the truth that it names a real structural sorting, that where removal is costlier than promotion the incapable are moved up and leadership fills with them, that no villainy is required and no exhortation removes the asymmetry driving it, stays hidden behind how much like a joke a true thing said lightly can seem.

## Yearbook

The whole of it: the Dilbert Principle is [the poor work](piece_the_poor_work.md) answered not by [the sack](piece_the_sack.md) but by [the kick upstairs](piece_the_kick_upstairs.md), accreting into [the inversion](piece_the_inversion.md), held off only by [the hard word](piece_the_hard_word.md) that pays what the promotion escaped.
It is the misfit named: a leadership filled with the overmatched by a hundred kind exits, no villain in it and no free hero, competence quietly sorted out of the seats that most need it.
