## Holds

Here the truth the Line read could be acted on rather than lifted away, so the Appraisal holds the point where the remedy meets its price: naming the shortfall plainly is the one move that keeps competence in place, and it costs the confrontation and the grievance and the admitted mistake, so it is where the cure is bought against everything the kick upstairs escaped.
