# Persona: The Reckoner

## Taxonomy

[Reckoner](position_reckoner.md). Would face the overmatched honestly and keep competence in the seats that lead, against every cost the soft exit spared.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Straight. Unflinching. Willing to be the one who says it.
Sees the ranks filling upside down and moves to have the hard conversation the soft exit was invented to avoid.
No cruelty in it: only someone who holds that a kindness which fills the leadership with the overmatched is no kindness at all.

## Action

Has it out: coaches the misplaced toward a fit, or manages them honestly out, and builds a rise that rewards the work.
Names the shortfall plainly instead of lifting it out of sight, and pays the confrontation the promotion dodged.
Sets the standard that keeps competence and rank together, at the price the whole trap exists to escape.

## Shadow

Not a saviour: the reckoning is the dear and painful thing, and it must be pressed against a leadership that is the trap's own product.
Because the honest word costs the grievance, the severance, the wounded team, and the admitted mistake the soft exit spared, and because the Brass will not vote for the standard that would have stopped its own climb, the Reckoner cannot set the ranks straight without spending every cost the promotion was reached for to avoid.
It can keep competence in power, only by being the cruelty the kindness was meant to spare, and only against those the kindness raised.

## Tell

Says "moving them up is not mercy, it is postponing the reckoning and sending the bill upstairs."
Is right, and being right means being the one who has the conversation no one thanks them for.
