# Position: Brass

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md). The seat of the accreted leadership, where the inversion locks and renews itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A stratum filled tier by tier with the overmatched the soft exit lifted, and the power to set course and judge who rises.
Both roles at once: the destination the managers send the weakest to, and the authority that decides the next promotion.
No memory of mastering the work, and no felt lack of it.

## Orders

Promote in kind.
Receive the lifted and pass the soft exit along, set direction and allocate the able, and raise those who look as you look.
Keep the ranks as they were filled, and judge leadership by your own reflection.

## Loses

The competence it governs, exactly by governing in its own image.
Because it never mastered the work, the seat cannot reliably know mastery in others or the want of it in itself, so it cannot hold the standard that would keep it out, and being both the destination of the overmatched and the arbiter of who rises, it locks the inversion it is made of.
It leads the work least where it judges it most, and renews that gap each cycle by its own hand.

## Drives

Toward a leadership self-perpetuating,
assembled from kind exits and blind to the work it governs,
so the ranks accreted upside down are the ranks that keep themselves so.
