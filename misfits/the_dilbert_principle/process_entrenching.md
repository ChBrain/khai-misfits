# Process: Entrenching

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md). The locking of the inversion as an accreted leadership promotes in its own image, no cabal in it and self-perpetuating.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A stratum filled, tier by tier, with the overmatched the soft exit lifted.
At [the heights](place_the_heights.md), the Brass now setting course and deciding who rises, judging leadership by its own likeness.

## Direction

Toward an inversion that renews itself.
The accreted leadership both receives the overmatched and controls the next promotion, so entrenching drives the ranks to reproduce in their own image, competence and authority parting further with every cycle as the seats that lead fill again with those the work defeated.

## Lever

The blindness of a leadership that never mastered the work.
Because those the work overmatched cannot reliably know mastery in others or feel its lack in themselves, they promote sincerely toward their own reflection, so entrenching runs on honest misjudgement, the inversion deepened by leaders doing their level best to pick good leaders.

## Echo

A leadership that keeps itself as it was filled.
The ranks have set their own course and raised more in their likeness, and the standard that would have kept them out is the one they never think to raise, so what reads as a settled, self-assured leadership is, unseen, an inversion made permanent by its own hand.
