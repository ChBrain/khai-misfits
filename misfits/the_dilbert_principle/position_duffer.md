# Position: Duffer

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md). The seat overmatched by its job, blameless for one worker and, lifted, the seed of the inversion.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A job that overmatches it, an honest willingness to try, and an output that falls short.
No vice to answer for, only a role pitched past the hand that holds it.
No say in what becomes of it, only the effort it can give and the shortfall that follows.

## Orders

Muddle through.
Do the work as best you can, turn up and try and give what you hold, though the seat asks more than you have.
Be the willing hand the job overmatches.

## Loses

The chance to fit, exactly because it cannot be cheaply removed.
Because dismissal is dear and the soft exit is cheap, the seat is not helped to a role it could fill nor told the plain truth, but lifted up and out to spare the work and the confrontation, so it cannot earn an honest place by failing honestly: its shortfall buys it a promotion, not a fit.
It means well and does poorly and is moved somewhere it will do poorly still, further from the work it might have matched.

## Drives

Toward a willing hand miscast,
blameless in itself and the seed of the sum,
so the honest failure that should have found it help instead sends it up.
