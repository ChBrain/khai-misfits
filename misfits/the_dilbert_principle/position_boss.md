# Position: Boss

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md). The seat that must move the overmatched worker, humane for one team and inverting for all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An overmatched worker damaging the output, and the duty to do something about it.
Two ways out: the sack, which is dear and cruel and fraught, and the promotion, which is cheap and kind and clean.
No cost-free option, only the choice of which cost to bear.

## Orders

Kick it upstairs.
Spare the dismissal and its grievance and its wound, and move the worker up and to the side, off the critical path.
Solve the problem the humane and practical way, and keep the floor and the person both whole.

## Loses

Nothing of its own, and the shape of the ranks it cannot see.
Because the upward exit is genuinely the cheaper and kinder move, the seat is right to take it, and cannot weigh its one humane promotion against the sum of all such promotions, so it does right by this worker and adds, unseeing, to a leadership filling with the overmatched.
It spares a cruelty and clears its floor, and helps staff the seats above with those the work defeated.

## Drives

Toward a kind exit taken,
right for one manager and upside-down for all,
so the mercy each boss owes its worker fills the ranks that lead with the ones the work beat.
