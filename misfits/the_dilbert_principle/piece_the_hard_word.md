# Piece: The Hard Word

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md). The remedy, the honest reckoning that keeps competence in power, at the cost the promotion was reached for to escape.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Spoken at [the appraisal](place_the_appraisal.md), the room the honest reckoning belongs to.
Set against [the kick upstairs](piece_the_kick_upstairs.md), the soft exit it refuses, and shadowed by [the sack](piece_the_sack.md), the cost it agrees to pay.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play has no answer, and the inversion runs unopposed to a leadership that cannot lead.
With it the misfit has its costly cure: the honest word, naming the shortfall and coaching to a fit or managing out and rebuilding the rise to reward the work, is the one move that keeps competence and rank together, and it works only by paying what the soft exit spared. The Hard Word is load bearing because it is the remedy that carries its own price (practitioner): it keeps the overmatched off the ranks solely by inflicting the confrontation, the grievance, and the admitted mistake, so the piece carries both the cure for the inversion and the cruelty the cure demands.

## Apparent

Just good management, the plain honesty any decent boss should manage without fuss.
Seen hopefully, the hard word looks like a simple duty, tell the truth and act on it, and the truth that it asks precisely the cost the whole trap was built to avoid, and must be pressed against a leadership that will not back the standard that would have kept it out, stays hidden behind how easy plain honesty sounds when it is not the one who must speak it.

## Yearbook

Spoken by [the reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md) through [facing](process_facing.md), the honest reckoning offered in place of [the kick upstairs](piece_the_kick_upstairs.md).
It is the cure the play cannot make painless: the Hard Word answers [the poor work](piece_the_poor_work.md) honestly and holds off [the inversion](piece_the_inversion.md), and cannot do either without paying [the sack](piece_the_sack.md) and defying the ranks the trap has already filled.
