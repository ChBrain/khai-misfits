# Persona: The Brass

## Taxonomy

[Brass](position_brass.md). The leadership stratum built one soft promotion at a time, which then promotes in its own image and locks the inversion.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Assembled, not chosen. Senior, and least at home in the work it governs.
Is the ranks above, filled tier by tier with those the work overmatched and the soft exit lifted, now setting course and judging the able below.
No cabal in it: only a stratum accreted from a hundred kind promotions, doing what such a stratum does.

## Action

Sets direction, allocates the able, and decides who rises, promoting in its own image and shielding its own kind.
Receives each overmatched worker the managers send up, and passes the same soft exit along in turn.
Governs the work from above, understanding it least where it is judged most.

## Shadow

Not malicious, self-perpetuating: a leadership that never mastered the work cannot easily know mastery in others or feel its lack in itself, so it renews itself in its own likeness without meaning harm.
Because it both receives the overmatched and controls who rises, the Brass is where the inversion locks: it will not set the standard that would have kept it out, and it lifts more like itself, so competence and authority part further with every cycle.
It is the sum of the kind exits given a will of its own, and the will is to keep the ranks as they were filled.

## Tell

Says "we know leadership when we see it," and sees it in its own reflection.
Promotes accordingly, and the standard that would have unseated it is the one thing it never thinks to raise.
