# Position: Reckoner

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md). The seat that would face the overmatched honestly, against every cost the soft exit spared.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A clear sight of the ranks filling upside down, and the will to set them right.
The honest tools: the hard conversation, the coaching to a fit, the managed exit, the rise rebuilt to reward the work.
No cheap way to wield them, and a leadership above that will not back the standard they require.

## Orders

Have it out.
Name the shortfall plainly, coach the misplaced to a fit or manage them honestly out, and build a way up that rewards the work and not escape from it.
Keep competence and rank together, whatever it costs to do.

## Loses

The ease and the mercy the soft exit bought, and the backing it needs from above.
Because the honest reckoning is exactly the confrontation, the grievance, the wounded team, and the admitted mistake the promotion was taken to escape, and because the Brass will not vote for the standard that would have stopped its own climb, the seat cannot set the ranks straight without spending every cost the trap was built to avoid, and against those the trap raised.
It can keep competence in power only by being the cruelty the kindness spared.

## Drives

Toward an inversion held off,
bought against every cost the promotion escaped and every rank it raised,
so the one seat that would keep competence in power must pay, alone, what all the kind exits deferred.
