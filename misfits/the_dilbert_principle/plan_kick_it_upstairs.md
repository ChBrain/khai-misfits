# Plan: Kick It Upstairs

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the boss's seat](position_boss.md), inside [The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Boss](position_boss.md)

## Direction

Spare the sack and move the worker up. Dismissal is dear and cruel and fraught, so take the cheaper, kinder way: a title and a desk off the critical path, where the overmatched hand can do less harm to the work. Keep the person's dignity, clear the floor, and avoid the confrontation.

## Orders

([The Boss](persona_the_boss.md) moves the overmatched worker up and to the side, choosing the humane, cheap promotion over the dear, cruel dismissal.)

## Implementation

([The Line](place_the_line.md) toward [the heights](place_the_heights.md); the [elevating](process_elevating.md) process; rather than pay [the sack](piece_the_sack.md), the boss reaches for [the kick upstairs](piece_the_kick_upstairs.md), lifting the harm off the work and onto the ranks.)

## Targets

- [ ] the costly, cruel dismissal is weighed and set aside
- [ ] the worker is moved up and to the side, off the critical path
- [ ] the floor is cleared and the confrontation spared, the person's dignity kept
- [ ] the harm is lifted off the work and, unseen, added to the ranks that lead
