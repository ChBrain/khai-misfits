# Plot: The Inversion

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Heights](place_the_heights.md), up is where every manager sends the overmatched.
The soft exits from every Line converging, the ranks above filling with those the work defeated.

## Action

[Entrenching](process_entrenching.md): [The Brass](persona_the_brass.md), holding [the brass's seat](position_brass.md), receives the Duffer among its own, while [The Duffer](persona_the_duffer.md), holding [the duffer's seat](position_duffer.md), takes its place in a stratum built the same way.

## Stage

[The Brass](persona_the_brass.md), on [Promote in Kind](plan_promote_in_kind.md), takes in each worker the managers lift and finds it fits, being made of the same, so the seats that lead accrete the overmatched tier over tier.
[The Duffer](persona_the_duffer.md), still on [Muddle Through](plan_muddle_through.md), governs now from a height where the work is a rumour, understanding it no better for the title.

## Tension

Every lift was a mercy, and the mercies sum to a leadership least able where it leads.
There is still no villain: each promotion was kind and each manager right, and the Brass receives in good faith what the Line sends up. But the destination of all the soft exits is the same, so the plot closes with competence run downhill and authority uphill, the seats that direct and judge filled with those the work overmatched, the two parted exactly where the stakes are highest.
