# Plot: The Kick

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Line](place_the_line.md), the sack dear and the promotion cheap.
The Boss at the fork, the cruel costly dismissal on one side and the kind clean lift on the other.

## Action

[Elevating](process_elevating.md): [The Boss](persona_the_boss.md), holding [the boss's seat](position_boss.md), moves the overmatched worker up and to the side, while [The Duffer](persona_the_duffer.md), holding [the duffer's seat](position_duffer.md), is lifted off the critical path into a title above.

## Stage

[The Boss](persona_the_boss.md), on [Kick It Upstairs](plan_kick_it_upstairs.md), sets the dismissal aside and promotes the worker sideways-and-up, sparing the grievance and the scene and the admitted mistake.
[The Duffer](persona_the_duffer.md), still on [Muddle Through](plan_muddle_through.md), is moved to a seat off the work it could not do, its dignity kept and the floor behind it cleared.

## Tension

The kindest, cheapest move for this worker is one rung of an upside-down ladder.
There is nothing to fault: the lift spares a real cruelty and a real cost, and the manager does right by the person in front of it. But up is where the overmatched are sent, so the plot closes with the floor cleaner and no one sacked, and the worker the work defeated deposited, unremoved, a step nearer the seats that lead.
