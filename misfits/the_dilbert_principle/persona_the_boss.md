# Persona: The Boss

## Taxonomy

[Boss](position_boss.md). Faces the overmatched worker and, the sack being dear, lifts them up and out, humanely and cheaply.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Decent. Practical. Reluctant to be cruel.
Manages the seat the Duffer cannot fill, sees the output suffer, and must do something that does not wound.
No harshness in it: only someone in a hard spot, weighing a painful sack against a kinder way out.

## Action

Moves the overmatched worker up and to the side, into a role off the critical path.
Chooses the promotion over the dismissal, sparing the grievance, the severance, the scene, and the admission of a bad hire.
Solves the problem in front of it the humane and practical way, and clears the floor of its weakest hand.

## Shadow

Not scheming, sparing: the lift is genuinely kinder and cheaper than the sack, and each is a real cost the manager rightly avoids.
Because up is the cheap exit and every manager takes it, the Boss's humane solution is one contribution to a leadership filling with the overmatched, and it cannot see its own promotion as part of that sum, only as this person, this seat, this mercy.
It does right by the worker in front of it and, with every peer doing the same, staffs the ranks above with those the work defeated.

## Tell

Says "I could not just let them go, so I found them something better."
Means the kindness, and the something better is a seat higher up, where the work is understood less and the harm is only moved.
