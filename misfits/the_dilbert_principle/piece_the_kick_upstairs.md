# Piece: The Kick Upstairs

## Taxonomy

[The Dilbert Principle](play_the_dilbert_principle.md). The soft exit, the cheap upward promotion that lifts the damage off the work and onto the ranks.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Taken at [the line](place_the_line.md), the manager's chosen way out, and landing in [the heights](place_the_heights.md), where the lifted worker joins the ranks.
Set against [the appraisal](place_the_appraisal.md), the honest room it is the avoidance of.

## Load Bearing

Without it the overmatched stay where the work reads them, or are honestly removed, and competence keeps its place.
With it the trap has its motion: the kick upstairs is the specific move that answers the shortfall by relocation rather than removal, lifting the incapacity out of the productive seat and depositing it, unremoved, in the seats that lead. The Kick Upstairs is load bearing because it is the mechanism that converts a hard case into a promotion (HR practice): it is the cheap, kind exit that clears the floor by filling the ranks, so the piece carries the upward move that turns each mercy into a rung of the inversion.

## Apparent

A promotion, a step up, even a reward, read by everyone as advancement.
Seen from outside, the kick upstairs looks like ordinary success, a worker rising, and the truth that it is an exit dressed as an ascent, a way to move the overmatched off the work while sparing everyone the sack, that the ascent is the damage relocating upward, stays hidden behind how exactly like a real promotion the soft exit is made to look.

Reached for by [the boss](persona_the_boss.md) through [elevating](process_elevating.md), the cheap upward move chosen over [the sack](piece_the_sack.md).
It is the engine of the trap: the Kick Upstairs answers [the poor work](piece_the_poor_work.md) by lifting it, feeds each lifted worker into [the inversion](piece_the_inversion.md), and is the exact move [the hard word](piece_the_hard_word.md) refuses in favour of an honest reckoning.
