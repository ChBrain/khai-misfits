# Position: Warden

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The seat of the keeper of the licence, where each stop is checked for its justification, the crisis waved through and the ordinary hour turned away.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A standard for leave and the standing to enforce it: the authority to grant a stop against a justification and to refuse one without.
A clear case: the standard is honest, output is real, and a life cannot be all rest, so asking of any stillness what earned it is not unreasonable.
No means to license the rest that justifies nothing, only the standard, so the seat can pass a disaster and never a bath.

## Orders

Refuse the leave.
Check each stop for its justification, admit the unarguable claim of the emergency, and turn the ordinary hour away.
Read rest that is its own end as a confession of not-enough, and, where it is taken regardless, spoil it with the sense that it is waste.

## Loses

Any leave to rest for its own sake, and with it the capacity to let the self be restored.
Because the seat can grant a stop only against a justification, it gives up the power to permit the bath, the book, the unhurried hour, keeping only the leave a crisis compels, so it loses the ability to license the very rest the person most needs.
It surrenders the restorative hour to keep the standard, guarding the gate against exactly what would refill the self.

## Drives

Toward the licence spent on disasters and never on the self,
the standard held so honestly it starves,
so the seat that keeps the gate refuses the one rest that would have restored.
