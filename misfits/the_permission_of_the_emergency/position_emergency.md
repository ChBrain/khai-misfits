# Position: Emergency

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The seat of the crisis that licenses, filled by whatever breaks, where an unarguable claim grants the leave to drop everything and be still.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A complete and unarguable justification: the standing to demand everything now and the leave to stop everything else that comes attached to it.
A clear case: the crisis is real and the cost of ignoring it is real, so dropping everything to attend is honestly right.
No need to be restful to be granted the hours, only to be unarguable, because the justification is enough and the stillness it forces is beside the point.

## Orders

Grant the leave.
Break in, press the claim, and demand that everything else be set down, so the hours to attend appear at once.
License the idle waiting along with the acting, handing the person a stop no one, least of all themselves, will question.

## Loses

Nothing, and that is the point: the seat need not rest the person to be granted the hours, only to be unarguable.
Because it carries a justification the bath cannot, the Emergency wins the leave the ordinary hour is refused, whatever the stillness it forces is worth as rest, so it takes the hours on the strength of its claim alone and the seat that rests least is granted first.
It enters no contest of worth as rest, and by never entering one it takes the leave from the rest that would have restored.

## Drives

Toward the stop fully licensed and the hours found at once,
granted on a claim and not on any rest it gives,
so the seat that rests the person least is the one that reliably lets them stop.
