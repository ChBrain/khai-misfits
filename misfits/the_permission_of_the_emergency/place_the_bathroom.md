# Place: The Bathroom

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). Where the ordinary hour would be spent, if any hour ever were: the site of [The Refusal](plot_the_refusal.md) and [The Fence](plot_the_fence.md), the rest that carries no licence and so is entered almost never.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The tub run or unrun, the book unread on its edge, the afternoon it presents as still and empty and entirely available.
Nothing here demands attention: no clock sits on the rim, no claim waits behind the door, only the quiet the Held would step into, if stepping into it needed nothing but the wanting.

## Holds

The bath, the book, the warm water going cold.
Here the wish to rest meets the room built to hold it, so the Bathroom holds the one hour in the day that asks for nothing and would return the most: a door that closes, water already run, a chapter waiting at the mark, none of it withheld by the room itself.

## Offers

Rest, freely, in principle.
Because the Bathroom asks nothing of the one who enters it, it offers, on the face of it, the whole of the ordinary hour: the tub to sit in, the book to open, the afternoon to spend on nothing at all, every door in the room standing open to it.

## Withheld

The permission to actually step through.
The Bathroom cannot manufacture what only [the Warden](persona_the_warden.md) grants, so what it withholds is not the water or the hour but the licence: rest that is its own end justifies nothing, and a room with nothing to justify releases its rest only to someone who can produce a reason the room itself was never built to ask for.
