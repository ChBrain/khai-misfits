# Plot: The Fence

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Bathroom](place_the_bathroom.md), the water running before anything has broken.
No disaster has granted this hour and none is coming: whoever takes it will have to grant it themselves.

## Action

[Fencing](process_fencing.md): [The Steward](persona_the_steward.md), holding [the seat](position_steward.md), draws a line around the restorative hour and claims it as its own end, needing no claim beyond itself.

## Stage

[The Steward](persona_the_steward.md), on [Take the Hour](plan_take_the_hour.md), steps into the bath the Held could never justify, paying in standing what the emergency would have charged nothing for.
[The Warden](persona_the_warden.md), on [Refuse the Leave](plan_refuse_the_leave.md), meets it with the same standard as ever, reading the hour as [the Confession](piece_the_confession.md) it always was, and asking what earned it.

## Tension

The hour signals no worth and closes no loop, so the Warden's standard is not wrong, only blind to the refill it starves.
The plot closes ambivalently: the hour fenced and [the Refill](piece_the_refill.md) reached without waiting for a disaster to permit it ... or the leave refused once more, the self left waiting for the next thing to break.
