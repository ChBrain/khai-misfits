# Piece: The Spoiled Bath

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The terminal rest undermined even when it is taken, spoiled from the inside by the sense that it is waste.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Run, on the rare day it is run, in [the bathroom](place_the_bathroom.md), the water going cold around a person who cannot settle into it.
Never a risk on [the flooded floor](place_the_flooded_floor.md), where the licensed hours of sitting and waiting are never once felt as wasted, because the crisis already answered for them.

## Load Bearing

Without it the refusal would be the whole cost, and a bath, once won, would at least be a bath, restful on its own uncomplicated terms.
With it the cost outlives the refusal: the rare hour that is taken is not simply enjoyed, it is taken under suspicion of itself, and the suspicion is what remains once the water has drained. The Spoiled Bath is load bearing because it carries the guilt that undermines rest even when entered (Tonietto, Malkoc, Reczek, and Norton), worst for the terminal rest pursued as its own end and for no other purpose: the belief that leisure is wasteful lowers the enjoyment of the leisure itself, so the person who finally takes the bath rises from it less rested than they lay down, having spent the hour proving to themselves that it should not have been spent. The piece carries the spoiling, the reason a won rest is not a rest recovered.

## Apparent

A bath enjoyed, an evening off, the ordinary picture of someone finally taking a moment for themselves.
Seen at the surface, the Spoiled Bath looks exactly like rest, water run, door closed, nothing amiss, and the truth that the person inside is doing arithmetic instead of resting, weighing the hour against what it cost and coming up short, stays hidden behind how completely a spoiled rest can look, from the doorway, like a rest.

## Yearbook

Worked by [The Warden](persona_the_warden.md) through [Depleting](process_depleting.md), the guilt that follows [The Unlicensed Hour](piece_the_unlicensed_hour.md) even past its refusal, into the rare hour it is finally taken.
It is why [The Refill](piece_the_refill.md) so often fails to land even when the hour is won, and one more line item [The Undrawn](piece_the_undrawn.md) sums across the years, the loss inside the loss that only [Fencing](process_fencing.md) against, held firmly by [The Steward](persona_the_steward.md), can keep from spoiling the hour twice.
