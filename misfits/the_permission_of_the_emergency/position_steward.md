# Position: Steward

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The seat of the one who grants the self its own leave, fencing the restorative hour and taking rest as its own end, without waiting for a disaster to permit it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The willingness to license a stop that justifies nothing: the standing to grant the self an hour that closes no loop and answers to no claim.
A clear case: the self is real and its depletion is real, so an hour kept for it is not indulgence but maintenance.
No excuse to offer for the hour, and no need of one, because the seat has decided the rest is reason enough in itself.

## Orders

Take the hour.
Fence the restorative hour before the week can fill it, run the bath while the work still waits, and take rest as its own justified end.
Bear the guilt of stillness that nothing licenses, and spend the time though it signals no status and shows nothing for itself.

## Loses

The cover of a justification, and the standing that busyness would have signalled.
Because the seat rests without an excuse, it gives up the licence a disaster grants free and the worth a full day would have shown, so it holds its rest against the guilt the Warden presses and the reading of leisure as waste, keeping the hour but paying for it.
It surrenders the comfort of an earned stop to take the stop unearned, and pays, in unease and in standing, the price the emergency never charged.

## Drives

Toward the self kept whole and the hour taken unlicensed,
bought with guilt and with standing,
so the seat that grants its own leave pays for the permit a disaster would have handed over free.
