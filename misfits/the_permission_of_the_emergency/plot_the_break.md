# Plot: The Break

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Flooded Floor](place_the_flooded_floor.md), water spreading under a burst pipe.
Nothing was found for the bath all week, and now, in an instant, hours are found for this.

## Action

[Licensing](process_licensing.md): [The Emergency](persona_the_emergency.md), holding [the seat](position_emergency.md), presses its unarguable claim and grants, at once, the leave to drop everything.

## Stage

[The Held](persona_the_held.md), on [Wait for Leave](plan_wait_for_leave.md), receives the leave it could never grant itself and stops without a flicker of the guilt the bath would have cost.
[The Emergency](persona_the_emergency.md), on [Grant the Leave](plan_grant_the_leave.md), hands over [the Permit](piece_the_permit.md), real and complete and instant, needing no defending.

## Tension

The permit is genuine, so nothing here is refused wrongly, and that is the difficulty: the leave the bath waited weeks for is granted in a breath the moment something breaks.
The plot closes with the emergency holding the hours, the stop fully licensed, no one questioning the time it takes.
