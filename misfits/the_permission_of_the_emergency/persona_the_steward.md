# Persona: The Steward

## Taxonomy

[Steward](position_steward.md). The one who would grant the self its own leave, fencing the restorative hour and taking rest as its own justified end, without waiting for a disaster to permit it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Deliberate. Unhurried. Willing to be thought idle.
Is the one who blocks the hour before the week can fill it, who runs the bath while the work still waits, who treats the ordinary rest as reason enough in itself.
No laziness in it: only a person who has decided the self is worth an hour that closes no loop and answers to no claim.

## Action

Fences the restorative hour and takes it unlicensed, on [Take the Hour](plan_take_the_hour.md), granting the leave [the Warden](persona_the_warden.md) would refuse and the emergency need not.
Takes rest as its own end and bears the guilt of it, spending time that signals no status and shows nothing for itself.
Does what a steward of the self does: pays, in standing and in unease, for the permit a disaster would have handed over free.

## Shadow

Not a person immune to the guilt, a person who pays it and rests anyway, and that is the whole difficulty: the leave is granted, and granting it costs exactly what the Warden warned.
Because the fenced hour signals no worth and closes no loop and shows no return, the Steward must trust a refill that proves nothing while every reading of rest as waste pulls the other way, so keeping the self whole is not a costless correction but a standing decision to be still without an excuse and to bear being thought idle for it.
It grants the leave the emergency would have granted free, and grants it at a price the emergency never charged.

## Tell

Says "the hour is not spare and it is not earned: I am taking it anyway."
Takes it, really, and feels the guilt the whole while, resting against the grain of every standard that says the self must break something before it is allowed to stop.
