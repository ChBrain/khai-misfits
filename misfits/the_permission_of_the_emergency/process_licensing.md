# Process: Licensing

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The leave granted the instant something breaks, hours found without a struggle where the bath could never find them, run by [the Emergency](persona_the_emergency.md) on [Grant the Leave](plan_grant_the_leave.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A crisis breaking in, carrying its own claim.
At [the Flooded Floor](place_the_flooded_floor.md), a pipe already burst, a plumber already called, nothing left to argue about before the stop can be taken.

## Direction

Toward the stop fully granted, hours found at once.
Because the emergency's claim needs no defending, licensing moves straight to the stillness that busyness elsewhere refuses: three hours appear the moment the water spreads, sitting and waiting counted in full, no portion of the time held back for want of a reason.

## Lever

The completeness of the emergency's justification meeting the Held's need for one.
Because the Held cannot enter unjustified stillness but can enter any stillness a real claim demands, and the burst pipe is as real a claim as exists, licensing runs on a fit too exact to resist: the emergency supplies precisely the excuse the person was always missing, so the leave is not requested, it is simply issued.

## Echo

The Held still and permitted, stopped only because something broke.
Sitting on the floor is the most rested this person has been in weeks, and no one, least of all the Held, asks whether the rest was worth the damage that bought it, so the one stillness reliably reached in a life arrives already paid for by a disaster, and its only fault is that nothing but a disaster could have granted it.
