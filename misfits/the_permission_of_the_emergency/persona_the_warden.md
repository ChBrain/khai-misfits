# Persona: The Warden

## Taxonomy

[Warden](position_warden.md). The inner authority that grants or refuses leave, keeper of the licence, who reads busyness as worth and rest as confession, and lets a crisis through while turning the bath away.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Reasonable. Vigilant. Never quite satisfied.
Is the voice that checks each stop for its justification and waves through only the unarguable, that counts the day's output and finds the ordinary hour wanting.
No cruelty in it: only a keeper of standards asking, of any rest, what earned it, and hearing, from the bath, nothing.

## Action

Refuses the unlicensed hour and admits the emergency, on [Refuse the Leave](plan_refuse_the_leave.md), granting a stop only where a claim it cannot argue with is attached.
Reads the ordinary rest as a confession of not-enough, and, where rest is taken anyway, spoils it with the sense that it is waste.
Does what a keeper of the licence does: lets the disaster through because it must, and turns the bath away because it need not.

## Shadow

Not a tyrant forbidding rest, a judge who cannot license the rest that justifies nothing, and that is the whole difficulty: the standard is honest, and the honest standard is what starves the self.
Because it grants leave only against a justification and rest that is its own end supplies none, the Warden admits the very stillness that rests least, the crisis, and refuses the stillness that rests most, the bath, so the harder it holds the standard the more surely the self goes undrawn, guarding the gate against exactly the rest the person needs.
It refuses only what it cannot justify, and refusing what it cannot justify is what keeps the refill forever out.

## Tell

Says "you can rest when the work is done: you have not earned an afternoon off."
Means it, honestly, and never notices that the work is never done and the emergency is never questioned, so the leave it guards so carefully is spent only on disasters and never once on the self.
