# Plot: The Refusal

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Bathroom](place_the_bathroom.md), water going cold before it is ever drawn.
Nothing has broken, so nothing yet grants the hour, and the day is already full of things that have.

## Action

[Refusing](process_refusing.md): [The Warden](persona_the_warden.md), holding [the seat](position_warden.md), asks the ordinary hour for its justification, hears nothing, and reads the silence as a confession of not-enough.

## Stage

[The Held](persona_the_held.md), on [Wait for Leave](plan_wait_for_leave.md), would take the bath and asks no one, since rest that is its own end needs no permission it could ever supply.
[The Warden](persona_the_warden.md), on [Refuse the Leave](plan_refuse_the_leave.md), turns it away, admitting only a claim it cannot argue with, and none is offered here.

## Tension

The bath is owed nothing and asks for nothing, and asking for nothing is exactly what disqualifies it.
[The Unlicensed Hour](piece_the_unlicensed_hour.md) is set aside once more, filed as [the Confession](piece_the_confession.md) it was never meant to be, and the plot closes with the hour unrun, the self telling itself there was no time.
