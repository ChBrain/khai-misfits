# Piece: The Undrawn

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The sum over a life, the self run down not for want of time but for want of leave, invisible in any single week and total across the years.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Kept in [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), where every unlicensed hour refused and every licensed hour spent sitting on a flooded floor are entered on the same page and never balanced.
Traced back to [the bathroom](place_the_bathroom.md), the baths never run, and to [the flooded floor](place_the_flooded_floor.md), the hours the disaster always found.

## Load Bearing

Without it each refused bath would be its own small, forgivable loss, gone by morning and costing nothing that compounds.
With it the refusals stop being separate and become a single account: a hundred unlicensed hours declined, a hundred licensed hours spent on other people's disasters, and a refill that came, when it came at all, spoiled or accidental, all entered against a self that was never once asked to draw on its own account for its own sake. The Undrawn is load bearing because it is the accrued cost, the sum, invisible week to week and total across a life: not a single dramatic failure but a self depleted the way a balance depletes, a little at a time, by a hundred individually reasonable refusals of a rest that nothing licensed. The piece carries the reckoning that only time, not any single day, can show.

## Apparent

A life well spent, a person who was always there for whatever needed them, nothing to regret in any of it.
Seen at the surface, the Undrawn does not appear at all, because every entry that built it looked, on its own day, like diligence or like care, and the truth that the total is a self spent on everything but itself, refilled only by accident and only when something broke, stays hidden behind a ledger whose every single line reads as reasonable and whose sum no single day ever shows you.

## Yearbook

Compounded by [Depleting](process_depleting.md), carried by [The Held](persona_the_held.md) across every week [The Unlicensed Hour](piece_the_unlicensed_hour.md) was set aside and every week [The Permit](piece_the_permit.md) was the only leave granted.
It is the total [The Confession](piece_the_confession.md) and [The Spoiled Bath](piece_the_spoiled_bath.md) both feed and [The Refill](piece_the_refill.md) was too rarely allowed to offset, the whole cost [The Permission of the Emergency](piece_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md) exacts, drawn down only where [The Steward](persona_the_steward.md) fences an hour against it.
