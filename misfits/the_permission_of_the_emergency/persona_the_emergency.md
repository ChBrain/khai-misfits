# Persona: The Emergency

## Taxonomy

[Emergency](position_emergency.md). The crisis that breaks in carrying its own complete and unarguable justification, the burst pipe and the fall and the fire, the claim that grants the leave to drop everything and be still.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sudden. Unarguable. Now.
Is the pipe that bursts, the alarm that sounds, the thing gone wrong that everyone agrees must be dealt with this minute, the claim no one would tell you to ignore.
No cunning in it: only a real crisis with a real cost, arriving with a reason to stop everything already attached.

## Action

Presses its claim and grants the leave, on [Grant the Leave](plan_grant_the_leave.md), so the hours to attend appear at once and the idle hours of waiting come with them.
Licenses the stillness the ordinary hour could never win, handing [the Held](persona_the_held.md) a stop that needs no defending.
Does what a crisis does: falls out of nowhere, demands everything, and, in demanding it, permits the person to set the rest of life down.

## Shadow

Not a gift of rest, a disaster, and that is the whole difficulty: the leave it grants is real and the stillness it forces is often no rest at all, and still it is the only leave that reliably comes.
Because it carries a justification the bath cannot, the Emergency licenses a stop that ordinary rest is refused, so the person learns that the way to be still is for something to break, and comes to reach stillness only through the very crises that rest was meant to prevent.
It asks only to be met, and being met is what teaches the self that only a catastrophe grants it leave to stop.

## Tell

Says "everything else can wait: this has to be dealt with right now."
Has to be, truly, and by truly having to be takes precedence over the bath that never could, licensing in a moment the stop the self would not grant itself in weeks.
