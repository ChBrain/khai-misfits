# Process: Fencing

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The restorative hour claimed without waiting for a disaster to permit it, run by [the Steward](persona_the_steward.md) on [Take the Hour](plan_take_the_hour.md) against the Warden's refusal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone who would grant the self its own leave, where no crisis has offered to.
At [the Bathroom](place_the_bathroom.md), the tub still run and the book still waiting, only now taken up without an emergency behind it.

## Direction

Toward the restorative hour, taken unlicensed.
Because no disaster is coming to supply the excuse, fencing moves the stop forward on nothing but its own decision: the door closes, the water is entered, the hour is spent on the self exactly as it would have been spent on a burst pipe, except that nothing broke to make it allowed.

## Lever

The decision to rest without an excuse meeting the guilt and the reading of rest as waste.
Because the Warden still holds that unjustified stillness is a confession and the Steward takes the hour regardless, fencing runs against its own cost every time: the hour is not free of the guilt that would have spoiled it, it is taken with the guilt intact and paid anyway, so the leave that the emergency would have handed over free is instead bought, in full, out of standing and comfort both.

## Echo

The self kept whole, at the price of a stillness that nothing licensed.
The bath was taken and the attention did reload, but no one applauded it and no crisis excused it, so what is left afterward is not relief without cost, it is a rest that restored the self and still had to be defended, the one hour in the whole account that was worth the most and came cheapest to no one.
