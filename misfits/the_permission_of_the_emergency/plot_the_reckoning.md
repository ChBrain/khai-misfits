# Plot: The Reckoning

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ledger](place_the_ledger.md), the tally of hours found and hours refused, kept without a hand ever writing it down.
Every crisis is entered on arrival, and the ordinary hour never earns a line.

## Action

[Depleting](process_depleting.md): [The Held](persona_the_held.md), holding [the seat](position_held.md), spends across the years on every claim that could justify itself and none on the self that could not, drawing down what nothing ever refilled.

## Stage

[The Held](persona_the_held.md), on [Wait for Leave](plan_wait_for_leave.md), waits out one more week, one more year, for a leave that ordinary rest was never built to carry.
The rare bath taken arrives already as [the Spoiled Bath](piece_the_spoiled_bath.md), the sense of waste rising before the water cools, and [the Refill](piece_the_refill.md) that only a crisis reliably grants comes too seldom to count against [the Undrawn](piece_the_undrawn.md) hours stacking underneath.

## Tension

Not one refusal was wrong: each was the reasonable triage of a self that must earn its stillness, and the sum of a hundred correct refusals is a self drawn down.
The plot closes with a life filled with hours found for disasters and none kept for the self, the attention never reloaded.
