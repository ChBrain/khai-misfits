# Piece: The Unlicensed Hour

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The ordinary rest, the bath, the book, the unhurried afternoon, that carries no justification of its own and so is refused week after week.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Waits in [the bathroom](place_the_bathroom.md), forty minutes never found, the water never run, the door never closed against the rest of the house.
Measured against [the flooded floor](place_the_flooded_floor.md), where the same length of stillness is granted the moment a pipe, not a person, asks for it.

## Load Bearing

Without it there would be no refusal to explain, every hour would be spent as its worth alone decided, and idleness would need no permit to be entered.
With it the trap has its cost centre: an hour that produces nothing, closes no loop, and answers to no claim, so it is the hardest stillness to permit and the first one set aside. The Unlicensed Hour is load bearing because it is the idleness that needs a justification and is never given one (Hsee, Yang, and Wang): the dread of unjustified stillness does not discriminate between a good rest and a bad one, it discriminates between a rest with a licence and a rest without, and the bath, however deserved, is always the one without. The piece carries the refusal itself, the ordinary hour standing empty at the centre of every week that finds three hours for a disaster and none for a bath.

## Apparent

Laziness postponed, a small indulgence a busy person simply hasn't gotten to yet, nothing urgent.
Seen at the surface, the Unlicensed Hour looks like a low-priority errand waiting its turn behind more pressing things, and the truth that it is refused not for lack of time, since the same person finds hours instantly for a crisis, but for lack of any claim it can make on its own behalf, stays hidden behind the reasonable-sounding excuse that there simply wasn't time today either.

## Yearbook

Refused by [The Warden](persona_the_warden.md) through [Refusing](process_refusing.md), the hour set aside once more, week after week, without a fight.
It is the hour the whole misfit is built around: the Unlicensed Hour is what [The Permit](piece_the_permit.md) alone can override, what [The Confession](piece_the_confession.md) makes doubly hard to claim, and what only [The Steward](persona_the_steward.md), through [Fencing](process_fencing.md), ever manages to take without waiting for the flooded floor.
