# Pitch: Wait for the Emergency

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Coping, present, on it.
The strange, plain relief of the crisis that finally lets you sit down: the hours that appear the instant a pipe bursts, the licensed stillness of the floor, the leave to drop everything and attend that no one questions.
Nothing false in it: the emergency is a real claim with a real cost, and dropping everything to meet it is genuinely right.

## Undertow

Under the coping, the ordinary rest that carries no licence and so is refused, week after week, the bath that cannot find forty minutes and the book left unread.
Because rest that is its own end justifies nothing and reads, in a life where busyness is the badge, as an admission of not-enough, the leave goes to the disaster and never to the self, and the rare hour taken is spoiled from within by the sense that it is waste.
The diligence meant to keep a person whole spends the self on everything but the self.

## Nerve

The feeling of finding three hours the moment something breaks, sure at last that you are allowed to stop,
because the emergency granted the leave and asked nothing of you but to be present to it,
while the bath that needed no crisis and would have rested you more waits another week, unlicensed, and is refused again.

## Echo

After the run: a life of hours found for disasters and none kept for the self, the only stillness the one a crisis forced,
no one idle by choice and no one foolish, only real emergencies met and ordinary rest refused for want of a licence,
and the refill that was worth the self, never drawn, because it never once had leave and never once broke anything by going without.
