# Plan: Take the Hour

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the steward's seat](position_steward.md), inside [The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Steward](position_steward.md)

## Direction

Fence the restorative hour before the week can fill it, and take it without waiting for a disaster to grant leave. Treat rest as its own justified end, run the bath while the work still waits, and bear the guilt and the loss of standing as the price of the permit the emergency would have handed over free.

## Orders

([The Steward](persona_the_steward.md) fences the restorative hour and takes it unlicensed, granting the leave [the Warden](persona_the_warden.md) refuses, at the cost of guilt and standing.)

## Implementation

([The Bathroom](place_the_bathroom.md); the [fencing](process_fencing.md) process; the hour is held for [the refill](piece_the_refill.md) against [the confession](piece_the_confession.md), keeping the self off [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md) and out of [the undrawn](piece_the_undrawn.md) ... if the guilt of stillness that nothing licenses can be borne without an excuse to justify it.)

## Targets

- [ ] the restorative hour is fenced before the week can fill it
- [ ] rest is taken as its own end, without waiting for a disaster to permit it
- [ ] the guilt and the loss of standing are borne as the price of the hour
- [ ] the self is kept whole, or the reading of rest as waste wins and the hour waits for the next thing to break
