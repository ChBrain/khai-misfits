# Piece: The Permit

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The crisis's complete, unarguable leave to drop everything, the one claim that licenses idleness without a moment's defending.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Issued on [the flooded floor](place_the_flooded_floor.md), the instant the pipe bursts, where the hours needed to sit and wait are granted before anyone thinks to ask for them.
Never issued in [the bathroom](place_the_bathroom.md), where the ordinary hour arrives daily and finds no such claim waiting to hand it leave.

## Load Bearing

Without it idleness would have to earn its own way in every time, and a person too diligent to grant themselves an unjustified hour would simply never stop, crisis or none.
With it the trap has its opening: a real claim, a leave to be still that needs no defending, and it arrives complete, asking nothing of the person it frees. The Permit is load bearing because it is the justification that alone licenses idleness (Hsee, Yang, and Wang): the mind that dreads unjustified stillness and needs a reason to escape busyness finds, in the disaster, a reason so total that it moves at once, granting in an instant what the same mind has refused itself for weeks. The piece carries the licence, the visible pole of the trap against which the unlicensed hour can make no claim at all.

## Apparent

Plain necessity, the obvious and unremarkable response to a real emergency, nothing to notice.
Seen at the surface, the Permit looks like nothing more than a person doing what a burst pipe requires, sitting and waiting because sitting and waiting is what the moment calls for, and the truth that this is the only leave the person reliably grants themselves, that the same three hours would be refused outright if a bath asked for them instead of a disaster, stays hidden behind how ordinary an emergency response looks from outside.

## Yearbook

Handed down by [The Emergency](persona_the_emergency.md) through [Licensing](process_licensing.md), the claim that ends the dread of idleness the instant it is raised.
It is the piece the whole misfit turns on: the Permit is what [The Unlicensed Hour](piece_the_unlicensed_hour.md) can never produce for itself, what makes the disaster in [The Permission of the Emergency](piece_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md) the one dependable stillness a life affords, and what [The Steward](persona_the_steward.md) must learn to grant without waiting for a floor to flood.
