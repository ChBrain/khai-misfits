# Persona: The Held

## Taxonomy

[Held](position_held.md). The one who cannot be still without a licence, diligent and depleted, who finds hours the instant a crisis grants leave and none when nothing does, the self the trap is drawn down through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Coping. Always something to do first.
Is the one who would never refuse an emergency and can never quite reach the bath, who runs the days full and tells themselves there was no time for the ordinary hour.
No sloth in it: only a diligent person who cannot sit still while a reason to be busy remains, and a reason always remains.

## Action

Refuses the unlicensed hour and takes up the next justified task, on [Wait for Leave](plan_wait_for_leave.md).
Finds the hours at once when [the Emergency](persona_the_emergency.md) grants leave, dropping everything to attend and sitting the licensed stillness without protest.
Does what a diligent person does under an idleness they cannot permit: stays busy while nothing licenses the stop, and stops only when something breaks.

## Shadow

Not a person who hates rest, a person who cannot justify it, and that is the whole difficulty: the bath would rest them and they want it, and wanting it is not a licence to take it.
Because they dread the stillness that carries no reason and rest that is its own end carries none, the Held reaches the bath only when a crisis forces a stop, so the busier and more coping they are, the more surely the ordinary hour is refused, and the only stillness they reliably reach is the one a disaster hands them, needed least and granted first.
It answers every real claim on its time, and answering every real claim is what leaves the self undrawn.

## Tell

Says "I would love to, honestly, but there is just no time right now."
Had the time, really, the moment a pipe burst, and never sees that what the bath lacked was not an hour but an excuse.
