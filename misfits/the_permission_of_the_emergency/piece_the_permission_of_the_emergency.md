# Piece: The Permission of the Emergency

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The phenomenon whole, the crisis as the one licence strong enough to override the aversion, so a person can only truly stop when a disaster gives them leave.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Granted on [the flooded floor](place_the_flooded_floor.md), where hours are found the instant they are asked for.
Refused in [the bathroom](place_the_bathroom.md), where the same hours are never found at all.
Totalled in [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), where the difference between the two, kept across years, is the only place the whole shape becomes visible.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be three separate observations, an aversion, a status cost, a spoiled leisure, none of them adding to a single trap.
With it they resolve into one law: idleness is permitted only when something licenses it, and a disaster is the readiest, most complete licence there is, so the same person who cannot find forty minutes for a bath finds three hours the instant a pipe bursts, not because the pipe is worth more but because it grants leave to stop and the bath cannot. The Permission of the Emergency is load bearing because it carries the whole structure at once: the dread of unjustified stillness and the pull of any justification that ends it (Hsee, Yang, and Wang), the status that busyness signals and the confession leisure reads as in its absence (Bellezza, Paharia, and Keinan), and the restorative good that only the licence unlocks, starved everywhere else (Kaplan). The piece carries the governing law itself, the reason a diligent person is brilliant at finding time for disaster and helpless at finding it for themselves.

## Apparent

Coping, plainly, a capable person who rises to whatever the moment demands and rests when the moment allows it.
Seen at the surface, the Permission of the Emergency looks like resilience, someone handling a crisis well and taking their stillness where they can get it, and the truth that the stillness they can get is the only stillness they will ever reliably be given, that the self is being spent on everything but itself for want of a leave no disaster was ever required to withhold, stays hidden behind how well, in the moment, the coping actually works.
