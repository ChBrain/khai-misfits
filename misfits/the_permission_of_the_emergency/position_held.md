# Position: Held

## Taxonomy

[The Permission of the Emergency](play_the_permission_of_the_emergency.md). The seat of the one whose stillness needs a licence, who finds the hours when a crisis grants leave and refuses them when nothing does.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A dread of unjustified stillness and a diligence that answers it: the standing to fill every hour with a reason to be busy, and no reason to hand the ordinary rest.
A clear case: the days are genuinely full and the tasks are genuinely real, so staying busy is honest work and the bath honestly can wait.
No licence of its own to grant the stop, only the aversion, and so the seat cannot rest until something outside it supplies the leave.

## Orders

Wait for leave.
Refuse the stillness that carries no reason, take up the next justified task, and hold until a claim you cannot argue with grants the stop.
Then drop everything and attend, and sit the licensed stillness the crisis forces, because now, at last, you may.

## Loses

The power to be still on its own say-so, and with it the ordinary hour.
Because the seat can permit no stillness that justifies nothing, it gives up the bath and the book and the unhurried afternoon, keeping only the rest a disaster forces, so it loses the refill it needs most and holds the stillness it needs least.
It surrenders the leave to rest to whatever outside it will grant one, and outside it only the emergency will.

## Drives

Toward a life run full and a self run down,
the hours found for every disaster and none kept for the bath,
so the seat that cannot license its own rest reaches stillness only when something breaks.
