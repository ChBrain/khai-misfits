# Piece: The Gate

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The source: the discretionary power to dispense privilege, which makes rents exist to be sought.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the ministry](place_the_ministry.md), the discretion held in one hand.
Where a signature can be worth a fortune to whoever receives it.

## Load Bearing

Without it there would be no rent, and nothing to seek.
With it privilege can be granted at will, so an ordinary act of office becomes the making of a prize, and the discretion itself manufactures the incentive to compete for what it dispenses. The Gate is load bearing because it is the discretionary power the constitutional account puts at the root (Buchanan, Tollison & Tullock): the transfers that call forth seeking exist only because some authority may grant them freely, so the gate is the tap the whole misfit flows from, and the one place the cure must reach.

## Apparent

The ordinary business of office.
Granting licences and setting tariffs looks like the normal work of authority, so the discretion reads as administration rather than as the manufacture of prizes, and nothing about a routine grant announces the scramble it invites.

## Yearbook

Kept by [the grantor](persona_the_grantor.md) through [granting](process_granting.md), the tap the play flows from.
It is the source of every prize: the Gate is what makes [the rent](piece_the_rent.md), and narrowing it is what [the rent-seeking](piece_the_rent_seeking.md) can finally be cured by.
