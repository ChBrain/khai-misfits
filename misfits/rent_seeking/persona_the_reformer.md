# Persona: The Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Reformer](position_reformer.md). The one who shrinks the rents and narrows the gate, so there is nothing to seek.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Determined, systemic, unwelcome at the gate.
Sees that the waste is not in the seekers but in the prize, and that no prize means no chase.
Aims not to shame the lobbyists but to remove the privilege they lobby for.

## Action

Closes the discretionary gate that dispenses rents.
Removes the licence, the tariff, the favour, or takes the discretion out of granting it, so capture stops paying and effort flows back to making things.
Fights the grantor who keeps the gate and the seekers who guard it.

## Shadow

The one move that works must cut a power everyone in the room is invested in.
The grantor likes the discretion, the seekers have paid for their rents, and some of what looks like rent is the honest reward that makes invention pay, so the reformer must narrow the gate without cutting the incentive, against everyone who profits from leaving it open.
Not a hero winning easily: a narrower of power, resisted by its keepers and its clients, and at risk of cutting the wrong thing.

## Tell

"Stop chasing them. Take away the prize."
The true remedy, aimed at the one thing the grantor and the seekers agree to defend.
