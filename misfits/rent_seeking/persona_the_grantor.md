# Persona: The Grantor

## Taxonomy

[Grantor](position_grantor.md). The one who holds the gate and dispenses the privilege.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Positioned, courted, comfortable with discretion.
Holds the power to grant a licence, set a tariff, award a monopoly, bestow a favour.
Sees the dispensing as the ordinary business of office, and the courting as its natural due.

## Action

Dispenses the privilege, and is paid, in coin or influence, for dispensing it.
Keeps the gate discretionary, because a gate that grants at will is a gate worth courting.
Grants more as more is sought, and can charge, too, simply to withhold a harm he might impose.

## Shadow

The value he gives away is what draws the waste, and the value he keeps is the power itself.
Every rent in his gift is a prize others will burn resources to capture, so his discretion manufactures the scramble, and he need not be corrupt to profit from being courted, only to enjoy the gate too much to close it.
Not a briber's mark: the standing source of the rents, and the one seat with no reason to end them.

## Tell

"It is mine to give, and they are welcome to make their case."
True, and the invitation that turns a favour into a fortune spent competing for it.
