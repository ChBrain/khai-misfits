# Plot: The Scramble

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The lobby](place_the_lobby.md), the rent worth almost as much as it costs to win, and rivals all after it.
Anterooms full of counsel and filings and gifts, the queue outside the gate.

## Action

[Scrambling](process_scrambling.md): [The Seeker](persona_the_seeker.md), holding [the seeker's seat](position_seeker.md), lobbies, litigates, and queues, outspending the rivals for one slice, while [The Reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md), holding [the reckoner's seat](position_reckoner.md), totals what all of them spend to win it.

## Stage

[The Seeker](persona_the_seeker.md) tables [Chase the Rent](plan_chase_the_rent.md), so [the scramble](piece_the_scramble.md) pours real resources into capturing the transfer.
[The Reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md) tables [Count the Waste](plan_count_the_waste.md), and finds the sum of the spending approaches the whole of the prize, so [the dissipation](piece_the_dissipation.md) is a fortune spent to move a fortune.

## Tension

Everyone competes hard and fair, and the competition burns the prize.
The seekers are rational, the contest is genuine, effort really does improve the odds, so nothing here is a cheat. That is the waste: a prize worth almost a fortune draws almost a fortune in spending to win, and all that spending, the losers' sunk and the winner's nearly so, builds not one new thing, a fair race whose running consumes exactly what it races for.
