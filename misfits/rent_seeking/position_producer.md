# Position: Producer

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The seat that creates value, and is out-earned by capture.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The skill to build a product, lower a cost, make a new thing.
A return that comes only from enlarging the pie.
No claim on any transfer, and no favour in the grantor's gift.

## Orders

Grow the pie.
Make something that was not there before.
Prosper by creating value.

## Loses

The comparison, to whoever seized a slice instead.
The rent pays better than the product, so the seat that enlarges the pie is out-earned by the seat that captures a piece of it, and its honest creation looks, on the ledger, like the poor career.
Punished for building by the same rules that reward capture.

## Drives

Toward creation that should pay and pays less,
against a gate that rewards seizing over making,
so the road that grows the wealth is the road the returns steer talent away from.
