# Piece: The Scramble

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The contest: the competition to capture the rent, spending real resources on the winning.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the lobby](place_the_lobby.md), in the counsel, the filings, the gifts, the queue.
Where rivals outspend rivals for one slice.

## Load Bearing

Without it the rent would be a transfer with no waste, a slice merely handed over.
With it the transfer is fought for, and the fighting costs: because a prize worth almost a fortune is worth almost a fortune to win, each contestant spends up toward its value, and the spending makes nothing. The Scramble is load bearing because it is the competition that converts a mere transfer into a social loss (Krueger), the mechanism by which real resources are drawn into capturing value already made, and the visible activity under which the dissipation hides.

## Apparent

Vigorous, legitimate competition.
Lobbying, litigating, and queuing for a valuable prize look like ordinary enterprise, so the scramble reads as healthy rivalry, and the fact that all this effort produces nothing but a change of owner is buried in how much it resembles real work.

## Yearbook

Run by [the seeker](persona_the_seeker.md) through [scrambling](process_scrambling.md), drawn by [the rent](piece_the_rent.md).
It is the contest that consumes the prize: the Scramble summed across every contestant is [the dissipation](piece_the_dissipation.md), a fortune spent to move a fortune.
