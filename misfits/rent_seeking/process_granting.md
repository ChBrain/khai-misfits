# Process: Granting

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The discretionary dispensing of privilege that makes a rent.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A power to grant held at discretion, and something valuable in its gift.
A licence, a tariff, a monopoly, a favour, and a grantor free to bestow it on whom he chooses.

## Direction

Toward a privilege placed in one pair of hands.
The favour is dispensed, worth a fortune to whoever receives it, and worth courting for, so the act of office becomes the making of a prize.

## Lever

Discretion over something worth having.
Because the grant is valuable and freely given, it is worth spending to obtain, so the discretion itself manufactures the incentive to seek, and a gate that grants at will is a gate worth besieging.

## Echo

A rent in being, and a scramble invited.
The privilege bestowed, its value now a target, and everyone who wanted it drawn to compete for the next one the gate dispenses.
