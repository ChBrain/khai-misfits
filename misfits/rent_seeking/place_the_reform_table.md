# Place: The Reform Table

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). Where the gate is narrowed, or defended.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A proposal to remove a privilege, a rule to take the discretion out of granting, a list of rents to cut.
The reformer with the remedy, and the grantor and the seekers arrayed to keep what they have.

## Holds

The cure, and everyone invested against it.
Here the one move that ends the waste, shrinking the prize, meets the grantor who keeps the gate and the seekers who paid for their rents, so the place holds the remedy and its whole resistance at once.

## Offers

An end to the seeking, if the prize can be removed.
Close the discretionary gate and there is nothing left to chase, so the reform table offers the only cure that works, a world where effort must go back to making things.

## Withheld

An easy or a clean cut.
The gate's keepers and clients defend it, and some of what sits on the table is the honest reward that makes invention pay, so the table withholds any narrowing that is simple, certain, or sure to cut the rent and spare the return.
