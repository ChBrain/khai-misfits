# Plan: Close the Gate

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), inside [Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md).

## Owner

## Direction

Aim at the prize, not the seekers. Remove the licence, the tariff, the favour, or take the discretion out of granting it, so that capture stops paying and there is nothing left to chase. Narrow the gate without cutting the honest reward that makes invention pay, against everyone who profits from leaving it open.
