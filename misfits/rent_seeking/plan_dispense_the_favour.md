# Plan: Dispense the Favour

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the grantor's seat](position_grantor.md), inside [Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md).

## Direction

Keep the gate discretionary and dispense the privilege to those who make their case. Enjoy the courting a valuable, freely given favour attracts, grant more as more is sought, and charge, in coin or influence, for granting, and for withholding a harm you might impose.
