# Plot: The Rent

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The ministry](place_the_ministry.md), a privilege sitting in the grantor's gift, worth a fortune and making nothing.
A licence, a tariff, a monopoly to award, and the discretion to bestow it held in one hand.

## Action

[Granting](process_granting.md): [The Grantor](persona_the_grantor.md), holding [the grantor's seat](position_grantor.md), keeps the gate discretionary and dispenses the favour, while [The Seeker](persona_the_seeker.md), holding [the seeker's seat](position_seeker.md), sees a prize plainly worth chasing.

## Stage

[The Grantor](persona_the_grantor.md) tables [Dispense the Favour](plan_dispense_the_favour.md), so [the gate](piece_the_gate.md) turns an act of office into the making of a prize.
[The Seeker](persona_the_seeker.md) eyes [Chase the Rent](plan_chase_the_rent.md), because [the rent](piece_the_rent.md) is value already made and merely up for capture, a fortune waiting for whoever wins the favour.

## Tension

A signature is worth a fortune, and nothing has yet been built or stolen.
Nothing is wrong: the grantor may give the favour, the seeker may compete for it, the privilege is real and valuable. That is the trap laid, not sprung: a discretionary gate that manufactures a prize, a transfer that creates nothing set out to be won, and every reason for the ablest and the hungriest to turn toward it and none yet to build.
