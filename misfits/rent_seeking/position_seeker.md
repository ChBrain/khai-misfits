# Position: Seeker

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The seat that spends real resources to capture a transfer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A prize in view: a privilege worth a fortune and making nothing.
The means to compete for it, in money, lawyers, and time.
No product to show for the spending, only a claim on the transfer.

## Orders

Chase the rent.
Outspend the rivals for the same slice.
Court the grantor and win the favour.

## Loses

Nothing on its own account, and everything on the society's.
Its spending is rational and its winnings are real, but the resources it pours into the contest make nothing, so its private prize is bought with a public loss the size of the fight.
Rewarded for capture, and productive of not one new thing.

## Drives

Toward the transfer worth seizing,
away from any building that would grow the pie,
so a rational chase for a fortune consumes a fortune in the chasing.
