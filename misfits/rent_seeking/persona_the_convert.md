# Persona: The Convert

## Taxonomy

[Defector](position_defector.md). The talented one who quits making things to chase rents, because that is where the returns are.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Able, adaptable, following the money.
Has the gifts that could build a company or a technology, and the clear eye to see what those gifts now pay.
Reads the returns and goes where they are highest, which is no longer the workshop.

## Action

Abandons production for capture.
Turns the same talent that would have grown the pie toward winning slices of it, into law, lobbying, finance for the favour rather than the firm.
Prospers, and proves the producer's suspicion that building is the mug's game.

## Shadow

The ability that could have made the pie larger is spent fighting over it instead.
Nothing is wrong with his choice: talent flows where the rules reward it, and the rules reward capture, so his defection is rational and his gifts are real and the loss is that they now make nothing, only redistribute.
Not a sell-out by weakness: talent allocated exactly as the payoffs dictate, and the payoffs point at the gate.

## Tell

"Why build the pie when the money's in the slicing?"
The rational question whose honest answer, under these rules, is that there is no reason at all.
