# Process: Scrambling

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The competition to capture the rent, spending real resources on the winning.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A rent worth capturing, and rivals who all want it.
A prize worth almost a fortune, and no one able to win it without outspending the rest.

## Direction

Toward a prize consumed in the contest for it.
Each contestant lobbies, litigates, queues, and courts harder than the last, and the harder all of them try, the more of the transfer's value is spent to seize it.

## Lever

A prize worth nearly what it costs to win.
Because the rent is worth almost a fortune, it is worth spending almost a fortune to secure, so competition drives the total spent up toward the whole value of the transfer, and the racing dissipates the prize.

## Echo

A winner, and a fortune burned to crown one.
The rent captured, the losers' spending sunk and the winner's nearly so, and a sum poured into the contest that built not one new thing.
