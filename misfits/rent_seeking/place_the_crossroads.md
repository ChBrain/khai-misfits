# Place: The Crossroads

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). Where talent chooses between making the pie and slicing it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Two roads from one point: the workshop, and the gate.
An able person standing at the fork, reading the returns down each way before choosing which to walk.

## Holds

The diversion, decided here.
Here the same talent that could build or could capture weighs the payoffs and picks, and because the gate pays better than the workshop, the place holds the moment the ablest hands turn toward the slices.

## Offers

An honest reading of the returns.
The signposts do not lie: capture really does pay more than creation under these rules, so the crossroads offers a true comparison, and the true comparison is what steers the talent wrong.

## Withheld

A payoff that rewards the productive road.
Nothing here makes building pay as well as seizing, so the crossroads withholds the one thing that would keep talent in the workshop, and the rational walker takes the road to the gate.
