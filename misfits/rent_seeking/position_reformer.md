# Position: Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The seat that narrows the gate, so there is no rent to seek.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The sight that the waste is in the prize, not the seekers.
The aim to remove the privilege rather than to shame the lobby.
The task of narrowing the grantor's discretion against everyone who profits from it.

## Orders

Close the discretionary gate.
Remove the licence, the tariff, the favour, or take the discretion out of granting it.
Make capture stop paying, so effort flows back to making things.

## Loses

Ease and certainty, cutting a power the whole room defends.
The grantor keeps the gate, the seekers have paid for their rents, and some of what looks like rent is the honest reward for invention, so the seat must narrow the gate without cutting the incentive, resisted by its keepers and its clients.
Right about the cure, and set against everyone invested in the disease.

## Drives

Toward a gate too narrow to dispense privilege,
against the grantor who keeps it and the seekers who guard it,
so the remedy is a contested narrowing of power, at risk of cutting the honest return with the rent.
