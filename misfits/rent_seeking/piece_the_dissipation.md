# Piece: The Dissipation

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The waste: up to the whole value of the transfer consumed in the contest for it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the lobby](place_the_lobby.md), in the sum of all the spending.
Where the losers' effort is sunk and the winner's nearly so.

## Load Bearing

Without it rent-seeking would be a mere redistribution, costless to the whole.
With it the redistribution has a price the size of the fight: because competition drives the total spent toward the value of the prize, the transfer is not merely moved but largely destroyed in the moving, so a slice handed over becomes a slice burned. The Dissipation is load bearing because it is the full-dissipation result that makes rent-seeking a loss and not just a transfer (Tullock; Posner), the reason the pie shrinks when it is fought over, and the exact quantity a narrowed gate would save.

## Apparent

The ordinary cost of competing.
Every contest has its expenses, so the resources spent seeking look like the normal price of winning, and the fact that here they buy no new value, only a change of owner, is hidden inside the familiar shape of competition's cost.

## Yearbook

Totalled by [the reckoner](persona_the_reckoner.md), grown from [the scramble](piece_the_scramble.md) summed whole.
It is the loss no ledger books: the Dissipation is what turns [the rent](piece_the_rent.md) from a transfer into destruction, the fortune the fight burned away.
