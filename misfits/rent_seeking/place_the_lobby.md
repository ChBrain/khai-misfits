# Place: The Lobby

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). Where the seekers compete, and the prize is burned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Anterooms full of counsel, filings, gifts, and waiting, the queue outside the gate.
The whole apparatus of persuasion and pressure, aimed at winning the grantor's favour.

## Holds

The scramble, and the dissipation inside it.
Here rivals spend to outdo rivals for one slice, and the sum of what they all spend approaches the whole of what the slice is worth, so the place holds a contest that consumes its own prize.

## Offers

A fair chance at the rent, to anyone who will pay to compete.
The lobby is open, the contest genuine, and effort really does improve the odds, so it offers an honest race, which is exactly what makes the racing worth the fortune poured into it.

## Withheld

Any product for all the effort.
Everything spent here persuades, pressures, and positions, and none of it builds, so the lobby withholds the one thing the workshop offers, and turns real resources into a claim on a transfer and nothing more.
