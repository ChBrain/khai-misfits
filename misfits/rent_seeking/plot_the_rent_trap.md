# Plot: The Rent Trap

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The ministry](place_the_ministry.md), the seekers returned, each rent won having taught the next lobby to come asking.
The gate that granted once is now the obvious place to prosper, and the queue for it is longer.

## Action

[Scrambling](process_scrambling.md): [The Seeker](persona_the_seeker.md), holding [the seeker's seat](position_seeker.md), comes back for more privilege, while [The Grantor](persona_the_grantor.md), holding [the grantor's seat](position_grantor.md), courted and paid, dispenses more.

## Stage

[The Seeker](persona_the_seeker.md) tables [Chase the Rent](plan_chase_the_rent.md) again, and [The Grantor](persona_the_grantor.md) tables [Dispense the Favour](plan_dispense_the_favour.md) again, so [the scramble](piece_the_scramble.md) feeds back on itself.
Each capture proves capture pays, drawing more seekers and more talent, so [the rent trap](piece_the_rent_trap.md) turns a discrete waste into a standing order, the surest road to fortune running through the gate.

## Tension

The seeking feeds itself, and the feeding looks like a thriving economy.
Every new rent won is a fresh argument to seek one, so the lobby grows, the advisers multiply, and the whole apparatus of capture matures, reading, from the outside, like sophistication. That is the trap closing: a society reorganising around seizing transfers rather than making value, capture breeding capture, until production is the eccentric choice and the gate the centre of the economy.
