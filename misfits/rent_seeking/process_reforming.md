# Process: Reforming

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The gate narrowed so there is no rent to seek, against those who keep it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A waste seen to sit in the prize, and someone willing to remove it.
The recognition that no rent means no chase, and the will to close the discretionary gate.

## Direction

Toward a gate too narrow to dispense privilege.
The licence, the tariff, the favour removed, or the discretion taken out of granting, so capture stops paying and effort turns back to making things.

## Lever

Shrinking the prize, not shaming the seekers.
The seeking ends only when the rent does, so the remedy aims at the grantor's discretion rather than the lobby's behaviour, removing the thing worth chasing instead of asking the rational not to chase it.

## Echo

A cure that works, and a fight to keep it from working.
The gate narrowed and effort returning to production, or the rent defended by its keepers and clients, or the wrong prize cut and the honest reward lost with it.
