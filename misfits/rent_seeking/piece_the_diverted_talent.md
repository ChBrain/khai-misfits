# Piece: The Diverted Talent

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The misallocation: the ablest hands pulled from production to capture.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the crossroads](place_the_crossroads.md), where talent picks the road to the gate.
Where the returns steer the able toward the slices.

## Load Bearing

Without it the waste would be only the money spent seeking, and the pie would still grow.
With it the loss reaches the future: the same ability that could build a firm or a technology is spent winning transfers instead, so the wealth those gifts would have created is never made, and the pie grows slower for want of the hands that turned to slicing it. The Diverted Talent is load bearing because it is the allocation-of-talent finding (Baumol), the deepest cost of rent-seeking, that a society's ablest go where the rules reward them and rent-rich rules reward capture, so the true price is not the fortune burned but the wealth never built.

## Apparent

Smart people making smart choices.
Talented individuals prospering at law, lobbying, and finance-for-the-favour looks like merit rewarded, so the diversion reads as success, and the loss of what those same people would have created is invisible, because a firm not founded leaves no trace.

## Yearbook

Chosen by [the convert](persona_the_convert.md) through [diverting](process_diverting.md), pulled by [the rent](piece_the_rent.md) that out-earns the workshop.
It is the future cost: the Diverted Talent deepens [the rent trap](piece_the_rent_trap.md), each able defection making capture the more obviously the place to be.
