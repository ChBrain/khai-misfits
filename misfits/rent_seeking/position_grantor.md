# Position: Grantor

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The seat that holds the gate and dispenses the privilege.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The discretionary power to grant licences, tariffs, monopolies, favours.
The courting that a valuable, freely given privilege attracts.
The ability to charge for granting, and to charge for withholding a harm.

## Orders

Keep the gate discretionary.
Dispense the privilege to those who make their case.
Be courted, and grant more as more is sought.

## Loses

Nothing, and gains by every rent it dispenses.
The value it gives away draws the scramble and the value it keeps is the power itself, so its discretion manufactures the waste and profits from it, and it need not be corrupt to have no reason to stop.
The one seat that would lose by closing the gate, and so the last to close it.

## Drives

Toward a gate worth courting,
away from any reform that would narrow it,
so the standing source of the rents is the seat with no interest in ending them.
