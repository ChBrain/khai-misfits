# Persona: The Reckoner

## Taxonomy

[Reckoner](position_reckoner.md). The one who tallies the dissipation, and finds the prize consumed in the chase.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Cool, numerate, counting what others celebrate.
Adds up not the winner's prize but the total spent by all the contestants to win it.
Sees that the sum of the seeking approaches the whole of the transfer, and makes nothing.

## Action

Counts the waste, contestant by contestant.
Totals the lobbyists, the lawyers, the years in the queue, and sets the figure against a transfer that only moved value, never made it.
Names the loss no ledger books, because it hides in effort that looks like enterprise.

## Shadow

Seeing the waste clearly changes nothing, because each expense in it was rational.
The seeker was right to spend, the grantor right to grant, so the dissipation is built entirely of sound decisions, and the reckoning proves the loss without dissolving a single reason for it.
The one who understands the waste best is the least able to stop anyone from adding to it.

## Tell

"Count what they all spent to win it, not what the winner won."
The finding that turns a fair contest into a measure of what the fight burned.
