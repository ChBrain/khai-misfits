# Persona: The Producer

## Taxonomy

[Producer](position_producer.md). The one who creates value, and watches capture out-earn creation.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent, productive, quietly proud of making things.
Builds a better product, a lower cost, a thing that was not there before.
Believes, reasonably, that creating value is how one is meant to prosper.

## Action

Grows the pie, and is out-earned by those who fight over its slices.
Keeps making things while the rent-seeker next door wins more by capturing a privilege than the producer wins by inventing.
Wonders, watching the returns, why he still bothers to build.

## Shadow

His honest creation is punished by the same rules that reward capture.
The rent pays better than the product, so the market that should reward him for enlarging the pie rewards the seeker more for seizing a slice, and his diligence looks, on the ledger, like a poor choice of career.
Not a loser by any fault of his work: the road not taken, undersold by the transfer he refused to chase.

## Tell

"I made something real, and he made more than me making nothing."
The true and galling comparison that tempts the next producer toward the gate.
