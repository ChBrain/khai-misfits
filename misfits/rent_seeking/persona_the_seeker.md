# Persona: The Seeker

## Taxonomy

[Seeker](position_seeker.md). The one who spends real resources to capture the rent.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ambitious, competitive, sharp about a prize.
Sees a privilege worth a fortune sitting in the grantor's gift, and moves to win it.
Reckons, correctly, that a rent worth almost a fortune is worth almost a fortune to secure.

## Action

Lobbies, litigates, queues, and courts the grantor.
Pours money and effort into the winning, outspending the rivals for the same slice.
Captures the transfer, and makes, in all that spending, nothing at all.

## Shadow

Every resource he spends winning the rent is a resource that built nothing.
He is right that the prize is worth chasing, and the chase creates no product, no lower cost, no new thing, only moves a transfer into his hands at a price that approaches the transfer itself, so his rational pursuit is the society's pure loss.
Not a thief: a competitor whose competition consumes the very prize it is for.

## Tell

"There's a fortune in that licence. Of course I'll fight for it."
The true and rational calculation whose sum, across all the fighters, is the fortune burned away.
