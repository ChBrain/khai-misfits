# Pitch: A Slice Worth Fighting For

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Enterprising, competitive, sharp-elbowed.
There is value to be won, so compete for it: invest to secure it, court the one who grants it, outbid the rest.
The voice of ambition that knows a prize when it sees one, spoken by people who are right that the rent is worth chasing and never ask what the chasing makes.

## Undertow

Under the enterprise, a fortune spent to move a fortune from one pocket to another.
The value is already made and merely up for capture, so the effort goes not into growing the pie but into winning a slice, and a prize worth almost a fortune is worth almost a fortune to fight over, so the whole of it is consumed in the fighting.
No one is a thief: they only compete, hard and rationally, for a transfer, and a transfer competed for that hard is a transfer burned away.

## Nerve

The feeling of shrewd, energetic competition,
every lobbyist and lawyer earning their keep,
the ablest hands drawn to the contest over the slices and away from the baking of the pie.

## Echo

After the run: a prize dissipated in the scramble to seize it,
the surest road to riches running through the gate and not the workshop,
and a society that spends itself deciding who gets the wealth instead of making more of it.
