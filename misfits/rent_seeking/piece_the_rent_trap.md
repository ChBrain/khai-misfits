# Piece: The Rent Trap

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The self-reinforcing society: each rent won breeding the next, until capture is the surest path to fortune.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the ministry](place_the_ministry.md), the seekers returned for more.
Where a rent granted teaches the next lobby to come asking.

## Load Bearing

Without it rent-seeking would be an occasional cost, not a way of life.
With it the seeking feeds itself: each rent won proves capture pays, drawing more seekers and more talent, who lobby the grantor for more rents, so the gate widens as it is used and the surest road to fortune becomes the road through it. The Rent Trap is load bearing because it is the rent-seeking society Krueger named (Krueger), the reinforcing loop that turns a discrete waste into a standing order, and the reason the misfit compounds rather than settles, capture breeding capture until production is the eccentric choice.

## Apparent

A thriving industry of advisers and advocates.
The growth of lobbying, law, and the whole apparatus of capture looks like a sophisticated economy maturing, so the trap reads as development, and the reorganisation of a society around seizing transfers passes for its coming of age.

## Yearbook

Deepened by [the seeker](persona_the_seeker.md) and [the grantor](persona_the_grantor.md) together, fed by [the diverted talent](piece_the_diverted_talent.md).
It is the loop that makes it permanent: the Rent Trap is what turns [the rent](piece_the_rent.md) from a prize into a system, capture become the ordinary way to prosper.
