# Piece: The Rent-Seeking

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The phenomenon whole: effort spent seizing transfers instead of creating value, and its remedy in a narrowed gate.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the reform table](place_the_reform_table.md), where the gate is narrowed or defended.
And across the whole economy, wherever a prize is fought for rather than made.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of mechanisms with no shape.
With it the mechanisms become one thing: the gate, the rent, the scramble, the dissipation, the diverted talent, and the rent trap are facets of a single law, that where value can be captured rather than created, rational effort pours into the capture up to the value of the prize, and only shrinking the prize returns that effort to production. The Rent-Seeking is load bearing because it is the whole finding (Tullock; Krueger) with its whole cost attached: it holds the misfit together, and it holds that the cure is a narrowed gate, contested by its keepers and hard to close without cutting the honest reward that makes invention pay.

## Apparent

Either a thriving competitive economy, or simple corruption.
Seen from one side the seeking looks like vigorous enterprise; from the other, like graft to be prosecuted; and both miss that it is neither, a lawful, rational competition for transfers that creates nothing, needing no crime and curable by no arrest, only by removing the prize.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) through [reforming](process_reforming.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: the Rent-Seeking is what [the gate](piece_the_gate.md) produces once the seeking runs, undone only by narrowing the gate, at the risk of cutting the honest return along with the rent.
