# Place: The Ministry

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). Where the privilege is dispensed and the rent is made.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An office with a stamp, a schedule of licences, a tariff to set, a monopoly to award.
The grantor at a desk, and the discretion to say yes or no held in one hand.

## Holds

The gate, and the rents it manufactures.
Here a signature is worth a fortune to whoever receives it, so the place turns an ordinary act of office into a prize, and every discretionary grant is a rent waiting to draw a scramble.

## Offers

A favour worth chasing.
The privilege is real, valuable, and freely in the grantor's gift, so the ministry offers exactly the thing worth spending a fortune to obtain, and invites everyone who wants it to make their case.

## Withheld

Any check on the discretion.
The gate grants at will and answers to no rule that would take the choosing out of it, so nothing here narrows the favour into a right, and the very openness of the discretion is what keeps the rent worth seeking.
