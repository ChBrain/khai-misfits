# Position: Reckoner

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The seat that totals the dissipation and names the loss.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The full set of what every contestant spent to win.
The arithmetic to sum it against a transfer that only moved value.
No lever to lower any single expense, since each was rational.

## Orders

Count the waste, contestant by contestant.
Set the total spent against the prize won.
Name the loss no ordinary ledger books.

## Loses

Its use, though never its accuracy.
The sum it computes is correct and inert, because every expense in it was a sound decision, so the reckoning proves the dissipation and dissolves not one reason for it.
The clearest eye on the waste is the one that can least prevent the next addition to it.

## Drives

Toward the true total of the contest,
which indicts no seeker,
and shows only that the fight for the transfer consumed the transfer.
