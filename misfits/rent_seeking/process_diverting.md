# Process: Diverting

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). Talent flowing from making the pie to fighting over its slices.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Returns higher at the gate than in the workshop, and talent free to follow them.
Able people reading the payoffs, and a rent that pays better than any product.

## Direction

Toward the ablest hands spent on capture.
The talent that could build a firm or a technology turns instead to law, lobbying, and the favour, because that is where the reward now is.

## Lever

Payoffs that reward seizing over making.
Talent flows where the rules point it, and the rules point at the gate, so the diversion needs no one to choose badly, only to choose rationally, and the rational choice is capture.

## Echo

A pie that grows slower, worked by fewer.
The best drawn to the contest over the slices, creation left to those the returns did not tempt, and the wealth those gifts would have built simply never made.
