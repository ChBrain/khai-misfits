# Position: Defector

## Taxonomy

[Rent-Seeking](play_rent_seeking.md). The seat that abandons production for capture, following the returns.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The talent that could grow the pie.
The clear sight of what that talent now pays, and where.
The freedom to spend its gifts on the slice or on the baking.

## Orders

Follow the money.
Turn the talent toward capture, where the returns are highest.
Leave the workshop for the gate.

## Loses

Nothing for itself, and the pie the talent would have grown.
Its gifts are real and its choice is rational, but spent on seizing rather than making they redistribute and create nothing, so the seat prospers and the society loses the wealth those gifts would have built.
Allocated exactly as the payoffs dictate, and the payoffs point away from production.

## Drives

Toward the highest return,
away from the making of things,
so the ablest hands go where the rules reward them, which is the contest over the slices.
