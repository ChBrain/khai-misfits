# Persona: The Rebuilder

## Taxonomy

[Engineer](position_engineer.md). The reversal: rebuilds from cause.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Stubborn, curious, unsatisfied.
He wants the mechanism, not the shape.
He asks how, not who.

## Action

He strips the ceremony back.
He finds what actually joins practice to result.
He builds that and drops the empty rites.

## Shadow

The cargo comes and the comfort goes.
His fix costs the easy legitimacy the ritual paid.
It costs the look of doing things properly.

## Tell

"Forget how they do it. What makes it work?"
Then he takes the ritual apart.
