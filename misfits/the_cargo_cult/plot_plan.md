# Plot: Plan

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The runway](place_the_runway.md), bare, and a team under pressure to make something land.
No one is sure what causes the success they are chasing, so they look to a model that plainly worked, and decide to build what worked there, here.

## Action

[Imitation](process_imitation.md): [The Copyist](persona_the_copyist.md), holding [the adopter's seat](position_adopter.md), takes the visible shape of a proven model and sets out to reproduce it.
Alongside, [The Patron](persona_the_patron.md) at [the sponsor's seat](position_sponsor.md) rewards the resemblance, funding the team for working the way the winners work.

## Stage

[The Copyist](persona_the_copyist.md) tables [Copy the Best](plan_copy_the_best.md): adopt the model's form entire, because under uncertainty copying a demonstrated winner is the safe and sensible bet.
[The Patron](persona_the_patron.md) tables [Reward the Look](plan_reward_the_look.md): credit the team for the adoption itself, so [the legitimacy](piece_the_legitimacy.md) arrives on [the form](piece_the_form.md) alone, before any result is in.

## Tension

Nothing here is foolish.
Copying a proven model when you cannot see what made it work is a reasonable wager, and being paid in trust for doing the recognised thing is a real and immediate return. But the wager takes [the form](piece_the_form.md) and leaves [the substance](piece_the_substance.md), because the substance was never visible to take, and the reward lands on the resemblance and asks the resemblance for nothing, so the sensible first move builds a shape that looks like success and need never become it.
