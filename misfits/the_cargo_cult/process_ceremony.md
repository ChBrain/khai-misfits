# Process: Ceremony

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The rite enacted.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A form adopted, now needing to be enacted.
The copy sits waiting to be performed.
Someone must carry it out.

## Direction

Toward the ritual kept to the letter.
On schedule, watch by watch.
Every rite done in its turn.

## Lever

The ceremony feels like work.
Because it feels like work, it is taken for work.
Effort and result are quietly confused.

## Echo

Perfect motion, faithfully performed.
Detached from any result.
The rite complete, the cargo still gone.
