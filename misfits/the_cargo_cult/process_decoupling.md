# Process: Decoupling

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The join loosened.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A ritual that must be displayed but cannot deliver.
The form is required, the result is not coming.
Something has to give.

## Direction

The ceremony up front, the work behind.
The two set on separate tracks.
Neither made to answer to the other.

## Lever

Loosen the join between show and substance.
Then the show survives whatever happens behind it.
The display floats free of the outcome.

## Echo

An empty ritual, made survivable.
It answers to no outcome now.
It can go on forever, landing nothing.
