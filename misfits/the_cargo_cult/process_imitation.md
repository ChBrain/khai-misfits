# Process: Imitation

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). Copying the winner.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A team unsure what causes success.
They look at one that worked.
They study its surface for the answer.

## Direction

Toward the model's visible form.
That form, reproduced here, faithfully.
The look brought across, line for line.

## Lever

Under uncertainty, the copy is the safe bet.
Nobody is blamed for following a proven winner.
Resemblance stands in for understanding.

## Echo

A faithful shape rises here.
The cause stays on the model's own runway.
What was copied was never the reason.
