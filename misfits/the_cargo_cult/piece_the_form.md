# Piece: The Form

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The visible shape of a success, the part that can be copied.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the runway](place_the_runway.md), in everything a watcher could see and write down.
The outward shape of a working thing: the standup and the board, the framework and its ceremonies, the org chart, the ritual a winning team was seen to keep. All of it on the surface, all of it copyable.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to imitate and no cult to build.
With it there is a shape to reproduce that looks exactly like success, because the Form is the whole of what an outsider can observe: not the judgement behind the ritual but the ritual, not the trust behind the ceremony but the ceremony. It is copyable precisely because it is visible, and visible is the one thing it shares with the cause it stands in for.

## Apparent

The success itself.
It looks like the thing that worked, because it is the face the thing that worked presented to the world. From outside there was never any other version to see, so copying the Form feels like copying the success, and the likeness is perfect right up to the part that matters.

## Yearbook

Copied by [the copyist](persona_the_copyist.md), performed by [the ritualist](persona_the_ritualist.md), scored by [the inspector](persona_the_inspector.md).
It is the half the whole play turns on: set against [the substance](piece_the_substance.md) it cannot carry, the Form is what gets adopted, displayed, and audited, while the cause it left behind is the reason [the empty runway](piece_the_empty_runway.md) stays empty.
