# Persona: The Screen

## Taxonomy

[Decoupler](position_decoupler.md). The one who keeps the display up, the work behind.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Pragmatic, smooth, unbothered.
He manages the audience with ease.
What they see is his craft.

## Action

He keeps the ceremony on show up front.
He gets the real work done, or not, out of sight.
The front stays clean whatever happens behind.

## Shadow

Parting the shown from the done lets an empty ritual survive.
His pragmatism shields the form from its own emptiness.
Nothing tests the display, so nothing corrects it.

## Tell

"Give them the ceremony they expect."
Behind it, whatever actually happens.
