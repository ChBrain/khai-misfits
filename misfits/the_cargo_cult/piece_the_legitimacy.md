# Piece: The Legitimacy

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The acceptance the form buys, whether or not it works.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the window](place_the_window.md), collected from every watcher who nods at a familiar shape.
The credit that comes from looking right: the board reassured that the team works the way the winners do, the peers who recognise the framework, the auditor who ticks the known ritual. Approval earned by resemblance.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no reward for the form and the copying would not pay.
With it the copying pays at once, because the Legitimacy is a real return the form earns before any result is in: a team that adopts the recognised model is credited with competence for the adoption itself, and that credit buys funding, cover, and trust regardless of whether the cargo ever lands. It is why the sensible move is to copy the proven shape, and why the shape need never prove itself, since it is already paid.

## Apparent

Deserved trust.
It looks like earned confidence, the reasonable regard owed to a team that does things properly. And it is not fraud: the resemblance is genuine and the recognition is sincere, which is exactly why the Legitimacy flows so freely and asks the form for nothing more.

## Yearbook

Granted by [the patron](persona_the_patron.md) under [reward the look](plan_reward_the_look.md), and spent to keep [the ritual](piece_the_ritual.md) funded.
It is the engine that makes the cult rational: because [the form](piece_the_form.md) collects the Legitimacy on sight, the reckoning that would have sent anyone back to [the substance](piece_the_substance.md) never arrives, and the empty runway is paid for anyway.
