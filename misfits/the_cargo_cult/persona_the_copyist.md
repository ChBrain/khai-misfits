# Persona: The Copyist

## Taxonomy

[Adopter](position_adopter.md). The one who adopts the proven form.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent, prudent, does his homework.
He looks to what works and follows it.
Nothing invented, nothing risked.

## Action

He studies the model closely.
He reproduces its form entire.
He sets it running here, same as there.

## Shadow

Copying under uncertainty is the safe bet.
The safe bet takes the shape and leaves the cause.
So his prudence builds a husk.

## Tell

"It works for the best in the business."
So he built what he could see of it.
