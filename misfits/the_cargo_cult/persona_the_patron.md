# Persona: The Patron

## Taxonomy

[Sponsor](position_sponsor.md). The one who rewards the look.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Reassured, invested, at ease.
He likes what he recognises.
The familiar shape settles him.

## Action

He funds the team for working the way the winners work.
He credits the resemblance.
He signs off on the likeness.

## Shadow

Paying for the look makes the form worth more than the function.
His trust is what the cult runs on.
The money follows the shape, so the shape is all it grows.

## Tell

"Good, you are doing it like the best."
He stops asking after that.
