# Persona: The Inspector

## Taxonomy

[Auditor](position_auditor.md). The one who audits fidelity to the form.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Thorough, fair, unhurried.
He scores against the manual.
Every box checked, every line read.

## Action

He ticks the ritual against the model.
He finds it faithful in every part.
He passes it, clean and stamped.

## Shadow

He measures the form, not the cargo.
A clean audit certifies an empty runway.
His fairness makes the hollow official.

## Tell

"Fully compliant with the model."
The planes are still not coming.
