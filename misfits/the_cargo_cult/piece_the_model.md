# Piece: The Model

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The proven exemplar being imitated.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the runway](place_the_runway.md), held up as the thing to become.
The success everyone points to: the famous team, the market leader, the company whose way of working is written up and admired. The airbase the islanders watched, from which the cargo actually came.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no direction to copy in, and the imitation has no target.
With it the copying has an authority behind it, because the Model is proof the form can work: it did work, there, once, and that fact is what makes reproducing its shape feel safe. Under uncertainty about what causes success, imitating a demonstrated winner is a rational bet, and the Model is the winner that makes the bet look sound. What it cannot supply is the reason it won, which stayed on its own runway.

## Apparent

A recipe.
It looks like a recipe to follow: do what they did and get what they got. The trap is that the Model shows only its outputs and its outward practices, never the conditions that joined the two, so the recipe is missing the step that cannot be written down.

## Yearbook

Chosen by [the copyist](persona_the_copyist.md), invoked by [the patron](persona_the_patron.md) as the standard to match.
It is where the play starts: the Model's [form](piece_the_form.md) is what gets taken and its [substance](piece_the_substance.md) is what gets left, and the gap between the two is the distance from the Model's full runway to the copy's empty one.
