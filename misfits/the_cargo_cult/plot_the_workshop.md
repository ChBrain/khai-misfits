# Plot: The Workshop

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The workshop](place_the_workshop.md), and someone who has stopped asking whether the ritual is faithful.
The audits keep passing and the runway keeps empty, so at last a different question is put: not is this like the model, but what actually made the model's cargo come.

## Action

[Reconstruction](process_reconstruction.md): [The Rebuilder](persona_the_rebuilder.md), holding [the engineer's seat](position_engineer.md), strips the ceremony back to its mechanism and rebuilds the practice from the cause up, keeping only what does real work.

## Stage

[The Rebuilder](persona_the_rebuilder.md) tables [Rebuild from Cause](plan_rebuild_from_cause.md): dig past the form to [the substance](piece_the_substance.md), find what actually joins the practice to the result, and build that, dropping every rite that turns out to carry nothing.
[The empty runway](piece_the_empty_runway.md) begins to take deliveries, and [the cargo cult](piece_the_cargo_cult.md) is named at last: a faithful copy that worked only once it was torn back to the thing the copy had left out.

## Tension

The cargo comes, and the comfort goes.
Rebuilding from cause works, and it costs: the familiar ceremony is broken, the easy [legitimacy](piece_the_legitimacy.md) the ritual paid on sight is spent, and the team that was praised for doing things properly now looks, for a while, like it is doing them wrong. It trades the safety of the recognised form for the risk of a practice that only earns its keep by working, and that trade is real and unprotected ... or the ceremony is too comfortable and too well rewarded to give up, the doubt is filed once more, and the runway stays swept, and empty, and certified.
