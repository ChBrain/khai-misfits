# Piece: The Cargo Cult

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The whole shape at once, and its costly cure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Over [the runway](place_the_runway.md) entire, from the copied form to the rebuilt cause.
The phenomenon named: a faithful reproduction of the visible shape of a success, kept and displayed and rewarded, that does not work, because the thing that made the original work was never on the surface to copy.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a heap of ceremonies with no shape.
With it the play has its thesis: the failure needs no fool and no fraud, only a proven model, a visible form, an invisible cause, and a sensible team copying what it can see under pressure to succeed. It is also, and this is the part the scorn usually skips, curable: strip the ceremony back to the mechanism, ask what actually joins the practice to the result, and rebuild from cause, so the Cargo Cult carries its own remedy, which is not more fidelity to the form but a return to the substance the form was always standing in for.

## Apparent

Someone else's silly mistake.
It looks like a thing only fools fall into, islanders and other teams, never us, since we are following a model that really worked. That confidence is the cult's best defence: naming the pattern is easy and seeing it in your own swept runway is hard, because from inside it never looks like a cult, only like doing things properly.

## Yearbook

Broken, when it is broken, by [the rebuilder](persona_the_rebuilder.md) through [reconstruction](process_reconstruction.md), at the price of the easy legitimacy the ritual paid.
It is the piece all the others resolve into: [the form](piece_the_form.md) copied without [the substance](piece_the_substance.md), kept by [the ritual](piece_the_ritual.md), parted from work by [the decoupling](piece_the_decoupling.md), and paid by [the legitimacy](piece_the_legitimacy.md), makes the Cargo Cult, and only a costly return to cause unmakes it.
