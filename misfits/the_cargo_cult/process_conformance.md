# Process: Conformance

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The review of the form.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A review, after a season with no cargo.
The books must still be squared.
Someone comes to check the work.

## Direction

Toward fidelity to the model.
The form scored, the form passed.
The copy weighed against its original.

## Lever

Measure the form, which can be ticked.
Not the result, which cannot be faked.
So the review scores what it can count.

## Echo

A clean report, signed and filed.
An empty runway underneath it.
Full marks, and no cargo at all.
