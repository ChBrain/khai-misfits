# Persona: The Doubter

## Taxonomy

[Skeptic](position_skeptic.md). The one who asks where the planes are.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Plain, unwelcome, unmoved.
He keeps count of what lands.
He counts, and the count stays at zero.

## Action

He points at the empty runway.
He asks what the ceremony produces.
He is heard once, then set aside.

## Shadow

He is right and off-book.
The question that would break the cult is the one it cannot hear.
So his doubt is filed and forgotten.

## Tell

"But where is the cargo?"
Nobody wants that asked at the review.
