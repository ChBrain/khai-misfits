# Plot: Legitimacy

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The window](place_the_window.md), the recognised form on show, and an audience that knows it when it sees it.
The team is working the way a proven winner works, and the people who fund and judge it can see the resemblance plainly, which is the moment the resemblance starts to pay.

## Action

[Legitimation](process_legitimation.md): [The Patron](persona_the_patron.md), holding [the sponsor's seat](position_sponsor.md), credits the team for adopting the recognised form and funds it on sight, before any cargo has landed.

## Stage

[The Patron](persona_the_patron.md) tables [Reward the Look](plan_reward_the_look.md): back the resemblance to [the model](piece_the_model.md), because working the way the winners work is taken as its own return.
So [the legitimacy](piece_the_legitimacy.md) is collected up front, on the form alone, and the form is paid whether or not it ever produces, which is exactly why it is never made to.

## Tension

The recognition is sincere, and it is the trap.
The Patron is not fooled and is not foolish: the resemblance is genuine, the model really did work, and rewarding a team for doing things the proven way is ordinary good judgement. But paying on the look means the look never has to become the result, so the legitimacy that should have been the reward for landing cargo becomes the reward for building the runway, and a form that is already paid has no reason left to work.
