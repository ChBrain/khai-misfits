# Persona: The Ritualist

## Taxonomy

[Ceremonialist](position_ceremonialist.md). The one who performs the ceremony faithfully.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Devoted, punctual, exact.
He keeps the rite to the letter.
Not a step missed, not a step early.

## Action

He runs the standup, the retro, the ceremony.
On schedule, in order, every time.
The calendar is full and the form is kept.

## Shadow

The ceremony feels like work and stands in for it.
His devotion keeps a motion that lost its cause.
The rite runs on, hollow at the center.

## Tell

"We never skip the ceremony."
They never ask what it is for.
