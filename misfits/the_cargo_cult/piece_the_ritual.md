# Piece: The Ritual

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The ceremony performed for its own sake.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the tower](place_the_tower.md), enacted on schedule, in the proper order.
The ceremony kept as an end in itself: the standup held because standups are held, the retro run because retros are run, the artifact produced because the model produces one. The wooden headphones worn at the bamboo controls, exactly as the airmen wore theirs.

## Load Bearing

Without it the form is inert, a shape no one enacts, and nothing happens either way.
With it the form is lived, performed daily and sincerely, and that sincerity is the trap, because the Ritual feels like work and stands in for it. Once the ceremony is the measure of diligence, keeping it well is proof enough, and whether the ceremony still connects to any outcome stops being asked. The Ritual is motion faithful to the form and detached from the cause, and the more devoutly it is kept the less anyone notices the detachment.

## Apparent

Discipline.
It looks like rigour and good practice, a team that shows up and keeps the ceremony without fail. That is exactly how it reads from inside: not empty motion but professionalism, the reassuring proof that things are being done properly.

## Yearbook

Performed by [the ritualist](persona_the_ritualist.md), kept on display by [the screen](persona_the_screen.md), passed by [the inspector](persona_the_inspector.md).
It is the form in action: the Ritual runs [the ceremony](process_ceremony.md) to the letter while [the decoupling](piece_the_decoupling.md) quietly parts it from the real work, so the show is perfect and the cargo, all the same, does not come.
