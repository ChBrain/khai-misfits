# Plot: Decoupling

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The tower](place_the_tower.md), built to the letter, and the ceremony ready to run.
The form is chosen, so now it is enacted: the ritual kept on the day, in the proper order, for anyone who cares to watch.

## Action

[Ceremony](process_ceremony.md): [The Ritualist](persona_the_ritualist.md), holding [the ceremonialist's seat](position_ceremonialist.md), performs the ritual faithfully, exactly as the model performs it.
[Decoupling](process_decoupling.md): [The Screen](persona_the_screen.md) at [the decoupler's seat](position_decoupler.md) keeps the ceremony on show at [the window](place_the_window.md) while the real work goes on, or does not, in [the back room](place_the_back_room.md).

## Stage

[The Ritualist](persona_the_ritualist.md) tables [Run the Ritual](plan_run_the_ritual.md): keep [the ritual](piece_the_ritual.md) to the letter, sincerely and on schedule, so the ceremony feels like the work and stands in for it.
[The Screen](persona_the_screen.md) tables [Keep Up Appearances](plan_keep_up_appearances.md): hold the display steady for the audience while [the decoupling](piece_the_decoupling.md) quietly parts the shown from the done, so the show can stay flawless whatever happens behind it.

## Tension

Everyone is doing exactly what the model does, and nothing is arriving.
The ritualist is diligent and sincere, the screen is only being pragmatic, keeping the required ceremony up front and getting the real work done however it gets done, and both are acting well. But a ceremony kept for its own sake is motion without the cause it once served, and a display parted from the work answers to no result, so the form runs perfectly and produces nothing, and the perfection is what hides the nothing.
