# Piece: The Substance

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The invisible cause that made the form work, the part not copied.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), if anywhere, because it is nowhere on the surface.
The thing that actually made the model succeed: the judgement that chose when to break the ritual, the trust the ceremony rested on, the years of fit between a practice and the exact problem it grew up solving. Real, decisive, and invisible.

## Load Bearing

Without it the Form is a husk, and the runway stays empty however faithful the copy.
With it the model worked, because the Substance is the causal mechanism the Form only wraps: it is what the ceremony was for, not the ceremony. And it is exactly what a copy cannot take, because you cannot see it to copy it, so the more faithfully the visible shape is reproduced, the more completely the one thing that made the shape matter is left behind.

## Apparent

Nothing at all.
It looks like nothing, because it never showed: the watcher who copied the model saw the standup, not the judgement in the room, saw the framework, not the trust that let it flex. The Substance is invisible by nature, which is why it is skipped without anyone deciding to skip it.

## Yearbook

Missed by [the copyist](persona_the_copyist.md), asked after by [the doubter](persona_the_doubter.md), rebuilt at last by [the rebuilder](persona_the_rebuilder.md).
It is the other half of the split: absent from [the form](piece_the_form.md) that gets copied, it is what [reconstruction](process_reconstruction.md) must dig back to, and its absence is the whole reason the faithful replica does not work.
