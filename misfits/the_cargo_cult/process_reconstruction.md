# Process: Reconstruction

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The reversal, form back to cause.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone who asks what actually makes cargo come.
Not what the winners look like: what they do.
The question the ceremony never allowed.

## Direction

The ceremony torn back to its mechanism.
Function rebuilt from cause.
Each rite tested for what it truly moves.

## Lever

Keep only the practices that join to a result.
Drop the rest, however familiar.
Fidelity to the form gives way to fidelity to the cause.

## Echo

The cargo lands.
The easy legitimacy is spent.
What is left has to earn its standing now.
