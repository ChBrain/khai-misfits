# Pitch: By the Book

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Earnest, procedural, proud.
Everyone is doing it properly, the way the best do it, the framework followed to the letter and the ceremony kept on the day.
The voice of a team that has read the manual and means to honour it, spoken by people who take real comfort in doing the recognised thing the recognised way.

## Undertow

Under the diligence, an empty runway.
The form is faithful and the cargo does not come, because the form was the visible husk of a success and the thing that made it work stayed behind, uncopied and unseen.
No one is careless: they follow the model exactly, and following it exactly is the whole mistake, because exactness copies the shape and never the cause.

## Nerve

The feeling of doing everything right,
the checklist complete, the ritual clean, the model honoured to the letter,
standing on a swept runway that no plane has any reason to visit.

## Echo

After the run: a perfect replica that does not work,
a ceremony everyone can pass and nothing can explain,
and a team that did it exactly by the book and waits, still, on the cargo.
