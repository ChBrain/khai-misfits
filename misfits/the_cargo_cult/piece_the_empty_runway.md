# Piece: The Empty Runway

## Taxonomy

[The Cargo Cult](play_the_cargo_cult.md). The faithful replica that produces nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the runway](place_the_runway.md), swept and marked and waiting, with nothing overhead.
The result that is no result: the framework fully adopted and the delivery no faster, the ceremony flawless and the product no better, the org redrawn to the model and the same problems exactly where they were. The strip is perfect. No plane is coming.

## Load Bearing

Without it the cult could claim a win and the play would have no wound.
With it the wound is plain and still deniable, because the Empty Runway is the outcome the form cannot fake: the cargo either lands or it does not, and here it does not. Yet the emptiness accuses no one, since the ceremony passed its audit and the resemblance earned its credit, so the failure floats free of any failing act, a runway that did everything right and received nothing.

## Apparent

Bad luck, or early days.
It looks like patience owed, not a verdict: the form is sound, the model proved it, surely the results are just late. That reading is what keeps the runway swept, because a faithful copy that has not yet paid off is always one more season from paying off, and the season never comes.

## Yearbook

Inherited by everyone on the strip, questioned only by [the doubter](persona_the_doubter.md) who asks where the planes are.
It is where the play would rest without a fix: past [the decoupling](piece_the_decoupling.md) and under [the legitimacy](piece_the_legitimacy.md) that pays anyway, the runway stays empty until someone digs back to [the substance](piece_the_substance.md) that would fill it.
