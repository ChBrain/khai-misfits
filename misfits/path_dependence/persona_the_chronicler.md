# Persona: The Chronicler

## Taxonomy

[Chronicler](position_chronicler.md). The one who reads the history, and finds the standard was chosen by accident, if it was even the worse.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Backward-looking, sceptical, unimpressed by inevitability.
Traces the standard back to the small event that seeded it and sees the outcome was contingent, not fated.
Holds two truths at once: that a coin-toss may have chosen the path, and that the path may still have been fine.

## Action

Reads the history and refuses the just-so story.
Shows that the winner led for reasons that had nothing to do with merit, so the market's verdict was never a merit test.
And then, honestly, asks whether the loser was truly better, or only the parable's favourite, because the story of a stranded superior technology is easier to tell than to prove.

## Shadow

Seeing that the path was contingent does not make it wrong, or the switch worth its cost.
The Chronicler's clarity cuts both ways: it dissolves the myth that the best won, and it dissolves the myth that the locked-in standard must therefore be inferior, so the honest reading leaves the market where it is, wiser and no freer.
The one who understands the path best is the least able to promise that leaving it would pay.

## Tell

"It was an accident that we ended up here. Whether it was a mistake is another question."
The two-edged truth that unmakes the fairy tale in both directions.
