# Process: Locking

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The lead hardening into a standard no one can leave alone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A base large enough that value lives in it, and investment sunk to match.
Users with tools, training, and files all built around the standard, and a better option they cannot reach by themselves.

## Direction

Toward a standard held by the cost of leaving it.
The switch becomes a loss for anyone who makes it alone, so each user stays, and the staying of each is a reason for the rest to stay, until the option is fixed in place.

## Lever

A switching cost payable now against a gain that needs everyone.
Moving alone strands a user from the base and wastes the sunk investment, so the rational move is to wait for the others, and since every user waits, no one goes and the lock holds.

## Echo

An inferior standard kept without anyone choosing to keep it.
The option locked in by the sum of individual costs to leave, the better challenger stranded outside it, and an exit that only the whole crowd, moving at once, could take.
