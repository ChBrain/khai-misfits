# Process: Compounding

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). Increasing returns turning an early lead into a self-reinforcing standard.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A leader with the early edge, and adopters who value the base.
The plain fact that the more who share a choice the more worth sharing it becomes.

## Direction

Toward a lead that widens on its own.
Each adopter picks the leader for its support and enlarges that support, and the option grows cheaper and better by being made and used the most, so the edge feeds itself.

## Lever

Value that rises with the base, through the network and the learning curve.
A bigger crowd means more parts, more training, more compatibility, and lower cost, so the leader improves precisely by leading, and every rational choice for it makes the next one more rational still.

## Echo

A standard pulling away from every rival.
The lead now self-reinforcing, the base too large to take one user at a time, and an accident hardening into the option everyone will build around.
