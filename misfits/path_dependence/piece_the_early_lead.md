# Piece: The Early Lead

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The compounding advantage: the head start that self-reinforces into a standard.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the crowd](place_the_crowd.md), widening with every adopter.
Where the gap between the leader and the rest grows on its own.

## Load Bearing

Without it an early advantage would be a transient thing that a better option could later erase.
With it the advantage is not transient but self-feeding: the network and the learning curve turn each unit of lead into more lead, so the leader pulls away and a rival cannot catch up by being good, only by somehow taking a base that grows faster than it can. The Early Lead is load bearing because it is the positive feedback that makes the outcome path-dependent (Arthur), the reason a small starting edge becomes a decisive one, and the bridge between the accident that seeded it and the lock-in that ends it.

## Apparent

The market efficiently converging on a winner.
The widening gap looks like everyone sensibly agreeing on the best option, so the compounding of an accident reads as the wisdom of the crowd, and the lead's momentum passes for merit confirmed.

## Yearbook

Grown from [the small event](piece_the_small_event.md) through [compounding](process_compounding.md), fed by [the network](piece_the_network.md) and [the learning curve](piece_the_learning_curve.md).
It is the accident magnified: the Early Lead hardens into [the lock-in](piece_the_lock_in.md), a standard too far ahead to be caught.
