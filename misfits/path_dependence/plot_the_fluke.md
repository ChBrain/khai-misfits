# Plot: The Fluke

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The fork](place_the_fork.md), two roughly even options and a small event about to tip between them.
A jammed typewriter, a studio's deal, a wartime rush: a cause with nothing to say about which option is better.

## Action

[Seeding](process_seeding.md): [The Adopter](persona_the_adopter.md), holding [the adopter's seat](position_adopter.md), picks the option the small event nudged ahead, while [The Chronicler](persona_the_chronicler.md), holding [the chronicler's seat](position_chronicler.md), notes that nothing about the choice was a test of merit.

## Stage

[The Adopter](persona_the_adopter.md) tables [Bet on the Base](plan_bet_on_the_base.md), joining the option that got the first users, so [the small event](piece_the_small_event.md) hands one way [the early lead](piece_the_early_lead.md).
[The Chronicler](persona_the_chronicler.md) tables [Read the History](plan_read_the_history.md) and marks the contingency, that at this moment either path was reachable and an accident chose.

## Tension

The whole future turns here, on something that had no right to decide it.
Nothing is wrong: the options are close, the event is trivial, the adopter picks reasonably. That is the trap seeded, not sprung: a lead handed to one option for a reason unrelated to its quality, at the one moment the choice was still open and cheap, with the compounding that will make the accident look like destiny not yet begun.
