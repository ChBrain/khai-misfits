# Place: The Fork

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The early moment where the path could still go either way.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Two roughly even options, and a small event about to tip the balance between them.
A jammed typewriter, a studio's deal, a wartime rush: a cause with no bearing on which option is better.

## Holds

The contingency, before it hardens.
Here the outcome is still open and cheap to change, and here the accident does its work, handing one option a lead that merit did not earn and history will magnify.

## Offers

A genuine choice, briefly.
For this one moment either path is reachable at little cost, so the fork looks like a fair, open contest that the best option could still win.

## Withheld

A second chance at neutrality.
Once the small event tips the balance and the compounding starts, the fork closes behind the crowd, and the even choice it offered will never come again at this price.
