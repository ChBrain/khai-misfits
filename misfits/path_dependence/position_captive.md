# Position: Captive

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The seat locked into the standard, that cannot switch alone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An investment sunk into the standard: tools, training, files, habits.
A clear preference for the better option, and no way to reach it by itself.
A switching cost payable in full now, for a gain that comes only if everyone moves.

## Orders

Weigh switching alone against staying.
Count the sunk investment and the stranding from the base.
Wait for the others, and until then, stay put.

## Loses

The better standard, to a cost only the crowd could pay.
Moving alone would strand it from the base and waste all it has sunk, so it rationally stays, and its staying is one more reason the next captive stays too.
Kept in place not by preference but by the price of acting on it first.

## Drives

Toward the better option it will not reach,
away from a solo switch that costs more than it yields,
so a crowd that would each prefer to move keeps itself exactly where it is.
