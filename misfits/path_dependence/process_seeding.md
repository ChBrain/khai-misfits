# Process: Seeding

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). A small event handing one option the early lead, with no bearing on merit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Two roughly even options, and an accident that tips between them.
A jammed typewriter, a studio's deal, a wartime rush: a cause that decides the lead and does not weigh the merit.

## Direction

Toward a head start for one option, unearned.
The balance tips, one way gets the first users, and the choice that a moment's chance made is the choice history will build on.

## Lever

Contingency at a point of near-indifference.
When the options are close, a trivial event can decide which leads, because there is little on the merits to resist it, so the outcome turns on the accident and not the analysis.

## Echo

A leader chosen by history, not by test.
One option ahead for a reason that had nothing to do with being best, and a lead now ready for the compounding that will make the accident look like destiny.
