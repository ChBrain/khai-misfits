# Place: The Crowd

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). Where adopters gather, and each arrival makes the leader lead more.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A growing base around one option: the parts, the trained hands, the ecosystem, the reviews that say everyone uses it.
Newcomers joining the largest gathering, and the gathering swelling with each one.

## Holds

The compounding, in motion.
Here the value of the choice rises with the number who share it, so the crowd is not just large but self-enlarging, each adopter both drawn by the base and adding to it.

## Offers

The practical choice, plainly marked.
The biggest base is the safe bet, well supported and cheap, so the crowd shows every newcomer exactly where the sensible option is, and it is right.

## Withheld

A hearing for the better option.
The crowd rewards the shared choice, not the good one, so a superior standard with no base is invisible here, passed over by adopters who are correct to want the support it cannot offer.
