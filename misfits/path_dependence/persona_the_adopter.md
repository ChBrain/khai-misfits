# Persona: The Adopter

## Taxonomy

[Adopter](position_adopter.md). The one who picks the option others already use, and by picking it makes it likelier still.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Practical, prudent, allergic to orphans.
Chooses the standard with the parts, the trained hands, the ecosystem, the safe bet.
Cares less which option is better than which one everyone else has already backed.

## Action

Bets on the base, and enlarges it.
Picks the leader because the leader has the support, and his choice adds one more reason for the next adopter to do the same.
Reads popularity as practicality, correctly, and takes the crowd for the merit.

## Shadow

His soundest choice is what selects the standard, not its quality.
The value of an option really does rise with its base, so backing the biggest is right, and every right choice compounds a lead that a small accident, not merit, began, until the crowd he joined is a rut he cannot leave.
Not a follower by weakness: a rational chooser in a market where the popular choice is the correct one and the correct one need not be the best.

## Tell

"Why would I pick the one nobody else uses?"
The reasonable question that, asked by everyone, locks in whatever got ahead first.
