# Place: The Rut

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The worn groove of the locked-in standard, deep and hard to climb out of.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The standard built into everything: the tools shaped to it, the training that assumes it, the files and habits that only it can read.
A track worn so deep by use that the wheels no longer need steering to follow it.

## Holds

The lock-in, made of everyone's investment.
Here the standard is held in place not by anyone's wish to keep it but by the sum of each user's cost to leave, so it is stable precisely because it is inconvenient to abandon.

## Offers

Smooth going, for anyone already in it.
Within the rut everything fits: the parts, the people, the formats all match, so staying is easy and cheap, and the groove rewards the crowd that wore it.

## Withheld

An exit any one can take.
The rut can be left only by everyone climbing out at once, so a lone user who tries is stranded from the base and out of pocket, and the depth that makes the track smooth is the depth that makes it a trap.
