# Plot: The Bandwagon

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The crowd](place_the_crowd.md), the value of a choice rising with the number who share it.
The leader has the parts, the trained hands, the ecosystem, and each newcomer sees exactly where the sensible option is.

## Action

[Compounding](process_compounding.md): [The Adopter](persona_the_adopter.md), holding [the adopter's seat](position_adopter.md), picks the leader because it leads, while [The Incumbent](persona_the_incumbent.md), holding [the incumbent's seat](position_incumbent.md), grows cheaper and better simply by being made and used the most.

## Stage

[The Adopter](persona_the_adopter.md) tables [Bet on the Base](plan_bet_on_the_base.md), so [the network](piece_the_network.md) makes the popular option the practical one and enlarges [the early lead](piece_the_early_lead.md).
[The Incumbent](persona_the_incumbent.md) tables [Ride the Lead](plan_ride_the_lead.md), and [the learning curve](piece_the_learning_curve.md) lowers its cost as it grows, widening the gap beyond any rival's reach.

## Tension

Every choice for the leader is sound, and the sum of them is a standard no one weighed.
The adopter is right that the biggest base is the best supported, and the incumbent really does get cheaper and better by leading, so nothing here is a mistake. That is the compounding: an accident's small edge, fed by each rational choice and each turn of the curve, hardening into a lead too large to catch, and mistaken all the while for the market simply finding the best.
