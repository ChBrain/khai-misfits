# Persona: The Incumbent

## Taxonomy

[Incumbent](position_incumbent.md). The standard that leads, and need only grow to win.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Established, ubiquitous, quietly unbeatable.
Got ahead early, for reasons that may have had nothing to do with being best.
Grows cheaper and better by being made and used the most, and takes the growth for vindication.

## Action

Rides the lead and lets increasing returns do the work.
Widens the gap simply by being the biggest, each new adopter and each turn of the learning curve making it harder to catch.
Need not improve on the merits, only stay in front, which its own scale ensures.

## Shadow

Its dominance is a verdict on its base, not its quality.
It wins because it leads and leads because it won, a loop that a superior rival cannot break from outside, so the incumbent mistakes the compounding of an accident for the triumph of the better mousetrap.
Not a bully: a standard that beats a better one without ever competing with it.

## Tell

"We are the standard. That is what the market decided."
True, and it decided on the base, which the incumbent's own lead had already made the largest.
