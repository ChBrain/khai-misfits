# Place: The Table

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). Where a switch is coordinated, or judged not worth its cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A standards body, a platform's roadmap, a mandate with a date, a converter that bridges old and new.
The one place a scattered market can be made to move together, and the bill for moving it laid on the table.

## Holds

The coordination, and its price.
Here the switch a single user could never make becomes possible, the whole base crossing at once, and here too the cost is counted and the destination chosen, perhaps well and perhaps not.

## Offers

A way out of the rut.
What no captive could do alone, the table can do for all at once, paying the switching cost together and stranding no one, so the better standard finally becomes reachable.

## Withheld

A free or a certain escape.
The move must be funded, the chosen winner may be steered by interest or simply wrong, and the honest question sits at the table unanswered: whether the old standard was ever bad enough to be worth the leaving.
