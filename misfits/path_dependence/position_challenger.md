# Position: Challenger

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The seat of the superior option, arriving without a base.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A plainly better standard, superior on every merit.
Nothing else: no installed base, no ecosystem, no crowd.
The cruel arithmetic that its value needs adopters and its adopters need value.

## Orders

Win on the merits.
Show that it is better, and expect better to be enough.
Offer the world a superior standard and wait for the world to take it.

## Loses

The contest it should win, for lack of the base it cannot start.
Its excellence is real and cannot be spent, because a standard is worth its crowd and it arrives with none, so it is stranded not by defeat but by having no one to matter to.
Beaten by a worse option that had only the head start.

## Drives

Toward a merit that goes unrewarded,
away from a base it can never bootstrap,
so the better technology is locked out by a market that pays for the shared choice, not the good one.
