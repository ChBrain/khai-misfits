# Process: Coordinating

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The whole crowd moved at once, escaping the rut at a price.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A rut worth leaving, and a mover large enough to shift everyone together.
A sponsor with a mandate, a platform, or a purse, and a bridge that can carry the base across.

## Direction

Toward a synchronised switch that strands no one.
The whole crowd crosses at once, or the old and new are bridged so migration costs nothing sudden, and the better standard becomes reachable because reaching it is no longer a solo loss.

## Lever

A payer big enough to meet a switching cost no single user can.
What a scattered market cannot do, one large mover can, setting the date, mandating the standard, or shipping the converter, and paying together the cost that alone was prohibitive.

## Echo

An escape that is real and never free.
The rut left at a price, the destination chosen and perhaps steered wrong, and the honest doubt trailing behind: whether the old standard was ever bad enough to be worth the leaving.
