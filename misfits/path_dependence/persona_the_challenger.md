# Persona: The Challenger

## Taxonomy

[Challenger](position_challenger.md). The better option, arriving late to a world already standardised.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Superior, well made, plainly the better way.
Wins every comparison on the merits and every review on its face.
Believes, reasonably, that being better should be enough.

## Action

Wins on the merits and loses on the base.
Offers a clearly better standard and finds no one will move to it, because its value waits on adopters and the adopters wait on its value.
Starts with a base of nothing against an incumbent with the world, and cannot buy its way past the gap.

## Shadow

Its excellence is real and irrelevant, because a standard is worth its crowd, and it has none.
The better it is, the crueller the trap: merit was never the currency, the base was, and the base is exactly what a latecomer cannot start, so the superior option is stranded not by defeat but by having no one to be valuable to.
Not outcompeted: locked out, by a market that rewards the shared choice over the good one.

## Tell

"But ours is measurably better. Why won't they switch?"
The true and useless question, because no one gains by switching to a better standard alone.
