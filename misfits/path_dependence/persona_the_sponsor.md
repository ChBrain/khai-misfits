# Persona: The Sponsor

## Taxonomy

[Sponsor](position_sponsor.md). The one big enough to move the whole crowd at once, and escape the rut.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Large, deliberate, able to coordinate what a market cannot.
A standards body, a dominant platform, a state, a buyer big enough that its move moves everyone.
Sees that no user will switch alone and that only a synchronised jump can pay.

## Action

Moves them all at once, or builds a bridge between old and new.
Sets a date, mandates a standard, ships a converter that lets the new work with the old, so the base can cross without anyone stranding.
Performs the coordination the scattered users could not, and pays for it.

## Shadow

The power to move the crowd is the power to pick the winner, and to pick it wrong.
Its jump must be funded, its bridge maintained, and its choice may be steered by interest rather than merit, so the cure carries its own lock-in, a standard chosen on purpose that the market must now live with, and it is worth doing only when the gain beats the very real cost of the move.
Not a liberator with clean hands: a coordinator who trades one settled path for another, at a price.

## Tell

"On this date we all move together, and here is the bridge across."
The one sentence a market cannot say to itself, and the bill that comes with saying it.
