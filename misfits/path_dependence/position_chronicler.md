# Position: Chronicler

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The seat that reads the history, and weighs whether the path was even a mistake.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The record of how the standard came to lead.
The sight to trace it back to a small event, not a merit test.
The honesty to ask whether the road not taken was truly better, or only the tidier story.

## Orders

Read the history.
Show that the winner was selected by accident, not by being best.
Then ask, as fairly, whether the loser was ever the superior option at all.

## Loses

The comfort of a clean moral, in both directions.
Its finding dissolves the myth that the best won and refuses the myth that the locked-in standard must be inferior, so it can indict the market's verdict without proving the switch would pay.
The clearest reader of the path is the one who can least promise that leaving it is right.

## Drives

Toward the contingency under the outcome,
away from every just-so story,
so the history is shown to be an accident, and left an open question whether it was a wrong one.
