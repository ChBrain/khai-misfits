# Pitch: Bet on the Base

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Sensible, forward-looking, allergic to orphans.
Pick the standard everyone supports, the one with the parts and the trained hands and the ecosystem, the safe bet no one is fired for making.
The voice of prudent choice, spoken by people who are right that the popular option is the practical one and cannot see that its popularity, not its merit, is what they are really choosing.

## Undertow

Under the prudence, a whole market picking the leader because it leads.
The more who use a thing the more worth using it becomes, so each adopter joins the biggest base and makes it bigger, and a small old accident that gave one option its head start is amplified by every reasonable choice that follows into a standard no one can leave alone.
No one is foolish: they only each bet on the base, and a base everyone bets on is a rut everyone is stuck in.

## Nerve

The feeling of a smart, safe, modern choice,
every adopter backing the option with the momentum,
the better way passed over not because it lost on merit but because it never had the crowd.

## Echo

After the run: a standard everyone uses and no one would design,
a better technology stranded for want of a base it could never start,
and a rut worn so deep by rational choices that only all of them, turning at once, could climb out of it.
