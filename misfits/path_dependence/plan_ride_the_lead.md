# Plan: Ride the Lead

## Direction

Stay in front and let scale do the competing. Grow cheaper and better simply by being made and used the most, widen the gap with every adopter, and let the growth be its own argument. Do not improve on the merits, only remain the biggest.

## Orders

([The Incumbent](persona_the_incumbent.md) rides increasing returns, winning because it leads and leading because it won.)

## Implementation

([The Crowd](place_the_crowd.md); the [compounding](process_compounding.md) process; [the learning curve](piece_the_learning_curve.md) lowers its cost as it grows, widening [the early lead](piece_the_early_lead.md) beyond any rival's reach.)

## Targets

- [ ] the lead is held by growth, not by improvement
- [ ] scale and the network do the competing
- [ ] the gap widens with every adopter and every turn of the curve
