## Load Bearing

With it the lead begins in an accident: at a point where the options were close, a trivial event with no bearing on quality tipped the first advantage, so the winner was selected by history and not by test. The Small Event is load bearing because it is the contingency at the heart of David's account (David; Arthur), the reason path dependence is not just increasing returns but increasing returns started by chance, and it is what makes the eventual standard an outcome that could have been otherwise.
