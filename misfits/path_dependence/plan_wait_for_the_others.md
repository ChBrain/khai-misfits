# Plan: Wait for the Others

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the captive's seat](position_captive.md), inside [Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Captive](position_captive.md)

## Direction

Weigh switching alone against staying, count the sunk investment and the stranding from the base, and find that moving by yourself costs more than it yields. Prefer the better option, wait for the whole crowd to move to it, and until they do, stay exactly where you are.

## Orders

([The Captive](persona_the_captive.md) would switch gladly if everyone did, and rationally does not go first.)

## Implementation

([The Rut](place_the_rut.md); the [locking](process_locking.md) process; [the switching cost](piece_the_switching_cost.md) makes a solo move a loss, so [the lock-in](piece_the_lock_in.md) is held by every captive's cost to leave.)

## Targets

- [ ] switching alone is weighed and found to cost more than it yields
- [ ] the better option is preferred and left unreached
- [ ] no captive goes first, and each one's staying keeps the next in place
- [ ] the standard is held by the crowd's cost to leave, not its wish to stay
