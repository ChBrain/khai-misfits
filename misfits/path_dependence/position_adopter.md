# Position: Adopter

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The seat that chooses among competing options, and joins the biggest base.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A free choice among the options, and a stake in choosing the practical one.
The plain fact that a standard is worth more the more who share it.
No reason to prefer merit over base, when the base is where the value is.

## Orders

Pick the option with the support.
Back the base, the ecosystem, the safe bet.
Do not be the one left holding the standard nobody else adopted.

## Loses

Nothing by joining the leader, and its say in which option is best.
Its rational choice tracks the size of the crowd, not the quality of the option, so it helps select the standard without ever weighing the standard.
Rewarded for reading the base, never for reading the merit.

## Drives

Toward the largest base,
away from the orphan option however good,
so each sound choice enlarges the lead a small accident began.
