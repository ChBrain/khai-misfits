# Position: Sponsor

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The seat large enough to coordinate the switch a market cannot.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The reach to move the whole crowd at once, or to bridge the old and the new.
A mandate, a platform, or a purse big enough that its move is everyone's.
The one lever that can pay a switching cost no single user can.

## Orders

Move them all at once.
Set the date, mandate the standard, or ship the converter that carries the base across.
Perform the coordination the scattered market could not.

## Loses

Neutrality, and sometimes the bet.
To move the crowd it must choose the destination, and the choice can be steered by interest or simply be wrong, so it trades a settled path for one it picked, at a cost that only a real gain justifies.
The power to free the crowd is the power to lock it in again, deliberately.

## Drives

Toward a coordinated jump,
away from the rut and toward a chosen standard,
so the escape is real and carries its own price and its own risk of picking the worse.
