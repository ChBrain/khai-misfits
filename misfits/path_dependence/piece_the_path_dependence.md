# Piece: The Path Dependence

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). The phenomenon whole: history selecting the standard, and its remedy in a coordinated switch at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the table](place_the_table.md), where the switch is coordinated or declined.
And across the whole market, in the path every rational choice deepened.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of mechanisms with no shape.
With it the mechanisms become one thing: the small event, the network, the learning curve, the early lead, the switching cost, and the lock-in are facets of a single law, that with increasing returns history selects the standard and the selected one need not be the best, and the only escape is a coordinated switch, which must be paid for and may pick wrong. The Path Dependence is load bearing because it is the whole finding (David; Arthur) with its whole honesty attached: it holds the misfit together, and it holds that the remedy is coordination at a price and that the locked-in standard was not always truly the worse.

## Apparent

Either the triumph of the fittest, or a scandal of markets stuck on the worst.
Seen from one side the outcome looks like efficient convergence on the best; seen from the other, like a world trapped on inferior standards everywhere; and both overreach, missing that history selected the path for certain, and whether it selected badly is a question, not a given.

It is the play in a single piece: the Path Dependence is what [the small event](piece_the_small_event.md) becomes once the compounding runs, escaped only by paying [the switching cost](piece_the_switching_cost.md) for the whole crowd at once, if the escape is worth it at all.
