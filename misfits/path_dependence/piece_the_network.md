# Piece: The Network

## Taxonomy

[Path Dependence](play_path_dependence.md). One engine of the compounding: the value of a choice rising with the number who share it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the crowd](place_the_crowd.md), in the parts, the trained hands, the compatibility.
Where each adopter is worth more to the next because they now share a standard.

## Load Bearing

Without it an adopter would weigh options on their own merits and the best could win.
With it the merits are not the whole of the value: a standard is worth more the more who use it, so the biggest base is the most valuable choice regardless of quality, and joining it is both rational and self-enlarging. The Network is load bearing because it is the network externality that makes the popular option the practical one (Katz & Shapiro), the reason betting on the base is correct and the reason the base, once ahead, pulls away.

## Apparent

Just the sensible preference for compatibility.
Wanting the standard others use looks like plain practicality, because it is, so the network effect hides inside ordinary good judgement, and no adopter sees that his practicality is what selects the standard.

## Yearbook

Worked by [the adopter](persona_the_adopter.md) through [compounding](process_compounding.md), one half of the engine beside [the learning curve](piece_the_learning_curve.md).
It is the value in the crowd: the Network builds [the early lead](piece_the_early_lead.md) and strands any challenger that arrives without a base.
