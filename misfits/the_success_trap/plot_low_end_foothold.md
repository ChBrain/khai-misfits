# Plot: Low-End Foothold

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The low end](place_the_low_end.md), the ground the incumbent just costed and declined.
The overshot customers are here, the ones paying for more than they use and glad of a cheaper thing that is finally good enough. Nobody upmarket is watching this tier, because there is no margin in it worth watching.

## Action

[Encroachment](process_encroachment.md): [The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md), holding [the entrant's seat](position_entrant.md), takes the customers the incumbent was right to walk away from, and builds a business on the return the incumbent was right to refuse.

## Stage

[The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) tables [Take the Low Ground](plan_take_the_low_ground.md): serve the overshot cheaply and well, and [the starved seed](piece_the_starved_seed.md) the incumbent could not fund becomes [the low-end foothold](piece_the_low_end_foothold.md) the entrant plants for free.
The foothold is small, low-margin, and beneath notice, which is exactly why it is left alone to grow.

## Tension

The entrant is no genius and the incumbent is no fool.
The Newcomer simply stands where the incumbent's own arithmetic sent it: on the ground a high-margin firm cannot profitably defend, serving customers a high-margin firm cannot profitably keep. There is no raid and no blind spot, only a tier the incumbent vacated for sound reasons and the entrant occupied for equally sound ones, and the foothold that will one day carry the climb is, today, the least interesting business in the market.
