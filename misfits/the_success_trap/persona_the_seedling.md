# Persona: The Seedling

## Taxonomy

[Disruptive Champion](position_disruptive_champion.md). Right too early, and a little exhausted.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Earnest, tired, sure of the ground others miss.
Sees the disruption clearly, before it arrives.
Carries a case the room is not built to hear.

## Action

Tables the low bet every cycle.
Asks for a little funding, a small start.
Is told: next cycle.

## Shadow

The only seat that names the future.
The seat with the weakest number to show for it.
Being right does not clear the hurdle: foresight has no return yet.

## Tell

Says "this will matter" to a room that funds what matters now.
Then stops asking, or leaves.
