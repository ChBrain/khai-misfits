# Piece: The Starved Seed

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md). The bet that would have saved the firm, defunded every cycle.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

On [the back bench](place_the_back_bench.md), alive but never watered.
The disruptive project the firm keeps: not killed, because killing it would be an admission, and not funded, because funding it would fail the hurdle.

## Load Bearing

Without it the firm never saw the disruption coming, and the tragedy is only ignorance.
With it the firm saw the whole thing and chose, correctly, not to feed it: the seed was on the table, championed, understood, and it lost the allocation to a sustaining bet that returned more this year. The misfit is not blindness. It is a firm that funds exploitation over exploration because exploitation always wins the yardstick, and starves the seed with its eyes open.

## Apparent

Prudence.
It looks like the firm sensibly declining to chase a low-margin distraction, keeping a token effort alive in case. The distraction really is low-margin. The token really is enough to say the firm looked.

## Yearbook

Championed by [the seedling](persona_the_seedling.md), refused by [the margin hurdle](piece_the_margin_hurdle.md).
It asks for a little, every cycle, and is told next cycle, every cycle, because a little for an uncertain market always loses to a lot for a certain one. By the time the market is certain, the seed has grown up somewhere else, in a firm that had nothing better to fund.
