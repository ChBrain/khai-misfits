# Persona: The Newcomer

## Taxonomy

[Entrant](position_entrant.md). No sunk cost, and grateful for the ground below.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Unbothered, grateful, light.
No sunk cost to defend.
Lives at the low end, and is glad to.

## Action

Takes the customers the incumbent sheds.
Ships good-enough, for cheap.
Reinvests thin revenue into climbing a tier.

## Shadow

Not a hero, just differently made.
Its values fit the ground the incumbent flees, so it funds the bet the incumbent cannot.
The reckoning is its incentive, not its virtue.

## Tell

Says "worse is fine" and means "for now."
Every year, worse is less worse.
