# Process: Encroachment

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md). The upward station: low end toward the middle.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The foothold's thin revenue.
The first tier, enough to stand on.

## Direction

Upward from below, tier by tier.
Into ground the incumbent is leaving as it climbs.

## Lever

Patient improvement against the incumbent's retreat.
The entrant rising into the room the firm vacated.

## Echo

The climb closing a distance no one is counting.
The gap, quietly gone.
