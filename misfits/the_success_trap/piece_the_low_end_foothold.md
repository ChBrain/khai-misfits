# Piece: The Low-End Foothold

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md). The entrant's beachhead, in the segment nobody defended.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the low end](place_the_low_end.md), among the customers the incumbent was glad to lose.
The unglamorous corner of the market where good enough for cheap is not a compromise but the whole requirement.

## Load Bearing

Without it the entrant has nowhere to stand, and the climb never starts.
With it the entrant has revenue, however thin, and revenue is a place to improve from: it serves the overshot customers who were paying for capability they never used, and it does the one thing the incumbent cannot, it takes them seriously. The foothold is not a threat yet. That is precisely why it is allowed to become one.

## Apparent

A cheap imitation.
It looks like a worse product for worse customers, correctly beneath the incumbent's attention. It is worse. The incumbent's own metrics say so, and its own metrics are why it looks away.

## Yearbook

Planted by [the newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md), on the ground [the rational retreat](piece_the_rational_retreat.md) left open.
It wins the customers the incumbent fled from and is grateful for them, and gratitude funds the next tier. Every year it is still worse, and every year it is less worse, and the incumbent reads only the first half of that sentence.
