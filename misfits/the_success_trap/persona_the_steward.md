# Persona: The Steward

## Taxonomy

[Line Manager](position_line_manager.md). Guardian of the core product, shipping on time.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Proud, competent, dependable.
Ships the release when the release was promised.
The core is in good hands, and it knows it.

## Action

Builds the sustaining version the roadmap asked for.
Adds capability, cycle after cycle.
Raises the product a notch each pass.

## Shadow

Does the job so well it overshoots the middle.
Its excellence opens the door beneath it.
The better it builds, the wider the low end grows.

## Tell

Measures success as capability added.
Never as the customers who needed less.
