# Plot: Carry Voice

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The account review](place_the_account_review.md), where the largest revenue speaks first.
[The best customer](piece_the_best_customer.md) asks for more along the dimension the firm already sells. In [the roadmap room](place_the_roadmap_room.md) the next cycle is about to be set, and nobody has funded the small bet yet.

## Action

[Account management](process_account_management.md): [The Loyal](persona_the_loyal.md), holding [the best customer's seat](position_best_customer.md), asks for more of what already works, and [The Listener](persona_the_listener.md) at [the account lead's seat](position_account_lead.md) carries the demand inside verbatim.
Alongside, in [roadmapping](process_roadmapping.md), [The Seedling](persona_the_seedling.md) at [the disruptive champion's seat](position_disruptive_champion.md) tables the low bet and asks it be funded now.

## Stage

[The Loyal](persona_the_loyal.md) tables [Ask for More](plan_ask_for_more.md): extend the lead on the axis that pays.
[The Listener](persona_the_listener.md) tables [Carry the Voice](plan_carry_the_voice.md): bring the best customer's want in, undiluted, because listening is the firm's virtue.
[The Seedling](persona_the_seedling.md) tables [Get the Seed Funded](plan_get_the_seed_funded.md): the one plan that points away from the margin, and the one with no number to defend itself.

## Tension

Nothing here is a lie.
The demand is real, the revenue behind it is real, and the seed really does return less this year than the roadmap does. The room turns upmarket because that is where the evidence points, and the seed is deferred to next cycle by a firm doing exactly what a good firm should.
