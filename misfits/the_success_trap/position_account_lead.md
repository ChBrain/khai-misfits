# Position: Account Lead

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md). The customer's ear, carried inside.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The customer's ear, and its confidence.
The firm's trust, to speak on the account's behalf.

## Orders

Bring the voice of the customer inside, undiluted.
Hold it against every roadmap, every cycle.

## Loses

The ability to hear customers it holds no account with.
The ones not yet on the books are silent to it,
and silence reads as absence.

## Drives

Toward serving the revenue it can hear.
The louder the account, the truer it sounds.
It calls this listening.
