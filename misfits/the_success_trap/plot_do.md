# Plot: Do

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The capital committee](place_the_capital_committee.md), where every bet is weighed against the hurdle.
The roadmap is locked upmarket and the seed is back from [the back bench](place_the_back_bench.md), on the table asking for its share. The sustaining line is ready to ship.

## Action

[Capital allocation](process_capital_allocation.md) and [portfolio screening](process_portfolio_screening.md): [The Hurdle](persona_the_hurdle.md), holding [the capital gate's seat](position_capital_gate.md), applies the criterion, and [The Steward](persona_the_steward.md) at [the line manager's seat](position_line_manager.md) ships the sustaining release that clears it.
Out of frame, at [the low end](place_the_low_end.md), [The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) at [the entrant's seat](position_entrant.md) takes the ground nobody costed.

## Stage

[The Hurdle](persona_the_hurdle.md) tables [Clear the Hurdle](plan_clear_the_hurdle.md): fund only what returns enough, soon enough, at scale enough, and by [the margin hurdle](piece_the_margin_hurdle.md) the seed does not.
[The Steward](persona_the_steward.md) tables [Protect the Roadmap](plan_protect_the_roadmap.md): ship the release the best customer asked for, and it lands as [the overshoot](piece_the_overshoot.md), more than the middle can use.
[The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) tables [Take the Low Ground](plan_take_the_low_ground.md): serve the overshot customers cheaply, and [the starved seed](piece_the_starved_seed.md) the incumbent refused becomes [the low-end foothold](piece_the_low_end_foothold.md) the entrant plants.

## Tension

The hurdle is fair.
It is applied to the seed exactly as it is applied to everything, and the seed fails it honestly, because a small return from a market that does not exist yet cannot out-argue a large return from one that does. The firm reports a strong quarter, and it is strong, and the entrant is beneath its notice, correctly, for now.
