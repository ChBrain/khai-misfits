# Piece: The Overshoot

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md). The sustaining line climbing past what the mainstream can use.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Along [roadmapping](process_roadmapping.md), on the trajectory the best customer set.
Each release adds performance on the dimension that already sells, faster than the middle of the market can absorb it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the low end stays closed, and there is no room beneath the firm for anyone to enter.
With it the firm sells more capability than most customers need, at a price most customers would rather not pay, and the gap it opens beneath itself is the doorway: the room where good enough for cheap is suddenly a market. The overshoot does not feel like a mistake. It feels like progress, because it is progress, aimed at the wrong tier.

## Apparent

Innovation.
It looks like the firm out-engineering everyone, extending its lead along the axis it has always led on. The lead is real. The customers cheering it are the ones who were already loyal.

## Yearbook

Shipped by [the steward](persona_the_steward.md), for [the best customer](piece_the_best_customer.md) who asked for it.
It is the most honest work in the play and the most dangerous: every increment is wanted by someone, and the sum is a firm selling too much to the top and nothing to the bottom, holding a door open for the one competitor it does not count.
