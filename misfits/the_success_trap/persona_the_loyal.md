# Persona: The Loyal

## Taxonomy

[Best Customer](position_best_customer.md). The largest revenue, and the clearest want.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Satisfied, and demanding in the same breath.
Certain of the axis it already buys along.
The customer the firm is right to serve.

## Action

Asks for more performance on the paying dimension.
Never for cheaper, never for simpler.
Pays, on time, for the better version of what it has.

## Shadow

Its wants become the roadmap, and its wants never point at the low end.
So the firm it funds goes blind exactly where it is most loyal.
It is doing right: it is buying what it needs.

## Tell

Says "make it better" and means more of the same.
Is heard as the whole market's voice.
