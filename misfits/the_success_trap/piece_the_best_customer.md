# Piece: The Best Customer

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md). The pull upmarket, wearing the face of listening.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the account review](place_the_account_review.md), where the largest revenue speaks first.
The one voice the firm is rightly built to hear, asking for more of the dimension it already buys.

## Load Bearing

Without it the firm has no reason to climb, and the roadmap could point anywhere.
With it the climb has a direction and a defence: the best customer wants more performance along the axis that already pays, and serving that want is not a mistake, it is good business, which is exactly why it cannot be refused.

## Apparent

Customer focus.
It looks like the firm at its healthiest, closest to the market and responsive to it. The demand is real and the revenue is real and the satisfaction is real.

## Yearbook

Voiced by [the loyal](persona_the_loyal.md) and carried in by [the listener](persona_the_listener.md).
It never asks for the cheap thing, because the cheap thing is beneath it, and the firm hears the whole market in the one customer that pays the most. The disruption never shows up in this room, because the people who want it are not yet worth listening to.
