# Persona: The Allocator

## Taxonomy

[Chief Executive](position_chief_executive.md). Steward of the whole, doing everything right.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Disciplined, accountable, above reproach.
Does the responsible thing, each time.
The board sees a steady hand, and it is.

## Action

Allocates to the biggest sure return.
Retreats the mix upmarket as margins improve.
Is rewarded for it, and keeps going.

## Shadow

Every rational allocation walks the firm further from the seed.
Improving numbers confirm the path, right up to the edge.
Discipline itself is the steering into the trap.

## Tell

Reads a record margin as proof of health.
When it is the sound of the low end being shed.
