# Position: Capital Gate

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md). The criterion, and the veto behind it.

## Owner

## Has

## Orders

Fund what clears the bar.
Defer what does not, and apply it evenly to all.

## Loses

and zero never clears the bar.

It is fairest exactly where it is most wrong.
