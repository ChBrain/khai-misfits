# Piece: The Success Trap

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md). The accumulated whole: good management as the engine of failure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

The gap between the two climbs.
The incumbent climbs the margin upward and away; [the climb](piece_the_climb.md) comes up from the low end to meet it. The Success Trap is the standing distance between them, and the certainty that it closes.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a run of sound decisions and one unlucky competitor.
With it those are one object: a firm whose competence is its cause of death, because every yardstick that made it excellent, serve the best customer, fund the best margin, clear the hurdle, hold the discipline, is a yardstick that points away from the seed. Held whole by nobody, because the seat that defends the margin is never the seat that walks the low end, and no seat is paid to hold both.

## Apparent

A well-run firm.
From every desk inside it, the firm is doing everything right, and it is. The trap is invisible from within precisely because there is no error to find: no lie, no laziness, no missed memo, only good management, working perfectly, toward the wrong ground.

## Yearbook

The margin held the top: [the best customer](piece_the_best_customer.md), [the margin hurdle](piece_the_margin_hurdle.md), [the rational retreat](piece_the_rational_retreat.md), each correct at its rung.
The low end held the future: [the starved seed](piece_the_starved_seed.md) refused, [the low-end foothold](piece_the_low_end_foothold.md) taken, [the climb](piece_the_climb.md) rising.
[The newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) never had to be smarter than the incumbent. It only had to fund the bet the incumbent was too disciplined to make. Nobody had a name for the trap until the market was already gone.
