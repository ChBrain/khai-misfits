# Pitch: Sound Management

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Competent, prudent, well run.
Every decision clears its hurdle and every hurdle is defended with a number.
The voice of a firm that is winning its market, spoken by people who are each, correctly, doing their jobs.

## Undertow

Under the discipline, a floor rising.
Each allocation is rational and each rational allocation walks the firm one step further upmarket.
The margin never falls; the ground beneath the firm only narrows.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching a firm do everything right,
funding its best customers and its best returns,
while something cheap and unloved gets a little better every year.

## Echo

After the run: a market lost all at once,
a disruption that was visible the whole time and never worth funding,
and a chain of decisions in which no single one was wrong.
