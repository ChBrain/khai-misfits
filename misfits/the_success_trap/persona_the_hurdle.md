# Persona: The Hurdle

## Taxonomy

[Capital Gate](position_capital_gate.md). Keeper of the criterion, unmoved by enthusiasm.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fair, rigorous, the same to everyone.
Enthusiasm does not move it.
It holds the bar, and the bar is honest.

## Action

Applies the one criterion to every bet.
Funds what returns, defers what does not.
Asks the numbers, and reads them straight.

## Shadow

The bar cannot price a market with no revenue yet.
So it rejects the seed honestly, and calls it discipline.
Correct arithmetic, run on the wrong horizon.

## Tell

Asks "what is the return, and how sure."
Of a thing whose whole point is that neither is knowable yet.
