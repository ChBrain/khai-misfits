# Plot: Check

## Taxonomy

[The Success Trap](play_the_success_trap.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The annual report](place_the_annual_report.md), where the year is read back as a number.
The entrant's foothold has improved a tier. The incumbent has ceded that tier and moved its mix upmarket, and the margins are the best they have ever been.

## Action

[Encroachment](process_encroachment.md): [The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) climbs one rung, and the incumbent does the sensible thing and retreats above it.
[The Allocator](persona_the_allocator.md), holding [the chief executive's seat](position_chief_executive.md), reads improving margins and allocates further toward them, while [The Loyal](persona_the_loyal.md) at [the best customer's seat](position_best_customer.md) applauds a firm serving the top of the market better every year.

## Stage

[The Allocator](persona_the_allocator.md) tables [Allocate to the Return](plan_allocate_to_the_return.md): put the money where the margin is fattest, and by [the rational retreat](piece_the_rational_retreat.md) that means shedding exactly the ground under attack.
[The Loyal](persona_the_loyal.md) tables [Ask for More](plan_ask_for_more.md) again, and is served again.
Beneath them, unread, [the climb](piece_the_climb.md) closes the distance one unremarkable tier at a time.

## Tension

The numbers really do improve.
Every retreat raises the average, because it drops the least profitable business, which is the business the entrant just took. The firm is congratulated for discipline at the exact moment discipline is walking it off the edge, and the applause is sincere, because from the top the year looks like the best one yet.
