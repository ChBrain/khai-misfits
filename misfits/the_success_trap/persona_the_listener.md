# Persona: The Listener

## Taxonomy

[Account Lead](position_account_lead.md). The customer's advocate, seated inside.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Attentive, trusted, close to the account.
Its virtue is hearing the customer perfectly.
It carries the room's respect, earned.

## Action

Brings the demand in undiluted.
Wins the roadmap toward what the account said.
Translates revenue into direction, faithfully.

## Shadow

Hears the loudest revenue as the market.
Customers not yet worth money are simply inaudible.
Perfect listening, aimed at the one signal that pays.

## Tell

Repeats the best customer's words as strategy.
Has no words for the customers it holds no account with.
