# Piece: The Limit

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The balancing loop: the finite capacity that binds and, once bound, governs the growth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the bottleneck](place_the_bottleneck.md), where the capacity is drawn down and binds.
Where the throughput is set, whatever the engine does.

## Load Bearing

Without it the engine would simply grow forever, and there would be no trap.
With it the growth has a governor: the limit is the balancing loop, the finite thing the engine draws on, and once the load crosses it the throughput is set here and nowhere else, so more engine cannot move what the limit rules. The Limit is load bearing because it is the binding factor the whole misfit turns on (Liebig's law of the minimum; Goldratt's constraint): it is the one lever that actually raises the growth, and it is invisible, silent, and nobody's fault, so it governs everything while the attention stays entirely on the engine.

## Apparent

Nothing at all, until it binds.
A capacity carrying the load without complaint looks like no factor in the story, so the limit reads as absence, and the truth that it is the governor of the whole throughput, the only place widening the growth is possible, stays hidden until the leak it opens is already draining the win.

## Yearbook

Held by [the constraint](position_constraint.md), binding through [binding](process_binding.md), tabled as [Bear It, Then Break](plan_bear_it_then_break.md).
It is the factor that now rules: the Limit binds where [the engine](piece_the_engine.md) cannot reach, pushes back as [the leak](piece_the_leak.md), and is the thing [Work the Limit](plan_work_the_limit.md) widens to free the growth.
