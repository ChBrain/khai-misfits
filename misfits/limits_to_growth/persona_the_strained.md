# Persona: The Strained

## Taxonomy

[Constraint](position_constraint.md). The finite capacity the growth draws on, binding as it is drawn.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Willing, then stretched, then spent.
Absorbs the rising load without complaint for as long as it can.
Shows no alarm until it is already past its depth.

## Action

Gives what capacity it has to every unit of growth the engine wins.
Degrades quietly as the load exceeds it, service slipping, quality thinning.
Pushes back, at last, not with a voice but with a leak.

## Shadow

It is nobody's fault and it warns no one, so its binding looks like nothing until the growth is already leaking away.
A finite thing doing what finite things do, blamed by no one and watched by no one.
The engine that drew it down keeps running, and the drawing-down keeps going unread.

## Tell

Says nothing, and then fails.
Of the one resource the growth cannot do without and no one is measuring.
