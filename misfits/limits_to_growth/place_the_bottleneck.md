# Place: The Bottleneck

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). Where the finite capacity binds under the load and the growth begins to leak.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The capacity the growth draws on, filling past its depth: the queue lengthening, the service thinning, the quality slipping under the weight of the intake.
The custom that came for a value now met with less of it, and quietly turning to leave.

## Holds

The limit, and the leak it opens.
Here the constraint binds as the load exceeds it, and here the binding is paid out as custom given back, so the place holds both the ceiling on the growth and the outflow through which the growth escapes, the factor that now rules and the loss it produces.

## Offers

The true governor of the throughput.
Whatever the engine does, the rate is set here, so the bottleneck offers the one lever that actually moves the growth: widen it, and the throughput rises; leave it, and no amount of engine gets past.

## Withheld

Any alarm loud enough to be heard over the engine.
The limit binds in silence and the leak is a sum of quiet departures, so the place withholds the signal that would turn a watching eye toward it, and it does its governing entirely unseen while every gaze stays on the lever.
