# Plot: The Relief

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The rethink](place_the_rethink.md), someone looking past the engine at the thing it draws on.
The proposal is to stop working the loved lever and put the effort where the growth actually binds, or to ease the engine before the next limit is reached.

## Action

[Relieving](process_relieving.md): [The Widener](persona_the_widener.md), holding [the widener's seat](position_widener.md), moves the effort onto the constraint, while [The Restrainer](persona_the_restrainer.md), holding [the restrainer's seat](position_restrainer.md), counsels pacing the engine to what the capacity can carry.

## Stage

[The Widener](persona_the_widener.md) tables [Work the Limit](plan_work_the_limit.md) and answers [limits to growth](piece_limits_to_growth.md) by widening [the limit](piece_the_limit.md) instead of driving [the engine](piece_the_engine.md).
[The Restrainer](persona_the_restrainer.md) tables [Ease Off Early](plan_ease_off_early.md), trading present growth against a bind not yet proven.

## Tension

The one move that frees the growth is the one the success never taught, and it cannot prove itself in advance.
Widening the limit raises the throughput where driving the engine cannot, but it means telling a winning team to leave its winning lever and feed a thing not visibly broken, so the correct leverage sounds like retreat. And easing early guards against the hard bind only by risking the symmetric error, a growth given up to a ceiling that was never there, since the same delay that fooled the driver will not tell the restrainer which wall is real. It closes with the growth breathing again, or the reflex holding, or an engine eased on a limit that would not have bound.
