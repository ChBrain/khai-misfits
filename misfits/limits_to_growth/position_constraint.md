# Position: Constraint

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The seat of the finite capacity that binds as the growth draws it down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A finite capacity, and the whole of the growth leaning on it.
The power to govern the throughput, once it binds, that no amount of engine can override.
No voice, only a threshold, crossed quietly and with a delay.

## Orders

Carry the load while there is room.
Bind when the load exceeds the capacity.
Push back, when it does, through the throughput and not through any warning.

## Loses

Nothing it was watching for, because no one was watching it.
It is nobody's fault and it raises no alarm, so it binds unseen while every eye stays on the engine, and its pushback is read as the engine's dip.
It sets the ceiling on everything and is the one thing no one is measuring.

## Drives

Toward the bind,
against an attention fixed entirely on the lever,
so the factor that actually rules the growth is the factor no one is looking at.
