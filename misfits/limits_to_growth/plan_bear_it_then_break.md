# Plan: Bear It, Then Break

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the constraint's seat](position_constraint.md), inside [Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Constraint](position_constraint.md)

## Direction

Carry the load while there is room, and give no sign while you can still cope. When the growth exceeds the capacity, bind, and let the throughput govern itself down. Push back not with a warning but with a leak, and let it land late, after the cause is out of sight.

## Orders

([The Strained](persona_the_strained.md) bears the rising load in silence and then binds, governing the growth from a place no one is watching.)

## Implementation

([The Bottleneck](place_the_bottleneck.md); the [binding](process_binding.md) process; [the limit](piece_the_limit.md) binds under the load and pushes back as [the leak](piece_the_leak.md), late and unattributed to the engine that caused it.)

## Targets

- [ ] the load is carried without alarm until the capacity is exceeded
- [ ] the limit binds and governs the throughput regardless of the engine
- [ ] the pushback lands as a leak, not a warning
- [ ] it arrives late, after the drawing-down is out of sight
