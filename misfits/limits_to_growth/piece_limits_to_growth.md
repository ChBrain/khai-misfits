# Piece: Limits to Growth

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The phenomenon whole: a reinforcing engine meeting a balancing limit through a delay, and its remedy in the limit at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the rethink](place_the_rethink.md), where the effort turns to the limit or the reflex holds.
And across both loops, in every push of the engine and every quiet drawing-down of the constraint.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of pushes and leaks with no shape.
With it the moves become one law: the engine, the limit, the delay, the harder push, the leak, and the stall are facets of a single structure, in which a reinforcing loop that built the growth meets a balancing loop that governs it, joined by a delay that hides the second from the first, so a stall born of the limit is read as a shortfall of the engine and answered with more engine that only widens the leak. Limits to Growth is load bearing because it is the whole finding with its whole remedy attached (Senge's archetype; Meadows on where leverage sits): the only move that frees the growth is to work the limit, not the engine, or to ease the engine before the limit binds, a leverage the record hides and the reflex resists, and one that cannot know in advance which limit will bind.

## Apparent

Either an engine that has run out of road, or a team that just needs to push harder.
Seen from one side the growth is simply over and the lever is spent; from the other, the stall is a failure of will curable by more force; and both miss the structure, that the engine is fine and a limit is binding, so neither surrender nor redoubled effort answers it, only moving the work to the constraint, which is the one place no one is looking and the one place the growth can still rise.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the widener](persona_the_widener.md) through [relieving](process_relieving.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: Limits to Growth is what [the engine](piece_the_engine.md) meets when [the limit](piece_the_limit.md) binds behind a delay, undone only by widening the limit or easing the engine, at the cost of a lever the success never taught and a wall that will not show until it is hit.
