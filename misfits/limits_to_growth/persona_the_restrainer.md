# Persona: The Restrainer

## Taxonomy

[Restrainer](position_restrainer.md). The one who eases the engine before the limit binds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Patient, wary of momentum, watching the horizon for the ceiling.
Wants to slow the engine while it is still winning, so the limit is met gently and not hit hard.
Trades some growth now against a bind avoided later.

## Action

Counsels easing the lever before the capacity runs short.
Paces the growth to what the constraint can carry, leaving room before the leak opens.
Chooses a smaller, steadier climb over a fast one that stalls.

## Shadow

It cannot always know which limit will bind or when, so easing early can mean giving up real growth to a ceiling that was never there.
The same delay that hides the limit from the driver hides it from the restrainer, only in reverse.
Prudent against a bind that may not come, and, when it does not, simply the one who left the win on the table.

## Tell

Says "ease off before we hit it."
Without being able to prove the wall is there until the engine has already run into it.
