# Persona: The Driver

## Taxonomy

[Driver](position_driver.md). Owner of the engine, and of the reflex to work it harder.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Driven, capable, sure of the lever.
Knows exactly which move made the growth, and reaches for it without hesitation.
When the numbers slow, works harder, not differently.

## Action

Drives the reinforcing engine, and drives it harder the moment it stalls.
Spends more on the lever that has always paid.
Reads a flat line as a call for more of the same.

## Shadow

Its whole competence is one engine, so it cannot see a stall that more engine will not cure.
When the limit binds, it does the rational, proven thing, and the proven thing does nothing.
The better the engine worked, the harder the reflex, and the harder the reflex, the wider the leak.

## Tell

Answers every slowdown with "push."
Of a system where the thing that binds is no longer the thing it can push.
