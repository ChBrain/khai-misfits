# Position: Trade

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The seat of the custom that flows in through the engine and out through the limit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A need, and the freedom to take it elsewhere.
The value the engine promised, for as long as the capacity supplies it.
No loyalty to the total it is counted in, only to the value it came for.

## Orders

Come in through the engine while the value is there.
Meet the service the strained capacity actually delivers.
Leave, quietly, when the value is gone.

## Loses

The value it was won for, and, on the way out, is not even counted.
It is tallied as growth on arrival and slips away untallied, so the engine claims it as a win while the limit takes it back, and no single exit is loud enough to be an alarm.
The growth that is real at the door is the growth the limit quietly reclaims.

## Drives

Toward the value,
against a capacity that can no longer supply it,
so the intake the engine celebrates becomes the leak the limit opens.
