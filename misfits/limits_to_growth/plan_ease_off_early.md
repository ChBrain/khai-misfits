# Plan: Ease Off Early

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the restrainer's seat](position_restrainer.md), inside [Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Restrainer](position_restrainer.md)

## Direction

Slow the engine before the capacity runs short, so the limit is met gently and never hit hard. Pace the growth to what the constraint can carry, leave room before the leak opens, and take the smaller steady climb over the fast one that stalls. Pay a little growth now against a bind avoided later.

## Orders

([The Restrainer](persona_the_restrainer.md) eases the lever ahead of the limit, trading present growth for a gentler meeting, unable to be sure the wall is there.)

## Implementation

([The Rethink](place_the_rethink.md); the [relieving](process_relieving.md) process; easing [the engine](piece_the_engine.md) before [the limit](piece_the_limit.md) binds forestalls [the stall](piece_the_stall.md), at the risk of a ceiling that was never there.)
