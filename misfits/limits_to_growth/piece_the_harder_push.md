# Piece: The Harder Push

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The reflex: more engine applied when the limit, not the engine, is what binds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the engine room](place_the_engine_room.md), where the stall is answered with more force.
Where the proven move is aimed, harder, at a factor it cannot shift.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a mere ceiling, hit once and understood.
With it the ceiling becomes a trap: the harder push is the reflex to drive the engine harder the moment it stalls, and because the binding factor has moved to the limit, the extra force wins nothing and only pulls more intake into a bucket that leaks faster. The Harder Push is load bearing because it is where the growth is spent: it turns a benign limit into active waste, converts effort straight into a wider leak, and is read, when it fails, not as the wrong lever but as not enough of the right one, so it renews itself out of its own failure.

## Apparent

Redoubled commitment.
A team putting more force behind the move that has always worked looks like resolve and seriousness, so the harder push reads as doing what it takes, and the truth that it is force aimed at a factor it cannot move, effort poured into the leak, stays hidden behind the look of a team refusing to slow down.

## Yearbook

Made by [the driver](persona_the_driver.md) through [driving](process_driving.md), the reflex [Drive the Engine](plan_drive_the_engine.md) enacts at the stall.
It is [the engine](piece_the_engine.md) worked past the point it helps: the Harder Push feeds [the leak](piece_the_leak.md) instead of the growth, and reads its own failure as a call for still more of itself.
