# Plot: The Flogging

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The engine room](place_the_engine_room.md), the reflex now in full force.
The lever is worked harder than ever, more spend going out to win back the flattening growth, against a limit that has already bound and cannot be moved from here.

## Action

[Driving](process_driving.md) at its peak: [The Driver](persona_the_driver.md), holding [the driver's seat](position_driver.md), pours more force into the engine, while [The Churned](persona_the_churned.md), holding [the trade's seat](position_trade.md), keeps arriving through the intake and leaving through the leak.

## Stage

[The Driver](persona_the_driver.md) tables [Drive the Engine](plan_drive_the_engine.md) at full pitch and makes [the harder push](piece_the_harder_push.md), aiming the proven move at a factor it cannot shift.
[The Churned](persona_the_churned.md) tables [Come for the Value](plan_come_for_the_value.md) again, drawn in faster and given back faster, so [the leak](piece_the_leak.md) only widens.

## Tension

Great effort is spent and the net does not move, and the failure is read as too little effort.
The harder the engine is driven the more intake pours into a bucket leaking faster than it fills, so the force converts straight into a wider leak, and the growth stays flat while the room exhausts itself. And because the move is the one that always worked, its failure looks like a shortfall of the move, so the flogging renews itself out of its own futility, more engine called for by the failure of more engine.
