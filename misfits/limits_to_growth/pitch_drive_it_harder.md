# Pitch: Drive It Harder

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Driven, proven, sure of the lever.
The engine has always delivered, so when it slows the answer is obvious and the whole room agrees on it: drive it harder.
The voice of a team that is winning, spoken by people who know exactly which move made them win.

## Undertow

Under the momentum, a limit binding.
The engine draws on a capacity that is running short, and the growth it wins is quietly leaking back out, so the lever that reads as the answer is aimed at the one thing it can no longer move.
The stall is not a dip. The binding factor has changed, and the reflex has not.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching a team pour more into the winning lever,
sure the slowdown is temporary because it always was,
while the thing that is actually short gets no attention at all.

## Echo

After the run: great effort spent and the net unmoved,
a limit that was binding the whole time and never the thing looked at,
and a failure read, to the last, as not enough of the engine.
