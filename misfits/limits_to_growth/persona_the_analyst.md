# Persona: The Analyst

## Taxonomy

[Analyst](position_analyst.md). Keeper of the record, reader of the stall as a dip.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful, empirical, guided by the trend.
Holds the history of the engine, every slowdown it drove through before.
Trusts the record, and the record says the engine recovers.

## Action

Logs the growth and, when it flattens, calls it a dip.
Points to the past recoveries as the base rate for this one.
Waits for the engine to do again what it has always done.

## Shadow

The pushback is delayed, so the data it trusts is a picture of a system that no longer exists.
Its base rate is honest and its inference is sound, and both are drawn from before the limit bound.
So it reads the one stall that will not recover exactly like all the ones that did.

## Tell

Says "it is only a dip, it always comes back."
Of the first slowdown whose cause is a limit and not the engine.
