# Persona: The Widener

## Taxonomy

[Widener](position_widener.md). The one who works the limit instead of the engine.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Quiet, contrarian, pointed at the bottleneck.
Unmoved by the engine's record, interested only in what actually binds.
Says the growth is not short of engine, it is short of the thing the engine draws on.

## Action

Finds the constraint and spends the effort there, widening what binds.
Leaves the winning lever alone and pours the work into the unglamorous capacity behind it.
Grows the throughput by growing the bottleneck, not the intake.

## Shadow

Its counsel is to stop working the proven lever and feed a thing not visibly broken, so it is right and unpersuasive at once.
The engine is loved and the limit is invisible, so the room hears "stop winning" where it means "win where it counts."
Correct about where the leverage sits, and standing exactly where no one is looking.

## Tell

Says "widen the limit, not the engine."
To a team whose every instinct and every past win says drive the engine.
