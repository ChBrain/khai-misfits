# Position: Restrainer

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The seat that eases the engine before the limit is reached.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A wariness of momentum, and an eye on the ceiling ahead.
The move to pace the growth to what the capacity can carry, so the limit is met gently.
A cost paid now, some growth given up, against a bind avoided later.

## Orders

Ease the engine before the capacity runs short.
Pace the climb to the constraint, leaving room before the leak opens.
Take the smaller steady growth over the fast one that stalls.

## Loses

The growth it forgoes, when the wall it braced for was not there.
The same delay that hides the limit from the driver hides it from this seat too, so it cannot always know which ceiling binds or when, and easing early against a bind that never comes is simply leaving the win unclaimed.
Prudent when the limit is real, and merely timid when it is not, and it cannot tell in advance which it is being.

## Drives

Toward a gentle meeting with the limit,
against a future it cannot read cleanly,
so the caution that would avoid the hard bind risks the symmetric error of the bind that never was.
