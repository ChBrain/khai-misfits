# Process: Misreading

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The stall read as a dip, because the delay keeps the limit out of the numbers.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A flat line, and a record of every flat line that climbed again.
At [the dashboard](place_the_dashboard.md), the growth curve levelling while the pushback is still in transit, unseen in the data.

## Direction

Toward a confident wrong diagnosis.
The stall is read against a history from before the limit bound, matched to the dips the engine drove through, and called the same, so the reading lands on a temporary slowdown and prescribes the move that always worked, more engine.

## Lever

An honest inference on data the delay has made obsolete.
Because the limit is not yet in the numbers and the base rate is genuinely sound, the misreading is not carelessness but correct reasoning on a system that no longer exists, so the more rigorous the read, the surer the wrong call.

## Echo

A stall renamed a dip, and the reflex cleared to fire.
The record invoked, the limit unseen, and the engine asked, with full confidence, for exactly the force that will not help.
