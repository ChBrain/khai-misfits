# Place: The Engine Room

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). Where the growth lever is worked, and worked harder when it slows.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The engine at full effort: more spend going out, more custom coming in, the lever pushed by a confident hand.
When the intake flags, the same hand pushing harder, sure that the answer to less growth is more of what made the growth.

## Holds

The engine, and the reflex to drive it harder.
Here the reinforcing loop is worked, and here, when it stalls, more force is put behind it, so the place holds both the lever that made the success and the reach for it that spends it, the proven move and the proven move overused.

## Offers

The feeling of decisive response.
The lever is being worked, the effort is visible, the move is the one that has always paid, so the engine room offers the certainty of doing the proven thing, hard, right when the numbers ask for reassurance.

## Withheld

Any purchase on what actually binds.
The engine is the one thing this room can push, and the limit is not in it, so however hard the lever is worked the place withholds the factor that now governs the growth, and more force here only feeds the leak elsewhere.
