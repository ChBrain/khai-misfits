# Place: The Dashboard

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). Where the numbers are read, and the stall shows up late and misnamed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The growth curve flattening, the intake still counted at the door, the record of every past dip laid beside this one.
A reading being made: the line has been here before and always climbed again, so this is called a dip, and the engine is asked for more.

## Holds

The delay, and the misreading it produces.
Here the pushback has not yet arrived in the data, and here the flat line is read against a history from before the limit bound, so the place holds both the lag that hides the cause and the honest inference that turns the stall into a call for more engine.

## Offers

A confident diagnosis, backed by the record.
The numbers show a familiar shape and a sound base rate, so the dashboard offers a reading everyone can trust: a temporary dip, cured by the move that has always cured it, all of it correct except the horizon.

## Withheld

The one variable that would change the reading.
The constraint is not on the dashboard and the leak is buried in the net, so the place withholds the limit that is actually binding, and the clearest, most honest reading of what it does show points exactly the wrong way.
