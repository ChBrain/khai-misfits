# Process: Driving

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The engine worked to make the growth, and worked harder the moment the growth slows.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A lever that has always paid, and a hand sure of it.
The driver, in [the engine room](place_the_engine_room.md), working the reinforcing loop, and reaching for more of it the instant the intake flags.

## Direction

Toward more engine, whatever the numbers do.
More spend wins more custom and more custom funds more spend, so the loop compounds while it can, and when it stalls the same loop is pushed harder, the proven move aimed with more force at whatever is holding the growth down.

## Lever

A record in which the engine has answered every slowdown before.
Because the lever made every past win and drove through every past dip, driving it harder is the rational response to this one, so the reflex needs no new reasoning, only the momentum of a move that has never yet failed.

## Echo

Effort poured into the one thing this hand controls.
The engine at full force, the growth still not climbing, and the failure read as a call for more force, the loop driving harder exactly as it stops mattering.
