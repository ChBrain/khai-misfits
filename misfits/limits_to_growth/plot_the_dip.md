# Plot: The Dip

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The dashboard](place_the_dashboard.md), the net gone flat.
The growth curve levels, the intake still counted at the door, and the record of every past slowdown lies open beside a line that has always climbed again.

## Action

[Misreading](process_misreading.md): [The Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md), holding [the analyst's seat](position_analyst.md), reads the flat line against the record and calls it a dip, while [The Driver](persona_the_driver.md), holding [the driver's seat](position_driver.md), takes the reading and reaches for the move that always worked.

## Stage

[The Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md) tables [Call It a Dip](plan_call_it_a_dip.md) and, with [the delay](piece_the_delay.md) keeping the limit out of the data, names [the stall](piece_the_stall.md) a passing slowdown.
[The Driver](persona_the_driver.md) tables [Drive the Engine](plan_drive_the_engine.md) and, trusting the record, readies more force.

## Tension

The most honest reading of the numbers points exactly the wrong way.
The analyst's base rate is sound and its inference is clean, and both are drawn from before the limit bound, so the one stall that will not recover is read like all the ones that did. The delay has made the data a portrait of a system that no longer exists, and on that portrait the rational move is more engine, so the reflex fires with full confidence at the moment it can least help.
