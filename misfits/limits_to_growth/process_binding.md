# Process: Binding

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The finite capacity tightening under the load, pushing back as a leak with a delay.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A growth drawing steadily on a capacity that is not infinite.
At [the bottleneck](place_the_bottleneck.md), the load rising toward the limit, the constraint carrying it in silence for as long as it can.

## Direction

Toward a bound throughput and an opening leak.
As the load exceeds the capacity the limit binds, the service thins, and the custom won for a value it no longer receives begins to leave, so the balancing loop pushes back and gives the growth away as fast as the engine wins it.

## Lever

Scarcity, working through a delay.
Because the capacity is finite it must bind once the load crosses it, and because the pushback lands late, after the drawing-down, the leak is never tied to the engine that caused it, so the limit governs the growth from a place no one connects to the lever.

## Echo

A ceiling set on the growth, unseen.
The throughput capped, the custom leaking out uncounted, and a binding factor that rules everything while every eye stays on the engine.
