# Position: Driver

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The seat that owns the engine, and answers every stall with more of it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The engine, and the record of every growth it won.
The proven move: work the lever that has always paid.
No other lever, because the success was built on this one and taught no other.

## Orders

Drive the engine.
When it slows, drive it harder.
Read the stall as a shortfall of the one thing this seat controls.

## Loses

The growth it is chasing, exactly when it chases hardest.
Its move is right until the limit binds and stays reflex long after, so it spends more on the engine as the engine stops mattering, and the harder it drives the wider the leak runs.
The most capable hand at the lever is the least able to see the lever has stopped being the answer.

## Drives

Toward more engine,
against a limit it does not hold and cannot push,
so the competence that made the success becomes the reflex that spends it.
