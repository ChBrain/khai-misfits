# Persona: The Churned

## Taxonomy

[Trade](position_trade.md). The growth won by the engine and handed back by the limit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Drawn in, then let down, then gone.
Comes for the value the engine promised, stays while the value is there.
Leaves without ceremony when the strained capacity stops delivering it.

## Action

Enters through the engine and counts, on the day it enters, as growth.
Meets the degraded service the limit has produced, and quietly departs.
Exits through the leak, uncounted, subtracted from a total the engine still claims to be filling.

## Shadow

It is counted at the door and not at the exit, so the engine takes credit for winning it while the limit gives it back.
No single departure is an alarm, and the sum of them is the stall.
The growth that is real on arrival is not the growth that is kept.

## Tell

Says "this is not what I came for," on the way out.
Of a value the engine keeps selling and the limit can no longer supply.
