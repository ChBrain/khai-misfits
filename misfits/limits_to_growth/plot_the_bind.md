# Plot: The Bind

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The bottleneck](place_the_bottleneck.md), the same growth leaning on a finite capacity.
The load rises toward the limit, the service begins to thin, and the value the engine sold starts to fall short of what the strained capacity can deliver.

## Action

[Binding](process_binding.md): [The Strained](persona_the_strained.md), holding [the constraint's seat](position_constraint.md), carries the load and then binds, while [The Churned](persona_the_churned.md), holding [the trade's seat](position_trade.md), meets the thinned service and begins, quietly, to leave.

## Stage

[The Strained](persona_the_strained.md) tables [Bear It, Then Break](plan_bear_it_then_break.md) and becomes [the limit](piece_the_limit.md), binding under the load without a warning.
[The Churned](persona_the_churned.md) tables [Come for the Value](plan_come_for_the_value.md) and, finding it gone, exits through [the leak](piece_the_leak.md), uncounted against the intake.

## Tension

The growth is being given back exactly as fast as it is won, and no one in the room can see it.
No single departure is loud and the capacity raised no alarm, so the binding that now governs everything happens in silence: the limit is nobody's fault, the leak is a sum of quiet exits, and the one factor that has taken control of the growth is the one factor no eye is on, while the engine runs on as though nothing has changed.
