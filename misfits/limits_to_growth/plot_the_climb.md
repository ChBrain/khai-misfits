# Plot: The Climb

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The engine room](place_the_engine_room.md), the growth compounding in its good years.
More spend wins more custom, more custom funds more spend, and every turn of the lever pays, so the engine earns the trust it will later be given past its due.

## Action

[Driving](process_driving.md): [The Driver](persona_the_driver.md), holding [the driver's seat](position_driver.md), works the reinforcing engine, while [The Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md), holding [the analyst's seat](position_analyst.md), logs the compounding wins into a record.

## Stage

[The Driver](persona_the_driver.md) tables [Drive the Engine](plan_drive_the_engine.md) and makes [the engine](piece_the_engine.md), the loop that builds the whole success.
[The Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md) tables [Call It a Dip](plan_call_it_a_dip.md) in its early form, marking each slowdown as one the engine drove through, and lays down the base rate.

## Tension

The engine is genuinely making the growth, and genuinely teaching the reflex that will spend it.
Nothing here is a mistake: the lever really does pay, the slowdowns really do recover, and the record really is sound, so the team learns, correctly, that the engine is the answer and that stalls pass. The climb is real, and it is exactly this reality that will make driving harder feel right on the one day it is wrong.
