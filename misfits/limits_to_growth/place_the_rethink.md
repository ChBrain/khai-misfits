# Place: The Rethink

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). Where the effort is turned to the limit, or the reflex holds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A move to look past the engine at the thing it draws on: the widener naming the constraint and spending the effort there, the restrainer counselling an easier climb before the ceiling.
The room pulled the other way by a lever that has always worked, and by the fear of a growth left unclaimed.

## Holds

The remedy, and its two ways of costing.
Here the effort can be moved onto the limit that governs the growth, and here that means either working a lever the success never taught or easing a lever that may not have needed easing, so the place holds both the correct leverage and the price it exacts, the way out and what the way out gives up.

## Offers

The one turn that frees the growth.
By widening what binds or pacing the engine to it, the rethink offers the throughput its only real increase, the move that raises the growth where the constraint actually sits instead of where the lever is.

## Withheld

A certainty about which limit binds, and when.
The rethink cannot prove the wall before the engine hits it, so it withholds the foreknowledge its caution needs, and the same delay that fools the driver can make the restrainer forgo a growth to a ceiling that was never there.
