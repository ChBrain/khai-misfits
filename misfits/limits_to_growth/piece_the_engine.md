With it the success has a real cause and a single shape: the engine is the reinforcing loop that built everything, so it earns total trust and becomes the one move the team knows, the answer reached for before the question is finished. The Engine is load bearing because it is both the maker of the growth and the trap's whole momentum: it is genuinely why the success happened, which is exactly why driving it harder feels right long after the binding factor has moved off it, so the thing that built the win commands the reflex that spends it.
