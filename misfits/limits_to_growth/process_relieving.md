# Process: Relieving

## Taxonomy

[Limits to Growth](play_limits_to_growth.md). The effort turned from the engine to the limit, and the two ways it costs.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A reading that the growth is short of the limit, not the engine.
At [the rethink](place_the_rethink.md), the widener naming the constraint and the restrainer watching the ceiling, both looking past the lever the room wants to work.

## Direction

Toward the factor that actually binds.
The effort is moved onto the constraint, widening what limits the throughput, or the engine is eased before the capacity runs short, so the growth is met where it is really governed and given room to breathe again.

## Lever

Leverage placed where the system binds, not where the habit points.
Because the throughput is set by the limit, widening the limit raises it and driving the engine cannot, so relief comes only from working the unglamorous constraint or pacing the loved lever, against a record that says push and a future that will not say which wall is real.

## Echo

Growth freed, or a cost paid for a wall that was not there.
The constraint widened and the throughput rising, or the engine eased against a bind that never came, so the remedy ends either in the growth breathing again or in the symmetric error of a win left unclaimed.
