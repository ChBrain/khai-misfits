# Position: Aggregator

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). The seat that pools the private signals instead of the actions.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A way to collect hints before they become echoes: a sealed guess, a secret ballot, an estimate made blind.
The sight that the crowd holds far more than it ever speaks.
The task of buying independence a herd will not keep on its own.

## Orders

Ask for the hint, not the choice.
Collect it before anyone hears the room.
Pool the signals, and let the aggregate decide.

## Loses

Ease and cheapness, in exchange for the crowd's real knowledge.
Independence is dear and imitation is usually right, so its mechanism forgoes the cheap efficiency of copying and must be built and defended against members each of whom would rather just follow.
Recovers what the crowd knows only by paying for the independence the cascade destroyed.

## Drives

Toward signals surfaced before actions can speak,
against a herd that prefers to copy,
so the crowd's knowledge is pooled only by machinery raised against the crowd's own inclination.
