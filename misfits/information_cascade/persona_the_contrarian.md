# Persona: The Contrarian

## Taxonomy

[Contrarian](position_contrarian.md). The one who acts loudly on its own hint against the herd, and can flip it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Bold, visible, unafraid to stand apart.
Acts on its own read in plain sight of the crowd, against the run of choices.
Trusts its hint enough to bet against the many, and to be seen doing it.

## Action

Breaks from the herd, publicly.
Makes a choice that contradicts the cascade and lets everyone see it, so its action lands as a new and loud signal on a crowd resting on very little.
Sets the herd reading again, and watches it turn.

## Shadow

The cascade it can flip was so thinly founded that flipping it proves nothing about being right.
Its bold break may rescue a crowd that had herded wrong, or wreck one that had herded right, because a cascade rests on so little that a single loud action can reverse it either way, so the contrarian's confidence is indistinguishable, from the outside, from the herd's it defies.
Not a truth-teller by default: a loud hint that a fragile crowd will follow, correct or not.

## Tell

"They're all wrong, and I'll show it."
The bold claim that can turn the herd, whether or not the claim is true.
