# Process: Aggregating

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). Pooling the private signals before the actions can speak, at the cost of independence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A crowd holding more than it speaks, and someone resolved to collect it.
The recognition that actions drown signals, and a will to gather hints before they become echoes.

## Direction

Toward the crowd's real knowledge, pooled and used.
Each hint is taken sealed, before anyone hears the room, so every private signal enters the record, and the aggregate of them, not the front of the line, decides.

## Lever

Independence bought before imitation can start.
A hint given before the actions are seen cannot be an echo of them, so surfacing signals first is the one thing that keeps them independent, and independence is exactly what makes the pooled crowd wise.

## Echo

A decision made on what the crowd knows, and a bill for the independence.
The signals aggregated instead of copied, the cascade unable to form, and a mechanism that had to be built and paid for against a herd each member would rather have joined.
