# Place: The Turn

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). Where the cascade meets a new signal and can reverse.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A loud break against the run, or a new fact landing, and a crowd pausing to read it.
The herd, moving one way, catching sight of something that reads as evidence and swinging toward it.

## Holds

The fragility, come to a head.
Here the crowd's certainty is revealed to rest on almost nothing, so the turn holds the moment a single new action can outweigh the whole thin cascade and send it the other way.

## Offers

A chance for the herd to correct.
A new signal can pull a crowd off a wrong cascade, so the turn offers the possibility of rescue, of a herd that had followed the first few now following something better.

## Withheld

Any guarantee the new way is the right one.
The turn reverses a cascade that rested on little by adding one more thing that is also little, so it can as easily swap a right herd for a wrong as a wrong for a right, and it withholds all assurance of which.
