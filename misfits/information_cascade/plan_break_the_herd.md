# Plan: Break the Herd

## Taxonomy

## Direction

Trust your own hint enough to bet against the many, and act on it where all can see. Land a loud choice against the run, and let the crowd, resting on so little, read it as the new signal it is. Turn the herd, and know you cannot prove the turn is true.
