# Plot: The Ballot

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The ballot](place_the_ballot.md), where someone moves to pool what the crowd privately knows.
Sealed guesses, a secret vote, estimates made before anyone hears the room.

## Action

[Aggregating](process_aggregating.md): [The Aggregator](persona_the_aggregator.md), holding [the aggregator's seat](position_aggregator.md), collects each hint before it can become an echo, while [The Dissenter](persona_the_dissenter.md), holding [the dissenter's seat](position_dissenter.md), sets down the contrary read it would have swallowed in the line.

## Stage

[The Aggregator](persona_the_aggregator.md) tables [Surface the Signals](plan_surface_the_signals.md): take each hint sealed and blind, so [the information cascade](piece_the_information_cascade.md) is answered by pooling every [private signal](piece_the_private_signal.md) instead of the actions.
The aggregate of hints decides, and the dissent that the cascade buried finally counts.

## Tension

The cure works by forbidding the one thing everyone wants to do: look at the crowd.
Surfacing signals recovers the knowledge the cascade buried, but only by buying an independence no herd keeps on its own and forgoing the cheap, usually-right efficiency of just copying, so the mechanism must be built and defended against members each of whom would rather glance at the board. The crowd's knowledge is pooled at last ... or the independence proves too dear, the room is read after all, and the actions speak over the signals again.
