# Piece: The Private Signal

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). Each actor's own noisy hint: the information that following will leave unspoken.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the line](place_the_line.md), held by each as they enter.
Where every chooser carries a small private read of their own.

## Load Bearing

Without it there would be nothing worth pooling and no loss when it goes unspoken.
With it each person holds a real, if noisy, piece of information, so a crowd of them holds a great deal, and the tragedy is that this is exactly what the cascade buries: the signal is acted on only at the front and swallowed everywhere after, so the mountain of private knowledge is never gathered. The Private Signal is load bearing because it is the dispersed information at the root of the theory (Bikhchandani, Hirshleifer & Welch), the thing a crowd collectively holds and individually stops expressing, and the whole quantity a good mechanism exists to recover.

## Apparent

Just one person's guess, easily outweighed.
A single hint looks small and dispensable next to a crowd, so setting it aside seems obviously right, and nothing marks it as part of a store of knowledge that vanishes precisely because each holder, reasonably, thinks their piece too small to matter.

## Yearbook

Held along [the line](place_the_line.md), acted on by [the first](persona_the_first.md) and swallowed by [the dissenter](persona_the_dissenter.md).
It is the knowledge the crowd forgets to gather: the Private Signal is what [the cascade](piece_the_cascade.md) silences and what [the information cascade](piece_the_information_cascade.md) can be cured only by surfacing.
