# Pitch: They Must Know Something

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Sensible, observant, quick to read the room.
Look at what everyone else is doing, reason that they must know something, and do as they do.
The voice of humble good judgement, spoken by people who are right that the crowd, so far, knows more than they do, and cannot see that by joining it they add nothing to what it knows.

## Undertow

Under the good sense, a crowd copying itself and learning nothing.
Each can see the choices before them but not the hints behind them, so each reads the run of choices as a run of hints and rationally defers, and their own hint, swallowed to defer, is never spoken, so the next reads one more echo and defers in turn.
No one is foolish: they only each set a single contrary guess against the apparent weight of the rest, which is the correct thing to do, and the sum of those correct deferrals is a herd resting on the first few guesses and blind to the mountain it privately holds.

## Nerve

The feeling of prudent, modest agreement,
each member deferring to a crowd that seems to know,
private knowledge swallowed one careful judgement at a time until the whole crowd is sure of something almost none of it actually checked.

## Echo

After the run: a certainty resting on the first few noisy hints,
a mountain of private knowledge that was never once spoken,
and a herd so lightly founded that a whisper can turn it, and stampede it, just as confidently, the other way.
