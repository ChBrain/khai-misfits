# Plot: The Fragility

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The turn](place_the_turn.md), the cascade resting on so little that a breath can move it.
A loud break against the run, or a new fact landing, and the crowd pausing to read it.

## Action

[Cascading](process_cascading.md) in reverse: [The Contrarian](persona_the_contrarian.md), holding [the contrarian's seat](position_contrarian.md), acts loudly on its own hint against the herd, while [The Follower](persona_the_follower.md), holding [the follower's seat](position_follower.md), reads the new action as it read the old and turns.

## Stage

[The Contrarian](persona_the_contrarian.md) tables [Break the Herd](plan_break_the_herd.md), landing a loud new signal, so [the fragility](piece_the_fragility.md) lets one break outweigh the whole thin run.
The herd stampedes the other way, just as rationally, its members again reading actions and again setting aside their own [private signal](piece_the_private_signal.md).

## Tension

The crowd reverses on a whisper, and the reversal proves nothing about being right.
Because the cascade rested only on the first few hints, a single loud action can outweigh it, so the herd turns, and the turning is the same rational reading of a new signal, applied to a base always too thin. It can swap a wrong herd for a right one or a right for a wrong, and from inside the stampede there is no telling which.
