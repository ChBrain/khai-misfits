# Position: Contrarian

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). The seat that acts against the herd in plain sight, and can flip it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A private hint it trusts enough to bet against the many.
The nerve to act on it visibly, against the run of choices.
A crowd resting on so little that one loud action can move it.

## Orders

Act on your own hint, against the herd.
Make the break visible.
Land a loud new signal on a crowd primed to read it.

## Loses

Its claim to be right, which the flip cannot prove.
A cascade rests on so little that a single loud break can reverse it either way, so breaking one may save a wrong herd or wreck a right one, and its confidence looks, from outside, exactly like the herd's it defies.
Able to turn the crowd, and unable to guarantee the turn is true.

## Drives

Toward a visible break from the herd,
which a fragile crowd will follow,
so the cascade reverses on its one loud hint, correct or not.
