# Persona: The Dissenter

## Taxonomy

[Dissenter](position_dissenter.md). The one whose private hint contradicts the herd, and who correctly swallows it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Thoughtful, uneasy, holding a hint against the tide.
Sees the crowd going one way and feels its own read pulling the other.
Knows enough to know that its one hint is a small thing against a long run of them.

## Action

Weighs its contrary hint against the herd, and defers.
Reasons, correctly, that the revealed hints of the many outweigh its single one, so it swallows its doubt and follows, keeping its dissent to itself.
Removes from the record the one signal that might have questioned the crowd.

## Shadow

The very correctness of its deference is what loses the information most worth keeping.
Its hint is the one that disagrees, and so the one the crowd most needs to hear, but speaking it would be the wrong move for the dissenter, whose lone guess really is outweighed, so the signal that could have checked the cascade is the signal it is most rational to suppress.
Not a coward: a sound reasoner silenced by its own correct arithmetic.

## Tell

"My gut says otherwise, but who am I against all of them?"
The honest, outvoted doubt that the cascade needed spoken and the dissenter is right to swallow.
