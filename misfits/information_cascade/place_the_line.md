# Place: The Line

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). Where people choose in sequence, each seeing those before.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A queue of choosers, each with a private hint, each in view of the choices already made.
The front acting on its own signals, the rest arriving to a lengthening run of the same choice.

## Holds

The sequence, and the cascade it breeds.
Here order is everything: the early act on their hints and reveal them, the late arrive to a crowd and copy it, so the line holds both the informative front and the echoing tail that follows.

## Offers

The wisdom of those who went first.
Each newcomer can see what the crowd has done and lean on it, so the line offers real evidence to anyone who reads it, which is exactly what makes copying the sensible move.

## Withheld

The hints behind the choices.
The line shows what each person did and never why, so a newcomer sees actions and not signals, and cannot tell an informed choice from a copied one, which is how the echoes come to look like evidence.
