# Plot: The Cascade

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The line](place_the_line.md) past the tipping point, each newcomer arriving to a crowd it is right to join.
Every copy is made on the crowd's information, not the copier's, so it reveals nothing.

## Action

[Cascading](process_cascading.md): [The Follower](persona_the_follower.md), holding [the follower's seat](position_follower.md), copies and adds no signal, while [The Dissenter](persona_the_dissenter.md), holding [the dissenter's seat](position_dissenter.md), feels its own read pull the other way and defers all the same.

## Stage

[The Follower](persona_the_follower.md) tables [Follow the Crowd](plan_follow_the_crowd.md), so [the cascade](piece_the_cascade.md) rolls on resting on the few signals before [the tipping point](piece_the_tipping_point.md).
[The Dissenter](persona_the_dissenter.md) tables [Swallow My Signal](plan_swallow_my_signal.md), correctly judging the herd knows more, so the one hint that disagreed never enters the record.

## Tension

Everyone defers correctly, and the crowd stops knowing anything new.
The follower is right that the crowd knows more, the dissenter is right that its lone contrary hint is outweighed, so no one errs and no one speaks. That is the cascade: a run that looks like overwhelming consensus and is made of echoes, a crowd holding a mountain of private signals and acting on the handful that happened to come first.
