# Place: The Ballot

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). Where signals are surfaced instead of actions.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Sealed guesses, a secret vote, estimates written before anyone hears the room.
Each person setting down its own hint privately, with no run of choices to read and no crowd to defer to.

## Holds

The aggregation, and the independence it demands.
Here the hints are collected before they can become echoes, so the ballot holds the crowd's real knowledge pooled at last, and the costly independence that pooling requires.

## Offers

The wisdom the crowd actually has.
By taking each hint before the actions can speak, the ballot offers the sum of what the crowd privately knows rather than a repeat of the first few, so it recovers the very knowledge the cascade buried.

## Withheld

The cheap comfort of copying.
The ballot forbids reading the room, so it withholds the easy, usually-right efficiency of following the crowd, and demands an independence each member, left alone, would happily trade for a glance at what the others did.
