# Process: Signalling

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). The early choices, made on private hints, that actually reveal them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A chooser at the front, with a private hint and no crowd to read.
The head of the line, where there is nothing to copy and only one's own signal to act on.

## Direction

Toward actions that carry the hints behind them.
The first movers choose on what they know, so each early action is a true sign of its hint, and the board begins to hold real evidence.

## Lever

A choice made on a signal, with nothing to imitate.
Because the front has no crowd to defer to, its actions can only reflect its hints, so early on the visible choices and the private signals point the same way, and reading one really does reveal the other.

## Echo

A short run of informative choices.
The first hints laid down as actions, still carrying their signals, and a direction set that the rest of the line will read as the wisdom of the front.
