# Persona: The Aggregator

## Taxonomy

[Aggregator](position_aggregator.md). The one who pools the private signals instead of the actions, so the cascade cannot form.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Deliberate, procedural, more interested in the hints than the crowd.
Sees that the crowd holds a mountain of information it never speaks, and moves to collect it.
Prefers a sealed guess to a visible one, an independent estimate to an echo.

## Action

Surfaces the signals before the actions can speak.
Collects sealed guesses, runs a secret ballot, asks each for an estimate made before anyone hears the room, so every private hint enters the record instead of being swallowed.
Pools what the crowd knows, and lets the aggregate, not the front of the line, decide.

## Shadow

The independence it needs is dear, and the imitation it prevents is usually right.
Its mechanism recovers the crowd's knowledge only by paying for independence the herd would not keep on its own, and by forgoing the cheap, usually-correct efficiency of just copying the crowd, so the cure is costly, easily gamed, and built against members each of whom would rather simply follow.
Not a hero freeing the truth: a builder of expensive machinery against a crowd that prefers the cascade.

## Tell

"Don't tell me what they did. Tell me what you know, before you hear them."
The one instruction that pools the crowd's knowledge, and the one a crowd will not give itself.
