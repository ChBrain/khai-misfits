# Piece: The Public Action

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). The only visible evidence: a person's choice, seen without the hint behind it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the board](place_the_board.md), tallied and countable.
Where each choice shows and no reason does.

## Load Bearing

Without it there would be no way to learn from others and no cascade to fear.
With it each actor can see what others did but never why, so actions are the only channel through which private hints travel, and a channel that carries the choice but not the signal is exactly what lets an echo pass for evidence. The Public Action is load bearing because it is the observability condition the cascade requires (Anderson & Holt): people see behaviour, not information, so a copied choice is indistinguishable from an informed one, and the crowd reads a tally of actions as if it were a tally of hints.

## Apparent

Evidence of what people know.
A visible choice looks like a window onto the chooser's information, so a run of them reads as a run of hints, when in truth most may be copies that reveal nothing, and the board cannot tell the reader which.

## Yearbook

Read at [the board](place_the_board.md) through [inferring](process_inferring.md), the channel that carries choices and not hints.
It is the visible half of a hidden thing: the Public Action is what [the inference](piece_the_inference.md) works on, and what lets a copied choice masquerade as a signal.
