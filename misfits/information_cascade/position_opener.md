# Position: Opener

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). The seat that moves first, with no crowd to read.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A private hint and nothing yet to copy.
The position at the head of the line, watched by all who follow.
No crowd to defer to, so no choice but to act on its own signal.

## Orders

Act on your own hint.
Choose plainly, having nothing better than what you know.
Set the first mark on the board.

## Loses

Nothing it can see, and control of the weight its choice will carry.
It acts correctly on its own signal, but the line reads that choice as the seed of the crowd and builds on it, so a small, possibly noisy hint is magnified far past its worth.
Right to choose as it does, and unable to stop the crowd from mistaking its hint for wisdom.

## Drives

Toward a choice made on its own signal,
which is all it has,
so the whole cascade comes to rest on whatever the front of the line happened to hold.
