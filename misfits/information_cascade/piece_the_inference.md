# Piece: The Inference

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). The correct update: reading others' actions as evidence of their hints.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the board](place_the_board.md), in the weighing of the tally against a hint.
Where a run of choices is read as a run of signals.

## Load Bearing

Without it following would be mere conformity, and the misfit a matter of weak character.
With it following is rational: reading actions as evidence of hints is the right thing to do, and a genuine one, because early actions really did carry signals, so the inference that leads a newcomer to defer is sound reasoning, not a failure of nerve. The Inference is load bearing because it is the rational-updating step that makes the cascade a trap of logic rather than of cowardice (Banerjee): it is correct, and its correctness is what makes it destroy the very information it seems to use.

## Apparent

Simply learning from others.
Updating on what other people did looks like exactly the humble, sensible thing a good reasoner should do, so the inference reads as wisdom, and its role in silencing the crowd's own knowledge is hidden inside how manifestly reasonable it is.

## Yearbook

Performed by [the follower](persona_the_follower.md) through [inferring](process_inferring.md), working on [the public action](piece_the_public_action.md).
It is the sound step that dooms the pooling: the Inference drives toward [the tipping point](piece_the_tipping_point.md), where the run outweighs the hint and the signal is set aside.
