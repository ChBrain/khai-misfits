# Persona: The Follower

## Taxonomy

[Follower](position_follower.md). The one who reads the run of choices, defers to it, and copies.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Modest, observant, sensibly humble.
Arrives to a line of the same choice and reasons that so many cannot all be wrong.
Holds a private hint, and holds it lightly against the apparent weight of the crowd.

## Action

Reads the actions as evidence and copies the crowd.
Weighs its single hint against the run of revealed hints, finds itself outvoted, and rationally sets its own aside to follow.
Adds a choice made on the crowd's information, not its own, that reveals nothing to whoever comes next.

## Shadow

Its correct deference is what removes its signal from the world.
It is right that the crowd, so far, knows more than it does, and right to defer, but its copy carries no hint, so the next reader learns nothing from it and defers in turn, and the sum of these sound judgements is a herd that has stopped gathering what its members know.
Not a sheep: a careful reasoner whose every careful choice makes the crowd know a little less than it should.

## Tell

"They can't all be wrong, so I'll go with them."
The reasonable deferral that, made by each in turn, empties the crowd of its own knowledge.
