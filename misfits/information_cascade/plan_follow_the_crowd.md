# Plan: Follow the Crowd

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the follower's seat](position_follower.md), inside [Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Follower](position_follower.md)

## Direction

Read the run of choices before you as evidence of the hints behind them. Weigh your single hint against that run, and when it is outweighed, do the sensible thing: set your own aside and copy the crowd, because so many cannot all be wrong.

## Orders

([The Follower](persona_the_follower.md) reads the actions as evidence, finds itself outvoted, and rationally copies.)

## Implementation

([The Board](place_the_board.md); the [inferring](process_inferring.md) process; [the inference](piece_the_inference.md) reads actions as signals until [the tipping point](piece_the_tipping_point.md), where the observed run outweighs a private hint.)

## Targets

- [ ] the run of prior choices is read as a run of hints
- [ ] the single private hint is weighed and found outvoted
- [ ] the crowd is copied, on its information rather than the copier's
- [ ] the copy reveals no hint, so the next reader learns nothing from it
