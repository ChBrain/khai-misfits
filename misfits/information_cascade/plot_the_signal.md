# Plot: The Signal

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The line](place_the_line.md), a sequence of choosers, each with a private hint and each able to see the choices before them.
At the front there is nothing yet to copy, only one's own read to go on.

## Action

[Signalling](process_signalling.md): [The First](persona_the_first.md), holding [the opener's seat](position_opener.md), acts on its own hint, while [The Follower](persona_the_follower.md), holding [the follower's seat](position_follower.md), arrives behind it and begins to watch.

## Stage

[The First](persona_the_first.md) tables [Act on My Signal](plan_act_on_my_signal.md), choosing plainly on its own hint, so early on [the public action](piece_the_public_action.md) still carries [the private signal](piece_the_private_signal.md) behind it.
[The Follower](persona_the_follower.md) reads the front, its own hint still in hand, the direction of the line not yet set.

## Tension

The front chooses honestly, and its honest hints become a foundation far heavier than they are.
Nothing is wrong: the first movers have only their own signals and are right to use them. That is the trap laid, not sprung: a few small, possibly noisy hints laid down as visible choices, about to be read by everyone behind as the wisdom of the front, and weighted, quietly, far past what one hint each is worth.
