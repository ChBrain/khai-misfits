# Position: Follower

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). The seat that arrives to a crowd, reads it, and copies.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A private hint, held lightly.
A run of choices before it, visible as actions but not as reasons.
The sound judgement that a long run of hints outweighs its single one.

## Orders

Read the actions as evidence.
Weigh your hint against the crowd's.
When outweighed, defer, and copy.

## Loses

Its own signal, by the very act of deferring.
Copying on the crowd's information rather than its own, its choice reveals no hint, so it takes from the crowd's knowledge without adding to it, and the next reader learns nothing from its move.
Correct to defer, and by deferring one more link that carries no signal.

## Drives

Toward the choice the crowd has already made,
away from the hint it holds and swallows,
so each sound deferral extends a herd that has stopped learning.
