# Piece: The Information Cascade

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). The phenomenon whole: a crowd blind to what it knows, and its remedy in surfaced signals.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the ballot](place_the_ballot.md), where the signals are pooled instead of the actions.
And along the whole line, in every hint swallowed to defer.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of moves with no shape.
With it the moves become one law: the private signal, the public action, the inference, the tipping point, the cascade, and the fragility are facets of a single structure, in which rational people reading actions instead of signals stop expressing what they know, so a crowd acts on almost none of its own information and can lock, or flip, on almost nothing. The Information Cascade is load bearing because it is the whole finding (Bikhchandani, Hirshleifer & Welch) with its whole remedy attached: it holds the misfit together, and it holds that the cure is to surface signals before actions can speak, bought at the price of an independence the herd will not keep and an imitation that is usually cheap and right.

## Apparent

Either the wisdom of crowds, or their madness.
Seen from one side a long consensus looks like collective wisdom, from the other a sudden reversal looks like collective madness, and both miss the single mechanism under them: rational inference from actions, which makes the crowd neither wise nor mad but simply blind to the knowledge it never pooled.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the aggregator](persona_the_aggregator.md) through [aggregating](process_aggregating.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: the Information Cascade is what [the private signal](piece_the_private_signal.md) suffers once actions speak louder than hints, undone only by surfacing every signal before the crowd can read itself.
