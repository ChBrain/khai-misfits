# Position: Dissenter

## Taxonomy

[Information Cascade](play_information_cascade.md). The seat whose hint contradicts the herd, and who swallows it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A private hint that points against the crowd.
The clear sight that its one hint is small against a long run of them.
The choice to speak the dissent or to swallow it.

## Orders

Weigh your contrary hint against the herd.
Grant that the many revealed hints outweigh your one.
Defer, and keep the dissent to yourself.

## Loses

The one signal the crowd most needed, exactly by being right to withhold it.
Its hint is the one that disagrees, so the one worth hearing, but voicing a lone outweighed guess would be the wrong move, so the signal that might have checked the cascade is the one it is most rational to suppress.
Sound in its silence, and the silence is the loss.

## Drives

Toward deferring to a crowd that outweighs it,
away from a dissent that would cost it to speak,
so the signal that could have questioned the herd is the one most surely lost.
