# Persona: The First

## Taxonomy

[Opener](position_opener.md). The early mover whose choice, made on its own hint, actually reveals it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Unhurried, unheralded, with no crowd to read.
Arrives when there is nothing yet to copy, so it has only its own private hint to go on.
Chooses on that hint, plainly, and thinks nothing of being watched.

## Action

Acts on its own signal, and by acting reveals it.
Makes the first choices, each one carrying the hint behind it, because there is no herd to defer to yet.
Sets, without meaning to, the direction the whole line will read and repeat.

## Shadow

Its small, possibly noisy hint becomes the foundation everyone builds on.
It is right to act on its own signal, having no better evidence, but its early choice is weighted far beyond its worth, because those behind read it as the seed of the crowd and follow, so a whole cascade comes to rest on a guess that was only ever one hint among many.
Not an oracle: an ordinary early mover whose ordinary hint the line mistakes for the wisdom of the front.

## Tell

"I'm just going with what I know."
The honest report of the one whose small knowing the whole crowd will magnify into certainty.
