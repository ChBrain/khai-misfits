# Position: Actor

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md). The seat that carries the hazardous activity and the capped liability, where the harm above the assets is treated as free and the assets are kept low to keep the ceiling low.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An activity that can cause more harm than the seat is worth, and a net worth that caps what a judgment can collect: the exposure held and the ceiling that bounds it.
A rational rule: guard up to the cost you will bear, and hold no more than you must.
No cost to the seat in the harm above its assets, because it will never pay for it, and every reason to keep the assets low, because every asset is one a judgment can reach.

## Orders

Guard up to the ceiling, and keep the ceiling low.
Take the activity, invest in care up to the harm you can be charged for, treat the harm above your assets as free, and hold as little as you can so the assets that cap your liability stay small.
Do the rational thing, because the rational thing is to price your care against what you will actually pay.

## Loses

The care against the largest harms, exactly because those are the harms it will never pay for.
Because the seat computes its precaution against the cost it will bear and bears damages only up to its assets, it under-invests in guarding against the harm above the ceiling and structures itself thin to lower the ceiling further, so it loses the deterrence on precisely the biggest harms to its own arithmetic, each choice private and rational and legal.
It gains a lawful use of limited liability and a care bill matched to what it will pay, and loses the guard on the harm that runs past what it owns.

## Drives

Toward the ceiling honoured and the harm above it treated as free,
the care priced against what will be paid and the assets kept low to lower what will be paid,
so the seat that carries the hazard guards up to its worth and no further, under-deterred on the largest harm by nothing but the cap on what it can lose.
