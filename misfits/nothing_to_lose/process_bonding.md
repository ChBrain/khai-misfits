# Process: Bonding

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md). The remedy: capital, insurance, or a bond required up front so the actor holds a stake up to the harm, the ceiling raised by hand at the cost of charging every actor.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An asset ceiling too low to deter the harm the activity can cause.
At [the escrow](place_the_escrow.md), the bonder requiring a stake posted up front, sized against the harm the activity could cause and not against the worth the actor chose to hold.

## Direction

Toward the ceiling raised to meet the harm and the excess made no longer free.
Because the harm above the actor's assets is free only while the assets stay low, bonding requires the actor to post capital, carry compulsory insurance, or hold a bond up to the harm, so the actor holds a stake up to what it can destroy and the care returns, and where the thinness is contrived the veil is pierced, the ceiling lifted by hand to meet the harm the market left it below.

## Lever

The stake required before the harm.
Because the actor guards up to what it can lose, and posting a stake up to the harm makes the harm above the old ceiling something it can now lose, bonding runs not on any accusation but on raising the worth at risk, so the restored deterrence is bought with a stake charged to every actor, including the small and honest, whether or not the harm ever comes.

## Echo

A ceiling raised, and a cost charged to raise it.
The bonder has made the actor hold a stake up to the harm, so what reads as a fix is, in truth, a deterrence bought with capital idled against a tail most will never trigger, enterprise priced out along with the judgment-proof, the line between lawful and contrived thinness drawn by hand, the care restored only by paying for the ceiling the market set for free.
