# Plot: The Surety

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Escrow](place_the_escrow.md), someone would set the ceiling by hand before the harm.
The Bonder moving to require a stake up front so the actor holds worth up to what it can destroy.

## Action

[Bonding](process_bonding.md): the Bonder, holding [the bonder's seat](position_bonder.md), requires minimum capital, compulsory insurance, or a posted bond up to the scale of the harm, or pierces the veil where the thinness is contrived.

## Stage

The bonder, on [Bond the Risk](plan_bond_the_risk.md), requires a stake at [the escrow](place_the_escrow.md) sized against [the scene](place_the_scene.md), lifting [the assets](piece_the_assets.md) up to meet [the excess](piece_the_excess.md) so [the precaution](piece_the_precaution.md) returns and [the thinness](piece_the_thinness.md) can no longer lower the ceiling.
The stake is charged to every actor, whether or not the harm ever comes.

## Tension

Raising the ceiling to meet the harm restores the care, and it can be done only by charging every actor for a harm most will never cause.
There is no free correction: the bond that answers the excess prices out the small and honest along with the judgment-proof and idles capital against a tail. So the plot closes with the actor made to hold a stake up to what it can destroy and the care restored ... or the requirement waived as too costly, the ceiling left where the market set it, and the excess left free.
