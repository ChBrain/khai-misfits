# Persona: The Victim

## Taxonomy

[Victim](position_victim.md). The one who bears the harm above the ceiling that liability cannot recover, holding a full judgment and collecting only up to the actor's assets, uncompensated for the excess and under-protected before it. The archetype of the harm the law can name but cannot reach.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Injured. In the right. Holding a judgment.
Is anyone hurt by an activity that could cost more than the injurer owns, the neighbour to the hazardous plant, the passenger, the downstream town.
No fault in it: the Victim bore a harm it did not choose, sued in good order, and won the whole of it in law.

## Action

Suffers the harm, sues for the full damages, wins the whole award, and collects only up to the actor's assets, the excess left as a number no one will pay.
Reaches the balance sheet and finds a shell, the hazardous work spun off into a firm with the assets of a shell.
Does what a wronged party must: it seeks to be made whole, and finds the law can award the whole and collect only the ceiling.

## Shadow

Not a loser of its case, a winner of a judgment it cannot collect, and that is the whole difficulty: the award is full and the pocket is empty, so being in the right buys the excess nothing.
Because the actor guarded only up to its assets, the Victim was under-protected before the harm as well as under-compensated after it, exposed to exactly the largest harms because those are the ones the actor had no reason to prevent, and it discovers this only at the sheriff's visit, when the full judgment meets the empty shell and the excess stands uncollected.
It wins the whole harm in law, and it is the harm above the ceiling, the part the law cannot reach, that fell on it hardest.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "I won, and there is nothing to collect."
Says it holding a full award against a shell, and cannot make the excess whole, because the actor never held enough to pay it and never guarded against it because it never would.
