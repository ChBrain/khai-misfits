# Place: The Scene

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md). Where the harm happens, the site of the accident whose cost can run far past the actor's assets, the harm that sets the scale the ceiling fails to meet.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the activity causes its harm, the spill, the collision, the failure, where the cost of what went wrong is counted and can run to many times what the actor owns.
The Actor's activity landing its harm here, the Victim bearing it, and the size of it set by the accident and not by the balance sheet.

## Holds

The harm at its true scale, and the gap between that scale and the ceiling.
Here the accident sets the cost, so the Scene holds the point where the harm is sized by the world and not by the actor's worth: a harm that can exceed the assets many times over, so it is where the excess is born, the part of the harm above the ceiling, real and counted and larger than anything a judgment can collect.

## Offers

A harm measured whole.
Because the Scene is where the accident lands, it offers the true size of what went wrong, the full cost counted at the site, so it hands out the scale of the harm, and hands it out untethered to the actor's worth, the harm as large as the activity can make it and the assets as small as the actor kept them.

## Withheld

Any tie between the size of the harm and the size of the pocket.
The Scene shows the harm whole but cannot bound it to what the actor can pay, because the accident sizes the harm and the balance sheet sizes the recovery and the two are set apart, so it withholds from the harm any assurance it can be met, letting the cost run past the ceiling, and, all true scale and no matching pocket, it withholds the excess into a region no judgment can reach.
