# Plan: Risk What I Have

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the actor's seat](position_actor.md), inside [Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Actor](position_actor.md)

## Direction

Guard up to the ceiling of your assets and treat the harm above it as free. Price your precaution against the cost you will actually bear, invest in care up to the harm you can be charged for, and stop, then hold as little as you can so the assets that cap your liability stay low, because every asset is one a judgment can reach.

## Orders

([The Actor](persona_the_actor.md) computes its care against what it will pay, guards up to its assets and treats the excess as free, then capitalises thin and spins the hazard into a shell to keep the ceiling low.)

## Implementation

([The Balance Sheet](place_the_balance_sheet.md) as the ceiling, [the scene](place_the_scene.md) where the harm can exceed it; the [discounting](process_discounting.md) process; [the assets](piece_the_assets.md) cap what will be paid, [the excess](piece_the_excess.md) above them is free, [the thinness](piece_the_thinness.md) lowers the ceiling and [the precaution](piece_the_precaution.md) is shed.)

## Targets

- [ ] precaution is priced against the cost the actor will actually bear, capped at its assets
- [ ] the harm above the ceiling is treated as free and left unguarded
- [ ] the assets are kept low so the ceiling on liability stays small
- [ ] the hazardous activity is held thin, in a shell, with no insurance beyond the mandatory
