# Process: Discounting

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md). The Actor discounting the harm above the ceiling to zero and under-precautioning, the rational pricing of care against the cost it will actually bear.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An asset ceiling below the harm the activity can cause.
At [the balance sheet](place_the_balance_sheet.md), the actor computing its precaution against what it will actually pay and finding the harm above its assets costs it nothing.

## Direction

Toward the largest harms left unguarded.
Because the actor bears damages only up to its assets, it prices its care against the cost it will bear and treats the harm above the ceiling as free, so it invests in precaution up to the harm it can be charged for and stops, and the care it sheds is precisely the care against the biggest harms, because those are the ones it will never pay for, the discounting running not on recklessness but on the arithmetic of a capped threat.

## Lever

The costlessness of the harm above the ceiling.
Because the harm above the actor's assets is one it will never pay, and a cost it will never bear does not enter its sum, discounting runs not on any disregard but on the plain fact that the excess is free, so the under-deterrence is assembled from no wrong, only from each actor pricing its care against the harm it can be charged for and shedding the guard on the harm it cannot.

## Echo

A care bill matched to the ceiling, and the largest harm left unguarded.
The actor has invested rationally up to what it will pay, so what reads as prudence is, in truth, an under-precaution against exactly the biggest harms, the excess discounted to zero because it costs the actor nothing, each actor sure it has guarded to the point that pays.
