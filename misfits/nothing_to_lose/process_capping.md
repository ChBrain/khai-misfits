# Process: Capping

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md). The asset ceiling capping the court's recoverable liability, the full award bounded at collection by what the actor actually has.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A full judgment meeting an actor worth less than the harm.
At [the balance sheet](place_the_balance_sheet.md), the court's whole award running to the actor's assets and stopping at the total on the page.

## Direction

Toward the whole threat landing as a partial one.
Because a judgment can be collected only against what the actor has, the full award is bounded at collection by the actor's assets, so the whole harm imposed in law is reduced in fact to the ceiling, and the deterrence the court means to carry lands on the actor as a capped threat, diminished not by any leniency but by the wall at the actor's worth, before the actor ever chooses its care.

## Lever

The limit of what can be collected.
Because no court can take from an actor more than the actor has, and the actor's assets stop at the ceiling, capping runs not on any softness of the court but on the plain limit of collection, so the diminished threat is assembled from no error, only from a full award meeting a capped pocket and being bounded to it.

## Echo

A full judgment in law, and a partial threat in fact.
The court has imposed the whole harm in good faith, so what reads as full liability is, in truth, a threat capped at the actor's assets, the award above the ceiling awarded and never paid, the deterrence bounded before the actor acts by the limit on what can be reached.
