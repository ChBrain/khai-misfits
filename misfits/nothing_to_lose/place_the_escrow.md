# Place: The Escrow

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md). The remedy place, where capital or insurance is posted up front, the stake held aside so the actor holds worth up to the harm before it can cause it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place a stake is set aside before the activity runs, the posted bond, the funded reserve, the compulsory policy, the capital held against the harm to come.
The Bonder requiring the stake, the Actor posting it, and the ceiling lifted by hand to meet the harm the activity can cause.

## Holds

The stake, and the cost of holding it.
Here the worth is posted up front, so the Escrow holds the point where the ceiling is raised to meet the harm: a fund set aside up to the scale of what the activity can destroy, so it is where the excess stops being free, the harm above the old ceiling now backed by a stake, and where the cost of that backing sits, capital idled against a harm most actors will never cause.

## Offers

A ceiling raised and a stake that can pay.
Because the Escrow is where the worth is posted, it offers a recovery up to the harm and a care restored, the actor now holding enough to answer for what it can break, so it hands out the deterrence the bare balance sheet could not, and hands it out at a price, the stake required of every actor and charged whether or not the harm ever comes.

## Withheld

Any way to charge only the actor who will cause the harm.
The Escrow shows a stake posted but cannot post it only where the tail harm will fall, because no one can tell in advance which actor will cause it, so it withholds the free ceiling the market gave, requiring the stake of all, and, all worth held aside and no way to hold it selectively, it withholds cheap entry from the small and honest along with the judgment-proof.
