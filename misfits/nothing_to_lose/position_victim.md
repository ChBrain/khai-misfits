# Position: Victim

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md). The seat that bears the harm above the ceiling, where a full judgment collects only up to the actor's assets and the excess stands as a loss no one will pay.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A harm suffered and a right to the whole of it: the injury borne and the full judgment won.
A sound claim: the liability is real and the award is complete.
No way to collect past the actor's assets, and no protection before the harm against the part of it that runs past those assets, because the actor had no reason to guard what it would never pay for.

## Orders

Sue for the harm.
Take the injury, seek the full damages, win the whole award, and collect against the actor's assets, up to the ceiling and no further.
Pursue the whole of it, because the whole of it is owed, even as only part of it can be reached.

The excess, the harm above the ceiling, both uncompensated after and unguarded before.
Because the actor guarded only up to its assets and holds only up to the ceiling, the seat is under-protected against exactly the largest harm and uncompensated for it when it lands, so it loses the excess twice, once to the shed precaution and once to the empty pocket, holding a full judgment against a shell.
It gains the whole harm in law, and loses the part of it the law can name but cannot reach.

the whole harm owed and the excess uncollectible,
so the seat that bore the harm wins its case and finds a shell, the largest part of what it suffered the part no one will pay.
