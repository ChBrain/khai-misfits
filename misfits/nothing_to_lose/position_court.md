# Position: Court

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md). The seat that imposes full liability in good faith and reaches only the actor's assets, where the whole harm is awarded and the ceiling bounds what can be collected.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A finding of liability and the power to award the whole harm: the full judgment ordered and the enforcement that runs to the actor's assets.
A complete threat in law: the actor owes the whole of the harm it caused.
No reach past the actor's assets, because a judgment can be collected only against what the actor has, so the whole award lands on a capped pocket.

## Orders

Award the damages.
Find the actor liable, impose the full harm as the judgment, and order it collected, holding the actor to the whole cost of what it caused.
Impose the whole, because the whole is what the law directs, even as only the ceiling can be reached.

## Loses

The bite of its own threat above the ceiling, exactly because it can collect only what the actor has.
Because the full award can be collected only up to the actor's assets, the seat's complete threat lands on the actor as a capped one, so the deterrence it means to impose is diminished not by leniency but by the wall at the actor's worth, and the actor, computing against that capped threat, under-guards the harm the seat can award but never make it pay.
It gains a full judgment in law, and loses the deterrence on the harm above the ceiling to a limit it cannot cross.

## Drives

Toward the whole harm awarded and only the assets collected,
the full liability imposed and the ceiling bounding its reach,
so the seat that holds the actor to the whole cost makes it pay only up to its worth, the largest harm awarded in full and deterred only in part.
