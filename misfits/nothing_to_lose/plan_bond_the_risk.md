# Plan: Bond the Risk

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the bonder's seat](position_bonder.md), inside [Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Bonder](position_bonder.md)

## Direction

Require capital or insurance up front, up to the scale of the harm. Set a floor of minimum capital, compulsory insurance, or a posted bond so the actor holds a stake up to what it can destroy, or pierce the veil when the thinness is contrived, so the excess stops being free and the care returns, at the cost of charging every actor for a harm most will never cause.

## Orders

([The Bonder](persona_the_bonder.md) requires the stake up front, up to the harm the activity can cause, raising the ceiling to meet the harm and charging all actors to do it.)

## Implementation

([The Escrow](place_the_escrow.md) where the stake is posted, sized against [the scene](place_the_scene.md); the [bonding](process_bonding.md) process; the posted stake lifts [the assets](piece_the_assets.md) up to meet [the excess](piece_the_excess.md), so [the thinness](piece_the_thinness.md) can no longer lower the ceiling and [the precaution](piece_the_precaution.md) returns.)

## Targets

- [ ] a stake of capital, insurance, or bond is required up front, sized to the harm the activity can cause
- [ ] the actor is made to hold a stake up to what it can destroy, so the excess is no longer free
- [ ] contrived thinness is met by piercing the veil where the structure is a device to lower the ceiling
- [ ] the cost is borne of charging every actor, including the small and honest, for a harm most will never cause
