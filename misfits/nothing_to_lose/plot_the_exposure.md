# Plot: The Exposure

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Balance Sheet](place_the_balance_sheet.md), an activity whose potential harm runs past everything the actor owns.
The Actor taking on the hazardous work and carrying a liability real in law and capped in fact at its assets.

## Action

The Actor, holding [the actor's seat](position_actor.md), takes on the activity, and its liability is set against [the assets](piece_the_assets.md) that cap what a judgment can collect.

## Stage

The actor at [the balance sheet](place_the_balance_sheet.md) carries an activity that, at [the scene](place_the_scene.md), can cause harm many times its worth, so the ceiling stands between the harm and the recovery and the harm above the ceiling is set as [the excess](piece_the_excess.md).
The liability is complete in law and bounded in fact, the actor fully answerable and answerable only up to what it holds.

## Tension

An actor can cause more harm than it owns, and no judgment can collect from it more than it has.
There is nothing wrong in the exposure: the activity is lawful and the liability real. But the actor's worth is the ceiling on what can be recovered, so the plot closes with the harm the activity can cause set above that ceiling, the excess born as harm no judgment will reach.
