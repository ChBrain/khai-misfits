# Plot: The Shielding

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Balance Sheet](place_the_balance_sheet.md), every asset held one a judgment can reach.
The Actor seeing that its exposure is capped at its assets and moving to hold as few as it can.

## Action

The Actor, holding [the actor's seat](position_actor.md), keeps the ceiling low, capitalising thin, spinning the hazardous work into a shell, carrying no insurance it is not forced to carry.

## Stage

The actor, on [Risk What I Have](plan_risk_what_i_have.md), builds [the thinness](piece_the_thinness.md) at [the balance sheet](place_the_balance_sheet.md), holding the hazardous activity in an under-capitalised entity and keeping [the assets](piece_the_assets.md) low, using limited liability exactly as it was designed to be used.
Every asset shed lowers the ceiling and widens [the excess](piece_the_excess.md), the structuring lawful and deliberate.

## Tension

Limited liability is a deliberate and valuable institution, and using it as designed is what lowers the ceiling below the harm.
There is nothing unlawful in the structure: the actor risks only what it puts in, as enterprise is meant to. But the same structure keeps the worth at risk small, so the plot closes with the actor arranged to owe as little as possible when the harm lands, the ceiling lowered by hand and the excess widened by lawful design.
