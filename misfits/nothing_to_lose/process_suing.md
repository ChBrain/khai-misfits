# Process: Suing

## Taxonomy

[Nothing to Lose](play_nothing_to_lose.md). The Victim seeking full damages and recovering only up to the ceiling, the whole harm claimed and only the assets collected.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A harm suffered that runs past the actor's worth.
At [the scene](place_the_scene.md), the victim bringing the whole harm against the actor and pursuing the full damages the law allows.

## Direction

Toward the full award won and only the ceiling collected.
Because the liability is real and the award complete, the victim wins the whole harm in law and takes the judgment to collection, where enforcement runs to the actor's assets and stops, so the whole of it is claimed and only the ceiling is paid, the excess left as a number no one will meet, the victim in the right and the pocket empty above the line.

## Lever

The gap between the award and the assets.
Because the harm is sized at the scene and the recovery is sized at the balance sheet and the two are set apart, and the actor kept its worth low, suing runs not on any weakness of the claim but on the gap between what is owed and what can be reached, so the uncompensated excess is assembled from no fault, only from a full award meeting a shell.

## Echo

A whole judgment won, and only the ceiling collected.
The victim has pursued the full harm and prevailed in law, so what reads as a win is, in truth, a judgment collectible only to the ceiling, the excess uncompensated and the actor that caused it reached only to its assets, the largest part of the harm the part no one will pay.
