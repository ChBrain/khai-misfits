## Place

## Load Bearing

Without it the whole misfit is a phantom: no genuine good bought, no real rejection anywhere, and the rise elsewhere would be a cost paid for nothing, a pure loss with no offsetting gain to explain it.
With it the trade has its other half honestly: the Bought Calm is load bearing because it is the real payoff, the sensitivity actually pushed below one in the band the designer cares about, the disturbance actually rejected there (Bode). The piece carries the truth the misfit refuses to erase: the quiet in the working band is not a trick and not a coincidence, it is exactly what good design buys, and it is genuinely worth having.

## Apparent

The whole story, the loop simply "working", a job well done and nothing more to say.
Seen at the surface, the Bought Calm looks complete in itself, a smooth and quiet output that reads as success plain and simple, and the truth that this quiet was purchased, that somewhere off the band being inspected an equal debt was incurred the instant the purchase was made, stays hidden behind how finished a calm working band looks when it is the only band anyone measured.
