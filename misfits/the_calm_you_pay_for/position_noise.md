# Position: Noise

## Taxonomy

[The Calm You Pay For](play_the_calm_you_pay_for.md). The seat of the far-band signal, small until the rise finds it, that the raised sensitivity amplifies into the output and toward the edge.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A place in the far band and a smallness that hides it: the standing to sit past the working band, negligible while the sensitivity there is low.
A clear case: the noise is real but tiny, ignored because it lives where nothing was expected and nothing was watched.
No weight of its own until the rise arrives, only a position out where the calm was not bought, waiting to be enlarged.

## Orders

Ride the rise.
Wait at the far frequencies until the sensitivity climbs above one, then be amplified into the output and push the margin toward the edge.
Grow where the loop grew sensitive, and bite where no one was looking.

## Loses

Its harmlessness, the moment the rise reaches it.
Because the seat sits exactly where the conserved total forces the sensitivity above one, the small signal is amplified by the loop's own calm, so the Noise loses the negligibility that let it be ignored, enlarged not by any change in itself but by the rejection bought in the band it does not occupy.
It gives up being beneath notice to the rise, its growth the cost of a calm bought elsewhere.

## Drives

Toward a small signal amplified and a margin eroded,
the far-band noise enlarged by the rise the calm forced,
so the seat that was beneath notice becomes the bill for the quiet bought elsewhere.
