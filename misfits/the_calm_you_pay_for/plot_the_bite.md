# Plot: The Bite

## Taxonomy

[The Calm You Pay For](play_the_calm_you_pay_for.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Far Band](place_the_far_band.md), the sensitivity already above one, no one watching.
Small noise sitting there all along, negligible only while the sensitivity above it was low.

## Action

[Amplifying](process_amplifying.md): [The Loop](persona_the_loop.md), holding [the Loop's seat](position_loop.md), takes the noise living out past the working band and magnifies it, the margin thinning cycle by cycle of the fast stuff.

## Stage

[The Noise](persona_the_noise.md), on [Ride the Rise](plan_ride_the_rise.md), waits at the far frequencies until the risen sensitivity reaches it, then grows in the output and pushes the loop toward the edge.
[The Loop](persona_the_loop.md), still bound by [Conserve the Sum](plan_conserve_the_sum.md), supplies exactly the rise it owes, no more and no less, and the noise it lands on does the rest.

## Tension

The payment was never hidden, only unwatched: it fell precisely where the inspection did not reach.
The plot closes with [the paid rise](piece_the_paid_rise.md) landed on real noise, [the sensitivity](piece_the_sensitivity.md) above one doing out of band exactly what it did below one in band, amplifying instead of rejecting, the margin eroded at the frequencies no one inspected.
