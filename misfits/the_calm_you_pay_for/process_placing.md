# Process: Placing

## Taxonomy

[The Calm You Pay For](play_the_calm_you_pay_for.md). The Designer setting the unavoidable rise where it can be spread widest or where the noise is quietest, the one move the trade leaves open and never a way out of it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A designer who would banish the rise rather than merely site it.
At [the Far Band](place_the_far_band.md), once the rise has already surfaced there, placing begins the moment the designer turns from rejecting the disturbance to managing what the rejection cost.

## Direction

Toward the rise spread wide and set where the noise is quietest.
Because the conserved area can be shaped even if it cannot be shrunk, placing drives the rise thin across a broad stretch, or narrow into a stretch the noise barely touches, so the direction is toward the least harm the fixed total allows, never toward less total.

## Lever

The wish to banish meeting the conserved sum and the Far Wall.
Because the designer wants the rise gone and the theorem will not grant it, placing runs on that wish meeting two limits at once, the integral that cannot be spent to zero and the bandwidth capped by the plant's own non-minimum-phase zeros and delays, so the lever is a negotiation, not a victory.

## Echo

A rise placed where it hurts least, but never abolished, and dearer where the plant is unstable.
The designer has done what the law allows, so what reads as the rise handled is, in truth, the rise only sited: real, still paid, and if the plant would fall on its own, paid at a surcharge the placing cannot discount away.
