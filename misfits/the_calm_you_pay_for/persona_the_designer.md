# Persona: The Designer

## Taxonomy

[Designer](position_designer.md). The one who shapes the loop's sensitivity, pushing it down where the disturbances live and placing the unavoidable rise as harmlessly as the law allows, the actor the trade is worked through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Skilled. Deliberate. Rejecting the trouble where it lives.
Is the one who tunes the loop to answer the disturbance in the working band, the sensitivity pushed down where it matters and the output smooth exactly where anyone would look.
No clumsiness in it: only a capable designer doing the right thing well, buying real rejection precisely where the trouble is.

## Action

Shapes the sensitivity, on [Shape the Sensitivity](plan_shape_the_sensitivity.md), pushing it below one in the working band and spreading the rise it cannot avoid as wide and as harmlessly as the loop allows.
Buys the calm where the disturbance lives and places the cost where the noise is quietest.
Does what a good designer does: rejects the trouble where it is, and manages, as best the law permits, the sensitivity that has to go somewhere.

## Shadow

Not a clumsy tuner making a peak by mistake, a skilled one buying calm by the book, and that is the whole difficulty: the rejection is correct, and the correctness is what forces the rise.
Because the integral of the log-sensitivity is conserved, every scrap of calm the Designer buys below one is paid for by an equal rise above one somewhere else, so the better the rejection they place in the band that matters, the sharper the sensitivity they raise in the band they left, the skill that makes the loop quiet the same skill that makes it peaky.
It rejects the trouble exactly where it lives, and rejecting it exactly there is what raises the sensitivity exactly elsewhere.

## Tell

Says "the loop is quiet: rejection is excellent across the working band."
Is right, truly, and never says what the same tuning did out past the band, so the clean report covers a rise it does not think to mention, at the frequencies no one inspected.
