# Position: Designer

## Taxonomy

[The Calm You Pay For](play_the_calm_you_pay_for.md). The seat of the one who shapes the sensitivity, buying calm where the disturbance lives and placing the rise it cannot avoid.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The freedom to shape the sensitivity and the duty to reject where it matters: the standing to push the loop's sensitivity down in the working band and to choose where the rise is spread.
A clear case: the disturbance is real and sits in the working band, so buying calm there is exactly the right thing to do.
No power to lower the sensitivity everywhere at once, only to move it, because the total is not the seat's to change.

## Orders

Shape the sensitivity.
Push it below one where the disturbance lives, buy the rejection the working band needs, and spread the rise it forces as wide and as harmlessly as the loop allows.
Choose where to be sensitive, since you cannot choose whether.

## Loses

The possibility of calm everywhere, and any rise it might have wished away.
Because the seat can move the sensitivity but not reduce its conserved total, it gives up the hope of a loop quiet across the whole spectrum, so it holds the power to place the rise and loses the power to abolish it, keeping only the choice of where the cost falls.
It surrenders whether to be sensitive to keep where, the total fixed above its reach.

## Drives

Toward calm bought in the band that matters and a rise placed where it hurts least,
the sensitivity moved and never removed,
so the seat that rejects the trouble where it lives can only choose where to pay, never whether.
