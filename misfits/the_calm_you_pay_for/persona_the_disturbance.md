# Persona: The Disturbance

## Taxonomy

[Disturbance](position_disturbance.md). The signal the loop is built to reject, living in the working band, the honest trouble whose presence is the whole reason calm is bought there.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Present. Low. Where the work is.
Is the load, the gust, the drift the loop must answer, sitting at the frequencies where the system does its work and the errors would cost.
No malice in it: only a real disturbance in the band that matters, honestly asking to be rejected where it lives.

## Action

Presses its signal in the working band, on [Press In Band](plan_press_in_band.md), giving the loop a real trouble to reject exactly where rejection counts.
Sits at the frequencies the designer must answer, and makes the calm there worth buying.
Does what a disturbance does: arrives in the band that matters and asks, honestly, to be rejected.

## Shadow

Not a trick that hides where it lives, a real trouble sitting plainly in the working band, and that is the whole difficulty: its honesty about where it lives is what tells the designer where to buy calm, and where to buy calm is where the rise is forced away from.
Because the Disturbance is concentrated in the working band, the loop is shaped to be least sensitive there, and by the conserved total most sensitive somewhere else, so the very clarity of where the trouble lives sends the rise to the band the Disturbance left empty, the honest signal steering the cost off to the frequencies it does not occupy.
It asks only to be rejected where it lives, and being rejected where it lives is what raises the sensitivity where it does not.

## Tell

Sits squarely in the working band, plain and low, exactly where it is expected.
Is honestly placed, truly, and by being honestly placed tells the loop where to be calm, and so where, out of sight, to be sharp.
