# Persona: The Noise

## Taxonomy

[Noise](position_noise.md). The high-frequency signal living out past the working band, small until the rise finds it, that the raised sensitivity amplifies into the output and toward the edge of stability.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Small. Far out. Beneath notice.
Is the measurement hiss, the fast disturbance, the ripple at the frequencies past the working band, tiny and ignored while the sensitivity there is low.
No menace in it, at first: only a little noise where no one was looking, harmless until the loop's rise reaches it.

## Action

Rides the rise, on [Ride the Rise](plan_ride_the_rise.md), waiting at the far frequencies for the sensitivity to climb above one and then being amplified into the output.
Grows where the loop grew sensitive, and pushes the margin toward the edge.
Does what out-of-band noise does: sits small until the raised sensitivity magnifies it, and then bites.

## Shadow

Not a large threat announcing itself, a small one the loop's own calm enlarges, and that is the whole difficulty: the Noise is negligible until the rise lands on it, and the rise lands on it precisely because the calm was bought elsewhere.
Because the sensitivity that was pushed below one in the working band is forced above one out where the Noise lives, the loop amplifies exactly the signal it was never watching, so the Noise that would have stayed small is enlarged by the very rejection that quieted the band that mattered, the cost of the calm made flesh at the frequencies no one inspected.
It is nothing until the rise arrives, and the rise arrives because the calm was bought where it was not.

## Tell

Sits quiet and far out, until the output starts to ripple and the margin thins.
Was beneath notice, truly, and shows itself only once the rise has found it, the first sign of the paid cost a peak where no one was watching.
