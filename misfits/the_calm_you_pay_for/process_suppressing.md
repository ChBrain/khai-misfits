# Process: Suppressing

## Taxonomy

[The Calm You Pay For](play_the_calm_you_pay_for.md). The sensitivity pushed below one in the working band, the disturbance met exactly where it lives, the first and correct half of the trade.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A disturbance already living in the working band.
At [the Working Band](place_the_working_band.md), the trouble arrives at frequencies the designer can see coming, and suppressing starts the moment those frequencies are known well enough to press against.

## Direction

Toward the sensitivity pushed below one there, calm bought.
Because the disturbance's frequencies are known, suppressing drives the gain from disturbance to error down across exactly that stretch, so the direction is a narrowing of the error where the trouble lives, the output made smooth precisely where anyone would look.

## Lever

The design meeting the loop where the trouble lives.
Because feedback can be shaped to answer a known band, suppressing runs on the designer's choice of where to press meeting the loop's capacity to be pressed there, so the lever is not force but placement: the same gain, aimed.

## Echo

The working band quiet, the error small, the rejection real and correctly placed.
The disturbance has been met exactly where it lives and the calm that follows is genuine, so what reads as a loop simply working well is, in truth, a trade half made: correct in every particular, and not yet the whole of the bill.
