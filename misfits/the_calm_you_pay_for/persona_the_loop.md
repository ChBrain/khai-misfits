# Persona: The Loop

## Taxonomy

[Loop](position_loop.md). The feedback itself, which conserves the integral of the log-sensitivity, paying every calm bought below one forward as an equal rise above one, the mechanism that makes the trade mandatory.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Balanced. Lawful. Total unchanged.
Is the feedback that carries the sensitivity, lowering it where the designer presses and raising it, by exactly as much, where the designer does not.
No spite in it: only a loop obeying a conservation law, keeping its total fixed while its shape is pushed about.

## Action

Conserves the sum, on [Conserve the Sum](plan_conserve_the_sum.md), matching every area of calm bought below one with an equal area of rise above one, and keeping the integral fixed however the loop is shaped.
Pays the calm forward to the far band and holds the total unmoved.
Does what feedback does: redistributes the sensitivity wherever it is pressed, and never lets the sum change.

## Shadow

Not a fault that adds a peak, a law that moves one, and that is the whole difficulty: the Loop authors no amplification of its own, it only conserves, and conserving is exactly what forces the rise.
Because the integral is fixed, the Loop cannot grant calm anywhere without taking it back somewhere, so it answers every attenuation with an equal amplification not out of malice but out of conservation, the trade mandatory because the total is; and where the plant is unstable the fixed total is not even zero but positive, so the Loop must raise more than it lowers.
It only keeps the sum, and keeping the sum is what binds every calm to an equal rise.

## Tell

Holds the total steady while the shape is pushed down here and up there.
Conserves, truly, and by truly conserving gives back as a rise every scrap of calm pressed into it, the balance kept and the cost merely moved.
