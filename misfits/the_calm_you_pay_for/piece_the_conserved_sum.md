# Piece: The Conserved Sum

## Taxonomy

[The Calm You Pay For](play_the_calm_you_pay_for.md). The integral of the log-sensitivity held fixed over all frequencies, the law that binds every calm bought in one band to an equal rise paid in another.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Held across the whole of [the spectrum](place_the_spectrum.md), the one total no single band, [the working band](place_the_working_band.md) or [the far band](place_the_far_band.md), can be judged apart from.

## Load Bearing

Without it the rise would be a choice, a bad tuning the designer could in principle correct away, and the whole misfit would collapse into a story of a design that could have been better.
With it the trap has its driver: a fixed total, the integral of the log of the sensitivity summing to zero for a stable loop with enough poles over zeros, so that every scrap of attenuation pressed below one anywhere is matched, exactly and not approximately, by an equal measure of amplification pressed above one somewhere else. The Conserved Sum is load bearing because it is the theorem itself (Bode): the quantity that turns a design choice into a law, so that pushing the sensitivity down in the working band does not just tend to raise it elsewhere, it must, and the piece carries that necessity, the visible pole of the trap against which no clever tuning can claim exemption.

## Apparent

No accounting at all, each frequency judged on its own, a good number here and a bad number there with nothing tying them together.
Seen at the surface, the Conserved Sum is invisible: no single measurement at any one frequency shows a total, and the truth that every one of those separate numbers is a term in one sum fixed in advance, that the good reading here was never free of the bad reading there, stays hidden behind how naturally an engineer inspects a design one frequency, one band, one bode plot at a time.

## Yearbook

Held by [the Loop](persona_the_loop.md) through [Conserving](process_conserving.md), the total neither raised nor lowered by any tuning, only redistributed.
It is the anchor of the trap: the Conserved Sum binds [the bought calm](piece_the_bought_calm.md) to [the paid rise](piece_the_paid_rise.md) as two readings of one fixed law, turns positive as [the unstable surcharge](piece_the_unstable_surcharge.md) when the plant would fall on its own, and is spent, never zeroed, against [the far wall](piece_the_far_wall.md) that [the Designer](persona_the_designer.md) presses on and cannot move.
