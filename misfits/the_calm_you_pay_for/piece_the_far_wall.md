# Piece: The Far Wall

## Taxonomy

[The Calm You Pay For](play_the_calm_you_pay_for.md). The bandwidth limit set by non-minimum-phase zeros, delays, and finite bandwidth, capping how far and how thin the paid rise can ever be spread, so it can be placed but never abolished.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Stands at the far edge of [the far band](place_the_far_band.md), the boundary [the spectrum](place_the_spectrum.md) will not let the rise cross without cost, no matter how it is placed.

## Load Bearing

Without it a designer could spread the paid rise as wide as wished, over an unbounded band, thin enough at every frequency to be, in practice, no cost at all: an escape by dilution.
With it the escape is closed at the root: the Far Wall is load bearing because non-minimum-phase zeros, delays, and finite bandwidth cap the band over which the rise can be distributed (Freudenberg and Looze), so the same total forced by [the conserved sum](piece_the_conserved_sum.md) must sit inside a narrower and narrower window the harder the plant's own structure limits it. The piece carries the no-hero limit of the misfit: the rise can be moved, it cannot be thinned to nothing, because the room to thin it into is not the designer's to set.

## Apparent

An obstacle of engineering, a limit better hardware or a cleverer controller could push back.
Seen at the surface, the Far Wall looks like a shortfall of means, as if enough ingenuity in [Placing](process_placing.md) could widen the room to spread the rise until it vanished, and the truth that the wall is set by the plant's own zeros, delays, and bandwidth, not by the skill of whoever tunes it, and moves for no controller however good, stays hidden behind how much a well-placed rise can be made to look like a solved problem rather than a bounded one.

## Yearbook

Met by [the Designer](persona_the_designer.md) through [Placing](process_placing.md), pressing [the paid rise](piece_the_paid_rise.md) as wide and as thin as the room allows.
It is the limit the whole trade runs into: the Far Wall bounds how far [the conserved sum](piece_the_conserved_sum.md) can be spread and sharpens the toll of [the unstable surcharge](piece_the_unstable_surcharge.md) into a narrower window, the wall the play's placing closes on and the one thing in the misfit that placement never moves.
