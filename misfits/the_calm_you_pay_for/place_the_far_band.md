# Place: The Far Band

## Taxonomy

[The Calm You Pay For](play_the_calm_you_pay_for.md). Out past the working band, where the paid rise lands and the noise already lives, capped at its widest by the Far Wall of zeros, delays, and finite bandwidth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The sensitivity risen above one, the noise amplified, the margin thinner than it was, at frequencies no one was watching.
Nothing here announces itself: the rise shows up only to an instrument tuned to look for it, so what is shown, to anyone who checks, is a stretch of the axis quietly paying for the calm bought somewhere else.

## Holds

The noise, the paid rise, and the wall that limits where it can spread.
The Far Band holds the frequencies the disturbance never reached and the amplification that arrived there anyway, and holds too the Far Wall itself: the plant's own non-minimum-phase zeros, delays, and finite bandwidth, past which the rise cannot be pushed.

## Offers

A place to put the rise that cannot be avoided, freely, and a hand in shaping how it lands.
Because the Far Band is where the conserved sensitivity goes once it is refused the working band, it offers the designer somewhere to set it: wide and shallow, or narrow and where the noise is quietest, the placement real even where the amount is not the designer's to choose.

## Withheld

Room to spread the rise arbitrarily thin.
The Far Band cannot absorb the rise without limit, because the Far Wall caps the bandwidth available to it, so what it withholds is any spreading past that wall: the rise can be placed within what the wall allows, never thinned past it, and never abolished.
