# Process: Amplifying

## Taxonomy

[The Calm You Pay For](play_the_calm_you_pay_for.md). The sensitivity above one in the far band meeting the noise that already lives there, the rise cashed out as louder output and a margin nearer the edge.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The sensitivity already above one in the far band.
At [the Far Band](place_the_far_band.md), once the conserved rise has surfaced there, amplifying begins the moment that raised gain meets a noise floor that was, until then, harmless.

## Direction

Toward the noise amplified into the output and the margin driven toward the edge.
Because a sensitivity above one takes whatever disturbs the loop at those frequencies and makes it larger rather than smaller, amplifying drives the far band's noise into the output cycle by cycle, so the direction is a loop growing louder and nearer instability exactly where it was never being watched.

## Lever

The raised sensitivity meeting the noise that lives there.
Because the far band is never empty, some noise floor always sits at those frequencies, amplifying runs on the rise meeting that floor, so the lever is not a new disturbance but an old one, small until the gain above it made it large.

## Echo

The output noisier, the loop nearer instability, the cost landing where no one watched.
The calm bought in the working band has arrived, at last, as a price, so what reads as an unrelated noisiness in the output is, in truth, the same trade's other half, cashed out at the frequencies the inspection never reached.
