# Plot: The Placing

## Taxonomy

[The Calm You Pay For](play_the_calm_you_pay_for.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Far Band](place_the_far_band.md), the rise sitting sharp and unmanaged.
The Designer would banish it, if banishing were on offer.

## Action

[Placing](process_placing.md): [The Designer](persona_the_designer.md), holding [the Designer's seat](position_designer.md), spreads the rise wide to keep it shallow or sets it where the noise is quietest, working the one trade the loop allows.

## Stage

[The Designer](persona_the_designer.md), on [Shape the Sensitivity](plan_shape_the_sensitivity.md), presses the rise thin across a wider stretch of the far band, or moves it to sit under the quietest noise, choosing where to be sensitive since whether is no longer a choice.
[The Loop](persona_the_loop.md), on [Conserve the Sum](plan_conserve_the_sum.md), holds the total the Designer is placing against, unmoved by where the rise is put, and against [the Far Wall](piece_the_far_wall.md) of the plant's own zeros, delays, and finite bandwidth, which caps how far and how thin it can be spread.

## Tension

The Designer can choose where the loop hurts, never whether it hurts, and an unstable plant makes the bill dearer still.
The plot closes with the rise placed where it lands lightest, spread or set against [the conserved sum](piece_the_conserved_sum.md) and the Far Wall ... but not abolished, [the paid rise](piece_the_paid_rise.md) still owed and, where the plant is unstable, owed at the surcharge rate.
