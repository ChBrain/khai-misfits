# Piece: The Sensitivity

## Taxonomy

[The Calm You Pay For](play_the_calm_you_pay_for.md). The gain from a disturbance to the error it leaves, read across every frequency at once, below one where the loop rejects and above one where it amplifies, the single quantity the whole design shapes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Read across [the spectrum](place_the_spectrum.md), the full frequency axis where the one function takes every value it will ever take.
Pushed down inside [the working band](place_the_working_band.md), where the disturbance lives and the design wants it small.
Pushed up out past it in [the far band](place_the_far_band.md), where nothing was asked of it and it rises anyway.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no loop to speak of, only a plant and a controller with nothing yet said about what either does to a disturbance: no below one, no above one, no trade to name.
With it the whole trap has its unit of account: a single function of frequency, the gain from disturbance to error, that the design pushes down here and that, by no separate decision, rises there. The Sensitivity is load bearing because it is the quantity Bode's integral is written over (Bode): every other piece, the calm bought, the rise paid, the sum conserved, the surcharge and the wall, is a statement about this one function at different frequencies, so without it there is nothing left for the law to bind.

## Apparent

A single verdict, is the loop sensitive or not, a yes-or-no read off one number.
Seen at the surface, the Sensitivity looks flat, as if a loop were simply calm or simply jumpy, and the truth that it is a curve, small in one band and large in another, the same loop rejecting here and amplifying there at once, stays hidden behind the habit of asking whether a design "works" as if one answer covered every frequency it will ever meet.

## Yearbook

Shaped by [the Designer](persona_the_designer.md) through [Shape the Sensitivity](plan_shape_the_sensitivity.md), pushed down where the disturbance lives and left to rise where it does not.
It is the substrate of the trap: the Sensitivity is bought low as [the bought calm](piece_the_bought_calm.md) and forced high as [the paid rise](piece_the_paid_rise.md), the two readings of one function bound by [the conserved sum](piece_the_conserved_sum.md), through [Suppressing](process_suppressing.md) in the band that matters and [Amplifying](process_amplifying.md) in the band that does not.
