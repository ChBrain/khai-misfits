# Persona: The Administrator

## Taxonomy

[Administrator](position_administrator.md). The one who adds each verification, form, and wait for a defensible reason, doing their duty in good faith, the earnest hand the burden accretes under.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Conscientious. Accountable. Adding each step for a reason.
Is the one who runs the programme by the book, requiring the proof, checking the eligibility, keeping the rolls accurate, answerable for every pound of public money.
No malice in them: only a diligent official adding what duty and audit and fairness require, each step defensible on its own.

## Action

Adds the step, on [Add the Step](plan_add_the_step.md), requiring a verification against fraud, a document to confirm eligibility, a wait to be sure, a renewal to keep the rolls true.
Justifies each requirement by the risk it guards and the duty it serves.
Does what a conscientious administrator does: asks only what is reasonable, and can give a good reason for every single ask.

## Shadow

Not an official building a barrier, one adding fair steps that sum to one, and that is the whole difficulty: each ask is defensible, and the defensibility of each is what hides the weight of all.
Because the Administrator sees the steps one at a time, each with its own good reason, and the Applicant bears them all at once, the burden that deters take-up is invisible from the desk that adds it, so the more scrupulously each requirement is justified the more surely the sum turns the neediest away, the conscience clear on every step and blind to their total.
It adds only what it can justify, and adding only what it can justify is what builds the burden no one meant.

## Tell

Says "it is only one more form, and we do need to verify: that is not a lot to ask."
Is right about the one, truly, and never adds it to the others, so the reasonable ask sits atop a stack it does not see, too much in sum and fair in every part.
