# Plot: The Abandonment

## Taxonomy

[Too Much to Ask](play_too_much_to_ask.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Kitchen Table](place_the_kitchen_table.md), the folder half filled and the evening spent again.
The cost already paid larger than what is left to spend, and the renewal date nearer than the next free hour.

## Action

[Deterring](process_deterring.md): [The Applicant](persona_the_applicant.md), holding [the applicant's seat](position_applicant.md), stops somewhere in the middle, and does not come back to finish.

## Stage

[The Applicant](persona_the_applicant.md), on [Try to Claim](plan_try_to_claim.md), has already spent [the compliance cost](piece_the_compliance_cost.md) it could afford, and finds no more time, health, or ease to spend on the rest.
Leaves the form unfinished, lets the renewal lapse, and no one at the counter ever says no.

## Tension

No one refuses the Applicant: the door stays open, the form stays available, the renewal stays possible.
There is nothing to fault, and nothing to point to: only a person who tried and stopped. But stopping is enough, so the plot closes with an eligible person deterred, folded into [the take-up gap](piece_the_take_up_gap.md), the benefit unclaimed and the door never formally shut.
