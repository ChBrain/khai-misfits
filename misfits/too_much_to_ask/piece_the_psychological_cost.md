# Piece: The Psychological Cost

## Taxonomy

[Too Much to Ask](play_too_much_to_ask.md). The cost of enduring: the stigma, the stress, the frustration, and the loss of standing in having to ask the state for help, paid quietly beneath the forms and the waits.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Felt at [the counter](place_the_counter.md), where the applicant states the need aloud, answers for it, and is looked over by whoever decides whether it is real enough.

## Load Bearing

Without it complying would be merely tedious, a matter of time and paperwork, and nothing in the asking itself would cost anything to the person doing it.
With it the ask carries a weight the forms do not show: proving to a stranger that you are poor enough, sick enough, deserving enough, and doing it again at every renewal, wears at something a document cannot restore. The Psychological Cost is load bearing because it is the cost of enduring (Moynihan, Herd, and Harbage): stigma, stress, frustration, and the loss of standing in asking the state for help, so the piece carries the toll no receipt is issued for, real enough to end a claim that every document was otherwise ready for.

## Apparent

Nothing, or nothing that should count: a little discomfort, a small awkwardness, not a reason to abandon a real entitlement.
Seen at the surface, the Psychological Cost looks like a feeling too soft to matter next to a benefit worth having, and the truth that shame is a cost like any other and spends from the same limited store as the forms and the queue, that it is heaviest exactly where the need and so the asking is greatest, stays hidden behind how easily a cost with no receipt is mistaken for no cost at all.

## Yearbook

Endured by [the Applicant](persona_the_applicant.md) through [burdening](process_burdening.md), the stigma and the standing spent in asking.
It is the third cost of the trap: the Psychological Cost is paid alongside [the compliance cost](piece_the_compliance_cost.md) at every [step](piece_the_step.md) the Administrator has set, outlasts the form once filled, and where it is what finally exceeds the store, tips the claim, quietly and without a refusal, into [the take-up gap](piece_the_take_up_gap.md).
