# Plan: Ease the Burden

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), inside [Too Much to Ask](play_too_much_to_ask.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Reformer](position_reformer.md)

## Direction

Auto-enrol the eligible, pre-fill what is known, drop the step that deters more than it catches, and staff the help that carries the applicant through. Spend real effort and money to reach people rather than filter them, and accept the risk the eased steps let back.

## Orders

([The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) lifts a [step](piece_the_step.md) to lower the burden on [the Applicant](persona_the_applicant.md), against [the Auditor](persona_the_auditor.md)'s count of the fraud or error it returns.)

## Implementation

([The Counter](place_the_counter.md) and [the Rolls](place_the_rolls.md); the [easing](process_easing.md) process; lifting a [step](piece_the_step.md) narrows [the take-up gap](piece_the_take_up_gap.md) and eases [the regressive load](piece_the_regressive_load.md) ... at the price of the risk the step guarded, admitted back.)

## Targets

- [ ] the eligible are auto-enrolled or the forms pre-filled where they can be
- [ ] the step that deters more than it catches is dropped
- [ ] real effort and money are spent to reach people rather than filter them
- [ ] the take-up gap narrows, or the risk let back is judged too dear and the steps stay
