# Persona: The Applicant

## Taxonomy

[Applicant](position_applicant.md). The intended beneficiary who faces the steps and bears their cost, the eligible person the programme is for and the one it deters, the actor the trap is spent through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Eligible. Trying. Short on time and ease.
Is the person who qualifies for the benefit and sets out to claim it, gathering the papers, filling the forms, sitting in the wait, doing what is asked.
No fault in them: only someone entitled to help, working through the steps with whatever time and health and patience they can spare.

## Action

Tries to claim, on [Try to Claim](plan_try_to_claim.md), finding out whether they qualify, gathering the documents, and enduring the queue and the questions.
Bears the cost of each step at the kitchen table and the counter, and carries on as long as they can.
Does what an eligible applicant does: works through the asks, one after another, until the benefit is claimed or the cost is more than they can pay.

## Shadow

Not a person who will not try, one who tries and is worn down, and that is the whole difficulty: they are exactly who the programme is for, and being exactly who it is for is being who has least to spend on reaching it.
Because the burden of claiming falls on the applicant and the neediest have the least time, health, and ease to bear it, the Applicant is likeliest to give up somewhere in the middle, so the more the programme is meant for them the more surely its cost deters them, the help built for them screened by the effort of asking.
It sets out to claim what it is owed, and being least able to bear the asking is what leaves it unclaimed.

## Tell

Says "I will get to the rest of the forms when I have a minute," and the minute does not come.
Meant to finish, truly, and never quite can, worn to giving up by a cost no single step looks large enough to explain.
