# Plot: The Requirement

## Taxonomy

[Too Much to Ask](play_too_much_to_ask.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Counter](place_the_counter.md), a form already in hand and one more line about to be added to it.
The ask ordinary, the reason at hand, the step about to be reasonable on its own.

## Action

[Adding](process_adding.md): [The Administrator](persona_the_administrator.md), holding [the administrator's seat](position_administrator.md), requires a verification, a document, a wait, each justified by the risk it guards.

## Stage

[The Administrator](persona_the_administrator.md), on [Add the Step](plan_add_the_step.md), asks only what duty and audit require, and can give a good reason for the single ask.
[The Auditor](persona_the_auditor.md), on [Guard the Funds](plan_guard_the_funds.md), names the fraud or error the step would catch, and the name is the warrant that puts it in place.

## Tension

Each step, alone, is fair, and the Auditor's risk is real enough to answer for it.
There is nothing to fault: the verification is defensible, the reason is sound, and no one at the counter is asking too much. But the step joins the ones already there, so the plot closes with [the step](piece_the_step.md) in place, fair on its own, and the interaction one requirement longer.
