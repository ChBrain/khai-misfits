# Position: Auditor

## Taxonomy

[Too Much to Ask](play_too_much_to_ask.md). The seat of the risk each step guards, the fraud and error made answerable, where every check finds its warrant and every easing its price.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A real leak to point to and a ledger that counts it: the standing to name the fraud, the error, the misallocation each step exists to catch.
A clear case: wrong payments happen and public trust demands they be caught, so each verification guards something genuine.
No column for the eligible deterred, only for the error found, so the seat sees every wrong payment and no unclaimed benefit.

## Orders

Guard the funds.
Tally the error rate, flag the ineligible claim, and set against any dropped check the fraud or error it would let through.
Make the return of the risk the price of any step removed.

## Loses

Any measure of the harm the checks themselves do, and so any reason to lift one.
Because the seat counts only what the steps catch and not whom they deter, it gives up all view of the take-up they cost, so it loses the ability to weigh a check's real catch against its unseen exclusions, holding the steps in place because their benefit is countable and their harm is not.
It surrenders the unclaimed to keep the caught, guarding a ledger with no line for the door it shuts.

## Drives

Toward every step defended by a real leak and no step ever lifted,
the caught error countable and the deterred eligible not,
so the seat that guards the funds is the reason the burden never falls.
