# Persona: The Auditor

## Taxonomy

[Auditor](position_auditor.md). The risk each step exists to guard, the fraud, the error, the misallocation, personified as the one who would find it, the reason no step can simply be dropped.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Watchful. Rigorous. Counting the errors.
Is the one who looks for the wrong payment, the ineligible claim, the pound that went astray, and reports each one as a failure to be answered.
No cruelty in them: only a guardian of accuracy and public trust, naming real fraud and real error that the steps are there to catch.

## Action

Guards the funds, on [Guard the Funds](plan_guard_the_funds.md), tallying the error rate, flagging the ineligible payment, and warning what each dropped check would let through.
Holds every verification to its purpose and every easing to its cost.
Does what an auditor does: finds the leak the steps were built to stop, and makes its return the price of any step removed.

## Shadow

Not a foe of the applicant, a foe of the error, and that is the whole difficulty: the risk it guards is real, and the reality of the risk is what keeps the steps in place.
Because every verification catches some genuine fraud or error, the Auditor can show a real cost for each step eased, so the burden that deters the eligible cannot be lightened without admitting more of what the steps controlled, the guardian of the funds the reason the door stays heavy, its rigour measuring only the errors caught and never the eligible turned away.
It guards against every wrong payment, and guarding against every wrong payment is what keeps the right ones from being claimed.

## Tell

Says "drop that check and here is the fraud rate that comes back: can we defend that?"
Is right about the leak, truly, and never counts the eligible the check deters, so the ledger it defends shows every pound lost to error and no line at all for the benefit never claimed.
