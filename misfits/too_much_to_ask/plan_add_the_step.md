# Plan: Add the Step

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the administrator's seat](position_administrator.md), inside [Too Much to Ask](play_too_much_to_ask.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Administrator](position_administrator.md)

## Direction

Require the proof, check the eligibility, keep the rolls accurate, and be able to give a defensible reason for every requirement. Ask only what is reasonable, one fair step at a time, each warranted by the risk it guards and the duty it serves.

## Orders

([The Administrator](persona_the_administrator.md) adds a verification, document, or wait, each defensible on its own, the risk [the Auditor](persona_the_auditor.md) names its warrant.)

## Implementation

([The Counter](place_the_counter.md); the [adding](process_adding.md) process; each [step](piece_the_step.md) is added singly and justified singly, the sum invisible from the desk, while the applicant meets them together.)

## Targets

- [ ] each requirement is added for a defensible reason
- [ ] eligibility is checked and the rolls kept accurate
- [ ] every ask is reasonable on its own
- [ ] the steps sum to a burden the desk that adds them cannot see
