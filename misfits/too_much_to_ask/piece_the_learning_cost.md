# Piece: The Learning Cost

## Taxonomy

[Too Much to Ask](play_too_much_to_ask.md). The cost of finding out: that one is entitled, and what the rules and demands are, the first barrier the applicant meets and the one that stands before any form is even reached.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

First met at [the kitchen table](place_the_kitchen_table.md), where an eligible person wonders whether the benefit is theirs to ask for, and what asking will require.
Tested at [the counter](place_the_counter.md), where not knowing the rule in advance costs a trip, a wrong document, a return visit.

## Load Bearing

Without it eligibility would simply be known, and every eligible person would arrive at the first form already sure they belonged there.
With it the barrier starts before any step is reached at all: knowing that one qualifies, and knowing what the programme will ask in return, is itself a thing to be learned, and not everyone learns it, or learns it in time, or learns it right. The Learning Cost is load bearing because it is the cost of finding out (Moynihan, Herd, and Harbage): entitlement and demand both unknown until sought, so the piece carries the first toll, paid or not paid before a single form is filled, deciding who even begins.

## Apparent

Common sense. Look it up, ask around, read the leaflet: a small thing to know before you act.
Seen at the surface, the Learning Cost looks like ordinary homework, easily done by anyone who cares to, and the truth that the rules are written for the office and not the applicant, that entitlement is not announced to the entitled, that not knowing is itself the state of most who qualify, stays hidden behind how obvious the rule looks to the desk that already knows it.

## Yearbook

Paid by [the Applicant](persona_the_applicant.md) through [burdening](process_burdening.md), the not-knowing spent before any form is touched.
It is the first cost of the trap: the Learning Cost opens onto [the compliance cost](piece_the_compliance_cost.md) and [the psychological cost](piece_the_psychological_cost.md) for those who clear it, meets [the step](piece_the_step.md) as its precondition rather than its content, and for those it never resolves for, opens straight onto [the take-up gap](piece_the_take_up_gap.md) without another cost ever being spent.
