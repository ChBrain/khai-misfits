# Piece: The Compliance Cost

## Taxonomy

[Too Much to Ask](play_too_much_to_ask.md). The cost of complying: the forms, the documents, the appointments, and the waits the applicant must produce and endure once the step is known and accepted.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Produced at [the kitchen table](place_the_kitchen_table.md), where the forms are filled and the documents gathered, one drawer and one evening at a time.
Spent at [the counter](place_the_counter.md), where the appointment is kept, the queue is sat, and the papers are handed across for checking.

## Load Bearing

Without it a known entitlement would need only be claimed, and the distance between qualifying and receiving would close in an afternoon.
With it every accepted step becomes a task: a form to fill correctly, a document to find or request, an appointment to keep on a day that is not the applicant's to choose, a wait that spends the hour whether or not it is free. The Compliance Cost is load bearing because it is the cost of complying (Moynihan, Herd, and Harbage): production and endurance both required of the applicant and neither supplied by the office, so the piece carries the labour of the ask, the part of the burden that is work and cannot be shortened by understanding the rule alone.

## Apparent

Paperwork. Tedious, ordinary, the price of any transaction with any office, public or private.
Seen at the surface, the Compliance Cost looks like a bit of admin anyone can push through with patience, and the truth that the form assumes a document you may not have, the appointment assumes an hour you may not own, the wait assumes a day you can afford to spend on it, stays hidden behind how routine the paperwork looks to whoever is not the one filling it in.

## Yearbook

Spent by [the Applicant](persona_the_applicant.md) through [burdening](process_burdening.md), the forms and documents and waits produced and endured.
It is the second cost of the trap: the Compliance Cost follows [the learning cost](piece_the_learning_cost.md) once the applicant knows what to ask, answers directly to [the step](piece_the_step.md) the Administrator has added, sits beside [the psychological cost](piece_the_psychological_cost.md) in what is spent at once, and where the store runs out before it is paid, empties into [the take-up gap](piece_the_take_up_gap.md).
