# Pitch: Only What Worked

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Rigorous, self-checking, evidence-led.
The plain sense that a published science is a tested science: every paper passed peer review, the findings are significant, the record is the accumulated knowledge of a field that holds itself to proof and prints what it proves.
Nothing false in any line of it: no data faked, no result invented, each study done as soundly as the next, and the significant ones not better done, only luckier or truer on the one draw that counts.

## Undertow

Under the rigour, a record edited by the result.
Because the literature keeps a study by whether it came out significant and not by how well it was run, the nulls file into the drawer and the significant fill the record, so the sample everyone reads and pools is selected on the very outcome it is read to learn, the effects in it larger than the truth by the tempering evidence that is missing.
The published record that reads as the knowledge of a field is a biased sample of the work the field did, surer than the science because its failures are not counted, filed unseen in a thousand drawers.

## Nerve

The feeling of a finding so peer-reviewed it must be sound, a literature that must know what it prints,
because no paper in it was faked and no reviewer waved a bad method through, only kept the significant over the null,
while underneath the record holds the wins and drawers the losses, so a field reads its own confidence back from a sample it filtered on the result and calls the overstatement knowledge.

## Echo

After the run: a field surer of an effect than its full evidence would allow, the estimate inflated by every null that never printed,
no fraud in the story, only sound studies and the sound preference of each author, reviewer, and editor for the finding over the nothing-to-report,
and the one move that would clear it, pre-registering the work and publishing the null, paying no author a citation and no editor a reader, so the drawer stays full where the correction was meant to land, and the record holds surer than the science without anyone ever faking a result.
