# Plot: The Pooling

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Meta-Analysis](place_the_meta_analysis.md), the published studies on one effect gathered for the sum.
A synthesist with an exhaustive search and a sound method, and a record that holds the significant and not the null.

## Action

[Inflating](process_inflating.md): the filtered record is pooled faithfully, and the estimate comes out larger than the truth.

## Stage

[The Synthesist](persona_the_synthesist.md), on [Trust the Record](plan_trust_the_record.md), gathers and combines [the record](piece_the_record.md) and gets [the inflation](piece_the_inflation.md), the effect above the real one by [the null](piece_the_null.md) that is missing, the bias inherited whole from the sample they were handed.
Every step is right, and the input is filtered.

## Tension

Pooling the published studies faithfully is exactly the rigorous thing to do, and the rigorous thing reproduces the filter that built the studies.
There is nothing sloppy: the search is exhaustive and the method is sound, and the drawered nulls are, by definition, not there to gather. But summing a record that over-represents the significant returns an effect above the truth, so the plot closes with a confident, precise, inflated estimate, the care that made it sharpening the bias rather than correcting it, a right method on a filtered record laundering the filter into a finding the field will trust the more for the rigour.
