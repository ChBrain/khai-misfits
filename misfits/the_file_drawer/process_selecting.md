# Process: Selecting

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md). The outcome filter that keeps the significant and drawers the null, falling first at the bench and again at the door.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Author at the bench and the Gatekeeper at the door, each with a scarce resource and a result to judge.
At [the bench](place_the_bench.md) the write-up is spent on the finding and withheld from the null, and at [the journal](place_the_journal.md) the page is spent on the significant and refused the null, the filter falling twice on the same outcome.

## Direction

Toward a record keyed to the result and not the method.
Because a finding is rewarded and a non-finding is not, selecting points the literature at the outcome: the significant is written up and admitted, the null is filed and refused, so the record admits a study by the very thing the study was run to learn, over-representing the large and the lucky and losing every null that would temper it, the screen reading as soundness while it selects on significance.

## Lever

The reward on the finding, at every gate.
Because the citation, the page, and the career all attach to the significant and none to the null, selecting runs on the plainest incentive, each author spending effort where it pays and each editor a page where it is read, so no one need prefer a false record to bias it, only prefer the finding to the nothing-to-report, and the filter falls with no malice at all.

## Echo

A record filtered on the outcome, and a drawer filling with sound nulls.
What leaves this process is a published literature that keeps the significant and shelves the null, the record edited at the bench and again at the door, so the tempering evidence sits unseen in drawers and the record reads surer than the work that made it, the filter that faked nothing having selected the record by the result.
