# Persona: The Author

## Taxonomy

[The Byline](position_the_byline.md). Runs the work, holds a significant result and a null from equally sound studies, and writes up the one that will print.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent. Honest. Doing good science.
Is the one who runs the experiments and gets, as anyone does, a mix of results: some significant, some null, none faked and none better done than another.
No corner cut: the null is as sound as the win, and the Author knows it, and still weighs which one is worth the months of writing up.

## Action

Writes up the significant result, drafts it, submits it, and files the null in the drawer.
Answers the return the record actually offers: a finding earns a citation, a line on the record, a step toward the next grant, and a non-finding earns a rejection and a shrug, so the labour goes where the reward is.
Does what a working scientist must under a scarce clock: spends the write-up on the result that will be read, and lets the null rest unwritten, not from shame but from arithmetic.

## Shadow

Not a fraud, but the first gate the null never passes, and that is the quiet of it: the drawer fills before any reviewer sees a thing.
Because the Author submits the win and shelves the null, the record is already filtered at the bench, the honest non-finding withdrawn by the very person best placed to know it was sound, so the literature loses its tempering evidence at the source, and the Author, having faked nothing, has still made the record surer than their own results were.
Every win written and every null filed is prudent, and the sum of prudent choices is a shelf of unseen evidence.

## Tell

Says "there is no paper in a null: nobody publishes it, nobody cites it, and I have three findings waiting."
Is right that the null will not print and cannot make it print, so the study that would have tempered the record is the study that stays in the drawer, sound and silent.
