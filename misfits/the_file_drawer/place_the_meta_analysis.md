# Place: The Meta-Analysis

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md). The pooling ground, where the published record is gathered and summed and the inflation surfaces that no single study shows.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The systematic review, the forest plot, the pooled estimate, the exhaustive search of the published literature on one effect.
The Synthesist gathering every printed study and combining them by a sound method, and the confident number that comes out the other side.

## Holds

The sum of the record, and the bias that only the sum reveals.
Here the filtered studies are combined, so the Meta-Analysis holds the place where the distortion becomes visible: invisible in any one paper, each sound on its own, the bias lives in the aggregate, where the pool of a record that over-represents the significant returns an effect larger than the truth, and the more rigorous the synthesis the more exactly it reproduces the filter that built its input.
The Meta-Analysis is where the drawer's absence is felt as a presence, an inflation with no author.

## Offers

A confident, precise, pooled estimate, and the authority of the whole literature behind it.
Because the Meta-Analysis combines every published study, it offers the field a single number with a tight interval and the weight of all the evidence, handing out exactly the authority a careful synthesis earns, and in the same motion it offers a number inflated by the nulls that are missing, so the estimate the field will trust the most is the one the filter has bent the most.

## Withheld

The drawered nulls, and with them the true effect.
The Meta-Analysis can gather everything published and pool it faultlessly, but it withholds what was never published, the nulls that sit in drawers and cannot be searched, so it withholds the one thing that would correct the estimate, and delivers instead a confidence drawn around a record the missing evidence would have pulled down.
