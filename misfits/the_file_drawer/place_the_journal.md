# Place: The Journal

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md). The record's door, where the significant is admitted and the null turned away, and the screen falls on the outcome.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The editor's desk, the reviewers' reports, the scarce pages of an issue, the submissions arriving as findings and non-findings.
The Gatekeeper weighing a significant paper and a null paper for the same limited space, and the reader waiting who came for a result.

## Holds

The screen on the record, and the page it spends.
Here the door decides what the literature keeps, so the Journal holds the second fall of the filter: the significant paper is admitted as a contribution and the null is refused as nothing to report, the screen falling on the result and not the method, so a study is kept by the very outcome it was run to learn, and the record is selected on significance while the door reads as quality.
The Journal is where the filter becomes the record, one accepted finding and one rejected null at a time.

## Offers

A page, a reader, and a citation to what it prints.
Because the Journal spends a scarce record where it is read, it offers the significant paper a place in the literature and the reader who comes for a finding, handing out the reward that makes a finding worth writing, and in the same motion it refuses the null the same page, so the admitted finding gains the citation and the turned-away null gains the drawer.

## Withheld

The null's admission, and any screen on soundness rather than result.
The Journal can accept the finding and reject the non-finding, but it withholds the page from the sound null, so it withholds the one thing that would keep the record honest, a door that admits by method and not by outcome, and the honest non-finding is refused at the threshold that was meant to guard the record's worth.
