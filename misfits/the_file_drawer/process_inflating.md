# Process: Inflating

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md). The pooling that turns a filtered record into an effect larger than the truth, the bias that lives only in the aggregate.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Synthesist, with the published record and a rigorous method to pool it.
At [the meta-analysis](place_the_meta_analysis.md), every printed study on the effect is gathered and combined, the honest synthesis of an accumulated literature.

## Direction

Toward an estimate larger than the truth.
Because the record over-represents the significant and hides the nulls, inflating points the pool upward: the studies that came out large and the false positives that came out lucky are all present and the tempering nulls are all missing, so summing them faithfully returns an effect above the real one, the distortion invisible in each study and manufactured by the aggregate the method is trusted to build.

## Lever

The missing nulls, and the rigour that cannot recover them.
Because the drawered nulls are, by definition, not in the record to gather, inflating runs on an absence no care can fix: the more exhaustive the search and the sounder the pooling, the more faithfully the estimate reproduces the filter that built its input, so the method's rigour sharpens the bias into a confident number rather than correcting it, a right method on a filtered record laundering the filter into a finding.

## Echo

A confident, precise, inflated estimate, and a field's trust behind it.
What leaves this process is a pooled effect above the truth with a tight interval around it, the authority of the whole published literature standing behind a number the missing evidence would have pulled down, so the estimate the field trusts the most is the one the drawer has bent the most, an inflation with no author.
