# Position: The Pool

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md). The synthesist's seat, where the published studies are gathered and combined, and a right method on a filtered record returns an inflated estimate.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The published record, and a rigorous method to pool it.
An exhaustive search and a sound synthesis, everything a reviewer should bring to the accumulated evidence of a field.
No way to gather what was never published, so the pool is built from the record that exists and not the work that was done.

## Orders

Trust the record.
Gather every published study on the effect and combine them, weighting and summing as the method demands, and let the accumulated evidence speak.
Pool what can be seen, since the drawered nulls are, by definition, not there to gather.

## Loses

The missing nulls, and with them the estimate's honesty.
Because the record over-represents the significant, the pool of it overstates the effect, and the more faithfully the seat combines the published studies the more exactly it reproduces the filter that built them, so the rigour sharpens the bias into a confident, precise, inflated number.
It pools the record faithfully and loses the truth, the care laundering the filter into a finding.

## Drives

Toward an effect larger than the truth,
and a tight interval drawn around it,
so the seat that does everything right delivers a bias the field will trust the more for the rigour that made it.
