# Piece: The Record

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md). The published literature, the biased sample that keeps the significant and turns away the null, read as the field's knowledge.

## Owner

## Place

Assembled at [the journal](place_the_journal.md), pooled at [the meta-analysis](place_the_meta_analysis.md), and read everywhere a field looks to know what it has found.
Flows from the door to the reader, the visible sample of the work a field did, standing in for the whole.

## Load Bearing

Without it the filter would sort results with nowhere to accumulate, and no one would mistake a selected sample for the evidence.
With it the selection has a body and an authority: the Record is the published literature, the thing a field reads, cites, and pools, and it holds the significant and not the null, so it is a sample of the work drawn on the outcome. The Record is load bearing because it is where the filter is mistaken for knowledge (Sterling): the biased sample read as the whole, so the piece carries the object the trap turns on, a literature that is not the evidence but the evidence that passed a screen, trusted as the field's knowledge because it is all the field can see.

## Apparent

Just the knowledge of a field, the accumulated, peer-reviewed literature, the evidence there is.
Seen at the surface, the Record looks like the sum of what is known, every paper reviewed and sound, and the truth that it is a sample selected on the result, that the nulls it lacks are as real as the findings it holds, that reading it as the evidence reproduces the filter that built it, stays hidden behind how much a peer-reviewed literature reads like the truth.
