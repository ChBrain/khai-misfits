# Position: The Threshold

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md). The record's door, where the significant is admitted and the null turned away, the screen falling on the outcome and not the method.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A scarce page and a mandate: to keep the record novel, sound, and read.
A real constraint: a limited literature that a reader comes to for findings, so the page must go where it is read.
No way to fill the record with nulls and keep it readable, and no reader who comes for a non-finding, so the sound null has no claim on the scarce page.

## Orders

Admit the novel.
Accept the significant paper and reject the null, screening by whether a submission carries a finding a reader will read.
Spend the record where it earns a reader, since a page on a non-finding is a page unread.

## Loses

The null, and with it any screen on method rather than result.
Because the Threshold keeps the significant and turns away the null, the record admits a study by the very outcome it was run to learn, so the literature over-represents the large and the lucky and refuses the honest non-finding at the door, the screen reading as quality while it selects on the result.
It keeps the finding and loses the null, and the record is selected on the outcome it was meant to report.

## Drives

Toward a record selected on significance,
and a sound null refused at the door,
so the seat that guards the record's worth is the seat that edits it by the result.
