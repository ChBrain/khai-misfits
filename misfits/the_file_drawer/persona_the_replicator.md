# Persona: The Replicator

## Taxonomy

[The Check](position_the_check.md). Runs the published study again, gets a null, and finds the correction is a non-finding the same filter drawers.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sceptical. Rigorous. Science's own correction.
Is the one who doubts a published finding and does the honest thing, runs it again, carefully and at power, to see whether it holds.
No agenda wished on them: they want only to know if the effect is real, and they carry the mechanism a science trusts to catch its false positives.

## Action

Replicates the published study, gets a null, and finds the effect does not hold, the correction the record needs.
Takes the failed replication to the same record, where it is a non-finding, screened by the same filter that kept the original, so the correction meets the door the error walked through.
Does what self-correction demands and runs into what self-correction cannot pass: a null is a null whether it is a first result or a check, and the filter does not ask which.

## Shadow

Not ignored on the merits, but drawered on the form, and that is the cruelty: the replication that would correct the record is refused by the record for the very reason it matters, that it found nothing.
Because a failed replication is a null and nulls do not print, the correction cannot enter the literature it would correct, so the false positive stands unchallenged in the record while the challenge sits in a drawer, and the science that is supposed to be self-correcting cannot correct through a filter that keeps only the positive.
The check is sound and the check is a null, and a filter blind to everything but significance drawers the check with every other null.

## Tell

Says "I ran it three times at high power and it is not there: the original was a fluke."
Is right that the effect is not there and cannot get the finding of its absence into print, so the fluke keeps its place in the record and the correction keeps its place in the drawer.
