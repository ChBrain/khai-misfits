# Plot: The Write-Up

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Bench](place_the_bench.md), one significant result and one null from equally sound work.
An author with months to write and a plain arithmetic: a finding pays and a non-finding does not.

## Action

[Selecting](process_selecting.md) at its first fall: the write-up is spent on the significant and withheld from the null, and the drawer takes the rest.

## Stage

[The Author](persona_the_author.md), on [Write the Win](plan_write_the_win.md), drafts and submits [the significant](piece_the_significant.md) and files [the null](piece_the_null.md), spending the labour where the citation is, so [the record](piece_the_record.md) is filtered before any reviewer sees it.
Nothing is faked, and the null is as sound as the win: only one of them is worth the months.

## Tension

Writing up the finding and filing the null is the prudent spend of a scarce clock, and the prudent spend is what filters the record at the source.
There is no failing: a working scientist puts the write-up where it earns a career, and the null earns none. But the non-finding that stays in the drawer is the evidence that would have tempered the record, so the plot closes with the win submitted and the null shelved, the literature edited at the bench by the one person best placed to know the non-finding was real.
