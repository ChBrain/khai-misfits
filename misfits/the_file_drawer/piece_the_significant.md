# Piece: The Significant

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md). The result that clears the threshold, the finding the record keeps because it came out significant.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Made at [the bench](place_the_bench.md) and admitted at [the journal](place_the_journal.md), the result that passes both falls of the filter.
Flows from the study to the record, the one outcome, of the several a field's honest work produces, that earns a write-up and a page.

## Load Bearing

Without it the filter would have nothing to keep and no record to fill, and the drawer would hold every result alike.
With it the record has its content: the significant is the result rewarded at each gate, so it is written up where the null is filed and admitted where the null is refused, and the literature is built of it. The Significant is load bearing because it is what the filter selects for (Sterling): not the better-done study but the one that came out significant, so the piece carries the outcome the record is keyed to, the finding that is kept not for how it was made but for how it turned out, the true effect and the lucky false positive admitted alike.

## Apparent

Just a discovery, a real effect found and reported, knowledge added to a field.
Seen at the surface, the Significant looks like the plain product of good science, a finding earned and published, and the truth that it was kept for its outcome and not its soundness, that a null from the same sound bench was refused beside it, that being significant is not being right, stays hidden behind how much a finding reads like knowledge.
