# Place: The Bench

## Taxonomy

[The File Drawer](play_the_file_drawer.md). The lab where the studies are run and the results sort into the write-up and the drawer, the filter's first fall.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The lab, the field site, the desk where the analysis lands and the results come in as they come, some significant and some null, all from the same sound work.
The Author reading the output, the finding on one side and the non-finding on the other, and the drawer at their elbow.

## Holds

The first fall of the filter, and the choice of what to write.
Here the results are equally sound and unequally rewarded, so the Bench holds the moment the record is filtered before any reviewer sees it: the finding goes to the draft and the null goes to the drawer, not by any failing of the null but by the arithmetic of a citation, so the literature is edited at the source by the person best placed to know the non-finding was real.
The Bench is where the drawer starts filling, quietly, one prudent choice at a time.

## Offers

A finding to write, and a career to build on it.
Because the Bench turns sound work into results that can be reported, it offers the Author the significant paper that earns a citation and a step toward the next grant, handing out real reward for real findings, and in the same motion it offers no home for the null, so the sound non-finding is set aside and the reward and the drawer fill together.

## Withheld

A place in the record for the null, and any reward for reporting it.
The Bench can run the study and read the result, but it withholds the citation, the page, and the career that would make writing up a null worth the labour, so the honest non-finding is the one thing the Bench produces and cannot pay to publish, filed at the source and withheld from the record it would have tempered.
