# Play: The File Drawer

## Estate

[khai-misfits](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-misfits): the house that holds this production and answers for the whole run.

## Name

The File Drawer.
A hundred labs run the same honest experiment, and by chance and by truth some come out significant and some come out null. Now watch which ones the record keeps. The significant result is a finding: it is novel, it is citable, it is worth an author's write-up and a reviewer's yes and an editor's scarce page. The null result is nothing to report: it reads as a failure, it does not advance a career, it does not draw a citation, and its author, weighing the labour of writing up a non-finding against the near-certainty it is rejected, files it in the drawer. Nobody lied. Every study was done as well as the next, and the significant ones were not better done, only luckier or truer on that one draw. But the record now holds the significant and not the null, and the record is what everyone reads. The meta-analyst pools it and gets an effect larger than the truth, because the tempering nulls are missing from the sum. The reader trusts it and believes the effect surer than it is. And the one who doubts and runs it again, and gets a null, has produced a non-finding too, and files it in the same drawer, so the correction cannot enter the record it would correct. Nothing in it is fraud. The author writes up the win, the reviewer prefers the novel, the editor spends the page where it is read, and the sum of every reasonable preference is a literature that overstates what it knows, its failures not burned but filed, present in a thousand drawers and absent from the one record that counts.

## Arc

The bend is this: when the record keeps a result by whether it came out significant and not by how well it was done, the significant enter and the nulls drawer, so the published literature is a biased sample of the work that was done and overstates the effects it holds, a false confidence manufactured by no fraud and corrected by no replication, because the replication that fails is a null and drawers too.
It runs on a filter that falls on the outcome.
The record does not keep a study by how sound its method was, which would keep the significant and the null alike, but by how its result came out, and a significant result is a finding while a null is nothing to report, so the filter selects on the very thing the study was run to learn (Sterling). The screen is not on quality but on outcome, and it falls after the fact.
And so the null files into the drawer.
Offered no publication, no citation, and no career for a non-finding, the author who ran an honest study to a null weighs the labour of writing it up against the near-certainty of rejection and files it, not destroyed but shelved, existing and unseen (Rosenthal). The drawer fills with the evidence that would have tempered the record.
And the record is a biased sample.
What remains in the literature is the significant selected out of the significant and the null, so the published record over-represents the true effects that drew a large sample and the false positives that drew a lucky one, and under-represents every null that would have pulled the estimate down (Sterling). The sample everyone reads is filtered on the result.
And the pool inflates.
The meta-analyst who gathers the published studies and pools them, doing everything right, gets an effect larger than the truth, because the drawered nulls are missing from the sum, and how many sit in drawers, and whether they would overturn the finding, no one can see (Rosenthal; Ioannidis). The distortion is invisible in any one study and lives only in the aggregate.
And the correction cannot enter.
The doubter who runs the study again and gets a null has produced a non-finding, which the same filter drawers, so the failed replication that would correct the record is subject to the record's own screen, and the false positive stands uncorrected while the correction sits in a drawer, a null like any other. The self-correcting science cannot self-correct through a filter that keeps only the positive.
None of it is fraud. The author writing up the win, the reviewer preferring the novel, the editor spending a scarce page where it is read: each is doing the reasonable thing, and the overstated record emerges from the sum, no faked data and no lie, which is exactly why it holds where a fabricated result, that a replication would expose, would not.
Pre-register the study and publish the null, and the drawer might open: but registration is slow and a registered null still wins no citation and no career, so each party's reward runs against the remedy, and the reckoning is a record cleared only by a discipline that pays no one to keep it, not a literature that corrects itself once enough eyes have read it.

## Triggers

**[The Write-Up](plot_the_write_up.md)**
The Author, holding one significant result and one null from equally sound work, writes up the significant and files the null, weighing a citable finding against the labour of a non-finding no journal will take. Closes when the win is submitted and the null is drawered, no fraud in it, only the reasonable spend of effort where it pays.

**[The Admission](plot_the_admission.md)**
Cue: a significant paper and a null paper arrive at the same desk. The Gatekeeper, with a scarce page and a mandate to publish what is novel and read, admits the significant and rejects the null as nothing to report. Closes when the record keeps the finding and turns the null away, the screen falling on the outcome and not the method.

**[The Pooling](plot_the_pooling.md)**
Cue: the published studies are gathered for a meta-analysis. The Synthesist pools them faithfully and gets an effect larger than the truth, because the nulls that would temper it are absent from the record and present only in drawers. Closes when the aggregate overstates the effect, the distortion invisible in each study and manufactured by the sum.

**[The Failed Check](plot_the_failed_check.md)**
Cue: a doubter runs the study again and gets a null. The Replicator's failed replication, the correction the record needs, is a non-finding the same filter drawers, so the false positive stands. Closes when the correction cannot enter the record it would correct, the self-correcting science screened by the very filter it would undo.

## Stakes

Whether a science can know what it has actually found, or is always shown a record edited by the result and made surer than the evidence warrants.
Every plot but the last moves the same way: toward the overstated record, which the author is reasonable to write up, the reviewer to prefer, and the editor to print, and away from the full evidence, which would ask a record to keep the null it has no reason to read. The production cannot make the literature hold its nulls, because each keeps only what its reader will read and the null is nothing to report, so writing up the win is the sound play and admitting the novel the sound screen: the author spending effort where it pays and the editor a page where it is read are each only doing the reasonable thing. It can only show that no one faked a thing, that a filter on the outcome met by a reasonable response overstates what a field knows and hides the tempering in drawers, and that the one move that would clear it, pre-registering the study and publishing the null, is met by the same rewards that filled the drawer, so the correction pays no one to keep it, and the record holds surer than the science, not by anyone's fraud but by the reasonable spend of everyone who reads it.
