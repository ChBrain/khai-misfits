# Plan: Fence the Hour

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the steward's seat](position_steward.md), inside [The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Steward](position_steward.md)

## Direction

Guard a block of time for the deadline-less work and triage the day by worth rather than clock. Act on the important before it becomes a crisis, let an urgent task slip when the important is worth more, and spend the hour where it counts most, not where it is demanded loudest.

## Orders

([The Steward](persona_the_steward.md) fences an hour for the important against the urgent and triages by worth, at the cost of an urgent thing let slip and the closure it would have brought.)

## Implementation

([The Diary](place_the_diary.md); the [fencing](process_fencing.md) process; the hour is held for [the payoff](piece_the_payoff.md) against [the deadline](piece_the_deadline.md), turning [the tyranny of the urgent](piece_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md) back ... if the cost of the urgent let slip can be borne without the closure to justify it.)

## Targets

- [ ] a block of time is fenced for the deadline-less important work
- [ ] the day is triaged by worth rather than by clock
- [ ] the important is acted on before it becomes a crisis
- [ ] the urgent is let slip and its present cost borne, or the loop that closes today wins and the important waits again
