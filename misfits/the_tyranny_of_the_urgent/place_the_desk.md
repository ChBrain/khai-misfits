# Place: The Desk

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md). Where the day's work is done and the urgent lands, the loud surface of ringing phone and pinging inbox where the pressing tasks arrive and are answered.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the work happens and the demands come in, a surface under a stream of calls and messages and forms, each with a clock, each waiting on a reply.
The doer answering what presses, the urgent arriving all day, the loops closing one after another as the desk is worked and cleared.

## Holds

The pressing and the answering, and the hour they take.
Here the urgent claim meets the doer, so the Desk holds the point where the pressing task gets answered: a deadline falling due and a worker who attends to it, so it is where the hour is spent on the thing with the clock, the loud demand always nearer to hand than the quiet worth.

## Offers

A stage for the answered task and the closed loop.
Because the Desk is where the pressing demands arrive and are dealt with, it offers the doer a steady supply of closable things and the quick relief of finishing them, so it hands out the productive, satisfying clearance of a busy day, and hands it out at the cost of the hours it spends on whatever fell due, never on the worth that made no sound.

## Withheld

The quiet the important work needs, and any sight of its worth.
The Desk cannot both answer the loud stream of the urgent and hold an undisturbed hour for the deadline-less important, so it withholds the quiet the worthy work requires, letting the pressing tasks fill the day, and, all closed loops and met deadlines, it withholds any sign that the busy hours it clears so well were spent almost entirely on the things that mattered least.
