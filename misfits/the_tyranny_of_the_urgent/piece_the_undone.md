# Piece: The Undone

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md). The sum over a life, the important never done, the few things that truly mattered left unfinished by a hundred reasonable deferrals.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Accrues across [the backlog](place_the_backlog.md), the worthy work that was deferred each day and never reached.
Weighed only from [the diary](place_the_diary.md), where a life of hours is seen for what it was spent on.

## Load Bearing

Without it the deferrals would be a daily inconvenience, not a life miscast, and the trap would cost little.
With it the whole story turns: a hundred sound postponements summing to the important never done, a working life of met deadlines with the few things worth it left unfinished. The Undone is load bearing because it is the cost the trap hides (Hummel): the sum of the deferrals, the worth never realised, so the piece carries the reckoning that lives only across time, invisible in any single hour and total across a life, the price of an urgency that won every day.

## Apparent

Nothing on any given day, a busy life well filled, a person who worked hard and got a lot done.
Seen at the surface, the Undone does not appear: each day looks productive and each deferral trivial, and the truth that the sum is a life spent on trivia, that the worthy work was never once done, that the busyness was the very thing that hid the loss, stays hidden behind a long record of full and diligent days that never adds itself up.

## Yearbook

Summed from every [deferral](process_deferring.md) of [the payoff](piece_the_payoff.md), the important never done.
It is the cost of the trap: the Undone is the reckoning of a hundred [someday](piece_the_someday.md) postponements, the worth [the deadline](piece_the_deadline.md) took an hour at a time, the life the [tyranny of the urgent](piece_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md) spends on what fell due and never on what mattered, undone only where [fencing](process_fencing.md) guards the hour.
