# Pitch: Answer the Urgent

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Busy, responsive, on top of it.
The plain satisfaction of answering the thing that falls due now, meeting the deadline, closing the loop, and clearing a crowded desk by evening.
Nothing false in it: each urgent task is real and has a real claim, and answering it is genuinely productive work.

## Undertow

Under the busyness, the important work that carries no deadline and so never shouts, deferred one more reasonable day and then another.
Because urgency and importance pull apart and only the urgent carries a clock, the hour goes to the thing that falls due now and the important slides to a someday it never quite reaches, so a life fills with answered trivia and the few things that truly mattered go undone.
The productivity meant to keep on top of the work spends the life on the wrong things.

## Nerve

The feeling of clearing the desk and meeting every deadline, sure you have had a productive day,
because every task you answered was real and closed with a satisfying click,
while the one thing worth more than all of them waits deadline-less in the backlog, asking nothing, and is deferred once more.

## Echo

After the run: a working life of met deadlines and closed loops, spent almost entirely on things that shouted,
no one idle and no one foolish, only real urgent tasks answered and the important postponed by a hundred sound choices,
and the few things that were worth a life, left undone, because each could always wait one more day and never once fell due.
