# Plot: The Sum

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Backlog](place_the_backlog.md), the urgent stream never ending and the deferrals compounding.
Day after day of pressing tasks answered, the someday receding week on week, the years quietly gathering behind the full days.

## Action

[Deferring](process_deferring.md): [The Important](persona_the_important.md), holding [the important's seat](position_important.md), is deferred again and again, each postponement sound, and the deferrals accrue across a working life.

## Stage

[The Doer](persona_the_doer.md), on [Answer the Urgent](plan_answer_the_urgent.md), serves the endless urgent stream day upon day, each hour spent on what fell due, each day full and productive.
[The Important](persona_the_important.md), on [Wait Its Turn](plan_wait_its_turn.md), is postponed each of those days by a reasonable choice, and the one-more-days pile silently into years.

## Tension

Every single deferral is sound, and the sum of the sound deferrals is the important never done.
There is nothing to fault in any one day: each was busy and productive and each postponement correct. But the urgent never stops and the important never shouts, so the plot closes with a working life filled by met deadlines and closed loops, and the few things that truly mattered standing undone, a life miscast by a hundred right choices.
