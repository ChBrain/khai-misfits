# Place: The Backlog

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md). Where the important waits, the someday list of deadline-less work that is deferred each day and never reached.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the worthy work is kept for later, a list of the big deadline-less things, the strategy and the skill and the deep work, all marked someday and none marked today.
The important sliding down it each day, patient and unpressing, deferred once more to a turn that does not come.

## Holds

The deferring, and the worth it keeps in waiting.
Here the important meets its postponement, so the Backlog holds the point where the worthy work is set aside: a deadline-less task pushed to someday because nothing forces it today, so it is where the great payoff waits unclaimed, the worth held intact and the hour never given.

## Offers

A place to keep the important safe for a better time.
Because the Backlog holds what there is no time for today, it offers the doer the comfort of not losing the worthy work, only postponing it, so it hands out the reassurance that the important is still there and still intended, and hands it out while quietly letting each day's deferral become the next, the someday receding as fast as it is approached.

## Withheld

The free hour the important is waiting for.
The Backlog cannot both keep the worthy work for later and ever supply the later it is kept for, because the urgent stream never leaves a free hour, so it withholds the turn the important waits its whole life for, letting the list grow and never clear, and, all good intentions and no done work, it withholds any sign that safe-for-later is, in the end, the same as never done.
