# Persona: The Doer

## Taxonomy

[Doer](position_doer.md). The one whose hours the tasks compete for, answering the urgent that falls due now, busy and productive, the actor whose day the trap is spent through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Busy. Responsive. Productive.
Is the one at the loud desk who answers what needs answering, meets the deadlines, clears the queue, and ends the day with the loops closed.
No idleness in it: only a diligent worker keeping on top of a stream of real and pressing demands.

## Action

Answers the urgent task in front of them, does the thing that falls due now, and feels the click of the loop closed.
Keeps the desk clear and the deadlines met, choosing, each hour, the claim that presses hardest.
Does what a diligent worker does: attends to what is pressing and gets through the day's demands.

## Shadow

Not a shirker wasting time, a genuinely productive worker, and that is the whole difficulty: answering the urgent is real work, and answering it is exactly what spends the life on trivia.
Because the urgent falls due now and the important can always wait, the Doer's every reasonable triage tilts the hour toward the thing with the clock, so the busier and more responsive they are, the more surely the important slides, and the productivity that clears the desk is the productivity that never reaches the work worth doing.
It answers every real claim, and answering every real claim is what leaves the few that mattered undone.

## Tell

Says "I have been flat out all day: so much to get through, no time for the big thing."
Was flat out, really, and never sees that the big thing has no deadline and so will lose to the small ones every single day.
