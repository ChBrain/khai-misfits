# Position: Important

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md). The seat of the work worth the most, carrying no deadline and so always deferrable, where worth alone commands nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A great payoff and no clock: the worth that would change everything and no deadline to force its doing.
A clear case: it matters more than any of the urgent tasks, and everyone knows it.
No deadline to press the hour and no closure to reward the doing, only a value that waits.

## Orders

Wait its turn.
Offer the payoff, hold the worth, and make no demand, setting no deadline and closing no quick loop.
Be worth the most and ask for nothing, and be deferrable precisely by being patient.

## Loses

Every hour, exactly by never demanding one.
Because it carries no deadline, the seat can always wait one more day, and one more day never costs much, so its very patience is what dooms it, and the work worth a life loses the hour to the smallest urgent thing, deferred not for being doubted but for making no claim.
It keeps its full worth and loses all its time, the thing most worth doing and the thing never done.

## Drives

Toward the payoff held and the hour never claimed,
bought against nothing, because it asks for nothing,
so the seat worth the most is the seat that is always deferred.
