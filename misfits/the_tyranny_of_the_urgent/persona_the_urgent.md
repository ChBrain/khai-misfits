# Persona: The Urgent

## Taxonomy

[Urgent](position_urgent.md). The task that shouts, carrying a deadline that falls due now and a small closable shape, the claim that commands the hour whatever it is worth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Insistent. Loud. Falling due now.
Is the task with the clock on it, the call to answer, the form due today, the small thing someone is waiting on this minute.
No malice in it: only a real claim with a real deadline and a real cost if it slips, asking, honestly, to be done now.

## Action

Presses its deadline and demands instant action, putting a clock on the hour and a cost on delay.
Offers a small, clear, closable shape, so that doing it brings the quick relief of a loop shut.
Does what an urgent claim does: falls due, insists, and gets answered because it must be.

## Shadow

Not a trick, a genuinely time-bound task, and that is the whole difficulty: the deadline is real, and the reality of the deadline is what lets urgency stand in for worth.
Because it falls due now and offers the closure of a quick finish, the Urgent commands the hour whether or not it is worth commanding, so the mind reads its clock as a claim on attention that the deadline-less important cannot make, and the smallest pressing thing outranks the largest patient one, not by mattering more but by falling due sooner.
It asks only to be done on time, and being done on time is what crowds out the work that matters.

## Tell

Says "this has to be done now: it cannot wait."
Cannot wait, truly, and by truly not waiting takes the hour from the thing that could, which is also the thing worth more.
