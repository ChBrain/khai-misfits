# Plan: Wait Its Turn

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the important's seat](position_important.md), inside [The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Important](position_important.md)

## Direction

Hold the worth and offer the payoff, and make no demand. Set no deadline, close no quick loop, and wait for a free hour that the urgent never leaves. Be worth the most and ask for nothing, deferrable precisely by being patient, waiting a turn that does not come.

## Orders

([The Important](persona_the_important.md) waits and offers a payoff that never falls due, making no claim on the hour, so it is deferred not for being doubted but for never insisting.)

## Implementation

([The Backlog](place_the_backlog.md); the [deferring](process_deferring.md) process; [the payoff](piece_the_payoff.md) waits deadline-less as [the someday](piece_the_someday.md) recedes, so the worthy work slides toward [the undone](piece_the_undone.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the worth is held and the payoff offered, without a deadline to force it
- [ ] no claim is made on the hour and no quick loop closed
- [ ] the work waits for a free hour the urgent stream never leaves
- [ ] the important is deferred by its own patience, worth no less and no nearer done
