# Position: Urgent

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md). The seat of the task that falls due now, pressing its deadline and offering closure, where urgency commands the hour whatever the task is worth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A deadline and a closable shape: the standing to fall due now and the small clear finish that rewards being done.
A clear case: the clock is real and the cost of delay is real, so the task honestly must be done today.
No worth of its own to justify the hour, only the deadline, and no need of worth, because the deadline is enough.

## Orders

Demand it now.
Press the deadline, put a clock on the hour and a cost on delay, and offer the quick relief of a loop shut.
Fall due, insist, and be answered because you must be.

## Loses

Nothing, and that is the point: the seat needs no worth to win the hour, only a deadline.
Because it falls due now and offers closure, the Urgent commands attention whether or not it is worth commanding, so it wins the hour on the strength of its clock alone, and the smallest pressing thing outranks the largest patient one, not by mattering more but by falling due sooner.
It loses no contest of worth, because it never enters one, taking the hour on urgency and leaving worth out of the reckoning.

## Drives

Toward the deadline pressed and the hour commanded,
bought with nothing but a clock,
so the seat that need not be worth the hour takes it from the seat that is.
