# Position: Doer

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md). The seat the tasks compete for, answering the urgent that falls due now, where the hour is spent and the important deferred.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A finite day and a stream of claims: the hours to spend and the demands, urgent and important, that press on them.
A clear case: the urgent has a real cost today and answering it is real, productive work.
No clock on the important to force it, and no reward for the hour given to a thing that never fell due.

## Orders

Answer the urgent.
Do the task that falls due now, meet the deadline, close the loop, and keep the desk clear.
Attend to what presses hardest, and get through the day's demands.

## Loses

The hours the important needed, exactly by answering the urgent that pressed for them.
Because the urgent falls due now and the important can always wait, the seat's every reasonable triage tilts the hour toward the thing with the clock, so it cannot keep on top of the pressing work without deferring the worthy work, and the more diligently it answers the stream the more surely the important slides.
It gains a cleared desk and met deadlines, and loses the hours that the few things worth a life required.

## Drives

Toward the urgent answered and the important deferred,
bought against the hours the worthy work needed,
so the seat that would spend the day well spends it on whatever falls due soonest.
