# Process: Answering

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md). The doer doing the urgent now, choosing the task that falls due over the one that is worth more, and taking the closure of the finished loop.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An urgent claim pressing and a doer with the hour to give.
At [the desk](place_the_desk.md), the worker facing the task that falls due now and the important that can wait, and reaching for the one with the clock.

## Direction

Toward the urgent done and the important left for later.
Because the urgent has a real cost today and the important can wait one more day, answering does the pressing task first and takes the click of the loop closed, so the hour goes to the thing that fell due, the choice sound in the moment and the deferral of the worthy work its unmarked cost.

## Lever

The soundness of doing the thing that falls due now.
Because the urgent carries a real deadline and the important genuinely can wait, choosing the urgent in the moment is the correct triage, so answering runs on defensible decisions, the life of trivia assembled from a hundred choices each right on its own, no single one a mistake.

## Echo

A closed loop and a worthy task deferred.
The urgent has been done and its closure felt and the choice was sound, so what reads as a productive hour is, in truth, an hour spent on what fell due and taken from what was worth more, the deferral of the important paid quietly for the relief of the finished thing.
