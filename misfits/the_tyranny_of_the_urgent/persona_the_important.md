# Persona: The Important

## Taxonomy

[Important](position_important.md). The task that is worth the most and asks for nothing, carrying no deadline and so always deferrable, the work a life is made of that never once shouts.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Quiet. Patient. Worth more than all the rest.
Is the large open work with no clock on it, the strategy, the skill, the relationship, the deep thing that would change everything and is due no particular day.
No urgency in it: only a great payoff that waits, making no demand and setting no deadline.

## Action

Waits, and offers a payoff that never falls due, holding its worth without ever pressing for the hour.
Sets no deadline, makes no claim, closes no quick loop, and so gives the Doer no reason today that it must be done today.
Does what important-but-unurgent work does: matters enormously and asks nothing, and is deferrable precisely because it is patient.

## Shadow

Not neglected out of blindness to its worth, deferred because it never insists, and that is the whole difficulty: everyone knows it matters most, and mattering most does not put a clock on it.
Because it carries no deadline, the Important can always wait one more day, and one more day never costs much, so its very patience is what dooms it, and the work worth a life loses the hour to the smallest urgent thing, not because its worth is doubted but because worth alone commands nothing.
It is the thing most worth doing, and being most worth doing is no help at all against being always postponable.

## Tell

Says nothing, sets no deadline, and waits.
Says nothing, and by saying nothing loses every hour to the tasks that do, though it is worth more than all of them together.
