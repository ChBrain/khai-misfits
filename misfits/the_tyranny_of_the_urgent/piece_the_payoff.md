# Piece: The Payoff

## Taxonomy

[The Tyranny of the Urgent](play_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md). The important's real worth, the great value that would change everything and never falls due, the reward that carries no deadline to claim the hour with.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Waits in [the backlog](place_the_backlog.md), held intact and unclaimed, worth the most and asking nothing.
Could be reached only from [the diary](place_the_diary.md), where an hour is fenced for it before the urgent fills the day.

## Load Bearing

Without it the important would be worth no more than the urgent, and there would be nothing lost in deferring it.
With it the trap has its stake: a great value, the strategy or the skill or the deep work, worth far more than any pressing task and due no particular day. The Payoff is load bearing because it is the worth that urgency does not track (the Eisenhower framing; Covey): the value with no clock, so the piece carries the reward that makes the deferral matter, the invisible pole against which the deadline wins the hour, the thing worth a life that never once asks for it.

## Apparent

A someday, a fine thing to get to eventually, no loss in leaving it a little longer.
Seen at the surface, the Payoff looks patient and safe, a value that will keep, and the truth that its worth dwarfs the urgent tasks that outrank it, that its lack of a deadline is exactly what dooms it, that leaving it a little longer each day is how it is never done, stays hidden behind how easily a thing with no clock can be left one more day.

## Yearbook

Held by [the important](persona_the_important.md) through [deferring](process_deferring.md), the worth that never falls due.
It is the stake of the trap: the Payoff is what [the deadline](piece_the_deadline.md) takes the hour from and what [the closure](piece_the_closure.md) cannot pay, the value that recedes into [the someday](piece_the_someday.md) and is lost to [the undone](piece_the_undone.md), the worth the [tyranny of the urgent](piece_the_tyranny_of_the_urgent.md) spends the life not reaching.
