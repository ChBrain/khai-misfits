# Persona: The Chaser

## Taxonomy

[The Chase](position_the_chase.md). Is second and about to forfeit, so bids just past the leader to escape the loss, and becomes the leader by doing it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Behind. Calculating. One bid from the prize.
Is whichever bidder currently sits second, their last bid about to be forfeited for nothing, weighing the one move that turns the loss around.
No stubbornness wished on them: they are not clinging to what they spent, they are looking forward at two outcomes and choosing the better one.

## Action

Bids just above the leader, choosing to pay a little more and win the prize over paying their last bid and winning nothing, the smaller loss taken with clear eyes.
Answers the arithmetic the rule sets: forfeit the whole stake now, or bid one step higher and take the prize, and the step higher is plainly the lesser loss, so the Chaser bids.
Does what being second demands: makes the sound forward-looking raise, and in making it becomes the leader, handing the other bidder the same reason to bid.

## Shadow

Not throwing good money after bad, but climbing a ladder with no top rung, and that is the trap: each raise is correct and the sequence of correct raises has no floor.
Because bidding past the leader is always the smaller loss, the Chaser is right at every step and right past the dollar too, where paying more than the prize to lose a little still beats forfeiting the last bid to lose it all, so the soundness of each move is exactly what carries the escalation past the prize, and two bidders each choosing the lesser loss ruin each other by never once being wrong.
The forward-looking choice is correct and it has no stop, so being right every time is what walks the Chaser toward ruin.

## Tell

Says "one more bid and the prize is mine, and one more bid costs me less than quitting."
Is right on both counts and that is the whole difficulty, because it stays true at every price, so the correct next bid never becomes the wrong one and the climb has no place it is sound to stop.
