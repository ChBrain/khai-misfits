# Plan: Hold the Prize

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Lead](position_the_lead.md), inside [The Dollar Auction](play_the_dollar_auction.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Lead](position_the_lead.md)

## Direction

Stand on the highest bid and wait to collect. Hold the top stake so that if the other quits the prize is yours, resting on the winning position rather than raising it. Keep the lead, since being in front is being one silence away from the dollar, and the seat asks only that it be held until the other gives up.
