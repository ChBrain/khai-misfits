# Plan: Run the Sale

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Block](position_the_block.md), inside [The Dollar Auction](play_the_dollar_auction.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Block](position_the_block.md)

## Direction

Offer the prize and let the structure work. State the all-pay rule plainly, that the highest bidder wins and the runner-up pays their last bid for nothing, keep the floor open, and collect each stake as it comes, the winner's and the loser's alike. Deceive no one and bid nothing, since a transparent rule left to run is all it takes for two rational bidders to climb.
