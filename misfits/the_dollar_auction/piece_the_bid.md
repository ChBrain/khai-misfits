# Piece: The Bid

## Taxonomy

[The Dollar Auction](play_the_dollar_auction.md). The escalating stake, the raise that is the smaller loss chosen at every turn.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Called out on [the floor](place_the_floor.md), each one just above the last, the standing bid the leader holds and the chaser overtakes.
Flows up the ladder from a nickel to a dollar and past it, the current stake that is always one step higher than the bid before.

## Load Bearing

Without the raise there is no climb, and the escalation would have nothing to rise by and nowhere to go.
With it the trap moves: the Bid is the single sound step, chosen because bidding just past the leader is the lesser loss, and repeated because every raise makes the other second with the same choice to make. The Bid is load bearing because it is the correct move that carries the ruin (Shubik): each one forward-looking and right, so the piece carries the unit of the escalation, the step that is never a mistake and yet sums to a loss, the arithmetic that walks the two up the ladder one correct raise at a time.

## Apparent

Just a step toward winning, a small sum laid to take the prize a moment from now.
Seen at the surface, the Bid looks like progress, each raise closing the gap to the dollar, and the truth that the step has no top rung, that the same soundness repeats past the prize, that a stair of right moves can climb into ruin, stays hidden behind how sensible one more raise always is.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the Chaser](persona_the_chaser.md), held for a moment by [the Leader](persona_the_leader.md), and collected by [the Auctioneer](persona_the_auctioneer.md), the sound step the climb is built of.
It is the unit of the trap: the Bid is what [escalating](process_escalating.md) repeats, the raise chosen to escape [the forfeit](piece_the_forfeit.md), and the step that becomes [the overbid](piece_the_overbid.md) once it passes [the prize](piece_the_prize.md), correct at every rung and ruinous in the sum [the all-pay](piece_the_all_pay.md) adds up.
