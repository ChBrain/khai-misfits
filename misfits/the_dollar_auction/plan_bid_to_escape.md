# Plan: Bid to Escape

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Chase](position_the_chase.md), inside [The Dollar Auction](play_the_dollar_auction.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Chase](position_the_chase.md)

## Direction

Raise just above the leader and turn the loss around. Weigh the two outcomes ahead: forfeit the whole last bid now for nothing, or bid one step higher and take the prize, and choose the step higher, the plainly smaller loss. Make the sound forward-looking raise, since being second gives the next bid a positive value at every price and there is no bid at which that stops being true.

## Orders

([The Chaser](persona_the_chaser.md) bids just past the leader to escape the forfeit, and in raising becomes the leader, handing the other the same reason to bid.)

## Implementation

([The Floor](place_the_floor.md); the [escalating](process_escalating.md) process, tightened by [locking](process_locking.md) as the bids near the prize; the Chaser raises [the bid](piece_the_bid.md) to escape [the forfeit](piece_the_forfeit.md), turning up [the overbid](piece_the_overbid.md) once the raises pass [the prize](piece_the_prize.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the raise is chosen as the smaller loss, forward-looking and not a sunk-cost error
- [ ] being second gives the next bid a positive value at every price, with no floor beneath it
- [ ] raising makes the chaser the leader, handing the other the same reason to bid
- [ ] the sound raise repeats past the prize, turning each further bid into pure overbid
