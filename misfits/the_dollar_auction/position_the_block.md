# Position: The Block

## Taxonomy

[The Dollar Auction](play_the_dollar_auction.md). The auctioneer's seat, where the prize is offered under the all-pay rule and both stakes are collected.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A prize of known worth, and a rule that pays the house from both bidders.
A transparent game: the terms stated aloud, the dollar plain to see, the runner-up's forfeit named before any bid is made.
No need to deceive and no bid to make, since the structure does the work and the bidders supply the escalation themselves.

## Orders

Run the sale.
Offer the prize, state the all-pay rule plainly, keep the floor open, and collect each stake as it is bid, the winner's and the loser's alike.
Hold the structure steady, since a rule left to run is all it takes for two rational bidders to climb.

## Loses

Nothing, and that is the point: the Block is the seat that only gains.
Because the rule forfeits the runner-up's bid, the Block collects from the loser as well as the winner, so the harder the two bid the more it takes, and the seat that deceived no one is paid from a ruin it merely opened the floor for.
It offers a fair game and loses nothing to it, the house's take drawn entirely from the bidders' sound choices.

## Drives

Toward a transparent rule left to run,
and two stakes collected from one prize given,
so the seat that plays fair and makes no bid is the one the escalation pays.
