# Piece: The Forfeit

## Taxonomy

[The Dollar Auction](play_the_dollar_auction.md). The all-pay teeth, the runner-up's last bid paid for nothing, the loss the next bid is meant to escape.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Looms over [the floor](place_the_floor.md) for whoever is second, and comes due at [the reckoning](place_the_reckoning.md) when the loser pays their last bid.
Flows from the all-pay rule onto the runner-up, the standing threat that turns a losing position into a paid one.

## Load Bearing

Without it coming second would cost nothing, and the one who was behind would simply stop, and the escalation would end at the prize like any auction.
With it every losing position carries a live loss: because the runner-up pays their last bid for nothing, the Forfeit is what makes bidding one more the smaller loss, so it is the engine of the whole escalation. The Forfeit is load bearing because it is the all-pay rule's teeth (Shubik): the loss that turns not-winning into paying, so the piece carries the reason each raise is rational, the standing penalty for coming second that no bidder can face without preferring to bid again.

## Apparent

Just a threat to escape, a loss looming that one more bid will lift, a spur to win rather than a trap.
Seen at the surface, the Forfeit looks like a reason to try harder, the cost of quitting that a raise avoids, and the truth that it grows with every bid, that escaping it once only sets a larger one behind the next raise, that the thing each bid flees is the thing each bid builds, stays hidden behind how naturally a looming loss reads as a reason to press on.

## Yearbook

Faced by [the Chaser](persona_the_chaser.md), inherited by [the Leader](persona_the_leader.md) the moment they are overtaken, and paid at last by the loser, the penalty the whole climb runs on.
It is the engine of the trap: the Forfeit is what [escalating](process_escalating.md) flees and [locking](process_locking.md) grows, the loss [the bid](piece_the_bid.md) is raised to escape, and the teeth that make [the all-pay](piece_the_all_pay.md) bite, the standing cost of second place that gives every next bid its reason.
