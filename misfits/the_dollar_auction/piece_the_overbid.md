# Piece: The Overbid

## Taxonomy

[The Dollar Auction](play_the_dollar_auction.md). The sum bid past the prize's worth, the pure loss the escalation adds beyond the value it was chasing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Accrues on [the floor](place_the_floor.md) once the bids climb past the prize, and is tallied at [the reckoning](place_the_reckoning.md) as the winner's overpayment.
Flows from every raise made beyond the dollar, the part of the bidding that buys nothing because the prize was already worth less than the bid.

## Load Bearing

Without it the escalation would stop at the prize and the trap would be no worse than a hard-fought auction that paid a fair price.
With it the ruin becomes real: because the same smaller-loss logic holds past the dollar, the Overbid is the amount bid beyond the prize's worth, the pure waste the structure adds once the bidding exceeds the value. The Overbid is load bearing because it is where the escalation becomes loss (O'Neill; Leininger): the bidding past the value, so the piece carries the measure of the ruin, the sum both bidders pour out beyond what the prize was ever worth, correct at every step and wasted in whole.

## Apparent

Just one more sensible raise, the smaller loss taken again, indistinguishable at the moment from the bids that came before.
Seen at the surface, the Overbid looks exactly like a sound bid, because it is one, and the truth that it buys a dollar for more than a dollar, that every raise past the prize is a pure loss dressed as a prudent step, that the arithmetic stays right while the sense of it is gone, stays hidden behind how identical the ruinous raise is to the reasonable one.

## Yearbook

Poured out by both bidders past the prize and counted against the winner at the end, the pure loss the trap is measured by.
It is the ruin of the trap: the Overbid is what [locking](process_locking.md) makes inevitable and [ruining](process_ruining.md) tallies, [the bid](piece_the_bid.md) grown past [the prize](piece_the_prize.md), the waste [the all-pay](piece_the_all_pay.md) extracts once the climb has passed the value it began by chasing.
