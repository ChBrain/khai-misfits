# Persona: The Leader

## Taxonomy

[The Lead](position_the_lead.md). Holds the highest bid with the prize in reach, and becomes the one who is second the moment the other raises.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ahead. Poised. Almost paid.
Is whichever bidder currently holds the top bid, the prize about to be theirs, the winning stake standing until someone speaks over it.
No greed wished on them: they are simply in front, one raise away from a dollar, holding the position anyone would want and no one keeps for long.

## Action

Holds the lead and waits to collect, standing on the highest bid until the other either quits, handing them the prize, or raises, taking it away.
Answers the position they are in, which is enviable until the instant it is not, since the same rule that would pay them the prize turns, on the next bid, into the forfeit that makes them bid again.
Does what leading invites: rests on the winning bid, and finds that resting is only a pause before becoming the one who is second.

## Shadow

Not safe, but a role that changes hands, and that is the turn of it: the Leader is only the Chaser between raises, the two positions swapping with every bid.
Because a single raise makes the Leader the runner-up who now forfeits, the lead is never held but only passed through, so the one in front is always about to be the one behind with every reason to bid, and the poise of almost-winning is the setup for the next sound raise, the two bidders trading the lead up the ladder toward ruin.
To lead is to be about to chase, so the enviable seat is the one the spiral hands back and forth.

## Tell

Says "I am ahead: the prize is as good as mine unless they are foolish enough to go higher."
Is right that they are ahead and wrong that ahead is safe, because one raise turns the lead into the forfeit, and the other bidder is not foolish to go higher but sound, so the seat changes hands and the climb goes on.
