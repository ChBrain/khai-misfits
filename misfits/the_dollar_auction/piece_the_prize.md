# Piece: The Prize

## Taxonomy

[The Dollar Auction](play_the_dollar_auction.md). The reward of known worth, the dollar no one misjudges and the escalation runs past.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Held up at [the threshold](place_the_threshold.md), chased across [the floor](place_the_floor.md), and handed to the winner at [the reckoning](place_the_reckoning.md).
Flows through the whole game as the fixed thing everyone is bidding for, its value plain from the first and unchanged to the last.

## Load Bearing

Without a prize worth a known, fixed amount there would be nothing to bid toward and no clean measure of how far the escalation has overshot.
With it the trap has its lure and its yardstick: the Prize is the bargain that draws the bidders in and the line the bidding climbs past, its worth agreed by everyone so that no overpayment can be blamed on misjudging it. The Prize is load bearing because it is the known value the escalation exceeds (Shubik): a dollar that is exactly a dollar to all, so the piece carries the fixed reward against which the overbid is measured, the thing chased so hard the chase costs more than the thing.

## Apparent

Just a bargain, free money on the block, a dollar to be had for a fraction of a dollar.
Seen at the surface, the Prize looks like an easy gain, the reason to step in and the reason to keep bidding, and the truth that its value is fixed and the bidding is not, that the thing worth one is being bought for many, that the lure never changes while the cost climbs without limit, stays hidden behind how plainly worth having a dollar is.

## Yearbook

Offered by [the Auctioneer](persona_the_auctioneer.md), held for a moment by [the Leader](persona_the_leader.md), and chased by [the Chaser](persona_the_chaser.md), the fixed reward the whole game turns on.
It is the lure of the trap: the Prize is what [baiting](process_baiting.md) dangles, what [the bid](piece_the_bid.md) reaches for, and the known worth [the overbid](piece_the_overbid.md) is measured past, the one thing everyone values right while [the all-pay](piece_the_all_pay.md) makes the chasing wrong.
