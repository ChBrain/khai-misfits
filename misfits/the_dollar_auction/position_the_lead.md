# Position: The Lead

## Taxonomy

[The Dollar Auction](play_the_dollar_auction.md). The seat of the highest bid, the prize in reach, and the runner-up's forfeit one raise away.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The top bid and the prize almost won.
A standing stake that takes the dollar if the other quits, the enviable position of being one raise from paid.
No hold on it, though, since a single bid from the other turns the lead into the forfeit and the seat is passed across the floor.

## Orders

Hold the prize.
Stand on the highest bid and wait to collect, letting the winning stake ride until the other either quits and hands over the prize or raises and takes it.
Rest on the lead, since being in front is being one silence away from the dollar.

## Loses

The lead itself, the instant the other raises, and with it the safety it seemed to carry.
Because one bid over the top turns the Leader into the runner-up who now forfeits, the Lead is never kept but only passed through, so the seat that is almost paid becomes the seat that is about to lose everything and must bid again.
It holds the winning stake and loses it to a single raise, the enviable seat handed straight into the chase.

## Drives

Toward a lead that cannot be held,
and a prize always one raise from lost,
so the seat that is almost paid is only the chase between bids, the two positions swapping up the ladder.
