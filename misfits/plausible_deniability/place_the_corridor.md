# Place: The Corridor

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The space between the office and the deed, where the wink travels and the trail is cut.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A quiet word in a hallway, a phrase over a drink, a hint passed hand to hand down a chain.
The broker carrying intent below and the agent receiving it, none of it written, all of it understood.

## Holds

The wink and the cut-out.
Here intent moves without an order moving with it, and here the layers of arm's length are laid, so the corridor holds both the message and the insulation that will keep it from ever climbing back.

## Offers

A way for a wish to reach a hand.
The corridor carries understanding from the top to the doer, so it offers the one channel the deed needs, intent delivered and no instruction created.

## Withheld

A record of the passage.
Nothing said in the corridor is written and no relay in it gave an order, so it withholds any proof that the wish ever travelled, and the point past the last cut-out is where the trail goes cold.
