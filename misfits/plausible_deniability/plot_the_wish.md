# Plot: The Wish

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The office](place_the_office.md), a principal wanting a result that cannot be asked for aloud.
A corner to be cut, a rule to be bent, a thing done that no memo could survive.

## Action

[Winking](process_winking.md) begins: [The Principal](persona_the_principal.md), holding [the principal's seat](position_principal.md), holds the wish and knows at once that it must never become an order.

## Stage

[The Principal](persona_the_principal.md) tables [Let It Fall](plan_let_it_fall.md): want the result, refuse to write it, and prepare to convey it another way, so [the wish](piece_the_wish.md) is kept off every page and readied to travel only as [the wink](piece_the_wink.md).
The wanting is clear, and the asking is already understood to be impossible.

## Tension

The clearest intention in the room is the one that must never be stated.
Nothing is wrong yet: a wish is not a deed, and wanting a thing is no crime. That is the trap laid, not sprung: an aim that cannot survive being written, held by someone with the power to have it done, who understands that the whole art from here is to get it done without ever having asked.
