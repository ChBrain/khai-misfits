# Piece: The Wink

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The displaced instruction: intent conveyed by hint, so it travels and no order does.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the corridor](place_the_corridor.md), a look, a phrase, a handle-it.
Where understanding passes and nothing is written.

## Load Bearing

Without it the wish could reach the hand only as an order, and the order would leave a trail.
With it intent moves as understanding rather than instruction: a look that means do it, a phrase that cannot be quoted as a command, so the doer knows exactly what is wanted and the record holds no order. The Wink is load bearing because it is the displacement of responsibility the psychology names (Bandura): the orderer conveys and does not command, the doer feels directed and was never told, so intent and accountability are split, the one travelling and the other left behind.

## Apparent

A casual remark, an offhand steer.
A wink looks like nothing more than a passing comment or a bit of guidance, deniable as banter, so its work of transmitting an unspeakable order hides inside the ordinariness of people understanding each other.

## Yearbook

Passed by [the principal](persona_the_principal.md) and carried by [the broker](persona_the_broker.md) through [winking](process_winking.md), grown from [the wish](piece_the_wish.md).
It is intent without instruction: the Wink travels through [the cut-out](piece_the_cut_out.md), so a want reaches a hand and no order reaches a page.
