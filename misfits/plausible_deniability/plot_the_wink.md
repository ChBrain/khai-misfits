# Plot: The Wink

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The corridor](place_the_corridor.md), where intent must travel without an order travelling with it.
A quiet word, a phrase, a handle-it, passed hand to hand down a chain.

## Action

[Winking](process_winking.md): [The Principal](persona_the_principal.md), holding [the principal's seat](position_principal.md), lets the wish fall as a look, while [The Broker](persona_the_broker.md), holding [the broker's seat](position_broker.md), carries it below as guidance and adds a length of arm.

## Stage

[The Principal](persona_the_principal.md) tables [Let It Fall](plan_let_it_fall.md) and [The Broker](persona_the_broker.md) tables [Pass It Down](plan_pass_it_down.md), so [the wink](piece_the_wink.md) moves the intent while [the cut-out](piece_the_cut_out.md) insulates the top.
Everyone who needs to understand does, and nothing is on the record.

## Tension

An order is given and no order exists.
The principal only made a remark, the broker only relayed a thought, the doer only understood, and each of those is innocent alone. That is the misfit's mechanism: intent delivered in full and instruction created nowhere, a chain in which every link can say, truthfully, that it gave no order, and the wish arrives at the hand with its fingerprints wiped before it was ever touched.
