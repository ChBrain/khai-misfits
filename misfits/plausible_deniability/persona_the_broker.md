# Persona: The Broker

## Taxonomy

[Broker](position_broker.md). The cut-out who carries the wish down and keeps the principal's hands off it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Trusted, fluent, comfortable in the space between.
Stands one layer down from the principal and one above the deed, and understands both.
Reads a look and turns it into a hint, and a hint into a thing that gets done.

## Action

Passes the wish along, and adds a length of arm.
Takes the wink from above and conveys it below as guidance, never as an order, so intent moves through it and no instruction sticks to it.
Keeps the chain clean upward, so nothing the agent knows can climb past it to the principal.

## Shadow

By insulating both ends it makes itself the seam where the trail goes cold.
It is only relaying, only advising, only smoothing, and that innocence is exactly the function: a cut-out exists to be the point past which nothing can be traced, so the broker's very deniability is the load it bears, and it too can say, truthfully, that it gave no order.
Not a conspirator on paper: the joint built to bend so the structure holds.

## Tell

"I just passed along what I understood the thinking to be."
The relay's true account, which carries everything and commits to nothing.
