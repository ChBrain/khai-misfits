# Persona: The Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Reformer](position_reformer.md). The one who makes arranged ignorance itself a fault, and pays for it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Doctrinal, determined, unwilling to accept the true denial.
Sees that the deniability works by law and can be answered only by law.
Aims not to find the missing order but to make its absence stop being a defence.

## Action

Names blindness as a kind of knowing, and command as a kind of ordering.
Builds the standard that a principal answers for what it arranged not to know, that willful ignorance counts as knowledge and a superior answers for what it should have known.
Reaches past the missing memo to the structure that removed it.

## Shadow

The doctrine that catches the deliberately blind cannot cleanly spare the honestly ignorant.
Its cure must punish a not-knowing, but not every not-knowing was arranged, so the same standard that pierces the shield can convict the principal who simply, truly, did not know, and the wink it must prove leaves no proof, so it either under-reaches and frees the guilty or over-reaches and snares the innocent.
Not a clean avenger: a maker of a necessary, dangerous doctrine that cannot tell the two ignorances apart.

## Tell

"Not knowing, when you arranged not to know, is no defence."
The rule that pierces the shield, and threatens the one who never held it.
