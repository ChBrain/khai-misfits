# Persona: The Investigator

## Taxonomy

[Investigator](position_investigator.md). The one who comes asking, and finds no thread to pull.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent, methodical, sure that a deed has an author.
Follows the trail from the act upward, looking for the order and the knowledge behind it.
Believes that somewhere there is a memo, a instruction, a fingerprint on the wish.

## Action

Traces the deed back, and hits the seam where the trail goes cold.
Finds the agent who did it and the wink it read, and then finds no order to produce and no principal who can be shown to have known.
Reaches the cut-out and stops, because past it there is genuinely nothing to find.

## Shadow

Its whole method assumes an order and a knowing, which is exactly what the structure was built not to have.
It is looking for a lie and there is none, for a document and there is none, so the more honest and thorough it is, the more cleanly the deniability defeats it, and it must either accept the true denial or reach for a standard the evidence cannot reach.
Not incompetent: a good investigator against a structure designed to leave nothing to investigate.

## Tell

"There has to be an order somewhere."
The reasonable certainty that a purpose-built deniability was made to disappoint.
