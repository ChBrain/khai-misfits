# Position: Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The seat that makes arranged ignorance a fault, and cannot do it cleanly.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The sight that the shield is legal and must be answered in law.
A doctrine to build: willful blindness as knowing, command as a broader kind of ordering.
A wink to prove that leaves no proof.

## Orders

Make the absence of an order stop being a defence.
Hold the principal for what it arranged not to know.
Treat a purpose-built ignorance as a kind of knowledge.

## Loses

Clean aim, because the same standard snares the innocent.
It must punish a not-knowing, and not every not-knowing was arranged, so its doctrine either under-reaches and frees the deliberately blind or over-reaches and convicts the honestly ignorant.
Right that the shield must be pierced, and unable to pierce it without cutting the wrong hand.

## Drives

Toward responsibility reattached to power,
against a wink that left no proof and an arrangement that looks like honest delegation,
so the cure is a necessary, dangerous doctrine that cannot tell the two ignorances apart.
