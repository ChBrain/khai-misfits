# Place: The Hearing

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). Where the deed surfaces and the denial is made.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A panel, a question, an oath: who ordered this, and who knew.
The agent in the chair holding the deed, and the principal answering that it gave no order and had no knowledge.

## Holds

The true denial, and the agent left alone.
Here the structure is tested and holds, because there is no order to produce and no knowledge to admit, so the hearing holds a disavowal that is honest and an accountability that cannot land.

## Offers

A fair proceeding and truthful answers.
Everyone may be asked and everyone answers truly, so the hearing offers exactly the process justice requires, and gets, from it, a principal cleared on the evidence.

## Withheld

The author of the deed.
The hearing can reach the hand and not the wish, because the wish was never spoken and the knowledge never held, so it withholds the one it most needs to find, and delivers the agent as the only one it can.
