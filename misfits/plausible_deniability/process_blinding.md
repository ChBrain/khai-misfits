# Process: Blinding

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The principal arranging not to know, so the future denial will be true.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A knowledge that would be a liability, and a will not to hold it.
A principal who understands that to answer for nothing it must know nothing.

## Direction

Toward a genuine ignorance, built on purpose.
The principal declines to be told, does not ask, and structures the reporting so the doing never reaches it, so the not-knowing is real and not a pose.

## Lever

Ignorance made true so the denial need not be a lie.
Because a denial only holds if the ignorance behind it is genuine, the process manufactures a real not-knowing, and its realness is precisely what makes the later disavowal honest and unbreakable.

## Echo

A principal that truly does not know.
The knowledge kept out by design, the denial primed to be true, and a responsibility that hides inside a genuine, engineered blindness.
