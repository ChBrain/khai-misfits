# Piece: The Blindfold

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The arranged ignorance: the not-knowing built on purpose, so the denial will be true.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the office](place_the_office.md), in the questions not asked and the reports not read.
Where the principal keeps the knowledge out.

## Load Bearing

Without it the principal might know, and a knowing principal can be held.
With it the ignorance is genuine and deliberate at once: the principal declines to be told, does not ask, structures the reporting so the doing never arrives, so it truly does not know, and its later denial is therefore not a lie. The Blindfold is load bearing because it is willful blindness made literal (Global-Tech; Jewell): a not-knowing arranged so as to avoid the liability knowing would bring, the exact device that turns a real ignorance into a real shield, and the thing the cure must learn to treat as knowledge.

## Apparent

Proper delegation, or a leader staying out of the weeds.
Not wanting the operational details looks like exactly what a senior figure should do, trusting subordinates and keeping the high view, so the blindfold wears the face of good management, and its work of manufacturing a defence hides inside a virtue.

## Yearbook

Drawn by [the principal](persona_the_principal.md) through [blinding](process_blinding.md), the ignorance the denial rests on.
It is not-knowing on purpose: the Blindfold makes [the denial](piece_the_denial.md) true, and is the very thing [plausible deniability](piece_plausible_deniability.md) can be pierced only by counting as knowledge.
