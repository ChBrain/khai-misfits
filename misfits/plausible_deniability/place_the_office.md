# Place: The Office

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The principal's room, where the wish is held and the blindfold drawn.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A clean desk, a spotless record, a door that closes.
A principal holding a wish it will not write and declining, with care, to be told what becomes of it.

## Holds

The wish and the arranged ignorance together.
Here the want that could never be an order is kept, and here the principal builds the not-knowing, so the office holds both the source of the deed and the emptiness that will answer for it.

## Offers

Clean hands and a clear record.
The office produces no order and retains no knowledge, so it offers the principal a genuine innocence on the evidence, every document blameless and every answer true.

## Withheld

Any fingerprint on the deed.
The office is arranged to hold nothing that could tie the wish to the doing, so it withholds the one thing an investigator needs, and its very cleanliness is the architecture of the deniability.
