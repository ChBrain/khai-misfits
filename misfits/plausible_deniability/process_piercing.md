# Process: Piercing

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). Making arranged ignorance count as knowledge, at the risk of catching the honestly ignorant.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A true denial that shields a real responsibility, and someone unwilling to accept it.
The recognition that the shield is legal, so it can be answered only in law.

## Direction

Toward a deed reattached to the wanting.
The absence of an order is made to stop being a defence: willful blindness is named as knowing, command as a wider ordering, so the principal answers for what it arranged not to know.

## Lever

A doctrine that treats a purpose-built ignorance as a kind of knowledge.
Because the shield is made of a true not-knowing, only a rule that counts arranged not-knowing as knowing can pierce it, and the same rule, reaching a not-knowing, reaches the honest one too.

## Echo

A shield pierced, or an innocent caught.
Responsibility landing at last on the one who willed the deed, or a wink that left no proof going unproven, or the doctrine convicting the principal who simply, truly, did not know.
