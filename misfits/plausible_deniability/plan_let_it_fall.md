# Plan: Let It Fall

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the principal's seat](position_principal.md), inside [Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Principal](position_principal.md)

## Direction

Want the result and never ask for it. Let the wish fall as a look or a phrase, put a cut-out between yourself and the deed, and then decline to be told how it is done. Keep your hands off it and your knowledge empty, so that when it surfaces every true word you say is a denial.

## Orders

([The Principal](persona_the_principal.md) conveys the wish without ordering it and arranges not to know, building the ignorance its denial will rest on.)

## Implementation

([The Office](place_the_office.md); the [winking](process_winking.md) process; [the wish](piece_the_wish.md) is let fall as [the wink](piece_the_wink.md), and the same seat draws [the blindfold](piece_the_blindfold.md) so the future denial will be true.)

## Targets

- [ ] the wish is held and never written as an order
- [ ] intent is let fall as a look or a phrase, not an instruction
- [ ] a cut-out is placed between the seat and the deed
- [ ] the seat declines to be told, so its later denial is genuine
