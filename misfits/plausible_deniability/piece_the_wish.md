# Piece: The Wish

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The unspeakable aim: the result wanted that no order could survive.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the office](place_the_office.md), held and never written.
Where the want that could not be an order is kept.

## Load Bearing

Without it there would be nothing to hide and no need for a shield.
With it there is a genuine intent that cannot be expressed as an instruction, because the instruction itself would be the crime, so the whole architecture exists to move the wish without recording it. The Wish is load bearing because it is the aim the deniability is built around (Church Committee): a result the principal truly wants and truly cannot ask for, so that everything downstream, the wink, the cut-out, the blindfold, is machinery for getting it done while leaving the wanting unprovable.

## Apparent

Nothing at all, which is the point.
An unspoken wish shows up nowhere, in no memo and no meeting, so it appears simply not to exist, and its absence from the record is read as its absence in fact, which is precisely the effect intended.

## Yearbook

Held by [the principal](persona_the_principal.md) through [winking](process_winking.md), the aim the play turns on.
It is the want that cannot be an order: the Wish is conveyed only as [the wink](piece_the_wink.md), so it can move without ever being written.
