# Piece: Plausible Deniability

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The phenomenon whole: responsibility severed from power by design, and its remedy in command responsibility at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the reform](place_the_reform.md), where the shield is pierced or holds.
And across the whole chain, in every step that carried the wish and kept the trail clean.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of moves with no shape.
With it the moves become one law: the wish, the wink, the cut-out, the blindfold, the deniable agent, and the denial are facets of a single architecture, in which a principal gets a deed done exactly as it wished while arranging that no order, no knowledge, and no trace can attach the wanting to the doing, so responsibility is severed from power by design. Plausible Deniability is load bearing because it is the whole finding (Church Committee) with its whole remedy attached: it holds the misfit together, and it holds that the only answer is to treat arranged ignorance as knowledge and command as a wider ordering, a doctrine dear to build, easy to resist, and unable to tell the deliberately blind from the honestly ignorant.

## Apparent

Either an innocent leader wronged by a rogue, or a witch-hunt against the merely uninformed.
Seen from one side the principal is a clean-handed figure betrayed by an overzealous subordinate; from the other, any not-knowing looks like guilt and every delegation like a plot; and both miss the structure, that the ignorance is real and arranged, so neither exoneration nor suspicion answers it, only a doctrine that can tell an engineered blindness from an honest one, which is the very thing no evidence cleanly supplies.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) through [piercing](process_piercing.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: Plausible Deniability is what [the wish](piece_the_wish.md) becomes when a principal builds the chain to carry it and the blindness to deny it, undone only by counting [the blindfold](piece_the_blindfold.md) as knowledge, at the risk of the wrong hand.
