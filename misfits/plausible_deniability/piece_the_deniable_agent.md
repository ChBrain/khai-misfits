# Piece: The Deniable Agent

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The disposable doer: the one who read the wink, did the deed, and can be disowned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the hearing](place_the_hearing.md), in the chair, holding the deed.
Where the one who understood is left alone.

## Load Bearing

Without it the deed could not be done at all, since the principal will not touch it.
With it there is a hand that acts on understanding rather than instruction, so the deed gets done and the doer, having been told nothing, can be cast off without a broken order to bind the principal to it. The Deniable Agent is load bearing because it is the disposable actor the apparatus requires (Arendt): the one who grasps the wish and executes it, useful precisely because it does not need an order, and disposable precisely because there is none, so the same understanding that made it valuable is what leaves it holding the deed when the shield comes down.

## Apparent

A rogue actor, or an overzealous subordinate.
When the deed surfaces the agent looks like someone who went too far on their own, so it reads as the lone author of its own excess, and its true role, executing an unspoken wish, hides inside the story of a subordinate exceeding a brief.

## Yearbook

Made of [the agent](persona_the_agent.md) through [denying](process_denying.md), the hand [the wink](piece_the_wink.md) recruited.
It is the link that cannot deny: the Deniable Agent holds the one knowledge the chain kept, and is cut loose so [the denial](piece_the_denial.md) can hold above it.
