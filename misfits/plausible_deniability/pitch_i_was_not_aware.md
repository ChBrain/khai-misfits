# Pitch: I Was Not Aware

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Correct, composed, hands demonstrably clean.
Nothing was ordered, nothing was known, the record is spotless, and the principal is as shocked as anyone.
The voice of proper procedure and honest surprise, spoken by someone who arranged, with great care, to be able to say every true word of it.

## Undertow

Under the correctness, a deed everyone wanted and no one asked for.
A wish went down as a wink, an agent who understood did it at arm's length, and the principal took pains not to be told, so the ignorance is genuine and the denial is not a lie.
No single step was a crime: a wish is not an order, not-asking is not lying, doing as you understood is not being told, and the sum of these innocent steps is a deed with no author and a hand left holding it.

## Nerve

The feeling of a clean pair of hands and a clear conscience,
every answer truthful, every document blameless,
the wanting alive in the room and nowhere in the record, and the one who read it correctly standing alone.

## Echo

After the run: a deed done and disowned,
a principal untouched behind an ignorance it built on purpose,
and an agent cut loose for having understood exactly what was meant and nothing that can be proved.
