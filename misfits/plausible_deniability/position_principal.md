# Position: Principal

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The seat that wants the deed and must not be caught wanting it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A wish that no order could survive.
The power to convey it without stating it, and the discipline not to state it.
The ability to structure its own ignorance, so its denial will be true.

## Orders

Let the wish fall, never write it.
Put a cut-out between yourself and the deed.
Decline to be told, so you can honestly say you were not.

## Loses

Nothing it can be shown to have held, and its innocence in fact.
It keeps its hands clean by touching nothing and its record clean by knowing nothing, so it authors the deed most completely by acting least, and its true denial hides a true responsibility.
Untouchable on the evidence, and the source of the whole thing.

## Drives

Toward the deed done and unowned,
which only its own silence and blindness secure,
so the more it wants, the less it says, orders, or knows.
