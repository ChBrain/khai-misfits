# Persona: The Agent

## Taxonomy

[Agent](position_agent.md). The one who reads the wink, does the deed, and is left holding it alone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable, loyal, good at understanding what is meant.
Hears no order but knows exactly what is wanted, and takes pride in not needing to be told.
Believes, as such people do, that having understood correctly will be remembered when it counts.

## Action

Does the deed the wink asked for.
Acts on an intent that was never an instruction, executes what the principal wished without a word that can be produced, and keeps the faith of the unspoken understanding.
Holds, at the end, the only knowledge in the chain that is not deniable: its own.

## Shadow

The very quality that made it useful, understanding without being told, is what leaves it holding the deed alone.
It did exactly what was wanted and nothing that was ordered, so there is no order to share the blame and no principal who can be shown to have known, and when the deed surfaces the structure that used its understanding disowns it, cutting loose the one link that actually knew.
Not a dupe by stupidity: a competent hand undone by being the only one who cannot deny.

## Tell

"I did what I knew they wanted. They'll stand by me."
The loyal, mistaken faith of the one the deniability is built to abandon.
