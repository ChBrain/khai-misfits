# Plot: The Piercing

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The reform](place_the_reform.md), where someone moves to make arranged ignorance itself a fault.
The missing order will never be found, so the search turns from the record to the structure that emptied it.

## Action

[Piercing](process_piercing.md): [The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md), holding [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), names willful blindness as knowing and command as ordering, while [The Investigator](persona_the_investigator.md), holding [the investigator's seat](position_investigator.md), presses the case the evidence alone could not carry.

## Stage

[The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) tables [Pierce the Shield](plan_pierce_the_shield.md): treat [the blindfold](piece_the_blindfold.md) as a kind of knowledge, so [plausible deniability](piece_plausible_deniability.md) can be answered without the memo it was built to omit.
And it must reckon that the same standard, reaching a not-knowing, reaches the honest one too.

## Tension

The only doctrine that catches the deliberately blind cannot cleanly spare the honestly ignorant.
To pierce the shield the reformer must make a not-knowing count as knowing, but not every not-knowing was arranged, and the wink that proved intent left no proof, so the rule either under-reaches and frees the guilty or over-reaches and convicts the innocent. Responsibility is reattached to power at last ... or the proof is not there, or the principal who simply, truly did not know is caught in the doctrine meant for the one who blinded itself.
