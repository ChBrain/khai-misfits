# Piece: The Denial

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The true disavowal: the principal answering, honestly, that it gave no order and had no knowledge.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the hearing](place_the_hearing.md), under oath and unshaken.
Where the disavowal is made and holds.

## Load Bearing

Without it the whole arrangement would be a lie waiting to be caught.
With it the disavowal is honest: there genuinely was no order and genuinely no knowledge, so the denial is true, survives any oath, and cannot be broken by an honest witness or an honest document, because none exists to break it. The Denial is load bearing because it is the payoff the structure was built for (Church Committee): not a falsehood to be exposed but an accurate account of a purpose-built emptiness, the reason the shield defeats even a diligent search, since it is asking for a truth that was engineered to acquit.

## Apparent

Vindication.
A principal who answers every question truthfully and is contradicted by no evidence looks simply innocent, so the denial reads as exoneration, and its dependence on a wish never spoken and a knowledge never held stays out of sight behind the plain truth of every word.
