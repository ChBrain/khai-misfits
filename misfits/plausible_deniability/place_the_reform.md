# Place: The Reform

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). Where arranged ignorance is made a fault, or the shield holds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A doctrine drafted: willful blindness named as knowing, command as a wider kind of ordering.
The reformer building a standard that reaches past the missing memo, and the arrangement's defenders calling it honest delegation.

## Holds

The cure, and its danger together.
Here the shield can be pierced by making a purpose-built ignorance count as knowledge, and here the same standard threatens the honestly ignorant, so the reform holds both the remedy and the wrong hand it might catch.

## Offers

A way to reattach the deed to the wanting.
The reform can make not-knowing stop being a defence, so it offers the one route to accountability a spotless record leaves open, responsibility assigned for what a principal arranged not to know.

## Withheld

A clean line between the two ignorances.
The reform cannot supply proof of a wink that left none, nor a test that spares the innocent while catching the blind, so it withholds the certainty its doctrine needs, and pierces the shield only at the risk of the wrong conviction.
