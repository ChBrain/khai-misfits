# Process: Denying

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The deed surfacing and the principal answering, truthfully, that it neither ordered nor knew.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A deed come to light, and a structure built to answer for it.
Someone asking who ordered this and who knew, and a chain designed so no true answer implicates the top.

## Direction

Toward accountability that fails to attach.
The principal answers that it gave no order and had no knowledge, which is true, the investigator finds no thread past the cut-out, and the agent, who alone knew, is left holding the deed.

## Lever

A denial made true by the winking and the blinding before it.
Because the order was never written and the knowledge never held, the disavowal is honest, so the process defeats the search not with a lie but with an accurate account of a purpose-built emptiness.

## Echo

A deed disowned and a hand cut loose.
The principal cleared on the evidence, the agent abandoned, and a responsibility that dissolves at the seam where the trail was cut.
