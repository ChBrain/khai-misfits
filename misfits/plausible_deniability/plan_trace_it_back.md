# Plan: Trace It Back

## Taxonomy

## Direction

Follow the deed upward from the hand that did it, looking for the order and the knowledge behind it. Find the memo, the instruction, the fingerprint on the wish, and attach the act to whoever willed it. Assume, as any investigator must, that a deed has an author.
