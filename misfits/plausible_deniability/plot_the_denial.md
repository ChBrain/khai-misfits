# Plot: The Denial

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The hearing](place_the_hearing.md), the deed surfaced and someone come asking who ordered it and who knew.
A panel, a question, an oath, and a chain built to answer without implicating the top.

## Action

[Denying](process_denying.md): [The Agent](persona_the_agent.md), holding [the agent's seat](position_agent.md), is found holding the deed alone, while [The Principal](persona_the_principal.md), holding [the principal's seat](position_principal.md), answers that it gave no order and had no knowledge, and [The Investigator](persona_the_investigator.md), holding [the investigator's seat](position_investigator.md), finds no thread to pull.

## Stage

[The Agent](persona_the_agent.md) tables [Read the Wink](plan_read_the_wink.md) and learns it holds the only knowledge that is not deniable, becoming [the deniable agent](piece_the_deniable_agent.md) cut loose.
[The Investigator](persona_the_investigator.md) tables [Trace It Back](plan_trace_it_back.md) and runs into [the denial](piece_the_denial.md), true and unbreakable, the trail cut at the cut-out.

## Tension

Everyone tells the truth, and the truth acquits the one who wanted it.
The principal really gave no order and really did not know, the investigator really can find nothing, and the agent really did it, so no one lies and the deed comes to rest on the only hand that touched it. That is the shield doing its work: an honest proceeding that reaches the doer and not the wish, and abandons the one link that understood.
