# Position: Agent

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The seat that does the deed on an understanding, and holds it alone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The skill to act on a wish that was never an order.
An understanding of what is wanted, clear enough to do and quiet enough to deny for everyone but itself.
A faith that having understood correctly will be repaid.

## Orders

Read the wink and act on it.
Do what is wanted without needing to be told.
Keep the unspoken understanding, and expect it kept in return.

## Loses

Everything, when the deed surfaces, and alone.
It did exactly what was wanted and nothing that was ordered, so no order shares its blame and no principal is shown to have known, and the structure that used its understanding disowns the one link that actually knew.
The only member of the chain that cannot deny, and so the only one left holding.

## Drives

Toward the deed done as understood,
which makes it useful and makes it disposable,
so the hand that grasped the wish is the hand the shield is built to abandon.
