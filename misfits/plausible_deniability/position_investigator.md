# Position: Investigator

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The seat that traces the deed upward and finds the trail cut.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A deed with a doer and a method built to leave no author.
The tools to follow an order and a knowing, and a structure that holds neither.
The duty to attach the act to whoever willed it.

## Orders

Trace the deed from the hand upward.
Find the order, find the knowledge, find the fingerprint on the wish.
Follow the chain to its source.

## Loses

The thread, at the cut-out, where there is genuinely nothing.
Its method assumes an order and a knowing, which is exactly what the structure was designed not to have, so its diligence runs cleanly into a true denial and stops.
The better it works, the more perfectly the deniability defeats it.

## Drives

Toward an author it is sure exists,
against a structure built to have none on the record,
so an honest search for a missing order is made to come up empty by design.
