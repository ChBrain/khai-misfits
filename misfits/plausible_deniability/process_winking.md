# Process: Winking

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). Intent conveyed without an order, so a wish travels and no instruction does.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A wish that cannot be spoken, and a chain that can be trusted to understand.
A principal who wants a thing and knows an order would damn it.

## Direction

Toward a doer who understands and a record that holds nothing.
The want is let fall as a look or a phrase, carried down as guidance through a cut-out, and received by an agent who grasps it, all of it understood and none of it written.

## Lever

Understanding that needs no instruction.
Because a capable chain can read intent from a hint, the wish reaches the hand without ever becoming an order, so the process conveys everything and commits nothing to the record.

## Echo

A deed set in motion and unauthored.
The agent knowing exactly what to do, the principal having ordered nothing, and a wish alive in every head and absent from every page.
