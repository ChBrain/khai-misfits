# Position: Broker

## Taxonomy

[Plausible Deniability](play_plausible_deniability.md). The seat between principal and deed, that carries the one and hides the other.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A place one layer below the wish and one above the hand.
The fluency to turn a look into guidance and guidance into a done thing.
The standing to relay without ordering, and to be believed when it says so.

## Orders

Take the wink from above and pass it below.
Convey intent as advice, never as instruction.
Keep the chain clean upward, so nothing climbs to the principal.

## Loses

Nothing, and becomes the seam where the trail dies.
It only relays, only smooths, only advises, and that innocence is its purpose, for a cut-out exists precisely to be the point past which the deed cannot be traced.
Deniable itself, and the reason the principal is too.

## Drives

Toward intent moved and no order stuck to it,
which its own only-relaying secures,
so the joint built to bend is what keeps the structure standing.
