# Persona: The Principal

## Taxonomy

[Principal](position_principal.md). The one who wants the deed, will not order it, and arranges not to know it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Senior, careful, correct in every visible thing.
Wants a result that could never survive being written down, and knows it.
Prizes clean hands above a clean conscience, and keeps them.

## Action

Lets the wish fall as a look, and then looks away.
Conveys what it wants without an order, has a cut-out carry it, and declines to be told how it is done.
Builds an ignorance on purpose, so that when the deed surfaces its denial will be true.

## Shadow

It authors the deed most completely by touching it least.
Every step it takes is to not-act: not to order, not to ask, not to know, and the sum of those omissions is a thing done exactly as it wished with nothing traceable to the wishing, so its innocence is not the absence of the deed but the architecture of it.
Not a liar: the designer of a truth that hides everything.

## Tell

"I don't want to know the details. Just handle it."
The clean-handed instruction that orders everything and records nothing.
