# Plot: The Reckoning

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Field](place_the_field.md), a hundred appeals answered elsewhere and none of them here, the sum already accruing.
No single moment to point to, only appeal after appeal closing the same way, gift by gift, ruling by ruling.

## Action

[Neglecting](process_neglecting.md): [The Countless](persona_the_countless.md), holding [the countless' seat](position_countless.md), goes underfunded not once but appeal by appeal, ruling by ruling, across the whole field at once.

## Stage

[The Countless](persona_the_countless.md), on [Wait Unseen](plan_wait_unseen.md), stands unanswered in every appeal at once, the shortfall compounding where no single giver can see it.
[The Giver](persona_the_giver.md), on [Answer the Named](plan_answer_the_named.md), across a thousand separate appeals, each time answers the one face shown to them, each choice sound on its own, and the sum of the sound choices is the field left short.

## Tension

Every single gift was rightly given, and the sum of the rightly given gifts is a population starved.
No one chose the shortfall: no giver weighed the thousand against the one and picked the one, each simply answered the face in front of them. The plot closes with a society's faces funded and its numbers starved, the cost real and total and visible only from far above, in an aggregate no single giver ever sees or authored.
