# Plot: The Presenting

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md)

## Owner

## Cue

[The Appeal](place_the_appeal.md), lit and framed for one face, the giver's sympathy idle with nothing yet to hold.
No case in view and no gift yet asked, only the frame waiting for whoever steps into it.

## Action

[Facing](process_facing.md): [The Named](persona_the_named.md), holding [the named's seat](position_named.md), comes before the Giver with a name, a photograph, a story, and the sympathy wakes to meet it.

## Stage

[The Named](persona_the_named.md), on [Draw the Eye](plan_draw_the_eye.md), presents a face and a story the compassion can hold, a need seeable and answerable.
[The Giver](persona_the_giver.md), on [Answer the Named](plan_answer_the_named.md), feels for the person now in view, the compassion proportionate, real, exactly what a decent heart owes a real need.

## Tension

The compassion here is genuine and earns nothing to apologize for: a real person, truly needing help, and a heart that answers.
No one in the room is weighing this child against a thousand elsewhere, because there is no thousand in the room, only a face, and the feeling answers exactly what is placed before it. The plot closes with the Giver moved, the sympathy live, a real need standing right in front of it, ready to be met.
