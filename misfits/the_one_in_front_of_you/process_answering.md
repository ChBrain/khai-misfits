# Process: Answering

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The sympathy spent: the live feeling for the Named converted into a gift, the appeal met.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The sympathy, already live, with a named person waiting on the other end of it.
At [the Appeal](place_the_appeal.md), the Giver stands moved and the Named stands in need, the feeling and the case already matched, only the giving left to happen.

## Direction

Toward resources flowing to the identified victim, and flowing quickly.
Because the feeling is live and the need is specific, answering carries the Giver straight from moved to giving with nothing in between to slow it, so the wallet opens, the checkbook is out, the sum needed appears and often appears almost overnight.

## Lever

The feeling meeting the resource the giver is able to direct.
Because a live sympathy wants an outlet and the Named is right there to be its outlet, answering runs on the shortest path between the two, no deliberation required and none particularly wanted, the giving following the feeling as directly as the feeling followed the face.

## Echo

The identified one funded, the gift given, the feeling answered.
The Named's need is met and the Giver's sympathy is spent well, on a real person, for a real need, so what closes here is entirely sound on its own terms: a good heart giving rightly to the one thing in front of it, the giver right to give, and right to feel right for having given.
