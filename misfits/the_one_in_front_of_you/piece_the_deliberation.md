# Piece: The Deliberation

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The perverse cure, analytic thought dampening the sympathy for the Face without raising any for the Number, so caring falls overall.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Weighed at [The Table](place_the_table.md), where the discrepancy between the identified and the statistical is put in front of the giver and reasoned over.

## Load Bearing

Without it, the obvious fix would simply work: tell the giver the number, ask them to reason past the face, and caring would even out between the two. With it, the fix backfires: analytic thought dampens the sympathy for the identified without kindling any for the statistical, so the giver gives less to the Named and no more to the Countless, and caring falls overall. The Deliberation is load bearing because it is the escape the house refuses to hand out cheaply (Small, Loewenstein, and Slovic): priming the discrepancy between an identified and a statistical life lowers donations to the identified without lifting them to the statistical, so the piece carries the reckoning that the feeling which misallocates is the same feeling that motivates any giving at all.

## Apparent

The reasonable answer, the mature correction, what any thoughtful giver should do on being shown the discrepancy: think it through and give accordingly.
Seen at the surface, the Deliberation looks like the cure: weigh the face against the number and let the larger, truer need win the larger gift. And the truth that thinking it through does not redirect the feeling but simply cools it, that the countless gain nothing from the identified's loss, that the correction spends the very warmth it meant to reallocate, stays hidden behind how sensible reasoning past a bias sounds in the abstract.
