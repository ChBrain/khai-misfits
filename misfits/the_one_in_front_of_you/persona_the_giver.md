# Persona: The Giver

## Taxonomy

[Giver](position_giver.md). The donor, court, or health system whose compassion answers the identified victim and directs resources there, the warm heart the trap is spent through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Moved. Generous. Answering a real need.
Is the one who sees the named victim, feels for them, and gives, meeting a genuine need with a real gift, the appeal answered and the person helped.
No coldness in it: only a decent heart doing what a decent heart should, giving well to the one in front of it.

## Action

Answers the Named, on [Answer the Named](plan_answer_the_named.md), feeling the compassion the identified victim draws and directing the gift to them.
Meets the face in the appeal with a real resource, and goes quiet, unmoved, before a statistical count.
Does what a compassionate giver does: answers the person it can see and feel, and cannot answer the number it cannot.

## Shadow

Not a giver who does not care, one who cares exactly and only where there is a face, and that is the whole difficulty: the compassion is real, and its realness depends on a person it can picture.
Because feeling fastens on the identified and falls silent before the statistical, the Giver funds the one in view and leaves the unseen many short, so the warmer and more responsive the heart the more surely it pours its aid on the few who can be seen, its very humanity the thing that misallocates, and the thousand with no face never once felt.
It answers every face it meets, and answering every face is what leaves the numbers in the dark.

## Tell

Says "how could I not help, when here is a real child who needs it," and never asks after the thousand.
Means it, truly, and never notices that the thousand were just as real and needed it as much, and drew nothing because they had no face to show.
