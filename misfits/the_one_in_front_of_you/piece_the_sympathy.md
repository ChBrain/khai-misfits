# Piece: The Sympathy

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The affect the Face evokes, real and proportionate, the motive force of every gift and the thing the Number cannot summon.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Felt at [The Appeal](place_the_appeal.md), where the Face is laid before the giver and the compassion first moves.

## Load Bearing

Without it, the Face would be seen and not felt, a name and a photograph passed over like any other fact, and no gift would ever move. With it, the identified victim's need becomes a pull: real, proportionate compassion that reaches for the wallet before the mind has weighed anything at all. The Sympathy is load bearing because it is the motive force of every gift (Small, Loewenstein, and Slovic): donations concentrate on the single identified victim even where dispersing them would help more, and the piece carries that pull, the feeling that moves resources and the only reason any of them move.

## Apparent

The whole of generosity, the plain goodness of a person moved to give.
Seen at the surface, the Sympathy looks complete: a decent heart answering a real need, nothing more to explain. And the truth that this same decent heart is what tilts the whole field toward the visible, that its goodness and its narrowness are the same motion, that a heart this warm to the one in front of it is, by the same warmth, quiet before the thousand behind it, stays hidden behind how right it feels every time it moves.
