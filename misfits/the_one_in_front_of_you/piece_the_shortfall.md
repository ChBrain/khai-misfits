# Piece: The Shortfall

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The sum over a field, the statistical many left underfunded gift by gift and ruling by ruling, the cost that lives only in the aggregate.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Accrues across [The Field](place_the_field.md), the unseen population left short appeal after appeal.
Weighed only from [The Table](place_the_table.md), where a society's giving is seen for what it was spent on.

## Load Bearing

Without it, the countless would simply be unfunded on any given day, a fact and not a wrong, each unanswered appeal its own small nothing. With it, the unfunded days sum: gift by gift and ruling by ruling the identified are funded and the statistical are not, and across a society resources flow to the few in view and away from the larger unseen population, a cost that lives only in the aggregate. The Shortfall is load bearing because it is the reckoning the trap hides (Schelling): the sharp gap between what is spent to save an identified life and an equal statistical one, summed across every giver who never chose it and never saw it, so the piece carries the total no single gift can show.

## Apparent

Nothing, on any single appeal: a fair, ordinary outcome, a fund that reached its goal for one need and simply did not reach another.
Seen at the surface, the Shortfall does not appear at all: each appeal looks complete in itself, funded or not on its own account, no wrongdoing visible in any one of them. And the truth that added together they are a society's resources routed by visibility rather than worth, that the same equal need is met a hundred times when it wears a face and missed a hundred times when it does not, stays hidden behind how sound each single decision looks in isolation.

## Yearbook

Accrued across [The Field](place_the_field.md), summed from every [Neglecting](process_neglecting.md) of [The Countless](persona_the_countless.md).
It is the cost of the trap: the Shortfall is the reckoning of a hundred sound gifts to [The Named](persona_the_named.md) through [The Face](piece_the_face.md), the need [The Numbing](piece_the_numbing.md) drained of feeling, closed only where [The Actuary](persona_the_actuary.md) weighs [The Number](piece_the_number.md) instead of waiting for a face that will not come.
