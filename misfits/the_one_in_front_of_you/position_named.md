# Position: Named

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The seat of the identified victim, whose face and name and story let the giver's compassion fasten and the gift arrive.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A face and a story, and an equal need dressed in them: the standing to be seen, named, and pictured while asking for help.
A clear case: the need is genuine and no smaller than the unseen many's, only visible where theirs is not.
No greater worth than the statistical many, only greater visibility, and the visibility is what the seat brings.

## Orders

Draw the eye.
Come before the giver with a name, a face, a story the compassion can hold, and present a need that can be seen and answered.
Be the person the giver can picture, and be helped for it.

## Loses

Nothing of its own, and that is the point: the seat need only be visible to draw the aid the faceless many cannot.
Because the giver's feeling needs a face and this seat has one, the identified victim is funded while the equally needy statistical go without, so the seat loses no ground and yet its funding is a fortune of visibility, every resource it draws a resource the faceless will not get, its face and not its need the reason it is served.
It gives up nothing and still takes the aid, its visibility the whole of its advantage over the unseen.

## Drives

Toward the identified funded on the strength of a face,
the seen victim served and the unseen passed over,
so the seat that can be pictured draws the aid away from the many who cannot.
