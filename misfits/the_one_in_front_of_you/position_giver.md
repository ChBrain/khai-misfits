# Position: Giver

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The seat of the one whose compassion allocates, funding the identified victim it can feel and passing over the statistical many it cannot.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A resource to direct and a feeling that directs it: the standing to give, rule, or fund, and a compassion that answers what it can see.
A clear case: the identified need is real and the gift meets it, so answering the face is a genuine good.
No feeling to spare for a count, only for a face, so the seat can move its resource toward the seen and never toward the unseen.

## Orders

Answer the Named.
Feel for the identified victim in view, and direct the gift to the person you can see and whose need you can hold.
Give well to the one in front of you, and pass, unmoved, over the number.

## Loses

Any pull toward the statistical many, and with it the aid they needed.
Because the seat's compassion fastens on a face and falls silent before a count, it gives up all motive to fund the unseen, so it loses the many to the very feeling that funds the one, its warmth spent entirely on the visible and none of it reaching the faceless.
It surrenders the number to the face, keeping only the aid the feeling can find.

## Drives

Toward the identified funded and the statistical passed over,
the warm heart pouring its aid on the one it can see,
so the seat whose compassion is realest gives least to the many who have no face.
