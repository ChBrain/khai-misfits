# Position: Countless

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The seat of the statistical many, present only as a count, whose equal and larger need draws no feeling because it has no face.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A number and a need with no face on it: the standing of an anonymous, future, numerous population that will live or die by whether it is funded.
A clear case: the deaths are real and the need is genuine and larger than the one's, only nameless and unpicturable.
No face to summon feeling, only a count, and the count grows more abstract the larger it is.

## Orders

Wait unseen.
Stand before the giver as a figure rather than a person, an estimate of preventable deaths, and summon no feeling because there is no face to hold.
Grow only more numerous and more abstract, and go unanswered.

## Loses

The very feeling that would fund it, and so the aid it most needs.
Because the seat presents a count and feeling fastens on a face, it draws less compassion the more numerous it is, so it gives up the sympathy that funds the identified and is left underfunded in proportion to its scale, its size, which should command the most aid, commanding the least, and its members reached only where a colder instrument counts them.
It surrenders the feeling to its own numbers, the larger it is the less it is felt and the less it is served.

## Drives

Toward the greatest need drawing the least aid,
the faceless many unfunded because they cannot be pictured,
so the seat that stands for the most lives is the seat the feeling passes over.
