# Place: The Field

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). Where the unseen many actually are, the anonymous, future population a programme would save, present everywhere and pictured nowhere.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Nothing to picture: no face, no name, no photograph, only a scale of need spread thin across a population no one can see whole.
A rate, a projection, four thousand preventable deaths a year, true and exact and entirely unable to summon the feeling a single face summons at a glance.

## Holds

The statistical many, and the shortfall that grows around them unattended.
Here the Countless waits as it actually is, in full, before any Table has counted it and before any Appeal has failed to show it, so the Field holds the real people the other places only represent or omit, each of them as real as the Named and none of them in view.

## Offers

Nothing to the eye and nothing to the heart, an equal need with no face to carry it.
Because there is no one here to picture, the Field cannot make its own case the way the Appeal makes the Named's, so whatever it offers a giver, and its need is as real and often larger, arrives with nothing attached that feeling can take hold of, and a need unfelt is a need answered by no one.

## Withheld

The feeling and the funding both, since there is no face here for sympathy to fasten on.
The Field is where the neglect actually lands, not chosen by anyone standing in it or looking at it, so it withholds even the comfort of a villain: no one decided against the Countless here, the giving simply never arrived, because arriving requires a face and the Field, by what it is, has none to give.
