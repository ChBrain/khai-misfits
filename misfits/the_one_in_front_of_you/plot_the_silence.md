# Plot: The Silence

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Field](place_the_field.md), a population no one can picture, present everywhere and pictured nowhere.
No name attached to any of it, no single face to stand out, only a rate spread thin across a scale too large to hold.

## Action

[Neglecting](process_neglecting.md): [The Countless](persona_the_countless.md), holding [the countless' seat](position_countless.md), stands before the giver as a count and goes unanswered, the silence complete before any appeal is even raised.

## Stage

[The Countless](persona_the_countless.md), on [Wait Unseen](plan_wait_unseen.md), presents an equal, larger need with no face to carry it, growing only more abstract as it grows more numerous, and summons nothing.

## Tension

The Countless is not less real for having no face, and its going unfunded is not anyone refusing it.
No giver stands here weighing the thousand against the one and choosing the one, because no giver stands here at all: the sympathy that answers a face has nothing in this field to answer, so the more numerous the need the less of it is felt. The plot closes with the statistical many waiting, unfunded, not by refusal but by a silence with no one at fault inside it.
