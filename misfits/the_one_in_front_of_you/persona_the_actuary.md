# Persona: The Actuary

## Taxonomy

[Actuary](position_actuary.md). The one who would fund by the numbers, weighing the statistical lives the feeling cannot and moving the choice off the warm giver, against the perverse cost of asking a heart to reason past a face.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Cool. Even-handed. Counting the lives.
Is the one who tallies the statistical many, sets a value on the life the feeling ignores, and would allocate by the count so the unseen thousand are funded too.
No cruelty in it: only a mind that will not let a face outvote a number, trying to give the unfeelable many their due.

## Action

Counts the lives, on [Count the Lives](plan_count_the_lives.md), pooling the giving and allocating by the statistic so the faceless population is funded.
Moves the choice off the feeling giver and onto a colder instrument that can weigh what sympathy cannot.
Does what an actuary does: values the statistical life the heart discounts, and funds the many the face crowds out.

## Shadow

Not a foe of feeling who would have no one give, one who needs the feeling and cannot use it, and that is the whole difficulty: the sympathy the Actuary would correct is the same sympathy that makes anyone give at all.
Because asking the giver to reason past the face dampens the compassion for the identified without kindling any for the statistical, the Actuary's appeal to the number cools the heart rather than redirecting it, so the correction cannot be made in the giver at all, only in the instrument, and the moment the Actuary speaks to the warm heart of a count, it gives less to everyone.
It would have the many funded, and speaking of the many to a feeling giver is what leaves them all colder.

## Tell

Says "look at the numbers, not the one face: the thousand matter just as much."
Is right, and never sees that saying so does not warm the thousand, it chills the one, so the ledger it would balance is balanced only by moving the choice out of the giver's hands.
