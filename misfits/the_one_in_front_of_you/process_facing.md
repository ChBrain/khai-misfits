# Process: Facing

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The identified victim brought into view, a name and a face and a story presented where a giver can meet them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A person the giver can picture: a name, a face, a story that fits inside a single, held-in-mind whole.
At [the Appeal](place_the_appeal.md), the Named comes before the Giver framed and lit, one case at a time, nothing behind them and nothing beside them to divide the giver's attention.

## Direction

Toward the giver's compassion engaged and fastened on the one in view.
Because a person who can be pictured is a person feeling can reach, facing moves the giver's sympathy from idle to live the moment the Named appears, so the attention that a moment ago had nowhere to land now lands whole on a single, particular need.

## Lever

The concrete, picturable person meeting the giver's plain capacity to feel.
Because feeling requires something to hold and a name and a face are exactly that, facing runs on the fit between the two: give it a person and it fires, ask it to fire on a number and it has nothing to close its grip around, so the lever works only where the Appeal has already done the work of making someone picturable.

## Echo

The giver moved, the sympathy live, a real need standing right in front of it.
The Named is seen and the Giver feels for them, genuinely and rightly, the response proportionate to a real person's real trouble, so what closes here is not yet a gift, only the condition a gift will need: a heart now open, and, because it opened on a face, open on exactly one.
