# Process: Counting

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The Actuary's answer: allocation moved off the feeling giver and onto the number, or, pressed on the heart instead, the giving cooled.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An Actuary who has seen the shortfall and would close it by the count.
At [the Table](place_the_table.md), the Countless is finally entered whole, a column instead of a face, and an instrument stands ready to weigh it evenly against the Named.

## Direction

Toward allocation by the statistic, the choice carried off the warm giver entirely.
Because a colder instrument can hold a number the way a giver can only hold a face, counting moves the deciding away from the sympathy that cannot reach the Countless and onto a rule that was built to, so the many stop waiting on a feeling that was never going to arrive for them.

## Lever

The even weighing of lives meeting the perverse fact that pressing the count on a warm heart only cools it.
Because deliberating on the discrepancy dampens the sympathy for the Named without kindling any for the Countless, counting works only where it bypasses the giver's feeling rather than arguing with it, so the same move that funds the many at the Table would, tried at the Appeal, only leave both funded less.

## Echo

The many funded by a colder instrument, where the choice was moved and not merely argued for.
The allocation is pooled, the statistical lives weighed, and the Countless is funded by a rule instead of a face, so what closes here is the one route out that works: never the giver made to feel a number, always the number given somewhere else to decide, or, tried the other way, on the heart itself, giving falls overall and the Countless is worse off than before the counting began.
