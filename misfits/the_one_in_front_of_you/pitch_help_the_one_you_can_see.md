# Pitch: Help the One You Can See

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Warm, moved, decent.
The plain good feeling of answering a real person in need: the named child, the face in the appeal, the one whose story you can hold and whose need you can meet.
Nothing false in it: the compassion is genuine and proportionate, and giving to the one in front of you is exactly what a decent heart should do.

## Undertow

Under the warmth, the statistical many who have no face and so summon no feeling, underfunded gift by gift though they are more and their need is equal.
Because sympathy fastens on the one it can see and goes silent before a count, the money flows to the identified and away from the anonymous, a shortfall that no giver weighs or chooses.
The heart that answers the face is the heart that cannot feel the number.

## Nerve

The clean warmth of giving to a real person in need, sure you have done a good thing, and you have,
because the child is real and the compassion is right and the gift meets a genuine need,
while the thousand who would be saved by the unglamorous programme go unfunded, drawing no tears and no checks, because they have no face to summon them.

## Echo

After the run: a society that funds its faces and starves its numbers, no one cold and no one choosing against the many,
only a warm and proper compassion answering the one in view, gift by gift, and a statistical many left in the dark,
and the cure that would fix it cooling the very heart that gave, so the humane response is the one that misallocates.
