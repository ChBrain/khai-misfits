# Persona: The Named

## Taxonomy

[Named](position_named.md). The identified victim, a specific person with a name and a face and a story, whose visible need draws the giver's compassion and the gift.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Specific. Present. A face and a name.
Is the real person in real need, the child in the photograph, the one whose story can be told and whose need can be seen and met.
No exaggeration in it: only a genuine victim who genuinely needs help, and who can be pictured while they ask.

## Action

Draws the eye, on [Draw the Eye](plan_draw_the_eye.md), coming before the giver with a face and a story that the compassion can fasten on.
Presents a need that can be seen and answered, and receives the gift the feeling directs.
Does what an identified victim does: is a person the giver can picture, and by being picturable, is helped.

## Shadow

Not a victim more deserving than the others, one more visible, and that is the whole difficulty: the need is real and no greater than the unseen thousand's, and only its visibility sets it apart.
Because the giver's feeling needs a face and the Named has one, the Named is funded while the equally needy statistical many are not, so the gift the Named receives is not a verdict that they matter more but that they can be seen more, the fortune of a face, and every resource that flows to them is a resource the faceless many will not get.
It asks only for the help it truly needs, and being the one that can be pictured is what draws the help away from the rest.

## Tell

Says "thank you, you have saved my life," and the gratitude is real and earned.
Was truly saved, and never knows that the saving came less for the depth of the need than for the face that carried it, the thousand behind them just as worthy and unseen.
