# Process: Neglecting

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The statistical many left unfunded, not by refusal but by the plain silence of feeling before a count.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The statistical many, present only as a count, with no name and no face attached to any of it.
At [the Field](place_the_field.md), the Countless stands in need exactly as real as the Named's, and stands there as a number, which is the only way it can stand.

## Direction

Toward the shortfall widening, the many going unfunded appeal after appeal.
Because a count draws no sympathy the way a face does, neglecting carries the giver straight past the Countless without an act of refusal ever taking place, so the funding that never comes never comes because it was never, for a moment, called for.

## Lever

The abstraction meeting the silence of a feeling with nothing to fasten on.
Because the sympathy flattens as the number climbs, the very scale that should weigh more weighs less, so neglecting runs on the numbing itself: the larger the Countless grows, the quieter the feeling it draws, until a thousand deaths move less than one.

## Echo

The unseen many underfunded, no feeling summoned and no one having chosen against them.
The Countless remains as it was, real and short of funds, and no Giver anywhere decided this, each of them simply having felt nothing to decide against, so what closes here is a neglect authored by no one, visible only from far above, in a sum no single giver ever sees.
