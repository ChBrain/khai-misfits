# Persona: The Countless

## Taxonomy

[Countless](position_countless.md). The statistical many, an anonymous, future, numerous population whose equal need draws no feeling because it has no face, and who go underfunded.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Numerous. Faceless. A figure on a page.
Is the thousand who will die if the programme is not funded and live if it is, present only as a count, a statistic no one can picture.
No unreality in it: the deaths are as real as any, only nameless, futured, and many, so that no single face stands out from them.

## Action

Waits unseen, on [Wait Unseen](plan_wait_unseen.md), standing before the giver as a number rather than a person, summoning no feeling and receiving no gift.
Grows only more abstract as it grows more numerous, the mass harder to feel than any one of it.
Does what a statistical many does: presents an equal and larger need with no face to carry it, and is not answered.

## Shadow

Not a lesser need, an unfeelable one, and that is the whole difficulty: the many need help as much as the one and more of them need it, and their very number is what empties them of a face.
Because feeling fastens on a person and flattens before a count, the Countless summons less compassion the more numerous it is, so the larger the population at stake the less it is felt and the less it is funded, its scale, which should weigh most, the very thing that makes it weigh least, and its members saved only where a colder instrument counts them.
It carries the greatest need and the least face, and carrying the least face is what leaves the greatest need unmet.

## Tell

Shows only as a line in a report, "an estimated four thousand preventable deaths a year," and no one reaches for a checkbook.
Is as real as the child, truly, and moves no one, because a number has nothing for the feeling to hold, and four thousand has less than one.
