# Position: Actuary

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md). The seat that would fund by the numbers, weighing the statistical lives the feeling cannot and moving the choice off the warm giver onto a colder instrument.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A count it will not discount and a rule to allocate by it: the standing to value the statistical life the feeling ignores and to fund the many by the number.
A clear case: the statistical deaths are real and equal, so weighing them evenly is the fair thing the feeling will not do.
No power to make the giver feel the count, only to allocate around the feeling, so the seat can correct the aim only by taking the choice out of warm hands.

## Orders

Count the lives.
Value the statistical life the heart discounts, pool the giving, and allocate by the number so the faceless population is funded.
Move the choice off the feeling giver and onto an instrument that can weigh what sympathy cannot.

## Loses

The warmth that would have to carry the correction, and any hope of persuading the giver by the count.
Because pressing the number on a feeling giver dampens the compassion for the face without kindling any for the many, the seat cannot reform the giver at all, so it gives up persuasion and keeps only the instrument, holding the correction in a place outside the heart because inside the heart the count only chills.
It surrenders the giver's warmth to save the many, funding the number only by moving the choice where no feeling reaches.

## Drives

Toward the many funded by a count and the choice moved off the heart,
the correction built into the instrument and never into the giver,
so the seat that would weigh the statistical life must first take the weighing out of warm hands.
