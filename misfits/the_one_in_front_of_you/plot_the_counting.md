# Plot: The Counting

## Taxonomy

[The One in Front of You](play_the_one_in_front_of_you.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Table](place_the_table.md), the count already tallied, waiting for the choice to be moved onto it or pressed back onto a heart.
No face on the page, only columns, and a giver somewhere still answering faces the columns do not show.

## Action

[Counting](process_counting.md): [The Actuary](persona_the_actuary.md), holding [the actuary's seat](position_actuary.md), weighs the statistical lives the feeling discounts and would allocate the giving by the number.

## Stage

[The Actuary](persona_the_actuary.md), on [Count the Lives](plan_count_the_lives.md), pools the giving and sets every statistical life beside every other at the same rate, an allocation the feeling structurally cannot make.
[The Giver](persona_the_giver.md), on [Answer the Named](plan_answer_the_named.md), stands at the edge of the count, a heart that funds a face well and is only chilled, never warmed, by being shown a number.

## Tension

Moving the choice off the heart and onto the instrument is the one path that reaches the Countless, and it works only where the heart is never asked to make the deliberation itself.
Press the number on the Giver directly, ask it to reason past the face and value the statistical life the same, and the compassion for the one in view cools without kindling any for the many, so it gives less to everyone. The plot closes one of two ways: the choice moves onto the colder instrument and the many are funded ... or the Giver is handed the deliberation, and gives less to all.
