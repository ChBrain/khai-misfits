# Piece: The Misdiagnosis

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The reading of arithmetic as waste: the honest cost mistaken for bloat, and a culprit sought.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), in the gap read as a fault.
Where a rising cost against falling goods is taken for mismanagement.

## Load Bearing

Without it the rising cost would simply be paid, and the misfit would end at arithmetic.
With it the cost is misread: a rise driven by uneven productivity looks identical to one driven by waste, so the payer hunts a culprit that is not there and reaches for the cure that fits bloat, not arithmetic. The Misdiagnosis is load bearing because it is the misreading that turns a benign relative-price rise into a false crisis (Baumol), the step that converts an honest cost into a hunt for fault, and the door through which the degrading cure walks in.

## Apparent

Sound cost discipline.
Reading a runaway cost as waste and demanding it be cut looks like exactly the vigilance good stewardship requires, so the misdiagnosis wears the face of prudence, and its error, mistaking arithmetic for bloat, hides inside a reflex everyone approves.

## Yearbook

Made by [the payer](persona_the_payer.md) reading [the rising cost](piece_the_rising_cost.md), the wrong turn the play hinges on.
It is bloat seen where none stands: the Misdiagnosis invites [the false cure](piece_the_false_cure.md), the factory's remedy brought to work it will only degrade.
