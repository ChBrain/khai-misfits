# Process: Performing

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The stagnant service given at a pace that cannot be raised without loss.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Work whose labour is its product, and an hour that is the doing.
A quartet, a ward, a classroom, where the time spent is the service rendered.

## Direction

Toward the service delivered whole, at fixed output per hour.
The hour is given as it always was, the care or the craft complete, and no more units come from the same hour than came a century ago.

## Lever

A pace set by the service, not by the clock's economy.
The hour cannot be compressed without the service becoming less, so output per hour holds flat by the very nature of the work, and effort cannot lift it as it lifts the factory's.

## Echo

A steady service, and a wage that will not hold steady with it.
The same hour delivered, the same output per worker, and a pay rate the wider economy keeps raising around an output that cannot rise.
