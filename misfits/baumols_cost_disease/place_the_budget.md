# Place: The Budget

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). Where the cost is either cut or paid.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A line item for the service and a decision to make: trim it or fund it.
The cutter with a saving to book, the funder with a price to pay, the same rising cost between them.

## Holds

The two answers, and their true prices.
Here the choice is made, so the budget holds both the squeeze that cuts the cost by cutting the care and the funding that keeps the service whole by paying its rising price.

## Offers

A real decision about a real cost.
The budget forces the question the ledger only posed, so it offers the point where the disease is answered, one way by the cut and one way by the payment.

## Withheld

A costless option.
The budget offers no way to keep the service whole and cheap at once, because the cost is arithmetic and the labour is the service, so it withholds the tidy saving the cutter wants and charges the funder an ever-larger share for the honest alternative.
