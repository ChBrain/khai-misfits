# Persona: The Funder

## Taxonomy

[Funder](position_funder.md). The one who accepts the rising price and pays it out of the general growth that can afford it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady, unfashionable, willing to pay for what cannot be cheapened.
Grants that the service will cost more every year and that this is right, not a failure to fix.
Prepared to spend an ever-larger share on care and teaching and art, out of a prosperity that keeps growing.

## Action

Funds the service at its true rising price.
Pays the climbing cost from the same general growth that made the goods cheap, holding that a richer society can well afford the things it cannot speed up.
Defends the budget against the reflex to cut, and does the harder work of finding the real waste without gutting the honest cost around it.

## Shadow

Paying the true price asks an ever-growing share, and looks, every year, like losing the argument.
The funder is right that the cost is affordable and that cutting it means cutting the care, but its answer is to spend more forever on things that never get cheaper, which reads as profligacy beside the cutter's tidy savings, and it must still tell the genuine bloat from the arithmetic or be accused of shielding both.
Not a spendthrift: the one who pays the honest price of the unfaster things, and is called wasteful for it.

## Tell

"It costs what it costs. We are rich enough to pay it, so we will."
The unfashionable answer that keeps the service whole by refusing to cut it.
