# Plot: The Advance

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The factory](place_the_factory.md), output per hour climbing year on year, and goods growing cheaper.
The method improves, the line speeds, and the pay rises on the strength of real gains.

## Action

[Advancing](process_advancing.md): [The Machinist](persona_the_machinist.md), holding [the machinist's seat](position_machinist.md), raises the line and turns genuine progress into cheaper goods and higher wages, and the higher wages flow into the labour market the whole economy shares.

## Stage

[The Machinist](persona_the_machinist.md) tables [Raise the Line](plan_raise_the_line.md): make more per hour and pass the saving on, so [the productive sector](piece_the_productive_sector.md) lifts pay across the board.
Through [the common wage](piece_the_common_wage.md), the rate it sets becomes the rate every skilled worker can command, including those whose hour cannot be sped up.

## Tension

Everything here is good news, and the good news is the seed of the trouble.
Nothing is wrong: the progress is real, the goods are cheaper, the wage is honestly earned. That is the setup, not yet the trap: a rising wage justified entirely by gains in one sector, about to become the going rate in another that made no such gains and cannot, and no one yet counting the cost that will fall on the work that cannot keep up.
