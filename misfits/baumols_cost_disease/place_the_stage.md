# Place: The Stage

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). Where the service is given, and the hour is the product.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A quartet at its desks, a nurse at a bedside, a teacher before a class.
The work done in real time, at the pace the work requires, the doing and the time inseparable.

## Holds

The stagnant hour, and the wage that rises around it.
Here output per hour cannot climb without the service falling, so the place holds a labour that is the product, paid a wage the wider economy keeps lifting.

## Offers

The service, whole and unhurried.
The stage gives exactly what it always gave, the full hour of care or craft, delivered at the only pace that keeps it what it is.

## Withheld

Any way to do it faster.
The stage cannot compress the hour without making the service less, so it withholds the one thing the factory offers, and its fidelity to the hour is what fixes its output while its wage climbs.
