# Process: Advancing

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). Productivity rising in the progressive sector, lifting wages across the whole economy.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A method, a machine, a chip that makes more per hour than before.
A sector where output per worker genuinely climbs year on year.

## Direction

Toward cheaper goods and higher pay.
The rising output per hour lets the same wage buy more, so goods fall in price and wages can rise without prices following, the plain benefit of progress.

## Lever

Real gains passed into the shared labour market.
Because the progress is genuine, the higher pay it justifies is real, and because the labour market is shared, that higher pay becomes the going rate for skill everywhere, not only where the gains were made.

## Echo

A richer economy, and a rate the stagnant service must match.
Goods cheaper and pay higher across the board, and a wage now set by a productivity the ward and the stage cannot share.
