# Plan: Give the Hour

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the server's seat](position_server.md), inside [Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Server](position_server.md)

## Direction

Give the work the hour it requires, at the pace the service needs, because the hour is the product and cannot be compressed without the service becoming less. Take the wage your skill could earn elsewhere, since the whole economy wants it too.

## Orders

([The Server](persona_the_server.md) gives the same hour it always gave, and is paid a wage the whole economy sets.)

## Implementation

([The Stage](place_the_stage.md); the [performing](process_performing.md) process; [the handicraft](piece_the_handicraft.md) is an hour that cannot be sped up, and [the common wage](piece_the_common_wage.md) lifts its pay though its output holds flat.)

## Targets

- [ ] the hour the work requires is given, whole and unhurried
- [ ] the service is kept intact, at the pace it needs
- [ ] the wage rises with the economy, though output per hour does not
- [ ] the steady hour, priced against cheapening goods, reads as a runaway cost
