# Position: Server

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The seat whose labour is the service, and whose hour cannot be sped up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An hour that is the product, not a means to it.
A skill the whole economy also wants, so a wage the whole economy sets.
No way to raise output per hour without making the service less.

## Orders

Give the hour the work requires.
Keep the service whole, at the pace the service needs.
Take a wage near what your skill could earn elsewhere.

## Loses

Nothing in its work, and all its standing in the ledger.
Its hour holds steady while goods grow cheaper, so its unchanged service reads as a runaway cost, and it is priced, fairly, out of easy reach.
Faithful to the hour, and blamed for the price of keeping it.

## Drives

Toward the hour kept whole,
which cannot be compressed,
so a steady, honest service grows dearer every year against a world that got faster.
