# Position: Funder

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The seat that pays the rising price out of the growth that can afford it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A general prosperity that keeps growing, the same that made the goods cheap.
The willingness to spend an ever-larger share on what cannot be cheapened.
The duty to find the real waste without gutting the honest cost around it.

## Orders

Fund the service at its true rising price.
Pay the climbing cost from the growth that causes it.
Hold the budget against the reflex to cut, and still root out genuine bloat.

## Loses

The argument, every year, though not the case.
Its answer is to spend more forever on things that never get cheaper, which is right and reads as profligacy beside the cutter's tidy savings, and it must tell the real waste from the arithmetic or be accused of shielding both.
Correct that the price is affordable, and called wasteful for paying it.

## Drives

Toward the true price paid,
out of the prosperity that raised it,
so the service is kept whole by refusing to cut it, at the cost of an ever-growing share and the look of losing.
