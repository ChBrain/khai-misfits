# Persona: The Payer

## Taxonomy

[Payer](position_payer.md). The one who faces the ever-rising bill and reaches for a culprit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Cost-conscious, aggrieved, watching goods get cheaper as the service gets dearer.
Sees the price of care and teaching and art climb year on year while televisions and chips fall.
Cannot believe a cost could rise so far without someone, somewhere, being wasteful.

## Action

Demands to know why it costs so much, and looks for the fault.
Sets the falling price of goods against the rising price of the service and reads the gap as proof of bloat.
Calls for the waste to be found and cut, sure that a runaway cost must have a runaway cause.

## Shadow

The bloat it is sure of is mostly arithmetic, and the search for a culprit finds none because there is none.
Its instinct is reasonable, that costs rising against a falling tide look like mismanagement, but the tide is the point: the goods fell because of progress, and the service rose because it could not share the progress, so the fault the payer hunts is the shadow of a real cause it cannot see.
Not unreasonable: a fair mind misreading arithmetic as blame.

## Tell

"Everything else got cheaper. Why did this get so expensive?"
The fair comparison that points at a culprit where only arithmetic stands.
