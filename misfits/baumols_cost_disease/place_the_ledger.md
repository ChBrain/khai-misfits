# Place: The Ledger

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). Where the rising cost is read, and mistaken.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Two lines on a chart: the price of goods falling, the price of the service climbing.
A payer reading the gap between them, and an economist reading the same gap.

## Holds

The rising cost, and the misreading it invites.
Here the two trends sit side by side, so the ledger holds a figure that is plain arithmetic and looks exactly like bloat, and the two readings of it, fault and mechanism, stand together.

## Offers

A clear, honest number.
The ledger shows the true cost of the service and the true cost of goods, so it offers an accurate account that anyone can read, and the account is not wrong, only easy to misread.

## Withheld

The reason behind the number.
The ledger shows that the service costs more and never why, so a rise driven by uneven productivity reads identically to one driven by waste, and it withholds the cause that would tell the arithmetic from the bloat.
