# Piece: The Common Wage

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The shared labour market: the link by which the stagnant sector's wages must track the progressive sector's.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the stage](place_the_stage.md), in the pay the server draws to stay.
Where a wage set elsewhere must be met here.

## Load Bearing

Without it the stagnant service could pay a flat wage to match its flat output, and its cost would not climb.
With it the two sectors draw from one pool of labour, so a nurse or a player who could take the factory's rising wage must be paid near it to remain, and the stagnant sector's pay is dragged up by gains it did not make. The Common Wage is load bearing because it is the transmission the model turns on (Baumol): it carries the progressive sector's productivity into the stagnant sector's wage bill, so the cost disease is not a fault of the service but the price of belonging to a growing economy.

## Apparent

Just fair pay for skilled work.
Paying a nurse or a teacher a wage near what their skill could earn elsewhere looks like plain fairness, because it is, so the common wage reads as justice rather than mechanism, and its role in transmitting the rising cost hides inside its obvious rightness.

## Yearbook

Set by [the productive sector](piece_the_productive_sector.md) and paid at [the stage](place_the_stage.md), the wire between the two.
It is the link that carries the disease: the Common Wage lifts [the handicraft](piece_the_handicraft.md) pay though its output holds, and turns progress into [the rising cost](piece_the_rising_cost.md).
