# Persona: The Server

## Taxonomy

[Server](position_server.md). The one whose labour is the service, and whose hour cannot be sped up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Skilled, present, giving the hour the work requires.
Plays the quartet, tends the ward, holds the classroom, where the doing is the thing and the time is the doing.
Knows the hour cannot be compressed without the service becoming something less.

## Action

Gives the same hour it always gave, and is paid a wage the whole economy sets.
Could have taken the factory's rising wage, so must be paid near it to stay, though its output per hour has not moved.
Costs more each year for the same true hour, and delivers exactly what it always did.

## Shadow

Its unchanging hour, priced against goods that keep getting cheaper, becomes a scandal it did not cause.
Nothing about its work got slower or worse, but the world around it got faster, so its steady hour reads as a runaway cost, and the very fidelity of the service, the hour kept whole, is what makes it look like waste.
Not overpaid: fairly paid for work that cannot be hurried, in an economy that hurried everything else.

## Tell

"It takes as long as it takes. That's the job."
The plain truth of the hour that cannot shrink, and the reason its price will climb forever.
