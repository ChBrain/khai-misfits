# Position: Cutter

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The seat that raises output per hour, and cuts the service to cut the cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

One cure for a rising cost: more units per worker.
A service it treats as a process with too few units per hour.
The power to raise the caseload, swell the class, speed the shift.

## Orders

Apply the factory's cure.
Get more output per hour: more patients per nurse, more pupils per teacher.
Book the saving the numbers demand.

## Loses

The service, in the very act of saving its cost.
Here the labour was the product, so fewer labour-hours per unit is not the same service made cheaper but a thinner service at the old price, and the saving is booked while the care quietly shrinks.
Efficient, and destroying the thing the efficiency was for.

## Drives

Toward more output per hour,
which on this work means less service per unit,
so the cost falls only because the care does, and the cure hollows out the thing it treats.
