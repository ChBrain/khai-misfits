# Process: Pricing

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The relative cost of the service climbing as the common wage rises and its output does not.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A wage lifted by the progressive sector, and a stagnant service that must pay it.
The going rate rising while output per hour on the stagnant work holds flat.

## Direction

Toward a service ever dearer against ever-cheaper goods.
Wages climb and output does not, so cost per unit rises without limit, and measured against goods that keep falling, the service's price climbs year on year.

## Lever

Rising pay divided by unchanging output.
Cost per unit is the wage over what an hour makes, so when the wage rises and the hour's output cannot, the cost must climb, mechanically, with nothing in the work having changed.

## Echo

A rising bill that is arithmetic, and a choice about how to meet it.
The service dearer every year through no fault of its own, and a decision waiting: read it as bloat and cut, or as the price of prosperity and pay.
