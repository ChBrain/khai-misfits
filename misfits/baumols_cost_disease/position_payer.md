# Position: Payer

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The seat that faces the rising bill and hunts a fault.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A bill for the service that climbs every year.
The plain sight of goods growing cheaper as the service grows dearer.
The reasonable belief that a runaway cost must have a runaway cause.

## Orders

Ask why it costs so much.
Set the falling price of goods against the rising price of care.
Demand the waste be found and cut.

## Loses

Its search, to a culprit that is not there.
The gap it reads as bloat is the gap between a sector that got faster and one that could not, so the fault it hunts is the shadow of a real cause, and the cutting it calls for lands on the service, not on any waste.
Right to watch the cost, and wrong about what it sees.

## Drives

Toward a culprit for the rising bill,
which arithmetic, not waste, has raised,
so the honest cost is read as a fault and answered with a cut.
