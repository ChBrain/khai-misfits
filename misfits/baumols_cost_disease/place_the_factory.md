# Place: The Factory

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). Where output per hour climbs and the common wage is set.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A line, a machine, a chip, each year making more per hour than the last.
Goods growing cheaper as the method improves, and pay rising on the strength of real gains.

## Holds

The progress, and the wage it justifies.
Here output per hour genuinely climbs, so the place holds both cheaper goods and a rising wage, the honest fruit of doing more with the same hour.

## Offers

More for less, honestly.
The factory turns real productivity into cheaper goods and higher pay, so it offers the plain benefit of progress, the reason the whole economy grows richer.

## Withheld

Any limit on where its wage reaches.
The rising pay it earns flows through the shared labour market to work that cannot match its gains, so the factory withholds nothing from the wage it sets, and its progress becomes the stagnant service's rising cost.
