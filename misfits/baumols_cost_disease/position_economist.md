# Position: Economist

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The seat that reads the rising cost as arithmetic, not fault.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The two sectors and the one wage in full view.
The finding that the rising cost is the price of prosperity, not waste.
The honest caveat that some real bloat still hides among the arithmetic.

## Orders

Name the mechanism, not a culprit.
Show that goods fell from progress and the service rose because it could not share it.
Say that more output per hour, here, is a thinner service, and the answer is to pay the price.

## Loses

Its audience, to a truth that offers no villain.
The diagnosis is correct and undramatic, set against a cost that feels like a scandal and a reflex that wants a fault, so it convinces no one who needs a culprit, and must still admit the genuine waste it cannot pretend away.
Right, and unpersuasive for having no one to blame.

## Drives

Toward the arithmetic under the rising cost,
against a reflex that reads every rise as a fault,
so the true account is told and, offering nothing to cut, is set aside.
