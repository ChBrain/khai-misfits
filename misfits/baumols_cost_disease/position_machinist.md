# Position: Machinist

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The seat in the progressive sector whose real gains set the common wage.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A line whose output per hour climbs year on year.
Genuine progress to turn into higher pay and cheaper goods.
A place in the same labour market as the service it will price out.

## Orders

Raise the line.
Make more per hour, and pass the saving on.
Earn the rising wage your gains have honestly justified.

## Loses

Nothing, and passes an unpayable bill to work that cannot follow.
Its progress lifts the wage across the shared labour market, so the stagnant service must be paid near its rising rate, and the machinist's honest advance becomes the ward's runaway cost.
Blameless, and the cause.

## Drives

Toward more output per hour,
which lifts every wage including those that cannot match it,
so real progress in one sector prices the unfaster things in another out of reach.
