# Piece: Baumol's Cost Disease

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The phenomenon whole: the unfaster things dearer forever, and its remedy in paying the price out of the prosperity that causes it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the budget](place_the_budget.md), where the cost is answered.
And across the whole economy, wherever a fixed hour is priced against a falling one.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of parts with no shape.
With it the parts become one law: the handicraft, the productive sector, the common wage, the rising cost, the misdiagnosis, and the false cure are facets of a single mechanism, in which uneven productivity plus a shared labour market makes the things that cannot be sped up grow dearer forever, and the only honest answer is to pay the rising price out of the general growth that raised it. Baumol's Cost Disease is load bearing because it is the whole finding (Baumol) with its whole reframe attached: it holds the misfit together, and it holds that the rising cost is affordable, not a fault, so the remedy is to fund it, at the price of an ever-larger share and the labour of telling the real waste from the honest cost.

## Apparent

Either a health-and-education crisis of runaway waste, or a counsel to spend without limit.
Seen from one side the rising costs look like sectors failing and needing to be cut; from the other, like a licence to pour money into anything that pleads it cannot be made efficient; and both miss the mechanism, that the cost is arithmetic, affordable, and worth paying, while genuine waste still exists and must be found within it. The name itself is half the misreading: a disease implies a cure, when the rising cost is the healthy sign of an economy that got richer everywhere else.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the economist](persona_the_economist.md) and [the funder](persona_the_funder.md) through [pricing](process_pricing.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: Baumol's Cost Disease is what [the handicraft](piece_the_handicraft.md) suffers in a growing economy, undone only by paying [the rising cost](piece_the_rising_cost.md) as the price of prosperity, not by a cut that would cure the cost by killing the care.
