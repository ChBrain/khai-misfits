# Persona: The Machinist

## Taxonomy

[Machinist](position_machinist.md). The one in the progressive sector whose real gains lift every wage.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Inventive, tooled-up, making more per hour every year.
Works where the machine, the method, and the chip keep raising output, so the same hour yields more and more.
Earns a rising wage for real progress, and sees the goods it makes grow cheaper.

## Action

Raises the line, and lifts the whole labour market with it.
Turns genuine productivity into higher pay and cheaper goods, the honest fruit of doing more per hour.
Sets, without meaning to, the wage the nurse and the player must be paid near, though their hour cannot follow.

## Shadow

Its blameless progress is what makes the stagnant service dear.
It did everything right, made more for less and passed the saving on, but the rising wage its gains justify flows through the shared labour market to work that cannot match the gains, so the machinist's virtue is the exact cause of the ward's runaway cost.
Not a culprit: an innocent engine whose real advance prices the unfaster things out of reach.

## Tell

"We make twice what we did, for less. Why can't they?"
The fair question whose answer is that some hours simply cannot be doubled.
