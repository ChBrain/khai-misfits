# Pitch: Find the Bloat

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Businesslike, impatient, sure there is waste to be found.
This costs more every year, and things that cost more every year are being run badly, so find the bloat and cut it.
The voice of hard-nosed efficiency, spoken by people who are right that costs should be watched and cannot see that this rising cost is not a fault but the arithmetic of a service that cannot be made faster.

## Undertow

Under the efficiency, a service dearer every year and no waste anywhere in it.
The factory learns to make more per hour, so wages rise across the whole economy, and the nurse and the player, who could have taken the factory's wage, must be paid near it, though their hour cannot be compressed, so their cost climbs with the economy while their output holds flat.
No one is wasteful: the progress is real, the wage is fair, the bill is arithmetic, and the cut that promises to save the money can only save it by cutting the hour that was the whole of the service.

## Nerve

The feeling of a tough, sensible search for savings,
every rising line read as a fault to be fixed,
the one cure that works on the factory, more output per hour, reached for on a ward where more output per hour is just a thinner service.

## Echo

After the run: a service priced honestly out of reach and blamed for it,
a squeeze that cut the cost by cutting the care,
and a rising bill that was never bloat but the plain price of the things that cannot be hurried.
