# Persona: The Cutter

## Taxonomy

[Cutter](position_cutter.md). The one who applies the factory's cure to the ward, and cuts the service to cut the cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Decisive, metric-driven, armed with the productivity playbook.
Knows one cure for a rising cost, the cure that tamed the factory: more output per hour.
Sees a ward or a classroom the same way, a process with too few units per worker.

## Action

Raises the caseload, swells the class, speeds the shift, thins the staff.
Wins the saving the numbers demanded, more patients per nurse, more pupils per teacher, more calls per hour.
Cuts the cost, and cuts, in the same motion, the very hour that was the service.

## Shadow

The cure that worked on goods destroys a service made of the hour it removes.
It is not wrong that fewer labour-hours cost less, but here the labour-hours were the care, so raising output per hour does not make the same service cheaper, it makes a thinner service at the old price, and the saving is booked while the thing bought quietly shrinks.
Not a butcher: an efficient hand applying the right cure to the wrong disease.

## Tell

"If each nurse just took more patients, the cost per patient would fall."
True, and the sentence under which the care per patient falls with it.
