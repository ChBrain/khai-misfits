# Process: Cutting

## Taxonomy

[Baumol's Cost Disease](play_baumols_cost_disease.md). The factory's cure applied to the service, raising output per hour by cutting the labour that was the service.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A rising cost read as waste, and the one cure that tamed the factory.
The demand for more output per hour, brought to work where the hour was the product.

## Direction

Toward a saving booked and a service thinned.
The caseload rises, the class swells, the shift speeds, so fewer labour-hours go into each unit, and the cost per unit falls because the service per unit does.

## Lever

More units per worker, on work where the labour was the unit's value.
On the factory this makes the same good cheaper; here, where the hour was the care, it makes a lesser service at the same price, so the lever cuts cost only by cutting the thing the cost was for.

## Echo

A cost tamed, and a service quietly hollowed.
The numbers improved and the saving claimed, the care per patient and the attention per pupil fallen, and the cure booked as a success while the thing it treated shrank.
