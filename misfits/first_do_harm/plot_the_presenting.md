# Plot: The Presenting

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Theatre](place_the_theatre.md), a real complaint arrived and a demand that it be met.
The Petitioner pressing that a condition this real cannot be left alone, the Healer set to respond.

## Action

[Demanding](process_demanding.md): [The Petitioner](persona_the_petitioner.md), holding [the petitioner's seat](position_petitioner.md), presses for the complaint to be treated, so that to act is expected and to forbear reads as failure.

## Stage

[The Petitioner](persona_the_petitioner.md), on [Demand a Cure](plan_demand_a_cure.md), makes [the complaint](piece_the_complaint.md) a call to act, and [the healer](persona_the_healer.md), on [Treat the Complaint](plan_treat_the_complaint.md), is set to apply the remedy the condition calls for.
The demand is fair, the complaint is real, and forbearance already reads as negligence and the cure as the responsible move.

## Tension

Demanding that a real complaint be met is proper, and the demand is what makes staying the hand look like failure.
There is nothing unreasonable: doing nothing about a genuine condition is often wrong. But a harm allowed is visible and a harm caused by a well-meant cure is hidden, so the plot closes with the pressure fallen toward intervention, the complaint on the chart, and the cure already the safe and responsible thing to give.
