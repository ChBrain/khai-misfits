# Persona: The Afflicted

## Taxonomy

[Afflicted](position_afflicted.md). The one who carries the complaint and seeks relief, and who bears, in the same body, both the cure that eases the condition and the new wound the cure inflicts.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Suffering. Seeking relief. Trusting the cure.
Is the one with the complaint, the patient, the ailing system, the body or the organisation that presents a real condition and wants it put right.
No foolishness in it: the complaint is genuine and relief is reasonable to seek, and the remedy really does ease the thing that hurts.

## Action

Brings the complaint, accepts the remedy, and is eased of the presenting condition it was suffering.
Takes the treatment offered in good faith and feels the real relief it gives.
Does what the afflicted does: it seeks relief and receives it, and receives, in the same body, the wound that came with the cure.

## Shadow

Not a fool for seeking relief, a real sufferer eased by a real cure, and that is the whole difficulty: the same body that carries the complaint carries the wound, so the relief and the harm arrive together and in the same place.
Because the cure that eases the complaint reaches past it, the Afflicted is left with a new condition it did not have before, a wound in a sound function the original complaint had spared, and because that wound presents as its own fresh trouble, the Afflicted brings it back as a new complaint, seeking relief again from the hand that made it, the suffering compounding through the very treatment that eased the first.
It is genuinely relieved of the complaint, and genuinely left with the wound, and cannot tell, because the wound wears a fresh face, that the second trouble is the price of the first cure.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "the first thing is better, but now something else is wrong."
Says it having been truly eased and truly harmed, and brings the new trouble back to be treated, not seeing it as the shadow of the relief it was given.
