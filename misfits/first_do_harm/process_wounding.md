# Process: Wounding

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The remedy reaching past the complaint to injure a sound part, the new and separate harm the cure inflicts on what the condition had spared.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A cure applied and a reach it cannot help.
At [the theatre](place_the_theatre.md), the remedy doing exactly what it does and touching a healthy function the complaint never reached.

## Direction

Toward a new wound in a new place.
Because the remedy is powerful enough to treat the complaint, wounding carries it past the presenting condition into a sound part, so the harm made is not the complaint worsened but a separate injury the condition never carried, a different trouble in a different place, arriving after the cure and looking nothing like it.

## Lever

The reach of a remedy real enough to work.
Because a cure that genuinely eases the complaint acts on more than the complaint, wounding is the shadow of efficacy and not of failure, so the process runs on the very power that makes the remedy worth giving, the new harm assembled from no malice and no mistake, only from a treatment strong enough to heal and therefore strong enough to injure what it was not aimed at.

## Echo

A sound part injured, and a fresh complaint born.
The remedy has reached past the condition as a real cure does, so what reads later as a new and unrelated trouble is, in truth, the wound the treatment made, a separate harm wearing its own face and carrying no mark of the cure that caused it.
