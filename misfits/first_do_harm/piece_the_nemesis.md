# Piece: The Nemesis

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The phenomenon whole, the harms of the cures grown past the conditions they treat, the treating-system's injury exceeding its healing and disabling the capacity to cope without it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Accumulated across [the theatre](place_the_theatre.md), where cure upon cure is given.
Unbuilt, if ever, at [the watch](place_the_watch.md), where the hand is stayed and no new harm is added.

## Load Bearing

Without it the harm is a wound or a chain of them, and the trap stays local.
With it the trap is complete and authorless: the harms of the treatments, compounded and never traced, grow past the conditions they were brought for, so the system that heals injures more than it cures and, expanding, disables the very capacity that would have coped without it (Illich, on medical nemesis, the social and cultural iatrogenesis by which the treating enterprise expropriates health). The Nemesis is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole: not one wound but the sum of all the cures' harms, a treating-system whose injury exceeds its healing and whose growth removes the ability to do without it, so the piece carries the reckoning at full strength, the first rule broken not once but structurally, by the enterprise of curing itself.

## Apparent

The march of treatment, a system doing ever more to heal, progress measured in interventions given.
Seen at the surface, the Nemesis looks like care advancing, more conditions met and more cures available, and the truth that the harms of the cures have grown past the conditions, that the system injures more than it heals, that its expansion has disabled the capacity to cope without it, stays hidden behind how much a treating-system doing more looks like healing doing better.

## Yearbook

Built by [the remedy](piece_the_remedy.md) given through [treating](process_treating.md) and cleared by [the alibi](piece_the_alibi.md), compounded through [the cascade](piece_the_cascade.md), unbuilt only by [the steward](persona_the_steward.md) at [the watch](place_the_watch.md).
It is the phenomenon whole: the Nemesis is the sum of [the wound](piece_the_wound.md) upon wound, the harms of the cures grown past [the complaint](piece_the_complaint.md)s they treated, the first rule broken structurally at the centre of first do harm.
