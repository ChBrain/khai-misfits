# Position: Petitioner

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The seat of the demand that something be done, the pressure to intervene that makes forbearance read as negligence and the cure the responsible move.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A real complaint and a fair claim that it be met: the standing to demand a response and the visibility of the harm that is allowed.
A sound basis: doing nothing about a genuine condition is often wrong, so the call to act is proper on its face.
No sight of the hidden harm the cure will do, and no credit for the wound a stayed hand would spare.

## Orders

Demand a cure.
Press for the complaint to be treated, make acting the expected thing, and read the healer who forbears as having failed.
Require that something be done, because a real complaint left alone is a dereliction.

## Loses

The one move that would break the cascade, exactly by demanding the cure.
Because a harm allowed is visible and a harm caused by a well-meant cure is hidden, the seat condemns the forbearing healer and thanks the treating one, so the pressure falls always toward intervention and the restraint that alone spares the sound is made to look like negligence, the demand to act sound in itself and fatal to the hand that would stay.
It gains the complaint met, and loses the forbearance that would have spared the wound the cure will make.

## Drives

Toward the cure demanded and the restraint condemned,
the visible complaint met and the hidden harm unweighed,
so the seat that demands a cure forces the hand, and the something that must be done is the something that wounds.
