# Process: Forbearing

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The Steward staying the hand, weighing the cure's hidden harm against the complaint's visible one and tolerating the condition rather than trading it for a new wound, the costly move that breaks the cascade.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A complaint that could be treated and someone unwilling to trade it for a worse harm.
At [the watch](place_the_watch.md), the steward weighing where a remedy would reach and holding the hand where the cure would cost more than the condition.

## Direction

Toward the complaint tolerated and the sound part spared.
Because the cure would wound a healthy part the complaint had left whole, forbearing weighs the hidden harm against the visible one, traces the wound back to the remedy that would make it, and lets the complaint stand, so the sound part is spared and the cascade is not fed, the first rule kept by the one move that adds no new harm.

## Lever

The willingness to bear the blame for the harm not caused.
Because a harm allowed is visible and a harm caused by a good cure is hidden, forbearing runs only on someone choosing to be read as negligent for the wound they prevent, so the process is exacted continuously and thanked rarely, the corrective assembled from no cheap restraint, only from a steward who will let a real complaint stand and take the blame, to spare the sound part the cure would reach.

## Echo

A hand stayed, and a wound not made.
The steward has forborne where the cure would cost more than the complaint, so what reads as a failure to act is, in truth, the one move that keeps the first rule, the sound part spared and the cascade broken, the prevented wound leaving no trace and the tolerated complaint plain for all to blame.
