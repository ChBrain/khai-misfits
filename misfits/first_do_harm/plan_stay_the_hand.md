# Plan: Stay the Hand

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the steward's seat](position_steward.md), inside [First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Steward](position_steward.md)

## Direction

Weigh the cure's hidden harm against the complaint's visible one, and forbear where the treatment would cost more than the condition. Trace the wound back to the remedy that made it, let a real complaint stand rather than trade it for a new harm, and break the cascade by not adding to it. Do no harm, first, by not doing the harm the cure would.

## Orders

([The Steward](persona_the_steward.md) stays the hand and tolerates the complaint to spare the sound part, tracing the wound to the cure, at the price of being read as negligent for the harm not caused.)

## Implementation

([The Watch](place_the_watch.md); the [forbearing](process_forbearing.md) process; the wound is traced to [the remedy](piece_the_remedy.md) through [the alibi](piece_the_alibi.md), [the complaint](piece_the_complaint.md) tolerated rather than traded for [the wound](piece_the_wound.md), and [the cascade](piece_the_cascade.md) broken.)

- [ ] the wound is traced back to the remedy that made it, past its alibi
