# Position: Steward

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The seat that would stay the hand and tolerate the complaint rather than trade it for a new harm, tracing the wound to the remedy at the price of being read as negligent.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The judgement to weigh a hidden harm against a visible one and the will to bear the blame for the harm not caused: the standing to forbear and the eye to trace the wound to the cure.
A real corrective: staying the hand is the one move that stops the remedy from wounding the sound.
No way to show the wound it prevents, and no welcome for the restraint that reads as failure to act.

## Orders

Stay the hand.
Weigh the cure's hidden harm against the complaint's visible one, trace the wound to the remedy that made it, and forbear where the treatment would cost more than the condition.
Let the complaint stand rather than trade it for a new wound, and break the cascade by not adding to it.

## Loses

The standing of one who acts, and the thanks the treating healer is given.
Because a harm allowed is visible and a harm caused by a well-meant cure is hidden, the seat that forbears is read as having failed while the seat that treats and wounds is praised, so the restraint that alone keeps the first rule is exacted against the demand to act and rewarded rarely, the wound it prevents leaving no trace and the complaint it tolerates plain for all to blame.
It gains the sound part spared and the cascade broken, and loses the standing that only acting confers.

## Drives

Toward the hand stayed and the blame borne,
the visible complaint tolerated to spare the hidden harm,
so the seat that stays its hand keeps the first rule, and is read as the failure to do the thing that harms.
