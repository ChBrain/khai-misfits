# Process: Treating

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The Healer applying the genuine remedy to the presenting complaint, the competent, good-faith cure that eases the condition and reaches past it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A real complaint and a remedy that works.
At [the theatre](place_the_theatre.md), the healer reading the presenting condition and applying the cure that eases it, under the demand that something be done.

## Direction

Toward the complaint eased and the remedy given.
Because the complaint is real and the cure works on it, treating applies the remedy in good faith and relieves the presenting condition, so the cure does exactly what it does, and doing exactly what it does is what carries it past the complaint into the sound part the condition had spared, the easing and the reach the same act.

## Lever

The genuine efficacy of the remedy.
Because the cure truly treats the complaint, treating is competent care and not quackery, so the process runs on a remedy that works and a healer who means well, the harm assembled from no error, only from a cure real enough to ease the complaint and, being that real, powerful enough to wound what the complaint had left whole.

## Echo

A complaint eased, and a sound part reached past.
The healer has treated the condition as care demands, so what reads as a cure delivered is, in truth, a remedy that eased the complaint and, in the same act, reached past it, the easing seen and the wounding not yet arrived.
