# Piece: The Wound

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The new and separate harm the remedy inflicts, a different pathology in a different place, injured on the sound part the complaint had left whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Made at [the theatre](place_the_theatre.md), where the cure reaches past the complaint.
Logged as a fresh condition at [the chart](place_the_chart.md), filed apart from the cure that made it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the cure only heals, and there is no trap, only a treatment that works.
With it the trap turns: a harm that is not the complaint worsened but a separate injury the condition never carried, made by the remedy on a sound part it reached past to touch (Illich, on clinical iatrogenesis, disease produced by the intervention itself). The Wound is load bearing because it is what makes the cure a trap and not a care: a new pathology in a new place, distinct from the complaint and caused by the treatment of it, so the piece carries the harm that is the shadow of a real cure, and carries it wearing a fresh face, arriving after the treatment and looking nothing like the hand that made it.

## Apparent

A new complaint, an unrelated condition, a fresh trouble that has arisen and must now be treated.
Seen at the surface, the Wound looks like its own problem, a separate ailment with no connection to the cure that preceded it, and the truth that it is the remedy's doing, a harm caused by the treatment and not a coincidence after it, that the sound part it struck was spared by the original complaint, stays hidden behind how completely a wound in a new place looks like a new complaint.
