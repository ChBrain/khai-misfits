# Plot: The Stayed Hand

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Watch](place_the_watch.md), and someone who would weigh the cure against the complaint before giving it.
The Steward tracing where the remedy would reach and asking whether the treatment would cost more than the condition.

## Action

[Forbearing](process_forbearing.md): [The Steward](persona_the_steward.md), holding [the steward's seat](position_steward.md), weighs the hidden harm against the visible one and holds the hand.

## Stage

[The Steward](persona_the_steward.md), on [Stay the Hand](plan_stay_the_hand.md), traces [the wound](piece_the_wound.md) back to [the remedy](piece_the_remedy.md) through [the alibi](piece_the_alibi.md), and lets [the complaint](piece_the_complaint.md) stand rather than trade it for a new harm.
Against the forbearance stand the demand that something be done and an eye that reads restraint as failure, the wound it would prevent leaving no trace to show.

## Tension

Staying the hand is the one move that keeps the first rule, and it asks the steward to be read as negligent for the harm not caused.
It weighs a hidden harm against a visible complaint, traces a wound to a cure that has an alibi, and lets a real condition stand. So the plot closes one of two ways: the hand stayed and the sound part spared and the cascade broken, at the price of looking like failure ... or restraint read as negligence, the cure demanded and given, and the wound made after all.
