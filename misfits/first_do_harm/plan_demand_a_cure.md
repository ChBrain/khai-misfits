# Plan: Demand a Cure

## Direction

Press for the complaint to be treated and make acting the expected thing. Read the healer who forbears as having failed and the one who treats as having done their duty, so that intervention is the safe move. Require that something be done, because a real complaint left alone is a dereliction.

([The Petitioner](persona_the_petitioner.md) demands the complaint be met, so forbearance reads as negligence and the cure as responsibility, the pressure falling always toward the intervention that wounds.)
