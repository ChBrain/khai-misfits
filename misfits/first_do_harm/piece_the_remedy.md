# Piece: The Remedy

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The genuine treatment itself, the cure that truly works on the complaint, real enough to ease the condition and powerful enough to wound what it had spared.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Applied at [the theatre](place_the_theatre.md), where the cure meets the complaint.
Recorded clean at [the chart](place_the_chart.md), where its tie to the wound is never drawn.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no cure, and the complaint is only untreated, not made worse by treatment.
With it the trap has its engine: a treatment that genuinely works on the complaint, applied in good faith, and reaching, by the same power that heals, past the condition into a sound part (Illich, on iatrogenesis, the injury that is the work of the healing itself, not of its failure). The Remedy is load bearing because it is the good cure that harms: not a quack's fraud nor a fix that fails, but a real treatment whose efficacy is exactly what lets it wound, so the piece carries the intervention that eases the complaint and, in easing it, injures what the complaint never touched, the healing and the harm one act.

## Apparent

A cure, a treatment that works, the responsible answer to a real complaint.
Seen at the surface, the Remedy looks like care delivered, a genuine relief of the thing that hurt, and the truth that the same treatment reaches past the complaint and wounds a sound part, that its efficacy and its harm are the one power, that a cure real enough to work is real enough to injure, stays hidden behind how plainly a working cure looks like nothing but help.

## Yearbook

Applied by [the healer](persona_the_healer.md) through [treating](process_treating.md), and demanded by [the petitioner](persona_the_petitioner.md), the genuine cure of the complaint.
