# Plot: The Cure

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Theatre](place_the_theatre.md), the complaint before the healer and the remedy in hand.
The Healer applying the treatment the condition calls for, competently and in good faith.

## Action

[Treating](process_treating.md): [The Healer](persona_the_healer.md), holding [the healer's seat](position_healer.md), applies the genuine remedy and eases the presenting complaint.

## Stage

[The Healer](persona_the_healer.md), on [Treat the Complaint](plan_treat_the_complaint.md), gives [the remedy](piece_the_remedy.md), and it works, relieving [the complaint](piece_the_complaint.md) the Afflicted brought.
The cure does exactly what it does, sound as a treatment of the thing it was for, and reaches, in the same act and unseen, past the complaint into the sound part.

## Tension

Applying a remedy that genuinely treats the complaint is competent care, and it is the same act that reaches past the complaint.
There is nothing false: the cure works and the healer means only to help. But a remedy real enough to ease the complaint is powerful enough to touch what the complaint had spared, so the plot closes with the condition genuinely treated and the cure already reaching, unseen, past the thing it healed.
