# Plan: Seek Relief

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the afflicted's seat](position_afflicted.md), inside [First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Afflicted](position_afflicted.md)

## Direction

Bring the complaint and accept the remedy offered in good faith. Take the real easing the cure gives, because the condition is genuine and relief is reasonable to seek. When a new trouble follows the treatment, bring that back too, as its own complaint, and seek relief again.

## Orders

([The Afflicted](persona_the_afflicted.md) seeks relief and receives it, and bears in the same body the wound the cure inflicts, bringing the wound back as a fresh complaint to the hand that made it.)

## Implementation

([The Theatre](place_the_theatre.md); [the remedy](piece_the_remedy.md) eases [the complaint](piece_the_complaint.md) and leaves [the wound](piece_the_wound.md) in the same body, brought back under [the alibi](piece_the_alibi.md) to be treated again.)

## Targets

- [ ] the complaint is brought and the remedy offered in good faith accepted
- [ ] the real easing the cure gives is received
- [ ] the wound the cure inflicts is borne in the same body that was eased
- [ ] the new wound, taken for a fresh trouble, is brought back to be treated again
