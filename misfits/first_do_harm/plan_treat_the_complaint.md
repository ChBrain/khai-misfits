# Plan: Treat the Complaint

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the healer's seat](position_healer.md), inside [First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Healer](position_healer.md)

## Direction

Read the presenting complaint and apply the remedy that eases it. Do the competent, demanded, responsible thing, treating what asks to be treated with a cure that genuinely works on it. Cure the condition, because the complaint is real and the treatment is sound, and when a new trouble follows, treat that too.

## Orders

([The Healer](persona_the_healer.md) applies the genuine remedy to the complaint in good faith, and the cure, doing exactly what it does, reaches past the condition and wounds the sound part, the harm unseen and treated again as a fresh complaint.)

## Implementation

([The Theatre](place_the_theatre.md); the [treating](process_treating.md) and [wounding](process_wounding.md) processes; [the remedy](piece_the_remedy.md) eases [the complaint](piece_the_complaint.md) and inflicts [the wound](piece_the_wound.md), which, wearing [the alibi](piece_the_alibi.md), is treated again into [the cascade](piece_the_cascade.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the presenting complaint is read and the remedy that eases it applied
- [ ] the cure is given competently and in good faith, and genuinely treats the condition
- [ ] the remedy reaches past the complaint and wounds a sound part it had spared
- [ ] the new wound, wearing a fresh face, is treated again as its own complaint
