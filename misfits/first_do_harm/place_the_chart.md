# Place: The Chart

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). Where the complaint is recorded and the wound is entered as a fresh condition, the record that shows each treatment and never the line from the cure to the harm it made.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the case is written down, a chart, a case file, a record where each complaint is logged and each treatment noted in turn.
The presenting condition entered, the remedy recorded, and then, later and lower, a new complaint logged as its own line, with no arrow drawn back to the cure above it.

## Holds

The record, and the alibi it keeps for the remedy.
Here each complaint and each treatment is entered in sequence, so the Chart holds the point where the cure is exonerated: a wound recorded as a fresh condition in its own right, arriving after the treatment and filed apart from it, so it is where the tie between the remedy and the harm is never drawn, each entry true and the line between them missing, the cure left with a clean record and the wound with a life of its own.

## Offers

A true account of each complaint and each treatment given.
Because the Chart records every condition and every cure faithfully, it offers a complete and honest log, each entry accurate on its own, so it hands out a record no line of which is false, and hands it out entry by separate entry, the wound logged as truly as the complaint and the remedy, and the causal thread between them nowhere written down.

## Withheld

The line from the remedy to the wound it made.
The Chart records each complaint and each cure but cannot record that this wound is that cure's doing, because the wound presents as its own condition and the tie was never seen to be entered, so it withholds the causal thread that would trace the harm to the treatment, letting every entry stand true and separate, and, all faithful record and no drawn connection, it withholds the one line that would put the remedy in the dock, so the cure keeps its clean chart and is given again.
