# Piece: The Cascade

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The chain of interventions, each treating the wound the last one made, the spiral of genuine cures compounding harm because no link is ever traced to the one before.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs through [the theatre](place_the_theatre.md), where each new wound is treated in turn.
Written as separate lines at [the chart](place_the_chart.md), where the chain is never drawn as one.

## Load Bearing

Without it the harm is a single wound, and the trap does not compound.
With it the trap becomes a spiral: the wound, taken for a fresh complaint, is treated by a second remedy that wounds again, which is treated by a third, each intervention genuine and each aimed at the harm the last one made (Rochon and Gurwitz, on the prescribing cascade, a drug's effect mistaken for a new condition and treated with another drug). The Cascade is load bearing because it is how one iatrogenic wound becomes many: a chain of real cures each treating the previous cure's harm, so the piece carries the compounding, the system filling with injuries no single intervention will own because each link looks like a new beginning and not a link.

## Apparent

A run of bad luck, a patient or a system that keeps developing new problems, each treated as it comes.
Seen at the surface, the Cascade looks like a sequence of unrelated troubles diligently addressed, and the truth that each new problem is the last cure's wound, that the chain is one causal thread and not a series of coincidences, that the diligence feeds the very spiral it means to end, stays hidden behind how much a string of treated complaints looks like care keeping pace with misfortune.

## Yearbook

Driven by [the remedy](piece_the_remedy.md) given again through [treating](process_treating.md), each link cleared by [the alibi](piece_the_alibi.md), the spiral of compounding cures.
It is the compounding of the trap: the Cascade is [the wound](piece_the_wound.md) treated into another wound, link after link, none traced to the last, mounting toward [the nemesis](piece_the_nemesis.md) and broken only by the stayed hand at [the watch](place_the_watch.md).
