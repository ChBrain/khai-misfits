# Piece: The Alibi

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The missing trace from the remedy to the harm, the fresh face the wound wears, by which the cure is never blamed and is given again.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Kept at [the chart](place_the_chart.md), where the wound is filed apart from the cure.
Broken at [the watch](place_the_watch.md), where the wound is traced back to the remedy.

## Load Bearing

Without it the wound is seen as the cure's doing, and the remedy is blamed and stayed.
With it the trap endures: the wound arrives after the treatment and presents as its own condition, so no one draws the line from the cure to the harm, and the remedy, never in the dock, is given again (Illich, on the mystification by which medical harm is absorbed as new sickness rather than traced to its source). The Alibi is load bearing because it is why the trap persists: the harm that never wears the face of the hand that made it, so the piece carries the exoneration of the cure, the reason a genuine remedy that wounds is applied and applied again, each wound taken for a fresh complaint and never for the treatment's shadow.

## Apparent

Simple coincidence, an unlucky sequence, a new condition that happens to follow a treatment with no link between them.
Seen at the surface, the Alibi looks like the plain absence of any connection, a wound and a cure that merely occurred in order, and the truth that the order is cause, that the missing trace is what clears the remedy, that a harm hidden inside a good treatment is a harm no one will stop giving, stays hidden behind how naturally a fresh-faced wound reads as unrelated to the cure before it.
