# Position: Afflicted

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The seat that carries the complaint and seeks relief, and bears in the same body both the cure that eases it and the new wound the cure inflicts.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A real complaint and a reasonable wish for relief: the suffering that presents and the trust to accept the cure offered.
A genuine easing: the remedy does relieve the presenting condition, so seeking it is sound.
No way to keep the cure and refuse its reach, and no sight, when the wound comes, of the treatment that made it.

## Orders

Seek relief.
Bring the complaint, accept the remedy offered in good faith, and take the real easing it gives.
Be treated, because the condition is real and the cure works.

## Loses

The sound function the cure reaches past, borne in the same body that was eased.
Because the remedy that relieves the complaint wounds a healthy part the complaint had spared, the seat is left with a new condition it did not have before, and because that wound presents as its own fresh trouble, the seat brings it back to be treated again, the suffering compounding through the very cure that eased the first.
It gains relief from the complaint, and loses the sound part the cure wounded, and carries both in the one body.

## Drives

Toward the complaint eased and the new wound borne,
relief and harm arriving together in the same place,
so the seat that seeks relief is relieved and wounded at once, and brings the wound back as a fresh complaint.
