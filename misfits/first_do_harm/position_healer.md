# Position: Healer

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The seat that applies the genuine remedy to the presenting complaint, where a cure real enough to treat the condition is powerful enough to wound what it had spared.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A real complaint to treat and a remedy that works: the skill to apply the cure and the good faith to mean only help.
A genuine treatment: the remedy does ease the presenting condition, so giving it is right by the thing it treats.
No sight of the wound the cure makes, because the wound arrives after and wears a fresh face, and no power to treat the complaint without the cure that reaches past it.

## Orders

Treat the complaint.
Read the presenting condition, apply the remedy that eases it, and do the competent, demanded, responsible thing.
Cure what asks to be cured, because the complaint is real and the treatment works.

## Loses

The sound function the cure wounds, exactly by treating the complaint.
Because the remedy does what it does and reaches past the presenting condition, the seat eases the complaint and injures a healthy part the condition had left whole, so what the treatment loses is not the complaint but a separate thing, wounded by the very cure that helped, and, the wound wearing a fresh face, treated again as a new complaint by the same good hand.
It gains the complaint eased, and loses the sound part the cure reached past to harm.

## Drives

Toward the complaint treated and the sound part wounded,
the cure doing exactly what it does and reaching past the thing it was for,
so the seat that treats the complaint harms what the complaint had spared, and treats the harm again unknowing.
