# Plot: The Harm

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Theatre](place_the_theatre.md), the cure delivered and its reach past the complaint.
The remedy touching a sound function the condition had left whole, the easing done and the wound beginning.

## Action

[Wounding](process_wounding.md): [the remedy](piece_the_remedy.md), applied by [the healer](persona_the_healer.md), injures a healthy part the complaint never reached.

## Stage

The cure, doing exactly what it does, inflicts [the wound](piece_the_wound.md) on the sound part, so [the afflicted](persona_the_afflicted.md) is left with a new condition it did not have before, a separate harm in a new place.
The complaint is eased and the sound is wounded in the one act, the injury real and distinct and the intent to heal wholly intact.

## Tension

The reach that wounds is the same power that heals, so the harm is the shadow of a real cure and not the failure of a false one.
There is nothing malicious and nothing botched: the remedy did what a working remedy does. But what it did reached a sound part the complaint had spared, so the plot closes with the complaint eased and a new wound made, a separate harm the condition never carried, arriving to wear its own face and carry no mark of the cure.
