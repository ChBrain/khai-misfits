# Pitch: Something Must Be Done

## Taxonomy

[First Do Harm](play_first_do_harm.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Competent, caring, responsible.
The plain good sense of treating a real complaint, a genuine remedy applied in good faith to a condition that presents and asks to be put right.
Nothing false in it: the complaint is real, the remedy works, and the healer means only to help the thing that hurts.

## Undertow

Under the care, the cure reaching past the complaint to wound what the condition had left whole, a new and separate harm the treatment made.
Because the wound presents as its own fresh complaint and never wears the face of the cure that caused it, the remedy is never traced and never blamed, so it is given again, and the interventions chain, each genuine and each treating the last one's harm.
What makes the remedy worth giving, that it truly treats the complaint, is exactly what makes it able to harm what the complaint had spared.

## Nerve

The feeling of doing the responsible thing, treating the complaint that presents,
sure it is only care, because the condition is real and the remedy works and something must be done,
while the cure wounds the sound part the disease had spared, and the wound is taken for a fresh complaint and treated again.

## Echo

After the run: a system filled with harms no single cure will own, each intervention genuine and caring,
no one having meant harm and no remedy having failed, only a true cure reaching past a real complaint and a wound that never wore its face,
and the one move that would keep the first rule, staying the hand and tracing the wound to the remedy, forgone because it means being read as negligent for the harm not caused while the treating healer is thanked for the harm they did.
