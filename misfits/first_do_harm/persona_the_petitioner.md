# Persona: The Petitioner

## Taxonomy

[Petitioner](position_petitioner.md). The demand that something be done, the pressure to intervene that makes forbearance read as negligence and the cure the responsible move.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Urgent. Insistent. Right to want action.
Is whoever demands the complaint be treated, the board, the public, the family, the stakeholders, the voice that says a condition this real cannot be left alone.
No unreason in it: the complaint is genuine and demanding a response to it is proper, so the call to act is fair on its face.

## Action

Presses for the complaint to be treated, and makes acting the expected thing and doing nothing the failure.
Reads the healer who forbears as negligent and the healer who treats as responsible, whatever each choice costs.
Does what the demand to act does: it makes intervention the safe move, so the remedy is given because not giving it looks like a dereliction.

## Shadow

Not wrong to want the complaint addressed, a fair demand that a real condition be met, and that is the whole difficulty: the demand to act is reasonable, and a reasonable demand to act is exactly what makes staying the hand impossible.
Because a harm allowed is visible and a harm caused by a well-meant cure is hidden, the Petitioner reads the forbearing healer as having failed and the treating healer as having done their duty, so the pressure falls always toward intervention, and the cure that wounds is demanded while the restraint that would spare is condemned, the call to act sound in itself and lethal to the one move that would break the cascade.
It rightly demands the complaint be met, and demanding it is what forces the cure that harms.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "we cannot just do nothing."
Says it truly, doing nothing about a real complaint is often wrong, and does not weigh that the something demanded is a cure whose hidden harm may cost more than the complaint it treats.
