# Position: The Diagnosis

## Taxonomy

[It Saved My Life](play_it_saved_my_life.md). The patient's seat, which holds the label, the treatment, and the survival that follows, able to record that a disease was found and dealt with and unable to record whether that disease was ever going to kill.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The label, the treatment received, and the life that continues after.
The seat holds the fact of a disease found and acted on, the record of care given, and the survival that follows, everything a single case can register about what happened.
The whole of the encounter and none of its counterfactual, since the seat can hold what was found and treated and what came after, and cannot hold the one thing that would give it meaning: what would have happened had nothing been found at all.

## Orders

Record the finding, accept the treatment, and read the survival as its result.
Take the disease as caught, the care as the reason it did no harm, and the continuing life as the rescue completed, assembling from a real finding and a real treatment and a real survival the natural story that ties them into a saving.
Read the sequence the only way one case allows, since a diagnosis followed by treatment followed by survival reads as cause and cure and outcome, the seat drawing the line through the three points it holds because it holds no fourth point to bend it.

## Loses

The counterfactual, which the seat has no instrument to reach.
The Diagnosis can hold that a disease was found and treated and that the person lived, and cannot hold whether the person would have lived anyway, because nothing available to a single case distinguishes the tumour that would have killed from the one that would have sat still. What the seat forfeits is the only comparison that would give the survival meaning: the same person, unscreened, whose course it can never observe, so every fact the seat holds is consistent with a rescue and equally consistent with an overdiagnosis, and no operation on the record separates them. The label attaches identically to the lethal and the indolent, the treatment is given identically to both, and the survival follows identically from a cure and from a disease that was never a threat, so the seat computes a saving from data that a relabelling would have produced unchanged, sealed inside a single life whose alternative it cannot run.

## Drives

Toward a survival read as a rescue, and a gratitude with no way to test itself,
and a record complete in everything but the one comparison that would tell what the finding was worth,
so the seat that holds the strongest evidence anyone brings, a living body, holds no evidence at all of the thing it is taken to prove, its every fact a rescue and a relabelling both, indistinguishable to any instrument the single case can hold.
