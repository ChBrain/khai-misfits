# Plot: The Catch

## Taxonomy

[It Saved My Life](play_it_saved_my_life.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Surgery](place_the_surgery.md), a screening round and a body that feels nothing.
A well person come to be checked, and a test that finds disease before it would ever have spoken, some of it lethal and some of it the harmless kind.

## Action

[Detecting](process_detecting.md): the Physician finds a disease early and treats it, with no way to tell at the moment of the catch whether it would ever have caused harm.

## Stage

[The Physician](persona_the_physician.md) acts from [the Programme](position_the_programme.md) on [Screen More](plan_screen_more.md), finding [the harmless kind](piece_the_harmless_kind.md) alongside the lethal and treating both the same, a real catch that cannot say which sort it caught.
The finding is genuine and the care is good, and one of the two kinds needed neither.

## Tension

The doctor does right by the patient and cannot know whether the finding helped or harmed.
There is no error: the disease is real, the treatment sound, the vigilance exactly what medicine asks. But the harmless kind looks identical to the lethal at the moment it is found, so the plot closes with a person diagnosed and treated who may have been in no danger at all, a rescue and an overdiagnosis recorded in the same hand, the harm invisible in the one encounter where it was done and indistinguishable from the good beside it.
