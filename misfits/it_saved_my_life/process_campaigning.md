# Process: Campaigning

## Taxonomy

[It Saved My Life](play_it_saved_my_life.md). The turning of gratitude into demand, the grateful survivor's advocacy that draws more of the well to be screened, the loop's public turn that makes the next round of the harmed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Patient](persona_the_patient.md), at [the Rally](place_the_rally.md), telling the story and pressing the test on others.
Begins whenever a grateful survivor carries their rescue outward, campaigning for the screening they credit with their life.

## Direction

Turns a survivor's gratitude into the programme's growth.
Because a moving testimony draws more of the well in to be screened, the survivor's advocacy expands the reach, so campaigning converts private gratitude into public demand, and the enlarged screening finds more disease of both kinds, minting more survivors to campaign in turn, the harm feeding the demand that makes more harm (Raffle and Gray).

## Lever

The testimony nothing outweighs.
Because a living, grateful survivor is the most persuasive evidence anyone can bring, campaigning works through the unmatched weight of the story: a face and a name and a life move the well as no statistic can, so the lever is the sincere testimony itself, which grows the demand precisely because it cannot be argued with, the overdiagnosed pressing the test in perfect good faith.

## Echo

A programme enlarged by the gratitude of the people it overdiagnosed.
The echo of campaigning is a demand that swells with each round of survivors, so the more harmless disease the programme finds the more advocates it fields and the more it grows, the loop turning on its own output, the harmed become the constituency that expands the thing that harmed them.
