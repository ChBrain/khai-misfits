# Place: The Rally

## Taxonomy

[It Saved My Life](play_it_saved_my_life.md). The public floor where the grateful gather, the awareness march and the fundraiser and the testimonial, where survival becomes advocacy and the demand that grows the programme is raised.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Survivors, stories, and a cause, all of it moving.
Shown at the surface is the best of a community: people who were frightened and are now well, telling what they went through, raising money, urging others to be checked, a room full of gratitude turned into a movement for a good and simple message, get screened.

## Holds

Holds the testimony and its multiplication, the loop's public turn.
The Rally is where the survivor's story is told and heard and repeated, so it holds the conversion of private gratitude into public demand: each testimonial draws more of the well to be screened, and each new round of screening sends its own survivors here to testify, the place where the harmed become the advocates and the advocates make more of the harmed.

## Offers

Offers a true feeling in service of a demand that harms.
Here the gratitude is real and the stories are honest and the wish to spare others is genuine, so the Rally offers everything sincere, and offers it as fuel for the demand that expands the overdiagnosis, the moving testimony of the overdiagnosed pressing the test on the well, a good feeling carrying a harm it cannot see and would never intend.

## Withheld

Withholds the overdiagnosed's own status from themselves and the room.
What the Rally hides is which of its survivors were saved and which were relabelled, because none of them can know and the room cannot tell, so the place that runs on testimony withholds the one fact that would qualify it: whether the life being credited to the test was ever the test's to save. The gratitude is offered whole and the harm inside it is kept invisible, the survivor and the audience alike sure of a rescue the Rally has no way to confirm or deny.
