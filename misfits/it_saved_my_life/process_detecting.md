# Process: Detecting

## Taxonomy

[It Saved My Life](play_it_saved_my_life.md). The finding of disease before the body would report it, the screening catch that pulls in the lethal early and the harmless alike, unable to sort the two at the moment it finds them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Physician](persona_the_physician.md), at [the Surgery](place_the_surgery.md), screening the well and finding disease that has not yet spoken.
Begins whenever a test looks for disease ahead of symptoms, and finds it in a body that felt nothing.

## Direction

Turns a population of the well into a supply of the diagnosed.
Because screening finds disease before it would ever surface, it catches the lethal in time and the indolent that would never have surfaced together, so detecting converts silent bodies into diagnoses of both kinds, the harmless entering the count exactly as the dangerous does, a real finding that cannot label which sort it has caught (Welch and Black).

## Lever

The catch that looks the same whichever kind it is.
Because the harmless kind is disease to the pathologist and no threat by its nature, detecting works through a finding that is genuine and undifferentiated: the test sees the tumour and not its future, so the lever is the honest detection itself, which produces a diagnosis identical for the disease that would have killed and the disease that would have sat still, the sorting impossible at the moment of the catch.

## Echo

A stream of diagnoses in which the harmful and the harmless are mixed and unmarked.
The echo of detecting is a growing count of found disease, some of it caught in time to save a life and some of it found in people who would have lived out their days never knowing, the two indistinguishable in the record, so everything downstream, the survival and the gratitude, is built on a catch that could not say which kind it was.
