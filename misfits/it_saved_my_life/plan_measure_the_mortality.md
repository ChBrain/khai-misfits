# Plan: Measure the Mortality

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Endpoint](position_the_endpoint.md), inside [It Saved My Life](play_it_saved_my_life.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Endpoint](position_the_endpoint.md)

## Direction

Judge the programme by disease-specific mortality in a randomised comparison, not by survival or by gratitude: split a population by chance into screened and unscreened, follow both for years, and read whether fewer died among the screened (Bailar). Discount the survival rate as inflated by early detection and harmless detection alike, and the testimony as unable to tell a saving from a relabelling, since both count from the moment of diagnosis and neither observes the death that did or did not come. Wait out the endpoint however long it takes, report the mortality whatever it says, and accept that the true finding arrives late and often null, less persuasive than a single grateful survivor and correct where the survivor cannot be.

## Orders

([The Trialist](persona_the_trialist.md) randomises, counts to death rather than to diagnosis, and carries the cold mortality against a room of testimony, right about the harm and cast as the enemy of the people it would spare.)

## Implementation

([The Long Run](place_the_long_run.md); through the [randomising](process_randomising.md) that builds the comparison the clinic cannot, against the [crediting](process_crediting.md) it corrects; the Trialist reads the mortality where the programme read [the survival rate](piece_the_survival_rate.md), and quantifies [the harmless kind](piece_the_harmless_kind.md) by the incidence the screened accrue and the unscreened do not.)

## Targets

- [ ] a population is randomised to screening or not and followed for years
- [ ] the endpoint is disease-specific mortality, not survival from diagnosis
- [ ] the survival rate and the testimony are discounted as unable to separate the two kinds
- [ ] the mortality is reported whatever it says, the null carried against the testimony
