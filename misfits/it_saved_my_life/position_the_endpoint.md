# Position: The Endpoint

## Taxonomy

[It Saved My Life](play_it_saved_my_life.md). The trialist's seat, which holds the one measure that separates a saving from a relabelling, disease-specific mortality in a randomised comparison, able to reach the truth of the programme and unable to make it weigh as much as a story.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The randomised comparison, the count to death, and the true measure of the programme.
The seat holds the only instrument that tells rescue from overdiagnosis: a population split by chance into screened and unscreened, followed for years, and read for whether fewer died among the screened, not whether more were diagnosed.
The right answer and no way to make it land, since the seat commands the measure that settles the question and commands none of the vividness that decides it, holding a truth that arrives as a rate where the programme offers a face.

## Orders

Randomise, count to death, and report the mortality whatever it says.
Compare the screened against the unscreened, wait out the years the endpoint takes, and read the disease-specific deaths and the total incidence, discounting the survival rate as inflated and the testimony as unable to tell the two kinds apart.
Report the null when the null is true, since the seat's whole worth is a measure that does not flatter, so it must carry a mortality unmoved against a programme that offers a survival transformed, and say the unwelcome thing the count found.

## Loses

The audience, which the seat's own honesty costs it.
The Endpoint holds the measure that is right and forfeits the power to make it heard, because the true finding is a delayed population statistic and its rival is a living, grateful survivor, and a rate that arrives years late persuades fewer than a face that speaks today. What the seat loses is not the argument but the weighing: a null mortality against a room of testimony is the correct answer against the compelling one, and the correctness does not close the gap, since the harm the seat reports has no victim who can feel it while the rescue the programme claims has a person who can testify to it. To act on the seat's measure is to curtail a beloved test over the objection of the constituency it created, so the seat that holds the truth holds it against the grain of every incentive to be believed, its finding structurally quieter than the stories it corrects and its remedy an argument it must win against the very people it is trying to spare.

## Drives

Toward a measure that is right and outweighed,
and a null that arrives too late and too cold to beat a single grateful voice,
so the seat that alone can tell a saving from a relabelling is the seat least able to be believed, its truth real and quiet and unwelcome, correct about the harm and structurally too faint to carry against the gratitude the harm produced.
