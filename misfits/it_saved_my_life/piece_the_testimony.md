# Piece: The Testimony

## Taxonomy

[It Saved My Life](play_it_saved_my_life.md). The grateful survivor's story, a living body offered as proof the test works, the most persuasive thing in any room and the one piece of evidence that cannot tell a rescue from a relabelling.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Told at [the Rally](place_the_rally.md), carried out to the well as the case for screening.
Flows as a first-person account of a frightening finding and a life saved, a face and a name and a survival, the testimony that moves an audience and grows the demand.

## Load Bearing

Without the Testimony the gratitude stays private and the loop does not turn: it is the survivor's story, offered as proof, that converts a felt rescue into public demand and grows the programme.
The Testimony is load bearing because it is the evidence that beats all others and settles nothing, a living body standing for a rescue it cannot demonstrate (Raffle and Gray). It is unfalsifiable by design: the survivor is genuinely alive and genuinely grateful, so the story is true in every particular and silent on the only question that matters, whether the test did the saving, and because it moves rooms as no statistic can, it drives the demand that expands the screening. The loop's whole momentum passes through this piece: gratitude becomes a story, the story becomes demand, the demand becomes more overdiagnosis and more survivors with more stories to tell.

## Apparent

Just a survivor telling the truth about being saved.
Seen at the surface, the Testimony looks like the strongest evidence there is, a real person alive because of the test, and the truth that the living body proves survival and not rescue, that a relabelled patient would give the identical account, that the most moving evidence in the room is exactly the evidence that cannot separate the two kinds, stays hidden behind how sincere and how alive the witness is.

## Yearbook

Given by [the Patient](persona_the_patient.md) in perfect good faith, welcomed by [the Physician](persona_the_physician.md) as confirmation, and set aside by [the Trialist](persona_the_trialist.md) who cannot read a rescue in it.
It is the loop's carrier: the Testimony is what [crediting](process_crediting.md) produces and [campaigning](process_campaigning.md) broadcasts, the story that grows [the constituency](piece_the_constituency.md) and the demand, offered against [the survival rate](piece_the_survival_rate.md) as its human face and against [the harmless kind](piece_the_harmless_kind.md) as its unknowing witness.
