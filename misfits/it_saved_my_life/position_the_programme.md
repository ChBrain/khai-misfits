# Position: The Programme

## Taxonomy

[It Saved My Life](play_it_saved_my_life.md). The screening seat, which offers the test, counts the cases found and the survivors made, and grows on the demand it draws, able to read every signal it collects as success and unable to see its own overdiagnosis in any of them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The test, the reach, the cases found, and the survival statistics.
The seat holds the screening apparatus and everything it generates: the rising count of disease detected, the survivors treated and living, the five-year survival that climbs, and the grateful demand that expands the reach.
Every internal instrument and not one that reads harm, since the seat can measure detection and survival and demand and finds all three rising, and holds no gauge that would show the overdiagnosis those same numbers are also made of.

## Orders

Screen more, count the finding and the survival, and grow on the demand.
Extend the test to more of the well, record each disease caught as a case the programme addressed and each survivor as a life it touched, and read the climbing survival rate and the swelling demand as the programme working, expanding on the strength of its own success signals.
Trust the numbers that all point one way, since detection rises and survival rises and demand rises together, and the seat's every measure agrees that the programme is succeeding, so growing the screening is the plain reading of instruments that show no cost.

## Loses

The sight of its own overdiagnosis, which no instrument the seat holds can register.
The Programme can count cases found, survivors made, and demand drawn, and every one of these rises with overdiagnosis exactly as it rises with genuine early rescue, so the seat's whole dashboard climbs when the programme overdiagnoses and reads that climb as success. What the seat forfeits is any internal signal of harm: finding harmless disease adds cases to the detection count, adds survivors to the survival rate, and adds advocates to the demand, so the overdiagnosis enters the programme's numbers only as more success, and the rising incidence that is the surest fingerprint of overdiagnosis reads, to the seat, as better detection. No operation on the programme's own data reveals the harm, because the harm is made of the same events the seat counts as wins, and the one comparison that would expose it, the mortality of a population left unscreened, is exactly the number a screening programme does not collect on itself.

## Drives

Toward a programme whose every gauge rises as it overdiagnoses,
and a success confirmed by detection, survival, and demand all climbing together,
so the seat most faithful to its own instruments is the seat most blind to its own harm, its dashboard reading pure success precisely when the overdiagnosis is greatest, the fingerprint of the harm indistinguishable, in its own numbers, from the proof of its worth.
