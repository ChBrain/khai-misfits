# Plot: The Campaign

## Taxonomy

[It Saved My Life](play_it_saved_my_life.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Rally](place_the_rally.md), a grateful survivor with a story to tell.
A person who believes the test saved them, moved to spare others the fear they felt, ready to campaign and raise money and press the test on the well.

## Action

[Campaigning](process_campaigning.md): the Patient tells the story, and the programme expands on the strength of it, screening more and finding more of the harmless kind.

## Stage

[The Patient](persona_the_patient.md) acts from [the Diagnosis](position_the_diagnosis.md) on [Tell My Story](plan_tell_my_story.md), carrying [the testimony](piece_the_testimony.md) that swells [the constituency](piece_the_constituency.md) and the demand, drawing more of the well toward the catch.
The advocacy is generous and the wish to help is real, and it grows the overdiagnosis it cannot see.

## Tension

The survivor spares others out of real feeling and enlarges the harm by doing it.
There is no malice: the campaigner is grateful, generous, and sincere, wanting only to save others the fright they had. But the demand they raise draws more of the well to be screened, and more screening finds more of the harmless kind, so the plot closes with the loop turning on its own output, each round of grateful survivors recruiting the next round of the overdiagnosed, the harmed grown into the constituency that expands the thing that harmed them, and the kindness of it the very engine of its growth.
