# Piece: The Matthew

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The phenomenon whole: to those who have, more is given, and the blinding that could hold it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the byline](place_the_byline.md), where the credit for a field's work can be seen concentrating on the names already known.
Not a single unfair award but the pattern of all of them: the documented fact that recognition, resources, and attention accrue to those who already have them, so a small advantage compounds into a large one and the gap comes to look like merit.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a run of lucky breaks and one sour underdog.
With it there is a named law it stands on: cumulative advantage, the Matthew effect, to those who have more is given and from those who have not is taken even what they have, because having is read as deserving and converts into the resource for the next having. The Matthew is load bearing because it holds both the mechanism and its remedy, that the gap is manufactured by visibility and undone only by judging blind.

## Apparent

Merit, plainly sorted.
It looks like a world where the best rose and the rest did not, a distribution that talent alone would produce, so the effect hides as the very meritocracy it undermines. That is the deepest disguise, because a manufactured hierarchy and an earned one are indistinguishable from the outside, and only a blind test can tell them apart.

## Yearbook

Assembled from [the lead](piece_the_lead.md) through [the gap](piece_the_gap.md), and answered by [the reset](piece_the_reset.md).
It is the law the play runs on: the Matthew is why [the flow](piece_the_flow.md) tracks the lead and not the work, and why the only counter is to hide the name and let the work be weighed alone.
