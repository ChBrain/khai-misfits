# Place: The Feed

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). Where attention aggregates, and the already-attended draws more.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Rankings, counts, charts, the list of what is trending.
The citation index, the bestseller list, the follower tally: the place a field's attention is pooled and displayed, sorted by how much attention there already is.

## Holds

The flow, and the preferential attachment that drives it.
The place visibility begets visibility, where the largest number draws the next glance because it is the largest.

## Offers

A guide to what matters.
Here the busy can find what others found worth their time, a filter that saves attention by pooling it.

## Withheld

The good work no one is pointing at yet.
The feed shows what is already attended and buries what is not, so it offers the leader an audience that grows because it is large, and the newcomer a silence that keeps them small.
