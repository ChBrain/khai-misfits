# Position: Co-Author

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The seat that shares the work, and whose credit flows to the known name.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A real share of the joint work, and a place beside an eminent name.
A contribution made, and a recognition assigned elsewhere.

## Orders

Lend the credit: do your full share of the work that goes out under both names.
Assume recognition follows contribution, and let the association stand.
Contribute, and watch the honour attach to the famous one.

## Loses

The credit, upward, to the halo.
A seat whose contribution is real but whose name is the lesser-known one cannot hold the recognition, because the field routes credit to the name it already knows.

## Drives

Toward shared work credited unequally, and away from the recognition contribution should have carried.
Toward feeding the lead with credit that was partly its own.
