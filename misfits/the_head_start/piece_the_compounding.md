# Piece: The Compounding

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). Success breeding success: the loop that turns a lead into a larger lead.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the feed](place_the_feed.md), turning: advantage becoming resource becoming advantage, cycle after cycle.
The raise that funds the hire that wins the deal that earns the raise, the citation that draws the citation, the win that buys the platform that produces the next win: the engine that runs the lead forward.

## Load Bearing

Without it a lead would be a one-time gift and the field could still be overtaken on merit.
With it the advantage reinvests: each turn converts the flow into a fresh lead, so the distance grows geometrically from a difference that may have been nothing, and grows on visibility rather than on any new gap in ability. The Compounding is load bearing because it is the multiplier, the reason a hair's head start becomes a chasm and merit stops being what decides.

## Apparent

Momentum. Earned dominance.
It looks like a performer pulling away on sheer quality, each success clearly built on the last, the very picture of talent realized. That reading is the trap, because the successes are built on the resources the prior success attracted, not on a widening edge in skill, and momentum is exactly what a lead looks like when it is feeding itself.

Turned by [the flow](piece_the_flow.md) through [compounding](process_compounding.md), around [the star](persona_the_star.md).
It is the engine of the play: the Compounding converts each advantage into the next, widening [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) and making [the matthew](piece_the_matthew.md) self-sustaining.
