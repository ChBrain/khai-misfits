# Persona: The Leveler

## Taxonomy

[Reformer](position_reformer.md). The reversal: judges the work without knowing whose it is.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Principled, systematic, willing to give up a signal.
Sees that the reward is tracking the lead, not the work, and reaches for the one cut that stops it.
Believes the only fair test hides the name.

## Action

Blinds the allocation: screens the audition, anonymises the submission, decouples the reward from the standing.
Strips the halo so the flow cannot attach to visibility.
Lets the overlooked be measured against the star on the work alone.

## Shadow

The remedy discards the real predictive value of a record to kill the false one.
The Leveler knows that reputation carries genuine signal, and blinds anyway, because the only way to break the compounding is to refuse the information that feeds it, and that refusal is a real and resented cost.

## Tell

"Take the names off. Judge the work."
The one move that lets merit decide again, and the one a field that loves its winners resists.
