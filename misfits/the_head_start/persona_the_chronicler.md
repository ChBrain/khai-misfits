# Persona: The Chronicler

## Taxonomy

[Historian](position_historian.md). The one who writes the field's history, and writes it around the eminent.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fair-minded, thorough, telling it as it looks.
Surveys the field after the fact and records who rose and who did not.
Reads the distribution as the record of merit, because that is what a distribution looks like.

## Action

Writes the history with the star at the center and the overlooked in the footnotes.
Attaches the discoveries, the movements, the era to the names that ended up largest.
Naturalizes the gap: the greats were great, the rest were the rest.

## Shadow

The history reads the outcome backward into the merit and erases the head start.
The Chronicler is honest and is recording a real distribution, but by reading the gap as talent all along, the account launders the compounding into destiny and writes the equally able out of the story.

## Tell

"History remembers the best."
It remembers the largest, and calls it the best.
