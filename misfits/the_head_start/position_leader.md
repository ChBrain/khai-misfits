# Position: Leader

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The seat out in front, where the lead attracts what makes the next lead.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The early advantage, and the flow it draws.
The standing that reads as merit, and converts into resource.

## Orders

Ride the lead: take the funding, the attention, the credit it attracts.
Convert each into the next win, and read the trajectory as earned.
Stay ahead, which the flow makes easy.

## Loses

The view of the head start, from inside the lead.
A seat that keeps winning cannot tell the compounding from its own skill, so it cannot see that the gap is built, not widened by ability.

## Drives

Toward a self-sustaining advantage, and away from the contest that would test it.
Toward a distance read as talent, grown on resources the standing supplied.
