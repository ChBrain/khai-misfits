# Piece: The Gap

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The widening inequality: the distance grown far past the difference in ability.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the long tail](place_the_long_tail.md), the space that opens between the one who pulled ahead and the many who did not.
The chart with a spike and a slope, the winner-take-most distribution, the roster where a handful hold nearly everything and the rest share what is left: the shape a field takes once the compounding has run.

## Load Bearing

Without it the effect is invisible and the head start looks harmless.
With it there is a measured inequality far larger than any measured gap in talent, the signature of cumulative advantage: a distribution that a difference in merit could never produce alone, opened by a loop that fed the lead. The Gap is load bearing because it is the harm made visible, the distance that proves the distance was built.

## Apparent

The natural spread of talent.
It looks like what you would expect if ability itself were unevenly distributed: a few greats, a long tail of the ordinary, exactly as merit would sort them. That reading is the last defence of the effect, because a manufactured gap and a natural one look identical once drawn, and calling it talent hides that it was accumulated.

## Yearbook

Opened by [the compounding](piece_the_compounding.md), and inhabited by [the overlooked](piece_the_overlooked.md).
It is the shape the play leaves: the Gap is what [the compounding](piece_the_compounding.md) produces and what [the chronicler](persona_the_chronicler.md) reads as merit, unless [the reset](piece_the_reset.md) is allowed to test it.
