# Persona: The Collaborator

## Taxonomy

[Co-Author](position_co_author.md). The one who shares the work, and whose credit flows to the known name.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent, junior, glad of the association.
Does a real share of the joint work, beside a partner who is already eminent.
Assumes credit will follow contribution, as it should.

## Action

Contributes fully to work that goes out under both names.
Watches the recognition attach to the famous one, the paper called the star's, the project remembered as theirs.
Lends, without meaning to, the credit that feeds the lead.

## Shadow

Shared work is not shared credit when one name already has the halo.
The Collaborator's contribution is real and their share of the recognition is not, because the field routes credit to the name it already knows, which is exactly Merton's finding, and the loss is invisible next to the honour of the association.

## Tell

"We did it together." ... and the world says the star's name.
Equal work, credited unequally, upward.
