# Place: The Long Tail

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). Where the overlooked sit, and the gap is measured.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The slope after the spike, the many with a little each, the roster below the stars.
The unfunded proposals, the uncited papers, the bands that never broke: the long low stretch of comparable performers who did not pull ahead.

## Holds

the gap, and the equally able it holds.
The place the inequality is visible as a shape, a distribution far more unequal than any spread in talent could explain.

## Offers

The proof the gap was built.
Here the control group lives: performers as able as the stars receiving a fraction of the reward, the evidence that the reward tracked the lead, not the merit.

## Withheld

A way up that runs on merit.
The long tail shows the able getting less and offers them no path a self-feeding lead does not outrun, so it holds the proof of the injustice and none of the remedy.
