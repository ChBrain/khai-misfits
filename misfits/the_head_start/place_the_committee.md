# Place: The Committee

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). Where resources are allocated, and allocated to the proven.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Applications ranked, a track record open on the table, a vote for the safe name.
The grant panel, the casting call, the hiring loop: the place a resource is directed by weighing who has done well before.

## Holds

The halo, turned into supply.
The place the standing is read as a signal and converted into funding, the safe and defensible bet on the one already ahead.

## Offers

A responsible decision.
Here a resource can be placed where the evidence points, backing the proven, in a choice no auditor will fault.

## Withheld

The equally able without a record.
The committee can weigh a track record and cannot see the unproven who would match it, so it offers the leader more and the equal nothing, one defensible allocation at a time.
