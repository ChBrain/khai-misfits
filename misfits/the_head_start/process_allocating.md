# Process: Allocating

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). Directing resources to the one already ahead.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A resource to place and a track record to weigh.
Once there is a leader, the safe way to spend is to back them.

## Direction

From a pool of resource toward the proven.
It moves funding, slots, and opportunity to the standing that reads as a signal.

## Lever

A defensible bet meeting a visible lead.
The force is prudent judgment weighting a record; the resistance is nothing, since backing the leader is the choice no one is faulted for.

## Echo

The halo turned into supply.
It hands forward resource attached to the lead rather than the work, the fuel the next advantage is bought with.
