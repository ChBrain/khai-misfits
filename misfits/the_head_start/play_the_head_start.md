# Play: The Head Start

## Estate

[khai-misfits](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-misfits): the house that holds this production and answers for the whole run.

## Name

The Head Start.
Two comparable performers, and one gets a small early lead, by a hair or by luck ... and the lead draws the credit, the resources, and the attention that produce the next lead, so the gap widens on its own until the one who started a step ahead is a world ahead, and everyone calls the distance talent.

## Arc

The bend is this: give one of two equals a head start, and the head start will compound into dominance.
It runs on a split between the merit that earned the first lead and the visibility that earns the rest.
An early advantage is conferred, sometimes on a razor's margin, sometimes on luck. It reads as a signal of quality, so the patron backs the one already ahead, the crowd attends to the one already attended, and the credit for shared work flows to the name already known. Each of those is resource, and resource makes the next advantage, so the lead feeds on its own visibility rather than on any widening gap in ability.
The equally able get less, and fall behind, and their falling is booked as proof they were lesser. Decouple the reward from the standing, judge the work blind, and the compounding stops: to those who have, more is given, unless someone decides to stop giving it for having.

## Triggers

**[The Lead](plot_the_lead.md)**
A small early advantage is conferred on one of two comparable performers, on a thin margin or on luck. The Star takes the first prize. Closes when one is marked ahead and the other not, by a difference smaller than it will come to look.

**[The Flow](plot_the_flow.md)**
Cue: the lead reads as a signal of quality. The Patron backs the one already ahead and the Crowd attends to the one already attended. Closes when resources, attention, and credit are flowing to the leader because the leader is visible, not because the gap in ability has grown.

**[The Compounding](plot_the_compounding.md)**
Cue: the flow becomes the next advantage. The loop turns, and the Collaborator's shared credit accrues to the known name. Closes when the lead is self-sustaining and merit has stopped deciding the distance.

**[The Gap](plot_the_gap.md)**
Cue: the equally able fall away, and their falling is read as proof they were lesser. The Chronicler writes the history as if the gap were talent all along. Closes when the inequality is naturalized as merit ... or named as accumulated advantage.

**[The Reset](plot_the_reset.md)**
Cue: someone judges the work without knowing whose it is. The Leveler blinds the field and decouples the reward from the standing. Closes when allocation stops following the lead ... or blinding is judged to throw away real signal, and the field backs the proven again.

## Stakes

Whether a field ever rewards the work, or only ever the lead.
Every plot but the last moves the same way: toward the one already ahead, whom it is safe and sensible to back, and away from the equally able, whose only fault was starting a step behind. The production cannot make a crowd attend to the unattended or a patron back the unproven, because following the leader is the rational bet every time. It can only show that no one played favourites and no one was cheated, that the leader really was, at first, a hair better or merely luckier, and that the single thing that would have kept merit deciding, judging the work without knowing whose it is, is the blindness a field that can see its winners will not choose.
