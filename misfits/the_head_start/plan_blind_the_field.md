# Plan: Blind the Field

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), inside [The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Reformer](position_reformer.md)

## Direction

Cut the loop at its hinge: judge the work without knowing whose it is. Screen the audition, anonymise the submission, decouple the reward from the standing, and let the work be weighed alone.

## Orders

([The Leveler](persona_the_leveler.md) strips the halo, hides the name, and lets the overlooked be measured against the star on the work alone.)

## Implementation

([The Blind Panel](place_the_blind_panel.md); the [blinding](process_blinding.md) process; a refusal of the information that feeds the lead, at the cost of the signal a record honestly carried.)

## Targets

- [ ] the name and standing are hidden from the judgment
- [ ] the work is weighed on its own
- [ ] reward is decoupled from the lead
- [ ] the real signal a track record carried is given up to break the false one
