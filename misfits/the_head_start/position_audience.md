# Position: Audience

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The seat of the many, whose attention flows to what already has it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Limited attention, and popularity as a filter for spending it.
A glance to give, and a pile it will most likely add to.

## Orders

Follow the crowd: attend to the already-attended, use the following as a guide.
Read popularity as a sign of worth, and spend your attention where others spent theirs.
Filter by what is already large.

## Loses

The unattended, and the sight of its own part in the pile.
A seat that uses popularity as a proxy cannot see that when all do, attention attaches to attention, and the filter makes the thing it was meant to detect.

## Drives

Toward visibility feeding visibility, and away from the good work no one is pointing at yet.
Toward a leader whose audience grows because it is already the largest.
