# Pitch: Nothing Succeeds Like Success

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Admiring, meritocratic, sure the winners earned it.
Look how far ahead they are: back the proven, follow the crowd, credit the great, because the best rise and the cream shows.
The voice of a field that trusts its rankings and reads its distribution as the record of merit.

## Undertow

Under the admiration, a lead feeding on its own visibility.
The first advantage was a hair or a fluke, and everything after was the loop: resources to the funded, attention to the attended, credit to the known, each turned into the next lead regardless of any widening gap in ability.
No one played favourites and no one was cheated: the leader really was, at first, a shade better or merely luckier, and the equally able fell away because the reward tracked the lead, not the work.

## Nerve

The feeling of a field certain its stars are its best,
a long tail of the equally able getting a fraction of the notice,
and a gap read as talent by everyone, including the ones it left behind.

## Echo

After the run: a distribution far more unequal than any spread in talent,
a history that remembers the largest and calls them the best,
and the one test that would have told a built gap from an earned one, judging the work with the name taken off, left unrun, because a field that can see its winners will not choose to stop looking.
