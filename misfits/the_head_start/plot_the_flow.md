# Plot: The Flow

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The committee](place_the_committee.md), and [the feed](place_the_feed.md).
The lead now reads as quality, so the resources and the attention begin to move toward the one already ahead.

## Action

[Allocating](process_allocating.md): [The Patron](persona_the_patron.md), holding [the allocator's seat](position_allocator.md), backs the proven.
Then [attending](process_attending.md): [The Crowd](persona_the_crowd.md) at [the audience's seat](position_audience.md) follows the followed.

## Stage

[The Patron](persona_the_patron.md) works [Back the Proven](plan_back_the_proven.md) and [The Crowd](persona_the_crowd.md) works [Follow the Crowd](plan_follow_the_crowd.md): [the flow](piece_the_flow.md) moves to the leader on the strength of [the halo](piece_the_halo.md), resource and attention attaching to visibility.
The funding follows the funded and the audience follows the audience.

## Tension

Every choice is sensible, and the sum rigs the field.
Backing a track record is prudent, and using popularity to filter attention is efficient, so no one does anything wrong; but each safe bet and each rational glance attaches to the lead, and the leader's supply grows because the lead is visible, not because the gap in ability has widened. The head start has started to feed.
