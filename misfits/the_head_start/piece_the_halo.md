# Piece: The Halo

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The lead read as quality: standing taken as a proxy for merit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the committee](place_the_committee.md), where a name already ahead is trusted before the work is weighed.
The reputation that precedes the submission, the pedigree on the application, the prior win that colours the next judgment: the glow a lead casts over everything the leader does next.

## Load Bearing

Without it a lead would be a fact about the past, not a claim on the future.
With it the past advantage becomes a present signal: because status reads as merit, the leader is judged as likely-better before the new work is even seen, so the halo converts a settled lead into fresh preference. The Halo is load bearing because it is the mechanism that lets a lead earn more than itself, turning what you have into evidence of what you deserve.

## Apparent

Reasonable prior. Track record.
It looks like sound judgment: past performance predicts future performance, so weighting the proven is efficient, not biased. And there is real signal in a track record, which is the trap, because the same weighting that rewards genuine skill also rewards a lead that was only ever a hair or a fluke.

## Yearbook

Cast over [the star](persona_the_star.md) and trusted by [the patron](persona_the_patron.md) through [allocating](process_allocating.md).
It is the reader of the lead: the Halo turns [the lead](piece_the_lead.md) into a claim on [the flow](piece_the_flow.md), the reason resources move to the one already ahead.
