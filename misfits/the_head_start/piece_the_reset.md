# Piece: The Reset

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The reversal: blind allocation that decouples reward from standing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the blind panel](place_the_blind_panel.md), where the work is judged without knowing whose it is.
The screen across the audition, the anonymised submission, the need-based allocation, the reset seeding: a deliberate refusal to let the name or the lead into the judgment, so only the work is weighed.

## Load Bearing

Without it the reward always follows the lead and the compounding never breaks.
With it the loop is cut at its hinge: strip the halo, hide the standing, and the flow can no longer attach to visibility, so an advantage stops converting into the next one and merit gets to decide again. The Reset is load bearing because it is the one move that severs reward from status, the only thing that lets the overlooked be measured against the star on the work alone.

## Apparent

Throwing away good information. Ignoring track record.
It looks wasteful and even unfair: discarding the real signal a proven record carries, treating a demonstrated great as an unknown, spending judgment to re-litigate settled standings. That reading is why it is resisted, because blinding gives up the genuine predictive value of reputation to break the false part, and a field that can see its winners will not want to stop looking.

## Yearbook

Instituted by [the leveler](persona_the_leveler.md) through [blinding](process_blinding.md), against [the halo](piece_the_halo.md).
It is the remedy the play holds: the Reset cuts the loop of [the compounding](piece_the_compounding.md), lets [the overlooked](piece_the_overlooked.md) compete, and breaks [the matthew](piece_the_matthew.md) ... at the cost of the real signal a lead once carried.
