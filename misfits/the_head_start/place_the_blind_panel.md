# Place: The Blind Panel

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). Where the work is judged without knowing whose it is: the reversal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A screen across the stage, a submission with the name removed, a score given before the identity is known.
The blind audition, the anonymised review, the allocation made on the work alone: a deliberate refusal to see the standing.

## Holds

the reset, and the one cut that severs reward from status.
The place the halo is stripped and the flow can no longer attach to visibility, so an advantage stops converting into the next.

## Offers

A fair test.
Here the overlooked can be measured against the star on the work itself, and merit can decide again, undistorted by who is already ahead.

## Withheld

The real signal a record carried.
The blind panel refuses the information that feeds the lead, and in doing so gives up the genuine predictive value of reputation too, so it offers fairness at the price of a signal the field trusted.
