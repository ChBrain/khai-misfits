# Place: The Podium

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). Where the first advantage is conferred.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A prize handed over, a name announced, a winner raised above a field that nearly tied.
The debut that landed, the first-place ribbon, the early break: the moment one of two comparable performers is marked ahead.

## Holds

The lead, and the thin margin that produced it.
The place the seed is planted, an advantage that may be a hair or a fluke, about to be read as a verdict.

## Offers

A clear winner.
Here the field gets a name to point to and back, a decisive result that resolves a near-tie into a first and a rest.

## Withheld

The size of the true margin.
The podium announces a winner and hides how close it was, so a hair's difference leaves the stage looking like a gap, and the luck in it disappears into the word won.
