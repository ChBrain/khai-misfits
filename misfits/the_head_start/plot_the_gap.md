# Plot: The Gap

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The long tail](place_the_long_tail.md), then [the byline](place_the_byline.md).
The distance has opened wide, the equally able sit in the slope below the spike, and the history is being written around the names on top.

## Action

[Crediting](process_crediting.md): [The Chronicler](persona_the_chronicler.md), holding [the historian's seat](position_historian.md), records the field as merit, while [The Equal](persona_the_equal.md) at [the peer's seat](position_peer.md) keeps running and keeps falling behind.

## Stage

[The Equal](persona_the_equal.md) works [Keep Up](plan_keep_up.md) and [The Chronicler](persona_the_chronicler.md) works [Write the History](plan_write_the_history.md): [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) stands as a distribution far past any spread in talent, and [the overlooked](piece_the_overlooked.md) are read as the lesser and footnoted.
The built distance is naturalized as who was always best.

## Tension

The distance is real, and calling it talent is the injustice.
The equally able did work as good and got a fraction of the reward, so their falling behind is the compounding's arithmetic, not their merit; but a manufactured gap and an earned one look identical once drawn, so the history that reads the outcome as ability is honest and wrong at once. Either the inequality is named as accumulated advantage ... or it is signed, forever, as the natural spread of talent.
