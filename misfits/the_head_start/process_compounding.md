# Process: Compounding

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The loop turning advantage into the next advantage.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A lead that has begun to draw resource, attention, and credit.
Once the flow arrives, it can be reinvested into a fresh advantage.

## Direction

From advantage toward larger advantage.
It moves each turn's flow into the next lead, widening the distance geometrically.

## Lever

Reinvestment meeting a lead fed on visibility.
The force is the flow converting into wins; the resistance is only merit, which the loop does not require, because the growth runs on standing, not on a widening gap in skill.

## Echo

A self-sustaining lead, and a manufactured gap.
It hands forward a distance grown far past any difference in ability, read by all as talent realized.
