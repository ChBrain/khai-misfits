# Position: Peer

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The seat as able as the leader, a step behind and falling.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Ability equal to the star's, and none of the flow.
Work as good, and a fraction of the notice for it.

## Orders

Keep up: compete on the merits, apply, submit, ship, and try to close the gap.
Do work as strong as the leader's, and reinvest what little it returns.
Earn your way from behind.

## Loses

The race, to a gap that widens faster than effort can close.
A seat that gets a fraction of the reward for equal work reinvests a fraction as much, so it falls further behind however hard it runs, and the falling is read as its merit.

## Drives

Toward comparable work rewarded incomparably less, and away from any way to catch a self-feeding lead.
Toward being the control the field ignores and calls the lesser talent.
