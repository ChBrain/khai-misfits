# Persona: The Equal

## Taxonomy

[Peer](position_peer.md). The one as able as the star, who started a step behind and fell away.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable, working hard, quietly baffled.
Does work as good as the leader's and cannot understand why it lands so much smaller.
Keeps trying to close a gap that widens faster than effort can.

## Action

Competes without the head start: applies, submits, ships, on the merits.
Gets a fraction of the notice for comparable work, and reinvests a fraction as much.
Falls further behind, not for lack of ability but for lack of the flow.

## Shadow

Doing equal work is not enough when the reward tracks the lead, not the work.
The Equal is the control the field ignores, the proof the gap was built, and their falling is read by everyone, sometimes even by themselves, as evidence they were simply the lesser talent.

## Tell

"My work is just as good. Why isn't it landing?"
The question with no answer in merit, because the answer is the head start.
