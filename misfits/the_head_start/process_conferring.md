# Process: Conferring

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). Awarding the initial lead.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A contest close enough to tie, resolved to a winner.
The moment a first prize must be given, one of the near-equals is marked ahead.

## Direction

From a near-tie toward a named winner.
It moves to a single lead, conferred on a hair's margin or on luck.

## Lever

A decision rule meeting two comparable performers.
The force is the need to pick; the resistance is that the margin is tiny, so the pick is close to arbitrary and reads as decisive.

## Echo

A lead, planted.
It hands forward one performer marked ahead by a difference smaller than it will come to look, the seed the compounding grows from.
