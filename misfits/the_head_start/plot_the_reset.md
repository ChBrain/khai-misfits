# Plot: The Reset

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The blind panel](place_the_blind_panel.md), and someone who has stopped asking whose work it is.
The question has changed: not who is already ahead and safe to back, but how good is this work when the name is off it.

## Action

[Blinding](process_blinding.md): [The Leveler](persona_the_leveler.md), holding [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), screens the judgment from the standing so only the work is weighed.

## Stage

[The Leveler](persona_the_leveler.md) tables [Blind the Field](plan_blind_the_field.md): strip the halo and hide the name, so [the reset](piece_the_reset.md) cuts the loop and [the matthew](piece_the_matthew.md) loses its grip.
The overlooked are measured against the star on the work alone, and merit gets to decide again.

## Tension

Merit decides again, and the field gives up a signal it trusted.
Blinding works by refusing the information that feeds the lead, and that refusal also discards the real predictive value a track record carried, so it looks wasteful and unfair to the proven. It trades a legible hierarchy of winners for a harder, fairer contest ... or the blinding is judged to throw away good information, the names go back on, and the field backs the proven again, and the head start resumes its only direction.
