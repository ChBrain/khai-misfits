# Plot: The Compounding

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The feed](place_the_feed.md), turning: the flow becomes the next advantage, cycle after cycle.
What the lead drew is reinvested into a fresh win, and the shared work goes out under the name already known.

## Action

[Compounding](process_compounding.md): [The Star](persona_the_star.md), holding [the leader's seat](position_leader.md), converts each advantage into the next, while [The Collaborator](persona_the_collaborator.md) at [the co-author's seat](position_co_author.md) does a full share whose credit accrues upward.

## Stage

[The Collaborator](persona_the_collaborator.md) works [Lend the Credit](plan_lend_the_credit.md): contribute fully and let the recognition attach to the eminent name, so [the compounding](piece_the_compounding.md) runs on borrowed credit and [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) begins to open.
The lead is now self-sustaining, growing on resources the standing supplied.

## Tension

It looks like momentum, and it is the loop.
The star seems to be pulling away on sheer quality, each success built on the last, exactly as talent should; but the successes are built on the flow the prior success attracted, and the collaborator's real share is quietly financing the name that gets remembered. Merit has stopped deciding the distance, and nothing about the trajectory shows it.
