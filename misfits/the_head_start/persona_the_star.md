# Persona: The Star

## Taxonomy

[Leader](position_leader.md). The one who took the early lead and rode it into dominance.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Confident, capable, sure the distance is earned.
Won early, and has been winning since, so reads the whole trajectory as talent realized.
Cannot see the head start from inside the lead.

## Action

Rides the advantage: takes the funding, the attention, the credit that the lead attracts.
Converts each into the next win, and calls the pattern merit.
Pulls further ahead on resources the standing supplied.

## Shadow

The lead was a hair or a fluke, and everything after was the loop.
The Star is not a fraud and may be genuinely good, but the size of the gap is the compounding's work, not a widening edge in skill, and the difference is invisible to the one it favours.

## Tell

"I earned every bit of this."
True of the effort, and blind to the head start that made the effort pay so much more.
