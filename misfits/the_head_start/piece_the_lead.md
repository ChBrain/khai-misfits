# Piece: The Lead

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The small early advantage: the first difference, earned or lucky, that the rest will compound from.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the podium](place_the_podium.md), a first prize handed to one of two who were near enough to tie.
The early win, the debut that landed, the first citation, the account that came in first: a real advantage, often razor-thin, sometimes just the luck of the draw, marking one performer ahead of an equal.

## Load Bearing

Without an initial difference there is nothing for advantage to accumulate from.
With it there is a starting gap, however small, that the field can read and reward, and a seed the compounding needs: the Lead is load bearing because everything downstream grows from it, and because how it was won, by a hair or by chance, is exactly what the later dominance will obscure.

## Apparent

Proof of being best.
It looks like the cream rising: the winner won, so the winner must be superior, and the margin, whatever it was, is taken as the measure of a real gap. That reading is the opening, because a lead read as merit is a lead that will be fed as if it were.

## Yearbook

Conferred on [the star](persona_the_star.md) through [conferring](process_conferring.md), at [the podium](place_the_podium.md).
It is the seed of the play: the Lead is what [the halo](piece_the_halo.md) reads as quality, and the first term of [the compounding](piece_the_compounding.md) that turns a step into a world.
