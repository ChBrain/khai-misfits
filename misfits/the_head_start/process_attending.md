# Process: Attending

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). Attention aggregating on the already-attended.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A crowd with limited attention and popularity as a filter.
The moment people use what others watch to choose what to watch, attention begins to pool.

## Direction

From scattered notice toward a concentrated pile.
It moves each new glance to the name that already has the most.

## Lever

A sensible shortcut meeting everyone using it at once.
The force is the rational filter of following the many; the resistance is that when all follow it, attention attaches to attention and detects nothing but itself.

## Echo

Visibility feeding visibility.
It hands forward an audience that grows because it is large, the leader's reach compounding on its own size.
