# Position: Reformer

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The reversal seat: it judges the work without knowing whose it is.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The authority to set the rules of allocation, and the will to hide the name.
The one cut that severs reward from standing.

## Orders

Blind the field: screen the audition, anonymise the submission, decouple the reward from the record.
Strip the halo so the flow cannot attach to visibility.
Let the work be weighed alone.

## Loses

The genuine signal a track record carries.
A seat that blinds allocation gives up the real predictive value of reputation to break the false part, and is resented for discarding information the field trusts.

## Drives

Toward reward decoupled from standing, and away from the loop that fed the lead.
Toward merit deciding again, at the cost of the signal a lead once honestly carried.
