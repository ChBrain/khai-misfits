# Persona: The Patron

## Taxonomy

[Allocator](position_allocator.md). The one who directs resources, and directs them to the proven.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Prudent, accountable, risk-aware.
Holds the grant, the budget, the slot, and answers for how it is spent.
Believes backing a track record is the responsible bet, because it is.

## Action

Allocates to the one already ahead, weighting the standing as a signal.
Funds the funded, casts the cast, hires from the pedigree.
Turns the halo into supply, safely and defensibly.

## Shadow

Backing the proven is rational each time and rigs the field over time.
The Patron cannot be faulted for any single choice, since the leader really is the safer bet, and the sum of those safe choices is exactly the flow that compounds a head start into a chasm.

## Tell

"You don't get fired for backing the leader."
The safe bet, made again and again, that builds the gap no one chose.
