# Process: Blinding

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The reversal: judging the work without knowing whose it is.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone deciding the reward is tracking the lead, not the work.
When the loop is named, the allocation can be cut off from the standing.

## Direction

From reward following the name toward reward following the work.
It moves the judgment behind a screen, stripping the halo so the flow cannot attach to visibility.

## Lever

Anonymity meeting a field that trusts its records.
The force is the refusal to see the standing; the resistance is that the same records carry real signal, so blinding gives up the true predictive value to break the false one.

## Echo

The loop cut, and a signal spent.
It hands forward a field where merit can decide again, bought with the genuine information a lead once honestly carried.
