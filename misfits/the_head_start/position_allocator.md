# Position: Allocator

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). The seat that directs resources, and directs them to the proven.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The grant, the budget, the slot, and the duty to spend it well.
The standing of each candidate as a signal to weigh.

## Orders

Back the proven: weight the track record and fund the one ahead.
Make the responsible, defensible bet.
Direct the resource to where the evidence points, which is the lead.

## Loses

The equally able without a record, and the sight of what the sum builds.
A seat judged on each allocation cannot be faulted for backing the leader, so it never sees that its safe choices, added up, are the flow that compounds the gap.

## Drives

Toward resources following the standing, and away from the unproven who might match it.
Toward the halo turned into supply, one defensible bet at a time.
