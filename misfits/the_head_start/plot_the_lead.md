# Plot: The Lead

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The podium](place_the_podium.md), where two who nearly tied are resolved into a winner.
A first prize is handed to one of a pair of comparable performers, on a hair's margin or on the luck of the draw.

## Action

[Conferring](process_conferring.md): [The Star](persona_the_star.md), holding [the leader's seat](position_leader.md), takes the early advantage and begins to ride it.

## Stage

[The Star](persona_the_star.md) works [Ride the Lead](plan_ride_the_lead.md): take what the win draws, so [the lead](piece_the_lead.md) is planted and [the halo](piece_the_halo.md) settles over it, the advantage read as a signal of quality.
One performer leaves the stage marked ahead of an equal, by a difference smaller than it will come to look.

## Tension

Nothing here is unfair, and it seeds the whole slide.
Someone had to win the near-tie, and the margin was real if tiny, so the award is clean; but a lead read as merit is a lead the field will feed as if it were, and from this prize on, the winner's next work will be judged as likely-better before it is even seen. The head start is set, and its size is already being forgotten.
