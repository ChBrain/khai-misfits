# Piece: The Flow

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). Resources and attention accruing to the visible: the preferential attachment of the field.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the feed](place_the_feed.md), where the already-attended draws the next attention.
The funding that follows the funded, the citations that pile on the cited, the followers who follow the followed, the coverage that covers the covered: everything a field has to give moving toward the name that already has the most.

## Load Bearing

Without it a lead would sit still, admired but not fed.
With it the lead is watered: attention, money, and opportunity flow to the leader in proportion to visibility, and visibility is what the lead already has, so the flow is the resource that the next advantage will be bought with. The Flow is load bearing because it is how a signal becomes a supply, turning the halo's preference into real fuel.

## Apparent

The market rewarding excellence.
It looks like a healthy system directing its resources to where they will do the most good, backing winners, amplifying the best. That framing is the disguise, because the flow attaches to attention rather than to merit, and attention attaches to attention, so the reward tracks the lead and not the work.

## Yearbook

Directed by [the crowd](persona_the_crowd.md) through [attending](process_attending.md), and by [the patron](persona_the_patron.md) backing the proven.
It is the supply line of the play: the Flow feeds [the lead](piece_the_lead.md) and powers [the compounding](piece_the_compounding.md), the resource that widens [the gap](piece_the_gap.md).
