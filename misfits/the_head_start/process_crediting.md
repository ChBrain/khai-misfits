# Process: Crediting

## Taxonomy

[The Head Start](play_the_head_start.md). Assigning recognition, and assigning it to the known name.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Shared work that must be attributed.
Once a piece is done by more than one hand, the credit has to be routed.

## Direction

From joint contribution toward a single name.
It moves the recognition to the most eminent contributor, and later to the largest name in the record.

## Lever

Attribution meeting a halo.
The force is the need to name who did it; the resistance is that the field routes credit to the name it already knows, so the share does not track the contribution.

## Echo

Credit accrued upward, and the overlooked footnoted.
It hands forward recognition concentrated on the eminent, feeding the lead and writing the equal share out.
