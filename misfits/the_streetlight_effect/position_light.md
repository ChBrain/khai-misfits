# Position: Light

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The seat of the easy ground, cheap to search and honest about it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Ground that can be seen at a glance: data at hand, a method that runs, a sample in reach.
A genuine ease of searching, and no claim at all to hold the thing.
The pull, by that ease, of every rational search.

## Orders

Offer the ground that can be worked.
Yield your contents readily, whatever they are.
Say only that here the looking is cheap, and never that here the thing lies.

## Loses

Nothing of its own, and yet it is the lure.
Its ease is genuine and says nothing about the thing, but is read as though it did, so the search hears the right ground where it only said the easy one.
The visibility that makes it worth searching is the visibility that has nothing to do with where the keys fell.

## Drives

Toward being searched, because it can be,
against a truth it never told,
so the honest easy ground is taken, wrongly, for the ground that holds the answer.
