# Position: Report

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The seat that writes up the lit search's yield as the finding.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The output of a thorough search: the contents of the lit patch, or its emptiness.
All the marks of a real result, and none of the mark that says where it came from.
The authority of a search conducted well.

## Orders

Report what the search turned up.
Take the lit-ground yield for the answer, and the lit-ground silence for the truth's absence.
Present either as the finding, cleanly and with confidence.

## Loses

The truth, by reporting a real result from the wrong ground.
It cannot record that its patch was chosen for ease, so a lit-ground answer is written as the answer and a lit-ground silence as proof the thing does not exist, when it only was not there.
The most convincing finding is the one drawn from precisely where the thing was not.

## Drives

Toward a clean result,
from a search that never entered the dark,
so the confident finding is exactly the misplacement that a thorough search makes hardest to doubt.
