# Process: Concealing

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The dark holding the thing where the cost of looking keeps it unfound.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A thing lost in ground that is dear to search.
At [the verge](place_the_verge.md), the answer lying just past the lamp, giving nothing to a glance.

## Direction

Toward an answer kept out of reach.
The dark turns back the cheap look, the ready data, the tractable method, so the thing stays where no affordable search will go, and its being there registers, to a search that cannot enter, as nothing being there.

## Lever

A cost of entry that steers the search away.
Because the ground is expensive or impossible to comb, the same cost that makes it hard to search makes it the ground least searched, so the concealment needs no trick, only the price of looking, which the cost gradient will not pay.

## Echo

An answer intact and unlooked-for.
The thing held where it fell, the dark reading as empty to every search that declined it, and the one ground with the truth left, by its own cost, the last ground anyone combs.
