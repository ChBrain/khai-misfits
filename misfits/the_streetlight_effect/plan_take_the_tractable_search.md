# Plan: Take the Tractable Search

## Direction

Comb the light, and do not pour cost into an unsearchable dark. Weigh the certain cost of lighting the dark against its uncertain return, and remember that some search beats none and the keys are sometimes near the lamp. Prefer the search you can actually run.
