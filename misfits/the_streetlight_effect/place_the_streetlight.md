# Place: The Streetlight

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The lit patch, where the searching is cheap and the search collects.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A circle of good light on open ground: data at hand, a method that runs, a sample in reach.
The searcher combing it thoroughly, on hands and knees, sure that so careful a search must be getting somewhere.

## Holds

The easy search and its thoroughness.
Here the ground is cheap to comb and here the combing is done well, so the streetlight holds both the search that flows to the light and the diligence that makes the wrong-ground search feel like progress, the ease and the effort that mask each other.

## Offers

A search that can actually be run, and run well.
The ground can be seen, the method works, the effort is real, so the streetlight offers a thorough, defensible, honest search, the comfort of looking hard in a place where looking is possible.

## Withheld

Any reason to think the thing is here.
The patch is lit, not chosen for holding the answer, so the streetlight withholds the one thing its ease is taken to promise, and the harder the ground is combed the more its emptiness of the thing is hidden by the quality of the search.
