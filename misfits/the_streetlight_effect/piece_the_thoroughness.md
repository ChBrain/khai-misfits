# Piece: The Thoroughness

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The diligence of the lit-patch search that reads as progress toward the answer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the streetlight](place_the_streetlight.md), where the combing is done well.
In the care and rigour poured over the visible ground.

## Load Bearing

Without it the wrong-ground search would feel as fruitless as it is, and the searcher might look up.
With it the detour feels like arrival: the thoroughness is the real diligence of the lit search, the care that combs the patch to its corners, and because it is genuine it is read as closing on the answer, so the harder the light is worked the surer the search grows. The Thoroughness is load bearing because it is the false comfort that keeps the search under the lamp: effort stands in for nearness, the quality of the search is taken for the quality of its ground, and diligence in the wrong place is mistaken for progress toward the right one.

## Apparent

Rigour, and the mark of a good search.
A patch combed carefully and completely looks like a search doing everything right, so the thoroughness reads as excellence, and the truth that it is excellence spent on ground chosen for ease, effort that cannot approach an answer that is elsewhere, stays hidden behind how well the search is run.

## Yearbook

Poured out by [the searcher](persona_the_searcher.md) through [searching](process_searching.md), the diligence [Search Where It's Lit](plan_search_where_its_lit.md) mounts.
It is effort mistaken for nearness: the Thoroughness dresses [the easy search](piece_the_easy_search.md) as progress and lends [the false find](piece_the_false_find.md) the authority that makes it hard to doubt.
