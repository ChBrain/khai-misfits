# Plot: The Combing

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The streetlight](place_the_streetlight.md), the lit patch searched and searched again.
The searcher is on hands and knees, working the ground to its corners, and the very care of it is starting to feel like closing on the answer.

## Action

[Searching](process_searching.md) at its most diligent: [The Searcher](persona_the_searcher.md), holding [the searcher's seat](position_searcher.md), combs the light thoroughly, while [The Pragmatist](persona_the_pragmatist.md), holding [the pragmatist's seat](position_pragmatist.md), defends the choice as the only tractable search there is.

## Stage

[The Searcher](persona_the_searcher.md) tables [Search Where It's Lit](plan_search_where_its_lit.md) and pours out [the thoroughness](piece_the_thoroughness.md), sure that so careful a search must be getting somewhere.
[The Pragmatist](persona_the_pragmatist.md) tables [Take the Tractable Search](plan_take_the_tractable_search.md), reminding the room that a real search you can run beats an impossible one you cannot.

## Tension

The search grows surer as it combs harder, and the combing cannot reach the thing.
The diligence is real and the pragmatist's point is fair: some search beats none, and this is the only one that can be run. But effort is standing in for nearness, so the quality of the search is being read as the quality of its ground, and the more thoroughly the lit patch is worked the more certain everyone grows of an answer that was never in it, diligence in the wrong place wearing the face of progress toward the right.
