# Position: Dark

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The seat of the ground that holds the thing and yields nothing to a cheap look.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The thing that matters, and no easy way in.
A cost of searching that turns back the glance, the ready data, the tractable method.
The answer, kept exactly where searching is dear.

## Orders

Hold the thing where no cheap search can reach it.
Give up nothing to a glance.
Wait, uncombed, because combing is expensive.

## Loses

No fault of its own, and every look it might have had.
Its cost of entry is exactly what steers the search away, so the ground with the answer is the ground least looked at, and its holding the thing reads as emptiness to a search that cannot afford to enter.
The place with the answer is not where the search fails to find it, but where the search declines to look.

## Drives

Toward keeping the answer,
against a search that cannot pay to enter,
so the one ground that holds the thing is the one the cost gradient makes sure is never combed.
