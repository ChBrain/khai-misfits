# Persona: The Illuminator

## Taxonomy

[Illuminator](position_illuminator.md). The one who would search the dark, or build a lamp where the thing actually lies.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Unimpressed by a clean finding from the easy ground, fixed on where the thing really is.
Wants to instrument the unmeasured and light the dark, so the search can look where the answer waits.
Willing to trade a tidy result for a true search.

## Action

Points the search back to the dark where the thing was lost.
Builds light there, instruments the costly, makes the unmeasured measurable.
Runs a worse, harder search in the right place over a clean one in the wrong.

## Shadow

Its counsel is right and dear, so it asks for cost and patience and may still come back with nothing.
The dark it would light can be genuinely unsearchable, so insisting on it can mean no search at all, and the answer was sometimes near the lamp after all.
The one who would look where the thing is cannot promise the looking will find it.

## Tell

Says "light the dark, and search where it actually fell."
Without being able to promise the dark, once lit, holds anything at all.
