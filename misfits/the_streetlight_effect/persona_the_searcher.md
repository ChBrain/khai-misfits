# Persona: The Searcher

## Taxonomy

[Searcher](position_searcher.md). The one looking, rational under cost, drawn to the ground that can be seen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent, practical, keen to make progress.
Wants to run a real search, not stand paralysed before an unsearchable dark.
Goes where the ground can actually be combed.

## Action

Moves to the lit patch, because that is where a search can be run at all.
Combs it thoroughly, carefully, by the book.
Takes what the lit ground yields, and reports it as the find.

## Shadow

Its move is rational under cost and blind to the cost's meaning: the ease that drew it is exactly why the thing is not there.
It mistakes a thorough search of the wrong ground for a search that is closing on the answer.
The more honestly it combs the light, the more sure it grows of a finding the light never held.

## Tell

Says "at least here we can actually look."
Of the one patch chosen for being lookable, and not for holding the thing.
