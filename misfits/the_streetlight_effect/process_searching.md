# Process: Searching

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The search flowing to the lit patch and combing it, by the cost of looking.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A thing to find, and a dark too dear to search.
At [the streetlight](place_the_streetlight.md), the searcher choosing the ground it can actually comb over the ground where the thing fell.

## Direction

Toward the lit patch, worked thoroughly.
The search settles where the looking is cheap, the data at hand and the method tractable, and combs it well, so the effort concentrates on the visible ground and the diligence mounts, all of it in the place chosen for ease.

## Lever

A cost gradient that makes the searchable beat the true.
Because a search that can be run beats one that cannot, the rational searcher goes to the light, so the pull is not laziness but economy, and the very reason the patch is worth searching, its cheapness, is the reason the thing is not in it.

## Echo

A wrong ground combed beautifully.
The lit patch searched to its corners, the diligence real and rising, and the answer no nearer, because the ground was chosen for being easy to see and not for holding the thing.
