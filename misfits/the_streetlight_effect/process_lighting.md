# Process: Lighting

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The move to search the dark or build a lamp there, and the doubt it carries.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A recognition that the answer is in the dark, not the lit patch, and a hand willing to pay to look there.
At [the lamplighting](place_the_lamplighting.md), the illuminator instrumenting the unmeasured and the pragmatist counting the cost.

## Direction

Toward the ground where the thing actually lies.
Light is built in the dark, the costly is instrumented, the unmeasured is made measurable, so the search can finally look where the keys fell, running the harder search in the right place instead of the clean one in the wrong.

## Lever

A search aimed by the answer's location, not the looking's cost.
Because only looking where the thing is can find it, the remedy must pay the price the cost gradient refused, spending on the dark against no guarantee, so the cure is right, dear, and unable to promise the lit dark holds anything.

## Echo

The right ground searched at last, or a true search that yields nothing.
The dark lit and the thing found where it fell, or the costly look returning empty and the lamp vindicated, so the remedy ends either in the answer recovered or in a true search traded for a false one that at least gave a result.
