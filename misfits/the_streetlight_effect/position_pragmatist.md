# Position: Pragmatist

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The seat that defends the lit search as the only tractable one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A cost-aware case for the lamp: a real search you can run beats an impossible one you cannot.
The true point that the dark may yield nothing and the light at least yields something.
No way to tell in advance the dark that hides the answer from the dark that hides nothing.

## Orders

Comb the light, and do not pour cost into an unsearchable dark.
Weigh the certain cost of lighting the dark against its uncertain return.
Remember that some search beats none, and the keys are sometimes near the lamp.

## Loses

The answer, on the occasions its right-sounding caution is wrong.
It cannot distinguish the fruitful dark from the empty one, so its prudence blesses the streetlight exactly when the keys are out past it, keeping the search where the thing is not.
The voice that rightly fears a fruitless search of the dark is the voice that most reliably keeps the search under the lamp.

## Drives

Toward the tractable,
against the risk of a costly search that finds nothing,
so the sound fear of wasting the search on the dark is what strands it, again, in the light.
