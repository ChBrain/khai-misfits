# Place: The Debrief

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). Where the lit search's yield is written up and taken for the answer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The search reporting: here is what the combing turned up, or here is proof there was nothing to find.
A clean result laid out with the confidence of a thorough search, and no line in it for where the ground was chosen.

## Holds

The false find.
Here the lit patch's contents, or its emptiness, are read as the truth, so the debrief holds the real result from the wrong ground, the finding that is convincing precisely because the search behind it was so careful.

## Offers

A clean, confident answer.
The search was thorough and the result is tidy, so the debrief offers a finding that reads as settled, an answer or an all-clear that carries the authority of diligence.

## Withheld

The mark of where the answer came from.
The report records the result and not the ground's chosen-for-ease, so the debrief withholds the one caveat that would puncture it, and a lit-ground yield passes for the answer and a lit-ground silence for the truth's absence.
