# Place: The Lamplighting

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). Where the remedy is weighed: light the dark, or keep to the tractable search.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A move to carry light out past the lamp: the illuminator instrumenting the unmeasured, proposing to search the ground where the thing really fell.
The pragmatist counting the cost, warning that the dark may hold nothing and the keys were sometimes near the lamp.

## Holds

The remedy, and the doubt that dogs it.
Here the dark can be lit so the search can look where the answer is, and here that costs dearly and may return nothing, so the lamplighting holds both the one move that looks in the right place and the real chance it buys only silence.

## Offers

The only search that could find the thing.
By lighting the dark and instrumenting the costly, the lamplighting offers the search its one look at the ground where the keys actually lie, the true search in place of the tractable one.

## Withheld

Any promise the dark holds the answer.
The lamplighting cannot tell in advance the fruitful dark from the empty, so it withholds the certainty the costly search would need, and lights the dark at the risk of a true, expensive look that finds nothing where the lamp at least found something.
