# Position: Illuminator

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The seat that would search the dark, or build a lamp where the thing lies.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The reading that the answer is in the dark, not the lit patch.
The move to instrument the unmeasured and light the ground where the thing actually fell.
No promise that the dark, once lit, holds anything.

## Orders

Point the search back to where the thing was lost.
Build light in the dark: instrument the costly, make the unmeasured measurable.
Run the harder search in the right place over the clean one in the wrong.

## Loses

The certainty of a tidy result, and sometimes the search entirely.
Its counsel is right and dear, asking for cost and patience and able to come back with nothing, since the dark can be genuinely unsearchable and the keys were sometimes near the lamp.
The one seat that would look where the thing is cannot promise the looking will find it.

## Drives

Toward the dark where the answer waits,
against a cost that buys no guarantee,
so the only move that looks in the right place risks a true search that yields silence for a false one that yielded a result.
