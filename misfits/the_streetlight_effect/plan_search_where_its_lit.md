# Plan: Search Where It's Lit

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the searcher's seat](position_searcher.md), inside [The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Searcher](position_searcher.md)

## Direction

Search where the ground can actually be combed. The dark cannot be afforded, so go to the lit patch, work it thoroughly and by the book, and take what it yields, because a search you can run beats one you cannot. Do not mistake the ease that drew you for evidence: only, do not waste it.

## Orders

([The Searcher](persona_the_searcher.md) combs the lit patch thoroughly and grows sure of a finding the light never held, the ease that drew it being why the thing is not there.)

## Implementation

([The Streetlight](place_the_streetlight.md); the [searching](process_searching.md) process; [the easy search](piece_the_easy_search.md) works the lit ground, and its [thoroughness](piece_the_thoroughness.md) makes the combing feel like closing on the answer.)

## Targets

- [ ] the lit patch is chosen because a search can be run there at all
- [ ] the ground is combed thoroughly, carefully, by the book
- [ ] the ease that drew the search is taken, wrongly, as nearness to the thing
- [ ] the diligence of the wrong-ground search reads as progress toward the answer
