## Shown

The ground just past the circle of light, where the keys fell: unlit, hard to reach, giving nothing to a glance.
No searcher here, only the thing itself, waiting where the cost of looking turned every rational search away.

## Holds

The dark and the lost.
Here the answer lies and here the ground refuses the cheap look, so the verge holds both the thing that matters and the cost of entry that keeps it unfound, the location of the truth and the reason no one searches it.

## Offers

The one ground where the thing actually is.
Whatever the lamp yields, the answer is here, so the verge offers the only search that could ever find it, the look in the place where the keys really fell.

## Withheld

Any cheap way in.
The ground is dark and dear to search, so the verge withholds the easy look entirely, and its holding the thing is read, by a search that cannot afford to enter, as simply nothing there.
