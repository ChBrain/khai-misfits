# Piece: The Dark

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The region costly or impossible to search, where the thing actually lies.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the verge](place_the_verge.md), just past the lamp.
The unlit ground that gives nothing to a glance.

## Load Bearing

Without it the answer could be searched as cheaply as anything, and the search would simply find it.
With it the answer is out of easy reach: the dark is the region dear to search, where the cost of entry turns back the glance and the ready data and the tractable method, so the thing can be held exactly where no affordable search will go. The Dark is load bearing because it is the far pole of the cost gradient (Simon on bounded, cost-bound search): its expense is not incidental but the whole engine, the reason the search is steered away from precisely the ground that holds the truth.

## Apparent

Empty, or not worth searching.
Ground that yields nothing to a glance looks like ground with nothing in it, so the dark reads as barren, and the truth that it holds the very thing being sought, and reads as empty only because entering it is dear, stays hidden behind the cost of ever looking.

## Yearbook

Held by [the dark](persona_the_dark.md) through [concealing](process_concealing.md), tabled as [Keep the Answer](plan_keep_the_answer.md).
It is the ground that holds the thing and refuses the cheap look: the Dark keeps [the lost](piece_the_lost.md) where the cost steers the search away, stands opposite [the light](piece_the_light.md), and is what [Light the Dark](plan_light_the_dark.md) would pay to enter.
