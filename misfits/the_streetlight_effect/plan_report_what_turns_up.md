# Plan: Report What Turns Up

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the report seat](position_report.md), inside [The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Report](position_report.md)

## Direction

Write up what the search turned up. Take the lit-ground yield for the answer, and the lit-ground silence for the truth's absence, and present either as the finding, cleanly and with confidence. Record the result, and not the reason the ground was chosen.

## Orders

([The False Find](persona_the_false_find.md) reports a real result from the wrong ground, its cleanliness exactly what makes it convincing and misplaced.)

## Implementation

([The Debrief](place_the_debrief.md); the [mistaking](process_mistaking.md) process; [the false find](piece_the_false_find.md) takes the lit patch's yield, or its emptiness, for the answer, a finding drawn from where the thing was not.)

## Targets

- [ ] the lit-ground yield is taken for the answer that was sought
- [ ] the empty patch is taken for proof the thing does not exist
- [ ] the result is presented cleanly, with the confidence of a thorough search
- [ ] the ground's chosen-for-ease is left out of the report, and the misplacement made hard to doubt
