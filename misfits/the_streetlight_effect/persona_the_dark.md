# Persona: The Dark

## Taxonomy

[Dark](position_dark.md). Where the thing actually lies, costly or impossible to search, yielding nothing to the easy eye.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Unlit, hard to reach, offering no foothold to a quick look.
Holds the thing that matters and shows nothing of it.
Not hostile: only expensive, only unmeasured, only out of easy reach.

## Action

Keeps the answer where no cheap search can find it.
Turns back the glance, the ready data, the tractable method, all the tools of the lit patch.
Waits, uncombed, precisely because combing it is dear.

## Shadow

It is nobody's fault and it warns no one, so its holding the thing looks like emptiness to a search that cannot afford to enter it.
The cost of searching it is exactly what steers the search away, so the place with the answer is the place least looked at.
The dark is not where the search fails to find the thing: it is where the search declines to look.

## Tell

Says nothing, and gives up nothing to a glance.
Of the one ground where the thing actually is, and the searching is dear.
