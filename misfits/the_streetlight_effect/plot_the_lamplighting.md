# Plot: The Lamplighting

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The lamplighting](place_the_lamplighting.md), a move to search where the thing actually fell.
The proposal is to carry light out past the lamp, instrument the unmeasured, and run the search on the dark ground where the answer waits.

## Action

[Lighting](process_lighting.md): [The Illuminator](persona_the_illuminator.md), holding [the illuminator's seat](position_illuminator.md), builds light in the dark and points the search back to the verge, while [The Pragmatist](persona_the_pragmatist.md), holding [the pragmatist's seat](position_pragmatist.md), counts the cost and warns the dark may hold nothing.

## Stage

[The Illuminator](persona_the_illuminator.md) tables [Light the Dark](plan_light_the_dark.md) and answers [the streetlight effect](piece_the_streetlight_effect.md) by paying to look where [the lost](piece_the_lost.md) lies.
[The Pragmatist](persona_the_pragmatist.md) tables [Take the Tractable Search](plan_take_the_tractable_search.md), unable to tell the fruitful dark from the empty one, and so blessing the lamp when the keys are past it.

## Tension

The only move that looks where the thing is cannot promise the looking will find it.
Lighting the dark is the one search that could ever recover the answer, but it is dear, and it may come back with silence, and the pragmatist's caution is often right, since some dark truly holds nothing and the keys were sometimes near the lamp. So the cure runs against a real risk, not a straw one, and it closes with the answer found where it fell, or the light holding, or a true and costly search that yields nothing where the lamp at least yielded a result.
