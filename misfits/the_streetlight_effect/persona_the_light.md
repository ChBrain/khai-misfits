# Persona: The Light

## Taxonomy

[Light](position_light.md). The lit patch, where the searching is cheap, honest that seeing is easy here.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Bright, open, easy to work.
Offers ground that can be seen at a glance, data at hand, a method that runs.
Makes no claim to hold the thing: only that here, you can look.

## Action

Draws the search by being the one place it is cheap to conduct.
Yields its contents readily, whatever they happen to be.
Lets a thorough combing feel like real progress, because the combing really is thorough.

## Shadow

Its ease is genuine and says nothing about the thing, yet is read as though it did.
It is honest that it is only the easy ground, and the search hears "the right ground" anyway.
The very visibility that makes it worth searching is what has nothing to do with where the keys fell.

## Tell

Says "look how clearly you can see here."
And is believed to be saying "the thing is here," which it never said.
