# Piece: The Lost

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The thing sought, the answer itself, waiting in the dark where it fell.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the verge](place_the_verge.md), where the keys lie.
Out past the lamp, on the ground the search declined to enter.

## Load Bearing

Without it the search has no stake, and the wrong ground would cost nothing.
With it the whole search has a point and a price: the lost is the answer being sought, the keys, the truth, the real effect, and it is located, all along, in the dark and not the light, so every hour under the lamp is an hour away from it. The Lost is load bearing because it fixes what the search is for and where it is: it is the thing whose true location, out in the costly dark, makes the cheap search of the light not a shortcut but a detour that can never arrive.

## Apparent

Absent, since the thorough search did not turn it up.
A thing not found by a careful search of the lit patch looks like a thing that is not there or not to be had, so the lost reads as missing or nonexistent, and the truth that it was simply never where the search looked, lying intact in the unsearched dark, stays hidden behind the confidence of the combing that missed it.

## Yearbook

Kept by [the dark](piece_the_dark.md), sought by [the illuminator](persona_the_illuminator.md) through [lighting](process_lighting.md).
It is what the search is for and where it is not looked for: the Lost waits in [the dark](piece_the_dark.md) while [the easy search](piece_the_easy_search.md) combs the light, and is recovered only if [Light the Dark](plan_light_the_dark.md) pays to look where it fell.
