# Persona: The Pragmatist

## Taxonomy

[Pragmatist](position_pragmatist.md). The one who defends the lit search: a real search you can run beats an impossible one you cannot.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Level-headed, cost-aware, wary of chasing the dark.
Points out that the dark may yield nothing and the lamp at least yields something.
Holds that a tractable search is not a mistake but the only search there is.

## Action

Argues for combing the light and against pouring cost into an unsearchable dark.
Reminds the room that some search beats none, and that the keys are sometimes near the lamp.
Weighs the certain cost of lighting the dark against its uncertain return.

## Shadow

Its caution is often right and is exactly what keeps the search under the lamp.
It cannot tell, in advance, the dark that hides the answer from the dark that hides nothing, so its prudence blesses the streetlight when the keys are truly out past it.
The voice that rightly fears a fruitless search of the dark is the voice that keeps the search where the thing is not.

## Tell

Says "better a real answer here than no answer out there."
Right often enough that it is trusted exactly when it is wrong.
