# Plot: The Finding

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The debrief](place_the_debrief.md), the search having to report.
The lit patch has been combed to its corners and now yields its verdict: either here is what turned up, or here is proof there was nothing to find.

## Action

[Mistaking](process_mistaking.md): [The False Find](persona_the_false_find.md), holding [the report seat](position_report.md), takes the lit ground's yield for the answer, while [The Light](persona_the_light.md), holding [the light's seat](position_light.md), stands as the honest, easy ground that happened to produce it.

## Stage

[The False Find](persona_the_false_find.md) tables [Report What Turns Up](plan_report_what_turns_up.md) and writes [the false find](piece_the_false_find.md): the patch's contents as the answer, or its emptiness as the thing's not existing.
[The Light](persona_the_light.md) offers only what it held, never having claimed to hold the thing, and the report omits that its ground was chosen for ease.

## Tension

A real result from the wrong ground is crowned as the truth, and the diligence guards the error.
Everything is honest in its parts: the light only ever offered ease, the search was genuinely thorough, the result really is what the patch held. But the report records the finding and not the reason the ground was chosen, so a lit-patch answer passes for the answer and a lit-patch silence for the truth's absence, and the very thoroughness that missed the mark is what makes the misplaced finding hardest to doubt.
