# Pitch: Where the Light Is

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Diligent, methodical, reasonable.
The voice of a search run properly, in good light, where the ground can actually be seen and combed.
Sensible about what can and cannot be done, and quietly proud of doing the doable well.

## Undertow

Under the diligence, a patch chosen for being easy to see.
The dark cannot be searched, so the search settles under the lamp, and the ground is worked not because the thing is there but because the looking is cheap.
The ease was never evidence, and the keys are exactly where the search declined to go.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching a careful, honest search comb the wrong ground beautifully,
sure it is closing on the answer because it is combing so thoroughly,
while the dark where the thing actually lies is never touched.

## Echo

After the run: a clean finding drawn from the lit patch and mistaken for the truth,
a search vindicated by its own diligence,
and the answer still out in the dark, exactly where no easy look would reach.
