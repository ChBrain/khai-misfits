# Piece: The Streetlight Effect

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The phenomenon whole: the search steered to the easy ground by the cost of looking, and its remedy in lighting the dark at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the lamplighting](place_the_lamplighting.md), where the dark is lit or the reflex holds.
And across the whole ground, in every hour spent under the lamp and every corner of the dark left uncombed.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of searches and silences with no shape.
With it the moves become one law: the light, the dark, the lost, the easy search, the false find, and the thoroughness are facets of a single mechanism, in which the cost of looking, not the location of the thing, decides where the search goes, so the effort flows to the lit patch, combs it diligently, and reports its yield as the truth while the answer lies in the unsearched dark. The Streetlight Effect is load bearing because it is the whole finding with its whole remedy attached (Kaplan's drunkard's search; Freedman): the only answer is to search where the thing is, lighting the dark and instrumenting the unmeasured, a move that costs dearly, may yield nothing, and cannot be told in advance from the case where the keys were near the lamp.

## Apparent

Either a diligent search let down by a hard problem, or a foolish one that looked in the wrong place.
Seen from one side the search did everything right and the thing was just not findable; from the other, it simply searched where it should not have; and both miss the structure, that the search was rational and thorough and the ground genuinely easy, and the ease was never evidence, so neither praise nor blame answers it, only searching where the thing is, which is the one place the cost of looking makes sure no one goes.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the illuminator](persona_the_illuminator.md) through [lighting](process_lighting.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: the Streetlight Effect is what [the easy search](piece_the_easy_search.md) makes of a search once [the dark](piece_the_dark.md) holds the thing, undone only by lighting the dark to look for [the lost](piece_the_lost.md), at the cost of a true search that may yield silence.
