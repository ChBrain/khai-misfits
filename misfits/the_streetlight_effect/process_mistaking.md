# Process: Mistaking

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The lit search's yield taken for the answer, or its emptiness for the truth's absence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A thorough search of the lit patch, and a result that must be reported.
At [the debrief](place_the_debrief.md), the combing done and its yield, or its silence, laid out as the finding.

## Direction

Toward a clean, misplaced conclusion.
Whatever the lit patch held is written up as the answer, and whatever it did not hold is written up as the thing's not existing, so a result from the wrong ground becomes the result, carrying the authority of the search behind it.

## Lever

A finding whose diligence hides its ground.
Because the search was real and careful, its yield reads as trustworthy, so the mistaking rides on genuine thoroughness, and the report that omits where the ground was chosen makes a lit-patch answer, or a lit-patch silence, the hardest kind of wrong to doubt.

## Echo

The wrong ground's contents crowned as the truth.
A confident finding drawn from where the thing was not, or a false all-clear from a patch the thing had already left, and the diligence of the search standing guard over the error.
