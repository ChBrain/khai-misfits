# Position: Searcher

## Taxonomy

[The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md). The seat that looks, and is drawn to the ground it can afford to search.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The task of finding the thing, and a budget that will not stretch to the dark.
A real method, tractable only where the ground can be seen.
The choice between a search it can run and a search it cannot.

## Orders

Go where the ground can be combed.
Search the lit patch thoroughly, by the book.
Take what the light yields, and call the search done.

## Loses

The thing, by looking where looking is cheap instead of where it fell.
Its move is rational under cost and blind to the cost's meaning, so it combs the wrong ground beautifully and grows surer of a finding the light never held, the ease that drew it being exactly why the thing is not there.
The most diligent search is the one most thoroughly conducted where the answer was not.

## Drives

Toward the searchable,
against a dark it cannot afford to enter,
so the honest need to run a real search becomes the reason the real thing is never looked for.
