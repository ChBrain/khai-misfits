# Persona: The False Find

## Taxonomy

[Report](position_report.md). What the lit search yields, taken for the answer: the patch's contents, or its emptiness, read as the truth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Clean, confident, backed by a thorough search.
Presents as the finding: here is what the combing turned up, or here is proof there was nothing to find.
Wears all the marks of a real result.

## Action

Takes whatever the lit patch held for the answer that was sought.
Or takes the empty patch as evidence the thing does not exist, when it only was not there.
Reports either as the finding, drawn from a search that never entered the dark.

## Shadow

It is a real result from the wrong ground, so its cleanliness is exactly what makes it convincing and wrong.
It cannot mark that its patch was chosen for ease, so it reads a lit-ground answer as the answer and a lit-ground silence as the truth's absence.
The most confident finding is the one drawn from precisely where the thing was not.

## Tell

Says "the search is complete, and here is the result."
Of a search that combed only the ground the thing had already left.
