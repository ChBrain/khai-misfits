# Plan: Light the Dark

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the illuminator's seat](position_illuminator.md), inside [The Streetlight Effect](play_the_streetlight_effect.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Illuminator](position_illuminator.md)

## Direction

Point the search back to where the thing was lost. Build light in the dark: instrument the costly, make the unmeasured measurable, and run the harder search in the right place over the clean one in the wrong. Pay the cost of looking where the answer is, knowing the dark, once lit, may still hold nothing.

## Orders

([The Illuminator](persona_the_illuminator.md) builds light where the thing lies and runs the harder search, unable to promise the lit dark holds anything at all.)

- [ ] the harder search in the right place is run over the clean one in the wrong
- [ ] the risk is borne that the lit dark yields nothing, and the lamp was sometimes right
