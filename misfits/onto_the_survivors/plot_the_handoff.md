# Plot: The Handoff

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The redistribution: the parted member's share goes onto the survivors, and every remaining share rises.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

At [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md), one member's load with nowhere to sit: the bundle still whole enough to carry it, but no longer with that member's help.

## Action

[Sharing](process_sharing.md) takes [the Share](piece_the_share.md) the parted strand carried and divides it across the members still holding, and each of them, [the Survivor](persona_the_survivor.md), runs [Take the Extra](plan_take_the_extra.md): absorbing a slightly heavier portion than it carried the instant before, exactly as the bundle was designed to allow.

## Stage

The transfer is small and orderly. A share that one member carried is now spread thin across the many that remain, so no single survivor takes on more than a sliver more than it had. The bundle's overall strain ticks upward, barely, and settles there. This is the design working precisely as intended: redundancy meeting a lost member with the rest closing ranks around the gap.

## Tension

The load has been shared onto the survivors exactly as it was built to be, and the bundle is, by that measure, exactly as safe as it was meant to be. But the sharing has not simply repaired the loss: it has moved the failed member's demand onto living members, and the next-weakest among them, drawn from the same spread that produced the first, is now carrying a share closer to its own limit than it was a moment ago. The rule that saved the bundle from one parting is the rule that has just handed the next candidate its opening.
