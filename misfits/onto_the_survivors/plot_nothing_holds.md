# Plot: Nothing Holds

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The rupture: past the critical fraction, every transfer breaks the next member, and the whole bundle goes at once.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

At [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md), past [the Critical Fraction](piece_the_critical_fraction.md) marked in [No Margin Left](plot_no_margin_left.md): one more member parts, and its share has nowhere safe to land.

## Action

[Cascading](process_cascading.md) runs without stopping: the load thrown from each parted member onto the survivors now exceeds even the strongest among them, so each transfer breaks the next member in the same instant it is made, and [the Avalanche](piece_the_avalanche.md) that stopped twice before does not stop again. [The Rupture](piece_the_rupture.md) takes the whole bundle at once.

## Stage

There is no longer a gap between one parting and the next for the bundle to settle in. A member parts, its share lands on survivors already carrying nearly all they can bear, and they part too, in the same breath, and their shares land on fewer still, until there are no survivors left to be next. What took the earlier bursts several moments to run through takes this one an instant.

## Tension

Complete rupture, from a state that a moment before was holding: the redundancy fully spent and no member left to survive it. No weak part failed anywhere in it, and no shock struck it from outside: every member held to its own true strength until its share exceeded that strength, and every transfer was the same lawful sharing the bundle was built on from [Built to Share](plot_built_to_share.md) onward. [The Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md)'s redundancy was real the whole time, sized honestly and margined wide, and it never once removed the fact that a shared load rises on every survivor as members drop. The load was only ever passed onto the survivors, one sound member at a time, until too few remained.
