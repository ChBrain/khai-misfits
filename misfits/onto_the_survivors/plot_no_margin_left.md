# Plot: No Margin Left

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The threshold: the fraction of members lost crosses the peak of the bundle's own load-extension curve.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

On [the Curve](place_the_curve.md), the point where the line that has been climbing turns over: the load the bundle can still sustain stops rising.

## Action

[The Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) reads [the Curve](place_the_curve.md) against the tally kept at [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md) and finds that the fraction of members already lost to the successive bursts has passed [the Critical Fraction](piece_the_critical_fraction.md), the single point past which the load-sharing no longer has enough survivors to divide a further parting among.

## Stage

Nothing at the Cross-Section itself looks different from the moment before: the bundle is still standing, still carrying its load, still, on the surface, exactly what it was after the last burst absorbed. But the arithmetic has turned. Each survivor's share, raised again by every parting so far, now sits so close to that survivor's own strength that the next transfer, whenever it comes, will not be small enough to be safe on the few members left to receive it.

## Tension

The last margin is gone, and no further rise in the external load is required for what follows: the same load, spread over fewer and fewer survivors, is already pressing harder on each of them than any of them can bear. The next burst is not another one the bundle will absorb. It is the one the cascade, finite every earlier time, is now positioned to carry to the end.
