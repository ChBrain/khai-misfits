# Pitch: The Survivors Were Only Next

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Structural, exact, sound.
A redundant bundle of parallel members carrying a load together, each holding a share far below its own strength, no single point of failure anywhere in it.
Nothing false in it: the redundancy is real, the members are sound, the load is genuinely shared, and every member checked on its own passes.

## Undertow

Under the soundness, the rule that spreads the load is the rule that spreads the failure.
Because a parted member's share does not vanish but falls onto the survivors, every remaining share rises, and since the members are drawn from a spread of strengths there is always a next-weakest the raised share can push over, so a parting causes a parting, and past a critical fraction of members lost each transfer breaks the next.
The sharing that made no member a single point of failure is the sharing that carries the first failure through all of them.

## Nerve

The feeling of a bundle that holds burst after burst, the survivors absorbing each small avalanche and settling at a higher strain, sure it will hold this one too,
until the fraction lost crosses the point past which the share on every survivor exceeds what the survivors can bear,
and the same handing-on that was safe a hundred times runs, this time, to the end, from a state that an instant before was carrying.

## Echo

After the run: a complete rupture from a bundle that was holding a moment before, no weak member and no shock anywhere in it,
every member sound and held to its true strength, every transfer small and exactly the sharing the design intended,
and the redundancy fully spent, real the whole time and never enough, because the survivors, taking each failed member's load exactly as they were built to, were never spared but only next.
