# Plan: Raise the Load

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the load's seat](position_load.md), inside [Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Load](position_load.md)

## Direction

Be carried by the bundle, divided across all its members while they hold, each taking a fraction below its strength, and rise only until the weakest reaches its limit. After the first parting, do not grow: hold the same total and let it be redistributed, the same weight across fewer shoulders, pressing harder on every survivor with each member lost.

## Orders

([The Load](persona_the_load.md) presses on the bundle through [Loading](process_loading.md) until the weakest member parts, then holds, unchanged, while the same weight rides onto the survivors.)

## Implementation

([The Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md) bears the divided load; the [loading](process_loading.md) process brings the weakest to its limit; thereafter the same total, redistributed by [Sharing](process_sharing.md), presses harder on each survivor as the members thin, driving the fraction lost toward [the Critical Fraction](piece_the_critical_fraction.md) without the load ever growing.)

## Targets

- [ ] the load is carried divided across the members, each holding a fraction below its strength
- [ ] the load rises only until the weakest member reaches its limit and parts
- [ ] after the first parting the total is held unchanged, never growing
- [ ] the same weight, shared across fewer survivors, presses harder on each until too few remain to hold it
