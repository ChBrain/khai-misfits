# Process: Cascading

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). No persona owns it: emergent, a chain of partings running through [the Strand](persona_the_strand.md) and [the Survivor](persona_the_survivor.md) at [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md), opening [The Run](plot_the_run.md) and, past the threshold, closing [Nothing Holds](plot_nothing_holds.md). Carries [the Avalanche](piece_the_avalanche.md), and past threshold [the Critical Fraction](piece_the_critical_fraction.md) and [the Rupture](piece_the_rupture.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A raised share that exceeds the next-weakest survivor's strength.
[Sharing](process_sharing.md) has already lifted every remaining share once; when that lift is enough to take one more member past its rating, that member parts too, and its own share now has to be shared in turn.

## Direction

Toward completion or toward absorption, decided by the fraction already lost.
Below [the Critical Fraction](piece_the_critical_fraction.md) each burst of successive partings is finite, the survivors take up what is shed and the bundle settles at a higher strain; past it, every transfer breaks the next member, and the process runs, member by member, straight through to [the Rupture](piece_the_rupture.md) of the whole bundle.

## Lever

Below the critical fraction the avalanche is finite and absorbed; past it every transfer breaks the next and it runs to completion, its size diverging near the critical point, sharpest under local sharing where it localizes into a running front (Pradhan, Hansen, and Chakrabarti).
The same mechanics govern a burst that stops after three members and a burst that does not stop until none are left; only the fraction already lost, and how near the load sits to the peak of [the Curve](place_the_curve.md), decides which one is running.

## Echo

A bundle that held burst after burst until the one burst that does not stop.
Every earlier avalanche looked like proof the bundle could take it, each one finite, each one absorbed, and the process gives no signal, at the start of the next burst, that this is the one that runs to the end rather than the one that stops.
