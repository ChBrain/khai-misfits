# Plan: Take the Extra

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the survivor's seat](position_survivor.md), inside [Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Survivor](position_survivor.md)

## Direction

When a member parts, absorb the load it sheds and carry the raised share, because taking up a parted neighbour's part is what a shared load asks and what redundancy is built to do. Hold at the higher strain while the raised share sits below your strength, and if it does not, part in turn and pass it on, becoming the next member the failure travels through.

## Orders

([The Survivor](persona_the_survivor.md) receives [the Share](piece_the_share.md) shed by a parted member through [Sharing](process_sharing.md), holds if the raised share is below its strength, and parts if it is not.)

## Implementation

([The Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md) holds the survivors; the [sharing](process_sharing.md) process raises every remaining share on each parting; taking the extra carries each survivor nearer its limit, so past [the Critical Fraction](piece_the_critical_fraction.md) the raised share exceeds every strength and [Cascading](process_cascading.md) runs to [the Rupture](piece_the_rupture.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the load shed by a parted member is absorbed, and the raised share carried
- [ ] the survivor holds while the raised share sits below its strength
- [ ] each absorbed share carries the survivor nearer its own limit
- [ ] past the critical fraction the raised share exceeds every survivor's strength, and the survivor parts in turn
