# Piece: The Avalanche

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The burst of successive partings once a raised share breaks the next member: usually finite and absorbed, its size growing without bound as the load nears the critical point.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Produced at [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md), where one parting causes another in the same instant of loading.

## Load Bearing

Redistribution avalanches in the fiber bundle have a size distribution whose tail diverges as the load approaches the critical point, and under local load sharing that burst localizes and sharpens into a running front of successive partings among neighbours rather than dispersing across the bundle (Pradhan, Hansen, and Chakrabarti). The Avalanche is load bearing because it names the burst as a distribution, not a single event: most avalanches, at loads well below critical, are small and stop, absorbed by survivors whose raised shares still sit below their strength. State it precisely and no further: only as the load nears or passes the critical point does the size distribution favour a burst that spans the whole bundle, and this piece claims that shift in the distribution, not that every avalanche completes.

## Apparent

An isolated incident, a run of bad luck contained to a few unlucky neighbours before the bundle settles again.

Seen at [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md), an avalanche that has just stopped reads as evidence the bundle is resilient, another burst absorbed, and the fact that the size of the next burst is drawn from a distribution now shifted closer to divergence stays hidden behind how ordinary a finite, absorbed avalanche looks each time one occurs.

## Yearbook

Produced by [Cascading](process_cascading.md), running through [the Strand](persona_the_strand.md) after [the Strand](persona_the_strand.md), each parting passing [the Share](piece_the_share.md) on to [the Survivor](persona_the_survivor.md) under [Take the Extra](plan_take_the_extra.md).
Its size is set against [the Spread](piece_the_spread.md) of remaining strengths and grows toward divergence as [the Load](persona_the_load.md) nears [the Critical Fraction](piece_the_critical_fraction.md); past that fraction it no longer stops short of [the Rupture](piece_the_rupture.md).
Runs through [The Run](plot_the_run.md), where it is still finite, and again through [Nothing Holds](plot_nothing_holds.md), where it is not.
