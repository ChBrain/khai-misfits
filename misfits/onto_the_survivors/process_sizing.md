# Process: Sizing

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). [The Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) running [Size the Redundancy](plan_size_the_redundancy.md) at [the Drawing](place_the_drawing.md), setting the member count, the margins, and the sharing rule before the bundle is built, closing [Built to Share](plot_built_to_share.md). Carries [the Bundle](piece_the_bundle.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A load to be carried, and a demand that the structure never rest on a single member.
Before anything is built, [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) sets [the Bundle](piece_the_bundle.md) against [the Load](persona_the_load.md) at [the Drawing](place_the_drawing.md), specifying how many members, what margin on each, and whether a parted member's load is to fall on every survivor or only its neighbours.

## Direction

Toward a whole far stronger than the load, and toward distance from the critical fraction rather than immunity from it.
Adding members widens the redundancy; widening margins lowers every member's share of the load it must eventually bear; breaking the local sharing keeps a parting from falling on its immediate neighbours; each of these moves [the Critical Fraction](piece_the_critical_fraction.md) further off, and none of them removes it.

## Lever

Redundancy buys distance from the critical fraction at real cost in material and weight, and never abolishes that a shared load rises on every survivor as members drop (Daniels; Pradhan, Hansen, and Chakrabarti).
Every member added, every margin widened, every unit of local sharing broken, costs capacity the load itself did not ask for, and none of it changes the mechanics of [Sharing](process_sharing.md) once the fraction lost is high enough.

## Echo

A bundle far stronger than the load and never proof against the sharing, the critical fraction held far off and never removed.
The Drawing reads as safety, the whole plainly outstrengthening the demand, and it is safety, for a long time, bought honestly; what it is not, and cannot be sized into, is a bundle in which the survivors stop being loaded toward their own limits as their neighbours part.
