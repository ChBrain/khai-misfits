# Piece: The Spread

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The distribution of member strengths across [the Bundle](piece_the_bundle.md): some a little stronger, some a little weaker, and always a weakest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set at [the Drawing](place_the_drawing.md), where [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) rates the members against a strength distribution.
Read at [the Curve](place_the_curve.md), where the ordering of strengths determines who parts next.

## Load Bearing

Member strengths in the bundle are drawn from a distribution, not a single fixed value, so the probabilistic bundle always has a weakest member and an ordering of who fails next as the load climbs (Daniels). The Spread is load bearing because it is the thing that guarantees there is always a next member to take: without variance in strength, every member would part at once or none would, and neither is the mechanism this misfit stages. State it precisely and no further: the spread is ordinary and unavoidable, real members are never identical, and this piece does not claim any particular shape of distribution, only that one exists and that it orders the members from weakest to strongest.

## Apparent

Noise, a rounding error in an otherwise uniform set of members, too small to matter next to the wide margin between load and rated strength.

Seen at [the Curve](place_the_curve.md), the Spread reads as a footnote to the Bundle's real strength, a little variation among members that are all, near enough, the same, and the fact that this same small variance is what supplies a weakest to part first and a next-weakest already waiting stays hidden behind how negligible an ordinary spread of strengths looks.

## Yearbook

Set by [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) through [Sizing](process_sizing.md), it is what every member of [the Bundle](piece_the_bundle.md) is drawn from, so [the Strand](persona_the_strand.md) always includes a weakest and [the Survivor](persona_the_survivor.md) a next-weakest.
Under [Loading](process_loading.md), the Spread is what [the Load](persona_the_load.md) presses against first: not the bundle's average strength but its lower tail.
Feeds [the Critical Fraction](piece_the_critical_fraction.md), whose exact threshold is set by this distribution together with [the Share](piece_the_share.md); opens [The First to Part](plot_the_first_to_part.md), where the weakest reaches its limit.
