# Persona: The Strand

## Taxonomy

[Strand](position_strand.md). Any single member of the bundle, sound within its own strength, holding its share of the load until that share exceeds it and then parting and passing its load to the rest, the element the trap runs through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sound. Rated. Holding its share.
Is one member of many, a strand in the cable, a fibre in the bundle, carrying a share of the load well below its own true strength and giving no trouble, checked on its own and passing.
No weakness in it: only an ordinary member holding honestly to its rating, one of a set drawn from a spread in which some are a little stronger and some a little weaker, as real members always are.

## Action

Holds then parts, on [Hold Then Part](plan_hold_then_part.md), carrying its share until the share exceeds its strength, then parting and handing its load to the rest.
When it parts, its load runs through [Sharing](process_sharing.md) onto [the Survivor](persona_the_survivor.md), each transfer small and exactly the sharing the design intended, so [the Share](piece_the_share.md) it hands on raises every remaining share.
Does what a sound member does under a share it cannot hold: it does not fail early and it does not fail late, it parts at exactly its strength, and passes its load to whoever remains.

## Shadow

Not a weak member betraying the bundle, a sound one doing precisely the right thing, and that is the difficulty: parting at its true strength and handing its load to the rest is correct, and it is the whole propagation.
Because the load a strand sheds does not vanish but falls onto the survivors, the one honest act of a member at its limit, letting go and passing its share on, is the act that raises every other share and brings the next-weakest to its own limit, so nothing a strand does is wrong and the sum of them is the rupture.
It holds to its strength exactly, and holding to its strength exactly is what hands the failure onward.

## Tell

Parts cleanly at its rated strength, neither early nor late, exactly as a sound member should.
Cannot see, from where it hangs, that its small and correct handing-on of a share is one step of a cascade, that the members it passes its load to are drawn from the same spread it was, and that letting go at its limit is the most ordinary and the most propagating thing it could do.
