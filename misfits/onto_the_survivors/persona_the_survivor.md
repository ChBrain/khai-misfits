# Persona: The Survivor

## Taxonomy

[Survivor](position_survivor.md). The members still holding after a parting, who take up the load shed onto them and carry a raised share, each closer to its own limit and each, in turn, the next that may part.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Intact. Holding. Taking up the slack.
Is the members that remain when one has gone, absorbing the load it shed, carrying on at a slightly higher strain, still sound and still within strength, the redundancy doing exactly what redundancy is for.
No frailty in it: only members taking up a parted neighbour's share the way a shared load is meant to be taken up, holding the whole together when one part lets go.

## Action

Takes the extra, on [Take the Extra](plan_take_the_extra.md), absorbing the load shed by a parted member and carrying the raised share.
Receives [the Share](piece_the_share.md) through [Sharing](process_sharing.md), and holds, if the raised share is still below its strength, or parts and passes it on if it is not, becoming in that instant [a Strand](persona_the_strand.md) that hands its load to the rest.
Does what a surviving member does when a neighbour parts: it takes up the shed load, because that is what sharing means, and holds at the higher strain until the higher strain is more than it can bear.

## Shadow

Not a rescuer that saves the bundle, a member holding a raised share until it too is over its limit, and that is the difficulty: taking up the slack is exactly what makes the survivor the next to go.
Because every share rises when a member parts, and the survivors are drawn from the same spread as the one that parted, absorbing the shed load carries each survivor closer to its own limit, so the act of surviving one parting is the act of being loaded toward the next, and past the critical fraction the survivor takes a share no member can hold.
It holds the bundle together for a while, and holding the bundle together for a while is what loads it to breaking.

## Tell

Carries the raised share without complaint, reading as sound because it is, right up until the share crosses its strength.
Cannot see that taking up the load of the parted is the same act, one step on, as parting: that every survivor is a future strand, that surviving is only holding a heavier share than before, and that past a fraction there is no share left that any survivor can survive.
