# Persona: The Load

## Taxonomy

[Load](position_load.md). The demand carried by the bundle, divided across the members and raised until the weakest parts, then held, unchanged, while its own weight rides onto the survivors.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ordinary. Divided. Well within the whole.
Is the weight the bundle was built to carry, shared across all the members so each holds only a fraction, a demand the design set the redundancy far above and nothing unusual on its own.
No excess in it: only the load the structure exists to bear, spread wide and safe, less on any member than that member can hold.

## Action

Raises the load, on [Raise the Load](plan_raise_the_load.md), pressing on the bundle through [Loading](process_loading.md) until the weakest member reaches its limit.
Then holds, unchanged, while the same total weight is divided across fewer and fewer members, so that without ever growing it presses harder on each survivor as the bundle sheds them.
Does what a load does on a bundle: it is carried, divided, until a member parts, and then it is carried by the rest, the same weight over fewer shoulders.

## Shadow

Not a crushing overload, an ordinary weight the bundle can bear, and that is the difficulty: the load never has to rise for the rupture, it only has to be held while the members thin.
Because the same total is always divided among whoever remains, the load presses harder on each survivor with every parting though its own magnitude never changes, so it drives the cascade not by growing but by being shared across fewer, an ordinary weight made fatal only by how few are left to hold it.
It stays exactly what it was, and staying exactly what it was is enough, once the survivors are few.

## Tell

Reads as a demand well within the bundle's rated strength, steady on the gauge, never spiking.
Cannot be caught overloading anything, because it never grows: it is the same weight throughout, and only its division across the dwindling survivors, not any rise in it, turns an easy load into one no remaining member can hold.
