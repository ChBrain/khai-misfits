# Plan: Hold Then Part

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the strand's seat](position_strand.md), inside [Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Strand](position_strand.md)

## Direction

Carry your share of the load while it sits below your strength, and when the share rises past your strength, part, because a sound member holds exactly to its rating and no further. Fail neither early nor late, only at your true strength, and when you part, pass your load to whoever remains, not by choice but because a shared load sheds onto the survivors.

## Orders

([The Strand](persona_the_strand.md) holds its share until the share exceeds its strength, then parts and hands [the Share](piece_the_share.md) to [the Survivor](persona_the_survivor.md) through [Sharing](process_sharing.md).)

## Implementation

([The Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md) carries the member; the [sharing](process_sharing.md) process sheds its load on parting; each parting is small and correct, and the sum of them, worked through [Cascading](process_cascading.md), is [the Avalanche](piece_the_avalanche.md) that runs to [the Rupture](piece_the_rupture.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the share is held while it sits below the member's strength
- [ ] the member parts at exactly its strength, neither early nor late
- [ ] on parting, the load is shed onto the survivors, raising every remaining share
- [ ] each parting is individually correct, and the run of them carries the failure onward
