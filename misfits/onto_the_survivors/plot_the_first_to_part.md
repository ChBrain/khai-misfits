# Plot: The First to Part

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The trigger: the load is raised, and the weakest member reaches its limit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

At [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md), the load rises past what it was: every member still within its rating, but the margin on the thinnest of them closing.

## Action

[The Load](persona_the_load.md), on [Raise the Load](plan_raise_the_load.md), runs [Loading](process_loading.md) across [the Bundle](piece_the_bundle.md), pressing evenly on every member at once. The members are drawn from [the Spread](piece_the_spread.md), so their strengths were never identical, and there is always a weakest among them. That member, [the Strand](persona_the_strand.md), on [Hold Then Part](plan_hold_then_part.md), holds up to its own true limit and then parts, first of the bundle to go.

## Stage

Nothing about the parting is sudden or wrong. The weakest strand was never asked to carry more than its share, and it held that share faithfully until the rising load finally matched its rated strength, and then it gave, cleanly, at the point the Spread always implied some member would. The rest of the bundle, hundreds strong, does not notice: each of them is still carrying its own small fraction, none of them near its own limit.

## Tension

One member gone, its share small, its parting exactly what a spread of ordinary strengths predicts. The bundle holds easily, and by every visible measure nothing has changed. But the parted member's share has not vanished: it is already, in the same instant, looking for somewhere to go, and the bundle that looks untouched is the one about to feel it.
