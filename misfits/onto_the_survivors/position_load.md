# Position: Load

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The seat of the demand the bundle carries, divided across the members and raised until the weakest parts, then held while the same weight rides onto fewer and fewer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A fixed weight and a division across the members: the standing to be carried by the whole, each member holding a fraction below its strength, a demand the redundancy was set far above.
An ordinary case: the load is what the bundle exists to bear and sits well within the whole, so carrying it is exactly the structure's function and nothing about it is excessive.
No power to grow, only to be divided across whoever holds, and so the seat presses harder on each survivor as the members thin, not by rising but by being shared across fewer.

## Orders

Be carried, and be shared across the rest.
Press on the bundle divided among all its members while they hold, each taking a fraction below its strength.
When a member parts, do not grow, only redistribute: the same total across fewer shoulders, a heavier share on every survivor with each one lost.

## Loses

Nothing, and it needs to lose nothing.
Because the seat never has to rise for the rupture, it gives up no magnitude and spikes on no gauge, and yet the same weight divided across the dwindling survivors is what drives every share past every strength in the end.
It stays exactly what it always was and, by staying, becomes fatal, an easy demand made lethal only by how few remain to hold it.

## Drives

Toward a weight unchanged and a bundle undone,
the same load pressing harder on each survivor as the members drop away,
so the seat that never grows is the constant that carries the cascade, ordinary throughout and enough, at the last, because too few are left.
