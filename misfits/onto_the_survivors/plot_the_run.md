# Plot: The Run

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The finite avalanche: a raised share breaks the next member, and the next, and the bundle absorbs the burst.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

At [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md), a share just raised by [The Handoff](plot_the_handoff.md) meets a survivor whose own limit sits just below it.

## Action

[Cascading](process_cascading.md) runs the same handoff again, and again, and again, in quick succession: a member parts, its share passes to the survivors, a raised share exceeds the next-weakest survivor, that member parts too, and its share passes on. The successive partings gather into [the Avalanche](piece_the_avalanche.md), a burst of failures set off by one another rather than by any further rise in the load.

## Stage

The burst runs through several members at once, each one parting for the same reason the first one did: its share, however small, has finally exceeded its own true strength. Nothing in any single parting looks different from the very first, at [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md), and nothing about the mechanism has changed. What has changed is the pace: this time the shares are rising faster than any one member can settle before the next is asked to take more.

## Tension

The avalanche runs, and this time it stops. The survivors that remain absorb what the burst has thrown at them, and the bundle settles again, at a strain higher than before, holding. It has now held through more than one such burst, each one finite, each one so far short of the fraction past which a burst does not stop. That is the shape of the dread here: it has held every time so far, and the bundle offers, at [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md), no visible sign of how much of that margin is left.
