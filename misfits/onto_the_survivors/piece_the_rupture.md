# Piece: The Rupture

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The complete whole-bundle failure once the cascade has run through every remaining member, from a state an instant before that was holding.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Struck at [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md), where the last survivors part together, and read after the fact on [the Curve](place_the_curve.md), past the peak.

## Load Bearing

The bundle fails as a whole once the load has passed its critical value, the peak of the load-extension curve (Daniels), and past that point the redistribution avalanche runs to completion rather than stopping, the whole set of remaining members parting in the cascade (Pradhan, Hansen, and Chakrabarti). The Rupture is load bearing because it is total, not partial: state precisely that past the critical fraction no arrangement of the sound survivors holds, because the shares thrown onto them by the preceding partings now exceed even the strongest member's rating, so the collapse is complete rather than a further, larger, but still-finite avalanche. Nothing before this piece was broken; the Rupture is the same members, sound throughout, arriving together at a load no survivor can carry.

## Apparent

A sudden catastrophe, arriving without warning out of a bundle that, a moment before, was reported whole and holding.

Seen at [the Cross-Section](place_the_cross_section.md), the Rupture reads as a shock from nowhere, instantaneous and unheralded, and the fact that it is the deterministic completion of the same sharing rule that had, until this load, always stopped short, one avalanche run all the way through instead of one more that halted, stays hidden behind how abrupt a total collapse looks against the steady bundle it followed.
