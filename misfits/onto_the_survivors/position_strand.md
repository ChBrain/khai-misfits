# Position: Strand

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The seat of a single member, sound within its strength, that holds its share of the load until the share exceeds it and then parts and passes its load to the rest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A rated strength and a share of the load below it: the standing to hold its part honestly, one member of a bundle drawn from a spread of strengths.
A clean case: the member is sound and its share sits below its strength, so holding is correct and the check it passes is honest.
No power to make its shed load vanish, only to hold until its limit and then let go, and so the seat cannot part without handing its share to the survivors, whatever that does to them.

## Orders

Hold, then part.
Carry your share while it sits below your strength, and when the share rises past your strength, part, because a member cannot hold more than it can hold.
Then pass your load to whoever remains, not by any choice but because a shared load sheds onto the survivors, and let it raise their shares as it must.

## Loses

Nothing of its own that it did wrong, and it is the step the failure travels by.
Because the seat only ever holds to its true strength and parts at its limit, it gives up no correctness and fails neither early nor late, and yet its one honest act, letting go and shedding its share onto the rest, is the act that raises every other share and brings the next-weakest to its limit.
It keeps its own behaviour exactly right and, by keeping it, hands the failure onward to the survivors it sheds onto.

## Drives

Toward a cascade made of members each doing right,
every strand parting only at its own strength and passing its load to the rest,
so the seat that never fails wrong is the seat the rupture runs through, one correct parting at a time.
