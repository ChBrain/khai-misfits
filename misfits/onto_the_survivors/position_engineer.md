# Position: Engineer

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The seat that sizes the redundancy, buying distance from the critical fraction at a real cost, and unable to abolish the fact that the sharing which spreads the load spreads the failure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The design and the standing to set it: the number of members, the margin on each, the choice of how the load is shared, the redundancy built far above the demand.
A hard case: every member added and every margin widened works and costs, so the seat holds the only safety there is and no version of it that is free.
No power over the sharing rule's double nature, only over how far the operating point sits from the fraction, and so the seat can hold the cascade off but never remove it.

## Orders

Size the redundancy, and pay for it.
Add members beyond what the load needs, widen the margins, break the local sharing where the design allows, and hold the bundle far below the critical fraction.
Do it knowing the margin buys distance and not immunity, that a shared load rises on every survivor as members drop, and that past the fraction no arrangement of sound members holds.

## Loses

The material, weight, and capacity spent on the redundancy, and the hope that redundancy could ever be made proof.
Because the safety is only ever the bundle sitting far below a fraction the sharing will still, past a point, reach, the seat pays in real capability for a distance the cascade is always able to close, so what it loses is capacity given up for margin.
It surrenders material on purpose to hold the fraction off, and holding the fraction off is a cost with no final payment, because the sharing that earns the payment is the sharing that spends it.

## Drives

Toward a redundancy real and never enough,
the whole built far stronger than the load and the sharing still carrying the failure through it past the fraction,
so the seat that does everything right buys the bundle a long life and never safety, and cannot, by any sound design, make the sharing spare the survivors.
