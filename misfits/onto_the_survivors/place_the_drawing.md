# Place: The Drawing

## Taxonomy

[Onto the Survivors](play_onto_the_survivors.md). The design where the bundle is sized: the member count, the margin on each, the choice of how a parted member's load is shared. Hosts [Built to Share](plot_built_to_share.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A bundle specified before it is built: how many members, what margin on each, global or local sharing.
The Drawing lays out [the Bundle](piece_the_bundle.md) as [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) sets it, member by member, each rated well above its intended share, the whole plainly and provably stronger than the load it will carry.

## Holds

The specification: the member count, the strength margins, and the sharing rule.
Here [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) fixes what [Sizing](process_sizing.md) will build, whether the load is to be shared equally across every survivor or only onto a parted member's neighbours, a choice that decides how the bundle will fail long before it decides how the bundle stands.

## Offers

A promise of redundancy: no single point of failure, the whole far stronger than the load.
Because the Drawing sets more members and wider margins than the demand requires, it offers exactly what redundancy is meant to offer, a structure that can lose a member and keep standing, sound on paper and sound, at first, in fact.

## Withheld

That the sharing designed in to make the bundle safe is the sharing that will carry a failure through it.
The Drawing shows the redundancy, the margin, the promise, but not that the very rule it specifies for dividing the load among survivors is, past [the Critical Fraction](piece_the_critical_fraction.md), the rule that divides the failure among them too, so what is drawn as safety is, unshown, the mechanism of the eventual rupture.
