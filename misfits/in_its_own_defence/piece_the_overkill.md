# Piece: The Overkill

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md). The response grown into the harm it was raised to prevent, the defence that destroys the host beyond what the pathogen would, the whole phenomenon of which the other pieces are parts.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Falls across [the Tissue](place_the_tissue.md), carried there and body-wide through [the Bloodstream](place_the_bloodstream.md), weighed at last at [the Ward](place_the_ward.md).
Flows as a defence turned lethal, the force raised against a threat exceeding it and wrecking the body it stands in, protection and ruin arrived as one.

## Load Bearing

Without the Overkill there is no misfit, only a defence that works: it is the Overkill that ties the proportion to the collateral to the storm, the one outcome in which a correct force becomes a fatal one and the guard destroys what it defends.
The Overkill is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole, the response overshooting into the very harm it was raised to prevent, the host failing of its defence with the invader all but cleared (Zinkernagel and Doherty). It carries the entire misfit: the Proportion is the rule that sizes it, the Collateral is the wound each act of it deals, the Storm is the loop that carries it past the threat, so the Overkill is not one more piece beside the others but the shape they all compose, the point at which defending and destroying are a single act. It is built of nothing but successful defence, each escalation correct and each weapon working as it should, which is why it cannot simply be called off: no part of the response was wrong, the ruin is only their sum, so stopping it means suppressing a guard that has done nothing but its job, against a threat that may not yet be beaten.

## Apparent

Just a hard-fought defence, a body that fought its infection with everything.
Seen at the surface, the Overkill looks like a war nearly won, the guard having given all it had against a serious threat, and the truth that the giving is what killed the host, that the response outgrew the danger and destroyed the tissue, that the war was lost to the defence and not the invader, stays hidden behind how much an all-out defence resembles a heroic one.

## Yearbook

Driven by [the Defender](persona_the_defender.md) doing its job, suffered by [the Host](persona_the_host.md) as its own guard, and faced by [the Clinician](persona_the_clinician.md) with a brake that disarms it.
It is the whole shape of the harm: the Overkill is what [mounting](process_mounting.md) begins, [amplifying](process_amplifying.md) carries past proportion, and [ravaging](process_ravaging.md) lands on the body, the response built of [the proportion](piece_the_proportion.md), [the collateral](piece_the_collateral.md), and [the storm](piece_the_storm.md), a defence correct in every part and lethal in the sum, halted only by the [damping](process_damping.md) that weakens the guard the threat still needs.
