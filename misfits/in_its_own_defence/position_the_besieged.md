# Position: The Besieged

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md). The defended seat, the body the response is raised to protect and fought upon, holding the need for the defence and no command over it, so it must endure the force that saves it as it wrecks it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The stake in the outcome, and the ground the battle is fought on.
The seat holds everything the defence is for, the tissue, the organs, the life, and it holds them as the field the response contends across, so its own substance is both the prize and the terrain.
The whole need for the guard and none of the command of it, since the seat depends utterly on the response to clear the invader and holds no lever to raise, lower, aim, or stand it down.

## Orders

Endure the defence, and depend on it.
Bear the response as it fights across the tissue, absorb the collateral as the cost of being defended, and wait on the outcome, since the force that clears the pathogen is the force that wrecks the body and the seat cannot take the one without the other.
Rely on the guard it cannot direct, since the seat's survival runs entirely through a response it does not control, so its whole part is to host the battle and hope the guard wins before the guard has ruined too much.

## Loses

The command of its own defence, which the seat structurally never holds.
The Besieged can need the response and cannot govern it, because control of the defence does not sit with the body defended, so the seat has no lever to moderate the force destroying it. What the position forfeits is any hand on the guard: it cannot lower the response to spare the tissue, cannot aim it away from itself, cannot stand it down when the invader is beaten, so the very force raised on its behalf runs without reference to the harm it does the seat. No operation available to the seat reaches the response, since the body is the object of the defence and not its commander, so the seat that most needs the guard is the seat least able to check it, wrecked by a force that is its own and answers to nothing it can do.

## Drives

Toward a body kept alive by a defence it cannot command and harmed by a defence it cannot refuse,
and a dependence total on a force wholly beyond its reach,
so the seat with the most at stake in the response holds the least say over it, needing the guard to live, suffering the guard as it fights, and unable to move the hand that would spare it, the defended ground destroyed by a battle it can only host.
