# Plot: The Dose

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ward](place_the_ward.md), someone who would stop the harm.
A response wrecking the tissue and a pathogen not yet cleared, and one lever that reaches the defence.

## Action

[Damping](process_damping.md): the Clinician suppresses the response to spare the tissue, and the same suppression blunts the guard still clearing the pathogen.

## Stage

[The Clinician](persona_the_clinician.md) acts from [the Brake](position_the_brake.md) on [Damp It Down](plan_damp_it_down.md), lowering [the storm](piece_the_storm.md) and with it the clearing, unable to separate [the collateral](piece_the_collateral.md) from [the proportion](piece_the_proportion.md), holding [the overkill](piece_the_overkill.md) back only by weakening the defence.
The brake reaches the harm, and it reaches the protection in the same pull.

## Tension

The one move that stops the harm also disarms the host, and there is no dose that does the first without the second.
There is no clean save: the suppression genuinely eases the storm, but the storm is the guard, so every measure that spares the tissue gives the infection room, and the right amount cannot be known in time and shifts as the battle does. So the plot closes with the harm damped only by disarming the body against an unbeaten threat, the Clinician balanced between two failures with a lever that cannot select, blamed for the infection if they suppress and the ruin if they do not, or the suppression withheld and the defence left to destroy what it defends.
