# Plot: The Onset

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Tissue](place_the_tissue.md), an infection taking hold.
A threat read in the body, and a defence that must be raised to meet it before it wins.

## Action

[Mounting](process_mounting.md): the Defender scales the response to the threat, meeting more invader with more force, summoning the first waves the danger calls for.

## Stage

[The Defender](persona_the_defender.md) acts from [the Response](position_the_response.md) on [Meet the Threat](plan_meet_the_threat.md), raising [the proportion](piece_the_proportion.md) the danger warrants and, in the same force, the harm it carries, the escalation correct and its cost unseen.
The response begins exactly as it should, sized to the threat and placed where the fight must be.

## Tension

The defence starts right, and the rightness is the first step toward the ruin.
There is no error: the threat is real, the force is scaled to it, and a lesser response would leave the body exposed, so the mounting is the guard doing precisely its duty. But the rule that sizes the force to the danger is the rule that will drive it to a harmful size, so the plot closes with a response correctly begun and already carrying, in its proportion, the harm to come, the defence placed in the tissue it will wreck and sound in every part of the placing.
