# Plot: The Flare

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Bloodstream](place_the_bloodstream.md), the response summoning itself.
A defence whose signals call for more defence, and reinforcements that send their own call in turn.

## Action

[Amplifying](process_amplifying.md): each wave draws the next and each signal raises the signal, so the response climbs past the threat that triggered it and spreads body-wide.

## Stage

[The Defender](persona_the_defender.md) acts from [the Response](position_the_response.md), driving [the storm](piece_the_storm.md) through its own summons, carrying [the proportion](piece_the_proportion.md) past itself with no fast brake in the loop.
The rallying looks like strength, and it is the response outgrowing its cause.

## Tension

The guard rallies hard, and the rallying is the loss of proportion.
There is no separate fault: the summoning that amplifies is the same summoning that brings help, so the feedback is not a malfunction but the reinforcement mechanism working, each call correct and answered. But the loop has no native limit, so the plot closes with a defence risen past the danger that set it off and spread through the whole body, the force now larger than the invader ever warranted and still climbing, the vigour that looks like winning the very thing carrying the guard past the threat and into the tissue at large.
