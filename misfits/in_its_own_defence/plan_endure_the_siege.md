# Plan: Endure the Siege

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Besieged](position_the_besieged.md), inside [In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Besieged](position_the_besieged.md)

## Direction

Depend on the response and endure it, absorbing the collateral as the price of being defended, since the force that clears the invader is the force that wrecks the tissue and the body has no way to take one without the other (Rouse and Sehrawat). Host the battle across its own substance, need the guard to win, and bear the guard as it fights, holding no lever to raise, lower, aim, or stand the defence down. Wait on an outcome it cannot steer, alive only if the response clears the threat before it has ruined too much, since command of the defence was never the body's to hold and its whole part is to survive its own protection.

## Orders

([The Host](persona_the_host.md) endures the response it cannot direct, bears the siege from the guard as heavily as from the invader, and waits on a rescue that is also a ruin, unable to call its own defence off or wrong.)
