# Plan: Damp It Down

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Brake](position_the_brake.md), inside [In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Brake](position_the_brake.md)

## Direction

Slow the response to spare the tissue, and stop short of disarming it, titrating suppression between two failures by the hour, since the only lever acts on the whole defence and too much loses the patient to the pathogen while too little loses the tissue to the storm (Tisoncik, Korth, Simmons, Farrar, Martin and Katze). Damp the harm knowing the same measure blunts the protection, because the harmful force and the protective force are one, so every milligram that eases the tissue also weakens the clearing. Judge by the sign and the hour, accept that the right dose cannot be known in advance and shifts as the battle does, and hold the response at a point with no safety in it, balancing rather than curing.

## Orders

([The Clinician](persona_the_clinician.md) titrates the suppression between the storm and the infection, trading one harm for a risk of the other, and answers for the outcome whichever way it breaks, holding a lever that helps and harms in the same motion.)

## Implementation

([The Ward](place_the_ward.md); through the [damping](process_damping.md) process, against the [amplifying](process_amplifying.md) it would slow; the Clinician lowers [the storm](piece_the_storm.md) and with it the clearing, unable to separate [the collateral](piece_the_collateral.md) from [the proportion](piece_the_proportion.md), holding [the overkill](piece_the_overkill.md) back only by weakening the guard.)

## Targets

- [ ] the response is damped enough to spare the tissue
- [ ] the suppression stops short of letting the pathogen win
- [ ] the dose is titrated by the hour, on a setting known to have no safety in it
- [ ] the harm is lowered only by lowering the protection, the trade accepted
