# Process: Ravaging

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md). The falling of the response's weapons on the host, the collateral realized, the tissue wrecked by the same force that clears the invader.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Defender](persona_the_defender.md), at [the Tissue](place_the_tissue.md), its weapons striking the body along with the pathogen.
Begins whenever the inflammation, the cell-killing, and the flood of signals act, since they act on host and invader alike.

## Direction

Turns a defence into a wound.
Because the means of the response act on the tissue as on the pathogen, the force raised to clear the infection clears the body's own cells with it, so ravaging converts protection into damage in a single motion, the guard wrecking the ground it defends by the ordinary working of its weapons, faster and wider than the invader would (Rouse and Sehrawat).

## Lever

Weapons that cannot tell host from pathogen.
Because the inflammation and the killing and the signalling strike whatever they reach, ravaging works through the imprecision of the defence itself: there is no aim to spare the tissue, so the lever is the weapon's own indiscriminacy, which makes every act of clearing an act of wounding, the harm not a side effect but the response landing on the body it stands in.

## Echo

A host failing of its own defence, the invader all but gone.
The echo of ravaging is tissue destroyed by the force raised to save it, so the body can fail with the pathogen nearly cleared, the cause of the ruin the guard and not the threat, the same weapons that beat the infection leaving the ground they defended wrecked behind them.
