# Place: The Bloodstream

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md). The channel that carries the signals and the reinforcements, where the summons travels and the response feeds itself, so a local defence becomes a body-wide storm.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A system of supply, carrying the guard where it is needed.
Shown at the surface is logistics working: the signals that call for reinforcement moving fast through the body, the defenders they summon riding the same current to the fight, a network delivering the response wherever the threat is read.

## Holds

Holds the feedback, the signal that calls the signal.
The Bloodstream is where each summons travels and where the answering wave sends its own summons in turn, so it holds the loop that amplifies the defence: a channel that carries not just force to the site but the call for more force everywhere, turning a response raised in one place into a rising tide through the whole body.

## Offers

Offers reinforcement to every part, and the storm to every part with it.
Here the summons reaches the whole system, so the Bloodstream offers a defence that can concentrate wherever it is needed, and offers, through the same channel, the amplification that carries the response past its cause: the signal that calls help also raises the level of help called, so the current that supplies the guard is the current that lets it feed itself, spreading protection and storm alike to every tissue the blood reaches.

## Withheld

Withholds the brake the feedback lacks, and the bound the spread would need.
What the Bloodstream cannot offer is a governor on its own loop: the channel that carries the call for more force carries no signal fast enough to say enough, so the amplification runs without a native limit and the response spreads body-wide before any local end is reached. The place that carries the defence carries no check on it, present with every summons and absent the counter-summons that would hold it, so a fight that began in one tissue becomes a storm through all of them, the same current that made the guard strong making it uncontainable.
