# Position: The Response

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md). The defending seat, which scales the immune force to the detected threat, able to raise as much force as a danger warrants and unable to aim that force so it spares the host from the pathogen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The full force of the defence, and the power to scale it to the threat.
The seat holds the whole armoury of the response, the inflammation, the cell-killing, the summoning signals, and the authority to raise them in proportion to what it detects, meeting a large threat with a large force.
Command of magnitude and none of aim, since the seat can set how much force is brought and holds no instrument to make that force fall on the invader alone and not the tissue it stands in.

## Orders

Meet the threat, and meet it in full.
Read the danger, scale the force to it, and summon each wave the last one calls for, since a threat under-met is a threat that wins, so the seat brings whatever the danger warrants and counts the bringing as the whole of its duty.
Judge the response by its size, since the seat's only measure is force against threat, so a larger response to a larger danger is a better defence, and holding force back reads only as leaving the body exposed.

## Loses

The aim, which the seat has no instrument to give its force.
The Response can set how much force to bring and cannot make that force selective, because its weapons act on host and pathogen alike, so no setting available to the seat defends without also damaging. What the position forfeits is any separation of protection from harm: the inflammation, the killing, and the signalling are single acts that strike whatever they reach, so the seat cannot raise the defence without raising the collateral in the same motion, and the more force a threat warrants the more tissue that force wrecks. No operation the seat can perform divides the two, since the harm is not a side effect of the response but the response itself falling on the body it defends, so the seat that meets a threat most fully is the seat that damages the host most, its every escalation a defence and a wound in one.

## Drives

Toward a force scaled perfectly to the threat and unaimed at everything but it,
and a defence whose fitness is its magnitude and whose magnitude is its harm,
so the seat that guards the body best is the seat that wounds it most, its command of how much force never a command of where the force lands, every right escalation striking the tissue with the same blow it strikes the invader.
