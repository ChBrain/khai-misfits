# Position: The Brake

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md). The damping seat, which would slow the response to spare the tissue, holding one lever, suppression, that acts on the whole defence at once, so it cannot lower the harm without lowering the protection.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The one lever that can slow the response, and the judgement of how far.
The seat holds suppression, the power to damp the defence, and the standing to apply it against the clock, reading the hour and the sign to decide how much.
A brake on the whole response and none on its parts, since the seat can lower the force but cannot lower only the harmful share of it, so what it holds acts on protection and damage together.

## Orders

Damp the response, and stop short of disarming it.
Slow the storm enough to spare the tissue and not so much that the pathogen wins, titrating between two failures by the hour, since the right amount cannot be known in advance and changes as the battle does.
Balance rather than cure, since the seat's only move trades one harm for a risk of the other, so it must hold the response at a point where the tissue is spared as far as it can be and the guard is left enough to clear the invader, a setting with no safety in it.

## Loses

The separation of harm from protection, which the seat's one lever cannot make.
The Brake can lower the response and cannot lower the damage without the defence, because suppression acts on the whole guard at once, so every measure that spares the tissue also blunts the clearing. What the position forfeits is a selective brake: there is no dose that damps the collateral and leaves the protection, since the harmful force and the protective force are the same force, so the seat spends the one only by spending the other. And it forfeits the timing too, since the right setting shifts with the battle and the sign that would fix it arrives late, so the seat must act before it can know and answer for the outcome either way. No operation available to the seat divides the response into a part to keep and a part to stop, because the division does not exist, so the seat that alone can halt the harm can halt it only by halting the help.

## Drives

Toward a damping that spares the tissue only by disarming the guard,
and a lever with no safe setting, too much losing the patient to the pathogen and too little to the response,
so the seat that alone can slow the harm is the seat that must weaken the protection to do it, its one brake acting on the whole defence and its every dose a trade paid on a knife-edge in the dark, no trial having yet found the brake that spares the host and leaves the defence (Angus and van der Poll).
