# Process: Amplifying

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md). The self-feeding of the response, the signal that raises the signal, the positive feedback that carries the defence past the threat that triggered it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Defender](persona_the_defender.md), at [the Bloodstream](place_the_bloodstream.md), each wave of the response summoning the next.
Begins whenever the defence's own signals call for more defence, and the answering force sends its own call in turn.

## Direction

Turns a proportioned response into one past proportion.
Because each summons draws reinforcements that summon in their turn, the response feeds on its own output, so amplifying converts a defence sized to the threat into a defence outgrowing it, a rising loop with no fast brake that carries the force beyond the danger it began against, the storm exceeding its cause (Tisoncik, Korth, Simmons, Farrar, Martin and Katze).

## Lever

The summoning that is also the amplifying.
Because the same signal that calls help raises the level of help called, amplifying works through the mechanism of reinforcement itself: there is no separate throttle, the call and the escalation are one act, so the lever is the feedback built into the summons, which makes the defence strong by the very process that makes it uncontainable, no native limit anywhere in the loop.

## Echo

A response body-wide and climbing, its cause outgrown.
The echo of amplifying is a defence that has spread through the bloodstream and risen past the threat that set it off, a storm carried to every tissue the current reaches, so the force now falling on the host is larger than the invader ever warranted, the guard grown beyond the danger and still climbing, with nothing quick enough to say enough.
