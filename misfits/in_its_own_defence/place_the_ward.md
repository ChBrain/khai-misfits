# Place: The Ward

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md). The bedside where the response is watched and weighed, where the one brake is held and dosed against the clock, and where the trade between the storm and the infection is paid.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Care at its most attentive, the patient watched and the numbers read.
Shown at the surface is medicine doing everything right: the signs tracked hour by hour, the trajectory charted, the one who would help standing over the bed with a remedy in hand, the whole apparatus of rescue arrayed around the failing body.

## Holds

Holds the brake, and the choice of how far to pull it.
The Ward is where the suppression is decided, so it holds the single lever that can slow the response and the judgement that must set it, the place the storm and the infection are held on one scale and a dose is chosen between them, by the hour and the sign.

## Offers

Offers a way to slow the harm, and no way to slow only the harm.
Here the response can at last be met with a counter-force, so the Ward offers the one intervention that reaches the defence, and offers it as a trade: the brake acts on the whole guard, so every measure that spares the tissue blunts the clearing, and the place that holds the remedy holds it inseparable from a disarming, a help that cannot be given without the harm it carries.

## Withheld

Withholds the safe dose, and the timing that would find it.
What the Ward cannot offer is a setting that only helps: no amount of suppression damps the collateral without damping the protection, and the right amount shifts with the battle while the sign that would fix it arrives late, so the place that holds the brake withholds any certainty in its use. The remedy is present and the safe way to give it is not, so the bedside offers a choice that must be made before it can be known, every dose a call answered for either way, the one place that can act on the defence unable to act on it cleanly.
