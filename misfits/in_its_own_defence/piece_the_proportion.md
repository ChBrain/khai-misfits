# Piece: The Proportion

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md). The rule that scales the force to the threat, more invader met with more defence, the soundness that makes a large response a correct one and the reason it cannot stop short of the harm.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set at [the Tissue](place_the_tissue.md), where the force is sized to the threat it meets.
Flows as a defence raised to match its danger, the response climbing with the invader, its magnitude the measure of its fitness for the fight.

## Load Bearing

Without the Proportion the defence would be arbitrary, too little or too much by accident: it is the scaling of force to threat that makes the response correct, and the same scaling that drives it to a size that harms.
The Proportion is load bearing because it is the soundness at the root, the rule that a threat must be met in kind (Zinkernagel and Doherty). It is what makes the response right, since under-meeting a real danger loses the body, and it is exactly what forbids restraint, because holding force back from a large threat is failing it. So the Proportion carries the trap's first turn: the defence is correct insofar as it is large, so a large threat draws a large force by the very rule that makes the force fit, and the harm that force does is the price of its correctness, not a departure from it. Every wound the response inflicts begins in this rule, which asks for exactly as much force as the danger and cannot ask for less.

## Apparent

Just good defence, force matched to the threat it faces.
Seen at the surface, the Proportion looks like nothing but competence, a guard that brings what the danger warrants and no more, and the truth that matching the threat is what drives the response to a harmful size, that the rule making it correct is the rule that cannot stop it short of the tissue, that its rightness and its overshoot are the same measure, stays hidden behind how plainly proportioned force is the right kind.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the Defender](persona_the_defender.md) to match the threat, borne by [the Host](persona_the_host.md) as the force it cannot lower, and lowered by [the Clinician](persona_the_clinician.md) only against the clearing.
It is the rule at the root: the Proportion is what [mounting](process_mounting.md) enacts and [amplifying](process_amplifying.md) carries past itself, the scaling that puts [the collateral](piece_the_collateral.md) into every escalation, the soundness [the overkill](piece_the_overkill.md) is built from and [damping](process_damping.md) can only weaken.
