# Plot: The Ravage

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Tissue](place_the_tissue.md), the weapons meeting the body.
An amplified response whose inflammation, killing, and signalling fall on the host as on the pathogen.

## Action

[Ravaging](process_ravaging.md): the defence destroys the tissue it defends, faster and wider than the infection, the same weapons clearing the invader wrecking the ground.

## Stage

[The Defender](persona_the_defender.md) strikes from [the Response](position_the_response.md) while [the Host](persona_the_host.md) endures from [the Besieged](position_the_besieged.md) on [Endure the Siege](plan_endure_the_siege.md), the body bearing [the collateral](piece_the_collateral.md) of a force it cannot lower, [the overkill](piece_the_overkill.md) landing on the ground it was raised to save.
The invader is nearly cleared, and the host is failing all the same.

## Tension

The defence wins the fight and loses the body, and no weapon did anything but its work.
There is no malfunction: the inflammation, the killing, and the signalling are the response operating exactly as it operates, striking whatever they reach because they cannot be aimed. But the tissue is where the threat is, so the plot closes with the host wrecked by its own guard, the pathogen all but gone and the body failing of the force raised against it, the ruin dealt not by the enemy but by the defence, in the same place and the same blow that cleared the invader from it.
