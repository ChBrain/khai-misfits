# Plan: Meet the Threat

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Response](position_the_response.md), inside [In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Response](position_the_response.md)

## Direction

Read the threat and scale the force to it, meeting more invader with more defence and summoning each wave the last one calls for, since a danger under-met is a danger that wins (Medzhitov, Schneider and Soares). Bring the whole armoury the threat warrants, the inflammation, the cell-killing, the flood of signals, and judge the response by its size, because the seat's measure is force against threat and a lesser response is only the body left exposed. Escalate without hesitation and read the escalation as protection, since the weapons cannot be aimed to spare the tissue and holding them back would fail the danger, so the plan meets the threat in full and lets the harm fall where the force does.

## Orders

([The Defender](persona_the_defender.md) raises the response to match the threat, summons every reinforcement the danger calls for, and strikes with everything, sure that force is protection and blind to the tissue behind it.)

([The Tissue](place_the_tissue.md); through the [mounting](process_mounting.md) process, feeding the [amplifying](process_amplifying.md) it triggers; the Defender brings [the proportion](piece_the_proportion.md) the threat warrants and, with the same force, [the collateral](piece_the_collateral.md), driving [the overkill](piece_the_overkill.md) it cannot see.)
