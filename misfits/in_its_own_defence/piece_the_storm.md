# Piece: The Storm

## Taxonomy

[In Its Own Defence](play_in_its_own_defence.md). The self-feeding surge of the response, the signal raising the signal until the defence runs body-wide and past its cause, the amplification with no fast brake.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Rises through [the Bloodstream](place_the_bloodstream.md), carried wherever the current reaches.
Flows as a defence feeding on its own output, each summons drawing reinforcements that summon in turn, a level of force climbing through the whole body rather than settling at the site.

## Load Bearing

Without the Storm the response would stop where the threat did: it is the positive feedback that carries the defence past its cause, turning a proportioned force into an overwhelming one.
The Storm is load bearing because it is the amplification at the trap's centre, the loop in which the call for force is itself force calling (Tisoncik, Korth, Simmons, Farrar, Martin and Katze). It has no fast brake: the same signal that summons help raises the level of help summoned, so nothing in the loop is quick enough to say enough, and a defence sound at the start climbs body-wide, outrunning the checks the body does have before any local end is reached. This piece is what makes a correct response lethal, since without it the harm would be bounded by the threat and with it the harm outgrows the threat, the guard's own reinforcement mechanism the thing that carries it past the danger and into the body at large.

## Apparent

Just a vigorous defence, the guard rallying hard.
Seen at the surface, the Storm looks like strength, a response mustering everything and rising to the occasion, and the truth that it has outgrown its cause, that the rising is a loop with no brake, that the vigour is the very thing carrying the defence past the threat and into the tissue at large, stays hidden behind how much a surging guard resembles a winning one.

## Yearbook

Driven by [the Defender](persona_the_defender.md) through its own summons, spread across [the Host](persona_the_host.md) body-wide, and reached by [the Clinician](persona_the_clinician.md) only by suppressing the guard entire.
It is the amplifier: the Storm is what [amplifying](process_amplifying.md) is, the loop that carries [the proportion](piece_the_proportion.md) past itself and spreads [the collateral](piece_the_collateral.md) through every tissue, the surge [the overkill](piece_the_overkill.md) rides and [damping](process_damping.md) can only lower by lowering the whole response.
