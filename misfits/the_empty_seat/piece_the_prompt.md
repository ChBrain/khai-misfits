# Piece: The Prompt

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md). The aid's confident output, the recommendation the operator accepts, delivered the same way whether right or wrong and unmarked as to which.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Delivered at [the console](place_the_console.md), where the operator meets and accepts it.
Weighed apart only at [the cross-check](place_the_cross_check.md), where an independent read is held beside it.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to defer to, and the trust has no object to land on.
With it the deferring has its occasion: a plain, confident recommendation the operator can take, delivered identically whether it is the ninety-ninth right answer or the one wrong one. The Prompt is load bearing because it is the output the operator accepts (Skitka, Mosier, and Burdick): the confident answer carrying no mark of its own rare wrongness, so the piece carries the thing that is followed, the recommendation that looks the same on the day it is right and the day it is wrong, so that the trust which is correct all but once follows it the once too.

## Apparent

An answer, the plain and useful output of a system that knows its job.
Seen at the surface, the Prompt looks like help, a clear recommendation to act on, and the truth that it is delivered with the same confidence whether right or wrong, that it carries no signal of the rare occasion it should be doubted, that its very plainness invites the deference that follows it off the cliff, stays hidden behind how useful a confident, usually-right answer plainly is.

## Yearbook

Delivered by [the aid](persona_the_aid.md) through [deferring](process_deferring.md), the confident output the operator accepts.
It is the occasion of the trap: the Prompt is what [the reliability](piece_the_reliability.md) makes trusted and what [the override](piece_the_override.md) follows against the operator's own signal, the answer weighed only at the [cross-check](place_the_cross_check.md) and otherwise waved through into the [empty seat](piece_the_empty_seat.md).
