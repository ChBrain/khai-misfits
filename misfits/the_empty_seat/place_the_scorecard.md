# Place: The Scorecard

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md). Where the work is measured and rewarded, the metrics of throughput on which deference reads as productivity and vigilance as a drag.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the operator's work is counted, a dashboard of volume and speed where getting through the queue is the number that shows.
The Overseer reading the throughput, the fast deferring operator rising on it, the slow checking one marked as a bottleneck.

## Holds

The rewarding, and the price it puts on vigilance.
Here the work meets its measure, so the Scorecard holds the point where deference is paid and scrutiny is charged: a metric of speed and volume on which trusting the aid moves the number and checking it does not, so it is where the incentive prices the guard out, the throughput the aid was bought for always the figure on the board, the rare failure it would catch nowhere on it.

## Offers

A clear reward for the productivity the aid was bought to give.
Because the Scorecard counts speed and volume, it offers the operator a plain path to standing, defer and keep the work moving, so it hands out the recognition the reliable aid's throughput deserves, and hands it out in a form that rewards the deference and penalises the checking, teaching the operator that vigilance is a drag on a system that is almost always right.

## Withheld

Any line for the guard the seat was for.
The Scorecard can count everything the aid speeds, but it cannot count the once-in-a-hundred failure caught, because a failure prevented leaves no mark and a vigilance spent shows only as slowness, so it withholds the value of the backstop, letting it read as pure cost, and, all throughput and no catch, it withholds any figure that would make keeping the seat live look like anything but a drag.
