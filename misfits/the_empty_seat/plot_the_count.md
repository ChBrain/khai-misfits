# Plot: The Count

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Scorecard](place_the_scorecard.md), the work measured on how much is got through.
The fast deferring operator rising on the throughput, the slow checking one marked a bottleneck, the aid's reliability making the checking look like waste.

## Action

[Rewarding](process_rewarding.md): [The Overseer](persona_the_overseer.md), holding [the overseer's seat](position_overseer.md), rewards the deference and prices the vigilance as a drag.

## Stage

[The Overseer](persona_the_overseer.md), on [Count the Throughput](plan_count_the_throughput.md), reads the volume, promotes the hand that keeps the queue moving, and grudges the hand that slows to check a system that is almost always right.
The incentive teaches what the reliability already suggested, that deferring is productivity and scrutinising is drag, and locks the complacency in.

## Tension

Rewarding the productivity the aid was bought for is fair, and the reward prices out the guard the seat was for.
There is nothing to fault: the aid was installed for throughput and the checking really is almost always wasted. But a failure prevented leaves no mark and a vigilance spent shows only as slowness, so the plot closes with deference paid and scrutiny charged, the metric prizing the trust that empties the seat and penalising the vigilance that would keep it full.
