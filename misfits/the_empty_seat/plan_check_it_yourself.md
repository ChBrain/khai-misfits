# Plan: Check It Yourself

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the skeptic's seat](position_skeptic.md), inside [The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Skeptic](position_skeptic.md)

## Direction

Force independent judgement before the aid's output is accepted, and cross-check the aid against other evidence rather than confirming it. Build the work so the operator must read the case before seeing the machine's answer, and keep the vigilance exercised rather than eased away. Make the seat hold a judgement, not just a body.

## Orders

([The Skeptic](persona_the_skeptic.md) forces independent judgement and cross-checks the aid, at the cost of attention almost always wasted and the throughput slowed, against the reliability and the reward that lull the seat.)

## Implementation

([The Cross-Check](place_the_cross_check.md); the [checking](process_checking.md) process; independent judgement is formed against [the prompt](piece_the_prompt.md) and cross-checked past [the reliability](piece_the_reliability.md), keeping [the backstop](piece_the_backstop.md) live and turning [the empty seat](piece_the_empty_seat.md) back ... if the wasted-seeming vigilance can be paid for daily.)

## Targets

- [ ] independent judgement is formed before the aid's output is accepted
- [ ] the aid is cross-checked against other evidence, not merely confirmed
- [ ] the work is built so the operator evaluates rather than approves
- [ ] the vigilance is kept live and paid for daily, or waived as paranoia and the seat left empty
