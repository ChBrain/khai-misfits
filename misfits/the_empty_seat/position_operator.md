# Position: Operator

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md). The seat kept in the loop to catch the aid's rare failure, where earned trust erodes into deference and the backstop is hollowed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A place over the aid and a judgement it could apply: the standing to accept or override the aid's output and the skill to evaluate it.
A clear case: the aid is right far more often than any override would be, so trusting it is calibration, not laziness.
No way to tell the rare wrong output from the many right ones, and no reward for the vigilance that is almost always wasted.

## Orders

Follow the aid.
Defer to the reliable output, let the aid carry the work it does well, and do not hand-check a system that is almost always right.
Work with the machine, not against it, and take the ease it was installed to give.

## Loses

The independent judgement the seat was for, exactly by trusting the aid that earned it.
Because deferring to a reliable aid is rational and watching a thing that is always right teaches it is always right, the seat's judgement goes slack from disuse, so at the rare moment the aid is wrong it follows the aid past its own signal or misses what the aid did not flag, and the very trust that made it a good operator makes it a poor guard.
It gains the ease and the good numbers of working with a reliable aid, and loses the scrutiny it was kept in the loop to hold.

## Drives

Toward the aid followed and the watch gone slack,
bought against the judgement the seat was posted to keep,
so the seat kept to catch the rare failure is the seat that, taught to trust, follows it.
