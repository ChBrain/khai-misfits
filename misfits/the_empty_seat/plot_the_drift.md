# Plot: The Drift

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Console](place_the_console.md), the aid being right day after day and the watch finding nothing to do.
The operator deferring, the outputs almost always correct, the vigilance kept over a thing that almost never needs it.

## Action

[Coasting](process_coasting.md): [The Operator](persona_the_operator.md), holding [the operator's seat](position_operator.md), lets active monitoring lapse, accepting outputs rather than evaluating them.

## Stage

[The Operator](persona_the_operator.md), on [Follow the Aid](plan_follow_the_aid.md), watches an aid that keeps being right until watching it teaches it is always right, and their independent judgement, unexercised, goes slack.
Outputs come to be accepted rather than read, and the not-flagging of the aid comes to mean all-clear, the scrutiny the seat was for dissolving into confirmation.

## Tension

Attention lapsing over a thing that almost never needs it is not negligence, and the lapse is what leaves the silent failure unseen.
There is nothing to fault: a reliable aid genuinely gives the watch almost nothing to catch. But vigilance eased away cannot catch what it no longer looks for, so the plot closes with the operator's judgement gone slack, the watch quiet, and the aid's next silent failure lined up to pass an attention that has learned to rest.
