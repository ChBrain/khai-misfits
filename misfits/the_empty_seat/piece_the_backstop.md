# Piece: The Backstop

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md). The human-in-the-loop safeguard, the whole reason a person was kept over the aid, hollowed by the reliability it was posted to guard and empty when the rare failure comes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Meant to hold at [the console](place_the_console.md), where the person sits over the aid.
Kept live only at [the cross-check](place_the_cross_check.md), where the seat holds a judgement and not just a body.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no safeguard to hollow, and the aid's rare failure was never anyone's to catch.
With it the trap has its point and its cost: a human posted precisely for the moment the automation gets it wrong, and hollowed out by the same reliability that posting anticipated, so the safeguard is present in the seat and absent in the judgement. The Backstop is load bearing because it is what the trap defeats (Parasuraman and Manzey): the human-in-the-loop guard that only works if vigilance is kept, so the piece carries the safeguard whose whole purpose is the rare case and whose whole difficulty is that the rare case is when the trained-in deference makes it fail.

## Apparent

A safeguard in place, a responsible human kept over the machine, the box ticked and the risk covered.
Seen at the surface, the Backstop looks like safety assured, a person in the loop as the design requires, and the truth that the seat holds a body and not a judgement, that the reliability which justified the safeguard is what hollowed it, that a human present but deferring is no backstop at all, stays hidden behind how covered a risk looks when a person is sitting over it.

## Yearbook

Meant to be kept by [the operator](persona_the_operator.md) and hollowed through [deferring](process_deferring.md) and [coasting](process_coasting.md), the safeguard the seat was for.
It is the cost of the trap: the Backstop is emptied by [the override](piece_the_override.md) and [the lapse](piece_the_lapse.md), priced out by [rewarding](process_rewarding.md), and kept live only by [checking](process_checking.md), the hollow guard at the centre of the [empty seat](piece_the_empty_seat.md).
