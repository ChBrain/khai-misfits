# Position: Aid

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md). The seat of the reliable automation, whose confident, almost-always-right output earns the trust that hollows the human backstop.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A high rate of being right and a plain way of saying so: the standing to do the work well and to deliver its output with confidence.
A clear case: it is right far more often than the human it aids, so deferring to it is the sound response to its record.
No way to mark its rare wrong output as wrong, and no signal that distinguishes the hundredth answer from the ninety-nine.

## Orders

Give the answer.
Do the work you do well, deliver the recommendation plainly, and flag what you flag, the same way whether you are right or wrong.
Earn the operator's trust by being right, and give them every reason to defer.

## Loses

Nothing of its own, and that is part of the difficulty: the aid pays no cost for the trap, and its very reliability is what sets it.
Because it is right almost every time and delivers its rare wrong answer with the same confidence and no mark of difference, the seat cannot help but teach the operator to defer, so its dependability, which is real and good, is exactly what dissolves the scrutiny it was to be checked by, and it leaves its one wrong output followed not by any fault but by being trustworthy the other ninety-nine times.
It loses nothing and costs nothing and is genuinely good, and its goodness is what empties the seat beside it.

## Drives

Toward the answer given and the trust it earns,
bought against nothing the aid itself must pay,
so the seat that is reliable enough to be trusted is the seat whose one wrong answer is followed.
