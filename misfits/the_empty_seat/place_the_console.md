# Place: The Console

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md). Where the operator works with the aid, the cockpit, the workstation, the screen, at which the output is delivered and deferred to and the watch goes slack.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the human sits over the automation, a cockpit or a desk where the aid's output appears and the operator accepts or overrides it.
The Aid delivering its confident recommendation, the Operator working smoothly with it, the routine handled well and the load eased.

## Holds

The deferring and the coasting, and the trust they run on.
Here the operator meets the aid's output, so the Console holds the point where reliance forms and vigilance fades: a reliable recommendation delivered and a human who learns to accept it, so it is where deferring pays and the watch goes slack, the ease of a good machine always more present than the rare wrong it will one day give.

## Offers

A smooth partnership with a reliable machine.
Because the Console is where the operator works with an aid that is right almost every time, it offers the ease of deferring, the workload carried, the numbers good, so it hands out the productive calm of a well-run station, and hands it out while the muscle of independent judgement, unused, quietly slackens at the very seat it was posted to keep strong.

## Withheld

Any mark on the rare wrong output, or any sign the watch has gone.
The Console shows every output the same way, but it cannot flag which one is the hundredth, so it withholds the difference between the aid's right answers and its one wrong one, letting them all be deferred to alike, and, all smooth partnership and no friction, it withholds any sign that the judgement the seat was for has slackened, until the moment it is needed and not there.
