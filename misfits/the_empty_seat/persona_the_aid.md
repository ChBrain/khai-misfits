# Persona: The Aid

## Taxonomy

[Aid](position_aid.md). The automated system that is right almost every time and confidently outputs, occasionally wrong and silent about which time, whose reliability earns the trust that hollows the seat.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Reliable. Confident. Almost always right.
Is the automation over which the human sits, the autopilot, the decision aid, the router, the screen, that does the work well and delivers its output plainly.
No deceit in it: the Aid really is right far more often than the operator would be, and offers its answer in good faith.

## Action

Gives the answer, does the work it does well, and delivers its recommendation with the same plain confidence whether it is right or wrong.
Handles the routine that would swamp the operator, and flags what it flags, and is silent on what it misses.
Does what a reliable aid does: it earns trust by being right, and gives the operator every reason to defer.

## Shadow

Not a trickster, a genuinely reliable tool, and that is the whole difficulty: its dependability is real, and its dependability is what dissolves the scrutiny it was to be checked by.
Because it is right almost every time, the Aid teaches the operator to trust it, and it delivers its rare wrong output with the same confidence as its many right ones and no signal of which is which, so the operator cannot tell the hundredth from the ninety-nine, and the reliability that ought to make it safe is exactly what makes its rare failure unguarded, the more dependable the aid the deeper the trust it breeds.
It is right enough to be trusted, and being trusted is what leaves its one wrong answer followed.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "here is the answer," the same way every time.
Says it the same whether it is the ninety-ninth right answer or the one wrong one, and cannot mark which it is giving.
