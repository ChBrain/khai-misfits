# Piece: The Reliability

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md). The aid's high rate of being right, the real base rate that makes deferring rational and, in the same stroke, erodes the vigilance the seat was for.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Earned at [the console](place_the_console.md), where the aid is right time after time.
Priced into the metric at [the scorecard](place_the_scorecard.md), where its throughput is the number that shows.

## Load Bearing

Without it the aid would not be trusted, and the operator would keep checking as a matter of course.
With it the trap has its seductive engine: an aid right far more often than the human, so that deferring is calibration and overriding is usually the error and hand-checking is waste. The Reliability is load bearing because it is what makes the trust correct (Parasuraman and Riley): the genuine high base rate that ought to be relied upon, so the piece carries the very dependability that both earns the deference and dissolves the scrutiny, the good record that makes the rational response, reliance, into the thing that empties the seat.

## Apparent

Pure advantage, a dependable machine that does the work well, exactly what was wanted.
Seen at the surface, the Reliability looks like nothing but good, an aid that earns its keep by being right, and the truth that its dependability is what erodes the watch, that the more reliable the aid the deeper the complacency it breeds, that the record which makes trust correct is the record that leaves the rare failure unguarded, stays hidden behind how plainly a system that is almost always right is worth relying on.

## Yearbook

Earned by [the aid](persona_the_aid.md) through [deferring](process_deferring.md), the base rate that makes trust correct.
It is the seductive engine of the trap: the Reliability delivers [the prompt](piece_the_prompt.md) trusted and drives [the override](piece_the_override.md) and [the lapse](piece_the_lapse.md), hollowing [the backstop](piece_the_backstop.md), the good record at the root of the [empty seat](piece_the_empty_seat.md).
