# Persona: The Skeptic

## Taxonomy

[Skeptic](position_skeptic.md). Would keep the human truly in the loop, forcing independent judgement and cross-checking the aid, at the cost of attention almost always wasted and throughput slowed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Watchful. Disciplined. Unwilling to just confirm.
Is the one who refuses to let the operator become a rubber stamp, who builds the work so a judgement must be formed before the output is accepted.
No distrust of the aid for its own sake in it: only the knowledge that a backstop that only confirms is no backstop at all.

## Action

Forces independent judgement before the aid's output is accepted, and cross-checks the aid against other evidence rather than confirming it.
Designs the work so the operator must evaluate, not merely approve, and keeps the vigilance exercised rather than eased away.
Does what keeps a human genuinely in the loop: it makes the seat hold a judgement, not just a body.

## Shadow

Not a paranoid who mistrusts good tools, a keeper of the backstop, and that is the whole difficulty: keeping the vigilance live is the one thing that catches the rare failure, and it spends attention that is almost always wasted.
Because the aid is right almost every time, the independent judgement the Skeptic forces is, in the great majority of cases, effort thrown away, slowing the throughput the aid was installed to speed and distrusting a system that has earned trust, so the move that keeps the seat from emptying looks, on every ordinary day, like paranoia and waste, and pays off once in a hundred times and never on the day it is spent.
It keeps the guard the seat was for, and keeping it costs a vigilance whose worth almost never shows.

## Tell

Says "form your own read first, then look at what the machine says."
Says it, and is told they are slowing a system that is almost always right, because the check that would catch the rare failure looks, every other day, like wasted effort.
