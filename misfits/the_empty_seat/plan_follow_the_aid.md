# Plan: Follow the Aid

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the operator's seat](position_operator.md), inside [The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Operator](position_operator.md)

## Direction

Defer to the reliable output and let the aid carry the work it does well. Do not hand-check a system that is almost always right, do not override it when your own read differs, because it is usually you who is wrong, and take the ease it was installed to give. Work with the machine, not against it.

## Orders

([The Operator](persona_the_operator.md) defers to the aid's output, correctly calibrated to its high rate of being right, and lets the vigilance the seat was for go slack.)

## Implementation

([The Console](place_the_console.md); the [deferring](process_deferring.md) and [coasting](process_coasting.md) processes; the trust rests on [the reliability](piece_the_reliability.md) and accepts [the prompt](piece_the_prompt.md), so [the backstop](piece_the_backstop.md) hollows as the watch goes slack.)

## Targets

- [ ] the aid's output is deferred to, overriding it forgone as usually the error
- [ ] the reliable system is not hand-checked, the ease it was installed to give taken
- [ ] active monitoring lapses as a thing always right teaches it is always right
- [ ] the judgement the seat was for goes slack, the backstop hollowed
