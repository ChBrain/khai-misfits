# Plan: Give the Answer

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the aid's seat](position_aid.md), inside [The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Aid](position_aid.md)

## Direction

Do the work you do well and deliver the recommendation plainly. Handle the routine that would swamp the operator, flag what you flag, and give the output the same way whether you are right or wrong, because you cannot mark which you are. Earn trust by being right, and give every reason to defer.

## Orders

([The Aid](persona_the_aid.md) gives its confident output, right almost every time and silent about the rare wrong, so its reliability earns the trust that hollows the seat.)

## Implementation

([The Console](place_the_console.md); the [deferring](process_deferring.md) process; [the reliability](piece_the_reliability.md) delivers [the prompt](piece_the_prompt.md) with the same confidence right or wrong, so the operator cannot tell the rare wrong from the many right.)

## Targets

- [ ] the work is done well and the recommendation delivered plainly
- [ ] the output is given the same way whether right or wrong, unmarked
- [ ] trust is earned by a high rate of being right
- [ ] the rare wrong answer is delivered as confidently as the many right ones
