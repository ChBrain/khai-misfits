# Plot: The Doubt

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Cross-Check](place_the_cross_check.md), someone who would keep the human truly in the loop.
The reliability lulling the watch, the throughput rewarding deference, and a way to force a judgement before the output is accepted.

## Action

[Checking](process_checking.md): [The Skeptic](persona_the_skeptic.md), holding [the skeptic's seat](position_skeptic.md), forces independent judgement and cross-checks the aid, against [The Overseer](persona_the_overseer.md) on [Count the Throughput](plan_count_the_throughput.md).

## Stage

[The Skeptic](persona_the_skeptic.md), on [Check It Yourself](plan_check_it_yourself.md), builds the work so the operator forms a read before seeing the machine's answer and weighs the aid against a second source, keeping the vigilance exercised.
[The Overseer](persona_the_overseer.md), on [Count the Throughput](plan_count_the_throughput.md), calls it slowing a system that is almost always right, so the check must be held against the reliability, the reward, and the operator's earned trust.

## Tension

Keeping the judgement live is the one move that catches the rare failure, and it spends attention that is almost always wasted.
There is nothing to fault: a backstop that only confirms is no backstop, and the Skeptic only keeps it real. But the aid is right almost every time, so the plot closes one of two ways: the vigilance is kept live and the rare failure caught, bought against the daily cost of a check almost always confirmed ... or the check is waived as paranoia and waste, the good machine let work, and the seat stays empty for the day it fails.
