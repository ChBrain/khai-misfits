# Piece: The Lapse

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md). The error of omission, the operator no longer watching missing what the aid did not flag, the silent failure the slackened vigilance lets pass.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Falls at [the console](place_the_console.md), where the unflagged failure passes an operator no longer watching.
Caught only at [the cross-check](place_the_cross_check.md), where an exercised judgement still looks for what the aid missed.

## Load Bearing

Without it the aid's silent failures would be caught by a watching operator, and complacency would cost nothing.
With it the quiet half of the trap does its damage: the aid fails without flagging it, and the operator, whose active monitoring has slackened, does not see, because the not-flagging had come to mean all-clear. The Lapse is load bearing because it is the omission error complacency produces (Parasuraman and Manzey): the failure missed for want of a watch, so the piece carries the silent counterpart to the override, the aid's unsignalled wrong passing an attention that a reliable record taught to rest.

## Apparent

Nothing at all, a quiet shift with nothing flagged, a system running clean.
Seen at the surface, the Lapse does not appear: no alarm sounds and no output is wrong to follow, and the truth that a failure the aid did not flag has passed unseen, that the operator's watch had slackened to where no-flag read as all-clear, that the quiet was not safety but an unwatched fault, stays hidden behind how much a system that flags nothing resembles a system with nothing to flag.

## Yearbook

Falls to [the operator](persona_the_operator.md) through [coasting](process_coasting.md), the unflagged failure missed.
It is the quiet face of the trap: the Lapse is what the slackened watch lets pass, the omission twin of [the override](piece_the_override.md)'s commission, the failure [the reliability](piece_the_reliability.md) taught the operator not to look for, the seat empty by inattention in the [empty seat](piece_the_empty_seat.md).
