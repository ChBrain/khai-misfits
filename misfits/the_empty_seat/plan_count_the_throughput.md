# Plan: Count the Throughput

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the overseer's seat](position_overseer.md), inside [The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Overseer](position_overseer.md)

## Direction

Measure the operator on volume and speed and reward the one who keeps the work moving. Treat the one who slows to hand-check a reliable aid as a drag, and hold the work to the productivity the aid was bought to give. Take the aid's reliability as licence to price the checking as wasted time.

## Orders

([The Overseer](persona_the_overseer.md) counts the throughput and rewards the deferring operator, pricing vigilance as a drag, the incentive locking in the complacency the reliability breeds.)

## Implementation

([The Scorecard](place_the_scorecard.md); the [rewarding](process_rewarding.md) process; the metric prizes speed off [the reliability](piece_the_reliability.md) and penalises the checking, so [the backstop](piece_the_backstop.md) is priced out of the work.)

## Targets

- [ ] the operator is measured on volume and speed
- [ ] the deferring, fast operator is rewarded and the checking one treated as a drag
- [ ] vigilance is priced as wasted time, since on a reliable aid it almost always is
- [ ] the incentive locks in deference and prices out the guard the seat was for
