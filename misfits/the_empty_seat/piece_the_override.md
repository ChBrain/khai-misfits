# Piece: The Override

## Taxonomy

[The Empty Seat](play_the_empty_seat.md). The error of commission, the operator following the aid against their own contradicting signal, the trust that gives the aid the benefit of every doubt.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Springs at [the console](place_the_console.md), where the operator follows the aid past their own instruments.
Averted only at [the cross-check](place_the_cross_check.md), where the independent read is given standing against the aid.

## Load Bearing

Without it the operator's own signal would still catch the aid's rare wrong, and the trust would have a floor.
With it the trap sprung: when the aid is wrong and the operator's own instruments say so, the operator follows the aid anyway, because the aid has earned the benefit of the doubt and the operator's own read is the thing that has usually been mistaken. The Override is load bearing because it is the commission error the trust produces (Skitka, Mosier, and Burdick): the active following of the aid over one's own contradicting judgement, so the piece carries the moment deference beats evidence, the operator overriding themselves in favour of the machine exactly when the machine is wrong.

## Apparent

Consistency, the plain good practice of not chopping and changing, of trusting the system you were given.
Seen at the surface, the Override looks like discipline, an operator who does not second-guess a reliable aid on a whim, and the truth that they have set aside their own correct signal in favour of the machine's wrong one, that the benefit of the doubt has been given to the very output that did not deserve it, that following the aid here was following it off the cliff, stays hidden behind how sensible not overriding a reliable system usually is.

## Yearbook

Sprung on [the operator](persona_the_operator.md) through [deferring](process_deferring.md), the aid followed against one's own signal.
It is the loud face of the trap: the Override follows [the prompt](piece_the_prompt.md) past the operator's own read, the commission twin of [the lapse](piece_the_lapse.md)'s omission, the moment [the backstop](piece_the_backstop.md) fails by acting, the seat filled by a body deferring in the [empty seat](piece_the_empty_seat.md).
