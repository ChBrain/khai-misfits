# Process: Shedding

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md). Run by [the Sink](persona_the_sink.md) on [Shed the Heat](plan_shed_the_heat.md), at [the Vessel](place_the_vessel.md), inside [The Standoff](plot_the_standoff.md) below the point and [The Turn](plot_the_turn.md) past it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Any temperature difference between the system and its surroundings, however small. Shedding starts the instant the system runs warmer than the air around it, and needs nothing else to begin.

## Direction

Toward more heat carried away the hotter the system runs, but only in plain proportion to the difference (Semenov): twice the temperature gap moves twice the heat, never more. Below [the Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md) this outpaces [Generating](process_generating.md) and holds [the Balance](piece_the_balance.md); past it, the same proportional carrying continues at its full linear best and is simply outrun.

## Lever

Heat crossing to the surroundings in plain proportion to the temperature difference, the ordinary physics of conduction, convection, and radiation near equilibrium (Semenov). Faithful and never failing: Shedding does not weaken at the point, it is out-climbed by a curve a straight line was never going to catch once the gap closed.

## Echo

[The Loss](piece_the_loss.md), the straight line on [the Diagram](place_the_diagram.md) that [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) reads against the generation curve: ahead for years, and then, at a single point, no longer.
