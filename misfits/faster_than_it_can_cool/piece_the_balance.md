# Piece: The Balance

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md). The stable operating point: the temperature at which loss outpaces generation so that any small warming is met by a larger cooling and erased, genuinely and provably steady, passing every check made on it at every instant.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Held inside [The Vessel](place_the_vessel.md) as the temperature the system settles to and returns to, and marked on [The Diagram](place_the_diagram.md) as the lower of the two points where the loss line crosses the generation curve.

## Load Bearing

It is load bearing as the proof that the system is not merely appearing safe but is safe: at the low intersection of the loss line and the generation curve, loss rises faster with temperature than generation does, so a perturbation upward is met by an excess of cooling over heating and the system is pulled back, and a perturbation downward is met by an excess of heating over cooling and the system is pushed back up, a true attractor in both directions (Semenov). This is the finding to hold exactly and not extend: the stability is real for as long as the crossing exists in this configuration, not a thin margin or a lucky reading, and every instantaneous test performed on the system at this point returns a pass, because the test is honest and the point is genuinely stable. It is not a claim that the point stays stable forever, only that it is stable now, at this loss and this generation; what can change the configuration underneath it belongs to drift, not to the balance itself.

## Apparent

Reads as safety with room to spare: a system that corrects itself, tested and found sound, no sign anywhere in its behaviour of what waits down the curve. It looks perfectly safe because, until the threshold, it is, and that is exactly what gives it no seam by which to be caught early.

## Yearbook

Held by [The Cell](persona_the_cell.md) through [Settle Back](plan_settle_back.md), formed each time [The Generation](piece_the_generation.md) is checked against [The Loss](piece_the_loss.md) and the line is still found ahead. Its steadiness is what [The Standoff](plot_the_standoff.md) stages, and it is the very point whose margin [The Drift](piece_the_drift.md) narrows and whose distance to [The Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md) is the whole of what [The Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) works to hold on [Hold the Margin](plan_hold_the_margin.md).
