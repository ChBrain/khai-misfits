# Process: Diverging

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md). No persona owns it, the outcome of the sign flip at [the Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md) rather than any actor's move, at [the Vessel](place_the_vessel.md), inside [The Turn](plot_the_turn.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md) crossed, by [Drifting](process_drifting.md) alone and not by any shock: the tangency reached, the stable operating point and its unstable neighbour merging and vanishing, the sign of the net feedback flipping from settling to amplifying at that single temperature.

## Direction

Exponential divergence to destruction. Past the point, [Generating](process_generating.md) outruns [Shedding](process_shedding.md) at every temperature above it, so each degree gained makes more heat than it sheds, accelerating without bound toward venting, fire, or fracture, whatever the vessel's particular failure is (Semenov; Frank-Kamenetskii; Feng and colleagues).

## Lever

The asymmetry finally unopposed: [the Sink](persona_the_sink.md) works at its full linear best the entire time, never slackening, simply outpaced by a curve a straight line cannot climb with once no equilibrium remains anywhere above to catch the temperature.

## Echo

[The Runaway](piece_the_runaway.md): destruction in seconds from a reading that an hour before sat in the same narrow band it always had. Past this point [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) has nothing left to hold, only [the Vessel](place_the_vessel.md)'s containment of a divergence already underway.
