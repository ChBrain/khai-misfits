# Place: The Vessel

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md). The physical arena where heat is made and shed, host to [The Standoff](plot_the_standoff.md) and [The Turn](plot_the_turn.md), where [the Cell](persona_the_cell.md) holds its operating point and where, past the point, the runaway physically happens.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A system running at a steady, unremarkable temperature: a casing warm to the hand and no warmer, a reading that has sat in the same narrow band for years. Nothing here asks to be watched closely.

## Holds

[The Balance](piece_the_balance.md), for as long as [the Sink](persona_the_sink.md)'s loss outpaces [the Reaction](persona_the_reaction.md)'s generation, and, past the critical point, [the Runaway](piece_the_runaway.md), the same casing accelerating from the same reading it held an hour before.

## Offers

The evidence of eyes and instruments: an operating temperature that, on any single reading, is indistinguishable from safe, because for the whole of the system's life it is exactly that.

## Withheld

Any difference between the hour before the runaway and the years of stable running that came before it. The Vessel gives no local sign that the margin has closed, because at the level of a single reading none has: what changed sits in the [Drifting](process_drifting.md) that carried [the Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md) down to meet the operating temperature, not in the temperature itself.
