# Plot: The Creep

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

On [The Rating](place_the_rating.md), the system is simply ageing: a datasheet still printed with the numbers that were true when it was set, and a life of ordinary use quietly running underneath them.

## Action

[Drifting](process_drifting.md) narrows the margin, owned by no persona because ageing is what happens to a system rather than what anyone does to it. A resistance rises with use, a cooling path fouls with dust, an ambient temperature climbs a few degrees across a season: each lifts [the Generation](piece_the_generation.md) curve a little or lowers [the Loss](piece_the_loss.md) line a little, and the gap that made [the Balance](piece_the_balance.md) safe closes by that much. What moves is [the Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md) itself, carried down toward the operating temperature the Vessel has held all along.

## Stage

None of it is damage in the sense of a crack or a flaw. The resistance that rose is still within its own spec; the fins that fouled still pass a cooling check; the ambient that climbed is still within the room's ordinary range. Each reading, taken alone on the day it is taken, is correct, and the Rating goes on saying the system sits well inside its limits, telling the truth on the day it is read. What it cannot show is that the true margin behind the printed number has gotten smaller since the number was set.

## Tension

The margin a shock never touched is spent by time alone. Nothing wears through and nothing accumulates as a flaw: the system stays fully sound in every part that can be checked, and only the point has moved, drifting quietly down the years toward the balance it once sat safely below. Nothing here yet looks wrong, because at the level of any single reading nothing is.
