# Process: Drifting

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md). No persona owns it, since it is what ordinary ageing becomes once summed across a system's life, at [the Rating](place_the_rating.md) and [the Vessel](place_the_vessel.md), inside [The Creep](plot_the_creep.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Nothing struck and nothing failed: an internal resistance rising with use, a cooling path fouling with dust, an ambient temperature climbing a few degrees across a season, the ordinary things that happen to ordinary systems over ordinary time.

## Direction

Toward the margin closed. Each of these lifts [the Generation](piece_the_generation.md) curve a little or lowers [the Loss](piece_the_loss.md) line a little, narrowing the gap between them on [the Diagram](place_the_diagram.md), and because the curve is exponential and the line is straight, the narrowing does not slow as it approaches, it closes to a single point of contact, carrying [the Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md) down to meet [the Balance](piece_the_balance.md) (Feng, Ouyang, and colleagues).

## Lever

Ordinary ageing, not damage and not a shock: nothing wears through, nothing accumulates as a flaw, the system stays sound in every part it can be checked. What moves is not the system's condition but the point itself, drifting down toward an operating temperature that never changed.

## Echo

A rating on [the Rating](place_the_rating.md) that reads the same as the day it was set, describing a margin that, underneath the unchanged number, has been getting quietly smaller the entire time.
