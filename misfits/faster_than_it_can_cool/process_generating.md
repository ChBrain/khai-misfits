# Process: Generating

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md). Run by [the Reaction](persona_the_reaction.md) on [Follow the Rate](plan_follow_the_rate.md), at [the Vessel](place_the_vessel.md), inside [The Standoff](plot_the_standoff.md) below the point and [The Turn](plot_the_turn.md) past it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The system's own temperature, nothing more. Wherever heat sits above rest, [the Reaction](persona_the_reaction.md)'s rate law is already running, so Generating needs no trigger to begin: it never stopped.

## Direction

Toward more heat the hotter the system already is: the rate rises exponentially with temperature, so each degree gained buys a larger next increment than the one before it (Semenov; Feng, Ouyang, and colleagues). Below [the Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md), [Shedding](process_shedding.md) carries away everything Generating makes and the climb goes nowhere; past it, the same climb, unchanged in shape, outruns the loss and feeds [the Runaway](piece_the_runaway.md).

## Lever

The exponential dependence of reaction rate on temperature, correct chemistry and not a fault of the system built around it (Semenov; Feng and colleagues); a lithium-ion cell self-heating above its onset temperature follows the identical law. Generating does not accelerate at the point, it was always this shape, and the point is only where the shape finally wins.

## Echo

[The Generation](piece_the_generation.md), climbing the same curve it always has, laid against [the Loss](piece_the_loss.md) on [the Diagram](place_the_diagram.md): out-shed for the whole ordinary range, and, once the margin is spent, the curve that finally shows what it was always going to do.
