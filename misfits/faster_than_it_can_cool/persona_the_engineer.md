# Persona: The Engineer

## Taxonomy

[Engineer](position_engineer.md). The one who sets and holds the margin, derating the operating point below the critical threshold at a real cost, and who cannot abolish the asymmetry or stop the point from drifting closer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Correct. Building in the distance.
Is the one who read the curves, computed the critical point, and set the operating temperature well below it, who added the cooling and fitted the vent and derated the system to buy the margin.
No negligence in it: only a person doing exactly the right thing, holding the operating point far from the threshold and paying, in capacity given up, for every degree of distance.

## Action

Holds the margin, on [Hold the Margin](plan_hold_the_margin.md), reading [the Diagram](place_the_diagram.md) and derating the system to sit far below [the Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md).
Fights [Drifting](process_drifting.md) with cooling and derating and monitoring, buying time against [the Drift](piece_the_drift.md) that narrows the margin over the system's life.
Does what a good engineer does with an exponential source: keeps the operating point a long way below the point, at the price of the capability spent to keep it there, and watches the threshold come down anyway.

## Shadow

Not a rescuer with a costless fix, a person paying a permanent tax against an asymmetry that cannot be paid off, and that is the difficulty: holding the margin buys time and never buys safety.
Because an exponential source will always eventually out-climb a linear sink, the Engineer can only set the operating point below a threshold that ageing keeps lowering toward it, so the margin is capacity spent to hold a distance the drift is always closing, and past the point there is no correction at all, only the containment of a runaway already begun.
It does everything right, and doing everything right holds the point off for a while and never removes it.

## Tell

Says "we have plenty of margin," and is telling the truth on the day, and cannot see how much of it the years will quietly spend.
Reads the derating as safety bought, when what it bought is time: a threshold held at arm's length, never abolished, drifting a little closer with every ordinary cycle of an ageing the margin was never able to stop.
