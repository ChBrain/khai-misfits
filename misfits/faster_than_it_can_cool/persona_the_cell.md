# Persona: The Cell

## Taxonomy

[Cell](position_cell.md). The sound system that makes heat and sheds it, genuinely stable for the whole of its life and carried by ordinary ageing to the one point past which its own steadying feedback runs it away, the self the trap destroys.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Cool. Stable. Well within its limits.
Is the system that has run at the same temperature for years, self-correcting whenever it warms, its margin wide and its checks all passing, a battery cell or a reactor charge that has given no trouble and shows none.
No fragility in it: only a system that is truly, provably steady, which is exactly why nothing about it warns of the point waiting at the end of its curve.

## Action

Settles back, on [Settle Back](plan_settle_back.md), erasing every small warming because the loss grows to meet the gain and returns it to rest.
Holds [the Balance](piece_the_balance.md) in [the Vessel](place_the_vessel.md), stable while [the Sink](persona_the_sink.md) sheds heat faster than [the Reaction](persona_the_reaction.md) makes it, and passes every instantaneous check exactly as a stable system should.
Does what a sound system does across its life: runs at its operating point, corrects the ordinary warmings, and gives no sign, because there is genuinely no sign to give.

## Shadow

Not a fragile system waiting to fail, a genuinely stable one being carried to a point, and that is the whole difficulty: nothing about it is wrong, and it is doomed anyway.
Because its stability comes from a loss that outpaces its generation, and ordinary drift narrows the gap between them until the loss can no longer outpace it, the Cell is stable right up to the tangency and unstable a degree past it, its own settling feedback reversing sign at the point that ordinary time alone brought down to meet it.
It corrects every warming faithfully, and correcting every warming is exactly what it can no longer do the instant the point is crossed.

## Tell

Runs at the same steady temperature it always has, days before the runaway, and reads as perfectly healthy because it is.
Cannot show, from where it sits, that the point it is drifting toward has come down to meet it, that the margin was quietly spent by ageing, and that the next ordinary warming will be the first it cannot settle.
