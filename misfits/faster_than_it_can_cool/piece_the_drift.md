# Piece: The Drift

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md). Ordinary ageing that narrows the margin: a resistance rising, a cooling path fouling, an ambient climbing, over the ordinary years of ordinary use, no shock in it and no damage, the system staying sound while the critical point alone is carried down toward the operating temperature.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Accrues across the life of [The Vessel](place_the_vessel.md) and is read off in [The Rating](place_the_rating.md), where the numbers that were true when the system was built go quietly stale as the years pass.

## Load Bearing

It is load bearing as the mechanism that closes the margin, bringing the threshold down toward the operating temperature: ageing in a real cell raises internal resistance and shifts the reaction pathways available at a given temperature, and this measurably lowers the onset temperature at which self-heating begins, narrowing the margin between normal operation and the characteristic temperatures that mark runaway (Feng, Ouyang, and colleagues). What must be held exactly here, and not blurred: this is not a hidden tally of accumulating micro-damage revealed only under a rare load, and it carries no flaw, no crack, no weakened fibre waiting to be found; nor is it slack being removed so an ordinary shock becomes fatal, there is no shock here and the load never changes. The part stays sound the entire time. What has moved is a parameter, a slope on the loss line or the onset on the generation curve, and with it the tangency has descended toward the operating temperature the system has held all along; the operating point never climbed, the threshold came down to meet it.

## Apparent

Reads as nothing at all, which is the point: no crack to inspect, no fault code to trip, a rating sheet that still says everything is within spec because each individual reading, taken alone, is. The apparent story is a system unchanged; the true story is a threshold approaching at the same ordinary pace as the years.

## Yearbook

Produced by no persona and by [Drifting](process_drifting.md) alone, the process without a wielder, since ageing is what happens to a system rather than what anyone does to it. It erodes [The Rating](place_the_rating.md) and narrows the gap that [The Balance](piece_the_balance.md) sits inside, bringing [The Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md) down toward the operating temperature across the whole of [The Creep](plot_the_creep.md), the one movement [The Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) can slow on [Hold the Margin](plan_hold_the_margin.md) but never stop.
