# Place: The Rating

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md). The safe operating limits [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) sets and holds, host to [The Creep](plot_the_creep.md) and [The Derating](plot_the_derating.md), where the numbers age quietly out from under the system they describe.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A datasheet, a spec sheet, a number: maximum operating temperature, rated cycle life, a margin stated once and printed as though it were fixed. What is visible is confidence, expressed as a figure.

## Holds

The derated operating point [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) chose, set below [the Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md) at the cost of capacity given up to buy the distance, and beside it, unlisted, the true margin that [Drifting](process_drifting.md) narrows a little further every ordinary day the system runs.

## Offers

A promise of safe distance from the point: the number on the page, read on the day it is read, says the system sits well inside its limits, and on the day it was written, it was telling the truth.

## Withheld

Its own erosion. The Rating cannot show that the true margin behind the printed number is smaller today than the day it was set, because the drift that spends it, a resistance rising, a cooling path fouling, an ambient climbing, leaves no mark on the number itself: the page stays the same while what it describes goes quietly stale.
