# Position: Engineer

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md). The seat that sets and holds the margin, buying distance from the critical point at a real and permanent cost, and unable to abolish the asymmetry or halt the drift.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The margin and the standing to set it: the derating that places the operating point far below the threshold, the cooling added, the vent fitted, the distance built in.
A hard case: every setting works and every setting costs, the capacity given up to buy the distance real, so the seat holds the only safety there is and no version of it that is free.
No power over the two laws, only over where the operating point sits between them, and so the seat can hold the point off but never remove it, and never stop it drifting closer.

## Orders

Hold the margin, and pay for it.
Set the operating point far below the critical threshold, add cooling, fit a vent, monitor the drift, because distance from the point is the whole of the safety and it must be bought.
Do it knowing the margin buys time and not immunity, that an exponential source will always eventually out-climb a linear sink, and that the point will keep coming down as the system ages however well the margin is held.

## Loses

The capacity spent to hold the distance, and the hope that the distance could ever be made permanent.
Because the safety is only ever the operating point sitting below a threshold that drift keeps lowering, the seat pays in capability for a margin the ageing is always closing, so what it loses is real performance given up for time, and past the point even that buys nothing, only the containment of a runaway underway.
It surrenders capacity on purpose to hold the point off, and holding the point off is a tax with no final payment, owed again every year the system ages.

## Drives

Toward a margin held at a permanent cost against a point that never stops approaching,
the operating temperature kept low and the threshold drifting down to meet it anyway,
so the seat that does everything right buys the system years and never safety, and cannot, by any correct move, remove the point its own ageing carries it toward.
