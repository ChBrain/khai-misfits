# Plot: The Standoff

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

At [The Vessel](place_the_vessel.md), a system running at a steady, unremarkable temperature: a casing warm to the hand and no warmer, a reading that has sat in the same narrow band for years and asks no one to watch it closely.

## Action

[Generating](process_generating.md) and [Shedding](process_shedding.md) stand matched, the loss just ahead: [the Sink](persona_the_sink.md), on [Shed the Heat](plan_shed_the_heat.md), carries warmth away faster than [the Reaction](persona_the_reaction.md), on [Follow the Rate](plan_follow_the_rate.md), can make it. [The Cell](persona_the_cell.md), on [Settle Back](plan_settle_back.md), erases every small warming the moment it appears, letting the loss grow to meet the gain and return the system to rest, and in doing so holds [the Balance](piece_the_balance.md).

## Stage

Nudge the temperature up a degree and it comes back down, every time, because below the operating point the loss line always outclimbs the generation curve by a widening margin. Nudge it down and it climbs back, for the same reason worked in reverse. Every instantaneous check made on the Cell, at every instant, returns a pass, and the pass is honest: this is not a system merely appearing safe, it is safe. Nothing in the Vessel behaves as though a curve is bending upward somewhere out past the range anyone is looking at.

## Tension

The standoff is genuine and provable, not a lucky reading and not a thin margin held by nerve. That is exactly the trap a merely fragile system would not set: fragility shows itself, and this shows nothing, the gap between loss and generation wide and comfortable for the whole of the ordinary range the Vessel has ever run in. Nothing here gives any sign of what waits, hard and computable, at the end of the curve.
