# Persona: The Reaction

## Taxonomy

[Reaction](position_reaction.md). The exothermic source inside the system, making heat at a rate that rises exponentially with temperature, faster the hotter it gets, obeying its rate law and nothing else.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady. Lawful. Warming the system as it always has.
Is the reaction that runs quietly at the operating point, producing the ordinary heat of ordinary work, a current drawn, a decomposition proceeding, a process doing exactly what it is meant to do.
No malice in it: only a rate that depends on temperature the way chemistry always does, exponentially, which is not a flaw but the plain form of the law it follows.

## Action

Follows the rate, on [Follow the Rate](plan_follow_the_rate.md), generating heat through [Generating](process_generating.md) at a pace that climbs with temperature.
Lays down [the Generation](piece_the_generation.md) in [the Vessel](place_the_vessel.md), matched below the point by [the Sink](persona_the_sink.md)'s faster-growing loss and, past the point, outrunning it.
Does what an exothermic reaction does: makes more heat when it is hotter, and hotter when it makes more heat, a curve that bends upward and never stops bending.

## Shadow

Not an explosion waiting to happen, a rate law being obeyed, and that is the difficulty: the exponential climb that runs the system away is the same lawful warming that ran it safely for years.
Because its generation rises faster with temperature than any linear loss can, the Reaction is out-shed for the whole ordinary range and out-sheds the cooling the instant the curve catches the line, so nothing about it changes at the point except that it finally wins a race it was always, by its shape, going to win if the gap ever closed.
It only ever makes heat by its law, and making heat by its law is the whole of the runaway.

## Tell

Warms the system at the operating point exactly as it did on the first day, giving the same reading it always gave.
Cannot be caught doing anything new at the moment of the turn, because it does nothing new: it climbs its exponential exactly as before, and only the closing of the margin, not any change in the Reaction, decides whether that climb is settled or runs away.
