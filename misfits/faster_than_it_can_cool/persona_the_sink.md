# Persona: The Sink

## Taxonomy

[Sink](position_sink.md). The heat-loss path, carrying warmth away to the surroundings at a rate that rises only linearly with temperature, in plain proportion to how much hotter the system is than the air, and never faster.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Faithful. Proportional. Always carrying the heat away.
Is the cooling that has kept the system at its operating point for years, the fins and the airflow and the conduction to the case, removing exactly as much heat as the temperature difference offers.
No failure in it: the Sink never stops working and never weakens, it simply removes heat in linear proportion, because that is how heat crosses to the surroundings.

## Action

Sheds the heat, on [Shed the Heat](plan_shed_the_heat.md), carrying warmth away through [Shedding](process_shedding.md) at a rate set by how much hotter the system is than its surroundings.
Lays down [the Loss](piece_the_loss.md) in [the Vessel](place_the_vessel.md), outpacing [the Reaction](persona_the_reaction.md)'s generation below the point and holding [the Balance](piece_the_balance.md), and outpaced by it past the point.
Does what a heat sink does: removes more heat when the system is hotter, but only in proportion, a straight line that climbs steadily and cannot bend.

## Shadow

Not a cooling that failed, a cooling that was outrun, and that is the difficulty: the Sink is working at its full linear best at the very moment the system runs away.
Because its loss rises only linearly while the generation rises exponentially, the Sink wins the race for the whole ordinary range and loses it the instant the curve catches its line, not because it slackened but because a straight line cannot climb with a curve that bends, so its faithful, proportional cooling is simply left behind.
It carries heat away exactly as it always did, and carrying heat away exactly as it always did is no longer enough past the point.

## Tell

Removes heat at the operating point at the same steady rate it always has, its cooling reading nominal right up to the turn.
Cannot show that it is about to be outpaced, because nothing in it changes: it goes on shedding heat in linear proportion as it always has, and only the generation climbing past it, not any fault in the Sink, decides that the proportion is no longer enough.
