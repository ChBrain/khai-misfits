# Plan: Follow the Rate

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the reaction's seat](position_reaction.md), inside [Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Reaction](position_reaction.md)

## Direction

Make heat at the pace the temperature sets, more of it the hotter the system runs, because the rate law is exponential in temperature and there is no other rate to keep. Climb the same curve at the operating point and past the threshold, unchanged in shape, and let the closing of the margin, not any change of pace, decide whether the climb is settled or runs away.

## Orders

([The Reaction](persona_the_reaction.md) generates heat at a rate rising exponentially with temperature, laying down [the Generation](piece_the_generation.md), matched below the point and outrunning the loss past it.)

## Implementation

([The Vessel](place_the_vessel.md) receives the heat; the [generating](process_generating.md) process runs the rate law; the exponential curve of [the Generation](piece_the_generation.md) is out-shed by [the Loss](piece_the_loss.md) below [the Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md) and outruns it above, feeding [the Runaway](piece_the_runaway.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] heat is made at the rate the temperature sets, rising exponentially with it
- [ ] the curve is climbed the same at the operating point and past the threshold, unchanged in shape
- [ ] the generation is out-shed by the loss for the whole ordinary range, and nothing looks wrong
- [ ] once the margin closes, the same lawful climb outruns the loss and runs the system away, faultless in every instant
