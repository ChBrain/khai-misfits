# Piece: The Loss

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md). The linear heat sink: the rate at which the vessel sheds heat to its surroundings, rising only in plain proportion to the temperature difference, the straight line on the diagram that the curve is measured against and, eventually, climbs past.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Carried away from [The Vessel](place_the_vessel.md) across its boundary to the surrounding air or fixture, and read as the line on [The Diagram](place_the_diagram.md) against which the generation curve is set.

## Load Bearing

It is load bearing as the sink term of the asymmetry, the half of the comparison that never accelerates: heat loss to the surroundings follows Newton's law of cooling, proportional to the difference between the system's temperature and the ambient, so it is linear by the physics of heat transfer itself, not by approximation or convenience (Semenov). This is what makes the well-stirred model tractable and its threshold exact: a line has one slope everywhere, so wherever it sits above the generation curve the gap is the whole story, and wherever the curve catches it there is exactly one point of tangency, not a smear. The Loss never fails and never weakens on its own; it works at whatever strength its slope allows, at every temperature, right up through the point where it is finally outrun. What changes over the life of a real system is not this law but the value of the slope, a cooling path fouling or an ambient climbing, and that belongs to drift, not to this piece.

## Apparent

Reads as the safe, dependable side of the picture: cooling, doing its job, always there and always working, the reassuring half of the diagram. Because it is a straight line it looks like it could never be beaten by anything, when in fact a straight line loses to an exponential as a matter of course, given enough temperature.

## Yearbook

Wielded by [The Sink](persona_the_sink.md) through [Shedding](process_shedding.md), the process that carries heat out of the Vessel continuously. Set against [The Generation](piece_the_generation.md) it forms [The Balance](piece_the_balance.md) while it still sits ahead; lowered by ordinary years through [The Drift](piece_the_drift.md), it is the line whose position, together with the curve's, fixes where [The Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md) is finally reached, and past that point it is the loss working at its linear best and simply outpaced inside [The Runaway](piece_the_runaway.md).
