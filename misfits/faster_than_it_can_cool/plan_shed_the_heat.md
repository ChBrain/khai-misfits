# Plan: Shed the Heat

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the sink's seat](position_sink.md), inside [Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Sink](position_sink.md)

## Direction

Carry warmth away at the rate the temperature difference sets, more of it the hotter the system runs, because heat crosses to the surroundings in plain proportion and the loss is linear. Hold the balance while the loss outpaces the generation, and go on shedding at the same linear best when it no longer does, because the line can only be raised in proportion and never bent to match the curve.

## Orders

([The Sink](persona_the_sink.md) sheds heat at a rate rising linearly with temperature, laying down [the Loss](piece_the_loss.md), outpacing the generation below the point and outrun past it.)

## Implementation

([The Vessel](place_the_vessel.md) is cooled; the [shedding](process_shedding.md) process runs the linear loss; [the Loss](piece_the_loss.md) outpaces [the Generation](piece_the_generation.md) and holds [the Balance](piece_the_balance.md) below [the Critical Point](piece_the_critical_point.md), and is outrun above it, working at its best and no longer enough.)

## Targets

- [ ] heat is carried away at the rate the temperature difference sets, rising linearly with it
- [ ] the loss outpaces the generation for the whole ordinary range, holding the balance
- [ ] the line is climbed steadily and never bent, because a linear loss cannot do otherwise
- [ ] past the point the loss is outrun at full strength, outpaced rather than failed, and cannot catch the runaway
