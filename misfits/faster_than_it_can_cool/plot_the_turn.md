# Plot: The Turn

## Taxonomy

[Faster Than It Can Cool](play_faster_than_it_can_cool.md).

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

At [The Vessel](place_the_vessel.md), past the point, a degree now makes more heat than it sheds: the same casing, the same reading it held an hour before, only now on the far side of the tangency.

## Action

[Diverging](process_diverging.md) takes the floor, owned by no persona, the outcome of the sign flip rather than any actor's move. [The Reaction](persona_the_reaction.md) outruns [the Sink](persona_the_sink.md) at every temperature above the point, and [the Cell](persona_the_cell.md) can no longer [Settle Back](plan_settle_back.md): there is no equilibrium left above to fall back on, no crossing of the curves anywhere higher, nothing to settle into. [The Runaway](piece_the_runaway.md) accelerates the temperature without bound.

## Stage

The Sink goes on shedding heat, working at its full linear best, exactly as it did the hour before and the years before that: it has not slackened and it has not failed. It is simply outpaced, a straight line left behind by a curve that bends. Each degree gained now buys a larger next increment of generation than the one before it, and the loss can only ever add its fixed proportion in return, so the gap that closed slowly across a whole life opens the other way in seconds.

## Tension

Destruction, venting or fire or fracture, in seconds, from a temperature that an hour before was merely warm. The only move left is the containment of a loss already underway, never correction: [the Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) has nothing left to hold, because holding was a move that belonged to the margin, and the margin is gone. Nothing in the system did wrong. The reaction obeyed its rate law, the cooling obeyed its own and never once stopped working, the point was truly stable for the whole of the system's life and ordinary time alone carried it here.
