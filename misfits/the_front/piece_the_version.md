# Piece: The Version

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The presented self, the processed and coherent account each performance produces, the resolved line the room reads as competence and the one thing that crosses from back stage to front.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Shown at [the stand-up](place_the_stand_up.md), where the resolved account is presented and received.
Worked out of [the corridor](place_the_corridor.md), where the raw is edited into the version before it crosses.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no performance, nothing shown to the room and nothing for the room to read as competence.
With it the trap has its surface: the processed, coherent account that presents a self, the front region made concrete, resolved enough to land and legible enough to trust (Goffman, on the presented self, the front the performer offers the room). The Version is load bearing because it is what the room actually receives, all the settled signal and none of the burning one: the account built by editing the raw away, so the piece carries the finished self the group trusts exactly because the unfinished thing was worked out of it before it crossed.

## Apparent

A clear account, a colleague on top of it, the work in hand.
Seen at the surface, the Version looks like competence itself, a resolved line from a person who has done the thinking, and the truth that it is the raw with the burning part edited out, that the room trusts it precisely because it reads as finished, that the clearer the version the more of the unresolved thing was removed to make it, stays hidden behind how capable a resolved account looks.
