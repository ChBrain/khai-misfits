# Plot: The Edit

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Corridor](place_the_corridor.md), the back stage where a burning thing is held.
The Burning holding something still unresolved, a doubt, a tangle, a thing still on fire, and weighing whether it can be shown.

## Action

[Editing](process_editing.md): [The Burning](persona_the_burning.md), holding [the withholder's seat](position_withholder.md), sets the raw against how it will read and works it down toward the presentable, or holds it when it will not go.

## Stage

[The Burning](persona_the_burning.md), on [Edit It Down](plan_edit_it_down.md), sets [the raw signal](piece_the_raw_signal.md) against [the premium](piece_the_premium.md) that reads the resolved as competence, and edits the burning thing toward what will cross [the curtain](piece_the_curtain.md).
While the thing is still raw it reads as noise and its cost in standing is real, so the honest reckoning finds the burning part too costly to show and leaves it in the working, the signal thinned to what is safe.

## Tension

Editing the raw into the presentable is not deceit, and the honest working is what removes the thing the group cannot get any other way.
There is nothing careless: the account is made genuinely clearer and the room genuinely reads the raw as noise. But the burning part is exactly what makes the signal worth hearing, so the plot closes with the raw worked down and the burning thing left back, the person right about how it would read and the doubt the group most needs edited, in good faith, out of the account.
