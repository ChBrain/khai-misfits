# Piece: The Premium

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The reward a group places on the legible, by which the resolved reads as competence and the raw as noise, the incentive that makes the edit rational and points, always, to showing the finished side.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set at [the stand-up](place_the_stand_up.md), where the resolved earns standing and the raw costs it.
Unlifted at [the all-hands](place_the_all_hands.md), where an invitation to candour cannot, by asking, change what the room rewards.

## Load Bearing

Without it the raw would cost nothing to show and there would be no reason to edit the burning thing away.
With it the trap has its engine: the premium on the legible, the motivated reading by which a resolved account reads as a capable colleague and a raw one as noise, so what is shown is driven by how it will land and the incentive points to the finished version (Leary & Kowalski, on impression motivation, the wish to make a given impression driving what is presented; Baumeister, on self-presentation as motivated cognition). The Premium is load bearing because it is why editing is rational and not vain: the reward that makes the sound choice the finished account, so the piece carries the incentive that turns an honest wish to be clear into the removal of the thing the group most needs.

## Apparent

Good judgement, a standard, a room that values clear thinking.
Seen at the surface, the Premium looks like a virtue, a group that rightly prizes colleagues who give resolved accounts, and the truth that the same reward reads the raw as noise, that valuing the legible is valuing the edited, that the standard which lifts the clear account is the standard which buries the burning one, stays hidden behind how reasonable prizing clarity looks.

## Yearbook

Felt by [the polished](persona_the_polished.md) and [the burning](persona_the_burning.md) as they weigh what to show, enforced by [the room](persona_the_room.md) through [face-work](process_face_work.md).
It is the engine of the trap: the Premium rewards [the version](piece_the_version.md) that crosses [the curtain](piece_the_curtain.md) and reads [the raw signal](piece_the_raw_signal.md) as noise, driving the editing whose sum is [the starvation](piece_the_starvation.md).
