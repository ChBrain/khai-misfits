# Persona: The Polished

## Taxonomy

[Performer](position_performer.md). The one who presents the resolved, coherent version, the tangle worked into a clean line before it is shown, and reads, to the room, as a colleague who has it handled. The archetype of the many who show the finished account because the finished account is what lands.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Composed. Articulate. Right about how it will read.
Is any one of the many giving an account of themselves, the person at the footlights, the colleague in the update, the one framing the problem and naming the path in the register a room trusts.
No deceit in it: the Polished has done real thinking, resolved a real tangle, and shows the version that is clearer, because the clear version is the courteous one and the one the room can use.

## Action

Works the raw into the presentable and shows that, the doubt resolved into a line, the worry framed into a plan, the still-burning thing left back until it is finished enough to read as competence.
Gives the account that lands, and earns, by giving it, the standing that comes to a colleague who has it handled.
Does what a self being watched does: it presents the version that reads as competent, because to be seen is already to perform and the resolved is the performance that wins the room.

## Shadow

Not a liar, an honest presenter of the version that reads well, and that is the whole difficulty: the account is true and worked and clear, and the part that got left back in the working is the part the group could not get any other way.
Because the resolved reads as competence and the raw as noise, the Polished cannot see editing as anything but diligence, so shows the finished account and holds the burning one, one of many each doing the same, and the room fills with what is settled and empties of what is still on fire, no one of them lying and the sum of them a group that never hears the thing it most needs.
It presents the clear version honestly, and the honest presenting is exactly what keeps the unresolved thing behind the curtain.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "here is where we are and here is the plan," clean and resolved and framed.
Says it having genuinely worked the tangle into that line, and cannot tell that the working is where the burning part was left, that the account the room trusts is the account with the raw edited out.
