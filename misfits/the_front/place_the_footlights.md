# Place: The Footlights

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The front region, wherever another's attention falls and so wherever the front comes up: the pressure of being seen, in any room with an audience however small, not a stage set apart but the plain fact of being looked at.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place one comes into view, and it is everywhere another's eyes rest: the dinner table and the ward round, the school gate and the group thread, the first date and the checkout line, the reunion and the daily call.
Wherever a person is seen by another, the footlights come up, the composed version offered and the watcher reading it, no threat needed and no stage built but the plain fact of an audience of one or many.

## Holds

The performance, and the attention that makes it one.
Here one is on view, so the Footlights hold the point where being seen does its work: a resolved self offered to whatever audience is present, a table of family or a queue of strangers or a single pair of eyes, the front up before any stake appears because to be looked at is already to be performed to, so it is where the finished version is shown and the still-burning one is not, and where the light on you is the only reason needed.

## Offers

A place to be seen, and an audience that reads a composed self as a capable one.
Because the Footlights are wherever attention falls, they offer a real chance to present the resolved version and be met as someone who has it together, and, any audience reading the composed as competence and the raw as the unformed, they offer that chance with the premium on the legible already set, so they hand out a light that feels like simply being oneself and hand it out with the pull to perform already lit.

## Withheld

Any room without an audience, and so any place the raw could simply be.
The Footlights are only ever up where someone is watching, so they withhold the unwatched moment, the room with no eyes in it, letting the composed self stand as the only self on view, and, all attention and no privacy, they withhold the sight that a self shown under the light is a self edited for the light, in every room the reader has ever been seen in.
