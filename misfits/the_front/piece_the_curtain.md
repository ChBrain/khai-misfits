# Piece: The Curtain

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The line between front stage and back, the standing split between the version presented and the version before presentation, up before any threat and drawn by the plain fact of being seen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Drawn at [the footlights](place_the_footlights.md), where the front stage begins and the presented self is shown.
Backed by [the green room](place_the_green_room.md), where the back stage holds the raw the curtain keeps from the room.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no front and no back, no split between the shown self and the held one and so no trap at all.
With it the whole structure stands: the boundary between the region presented and the region before presentation, the split Goffman names as the standing architecture of interaction and not a mask worn under threat, so the front is up before any stake appears and there is no unperformed moment behind it to recover (Goffman, on front and back regions, the universal structure of the self in company). The Curtain is load bearing because it is what makes performance the baseline and not the exception: the line that is always already drawn, so the piece carries the reason the misfit is a structure and not a fault, that to be seen is to be in front of the curtain and the back stage is sealed by the fact of an audience, no threat required.

## Apparent

Nothing, the ordinary transparency of a person just being themselves.
Seen at the surface, the Curtain is invisible, because a presented self looks simply like a self and no one experiences the line as a line, and the truth that there is a front region and a back one running under every exchange, that the self in the room is always the version this side of the curtain, that the split is the condition of being seen and not a thing done under pressure, stays hidden behind how natural just being oneself feels.

## Yearbook

Held up on one side by [the polished](persona_the_polished.md) and [the burning](persona_the_burning.md), sealed on the other by [the room](persona_the_room.md) through [face-work](process_face_work.md).
It is the structure of the trap: the Curtain divides [the version](piece_the_version.md) that crosses from [the raw signal](piece_the_raw_signal.md) that stays back, the split before any threat that makes [the premium](piece_the_premium.md) bite and the [starvation](piece_the_starvation.md) follow, up whether or not anything is at stake.
