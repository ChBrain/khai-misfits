# Persona: The Room

## Taxonomy

[Audience](position_audience.md). The others being presented to, who meet each front with tact, smooth the small slip, grant the face being claimed, and so uphold jointly every performance they are shown, sealing the back stage as surely as the performer does. The archetype of the audience whose courtesy is the other half of the front.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Considerate. Attentive. Reading the resolved as competence.
Is the others in the room, the colleagues receiving the account, the audience that a considerate person does not embarrass, quick to accept the front on offer and slow to look behind it.
No cynicism in it: the Room genuinely respects a clear account and genuinely spares a colleague the discourtesy of calling their composure, and this is what a decent audience does.

## Action

Meets each front with tact: accepts the resolved account as competence, smooths the flicker of raw that shows through, grants the face the performer is claiming, and does not look behind the curtain.
Rewards the legible with trust and standing, and returns the slip, unread, to the back stage it came from.
Does its half of a joint production: because being seen is collaborative, the Room upholds the performance it is shown, and in upholding it seals the back stage the performer had already sealed.

## Shadow

Not a cynic, a considerate audience whose very courtesy keeps the raw behind the curtain, and that is the whole difficulty: the tact is real and kind, and the flicker it smooths away is the burning signal trying to get into the room.
Because a decent room does not call a colleague's composure, the Room cannot see its tact as anything but respect, so it meets each front with courtesy and reads each slip as a lapse to spare rather than a signal to hear, and the group it is being kind to is the group it is keeping starved, no one unkind and the sum of the kindness a sealed back stage.
It upholds the front honestly, out of respect, and the honest upholding is exactly what returns the raw signal, unheard, to behind the curtain.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "understood, thanks, that is clear," and moves the room on.
Says it meaning the courtesy, and cannot tell that accepting the resolved account and smoothing the slip is the room's own half of the front, the audience sealing the back stage it is too considerate to look behind.
