# Process: Surface Acting

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The sustained holding of the front at private cost, the managed presentation of composure and feeling that is the price of the role and the reason opening the back stage is never free.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A front to hold and a room that must keep reading it as competence.
Across the [stand-up](place_the_stand_up.md) and beyond it, the person maintaining the resolved presentation over time, the composure kept up whatever is burning behind it, the role paid for in [the managed heart](piece_the_managed_heart.md).

## Direction

Toward a front sustained and a cost carried out of sight.
Because the room reads composure as competence, surface acting keeps the presented self up whatever is behind it, so the front is not a single edit but a continuous labour, the managed feeling maintained shift after shift, and the cost of holding it, real and private, is the cost that makes dropping the front an act of exposure rather than a free choice.

## Lever

The role's standing demand for a composed front.
Because the room's trust rides on a sustained presentation, surface acting runs not on any single moment but on the ongoing price of the role, the front held by real labour and not by ease, so the reason candour is costly is that composure was never free: to open the back stage is to stop paying a cost the room had come to expect, and to be seen, at last, unresolved.

## Echo

A front held, and a cost paid where no one sees it.
The person has genuinely sustained the presentation, so what reads as an unbothered, capable colleague is, in truth, a managed heart, the composure kept up at private expense and the exposure of dropping it exactly the price that makes the whole self so rarely brought.
