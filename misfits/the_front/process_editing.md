# Process: Editing

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The working of the raw into the presentable before it is shown, the honest processing that, because the room reads the resolved as competence, thins the burning thing toward what is safe or holds it back entirely.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A raw signal to show and a room that reads it as noise.
At [the green room](place_the_green_room.md) or in the moment before the [footlights](place_the_footlights.md), the person setting the unresolved thing against how it will read, the resolved version the one the room rewards.

## Direction

Toward the raw worked into a line and the burning part left in the working.
Because the resolved reads as competence and the raw as noise, editing turns the tangle into the presentable, so the honest processing that makes an account clearer is the same processing that removes the unfinished thing the group cannot get any other way, and what crosses to the front is the finished version, the still-burning part thinned away or held back, the signal quietly lost in the making of the account.

## Lever

The premium the room places on the legible.
Because the resolved earns standing while the raw costs it, editing runs not on any wish to deceive but on the plain reward for clarity, the burning thing removed from the account by no lie, only by the honest work of making it read as competence, so the signal is edited out not against the group's interest but in the service of an account the group will trust.

## Echo

A raw signal worked into a resolved one, and the burning part left behind.
The person has genuinely made the account clearer, so what reads as diligence is, in truth, the removal of the unfinished thing, the doubt worked into a line and the line shown while the doubt stays back, the account trusted exactly because the raw was edited out of it.
