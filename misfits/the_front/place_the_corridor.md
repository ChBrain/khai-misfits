# Place: The Corridor

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The back stage, where the raw version lives before presentation and the burning thing is worked or held, off the record and out of the room.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place behind the front, a corridor, a private message, the walk to the car, where the version before presentation is worked and the thing still on fire is actually held.
The Burning turning the tangle over, the doubt not yet argued said only here if said at all, the raw signal alive in the one place the room does not reach.

## Holds

The raw before it is edited, and the choice of whether it ever crosses.
Here the burning thing exists in full, so the Corridor holds the back stage where the real signal lives and where the editing is done that decides how much of it, if any, reaches the front, so it is where a doubt is either worked into a line for the Stand-Up or kept back for good, and where the thing the group most needs is closest to being said and furthest from the room that needs it.

## Offers

A place to hold the raw safely, and a reason it stays there.
Because the Corridor is off the record, it offers the one space the burning thing can exist unedited, and, that space being outside the room, it offers that safety at the price of the signal never reaching the group, so it hands out a back stage where the raw is real and hands it out with the front stage still deciding what may cross.

## Withheld

Any bridge that carries the raw across without cost.
The Corridor holds the burning thing whole but cannot deliver it to the room unedited, because the front stage reads the raw as noise, so it withholds the passage by which a doubt could cross from back to front still on fire, letting the raw stay real only where the group cannot use it, and, all safety and no crossing, it withholds the one thing that would matter: the burning signal, in the room, unresolved.
