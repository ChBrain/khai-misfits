# Position: Convener

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The seat that sets the key and would open the back stage, where the invitation to the whole self is real only when someone pays the exposure to honour it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A room to convene and a door to hold open: the wish for the raw signal and the invitation to be seen unresolved.
A true reading: the group is starving for the burning thing, and the whole self is exactly what it needs in the room.
No power to lift, by asking alone, the premium the room places on the legible, and no way to make the invitation real except by paying, in standing, to go first.

## Orders

Open the floor.
Ask for the whole self and mean it, invite the doubt and the tangle and the thing still on fire, and work to make the room safe to show them.
Pay the exposure the invitation costs: go first, be seen mid-thought, refuse to read a colleague's raw as noise, because an open door in a room that still rewards the resolved is a door no one can afford to walk through until someone does.

## Loses

The candour it asked for, exactly when the asking is not backed by the exposure of going first.
Because the incentive on legibility runs before any threat and does not lift because a host requests it, the seat cannot make the room safe by inviting alone, so the whole-self invitation, unpaid, is received as one more thing to perform, and it loses the raw signal to the very candour it called for, candour hollowed into a front, until the group stays as starved as before.
It gains a room that performs openness, and loses, unless it pays, the burning thing the openness was meant to let in.

## Drives

Toward the back stage opened and the whole self invited,
the door held wide and the room asked for the thing still on fire,
so the seat that convenes must carry, itself and first, the exposure of being seen unresolved, or watch the invitation it meant become the newest front the room learns to keep.
