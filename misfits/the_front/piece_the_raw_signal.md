# Piece: The Raw Signal

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The unresolved, still-burning thing, the doubt, the tangle, the objection not yet argued, that reads as noise while it is raw and is edited away or held back, the one thing the group cannot get any other way.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Alive in [the green room](place_the_green_room.md), the back stage where the burning thing exists unedited.
Kept from [the footlights](place_the_footlights.md), the front stage where the raw reads as noise and cannot cross.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing the group is starving for, no burning thing behind the front and no signal lost in the editing.
With it the trap has its stakes: the unresolved matter that the group most needs and the room most reads as noise, the backstage thing that would matter in the room if it could cross and reads as not-yet-competent if it tries (Goffman, on the back region, the reality kept from the audience). The Raw Signal is load bearing because it is the very thing the front is built to keep back: the doubt worth hearing exactly while it is still unresolved, so the piece carries the burning thing whose value and whose unshowability are the same fact, needed most where it reads worst.

## Apparent

Noise, half-formed, a colleague not yet on top of it.
Seen at the surface, the Raw Signal looks like a lapse, an account not ready, a person who should come back when they have thought it through, and the truth that it is the one thing the group could not get from anyone else, that its being unresolved is exactly why it is worth hearing, that what reads as not-yet-competent is the signal itself, stays hidden behind how unfinished a raw account looks.

## Yearbook

Held by [the burning](persona_the_burning.md) through [editing](process_editing.md) turned inward, smoothed by [the room](persona_the_room.md) through [face-work](process_face_work.md) when it slips across [the curtain](piece_the_curtain.md).
It is the stakes of the trap: the Raw Signal reads as noise because [the premium](piece_the_premium.md) rewards the legible, is left back while [the version](piece_the_version.md) crosses, and its absence, summed across the room, is [the starvation](piece_the_starvation.md).
