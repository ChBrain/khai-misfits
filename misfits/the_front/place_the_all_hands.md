# Place: The All-Hands

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The stage of the openness itself, where the whole self is invited and candour is asked for, and where the invitation is either paid for or performed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the room is told to be open, an all-hands, a values session, a culture offsite, where the Host asks for the whole self and the room is invited to bring the messy version.
The Host meaning every word of the invitation, the room receiving it, and the question, unshown on the surface, of whether anyone will pay the exposure to test it.

## Holds

The invitation, and the cost that decides whether it is real.
Here the openness is declared to all, so the All-Hands holds the point where candour is either backed or hollowed: an invitation to be seen unresolved, meant in good faith, set against a room that still reads the resolved as competence, so it is where the whole self is asked for and where, unless someone goes first and pays the standing, the asking becomes one more thing to perform.

## Offers

A stage for candour, and the same stage for its performance.
Because the All-Hands is where openness is declared, it offers a real chance to open the back stage and let the raw in, and, the premium on the legible still unlifted, it offers that same chance as a chance to perform openness instead of pay for it, so it hands out an invitation that can free the burning signal and hands it out able to become the newest front the room keeps.

## Withheld

Any safety the invitation does not pay for.
The All-Hands declares the room open but cannot make it safe by declaring, because the incentive on legibility runs before any threat and does not lift because a host asks it to, so it withholds the safety that only the exposure of going first can buy, letting the invitation stand or hollow on whether it is paid, and, all warmth and no cost yet carried, it withholds the sight that a whole self invited without that cost is a whole self the room learns to stage.
