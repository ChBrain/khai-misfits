# Place: The Invitation

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The occasion where candour is invited and meant, the door held open to the back region, real only when someone pays the exposure to cross, in friendship, in family, in love, in any room told to be open.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the audience is asked behind the light, and it is everywhere candour is invited and meant: the friend who asks how you are and waits for the true answer, the parent who says you can tell me anything, the partner who says talk to me, the room told to bring its whole self, the stranger met with sudden intimacy.
Someone opens the door to the green room and means it, and, unshown on the surface, the question of whether anyone will pay the exposure to walk through.

## Holds

The invitation, and the cost that decides whether it is real.
Here candour is asked for openly, so the Invitation holds the point where it is either paid for or performed: a door held open to the back region, meant in good faith, set against a light that still reads the composed as the capable, so it is where the whole self is asked for and where, unless someone goes first and pays the standing, the asking becomes one more thing to perform.

## Offers

An opening for the raw, and the same opening for its performance.
Because the Invitation is where candour is declared, it offers a real chance to let the burning self cross into the light, and, the premium on the legible still unlifted, it offers that same chance as a chance to perform openness instead of pay for it, so it hands out a door that could free the raw signal and hands it out able to become the newest front kept.

## Withheld

Any safety the invitation does not pay for.
The Invitation declares the door open but cannot make crossing safe by declaring, because the pull toward the composed runs before any threat and does not lift because someone asks it to, so it withholds the safety that only the exposure of going first can buy, letting the invitation stand or hollow on whether it is paid, and, all warmth and no cost yet carried, it withholds the sight that a whole self invited without that cost is a whole self the room learns to stage.
