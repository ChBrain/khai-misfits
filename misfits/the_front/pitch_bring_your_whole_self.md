# Pitch: Bring Your Whole Self

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Open, candid, self-aware.
The plain good sense of a team that prizes clear accounts and reads a resolved, well-framed update as the mark of a colleague who has done the thinking.
Nothing false in it on its face: the front really is up in good faith, the resolved version really is clearer, and showing the finished account is the honest courtesy of a person who has worked the tangle into something the room can use.

## Undertow

Under the openness, a front stage and a back stage that are the standing structure of being seen and not a mask worn under threat.
Because to be in a room is already to present a self, and because the room reads the resolved as competence and the raw as noise, each person edits the burning thing into the presentable before showing it, and one by one the group comes to hold only what is already finished, until the day it needs the doubt no one resolved enough to say and finds that signal was never in the room.
What makes a colleague valuable, that they give clear and resolved accounts, is what makes each of them edit away the unresolved thing the group cannot get any other way.

## Nerve

The feeling of holding something still burning, still unresolved, and reaching for the version of it that will land,
sure you are being helpful, because the clean account is what the room can use and the tangle would only read as noise,
while every other person does the same in good faith, each right about how their own raw signal would read, and the room fills with finished accounts and empties of the burning things behind them.

## Echo

After the run: the group moving only on what was already settled, and the one signal it most needed, the doubt, the tangle, the thing still on fire, never in the room at all,
no one having lied and no one a coward, only a self that is always already presented and a room that reads the resolved as competent, so each edited the burning thing away exactly when the group most needed it shown,
and the one move that would have let it in, opening the back stage and paying the exposure of being seen unresolved, forgone because it costs real standing to show the very thing a room is built to reward hiding.
