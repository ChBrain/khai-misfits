# Persona: The Host

## Taxonomy

[Convener](position_convener.md). The one who runs the room and would open the back stage, invite the whole self and mean it, make it safe to be seen unresolved, and finds that the invitation costs real exposure to honour and reads, unpaid, as one more front. The archetype of the opener who must pay to open.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Warm. Sincere. Wanting the burning thing in the room.
Is the one who convenes and sets the key, the lead who says bring your whole self, the host who keeps the door open and asks for candour and means every word of it.
No pretence in it: the Host genuinely wants the raw signal, sees that the group is starving for it, and genuinely means the invitation to be seen unresolved.

## Action

Opens the floor and asks for the whole self: invites the doubt, the tangle, the thing still on fire, and works to make the room safe to show it.
Would go first, be seen mid-thought, refuse to read a colleague's raw as noise, and pay the exposure that lets the back stage open.
Does the one thing that lets the raw in, and discovers its cost: the invitation is only real when someone pays, in standing, to be seen unresolved, and the invitation unpaid is a front like any other.

## Shadow

Not a hypocrite, a sincere opener who finds that opening costs more than asking, and that is the whole difficulty: the wish for candour is real and the door is genuinely open, and a door held open in a room that still reads the resolved as competence is a door no one can afford to walk through.
Because the incentive on legibility runs before any threat and does not lift because a host asks it to, the Host cannot make the room safe by inviting alone, so the whole-self invitation, unbacked by the exposure of going first, is received as one more thing to perform, candour itself becoming a front to keep, and the group stays as starved as before, no one insincere and the invitation quietly hollowed.
It opens the floor honestly, and the honest opening, unpaid, is exactly the front the room learns to perform.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "this is a safe room, bring your whole self, I want to hear the messy version."
Says it meaning it, and cannot tell, until someone pays the exposure to test it, whether the room is safe or whether the whole self is now just the newest front the room knows to put up.
