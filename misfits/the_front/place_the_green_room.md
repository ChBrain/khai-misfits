# Place: The Green Room

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The back region, wherever no one is watching and so wherever the act can be set down: the one place the raw self lives, off the light and out of view, in any life that has a moment unseen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place behind the light, and it is everywhere no one is watching: the drive home alone, the message typed and not sent, the bathroom mirror, the walk with the one person you do not perform for, the small hours before anyone is up.
Where the audience falls away, the green room opens, the act set down and the raw self, the tangle, the doubt, the thing still on fire, alive in the one place it is not being read.

## Holds

The raw before it is edited, and the choice of whether it ever crosses.
Here the burning thing exists in full, so the Green Room holds the back region where the real signal lives and where the editing is done that decides how much of it, if any, reaches the light, so it is where a doubt is either worked into something showable or kept back for good, and where the thing a person most is sits closest to being said and furthest from the room that would need it.

## Offers

A place to set the act down, and a reason the raw stays there.
Because the Green Room is where no one watches, it offers the one space the burning thing can exist unedited, and, that space being off the light, it offers that relief at the price of the raw never crossing to where it would matter, so it hands out a backstage where the self is real and hands it out with the front region still deciding what may cross.

## Withheld

Any way to carry the raw into the light without cost.
The Green Room holds the burning thing whole but cannot deliver it to the room unedited, because the light reads the raw as unformed, so it withholds the passage by which a doubt could cross from behind the scenes into view still on fire, letting the raw stay real only where no one needs it, and, all relief and no crossing, it withholds the one thing that would matter: the burning self, seen, unresolved.
