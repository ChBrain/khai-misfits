# The Front: Reference

The research warrant for [The Front](play_the_front.md).
The dynamic is impression management: Goffman's dramaturgical account of the self as presented, the front and back
regions as the standing structure of interaction, and face-work as the mutual tact that upholds it; Hochschild's
emotional labour as the sustained cost of the front; and the empirical spine of self-presentation in Leary and
Kowalski and in Baumeister, all cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging the presented self as a **self-starving group**: to be seen is already to perform, so front stage and back
stage are the standing architecture of interaction and not a mask worn under threat, and because a group that
prizes legibility reads the resolved account as competence and the raw account as noise, each member rationally
edits the still-burning thing into the presentable before showing it, or holds it when it will not process, so the
group comes to share only what is already resolved and never what is still burning, and the one signal it most
needs, the doubt, the tangle, the unfinished thing, never enters the room. The play models the documented
mechanics, the front and back regions as the universal split, the impression motivation that makes the resolved
read as competent and the raw as noise, the emotional labour of holding the front, the face-work by which the room
jointly upholds every performance it is shown, and the aggregate starvation of a group that moves only on settled
signal, as the load-bearing vectors of the structure, not as a tale of liars or cowards. The governing finding of
the sources is the play's governing law: the performance of self is the baseline condition of being seen and not a
deviation from an authentic default, so the suppression of the raw runs before any threat, in every direction at
once, and the information a group most needs is precisely the information that cannot be made to read as competence
and so is precisely the information that never gets shown.

It is distinct from the house's Saving Face, and the two are close enough that the cut must be exact: there the
defensive routine fires in the moment embarrassment threatens, a face protected under a specific threat; here the
front is up before any threat appears, the standing structure of being seen and not a response to a stake, so the
engine is not threat-response but the baseline performance that runs whether or not anything is at risk. It is
distinct from Watermelon Reporting, where bad news is suppressed as it travels upward toward a seat that could act:
there the direction is up and the withheld thing is a status; here the suppression runs in every direction at once,
peer and downward and toward the self, and the withheld thing is the unresolved raw signal in general, not the red
status alone. It is distinct from The Leadership Script, which stages one performed register, the naturalised
discourse of leadership: there a specific idiom is performed; here the play stages the universal mechanism of
self-presentation that every register, leadership included, runs on top of. And it is distinct from The Trip to
Abilene and the held-off preference falsification, which stage a false consensus about a decision or a misreported
preference: there a choice is on the table and each privately dissents while publicly assenting; here no decision
need be on the table and no preference is misreported, the play staging the standing structure of the presented
self that those collisions run on rather than any one collision it produces.

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast the performers as
  liars or the room as a cynical audience, because the source finding is precisely that no one deceives: to be seen
  is already to present a self, and each shows the resolved version because the room reads the resolved as
  competence and the raw as noise, choosing as any person being watched would. The starvation is authored by no one
  and emerges from the front-and-back structure of interaction and the premium a group places on the legible, so
  the misfit is that a group whose members each honestly present the processed self is a group that shares what it
  has finished and starves for what it has not.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into a costless correction. The one move that lets the raw in
  is to open the back stage, build the trust and pay the exposure that let a person be seen unresolved, and make
  the room slow to read the raw as noise, and it means paying, in real standing and real exposure, to show the very
  thing a room is built to reward hiding, against an incentive that runs before any threat and never lets up. The
  reckoning is a front lowered only by someone carrying, each time, the cost of being seen mid-thought in a room
  that reads the resolved as competence, not a free correction; and the whole-self invitation, offered without that
  cost paid, is itself one more front.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here, never claimed as
  the house's. The staging is the house's; the dramaturgical self and the front and back regions are Goffman's, the
  face-work by which a room jointly upholds a performance is Goffman's, the emotional labour that is the cost of the
  front is Hochschild's, and the two-component impression motivation and the motivated cognition of self-presentation
  are Leary and Kowalski's and Baumeister's; the honest boundary, that real backstage access and a room safe to be
  seen unresolved can let the raw signal in at a cost, is carried as carefully as the finding itself.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **[The Version](piece_the_version.md)**: carries the presented self (Goffman), the processed, coherent account
  each performance produces and the room reads as competence.
- **[The Raw Signal](piece_the_raw_signal.md)**: carries the backstage matter (Goffman), the unresolved,
  still-burning thing that reads as noise and is edited away, the signal the group cannot get any other way.
- **[The Curtain](piece_the_curtain.md)**: carries the front/back boundary (Goffman), the standing split between
  the version presented and the version before presentation, up before any threat.
- **[The Premium](piece_the_premium.md)**: carries the impression motivation (Leary & Kowalski; Baumeister), the
  reward on legibility by which the resolved reads as competent and the raw as noise.
- **[The Managed Heart](piece_the_managed_heart.md)**: carries the emotional labour (Hochschild), the sustained
  private cost of holding the front, the price of the role.
- **[The Starvation](piece_the_starvation.md)**: carries the phenomenon whole (Goffman, with the group-level
  reading), the aggregate room that holds only the resolved and never the burning thing it most needs.
