# Place: The Stand-Up

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The front stage, the room where accounts are given and received, where each is in front of all and the resolved version is the version shown.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the account is presented, a stand-up, a status round, a review, where each in turn gives their update and the others receive it.
The Polished framing the problem and naming the path in the register the room trusts, the Room accepting it as competence, the raw version nowhere in sight and the resolved account the only account on the table.

## Holds

The performance, and the reception that upholds it.
Here each presents a self to all and all present back, so the Stand-Up holds the point where being seen does its work: a resolved account offered and met with tact, the front up before any stake appears because to be in the room is already to be in front of it, so it is where the finished version is shown and the still-burning one is not, and where a doubt not yet worked into a line is a doubt no one in the room can hear.

## Offers

A turn to give an account, and a room that reads a clear one as competence.
Because the Stand-Up is where each is seen, it offers a real chance to land the resolved version and earn the standing that comes with it, and, the room reading the resolved as competence and the raw as noise, it offers that chance with the incentive already pointing to the finished account, so it hands out a floor that looks like candour and hands it out with the premium on the legible already set.

## Withheld

Any place for the thing still on fire.
The Stand-Up shows each their turn and the room's reception of it but cannot hold the raw signal, because the raw reads as noise on this floor and the floor rewards the resolved, so it withholds the room where a doubt could be said before it is resolved, letting the finished account stand alone, and, all legibility and no safe way to be seen mid-thought, it withholds the sight that the account it trusts is the account with the burning part edited out.
