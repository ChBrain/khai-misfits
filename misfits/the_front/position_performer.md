# Position: Performer

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The seat that presents a self to the room, where the resolved version reads as competence and so is the version the seat is paid to show.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A self to present and a room reading it: the raw account before presentation and the resolved account the room rewards.
A sound calculation: the resolved version is genuinely clearer and reads as a colleague who has it handled, while the raw version reads as noise.
No unperformed moment behind the front to fall back to, because to be seen is already to present, and no way to show the burning thing without it reading as not-yet-competent.

## Orders

Present the resolved.
Work the tangle into a line, frame the worry into a plan, and show the version that lands, because the room reads the resolved as competence and the raw as noise, and the standing goes to the account that has it handled.
Hold the still-burning thing back until it is finished enough to read well, which is until it is no longer the burning thing.

## Loses

The one signal the group could not get any other way, exactly by rationally showing only the resolved.
Because the resolved reads as competence and the raw as noise, the seat cannot see editing as anything but diligence, so it shows the finished account and holds the unfinished one, one of many doing the same, and loses, in the sum of all those sound choices, the burning signal the group most needs, until the room holds only what is settled.
It gains standing and a room that trusts it, and loses the chance to put the thing still on fire where the group could use it.

## Drives

Toward the resolved version shown and the burning one held back,
the finished account reading as competence and the raw reading as noise,
so the seat that presents a self shows the version that lands, in good faith, and keeps, with every seat like it, the unresolved thing the group most needs behind the curtain.
