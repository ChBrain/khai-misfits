# Position: Withholder

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The seat holding a raw signal that will not read as competent, where the cost of showing the unresolved thing is the heavier side and so the burning thing is held.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Something still on fire and a room that reads the raw as noise: the unresolved doubt, the tangle mid-work, the objection not yet argued, and the standing it would cost to show them.
A sound calculation: while the thing is still raw it genuinely reads as a colleague not yet on top of it, and the cost in standing is genuinely real.
No way to make the burning thing read as competent while it is still burning, and no reward in the room for the raw signal the group cannot get any other way.

## Orders

Weigh the raw against how it will read.
Set the standing it would cost to show the unresolved thing against the gain of showing it, which the room does not give, and choose the cheaper side, which is to edit it down or hold it.
Show the part that finished; keep the part still on fire until it is safe to read as competence, which is until it is no longer the signal.

## Loses

The signal itself, exactly by rationally declining to show it while it is still raw.
Because the raw reads as noise precisely while it is unresolved, the seat cannot see holding it as anything but sense, so it keeps the burning thing back, one of many each holding their own, and loses, in the sum of all those sound choices, the very doubt the group most needs, until the candid room is starved of candour.
It gains standing kept and composure intact, and loses the one thing it alone could have put in the room.

## Drives

Toward the burning thing held and the finished part shown,
the raw reading as noise and the cost in standing left whole,
so the seat that holds a raw signal keeps it back in good faith, and starves, with every seat like it, the group of the thing it most needed said.
