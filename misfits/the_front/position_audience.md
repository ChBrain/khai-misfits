# Position: Audience

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The seat presented to, whose tact upholds each front it is shown, where a considerate reception of the resolved is what seals the back stage.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A performance to receive and the courtesy to receive it well: the resolved account on offer and the tact that meets it.
A sound instinct: a decent audience accepts a colleague's clear account and does not embarrass them by calling their composure or looking behind the front.
No standing to gain by reading the slip as a signal, and every reason of courtesy to smooth it away.

## Orders

Uphold the front.
Accept the resolved account as competence, smooth the flicker of raw that shows through, grant the face the performer is claiming, and reward the legible with trust, because that is what a considerate room does.
Do not look behind the curtain; return the slip, unread, to the back stage it came from.

## Loses

The raw signal it is being kept from, exactly by rationally sparing the performer the discourtesy of hearing it.
Because a decent room does not call a colleague's composure, the seat cannot see its tact as anything but respect, so it meets each front with courtesy and reads each slip as a lapse to spare, and loses, in the sum of all that kindness, the burning thing it is too considerate to let in, until the room it is being kind to is the room it has starved.
It gains a room of clear accounts and mutual respect, and loses the doubt the flicker was trying to bring.

## Drives

Toward the front upheld and the slip smoothed away,
the resolved read as competence and the raw returned unheard,
so the seat presented to seals, out of courtesy, the back stage the performer had already sealed, and keeps, with every seat like it, the burning signal behind the curtain.
