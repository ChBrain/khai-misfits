# Process: Starving

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md). The aggregate by which a group that receives only resolved accounts comes to hold only what is settled, moving on the safe signal and going without the burning thing no one could show, in every direction at once.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Many fronts, each honestly kept, summed across a group.
Across the [stand-up](place_the_stand_up.md) and the room, each member showing the resolved and holding the raw, the finished accounts entering and the burning ones staying back, one edit at a time.

## Direction

Toward a group that shares what it has finished and goes without what it has not.
Because each rationally shows the resolved and holds the raw, starving sums those choices into a room that holds only the settled signal, so the suppression that was individual becomes collective and runs in every direction at once, upward and sideways and down and toward the self, and the group moves on what was safe to show while the doubt it most needs, unresolved and unshowable, never enters the room.

## Lever

The sum of many sound edits over a shared room.
Because what is true of each, that the resolved lands and the raw reads as noise, is true of all, starving runs not on any one failure but on the aggregation of honest self-presentations, the group starved by no villain, only by every member rightly showing the version that reads as competence, so the room ends candid in manner and empty of the burning thing in fact.

## Echo

A group moving on the settled and blind to the burning.
Each has shown the resolved in good faith, so what reads as a candid, well-run room is, in truth, a room starved of its own raw signal, the finished accounts all present and the one doubt the group most needed nowhere in it, no one having withheld in bad faith and the withholding, summed, the group's way of never hearing what it most needs.
