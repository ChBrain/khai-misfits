# Persona: The Burning

## Taxonomy

[Withholder](position_withholder.md). The one holding something still unresolved, a doubt, a tangle, a thing still on fire, that will not process into anything the room reads as competent, and so edits it down, or holds it, exactly when the group most needs it shown. The archetype of the signal that never enters the room.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Uncertain. Mid-thought. Sitting on something that has no clean shape yet.
Is any one of the many holding a raw signal, the half-formed worry, the objection not yet argued, the tangle still being worked, the thing that matters and cannot yet be said in a line.
No cowardice in it: the Burning reckons truly that the raw will read as noise and cost them standing, and weighs that against a gain the room does not reward, and holds.

## Action

Weighs the burning thing against how it will read, finds it will land as noise, as a colleague not yet on top of it, and edits it down toward the presentable, or, when it will not go, holds it back.
Shows the part that finished, keeps the part still on fire, and takes the standing that comes from not being seen unresolved.
Does what a self being read does: it compares a raw signal that costs real standing against a resolved one that earns it, and shows the side that reads as competent, the burning part left back.

## Shadow

Not a coward, a rational weigher of a real cost in standing against a gain the room does not give, and that is the whole difficulty: the calculation is sound, and the thing edited out is the thing the group could not get from anyone else.
Because the raw reads as noise exactly while it is still raw, the Burning cannot see holding it as anything but sense, so keeps the unfinished thing back, one of many each holding their own, and the doubt the group most needs stays unsaid in the very room built to prize candour, no one wrong and the sum of them a silence.
It holds the burning thing honestly, and the honest holding is exactly what keeps the signal the group most needs out of the room.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "let me come back when I have thought it through," or says nothing at all.
Says it having reckoned truly that the raw would read as noise, and cannot tell that the thinking-through is where the signal gets edited away, that the thing worth saying was the thing still on fire.
