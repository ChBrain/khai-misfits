# Plot: The Price

## Taxonomy

[The Front](play_the_front.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Invitation](place_the_invitation.md), the openness declared and the whole self invited.
The Host wanting the raw signal in the room, meaning the invitation, and the question, unshown on the surface, of whether anyone will pay the exposure to test it.

## Action

[Surface Acting](process_surface_acting.md) set against the invitation: [The Host](persona_the_host.md), holding [the convener's seat](position_convener.md), opens the floor and would go first, paying the exposure that makes the back stage safe to open, or watch the invitation hollow.

## Stage

[The Host](persona_the_host.md), on [Open the Floor](plan_open_the_floor.md), invites the whole self against [the premium](piece_the_premium.md) the room still places on the legible, and the cost of it lands in [the managed heart](piece_the_managed_heart.md): the front is sustained by real labour, so dropping it is exposure and not ease.
The invitation is real only if someone pays, in standing, to be seen unresolved; unpaid, it is received as one more thing to perform, candour itself becoming a front.

## Tension

Opening the back stage is the one move that lets the raw in, and it cannot be made by asking alone.
There is nothing false in the Host: the wish for candour is real and the door is open. But the premium on the legible runs before any threat and does not lift because a host requests it, so the plot closes either with the front lowered for someone who paid, in real exposure, to be seen mid-thought ... or with the whole-self invitation, unpaid, performed as the newest front, the back stage left sealed and the burning thing left back, the remedy real and never free.
