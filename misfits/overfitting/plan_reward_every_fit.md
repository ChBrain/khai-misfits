# Plan: Reward Every Fit

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the score's seat](position_score.md), inside [Overfitting](play_overfitting.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Score](position_score.md)

## Direction

Fall with every turn of the crank. Drop as the model fits the signal, and drop just the same as it fits the noise, rewarding more capacity and more tuning with a better number every time. Say nothing at the point where fitting stops being learning, because you are measured on the sample and cannot see it.

## Orders

([The Score](persona_the_score.md) drops the whole way down, praising the fit through the signal and on into the noise, and never once says stop.)

## Implementation

([The Training Set](place_the_training_set.md); the [fitting](process_fitting.md) it measures; [the score](piece_the_score.md) is a true measure of the wrong thing, falling as [the fit](piece_the_fit.md) tightens whether it learns [the signal](piece_the_signal.md) or the noise.)

## Targets

- [ ] the score falls with every turn of the crank
- [ ] it drops the same for fitting the noise as for fitting the signal
- [ ] more capacity and more tuning are rewarded with a better number
- [ ] it never signals the point where fitting stops being learning
