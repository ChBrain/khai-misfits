# Plot: The Gap

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The field](place_the_field.md), the model's error on the world set beside the error it reported on the sample.
The two numbers stand together at last, and the distance between them is not a fluke but a measurement.

## Action

[Failing](process_failing.md): [The Unseen](persona_the_unseen.md), holding [the unseen's seat](position_unseen.md), reads the true error on new data, while [The Score](persona_the_score.md), holding [the score's seat](position_score.md), stands with its low in-sample number, honest and now shown to have measured the wrong thing.

## Stage

[The Unseen](persona_the_unseen.md), having tabled [Break the Model](plan_break_the_model.md), gives the reading only new data can, and [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) opens between world and sample.
[The Score](persona_the_score.md), having tabled [Reward Every Fit](plan_reward_every_fit.md), is left holding a true measure of the training error and no measure of the field, the whole overfitting counted in the distance between them.

## Tension

Two honest numbers, and the truth living only in the space between.
Neither reading lies: the score measured the sample exactly, the unseen measures the world exactly, and each on its own looks fine. But the fit was made and judged where only the score could see, so the gap that is the whole of the overfitting was invisible the entire time it was being written, and becomes a fact only in the field, quantified precisely when it is already too late to have prevented it.
