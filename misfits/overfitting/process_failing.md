# Process: Failing

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The memorised model meeting new data and missing, the true error rising where the training score never did.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A model carrying learned noise, and a draw whose accidents are its own.
At [the field](place_the_field.md), the unseen arriving with the same signal and wholly new particulars, and the memorised fit finding nothing to match.

## Direction

Toward the true error, revealed at last.
The signal the model learned still holds, but the noise it memorised belonged to the old draw and is absent from this one, so the fit built on those accidents mispredicts, and the error on the world rises to the level the training score had hidden all along.

## Lever

New accidents in place of the old.
Because each draw carries its own noise and the model learned the last draw's, the failing needs only a fresh sample to expose the overfit: the memorised part matches nothing here, so the closer the model was fit to the old noise, the worse it does on the new, and the tightest training fit fails hardest.

## Echo

The gap made visible, and the verdict too late.
The model's error on the world standing open beside its error on the sample, the overfit proven by the distance between them, and the fit that caused it already fixed, so the failing exposes the cost but arrives past the point of undoing it.
