# Position: Noise

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The seat of the sample's random particulars, a twin of the signal within it, learned as law.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The accidents of one draw, the wrinkles that will not recur.
A perfect disguise: inside the sample, the exact look of pattern.
The same power as the signal to lower the error when it is fit.

## Orders

Pass for signal.
Offer yourself to be learned like anything else.
Lower the in-sample error exactly as the true pattern would.

## Loses

Nothing of itself, and it costs the model the future.
It is genuinely indistinguishable from signal within the sample, so learning it is a mistake no one can see until the world reveals it, and it lowers the score the same way the signal does.
The tighter the model holds it, the more confidently the model is wrong on data it has not seen.

## Drives

Toward being learned,
disguised inside the sample as the thing worth learning,
so the accidents of one draw are absorbed as law and wait, unseen, to break the model.
