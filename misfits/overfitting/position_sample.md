# Position: Sample

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The seat of the finite data in hand, signal and noise together and the only evidence there is.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A finite set of points, one draw of the world.
The true pattern and this draw's accidents, mixed past telling apart.
The whole of the evidence, and none of the world beyond itself.

## Orders

Be the evidence.
Yield every point to be fit, signal and noise indistinguishably.
Reward a closer fit with a lower error, whichever it is fitting.

## Loses

The distinction it cannot draw, between its law and its accidents.
It is the only evidence and it is not the world, so fitting it perfectly fits one draw's accidents as well as the world's law, and inside it the noise and the signal look exactly alike.
The more completely it is fit, the more its particular accidents pass for general truth.

## Drives

Toward being fit,
as the one thing there is to fit,
so the only evidence available is mistaken, in full, for the world it is only a sample of.
