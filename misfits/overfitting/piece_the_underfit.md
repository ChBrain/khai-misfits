# Piece: The Underfit

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The error on the far side of the remedy, a model pulled back so hard it misses real signal, the symmetric cost of the cure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the retuning](place_the_retuning.md), waiting past every cut the regularizer makes.
The model made too simple, its training error and its true error both high, the pattern itself now missed.

## Load Bearing

Without it the remedy would be free and the misfit would have a hero, a regularizer who simply cuts away the overfit at no cost.
With it the trap closes on both sides: the same pull that drops the memorised noise drops nearby signal too, so past some point the model is too plain to hold the pattern, and its error rises again, now for want of fit rather than excess of it. The Underfit is load bearing because it makes the cure symmetric with the disease (Geman, Bienenstock and Doursat): overfitting and underfitting are the two arms of one tradeoff, and the optimum between them cannot be located from a finite sample, so the remedy can only trade one error for the other, never escape both.

## Apparent

A safe, simple, honest model, and no sign it is leaving learning on the table.
Pulled back from the overfit, the model looks prudent and its smaller gap looks like success, so underfitting reads as caution rewarded, and the truth that it is now missing real pattern, failing on the world for too little fit rather than too much, stays hidden because its training score and its field score agree, both merely high.

## Yearbook

Risked by [the regularizer](persona_the_regularizer.md) through [regularising](process_regularising.md), tabled inside [Hold Some Back](plan_hold_some_back.md).
It is the cost that makes the remedy no rescue: the Underfit is what waits when the cutting of [the noise](piece_the_noise.md) goes on into [the signal](piece_the_signal.md), the far arm of the tradeoff whose near arm is [the gap](piece_the_gap.md), and the reason [the fit](piece_the_fit.md) has no free right setting.
