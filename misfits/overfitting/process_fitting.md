# Process: Fitting

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The model bent to match the sample, turn by turn, the training error driven down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A model with capacity to spare and a sample to match.
At [the training set](place_the_training_set.md), the fitter turning the crank and the score falling to prove each turn worth taking.

## Direction

Toward an ever closer match to the data in hand.
Each turn bends the model nearer the sample and drops the training error, and because the score falls whether the turn catches signal or noise, the fitting has no reason of its own to stop, so it runs on past the point where matching the sample stops being learning the pattern.

## Lever

Capacity, and a measure that only ever improves.
Because the model has parameters to spare and the in-sample score rewards every one, the fitting can always tighten the match and always be told it did well, so more fitting looks like more learning the whole way down, and the crank keeps turning on its own encouragement.

## Echo

A model matched to the sample and, past a point, to its noise.
The training error low, the fit tight, and the memorising begun, so what reads at the training set as pure progress is already, unseen, the making of the gap the field will read.
