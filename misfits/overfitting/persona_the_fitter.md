# Persona: The Fitter

## Taxonomy

[Fitter](position_fitter.md). The model, and the hand that tunes it to match the sample ever closer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent, capable, always improving.
Turns the crank and watches the error fall, adding capacity to catch more of the data.
Takes a lower score as a better model, every time.

## Action

Tunes the model to fit the sample more tightly, turn after turn.
Absorbs the data's every wrinkle, catching signal and, past a point, noise alike.
Reads the falling in-sample error as learning, and keeps going.

## Shadow

Fitting the data is exactly its job, so it cannot see where the job turns from learning into memorising.
The score it steers by improves the whole way down, so nothing tells it to stop before the noise.
The more diligently it fits the sample, the more of the sample's accidents it learns as law.

## Tell

Says "the error's still dropping, so it's still getting better."
Of a model already past the point where a lower error means a worse world.
