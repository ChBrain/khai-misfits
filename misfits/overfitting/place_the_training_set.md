# Place: The Training Set

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The finite sample, where the model is fit and the in-sample score watched.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The data in hand and the model bending to meet it: the curve threading the points, the error falling, the fit tightening turn by turn.
Signal and noise mixed through the sample past telling apart, and the score dropping the whole way down as both are learned.

## Holds

The signal, the noise, the fit, and the score.
Here the true pattern and the sample's accidents lie together, here the model matches them ever more closely, and here the number that guides the search keeps improving, so the training set holds both the evidence and the fit to it, and the measure that cannot tell learning the one from memorising the other.

## Offers

A closer and closer fit, and a falling error to prove it.
The sample can be matched as tightly as the model has capacity for, and the score rewards every turn, so the training set offers the steady, measurable satisfaction of a model getting better on the data in hand.

## Withheld

Any sign of the world beyond the sample.
The training set is one finite draw and the only data the fit and the score can see, so it withholds the world the model is meant to learn, and cannot show where matching the sample stops being learning the pattern.
