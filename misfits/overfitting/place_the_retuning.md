# Place: The Retuning

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). Where the remedy is weighed: complexity penalised, capacity pulled back, the fit loosened against the risk of loosening too far.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The model pulled back from the tightest fit: a parameter penalised, a branch pruned, the crank stopped short of the noise.
The training score worsening on purpose, the holdout error watched to see if the world's error falls with it, and the constant search for how much to let go before real signal goes too.

## Holds

The whole remedy and its whole cost, held together.
Here the fit is deliberately made worse on the sample to make it better on the world, and here the pruning that catches noise threatens the signal beside it, so the retuning holds both the cure for overfitting and its symmetric danger, the underfit waiting on the far side of every cut.

## Offers

A dial, and no mark on it for where to stop.
The retuning offers every degree of pull-back between memorising everything and learning too little, and offers the holdout as a noisy guide, but because signal and noise part only in probability, it offers no certain setting, only a bet on how much complexity the sample can afford.

## Withheld

The exact optimum, and any free correction.
The retuning withholds the true best point, since it cannot be located from one finite sample, and it withholds a cure that costs nothing: every turn away from the overfit is a step toward the underfit, so it can trade one error for the other but never be rid of both.
