# Piece: The Noise

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The accident of the one draw, the part of the data that will not return, learned as though it were law.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the training set](place_the_training_set.md), mixed through the sample past telling from the signal.
The particular scatter of this draw, present here and absent from the next.

## Load Bearing

Without it a model could fit the sample all the way down and lose nothing, and there would be no misfit.
With it the trap is set: the noise is real in the sample and false to the world, it lowers the training error exactly as the signal does, and it is the accident of one draw, so a model with capacity to spare learns it as readily as the pattern and cannot, from the sample alone, be warned off. The Noise is load bearing because it is what the extra fitting captures once the signal is caught (Geman, Bienenstock and Doursat): every turn past the pattern is a turn spent learning noise, and the gap the field reads is the size of what was memorised.

## Apparent

More signal, more progress, a still lower error.
Inside the sample the noise offers itself like any other regularity and drops the score just as the pattern would, so learning it reads as the model getting better, and the truth that it is the one part of the fit certain to be absent next time stays hidden behind how exactly it fits now.

## Yearbook

Tabled as [Pass for Signal](plan_pass_for_signal.md) by [the noise](persona_the_noise.md), learned through [memorising](process_memorising.md).
It is the thing that must not be learned and cannot be told apart in time: the Noise lowers [the score](piece_the_score.md) exactly as [the signal](piece_the_signal.md) does, so its learning tightens [the fit](piece_the_fit.md) and opens [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) that only new data can read.
