# Plot: The Fit

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The training set](place_the_training_set.md), a sample offered and a model with capacity to match it.
The data in hand against the model's reach, and every turn of the crank drops the error, so tightening the fit is, plainly, the work.

## Action

[Fitting](process_fitting.md): [The Fitter](persona_the_fitter.md), holding [the fitter's seat](position_fitter.md), bends the model closer to the sample turn by turn, while [The Sample](persona_the_sample.md), holding [the sample's seat](position_sample.md), offers all it has, signal and noise together, to be matched.

## Stage

[The Fitter](persona_the_fitter.md) tables [Fit It Closer](plan_fit_it_closer.md) and builds [the fit](piece_the_fit.md), catching [the signal](piece_the_signal.md) and driving the training error down.
[The Sample](persona_the_sample.md) tables [Offer the Evidence](plan_offer_the_evidence.md) and lays out the one finite draw, unable to mark where its signal ends and its noise begins.

## Tension

The fit is doing exactly its job, and building the trap while it does.
Nothing here is a mistake: catching the pattern is the whole point, the sample is honest evidence, and the falling score says the model is getting better. But the sample is one draw of signal mixed with noise past telling apart, and the same fitting that catches the pattern will, with no change of feel, keep going into the accidents, so the good work and the overfitting are one motion, and the fit that looks best in hand is on its way past the point worth stopping.
