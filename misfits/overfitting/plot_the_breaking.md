# Plot: The Breaking

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The field](place_the_field.md), the tightest-fit model asked to predict data it never saw.
The same signal returns, but a whole new set of accidents in place of the memorised ones, and the fit built on the old noise meets nothing here to match.

## Action

[Failing](process_failing.md): [The Fitter](persona_the_fitter.md), holding [the fitter's seat](position_fitter.md), sees the model that scored best in hand do worst in use, while [The Unseen](persona_the_unseen.md), holding [the unseen's seat](position_unseen.md), arrives as the new draw and the memorised noise finds nothing to predict.

## Stage

[The Fitter](persona_the_fitter.md), having tabled [Fit It Closer](plan_fit_it_closer.md), watches the closest fit fail hardest, the learned noise mispredicting on data it cannot have seen.
[The Unseen](persona_the_unseen.md) tables [Break the Model](plan_break_the_model.md) and delivers the verdict, the true error rising where [the score](piece_the_score.md) had only fallen.

## Tension

The best model in the room is the one that breaks worst.
There is no misconduct to find: the fitter did the work, the score was honest, the model that fit the sample tightest was, on the sample, genuinely the best. But the closer the fit was pulled to the old draw's noise, the more it staked on accidents that do not return, so the unseen exposes the overfit precisely in proportion to how well it scored, and the breaking lands too late to change the fit, only in time to prove it.
