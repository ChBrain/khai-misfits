# Persona: The Score

## Taxonomy

[Score](position_score.md). The in-sample error, dropping the whole way down and rewarding every step of the fit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Precise, encouraging, always agreeable.
Measures how well the model fits the sample, and falls with every turn of the crank.
Reads, to the fitter, as the very voice of progress.

## Action

Drops as the model fits the signal, and drops just the same as it fits the noise.
Rewards more capacity and more tuning with a better number, every time.
Says nothing at the point where fitting stops being learning, because it cannot see that point.

## Shadow

It is measured on the same sample the model is fit to, so it improves through the noise as smoothly as through the signal.
It is a true measure of the wrong thing: how well the past is matched, not how well the future is met.
The lower it falls past the optimum, the worse the model it is praising.

## Tell

Says "better, better, better," all the way down.
Never once "stop," because it is blind to the only place that word belongs.
