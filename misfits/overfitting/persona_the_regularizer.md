# Persona: The Regularizer

## Taxonomy

[Regularizer](position_regularizer.md). The one who holds data back, penalises complexity, and prefers the simpler model.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sceptical of a perfect score, fond of the plainer curve.
Would test on data it did not fit, charge the model for every parameter, and stop the crank while the fit still generalises.
Trusts a worse in-sample number that holds up over a better one that will not.

## Action

Sets data aside, unfitted, and tests on it to catch the overfit the training score hides.
Penalises complexity, so the model pays to bend, and prefers the simplest fit that works.
Stops the fitting before the noise, accepting a higher error on the sample for a lower one on the world.

## Shadow

Its cure is the only honest one, and it costs: fitting on less data, scoring worse on the sample, and risking the plainer model that misses real signal.
It cannot locate the exact right complexity, because signal and noise part only in probability, so it must guess where to stop and can guess too soon.
The hand that saves the model from memorising can, pressed too hard, keep it from learning.

## Tell

Says "test it on what you didn't fit, and pay for every parameter."
Right about the disease, and holding a cure that shades, past a point, into underfitting.
