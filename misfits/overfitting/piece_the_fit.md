# Piece: The Fit

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The model's match to the sample, tightening turn by turn, catching signal first and then noise with no change of feel.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the training set](place_the_training_set.md), where it is made and measured.
The curve threaded through the points, bending closer with every turn of the crank.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no model and no learning, and without its capacity to reach past the signal there is no overfitting.
With it the mechanism turns: the fit is the match the fitter builds, good and necessary as it catches the pattern, and dangerous only because it does not stop there, tightening on into the noise with the same motion and the same falling score. The Fit is load bearing because it is one continuous act that is learning at first and memorising after (Hastie, Tibshirani and Friedman): nothing in the fitting marks the crossing, so the very capacity that lets it catch the signal lets it swallow the noise, and more fit always looks like more learning.

## Apparent

A better and better model, proven by a lower and lower error.
The tightening fit looks like nothing but progress, each turn closer to the data and cheaper on the score, so the fit reads as pure improvement, and the truth that past a point it is fitting accidents that will not return stays hidden behind how well it matches the sample in hand.

## Yearbook

Made by [the fitter](persona_the_fitter.md) through [fitting](process_fitting.md), tabled as [Fit It Closer](plan_fit_it_closer.md).
It is the act that is right then wrong with no seam between: the Fit catches [the signal](piece_the_signal.md) and then [the noise](piece_the_noise.md), drops [the score](piece_the_score.md) the whole way down, and is pulled back only at [the retuning](place_the_retuning.md), at the risk of loosening into [the underfit](piece_the_underfit.md).
