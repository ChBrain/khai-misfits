# Persona: The Sample

## Taxonomy

[Sample](position_sample.md). The finite data in hand, signal and noise together, and the only evidence there is.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Concrete, particular, all there is to go on.
Holds the pattern the world made and the accidents of one draw of it, mixed past telling apart.
Offers itself as the whole of the evidence, because it is.

## Action

Yields its every point to the fitter, signal and noise indistinguishably.
Rewards a closer fit with a lower error, whichever it is fitting.
Stands in, silently, for a world it is only one finite draw of.

## Shadow

It is the only evidence and it is not the world, so fitting it perfectly is fitting one draw's accidents as well as the world's law.
Inside it, the noise and the signal look exactly alike, so it cannot itself say which is which.
The more completely it is fit, the more its particular accidents are mistaken for the general truth.

## Tell

Says "this is the data: fit it."
Being the only thing there is to fit, and not the thing you meant to learn.
