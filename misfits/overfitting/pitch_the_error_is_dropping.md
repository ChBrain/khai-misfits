# Pitch: The Error Is Dropping

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Rigorous, diligent, improving.
The satisfaction of a model that fits the data better with every turn, its error falling toward zero.
Each step is measured, each step is progress, and the number keeps getting better.

## Undertow

Under the improvement, a fit closing on the noise.
Past a certain point the model stops learning the signal and starts memorising the accidents of this exact sample, and the score, measured on that same sample, keeps rewarding it.
The number falls the whole way down, through the pattern and on into the noise, and never once says stop.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching a model get better and better on the data in hand,
sure it is learning, because the error keeps dropping,
while it quietly memorises a world that will never come again.

## Echo

After the run: a model that explains every point it was shown and breaks on the first it was not,
a training error near zero and a test error high,
and a score that measured, the whole time, the one thing it was not supposed to.
