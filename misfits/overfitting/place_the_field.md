# Place: The Field

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The world the model was meant for, where it meets data it never fit and the true error is finally read.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The model in use, predicting data it did not train on: the same signal returning, and a whole new set of accidents in place of the ones it memorised.
The error rising where the training score had only fallen, and the gap between the two standing open for anyone to see.

## Holds

The gap, and the verdict the score could not give.
Here the model's error on the world is measured at last, against the error it reported on the sample, so the field holds the difference between them, the generalisation gap that is the whole of the overfitting, and holds it as a fact arriving too late to change the fit that caused it.

## Offers

The one honest test, and only after the fit is fixed.
The field offers data the model has not seen and cannot have learned, so its error here is a true reading of how well the pattern was caught, and it offers this reading only once, in use, when the tuning is already done and the verdict can expose the overfit but not undo it.

## Withheld

Any second chance to fit.
The field is not more training data: its accidents are its own and will not be met again, so it withholds the ability to learn from them, and shows the cost of the memorised noise without offering the model a way, here, to unlearn it.
