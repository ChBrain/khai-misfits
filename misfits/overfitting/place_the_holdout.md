# Place: The Holdout

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The data set aside before fitting, kept unlearned so it can stand in for the world and read the gap early.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Data drawn from the same source and then withheld from the fit: points the model never sees while training, saved to be shown only after.
The training score falling as always, and beside it a second number, measured here, that stops falling and turns back up where the fitting crosses into noise.

## Holds

A rehearsal of the field, bought with data not fitted.
Here sits a sample that carries the signal but new accidents, spent not on the fit but on testing it, so the holdout holds an early reading of the gap, the same verdict the field would give but delivered in time to act, at the price of every point kept out of the training.

## Offers

The turning point the training score hides.
Because the holdout is scored but not learned, its error follows the true generalisation, falling while the model catches signal and rising once it starts memorising, so the holdout offers the one in-house sign of where fitting stops being learning, the place to stop the crank.

## Withheld

The data it costs, and certainty about the optimum.
The holdout is points taken from the fit and never returned to it, so it withholds them from the model's learning, and being itself one finite draw, it withholds any exact fix on the best stopping point, only a noisy estimate of where the gap begins to open.
