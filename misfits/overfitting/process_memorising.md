# Process: Memorising

## Taxonomy

[Overfitting](play_overfitting.md). The fitting gone past the signal, learning the sample's accidents as though they were law.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Capacity left over once the signal is caught, and noise still lowering the score.
At [the training set](place_the_training_set.md), the fit reaching past the pattern into the particular draw, and the score falling on just as before.

## Direction

Toward a model that knows this sample and no other.
Once the real pattern is learned, the remaining error is noise, and further fitting can only match that, so the model takes the accidents of the one draw into itself as if they were signal, growing more exact on this sample and less able on any other.

## Lever

A score that cannot tell the two apart.
Because noise lowers the in-sample error exactly as signal does, the measure guiding the fit rewards memorising with the same fall it gives learning, so nothing in the training set marks the turn from one to the other, and the memorising proceeds looking identical to progress.

## Echo

A model fit to accidents that will not return.
The training error lower than the signal alone could make it, the noise learned and waiting to be absent, so the memorising banks a fit that must fail the moment the draw changes, and writes the gap that the failing will later read.
