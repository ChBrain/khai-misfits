# Persona: The Guest

## Taxonomy

[The Passage](position_the_passage.md). A parasite lineage selected only to spread, multiplying inside its host to be passed on and harming the host in the same multiplying, so it does no cruelty and cannot help the cost, settling at the harm that spreads best.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Multiplying to be passed on. Judged only by how far it spreads. Harming in the very act of spreading.
Is the lineage that fills a host with copies of itself so that some may reach the next host, and is measured by nothing but that reach, the strains that spread more persisting and the strains that spread less fading out.
No cruelty wished on them: the Guest means nothing and intends no harm, it only leaves more copies where more copies spread, so the damage it does is not a purpose but a price it pays in the act of spreading, and it is a guest that consumes its lodging because consuming is how it travels on.

## Action

Multiplies inside the host toward transmission, and in the multiplying spends the host, since the replication that makes copies to pass on is the same replication that does the harm, the two the one act.
Pays the harm the spread requires and no more than pays, because a strain harming less than that spreads less and a strain harming more burns its host too fast, so it is drawn to the level in between where its reach is greatest.
Settles where the spread is best and holds there across the generations, outspreading the gentler strains and outlasting the deadlier ones, doing only what a lineage selected to travel does.

## Shadow

From the inside there is no inside for the host: the Guest is all forward, the flat and tireless pressure of one more copy and one more, and nothing in it can register the host it lodges in as anything but the ground it grows through, because it is not the kind of thing that holds a host in view. It does not weigh the harm; it does not know there is harm.
What it cannot do is choose to be gentle. Gentleness is not a thing the Guest could hold even if there were anything in it to want, because a lineage that held its harm down would be outspread by one that did not, and the outspread lineage is the one that vanishes, so restraint is not a road refused but a road that closes on whoever takes it, and the Guest is only ever the descendant of the strains that did not take it.
The bind is that it is most itself exactly when it is most costly: to multiply and to reach and to settle at the level that spreads best is to be a fit parasite in every particular, and the fitness is the harm, so the Guest is never more than a germ spreading well, and the spreading-well is the sickness, with nothing in the germ that could ever tell it so or wish it otherwise.

## Tell

Says "I am only as bad as it pays to be."
Is the whole misfit in one line, since it is true and it is no confession: the Guest is exactly as harmful as spreading rewards, no gentler because gentleness spreads too little and no crueller because cruelty burns the host too fast, so the sentence that sounds like an admission is the plain arithmetic of a lineage settled at the harm that buys the most passage, meaning nothing by it and unable to mean less.
