# Plan: Cut the Route

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Cordon](position_the_cordon.md), inside [Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Cordon](position_the_cordon.md)

## Direction

Close the roads that let the germ spread from a failing host, cleaning the water, cutting the vector, parting the sick from the well, so that only a mobile host still passes it on and lethality is punished where before it was not. Aim at the road and never at the germ, because the seat has no reach into the parasite and every reach into the world it travels, so the plan changes what spread rewards rather than treating the disease. Hold the change and wait on selection, since the milder strains outspread the deadlier only over the generations selection takes, and reckon the limit as you go, because the harm falls slowly and partially and only to the new road's optimum, a disease made milder and never made harmless.
