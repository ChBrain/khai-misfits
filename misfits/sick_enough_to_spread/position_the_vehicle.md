# Position: The Vehicle

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md). The seat of the host, which carries the parasite and is the means of its spread, and cannot be transmitted from without being spent, its own survival no term in what sets the harm it bears.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A body that carries the germ, and a life of its own.
The seat holds the host's tissue in which the parasite multiplies and by which it travels onward, so what it has is the vehicle of the spread, and it holds its own survival, the life the harm is done to.
It holds the constraint that where the germ needs it mobile and well to travel, its health is worth something to the spread, and where the germ can travel from it sick, its health is worth nothing.

## Orders

Carry on, and ride it out.
Bear the parasite and go about living, since a host that stays up and about is a host that can still carry a germ whose road needs it mobile, and survive the infection where survival is possible.
Endure what the carrying costs, because the seat cannot set that cost, only bear it, and its part is to outlast the harm where the harm allows.

## Loses

The separation of carrying from being spent, which the coupling denies.
The Vehicle can carry the germ and cannot carry it cheaply, because the multiplying that makes the host infectious is the same multiplying that harms it, so there is no state in which the seat transmits the parasite and is not spent by it. What the position forfeits is any weight on its own survival in what sets the harm: the parasite's fitness optimises transmission and the host's longevity is not in that sum, so the seat bears a harm decided without reference to it, high where the germ can spread from a failing body and lower only where the germ happens to need it whole. It loses the power to lower its own fare by any effort, since the level is fixed by the germ's road and not by the host's care, so the seat can endure the harm but never negotiate it.

## Drives

Toward a host consumed to the degree the spread requires,
and a seat whose survival is beside the point of the thing riding it, spared only where the germ needs it and spent where the germ does not,
so the position that is the vehicle of the spread is the position that pays for it, carrying and being spent the one act and the fare set by a road it does not lay.
