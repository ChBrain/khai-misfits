# Position: The Passage

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md). The seat of transmission, which a parasite lineage occupies as the thing selected for, measured only by how far it is passed on, and holding no term for the host it spends in the passing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The reach to the next host, and the replication that earns it.
The seat holds the parasite's transmission, the passing-on that is its whole fitness, and the multiplying inside a host that produces the copies to be passed, so what it has is spread and the means of spread in one.
It holds a measure with a single term: how many new hosts the lineage reaches, and nothing beside.

## Orders

Multiply, be passed on, be judged by reach.
Fill the host with copies and send them onward, and let the level of harm fall where the spreading is greatest, since the seat's whole measure is transmission and a lineage that reaches fewer hosts is a lineage replaced.
Answer to nothing but spread, because the seat carries no term for the host's survival and no instruction to spare, so it proceeds by the single rule that more passage is more.

## Loses

The host's survival as a term, and the option of restraint.
The Passage can be selected for spread and cannot be selected to spare, because its measure contains no weight on the host's longevity: the seat optimises transmission alone, so the harm the transmission costs is not a term it can hold down, and a variant that held it down would spread less and be replaced. What the position forfeits is the gentler road: it cannot choose to harm less, since restraint is outspread wherever a rival will not restrain, so the seat has no reachable state below the transmission optimum. It loses, too, any stake past the infectious window, because the seat is done with a host once the germ has been passed on, so the host's fate after carrying is nowhere in what the seat is selected on.

## Drives

Toward a parasite settled at the harm that spreads best,
and a seat that cannot value the host or hold its harm down, restraint foreclosed by the rival who would not hold it,
so the position selected purely to travel is the position that spends the host to the degree the travelling takes, its reach the only term and the harm the price it cannot decline to pay.
