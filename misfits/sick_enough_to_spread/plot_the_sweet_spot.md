# Plot: The Sweet Spot

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Outbreak](place_the_outbreak.md), many strains across many hosts.
Lineages of the germ differing in how hard they harm, tried against each other by how far each spreads.

## Action

[Winnowing](process_winnowing.md): the gentlest strains spread too little and fade, the deadliest burn their hosts before they carry far, and the strain that harms exactly enough to spread most is left in possession of the field.

## Stage

[The Guest](persona_the_guest.md), as many strains from [the Passage](position_the_passage.md), is sorted across the population, [the span](piece_the_span.md) bending the spread back down past a point, until the lineage settles at [the set point](piece_the_set_point.md), the top of [the curve](piece_the_curve.md).
The fittest strain wins, and the fittest strain is the one whose harm buys the most passage.

## Tension

The best strain is neither the kindest nor the cruellest, and the best is what wins.
There is no design: the sorting is only by reach, so the gentle strain is not spared for its mercy but dropped for its weakness, and the deadly one not punished for its harm but for burning the hosts that would have carried it, each outcome the plain result of what spreads. But the level that spreads best is sicker than any host would keep, so the plot closes with the disease settled at its transmission optimum and held there, a severity chosen by no one and fixed by everyone's spread, durable because a gentler strain would be outspread and a harsher one would burn out, the sweet spot for the germ's passage the worst the hosts can be made to bear and still carry it.
