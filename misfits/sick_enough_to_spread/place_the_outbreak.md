# Place: The Outbreak

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md). The population of hosts over many infections and generations, the arena where strains compete by their spread and the transmission-optimal one wins, the harm decided not in any host but across them all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An epidemic running through a population, a disease on the move.
Shown at the surface is spread at scale: many hosts infected, the germ passing from one to the next through a population, the case counts rising and falling, a disease doing what a disease does across a people.

## Holds

Holds the contest of strains, and the winnowing it is.
The Outbreak is where many lineages of the germ meet many hosts across many generations, so it holds the competition by which the strain that spreads most comes to dominate: the gentlest strains, spreading too little, fade, and the deadliest, burning their hosts before they carry far, fade too, so the place holds the sorting that leaves the transmission-optimal strain in possession of the field.

## Offers

Offers the germ its selection, and the host the level that selection settles.
Here the strains are tried against each other by their reach, so the Outbreak offers the parasite the winnowing that finds the harm that spreads best; and offers, through the same winnowing, the harm the host will bear, since the level is set across the population and delivered to each host as the strain that won, and where hosts carry more than one strain the contest within them can push that level higher still, the shared host over-spent by rivals none of which can hold back.

## Withheld

Withholds a winner that is not the transmission-optimal strain, and any gentler level the population would keep.
What the Outbreak cannot offer is a strain that wins by sparing rather than spreading, because the sorting is by reach alone, so a gentler lineage that would serve the hosts better is not selected but outspread. The place that decides the harm holds no mechanism for the hosts' preference, only for the germ's spread, so the level that settles is the one that travels furthest and not the one a host would choose, and it is durable, since any strain that undercut it in mercy would be undercut in reach.
