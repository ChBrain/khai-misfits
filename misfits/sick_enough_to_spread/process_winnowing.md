# Process: Winnowing

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md). The sorting of strains by their spread across the population, which discards the too gentle and the too deadly and leaves the transmission-optimal one, settling virulence at an intermediate level and holding it there.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Guest](persona_the_guest.md) as many strains, at [the Outbreak](place_the_outbreak.md), each tried against the others by its reach.
Begins wherever many lineages of the germ meet many hosts across enough generations to be sorted.

## Direction

Turns a spread of strains into a settled level of harm.
Because a strain that harms too little spreads too little and a strain that harms too much burns its hosts before they carry it far, winnowing discards both tails and keeps the strain whose harm buys the most passage, so the process converts variation in virulence into a single durable optimum, the level that maximises the reproduction number and neither the least harm nor the most.

## Lever

The peak of the reproduction number between too gentle and too deadly.
Because transmission rises with the parasite's replication while a host once killed transmits no more, fitness peaks at an intermediate virulence, so winnowing works through that peak: the lever is selection on reach, which rewards harm up to the point where more harm shortens the host's carrying more than it adds to the spread, and punishes it past there, fixing the germ at the top of the curve. Where hosts carry rival strains, the sorting inside them can push that peak higher, a competition among lineages that lifts the optimum but does not create it.

## Echo

A lineage settled at the harm that spreads best, and held there.
The echo of winnowing is a disease fixed at its transmission optimum, sicker than the host would choose and stable, since any strain that undercut it in harm would be undercut in spread and any that exceeded it would burn out, so the level does not drift toward mercy but sits where reach is greatest, a durable equilibrium and not a passing severity, kept in place by the same sorting that found it.
