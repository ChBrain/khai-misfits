# Plot: The Lodging

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Reservoir](place_the_reservoir.md), a parasite taking hold.
A host newly infected, and a germ beginning to multiply toward being passed on.

## Action

[Multiplying](process_multiplying.md): the Guest fills the host with copies of itself to transmit, and the same multiplying that stocks the spread wears the host down, the two the one act.

## Stage

[The Guest](persona_the_guest.md) acts from [the Passage](position_the_passage.md) on [Get Passed On](plan_get_passed_on.md), bringing [the curve](piece_the_curve.md) into play as it multiplies, stocking the spread and spending [the span](piece_the_span.md) in the same replication.
The germ is readying to travel, and the host is being drawn down to ready it.

## Tension

The germ does nothing but spread, and the spreading is the harm.
There is no cruelty here: the parasite multiplies because multiplying is how it is passed on, and the copies it makes are built from the host, so the damage is not an aim laid on top of the spread but the spread's own substance, the germ harming in the only act it has. But the copies and the cost are the one replication, so the plot closes with a host stocked full of the germ to pass on and drawn down exactly as full as it is stocked, the transmission and the harm bound in the lodging, and no way for the germ to take the room without spending the house.
