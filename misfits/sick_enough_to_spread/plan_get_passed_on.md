# Plan: Get Passed On

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Passage](position_the_passage.md), inside [Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Passage](position_the_passage.md)

## Direction

Multiply inside the host and reach the next one, filling the body with copies and sending them onward, since the seat's whole measure is transmission and a lineage that reaches fewer hosts is a lineage replaced. Let the harm fall where the spreading is greatest and read it as no concern, because the seat carries no term for the host's survival, so it neither spares the host nor spends it on purpose, only pays whatever damage the best spread costs. Hold no restraint, since a strain that harmed less than the spread rewards would be outspread and lost, so the plan reaches for the level that travels furthest and lets the host bear the fare, blind to the cost because the cost is nowhere in what it is judged by.

## Orders

([The Guest](persona_the_guest.md) multiplies toward transmission, harms the host in the multiplying, and settles at the level that spreads best, meaning nothing by the harm and unable to hold it down.)

## Implementation

([The Reservoir](place_the_reservoir.md); through the [multiplying](process_multiplying.md) that couples spread to harm, winnowed across [the Outbreak](place_the_outbreak.md) toward the level that travels furthest; the Guest brings [the curve](piece_the_curve.md) into play and spends [the span](piece_the_span.md), settling at [the set point](piece_the_set_point.md) it cannot fall below.)

## Targets

- [ ] the host is filled with copies and the germ passed on
- [ ] the harm is paid where the spreading is greatest, no term held for the host
- [ ] no restraint is kept, since a gentler strain is outspread
- [ ] the lineage settles at the level that travels furthest, the fare left to the host
