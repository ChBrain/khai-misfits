# Plot: The Turning

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Route](place_the_route.md), someone who would lower the harm.
A disease settled at the harm that spreads best, and a road that can be changed though the germ cannot be reached.

## Action

[Attenuating](process_attenuating.md): the Sanitarian cleans the water, cuts the vector, parts the sick from the well, so that only a mobile host still passes the germ on, and lethality becomes a dead end selection now turns against.

## Stage

[The Sanitarian](persona_the_sanitarian.md) acts from [the Cordon](position_the_cordon.md) on [Cut the Route](plan_cut_the_route.md), shifting [the set point](piece_the_set_point.md) downward by changing what [the curve](piece_the_curve.md) rewards, and waits on the winnowing to pull the harm toward mild.
The road is closed, and over seasons the disease evolves kinder, not by mercy but because gentleness now spreads best.

## Tension

The harm falls at last, and it falls only because the road that paid for it was shut.
There is no cure in the germ: the Sanitarian cannot ask the parasite to spare the host, since the harm is set by its spread, so the only move is oblique, a change to the water and the vectors and the keeping of the sick that leaves the germ untouched and rearranges the world it travels through. And the change is slow and partial and costly, worked in infrastructure and paid in years, and even done it lowers the harm only to the new road's optimum and not to none, as the myxoma virus fell to a milder grade and no further. So the plot closes with a disease made kinder from outside and never from within, the germ no gentler in itself but selected toward gentleness by a road that now rewards it, a mercy built into the world and bounded by the level the new road still pays.
