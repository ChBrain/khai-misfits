# Piece: The Set Point

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md). The phenomenon whole: the durable virulence selection settles a parasite at, the harm that spreads best, sicker than the host would choose, held in place because a gentler strain is outspread.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Reached across [the Outbreak](place_the_outbreak.md), set by the character of [the Route](place_the_route.md).
Flows as the level the whole trap composes, the trade-off climbed to its peak, the harm the host bears and the germ cannot fall below, an equilibrium that sits and stays.

## Load Bearing

Without the Set Point there is no misfit, only a fact about a germ: it is the Set Point that ties the curve to the span to the fare, the one outcome in which a parasite selected purely to spread comes to rest at a harm that is worse than it need be and cannot be talked down.
The Set Point is load bearing because it is the phenomenon entire and the seat of the misfit's whole point, a virulence fixed at the transmission optimum by selection and held there as a durable equilibrium, sicker than any host would choose and held there because a gentler strain would simply be outspread (Anderson and May; Ewald). It carries the whole trap: the Curve is the coupling it sits on, the Span is the arm that keeps it intermediate, the Fare is the harm it fixes, so the Set Point is not one more piece beside the others but the level they compose. And it is where the misfit parts from a collapse, since the germ does not crash its hosts to extinction but settles among them at a bad, stable level and stays, the harm persisting rather than passing, a durable bad bargain and not a self-termination. It is built of nothing but fitness, each strain reaching for the spread and the winning one paying its spread in harm, so it cannot be argued down: there is no germ to appeal to and no restraint that a rival will not undercut, only a level that holds because the strain which harms exactly enough to spread most is the strain that inherits the world, and moves only when the road beneath it moves.

## Apparent

Just how bad a disease is, the settled severity of a known germ.
Seen at the surface, the Set Point looks like a fixed fact of a disease, its characteristic severity, a thing simply true of the germ, and the truth that it is an equilibrium and not a trait, that it sits where the spread rewards and would move if the road did, that it is higher than the hosts would keep and durable only because mercy is outspread, stays hidden behind how settled a stable severity seems, as though the germ were just that bad by nature.
