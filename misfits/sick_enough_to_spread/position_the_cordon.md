# Position: The Cordon

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md). The seat of the remedy, which can change how the disease travels but cannot touch the germ directly, so it lowers virulence only by closing the roads that reward it, slowly, partially, and never to none.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The power over the road, and none over the germ.
The seat holds the levers of transmission ecology, the clean water, the killed vector, the parted sick and well, so what it has is the ability to change which routes are open to the parasite.
It holds the knowledge that closing a route that carries the germ from a failing host shifts what the germ's spread rewards, and so, over time, what selection settles its harm at.

## Orders

Close the roads that reward lethality, and let selection do the rest.
Cut the transmission that does not need a healthy host, so that only a mobile one can pass the germ on, and hold that change long enough for the milder strains to outspread the deadlier, since the seat's move is to the ecology and not to the disease.
Aim at the road and not the germ, because the seat has no reach into the parasite and every reach into the world it travels through.

## Loses

The direct lever on virulence, which the seat does not hold.
The Cordon can change the road and cannot change the germ, because it has no means to lower the parasite's harm in the parasite: it can only shift the optimum by altering what spread rewards, so its remedy is always indirect, applied to the water and the vector and the ward and never to the disease. What the position forfeits is a cure in the pathogen and a fast one: the change is to infrastructure and works only over the generations selection takes, so the harm falls slowly and partially, and only to the new road's optimum, never to zero. It loses, too, the completeness of the fix, since a germ with more than one road keeps the harm the open roads still reward, so the seat that closes one route has lowered the harm only by as much as that route paid.

## Drives

Toward a remedy oblique and costly and slow,
and a harm loosened from outside and never in the germ, pared to the new road's optimum and not below it,
so the seat that alone can lower the harm is the seat that cannot touch the thing that does it, changing the world the disease moves through and waiting on selection, a mercy built into the road and never granted to the germ.
