# Place: The Reservoir

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md). The host's body as the pool the parasite multiplies in, where the copies that will be passed on are made and, in the making, the host is spent, the one place where the spread and the harm are the same act.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A body carrying an infection, doing what an infected body does.
Shown at the surface is illness in its ordinary form: a host in which a germ has taken hold and is multiplying, the symptoms of a disease running its course, the body labouring to clear what has lodged in it.

## Holds

Holds the multiplying, and the spending it is.
The Reservoir is where the parasite makes its copies, so it holds the replication that is at once the source of the spread and the cause of the harm, keeping the two inseparable: the fuller the host of copies to pass on, the more it has been consumed to make them, so the place that supplies the next host is the place the present one is spent in.

## Offers

Offers the copies for the spread, and the host's substance to make them from.
Here the germ multiplies toward transmission, so the Reservoir offers the parasite everything it needs to reach the next host; and offers, through the same multiplying, the host itself as the material, since the copies are built from the host and the building is the harm, so the pool that stocks the spread is drained to stock it, and some of what it spends buys no spread at all, a harm the germ does in tissue it will never travel from.

## Withheld

Withholds a multiplying that does not spend the host, and any spread bought cheap.
What the Reservoir cannot offer is copies without cost, because the making of them is the harm, so there is no version of the infection that fills the host to pass the germ on and does not draw the host down. The place that stocks the spread holds no stock that is not the host's own substance, so the germ cannot be supplied except at the host's expense, and the host cannot host the germ except by being consumed to the degree the spreading takes.
