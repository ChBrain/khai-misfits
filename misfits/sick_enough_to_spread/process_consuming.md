# Process: Consuming

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md). The spending of the host at the level the spread requires, the harm the parasite's replication does as it builds its copies, the fare the host pays for a passage it never chose.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Carrier](persona_the_carrier.md) as the one it happens to, at [the Reservoir](place_the_reservoir.md), spent as the germ multiplies within.
Begins as the parasite replicates, since the replication is the harm and the harm falls on the host it happens in.

## Direction

Turns a host into the material of the spread.
Because the copies to be passed on are made from the host and the making is the damage, consuming converts the host's own substance into the germ's transmission, so the process spends the host to the degree the spreading takes, drawing it down not by any attack but by being the thing the germ is built from, the carrying and the wasting one.

## Lever

The host as the stuff the germ is made of.
Because the parasite has nothing to build its copies from but the host, consuming works through the plain fact that the germ's increase is the host's decrease: the lever is that the spread is paid in the host's substance, so the harm is not inflicted on the host from outside but drawn out of it from within, the fare taken in the same act that loads the germ for its passage, and some of it taken in tissue that carries the germ nowhere.

## Echo

A host spent to the measure of the spread, and no enemy to show for it.
The echo of consuming is a body harmed exactly to the degree the germ's transmission required, a fare paid and no malice behind it, so the host bears a sickness set by the spread and not by any will, worse than it would choose, the damage the price of a passage and the host the one who paid it.
