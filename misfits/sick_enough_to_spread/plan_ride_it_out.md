# Plan: Ride It Out

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Vehicle](position_the_vehicle.md), inside [Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Vehicle](position_the_vehicle.md)

## Direction

Carry the parasite and go about living, staying up and about and enduring the infection, since a host that keeps moving is a host whose survival is worth something to a germ whose road needs it mobile, and a host that outlasts the illness is one the germ did not spend. Bear the harm the carrying costs without any means to set it, because the level is fixed by the germ's road and not by the host's care, so the plan can endure the fare but never lower it. Hope the road is one that needs the host alive, since where the germ can spread from a failing body the host's survival is no term in the harm, so the plan's whole reach is to last through what it cannot change and to matter to the germ only where the germ needs it whole.
