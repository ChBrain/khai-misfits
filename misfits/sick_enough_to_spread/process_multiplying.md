# Process: Multiplying

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md). The replication of the parasite inside its host, the making of the copies to be passed on, which is at once the source of the spread and the cause of the harm, the coupling at the root.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Guest](persona_the_guest.md), at [the Reservoir](place_the_reservoir.md), filling the host with copies of itself.
Begins when a parasite takes hold in a host and starts to replicate toward being passed on.

## Direction

Turns a host into a source of the germ, and spends it in the turning.
Because the parasite spreads by multiplying and multiplying is what draws the host down, multiplying converts the host at once into a stock of copies to transmit and into a body consumed to make them, so the process is the coupling itself, the single act that supplies the spread and does the harm, neither reachable without the other.

## Lever

The identity of the spread and its cost.
Because the copies that will be passed on are built from the host and the building is the damage, multiplying works through a coupling that cannot be undone: the lever is that transmission and harm are the same replication, so the germ cannot make more of what it spreads without spending more of what it spreads through, and there is no throttle that raises the copies and spares the host.

## Echo

A host full of the germ to pass on, and drawn down to fill it.
The echo of multiplying is a body stocked with copies for the next host and spent in the stocking, so the parasite stands ready to spread and the host stands harmed to the degree it is ready, the same replication having furnished the transmission and taken the toll, and some of it having harmed in tissue the germ will never travel from, a cost that bought no spread at all.
