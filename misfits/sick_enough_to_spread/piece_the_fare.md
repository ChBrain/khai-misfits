# Piece: The Fare

## Taxonomy

[Sick Enough to Spread](play_sick_enough_to_spread.md). The harm the host bears, the price paid in its own substance for a passage it never chose, some of it the coupled cost of the spread and some of it a byproduct that buys the germ nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Drawn from [the Reservoir](place_the_reservoir.md), out of the host's own body.
Flows as the damage of the infection, the wasting the host undergoes as the germ multiplies within, taken from it in the same act that loads the germ for its onward journey.

## Load Bearing

Without the Fare the trade-off would cost nothing: it is the harm borne by the host that makes the spread a price and not a free good, the damage that the coupling delivers and the host pays.
The Fare is load bearing because it is the cost side made concrete, the virulence itself as it falls on the host, the parasite-induced harm the trade-off is measured in (Anderson and May). It is drawn from the host's own substance, since the germ is built from the host and the building is the damage, so the Fare is not inflicted from outside but taken from within, in the same replication that stocks the spread. And not all of it buys spread: some harm is done in tissue the germ will never travel from, a short-sighted cost that aids no transmission at all (Levin and Bull), so the host pays even for damage the germ gets nothing back for, the whole of it borne by the one that never booked the passage.

## Apparent

Just the symptoms, the toll of being ill.
Seen at the surface, the Fare looks like the ordinary suffering of a disease, the fever and the wasting and the worse, and the truth that it is a fare, a price paid in the host's own body for the germ's passage, that it is set by what the germ's spread costs and not by any malice, that part of it is spent for nothing even the germ can use, stays hidden behind how much the harm of an illness feels like an attack rather than a bill.

## Yearbook

Paid by [the Carrier](persona_the_carrier.md) out of itself, exacted by [the Guest](persona_the_guest.md) without meaning it, and lowered by [the Sanitarian](persona_the_sanitarian.md) only by changing what the passage costs.
It is the borne harm: the Fare is what [consuming](process_consuming.md) draws from the host, the price [the curve](piece_the_curve.md) sets and [the set point](piece_the_set_point.md) fixes, the toll the host pays that no [winnowing](process_winnowing.md) counts on its behalf, since the sorting weighs only the germ's spread and never the host's cost.
