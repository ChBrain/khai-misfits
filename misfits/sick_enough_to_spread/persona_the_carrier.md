# Persona: The Carrier

## Taxonomy

[The Vehicle](position_the_vehicle.md). The host that carries the parasite and passes it on, spent in the carrying to the degree the germ's spread requires, harmed not by any malice but as the fare of a passage it never chose.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Carrying the germ, and passing it on. Wasted in the carrying. Made sicker than any sense would choose.
Is the host in which the parasite multiplies and from which it travels, the ground the germ grows through and the vehicle that bears it to the next, harmed exactly to the degree the spreading takes.
No fault wished on them: the Carrier did nothing to earn the harm and holds no enemy that meant it, only a lodger that spends the lodging because spending is how it moves on, so the sickness is a cost visited by no one and borne all the same.

## Action

Carries the parasite as it multiplies, becoming the vehicle of its spread, so that the host's own body is the means by which the germ reaches the next.
Bears the harm bound to that spread, since the host cannot be a vehicle without being spent, the replication that fills it to pass the germ on the same that wears it down.
Survives or does not by where the optimum sits, spared where the germ needs it mobile and healthy to travel and consumed where the germ can spread from a body already failing.

## Shadow

What is hardest from the inside is that there is no one to blame and it does not help. The Carrier is made grievously sick and can find no cruelty behind it, no enemy that willed the harm, only a thing that is spreading, so the suffering has the wrong shape for anger and the wrong shape for bargaining, a wound with no wounder to appeal to.
What wears is being beside the point of the thing that is killing it. The germ does not hate the Carrier and does not need it either, past the window in which it carries, so the host feels itself used as a road is used, its own survival simply not a term in what is deciding how sick it gets, a life reduced to a distance the germ travels over.
The bind is that its carrying and its wasting are one: to be the vehicle is to be spent, since the very multiplying that makes it infectious is the multiplying that harms it, so the Carrier cannot hold the germ without paying for it, and cannot pay less by any effort of its own, only endure the fare set by how the germ travels and hope the road it is on is one that needs it alive.

## Tell

Says "I am carrying the thing that is spending me."
Is the whole misfit in one line, since both halves are the same act: the carrying is the spending, because the germ travels by the multiplying that harms, so the sentence that sounds like a complaint is the plain description of a host that is the vehicle and the fare at once, wasted not against its use but by it.
