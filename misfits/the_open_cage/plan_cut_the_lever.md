# Plan: Cut the Lever

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the overrider's seat](position_overrider.md), inside [The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Overrider](position_overrider.md)

## Direction

Make the Subject's action not count. Override the initiative, ignore the input, deliver the same outcome whatever they do, and sever effort from result, whether by design, by constraint, or by plain indifference. Leave action and outcome with nothing to do with each other.

## Orders

([The Overrider](persona_the_overrider.md) severs action from outcome, by intent or by chance, teaching the Subject that effort does not pay, and often moving on before the lesson does.)

## Implementation

([The Grind](place_the_grind.md); the [overriding](process_overriding.md) process; the severance is [the uncoupling](piece_the_uncoupling.md) that teaches [the expectation](piece_the_expectation.md), the lesson left whether or not harm was meant.)

## Targets

- [ ] the Subject's action is made not to count, the outcome the same whatever they do
- [ ] effort and result are severed, by design, constraint, or indifference
- [ ] the lesson that effort does not pay is left, whatever the intent behind it
- [ ] the severer moves on or relents long before the expectation it planted does
