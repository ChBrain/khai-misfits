# Piece: The Dead Lever

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The control that has returned and goes untested, the lever that now works but is never pulled, the available escape the expectation forbids.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Waits at [the open door](place_the_open_door.md), a working lever the Subject treats as dead.
Would be revived only at [the ramp](place_the_ramp.md), where the Coach makes its working felt.

## Load Bearing

Without it the trap would be a real cage, and the staying no misfit but a fact.
With it the trap turns cruel: the control has genuinely come back, the lever works, the door is open, and the Subject does not test it, so the escape is real and the confinement self-imposed. The Dead Lever is load bearing because it is the returned control the expectation will not let be tried (Maier and Seligman): the working mechanism treated as broken, so the piece carries the gap between the world, in which action now pays, and the actor, for whom it does not, the live handle the caged one is certain is dead.

## Apparent

A dead thing, a lever plainly not worth pulling, an option not really there.
Seen at the surface, the Dead Lever looks to the Subject like no option at all, a handle that will not turn, and the truth that it works, that a single test would show it, that only the expectation and not the lever is holding the door shut, stays hidden behind how completely a control one is certain is absent reads as a control that is not there.

## Yearbook

Left untested by [the subject](persona_the_subject.md) under [the expectation](piece_the_expectation.md), the returned control treated as dead.
It is the cruelty of the trap: the Dead Lever is the working escape [resigning](process_resigning.md) will not let be tried and [the judge](persona_the_judge.md) condemns the Subject for refusing, the live handle only [restoring](process_restoring.md) can make felt again, the open door at the centre of the [open cage](piece_the_open_cage.md).
