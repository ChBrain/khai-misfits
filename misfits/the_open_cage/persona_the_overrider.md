# Persona: The Overrider

## Taxonomy

[Overrider](position_overrider.md). The source of the powerlessness, by design or by chance, who severs the Subject's action from its outcome so that effort counts for nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Arbitrary. Immovable. Beyond appeal.
Is whatever makes the Subject's effort not count, the manager who overrides every initiative, the system that ignores the input, the run of events no action reaches.
No cackling in it: often the Overrider is itself constrained, or indifferent, or simply the way a situation happens to fall, not a tormentor by intent.

## Action

Cuts action from outcome, so that whatever the Subject does, the result is the same.
Overrides the initiative, ignores the effort, delivers the outcome regardless, until effort and result have plainly nothing to do with each other.
Does what an uncontrollable situation does: it makes the Subject's actions not matter, whether or not anyone means it to.

## Shadow

Not always a villain, often no one's design, and that is part of the difficulty: the powerlessness can be real without a tormentor, a system or a season that simply does not answer to effort.
Because the Subject learns from the contingency and not from the intent, it makes no difference whether the Overrider meant harm: the lesson taught is the same, effort does not pay, so a merely indifferent hand teaches helplessness as surely as a cruel one, and the Overrider often moves on or relents long before the lesson it left does.
It severs action from outcome, and the severance teaches, whatever the heart behind it.

## Tell

Says "nothing personal: that's just how it is here."
Means it, often, and never sees that "how it is here" is the whole of what the Subject is learning, and will carry elsewhere.
