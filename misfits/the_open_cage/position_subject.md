# Position: Subject

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The seat that learns effort does not pay and goes on not trying once control returns, held shut by a true lesson kept past its expiry.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A stretch of real powerlessness behind it and a lesson learned from it: the standing of one who found, correctly, that action and outcome had come apart, and stopped spending effort.
A clear case: in that situation, trying genuinely did not help, so ceasing to try was accurate, not weak.
No way to feel that the situation has changed, and no reason, from inside, to test a control it expects is not there.

## Orders

Stop trying.
Cease to spend effort on a thing that does not respond, take what comes, and carry the lesson forward, so no door is tested and no lever tried.
Do not waste yourself on what has taught you it will not answer.

## Loses

The chance to find that control has returned, exactly by keeping the lesson that it has not.
Because the expectation that nothing it does matters is a true lesson generalised, the seat carries it into a changed room and, expecting no control, will not perform the one act that would reveal the door is open, so it cannot correct the expectation from within, its own accuracy in the old room holding it shut in the new.
It gains the truth of the room that taught it and loses the truth of every room after, staying where the door has opened.

## Drives

Toward effort withheld and the open door unwalked,
bought against the control it will not test for,
so the seat that learned the truth of one room is held by it in a room where it is false.
