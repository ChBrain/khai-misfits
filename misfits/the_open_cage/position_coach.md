# Position: Coach

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The seat that would restore the expectation of control by graded, unmissable demonstration, at the cost of spending the effort the Subject will not.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A refusal to take the passivity as character and a method to change it: the standing to arrange the small win the Subject cannot fail to see, and the knowledge that expectation shifts on evidence, not exhortation.
A clear case: an expectation of no-control is changed by a felt link between action and outcome, supplied from outside when the actor will not seek it.
No way to make the Subject test control for itself, and no cover from the charge of coddling the workshy.

## Orders

Show it works.
Arrange a step small enough that the Subject cannot miss their action landing, credit the passivity to its cause, and repeat the demonstration until the expectation shifts.
Supply the experience of control the Subject will not seek, one rung at a time.

## Loses

The ease of leaving the actor to try for itself, exactly by doing for it what it cannot yet do.
Because the Subject expects no control and will not test for it, the seat must arrange the test on their behalf, against their settled certainty and the Judge's verdict of laziness, and can only install the expectation of control rung by rung, never summon it whole, so the freeing move is slow, effortful, easily called coddling, and never finished in a single demonstration.
It buys the Subject's expectation restored against the effort it must carry for them, and is taken, for a while, as the one who excuses the workshy.

## Drives

Toward control shown small and felt and the expectation restored,
bought against the effort the Subject will not spend and the charge of coddling,
so the one seat that would free the caged must carry, again and again, the trying the cage taught them to withhold.
