# Piece: The Open Cage

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The phenomenon whole, a true lesson of powerlessness carried past its expiry, so the actor stays in a cage whose door has opened.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across all three: taught in [the grind](place_the_grind.md), suffered at [the open door](place_the_open_door.md), reversed only at [the ramp](place_the_ramp.md).
It is the whole circuit, the escapable trap not escaped.

## Load Bearing

Without it the pieces are separate: a severance, an expectation, a working lever, a misjudgement, a missing history, with nothing to bind them.
With it they are one mechanism: the uncoupling teaches the expectation, the expectation outlives the room and leaves the dead lever untested, the verdict blames the giving-up and deepens it, and only the immunity restored can open the door, each part holding the next. The Open Cage is load bearing because it is the structure entire (Seligman and Maier; Abramson, Seligman, and Teasdale; Maier and Seligman, 2016): learned helplessness, a true lesson kept past its expiry, so the piece carries the trap whole, the reason an actor stays where the door has opened and no one need be lazy or cruel for a cage to hold that is no longer locked.

## Apparent

Laziness, or realism, an actor who has stopped trying and either knows better or lacks the drive.
Seen at the surface, the whole trap looks like a person who will not help themselves, and the truth that the giving-up was a true lesson learned, that the lesson has outlived its cause, that the door is open and unfelt and the passivity taught rather than chosen, stays hidden behind how exactly like either wisdom or sloth a settled refusal to try appears.

## Yearbook

Assembled from every element: [the uncoupling](piece_the_uncoupling.md) that teaches, [the expectation](piece_the_expectation.md) that carries, [the dead lever](piece_the_dead_lever.md) left untested, [the verdict](piece_the_verdict.md) that blames, and [the immunity](piece_the_immunity.md) that would free.
It is the trap named: the Open Cage binds [overriding](process_overriding.md), [resigning](process_resigning.md), and [judging](process_judging.md) into one held-shut door, opened only where [restoring](process_restoring.md) shows control has returned.
