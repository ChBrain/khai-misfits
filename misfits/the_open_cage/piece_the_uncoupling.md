# Piece: The Uncoupling

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The severance of action from outcome, the objective contingency in which what the Subject does makes no difference to what results, the cause of the lesson.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs through [the grind](place_the_grind.md), where effort meets no answer.
Ends, unnoticed, at [the open door](place_the_open_door.md), where the coupling has quietly returned.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to learn and no lesson to carry, only an actor who could act and does.
With it the trap has its cause: a real stretch in which outcomes are independent of responses, so that trying and not trying come to the same thing and the Subject's effort genuinely does not count. The Uncoupling is load bearing because it is the objective contingency the whole trap grows from (Seligman and Maier): the severance of action from outcome, so the piece carries the true powerlessness that teaches the lesson, the fact about one room that is accurate while it lasts and disastrous once carried past it.

## Apparent

Bad luck, or a hard situation, a stretch where effort simply did not pay off, nothing more.
Seen at the surface, the Uncoupling looks like a rough patch to be waited out, and the truth that it is teaching a lesson that will generalise and persist, that the Subject is learning not just about this room but about acting anywhere, that a contingency true only here and now is being written as a truth without limit, stays hidden behind how ordinary a stretch of effort-without-reward appears.
