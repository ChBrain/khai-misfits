# Piece: The Verdict

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The misattribution of the passivity to character, the giving-up read as a stable flaw, laziness or want of grit, that deepens the trap and lands as one more uncontrollable outcome.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Handed down at [the open door](place_the_open_door.md), where the unwalked escape is read as a flaw.
Overturned only at [the ramp](place_the_ramp.md), where the passivity is credited to its cause.

## Load Bearing

Without it the passivity would be merely unfortunate, and the Subject not blamed for what was taught.
With it the trap acquires a second turn: the observer reads the not-trying as a stable trait in the person, credits a flaw where there is a taught expectation, and blames the Subject, so the misdiagnosis both misses the cause and, landing as one more outcome no effort can change, feeds the very passivity it names. The Verdict is load bearing because it is the attribution the trap runs through (Abramson, Seligman, and Teasdale): the internal, stable, global reading of the giving-up, so the piece carries the misjudgement that turns a taught helplessness into a character indictment and deepens it in the reading.

## Apparent

A fair assessment, the plain and reasonable judgement that someone who will not try lacks drive.
Seen at the surface, the Verdict looks like accountability, holding a person to a standard of effort, and the truth that it mistakes a taught expectation for a flaw, that it blames the Subject for a thing that was done to them, that the blame becomes another lever pressed in vain, stays hidden behind how fair it seems to call a refusal to try a want of will.

## Yearbook

Handed down by [the judge](persona_the_judge.md) through [judging](process_judging.md), the passivity read as character.
It is the second turn of the trap: the Verdict misreads [the dead lever](piece_the_dead_lever.md) left untested as a flaw, missing [the uncoupling](piece_the_uncoupling.md) behind it, and lands as one more outcome that deepens [the expectation](piece_the_expectation.md), the blame that hardens the [open cage](piece_the_open_cage.md).
