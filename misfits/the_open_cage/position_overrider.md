# Position: Overrider

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The seat that severs the Subject's action from its outcome, by design or by chance, teaching that effort does not pay.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A grip on the outcome the Subject cannot reach: the standing to override the initiative, ignore the input, or deliver the result regardless.
A clear case, sometimes: the override is warranted, the system genuinely cannot answer, the season simply does not turn on any one actor's effort.
No stake in the lesson it leaves, and often no intent to leave one, moving on or relenting long before the expectation it planted does.

## Orders

Cut the lever.
Make the Subject's action not count, override what they attempt, ignore what they offer, and deliver the same outcome whatever they do.
Sever effort from result, whether by design, by constraint, or by indifference.

## Loses

Nothing it was holding, and that is part of the difficulty: the seat spends no cost to teach the lesson, and often does not know it taught one.
Because the Subject learns from the contingency and not the intent, the seat leaves an expectation of no-control behind it whether it meant harm or merely happened, so an indifferent or constrained hand teaches helplessness as surely as a cruel one, and the seat is long gone or long since relented while the lesson it left holds the Subject shut.
It loses nothing and leaves everything, a severance that costs the severer little and teaches the severed a truth they carry for years.

## Drives

Toward action severed from outcome and the lesson left,
bought against nothing the seat itself must pay,
so the seat that made effort not count is gone before the actor it taught stops paying for it.
