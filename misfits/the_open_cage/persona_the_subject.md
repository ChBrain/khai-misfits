# Persona: The Subject

## Taxonomy

[Subject](position_subject.md). The one who learns, correctly, that effort does not pay, stops trying, and goes on not trying once control returns, staying in a cage whose door has opened.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Resigned. Calm. Done reaching.
Is the one who has been through a stretch where nothing they did changed anything, and who has stopped trying, quietly, because trying never worked.
No sulking in it: only an actor who learned the truth of a hopeless situation and stopped spending effort on it.

## Action

Stops trying, and takes what comes, having learned that here effort and outcome have nothing to do with each other.
Carries the lesson forward, so when a door opens and control returns, it does not test the lever or try the handle.
Does what a well-taught actor does: it does not waste effort on a thing it has learned does not respond.

## Shadow

Not a quitter without cause, an actor taught by a real powerlessness, and that is the whole difficulty: the giving-up was the correct response, and correctness is what makes it stubborn.
Because the expectation that nothing it does matters is a true lesson generalised, the Subject carries it into a changed room where it is false, and, expecting no control, will not perform the one act that would reveal control has returned, so it cannot correct the expectation from within, the door standing open and its own certainty holding it shut.
It learned the truth of one room, and the truth of that room keeps it from testing the next.

## Tell

Says "why bother: it never makes any difference."
Says it out of hard-won experience, and cannot feel that the difference it once could not make is a difference it now could.
