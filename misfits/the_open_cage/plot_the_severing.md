# Plot: The Severing

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Grind](place_the_grind.md), a stretch in which action meets no answer.
The Subject's effort there to be spent, the outcome held beyond its reach, the coupling of action and result there to be cut.

## Action

[Overriding](process_overriding.md): [The Overrider](persona_the_overrider.md), holding [the overrider's seat](position_overrider.md), makes the Subject's effort count for nothing, the same result whatever it does.

## Stage

[The Overrider](persona_the_overrider.md), on [Cut the Lever](plan_cut_the_lever.md), overrides the initiative, ignores the input, or delivers the outcome regardless, by design, by constraint, or by plain indifference.
Whatever the Subject tries, the answer is the same, so action and outcome are shown, plainly and truly, to have nothing to do with each other.

## Tension

The powerlessness is often real and often no one's design, and the reality of it is what makes the lesson stick.
There is nothing necessarily cruel: the override may be warranted, the system may genuinely not answer. But the Subject learns from the contingency and not the intent, so the plot closes with action severed from outcome on the Subject, the exact truth that teaches helplessness laid down whatever the heart behind it.
