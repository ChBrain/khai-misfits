# Process: Overriding

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The severing of action from outcome, the Overrider making the Subject's effort not count so that trying and not trying come to the same thing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A grip on the outcome the Subject cannot reach, held by design, by constraint, or by chance.
At [the grind](place_the_grind.md), the Overrider overriding the initiative, ignoring the input, or delivering the same result whatever the Subject does.

## Direction

Toward action and outcome with nothing to do with each other.
Because the outcome is delivered regardless of what the Subject does, overriding severs effort from result, so whatever the Subject tries the answer is the same, and the situation comes to hold the plain contingency that here, action does not change anything, the raw material of the lesson.

## Lever

The reality of the powerlessness, whatever its source.
Because the override may be warranted, the system may genuinely not answer, and the season may simply not turn on any one actor's effort, the severance is often real and often no one's design, so overriding runs on true uncontrollability, the lesson left assembled from no necessary cruelty, only from an outcome that does not respond to the Subject's hand.

## Echo

An action severed from its outcome, and a lesson waiting to be learned.
Effort has been made not to count, and the severance stands whatever the heart behind it, so what reads as a hand or a system that simply does not answer is, in truth, the exact contingency that teaches helplessness, the uncoupling from which the expectation will form.
