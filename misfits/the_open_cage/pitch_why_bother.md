# Pitch: Why Bother

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Reasonable, resigned, hard-won.
The plain sense of an actor who has stopped trying because trying never paid: why bother, it never makes a difference, a calm that has learned its own uselessness.
Nothing false in it: for a real stretch, effort truly did not change the result, and ceasing to spend it was the accurate response.

## Undertow

Under the resignation, a lesson true in one room carried faithfully into a room where it is false.
Because the expectation that nothing I do matters generalises and outlives the stretch that taught it, the actor goes on not trying once the door has opened and effort would now work, and, expecting no control, never tests for the control that has returned.
The calm that spared wasted effort in a hopeless room keeps effort unspent in a room where it would now save.

## Nerve

The feeling of not reaching for the handle because handles have never turned,
sure it is only sense, because for so long it was,
while the door stands open a step away and the expectation that it is locked will not let the hand be tried.

## Echo

After the run: a cage no longer locked and an actor who stays, effort unspent where it would now work,
no one lazy and no one at fault, only a true lesson kept past its expiry and a passivity read as a flaw of character,
and the one thing that would free them, an outside hand showing, small and unmissable and again, that action now works, forgone because the actor will not try for itself and the watchers call the staying laziness.
