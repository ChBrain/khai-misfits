# Process: Resigning

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The learning of no-control, the Subject correctly ceasing to try and carrying the expectation, generalised, into the changed room where control has returned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An action severed from outcome, learned from.
At [the grind](place_the_grind.md), the Subject registering that effort does not pay here and stopping, then carrying the giving-up to [the open door](place_the_open_door.md) where a way out has quietly appeared.

## Direction

Toward effort withheld and the withholding outliving its cause.
Because the expectation that nothing it does matters is a true reading that generalises and persists, resigning stops the Subject trying and carries the stop into new situations, so when control returns the Subject, expecting none, does not test for it, and the giving-up that was accurate in the grinding room becomes a self-imposed confinement in the open one.

## Lever

The accuracy of the lesson and the reach of learning.
Because the giving-up was the correct response to a real contingency and generalising a learned truth is what learning is for, ceasing to try and carrying the expectation forward are both sound moves, so resigning runs on well-calibrated learning, the trap assembled from no weakness of will, only from a truth about one room applied faithfully to the next.

## Echo

An actor that has stopped trying, and cannot feel that trying would now work.
The expectation has formed and generalised and outlived the stretch, so what reads as a settled unwillingness to try is, in truth, a true lesson kept past its expiry, the open door unwalked because the expectation that it is locked does not know it has opened.
