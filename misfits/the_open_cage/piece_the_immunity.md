# Piece: The Immunity

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The prior experience of control that prevents or reverses the trap, the learnable expectation the caged one was never given and the Coach must install.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Missing where [the grind](place_the_grind.md) taught only powerlessness.
Built, rung by rung, at [the ramp](place_the_ramp.md), where control is shown and felt.

## Load Bearing

Without it the trap is a dead end, a helplessness with no way back and no way to have been spared.
With it the trap has its remedy and its prevention: a prior, felt experience that action works, which inoculates against helplessness before the fact and restores the expectation of control after. The Immunity is load bearing because it is the learnable other side of the trap (Maier and Seligman, 2016): the expectation of control is what is actually learned, inhibiting a passivity that is otherwise the default, so the piece carries the thing the caged one lacked and can be given, the felt history of mattering that either keeps the cage from forming or, supplied late and in steps, opens it.

## Apparent

Confidence, or resilience, the plain trait of someone who keeps trying, easily mistaken for character.
Seen at the surface, the Immunity looks like a disposition some people simply have, a natural grit, and the truth that it is a learned expectation built from a felt history of control, that its absence in the caged one is not a flaw but a thing they were never given, that it can be installed from outside in graded steps, stays hidden behind how much a learned expectation of control resembles an inborn strength of will.

## Yearbook

Lacked by [the subject](persona_the_subject.md) and built by [the coach](persona_the_coach.md) through [restoring](process_restoring.md), the learnable expectation of control.
It is the remedy of the trap: the Immunity is what would have kept [the expectation](piece_the_expectation.md) from forming and what, supplied late, revives [the dead lever](piece_the_dead_lever.md), the felt history of mattering that alone opens the [open cage](piece_the_open_cage.md).
