# Plan: Call It Character

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the judge's seat](position_judge.md), inside [The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Judge](position_judge.md)

## Direction

Read the passivity as a flaw in the person. Attribute the not-trying to laziness or want of grit, hold the Subject responsible for the door they will not try, and judge the disposition by the behaviour. Name the failure to reach for the open door a failure of will.

## Orders

([The Judge](persona_the_judge.md) credits the Subject's giving-up to character, blaming them for the thing that was taught, and the blame becomes another outcome the Subject cannot change.)

## Implementation

([The Open Door](place_the_open_door.md); the [judging](process_judging.md) process; the passivity is read as [the verdict](piece_the_verdict.md) of character, missing [the uncoupling](piece_the_uncoupling.md) behind it, so the blame deepens [the expectation](piece_the_expectation.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the not-trying is read as a stable flaw in the person
- [ ] the passivity is attributed to the Subject, not to the stretch that taught it
- [ ] the Subject is blamed for the open door they will not try
- [ ] the verdict lands as one more outcome no effort can change, deepening the giving-up
