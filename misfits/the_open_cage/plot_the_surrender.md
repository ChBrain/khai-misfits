# Plot: The Surrender

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Grind](place_the_grind.md), action severed from outcome and the lesson there to be learned.
The Subject having tried and tried, the results unmoved, the truth of the room plain: effort here does not pay.

## Action

[Resigning](process_resigning.md): [The Subject](persona_the_subject.md), holding [the subject's seat](position_subject.md), stops trying and takes what comes, the giving-up an accurate reading of a room that does not answer.

## Stage

[The Subject](persona_the_subject.md), on [Stop Trying](plan_stop_trying.md), ceases to spend effort on a thing that does not respond, correctly, and settles into taking what comes.
The lesson is true and well learned, and it does not stay pinned to the room: it forms into an expectation the Subject will carry wherever it goes.

## Tension

Giving up here is the correct response to a real contingency, and its correctness is what will make it a trap.
There is nothing to fault: in this room effort genuinely does not help, so ceasing to spend it is accurate, not weak. But a true lesson generalises, so the plot closes with the Subject resigned and the expectation formed, a well-calibrated update that is about to be carried, unqualified, into a room where it is false.
