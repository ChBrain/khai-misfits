# Piece: The Expectation

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md). The learned belief that nothing the actor does will matter, generalising beyond the room that taught it and outliving the conditions, the engine of the trap.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Forms in [the grind](place_the_grind.md), where the lesson is learned.
Persists into [the open door](place_the_open_door.md), where it denies the control that has returned.

## Load Bearing

Without it the severance would be forgotten when the room changed, and no trap would follow the actor out.
With it the lesson becomes portable and durable: the belief that nothing I do matters, formed from a true contingency, spreads to new situations and outlives the one that taught it. The Expectation is load bearing because it is the engine that carries the trap past its cause (Maier and Seligman; Hiroto and Seligman): the generalising, persisting belief in no-control, so the piece carries the mechanism by which a true lesson becomes a false one, the expectation that, meeting an open door, does not know it has opened and will not let the hand be tried.

## Apparent

Realism, the plain good sense of an actor who knows, from experience, that trying does not help.
Seen at the surface, the Expectation looks like hard-won wisdom, a sober refusal to waste effort, and the truth that it has outlived the conditions that made it accurate, that it now forbids the one test that would show control has returned, that its very reasonableness is what keeps it from being corrected, stays hidden behind how much a settled belief in one's own powerlessness resembles knowing the score.

## Yearbook

Formed from [the uncoupling](piece_the_uncoupling.md) through [resigning](process_resigning.md), the belief that nothing the actor does matters.
It is the engine of the trap: the Expectation carries the lesson past its cause, so [the dead lever](piece_the_dead_lever.md) goes untested, and only [the immunity](piece_the_immunity.md) restored can shift it, the persisting no-control belief at the heart of the [open cage](piece_the_open_cage.md).
