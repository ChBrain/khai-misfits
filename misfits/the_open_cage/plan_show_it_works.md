# Plan: Show It Works

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the coach's seat](position_coach.md), inside [The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Coach](position_coach.md)

## Direction

Arrange a step small enough that the Subject cannot miss their action landing, and then another. Credit the passivity to its cause rather than to character, supply the experience of control the Subject will not seek, and repeat the demonstration until the expectation shifts. Change the expectation by evidence, not by exhortation.

## Orders

([The Coach](persona_the_coach.md) arranges the graded, unmissable demonstration that action now works and walks the Subject through the open door, at the cost of spending the effort they will not.)

## Implementation

([The Ramp](place_the_ramp.md); the [restoring](process_restoring.md) process; the graded win supplies [the immunity](piece_the_immunity.md) the Subject lacked and revives [the dead lever](piece_the_dead_lever.md), turning [the open cage](piece_the_open_cage.md) back ... if the outside effort can be carried again and again.)

## Targets

- [ ] a step is arranged small enough that the Subject cannot miss their action landing
- [ ] the passivity is credited to its cause, not to character
- [ ] the experience of control is supplied from outside and repeated until the expectation shifts
- [ ] the effort the Subject will not spend is carried for them, against the charge of coddling
