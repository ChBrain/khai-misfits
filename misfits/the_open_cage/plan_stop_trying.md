# Plan: Stop Trying

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the subject's seat](position_subject.md), inside [The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Subject](position_subject.md)

## Direction

Cease to spend effort on a thing that does not respond. Take what comes, carry the lesson forward, and do not test the lever or try the door, because trying has taught you it will not help. Spare yourself the waste of reaching for handles that never turn.

## Orders

([The Subject](persona_the_subject.md) stops trying, having learned correctly that effort does not pay here, and carries the giving-up into the changed room where control has returned.)

## Implementation

([The Grind](place_the_grind.md); the [resigning](process_resigning.md) process; the giving-up hardens [the expectation](piece_the_expectation.md) from [the uncoupling](piece_the_uncoupling.md), so [the dead lever](piece_the_dead_lever.md) goes untested when the door opens.)

## Targets

- [ ] effort is withdrawn from a situation where action does not change the outcome
- [ ] the giving-up is registered as the correct response to a real contingency
- [ ] the expectation of no-control is carried forward into the changed room
- [ ] the lever that now works goes untested, the open door unwalked
