# Plot: The Rungs

## Taxonomy

[The Open Cage](play_the_open_cage.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ramp](place_the_ramp.md), someone who would open the door and walk the Subject through.
The passivity settled, the Judge's verdict standing, and a step there to be made small enough that the Subject cannot miss their action landing.

## Action

[Restoring](process_restoring.md): [The Coach](persona_the_coach.md), holding [the coach's seat](position_coach.md), arranges a graded, unmissable demonstration that action now works, against [The Judge](persona_the_judge.md) on [Call It Character](plan_call_it_character.md).

## Stage

[The Coach](persona_the_coach.md), on [Show It Works](plan_show_it_works.md), sets a step small enough that the Subject sees their effort land, credits the passivity to its cause, and repeats the win rung by rung until the expectation begins to give.
[The Judge](persona_the_judge.md), on [Call It Character](plan_call_it_character.md), calls the demonstration coddling, so the freeing move must be carried against both the Subject's certainty and the verdict of laziness.

## Tension

Showing control small and felt is the one move that shifts the expectation, and it means spending, from outside, the effort the Subject will not spend for itself.
There is nothing to fault: expectation changes on evidence, not exhortation, and the Coach only supplies the evidence. But the Subject will not test control and the Judge calls the testing an excuse, so the plot closes one of two ways: the win lands, small and felt and again, until the expectation gives and the Subject tries ... or the demonstration is waved off as coddling the workshy, and the cage stays open and unwalked.
