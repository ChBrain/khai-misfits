# Process: Recombining

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The mixing of two genomes into one, the combining of their least-mutated stretches into an offspring cleaner than either, the sole operation that lowers the floor, bought at the cost of sex.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Recombiner](persona_the_recombiner.md), at [the Braid](place_the_braid.md), combining two mutated genomes into a cleaner one.
Begins whenever reproduction mixes the inheritances of two parents rather than copying one.

## Direction

Turns two loaded genomes into one that is lighter.
Because recombination can bring the fewest-mutations stretches of two parents together in a single offspring, it can assemble a genome carrying fewer mutations than either made it, so recombining converts existing variation into a reconstituted least-loaded class, the one process that lowers the floor the ratchet raised rather than only carrying the load forward (Felsenstein).

## Lever

The mixing copying cannot do.
Because recombination combines two genomes where copying transmits one, it works through the reassembly of good parts: the lever is the mixing itself, which reaches back to build a clean class from the variation still alive, doing at a price exactly what an asexual lineage has no means to do, and halting the climb where it operates.

## Echo

A floor lowered, a next click prevented, and a bill paid to do it.
The echo of recombining is a least-loaded class rebuilt and the load turned down, at the two-fold cost of sex and the broken combinations and the partner required, so the remedy is real and dear and forward-facing, reaching the variation still present and not the mutations already fixed, holding the next click better than it undoes the last, outbred in every present season by the copier that pays none of its cost.
