# Position: The Roll

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The sampling seat, the finite generational draw that decides who reproduces by chance, able to represent a common class reliably and unable to be sure of a rare one, so it drops the least-loaded by the mathematics of small numbers.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The draw, the finite number of places in the next generation, and the chance that fills them.
The seat holds the sampling that turns one generation into the next, a fixed and finite set of parents drawn from the present crowd, filled by luck rather than by any reading of fitness.
Reliable coverage of the many and none of the few, since the seat can be counted on to carry a large class forward and cannot be counted on to carry a small one, its certainty scaling with a class's size.

## Orders

Draw the next generation, and draw it by chance.
Fill the finite places from the present population without regard to fitness, so each individual reproduces or not as luck falls, and the composition of the next generation is a sample of the last.
Sample blindly, since the seat's whole nature is to draw without aiming, so it represents a class in proportion to its number and by that same rule leaves a small enough class sometimes wholly undrawn, the impartiality that spares no one falling hardest on the fewest.

## Loses

The certainty of the rare, which finite sampling structurally cannot give.
The Roll can be sure of a common class and never of the least-loaded one, because a finite draw represents a class only in proportion to its size, so the smallest class, which is the cleanest, carries a real chance of not being sampled at all in a given generation. What the position forfeits is any guarantee for the best: the seat does not aim and cannot aim, so it holds no means to ensure the rarest genomes a place, and the same blind draw that reliably keeps the common reliably risks the rare. No operation available to the seat closes this gap, since aiming would require a reading of fitness the draw does not have and the loss of a small class is not an error in the sampling but the sampling working exactly as a finite draw must, so the seat that plays no favourites forfeits the least-loaded class not against its rule but by it, the impartiality itself the thing that takes the best.

## Drives

Toward a next generation that keeps the many and risks the few,
and a draw whose very fairness falls hardest on the rarest and so on the cleanest,
so the seat that aims at nothing is the seat that reliably removes the best, its blind proportional sampling certain of the common and, by the same mathematics, the standing hazard of the least-loaded class.
