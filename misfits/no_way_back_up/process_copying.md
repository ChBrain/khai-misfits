# Process: Copying

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The faithful asexual transmission of a genome to the next generation, the passing on of the load intact, the copying that carries mutations forward and has no means to remove one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Copier](persona_the_copier.md), at [the Colony](place_the_colony.md), reproducing by making faithful copies of itself.
Begins whenever a lineage passes its genome to its offspring without mixing it with another.

## Direction

Turns a genome into more of itself, load and all.
Because copying transmits an inheritance and does not repair it, each offspring carries its parent's mutations exactly, plus whatever new ones arose, so copying converts one loaded genome into many, the line a chain of faithful likenesses that can match or exceed the load before it and never fall below it (Muller).

## Lever

Transmission without repair.
Because the only operation copying performs is to carry a genome forward, it works through faithful reproduction alone: there is no step in it that removes a mutation, so the lever is the fidelity itself, which passes on the best the parent had and, in the same motion, everything else the parent carried, fixing the load at least at what it was.

## Echo

A lineage that holds its load or gains, never sheds.
The echo of copying is a chain of generations each carrying at least the mutations of the last, so the floor of the load is set by transmission and can only be raised from there, the faithful copying that fills the colony the same faithful copying that leaves no route by which the load could fall.
