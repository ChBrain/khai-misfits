# Plot: The Copy

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Colony](place_the_colony.md), a generation reproducing.
A lineage passing its genome to its offspring, needing no partner, carrying whatever load it holds.

## Action

[Copying](process_copying.md): the Copier transmits its genome intact, adding any new mutations and shedding none, filling the next generation with faithful likenesses.

## Stage

[The Copier](persona_the_copier.md) acts from [the Clone](position_the_clone.md) on [Copy True](plan_copy_true.md), passing [the least-loaded](piece_the_least_loaded.md) forward while it survives and holding [the rising floor](piece_the_rising_floor.md) at least where it stood.
The copying is faithful and efficient, and it carries the load along with the line.

## Tension

The lineage reproduces perfectly and by reproducing perfectly forecloses every way its load could fall.
There is no flaw in the copying: the genome is transmitted exactly, the population fills fast, no partner is wasted, and each offspring is as fit as its parent. But faithful transmission is all copying can do, so the plot closes with a line that carries its mutations forward intact, the best passed on while it lasts and the load fixed at least where it was, the very fidelity that makes the copier efficient the same fidelity that leaves no route by which what it carries could ever be lightened.
