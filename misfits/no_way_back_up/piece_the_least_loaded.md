# Piece: The Least-Loaded

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The class carrying the fewest mutations in the population, the best it has and the rarest, the leading edge whose loss is each click of the climb.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Lives at the edge of [the Colony](place_the_colony.md), the thinnest class, and is lost at [the Narrows](place_the_narrows.md).
Flows as the fewest-mutations individuals alive, the front of the whole distribution of load, few because they are the best and exposed because they are few.

## Load Bearing

Without the Least-Loaded there is no floor to lose: it is the cleanest surviving class that sets the population's minimum load, so its presence is the floor and its loss is the click.
The Least-Loaded is load bearing because it is the quantity the whole ratchet turns on, the current best and, by the shape of the distribution, the rarest (Haigh). It is small precisely because it is least-loaded: mutations accumulate, so the fewest-mutations individuals are the ones fewest have matched, and the leading edge of the population's fitness rests on its thinnest class. That thinness is the trap's hinge: a small class is the one a finite draw can miss, so the best is also the most exposed, and when it is lost the floor rises to the next class, which is again the smallest and again the most exposed, so every other piece turns on this one, the class that is both the population's best and its likeliest next loss.

## Apparent

Just the healthiest individuals, the pride of the population.
Seen at the surface, the Least-Loaded looks like strength, the fittest members carrying the lightest load, and the truth that they are the rarest and so the most precarious, that the population's best is one unlucky draw from gone, that being the cleanest is exactly what makes a class thin enough to lose, stays hidden behind how plainly the least-loaded look like the fittest.

## Yearbook

Passed on by [the Copier](persona_the_copier.md) while it lasts, dropped by [the Toss](persona_the_toss.md) when the draw misses it, and rebuilt only by [the Recombiner](persona_the_recombiner.md).
It is the class at the hinge: the Least-Loaded is what [copying](process_copying.md) carries forward until [dropping](process_dropping.md) removes it, the loss [loading](process_loading.md) turns into a raised [rising floor](piece_the_rising_floor.md), reassembled only by the mixing at [the Braid](place_the_braid.md) that [the back-mutation](piece_the_back_mutation.md) cannot supply.
