# Piece: The Rising Floor

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The minimum mutational load of the population, the fewest mutations any lineage carries, the floor that steps up with each lost class and by every available means never down, the whole phenomenon of which the other pieces are parts.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set across [the Colony](place_the_colony.md), raised at [the Narrows](place_the_narrows.md), and lowered only at [the Braid](place_the_braid.md).
Flows as the load of the cleanest surviving class, a single number that stands for the whole population's best, climbing notch by notch as the draw takes its leading edge.

## Load Bearing

Without the Rising Floor there is no misfit, only reproduction and chance in the abstract: it is the Floor that ties the least-loaded class to the back-mutation to the cost of sex, the one number in which a lost class becomes a permanent rise and faithful copying becomes a one-way decline.
The Rising Floor is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole, the population's minimum load moving in one direction only (Muller). It carries the entire misfit: the Least-Loaded is the class whose loss raises it, the Back-Mutation is the reversal that would lower it and does not, the Cost of Sex is the price of the recombination that alone can, so the Rising Floor is not one more piece beside the others but the shape they all compose, the single quantity in which every other piece does its work. It is built of blameless parts, faithful copying and a blind draw and a shut exit, which is why it cannot simply be talked down: no class was lost to any fault and no mutation was selected for, the climb is only their sum, so lowering the floor means paying the whole cost of sex for a mixing that reaches only what is not yet fixed, and short of that the floor holds at its height and waits to rise again.

## Apparent

Just a number, and a population thriving besides.
Seen at the surface, the Rising Floor is invisible, since a colony mid-climb reproduces as fully as one holding steady and no single generation shows the minimum moving, and the truth that the floor has a direction, that it climbs and cannot fall by any means the population has, that the thriving hides a one-way decline, stays hidden behind how well a loaded population still lives.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the Toss](persona_the_toss.md)'s losses, held up by [the Copier](persona_the_copier.md)'s faithful carrying, and lowered only by [the Recombiner](persona_the_recombiner.md)'s costly mixing.
It is the whole shape of the climb: the Rising Floor is what [dropping](process_dropping.md) and [loading](process_loading.md) raise and [copying](process_copying.md) fixes in place, the minimum that [recombining](process_recombining.md) alone can lower, built of [the least-loaded](piece_the_least_loaded.md) lost, [the back-mutation](piece_the_back_mutation.md) that will not come, and [the cost of sex](piece_the_cost_of_sex.md) that the cure demands, a floor with only one way to go.
