# Position: The Cross

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The recombining seat, which reproduces by mixing two genomes into one, able to reassemble a least-loaded class from existing variation and unable to reach what is already fixed or to escape the cost of sex.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Two genomes to mix, and the power to build one cleaner than either.
The seat holds the mechanism of recombination, the combining of the good stretches of two parents into a single offspring, so it commands the one operation that can produce a genome carrying fewer mutations than either that made it.
The power to lower the floor and the whole bill for it, since the seat can rebuild a clean class from the variation still present and pays, to do so, the full cost of pairing, halving, and breaking good combinations.

## Orders

Mix two genomes, and build the cleaner offspring.
Combine the least-mutated stretches of two parents into one inheritance, reconstituting a least-loaded class the draw had dropped, so the floor that copying could only raise is lowered by the one act that assembles rather than transmits.
Pay the cost and mix anyway, since the seat's whole worth is the recombination that halts the ratchet, so it must bear the two-fold cost of sex and the broken combinations to buy the escape, spending in the present the price of a floor held down.

## Loses

The fixed mutations, and the present race, both beyond the seat's reach.
The Cross can reassemble a clean genome from variation still present and cannot recover what the whole population has already lost, because recombination shuffles the genomes that exist and creates none, so a mutation fixed in every member is in every genome the seat has to mix and appears, unshuffled, in every offspring. What the position forfeits is the past: it prevents the next click far better than it undoes the last, since the diversity a click destroyed is gone from the pool it draws on, and it forfeits the present race as well by the plain arithmetic of the cost, reproducing at half the rate of a copier that needs no mate, so the seat that alone can lower the floor breeds slower by a factor of two and can reach only the losses not yet made permanent, its remedy real, forward-looking, and structurally outpaced.

## Drives

Toward a floor that can at last be lowered, at a cost that leaves the seat outbred,
and a remedy that holds the next click better than it lifts the last,
so the seat that alone can rebuild the clean class is the seat that pays the most to do it and reaches the least of what is already gone, its costly mixing the one force against the climb and always, in the present, the dearer way to breed.
