# Plan: Take What Comes

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Roll](position_the_roll.md), inside [No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Roll](position_the_roll.md)

## Direction

Draw the next generation from the present one by chance, filling the finite places without regard to fitness, so each individual reproduces or not as luck falls (Haigh). Represent a class in proportion to its number, which keeps a common class reliably and leaves a small enough class sometimes wholly undrawn, since the draw does not aim and cannot aim, so it holds no means to ensure the rarest a place. Take what the sampling happens to take, and by taking without favour fall hardest on the fewest, the least-loaded class carrying a real chance of vanishing in any generation not because it is unfit but because it is small.

## Orders

([The Toss](persona_the_toss.md) settles who leaves offspring by luck alone, dropping the cleanest class when the draw runs that way, meaning nothing by it and knowing nothing of what it took.)

## Implementation

([The Narrows](place_the_narrows.md); through the [dropping](process_dropping.md) process, feeding the [loading](process_loading.md) each loss advances; the Toss removes [the least-loaded](piece_the_least_loaded.md) by chance and raises [the rising floor](piece_the_rising_floor.md), the loss unreachable by [the back-mutation](piece_the_back_mutation.md) that will not come.)

## Targets

- [ ] the next generation is drawn by chance, without regard to fitness
- [ ] a common class is carried forward and a rare one only sometimes
- [ ] the least-loaded class is dropped when the draw misses it
- [ ] the loss falls on the best for being fewest, not for being unfit
