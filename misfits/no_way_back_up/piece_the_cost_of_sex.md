# Piece: The Cost of Sex

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The price of the remedy, the two-fold cost of pairing and the broken combinations, what recombination charges to lower the floor and what an efficient copier is always tempted to stop paying.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Paid at [the Braid](place_the_braid.md), each time two lineages mix rather than one copies.
Flows as the whole expense of sex, the sons who bear no young, the half of each genome given to a partner, the good stretches broken up along with the bad.

## Load Bearing

Without the Cost of Sex the remedy would be free and the ratchet would never run: every lineage would mix, no floor would climb, and there would be no trap. It is because the cure is dear that copiers abandon it and the climb begins.
The Cost of Sex is load bearing because it is what makes the remedy refusable, the two-fold cost that lets an asexual copier out-reproduce a recombiner in every present season (Maynard Smith). It is the reason the trap exists at all: recombination halts the ratchet, but it charges a partner, a halving, and a son who bears nothing, so an efficient lineage is forever tempted to drop the mixing and take the doubled rate, and the moment it does the floor is free to climb. This piece is the hinge between remedy and trap: the cure works and the cure is expensive, so the escape from the ratchet is exactly the cost a copier is rewarded, in the short run, for not paying.

## Apparent

Just an inefficiency, the obvious waste of needing a mate.
Seen at the surface, the Cost of Sex looks like plain waste, half of every offspring spent on a partner and sons who bear no young, so copying seems the sensible economy, and the truth that this waste is the price of the only thing that lowers the load, that the economy of skipping it is what starts the climb, that the expensive way is the one that lasts, stays hidden behind how obviously cheaper it is to copy.

## Yearbook

Declined by [the Copier](persona_the_copier.md) for the doubled rate, and borne by [the Recombiner](persona_the_recombiner.md) for the floor it lowers.
It is the price of the cure: the Cost of Sex is what [recombining](process_recombining.md) pays and [copying](process_copying.md) skips, the charge that makes [the Recombiner](persona_the_recombiner.md)'s remedy dear and [the Copier](persona_the_copier.md)'s efficiency tempting, so the mixing that alone lowers [the rising floor](piece_the_rising_floor.md) is the mixing a copier is rewarded for dropping.
