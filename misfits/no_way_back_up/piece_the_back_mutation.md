# Piece: The Back-Mutation

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The reversal that would undo a mutation at its own site, real in principle and vanishingly rare in fact, the escape that cannot be counted on and so leaves the floor with no cheap way down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Would occur in [the Colony](place_the_colony.md), at any loaded genome, and almost never does.
Flows as a mutation reversing itself, the exact site that changed changing back, a thing that can happen and happens far too seldom to rebuild a lost class.

## Load Bearing

Without the Back-Mutation's rarity there would be a way back: if reversals were common, a lost least-loaded class could reappear on its own and the floor could fall. It is because the reversal is vanishing that the loss is permanent.
The Back-Mutation is load bearing because it is the closed exit, the one route down that copying might have had and does not, since a reversal at the precise site of a mutation is so rare that no lineage can rely on it to lighten its load (Muller). It carries the irreversibility: the ratchet is a ratchet only because this escape is shut, so with recombination absent and back-mutation negligible, an asexual population has no means at all to reassemble a genome cleaner than its current best. The whole one-way character of the climb rests on this piece, the reversal that is possible and might as well not be, leaving loading with nothing to undo it.

## Apparent

Just a symmetry, what changed could change back.
Seen at the surface, the Back-Mutation looks like a way out, since a mutation is a change and a change could reverse, so surely a load could fall as easily as it rose, and the truth that the reversal is so rare as to be no help at all, that the symmetry is real and useless, that possible and negligible are the same here, stays hidden behind how naturally a change seems to imply its own undoing.

## Yearbook

Hoped for against [the Toss](persona_the_toss.md)'s losses, absent from [the Copier](persona_the_copier.md)'s means, and stood in for only by [the Recombiner](persona_the_recombiner.md)'s mixing.
It is the shut exit: the Back-Mutation is the reversal [loading](process_loading.md) would need and never gets, the way down [copying](process_copying.md) does not hold, so [the least-loaded](piece_the_least_loaded.md) once dropped stays dropped and [the rising floor](piece_the_rising_floor.md) stays risen, until the costly [recombining](process_recombining.md) does what the back-mutation cannot.
