# Plan: Copy True

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Clone](position_the_clone.md), inside [No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Clone](position_the_clone.md)

## Direction

Copy the genome forward faithfully and reproduce at the full rate a mateless copier allows: pass the inheritance on intact, add the mutations that arise, and keep the best individuals reproducing, since faithful transmission is exactly what reproduction is (Muller) and asking no partner doubles the rate (Maynard Smith). Reproduce without mixing, taking the whole asexual advantage and foregoing the one operation that could lighten the load, because copying carries a genome forward and has no means to repair it, so the plan gains a population filled fast and a load that can only be held or added to, never subtracted.

## Orders

([The Copier](persona_the_copier.md) transmits its genome whole, mutations and all, needing no partner and dividing nothing, content in each copying as a plain success and blind to the floor it cannot feel rising.)

## Implementation

([The Colony](place_the_colony.md); through the [copying](process_copying.md) process, carrying the load that [loading](process_loading.md) raises; the Copier passes [the least-loaded](piece_the_least_loaded.md) forward while it lasts and cannot lower [the rising floor](piece_the_rising_floor.md), holding no route down that [the back-mutation](piece_the_back_mutation.md) could open.)

## Targets

- [ ] the genome is transmitted forward intact, mutations included
- [ ] reproduction runs at the doubled rate of a mateless copier
- [ ] no mixing is done and no mutation is shed
- [ ] the load is carried forward, held or heavier, never lighter
