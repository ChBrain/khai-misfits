# Place: The Narrows

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The finite passage between generations, the strait a population must pass through where only so many are drawn forward, where the small classes are most easily missed and the smaller the population the sharper the loss.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A passage every generation must cross, a fixed number of places on the far side.
Shown at the surface is an ordinary bottleneck of reproduction: not every individual leaves offspring, the next generation is finite, and the crowd narrows into the places there are, a strait the population passes through and out of, apparently none the worse.

## Holds

Holds the chance that keeps the many and risks the few.
The Narrows is where the finite draw happens, so it holds the sampling that carries a common class through reliably and a rare one only sometimes, the passage where a class small enough can fail to send a single member across, its whole hazard concentrated in the fewness of the best.

## Offers

Offers safe passage to the common and a standing risk to the cleanest.
Here the large classes cross without trouble and the population continues, so the Narrows offers ordinary generational renewal, and offers, to the least-loaded class alone, a real chance of not crossing at all: the same strait that is nothing to the many is, to the fewest, the place they can simply be left behind, the risk sharpest where the population is smallest and the draw most likely to miss.

## Withheld

Withholds the guarantee the best would need, and hastens where the crowd is thin.
What the Narrows withholds is any assurance for the rare: it cannot promise the cleanest class a crossing, because a finite draw covers a class only in proportion to its size, so the place that renews the population keeps from its best the one thing that would save it, and in a small population it withholds even time, the losses coming faster and the floor climbing toward a load the lineage cannot bear, the strait narrowing hardest exactly where the crowd is already thin.
