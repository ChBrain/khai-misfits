# Place: The Braid

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The meeting of two lineages, where genomes are mixed rather than copied and the good stretches of each can be combined into one, the only ground on which a clean class is rebuilt, and never a cheap one to reach.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Two lineages meeting, and a cost paid to meet.
Shown at the surface is the expense of it: a partner sought and found, two genomes brought together where one would have sufficed, offspring that carry only half of each parent, the whole visible price of mixing where copying asked for none.

## Holds

Holds the one operation that lowers the floor.
The Braid is where recombination happens, the combining of the least-mutated stretches of two parents into a single cleaner offspring, so it holds the sole means by which a least-loaded class the draw destroyed can be reassembled from the variation still alive, the one place the load can go down rather than up.

## Offers

Offers a clean genome rebuilt, at the full cost of sex.
Here two mutated genomes can meet and yield one carrying fewer mutations than either, so the Braid offers the escape from the ratchet, the floor lowered and the climb reversed, and offers it dear: the two-fold cost of sex, the sons who bear no young, the good combinations broken along with the bad, a remedy real and priced at exactly what an efficient copier declines to pay.

## Withheld

Withholds what is already fixed, and the present race.
What the Braid cannot offer is recovery of what the whole population has lost: recombination shuffles the genomes that exist and makes none, so a mutation fixed in every member is in every strand it has to weave and passes into every offspring, beyond the mixing to reach. So the place that alone can lower the floor withholds the past, reaching only the losses not yet made permanent, and withholds the present too, since the pairing it requires leaves its lineages outbred by copiers that need no partner, the cure real and forward-facing and always, in the moment, the slower way to fill a world.
