# Persona: The Toss

## Taxonomy

[The Roll](position_the_roll.md). The blind chance of a finite draw, which decides who reproduces without regard to fitness, and by the plain luck of small numbers eventually leaves the rarest class uncopied, dropping the least-loaded not for any fault but for being few.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Impartial and unaimed. Sparing no one and favouring no one. Deciding by luck alone.
Is the one that settles which individuals leave offspring in a finite generation, and settles it by chance, so a class small enough is sometimes simply not drawn, gone by the arithmetic of luck and not by any disadvantage.
No malice wished on them: the draw means nothing by what it takes, since it does not read fitness and cannot aim, and the loss of the best is not a verdict on the best but the ordinary outcome of sampling a small class from a finite crowd.

## Action

Draws the next generation from the present one, letting each individual reproduce or not by chance, so a large class is reliably represented and a small one is not.
Drops the least-loaded class when the luck runs that way, removing the cleanest genomes the population had for no reason but that they were too few to be sure of, the same draw that keeps the common keeping nothing of the rare.
Does what a finite sample does: takes what it happens to take, plays no favourites, and by playing no favourites falls hardest on the rarest, the best class lost exactly because it was the smallest and chance does not spare the small.

## Shadow

There is nothing inside it to appeal to, and that is the whole of its horror: it does not know what the cleanest class was worth, does not weigh it, does not even register that it took the best rather than the worst, because it is not choosing at all, only landing, and a thing that only lands cannot be reasoned with or resented into mercy.
What there is no way around is the blankness. One could bear a loss that meant something, that answered to a fault or a fight, but the least-loaded class is not taken for being unfit or for losing to anything, it is taken for nothing, dropped by a draw that would as readily have dropped the worst, so the loss carries no lesson and offers no purchase, an emptiness where a reason should be, and the population is left poorer with no one and nothing to hold it against.
The bind is interior only in the way an absence is: it must decide and decides by luck, holds no regard for what it removes, and cannot be made to spare the best because sparing would require a seeing it does not have, so the cleanest are lost to a thing that never meant to take them and never knew it did.

## Tell

Says nothing, and takes the best as readily as the rest.
Is the whole indifference in its silence, since a draw that spoke, that named or aimed or warned, could be met, and this one does none of those, so the absence of any word is the truest sign of what it is, a chance that removes the least-loaded class without knowing it held anything, the loss falling in a silence that offers nothing to answer.
