# Place: The Colony

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The asexual population, the finite crowd of copied lineages where the classes of load live and reproduce, thriving and filling and carrying, generation on generation, a burden it cannot see mounting.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A thriving population, copying itself, full and fast and alive.
Shown at the surface is health: a colony reproducing at speed, every lineage faithfully continued, numbers strong and generations turning, the plain picture of a population doing well.

## Holds

Holds the classes of load, and the cleanest among them.
The Colony is where the individuals of every mutation count live together, from the many carrying more to the few carrying fewest, so it holds the whole distribution of the load and, at its leading edge, the least-loaded class, the best the population has and the rarest, kept only so long as the draw keeps drawing it.

## Offers

Offers a full population and a floor already set, with only the fullness in view.
Here the colony reproduces abundantly and looks entirely well, so the Colony offers thriving and continuity, and offers, unshown, the state of its load: the minimum it carries is a fact about the whole crowd that no single thriving lineage displays, so the place hands over a picture of health and keeps, invisible within it, how high the floor has already climbed.

## Withheld

Withholds the trend, and the rarity of its best.
What the Colony hides is the direction of its load over time: each generation looks like the last, thriving and full, so the place shows no sign that the floor is rising or that the cleanest class is a thin edge one unlucky draw from gone, present with all its abundance and absent the one reading that matters, whether the load it carries is level or climbing, kept off the surface because a colony mid-decline and a colony holding steady wear the same thriving face.
