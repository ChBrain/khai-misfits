# Position: The Clone

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The asexual seat, which reproduces by copying a single genome forward, able to transmit its load and add to it and lacking any operation that would remove a mutation once carried.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The genome, the power to copy it, and the whole efficiency of pairing with no one.
The seat holds a single lineage's inheritance and the means to pass it on intact, reproducing at the full rate of a copier that needs no mate and divides nothing.
Transmission and no repair, since the seat can carry a genome forward exactly and can add the mutations that arise, and holds no instrument that would take a mutation back out.

## Orders

Copy the genome forward, faithfully, and reproduce as fast as copying allows.
Pass the inheritance to the next generation intact, keep the best individuals reproducing, and fill the population at the doubled rate a mateless copier enjoys, since faithful transmission is exactly what the seat is built to do.
Reproduce without mixing, since the seat's whole efficiency is in copying one genome rather than combining two, so it carries its load forward as it is, gaining the asexual advantage and foregoing the one act that could lighten what it carries.

## Loses

The power to lower its load, which asexual copying structurally lacks.
The Clone can hold its load or add to it and can never subtract, because copying transmits a genome and combines none, so no operation available to the seat produces an offspring carrying fewer mutations than its parent held. What the position forfeits is any route downward: a mutation once in the line is copied forward by the only mechanism the seat has, and the sole exits, a reversal at the same site or the recombination of two genomes into a cleaner one, are a vanishing rarity and a thing the seat by definition does not do. So the seat's minimum can only stand or rise, since the least it can pass on is exactly what it holds, and once the cleanest genomes are lost from the lineage the floor beneath its load has risen with no instrument in the seat to bring it down, the faithful copying that is its whole advantage the same faithful copying that seals every route back.

## Drives

Toward a lineage that carries its load forward and never sheds it,
and a floor that can hold or climb and by the seat's own means never fall,
so the seat most efficient at reproduction is the seat least able to repair it, its faithful copying filling the population and closing, in the same act, every path by which the load it carries could ever come back down.
