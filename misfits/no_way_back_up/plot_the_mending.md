# Plot: The Mending

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Braid](place_the_braid.md), two lineages that could mix.
Two mutated genomes, each carrying different faults, and the chance to combine their good stretches into one.

## Action

[Recombining](process_recombining.md): the Recombiner mixes the two, building an offspring cleaner than either parent, and pays the full cost of sex to do it.

## Stage

[The Recombiner](persona_the_recombiner.md) acts from [the Cross](position_the_cross.md) on [Cross to Clean](plan_cross_to_clean.md), reassembling [the least-loaded](piece_the_least_loaded.md) the draw had dropped and lowering [the rising floor](piece_the_rising_floor.md), doing what [the back-mutation](piece_the_back_mutation.md) cannot and paying [the cost of sex](piece_the_cost_of_sex.md) for it.
The floor came down where variation remained, and the bill for it was steep.

## Tension

The one move that lowers the floor is dear, partial, and outbred in every season it wins.
There is no free repair: recombination genuinely rebuilds a clean class and turns the load down, but it charges the two-fold cost of sex, breaks up good combinations with bad, and reaches only the variation still alive, not the mutations already fixed in the whole population. So the plot closes with the floor lowered and the next click prevented at a price that leaves the recombiner out-reproduced by the copier beside it, the remedy real and forward-facing and always, in the present, the slower way to fill a world, or the mixing forgone for the doubled rate and the floor left free to climb.
