# Plot: The Drop

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Narrows](place_the_narrows.md), the cleanest class small and the draw finite.
A least-loaded class of only a few individuals, and a generation with only so many places to fill.

## Action

[Dropping](process_dropping.md): by the luck of which individuals leave offspring, not one of the fewest-mutations class is drawn forward, and it is gone.

## Stage

[The Toss](persona_the_toss.md) acts from [the Roll](position_the_roll.md) on [Take What Comes](plan_take_what_comes.md), removing [the least-loaded](piece_the_least_loaded.md) by the plain hazard of small numbers, meaning nothing by it and knowing nothing of what it took.
The draw played no favourites, and by playing none it took the best.

## Tension

The best class is lost to nothing, taken by a draw that did not aim and cannot be blamed.
There is no selection against it: the least-loaded class was the fittest the population had, punished by nothing, out-competed by nothing, simply too few to be sure of in a finite draw. So the plot closes with the cleanest genomes gone for no fault, removed by blind chance that would as readily have taken the worst, the loss carrying no lesson and offering no purchase, the population a step poorer and no one and nothing to hold it against.
