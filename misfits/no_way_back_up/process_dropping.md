# Process: Dropping

## Taxonomy

[No Way Back Up](play_no_way_back_up.md). The chance loss of the least-loaded class, the finite draw that by luck leaves the rarest genomes uncopied, removing the best the population had for no fault but their fewness.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Toss](persona_the_toss.md), at [the Narrows](place_the_narrows.md), drawing the next generation by chance from the present one.
Begins whenever a finite generation is sampled and a class small enough is not represented in it.

## Direction

Turns a rare class into a lost one.
Because the cleanest class is the smallest and a finite draw covers a class only in proportion to its size, some generation simply fails to copy the least-loaded forward, so dropping converts the population's best into its former best, removed not for any disadvantage but for being too few to be sure of, the loss blind and the target the rarest (Haigh).

## Lever

The fewness of the best.
Because the least-loaded class is by its nature the rarest, dropping works through the plain hazard of small numbers: a large class is reliably drawn and a small one is not, so the lever is the size of the class itself, which makes the cleanest the most exposed, the same rarity that marks it as the best marking it as the likeliest to vanish.

## Echo

A population without its cleanest genomes, and no fault to blame.
The echo of dropping is a colony that has lost its least-loaded class to a draw that meant nothing by it, so the best is simply gone, taken by luck and not by selection, the loss carrying no lesson and leaving the population a step poorer with nothing and no one to hold it against.
