# Position: Bidder

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The seat that turns the estimate into an offer and plays to win.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The estimate, and the authority to put a price on the table.
The choice of how near the number to bid, and the wish to take the prize.

## Orders

Bid close to the estimate, because less feels like conceding.
Back the analysis with a real offer, and enter to win.
Commit to a price on the firm's own read.

## Loses

The information that winning would carry.
A seat that bids near its estimate cannot see that it wins exactly when the estimate was highest, so it treats a win as success rather than as a warning.

## Drives

Toward an offer near the estimate, and away from the discount the field's size demands.
Toward taking the prize precisely on the days the estimate was too high.
