# Process: Realizing

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The true value coming in, and the overpay appearing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A prize won and time passing.
Once the winner holds it, the asset must eventually earn or fail to.

## Direction

From price paid toward value delivered.
It moves to the truth on the books, set against the winning bid.

## Lever

Reality meeting the highest estimate.
The force is the value arriving as it always would; the resistance is the winner's hope that the rosy number was right.

## Echo

An overpayment, recorded.
It hands forward the gap between bid and value as a loss, and a choice: name it the curse, or blame the market.
