# Place: The Boardroom

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). Where the bid is approved, the win celebrated, and the curse seen whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A table, a deal deck, an announcement drafted, and, later, an impairment to explain.
The place the winning bid is authorised and applauded, and where, quarters on, the writedown is discussed.

## Holds

The curse, and the pattern across many deals.
The place the approval and the writedown can finally be seen as one decision viewed at two times, if anyone reads them together.

## Offers

The vantage to name the mechanism.
Here the run of overpayments could be recognised as adverse selection rather than a string of bad luck, and the strategy corrected.

## Withheld

The will to indict its own wins.
The boardroom applauded the deals it must now question, so it holds the evidence of the curse and the strongest reason not to name it.
