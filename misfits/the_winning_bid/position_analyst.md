# Position: Analyst

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The seat that appraises the prize and produces the estimate.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The evidence, the methods, and the duty to produce a value.
A best guess at the hidden truth, honest and backed by data.

## Orders

Appraise the prize and hand over a defensible number.
Reason from the signals available, and land where they point.
Give the bid its honest input.

## Loses

Any way to know if this estimate is above or below the truth.
A seat that can produce a good number cannot tell which side of the value it fell on, so it cannot warn that this is the read that will win and regret it.

## Drives

Toward a careful estimate scattered honestly around the truth, and away from the knowledge of which way it erred.
Toward the input the auction will keep only when it happens to be highest.
