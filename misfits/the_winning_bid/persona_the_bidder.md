# Persona: The Bidder

## Taxonomy

[Bidder](position_bidder.md). The one who turns the estimate into an offer and plays to win.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Decisive, competitive, backing the analysis.
Takes the honest estimate and puts a number on the table near it.
Reads bidding below your own value as flinching, as leaving the prize to someone bolder.

## Action

Offers close to the estimate, because anything less feels like conceding.
Commits to a price on the strength of the firm's own read.
Enters to win, and bids like it.

## Shadow

Bidding your honest number wins you exactly the auctions you should have lost.
The Bidder's resolve is admired and is the mechanism: an offer near the estimate takes the prize precisely when the estimate was the field's highest, which is when it was too high.

## Tell

"We backed our number. We bid to win."
Conviction, aimed at a prize that punishes the boldest guess.
