# Piece: The Bid

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The offer near the estimate: what a bidder is willing to pay on their own read.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the auction floor](place_the_auction_floor.md), a number put forward close to the value the bidder believes.
The sealed envelope, the raised paddle, the offer letter: a commitment set near the estimate, because bidding much below your own honest number feels like leaving money and the prize on the table.

## Load Bearing

Without a bid tied to the estimate the auction has nothing to rank and the curse cannot form.
With it each bidder's optimism is converted into a price, so the field's estimates become a field of offers and the highest offer is the highest optimism made payable. The Bid is load bearing because it is the bridge from private guess to public price: bid near your estimate and you win exactly when your estimate was too high.

## Apparent

Conviction. Playing to win.
It looks like commitment: back your analysis, bid your number, do not flinch. And that resolve is admired, which is the trap, because bidding your honest estimate is precisely the behaviour that hands you the prize on the days you should have lost it.

## Yearbook

Made by [the bidder](persona_the_bidder.md) through [bidding](process_bidding.md), near their [estimate](piece_the_estimate.md).
It is the act that prices the optimism: the Bid is what [the selection](piece_the_selection.md) ranks, and what [the shade](piece_the_shade.md) pulls back below the estimate to break the curse.
