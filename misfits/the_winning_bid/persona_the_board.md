# Persona: The Board

## Taxonomy

[Principal](position_principal.md). The one who approves the winning bid, celebrates it, and inherits the overpay.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ambitious, accountable, hungry for the deal.
Approves the bid, applauds the win, announces the acquisition as a strategic triumph.
Reads a closed deal as value created, and books the applause before the value arrives.

## Action

Signs off on the top of the field and celebrates taking the prize.
Later, quietly, writes the value down when it comes in below the price.
Owns the overpayment it approved, and reaches for a reason that is not the bid.

## Shadow

The applause and the writedown are the same decision, seen at two times.
The Board cannot easily call its own celebrated win an overpayment, so the loss is more comfortably blamed on the market or the managers than on the strategy of bidding to win.

## Tell

"A transformational deal." ... and, later, "a challenging environment."
Two verdicts on one bid, and neither of them the curse.
