# Process: Bidding

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). Turning estimates into offers on the floor.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A field of estimates and a prize to compete for.
Once bidders hold their numbers, offers must be made.

## Direction

From private estimate toward public offer.
It moves each bidder's value into a price, set near the estimate.

## Lever

Conviction meeting the fear of leaving the prize to a bolder rival.
The force is the wish to win; the resistance is only the discipline to bid below one's own number, which the floor punishes.

## Echo

A field of offers with a top.
It hands forward the bids the auction will rank, the highest of which is the highest optimism made payable.
