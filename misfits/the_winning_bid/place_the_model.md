# Place: The Model

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). Where the shade is computed and the bid is pulled below the estimate: the reversal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An equation for the discount, a curve of shade against field size, the estimate and the bid set apart on purpose.
The place the logic of the auction is folded into the number: if a win means I was highest, bid as if I already know it.

## Holds

The reset, and the one correction that prices winning.
The place the offer is separated from the estimate, discounted for the selection the auction performs.

## Offers

A bid that lifts the curse.
Here a firm can compute how far below its honest read to bid, so that winning becomes a deal rather than a diagnosis.

## Withheld

The prizes and the applause a bolder bid would win.
The model gives the discipline that turns wins profitable and takes, in return, most of the wins, offering nothing the room can celebrate in the moment.
