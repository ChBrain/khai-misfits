# Persona: The Auctioneer

## Taxonomy

[Seller](position_seller.md). The one who runs the sale and draws the field upward.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Genial, energising, working the room.
Invites more bidders, stokes the competition, celebrates the rising price.
Believes a bigger, keener field is a better sale, and for the seller it is.

## Action

Widens the field and lifts the bids, extracting the top of the spread.
Rewards the highest offer and calls it the market's verdict.
Turns dispersion into revenue, one raised paddle at a time.

## Shadow

The seller's best outcome is the buyer's worst, and the mechanism serves the seller.
The Auctioneer is not cheating anyone: the more bidders drawn and the higher they are pushed, the further above the truth the winning bid sits, and that overpayment is exactly the seller's gain.

## Tell

"Do I hear more? A strong field tonight."
A better sale for the house, and a deeper curse for whoever wins it.
