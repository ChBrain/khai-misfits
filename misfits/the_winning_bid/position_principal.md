# Position: Principal

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The seat that approves the winning bid and inherits the overpay.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The authority to approve the bid, and the books that will carry the result.
The applause at the announcement, and the writedown that follows.

## Orders

Sign off on the top of the field and celebrate the prize taken.
Announce the deal as a triumph, and later reconcile the value that comes in below the price.
Own the outcome of the bid you approved.

## Loses

The ability to name its own celebrated win an overpayment.
A seat that applauded the deal is the seat least able to call it the curse, so the loss is blamed on the market or the managers instead.

## Drives

Toward the win booked as value created, and away from the overpayment it approved.
Toward a reason for the writedown that is anything but the bid.
