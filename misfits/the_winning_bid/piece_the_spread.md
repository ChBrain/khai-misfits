# Piece: The Spread

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The dispersion of estimates: the field of guesses whose top the auction will reward.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the auction floor](place_the_auction_floor.md), the range of numbers the bidders arrived at, scattered about a truth none of them can see.
Not one estimate but all of them together: the low reads and the high, the cautious and the hopeful, spread wide because the value is uncertain and each bidder saw the evidence a little differently.

## Load Bearing

Without a spread every bidder would hold the same number and winning would carry no information.
With it there is a top to the field, and the wider the spread the further above the truth that top sits, so more bidders and more uncertainty make the curse worse, not better. The Spread is load bearing because it is where the selection bites: the auction does not sample the field at random, it takes the maximum, and the maximum of many honest guesses is a biased one.

## Apparent

A healthy, competitive field.
It looks like the market working: many informed bidders, a range of views, price discovery in action, the more the better. That reading is the trap, because a bigger, more dispersed field is a field with a higher maximum, so the very competitiveness that looks like strength is what deepens the winner's overpayment.

## Yearbook

Formed from every [estimate](piece_the_estimate.md) at once, and harvested by [the selection](piece_the_selection.md).
It is the field the play measures: the Spread is what [the auctioneer](persona_the_auctioneer.md) widens by drawing more bidders in, and its top is exactly where the winning bid comes from.
