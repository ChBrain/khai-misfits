# Position: Underbidder

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The seat that lost the auction, and was spared by losing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A lower number, and the capital not spent on it.
The loss the room sees, and the escape it does not.

## Orders

Bid your number and, when it falls short, go home.
Accept the defeat and keep the money.
Do not chase the prize past your read.

## Loses

The prize, and the appearance of success.
A seat that loses cannot claim it was wiser, only lower, so its good fortune looks like failure until the winner's writedown arrives.

## Drives

Toward the capital preserved and the curse avoided, and away from the applause the winner gets.
Toward being, in hindsight, the protected majority the field concealed.
