# Piece: The Estimate

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The honest noisy guess: one bidder's read of the hidden value.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the data room](place_the_data_room.md), worked up from the same evidence everyone else is studying.
The seismic read, the valuation model, the scouting report: a careful number arrived at in good faith, landing somewhere around the truth, high or low by an amount no one can know in advance.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to bid from and no error to be selected for.
With it each bidder carries a value that is right on average and wrong in particular, and the auction will pick out whichever one erred highest. The Estimate is load bearing because it is honest and noisy at once: the honesty makes the bidder blameless, and the noise is the raw material the auction refines into a curse.

## Apparent

Rigor. A defensible number.
It looks like exactly what good analysis produces: a value backed by data, method, and judgment, the best guess anyone could make. And it is the best guess, which is the trap, because a field of best guesses still has a highest one, and the highest is the one that wins.

## Yearbook

Produced by [the geologist](persona_the_geologist.md) through [appraising](process_appraising.md), one of many in [the spread](piece_the_spread.md).
It is the bidder's honest input: the Estimate is what [the bid](piece_the_bid.md) is built near, and its place in the spread is what decides whether this bidder wins and regrets it.
