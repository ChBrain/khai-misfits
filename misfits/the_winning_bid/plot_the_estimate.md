# Plot: The Estimate

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The data room](place_the_data_room.md), where every bidder studies the same evidence for the same prize.
A thing of one hidden value is up for sale, and each firm must work up a number from the disclosures none of them can see past.

## Action

[Appraising](process_appraising.md): [The Geologist](persona_the_geologist.md), holding [the analyst's seat](position_analyst.md), reads the evidence and produces an honest estimate.

## Stage

[The Geologist](persona_the_geologist.md) tables [Estimate the Value](plan_estimate_the_value.md): appraise [the prize](piece_the_prize.md) and hand over [the estimate](piece_the_estimate.md), a defensible number landing somewhere around a truth no one can measure.
Across the field, the same work produces a scatter of such numbers, high and low.

## Tension

Nothing here is careless, and it seeds the whole curse.
The estimate is the best the evidence allows, arrived at in good faith, and it is right only on average: this particular read sits above the truth or below it by an amount no one can know. A field of such honest numbers has a highest, and the auction that follows will do exactly one thing with it, keep it.
