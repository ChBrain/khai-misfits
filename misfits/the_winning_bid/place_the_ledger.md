# Place: The Ledger

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). Where the true value comes in and the overpay is realized.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The books, quarters later: the returns, the impairment line, the value marked to what it actually was.
The place the prize stops being a story and becomes a number, set against the price that was paid for it.

## Holds

The overpay, made concrete.
The place the gap between the winning bid and the truth is finally recorded, the winner's optimism priced out.

## Offers

An honest accounting, in time.
Here the deal can be seen for what it was worth rather than what it was hoped to be, the curse rendered as a figure.

## Withheld

The chance to have known sooner.
The ledger tells the truth only after the payment is made and irreversible, so its clarity arrives too late to change the bid, useful only to the next one.
