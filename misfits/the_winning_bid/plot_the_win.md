# Plot: The Win

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The gavel](place_the_gavel.md), then [the boardroom](place_the_boardroom.md).
The hammer falls to the highest bid, and the room celebrates: the field was beaten, the prize is taken.

## Action

[Selecting](process_selecting.md): [The Winner](persona_the_winner.md), holding [the winner's seat](position_winner.md), claims the prize, and [The Board](persona_the_board.md) at [the principal's seat](position_principal.md) approves and applauds.

## Stage

[The Winner](persona_the_winner.md) works [Take the Prize](plan_take_the_prize.md) and [The Board](persona_the_board.md) works [Approve the Deal](plan_approve_the_deal.md): [the selection](piece_the_selection.md) has handed the prize to the top of the field, and the win is booked as vindication.
The number that won is read as the market confirming it, not as the highest guess in the room.

## Tension

The celebration is exactly backward.
Winning a common prize is not proof the estimate was right, it is proof it was the highest, which on a shared value is the read most likely above the truth. So the room applauds a diagnosis of its own overoptimism as if it were a verdict of worth, and books an overpayment, not yet realized, as a triumph.
