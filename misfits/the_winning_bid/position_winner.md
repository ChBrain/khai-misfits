# Position: Winner

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The seat that took the prize, holding the highest guess in the field.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The prize, and the celebration that comes with it.
The top of the spread, priced and paid.

## Orders

Claim the win and book it as vindication.
Read the market as having confirmed the number, and keep the strategy that took the prize.
Carry the prize home.

## Loses

The true reading of the win.
A seat that treats winning as being right cannot see it was only being highest, so it mistakes a diagnosis of overoptimism for a verdict of worth.

## Drives

Toward the win read as confirmation, and away from the win read as warning.
Toward doubling down on the bid that took the top of the field.
