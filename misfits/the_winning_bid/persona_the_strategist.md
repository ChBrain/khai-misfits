# Persona: The Strategist

## Taxonomy

[Bid Strategist](position_bid_strategist.md). The reversal: bids as if winning already carries bad news.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Cool, disciplined, unmoved by the room's heat.
Understands that the estimate is not the bid, and that a win is a signal about the estimate.
Reaches for the discount the field's size demands, not the number the analysis produced.

## Action

Shades the bid below the honest estimate, deeper the larger the field.
Prices the fact of winning, not just the prize, and accepts losing most auctions.
Wins less often and, when it wins, wins a deal instead of a curse.

## Shadow

The discipline is right and looks like fear, and its wins are the ones that never happened.
The Strategist is judged on visible defeats while the invisible reward, the overpayments avoided, cannot be shown, so being correct here reads as timidity to a board that measures deals won.

## Tell

"If we'd win it at that price, it's too high. Bid less."
The one correction that lifts the curse, said into a room that came to win.
