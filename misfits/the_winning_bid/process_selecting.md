# Process: Selecting

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The auction keeping the maximum of the field.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A field of offers and one rule: highest bid wins.
The moment the bids are in, the auction resolves them.

## Direction

From many offers toward one winner.
It moves to the top of the spread, awarding the prize to the largest number.

## Lever

A neutral rule meeting a biased field.
The force is the fairness of taking the highest offer; the resistance is nothing, which is the problem, because taking the maximum keeps the largest overestimate.

## Echo

A winner selected for optimism.
It hands forward the prize to the bidder whose estimate was highest, converting an unbiased field into a biased outcome.
