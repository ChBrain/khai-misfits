# Persona: The Underbidder

## Taxonomy

[Underbidder](position_underbidder.md). The one who lost the auction, and was spared by losing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Disappointed, at first, and quietly relieved later.
Bid a lower number, lost the prize, went home with the money.
Reads the loss as a defeat, until the winner's writedown reframes it.

## Action

Falls short at the auction and keeps the capital the winner spent.
Avoids the overpayment without meaning to, simply by having guessed lower.
Becomes, in hindsight, the field's protected majority.

## Shadow

Losing was the good outcome, and it did not feel like one.
The Underbidder was not wiser, only lower, so the escape from the curse looks like failure in the room and reveals itself as fortune only when the true value comes in.

## Tell

"We got outbid."
Said as a loss, and revealed, quarters later, as the win.
