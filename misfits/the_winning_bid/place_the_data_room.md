# Place: The Data Room

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). Where the prize is appraised and the estimate is formed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Folders of evidence, models on screens, the same disclosures every bidder is reading.
The place of diligence: seismic surveys, financials, scouting reports, worked over carefully to arrive at a number.

## Holds

The estimate, and the honest noise in it.
The place a bidder's read is built, right on average and wrong by an unknown amount.

## Offers

A defensible value.
Here the prize can be studied hard and reduced to a number backed by method, the best guess anyone could make from the evidence.

## Withheld

The truth, and which way the estimate erred.
The data room gives every bidder a careful figure and withholds the one thing that would save the winner: whether this particular read sits above the value or below it.
