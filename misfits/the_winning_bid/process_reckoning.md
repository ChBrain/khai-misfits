# Process: Reckoning

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). Reading the run of overpayments as one pattern.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A writedown, and someone asking whether it was the first.
Once the loss is booked, the pattern across deals can be examined.

## Direction

From a single loss toward the mechanism behind many.
It moves to see whether the overpayments were bad luck or adverse selection.

## Lever

The record across auctions meeting the wish to blame the market.
The force is a pattern that indicts the strategy; the resistance is a boardroom that applauded the wins it must now question.

## Echo

The curse named, or luck blamed.
It hands forward either a correction to how the firm bids, or a story that lets the winning bid be made again.
