# Plan: Shade the Bid

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the bid strategist's seat](position_bid_strategist.md), inside [The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Bid Strategist](position_bid_strategist.md)

## Direction

Bid as if winning already told you that you were the highest. Discount the offer below the honest estimate, deeper the larger the field, and accept losing most auctions.

## Orders

([The Strategist](persona_the_strategist.md) shades the bid for the selection, winning less often and, when it wins, winning a deal rather than a curse.)

## Implementation

([The Model](place_the_model.md); the [shading](process_shading.md) process; a discount that prices the fact of winning, held against a room that came to win.)

## Targets

- [ ] the bid is set below the honest estimate
- [ ] the discount deepens with the size of the field
- [ ] the fact of winning is priced, not just the prize
- [ ] fewer auctions are won, and the wins are profitable
