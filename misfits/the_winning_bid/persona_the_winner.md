# Persona: The Winner

## Taxonomy

[Winner](position_winner.md). The one who took the prize, and with it the highest guess in the field.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Elated, vindicated, sure the win proves the read.
Beat the field, took the prize, feels the market confirmed the number.
Reads winning as being right, when it was only being highest.

## Action

Claims the prize and books the victory.
Treats the win as evidence the estimate was sound, and doubles down on the strategy that produced it.
Carries home a prize priced by the rosiest view in the room.

## Shadow

The win is not vindication, it is diagnosis: you were the most optimistic.
The Winner reads a signal exactly backward, because on a common value the one who wins is the one whose estimate the auction should have warned them about, and the celebration is over an overpayment not yet realized.

## Tell

"We won. The market agreed with us."
The market only agreed you would pay most, which is not agreement about worth.
