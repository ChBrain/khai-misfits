# Pitch: We Won

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Triumphant, competitive, sure the win is the proof.
We studied it, we bid, we beat the field: the prize is ours and the market agreed with our number.
The voice of a room that came to win and reads winning as being right.

## Undertow

Under the triumph, a selection quietly at work.
The prize had one hidden value and the auction handed it to the highest guess, so winning is not that we were right, it is that we were the most optimistic, which on a common value is being the most wrong.
No one bid dishonestly and no one appraised carelessly: the winner is simply the top of an honest spread, and the top of the spread is where the error lives.

## Nerve

The feeling of a room raising a glass to a deal
it will write down in a year,
the underbidders going home stung and safe, and the victor certain the applause is about worth.

## Echo

After the run: a prize carried home above its value,
a writedown blamed on the market and never on the bid,
and the one correction that would have made winning a deal, bidding as if a win already told you that you bid too high, still unmade, because a room that came to win cannot hear that winning is bad news.
