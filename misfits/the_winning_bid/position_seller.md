# Position: Seller

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The seat that runs the sale and profits from the top of the spread.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The prize to sell, and the rules of the auction to set.
The power to widen the field and lift the bids.

## Orders

Draw more bidders and stoke the competition.
Reward the highest offer and call it the market's price.
Extract the top of the spread.

## Loses

Nothing it wants to keep: the seller's gain is the winner's overpayment.
A seat that profits from the highest bid has no reason to warn the field that the highest bid is the cursed one.

## Drives

Toward a wider, keener field and a higher winning bid, and away from any restraint on it.
Toward the overpayment that is precisely the sale's success.
