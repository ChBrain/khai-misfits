# Persona: The Geologist

## Taxonomy

[Analyst](position_analyst.md). The one who appraises the prize and produces an honest number.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful, quantitative, proud of the work.
Reads the same evidence everyone else has and lands on a value, backed by method and data.
Believes a good estimate is the best a bidder can bring.

## Action

Works up the value from the signals available and hands over a number.
Lands somewhere around the truth, high or low by an amount no one can know.
Delivers the honest input the bid will be built near.

## Shadow

The estimate is right on average and wrong in particular, and the auction keeps only the wrong-high ones.
The Geologist cannot know whether this read is above or below the truth, so a blameless, careful number is exactly what becomes a curse when it happens to be the field's highest.

## Tell

"This is our best estimate of what it's worth."
True, and best is still one of many, with a highest that wins.
