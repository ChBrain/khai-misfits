# Place: The Auction Floor

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). Where the bids are made and the field's spread becomes visible.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Paddles, sealed envelopes, a rising number and a room reading each other.
The place the private estimates become public offers: the bids laid down, the competition felt, the price climbing.

## Holds

The spread, and the bid drawn near each estimate.
The place the field's dispersion turns into a range of offers with a top the auction will keep.

## Offers

A price, and the thrill of the contest.
Here a bidder can commit, compete, and feel the market form, the energy of many parties chasing one prize.

## Withheld

A reason not to bid your number.
The floor rewards conviction and punishes restraint in the moment, so it offers every incentive to bid near the estimate and none to shade below it.
