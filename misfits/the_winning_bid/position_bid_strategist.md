# Position: Bid Strategist

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The reversal seat: it prices the fact of winning, not just the prize.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The theory of the auction, and the discipline to bid against the room.
The authority to shade the offer below the honest estimate.

## Orders

Bid as if winning already told you that you were the highest.
Discount the offer for the size of the field, and accept losing most auctions.
Price the win, not just the prize.

## Loses

The prizes a bolder bid would have taken, and the room's approval.
A seat measured on deals won cannot show the overpayments it avoided, so its correctness reads as timidity.

## Drives

Toward a shaded bid that wins less and profits when it wins, and away from the offer that tracks the estimate.
Toward the curse lifted, at the cost of the visible victories declined.
