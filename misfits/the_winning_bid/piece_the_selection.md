# Piece: The Selection

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). Adverse selection by the auction: the mechanism handing the prize to the maximum estimate.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the gavel](place_the_gavel.md), in the single rule that decides everything: highest bid wins.
The fall of the hammer, the opened envelopes ranked, the award to the top number: not a judgment of who was right, only a sort that keeps the largest, whatever produced it.

## Load Bearing

Without it a bid would win or lose at random and optimism would not be rewarded.
With it the auction performs a precise, hidden operation: it takes the maximum of the field, and the maximum of many honest guesses is systematically above the truth, so the rule that looks neutral is the rule that guarantees the winner overestimated. The Selection is load bearing because it is the machine that turns an unbiased field into a biased outcome, the exact step where honest estimates become a curse.

## Apparent

Fairness. The best bid wins.
It looks like the cleanest possible rule: no favourites, no judgment, the highest offer takes the prize. That neutrality is the disguise, because a rule that always keeps the maximum is a rule that always keeps the error, and selecting for the highest bid is selecting for the largest overestimate.

## Yearbook

Performed at [the gavel](place_the_gavel.md) by [the auctioneer](persona_the_auctioneer.md) through [selecting](process_selecting.md), over [the spread](piece_the_spread.md).
It is the hinge of the play: the Selection takes the top of the field and makes it [the winner](persona_the_winner.md), converting [the bid](piece_the_bid.md) into [the overpay](piece_the_overpay.md).
