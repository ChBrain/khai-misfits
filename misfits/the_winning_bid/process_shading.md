# Process: Shading

## Taxonomy

[The Winning Bid](play_the_winning_bid.md). The reversal: pulling the bid below the estimate to price the win.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A bidder who treats a win as bad news about their estimate.
Once winning is understood as selection, the bid can be discounted for it.

## Direction

From an offer near the estimate toward one below it.
It moves the bid down, deeper the larger the field, to account for the maximum being biased high.

## Lever

The logic of the auction meeting the room's hunger to win.
The force is the discipline to bid what the prize is worth given a win; the resistance is that every forgone win is a visible defeat.

## Echo

Fewer wins, and profitable ones.
It hands forward a bid that lifts the curse, at the cost of the prizes and applause a bolder offer would have taken.
