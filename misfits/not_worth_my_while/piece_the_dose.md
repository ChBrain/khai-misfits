# Piece: The Dose

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The measure each person is asked to take, the swallow or the habit or the appointment, carrying a real and certain cost paid now against a benefit that will be negligible to the one who pays it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Offered at [the consulting room](place_the_consulting_room.md), where it is held out to one person and weighed.
Aimed, in the tempting escape, at the tail of [the distribution](place_the_distribution.md), where the few for whom it pays are found.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no cost to weigh, and the refusal has nothing to be against.
With it the trap has its scale: the measure carries a real cost, a nuisance or a price paid now and certain, and this cost is what the negligible share must repay and cannot (Rose, on the individual calculus, a real present cost set against a tiny expected benefit). The Dose is load bearing because it is the concrete thing the individual weighs: not an abstraction but a swallow, a habit given up, an appointment kept, so the piece carries the real side of the scale, the certain cost that makes a sound person decline a benefit too small to feel.

## Apparent

A small ask, a sensible precaution, the least anyone could do.
Seen at the surface, the Dose looks like a trivial thing to accept, so the refusal looks like laziness, and the truth that the cost is real and certain while the personal benefit is negligible, that a sound person weighing them declines correctly, that the ask is small only against a sum no individual holds, stays hidden behind how minor a single dose appears.

## Yearbook

Offered through [urging](process_urging.md), weighed and refused through [declining](process_declining.md), aimed at the few through [targeting](process_targeting.md).
It is the real side of the scale: the Dose is set against [the share](piece_the_share.md) and found too costly for it, refused as [the decline](piece_the_decline.md), and asked of no one when the remedy shifts to [the environment](place_the_environment.md) instead.
