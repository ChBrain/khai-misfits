# Plot: The Shift

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Environment](place_the_environment.md), the harm that is the sum of many small risks, which no personal offer can reach.
The Shifter turning from the choices to the conditions, from persuading each to moving all.

## Action

[Shifting](process_shifting.md): [The Shifter](persona_the_shifter.md), holding [the shifter's seat](position_shifter.md), moves the whole distribution downward by structural means so the many shift together.

## Stage

The [shifter](persona_the_shifter.md), on [Shift the Curve](plan_shift_the_curve.md), moves the whole [distribution](place_the_distribution.md) downward by regulating the environment and reformulating the supply, so [the bulk](piece_the_bulk.md) carries the harm down with it and [the sum](piece_the_sum.md) is gathered without any share being weighed.
Asks no individual to accept a bad bargain and pays for the remedy collectively, so [the dilution](piece_the_dilution.md) is undone by moving the whole and not the person.

## Tension

Reaching the sum by moving the whole population is the only remedy that works, and it is costly and chosen by no one who benefits.
There is nothing to fault: the shift recovers the aggregate, and it does so without asking anyone to make the bad bargain. But the cost falls collectively and spares no visible person a nameable outcome, so the plot closes with the sum recovered over the sound objection of every individual that it was never worth their while, the remedy paid structurally and credited to no one.
