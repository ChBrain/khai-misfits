# Process: Declining

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The Individual weighing the measure and refusing it, the sound calculation that sets a real cost against a negligible benefit and correctly declines.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A measure offered to a person at low or moderate risk.
At [the consulting room](place_the_consulting_room.md), each individual taking the dose held out to them and weighing what it gives them against what it costs them.

## Direction

Toward the share weighed and the dose declined.
Because the personal expected benefit is a fraction of a percent off a risk already low and the dose carries a real and certain cost, the weighing sets a negligible benefit against a genuine cost and finds it does not pay, so the individual declines correctly, the refusal running not on any error but on the plain arithmetic of a share too small to repay the dose.

## Lever

The negligibility of the personal share.
Because the sum lives only across the whole population and no individual holds a felt piece of it, the benefit that reaches any one person is negligible, so declining runs not on selfishness but on the sound necessity of refusing a bad personal bargain, the lost sum assembled from no fault, only from each weighing the real cost against the tiny share nearest to them and refusing.

## Echo

A dose declined, and a slice of the sum forfeited.
The individual has refused soundly, so what reads as thrift is, in truth, one rational decline among many, each subtracting the slice that was their part of a total only the population could keep, every individual right to the last that there was nothing in it for them.
