# Position: Advocate

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The seat that holds the real aggregate benefit in view and urges the measure on each person, able to offer each only a negligible share of a sum none of them will hold.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The whole sum in view and each individual to offer it to: the real population benefit, and the measure to press on the many who make the population.
A truthful case: the aggregate benefit is real, the personal benefit is small, and the seat names both honestly.
No way to make the measure worth one person's while, because the sum was never in any share, so the seat can offer each person only a negligible slice of a total no one holds.

## Orders

Urge the measure.
Carry the real population benefit to each person, name it truthfully, and ask each to take the dose, because a population is reached one person at a time.
Press the good that lives in the sum, even though the share you can offer is small.

## Loses

The sale, exactly because the seat tells the truth on both sides of the scale.
Because the aggregate benefit is real and the personal benefit is negligible and the seat hides neither, it must ask each person to accept a bad bargain for a sum they will not hold, and cannot scold the sound refusal or sweeten the tiny share, so it loses a real total one rational decline at a time.
It gains an honest case for a real benefit, and loses the many whose sound refusals leave the sum ungathered.

## Drives

Toward the measure urged and honestly offered,
the real aggregate named and the small share not hidden,
so the seat that holds the whole sum can hand each person only a negligible slice, and watches the total slip away one correct refusal at a time.
