# Plan: Urge the Measure

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the advocate's seat](position_advocate.md), inside [Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Advocate](position_advocate.md)

## Direction

Carry the real aggregate benefit to each person and ask them to take the dose. Name the population benefit truthfully, do not hide the small personal one, and press the measure on the many who make the population, because a population is reached one person at a time and the sum is gathered only if the shares are.

## Orders

([The Advocate](persona_the_advocate.md) offers the measure to each individual and names the real sum honestly, able to hand each only a negligible share and unable to make the dose worth any one person's while.)

## Implementation

([The Consulting Room](place_the_consulting_room.md), where the measure is offered; the [urging](process_urging.md) process; [the dose](piece_the_dose.md) is pressed toward [the sum](piece_the_sum.md), the real aggregate offered one negligible [share](piece_the_share.md) at a time.)

## Targets

- [ ] the real aggregate benefit is named truthfully to each person
- [ ] the small personal benefit is not hidden on the other side of the scale
- [ ] the measure is pressed on the many who together make the population
- [ ] the sum is offered one negligible share at a time, worth no one person's while
