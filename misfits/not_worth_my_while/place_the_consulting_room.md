# Place: The Consulting Room

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). Where the individual is counselled and the measure offered one person at a time, and where the high-risk few are screened, the room in which the share is weighed against the cost and the dose is rationally declined.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the measure is offered and weighed, a clinic, a consultation, a counselling session, where the dose is held out to one person and that person counts what it gives them.
The Advocate urging the measure and naming the real sum, the Individual weighing their negligible share against a real cost, and the Screener finding the few for whom the offer is worth it.
Everyone reasoning correctly, the offer honest and the refusal sound.

## Holds

The personal weighing, and the sum it cannot see.
Here the measure meets one person at a time, so the Consulting Room holds the point where a real cost is set against a negligible benefit: a dose offered honestly and weighed soundly, so it is where the individual calculus runs its correct course and the share is found too small to repay the dose, and where the sum, which lives only across the whole population, is nowhere to be seen because no share of it is large enough to feel.

## Offers

A sound refusal and a spared bother.
Because the Consulting Room is where the dose is weighed one person at a time, it offers each individual the correct move on the numbers that reach them, the negligible benefit declined and the real cost avoided, so it hands out rational refusals, and hands them out one at a time, each slice of the sum forfeited soundly and the aggregate ungathered beneath a room full of correct decisions.

## Withheld

Any sight of the sum the many shares would have made.
The Consulting Room shows one person weighing one offer but cannot show the total that lives only across the whole population, because the sum is in the adding up and no share of it reaches the room, so it withholds the aggregate beneath the individual calculus, letting a real total slip away one sound refusal at a time, and, all correct decisions and no visible sum, it withholds the benefit that could be gathered only where the many are counted together and not here, one at a time.
