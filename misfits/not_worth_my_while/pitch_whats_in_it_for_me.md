# Pitch: What's In It For Me

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Thrifty, rational, individually sound.
The plain good sense of a person offered a measure, weighing what it costs them against what it gives them, and finding the personal gain too small to repay the bother.
Nothing false in it on its face: the cost is real, the personal benefit is genuinely tiny, and the refusal is the correct move on the numbers the person can see.

## Undertow

Under the thrift, a benefit real and large across the whole population but diluted so thin that each participant's share is negligible.
Because the sum lives only in the adding up and no one holds it, each sound refusal of a negligible share subtracts a slice, and the slices summed over the many were the sum, so the aggregate is lost by no error, and the arithmetic sits not in the few at high risk but in the many at modest risk who supply most of the cases.
What makes the measure worth doing at all, that it helps the whole population, is what leaves it not worth any one person's while.

## Nerve

The feeling of weighing the offer and rightly declining, the cost plain and the personal benefit too small to feel,
sure the refusal is sound, because the expected gain really is a fraction of a percent off a risk already low,
while the same sound refusal, made by each of the many, subtracts the slice that was their part of a sum no one held and that only the adding up could keep.

## Echo

After the run: a real aggregate benefit lost to many rational refusals of many negligible shares,
no one foolish and no one careless, only a sum spread so thin across a population that each part rightly declined it, and a high-risk strategy that was individually worth it missing the many at modest risk whose small risks were most of the harm,
and the one remedy that would have gathered the sum, shifting the whole distribution structurally so the many moved together, paid by no one who chose it and felt by no one who could point to their own saved outcome.
