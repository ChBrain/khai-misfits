# Plot: The Offer

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Consulting Room](place_the_consulting_room.md), a measure that would help the whole population, offered to each person one at a time.
The Advocate holding the real aggregate benefit in view and reaching for the individual who must take the dose.

## Action

[Urging](process_urging.md): [The Advocate](persona_the_advocate.md), holding [the advocate's seat](position_advocate.md), presses the measure on each person and names the real sum truthfully.

## Stage

The [advocate](persona_the_advocate.md), on [Urge the Measure](plan_urge_the_measure.md), holds out [the dose](piece_the_dose.md) and names [the sum](piece_the_sum.md), the real benefit that would fall across the whole population if the many took it.
Names the aggregate honestly and does not hide the small personal share, because a population is reached one person at a time and the sum is gathered only if the shares are.

## Tension

Offering a real benefit truthfully is right, and the truthful offer can promise each person only a negligible share.
There is nothing to fault: the measure is real, the sum is large, and the case is honest on both sides of the scale. But the benefit lives only in the adding up and no individual holds a felt piece of it, so the plot closes with the dose on the table before one person, the offer honest and the sum real and the share not yet counted.
