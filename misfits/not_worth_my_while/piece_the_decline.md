# Piece: The Decline

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The rational refusal, the sound move that sets a negligible share against a real cost and declines, the correct decision that, made by each of the many, forfeits the sum.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Made at [the consulting room](place_the_consulting_room.md), where the dose is weighed and refused one person at a time.
Repeated across [the distribution](place_the_distribution.md), where the many at modest risk each decline the offer that was never worth their while.

## Load Bearing

Without it the shares are gathered and the sum is kept, and there is no loss to reckon.
With it the trap has its mechanism: each individual, weighing a real cost against a negligible benefit, declines correctly, and the refusal is sound and not lazy (Rose, on the individual calculus, a rational refusal of a measure whose personal benefit is truly negligible). The Decline is load bearing because it is how the sum is lost without error: not a failure of will but a correct decision repeated across the many, so the piece carries the sound refusal whose very soundness subtracts the slice that was each person's part of a total only the population could keep.

## Apparent

Apathy, short-sightedness, a failure to act on one's own good.
Seen at the surface, the Decline looks like carelessness or free-riding, so it invites scolding and campaigns and appeals, and the truth that the refusal is rational, that a negligible benefit genuinely does not repay a real cost, that no scolding can change an arithmetic that is already correct, stays hidden behind how much a sound refusal of a real benefit looks like a mistake.

## Yearbook

Made through [declining](process_declining.md), setting [the dose](piece_the_dose.md) against [the share](piece_the_share.md) at [the consulting room](place_the_consulting_room.md).
It is the mechanism of the trap: the Decline is the sound refusal of a negligible share, repeated across [the bulk](piece_the_bulk.md) until [the sum](piece_the_sum.md) is lost, the correct decision whose sum is [the dilution](piece_the_dilution.md) that no personal appeal can answer.
