# Persona: The Shifter

## Taxonomy

[Shifter](position_shifter.md). The one who stops persuading individuals one by one and moves the whole population's risk distribution downward by structural means, at a cost no individual pays or chooses. The archetype of the radical remedy that reaches the sum by not asking anyone to make the bad personal bargain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Structural. Patient. Willing to move the whole population at once.
Is the one who changes the conditions rather than the choices, the regulator, the reformer, the one who reformulates the supply so the many shift together.
No coercion of the individual in it: the Shifter does not ask any one person to accept a bad bargain, but moves the environment they all share, so the whole curve slides and the many move with it.

## Action

Moves the whole distribution downward by structural means, regulating the environment, reformulating the supply, altering the conditions that set everyone's risk a little.
Recovers the sum by not asking any individual to weigh a negligible share against a real cost, and pays for it collectively instead.
Does what reaches the sum: it shifts the causes of incidence in the population rather than persuade the participants one at a time.

## Shadow

Not a free correction, a costly and unchosen collective move, and that is the whole difficulty: the remedy that reaches the sum falls on no one who chose it and benefits no one who can feel it.
Because the harm is the sum of many small risks and no personal offer can gather it, the Shifter must move the whole population's distribution at once, paying a structural cost that no individual selected and that spares no visible person a nameable outcome, working over the sound objection of every individual that the measure was never worth their while, the saved cases counted only in the aggregate and credited to no one.
It reaches the sum by moving the many together, and moving the many together is a cost no one who benefits ever chose to pay.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "change the conditions, not the choices," and reaches the sum, and can point to no one saved.
Says it moving the whole curve by structural means, over the objection of each individual who saw nothing in it, so the remedy that gathers the aggregate is paid collectively and felt by no one, the many shifted a little and no one able to name their own saved outcome.
