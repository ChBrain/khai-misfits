## Direction

Stop persuading individuals one by one and move the whole population's distribution downward by structural means. Regulate the environment, reformulate the supply, alter the conditions that set everyone's risk a little, so the many shift together and the sum is gathered without asking any one person to accept a bad personal bargain, at a cost that falls collectively and on no one who chose it.
