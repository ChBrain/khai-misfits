# Piece: The Bulk

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The many at low and moderate risk, the source of most of the cases, where a small risk borne by very many becomes most of the harm, the arithmetic heart of the paradox.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Sits in the middle of [the distribution](place_the_distribution.md), the vast modest majority where most of the cases arise.
Reached only from [the environment](place_the_environment.md), where a shift of the whole curve moves the many together.

## Load Bearing

Without it the harm sits in the few and the high-risk strategy gathers it, and there is no paradox.
With it the trap has its heart: most of the population's risk sits not in the few at highest risk but in the large body of people at low and moderate risk, because a small risk times a great many is more than a large risk times a few (Rose, on the many at moderate risk supplying more cases than the high-risk tail). The Bulk is load bearing because it is why both personal strategies fail: the offer is not worth the while of these many, and yet these many are most of the cases, so the piece carries the arithmetic that makes the sum unreachable one person at a time.

## Apparent

The low-risk crowd, the people who least need the measure and most sensibly decline it.
Seen at the surface, the Bulk looks like exactly where the measure matters least, so aiming at the high-risk few looks obviously right, and the truth that most of the cases arise from this modest majority, that a small risk borne by so many is most of the harm, that the crowd who least needs it each is where the sum lives together, stays hidden behind how little any one of them stands to gain.

## Yearbook

Sits in the middle of [the distribution](place_the_distribution.md), missed by [targeting](process_targeting.md), reached only by [shifting](process_shifting.md).
