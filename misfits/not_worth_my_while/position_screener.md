# Position: Screener

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The seat that runs the high-risk strategy, aiming the measure at the few for whom it is individually worth it, efficient and sound and unable to reach the many whose small risks are most of the harm.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The few at highest risk to target and a measure worth their while: the risk score, the screening, and the dose aimed where the personal benefit is largest.
An efficient aim: for the high-risk few the measure genuinely pays, so targeting them is individually correct and spares the many the bad bargain.
No reach on the bulk, because most of the cases arise from the many at modest risk, so the seat that aims where each unit does the most personal good aims away from where most of the harm is.

## Orders

Screen the high-risk.
Find the few at highest risk, offer the measure where the personal benefit is large enough to repay the cost, and concentrate the dose where it does the most personal good.
Aim where it is worth their while, and leave the many the offer would not pay.

## Loses

The sum, exactly because the seat aims where the measure is individually worth it.
Because the harm is a small risk times a large number and most of the cases come from the many at modest risk, the seat that targets only the high-risk few, helping them completely, still leaves the bulk of the cases untouched, so it loses the sum to the very efficiency that made the aim sound.
It gains a targeted strategy that pays for each chosen person, and loses the many at modest risk whose small risks were most of the harm.

## Drives

Toward the few targeted and genuinely helped,
the measure aimed where the personal benefit repays the cost,
so the seat that screens the high-risk gathers the cases of the few and misses the sum that lived in the many it did not target.
