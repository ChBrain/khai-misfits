# Plot: The Loss

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Distribution](place_the_distribution.md), the benefit that lived only in the adding up, and each holding only a negligible slice.
Each rational decline, made across the many, subtracting the share that was their part of the sum.

## Action

[Declining](process_declining.md): [The Individual](persona_the_individual.md), repeated across [the bulk](piece_the_bulk.md), forfeits each negligible [share](piece_the_share.md), and the shares summed were [the sum](piece_the_sum.md).

## Stage

The many [individuals](persona_the_individual.md), each on [Weigh My Share](plan_weigh_my_share.md), each decline [the dose](piece_the_dose.md) soundly, and the shares that would have summed to [the sum](piece_the_sum.md) go ungathered, the aggregate the population would have gained forfeited slice by slice.
No one errs, and yet the real total is gone, because it was only ever the adding up of the shares that each was right to keep.

## Tension

Every refusal that lost the sum was individually sound, and the sum was real and worth gathering.
There is nothing to fault: each decline was correct and the aggregate was genuine. But the benefit lived in no one's part and only in the whole, so the plot closes with the sum lost by no error, only by many sound refusals of many small shares, the real total gone because it was never in any one person's part to keep.
