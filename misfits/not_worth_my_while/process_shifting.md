# Process: Shifting

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The Shifter moving the whole population's distribution downward by structural means, the remedy that reaches the sum by not asking any individual to make the bad personal bargain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A sum that no personal offer can gather.
At [the environment](place_the_environment.md), the shifter changing the shared conditions rather than the individual choices, regulating the supply and the surroundings that set everyone's risk.

## Direction

Toward the whole distribution moved and the sum gathered.
Because the harm is the sum of many small risks and no individual holds a worthwhile share, shifting moves the whole curve downward at once by structural means, so the many carry the bulk with them without any of them weighing a personal offer, and the sum is recovered by altering the causes of incidence in the population rather than persuading the participants one at a time.

## Lever

The reach of moving the whole at once.
Because a structural change to the shared conditions moves everyone a little and does not depend on any one person accepting a bad bargain, shifting runs not on persuasion but on regulation, so the gathered sum is assembled from no personal consent, only from a collective move that falls on no one who chose it and spares no visible person a nameable outcome.

## Echo

A whole curve moved, and no one to point to as saved.
The shifter has reached the sum, so what reads as a costly and unchosen intervention is, in truth, the only remedy that gathers the aggregate, paid collectively over the sound objection of every individual that the measure was never worth their while, the saved cases counted only in the total and credited to no one.
