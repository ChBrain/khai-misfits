# Persona: The Advocate

## Taxonomy

[Advocate](position_advocate.md). The one who sees the real aggregate benefit and urges the measure on each person, truthfully, and cannot make it worth any one person's while. The archetype of the reformer who holds the whole sum in view and can offer each individual only a negligible share of it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Earnest. Truthful. Holding the whole benefit in view.
Is the one who presses the measure on the population, the public health officer, the campaigner, the physician who counsels each patient toward the dose.
No exaggeration in it: the Advocate names the real population benefit and does not hide the small personal one, so the case is honest on both sides of the scale.

## Action

Offers the measure to each person, names the large aggregate benefit truthfully, and asks each to take the dose.
Presses a real good that lives in the sum, and cannot answer the person who asks what is in it for them, because the honest answer is almost nothing.
Does what an advocate must: it carries the population benefit to the individuals who together make the population, one at a time, because a population is reached one person at a time.

## Shadow

Not a manipulator, a truthful bearer of a benefit that cannot be delivered to any one person, and that is the whole difficulty: the sum is real, the share is negligible, and there is no honest way to make the measure worth one person's while.
Because the benefit lives only across the whole population and no individual holds a felt piece of it, the Advocate can offer each person only a negligible share, so must ask the many to accept a bad personal bargain for a sum none of them will hold, and cannot scold the sound refusal or sweeten the tiny share, watching a real total slip away one rational decline at a time.
It tells the truth on both sides of the scale, and telling the truth is exactly what leaves the measure not worth any one person's while.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "this would save so many," and is right, and cannot say what it saves for you.
Says it holding the whole sum in view, and is met by a person weighing their own share, so the real aggregate is answered by a negligible personal benefit, and the honest case cannot close the sale.
