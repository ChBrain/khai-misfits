# Position: Shifter

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The seat that stops persuading individuals one by one and moves the whole distribution downward by structural means, reaching the sum at a cost no individual pays or chooses.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The whole population's distribution to move and the structural means to move it: the environment to regulate, the supply to reformulate, the conditions that set everyone's risk a little.
A remedy that reaches the sum: moving the whole curve gathers the harm that lived across the many, because it does not depend on any one person accepting a bad bargain.
No individual who chose it and no individual who can feel it, because the cost falls collectively and the saved cases are counted only in the aggregate.

## Orders

Shift the curve.
Move the whole population's distribution downward by structural means, regulating the environment and reformulating the supply, so the many shift together and the sum is gathered without a personal offer.
Change the conditions, not the choices, and pay for it collectively.

## Loses

The consent of the individual, exactly because the remedy asks no one and moves everyone.
Because the sum can be reached only by moving the whole distribution and the cost falls on no one who chose it and spares no visible person a nameable outcome, the seat must act over the sound objection of every individual that the measure was never worth their while, so it loses the assent of the many it saves and the credit for the cases it prevents.
It gains the sum, gathered structurally, and loses the individual's consent and any nameable person to point to as saved.

## Drives

Toward the whole distribution shifted downward,
the many moved together by structural means at a cost no one chose,
so the seat that shifts the curve recovers the sum over the objection of every individual in it, and can point to no one saved.
