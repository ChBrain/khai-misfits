# Place: The Distribution

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The population's risk curve, where the few at highest risk sit in the tail and most of the cases arise from the many at modest risk in the middle, the shape in which the arithmetic of the paradox lives.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the population's risk is laid out, the curve, the spread of risk across everyone, where the tail holds the few at highest risk and the middle holds the many at modest risk.
The Screener reading the tail and aiming at it, the Bulk sitting in the middle where a small risk times a large number becomes most of the harm, and the whole shape waiting to be moved.
The many outnumbering the few so far that the modest middle, not the dangerous tail, is where the cases come from.

## Holds

The arithmetic of the paradox, the shape that makes the sum live in the many.
Here the risk is spread across everyone, so the Distribution holds the fact that undoes both personal strategies: most of the population's risk sits not in the few at highest risk but in the large body of people at low and moderate risk, because there are so many of them, so it is where the bulk of the cases arise from the modest middle, and where a strategy that targets the tail, however efficient, must miss most of the harm.

## Offers

A true picture of where the cases come from.
Because the Distribution is where the risk is laid out across the whole population, it offers the shape of the harm plainly, the tail small and the middle vast, so it hands out the one fact that governs the whole production, that a small risk borne by very many is most of the harm, and hands it out as a curve that no personal offer can gather and only a shift of the whole can move.

## Withheld

Any share large enough to make the measure worth one person's while.
The Distribution shows where the cases come from but cannot make any individual's slice of the benefit large, because the risk is spread so thin across the many that each person's own stake is negligible, so it withholds the felt personal benefit beneath the real aggregate one, letting the sum sit visible in the shape and invisible in every share, and, all true picture and no worthwhile slice, it withholds the reach that a personal offer would need, leaving the curve movable only by shifting the whole of it at once.
