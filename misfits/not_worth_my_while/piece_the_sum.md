# Piece: The Sum

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The population's large aggregate benefit, real and countable across the whole, held by no individual and gathered only in the adding up of all the negligible shares.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Real across [the distribution](place_the_distribution.md), where the whole population's benefit is counted.
Gathered only at [the environment](place_the_environment.md), where the whole curve is moved so the many carry it together.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing worth gathering, and the refusals cost nothing.
With it the trap has its stake: the benefit summed over the whole population is large and real, but it lives only in the adding up and no individual holds a felt piece of it, so it can be reached only if the many shares are (Rose, on the population strategy, an aggregate benefit vast in total and negligible in each part). The Sum is load bearing because it is the real thing lost: not an abstraction but a countable total of prevented cases, so the piece carries the whole stake of the production, a genuine benefit that exists only across everyone and in no one.

## Apparent

An easy win, a large benefit anyone would take.
Seen at the surface, the Sum looks like something a rational population would obviously gather, so its loss looks like a failure of will, and the truth that no individual holds it, that it lives only in the adding up, that each rational refusal of a negligible share subtracts a slice of it, stays hidden behind how plainly a large aggregate benefit reads when it is named as a total.

## Yearbook

Real across [the distribution](place_the_distribution.md), urged one share at a time through [urging](process_urging.md), gathered only by [shifting](process_shifting.md).
It is the stake of the trap: the Sum is the adding up of every negligible [share](piece_the_share.md), living in [the bulk](piece_the_bulk.md) of the many, lost to each [decline](piece_the_decline.md), and forfeited whole through [the dilution](piece_the_dilution.md) that leaves each part too small to keep.
