# Process: Urging

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The Advocate pressing the measure on each person, the truthful case for a real aggregate benefit that can offer each individual only a negligible share.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A real population benefit that lives only in the sum.
At [the consulting room](place_the_consulting_room.md), the advocate carrying the aggregate good to each individual, because a population is reached one person at a time.

## Direction

Toward the measure offered and the sum named.
Because the aggregate benefit is real and lives only across the whole population, urging carries it to the many who make the population, naming the sum truthfully and not hiding the small personal share, so each person is asked to take the dose for a total none of them will hold, the case honest on both sides of the scale and unable to make the measure worth any one person's while.

## Lever

The truth of the aggregate benefit.
Because the sum really is large and really is worth gathering, urging runs not on exaggeration but on an honest case for a real good, so the advocate presses a genuine benefit that can be delivered only in the adding up, the ungathered sum assembled from no dishonesty, only from a truthful offer of a share too small for anyone to accept.

## Echo

A measure pressed, and a sum that cannot be sold one share at a time.
The advocate has told the truth on both sides, so what reads as persuasion is, in truth, an honest offer of a negligible share, the real aggregate slipping away one sound refusal at a time, the case unanswerable and the sale unclosable at once.
