# Piece: The Dilution

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md). The phenomenon whole: the benefit so diluted across the population that each share is negligible while the sum is vast, so every rational refusal is sound and the real total is still lost. The load-bearing piece the whole production is built to carry.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across [the distribution](place_the_distribution.md), where the benefit is spread thin over the many and the sum lives in the middle.
Undone only at [the environment](place_the_environment.md), where the whole curve is moved so the diluted benefit is gathered without any share being weighed.

## Load Bearing

Without it the pieces are only a cost, a share, a sum, a crowd, a refusal, and the trap does not close.
With it the whole paradox stands in one place: a benefit real and large in the sum but spread across the population so thinly that each participant's slice is negligible, so each person rationally declines a negligible share against a real cost, and the sum, which was only ever the adding up of those shares, is lost precisely because every refusal was sound (Rose, the prevention paradox whole, a measure bringing large benefit to the population and little to each individual, so what is worth doing collectively is not worth doing individually). The Dilution is load bearing because it is the phenomenon itself and not a part of it: the same spreading that makes the benefit real across the many makes it negligible to each, so the sum and the share are one distribution seen at two scales, vast in total and nothing in part, and the loss is authored by no one, only by a benefit thin enough that every part is right to let it go.

## Apparent

A collective failure of will, a population that would not do the small thing that would have helped it.
Seen at the surface, the Dilution looks like stubbornness or short-sightedness across a whole population, so it invites louder appeals and firmer campaigns, and the truth that no appeal can help, that each refusal is individually sound, that the benefit is diluted by the same arithmetic that makes it real, that only a structural shift of the whole can gather what no share is worth keeping, stays hidden behind how much a rationally forfeited sum looks like a failure of resolve.
