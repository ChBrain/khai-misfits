# Plot: The Weighing

## Taxonomy

[Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Consulting Room](place_the_consulting_room.md), a person offered the dose, weighing what it costs against what it gives them.
Each Individual counting the personal benefit and finding it a fraction of a percent off a risk already low.

## Action

[Declining](process_declining.md): [The Individual](persona_the_individual.md), holding [the individual's seat](position_individual.md), sets the negligible share against the real cost and rationally declines.

## Stage

The [individual](persona_the_individual.md), on [Weigh My Share](plan_weigh_my_share.md), sets [the dose](piece_the_dose.md) against [the share](piece_the_share.md), a real and certain cost against a benefit too small to feel, and refuses as [the decline](piece_the_decline.md).
Weighs soundly and declines correctly, because a negligible benefit does not repay a real cost, and there was, on the numbers that reach them, nothing in it for them.

## Tension

Refusing a negligible benefit against a real cost is sound, and the soundness is what loses the slice of the sum.
There is nothing to fault: the cost is genuine, the personal benefit really is negligible, and the refusal is the correct move. But the sum lived only in the adding up of every share, so the plot closes with the person having declined correctly, the personal expected benefit genuinely too small to repay the dose, the refusal rational and not lazy.
