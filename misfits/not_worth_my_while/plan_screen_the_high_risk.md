# Plan: Screen the High-Risk

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the screener's seat](position_screener.md), inside [Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Screener](position_screener.md)

## Direction

Find the few at highest risk and aim the measure at them, where the personal benefit is large enough to repay the cost. Concentrate the dose where each unit does the most personal good, spare the many the bad bargain, and accept that the aim which is individually worth it reaches only the few and not the many whose small risks are most of the harm.

## Orders

([The Screener](persona_the_screener.md) identifies the high-risk few and offers the measure where it genuinely pays, helping them completely and missing the bulk of the cases that arise from the many at modest risk.)
