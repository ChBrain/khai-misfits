# Plan: Weigh My Share

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the individual's seat](position_individual.md), inside [Not Worth My While](play_not_worth_my_while.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Individual](position_individual.md)

## Direction

Take the measure offered and weigh what it gives you against what it costs you. Count the personal expected benefit, find it a fraction of a percent off a risk already low, set it against a real and certain cost, and decline when the negligible benefit does not repay the dose, because on the numbers that reach you the refusal is sound.

## Orders

([The Individual](persona_the_individual.md) counts the personal benefit against the personal cost and rationally declines, one of the many whose sound refusals each subtract the slice that was their part of a sum only the population could keep.)

## Implementation

([The Consulting Room](place_the_consulting_room.md), where the measure is offered and weighed; the [declining](process_declining.md) process; [the dose](piece_the_dose.md) is weighed against [the share](piece_the_share.md), the negligible benefit set against the real cost and refused as [the decline](piece_the_decline.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the measure is weighed, personal benefit against personal cost
- [ ] the personal expected benefit is found negligible against a real and certain cost
- [ ] the dose is rationally declined on the numbers that reach the individual
- [ ] the slice forfeited is a part of a sum only the population could hold
