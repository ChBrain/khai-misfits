# Plot: The Crisis

## Taxonomy

[The High-Water Mark](play_the_high_water_mark.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Surge](place_the_surge.md), an emergency that the ordinary budget cannot meet.
A war, a slump, a disaster, a real and present need arriving all at once and demanding an answer the calm years never had to give.

## Action

[Cresting](process_cresting.md): the spending lifts to meet the emergency, taxes borne and programmes stood up that answer the moment exactly, the level rising to whatever the need demands.

## Stage

[The Provider](persona_the_provider.md) raises the spending at [the Surge](place_the_surge.md), on [Meet the Need](plan_meet_the_need.md), granting [the outlay](piece_the_outlay.md) that the crisis calls for and lifting the level past the line the ordinary years would bear.
Every measure of it is right: the need is real, the response is proportionate, and funding it is simply what a prudent body does.

## Tension

Meeting the crisis is the sound and obvious thing, and the sound and obvious thing lays the floor that will not fall.
There is no error in the rise: the emergency is genuine and the outlay the correct answer to it, so the Provider does exactly what they should. But the level raised to meet the crisis will outlive it, gathering a constituency and bedding into the baseline, so the plot closes with the spending crested and the need answered, the outlay sound at the moment of granting and already carrying, unseen, the mark it will leave when the water goes back down.
