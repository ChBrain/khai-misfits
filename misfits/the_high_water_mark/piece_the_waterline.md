# Piece: The Waterline

## Taxonomy

[The High-Water Mark](play_the_high_water_mark.md). The level the constituency built at, the line a cut must fall below to save anything, where the diffuse gain of lowering meets the concentrated loss of the people standing on it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs along [the Plateau](place_the_plateau.md) at the height the commitment settled to, the line [the Knife](position_the_knife.md) must cut beneath and [the Entitlement](position_the_entitlement.md) defends.
Flows as the boundary between saving and loss, the mark below which every reduction takes something real from someone named.

## Load Bearing

Without the Waterline the cut would be costless and the floor would fall freely: if no one had built at the level, lowering it would take nothing from anyone and the ratchet would have no pawl.
It is because a constituency has built its life at the commitment's height that any cut below the line lands on real people, so the Waterline is load bearing because it is the asymmetry made a place, the boundary that turns a diffuse saving into a concentrated loss and so makes the floor defensible (Olson). Its being inhabited is the whole mechanism: the gain of lowering the level is spread thin across all who fund it and the loss is borne wholly by those below the line, so the same waterline that measures the saving measures the harm, and because harm to the few is felt and saving to the many is not, the line holds, the cut always facing a victim and the status quo never showing one.

## Apparent

Just the current level of provision, the ordinary height of the floor.
Seen at the surface, the Waterline looks like a neutral number, the settled amount of a settled commitment, and the truth that it is a threshold with people living on it, that to cross it downward is to take from the named and give to the unnamed, that its whole force is the concentration of loss it marks, stays hidden behind how much an inhabited line resembles a mere measure.

## Yearbook

Held by [the Entitlement](position_the_entitlement.md) whose constituency stands on it, defended through [digging in](process_digging_in.md), and faced by [the Cutter](persona_the_cutter.md) who cannot lower [the watermark](piece_the_watermark.md) without cutting beneath it.
It is the asymmetry made a place: the Waterline is where [the outlay](piece_the_outlay.md) has gathered its people, the height below which [Bring It Down](plan_bring_it_down.md) must reach to save and where it meets the concentrated loss that defeats it, the line that makes the diffuse saving cost a named person everything.
