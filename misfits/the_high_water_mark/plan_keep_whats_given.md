# Plan: Keep What's Given

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Entitlement](position_the_entitlement.md), inside [The High-Water Mark](play_the_high_water_mark.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Entitlement](position_the_entitlement.md)

## Direction

Defend the commitment you have come to depend on against any move to cut it, turning out with the full force of a concentrated stake while the taxpayer who funds it stays home (Olson). Rely on the thing you were invited to rely on, and treat its ending not as a saving foregone but as a loss inflicted, because that is what it is to you. Do not volunteer to be the one who gives ground, since standing down alone means bearing the whole concentrated loss so the diffuse many can each keep a little, a sacrifice the structure asks of you and of no one else. Accept that your defence, honest and proportionate, is also the very thing that holds the floor up, and that you cannot fairly be asked to break the ratchet on yourself.

## Orders

([The Dependent](persona_the_dependent.md) builds on the commitment as though it were permanent, because for all they were told it is, and defends it against any cut with the attention of someone whose stake is concentrated and clear.)

## Implementation

([The Plateau](place_the_plateau.md); through the [digging in](process_digging_in.md) process; the Dependent gathers around [the outlay](piece_the_outlay.md) and holds it at [the waterline](piece_the_waterline.md), entrenching the commitment against the cut and letting [the sunset](piece_the_sunset.md) lapse unenforced.)

## Targets

- [ ] the commitment is relied upon as the permanent thing it was made to be
- [ ] its ending is met as a concentrated loss, not a saving foregone
- [ ] the diffuse funders are outfought by intensity, not numbers
- [ ] no ground is given alone, since the whole loss cannot fairly fall on one beneficiary
