# Position: The Knife

## Taxonomy

[The High-Water Mark](play_the_high_water_mark.md). The reforming seat, which would lower the floor when its crisis is over and can offer only a diffuse saving against a concentrated loss, right on the total and outgunned on the particular.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A mandate to cut, and a saving spread too thin to defend itself.
The seat holds the charge of bringing the spending back down where no present crisis justifies it, and the gain it can point to is real but dispersed, a little returned to each of the many who fund the floor.
An argument as its only instrument, since the seat has arithmetic on its side and no constituency behind it, the beneficiaries of the cut too diffuse to gather and too lightly served to care.

## Orders

Lower the floor the crisis no longer holds up.
Move to end the commitment whose emergency has passed, returning its cost to the many who bear it, and make the case that the saving, summed, is large and the outlay, now, is unjustified.
Expect no army, since the seat's gain falls so thinly that no one it helps will fight for it, so the office must act on the strength of the total alone, against a loss that has a face and a saving that has none.

## Loses

The contest, more often than not, which the seat enters already outmatched by the shape of the stakes.
The Knife brings a diffuse, deferred, unowned gain to a fight against a concentrated, immediate, nameable loss, and loses because intensity beats breadth: the few who would lose much will spend everything to stop the cut, and the many who would save little will spend nothing to make it, so the seat's correctness is worthless as force. What the seat forfeits is the moral cover its rightness ought to buy, since it cannot show a victim of the standing floor the way the constituency can show a victim of the cut, the cost of the commitment spread too thin for anyone to bleed from visibly, so the seat arrives with a total and the other side with a person, and in the eye that counts only who is hurt today the person wins and should, leaving the seat right in a way that helps no one who can feel it and hurts everyone who can.

## Drives

Toward a saving no one will fight for and a loss everyone it touches will,
and a rightness that buys no force and wins no thanks,
so the seat that alone would lower the floor is the seat the asymmetry defeats, correct on the sum and, on every particular cut, the one who took a real thing from a real person for a gain no one will trace.
