# Place: The Plateau

## Taxonomy

[The High-Water Mark](play_the_high_water_mark.md). The settled level after the crisis, where the spending rests above the old line, the settled floor the receding water leaves and the next surge climbs from.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A steady level and a quiet budget, the spending settled and the emergency over.
Shown as calm restored: the crisis has passed, the outlays continue at their new height without alarm, and the plateau looks like nothing but the ordinary, funded state of things, the way the budget simply is.

## Holds

Holds the level that did not recede, the height the crisis raised and the calm kept.
The Plateau is where the spending rests above the old floor: the emergency is gone but its outlays remain, bedded into the standing baseline, so the level that would have looked extraordinary before the crisis is now merely current, the plateau holding at whatever height the last surge left it.

## Offers

Offers a stable, funded present, and a higher starting line for the next rise.
Here the settled spending is real and its services genuinely delivered, so the Plateau offers a functioning state at its new height, and by making that height the accepted floor it offers the next crisis a raised base to climb from, each plateau the platform of the plateau above it.

## Withheld

Withholds that this floor was once a crisis ceiling.
What the Plateau hides is its own history: the level that now reads as ordinary was, a crisis ago, the extraordinary crest of an emergency, so the plateau conceals that the floor everyone stands on was built as a temporary answer, the height naturalised into the baseline and the mark forgotten as a mark, taken for the ground it was never meant to be.
