# Plot: The Cut That Isn't

## Taxonomy

[The High-Water Mark](play_the_high_water_mark.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Accounts](place_the_accounts.md), a reformer reading the standing lines and a crisis long over.
A commitment whose emergency has passed sitting as a permanent entry, and a hand that would lower the floor to where no present need holds it up.

## Action

[Climbing](process_climbing.md) met head on: the Cutter moves to enforce the sunset and lower the mark, and finds the concentrated resistance of the constituency against a diffuse saving no one champions.

## Stage

[The Cutter](persona_the_cutter.md) moves against [the outlay](piece_the_outlay.md) from [the Knife](position_the_knife.md), on [Bring It Down](plan_bring_it_down.md), reaching for [the sunset](piece_the_sunset.md) to cut beneath [the waterline](piece_the_waterline.md), and meets [the Dependent](persona_the_dependent.md) turned out in full while their own side, the diffuse many who would save a little, never arrives.
They are right about the total and alone on the particular, and the arithmetic that is on their side cannot be brought to the fight.

## Tension

Lowering the floor is correct on the sum and inflicts a visible loss on named people for an invisible gain to everyone, and there is no way through that is both right and kind.
The remedy is real: enforce the sunset, return the cost to the many, and the mark comes down. But the loss is concentrated on identifiable people who will spend everything to stop it, and the gain is spread so thin that no one it helps will spend anything to win it, so the reformer arrives with an argument and the constituency arrives with a person, and the person prevails. The plot closes with the choice laid bare and no free way through it: take the cut and be the one who ended a real thing for a saving no one will trace, or leave the mark where it stands and let the floor keep climbing, the correction always costing the cutter and the constituency more than the diffuse many will ever notice they were spared.
