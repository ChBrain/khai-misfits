# Plan: Bring It Down

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Knife](position_the_knife.md), inside [The High-Water Mark](play_the_high_water_mark.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Knife](position_the_knife.md)

## Direction

Lower the floor the passed crisis no longer holds up, moving to end the commitment and return its cost to the many who bear it, on the strength of a saving that is large in sum and unowned in particular. Enforce the sunset the crisis wrote and the calm let lapse, and make the case that the outlay, now, answers no present emergency. Expect no army behind you, since the gain falls so thinly that no one it helps will fight for it, and expect the full force of the constituency in front of you, whose loss is concentrated and clear. Accept that being right about the total buys no cover against the particular, that you will inflict a nameable harm on identifiable people for a diffuse gain no one will trace, and that you will more often lose the trade than win it.

## Orders

([The Cutter](persona_the_cutter.md) moves against the standing commitment when its crisis is over, offering a diffuse saving against a concentrated loss and bearing the resistance of those who depend on it with no answering force of their own.)

## Implementation

([The Accounts](place_the_accounts.md); against the [climbing](process_climbing.md) process; the Cutter would enforce [the sunset](piece_the_sunset.md) to lower [the watermark](piece_the_watermark.md), cutting below [the waterline](piece_the_waterline.md) where the constituency stands and bearing their concentrated loss for the diffuse saving.)

## Targets

- [ ] the sunset is enforced where it was written and let lapse
- [ ] the diffuse saving is pursued knowing it summons no constituency to defend it
- [ ] the concentrated, nameable loss to real people is accepted as the price of the correction
