# Position: The Entitlement

## Taxonomy

[The High-Water Mark](play_the_high_water_mark.md). The beneficiary seat, a standing call on the floor held by the group that depends on it, concentrated and organised where the funding side is dispersed and inattentive.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A standing claim on the spending, and a reason to guard it.
The seat holds a commitment relied upon, a service or payment or protection built into the plans of those it serves, so its continuation is not a preference but a stake, and the seat is organised around defending it.
Concentration as its whole strength, since the seat's power is that its benefit falls sharply on a few who will therefore act, against a cost spread so thin on the many that none of them will (Olson).

## Orders

Hold what you were given.
Defend the commitment against any move to cut it, turning out with the full force of a concentrated stake to protect the floor you stand on.
Rely on the diffusion of the other side, since the seat wins not by numbers but by intensity, the few who would lose much outfighting the many who would each save little, so the seat's method is simply to care more, per head, than anyone funding it can afford to.

## Loses

The standing to give ground alone, which the seat cannot yield without bearing the whole loss for a saving shared by all.
The Entitlement can hold its floor only by refusing to be the one that lowers, because to volunteer its own commitment for the cut is to take the entire concentrated loss so that the diffuse many can each keep a little, a sacrifice the structure asks of it and of no one else. What the seat forfeits is the ability to be reasonable about the sum without being ruined by it, since it may see plainly that the aggregate of all such defences is a floor that only climbs, and still cannot act on the seeing, because the fair share of the correction that would fall on it is not a share but the whole, so the seat that could ease the ratchet by standing down is the seat that standing down would cost everything, its own good faith in relying on the commitment the very thing the saving must override.

## Drives

Toward a commitment defended because it is depended on,
and a correction that cannot fairly land on the one who would volunteer for it,
so the seat that holds the floor up is the seat that could not lower it without becoming the sole victim of a gain meant for everyone, its justice and its immovability the same fact.
