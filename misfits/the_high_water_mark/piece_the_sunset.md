# Piece: The Sunset

## Taxonomy

[The High-Water Mark](play_the_high_water_mark.md). The expiry the crisis measure was given, the clause that was meant to end it when the emergency passed, and that lapses unenforced because enforcing it means taking the commitment from those who now depend on it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Written into [the Outlay](piece_the_outlay.md) at [the Surge](place_the_surge.md), and due at [the Plateau](place_the_plateau.md) where it fails to fire.
Flows as the promised end that never comes, the remedy built into the commitment at its birth and quietly let lapse at its maturity.

## Load Bearing

Without the Sunset there would be no remedy in the play at all, only a floor that rises with no design to lower it: the sunset is the one device that was meant to make the temporary temporary.
It is because the expiry exists and does not fire that the trap shows its shape, so the Sunset is load bearing because it is the remedy that fails on contact with the constituency, the honest attempt to bound a commitment that lapses precisely when the commitment has become dear to end. Its lapsing is the whole lesson: a sunset is easy to write when no one yet depends on the outlay and impossible to enforce once they do, so the clause that would have lowered the floor is disarmed not by being repealed but by arriving at the moment its enforcement costs a named person everything, the remedy present on paper and absent in force, the one part of the machine that was supposed to turn the ratchet back and the one part the constituency reliably jams.

## Apparent

Just a formality, a clause everyone expects to be renewed.
Seen at the surface, the Sunset looks like prudence already exercised, proof that the measure was meant to be temporary, and the truth that it will not be enforced, that its expiry is the moment its enforcement becomes unbearable, that a remedy timed to fire exactly when a constituency has formed is a remedy designed to lapse, stays hidden behind how responsible a sunset clause looks on the day it is written.

## Yearbook

Written by [the Provider](persona_the_provider.md) in good faith, let lapse by [the Dependent](persona_the_dependent.md)'s reliance, and reached for by [the Cutter](persona_the_cutter.md) who would enforce it against the floor.
It is the remedy that fails when it is needed: the Sunset is what [Bring It Down](plan_bring_it_down.md) would fire and [Keep What's Given](plan_keep_whats_given.md) lets lapse, the clause meant to lower [the watermark](piece_the_watermark.md) that the constituency at [the waterline](piece_the_waterline.md) reliably disarms, present on paper and never in force.
