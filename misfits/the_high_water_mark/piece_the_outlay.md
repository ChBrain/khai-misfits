# Piece: The Outlay

## Taxonomy

[The High-Water Mark](play_the_high_water_mark.md). The individual commitment, the spend raised to meet a crisis, sound when made and permanent once relied upon, the good decision whose accumulation is the rising floor.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Raised at [the Surge](place_the_surge.md) to meet the crisis, and comes to rest at [the Plateau](place_the_plateau.md) as a standing line in [the Accounts](place_the_accounts.md).
Flows as the funded answer to a present need, granted with little resistance and kept with great, the unit the whole ratchet is built from.

## Load Bearing

Without the Outlay being genuinely sound the trap would not hold: a wasteful spend could be named and cut, and the floor would fall when the waste was found.
It is because each outlay answers a real and present need, correct at the moment it is granted, that there is no blunder to reverse, so the Outlay is load bearing because it is the good decision the floor is made of, the sound commitment whose only fault is that it outlives its crisis and cannot be unmade without taking it from the people who came to depend on it. Its rightness is the whole mechanism: were it waste it could be defended against, but a commitment that met a true need is defended by that need's memory and by the constituency it gathered, so the rising floor is not an accumulation of errors but an accumulation of correct answers that each stayed longer than the question, the good grant becoming the standing cost precisely by having been good.

## Apparent

Just a line in the budget, one funded commitment among many.
Seen at the surface, the Outlay looks like ordinary provision, a need met and a service delivered, and the truth that it was once a crisis crest and is now a permanent floor, that it beds in and gathers a constituency and cannot be lowered, that its soundness is exactly what makes it immovable, stays hidden behind how completely a settled outlay resembles a thing that was always there.

## Yearbook

Granted by [the Provider](persona_the_provider.md) to meet the need, relied upon by [the Dependent](persona_the_dependent.md) who builds on it, and moved against by [the Cutter](persona_the_cutter.md) who would end it when its crisis is over.
It is the good decision that becomes the standing cost: the Outlay is what [cresting](process_cresting.md) raises and [bedding in](process_bedding_in.md) settles, the commitment [digging in](process_digging_in.md) entrenches and [the sunset](piece_the_sunset.md) was meant to end, each one a stroke of [the watermark](piece_the_watermark.md) the receding water leaves.
