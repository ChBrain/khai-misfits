# Persona: The Namer

## Taxonomy

[Surfacer](position_surfacer.md). Puts the undiscussable on the table as information.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Plain, steady, hard to embarrass.
She has noticed that the same failure keeps returning and that no one will say why, and she has noticed the second thing too: that not saying why is itself the thing no one will say.
She is willing to be the one who looks difficult.

## Action

She names it: not the person, the frame, and she names it as the thing to learn from, not the thing to blame for.
She holds the room in the discomfort it is built to flee, keeps the account separate from the verdict, and asks the question the sum was designed to skip.
She spends her own face to buy the room a lesson.

## Shadow

Candour that the room can absorb without changing.
Naming it once tests the frame, but the pull back to comfort is constant, and the easiest way to keep the peace with a Namer is to thank her, call her brave, take the note, and quietly learn what not to raise, so the reckoning becomes one more thing the room handled gracefully.
She is heard, or she is shelved, and both look like listening.

## Tell

"Can we talk about why this keeps happening?"
The room goes quiet, because that was the undiscussable part.
