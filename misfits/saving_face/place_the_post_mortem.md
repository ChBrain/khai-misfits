# Place: The Post-Mortem

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The reckoning after a failure too large to smooth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The table again, after the thing went wrong in the open.
A failure past deflecting, with everyone who touched it in the room.
The moment the frame could be questioned, or fixed once more.

## Holds

The choice, laid bare: the cause named, or the symptom closed again.
The pull of blame, and the harder pull of the clean patch that ends the meeting.
A lesson, present and unclaimed, waiting for someone to spend on it.

## Offers

The one opening where naming the frame is almost expected.
Cover for the Namer, if the failure is large enough.
The chance to separate the account from the verdict, at last.

## Withheld

The comfort the routine restored everywhere else.
The easy exit, if the room decides to hold itself in the discomfort.
The face the surfacing will cost, whoever chooses to pay it.
