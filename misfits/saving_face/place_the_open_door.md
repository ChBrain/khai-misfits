# Place: The Open Door

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The openness that is declared, and the gap under it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A door held open, a standing invitation to come with anything.
A leader sure the room can always reach her.
The espoused welcome, sincere and repeated.

## Holds

The gap between the door as declared and the door as used.
The verdict waiting the far side of it, learned from once and remembered.
An invitation no one tests twice.

## Offers

The belief, held by the chair, that nothing is being missed.
The look of a safe room, from the seat that never has to walk in.
A conscience clear that the way was always open.

## Withheld

The safety the sign promised, absent at the cost of using it.
The bad news that would have to be carried through it.
The one who tried the door and priced it, and did not return.
