# Persona: The Patch

## Taxonomy

[Fixer](position_fixer.md). Fixes the symptom, cleanly, and leaves the frame.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Practical, reliable, the one who gets it working again.
He likes a problem he can close, and he closes them well, and he is thanked each time.
The question of why the same problem keeps coming is not, to him, a problem he can close.

## Action

He takes the failure, finds the symptom, fixes it, ships it, and marks it resolved.
He works inside the frame he was handed, because the frame is not the kind of thing one questions here, and questioning it would mean saying the last fix, and the one before, treated the wrong thing.
He is fast, and he is right, and it comes back.

## Shadow

Competence aimed one loop too shallow.
Every clean fix of the symptom is a reason not to touch the cause, because the cause lives in the frame, and opening the frame would embarrass the people who built it, so the reliable fixer becomes the reason the real fault is never named.
The better the patch, the safer the fault.

## Tell

"Fixed it. Same as last time."
Same as last time is the finding. He files it as a fix.
