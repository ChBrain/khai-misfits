# Place: The Quiet Word

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The offstage channel where the real thing is said, and stays.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Two people, after, away from the table.
The corridor, the message, the coffee taken aside.
Where the thing no one would say in the room gets said at last.

## Holds

The truth, spoken where it costs the least and reaches the fewest.
The correction, delivered as a favour, off the record.
The undiscussable, discussed on condition it stay here.

## Offers

Relief, that someone else saw it too.
A face saved for both, said kindly and unwitnessed.
The sense of having handled it, without having to raise it.

## Withheld

The table, where the point could have changed the answer.
The record, where the lesson could have outlived the moment.
The room, spared the truth two people now privately share.
