# Position: Deflector

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The seat that takes the sting off, and the point with it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The room's trust as the one who keeps things kind.
The skill to meet a hard point with a soft one before it lands.

## Orders

Protect the people in the room from needless embarrassment.
Keep the exchange constructive, the tone even, the moment moving.

## Loses

The correction, deflected along with the discomfort it rode in on.
The record that a correction was ever there to lose.

## Drives

Toward a comfortable room, away from the wince that carries the lesson.
Kindness turned into a seal: the defensive routine worn as grace.
