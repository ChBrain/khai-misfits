# Position: Tone-Setter

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The seat whose first reaction prices every reaction after.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The authority the room reads for what is safe.
The first response to the first problem, watched by everyone who might raise the next.

## Orders

Run a tight, capable room, and handle problems decisively.
Keep standards high and let no slip pass without a clear account of it.

## Loses

The early warning, taught to arrive late or not at all.
The trust that a problem brought forward will be met with help, not a verdict.

## Drives

Toward a calm, controlled room, away from the mess of surfaced error.
The climate set from the chair: the safety the whole play runs on, or does not.
