# Pitch: Keep It Professional

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Measured, respectful, constructive.
A team that never raises its voice and never makes it personal, that keeps feedback kind and problems calm, proud of how well it handles hard things.
The voice of professionals at their best, sparing each other the sting, keeping the room safe and the tone even, sure that this is what maturity sounds like.

## Undertow

Under the courtesy, a correction no one will pay to make.
Sparing the sting is free and gracious and keeps the peace; making it means being the one who was rude, or slow, or difficult, so the questions are held, the errors are worked around, the hard points are softened until they carry nothing.
No one is dishonest: they are being kind, and the room, hearing only smooth exchanges, reads its own comfort as health.

## Nerve

The feeling of a room handling a problem beautifully,
everyone calm, everyone gracious, the mistake quietly absorbed and no one made to squirm,
a revision to the plan received as a slight to the one who made it, so the checkpoint goes unspoken,
and one person deciding, kindly, that naming the real thing now would only embarrass someone who is trying their best.

## Echo

After the run: a courteous team and a failure it saw coming,
a lesson that was there to take and never taken,
and a room so skilled at keeping everyone comfortable that it kept itself from ever learning.
