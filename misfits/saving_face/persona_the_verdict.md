# Persona: The Verdict

## Taxonomy

[Tone-Setter](position_tone_setter.md). Meets the news with a judgement, and sets the price of speaking.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Decisive, capable, quick to a view.
She runs a tight room and is proud of it, and she believes her door is open, because she has never once been told it is not.
Bad news reaches her and she reaches, at once, for what to do about the person who brought it.

## Action

She takes the first real problem and returns a verdict on it: who slipped, why it should not have happened, how it will not again.
It is fair, and fast, and it is the last time that problem is raised early.
She never asks for the next one, and does not notice it stops coming.

## Shadow

Decisiveness read as danger.
The quicker she judges, the faster the room learns that a problem is a thing you get blamed for holding, not a thing you get helped to solve, so the problems arrive later, larger, and already smoothed.
She mistakes the quiet for competence and the calm for trust.

## Tell

"My door is always open."
Nobody walks through it with anything that costs them.
