# Process: Face-Work

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The mutual, tacit upkeep of everyone's standing in the room.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Any two people who would rather not see each other lose standing.
The ordinary courtesy that keeps a room civil.
A shared, unspoken agreement to protect the line each of us is playing.

## Direction

Every exchange kept safe for the faces in it.
The slip absorbed, the ignorance covered, the challenge softened.
A room where no one is left visibly wrong.

## Lever

The reflex to spare, and the trust that it will be returned.
Manners standing in for the harder honesty.
The image cost, priced into every choice to speak.

## Echo

A civil, comfortable room, and a correction it cannot hold.
The kindness real, the learning gone with the sting.
Standing kept all round, at the price of the thing worth saying.
