# Process: Sealing

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The making of a topic off-limits, and its off-limits off-limits too.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A room that has learned which subjects cost too much to raise.
The quiet consensus that some things are simply not discussed.
The second silence: not naming that they are not named.

## Direction

The correction placed beyond reach, and the placing hidden.
A topic no one will raise, protected by a rule no one will state.
The feedback that would reveal the trap, sealed inside it.

## Lever

The undiscussable, and its undiscussability.
A cost so understood it needs no naming to be paid.
The absence of any signal that a signal is being suppressed.

## Echo

A room that cannot learn, and cannot say why.
Self-sealing: the one door to the fault locked from inside and painted over.
The look of a team with nothing wrong, kept by never asking.
