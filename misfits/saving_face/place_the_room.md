# Place: The Room

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The public floor where the look of a thing is highest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A full table, every seat watched by the others.
Faces read for a lead, questions read for weakness.
The place where speaking is a thing done in front of everyone.

## Holds

The image cost, priced by the size of the audience.
The standing each seat brought in and means to keep.
An account, kept, of who asked what.

## Offers

A clean, moving discussion, on the surface.
The comfort of a room where nothing lands wrong.
The look of a team with no objections.

## Withheld

The exposing question, too costly here to ask.
The half-formed doubt, spent nowhere near this many eyes.
The pause where a person could be wrong without paying for it.
