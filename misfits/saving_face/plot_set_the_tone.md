# Plot: Set the Tone

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Open Door](place_the_open_door.md), declared open, and the first real problem carried toward it.
A capable team brings its earliest bad news, a fault in the work, or a doubt about the leader's own plan, to the one who leads, and every seat that might carry the next is watching how this one lands.

## Action

[Face-Work](process_face_work.md): [The Verdict](persona_the_verdict.md), holding [the tone-setter's seat](position_tone_setter.md), meets the news with a judgement of who slipped and why, fair and fast.
The room reads the price off her face, and [the image cost](piece_the_image_cost.md) is set for everyone at once.

## Stage

[The Verdict](persona_the_verdict.md) tables [Set the Tone](plan_set_the_tone.md): handle the problem decisively and keep the standard high, sure that the calm which follows is trust, so the door stays declared open while the verdict waiting behind it teaches the room what open costs.
The bearer of the next problem does the sum before speaking, and begins to arrive late, or smoothed, or not at all.

## Tension

She is doing exactly what a strong leader is meant to do, and it is the doing that seals the room.
A verdict on a problem is decisive and fair, and standards held are a virtue, and an open door sincerely meant is a kindness. But to question the plan is heard as questioning the planner, so a checkpoint that should have been routine lands as a challenge to her judgement, and the room does not hear a standard, it hears the price of being the one who brings bad news, and it adjusts, so the very decisiveness that looks like command is what teaches the early warnings to stop coming, and the calm she reads as trust is the quiet of a room that has learned to wait until a problem is too large to pin on the bearer.
