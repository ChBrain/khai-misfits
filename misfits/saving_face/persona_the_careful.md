# Persona: The Careful

## Taxonomy

[Withholder](position_withholder.md). Weighs the look of speaking, and keeps it in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Competent, observant, keen to be seen as competent.
He notices the flaw, and he notices who is in the room, and he notices what asking would make him look like.
He would rather be quietly right later than loudly wrong now.

## Action

He does the image sum: ask, and risk looking like he did not already know; flag it, and risk looking like the one who cannot keep up; press it, and risk looking difficult.
He decides the look costs more than the flaw, and lets it pass.
He keeps a clean record and an unspent question.

## Shadow

The image cost falling on exactly the right doubts.
The questions worth asking are the ones that admit he does not know, and those are the ones that cost the most to ask, so the care that protects his standing buries the very thing the room needed said.
His silence looks like agreement, and joins the rest.

## Tell

"I did not want to look like I was not following."
He was following. He saw it first.
