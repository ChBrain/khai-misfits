# Position: Withholder

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The seat that holds a question and keeps it for its own sake.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A question, or a doubt, real and unspoken.
A reckoning of how asking it would look, and to whom.

## Orders

Keep a competent standing, do not look lost or slow or difficult.
Do not spend credibility on a point that might be nothing.

## Loses

The question, unasked, and the answer it would have forced.
The one contribution that reads as weakness to give and as insight only in hindsight.

## Drives

Toward a clean record, away from the exposing ask.
Self-protection joining the silence: the image cost paid in learning.
