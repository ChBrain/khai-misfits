# Process: Surfacing

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The reversal: the undiscussable made discussable, error taken as information.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone willing to name the frame and spend the face it costs.
A checkpoint built into the plan on purpose, so asking how it is holding up is the routine, not an affront.
An account of error kept clear of a verdict on the person.
A discipline chosen over the comfort the room prefers.

## Direction

The real cause on the table, examined before it is expensive.
The frame questioned, not just the symptom closed.
The lesson taken while it can still be taken.

## Lever

The undiscussable named as information, not indictment.
The room held in the discomfort long enough to learn.
Blameless account: the error separated from the one who erred.

## Echo

A lesson learned, and a face spent to learn it.
Held only while the naming is renewed against the pull back to comfort.
A room made, for a moment, able to hear the thing it least wants ... or thanking the Namer and sealing again.
