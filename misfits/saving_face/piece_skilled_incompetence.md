# Piece: Skilled Incompetence

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The competence that reliably produces the learning-proof result.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the retro](place_the_retro.md) and [the room](place_the_room.md), in the smoothest exchanges of the ablest people.
The paradox named by the source: people are highly skilled at the behaviour that keeps them from learning. The deft deflection, the graceful non-answer, the question folded away so cleanly no one feels it go. Practised, fluent, and aimed, by long habit, at protection rather than truth.

## Load Bearing

Without it defensiveness is clumsy and gets caught.
With it the defence is seamless, and the seamlessness is the problem, because the more skilled the routine the less anyone can see a correction was lost, only that a hard moment was handled well. And the ablest are the most fluent at it, having had the least practice at being visibly wrong, so competence and defensiveness rise together. The skill is real, and it is spent, expertly, on not learning.

## Apparent

Emotional intelligence, poise, mastery of the room.
It looks like the highest professional skill, because in every respect but one it is. That the one exception is the whole game, keeping the room from the correction, is hidden by how well everything else is done.

## Yearbook

Embodied by [the smoother](persona_the_smoother.md), and worn as poise by [the careful](persona_the_careful.md) who never once looks lost.
It is [the defensive routine](piece_the_defensive_routine.md) raised to an art, the reason [the undiscussable](piece_the_undiscussable.md) is sealed so invisibly, and the skill [the namer](persona_the_namer.md) must be willing to look clumsy against.
