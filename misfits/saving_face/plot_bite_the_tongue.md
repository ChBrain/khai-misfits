# Plot: Bite the Tongue

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Room](place_the_room.md), full and moving, one plan on the table and the flaw visible to at least one seat.
Someone sees it and reaches, almost, for the question, then reads the eyes that would be on the asking.

## Action

[Face-Work](process_face_work.md): [The Careful](persona_the_careful.md), holding [the withholder's seat](position_withholder.md), weighs the look of asking against the look of silence and lets silence win.
[The image cost](piece_the_image_cost.md) is paid in advance, in the head, and the question stays behind the teeth.

## Stage

[The Careful](persona_the_careful.md) tables [Save My Own Face](plan_save_my_own_face.md): do not risk looking ignorant, or slow, or difficult over a point that might be nothing, so the doubt is kept and the record shows a room with no objection raised.
His clean, following silence joins the others, and the plan reads as agreed by all, and the doubt, if it travels anywhere, travels only later to [the quiet word](place_the_quiet_word.md), said off the record where it reaches no one who could act on it.

## Tension

He is being competent and considerate, and both point the same way, toward not speaking.
Holding a half-formed doubt rather than derailing a moving room is real judgement, and the point genuinely might be nothing, and no one wants to be the seat that cannot keep up. But the questions that cost the most to ask are exactly the ones that admit not-knowing, which are exactly the ones worth asking, so the image cost falls heaviest on the most useful doubts, and a room of careful, capable people goes silent on precisely the things it most needed said, each reading the others' silence as proof there was nothing to say.
