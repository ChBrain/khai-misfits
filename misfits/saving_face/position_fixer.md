# Position: Fixer

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The seat that closes the symptom and leaves the cause.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The craft to make the broken thing work again, fast.
A queue of symptoms, each closable inside the frame as given.

## Orders

Restore service, clear the fault, mark it resolved.
Work within the design; do not reopen what is settled.

## Loses

The cause, untouched under a frame no one will question.
The recurrence, filed each time as fresh bad luck.

## Drives

Toward a closed ticket, away from the opened frame.
Single-loop correction: the symptom fixed so the system need not be.
