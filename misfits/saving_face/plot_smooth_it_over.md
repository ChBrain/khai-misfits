# Plot: Smooth It Over

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Retro](place_the_retro.md), the forum built for honesty, and a hard point that reaches the table anyway.
A correction is in the room, pointed and true, and the wince is already gathering on the face it names.

## Action

[Bypass](process_bypass.md): [The Smoother](persona_the_smoother.md), holding [the deflector's seat](position_deflector.md), meets the sharp point with a softer one and moves the room past the wince before it lands.
[Sealing](process_sealing.md): the avoided subject, and the fact that it was avoided, are set quietly beyond reach, so the deflection covers its own tracks.
[The defensive routine](piece_the_defensive_routine.md) does its double work: the face is spared, and the reason it needed sparing is spared too.

## Stage

[The Smoother](persona_the_smoother.md) tables [Smooth It Over](plan_smooth_it_over.md): keep it constructive, protect the person, keep the room moving, and fold the deflection in so cleanly that to name it would be the only rudeness present, so [skilled incompetence](piece_skilled_incompetence.md) carries the correction off and [the undiscussable](piece_the_undiscussable.md) gains one more sealed subject.
Everyone leaves comfortable, and the lesson leaves with the discomfort.

## Tension

She is the kindest and most skilled person in the room, and that is precisely why the room does not learn.
Sparing a colleague a public sting is genuine care, and keeping an exchange constructive is a real skill, and she is the best at both. But the same move that removes the sting removes the correction, and she does it so well that no one registers a loss, only a hard moment handled with grace, so her competence is spent, expertly, on not learning, and the better she gets the more invisibly the room seals, until the deflection of the deflection is itself the thing no one can name.
