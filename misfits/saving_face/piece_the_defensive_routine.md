# Piece: The Defensive Routine

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The skilled move that deflects a threat and blocks its examination.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the retro](place_the_retro.md), the moment a hard point reaches the table.
The practised action or policy that keeps embarrassment and threat away, and keeps the causes of the embarrassment away too. The soft answer to the sharp question, the constructive reframe, the change of subject that reads as grace. Present wherever a correction is due and none arrives.

## Load Bearing

Without it a threat is felt, examined, and learned from.
With it the threat is deflected and the deflection is covered, because the routine does two things at once: it spares the face, and it prevents anyone looking at why the face needed sparing. That double action is what makes it a defence and not just a kindness, and it is anti-learning by design, since the thing it removes from the room is the correction. It runs below awareness, so the people performing it are protecting, not hiding, and would be surprised to hear the two are the same.

## Apparent

Kindness, diplomacy, keeping things constructive.
It looks like the most emotionally skilled person in the room doing their best work, and in a sense it is. That the same skill reliably buries the lesson is invisible, because burying it is exactly what the routine is good at.

## Yearbook

Performed best by [the smoother](persona_the_smoother.md) under [smooth it over](plan_smooth_it_over.md), through the [bypass](process_bypass.md) process.
It is [skilled incompetence](piece_skilled_incompetence.md) in a single move, it builds [the undiscussable](piece_the_undiscussable.md) one deflection at a time, and it is the routine [the namer](persona_the_namer.md) must interrupt to surface anything.
