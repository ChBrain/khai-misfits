# Persona: The Smoother

## Taxonomy

[Deflector](position_deflector.md). Meets a hard point with a soft one, and spares the face.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Warm, deft, genuinely kind.
She reads the sting coming before anyone else and steps in to take it off, and she is good at it, the best in the room.
She thinks of it as protecting people, and it is.

## Action

She meets the hard point with a softer one, moves the room past the wince, keeps it constructive, keeps it moving.
She folds the deflection in so smoothly that to name it would be the only rudeness in a gracious exchange.
Everyone leaves comfortable, and nothing was said.

## Shadow

Kindness that is skilled at the wrong thing.
The better she is at sparing the face, the more reliably the correction is spared with it, and because she does it so well, no one sees a correction was lost, only that a moment was handled beautifully.
Her skill is the seal.

## Tell

"Let's keep this constructive."
The constructive thing was the point she just smoothed away.
