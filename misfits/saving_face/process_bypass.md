# Process: Bypass

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). Routing around the error, and acting as if nothing was routed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Anyone who sees the threat coming and steps to take it off.
The deflector meeting a hard point before it lands.
The instinct to spare the room the wince.

## Direction

The embarrassment avoided, and the avoidance hidden too.
The correction carried off with the discomfort it rode in on.
A path around the fault that leaves no sign a fault was there.

## Lever

A softer point offered in place of the sharp one.
The move made smoothly enough that naming it would be the rudeness.
Grace deployed exactly where a reckoning was due.

## Echo

A comfortable exchange, and a lesson steered past.
The bypass covering the bypass: nothing to see, nothing said.
The fault intact behind a room that handled it beautifully.
