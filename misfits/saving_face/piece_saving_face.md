# Piece: Saving Face

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The phenomenon whole: the courteous team that defends instead of learns.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across the whole run, in no single seat: the organisation that protects every face so well it cannot correct itself, and cannot say so.
The pattern entire, the image cost and the face-work and the defensive routine and the sealed undiscussable and the single loop, running together as one system. Present in the aggregate, owned by nobody.

## Load Bearing

Without it each part is a manageable human habit.
With it the habits compose into a trap, because each one removes a correction and hides the removal, and together they make a room that is skilled, kind, professional, and structurally unable to learn, defending where it means to be considerate. It is held by no one: no seat chose it, each only spared a face, and the sum of the sparing is an organisation that has quietly stopped learning and started defending, exactly as the source warns, and cannot discuss that it has.

## Apparent

A mature, harmonious, high-functioning team.
It looks like the good ones: calm under pressure, respectful, never dramatic, handling every problem with grace. That reading is what keeps the trap safe, because the symptoms of the disease are indistinguishable from the marks of a healthy team, until the failure it could not name arrives.

## Yearbook

The whole cast builds it and no one holds it: set in tone by [the verdict](persona_the_verdict.md), fed by [the careful](persona_the_careful.md), sealed by [the smoother](persona_the_smoother.md), preserved by [the patch](persona_the_patch.md).
It is broken, if at all, only when [the namer](persona_the_namer.md) spends [the face](piece_the_face.md) that [surfacing](process_surfacing.md) costs, and only for as long as someone keeps paying it.
