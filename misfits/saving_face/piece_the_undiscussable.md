# Piece: The Undiscussable

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The topic that cannot be raised, whose unraisability cannot be raised either.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Everywhere and nowhere: the subject each seat knows to route around, present in the gaps of every meeting and named in none.
The thing the room does not talk about, the recurring fault, the leader's blind spot, the design no one will reopen. And beneath it the second layer: that it is not talked about is itself not talked about, so the silence has no visible edge.

## Load Bearing

Without it a hard subject is merely hard, and can be brought to the table with effort.
With it the subject is sealed, because the undiscussability is recursive: you cannot raise the topic, and you cannot raise the fact that the topic cannot be raised, so there is no legitimate move that opens it. This is the self-sealing core of the trap. The one signal that would reveal the routine, someone saying "notice we never discuss this," is the signal the routine most reliably suppresses, which is why the room cannot correct itself from inside.

## Apparent

Nothing at all: that is the point.
It does not look like a locked door, it looks like an absence, a subject that simply never comes up, which reads as a subject there is no need to raise. The invisibility is the mechanism, not a side effect.

## Yearbook

Built by [the defensive routine](piece_the_defensive_routine.md) and kept by the [sealing](process_sealing.md) process, one avoided subject at a time.
It is what [the quiet word](place_the_quiet_word.md) half-opens and reseals, and what [the namer](persona_the_namer.md) breaks by naming, at the cost of [the face](piece_the_face.md).
