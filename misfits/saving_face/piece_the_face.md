# Piece: The Face

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The standing each person stages for the others, and means to keep.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the room](place_the_room.md), on every seat at once: the competent, in-control self each member is showing the rest.
The line a person is playing, the look of knowing, of coping, of belonging, that the whole table is quietly invested in maintaining, its own and each other's. Present in every exchange, named in none.

## Load Bearing

Without it a mistake is just a fact and a question is just a question.
With it a mistake is a threat to standing and a question is an admission, because the Face is the thing being protected, and to be corrected is to have it slip in front of others. The Model I drive is to keep it: stay in control, do not be embarrassed, do not embarrass. So the room runs on protecting faces, its own and its neighbours', and every act of protection removes a correction, because the correction is exactly what would have cost a face.

## Apparent

Ordinary professionalism and respect.
It looks like a mature room where people are considerate and composed, not a room organised around never being seen to be wrong. That reading is sincere, which is why the drive to save face passes as simple good manners.

## Yearbook

Protected by every seat, and staked highest by [the careful](persona_the_careful.md) and spared most skilfully by [the smoother](persona_the_smoother.md).
It is what [the image cost](piece_the_image_cost.md) is the price of, what [the defensive routine](piece_the_defensive_routine.md) defends, and what [the namer](persona_the_namer.md) must spend to break the seal.
