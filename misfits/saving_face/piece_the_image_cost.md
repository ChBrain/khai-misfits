# Piece: The Image Cost

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md). The price a person pays, alone, for speaking up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the room](place_the_room.md), in the head of the member deciding whether to ask.
The tax on interpersonal risk: ask and look ignorant, admit an error and look incompetent, raise a doubt and look negative, press a point and look difficult. Levied on the one who speaks, paid in standing, and reckoned before a word is said.

## Load Bearing

Without it people say what they see and the room learns in real time.
With it the safe move is silence, because the cost of the flaw is spread across everyone and deferred, while the cost of speaking is personal and immediate. This is the absence of psychological safety made concrete: not fear of the work, fear of the look. So the questions worth asking, the ones that admit not-knowing, are exactly the ones most expensive to ask, and the room's silence is not consent but arithmetic.

## Apparent

Reading the room, picking your moment, professional restraint.
It looks like judgement and tact, a person choosing not to spend capital on a half-formed point. That reading is often right, which is what makes the tax so easy to keep paying.

## Yearbook

Weighed and paid by [the careful](persona_the_careful.md) under [save my own face](plan_save_my_own_face.md), and set from the chair by [the verdict](persona_the_verdict.md), whose first judgement prices it for everyone.
It is the toll on [the face](piece_the_face.md), and the tax that [surfacing](process_surfacing.md) must overpay to lift.
