# Plot: Patch It

## Taxonomy

[Saving Face](play_saving_face.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Post-Mortem](place_the_post_mortem.md), the same failure back again, wearing a new symptom.
The fault has returned, as it did last time and the time before, and the room turns to the one who can close it.

## Action

[Patching](process_patching.md): [The Patch](persona_the_patch.md), holding [the fixer's seat](position_fixer.md), finds the symptom, clears it, and marks it resolved, working inside the frame as handed.
[The single loop](piece_the_single_loop.md) succeeds again, and each success is one more reason not to open the frame that keeps producing it.

## Stage

[The Patch](persona_the_patch.md) tables [Just Fix It](plan_just_fix_it.md): restore the thing, close the ticket, file the recurrence as fresh bad luck, because opening the design would mean saying the last fix treated the wrong layer, and the makers of that design are in the room.
The fault is preserved, whole, beneath a run of clean and honest fixes.

## Tension

He is doing good, real work, and the goodness of it is what buries the cause.
The fix genuinely works, service is genuinely restored, and pragmatism over endless re-litigation is a virtue on any team. But the cause lives in the frame, and the frame is the undiscussable thing, so reaching it would embarrass the people who built it and cost the faces the room is organised to save, and the single loop offers a way to be competent without paying that cost, so the better and faster the patching, the safer and more permanent the fault, corrected forever and never learned.
