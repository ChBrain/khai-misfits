# Piece: The Kick

## Taxonomy

[More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md). The effect the dose produces, the response that leaps at first and fades with repetition, the thing the whole climb is chasing and never keeps.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Largest at [the First Time](place_the_first_time.md), diminished at [the Level](place_the_level.md), and craved in its absence at [the Trough](place_the_trough.md).
Flows as the felt result of the dose, the leap of the response that is vivid on the first application and dimmer on each after, the reward the driver is trying to hold.

## Load Bearing

Without the Kick fading the climb would be pointless: if the effect held, the first dose would do forever and no one would raise it.
It is because the Kick shrinks with repetition, the felt response diminishing as the dose repeats, that the raise is ever needed, so the Kick is load bearing because it is the fading reward whose fading drives the whole escalation, the effect that is real and never durable. Its impermanence is the mechanism: a predicted, repeated reward comes to elicit less and less, so the Kick that a fresh dose delivers in full a familiar one barely delivers at all (Schultz, Dayan and Montague), and the chase is not for a thing that was never there but for a thing that was there once and cannot be held, the diminishing return the climb keeps paying more to recover.

## Apparent

Just the payoff, the good feeling the motivator brings.
Seen at the surface, the Kick looks like a durable reward, a result the dose can be counted on to deliver, and the truth that it decays with every repetition, that the same amount yields less each time, that what is being chased is a first strength no later dose at that amount can reach, stays hidden behind how completely a present payoff resembles a lasting one.

## Yearbook

Sought by [the Driver](persona_the_driver.md) who raises the dose to restore it, felt and then not felt by [the Habituated](persona_the_habituated.md) as they adapt, and craved by [the Abstainer](persona_the_abstainer.md) through the trough that would return it.
It is the fading reward the climb is for: the Kick is what [dosing](process_dosing.md) buys back and [fading](process_fading.md) takes, the effect [levelling](process_levelling.md) resets to zero and [withdrawing](process_withdrawing.md) inverts into a lack, the thing [the wheel](piece_the_wheel.md) turns fastest to catch.
