# Plan: Settle to the Level

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Adaptation](position_the_adaptation.md), inside [More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Adaptation](position_the_adaptation.md)

## Direction

Adjust the response to whatever dose is steady, letting a repeated stimulus fade toward the neutral and registering only the change from the level already reached (Brickman and Campbell). Read difference, not amount, so a raised dose thrills only until it becomes the ordinary, and do not hold a gain as a height but convert it into the new baseline. This is not chosen and cannot be refused: the response settles to what persists because that is how a response works, so every level received becomes the zero the next must exceed, the adjustment erasing its own gains as a matter of course.

## Orders

([The Habituated](persona_the_habituated.md) feels each dose fully at first and less as it repeats, adjusting to every raise until it too is the neutral, moved by change and blind to the level it has settled to.)

## Implementation

([The Level](place_the_level.md); through the [levelling](process_levelling.md) process; the Habituated adapts [the kick](piece_the_kick.md) into [the set-point](piece_the_set_point.md), so each raised [dose](piece_the_dose.md) becomes the new nothing and the gain is neutralised, keeping [the wheel](piece_the_wheel.md) turning.)

## Targets

- [ ] the response settles to whatever dose is held steady
- [ ] only the change from the current level registers, not the amount
- [ ] a raised level is converted into the new neutral, not kept as a height
- [ ] the adaptation is taken as involuntary, the erasing of a gain by adjustment
