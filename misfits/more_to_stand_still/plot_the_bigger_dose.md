# Plot: The Bigger Dose

## Taxonomy

[More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Level](place_the_level.md), the old dose no longer moving the needle.
A response that has adapted to the current amount, the effect faded to a stall, and the plain question of what to do about it.

## Action

[Dosing](process_dosing.md): the Driver raises the amount to restore the effect, and the larger dose clears the raised neutral by enough to register again.

## Stage

[The Driver](persona_the_driver.md) meets the stalled result by raising [the dose](piece_the_dose.md), on [Raise the Dose](plan_raise_the_dose.md), and the larger amount brings [the kick](piece_the_kick.md) back, moving the response that the smaller one had stopped moving.
The raise works, and working, it is plainly the right thing to have done.

## Tension

Raising the dose is the correct answer to a faded effect and the act that builds the next fade.
The increase is sound: more genuinely restores the response, and to have held the old amount would have been to accept a stall, so the Driver does exactly what the situation calls for. But the larger dose is now the level the response will adapt to, so the plot closes with the effect restored and the floor raised, the increase right at the moment it is made and already loading the neutral it will fade into, the correct move and the next turn of the wheel the same act.
