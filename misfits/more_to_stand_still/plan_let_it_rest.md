# Plan: Let It Rest

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Rest](position_the_rest.md), inside [More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Rest](position_the_rest.md)

## Direction

Take the dose away and hold it away, letting the response go unfed so the opponent that grew against it subsides and sensitivity returns (Solomon and Corbit). Bear the trough this opens, the level climbed to now the neutral fallen below, and do not resume the dose to relieve the low, since resuming abandons the reset at the moment it begins (Frederick and Loewenstein). Accept that the cost is front-loaded and certain while the benefit is deferred and unshowable, that the same dose withheld would end the discomfort at once by restarting the climb, and that the discipline is therefore a standing refusal of the easy relief always to hand, held on faith through a slump everyone can see toward a recovery no one will.

## Orders

([The Abstainer](persona_the_abstainer.md) withdraws the dose and holds it away through the after-reaction, bearing the trough rather than resuming, and waits out the low until a smaller dose can move the recovered response again.)

## Implementation

([The Trough](place_the_trough.md); through the [withdrawing](process_withdrawing.md) process, against the [dosing](process_dosing.md) it refuses; the Abstainer withholds [the dose](piece_the_dose.md) so [the set-point](piece_the_set_point.md) can fall back and [the kick](piece_the_kick.md) return at a smaller amount, stepping off [the wheel](piece_the_wheel.md) by way of the low.)

## Targets

- [ ] the dose is withheld and kept withheld through the after-reaction
- [ ] the trough is borne rather than relieved by resuming the dose
- [ ] the front-loaded, certain cost is accepted against a deferred, unshowable gain
- [ ] the reset is held on faith through a visible slump toward an invisible recovery
