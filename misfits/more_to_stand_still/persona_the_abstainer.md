# Persona: The Abstainer

## Taxonomy

[The Rest](position_the_rest.md). Withdraws the dose to let the response recover its sensitivity, and pays for the reset with the trough the climb has loaded, worse before neutral and doubted by those who see only the slump.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Withdrawing by discipline. Choosing the low to buy back the effect. Willing to feel worse to feel again.
Is the one who steps off the treadmill by taking the dose away, knowing that only rest returns a response to sensitivity, and that rest is not comfort but a drop.
No serenity wished on them: the abstinence is not calm but a deliberate descent, undertaken against the pull of a dose that would end the discomfort at once by resuming the very climb that caused it.

## Action

Takes the dose away and holds it away, letting the response go unfed so the opponent that dulled it can subside and sensitivity return.
Bears the trough that withdrawal opens, the level they climbed to now the neutral they fall below, and does not resume the dose to escape it, though resuming would relieve the low in an instant.
Does what a resetting mind does: pays the whole cost of the climb at once, front-loaded as a low deeper than the flatness that made them want off, and waits out the after-reaction until the response, unfed, recovers the sensitivity a smaller dose can move again.

## Shadow

Not a serenity, but the loneliness of a chosen low that looks to everyone like a fall, and that is the weight: they are worse than when they were on the dose, visibly and for a while, and the improvement they are paying for is invisible until it arrives, if it arrives.
What gnaws is the pull of the easy relief. The trough could be ended in a moment by taking the dose again, and every hour of the low is an hour they know the cure for and refuse, so the discipline is not a single choice but a standing one, remade against the same temptation each time the withdrawal bites, with no proof in hand that the reset is coming and every incentive to end the pain the way that caused it.
The bind is interior and unwitnessed: the reset, if it works, will look like nothing, just a response that a small dose can move again, so the low they bore will leave no monument and earn no credit, and they must hold the abstinence on faith through a slump that everyone can see and a recovery no one will, worse in the open and better, if at all, in private.

## Tell

Says "yes, I feel worse; that is the price, not a mistake."
Is sound and sounds like self-harm, because the low is real and chosen and the gain it buys cannot yet be shown, so the sentence that seems to defend suffering is the plain logic of the reset, a mind bearing the trough the climb loaded because there is no way back to sensitivity that does not pass through it.
