# Persona: The Habituated

## Taxonomy

[The Adaptation](position_the_adaptation.md). The one the motivator acts on, whose response adapts so the same dose stops moving them, adjusting to each raise until it too is the neutral, not ungrateful but built to read change and not level.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Adapting. Moved by the new, blind to the steady. Settling to whatever persists without choosing to.
Is the one on whom the dose lands, and whose response to it shrinks as it repeats, so the amount that thrilled them once comes to register as nothing, the adjustment involuntary and complete.
No greed wished on them: the fading is not ingratitude but the plain working of a response, the body and the mind tuning to the level they are held at and answering only to a change from it.

## Action

Feels the first dose fully and the repeated dose less, the same amount moving them by smaller and smaller degrees as their response settles to it.
Adjusts to each raise as it comes, so the larger dose thrills briefly and then becomes the ordinary against which only a larger one still would register, the neutral climbing with every increase they receive.
Does what an adapting response does: reads the difference from what it has grown used to rather than the absolute amount, so it cannot hold onto a gain, converting every raised level into the new zero from which it feels nothing until more arrives.

## Shadow

Not that they are hard to please, but that they cannot feel their own good fortune, and that is the ache: by every measure they receive more than they once did, and it lands as nothing, the abundance real on paper and absent in the feeling.
What wears on them is that they cannot explain the flatness without sounding like they are asking for more. They know the dose is larger than the one that once delighted them, and they cannot make it delight them again, so the lack they feel is invisible to everyone including, almost, themselves, a hunger that looks like ingratitude and is really just a response doing the only thing it can, reading change and finding none.
The bind is interior and mute: they did not decide to adapt and cannot decide to stop, so they are held responsible, by their own conscience most of all, for a flatness they did not author, wanting more not because more would satisfy them but because only more still registers at all, and knowing, dimly, that it would fade too.

## Tell

Says "I know it is more than before, and I still do not feel it."
Is the honest report of an adapted response and sounds like a complaint, because the amount really is larger and the feeling really is absent, so the sentence that seems to ask for still more is the plain account of a neutral that has climbed to meet the dose, a person telling the truth about a gain their own adjustment has already spent.
