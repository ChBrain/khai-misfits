# Position: The Adaptation

## Taxonomy

[More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md). The habituating seat, whose response shrinks with repetition and whose neutral resets to whatever level persists, so it reads change and never level and cannot bank a gain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A response that reads difference, and a zero that moves.
The seat holds a sensitivity tuned to change rather than amount, so a stimulus registers by how much it differs from the level already adjusted to, and fades as that level catches up to it.
An adjusting baseline as its whole nature, since the seat cannot fix its zero but resets it to whatever it is steadily given, so every level it is held at becomes the new nothing against which the next is measured.

## Orders

Adapt to what persists.
Settle the response to whatever dose is steady, letting the effect of a repeated stimulus shrink toward the neutral, and register only the change from the current level.
Do not hold a gain, since the seat's whole method is to normalise whatever it receives, so a raised level is not kept as a height but converted into the new baseline, the seat built to erase its own gains by adjusting to them.

## Loses

The ability to keep what it is given, which the seat forfeits by the very act of adapting to it.
The Adaptation cannot bank a raise, because the same adjustment that lets it settle to a level is the adjustment that turns that level into zero, so whatever it gains it loses to its own recalibration, not slowly but as a matter of course. What the seat forfeits is much of the capacity to be better off: it can be moved but holds a raise poorly, since any new height it is brought to becomes, once held, largely the neutral it feels little from, so the seat can bank scarcely any of what it gains, most of each increase spent on resetting the point the next must exceed, and the more it is given the higher the floor it feels little above, its adjustment the reason no amount stays enough for long.

## Drives

Toward a gain neutralised the moment it is held,
and a neutral that climbs to meet whatever it is fed,
so the seat that could be moved by anything is the seat that can keep nothing, its own adjustment converting every height into the zero the next must clear.
