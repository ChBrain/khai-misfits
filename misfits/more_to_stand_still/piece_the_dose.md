# Piece: The Dose

## Taxonomy

[More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md). The input applied to drive the behaviour, sound and effective each time it is raised, the amount whose every increase becomes the floor of the next.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Applied at [the First Time](place_the_first_time.md) and raised at [the Level](place_the_level.md), withheld at [the Trough](place_the_trough.md) where its absence is felt.
Flows as the motivator itself, the bonus or the jolt or the stimulant, given, adapted to, and given again larger, the unit the whole climb is built from.

## Load Bearing

Without the Dose being genuinely effective the trap would not hold: an input that did not work could be dropped, and no one would raise it.
It is because each larger dose really does restore the effect, works before it fades, that the raise is always the correct move, so the Dose is load bearing because it is the sound increase whose soundness drives the climb, the amount that is right to give at every step and ruinous only in the sequence. Its working is the whole mechanism: were it useless it could be abandoned, but a dose that reliably moves the response invites the next raise as the plain answer to the last fade, so the climb is not an accumulation of errors but of correct increases, each dose effective and each becoming the neutral the next must exceed, the escalation built entirely of amounts that were right to give.

## Apparent

Just the input, the motivator doing its job.
Seen at the surface, the Dose looks like a lever that works, a clean cause of a clean effect, and the truth that its effect is temporary and its level self-erasing, that each amount becomes the baseline the next must clear, that its reliability is exactly what makes the climb inevitable, stays hidden behind how plainly a working dose resembles a solution rather than a step on a treadmill.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the Driver](persona_the_driver.md) to restore the effect, adapted to by [the Habituated](persona_the_habituated.md) until it is the neutral, and withheld by [the Abstainer](persona_the_abstainer.md) to reset the response.
It is the sound increase that builds the climb: the Dose is what [dosing](process_dosing.md) raises and [fading](process_fading.md) empties, the amount [levelling](process_levelling.md) turns into [the set-point](piece_the_set_point.md) and [withdrawing](process_withdrawing.md) leaves an ache for, each one a tread of [the wheel](piece_the_wheel.md) that turns underfoot.
