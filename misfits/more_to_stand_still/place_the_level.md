# Place: The Level

## Taxonomy

[More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md). The moving neutral, what it now takes just to feel that nothing is wrong, the zero the response resets to whatever dose is held, climbing with each sustained raise.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A settled state, the ordinary against which everything is felt.
Shown as normal: the current level is simply how things are, the amount now required to feel neutral reading not as a height reached but as the plain baseline, invisible as a baseline always is to the one standing on it.

## Holds

Holds the reset zero, the point the response has adjusted to and feels nothing above.
The Level is where each raised dose comes to rest: the response settles to whatever it is steadily given, so the amount that was a gain becomes the neutral, and the effect is felt only as the difference from here, the zero silently climbing to sit just beneath whatever dose is now routine.

## Offers

Offers a stable sense of normal, and a floor that rises to swallow every raise.
Here the current level genuinely feels like nothing much, so the Level offers the ordinary calm of an adapted state, and by resetting to each new dose it offers the treadmill its footing, every increase absorbed into the baseline so that the next must exceed a higher floor to register at all.

## Withheld

Withholds that this neutral was once a peak.
What the Level hides is its own history: the amount that now feels like nothing was, not long ago, the dose that thrilled, so the Level conceals that the baseline everyone measures against is itself the sum of past raises adapted to, the effect of a real, hard-won climb rendered invisible by having become the zero it is now judged from. It is not a drift in what is perceived as normal but a reset of what it takes to feel it, the response's own floor rising rather than the eye's reading changing.
