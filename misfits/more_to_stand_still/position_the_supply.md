# Position: The Supply

## Taxonomy

[More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md). The dosing seat, which can raise the motivator to restore a faded effect but cannot hold a steady dose without the effect shrinking, the office that maintains only by escalating.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The power to raise the dose, and an effect that answers only to the raising.
The seat holds the means to drive the behaviour with a motivator and to increase it when it fades, so a stalled result is always within one more increment of moving again.
A single direction as its whole lever, since the seat can restore the effect by giving more but cannot restore it by giving the same, a held dose being a fading one, so its only working move is up.

## Orders

Raise the dose when the effect fades.
Meet a stalled result with a larger amount, since more restores the response and the response is what the seat exists to move, and read a flat effect as the signal to increase.
Do not hold steady expecting the effect to hold, since the seat's whole situation is that a constant dose yields a shrinking return, so standing pat is not maintenance but slow decline, and only the raise keeps the effect alive.

## Loses

The power to maintain without escalating, which the seat cannot keep because a steady dose does not hold a steady effect.
The Supply can buy the effect only by raising the dose, and every raise it makes becomes the level the response adapts to, so the seat is pushed to give more merely to stay where it was, its maintenance and its escalation the same motion. What the seat forfeits is a stable operating point: there is no dose it can settle on, because any dose held constant fades, so the seat is denied the option of steadiness and driven up a ladder with a ceiling, spending its capacity to raise on holding a line rather than crossing one. The structure ends at that ceiling: the seat's single direction is finite, so when the dose can go no higher the effect it was maintaining is left to fall, a hard limit set not by any choice but by the plain exhaustion of the only move the office has, the sole lever it holds having a top and the response no way to be held once it is reached.

## Drives

Toward a dose forever rising to hold a constant effect,
and a ceiling that ends the only move the seat has,
so the office that can always restore the response is the office that can never rest it, its maintenance indistinguishable from its escalation until the escalation runs out.
