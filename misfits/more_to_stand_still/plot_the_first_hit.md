# Plot: The First Hit

## Taxonomy

[More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The First Time](place_the_first_time.md), a motivator applied to an unadapted response.
A dose given for the first time, landing on a baseline it has not yet raised, the effect at its fullest possible strength.

## Action

[Fading](process_fading.md) at its start: the dose lands with full force on a response that has not adjusted, the Kick at its brightest and the neutral at its lowest.

## Stage

[The Driver](persona_the_driver.md) applies the motivator and [the Habituated](persona_the_habituated.md) feels it fully, [the dose](piece_the_dose.md) and [the kick](piece_the_kick.md) matched at their best on a response whose [set-point](piece_the_set_point.md) has not yet moved.
The effect is real and complete, and it looks like the plain power of the dose rather than a reading taken before adaptation.

## Tension

The first effect is the truest the motivator will give and the standard that dooms every repeat.
There is nothing wrong here: the dose works, the response leaps, and the result is everything the motivator promised. But the height came from a neutral not yet raised, so it will never be reached again at this amount, and the peak sets the mark the climb will chase, so the plot closes with the effect at its best and already becoming a memory, the first hit the height every later dose is measured against and, at the same amount, must fall short of.
