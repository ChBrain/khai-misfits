# Plot: The Fast

## Taxonomy

[More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Trough](place_the_trough.md), someone ready to step off the treadmill.
A dose withheld, the climb halted, and the opponent it built standing exposed with nothing left to oppose.

## Action

[Withdrawing](process_withdrawing.md): the Abstainer takes the dose away and the after-reaction carries the response below neutral, the low the mirror of the height.

## Stage

[The Abstainer](persona_the_abstainer.md) withholds [the dose](piece_the_dose.md), on [Let It Rest](plan_let_it_rest.md), and drops into the trough as [the set-point](piece_the_set_point.md) begins its slow fall, bearing the low that resuming would end in an instant, so that in time a smaller dose can move [the kick](piece_the_kick.md) again and [the wheel](piece_the_wheel.md) be stepped off.
They are worse than they were on the dose, plainly and for a while, and the recovery they are paying for cannot yet be seen.

## Tension

The only reset is a low deeper than the flatness that made you want off, and the cure sits within one dose of being abandoned.
The remedy is real: withhold the dose, let the opponent subside, and sensitivity returns so that less can move the response again. But the trough is front-loaded and certain while the recovery is deferred and unshowable, and the same dose withheld would lift the low at once by restarting the climb, so the plot closes with the choice laid bare and no painless way through it: bear the trough on faith and reset the response, or take the easy relief and resume the wheel, the exit priced at exactly the height the climb reached and payable only in the low no one else will see as anything but a fall.
