# Piece: The Wheel

## Taxonomy

[More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md). The escalation to stand still itself, the dose forever rising to hold a constant effect, the whole phenomenon of which every other piece is a part.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Turns at [the Level](place_the_level.md) where each raise resets the neutral, is fed from [the First Time](place_the_first_time.md)'s vanished peak, and can be stepped off only through [the Trough](place_the_trough.md).
Flows as the running-to-stand-still whole, the climb that spends more and more to hold the effect it began with, the shape of the trap in motion.

## Load Bearing

Without the Wheel there is no trap, only a set of separate raises: it is the Wheel that ties the fading to the dosing to the levelling, the one turning that makes a faded effect call a larger dose that becomes the neutral that fades again.
The Wheel is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole, the response that reads change and not level neutralising whatever it is given, so the system escalates merely to stand still (Solomon and Corbit; Brickman and Campbell). It carries the entire misfit: the effect that fades is the Wheel turning, the dose that rises is the Wheel climbing, the gain that resets to zero is the Wheel holding its place, so the Wheel is not one more piece beside the others but the shape they all compose, the turning whose every motion is another of the play's pieces at work. It is built of sound moves, which is why it cannot simply be stopped: each raise answered a real fade and each adaptation was involuntary, so stepping off means not correcting a mistake but bearing the trough the climb has loaded, taking the low that resetting the response demands.

## Apparent

Just effort producing results, a motivator driving a behaviour.
Seen at the surface, the Wheel looks like progress, the dose rising and the effect returning, motion that reads as getting somewhere, and the truth that it holds its place while it climbs, that the higher dose buys the same effect it started with, that the whole ascent is a run to stand still, stays hidden behind how much a spinning wheel underfoot resembles a road.

## Yearbook

Driven by [the Driver](persona_the_driver.md) who keeps raising the dose, turned by [the Habituated](persona_the_habituated.md) whose adaptation resets the ground, and stepped off only by [the Abstainer](persona_the_abstainer.md) through the low.
It is the whole shape of the treadmill: the Wheel is what [fading](process_fading.md) and [dosing](process_dosing.md) and [levelling](process_levelling.md) turn together, the climb [the dose](piece_the_dose.md) builds and [the set-point](piece_the_set_point.md) neutralises, the running-in-place that [the kick](piece_the_kick.md) leads and [withdrawing](process_withdrawing.md) alone can end, the escalation that holds its ground.
