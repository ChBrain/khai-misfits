# Position: The Rest

## Taxonomy

[More to Stand Still](play_more_to_stand_still.md). The withdrawing seat, which restores the response's sensitivity by taking the dose away, and must pay for the reset with the trough the climb has loaded, its cure front-loaded as a low.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The power to reset the response, and a cost paid before the benefit.
The seat holds the one move that returns a dulled response to sensitivity, the withholding of the dose, so that the opponent which grew against it can subside and a smaller amount can move it again.
A trough as the price of its instrument, since the seat cannot restore sensitivity without first passing through the withdrawal the climb built, the reset available only on the far side of a low.

## Orders

Take the dose away and hold it away.
Withhold the motivator so the response goes unfed and its opponent subsides, and keep it withheld through the after-reaction rather than resuming to relieve the discomfort.
Bear the trough as the price, since the seat's method works only by letting the low run its course, so resuming the dose to end the pain is abandoning the reset at the exact moment it has begun.

## Loses

The comfort of the present, which the seat trades away to buy the sensitivity of the future.
The Rest cannot reset the response without dropping it below neutral first, because the opponent that dulled the dose does not vanish when the dose stops but is left exposed, so the seat's cure opens with a low deeper than the flatness it means to cure. What the seat forfeits is any immediate case for itself: its cost is certain, immediate, and felt, while its benefit is deferred, invisible, and merely probable, so it must spend a real low now against a recovery it cannot show, and the same dose it withholds would end the trough at once by resuming the climb, so the seat is defined by refusing the easy relief it always has to hand, its whole discipline the holding of a line no present feeling rewards.

## Drives

Toward a reset that can be reached only through a trough,
and a cure whose price is paid in full before its benefit appears,
so the seat that alone can step off the treadmill is the seat that must fall before it recovers, its remedy indistinguishable, while it works, from the harm it treats.
