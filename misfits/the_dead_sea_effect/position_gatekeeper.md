# Position: Gatekeeper

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The seat that guards its ground against the fresh talent that could dilute it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A position it could not easily win again elsewhere.
A hand on the hiring, the onboarding, the daily welcome.
A well-founded fear that able newcomers could expose or replace it.

## Orders

Guard the ground.
Screen out the candidate too good, chill the one who slips through.
Keep the room as it is, and yourself in it.

## Loses

Nothing of its own, and the team the cure it repels.
It protects its place by turning away the talent that might have freshened the water, so its rational self-defence blocks the only remedy, and the salinity it depends on rises around it.
Safe in its position, and the reason the sea stays dead.

## Drives

Toward its own security,
which fresh talent threatens,
so the residue defends itself by repelling exactly the new blood the team needs.
