# Plan: Let It Sour

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the manager's seat](position_manager.md), inside [The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Manager](position_manager.md)

## Direction

Tend the urgent fires and trust the calm. Let the pay drift, the work go stale, the recognition lapse, not from malice but from having other things to watch, and read the low turnover as proof the team is healthy and needs nothing from you.

## Orders

([The Manager](persona_the_manager.md) lets the conditions curdle and mistakes the settling for loyalty.)

## Implementation

([The Basin](place_the_basin.md); the [souring](process_souring.md) process; neglect raises [the heat](piece_the_heat.md), and through [the mobility gap](piece_the_mobility_gap.md) the heat drives the able out while the calm hides it.)

## Targets

- [ ] the conditions are left to drift, from neglect not malice
- [ ] the able leaving is noticed only as ordinary churn
- [ ] the low turnover is read as health, not warning
- [ ] the heat rises unwatched and the evaporation runs beneath the calm
