# Pitch: The Loyal Core Remains

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Reassuring, steady, proud of its stability.
Turnover is normal, the ship is steady, and the ones who matter have stayed through thick and thin.
The voice of a settled team that mistakes low churn for health, spoken by people who are right that some churn is fine and cannot see that theirs has been sorting the able out the door.

## Undertow

Under the stability, a sea losing its fresh water and keeping its salt.
The ones with the most options left first and fastest when the place soured, and the ones with the fewest stayed, so the calm is not loyalty winning but mobility leaving, and every steady quarter has quietly raised the salinity.
No one is a villain: the talented owed a souring team nothing and were right to go, the stayers are not lesser people, and the manager only let it drift, and the sum of these fair moves is a team too settled to do the work and too still to notice.

## Nerve

The feeling of a stable, loyal team,
low turnover read as a sign of health,
the water going saltier with every good departure while the calm surface holds and no one reads the level.

## Echo

After the run: a team that kept everyone it could least afford to lose the least,
a residue entrenched enough to repel the new blood that might have freshened it,
and a stillness taken for calm that is really a sea nothing able can live in.
