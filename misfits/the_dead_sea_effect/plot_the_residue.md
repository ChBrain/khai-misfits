# Plot: The Residue

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The basin](place_the_basin.md), the water evaporating and the sediment left behind.
The ones with the fewest options remain, and become a larger share of a smaller team.

## Action

[Settling](process_settling.md): [The Stayer](persona_the_stayer.md), holding [the stayer's seat](position_stayer.md), sits tight while the able go, whether from want of a door or from love of the place, and [The Manager](persona_the_manager.md), holding [the manager's seat](position_manager.md), takes the remaining as a loyal core.

## Stage

[The Stayer](persona_the_stayer.md) tables [Sit Tight](plan_sit_tight.md): weigh the cost of leaving, find it too high, and remain, so it becomes part of [the residue](piece_the_residue.md) and each good departure raises [the rising salinity](piece_the_rising_salinity.md).
[The Manager](persona_the_manager.md) reads the steady stayers as loyalty rewarded, and misses that the sorting kept the least mobile.

## Tension

The residue is not lesser people, and the team is worse for concentrating it.
Some who stay are the backbone, staying for love and doing the work, and some stay only for want of a door, and the same stillness holds them both. That is the cruelty of the sorting: it keeps by mobility, not by merit, so the team thickens with those who could not leave, and cannot tell, from the calm, its steady souls from its dead weight.
