# Persona: The Gatekeeper

## Taxonomy

[Gatekeeper](position_gatekeeper.md). The entrenched residue that freezes out fresh talent to protect its ground.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Established, territorial, long past feeling replaceable and afraid of it.
Holds a position it could not easily win again elsewhere, and knows able newcomers could expose or supplant it.
Sits astride the hiring, the onboarding, the daily welcome, with the power to make or break a new arrival.

## Action

Guards the ground against the new blood.
Screens out the candidate too good, buries the new hire in busywork, withholds the help that would let them thrive, until the recruit is rejected or gives up and goes.
Keeps the water salty by refusing the fresh, protecting its place at the cost of the team's revival.

## Shadow

The residue defends itself, and in defending itself blocks the only cure.
It is not evil, only threatened, acting on the same self-interest anyone might, but its self-protection is precisely what repels the talent that could dilute the salinity, so the entrenched grow more entrenched and the sea cannot be freshened while they hold the gate.
Not a monster: a frightened incumbent whose reasonable defence is the disease deepening.

## Tell

"They wouldn't fit in here. We do things our own way."
The protective verdict that turns away exactly the talent the team needed.
