# Piece: The Heat

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The souring conditions: the dead pay, dead work, and neglect that drive the able to leave.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the basin](place_the_basin.md), rising unwatched beneath the calm.
Where the conditions curdle and the temperature climbs.

## Load Bearing

Without it no one would leave and there would be no sorting, only a steady team.
With it there is a reason to go, and the reason falls hardest on those with somewhere to go to: dead pay, stale work, and lapsed care make staying cost more than leaving, and the able feel that cost first. The Heat is load bearing because it is the degradation the turnover research puts at the root (Trevor, Gerhart & Boudreau): high performers leave when conditions and rewards fall short of their market value, so the heat is the driver, and everything downstream, the evaporation, the residue, the salinity, is what the heat sets in motion.

## Apparent

Ordinary friction, or a rough patch.
Souring conditions look like the normal wear of any workplace, a temporary dip, so the heat reads as a phase to ride out rather than a force selecting the team, and its steady rise hides inside the ordinary grumbling of work.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the manager](persona_the_manager.md) through [souring](process_souring.md), the driver the play opens on.
It is the temperature that boils off the best: the Heat acts through [the mobility gap](piece_the_mobility_gap.md), so the able evaporate and the immobile stay.
