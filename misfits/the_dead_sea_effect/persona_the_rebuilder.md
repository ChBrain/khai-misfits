# Persona: The Rebuilder

## Taxonomy

[Rebuilder](position_rebuilder.md). The one who moves to freshen the sea, against the residue that holds the ground.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Determined, clear-eyed, willing to spend and to fight.
Sees that the calm is salinity and that headcount held steady while talent drained away.
Aims to cool the conditions, keep the able, clear the blockers, and shelter the new.

## Action

Freshens the water at the source.
Fixes the pay and the work and the neglect that raised the heat, makes it worth the able's while to stay, removes the gatekeepers, and protects the recruit long enough to take root.
Does the slow, dear, contested work of reversing a sorting that ran downhill for years.

## Shadow

The hardest cut is the one it cannot make cleanly, telling the settled residue from the steady soul.
The same stillness hides the dead weight and the loyal backbone, so its purge risks losing the very people who stayed for love and carried the place, and its whole effort is resisted by the residue it must move, who hold the ground and know how to outlast a reformer.
Not a saviour with a clean sword: a rebuilder who must freshen a sea without draining the good water left in it.

## Tell

"Cool the water, keep the good, and don't mistake the loyal for the dead weight."
The whole cure and its whole difficulty in a single, hard-to-follow line.
