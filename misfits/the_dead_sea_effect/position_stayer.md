# Position: Stayer

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The seat that remains, held by fewer options or deeper ties.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Fewer offers, or heavier roots, or more to lose by moving.
A place it will not leave, from want of a door or from love of the room.
No easy outside to weigh against a souring team.

## Orders

Sit tight.
Weigh the cost of leaving, and find it too high.
Stay through what the mobile flee.

## Loses

Little by staying, and grows as a share of a shrinking whole.
It remains while the able evaporate, so the team's composition shifts toward it, and it becomes a larger part of what the team now is, for better if it is the backbone and for worse if it is the salt.
Sorted by mobility, not by merit, into the residue that thickens.

## Drives

Toward remaining,
which its ties or its want of options dictate,
so the least mobile are what a souring team keeps, whatever their worth.
