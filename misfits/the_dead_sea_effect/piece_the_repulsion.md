# Piece: The Repulsion

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The entrenched residue freezing out fresh talent before it can dilute the salt.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the gate](place_the_gate.md), where the new blood is admitted or turned away.
Where the residue meets the talent meant to dilute it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the team could be freshened simply by hiring, and the cure would be easy.
With it the cure is blocked at the source: the entrenched residue, threatened by able newcomers who could expose or replace it, screens them out, chills them, withholds the help they need, so the infusion meant to lower the salinity is repelled before it can mix in. The Repulsion is load bearing because it is the active defence that makes the effect self-sealing (Webster): the salinity does not merely rise, it protects itself, and this is why a Dead Sea cannot be freshened by new hires alone, since the residue guards the very gate the fresh water must come through.

## Apparent

Protecting the culture, or maintaining standards.
Turning away a candidate as a poor fit looks like careful hiring, and chilling a newcomer as not-yet-proven looks like proper seasoning, so the repulsion wears the face of quality control, and its work of keeping the water salty hides inside the language of guarding the team.

## Yearbook

Worked by [the gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md) through [settling](process_settling.md), defending [the rising salinity](piece_the_rising_salinity.md).
It is the salt guarding itself: the Repulsion turns away [the recruit](persona_the_recruit.md), so the sea cannot be freshened from the gate and the cure must reach deeper.
