# Persona: The Stayer

## Taxonomy

[Stayer](position_stayer.md). The one who remains, not lesser but less mobile.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Rooted, dependable, still at the desk when others have gone.
Has fewer offers, or deeper ties, or more to lose by moving, so the outside that beckons the talented does not beckon it.
Stays through the souring the able flee, whether from want of options or from love of the place.

## Action

Sits tight while the water evaporates.
Weighs the cost of leaving, finds it too high or the ties too strong, and remains, so the team's composition shifts toward it with every good departure.
Becomes, without choosing to, a larger share of what the team now is.

## Shadow

Its staying is read, wrongly, as either loyalty rewarded or dead weight retained, and it may be either.
The same stillness holds the steady soul who stayed for love and does the work, and the settled residue who stayed for lack of a door, so the stayer is at once the team's backbone and, if it is the latter, the salt that thickens, and no calm surface tells the two apart.
Not lesser by staying: only sorted by the same mobility that let the others go.

## Tell

"I'm not going anywhere. This is my place."
True of the backbone and true of the residue, and the sentence that hides which.
