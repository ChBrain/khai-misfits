# Place: The Refit

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). Where the sea is freshened, or the cure refused.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A plan to cool the conditions, retain the able, clear the blockers, shelter the new.
The rebuilder at work, the residue arrayed against it, and the hard sorting of backbone from salt underway.

## Holds

The cure, and its whole difficulty.
Here the heat can be cooled and the able kept, and here the judgement must be made that cannot be made cleanly, so the refit holds both the reversal of the effect and the risk of draining the good with it.

## Offers

A way to freshen the water.
The refit is where the sorting can be turned, the conditions mended and the level raised, so it offers the one real path from a dead sea back to a living one.

## Withheld

A clean or a cheap cure.
The refit demands spending, time, and a fight with the entrenched, and a distinction between the settled and the steady that no calm surface will supply, so it withholds any freshening that is fast, painless, or sure not to lose the loyal with the salt.
