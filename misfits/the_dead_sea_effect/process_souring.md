# Process: Souring

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The conditions curdling, raising the heat that drives the able out.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A team left untended, and a manager watching other fires.
Dead pay, dead work, lapsed recognition, and no one minding the temperature.

## Direction

Toward conditions the able will not endure.
The pay drifts, the work goes stale, the neglect accumulates, until staying costs the talented more than leaving, and the heat is high enough to boil the best off.

## Lever

Neglect that reads as stability.
Because a souring team can stay fully staffed and calm for a long while, the decay does not announce itself, so the heat rises unwatched, mistaken for the steadiness of a healthy core.

## Echo

A place gone bad beneath a calm surface.
The conditions curdled, the able beginning to reckon, and the evaporation ready to run under a stillness no one has yet learned to distrust.
