# Piece: The Evaporation

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The selective exit of the able: the best leaving first and fastest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the door](place_the_door.md), in the resignations of the skilled.
Where the top of the talent passes out and does not return.

## Load Bearing

Without it the souring would cost the team no more than any other, an even loss it could rehire against.
With it the loss is aimed: the ablest reckon soonest and leave soonest, so the team loses precisely its best and keeps precisely its least mobile, and the water of talent drops from the top down. The Evaporation is load bearing because it is the performance-linked turnover the studies find (Jackofsky; Trevor et al.): dissatisfaction acts most on those most able to act on it, so the exit is not random churn but a skimming of the cream, the mechanism that hollows a team while its numbers hold.

## Apparent

Normal, healthy turnover.
People leaving for better jobs looks like the ordinary churn every team has, a sign of a living labour market, so the evaporation reads as routine, and the fact that it is removing the top and only the top hides inside the normal shape of a resignation.

## Yearbook

Run by [the talent](persona_the_talent.md) through [evaporating](process_evaporating.md), driven by [the mobility gap](piece_the_mobility_gap.md).
It is the skimming of the best: the Evaporation leaves behind [the residue](piece_the_residue.md), the least mobile, as a larger and larger share.
