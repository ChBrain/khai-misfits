# The Dead Sea Effect: Reference

The research warrant for [The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The title is Webster's
coinage; the play runs on the turnover research linking performance to mobility, the adverse-selection
theory beneath it, and the job-embeddedness account of who stays, all cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging the Dead Sea effect as a **systemic system**: when a team's conditions sour, the ablest
employees, who have the most outside options, leave first and fastest, while the least mobile stay,
so voluntary turnover sorts by who can leave rather than at random, the level of talent falls, the
residue thickens, and each departure of the able worsens the place and drives out the next. The play
models the documented mechanics, the souring that raises the heat, the mobility gap between skill with
options and skill without, the evaporation of the talented, the residue that settles and stays, the
rising salinity by which a worse team drives out more of the able, the repulsion by which the
entrenched residue freezes out fresh talent, and the freshening that alone reverses it at a cost, as
the load-bearing vectors of the structure, not as a tale of the stayers being lesser people. The
governing finding of the source literature is the play's governing law: where exit is easier for the
able than the unable, degradation selectively removes the top of the distribution and retains the
bottom, so a team can hollow out while its headcount and its calm hold steady. It is the workforce
cousin of the house's Bad Coin and Market for Lemons: adverse selection turned on retention, where the
good, having options, are the coin quietly withdrawn.

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast the
  stayers as dead weight or the leavers as disloyal, because the source finding is precisely that the
  sorting needs no fault: the talented owe a souring team nothing and are right to go, the immobile
  stay because moving costs them more, not because they are lesser, and the manager who let it sour
  meant neglect, not harm. The misfit is that fair, individual choices sort a team downhill, keeping
  the least mobile and losing the most able.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into simply firing the residue and hiring stars.
  Freshening the water means cooling the conditions, paying to keep the able, clearing the blockers,
  and sheltering new talent, all slow and dear and resisted by the residue that holds the ground, and
  it turns on a judgement that cannot be made cleanly, telling the settled residue from the steady soul
  who stayed for love and does the work, because the same stillness hides them both. The reckoning is a
  costly, error-prone rebuild, not a purge.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here,
  never claimed as the house's. The staging is the house's; the turnover and embeddedness findings and
  the adverse-selection theory are theirs, and the honesty that Webster's metaphor is a vivid
  observation and not a law, that many stayers are excellent and many teams retain their best, is
  carried as carefully as the effect itself.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **[The Heat](piece_the_heat.md)**: carries the souring conditions (Trevor, Gerhart & Boudreau), the dead pay,
  dead work, and neglect that make the able leave.
- **[The Mobility Gap](piece_the_mobility_gap.md)**: carries the asymmetry of options (Akerlof), skill with an
  outside and skill without, that decides who can leave.
- **[The Evaporation](piece_the_evaporation.md)**: carries the selective exit of the able (Jackofsky; Trevor et
  al.), the best leaving first and fastest.
- **[The Residue](piece_the_residue.md)**: carries the retained least-mobile (Mitchell, Holtom & Lee), the ones
  who stay, whether from embeddedness or from having nowhere better to go.
- **[The Rising Salinity](piece_the_rising_salinity.md)**: carries the reinforcing loop (Webster), each good
  departure worsening the team and driving out the next of the able.
- **[The Repulsion](piece_the_repulsion.md)**: carries the entrenched residue freezing out fresh talent (Webster),
  the new blood rejected or driven off before it can dilute the salt.
- **[The Dead Sea Effect](piece_the_dead_sea_effect.md)**: carries the phenomenon whole (Webster), the team
  hollowed while its calm holds, and its remedy in a costly freshening that must not cut the steady with the settled.
