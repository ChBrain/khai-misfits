# Piece: The Dead Sea Effect

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The phenomenon whole: the team hollowed while its calm holds, and its remedy in a costly freshening.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the refit](place_the_refit.md), where the sea is freshened or the cure refused.
And across the whole basin, in every calm quarter that raised the salt.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of departures with no shape.
With it the departures become one law: the heat, the mobility gap, the evaporation, the residue, the rising salinity, and the repulsion are facets of a single sorting, in which souring drives out the able, mobility decides who can go, and the residue thickens and defends itself, so a team drains from the top while its headcount and its calm hold steady. The Dead Sea Effect is load bearing because it is the whole finding (Webster) with its whole cost attached: it holds the misfit together, and it holds that the cure is to freshen the water at the source, cooling the heat and keeping the able and clearing the blockers, slow and dear and error-prone, and never to be done by mistaking the loyal for the dead weight.

## Apparent

Either a proudly stable team, or a case for a clean sweep.
Seen from one side low turnover looks like health and loyalty; from the other, a hollowed team looks like it needs its stayers purged and stars hired in; and both miss the mechanism, that the calm is salinity and the stayers are sorted by mobility, so neither complacency nor a purge answers it, only a freshening that keeps the good water it still holds. The name flatters the danger: a sea can look its calmest exactly when it is least alive.
