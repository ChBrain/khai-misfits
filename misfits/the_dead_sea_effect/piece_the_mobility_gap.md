# Piece: The Mobility Gap

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The asymmetry of options: skill with an outside, and skill without.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the door](place_the_door.md), made a threshold between who can leave and who cannot.
Where ability opens the exit and its lack keeps it shut.

## Load Bearing

Without it the heat would drive everyone out equally and the team would simply shrink, not sour.
With it the exit is selective: the able have offers and can walk, the less able have few and must stay, so the same heat sorts rather than empties, subtracting the top of the distribution and leaving the bottom. The Mobility Gap is load bearing because it is the adverse selection beneath the metaphor (Akerlof): where the good can exit and the poor cannot, degradation quietly withdraws the good, and it is this gap, not any difference of loyalty or worth, that decides who the souring keeps.

## Apparent

Just the natural spread of ambition.
Some people move on and some settle down, which looks like ordinary variety of temperament, so the mobility gap reads as personality rather than mechanism, and its role in sorting the team by who can leave hides inside the ordinariness of some people having more options than others.

## Yearbook

Made a threshold at [the door](place_the_door.md), the asymmetry the whole sorting turns on.
It is the reason the exit runs downhill: the Mobility Gap turns [the heat](piece_the_heat.md) into [the evaporation](piece_the_evaporation.md), the best gone and the rest retained.
