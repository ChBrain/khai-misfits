# Position: Manager

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The seat that tends the conditions, and lets them sour.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The levers of pay, work, recognition, and care.
A team whose low turnover it reads as health.
The power to raise or lower the heat, and the habit of not looking.

## Orders

Keep the team stable.
Tend the urgent fires, and trust the calm.
Take low turnover as proof that all is well.

## Loses

Its best people, quietly, while congratulating itself on the calm.
By neglecting the conditions it raises the heat that drives the able out, and by reading the resulting stillness as loyalty it removes its own reason to act, so it loses the talent and the warning at once.
Not malicious, and the author of the souring all the same.

## Drives

Toward a stability it does not question,
which is the salinity it does not read,
so neglect runs the evaporation and the calm hides it.
