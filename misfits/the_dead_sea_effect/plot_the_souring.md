# Plot: The Souring

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The basin](place_the_basin.md), a team turning bad: dead pay, dead work, a manager watching other fires.
The surface is calm and fully staffed, and the temperature underneath is beginning to climb.

## Action

[Souring](process_souring.md): [The Manager](persona_the_manager.md), holding [the manager's seat](position_manager.md), lets the conditions curdle and reads the low turnover as health, while [The Talent](persona_the_talent.md), holding [the talent's seat](position_talent.md), begins to feel that staying is costing it something.

## Stage

[The Manager](persona_the_manager.md) tables [Let It Sour](plan_let_it_sour.md): tend the urgent fires, trust the calm, so [the heat](piece_the_heat.md) rises unwatched.
[The Talent](persona_the_talent.md), through [the mobility gap](piece_the_mobility_gap.md), starts to weigh the souring place against a market that would have it.

## Tension

Nothing looks wrong, and the temperature is already sorting the room.
The team is stable, staffed, and calm, and the manager reads all three as health. That is the trap laid, not sprung: conditions curdling just enough to make the able reckon, a heat rising beneath a surface no one distrusts, and the first of the best beginning, quietly, to look at the door.
