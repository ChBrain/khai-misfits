# Process: Rebuilding

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The water freshened at the source, against the residue and the risk of cutting wrong.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A calm read at last as salinity, and someone willing to spend against it.
The recognition that headcount held while talent drained, and the will to reverse the sorting.

## Direction

Toward a sea that freshens and holds its best.
The conditions cooled, the able paid and grown so staying is worth their while, the gatekeepers cleared, the recruit sheltered to take root, so talent begins to stay and the level rises.

## Lever

Cooling the heat and keeping the able, not purging the stayers.
The evaporation runs on bad conditions and mobility, so the cure is to mend the conditions and give the mobile a reason to remain, and the danger is the one cut it cannot make cleanly, telling the settled residue from the steady soul.

## Echo

A freshening bought dear, or a cure refused.
The water rising as the able stay and the new take root, at the cost of a slow, contested fight, or the reflex to cut winning and the loyal drained with the salt.
