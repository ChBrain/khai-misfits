# Piece: The Residue

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The retained least-mobile: the ones who stay, whether from embeddedness or from having nowhere better to go.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the basin](place_the_basin.md), the sediment left as the water goes.
Where the stayers become a larger share of a smaller team.

## Load Bearing

Without it the evaporation would just be an empty team, not a concentrated one.
With it the loss becomes a composition: as the able leave, those who remain, held by ties or want of a door, make up more and more of what the team is, so the average shifts toward them, and the team is not merely smaller but denser in the immobile. The Residue is load bearing because it is the who-stays that the embeddedness research explains (Mitchell, Holtom & Lee): people are held by links, fit, and the cost of leaving, so the residue is not simply the incompetent, it is the least mobile, and telling the loyal backbone from the settled dead weight within it is the whole difficulty of the cure.

## Apparent

The loyal core, or the dead weight, and it wears the same face as both.
A team's long-stayers look like its devoted heart or its stuck sediment depending on the eye, so the residue reads as loyalty to the fond and as deadwood to the harsh, and nothing in the staying itself tells which any given stayer is.
