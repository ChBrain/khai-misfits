# Persona: The Recruit

## Taxonomy

[Recruit](position_recruit.md). The fresh talent brought in to dilute the residue, repelled or evaporated in turn.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Able, hopeful, newly hired to freshen the team.
Arrives with skill and an outside of its own, meant to raise the water back up.
Does not yet know how salty the sea has grown, or who guards the gate.

## Action

Tries the water, and finds it will not have it.
Meets the gatekeeper's freeze and the residue's chill, sees the level of talent it was meant to raise, and, having options, does the same arithmetic the first talent did.
Leaves, or is never truly let in, so the infusion meant to dilute the salt adds nothing.

## Shadow

Being able and mobile, it is the least likely of all to stay in a place that repels it.
The very qualities that made it worth hiring, skill and an outside, are the qualities that let it leave the moment it reads the water, so the cure of new talent evaporates fastest of all, and each failed infusion teaches the residue that it can outlast the new.
Not a quitter: a good hire doing, on arrival, exactly what good people do to a Dead Sea.

## Tell

"This isn't what I signed up for. I'm out."
The newcomer's quick verdict that leaves the salinity exactly where it was.
