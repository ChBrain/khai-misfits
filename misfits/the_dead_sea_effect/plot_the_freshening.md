# Plot: The Freshening

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The refit](place_the_refit.md), where someone reads the calm at last as salinity and moves to reverse it.
The plan is to cool the conditions, keep the able, clear the blockers, and shelter the new.

## Action

[Rebuilding](process_rebuilding.md): [The Rebuilder](persona_the_rebuilder.md), holding [the rebuilder's seat](position_rebuilder.md), freshens the water at the source, while [The Stayer](persona_the_stayer.md), holding [the stayer's seat](position_stayer.md), stands among the residue as backbone and salt alike, hard to tell apart.

## Stage

[The Rebuilder](persona_the_rebuilder.md) tables [Freshen the Sea](plan_freshen_the_sea.md): cool [the heat](piece_the_heat.md), pay and grow the able, remove the gatekeepers, shelter the recruit, so [the dead sea effect](piece_the_dead_sea_effect.md) is answered at the source.
And it must tell the settled residue from the steady soul, the one cut it cannot make cleanly.

## Tension

The one cure that works turns on a judgement that cannot be made cleanly.
Freshening the water means cooling the heat and keeping the able and clearing the blockers, all slow and dear and fought by the residue that holds the ground, and its hardest move is to purge the dead weight without draining the loyal, when the same stillness hides them both. The sea begins to freshen ... or the cure is refused, or the steady souls are cut with the salt, and the salinity climbs back.
