# Persona: The Talent

## Taxonomy

[Talent](position_talent.md). The able one with options, who leaves first because it can.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Skilled, sought-after, holding an offer or three.
Has an outside: its ability is portable and the market wants it.
Owes a souring team no more than the work it was paid for, and knows it.

## Action

Takes the offer and goes.
Reads the souring conditions, weighs them against a market that will have it, and leaves first and fastest, the abler it is the sooner.
Removes, in one resignation, the skill the team could least afford to lose.

## Shadow

Its blameless, rational exit is the first drop of the evaporation.
It is right to go, owes nothing, and does no wrong, yet its leaving lowers the water of talent and worsens the place for the next tier of the able, who will then leave too, so the correct choice of each capable person is the mechanism that hollows the team.
Not disloyal: simply mobile, and mobility, under souring, means gone.

## Tell

"I've got a better offer. Nothing personal."
The fair and final word of the one the team most needed and least could keep.
