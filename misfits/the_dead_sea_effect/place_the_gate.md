# Place: The Gate

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The entrance, where fresh talent is admitted or turned away.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An interview, an onboarding, a first week: the point where new blood meets the team.
The gatekeeper deciding who fits, and the recruit reading the water it has been poured into.

## Holds

The repulsion, and the failed infusion.
Here the entrenched residue meets the talent meant to dilute it, so the gate holds the moment the cure is admitted or rejected, and, more often, the moment it is chilled until it leaves.

## Offers

A way in for new talent.
The gate is where the water could be freshened, where an able recruit might be added to raise the level, so it offers the team its one route to renewal.

## Withheld

A welcome that would let the new take root.
The gate is guarded by those the newcomer threatens, so it withholds the help and the fit that would keep fresh talent, and the very route to renewal becomes the place renewal is turned back.
