# Place: The Door

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The exit, where the able evaporate and the immobile cannot follow.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A resignation handed in, an offer accepted, a desk cleared.
The able passing through, and the less mobile watching them go, held back by ties or want of a door of their own.

## Holds

The mobility gap, made a threshold.
Here it is decided who can leave and who cannot, so the door holds the exact asymmetry that sorts the team: skill with an outside passes, skill without remains.

## Offers

A clean, honest exit to whoever can take it.
The door is open and leaving is nobody's disgrace, so it offers the able a fair way out, which is exactly why the able are the ones who take it.

## Withheld

An equal exit for all.
The door swings freely for those with offers and barely at all for those without, so it withholds escape from the immobile and grants it to the mobile, and the sorting it performs runs entirely downhill.
