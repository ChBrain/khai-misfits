# Plot: The Evaporation

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The door](place_the_door.md), the able holding offers and the least reason to endure the souring.
Resignations handed in, desks cleared, the best passing out and not returning.

## Action

[Evaporating](process_evaporating.md): [The Talent](persona_the_talent.md), holding [the talent's seat](position_talent.md), takes the outside offer and goes, first and fastest, while [The Manager](persona_the_manager.md), holding [the manager's seat](position_manager.md), reads the departures as ordinary churn.

## Stage

[The Talent](persona_the_talent.md) tables [Take the Offer](plan_take_the_offer.md): weigh a souring place against a market that will have it, and leave early, so [the mobility gap](piece_the_mobility_gap.md) turns the heat into [the evaporation](piece_the_evaporation.md).
[The Manager](persona_the_manager.md) books each exit as a normal resignation, not as the skimming of the top it is.

## Tension

Every leaver is right to go, and the leaving takes only the best.
No one betrays anyone: the able owe a souring team nothing, and each resignation is a fair, private choice. That is the misfit's engine: the exit is not random but aimed, the ablest reckoning soonest and going soonest, so the team loses precisely the skill it cannot spare, and calls the loss routine.
