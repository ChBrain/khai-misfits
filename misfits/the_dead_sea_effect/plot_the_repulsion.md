# Plot: The Repulsion

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The gate](place_the_gate.md), fresh talent brought in to dilute the residue.
An able recruit arrives, hopeful, not yet knowing how salty the sea has grown or who guards the entrance.

## Action

[Settling](process_settling.md) turned to defence: [The Gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md), holding [the gatekeeper's seat](position_gatekeeper.md), freezes the newcomer out to protect its ground, while [The Recruit](persona_the_recruit.md), holding [the recruit's seat](position_recruit.md), tries the water and reads the level.

## Stage

[The Gatekeeper](persona_the_gatekeeper.md) tables [Guard the Ground](plan_guard_the_ground.md): chill the new hire, withhold the help, keep the room unchanged, so [the repulsion](piece_the_repulsion.md) protects [the rising salinity](piece_the_rising_salinity.md) rather than diluting it.
[The Recruit](persona_the_recruit.md) tables [Try the Water](plan_try_the_water.md), does the arithmetic the first talent did, and leaves.

## Tension

The cure is repelled by the thing it was meant to cure.
The gatekeeper is not evil, only threatened, defending a position it could not easily win again; the recruit is not weak, only mobile, doing what able people do to a place that will not have them. That is the effect sealing itself: the salt guards the gate the fresh water must come through, so new hiring alone cannot freshen the sea, and each failed infusion teaches the residue it can outlast the new.
