# Process: Settling

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The less mobile remaining and thickening, and the residue defending its ground.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A departure of the able, and those who cannot or will not follow.
The immobile held by ties or want of a door, staying while the water evaporates around them.

## Direction

Toward a residue that concentrates and entrenches.
The stayers become a larger share of a smaller team, the average helplessness rises, and the entrenched, threatened by talent, learn to repel the new that might dilute them.

## Lever

Retention sorted by who cannot leave.
Because the sorting keeps the least mobile, the residue thickens by subtraction, not by any gain, and its growing entrenchment turns from passive settling into active defence of the ground.

## Echo

A team stable, salty, and closed.
The residue concentrated, the salinity self-protecting, and the gate guarded against exactly the fresh talent the team would need to freshen.
