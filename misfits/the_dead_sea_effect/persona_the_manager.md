# Persona: The Manager

## Taxonomy

[Manager](position_manager.md). The one who lets the conditions sour, meaning no harm.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Busy, distracted, sure the team is fine because it is stable.
Lets the pay drift, the work go stale, the recognition lapse, not from malice but from neglect.
Reads low turnover as a sign of health and asks no harder question of it.

## Action

Lets it sour, and mistakes the settling for calm.
Tends other fires while the conditions curdle, notices the able leaving only as ordinary churn, and is quietly relieved that the core seems loyal.
Does nothing in particular, which over a souring team is enough to run the evaporation.

## Shadow

Its neglect is the heat, and its comfort is the symptom.
It meant no harm and did the worst kind, letting a place decay just enough to drive out the best while keeping the rest, and taking the resulting stillness as proof it need not act, so the very calm it trusts is the salinity it failed to read.
Not a tyrant: a neglectful steward who mistook a hollowing team for a steady one.

## Tell

"Turnover's low, the team's stable. We're doing fine."
The comfortable reading that lets the water evaporate unwatched.
