# Position: Rebuilder

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The seat that freshens the sea, against the residue and the risk of cutting wrong.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The sight that the calm is salinity and the will to spend against it.
The levers to cool the heat, pay to keep the able, clear the blockers, shelter the new.
A judgement it cannot make cleanly: which stayer is backbone and which is salt.

## Orders

Freshen the water at the source.
Fix the conditions, retain the able, remove the gatekeepers, protect the recruit.
Tell the loyal from the dead weight, and do not cut them together.

## Loses

Ease and certainty, and risks losing the good water with the salt.
Its cure is slow, dear, and resisted by the residue that holds the ground, and its hardest move, distinguishing the settled from the steady, cannot be made without error, so it may drain the loyal it meant to keep.
Right about the disease, and unable to treat it without wounding.

## Drives

Toward a freshened sea,
against a residue that outlasts reformers,
so the rebuild is a costly, error-prone fight to keep the good and move the settled without confusing the two.
