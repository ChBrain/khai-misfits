# Position: Talent

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The seat with the skill and the options to leave.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Portable ability, and a market that wants it.
An outside: offers, options, somewhere better to go.
No debt to a souring team beyond the work it was paid for.

## Orders

Read the conditions.
Weigh a souring place against a market that will have you.
Take the better offer, and take it early.

## Loses

Nothing for itself, and takes the team's best skill out the door.
Its exit is rational and costless to it, but it removes exactly the ability the team could least spare, and worsens the place for the next able person deciding whether to stay.
Right to go, and the first drop of the evaporation.

## Drives

Toward the outside offer,
which its ability makes available,
so under souring the most able leave first and the water of talent falls.
