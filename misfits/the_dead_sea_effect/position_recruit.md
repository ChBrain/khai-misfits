# Position: Recruit

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The seat brought in to freshen the team, and most able to leave it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Skill, hope, and an outside of its own.
A brief given to it: raise the water, dilute the salt.
No knowledge yet of how salty the sea has grown or who guards the gate.

## Orders

Try the water.
Bring your skill to the team you were hired to freshen.
See what it is really like, then decide.

## Loses

Its illusions fast, and the team its infusion.
Meeting the gatekeeper's freeze and reading the low water, it does the same arithmetic the first talent did, and, being mobile, leaves, so the dilution it was meant to bring never mixes in.
The cure that evaporates fastest, because it is made of the very mobility it was meant to restore.

## Drives

Toward the exit, on arrival,
which its skill and its outside make easy,
so fresh talent poured into a Dead Sea runs off before it can lower the salt.
