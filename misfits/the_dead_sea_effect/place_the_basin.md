# Place: The Basin

## Taxonomy

[The Dead Sea Effect](play_the_dead_sea_effect.md). The team itself, the landlocked sea where the water sours and the salt collects.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A team at its desks, headcount steady, the surface calm.
Conditions curdling slowly underneath, and the composition shifting quarter by quarter toward those who stay.

## Holds

The heat and the residue together.
Here the souring raises the temperature that drives the able out, and here the less mobile settle and thicken, so the basin holds both the cause of the evaporation and the sediment it leaves.

## Offers

Stability, and the comfort of a steady core.
The basin looks calm and fully staffed, so it offers the reassurance of a team that endures, low turnover worn as a badge of health.

## Withheld

Any reading of the salinity.
The basin shows headcount and calm, not the level of talent within it, so a sea losing its fresh water and keeping its salt looks identical to a healthy one, and it withholds the very measure that would tell them apart.
