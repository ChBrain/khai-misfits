# Plot: The Closed Door

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Impasse](place_the_impasse.md), an outsider seeking a way in and an accountable answer.
A member outside the network, feeling a wall they cannot point to, asking who decides and how one joins the deciding.

## Action

[Excluding](process_excluding.md): the search for a formal route and a responsible party finds neither, and the call to formalise is refused for the openness it would cost.

## Stage

[The Outsider](persona_the_outsider.md), on [Name the Structure](plan_name_the_structure.md), demands the offices and votes that would give a door and an account, and runs into [the hidden hierarchy](piece_the_hidden_hierarchy.md), met with the reply that no one is in charge and the floor is open to all.
The question is fair and the wall is real, and the remedy that would answer it is refused as a betrayal of the ideal.

## Tension

Asking for a door and an accountable answer is the reasonable request, and the reasonable request is refused because granting it would rebuild the hierarchy the group fled.
There is no villain to name: the group sincerely believes its floor open, so the Outsider's exclusion sounds like sour grapes, and the informal power, denied, offers no one to hold. But the only remedy is to formalise, to build the offices and rules the group formed to escape, so the plot closes with the shut-out left no door and no lever, and the cure declined for the openness it would spend, the reckoning a structure named and answerable at the cost of the ideal, or the ideal kept at the cost of a power no one can reach.
