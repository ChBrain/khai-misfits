# Position: The Sidelines

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The seat outside the informal centre, with no formal route in and no channel of appeal, kept out by a structure that denies existing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A place in the group, and no way to the middle of it.
A nominal membership: the seat is inside the group by the open floor's own promise, yet outside the network where the deciding happens, holding the right to attend and none of the access that matters.
No entry point, since there is no office to stand for and no vote to win, and no one to appeal to, since the group insists no one is in charge.

## Orders

Name the structure.
Ask who decides and how one joins the deciding, and press for the offices, rules, and votes that would give a formal way in and an accountable answer.
Make the wall visible, since the seat's only hope of a lever is to get the group to admit that a structure exists at all.

## Loses

Every instrument at once, because the structure it would use is the one denied.
The Sidelines has no office to contest, no vote to cast, no leader to petition, and no standing to name its own exclusion, since the group's belief in its openness makes the wall unspeakable, so the seat is barred by a hierarchy that answers each objection with the truth that it does not exist.
What the seat forfeits is the very ground to complain from: unable to point to the structure that shuts it out, it cannot demand entry to a thing no one admits is there, so the seat kept furthest from power is the one with the least means to reach it, its exclusion real and, to the group, invisible.

## Drives

Toward a question that cannot be answered,
and a wall that cannot be named,
so the seat outside the ring is the one whose exclusion the group's own openness renders both total and deniable.
