# Piece: The Disclaimer

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The denial of leadership, the sincere and true claim that no one is in charge, which armours the ones who are.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Spoken at [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md), where the group disowns its leaders, and wielded at [the impasse](place_the_impasse.md), where it closes the case against the questioner.
Flows from the group's real belief in its own leaderlessness, a disavowal held in good faith and, because the group has no formal offices, entirely true.

## Load Bearing

Without the Disclaimer the informal power would still be informal, but it could at least be pointed at and named.
With it the power becomes untouchable: the Disclaimer is the group's true claim that no one leads (Freeman), which turns the absence of a formal office into a shield around the informal one, since a leader who holds no post can honestly deny leading. The Disclaimer is load bearing because it is what converts unequal influence into unaccountable power, the honest denial that leaves nothing false to expose and no handle to grip, so the piece carries the armour of the trap, the sincere disavowal that makes the hidden hierarchy not merely hidden but, on its own terms, deniable and therefore beyond reach.

## Apparent

Just the truth, the plain and honest fact that the group has no leaders.
Seen at the surface, the Disclaimer looks like candour, a group correctly stating that it holds no offices, and the truth that this candour is the armour, that a true denial shields power better than a false one, that saying no one is in charge is exactly what keeps the ones in charge from account, stays hidden behind how obviously honest the statement is.

## Yearbook

Spoken sincerely by [the Insider](persona_the_insider.md), believed by [the Idealist](persona_the_idealist.md), and used to turn away [the Outsider](persona_the_outsider.md), the true denial that armours the trap.
It is the armour of the trap: the Disclaimer is what [denying](process_denying.md) makes of the group's honest belief, the shield over [the sway](piece_the_sway.md) that meets every call to account with a truth, keeping [the hidden hierarchy](piece_the_hidden_hierarchy.md) unnameable and so unreachable.
