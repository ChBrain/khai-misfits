# Plan: Keep It Informal

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Inner Ring](position_the_inner_ring.md), inside [No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Inner Ring](position_the_inner_ring.md)

## Direction

Settle the real questions among the people who are trusted and present, in the friendships and side conversations the meeting cannot see, since that is where the work already flows. Speak up in the open and decide in the quiet, and never seek a title, because a title would make the power visible and accountable and the informal channel is the one that works. Let the sway gather by presence and friendship, granted by no vote and owed to no one.

## Orders

([The Insider](persona_the_insider.md) makes the real decisions among friends out of the meeting's sight, holding the informal power without ever taking an office that could be named.)

## Implementation

([The Kitchen](place_the_kitchen.md); the [forming](process_forming.md) process; the Insider gathers [the sway](piece_the_sway.md) by friendship and presence, deciding off the record and leaving the group's open floor a formality.)

## Targets

- [ ] the real decisions are settled among the trusted and available
- [ ] the deciding happens where the open meeting cannot follow
- [ ] no title is sought, so the power stays informal and unseen
- [ ] the sway gathers by friendship and presence, not by any vote
