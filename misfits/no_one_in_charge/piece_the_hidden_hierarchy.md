# Piece: The Hidden Hierarchy

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The phenomenon whole, the informal, unaccountable structure that forms when the formal kind is refused and cannot be reached because it is denied.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md), [the kitchen](place_the_kitchen.md), and [the impasse](place_the_impasse.md) as the whole structure, the floor open, the deciding hidden, and the account refused.
Flows through the entire play, from the sincere refusal of formal power to the unreachable informal power it installs, the trap seen whole.

## Load Bearing

Without it there are only parts: an openness, a sway, a disclaimer, with no name for the structure they compose.
With it the parts become one order: a group refuses formal structure to protect its openness, an informal power forms in the vacuum, and the sincere denial of leadership shields it from account. The Hidden Hierarchy is load bearing because it is the phenomenon entire (Freeman; Polletta): the tyranny of structurelessness, the self-selected elite that forms with no design and is harder to check than any named hierarchy precisely because the group denies it exists, recurring wherever openness is prized enough to refuse structure. So the piece carries the whole trap, the passage from an equal floor to an unaccountable elite, a hierarchy no one built and no one can dislodge without rebuilding the structure the group was formed to escape.

## Apparent

Just an open group, a collective of equals with no one giving orders.
Seen at the surface, the Hidden Hierarchy looks like its own opposite, a leaderless group living its ideal, and the truth that the absence of formal power is the presence of informal power, that the group is steered by an elite it cannot see, that the openness on show is the cover of a hierarchy, stays hidden behind how sincerely the group believes itself flat.

## Yearbook

Built unknowingly by [the Idealist](persona_the_idealist.md), inhabited by [the Insider](persona_the_insider.md), and run into by [the Outsider](persona_the_outsider.md), the whole order no one designed.
It is the whole of it: the Hidden Hierarchy is [refusing](process_refusing.md), [forming](process_forming.md), [denying](process_denying.md), and [excluding](process_excluding.md) run as one, [the openness](piece_the_openness.md) opening the ground for [the sway](piece_the_sway.md) that [the disclaimer](piece_the_disclaimer.md) armours, an unaccountable structure standing where the group swore none would.
