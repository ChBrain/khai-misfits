# Place: The Meeting

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The open assembly, where the group gathers with no chair and no agenda and the equality is on display, the scene of structurelessness in action.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A room with no head of the table, everyone free to speak, no one calling the order, decisions reached by feel.
Shown as equality made visible, the open floor working exactly as intended, proof that a group can run without bosses.

## Holds

Holds the appearance of the open floor, and the form the real deciding has already left.
The Meeting is where the group performs its leaderlessness: talk circulates and no one presides, so the scene looks like the whole of the group's life, while the questions that matter have been or will be settled elsewhere, the meeting ratifying more than it resolves.

## Offers

Offers each member the floor, and the sense that the floor is where the power is.
Here anyone may speak and no office silences them, so the Meeting offers the felt experience of an equal group, and the belief that because the visible room is open, the group is open.

## Withheld

Withholds the fact that the room is not where it happens.
What the Meeting hides is that an open floor is not an open group: the absence of a chair does not distribute the power, it only moves it off the record, so the equality on show at the meeting coexists with a deciding it cannot see, in rooms the floor was never told about.
