# Position: The Inner Ring

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The informal centre, an unofficial seat with real function and no formal existence, where the decisions actually settle.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A network, and no name on any door.
An unwritten centrality: the seat is where the trusted and the available converge, so the real deciding happens here, in the friendships and side conversations the meeting cannot see, without any title conferring it.
No office to point to, since the seat exists only informally, which is precisely what lets it act without being seen to.

## Orders

Keep it informal.
Settle the real questions among the people who are trusted and present, out of the meeting's sight, because that is where the work already flows and no formal channel competes with it.
Never seek a title, since a title would make the seat visible and accountable, and the seat's power depends on remaining unofficial.

## Loses

Its own legitimacy, which an unofficial seat can never claim.
The Inner Ring can act but cannot be acknowledged: it holds no mandate, stands for nothing anyone endorsed, and can never openly defend a decision as its own, so its power buys unaccountability at the price of standing.
What the seat forfeits is the right to lead in the open: able to decide only by staying deniable, it can never be a legitimate authority the group has chosen and could defend, so the seat that holds the real power is the one that can never own it, ruling only so long as it is never seen to rule.

## Drives

Toward decisions made off the record,
and an authority that must never admit itself,
so the seat that gathers the real power is the one condemned to hold it deniably, unaccountable and illegitimate at once.
