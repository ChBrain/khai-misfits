# Process: Forming

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The coalescence of the informal structure, an elite gathering from friendship, confidence, time, and presence where the formal kind was refused.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Insider](persona_the_insider.md), at [the Kitchen](place_the_kitchen.md), settling the real questions among the trusted and present.
Begins as soon as the group must actually decide and act, since work needs a channel, and with no formal one the informal one forms of its own accord.

## Direction

Turns a vacuum into a structure no one designed.
A group that cooperates develops norms, status, and roles whether it means to or not, so the deciding flows to the trusted, the articulate, and the available, and an elite coalesces from who knows whom, self-selected and unchosen, filling the space the refusal left with a structure as real as any office and none of the marks of one.

## Lever

The certainty that people who work together will structure themselves.
Because no group is truly structureless, forming needs no organiser and no plan, only cooperation over time, so its lever is the plain sociology of human groups, which produces a hierarchy of trust and access wherever the formal kind is absent.

## Echo

An informal elite that holds the real power and wears no title.
The echo of forming is a group steered by a small, familiar circle it never elected, so the sway sits with the well-placed and the deciding leaves the open floor, an unwritten structure standing exactly where the group swore none would.
