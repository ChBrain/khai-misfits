# Process: Denying

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The disavowal of leadership, the group's sincere claim that no one is in charge, which shields the informal power from account.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Insider](persona_the_insider.md), at [the Meeting](place_the_meeting.md), answering, honestly, that they hold no office and lead no one.
Begins the moment the informal power is questioned, since the group and the elite alike believe the group leaderless and say so in good faith.

## Direction

Turns a true statement into an impenetrable armour.
The group has no formal leaders, so the claim that no one is in charge is honest, but that same honesty makes the informal power unnameable: a leader who holds no office can truthfully deny leading, so denying does not lie about the power, it removes the handle by which the power could be gripped, converting a fact about form into a shield around substance.

## Lever

The truth of the disclaimer.
Because the ones who lead genuinely hold no title, denying costs no deceit, so its lever is sincerity itself: the group can disown its power in perfect good faith, and a denial that is true is far harder to break than a lie, since there is nothing false in it to expose.

## Echo

A power that cannot be called power, and so cannot be checked.
The echo of denying is an elite armoured by the group's own conviction, its sway real and its title absent, so every attempt to hold it to account meets the honest reply that there is nothing to hold, the denial the safer for being sincerely and correctly made.
