# Process: Excluding

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The shutting-out of those with no way into the informal elite, by a structure that offers no door and denies existing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The informality of the door itself, at [the Impasse](place_the_impasse.md), admitting only those already inside.
Begins when someone outside the network seeks entry or an accountable answer and finds neither, the shutting-out done by the structure and merely met by [the Outsider](persona_the_outsider.md) who runs into it.

## Direction

Turns an open floor into a closed room with no handle.
Because the informal elite is entered by friendship and history rather than by any rule, there is no office to stand for, no vote to win, and no leader to petition, so the shut-out cannot earn their way in, unseat anyone, or even name their exclusion, the structure barring them being one the group denies exists.

## Lever

The informality of the door.
Because access runs through relationships, not procedures, excluding needs no gatekeeper and no rejection, only the absence of any formal way in, so its lever is the very formlessness that was meant to be open, which turns out to admit only those already inside.

## Echo

An exclusion that is total and deniable at once.
The echo of excluding is a member kept from the real deciding by a wall no one will admit is there, so their complaint sounds like sour grapes and their exclusion leaves no mark, the openness that promised entry to all delivering it only to the connected, and denying the wall to everyone else.
