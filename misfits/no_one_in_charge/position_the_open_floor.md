# Position: The Open Floor

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The leaderless assembly, a seat that has abolished its own offices and rules and kept only the declaration that everyone is equal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A declaration of equality, and no instrument to enforce it.
A cleared ground: the seat has thrown out chairs, offices, votes, and rules in the name of openness, so it holds nothing but the standing claim that no one leads and the floor is open to all.
No procedure of any kind, since the seat abolished its procedures on purpose, and what it kept is a principle, not a mechanism.

## Orders

Refuse the structure.
Keep the floor open and the group leaderless, admitting no office that anyone could occupy and no rule that anyone could wield, because structure is the thing that crushed the group before.
Trust the declaration to do the work, since the seat has resolved that where there is no named leader there can be no boss.

## Loses

The means to see or govern the power it disowns.
Having abolished every office, vote, and rule, the Open Floor keeps no instrument for locating who decides, checking them, or removing them, so when power forms it forms beyond the reach of a seat that gave away all its levers.
What the seat forfeits is not equality's aim but its tools: a floor that kept only a principle cannot act on the informal power the principle failed to prevent, so the seat that threw out its structure to stop domination is the one with nothing left to stop it with.

## Drives

Toward a floor open in name,
and a power it can neither see nor reach,
so the seat that abolished its own instruments is the one that cannot govern what fills the space they left.
