# Plot: The Kitchen Cabinet

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Kitchen](place_the_kitchen.md), the work to be done and no formal channel to do it through.
A few friends who know each other well, trusted and available, talking things over after the meeting.

## Action

[Forming](process_forming.md): the informal structure coalesces, the real decisions settling among the connected where the open floor cannot follow.

## Stage

[The Insider](persona_the_insider.md), on [Keep It Informal](plan_keep_it_informal.md), gathers [the sway](piece_the_sway.md) by friendship and presence, deciding off the record and never taking a title that could be named.
The decisions are sound and the friendship is genuine, and the group is steered from a room the open floor was never told about.

## Tension

Getting the work done through the people you trust is the natural act, and the natural act installs an unelected elite.
There is no scheme: the Insider only speaks up and shows up, and with no formal channel the informal one they are at the centre of becomes the only one that works, so nothing here is a power grab. But cooperation without structure flows to the well-placed, so the plot closes with the real deciding settled among friends and the sway gathered to a small circle, an unwritten hierarchy standing exactly where the group swore none would, granted by no vote and owed to no one.
