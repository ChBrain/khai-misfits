# Persona: The Insider

## Taxonomy

[The Inner Ring](position_the_inner_ring.md). Holds the informal power that fills the vacuum, gathered by friendship and presence, and can truthfully deny holding any office at all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Connected. Present. Fluent in the room.
Is the one who knows everyone, speaks well, has the time, and was there at the start, so decisions flow through them not by appointment but by gravity, the natural centre of an unwritten network.
No scheming wished on them: they seek no office and pull no strings, only show up, speak up, and stay in touch, and the power accrues to them the way water finds a low place.

## Action

Speaks up in the meeting and settles things outside it, making the real decisions among friends where the group's open floor cannot reach.
Answers the ordinary pulls of friendship and competence, so the work gets done through the people who are trusted and available, which happens to be a small and familiar circle, and the sway gathers there without anyone granting it.
Does what a well-placed person does: uses the relationships they have to move things along, and because there is no formal channel, the informal one they are at the centre of becomes the only one that works.

## Shadow

Not a schemer, but the one whose real power is protected by the very fact that it is unofficial, and that is the quiet of it: they hold no office, so they can hold no blame, and the sway is theirs precisely because no one voted it to them.
Because the Insider leads without a title, they cannot be voted out, questioned as a leader, or held to a leader's account, and when asked they can say in perfect honesty that they are not in charge, so the informal power is not only real but armoured, safer than any office because it can be truthfully denied.
The influence is genuine and the deniability is genuine, so the one who leads the group is the one who can least be called its leader, unaccountable not despite holding no office but because of it.

## Tell

Says, and means it, "I'm not in charge here; I'm just one of us, same as anyone."
Is telling the truth, since they hold no post and claim no authority, and cannot see that the truth of it is the armour, that leading without a title is leading no one can reach, so the honest disclaimer that clears their name is what keeps their power beyond question.
