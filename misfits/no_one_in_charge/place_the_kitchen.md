# Place: The Kitchen

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The informal room off the meeting, the kitchen and the phone call and the friendship, where the real decisions actually settle.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A few friends talking after the meeting, over coffee, on the walk home, nothing that looks like governance at all.
Shown as ordinary sociability, people who know each other well sorting things out, so the deciding here reads as friendship rather than power.

## Holds

Holds the real deciding, done among the trusted and present where no floor can follow.
The Kitchen is where the sway lives: the questions the open meeting seemed to leave hanging are resolved here, by the people at the centre of the network, so the informal room, not the formal one, is where the group is actually steered.

## Offers

Offers the insiders a channel that works, and the comfort that it is only friends talking.
Here the well-connected can move things along without procedure or delay, and the setting itself, a kitchen and not a chamber, offers everyone the reassurance that nothing so informal could really be power.

## Withheld

Withholds its own doorway, which opens only to those already inside.
What the Kitchen hides is that it has no entrance a stranger can use: one arrives here by friendship and history, not by any rule or vote, so the room where the group is really run is closed to anyone not already in it, and its closedness looks, from outside, like nothing at all.
