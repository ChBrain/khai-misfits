# Piece: The Openness

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The ideal the refusal means to protect, the equal, unstructured floor a group forms against hierarchy to keep.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Declared at [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md), where the group shows its equality, and defended against [the impasse](place_the_impasse.md), where the remedy would cost it.
Flows from a real grievance against hierarchy, the value the whole group is built to hold, sincere and cherished and the thing any formalising would spend.

## Load Bearing

Without the Openness there is no misfit, only a group that never tried: it is the good the refusal serves and the good the remedy sacrifices.
With it the trap has both its motive and its cost: the Openness is the equal, unstructured floor the group prizes (Freeman; Homans), a genuine ideal that formal hierarchy has so often crushed, held so dear that the group will not build the offices that would check its power. The Openness is load bearing because it is why the structure is refused and why it cannot be restored, the sincere value that opens the vacuum and then guards it, so the piece carries the innocent heart of the trap, the good intention that makes the informal power both possible and, once formed, unnameable without betraying the ideal.

## Apparent

Just an ideal, the decent wish for a group where no one lords it over anyone.
Seen at the surface, the Openness looks like the whole answer to hierarchy, an equality secured by refusing structure, and the truth that a floor declared open is not an open floor, that the ideal cannot be had by refusal alone, that the value protected is the value that shields the hidden power, stays hidden behind how plainly good the wish itself is.

## Yearbook

Held by [the Idealist](persona_the_idealist.md), invoked against [the Outsider](persona_the_outsider.md)'s remedy, and quietly served by [the Insider](persona_the_insider.md) whom it shelters, the cherished good the whole trap turns on.
It is the innocent heart of the trap: the Openness is what [refusing](process_refusing.md) means to protect, the ideal that opens the ground for [the sway](piece_the_sway.md) and, guarded by [the disclaimer](piece_the_disclaimer.md), keeps [the hidden hierarchy](piece_the_hidden_hierarchy.md) beyond the reach of any formalising that would spend it.
