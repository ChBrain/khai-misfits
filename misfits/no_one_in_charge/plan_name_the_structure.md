# Plan: Name the Structure

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Sidelines](position_the_sidelines.md), inside [No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Sidelines](position_the_sidelines.md)

## Direction

Ask who decides and how one joins the deciding, and press for the offices, rules, and votes that would give a formal way in and an accountable answer. Make the informal wall visible, since the only hope of a lever is to get the group to admit that a structure exists at all. Push to formalise, knowing it asks the group to rebuild the very thing it was formed to refuse.

## Orders

([The Outsider](persona_the_outsider.md) demands to know who is in charge and calls for a formal, accountable structure, and is met with the reply that no one leads.)

## Implementation

([The Impasse](place_the_impasse.md); against the [excluding](process_excluding.md) process; the Outsider would replace [the disclaimer](piece_the_disclaimer.md) with a named structure, breaking [the hidden hierarchy](piece_the_hidden_hierarchy.md) only by sacrificing [the openness](piece_the_openness.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the question of who decides is put to the group directly
- [ ] a formal, accountable structure is proposed in place of the informal one
- [ ] the informal wall is named so it can be argued with at all
- [ ] the remedy is weighed as a rebuilt hierarchy, the cost of the openness
