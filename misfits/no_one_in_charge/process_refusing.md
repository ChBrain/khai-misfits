# Process: Refusing

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The founding move, rejecting formal structure to protect the group's openness, clearing the ground the informal power will fill.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Idealist](persona_the_idealist.md), at [the Meeting](place_the_meeting.md), tearing out the offices and rules in the name of equality.
Begins whenever a group forms against hierarchy and resolves to have none, refusing the chairs, votes, and rules it associates with domination.

## Direction

Turns a reaction against hierarchy into a vacuum where power can pool.
The group abolishes its formal structure to keep anyone from lording it over the rest, but removing the offices removes only the visible power, so refusing does not empty the group of authority, it empties it of the instruments that would name and check authority, leaving a space rather than an equality.

## Lever

The equation of formal structure with power itself.
Because the group believes that tearing out the offices tears out the domination, refusing feels like liberation, so its lever is the sincere and mistaken conviction that where there is no named leader there can be no leader, which licenses the removal of the very tools that would keep the informal one in view.

## Echo

An open floor, and a space that will not stay empty.
The echo of refusing is a group that has cleared away its structure and believes itself equal, so the ground is prepared for an informal power to form unseen, the refusal that meant to prevent domination having removed the only means of noticing it.
