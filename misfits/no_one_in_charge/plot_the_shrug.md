# Plot: The Shrug

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Meeting](place_the_meeting.md), a question put to the room: who decided this?
An informal power that has steered the group, and a group that holds, sincerely, that it has no leaders.

## Action

[Denying](process_denying.md): the group and the elite answer, honestly, that no one is in charge, and the true denial closes over the real power.

## Stage

[The Insider](persona_the_insider.md), holding [the sway](piece_the_sway.md), says in perfect honesty that they hold no office and lead no one, so [the disclaimer](piece_the_disclaimer.md) turns the absence of a title into a shield around the influence.
The denial is true and it is total, the leader who holds no post able to disown leading with nothing false to expose.

## Tension

Telling the truth that you hold no office is the honest answer, and the honest answer is the armour the power hides behind.
There is no lie: the Insider genuinely has no title, the group genuinely believes itself leaderless, and the disclaimer is candour, not cover story. But a leader who can truthfully deny leading cannot be named, questioned, or removed, so the plot closes with the real power disowned in good faith and, by that disowning, placed beyond account, the sway the safer for a denial that is sincere and correct and impossible to break.
