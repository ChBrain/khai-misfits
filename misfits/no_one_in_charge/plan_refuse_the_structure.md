# Plan: Refuse the Structure

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Open Floor](position_the_open_floor.md), inside [No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Open Floor](position_the_open_floor.md)

## Direction

Form the group against hierarchy and keep it leaderless: no chairs, no offices, no rules, so the floor stays open and no one gives orders. Aim the refusal at the visible power that has crushed people before, and trust that where there is no named leader there can be no boss. Keep only the declaration of equality, since structure itself is taken to be the danger.

## Orders

([The Idealist](persona_the_idealist.md) tears out the offices and rules and keeps the group open and leaderless, believing the absence of a named leader to be the absence of a leader.)

## Implementation

([The Meeting](place_the_meeting.md); the [refusing](process_refusing.md) process; the Idealist refuses formal structure to protect [the openness](piece_the_openness.md), clearing the ground the informal [sway](piece_the_sway.md) will fill.)

## Targets

- [ ] the group is formed against hierarchy and resolves to have no leaders
- [ ] offices, votes, and rules are refused to keep the floor open
- [ ] the openness is held as the ideal the refusal means to protect
- [ ] the absence of a named leader is trusted as the absence of a leader
