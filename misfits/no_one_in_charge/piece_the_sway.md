# Piece: The Sway

## Taxonomy

[No One in Charge](play_no_one_in_charge.md). The informal power that fills the vacuum, the real influence over decisions gathered by friendship and presence rather than by any vote.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Gathers at [the kitchen](place_the_kitchen.md), where the real deciding happens, and stays invisible at [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md), where the open floor performs.
Flows to the trusted, the articulate, and the available, the actual steering of the group as it settles among those at the centre of the network.

## Load Bearing

Without the Sway the refusal would leave a real emptiness, and the group would simply fail to act.
With it the vacuum is filled: the Sway is the informal influence that forms where formal power was refused (Mansbridge), the deciding that flows to the well-placed because the work must go somewhere and there is no other channel. The Sway is load bearing because it is the power the group swore it had abolished, real and unequal and gathered by no election, so the piece carries the substance of the trap, the authority that is none the less authority for having no name, no office, and no vote behind it.

## Apparent

Just friends helping, the natural way things get done among people who know each other.
Seen at the surface, the Sway looks like sociability, competent people pitching in and coordinating, and the truth that it is power, that a small circle steers the group without a mandate, that friendship has become a channel of authority no one granted, stays hidden behind how ordinary and how helpful it all appears.

## Yearbook

Held by [the Insider](persona_the_insider.md), unreachable to [the Outsider](persona_the_outsider.md), and unseen by [the Idealist](persona_the_idealist.md) who abolished the offices but not the influence, the real power the trap runs on.
It is the substance of the trap: the Sway is what [forming](process_forming.md) gathers into the informal elite, the influence [the disclaimer](piece_the_disclaimer.md) shields and [excluding](process_excluding.md) keeps to the connected, the power at the centre of [the hidden hierarchy](piece_the_hidden_hierarchy.md).
