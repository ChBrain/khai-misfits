# Position: The Ledger

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The seat that anchors on what the thing is worth and trades against the crowd, and bears the divergence the game runs against it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A valuation and the capital to back it: the judgement of what the thing will earn and what it is, and the position it takes against a price adrift from that.
A clear case: pricing the thing correctly and backing it is exactly the work a market is meant to reward.
No way to make the crowd agree on cue, and only capital enough to hold the position for so long.

## Orders

Weigh the thing.
Judge the fundamental, find the price adrift from it, sell the swell and buy the wreck, and hold for value to out.
Back the sound valuation with capital, and wait for the price to come back to worth.

## Loses

Its footing, not to any error in the valuation but to the game's indifference to it.
Because arbitrage against a self-referential price is risky and capital-constrained, the seat that anchors on worth can be carried out on the tide before the price corrects, margin called and conviction spent while the mispricing runs on, so being right about the thing is disciplined by no fundamental that reasserts on cue.
It holds the anchor the market needs, and can be drowned holding it, right about the thing and ruined before the worth is proved.

## Drives

Toward value backed and the price called wrong,
against a crowd that need not agree before the capital runs out,
so the seat that does the market's own work of pricing the thing can be broken for being right too early.
