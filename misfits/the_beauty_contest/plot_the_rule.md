# Plot: The Rule

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Floor](place_the_floor.md), the payoff set for whoever matches the crowd.
A market where the prize goes to the one who anticipates the room, not to the one who judges the thing rightly, and a player reading which game is being played.

## Action

[Guessing](process_guessing.md): the player sets the fundamental aside and turns to the only question the payoff rewards, what the room will do.

## Stage

[The Speculator](persona_the_speculator.md), on [Guess the Room](plan_guess_the_room.md), reads [the prize](piece_the_prize.md) on matching and lets go of [the fundamental](piece_the_fundamental.md), because valuing the thing correctly against the crowd earns nothing while guessing the crowd earns everything.
The thing's worth is not misjudged, only set aside, the wrong question for the game being played.

## Tension

Setting the fundamental aside is the sound move, and it is the move that starts the detachment.
There is nothing foolish: when the prize is matching the crowd, guessing the crowd is exactly right and holding to value exactly wrong. But the question the payoff rewards is not what the thing is worth, so the plot closes with the fundamental abandoned by the very players best able to judge it, the market turned from pricing the thing to pricing the room, worth set down and not yet missed.
