# Plot: The Reckoning

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Long View](place_the_long_view.md), someone who would tie the price back to value.
A valuer with a sound reckoning of worth, a price adrift from it, and the capital to take a position against the crowd.

## Action

[Anchoring](process_anchoring.md): the valuer holds to the fundamental and trades against the self-referential price, betting that value will out.

## Stage

[The Valuer](persona_the_valuer.md), on [Weigh the Thing](plan_weigh_the_thing.md), sells the swell and buys the wreck, anchoring on [the fundamental](piece_the_fundamental.md) against the whole self-referential [contest](piece_the_contest.md), the one discipline the game admits.
The valuation is right and the position sound, and the correction is owed on no schedule.

## Tension

The one move that could discipline the price is the one the game can defeat.
The valuer is not wrong about the thing, only about the game, whose prize is agreement and not accuracy: arbitrage against a self-referential price is risky and capital-constrained, so the plot closes with the price tied back to value and the anchor holding ... or the margin called, the conviction spent, and the valuer carried off on the tide before the correction comes, right about the thing and ruined being right too early, the market pricing opinion about opinion for as long as the game runs.
