# Persona: The Valuer

## Taxonomy

[The Ledger](position_the_ledger.md). Anchors on what the thing is worth and trades against the crowd, and bears the divergence the game runs against them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Patient. Disciplined. Anchored on worth.
Is the one who does the work the market is meant to do, judges the thing by what it will earn and what it is, and buys below that and waits.
No error in it: the valuation is sound and the thing is worth what they say it is worth.

## Action

Weighs the fundamental, finds the price adrift from it, and takes the position that value will out: sells the swell, buys the wreck, and holds.
Trades against the crowd on the strength of being right about the thing, expecting the price to come back to worth.
Does what a market is supposed to reward: prices the thing correctly and backs the judgement with capital.

## Shadow

Not wrong about the thing, wrong about the game, and that is the whole difficulty: the prize was never accuracy but agreement, so being right about the fundamental earns nothing while the crowd disagrees.
Because arbitrage against a self-referential price is risky and capital-constrained, the Valuer who anchors on worth can be carried out on the tide before the price corrects, margin called and conviction spent while the mispricing runs on, so the one move that would tie price back to value is a bet that goes broke being right too early, disciplined by no fundamental that reasserts on cue.
It holds the anchor the market needs, and the market can drown the one who holds it before the worth is proved.

## Tell

Says "the price is wrong, and the price will come back to what the thing is worth."
Is right about the thing and may be ruined by it, unable to make the crowd agree before the capital to wait runs out.
