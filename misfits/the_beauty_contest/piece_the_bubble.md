# Piece: The Bubble

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The self-fulfilling price that rises because everyone expects everyone to keep buying, the tower's height made a feedback loop.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Inflates at [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the price rising on the expectation of its own rise.
Flows upward while the expectation holds and collapses when it turns, carried by every buyer who expects the next.

## Load Bearing

Without it the regress would be a mispricing that sat still: opinion about opinion, but not a loop that feeds itself.
With it the price acquires its own momentum: because everyone expects everyone to keep buying, the buying comes, the rise confirms the expectation, and the confirmed expectation draws the next buyer, a feedback loop that inflates the price free of any worth beneath it for as long as the expectation holds. The Bubble is load bearing because it is where opinion about opinion becomes a self-fulfilling price (Shiller; Allen, Morris & Shin): the swell that rises on the belief in its rising, so the piece carries the reason the detached price does not merely sit wrong but climbs, momentum standing in for value.

## Apparent

A market discovering value, a price going up because the thing is good and getting better, the trend that proves the story.
Seen at the surface, the Bubble looks like vindication, the rise itself the evidence that the buyers were right, and the truth that the price rises because everyone expects it to rise and not because the thing is worth more, that the trend is the crowd pricing its own momentum, that the loop inflates and collapses on expectation alone, stays hidden behind how convincingly a rising price reads as value found.

## Yearbook

Inflated by [the Crowd](persona_the_crowd.md) through [reflecting](process_reflecting.md), the self-fulfilling swell of the game.
