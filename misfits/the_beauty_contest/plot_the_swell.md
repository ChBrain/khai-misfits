# Plot: The Swell

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Floor](place_the_floor.md), the self-referential price starting to rise.
A market in which everyone expects everyone to keep buying, and the expectation is about to act on itself.

## Action

[Swelling](process_swelling.md): the expectation of a rise draws the buying that makes the rise, and the rise draws the next buyer, a feedback loop closing on itself.

## Stage

[The Crowd](persona_the_crowd.md), moving as it expects itself to move, inflates [the bubble](piece_the_bubble.md): the rise confirms the expectation, the confirmed expectation draws the next buyer, and the price climbs free of any worth beneath it for as long as the belief holds.
Each buyer is right that the price is rising, and right that others expect it to rise, so each buys and each is confirmed.

## Tension

The rise is real and self-made, true while it lasts and made of nothing but the expectation of itself.
There is nothing false in the moment: the price genuinely is going up, and the one who bought genuinely gained. But it rises because everyone expects everyone to keep buying and not because the thing is worth more, so the plot closes with the bubble standing, the market pricing its own momentum and reading the trend as value found, a swell that will hold exactly as long as the expectation does.
