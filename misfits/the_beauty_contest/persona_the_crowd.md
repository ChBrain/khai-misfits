# Persona: The Crowd

## Taxonomy

[The Field](position_the_field.md). The average opinion the prize is matched against, made of players each guessing the crowd, so it is both the target and a mirror of the one who reads it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Many. Faceless. The room itself.
Is the average opinion the game is played toward, the aggregate every player tries to match, and it is not a mob but a thousand Speculators, each also reading the room.
No single mind in it: only the sum of players each guessing what the sum will do.

## Action

Moves as its members move, and its members move by guessing how it will move, so it forecasts itself.
Bids up what it expects itself to bid up, and its expectation, acted on, becomes the fact it expected, the average opinion made of expectations of the average opinion.
Does what no one in it intends: sets the target that every one of them is aiming at, a mirror each reads and each helps write.

## Shadow

Not a fool and not a herd driven, a mirror, and that is the whole difficulty: the Crowd is the very thing each player is trying to match, and it is made of those players, so the target is a reflection of the aim.
Because each member prices by guessing the Crowd and the Crowd is the sum of those guesses, there is no outside fact the room converges on, only the room converging on its own expectation, so the average opinion the prize rewards matching is itself opinion about opinion, and the thing's worth enters nowhere in the loop.
It is the room everyone reads, and the room is only everyone reading it.

## Tell

Says nothing in one voice, and yet every player claims to hear it say buy, or say sell.
Is heard by each as a signal from outside, and is in truth each of them, guessing the others, reflected back as a verdict none of them alone gave.
