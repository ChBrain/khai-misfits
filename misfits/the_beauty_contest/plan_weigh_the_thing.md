## Direction

Do the work the market is meant to do: judge the thing by what it will earn and what it is, find the price adrift from that, sell the swell and buy the wreck, and hold for value to out. Back the sound valuation with capital, and wait for the price to come back to worth, trading against the crowd on the strength of being right about the thing.
