# Piece: The Fundamental

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The thing's real worth, what it will earn and what it is, set aside by the players and held only by the one the game runs against.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Judged at [the long view](place_the_long_view.md), apart from the tape, and set aside on [the floor](place_the_floor.md).
Flows to the Valuer, who holds it, and away from the Speculators, who let it go.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing for the price to float free of: no worth, no divergence, no anchor to be carried off.
With it the trap has a measure and a casualty: the fundamental is what a market is meant to price and what the payoff on matching makes worthless to hold to, so it is the value the Speculators rightly abandon and the anchor the Valuer rightly keeps and is drowned keeping. The Fundamental is load bearing because it is both the thing the price detaches from and the discipline that cannot enforce it (Keynes; Shleifer & Vishny): the worth that drops out of the game and the one that could tie it back, so the piece carries the divergence and its cost at once.

## Apparent

The obvious point of the whole exercise, what the thing is actually worth, the number every valuation is trying to find.
Seen at the surface, the Fundamental looks like the thing the market exists to discover, and the truth that the payoff has made holding to it a losing play, that the price can float free of it for as long as the game runs, that the one who prices it correctly can be ruined before the price agrees, stays hidden behind how plainly the thing does have a worth.
