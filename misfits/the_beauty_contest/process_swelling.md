# Process: Swelling

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The regress turned into a feedback loop, the price rising because everyone expects everyone to keep buying and the rise drawing the next buyer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Crowd, with a price already adrift from worth and rising on the expectation of its own rise.
At [the floor](place_the_floor.md), everyone expecting everyone to keep buying, the expectation about to act on itself and make itself true.

## Direction

Toward a self-fulfilling climb, the loop closing on itself.
Because the expectation of a rise draws the buying that makes the rise, and the rise confirms the expectation and draws the next buyer, swelling points the price up a feedback loop, each round of buying justified by the last and calling the next, the momentum carrying the price higher free of any worth beneath it.

## Lever

The expectation of others' buying, made true by acting on it.
Where reflecting runs on belief about belief held still, swelling runs on that belief in motion: each buyer is right that the price is rising and right that others expect it to rise, so buying is the sound response, and the sound response is what confirms the expectation, the loop turning on no error but on everyone correctly reading a rise they are together making.

## Echo

A price with its own momentum, and a turn that will end it.
The loop has closed and the price climbs on the belief in its climbing, so what this process leaves is a bubble that rises as long as the expectation holds and collapses when it turns, a self-fulfilling swell that reads as value discovered and is only the crowd pricing its own momentum.
