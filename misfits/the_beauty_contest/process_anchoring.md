# Process: Anchoring

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The valuer holding to the thing's worth against the self-referential price, the one discipline the game admits and the one it can defeat.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Valuer, with a sound valuation and capital to back it.
At [the long view](place_the_long_view.md), the fundamental judged and the price found adrift from it, the player taking the position that value will out and holding it against the crowd.

## Direction

Toward the price called wrong and the correction waited for.
Because the valuation is sound and the price is adrift, anchoring points the player against the crowd, selling the swell and buying the wreck, so the process aims the one force that could tie price back to worth, a bet on value held until the correction comes.

## Lever

Being right about the thing, against a game that does not reward it.
Because the fundamental is real and the price will meet it in the end, anchoring runs on a true valuation, but the game rewards matching and not truth and the correction comes on no schedule, so the lever is sound and slow, a rightness the market can run against for longer than the capital can hold.

## Echo

A discipline that may not outlast the tide.
The player has anchored on worth and traded against the crowd, so what this process leaves is either a price tied back to value or a valuer carried off before the correction, arbitrage risky and capital-constrained, the anchor holding only if it can be held to the turn, right about the thing and perhaps ruined being right too early.
