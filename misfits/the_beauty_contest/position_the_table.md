# Position: The Table

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The seat that plays for the prize on matching, forecasting where the crowd will go rather than what the thing is worth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A stake and a read on the room: the capital to take a position, and the skill to anticipate where the crowd will move.
A clear case: when the payoff rewards matching average opinion, reading the room and going with it is the sound, rewarded play.
No way to earn from being right about the thing while the crowd disagrees, and every reason to set the fundamental aside and play the others.

## Orders

Guess the room.
Forecast where the crowd will go, and the crowd's forecast of the crowd, take the position the tower of expectations points to, and ride the rise before it turns.
Read the market, not the thing, because the prize is the match and not the fact.

## Loses

The tie between price and worth, exactly by playing the payoff as it is set.
Because the reward is matching the crowd and not judging the thing, the seat lets go of the fundamental and turns to the others' expectations, so its soundest play, best anticipating the room, is the very move that unmoors the price from value, and the better the room is read the further the price floats from worth.
It gains the prize for matching and loses the anchor to value, its own skill the thing that prices the market by opinion about opinion.

## Drives

Toward the crowd anticipated and the rise ridden,
bought by setting the thing's worth aside,
so the seat that plays the market best is the one that prices it furthest from the thing.
