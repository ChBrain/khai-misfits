# Piece: The Tower

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The regress of iterated expectations, the climb from what the room thinks to what the room thinks the room thinks, and up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Built at [the floor](place_the_floor.md), rising a degree with every player who reads the players.
Flows nowhere and stacks upward: each level a guess about the level below, the height set by how far the room reasons.

## Load Bearing

Without it guessing the crowd would stop at the first degree, a single read of average opinion and no regress.
With it the guessing has no natural top: because each player knows the others are also forecasting the crowd, each must forecast the crowd's forecast, and then the forecast of that, so judgement climbs from the first degree to the second to the third and the price becomes a weighted average of expectations of expectations. The Tower is load bearing because it is the regress that carries price away from value (Nagel; Allen, Morris & Shin): the iterated expectations that every rational player must climb because everyone else is climbing, so the piece carries the mechanism by which sound reasoning, compounded, prices the thing furthest from its worth.

## Apparent

Just being thorough, thinking one step ahead of the other traders, the ordinary edge of the sharp player.
Seen at the surface, the Tower looks like diligence, the smart money reasoning further than the rest, and the truth that each further step is a step away from the thing, that the whole climb prices opinion about opinion and never the thing itself, that no one reaching higher brings the price closer to worth, stays hidden behind how plainly it pays to think one degree beyond the room.

## Yearbook

Climbed by [the Speculator](persona_the_speculator.md) and [the Crowd](persona_the_crowd.md) through [reflecting](process_reflecting.md), the regress made of their mutual reading.
It is the engine of the trap: the Tower is raised because [the prize](piece_the_prize.md) rewards matching, it carries judgement away from [the fundamental](piece_the_fundamental.md), and its height is the self-referential price that swells into [the bubble](piece_the_bubble.md), the degrees of the whole [contest](piece_the_contest.md).
