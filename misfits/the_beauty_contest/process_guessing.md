# Process: Guessing

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The player turning from the thing's worth to the others' expectations, because the payoff rewards matching the crowd and not being right.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Speculator, with a stake and a payoff on matching.
At [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the prize set for whoever anticipates the crowd, the player setting the fundamental aside and asking not what the thing is worth but what the room will do.

## Direction

Toward the fundamental set aside and the crowd played.
Because being right about the thing earns nothing while the crowd disagrees and guessing the crowd earns everything, guessing points the player away from worth and toward the others' expectations, so the question the payoff rewards, what will the room do, replaces the question the market was meant to ask.

## Lever

The payoff on matching, not on truth.
Because the reward is agreement and not accuracy, guessing runs on sound self-interest: forecasting the crowd is the rational play and holding to value the losing one, so the process turns on no folly, only on a player answering the payoff exactly as it is set.

## Echo

The thing's worth let go, and the room taken up.
The player has set the fundamental aside and turned to the others' expectations, so what leaves this process is a market no longer pricing the thing but pricing the room's move, the first turn by which value drops out and opinion about the others takes its place.
