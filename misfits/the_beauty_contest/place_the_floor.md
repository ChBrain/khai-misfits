# Place: The Floor

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The market where the contest plays, every player reading every other and the price forming out of the reading.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The trading floor, the exchange, the order book, where prices form from bids and every bid is a read on the room.
The Speculators watching the tape and one another, the Crowd that is all of them together, the number moving as each moves by anticipating how the number will move.

## Holds

The guessing and the reflecting, and the price made of them.
Here every player prices by forecasting the others, so the Floor holds the one condition the contest needs: the payoff on matching, in a room where each is trying to match the rest and the rest are trying to match each, so a price forms not from the thing's worth but from the tower of guesses about guesses, the market pricing its own expectation in plain sight and calling the number value.

## Offers

A verdict, and the sense that it is wise.
Because the Floor gathers every player's read into one moving number, it offers that number as the market's verdict on the thing, handing out the look of discovered truth, a price that seems to weigh all that is known, while the substance it hands out is a guess about a guess, the room's opinion of the room dressed as the thing's worth.

## Withheld

Any anchor to value, and any sign the number is self-referential.
The Floor can gather every read and form a price, but it withholds the fundamental that would tie the price to worth, no one on it being paid to hold that anchor, and, the number moving so plausibly with the flow of bids, it withholds any sign that the price is the room pricing itself, the verdict reading as a judgement on the thing while it is only the crowd's guess about the crowd.
