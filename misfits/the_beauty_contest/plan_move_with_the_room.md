# Plan: Move with the Room

## Taxonomy

## Direction

Be the room and read the room at once: bid up what the room expects the room to bid up, act on the expectation, and let the acting make the expected the fact. Set the target every player aims at, and aim, as each member does, at the target, so the average opinion moves by its own forecast of its own move.
