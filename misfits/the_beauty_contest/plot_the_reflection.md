# Plot: The Reflection

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Floor](place_the_floor.md), everyone guessing the crowd and everyone knowing it.
A room in which each player is forecasting the others, and each knows the others are forecasting them, so no one can stop at a single read.

## Action

[Reflecting](process_reflecting.md): each player forecasts not just the crowd but the crowd's forecast of the crowd, and judgement climbs a degree, and another.

## Stage

[The Speculator](persona_the_speculator.md) and [the Crowd](persona_the_crowd.md), each guessing a room made of guessers, climb [the tower](piece_the_tower.md) from the first degree to the second to the third, and the price becomes a weighted average of expectations of expectations.
No one is being irrational: each correctly assumes the others reason as they do, and reasons one step further.

## Tension

The regress is built by soundness, not by error, and it carries the price away from the thing.
There is nothing to fault: each player is right that the others are also guessing the crowd, and right to guess a degree beyond them. But every degree climbed is a degree further from the fundamental, so the plot closes with judgement resting entirely on opinion about opinion, the price a tower of guesses about guesses, further from worth than any single belief and built by every player reasoning well.
