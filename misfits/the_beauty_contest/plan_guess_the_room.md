# Plan: Guess the Room

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Table](position_the_table.md), inside [The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Table](position_the_table.md)

## Direction

Play the payoff as it is set: not to be right about the thing but to be right about everyone else. Forecast where the crowd will go, and the crowd's forecast of the crowd, take the position the tower of expectations points to, and ride the rise before it turns. Read the market, not the fundamental, because the prize is the match and not the fact.

## Orders

([The Speculator](persona_the_speculator.md) sets the fundamental aside and forecasts the crowd's guess, taking the position the room will bid up, the sound play that the payoff on matching rewards.)
