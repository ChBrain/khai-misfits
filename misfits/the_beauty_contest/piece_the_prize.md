# Piece: The Prize

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The payoff rule that rewards matching the crowd rather than being right about the thing, the root the whole game grows from.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set at [the floor](place_the_floor.md), where the reward goes to whoever the market proves to have anticipated.
Flows to no one thing: it is the rule of the game, the condition that pays for agreement and not for accuracy.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no contest, only valuation: reward being right about the thing and each player weighs the thing.
With it the game changes at the root: because the prize goes to whoever best anticipates the crowd and not to whoever best judges the thing, valuing the fundamental correctly earns nothing while the crowd disagrees, and guessing the crowd earns everything even when the crowd is wrong. The Prize is load bearing because it is the payoff that turns a market of valuers into a room of guessers (Keynes): the reward on matching, so the piece carries the reason a rational player sets worth aside, the first cause from which the tower and the bubble follow.

## Apparent

Just how markets pay, the plain fact that you profit by buying low and selling high to the next buyer.
Seen at the surface, the Prize looks like ordinary trading sense, and the truth that a reward for matching the crowd is a reward for anticipating opinion and not for judging value, that a market paid on agreement will price agreement and not the thing, that the payoff itself is what unmoors the price, stays hidden behind how obvious it is that you sell to whoever will pay more.

## Yearbook

Set by the game and read first by [the Speculator](persona_the_speculator.md) through [guessing](process_guessing.md), the rule the room answers.
It is the root of the trap: the Prize is why [the fundamental](piece_the_fundamental.md) is set aside, why [the tower](piece_the_tower.md) is climbed, and why the swell of [the bubble](piece_the_bubble.md) pays while it lasts, the payoff on matching that the whole [contest](piece_the_contest.md) grows from.
