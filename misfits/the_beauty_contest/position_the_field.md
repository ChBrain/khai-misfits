# Position: The Field

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The seat of the average opinion the prize is matched against, made of players each guessing the crowd, so it is both the target and its own mirror.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Numbers and no centre: the aggregate weight of every player, and no single mind behind it, only the sum of players each reading the sum.
A plain truth: the average opinion is real, it is what the prize is matched against and what every player aims at.
No fact outside itself that it converges on, and every reason to move by its own expectation of its own move.

## Orders

Move with the room.
Bid up what the room expects the room to bid up, act on the expectation, and let the acting make the expected the fact.
Be the target every player aims at, and aim, as each of its members does, at itself.

## Loses

Any anchor to worth, by being made of the very guessing it is guessed by.
Because each member prices by forecasting the Field and the Field is the sum of those forecasts, there is no outside value it settles on, only the room converging on its own expectation, so the average opinion the prize rewards matching is itself opinion about opinion and the thing's worth enters the loop nowhere.
It is the verdict every player reads, and the verdict is only every player reading it, a target that is its own reflection.

## Drives

Toward the room aimed at the room,
its expectation made real by being acted on,
so the seat that sets the target for all is a mirror, and what it prices is itself.
