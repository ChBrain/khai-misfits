# Process: Reflecting

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). Each player guessing the others who are guessing them, so judgement climbs the tower of expectations of expectations.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The Speculator and the Crowd, each guessing a room made of guessers.
At [the floor](place_the_floor.md), every player knowing the others are also forecasting the crowd, so each must forecast not just the crowd but the crowd's forecast of the crowd.

## Direction

Toward the regress climbed and value left behind.
Because everyone is guessing the crowd and everyone knows it, reflecting points each player up a degree and then another, from what the room thinks to what the room thinks the room thinks, so judgement climbs the tower and the thing's worth falls further below with every level.

## Lever

The mutual knowledge that all are playing the same game.
Because each player knows the others are matching the crowd too, reflecting runs on shared rationality: no one can stop at the first degree while the rest go higher, so the process is driven by the soundness of every player, the regress built from each correctly assuming the others reason as they do.

## Echo

A price made of opinion about opinion.
Judgement has climbed from the thing to the room to the room's read of the room, so what this process leaves is a price that is a weighted average of expectations of expectations, sitting further from the fundamental than any single belief, the market now pricing its own guess about itself.
