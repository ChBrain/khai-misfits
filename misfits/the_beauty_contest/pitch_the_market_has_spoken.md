# Pitch: The Market Has Spoken

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Shrewd, worldly, and clear-eyed.
The plain sense that the price is the verdict: a thousand traders weighing everything known, and the number they settle on is what the thing is worth, so the smart move is to read the room and go with what rises.
Nothing naive in it: matching the crowd is exactly what the market pays for, and the one who reads it well is rewarded.

## Undertow

Under the verdict, a game whose prize is agreement, not truth.
Because the reward goes to whoever matches the crowd and not to whoever judges the thing rightly, each player guesses the others' guesses in an unending regress, and the price becomes opinion about opinion, a tower of expectations of expectations that floats free of any worth beneath it.
The number that reads as a thousand traders' verdict on the thing is a thousand traders' guess about each other.

## Nerve

The feeling of a market so wise it needs no second-guessing, its price the last word on value,
because every participant is rationally reading every other and the number they reach looks like discovered truth,
while underneath no one is pricing the thing at all, each pricing the others' pricing, the worth of the thing having dropped out of the question the first moment matching paid better than being right.

## Echo

After the run: a price that rose because everyone expected everyone to keep buying and fell when that turned, a bubble that read as value the whole way up,
no one foolish and no one lying, only sound players each anticipating the room exactly as the payoff rewards,
and the one who valued the thing correctly and held, carried off on the tide before the price came back, broke being right too early, so the market goes on pricing its own guess about itself.
