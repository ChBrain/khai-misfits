## Place

## Load Bearing

Without it there are only pieces: a payoff, a set-aside worth, a regress, a swell, with no name for the game that binds them.
With it the parts become one self-referential engine: the prize on matching turns valuers into guessers, the guessers climb the tower of iterated expectations, the tower's height inflates into a self-fulfilling bubble, and the whole prices a thing by opinion about opinion while the anchor that could discipline it is carried off. The Contest is load bearing because it is the phenomenon entire (Keynes): the market that has stopped pricing the thing and started pricing itself, so the piece carries the trap whole, the reason a room of sound forecasters floats a price free of worth for as long as the game runs.

## Apparent

Price discovery, the market doing its work, a thousand judgements resolving into the truest number available.
Seen at the surface, the Contest is invisible and only its output is seen, a moving price that reads as the collective verdict on the thing, and the truth that the verdict is the crowd's guess about the crowd, that a market paid on matching prices agreement and not value, that the number can float free of worth for the whole run of the game, stays hidden behind how much a market price looks like the last word on what a thing is worth.
