# Place: The Long View

## Taxonomy

[The Beauty Contest](play_the_beauty_contest.md). The vantage of fundamental worth, from which the price is judged against the thing, and from which the divergence must be outlasted.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The quiet room off the Floor, the balance sheet and the discounted flow, where the thing is weighed by what it will earn and what it is.
The Valuer at the ledger, reckoning worth apart from the tape, finding the price adrift from the value and taking the position that value will out.

## Holds

The anchoring, and the wait it demands.
Here worth is judged apart from the room, so the Long View holds the one stance that could discipline the contest: the fundamental measured and the price called wrong against it, the Valuer's position taken on being right about the thing, and, because the correction comes on no schedule, the holding of that position through a divergence that runs against it for as long as the game runs.

## Offers

A true valuation, and a bet that must outlast the crowd.
Because the Long View prices the thing by what it is, it offers the Valuer a sound judgement and the ground to trade against the price, handing out the one anchor the market lacks, and, with it, the burden that the anchor pays only if it can be held to the correction, a right valuation whose reward waits on a crowd that need not agree before the capital does.

## Withheld

The power to make the price come back, and the capital to wait forever.
The Long View can value the thing and call the price wrong, but it withholds any lever to make the crowd agree, the correction owed to no schedule, and it withholds the endless capital the wait might need, so it holds the anchor while it can and withholds any promise that the anchor outlasts the tide, value certain and its vindication not.
