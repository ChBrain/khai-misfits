# Piece: The Higher Table

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The escape and its price, the land-use decision raised to a table where the excluded finally count, bought by stripping the incumbent of the control that insures their basket.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Convened at [the statehouse](place_the_statehouse.md), the table above the town where the newcomer's absence from the local hearing stops mattering.

## Load Bearing

Without it the shortage would have no remedy inside the structure that produces it, every town locked to its own hearing and every newcomer permanently outside every vote that concerns them, the wall permanent by design.
With it the decision can move: preemption, upzoning by right, a regional or state body that sets the rule the town's hearing no longer can, so that the housing the metro needs stops depending on the consent of every town it needs it from. The Higher Table is load bearing because it is the level to which the land-use decision is raised, past the town where the newcomer had no vote, bought by stripping the incumbent of the local control that insures their one basket (Fischel): the piece that carries the trade itself, supply bought only by exposing the very stake the restriction existed to protect.

## Apparent

A bureaucratic reshuffling, one government layer trading authority with another, a technical fix to a technical problem of jurisdiction.
Seen at the surface, the Higher Table looks like tidying, an efficiency of scale, moving a decision to wherever it is administratively cleanest to make, and the truth that what is moved is the owner's only instrument for insuring an undiversifiable asset, that the tidying costs the incumbent the very control the whole misfit turns on, stays hidden behind how neutral raising a decision to a bigger table sounds next to what the owner loses in the raising.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the state](persona_the_state.md) through [preempting](process_preempting.md), against [the homeowner](persona_the_homeowner.md) who insured [the only basket](piece_the_only_basket.md) at [the hearing](place_the_hearing.md).
It is the last move of the misfit: the Higher Table answers [the shortage](piece_the_shortage.md) by taking from the owner the same [ballot](piece_the_ballot.md) that once defended them, so that [the newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) counts at last, at the cost of the control the whole trap was built to protect.
