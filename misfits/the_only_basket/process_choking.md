# Process: Choking

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). Many towns' restrictions, summed at [the Metro](place_the_metro.md), the point where a thousand sound defences become one regional wall.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Many towns' restrictions, each locally sound, arriving at the same regional market.
At the Metro, the sum of every Hearing's vote, no two towns coordinating and every one of them reasoning the same way.

## Direction

Toward a regional housing supply choked below what the region needs, prices raised above it.
Because each restriction removes supply that the whole region, not just the one town, would otherwise have drawn on, choking turns town-level prudence into region-level scarcity, so it runs from a hundred separate votes to one wedge between the cost of building and the price of buying.

## Lever

Each locally sound defence meeting the region's total demand.
Because no single restriction is large enough to matter at the regional scale and every restriction together is more than large enough, choking runs on an aggregation problem and not a bad decision, the lever the arithmetic of many rational parts summing to an irrational whole.

## Echo

A metro-wide shortage no jurisdiction chose, prices standing above the cost of construction wherever the zoning binds.
Every town can point to its own Hearing and find nothing wrong with the vote it took, so what stands at the end is a shortage authored by no one and felt by everyone who searches, the choking visible only once the whole map is read at once.
