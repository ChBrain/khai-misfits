# Plot: The Reckoning

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Metro](place_the_metro.md), town after town down-zoned, the same sound vote repeated at every hearing.
The permits thin across the map, and the price climbs where the zoning binds tightest.

## Action

[Choking](process_choking.md): [The Homeowner](persona_the_homeowner.md), holding [the homeowner's seat](position_homeowner.md), repeats the same sincere defence in town after town, and the sum chokes the region's supply below what it needs.

## Stage

[The Homeowner](persona_the_homeowner.md), on [Insure the House](plan_insure_the_house.md), guards their own street again and again, each vote sound and none of them coordinated with the next.
[The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md), on [Seek a Home](plan_seek_a_home.md), widens the search across the whole metro and meets the same shortage everywhere the restrictions have summed.

## Tension

No single town chose the shortage: each voted only on its own streets, and the region-wide wall was never anyone's ballot measure.
There is nothing to fault town by town: each restriction is a sound local choice. But the plot closes with a whole region short of housing, the shortage a sum no jurisdiction chose and no owner voted for as a goal.
