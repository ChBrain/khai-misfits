# Piece: The Boundary

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The line the restriction's cost crosses, the municipal edge that holds the vote inside and lets the cost fall outside it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Drawn across [the metro](place_the_metro.md), the region the boundary cuts into towns that vote and towns that pay.

## Load Bearing

Without it the restriction's cost would land on the same people who voted it in, felt and weighed by the very room that cast it, and the vote would correct itself the way any cost borne by the voter tends to.
With it the cost crosses a line the vote never reaches: the franchise stops at the municipal edge, and the town that restricts is not the town the newcomer would have lived in, so the ones who pay for the restriction are precisely the ones who had no hand in casting it. The Boundary is load bearing because it is the jurisdiction line the local franchise cannot cross (Fischel): the piece that carries the incidence itself, the reason a sincere and locally sound vote produces a cost that its own voters never feel.

## Apparent

Just the edge of a map, an administrative line no more consequential than a zip code, the ordinary fact that towns have borders.
Seen at the surface, the Boundary looks like bookkeeping, the line a map needs to say where one town's authority ends, and the truth that this line is also where accountability ends, that a vote cast inside it can impose a cost that lands entirely outside it, stays hidden behind how mundane a municipal border looks next to the decisions made within it.

## Yearbook

Drawn by [the state](persona_the_state.md) long before any [restriction](piece_the_restriction.md) crossed it, the edge [excluding](process_excluding.md) works along.
It is the fault line of the misfit: the Boundary is where [the newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) is shut out of a vote that shapes their price, and it is the same line [the higher table](piece_the_higher_table.md) proposes to draw again, higher up, where the excluded are finally inside the room.
