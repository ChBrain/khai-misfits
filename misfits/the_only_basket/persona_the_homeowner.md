# Persona: The Homeowner

## Taxonomy

[Homeowner](position_homeowner.md). The incumbent owner defending an undiversifiable home through the local ballot, prudent and sincere, the actor the trap is spent through.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Responsible. Protecting the family's security.
Is the owner whose house is the largest thing they own, who goes to the town meeting to guard its value against development that might depress it, doing the prudent thing by the rules.
No malice in it: only a person protecting the one asset they cannot diversify, the security they will retire on, the only way open to them.

## Action

Insures the house, on [Insure the House](plan_insure_the_house.md), voting at the hearing to restrict the nearby development that threatens the value.
Defends the undiversifiable stake with the only instrument they have, the local ballot.
Does what a careful owner does: guards the biggest investment of their life against a risk they cannot hedge.

## Shadow

Not an owner who hates newcomers, one guarding a stake they cannot spread, and that is the whole difficulty: the defence is prudent, and its prudence is what walls the region.
Because the home is undiversifiable and the ballot the only hedge, the Homeowner rationally restricts nearby building, and every owner reasoning the same way sums into a supply choked below the region's need, so the more responsibly each guards their basket the higher the wall grows, the sincerity of the defence the very thing that excludes, and the newcomer who bears it never in the room.
It protects only what it cannot afford to lose, and protecting only what it cannot afford to lose is what shuts the region out.

## Tell

Says "I have nothing against more housing, just not the project that puts my savings at risk."
Means it, truly, and never sees that every owner says the same of every project, so the sum of all the reasonable exceptions is that almost nothing is built.
