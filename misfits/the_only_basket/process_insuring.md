# Process: Insuring

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The Homeowner's rational defence of an undiversifiable home through the local ballot, the first and most sincere turn of the wall.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A value-threatening development proposed near a home that cannot be diversified.
At [the Hearing](place_the_hearing.md), the Builder's plan on the agenda and the Homeowner's stake exposed to it, a threat to the one basket that holds nearly everything.

## Direction

Toward the restriction enacted, the value defended.
Because the home is the largest, most concentrated share of what the Homeowner owns, insuring turns toward the ballot every time a threat appears near it, so it runs from proposal to objection to vote, ending, again and again, with the motion carried and the parcel down-zoned.

## Lever

The unhedgeable stake meeting the local ballot.
Because the Homeowner cannot spread the risk the way a diversified investor could, and the ballot is the only instrument close enough to reach it, insuring runs on real exposure met by real power, the lever not a bias to be corrected but a stake and an instrument, each genuinely fitted to the other.

## Echo

A value protected, and one more sound restriction on the books.
The threat that opened the Hearing has been answered and the parcel is safe and the meeting adjourns having done exactly what it was called to do, so what reads as one town's prudent housekeeping is, echoed once more, a wall gaining a single sincere brick, indistinguishable at this range from care.
