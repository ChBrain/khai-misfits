# Plot: The Shut Door

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Metro](place_the_metro.md), the restriction now binding, the parcel down-zoned and the project withdrawn.
Rent listed where the jobs are, and none of it within reach.

## Action

[Excluding](process_excluding.md): [The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md), holding [the newcomer's seat](position_newcomer.md), searches for a home near the work and finds the housing not built and the price already past them.

## Stage

[The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md), on [Seek a Home](plan_seek_a_home.md), looks at the town the jobs are in and finds nothing they can afford, the search moving further out with each listing.

## Tension

No one refused the Newcomer at a door: they never reached a door, because the meeting that decided this was one they had no vote inside.
There is nothing to fault in any single decision: the restriction was sound and sincere and decided by those it belonged to. But the plot closes with a would-be resident shut out, bearing a cost settled across a boundary they had no voice inside.
