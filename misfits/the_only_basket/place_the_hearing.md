# Place: The Hearing

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The local zoning board or town meeting where the restriction is put to a vote, the room the ballot belongs to and the room the newcomer cannot enter.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A room packed with the people who already live there, folding chairs and a sign-up sheet for public comment, an agenda item with the Builder's plan pinned to the wall.
The Homeowner rising to object, one after another, each citing traffic or schools or character, the Builder's plan taking the objections one at a time, and at the end of the night a show of hands or a roll call, the motion carried.

## Holds

The zoning code and the ballot, and the motion that amends one by the other.
Here the town's power over its own land meets the people who hold it, so the Hearing holds the point where a down-zoning motion becomes law by a vote of the room, the code rewritten one restriction at a time by whoever is present to write it.

## Offers

A say, to whoever already lives inside the line.
Because the Hearing is called by the town for the town, it offers the Homeowner a real vote on a real threat to a real asset, the hearing genuinely responsive to whoever shows up, so it hands out the clean, democratic legitimacy of a decision made in public, by the people it will bind.

## Withheld

Any seat for the ones the vote will bind hardest.
The Hearing cannot both belong to the town that calls it and seat the Newcomer who lives outside the line the town draws, so it withholds the Newcomer's vote entirely, the franchise stopping at the boundary the meeting sits inside of, and it withholds more than a vote from the Builder, who may propose the plan on the wall but never carries a ballot to decide its fate, present only as the thing the room votes on.
