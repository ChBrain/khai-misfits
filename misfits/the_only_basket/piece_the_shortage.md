# Piece: The Shortage

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The sum across the region, the supply choked below what the metro needs by a thousand restrictions none of which set out to choke it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Felt across [the metro](place_the_metro.md), where no single town's restriction is visible but the region's total is.

## Load Bearing

Without it every restriction would stay a local matter, sound in its own town and inconsequential beyond it, and the misfit would carry no cost worth the name.
With it the restrictions have a total: town by town the sound defences accumulate into a constraint on housing supply that is not the cost of building but the cost of permission, and prices rise above the cost of construction wherever the regulation binds. The Shortage is load bearing because it is the accrued sum of land-use restriction across a metro that drives the price of housing above its cost of construction (Glaeser and Gyourko): the piece that carries the reckoning itself, invisible in any one town's meeting minutes and total only once every town's minutes are read together.

## Apparent

A hot housing market, prices simply reflecting demand, a region so popular that everyone wants in and not everyone can have it.
Seen at the surface, the Shortage looks like success, a place so desirable its housing costs what desirability costs, and the truth that the gap between price and the cost of building is regulation, not popularity, that it is the sum of restrictions no single town intended as a regional outcome, stays hidden behind how naturally an expensive region reads as merely wanted.

## Yearbook

Summed from every [restriction](piece_the_restriction.md) enacted in [excluding](process_excluding.md), the total no [homeowner](persona_the_homeowner.md) ever voted for.
It is the reckoning of the misfit: the Shortage is what [choking](process_choking.md) leaves behind at [the metro](place_the_metro.md), the price [the newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) pays for a vote they never cast, and the cost that makes the case for [the higher table](piece_the_higher_table.md) impossible to keep dismissing.
