# Position: Newcomer

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The seat of the would-be resident, bearing the shortage and holding no vote in the jurisdiction whose restriction produces it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A need for the foothold and no franchise to secure it: the standing of one who would live and work in the town and cannot yet, outside the boundary that governs it.
A clear case: the demand is real and the person is willing, only the housing is not built and the price is out of reach.
No vote in the town they would enter, because the franchise stops at the line and they live on the other side of it.

## Orders

Seek a home.
Look to move in where the work and the schools are, to rent or buy, and press against a price the restriction has raised.
Bear the cost of a decision you had no vote in, and search further out.

## Loses

The foothold itself, and any voice in the body that denied it.
Because the seat sits outside the boundary that imposes the restriction, it gives up all say in the decision that prices it out, so it bears the full cost of the wall with no vote in building it, losing both the home and the standing to change the rule, excluded by a franchise it is not inside.
It surrenders the vote it never had to the boundary, left to pay for a choice made without it.

## Drives

Toward the foothold denied and the cost borne in silence,
the one who would enter kept out by votes they could not cast,
so the seat that bears the shortage is the seat with no voice in the decision that made it.
