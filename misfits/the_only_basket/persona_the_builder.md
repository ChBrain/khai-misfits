# Persona: The Builder

## Taxonomy

[Builder](position_builder.md). The developer who would supply the housing the region needs and is blocked by the restriction the homeowners vote, the thwarted supply.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ready. Capable. Held at the permit desk.
Is the developer with a plan for denser housing, the land, the finance, and the willingness to build what the region is short of, waiting on a permission that does not come.
No recklessness in it: only someone who would build the homes people need, stopped not by the cost of building but by the cost of being allowed to.

## Action

Builds the homes, on [Build the Homes](plan_build_the_homes.md), bringing a plan for density to the hearing and seeking the permission to raise it.
Meets the homeowners' restriction and the down-zoned parcel, and is turned back.
Does what a builder does: proposes the supply the shortage calls for, and finds the gate shut by those who live nearby.

## Shadow

Not a builder the market cannot support, one the ballot will not permit, and that is the whole difficulty: the demand is real and the finance is there, and only the permission is missing.
Because the ones who vote on the permission are the incumbents whose values the density threatens, the Builder is blocked not where building is uneconomic but where it is most needed, so the projects that would ease the shortage are exactly the ones the meeting kills, the supply thwarted by permission and not by cost, and the region left short of what the Builder stood ready to raise.
It offers precisely the housing the shortage needs, and offering it where the incumbents vote is what gets it refused.

## Tell

Says "the numbers work, the demand is there, I just cannot get it approved."
Is right about the numbers, truly, and keeps meeting a no that has nothing to do with them, the plan sound and the permission withheld.
