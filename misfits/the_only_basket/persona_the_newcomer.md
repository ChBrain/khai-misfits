# Persona: The Newcomer

## Taxonomy

[Newcomer](position_newcomer.md). The would-be resident, the young family or worker, who bears the shortage and has no vote in the town whose restriction produces it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Hopeful. Priced out. Somewhere else, for now.
Is the young family, the worker, the one who would move into the town for its jobs or schools and finds the housing not built and the price beyond reach.
No fault in them: only a person who would live and work here and cannot, wanting the same foothold the incumbents already hold.

## Action

Seeks a home, on [Seek a Home](plan_seek_a_home.md), looking to move in, to rent or buy where the work and the schools are, and meeting a price the restriction has raised.
Bears the cost of a decision made in meetings they could not attend and votes they could not cast.
Does what a newcomer does: tries to enter, and is kept out by a wall they had no hand in building.

## Shadow

Not a person refused at a door, one who never reaches the door, and that is the whole difficulty: no one denies them, and yet the housing is not there and the price is out of reach, and they cannot vote to change it.
Because the franchise stops at the municipal line and the Newcomer lives outside it, they bear the full cost of the restriction with no voice in the body that imposed it, so the very people the shortage falls on are the people with no say in it, priced out by a prudence that was never accountable to them, kept from the town by the votes of those already in it.
It asks only for the foothold the incumbents have, and having no vote in the town is what leaves it shut out.

## Tell

Says "we looked there, it is where the jobs are, but there is nothing we can afford," and moves the search further out.
Wanted only to live where the work was, and never gets a say in the meeting that made it impossible, absent from the decision that decided them.
