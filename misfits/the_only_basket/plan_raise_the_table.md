# Plan: Raise the Table

## Taxonomy

## Direction

Preempt the local restriction, upzone by right, and move the land-use decision to a level where the excluded newcomers are represented. Free the supply the towns held back, counting the region rather than the street, and accept that housing it means exposing the values the owners voted to protect.
