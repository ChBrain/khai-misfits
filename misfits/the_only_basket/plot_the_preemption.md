# Plot: The Preemption

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Statehouse](place_the_statehouse.md), the regional shortage now a line item, the bill to preempt local zoning drafted and read.
The excluded newcomers counted at last, at a table the town meeting never sat them at.

## Action

[Preempting](process_preempting.md): [The State](persona_the_state.md), holding [the state's seat](position_state.md), moves to preempt local zoning and raise the land-use decision to a table where the excluded count.

## Stage

[The State](persona_the_state.md), on [Raise the Table](plan_raise_the_table.md), pushes the bill to upzone by right, weighing the newcomers the town meeting could not.
[The Homeowner](persona_the_homeowner.md), on [Insure the House](plan_insure_the_house.md), testifies against it, the only instrument that hedges their basket now itself on the floor.

## Tension

The State is not wrong to count the excluded, and the Homeowner is not wrong to fear losing the only hedge they have.
There is nothing to fault on either side: the region truly needs the homes, and the local control truly is the owner's sole insurance. So the plot closes ambivalently: the decision raised and the housing able to be built ... or local control holding, the owner keeping the only instrument that guards their basket, and the wall standing.
