# Persona: The State

## Taxonomy

[State](position_state.md). The higher level of government that would raise the land-use decision to a table where the excluded newcomers count, at the cost of the homeowner's local control.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Regional. Even-handed. Looking past the town line.
Is the legislature or agency that sees the whole metro's shortage and would preempt local zoning, upzone by right, and weigh the newcomers the town meeting cannot.
No highhandedness in it: only a body accountable to the region trying to house it, counting the people the municipal franchise leaves out.

## Action

Raises the table, on [Raise the Table](plan_raise_the_table.md), preempting local restriction and moving the land-use decision to a level where the excluded are represented.
Frees the supply the towns held back, and takes the choice out of the hearing.
Does what a regional authority does: overrides the local wall to house the region, at the incumbents' expense.

## Shadow

Not an enemy of the homeowner, one who must take their only hedge to house the region, and that is the whole difficulty: the local control it strips is the incumbent's sole instrument for insuring an undiversifiable asset.
Because the shortage can be eased only by overriding the restrictions that cause it, the State cannot house the newcomers without exposing the home values the owners voted to protect, so it buys supply by taking security, asking sincere people to bear a real risk to their largest holding for the sake of others who do not vote in their town, the correction unavoidable and its cost landing on the very prudence it overrules.
It would house the region, and housing the region means stripping the owner of the only hedge they had.

## Tell

Says "local control has to yield: the region needs the homes and the town will not allow them."
Is right about the region, and never fully answers the owner who asks who now insures the basket the state has just exposed, the supply freed and the hedge removed.
