# Pitch: Protect Your Home

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Prudent, responsible, protective.
The plain good sense of guarding the biggest investment of your life: the one asset you cannot diversify, the security your family retires on, defended at the town meeting against the development that might depress its value.
Nothing false in it: the home really is undiversifiable, the threat to its value really is unhedgeable, and defending it is exactly what a careful person should do.

## Undertow

Under the prudence, town by town the sound defences sum, the supply chokes below what the region needs, and the newcomer who would have moved in is priced out.
Because each owner rationally insures the one basket they cannot spread their risk across, and the ballot is their only instrument, the restrictions accumulate into a regional shortage no one voted for, its cost borne by those with no vote in the meeting.
The responsibility that guards the home is the responsibility that walls the region off.

## Nerve

The clean conscience of protecting your family's security, sure you have done the prudent thing, and you have,
because the home is the only basket you have and the ballot is the only way to guard it,
while across the metro a thousand owners guard theirs the same way, and the young family that would have moved in finds no home built and the price out of reach.

## Echo

After the run: a region walled off by the sum of a thousand sound defences, no owner selfish and no town choosing the shortage,
only prudent people guarding undiversifiable stakes at the only meeting open to them, ballot by ballot,
and the newcomer priced out, bearing a cost settled across a boundary they never had a vote inside.
