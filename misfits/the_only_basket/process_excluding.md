# Process: Excluding

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The restriction, once on the books, meeting the Newcomer who has no vote in the town that wrote it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A restriction that binds and a unit of housing that is never built.
At [the Hearing](place_the_hearing.md)'s aftermath, the parcel down-zoned and the Builder's plan shelved, the supply the Newcomer needed absent before the search even begins.

## Direction

Toward the Newcomer priced out, zoned out, or both.
Because the restriction removes the unit before demand ever meets it, excluding turns toward a closed door with no one standing at it to close it, so it runs from a vote no Newcomer attended to a price no Newcomer can meet, the exclusion accomplished without a single refusal.

## Lever

The supply cap meeting a newcomer who has no local vote.
Because the franchise stops at the boundary the town draws around itself, and the Newcomer stands, or would stand, outside that line, excluding runs on a mismatch built into the map itself, the cost falling precisely on the one party the Hearing was never structured to hear.

## Echo

A would-be resident kept out, and a cost decided across a line they sit outside.
The family that would have moved in widens its search instead, so what looks, from inside the town, like a restriction quietly doing its job is, from the Newcomer's side of the boundary, a home that was never on offer, the decision closed before they knew there had been one.
