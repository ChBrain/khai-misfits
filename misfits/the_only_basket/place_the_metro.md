# Place: The Metro

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The regional scale where every town's restriction adds into a single housing market, visible only once the town lines are crossed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A skyline of many towns and one commute, listings thinning out and prices climbing across all of them at once, a family's search radius widening week by week.
The Newcomer's search sliding further from the jobs, town after town offering the same "nothing we can afford," the gap between what is needed and what stands built growing wider than any single meeting could explain.

## Holds

The regional demand and the sum of every town's restriction, held against each other.
Here the whole market meets the whole of what has been zoned out of it, so the Metro holds the point where a thousand locally sound votes add into one regional number, the wedge between the cost of building and the price of buying, held in full only at this scale.

## Offers

A full view of the shortage, to whoever looks across the town lines.
Because the Metro is the only place the restrictions are summed rather than voted one at a time, it offers a vantage no Hearing has, the aggregate visible only from above, so it hands out the one thing no single town can see about itself: how much of the wall its own sound vote built.

## Withheld

Any single culprit, and any door back down to one.
The Metro cannot both show the shortage as a sum and hand back the one town or the one owner who caused it, so it withholds a name to blame, the wall the work of every Hearing and no Hearing at once, and it withholds a level at which the problem could be solved the way it was made, one restriction at a time, since undoing it town by town only re-creates, vote by vote, the same wall it removes.
