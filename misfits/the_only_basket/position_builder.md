# Position: Builder

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The seat of the developer who would supply the housing and is blocked by the restriction, where demand meets a permission it cannot win.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The means to build and the need for permission: the standing of one with land, finance, and a plan for density, dependent on a local approval to raise it.
A clear case: the demand is real and the project is sound, so the housing would be built if it were allowed.
No power to permit itself, only to propose, so the seat can supply the region only where the incumbents will let it.

## Orders

Build the homes.
Bring the plan for density to the hearing, seek the permission to raise it, and stand ready to supply what the region is short of.
Meet the demand where it is, and wait on the approval.

## Loses

The permission, precisely where the housing is most needed.
Because the ones who vote on the approval are the incumbents whose values the density threatens, the seat is refused not where building is uneconomic but where it is most in demand, so it loses its permission at the exact parcels that would ease the shortage, the supply thwarted by the ballot and not by the cost, the readiest projects the deadest on arrival.
It surrenders the approval to the incumbents' vote, held back where it is wanted most.

## Drives

Toward housing offered and permission refused,
the supply the region needs killed at the meeting that decides it,
so the seat that stands ready to build is stopped exactly where building would help.
