# Piece: The Only Basket

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The play's title piece, the undiversifiable home that makes defending its value rational, the anchor stake beneath every restriction that follows.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Held at [the hearing](place_the_hearing.md), where the value it carries is what the room turns out to defend.

## Load Bearing

Without it the owner's vote would be one preference among many, a taste to be outweighed, and no restriction would carry more weight than any other.
With it the whole structure has its stake: a home that cannot be diversified, cannot be spread across a hundred small holdings so that no single blow lands too hard, all the owner's savings sitting on one street. The Only Basket is load bearing because it is the undiversifiable asset that makes defending its value rational in a way a diversified investor's caution would not be (Fischel): the piece that carries the exposure itself, so that every vote cast afterward is not a preference weighed against other preferences but an owner insuring the one thing they cannot afford to lose.

## Apparent

Just a house, one asset among the many an adult accumulates, no different in kind from a retirement account or a share portfolio.
Seen at the surface, the Only Basket looks like ordinary property, a thing owned and occasionally worried over, and the truth that it is not diversified, that it cannot be spread the way a portfolio spreads risk, that the whole of a family's security rests on this one parcel holding its value, stays hidden behind how unremarkable owning a home appears next to owning stock.

## Yearbook

Carried by [the homeowner](persona_the_homeowner.md) into [insuring](process_insuring.md), the stake that gives the vote its weight.
It is the anchor of the misfit: the Only Basket underwrites [the ballot](piece_the_ballot.md) that casts [the restriction](piece_the_restriction.md), and it is the same undiversifiable stake that [the higher table](piece_the_higher_table.md) later asks the owner to risk, exposed wherever local control is stripped away.
