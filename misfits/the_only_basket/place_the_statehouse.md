# Place: The Statehouse

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The higher table, the state legislature or regional authority with the power to preempt the Hearing and count the Newcomer in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A chamber and a bill, a map of the whole state instead of one town's parcels, a column of housing counts beside a column of towns that never built them.
The State weighing a preemption law against a room of Homeowners who did not send it there, the excluded Newcomer, for once, a line item the vote must answer to.

## Holds

The preemption power and the regional franchise, and the by-right upzoning that follows from holding both.
Here the reach of the vote is the whole state rather than one town, so the Statehouse holds what no Hearing can, the authority to overrule a local restriction in the name of a resident that restriction never had to answer to.

## Offers

A table where the newcomers count and the supply can be freed.
Because the Statehouse answers to the whole state and not to one town's meeting, it offers the Newcomer the vote the Hearing withheld, the excluded counted here even where they hold no local ballot, so it hands out the one thing the lower table structurally cannot: a body big enough to weigh the shortage against the restriction that makes it.

## Withheld

The incumbents' consent, and any way to free the supply without a cost to somebody's basket.
The Statehouse cannot both override the local restriction and leave the Homeowner's local control intact, so it withholds a costless fix, freeing the supply only by exposing the values the restriction was there to protect, and it withholds the Homeowner's consent by design, since a table where the incumbents kept their veto would return, restriction by restriction, to the room this one exists to override.
