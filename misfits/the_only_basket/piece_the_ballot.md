# Piece: The Ballot

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The instrument at the owner's hand, the zoning vote through which the undiversifiable stake gets defended, cast by those enfranchised and closed to those who are not.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Cast at [the hearing](place_the_hearing.md), the room where the local franchise is exercised and the restriction decided.

## Load Bearing

Without it the owner could worry over the threat to their value and do nothing about it, the stake real but undefended, and no restriction would ever reach the books.
With it the owner has a lever: local government answers to the residents who vote in it, and zoning is the tool by which they translate that vote into a defence, restricting the density and development nearby that might depress the value they hold. The Ballot is load bearing because it is the local franchise through which the owner insures the home's value (Fischel): the piece that carries the instrument itself, cast by the homeowner and closed to the newcomer, who is not yet a resident of the town whose meeting decides what gets built in it.

## Apparent

A civic duty, an evening at the town hall, one vote among the ordinary business of local government.
Seen at the surface, the Ballot looks like routine democracy, a resident exercising a right common to every jurisdiction, and the truth that this particular vote insures a particular asset, that it is cast only by those already inside the boundary and never by those it will exclude, stays hidden behind how unremarkable a zoning hearing looks next to any other item on a town's agenda.
