# Process: Preempting

## Taxonomy

[The Only Basket](play_the_only_basket.md). The State, seeing the shortage from [the Statehouse](place_the_statehouse.md), overriding the Hearing's restriction and freeing the supply it held down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A state or regional body that can see the shortage the Metro made visible.
At the Statehouse, the regional housing count set beside the towns that never built it, a bill drafted to reach past the boundary the Hearing sits inside of.

## Direction

Toward local restriction overridden, by-right supply freed.
Because the Statehouse answers to the whole state and the Hearing answers only to itself, preempting turns toward the table where the Newcomer counts, so it runs from a state law to a parcel that can be built on again without a town's vote to permit it.

## Lever

The regional interest meeting the incumbent's insurance.
Because freeing the supply means overriding the very restriction that was protecting somebody's undiversifiable home, preempting runs on a real cost to a real stake, the lever not a technical fix but a transfer, the region's need weighed against the basket the Homeowner has no other way to hedge.

## Echo

Housing allowed to be built, and the Homeowner's local control, their only insurance, stripped from them.
The parcel that could not be touched can now be built on, so what reads at the Statehouse as a shortage finally addressed reads, back at the Hearing, as the one instrument the Homeowner ever had taken out of their hands, the wall breached at the cost of the wall's own reason for being.
