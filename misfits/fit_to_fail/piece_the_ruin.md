# Piece: The Ruin

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The phenomenon whole, the population evolving past its own viability into collapse, self-inflicted and villain-less, driven to extinction by selection itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Built across [the range](place_the_range.md) and the generations, where the trait wins and fixes and the level sinks.
Complete at [the brink](place_the_brink.md) and the crash, where the threshold is crossed and the population falls.

## Load Bearing

Without it the trait, the edge, the ratchet and the sinking viability are just parts, and no one is destroyed by their own fitness.
With it the trap is complete and authorless: a population bred fitter and fitter individual against individual and less and less viable whole against its threshold, carried across the floor and extinguished by the same selection that rewarded every carrier all the way down (Rankin and Lopez-Sepulcre, on evolutionary suicide, adaptation driving the population to extinction with no defector and no error). The Ruin is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole: not one greedy individual but a whole population doing the fit thing into its own collapse, so the piece carries the extinction selection itself produces, the crash with no cheat to blame, the fittest move made faithfully by all being the fatal one.

## Apparent

An adaptation running its course, on the surface, the population fit and the individuals winning, right to the edge.
Seen at the surface, the Ruin looks like exactly the opposite of ruin, a population getting fitter, each individual rewarded, and the truth that the fitness is the ruin, that the winning is the collapse, that the population is being bred past its own viability by the very move that pays, stays hidden behind how fit a doomed population looks, right up until it crashes.

## Yearbook

Built by [the trait](piece_the_trait.md) through [competing](process_competing.md), [spreading](process_spreading.md) and [selecting](process_selecting.md), undone only at [the refuge](place_the_refuge.md) through [restraining](process_restraining.md).
It is the phenomenon whole: the Ruin is [the edge](piece_the_edge.md) banked by all and [the ratchet](piece_the_ratchet.md) turned past reversal drawing [the viability](piece_the_viability.md) across [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md), the self-inflicted extinction at the centre of Fit to Fail, authored by no villain and driven by selection itself.
