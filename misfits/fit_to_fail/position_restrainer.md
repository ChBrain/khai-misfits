# Position: Restrainer

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The seat of restraint, where a limit is imposed from outside or selection is lifted above the individual, holding the whole against a move that genuinely wins.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A view of the whole and a lever above the individual: the sight of the viability drawn down, and the limit or higher-level selection that could hold it.
A real remedy: restraint from outside, or selection on the group, genuinely holds the trait back before it fixes.
No way to make the remedy free, and no way to hold it once and be done, because selection reinstalls the trait and each individual is rewarded for taking it.

## Orders

Hold the line.
Impose a limit no individual would choose, or lift selection to the group, and favour the survival of the whole over the margin of the one.
Forbid the winning move at the level where it wins, and pay to keep it forbidden.

## Loses

Nothing of the whole if the line holds, but everything the moment the cost is not paid, exactly because the move it forbids genuinely wins.
Because selection reinstalls the trait every generation and every individual is rewarded for taking it, the seat cannot correct the trap once, so it must impose the limit continuously against selection itself, holding each individual back from a move it would be paid for, the remedy standing only as long as the price is paid.
It holds the population's viability, and pays for the holding without end.

## Drives

Toward the limit imposed and the whole favoured,
the winning move forbidden at the level where it wins,
so the seat that holds the line keeps the viability only by paying, continuously, the cost of overriding a genuine advantage against selection that reinstalls it.
