# Pitch: The Edge Pays

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Competitive, rewarded, plain.
The plain good sense of a move that pays: the individual who presses a little harder out-competes the one who does not, leaves more offspring, wins more of the contested stock, and is rewarded for it every time it is tested.
Nothing false in it on its face: the advantage is real, the reward is banked generation on generation, and taking the edge is the fit thing to do.

## Undertow

Under the reward, a trait that raises each individual's relative fitness while lowering the whole population's viability.
Because selection sees the margin between rivals and never the level of the population, the winning trait spreads by a one-way ratchet as those without it are out-competed and must match it, fixes where no individual can afford to drop it, and is reinstalled each generation blind to the cost, so the population is bred fitter and fitter past its own viability threshold.
What makes the trait fit, that it beats the individual beside it, is what makes the population fail, that it draws down the whole they all depend on.

## Nerve

The feeling of taking the edge and winning, the advantage real and the reward banked,
sure the fit move is the right move, because it pays every time it is tested against the one beside you,
while the very pressing that lifts each above its rivals draws the whole down together, every individual fitter and the population nearer the floor.

## Echo

After the run: a population extinguished at the threshold, bred past its own viability by the trait it selected,
no one having defected and no one having cheated, only a genuine advantage taken by all and reinstalled each generation by selection blind to the whole,
and the one move that would have held the line, restraint imposed from outside or selection lifted above the individual, forgone because it forbids a move that genuinely wins to every individual that would be rewarded for making it.
