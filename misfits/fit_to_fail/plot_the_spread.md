# Plot: The Spread

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Range](place_the_range.md), a trait that wins locally and rivals who lose to it.
Each rival out-competed by the carriers, facing the choice to match the winning move or be gone.

## Action

[Spreading](process_spreading.md): [The Rival](persona_the_rival.md), holding [the rival's seat](position_rival.md), takes the trait too, matching the move that beat it and by matching removing the last holdouts.

## Stage

Each [rival](persona_the_rival.md), on [Keep Up](plan_keep_up.md), matches [the trait](piece_the_trait.md) on [the range](place_the_range.md) to hold its place against the carriers, turning [the ratchet](piece_the_ratchet.md) one notch with each match.
None takes the trait to get ahead, only to keep from being out-competed, and by keeping up each removes another who did not carry it, until the trait fixes and no individual can drop it.

## Tension

Matching a winning move to survive is forced, and the forcing is what fixes the trait for all.
There is nothing to fault: the rival cannot hold back without being out-competed to nothing. But the individual that keeps the trait beats the one that drops it, so the plot closes with the trait universal and past reversal, every individual carrying it because to drop it is to lose, the ratchet turned one way and no lever left for any single agent to pull.
