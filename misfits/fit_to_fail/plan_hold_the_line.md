# Plan: Hold the Line

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the restrainer's seat](position_restrainer.md), inside [Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Restrainer](position_restrainer.md)

## Direction

Impose a limit no individual would choose, or lift selection above the individual to the group, and favour the survival of the whole over the margin of the one. Hold the trait back before it fixes, and pay to keep it forbidden, because the move restrained genuinely wins and selection reinstalls it every generation.

## Orders

([The Restrainer](persona_the_restrainer.md) imposes restraint from outside or selects above the individual, holding each back from a winning move, at the cost of overriding a genuine advantage against selection that reinstalls it.)

## Implementation

([The Refuge](place_the_refuge.md); the [restraining](process_restraining.md) process; the limit is set on [the trait](piece_the_trait.md) before it turns [the ratchet](piece_the_ratchet.md), holding [the viability](piece_the_viability.md) above [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) at a cost paid without end.)

## Targets

- [ ] a limit no individual would choose is imposed, or selection is lifted to the group
- [ ] the survival of the whole is favoured over the margin of the one
- [ ] the trait is held back before it fixes and the threshold is not crossed
- [ ] the cost of forbidding a winning move is paid continuously, against selection
