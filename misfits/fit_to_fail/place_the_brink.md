# Place: The Brink

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). Where the population stands at the viability edge, the trait fixed and the level sunk to the threshold, the last ground before the crash.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the population stands at the edge of its own viability, the stock thinned, the level near the floor, every individual still doing the fit thing.
The trait fixed and reinstalled, the descent run to its end, the population a shade of relative fitness above its rivals and a hair of viability above the threshold below which it cannot persist.

## Holds

The population at its viability edge, and the crossing it cannot see.
Here the descent has run and the level has sunk, so the Brink holds the point where viability meets its floor: a population still fit individual against individual and no longer viable whole against its threshold, so it is where the winning move has been made all the way down and the last margin of viability stands, the crash one turn of selection away and no individual able to see it.

## Offers

A last stand of relative fitness, and no ground beyond it.
Because the Brink is where viability meets the floor, it offers the population one more generation of the fit move, each individual still rewarded against its rivals, so it hands out the last of the advantage, and hands it out over a threshold, the reward banked and the viability spent, nothing beneath the floor to catch the fall.

## Withheld

Any margin below the threshold.
The Brink shows the population still fit but cannot show it any viability beyond the floor, because the level has sunk to the edge and the trait keeps drawing it down, so it withholds the ground beyond the crossing, letting one more fit move be made over the threshold, and, all last-stand fitness and no viability left, it withholds any survival past the crash the next selection brings.
