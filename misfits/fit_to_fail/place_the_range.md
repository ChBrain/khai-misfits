# Place: The Range

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The environment and shared resource the trait exploits, where relative fitness is contested and the winning move is made and rewarded.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the individuals contend, a habitat, a market, a fishery, a common stock, where the trait is taken and the rivals are met.
The Competitor pressing harder and winning, the Rivals losing and then matching, the shared resource drawn on by all, and the reward measured against the individual beside you.

## Holds

The contest of relative fitness, and the shared resource it draws down.
Here the trait is taken and rewarded against rivals, so the Range holds the point where fitness is measured: a margin ranked individual against individual over a stock they all depend on, so it is where the winning move is banked and the population's viability drawn down at once, the gain visible in the margin and the cost invisible in the level.

## Offers

A contest to win and a resource to take.
Because the Range is where fitness is measured against rivals, it offers a real advantage to the individual who presses hardest, the reward banked and the rivals out-competed, so it hands out the win to the fitter, and hands it out on the margin and not the level, the shared stock drawn down beneath every individual reward.

## Withheld

Any sight of the whole the contest draws down.
The Range shows the margin between individuals but cannot show the level of the population, because fitness is ranked one against another and the viability is a property of all of them, so it withholds the descent beneath the reward, letting the winning move stand as vindication, and, all visible contest and no visible level, it withholds the drawing-down until the population nears the brink.
