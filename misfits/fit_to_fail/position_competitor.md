# Position: Competitor

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The seat that takes the trait raising its relative fitness, where the winning move is made and rewarded against the rivals beside it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A trait that raises relative fitness and a range to contest it on: the winning move available and the rivals to win it against.
A real advantage: the trait genuinely out-competes those without it, more offspring, more of the contested stock.
No way to see the whole from the seat, and no sign that the gain banked against a rival is the population's viability drawn down.

## Orders

Take the edge.
Take the trait that raises your relative fitness, press harder than the individual beside you, and out-compete the ones without it.
Do the fit thing, because the fit thing genuinely wins.

## Loses

The sight that its winning is the whole's losing, exactly because the advantage is measured against rivals and not against the population.
Because the reward is real and banked individual against individual, the seat cannot see the same pressing draw the viability down, so takes the edge and thins the whole, one of a population each doing the fit thing, and loses the descent to its own reward until the threshold.
It gains the advantage that genuinely wins, and loses the sight that the population is bred down by every individual winning it.

## Drives

Toward the trait taken and the reward banked,
the fit move made and rewarded against the rival beside it,
so the seat that takes the edge presses on, sure to the crash that the move that pays is the move that is right.
