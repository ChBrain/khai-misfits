# Piece: The Viability

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The population's capacity to persist, a property of the whole, lowered by the trait each individual is rewarded for taking.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Drawn down at [the range](place_the_range.md), where the shared resource is taken.
At its edge at [the brink](place_the_brink.md), where the level meets its floor.

## Load Bearing

Without it the trait costs nothing to the whole and there is nothing for selection to sink, and no crash follows.
With it the trap has its stake: the population's capacity to persist, the level of the whole rather than the margin of the one, lowered by the very trait that raises each carrier's fitness (Gyllenberg and Parvinen, on the conditions for a selected trait to drive its population extinct, the viability declining as the strategy fixes). The Viability is load bearing because it is what selection cannot see: a property of all the individuals together, drawn down by each doing the fit thing, so the piece carries the level that sinks beneath the margin, the whole's capacity to persist spent by the winning of every one.

## Apparent

Nothing, on the surface, while the margins are still won and the individuals still fit.
Seen at the surface, the Viability is invisible, because fitness is measured individual against individual and the level is a property of all of them at once, so the population looks fit while the whole thins, and the truth that the capacity to persist is sinking under every rewarded move stays hidden behind how fit each individual still is, right up until the brink.

## Yearbook

Drawn down by [the trait](piece_the_trait.md) through [competing](process_competing.md), sunk each generation by [selecting](process_selecting.md), held only at [the refuge](place_the_refuge.md).
It is the stake of the trap: the Viability is the whole's capacity to persist, lowered beneath [the edge](piece_the_edge.md) each carrier banks, sinking toward [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) whose crossing is [the ruin](piece_the_ruin.md), unseen by the selection that spends it.
