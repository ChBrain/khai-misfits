# Position: Rival

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The seat that lacks the trait and is out-competed, where keeping up means adopting the winning move and so spreading it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A place in the population without the winning trait and carriers already ahead: the losing hand and the move that would match them.
A forced choice: match the trait or be out-competed to nothing, no third option that survives.
No way to hold back without losing, and no sign that matching to survive is the turn that fixes the trait for all.

## Orders

Keep up.
Take the trait that beat you, match the winning move, and hold your place against the carriers already ahead.
Adopt it not to get ahead but to keep from being gone.

## Loses

The chance to be the holdout that keeps the trait from fixing, exactly because holding out means being out-competed.
Because the individual that carries the trait beats the one that drops it, the seat cannot afford to hold back, so it matches the move to survive and by matching removes the last who did not carry it, and loses the reversal to its own need to keep up.
It gains its survival by matching, and loses the possibility that anyone remains who does not carry the trait.

## Drives

Toward the trait matched and the place held,
the winning move adopted to keep from being gone,
so the seat that keeps up spreads the trait one more notch, sure it had no choice and right that it had none.
