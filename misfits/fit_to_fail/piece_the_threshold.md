# Piece: The Threshold

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The viability floor, the level below which the population cannot persist, whose crossing is the crash.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Approached at [the brink](place_the_brink.md), where the level sinks to the edge.
Crossed at the crash, where the population falls below the floor and collapses.

## Load Bearing

Without it the sinking viability is merely a decline that could level off, and there is no crash and no extinction.
With it the trap has its edge: the level below which the population cannot persist, the floor the descending viability crosses in one direction and does not return from (Webb, on the classification of Darwinian extinction, the population evolving across the point of no return into collapse). The Threshold is load bearing because it is where decline becomes extinction: the descent does not slow at the floor because selection cannot see it, so the population is carried across and crashes, the piece carrying the point at which the fittest move made all the way down turns from cost into ruin, sudden where the level had seemed merely to sink.

## Apparent

A distant floor, on the surface, if it is seen at all, the level still above it and the individuals still fit.
Seen at the surface, the Threshold looks far off or looks like nothing, because the viability is invisible and the margins are still won, and the truth that the descending level will cross it and not return, that the crossing is a collapse and not a slowing, stays hidden behind how fit the population still looks, right up until it falls.

## Yearbook

Approached as [the viability](piece_the_viability.md) sinks under [the trait](piece_the_trait.md), reached across the generations by [selecting](process_selecting.md), held off only at [the refuge](place_the_refuge.md).
It is the edge of the trap: the Threshold is the floor beneath [the brink](place_the_brink.md), crossed when the descent driven by [the ratchet](piece_the_ratchet.md) carries the level below it, the crossing that is [the ruin](piece_the_ruin.md), the point past which the population cannot persist.
