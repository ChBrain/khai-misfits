# Plot: The Crash

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Brink](place_the_brink.md), viability at the edge and selection still favouring the trait.
The population a hair above the floor, one more generation of the fit move ready to be made over the threshold.

## Action

The population crosses [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) below which it cannot persist and collapses, driven to extinction by the selection that rewarded every individual all the way down.

## Stage

At [the brink](place_the_brink.md), the [selector](persona_the_selector.md) reinstalls [the trait](piece_the_trait.md) one last generation and [the viability](piece_the_viability.md) crosses [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md), the descent driven by [the ratchet](piece_the_ratchet.md) carrying the level below the floor.
The population falls, and [the ruin](piece_the_ruin.md) is complete: no defector took more than its share against a rule, every carrier did the fit thing and was rewarded for it, and the extinction is authored by no villain, only by the fittest move made faithfully by all.

## Tension

Crossing the threshold is the direct work of the selected trait, and there is no cheat to blame for it.
There is nothing to fault: every individual did the fit thing and was paid exactly as selection promised. But the fittest move for the individual was the fatal move for the population, so the plot closes with the population gone, extinguished by its own selection, the ruin real and villain-less, the winning of all being the collapse of all.
