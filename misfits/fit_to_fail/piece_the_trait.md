# Piece: The Trait

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The individually-advantageous, collectively-ruinous character, the move that raises the carrier's relative fitness and lowers the population's viability at once.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Taken at [the range](place_the_range.md), where the move is made and rewarded against rivals.
Fixed across the generations, where selection reinstalls it and no individual can drop it.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no move that pays the one and costs the whole, and the population is not bred against itself.
With it the trap has its character: a heritable move that genuinely raises the carrier's relative fitness while drawing down the shared resource and lowering the population's viability, fit against a rival and fatal to the whole (Matsuda and Abrams, on the evolution of foraging effort, a trait selected for its relative advantage carrying the population toward collapse). The Trait is load bearing because it is where the two levels split: what selection rewards in the individual is what ruins the population, so the piece carries the single character that is advantageous where fitness is measured and ruinous where viability is kept, the same move winning locally and failing globally.

## Apparent

An adaptation, plainly, the fit move, the character that wins its contests and leaves more offspring.
Seen at the surface, the Trait looks like exactly what selection makes it look like, a genuine advantage, and the truth that the very move that raises the carrier's fitness lowers the population's viability, that its gain is banked against a rival and its cost paid by all, stays hidden behind how plainly the fit move pays, right up until the descent.

## Yearbook

Taken by [the competitor](persona_the_competitor.md) through [competing](process_competing.md), matched by [the rival](persona_the_rival.md), reinstalled by [the selector](persona_the_selector.md).
It is the character of the trap: the Trait confers [the edge](piece_the_edge.md) that wins locally, spreads by [the ratchet](piece_the_ratchet.md) as rivals must match, draws down [the viability](piece_the_viability.md) toward [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md), and is the seed of [the ruin](piece_the_ruin.md) the population evolves into.
