# Process: Spreading

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The trait spreading by the ratchet as rivals must match, the one-way fixing where those without it are out-competed and take it too until none can drop it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A trait that wins locally, and rivals who lose to it.
At [the range](place_the_range.md), each rival out-competed by the carriers, matching the winning move so as not to be gone.

## Direction

Toward the trait fixed and the ratchet turned.
Because the individual with the trait out-competes the one without it, spreading forces each rival to match, and because the individual that keeps the trait beats the one that drops it, once some carry it all must and once all carry it none can drop it, so the trait fixes by a one-way turn, no reversal, the last holdouts removed by their own need to keep up.

## Lever

The cost of being the holdout.
Because holding back means being out-competed to nothing while whoever keeps the trait wins, spreading runs not on any appetite for the trait but on the plain impossibility of survival without it, the fixing assembled from no choice, only from each rival matching the move to keep from being gone.

## Echo

A trait matched, and the ratchet turned one notch.
The rival has kept its place by matching, so what reads as survival is, in truth, the trait spread one further and the last holdout removed, the population nearer to universal carriage and no individual able to drop what all now hold.
