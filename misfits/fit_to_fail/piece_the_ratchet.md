# Piece: The Ratchet

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The one-way spread, once some carry the trait all must, and once all carry it none can drop it, no reversal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Turned at [the range](place_the_range.md), where each rival must match to keep up.
Locked across the generations, where the fixed trait cannot be selected back out.

## Load Bearing

Without it the trait could recede when it hurt the whole, and there would be a lever a single individual could pull.
With it the trap has its one-way turn: the individual with the trait out-competes the one without it, so each must match, and the individual that keeps it beats the one that drops it, so none can let it go (Gyllenberg and Parvinen, on the strategy fixing where no individual can afford the alternative). The Ratchet is load bearing because it takes the exit away: the trait spreads because to lack it is to lose and holds because to drop it is to lose, so the piece carries the asymmetry that lets the trait only in and never out, the reason no single agent can restrain it and the descent cannot reverse itself.

## Apparent

Keeping up, on the surface, each individual simply matching the winning move to hold its place.
Seen at the surface, the Ratchet looks like ordinary competition, everyone doing what they must, and the truth that the matching only ever adds carriers and never removes them, that the trait can enter the population but cannot leave, stays hidden behind how reasonable each individual match looks, right up until there is no one left who does not carry it.

## Yearbook

Turned by [the rival](persona_the_rival.md) through [spreading](process_spreading.md), locked by [the selector](persona_the_selector.md) through [selecting](process_selecting.md).
It is the one-way of the trap: the Ratchet spreads [the trait](piece_the_trait.md) as each must match for [the edge](piece_the_edge.md), fixes it past reversal, and drives [the viability](piece_the_viability.md) down toward [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) with no exit any individual can take.
