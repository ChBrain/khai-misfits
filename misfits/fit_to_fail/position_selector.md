# Position: Selector

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The seat of selection, where the fitter is favoured and the winning trait reinstalled each generation, blind to the population's viability.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A rule and a population to apply it to: favour the fitter, and the individuals whose relative fitness it ranks.
A perfect consistency: the higher relative fitness is favoured every generation, without error and without aim.
No sight of the level of the whole, only the margin between individuals, and no capacity to weigh what the favoured trait does to the population.

## Orders

Favour the fitter.
Keep the individuals that carry the winning trait, drop the ones that do not, and reinstall the trait each generation.
Reward relative fitness wherever it appears, because that is the rule and the rule has no exception.

## Loses

Nothing it can feel, because it wills nothing, but the population loses under it exactly because the seat sees the margin and never the level.
Because selection cannot see the viability of the whole, the seat cannot stop before the floor, so it re-chooses the self-terminating trait every generation, breeding the population fitter individual against individual and less viable whole against its threshold, the descent driven by the very rule it keeps.
It favours the fitter faithfully, and the population loses its viability to the faithfulness.

## Drives

Toward the fitter favoured and the trait reinstalled,
the winning move re-chosen every generation, blind to the whole,
so the seat that favours the fitter carries the population past its own viability, aiming at nothing and stopping at nothing before the threshold.
