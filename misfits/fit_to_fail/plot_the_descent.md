# Plot: The Descent

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Range](place_the_range.md) across the generations, the trait fixed and selection running.
The higher relative fitness there to be favoured, and the winning trait ready to be re-chosen each generation.

## Action

[Selecting](process_selecting.md): [The Selector](persona_the_selector.md), holding [the selector's seat](position_selector.md), favours the fitter and reinstalls the trait each generation, blind to the level of the whole.

## Stage

The [selector](persona_the_selector.md), on [Favour the Fitter](plan_favour_the_fitter.md), re-chooses [the trait](piece_the_trait.md) for [the edge](piece_the_edge.md) each generation, breeding the population fitter individual against individual and sinking [the viability](piece_the_viability.md) toward [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md).
Each generation the population is a shade fitter and a shade less viable, the very rule that rewards each carrier lowering the whole, no individual able to see the level and the selector unable to weigh it.

## Tension

Favouring the fitter each generation is faithful, and the faithfulness is what carries the population down.
There is nothing to fault: selection wills nothing and errs in nothing, only reinstalls the higher relative fitness. But it sees the margin and never the level, so the plot closes with the viability sunk generation on generation under the winning trait, the population bred nearer the floor with every individual still doing the fit thing and nothing in selection able to stop before it.
