# Process: Restraining

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The remedy, restraint imposed from outside or selection lifted above the individual, holding each back from a move that genuinely wins, at a cost paid without end.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A view of the whole and a lever above the individual.
At [the refuge](place_the_refuge.md), a limit imposed from outside or selection acting on the group, favouring the survival of the whole over the margin of the one.

## Direction

Toward the trait held and the viability kept.
Because the whole is favoured over the margin, restraining imposes a limit no individual would choose or selects at the group level, holding the trait back before it fixes and keeping the viability above the threshold, and because the move it forbids genuinely wins and selection reinstalls it every generation, the limit must be imposed continuously, the line holding only as long as the cost is paid.

## Lever

The willingness to pay against the advantage.
Because the move restrained is a real advantage that each individual would be rewarded for taking, restraining runs not on correcting any fault but on overriding a genuine win, the remedy assembled from no free repair, only from the cost of forbidding the fit move levied without end against selection that reinstalls it.

## Echo

A line held, and a price paid without end.
The whole has been favoured over the margin, so what reads as a fix is, in truth, a restraint standing only while it is paid for, the viability kept and the advantage overridden, the moment the cost is set down the ratchet turning again.
