# Place: The Refuge

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The remedy place, where restraint is imposed or higher-level selection protects viability, the whole favoured over the margin at a cost paid without end.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place restraint is imposed from outside or selection acts above the individual, a quota, a covenant, a group whose survival is favoured over the margin of the one.
The Restrainer setting the limit, holding each individual back from a move it would be rewarded for, the trait held before it fixes and the viability kept above the threshold.

## Holds

The limit on the winning move, and the cost of keeping it.
Here the whole is favoured over the margin, so the Refuge holds the point where restraint stands against selection: a limit no individual would choose, imposed from outside or selected at the group level, so it is where the trait is held back before it fixes and where the price of forbidding a genuine advantage is levied, the viability kept only as long as the cost is paid.

## Offers

A held line and a persisting whole.
Because the Refuge is where restraint is imposed, it offers a population above its threshold, the trait held back and the viability kept, so it hands out survival to the whole, and hands it out against the margin, each individual forbidden a move it would win by, the persistence real and bought continuously.

## Withheld

Any escape from the cost.
The Refuge shows the whole persisting but cannot show the restraint holding for free, because selection reinstalls the trait every generation and every individual is rewarded for taking it, so it withholds any correction that stands once and is done, letting the line hold only while the price is paid, and, all held viability and no free repair, it withholds the moment the cost could ever be set down.
