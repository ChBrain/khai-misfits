# Plan: Favour the Fitter

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the selector's seat](position_selector.md), inside [Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Selector](position_selector.md)

## Direction

Favour the higher relative fitness, every generation. Keep the individuals that carry the winning trait, drop the ones that do not, and reinstall the trait each generation, blind to the level of the whole, because the rule sees the margin between individuals and never the viability of the population.

## Orders

([The Selector](persona_the_selector.md) reinstalls the winning trait each generation, favouring the fitter without error and without aim, blind to what the trait does to the population's viability.)

## Implementation

([The Range](place_the_range.md) across the generations; the [selecting](process_selecting.md) process; [the trait](piece_the_trait.md) is re-chosen for [the edge](piece_the_edge.md) each generation, [the viability](piece_the_viability.md) sinking under the very rule that rewards each carrier.)

## Targets

- [ ] the higher relative fitness is favoured each generation
- [ ] the winning trait is reinstalled, carriers kept and non-carriers dropped
- [ ] the level of the whole is unseen, only the margin between individuals ranked
- [ ] the viability sinks under the rule, the population bred toward the threshold
