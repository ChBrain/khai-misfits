# Persona: The Selector

## Taxonomy

[Selector](position_selector.md). Selection itself, which each generation favours and reinstalls the winning trait, blind to the population's viability. The archetype of the force that rewards the fitter and cannot see the whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Impartial. Consistent. Rewarding the fitter.
Is selection as a force, the differential survival and reproduction that favours whatever leaves more offspring, the market that rewards the firm that outgrows, the sorting that keeps the winner and drops the rest.
No malice in it and no aim: the Selector wills nothing, it only favours the fitter, faithfully, every generation.

## Action

Favours the fitter, reinstalling the winning trait each generation, keeping the individuals that carry it and dropping the ones that do not.
Rewards relative fitness wherever it appears, so re-chooses the trait every time it is tested, blind to what the trait does to the whole.
Does what selection does: it favours the higher relative fitness, and re-selects the same trait generation on generation, seeing the margin and never the level.

## Shadow

Not a villain, a faithful favourer of the fitter, and that is the whole difficulty: it reinstalls the winning trait with no error and no aim, and the trait it reinstalls is the one that draws the population down.
Because selection sees only the margin between individuals and never the viability of the population, the Selector cannot stop before the floor, so it re-chooses the self-terminating trait every generation, breeding the population fitter individual against individual and less viable whole against its threshold, the very force that rewards each carrier the force that drives the descent.
It favours the fitter faithfully, and the faithful favouring is what carries the population past its own viability.

## Tell

Says nothing, and reinstalls the trait anyway.
Wills nothing and aims at nothing, so cannot be blamed and cannot be reasoned with, only favours the fitter each generation, blind to the whole, re-choosing the winning move all the way to the threshold.
