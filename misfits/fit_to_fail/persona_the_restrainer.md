# Persona: The Restrainer

## Taxonomy

[Restrainer](position_restrainer.md). The reformer who would impose restraint from outside or select above the individual, at cost, holding each back from a winning move. The archetype of the remedy the run resists.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Prudent. Outside the contest. Willing to pay.
Is the regulator setting a quota, the covenant above the individuals, the group-level selection that favours the survival of the whole, the one who would forbid the fit move to save the population.
No moralising in it: the Restrainer does not call the Competitor wrong, it only holds the whole against a move that genuinely wins.

## Action

Imposes a limit no individual would choose, or lifts selection above the individual to the group, so the survival of the whole is favoured over the margin of the one, holding the trait back before it fixes.
Sets the quota, writes the covenant, protects the population from the trait that each individual is rewarded for taking.
Does what the remedy requires: it forbids the winning move at the level where it wins, and pays the cost of overriding a genuine advantage.

## Shadow

Not a saviour, a reformer forbidding a real advantage, and that is the whole difficulty: the move it holds back genuinely wins, so restraining it costs, and the cost never ends.
Because selection reinstalls the trait every generation and every individual is rewarded for taking it, the Restrainer cannot correct the trap once and be done, so it must impose the limit continuously against selection itself, holding each individual back from a move it would be paid for, the remedy real but standing only as long as the cost is paid, never a free repair.
It forbids the move that would ruin the whole, and the forbidding is a price levied without end on every individual that would win by making it.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "hold back, though it costs you the win," and is asking each to lose a real advantage.
Says it against selection that reinstalls the trait and against individuals rewarded for taking it, so holds the line only by paying continuously, knowing the moment the restraint lifts the ratchet turns again.
