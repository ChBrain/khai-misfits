# Process: Competing

## Taxonomy

[Fit to Fail](play_fit_to_fail.md). The Competitor taking the edge, the winning move made and rewarded against rivals, the genuine advantage that lifts the individual and lowers the whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A move that pays, present on the range.
At [the range](place_the_range.md), an individual taking the trait that raises its relative fitness and pressing harder than the one beside it.

## Direction

Toward the edge taken and the reward banked.
Because the trait raises relative fitness, competing takes it and out-competes the rivals without it, and because the advantage is genuine the reward is real and banked individual against individual, so the winning move reads as the right move, the gain measured against a rival and the population's viability drawn down unseen beneath it.

## Lever

The reality of the advantage.
Because the trait genuinely out-competes those without it and the reward is banked every time it is tested, competing runs not on any greed or cheat but on the plain fitness of the move, the descent assembled from no fault, only from each individual taking the edge that genuinely pays against the one beside it.

## Echo

An edge taken, and the whole drawn down.
The individual has done the fit thing and been rewarded for it, so what reads as winning is, in truth, the population's viability lowered by the winning, the gain banked and the cost paid by all, each competitor sure the move that pays is the move that is right.
