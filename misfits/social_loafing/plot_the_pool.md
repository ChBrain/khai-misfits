# Plot: The Pool

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The group grade](place_the_group_grade.md), a task set for the team and its output to be scored as a whole.
Many hands, one result, and the choice of whether to measure the sum or the parts.

## Action

[Pooling](process_pooling.md): [The Grader](persona_the_grader.md), holding [the manager's seat](position_manager.md), hands the task to the group and takes the sum as the result, no line drawn to any hand.

## Stage

[The Grader](persona_the_grader.md) tables [Grade the Group](plan_grade_the_group.md): reward the whole, one mark for all, in the name of collaboration, so [the pool](piece_the_pool.md) folds every contribution into a sum that cannot be broken back into hands.
The grade is fair and even and warmly meant, and it prices slack and effort exactly the same.

## Tension

The fairness is real, and it is the cover.
Scoring the team rather than the person feels collaborative and generous, and is, so nothing here is a mistake. But by measuring the pool the group makes each pull invisible, and an invisible pull is one that can ease with nothing to show for it, so the collegial grade lays, in perfect good faith, the precondition for the loafing to come.
