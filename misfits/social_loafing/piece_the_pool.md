# Piece: The Pool

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The merged output in which no single contribution can be identified.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the group grade](place_the_group_grade.md), in the one result scored for all.
And on [the rope](place_the_rope.md), in the single force at the end of many hands.

## Load Bearing

Without it every effort would show, and slack would show with it, so each would pull its weight.
With it the contributions merge into a sum that cannot be broken back into hands, so easing leaves no trace and pulling earns no mark, and the whole misfit has room to form. The Pool is load bearing because it is the un-identifiability the effect requires (Latané et al.): social loafing appears exactly when a contribution is combined and cannot be individually assessed, so the pool is not a backdrop but the precondition, the thing that turns a private effort into a hidden one.

## Apparent

Collaboration, and fairness.
It looks like the good of teamwork: shared credit, a common result, no one singled out. Pooling the output feels like the collegial thing to do, which is why it is done in good faith and never seen as the cover it is.

## Yearbook

Made by [the grader](persona_the_grader.md) through [pooling](process_pooling.md), and relied on by [the loafer](persona_the_loafer.md).
It is the precondition of the misfit: the Pool hides the hand, and a hidden hand is a hand free to [ease the pull](piece_the_slack.md) with nothing to show for it.
