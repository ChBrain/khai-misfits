# Position: Group Member

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The seat that pulls as one of many, its grip hidden in the sum.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A place in the group and a share of the work.
An output pooled with the others, where no single pull can be traced.
The ordinary wish to take part and not to be a shirker.

## Orders

Pitch in, pull with the team, take part in the shared work.
Give what the moment seems to ask, in a group where the asking is diffuse.
Trust that everyone is pulling their weight, as it is trusting.

## Loses

The visibility that once kept its effort honest.
Alone, its slack would show; in the pool it shows nothing, so the effort settles to the level a hidden, dispensable hand is worth, without any decision to slack.
The reason to pull full is what the pool takes away.

## Drives

Toward the effort of a hand that cannot be seen,
which is a little less than it would give alone,
eased quietly and sincerely and never felt as shirking.
