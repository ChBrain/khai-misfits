# Pitch: Many Hands

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Warm, collaborative, all in it together.
The good feeling of a team pulling as one, everyone pitching in, many hands making light work.
The voice of a group that likes itself and its togetherness, spoken by people who really are taking part.

## Undertow

Under the togetherness, each grip easing a shade it thinks no one can feel.
The pull is summed and no single hand can be picked out of the sum, so slack costs nothing that shows, and the effort quietly settles to what a hidden, dispensable hand is worth.
Nobody shirks: they only relax, together, into a pool that hides the relaxing.

## Nerve

The feeling of contributing, genuinely,
each easing small enough to be invisible and reasonable,
the total quietly less than the same hands would have made apart.

## Echo

After the run: a group that pulled less than its parts,
a hard worker worn down by carrying the rest for an equal share,
and an effort that slackened not from any shirker but from every hand it hid.
