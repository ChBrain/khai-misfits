# Place: The Group Grade

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). Where the pooled output is scored, one mark for all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A single result, a shared reward, one grade written across every name.
The team's output measured whole, and split evenly back.

## Holds

The un-identifiability made institutional.
Here the choice to score the pool rather than the person turns the hidden ease into a paying strategy, because slack and effort earn exactly the same mark.

## Offers

Fairness, and team spirit.
Equal credit for a shared job, no one singled out, the warm even hand of a manager who believes in collaboration.

## Withheld

The link from reward to effort.
The grade attaches to the group and never to the pull, so the hardest hand and the softest are paid alike, and the incentive to pull full is scored at nothing.
