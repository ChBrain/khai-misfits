# Process: Naming

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The pool broken back into hands, each pull made visible.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone who sees that what cannot be seen is what slackens.
The move to make each contribution identifiable and each part felt to matter.

## Direction

The sum split back into named pulls, the group shrunk, the effort tied to the person.
Identifiability restored, so easing shows again and pulling counts again.

## Lever

Being seen, the plain fact that a traced effort is a fuller one.
Name the hand and the ease it could take in the pool costs it something in the open, so the pull returns.

## Echo

Effort lifted because it can be watched.
A team turned into scored individuals ... and a chill on the group warmth, so the naming holds only against the constant pull to pool the credit and be a team again.
