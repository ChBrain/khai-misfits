# Plan: Ease the Pull

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the group member's seat](position_group_member.md), inside [Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Group Member](position_group_member.md)

## Direction

Give what the moment seems to ask, in a group where the asking is diffuse. The pull is pooled and no single hand can be traced, so the effort settles to the level a hidden, dispensable grip is worth: a shade less than alone, unseen and unmeant.

## Orders

([The Loafer](persona_the_loafer.md) eases the pull a shade, feeling one hand among many can hardly matter.)

## Implementation

([The Rope](place_the_rope.md); the [easing](process_easing.md) process; [the slack](piece_the_slack.md) leaves no trace, because [the dispensable hand](piece_the_dispensable_hand.md) feels too small to matter.)

## Targets

- [ ] the pull is eased a shade below the solo effort
- [ ] the ease leaves no trace in the pooled output
- [ ] the hand feels dispensable and settles accordingly
- [ ] the slack is never felt as shirking, only as being on a team
