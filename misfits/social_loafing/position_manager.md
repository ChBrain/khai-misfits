# Position: Manager

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The seat that assigns to the group and scores the pool.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The task to hand out and the result to measure.
The choice to grade the group or the person.
A belief that shared credit builds a shared team.

## Orders

Set the work for the group and take the sum as its result.
Reward the whole, one mark for all, in the name of collaboration.
Build spirit by pooling the credit.

## Loses

The trace from output back to effort.
By scoring the pool it makes each hand invisible, so easing costs nothing and hauling earns nothing, and the fair, equal grade becomes the exact cover the loafing needs.
Its fairness is what dissolves the pull.

## Drives

Toward a team measured as a team,
which feels collaborative and is,
and which quietly prices every hidden ease at zero.
