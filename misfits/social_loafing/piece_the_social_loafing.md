# Piece: The Social Loafing

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The phenomenon whole: effort falling because it is pooled and dispensable, held whole by nobody.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across the whole run, from [the rope](place_the_rope.md) to [the name tag](place_the_name_tag.md).
And wherever effort is combined into a sum in which no single hand can be seen.

## Load Bearing

Without it the parts are a scatter: a pool, an ease, a dispensable feeling, a sucker's retreat, a curve, a loss.
With it they are one law: pool effort where no hand can be seen or felt to matter, and each hand eases, so the group falls below its members and falls further as it grows. The Social Loafing is load bearing because it is the whole read at once (Latané et al.; Karau & Williams), and it names the only exit: not more team spirit but identifiability, contributions named and made to matter and the group kept small, so effort seen is effort restored. It is the quiet counterpart of shirking, a slackening no one chooses and no one can be caught at.

## Apparent

A team, collaborating.
It looks like people working together in the ordinary way: participating, pitching in, sharing the load and the credit. Nothing on the surface is a failure, and that is the whole of it, because the effort is lost not to any shirker but to every hand the pool made invisible.

## Yearbook

Held whole by nobody: assembled from [the pool](piece_the_pool.md), [the slack](piece_the_slack.md), and [the ringelmann curve](piece_the_ringelmann_curve.md), and answered only by [the namer](persona_the_namer.md).
It is the misfit named: effort that cannot be seen or felt to count quietly falls, so a group is worth less than its parts until each hand is named and made to matter again.
