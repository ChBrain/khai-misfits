# Process: Easing

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The quiet fall of effort to the level a hidden hand is worth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A pull that cannot be seen and a hand that feels dispensable.
The moment effort is pooled and easing off carries no visible cost.

## Direction

The grip relaxing a shade, unmeant and unmarked, toward what a hidden contribution is worth.
Not a decision to shirk: a settling that no one, including the hand, quite notices.

## Lever

The absence of a watcher, and the sense that one pull among many can hardly matter.
Take away the trace from effort to person, and effort drifts down to where it is no longer worth the giving.

## Echo

A hand pulling less than it would alone, and sure it is pulling its weight.
The rope a little slacker for every grip, and no slack anywhere that can be pointed to.
