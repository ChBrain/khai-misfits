# Plot: The Dilution

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The group task](place_the_group_task.md), the work behind and more hands to be thrown at it.
A shortfall answered with headcount, on a rope already pooled and easing.

## Action

[Diluting](process_diluting.md): [The Piler](persona_the_piler.md), holding [the recruiter's seat](position_recruiter.md), adds hands to help, while [The Sucker](persona_the_sucker.md), tired of hauling the easers for a shared credit, finally eases too.

## Stage

[The Piler](persona_the_piler.md) tables [Add More Hands](plan_add_more_hands.md): grow the group to grow the output, so [the ringelmann curve](piece_the_ringelmann_curve.md) drops the pull of each as the crowd swells.
[The Sucker](persona_the_sucker.md) tables [Stop Carrying](plan_stop_carrying.md), raising [the sucker's guard](piece_the_suckers_guard.md), and with the last full hand eased, [the process loss](piece_the_process_loss.md) opens to its widest.

## Tension

More hands, and less work each, and the one holdout gives way.
Adding people looks like adding strength, and declining to be the sucker looks like simple fairness, so both moves are reasonable and both make it worse. The per-head pull sinks under the growing crowd, the last diligent hand refuses to subsidise the rest, and the group, now at its largest, delivers least per person and furthest below what its members could.
