# Plot: The Name

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The name tag](place_the_name_tag.md), where someone has seen that what cannot be seen is what slackens.
The pool to be broken back into hands, each pull tied to its person.

## Action

[Naming](process_naming.md): [The Namer](persona_the_namer.md), holding [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), names the contribution, shrinks the group, and makes each part felt to matter.

## Stage

[The Namer](persona_the_namer.md) tables [Name the Contribution](plan_name_the_contribution.md): tie the effort to the person and keep the group small, so [the social loafing](piece_the_social_loafing.md) lifts because an effort that can be seen is an effort restored.
Each hand made visible finds its ease now costs it something in the open, and the pull comes back.

## Tension

The one move that works spends the warmth it was all built on.
Naming each hand restores the effort, but only by turning a team into scored individuals, so it lands as surveillance and a chill on the group feeling, and the very togetherness that pooled the credit resents being broken up. It holds while it is held ... or the naming is judged to spoil the team spirit, the credit is pooled again, and the pull settles back into the anonymity that hides it.
