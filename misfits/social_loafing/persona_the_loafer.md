# Persona: The Loafer

## Taxonomy

[Group Member](position_group_member.md). The hand that eases, because the pull is hidden in the pool.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Willing, agreeable, part of the team.
Turns up, takes part, would never call itself a slacker, and is not one.
Feels like a contributor, because it is contributing.

## Action

Eases the pull a shade, unseen.
Gives the level of effort a hidden, dispensable hand is worth, no more.
Settles, without deciding to, below what it would give alone.

## Shadow

Its ease is invisible even to itself.
The output is pooled, so the slack leaves no trace, and one grip among many feels too small to matter, so the relaxing never registers as shirking, only as being on a team.
Not lazy: merely unwatched, and unwatched effort drifts down.

## Tell

"We are all in this together."
Said while pulling a little less than it would alone.
