# Position: Conscientious Member

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The seat that pulls hard, and carries the easers for its trouble.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A conscience that will not let the work drop.
The strength and the will to pull full and cover the slack.
A share of the credit no larger than anyone else's.

## Orders

Pull your weight and then the weight the others let fall.
Keep the work up when the rope goes soft, at first.
Hold the standard the pool would otherwise lower.

## Loses

The fairness of its own effort.
Carrying the group for an equal share makes it the sucker, doing more for the same, so the diligence is a subsidy to the coasters, and no one subsidises others forever.
Punished for pulling hard by being made to pull hardest.

## Drives

Toward the work done, at first, and its own fair share, in the end,
until being the sap costs more than easing off,
and the last hard hand eases too.
