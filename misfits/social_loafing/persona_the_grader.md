# Persona: The Grader

## Taxonomy

[Manager](position_manager.md). The one who assigns to the group and measures the pool.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fair-minded, team-oriented, keen on collaboration.
Sets the work for the group and scores the group, believing shared credit builds shared spirit.
Sees a team, and measures a team.

## Action

Hands the task to the group and takes the sum as the result.
Grades the pooled output, one mark for all, no line drawn to any single hand.
Rewards the whole and never traces the parts.

## Shadow

Its fairness is the very thing that hides the slack.
By measuring the pool it makes each contribution invisible, so easing costs nothing and pulling hard earns nothing extra, and the equal grade it gives in good faith is the cover the loafing needs.
Builds the pool that dissolves the effort.

## Tell

"Great teamwork, everyone."
Awarded to a sum it cannot break back into hands.
