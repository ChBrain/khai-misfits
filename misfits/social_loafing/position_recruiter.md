# Position: Recruiter

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The seat that answers a shortfall with more hands.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The power to add people to the group.
The intuition that more hands mean more done.
A struggling task and a headcount to throw at it.

## Orders

Grow the group when the work is behind.
Add hands to the rope and expect the pull to grow with them.
Meet a shortfall of output with a surplus of people.

## Loses

The per-head effort of everyone already pulling.
Each added hand makes every hand matter less and hide better, so the pull per person falls as the group grows, and the total gains little for all the added arms.
Deepens the dilution by treating it as a shortage of people.

## Drives

Toward a bigger group and a smaller share each,
sure the sum will rise with the count,
while the per-head pull sinks beneath the crowd it keeps adding.
