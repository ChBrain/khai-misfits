# Plot: The Slack

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The rope](place_the_rope.md), the effort pooled and a single grip impossible to pick from the sum.
A hand free to relax a shade with nothing to show for it, and a feeling that one pull among many can hardly matter.

## Action

[Easing](process_easing.md): [The Loafer](persona_the_loafer.md), holding [the group member's seat](position_group_member.md), eases the pull a shade unseen, while [The Sucker](persona_the_sucker.md) at [the conscientious member's seat](position_conscientious_member.md) hauls harder to take up the drop.

## Stage

[The Loafer](persona_the_loafer.md) tables [Ease the Pull](plan_ease_the_pull.md): give the level a hidden hand is worth, so [the slack](piece_the_slack.md) settles below the solo effort and [the dispensable hand](piece_the_dispensable_hand.md) makes the easing feel like nothing.
The Sucker covers the drop for now, doing the loafer's share for the loafer's equal credit, and the rope holds only on its back.

## Tension

No one shirks, and the pull quietly falls.
Every ease is small, invisible, and reasonable, taken by a hand that feels dispensable and sure it is still a team player, so there is nothing to point at and no one to blame. The effort settles below what each would give alone, and the only hand still pulling full is the one being quietly punished for it.
