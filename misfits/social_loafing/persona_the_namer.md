# Persona: The Namer

## Taxonomy

[Reformer](position_reformer.md). The one who makes each hand visible and each part matter.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Clear, a little cold to the team spirit, focused on the work.
Sees that what cannot be seen is what slackens, and moves to make the effort visible.
Willing to trade some togetherness for some accountability.

## Action

Names the contribution, shrinks the group, ties the effort to the person.
Breaks the pool back into hands, so each pull can be seen and each part felt to matter.
Restores the effort by restoring the being-seen.

## Shadow

Its cure spends the easy, equal warmth of the pool.
Making each hand visible turns a team into a set of scored individuals, so the naming lands as surveillance and a chill on the group spirit, and one complaint that it spoils the team can close the pool back over.
Right, unloved, and easily reversed.

## Tell

"Who did which part?"
The question the pool was built so no one would have to answer.
