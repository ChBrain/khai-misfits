# Plan: Grade the Group

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the manager's seat](position_manager.md), inside [Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Manager](position_manager.md)

## Direction

Set the work for the group and take the sum as its result. Reward the whole, one mark for all, in the name of collaboration, and build spirit by pooling the credit. Measure a team, because a team is what you see.

## Orders

([The Grader](persona_the_grader.md) assigns the task to the team and scores the pooled output, no line drawn to any hand.)

## Implementation

([The Group Grade](place_the_group_grade.md); the [pooling](process_pooling.md) process; [the pool](piece_the_pool.md) hides each contribution in the sum.)

## Targets

- [ ] the task is set for the group, not the person
- [ ] the pooled output is scored as one
- [ ] no contribution is traced back to a hand
- [ ] the equal grade becomes the cover the loafing needs
