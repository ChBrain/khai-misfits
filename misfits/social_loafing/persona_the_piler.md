# Persona: The Piler

## Taxonomy

[Recruiter](position_recruiter.md). The one who adds more hands, and lowers the pull of each.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Helpful, resourceful, sure that more people mean more done.
Answers a struggling group by growing it, because many hands make light work.
Believes headcount and output rise together.

## Action

Adds hands to the rope to help.
Grows the group whenever the work is behind, confident the total will grow with it.
Piles on people and waits for the extra pull.

## Shadow

Each hand it adds makes every hand matter less and hide better.
More people means a smaller share of the credit and a smaller share of the blame per grip, so the per-head pull falls as the group grows, and the total barely moves for all the added arms.
Solves a shortfall by deepening the very thing that caused it.

## Tell

"Let us put more people on it."
Said to a problem that more people quietly make worse.
