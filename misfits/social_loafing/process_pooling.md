# Process: Pooling

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The merging of contributions into one sum where no hand can be told from the rest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A task set for the group and scored as a whole.
The decision to measure the team, not the person, and to reward the sum.

## Direction

Individual pulls combined into a single output, and split evenly back.
Effort and slack folded together, indistinguishable, into one result and one grade.

## Lever

Un-identifiability: what cannot be traced cannot be held to account.
Once a contribution disappears into the pool, easing costs nothing that shows and pulling earns nothing extra.

## Echo

A shared result that hides every hand behind it.
An equal grade over unequal effort, and the exact cover under which a grip can slacken unseen.
