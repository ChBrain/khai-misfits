# Plan: Stop Carrying

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the conscientious member's seat](position_conscientious_member.md), inside [Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Conscientious Member](position_conscientious_member.md)

## Direction

Pull full and cover the slack, at first, and hold the standard the pool would lower. But carrying the easers for an equal share is being the sucker, and no one hauls for others forever, so rather than be the sap, ease off at last and match the rope.

## Orders

([The Sucker](persona_the_sucker.md) hauls harder to cover the drop, then, tired of subsidising the coasters, eases too.)

## Implementation

([The Rope](place_the_rope.md); the [easing](process_easing.md) process it finally joins; [the sucker's guard](piece_the_suckers_guard.md) is the pull-back that refuses to be the sap.)

## Targets

- [ ] the slack is covered by extra effort, at first
- [ ] the extra effort earns only the same shared credit
- [ ] being the sucker is felt as the cost of pulling hard
- [ ] the last hard hand eases, rather than carry the rest
