# Place: The Rope

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). Where many hands pull one line and each pull is summed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A tug-of-war line, eight hands on it, one combined force at the end.
Ringelmann's own image: bodies straining, and a total that is only the sum.

## Holds

The hidden ease.
Here a single grip cannot be told from the others, so a hand can relax a shade with nothing to show for it, and the rope reports only the whole.

## Offers

Shared effort, and the feel of pulling together.
A common load, a common goal, the camaraderie of a line of people leaning back as one.

## Withheld

The reading of any single hand.
The rope measures force in sum and never in parts, so it cannot say who pulled hard and who eased, which is exactly the cover under which the easing happens.
