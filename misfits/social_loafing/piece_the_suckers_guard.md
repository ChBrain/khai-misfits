# Piece: The Sucker's Guard

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The hard worker easing off rather than carry the coasters for an equal share.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

In the conscientious hand on [the rope](place_the_rope.md), the moment it feels itself hauling the rest.
At the tipping point where pulling full stops being pride and starts being a subsidy.

## Load Bearing

Without it the diligent would go on covering the slack, and the group might hold up on their backs.
With it even the willing pull back, because carrying the easers for the same reward is being the sucker, and no one hauls for others forever, so the last full pull eases too and the floor drops out. The Sucker's Guard is load bearing because it is why loafing spreads rather than settles (Kerr & Bruun): the sucker effect turns a few easers into all of them, since the fair-minded reduce effort not from sloth but to refuse an unfair share, and the group converges downward.

## Apparent

Fairness, finally claimed.
It looks like a reasonable person declining to be exploited: why should I do more for the same? That is not shirking but justice, which is exactly why it is unanswerable, and why it carries the last hard worker down into the ease with the rest.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the sucker](persona_the_sucker.md) when it moves to [stop carrying](plan_stop_carrying.md), against [the pool](piece_the_pool.md) that pays it no more than the coasters.
It is the misfit's ratchet down: the Sucker's Guard removes the last hand holding the effort up, so [the slack](piece_the_slack.md) becomes the whole rope's, not a few hands'.
