# Persona: The Sucker

## Taxonomy

[Conscientious Member](position_conscientious_member.md). The one who pulls hard, and carries the rest for it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent, conscientious, unwilling to let the work drop.
Pulls its full weight and then some, covering the slack it feels in the rope.
Takes pride in carrying the load.

## Action

Hauls harder to make up the group's ease, at first.
Does the work the easers do not, for the same shared credit they receive.
Watches the pool take its extra effort and give it away to all.

## Shadow

Its diligence is a subsidy to the coasters, and it knows.
Carrying the group for an equal share is being the sucker, and no one hauls for others forever, so at last, rather than be the sap, it eases too, and the last hard hand joins the soft ones.
Punished for pulling, until it stops.

## Tell

"Why am I the only one doing this?"
The question that comes just before it, too, lets go.
