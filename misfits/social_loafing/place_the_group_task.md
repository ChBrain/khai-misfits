# Place: The Group Task

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). Where the shared work is done and hands are added to it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A project assigned to a team, a roster that can grow, a deadline met by numbers.
The place where a shortfall is answered by more people.

## Holds

The dilution.
Here adding hands lowers the pull of each, because every new grip makes the others matter less and hide better, so the group grows and the per-head effort sinks.

## Offers

Capacity, apparently.
More people to spread the load, a bigger team for a bigger job, the reassuring sense that arms and output rise together.

## Withheld

The output the numbers promised.
The task returns less per head with every hand added, so the total lags the headcount, and the room where the group was grown to do more is the room where each does less.
