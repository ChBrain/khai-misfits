# Position: Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The seat that makes each hand visible and each part matter.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The authority to name contributions and shrink the group.
The lever of identifiability, and of a task made to feel it matters.
The unpopularity of the one who ends the anonymous ease.

## Orders

Break the pool back into hands and tie the effort to the person.
Keep the group small, name the parts, make each input felt to matter.
Restore the being-seen that keeps the pull honest.

## Loses

The easy, equal warmth of the pool.
Making each hand visible turns a team into scored individuals, so the fix reads as surveillance and a chill on the group spirit, and one complaint that it spoils the team can close the pool back over the hands.
Buys the effort back at the price of the togetherness.

## Drives

Toward an effort held up by being seen,
bought by trading anonymity for accountability,
and never more than one complaint from being reversed.
