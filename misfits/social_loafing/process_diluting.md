# Process: Diluting

## Taxonomy

[Social Loafing](play_social_loafing.md). The per-head pull falling as the group grows.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A shortfall answered with more hands.
Each new grip added to a rope already pooled and unidentifiable.

## Direction

The effort per person sinking as the headcount climbs.
Every added hand a smaller share of the credit and the blame, so every hand matters less and hides better.

## Lever

Group size itself, the Ringelmann decline: the larger the crowd, the thinner each contribution and the safer each ease.
More people is not more accountability spread thin but less accountability felt by each.

## Echo

A bigger group pulling barely more than a smaller one.
The total lagging the count, and the gap between what the group could do and what it does opening wider with every hand added.
