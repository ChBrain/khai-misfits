# Persona: The Holder

## Taxonomy

[Sandbagger](position_sandbagger.md). The old hand who hits the number exactly and parks the rest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Calm, canny, an old hand who has seen every cycle.
He knows what a record costs the man who posts it.
So he keeps his powder dry, and his surplus darker still.

## Action

He makes the number to the decimal, no more.
He keeps the extra capacity behind the line, unbooked.
He carries the strength quietly, ready and unseen.

## Shadow

Hitting exactly hides capability from the books.
So the ledger measures the sandbag, never the strength beneath it.
The plan is built on a number he chose, not a limit he found.

## Tell

"We will come in right on target."

He always does. Exactly on target, and not a decimal past it.
