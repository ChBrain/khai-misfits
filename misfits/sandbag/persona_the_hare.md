# Persona: The Hare

## Taxonomy

[Rate-Buster](position_rate_buster.md). The eager one who over-delivers in the open and gets cooled.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Eager, fast, a little naive about the room.
He runs because running is what he is for.
He does not yet feel the eyes on his pace.

## Action

He sprints in full view, the whole floor watching.
He posts well above the pace, proud of the gap.
He feels the chill come off the people beside him.

## Shadow

His sprint raises the bar for everyone in the room.
So the group closes ranks and cools him back into line.
The pace is set by the many, and the many will defend it.

## Tell

"Why is everyone going so slow?"

He asks it once, honestly. Then he learns, or he leaves.
