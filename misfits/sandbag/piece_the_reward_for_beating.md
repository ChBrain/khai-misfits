# Piece: The Reward for Beating

## Taxonomy

[Sandbag](play_sandbag.md). The prize for over-performance: a harder target, paid by the winner.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the review](place_the_review.md), in the handshake that congratulates a record and raises the quota in the same breath.
The payoff for exceeding: praise now, and a bigger number forever, the applause and the penalty delivered together and by the same hand.

## Load Bearing

Without it beating the target pays and everyone reaches.
With it beating the target costs, because the Reward for Beating is punishment wearing the face of praise: the person who over-delivers is thanked, and then handed the higher baseline their over-delivery just proved possible, so the true price of a record is every future year spent defending it. It is the incentive turned inside out, where the visible reward is real and the hidden cost is larger, and a rational person subtracts the second from the first and stops beating the number.

## Apparent

Recognition and a stretch goal.
It looks like a team rewarding its best and asking for more, the natural way to treat a strong performer. The sting is deferred and disguised: the raise in the target does not feel like the cost of the praise, it feels like a separate act of ambition, so the person learns the lesson without ever being able to point to the moment they were taught it.

## Yearbook

Handed out by [the setter](persona_the_setter.md), swallowed once by [the beater](persona_the_beater.md) and never again.
It is the play's lesson delivered: the Reward for Beating is what converts [the beater](persona_the_beater.md) into [the holder](persona_the_holder.md), and teaches the whole room that the safe place for excellence is [hidden](piece_the_hidden_capacity.md).
