# Plot: True Ceiling

## Taxonomy

[Sandbag](play_sandbag.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The review](place_the_review.md), a decade of numbers laid out, every one of them met.
Someone asks the simple question the record cannot answer: not did we hit the target, but what could this outfit actually do.

## Action

[Target-Setting](process_target_setting.md), looked at from above: [The Setter](persona_the_setter.md), holding [the target-setter's seat](position_target_setter.md), runs back through years of clean, exact hits and finds not a measure of the outfit but a history of its negotiations with itself.

## Stage

[The Setter](persona_the_setter.md) reads the flat, faithful line and sees at last what it is: not the shape of what the team can do but the shape of what it judged safe to show, so [the true ceiling](piece_the_true_ceiling.md) stands revealed as the one height the record was built never to touch.
[The ratchet effect](piece_the_ratchet_effect.md) is named: an incentive meant to drive performance that taught a decade of people to cap it, and left the outfit blind to its own limit.

## Tension

Every target was met, and no one knows what the team is worth.
There is no failure in the file to point at, no missed number, no scandal: the record is a wall of green, and that is precisely the wound, because a wall of green is what you get from a team performing to its limit and from a team hiding its limit, and nothing in the numbers tells the two apart. The outfit has spent ten years steering by a figure it chose to be beatable, and the cost is not a target dropped but a potential never learned ... or no one asks, the wall of green is filed as success, and next year's number is set from the last, just the same.
