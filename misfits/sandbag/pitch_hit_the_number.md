# Pitch: Hit the Number

## Taxonomy

[Sandbag](play_sandbag.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Prudent, steady, quietly wise.
Make the number, every time, and never make a fuss of it: no records, no misses, a clean line of exact hits year on year.
The voice of an old hand who has learned the game, calm and unhurried, taking the target seriously and no more than that.

## Undertow

Under the steadiness, a whole capacity parked out of sight.
A record is not a triumph, it is a debt: beat the target once and you owe a bigger one forever, so the smart play is to hold the surplus back and keep a little in the jar.
No one is idle: the number is hit in full, on time, every time, and the only thing missing is the thing that would be punished if it showed, which is how good they actually are.

## Nerve

The feeling of a safe pair of hands,
the target met to the decimal, the room calm, the plan on track,
and behind it a quiet, deliberate care never to be caught doing better than next year can afford.

## Echo

After the run: a decade of numbers all met,
a ceiling no one has ever touched on the record,
and an outfit that has no idea what it could do, because knowing would only raise the bar.
