# Piece: The Ratchet Effect

## Taxonomy

[Sandbag](play_sandbag.md). The whole shape at once, and its costly cure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Over [the review](place_the_review.md) and [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md) together, the target set from the past and the capacity hidden from the target.
The phenomenon named: when tomorrow's goal is set from today's result, agents suppress today's performance to keep tomorrow's goal soft, so an incentive meant to drive output quietly teaches everyone to cap it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a heap of dodges with no thesis.
With it the play has its law: the failure needs no idler and no cheat, only a target set from your own past and people rational enough to see where beating it leads. It is also, and this is the half the scolding leaves out, curable: cut the tie between a seat's history and its target, benchmark to the outside or the peer group, reward an accurate forecast over a beaten one, and holding capacity back stops paying. So the Ratchet Effect carries its own remedy, which is not more stretch in the target but a target that no longer punishes the truth.

## Apparent

Just people being cautious.
It looks like ordinary conservatism, a bit of gaming around the edges of a sound system, nothing a firmer target would not fix. That reading is the trap, because a firmer target set the same way ratchets harder, and the fix is not more pressure on a broken linkage but breaking the linkage, which is exactly the move a system built on last year's number finds hardest to make.

## Yearbook

Broken, when it is broken, by [the reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) through [benchmarking](process_benchmarking.md), at the price of the simple, comfortable deal of setting the number from the last.
It is the piece all the others resolve into: [the rising bar](piece_the_rising_bar.md) making [the reward for beating](piece_the_reward_for_beating.md) a punishment, and [the hidden capacity](piece_the_hidden_capacity.md), [the lowball](piece_the_lowball.md), [the reserve](piece_the_reserve.md), and [the soldiering](piece_the_soldiering.md) burying the surplus, make the Ratchet Effect, and only cutting the tie unmakes it.
