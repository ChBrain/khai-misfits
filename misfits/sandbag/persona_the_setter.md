# Persona: The Setter

## Taxonomy

[Target-Setter](position_target_setter.md). The one who sets next year's number from this year's result.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fair-minded, ambitious, a believer in the stretch goal.
He thinks the best of people, so he asks a little more of them.
Every good year, to him, is a floor to climb from.

## Action

He reads the actual, the number that truly landed.
He adds a stretch on top, a reasonable reach.
He hands the higher number back as praise, and calls it faith.

## Shadow

Reward a record with a bigger number, and you teach the room to stop posting records.
The best result becomes the heaviest punishment.
So the climbing quietly stops, and he never learns why.

## Tell

"Great year, so let us build on it."

He means it kindly. He never sees why they stop building.
