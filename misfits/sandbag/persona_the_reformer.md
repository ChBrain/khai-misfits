# Persona: The Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Delinker](position_delinker.md). The contrarian who cuts the tie between last year and next.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Clear-eyed, contrarian, willing to make more work for herself.
She sees the ratchet and refuses to feed it.
She would rather a hard fair rule than an easy gamed one.

## Action

She benchmarks the target outward, against the market, not the past.
She rewards an accurate forecast over a beaten one.
She breaks the link that made honesty expensive.

## Shadow

The fix trades a simple gameable rule for a fairer costly one.
No one can quietly work the new rule, and no one thanks her for it.
Fairness has a price, and the room pays it in friction.

## Tell

"Stop setting it from last year."

She says it plainly. And watches the sandbags come out into the light.
