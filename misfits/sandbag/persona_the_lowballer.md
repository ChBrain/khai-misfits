# Persona: The Lowballer

## Taxonomy

[Forecaster](position_forecaster.md). The cautious one who talks the commit down before it hardens.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Cautious, gloomy exactly when the number is being set.
He never over-promises, and he is proud of that.
When the plan is soft, he is at his most sober.

## Action

He stresses the headwinds and dwells on the risk.
He plays the pipeline modest, the demand uncertain.
He lowers the ask before it hardens into a target.

## Shadow

A conservative forecast can never be disproven.
So the slack goes in at the source, where the plan is born.
The corruption is invisible because it was there from the start.

## Tell

"Let us not get ahead of ourselves."

By the time the number is set, the slack is already in.
