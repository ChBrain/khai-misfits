# Piece: The True Ceiling

## Taxonomy

[Sandbag](play_sandbag.md). The real potential no one will ever put on the books.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Above [the review](place_the_review.md), off every chart, in the height the outfit has never let itself be seen to reach.
What the team could actually deliver if excellence were safe to show: the real capacity, summed across every parked surplus and banked quarter and lowballed commit, the ceiling the whole outfit runs its life beneath.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit is a set of small games with no stake.
With it the misfit has its cost, because the True Ceiling is the thing the ratchet spends: the outfit does not just lose this year's stretch, it loses knowledge of its own limit, since the one number that would reveal it is the one number everyone is paid to bury. The tragedy is not a target missed, every target is met, it is a potential never learned, a company steering by a figure it chose to be beatable and blind to how high it could actually have set it.

## Apparent

Nothing missing at all.
It looks like full success: every number met, every plan delivered, a clean record with no visible shortfall anywhere. The loss is invisible by its nature, because you cannot see a ceiling no one ever touched, and an outfit that hits every target it sets looks like one performing to its limit rather than one that has hidden its limit from itself.

## Yearbook

Never reached on the record, named at last by [the reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) as the price of the game.
It is the wound the whole play points to: the True Ceiling is [the hidden capacity](piece_the_hidden_capacity.md) and [the reserve](piece_the_reserve.md) summed over all time, the real height that [the ratchet effect](piece_the_ratchet_effect.md) keeps forever dark.
