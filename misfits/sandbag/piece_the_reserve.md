# Piece: The Reserve

## Taxonomy

[Sandbag](play_sandbag.md). The good period banked against a lean one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the reserve jar](place_the_reserve_jar.md), in the deals held over and the results kept back for later.
The smoothing stock: a strong quarter's surplus not booked but saved, a win parked to cover a coming shortfall, output shifted across the line between periods so each one lands on target and none stands out.

## Load Bearing

Without it a good period shows as good and a bad one as bad, and the record tracks reality.
With it every period shows the same, because the Reserve is capacity moved through time to flatten the line: the holder does not just cap the peak, they carry it forward, banking the excess where the bar cannot see it and spending it when they would otherwise miss. It turns a jagged truth into a smooth fiction, and a smooth line of exact hits is worth more to the holder than a true line of highs and lows, because the smooth line never gives the setter a reason to raise the bar or a peak to chase.

## Apparent

Steady, dependable delivery.
It looks like the mark of a well-run unit: no drama, no surprises, a reliable number every period. That reputation is exactly the cover, because reliability is indistinguishable from smoothing on the face of the record, and a manager who never misses and never spikes reads as safe hands rather than as someone quietly running a jar.

## Yearbook

Filled and drawn by [the squirrel](persona_the_squirrel.md) through [smoothing](process_smoothing.md), invisible to the bar it is built to fool.
It is the play's time machine: the Reserve carries [hidden capacity](piece_the_hidden_capacity.md) across periods, so the outfit's real rhythm is buried under a flat line and [the true ceiling](piece_the_true_ceiling.md) is hidden not just in size but in shape.
