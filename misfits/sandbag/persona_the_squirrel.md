# Persona: The Squirrel

## Taxonomy

[Banker](position_banker.md). The dependable one who banks a good period against a lean one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Dependable, unflashy, a creature who thinks in reserves.
He does not trust a good quarter to stay good.
So he saves, quietly, for the winter he knows is coming.

## Action

He moves a strong quarter into the jar, off the books.
He holds the surplus dark until the shortfall arrives.
He spends the banked result to cover the lean one.

## Shadow

Smoothing hides even the shape of the truth.
The peaks flatten, the troughs fill, the real rhythm disappears.
So no one can read the season, only the smoothed line.

## Tell

"Let us not book it all this quarter."

Save some for a rainy day, he says, off the record.
