# Persona: The Beater

## Taxonomy

[Striver](position_striver.md). The keen one who beats the number, once.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Keen, hardworking, out to impress.
The first time, he does not know the game yet.
He thinks the number is the point.

## Action

He gives it everything, the whole honest year of it.
He posts a record and waits for the reward.
He watches the record become his floor.

## Shadow

His one honest record raised his own bar for good.
The prize for beating the number was a harder number.
So he never posts another, and who could blame him.

## Tell

"I smashed it this year."

He said it once, proud. And paid for it every year after.
