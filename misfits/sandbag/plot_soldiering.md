# Plot: Soldiering

## Taxonomy

[Sandbag](play_sandbag.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The shop floor](place_the_shop_floor.md), the group settled into a comfortable pace, and one seat running visibly faster than the rest.
The room has an understanding about how hard is hard enough, and someone is about to break it in the open.

## Action

[Soldiering](process_soldiering.md): [The Hare](persona_the_hare.md), holding [the rate-buster's seat](position_rate_buster.md), sprints in full view and posts numbers well above the pace, and the room cools toward him.
[Smoothing](process_smoothing.md): [The Squirrel](persona_the_squirrel.md) at [the banker's seat](position_banker.md) quietly moves a strong quarter into [the reserve jar](place_the_reserve_jar.md) against a leaner one to come.

## Stage

[The Hare](persona_the_hare.md) tables [Go Flat Out](plan_go_flat_out.md): work at full stretch where everyone can see, and learn that [the soldiering](piece_the_soldiering.md) meets a rate-buster with a chill, because his sprint raises the bar for the whole room.
[The Squirrel](persona_the_squirrel.md) tables [Bank It](plan_bank_it.md): carry the surplus across the line between periods, so [the reserve](piece_the_reserve.md) flattens the record into a smooth string of exact hits.

## Tension

No one is cruel, and no one is caught, and the room polices itself.
The hare is only working hard, which should be a virtue, and the group is only protecting a pace that keeps all their bars low, which from every seat is sensible; the squirrel is only being dependable, and a steady line reads as good management. But the mild cooling of the rate-buster teaches the whole floor that visible excellence has a social price as well as a target one, and the banked reserve hides even the shape of the truth, so between the peer norm and the jar the outfit's real rhythm disappears, and it disappears by everyone's quiet, reasonable consent.
