# Plot: Hit and Hold

## Taxonomy

[Sandbag](play_sandbag.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The ledger](place_the_ledger.md), the target in sight and the quarter running ahead of it.
The work is going better than the number requires, and the seat that once would have booked it all now knows better, and reaches for the brake.

## Action

[Holding](process_holding.md): [The Holder](persona_the_holder.md), holding [the sandbagger's seat](position_sandbagger.md), makes the target exactly and parks the surplus out of sight.
[Negotiation](process_negotiation.md): [The Lowballer](persona_the_lowballer.md) at [the forecaster's seat](position_forecaster.md) works [the forecast](place_the_forecast.md), talking the next commit down before it hardens.

## Stage

[The Holder](persona_the_holder.md) tables [Hit and Hold](plan_hit_and_hold.md): meet the number to the decimal and keep the rest dark, so [the hidden capacity](piece_the_hidden_capacity.md) grows behind a clean, exact hit.
[The Lowballer](persona_the_lowballer.md) tables [Lowball the Commit](plan_lowball_the_commit.md): make the modest, gloomy case at planning time, so [the lowball](piece_the_lowball.md) builds slack into the target before the year even starts.

## Tension

Both are being prudent, and the truth goes underground.
The holder is not idle, he hits the number in full and on time, he is simply declining to hand next year a rod to beat him with; the lowballer is not lying, a conservative forecast is defensible and unprovable. And that is the trap: a target met exactly looks like fine judgement, and a modest commit looks like caution, so nothing on the record distinguishes a well-run unit from a well-hidden one, and the books fill with numbers that are accurate, met, and quietly false about what the outfit could do.
