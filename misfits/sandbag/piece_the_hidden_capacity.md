# Piece: The Hidden Capacity

## Taxonomy

[Sandbag](play_sandbag.md). The spare ability kept deliberately off the record.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), in the gap between what was done and what could have been.
The surplus parked behind the line: the extra sales not booked this quarter, the output slowed to match the plan, the effort held in reserve, all of it real and all of it invisible, because showing it would move the bar.

## Load Bearing

Without it the record shows what the outfit can do and the target can be set from truth.
With it the record shows only what the outfit chose to reveal, because the Hidden Capacity is capability withheld on purpose: the holder makes the number and keeps the rest dark, so the books measure the sandbag and not the strength behind it. It is the load the whole misfit rests on, the difference between a figure and a limit, and it grows every year the bar is set from the visible number alone.

## Apparent

A number met, cleanly and in full.
It looks like a target hit exactly, which reads as a job well judged, not a capability concealed. Nothing on the ledger announces what was held back, because held-back work leaves no entry, so the hidden capacity is invisible by construction and the tidy hit that hides it looks like the whole of the story.

## Yearbook

Parked by [the holder](persona_the_holder.md) under [hit and hold](plan_hit_and_hold.md), and never counted until [the reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) makes revealing it safe.
It is the play's buried treasure and its wound: the Hidden Capacity is what the [rising bar](piece_the_rising_bar.md) drives underground, and its sum, across every seat and year, is [the true ceiling](piece_the_true_ceiling.md) no one will ever see.
