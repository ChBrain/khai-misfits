# Process: Booming

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The arrival of the windfall and the pull of the boom, a genuine gain drawing factors and flooding the country with money.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A resource struck or its price soaring.
At [the field](place_the_field.md), the boom earning foreign exchange and offering the highest wages, and the country's factors beginning to move toward it.

## Direction

Toward factors drawn in and income flooding out.
The boom pays the most and earns the foreign exchange, so booming draws labour and capital to the fields and pours new money into the economy, the resource-movement pull and the spending flood both set in motion by one genuine gain.

## Lever

The reality and welcome of the fortune.
Because a struck resource is real wealth and the highest wage is worth chasing, nothing about booming invites suspicion, so the process runs on good fortune, the trap seeded by a windfall no one would refuse and everyone rightly enjoys.

## Echo

A country richer and pulling toward the fields.
The boom has its workers and the nation its new money, and the works are already a little emptier and the shops a little fuller, so what reads as plain good fortune is, unseen, the start of the pull and the flood that will raise the currency and hollow the base.
