# Place: The Field

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). Where the resource is worked, the gas field or mine that earns the foreign exchange and pays the highest wages.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The rigs and shafts of the struck resource, the sector the world pays a premium for, humming with the money pouring in.
Workers arriving from everywhere for the best wages in the land, and capital following them, the field the one place in the country that only grows.

## Holds

The booming, and the pull it exerts.
Here the windfall is earned and the highest wage is paid, so the Field holds the point where fortune becomes a magnet: it draws labour and capital off every other sector and earns the foreign exchange the spending will convert, so it is where a genuine gain begins to bend the whole economy toward itself.

## Offers

The best return in the country.
Because the Field is where the resource pays a premium the world sustains, it offers factors the highest wage and the country its new foreign exchange, so it hands out the fortune everyone rightly chases, and hands it out in a way that pulls the works empty and floods the land with money to spend.

## Withheld

The learning that outlasts the boom.
The Field cannot build the productivity growth and export skill that manufacturing compounds, so it withholds the dynamic base the country will need, offering a rent that flows while the resource lasts and no capacity that survives it, and, being finite, it withholds any promise that the premium will hold when the well runs low.
