# Position: Boom

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The seat of the resource windfall, a genuine gain that draws the country's factors and floods it with money to spend.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A struck resource and a soaring price, earning foreign exchange the country did not have.
The highest wages in the land to offer, and a flood of new income to pour into it.
No malice and no plan, only fortune, and a finite well beneath it that will one day run dry.

## Orders

Pay the most.
Earn the foreign exchange, offer the best returns, and draw the country's labour and capital to the fields.
Flood the nation with the new money a windfall brings.

## Loses

The base the country will need, exactly by being the gift it is.
Because it pays the most it pulls factors from the factories, and because it floods the country with money it drives the prices that raise the currency, so the seat cannot enrich the nation without undercutting its traded sector, its generosity the very thing that hollows the base.
It is real wealth, and being spent as real wealth is, it dismantles what would have outlasted it.

## Drives

Toward a windfall enjoyed,
a genuine gain that guts the base by being spent,
so the fortune that enriches the country is what prices its factories out of the world.
