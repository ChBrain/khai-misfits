# Piece: The Strong Rate

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The real exchange rate appreciated, the risen domestic prices and wages that price the traded sector out.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set at [the exchange](place_the_exchange.md), where the windfall's spending lifts non-traded prices.
Felt at [the works](place_the_works.md), where it lands as a cost base the world price will not cover.

## Load Bearing

Without it the boom is just extra income, and the factories keep their margin and their trade.
With it the whole trap has its fulcrum: a real appreciation, risen domestic costs against a fixed world price, so that the traded sector is undercut abroad and outbid at home. The Strong Rate is load bearing because it is the mechanism that converts the windfall into ruin (Corden & Neary; Corden): the spending effect made concrete, so the piece carries the appreciation that turns a genuine gain into a squeeze no manufacturer can escape, the strong currency that does the deindustrialising.

## Apparent

A sign of strength, a proud currency, evidence the country is doing well.
Seen at the surface, the strong rate looks like success, a nation whose money buys more and whose prices reflect its new wealth, and the truth that it is exactly what prices the factories out, that a currency risen against the world is a traded sector priced out of it, stays hidden behind how healthy a strong currency is taken to be.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the mover](persona_the_mover.md) through [appreciating](process_appreciating.md), the real appreciation the spending of [the windfall](piece_the_windfall.md) drives.
It is the fulcrum of the trap: the Strong Rate is what crushes the traded sector into [the lost trade](piece_the_lost_trade.md), and the one thing [the nest egg](piece_the_nest_egg.md), by keeping the windfall offshore, holds down.
