# Process: Appreciating

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The rise of the real exchange rate as the windfall is spent on what cannot be imported.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A windfall being spent through the economy.
At [the exchange](place_the_exchange.md), the new income chasing homes and meals and services, the things a richer country buys and cannot import.

## Direction

Toward domestic prices risen and the currency strong.
The spending falls on non-traded goods whose supply cannot be met from abroad, so appreciating drives their prices and the wages behind them up, and rising domestic costs are a real appreciation of the currency, whatever the nominal rate does.

## Lever

The reasonableness of spending a windfall.
Because a richer people rightly buys more and much of what it buys cannot be imported, the extra demand and the price rise it causes are the natural result of enjoyed wealth, so appreciating runs on sensible spending, the strong currency built from purchases no one can fault.

## Echo

A country enjoying its wealth, and a currency risen against its own factories.
The homes and services are dearer and the wages higher and the money well spent, and the real exchange rate has climbed above the world's, so what reads as the good life a boom pays for is, unseen, the cost base that will price the traded sector out.
