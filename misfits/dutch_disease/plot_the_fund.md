# Plot: The Fund

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Fund](place_the_fund.md), the boom pricing the factories out and one actor moving to save it instead of spending it.
The means to bank the windfall offshore and hold the currency down at hand.

## Action

[Banking](process_banking.md): [The Steward](persona_the_steward.md), holding [the steward's seat](position_steward.md), would save the windfall abroad and spend only its return, against [The Mover](persona_the_mover.md), holding [the mover's seat](position_mover.md), whose pull is to spend the new money now.

## Stage

[The Steward](persona_the_steward.md), having tabled [Bank It Abroad](plan_bank_it_abroad.md), banks the resource revenue offshore and sterilises the boom, keeping the currency from rising so the traded sector is spared.
[The Mover](persona_the_mover.md), still on [Follow the Boom](plan_follow_the_boom.md), and the ballot behind it, feels every reason to spend the fortune now, on the schools and roads and wages going wanting.

## Tension

The one move that keeps the base is the one a flush country is least willing to make.
There is no easy remedy: saving the windfall offshore plainly spares the factories, yet it means locking a fortune away untouched while present needs press, against the boom and the ballot, and holding that discipline for the life of the resource. So the play closes where it must, the windfall saved and the base spared ... or the money is spent, the currency rises, and the hollowing runs on.
