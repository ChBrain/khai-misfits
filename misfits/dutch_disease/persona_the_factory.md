# Persona: The Factory

## Taxonomy

[Factory](position_factory.md). The traded manufacturing sector, a price-taker on world markets, crushed between the strong currency and the boom's risen costs.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent. Competitive. Suddenly undercut.
Is the country's manufacturing, the sector that earned its way selling abroad at the world's price, now watching its costs climb and its buyers slip away.
No failing in it: only a good exporter caught in a squeeze it did not make and cannot answer.

## Action

Sells at the world's price, which it cannot lift, and pays the country's risen wages and rents, which it cannot escape.
Loses its workers to the better-paying fields and its buyers to cheaper foreign rivals.
Does everything right and still cannot compete, its margin crushed from both sides at once.

## Shadow

Not uncompetitive, undercut: it was competitive until the boom, and it is priced out not by any fault of its own but by the strong currency the windfall raised.
Because it must meet the world's price while paying the country's risen costs, it cannot pass the boom's inflation on, so it shrinks, and shrinking it takes with it the learning and the productivity growth and the export capacity that the resource in the ground never built, the very things the country will need when the boom fades.
It is the sector that would have carried the future, dismantled by a present fortune it had no part in.

## Tell

Says "our costs have risen and the world's price has not, so we cannot sell."
Shuts, and the country loses the base that would have outlasted the boom.
