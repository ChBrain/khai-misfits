# Pitch: Struck It Rich

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Fortunate, flush, spending.
The plain good luck of a windfall struck, incomes up and the currency strong, a country richer overnight and buying what a richer country buys.
Nothing wrong in it: only good fortune enjoyed, the money going where the pay is best and the new wealth spent.

## Undertow

Under the fortune, the strong currency pricing the country's factories out of the world.
Because the windfall is spent on what cannot be imported, domestic prices and wages rise, the currency strengthens in real terms, and the manufacturing that must sell at the world's price and pay the country's risen costs is undercut abroad and outbid at home, and it shuts.
What enriches the country in the moment dismantles the traded base that would have carried it after the boom.

## Nerve

The feeling of good fortune well spent, the windfall enjoyed as any windfall should be,
sure the boom has made the country richer, because in the moment it has,
while the strong currency quietly guts the factories, and the resource that funds it all is finite and will one day run dry.

## Echo

After the run: an economy hollowed by its own bonanza, the factories gone and the boom fading, poorer than before it struck it rich,
no one corrupt and no one wasteful, only factors chasing the best wage and citizens spending a windfall and an exchange rate reflecting the prices,
and a sovereign fund that would have saved the boom and spared the base, refused as long as the money was flush, because saving a fortune felt absurd while the schools and roads went wanting now.
