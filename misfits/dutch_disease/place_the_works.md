# Place: The Works

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The traded manufacturing sector's floor, where the strong currency and the boom's costs crush the price-taker.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The factories that earned their way selling abroad, their order books thinning as foreign rivals undercut them and their workers leaving for the fields.
The line running slower, the margin gone, the sector that competed on the world's terms now unable to meet its own country's costs.

## Holds

The hollowing, and the base it takes.
Here the world's price meets the boom's risen costs, so the Works hold the point where a competitive sector becomes a closing one: the factory must sell at the price it cannot lift and pay the wages it cannot escape, so it is where the appreciation lands as ruin and the traded base is dismantled.

## Offers

The learning and export capacity a resource cannot build.
Because the Works are where productivity grows and skill compounds and the country earns its way on merit, they offer the dynamic base that would outlast the boom, so they hand out the future the resource does not, and hand it out precisely as the boom prices it out of existence.

## Withheld

A price it can set and a cost it can escape.
The Works cannot lift the world's price or shed the country's risen wages, so they withhold any margin to survive on, letting the sector be squeezed from both sides at once, and, once shut, they withhold the learning and capacity for good, since a resource in the ground rebuilds neither.
