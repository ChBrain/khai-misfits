# Plot: The Rush

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Exchange](place_the_exchange.md), everyone doing the sensible thing with a windfall.
Factors moving to the best-paid work and the new income being spent through the economy.

## Action

[Booming](process_booming.md): [The Mover](persona_the_mover.md), holding [the mover's seat](position_mover.md), sends its labour and capital to the fields and spends the windfall, while [The Boom](persona_the_boom.md), holding [the boom's seat](position_boom.md), draws them in with the highest returns.

## Stage

[The Mover](persona_the_mover.md), on [Follow the Boom](plan_follow_the_boom.md), moves its factors to the booming fields and spends the new wealth on homes and meals and services, as a richer country would.
[The Boom](persona_the_boom.md), still on [Pay the Most](plan_pay_the_most.md), keeps offering the best wage, pulling workers and capital off every other sector.

## Tension

Every move is right, and the moves together bend the whole economy toward the boom.
There is nothing to fault: factors go where the return is and a richer people spends more, each choice sound. But the factors flow to the fields and the windfall is spent on what cannot be imported, so the plot closes with the boom drawing the economy in and the new money coursing through it, the pressure on prices and the currency, unseen, beginning to build.
