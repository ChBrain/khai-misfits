# Process: Banking

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The saving of the windfall offshore that sterilises the boom and spares the traded sector, at a hard cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A boom seen to be raising the currency, and someone unwilling to let it be spent at home.
At [the fund](place_the_fund.md), the steward paying the resource revenue into an offshore account and drawing only its return.

## Direction

Toward a windfall held out of the domestic economy.
The steward saves the revenue abroad and spends only its steady yield, so banking drives the new money away from the goods it would bid up, keeping domestic prices and the real exchange rate from rising and leaving the traded sector its margin.

## Lever

The plain truth that a windfall spent at home guts the factories.
Because spending the boom raises the rate that prices manufacturing out, and the resource is finite while the base is not, the case to save is sound, so banking runs on the same prudence any household knows, turned to the hard national discipline of not spending a fortune.

## Echo

A base spared, at the price of a fortune forgone.
The currency has held and the factories run and the fund grows offshore, and the schools and roads and wages the money could have paid for now go wanting, so what reads as a country denying itself its own good fortune is, in truth, the one move that keeps the boom from hollowing it out.
