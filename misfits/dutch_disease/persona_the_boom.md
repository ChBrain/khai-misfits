# Persona: The Boom

## Taxonomy

[Boom](position_boom.md). The resource windfall that earns foreign exchange and pays the highest wages, drawing the country's factors to itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Lucky. Generous. The best-paying thing in the land.
Is the struck resource and its soaring price, the gas under the sea or the mineral the world wants, pouring money into the country.
No scheme in it: only good fortune arrived, offering the highest returns to whoever will work it.

## Action

Earns foreign exchange and pays the most, drawing labour and capital to the fields.
Lifts the nation's income and floods it with new money to spend.
Does what a windfall does: makes the country richer and offers the best wage in it.

## Shadow

Not a curse, a gift, and that is the whole of the trouble: the harm rides on real fortune, not on any fault.
Because it pays the most, it pulls factors from the factories, and because it floods the country with money to spend, it drives up the prices that strengthen the currency, so the very generosity of the boom is what undercuts the traded sector and hollows it.
It is genuine wealth, and being spent as genuine wealth is, it dismantles the base the country will need when the resource, which is finite, runs dry.

## Tell

Says "we have struck it rich, the money is pouring in."
Pours it, and the pouring is what prices the country's factories out of the world.
