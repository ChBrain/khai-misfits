# Place: The Fund

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). Where the windfall would be saved, the offshore store that sterilises the boom and spares the traded sector.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An account held abroad, the resource revenue paid into it and invested overseas, only its steady return drawn home.
The steward guarding it, and the pressure against it: a country flush with money it is told not to spend, and every present need pressing to break the discipline.

## Holds

The banking, and the restraint that resists it.
Here the windfall is kept out of the domestic economy, so the Fund holds the point where the remedy meets its price: saving the revenue offshore keeps the currency from rising and the factories open, and it means locking a fortune away untouched, so it is where the base is spared against the temptation of the boom.

## Offers

A future for the base, and a boom that outlives the resource.
Because the Fund is where the windfall is saved rather than spent, it offers the traded sector its survival and the country a return that flows long after the well runs dry, so it hands out the one defence against the disease, and hands it out at the cost of the fortune enjoyed now.

## Withheld

The windfall's enjoyment in the present.
The Fund cannot both hold the money offshore and let the country spend it, so it withholds the schools and roads and wages the boom could pay for today, and, a discipline that must hold for decades and breaks at a single lapse, it withholds any ease, since the moment the money is spent the currency rises and the disease returns.
