# Piece: The Nest Egg

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The remedy, the windfall saved offshore and spent only from its return, at the cost of the fortune forgone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Held at [the fund](place_the_fund.md), invested abroad and kept from the domestic economy.
Set against [the exchange](place_the_exchange.md), the market whose appreciation it exists to prevent.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play has no answer, and the windfall runs to the hollowing with nothing to hold the rate.
With it the misfit has its costly cure: the resource revenue saved offshore and only its return drawn home keeps the new money from bidding up domestic prices, so the currency does not rise and the traded sector is spared. The Nest Egg is load bearing because it is the remedy that carries its own price (sovereign-fund practice): it works only by locking a fortune away untouched while present needs go wanting, and holding that discipline for the life of the boom, so the piece carries both the cure for the disease and the temptation, renewed daily, that undoes it.

## Apparent

Excessive caution, a country hoarding money it could be using, wealth left idle offshore.
Seen with present needs pressing, the nest egg looks like miserliness, a fortune withheld from the schools and roads it could build now, and the truth that spending it would raise the currency and gut the factories, that the saving is the one thing sparing the base, stays hidden behind how absurd hoarding a windfall seems while the money is flush and the needs are real.

## Yearbook

Kept by [the steward](persona_the_steward.md) through [banking](process_banking.md), the windfall held offshore against the pull to spend.
It is the cure the play cannot make painless: the Nest Egg holds [the strong rate](piece_the_strong_rate.md) down and spares [the lost trade](piece_the_lost_trade.md), and cannot do so without saving [the windfall](piece_the_windfall.md) untouched, a fortune received and deliberately forgone.
