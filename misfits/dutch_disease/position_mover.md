# Position: Mover

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The seat of the country's factors and spenders, whose rational moves lift the real exchange rate.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Labour and capital free to move to the best return, and new income to spend.
A sound instinct: go where the pay is highest, and enjoy a windfall as a richer people would.
No power over the exchange rate, only over where it works and what it buys.

## Orders

Follow the boom.
Send your labour and capital to the best-paid work, and spend the new income on what a richer country buys.
Chase the return and enjoy the gain, the way any economy would.

## Loses

Its own factories, exactly by pursuing the best return and spending the gain.
Because its extra spending falls on what cannot be imported, it drives up domestic prices and so the real exchange rate, and because it moved to the fields, the works lose their workers too, so the seat cannot both chase the boom's returns and keep the traded sector competitive.
It does right by every wage and purchase and, in the sum, prices its own country's manufacturing out of the world.

## Drives

Toward factors and spending chasing the boom,
right for each mover and hollowing for all,
so the sensible pursuit of returns is what raises the rate that shuts the works.
