# Plot: The Hollowing

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Works](place_the_works.md), manufacturing selling at the world's price and paying the country's risen costs.
Order books thinning, workers leaving for the fields, the margin gone.

## Action

[Hollowing](process_hollowing.md): [The Factory](persona_the_factory.md), holding [the factory's seat](position_factory.md), is crushed from both sides, while [The Mover](persona_the_mover.md), holding [the mover's seat](position_mover.md), draws off its workers and its buyers turn to cheaper foreign rivals.

## Stage

[The Factory](persona_the_factory.md), still on [Meet the World Price](plan_meet_the_world_price.md), cannot lift the price it sells at abroad nor escape the wages it pays at home, and its margin fails.
[The Mover](persona_the_mover.md), still on [Follow the Boom](plan_follow_the_boom.md), has taken its workers to the fields and its custom to imports the strong currency makes cheap, so the works run slower and shut.

## Tension

A competitive sector is dismantled having done nothing wrong.
There is no villain still: the world sets the price, the boom sets the wage, and the factory is simply caught between them. But it cannot pass the appreciation on, so the plot closes with the traded base hollowed out, its learning and export capacity gone where the resource cannot rebuild them, the future traded for a finite windfall.
