# Piece: The Windfall

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The resource revenue, the genuine gain in foreign exchange that draws the factors and is spent.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Earned at [the field](place_the_field.md), where the resource pays a premium the world sustains.
Either spent at [the exchange](place_the_exchange.md) or saved at [the fund](place_the_fund.md), and the whole play turns on which.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no boom, no appreciation, no hollowing: an ordinary economy competing at an ordinary rate.
With it the whole trap has its source: a real inflow of foreign exchange that pays the highest wages and floods the country with money, so that its spending raises the rate and its pull empties the works. The Windfall is load bearing because it is the genuine gain the misfit runs on (The Economist; Corden & Neary): the trouble is not a shortfall or a mistake but a real bonanza, so the piece carries the fortune whose ordinary, reasonable enjoyment is exactly what does the damage.

## Apparent

Pure good fortune, money the country did not have and cannot be wrong to take.
Seen alone, the windfall looks like unmixed blessing, a resource struck and wealth pouring in, and the truth that spending it raises the currency and guts the factories, that a real gain enjoyed as any gain would be dismantles the traded base, stays hidden behind how plainly good a fortune received appears.

## Yearbook

Earned by [the boom](persona_the_boom.md) through [booming](process_booming.md), the genuine gain that pays the most and floods the country.
It is the source of the trap: the Windfall spent raises [the strong rate](piece_the_strong_rate.md) and forces [the lost trade](piece_the_lost_trade.md), and only saved as [the nest egg](piece_the_nest_egg.md) does it spare the base rather than hollow it.
