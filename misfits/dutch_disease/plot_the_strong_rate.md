# Plot: The Strong Rate

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Exchange](place_the_exchange.md), the spending falling on what cannot be imported.
Homes and meals and services bid up, domestic costs and wages climbing, the currency strengthening in real terms.

## Action

[Appreciating](process_appreciating.md): [The Mover](persona_the_mover.md), holding [the mover's seat](position_mover.md), bids up the price of the non-traded, while [The Factory](persona_the_factory.md), holding [the factory's seat](position_factory.md), watches its cost base climb against a fixed world price.

## Stage

[The Mover](persona_the_mover.md), still on [Follow the Boom](plan_follow_the_boom.md), spends the windfall on goods that cannot be imported, so their prices and the wages behind them rise.
[The Factory](persona_the_factory.md), on [Meet the World Price](plan_meet_the_world_price.md), sees the real exchange rate strengthen against it, its costs rising while the price it sells at abroad holds flat.

## Tension

The country enjoying its wealth is the country pricing its own factories out.
There is nothing to fault: a richer people rightly buys more, and much of what it buys cannot be imported, so its prices rise. But rising domestic costs are a real appreciation, so the plot closes with the currency strong and the country's costs above the world's, the squeeze on the traded sector, unseen, now set.
