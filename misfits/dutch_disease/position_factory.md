# Position: Factory

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The seat of the traded manufacturing sector, a price-taker crushed between the strong currency and the boom's risen costs.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A place on world markets, where it sells at a price it must take and cannot set.
A cost base of wages and rents and inputs, rising with the boom around it.
No way to lift its selling price, and no escape from the country's risen costs.

## Orders

Meet the world price.
Sell abroad at the price the world sets, hold your buyers, and keep your works running.
Compete as you always have, on quality and cost.

## Loses

Its margin and then its existence, exactly because it cannot pass the boom on.
Because it must take the world's price while paying the country's risen wages, and loses its workers to the better-paid fields, the seat is squeezed from both sides at once, so it cannot both stay a price-taker abroad and survive the boom's inflation at home.
It shrinks and shuts, and takes with it the learning and export capacity the resource never built.

## Drives

Toward a competitive sector priced out,
undercut abroad and outbid at home through no fault of its own,
so the base that would have carried the country is the thing the boom dismantles.
