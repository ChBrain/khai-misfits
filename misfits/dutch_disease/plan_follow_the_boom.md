# Plan: Follow the Boom

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the mover's seat](position_mover.md), inside [Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md).

## Direction

Go where the pay is highest and spend the new income. Send labour and capital to the booming fields, and enjoy the windfall as a richer country would, buying homes and meals and services. Chase the return and spend the gain, the sensible thing for any factor and any household.

## Orders

([The Mover](persona_the_mover.md) sends its labour and capital to the fields and spends the windfall, bidding up the price of the non-traded goods that cannot be imported.)

## Implementation

([The Exchange](place_the_exchange.md); the [appreciating](process_appreciating.md) process; the spending falls on the non-traded and raises domestic prices, so [the strong rate](piece_the_strong_rate.md) rises against the traded sector.)

## Targets

- [ ] labour and capital move to the best-paid work in the booming fields
- [ ] the windfall is spent on homes, meals, and services that cannot be imported
- [ ] the extra demand raises the prices of non-traded goods and so domestic wages
- [ ] the risen prices are a real appreciation of the currency against the traded sector
