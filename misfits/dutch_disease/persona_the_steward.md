# Persona: The Steward

## Taxonomy

[Steward](position_steward.md). Would bank the windfall abroad and live on its return, sterilising the boom so the currency does not rise, against the pull to spend now.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Prudent. Far-sighted. Asking a hard restraint of a flush country.
Sees the boom pricing the factories out and moves to save the windfall rather than spend it, holding a fortune offshore.
No miserliness in it: only someone who knows the resource is finite and the base is not, and would keep both.

## Action

Banks the resource revenue abroad, in a fund held offshore, and spends only its steady return.
Sterilises the boom, keeping the new money out of the domestic economy so the currency does not rise and the traded sector is spared.
Does the one thing that keeps the base: refuses to let the windfall be enjoyed at home.

## Shadow

Not a saviour: the discipline it asks is brutal and must hold for the life of the boom, and every pressure runs against it.
Because it means locking a fortune away untouched while schools and roads and wages go wanting now, against the boom's flood of money and the ballot's demand to spend it, the Steward cannot spare the factories without denying the country the windfall exactly when the windfall is most abundant and most wanted, and one lapse in the discipline lets the currency rise again.
It can keep the traded base, only by receiving a fortune and deliberately not enjoying it, for decades, against everyone who would rather spend.

## Tell

Says "save it offshore and live on the interest, or the boom will hollow us out."
Is right, and being right means telling a country flush with new money to lock it away and go without.
