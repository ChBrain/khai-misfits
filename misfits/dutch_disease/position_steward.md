# Position: Steward

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The seat that would save the windfall offshore against the pull to spend it, sparing the traded sector at a hard cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A means to sterilise the boom: a fund held abroad, and the authority to save the resource revenue in it.
A clear case: the resource is finite and the traded base is not, and a windfall spent at home raises the rate that guts it.
No way to make saving a fortune feel anything but absurd while present needs go wanting.

## Orders

Bank it abroad.
Save the resource revenue in a fund held offshore, spend only its steady return, and keep the new money out of the domestic economy so the currency does not rise.
Hold the discipline for the life of the boom.

## Loses

The windfall's enjoyment, exactly by sparing the base.
Because sterilising the boom means locking a fortune away untouched while schools and roads and wages go wanting, against the flood of new money and the ballot's demand to spend it, the seat cannot keep the traded sector without denying the country the gain when the gain is most abundant, and a single lapse lets the rate rise again.
It buys the base's survival with a fortune received and forgone, and must pay it over and over for decades.

## Drives

Toward a windfall saved and not enjoyed,
the base spared against the temptation of the present,
so the one seat that would keep the factories must tell a flush country to lock its fortune away.
