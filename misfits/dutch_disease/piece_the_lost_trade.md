# Piece: The Lost Trade

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md). The hollowed traded sector, the manufacturing base gone with its learning and export capacity.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Suffered at [the works](place_the_works.md), where the factories shrink and shut.
The shadow of [the field](place_the_field.md), the dynamic base the resource cannot replace.

## Load Bearing

Without it the boom is only a strong currency, an inconvenience that passes when the resource does.
With it the misfit has its wound: the traded sector was where productivity grew and skill compounded and the country earned its way on merit, and it is gone, so the boom does not merely inconvenience the economy but dismantles the base that would have carried it after. The Lost Trade is load bearing because it is the cost that makes the disease a tragedy and not a phase (Corden & Neary; Sachs & Warner): the resource depletes and the learning does not return, so the piece carries the forfeited dynamic base, the future traded away for a finite windfall.

## Apparent

An old sector fading, an economy moving on to where the money is, progress of a kind.
Seen in the moment, the lost trade looks like natural adjustment, resources flowing to their best use, and the truth that the sector lost carried the learning and export capacity a resource never builds, that the country has swapped a base that compounds for a windfall that runs dry, stays hidden behind how reasonable moving to the boom appears.

## Yearbook

Suffered by [the factory](persona_the_factory.md) through [hollowing](process_hollowing.md), the traded base crushed by [the strong rate](piece_the_strong_rate.md).
It is the wound of the trap: the Lost Trade is what the appreciation forces and what the resource cannot rebuild, the future only [the nest egg](piece_the_nest_egg.md), by holding the rate down, could have kept.
