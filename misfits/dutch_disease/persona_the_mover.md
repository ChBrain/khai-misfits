# Persona: The Mover

## Taxonomy

[Mover](position_mover.md). The country's factors and spenders, moving to the best-paid work and spending the windfall, so the currency rises.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Responsive. Sensible. Following the money.
Is the nation's labour and capital and its households, going where the pay is highest and spending the new income the boom brings.
No greed in it: only the ordinary response of factors to returns and of a richer people to their wealth.

## Action

Sends its labour and capital to the booming fields and spends the windfall through the economy.
Bids up the price of homes and meals and services, the things a richer country buys and cannot import.
Does what any economy does with a windfall: moves to the best return and spends the gain.

## Shadow

Not profligate, rational: factors go where the return is and a richer people spends more, each choice sound for the one who makes it.
Because its extra spending falls on what cannot be imported, it drives up domestic prices and wages, which is a real appreciation of the currency, and because it drew off to the fields, the factories lose their workers too, so the sensible pursuit of returns and the reasonable enjoyment of wealth are together what strengthen the currency and gut the traded sector.
It does right by every wage and every purchase, and in the sum prices its own country's factories out of the world.

## Tell

Says "the pay is better in the fields, and we can afford the nicer things now."
Moves and spends, and the moving and spending are what lift the rate that shuts the works.
