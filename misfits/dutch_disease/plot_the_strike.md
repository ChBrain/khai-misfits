# Plot: The Strike

## Taxonomy

[Dutch Disease](play_dutch_disease.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Field](place_the_field.md), a country striking a resource or its price soaring.
The money pouring in, foreign exchange earned, the boom the best-paying thing in the land.

## Action

[Booming](process_booming.md): [The Boom](persona_the_boom.md), holding [the boom's seat](position_boom.md), earns the foreign exchange and pays the highest wages, while [The Mover](persona_the_mover.md), holding [the mover's seat](position_mover.md), feels the new wealth arrive.

## Stage

[The Boom](persona_the_boom.md), on [Pay the Most](plan_pay_the_most.md), pours money into the country and offers the best returns in it, the windfall real and welcome.
[The Mover](persona_the_mover.md), the country's factors and households, sees its income rise and the fields paying more than anywhere, and begins to turn toward them.

## Tension

The good fortune is real, and it is the whole of the trouble.
There is nothing to fault: a struck resource is wealth, not fault, and the highest wage is worth chasing. But the boom pays the most and floods the country with money, so the plot closes with the windfall flowing and incomes rising, the pull toward the fields and the flood of new money to spend, unseen, already set to raise the currency and hollow the base.
