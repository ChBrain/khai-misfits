# Persona: The Plain Hand

## Taxonomy

[Plain Hand](position_plain_hand.md). The one who does the work and does not speak the register, kept where the doing is needed and passed over for the chair, the substance the Script never learned to hear.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Plain-spoken. Load-bearing.
Is the one who carries the work, the engineer, the operator, the maker, whose competence is real and whose account of it is flat, specific, and slow to read.
No sullenness in it: the Plain Hand does the job and describes it plainly, offering the substance without the sound.

## Action

Does the work, and speaks of it in the plain register of the work, specific and unadorned and long to judge.
Stands for the chair with a month of substance no panel has time to open, against a rival with an hour of sound that reads at once.
Does what a doer does: it carries the job and reports it plainly, and plain report is what the house has learned to hear as staff.

## Shadow

Not overlooked by malice, a substance the selecting ear was never trained to read, and that is the whole difficulty: the work is real and illegible, and real-and-illegible loses to legible-and-part-true every time the choice is made under constraint.
Because the Plain Hand's competence takes a month and a specialist to judge and the register takes an hour, the panel reaches past the plain hand for the sound, and the plain hand is thanked and kept where the doing is needed, so the work that holds the place up is the work least likely to rise from it, the substance load-bearing and unpromoted and, over rounds, either taught to speak the register or left below while the sound goes up.
It carries the job the house runs on, and carrying it plainly is what keeps it heard as staff and not as leadership.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "here is what I did and how it works," flat and specific and long.
Says it in the register of the work and not the register of leadership, and is heard, across the table, as the one who does the thing and not the one who leads it.
