# Pitch: Sounds Like a Leader

## Taxonomy

[The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Assured, articulate, sound.
The plain good sense of promoting the one who sounds like a leader, who frames the choice and holds the room and states the direction in the confident register everyone knows.
Nothing false in it: articulacy is a real part of leading, the register carries a true piece of the job, and the sound reads fast where the work reads slow.

## Undertow

Under the assurance, a register legible enough to select on, selected round after round, becoming the mark of the thing it was only a loose sign of.
Because the sound reads in an hour and the work takes a month, the house reaches for the sound, and each rise makes the register more the definition of leadership, until the ear cannot tell the sign from the substance and the work goes on unheard on the Floor.
What makes the register worth hearing is exactly what lets it stand in for the whole.

## Nerve

The feeling of hearing leadership in a voice and reaching for it,
sure it is only good judgement, because the register really is part of the job and reads where the work does not,
while the substance stays buried in the plain hand you kept below, and the sound you promoted becomes the thing you now call leadership.

## Echo

After the run: a top selected for the register and a competence beneath left to be hoped for,
no one having rigged it and no one having faked, only a panel reading the best signal it had and aspirants learning what rose,
and the one move that would put the sound back in its place, reading the work and hearing the plain hand, forgone because it costs the judging of what takes a month over what reads in an hour, against every incentive to reach again for the legible sound.
