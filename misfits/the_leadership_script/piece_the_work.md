## Load Bearing

Without it the register stands in for nothing, and there is no substance for the sound to be selected over.
With it the trap has its cost: the real competence the role was for, carried by the hands kept at it, and illegible from the table where the chair is filled, so the thing the place runs on is the thing the selection cannot read (Alvesson and Sveningsson, on the ordinary work the leadership talk is extra-ordinarised above). The Work is load bearing because it is the substance the sound displaces: what the register was only ever a loose sign of and what the selection, reaching for the legible, passes by, so the piece carries the competence that holds the place up and is least able to rise from it, present exactly where it cannot be seen.

## Apparent

Staff work, the doing, the ordinary business the leaders above it direct.
Seen at the surface, the Work looks like the lower thing, the execution beneath the vision, the job the register-speakers lead, and the truth that it is the substance the whole place runs on, that the leadership above was only ever a loose sign of it, that it is load-bearing and unheard and kept below because it cannot be read in an hour, stays hidden behind how much the plain register of the doing sounds like staff and not like leadership.
