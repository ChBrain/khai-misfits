# Piece: The Script

## Taxonomy

[The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md). The naturalised register itself, the way leadership is heard to sound, the confident frame and strategic vocabulary taken as the mark of the thing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Performed at [the review](place_the_review.md), where the register is spoken and heard as leadership.
Learned around it through [rehearsing](process_rehearsing.md), where aspirants cast themselves to the sound.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no register to select on, and leadership is judged, however slowly, on the work.
With it the trap has its engine: a way of speaking that carries a true part of leading and reads across a table, so it can be selected on, and selected on becomes the mark of the thing it was only a loose sign of (Fairclough, on the order of discourse, a dominant way of speaking naturalised until it seems the only natural one). The Script is load bearing because it is the sign that becomes the definition: legible, part-true, and reproducible, so the piece carries the register that stands in for competence and, selected round after round, comes to be heard as leadership itself, imposed by no one and confirmed by all.

## Apparent

The sound of a leader, the natural voice of anyone fit to lead, the mark by which the thing is plainly known.
Seen at the surface, the Script looks like leadership itself, the way a real leader simply speaks, and the truth that it is a register and not the thing, a loose partial sign selected until it stands for the whole, a sound learned because it rises and not because it leads, stays hidden behind how natural the voice of authority sounds once the ear is trained to it.

Spoken by [the fluent](persona_the_fluent.md) through [speaking](process_speaking.md), and learned by the aspirants through [rehearsing](process_rehearsing.md), the register the house hears as leadership.
