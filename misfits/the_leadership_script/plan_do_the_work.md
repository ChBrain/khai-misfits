# Plan: Do the Work

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the plain hand's seat](position_plain_hand.md), inside [The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Plain Hand](position_plain_hand.md)

## Direction

Carry the job and report it plainly. Hold the Floor, do the substance the place runs on, and give a flat, specific, honest account of it in the register of the work. Be the competence, because the competence is what the place is finally for, whether or not it reads as leadership.

## Orders

([The Plain Hand](persona_the_plain_hand.md) does the work and speaks it plainly, and is kept where the doing is needed and passed for the chair, the substance load-bearing and unpromoted while the sound goes up.)

## Implementation

([The Floor](place_the_floor.md); the work carried plainly and reported flat, [the work](piece_the_work.md) held below the [ladder](piece_the_ladder.md) because the [script](piece_the_script.md) never learned to hear the plain register it speaks in.)

## Targets

- [ ] the work the place runs on is carried and held on the Floor
- [ ] the substance is reported plainly, flat and specific and slow to read
- [ ] the plain hand is kept where the doing is needed and passed over for the chair
- [ ] the substance stays load-bearing and unpromoted, or is taught the register and pulled off the work
