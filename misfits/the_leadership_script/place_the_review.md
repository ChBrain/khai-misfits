## Holds

The selecting, and the moment the sound is taken for the substance.
Here the register meets the ear that rewards it, so the Review holds the point where the sign becomes the mark: a candidate heard in the time a panel has and a panel that can read the sound but not the work, so it is where the fluent rises and the plain hand is passed, the legible signal chosen because it is the one that can be judged when the chair must be filled.

## Offers

A choice that can be made in the time there is.
Because the Review must fill the chair and the register reads across a table while the work does not, it offers the Panel a signal it can actually judge, the sound weighed and the leader named, so it hands out a decision reachable in the hour it has, and hands it out on the one thing legible in that hour, the register, the substance it would prefer left unopened for want of the month it would take.

## Withheld

The work, and any sign of which fluent had it beneath the words.
The Review can hear every candidate the same way but cannot open the substance behind the sound, so it withholds the difference between the fluent who has the work and the fluent who has only the register, letting both read as leadership alike, and, all confident hearing and no time to read, it withholds the plain hand's competence entirely, filed as staff in a tongue the room is not trained to hear, until the chair is given to the sound and the substance is left below.
