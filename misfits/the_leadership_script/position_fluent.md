# Position: Fluent

## Taxonomy

[The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md). The seat that speaks the register and rises on the sound, and once risen becomes the mark the next selection reads.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A native command of the register and a stage to speak it on: the fluency the house hears as leadership and the room in which it reads.
A real basis: articulacy is part of the job, so the sound the seat commands carries a true piece of what the role wants.
No need of the substance beneath the words to be selected, and no way, from the seat, to be told across a table from the one who has it.

## Orders

Speak the Script.
Frame the choice, hold the room, state the direction in the confident register, and be heard as leadership in the time a panel has to listen.
Sound the part, because sounding the part is what the house has learned to promote.

## Loses

Nothing it was asked to keep, and that is the point: the seat is not required to hold the substance, only the sound, so it loses nothing by lacking the work and gains the chair by having the register.
Because the seat rises on the legible signal and the signal is the sound, the Fluent is promoted whether or not the work is beneath the words, and by being promoted becomes the confirmation the next panel hears, so what the arrangement loses is not the seat's but the house's: the tie between the leadership it selects and the competence it meant to.
It gains the chair by the register, and the house loses the assurance that the chair holds the substance.

## Drives

Toward the sound selected and the substance unasked,
the register rising whether or not the work is under it,
so the seat that speaks the part rises by it, and becomes the mark by which the next part is known.
