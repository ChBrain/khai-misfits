# Process: Selecting

## Taxonomy

[The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md). The Panel choosing the register over the work, the legible signal taken under constraint, the selection that makes the sound the risen and the sign the mark.

## Owner

## Initiated by

A chair to fill and an hour to fill it in.
At [the review](place_the_review.md), the panel weighing a sound it can read against a substance it cannot, and reaching for the one that judges in the time there is.

## Direction

Toward the fluent chosen and the plain hand passed.
Because the register reads across a table and the work takes a month, selecting reaches for the legible signal and gives the chair to the sound, and by giving it makes the fluent the risen, so the register stops being a signal of leadership and starts being the mark of it, confirmed for the next panel in the ones already up.

## Lever

The real constraint of choosing on what can be read.
Because the chair must be filled in the time there is and only the register reads in that time, selecting on the sound is the rational choice under constraint, so the process runs not on prejudice but on legibility, the top-selected-for-sound assembled from no bad faith, only from a panel reading the best signal it could hear in the hour it had.

## Echo

A leader chosen, and the sign made the definition.
The panel has selected on the best legible signal, so what reads as sound judgement is, in truth, the register chosen over the unread work, and the choosing itself turning the sign into the mark the next choosing will confirm.
