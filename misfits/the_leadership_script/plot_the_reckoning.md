# Plot: The Reckoning

## Taxonomy

[The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

The top of the house, looked at plainly, and the register everywhere upon it.
Selection has run on the sound so long that the risen all speak it, and no one can now read, in them, which had the work beneath the words.

## Action

[Selecting](process_selecting.md) come to its sum: the house reads its leadership through [the ear](piece_the_ear.md) it has tuned, and cannot tell the register from the substance it stood for.

## Stage

Round after round of [the ladder](piece_the_ladder.md) given to the sound has thinned the tie between the register and the work, so [the script](piece_the_script.md) is worn by all the risen and marks none of them as competent or not.
[The plain hand](persona_the_plain_hand.md) and [the work](piece_the_work.md) are still below, load-bearing and unread, and the house runs on a substance it does not select for and can no longer, at the top, hear apart from the sound.

## Tension

Optimising selection on the best legible signal is rational at every step, and optimising on it is what strips the signal of its meaning.
There is no villain and no fraud: each panel chose the register in good faith, each aspirant learned it in good sense. But a sign selected on hard enough stops distinguishing what it once loosely marked, so the plot closes with the house unable to tell sound from substance at the level it selects, the register perfected into the mark of leadership and emptied, by the perfecting, of the thing it was a mark of.
