# Persona: The Reader

## Taxonomy

[Reader](position_reader.md). The one who would judge the work and not the register, reading the demonstrated substance a panel has no time for and hearing the plain hand, at the cost the legible sound was the way to avoid.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Patient. Sceptical of the sound. Willing to read slowly.
Is the one who would select on substance, the assessor who opens the work, the leader who promotes the doer, the panellist who distrusts the register.
No cynicism in it: the Reader does not deny the sound carries part of the job, only refuses to let the part stand for the whole.

## Action

Opens the work the register let a panel skip, reads the month of substance and calls the specialist, and hears leadership in the plain hand the ear had filed as staff.
Weighs the demonstrated record over the audible performance, and promotes on what was done rather than how it was said.
Does what keeps the sound in its place: it reads the work, and reading the work is the one thing that catches the register standing in for the substance.

## Shadow

Not a free corrective, a costly and resented labour, and that is the whole difficulty: reading the work takes the month the register saved, and the register saved it for good reason.
Because the substance is illegible and slow and the sound is legible and fast, the Reader must spend what the Panel could not, judging what takes a specialist over what reads across a table, and must tell the aspirants who learned the register that the register is not the thing after the house taught them for years that it was, and must hear leadership in a voice trained to sound like staff, so the read that alone puts the sound back in its place is exacted continuously and rewarded rarely, against every incentive to reach again for the legible signal.
It reads the work the register was the cheap way not to read, and the reading is the cost the trap is built to make unpayable.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "show me what you did, not how you say it."
Says it reaching for the month the panel did not have, and is heard, often, as slow and unfair and blind to a leadership everyone else can plainly hear.
