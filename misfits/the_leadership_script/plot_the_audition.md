# Plot: The Audition

## Taxonomy

[The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Review](place_the_review.md), a chair fallen open and a candidate speaking the register.
The Fluent framing the choice and holding the room, the Panel listening in the hour it has to decide.

## Action

[Speaking](process_speaking.md): [The Fluent](persona_the_fluent.md), holding [the fluent's seat](position_fluent.md), performs the register so it reads as leadership across the table.

## Stage

[The Fluent](persona_the_fluent.md), on [Speak the Script](plan_speak_the_script.md), delivers [the script](piece_the_script.md) in the confident frame, and [the panel](persona_the_panel.md) hears the mark of leadership in it.
The register carries a true part of the job and reads at once, so the sound is taken, in the room, as the sign of the thing, the part standing in for a whole no one has time to open.

## Tension

Hearing leadership in a register that genuinely carries part of it is not folly, and hearing it is what lets the part stand for the whole.
There is nothing false: articulacy is a piece of leading and the sound really does read where the work does not. But a signal legible enough to be taken in the hour there is is a signal that can be taken for the whole, so the plot closes with the register heard as the mark of leadership and the substance behind it, present or absent, left unread.
