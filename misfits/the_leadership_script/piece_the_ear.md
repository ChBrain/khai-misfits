# Piece: The Ear

## Taxonomy

[The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md). The organisation's trained hearing, the collective habit that takes the register for the sound of competence, the naturalisation itself and the phenomenon whole.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Formed across [the review](place_the_review.md), where round after round the register is heard as leadership.
Unlearned, if ever, at [the close read](place_the_close_read.md), where the ear is made to hear the plain hand.

## Load Bearing

Without it the register is a sound some prefer, and the substance can still be heard beneath it.
With it the trap is complete and authorless: the whole house hears leadership as the Script, so the register is not a bias some hold but the natural sound of the thing to everyone, imposed by no one and confirmed by all (Fairclough, on naturalisation, the point at which a dominant discourse stops being heard as a choice). The Ear is load bearing because it is the naturalisation whole: the collective, trained hearing that takes the sign for the substance so completely it no longer registers as a preference, so the piece carries the phenomenon at full strength, an organisation that cannot easily tell, at the top, the register from the leadership it stood for, because its very ear has been tuned to the sound.

## Apparent

Simply knowing a leader when you hear one, the shared good sense of the whole house.
Seen at the surface, the Ear looks like collective discernment, everyone agreeing on what leadership sounds like because that is what it sounds like, and the truth that the agreement is a trained habit, that the ear was tuned by rounds of selecting the sound, that a whole house hearing the register as leadership is the naturalisation and not a proof, stays hidden behind how obvious the sound of leadership seems once everyone has learned to hear it.

## Yearbook

Tuned by [the panel](persona_the_panel.md) and the house through [selecting](process_selecting.md) and [rehearsing](process_rehearsing.md), retuned, at cost, by [the reader](persona_the_reader.md).
It is the phenomenon whole: the Ear takes the [script](piece_the_script.md) for the thing, prices its [currency](piece_the_currency.md) as leadership, sends the [ladder](piece_the_ladder.md) to the sound and [the work](piece_the_work.md) below, and is hardened by the [mould](piece_the_mould.md) into the naturalised hearing at the centre of the leadership script.
