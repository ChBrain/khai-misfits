# Piece: The Mould

## Taxonomy

[The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md). The self-reinforcing form the aspirants are cast to, the register worn as the identity of leadership, the loop by which the sign reproduces itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Cast around [the review](place_the_review.md), where what rises is watched and learned.
Set through [rehearsing](process_rehearsing.md), where the aspirants form themselves to the register that rose.

## Load Bearing

Without it the register selects one round and does not perpetuate, and the sign stays a sign.
With it the trap closes into a loop: the aspirants, seeing exactly what rises, cast themselves to the register and wear it as the identity of leadership, so the sound is not selected once but reproduced, each generation formed to the mould the last rose by (Bourdieu, on the reproduction of the legitimate tongue; Meindl, on the romance that makes the risen the template). The Mould is load bearing because it is the self-perpetuation: the sign hardened into an identity people become, so the piece carries the loop that makes the register not a thing the house happens to reward but the thing anyone fit to lead is taken to be, learned because it rises and rising because it is learned.

## Apparent

Leaders developing, talent forming itself, the next generation growing into the role.
Seen at the surface, the Mould looks like leadership reproducing healthily, aspirants growing into able leaders, and the truth that it is the register reproducing itself, an identity cast to the sound that rose and not to the work that runs the place, a mould people are formed to because the house taught it was the thing, stays hidden behind how much learned leadership looks like grown leadership.

## Yearbook

Cast by the aspirants through [rehearsing](process_rehearsing.md), on the model of [the fluent](persona_the_fluent.md) risen, the loop that reproduces the register.
It is the closing of the trap: the Mould reproduces the [script](piece_the_script.md) as [currency](piece_the_currency.md), sends each new cohort up the [ladder](piece_the_ladder.md) on the sound and past [the work](piece_the_work.md), hardening [the ear](piece_the_ear.md) that takes the register for leadership.
