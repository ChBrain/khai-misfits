# The Leadership Script: Reference

The research warrant for [The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md).
The dynamic is the naturalised order of discourse turned into a selection standard: Fairclough's order of
discourse and naturalisation, Bourdieu's linguistic capital, Meindl's romance of leadership, Alvesson and
Sveningsson's extra-ordinarisation of the mundane, and Khurana's market for the charismatic leader, all cited
below.

## Line of Work

Staging the leadership register as a **selection-and-naturalisation structure**: an organisation comes to hear
leadership as a way of speaking, a legible, part-true register, and because the register reads across a table
while the work takes a month and a specialist to judge, selection runs on the sound, so promotion flows to the
fluent over the doer, each rise makes the register more the mark of leadership, aspirants cast themselves to it,
and the sign hardens into the naturalised identity of the thing it was only a loose sign of. The play models the
documented mechanics, the register that carries a real partial signal because articulacy is genuinely part of
leading, the legibility gap that makes the sound selectable where the substance is not, the selection under real
constraint that reaches for the readable signal, the linguistic capital unevenly held that buys standing, the
self-reproducing loop by which the risen become the template and the aspirants the next cohort of fluent, the
work carried load-bearing and unpromoted on the floor, and the costly close read that alone puts the sound back
in its place, as the load-bearing vectors of the structure, not as a tale of empty talkers and cynical panels.
The governing finding of the sources is the play's governing law: a dominant way of speaking becomes naturalised
until it is heard as the natural sound of the thing rather than a choice, and where that register is selected on
it comes to define what it once only loosely marked, so a house selecting rationally on the legible sound builds
a leadership chosen for the register and a competence beneath it left to be hoped for.

It is distinct from the house's Rising to Unfit, and the two are close enough that the cut must be exact: there
the engine is the Peter Principle, the competent are promoted on their record until they reach a level they cannot
do, a competence-based selection that overshoots; here the engine is the register selected in place of competence
at all, the fluent promoted on the sound whether or not the work is beneath it, so Rising to Unfit promotes the
able past their range while the Leadership Script promotes the audible over the able. It is distinct from The
Mirror, where the engine is Conway's structural imprint of the org chart on the product, a matter of shape and
not of speech. It is distinct from The Cargo Cult, where the engine is the copying of a specific success's
visible rituals in hope of its result: the Leadership Script is not the imitation of one model but the field-wide
naturalisation of a register into the identity of leadership, selected and reproduced with no original success
being copied. And it is distinct from Metric Fixation, where a chosen, explicit measure is served in place of the
goal: the register here is no one's chosen metric but a tacit, naturalised sound taken for the thing itself, never
set as a target and all the harder to see for never having been declared one. (Pournelle's Iron Law of
Bureaucracy, the drift of a body toward those who serve it over those who serve its goal, is a cousin and not the
same engine: the Script is about the sound selected for, not the constituency served.)

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast the fluent as an
  empty talker or the panel as prejudiced, because the source finding is precisely that the register is part-true
  and the selection rational: articulacy is a real piece of leading, the sound reads in the hour a panel has while
  the work takes a month and a specialist, the aspirant who learns the register is responding sensibly to a real
  signal, and the fluent leader may be able as well as audible. The naturalised Script is authored by no one and
  confirmed by all, so the misfit is that a register legible and part-true enough to select on, selected round
  after round, becomes the mark of the thing it was only a loose sign of, the house tuning its own ear until it
  hears the sound as leadership itself.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into a costless correction. The one move that puts the sound
  back in its place is to read the work and not the register, promote on demonstrated substance, and hear the
  plain hand, and it means judging what takes a month and a specialist over what reads in an hour, distrusting a
  signal that is genuinely part-right, telling the aspirants the register is not the thing after the house spent
  years teaching them it was, and hearing leadership in a voice the ear was trained to file as staff. The
  reckoning is a substance heard only by paying, continuously, the cost of reading the work the register was the
  cheap way not to read, against every incentive to reach again for the legible sound, not a free correction.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here, never claimed as
  the house's. The staging is the house's; the order of discourse and its naturalisation are Fairclough's, the
  linguistic capital and its reproduction are Bourdieu's, the romance that reads leadership into a performance is
  Meindl and colleagues', the market for the charismatic leader over the record is Khurana's, and the
  extra-ordinarisation of the mundane work is Alvesson and Sveningsson's. The honest boundary, that the register
  is a true partial signal and not empty, so selection on it is rational and that is what makes it a trap, is
  carried as carefully as the finding itself.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **[The Script](piece_the_script.md)**: carries the naturalised register (Fairclough), the way of speaking taken
  as the mark of leadership rather than a choice.
- **[The Currency](piece_the_currency.md)**: carries the linguistic capital (Bourdieu), the legitimate tongue,
  unevenly held, that buys standing where the plain register cannot.
- **[The Ladder](piece_the_ladder.md)**: carries the selection on image over record (Khurana) and the romance
  read into the risen (Meindl), the rise given to the sound that makes the sound the mark.
- **[The Work](piece_the_work.md)**: carries the extra-ordinarised mundane (Alvesson & Sveningsson), the real
  substance the leadership talk is constructed above and the selection cannot read.
- **[The Mould](piece_the_mould.md)**: carries the reproduction of the legitimate tongue (Bourdieu; Meindl), the
  self-perpetuating loop that casts each cohort to the register that rose.
- **[The Ear](piece_the_ear.md)**: carries naturalisation whole (Fairclough), the collective trained hearing that
  takes the register for competence so completely it no longer registers as a choice.
