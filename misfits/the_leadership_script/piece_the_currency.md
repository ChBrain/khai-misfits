With it the trap has its market: the register is a capital, a legitimate tongue that reads as standing and trades for the chair, so the fluent are rich in the currency the selection spends and the plain hands are poor in it however deep their work (Bourdieu, on linguistic capital, the legitimate language that confers authority and is unevenly held). The Currency is load bearing because it is what makes the register selectable: not merely a sound but a holding that buys, so the piece carries the fluency-as-capital that the Review pays the chair for, accrued by those with access to the tongue and lacked by those with only the work.
