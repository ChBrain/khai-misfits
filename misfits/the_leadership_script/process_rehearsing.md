# Process: Rehearsing

## Taxonomy

[The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md). The aspirants learning the register that rises, coaching and casting themselves to the mould, the self-perpetuation that hardens the sign into the identity of leadership.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Aspirants below who hear exactly what rises.
At [the review](place_the_review.md) and around it, the ones who watch the fluent promoted and the plain hand kept, and learn from the promotion what leadership is heard to be.

## Direction

Toward the register learned and worn as identity.
Because to speak the Script is to be heard as a leader and to speak plainly is to be heard as staff, rehearsing has the aspirants coach the register, practise the frame, and cast themselves to the mould, so the sound spreads and the ear hardens, the register becoming not a thing some do but the identity anyone fit to lead is taken to have, learned because it is what the house rewards.

## Lever

The plain rationality of learning what rises.
Because the register is what gets promoted and the aspirants are not fools, rehearsing is the sensible response to a real signal, so the process runs on nobody's vanity but everybody's good sense, the sign hardening into the definition assembled from no pretence, only from people learning, correctly, the sound the house selects for.

## Echo

A register spread, and the sign become the identity.
The aspirants have learned what rises, so what reads as talent developing itself is, in truth, the mould reproducing itself, the register worn as the identity of leadership because the house taught, round after round, that it was.
