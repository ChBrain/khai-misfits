# Plot: The Casting

## Taxonomy

[The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Review](place_the_review.md) and the building around it, the aspirants below hearing exactly what rises.
The fluent promoted and the plain hand kept, and the ones watching learning from it what leadership is heard to be.

## Action

[Rehearsing](process_rehearsing.md): the aspirants, watching [the fluent](persona_the_fluent.md) rise, learn and coach the register and cast themselves to [the mould](piece_the_mould.md).

## Stage

The aspirants, seeing the [ladder](piece_the_ladder.md) flow to the sound, practise [the script](piece_the_script.md) and wear it as the identity of leadership, because to speak it is to be heard as a leader and to speak plainly is to be heard as staff.
The register spreads and [the ear](piece_the_ear.md) hardens, the sign no longer a thing some do but the thing anyone fit to lead is taken to be, learned because it is what the house rewards.

## Tension

Learning the register that rises is the sensible response to a real signal, and learning it is what turns the sign into the identity.
There is nothing foolish in the aspirants: they hear, correctly, what the house promotes, and adopt it. But each cohort cast to the mould makes the register more the definition of leadership and the work less its mark, so the plot closes with the sign reproduced as identity, the loop shut and the ear hardened, and the substance ever further from the sound the house now hears as the thing.
