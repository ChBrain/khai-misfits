# Piece: The Ladder

## Taxonomy

[The Leadership Script](play_the_leadership_script.md). The promotion track that flows to the fluent, the rise given to the sound, each rung to the register making the register more the mark of the next.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Given at [the review](place_the_review.md), where the rise is awarded to the register.
Climbed away from [the floor](place_the_floor.md), where the work is held and the plain hand is kept.

## Load Bearing

Without it the register wins nothing, and the sound and the substance stand level.
With it the trap turns: the rise flows to the fluent, and each fluent risen becomes the confirmation the next panel reads, so the Ladder does not merely reward the register, it makes the register the mark by climbing (Khurana, on the selection of leaders by charismatic image over demonstrable record; Meindl, on leadership read into the risen). The Ladder is load bearing because it is where the sign becomes the definition: promotion given to the sound turns the sound into what leadership looks like at the top, so the piece carries the self-confirming rise, the register selected until the risen all speak it and the speaking is taken for the leading.

## Apparent

Merit rising, the able promoted, the leaders the process was meant to find.
Seen at the surface, the Ladder looks like advancement working, the best rising to the top, and the truth that it flows to the sound and not the substance, that each rise makes the register more the mark and the work less able to climb, that the top it builds is selected for the tongue, stays hidden behind how much a promotion looks like merit found.
