# Persona: The Operator

## Taxonomy

[Operator](position_operator.md). The one who runs the system knowing a backup exists, and, trusting the spare and the guard beside them, watches a little less and runs a little closer to the edge. The archetype of diffused responsibility and risk taken up to the margin.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Confident. At ease. Sure the net is there.
Is the one at the controls, the crew, the pilot, the operator who works the system in the knowledge that a backup stands behind them and another watcher shares the watch.
No negligence in it: the Operator works within the margins the system advertises, and the system advertises the margin the backup seems to buy.

## Action

Runs the system trusting the spare to catch what they miss, and, knowing a backup exists, takes the risk they would not take without it, running nearer the edge because the edge has a net.
Watches a little less closely because another guard shares the watch, letting responsibility for catching the failure rest across the redundant set rather than on their own eyes.
Does what a rational operator does with a known reserve: it uses the margin the backup provides, and spends the safety the redundancy added on the speed or the load or the ease it makes possible.

## Shadow

Not reckless, an operator responding sensibly to a real reserve, and that is the whole difficulty: the reserve is real, and using it is exactly how the added safety is quietly spent back.
Because a guard stands behind each guard, responsibility for the catch diffuses across the set and each watches assuming the other watches, so the vigilance the backup was to add is subtracted, and because the operator knows a net exists they run closer to the fall, the risk compensating the reserve until the system sits as near the edge as it did before the backup, only now with more parts to fail.
It leans on the spare in good faith, and the leaning is exactly what turns the added chance back into the margin it was meant to widen.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "there is a backup, we are covered," and runs accordingly.
Says it trusting a spare that shares its cause and a watcher who is trusting them back, and cannot see that the net it is counting on has moved the edge it runs to, the reserve mistaken for room when it is only spent.
