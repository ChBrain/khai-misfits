# Plan: Cover the Gap

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the backup's seat](position_backup.md), inside [Belt and Braces](play_belt_and_braces.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Backup](position_backup.md)

## Direction

Stand behind the primary and cover the gap it might leave. Take over the load the moment the primary fails, from where a backup must sit, wired and cooled and crewed with the rest, because a spare apart from the system could not back it.

## Orders

([The Backup](persona_the_backup.md) covers the gap from inside the system, and by sitting there opens one more path between the parts and rests on one more shared cause, the cover genuine and the coupling unseen.)
