# Process: Auditing

## Taxonomy

[Belt and Braces](play_belt_and_braces.md). The Auditor tracing the shared cause and pricing the complexity, the costly count that surfaces the coupling and catches the turn before the common cause finds it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A figure that only climbs and someone unwilling to trust it.
At [the review](place_the_review.md), the auditor tracing whether each backup shares power, crew, premise or environment with what it backs before another is added.

## Direction

Toward the true independence counted and the coupling priced.
Because the figure counts spares and not independence, auditing traces the cause each backup shares and prices the complexity each brings, so the many chances the arithmetic called independent are separated into the ones that truly are and the ones felled by a single cause, and the turn is caught in the review rather than in the failure, the reliability tested instead of the count.

## Lever

The willingness to spend the cost of refusing a backup that reads as safe.
Because counting the independence means refusing a spare that looks sound and doubting a figure that climbs, auditing runs only on someone choosing to pay that cost, so the corrective is exacted continuously and thanked rarely, the common cause it forestalls leaving no trace of the failure it spared and the refused backup remembered as safety declined.

## Echo

The independence counted, and the turn caught in the review.
The auditor has traced what each backup truly shares, so what reads as arguing against safety is, in truth, the one act that counts the reliability and not the spares, the coupling surfaced while a backup can still be refused, the common cause that now never fires leaving nothing to thank it for.
