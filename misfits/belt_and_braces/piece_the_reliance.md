# Piece: The Reliance

## Taxonomy

[Belt and Braces](play_belt_and_braces.md). The trust the backup invites, the diffused responsibility and the risk taken up to the margin, the reliance that spends the very vigilance the redundancy was meant to add.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Grows across [the system](place_the_system.md), where each guard trusts the guard behind and each operator the net.
Named, if ever, at [the review](place_the_review.md), where the reserve spent back is counted against the reserve added.

## Load Bearing

Without it each guard watches as if alone and the reserve the backup added stays added, and no risk is compensated back.
With it the trap suppresses its own cure: because another guard stands behind each guard, responsibility for the catch diffuses across the redundant set and the operators, knowing a net exists, run closer to the edge (Latane and Darley, on the diffusion of responsibility across a group, each less likely to act as others could; Sagan, on social shirking and risk compensation, the backup relaxing the vigilance and inviting the risk it was added to guard). The Reliance is load bearing because it is how the added safety is spent back: the trust the spare invites subtracting the watch and moving the edge out to meet the run, so the piece carries the reserve consumed by the very existence of the reserve, the net that was to catch the fall inviting the leap.

## Apparent

Confidence, a crew at ease with a system well covered, working sensibly within a real margin.
Seen at the surface, the Reliance looks like justified confidence, operators using a reserve that is really there, and the truth that the shared watch is a dropped watch, that the used margin is spent safety, that the backup relaxed the very vigilance it was added to reinforce, stays hidden behind how reasonable it is to lean on a net that genuinely exists.

## Yearbook

Grows from [the backup](piece_the_backup.md) through [shirking](process_shirking.md), named only by [the auditor](persona_the_auditor.md) through [auditing](process_auditing.md).
It is the spent cure of the trap: the Reliance is the trust [the backup](piece_the_backup.md) invites, spending the margin the [reliability](piece_the_reliability.md) figure counts, so the vigilance the redundancy was to add is subtracted and the risk taken up toward the fall that [the overrun](piece_the_overrun.md) names.
