## Load Bearing

Without it there is no redundancy, no coupling it brings, and no shared cause to fell the set.
With it the trap has its object: a component added in good faith to remove a single point, which the arithmetic rewards as more safety and the coupled system will not let be independent (Sagan, on redundancy as the reflexive answer to risk, more security forces added as more safety when added chances may not be added independence). The Backup is load bearing because it is the thing that looks like pure gain: the spare that genuinely covers a gap and, by that very cover, adds one more part, one more path, one more shared cause, so the piece carries the addition whose usefulness, its standing ready behind the primary, is exactly what wires it into the system it was meant to defend.

## Apparent

A second chance, a spare that can only make the system safer.
Seen at the surface, the Backup looks like added reliability, a component in parallel that removes a real single point and lifts the figure, and the truth that a spare wired and cooled and crewed with what it backs is not the independent part the arithmetic assumed, that its cover comes with a coupling, that adding it may subtract, stays hidden behind how plainly a second chance reads as more safety.
