## Tension

Counting the true independence is the one move that catches the turn before the common cause finds it, and it asks the cost the climbing figure made dear.
It refuses a spare that reads as safe, spends to find a cause no incident has shown, and doubts a number that only climbs. So the plot closes one of two ways: the independence counted and the turn caught before another backup is added ... or the audit waived as arguing against safety, the climbing figure taken for the reliability, and the coupling left for the common cause to find.
