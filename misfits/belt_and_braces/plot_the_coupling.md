# Plot: The Coupling

## Taxonomy

[Belt and Braces](play_belt_and_braces.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The System](place_the_system.md), a backup that must sit inside the whole to back it.
The added spare taking its place, wired and cooled and crewed with the part it covers.

## Action

[Coupling](process_coupling.md): [The Backup](persona_the_backup.md), holding [the backup's seat](position_backup.md), covers the gap from inside the system and opens the paths its presence brings.

## Stage

[The Backup](persona_the_backup.md), on [Cover the Gap](plan_cover_the_gap.md), stands ready behind the primary and, by sitting among the parts, opens [the complexity](piece_the_complexity.md) and rests on [the common mode](piece_the_common_mode.md) it shares with what it backs.
The cover is genuine and the coupling grows with it, one more path between the parts and one more shared cause, unseen because each addition looks local and only the whole is coupled.

## Tension

A backup must sit inside the system to be a backup, and sitting there is exactly what couples it to the part it backs.
There is nothing idle: the spare really covers its gap. But a part wired and cooled and crewed with the rest opens interactions no one drew and shares a cause with the primary, so the plot closes with the gap covered and the system more coupled, the redundancy grown and the independence quietly spent.
