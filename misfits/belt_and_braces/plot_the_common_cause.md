# Plot: The Common Cause

## Taxonomy

[Belt and Braces](play_belt_and_braces.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The System](place_the_system.md), the redundant backups sharing a power, a crew, a premise, an environment.
The one cause gathering, latent, that reaches every spare the arithmetic called independent.

## Action

The coupling come to its reckoning: the one shared fault reaches the redundant set at once and fells the belt and the braces together, [the common mode](piece_the_common_mode.md) firing at last.

## Stage

Each [backup](piece_the_backup.md), stood ready as an independent chance, is reached by the single cause it shared with the rest through [coupling](process_coupling.md), and the many guards go down together.
Only here does it come out that the redundant set was one chance wearing the face of many, [the overrun](piece_the_overrun.md) shown in the open and no one having been reckless.

## Tension

Every backup stood genuinely ready, and the readiness is exactly why the joint failure is a surprise.
There is no recklessness in the set: each spare covered its gap and would have caught a failure that came alone. But nothing separated the shared causes until the fault arrived, so the plot closes with the redundant set felled at a stroke, the many guards revealed as one, and the reliability found, too late, to have turned down beneath a figure that still climbed.
