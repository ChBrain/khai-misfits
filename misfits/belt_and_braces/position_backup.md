# Position: Backup

## Taxonomy

[Belt and Braces](play_belt_and_braces.md). The seat of the added guard, where a gap really covered comes wired and cooled and crewed with the part it backs, so the second chance shares the cause of the first.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A gap to cover and a place inside the system to cover it from: the primary's possible failure and the spare put behind it.
A real cover: the backup genuinely takes the load when the primary fails in the way it was designed to fail alone.
No place apart from the system to stand, and no independence from the power, the crew, the premise or the environment it shares with what it backs.

## Orders

Cover the gap.
Stand behind the primary, take over the load the moment it fails, and be the second chance the design placed you to be, because that is what an added guard is for.
Sit where a backup must sit, wired and cooled and crewed with the rest, since a spare apart from the system could not back it.

## Loses

The independence the arithmetic counted on it, exactly by sitting where a backup must sit.
Because the seat is inside the system and not apart from it, it cannot be the independent chance the figure counted, so it opens one more path between the parts and rests on one more shared cause, and the failure that takes the primary reaches it too, the second pump dying of the same water as the first.
It gains a gap covered when the failure comes alone, and loses the independence that would have made it a second chance when the failure comes shared.

## Drives

Toward the gap covered and the coupling brought,
the second chance that shares the cause of the first,
so the seat that covers the gap stands ready inside the system, and is felled by the one cause that reaches every part it was wired and cooled and crewed among.
