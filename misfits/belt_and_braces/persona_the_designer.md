# Persona: The Designer

## Taxonomy

[Designer](position_designer.md). The one who sees a real single point and adds a backup to remove it, defence in depth, and reads the climbing figure as the system made safer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Thorough. Sure the system is safer for the spare.
Is the one who adds the backup, the engineer, the safety lead, the architect who looks at a single point of failure and puts a second component behind it, and moves on.
No recklessness intended: the Designer sees exactly the failure the backup catches and adds it in good faith, meaning only to close a real gap.

## Action

Adds a redundant component behind the one that could fail, computes the parallel reliability, and takes the higher figure for a safer system.
Removes the single point, wires in the spare, and does not trace whether the spare shares the power, the crew, the premise or the environment of what it backs, because to them the point is that it is a second chance.
Does what a designer does with a known risk: it adds defence in depth, and trusts the added chance to raise the reliability the arithmetic says it raises.

## Shadow

Not careless, an engineer whose backup genuinely closes a real gap, and that is the whole difficulty: the gap is real, and closing it reads as pure gain when the same addition couples the system a little more.
Because the Designer sees the failure the backup catches and not the interactions it opens, the shared cause it rests on, or the watch it relaxes, they take the climbing figure for the true reliability, adding their certainty to the arithmetic's, the defence in depth sending the system out safer on the page and more coupled underneath.
It adds the backup in good faith, and the good faith is exactly what keeps it from seeing that the added part is not the independent one the arithmetic assumed.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "a spare can only make it safer," and believes it.
Says it with a real gap closed and a figure climbing, and cannot see that a backup wired and cooled and crewed with the rest is not the independent chance the number counts, the cover on the page mistaken for reliability in the system.
