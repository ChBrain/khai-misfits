# Position: Auditor

## Taxonomy

[Belt and Braces](play_belt_and_braces.md). The seat that would count the true independence before another backup is added, at the cost of refusing a spare that reads as safe.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A doubt about a figure that only climbs and the standing to trace the shared cause: the will to test the redundancy and the authority to price the coupling.
A real corrective: separating the parts that share a cause from the parts that do not is the one thing that shows whether a backup adds a chance or a coupling.
No cheap way to do it, and no welcome for the one who argues against a defence in depth everyone can see is prudent.

## Orders

Count the independence.
Trace whether each backup shares power, crew, premise or environment with what it backs, price the complexity it brings, and count the true independent chances rather than the number of spares, before another is added.
Doubt the figure that climbs, and treat an easy addition as suspect for reading so plainly as safety.

## Loses

The standing of one who would add safety, and the ease of a figure left to climb.
Because each backup reads as more safety and the number rewards it, the seat must spend to find a shared cause no incident has shown, refuse a spare that looks sound, and doubt a figure that climbs, so it buys the count against every signal that another guard is safer, and the coupling it prevents leaves no trace while the backup it refused is remembered as safety declined.
It gains the true reliability held and the turn caught, and loses the standing and the ease that adding another would have kept.

## Drives

Toward the independence counted and the coupling priced,
at the cost of refusing a backup the figure rewards,
so the seat that counts the independence catches the turn before the common cause finds it, and is read as the one who argued against safety.
