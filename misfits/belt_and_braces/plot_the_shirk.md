# Plot: The Shirk

## Taxonomy

[Belt and Braces](play_belt_and_braces.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The System](place_the_system.md), a guard standing behind each guard and a reserve the operators can feel.
The crew running the system in the knowledge that a backup catches what they miss.

## Action

[Shirking](process_shirking.md): [The Operator](persona_the_operator.md), holding [the operator's seat](position_operator.md), leans on the spare and runs the system to the margin the backup seems to buy.

## Stage

[The Operator](persona_the_operator.md), on [Lean on the Spare](plan_lean_on_the_spare.md), trusts [the backup](piece_the_backup.md) and lets [the reliance](piece_the_reliance.md) spend the margin, watching a little less as responsibility diffuses and running a little closer to the edge.
The reserve the redundancy added is spent back as speed or load or ease, the system sitting as near the fall as before with more parts to fail.

## Tension

Using a real reserve and trusting a real net is not negligence, and the use is exactly how the added safety is spent back.
There is nothing reckless: the margin is real and a covered risk is smaller. But the shared watch is a dropped watch and the used margin is spent safety, so the plot closes with the vigilance diffused and the edge moved out to meet the run, the net that was to catch the fall inviting the leap.
