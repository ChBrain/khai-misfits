# Persona: The Backup

## Taxonomy

[Backup](position_backup.md). The redundant component added to catch what fails, which does its narrow job and, sitting inside the system, brings the coupling and shared cause it cannot see it brings. The archetype of the added guard.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ready. Standing by. There for the moment the first one fails.
Is the second pump, the relief valve, the standby generator, the parallel channel, the part put in to cover the gap the primary might leave.
No fault in it: the Backup does exactly what it was added to do, catching the specific failure it was placed to catch when that failure comes alone.

## Action

Stands behind the primary and covers the gap, taking over the load the moment the first component fails in the way it was designed to fail.
Sits wired to the same bus, cooled by the same water, maintained by the same crew, and trusted by the same operators, because that is where a backup must sit to be a backup.
Does what an added guard does: it covers one gap, and by being one more part inside the system it opens one more path between the parts and rests on one more shared cause.

## Shadow

Not idle, a real component that really covers a real gap, and that is the whole difficulty: the cover is genuine, and the same part that covers the gap couples the system and shares the cause that can fell it with the rest.
Because the Backup is inside the system and not apart from it, it cannot be the independent chance the arithmetic counted, so the second pump dies of the same water as the first, the standby of the same power as the primary, and the guard added to catch the failure is reached by the one cause that reaches them all, its cover on the page and its coupling in the system.
It stands ready in good faith, and the readiness is exactly what hides that the spare and the primary are not two chances but one.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "I am here for when the first one fails," and means it.
Says it wired and cooled and crewed with the very part it backs, and cannot see that the failure which takes the primary may be the failure which takes it too, the second chance and the first sharing the cause that was never redundant.
