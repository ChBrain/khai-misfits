# Process: Shirking

## Taxonomy

[Belt and Braces](play_belt_and_braces.md). Responsibility for the catch diffusing across the redundant set and the operators running closer to the edge, the risk compensation that spends the reserve the backup added.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Another guard standing behind each guard, and a reserve the operators can feel.
At [the system](place_the_system.md), each watcher knowing a backup catches what they miss and running the system accordingly.

## Direction

Toward the watch dropped and the reserve spent.
Because a guard stands behind each guard, shirking lets responsibility for the catch diffuse across the redundant set, each watching a little less on the trust that the other watches, and because the operators know a net exists they run closer to the edge, so the margin the backup added is spent back as speed or load or ease, the system sitting as near the fall as before with more parts to fail.

## Lever

The reality of the reserve the backup provides.
Because the backup genuinely widens the margin and a covered risk genuinely is smaller, shirking runs on a rational response to a real reserve and not on negligence, so the spent safety is assembled from no bad faith, only from operators using a margin that is really there and watchers trusting a guard that really stands behind them.

## Echo

A reserve provided, and a reserve spent back.
The operators have used a real margin as sensibly as it was offered, so what reads as safety in hand is, in truth, the added reliability compensated back into risk, the vigilance diffused and the edge moved out to meet the run, the net that was to catch the fall inviting the leap.
