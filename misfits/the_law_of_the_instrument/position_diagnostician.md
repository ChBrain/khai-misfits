# Position: Diagnostician

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The seat that names the problem before the tool is chosen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The question the reflex skips: what is the problem, actually.
No favourite instrument, and so no reason to see a nail before there is one.
The one order that would meet the case on its own terms.

## Orders

Name the problem first.
Ask whether it is a nail at all, and let the answer choose the tool.
Reach for fit, even when fit means the instrument the hand is worst with.

## Loses

The room, and the fluent expert reach it interrupts.
Its counsel asks the master to set down the mastery, so it is right and slow and unwelcome, and it cannot promise the fitted tool will be wielded as well as the reflex one, especially when the reflex one was correct.
The seat that would meet the problem honestly is the one that costs the house its speed and its certainty.

## Drives

Toward the problem named whole,
against a reflex that already knows the answer,
so the honest diagnosis arrives too slow and too humble to beat the master's practised reach.
