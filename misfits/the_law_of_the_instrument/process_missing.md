# Process: Missing

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The strike landing clean on the nail while the real problem, trimmed away, goes unreached.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A nail struck home, and a residue left where the tool could not go.
At [the aftermath](place_the_aftermath.md), the treatment complete on the reshaped case and the remainder beginning to surface.

## Direction

Toward a problem solved on the wrong target.
The instrument does exactly what it does, cleanly and well, so the reshaped face is handled and the amputated part is not, and the case reads as solved while the aspect that was the real problem festers untouched and returns.

## Lever

A fix that is genuine on its nail and absent everywhere else.
Because the tool truly solves what it fits, the strike is real and looks like success, so the miss hides inside a true result, and the residue is not seen as unsolved but as a new and separate trouble with no line back to the treatment that skipped it.

## Echo

A perfect strike and an open problem.
The nail driven home, the instrument vindicated on its own terms, and the real problem still there, exactly where the tool was never able to reach.
