# Plan: Name the Problem First

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the diagnostician's seat](position_diagnostician.md), inside [The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Diagnostician](position_diagnostician.md)

## Direction

Name the problem whole before any tool is reached for. Ask whether it is a nail at all, let the answer choose the instrument, and reach for fit even when fit means the tool the hand is worst with. Meet the case on its own terms, and put the diagnosis before the reflex.

## Orders

([The Diagnostician](persona_the_diagnostician.md) names the problem before choosing the tool, right and slow and unwelcome, unable to promise the fitted tool is wielded as well as the reflex one.)

## Implementation

([The Toolroom](place_the_toolroom.md); the [diagnosing](process_diagnosing.md) process; naming the problem first answers [the law of the instrument](piece_the_law_of_the_instrument.md) by putting diagnosis before [the reflex](piece_the_reflex.md), at the cost of the master's practised reach.)

## Targets

- [ ] the problem is named whole before any tool is reached for
- [ ] whether it is a nail at all is asked, and the answer chooses the tool
- [ ] fit is reached for over familiarity, even into an unmastered instrument
- [ ] the cost is borne that the diagnosis is slow, humble, and sometimes beaten by a correct reflex
