# Persona: The Wielder

## Taxonomy

[Wielder](position_wielder.md). Master of the one tool, and reaching for it before the problem is read.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Expert, fluent, quick to act.
Has poured years into one instrument and wields it beautifully, and knows it.
Sees a problem and already knows how it will be solved.

## Action

Reaches for the mastered tool the moment a problem appears.
Sees the problem in the tool's terms, promotes its strikeable face to the whole, and works.
Solves, cleanly and expertly, the version of the problem the tool fits.

## Shadow

Its mastery is real and specific, so the reflex that makes it excellent is the reflex that makes it blind, reaching before it diagnoses.
It cannot see the problem outside the tool, because the tool is how it sees.
The deeper the skill, the surer the reach, and the surer the reach, the less the problem is ever met on its own terms.

## Tell

Says "I know exactly what this needs," before asking what it is.
Of a problem it has already, unknowingly, reshaped into its tool's nail.
