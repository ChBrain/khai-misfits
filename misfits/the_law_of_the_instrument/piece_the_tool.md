# Piece: The Tool

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The mastered instrument, genuinely excellent at its nail and blind to all else.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), where it is wielded.
The one sharp instrument, reached for the instant a problem appears.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no law, only a hand with no particular skill.
With it the trap has its engine: the tool is a real, narrow excellence, superb on the problems shaped like its nail, so it earns the trust that makes it the first reach, and it shapes the hand and the eye that use it until problems come to be seen in its terms. The Tool is load bearing because its virtue is its danger (Maslow's hammer): it is not a bad instrument misused but a good one over-reached, and the better it is the more it is reached for, so the very fitness that makes reaching rational is what makes the world start to look like its nails.

## Apparent

Simply the right instrument.
A tool that solves its nail cleanly every time looks like pure capability, so it reads as the answer, and the truth that it is one narrow motion, powerless on everything it does not fit and unable to flag what it cannot touch, stays hidden behind the record of everything it does when the problem really is its nail.

## Yearbook

Wielded by [the wielder](persona_the_wielder.md) through [reaching](process_reaching.md), tabled as [Be the Answer](plan_be_the_answer.md).
It is the instrument the law turns on: the Tool shapes the hand through [the mastery](piece_the_mastery.md), is reached for by [the reflex](piece_the_reflex.md), and answers every case as its nail, right exactly as often as the case really was one.
