# Persona: The Nail

## Taxonomy

[Casework](position_casework.md). The problem recast as the tool's target, its strikeable face made the whole of it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Presentable, tractable, exactly the kind of thing the tool handles.
Arrives as one problem and is received as another: the version the instrument fits.
Wears, by the time it reaches the bench, the shape of a nail.

## Action

Offers the face the tool can strike and lets the rest fall from view.
Takes the instrument's shape, so the strike can land square and look like a solution.
Presents as solved the moment it is struck, whatever remained of the real problem underneath.

## Shadow

It is not the problem that came in but the problem the tool could see, so its clean fit is the sign of how much was left out.
The better it suits the instrument, the more of the original it has shed to do so.
The case that fits the tool perfectly is the case most reshaped to fit it.

## Tell

Looks, by the bench, precisely like the tool's kind of job.
Because everything about it that was not has already been trimmed away.
