# The Law of the Instrument: Reference

The research warrant for [The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The title is
Kaplan's law of the instrument, popularised as Maslow's hammer; the play runs on the cognitive account
of tool-driven problem framing, from functional fixedness and the Einstellung effect, all cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging the law of the instrument as a **cognitive system**: a hand that has mastered one tool reaches
for it before the problem is diagnosed, so the problem is reshaped to the tool rather than the tool
chosen to the problem, its strikeable face promoted to the whole and the part the tool cannot reach
trimmed off and lost. The play models the documented mechanics, the mastered tool that is genuinely
excellent at its nail, the reflex that reaches before diagnosing, the nail that is the problem recast
as the tool's target, the amputation that cuts away whatever will not fit, the miss by which the real
problem returns unsolved where the tool could not reach, and the mastery whose depth fixes the reach
and prices the switch, as the load-bearing vectors of the structure, not as a tale of incompetence.
The governing finding of the source literature is the play's governing law: the tools one has mastered
shape the problems one perceives, so that to a hand with a hammer everything looks like a nail, and the
instrument comes to decide not only how the problem is solved but what the problem is taken to be. It
is a cousin of the house's Cargo Cult, and its distinct engine: not the empty form of a success copied
without its cause, but a genuinely powerful tool over-reached because it is the one the hand has
mastered, with the problem bent to fit it. Its near-neighbour, the Streetlight Effect, turns on a
different pivot: searching where the light is easiest, not applying the tool one is expert in.

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast the
  wielder as incompetent, because the source finding is precisely that the tool works and the reach is
  rational: the mastery is real, the instrument genuinely is the best answer to everything shaped like
  its nail, and reaching for it first is the honest return on years of specialisation. The misfit is
  that the same mastery that makes the tool worth reaching for is what makes every problem look like
  its nail, so a real expert solves, cleanly, a problem it half invented.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into a costless correction. The one move that
  works is to diagnose the problem before choosing the tool and reach for fit over familiarity, and it
  means setting down a mastered instrument for one the hand is a novice with, worse before better,
  against the symmetric error that the generalist who holds many tools masters none and strikes the
  right nail clumsily, and the plain fact that sometimes the reflex tool was correct. The reckoning is
  a master's clean strike traded for a fitter but weaker one, not a free upgrade.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here,
  never claimed as the house's. The staging is the house's; the law of the instrument, Maslow's
  hammer, functional fixedness, and the Einstellung effect are theirs, and the condition the law
  actually requires, a genuinely mastered tool whose fitness narrows the sight of the hand that holds
  it, is carried as carefully as the finding itself.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **[The Tool](piece_the_tool.md)**: carries the mastered instrument (Kaplan; Maslow), the real, narrow excellence that
  earns the first reach and shapes the eye that uses it.
- **[The Reflex](piece_the_reflex.md)**: carries the fixation of set (Luchins; Duncker), the reach that fires before the
  diagnosis and forecloses the problem's own terms.
- **[The Nail](piece_the_nail.md)**: carries the tool-shaped problem (Maslow's hammer), the case recast as the
  instrument's target, one face promoted to the whole.
- **[The Amputation](piece_the_amputation.md)**: carries the narrowing of perception (Duncker), the parts that do not fit
  the tool cut away as noise to make the fit.
- **[The Miss](piece_the_miss.md)**: carries the blocked better solution (Luchins), the real problem left unsolved where
  the instrument could not reach, returning behind a clean strike.
- **[The Mastery](piece_the_mastery.md)**: carries the specialised investment that fixes the reach, the house's Path
  Dependence worn as skill: depth so worth having it stops the hand from choosing the fitting tool.
- **[The Law of the Instrument](piece_the_law_of_the_instrument.md)**: carries the phenomenon whole (Kaplan; Maslow), the
  mastered tool deciding what the problem is, and its remedy in diagnosis before choice at a cost.
