# Position: Generalist

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The seat that holds many tools, and carries the remedy's own cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A whole kit, and no attachment to any one tool in it.
The reach to match the problem to the means across more kinds of case than any master could.
Less depth in every tool than the specialist who lives inside it.

## Orders

Hold many tools.
Pick by fit, not by familiarity, and match the instrument to the problem.
Meet the case on its own terms, with whatever in the kit suits it.

## Loses

The clean strike, in trade for the right target.
Its breadth is its bind, so the hand that holds many tools masters none, fitting the problem better and striking it worse, and it stands as the proof that the remedy has a price rather than the hero who escapes the law.
Reaching for fit over mastery can trade an expert blow on the wrong nail for a clumsy one on the right.

## Drives

Toward fit,
at the cost of mastery,
so the seat that answers the law is the seat that shows its cure and its disease are two faces of one coin.
