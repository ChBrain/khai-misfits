# Position: Casework

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The seat where the problem arrives and is recast as the tool's target.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A real problem, whole, with many faces.
One face the tool can strike, and several it cannot.
No say in which face is taken for the problem.

## Orders

Take the tool's shape.
Offer the strikeable face and let the rest fall from view.
Present as the tool's kind of job, so the strike can land square.

## Loses

Everything about itself that the tool could not see.
It is received not as the problem that came in but as the version the instrument fits, so its clean fit is the measure of how much was shed, and the better it suits the tool the more of the original it has lost.
The case that fits the instrument perfectly is the case most reshaped to fit it.

## Drives

Toward the tool's shape,
by shedding whatever did not fit,
so the problem is solved as a nail exactly to the degree it has stopped being itself.
