# Place: The Toolroom

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). Where the remedy is weighed: diagnose first and reach for fit, or the reflex holds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A whole wall of tools, and a move to choose among them by the problem rather than the habit.
The diagnostician asking what the problem is before any instrument is lifted, the generalist reaching for fit across the kit, the wielder pulled back to the one tool it has mastered.

## Holds

The remedy, and its two costs.
Here the problem can be named before the tool is chosen and matched to the instrument that fits, and here that means setting down a mastery for a novice's grip and betting the many-tooled hand can strike at all, so the toolroom holds both the honest cure and the price it charges, the right tool and the worse hand that now wields it.

## Offers

The chance to meet the problem on its own terms.
By putting diagnosis before the reach and fit before familiarity, the toolroom offers the one route to the right treatment of the real problem, the case answered as itself and not as a nail.

## Withheld

The master's clean strike, and any promise the cure was needed.
The fitted tool is wielded worse than the reflex one, and sometimes the reflex one was right, so the toolroom withholds both the expert blow and the certainty that trading it was wise, and reaches for fit at the risk of a master traded for a dabbler.
