# Process: Reaching

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The mastered tool reached for by reflex, before the problem is diagnosed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A problem appearing, and a hand that already knows how it will be solved.
In [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), the wielder's reach firing the instant a case arrives, ahead of any question about what it is.

## Direction

Toward the instrument, chosen before the problem is understood.
The tool that has solved a hundred like this is lifted first, the case is seen in its terms, and the reflex settles the how before anyone has settled the what, so the problem is committed to the tool at the moment it is noticed.

## Lever

A mastery so deep the reach is faster than the thought.
Because years poured into one instrument make reaching for it the honest return on the skill, the reflex fires before the diagnosis and feels like competence, so the tool is picked not despite expertise but because of it.

## Echo

An instrument chosen, and a problem not yet met.
The reach complete, the terms set by the tool, and the case already on its way to being reshaped into the nail the instrument can strike.
