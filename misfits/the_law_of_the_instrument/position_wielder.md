# Position: Wielder

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The seat that has mastered the one tool and reaches for it first.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A deep, specific mastery, poured into one instrument over years.
The reflex to reach for it, and the record of a hundred problems it has solved.
No second tool it can wield with the same hand.

## Orders

Reach for the mastered tool.
See the problem in its terms, promote the strikeable face to the whole, and work.
Trust the reflex, because the reflex is the skill.

## Loses

The problem itself, met on its own terms.
Its mastery is real and reaches before it diagnoses, so it solves the version of the problem the tool fits and never sees the version that came in, and the deeper the skill the surer and blinder the reach.
The most expert hand is the one that most reshapes the problem before it looks at it.

## Drives

Toward the mastered motion,
against a problem it has already recast as the tool's nail,
so the competence that makes the tool worth reaching for is the competence that hides everything the tool cannot strike.
