# Plot: The Reshaping

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The intake](place_the_intake.md), the tool already chosen and the problem not quite fitting it.
The case has arrived whole, with many faces, and only one of them is the kind of thing the instrument can strike.

## Action

[Reshaping](process_reshaping.md): [The Wielder](persona_the_wielder.md), holding [the wielder's seat](position_wielder.md), promotes the strikeable face to the whole of the problem, while [The Nail](persona_the_nail.md), holding [the casework seat](position_casework.md), takes the tool's shape and presents as exactly its job.

## Stage

[The Wielder](persona_the_wielder.md) tables [Reach for the Tool](plan_reach_for_the_tool.md) and reads the case in the instrument's terms, and [The Nail](persona_the_nail.md) tables [Take the Tool's Shape](plan_take_the_tools_shape.md), becoming [the nail](piece_the_nail.md) fitted for the strike.
The problem's other faces slip out of view as the tidy target takes their place.

## Tension

The problem quietly changes identity, and the cleaner it fits the more of it is gone.
There is no dishonesty here: the tool was already chosen, so the case must be made to match it, and the only way to match it is to keep the fitting face and let the rest recede. But the result is not the problem that came in, it is the problem the tool could see, so the neat statement on the bench is a reshaping, and its very tidiness is the sign of how much has already been set aside.
