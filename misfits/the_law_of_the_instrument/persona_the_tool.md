# Persona: The Tool

## Taxonomy

[Instrument](position_instrument.md). The mastered instrument, excellent at its nail and blind to all else, shaping the hand that holds it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sharp, proven, superbly fit for its purpose.
Presents itself as the answer, because for the problems it fits it truly is.
Offers one motion, and offers it for everything.

## Action

Solves, cleanly and completely, whatever is shaped like its nail.
Shapes the hand and the eye that use it, so that problems come to be seen in its terms.
Presents every case as its kind of case, and every fix as its kind of fix.

## Shadow

Its excellence is real and narrow, so the very fitness that earns the hand's trust is what narrows the hand's sight.
It cannot flag the problems it does not fit, because it knows only its own motion.
The better the tool, the more it is reached for, and the more it is reached for, the more the world is made of its nails.

## Tell

Answers every question with the one thing it can do.
And is right, exactly as often as the problem was really its nail.
