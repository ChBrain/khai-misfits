# Piece: The Nail

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The problem recast as the tool's target, its strikeable face made the whole of it.

## Owner

## Place

At [the intake](place_the_intake.md), where the case is reshaped.
Where the problem arrives whole and leaves shaped like the tool's job.

## Load Bearing

Without it the tool could not be applied at all, and the mismatch would simply stop the work.
With it the mismatch is hidden instead of felt: the nail is the problem after reshaping, the one face the instrument can strike promoted to the whole, so the case can be worked as though it were exactly the tool's kind of job. The Nail is load bearing because it is where the problem quietly changes identity: it is not the problem that came in but the problem the tool could see, so its clean fit is the proof of how much was left out, and the case that suits the instrument best is the one most reshaped to suit it.

## Apparent

Just the problem, clearly stated.
A case that fits the tool so neatly looks like a well-understood problem, so the nail reads as clarity, and the truth that the fit was manufactured by promoting one face and cutting the rest, that the tidy statement is a reshaping, stays hidden behind how obviously it is the tool's kind of job.
