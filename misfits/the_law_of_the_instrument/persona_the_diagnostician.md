# Persona: The Diagnostician

## Taxonomy

[Diagnostician](position_diagnostician.md). The one who would name the problem before choosing the tool, and reach for fit over familiarity.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Patient, tool-agnostic, interested first in what the problem actually is.
Wants the diagnosis to precede the instrument, so the case is met on its own terms and not the tool's.
Holds no favourite, only the question.

## Action

Names the problem whole, before any tool is reached for.
Asks whether it is a nail at all, and lets the answer choose the instrument.
Reaches for fit, even when fit means the tool the hand is worst with.

## Shadow

Its counsel asks the master to set down the mastery, so it is right and slow and unwelcome.
It cannot promise the diagnosed tool will be wielded as well as the reflex tool would have been, and sometimes the reflex tool was correct.
The voice that would meet the problem honestly is the voice that costs the room its fluent, expert reach.

## Tell

Asks "what is the problem," when the wielder already knows the answer.
And is resented for it, most when the wielder's answer would have been right.
