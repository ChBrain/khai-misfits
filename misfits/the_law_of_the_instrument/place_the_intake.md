# Place: The Intake

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). Where the problem is received and reshaped into the tool's nail.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The problem arriving whole and leaving as a nail: its strikeable face promoted to the whole of it, its other faces trimmed away.
The case recast as exactly the job the hand is best at, and the remainder set quietly aside.

## Holds

The nail and the amputation.
Here the problem is fitted to the instrument by making one face the whole and cutting the rest off as noise, so the intake holds both the reshaped case that will be struck and the residue removed to let it fit, the construction of the fit and its cost.

## Offers

A problem that fits the tool perfectly.
By promoting the strikeable face and trimming the rest, the intake offers a clean nail for a clean strike, a case that suits the mastered instrument as though it had been made for it.

## Withheld

The problem as it actually came in.
The reshaping keeps only the face the tool fits, so the intake withholds the whole of the real problem, and the very cleanness of the nail it hands on is the sign of how much was left behind.
