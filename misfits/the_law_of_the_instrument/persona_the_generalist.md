# Persona: The Generalist

## Taxonomy

[Generalist](position_generalist.md). The one who holds many tools, and the trap in that remedy.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Broad, adaptable, carrying a whole kit rather than one loved instrument.
Reaches for the tool that fits, unattached to any, ready to match the problem to the means.
Wide where the wielder is deep.

## Action

Holds many tools and picks by fit, not by familiarity.
Meets more kinds of problem than any single master could.
Wields each one less well than the specialist who lives inside it.

## Shadow

Its breadth is its own bind: the hand that holds many tools masters none, so it fits the problem better and strikes it worse.
It is the standing proof that the remedy has a cost, not a hero who escapes the law but the other face of it.
Reaching for fit over mastery can trade a clean strike on the wrong nail for a clumsy one on the right.

## Tell

Says "the right tool for the job," and reaches for the one it can barely use.
Fitting the problem better than the master, and serving it, sometimes, worse.
