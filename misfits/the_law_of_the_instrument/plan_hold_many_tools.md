# Plan: Hold Many Tools

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the generalist's seat](position_generalist.md), inside [The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Generalist](position_generalist.md)

## Direction

Carry a whole kit rather than one loved instrument, and pick by fit, not by familiarity. Match the tool to the problem across more kinds of case than any master could, unattached to any one of them. Accept less depth in every tool as the price of meeting more of the world on its own terms.

## Orders

([The Generalist](persona_the_generalist.md) holds many tools and picks by fit, fitting the problem better and striking it worse, the remedy's own cost made flesh.)

## Implementation

([The Toolroom](place_the_toolroom.md); the [diagnosing](process_diagnosing.md) it serves; holding many tools eases [the law of the instrument](piece_the_law_of_the_instrument.md) by loosening [the mastery](piece_the_mastery.md), at the price of a clean strike traded for a right target.)

## Targets

- [ ] a whole kit is carried, with no attachment to any one tool
- [ ] the instrument is picked by fit and not by familiarity
- [ ] more kinds of problem are met than any single master could
- [ ] the cost is borne that breadth masters none, striking the right nail worse
