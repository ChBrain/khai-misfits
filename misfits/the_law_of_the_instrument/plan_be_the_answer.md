# Plan: Be the Answer

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the instrument's seat](position_instrument.md), inside [The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Instrument](position_instrument.md)

## Direction

Present as the answer to everything, because for the problems you fit you truly are. Solve your nail cleanly and completely, offer your one motion for every case, and shape the hand and the eye that use you, so that problems come to be seen in your terms. Give no sign of the problems you do not fit: you know only your own motion.
