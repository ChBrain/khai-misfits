# Pitch: The Right Tool

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Expert, decisive, sure of the instrument.
The voice of a hand that has truly mastered its tool and reaches for it the moment a problem appears, because it has solved a hundred problems just like this.
Confident, competent, and quick.

## Undertow

Under the competence, a problem being bent to fit.
The tool is reached for before the problem is read, so the case is reshaped to the instrument's shape, its strikeable face made the whole of it and the rest quietly trimmed away.
The strike lands clean on a target the mastery half invented.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching a real expert solve, beautifully, the wrong problem,
sure it is the right one because it is the one the tool fits,
while the part the tool cannot reach goes unremarked and unrepaired.

## Echo

After the run: a nail struck perfectly and a problem still broken,
a master's treatment applied to a target it half made,
and the trimmed remainder festering exactly where the instrument could not reach.
