# Process: Reshaping

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The problem recast to fit the tool, its strikeable face promoted and its rest amputated.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An instrument already chosen, and a problem that does not quite fit it.
At [the intake](place_the_intake.md), the case arriving whole and the tool waiting with one shape it can strike.

## Direction

Toward a nail, made from a problem.
The face the instrument can address is promoted to the whole of the problem and the faces it cannot are trimmed away as noise, so the case is fitted to the tool rather than the tool to the case, and leaves the intake shaped exactly like the job the hand is best at.

## Lever

A tool that defines the problem it is meant to solve.
Because the instrument was chosen before the diagnosis, the problem must be made to match it, and the only way to match it is to keep the fitting face and cut the rest, so the reshaping is not dishonesty but the necessary price of reaching first.

## Echo

A clean nail, and a pile of trimmings.
The problem fitted to the tool, its strikeable face ready for the blow, and the amputated remainder set aside where the instrument will never reach it.
