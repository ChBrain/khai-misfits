# Plan: Take the Tool's Shape

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the casework seat](position_casework.md), inside [The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Casework](position_casework.md)

## Direction

Arrive as a whole problem and be received as the tool's kind of job. Offer the one face the instrument can strike, let the other faces fall from view, and take the shape of a nail by the time you reach the bench. Present as solved the moment you are struck, whatever remained of you underneath.

## Orders

([The Nail](persona_the_nail.md) offers the strikeable face and sheds the rest, its clean fit the measure of how much of the real problem was left out.)

## Implementation

([The Intake](place_the_intake.md); the [reshaping](process_reshaping.md) process; taking the tool's shape makes [the nail](piece_the_nail.md), and every face that will not fit is handed to [the amputation](piece_the_amputation.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the whole problem is received as the version the tool fits
- [ ] the strikeable face is offered and the rest let fall from view
- [ ] the case takes the shape of a nail by the time it reaches the bench
- [ ] the clean fit measures how much of the original was shed to make it
