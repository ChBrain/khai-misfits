# Persona: The Residue

## Taxonomy

[Remainder](position_remainder.md). The part of the real problem the tool cannot reach, trimmed off to make the fit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Quiet, off to the side, not the tool's kind of thing.
The aspect of the problem the instrument has no motion for, set aside as noise so the strike can land clean.
Present the whole time, and never on the bench.

## Action

Falls outside the tool's reach and is trimmed away to make the case fit.
Waits, unaddressed, where the instrument could not go.
Festers, and returns, as the part that was the real problem all along.

## Shadow

It is cut off precisely because it did not fit, so the cleaner the strike, the more of it was left behind.
It is invisible in the wielder's terms, because the tool that defines the problem cannot name what it cannot touch.
The part amputated to fit the tool is the part the tool most needed to reach.

## Tell

Returns, later, as the thing that was never actually solved.
Having been, from the first, the piece the instrument was chosen not to see.
