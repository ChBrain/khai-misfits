# Piece: The Reflex

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The reach for the mastered tool, fired before the problem is diagnosed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), where the reach is trained and fires.
In the instant between a problem appearing and the tool being lifted.

## Load Bearing

Without it the tool would wait to be chosen, and the diagnosis could come first.
With it the order is fixed the wrong way: the reflex is the reach for the instrument the moment a case appears, faster than the thought, so the how is settled before the what and the problem is committed to the tool at the instant it is noticed. The Reflex is load bearing because it is where the trap actually springs (Luchins' Einstellung; functional fixedness): it is not carelessness but trained skill, the honest return on deep practice, so the very reflex that marks the master is the one that reshapes the problem before it is ever seen.

## Apparent

Decisiveness.
A hand that knows at once how a problem will be solved looks like expertise in action, so the reflex reads as competence, and the truth that it fires before the diagnosis and forecloses the problem's own terms stays hidden behind the speed and sureness that make it look like mastery.

## Yearbook

Fired by [the wielder](persona_the_wielder.md) through [reaching](process_reaching.md), tabled as [Reach for the Tool](plan_reach_for_the_tool.md).
It is the reach that comes too soon: the Reflex is [the mastery](piece_the_mastery.md) turned to speed, lifting [the tool](piece_the_tool.md) before the problem is met, and is what [Name the Problem First](plan_name_the_problem_first.md) must put a diagnosis in front of.
