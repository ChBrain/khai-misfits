# Plot: The Reach

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The workshop](place_the_workshop.md), a problem appearing on the bench.
Before anyone asks what it is, the mastered tool is already being lifted, and the instrument sits ready, presenting itself as the answer to whatever has come.

## Action

[Reaching](process_reaching.md): [The Wielder](persona_the_wielder.md), holding [the wielder's seat](position_wielder.md), reaches for the tool the instant the problem appears, while [The Tool](persona_the_tool.md), holding [the instrument's seat](position_instrument.md), offers its one clean motion for the case.

## Stage

[The Wielder](persona_the_wielder.md) tables [Reach for the Tool](plan_reach_for_the_tool.md) and fires [the reflex](piece_the_reflex.md), settling the how before the what.
[The Tool](persona_the_tool.md) tables [Be the Answer](plan_be_the_answer.md), and its fitness draws the reach, right exactly as often as the case really is its nail.

## Tension

The problem is committed to the tool at the instant it is noticed, and by the most skilled hand in the room.
Nothing here is a mistake to scold: the wielder's mastery is real, the reflex is the honest return on years of it, and the tool genuinely is the best answer to everything shaped like its nail. But the reach comes before the diagnosis, so the instrument is chosen before the problem is understood, and the case is already, silently, on its way to being reshaped into the one thing the tool can strike.
