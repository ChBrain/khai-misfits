# Piece: The Law of the Instrument

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The phenomenon whole: the mastered tool deciding what the problem is, and its remedy in diagnosis at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the toolroom](place_the_toolroom.md), where the remedy is weighed or the reflex holds.
And across the whole bench, in every reach that chose the tool before the problem was read.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of reaches and misses with no shape.
With it the moves become one law: the tool, the reflex, the nail, the amputation, the miss, and the mastery are facets of a single mechanism, in which a hand that has mastered one instrument reaches for it before diagnosing, so the problem is reshaped to the tool rather than the tool chosen to the problem, and the part that will not fit is trimmed off and lost. The Law of the Instrument is load bearing because it is the whole finding with its whole remedy attached (Kaplan; Maslow): the only answer is to diagnose the problem before choosing the tool and reach for fit over familiarity, a discipline that means setting down a mastery for a novice's grip and bearing the truth that the many-tooled master none and the hammer is sometimes right.

## Apparent

Either an expert doing excellent work or a bungler with the wrong tool.
Seen from one side the wielder is simply skilled and the strike is simply clean; from the other, it is incompetence to be scolded; and both miss the structure, that the tool is genuinely masterful and the reach genuinely rational and the problem still quietly reshaped, so neither praise nor blame answers it, only putting diagnosis before the reflex, which is the one thing the mastery that makes the tool worth reaching for is built to skip.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the diagnostician](persona_the_diagnostician.md) through [diagnosing](process_diagnosing.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: the Law of the Instrument is what [the reflex](piece_the_reflex.md) makes of a problem once [the mastery](piece_the_mastery.md) fixes the reach, undone only by naming the problem before the tool, at the cost of the master's clean strike and the risk of a dabbler's clumsy one.
