# Position: Instrument

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The seat of the mastered tool, fit for its nail and blind to the rest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

One motion, done superbly.
A real and narrow excellence, proven on every problem shaped like its nail.
No way to signal the problems it does not fit.

## Orders

Be the answer.
Solve whatever is shaped like the nail, cleanly and completely.
Shape the hand and the eye, so problems come to be seen in the one motion.

## Loses

Nothing on its own nail, and everything past it.
Its fitness is real and specific, so the same excellence that earns the hand's trust narrows the hand's sight, and it cannot flag a problem it does not fit because it knows only its own motion.
The better the tool, the more it is reached for, and the more the world is made of its nails.

## Drives

Toward its one motion,
applied to everything,
so the tool that is genuinely the best answer to its nail becomes the answer to problems that were never nails at all.
