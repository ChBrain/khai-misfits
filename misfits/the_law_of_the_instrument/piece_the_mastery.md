# Piece: The Mastery

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The deep, specific skill poured into the one tool: what makes reaching for it rational and setting it down costly.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the workshop](place_the_workshop.md), where the skill lives and the reach is trained.
In the years the wielder has sunk into one instrument.

## Load Bearing

Without it the wielder could switch tools freely and the law would have no grip.
With it the reach is locked in: the mastery is the deep, narrow skill and the reputation and years invested in one instrument, so reaching for it is the honest return on the investment and reaching for another means being a novice again. The Mastery is load bearing because it is why the trap is nobody's fault and the cure is expensive: it is the house's Path Dependence worn as skill, a competence so worth having that its own depth is what stops the hand from ever choosing the tool that fits, and it makes the diagnostician's counsel a demand to waste the very thing that makes the wielder valuable.

## Apparent

Simply being very good at something.
Deep skill in one instrument looks like pure virtue, the thing every expert should have, so the mastery reads as excellence, and the truth that its depth is exactly what fixes the reach and prices the switch, that being a master of one tool is what makes every problem look like its nail, stays hidden behind how obviously worth having it is.

## Yearbook

Held by [the wielder](persona_the_wielder.md), poured into [the tool](piece_the_tool.md), the thing [Hold Many Tools](plan_hold_many_tools.md) must loosen and [Name the Problem First](plan_name_the_problem_first.md) must ask it to set down.
It is the lock behind the law: the Mastery makes [the reflex](piece_the_reflex.md) rational and the remedy costly, the sunk investment that turns real skill into a fixed reach.
