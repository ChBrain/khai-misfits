# Plot: The Trimming

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The intake](place_the_intake.md), the fit not yet clean and something having to give.
The nail is nearly ready, but faces of the real problem still stand outside the tool's reach, and the strike cannot land square until they are gone.

## Action

[Reshaping](process_reshaping.md) at its cost: [The Nail](persona_the_nail.md), holding [the casework seat](position_casework.md), sheds the last unfitting faces, while [The Residue](persona_the_residue.md), holding [the remainder's seat](position_remainder.md), is trimmed away as noise so the case can sit clean.

## Stage

[The Nail](persona_the_nail.md) tables [Take the Tool's Shape](plan_take_the_tools_shape.md) and lets the crux fall from view, and [The Residue](persona_the_residue.md) tables [Fall Outside the Reach](plan_fall_outside_the_reach.md), becoming [the amputation](piece_the_amputation.md) set aside where the tool will never go.
What is cut is called noise, and is often the real problem.

## Tension

The fit is bought by discarding exactly the parts that did not fit, and no one sees the price.
Trimming the case to its strikeable essentials looks like sensible focus, so the amputation passes as discipline, not damage. But the instrument that defines the problem cannot name what it cannot touch, so the residue is invisible in the wielder's terms, cut without a mark, and the crux of the matter is set aside precisely because it was the part the tool could not reach.
