# Place: The Aftermath

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). Where the struck nail looks solved and the trimmed residue returns.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The strike landed clean and the nail driven home, the treatment textbook and complete.
And, off where the tool could not reach, the residue that was trimmed away beginning to fester, the real problem surfacing behind the perfect fix.

## Holds

The miss, in full.
Here the reshaped problem is solved and the real one is not, so the aftermath holds both the clean success on the tool's nail and the untouched remainder that was the problem all along, the treatment that worked and the target it worked on.

## Offers

The look of a job well done.
The nail was struck expertly and the instrument performed exactly as it should, so the aftermath offers every sign of success, a clean result that reads as the problem solved.

## Withheld

Any credit for solving the actual problem.
The tool reached only its nail, so the aftermath withholds the outcome the case really needed, and the festering residue proves the fix perfect and the problem still open, the strike true and the target half invented.
