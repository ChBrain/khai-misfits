# Plot: The Miss

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The aftermath](place_the_aftermath.md), the strike landed and the nail driven home.
The treatment was textbook and complete, and off where the tool could not reach, the trimmed part has begun to surface as the real problem all along.

## Action

[Missing](process_missing.md): [The Residue](persona_the_residue.md), holding [the remainder's seat](position_remainder.md), returns as the unsolved crux, while [The Tool](persona_the_tool.md), holding [the instrument's seat](position_instrument.md), stands vindicated on its nail and untouched by what it could not fit.

## Stage

[The Residue](persona_the_residue.md) tables [Fall Outside the Reach](plan_fall_outside_the_reach.md) once more and comes back as [the miss](piece_the_miss.md), the crux the strike was aimed away from.
[The Tool](persona_the_tool.md) rests on a clean, complete performance, so the failure reads as a new and separate trouble with no line back to it.

## Tension

The fix is perfect and the problem is still open, and the two facts do not seem to touch.
The tool did exactly what it does, cleanly and well, so the strike is a genuine success and hides the miss inside itself: the reshaped face is solved, the amputated crux is not, and the returning residue looks like fresh bad luck rather than the part the treatment was chosen to skip. The instrument is apt and partial, the strike true and the target half invented, and no one traces the open problem back to the clean blow that missed it.
