# Place: The Workshop

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). Where the mastered tool is wielded and reached for first.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The bench of a real expert: the one instrument, kept sharp, reached for the instant a problem appears.
The wielder working it beautifully, the tool answering every case with its one clean motion.

## Holds

The tool, the reflex, and the mastery behind them.
Here the instrument is wielded and here the reach for it is trained into a reflex, so the workshop holds both the genuine excellence and the reflex that fires it before any diagnosis, the skill and the blindness that are the same skill.

## Offers

A fast, expert, confident solution.
The tool is mastered and the reach is practised, so the workshop offers a clean strike and a sure hand, the comfort of a problem met by someone who has met a hundred like it.

## Withheld

Any pause to ask what the problem is.
The reflex fires ahead of the diagnosis and the tool defines the terms, so the workshop withholds the moment that would let the problem be seen outside the instrument, and the strike lands before the question is ever asked.
