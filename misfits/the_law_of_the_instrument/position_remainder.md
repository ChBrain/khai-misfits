# Position: Remainder

## Taxonomy

[The Law of the Instrument](play_the_law_of_the_instrument.md). The seat of the part the tool cannot reach, trimmed off to make the fit.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The aspect of the problem the instrument has no motion for.
Often the aspect that was the real problem all along.
No place on the bench, and no name in the wielder's terms.

## Orders

Fall outside the reach.
Be trimmed away as noise so the strike can land clean.
Wait, unaddressed, where the tool could not go.

## Loses

Its whole claim on being solved, precisely by not fitting.
It is cut off because it did not suit the tool, so the cleaner the strike the more of it was left behind, and it is invisible in the wielder's terms because the tool that defines the problem cannot name what it cannot touch.
The part amputated to fit the instrument is the part the instrument most needed to reach.

## Drives

Toward the edge and off it,
unreached and unnamed,
so the piece cut away to make the problem fit is the piece that returns as the problem unsolved.
