# Position: Cause

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The seat of the declining body, which reads the loyal quiet as content and so sails on uncorrected.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A quality that is slipping, and two channels that would tell it so: the members who leave and the members who complain.
A membership devoted to it, stable on the roll and silent in the room.
No other way to learn of its decline, and no signal reaching it through either channel.

## Orders

Take the quiet.
Read the members who stay as satisfied and the room that does not complain as content, and carry on.
Lean on the devotion of the membership, and take its stability for health.

## Loses

Its correction, exactly by trusting the quiet.
Because it learns of decline only through exit and voice, and its loyalty has stilled both, the seat mistakes the devotion of its members for a verdict that all is well, so it cannot both be reassured by the silence and be warned by it, and it declines precisely because it is loved.
It is failing and content, and the content is what keeps it failing.

## Drives

Toward a body reassured by the silence that dooms it,
deaf through no tyranny, only through being loved,
so the loyalty it leans on is the very thing that keeps it from hearing it must change.
