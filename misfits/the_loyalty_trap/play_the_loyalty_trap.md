# Play: The Loyalty Trap

## Estate

[khai-misfits](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-misfits): the house that holds this production and answers for the whole run.

## Name

The Loyalty Trap.
The firm you gave your years to, the party you believe in, the country you love, begins to slip: the quality drops, the direction wanders, something is going wrong. And you are loyal, so you do the loyal thing. You do not walk out at the first fault, because loyalty means staying when it is hard, giving it the benefit of the doubt, standing by it through a bad patch ... and you do not make a scene, because loyalty means not airing the dirty laundry, not being the malcontent, trusting that those in charge know what they are doing. So you stay, and you hold your tongue, and so does every other loyal member, each for the same good reason. But an organisation learns it is failing in only two ways: by the members who leave, and by the members who complain, and loyalty has stilled them both. No one has walked, so there is no alarm in the numbers, and no one has spoken, so there is no alarm in the room, and the leadership, seeing a stable and quiet membership, reads it as content and sails on. The very devotion that should have been the organisation's strength has muffled the only signals that could have saved it, and it declines in a silence its most loyal members are keeping for its sake.

## Arc

The bend is this: loyalty rightly holds a member from walking out at the first fault and from airing every grievance, and by holding both it strips a declining body of the exit and the voice that alone could correct it, so the most loyal members keep the silence in which it fails.
It runs on a decline met by an attachment that mutes.
An organisation in decline has two feedback channels: exit, the members who leave and so discipline it through the numbers, and voice, the members who stay and complain and so correct it through the argument. Loyalty is meant to help, and often does: it holds a member from bolting at the first stumble, buying time for voice to work. But loyalty holds voice too, because the loyal member does not want to be the troublemaker, gives the benefit of the doubt, trusts the leadership, and keeps the faith by keeping quiet, so the same attachment that should have converted exit into voice converts it into silence instead.
And the silence is read as consent. The leadership sees a membership that neither leaves nor complains and takes the stability for satisfaction, the quiet for endorsement, so the decline draws no response and continues, and continuing, it asks more loyalty of the members who might have checked it, and the most devoted, precisely because they are most devoted, are the least likely to walk and the least likely to speak. When at last a member's loyalty fails, they slip away without a word, unwilling even in leaving to make a scene, so even the exit that comes carries no lesson, and the body loses its people one quiet resignation at a time and learns nothing from any of them.
None of it is malice or folly. Loyalty is a virtue and staying through hard times is admirable, giving the benefit of the doubt is decent, not airing every grievance is good manners, and leaving quietly is only sparing everyone a scene: no one betrays the organisation, and the decline runs uncorrected out of the sum of loyal restraint. What should have been the body's greatest strength, the devotion of its members, is what keeps it from hearing that it is failing.
Voice it, make loyalty mean the loyal opposition rather than the loyal silence, hold a credible readiness to leave so the threat of exit carries weight, build channels that treat a complaint as care and a resignation as a lesson, and the signals can be restored: but that asks the loyal member to risk looking disloyal, to spend the very standing that loyalty earned on criticism that may wound what they love, and asks the leadership to hear dissent as devotion rather than betrayal, against every instinct that reads a quiet membership as a happy one, so the correction is bought by the faithful speaking against the grain of their own faith.

## Triggers

**[The Slip](plot_the_slip.md)**
The organisation begins to decline: the quality drops, the direction wanders, something goes wrong. The Cause slips, needing correction, and the Faithful, who love it, see the fault and feel the pull to respond. Closes when the decline is real and the loyal members must choose how to answer it.

**[The Staying](plot_the_staying.md)**
Cue: loyalty means staying when it is hard. The Faithful, giving the benefit of the doubt, does not walk out at the fault but stands by the Cause through the bad patch, so the exit that would have disciplined it is not taken. Closes when the members are staying despite the decline, and the numbers show no alarm.

**[The Swallowed Word](plot_the_swallowed_word.md)**
Cue: loyalty means not making a scene. The Faithful, unwilling to be the malcontent and trusting the leadership, holds back the complaint, so the voice that would have corrected the Cause is swallowed. Closes when the members are silent as well as staying, and the room shows no alarm either.

**[The Quiet Exit](plot_the_quiet_exit.md)**
Cue: the leadership reads the quiet as content. The Cause, losing no members and hearing no complaint, takes the stability for satisfaction and sails on, and when at last the Leaver's loyalty fails, it slips away without a word, so even the departure teaches nothing. Closes when the body is declining in silence, shedding its people one quiet resignation at a time and learning from none.

**[The Loyal Opposition](plot_the_loyal_opposition.md)**
Cue: someone would make loyalty speak. The Dissenter would voice the fault and hold a credible readiness to leave, spending the standing loyalty earned on criticism that may wound what it loves, against the norm that loyalty means silence and a leadership that hears dissent as betrayal. Closes when the signal is restored and the decline is met ... or the faithful hold their tongues once more, and the body slides on unheard.

## Stakes

Whether a body can hold its members' devotion and still hear that it is failing.
Every plot but the last moves the same way: toward the loyal silence, which each member is right to keep and all leave the body deaf by keeping, and away from the loyal opposition, which would restore the signal and which asks the faithful to risk looking disloyal and wound what they love. The production cannot make loyalty hold the member without stilling the feedback, because an organisation learns of its decline only through the members who leave and the members who complain, and loyalty holds both, so the quiet is the devotion working, not failing. It can only show that no one betrays the body, that loyalty is a virtue and standing by it through hard times is admirable and not making a scene is decent and leaving quietly spares everyone the scene, and that the one move that lets the decline be heard, voicing it against the grain of one's own faith, must be made at the risk of seeming disloyal and against a leadership that reads a quiet membership as a happy one, so the devotion is real and the deafness is real and the body most loved is the one kept from learning it is failing.
