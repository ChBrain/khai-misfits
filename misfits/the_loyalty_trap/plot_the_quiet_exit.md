# Plot: The Quiet Exit

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Door](place_the_door.md), the leadership reading the quiet as content and a member's loyalty at last failing.
The body sailing on, and one worn-out member finally unable to hold.

## Action

[Holding](process_holding.md): [The Leaver](persona_the_leaver.md), holding [the leaver's seat](position_leaver.md), slips away without a word, while [The Cause](persona_the_cause.md), holding [the cause's seat](position_cause.md), records a seat emptied and learns nothing from it.

## Stage

[The Leaver](persona_the_leaver.md), on [Slip Away](plan_slip_away.md), holds on until loyalty can hold no longer, then goes gracefully, taking the reason out the door unspoken.
[The Cause](persona_the_cause.md), still on [Take the Quiet](plan_take_the_quiet.md), notes one more quiet resignation and, hearing no complaint even in the leaving, keeps sailing on.

## Tension

Even the exit loyalty finally permits comes stripped of its lesson.
There is no villain still: the leaver is only sparing everyone a scene, and the leadership only reading the calm it is given. But the departure carries no word of why, so the plot closes with the body shedding its people one quiet resignation at a time and learning from none, declining in a silence kept to the very threshold and past it.
