# Process: Declining

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The slip in the body's quality that needs a corrective signal, and, getting none, continues.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A body beginning to fail: the quality dropping, the direction wandering, something going wrong.
At [the house](place_the_house.md), the slip setting in beneath a surface of stability, the members beginning to feel it.

## Direction

Toward a decline that draws no response and so goes on.
The body slips and would be corrected by a signal from its members, but loyalty stills both channels, so declining meets no discipline and no argument and continues, the leadership reading the quiet as content and carrying on.

## Lever

The silence that reads as consent.
Because a stable roll and a quiet floor look exactly like a satisfied membership, the decline draws no alarm, so declining runs on a misread, the body taking the devotion of its members for a verdict that all is well and needing no change.

## Echo

A body slipping and reassured.
The quality has dropped and the membership has neither left nor complained, and the leadership sees stability and hears calm, so what reads as a healthy, loyal organisation is, unseen, a declining one mistaking its members' faith for its own good health.
