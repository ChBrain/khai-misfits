# Position: Faithful

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The seat that stays and stays silent out of devotion, virtuous for one member and deafening for all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An attachment to the body, and two responses it declines to use: the exit it will not take and the voice it will not raise.
A sound reason for each: loyalty means standing by through a bad patch, and loyalty means not airing the dirty laundry.
No wish to harm the body, only to keep faith with it.

## Orders

Stand by it.
Do not walk at the first fault, and do not make a scene: give it the benefit of the doubt and keep the faith.
Honour the attachment through the decline, the way a devoted member should.

## Loses

The body's correction, exactly by keeping faith with it.
Because a declining organisation learns of its trouble only from those who leave and those who complain, and the seat does neither, its devotion stills both signals, so it cannot both stand loyally by and let the body hear that it is failing.
It keeps the faith and, keeping it, keeps the silence in which the thing it loves declines.

## Drives

Toward a member staying and silent,
right for each and deafening for all,
so the devotion that would never abandon the body is what keeps it from hearing it must change.
