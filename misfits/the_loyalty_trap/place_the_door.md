# Place: The Door

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The exit, the threshold members leave by, held shut by loyalty and, when at last opened, passed through in silence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The way out of the body, the threshold a member crosses to take their custom or their membership elsewhere.
The faithful standing before it and not going, held by loyalty, and the leaver at last passing through it without a word, sparing the fuss.

## Holds

The holding, and the exit it stills.
Here loyalty meets the option of leaving, so the Door holds the point where the market signal is stilled: the faithful will not cross it though the body slips, and the leaver crosses it mute, so it is where the departure that should discipline the body either does not happen or happens without a message.

## Offers

A way out, and the discipline a departure could carry.
Because the Door is where members leave, it offers the body its sharpest signal, the numbers falling that force a reckoning, so it hands out the exit that would discipline through the roll, and hands it out held shut by the faithful or passed in silence by the leaver, so the discipline it could carry rarely arrives.

## Withheld

The lesson of a leaving.
The Door cannot make a silent exit speak, so it withholds the reason behind each departure, letting a resignation be a number and not a message, and, held shut by loyalty for so long, it withholds even the numbers, so the body is neither disciplined by the members who stay nor taught by the few who go.
