# Process: Holding

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The stilling of exit as loyalty holds the member in place, so the body loses the discipline of departure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A member who loves the body and a fault that might have sent a lesser attachment out the door.
At [the door](place_the_door.md), the faithful choosing to stay rather than walk, giving the benefit of the doubt.

## Direction

Toward an exit not taken, or taken too late and in silence.
Loyalty holds the member from bolting at the first fault, so holding keeps the faithful in their places despite the decline, and when at last an attachment fails, it lets the leaver go quietly, so the market signal is either withheld or, when it comes, comes mute.

## Lever

The virtue of standing by.
Because staying through a bad patch is admirable and bolting at the first fault would be fickle, holding runs on a genuine loyalty, the exit stilled not by any chain but by the member's own devotion, so the discipline of departure is withheld by a virtue.

## Echo

A roll that holds, and a body undisciplined.
The members are staying and the numbers show no fall, and the departures that do come are quiet, so what reads as a devoted, stable membership is, unseen, the market signal stilled, the body kept from the discipline that falling numbers would have forced.
