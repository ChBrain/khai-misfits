# Position: Leaver

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The seat whose loyalty at last fails and who exits without a word, so even the departure teaches nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A loyalty that held as long as it could and finally cannot, and a way out at last taken.
A reason to go quietly: no wish, even in leaving, to make a scene or run the body down.
No inclination to explain, only to be gone.

## Orders

Slip away.
When it is finally too much, go, and go gracefully: hand in the quiet resignation and spare everyone the fuss.
Take your leave without a word against the thing you once loved.

## Loses

The one lesson its exit could have carried, exactly by leaving with grace.
Because it goes without voicing why, the seat's departure is a number and not a message, so it cannot both leave courteously and let the body learn from the leaving, the reason walking out the door with it.
It kept faith to the end and, in the manner of its going, keeps it still, teaching nothing as it leaves.

## Drives

Toward an exit taken in silence,
courteous for the one leaving and useless to the body left,
so even the departure loyalty finally permits is stripped of the lesson it might have taught.
