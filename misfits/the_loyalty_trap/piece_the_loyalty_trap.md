# Piece: The Loyalty Trap

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The phenomenon whole, a body kept from hearing its decline by the devotion of its members.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Everywhere the production runs: from [the house](place_the_house.md) to [the door](place_the_door.md) and [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the two channels it stills.
It is the whole arc, the decline unremarked, both signals muted, the body sailing on in a silence its members keep for its sake.

## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without a name: a decline, two channels, an attachment, a dissenter, and no account of how one silences the rest.
With it the misfit is whole: the loyalty trap is the structure entire, a declining body whose members' devotion stills both the exit that would discipline it and the voice that would correct it, the virtue and the deafness shown to be one thing. It is load bearing because it is the structure the whole production exists to stage (Hirschman): the frame that binds the decline, the stilled exit, the swallowed voice, the muting faith, and the loyal opposition into a single bend, so the piece carries the phenomenon as a whole, a body most loved and least able to learn it is failing.

## Apparent

A model of devotion, a body blessed with loyal members who neither desert it nor run it down.
Seen at the surface, the loyalty trap looks like an organisation's dream, a stable and uncomplaining membership, and the truth that this very devotion has stilled the only signals a declining body has, that the loyalty prized as strength is the deafness that dooms it, that a quiet membership can be a failing one, stays hidden behind how healthy a devoted, uncomplaining body appears.

## Yearbook

The whole of it: the Loyalty Trap is [the decline](piece_the_decline.md) unheard because [the faith](piece_the_faith.md) has stilled both [the exit](piece_the_exit.md) and [the voice](piece_the_voice.md), answered only by [the loyal opposition](piece_the_loyal_opposition.md) that makes devotion speak.
It is the misfit named: a body kept from hearing its own decline by the love of its members, no traitor in it and no tyrant, the most loyal keeping the silence in which it fails.
