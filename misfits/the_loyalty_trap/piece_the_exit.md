# Piece: The Exit

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The market channel, the leaving that would discipline the body through the numbers, held by loyalty.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Lies at [the door](place_the_door.md), the threshold members would cross to leave.
Held shut against [the decline](piece_the_decline.md), which it exists to discipline and cannot while loyalty holds it.

## Load Bearing

Without it a declining body faces no discipline from the numbers, and only voice remains to correct it.
With it the body has one of its two feedback channels: departures that fall the roll and force a reckoning, a market discipline that answers a slip in quality with a loss of members. The Exit is load bearing because it is one of the two signals the trap stills (Hirschman): the leaving that would discipline through the numbers, so the piece carries the market channel whose stilling by loyalty, or whose passing in silence, leaves the body undisciplined, half of the feedback the devotion mutes.

## Apparent

Disloyalty itself, the thing a faithful member would never do, walking out on the body.
Seen through loyalty, the exit looks like betrayal, the abandonment a devoted member refuses, and the truth that it is a signal the body needs, that departures are the discipline a slipping quality should draw, that even a threatened exit gives voice its weight, stays hidden behind how much like desertion leaving is made to seem.

## Yearbook

Held shut by [the faithful](persona_the_faithful.md) through [holding](process_holding.md), the market channel loyalty stills.
It is half the muted feedback: the Exit is the discipline [the decline](piece_the_decline.md) should draw and [the faith](piece_the_faith.md) withholds, passed in silence by [the leaver](persona_the_leaver.md), and given weight again only when [the loyal opposition](piece_the_loyal_opposition.md) holds a credible readiness to take it.
