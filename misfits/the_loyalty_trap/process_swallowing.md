# Process: Swallowing

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The stilling of voice as loyalty holds the member from complaint, so the body loses the correction of argument.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A member who loves the body and a fault that might have moved a less devoted one to speak.
At [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the faithful holding back the complaint, unwilling to be the malcontent.

## Direction

Toward a complaint held behind the teeth.
Loyalty forbids the scene and trusts the leadership, so swallowing keeps the faithful silent on the very faults they see, and the voice that would have named the trouble and argued the change is kept in, the room as quiet as the roll is stable.

## Lever

The good manners of not airing the dirty laundry.
Because making a scene feels disloyal and trusting those in charge feels decent, swallowing runs on courtesy and faith, the voice stilled not by any gag but by the member's own sense that a loyal member does not run the body down.

## Echo

A floor that stays quiet, and a body uncorrected.
The members are present and saying nothing and the faults go unnamed, so what reads as a harmonious, trusting membership is, unseen, the political signal stilled, the body kept from the correction that a raised voice would have argued for.
