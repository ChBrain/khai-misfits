# Plot: The Slip

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The House](place_the_house.md), the body beginning to decline.
The quality dropping, the direction wandering, something going wrong beneath a calm surface.

## Action

[Declining](process_declining.md): [The Cause](persona_the_cause.md), holding [the cause's seat](position_cause.md), slips in quality, while [The Faithful](persona_the_faithful.md), holding [the faithful's seat](position_faithful.md), who love it, see the fault and feel the pull to respond.

## Stage

[The Cause](persona_the_cause.md), on [Take the Quiet](plan_take_the_quiet.md), slips without alarm, its surface stable and its rooms calm as the trouble sets in.
[The Faithful](persona_the_faithful.md), who would never abandon it, notice the decline and face the choice of how a loyal member answers a body they love going wrong.

## Tension

The fault is real, and the members who see it are the ones most bound not to act on it.
There is nothing to fault: the body has genuinely slipped, and the members genuinely love it. But loyalty is about to counsel both the staying and the silence, so the plot closes with the decline real and unremarked, and the faithful, who alone could raise the alarm, feeling the pull of a devotion that will keep them from raising it.
