# Piece: The Decline

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The slip in the body's quality, the drop that needs a corrective signal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Sets in at [the house](place_the_house.md), beneath a surface of stability.
Would be answered at [the door](place_the_door.md) or [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the two channels that could correct it.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing for the signals to carry, and loyalty is simply a virtue with no cost.
With it the whole trap has its occasion: a body slipping in quality, which exit and voice exist to catch and correct, so that stilling those channels has a price, the decline left to run. The Decline is load bearing because it is the thing the muted feedback fails to catch (Hirschman): the trap matters only because the body is failing and could be recovered, so the piece carries the slip that makes the silence costly, the trouble the devotion of the members keeps the body from hearing.

## Apparent

A rough patch, a temporary dip, nothing a loyal member should abandon a body over.
Seen from inside loyalty, the decline looks like the kind of trouble that passes, a reason to stand by rather than walk or complain, and the truth that it is exactly what the stilled signals were meant to catch, that a slip no one reports is a slip no one corrects, stays hidden behind how much like a passing bad patch a real decline first appears.

## Yearbook

Suffered by [the cause](persona_the_cause.md) through [declining](process_declining.md), the slip that draws no response.
It is the occasion of the trap: the Decline is what [the exit](piece_the_exit.md) and [the voice](piece_the_voice.md) would correct and [the faith](piece_the_faith.md) keeps them from correcting, the trouble only [the loyal opposition](piece_the_loyal_opposition.md), speaking, lets the body finally meet.
