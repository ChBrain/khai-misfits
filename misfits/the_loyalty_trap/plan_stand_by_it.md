# Plan: Stand By It

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the faithful's seat](position_faithful.md), inside [The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md).

## Owner

## Direction

Keep faith through the bad patch. Do not walk out at the first fault, and do not make a scene: give the body the benefit of the doubt, trust that those in charge know what they are doing, and honour the attachment by staying and holding your tongue. Loyalty means standing by.

## Orders

([The Faithful](persona_the_faithful.md) stays despite the decline and holds back the complaint, keeping faith by keeping still.)

- [ ] the complaint is held back, no scene made, the leadership trusted

- [ ] both the exit and the voice that would correct the body are stilled
