# Plan: Speak Up

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the dissenter's seat](position_dissenter.md), inside [The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Dissenter](position_dissenter.md)

## Direction

Make loyalty speak. Voice the fault plainly, hold a credible readiness to leave so the threat of exit carries weight, and make loyalty mean the loyal opposition rather than the loyal silence. Say out loud what the faithful are all keeping quiet, because the loving thing is to name the fault, not to nurse it.

## Orders

([The Dissenter](persona_the_dissenter.md) voices the fault and holds a credible readiness to leave, spending the standing loyalty earned on the truth the body needs to hear.)

## Implementation

## Targets

- [ ] the fault is voiced plainly, the silence broken
- [ ] a credible readiness to leave gives the exit-threat weight
- [ ] loyalty is made to mean the loyal opposition, not the loyal silence
- [ ] the cost is borne: the standing spent, the love risked, the disloyalty misread
