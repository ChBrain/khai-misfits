# Piece: The Faith

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). Loyalty itself, the attachment that mutes both channels, functional in delaying exit and pathological in suppressing voice.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Lives in [the house](place_the_house.md), the devotion that keeps the members in their places and their peace.
Bears on [the door](place_the_door.md) and [the floor](place_the_floor.md) alike, stilling the one and swallowing the other.

## Load Bearing

Without it the member leaves or complains as any dissatisfied party would, and a declining body hears its signals plainly.
With it the whole trap has its fulcrum: an attachment that holds the member from bolting at the first fault and, holding, holds the complaint too, so both channels are stilled by one devotion. The Faith is load bearing because it is the single force that mutes exit and voice at once (Hirschman): loyalty is meant to convert exit into voice and, grown strong, converts both into silence, so the piece carries the attachment whose double action is the engine of the trap, the virtue that becomes the deafness.

## Apparent

Pure virtue, the devotion that is a body's greatest strength, loyalty as a thing only ever to be prized.
Seen alone, the faith looks like unmixed good, the steadfastness any organisation would want in its members, and the truth that the same attachment that holds a member through a bad patch also holds back the signals that would end it, that loyalty grown strong enough stills the very feedback that could save what it loves, stays hidden behind how plainly a virtue devotion appears.

## Yearbook

Kept by [the faithful](persona_the_faithful.md), the attachment that works through [holding](process_holding.md) and [swallowing](process_swallowing.md) at once.
It is the fulcrum of the trap: the Faith stills both [the exit](piece_the_exit.md) and [the voice](piece_the_voice.md), leaving [the decline](piece_the_decline.md) unheard, and is turned to the body's good only when [the loyal opposition](piece_the_loyal_opposition.md) makes devotion speak rather than keep silent.
