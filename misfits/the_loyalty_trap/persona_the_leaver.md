# Persona: The Leaver

## Taxonomy

[Leaver](position_leaver.md). The member whose loyalty at last fails, who exits, but quietly, so even the departure teaches nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Worn out. Resigned. Unwilling, even in leaving, to make a scene.
Is the member who held on as long as loyalty could and finally cannot, and who goes without a word, sparing everyone the fuss.
No spite in it: only someone quietly giving up on a thing they once kept faith with.

## Action

Slips away when it is finally too much, and takes the grievance out the door unspoken.
Hands in a quiet resignation, declines to explain, and lets the leaving pass without a scene.
Does what a decent person does on the way out: goes gracefully, and says nothing.

## Shadow

Not bitter, spent, and the grace of the going is the last of the harm: even the exit that comes carries no lesson.
Because it leaves without voicing why, the departure is a number and not a message, so the body loses its people one quiet resignation at a time and learns from none of them, the very courtesy of the exit stripping it of the one thing an exit could have taught.
It kept faith to the end and, at the end, keeps it still, taking the reason away with it.

## Tell

Says "I did not want to make a fuss, so I just left."
Goes, and the going tells the leadership only that one more seat is empty, never why.
