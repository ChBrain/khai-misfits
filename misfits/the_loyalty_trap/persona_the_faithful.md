# Persona: The Faithful

## Taxonomy

[Faithful](position_faithful.md). Loves the body and, when it slips, neither leaves nor complains, so both its signals are stilled.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Devoted. Steadfast. Sure that loyalty means staying and holding one's tongue.
Loves the firm or the party or the country, and when it slips does the loyal thing: stands by it, gives it the benefit of the doubt, keeps the faith.
No treachery in it: only a member honouring an attachment through a bad patch.

## Action

Stays despite the decline and holds back the complaint, out of love.
Does not walk at the first fault, because loyalty means standing by, and does not make a scene, because loyalty means not airing the dirty laundry.
Does what a devoted member does: keeps faith by keeping still.

## Shadow

Not disloyal, too loyal: staying and keeping quiet are both virtues, and each is done for the body's own sake.
Because a declining organisation learns of its trouble only from the members who leave and the members who complain, and the Faithful does neither, its very devotion stills both signals, so the love that should have been the body's strength is what keeps it from hearing it is failing.
It is right to stay and right to spare the scene, and the rightness, kept by every loyal member at once, is the trap.

## Tell

Says "I would never walk out on it, and I would never run it down."
Does neither, and the thing it will never abandon declines in the silence it keeps for its sake.
