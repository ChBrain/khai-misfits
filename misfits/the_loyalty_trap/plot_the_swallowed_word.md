# Plot: The Swallowed Word

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Floor](place_the_floor.md), loyalty counselling the member not to make a scene.
The fault there to be named and the room open to it, and the faithful present and silent.

## Action

[Swallowing](process_swallowing.md): [The Faithful](persona_the_faithful.md), holding [the faithful's seat](position_faithful.md), holds back the complaint, while [The Cause](persona_the_cause.md), holding [the cause's seat](position_cause.md), hears a room that says nothing wrong.

## Stage

[The Faithful](persona_the_faithful.md), still on [Stand By It](plan_stand_by_it.md), keeps the complaint behind its teeth, unwilling to be the malcontent and trusting the leadership to know its business.
[The Cause](persona_the_cause.md), still on [Take the Quiet](plan_take_the_quiet.md), takes the silent floor for a content one, adding the quiet to the stable roll as further proof that all is well.

## Tension

The good manners of not airing the laundry are the stilling of the body's gentlest signal.
There is nothing to fault: making a scene feels disloyal, and trusting those in charge feels decent. But the voice that would have named the fault and argued the change, and cost the body no members to do it, is swallowed, so the plot closes with the room as quiet as the roll, both signals now stilled and the body sailing on doubly reassured.
