# Process: Speaking

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The raising of voice and the credible readiness to leave that restore the signal, the remedy bought against the grain of one's own faith.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A body declining unheard, and a member devoted enough to criticise it.
At [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the dissenter rising to name the fault the faithful are keeping quiet.

## Direction

Toward the signal restored.
The dissenter voices the fault and holds a credible readiness to leave, so speaking gives the body back both channels at once, the argument that would correct it and the exit-threat that would discipline it, breaking the silence the faithful keep.

## Lever

The truth that the loving thing is to name the fault, not to nurse it.
Because a body corrected is a body kept, the case to speak is loyalty's own, so speaking runs on the same devotion that fed the silence, turned now to the harder service of criticism, loyalty made to mean the loyal opposition.

## Echo

A signal restored, at the price of seeming disloyal.
The fault has been named and the threat of exit given weight and the decline at last met, and it has cost the dissenter its standing and risked the love it spoke for, so what reads as one member breaking ranks is, in truth, the devotion of the faithful turned to the criticism the body needed to hear.
