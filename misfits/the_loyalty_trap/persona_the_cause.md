# Persona: The Cause

## Taxonomy

[Cause](position_cause.md). The declining body, which loses no members and hears no complaint, and so mistakes the quiet for content and sails on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Beloved. Slipping. Reassured by a silence it misreads.
Is the firm or the party or the country in decline, leaning on the devotion of its members, seeing a stable and quiet roll and taking it for health.
No tyranny in it: only a body that hears nothing wrong because nothing wrong is said.

## Action

Slips in quality and, getting no signal, does not correct.
Reads the members who stay as satisfied and the room that does not complain as content, and sails on unchecked.
Asks, as it declines, still more loyalty of the members who might have checked it.

## Shadow

Not a tyrant, deaf: it silences no one, and that is the trap, for the quiet it takes as consent is the quiet its own members keep for love of it.
Because it learns of its decline only through exit and voice, and its loyalty has stilled both, the Cause cannot see what it is doing, mistaking the devotion of its members for a verdict that all is well, so it declines precisely because it is loved, the stability that reassures it the very thing that dooms it.
It is failing and content, and the content is what keeps it failing.

## Tell

Says "the members are staying and no one is complaining, so we must be doing fine."
Sails on, and the staying and the silence it trusts are the alarm it is not hearing.
