# Persona: The Dissenter

## Taxonomy

[Dissenter](position_dissenter.md). Would make loyalty speak, voicing the fault and holding a credible readiness to leave, against the norm that loyalty means silence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Loyal and unquiet. Devoted enough to criticise, brave enough to be misread.
Loves the body as much as any, and holds that the loving thing is to name the fault, not to nurse it in silence.
No disloyalty in it: only someone who would spend their standing on the truth the body needs to hear.

## Action

Voices the fault plainly and holds a credible readiness to leave, so the threat of exit carries weight.
Makes loyalty mean the loyal opposition rather than the loyal silence, and treats a complaint as care.
Does the one thing that restores the signal: says out loud what the faithful are all keeping quiet.

## Shadow

Not a saviour: the voice it raises risks the very love it is spent for, and the room is built to mistake it.
Because speaking the fault means looking disloyal to the faithful and hostile to a leadership that reads a quiet membership as a happy one, the Dissenter cannot restore the signal without spending the standing loyalty earned and wounding what it loves, and a readiness to leave, to mean anything, must be one it would truly act on.
It can make the decline be heard, only by being taken, for a while, as the traitor the trap needs no one to be.

## Tell

Says "I am saying this because I am loyal, not in spite of it."
Says it, and is heard, at first, as the one member who was not.
