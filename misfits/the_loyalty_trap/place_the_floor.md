# Place: The Floor

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). Where voice would be raised, the meeting or the room in which a complaint could correct the body, and mostly is swallowed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The room where members might speak, the meeting and the open floor where a fault could be named and a change argued for.
The faithful present and silent, the complaint held behind their teeth, and, rarely, the dissenter rising to say what the rest are keeping quiet.

## Holds

The swallowing, and the speaking that would answer it.
Here loyalty meets the option of complaint, so the Floor holds the point where the political signal is stilled or restored: the faithful swallow the word for fear of the scene, and only the dissenter raises it, so it is where the voice that would correct the body is either kept back or, at a cost, let out.

## Offers

A place for the complaint that would correct.
Because the Floor is where voice can be raised, it offers the body its subtlest signal, the argument that would change its course without losing its members, so it hands out the channel that corrects through persuasion, and hands it out into a silence the faithful keep, so the correction it could carry is rarely spoken.

## Withheld

A hearing that takes dissent for devotion.
The Floor cannot make a leadership welcome the complaint it reads as disloyalty, so it withholds the safe reception a voice would need, letting the dissenter be taken for the traitor, and, quiet by the faithful's own choice, it withholds the very argument that would have corrected the body while it still could be corrected.
