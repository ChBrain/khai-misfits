# Place: The House

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The body itself, the firm or party or country where the members keep faith and the decline goes unremarked.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The organisation as a whole, its roll of devoted members steady and its rooms calm, the quality quietly slipping beneath a surface of stability.
The leadership looking out over a membership that stays and does not complain, and taking the sight for a good one.

## Holds

The declining, and the loyalty that veils it.
Here the body's slip meets its members' devotion, so the House holds the point where a decline becomes invisible: the quality drops while the roll holds and the rooms stay quiet, so it is where a failing body wears the calm of a content one, its trouble hidden by the very faith of those who serve it.

## Offers

The reassurance of a devoted, stable membership.
Because the House is where loyalty keeps the members in their places and their peace, it offers the leadership the picture of a healthy body, a full roll and a quiet floor, so it hands out the comfort of apparent content, and hands it out over a decline the comfort is hiding.

## Withheld

The alarm that a decline should raise.
The House cannot sound an alarm its members are keeping silent, so it withholds the warning the leaving and the complaining would have carried, and, calm on the surface while it slips beneath, it withholds from the body the one thing that would move it, the sense that anything is wrong.
