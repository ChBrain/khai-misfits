# Position: Dissenter

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The seat that would make loyalty speak, restoring the signal against the norm of loyal silence, at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A devotion equal to the faithful's, and the will to spend it on the truth the body needs to hear.
The two restored signals in hand: a voice it will raise, and a readiness to leave it would truly act on.
No cover from the charge of disloyalty, and no leadership eager to hear it.

## Orders

Speak up.
Voice the fault plainly, hold a credible readiness to leave so the threat of exit carries weight, and make loyalty mean the loyal opposition rather than the loyal silence.
Say out loud what the faithful are all keeping quiet.

## Loses

Its standing, exactly by using it for the body's good.
Because voicing the fault looks disloyal to the faithful and hostile to a leadership that reads quiet as content, the seat cannot restore the signal without spending the standing loyalty earned and wounding what it loves, and its threat of exit means nothing unless it is one it would carry out.
It gives the body back its hearing at the price of being taken, for a while, as the traitor.

## Drives

Toward a signal restored by dissent,
bought against its own standing and the body's instinct to mistrust it,
so the one seat that would make devotion speak must first be heard as the one that lacked it.
