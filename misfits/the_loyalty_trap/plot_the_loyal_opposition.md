# Plot: The Loyal Opposition

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Floor](place_the_floor.md), the body declining unheard and one member moving to make loyalty speak.
The faults plain and the silence long, and a devotion ready to spend itself on the truth.

## Action

[Speaking](process_speaking.md): [The Dissenter](persona_the_dissenter.md), holding [the dissenter's seat](position_dissenter.md), voices the fault and holds a credible readiness to leave, against [The Cause](persona_the_cause.md), holding [the cause's seat](position_cause.md), which reads a quiet membership as a happy one.

## Stage

[The Dissenter](persona_the_dissenter.md), having tabled [Speak Up](plan_speak_up.md), names the fault the faithful are keeping quiet and backs it with an exit-threat it would truly act on, making loyalty mean the loyal opposition.
[The Cause](persona_the_cause.md), still on [Take the Quiet](plan_take_the_quiet.md), hears the dissent as betrayal at first, its instinct reading the one raised voice as the one disloyal member.

## Tension

The one move that lets the decline be heard is the one the faithful and the leadership both read as disloyalty.
There is no easy remedy: voicing the fault plainly restores the signal, yet it means risking the look of betrayal, spending the standing loyalty earned, and wounding what one loves, against a body that mistakes a quiet membership for a happy one. So the play closes where it must, the signal restored and the decline at last met ... or the faithful hold their tongues once more, the dissenter is taken for the traitor, and the body slides on unheard.
