# Piece: The Voice

## Taxonomy

[The Loyalty Trap](play_the_loyalty_trap.md). The political channel, the complaint that would correct the body through the argument, swallowed by loyalty.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Belongs on [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the room where a complaint could be raised.
Swallowed before [the decline](piece_the_decline.md), which it exists to correct and cannot while loyalty holds it back.

## Load Bearing

Without it a declining body has only the blunt discipline of departures, and no way to be corrected without losing its members.
With it the body has its subtler feedback channel: the complaint that names the fault and argues the change, correcting a slip through persuasion rather than loss. The Voice is load bearing because it is the other of the two signals the trap stills (Hirschman): the argument that would correct through discourse, so the piece carries the political channel whose swallowing by loyalty leaves the body uncorrected, the half of the feedback that could have saved it without a single member lost.

## Apparent

Making a scene, running the body down, the malcontent's grievance a loyal member keeps to themselves.
Seen through loyalty, the voice looks like disloyalty aired, a lack of faith in the leadership, and the truth that it is the gentlest signal the body has, that a complaint is the correction that costs no members, that swallowing it is the decline left unspoken, stays hidden behind how much like ill-mannered disloyalty an honest complaint is made to seem.

## Yearbook

Swallowed by [the faithful](persona_the_faithful.md) through [swallowing](process_swallowing.md), the political channel loyalty stills.
It is the other half of the muted feedback: the Voice is the correction [the decline](piece_the_decline.md) needs and [the faith](piece_the_faith.md) keeps in, restored only when [the loyal opposition](piece_the_loyal_opposition.md) raises it against the grain of its own devotion.
