# Piece: The Set

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md). The disposition itself, the readiness to meet the new problem as the old one, the whole phenomenon of which every other piece is a part: Einstellung, the mind set.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Laid down at [the Beaten Path](place_the_beaten_path.md) by repeated success, and carried onto [the Board](place_the_board.md) where it fires, reaching across every place the production holds.
Flows as the standing lean toward the familiar answer, present before the problem is read and shaping everything the looking does after.

## Load Bearing

Without the Set there is no trap, only a series of separate choices: it is the Set that ties the priming to the firing to the block, the one disposition that turns a history of success into a first answer, a first answer into a fixation, and a fixation into a miss.
The Set is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole, the readiness installed by practice that meets the new as the old, sound wherever the old answer fits and blinding wherever a better one is present (Luchins). It carries the entire misfit: the good move that blocks the better is the Set firing, the gaze that will not leave the first answer is the Set holding, the search that closes on a working solution is the Set resting, so the Set is not one more piece beside the others but the shape they all compose, the disposition whose every motion is another of the play's pieces at work. It is worth having, which is why it cannot simply be dropped: the Set is what expertise is, the same readiness that solves at a glance nearly everywhere being the exact thing that hides the better move where one is there to find.

## Apparent

Just expertise, the practised readiness of a skilled mind.
Seen at the surface, the Set looks like nothing but mastery, the quick sure reach of a solver who knows their kind of problem, and the truth that the same readiness is the block, that the disposition which answers so fast is the disposition which stops looking, that to be set is to be at once expert and blind, stays hidden behind how completely a helpful readiness resembles a pure gift, the set that serves well nearly everywhere wearing the face of skill even where it costs.

## Yearbook

Held by [the Master](persona_the_master.md) as both their skill and their blindness, absent in [the Novice](persona_the_novice.md) whose empty store leaves the field open, and fought by [the Doubter](persona_the_doubter.md) who distrusts every answer it makes ready.
It is the whole shape of the trap: the Set is what [priming](process_priming.md) installs and [firing](process_firing.md) discharges, the disposition [the first answer](piece_the_first_answer.md) issues from and [the fixation](piece_the_fixation.md) enacts, the readiness that keeps [the simpler way](piece_the_simpler_way.md) unseen by keeping the good move ready, the phenomenon every other piece is a face of.
