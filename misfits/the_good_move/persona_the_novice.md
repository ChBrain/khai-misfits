# Persona: The Novice

## Taxonomy

[The Fresh Eye](position_the_fresh_eye.md). Holds no set method for this kind of problem to reach for, so meets it whole and finds the path the practised mind is blind to, winning here only by lacking what wins everywhere else.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Unpractised. Open by default. Reaching for nothing because nothing is yet installed.
Is the solver who has no set to fire, so the familiar answer does not rise to hold the attention, and the field is met plainly, the whole board available because none of it is spoken for.
No brilliance wished on them: the openness is not insight but the absence of a groove, an advantage they did not earn and cannot claim, the plain sight of a mind with nothing yet in the way.

## Action

Comes to the problem with no method ready for its kind, so looks at all of it, and where a better path is the only path, sees it, the region the practised gaze skips being simply part of the board to them.
Solves the extinction problem the master fails, not by searching harder but by searching evenly, having no first answer to spend the looking on.
Does what an unset mind does: reads the problem as itself rather than as one of a known kind, and finds the plain path plainly, passing exactly where the expert is held.

## Shadow

Not a better mind, but one that wins only by lacking what usually helps, and that is the ache: the advantage is not skill and cannot be kept, and everywhere the old method works, which is nearly everywhere, the empty repertoire is not freedom but helplessness.
What wears is the knowledge that the openness is on loan. Every problem solved the ordinary way teaches the method, and the method installs the set, so the same practice that would make them capable is the practice that will close the fresh eye, and the clarity they have now is the clarity of not yet knowing enough to be blind.
The interior bind is that they cannot both keep the sight and gain the skill: to become the Master is to acquire the groove, and to stay the Novice is to stay unable, so the fresh eye is a stage passed through, valuable precisely and only while it lasts, and lost by the very learning that would let them use it.

## Tell

Says "I did not know you were supposed to use that method, so I just looked at the problem."
Is right without merit, because the not-knowing is the whole of the advantage, and the plain look that found the path the experts missed is available to them only because no practice has yet taught them where not to look, so the sentence that sounds like an admission of ignorance is the exact description of the fresh eye, a mind that sees the board whole by not yet having learned to skip a part of it.
