# Plot: The Extinction

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Board](place_the_board.md), a problem the old method cannot solve at all.
A case that will not yield to the familiar answer, where the primed method runs and arrives at nothing, and only a path off the beaten track will do.

## Action

[Blocking](process_blocking.md): the set fires as ever and produces no solution, the mind held on a method that here reaches nothing while the fresh eye, unset, sees the only path plainly.

## Stage

[The Master](persona_the_master.md) applies the trusted method and it fails, [the set](piece_the_set.md) delivering no answer and holding them there, while [the Novice](persona_the_novice.md), on [Come to It Fresh](plan_come_to_it_fresh.md), meets the board whole and finds [the simpler way](piece_the_simpler_way.md) at once; and [the Doubter](persona_the_doubter.md), on [Search Past the Answer](plan_search_past_the_answer.md), shows the remedy at its price, distrusting the method to reach what it hid.
The expert fails where the beginner passes, and the cure is to become, on purpose, a little like the beginner.

## Tension

The only way to find the better move is to distrust the method that has never failed before, and be worse at everything it solves to be better at the one thing it cannot.
The remedy is real: look again, come fresh, refuse to let the good answer close the search, and the better move can be found (Luchins and Luchins). But it costs on every problem to pay off on a few, since the set is invisible from inside and the good method is right nearly everywhere, so to guard against the block is to treat all your competence as suspect and forgo the speed that practice earned. The plot closes with the choice laid bare and no free way through it: distrust the good move and pay in effort and doubt on every problem, or keep the good move and its speed and never learn what it hid, the cure for a set being a deliberate refusal of the answer you have most reason to trust.
