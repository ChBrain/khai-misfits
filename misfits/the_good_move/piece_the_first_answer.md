# Piece: The First Answer

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md). The workable solution that arrives first, correct and fast, the good move whose very success closes the search on the better one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Rises at [the Board](place_the_board.md) where the primed method meets the matching problem, and runs down [the Beaten Path](place_the_beaten_path.md) that repeated success has worn.
Flows as the solution already in hand, produced by the set before the search begins, the answer the whole trap is built to keep the mind resting on.

## Load Bearing

Without the First Answer being genuinely good the trap would not hold: a wrong answer would be checked and dropped, and the search would go on to find the better move.
It is because the first answer is correct, works, and solves the problem that the mind has every reason to stop, so the First Answer is load bearing because it is the good move itself, the sound solution whose success is exactly what forecloses the search, not a blunder to be caught but a right answer that leaves no felt reason to look further. Its goodness is the whole mechanism: were it merely adequate it could be doubted, but a solution that plainly works closes the case honestly, and the better move is lost not to an error preferred over a truth but to a truth that arrived first and was reason enough to rest, the good standing in the way of the better precisely by being good.

## Apparent

Just the solution, the problem correctly solved and done.
Seen at the surface, the First Answer looks like the whole of the work: the method fit, the answer is right, the case is closed, and the truth that it is only the first of the board's solutions and not the best, that a better move sat on the same problem and was blocked by this one, that stopping here was a choice the answer's success made for the mind, stays hidden behind how completely a correct answer resembles a finished one.

## Yearbook

Produced by [the Master](persona_the_master.md) whose set fires it ready, taken under [Take the Good Move](plan_take_the_good_move.md), and refused as a stopping point by [the Doubter](persona_the_doubter.md) who will not let it close the case.
It is the good move that blocks the better: the First Answer is what [firing](process_firing.md) delivers and [settling](process_settling.md) rests on, the solution [the fixation](piece_the_fixation.md) holds the gaze to and [the set](piece_the_set.md) made ready, standing in the light where [the simpler way](piece_the_simpler_way.md) is left dark.
