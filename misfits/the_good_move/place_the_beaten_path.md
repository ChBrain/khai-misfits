# Place: The Beaten Path

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md). The worn route repeated success has carved, the groove the set runs down, where the mind travels the method it has always travelled and arrives fast at the answer it always finds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A clear road and a quick arrival.
Shown as fluency: the route is known, the going smooth, the answer reached without hesitation, so travelling the path looks like nothing but the reward of having walked it before, the ease that practice is supposed to buy.

## Holds

Holds the groove that the reproduction of a learned method carves, deeper with each use.
The Beaten Path is where the primed solution runs, the channel worn by every past success, so the mind set on the new problem is carried down the familiar route to the familiar answer, the reproduction of what worked before running quicker the more it has been run (Wertheimer).

## Offers

Offers speed and a sure arrival, and a channel that carries the eye past the fork.
Here the worn route really is fast and really does arrive, so the Beaten Path offers the true economy of practice, and by the same walls that make it quick it carries the attention straight past the branch where the better route turns off, so the road that delivers the good answer is the road that never shows the fork to the better one.

## Withheld

Withholds that the groove is walls as well as a road.
What the Beaten Path hides is that its smoothness is confinement: the channel that speeds the going is the channel that keeps the eye on the route, so the fork to the better path, standing just off the worn track, passes unseen behind the very walls that make the familiar way so fast, the reproduction of the known crowding out the production of anything new.
