# Position: The Second Look

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md). The re-searching seat, which refuses to let a working answer close the case and looks again on purpose, spending effort past the point a good solution has already solved it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A mandate to keep looking after the answer is found.
The seat holds the standing order to treat a good solution as a suspect rather than a stop, so the search does not end where it feels ended, but is pushed on into the ground the first answer would have it skip.
A warning as its instrument, since the seat's only tool is the deliberate reminder that a good move may be hiding a better one, applied against the pull of a solution that already works.

## Orders

Search past the answer.
Take the good solution, and then look again, deliberately away from its ground, into the region the set marks empty, since that is exactly where a better move would sit unseen.
Distrust the sufficiency of a correct answer, since the seat's method is to refuse the plain signal that the problem is solved and to spend the search evenly across the whole board rather than let it pool on the first solution.

## Loses

The economy of stopping, which the seat gives up on every problem to catch the better move on a few.
The Second Look forgoes the whole point of a good answer, which is that it lets you stop, so it pays the cost of the extra search on every case, and since the set is invisible from inside, it has no way to tell the rare problem with a better move hiding from the many where the first answer was already best, and must therefore doubt them all. What the seat forfeits is proportion: it looks like waste on nearly every problem, confirming a first answer that needed no confirming, and earns its keep only in the case it cannot predict, so its method is indefensible in the moment and vindicated only in retrospect, a tax paid on all correct answers for the sake of the few that were not best, and the seat that alone can recover the better move is the one whose discipline reads as second-guessing right up to the moment it saves.

## Drives

Toward a search that will not close and an effort spent past its answer,
and a discipline that looks like waste until the rare case it repays,
so the seat that finds the better move is the one that must distrust every good one, buying the answer that was hiding with the doubt of all the answers that were not.
