# Plot: The Unseen

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Blind Spot](place_the_blind_spot.md), the solver setting out to find a better move.
A mind not content with the good answer, sure there may be more, turning deliberately to look for a better one.

## Action

[Blocking](process_blocking.md): the search sets out honestly and the gaze returns to the first answer's ground, the looking spent where the better move is not.

## Stage

[The Master](persona_the_master.md) means to look further and does, but [the fixation](piece_the_fixation.md) holds the gaze on [the first answer](piece_the_first_answer.md)'s squares, so the search crosses everything except [the blind spot](place_the_blind_spot.md), and [the simpler way](piece_the_simpler_way.md) stays in the dark though the solver swears they looked.
The looking is real, and that is what makes it so hard to catch: the intention is honest, only the direction is captured.

## Tension

The solver searched for a better move and found nothing, and the better move was there the whole time.
The search is not skipped or refused: the mind sets out to look, believes it looks, and comes away certain it found nothing, so from the inside the diligence is complete. But the attention never left the good answer's ground, and the better move sits in the region the gaze never crossed, so the plot closes with an honest report of an empty search and a better solution still on the board, unseen not for want of trying but because the good move had already taken the eye, the failure of the looking indistinguishable, from within, from there being nothing to find.
