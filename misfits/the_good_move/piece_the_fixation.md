# Piece: The Fixation

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md). The gaze locked on the found answer's ground, the attention that will not leave the first solution's features, the mechanism of the block made visible in where the eye goes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Holds at [the Board](place_the_board.md), on the squares of the found answer, and by holding there leaves [the Blind Spot](place_the_blind_spot.md) uncrossed.
Flows as the pull that keeps the looking on the first solution, the attention returning to the good move's ground each time it tries to leave.

## Load Bearing

Without the Fixation the block would be mere choice, and could be argued away: a mind that had simply decided the first answer was best could be asked to reconsider and would.
It is because the hold is on the attention and not the reasoning that the block resists intention, so the Fixation is load bearing because it is the mechanism, the reason an honest search fails, shown in the gaze that returns to the first solution's features even as the solver means to look elsewhere (Bilalic, McLeod and Gobet). Its being attentional is the whole point: the mind reports looking and believes it, because the intention to search is real, and only the direction is captured, so what carries the trap is not a lazy stop but a gaze that cannot get off the good move, the eye held where the answer was found while the will to look further goes nowhere.

## Apparent

Just concentration, a mind focused on the solution in front of it.
Seen at the surface, the Fixation looks like attentiveness, the proper focus of a solver on the problem, and the truth that the focus is the trap, that the same gaze which studies the good answer so closely is the gaze that never reaches the better one, that concentration on the first solution is exactly what starves the search for the second, stays hidden behind how much a captured attention resembles a diligent one.

## Yearbook

Suffered by [the Master](persona_the_master.md) whose eye it holds, broken on purpose by [the Doubter](persona_the_doubter.md) who forces the gaze off the found answer, and never formed in [the Novice](persona_the_novice.md) who had no first solution to fix on.
It is the hold that does the hiding: the Fixation is what [blocking](process_blocking.md) works through and [Search Past the Answer](plan_search_past_the_answer.md) fights, the grip [the first answer](piece_the_first_answer.md) has on the gaze and [the set](piece_the_set.md) tightens, keeping [the simpler way](piece_the_simpler_way.md) in the dark by keeping the eye in the light.
