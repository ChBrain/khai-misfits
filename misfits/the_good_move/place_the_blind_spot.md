# Place: The Blind Spot

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md). The region of the board the gaze never reaches, where the better move sits present and legal in the ground the found answer sends the attention away from.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Nothing, which is the whole of it.
Shown as empty: the region reads as already searched and found bare, so the mind passes over it satisfied, the absence of anything to see standing in for a place that was never truly looked at.

## Holds

Holds the better move, in the part of the board the eye skips.
The Blind Spot is where the better answer waits, fully present and legal, in the region the attention never reaches, so what sits there is not hidden by cover but hidden by neglect of gaze, the looking held on the first solution's ground and never spent here (Bilalic, McLeod and Gobet).

## Offers

Offers a better move to anyone who looks, and to the set-bound mind, nothing at all.
Here the better answer is available to any even gaze, so the Blind Spot offers the whole solution the good move blocked, and offers it plainly, withholding it only from the mind whose attention the first answer has already claimed, so the same ground is open field to the fresh eye and empty dark to the practised one.

## Withheld

Withholds its own existence from the one who most needs it.
What the Blind Spot hides is that it is there to be searched: to the set-bound solver it does not read as an unlooked-at region but as already-looked and empty, so the very fact that a place remains to be examined is what is withheld, and the mind reports a complete search precisely because the ground it skipped never announced itself as skipped.
