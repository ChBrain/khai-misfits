# Piece: The Simpler Way

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md). The better path present on the same problem, shorter or plainer than the found answer, available the whole time and left unreached.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Sits in [the Blind Spot](place_the_blind_spot.md), on the same [Board](place_the_board.md) as the found answer, just off [the Beaten Path](place_the_beaten_path.md) at the fork the groove runs past.
Flows as the solution that was there from the first, legal and reachable, waiting in the region the gaze never crosses.

## Load Bearing

Without the Simpler Way actually being there the misfit would be nothing: if the first answer were the only solution, stopping at it would be simply correct and there would be no better move to miss.
It is because a better path is genuinely present, on the same board and open to any even gaze, that the block has a cost, so the Simpler Way is load bearing because it is the loss the trap inflicts, the productive solution the set-bound mind never reaches while the reproductive one runs (Wertheimer). Its presence is what turns a fast correct answer into a miss: the better move is not a thing that might have been invented with more genius but a thing that was already on the board, shorter and plainer, so what the set costs is measured exactly by this piece, the available answer that the good one blocked and the fresh eye finds at a glance.

## Apparent

Once shown, so obvious it seems it could never have been missed.
Seen at the surface after the fact, the Simpler Way looks like something no competent mind could have overlooked, and that plainness is the cruelest part, because the truth that it was invisible not for being hidden but for being unlooked-at, that the same obviousness which makes it seem unmissable now is what made it seem already-searched then, stays behind how impossible a plain answer is to un-see once the gaze has finally reached it.

## Yearbook

Missed by [the Master](persona_the_master.md) whose looking never reached it, found plainly by [the Novice](persona_the_novice.md) who came with no set to skip it, and recovered by [the Doubter](persona_the_doubter.md) who searched past the answer to reach it.
It is the better move the good one blocks: the Simpler Way is what [blocking](process_blocking.md) keeps in the dark and [Come to It Fresh](plan_come_to_it_fresh.md) walks straight to, the path [the first answer](piece_the_first_answer.md) stands in front of and [the set](piece_the_set.md) hides, present on the board the whole while it goes unseen.
