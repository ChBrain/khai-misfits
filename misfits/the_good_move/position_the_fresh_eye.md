# Position: The Fresh Eye

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md). The unset seat, which holds no method to reach for and so meets the board whole, seeing the plain path the practised seat skips and helpless on everything the practised seat solves.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Nothing installed, and so an open field.
The seat holds no stock of solutions, so nothing fires ahead of the search, and the whole problem is available to it, none of the board spoken for by an answer already in hand.
An even attention as its only instrument, since with no first answer to hold the gaze, the looking spreads across the whole rather than pooling on the ground of a solution the seat does not have.

## Orders

Look at the problem, not at a method.
Meet the case as itself, since there is no known kind to assign it to, and read the whole board evenly, since nothing pulls the eye to one region over another.
Find the path that is there, since with no primed answer to prefer, the only move the seat can make is the one the problem actually offers, seen plainly for want of anything in the way.

## Loses

The competence that a stock of methods buys, which the seat has traded away entire.
The Fresh Eye has no answer ready for the ordinary problem, so where the Repertoire solves at a glance it must labour or fail, and its whole advantage holds only in the narrow case where a better path is present and a primed answer would have hidden it: everywhere else, which is nearly everywhere, the empty store is not clarity but incapacity. What the seat forfeits is durability, since the openness cannot be kept: every problem it solves teaches a method, and a method installed is a set installed, so the seat closes its own clear field by the very act of gaining skill, and the fresh sight it offers is a thing of the moment before expertise, valuable exactly while the seat is still unable and lost the instant it becomes able, so the seat that alone can see the better move is the seat that can least afford to stay itself.

## Drives

Toward a whole board seen plainly and no method to solve it with,
and an advantage that dissolves the moment it is used,
so the seat that keeps the field clear for the better move is the one that cannot hold its own clarity, unset only until it learns and helpless until then.
