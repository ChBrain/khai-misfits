# Plan: Take the Good Move

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Repertoire](position_the_repertoire.md), inside [The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Repertoire](position_the_repertoire.md)

## Direction

Meet the problem with the method that fits it, take the answer that method produces, and stop, because the answer works. Match the new case to the kind you know, apply the solution that kind has always yielded, and trust it, since the method is sound and the match is real (Luchins). Do not spend effort searching past a correct answer, since a solution that solves the problem is a reason to move on, and the practice that made the method ready is precisely what lets you answer at a glance. Accept that the speed comes from a set, and that on the problem where a better move is hiding, the same set that answered fast will have closed the looking first.

## Orders

([The Master](persona_the_master.md) reads the new problem as one of a known kind and lets the primed method fire, taking the good answer it produces and moving on, correct and fast and blind to the better move on the same board.)

## Implementation

([The Board](place_the_board.md); through the [firing](process_firing.md) and [settling](process_settling.md) processes; the Master applies the method to produce [the first answer](piece_the_first_answer.md), which [the set](piece_the_set.md) delivered ready, and stops there, the search closed before [the simpler way](piece_the_simpler_way.md) is ever reached.)

## Targets

- [ ] the problem is matched to a known kind and the fitting method applied
- [ ] the good answer it produces is taken, because it works
- [ ] no search is spent past a correct solution
- [ ] the speed of the answer is accepted as the set, block and expertise at once
