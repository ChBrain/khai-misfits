# Process: Priming

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md). The installing of the set, repeated success teaching the mind to meet the next problem as the last one, the disposition laid down by the very practice that makes it expert.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A run of problems all yielding to one method, at [the Beaten Path](place_the_beaten_path.md), each success deepening the groove the next problem will run down.
Begins whenever a solver solves the same kind of problem the same way often enough that the method stops being chosen and starts being ready, rising before it is called for.

## Direction

Turns repeated success into a disposition.
Because the method has worked every time, the mind learns not just the method but the reflex to reach for it, so priming converts a history of correct answers into a readiness to meet the new problem as one of the known kind, the set installed by the practice as its honest residue (Luchins).

## Lever

The reliability of the method.
Because the solution has never failed on its kind, there is every reason to keep it ready, so priming works through the plain soundness of the method: a thing that has always worked is a thing worth reaching for first, and the readiness that follows is the correct lesson of a real success, not a mistake in the learning.

## Echo

A mind set to see this problem as that kind.
The echo of priming is a disposition standing ready before the next problem is read, so the solver arrives already leaning toward the familiar answer, the set laid down and waiting, sound wherever the old method fits and a block wherever a better move is there to find.
