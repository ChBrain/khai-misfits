# Plan: Come to It Fresh

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Fresh Eye](position_the_fresh_eye.md), inside [The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Fresh Eye](position_the_fresh_eye.md)

## Direction

Meet the problem as itself, with no method held ready, so nothing fires ahead of the looking and the whole board stays open. Read the case evenly rather than assigning it to a known kind, since with no primed answer to prefer, the eye spreads across the whole and the plain path, wherever it sits, is simply part of what is seen (Luchins). Solve the problem the old method cannot reach by having no old method to reach for. Accept that this works only while the store is empty: the openness is not a skill but the absence of one, it fails on every ordinary problem an expert answers at a glance, and it is lost the moment the practice that would make you capable installs the set that would make you blind.

## Orders

([The Novice](persona_the_novice.md) comes to the problem with nothing installed, looks at all of it, and finds the plain path the practised gaze skips, winning on the extinction problem by lacking the very method that wins everywhere else.)

## Implementation

([The Board](place_the_board.md); against the [firing](process_firing.md) process; the Novice, holding no [set](piece_the_set.md), lets no [first answer](piece_the_first_answer.md) rise to spend the looking, so the even gaze reaches [the blind spot](place_the_blind_spot.md) and finds [the simpler way](piece_the_simpler_way.md) plainly.)

## Targets

- [ ] the problem is met as itself, with no method assigned to it
- [ ] the whole board is read evenly, none of it spoken for
- [ ] the plain path is found for want of anything in the way
- [ ] the advantage is accepted as the empty store, lost the moment it is filled
