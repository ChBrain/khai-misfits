# Plot: The Lock

## Taxonomy

[The Good Move](play_the_good_move.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Board](place_the_board.md), a problem that fits the primed pattern and also admits a better path.
A new case matching the known kind, the familiar answer rising to meet it, and a better move present on the same field.

## Action

[Firing](process_firing.md): the set fires, the familiar answer rises at once and works, and the mind locks onto it, correct and worse in the same stroke.

## Stage

[The Master](persona_the_master.md) reads the problem as one of the kind and [the set](piece_the_set.md) delivers [the first answer](piece_the_first_answer.md) ready, on [Take the Good Move](plan_take_the_good_move.md), the good solution in hand before a search begins while [the simpler way](piece_the_simpler_way.md) sits unremarked on the same board.
The answer works, which is the whole trouble: it is correct, and its correctness is what closes the case.

## Tension

The good move is right and worse at once, and the solver cannot tell, because the lock feels exactly like knowing.
The answer genuinely solves the problem, so taking it is sound, and there is no felt signal that a better move is present, since the found solution arrives with all the confidence of a hundred that were best. But a better path is there on the same board, and the firing has already spent the moment a search would have used, so the plot closes with the mind locked onto a correct answer it has every reason to trust, the better move present and unlooked-at, the good move taken as the move and the case shut on it.
