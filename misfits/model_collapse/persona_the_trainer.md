# Persona: The Trainer

## Taxonomy

[Trainer](position_trainer.md). Feeds each new model the abundant data at hand, rationally, more of it synthetic each cycle.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Practical. Resourceful. Watching the budget.
Builds the next model and needs data to train it, and draws the ocean at hand, cheap and plentiful and reading just fine.
No corner-cutting in it: only a builder using the abundant resource in front of it, the way anyone sensible would.

## Action

Trains the new model on the data at hand, taking what is cheap and plentiful without sorting made from real.
Scrapes the pool as it is, machine-made and genuine alike, because gathering fresh human data is slow and dear.
Does the obvious, efficient thing: uses what is there.

## Shadow

Not lazy, rational: training on the abundant data at hand is sound, synthetic data reads as well as any and costs far less, and no single builder's draught fouls a whole commons.
Because the pool it draws is already part model output, and it cannot cheaply tell which, the trainer feeds its model a corpus already thinned, and its own model's output pours back to thin it further, so the sensible use of what is there is what closes the loop.
It does right by its budget and its schedule, and helps, unseeing, to narrow the world every next model will learn.

## Tell

Says "there is data everywhere, and it is free, so why gather more."
Draws it, and the ocean it drank was already an echo of the models before.
