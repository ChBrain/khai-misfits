# Process: Generating

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The learning of a distribution from finite samples and the producing of more like it, faithful to the middle and poorer at the tails.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A model and a corpus to learn from.
At [the corpus](place_the_corpus.md), the learner fitting the dense middle from many sightings and the sparse tails from few, then producing samples of its own.

## Direction

Toward samples faithful to the common and thin at the rare.
The model fits what it saw often far better than what it saw seldom, so generating drives its output to reproduce the dense middle in full and the tails as thinly as it learned them, each batch a shade poorer in the rare than the data behind it.

## Lever

The nature of learning from finite samples.
Because a learner can only fit what it has seen enough of, and the rare is by definition seen too seldom, undersampling the tails is not an error but what generating from limited data must do, so the process runs on sound learning, the narrowing built into fidelity itself.

## Echo

A model faithful to its training, and a shade blander than the world.
The samples read as clean and true and reproduce the everyday exactly, and they carry a fraction less variance than the data that made them, so what reads as a model faithfully giving back what it learned is, unseen, the first shave off the tails of the world.
