# Place: The Corpus

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). Where a model is trained, the body of data drawn from the pool and fed to the learner.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The training set assembled for a build, a great body of text and image scraped from the common and handed to the model.
Real and synthetic mixed through it unmarked, the tails already thinner than the world's, and the model learning it as if it were the world entire.

## Holds

The generating, and the corpus's silent thinning.
Here the model meets the data it will learn, so the Corpus holds the point where a drawn sample becomes a learned distribution: whatever is dense in it the model masters and whatever is sparse it half-learns, so it is where a corpus already poorer in the tails becomes a model poorer still, the narrowing passed from data to learner.

## Offers

A world to learn, cheap and ready.
Because the Corpus is the assembled data a model trains on, it offers the builder a whole distribution to fit without the cost of gathering it fresh, so it hands out the training set the schedule needs, and hands out one already part echo, its tails already shaved, so the model learns a world a shade narrower than the real.

## Withheld

The rare it never held enough of.
The Corpus cannot give the model what it barely contains, so it withholds the tails that were already thin in it, and, keeping no mark of provenance, it withholds any sign that its water is part echo, so the model learns the narrowed corpus as the whole world and cannot know what was missing.
