# Place: The Vault

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). Where genuine human data is held back and marked, the store of the real kept outside the loop.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A set-aside store of genuine human data, gathered, marked for provenance, and guarded from the recursion.
The wellkeeper blending it into each run, and the pull against it: the free ocean outside, and every builder who would rather drink cheap than pay to keep the store.

## Holds

The preserving, and the cost that resists it.
Here the real is kept apart from the pool and fed deliberately into training, so the Vault holds the point where the remedy meets its price: a store of true data blended into every run holds the tails open, and it costs the dear and finite against the free and endless, so it is where keeping the world in the models is bought against the abundance of the made.

## Offers

The world, kept outside the loop.
Because the Vault is where genuine data is held and marked, it offers the one source that is not an echo, so it hands out the real that holds the tails open, and hands it out dear and slow and finite, so the fidelity it preserves is exactly as costly as the synthetic is cheap.

## Withheld

The abundance and the ease of the free pool.
The Vault cannot make the real as endless or as instant as the made, so it withholds the cheap plenty the cheap pool offers, and, a store that only dwindles once the commons is fouled and that one hand cannot keep alone, it withholds any promise that the well will hold if others drink free, so the remedy is real and dear and never safe from the free-rider.
