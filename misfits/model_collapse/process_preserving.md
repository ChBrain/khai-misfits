# Process: Preserving

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The holding back and blending of genuine data against the recursion, the remedy bought dear against the free pool.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A commons seen to be filling with echoes, and someone unwilling to train on output alone.
At [the vault](place_the_vault.md), the wellkeeper holding back real data, marking its provenance, and blending it into each run.

## Direction

Toward a source of the world kept outside the loop.
The wellkeeper sets the genuine apart and feeds it deliberately into training, so preserving drives real human data into every run against the pull of the cheap pool, holding the tails open by keeping the models drinking from more than each other.

## Lever

The plain truth that a model drinking only from models forgets the world.
Because output trained on output alone collapses, the case to blend the real is sound, so preserving runs on the same sense that built the models, turned now to the dear discipline of paying for the genuine where the synthetic is free.

## Echo

The tails held open, at a price that only holds if others pay it.
The store of real data has kept the world in this run and the recursion is checked where it is blended, and the real spent was dear and finite and the free pool still undercuts it, so what reads as a solved problem is, in truth, a well kept against every builder's incentive to drink cheap and let it dry.
