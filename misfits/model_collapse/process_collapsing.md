# Process: Collapsing

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The cumulative narrowing across generations, the tails then the variance then the shape lost to a self-referential mean.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A loss that feeds the loss, generation over generation.
At [the common](place_the_common.md), each model learning from a world narrowed by the last and narrowing it again, the shave compounding turn by turn.

## Direction

Toward a distribution creeping to a flat self-referential mean.
Each generation reproduces a world already poorer in the tails and drops a little more, so collapsing drives the sequence downward, the rare gone first, then the variance, then the shape itself, until the models resemble the world less than they resemble each other.

## Lever

The compounding of a per-generation loss no single step would notice.
Because each model's shave is a shade too small to mark, and each feeds the next, the losses accumulate unremarked, so collapsing runs on the invisibility of the single step, the forgetting assembled from narrowings none of which alone looked like harm.

## Echo

Models that know the middle and have forgotten the edges.
The output is fluent and self-consistent and thinner than the world by more each generation, the rare gone past recovering, so what reads as models steadily reproducing themselves is, in truth, a distribution collapsed to a bland average of an average.
