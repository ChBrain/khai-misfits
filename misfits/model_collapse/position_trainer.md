# Position: Trainer

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The seat that trains on the data at hand, sensible for one build and loop-closing for all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A model to build, a budget and a schedule, and an ocean of cheap data at hand.
A sound reason to use it: the pool is abundant and free, and gathering fresh human data is slow and dear.
No cheap way to tell the made from the real in what it draws, only the choice to draw it.

## Orders

Train on what's there.
Take the abundant data at hand, machine-made and genuine alike, and feed it to the model.
Use what is cheap and plentiful, the way any sensible builder would.

## Loses

The freshness of its training data, exactly by drawing the cheap pool.
Because the pool is already part model output and it cannot cheaply mark which, the seat trains on a corpus already thinned, and its own model's output pours back to thin it more, so it cannot both draw the abundant cheap water and keep the world in its data.
It builds on schedule and under budget, and drinks an ocean that was already an echo.

## Drives

Toward a model trained on the data at hand,
sensible for one builder and narrowing for all,
so the efficient use of what is there is the move that closes the loop on the tails.
