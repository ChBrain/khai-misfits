# Persona: The Wellkeeper

## Taxonomy

[Wellkeeper](position_wellkeeper.md). Would hold back real data, mark provenance, and blend the genuine into every run, against the pull of the cheap pool.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Far-sighted. Willing to pay for what is real.
Sees the pool filling with echoes and moves to keep a store of genuine human data, marked and set apart.
No hoarding in it: only someone who holds that a model must drink from the world, not only from other models.

## Action

Keeps the well: holds back genuine human data, marks its provenance, and blends the real into every training run.
Refuses to train on output alone, and pays to gather and label the true where the synthetic is free.
Does the one thing that holds the tails open: keeps a source of the world outside the recursion.

## Shadow

Not a saviour: real data is finite and slow and dear where synthetic is endless and instant and free, and the well cannot be kept by one hand alone.
Because any builder gains by drinking the cheap pool while others pay to keep it clean, and marking provenance asks every publisher to label what they need not, the Wellkeeper spends against the abundance of the made and the free-rider's incentive at once, and the store of true data, once the commons is fouled, is a fixed and dwindling thing no diligence refills.
It can hold the tails open, only by paying for the real that the free synthetic will always undercut, and only if others pay too.

## Tell

Says "keep some of the world outside the loop, or the models will only ever echo each other."
Is right, and being right means paying for the dear real while everyone else drinks free.
