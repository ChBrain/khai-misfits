# Piece: The Tail

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The rare cases, the sparse edges of the distribution, lost first and then past recovering.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Undersampled at [the corpus](place_the_corpus.md), too seldom seen to be learned.
Absent from [the common](place_the_common.md) a little more each generation, until it is gone from the water entirely.

## Load Bearing

Without it the collapse has no first casualty, and the narrowing is a vague loss with nothing specific to name.
With it the mechanism has its point of entry: the rare event, seen too seldom for the learner to hold, is the first thing each generation drops, so the tails go before the variance and the variance before the shape. The Tail is load bearing because it is where the forgetting begins and where the world's diversity lives (Shumailov et al.): the rare is what a finite learner cannot keep and a recursion cannot recover, so the piece carries the sparse edge whose loss is the leading edge of the collapse, the first of the world to be forgotten.

## Apparent

A negligible fraction, an edge case, a sliver the model can surely spare.
Seen alone, the tail looks like a rounding error, a rare event of no great weight, and the truth that the tails hold the diversity and truth of the distribution, that losing them first is the collapse beginning and that once gone from the recursion they cannot be relearned, stays hidden behind how small a fraction the rare seems next to the dense common middle.

## Yearbook

Dropped by [the model](persona_the_model.md) through [collapsing](process_collapsing.md), the rare edge of [the distribution](piece_the_distribution.md) too seldom seen to keep.
It is the leading edge of the trap: the Tail is the first thing each generation forgets, the diversity [the made](piece_the_made.md) lacks, the world's variety that only [the real well](piece_the_real_well.md), blended back, can hold open against the narrowing.
