# Piece: The Real Well

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The remedy and its scarcity, the finite store of genuine human data that alone holds the tails open.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Kept at [the vault](place_the_vault.md), held back and marked and blended into each run.
Set against [the common](place_the_common.md), the free fouled pool it is the costly alternative to.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play has no answer, and the recursion runs to a flat mean with nothing to hold the tails.
With it the misfit has its costly cure: a store of genuine human data, marked for provenance and blended into every training run, is the one thing that keeps the world in the models, and it works only where the real is paid for against the free synthetic. The Real Well is load bearing because it is the remedy that carries its own scarcity (provenance and data-ecology work): the genuine is finite, slow, and dear where the made is endless and free, and once the commons is fouled the store only dwindles, so the piece carries both the cure for the collapse and the free-rider trap that undoes it.

## Apparent

An easy fix, just use real data, keep some human text aside and be done.
Seen hopefully, the real well looks like a simple safeguard, a reserve anyone can draw on, and the truth that genuine data is finite and dear where synthetic is free and endless, that marking provenance asks every publisher to label what they need not, and that any builder gains by drinking the cheap pool while others pay to keep the well, stays hidden behind how obvious keeping some real data sounds.

## Yearbook

Kept by [the wellkeeper](persona_the_wellkeeper.md) through [preserving](process_preserving.md), the store of the genuine held outside the loop.
It is the cure the play cannot make painless: the Real Well blends [the distribution](piece_the_distribution.md) back into training to hold [the tail](piece_the_tail.md) open against [the made](piece_the_made.md), and cannot do so without paying the dear finite real against the free endless synthetic, undone by any builder who drinks the cheap pool while others keep it clean.
