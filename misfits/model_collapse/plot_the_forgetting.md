# Plot: The Forgetting

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Common](place_the_common.md), the loss feeding the loss, generation over generation.
The tails gone first, the variance thinning, the water flatter with every turn.

## Action

[Collapsing](process_collapsing.md): [The Model](persona_the_model.md), holding [the model's seat](position_model.md), and [The Pool](persona_the_pool.md), holding [the pool's seat](position_pool.md), turn each other downward, the distribution creeping toward a self-referential mean.

## Stage

[The Model](persona_the_model.md), still on [Generate the Likely](plan_generate_the_likely.md), reproduces a world already narrowed and drops a little more, generation after generation.
[The Pool](persona_the_pool.md), still on [Take It All In](plan_take_it_all_in.md), feeds each thinner corpus to the next model, so the loss compounds, the rare gone first, then the variance, then the shape.

## Tension

Each faithful reproduction leaves the world a shade further behind.
There is no villain still: the model reproduces what it learned and the pool holds what it is given, each step too small a loss to mark. But the losses compound, so the plot closes with the rare forgotten past recovering and the models resembling one another more than the world, a distribution collapsed to a bland average of an average of an average.
