# Piece: The Distribution

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The true shape of the data, its dense middle the common case and its sparse tails the diversity of the real.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Learned at [the corpus](place_the_corpus.md), where the model fits its middle well and its tails barely.
Held, in its genuine form, only in [the vault](place_the_vault.md), the store of the real kept outside the loop.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to narrow, and a model's samples are just samples, neither faithful nor forgetful.
With it the whole trap has its measure: a real shape with a dense middle and sparse tails, so that a learner fitting finite samples captures the middle and misses the edges, and every reproduction is judged against the true shape it falls short of. The Distribution is load bearing because it is the standard the collapse is a departure from (statistical-learning theory): the tails are where the diversity of the world lives, so the piece carries the true shape whose rare edges the recursion strips away, the world the models are supposed to hold and steadily forget.

## Apparent

An abstraction, a statistician's curve, a thing the model surely reproduces well enough.
Seen at the surface, the distribution looks fully captured, the model fluent across it, and the truth that its sparse tails are undersampled and lost first, that the rare edges carrying the world's diversity are exactly what a finite learner cannot keep, stays hidden behind how complete a fluent model's coverage of the common middle appears.

## Yearbook

Approximated by [the model](persona_the_model.md) through [generating](process_generating.md), the true shape it fits in the middle and misses at the edges.
It is the measure of the trap: the Distribution's sparse [tail](piece_the_tail.md) is what the model undersamples and the recursion forgets, its middle all that survives the [collapse](piece_model_collapse.md), and its genuine form is what only [the real well](piece_the_real_well.md) keeps outside the loop.
