# Pitch: Data at Hand

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Sensible, efficient, unbothered.
The plain good sense of training the next model on the abundant data at hand, more of it machine-made each year, because it is cheap and plentiful and reads just fine.
Nothing reckless in it: only a builder using what is there, the way anyone would.

## Undertow

Under the efficiency, each generation learning from the last one's output and losing the rare first.
Because a model fits the dense middle and misses the sparse tails, its samples come out a shade blander than its training data, and fed back into the pool they thin the world the next model learns, and the next, so the rare becomes the unseen becomes the forgotten.
What is efficient for each builder is, down the generations, the models forgetting the world and echoing only themselves.

## Nerve

The feeling of doing the obvious thing, training on the ocean of cheap data right there,
sure it is clean enough, because for one model it is,
while the tails thin with every generation and the distribution creeps toward a flat average of an average, resembling the world a little less each turn.

## Echo

After the run: models that know only the middle, the rare gone past recovering, a bland mean echoing itself,
no one poisoning the well, only each builder drawing the cheap pool and each model pouring back something flatter than it took,
and a store of real human data that would have held the tails open, spent down and free-ridden away, because the well is dear and finite and the made is free and endless.
