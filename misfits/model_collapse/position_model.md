# Position: Model

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The seat that fits the middle and misses the tails, faithful for one generation and narrowing across many.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A distribution to learn from finite samples, and the means to generate more like it.
A sound competence: the common case mastered from many sightings, the rare one glimpsed too seldom to hold.
No way to keep what it never saw enough of, only to reproduce what it learned.

## Orders

Generate the likely.
Learn the distribution you are shown and give it back, the everyday in full and the rare as best you can.
Reproduce the world you saw, faithfully.

## Loses

The tails, exactly by learning from finite samples.
Because it fits the dense middle far better than the sparse edges, the seat cannot reproduce the rare it barely saw, so every batch of its samples is a shade poorer in the tails than its training data, and it has no power to keep a variance it was never shown enough of to learn.
It is faithful to what it saw and, being faithful, gives back a world already a fraction narrower.

## Drives

Toward a world reproduced a shade blander,
faithful for one generation and forgetting across many,
so the fidelity that serves each model is what carries the narrowing to the next.
