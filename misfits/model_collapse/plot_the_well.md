# Plot: The Well

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Vault](place_the_vault.md), the pool gone part echo and one actor moving to keep a store of the real.
The tails thinning, and the means to hold back genuine data and mark provenance at hand.

## Action

[Preserving](process_preserving.md): [The Wellkeeper](persona_the_wellkeeper.md), holding [the wellkeeper's seat](position_wellkeeper.md), would blend real data into every run, against [The Trainer](persona_the_trainer.md), holding [the trainer's seat](position_trainer.md), whose pull is to the cheap abundant pool.

## Stage

[The Wellkeeper](persona_the_wellkeeper.md), having tabled [Keep the Well](plan_keep_the_well.md), holds back genuine human data, marks its provenance, and blends the real into training so no model drinks output alone.
[The Trainer](persona_the_trainer.md), still on [Train on What's There](plan_train_on_whats_there.md), feels every reason the free pool is cheaper, and any builder who drinks it while others keep the well comes out ahead.

## Tension

The one move that keeps the world in the models is the one the free pool always undercuts.
There is no easy remedy: blending real data plainly holds the tails open, yet the genuine is finite and dear where the synthetic is endless and free, marking provenance asks what no one need give, and a well one hand keeps is drunk dry by the rest. So the play closes where it must, the well kept and the world held in the models a while ... or the cheap pool is drunk once more, the store dwindles, and the collapse runs on.
