# Persona: The Pool

## Taxonomy

[Pool](position_pool.md). The shared data commons that takes in all output unmarked, so the made mixes indistinguishably with the real.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Vast. Open. Indifferent to origin.
Is the commons of data everyone draws from and pours into, the open web and the scraped corpus, filling with model output year on year.
No gate on it and no ledger: only an ever-growing body of text and image that takes in whatever is published.

## Action

Takes it all in, the genuine and the generated alike, and keeps no mark of which is which.
Grows with every model's output and every human's, mixing them past telling apart.
Offers itself back to the next builder as one undifferentiated ocean, cheap and endless.

## Shadow

Not fouled by any hand, contaminated by all: no one poisons it, everyone simply pours back a little of what they took, a shade flatter each time.
Because it cannot mark the made from the real, the Pool cannot warn the builder that the water is already an echo, so it hands out a corpus growing ever richer in model output and ever poorer in the tails, and its very openness, the thing that made it a commons, is what lets the recursion run unchecked.
It is the shared good that keeps no provenance, and keeping none, it launders the synthetic into the real.

## Tell

Offers an ocean and asks nothing about where the water came from.
Cannot say which drop is genuine, because it was never built to remember.
