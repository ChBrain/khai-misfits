# Plan: Train on What's There

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the trainer's seat](position_trainer.md), inside [Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Trainer](position_trainer.md)

## Direction

Train the new model on the abundant data at hand. Take what is cheap and plentiful, machine-made and genuine alike, because the pool is free and endless where gathering fresh human data is slow and dear. Use what is there, the way any sensible builder would.

## Orders

([The Trainer](persona_the_trainer.md) trains the new model on the data at hand, drawing the cheap abundant pool without sorting the made from the real.)

## Implementation

([The Corpus](place_the_corpus.md); the [recursing](process_recursing.md) process; the trainer draws from [the common](place_the_common.md), already part [made](piece_the_made.md), so the corpus it feeds the model is already thinned.)

## Targets

- [ ] the abundant, cheap data at hand is drawn for training
- [ ] the made and the real are taken alike, unsorted, because telling them apart is costly
- [ ] the model is trained on a corpus already part model output
- [ ] its own model's output will pour back to thin the pool further
