# Process: Recursing

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The return of model output into the pool the next model trains on, closing the loop that carries the loss forward.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A model's output published, and a builder needing data.
At [the common](place_the_common.md), the samples pouring back into the shared pool unmarked, and the next trainer drawing from it for the next build.

## Direction

Toward each generation training on the last one's output.
The model's samples flow into the pool and the next corpus is drawn from that pool, so recursing drives the output of one generation to become the training data of the next, the made mixed unmarked with the real and the loop closing turn by turn.

## Lever

The abundance of the pool and the openness that keeps no mark.
Because the cheap pool is what every builder sensibly draws and the commons keeps no provenance to warn them, each draught of already-thinned water feeds the next model, so recursing runs on efficiency and openness together, the loop closed by moves no one can fault.

## Echo

A corpus drawn, and an echo passed on.
The new model has its abundant cheap training data and the pool has taken in another round of output, and the water is a shade more echo and a shade less world, so what reads as reusing the plentiful data at hand is, unseen, the loop tightening on the tails.
