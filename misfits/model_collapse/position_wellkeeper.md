# Position: Wellkeeper

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The seat that would keep a store of real data against the recursion, at a cost, and only if others pay too.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A means to hold back genuine human data, to mark provenance, and to blend the real into every run.
A clear case: a model that drinks only from other models forgets the world.
No way to make the real as cheap as the synthetic, and no power to make other builders pay to keep the well.

## Orders

Keep the well.
Hold back genuine human data, mark what is real, and blend it into every training run so no model trains on output alone.
Keep a source of the world outside the loop.

## Loses

The savings and the ease, exactly what the cheap pool offers.
Because real data is finite and slow and dear where synthetic is endless and free, and any builder gains by drinking the cheap pool while others keep it clean, the seat cannot hold the tails open without paying the price the pool waives and bearing the free-rider's undercut, and the store of true data, once the commons is fouled, only dwindles.
It buys the world's presence in the models against the abundance of the made and the incentive of every builder to free-ride, and cannot hold it alone.

## Drives

Toward a store of the real kept outside the loop,
bought dear against the free synthetic and the free-rider,
so the one seat that would keep the world in the models must pay for it while the rest drink free.
