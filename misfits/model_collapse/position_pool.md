# Position: Pool

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The seat of the shared data commons, open to all and marking none, so the made launders into the real.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A vast open body of data that everyone draws from and pours into.
An openness that made it a commons: no gate, no toll, no ledger of where anything came from.
No means to mark the made from the real, only to hold whatever is published.

## Orders

Take it all in.
Absorb every output, genuine and generated alike, and offer it back as one cheap endless ocean.
Keep no provenance, ask no origin, gate nothing.

## Loses

Its fidelity, exactly by the openness that makes it useful.
Because it takes in all output and cannot mark the synthetic, the seat fills year on year with model echoes it cannot flag, so it cannot both stay the open commons everyone draws from and keep the water true, and its refusal to gate is what lets the recursion run unchecked through it.
It grows ever larger and ever more an echo, laundering the made into the real by keeping no record of the difference.

## Drives

Toward a commons that keeps no provenance,
open to all and true to none,
so the very openness that made it a shared good is what lets it drown the real in the made.
