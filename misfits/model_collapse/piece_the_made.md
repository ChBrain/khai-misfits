# Piece: The Made

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The synthetic output, model-generated data flowing back into the pool unmarked, the substance of the loop.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Poured into [the common](place_the_common.md), where it mixes indistinguishably with the real.
Drawn back at [the corpus](place_the_corpus.md), where it becomes the next model's training data.

## Load Bearing

Without it the loop does not close, and a model's undersampling is a one-time shave with nowhere to compound.
With it the recursion has its carrier: the model's output, poorer in the tails, returns to the pool and becomes training data, so the loss of one generation is fed to the next as fact. The Made is load bearing because it is what turns a single shave into a spiral (Shumailov et al.; Alemohammad et al.): synthetic data indistinguishable from the real and thinner in the tails is what each generation drinks, so the piece carries the model output whose unmarked return closes the loop and compounds the forgetting.

## Apparent

Ordinary data, clean and useful, as good as any and cheaper to gather.
Seen in the pool, the made looks like any other text or image, fluent and serviceable, and the truth that it is an echo already poorer in the tails, that training on it feeds the next model a narrowed world, that its very indistinguishability from the real is what launders the loss into the commons, stays hidden behind how genuine well-formed model output reads.

## Yearbook

Produced by [the model](persona_the_model.md) and absorbed by [the pool](persona_the_pool.md) through [recursing](process_recursing.md), the output poured back unmarked.
It is the carrier of the trap: the Made is [the distribution](piece_the_distribution.md) minus its [tail](piece_the_tail.md), fed back as training data to compound the [collapse](piece_model_collapse.md), and told from the genuine only where [the real well](piece_the_real_well.md) keeps a mark of provenance.
