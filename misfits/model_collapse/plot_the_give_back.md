# Plot: The Give-Back

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Common](place_the_common.md), the model's output flowing back into the open pool.
Samples poured into the commons unmarked, settling among the genuine, the tails already thin.

## Action

[Recursing](process_recursing.md): [The Model](persona_the_model.md), holding [the model's seat](position_model.md), gives back the likely and drops the rare, while [The Pool](persona_the_pool.md), holding [the pool's seat](position_pool.md), takes it all in unmarked.

## Stage

[The Model](persona_the_model.md), still on [Generate the Likely](plan_generate_the_likely.md), publishes its samples into the world, fluent and clean-reading and poorer in the tails.
[The Pool](persona_the_pool.md), on [Take It All In](plan_take_it_all_in.md), absorbs them among the genuine and keeps no mark, so the made and the real settle together past telling apart.

## Tension

The commons cannot flag the echo it just drank.
There is no poisoner: the model only publishes its output and the pool only takes in what is published, each doing its open, ordinary work. But the pool keeps no provenance, so the plot closes with the commons holding a growing share of model output, indistinguishable from the real and thinner in the tails, the loop's return leg quietly in place.
