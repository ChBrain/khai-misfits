# Piece: Model Collapse

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The phenomenon whole, a field training on its own output until it forgets the world and echoes only itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Everywhere the production runs: from [the corpus](place_the_corpus.md) through [the common](place_the_common.md) down the generations, and against [the vault](place_the_vault.md) where it is resisted.
It is the whole arc, the learner undersampling the tails and the pool laundering the made until the distribution collapses to a self-referential mean.

## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without a name: a shape, a lost edge, an echo, a store of the real, and no account of how one becomes the next.
With it the misfit is whole: model collapse is the trap entire, a learner that must miss the rare and a pool that cannot mark the made summing, generation over generation, to models that forget the world and resemble only each other, the efficiency and the forgetting shown to be one thing. It is load bearing because it is the structure the whole production exists to stage (Shumailov et al.): the frame that binds the undersampled tail, the recursive pool, the compounding narrowing, and the finite real well into a single bend, so the piece carries the phenomenon as a whole, a field that feeds on its own output until the world is gone from it.

## Apparent

Progress, more data and better models every year, an ecosystem thriving on its own abundance.
Seen at the surface, model collapse looks like the opposite of itself, a field awash in cheap plentiful data and models growing more fluent, and the truth that the abundance is increasingly echo, that fluency on the common middle hides the loss of the rare, that a field training on its own output is quietly forgetting the world, stays hidden behind how healthy an ocean of cheap data and a fluent model appear.

## Yearbook

The whole of it: Model Collapse is [the model](persona_the_model.md) undersampling the [tail](piece_the_tail.md) of [the distribution](piece_the_distribution.md) and pouring [the made](piece_the_made.md) back through [the pool](persona_the_pool.md), compounding down the generations, answered only by [the real well](piece_the_real_well.md) kept dear against the free.
It is the misfit named: a field feeding on its own output until it echoes only itself, no poisoner in it and no free hero, the world forgotten a shade at a time.
