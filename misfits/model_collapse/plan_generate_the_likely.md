# Plan: Generate the Likely

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the model's seat](position_model.md), inside [Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Model](position_model.md)

## Direction

Learn the distribution you are shown and give it back. Reproduce the common case in full and the rare one as best you can from the few times you saw it. Generate the likely, faithfully, the everyday mastered and the tails as thin as your finite view of them allows.

## Orders

([The Model](persona_the_model.md) generates the likely and drops the rare, producing samples faithful to the dense middle and poorer at the sparse tails.)

## Implementation

([The Corpus](place_the_corpus.md); the [generating](process_generating.md) process; the model fits [the distribution](piece_the_distribution.md) but undersamples its tails, so its samples come out a shade blander than what it learned.)

## Targets

- [ ] the distribution is learned, the dense middle far better than the sparse tails
- [ ] the likely is generated in full and the rare as thinly as it was seen
- [ ] each batch of samples is a shade poorer in the tails than the training data
- [ ] the loss is a shade no one built the model to keep, and none would notice in one generation
