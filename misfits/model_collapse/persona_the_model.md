# Persona: The Model

## Taxonomy

[Model](position_model.md). Fits the dense middle and misses the sparse tails, so it generates a world a shade blander than it learned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Fluent. Sure of the common case.
Learns the distribution it is shown and reproduces it, the everyday example mastered, the rare one half-seen.
No fault in it: only a learner doing what learning from finite samples does, fitting the middle far better than the edges.

## Action

Generates the likely and drops the rare, producing samples faithful to the common and thin at the tails.
Reproduces the shape of its training data, minus a shade of variance no one built it to keep.
Does what a good model does: gives back the world it saw, a fraction narrower than it was.

## Shadow

Not broken, finite: undersampling the rare is not a defect to fix but what a learner trained on limited samples must do, and for one model the loss is a shade no one would notice.
Because its output flows back into the pool and the next model learns from it, that unnoticed shade compounds, each generation reproducing a world already narrowed and narrowing it again, so its very fidelity to what it saw is what carries the forgetting forward.
It is faithful to its training and, being faithful, passes on a training a little poorer than the world.

## Tell

Says "here is the world as I learned it," and means it.
Gives back the middle in full and the tails half-gone, and calls it the world.
