# Place: The Common

## Taxonomy

[Model Collapse](play_model_collapse.md). The open data commons where model output floods and mixes with the real, and where the next corpus is drawn.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The open web and the shared corpus, an ocean everyone draws from and pours into, growing by the day.
Model output flowing in unmarked and settling among the genuine, the water reading clean while its share of echo climbs year on year.

## Holds

The recursing, and the contamination it carries.
Here every model's output returns to the water the next model will drink, so the Common holds the point where a generation's samples become a generation's training data: the made pours in and cannot be flagged, so it is where the loop closes, one model's narrowed world becoming the next one's source.

## Offers

An ocean of data, free and endless.
Because the Common is where all output collects, it offers every builder abundant cheap water without the cost of gathering the real, so it hands out the pool the budget loves, and hands out a pool ever more an echo of the models that fed it, so the abundance is genuine and the freshness is not.

## Withheld

The mark that would tell the made from the real.
The Common cannot flag what it never recorded, so it withholds the provenance that would let a builder drink only the genuine, and, being open to all and gating none, it withholds any brake on the recursion running through it, so the loop tightens unseen and the water grows flatter with every turn.
