# Persona: The Public

## Taxonomy

[Citizen](position_citizen.md). The diffuse many, each with too little at stake to show up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Numerous, busy, mildly affected.
Bears the cost of the rules spread thin across millions, a sliver each.
Would object, in the abstract, if it ever had a reason to look.

## Action

Stays home, rationally.
Weighs a day fighting the rule against a sliver of harm, and does the sensible thing, which is nothing.
Leaves the hearing to the one interest for whom it is worth attending.

## Shadow

Its reasonable absence is the whole opening.
No citizen's share of the harm is worth the fight, so the diffuse interest is never carried into the room, and the agency, hearing no one for the public, is left with no one but the industry.
Not apathetic: simply unable to organise a stake too thin to feel.

## Tell

"What difference would my one voice make?"
The true and fatal calculation that empties the public's chair.
