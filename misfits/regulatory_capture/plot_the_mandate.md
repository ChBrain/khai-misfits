# Plot: The Mandate

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The hearing](place_the_hearing.md), the first of many, with a rule to be written and chairs for every interest it will touch.
An agency newly founded to guard the public against the industry, its charge fresh and its faith intact.

## Action

[Lobbying](process_lobbying.md): [The Lobby](persona_the_lobby.md), holding [the industry's seat](position_industry.md), fills the room with counsel and data, while [The Public](persona_the_public.md), holding [the citizen's seat](position_citizen.md), weighs a day against a sliver and stays home, and [The Watchdog](persona_the_watchdog.md), holding [the regulator's seat](position_regulator.md), takes up the charge and hears only the side that came.

## Stage

[The Watchdog](persona_the_watchdog.md) tables [Guard the Public](plan_guard_the_public.md): take up [the mandate](piece_the_mandate.md) in good faith and regulate the trade well.
[The Lobby](persona_the_lobby.md) tables [Work the Agency](plan_work_the_agency.md) and [The Public](persona_the_public.md) tables [Stay Home](plan_stay_home.md), so [the concentrated interest](piece_the_concentrated_interest.md) seats one side and empties the other before a rule is drafted.

## Tension

The agency is honest, the charge is real, and the room is already lopsided.
Nothing has gone wrong: the mandate is meant, the door is open, the public is free to come and rationally does not. That is the trap loading, an agency that truly began as the public's, taking its first testimony from the only interest that showed up, with no one yet aware which way the room is tilted.
