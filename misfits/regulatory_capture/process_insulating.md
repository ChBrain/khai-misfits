# Process: Insulating

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The distance built on purpose, trading the industry's access for the agency's independence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A watchdog seen to serve the industry, and someone resolved to wall it off.
The closeness that captured the agency, named at last as the thing to cut.

## Direction

The agency severed from the trade far enough to hear the public again.
Independent funding, a cooled door, an advocate for the diffuse, facts from beyond the sector: the proximity undone where the capture fed on it.

## Lever

Distance imposed against expertise that only closeness supplied.
Every wall that frees the agency from the industry also cuts it off from the industry's data, cooperation, and ease, so independence is bought with access.

## Echo

An agency more independent and more estranged at once.
The public interest audible again, the industry's expertise no longer flowing free, and the wall always one sharp flare from being judged a hobble and torn down.
