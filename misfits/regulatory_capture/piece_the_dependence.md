# Piece: The Dependence

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The information asymmetry: an agency that cannot see the trade except through the trade's own facts, models, and staff.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the agency](place_the_agency.md), in every briefing taken on the industry's word.
Where the facts the regulator rules by are the facts the regulated supplies.

## Load Bearing

Without it the agency could simply check the industry against its own knowledge.
With it there is no independent knowledge to check against: to regulate a trade the agency must understand it, and only the trade can teach it, so the source of the facts becomes the source of the frame. The Dependence is load bearing because it is the information asymmetry at the heart of the formal model (Laffont & Tirole), the lever by which the regulated shapes the regulator without a single falsehood, and it makes competence and capture the same act of listening.

## Apparent

Diligence, and good use of expertise.
Leaning on the people who know the work looks like exactly what a responsible agency should do, and it is, which is why the reliance never registers as surrender: it wears the face of doing the job well.

## Yearbook

Lived by [the watchdog](persona_the_watchdog.md) through [depending](process_depending.md), grown out of [the mandate](piece_the_mandate.md) taken seriously.
It is the frame arriving with the facts: the Dependence is what opens the agency to [the cultural capture](piece_the_cultural_capture.md), the worldview that comes free with the data.
