# Piece: The Revolving Door

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The career channel: the flow between regulator and regulated that aligns the agency's future with the industry's.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the revolving door](place_the_revolving_door.md), turning both ways.
Where the agency's experts arrive from the industry and depart back into it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the regulator would owe the industry nothing and could rule as a stranger.
With it the next job is on the far side of the door, so the rule softens toward the hand that will pay next, and no line need be crossed for it to bend: the mere shape of the career does the work. The Revolving Door is load bearing because it is the second lever of the formal model (Laffont & Tirole), the way the regulated captures the regulator through incentive rather than corruption, and it turns an agency's own competence, drawn from the trade, into its own dependence on the trade's favour.

## Apparent

The only place the expertise could come from.
Who else would understand the work well enough to regulate it but the people who did it and will again? The door looks like a staffing necessity, and it is, which is exactly why its pull on the rules goes unnamed.

## Yearbook

Lived by [the alumnus](persona_the_alumnus.md) through [revolving](process_revolving.md), riding on [the dependence](piece_the_dependence.md) that made the trade's people indispensable.
It is the alignment made of careers: the Revolving Door deepens [the cultural capture](piece_the_cultural_capture.md), because a regulator who will rejoin the industry already half belongs to it.
