# Plot: The Revolving Door

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The revolving door](place_the_revolving_door.md), turning both ways.
The experts the agency needs come from the industry and return to it, and the next job is always on the far side.

## Action

[Revolving](process_revolving.md): [The Alumnus](persona_the_alumnus.md), holding [the revolver's seat](position_revolver.md), crosses from firm to agency and back, regulating today the firms that may employ it tomorrow, and softening toward the hand that will pay next without ever naming the trade.

## Stage

[The Alumnus](persona_the_alumnus.md) tables [Walk Through the Door](plan_walk_through_the_door.md): carry the expertise across, serve the public now and the sector later, and keep the knowledge flowing.
So [the revolving door](piece_the_revolving_door.md) aligns the regulator's future with the regulated, and a regulator already half returned to the industry deepens [the cultural capture](piece_the_cultural_capture.md).

## Tension

No line is crossed, and the rules bend anyway.
There is no deal and no bribe, only the shape of a career whose next chapter the industry will write, and that shape is enough: a regulator whose future employer is the sector does not judge it as a stranger would. The door looks like the only way to staff the work, and it is, which is exactly why its pull goes unnamed.
