# Persona: The Alumnus

## Taxonomy

[Revolver](position_revolver.md). The one whose career runs from the industry to the agency and back.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable, credentialled, moving easily between the two worlds.
Learned the trade in the industry, joined the agency to serve, will return to the industry to earn.
Sees the door as where the expertise naturally lives.

## Action

Crosses from firm to agency and back, carrying the industry's people and its frame.
Regulates today the firms that may employ it tomorrow.
Softens toward the hand that will pay next, without ever naming the trade.

## Shadow

Its future employer is the industry it now judges.
No line is crossed and no deal is struck, yet the mere shape of the career bends the rules, because a regulator whose next job is in the sector does not regulate that sector as a stranger would.
The door aligns the agency's interest with the industry's, quietly and legally.

## Tell

"Who else would have the expertise to do this job?"
The question that keeps the door turning and the regulator on the industry's side of it.
