# Place: The Revolving Door

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The threshold between agency and industry, turning both ways.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A door that opens from the firm into the agency and from the agency back into the firm.
The same people passing through it, each way, carrying credentials and contacts and a frame.

## Holds

The alignment, made of careers.
Here the regulator's future and the regulated's interest are quietly joined, because the next job is on the far side of the door, and the door never stops turning.

## Offers

Expertise the agency could get nowhere else.
The only people who understand the trade well enough to regulate it are the people who worked in it and will again, so the door is where the competence lives.

## Withheld

A regulator who owes the industry nothing.
No one passes through without a past employer and a likely next one, so the agency is staffed by people the industry has hired or may hire, and independence is the one thing the door cannot supply.
