# Piece: The Mandate

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The founding charge: an agency created to guard the public against the industry, given the charter and the power.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the agency](place_the_agency.md), written on the wall and meant in full.
The public-interest charter under which the watchdog takes its post.

## Load Bearing

Without it the capture would be only a firm's influence, not a betrayal.
With it the agency begins in good faith and on the public's side, so what follows is a change of allegiance and not a lack of one: the mandate is the height from which the fall is measured. The Mandate is load bearing because it is the crusading youth of the agency's life cycle (Bernstein), the honest starting state that makes the drift toward the industry a capture rather than a founding, and only a body that truly began as the public's can come, in time, to be the industry's.

## Apparent

A guarantee that the public is protected.
It reads as the whole story: the agency exists, the charge is public, the power is real, so surely the interest served is the one named. The mandate looks like the outcome when it is only the intent.

## Yearbook

Taken up by [the watchdog](persona_the_watchdog.md) at its founding, the charge that opens the play.
It is where the arc begins: the Mandate is what [the served industry](piece_the_served_industry.md) quietly displaces, the public interest present in the charter and absent from the room.
