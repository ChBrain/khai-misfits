# Process: Lobbying

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The concentrated interest showing up, with facts true and framed to suit it, to fill the room the public leaves empty.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A rule worth a livelihood, and a hearing anyone may attend.
The stake concentrated enough to be worth every hearing, and a public too diffuse to send anyone to any.

## Direction

The rule bent toward the industry that showed up to shape it.
Not by falsehood but by presence: the only evidence on the record, the only frame in the room.

## Lever

A stake worth fighting for against a stake too thin to feel.
The industry can afford the counsel, the data, and the days, and no citizen can afford them for a sliver of harm, so presence wins by simply being the only presence.

## Echo

A rule written from one side's testimony.
The public interest unopposed and so unrepresented, and a record that looks complete because every voice that came was heard.
