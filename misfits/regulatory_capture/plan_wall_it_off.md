# Plan: Wall It Off

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), inside [Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Reformer](position_reformer.md)

## Direction

Put distance between the regulator and the regulated on purpose: independent funding, a cooling-off on the door, an advocate seated for the diffuse public, facts drawn from beyond the industry. End the closeness that captured the agency, knowing the closeness was also what made it expert.

## Orders

([The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) walls the agency off and restores the independence the capture fed on.)

## Implementation

([The Firewall](place_the_firewall.md); the [insulating](process_insulating.md) process; [the regulatory capture](piece_the_regulatory_capture.md) is answered by trading access for distance, at the cost of the industry's expertise and ease.)

## Targets

- [ ] the agency is funded and staffed apart from the industry
- [ ] the door is cooled and an advocate seated for the public
- [ ] the agency can hear the public interest again
- [ ] the independence holds, unless it is judged to hobble expert oversight and repealed
