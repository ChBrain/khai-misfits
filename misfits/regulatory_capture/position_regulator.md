# Position: Regulator

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The seat set over the industry to guard the public.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A public-interest mandate and the power to make rules.
A trade to oversee that it cannot fully see without the trade's own help.
A staff and a body of facts drawn largely from the industry.

## Orders

Regulate the industry in the interest of the public.
Hear the evidence and write the rules that protect the many from the few.
Use the expertise available, which is the industry's.

## Loses

Its independence, by depending.
It cannot regulate blind, so it leans on the regulated for facts, models, and staff, and hears one frame for so long that the frame becomes its own.
Captured by the closeness that makes it expert, not by any bribe.

## Drives

Toward expert oversight,
which requires the industry's knowledge,
and delivers the industry's interest wearing the public's name.
