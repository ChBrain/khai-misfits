# Piece: The Served Industry

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The captured outcome: rules written with the industry, then for it, then against new entrants, the public interest displaced.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the hearing](place_the_hearing.md), on the record and in the rule that leaves it.
Where the only testimony given becomes the rule for everyone.

## Load Bearing

Without it the capture would be a mood in the agency with no mark on the world.
With it the drift shows up as law: the rule is drafted with the sector, tuned to its interest, and set to keep newcomers out, so the agency's charge is not repealed but inverted in practice. The Served Industry is load bearing because it is the finding itself (Stigler), regulation operated primarily for the benefit of the regulated, weighted by whose pressure the agency actually feels (Peltzman), the outcome that proves the capture real and not merely felt.

## Apparent

Sound, expert regulation of a complex field.
The rules are informed, workable, and defended by the people who know the trade, so the outcome looks like good oversight, and its tilt toward the industry reads as the natural shape of getting the details right.
