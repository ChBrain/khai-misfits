# Plot: The Dependence

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The agency](place_the_agency.md), and a trade too complex to oversee from outside.
The rules must be written, and the facts, the models, and the expert staff to write them exist in only one place.

## Action

[Depending](process_depending.md): [The Watchdog](persona_the_watchdog.md), holding [the regulator's seat](position_regulator.md), takes the industry's data and people because there are no others, while [The Lobby](persona_the_lobby.md), holding [the industry's seat](position_industry.md), supplies them freely and frames the interest as the informed, practical position.

## Stage

[The Watchdog](persona_the_watchdog.md) tables [Guard the Public](plan_guard_the_public.md): regulate well, using the best expertise available, which is the trade's own.
[The Lobby](persona_the_lobby.md) tables [Work the Agency](plan_work_the_agency.md), so [the dependence](piece_the_dependence.md) carries the industry's frame in with its facts, and hearing one worldview for years hardens it into [the cultural capture](piece_the_cultural_capture.md).

## Tension

Doing the job well is the very act that turns the agency.
There is no lapse to point to: leaning on the people who know the work is diligence, and the frame arrives free with the data, so the watchdog is briefed, sincerely and expertly, into the industry's view of the world. It comes to hold that view as plain realism, and would be offended to be told whose interest it now serves.
