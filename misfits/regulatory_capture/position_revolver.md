# Position: Revolver

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The seat whose career runs between the agency and the industry.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Expertise learned in the industry and carried into the agency.
A future that runs back out into the firms it now regulates.
The credential that makes it the obvious hire on both sides of the door.

## Orders

Bring the industry's knowledge into the agency and the agency's into the industry.
Serve the public now and the sector later, as the career naturally runs.
Keep the expertise flowing through the door.

## Loses

The stranger's distance that honest oversight needs.
A regulator whose next employer is the regulated cannot judge it as an outsider, so the door bends the rules toward the paying hand with no line ever crossed.
Aligns the agency's careers with the industry's interest, legally.

## Drives

Toward a career that spans both sides,
where the expertise lives and the loyalty follows it,
and the regulator is never quite a stranger to the regulated.
