# Piece: The Concentrated Interest

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The asymmetry that decides the room: a stake worth fighting for on one side, a stake too thin to feel on the other.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the hearing](place_the_hearing.md), in the filled chairs and the empty ones.
The industry present at every one, the public at none.

## Load Bearing

Without it the public would simply outvote the firm, being so many more.
With it the many cannot organise and the few need not be many: a large group with a sliver each stays home while a small group with everything at stake shows up, so numbers lose to concentration. The Concentrated Interest is load bearing because it is the logic of collective action (Olson), the reason presence tilts before a word is spoken, and the whole capture rests on a public that is right not to come and an industry that cannot afford to stay away.

## Apparent

An open and fair proceeding.
Anyone may attend and be heard, so the process looks impartial, and the tilt hides inside the fairness: the door is genuinely open, and only one interest can afford to walk through it.

## Yearbook

Worked by [the lobby](persona_the_lobby.md) through [lobbying](process_lobbying.md), suffered by [the public](persona_the_public.md) in its rational absence.
It is the engine under the room: the Concentrated Interest is what turns the hearing toward [the served industry](piece_the_served_industry.md), presence bought by the one side that can pay for it.
