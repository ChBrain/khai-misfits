# Place: The Firewall

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The distance built on purpose between the agency and the trade.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Independent funding, a cooling-off period on the door, an advocate seated for the diffuse public, facts drawn from beyond the industry.
The closeness cut, deliberately, where the capture fed on it.

## Holds

The recovered independence, and its cost.
Here the agency is walled off far enough to hear the public again, and far enough that the industry's expertise, cooperation, and ease no longer flow in freely.

## Offers

An agency that answers to the public once more.
With the door cooled and the funding severed, the regulator can weigh a diffuse interest that finally has a chair, and rule against the trade without ruling against its own next employer.

## Withheld

The ease that proximity bought.
The firewall spends the very access that made the agency expert, so oversight grows more independent and more estranged at once, and the distance is always one sharp flare from being judged a hobble and torn down.
