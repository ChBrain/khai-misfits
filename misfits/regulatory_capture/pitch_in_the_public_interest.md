# Pitch: In the Public Interest

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Dutiful, expert, public-minded.
The steady voice of an agency doing careful oversight, working hand in glove with the sector it knows best.
Spoken by people who came to serve the public and believe they still do.

## Undertow

Under the duty, a room with only one side in it.
The industry brings the data, the drafts, the experts, and the next job; the public brings a sliver of stake spread too thin to show up, so the agency hears one voice for years and comes to call it reality.
Nobody is bribed: the watchdog simply learns the world from the ones it watches.

## Nerve

The feeling of expert, responsible oversight,
each deference to the industry's knowledge sensible on its own,
the public interest quietly unrepresented because no one is paid to carry it into the room.

## Echo

After the run: rules written with the industry and then for it,
a door the agency's careers revolve through into the firms they judged,
and a watchdog that guards the industry against the public and calls it the public interest.
