# Plot: The Firewall

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The firewall](place_the_firewall.md), where someone has seen that the closeness is the capture.
The move is to wall the agency off, fund it apart, seat an advocate for the diffuse public, and cool the door.

## Action

[Insulating](process_insulating.md): [The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md), holding [the reformer's seat](position_reformer.md), builds the distance on purpose, severing the agency from the trade far enough to hear the public again, and far enough to lose the trade's expertise and ease.

## Stage

[The Reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) tables [Wall It Off](plan_wall_it_off.md): independent funding, a cooled door, an advocate for the diffuse, facts from beyond the sector, so [the regulatory capture](piece_the_regulatory_capture.md) is answered by trading access for distance.
The cure spends the very closeness that made the agency expert, so oversight grows more independent and more estranged at once.

## Tension

The one fix that frees the agency is the one that hobbles it.
Every wall against the industry is also a wall against the industry's data, cooperation, and competence, so the reformer buys the public's voice with the agency's ease, and the distance holds ... or one sharp flare proves the agency now too slow and too blind, the firewall is judged a hobble and torn down, and the room fills with the industry once more.
