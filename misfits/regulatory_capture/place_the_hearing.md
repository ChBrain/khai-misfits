# Place: The Hearing

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The room where the rule is decided, and only one side attends.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A docket, a table, chairs for every interest the rule will touch.
The industry's chairs filled with counsel and data; the public's chairs empty, as they are every time.

## Holds

The asymmetry, staged as a room.
One interest is concentrated enough to send its people to every hearing, and one is too diffuse to send anyone to any, so presence itself decides before a word is spoken.

## Offers

An open door and a fair procedure.
Anyone may come and be heard, which is precisely why the outcome tilts: the openness is real, and only the industry can afford to walk through it.

## Withheld

The voice of the diffuse.
The public's chair is available, and empty, so the record shows the industry's evidence unopposed, and the rule is written from the only testimony given.
