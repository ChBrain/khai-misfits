# Persona: The Lobby

## Taxonomy

[Industry](position_industry.md). The concentrated interest that shows up, every time, with everything.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Organised, informed, always present.
Has its whole livelihood in the rules and the expertise to shape them.
Frames its interest as the practical, expert, get-it-right position, and half believes it.

## Action

Brings the data, the models, the draft rules, and the staff, because it alone has them.
Attends every hearing, funds the research, supplies the experts, offers the next job.
Fills the room the public leaves empty.

## Shadow

Its help is capture wearing the face of expertise.
Every fact it supplies is true and framed to suit it, and by being the only voice with the resources to be heard, it writes the rules simply by being the only one in the room.
Does not corrupt the agency: it just never leaves it alone.

## Tell

"We are the ones who actually understand how this works."
Said truly, and used to be the only understanding the agency hears.
