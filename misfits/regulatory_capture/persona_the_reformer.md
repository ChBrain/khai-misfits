# Persona: The Reformer

## Taxonomy

[Reformer](position_reformer.md). The one who walls the agency off from the trade it judges.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Structural, a little cold to the expert cosiness, focused on independence.
Sees that closeness, not corruption, is what captured the agency, and moves to end the closeness.
Willing to trade access for distance.

## Action

Walls the agency off: independent funding, a cooling-off on the door, an advocate seated for the diffuse public, sources beyond the industry.
Puts distance between the regulator and the regulated on purpose.
Restores the independence the capture fed on.

## Shadow

Its firewall spends the very expertise it distrusts.
The industry was the source of the facts, the staff, and the cooperation, so walling it off makes the agency slower, poorer, and less expert, and the reform lands as hobbling capable oversight for the sake of suspicion.
Buys independence by giving up the closeness that made the agency good at the work.

## Tell

"The regulator should not be able to take a job at the firms it regulates."
The one rule the whole revolving expertise is arranged to call unworkable.
