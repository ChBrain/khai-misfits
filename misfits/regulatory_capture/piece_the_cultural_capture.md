# Piece: The Cultural Capture

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The change of worldview: the agency adopting the industry's assumptions through identity, status, and relationship, with no quid pro quo.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the agency](place_the_agency.md), in the frame everyone now shares and no one recalls importing.
Where the industry's view of the world has become simply the view.

## Load Bearing

Without it capture would need a bribe, and could be caught by watching the money.
With it there is nothing to catch: no payment changes hands, only a mind changes, as years of hearing one side make its frame feel like plain realism and its people feel like the reasonable ones. The Cultural Capture is load bearing because it is the mechanism Kwak names, capture by identity and relationship rather than exchange, the reason a wholly honest regulator can serve the industry sincerely, and it is what makes the misfit proximity and not corruption.

## Apparent

Simple good sense, and getting along with the people you work with.
Sharing the industry's assumptions looks like understanding the field, and warm relations with the sector look like professionalism, so the captured worldview wears the face of expertise and collegiality both.

## Yearbook

Lived by [the watchdog](persona_the_watchdog.md), deepened by [the alumnus](persona_the_alumnus.md), grown out of [the dependence](piece_the_dependence.md) and [the revolving door](piece_the_revolving_door.md).
It is the capture completed on the inside: the Cultural Capture is where the agency stops importing the industry's frame and starts holding it as its own, the quiet turn that yields [the served industry](piece_the_served_industry.md).
