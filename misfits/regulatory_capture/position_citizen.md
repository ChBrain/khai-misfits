# Position: Citizen

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The seat that holds the diffuse public interest, and cannot fill it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A sliver of the stake, spread across millions.
A real interest in the rules, too thin per head to act on.
No organisation, because none is worth any one citizen's while to build.

## Orders

Be protected by the agency, in principle.
Object if the harm ever grows large enough for one citizen to feel.
Trust that someone is carrying the public interest into the room.

## Loses

Its own representation.
Each citizen's share of the harm is worth less than a day of the fight, so no one shows up, and the interest the agency was built to serve is present nowhere in the agency.
Absent not from apathy but from arithmetic.

## Drives

Toward a rational staying-home,
each sliver too thin to fight for,
so the public's chair sits empty and the industry's does not.
