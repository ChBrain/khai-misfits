# Process: Depending

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The agency regulating a trade it cannot fully see, on the trade's own facts and staff.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A trade too complex to oversee from outside, and one source that holds the facts.
The agency needing data, models, and expert hands, and the industry being the only place they exist.

## Direction

The industry's frame heard for so long it becomes the agency's.
The reliance on the regulated for what oversight requires, until the source of the facts is also the source of the worldview.

## Lever

Information the regulator cannot get except from the regulated.
The asymmetry is structural: the agency must know the trade to judge it, and only the trade can teach it, so dependence is the price of competence.

## Echo

An agency that sees the world the way the industry does.
The facts and the frame both imported from the regulated, and a watchdog briefed into the very view it was meant to check.
