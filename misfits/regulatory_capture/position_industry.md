# Position: Industry

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The seat with everything at stake in the rules and the means to shape them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A concentrated stake in every rule and the resources to pursue it.
The expertise, the data, and the staff the agency needs.
The next jobs the agency's experts will take.

## Orders

Show up to every hearing with the facts and the drafts.
Supply the expertise, fund the research, and frame the interest as the practical, get-it-right position.
Be the one voice in the room, by being the only one who can afford to be.

## Loses

Nothing, and that is the asymmetry.
Its costs of shaping the rules are small against its stake, so it organises where the public cannot, and captures the agency simply by never leaving it alone with anyone else.
Wins not by corruption but by presence.

## Drives

Toward rules written with it, then for it,
raised as barriers against new entrants,
and defended, sincerely, as the informed and expert position.
