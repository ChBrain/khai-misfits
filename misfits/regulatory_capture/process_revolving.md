# Process: Revolving

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The expertise crossing from industry to agency and back, aligning the regulator's future with the regulated.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An agency that needs people who know the trade, and a trade that is where they come from and go.
The careers of the agency's experts running back into the industry it oversees.

## Direction

The rule softened toward the hand that will pay next.
Not by any deal, but by the shape of a career whose next chapter is written by the regulated.

## Lever

The next job on the far side of the door.
A regulator whose future employer is the sector does not judge it as a stranger would, so the door bends the rules with no line ever crossed.

## Echo

An agency whose interest is joined to the industry's.
The regulator serving the firms that may hire it, quietly and legally, and the door still turning for the next one through.
