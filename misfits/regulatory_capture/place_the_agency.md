# Place: The Agency

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The body set over the industry, and slowly at home in it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Offices, a mandate on the wall, staff who know the trade cold.
A watchdog at its post, dutiful and expert, working closely with the sector it was built to oversee.

## Holds

The dependence, thickening with every briefing.
Here the trade's facts, models, and people become the agency's own, until the frame in the room is the industry's frame and no one recalls importing it.

## Offers

Expert oversight, at the price of proximity.
The closeness that lets the agency understand the work is the same closeness that lets the work understand the agency, and only the first is ever named.

## Withheld

A view from outside the industry.
The agency sees the trade only through the trade's eyes, so the public interest it was charged with is present in the mandate and absent from the room.
