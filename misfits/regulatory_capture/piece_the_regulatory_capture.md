# Piece: The Regulatory Capture

## Taxonomy

[Regulatory Capture](play_regulatory_capture.md). The phenomenon whole: the guardian come to serve the guarded, and its remedy in independence bought at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the firewall](place_the_firewall.md), where the capture is named and answered.
And across the whole agency, in the sum of every closeness that fed it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of mechanisms with no shape.
With it the mechanisms become one thing: the concentrated interest, the dependence, the revolving door, and the cultural capture are facets of a single turn, a watchdog captured by proximity and not corruption, and the only cure is structural, a firewall that spends the very expertise, cooperation, and ease the closeness supplied. The Regulatory Capture is load bearing because it is the whole finding (Stigler) with its whole price attached: it holds the misfit together, and it holds that the fix is independence traded for access, virtue never being the missing part.

## Apparent

Either a scandal of corrupt officials, or nothing at all.
Seen from outside it looks like graft to be prosecuted or a conspiracy to be exposed, and finding neither, the observer concludes there is no problem, missing that the capture needs no crime and leaves no culprit, which is precisely what makes it hard to see and harder to undo.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the reformer](persona_the_reformer.md) through [insulating](process_insulating.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: the Regulatory Capture is what [the mandate](piece_the_mandate.md) becomes when the agency is left alone with the trade, undone only when someone builds [the firewall](place_the_firewall.md) and pays for the distance in expertise.
