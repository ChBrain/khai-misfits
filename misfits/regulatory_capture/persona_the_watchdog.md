# Persona: The Watchdog

## Taxonomy

[Regulator](position_regulator.md). The agency set over the industry, and slowly at home in it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Dutiful, expert, sure it serves the public.
Founded to guard against the industry and proud of the charge.
Believes close work with the sector is just how good oversight is done.

## Action

Regulates the trade using the trade's own facts and people.
Hears the industry at every hearing and the public at none, and takes the informed voice as the true one.
Writes the rules with the sector it judges, and calls the closeness expertise.

## Shadow

It is captured by proximity, not corruption.
Hearing one frame for years, staffed and briefed by the regulated, it comes to hold the industry's worldview as plain realism, and would be genuinely offended to be told whose interest it now serves.
Guards the industry against the public, sincerely, in the public's name.

## Tell

"We work closely with the industry to get the details right."
True, and the sentence under which the watchdog changes sides.
