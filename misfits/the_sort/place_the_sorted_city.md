# Place: The Sorted City

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md). The segregated end, a whole map of blocks each gone to one kind, the aggregate the local tips compose, matching no one's wish.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A city drawn in clean lines: this quarter one kind, that quarter the other, the boundaries sharp and the blocks uniform.
Shown as if designed, a separation so orderly it looks intended, the map reading like the product of a policy no one wrote.

## Holds

Holds the emergent sort, the stark macro-pattern summed from a thousand tolerant moves.
The Sorted City is where the local reads compose: every block having tipped, the whole has sorted into near-total separation, an aggregate far more extreme than any resident's mild preference and held by nobody's design.

## Offers

Offers each resident a block above their line, and the city a stable, sorted rest.
Here everyone is now comfortably among their own, every preference met, so the separation is self-maintaining, no one wishing to move and the map at a durable equilibrium that no free choice will disturb.

## Withheld

Withholds the author, because there is none.
What the Sorted City hides is that its designed-looking order was drawn by no one: the line so clean it seems chosen is the sum of moves none of which chose it, so the map that invites the search for a policy or a bigot behind it yields neither, only the aggregate of tolerant wishes that a mixed street would each have met.
