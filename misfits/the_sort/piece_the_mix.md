# Piece: The Mix

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md). The local composition each resident reads and each move shifts, the state that drifts smoothly until it crosses a threshold.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Read at [the address](position_the_address.md) and altered at [the vacancy](position_the_vacancy.md), the composition of a single block as it stands moment to moment.
Flows with every move, thinned by each departure and tilted by each arrival, the running state that couples every resident's choice to every neighbour's read.

## Load Bearing

Without the Mix there is nothing to read and nothing to shift: it is the state the whole dynamic runs on, the variable every move changes and every preference judges.
With it the model has a medium: the Mix is the local share of each kind (Sakoda; Schelling), a composition that drifts continuously as households come and go and that each resident measures against the threshold in their wish. The Mix is load bearing because it is the coupling, the shared state through which one resident's private move becomes another's changed circumstance, so a departure is not an isolated act but a lowering of the Mix that the next read will weigh, and the piece carries the connective tissue of the trap, the running composition that carries each move's effect into the next resident's decision.

## Apparent

Just who lives on the block, the ordinary makeup of a neighbourhood at any given time.
Seen at the surface, the Mix looks like a fact with no force, a snapshot of residents, and the truth that it is the channel through which the sort propagates, that each move rewrites it for everyone near, that a drift in this one number is what carries a mild wish into a segregated map, stays hidden behind how plainly it is just who happens to live there now.

## Yearbook

Read by [the Neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md), shifted by [the Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md), and watched from above by [the Integrationist](persona_the_integrationist.md) who would hold it steady, the running state the sort moves through.
It is the medium of the trap: the Mix is what [reading](process_reading.md) measures and [moving](process_moving.md) alters, the composition weighed against [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) inside [the preference](piece_the_preference.md), whose drift across that line is [the tipping](piece_the_tipping.md).
