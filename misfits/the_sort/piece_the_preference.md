# Piece: The Preference

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md). The mild, tolerant wish each resident holds, the reluctance to be too small a minority that a mixed street satisfies for all at once.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Held at [the address](position_the_address.md), where each resident reads their block, and satisfied on [the mixed street](place_the_mixed_street.md), where the composition sits above every line.
Flows from every resident alike, the same modest wish repeated across the city, tolerant enough that a mixed neighbourhood meets it and lawful enough that no one can be faulted for holding it.

## Load Bearing

Without the Preference there is no engine: it is what every move answers, and its mildness is the whole point of the trap.
With it the sort has a cause that is no one's fault: the Preference is the wish not to be too small a minority (Schelling; Clark), a moderate, empirically real taste that a wide range of mixes satisfies, so no resident prefers segregation and yet each will move to keep from dropping too few. The Preference is load bearing because its very tolerance is what makes the sort inexplicable as bigotry, a wish a mixed street would meet held by everyone and still, through the threshold inside it, composing into separation, so the piece carries the innocent half of the misfit, the modest preference that is the whole of what anyone wants and more than enough to sort a city.

## Apparent

Just a mild wish, the ordinary reluctance to be the last of one's kind on the street.
Seen at the surface, the Preference looks like tolerance itself, a taste so moderate a mixed neighbourhood satisfies it, and the truth that it carries a threshold, that the same mild wish held by all sorts the whole once a line is crossed, that a preference no mixed street offends still produces a segregated map, stays hidden behind how reasonable and how blameless the wish plainly is.

## Yearbook

Held by [the Neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md), shared by [the Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md), and unarguable to [the Integrationist](persona_the_integrationist.md) who cannot talk anyone out of it, the modest wish the whole sort runs on.
It is the innocent root of the trap: the Preference is what [reading](process_reading.md) measures and [moving](process_moving.md) answers, the mild taste with [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) inside it, read against [the mix](piece_the_mix.md) and composing, through it, into [the tipping](piece_the_tipping.md).
