# Position: The Overview

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md). The whole-map seat, the vantage from which the sort is visible and from which no single move can be reached except by constraint.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Sight of the whole, and no hand on the parts.
A view no resident occupies: the seat sees the city sorting from a height, reads the pattern the local reads compose, and knows the drift before any block completes.
No lever on the individual move, since the seat commands no resident's choice and can touch the moves that make the sort only by ruling them, not by seeing them.

## Orders

Hold the mix.
Watch the composition of each block and keep it off the threshold, because a mix held below the tipping line stays mixed and a mix let past it sorts.
Reach for the only instrument that works, since persuasion moves no one who is doing no wrong, so holding the mix means a rule on where residents may settle, a constraint applied from the height at which the sort is seen.

## Loses

The one thing sight was supposed to buy: the power to act on what is seen without coercing the seen.
The Overview perceives the aggregate that no address can, but the aggregate is made of lawful moves it cannot argue away, so its knowledge yields no gentle lever, only the choice between constraint and spectatorship.
What the seat forfeits is innocent efficacy: able to see the sort and unable to stop it except by ruling the blameless, the vantage that understands the pattern best is the one whose only remedy is the one a free city refuses, so to know the harm is not to be able to prevent it cleanly.

## Drives

Toward a pattern seen whole and unreachable clean,
and a remedy that is all constraint or nothing,
so the seat that alone perceives the sort is the one left choosing between coercing free residents and watching the city divide.
