# Piece: The Threshold

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md). The tip point hidden inside the preference, the like-share below which content turns to departure and a block begins to flip.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Lives inside [the preference](piece_the_preference.md) at every [address](position_the_address.md), and does its work at [the turning block](place_the_turning_block.md), where a mix first crosses it.
Flows unseen beneath the tolerance, a line each resident carries without knowing it shared, crossed silently and decisively when the local mix drifts below it.

## Load Bearing

Without the Threshold the preference would be a smooth dial and the mix would drift and recover, no tipping and no sort.
With it the mild wish becomes a cliff: the Threshold is the share of like neighbours below which a resident moves (Grodzins; Granovetter), so a smooth change in composition produces a discontinuous change in behaviour, content one side of the line and departure the other. The Threshold is load bearing because it is what turns aggregation into tipping, the sharp trigger that makes a small nudge decisive and a mixed block unable to hold once nudged, so the piece carries the discontinuity of the trap, the reason a tolerant preference does not average out into a stable mix but tips, past a point, into a rout.

## Apparent

Just a comfort level, the reasonable point at which anyone might prefer to live elsewhere.
Seen at the surface, the Threshold looks like ordinary preference expressed as a number, a line no more troubling than the wish that holds it, and the truth that it is a cliff not a slope, that crossing it flips a block rather than nudging it, that a smooth spread of such lines yields a discontinuous sort, stays hidden behind how modest the comfort level itself appears.

## Yearbook

Carried unknowingly by [the Neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md) and by [the Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) alike, and the one thing [the Integrationist](persona_the_integrationist.md) must keep a mix clear of, the hidden line the whole tips on.
It is the trigger of the trap: the Threshold is what [tipping](process_tipping.md) crosses, the cliff inside [the preference](piece_the_preference.md) that [the mix](piece_the_mix.md) drifts below, turning a smooth taste into [the tipping](piece_the_tipping.md) that sorts the block and, block by block, the city.
