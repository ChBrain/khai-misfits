# Plan: Hold the Mix

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Overview](position_the_overview.md), inside [The Sort](play_the_sort.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Overview](position_the_overview.md)

## Direction

Watch each block's composition from the height at which the sort is visible, and keep it off the threshold, because a mix held below the tipping line stays mixed and a mix let past it sorts. Reach, then, for the only lever that holds, since no resident can be argued out of a lawful and mild preference: a rule on where residents may settle, a constraint applied to blameless choice for the sake of the mixed street it alone preserves.

([The Integrationist](persona_the_integrationist.md) would keep each block's mix below the tipping line and finds that the only working instrument is a limit on the free move of every resident.)
