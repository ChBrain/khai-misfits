# Plot: The Turn

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Turning Block](place_the_turning_block.md), a block sitting right at its tip point.
A vacancy open in a street that has already thinned, the mix poised on the line from which it will not return.

## Action

[Tipping](process_tipping.md): the Newcomer settles the vacancy where they will not be too few, and the block crosses its threshold and cascades.

## Stage

[The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md), on [Settle Where I Fit](plan_settle_where_i_fit.md), fills the vacancy where [the mix](piece_the_mix.md) already suits them, pushing it past [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) so the block empties of one kind and fills with the other into [the tipping](piece_the_tipping.md).
The fit is genuine and the arrival intends nothing, and the block tips the rest of the way and sets.

## Tension

Settling where you will not be too few is exactly what anyone looking for a home does, and it sets the block the last of the way over.
There is no design: the Newcomer weighs only their own comfort, takes the open spot that fits them, and would as gladly have chosen a mixed street had one fit them as well, so the arrival is as blameless as any. But the vacancies a thinning block sheds fit the kind it is tilting toward, so each honest arrival deepens the tilt, and the plot closes with the block tipped and set, sorted, no one in it having willed the change, the openness of the vacancy indistinguishable from a welcome and the flip complete.
