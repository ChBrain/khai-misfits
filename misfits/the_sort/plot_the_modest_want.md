# Plot: The Modest Want

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Mixed Street](place_the_mixed_street.md), a block of both kinds where everyone is content.
A neighbourhood that satisfies each resident at once, no one the last of their own and no one wishing to move.

## Action

[Reading](process_reading.md): each resident measures the mix around them and finds their like-share comfortably above their line.

## Stage

[The Neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md), on [Leave When Few](plan_leave_when_few.md), reads [the mix](piece_the_mix.md) against [the preference](piece_the_preference.md) and is content, wanting only not to be too small a minority and finding the mixed street meets the wish.
The preference is mild and the street satisfies it, and nothing in the moment shows the line hidden inside the wish.

## Tension

Wanting only not to be the last of one's kind is the most tolerant of preferences, and the most tolerant preference is the whole engine of the sort.
There is no bigotry: the Neighbour seeks no separation, holds no ill will, and would live on a mixed street indefinitely so long as they are not reduced too few, so the wish is blameless and, here, entirely met. But the wish has a threshold in it, and the street stays mixed only while the mix sits above every line, so the plot closes with the block content and integrated and one move from a slope, the tolerant preference established as exactly the thing that, held by all, will sort the city once a threshold is crossed.
