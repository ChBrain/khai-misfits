# Process: Reading

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md). The local evaluation, each resident measuring the mix around them against the threshold inside their preference.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md), at [the Mixed Street](place_the_mixed_street.md), measuring their own block against their comfort line.
Begins whenever a resident takes stock of who lives near them and asks whether their like-share still sits above the share at which they would rather be elsewhere.

## Direction

Turns a mild preference into a verdict on the here and now.
The resident compares the local mix to the threshold in their wish and returns one of two answers, content or too few, so reading converts a standing tolerance into a live decision about this block at this moment, the whole of the judgement made on what is near.

## Lever

The locality of the view.
Because a resident can see only their own block, reading weighs the near and nothing else, so the verdict is accurate about the immediate mix and blind to the map, its lever the plain limit of a vantage that reaches no further than the street.

## Echo

A block full of correct local reads, and no eye on the whole.
The echo of reading is a neighbourhood of residents each judging truly at close range, so every move that follows is well-founded on its own block, and the pattern those true reads compose is one no reader ever measured, the sort assembled from a thousand accurate local verdicts.
