# Piece: The Tipping

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md). The phenomenon whole, the cascade by which mild preferences compose, through a threshold, into a stark and sorted macro-segregation no one chose.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across [the mixed street](place_the_mixed_street.md), [the turning block](place_the_turning_block.md), and [the sorted city](place_the_sorted_city.md) as the whole arc, the mix satisfied, the block flipping, and the map set.
Flows through the entire play, from the tolerant wish that a mixed street meets to the segregated city it composes, the dynamic seen from end to end.

## Load Bearing

Without it there are only parts: a preference, a threshold, a mix, with no name for the motion that carries a mild wish into a hard map.
With it the parts become one dynamic: tolerant preferences read against a shared mix cross a hidden threshold and cascade, block by block, into a stark separation. The Tipping is load bearing because it is the phenomenon entire (Schelling; Granovetter): the aggregation of weak, same-kind preferences, through a tipping threshold, into a discontinuous, sorted macro-state, the micro-motive nothing like the macro-behaviour it produces, so the piece carries the whole trap, the passage from a mixed street everyone is content with to a segregated city no one chose, made by the sum of choices each of which a mixed street would have satisfied.

## Apparent

Just a neighbourhood changing, the ordinary turnover of who lives where.
Seen at the surface, the Tipping looks like the sum of individual moves, people going where they please, and the truth that it is a discontinuity, that mild wishes compose into an intolerant map, that the sorted city matches no preference that made it and reverses no more easily than it formed, stays hidden behind how much a sorting neighbourhood looks like people simply choosing homes.
