# Position: The Address

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md). The local seat, a single fixed spot that reads only its own neighbours and can act only by staying put or moving on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A location, and a view that reaches no further than the block.
A purely local vantage: the seat sees the mix immediately around it and nothing of the city, so every judgement it makes is made on local information and no other.
Two levers and no more, since a fixed address can do exactly two things with its dissatisfaction, hold its place or vacate it, and nothing in between.

## Orders

Leave when few.
Read the local mix, stay while the like-share sits above the line, and vacate the spot when it falls below, because the seat has no other response to discomfort than to move.
Judge only what is near, since the address can see only its own block and is not built to weigh what its leaving does to the map.

## Loses

The whole, which the seat is structurally blind to.
An address reads its own neighbours and never the city, so it cannot see that its vacating lowers the like-share for the spot beside it, nor that a thousand such local reads compose into a sorted map, and it has no instrument for taking the aggregate into account even if it would.
What the seat forfeits is the view that would let it act otherwise: confined to a local vantage and a binary lever, the Address is a node that responds correctly to its own block and cannot register the pattern its response helps build, so the seat that reads truly at close range is the one that is blind at the scale where the harm appears.

## Drives

Toward a map made of local reads,
and a pattern no single address can see,
so the seat that judges only its own block is the one whose accurate, blinkered response composes into a sort no node ever perceived.
