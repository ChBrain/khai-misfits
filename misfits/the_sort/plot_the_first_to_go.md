# Plot: The First to Go

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Mixed Street](place_the_mixed_street.md), one household gone for reasons of its own.
A single departure, no statement in it, that leaves the block a little more one-sided than before.

## Action

[Moving](process_moving.md): the Neighbour, now just below their comfort line, vacates their spot, thinning the block and opening a vacancy.

## Stage

[The Neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md), on [Leave When Few](plan_leave_when_few.md), reads [the mix](piece_the_mix.md) after the first exit, finds it has slipped below [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) in [the preference](piece_the_preference.md), and moves.
The read is honest and the move is lawful, and the leaving lowers the like-share once more for whoever remains.

## Tension

Moving on when you have become too small a minority is a plain and blameless choice, and the plain choice thins the block for the next resident.
There is no malice: the Neighbour answers their own comfort, weighing only their block and nothing of the map, and a household that leaves may go for any reason at all. But every move is an exit that lowers the like-share behind it, so the plot closes with the block thinner by one and a vacancy open, the departing resident having pushed the next just nearer their own line, one blameless move begetting the conditions of the next.
