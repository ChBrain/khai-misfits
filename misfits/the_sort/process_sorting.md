# Process: Sorting

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md). The emergence, the local tips composing across the city into a stark, stable separation no one designed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The many local tips composing, block by block, across the whole map.
Begins as one tipped block lowers the like-share at its edges and sends the drift into the next, so the flips accumulate without any hand gathering them, the city sorting as the tips add up.

## Direction

Turns a set of local flips into a single global pattern.
Each block that tips is one tile, and the tiles compose: a sorted block makes its neighbours more one-sided, which tips them, so sorting propagates the separation across the map until the whole has resolved into quarters of one kind and quarters of the other, a macro-pattern assembled from micro-moves.

## Lever

The repetition of the same tolerant read everywhere.
Because every resident holds the same kind of mild preference and every block runs the same threshold, sorting needs no coordination to spread, only the same local rule applied across the city, its lever the uniformity of a wish held in common and acted on apart.

## Echo

A separated map at rest, matching no one's preference and holding itself.
The echo of sorting is a stable, stark segregation: every resident now comfortably among their own, so no one moves and the pattern sets, an equilibrium far more extreme than any wish that built it and durable precisely because it satisfies, at last, the mild preference of everyone it divided.
