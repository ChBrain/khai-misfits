# Plan: Leave When Few

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Address](position_the_address.md), inside [The Sort](play_the_sort.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Address](position_the_address.md)

## Direction

Read the local mix and stay while comfortable, and move on when the like-share falls below the line at which you would rather be too few no longer. Judge only the block around you, since that is all the seat can see, and answer your own comfort, not any wish about the city. The move is a private reluctance to be too small a minority, lawful and mild, made without reference to the map it helps compose.

## Orders

([The Neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md) stays while their like-share sits above their line and vacates the spot when it drops below, reading only the block and weighing nothing of the whole.)

## Implementation

([The Mixed Street](place_the_mixed_street.md); the [reading](process_reading.md) process, then [moving](process_moving.md); the Neighbour reads [the mix](piece_the_mix.md) against [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) inside [the preference](piece_the_preference.md) and leaves when it falls below, thinning the block behind them.)
