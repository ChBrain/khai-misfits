# Process: Tipping

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md). The discontinuity, a block crossing its threshold and flipping from a stable mix to a one-sided state that, once underway, settles rather than reverting.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md), at [the Turning Block](place_the_turning_block.md), filling a vacancy in a block already near its line.
Begins when enough departures have lowered a block's like-share that the next read tips a resident over, and the vacancies start filling with the other kind.

## Direction

Turns a smooth drift into a sudden flip.
Below the threshold the mix drifts and recovers, but once it crosses the line each departure pushes another past their own, and the openings fill with the kind the block is tilting toward, so tipping converts a gradual change into a discontinuous one, a mixed block becoming a sorted block with no gradual middle that holds.

## Lever

The threshold, which makes a small change decisive.
Because content turns to departure at a line rather than by degrees, tipping needs only to nudge a block just across it, and the cascade does the rest, its lever the sharp trigger hidden inside a smooth and tolerant preference.

## Echo

A block emptied of one kind and set in the other, at rest where it lands.
The echo of tipping is a segregated block where a mixed one stood, and once the moves have run out no one left wants to leave, so the tipped block sets, and the local flip becomes one more tile in a map that no single tip could show and no tip meant to draw.
