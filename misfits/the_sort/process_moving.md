# Process: Moving

## Taxonomy

[The Sort](play_the_sort.md). The relocation, an unsatisfied resident leaving one block and taking a spot in another, both thinning and filling as they go.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md), at [the Mixed Street](place_the_mixed_street.md), vacating a spot once their like-share has fallen below their line.
Begins the moment a resident's read returns too few, since the seat's only response to discomfort is to leave, and a departure is set in motion.

## Direction

Turns one resident's discomfort into two blocks' change at once.
A move is an exit and an entry together: it lowers the like-share of the block left behind and raises it in the block arrived at, so a single relocation shifts the composition in two places, nudging the residents of each toward their own thresholds.

## Lever

The coupling of every resident's mix to every other's move.
Because one household's departure is the change in another household's block, moving links the residents together, so a move made for private comfort alters the calculation of strangers, its lever the fact that no one's mix is theirs alone to hold steady.

## Echo

A departure that lowers the line for whoever remains.
The echo of moving is the next resident's read turned from content to too few: the leaver's exit thins the block behind them just enough to push a neighbour below their threshold, so one move begets the next, the relocation propagating through a street none of its movers meant to sort.
