# Plan: Settle Where I Fit

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Vacancy](position_the_vacancy.md), inside [The Sort](play_the_sort.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Vacancy](position_the_vacancy.md)

## Direction

Take the open spot where you will not be too small a minority, choosing among the vacancies the block that suits you best. Answer your own comfort, as any resident does, and intend nothing about the neighbourhood's balance. Because the openings a tipping block sheds suit the kind it is tipping toward, settling where you fit lands you where your arrival deepens the tilt, though nothing but a fit for a home was sought.

## Orders

([The Newcomer](persona_the_newcomer.md) fills a vacancy where the mix suits them, raising that block's like-share toward their own kind by the plain act of moving in.)

## Implementation

([The Turning Block](place_the_turning_block.md); the [tipping](process_tipping.md) process; the Newcomer settles a vacancy where [the mix](piece_the_mix.md) already fits their [preference](piece_the_preference.md), pushing the block past [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) the rest of the way.)

## Targets

- [ ] the vacancy taken is one where the arrival will not be too few
- [ ] the openings of a tilting block are filled by the kind it tilts toward
- [ ] the arrival raises the block's like-share by the act of settling
- [ ] no wish about the block's balance is needed to deepen its tilt
