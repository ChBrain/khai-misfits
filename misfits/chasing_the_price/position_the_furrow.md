# Position: The Furrow

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md). The production seat, where output is committed before it exists and cannot be changed until it is delivered.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An output to commit, and only one price to commit it on.
A decision fixed in time: the seat must set its quantity at planting, on the price that stands then, and has no later moment to revise it, so the whole choice is made once and lives or dies at a harvest it cannot yet see.
No sight of the delivery price, since the seat acts at the start of the lag and sells at its end, blind at commitment to the market it will meet.

## Orders

Plant to the price.
Read the going price and set output to match it, large when it is high and small when it is low, because that is the correct response to the market the seat can see.
Commit and wait, since the decision cannot be unmade once the seed is in, and the seat's only task is to answer the price in front of it as soundly as it can.

## Loses

The chance to answer the price it will actually face.
The Furrow decides at the start of the lag and delivers at the end, so it can only ever respond to the price at planting, never to the price at harvest, and by harvest that price has moved, pushed by every other seat committing the same way at the same time.
What the seat forfeits is timeliness itself: bound to act before it can know, it commits a sound answer to a question the market will have changed, so the seat that plants correctly to the price it sees is the seat that always arrives too late to the price it gets.

## Drives

Toward output fixed on a passing price,
and a delivery into a market it has moved,
so the seat that must commit before it can see is the one whose sound decision is always answered by the wrong price.
