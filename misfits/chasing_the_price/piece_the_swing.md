# Piece: The Swing

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md). The overshoot of the synchronised response, the boom-to-bust oscillation the market produces on its own.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Turns downward at [the glut](place_the_glut.md) and upward at [the dearth](place_the_dearth.md), realised each time at [the harvest](place_the_harvest.md) where the delivered crop sets the price.
Flows as the alternation of glut and dearth, the price flung past equilibrium first one way and then the other, the visible motion of the whole trap.

## Load Bearing

Without the Swing the lag would be harmless: a mistimed plan would miss the mark by a little and the market would settle.
With it the miss becomes a cycle whose amplitude can hold, grow, or shrink by how sharply supply answers price against how sharply demand does: the Swing is the overshoot the synchronised response produces, seen in the hog cycle (Hanau) and classified by those slopes (Kaldor), the price crashing under everyone's big crop and spiking under everyone's small one. The Swing is load bearing because it is the shape the trap takes, the boom and bust that a true signal read on a lag and answered in chorus produces where the slopes do not damp it, so the piece carries the outcome of the cycle, the price thrown past the mark that a well-timed market would have found.

## Apparent

Just volatility, the ordinary ups and downs of a market finding its level.
Seen at the surface, the Swing looks like noise, prices wobbling as supply and demand jostle, and the truth that it is endogenous, that the market makes the swing itself from a lag and a shared response, that the overshoot is not a shock from outside but the necessary result of everyone answering the same signal, stays hidden behind how much a self-made cycle resembles simple bad luck.

## Yearbook

Suffered by [the Grower](persona_the_grower.md) whose crop arrives into it, priced by [the Buyer](persona_the_buyer.md)'s steady demand, and leaned against by [the Steady Hand](persona_the_steady_hand.md) who plants to still it, the outcome the trap produces.
It is the shape of the trap: the Swing is what [swinging](process_swinging.md) flings past the mark, the overshoot [the delay](piece_the_delay.md) makes possible and [the buyer](persona_the_buyer.md)'s steady want prices, each extreme becoming the [price](piece_the_price.md) that [chasing](process_chasing.md) reads into [the cobweb](piece_the_cobweb.md).
