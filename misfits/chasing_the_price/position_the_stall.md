# Position: The Stall

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md). The demand seat, a steady want that prices whatever supply arrives and sets no quantity of its own.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A fixed appetite, and a price to pay it at.
A steady want: the seat needs roughly the same amount each season and adjusts not its quantity but its price, paying more when supply is short and less when it is plenty.
No hand on supply, since the seat produces nothing and orders nothing ahead, and can only meet the crop that comes with the price that clears it.

## Orders

Buy what comes.
Take the supply the growers deliver and pay the price that empties the market of it, holding the want steady while the volume swings.
Set the price, not the quantity, since the seat's only lever is what it will pay, and it pays whatever it must to clear what has arrived.

## Loses

Any power to steady the swing, which the seat can only price.
The Stall meets a variable supply with a constant demand, so it converts every glut into a low price and every dearth into a high one, and having no way to order supply ahead or hold it back, it cannot smooth the volume it receives, only translate it into price.
What the seat forfeits is influence over the cause: able to react but never to commission, it transmits the growers' overshoot faithfully into the price rather than absorbing any of it, so the seat that starts no cycle is the one that turns the cycle into the very number the growers will chase next.

## Drives

Toward a price that swings with the crop,
and a demand that only ever answers,
so the seat that wants the same each year is the one whose steadiness prices every overshoot into the signal the next planting reads.
