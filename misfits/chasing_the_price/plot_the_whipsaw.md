# Plot: The Whipsaw

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Glut](place_the_glut.md), the crashed price plain for every grower to read.
A market loudly warning whoever grew, the signal to plant small now as clear and honest as the high price was before it.

## Action

[Chasing](process_chasing.md): the Grower answers the low price with a small planting, and the short harvest that follows spikes the price, swinging the market back the other way.

## Stage

[The Grower](persona_the_grower.md), on [Plant to the Price](plan_plant_to_the_price.md), reads the crashed [price](piece_the_price.md) and plants little, so the small harvest meets the steady demand as a shortage and the price spikes into a new dearth, turning [the swing](piece_the_swing.md) into [the cobweb](piece_the_cobweb.md).
The retreat is as sound as the advance was, and it seeds the next boom exactly as the boom seeded this bust.

## Tension

Planting small into a crashed price is as correct as planting big into a high one, and the correct retreat spikes the price right back.
There is no learning that helps: each grower answers the new extreme as soundly as they answered the last, so the low price draws a small crop that becomes a dearth and the coming high price will draw a big one that becomes a glut. So the plot closes with the swing reversed and the cycle seen to feed itself, boom calling bust and bust calling boom, a market whipsawing on nothing but the repeated soundness of answering the price it is shown.
