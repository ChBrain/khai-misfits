# Plan: Buy What Comes

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Stall](position_the_stall.md), inside [Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Stall](position_the_stall.md)

## Direction

Want roughly the same amount each season and pay whatever price clears the supply that arrives, more per unit when the crop is short and less when it is plenty. Set the price, not the quantity: hold the appetite steady and let it meet the growers' variable volume, taking what comes at the rate that empties the market. Start no trend and hold nothing back, since the seat's only move is to price what is delivered.

## Orders

([The Buyer](persona_the_buyer.md) meets the delivered crop with a steady want, paying the clearing price, high when supply is short and low when it is plentiful.)

## Implementation

([The Glut](place_the_glut.md); across the [swinging](process_swinging.md) process; the Buyer's fixed demand meets the growers' volume so [the price](piece_the_price.md) clears where they cross, turning each over-planting into [the swing](piece_the_swing.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the same want is held steady across seasons
- [ ] the arriving supply is bought at the price that clears it
- [ ] the price rises when the crop is short and falls when it is plentiful
- [ ] the quantity is never set, only the price paid for what comes
