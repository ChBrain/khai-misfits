# Plot: The Long View

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Dearth](place_the_dearth.md), the high price tempting the whole market to plant big.
A grower who has seen the swing before and reads the high price as the sign of the coming glut, not the reward it looks like.

## Action

[Chasing](process_chasing.md), refused: the Steady Hand plants to the expected price rather than the current one, holding output level against the swing.

## Stage

[The Steady Hand](persona_the_steady_hand.md), on [Plant to the Forecast](plan_plant_to_the_forecast.md), plants small into the high price, forgoing [the price](piece_the_price.md) on offer to answer the one it foretells, leaning against [the cobweb](piece_the_cobweb.md) instead of feeding it.
The forecast is right over the cycle and, this season, looks like refusing money the market is plainly offering.

## Tension

Planting against the visible price is the only move that stills the swing, and it is the move that looks, in the moment, like throwing away a sure thing.
There is no proof to hand: the Steady Hand cannot show the coming glut, only forecast it, so their small planting into a high price reads as stubbornness against a market plainly saying grow. And if too few join them, the swing rolls on and they have borne the cost of looking foolish without damping it, so the plot closes with the remedy taken at the price of the visible signal, the reckoning a producer who distrusts the truest number the market shows, or a market that swings because everyone, sensibly, trusts it.
