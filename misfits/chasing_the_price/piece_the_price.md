# Piece: The Price

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md). The current market price, the true signal every producer reads and plans on, and the number the harvest will have moved.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Read at [the dearth](place_the_dearth.md) and at [the glut](place_the_glut.md), wherever the market stands when the producer must plan, and paid, changed, at [the harvest](place_the_harvest.md).
Flows from the meeting of supply and demand as a single honest number, the guide each producer answers and the thing their answer, shared, will move.

## Load Bearing

Without the Price there is nothing to read and nothing to chase: it is the signal the whole cycle turns on.
With it the producers have a true guide and a treacherous one: the Price is the current market number (Ezekiel; Nerlove), real and correct to plan on at the moment of planting, but read on a lag and answered by all at once it becomes the very thing the answer moves. The Price is load bearing because it is both the honest signal and the trap, the number that rewards a sound response and, because everyone gives the same response, punishes it a season later, so the piece carries the misleading heart of the cycle, a true price that is a good guide to the present and a false guide to the harvest it will have changed.

## Apparent

Just a signal, the plain honest number that says how much the crop is wanted.
Seen at the surface, the Price looks like the perfect guide, the market telling each producer exactly what to make, and the truth that it is read too soon, that a signal answered by everyone at once destroys itself, that the number rewarding a big planting is the number that big planting will crash, stays hidden behind how obviously right it is to plan on the going price.

## Yearbook

Read by [the Grower](persona_the_grower.md), set by [the Buyer](persona_the_buyer.md)'s steady want against the crop, and distrusted by [the Steady Hand](persona_the_steady_hand.md) who plans on the price to come, the true signal the whole trap turns on.
It is the misleading guide of the trap: the Price is what [reading](process_reading.md) takes and [committing](process_committing.md) answers, the number [the delay](piece_the_delay.md) divides from the price got, flung past the mark by [swinging](process_swinging.md) and made anew each season for [chasing](process_chasing.md) to read.
