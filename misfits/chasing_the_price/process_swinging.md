# Process: Swinging

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md). The overshoot of the delivered supply, the price crashing under a glut and spiking into a dearth as the committed output arrives.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The delivered supply meeting a steady demand, at [the Harvest](place_the_harvest.md), where the crop everyone planted comes in.
Begins when the committed output arrives all at once and must be cleared against a fixed appetite, the price forced to wherever the two cross.

## Direction

Turns a synchronised plan into a price that overshoots.
Because every producer committed the same way on the same price, the delivered supply arrives as a flood or a trickle, and meeting a steady demand it clears only by the price falling far or rising sharply, so swinging pushes the price past equilibrium first one way and then, next season, the other.

## Lever

The steadiness of demand against a variable supply.
Because the Buyer's want holds constant while the crop swings, a small change in the mistimed volume forces a large change in price, so swinging works through the fixed appetite that translates every overshoot of quantity into an overshoot of price, its height set by how sharply supply answered the last signal.

## Echo

A price flung past the mark, and a new signal made.
The echo of swinging is a market that overshoots equilibrium and hands the growers a fresh extreme to read, so the crash the glut produced becomes the low price that discourages planting and the spike the dearth produced the high price that tempts it, each overshoot becoming the next period's guide.
