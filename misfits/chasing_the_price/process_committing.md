# Process: Committing

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md). The locking of output at planting, fixing the quantity ahead of a delivery that cannot be revised.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Grower](persona_the_grower.md), at [the Dearth](place_the_dearth.md), putting the seed in the ground on the price just read.
Begins the moment the plan becomes an act, since planting, breeding, or enrolling sets the output and starts the clock that will not stop until harvest.

## Direction

Turns a reading into an irrevocable quantity.
The producer commits the crop to the price they read, and from that moment the output is fixed and the lag runs, so committing binds a present decision to a future market, making a quantity that can no longer answer the price by the time the price is paid.

## Lever

The irreversibility of production once begun.
Because seed sown, a herd bred, or a cohort trained cannot be recalled midway, committing locks the plan in, so its lever is the plain physics of making things that take time: the decision is spent at the start of the lag and cannot be adjusted at its end.

## Echo

An output set on an old price and delivered to a new one.
The echo of committing is a quantity that arrives fixed into a market that has moved, so the plan meets a price it can no longer respond to, the whole trap resting on the gap between the moment output is locked and the moment it is sold.
