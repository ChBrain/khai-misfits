# Place: The Glut

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md). The low-price pole, a market drowned in supply where the price collapses and discourages the planting that would relieve it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A flood of crop and a fallen price, sellers underbidding to clear a supply no one can absorb, the market plainly punishing whoever grew.
Shown as a warning, a clear and honest signal that the crop is unwanted and unpaid, so planting little looks like the obvious good sense of retreating from weak demand.

## Holds

Holds the crash, the low price the flood of everyone's big crop produces.
The Glut is where the swing turns downward: the large planting all did in the dearth arrives together and overwhelms the steady demand, so the price collapses far below the cost, and every grower, reading the same low price, prepares the small planting that will answer it.

## Offers

Offers each grower a true reason to plant small, and the whole market the seed of its next dearth.
Here the low price is real and the retreat correct, so the Glut offers sound individual sense, and by offering it to everyone at once it sows, in the same stroke, the shortage that will spike the price a season on.

## Withheld

Withholds the fact that the crash was self-inflicted.
What the Glut hides is that the flood drowning the price is the market's own answer to the last high price, so the signal now screaming retreat was made by the very response it is punishing, and the small planting it now tempts will, shared by all, become the next dearth just as the last boom became this bust.
