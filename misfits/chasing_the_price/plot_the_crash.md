# Plot: The Crash

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Harvest](place_the_harvest.md), the big crop everyone planted coming in at once.
A flood of supply arriving into a market whose want has not grown, the plan made a season ago meeting the price at last.

## Action

[Swinging](process_swinging.md): the delivered flood meets a steady demand and clears only by the price collapsing, the boom answered by a glut.

## Stage

[The Grower](persona_the_grower.md)'s harvest meets [the Buyer](persona_the_buyer.md)'s fixed want, and [the price](piece_the_price.md) falls to whatever will empty the market, turning the high price into [the swing](piece_the_swing.md)'s crash.
The crop is exactly what the old price called for, and it arrives to spoil the price by arriving all together.

## Tension

Delivering the crop the market asked for is the plan working, and the plan working, for everyone at once, is the crash.
There is no shock from outside: the Buyer wants what they always wanted and pays what the flood clears at, and the growers grew only what the high price sensibly asked. But a steady demand meets a synchronised supply, so the plot closes with the price crashed far below the cost, the boom answered by a glut of the market's own making, and the number that rewarded planting replaced by the number that punishes it, the swing's first arc complete.
