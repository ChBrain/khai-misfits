# Plot: The Tempting Price

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Dearth](place_the_dearth.md), a short crop and a high price plain for every grower to see.
A market loudly rewarding whoever can grow, the signal to plant big as clear and honest as a signal can be.

## Action

[Reading](process_reading.md): the Grower takes the high price as the guide and commits a large crop to match it.

## Stage

[The Grower](persona_the_grower.md), on [Plant to the Price](plan_plant_to_the_price.md), reads [the price](piece_the_price.md) and plants big, the correct response to a market that is truly short.
The price is real and the plan is sound, and nothing at planting shows that every other grower is reading the same number the same way.

## Tension

Planting big into a high price is exactly what good business asks, and every grower asking it at once is the seed of the glut.
There is no error: the Grower answers a true signal with a correct plan, and could not do better on the price they can see. But the price is shared, read by all and answered by all, so the plot closes with a large crop committed across the whole market on the same high number, the sound individual response already, unseen, the oversupply that will crash the very price that called it forth.
