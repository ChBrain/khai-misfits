# Process: Reading

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md). The taking of the current price as the guide to output, the signal each producer plans on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Grower](persona_the_grower.md), at [the Dearth](place_the_dearth.md), taking the going price as the measure of how much to make.
Begins whenever a producer must decide output and looks to the market, finding the current price the one number available to plan on.

## Direction

Turns a true signal into a plan.
The producer reads the price the market shows and sets output to match it, big into a high price and small into a low, so reading converts the going number into a committed quantity, the correct response to the market at the moment it is read.

## Lever

The truth of the current price.
Because the price is real and the response to it sound, reading feels like nothing but good business, so its lever is the plain honesty of the signal: there is no better number to plan on than the one the market is showing right now.

## Echo

A plan made on a price that is passing.
The echo of reading is output fixed to the market as it stands, so the plan is right for today and blind to the harvest, the sound reading of a true price setting a quantity that will be delivered against a price the reading itself will help move.
