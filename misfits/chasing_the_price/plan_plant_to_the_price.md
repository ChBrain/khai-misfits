# Plan: Plant to the Price

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Furrow](position_the_furrow.md), inside [Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Furrow](position_the_furrow.md)

## Direction

Read the going price and set output to match it, a large crop into a high price and a small one into a low, because that is the correct response to the market as it stands. Commit at planting on the only price available, the current one, and wait out the lag to sell. Do not distrust the number in front of you: it is real, and answering it soundly is what good business asks.

## Orders

([The Grower](persona_the_grower.md) commits output to the current price, big when high and small when low, and delivers at harvest into whatever price the market then shows.)

## Implementation

([The Dearth](place_the_dearth.md); the [reading](process_reading.md) process, then [committing](process_committing.md); the Grower answers [the price](piece_the_price.md) at planting and is bound by [the delay](piece_the_delay.md) until the crop is sold.)

## Targets

- [ ] a large crop is planted into a high price, a small one into a low
- [ ] the plan is committed at planting on the only price available
- [ ] the decision is held until harvest, unrevised across the lag
