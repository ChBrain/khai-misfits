# Place: The Harvest

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md). The delivery moment, where the committed output arrives at last and meets a market the plan can no longer answer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The crop coming in, the season's planting made real all at once, delivered to a market that has moved since the seed went down.
Shown as the reckoning of a decision made long before, the volume now fixed and the price now whatever the volume makes it, the plan and its result finally meeting.

## Holds

Holds the resolution of the lag, where the price read at planting meets the price got at selling.
The Harvest is where the delay pays out: the output set on an old price arrives against a new one, and because the whole market set the same output on the same old price, the crop comes in as a flood or a trickle and the price it fetches is the one that clears it, not the one that called it.

## Offers

Offers the plain lesson the swing teaches too late.
Here the gap between the planting price and the selling price is finally visible, the grower seeing at last that the number they answered is gone, so the Harvest offers the truth of the cycle in hindsight, exactly when the output can no longer be changed.

## Withheld

Withholds any second chance, since the decision is already spent.
What the Harvest hides while the lag runs is that nothing can be done here but sell: the output was fixed at planting and the price is fixed at delivery, so the grower who meets a spoiled price has no move left but to read the new price and plant to it again, carrying the mistiming forward into the next turn of the swing.
