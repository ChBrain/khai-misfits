# Persona: The Grower

## Taxonomy

[The Furrow](position_the_furrow.md). Plans output on the current price, soundly, and delivers into the market that price has moved, the engine of the swing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Practical. Market-wise. Planting to the number.
Is the producer who must decide how much to make before it exists, and takes the going price as the guide, planting big when it is high and small when it is low, exactly as good business says.
No folly wished on them: the price they read is real, the plan they make on it is correct for the market as it stands, and they are one grower among many all reading the same true signal.

## Action

Reads the current price and commits output to match it, a large crop into a high price and a small one into a low, then waits out the lag to sell.
Answers the market as it is at planting, since that is the only price they have, and cannot know that every other grower is answering the same number the same way, so the sound individual plan is made in ignorance of the chorus it joins.
Does what a good producer does: plants to the price, delivers at harvest, and takes what the market then offers, no reason at the moment of planting to distrust the plainest signal the market shows.

## Shadow

Not a blunderer, but the one whose correct response, shared by all, is the whole engine of the swing, and that is the rub: the plan is right on the price it saw and ruinous in the aggregate it joins.
Because the Grower plants to the current price and so does everyone, the harvest arrives as a flood when the price was high and a trickle when it was low, so the very soundness of the response, multiplied across the market, overshoots the price it answered, and the crop comes in to spoil the number that called it.
The plan is sound and the outcome is a glut, so the grower who did good business is the one who helped crash the price they planted for, punished not for an error but for answering a true signal at the same moment as all the rest.

## Tell

Says "the price is good this year, so I am planting all I can."
Is right about the price and reading it truly, and cannot see that the truth of it is the trap, that a good price answered by everyone at once becomes a glut, so the sound decision to plant to the number is the same decision that, summed, will undo it.
