# Piece: The Delay

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md). The production lag between decision and delivery, the interval that divides the price read from the price got.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs from the planting at [the dearth](place_the_dearth.md) to the selling at [the harvest](place_the_harvest.md), the season, the herd, the years of training that separate the two.
Flows as time itself, the fixed gap every producer must plan across and none can plan around, the room the whole cycle needs to exist.

## Load Bearing

Without the Delay there is no cycle: output would answer price at once, and a high price would draw supply that lowered it the same instant, settling the market.
With it the answer always arrives late: the Delay is the lag between committing output and delivering it (Ezekiel; Kaldor), so the plan made on today's price is realised against a price that has moved, and the market can overshoot because supply cannot respond until the moment for that response has passed. The Delay is load bearing because it is the structural cause, the reason a sound reading becomes a mistimed delivery, so the piece carries the mechanism of the trap, the interval that turns answering the price into answering it too slowly to matter.

## Apparent

Just a wait, the ordinary time it takes to grow a crop or raise a herd.
Seen at the surface, the Delay looks like a mere fact of production, a season to be got through, and the truth that the wait is the trap, that a decision separated from its result by a lag can only ever answer a price that will be gone, that the whole swing lives in the gap between planting and selling, stays hidden behind how unremarkable it is that things take time to make.

## Yearbook

Borne by [the Grower](persona_the_grower.md) who commits across it, felt at [the harvest](place_the_harvest.md) where it pays out, and reckoned with by [the Steady Hand](persona_the_steady_hand.md) who plans for its far end, the structural cause the trap needs.
It is the mechanism of the trap: the Delay is what [committing](process_committing.md) starts and [the harvest](place_the_harvest.md) ends, the gap that separates [the price](piece_the_price.md) read from the price got, the room in which [swinging](process_swinging.md) can overshoot and [the cobweb](piece_the_cobweb.md) can turn.
