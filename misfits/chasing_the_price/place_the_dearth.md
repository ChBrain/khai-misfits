# Place: The Dearth

## Taxonomy

[Chasing the Price](play_chasing_the_price.md). The high-price pole, a market short of supply where the price stands tall and tempts every grower to plant big.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A short crop and a high price, buyers competing for a scarce supply, the market plainly rewarding whoever can grow.
Shown as opportunity, a clear and honest signal that the crop is wanted and pays, so planting big looks like the obvious good sense of answering strong demand.

## Holds

Holds the tempting signal, the high price that every grower reads at once.
The Dearth is where the swing turns upward: supply is short and the price is high, so the market calls loudly for more, and every grower, reading the same call, prepares the large planting that will answer it, the loudness of the signal the measure of the flood to come.

## Offers

Offers each grower a true reason to plant big, and the whole market the seed of its next glut.
Here the high price is real and the response correct, so the Dearth offers sound individual sense, and by offering it to everyone at once it plants, in the same stroke, the oversupply that will crash the price a season on.

## Withheld

Withholds the fact that the signal is shared.
What the Dearth hides is that the price each grower reads privately is read by all of them together, so the plan that is right for one is ruinous for the sum, and the strong signal that rewards planting is precisely the signal that, answered in chorus, guarantees the glut that will punish it.
