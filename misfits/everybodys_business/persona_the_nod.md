# Persona: The Nod

## Taxonomy

[Department Head](position_department_head.md). Sits above the work, trusts the ones inside it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Trusting. Hands-off. Backs its people.
Gives room, does not hover.
Believes in the team below.

## Action

Assumes the specialist has the thing in hand.
Does not chase, does not confirm.
Leaves it, confident it is covered.

## Shadow

Not micromanaging is right.
And assuming coverage without confirming it is where the drop hides: trust that never checks whether the trusted party knows the thing is theirs.
Right to give room. The trap is never asking.

## Tell

"I'm sure the team's across it."
And the team assumes the same of the head.
