# Piece: The Someone-Else

## Taxonomy

[Everybody's Business](play_everybodys_business.md). Each seat's reasonable assumption that another will hold it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

In every willing head at once, pointing at the next desk over.
The quiet inference: this is more their area, or above my level, or surely already someone's, so it is not, this time, mine to pick up.

## Load Bearing

Without it a capable person would see the open thing and simply take it.
With it the capable person sees the open thing, sees nine others who could take it, and concludes one of them will: the Someone-Else is the individual move that makes the group failure, a private, sensible guess that responsibility lies just outside oneself. It is never a refusal, it is a redirection, and it is usually correct in form, because there almost always is someone else who could. The error is only that everyone makes it at the same time about each other.

## Apparent

Deference, and good sense.
It looks like not overstepping, not duplicating effort, trusting colleagues to their patch. All of those are virtues. Together, held by everyone, they are a room pointing in a circle.

## Yearbook

Made by every seat, and felt most plainly by [the onlooker](persona_the_onlooker.md).
It is the atom of [the diffusion](process_diffusion.md): each Someone-Else is reasonable alone and fatal in aggregate, because the person each one is deferring to is deferring right back, and the thing they are all so sure is in hand is in no hand at all.
