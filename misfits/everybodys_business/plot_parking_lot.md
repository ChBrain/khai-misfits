# Plot: Parking Lot

## Taxonomy

[Everybody's Business](play_everybodys_business.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The parking lot](place_the_parking_lot.md), where the item is sent to be handled later, by the group.
The room is asked, plainly, who is taking this, and a shared silence answers.

## Action

[Parking](process_parking.md): [The Committee](persona_the_committee.md), holding [the working group's seat](position_working_group.md), takes the item on behalf of everyone, which is to say no one.
[Assumption](process_assumption.md): [The Onlooker](persona_the_onlooker.md) at [the bystander's seat](position_bystander.md) sees the roomful of capable people and assumes, with so many across it, that it must be in hand.

## Stage

[The Committee](persona_the_committee.md) tables [Park It with the Group](plan_park_it_with_the_group.md): assign it collectively and move on, and [the unraised hand](piece_the_unraised_hand.md) is the whole room declining, in unison, to be the one.
[The Onlooker](persona_the_onlooker.md) tables [Assume Someone's Got It](plan_assume_someones_got_it.md): trust the numbers, and [the many hands](piece_the_many_hands.md) make the item nobody's.

## Tension

The silence looks like calm.
No one is worried, so it reads as a thing that can wait, when it is a thing that ten people each expect one of the others to hold. Parking it with the group feels like assigning it, and is the opposite, because a duty given to all is divided by all. So the item is officially with the working group, on the record as owned, and actually with no one, waiting to fail.
