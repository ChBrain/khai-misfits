# Piece: The Blank Box

## Taxonomy

[Everybody's Business](play_everybodys_business.md). The place on the chart where the single owner should be, and is not.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

On [the RACI](place_the_raci.md), in the Accountable column, opposite the open item.
The cell that should hold exactly one name and holds none, or holds several, which is the same thing.

## Load Bearing

Without it the structure names an owner and the diffusion has no room to work.
With it the structure leaves the ownership open, and an open structure is where responsibility goes to divide: the Blank Box is the diffusion written into the org chart, the difference between a duty assigned to one and a duty assigned to all. Its rule is exact and quiet: one name in the box and the thing is done, no name or many names and it is everybody's, which is to say safe from being anybody's. The chart looks complete, every row filled with Consulted and Informed, and the one column that decides is the one left open.

## Apparent

A shared responsibility.
It looks inclusive, collaborative, the modern flat way: many owners, joint accountability, a team that holds it together. Joint accountability sounds stronger than single. It is weaker, because it is divisible, and what divides is duty.

## Yearbook

Left blank by everyone and filled by no one, defended by [the committee](persona_the_committee.md) as collective ownership.
It is the structural twin of [the someone-else](piece_the_someone_else.md): the private assumption that it is another's, made permanent as a policy that it is everyone's, so the chart and the instinct agree, and both point away from a single hand.
