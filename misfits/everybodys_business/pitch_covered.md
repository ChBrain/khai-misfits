# Pitch: Covered

## Taxonomy

[Everybody's Business](play_everybodys_business.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Reasonable, collegial, willing.
Everyone agrees the thing matters, and everyone agrees someone should pick it up.
The voice of a helpful room, spoken by people who each assume, kindly and plausibly, that the one who picks it up will be somebody else.

## Undertow

Under the goodwill, a share that keeps dividing.
Each willing seat lowers the odds of the next, because ten people ready to help is ten reasons to think it is handled.
The agreement never breaks: it matters, someone should, and the someone is never named.

## Nerve

The feeling of a room full of capable, willing people,
all nodding that the thing must be done,
watching it sit there, sure between them that it is in hand.

## Echo

After the run: a failure with no author,
a dozen people who each assumed another had it,
and a task that a single person alone would simply have done.
