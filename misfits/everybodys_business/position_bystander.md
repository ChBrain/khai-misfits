# Position: Bystander

## Taxonomy

[Everybody's Business](play_everybodys_business.md). The capability to act and the crowd to defer to.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The capability to act and the crowd to defer to.
A clear view, and good reasons to wait.

## Orders

Help where needed.
Step in if it is truly required.
Read the room before you reach.

## Loses

Nothing it can feel.
Its share of the duty thinned by every other capable seat.
A hundred could; therefore none must.

## Drives

Toward pitching in, once it is sure it is needed.
Which the crowd ensures it never is.
Willing, watchful, and still.
