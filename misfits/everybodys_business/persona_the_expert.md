# Persona: The Expert

## Taxonomy

[Specialist](position_specialist.md). The hands nearest the thing, waiting for the word.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Precise. Closest to the work.
Knows the thing better than anyone.
Ready the moment it is called.

## Action

Waits for a mandate.
Treats the call as a management or policy decision, above its remit.
Holds, poised, until told.

## Shadow

Not making a policy call unasked is correct.
And the one seat technically nearest the thing is the one that most reasonably defers upward: competence without the authority it believes it needs.
Right to wait for a mandate. The trap is that none comes.

## Tell

"Happy to do it once someone owns the decision."
And no one owns the decision.
