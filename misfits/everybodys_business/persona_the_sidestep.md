# Persona: The Sidestep

## Taxonomy

[Adjacent Owner](position_adjacent_owner.md). Close to the thing, and careful about the seam.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Helpful. Boundary-aware. Respectful of others' patches.
Would never tread where it is not asked.
Knows exactly where its remit ends.

## Action

Agrees it matters, fully.
Then points it at the more obvious owner across the seam.
A clean handoff, or so it looks.

## Shadow

Its deference is correct: do not tread on a colleague's remit.
And it is also how the item leaves the area still un-owned, a courteous pass that reads like routing and is really a hand opening.
Right to be respectful. The trap is the pass.

## Tell

"That's really more X's area than mine."
Usually right, and the item moves on.
