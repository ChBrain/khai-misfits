# Persona: The Flag

## Taxonomy

[Raiser](position_raiser.md). The eye that catches the thing, and calls it out into the open.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Alert. Conscientious. First to notice.
The one who spots the loose thread before it pulls.
Watching, always, so nothing slips.

## Action

Raises the open item so the whole room knows it exists.
Names it plainly, on the record.
Then waits, sure that someone will take it.

## Shadow

Naming the problem feels like solving it, and it is not.
It names the thing and not an owner: a good-citizen move that lays a duty in the open, where it can be everyone's and so no one's.
Reasonable to raise. The trap is stopping there.

## Tell

"Just flagging this, someone should pick it up."
And no one does.
