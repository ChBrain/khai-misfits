# Plot: Thinning

## Taxonomy

[Everybody's Business](play_everybodys_business.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The corridor](place_the_corridor.md), between the meeting and the next, where the item is passed along informally.
It travels the circle of plausible owners, and each hand that touches it opens rather than closes.

## Action

[Deferral](process_deferral.md): [The Nod](persona_the_nod.md), holding [the department head's seat](position_department_head.md), assumes the specialist has it in hand.
[Diffusion](process_diffusion.md): [The Expert](persona_the_expert.md) at [the specialist's seat](position_specialist.md) waits for a mandate it takes to be a management call, and each deferral thins the next holder's share.

## Stage

[The Nod](persona_the_nod.md) tables [Assume It's Covered](plan_assume_its_covered.md): trust the nearest expert to own it, and [the someone-else](piece_the_someone_else.md) points quietly at the next desk.
[The Expert](persona_the_expert.md) tables [Wait for the Mandate](plan_wait_for_the_mandate.md): hold until told it is mine, and [the thinning](piece_the_thinning.md) divides the duty a little further with every capable seat it reaches.

## Tension

No one refuses.
The head is right not to micromanage a specialist, the specialist is right not to make a policy call unasked, and each is deferring to a real and reasonable other. But the other is deferring straight back, so the item passes around a ring of the willing, its share of duty splitting at every hand, until no seat holds enough of it to be the one that acts.
