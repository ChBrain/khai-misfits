# Piece: The Thinning

## Taxonomy

[Everybody's Business](play_everybodys_business.md). The share of duty per person, falling as the number who could act rises.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Along [the diffusion](process_diffusion.md), measured in inverse to the heads in the room.
The felt weight of ought-to that each seat carries, dividing every time another capable person is added who could carry it too.

## Load Bearing

Without it more people would mean more chance the thing gets done.
With it more people mean less: the Thinning is the counterintuitive core, that adding capable hands lowers the odds of action, because responsibility behaves like a fixed sum split among all who could hold it, so each additional owner shrinks every existing owner's share of the duty to act. One person feels the whole weight and moves. Ten feel a tenth each, and a tenth of an ought is not enough to lift a hand, so the room that could most easily act is the room least likely to.

## Apparent

Safety in numbers.
It looks reassuring: so many people are across this, it cannot fall. The number reads as a margin. It is the opposite of a margin, because the same number that looks like backup is the number the duty is divided by.

## Yearbook

Felt by every seat as a lighter and lighter pull, and named by no one because each feels only its own thin share.
It is the downward line of the play, the ownership falling as the visibility rises, and it is why [the open item](piece_the_open_item.md) is safest in the fullest room: the more who watch it, the less anyone must, and the crowd that could catch it is exactly why it drops.
