# Persona: The Holder

## Taxonomy

[Inheritor](position_inheritor.md). The one the music stopped on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Plain. A little unlucky.
Or simply new, and unaware of the passing.
Nothing special, just here.

## Action

Stops the passing and puts a single name against the item.
Once it fails, or because they did not know it was not theirs.
Takes it, and it is taken.

## Shadow

Not a hero: just the one the music stopped on.
It owns the thing too late to prevent, and proves by doing that one owner was always enough, a reckoning that is nearness, not virtue.
No blame in it. The trap sprung on whoever stood closest.

## Tell

"Fine, I'll take it."
And it is done in a week.
