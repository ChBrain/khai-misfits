# Persona: The Committee

## Taxonomy

[Working Group](position_working_group.md). Many seats around one table, holding the thing together.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Inclusive. Collaborative. Holds things together.
Brings everyone in, leaves no one out.
Stronger as a room than any one voice.

## Action

Takes the item on behalf of the group.
Parks it as collectively owned.
Minutes it, shared, agreed.

## Shadow

Collective ownership sounds stronger and is weaker, because it is divisible.
The seat that turns a single missing owner into a policy that the thing is everyone's.
Right to collaborate. The trap is ownership with no name in it.

## Tell

"Let's own this as a group."
And a duty owned by all is divided by all.
