# Piece: The Open Item

## Taxonomy

[Everybody's Business](play_everybodys_business.md). The decision that needs an owner, and has none.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

On [the meeting](place_the_meeting.md) list, under any other business, with no name beside it.
The thing that must be decided or done: the risk nobody logged as theirs, the customer between two teams, the call that fits several desks and belongs to none.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to drop and the play has no charge.
With it there is a live duty in the open, visible to everyone and assigned to no one, and the whole drama is what a group of willing people does with a duty that has no name on it. The Open Item is not hard: a single person alone with it would simply do it. It becomes a misfit only in company, where its very visibility to many is what keeps any one from taking it, because each can see the others who could.

## Apparent

A thing in hand.
It looks covered: it was raised, it was discussed, heads nodded, it matters and everyone knows it. All of that happened. None of it put a name against the item.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the flag](persona_the_flag.md), who named the thing and not an owner.
It is the lightest duty in the play and the most durable: because it is nobody's in particular, no one is moved to close it, so it stays open through every meeting that agrees it should not, waiting for a name that a helpful room hands around and never writes down.
