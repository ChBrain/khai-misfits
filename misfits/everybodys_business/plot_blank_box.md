# Plot: Blank Box

## Taxonomy

[Everybody's Business](play_everybodys_business.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The meeting](place_the_meeting.md), under any other business.
An open item is raised. It sits at [the seam](place_the_seam.md) between two remits, and on [the RACI](place_the_raci.md) its Accountable box is blank, or shared among several, which comes to the same thing.

## Action

[Flagging](process_flagging.md): [The Flag](persona_the_flag.md), holding [the raiser's seat](position_raiser.md), names the thing that must be done, and names no owner for it.
[The Sidestep](persona_the_sidestep.md) at [the adjacent owner's seat](position_adjacent_owner.md) agrees it matters and points it, reasonably, at the more obvious owner across the seam.

## Stage

[The Flag](persona_the_flag.md) tables [Raise It](plan_raise_it.md): put the thing in the open, and [the open item](piece_the_open_item.md) is on the table with no name against it.
[The Sidestep](persona_the_sidestep.md) tables [Pass It Over](plan_pass_it_over.md): defer to whoever it is more clearly for, and [the blank box](piece_the_blank_box.md) stays empty on the chart.

## Tension

Everyone is helpful.
Raising the item is right, and passing it to its more obvious owner is right, and there genuinely is a more obvious owner, or nearly. But raising a thing is not owning it, and pointing at the right desk is not the same as a name landing on one, so the item leaves its first hearing agreed to matter and assigned to no one.
