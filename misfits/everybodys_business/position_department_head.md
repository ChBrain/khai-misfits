# Position: Department Head

## Taxonomy

[Everybody's Business](play_everybodys_business.md). The authority to assign it, and the habit of not.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The authority to assign it, and the habit of not.
A team, presumed to be on top of things.

## Orders

Back your people, do not micromanage.
Assume competence; name nothing you can imply.
Leadership is knowing what not to touch.

## Loses

The assignment, never made.
Left to an assumption of coverage.
Everyone reports up; the gap reports to no one.

## Drives

Toward a team it trusts to own things.
Things it never named, and so never gave.
Trust, standing in for a decision.
