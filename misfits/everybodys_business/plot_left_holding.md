# Plot: Left Holding

## Taxonomy

[Everybody's Business](play_everybodys_business.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The aftermath](place_the_aftermath.md), the open item failed and the failure landed.
The thing no one owned has gone wrong in the world, and now, suddenly, it needs an owner, if only to answer for it.

## Action

[Reckoning](process_reckoning.md): [The Holder](persona_the_holder.md), holding [the inheritor's seat](position_inheritor.md), stops the passing and puts a single name against the item, because the potato has to land somewhere and it landed on them, or because they arrived new and did not know it was not theirs to take.

## Stage

[The Holder](persona_the_holder.md) tables [Pick It Up](plan_pick_it_up.md): own it at last, and [the hot potato](piece_the_hot_potato.md) finally stops, on whoever is standing nearest when the music does.
[The orphan](piece_the_orphan.md) is named: a duty everyone saw and no one held, and the proof of it is that the moment one name went on the box, the thing that a room could not do for a year was done in a week.

## Tension

Nobody dropped it on purpose.
Every deferral was reasonable, every assumption fair, every hand that passed it was being helpful and respecting a boundary. The reckoning is not a shirker exposed, because there was no shirker: there was a surplus of the willing, each a reason the others need not act. One owner would always have been enough ... or no one is named, the item is re-parked with the group, and the room that could most easily have caught it lets it drop again.
