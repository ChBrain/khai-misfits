# Persona: The Onlooker

## Taxonomy

[Bystander](position_bystander.md). Present, able, and sure the room has it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Present. Willing.
Would step in, gladly, if needed.
Part of a full and able room.

## Action

Sees the room full of able people.
Assumes that with so many across it, it is surely handled.
Stays ready, and stays still.

## Shadow

Its assumption is the most reasonable of all, and the most fatal, because every other seat is making it too.
The willing bystander who does nothing precisely because so many other willing bystanders could.
Right to trust the room. The trap is that the room is only this.

## Tell

"There are so many smart people on this, it'll be fine."
And that is exactly why it is not.
