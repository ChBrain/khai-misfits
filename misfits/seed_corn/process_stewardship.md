# Process: Stewardship

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The reversal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone lengthens the measure past the quarter.
The look stretches to where the crop actually comes in.

## Direction

The future is put on the sheet, the base rewarded.
Keeping the seed becomes a thing that counts.

## Lever

A longer span, and a deferred reward.
Payment held back until the bill has matured.

## Echo

Eating the seed stops paying.
The cost of that is the quarter's fast reward, given up.
