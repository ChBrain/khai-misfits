# Position: Cutter

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The seat that trims cost, and calls the removal a saving.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The investment lines, the ones that pay in years not months.
The knife, and a mandate to bring the total down.
A saving that books the moment the line goes dark.

## Orders

Trim the cost that no current period will miss.
Book the saving, clean, against this quarter's margin.
Show the leaner sheet, and let the leanness read as skill.

## Loses

The capability the cut line would have funded.
The crop that research, tooling, training would have grown.
The muscle traded for a number, gone before it was noticed missing.

## Drives

Toward margin today, wider by exactly what was removed.
Away from a crop next year, unplanted because the seed was cut.
Thrift that is a withdrawal, dressed in the language of care.
