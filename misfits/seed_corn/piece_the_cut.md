# Piece: The Cut

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The future investment stopped, and booked as this year's saving.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the lab](place_the_lab.md), in the line item that funds next year and is struck this year.
The withdrawal dressed as thrift: the research programme paused, the maintenance deferred, the training budget frozen, the replacement not bought. A cost removed from the present, and a capability removed from the future in the same stroke.

## Load Bearing

Without it the number must be made from real current work, and the future is left intact.
With it the number can be made by not doing something, because the Cut converts a future capability directly into a present saving: money not spent on tomorrow shows immediately as margin today, and the thing that was cut fails slowly, later, off this sheet. It is the cleanest of the harvests, needing no sale and no customer, only the decision to stop feeding the future, and it is invisible precisely because its result is an absence, a crop that simply does not come up next year.

## Apparent

Discipline and efficiency.
It looks like exactly the tough, admirable call a good manager makes: trimming cost, cutting fat, focusing spend, doing more with less. The framing is not even wrong in the present, where the saving is real and the loss has not yet shown, which is why the Cut is applauded in the quarter it is made and mourned only in the year it comes due.

## Yearbook

Made by [the pruner](persona_the_pruner.md) through [cutting](process_cutting.md), applauded as thrift and paid for later.
It is the first and quietest harvest: the Cut eats [the seed corn](piece_the_seed_corn.md) without a transaction, so it makes [the number](piece_the_number.md) look earned by prudence, and leaves its share of [the bare cupboard](piece_the_bare_cupboard.md) as a crop that never arrives.
