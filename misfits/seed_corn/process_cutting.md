# Process: Cutting

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). Saving by not planting.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A shortfall shows against the target.
A future line sits there, ready to be struck.

## Direction

The investment is stopped, the saving is booked.
Tomorrow's spend becomes today's margin.

## Lever

Money not spent on tomorrow, shown as margin today.
A subtraction that reads on the sheet as a gain.

## Echo

A crop that does not come up.
Read on a later page, by someone else.
