# Position: Puller

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The seat that borrows demand from the next quarter to fill this one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The channel, ready to buy early if the price bends.
The discount, and the authority to offer it now.
A quarter that can be filled from a quarter not yet begun.

## Orders

Close hard, before the window shuts on this period.
Bring the demand in, pull it forward across the line.
Book it here, and let next quarter start from where it can.

## Loses

Next period's sales, spent in advance to swell this one.
The natural order that would have arrived on its own.
The level ground next quarter stood on, lowered by exactly what was pulled.

## Drives

Toward a full quarter, brimming with tomorrow's demand.
Away from the next, emptied to feed the one that closed.
A debt to the future rolled and grown, each pull deeper than the last.
