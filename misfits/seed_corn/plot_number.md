# Plot: Number

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The quarter](place_the_quarter.md), its target set, and the work in hand not quite enough to reach it.
The period is what will be scored and what will be paid, and there is a gap between what the honest quarter will earn and what the quarter must show.

## Action

[Scoring](process_scoring.md): [The Scorer](persona_the_scorer.md), holding [the measurer's seat](position_measurer.md), measures and rewards on the period alone, and [The Deliverer](persona_the_deliverer.md) at [the manager's seat](position_manager.md) is told to hit the number regardless.

## Stage

[The Scorer](persona_the_scorer.md) tables [Score the Quarter](plan_score_the_quarter.md): reward this period's result and read no further, so [the number](piece_the_number.md) becomes the only thing that pays.
[The Deliverer](persona_the_deliverer.md) tables [Make the Number](plan_make_the_number.md): close the gap by whatever means the sheet will accept, and since the sheet accepts anything raised this quarter, it will accept a harvest of [the seed corn](piece_the_seed_corn.md).

## Tension

Nothing here is dishonest, and it points the knife at the future.
The scorer is doing plain accountability, measuring what was promised and paying for what was delivered, and the deliverer is doing their job, meeting a commitment they were set. But the measure covers the present only and prices the future at nothing, so the moment the honest quarter falls short, every legitimate way to close the gap that draws on this period is exhausted and every remaining way draws on the next, and a manager paid on the present is handed both the shortfall and the larder in the same breath.
