# Piece: The Seed Corn

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The future value held in the present, meant to grow next year's crop.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the lab](place_the_lab.md), and everywhere a firm stores tomorrow inside today.
The stock of the future kept in the present: the research half-done, the maintenance due, the brand goodwill, the trained bench, the customer not yet sold, the asset that throws off a yield. All of it edible now, all of it meant to be planted.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to eat and no future to spend, only this quarter's work.
With it there is a larder the present can raid, because the Seed Corn is capacity that has not yet become result: it can be consumed for an immediate gain or kept to grow a larger one later, and the whole misfit turns on which. Eaten, it books as a saving or a sale today; planted, it makes next year's crop; and the one thing it cannot do is both. It is the future made tangible and therefore spendable, and a present under pressure will spend it.

## Apparent

Slack, or fat to be trimmed.
It looks like cost without immediate return: research that has not paid off, upkeep on a thing not yet broken, an asset underused, a bench larger than this quarter needs. Read that way it invites the knife, because seed corn and waste look identical from the front, and the difference, that one grows next year and the other does not, is exactly the difference a present-tense measure cannot see.

## Yearbook

Guarded by [the steward](persona_the_steward.md), eaten by [the pruner](persona_the_pruner.md) and [the harvester](persona_the_harvester.md), inherited spent by [the heir](persona_the_heir.md).
It is the stake the whole play plays for: the Seed Corn set against [the number](piece_the_number.md) is a choice between this crop and next, and every plot spends it the same way, into [the bare cupboard](piece_the_bare_cupboard.md).
