# Persona: The Steward

## Taxonomy

[Custodian](position_custodian.md). The reversal: measures the future too.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Patient, long-sighted, willing to be paid later.
She counts the store as well as the harvest.
A good year is one that leaves the next one able.

## Action

Puts the future on the sheet, where it can be seen.
Rewards the base kept whole, not only the total booked.
Holds the maker to the matured bill, when it finally comes due.

## Shadow

The fix trades a fast clean reward for a slow contestable one.
It runs against the grain of every quarter, every close, every applause.
A base kept whole is invisible on the day, and that is exactly the day people are paid.

## Tell

"What did the quarter cost the years?"
A question the sheet was never built to answer.
