# Plot: Cut

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The lab](place_the_lab.md), the shortfall waiting, and the fastest saving being a future one.
The gap can be closed without selling a thing or finding a customer, simply by ceasing to feed tomorrow, and the line that funds next year is right there to be struck.

## Action

[Cutting](process_cutting.md): [The Pruner](persona_the_pruner.md), holding [the cutter's seat](position_cutter.md), stops the research and defers the upkeep, and the money not spent on the future lands at once as this year's margin.

## Stage

[The Pruner](persona_the_pruner.md) tables [Cut the Investment](plan_cut_the_investment.md): freeze the programme, defer the maintenance, book the gap as a saving, so [the cut](piece_the_cut.md) turns a future capability straight into a present result.
[The seed corn](piece_the_seed_corn.md) is eaten without a transaction, and [the deferred bill](piece_the_deferred_bill.md) for a crop that will not come up is written on a page no one is reading yet.

## Tension

The cut looks like discipline, and it is a withdrawal from next year.
The pruner is doing the admired thing, trimming cost, focusing spend, doing more with less, and in the present the saving is entirely real and the loss has not yet shown. That is the whole trap: seed corn and fat look identical from the front, so cutting the future to feed the quarter reads as good management, is applauded as good management, and is indistinguishable from good management until the year the research would have shipped and does not, on someone else's watch.
