# Persona: The Scorer

## Taxonomy

[Measurer](position_measurer.md). Measures and rewards on the present period.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Plain, exacting, a believer in accountability.
He counts what he can count, and pays out on it.
The period closes, the ledger tells the truth.

## Action

Sets the quarter's target, clear and hard.
Rewards the hit, cleanly, on the day it lands.
Reads no further than the line he drew.

## Shadow

A present-only measure prices the future at nothing.
So it rewards eating the seed exactly as if it were grown.
The ledger cannot tell the two apart, and neither, therefore, can he.

## Tell

"Did we hit the number?"
That is the only question he asks.
