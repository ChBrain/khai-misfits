# Piece: The Number

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The current-period measure that is all anyone is paid for.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the quarter](place_the_quarter.md), on the line the whole outfit is scored and rewarded by.
The measure that counts: this period's earnings, this quarter's target, the figure the market watches and the board rewards and the bonus turns on. Present-tense, and the only tense that pays.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no pressure to eat the seed and the future is safe by default.
With it the pressure is total and one-directional, because the Number is what you get what you measure means in practice: it is real, it must be made, and it accounts for the present only. Nothing on it registers what the present cost the future, so any way of raising it counts as success whether it grew the crop or ate it, and a measure that cannot tell those apart will reward the manager who ate it exactly as if they had grown it.

## Apparent

Simple accountability.
It looks like the fairest thing in the world: a clear target, met or missed, no hiding. And it is fair, about the present, which is the trap, because a scrupulously honest measure of the wrong span, the quarter alone, turns every honest report into a lie of omission about the year, without a single false figure anywhere on it.

## Yearbook

Set by [the scorer](persona_the_scorer.md) through [scoring](process_scoring.md), made by [the deliverer](persona_the_deliverer.md) at any cost to the base.
It is the engine of the misfit: the Number is what turns [the seed corn](piece_the_seed_corn.md) into food, because it rewards the present in full and prices [the deferred bill](piece_the_deferred_bill.md) at nothing.
