# Position: Successor

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The seat that arrives after the larder is empty, holding the same target.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The depleted base, spent by the ones who left before them.
The same number, unchanged, waiting on the desk.
A bare cupboard, and an order to fill the plate from it.

## Orders

Keep hitting the target, from stores that are already gone.
Make the season's yield without the seed to plant it.
Deliver, and explain the shortfall no store will cover.

## Loses

The inheritance, eaten before they ever arrived.
The base a fair start would have handed down intact.
The years of harvest already borrowed against and consumed.

## Drives

Toward a winter blamed on them, though they set no fire.
Away from a base they never had, spent in another's name.
The heir handed the bill, for a feast they did not eat.
