# Piece: The Bare Cupboard

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The depleted future the successor inherits, the winter after.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the handover](place_the_handover.md), in the base handed on with the seed already eaten.
The emptied larder: the product pipeline with nothing in it, the plant worn past repair, the market stuffed and sullen, the assets sold, the bench gone thin. Everything that would have made next year, spent to make the years before.

## Load Bearing

Without it the harvest is free and the play has no wound.
With it the wound is total and belongs to the wrong person, because the Bare Cupboard is where every deferred bill comes due at once: the crop that was never planted does not come up, the maintenance not done breaks, the pulled-forward demand is gone, the sold asset is another's, and the seat holding the number in that season must grow food from a field that was eaten. The cost was always real, only postponed, and postponed onto whoever is standing here when the future finally becomes the present.

## Apparent

Bad luck, or a downturn, or a weak successor.
It looks like the new manager simply is not as good as the last, whose numbers were so strong: the decline reads as their failure, not their inheritance, because the harvest that hollowed the base is long booked and long applauded and nowhere on the current sheet. The person who ate the seed is remembered for the crop; the person who finds none left is blamed for the winter.

## Yearbook

Inherited by [the heir](persona_the_heir.md) through [reckoning](process_reckoning.md), after [the deferred bill](piece_the_deferred_bill.md) has matured.
It is the play's arrival at its cost: the Bare Cupboard is [the seed corn](piece_the_seed_corn.md) summed over every harvest and finally absent, the winter that [the short-termism](piece_the_short_termism.md) was always heading for.
