# Persona: The Harvester

## Taxonomy

[Puller](position_puller.md). Pulls demand forward.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Energetic, a strong finisher, in love with a big close.
He can hear a quarter ending and he sprints for it.
Nobody brings a period home like he does.

## Action

Discounts hard, stuffs the channel full.
Brings tomorrow's orders into today's total.
Ends loud, ends high, ends on time.

## Shadow

A pulled sale is a hole in next quarter.
Dug deeper each time it is rolled, because the hole must be refilled and then some.
The close looks like strength, and it is the strength spent early.

## Tell

"Great quarter, we closed hard."
On demand borrowed from the next.
