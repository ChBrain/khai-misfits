# Persona: The Stripper

## Taxonomy

[Divester](position_divester.md). Sells the asset, books the gain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Shrewd, portfolio-minded, unsentimental.
He sees value sleeping on the balance sheet and wakes it.
No attachment, only the multiple.

## Action

Sells the building, the division, the patent.
Books the proceeds as performance, this period, in full.
Signs the paper, banks the gain, moves on.

## Shadow

A sold asset's yield is gone forever, and only once.
The proceeds arrive one time and the earnings it made every year do not.
He counts the sale and never counts the harvests he sold with it.

## Tell

"We unlocked the value in it."
And the value it would have made every year.
