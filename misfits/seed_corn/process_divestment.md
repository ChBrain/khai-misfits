# Process: Divestment

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). Selling the ground.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A shortfall too large to cut or pull.
A gap the ordinary levers cannot reach.

## Direction

An asset is sold, its proceeds booked as result.
The base shrinks, the period balances.

## Lever

An earning base exchanged for a single sum.
Ground that paid each year, traded for one payment.

## Echo

A standing yield surrendered forever.
Taken once, and never again.
