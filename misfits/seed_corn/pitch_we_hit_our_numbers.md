# Pitch: We Hit Our Numbers

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Confident, delivering, on the record.
We said we would hit the number and we hit it, every quarter, whatever it took: no misses, no excuses, results in the bank and reported in full.
The voice of an outfit that meets its commitments, proud of a clean line of made numbers and impatient with anyone who calls that anything but success.

## Undertow

Under the delivery, a future being spent to pay for it.
The number is true, and the ways it was made are all borrowed: the research not done, the upkeep not paid, next quarter's sales pulled into this one, the asset sold and its loss postponed to a sheet no one is reading yet.
No one lies: the present is delivered exactly as promised, and the only thing wrong is invisible, which is the price the future paid so the present could look this good.

## Nerve

The feeling of an outfit that always delivers,
the target met, the market pleased, the bonus earned and the promotion won,
and behind it a slow, quiet withdrawal from everything that would have made next year, made instead into this one.

## Echo

After the run: a record of quarters all hit,
a base worn thin and a bill coming due,
and a winter with no seed to plant, inherited by whoever is holding the number when it finally has to be grown instead of eaten.
