# Persona: The Deliverer

## Taxonomy

[Manager](position_manager.md). Hits the number by any means.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable, committed, the one you can count on.
A closer who delivers when the room has stopped believing.
Give him the gap and he shuts it.

## Action

Takes the shortfall and takes the larder with it.
Makes the number regardless of what the number costs.
Whatever is on the shelf, it is now on the sheet.

## Shadow

Paid on the present, he closes the gap with the future.
Rationally, coolly, because that is where the slack still is.
Every close is honest to the quarter and quietly borrowed from the next.

## Tell

"We will hit it, whatever it takes."
Whatever it takes is next year.
