# Piece: The Short-Termism

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The whole shape at once, and its costly cure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across [the quarter](place_the_quarter.md) and [the horizon](place_the_horizon.md), the present measured in full and the future not measured at all.
The phenomenon named: when only the near term is rewarded, managers spend the far term to deliver it, so a firm consumes its own future to keep hitting numbers that were never false, only short.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a set of clever transactions with no thesis.
With it the play has its law: the ruin needs no fraud and no fool, only a measure of the present, a store of the future, and people paid to raise the first. It is also, and this is the half the outrage skips, curable: measure the future too, put the cost of the harvest on today's sheet, reward the base kept whole and the horizon over the quarter, hold the maker of the number to the year it matures, and eating the seed stops paying. So the Short-Termism carries its own remedy, which is not more pressure on the quarter but a longer span brought into the measure.

## Apparent

Just good, hard-nosed management.
It looks like delivering, like focus and accountability and hitting what you promised, the very things a firm is told to prize. That is the deepest cover of all, because the harvest wears the face of the virtue, and telling a manager who ate the future from one who grew it is impossible from the present alone, which is exactly the vantage the measure confines everyone to.

## Yearbook

Broken, when it is broken, by [the steward](persona_the_steward.md) through [stewardship](process_stewardship.md), at the price of the fast, clean reward the quarter gave.
It is the piece all the others resolve into: [the number](piece_the_number.md) rewarding the present while [the deferred bill](piece_the_deferred_bill.md) hides the cost, and [the cut](piece_the_cut.md), [the pull-forward](piece_the_pull_forward.md), and [the one-time gain](piece_the_one_time_gain.md) eating [the seed corn](piece_the_seed_corn.md), make the Short-Termism, and only measuring the future unmakes it.
