# Piece: The Pull-Forward

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). Tomorrow's demand borrowed into today's number.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the channel](place_the_channel.md), in the deals closed early and the stock pushed out ahead of need.
The revenue moved through time toward the present: the discount that brings next quarter's order into this one, the stock loaded onto distributors before they can sell it, the deal pulled forward to land inside the period. Real sales, borrowed from a future that will now be short of them.

## Load Bearing

Without it a period can book only the demand it actually earned, and the future keeps its own.
With it a period can book demand that has not happened yet, because the Pull-Forward moves sales across the line between quarters: an incentive today borrows an order from tomorrow, so the present swells and the next period opens already emptied of the demand it was meant to sell. It costs more than a cut, being paid for in discounts and returns, but it turns a shortfall in real activity into a full number, at the price of a hole in the next one that must then be pulled forward from the quarter after.

## Apparent

A strong close and good salesmanship.
It looks like a team finishing the quarter hard, landing deals, moving product, closing with a flourish. And the effort is real, which hides the borrowing: a pulled-forward sale looks identical to a newly won one on the day it books, so the strong finish reads as strength rather than as tomorrow arriving early to cover for today.

## Yearbook

Worked by [the harvester](persona_the_harvester.md) through [acceleration](process_acceleration.md), and paid back by every period that inherits the hole.
It is the harvest that compounds: the Pull-Forward feeds [the number](piece_the_number.md) with borrowed demand, so each quarter starts poorer than the last and must borrow harder, a debt to the future that grows every time it is rolled.
