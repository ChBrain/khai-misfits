# Plot: Pull Forward

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The channel](place_the_channel.md), the saving spent and the number still short.
Cutting the future has not closed the whole gap, so the harvest goes deeper: not into what the firm will build, but into what it will sell, and what it owns.

## Action

[Acceleration](process_acceleration.md): [The Harvester](persona_the_harvester.md), holding [the puller's seat](position_puller.md), pulls next quarter's demand into this one with a discount and a push of stock.
[Divestment](process_divestment.md): [The Stripper](persona_the_stripper.md) at [the divester's seat](position_divester.md) sells an asset at [the estate](place_the_estate.md) and books the proceeds as the period's result.

## Stage

[The Harvester](persona_the_harvester.md) tables [Pull It Forward](plan_pull_it_forward.md): bring tomorrow's orders into today with an incentive, so [the pull-forward](piece_the_pull_forward.md) fills the number with borrowed demand and empties the next period in advance.
[The Stripper](persona_the_stripper.md) tables [Sell the Silver](plan_sell_the_silver.md): turn a productive asset into a single sum, so [the one-time gain](piece_the_one_time_gain.md) lands as performance and the yield it would have thrown off for years is gone.

## Tension

Both look like strength, and both are the base being sold to the quarter.
The harvester is closing deals and finishing hard, the stripper is unlocking value and sharpening focus, and in the period each is real money honestly booked. But a pulled-forward sale is a hole dug in next quarter, and a sold asset is a yield surrendered forever, and neither cost appears beside the gain that hides it, so the strong finish and the shrewd disposal read as a firm winning, at the exact moment it is trading its future for its present at a rate the sheet is built not to show.
