# Position: Custodian

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The reversal seat: the one who lengthens the measure so the base can survive it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The will to lengthen the measure past the closing date.
A longer horizon, held against a house that reads only the quarter.
The nerve to count what the present sheet leaves off.

## Orders

Put the future on the sheet, as a line that can be seen.
Reward the base kept whole, not only the number made.
Price the seed, so eating it shows up as a cost.

## Loses

The fast, clean reward of the quarter, given up on purpose.
The bright figure a raided larder would have shown.
The applause that comes to the seat that spends, not the seat that spares.

## Drives

Toward a base worth planting, standing for the season after this.
Away from a present bought with the future, refused at the desk.
The seed spared at a cost, paid now so a harvest can come.
