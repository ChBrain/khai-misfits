# Plot: Bare Cupboard

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The handover](place_the_handover.md), a new season beginning, and the person who made the numbers already gone.
The years of clean quarters are booked and applauded, the maker promoted out, and the base they were made from is about to be counted by whoever came next.

## Action

[Reckoning](process_reckoning.md): [The Heir](persona_the_heir.md), holding [the successor's seat](position_successor.md), opens the books and finds the pipeline empty, the plant worn, the market stuffed, the assets sold, and [the deferred bill](piece_the_deferred_bill.md) now due.

## Stage

[The Heir](persona_the_heir.md) tables [Open the Books](plan_open_the_books.md): take stock of what was really inherited, and find [the bare cupboard](piece_the_bare_cupboard.md), a field eaten down to nothing and a number still expected from it.
[The short-termism](piece_the_short_termism.md) is named at last: years of results that were never false, only borrowed, and a winter with no seed to plant handed to the one who did not eat it.

## Tension

Every number was made, and there is nothing left to make the next one from.
There is no fraud in the file to point at, no restatement, no lie: the harvests were all real and all reported true, and that is the cruelty, because the maker is remembered for the crop and the heir is blamed for the winter, when the winter was the crop all along. The decline reads as the successor's weakness rather than the predecessor's feast ... or no one traces it back, the shortfall is called a downturn, and the heir, under the same present-tense number, reaches for the same knife.
