# Persona: The Pruner

## Taxonomy

[Cutter](position_cutter.md). Cuts research and maintenance, books the saving.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Tough, efficient, proud of the discipline.
He sees fat where softer eyes see budget.
Lean is a virtue, and he keeps the house lean.

## Action

Freezes the programme, defers the upkeep.
Books the gap it leaves as margin, cleanly.
Calls it thrift, and the numbers agree.

## Shadow

Seed corn and fat look alike from the front.
So his thrift is a withdrawal from next year, dressed as economy.
The saving shows this period, the shortfall arrives when he is gone.

## Tell

"We can trim that."
What trimming it was is next year's crop.
