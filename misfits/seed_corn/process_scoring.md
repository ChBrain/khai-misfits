# Process: Scoring

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). Pricing the present.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A period arrives with a target on it.
A reward is pinned to that target, payable on the day.

## Direction

The present is measured and paid in full.
Nothing outside the period enters the count.

## Lever

A sheet that prices the quarter and nothing beyond.
One column, closed at the last day.

## Echo

Making the number becomes the only thing that pays.
Everything else drops quietly off the desk.
