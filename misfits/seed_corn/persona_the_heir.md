# Persona: The Heir

## Taxonomy

[Successor](position_successor.md). Inherits the depleted base.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Earnest, unlucky, arriving to applause meant for the last regime.
He steps in ready to build on what he was told was strong.
The handover was warm, the outlook glowing.

## Action

Opens the books on his first morning.
Finds the cupboard bare, the maintenance skipped, the bill due.
Reads the real state under the reported one.

## Shadow

Blamed for the winter that was the previous crop.
Tempted, under the same number, to reach for the same knife.
The measure that emptied the store is the measure he now answers to.

## Tell

"Where did it all go?"
It went into the numbers he is now measured against.
