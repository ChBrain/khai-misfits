# Position: Measurer

## Taxonomy

[Seed Corn](play_seed_corn.md). The seat that decides what counts, and so what is done.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The scorecard, the one number the whole house reads.
The reward, hung on that number like a lantern.
The period: this quarter, and the sheet that closes it.

## Orders

Measure what happened between these two dates.
Pay on the total that lands inside the window.
Report it clean, and let the window frame the truth.

## Loses

The future, which files no line on a present-tense sheet.
The base, counted the same whether grown or spent.
The difference between a harvest and a theft, invisible to the count.

## Drives

Toward a made number, delivered on time and in full.
Away from a grown base, which the scorecard cannot see.
A measure that cannot tell eaten from grown, and so rewards the eating.
