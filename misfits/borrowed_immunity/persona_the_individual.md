# Persona: The Individual

## Taxonomy

[Individual](position_individual.md). The one who weighs their own shot against a herd that already covers them, and, finding the marginal benefit small and the cost real, rationally declines. The archetype of the many who free-ride on a protection others produce.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Rational. Unalarmed. Right about their own case.
Is any one of the many deciding whether to be vaccinated, the parent at the clinic, the adult reading the leaflet, the person weighing one small shot against a disease they cannot see.
No recklessness in it: the Individual reckons honestly, sees a real cost in the shot and a real protection in the herd, and chooses the way the numbers point.

## Action

Weighs the shot against the herd's protection, finds that with coverage already high their own shot adds little while its cost stays real, and declines.
Takes the herd's protection, borrowed and free, and skips the shot's cost, sitting out with the disease nowhere in sight.
Does what a self-interested person does: it compares a marginal benefit that the herd has driven toward nothing against a personal cost that has not moved, and picks the cheaper side.

## Shadow

Not a cheat, a rational weigher of a real cost against a benefit the herd already supplies, and that is the whole difficulty: the calculation is sound, and the shot the herd has made redundant to the taker is the shot the population most needs given.
Because the marginal benefit of their own shot falls exactly as others' coverage rises, the Individual cannot see their decline as anything but sensible, so takes the herd's protection for granted and sits out, one of many each doing the same arithmetic, and the coverage that held the line thins by their choice and every choice like it, no one of them wrong and the sum of them the disease's way back.
It weighs the shot honestly, and the honest weighing is exactly what makes the shot look like a cost worth avoiding.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "the disease is gone and everyone's covered, I do not need it."
Says it having reckoned truly that the herd already shields them, and cannot tell that the protection they are counting on is the very thing their declining, and every declining like it, quietly stops renewing.
