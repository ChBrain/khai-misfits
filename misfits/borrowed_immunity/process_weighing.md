# Process: Weighing

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). The Individual setting the shot's personal cost against the marginal protection it would add, the honest private calculation that, once the herd is high, points to declining.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A shot to take and a herd already covering it.
At [the clinic](place_the_clinic.md), the individual setting the vaccine's real cost against the protection the coverage already supplies, the disease nowhere in sight.

## Direction

Toward the cost of the shot weighed against a benefit the herd has driven toward nothing.
Because the marginal benefit of one's own shot falls as others' coverage rises, weighing sets a personal cost that has not moved against a protection the herd already supplies, so the honest reckoning finds the shot's cost the heavier side once the coverage is high, and the sound arithmetic points to sitting out, the disease held off by the others invisible on the benefit side of the scale.

## Lever

The inverse relation between coverage and the marginal private incentive.
Because each further point of coverage lowers the good one's own shot would add while the shot's cost stays what it was, weighing runs not on any miscalculation but on the plain arithmetic of a benefit the herd already supplies, the decline to vaccinate assembled from no error, only from a marginal benefit the others' coverage has honestly reduced to less than the cost.

## Echo

A shot weighed, and its cost found the heavier side.
The individual has reckoned truly that the herd already shields them, so what reads as a personal choice is, in truth, a rational response to a protection others produced, the shot declined not from carelessness but because, the herd being high, its cost outweighs what it would add.
