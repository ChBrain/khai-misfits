# Position: Officer

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). The seat that would hold coverage above the threshold before it slips, supplying from outside the individual's calculation the reason to vaccinate the herd's own success has taken away, at a cost that never ends.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A view of the coverage against the threshold and the levers to raise it: the ledger of who is covered, and the outreach, requirements, and trust that can carry people past their own arithmetic.
A real corrective: supplying the incentive the herd's success removed is the one thing that keeps the coverage above the line.
No cheap way to do it, and no visible disease to justify it, because the effort works precisely while the threat is out of sight.

## Orders

Hold the line.
Keep coverage above the threshold before it slips, by outreach and reminder and mandate and the building of trust, and treat a coverage that is only just enough as already failing.
Sustain the contribution, and spend against the disease you cannot see.

## Loses

The thanks and the standing of one who does not spend against invisible threats.
Because the disease is gone precisely while the effort is working, the seat must chase a coverage no visible threat is asking for, press shots the herd has made feel redundant, and spend continuously to hold a line each individual can rationally let fall, and the outbreak it prevents leaves no trace while the cost and friction of prevention are plain, so the held line looks, after, like an expense against a danger that was never coming.
It gains the coverage held and the return spared, and loses the ease and standing that leaving it to the individuals' arithmetic would have kept.

## Drives

Toward the coverage held above the threshold and the incentive supplied from outside,
at the continuous cost of spending against a disease no one can see,
so the seat that holds the line keeps the herd high, and is read as pressing shots on a population the herd already protects.
