# Plot: The Sit-Out

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Population](place_the_population.md), the disease unseen and the herd high, the shot looking least necessary.
Each Individual, covered by the herd, finding the shot's cost the heavier side and about to decline.

## Action

[Free-Riding](process_free_riding.md): [The Individual](persona_the_individual.md), holding [the individual's seat](position_individual.md), takes the herd's protection and declines the shot.

## Stage

Each [individual](persona_the_individual.md), on [Sit It Out](plan_sit_it_out.md), borrows [the herd effect](piece_the_herd_effect.md) and declines to pay [the private cost](piece_the_private_cost.md), no worse protected while the line holds.
One by one, as the visible risk falls, more sit out, and [the coverage](piece_the_coverage.md) thins shot by unrenewed shot, [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) widening beneath a protection that still, for now, holds.

## Tension

Declining a benefit the herd already supplies is rational, and the rationality is what makes the free-ride spread as the visible risk falls.
There is nothing to fault: each decline is sound and none of them alone moves the line. But sound choices sum, so the plot closes with the free-ride spreading and the coverage thinning, no one cheating and no one alarmed, the herd quietly stopping being renewed as each borrows the protection all were counting on.
