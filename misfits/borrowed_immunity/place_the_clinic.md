# Place: The Clinic

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). Where the shot is chosen or declined, the room in which the individual weighs a real cost against a protection the herd already supplies, the disease nowhere in sight.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the decision is made, a clinic, a surgery, a pharmacy counter, where the shot is offered and the individual chooses whether to take it.
The Individual weighing the cost of the vaccine against the herd's coverage, the disease absent from the room, and the shot's small risk the only risk on the table.

## Holds

The private calculation, and the choice it points to.
Here the individual sets the shot's cost against its marginal benefit given the coverage, so the Clinic holds the point where the free-ride becomes rational: a real personal cost weighed against a protection the herd supplies whether or not the shot is taken, so it is where the sound arithmetic of declining is done, and where a disease held off by the others is a disease no one in the room can see.

## Offers

A shot that can be taken or declined, and a decision that feels wholly one's own.
Because the Clinic is where the individual chooses, it offers a real choice with a real cost on one side, and, once the herd is high, offers that choice with the benefit already largely supplied by others, so it hands out a decision that looks purely personal, and hands it out with the collective stakes invisible, the coverage the choice affects nowhere shown in the room.

## Withheld

Any sight of what the one choice does to the whole.
The Clinic shows the individual their own shot and its own cost but cannot show the coverage their choice moves, because the herd effect lives in the population and not the room, so it withholds the collective consequence of the private decision, letting the sound arithmetic of declining stand alone, and, all personal cost and no visible disease, it withholds the sight that a benefit the herd supplies is a benefit each declining, summed, stops renewing.
