# Position: Susceptible

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). The seat that can take no shot at all and so depends wholly on the threshold, protected only while the herd stays above it and reached first when it slips.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

No shot to take and a total reliance on the coverage: a body that cannot bear the vaccine, and a protection that is entirely other people's.
A real exposure: the threshold is the only thing between the Susceptible and the disease, and it is held up by contributions everyone else may withhold.
No way to add to the coverage it lives on, and no signal that the line is slipping, because the herd doing its job is a disease no one can see.

## Orders

Depend on the line.
Rely on the herd's coverage, having no shot of your own to take, and be protected exactly as long as it stays above the threshold.
Live on the others' contributions, since you have no contribution to make.

## Loses

Everything, the moment the coverage slips below the line it never had a hand in holding.
Because its whole protection is the herd's coverage, the seat is carried, invisibly, by every contribution the others make, and exposed, first and hardest, when enough of them rationally decline, so it bears the full cost of a calculation it was never part of, safe while the line holds and struck the instant it does not.
It gains the herd's protection while coverage is high, and loses its life on the line when the coverage that was never its own falls.

## Drives

Toward a total dependence on a threshold others can each rationally let fall,
protected by contributions it cannot make and cannot guard,
so the seat that can take no shot lives on the herd, and is reached first by the disease when the herd, one by one, is let thin.
