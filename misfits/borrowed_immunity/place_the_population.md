# Place: The Population

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). Where the herd effect lives, the whole in which many private choices become one coverage, the protection built and, one sound decline at a time, thinned toward the threshold.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the private choices sum, the population, the community, the whole body across which the disease would or would not spread.
The many individuals' shots aggregating into one coverage, the herd effect conferred on everyone behind the line, and, as the visible risk falls, the coverage thinning shot by unrenewed shot.

## Holds

The aggregate protection, and the slip waiting below the threshold.
Here the individual choices become a coverage that either holds the line or does not, so the Population holds the point where the free-rides sum: each decline sound on its own and the coverage falling by all of them together, so it is where a protection built by contributions is thinned by the withholding of them, every abstainer shielded by the herd until enough have abstained that the herd cannot shield anyone, the threshold approached in full view and crossed unseen.

## Offers

A protection conferred on all, and a coverage free to fall.
Because the Population is where the shots become one shared shield, it offers real herd immunity to everyone behind the line, contributor and abstainer alike, so it hands out protection on every side, and hands it out on a coverage no one is required to renew, the shield held up by contributions each individual is free, and rational, to withhold.

## Withheld

Any alarm, until the slip, that the coverage is falling.
The Population confers the protection but cannot signal its own thinning, because a herd doing its job is a disease no one can see, so it withholds the sight of the coverage sliding toward the threshold, letting each sound decline pass unremarked, and, all conferred protection and no visible disease, it withholds the warning until enough have sat out that the line slips and the return makes the fall visible at last.
