# Plan: Weigh the Shot

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the individual's seat](position_individual.md), inside [Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Individual](position_individual.md)

## Direction

Set the personal cost of the vaccine against the marginal protection it would add, given the herd's coverage. Reckon honestly: with the herd already high, the shot buys little to its taker while its cost stays real, so weigh the two and choose the cheaper side.

## Orders

([The Individual](persona_the_individual.md) compares a marginal benefit the herd has driven toward nothing against a personal cost that has not moved, at the clinic, with the disease nowhere in sight.)

## Implementation

([The Clinic](place_the_clinic.md); the [weighing](process_weighing.md) process; [the private cost](piece_the_private_cost.md) of the shot set against [the herd effect](piece_the_herd_effect.md) that [the coverage](piece_the_coverage.md) already supplies past [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the personal cost of the shot is set against the protection it would add
- [ ] the herd's coverage is taken as given, and the marginal benefit reckoned small
- [ ] the disease is nowhere in sight, so the shot's cost is the only visible cost
- [ ] the cheaper side is chosen, which, once the herd is high, is to decline
