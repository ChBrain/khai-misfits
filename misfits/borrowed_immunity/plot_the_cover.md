# Plot: The Cover

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Population](place_the_population.md), shots given and the coverage rising toward the herd-immunity threshold.
The many private contributions summing into one protection, the coverage about to cross the line.

## Action

[Covering](process_covering.md): [The Herd](persona_the_herd.md), holding [the herd's seat](position_herd.md), turns the private shots into one shared protection and crosses the threshold.

## Stage

[The Herd](persona_the_herd.md), on [Cover the Ground](plan_cover_the_ground.md), builds [the coverage](piece_the_coverage.md) past [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) and confers [the herd effect](piece_the_herd_effect.md) on everyone behind the line.
The chain of transmission breaks and the disease vanishes from view, the protection at its height and, with the disease unseen, the only visible risk left the risk of the shot itself.

## Tension

Building the protection to its height is the programme succeeding, and the success is what makes each further shot look surplus.
There is nothing wrong: the coverage is high and the disease is gone. But a good conferred on all makes each contribution feel redundant to its giver, so the plot closes with the threshold crossed and the disease out of sight, the protection at its fullest and the incentive to renew it at its lowest, success and its own undoing arriving in the same moment.
