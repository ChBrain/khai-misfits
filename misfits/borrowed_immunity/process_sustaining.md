# Process: Sustaining

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). The Officer holding coverage above the threshold, the costly upkeep that supplies from outside the incentive the herd's success removed and forestalls the return no one can see coming.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A coverage watched against the threshold and someone unwilling to let it slip.
At [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), the officer reading the margin narrow while the disease is still out of sight, and moving to hold the line before it falls.

## Direction

Toward the coverage held and the incentive supplied from outside.
Because the herd's own success has removed each individual's reason to vaccinate, sustaining supplies that reason from outside the calculation, by outreach, reminder, mandate, and trust, so the coverage is kept above the threshold that the voluntary equilibrium sits below, the gap closed not by changing anyone's arithmetic but by adding to it, the line held against a disease that stays invisible precisely while the holding works.

## Lever

The willingness to spend continuously against an invisible threat.
Because holding the line means pressing shots the herd has made feel redundant and spending on a coverage no visible disease is asking for, sustaining runs only on someone choosing to pay that cost, so the corrective is exacted continuously and thanked rarely, the return it forestalls leaving no trace and the cost and friction of forestalling it plain for all to feel.

## Echo

The line held, and the return forestalled unseen.
The officer has supplied the incentive the herd's success removed, so what reads as spending against nothing is, in truth, the one upkeep that keeps the coverage above the threshold, the outbreak that now never comes leaving nothing to show for the cost that kept it away.
