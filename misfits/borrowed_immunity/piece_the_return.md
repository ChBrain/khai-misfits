# Piece: The Return

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). The phenomenon whole, the coverage slipping below the threshold as success breeds free-riding and the disease coming back, first and hardest, to the ones who could never be covered.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Strikes across [the population](place_the_population.md), where the coverage has fallen below the line.
Reaches first [the susceptible](persona_the_susceptible.md), who lived wholly on a coverage others rationally let thin.

## Load Bearing

Without it the falling coverage is just a number and no one is harmed, and the trap has no reckoning.
With it the trap is complete and authorless: a disease that was gone coming back because the very success that removed it, high coverage and no visible threat, made each contribution to the coverage feel redundant, so the voluntary equilibrium drifted below the threshold and the pathogen found the gap (Bauch & Earn, on the structural shortfall of voluntary vaccination, the coverage self-interest settles at sitting below the level that keeps the disease out). The Return is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole: not one reckless refusal but a protection dissolved by its own success, the coverage that success produced thinned by the free-riding that success made rational, so the piece carries the disease's coming back to a population that let its coverage fall, striking first the ones who could take no shot at all.

## Apparent

A sudden, shocking outbreak, a disease erupting where it had long been absent.
Seen at the surface, the Return looks like a fresh calamity, an inexplicable failure or a new recklessness, and the truth that it was set in motion by the programme's own success, that each declined shot was rational and none of them the cause, that the coverage fell because a protection high enough to shield everyone made each contribution feel surplus, stays hidden behind how much a returning disease looks like a new failure rather than an old success undone.

## Yearbook

Grows from [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) that [free-riding](process_free_riding.md) widens, forestalled only by [the officer](persona_the_officer.md) through [sustaining](process_sustaining.md), and borne first by [the susceptible](persona_the_susceptible.md).
It is the phenomenon whole: the Return is [the coverage](piece_the_coverage.md) slipped below [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) as [the herd effect](piece_the_herd_effect.md) borrowed against [the private cost](piece_the_private_cost.md) thinned the herd, the disease released at the centre of borrowed immunity to the ones who could never be covered.
