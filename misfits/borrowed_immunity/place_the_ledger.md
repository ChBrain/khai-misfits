# Place: The Ledger

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). Where coverage is tracked against the threshold, the one arrangement in which the falling of the line can be seen before the disease returns, and where the officer would act on it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the coverage is counted, a register, a dashboard, a coverage report, where the fraction immunised is set against the threshold it must stay above.
The Officer reading the coverage against the line, watching the margin narrow while the disease is still nowhere in sight, and the shortfall between voluntary and protective coverage shown as a number before it shows as an outbreak.

## Holds

The one early sight of the slip, and the case for acting on it.
Here the coverage is measured against the threshold before the disease returns, so the Ledger holds the point where the fall can be caught: the margin narrowing in plain figures while nothing yet seems wrong, so it is where the invisible thinning of the herd is made visible, the one arrangement in which the gap can be read as a number and closed before it is read as a return.

## Offers

A way to see the coverage fall before the disease comes back, and the case to hold the line early.
Because the Ledger counts the coverage against the threshold, it offers the one thing the Clinic and the Population cannot, the slip made visible while it can still be forestalled, so it hands out an early warning, and hands it out against a disease no one can see, at the price of arguing for costly effort on the strength of a number when nothing is visibly wrong.

## Withheld

Any force to make the number felt, or any proof the effort was needed.
The Ledger can show the coverage falling but cannot make a narrowing margin alarm a population that sees no disease, because the outbreak it would forestall leaves no trace when forestalled, so it withholds the urgency that only a visible threat supplies, exacting the look of spending against nothing, and, all early sight and no felt danger, it withholds the thanks for a return that now never comes, the held line seeming, after, like an expense against a disease that was never going to arrive.
