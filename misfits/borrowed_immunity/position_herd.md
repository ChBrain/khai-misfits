# Position: Herd

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). The seat of the aggregate coverage, where many private contributions become one public protection that reaches contributor and abstainer alike, and whose height lowers each member's reason to add to it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The sum of everyone's choices and the protection that sum confers: the coverage, and the herd immunity it buys once it is high enough.
A real good: the protection is genuine and, past the threshold, complete, shielding even those with no shot at all.
No agency to hold itself up, and no way to withhold its protection from those who did not contribute, because the good it confers is public and reaches everyone behind it.

## Orders

Cover the ground.
Turn the individuals' contributions into a single shared protection, and confer it, once past the threshold, on the whole population, contributor and abstainer alike.
Protect everyone, since the protection cannot be given to some and withheld from others.

## Loses

Its own upkeep, exactly by protecting everyone so well that no one needs to add to it.
Because the coverage protects the abstainer as fully as the contributor, the seat makes each marginal shot worthless to its taker while holding the disease so far off that it disappears, so it suppresses the very renewals it depends on, and loses height as, one by one, the hands holding it up rationally let go.
It gains a protection conferred on all, and loses the contributions that conferring it on all made each individual free to withhold.

## Drives

Toward the protection conferred and the incentive to renew it dissolved,
the shield held highest just as each holder's reason to hold it is lowest,
so the seat that covers the ground shields everyone, and thins as the everyone it shields rationally declines to keep it up.
