# Persona: The Herd

## Taxonomy

[Herd](position_herd.md). The aggregate of coverage that confers protection, the collective force that, once past the threshold, shields everyone and, by shielding everyone, makes each individual's own shot feel redundant. The whole in which the free-ride becomes rational.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Protective. Impersonal. High, for now.
Is the population as a body, the sum of everyone's choices, the coverage that either does or does not hold the line, the herd whose immunity is no one's and everyone's.
No agency of its own: the Herd does not choose; it is only the running total of the individuals' choices, and it protects exactly to the height they leave it at.

## Action

Confers protection in proportion to its coverage, and, once past the threshold, shields even those with no shot at all, breaking the chain of transmission.
Holds the disease off so completely that the disease disappears, and with it the visible reason for any one more shot.
Does what an aggregate does: it turns many private contributions into one public protection, and hands that protection out to contributor and abstainer alike.

## Shadow

Not a shirking mob, an impersonal sum whose very height is what lowers each member's reason to add to it, and that is the whole difficulty: the protection is real and it is shared by all, so it reaches the one who did not pay for it exactly as it reaches the one who did.
Because the Herd protects everyone once it is high, it makes the marginal shot worthless to its taker while the disease it holds off stays invisible, so it is the collective good whose success suppresses its own upkeep, the shield that, held high, tells each hand holding it up that it may let go, and thins as, one by one, hands do.
It protects contributor and free-rider alike, and protecting the free-rider alike is exactly what makes free-riding rational.

## Tell

Says, in effect, nothing, being no one; it only stands as high as the coverage, and shields whatever is behind it.
Stands highest just before it thins, the protection at its fullest when the incentive to renew it is at its lowest, and cannot signal the danger of its own falling, because a herd doing its job is a disease no one can see.
