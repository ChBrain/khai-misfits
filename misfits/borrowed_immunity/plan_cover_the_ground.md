# Plan: Cover the Ground

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the herd's seat](position_herd.md), inside [Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md).

## Owner

## Direction

Turn the individuals' contributions into one shared protection and confer it on the whole population. Build the coverage shot by shot, and, once past the threshold, break the chain of transmission so completely that even those with no shot at all are shielded, contributor and abstainer alike.

## Orders

([The Herd](persona_the_herd.md) aggregates the private shots into a public protection and hands it out to everyone behind it, protecting the abstainer as fully as the contributor.)
