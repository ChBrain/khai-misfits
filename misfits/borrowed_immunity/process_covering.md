# Process: Covering

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). The coverage building the herd effect, the many private shots becoming one shared protection that, past the threshold, shields everyone and, by shielding everyone, dissolves the reason to add to it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Shots given, one by one, adding to the coverage.
Across [the population](place_the_population.md), the individual contributions aggregating into a coverage that rises toward the threshold.

## Direction

Toward the protection conferred and the incentive to renew it dissolved.
Because the coverage confers a public good, covering turns each private shot into protection for the whole, and once the coverage passes the threshold the chain of transmission breaks and the disease disappears, so the protection reaches its height exactly as the disease it holds off vanishes from sight, and the reason for any one further shot falls with it, the good conferred on all making each contribution feel redundant to the one asked to give it.

## Lever

The public, non-excludable nature of herd immunity.
Because the protection the coverage confers cannot be given to contributors and withheld from abstainers, covering runs on a good that reaches everyone behind the line, so the free-ride is built not from any failure of the protection but from its very success, the coverage shielding the abstainer as fully as the contributor and thereby making abstention the sound choice.

## Echo

A protection conferred, and its own upkeep undercut.
The coverage has built a shield over the whole population, so what reads as the programme succeeding is, in truth, the moment its success begins to dissolve it, the disease held so far off that no one can see the reason to keep the coverage that holds it there.
