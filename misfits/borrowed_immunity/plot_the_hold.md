# Plot: The Hold

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ledger](place_the_ledger.md), and an officer unwilling to let a coverage only just enough keep sliding.
The Officer reading the margin narrow while the disease is still out of sight, and setting out to hold the line before it slips.

## Action

[Sustaining](process_sustaining.md): [The Officer](persona_the_officer.md), holding [the officer's seat](position_officer.md), works to keep coverage above the threshold, supplying from outside the incentive the herd's success removed.

## Stage

[The Officer](persona_the_officer.md), on [Hold the Line](plan_hold_the_line.md), spends outreach, reminder, mandate, and trust to sustain [the coverage](piece_the_coverage.md) above [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md), closing [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) before it becomes [the return](piece_the_return.md).
Against the hold stand a disease no one can see, a shot the herd has made feel redundant, and a coverage each individual can rationally decline to renew.

## Tension

Holding the line is the one move that keeps the disease gone, and it asks the cost the herd's own success made hardest to justify.
It spends continuously against an invisible threat, presses shots on a population the herd already protects, and treats a bare margin as failing. So the plot closes one of two ways: the coverage held above the threshold and the return forestalled, at a cost that never ends and is rarely thanked ... or the effort judged unneeded against a disease no one can see, the coverage left to the individuals' arithmetic, and the gap left to widen toward the slip.
