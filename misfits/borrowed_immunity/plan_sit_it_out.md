# Plan: Sit It Out

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the individual's seat](position_individual.md) once the herd is high, inside [Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md).

## Owner

## Direction

Decline the shot and take the herd's protection. Having weighed a marginal benefit the coverage has driven toward nothing against a cost that stays real, sit out: borrow the protection the herd supplies for free, skip the cost the shot demands, and be no worse protected for it while the line holds.
