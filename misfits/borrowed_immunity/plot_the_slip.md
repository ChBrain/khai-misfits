# Plot: The Slip

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Population](place_the_population.md), enough sat out that the coverage has fallen toward the line.
The free-rides summed, the coverage at the threshold, and the chain of transmission needing only a gap.

## Action

The slip come to its reckoning: [the coverage](piece_the_coverage.md) falls below [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md), the chain of transmission finds the gap, and [the return](piece_the_return.md) arrives.

## Stage

The coverage, thinned shot by unrenewed shot, drops under the line, and the disease that was gone comes back, [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) between voluntary and protective coverage surfacing at last as an outbreak.
It reaches first and hardest [the susceptible](persona_the_susceptible.md), the infant and the immunocompromised who could take no shot and were living wholly on a coverage the others rationally let fall.

## Tension

Every declined shot was rational and none of them was the cause, and the rationality is exactly why the return is a shock.
There is no single choice to blame: each was sound and the sum of them fell the line. But nothing held the coverage against the arithmetic, so the plot closes with the disease returned to a population that let its protection thin, the shortfall visible at last as an outbreak, and borne first by the ones who were never protected by anything but the herd.
