# Piece: The Threshold

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). The coverage above which the pathogen cannot sustain transmission, 1 minus 1/R0, the line whose crossing makes the disease vanish and each further shot look surplus, and whose slipping brings the disease back.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Crossed across [the population](place_the_population.md), where the coverage passes it and the disease disappears.
Marked at [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), where the coverage is measured against it before it slips.

## Load Bearing

Without it coverage is just a number and there is no line to cross or slip below, and no sudden return.
With it the trap has its edge: the coverage above which each infection produces on average fewer than one further case, 1 minus 1/R0, so that past it the chain of transmission breaks and even the unvaccinated are shielded (Anderson & May, on the herd-immunity threshold, the critical proportion above which the pathogen cannot spread). The Threshold is load bearing because it is what makes the good a threshold good: above it protection is complete and the marginal shot worthless to its taker, below it the disease returns, so the piece carries the line whose crossing hides the disease and whose slipping, reached one decline at a time, releases it.

## Apparent

A comfortable margin, a line safely behind us, a level long since reached.
Seen at the surface, the Threshold looks like a hurdle already cleared, a mark the coverage sits well above and need not be watched, and the truth that it is the edge the voluntary coverage drifts back toward, that crossing it is what makes the disease vanish and the shots feel surplus, that it is silently approached from above as each rationally declines, stays hidden behind how far off a line looks while the coverage still clears it.

## Yearbook

Crossed by [the coverage](piece_the_coverage.md) as [the herd](persona_the_herd.md) covers the ground, approached from above as [the individual](persona_the_individual.md) free-rides, watched by [the officer](persona_the_officer.md) at the ledger.
It is the edge of the trap: the Threshold turns [the coverage](piece_the_coverage.md) into [the herd effect](piece_the_herd_effect.md) once crossed, is drifted back toward as [the gap](piece_the_gap.md) widens, and its slipping is [the return](piece_the_return.md), the disease released to [the susceptible](persona_the_susceptible.md) first.
