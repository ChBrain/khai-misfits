# Piece: The Coverage

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). The fraction of the population immunised, the shared protection built shot by shot and thinned shot by unrenewed shot, high enough to shield everyone and free for anyone to decline to renew.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Built across [the population](place_the_population.md), where the private shots sum into one protection.
Counted at [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), where the fraction immunised is set against the threshold.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no shared protection and no herd to borrow from, and nothing thins toward the line.
With it the trap has its stock: the fraction of the population immunised, which confers protection in proportion to its height and, once high, makes each further shot redundant to its taker (Fine, on herd immunity, the coverage that carries those not themselves immunised). The Coverage is load bearing because it is the shared thing each is free to decline to renew: the protection built by contributions and held up by nothing that requires them, so the piece carries the stock whose very height, the coverage that shields everyone, is what makes each contribution to it feel unnecessary and lets it thin, one sound decline at a time.

## Apparent

A protection safely high, a job done, the population covered.
Seen at the surface, the Coverage looks like a settled achievement, a number comfortably above the line and needing no attention, and the truth that it is held up by contributions no one is required to renew, that it thins invisibly as each rationally declines, that a coverage high enough to protect everyone is high enough to make each shot feel surplus, stays hidden behind how safe a high number looks while the disease is gone.
