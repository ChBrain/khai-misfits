# Persona: The Officer

## Taxonomy

[Officer](position_officer.md). The public-health officer who would hold coverage above the threshold before it slips, sustaining a contribution each individual can rationally decline, against a disease no one can see, at a cost that never ends. The one who would close the gap and must keep paying to.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Watchful. Willing to spend against an invisible threat. Suspicious of a coverage that is only just enough.
Is the one who tracks the coverage against the threshold, the programme lead, the officer who runs the outreach and argues for the mandate and tends the population's trust.
No alarmism in it: the Officer does not doubt the disease is gone, only doubts that a coverage held up by declinable shots will stay above the line once the disease is out of sight.

## Action

Watches the coverage against the threshold, and works to hold it above the line before it slips: outreach, reminders, schools and requirements, and the patient building of the trust that carries people past their own arithmetic.
Sustains a contribution each individual can rationally decline, spending continuously to keep a coverage no visible threat is asking for.
Does what holds the line: it supplies, from outside the individual's calculation, the reason to vaccinate that the herd's own success has taken away.

## Shadow

Not a free corrective, a costly and unthanked upkeep, and that is the whole difficulty: the coverage the Officer holds up must be held up against the very success that makes each shot feel unnecessary.
Because the disease is gone precisely while the effort is working, the Officer must spend against an invisible threat, chase a coverage no one can see the need for, and press people toward a shot the herd has already made feel redundant, so the line is held only by paying, forever, and the outbreak prevented leaves no trace while the cost and the friction of prevention are plain, the held line looking, afterward, like an expense against a danger that was never going to come.
It holds the line to spare the return, and holding it is read as spending on a disease that is not there.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "keep the coverage up now, while nothing is happening, because the moment it slips is the moment it is too late."
Says it into a population that sees no disease and feels no need, and is heard, often, as overcautious, costly, pressing shots on people the herd already protects, the one raising an alarm about a threat no one else can see.
