# Position: Individual

## Taxonomy

[Borrowed Immunity](play_borrowed_immunity.md). The seat that weighs its own shot against the herd's protection, where a benefit the herd already supplies makes the cost of the shot the heavier side.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A shot to weigh and a herd already covering it: the personal cost of the vaccine and the protection the coverage supplies for free.
A sound calculation: with the herd high, the marginal benefit of its own shot is genuinely small and the cost is genuinely real.
No way to keep its own contribution from spilling onto everyone, and no visible disease to set against the shot's cost, because the herd doing its job is a disease unseen.

## Orders

Weigh the shot.
Set the personal cost of the vaccine against the marginal protection it would add given the herd's coverage, and choose the cheaper side, which, once the herd is high, is to sit out.
Take the protection the herd supplies, since it is supplied whether you pay or not.

## Loses

The coverage it is standing on, exactly by rationally declining to renew it.
Because the marginal benefit of its own shot falls as others' coverage rises, the seat cannot see declining as anything but sensible, so it borrows the herd's protection and skips the shot, one of many doing the same arithmetic, and loses, in the sum of all those sound choices, the very coverage that made each choice sound, until the line slips.
It gains a cost avoided and a protection kept for free, and loses the herd it was counting on.

## Drives

Toward the shot declined and the herd's protection borrowed,
the marginal benefit driven toward nothing by the others' coverage and the cost of the shot left whole,
so the seat that weighs the shot sits out in good faith, and thins, with every seat like it, the coverage it was relying on.
