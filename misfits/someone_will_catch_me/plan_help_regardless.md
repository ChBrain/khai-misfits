# Plan: Help Regardless

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Open Hand](position_the_open_hand.md), inside [Someone Will Catch Me](play_someone_will_catch_me.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Open Hand](position_the_open_hand.md)

## Direction

Relieve the suffering in front of you whenever it is real, because ending a present harm you have the means to end is the decent thing and the thing you cannot make yourself refuse (Buchanan). Do not condition the help on the recipient's effort, since any condition you will not keep when the moment comes is one they will rightly discount, and you will not keep it: when the need is real again, you will help again (Kydland and Prescott). Accept that the rescue is therefore certain, that a certain rescue can be planned around, and that your reliability, the very thing that makes you good in each moment, is what the recipient will lean on and what will make the need recur.

## Orders

([The Samaritan](persona_the_samaritan.md) steps in when the need is real and relieves it with the means at hand, unable to stand by, and finds the threat to condition the help dissolve the moment the next suffering appears.)

## Implementation

([The Brink](place_the_brink.md); through the [rescuing](process_rescuing.md) process; the Samaritan delivers [the rescue](piece_the_rescue.md), correct in the moment, and by making it certain hands the recipient the guarantee that leaves [the unbuilt](piece_the_unbuilt.md) unbuilt, [the empty threat](piece_the_empty_threat.md) empty, and [the soft heart](piece_the_soft_heart.md) at work.)

## Targets

- [ ] real present suffering is relieved with the means to hand
- [ ] no condition is placed on the help that will not be kept when the moment comes
- [ ] the rescue is accepted as certain, and certain help as un-conditionable
- [ ] the reliability of the kindness is recognised as the source of the coming reliance
