# Piece: The Soft Heart

## Taxonomy

[Someone Will Catch Me](play_someone_will_catch_me.md). The compassion that cannot commit, the whole phenomenon of which every other piece is a part: a goodness so reliable it manufactures the dependence it relieves.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Beats at [the Brink](place_the_brink.md) where it cannot refuse, is rested on from [the Hammock](place_the_hammock.md), and is bound, against itself, only at [the Cold Shoulder](place_the_cold_shoulder.md).
Flows as the reliable kindness itself, present in every rescue and behind every reliance, the trait that does right in each moment and wrong across them.

## Load Bearing

Without the Soft Heart there is no trap, only separate acts of aid: it is the Soft Heart that ties the rescue to the reliance to the empty threat, the one certainty from which the recipient's under-provision and the bluff of every warning follow.
The Soft Heart is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole, the compassion that cannot commit to refusing, so its help is certain, its threat is empty, and its object is left dependent (Buchanan). It carries the entire misfit: the rescue given is the Soft Heart acting, the footing unbuilt is the Soft Heart planned around, the warning discounted is the Soft Heart read correctly, so it is not one more piece beside the others but the shape they all compose, the heart whose every beat is another of the play's pieces at work. It is a virtue, which is why it cannot simply be hardened: the reliability that makes it a trap is the same reliability that makes it good, so to break the cycle is not to correct a flaw but to bind a virtue against itself, letting a real suffering stand that the heart is right to want to end.

## Apparent

Just goodness, a kind person who helps.
Seen at the surface, the Soft Heart looks like the plain and admirable thing it is, a compassion that relieves suffering, and the truth that its very reliability is the trap, that a help certain enough to be counted on is a help that will be planned around, that the kindness under which no one is let to fall is the kindness under which no one learns to stand, stays hidden behind how completely a good heart resembles a good outcome.

## Yearbook

Held by [the Samaritan](persona_the_samaritan.md) as the virtue they cannot give up, leaned on by [the Sheltered](persona_the_sheltered.md), and bound against itself by [the Hard Hand](persona_the_hard_hand.md).
It is the whole shape of the trap: the Soft Heart is what [rescuing](process_rescuing.md) enacts and [softening](process_softening.md) reveals, the certainty [the rescue](piece_the_rescue.md) issues from and [the empty threat](piece_the_empty_threat.md) founders on, the good will that leaves [the unbuilt](piece_the_unbuilt.md) unbuilt and can be freed from its own trap only by [vowing](process_vowing.md) against itself.
