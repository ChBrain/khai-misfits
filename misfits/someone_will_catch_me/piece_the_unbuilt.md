# Piece: The Unbuilt

## Taxonomy

[Someone Will Catch Me](play_someone_will_catch_me.md). The footing the recipient never builds because the rescue is certain, the provision rationally foregone, the missing strength that makes the next need real.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Left unmade at [the Hammock](place_the_hammock.md) where the reliance rests, felt at [the Brink](place_the_brink.md) where its absence brings the fall, and built at last only at [the Cold Shoulder](place_the_cold_shoulder.md).
Flows as the savings not laid by, the brace not set, the guard not kept, the capacity that was never grown because a catch stood where it would have gone.

## Load Bearing

Without the Unbuilt the rescue would be harmless: if the recipient built their footing regardless, the catch would be a spare and not a substitute, and the need would not recur.
It is because the footing is rationally left unbuilt, foregone as a cost with no return while the rescue covers it, that the reliance has teeth, so the Unbuilt is load bearing because it is the manufactured deficit, the strength not grown that turns a one-time help into a standing dependence. It is a foregoing and not an erosion: the recipient does not lose a capacity they had but declines to build one they would have, the difference the whole distinction from a strength worn away by disuse, since here nothing withers, it simply never rises, the provision skipped in advance because a guarantee made it waste.

## Apparent

Just prudence, a sensible person not paying for what they will not need.
Seen at the surface, the Unbuilt looks like good economy, the covered risk left to its cover, and the truth that the foregone footing is a capacity surrendered, that the sum of these sound skips is a person less able to stand, that the prudence of today is the incapacity of tomorrow, stays hidden behind how completely a skipped expense resembles a saving.

## Yearbook

Foregone by [the Sheltered](persona_the_sheltered.md) as sound economy, permitted by [the Samaritan](persona_the_samaritan.md)'s certain catch, and built at last under [the Hard Hand](persona_the_hard_hand.md)'s withheld one.
It is the manufactured deficit: the Unbuilt is what [relying](process_relying.md) leaves undone and [vowing](process_vowing.md) at last restores, the footing [the rescue](piece_the_rescue.md) makes unnecessary and [the soft heart](piece_the_soft_heart.md) unwittingly prevents, the missing strength that brings the recipient back to the brink.
