# Process: Rescuing

## Taxonomy

[Someone Will Catch Me](play_someone_will_catch_me.md). The giving of aid ex post, the Samaritan relieving the present suffering they cannot stand by, correct in the moment and certain enough to be planned around.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Samaritan](persona_the_samaritan.md), at [the Brink](place_the_brink.md), meeting a real and present need with the means to relieve it.
Begins whenever suffering is genuine and immediate and a helper with the means to end it is present, the need itself the whole of the trigger.

## Direction

Turns a present suffering into a relief.
Because the need is real and the means are to hand, the helper ends the harm, so rescuing converts an imminent fall into a catch, the aid the correct response to the suffering in front of it and delivered whatever was resolved beforehand (Buchanan).

## Lever

The reality of the need.
Because the suffering is genuine and immediate, no prior resolve to withhold survives the sight of it, so rescuing works through the plain force of a present harm on a decent heart: there is no better reason to help than a real person in real trouble, and that reason is exactly what makes the help impossible to condition.

## Echo

A suffering relieved, and a rescue shown to be certain.
The echo of rescuing is a need met and, by being met, a guarantee confirmed, so the recipient reads the catch as sure and the next fall as covered, the correct relief of a present harm teaching, in the same stroke, that the harm need not be provisioned against.
