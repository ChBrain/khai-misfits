# Place: The Cold Shoulder

## Taxonomy

[Someone Will Catch Me](play_someone_will_catch_me.md). The withheld state, where the Vow holds and the recipient meets the brink alone, the place a present suffering is allowed to stand so a future standing can be built.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A hand not offered, a suffering left unrelieved.
Shown as coldness: someone is in real trouble and the help that could reach them does not, so the surface reads as hardness or neglect, a person let to fall who might have been caught.

## Holds

Holds the kept vow, the refusal that is bound and does not bend.
The Cold Shoulder is where the precommitment meets the real need it was made for: the brink comes, the suffering is genuine, and the hand stays closed because it was tied in advance, the withholding not a failure of compassion but its deliberate binding, held precisely where compassion would break it.

## Offers

Offers the recipient a fall that is theirs to bear, and with it the reason to stand.
Here the rescue is refused, so the Cold Shoulder offers the one thing the certain catch never could, a consequence that lands, and by letting the fall reach the person it offers them back the incentive to build their own footing, the harm and the restored capacity the same withheld hand.

## Withheld

Withholds that the hardness is the love, not its absence.
What the Cold Shoulder hides is that the refusal is the costliest thing the helper does and the most for the recipient's sake, since every visible sign says cruelty while the aim is the standing no rescue could teach, so the place conceals that the withheld hand and the open one come from the same care, and that the one that looks unkind is the one betting on the person's future rather than spending it.
