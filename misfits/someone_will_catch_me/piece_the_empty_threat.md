# Piece: The Empty Threat

## Taxonomy

[Someone Will Catch Me](play_someone_will_catch_me.md). The non-credible refusal, the warning that next time will be different that both sides know will not hold, the commitment problem made a thing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Uttered at [the Hammock](place_the_hammock.md) where it is discounted, and dissolved at [the Brink](place_the_brink.md) where the real need arrives, made real only by the binding of [the Cold Shoulder](place_the_cold_shoulder.md).
Flows as the words of firmness with no deeds behind them, the resolve that is sincere in the calm and gone in the moment, the threat that carries no weight because everyone can price it.

## Load Bearing

Without the Empty Threat there would be no trap, only a choice: if the Samaritan could make a refusal stick, the help would be a real incentive and the recipient would build. It is because the threat cannot be made credible that the reliance is rational, so the Empty Threat is load bearing because it is the commitment problem itself, the reason the certain help cannot be conditioned into anything but certain help. Its emptiness is the mechanism: the recipient discounts it not from stubbornness but from correct reading, since a threat the threatener will not keep when the moment comes is no threat, and both sides know the compassion will win at the brink, so the warning is understood by all to be a bluff and changes nothing, the words firm and the deeds foreknown to be soft (Kydland and Prescott).

## Apparent

Just a warning, a firm word about how it will be next time.
Seen at the surface, the Empty Threat looks like a real condition, a line drawn that ought to change behaviour, and the truth that it is priced as a bluff the instant it is heard, that its speaker will not keep it and the hearer knows, that the firmness of the words is exactly contradicted by the certainty of the help, stays hidden behind how much a stated resolve resembles a binding one.

## Yearbook

Spoken by [the Samaritan](persona_the_samaritan.md) who means it and will not keep it, seen through by [the Sheltered](persona_the_sheltered.md) who reads the incentive, and made at last real by [the Hard Hand](persona_the_hard_hand.md) who binds it.
It is the commitment problem made a thing: the Empty Threat is what [softening](process_softening.md) dissolves and [vowing](process_vowing.md) converts into a credible no, the bluff [relying](process_relying.md) correctly discounts, the firm word [the soft heart](piece_the_soft_heart.md) can never make bind.
