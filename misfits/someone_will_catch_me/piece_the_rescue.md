# Piece: The Rescue

## Taxonomy

[Someone Will Catch Me](play_someone_will_catch_me.md). The aid itself, genuinely right each time it is given, the catch that relieves a real suffering and, by being certain, becomes the thing the recipient plans around.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Given at [the Brink](place_the_brink.md) where the need is real, and counted on from [the Hammock](place_the_hammock.md) where the reliance rests, withheld only at [the Cold Shoulder](place_the_cold_shoulder.md).
Flows as the help delivered, the hand that opens when suffering appears, the good act the whole trap is built from.

## Load Bearing

Without the Rescue being genuinely good the trap would not hold: an aid that did harm could be condemned and refused, and no one would need to reason about commitment at all.
It is because each rescue is correct in the moment, a real suffering really relieved, that there is no wrong act to point to, so the Rescue is load bearing because it is the good deed whose goodness is the mechanism, the right relief of a present harm that, by being reliable, teaches the recipient not to prepare for the harm (Buchanan). Its rightness is the whole of it: were the help harmful it could be stopped, but a rescue that truly relieves suffering cannot be faulted in any instance, so the dependence is not built of bad acts but of good ones, each catch justified and the pattern of catches the trap, the kindness doing right every time and wrong in sum.

## Apparent

Just help, a suffering relieved by someone who could.
Seen at the surface, the Rescue looks like the whole of the good, a decent act with a grateful object, and the truth that its certainty is planned around, that the reliable catch is what leaves the footing unbuilt, that the very dependability which makes it kind is what makes it a trap, stays hidden behind how plainly a relieved suffering resembles a problem solved.

## Yearbook

Given by [the Samaritan](persona_the_samaritan.md) who cannot stand by, leaned on by [the Sheltered](persona_the_sheltered.md) who counts on it, and refused by [the Hard Hand](persona_the_hard_hand.md) who binds against it.
It is the good act that builds the dependence: the Rescue is what [rescuing](process_rescuing.md) delivers and [vowing](process_vowing.md) forgoes, the certain catch [relying](process_relying.md) plans around and [the soft heart](piece_the_soft_heart.md) cannot help giving, the relief that leaves [the unbuilt](piece_the_unbuilt.md) unbuilt.
