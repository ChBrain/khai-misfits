# Plan: Withhold the Hand

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Vow](position_the_vow.md), inside [Someone Will Catch Me](play_someone_will_catch_me.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Vow](position_the_vow.md)

## Direction

Bind yourself in advance against the ex-post pull to help, in a form you cannot revise when the suffering is in front of you, so that the threat to withhold becomes credible and the recipient has reason to build their own footing again (Schelling; Kydland and Prescott). Hold the line when the brink comes, since a vow that bends at the first real need is no vow and takes the credibility with it. Accept that this means letting a present, relievable suffering stand, inflicting a certain harm now for an uncertain capacity later, and being called cruel by everyone who sees the fall and not the standing it is meant to buy, the cure indistinguishable in the moment from the coldness it is not.

## Orders

([The Hard Hand](persona_the_hard_hand.md) ties their own hands against their compassion and holds to the refusal when the need is real, bearing the sight of a suffering they could relieve so the recipient meets the fall that teaches standing.)

## Implementation

([The Cold Shoulder](place_the_cold_shoulder.md); through the [vowing](process_vowing.md) process, against the [rescuing](process_rescuing.md) it forgoes; the Hard Hand makes [the empty threat](piece_the_empty_threat.md) real by binding [the soft heart](piece_the_soft_heart.md), letting [the rescue](piece_the_rescue.md) go unmade so [the unbuilt](piece_the_unbuilt.md) is built at last.)

## Targets

- [ ] the commitment to withhold is made in advance and beyond revision
- [ ] the line is held when the need is real, not only when it is hypothetical
- [ ] a present relievable suffering is allowed to stand for the future capacity it buys
- [ ] the charge of cruelty is borne, the standing it purchases being unprovable in the moment
