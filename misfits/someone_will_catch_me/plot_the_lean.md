# Plot: The Lean

## Taxonomy

[Someone Will Catch Me](play_someone_will_catch_me.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Hammock](place_the_hammock.md), the recipient certain the catch will come.
A guarantee that can be counted on, and a person sensibly arranging their affairs around it, weighing whether to build a footing the rescue would make unnecessary.

## Action

[Relying](process_relying.md): the recipient builds less of their own footing, provisioning against uncovered risks only and letting the rest go, the sound response to a sure thing.

## Stage

[The Sheltered](persona_the_sheltered.md) leans on [the rescue](piece_the_rescue.md) from [the Hammock](place_the_hammock.md), on [Lean on the Rescue](plan_lean_on_the_rescue.md), letting [the unbuilt](piece_the_unbuilt.md) go unbuilt and reading [the empty threat](piece_the_empty_threat.md) as the bluff it is.
No single choice to lean is wrong, and the sum is a person growing less able to stand.

## Tension

Provisioning around a certain rescue is sound economy, and sound economy is the manufacture of the need.
There is no fault in the reliance: building a footing the catch makes unnecessary would be paying for a strength that will not be used, so the recipient does exactly what a rational person does with a guarantee. But each covered risk left uncovered is a capacity not grown, so the plot closes with the footing quietly unbuilt and the recipient nearer the next brink than they would have been unaided, the reliance right in every instance and, over cycles, the maker of the suffering the rescue will relieve.
