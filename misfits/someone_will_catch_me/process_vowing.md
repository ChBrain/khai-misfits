# Process: Vowing

## Taxonomy

[Someone Will Catch Me](play_someone_will_catch_me.md). The binding of one's own hands in advance, the precommitment that makes the refusal credible by removing the freedom to relent, the remedy that works only by foregoing the help.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Hard Hand](persona_the_hard_hand.md), before [the Cold Shoulder](place_the_cold_shoulder.md), tying the resolve into a form the moment cannot loosen.
Begins in the calm before the need, when the helper commits against their own future compassion rather than trusting it to hold.

## Direction

Turns a soft resolve into a binding one.
Because a mere intention to withhold dissolves at the brink, only a commitment made unrevisable in advance can survive the moment, so vowing converts a wish to be firm into a structure that enforces it, the freedom to relent given up so the refusal can be believed (Schelling).

## Lever

The surrender of one's own discretion.
Because the threat is credible only if the helper cannot take it back, vowing works by removing the ex-post choice: the hand is tied so that when the suffering appears there is no decision left to soften, so the lever is a deliberate loss of freedom, the power of the no bought precisely by giving up the power to say yes.

## Echo

A threat made real, and a suffering that must now be allowed.
The echo of vowing is a refusal the recipient can finally believe, so the incentive to build a footing returns, but the same binding that makes the no credible means the helper must let a real need go unmet when it comes, the restored capacity bought with a present harm the vow will not permit them to relieve.
