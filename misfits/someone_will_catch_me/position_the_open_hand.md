# Position: The Open Hand

## Taxonomy

[Someone Will Catch Me](play_someone_will_catch_me.md). The helping seat, which relieves real suffering whenever it appears and therefore cannot condition its help, the office whose reliability is exactly what disarms its every threat to withhold.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The will and the means to rescue, and the certainty that it will.
The seat holds the capacity to relieve a present need and the disposition to use it, so a real suffering in front of it is a suffering it will end, dependably and every time.
Ex-post reliability as its whole nature, since the seat cannot see genuine need and refuse, so its help is not a choice made afresh each time but a certainty the whole situation is read against.

## Orders

Relieve the suffering in front of you.
Meet real need with the means to hand, ending the present harm because ending present harm you can end is what the seat is for, and do not stand by on a suffering you could relieve.
Condition nothing you cannot commit to, since the seat's help is certain ex post and a condition it will not keep when the moment comes is no condition at all, so its every attempt to make the rescue contingent dissolves the instant the need is real again.

## Loses

The power to refuse, which the seat forfeits by the very reliability that defines it.
The Open Hand cannot make a credible threat to withhold, because everyone including the seat knows it will help when the need is real, so its warnings carry no weight and its conditions do not bind, the office that always catches unable ever to promise not to. What the seat forfeits is leverage over the recipient's provisioning: it would like the help to be an incentive to build one's own footing, and it cannot, since a rescue that is certain is a rescue that can be planned around, so the seat's kindness is not a lever but a floor, and the more dependably it helps the less it can shape what the helped will do, its reliability buying relief in each moment at the cost of all influence over the pattern, until it carries as a standing burden what it meant only to catch in a fall.

## Drives

Toward a help that cannot be conditioned and a threat that cannot be believed,
and a rescue leaned on precisely because it is sure,
so the seat that relieves every suffering is the seat that can shape none of the conduct that produces it, its reliability the source of both the relief it gives and the dependence it feeds.
