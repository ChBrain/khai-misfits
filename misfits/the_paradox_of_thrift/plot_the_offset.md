# Plot: The Offset

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Treasury](place_the_treasury.md), the flow shrinking and one actor weighing whether to spend against the tide.
The saving heavy, the borrowers gone, and the demand that would end the slump waiting for someone to supply it.

## Action

[Injecting](process_injecting.md): [The Spender](persona_the_spender.md), holding [the spender's seat](position_spender.md), opens a deficit and puts demand back, while [The Hoard](persona_the_hoard.md), holding [the hoard's seat](position_hoard.md), sits idle as the one pool of saving no confidence will move.

## Stage

[The Spender](persona_the_spender.md), having tabled [Spend Against the Tide](plan_spend_against_the_tide.md), spends into the slump and refills the flow, so the incomes return and the households' thrift turns safe at last.
[The Hoard](persona_the_hoard.md), still on [Hold the Cash](plan_hold_the_cash.md), stays put, and [The Flow](persona_the_flow.md), on [Carry the Coin](plan_carry_the_coin.md), carries the injected demand round as wages that fill the tills again.

## Tension

The one move that ends the slump is the one that looks most reckless while it lasts.
There is no easy version of it: the offset is plainly the cure, yet it means spending freely and running debt exactly when every private instinct says to hoard, and asks one actor to move against a prudence all the rest are heeding. So the play closes where it must, the demand offset and the flow refilled by a hand willing to spend against the tide ... or every household keeps tightening, no one supplies the demand, and the slump deepens on its own good sense.
