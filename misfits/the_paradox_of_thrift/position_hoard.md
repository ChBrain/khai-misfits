# Position: Hoard

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The seat of the saved coin that finds no borrower, resting out of the flow.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The money the savers have set aside, held safe and waiting.
The need of a borrower to return it to the flow as spending.
No borrower, in a slump, for no one invests into falling demand.

## Orders

Hold the cash.
Keep the saving safe until it is wanted, and wait for confidence to bring a borrower.
Rest, harmlessly, until the money can be put to work.

## Loses

Its harmlessness, exactly by resting when no one will borrow.
Because a coin saved and lent is spent by another and does no damage, but a coin saved and hoarded is a leak, the seat cannot be neutral in a slump, when the saving is heaviest and the borrowers fewest, so its rest is the point at which the withdrawal becomes a loss.
It drains the flow by doing nothing, precisely because nothing is what a slump lets it do.

## Drives

Toward a saving that sits instead of circulating,
sure it does no harm and doing it by sitting,
so the coin that might have funded investment pools and stalls, a leak and not a loan.
