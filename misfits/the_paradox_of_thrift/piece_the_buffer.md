# Piece: The Buffer

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The store each household seeks against hard times, sound for one and self-defeating for all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the household](place_the_household.md), where it is built by paring the spending, one budget at a time.
Undone across [the high street](place_the_high_street.md), where the spending pared to fill it is the income that fills nobody.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no motive, no prudent reason for the saving that drives the whole trap.
With it the misfit has its honest engine: a goal that is genuinely wise for a household, a cushion against uncertain months. The Buffer is load bearing because it is the good and rational aim the paradox is built on (Keynes): the saving is not greed or error but sound household prudence, so the trouble cannot be blamed on the wish for it, only on the fact that a store safe to build alone cannot be built by all at once without shrinking the incomes it is built from.

## Apparent

Simple good sense, a household making itself safe.
Seen from one budget, the buffer looks like unarguable prudence, and the truth that the spending set aside to build it is another household's lost wage, that a nation all building buffers dries the incomes each is saving, stays hidden behind how plainly right it is for one home to keep something back.

## Yearbook

Sought by [the saver](persona_the_saver.md) through [saving](process_saving.md), the prudent goal that starts the circle.
It is the sound aim behind the loss: the Buffer is what each coin lifted from [the circular flow](piece_the_circular_flow.md) is meant to grow, the store that [the shortfall](piece_the_shortfall.md) proves no larger for all the tightening, and the safety that only [the injection](piece_the_injection.md), by refilling the flow, can actually make safe.
