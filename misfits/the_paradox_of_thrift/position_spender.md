# Position: Spender

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The seat that spends against the tide when all others save, refilling the flow.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The capacity to spend or invest when others will not: the nerve, or the deficit, to put demand back.
The one move that refills the flow and returns the incomes.
The look of folly, for spending exactly when every private hand is closing.

## Orders

Spend against the tide.
Invest with nerve or run a deficit, and put demand into the flow while all others withdraw it.
Refill the stream, so the saving becomes safe once something offsets it.

## Loses

Its ease and its cover, exactly because the move runs against every private instinct.
Because the offset means spending freely and running debt when both feel most reckless, the seat cannot act without bearing the appearance of recklessness, and it stands alone against the grain of a prudence every household is heeding at once.
It does the necessary thing at the moment it looks most unwise.

## Drives

Toward a flow refilled by spending into the slump,
the one move that works and the hardest to dare,
so the cure for a nation saving itself poor is the act no private saver will make first.
