# Persona: The Spender

## Taxonomy

[Spender](position_spender.md). Spends against the tide when all others save, refilling the flow at the cost of acting against every private instinct.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady-nerved. Countercyclical. Willing to move the wrong way on purpose.
Sees that when all withdraw, someone must put in, and reaches to be the one.
No recklessness in it, though it looks like it: a considered choice to spend where every private hand is closing.

## Action

Spends into the slump: invests with nerve, or runs a deficit, and puts demand back into the flow.
Refills the stream so the incomes return and the households' saving turns safe again.
Does, for the whole, the opposite of what looks prudent to each part.

## Shadow

It is the one move that works and the hardest of all to make, because it asks for spending exactly when every instinct says to hoard.
The offset means running a debt when debt feels most reckless, spending freely as others tighten, and bearing the look of folly for doing the one thing the slump requires, so it stands against the grain of every private prudence at the moment that prudence is strongest.
It answers a nation saving itself poor with an act no saver will be first to make.

## Tell

Says "when everyone else is saving, that is exactly when someone must spend."
And spends, against the tide and the dread of the debt, because no private hand will.
