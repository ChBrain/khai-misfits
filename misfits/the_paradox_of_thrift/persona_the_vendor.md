# Persona: The Vendor

## Taxonomy

[Vendor](position_vendor.md). Takes the spending as income, and cuts to the falling till, passing the thrift on.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Practical. Watching the takings. Trimming to fit.
Lives on what the customers spend, and adjusts as the spending moves.
No cruelty in it: only a business meeting a smaller till with a smaller wage bill.

## Action

Receives less as the savers withhold their spending, and trims to match.
Cuts wages, hours, orders, staff, whatever the fallen takings will no longer bear.
Sends, in trimming, the fall in demand onward as a fall in someone else's income.

## Shadow

It is the saver seen from the other side of the counter, and its prudent cut is the next household's lost wage.
Because it must trim to the takings, its response to weak demand weakens demand again, so the households it cuts become savers in their turn, and the circle tightens by its own good management.
It is right to trim, and its rightness turns one thrift into many.

## Tell

Says "sales are down, so I have to cut my costs."
Cuts, and the costs it cuts are the wages that were someone's spending.
