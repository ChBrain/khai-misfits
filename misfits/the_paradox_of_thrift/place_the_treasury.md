# Place: The Treasury

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). Where the offset is decided, the one purse that can spend into the slump when every private hand is closing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A ledger with the nerve to run red on purpose: spending set out while the tills fall, a deficit opened, demand put back into the flow.
The one actor moving the wrong way by design, and around it the chorus of private prudence calling the move reckless while the flow it refills begins to return the incomes.

## Holds

The remedy, and the whole weight of what makes it hard.
Here the offset is possible, so the treasury holds the one move that answers the paradox, spending against the tide to refill the flow, and holds with it everything that resists the move: the dread of the debt, the look of folly, the instinct shared by all to hoard, which the treasury alone must act against.

## Offers

A flow refilled and incomes returned, at no household's having to break ranks first.
Because the treasury can spend when others cannot, it offers the economy the injection no private saver would supply, so it offers the exit from the paradox, the demand restored by an actor able and willing to do the opposite of what looks prudent to each.

## Withheld

Ease, and the appearance of good sense.
The treasury cannot make the offset feel prudent, because spending freely and running debt in a slump run against every private instinct, so it withholds the comfort of acting wisely, and offering a cure built against the grain it withholds any version of the remedy that does not cost nerve and bear the look of recklessness.
