# Process: Saving

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The household trimming its spending to build a store, the coin lifted out of the flow.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Hard times ahead and a household minded to be ready.
At [the household](place_the_household.md), the saver weighing its budget and paring the spending to grow the store.

## Direction

Toward a larger buffer built from a smaller outlay.
Each round the household spends less and saves more, and because for one home this truly grows the store, the saving has every reason to continue, so it lifts coin after coin out of the flow, each withdrawal sound for the saver and, unseen, a wage withheld from someone else.

## Lever

The plain rightness of thrift for a single budget.
Because a household that spends less than it earns is genuinely safer, the saver is rewarded for saving and never shown the cost, so the saving runs on its own good sense, the virtue for one pulling steadily toward the loss for all.

## Echo

A store grown and a coin gone from the flow.
The buffer is larger, the budget balanced, and the withdrawn spending is already a shortfall in another's income, so what reads at the household as pure prudence is, unseen, the first turn of the circle that will bring the saver's own income down.
