# Persona: The Saver

## Taxonomy

[Saver](position_saver.md). Spends less and puts more by against hard times, and is right to, for one household.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Prudent. Careful. Reading the weather ahead.
Sees hard times coming and does the responsible thing: trims the spending, builds the store.
No panic in it: only the household sense of living within its means and keeping something back.

## Action

Cuts its spending and lifts the difference into savings.
Builds a buffer against the lean months, earning as before and laying out less.
Does, for itself, exactly what makes a household safer.

## Shadow

Not a hoarder, a saver: the move is sound for one and cannot be sound for all, because its withheld spending is another's lost wage.
When every household saves at once, the incomes they all save from fall, so the buffer it reaches for shrinks in the reaching, and its own wage may be the next to go.
It is right to save, and the rightness, shared by all, is the trap.

## Tell

Says "hard times are coming, so I had better spend less and save."
Does, and finds the times harder for everyone having said it.
