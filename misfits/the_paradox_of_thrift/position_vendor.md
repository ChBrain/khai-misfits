# Position: Vendor

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The seat that lives on the spending and trims to the falling till.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A trade that runs on what the customers spend, and a wage bill it must cover from the takings.
The takings themselves, rising and falling with the demand.
The means to cut its costs when the till comes up short.

## Orders

Trim to sales.
Match the wage bill to the takings, and cut wages, hours, and orders when the spending falls.
Keep the business sound against a shrinking demand.

## Loses

The demand it depends on, by the very cuts that keep it solvent.
Because the wages it trims were someone's spending, the seat's prudent economy lowers the incomes that feed its own till, so it cannot protect its trade without weakening the demand its trade needs.
It keeps itself afloat by passing the thrift on, and the thrift returns as a smaller till.

## Drives

Toward a business trimmed to fit a falling demand,
right to cut and cutting the demand as it does,
so each sound economy on the counter is the next household's lost wage.
