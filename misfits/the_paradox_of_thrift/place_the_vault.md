# Place: The Vault

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). Where the saved coin rests when no borrower comes, out of the flow and into no one's income.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The savings pooled and still: money set aside, safe, waiting to be put to work.
In good times a borrower calling to take it out and spend it as investment, and in the slump the calls gone quiet, the coin sitting where the saving left it, lent to no one and spent by no one.

## Holds

The saving in the form that harms, the leak rather than the loan.
Here the withdrawn coin waits, so the vault holds the difference between a saving that circulates and one that stalls: a coin lent is spent by another and the flow holds, a coin hoarded is a hole in the flow, so it is where the saving, unborrowed, becomes a subtraction from demand rather than a source of it.

## Offers

Safety to the saver, and nothing to the flow.
Because the vault keeps the coin secure and idle, it offers the household its buffer intact and offers the economy no spending in return, so it shows the saving at its most harmless-seeming and most draining, the money safe precisely by being kept out of everyone's income.

## Withheld

The borrower, when the borrower is most needed.
The vault cannot conjure the investment that would return its coin to the flow, because in a slump confidence is low and no one borrows to build into falling demand, so it withholds the loan exactly when the saving is heaviest, and holding the coin idle it withholds the spending that would have made the thrift harmless.
