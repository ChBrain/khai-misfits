# Piece: The Paradox of Thrift

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The phenomenon whole: a nation impoverished by its own prudence, and its remedy in a demand no private saver will supply.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Born at [the household](place_the_household.md), where the saving is prudent, and proved along [the high street](place_the_high_street.md), where it is another's lost wage.
Answered only at [the treasury](place_the_treasury.md), where an actor spends against the tide.

## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without the mechanism that binds them.
With it the whole trap stands in one object: a buffer sound for one household, a circular flow that makes spending income, a fallacy of composition that voids the sum, a contraction that feeds itself, a leak that drains the flow, and an injection that alone refills it. The Paradox of Thrift is load bearing because it is the structure entire (Keynes; Samuelson): not a failure of virtue but its aggregation, each household right to save and the nation poorer for the saving, correctable only by a spender who does the opposite of what looks prudent to each.

## Apparent

A morality tale with an easy lesson: people should simply spend more and worry less.
Seen from outside, the paradox looks like a scolding of the thrifty, and the truth that the saving is genuinely wise for each household, that the fault is in the composition and not the character, that the remedy costs nerve and debt and the appearance of folly, stays hidden behind how tempting it is to blame the savers for saving.

## Yearbook

The trap entire, staged across the run.
The Paradox of Thrift is [the buffer](piece_the_buffer.md) each seeks by draining [the circular flow](piece_the_circular_flow.md), the [contraction](piece_the_contraction.md) that turns the thrift into [the shortfall](piece_the_shortfall.md), and the loss that only [the injection](piece_the_injection.md) can end, by a demand no private hand will be first to supply.
