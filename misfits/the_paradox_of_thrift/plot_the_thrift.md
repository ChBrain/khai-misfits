# Plot: The Thrift

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Household](place_the_household.md), hard times looming and a budget weighed.
The saver paring its spending to build a store, and for one home the sum plainly working.

## Action

[Saving](process_saving.md): [The Saver](persona_the_saver.md), holding [the saver's seat](position_saver.md), trims its spending and lifts the difference by, while [The Flow](persona_the_flow.md), holding [the flow's seat](position_flow.md), registers the coin lifted out of the circuit.

## Stage

[The Saver](persona_the_saver.md), having tabled [Build the Buffer](plan_build_the_buffer.md), spends less and saves more, and watches its own store grow, prudent and alone.
[The Flow](persona_the_flow.md), on [Carry the Coin](plan_carry_the_coin.md), carries onward only what is spent, so the withheld coin is already, unseen, a wage it will not pass to someone.

## Tension

The move is right, and its rightness is the whole trouble to come.
There is no error to find: for this household, spending less than it earns genuinely builds a buffer, and saving against hard times is simple prudence. But the coin set aside is a coin lifted out of the flow that pays every wage, so the very soundness of the thrift, once every household reaches for it, is what will bring the incomes down, and the plot closes with the saver richer and the circle beginning, unseen, to turn.
