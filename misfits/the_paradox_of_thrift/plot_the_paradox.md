# Plot: The Paradox

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Household](place_the_household.md), the belt tighter than ever and the store somehow no larger.
The nation's saving added up, and the incomes fallen to meet it, so the sum put by has not risen at all.

## Action

[Contracting](process_contracting.md): [The Flow](persona_the_flow.md), holding [the flow's seat](position_flow.md), shows the incomes fallen to the level of the spending, while [The Saver](persona_the_saver.md), holding [the saver's seat](position_saver.md), finds the buffer it tried hardest to build no bigger.

## Stage

[The Flow](persona_the_flow.md), having tabled [Carry the Coin](plan_carry_the_coin.md), delivers the verdict its circuit held all along: the incomes are the spending, so a nation that all spent less all earned less.
[The Saver](persona_the_saver.md), still on [Build the Buffer](plan_build_the_buffer.md), sees it saved a larger share of a smaller wage, the extra saving it meant to do thwarted by the fall its saving caused.

## Tension

Everyone tried harder to save, and the nation saved no more.
There is no culprit in the reckoning: each household did the prudent thing, saving a bigger fraction of its income. But the incomes fell to meet the general thrift, so a larger share of a smaller whole is no larger a sum, and the saving intended never materialised, and the plot lays the paradox bare, true of one household and false of all, the thrift defeating its own purpose through the very fall in income it set off.
