# Plan: Hold the Cash

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the hoard's seat](position_hoard.md), inside [The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Hoard](position_hoard.md)

## Direction

Keep the saving safe until it is wanted. Sit the money out of harm's way, wait for a borrower to put it to work, and rest, harmlessly, until confidence returns. Do nothing with it, which in ordinary times is fine.

## Orders

([The Hoard](persona_the_hoard.md) sits where the saving left it, finds no borrower in the slump, and drains the flow by resting when it should have been lent and spent.)

## Implementation

([The Vault](place_the_vault.md); the [leaking](process_leaking.md) process; the idle coin leaves [the circular flow](piece_the_circular_flow.md) as a leak and not a loan, deepening [the contraction](piece_the_contraction.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the saving is held safe and set aside to wait for a borrower
- [ ] no borrower comes, for no one invests into falling demand
- [ ] the idle coin sits as a leak from the flow, not a loan back into it
- [ ] the saving that might have funded investment pools and stalls
