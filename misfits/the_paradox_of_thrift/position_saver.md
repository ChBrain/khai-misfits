# Position: Saver

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The seat that trims its spending to build a store, sound for one household.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An income, and the choice of how much of it to spend and how much to keep.
A reason to save: hard times ahead, and the wish for a buffer against them.
No sight of the others' saving, only its own household to manage.

## Orders

Build the buffer.
Spend less, put more by, and live within the means the coming lean months allow.
Read saving as safety, because for a household it is.

## Loses

The safety, when every household saves at once.
Because its withheld spending is another's lost income, and that fall comes round as its own, the seat cannot secure itself by a move that, shared by all, shrinks the incomes it saves from.
It ends saving a larger share of a smaller wage, the buffer no bigger for the tightening.

## Drives

Toward a store built by spending less,
right for one household and ruinous for all,
so the prudence that would make it safe alone makes it poorer among the prudent.
