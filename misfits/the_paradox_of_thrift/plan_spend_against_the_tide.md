# Plan: Spend Against the Tide

## Taxonomy

## Direction

Put demand back into the flow when all others withdraw it. Invest with nerve, or run a deficit, and spend into the slump so the incomes return and the households' saving turns safe. Do, for the whole, the opposite of what looks prudent to each part.
