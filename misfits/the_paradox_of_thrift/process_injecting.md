# Process: Injecting

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The offset spending put into the flow when all others withdraw, the remedy applied at its cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A flow shrinking and one actor able to spend against the tide.
At [the treasury](place_the_treasury.md), the spender opening a deficit or investing with nerve, putting demand back where the saving took it out.

## Direction

Toward a flow refilled and the incomes returned.
The injected spending becomes someone's wage, which becomes their spending, so the injecting drives the circle back upward, offsetting the withdrawal the savers made and turning their thrift safe again, and it does so against the grain, adding demand exactly where every private hand is subtracting it.

## Lever

An actor willing to do the opposite of what looks prudent to each.
Because only spending offsets a shortfall of spending, and no private saver will supply it in a slump, the injecting works through the one purse that can run red on purpose, the treasury or the investor with nerve, so the lever is the nerve to spend and bear the debt when both feel most reckless.

## Echo

A demand restored and a debt taken on.
The flow refills, the incomes come back, the saving turns harmless at last, and the cost is a deficit run against every instinct and the look of folly borne for the one move the slump required, so what seems reckless is the recovery, bought by the actor willing to move against the tide.
