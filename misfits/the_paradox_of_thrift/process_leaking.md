# Process: Leaking

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The saved coin draining from the flow when no investment borrows it back.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A saving set aside and no borrower to spend it.
At [the vault](place_the_vault.md), the hoard resting idle while the slump keeps every investor from building into falling demand.

## Direction

Toward a withdrawal that is never returned.
A coin saved and lent would be spent by another and the flow would hold, but here the coin sits unborrowed, so the leaking drives the saving out of the circuit for good, a subtraction from demand rather than a source of it, the difference between a thrift that would be harmless and the one that bites.

## Lever

Low confidence at the moment saving is heaviest.
Because no one invests into a shrinking economy, the saved coin finds no borrower exactly when the most is being saved, so the leaking works on the timing of the slump itself, the hoard idle precisely when its spending is most needed, the loan absent when it would have done the most good.

## Echo

A saving that stalls instead of circulating.
The coin is safe and idle, the flow is a coin lighter with no spending to replace it, and the thrift that might have funded investment has instead drained demand, so what looks like harmless caution is the leak that turns a nation's saving into its shrinking.
