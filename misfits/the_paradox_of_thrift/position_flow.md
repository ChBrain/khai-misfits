# Position: Flow

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The seat of the circular flow, where each coin spent becomes another's income.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The whole circuit of income: every payment a wage, every wage a payment in turn.
A level set entirely by what is spent into it, no more and no less.
No store of its own, only what passes through.

## Orders

Carry the coin.
Pass each coin spent onward as someone's income, and return it as their spending after.
Feed every income out of the sum of all the spending, faithfully.

## Loses

Its fullness, exactly as the spending into it falls.
Because it carries only what is spent and never what is saved, the seat cannot keep an income flowing that no spending feeds, so when all withhold at once it has nothing to pass on, and the incomes it fed fall with the spending.
It is as full as the spending, and the saving is the part that never reaches it.

## Drives

Toward incomes that rise and fall with the spending alone,
holding a coin lifted out by one and losing a coin lifted out by all,
so it is the reason a household's thrift and a nation's are not the same act.
