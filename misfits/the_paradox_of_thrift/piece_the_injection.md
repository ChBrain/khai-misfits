# Piece: The Injection

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The offset spending put back into the flow, the remedy that refills the circuit at the cost of acting against every private instinct.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the treasury](place_the_treasury.md), where the deficit is opened and the demand put back.
Felt along [the high street](place_the_high_street.md), where the injected spending returns as someone's wage and the tills fill again.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play has no remedy, only a spiral that runs until it exhausts itself.
With it the misfit is answered, and answered at a price: spending put in where the saving took it out, refilling the flow and turning the thrift safe. The Injection is load bearing because it is the one correction the structure allows and the hardest to make (Keynes): only spending offsets a shortfall of spending, and no private saver will supply it in a slump, so the piece carries both the exit and its cost, a demand restored by an actor willing to run debt and bear the look of folly at the worst moment.

## Apparent

Recklessness, spending and borrowing exactly when caution seems most called for.
Seen against the general thrift, the injection looks like the one imprudent actor in a room of sensible ones, and the truth that it is the only move that ends the spiral, that its apparent folly is precisely the medicine the slump needs, stays hidden behind how right hoarding feels to everyone else at the time.

## Yearbook

Made by [the spender](persona_the_spender.md) through [injecting](process_injecting.md), the offset that refills the flow.
It is the exit and its cost: the Injection is what puts demand back into [the circular flow](piece_the_circular_flow.md), reverses [the contraction](piece_the_contraction.md), and closes [the shortfall](piece_the_shortfall.md), by the one act no private saver will be first to make.
