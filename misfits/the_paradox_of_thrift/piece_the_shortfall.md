# Piece: The Shortfall

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The thwarted saving, the nation saving a larger share of a smaller whole, the sum put by no greater than before.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the household](place_the_household.md), where the store fails to grow though the belt is tighter than ever.
Totalled across [the high street](place_the_high_street.md), where the fallen incomes show why the extra saving never materialised.

## Load Bearing

Without it the paradox would have no sting, only a general belt-tightening with no twist in the tail.
With it the finding lands: the households save a bigger fraction and, their incomes having fallen to meet it, save no more in sum, perhaps less. The Shortfall is load bearing because it is the paradox itself, quantified (Samuelson): the gap between the saving intended and the saving realised, the proof that thrift pursued by all defeats its own purpose, so the piece carries the exact way in which trying to save more can leave a nation saving the same or less.

## Apparent

A disappointment, savings that somehow did not grow despite everyone's care.
Seen from the ledger, the shortfall looks like a puzzle or a failure of effort, and the truth that the effort caused it, that the very act of all saving lowered the incomes that the saving was a share of, stays hidden behind how hard everyone plainly tried to put more by.

## Yearbook

Read across the flow through [contracting](process_contracting.md), the verdict [the buffer](piece_the_buffer.md) could not deliver on itself.
It is the paradox counted: the Shortfall is what becomes of [the buffer](piece_the_buffer.md) once [the circular flow](piece_the_circular_flow.md) carries the thrift into [the contraction](piece_the_contraction.md), and the loss that only [the injection](piece_the_injection.md), by returning the incomes, can undo.
