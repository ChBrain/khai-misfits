# Place: The Household

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). Where the saving is decided, one budget at a time, against the hard times ahead.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A budget weighed and a belt tightened: income on one side, spending pared on the other, the difference set by.
The plain arithmetic of one home living within its means, the store growing as the outgoings shrink, and no view from here of the thousand other homes doing the very same sum.

## Holds

The whole case for saving, seen from inside one budget.
Here the prudence is real and complete: for this household, spending less does build a buffer, so the household holds the truth that saving is safety, and holds it in the one frame, the single budget, where that truth is not yet false, the frame that cannot see the other homes at all.

## Offers

A safer household, and the confidence to tighten.
Because the budget balances and the store grows, the household offers each saver genuine reassurance that the move is sound, so it hands out, home by home, the private rightness that will sum across all homes to a public loss.

## Withheld

The other budgets, and the flow that joins them.
The household cannot see that its withheld spending is a neighbour's lost wage, because from inside one budget the circuit is invisible, so it withholds the only fact that would change the sum, that a move safe for one is ruinous for all making it at once.
