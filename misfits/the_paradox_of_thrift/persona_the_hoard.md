# Persona: The Hoard

## Taxonomy

[Hoard](position_hoard.md). The saved coin that finds no borrower, sitting idle as a leak from the flow, not a loan back into it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Still. Waiting. Sure it is doing no harm.
Is the money set aside, resting safe until it is wanted.
No greed in it: only cash held back, biding its time until confidence returns.

## Action

Sits where the saving left it, out of the flow and into no one's income.
Waits for a borrower to put it to work, and in a slump finds none, for no one invests into falling demand.
Drains, by sitting, the very stream the spending would have fed.

## Shadow

It is the difference between a saving that harms and one that does not: a coin saved and lent is spent by another and the flow holds, but a coin saved and hoarded is a leak.
Because low confidence leaves the saved coin unborrowed exactly when the saving is heaviest, the thrift that might have funded investment instead pools and stalls, so the hoard is where the withdrawal becomes a loss rather than a loan.
It means no harm and does it precisely by resting.

## Tell

Says "the money is safe, it is only sitting there."
And sits there, unspent and unlent, while the flow it left runs dry.
