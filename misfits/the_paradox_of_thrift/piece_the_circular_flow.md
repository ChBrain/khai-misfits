# Piece: The Circular Flow

## Taxonomy

[The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md). The circuit in which one agent's spending is another's income, the structure that makes a general thrift self-defeating.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the high street](place_the_high_street.md), where the coin passes from spender to earner and the circuit is visible.
Unseen from [the household](place_the_household.md), where the single budget cannot make out the circle it sits in.

## Load Bearing

Without it saving would simply be saving, a private matter with no public cost, and there would be no paradox.
With it the trouble is built in: because every coin spent is a coin earned, a coin withheld is a wage unpaid, so the flow is what turns one household's thrift into another's lost income. The Circular Flow is load bearing because it is the structure the whole finding rests on (Keynes): it is the reason a move true for one saver is false for all savers, spending and income being two names for the same coin, so a general withdrawal cannot leave the incomes untouched.

## Apparent

A background fact, the ordinary way money moves, nobody's concern.
Seen in normal times, the flow looks like a neutral plumbing that carries whatever is put through it, and the truth that it is the exact mechanism converting a nation's saving into a nation's lost wages, the fallacy of composition made of pipes and coin, stays hidden behind how unremarkable the circulation of money seems.

## Yearbook

Carried by [the flow](persona_the_flow.md) through [contracting](process_contracting.md), the circuit that spending fills and saving drains.
It is why one and all are different sums: the Circular Flow is what [the buffer](piece_the_buffer.md) is filled by draining, what turns a withheld coin into [the shortfall](piece_the_shortfall.md), and what [the injection](piece_the_injection.md) refills to undo [the contraction](piece_the_contraction.md).
