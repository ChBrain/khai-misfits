# Plan: Carry the Coin

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the flow's seat](position_flow.md), inside [The Paradox of Thrift](play_the_paradox_of_thrift.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Flow](position_flow.md)

## Direction

Pass each coin spent onward as someone's income, and return it later as their spending. Feed every wage out of the sum of all the spending, faithfully, and carry nothing that is not spent. Keep the circuit turning on what is put into it, no more and no less.

## Orders

([The Flow](persona_the_flow.md) carries each coin spent round as another's income, and passes on nothing that is withheld, so the incomes fall exactly as the spending does.)

## Implementation

([The High Street](place_the_high_street.md); the [contracting](process_contracting.md) process; [the circular flow](piece_the_circular_flow.md) carries only what is spent, so the withheld coin becomes [the shortfall](piece_the_shortfall.md) in someone's income.)

## Targets

- [ ] each coin spent is carried onward as another household's income
- [ ] nothing withheld is carried, so a coin saved by one still flows for the rest
- [ ] a coin saved by all flows for no one, and the incomes fall to match
- [ ] the circuit stays as full as the spending, and no fuller for the saving
