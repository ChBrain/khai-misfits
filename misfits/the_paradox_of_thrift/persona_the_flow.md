# Persona: The Flow

## Taxonomy

[Flow](position_flow.md). The circular flow of income itself, carrying each coin spent round as another's wage.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ceaseless. Circular. Indifferent to whose coin it carries.
Is not a purse but a current: every payment a wage to someone, every wage a payment in its turn.
Cares nothing for intent, only for what is put in and what is held back.

## Action

Carries each coin spent onward as someone's income, and returns it as their spending later.
Passes on, faithfully, whatever is put into it, and passes on nothing that is withheld.
Feeds every income out of the sum of everyone's spending, and shrinks the moment that sum falls.

## Shadow

It is why one household's thrift and a nation's thrift are not the same thing at all.
Because it makes each person's spending another's income, a coin lifted out by one is a coin still flowing for the rest, but a coin lifted out by all is a wage no one receives, so it turns a virtue done alone into a poverty done together.
It stays exactly as full as the spending poured into it, and no fuller for the saving.

## Tell

Says "every coin you do not spend is a wage someone does not earn."
And carries only what is spent, never what is saved.
