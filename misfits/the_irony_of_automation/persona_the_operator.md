# Persona: The Operator

## Taxonomy

[Operator](position_operator.md). The human left to oversee the machine, rusting and drifting, who must take over when it cannot.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Attentive at first. Freed from the routine. Set to watch.
Once did the work by hand and knew it in the fingers; now oversees a machine that does it better.
No fault in it: a professional relieved of drudgery and asked, sensibly, to keep an eye on the automation.

## Action

Watches the machine run the routine it used to run itself.
Loses, without noticing, the sharpness that daily doing had kept, and the focus that a demanding task had held.
Takes over when the rare case comes, and finds its hand cold and its attention elsewhere.

## Shadow

Not lazy, unpractised: it cannot keep a skill with nothing to exercise it, nor stay vigilant on a machine that never fails.
Because the automation removed both the practice and the demand, the operator's competence fades exactly as the system grows more reliable, so it is least ready at the one moment, the handover, when the system needs it most.
It is asked to be the expert backup to a machine that has quietly made it a novice.

## Tell

Says "the machine has it, I am just here to monitor."
Is, until the day it must fly by hand and finds it has forgotten how.
