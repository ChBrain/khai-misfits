# Plan: Watch and Wait

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the operator's seat](position_operator.md), inside [The Irony of Automation](play_the_irony_of_automation.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Operator](position_operator.md)

## Direction

Oversee the automation, trust it to run the routine, and stand ready to step in for the rare case it cannot handle. Be the expert backup: keep an eye on the machine, and take over when it hands control back.

## Orders

([The Operator](persona_the_operator.md) watches the machine run the work it used to run itself, and loses the sharpness and focus that daily doing had kept.)

## Implementation

([The Control Room](place_the_control_room.md); the [atrophying](process_atrophying.md) process; monitoring without doing breeds [the rust](piece_the_rust.md) in the hand and [the drift](piece_the_drift.md) in the attention.)

## Targets

- [ ] the operator oversees the automation and trusts it with the routine
- [ ] with nothing to practise on, the manual skill quietly fades
- [ ] with a system that rarely fails to watch, the attention drifts
- [ ] the expert backup is made a rusty, distracted one
