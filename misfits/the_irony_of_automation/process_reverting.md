# Process: Reverting

## Taxonomy

[The Irony of Automation](play_the_irony_of_automation.md). Control thrown back to the operator when the exception exceeds the machine, the worst case to the least ready hand.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A rare case beyond the automation's design and an operator who must take it.
At [the handover](place_the_handover.md), the exception exceeding the machine and the system suddenly the human's again.

## Direction

Toward command handed over at the worst possible moment.
When the automation reaches its limit it returns control to the operator, and because that operator has rusted and drifted, reverting drives the hardest problem in the system onto the least prepared hand, cold and out of the loop, at the instant the demand is highest.

## Lever

The automation's limit meeting the operator's unreadiness.
Because the machine hands back exactly what it cannot do, and the human it hands to has been eroded by the very reliability that preceded this moment, reverting works on the collision the calm arranged, the residue and the unready operator arriving together by design of the whole structure.

## Echo

A crisis met by a hand too cold to meet it.
The operator has the controls and the worst case at once, with no warm-up and no warning, so the erosion that was invisible through every quiet shift becomes, at the handover, the whole of the problem, the backup found wanting exactly when it is finally called.
