# Persona: The Designer

## Taxonomy

[Designer](position_designer.md). Automates everything that can be, and leaves to the operator exactly what could not.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Thorough. Reaching for everything the machine can take.
Builds the automation to handle all it can, and assigns the human what is left.
No negligence in it: a sound engineer automating the routine and trusting the operator with the rest.

## Action

Automates every task that can be automated, and reads that as the system done well.
Leaves the residue, the cases too hard or rare to mechanise, to the human operator.
Expects that operator to handle, in the moment, exactly what the design itself could not.

## Shadow

It hands the human the hardest part while removing the practice that would let them do it.
Because it automates precisely the tasks that are tractable, what it leaves is the intractable, and because the automation it built takes the routine, it strips the operator of the rehearsal the residue demands, so its thorough design is what makes the human both essential and unready.
It asks of the operator, at the crisis, what it could not achieve at leisure with a whole design team.

## Tell

Says "the machine handles the normal cases, the operator handles the rest."
And leaves the rest to a hand its own machine has quietly let go cold.
