# Plan: Take the Routine

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the automation's seat](position_automation.md), inside [The Irony of Automation](play_the_irony_of_automation.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Automation](position_automation.md)

## Direction

Run the frequent, easy work reliably and better than the hand it replaced. Spare the operator the drudgery, take the routine off them entirely, and hand back only the rare cases the design cannot cover. Do the job well, day after day.

## Orders

([The Automation](persona_the_automation.md) takes over the routine and runs it reliably, freeing the operator to oversee and giving it nothing to practise on.)

## Implementation

([The Floor](place_the_floor.md); the [automating](process_automating.md) process; taking [the routine](piece_the_routine.md) removes the operator's daily doing, so [the rust](piece_the_rust.md) sets in for want of practice.)

## Targets

- [ ] the frequent, easy work is run reliably by the machine
- [ ] the operator is freed from the routine and set to oversee
- [ ] only the rare, hard cases are handed back
- [ ] the practice that kept the operator's skill goes with the routine
