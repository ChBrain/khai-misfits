# Persona: The Exception

## Taxonomy

[Exception](position_exception.md). The rare, hard case the automation cannot handle, which falls to the human at the worst moment.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Rare. Difficult. Unlike the routine in every way.
Is the case the machine was never built for: the odd fault, the strange combination, the situation off the map.
No cruelty in it: only the residue, whatever was too hard or too infrequent to automate.

## Action

Arrives unannounced and exceeds what the automation can do.
Triggers the handover, throwing control to the human at the very moment the problem is hardest.
Presents, to a rusty and drifting operator, the single most demanding task in the whole system.

## Shadow

It is not merely a hard case, it is the hard case selected by the automation and saved for the least ready hand.
Because everything the machine could do it did, what remains for the human is by definition the difficult residue, delivered cold and in crisis, so the operator meets not an average problem well-rehearsed but the worst one with no rehearsal at all.
It is the exact task the automation left behind, arriving to the exact person it left unready.

## Tell

Says "here is the one thing the machine could not do."
And hands it to the human the machine made least able to do it.
