# Process: Training

## Taxonomy

[The Irony of Automation](play_the_irony_of_automation.md). The operator's hand kept in by deliberate drill, the remedy raised against the pull of efficiency.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An unreadiness foreseen and a will to keep the operator able.
At [the simulator](place_the_simulator.md), the trainer drilling the manual task and the rare case the automation no longer exercises.

## Direction

Toward an operator kept ready for the handover.
By manufacturing the practice the floor no longer supplies, training drives the skill and the engagement back up, so it works against the grain of the automation, restoring by deliberate effort the competence the machine erodes by simply running well.

## Lever

Practice supplied on purpose against the cost of supplying it.
Because only exercise keeps a skill and only engagement keeps attention, and the automation removes both, training works by paying for them directly, drilling and in-the-loop design bought at the expense of the efficiency the automation promised, held only as long as the paying continues.

## Echo

A readiness restored and never quite owned.
The operator is sharper and more engaged, ready for a handover that may not come, and the moment the drilling stops the automation idles the skill again, so what training achieves is real and provisional, a competence kept warm only for as long as its cost is met.
