# Position: Designer

## Taxonomy

[The Irony of Automation](play_the_irony_of_automation.md). The seat that automates all it can and assigns the human whatever is left.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The power to automate the tractable tasks, and the judgement of where the machine's reach ends.
A human operator to assign the residue to.
The expectation that the operator will handle, in the moment, what the design could not.

## Orders

Leave the rest.
Automate every task that can be automated, and hand the operator the cases that cannot.
Trust the human with the residue, and read the automated system as the job done well.

## Loses

The operator's readiness, exactly by automating so thoroughly.
Because the seat mechanises the tractable and leaves the intractable, what it assigns the human is the hardest part, and the automation it builds strips away the practice that part demands, so it cannot design a capable machine without leaving both the worst task and the least prepared hand.
It asks of the operator at the crisis what it could not achieve at leisure.

## Drives

Toward a machine that takes all it can and a human left the rest,
thorough in the automating and blind to the unfitting,
so the better the design, the harder the residue and the colder the hand it falls to.
