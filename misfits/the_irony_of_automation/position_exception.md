# Position: Exception

## Taxonomy

[The Irony of Automation](play_the_irony_of_automation.md). The seat of the rare, hard case the automation cannot handle, which falls to the human at the worst moment.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The residue: whatever was too hard or too rare for the machine to be built to handle.
The power to exceed the automation and force the handover.
The worst timing: it arrives suddenly, in an abnormal state, with the operator cold.

## Orders

Fall to the human.
Arrive as the case the machine cannot manage, and throw control to the operator.
Present the hardest problem in the system, at the moment least warmed up for it.

## Loses

Nothing, for it is the residue by construction and lands where it must.
Because the automation handled everything it could, the seat is by definition the difficult remainder, and it is delivered not to a rehearsed operator but to a rusty one in crisis, so it cannot be an easy case met well, only the hard one met cold.
It is the exact task the machine left behind, arriving to the exact hand the machine left unready.

## Drives

Toward the hardest case handed over at the worst time,
the residue of all the automation could not do,
so the human meets not an average problem prepared but the worst one unprepared.
