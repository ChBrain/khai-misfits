# Persona: The Trainer

## Taxonomy

[Trainer](position_trainer.md). Keeps the operator's hand in against the day of the handover, and pays for it in the automation's savings.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Deliberate. Forward-looking. Willing to spend on readiness.
Sees that the operator the automation left will one day have to take over, and works to keep them able.
No nostalgia in it: a considered choice to drill the skills the machine no longer exercises.

## Action

Drills the operator on the manual task and the rare case, on the simulator and in real spells at the controls.
Hands control back before the crisis, and designs the machine to keep its human engaged and in the loop.
Carries the cost of practising a skill the automation was meant to make unnecessary.

## Shadow

It keeps the one thing that answers the trap and pays for it in the very efficiency the automation promised.
Because practice costs the savings the machine was bought for, engages a human who is not strictly needed, and can never quite match the real rare case, the trainer's remedy always looks like waste on the days the machine does not fail, so it is forever pressed to cut, and can never fully close the gap.
It buys back a readiness the automation keeps eroding, and can never own it outright.

## Tell

Says "keep flying it by hand now and then, or you will not be able to when it counts."
Drills, at a cost, against a failure that may not come, to keep a hand the machine keeps cooling.
