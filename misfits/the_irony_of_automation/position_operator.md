# Position: Operator

## Taxonomy

[The Irony of Automation](play_the_irony_of_automation.md). The seat left to oversee the machine, its skill rusting and attention drifting, that must take over at the crisis.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A skill once kept sharp by daily doing, and now a machine that does the doing.
The task of monitoring a system that almost never fails, and the duty to take over when it does.
No practice to keep the hand in, and no demand to hold the attention.

## Orders

Watch and wait.
Oversee the automation, trust it to run the routine, and step in for the rare case it cannot handle.
Be the expert backup, ready when the machine hands control back.

## Loses

Both its skill and its focus, exactly by being reduced to a monitor.
Because it cannot keep a skill with nothing to practise on, nor stay vigilant on a machine that rarely errs, the seat fades on both counts as the automation runs well, so it is asked to be an expert backup while being made a rusty, distracted one.
It is least ready precisely at the handover, when the system needs it most.

## Drives

Toward a hand gone cold and an eye gone elsewhere,
unpractised and disengaged by the very reliability it oversees,
so the backup the system counts on is the one the system quietly unfits.
