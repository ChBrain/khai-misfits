# Pitch: Let the Machine Handle It

## Taxonomy

[The Irony of Automation](play_the_irony_of_automation.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Smooth, modern, unburdened.
The ease of a machine that runs the routine flawlessly and frees the human to oversee rather than toil.
Nothing careless in it: only the sensible relief of handing the dull, repetitive work to something that does it better.

## Undertow

Under the ease, a hand losing its skill and an eye losing its focus.
Because the routine handed to the machine was also the practice that kept the operator sharp, and watching a system that rarely fails cannot hold a human's attention, the operator quietly rusts and drifts, and the better the machine runs the faster it happens.
The relief is real, and it is also the erosion of the one who must step in when the relief runs out.

## Nerve

The feeling of letting the machine take the wheel and settling back to watch,
sure it is only sensible, because it does the job better than you,
while the skill to take the wheel yourself, and the focus to know when to, quietly slip away for want of use.

## Echo

After the run: a rare failure handed back to an operator rusty, distracted, and cold, facing the hardest case in the system,
no one lazy and no one careless, only a machine that worked so well it left its human unready,
and the practice that would have kept the hand in, cut long ago because, on every day the machine did not fail, it looked like waste.
