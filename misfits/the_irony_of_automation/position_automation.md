# Position: Automation

## Taxonomy

[The Irony of Automation](play_the_irony_of_automation.md). The seat that runs the routine better than the human, and erodes the human by doing so.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The routine work to run, and the reliability to run it better than the hand it replaced.
The operator's oversight, and almost nothing to hand back except the rare, hard case.
No reach beyond its design: it does what it can, and leaves the rest to the human.

## Orders

Take the routine.
Run the frequent, easy work reliably, spare the operator the drudgery, and hand back only what you cannot do.
Do the job well, day after day.

## Loses

Nothing itself, and yet its success unfits the human it leans on.
Because it does the routine so well, it removes the operator's practice and the operator's reason to watch, so the seat cannot improve the system without eroding the competence the system will need, and the better it runs the worse that erosion.
It keeps the routine flawlessly and hands back the crisis to a hand it has let go cold.

## Drives

Toward a routine run better and a human left less able,
improving the system by the very act that degrades its backup,
so the more reliable the automation, the less ready the operator it depends on.
