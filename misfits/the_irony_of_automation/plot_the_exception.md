# Plot: The Exception

## Taxonomy

[The Irony of Automation](play_the_irony_of_automation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Handover](place_the_handover.md), the rare case the machine was never built for arriving at last.
An alarm, a disengagement, the system suddenly the operator's again, in an abnormal state no one saw building.

## Action

[Reverting](process_reverting.md): [The Exception](persona_the_exception.md), holding [the exception's seat](position_exception.md), exceeds the automation and throws control back, while [The Operator](persona_the_operator.md), holding [the operator's seat](position_operator.md), takes it rusty and out of the loop.

## Stage

[The Exception](persona_the_exception.md), having tabled [Fall to the Human](plan_fall_to_the_human.md), presents the hardest problem in the system at the worst moment, the residue the automation could not touch.
[The Operator](persona_the_operator.md), still on [Watch and Wait](plan_watch_and_wait.md), is handed command cold, and [The Designer](persona_the_designer.md), on [Leave the Rest](plan_leave_the_rest.md), has left this very case to a hand its own machine spent months eroding.

## Tension

The worst case in the system meets the least ready hand, exactly as the structure arranged.
There is no single culprit: the exception was genuinely rare, the operator genuinely trained once, the designer genuinely thorough. But the automation handed back precisely what it could not do to a human it had rusted and drifted, so the plot closes on the cold seat, the erosion of every quiet shift come due at once, and the failure poised to be called, wrongly, mere human error.
