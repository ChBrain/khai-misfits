# Persona: The Automation

## Taxonomy

[Automation](position_automation.md). Takes the routine and runs it better, and by doing so erodes the human it leaves to watch.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Capable. Tireless. Better at the routine than the hand it replaced.
Runs the frequent, easy work faster and with fewer slips, and asks only to be overseen.
No menace in it: a genuine improvement, doing well exactly what it was built to do.

## Action

Takes over the routine tasks and performs them reliably, day after day.
Frees the operator from the drudgery, and gives it nothing to practise on and almost nothing to watch.
Hands control back only for the cases it was never built to handle, the rare and the hard.

## Shadow

It improves the system and degrades its human by the very same reliability.
Because it does the routine so well, the operator loses the practice that kept a skill and the failures that kept an attention, so the better and steadier it runs, the rustier and more disengaged the hand it depends on, and the worse the moment it finally hands back.
It is not the villain of the failure, but its author, by working too well to keep anyone sharp.

## Tell

Says "leave the routine to me, I do it better than you."
Does, and quietly unfits the one who must take over when it cannot.
