# Plot: The Change

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ground](place_the_ground.md), and human forces altering the land for their own reasons, faster than the lineage can re-learn.
The feature the cue reads left standing while the quality behind it is changed.

## Action

[Decoupling](process_decoupling.md): the ground is altered under [the cue's seat](position_cue.md), and [The Cue](persona_the_cue.md) goes on firing the same feature over the changed land.

## Stage

Following [Change the Ground](plan_change_the_ground.md), the land is mown or lit or drained or built, and [the cue](piece_the_cue.md) fires unchanged while [the quality](piece_the_quality.md) falls, so [the decoupling](piece_the_decoupling.md) breaks the link the signal rode.
The people who change the ground mean the animal no harm and mostly never think of it, and the cue keeps saying good over a place gone bad.

## Tension

Altering the ground for a human purpose is nobody's wrong, and it is what breaks the correlation the cue was built on.
There is no malice: each change is reasonable to the one who made it. But the change is faster than the lineage can re-read, so the plot closes with the ground gone bad and the cue still firing over it, the link severed and the signal unchanged, [the apparent fit](piece_the_apparent_fit.md) left high above lost fitness.
