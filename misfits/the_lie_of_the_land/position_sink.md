# Position: Sink

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). The seat that presents the good cue and takes in the settlers, where an altered site keeps the mark of quality while returning fewer than it draws.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The feature the cue reads and none of the quality behind it: the mark of a good place kept, the fitness that mark once carried lost.
A real draw: the site fires the cue as strongly as a true habitat, so it gathers the settlers a good place would gather.
No way to show its poverty, and no defence against the settlers it takes in, because everything a chooser can read still says good.

## Orders

Show well.
Present the mark of quality the cue reads, take in the settlers the cue sends, and hold them, because the feature survives and the settlers come to the feature.
Draw as a good site draws, since to every reading the site looks like one.

## Loses

Nothing of its own, and everything of the settlers it draws.
Because the site keeps the cue while losing the quality, it gathers settlers as strongly as the source and returns them worse than empty ground, so it fills by faithful choice, is preferred in a hard case over the good, and drains the settlers below the rate at which they replace themselves, its whole danger being that it looks, to the only reading available, like the best choice there is.
It gains the settlers a good site would gain, and loses them the fitness the true source would have kept.

## Drives

Toward the good cue shown and the settlers drawn,
the mark of quality kept over quality gone,
so the seat that shows well fills with the settlers the cue sends, and returns them below replacement while the source stands empty.
