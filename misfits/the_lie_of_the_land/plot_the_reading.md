## Cue

## Action

## Stage

[The Settler](persona_the_settler.md), on [Read the Sign](plan_read_the_sign.md), reads [the cue](piece_the_cue.md) as [the apparent fit](piece_the_apparent_fit.md) and prefers the ground it fires over, taking the signal for the quality it stands for.
The preference forms on the feature the settler can read, [the quality](piece_the_quality.md) behind it unweighed, because the cue was always ground enough to choose by.

## Tension

Reading the strong cue is the adaptive choice, and it is what keeps the settler from weighing the ground behind it.
There is nothing reckless: the reading tracked quality for as long as the lineage can remember. But the cue reads a feature and never a fitness, so the plot closes with the signal read and the place preferred, the settler sure of the choice and the ground beneath the cue still unexamined.
