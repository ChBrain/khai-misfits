# Persona: The Settler

## Taxonomy

[Settler](position_settler.md). The one who chooses where to settle by the cue its lineage earned, preferring the ground where the signal fires strongest, and takes the strong cue for the good place because it always was. The archetype of any organism inside the trap.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Competent. Well adapted. Sure of the reading.
Is any organism that must choose a place and choose it by a cue, the bird picking a nest site, the insect picking where to lay, the settler reading tall grass or open water or a sheen of light and moving toward it.
No recklessness in it: the Settler reads the cue exactly as its lineage was shaped to read it, and that reading, for a very long time, was the difference between more young and fewer.

## Action

Reads the cue, prefers the place where it fires strongest, and settles there, the way that always paid.
Takes tall grass for cover, open water for safe nesting, a bright wet glare for a pond, and moves to the strongest signal without weighing the ground behind it, because the cue has never before needed weighing.
Does what a settler must: it chooses habitat by the cue it can read, not by a quality it cannot, and trusts the cue because trusting it is what filled good habitat for ten thousand years.

## Shadow

Not a poor chooser, a faithful reader of a cue that used to be true, and that is the whole difficulty: the reading is adaptive, and a cue that tracked quality for so long is trusted exactly because it never failed.
Because the Settler can read the cue but not the quality, it cannot see that the ground has been changed under the signal, so it prefers the strong cue over the poor site as surely as it once preferred it over the good, settles in the sink, does everything right, and still returns fewer young than it takes, having chosen well by the only measure it had.
It reads the sign the way the sign had earned, and the earned trust is exactly what keeps it from seeing the ground has gone bad beneath it.

## Tell

Settles where the cue is strong and does not think to check the ground, because the cue was always ground enough.
Chooses the way its lineage always chose, and cannot tell a cue still firing from a promise still kept, so takes the strongest signal for the best place and prefers the sink to the source.
