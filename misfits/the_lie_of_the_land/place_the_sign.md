# Place: The Sign

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). Where the cue is read and the choice is made, the point at which the settler meets the signal and reads it the way the lineage earned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the cue is read and the settling begins, the moment of choice, the bird over the field, the insect over the surface, the settler weighing where to go by what it can see and smell and read.
The Settler reading the strong cue, the Cue firing over whatever ground it sits on, and the preference forming on the signal before the settler has set foot on the ground.

## Holds

The reading, and the quality it cannot reach.
Here the cue is met and weighed, so the Sign holds the point where the choice is made on the feature and never the fitness: a signal read exactly as the lineage was shaped to read it, taken for the quality it once tracked, so it is where the strong cue becomes the chosen place, and where the ground behind the cue goes unexamined because the cue was always ground enough to choose by.

## Offers

A place chosen and a reading trusted.
Because the Sign is where the cue is read and preferred, it offers a decision made and a signal followed, the settler moving to the strongest cue as its lineage always did, so it hands out the confidence of a competent reading, and hands it out on the feature and not the quality, the fitness of the ground left unread beneath a signal taken for the whole of it.

## Withheld

Any sight of the quality behind the cue.
The Sign shows the signal but cannot show the fitness, because the settler reads the feature and the feature is all the Sign presents, so it withholds whether the ground still keeps the promise the cue makes, letting a strong signal stand for a good place, and, all read cue and no read quality, it withholds the difference between a cue still true and a cue outlived until the settler has settled and the ground is lived on.
