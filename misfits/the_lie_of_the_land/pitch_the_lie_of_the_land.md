# Pitch: The Lie of the Land

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Faithful, well chosen, sure of the ground.
The plain good sense of an animal settling where every sign says good, reading the cue its lineage earned and choosing the way that always paid, the strong signal trusted because for so long it never failed.
Nothing false in it on its face: the reading is adaptive, the choice is competent, and the cue really did track quality for as long as the lineage can remember.

## Undertow

Under the trust, a cue decoupled from quality by change faster than the chooser can re-learn, still firing at full strength over ground that no longer holds what it once promised.
Because the signal fires as strong as ever, the poor site keeps every mark of quality the chooser reads, so the strong cue draws the settler into the sink and even past the good source, the site filling to below replacement by faithful choice.
What made the cue trustworthy, that it tracked quality for so long, is exactly what makes it dangerous once the ground is changed underneath it.

## Nerve

The feeling of settling where every sign says good, the cue strong and the choice sure,
certain the place is right because the reading has never failed the lineage before,
while the ground under it no longer holds what the cue promised, and the site returns fewer settlers than it takes, and the true source stands empty for want of a signal to fire.

## Echo

After the run: an attractive sink filled by faithful choice, the true source empty for want of a cue, each settler having chosen well by the only measure it had,
no lie told and no error made, only a signal that outlived the ground it tracked and a promise still spoken heard as a promise still good,
and the one move that would realign cue and quality, restoring the ground or dulling the signal before the draw fills the sink again, forgone because the site still looks right and the animals still choose it.
