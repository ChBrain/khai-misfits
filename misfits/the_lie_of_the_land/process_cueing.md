# Process: Cueing

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). The Settler reading the signal and preferring the place where it fires, the adaptive reading the lineage earned for tracking quality, turned now on ground the cue no longer tracks.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A cue to read and a lineage shaped to trust it.
At [the sign](place_the_sign.md), the settler meeting the signal and reading it the way that always paid.

## Direction

Toward the strong cue read and the place preferred.
Because the settler can read the feature and not the fitness, cueing reads the signal and prefers where it fires strongest, so the choice is made on the mark the animal can see and the quality behind it goes unweighed, the reading exactly the one that filled good habitat for as long as the lineage can remember.

## Lever

The long reliability of the cue.
Because the signal tracked quality for a very long time, cueing runs on a reading that was adaptive and earned, not on recklessness, so the preference is assembled from no carelessness, only from a chooser trusting a cue that never failed it before the ground was changed.

## Echo

A cue read, and a place preferred on it.
The settler has read the signal as clearly as its lineage was shaped to, so what reads as a competent choice is, in truth, a preference formed on the feature alone, the quality unread and the settler about to settle where the cue fires strongest.
