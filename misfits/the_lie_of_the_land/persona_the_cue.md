# Persona: The Cue

## Taxonomy

[Cue](position_cue.md). The environmental signal, once honest and now lying, that fires exactly as strong as ever over ground that has been changed beneath it, reporting the feature it always reported and no longer the quality it once tracked.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Constant. Reliable. Reporting as it always has.
Is the signal the Settler reads, the height of the grass, the shine of the water, the polarisation of light off a flat wet surface, the presence of a plant, whatever feature the lineage learned to trust.
No deceit in it: the Cue does not choose to fire, and it does not change its reading, it reports the same feature it has reported for as long as there has been anything to read it.

## Action

Fires over the ground it sits on, at full strength, reporting the feature it has always reported, whether or not the quality still follows.
Shows the height of grass over a field about to be mown, the shine of asphalt as if it were water, the plant that no longer feeds, exactly as it once showed cover and safety and food.
Does what a cue does: it reports a feature, not a fitness, so when the feature is left standing over changed ground, the Cue goes on saying good over a place gone bad, faithful to the reading and blind to the quality.

## Shadow

Not a liar, a signal that kept reporting the same feature after the ground it once tracked was changed, and that is the whole difficulty: the Cue is honest about the feature and can say nothing about the quality that the feature no longer delivers.
Because the Cue reports what it always reported, it cannot know the correlation it rode has been decoupled, so it fires as strong over the sink as over the source, and the very constancy that made it trustworthy for so long is what makes it dangerous now, a promise the ground used to keep still spoken by a signal that cannot tell the promise has gone bad.
It reports the feature faithfully, and the faithful report is exactly what carries the lie once the ground beneath it is changed.

## Tell

Fires the same over good ground and bad, and cannot mark the difference, because it reads the feature and never the fitness.
Says good as loudly over the sink as it once said good over the source, and cannot hear that the ground has stopped keeping the promise the signal still makes.
