# Plot: The Realign

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ground](place_the_ground.md), a site that draws well and returns poorly, and someone unwilling to leave it be.
The Steward reading the fitness the cue cannot reach and setting out to match the signal to it.

## Action

[Restoring](process_restoring.md): [The Steward](persona_the_steward.md), holding [the steward's seat](position_steward.md), restores the ground or dulls the cue so the signal tracks quality again.

## Stage

[The Steward](persona_the_steward.md), on [Match the Cue](plan_match_the_cue.md), works on [the decoupling](piece_the_decoupling.md) itself, mending the link so [the cue](piece_the_cue.md) tracks [the quality](piece_the_quality.md) once more and [the attractive sink](piece_the_attractive_sink.md) drains.
Against the fix stand a site that still shows every mark of quality, and settlers that still choose it willingly.

## Tension

Matching cue to quality is the one move that dampens the trap, and it asks a correction against no visible failure.
It alters good-looking ground, dulls a signal the settlers still follow, and holds the fix as new change lays new traps under the same cues. So the plot closes one of two ways: cue and quality realigned and the sink drained before it fills again ... or the fix waived as meddling with ground that looked perfectly good, the strong cue left firing over poor quality, and the draw left to fill the sink once more.
