# Process: Settling

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). The Settler moving to the strong cue and building in the site it fires over, the faithful settling that fills the sink and, in a hard case, passes the source.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A preference formed on the cue and a site that fires it.
At [the ground](place_the_ground.md), the settler arriving on the strength of the signal and settling where it is strongest.

## Direction

Toward the sink filled and the source passed.
Because the altered site fires the cue as strong or stronger than the true habitat, settling moves the settler to the sink and builds it there, so the poor ground fills by faithful choice and, where the cue is strongest over it, is preferred over the good ground, the source left open beside a sink that draws them all.

## Lever

The draw of the strong cue over the read of the quality.
Because the settler chooses by the feature it can read and not the fitness it cannot, settling runs on the pull of the signal, so the site fills from no error, only from a chooser going where the cue is strongest, exactly as its lineage was shaped to do when the cue was true.

## Echo

A site settled, and the return not yet in.
The settler has built where the cue fired strongest, so what reads as a place well chosen is, in truth, a nest in the sink, the settling faithful and the ground unable to keep it, the reckoning held off until the young are due and the site returns fewer than it took.
