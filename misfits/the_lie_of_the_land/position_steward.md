# Position: Steward

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). The seat that would put cue and quality back in step, restoring the ground or dulling the signal, at the cost of correcting against a site that still looks right.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A reading of the fitness the cue cannot reach and the standing to alter the ground: the sight of where signal outran quality and the means to change land or cue.
A real corrective: working on the correlation itself is the one thing neither the settler nor the cue can do from inside the trap.
No cheap way to do it, and no welcome for one who alters a site that shows every mark of quality and draws every settler willingly.

## Orders

Match the cue.
Restore the ground so the signal tracks quality again, or remove or dull the cue so it no longer fires over the sink, or buy the lineage time to re-learn, before the draw fills the sink again.
Put signal and fitness back in step, and treat a site that draws well and returns poorly as suspect for drawing well.

## Loses

The ease of leaving good-looking ground alone, and the standing of one who does not meddle.
Because the sink still fires the cue and the settlers still prefer it, the seat must change ground or signal with no visible failure to point to, find every altered place where the cue outran the quality, and hold the fix as new change lays new traps under the same cues, so the realignment is bought continuously and the sink it drains leaves no trace of the loss it spared.
It gains cue and quality realigned and the sinking spared, and loses the ease and the standing that leaving the ground alone would have kept.

## Drives

Toward the signal matched to the fitness and the sink drained,
at the cost of altering a site that still looks right and animals that still choose it,
so the seat that matches the cue puts ground and signal back in step, and is read as meddling with ground that looked perfectly good.
