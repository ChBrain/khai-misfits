# Place: The Ground

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). The altered land whose quality has changed while its cue has not, the sink where the strong signal now sits over poor fitness and the settlers gather.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the ground was changed under the cue, the mown hayfield, the lit shore, the drained marsh under a bright lawn, the tall verge beside the road, the flat wet asphalt shining like water.
The land altered by human forces for their own reasons, the feature the cue reads still standing, and the settlers arriving on the strength of a signal the ground no longer answers to.

## Holds

The changed quality, and the cue that outlived it.
Here the ground has gone bad beneath a signal that still fires, so the Ground holds the point where cue and quality have come apart: a feature kept over a fitness lost, the mark of a good place on land that is now a poor one, so it is where the strong cue draws settlers to the sink, and where everything a chooser can read still says good over ground that returns fewer than it takes.

## Offers

An attractive site and every mark of quality.
Because the Ground keeps the feature the cue reads, it offers a place that draws as strongly as a true habitat, the settlers gathering and the nests filling on the strength of the signal, so it hands out the pull of a good site, and hands it out over lost quality, the fitness gone beneath a cue that fires as loud as ever, the sink filling by faithful choice.

## Withheld

Any answer the cue can read to the promise gone bad.
The Ground shows the feature but cannot show the fallen quality, because the change altered the fitness and left the signal, so it withholds the poverty beneath the mark, letting a strong cue stand over a poor place, and, all kept feature and no kept quality, it withholds the reckoning until the settlers have built and the site has returned fewer than it drew.
