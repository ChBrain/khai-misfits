# Plot: The Sinking

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ground](place_the_ground.md), the settlers built in and doing everything right.
The nests laid, the eggs down, the young due, in a site that draws well and returns poorly.

## Action

[Settling](process_settling.md): the settlers live out the choice, and [the ground](place_the_ground.md) returns fewer than it took, the draw of [the sink](persona_the_sink.md) come due.

## Stage

Having settled by [Read the Sign](plan_read_the_sign.md) the site [The Sink](persona_the_sink.md) showed well, the settlers meet the reckoning: nests mown, eggs baked on the asphalt, young unfed where the plant no longer feeds, and [the quality](piece_the_quality.md) gone that [the apparent fit](piece_the_apparent_fit.md) still reported.
The site takes in more settlers than it returns, so [the attractive sink](piece_the_attractive_sink.md) draws the population below the rate at which it replaces itself.

## Tension

Each settler chose well by the only measure it had, and the site it chose is the one place that cannot keep it.
There is no fault in the settling: it was faithful and the reading earned. But the ground was decoupled from the cue, so the plot closes with the population drawn below replacement in the site it preferred, the reckoning arriving where nothing about the choice was wrong.
