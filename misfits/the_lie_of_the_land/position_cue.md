# Position: Cue

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). The seat that fires over the ground, where a signal that faithfully reports its feature goes on saying good after the ground it once tracked is changed beneath it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A feature to report and a long history of it tracking quality: the mark the lineage reads and the correlation that made the mark worth reading.
A real honesty: the signal reports the same feature it always reported, so its firing is fidelity, not deceit.
No knowledge of the quality behind the feature, and no way to tell the ground has changed, because a cue reads a feature and never a fitness.

## Orders

Fire as ever.
Report the feature over whatever ground it sits on, at full strength, whether or not the quality still follows, because the cue reports what it reads and cannot read anything else.
Say the same over good ground and bad, since to the cue the feature is all there is to report.

## Loses

The truth it used to carry, exactly by reporting the feature faithfully after the ground was changed.
Because the seat reports the feature and not the fitness, it cannot know the correlation it rode has been decoupled, so it fires as strong over the sink as over the source, and the constancy that made it trustworthy carries the lie, a promise the ground used to keep still spoken by a signal that cannot tell the promise has gone bad.
It gains a feature reported faithfully, and loses the quality the feature no longer delivers.

## Drives

Toward the feature reported and the quality assumed to follow,
the same firing over good ground and bad,
so the seat that fires as ever goes on saying good over a place gone bad, faithful to the reading it can no longer make true.
