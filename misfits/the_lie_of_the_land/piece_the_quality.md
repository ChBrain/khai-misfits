# Piece: The Quality

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). The true fitness value of a habitat, what the ground actually returns to the settler, the thing the cue once tracked and the settler cannot read.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Fallen at [the ground](place_the_ground.md), where the fitness has gone though the feature stayed.
Kept at [the source](place_the_source.md), where the fitness is real and the cue too quiet to draw.

## Load Bearing

Without it the cue reads onto nothing, and there is no good place or bad for the reading to hit or miss.
With it the trap has its measure: the true value of a site to the animal that settles it, the survival and the young it actually yields, which a good habitat delivers and a poor one does not, and which the cue is supposed to indicate (Robertson and Hutto, on the quality of a habitat as its fitness consequences, distinct from the cue an animal uses to assess it). The Quality is load bearing because it is what the trap divorces from the signal: the fitness that used to move with the cue and now does not, so the piece carries the thing the settler is really choosing and cannot see, the value behind the feature that the feature no longer delivers.

## Apparent

The obvious content of the cue, the good the strong signal seems to promise.
Seen at the surface, the Quality looks identical to the cue that stands for it, the fitness and the feature so long joined that no one reads them apart, and the truth that quality is a fitness the settler cannot directly read, that it can fall while the feature it once rode stays high, that a strong cue is not a kept promise, stays hidden behind how completely the signal used to carry the value it named.

## Yearbook

Tracked once by [the cue](piece_the_cue.md), lost under [the decoupling](piece_the_decoupling.md), read by no [settler](persona_the_settler.md) directly.
It is the measure of the trap: the Quality is the fitness the [ground](place_the_ground.md) no longer returns though [the apparent fit](piece_the_apparent_fit.md) stays high, the value the [source](place_the_source.md) keeps and the settlers pass, restored only when [restoring](process_restoring.md) matches the cue to it again.
