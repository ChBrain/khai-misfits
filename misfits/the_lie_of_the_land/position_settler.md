# Position: Settler

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). The seat that chooses where to settle by the cue, where a reading the lineage earned for tracking quality is turned on ground the cue no longer tracks.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A cue it can read and a lineage shaped to trust it: the strong signal in front of it and the adaptive preference for the place where it fires.
A real competence: the reading tracked quality for a very long time, so choosing by it is not recklessness but the thing that always paid.
No way to read the quality itself, and no sight of the change beneath the cue, because the cue was always ground enough to choose by.

## Orders

Read the sign.
Prefer the place where the cue fires strongest, settle there, and trust the signal, because the cue is the one thing the seat can read and it has never before needed checking.
Choose habitat by the cue, since to the settler the cue is the quality it stands for.

## Loses

The fitness it meant to choose, exactly by trusting a cue that used to carry it.
Because the seat can read the cue but not the quality, it cannot see the ground has been changed under the signal, so it settles where the cue is strong even when the quality is gone, prefers the sink over the source, and does everything right in a place that returns fewer young than it takes, the reading faithful and the ground beneath it bad.
It gains a place chosen by a signal that always paid, and loses the fitness the signal no longer tracks.

## Drives

Toward the strong cue read and the place settled,
the reading trusted because it never failed the lineage before,
so the seat that reads the sign chooses by the cue, and settles in the sink the cue still fires over.
