# Process: Restoring

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). The Steward putting cue and quality back in step, the costly realignment that restores the ground or dulls the signal so the trap can be dampened, worked against a site that still looks right.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A site that draws well and returns poorly, and someone unwilling to leave it be.
At [the ground](place_the_ground.md), the steward reading the fitness the cue cannot reach and moving to match the signal to it.

## Direction

Toward the cue tracking quality again and the sink drained.
Because the link between signal and fitness was broken, restoring works on the correlation itself, so it restores the ground until the cue is true over it again, or removes or dulls the cue so it no longer fires over the sink, or buys the lineage time to re-learn, the site made either genuinely good or no longer attractive.

## Lever

The willingness to alter good-looking ground with no visible failure to point to.
Because the sink still shows every mark of quality and the settlers still choose it, restoring runs only on someone choosing to correct against the look of the place, so the fix is exacted continuously and thanked rarely, the drained sink leaving no trace of the loss it spared and new change laying new traps under the same cues.

## Echo

The signal matched to the fitness, and the sink drained before it fills again.
The steward has put cue and quality back in step, so what reads as meddling with ground that looked perfectly good is, in truth, the one act that dampens the trap, the correlation mended while it can still be mended, the sinking that now never comes leaving nothing to thank the fix for.
