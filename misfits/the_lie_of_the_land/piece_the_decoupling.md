# Piece: The Decoupling

## Taxonomy

[The Lie of the Land](play_the_lie_of_the_land.md). The broken correlation between cue and quality, the link that human change severs faster than the lineage can re-learn, the fault line the whole trap runs along.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Made at [the ground](place_the_ground.md), where the fitness falls and the feature stays.
Closed, if ever, by [restoring](process_restoring.md), where the steward mends the link the cue rode.

## Load Bearing

Without it the cue tracks the quality, the reading stays true, and there is no trap at all.
With it the trap has its cause: the correlation the cue depended on broken by rapid, human-induced environmental change, so a signal that reliably indicated quality no longer does while it goes on firing exactly as before (Schlaepfer, Runge and Sherman, on human-induced rapid environmental change decoupling an established cue from the quality it evolved to track). The Decoupling is load bearing because it is the single break that makes an adaptive reading maladaptive: not a fault in the cue and not a fault in the chooser but the severing of the link between them, so the piece carries the exact place the trap is born, where the ground stops keeping the promise the signal still makes.

## Apparent

Improvement, use, development, ground put to human purpose.
Seen at the surface, the Decoupling looks like nothing gone wrong at all, land mown or lit or drained or built for reasons that had nothing to do with the animal, and the truth that this ordinary change severed a correlation a lineage was built on, that the cue now fires over a promise it can no longer keep, that the break is invisible to everything that reads only the cue, stays hidden behind how reasonable each alteration was to the ones who made it.

## Yearbook

Made by [the change](plot_the_change.md) through [decoupling](process_decoupling.md), running under the whole production.
