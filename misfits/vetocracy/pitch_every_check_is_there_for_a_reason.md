# Pitch: Every Check Is There for a Reason

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Procedural, responsible, proud of its safeguards.
The voice of a system that checks everything, reviews everything, and can point to the good reason behind each gate.
Careful, accountable, and sure that careful is the same as good.

## Undertow

Under the safeguards, a capacity to act quietly draining away.
Each check is a legitimate guard, and the sum of them is a wall no proposal can clear, since one hand can stop what all hands are needed to move.
The system keeps every protection and loses the power the protections were meant to make safe.

## Nerve

The feeling of watching a system defend its every check, each defensible on its own,
while nothing it needs to do can be done,
the paralysis built entirely of reasonable noes.

## Echo

After the run: no visible wreck, only the roads unbuilt and the reforms unpassed,
a cost paid in decisions that never got made,
and no one, ever, to blame for a thing that simply never happened.
