# Plan: Cut the Thicket

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the consolidator's seat](position_consolidator.md), inside [Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Consolidator](position_consolidator.md)

## Direction

Restore the capacity to act by cutting the veto points. Remove gates, gather the scattered power to decide into fewer, answerable hands, and trade many checks for a system that can move. Accept that you cannot spare only the vital checks, and cut anyway, because a system that cannot act is its own disease.

## Orders

([The Consolidator](persona_the_consolidator.md) cuts veto points and concentrates the authority to act, unable to tell a vital check from a mere obstruction, and so removing both.)
