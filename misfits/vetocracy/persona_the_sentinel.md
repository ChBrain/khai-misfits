# Persona: The Sentinel

## Taxonomy

[Sentinel](position_sentinel.md). The defender of the checks, who holds that each veto guards against unaccountable power.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Vigilant, historically minded, wary of any hand that wants fewer restraints.
Remembers why each check was built: a haste that harmed, a power that overreached, an interest once trampled.
Holds that the guards are the point, and their cost the price of safety.

## Action

Defends each veto as a legitimate check earned by a past abuse.
Warns that every gate the consolidator cuts is a protection removed.
Guards the restraints, and, in guarding them, guards the paralysis too.

## Shadow

Its case is true and is exactly what keeps the thicket standing: each check really does guard something, so defending them all defends the wall.
It cannot tell, any better than the consolidator, the check that still protects from the one that only obstructs.
The honest guardian of the restraints is the honest guardian of the gridlock.

## Tell

Says "remove that check and see what gets through next."
Right about the danger, and holding the door on the paralysis while it warns.
