# Process: Foregoing

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The decisions dying unmade, the cost accruing where nothing occurs.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A proposal that could not gather every yes, and a queue that never clears.
In [the limbo](place_the_limbo.md), the decision stalling for want of one consent and quietly ceasing to be pursued.

## Direction

Toward a cost paid in things that never happen.
Each proposal that cannot clear every gate is abandoned, so the road goes unbuilt and the reform unpassed, and the loss is real and total, borne entirely in the form of an event that does not occur.

## Lever

A cost that takes the shape of an absence.
Because a thing that never happened has no wreck and no author, the foregone leaves nothing to point to, so the loss cannot be charged to the system, which reads its own inaction as caution and never counts what the caution cost.

## Echo

A pile of unmade decisions no one weighs.
The road not built, the reform not passed, the call not made, each a total loss and an invisible one, and a system that pays its largest price exactly where it can see nothing at all.
