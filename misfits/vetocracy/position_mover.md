# Position: Mover

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The seat that would make a decision, and must assemble every yes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A proposal that needs to pass, and a duty to move it.
The whole burden of assembling consent from every veto-holder at once.
No power to act that any single veto cannot erase.

## Orders

Carry the proposal through the gauntlet.
Assemble every yes, gate by gate, paying tolls and softening terms.
Move the thing, against a system where one no is enough to stop it.

## Loses

The decision, most times, for want of a single consent.
It needs everyone and can be halted by anyone, so it bears alone the whole cost of an action any one veto can undo, and each concession it pays to win a yes leaves the proposal a little less worth passing.
The hand that would act pays, by itself, for a capacity the system scatters across every veto.

## Drives

Toward a decision made,
against an asymmetry that prices acting at every yes and blocking at one,
so the seat that carries the action carries the whole cost of a power anyone can veto.
