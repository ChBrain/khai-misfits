# Persona: The Vetoer

## Taxonomy

[Vetoer](position_vetoer.md). A holder of one legitimate veto, blocking to guard a real stake.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Conscientious, watchful, sure of its charge.
Holds a veto given it for a reason, and uses it to guard the thing it was given to guard.
Sees each block it casts as a duty done, not an obstruction.

## Action

Blocks the proposal that threatens its stake, one legitimate no among many.
Uses the veto mostly as intended, against haste or harm to what it protects.
Guards its own interest, and, in doing so, adds one more gate to the gauntlet.

## Shadow

Its veto is legitimate and its block is a duty, so it cannot see its no as part of a paralysis it never chose.
It guards one real stake and is blind to the sum of all such guards.
The most conscientious use of a single check is exactly what, multiplied, freezes the whole.

## Tell

Says "I'm only doing what this check is for."
And is right, one gate at a time, all the way to a wall.
