# Plot: The Gauntlet

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The gauntlet](place_the_gauntlet.md), a decision that must pass every check in turn.
The proposal enters a line of gates, each of which must open and any of which can close, and the mover starts down it.

## Action

[Paralysing](process_paralysing.md): [The Mover](persona_the_mover.md), holding [the mover's seat](position_mover.md), carries the proposal gate to gate, while [The Vetoer](persona_the_vetoer.md), holding [the vetoer's seat](position_vetoer.md), blocks it at its own gate to guard a real stake.

## Stage

[The Mover](persona_the_mover.md) tables [Get It Through](plan_get_it_through.md) and runs [the asymmetry](piece_the_asymmetry.md), needing every yes.
[The Vetoer](persona_the_vetoer.md) tables [Block to Protect](plan_block_to_protect.md) and casts one legitimate no, one gate in [the thicket](piece_the_thicket.md) that any proposal must clear.

## Tension

Any single hand can stop the thing, and no single hand can move it.
The vetoer is doing its duty, guarding a genuine stake exactly as its check was meant to let it, and the mover is doing its duty, trying to make a decision the system needs. But the costs are not equal: the block takes one no and a reason, the passage takes every yes at once, so the mover bears alone a burden any veto can erase, and the gauntlet is already tilting, gate by legitimate gate, toward the thing never passing at all.
