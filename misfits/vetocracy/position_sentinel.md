# Position: Sentinel

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The seat that defends the checks as guards against unaccountable power.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The memory of why each check was built: a haste that harmed, a power that overreached.
The true case that every gate removed is a protection removed.
No cleaner sight than the consolidator of which checks still protect.

## Orders

Guard the checks.
Defend each veto as a legitimate restraint earned by a past abuse.
Warn against every gate the consolidator would cut.

## Loses

The distinction that would let it defend the guards without defending the gridlock.
Its case is true, and each check really does guard something, so defending them all defends the wall, and it cannot tell the check that still protects from the one that only obstructs.
The honest guardian of the restraints is the honest guardian of the paralysis.

## Drives

Toward the checks preserved,
against a cut it cannot make cleanly either,
so the true defence of the guards against abuse is the true defence of the gridlock they became.
