# Place: The Limbo

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). Where the unmade decisions wait, and the cost is paid unseen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A queue that never clears: the road unbuilt, the reform unpassed, the call never made, each stalled for want of one consent.
No wreck, no headline, only a pile of proposals that died quietly and a system that never notices the weight of it.

## Holds

The foregone, whole.
Here every decision that could not assemble every yes comes to rest, so the limbo holds the true cost of the paralysis, a sum of things that never happened, present only as an absence.

## Offers

The appearance of a system that made no mistakes.
Nothing here failed loudly, nothing wrecked, so the limbo offers a clean record, a caution that reads as prudence because its cost took the form of events that never occurred.

## Withheld

Any ledger that counts what was foregone.
The limbo holds only absences, and an absence has no author and no line item, so it withholds the accounting that would charge the system for its inaction, and the largest cost stays the one thing no one can see.
