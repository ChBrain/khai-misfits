# Persona: The Consolidator

## Taxonomy

[Consolidator](position_consolidator.md). The one who would cut the veto points and concentrate the authority to act.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Impatient with the thicket, fixed on restoring the capacity to act.
Wants fewer gates, clearer lines, an authority that can decide and be answerable for it.
Sees paralysis as the disease and consolidation as the cure.

## Action

Cuts veto points and gathers the scattered power to act into fewer hands.
Trades many checks for a capacity to decide.
Restores motion, and, in the same stroke, removes guards.

## Shadow

Its cure for a system that cannot act is a step toward one that cannot be stopped, since the power it consolidates is the power the checks restrained.
It cannot cleanly tell a veto that is a vital check from one that is mere obstruction, so it cuts both together.
The hand that frees the system to act is the hand that loosens what the vetoes were holding.

## Tell

Says "someone has to be able to just decide."
Which is true, and is the first sentence of every unchecked power.
