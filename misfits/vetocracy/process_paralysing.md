# Process: Paralysing

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The many legitimate checks compounding, through the asymmetry, into an inability to act.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A thicket of gates, and a proposal that must clear every one.
In [the gauntlet](place_the_gauntlet.md), the mover assembling yeses while any single no can end it.

## Direction

Toward a system that can be stopped but not moved.
Blocking takes one veto and a reason; acting takes every veto-holder at once; so as the checks multiply, the cost of moving climbs with each gate while the cost of stopping stays at one, and the equilibrium settles into inaction.

## Lever

An asymmetry between the price of no and the price of yes.
Because one hand can block and all hands are needed to act, the many small legitimate checks do not merely add but compound, so the paralysis is emergent, produced by the structure and chosen by none of the hands inside it.

## Echo

A capacity to act drained away.
The gates each defensible and the whole impassable, the mover bearing alone a cost any veto can undo, and a system frozen not by any decision but by the sum of its own protections.
