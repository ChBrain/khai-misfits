# Persona: The Mover

## Taxonomy

[Mover](position_mover.md). The one trying to make a decision, who must assemble every yes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Determined, weary, carrying a proposal that needs to pass.
Wants to build the road, pass the reform, make the call, and has to get every gate to open.
Feels the asymmetry in its bones: one no stops it, and no single yes moves it.

## Action

Carries the proposal through the gauntlet of checks.
Assembles consent gate by gate, paying tolls and softening terms to win each yes.
Watches most proposals die for want of a single one.

## Shadow

It needs everyone and can be stopped by anyone, so it bears the whole cost of an action that any one veto can erase.
It can bend the proposal to buy assent, but each bend is a price, and enough prices leave a thing not worth passing.
The hand that would act is the hand that pays, alone, for a capacity the system spreads across every veto.

## Tell

Says "I just need one more yes."
In a place where one more yes is the whole distance, every time, to the end.
