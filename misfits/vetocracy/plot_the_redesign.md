# Plot: The Redesign

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The redesign](place_the_redesign.md), a move to restore the capacity to act.
The proposal is to cut veto points and gather the scattered power to decide into fewer, answerable hands.

## Action

[Consolidating](process_consolidating.md): [The Consolidator](persona_the_consolidator.md), holding [the consolidator's seat](position_consolidator.md), would cut the thicket and concentrate authority, while [The Sentinel](persona_the_sentinel.md), holding [the sentinel's seat](position_sentinel.md), warns what each cut removes.

## Stage

[The Consolidator](persona_the_consolidator.md) tables [Cut the Thicket](plan_cut_the_thicket.md) and answers [vetocracy](piece_vetocracy.md) by removing veto points, unable to spare only the obstructions.
[The Sentinel](persona_the_sentinel.md) tables [Guard the Checks](plan_guard_the_checks.md), holding that the power freed to act is the power [the guard](piece_the_guard.md) restrained.

## Tension

The one move that lets the system act is a step toward a system that cannot be stopped.
Cutting the thicket is the only cure for the paralysis, and it works: fewer gates, an authority that can decide. But the power it consolidates is the very power the checks were built to restrain, and because a vital guard and a mere obstruction wear the same face, the cut that frees the system also loosens what the vetoes held. It closes with the capacity to act restored, or the checks held and the gridlock kept, or a real guard cut with the obstruction and the power it held now loose.
