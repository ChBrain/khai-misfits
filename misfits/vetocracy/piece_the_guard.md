# Piece: The Guard

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). What each veto legitimately protects, indistinguishable from mere obstruction.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the redesign](place_the_redesign.md), where the remedy must weigh it.
In every check's true purpose: a haste to prevent, a power to restrain, an interest to shield.

## Load Bearing

Without it the cure would be simple: cut the veto points and free the system.
With it the cure is a hazard: the guard is the real protection each veto affords, against overreach, against a trampled minority, against unaccountable power, so removing the checks to end the paralysis removes the safeguards too. The Guard is load bearing because it is what makes the remedy dangerous and the misfit honest (Fukuyama on the balance of capacity and accountability): the power a consolidator would free to act is the power the checks were built to restrain, and because a vital guard and a mere obstruction wear the same face, no one can cut the gridlock without risking the protections it is made of.

## Apparent

Either dead weight to clear or sacred restraint to defend, depending who is asked.
To the consolidator it can look like all obstruction to cut; to the sentinel, all vital guard; and both are wrong, because it is genuinely a mix with no clean seam, so the guard reads as one or the other, and the truth that the check that protects and the veto that only obstructs are the same act stays hidden behind whichever case is being pressed.

## Yearbook

Defended by [the sentinel](persona_the_sentinel.md) within [consolidating](process_consolidating.md), the peril [Cut the Thicket](plan_cut_the_thicket.md) must cut around.
It is the protection tangled in the gridlock: the Guard is why removing [the veto](piece_the_veto.md) is never safe, the reason answering [vetocracy](piece_vetocracy.md) cannot be done with a clean blade.
