# Place: The Gauntlet

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The run a proposal must make through every gate to pass.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A proposal moving down a line of checks, each of which must open and any of which can close.
The mover paying tolls and softening terms at each, the holdouts charging for their yeses, the thing surviving only if every gate lets it through.

## Holds

The asymmetry and the extraction.
Here acting is priced at every yes and blocking at a single no, and here a held yes is sold for a concession, so the gauntlet holds both the imbalance that makes stopping cheap and moving dear, and the tolls that bend a proposal to survive it.

## Offers

A thorough vetting of anything that would pass.
Every proposal is examined at every gate, so the gauntlet offers a filter that nothing ill-considered clears without answering each check, a real scrutiny bought with each pass.

## Withheld

A price on acting that anyone but the mover shares.
The gauntlet loads the whole cost of assembling every yes onto the one hand that would act, so it withholds any sharing of that burden, and lets a single no undo, for free, what all the yeses were needed to build.
