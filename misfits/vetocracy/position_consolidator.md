# Position: Consolidator

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The seat that would cut the veto points and gather the authority to act.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The reading that the paralysis is the disease and the checks are its cause.
The move to remove veto points and concentrate the scattered power to decide.
No way to cut only the obstructions and spare the vital checks.

## Orders

Cut the thicket.
Remove veto points and gather the authority to act into fewer, answerable hands.
Trade many checks for a capacity to decide.

## Loses

The guards, cut together with the gridlock.
Its cure for a system that cannot act is a step toward one that cannot be stopped, since the power it consolidates is the power the checks restrained, and it cannot tell a vital check from a mere obstruction, so it removes both.
The hand that frees the system to act is the hand that loosens what the vetoes were holding.

## Drives

Toward a restored capacity to act,
by removing the restraints that also restrain abuse,
so the cure for paralysis is the first step toward the unchecked power the checks were built to stop.
