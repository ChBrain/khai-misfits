# Piece: The Veto

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The single power to block, legitimate, guarding a real stake or check.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the chamber](place_the_chamber.md), where it is held and cast.
The one hand's no over any proposal that touches its charge.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no block, and no paralysis to build from blocks.
With it the system has its atom: the veto is one hand's legitimate power to stop, given for a reason, to guard against haste or overreach or a trampled interest, and mostly used exactly as meant. The Veto is load bearing because its legitimacy is the whole trap (Tsebelis on veto players): it is not a flaw to be removed but a guard to be kept, so each one is defensible on its own, and the misfit is that a thing defensible one at a time becomes, in sum, a wall.

## Apparent

Simply accountability, doing its job.
A check that lets a stakeholder stop a harmful proposal looks like exactly the safeguard any careful system should have, so the veto reads as pure protection, and the truth that it is also a gate any proposal must clear, one of many that together freeze the whole, stays hidden behind how plainly right a single check is.

## Yearbook

Cast by [the vetoer](persona_the_vetoer.md) through [vetoing](process_vetoing.md), tabled as [Block to Protect](plan_block_to_protect.md).
It is the legitimate block the misfit is built from: the Veto joins every other into [the thicket](piece_the_thicket.md), and, priced against action, powers [the asymmetry](piece_the_asymmetry.md).
