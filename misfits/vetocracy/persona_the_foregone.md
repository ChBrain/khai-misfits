# Persona: The Foregone

## Taxonomy

[Foregone](position_foregone.md). The decision never made, the good that never happens, the invisible cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Absent, uncounted, present only as a lack.
The road unbuilt, the reform unpassed, the call never made, waiting in a queue that never clears.
Leaves no wreck, only a nothing where a something should have been.

## Action

Fails to assemble every yes, and so never comes to be.
Joins a pile of proposals that died for want of one consent.
Costs the system exactly what it would have been worth, and shows up nowhere.

## Shadow

It is the whole cost of the paralysis and the one cost no one sees, because a thing that never happened has no author and no funeral.
It cannot be pointed to, so the system that killed it is never charged for it, and reads its own caution as prudence.
The largest price is paid where nothing visible occurs.

## Tell

Says nothing, because it never arrived.
And is mourned by no one, because no one was ever shown what it was.
