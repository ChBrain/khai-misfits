# Position: Vetoer

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The seat of one legitimate veto, guarding a real stake.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A veto, given for a reason, over any proposal touching its stake.
A duty to guard the thing the veto was made to guard.
A view of its own charge, and none of the sum of all the charges.

## Orders

Guard your stake.
Block the proposal that would harm it, as the check was designed to let you.
Use the veto as intended, one legitimate no.

## Loses

Nothing on its own terms, and the system's capacity to act on all of them.
Its veto is legitimate and its block a duty, so it cannot see its no as part of a paralysis it never chose, guarding one real stake while blind to the sum of such guards.
The most conscientious use of a single check is what, multiplied, freezes the whole.

## Drives

Toward its own stake guarded,
against no fault it can see in itself,
so the dutiful use of one legitimate veto becomes, in the aggregate, the wall that stops everything.
