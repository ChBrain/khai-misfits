# Piece: The Foregone

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The decision never made, the good that never happens, the cost paid as an absence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the limbo](place_the_limbo.md), where the unmade decisions rest.
In the road unbuilt, the reform unpassed, the call never made.

## Load Bearing

Without it the paralysis would be a visible failure someone could be charged for.
With it the cost hides where no one looks: the foregone is the decision that could not assemble every yes and so never came to be, a total loss borne entirely as a thing that did not occur. The Foregone is load bearing because it is why vetocracy persists unpunished (the inaction that leaves no wreck): a thing that never happened has no author, no headline, and no line in any ledger, so the system's largest cost is the one it is structurally unable to see, and its paralysis reads, from the inside, as prudence.

## Apparent

A clean record, and a system that made no mistakes.
Where nothing was built, nothing collapsed, and nothing wrecked can be blamed, so the foregone reads as a cautious system's spotless account, and the truth that the empty space is the cost, that the reform not passed and the road not built were the price of the gridlock, stays hidden behind the absence of any visible failure.
