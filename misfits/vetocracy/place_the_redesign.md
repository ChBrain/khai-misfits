# Place: The Redesign

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). Where the remedy is weighed: cut the checks and concentrate authority, or guard the restraints.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A move to restore the capacity to act: the consolidator proposing to remove veto points and gather the power to decide.
The sentinel across the table, naming what each check was built to guard, and warning that the power freed to act is the power the checks restrained.

## Holds

The remedy and its danger.
Here the thicket can be cut so the system can decide again, and here that cut removes guards and concentrates the very power the vetoes held back, so the redesign holds both the one move that ends the paralysis and the abuse that move risks unleashing.

## Offers

A way to make the system able to act.
By removing veto points and gathering authority, the redesign offers the recovered capacity to decide, the cure for a system that can be stopped but never moved.

## Withheld

A blade that cuts the obstruction and spares the check.
The redesign cannot tell a veto that still protects from one that only obstructs, so it withholds a clean cut, and can restore the power to act only by loosening, in the same stroke, the restraints on its abuse.
