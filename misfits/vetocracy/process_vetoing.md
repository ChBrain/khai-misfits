# Process: Vetoing

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). Each legitimate block cast, whether to guard a stake or to extract a price.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A proposal that touches many stakes, and many hands each holding a check.
In [the chamber](place_the_chamber.md), the vetoer guarding its charge and the holdout weighing its yes.

## Direction

Toward a wall of legitimate noes.
Each veto-holder blocks what threatens its stake or withholds assent until paid, so the checks are cast one by one, each defensible, and accrete into the thicket every proposal must clear.

## Lever

A veto that is legitimate however it is used.
Because each block guards something real, the sincere guard and the strategic hold defend the same stake and cannot be told apart, so the vetoing needs no bad faith, only many hands each doing, defensibly, what its check allows.

## Echo

A thicket of reasonable noes.
Each stake guarded, each toll defensible, and a maze of gates assembled that no single hand built and no proposal can pass.
