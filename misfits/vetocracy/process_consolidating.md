# Process: Consolidating

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The move to cut veto points and gather authority, and the guard it removes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A recognition that the checks have summed to paralysis, and a will to restore the capacity to act.
At [the redesign](place_the_redesign.md), the consolidator proposing to cut gates and the sentinel defending each.

## Direction

Toward a system that can decide again.
Veto points are removed and the scattered power to act is gathered into fewer, answerable hands, so decisions can be made once more, and, in the same motion, guards come down and power collects where the checks once restrained it.

## Lever

A cut that cannot spare only the obstructions.
Because a vital check and a mere obstruction wear the same face, removing veto points to free the system removes protections too, so the cure for a body that cannot act is a step toward one that cannot be stopped, bought by loosening what the vetoes held.

## Echo

Motion restored, and a restraint let go.
The system able to decide again, or the checks defended and the gridlock kept, or a vital guard cut with the obstruction and the power it held now loose, the remedy ending in capacity regained or a protection lost.
