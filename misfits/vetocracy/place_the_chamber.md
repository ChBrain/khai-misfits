# Place: The Chamber

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). Where the veto-holders sit, each with a legitimate no.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A room full of seats, each with a check earned by some past reason, each able to stop a thing.
The vetoers casting their legitimate noes and the holdouts weighing what their yeses are worth.

## Holds

The veto and the thicket.
Here each single legitimate block lives, and here they accrete into a maze of gates, so the chamber holds both the reasonable no of one seat and the impassable sum of them all, the check and the thicket it belongs to.

## Offers

A guard at every stake.
Each seat protects something real against haste, overreach, or a trampled interest, so the chamber offers a wall of legitimate protections, an accountability posted at every point a decision could go wrong.

## Withheld

Any hand that can say yes for all.
The chamber is built to let each seat block and none to move the whole, so it withholds the capacity to act, and offers, to any proposal, only a row of gates that each must open and any can keep shut.
