# Piece: Vetocracy

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The phenomenon whole: legitimate checks summed into an inability to act, and its remedy in consolidation at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the redesign](place_the_redesign.md), where the thicket is cut or the gridlock holds.
And across the whole system, in every gate a decision must clear and any hand can shut.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of noes and stalled proposals with no shape.
With it the moves become one law: the veto, the thicket, the asymmetry, the extraction, the foregone, and the guard are facets of a single mechanism, in which many legitimate checks, each guarding something real, compound through the asymmetry between blocking and acting into a system that can prevent anything and accomplish nothing, at a cost paid invisibly in decisions never made. Vetocracy is load bearing because it is the whole finding with its whole remedy attached (Fukuyama): the only answer is to cut veto points and concentrate the authority to act, a consolidation that removes the guards and looses the power the checks restrained, with no clean line between the check that protects and the veto that only obstructs.

## Apparent

Either a wisely cautious system or a hopelessly broken one.
Seen from one side it is prudence, every safeguard in place; from the other, it is dysfunction, unable to do anything; and both miss the structure, that each check is legitimate and the paralysis emergent and the cost invisible, so neither praise of the safeguards nor scorn for the gridlock answers it, only cutting the checks at the risk of the power they held, which is the one move that trades a system that cannot act for one that cannot be stopped.

## Yearbook

Answered by [the consolidator](persona_the_consolidator.md) through [consolidating](process_consolidating.md), held whole by nobody.
It is the play in a single piece: Vetocracy is what [the thicket](piece_the_thicket.md) of legitimate vetoes becomes once [the asymmetry](piece_the_asymmetry.md) prices action out, undone only by cutting the checks, at the cost of loosing what [the guard](piece_the_guard.md) restrained.
