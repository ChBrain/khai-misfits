# Persona: The Holdout

## Taxonomy

[Holdout](position_holdout.md). The vetoer that withholds its assent to extract a price.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Shrewd, patient, aware of what its yes is worth.
Holds a veto and knows that a proposal needing every yes will pay for its.
Frames the price as protecting its stake, and, mostly, it is.

## Action

Withholds assent until a concession is paid.
Trades its veto rather than merely casting it, bending the proposal to its terms.
Extracts a toll, and defends a genuine interest in the same motion.

## Shadow

Its leverage is real and its stake is real, so its toll is not extortion but a defensible price, and that is what makes it impossible to forbid.
It uses the veto strategically and still guards something legitimate, so cutting out the cynic would not end the paralysis the sincere vetoer also builds.
The strategic hold and the honest guard defend the same stake, and cannot be told apart.

## Tell

Says "I'll say yes, once you've addressed my concern."
Of a concern that is real, and a price that is the point.
