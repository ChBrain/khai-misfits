# Position: Holdout

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The seat that withholds its assent to extract a price.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A veto, and the knowledge that a proposal needing every yes will pay for its.
A genuine stake, and the leverage to charge for releasing it.
A price framed as a concern, and mostly resting on a real one.

## Orders

Withhold assent until the price is paid.
Trade the veto rather than merely cast it, and bend the proposal to your terms.
Extract the toll, and defend your stake in the same motion.

## Loses

Its distinguishability from the honest guard.
Its leverage is real and its stake is real, so its toll is a defensible price and not extortion, which is exactly what makes it impossible to forbid, and cutting the cynic out would not end the paralysis the sincere vetoer also builds.
The strategic hold and the honest guard defend the same stake and cannot be told apart.

## Drives

Toward the price,
extracted behind a concern that is genuinely held,
so the veto spent as leverage wears the same face as the veto held as duty.
