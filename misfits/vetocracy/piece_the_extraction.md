# Piece: The Extraction

## Taxonomy

[Vetocracy](play_vetocracy.md). The veto spent as leverage: assent withheld until a price is paid.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the gauntlet](place_the_gauntlet.md), where the mover meets the toll.
In the yes that will not come until a concession does.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be pure blockage, with nothing bending what does pass.
With it the paralysis also corrupts what survives it: the extraction is the veto held as a bargaining chip, assent withheld until the proposal is bent to pay for it, so the few things that clear the gauntlet arrive laden with tolls. The Extraction is load bearing because it shows the veto need not even be cast to do harm (the holdout's leverage): its mere threat prices every yes, and because the holdout defends a genuine stake, the toll is a defensible price and cannot be forbidden, so removing the cynic would not free the system the sincere vetoer also binds.

## Apparent

Fair negotiation over a real concern.
A stakeholder trading its assent for a concession looks like ordinary bargaining, each party protecting its interest, so the extraction reads as reasonable give-and-take, and the truth that the leverage comes from a structural power to halt everything, that the concern is real and the price is the point, stays hidden behind how normal a negotiated yes appears.

## Yearbook

Worked by [the holdout](persona_the_holdout.md) through [vetoing](process_vetoing.md), tabled as [Extract the Price](plan_extract_the_price.md).
It is the veto turned to leverage: the Extraction trades a single [veto](piece_the_veto.md) for a concession, bending what little clears [the asymmetry](piece_the_asymmetry.md) to the tolls it had to pay.
