# Place: The Court

## Taxonomy

[Only the Close Ones](play_only_the_close_ones.md). Where the toss-ups are tried and the record is made, the visible stage that holds only the cases no settlement could resolve, its verdicts the sample the reader counts.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Cases decided in the open, verdicts entered and recorded.
Shown as the whole of the evidence: the trials are public and their outcomes plain, so the Court looks like the place where the disputes are resolved and the record of them made, the natural body of data to read.

## Holds

Holds only the cases that would not settle.
The Court is where the disagreements land: because a case reaches trial only when the two sides could not agree on its outcome, what is decided here is the set of genuine uncertainties, so the Court holds the toss-ups and none of the clear cases, its docket the residue of the filter and its verdicts the split of the close calls.

## Offers

Offers a full and open record, and a sample selected before it arrived.
Here every trial is visible and every verdict countable, so the Court offers a clean and complete body of outcomes, and by holding only the cases that could not settle it offers a sample chosen on the very outcome it records, complete as a record of trials and unrepresentative as a sample of the disputes.

## Withheld

Withholds that its completeness is a selection.
What the Court hides is that being a full record of trials is not being a sample of disputes, so its very completeness misleads: the reader sees every decided case and takes the set for the whole, not seeing that the whole was defined by exclusion at the Table, the Court's open and total record concealing that it is the total only of what could not be settled.
