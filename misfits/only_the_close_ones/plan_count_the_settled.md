# Plan: Count the Settled

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Estimate](position_the_estimate.md), inside [Only the Close Ones](play_only_the_close_ones.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Estimate](position_the_estimate.md)

## Direction

Read the trial record as the residue of a filter, not a sample of the world, and ask what settled before asking what the verdicts show, modelling the selection that chose the tried cases (Heckman). Correct the win rate for that selection, discounting the near-even rate as the sample's and estimating the merits from the disputes rather than the leftover, reconstructing the settled population from the settlement rate and whatever terms leak. Accept that the answer must rest on the cases you cannot observe, since settlements are private and unrecorded, so the correction is truer and less certain than the plain count, and offer the honest range rather than the false number.

## Orders

([The Realist](persona_the_realist.md) treats the tried cases as the ones that would not settle, works backward to the shape of the disputes that never reached a verdict, and trades the firm wrong answer for the provisional right one.)
