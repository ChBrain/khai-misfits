# Position: The Settlement

## Taxonomy

[Only the Close Ones](play_only_the_close_ones.md). The filtering seat, which resolves the cases both sides can call and passes on only the ones they cannot, so its sound economy is the selection that fills the record with toss-ups.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The power to resolve a case without a verdict, and the cost that makes it worth using.
The seat holds the option to settle, to close a dispute on terms both sides accept, and the trial cost that makes settling the sure cases the cheaper course.
Agreement as its whole instrument, since the seat can remove a case from the record only when both parties will take the same terms, so it works exactly on the cases whose outcome the two foresee alike.

## Orders

Settle what both sides can call.
Resolve the disputes whose result is plain, on terms that price the outcome in, since paying to try a case whose end is known is spending for a certainty already had.
Pass to trial only the disagreements, since a case settles only where the two sides agree on where it lands, so what the seat cannot resolve is precisely the set of genuine uncertainties.

## Loses

The representativeness of what it passes on, which the seat destroys by the very selectivity that defines it.
The Settlement removes a case exactly when its outcome is predictable, so what it leaves for the record is the complement of the predictable, the toss-ups alone, and the seat cannot filter by cost without filtering by certainty, its economy and its distortion the same act. What the seat forfeits is any hope that the tried cases resemble the disputes: because it resolves every case the parties can call and passes on only the ones they cannot, the sample it hands to the record is selected on the outcome itself, so the harder the seat works and the more sensibly it settles, the less the residue looks like the world, the seat that saves the parties their trial costs the same seat that guarantees the record is the leftover of what could not be foreseen.

## Drives

Toward a record emptied of every case whose outcome was known,
and a residue selected on the very predictability it hides,
so the seat that resolves the disputes soundly is the seat that leaves behind only the toss-ups, its economy the exact reason the tried sample cannot represent the disputes it came from.
