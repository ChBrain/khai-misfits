# Position: The Tally

## Taxonomy

[Only the Close Ones](play_only_the_close_ones.md). The counting seat, which reads the decided cases for the pattern they should show and takes the win rate for the state of the world, seeing the record and not the filter that made it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The record of verdicts, and the tools to count it.
The seat holds the decided cases, the outcomes entered and countable, and the methods to compute a rate from them.
Visibility of the tried alone as its whole field, since the seat can see and count only what reached a verdict, the settled cases leaving no row to tally, so its data is exactly the residue of the filter.

## Orders

Count the record and read the rate.
Gather the decided cases, compute who won, and infer the merits or the lean of the law from the tally, treating the outcomes as evidence of the disputes.
Trust the data, since the seat's method is to let the record speak, and the record it holds is clean, careful, and complete as a record of trials, whatever it is as a sample of the world.

## Loses

The truth of its own measure, which the seat has no instrument to reach.
The Tally can only count what was tried, and what was tried was selected before it arrived, so the seat computes an honest rate over a sample chosen on the outcome, its work spent on the wrong population. What the seat forfeits is any internal check: every test it can run is a test of the record it holds, and the record it holds is clean, so no diagnostic available to the seat reveals that the sample was filtered, because the filter operated before the data arrived and left nothing in the data to find. The seat cannot recover the missing cases by processing the present ones, since no function of the tried record reconstructs the settled ones it never contained, so more computation only refines an estimate of the wrong quantity, the seat sealed inside a sample whose bias is invisible to every operation it can perform on it.

## Drives

Toward an honest count of a selected sample,
and a confidence that grows with the rigour applied to the wrong population,
so the seat that reads the record most carefully is the seat most sure of a number that means nothing about the world, its diligence polishing an error the record gives it no way to find.
