# Place: The Settled

## Taxonomy

[Only the Close Ones](play_only_the_close_ones.md). The unrecorded mass of resolved disputes, the cases that left at the Table and never reached a verdict, where the population the record needs sits unseen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Nothing, or almost nothing, a private resolution behind a closed door.
Shown as absence: the settled cases produce no verdict, no public row, only terms known to the parties, so the Settled reads as empty, a place there is nothing to count.

## Holds

Holds the disputes with known outcomes, the clear cases the record lacks.
The Settled is where the predictable go: every case both sides could call was closed here on terms that priced its outcome, so this is where the informative disputes are, the ones whose results would have shown the pattern, all of them resolved out of sight and none of them entered anywhere a reader can reach.

## Offers

Offers the true shape of the disputes, and no way to see it.
Here lies the population the trial record filtered out, so the Settled offers everything the inference needs, the clear cases and their known outcomes, and offers it locked away, its terms private and its verdicts never rendered, the very data that would correct the count held exactly where the count cannot go.

## Withheld

Withholds itself entirely, and by that the truth of the record.
What the Settled hides is its own contents: because settlements are private and unrecorded, the place that holds the answer holds it invisibly, so the reader cannot even see the size of what they are missing, and the correction that would need this population must estimate it rather than read it, the Settled withholding not just its cases but any measure of how much the record leaves out.
