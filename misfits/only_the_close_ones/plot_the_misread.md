# Plot: The Misread

## Taxonomy

[Only the Close Ones](play_only_the_close_ones.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Court](place_the_court.md), the record of verdicts gathered and ready to count.
A full body of decided cases, complete and clean, and a reader who takes it for a sample of the disputes.

## Action

[Tallying](process_tallying.md): the Tallyman counts the verdicts, finds the win rate near even, and concludes the claims are a wash or the law has no tilt.

## Stage

[The Tallyman](persona_the_tallyman.md) counts [the docket](piece_the_docket.md) from [the Tally](position_the_tally.md), on [Read the Verdicts](plan_read_the_verdicts.md), reading [the coin flip](piece_the_coin_flip.md) clustering as a fact about the world, blind to [the sieve](piece_the_sieve.md) that selected it and to [the unlitigated](piece_the_unlitigated.md) it cannot see.
The count is careful and the number real, and the conclusion drawn from it is false.

## Tension

The count is honest and the conclusion is wrong, and nothing in the record reveals the gap.
There is no miscount: the verdicts are real, the arithmetic sound, the win rate genuinely near even. But the sample was selected on the outcome before it was counted, so the plot closes with a rigorous measure of a filtered record read as a finding about the disputes, the Tallyman's very care deepening their confidence in a number that describes the residue and not the world, the error hidden precisely where the method cannot look, in the cases that never reached the page.
