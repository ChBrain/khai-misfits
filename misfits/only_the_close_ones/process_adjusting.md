# Process: Adjusting

## Taxonomy

[Only the Close Ones](play_only_the_close_ones.md). The correcting of the inference for the selection, the modelling of the settled cases that never appear so the record can be read for the world rather than the residue.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Realist](persona_the_realist.md), at [the Settled](place_the_settled.md), reasoning about the cases that left before trial.
Begins when a reader treats the trial record as a filtered residue and asks what settled, rather than taking the verdicts at face value.

## Direction

Turns a filtered record into an estimate of the world.
Because the tried cases are known to be selected, the reader models the settlement that removed the clear ones and corrects the win rate for it, so adjusting converts a near-even tally into an estimate of the disputes, reweighting the residue by what it left out (Heckman).

## Lever

A model of the missing cases.
Because the settled population cannot be read, adjusting works only by assuming a shape for it, inferring the disputes from the settlement rate and whatever terms leak, so the lever is a reconstruction rather than a measurement, the correction resting on a model of exactly the cases the record omits.

## Echo

A truer answer that must sound less sure.
The echo of adjusting is an estimate corrected for the filter and honest about its limits, offering a range where the tally offered a number, so the right reading arrives less certain than the wrong one, its confidence bounded by the darkness of the settled cases it had to model rather than observe.
