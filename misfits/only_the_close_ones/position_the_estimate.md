# Position: The Estimate

## Taxonomy

[Only the Close Ones](play_only_the_close_ones.md). The correcting seat, which reads the trial record as a filtered residue and rebuilds the disputes it omits, resting its answer on the settled cases it can never observe.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A model of the selection, and the discipline to trust it over the plain count.
The seat holds an account of how settlement chose the tried cases, and uses it to correct the win rate rather than read it, asking what the filter removed before asking what the record shows.
An inference from absence as its instrument, since the seat's whole method is to estimate the population from its residue, reasoning about the cases that are missing precisely because they settled.

## Orders

Correct the count for the filter that made it.
Treat the tried cases as selected, discount the near-even rate as the sample's and not the world's, and estimate the merits from the disputes rather than the leftover.
Model what did not reach trial, since the answer the seat needs lives in the settled cases, so it must reconstruct the unobserved from the settlement rate and whatever terms leak, and rest its correction on that reconstruction.

## Loses

The firmness of the answer, which the seat trades away for its truth.
The Estimate can read the record rightly only by leaning on the cases the record omits, and those cases are unobserved, so the seat's correction rests on estimated inputs where the Tally's rests on counted ones, and an estimate built on an unobserved population carries wider bounds than a count of an observed one. What the seat forfeits is determinacy as a structural matter: its output must be an interval where the Tally's is a point, because the quantity it needs was never measured, so the correction is not merely harder to sell but genuinely looser, its error bars set by how much of the settled population it must assume rather than see. The seat that alone reads the record rightly is the seat whose answer is of necessity the wider one, precision surrendered as the price of aiming at the right quantity instead of the merely measurable one.

## Drives

Toward an answer built on the cases it cannot see,
and a correction truer and less certain than the error it fixes,
so the seat that alone can read the filtered record is the seat whose rightness rests on the unobserved, its honesty about the missing cases the very thing that makes it sound less sure.
