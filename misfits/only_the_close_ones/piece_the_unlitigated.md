# Piece: The Unlitigated

## Taxonomy

[Only the Close Ones](play_only_the_close_ones.md). The settled cases absent from the record, the clear disputes with known outcomes that left before trial, the population the inference needs and cannot see.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Rests in [the Settled](place_the_settled.md), never reaching [the court](place_the_court.md), removed at [the Table](place_the_table.md) by settlement.
Flows as the disputes that produced no verdict, the informative cases resolved out of sight and entered nowhere a reader can reach.

## Load Bearing

Without the Unlitigated the record would be complete and reading it sound: it is the mass of settled cases, missing exactly where the inference needs them, that makes the trial record a residue rather than a sample.
The Unlitigated is load bearing because it is the informative absence, the clear cases whose known outcomes would have shown the pattern, removed precisely because they were clear (Priest and Klein). Its being unobserved is the whole difficulty: the correction that would read the record rightly needs this population (Heckman), and this population is private, its terms unrecorded and its size unknown, so the data most needed is the data least available, and the reader cannot even measure how much is missing, the Unlitigated withholding not just its outcomes but any count of itself.

## Apparent

Just cases closed quietly, matters that never came to court.
Seen at the surface, the Unlitigated looks like nothing at all, an absence with no rows to notice, and the truth that these are the informative cases, that their silence is a selection, that the record is incomplete in exactly the way that matters and gives no sign of it, stays hidden behind how completely a settled case leaves no trace to be missed.

## Yearbook

Created by [the Litigant](persona_the_litigant.md)'s settling, invisible to [the Tallyman](persona_the_tallyman.md) who counts only the tried, and modelled by [the Realist](persona_the_realist.md) who must estimate it unseen.
It is the informative absence: the Unlitigated is what [the sieve](piece_the_sieve.md) drains into [the settled](place_the_settled.md), the population [adjusting](process_adjusting.md) must reconstruct and [the docket](piece_the_docket.md) can never contain, the missing cases whose loss is the reason the record cannot be read straight.
