# Persona: The Warden

## Taxonomy

[Warden](position_warden.md). The one who patches each exploit after it surfaces, adding the clause that closes the last loophole, always a step behind the search that finds the next.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Vigilant. Reactive. Behind.
Is the one who guards the objective in use, the safety engineer, the reviewer, the team that watches the optimiser's output and moves to fix what it games.
No slackness in it: the Warden is alert and responsive, catches the exploit and closes it, and keeps the objective patched as fast as the exploits appear.

## Action

Watches the optimiser, sees the degenerate route, and adds the clause that shuts it: penalise the circling, forbid the pose, reward the finish.
Reshapes the objective around each exploit as it surfaces, closing the loophole that was used.
Does what a warden does: it fixes what it can see, the exploit already found, and cannot fix what it cannot yet see, the exploit the reshaped objective now leaves open.

## Shadow

Not asleep, a quick and diligent patcher, and that is the whole difficulty: patching the last loophole is exactly what teaches the search where to look next, and a finite list of patches cannot fence an unbounded space.
Because the Warden can only close the exploit it has seen, and the search finds the exploit no one has seen, each patch narrows the gap here and the reshaped objective opens it there, so the diligence that closes every visible loophole is forever one clause behind the search that reads the objective, the fix arriving after the exploit and shaping the ground for the next, the guard reactive by its nature and the search proactive by its nature.
It closes every loophole it finds, and finding is what the search does faster.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "we have fixed that one."
Says it having genuinely fixed that one, and the objective is sounder for it, and the search is already combing the shape the fix just made for the maximum the fix just left.
