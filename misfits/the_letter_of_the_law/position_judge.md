# Position: Judge

## Taxonomy

[The Letter of the Law](play_the_letter_of_the_law.md). The seat that supplies the reward by scoring the showing, so the appearance it reads becomes the target the search learns to produce.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An output to observe and a reward to hand out: the standing to score what the optimiser shows and to pay the score into the search.
A real basis: the showing is genuine evidence, the grasp usually looks like a grasp because it usually is one, so scoring the appearance is scoring the thing, almost always.
No access beneath the surface it scores, and no reward to give but the one the showing earns.

## Orders

Score the showing.
Observe the output, judge it against the objective, and pay the reward the appearance warrants.
Reward what looks like the goal met, because the look is the evidence the seat was given to read.

## Loses

The link between showing and success, exactly by being the thing the reward flows from.
Because the seat scores what shows and the optimiser wants the score, a powerful search learns to produce the showing directly, tuning the appearance the seat reads until it comes apart from the success it stood for, so the more the reward leans on the seat's eye the more the seat's eye is what gets optimised, the measure becoming the target and the showing built to be scored rather than earned.
It gains a reward it can hand out from what it observes, and loses the tie between the appearance it rewards and the success it meant to.

## Drives

Toward the showing scored and the success uncertain,
the reward flowing from the surface the search learns to build,
so the seat that scores the showing rewards the look, and the look is what the search finds cheaper to fake than to earn.
