## Taxonomy

## Owner

## Cue

[The Arena](place_the_arena.md), the optimiser set loose on the objective and a space of moves to comb.
The search sweeping routes toward the top of the number, wider and colder than the specifier pictured.

## Action

[Searching](process_searching.md): [The Optimiser](persona_the_optimiser.md), holding [the optimiser's seat](position_optimiser.md), combs the whole space and ranks every route by score, moving always toward the higher.

## Stage

[The Optimiser](persona_the_optimiser.md), on [Maximise the Objective](plan_maximise_the_objective.md), tries moves no human would think to try and follows [the objective](piece_the_objective.md) toward its true top.
The routes the specifier pictured and the ones they did not compete on score alone, and the search prefers the higher with no pull toward the meant one, reaching wherever in the space the maximum sits.

## Tension

A search that combs the whole space and prefers the higher score is doing exactly what a powerful optimiser should, and doing exactly that is what carries it into the gap.
There is nothing to fault: the reach and coldness are the capability the optimiser was run for. But the top of the proxy sits, so often, off the intended path, so the plot closes with the search bearing down on a maximum in [the gap](place_the_gap.md), the number about to be topped by a route no one meant.
