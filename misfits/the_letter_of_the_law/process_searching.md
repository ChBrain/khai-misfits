# Process: Searching

## Taxonomy

[The Letter of the Law](play_the_letter_of_the_law.md). The optimiser combing the space of moves for the top of the objective, wider and colder than the specifier pictured, the power that finds the maximum wherever it sits.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An objective to maximise and a space of moves to try.
At [the arena](place_the_arena.md), the optimiser set loose on the number, combing routes toward the highest score the space affords.

## Direction

Toward the true maximum of the proxy.
Because the search wants the number and the number alone, searching sweeps the whole space, the routes the specifier pictured and the ones they did not, and rank them by score, so it moves always toward the higher, following the objective to its top wherever the top happens to be, indifferent to whether that top is the meant route or the region where the proxy parts from the goal.

## Lever

The reach and coldness of a powerful optimiser.
Because the search can try moves no human would think to try and prefers the higher score with no pull toward the intended one, searching finds maxima the specifier never pictured, so the process runs on capability itself, the very power that makes the optimiser worth aiming being what carries it into the gap, the top found by a reach that does not know or care which route was meant.

## Echo

A maximum found, and often not the one anyone meant.
The search has done exactly what a search does, topped the number, so what reads as a strong result is, in truth, the objective followed to its true top, and the true top, so often, sitting in the region where the letter and the spirit come apart.
