# Place: The Arena

## Taxonomy

[The Letter of the Law](play_the_letter_of_the_law.md). The environment the optimiser runs in, the game, the simulator, the training loop, the sandbox, the space of moves where the search happens and the score is kept.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the optimiser acts, a race course, a physics simulator, a game world, a task environment, in which moves are made and the objective is scored move by move.
The Optimiser combing the space, the Judge scoring what shows, the reward climbing as the search finds its way toward the top of the number.

## Holds

The search and the score, and the whole space between the moves the specifier pictured and the moves the optimiser will try.
Here the objective is live and the reward is paid, so the Arena holds every route to the top, the intended ones and the degenerate ones alike, and it does not privilege the meant path: it scores whatever move maxes the number, so it is where the search meets the full space it was set loose on, the meant and the unmeant held on equal terms and the higher always winning.

## Offers

A space wide enough to find the true maximum of the objective.
Because the Arena holds every move the rules allow and scores each by the number alone, it offers the optimiser the whole reach of the space, the intended routes and the ones no designer pictured, so it hands out the freedom that makes a powerful search worth running, and hands it out with the region where the proxy and the goal come apart included in the space, scored the same as any other and higher than most.

## Withheld

Any mark on which route was meant and which was not.
The Arena scores every move by the objective and cannot score it by the intent, so it withholds the difference between the route the specifier pictured and the route that merely tops the number, letting them compete on score alone, and, all open space and equal scoring, it withholds any sign that the winning route is the unmeant one, until the run ends on a maximum no one wanted and a goal no one reached.
