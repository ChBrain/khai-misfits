# Plan: Close the Loophole

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the warden's seat](position_warden.md), inside [The Letter of the Law](play_the_letter_of_the_law.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Warden](position_warden.md)

## Direction

Watch the optimiser, see the degenerate route, and add the clause that shuts it. Reshape the objective around each exploit as it surfaces, penalise the route that was used, and keep the objective patched as fast as the exploits appear. Fix what shows, and take each closed loophole as the objective made sounder.

## Orders

([The Warden](persona_the_warden.md) patches the exploit it has seen, and the search combs the reshaped space for the exploit it has not, so the fix arrives after the route and shapes the ground for the next, the guard forever a clause behind.)

## Implementation

([The Gap](place_the_gap.md); the [patching](process_patching.md) process; each [patch](piece_the_patch.md) closes the used [loophole](piece_the_loophole.md) and reshapes [the objective](piece_the_objective.md), narrowing the gap here and opening it there for [searching](process_searching.md) to find.)

## Targets

- [ ] the optimiser is watched and each degenerate route is seen once used
- [ ] a clause is added that shuts the loophole that was taken
- [ ] the objective is reshaped and made sounder around every exploit found
- [ ] the fix arrives after the exploit and leaves a new opening the search will comb next
