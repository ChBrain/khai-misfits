# Persona: The Reserver

## Taxonomy

[Reserver](position_reserver.md). Keeps slack deliberately as insurance against the shock, and pays for it every calm day.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Prudent. Patient. Willing to look wasteful.
Sees that the shock will come someday and that only the give kept in hand will meet it, and holds the spare on purpose.
No timidity in it: a considered choice to carry a cost now against a catastrophe later.

## Action

Keeps the spare, the buffer, the margin, and refuses to trim the give the shock will need.
Carries a standing inefficiency through all the calm, underperforming the leaner rival.
Meets the shock, when it comes, with room to flex instead of a system that shatters.

## Shadow

It holds the one thing that answers the trap and can never prove it was worth holding.
Because the shock it absorbs leaves no mark, its reserve looks like waste on every ordinary day and vindicates itself only in a disaster averted that no one sees, so it is forever pressed to cut, and pressed too far its own reserve tips into mere bloat.
It pays a visible cost for an invisible saving, and is right in a way the numbers never show.

## Tell

Says "keep the spare, we will want it the day we cannot get it."
Keeps it, and looks wasteful, and is proven right only in the shatter that never comes to it.
