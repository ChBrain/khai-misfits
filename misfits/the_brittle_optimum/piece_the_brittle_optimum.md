# Piece: The Brittle Optimum

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). The phenomenon whole: a system optimised to the point that it shatters, and its remedy in a reserve that always looks like waste.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Reached at [the works](place_the_works.md), through cuts made at [the margin](place_the_margin.md), and broken at [the break](place_the_break.md).
Answered only at [the backup](place_the_backup.md), where slack is kept against the shock.

## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without the mechanism that binds them.
With it the whole trap stands in one object: slack that is idle waste in calm, an efficiency that rewards each cut, a coupling that tightens as the give is removed, a shock that eventually comes, a shatter through a system with no give, and a reserve that alone lets it flex. The Brittle Optimum is load bearing because it is the structure entire (Perrow; Taleb): not a failure of discipline but its excess, each cut a true saving and the sum a system that breaks, correctable only by a standing inefficiency held against a shock that may never come to justify it.

## Apparent

Peak efficiency, a system optimised as far as it will go and simply unlucky in the end.
Seen from outside, the brittle optimum looks like good management met by bad fortune, and the truth that the optimisation built the fragility, that the lean system was brittle by construction, that the cure costs a robustness the numbers punish, stays hidden behind how admirable the lean run looked and how external the shock that felled it seemed.

## Yearbook

The trap entire, staged across the run.
The Brittle Optimum is [the slack](piece_the_slack.md) traded for [the efficiency](piece_the_efficiency.md) until [the coupling](piece_the_coupling.md) leaves no give, the [shatter](piece_the_shatter.md) the next shock then runs through it, and the break that only [the insurance](piece_the_insurance.md), kept at a cost that always looks like waste, could have turned into a flex.
