# Position: Cascade

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). The seat of the failure propagating through the tight coupling, unchecked once the slack is gone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A first failure, and the tight couplings that carry it onward.
No buffer to cross, because the give that would have damped it was trimmed.
The reach of the whole system, every part now leaning on every other.

## Orders

Run unchecked.
Carry the first failure into the next part, and the next, following the couplings wherever they lead.
Take the whole system down in a chain no single part can stop.

## Loses

Nothing of its own, for it is only the coupling paying out.
Because the slack that would have broken its stride was cut for efficiency, the seat meets no give to stop it, so it is not a fault in itself but the structure discharging at once what a loose system would have absorbed in pieces.
It runs exactly as far as the tightness allows, which at the optimum is all the way.

## Drives

Toward a failure that spreads part to part with nothing between,
doing only what the coupling permits,
so the tighter the optimum, the further one break travels.
