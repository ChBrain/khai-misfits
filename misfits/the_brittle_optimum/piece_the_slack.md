# Piece: The Slack

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). The redundancy, buffer, and margin, pure cost in calm and the give that absorbs a shock.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the margin](place_the_margin.md), where it sits idle and is trimmed slice by slice.
Kept, when it is kept at all, at [the backup](place_the_backup.md), held on purpose against the shock.

## Load Bearing

Without it the system has no give, and a shock has nowhere to go but through.
With it the system can flex: the spare, the buffer, the margin that does nothing on an ordinary day is exactly the capacity that takes the jolt on the rare one. The Slack is load bearing because it is the give the whole trap turns on (Taleb): its value is real but shows only under a shock that has not come, so in calm it reads as waste and is cut, and the piece carries the cruel timing by which the most worth keeping looks, when the keeping is decided, like the least.

## Apparent

Waste, an idle cost earning nothing, an obvious thing to cut.
Seen on any ordinary day, the slack looks like pure inefficiency, and the truth that it is insurance, that the idle margin is the give that would absorb the next shock, that its worthlessness is only the calm's verdict, stays hidden behind how plainly it does nothing right now.

## Yearbook

Held by [the slack](persona_the_slack.md) through [trimming](process_trimming.md), the give judged worthless in calm.
It is the insurance cut as waste: the Slack is what [the efficiency](piece_the_efficiency.md) is won by removing, whose loss tightens [the coupling](piece_the_coupling.md), and which [the insurance](piece_the_insurance.md) keeps on purpose to prevent [the shatter](piece_the_shatter.md).
