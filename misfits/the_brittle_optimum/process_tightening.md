# Process: Tightening

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). The system coupling ever tighter as the give is removed, each part coming to lean on every other.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A run of cuts rewarded, and a lean system outperforming the slack-heavy one.
At [the works](place_the_works.md), the optimiser praised for the leaner run and the reserver looking wasteful beside it, so the trimming is reinforced.

## Direction

Toward an optimum where every part depends on every other holding.
As slice after slice of give is cut, the parts that the slack held loose couple tight, so tightening drives the system past robust and into a taut configuration where nothing can fail alone, the same optimisation raising the efficiency and the coupling together.

## Lever

The calm rewarding lean over loose.
Because through all the ordinary days the lean system wins on the numbers and the reserved one merely costs, the pressure runs one way, so tightening works by rewarding every cut and punishing every kept margin, driving the whole system toward the edge where give is gone.

## Echo

An optimum reached, lean and taut and admired.
The system runs at its best on paper, every part earning, none idle, and every part now leaning on the rest, so what looks like the peak of good management is the point at which one failure can reach them all.
