## Shown

The system at work on an ordinary day: parts humming, output steady, the books tallying what each piece costs and earns.
The spare and the buffer sitting off to the side, earning nothing, and every cut to them showing up at once as a leaner, cheaper run.

## Holds

The whole case for trimming, laid out in calm.
Here the system performs and the numbers reward what performs, so the works holds the daily proof that slack is waste and cutting is gain, the vantage from which every trim looks right and the fragility it buys is nowhere to be seen.

## Offers

A better number for every slice of slack removed.
Because the works measures the ordinary day and slack earns nothing on it, the place offers the optimiser a real, immediate saving for each cut, so it hands out, trim by trim, the local rightness that sums to a system with no give left.

## Withheld

The shock, and the value of the give against it.
The works cannot show the disturbance that has not come, so it withholds the one condition under which slack pays, and running only the calm it withholds the whole case for the reserve, pricing the give at its idle cost and never at its worth under shock.
