# Process: Reserving

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). Slack held deliberately as insurance against the shock, the give kept at a standing cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A fragility seen and a will to hold the give against it.
At [the backup](place_the_backup.md), the reserver keeping the spare and the buffer and the margin on purpose, refusing to trim what the shock will need.

## Direction

Toward a system that can flex instead of shatter.
By holding slack through the calm, reserving keeps the capacity to absorb a disturbance, so it drives the system back from the taut optimum toward robustness, and it does so against the grain, carrying a cost every ordinary day for a return that comes only under a shock that may not come at all.

## Lever

A judgement of the rare day over the common one.
Because only kept slack meets the shock, and the shock is certain in the long run and invisible in the short, reserving works by valuing the exceptional day the books ignore, holding the give against every calm-day argument to cut it, and stopping short of the bloat too much slack becomes.

## Echo

A robustness bought and never proven.
The system can flex, the shock when it comes is absorbed without a mark, and the cost of the reserve sits plain on the books while its saving leaves none, so what reserving achieves is real and forever unvindicated, insurance that looks like waste until the shatter it quietly prevents.
