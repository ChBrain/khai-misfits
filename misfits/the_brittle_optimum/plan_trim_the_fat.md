## Direction

Cut what sits idle. Find the spare, the buffer, the margin doing nothing, and trim it, booking the saving from every slice removed. Run the system tight, read a leaner one as a better one, and take each cut that breaks nothing as proof it was right, so keep cutting toward the optimum.
