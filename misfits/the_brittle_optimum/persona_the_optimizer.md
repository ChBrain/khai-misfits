# Persona: The Optimizer

## Taxonomy

[Optimizer](position_optimizer.md). Trims each slice of idle slack as waste, rewarded for the efficiency, and right every time.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Disciplined. Sharp-eyed. Impatient with waste.
Sees the spare supplier, the buffer, the margin sitting idle and reckons what they cost to keep.
No recklessness in it: only the sound instinct to cut what earns nothing and run the system tight.

## Action

Cuts the idle slack, one slice at a time, and books the saving.
Reads a leaner system as a better one, and each cut that breaks nothing as proof it was right.
Trims again, and again, toward the optimum.

## Shadow

Not wasteful, disciplined: each cut is a true gain and the slack really was idle, and yet the give it removes is what a shock would have needed.
Because the saving is immediate and the fragility deferred and invisible, it is rewarded for every trim and never charged for the coupling it tightens, so it optimises the system straight past robust and into brittle without a single wrong step.
It is right on every ordinary day, and the sum of those right days is a system that shatters.

## Tell

Says "this margin does nothing, cut it, look, nothing broke."
Cuts, and nothing breaks ... until the day it does.
