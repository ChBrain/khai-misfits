# Piece: The Efficiency

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). The gain that rewards each cut, the immediate visible saving that makes trimming rational and certain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the works](place_the_works.md), where it is measured on the ordinary day and every cut improves it.
Set against [the backup](place_the_backup.md), whose kept slack it always seems to indict as waste.

## Load Bearing

Without it there would be no reward for cutting, and the slack might simply be left alone.
With it every trim pays at once: the cost of the slice removed becomes a saving on the books that day. The Efficiency is load bearing because it is the reward that drives the whole descent (Ohno; Hollnagel): it is real, immediate, and certain, where the fragility it buys is deferred and invisible, so the piece carries the asymmetry that makes each cut rational and the sum of them ruinous, a present gain always outweighing an absent risk.

## Apparent

Simply good management, a system run without waste.
Seen on the ordinary day, the efficiency looks like unarguable virtue, the mark of a well-run system, and the truth that each increment of it was bought by removing give, that the leaner the number the tighter the coupling, stays hidden behind how plainly better a cheaper, tighter run appears.

## Yearbook

Won by [the optimizer](persona_the_optimizer.md) through [trimming](process_trimming.md), the reward that makes cutting certain.
It is the gain that hides the cost: the Efficiency is what each slice of [the slack](piece_the_slack.md) is traded for, the number that praises [the optimizer](persona_the_optimizer.md) and shames [the insurance](piece_the_insurance.md), and the immediate saving set against the deferred [shatter](piece_the_shatter.md).
