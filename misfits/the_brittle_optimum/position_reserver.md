# Position: Reserver

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). The seat that keeps slack as insurance, and carries its cost through the calm.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The give the shock will need: the spare, the buffer, the margin, held on purpose.
The judgement that the disturbance will come and that only kept slack will meet it.
A standing cost each calm day, and no proof to show for it.

## Orders

Keep the reserve.
Hold the slack against the shock, refuse to trim the give, and carry the inefficiency as insurance.
Meet the disturbance with room to flex rather than a system that shatters.

## Loses

The argument, every calm day, exactly because its saving is invisible.
Because the shock it absorbs leaves no mark, the seat cannot prove the reserve was worth its cost, so it is forever pressed to cut and easily overruled, and pressed too far its own reserve becomes mere bloat.
It pays a cost the books can see for a catastrophe the books never will.

## Drives

Toward slack kept against a shock that may not come in time to vindicate it,
right in a way no calm day can show,
so the reserve that would save the system is the hardest cost to justify keeping.
