# Place: The Backup

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). Where slack is deliberately kept as insurance, the reserve held against the shock and the cost of it met.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The give held on purpose: the second supplier retained, the buffer stocked, the margin left in the schedule, the backup kept warm.
The reserver carrying it through the calm, the standing cost showing on every ordinary day, and the leaner rival, for now, ahead.

## Holds

The remedy, and the whole price of it.
Here the slack is kept against the shock, so the backup holds the one thing that lets the system flex, a reserve of give maintained on purpose, and holds with it everything that argues against it: the daily cost, the invisible saving, the temptation to trim, the risk of a reserve so large it is only bloat.

## Offers

A system that can flex, at the cost of one that looks lean.
Because the backup keeps the give the shock will need, it offers the reserver room to absorb the disturbance instead of shattering under it, so it is where robustness is actually bought, paid for in a standing inefficiency carried through all the calm.

## Withheld

Any proof that the cost was worth it.
The backup absorbs the shock without a mark, so it withholds the evidence of its own value, the disaster it prevents leaving nothing to point to, and holding a give that always looks like waste it withholds the vindication that would keep it from being cut when the calm returns.
