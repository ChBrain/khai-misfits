# Process: Shattering

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). The shock striking the taut system and the failure cascading through it, unchecked because no slack remains.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A disturbance arriving and a system with no give left.
At [the break](place_the_break.md), the shock landing on a part and finding, where a looser system would have flexed, nothing to absorb it.

## Direction

Toward the whole coming apart at once.
The shock hits, and because the slack that would have damped it was cut, the failure runs from the struck part straight into the next, so shattering drives one break across the tight couplings into all of them, the system discharging in a cascade what a slacker one would have taken in pieces.

## Lever

Tight coupling with no buffer to cross.
Because every part now leans on every other and no give sits between them, a single failure meets no resistance, so shattering works on the coupling the trimming built, turning what would have been a local jolt into a total collapse for want of any slack to stop it.

## Echo

A lean optimum in ruins from a shock it could not take.
The system that ran so cleanly is down all at once, the deferred cost of the cutting come due in full, so the fragility that was invisible through every calm day is, in the shatter, suddenly the only thing there was to see.
