# Position: Optimizer

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). The seat that trims idle slack for efficiency, rewarded at each cut.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A system with slack in it, and the books that show what the slack costs to keep.
The authority to cut, and the saving each cut returns at once.
No sight of the shock to come, only the idle margin in front of it now.

## Orders

Trim the fat.
Cut what sits idle, run the system tight, and book the saving from every slice removed.
Read a leaner system as a better one, and each cut that breaks nothing as proof it was right.

## Loses

The give, exactly by cutting what looks like waste.
Because the slack it removes earns nothing in calm and everything under shock, the seat cannot see the cost of the cut until a shock arrives, so it optimises past robust into brittle while every single step improves the numbers.
It gains on every ordinary day and loses the whole system on the one that is not.

## Drives

Toward a leaner, tighter optimum,
right at each cut and ruined by their sum,
so the discipline that removes the waste removes the give that would have held.
