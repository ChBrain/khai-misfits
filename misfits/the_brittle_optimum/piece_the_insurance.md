# Piece: The Insurance

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). The slack held deliberately against the unforeseeable shock, the remedy at a standing cost that cannot prove its worth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the backup](place_the_backup.md), where the give is kept on purpose against the shock.
Set against [the works](place_the_works.md), whose calm-day numbers argue every day to cut it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play has no remedy, only a system trimmed to shatter every time.
With it the misfit is answered, and answered at a price: slack kept as a reserve, the spare and buffer and margin held so the system can flex under shock. The Insurance is load bearing because it is the one correction the structure allows and the hardest to keep (Taleb): it costs on every ordinary day, underperforms the leaner rival, cannot prove its value because the shock it absorbs leaves no mark, and pressed too far becomes bloat, so the piece carries both the exit and its cost, a standing inefficiency that always looks like the very waste it prevents.

## Apparent

An indulgence, money spent on margins the lean rival does without.
Seen in calm, the insurance looks like a failure to optimise, a system carrying fat its competitors have cut, and the truth that it is the give that will meet the next shock, that its cost buys a robustness the numbers cannot show, that the rival's leanness is a fragility not yet triggered, stays hidden behind how plainly it underperforms today.

## Yearbook

Kept by [the reserver](persona_the_reserver.md) through [reserving](process_reserving.md), the give held against the shock.
