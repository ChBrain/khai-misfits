# Persona: The Slack

## Taxonomy

[Slack](position_slack.md). The redundancy, buffer, and margin, idle in calm and the give that absorbs a shock.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Quiet. Unused. Seemingly pointless.
Is the spare, the buffer, the extra margin, doing nothing at all on an ordinary day.
No case to make for itself in the calm: it only sits, and costs, and waits.

## Action

Absorbs a shock when one comes, taking the jolt so the parts around it need not.
Gives the system room to flex, one failure held clear of the next.
Earns its whole keep on rare days and none of it on the many between.

## Shadow

It is worthless exactly when the decision to cut it is made, and priceless exactly when it is gone.
Because its value shows only under a shock that has not come, it looks in calm like pure waste, so it is the first thing trimmed and the thing most missed, and it cannot argue for its keep because the disaster it would prevent has left no mark to point to.
It is the insurance no one values until the fire.

## Tell

Says nothing, most days, and costs its keep.
And on the one day it is needed, it is the difference between a flex and a shatter, and it has been cut.
