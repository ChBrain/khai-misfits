# Position: Slack

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). The seat of the give in the system, idle in calm and load-bearing under shock.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A place in the system that does nothing on an ordinary day: a spare, a buffer, a margin.
The capacity to absorb a jolt, and to hold one failure clear of the next.
No showing for itself in calm, only a cost and a wait.

## Orders

Sit idle.
Cost your keep through the calm, and be ready to take the shock when it comes.
Give the system room to flex, though the room looks like waste until it is needed.

## Loses

Its place, exactly because its worth is invisible when it is judged.
Because it earns only under a shock that has not arrived, the seat cannot justify itself on any ordinary day, so it is the first thing cut and the thing most missed, unable to point to the disaster it would have prevented.
It is worthless the moment it is weighed and priceless the moment it is gone.

## Drives

Toward a give that saves the system and cannot defend itself,
pure cost in calm and the whole difference under shock,
so the capacity most worth keeping is the one hardest to keep.
