# Persona: The Cascade

## Taxonomy

[Cascade](position_cascade.md). The failure running from part to tight-coupled part, unchecked because no slack remains to stop it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fast. Relentless. Following the couplings.
Is the failure in motion, jumping from the part that broke to the next that depends on it.
No hesitation in it: it goes wherever the tight coupling leads, which is everywhere at once.

## Action

Runs the first failure straight into the second, the second into the third, with nothing between them.
Finds no buffer to break its stride, because the give that would have was trimmed away.
Takes the whole system down in a chain no single part could have stopped alone.

## Shadow

It is the shatter made of the coupling, doing only what the tightness allows.
Because the slack that would have damped each jump was cut for efficiency, one part's failure is every part's, so the cascade is not a fault of its own but the structure paying out, the tight optimum discharging all at once what a loose system would have absorbed in pieces.
It runs exactly as far as the coupling lets it, which, at the optimum, is all the way.

## Tell

Says nothing, and moves too fast to answer.
And where a looser system would have stopped it at the first part, it finds no give, and does not stop.
