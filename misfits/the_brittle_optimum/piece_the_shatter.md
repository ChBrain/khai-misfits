# Piece: The Shatter

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). The cascade under shock, the lean system coming apart where a slacker one would have flexed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the break](place_the_break.md), where the shock lands and the failure runs the whole system down at once.
Foretold at [the margin](place_the_margin.md), in every slice of give that was trimmed away.

## Load Bearing

Without it the fragility would stay a theory, and the cutting would look costless forever.
With it the deferred bill comes due: the shock meets a system with no give, and the failure cascades through the tight coupling into total collapse, where slack would have absorbed it in pieces. The Shatter is load bearing because it is the trap's outcome made real (Perrow; Bak): not a stumble but a system-wide break, proportioned to how lean the optimum was, so the piece carries the catastrophe that was written into every cut and invisible until the shock arrived to read it.

## Apparent

Bad luck, a freak shock that would have felled anyone.
Seen from the wreckage, the shatter looks like misfortune, an unforeseeable blow, and the truth that a slacker system would have shrugged the same shock off, that the brittleness was built by the trimming and merely triggered by the shock, that the disaster was the bill for the efficiency, stays hidden behind how sudden and external the break appears.

## Yearbook

Run by [the cascade](persona_the_cascade.md) through [shattering](process_shattering.md), the collapse the trimming made possible.
It is the deferred cost paid in full: the Shatter is what [the shock](persona_the_shock.md) triggers and [the coupling](piece_the_coupling.md) carries once [the slack](piece_the_slack.md) is gone, the catastrophe that only [the insurance](piece_the_insurance.md), by keeping the give, could have turned into a flex.
