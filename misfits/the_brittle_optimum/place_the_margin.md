# Place: The Margin

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md). Where the slack lives and the cuts are made, the give of the system thinned slice by slice.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The edges where the give sits: the spare supplier, the buffer stock, the extra time in the schedule, the second way through.
The optimiser at work here, paring each margin down, the system tightening a notch with every slice taken and nothing breaking as it does.

## Holds

The give itself, and the act of removing it.
Here the slack is both stored and trimmed, so the margin holds the difference between a system that can flex and one that cannot, the exact place where the capacity to absorb a shock is kept or cut, and where each cut looks like pure saving.

## Offers

Room to flex, or a saving to book, but not both.
Because the margin is where the give is, it offers the reserver a buffer against the shock and the optimiser a cost to cut, the same slack read as insurance by one and as waste by the other, so the place is where the trade between efficiency and robustness is actually struck.

## Withheld

The verdict on the cut.
The margin cannot say whether a slice trimmed will be missed, because that turns on a shock not yet arrived, so it withholds the consequence of each cut until far too late, letting the give be thinned on the calm's terms and judged only on the shock's.
