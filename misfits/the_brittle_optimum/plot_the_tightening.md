# Plot: The Tightening

## Taxonomy

[The Brittle Optimum](play_the_brittle_optimum.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Works](place_the_works.md), the lean system outperforming the slack-heavy one through a long calm.
The optimiser praised for the leaner run, and the reserver, with its margins intact, looking wasteful beside it.

## Action

[Tightening](process_tightening.md): [The Optimizer](persona_the_optimizer.md), holding [the optimizer's seat](position_optimizer.md), trims again on the strength of the calm, while [The Reserver](persona_the_reserver.md), holding [the reserver's seat](position_reserver.md), is pressed to cut the give it kept.

## Stage

[The Optimizer](persona_the_optimizer.md), still on [Trim the Fat](plan_trim_the_fat.md), reads every calm day as vindication and pares the system tighter, each part now leaning on the rest.
[The Reserver](persona_the_reserver.md), on [Keep the Reserve](plan_keep_the_reserve.md), watches its caution count against it on the numbers, unable to point to a shock that has not come, and holds its slack under mounting pressure to cut.

## Tension

The calm rewards the lean and shames the reserved, and so drives the system to the edge.
There is no error in the reward: through all the ordinary days the lean system genuinely wins and the kept margin genuinely costs. But the winning tightens the coupling notch by notch, and the losing is a robustness the numbers cannot price, so the plot closes at the optimum, lean and taut and admired, every part now depending on every other holding, the give all but gone.
