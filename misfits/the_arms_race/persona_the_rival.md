# Persona: The Rival

## Taxonomy

[Rival](position_rival.md). Faces the same choice as the contender and makes the same move, so the gains cancel.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Equal. Answering. Refusing to be left behind.
Sees the contender rise and does the plain arithmetic: match the spend, or drop a place.
No malice in it: only the same prudence, from the other side.

## Action

Meets each escalation with its own, spend for spend.
Restores the place the contender's climb had taken, and holds the line level.
Makes, in answering, the field's gains net exactly to nothing.

## Shadow

It is the contender seen from across, and its matching is what turns two rational climbs into one shared waste.
Because it cannot let a rival's rise stand unanswered without losing rank, it must spend, and its spending is the very thing that cancels the contender's, so both pay and neither moves.
It is right to match, and its rightness is the trap's other jaw.

## Tell

Says "if they spend more, I have to spend more, or I fall."
Does, and both stand where they started, lighter in the pocket.
