# Position: Broker

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The seat that lays a limit on all at once, and answers for the cost of holding it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The whole field in view, and the one insight that all together could stop where none alone can.
A limit to propose: a treaty, a cap, a tax on the escalation itself.
The burden of enforcing it, since it will not hold on its own.

## Orders

Call the truce.
Bind every contender to the same limit at the same moment, and free the field of the cost at no one's loss of rank.
Hold the cap against each player's pull to slip it.

## Loses

Ease, and certainty of the peace, exactly because the limit runs against every private drive.
Because the cap must catch all or catch none, and each contender has a live reason to break it and pull ahead, the seat cannot rest on goodwill, only on trust backed by enforcement, and the peace it builds falls the moment one defects.
It answers a race no one can win with a limit no one can safely be first to break.

## Drives

Toward a field freed by a limit on all,
built against every incentive that made the race,
so the one move that works is the one hardest to raise and hardest to keep.
