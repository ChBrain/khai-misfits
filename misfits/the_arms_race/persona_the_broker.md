# Persona: The Broker

## Taxonomy

[Broker](position_broker.md). Proposes the limit that binds every contender at once, and answers for its cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Even-handed. Patient. Working the whole field, not a side.
Sees that no contender can stop alone and that all together could, and reaches for the agreement that would let them.
No idealism in it: only the recognition that the peace has to be built, because it will not arise on its own.

## Action

Lays a cap on the spend for everyone at the same moment: a treaty, a limit, a tax on the escalation itself.
Frees the field of the cost without moving a single rung, the standings kept and the waste removed.
Holds the limit against the pull of each player to slip it.

## Shadow

It is the one move that works and the hardest thing to hold, because it is built against every private incentive that made the race.
The cap must catch all or catch none, each contender has a live reason to break it and pull ahead, and it fences a freedom each would rather keep for itself, so the peace stands only as long as the trust and the enforcement behind it, and falls the moment one defects.
It answers a race no one can win with a limit no one can safely be first to break.

## Tell

Says "we all put our arms down together, or not at all."
And spends its labour keeping the one who would pick theirs back up from starting it again.
