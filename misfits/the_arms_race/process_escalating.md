# Process: Escalating

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The contender raising its effort to get ahead, round by round, the spend climbing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A place to be won and a rival to be passed.
At [the field](place_the_field.md), the contender reckoning that a little more effort buys a higher rung, and being right that it does when the move is made alone.

## Direction

Toward an ever-higher spend.
Each round the contender puts in more and takes the place the spend buys, and because a lead won truly rewards the mover, the escalating has every reason to continue, so it runs the effort up and up, each rise real in the moment it is made and answered before the next round begins.

## Lever

A genuine gain to whoever moves first.
Because a unilateral spend really does buy rank, the contender is rewarded for escalating and threatened by any rival who does, so the escalating is driven by a true prize and a true fear together, and the crank turns on the soundness of the move, not any error in it.

## Echo

A field higher in effort and set for the answer.
The spend has bought a place, the lead stands for a moment, and the rivals are already moving to match, so what reads on the field as getting ahead is, unseen, the opening of a round that will end with everyone raised and no one moved.
