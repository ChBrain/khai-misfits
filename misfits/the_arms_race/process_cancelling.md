# Process: Cancelling

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The matched spends netting every gain to zero, so the standings hold while the cost mounts.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A lead bought by one contender and faced by the rest.
At [the ladder](place_the_ladder.md), the rivals answering the escalation to hold their places, and the rank granting no more of itself than it takes from another.

## Direction

Toward standings unchanged and costs unrecovered.
As each contender matches, the gains are set against equal gains and net to nothing, so the cancelling drives the field's whole effort into a wash, the order restored exactly as it was, while the spending that produced the wash is not returned but kept, paid in full.

## Lever

The zero-sum shape of the prize.
Because rank is a position and not a quantity, a rise for one is a fall for another, so a general rise is no rise at all, and the cancelling works on the arithmetic of the ladder itself, converting matched effort into preserved order and nothing else.

## Echo

A line held level at a price all paid.
The standings read the same before and after, the effort spent and the rank untouched, so what looks round by round like each holding its own is, in sum, the field poorer by the whole cost and richer by none of the gain.
