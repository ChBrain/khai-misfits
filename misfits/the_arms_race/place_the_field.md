# Place: The Field

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The arena where the contenders compete and the escalation is spent, round after round.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Contenders raising their effort against one another, each spend answered by the next.
The moves that look, up close, like progress: more arms, more hours, more show, every one of them a real attempt to get ahead, and the field as a whole climbing while its order holds.

## Holds

The whole contest, and the compulsion that keeps it running.
Here the spending is made and matched, so the field holds the race in motion, the place where a lead is bought and instantly contested, and where the one who would stop finds that stopping on this ground is not resting but falling, so the field holds not just the effort but the reason no one can withhold it.

## Offers

A real gain to whoever escalates first, and a reason to escalate to everyone else.
Because a move made alone truly buys a place, the field offers each contender a genuine prize for spending, and offers the rest the genuine threat of that spend, so it hands out, round on round, the individual rightness that sums to collective waste.

## Withheld

Any gain that survives the answer.
The field lets a lead be won and never lets it last, because every rise summons its match, so it withholds the durable advantage the spending seems to promise, and being the ground of the race it withholds the exit too: there is no standing still here, only climbing or dropping.
