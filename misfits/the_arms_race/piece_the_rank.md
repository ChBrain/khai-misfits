# Piece: The Rank

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The positional prize, relative standing scarce by its nature, the good the whole race is run for.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the ladder](place_the_ladder.md), where the order is read and a general climb leaves it unchanged.
Contested on [the field](place_the_field.md), where each contender spends to take a rung from another.

## Load Bearing

Without it the spending would buy real gains and there would be no trap, only competition that pays.
With it the whole futility follows: the prize is a position, so to hold it is to hold it over someone, and the top is scarce by definition. The Rank is load bearing because it is the source of the structure (Hirsch): a good wanted for its relative place cannot be made more of by effort, only redistributed, so the field's total spend buys the field no total gain, and every rise is drawn from another's fall. It is the one fact that turns diligent competition into shared waste.

## Apparent

A prize like any other, a thing worth working to win.
Seen from the contest, rank looks like an ordinary reward that effort can earn, and the truth that it is a fixed order and not a growing quantity, that winning it takes it from someone rather than making it, stays hidden behind how real and worth-having a higher place feels.

## Yearbook

Held by [the rank](persona_the_rank.md) through [cancelling](process_cancelling.md), the prize that cannot grow.
It is why the race moves no one: the Rank is what [the escalation](piece_the_escalation.md) buys from another and never adds, the fixed order behind [the cancellation](piece_the_cancellation.md), and the standing [the truce](piece_the_truce.md) can leave untouched precisely because stopping the spend never threatened it.
