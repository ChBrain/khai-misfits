# Position: Rival

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The seat that matches every escalation to hold its place, and cancels the gain in doing so.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The same effort to raise as the contender, and the same stake in the line.
A place that a rival's climb would cost it, unless it answers.
The plain arithmetic that matching holds rank and standing still drops it.

## Orders

Keep pace.
Meet each spend with a spend, and refuse to be the one left behind.
Hold the line level, whatever it takes to hold it.

## Loses

Its money for nothing, exactly by being right to match.
Because it must answer or fall, it spends, and its spend cancels the contender's, so both climb and neither moves, and the seat cannot decline the waste without accepting the fall.
It pays the full cost of a place it merely kept.

## Drives

Toward a rank preserved by an ever-heavier spend,
prudent in every round to match,
so the sensible answer to a rival is the second half of a waste they share.
