# Place: The Tally

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). Where the whole spend is added against the whole gain, and the deadweight is read.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The sum of everything poured in, set beside the sum of what it moved.
The effort of every contender totalled on one side and the change in the standings on the other, the first a great figure and the second a zero, the cost entire and the gain none.

## Holds

The reckoning of the race, the two nets laid side by side.
Here the gains are shown to have cancelled and the costs to have not, so the tally holds the exact shape of the waste: effort netted to nothing across the field and price netted to its full amount, the one number that says the race was paid for and bought no one anything.

## Offers

The truth the single rounds concealed.
Because the tally counts the whole and not the move, it offers what no contender could see mid-climb, that all the spending summed to no change in rank, so it offers the case for stopping in its clearest form, the deadweight made a single figure that no round on its own revealed.

## Withheld

The power to act on what it shows.
The tally proves the race a waste and cannot end it, because reading the cost does not free any contender to stop alone, so it withholds the remedy, and being only a reckoning it withholds even its own force, easily seen and still not enough, on its own, to make one player be the first to step off.
