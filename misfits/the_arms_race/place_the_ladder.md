# Place: The Ladder

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). Where relative rank is read, the standings that a general climb leaves unchanged.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The order of the field, first to last, and who holds which rung.
The standings after a round of spending set beside the standings before, and the two the same: every contender higher in effort, the ladder itself no taller and its order intact.

## Holds

The prize in the only form it takes, a position and not a quantity.
Here rank is displayed as what it is, a fixed set of rungs, so the ladder holds the zero-sum truth of the contest: a place gained is a place taken, the height of the whole unchanged, so it is where the cancellation becomes visible, the effort poured in and the order untouched.

## Offers

A place to whoever climbs alone, and the same order to a field that all climbs together.
Because the ladder measures rank and not effort, it offers a real rise to the single mover and nothing at all to the general one, so it shows, plainly, that the spend buys position only while others hold still, and buys none once they move too.

## Withheld

More rungs.
The ladder cannot grow to meet the effort spent reaching up it, because a position is scarce by definition, so it withholds any total gain from a total climb, and holding only the order it withholds the reward the contenders feel they earned, granting the reshuffle and never the raise.
