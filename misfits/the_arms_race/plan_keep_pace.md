# Plan: Keep Pace

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the rival's seat](position_rival.md), inside [The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Rival](position_rival.md)

## Direction

Meet every escalation with one of your own. When a rival spends to rise, spend to match, and refuse to be the one left a place behind. Hold the line level, because standing still while another climbs is not holding your rank but losing it.

## Orders

([The Rival](persona_the_rival.md) answers each spend with its own, restores the place the contender's climb had taken, and cancels the gain in matching it.)

## Implementation

([The Ladder](place_the_ladder.md); the [cancelling](process_cancelling.md) process; the matched [escalation](piece_the_escalation.md) drives [the cancellation](piece_the_cancellation.md), every gain netted to zero and the standings restored.)

## Targets

- [ ] each escalation is met with a matching spend
- [ ] the place a rival's climb had taken is restored
- [ ] the standings are held level, at the full cost of holding them
- [ ] the two gains cancel, both higher in effort and neither in rank
