# Piece: The Escalation

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The raised spend, the move to get ahead, real in its gain when it is made alone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the field](place_the_field.md), where the contender puts in more and the rival answers in kind.
Read at [the ladder](place_the_ladder.md), where a lone escalation lifts a place and a general one lifts none.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no race, only a settled order no one contests.
With it the trap has its engine: a spend that truly buys rank when one makes it, and truly must be matched when another does. The Escalation is load bearing because it is the rational move at the heart of the waste (Schelling): each instance is correct, a real gain to the first mover and a real threat to the rest, so it cannot be waved off as folly, and it is precisely its soundness, repeated across the field, that drives the cost up while the standings hold.

## Apparent

Progress, a contender bettering itself by effort.
Seen in the moment, an escalation looks like ordinary striving that earns its reward, and the truth that its gain is borrowed from the others' places and returned the instant they match, that the move is right and the sum of the moves is loss, stays hidden behind how well the single spend works.

## Yearbook

Made by [the contender](persona_the_contender.md) and answered by [the rival](persona_the_rival.md) through [escalating](process_escalating.md).
It is the sound move that sums to waste: the Escalation is what takes a rung of [the rank](piece_the_rank.md) for a moment, what the matching turns into [the cancellation](piece_the_cancellation.md), and what [the ratchet](piece_the_ratchet.md) forbids any contender from lowering alone.
