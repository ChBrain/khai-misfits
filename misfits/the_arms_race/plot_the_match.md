# Plot: The Match

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ladder](place_the_ladder.md), the contender's rise showing as a place lost by everyone below it.
The rivals facing the plain choice: match the spend, or drop a rung.

## Action

[Cancelling](process_cancelling.md): [The Rival](persona_the_rival.md), holding [the rival's seat](position_rival.md), meets the escalation with its own, while [The Rank](persona_the_rank.md), holding [the rank's seat](position_rank.md), restores the order the two climbs cancel to.

## Stage

[The Rival](persona_the_rival.md), having tabled [Keep Pace](plan_keep_pace.md), answers spend with spend and reclaims the place the contender had taken.
[The Contender](persona_the_contender.md), still on [Get Ahead](plan_get_ahead.md), watches its lead erased, and [The Rank](persona_the_rank.md), on [Ration the Rank](plan_ration_the_rank.md), shows the line restored exactly as it was, every contender higher in effort and none in standing.

## Tension

Both are right to spend, and both are poorer for it, and neither has moved.
There is no aggression to blame: the rival must match or fall, so matching is prudence, not spite. But its prudent answer cancels the contender's prudent climb, so the two sound moves net to a wash, the standings unchanged and the cost kept, and the field learns that the only thing a general escalation reliably raises is the price of standing where you already stood.
