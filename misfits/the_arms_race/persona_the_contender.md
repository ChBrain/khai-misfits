# Persona: The Contender

## Taxonomy

[Contender](position_contender.md). Spends a little more than the rest to get ahead, and is right that it works.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Driven. Rational. Watching the ones in front.
Sees a place further up the line and reckons the effort to reach it worth the gain.
No arrogance in it: only the fair wish to do better, and the sense to spend for it.

## Action

Raises its effort past the field's: more arms, more hours, more show.
Buys the higher place the spend really can buy, when it moves alone.
Reads the gain as proof the spending works, and readies the next round of it.

## Shadow

Not vain, competing: it makes the move that would put anyone ahead, and would, if only it moved.
But its gain is taken from the others' places, so it summons their matching spend, and once all have climbed its rise is gone and only the cost remains.
Every round it is right to spend and poorer for having spent.

## Tell

Says "if I put in a little more, I will pull ahead."
Does, and would have ... had the others not thought the same.
