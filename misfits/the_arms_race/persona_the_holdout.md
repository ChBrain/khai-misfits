# Persona: The Holdout

## Taxonomy

[Holdout](position_holdout.md). Reasons that if all stopped all would gain, tries to stop, and falls behind for it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Clear-eyed. Weary of the spend. Willing to go first.
Sees plainly that the race costs everyone and moves no one, and judges that the sane thing is to step off it.
No naivety in it, at first: only the true sum that the escalation is waste.

## Action

Sits out the next round, holds its effort where it is while the others raise theirs.
Does not bring the field level, as it hoped, but drops a place, alone.
Proves, by falling, that the race cannot be left by one.

## Shadow

It is right about the whole and ruined by the part: stopping would help all, and stopping alone helps none and costs itself.
Because the others cannot follow its lead without dropping too, its unilateral restraint is not a signal but a sacrifice, so the ratchet holds and the one who saw clearest is the one who loses rank.
The truth it acts on is exactly the truth that punishes acting on it alone.

## Tell

Says "this is madness, I will simply stop."
Stops, and falls behind, and shows why no one else did.
