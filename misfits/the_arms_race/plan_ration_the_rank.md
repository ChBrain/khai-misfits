# Plan: Ration the Rank

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the rank's seat](position_rank.md), inside [The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Rank](position_rank.md)

## Direction

Keep the order and never the size. Grant the higher place to the one who spends, but grant it only by taking it from another, and stay exactly as large however hard the field climbs. Reward the first mover, and erase that reward the moment the rest catch up.

## Orders

([The Rank](persona_the_rank.md) grants a higher place to the spender and takes it from another to give, holding the same shape however much effort is poured at it.)

## Implementation

([The Ladder](place_the_ladder.md); the [cancelling](process_cancelling.md) process; [the rank](piece_the_rank.md) redistributes place and never enlarges it, so a general climb feeds [the cancellation](piece_the_cancellation.md) and no one rises.)

## Targets

- [ ] the higher place is granted to the spender, and taken from another to grant
- [ ] the size of the rank is held fixed however hard the field climbs
- [ ] the first mover's reward is erased the moment the rest catch up
- [ ] the field's whole effort buys a reshuffle and no net gain
