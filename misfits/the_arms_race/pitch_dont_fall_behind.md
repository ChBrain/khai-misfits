# Pitch: Don't Fall Behind

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Competitive, prudent, alert.
The steady sense that a little more effort keeps you in front, and the only real danger is being overtaken.
Nothing reckless in it: just the care not to be the one who slips a place while the others climb.

## Undertow

Under the prudence, a move that every contender is making at once.
Because the fear of falling behind is universal and rational, all escalate together, so the spend that would lift one lifts none, and the line that each strains to climb is held exactly in place by everyone's straining.
The danger avoided by the move is the very danger the move creates for everyone else.

## Nerve

The feeling of having to keep spending just to stay where you are,
sure that stopping would cost you your place,
while every rival is just as sure, so the whole field runs harder and stands still.

## Echo

After the run: a field higher in effort and unchanged in rank, poorer by all it poured in,
each contender safe from the fall it feared and none any further ahead,
and a peace that would free them all, wanted by everyone and trusted by no one to hold.
