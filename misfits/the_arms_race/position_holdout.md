# Position: Holdout

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The seat that refuses the next escalation, and falls behind alone for it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A clear sight of the whole: that the race costs all and moves none.
The option to hold its own effort where it is while the field raises theirs.
No means to make the others stop with it, only to stop itself.

## Orders

Sit it out.
Decline the next round, and let restraint stand as the sane response to a waste.
Show, by stopping, that the spending was never worth it.

## Loses

Its place, exactly by being the only one to stop.
Because unilateral restraint does not bring the field level but drops the one who tries, the seat's clear-sightedness is punished, not rewarded, and it cannot turn a true judgement about all into a safe move for one.
It keeps its money and loses its rank, and proves why no one else stepped off.

## Drives

Toward a restraint that is right for the field and ruinous for the seat,
correct about the whole and beaten in the part,
so the one who sees the race clearest is the one the ratchet drops.
