# Position: Contender

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The seat that spends to get ahead, and buys a real place when it moves alone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The effort to raise: more arms, more hours, more show, whatever the field is run in.
A place in the line it means to improve, and a rival it means to pass.
No power over the others' choices, only over its own spend.

## Orders

Get ahead.
Put in a little more than the rest and take the higher place it buys.
Read each gain as reason to spend again.

## Loses

The gain, exactly by summoning the answer to it.
Because its rise is taken from the others' rank, they must match to hold their places, and once they have the rise is gone, so the seat cannot keep a lead the same move gives everyone the reason to erase.
It ends higher in effort and level in rank, poorer by the climb.

## Drives

Toward a higher place bought with a heavier spend,
right in every single round to make the move,
so the rational climb repeated across the field lifts its cost and never its standing.
