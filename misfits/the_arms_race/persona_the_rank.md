# Persona: The Rank

## Taxonomy

[Rank](position_rank.md). The positional prize itself, relative standing, scarce by its nature and unable to grow.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fixed. Zero-sum. Indifferent to effort.
Is not a quantity to be added to but an order to be held: a first, a second, a last.
Cares nothing for how hard the field climbs, only for who is above whom.

## Action

Grants a higher place to the one who spends, and takes it from another to give it.
Holds the same shape however much effort is poured at it, redistributing rank and never enlarging it.
Rewards the first mover and erases the reward the moment the rest catch up.

## Shadow

It is the reason the whole race is futile: a prize that is a position cannot be made more of, only moved.
Because to be ahead is to be ahead of someone, the top is scarce by definition, so the field's total effort buys the field no total gain, and every climb is matched from someone else's fall.
It is the same size at the end of the race as the start, and only the spending is different.

## Tell

Says "there is no more of me to win, you can only take mine from one another."
And stays exactly as large however hard they all climb.
