# Process: Capping

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The limit laid on all contenders at once, freeing the field at the price of binding each.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The whole field seen to be losing, and one move that could free it.
At [the table](place_the_table.md), the broker proposing a common limit on the spend, the standings to be kept and the cost to be lifted from everyone together.

## Direction

Toward a race stood down at no one's loss of rank.
The cap binds every contender at the same moment, so the capping drives the spending back down without touching the order, freeing the field of the cost the ladder could never refund, and it moves against the grain, undoing by agreement the escalation that each incentive still pushes to renew.

## Lever

A limit that catches all at once, backed by enforcement.
Because the cap works only if it binds everyone together, and because each retains a live reason to slip it, the capping cannot lean on goodwill, only on a bound made mutual and kept by trust and its enforcement, so the lever is the collective commitment itself, and it holds exactly as far as that commitment does.

## Echo

A peace real and unstable.
The cost is lifted and the standings kept, the field freed of the waste at last, and the whole of it resting on a limit each player has reason to break, so what the capping achieves is the one good outcome the race allows, held only as long as no one is first to pick their arms back up.
