# Place: The Table

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). Where the limit is proposed to bind all at once, and its price is met.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The contenders brought off the field to set a common cap on the spend.
The terms of a treaty drawn: a ceiling all accept together, the standings kept exactly as they are, the cost lifted from everyone at the same moment, and each party watching the others for the first sign of a slip.

## Holds

The one exit from the race, and everything that makes it fragile.
Here the mutual limit is built, so the table holds the peace that no contender could reach alone, the agreement to lower arms together, and holds with it the whole weight of what threatens the peace: the incentive each keeps to defect, the freedom each gives up, the enforcement without which the words are air.

## Offers

A field freed of the cost at no one's loss of rank.
Because the cap catches all at once, the table offers what the race never could, the standings preserved and the spending stopped, so it offers the only correction that leaves every contender better off and none worse, the waste removed and the order untouched.

## Withheld

Any peace that holds itself.
The table can strike the agreement and cannot make it self-keeping, because each party retains a live reason to break it and pull ahead, so it withholds security, and offering a limit built against every private drive it withholds ease, handing the field a peace that stands only as long as the trust and the enforcement laid under it.
