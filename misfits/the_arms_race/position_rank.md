# Position: Rank

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The seat of the prize itself, relative standing, scarce by nature and fixed in size.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An order to keep: who stands above whom, first to last.
A fixed size, no rungs to add however hard the field climbs.
The power to grant a place only by taking it from another.

## Orders

Ration the rank.
Give the higher place to the one who spends, and take it from someone to give.
Stay the same size, whatever effort is poured in.

## Loses

Nothing, and that is the trap: it cannot be enlarged to reward the effort spent at it.
Because a position is scarce by definition, the seat has no more of itself to hand out, so the field's whole spend buys the field no gain, only a reshuffling, and the seat cannot convert effort into rank that was not taken from another.
It ends the race exactly as large as it began.

## Drives

Toward a standing redistributed and never grown,
indifferent to how hard all climb,
so every gain it grants is a loss it takes, and the sum of them is zero.
