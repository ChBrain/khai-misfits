# Piece: The Cancellation

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md). The netting of every gain to zero, the matched climbs that leave the standings exactly as they were.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the ladder](place_the_ladder.md), where the order after a round of spending is set beside the order before and found the same.
Totalled at [the tally](place_the_tally.md), where the summed gains show as nothing against the summed cost.

## Load Bearing

Without it the escalations would accumulate into advantage and the race would reward its runners.
With it the field's whole effort resolves to no change: gain set against equal gain, the rises offsetting to leave the line level. The Cancellation is load bearing because it is the mechanism that voids the spending (Frank): because the prize is positional, one contender's gain is another's loss, so simultaneous escalation nets to zero across the field, and the piece carries the exact way in which real, rewarded moves sum to no collective result at all.

## Apparent

A stalemate, a field that simply competed to a draw.
Seen from the standings, the cancellation looks like an even contest that happened to end level, and the truth that the draw was paid for in full by everyone, that the effort did not vanish but was spent to hold a line that would have held for free, stays hidden behind how ordinary an unchanged order looks.

## Yearbook

Wrought by [the rival](persona_the_rival.md)'s matching through [cancelling](process_cancelling.md), the gains netted out.
It is the wash the race resolves to: the Cancellation is what becomes of [the escalation](piece_the_escalation.md) once all match, the proof that [the rank](piece_the_rank.md) never grew, and the empty result beside which [the tiptoe](piece_the_tiptoe.md) shows the cost that was nonetheless paid.
