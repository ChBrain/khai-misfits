# Plot: The Ratchet

## Taxonomy

[The Arms Race](play_the_arms_race.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Field](place_the_field.md), a contender reasoning that if all stopped, all would be better off, and moving to stop.
The spend sitting at its height, and one player deciding to hold there while the rest go on.

## Action

[Ratcheting](process_ratcheting.md): [The Holdout](persona_the_holdout.md), holding [the holdout's seat](position_holdout.md), keeps its effort steady, while [The Rival](persona_the_rival.md), holding [the rival's seat](position_rival.md), climbs on and leaves it behind.

## Stage

[The Holdout](persona_the_holdout.md), having tabled [Sit It Out](plan_sit_it_out.md), declines the next round and waits for the field to see sense and level with it.
[The Rival](persona_the_rival.md), still on [Keep Pace](plan_keep_pace.md), does not follow but passes, so the holdout does not bring the line level, it simply drops, and the others read the lesson.

## Tension

The one who sees the race clearest is the one it drops.
There is no error in the holdout's sight: the race truly costs all and moves none, and stopping truly would help everyone. But stopping alone helps no one and costs the stopper its rank, so restraint is not a signal the others can follow, only a sacrifice they learn from, and the ratchet closes: the spend can rise and never fall, because whoever lowers it first simply loses.
