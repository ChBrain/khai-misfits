# Plot: React

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The trough](place_the_trough.md), the worst of it, where every eye and every hand now turns.
No one calls a meeting about an average week, so the response arrives at the extreme, and the extreme is where reversion is already on its way.

## Action

[Intervention](process_intervention.md): [The Boss](persona_the_boss.md), holding [the intervener's seat](position_intervener.md), comes down hard on the slump, while [The Axe](persona_the_axe.md) at [the blamer's seat](position_blamer.md) removes the one the slump landed on and brings in [The Rescuer](persona_the_rescuer.md) at the bottom.

## Stage

[The Boss](persona_the_boss.md) tables [React to the Extreme](plan_react_to_the_extreme.md): crack down where it is worst and praise where it is best, placing the action exactly at [the extreme](piece_the_extreme.md).
[The Axe](persona_the_axe.md) tables [Fire the Trough](plan_fire_the_trough.md): pin the bad draw on the person and replace them, so a fresh face stands at the bottom just as the rise begins.

## Tension

Every response is reasonable, and every one is mistimed by design.
Acting when things are worst is not just human but responsible: you intervene where the need is greatest, you do not shake up a team that is winning. But greatest need is measured by distance from the mean, and distance from the mean is mostly luck that will not repeat, so the diligent instinct to act at the extreme guarantees the action lands in the one spot where the numbers were about to improve on their own, and hands whoever acted a rise they did not make.
