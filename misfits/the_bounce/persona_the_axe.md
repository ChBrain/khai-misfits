# Persona: The Axe

## Taxonomy

[Blamer](position_blamer.md). Fires at the trough.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Unsentimental, accountable, clear.
No passengers, no excuses.
Reads a bad number and moves.

## Action

Pins the slump on the person.
Removes them at the low.
The replacement rides the bounce up.

## Shadow

The slump was a bad draw, not the person.
The fresh face inherits an unearned recovery.
The number rises, and confirms the wrong call.

## Tell

"Results are results, they had to go."
The results were about to rise on their own.
