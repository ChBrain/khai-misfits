# Persona: The Performer

## Taxonomy

[Operator](position_operator.md). Does the work; results swing on luck around a true mean.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady, competent, reliable.
Works at their real standard day to day.
The level holds even when the number does not.

## Action

Performs at a stable true level.
The result lands far from it on the draw of the day.
Some days the draw flatters, some days it stings.

## Shadow

Their true level never moved.
A lucky or unlucky draw reads as a change of standard.
That misread invites a reaction the level never called for.

## Tell

"Some weeks it just goes your way."
And some it does not, for no reason that lasts.
