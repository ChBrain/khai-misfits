# Piece: The Mean

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The true underlying level that every extreme drifts back toward.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Under [the baseline](place_the_baseline.md), the level the scatter is scattered around.
The real quality of the thing, held steady while its results swing: the team's actual capability, the process's actual rate, the performer's actual standard. Present in every result and visible in none of them alone.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no reversion and no misfit, only results that wander at random.
With it every extreme has a home to return to, because the Mean is the level the noise sits around: a result far from it is mostly a large draw of luck, and the next result, drawn again around the same Mean, will almost certainly sit closer in, all by itself. It is the quiet engine of the whole play, the thing that makes troughs rise and peaks fall with nothing done, and it is invisible precisely because it is never any single number, only the centre they are all thrown around.

## Apparent

Nothing at all, or last quarter's figure.
It looks like there is no such thing, only the results themselves, each taken as the truth of the moment. The Mean does not appear on the scoreboard, which shows outcomes and not the level they vary around, so the eye reads each extreme as a real change of standard rather than as a swing around a standard that never moved.

## Yearbook

Named by [the doubter](persona_the_doubter.md), and shown at last by [the designer](persona_the_designer.md) who plots the trend.
It is the truth the play turns on: the Mean is what [the bounce](piece_the_bounce.md) returns to, so every reversion credited to an action was only a result coming home to the level it never really left.
