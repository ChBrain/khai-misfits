# Persona: The Rescuer

## Taxonomy

[Turnaround](position_turnaround.md). Credited with the bounce.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Confident, a fixer, in demand.
Arrives when things are worst.
Walks in already sure it will turn.

## Action

Parachutes in at the trough.
Rides the recovery up.
Takes the credit at the top.

## Shadow

He merely timed the trough.
The rise would have come anyway.
His arrival was the coincidence, not the cause.

## Tell

"Give me a mess and I will fix it."
He gives you the mean, and a bill.
