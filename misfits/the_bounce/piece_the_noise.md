# Piece: The Noise

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The luck around the mean that makes any single result swing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the scoreboard](place_the_scoreboard.md), in the difference between this result and the true level under it.
The scatter of chance in every outcome: the deal that happened to close, the bug that happened to bite, the customer who happened to churn, the week the weather turned. Real events, unrepeatable, adding up to a result above or below the mean for no reason that will recur.

## Load Bearing

Without it every result equals the mean and there are no extremes to react to.
With it results scatter, and the scatter is what gets mistaken for signal, because the Noise is large in any single number and invisible as noise: a result far from the mean is mostly a big draw of it, but nothing labels it luck, so the whole distance reads as real performance. It is what makes the extreme extreme, and what guarantees the next draw will be tamer, and it is the raw material the misfit is built from, chance dressed as change.

## Apparent

Signal. Real, meaningful performance.
It looks like the result means what it says: a bad quarter looks like a bad team, a great month like a breakthrough, because the Noise wears the same face as the truth and carries no mark of its own. The one thing that would tag it, a sense of how much any single result should be trusted, is exactly what the eye reacting to a lone number does not compute.

## Yearbook

Read as signal by [the boss](persona_the_boss.md), and sized for what it is only by [the statistician's seat](position_statistician.md).
It is the play's raw material: the Noise throws a result out to [the extreme](piece_the_extreme.md), and because it will not recur, the return toward [the mean](piece_the_mean.md) is guaranteed, and waiting to be claimed.
