# Persona: The Analyst

## Taxonomy

[Learner](position_learner.md). Draws the false causal lesson.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Observant, empirical, patient.
Trusts what he sees repeated.
Counts before he concludes.

## Action

Reads the pattern: punish then up, praise then down.
Banks the finding that toughness works.
Files it as settled, backed by the tape.

## Shadow

The pattern is real and reproducible.
So the lesson is confirmed forever, and wrong.
Every honest replication deepens the error.

## Tell

"Every time we get tough, it works."
Every time, because the mean obliges.
