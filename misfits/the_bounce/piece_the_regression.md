# Piece: The Regression

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The whole shape at once, and its costly cure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across [the scoreboard](place_the_scoreboard.md) and [the baseline](place_the_baseline.md), the extremes reacted to and the mean that reclaims them.
The phenomenon named: extremes regress toward the mean on their own, we act only at extremes, and we mistake the reversion for the fruit of the action, so a purely statistical fact becomes a causal lesson learned backwards.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a run of coincidences with no thesis.
With it the play has its law: the error needs no liar and no fool, only a mean, some noise, an eye that acts at extremes, and no view of the counterfactual. It is also, and this is the half the shrug leaves out, curable: judge the trend across many results rather than the last one, hold a control group back, weigh the base rate, and the bounce is unmasked as reversion, not effect. So the Regression carries its own remedy, which is not sharper reaction to extremes but the discipline to reach for the counterfactual the extreme destroys.

## Apparent

A textbook curiosity, safely far from real life.
It looks like a statistics-class footnote about heights and exam scores, nothing to do with how a real firm is run on real judgement and hard experience. That distance is the deepest cover, because the Regression works most powerfully exactly where people trust their own eyes and act on what they see, and it is invisible in the mirror, where every manager is sure their toughness is what turned things around.

## Yearbook

Broken, when it is broken, by [the designer](persona_the_designer.md) through [control](process_control.md), at the price of the fast, flattering certainty the bounce supplied.
It is the piece all the others resolve into: [the noise](piece_the_noise.md) throwing results to [the extreme](piece_the_extreme.md), [the bounce](piece_the_bounce.md) returning them to [the mean](piece_the_mean.md), and [the credit](piece_the_credit.md) minting [the false lesson](piece_the_false_lesson.md), make the Regression, and only the missing counterfactual unmakes it.
