# Piece: The Credit

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The reversion booked to the intervention that happened to precede it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the review](place_the_review.md), in the account that pairs the action with the recovery.
The misattribution made official: the turnaround credited to the new hire, the recovery credited to the crackdown, the decline blamed on the praise, the improvement blamed on nobody's luck. A real movement, assigned to a cause that did not make it.

## Load Bearing

Without it the bounce is just a fact and teaches nothing.
With it the bounce becomes a lesson, because the Credit is the step that turns a coincidence of timing into a claim of cause: the intervention sat before the reversion, the reversion pointed the right way, and the eye that cannot subtract what would have happened anyway books the whole of it to the act. It is the accounting error at the heart of the misfit, a genuine effect posted against the wrong ledger, and it balances perfectly because the true creditor, the mean, never files a claim.

## Apparent

Fair attribution, richly deserved.
It looks like giving credit where it is due: someone acted, things improved, so they earned the win. The Credit is persuasive exactly because a real improvement really did occur, and gratitude for a real thing feels like sound judgement, not error, so the wrong party is thanked with a completely clear conscience.

## Yearbook

Collected by [the rescuer](persona_the_rescuer.md) through [attribution](process_attribution.md), and disputed only by [the doubter](persona_the_doubter.md).
It is the hinge between fact and fallacy: the Credit takes [the bounce](piece_the_bounce.md), which owed nothing to anyone, and signs it over to the intervention, and from that signature [the false lesson](piece_the_false_lesson.md) is drawn.
