# Pitch: It Worked

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Confident, causal, drawn from experience.
We had a problem, we acted, it got better: we watched it happen, the sequence is plain, and only a fool argues with results.
The voice of hard-won practical wisdom, of a manager who learns from what they see, and what they see is that stepping in at the bottom turns things around.

## Undertow

Under the experience, a mean quietly doing the work.
The trough was going to rise and the peak was going to fall, with nothing done at all, because that is what extremes do, and the action that happened to sit before the reversion collects a credit it never earned.
No one is fooled by a trick: the numbers are real, the improvement is real, and the only thing wrong is invisible, which is the rise that would have come anyway, standing exactly where the proof should be.

## Nerve

The feeling of learning from results,
acting decisively and being vindicated by the very next number,
building a whole philosophy of management on a wave the mean was always going to send back.

## Echo

After the run: a firm certainty that toughness works,
a distrust of praise that punished the people who deserved it,
and a lesson learned so many times, and so wrongly, that no amount of experience will now unteach it.
