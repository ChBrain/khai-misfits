# Plot: Extreme

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The scoreboard](place_the_scoreboard.md), and a number well off its usual place.
A performance that has swung hard, mostly on a run of luck good or bad, sits at the far end of the range, too far from normal to be left alone.

## Action

[Variation](process_variation.md): [The Performer](persona_the_performer.md), holding [the operator's seat](position_operator.md), does the work at a steady true level, and the result lands far from it on the draw of the day.

## Stage

[The Performer](persona_the_performer.md) tables [Do the Work](plan_do_the_work.md): perform at their real standard, over which [the noise](piece_the_noise.md) scatters the outcome, so a single result can sit far above or below [the mean](piece_the_mean.md).
[The extreme](piece_the_extreme.md) stands on the board: an outlier that reads as a real, urgent change of standard, when it is mostly a large draw of luck that will not come again.

## Tension

Nothing has changed, and everything looks like it has.
The performer's true level held steady, and only the luck around it moved, but the scoreboard shows outcomes and not the level they vary around, so an ordinary swing reads as an emergency or a triumph. And the further out the result, the more of it is noise and the surer its retreat, yet the further out it is, the more it demands a response, so the board hands up, at exactly the wrong moment, the exact number most certain to be acted on.
