# Process: Intervention

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The hand that always arrives at the edge.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An extreme that demands a response.
A number loud enough to pull an actor in.

## Direction

The action placed at the edge, never the middle.
The hand reaching in exactly where the draw was wildest.

## Lever

Need measured by distance from the mean, which is mostly luck.
The further the outlier, the surer the call to act.

## Echo

The hand landing just before the reversion.
An arrival timed, by the extreme itself, to look decisive.
