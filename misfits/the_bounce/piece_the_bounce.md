# Piece: The Bounce

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The natural return of an extreme toward the mean, owing nothing to any action.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Along [the baseline](place_the_baseline.md), in the drift of the next result back toward the middle.
The reversion itself: the terrible quarter followed by a merely ordinary one, the record month followed by a good-not-great one, the collapse that steadies, the streak that cools. A movement toward the mean that happens on its own, because the luck that caused the extreme does not repeat.

## Load Bearing

Without it the extreme would persist and there would be nothing to misattribute.
With it there is always a rise after a trough and a fall after a peak, ready to be claimed, because the Bounce is regression to the mean in motion: an outlier is mostly noise, the noise will not recur, so the next draw lands closer to the centre with mathematical reliability and no cause at all. It is the free reversion that every intervention at an extreme gets to stand in front of, the wave the swimmer did not make but is seen to ride.

## Apparent

The result of whatever was just done.
It looks like an effect, because it follows an action and points the right way: we cracked down and the numbers rose, so the crackdown worked. The Bounce is the most convincing counterfeit of causation there is, since it reliably appears right after the intervention and in the hoped-for direction, and nothing about it announces that it would have come regardless.
