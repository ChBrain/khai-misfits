# Process: Variation

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The steady level that never shows a steady number.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Work done at a steady true level.
A hand that has not changed at all.

## Direction

Results scattered around the mean by luck.
Each outcome a draw, high or low, off the same center.

## Lever

Noise large in any single result, and unlabelled.
No mark on the number to say how much was chance.

## Echo

An extreme that reads as a change of standard.
A draw mistaken for a decision.
