# Persona: The Designer

## Taxonomy

[Experimenter](position_experimenter.md). The reversal: runs a control.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Patient, rigorous, unhurried.
Willing to leave a trough alone to learn.
Trades a good story for a true answer.

## Action

Holds some troughs back untouched.
Weighs the base rate against the intervention.
Plots the trend before he names a cause.

## Shadow

The fix is slow, and feels like negligence.
Doing nothing looks like doing nothing.
He spends the flattering story to buy the real one.

## Tell

"Let us see what it does if we do not touch it."
A cold, correct question.
