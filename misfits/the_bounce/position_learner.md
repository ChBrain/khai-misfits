# Position: Learner

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The honest student who draws the wrong rule from a true record.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The record, complete and faithfully kept.
The wish to learn from it, to turn observation into a rule.

## Orders

Draw the rule from what is observed.
Trust the pattern, since it is real and it repeats.

## Loses

The truth, hidden inside a pattern that is genuine and recurring.
The lesson feels earned because the correlation is there every time.

## Drives

Toward a confirmed lesson, reinforced on every trial.
Away from the counterfactual, never asked and never seen.
Harshness learned as wisdom, punishment credited with the fall that followed a rise.
