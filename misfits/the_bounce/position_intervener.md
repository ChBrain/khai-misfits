# Position: Intervener

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The authority that acts exactly where the number is most extreme.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The authority to act, to step in and change the case.
The instinct to act at extremes, where the pull to do something is loudest.

## Orders

Step in where it is worst, right at the trough.
Celebrate where it is best, right at the peak.

## Loses

The counterfactual, since the act destroys the untouched case.
No way left to see what the extreme would have done alone.

## Drives

Toward action at the edge, where the reversion is largest.
Away from the middle, where nothing seems to demand a hand.
The hand placed just before the bounce, and the bounce read as its work.
