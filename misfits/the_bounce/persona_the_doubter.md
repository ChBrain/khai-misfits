# Persona: The Doubter

## Taxonomy

[Statistician](position_statistician.md). Names the mean, unheard.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful, contrary, quiet.
Thinks in distributions.
Sees a spread where others see a story.

## Action

Points to the mean and the counterfactual.
Asks what would have happened untouched.
Is waved off before he finishes.

## Shadow

Right, and unwelcome.
He asks a room to distrust its own repeated observation.
That is a hard thing to sell to people who saw it work.

## Tell

"It would have gone up anyway."
Nobody wants that at the review.
