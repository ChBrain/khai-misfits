# Position: Experimenter

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The reversal seat, the one who holds a case back to see the mean move.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The will to hold a case back, to leave one extreme untouched.
The nerve to wait while others rush to act.

## Orders

Run a control, keep the untreated case beside the treated one.
Weigh the base rate, judge the trend, not the single swing.

## Loses

The fast, flattering certainty of the bounce.
The clean story that the act caused the recovery, given up for a slower truth.

## Drives

Toward the counterfactual, the case that shows what the mean would have done.
Away from acting at every extreme, the reflex the world rewards.
The reversion unmasked at a cost: the credit surrendered, the truth kept.
