# Position: Operator

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The steady hand that owns a level but not the luck laid over it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A steady true level, the honest average of the work.
No control of the luck, the swing the world adds on top.

## Orders

Do the work, the same work, day after day.
Hit the standard and let the number land where it lands.

## Loses

Nothing done differently, yet blamed on a bad draw.
Nothing done differently, yet praised on a good one.

## Drives

Toward a stable mean, the level that outlasts the day.
Away from the swing that gets read as change.
The level mistaken for its noise, and the noise credited to a cause.
