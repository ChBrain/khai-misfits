# Piece: The Extreme

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The outlier result that triggers the reaction, and guarantees the reversion.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the scoreboard](place_the_scoreboard.md), at the far end of the range, the number no one can ignore.
The result that stands out: the worst month in years, the record quarter, the sudden collapse, the dazzling streak. A figure so far from the usual that it demands a response, and gets one.

## Load Bearing

Without it no one acts, and the mean reverts unwatched and uncredited.
With it action is all but forced, because the Extreme is where attention and intervention land: nobody calls a meeting about an average week, so the reaction always arrives at the outlier, which is precisely the result most made of noise and most certain to be followed by a tamer one. The Extreme is the trigger and the trap in a single number, drawing the intervention to the exact spot where reversion was already on its way.

## Apparent

An emergency, or a triumph, demanding a response.
It looks like the moment that matters most, the one thing you must act on, because an outlier feels like the truest signal of all: surely a result this far out means something real and urgent. That felt urgency is the hook, since the further the result is from the mean, the larger its share of luck and the surer its retreat, and yet the further it is, the more it seems to compel a hand.

## Yearbook

Seized on by [the boss](persona_the_boss.md) and [the axe](persona_the_axe.md), and timed by [the rescuer](persona_the_rescuer.md) who arrives at the bottom.
It is the play's stage: because we act at the Extreme and not at [the mean](piece_the_mean.md), the intervention always sits just before [the bounce](piece_the_bounce.md), and inherits a reversion it did not cause.
