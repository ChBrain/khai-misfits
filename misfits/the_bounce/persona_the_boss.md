# Persona: The Boss

## Taxonomy

[Intervener](position_intervener.md). Acts at the extreme.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Decisive, hands-on, present.
Acts where the need is greatest.
Waits for no one to ask twice.

## Action

Comes down hard on the slump.
Praises the peak while it shines.
Times the move to the moment of most heat.

## Shadow

Acting at the extreme lands the action right before the reversion.
The reversion then takes the credit, or the blame.
Either way the story writes itself around the intervention.

## Tell

"I stepped in and turned it around."
The mean turned it around.
