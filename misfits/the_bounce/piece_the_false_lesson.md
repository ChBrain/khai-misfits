# Piece: The False Lesson

## Taxonomy

[The Bounce](play_the_bounce.md). The backwards rule drawn from the credited reversion.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the review](place_the_review.md), in the principle written up from the pattern.
The conclusion banked: toughness works, because we came down hard at the bottom and things improved; praise backfires, because we celebrated at the top and things got worse; rescuers turn firms around; the person the slump landed on had to go. A rule, drawn straight and drawn wrong.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit is a single mistimed pat on the back and harms little.
With it the mistake becomes doctrine, because the False Lesson generalises the misattribution into policy: since reward at a peak is reliably followed by a fall and punishment at a trough by a rise, an observer learns, correctly from the data and backwards about the world, that punishment helps and reward hurts. It is the misfit's cruelty compounded, a lesson that is not only wrong but wrong in the direction of harshness, and that experience will confirm again every time it is applied, because the mean will oblige again every time.

## Apparent

Wisdom earned the hard way.
It looks like exactly the kind of lesson worth trusting: not theory but observation, repeated, consistent, borne out by results time after time. That track record is the trap, because regression will reproduce the pattern for as long as anyone tests it, so the False Lesson is self-confirming, and the more experience a person has, the more certain of it they become.

## Yearbook

Banked by [the analyst](persona_the_analyst.md) under [bank the lesson](plan_bank_the_lesson.md), and hardened by [entrenchment](process_entrenchment.md) into how the place is run.
It is where the misfit does its damage: built from [the credit](piece_the_credit.md), the False Lesson turns [the regression](piece_the_regression.md) into a management philosophy that punishes the unlucky and mistrusts the deserving.
