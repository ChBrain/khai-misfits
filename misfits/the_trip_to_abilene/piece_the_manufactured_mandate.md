# Piece: The Manufactured Mandate

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The consensus no seat holds, read off the table and cited as authority.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the record](place_the_record.md), where the nods are totalled and named.
The moment someone says it plainly: great, so we're all agreed, and no hand goes up to say otherwise.

## Load Bearing

Without it the accord stays a mood and could still dissolve.
With it the mood becomes a fact with a citation: the Manufactured Mandate is the agreement no one privately holds, converted into the authority everyone must now defer to. Its power is that it is nobody's and therefore unanswerable: to challenge it is to challenge the group, and no seat will set itself against a will that every seat believes every other seat holds. It is a mandate assembled entirely from misreadings, and it governs as firmly as a real one.

## Apparent

The will of the room.
It looks like democracy working: a proposal, assent all round, a decision carried. Every procedural box is ticked. The only thing missing is a single person who wanted the outcome.

## Yearbook

Declared by [the tally](persona_the_tally.md), who counts the yeses and never the hearts.
Once spoken, it is load-bearing for everything after: [the chair](persona_the_chair.md) ratifies on its authority, and the trip proceeds as the settled will of a room that, to a person, was hoping to be talked out of it.
