# Piece: The Silence Travels

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The travelling axis: each public assent becoming the next seat's evidence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Around [the round](place_the_round.md), seat to seat, gathering as it goes.
The nod at the first chair is watched by the second, whose nod is watched by the third, each yes joining the pile the next person weighs themselves against.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit is one shy person and a bad afternoon.
With it it is a system: the Silence Travels because every swallowed objection presents, to the next seat, as one more contented face, so the apparent consensus grows exactly as the real one does not. It is the compounding line of the play, the false majority accumulating around the table one courtesy at a time, and it runs one way only, because a nod can be added to the pile but a doubt, unspoken, never subtracts from it.

## Apparent

Momentum.
It looks like agreement building naturally, warmth gathering into a decision, the room finding its mind. What is actually gathering is evidence for an error, each person's silence confirming the next person's misread.

## Yearbook

Carried by all seven seats and stopped by none, until [the drive back](plot_the_drive_back.md).
It crosses no one's view as a total, because each seat sees only the faces before its turn, never the truth behind them. By the time it reaches [the record](place_the_record.md) it is [a mandate](piece_the_manufactured_mandate.md), and the pile of courtesies is called the will of the group.
