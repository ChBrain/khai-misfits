# Persona: The Chair

## Taxonomy

[Ratifier](position_ratifier.md). The one who makes it official, and holds none of it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Procedural, correct, even-handed.
Trusts the process to hold the room.
Only ever ratifies what it hears.

## Action

Takes the declared consensus at its word.
Formalises the decision on it.
Closes the item cleanly.

## Shadow

Ratifies a will no seat holds.
Its correctness is the trap: every box ticked.
The one missing thing is a person who wanted it, and no box asks for that.

## Tell

Says, "hearing no objections, it's carried."
There were objections, all swallowed.
