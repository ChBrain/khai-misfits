# Position: Reckoner

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The private truth, spoken once it is too late.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The private truth and, at last, nothing to lose by it.
The words that would have stopped it, now only good for naming it.

## Orders

Say what happened.
Name the trip, the nods, the silence that carried them.
Tell it plain, the way it should have been told at the table.

## Loses

Nothing now.
The cost of speaking is gone with the trip.
There was a price for the word once, and the trip already paid it.

## Drives

Toward the truth, after it can only name the damage, not prevent it.
Toward the reckoning, honest and far too late.
Toward the room finally speaking, once speaking changes nothing.
