# Plot: Drive Back

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The drive back](place_the_drive_back.md), the trip over and the cost plainly landed.
It was hot, it was far, it was nobody's good idea, and the car is quiet in a new way. Someone is about to say the private thing at last.

## Action

[Recrimination](process_recrimination.md): [The Breaker](persona_the_breaker.md), holding [the reckoner's seat](position_reckoner.md), says out loud the sentence every chest has been holding, that they never wanted to go, and waits to be the only one.

## Stage

[The Breaker](persona_the_breaker.md) tables [Break the Silence](plan_break_the_silence.md): confess the private no after the fact, and instead of standing alone is answered by six others saying the same.
[The agreement](piece_the_agreement.md) falls in a single sentence, revealed as held by nobody: total in public, empty in every private chest, standing the whole time on each seat's fear of being the first to sit down.

## Tension

Nobody was a villain.
No one lied, no one bullied, every yes was kindly meant. The reckoning is not courage arriving in time: it arrives after the road has run out, when the truth is free to say because the trip is already taken. One admission would have stopped it at any point ... or the silence holds, the confession is swallowed with the rest, and the room learns nothing and drives to Abilene again.
