# Position: Consensus Reader

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The count of nods, mistaken for the hearts behind them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The count of nods and the floor to name it.
A tally, and the authority to call it agreement.

## Orders

Read the room, report the agreement.
Add the yeses, announce the number.
Say we are all on board, and make it so by saying.

## Loses

The hearts behind the faces.
Every private no, uncounted, invisible in the tally.
The sum is clean and the sum is wrong.

## Drives

Toward a clean consensus, assembled from misreads.
Toward the neat report over the true one.
Toward agreement declared, which nobody quite feels.
