# Plot: Trial Balloon

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The porch](place_the_porch.md), in the easy part of an afternoon.
An idea is floated to fill a silence, not to start a journey. At [the table](place_the_table.md) the first responses are about to be given, and nobody has weighed the thing yet.

## Action

[Flotation](process_flotation.md): [The Floater](persona_the_floater.md), holding [the initiator's seat](position_initiator.md), tosses out the suggestion lightly, owning it barely.
[Accommodation](process_accommodation.md): [The Obliger](persona_the_obliger.md) at [the accommodator's seat](position_accommodator.md) nods to keep the room warm, and [The Withholder](persona_the_withholder.md) at [the dissenter's seat](position_dissenter.md) counts the nods and says nothing.

## Stage

[The Floater](persona_the_floater.md) tables [Float the Idea](plan_float_the_idea.md): put it out without standing behind it, and [the trial balloon](piece_the_trial_balloon.md) is on the air.
[The Obliger](persona_the_obliger.md) tables [Keep the Peace](plan_keep_the_peace.md): agree to be agreeable, and gives [the nod](piece_the_nod.md).
[The Withholder](persona_the_withholder.md) tables [Read the Room](plan_read_the_room.md): see the nods already given, and keeps [the swallowed objection](piece_the_swallowed_objection.md) behind its teeth.

## Tension

Nobody here wants the trip.
The idea is floated without conviction, the nod is a courtesy, and the objection is real and unspoken. A rough accord forms that no seat has endorsed, and it forms precisely because each seat is being kind: the friction they spare the room is the friction that would have saved it.
