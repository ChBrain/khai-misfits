# Process: Inference

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The sideways station: calm faces read as agreement.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A table of calm faces.
Each one still, each one reading stillness in the rest.

## Direction

Silence to imagined consensus.
The quiet gets translated into a yes no one said.

## Lever

The fear of standing alone against the private no.
Each swallows the objection rather than be the only one.

## Echo

Many noes locking into one yes.
Private refusals, added up, come out as public agreement.
