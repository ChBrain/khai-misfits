# Piece: The Read

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). Each seat inferring the others are for it, and being wrong.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Inside every head at [the round](place_the_round.md), doing the same silent arithmetic.
Looking around, counting the calm faces, and concluding: they all seem to want this, so I must be the odd one out.

## Load Bearing

Without it the swallowed objections would stay merely private, and someone might still ask.
With it they lock: the Read is the mechanism that turns many private noes into one public yes, because each person mistakes everyone else's identical silence for a contentment they alone lack. It is pluralistic ignorance made load-bearing, a whole table each misreading the others by exactly the same error, and each treating that misreading as the fact they must accommodate.

## Apparent

Perceptiveness.
It feels like reading the room well, like social skill: sensing the group's wish and not making a scene against it. It is reading the room precisely wrong, and calling the error tact.

## Yearbook

Run by every seat at once, and named by [the withholder](persona_the_withholder.md) who feels it most.
It is the play's cruelest symmetry: if any two of them compared reads, the spell would break, but comparing reads is the one thing the fear of being the holdout forbids. So [the silence travels](piece_the_silence_travels.md) instead, and every seat stays sure it is alone.
