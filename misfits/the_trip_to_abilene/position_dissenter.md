# Position: Dissenter

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The one seat that could stop it and doesn't.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The true objection, whole and unspoken.
A word in the throat that would end the whole trip.

## Orders

Go along, do not make a scene.
Swallow it, nod with the rest.
Save the objection for a later that never comes.

## Loses

Its voice, to the fear of being the lone holdout.
The one seat that could stop it and the one that will not.
The saving word, kept safe and useless in the pocket.

## Drives

Toward not standing alone, with the saving word right there and unsaid.
Toward the crowd, away from the true thing it knows.
Toward the trip it hates, rather than the silence it dreads.
