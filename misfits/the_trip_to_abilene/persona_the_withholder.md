# Persona: The Withholder

## Taxonomy

[Dissenter](position_dissenter.md). The fullest objection in the room, and the quietest.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Privately unconvinced, outwardly calm.
Carries the whole no, folded, unspoken.
Smiles at the table, doubts under it.

## Action

Reads the room, counts the nods.
Decides it is the lone holdout.
Swallows the no to spare everyone the friction.

## Shadow

Holds the true state of the room and reveals none of it.
The cheapest saving move, just saying it, is the one it cannot afford socially.
Its silence is a courtesy, and the courtesy is what buys the ticket.

## Tell

Opens its mouth, sees a nod, closes it.
Says, "no, it's fine," when it is not.
