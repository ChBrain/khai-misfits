# Persona: The Tally

## Taxonomy

[Consensus Reader](position_consensus_reader.md). The one who counts faces and calls it a vote.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Attentive, fair, good at reading a room.
Prides itself on catching the mood.
Wants to name what everyone feels.

## Action

Counts the calm faces around the table.
Names the agreement it sees there.
Calls it settled, gently.

## Shadow

Totals the yeses and never the hearts.
A pile of courtesies converts into a mandate.
Reads the room precisely wrong and calls it perception, kindly, sure it is helping.

## Tell

Says, "great, so we're all agreed?"
To a silence it mistakes for assent.
