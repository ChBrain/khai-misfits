# Position: Accommodator

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The warm yes, mistaken for a vote.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A warm yes ready.
A face that softens before the sentence lands.

## Orders

Agree, keep the peace.
Say sure, say why not, say sounds good.
Fill the pause so no one has to sit in it.

## Loses

The distinction between courtesy and consent.
A kindness gets counted as a ballot.
Nobody hears the difference, least of all the room.

## Drives

Toward harmony, taken for endorsement.
Toward the smooth surface over the honest one.
Toward pleasing, which the table reads as approving.
