# Persona: The Goer

## Taxonomy

[Executor](position_executor.md). The one who drives, doubting, because it is decided.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Dependable, sees things through.
Trusts a settled thing to be worth doing.
Not one to reopen what the room closed.

## Action

Carries out the decided thing.
Privately doubtful, publicly moving.
Does it because it is settled, and settled is enough.

## Shadow

Drives past the point of no return.
Its own follow-through becomes a reason to keep the doubt swallowed.
Commitment to the mistake hardens it, and loyalty wears the mask of resolve.

## Tell

Says, "well, it's decided now."
And drives.
