# Position: Initiator

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The first voice, floating a plan it never wanted.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The first word, and no stake in it.
A room warm enough to say anything into.

## Orders

Float the idea, keep it light.
Toss it out like weather, no weight on it.
Watch the nods, mistake them for wanting.

## Loses

Any grip on the thing once it is out.
Cannot recall a balloon everyone has nodded to.
The idea belongs to the room now, and the room is agreeing.

## Drives

Toward an easy room, not a real trip.
Toward the comfort of a plan that fills the silence.
Toward yes, and away from meaning it.
