# Process: Flotation

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The sideways station: a maybe let loose to drift.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A silence someone fills with a maybe.
The quiet grew heavy, so a light word was offered.

## Direction

An idea onto the air, unowned.
It leaves the mouth and belongs to no one after.

## Lever

Lightness against scrutiny, too casual to weigh.
The idea floats precisely because nobody is asked to catch it.

## Echo

An unopposed plan nobody argued for.
Silence around it reads as a room that agrees.
