# Plot: Do

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The round](place_the_round.md), where the table goes seat to seat for its yes.
The rough accord is being read back as settled, and each person is about to weigh their private doubt against the public faces before them.

## Action

[Inference](process_inference.md): [The Tally](persona_the_tally.md), holding [the consensus reader's seat](position_consensus_reader.md), reads the calm faces as agreement and each private doubt does the same arithmetic, taking the assent already given for a majority and falling in line.

## Stage

[The Tally](persona_the_tally.md) tables [Call the Consensus](plan_call_the_consensus.md): total the nods and name them as the room's mind, and [the read](piece_the_read.md) locks, every seat sure it is the lone holdout.
Seat to seat, [the silence travels](piece_the_silence_travels.md): each swallowed no presents as one more contented face, so the apparent agreement grows exactly as the real one does not.

## Tension

Any two compared reads would break it.
The doubt is in every chair and the spell needs only one person to ask the next whether they truly want this. Nobody asks, because asking risks being the one who spoiled the accord, and so the misread compounds one courtesy at a time into a mandate no one holds.
