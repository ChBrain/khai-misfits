# Persona: The Floater

## Taxonomy

[Initiator](position_initiator.md). The one who drops a stone and calls it a ripple.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Casual, unattached, floating a maybe.
Wants the room easy more than the trip.
Just thinking aloud, nothing settled.

## Action

Floats the idea lightly, palms open.
Ready to disown it the second a face turns.
Never once says, "I want this."

## Shadow

No conviction, so no one scrutinises.
An unwanted idea drifts through as an unopposed plan.
The lightest touch starts the heaviest drift, and the touch felt like kindness.

## Tell

Says, "we could always..." and means nothing by it.
The room takes it for a proposal.
