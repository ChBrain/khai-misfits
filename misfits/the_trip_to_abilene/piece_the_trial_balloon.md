# Piece: The Trial Balloon

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The idea floated without being wanted, owned by no one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

On [the porch](place_the_porch.md), tossed out lightly, half as a question.
The offhand suggestion made to fill a silence, not to start a journey: we could always drive to Abilene.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to agree to, and the room stays where it is.
With it there is a proposal on the air that nobody has actually endorsed, including the one who made it. The Trial Balloon is deniable by design: floated so lightly that its own author could disown it, which is exactly why no one weighs it, and exactly why it survives. A thing no one is committed to is a thing no one is moved to stop.

## Apparent

A passing thought.
It looks like small talk, a low-stakes maybe, the kind of thing a room bats down without a second thought. It expects to be bounced. It is not.

## Yearbook

Floated by [the floater](persona_the_floater.md), who wanted the room's ease more than the trip.
It is the lightest thing in the play and the seed of the whole weight: because it was offered without conviction, it is met without scrutiny, and an idea nobody argued for becomes a plan nobody argued against.
