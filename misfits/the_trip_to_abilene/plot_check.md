# Plot: Check

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The record](place_the_record.md), where the accord is written down as a decision.
The mandate is being cited as authority. Nobody has said no, so the will of the room is declared, and the car is warming up on [the road](place_the_road.md).

## Action

[Ratification](process_ratification.md): [The Chair](persona_the_chair.md), holding [the ratifier's seat](position_ratifier.md), formalises the decision on the strength of a consensus no seat holds.
[Execution](process_execution.md): [The Goer](persona_the_goer.md) at [the executor's seat](position_executor.md) drives it out, privately doubtful, because it is decided now.

## Stage

[The Chair](persona_the_chair.md) tables [Ratify the Will](plan_ratify_the_will.md): carry the motion on the mandate, and [the manufactured mandate](piece_the_manufactured_mandate.md) governs as firmly as a real one.
[The Goer](persona_the_goer.md) tables [Carry it Out](plan_carry_it_out.md): see it through now that it is settled, and the trip rolls past [the point of no return](piece_the_point_of_no_return.md), where turning back would cost more face than going on.

## Tension

Every box is ticked.
A proposal, assent all round, a motion carried, a plan executed: the procedure is flawless and the outcome is wanted by no one. Past the point of no return each seat has its own going-along invested in the trip being fine, so the momentum of the mistake becomes the argument for finishing it, and the thing nobody wanted is done, on the record, unanimously.
