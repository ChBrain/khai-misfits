# Pitch: All in Favour

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Warm, easy, agreeable.
Every face around the table is nodding, and every nod is offered kindly.
The voice of a room getting along, spoken by people who each want, above all, not to be the one who spoils it.

## Undertow

Under the accord, an empty centre.
Each yes is given to the others' yes, and no yes is given to the thing.
The warmth never breaks: it only carries the table somewhere none of them chose.

## Nerve

The feeling of a room in perfect agreement,
made of people who are each privately elsewhere,
all waiting for someone else to say the thing they are all thinking.

## Echo

After the run: a decision no one defends,
a table of people who each thought they were the only holdout,
and a trip that was unanimous, and wanted by nobody.
