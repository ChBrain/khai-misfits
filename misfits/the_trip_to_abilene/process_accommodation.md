# Process: Accommodation

## Taxonomy

[The Trip to Abilene](play_the_trip_to_abilene.md). The sideways station: a wish to be agreeable, folded into a yes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A floated idea and a wish to be agreeable.
The maybe lands beside a person who hates to be difficult.

## Direction

Assent to the mood.
Not toward the plan, toward the warmth in the room.

## Lever

Warmth against candour, kindness read as consent.
Being nice costs less than being honest, so nice wins.

## Echo

A courtesy filed as a vote.
A gesture meant to soothe gets counted as a choice.
