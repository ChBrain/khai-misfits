# Persona: The Obliger

## Taxonomy

[Accommodator](position_accommodator.md). The one who says yes to the mood, not the map.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Warm, easy, keen to please.
Reads the room's wish and wants to meet it.
Happiest when everyone is happy.

## Action

Nods quickly to keep the mood soft.
Agrees to be agreeable, before the question lands.
Gives the room what it seems to want.

## Shadow

Its courtesy photographs as consent.
A gift to the room's mood gets entered as a vote on the room's course.
The kindest reflex in the room is the one that seals the trip.

## Tell

Says, "sounds good, whatever everyone wants."
The "whatever everyone wants" is the whole trap.
