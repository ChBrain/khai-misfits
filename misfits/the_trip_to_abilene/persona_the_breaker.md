# Persona: The Breaker

## Taxonomy

[Reckoner](position_reckoner.md). The one who says it out loud, once it costs nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Plain, a little rueful, done pretending.
Carries the private thing to the surface.
Expects to be alone in it.

## Action

Says the private thing out loud, after.
Names what the trip actually was.
Braces for the room to turn.

## Shadow

Not brave, just late.
Speaks when the truth is finally cheap, once the trip is taken.
The reckoning cascades but saves nothing already spent.

## Tell

Says, "honestly, I never wanted to go."
And watches six faces fall in relief.
