# Position: Dispatcher

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The seat that routes the many: one shortest path, applied at scale.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The reach to route a fleet, or a city of phones, at once.
One algorithm, one map, and every trip sent by the same rule.

## Orders

Send each driver by the path the model calls quickest.
Optimise every trip in isolation, and trust that the sum takes care of itself.
Scale the selfish route until it is an instruction, not an accident.

## Loses

The load its own routing creates, invisible to a per-trip optimum.
A seat that reads advertised times, not the congestion its dispatch pours onto them.

## Drives

Toward a coordinated rush onto the shortest link, and away from the spread that would ease it.
Toward the equilibrium hardened by scale, where each best trip makes the whole worse.
