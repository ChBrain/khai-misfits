# Plot: The Jam

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The commute](place_the_commute.md), and a congestion everyone can see and everyone wants gone.
The old roads are full at rush hour, the pressure is real, and the answer seems obvious: the network is short of road, so give it more.

## Action

[Rerouting](process_rerouting.md): [The Planner](persona_the_planner.md), holding [the planner's seat](position_planner.md), opens a new link to relieve the load, in good faith and to spec.

## Stage

[The Planner](persona_the_planner.md) tables [Add the Road](plan_add_the_road.md): answer the jam with capacity rather than a price, so [the extra road](piece_the_extra_road.md) is built where [the jam](piece_the_jam.md) was worst and opened free to every driver.
The link is real relief for the first cars onto it, and counted, at the ribbon, as the network improved.

## Tension

Nothing here is careless, and it lays the whole trap.
Answering a jam with a road is the picture of responsible planning: real capacity, honestly built, exactly where the congestion is. But the road is opened free, so the cost each driver will add to it is priced at zero, and from this ribbon on, the network has a link that is best for each to take and worst for all to load ... and no one has decided anything but to help.
