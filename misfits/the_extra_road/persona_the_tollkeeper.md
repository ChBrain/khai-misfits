# Persona: The Tollkeeper

## Taxonomy

[Pricer](position_pricer.md). The reversal: puts a price on the shared stretch instead of leaving it free.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Unsentimental. Willing to charge for a road, or take one away.
Believes the network was never short of asphalt, only short of a price on the load.
Reads the paradox and reaches for the one lever that moves the whole at once.

## Action

Prices the loaded link, so each driver pays the delay they impose.
Or closes the extra road entirely, and lets the old balance return.
Aligns the selfish route with the system route, and the network speeds up.

## Shadow

The cure is a charge on the free, or the loss of the built.
Pricing works, and it is resented: it asks drivers to pay for a road that was free, or gives back a road that was new.
The honest cost of the optimum is that someone must defend a toll no one wanted and a closure that looks like waste.

## Tell

"The road isn't free. It never was. Now the price is visible."
The move that recovers the whole, and the one hardest to keep.
