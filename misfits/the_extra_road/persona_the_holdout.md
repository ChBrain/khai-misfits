# Persona: The Holdout

## Taxonomy

[Keeper](position_keeper.md). The one who stays on the old route, for the good of a whole that does not reward it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady. A little stubborn. Loyal to the road that used to work.
Keeps to the old way while everyone peels off onto the new link, out of habit, principle, or a sense that the balance was better.
Would be right if others held with them, and is punished for holding alone.

## Action

Refuses the shortcut and drives the route that was fast before the road came.
Bears a rising time while the loaded link gives nothing back, because holding helps only if held together.
Becomes, slowly, the last one on a road the network has abandoned.

## Shadow

Right for the whole, and worse off for it.
The Holdout's route is the one a coordinated network would want more drivers on, and unilateral loyalty just loses time.
Doing the system-optimal thing alone is not a stand, it is a handicap.

## Tell

"Someone has to stay off it. It just costs me to be the one."
The virtue that only pays if it is shared, and never is.
