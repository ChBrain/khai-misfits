# Position: Follower

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The seat that gives way: it held the old route until holding stopped paying.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A breaking point, and the arithmetic that reaches it.
The option to keep losing time alone, or to join the link like everyone else.

## Orders

Hold while holding helps, and switch the moment it does not.
Watch the old route empty and slow, and do not be the last one on it.
Convert, late and rationally, and close the equilibrium over the last gap.

## Loses

The stand, once the numbers turn against it.
A seat that can only justify loyalty by shared sacrifice loses the justification when the sharing ends.

## Drives

Toward the shortcut the network has already chosen, and away from the route no one else will hold.
Toward the total equilibrium, where even the reluctant have been priced onto the link.
