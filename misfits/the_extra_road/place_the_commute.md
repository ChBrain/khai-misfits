# Place: The Commute

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The daily flow, where the jam is felt and the slowdown is measured.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Rush hour, every morning, the same roads carrying the same crowd.
The lived experience of the network: the queue, the wait, the clock that reads the trip.

## Holds

The congestion, and the equilibrium the flows settle into.
The steady pattern that stops changing because no driver can gain by changing it.

## Offers

A true reading of the whole, for anyone who measures the average.
The one place the slowdown is undeniable: the trip that takes longer than it did, on more road.

## Withheld

The counterfactual. The commute shows today's time, never the faster time the network would hold without the link.
