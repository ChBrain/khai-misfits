# Piece: The Toll

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The reversal: the price on the shared stretch that puts each driver's cost back on the driver.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the toll plaza](place_the_toll_plaza.md), where using the loaded link finally costs the one who loads it.
The charge on the bypass, the congestion price on the shared road, the closure that takes the link away entirely. A cost placed exactly where the network was leaking one: on the driver whose choice to join makes everyone else slower.

## Load Bearing

Without it the shared stretch stays free to load, and the selfish route stays rational all the way down.
With it the gap between private and social cost is closed: charge a driver for the delay they impose, and the minimum-time choice and the least-total-time choice come back into line, so the equilibrium moves off the slow point without anyone having to drive against their own interest. The Toll is load bearing because it is the one lever that can shift the whole network at once, the price that makes each driver's sensible choice finally add up to a sensible whole.

## Apparent

A tax. A barrier. Punishment for driving.
It looks like the network being made worse on purpose: a charge where there was a free road, a gate where there was open capacity, a cost imposed on drivers who did nothing wrong. That reading is why the Toll is so easily pulled, because it asks people to pay for a road that used to be free, and the relief it buys is invisible next to the charge it levies.

## Yearbook

Set by [the tollkeeper](persona_the_tollkeeper.md) through [pricing](process_pricing.md), against every [selfish route](piece_the_selfish_route.md).
It is the remedy the play holds: the Toll is what breaks [the equilibrium](piece_the_equilibrium.md), undoes [the slowdown](piece_the_slowdown.md), and gives [the braess](piece_the_braess.md) its answer ... at the cost of a road no longer free, or no longer there.
