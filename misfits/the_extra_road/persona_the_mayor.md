# Persona: The Mayor

## Taxonomy

[Steward](position_steward.md). The one who answers for the whole network, and must read the average.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Accountable. Public. Judged on how the city moves, not on any one trip.
Opened the road to a ribbon and a promise of relief, and now holds the numbers that came back.
Wants the whole to be faster and has to say, out loud, whether it is.

## Action

Measures the network end to end and finds every driver slower than before.
Names the slowdown, or does not: credits the road, or indicts it.
Decides whether the paradox is seen or the demand is blamed.

## Shadow

The honest average is the one number the ribbon-cutting cannot survive.
To read the slowdown truly is to admit the new road made the city worse, which is the hardest finding to sign.
The easy move is to call it growth and ask for another link, and the paradox rewards the easy move.

## Tell

"We built the road. Why is the commute longer?"
The question that either opens the toll or buys the next road.
