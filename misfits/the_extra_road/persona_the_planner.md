# Persona: The Planner

## Taxonomy

[Planner](position_planner.md). The one who answers a real jam by adding a real road.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Practical. Responsible. Sure that more capacity is more relief.
Sees a congested network and a clear duty: add a link, widen the way, give the traffic somewhere to go.
Reads the road as a gift and cannot see how a gift could hurt.

## Action

Opens the extra road to relieve the load.
Builds true capacity where the jam was worst, in good faith and to spec.
Counts links added, not loads moved, and calls the network improved.

## Shadow

The relief is for each driver and the cost is to all of them.
The road that helps one at a time draws everyone at once, and the whole slows.
The Planner improved a link and degraded a graph, and never meant to.

## Tell

"We added a road. How could that make it worse?"
The most reasonable question in the play, and the one the paradox exists to answer.
