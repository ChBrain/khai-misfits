# Process: Equilibration

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The network settling into the state no one can beat by switching.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Drivers switching routes until switching stops helping.
The last drivers leaving the old route, the flows finding their level.

## Direction

The routing settling onto a fixed pattern, stable and slow.
Every driver already on their best remaining option, so none has a move that gains.

## Lever

The absence of any unilateral improvement.
The stability that makes the bad balance persist without a hand holding it there.

## Echo

A settled equilibrium at the slow point.
Worse for all, unbeatable by any one, and immovable except together.
