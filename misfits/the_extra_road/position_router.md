# Position: Router

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The seat that picks a path: it answers only for its own travel time.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The choice of route, and a reason to make it selfishly.
The freedom to take whatever link is quickest right now.

## Orders

Minimise your own time. Take the fast road. Respond to conditions.
Owe the network nothing: the trip is yours, the total is no one's.
Switch whenever switching helps you, and never for the whole.

## Loses

The total time, which no router is asked to carry.
A seat measured on its own arrival cannot price the delay it hands the rest.

## Drives

Toward the minimum-time route, whatever it loads.
Away from the coordinated choice that no single router can be asked to make, and toward the equilibrium their sensible choices build.
