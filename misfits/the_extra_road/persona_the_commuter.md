# Persona: The Commuter

## Taxonomy

[Router](position_router.md). The one who takes the route that is quickest for them, exactly as they should.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ordinary. Sensible. Just trying to get home.
Reads the road ahead, sees the new link is faster, and takes it, the way anyone would.
Owes the network nothing and knows it: the job is their own travel time, not the total.

## Action

Reroutes onto the extra road because it saves them a minute.
Responds to conditions, not to consequences, and there is no better advice to give them.
Adds their car to the shared stretch and never feels the delay they hand the rest.

## Shadow

Every Commuter is right, and together they are the load.
The minute saved by taking the link is paid, many times over, by everyone the link now carries.
No one drove badly, and the network is slower for all their good driving.

## Tell

"It's faster this way. Why would I take the long road?"
Optimal play, stated plainly, and the reason the whole thing settles slow.
