# Plan: Price the Road

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the pricer's seat](position_pricer.md), inside [The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Pricer](position_pricer.md)

## Direction

Put a price on the load. Charge each driver for the delay they impose on the shared stretch, so the selfish route and the system route come back into line. Or close the link, and give the old balance back.

## Orders

(The Pricer tolls the loaded link, or removes it, and the network shifts off the slow equilibrium at last.)

## Implementation

([The Toll Plaza](place_the_toll_plaza.md); the [pricing](process_pricing.md) process; a charge that meets private cost to social cost, defended against every driver who resents it.)

## Targets

- [ ] the shared stretch carries a price on the load
- [ ] each driver pays the delay they impose
- [ ] the selfish route is aligned with the system route
- [ ] the optimum is recovered, at the cost of a free or a built road
