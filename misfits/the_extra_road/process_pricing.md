# Process: Pricing

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The reversal: charging for the load instead of leaving the link free.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone putting a price on the shared stretch, or closing it.
A hand that charges the delay each driver imposes, where it was charged to no one.

## Direction

The cost of the load moved back onto the driver who creates it.
Private cost lifted to meet social cost, so the selfish route and the system route align.

## Lever

A toll that makes each sensible choice add up to a sensible whole.
The one lever that shifts the network off the slow point at once.

## Echo

The optimum recovered, the flow restored.
Bought at the price of a road no longer free, or a road no longer there.
