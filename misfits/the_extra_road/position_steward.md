# Position: Steward

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The seat that answers for the whole: it must read the average and say what it means.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The one view of the total, and the duty to report it.
The numbers for the whole network, and a name to sign under them.

## Orders

Measure end to end, and tell the truth about the average.
Credit the road or indict it, name the slowdown or blame the demand.
Own the outcome of a link you were told would help.

## Loses

The clean story of a road that relieved a jam.
A seat that reads the honest total cannot keep the ribbon-cutting's promise once the paradox has bitten.

## Drives

Toward the whole measured plainly, or toward the easy demand-story that buys the next road.
Toward the toll, if the slowdown is faced, and toward more asphalt, if it is not.
