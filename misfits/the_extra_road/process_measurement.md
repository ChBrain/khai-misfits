# Process: Measurement

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). Reading the whole network end to end, and seeing it slow.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Someone averaging the trips instead of praising the road.
A hand that reads the total time, not the ribbon.

## Direction

The network's cost taken as a whole, before and after the link.
Every driver's minutes summed, and compared to what they were.

## Lever

The honest average, which the added road cannot survive.
A number that credits the link with relief or indicts it with delay.

## Echo

The slowdown, named or blamed.
The paradox made visible, or the demand-story that buys another road.
