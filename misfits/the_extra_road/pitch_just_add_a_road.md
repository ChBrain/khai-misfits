# Pitch: Just Add a Road

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Confident, practical, sure of the fix.
The jam is a shortage of road, so build more road: another lane, another link, somewhere for the traffic to go. It is the most sensible answer in the room, and everyone nods, because more capacity has to mean more flow.
The voice of a planner who trusts the map, a city that trusts the ribbon, a driver who trusts the quick way.

## Undertow

Under the confidence, a network routing itself into a slower whole.
Every driver takes the new link because it is faster for them, and by all taking it they load it, and the load is a cost none of them feels and all of them pay. No one drove wrong, and the trip takes longer than it did.
The road did exactly what a road does, and the whole did exactly what an unpriced network does, and the two answers are opposite.

## Nerve

The feeling of a city that built the fix and got the jam,
a ribbon cut over a bypass, a promise of relief on every sign,
and the commute, quietly, a little longer than the year before the road was there.

## Echo

After the run: a network with more asphalt and less flow,
an average no one wants to read and a demand-story ready to explain it,
and the one road that would have helped, the price on the shared stretch, still unbuilt, because a free road was the thing everyone agreed to want.
