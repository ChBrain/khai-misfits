# Position: Planner

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The seat that shapes the network: it can add a link where there was none.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The power to add capacity to the graph.
A budget, a mandate, and a map with room for one more road.

## Orders

Relieve the jam by building the link the traffic seems to want.
Count the capacity added, and call the network improved.
Answer congestion with asphalt, because asphalt is what a planner has.

## Loses

The whole, held while attending to the link.
A seat that sees roads added cannot easily see loads moved, so the graph slips out of view.

## Drives

Toward more links on the map, capacity as the measure of relief.
Away from the load that a new link redistributes, and toward the ribbon that says the jam was answered.
