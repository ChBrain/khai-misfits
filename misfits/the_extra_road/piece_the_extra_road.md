# Piece: The Extra Road

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The new link, faster for any one driver, added in good faith to relieve the jam.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the new link](place_the_new_link.md), the fresh road cut between two points that used to be far apart.
The bypass, the connector, the extra lane, the shortcut that lets a driver skip the slow way round. Real capacity, honestly built, and genuinely quicker for the driver who takes it while few others do.

## Load Bearing

Without it the network keeps its old, slower-looking balance, and no one is made worse off.
With it there is a route that dominates for each driver alone, so every driver has reason to take it, and by all taking it they pour their loads onto the shared stretches it connects. The Extra Road is load bearing because it is the addition that lets the bad equilibrium exist: it is not a worse road, it is a better one for each, and that is exactly why it is worse for all. Take it away and the paradox has nothing to stand on.

## Apparent

Pure relief. A gift to the network, capacity where there was congestion.
It looks like an unambiguous improvement: a new option can only help, since no one is forced to use it and anyone who does goes faster. That reasoning is airtight for one driver and false for the network, because the option everyone can improve by taking is the option everyone will take, and then it is not an option, it is the new load.

## Yearbook

Opened by [the planner](persona_the_planner.md) through [rerouting](process_rerouting.md), and taken, in time, by every driver.
It is the intervention the whole play turns on: [the commuter](persona_the_commuter.md) reroutes onto it, [the equilibrium](piece_the_equilibrium.md) forms around it, and only [the toll](piece_the_toll.md) or its closure undoes what the Extra Road set in motion.
