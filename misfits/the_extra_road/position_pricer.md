# Position: Pricer

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The reversal seat: the one that puts a price on the load instead of leaving it free.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The authority to charge for a road, or to close one.
A lever that moves the whole network at once, where each driver could move only themselves.

## Orders

Price the shared stretch, so each driver pays the delay they impose.
Align the selfish route with the system route, and let the equilibrium shift.
Or take the extra road away, and give the old balance back.

## Loses

The free road, and the goodwill of charging for it.
A seat that prices the load must defend a toll drivers resent and a closure that looks like waste.

## Drives

Toward a network where private cost meets social cost, and away from the unpriced link that fed the slowdown.
Toward the optimum recovered, at the price of a road no longer free, or no longer there.
