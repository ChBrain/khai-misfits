# Process: Congestion

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). Load raising travel time on a shared stretch.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

One more car joining a link whose cost climbs with use.
The load-dependent road filling toward its slow end.

## Direction

Time on the shared stretch rising with every driver who takes it.
The cost each joiner adds spread silently across all already there.

## Lever

The externality: a delay imposed, and felt by everyone but the one who caused it.
A cost real to the network and invisible on the driver's ledger.

## Echo

A slower link for all, chosen by each.
The unpriced load that the whole paradox turns on.
