# Process: Routing

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). Each driver choosing the path that is quickest for them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A driver reading the roads ahead and picking the fastest.
Every trip, at every junction, answering only its own clock.

## Direction

Traffic distributed by a million private minimisations.
The network filled not by a plan but by the sum of selfish choices.

## Lever

Each driver's own travel time, and nothing above it.
The rule that no one can be asked to route for the whole.

## Echo

A user equilibrium: the state where no driver can gain by switching.
Best for each, and not for all.
