# Place: The Toll Plaza

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). Where using the loaded link finally costs the one who loads it.

## Owner

## Shown

The gantry over the bypass, the charge that reads a plate, the barrier where a free road used to run.
The visible price on the shared stretch, or the closure sign where the link once was.

## Holds

The reversal, and the one lever that moves the whole network at once.
The cost of the load, placed exactly where the network was leaking it.

## Offers

A network where private cost meets social cost, and the flow recovers.
The optimum, bought back by charging for the road that was free.

## Withheld

The free road. The toll plaza gives the whole its speed and takes, in return, the road no one had to pay for.
