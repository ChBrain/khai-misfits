# Place: The Junction

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The decision point, where a driver reads the roads and picks one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The fork where the old route and the new link part.
A sign, an arrow, an app's blue line: the moment of choosing which way to go.

## Holds

The selfish route, made one driver at a time.
Every private decision that, summed, routes the whole network.

## Offers

A faster way, for the driver who takes it now.
The minute saved by turning onto the link the others have not yet flooded.

## Withheld

The load. The junction shows a driver their time, never the delay their turn adds to everyone else's.
