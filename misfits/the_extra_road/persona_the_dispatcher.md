# Persona: The Dispatcher

## Taxonomy

[Dispatcher](position_dispatcher.md). The one who routes many at once, all by the same shortest path.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Efficient. Systematic. Trusts the map and the algorithm.
Routes a fleet, or a million phones, every one down the route the model calls quickest.
Sees only the link's advertised time, never the load its own routing pours onto that link.

## Action

Sends everyone by the shortest path, hardening the equilibrium into place.
Turns a million private choices into one coordinated rush onto the same new road.
Optimises each trip and, doing it at scale, locks the slow whole.

## Shadow

Routing each best routes all worst, and the scale is what does it.
When one navigation service sends every driver the same way, the selfish route stops being an accident and becomes an instruction.
The Dispatcher is the modern edge of the paradox: shortest-path for each, at fleet scale, is the fastest way into Braess.

## Tell

"The model says this is quickest. So that's where they go."
Correct for every single trip, and the reason the road it names is jammed.
