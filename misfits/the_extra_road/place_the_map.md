# Place: The Map

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The whole network seen at once, where the paradox becomes legible.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Every route together, the graph laid flat: the links, the junctions, the loads.
The planner's view, the dispatcher's view, the one picture in which a road and the network are not the same thing.

## Holds

The network whole, and the Braess that lives in the gap between link and graph.
The relation a single driver never sees: what one link does to all the others when it fills.

## Offers

The chance to reason about the total, not just the trip.
The only vantage from which adding a road can be seen to subtract, and closing one to add.

## Withheld

The felt minute. The map shows the whole and hides the driver's own clock, which is all the driver ever answers to.
