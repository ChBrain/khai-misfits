# Persona: The Convert

## Taxonomy

[Follower](position_follower.md). The one who held out, watched the old road lose, and dropped onto the link to keep up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Reluctant, then resigned. Tired of losing time on principle.
Watched the holdout's route slow as everyone left it, did the arithmetic, and stopped being the last one on the long way round.
Not converted by belief but by the plain fact that holding alone only cost them.

## Action

Abandons the old route and joins the shortcut, completing the rush.
Removes the last drag on the equilibrium, so the slow state settles fully in.
Does the rational thing, late, and makes the bad balance total.

## Shadow

The last defence of the fast whole gives way, and no one could blame them.
Every Convert is a driver who tried to do right by the network until the network made it pointless.
Conversion is not weakness, it is the equilibrium closing over the last holdout.

## Tell

"I held out as long as it made sense. It stopped making sense."
The moment the system-optimal route loses its last volunteer.
