# Position: Keeper

## Taxonomy

[The Extra Road](play_the_extra_road.md). The seat that holds the old route: the system-optimal path, kept alone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A route worth keeping, if others kept it too.
The stubbornness to stay off the link the network is piling onto.

## Orders

Hold the road that was fast before the shortcut came.
Keep to the balance the whole would want, and bear the cost of keeping it alone.
Do the system-optimal thing, without the coordination that would reward it.

## Loses

The time, paid for a stand no one else joins.
A seat that holds alone gets none of the gain that holding together would bring.

## Drives

Toward the route a coordinated network would favour, and away from the crowd on the link.
Toward a loyalty that only pays if shared, and toward the moment it stops paying at all.
