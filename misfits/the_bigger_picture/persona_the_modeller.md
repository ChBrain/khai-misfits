# Persona: The Modeller

## Taxonomy

[Modeller](position_modeller.md). Would draw the causal structure to decide which level to trust, at the cost of an assumption the data cannot supply or confirm.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Thoughtful. Causal. Looking behind the table.
Is the one who asks not what the numbers say but what produced them, drawing the arrows between the variables to see which is confounder and which is cause.
No mysticism in it: only the discipline of knowing that a table shows association and a decision needs causation.

## Action

Draws the causal structure, deciding which variable to adjust away as a confounder and which to leave alone as a mediator, and so picks the level to trust.
Brings to the table the one thing it lacks, a model of what caused what, and reads the reversal through it.
Does what causal reasoning does: settles which level licenses the decision, by an assumption about the world the data cannot hold.

## Shadow

Not an oracle reading truth from the figures, a modeller staking a claim, and that is the whole difficulty: the causal structure is what settles the paradox, and it cannot be proven by the data it interprets.
Because the arrows are an assumption brought from outside and the table can neither supply nor confirm them, the Modeller who draws the wrong structure adjusts the wrong way and reverses the reversal with the same confidence as the right one, so the move that resolves the paradox also stakes the answer on a judgement the numbers will never settle, and being right requires a cause correctly guessed.
It brings the cause the table lacks, and the cause it brings is a bet the table cannot check.

## Tell

Says "the numbers cannot tell you: what caused the grouping decides which level is real."
Says it, and must then commit to a causal story the data can neither prove nor refute, right only if the story is.
