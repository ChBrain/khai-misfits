# Plot: The Turn

## Taxonomy

[The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md)

## Owner

## Cue

[The Ledger](place_the_ledger.md), a decision that will not wait and two readings that disagree.
The total authoritative and clear, every subgroup against it, a treatment to choose or a policy to set, and no time to wait for a certainty that will not come.

## Action

[Pooling](process_pooling.md): [The Decider](persona_the_decider.md), holding [the decider's seat](position_decider.md), acts on the aggregate and against every subgroup, trusting the larger sample and the clearer whole.

## Stage

[The Decider](persona_the_decider.md), on [Act on the Number](plan_act_on_the_number.md), reads the biggest, most authoritative summary and commits, taking the total as the truth to act on because rising above the slices feels like rising above the noise.
The call is made on the whole and against the parts, confidently, and nothing on the page can say whether the level chosen was the right one.

## Tension

Acting on the clearest, largest number is the sound instinct, and it commits against everything every subgroup shows.
There is nothing to fault: a decision must be made and the aggregate wears the authority of size. But the data cannot license a level, so the plot closes with the verdict taken on the total, right or wrong with no way to tell, the accountability that forced the call discharged on a number that could not ground it.
