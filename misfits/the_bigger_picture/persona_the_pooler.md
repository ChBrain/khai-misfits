# Persona: The Pooler

## Taxonomy

[Pooler](position_pooler.md). Combines all the data and reads the aggregate trend, honestly and with the authority of the big number, one of the two correct readings that disagree.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sensible. Rigorous. Above the noise.
Is the one who gathers all the rows into one table and reads the overall trend, trusting the larger sample and the fuller picture.
No sloppiness in it: only a careful analyst computing the honest aggregate from every piece of the data there is.

## Action

Pools the data and reads the total, combining the subgroups into one number and reporting the direction it points.
Trusts the size of the sample and the completeness of the whole, and steps back from the slices to the picture they sum to.
Does what a careful analyst does: builds the biggest honest number available and reads the trend it shows.

## Shadow

Not a careless counter, a rigorous one, and that is the whole difficulty: the aggregate is honestly computed and truly built from all the data, and it can still point the opposite way from every part.
Because a lurking variable has loaded the subgroups unevenly, pooling them mixes unlike things with unlike weights, so the Pooler's honest total can reverse the trend that holds in each group, and the very completeness that makes the number authoritative is what lets the confounder flip it.
It reads the whole correctly, and reading the whole correctly can be reading the wrong thing.

## Tell

Says "look at the big number: it is built from all the data, so it is the one to trust."
Built it honestly, and cannot see that a whole built from unlike parts can say the opposite of every part.
