# Plot: The Tally

## Taxonomy

[The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ledger](place_the_ledger.md), all the rows gathered and the whole there to be read.
The subgroups summed into one table, the larger sample to hand, the instinct to step back to the fuller picture.

## Action

[Pooling](process_pooling.md): [The Pooler](persona_the_pooler.md), holding [the pooler's seat](position_pooler.md), combines the data and reads the aggregate trend, one clear direction with the authority of the big number.

## Stage

[The Pooler](persona_the_pooler.md), on [Trust the Total](plan_trust_the_total.md), sums every subgroup into the total and reads the overall trend, honestly and completely, from all the data there is.
The number is large and clean and points one way, and the completeness that built it makes it feel like the truth risen above the noise of the parts.

## Tension

Reading the biggest honest number is the sound instinct, and the pooling that built it mixed groups that should not have been mixed.
There is nothing to fault: the aggregate is computed correctly from every row. But the subgroups were loaded unevenly, so the plot closes with the total giving its confident answer, authoritative and clean, over a confounder it averaged away and cannot show.
