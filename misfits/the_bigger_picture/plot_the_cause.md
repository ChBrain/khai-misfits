# Plot: The Cause

## Taxonomy

[The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Diagram](place_the_diagram.md), someone who would settle the reversal by a cause.
The two true readings at odds, the lurking variable behind them, the arrows there to be drawn and the assumption there to be staked.

## Action

[Adjudicating](process_adjudicating.md): [The Modeller](persona_the_modeller.md), holding [the modeller's seat](position_modeller.md), draws the causal structure to pick the level to trust, against the pull of the authoritative total and the honest split.

## Stage

[The Modeller](persona_the_modeller.md), on [Draw the Cause](plan_draw_the_cause.md), sets the arrows between the variables, judges which is confounder to adjust and which mediator to leave, and chooses the level the model licenses.
The paradox can be resolved this way, but only by a causal claim brought from outside, one the table can neither prove nor refute.

## Tension

Drawing the cause is the one move that can choose between the levels, and it stakes the answer on an assumption the data cannot check.
There is nothing to fault: the choice of level is a causal question and the modeller answers it in the only currency that can. But the arrows are guessed, not measured, so the plot closes one of two ways: the structure is drawn correctly and the right level chosen, bought against a cause the numbers cannot confirm ... or the arrows are wrong, the wrong variable adjusted, and the reversal reversed once more with equal confidence.
