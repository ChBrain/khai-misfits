# Piece: The Verdict

## Taxonomy

[The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md). The decision taken on the chosen level and acted on, the call made on the total against every subgroup, right or wrong with no way to tell.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Handed down at [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), where the decision is made on the total.
Its rightness settled only at [the diagram](place_the_diagram.md), where a cause would say if the level was correct.

## Load Bearing

Without it the reversal is a curiosity, a table that contradicts itself and harms no one.
With it the paradox bites: a decision is made on one level, a treatment chosen or a policy set, and it commits against the other level's reading. The Verdict is load bearing because it is where the trap costs (Bickel; Charig): the call taken on the aggregate against every subgroup, so the piece carries the decision that turns a contradiction on paper into an action in the world, the choice made on the level that persuaded and grounded on numbers that could not license it.

## Apparent

A sound call, the plain result of acting on the clearest, largest number available.
Seen at the surface, the Verdict looks like good decision-making, a choice made on the fullest evidence, and the truth that it committed against everything every subgroup showed, that whether it was right turns on a cause the data never held, that the same table would have justified the opposite call, stays hidden behind how responsible acting on the biggest number plainly looks.
