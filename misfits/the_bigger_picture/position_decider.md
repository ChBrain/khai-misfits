# Position: Decider

## Taxonomy

[The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md). The seat that must act on a conclusion, where trusting the authoritative total means acting against every subgroup, with no way to know which was right.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A decision that will not wait and the clearest number to hand: the standing to choose a treatment or set a policy, and the pull of the largest, most authoritative summary.
A clear case: a decision must be made, and the number built from all the data is the one to make it on.
No way to tell, from the table, whether the whole or the parts should carry the call, and no time to wait for a certainty that will not come.

## Orders

Act on the number.
Read the clearest, largest summary, take the aggregate as the truth to act on, and commit, because rising above the slices feels like rising above the noise.
Choose, and move.

## Loses

The chance to be right for a reason it can defend, exactly by acting on the level that carries the most authority.
Because the data cannot license a level and the aggregate wears the authority of size, the seat commits to the total and, where the grouping was the cause, moves exactly the wrong way, or the right way, with nothing on the page to tell which, so the accountability that forces a decision is discharged on a number that cannot ground it.
It gains a decision made and a picture that looked clearest, and loses any way to know the decision was right.

## Drives

Toward the call made on the total,
bought against the subgroups it acts against and the cause it cannot see,
so the seat that must decide commits to the level that persuades, not the level that is right.
