# Persona: The Decider

## Taxonomy

[Decider](position_decider.md). Must act on a conclusion, trusts the authoritative total, and acts against every subgroup, in good faith and with no way to know which level was right.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Decisive. Responsible. Needing an answer.
Is the one who cannot stare at the table forever, who has a treatment to choose or a policy to set and must read the data and act.
No rashness in it: only someone accountable for a decision, taking the clearest, largest, most authoritative number and moving on it.

## Action

Acts on the total, choosing the level that carries the most data and the plainest signal, and setting the policy or the treatment accordingly.
Takes the aggregate as the truth to act on, because it is built from everything and rising above the slices feels like rising above the noise.
Does what a decision-maker must: reads the clearest summary and commits.

## Shadow

Not a reckless chooser, an accountable one, and that is the whole difficulty: acting on the biggest honest number is the sound instinct, and it can act against everything every subgroup shows.
Because the data cannot say which level to trust and the aggregate wears the authority of size, the Decider commits to the total and, when the grouping was the thing that caused, moves exactly the wrong way, confidently, or the right way, with no way to tell which from the table, so the accountability that demands a decision is discharged on a number that cannot license it.
It acts on the clearest picture, and the clearest picture can be the reversed one.

## Tell

Says "I have to choose, and I will go with the number built from all of it."
Chooses in good faith, and cannot know, from the data alone, whether the whole or the parts should have carried the call.
