# Piece: The Reversal

## Taxonomy

[The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md). The paradox itself, the trend that holds in every subgroup and points the opposite way in the pooled whole, both readings true and at odds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Appears between [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md) and [the breakdown](place_the_breakdown.md), where the whole and the parts are read against each other.
Resolved or missed at [the diagram](place_the_diagram.md), where a cause would say which to believe.

## Load Bearing

Without it the whole and the parts agree, and there is nothing surprising and no trap.
With it the paradox is sprung: the same honest data points one way pooled and the opposite way split, and both readings are correct, neither a miscount. The Reversal is load bearing because it is the paradox the trap is named for (Simpson; Blyth): the direction that flips between the aggregate and the strata, so the piece carries the contradiction between two true readings, the surprise that the parts and the whole of one honest table can disagree, and that the numbers cannot say which of them to act on.

## Apparent

A mistake, the plain look of a figure that must have been miscomputed to contradict itself.
Seen at the surface, the Reversal looks like an error to be found and fixed, and the truth that both readings are honestly correct, that the contradiction is a genuine feature of an unevenly confounded table, that no recount will remove it because it is not a mistake but a property, stays hidden behind how much a whole that contradicts its parts looks like something someone got wrong.

## Yearbook

Laid bare when [the total](piece_the_total.md) meets [the strata](piece_the_strata.md), the flip driven by [the lurking variable](piece_the_lurking_variable.md).
It is the paradox of the trap: the Reversal sets the true whole against the true parts, sprung by the confounder and settled only by [adjudicating](process_adjudicating.md), the contradiction the [bigger picture](piece_the_bigger_picture.md) is built on and the numbers cannot resolve.
