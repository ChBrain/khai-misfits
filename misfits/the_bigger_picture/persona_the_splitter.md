# Persona: The Splitter

## Taxonomy

[Splitter](position_splitter.md). Separates the data into subgroups and reads the local trend in each, honestly and equally true, the other of the two correct readings that disagree.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Granular. Alert to the detail.
Is the one who breaks the table into its groups and reads the trend within each, unwilling to let the slices be blurred into one.
No pedantry in it: only a careful analyst who sees that the parts may not behave like the whole and looks at each on its own.

## Action

Splits the data by group and reads each subgroup's trend, reporting that within this stratum and that one the direction is the same, and opposite to the total.
Holds that the parts deserve their own reading, and refuses to let an average hide what every group plainly shows.
Does what a careful analyst does: disaggregates, and lets each subgroup speak for itself.

## Shadow

Not a nitpicker splitting hairs, a rigorous reader of the parts, and that is the whole difficulty: each subgroup trend is honestly computed and truly holds, and it can still be the wrong level to act on.
Because whether the grouping should be adjusted away or kept depends on what caused it, the Splitter's honest strata are the right guide only if the grouping is on the causal path, and the data cannot say that it is, so disaggregating correctly can be disaggregating on a variable that should have been left pooled, splitting the truth into the wrong answer with equal rigour.
It reads the parts correctly, and reading the parts correctly can be reading the wrong level.

## Tell

Says "do not blur the groups: every single one of them says the opposite of your total."
Reads them truly, and cannot show, from the numbers alone, that the groups are the level the decision should be made on.
