# Process: Pooling

## Taxonomy

[The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md). The combining of the subgroups into one aggregate, the total read with the authority of size and acted on, over the confounder it averages away.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

All the rows and the instinct to trust the larger sample.
At [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), the pooler summing every subgroup into one table and reading the overall trend, the decider taking the big number to act on.

## Direction

Toward one authoritative number that can point against every part.
Because the subgroups were loaded unevenly and pooling weights them by their sizes, combining them mixes unlike things, so the aggregate trend can reverse the trend that holds in each group, and the total, built honestly from all the data, carries the authority of size while hiding the sorting that flipped it.

## Lever

The soundness of trusting more data over less.
Because a larger sample really is a fuller picture and the aggregate really is computed from every row, reading the total is the sensible instinct, so pooling runs on good statistical sense, the reversal assembled from no miscount, only from summing groups that should not have been summed as if they were alike.

## Echo

A big honest number that points the wrong way, and feels the most right.
The subgroups have been combined and the total read with the authority of the larger sample, so what reads as rising above the noise to the truth is, in truth, a mixture of incomparable groups, the clearest number in the house the one most able to reverse every part it was built from.
