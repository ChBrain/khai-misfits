# Place: The Ledger

## Taxonomy

[The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md). The combined table where all the data is gathered and the aggregate read, the whole from which the total speaks and the decision is taken.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place every row is gathered into one, a single table where the data is summed and the overall trend read off with the authority of size.
The pooler combining the subgroups into the total, the decider reading the big number and committing to it, the groups blurred into one figure.

## Holds

The pooling and the deciding, and the authority of the whole.
Here the data meets the aggregate, so the Ledger holds the point where the parts become one number: every subgroup summed into the total, so it is where the fuller picture is read and acted on, the larger sample always more persuasive than the slices it hides.

## Offers

A single authoritative number, and the clarity of the whole.
Because the Ledger gathers all the data into one, it offers the pooler the biggest honest figure and the decider the plainest signal to act on, so it hands out the authority of size and the relief of one clear answer, and hands it out having blurred the unlike groups whose difference would have changed the reading.

## Withheld

Any sight of the confounder the pooling hid.
The Ledger cannot both sum the groups into one authoritative total and show how unevenly they were loaded, so it withholds the lurking variable it pooled over, letting the aggregate stand clean and unqualified, and, all whole and no parts, it withholds any sign that the biggest, clearest number in the house is the one that can point against every group it was built from.
