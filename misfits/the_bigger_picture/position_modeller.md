# Position: Modeller

## Taxonomy

[The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md). The seat that would draw the causal structure to pick the level to trust, at the cost of an assumption the data can neither supply nor confirm.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The variables and the question of what caused what: the standing to draw the arrows between them and read the reversal through a model of the world.
A clear case: a table shows association and a decision needs causation, so the level to trust is chosen by the causal structure, not the counts.
No way to read the arrows off the data, and no cover from the charge of guessing at a cause the numbers cannot check.

## Orders

Draw the cause.
Set the causal structure, decide which variable is a confounder to adjust away and which a mediator to leave alone, and pick the level the model licenses.
Bring the table the one thing it lacks, a claim about what produced it.

## Loses

The safety of a conclusion the data can back, exactly by settling the paradox with a cause.
Because the causal structure is brought from outside and the table can neither prove nor refute it, the seat that draws the wrong arrows adjusts the wrong way and reverses the reversal with equal confidence, so the move that resolves the paradox stakes the answer on a judgement the numbers will never settle, and being right requires a cause correctly guessed.
It buys a decision the model can license against a model the data cannot confirm, right only if the causal story it committed to is.

## Drives

Toward the cause drawn and the level chosen,
bought against an assumption the data cannot supply or check,
so the one seat that can settle the paradox must stake it on a cause the numbers never carry.
