# Piece: The Strata

## Taxonomy

[The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md). The subgroup trends, each honestly computed and consistent within its group, pointing the opposite way from the total and mute on which level should rule.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Laid out at [the breakdown](place_the_breakdown.md), where the data is read group by group.
Against the whole read at [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), whose total they contradict.

## Load Bearing

Without them there is only the aggregate, and no parts to contradict it and no reversal to see.
With them the trap has its other true pole: the trend within each subgroup, honestly computed and holding in every one, and opposite to the total. The Strata are load bearing because they are the parts the reversal sets against the whole (Simpson; Blyth): the within-group trends, equally correct and pointing the other way, so the piece carries the honest parts whose disagreement with the honest whole is the paradox itself, true of the groups they describe and unable to prove their level is the one to act on.

## Apparent

The real story, the granular truth the crude aggregate blurred away.
Seen at the surface, the Strata look like the finer and truer reading, the detail an average concealed, and the truth that reading each group correctly can still be the wrong level, that whether to trust the parts turns on a cause the numbers do not hold, that the granular reading is right only if the grouping is on the causal path, stays hidden behind how much a finer view feels like a truer one.

## Yearbook

Read by [the splitter](persona_the_splitter.md) through [splitting](process_splitting.md), the within-group trends that hold in every part.
They are the true parts of the trap: the Strata point against [the total](piece_the_total.md) and lay [the reversal](piece_the_reversal.md) bare, the honest slices [the lurking variable](piece_the_lurking_variable.md) loaded unevenly, one of the two readings the [bigger picture](piece_the_bigger_picture.md) cannot choose between.
