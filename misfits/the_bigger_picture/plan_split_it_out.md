# Plan: Split It Out

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the splitter's seat](position_splitter.md), inside [The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Splitter](position_splitter.md)

## Direction

Break the table into its subgroups and read the trend within each. Refuse to let an average blur what every group plainly shows, hold the parts against the whole, and let each stratum speak for itself, even against the authority of the total.

## Orders

([The Splitter](persona_the_splitter.md) disaggregates the data and reads each subgroup, honestly and equally true, the reading that points the opposite way from the pooled total.)

## Implementation

([The Breakdown](place_the_breakdown.md); the [splitting](process_splitting.md) process; the reading lays out [the strata](piece_the_strata.md) and shows [the reversal](piece_the_reversal.md) against [the total](piece_the_total.md), true within each group and mute on which level should rule.)

## Targets

- [ ] the table is broken into its subgroups and each trend read
- [ ] every stratum is shown to point the opposite way from the total
- [ ] the parts are held against the whole and not blurred into an average
- [ ] the split is left unable to prove, from the data, that its level is the one to act on
