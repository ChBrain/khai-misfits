# Position: Pooler

## Taxonomy

[The Bigger Picture](play_the_bigger_picture.md). The seat that combines the data and reads the aggregate, where the honest whole can point the opposite way from every part.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

All the rows and the authority of the larger sample: the standing to gather the data into one table and read the trend it sums to.
A clear case: more data is a fuller picture, and the number built from all of it deserves the most trust.
No way to see the lurking variable in the total, and no sign, in the aggregate alone, that the groups it pooled were unalike.

## Orders

Trust the total.
Combine every subgroup into one table, read the overall trend, and step back from the slices to the whole they make.
Take the biggest honest number as the truth, and rise above the noise of the parts.

## Loses

Any sight of the confounder it pooled over, exactly by combining the groups into one.
Because a lurking variable has loaded the subgroups unevenly, the seat's honest total mixes unlike things with unlike weights, so the completeness that makes the aggregate authoritative is what lets the confounder flip its direction, and reading the whole correctly can be reading the reverse of every part.
It gains the authority of the larger number, and loses the difference between the groups that the larger number hides.

## Drives

Toward the total read and trusted whole,
bought against the confounder it pools away,
so the seat that reads the fullest picture can read the reversed one.
