# Position: The Sharp End

## Taxonomy

[The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md). The operating seat, where the work meets the world and the adaptations are made, able to act on the local task and its immediate result and unable to see the system-level position of the point it is moving.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The task, the hands to do it, and the local outcome of each adaptation.
The seat holds the work as it actually happens and the immediate result of every change to it: the job done or not, the effort spent or saved, the incident that came or did not.
Command of the local and none of the global, since the seat can act on the task in front of it and read what happens next, and has no view of where its accumulated adaptations have placed the operating point in the larger space.

## Orders

Do the job with what there is, and keep what works.
Meet the task under the time and effort available, adapt where the method asks more than the moment allows, and retain any change that finishes the work and causes no incident, since a shortcut that succeeds is by every visible measure an improvement.
Read the local result as the whole verdict, since the seat sees the task and its outcome and nothing beyond, so an adaptation that pays and does no harm is confirmed, the sharp end judging each move by exactly the evidence it can hold.

## Loses

The location of the boundary, which the seat has no instrument to find.
The Sharp End can measure the local outcome of an adaptation and cannot measure the margin it spent, because the boundary of safe operation is unmarked and the seat sees only the task, so it moves the operating point without any reading of where the point now sits. What the position forfeits is the system-level view: every adaptation returns a clear local signal, done and safe, and no signal at all about accumulated position, so the seat spends margin it cannot meter and banks each successful move as pure gain because the cost of the move, distance closed to an unseen edge, appears in none of the data the sharp end holds. No operation available to the seat recovers the missing coordinate, since the boundary leaves no mark until it is crossed and the record of safe adaptations is exactly what a still-safe run and a nearly-spent one have in common, so the seat that reads its own results most faithfully is the seat most sure of a margin it has no way to measure.

## Drives

Toward a task done and a point moved, with only the first of the two seen,
and a run of local successes each read as gain and none as distance closed,
so the seat that judges every move by its immediate result is the seat that cannot register the drift its moves compose, spending an unmeasured margin one confirmed adaptation at a time.
