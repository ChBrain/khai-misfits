# Plot: The Shortcut

## Taxonomy

[The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Line](place_the_line.md), a job to be done and less time or energy than it asks.
A task that the method budgets more effort for than the moment allows, and a worker who must close the gap.

## Action

[Adapting](process_adapting.md): the Hand drops a step that has never mattered, finds the leaner way, and finishes the job, saving the effort and causing no incident.

## Stage

[The Hand](persona_the_hand.md) acts from [the Sharp End](position_the_sharp_end.md) on [Get It Done](plan_get_it_done.md), moving [the operating point](piece_the_operating_point.md) under [the pull](piece_the_pull.md), the move confirmed by [the quiet](piece_the_quiet.md) that no incident followed.
The adaptation is skill and the result is clean, and the margin it spent goes unread.

## Tension

The worker does the job well and moves the system toward the edge by doing it, and nothing at the sharp end shows the cost.
There is no recklessness: the shortcut finished the work, saved the effort, and caused no harm, so by every measure the front can read it was an improvement, kept and repeated. But the boundary is unmarked and the front sees only the local result, so the plot closes with a successful adaptation that has spent a margin no one could meter, the operating point a little nearer the edge and the record of the move a plain success, the drift advanced by a piece of good work.
