# Plot: The Slide

## Taxonomy

[The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Brink](place_the_brink.md), the adaptations and economies accumulating.
A run of shortcuts kept and cuts banked, each rewarded and none crossing, the operating point settling further out with every one.

## Action

[Migrating](process_migrating.md): the point drifts toward the boundary under the combined pull, the string of safe days read as proof of room to spare.

## Stage

The [operating point](piece_the_operating_point.md) moves across the Brink under [the pull](piece_the_pull.md), nearing [the unmarked boundary](piece_the_unmarked_boundary.md) while [the quiet](piece_the_quiet.md) reassures, [the Hand](persona_the_hand.md) and [the Purser](persona_the_purser.md) each supplying their gradient and neither seeing the sum.
No step among them was a decision to be unsafe, and together they are a system at the edge.

## Tension

The drift is made entirely of successes and arrives at the edge without a single wrong move.
There is no fault to find: every adaptation worked, every cut paid, every day passed without incident, so the record behind the point is spotless. But the moves all leaned the same way and the boundary left no mark, so the plot closes with the system resting at the brink of an edge it cannot see, its margin spent on a hundred good decisions, the drift complete and invisible precisely because nothing in it ever failed, the calm of the record the surest sign of how far it has come.
