# Piece: The Operating Point

## Taxonomy

[The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md). The way the work is actually done today, the system's position in the space bounded by cost, effort, and safety, the whole phenomenon of which every other piece is a part: a point migrating under two gradients toward an edge it cannot see.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Sits in the space bounded by [the Boardroom](place_the_boardroom.md)'s economics and [the Line](place_the_line.md)'s effort, drifting across [the Brink](place_the_brink.md) toward the unmarked edge.
Flows as the live position of the system, not the rules on paper but the practice in fact, the single point that says how cheaply, how easily, and how safely the work is really being done.

## Load Bearing

Without the Operating Point there is no misfit, only pressures and boundaries in the abstract: it is the Point that ties the pull to the quiet to the unmarked boundary, the one position in which two sound pressures become a drift and a drift becomes a crossing.
The Operating Point is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole, the actual practice as a position in the space of the possible, pushed by the cost and effort gradients toward the boundary of safe operation and reading its own approach as room (Rasmussen). It carries the entire misfit: the Pull is what moves the Point, the Quiet is what licenses each move, the Unmarked Boundary is the edge it nears, so the Operating Point is not one more piece beside the others but the shape they all compose, the live practice in which every other piece is at work. It is built of sound moves, each adaptation skilful and each economy prudent, which is why it cannot simply be halted: no single move was wrong, the drift is only their sum, so holding the Point back means arguing against a hundred good decisions on the strength of an edge no one can mark, spending real margin to keep a position the record calls safe.

## Apparent

Just how things are done, the ordinary practice of a well-run system.
Seen at the surface, the Operating Point looks like sound operation, work done cheaply and skilfully with a clean record behind it, and the truth that it is a position and not a fixture, that it has been moving, that it sits nearer the edge than any of its good decisions could tell, stays hidden behind how settled and how reasonable the current way of working appears.

## Yearbook

Moved by [the Hand](persona_the_hand.md)'s adaptations and [the Purser](persona_the_purser.md)'s cuts, and held back, alone and uphill, by [the Warden](persona_the_warden.md).
It is the whole shape of the drift: the Operating Point is what [adapting](process_adapting.md) and [pressing](process_pressing.md) move, what [migrating](process_migrating.md) carries and [marking](process_marking.md) would hold, the live position pulled by [the pull](piece_the_pull.md) toward [the unmarked boundary](piece_the_unmarked_boundary.md) under cover of [the quiet](piece_the_quiet.md), a practice sound in every move and drifting in the sum.
