# Place: The Verge

## Taxonomy

[The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md). The near ground of the boundary, where the operating point comes to rest closer than anyone can tell, where the Warden watches and the drift accumulates, and where the edge is not drawn.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Nothing that looks like danger: a system running well, close to a line no one can see.
Shown at the surface is calm, a point sitting where a hundred rewarded moves have left it, working smoothly and cheaply, with no visible sign that the margin is nearly gone, because the ground here looks exactly like the ground well inside.

## Holds

Holds the spent margin and the unmarked edge, the approach the record cannot show.
The Verge is where the operating point has migrated to, the near side of a boundary that leaves no mark, so it holds the drift made physical: the distance closed, the slack consumed, the point poised close to the edge, all of it invisible because a nearly-crossed system and a safely-inside one keep the same untroubled surface.

## Offers

Offers the last of the margin and no reading of how much is left.
Here is where the warning would matter most and where there is least to see, so the Verge offers the Warden their whole case and denies them the proof: the edge is near and the point is close and the record is calm, so the place holds the danger and withholds the sign of it, everything the counter-pressure would need to be believed present and unshowable.

## Withheld

Withholds the line, until the crossing draws it.
What the Verge hides is the boundary itself: the edge is unmarked and stays unmarked, knowable only by being crossed, so the place that holds the danger keeps its coordinate to the last, present as a risk and absent as a mark, until the operating point goes over and the accident draws the line exactly, the Verge yielding the location of the edge only at the price of the crossing that locates it.
