# Position: The Envelope

## Taxonomy

[The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md). The safety seat, which holds the case for the margin and would keep the point inside the safe space, able to argue that the boundary is near and unable to say where it is.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The safety case, the duty to keep the margin, and the authority to call for a hold.
The seat holds the standing that lets it argue against the pull, the mandate to defend the operating envelope, and the reasoning that the boundary is closer than the record suggests.
A case without a coordinate, since the seat can hold every argument that the edge is near and cannot hold the one thing that would settle it, the location of the boundary, which is unmarked and unreadable until crossed.

## Orders

Mark the boundary, hold the point, and spend to keep the margin.
Reason to where the edge must be, press the operating point back from it, and defend a slack the cost and effort gradients both want gone, arguing that a margin unspent is the price of an accident unhad.
Make the case that cannot be shown, since the seat's whole task is to defend a boundary it can only infer, so it must ask the system to hold back on the strength of an argument, the envelope pressing for a cost against a risk it can name but not display.

## Loses

The coordinate, which the seat needs and the boundary withholds.
The Envelope can argue that the edge is near and cannot mark where it is, because the boundary of safe operation leaves no sign until it is crossed, so the seat carries a case with no coordinate and must outbid a concrete saving with an abstract, unprovable risk. What the position forfeits is the one thing that would let it win: a mark on the floor, a line it could point to, since without it every call for margin is a claim against evidence, contradicted by each safe day and outweighed by each real economy, so the seat holds the correct position and none of the proof, its counter-pressure structurally lighter than the savings it opposes because the savings are measured and the risk is only reasoned. No operation available to the seat supplies the missing mark, since the boundary is knowable only by the crossing the seat exists to prevent, so the Envelope argues hardest exactly where it can show least, right about the edge and unable to locate it, always the guessed line against the counted gain.

## Drives

Toward a margin argued for and never proved, a case that strengthens only when it is too late,
and a counter-pressure outbid by every saving because the saving is seen and the risk is only inferred,
so the seat that alone would hold the point back is the seat least able to compel it, its truth real and unlocatable, correct about the edge and structurally too light to keep the system off it.
