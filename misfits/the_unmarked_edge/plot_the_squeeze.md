# Plot: The Squeeze

## Taxonomy

[The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Boardroom](place_the_boardroom.md), a budget under pressure and slack that shows no return.
A cost that could come out, a competitor at the gate, and a margin that has bought no visible incident in memory.

## Action

[Pressing](process_pressing.md): the Purser trims the budget, tightens the schedule, and cuts the slack, and the quarter comes in cheaper with nothing to show for the loss.

## Stage

[The Purser](persona_the_purser.md) acts from [the Blunt End](position_the_blunt_end.md) on [Trim the Cost](plan_trim_the_cost.md), adding to [the pull](piece_the_pull.md) that moves [the operating point](piece_the_operating_point.md), the cut absolved by [the quiet](piece_the_quiet.md) of a period with no incident.
The economy is prudent and the quarter is better, and the margin it removed sends no bill.

## Tension

The blunt end saves money and pushes the system toward the edge by saving it, and the ledger shows only the saving.
There is no greed: the cut removed slack that had produced nothing visible, lowered the cost, and was followed by no incident, so on the books it was a clean economy, banked and repeated. But the risk it added is remote in time and place and reaches no account the room reads, so the plot closes with a saving vindicated and a margin quietly spent, the operating point pushed further toward the edge from a distance that priced the economy and never the risk, the drift advanced by a piece of good management.
