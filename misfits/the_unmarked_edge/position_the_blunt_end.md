# Position: The Blunt End

## Taxonomy

[The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md). The directing seat, remote from the work, which sets the cost pressure and reads the results, able to see the saving a cut returns at once and unable to be charged for the margin the cut removes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The budget, the pressure to trim it, and the quarterly result.
The seat holds the levers of cost and the numbers they move: the schedule, the headcount, the slack, and the periodic report that says whether the system came in cheaper and whether anything went wrong.
The saving in full and the risk not at all, since the seat can read the economy a cut produced this quarter and holds no instrument that would show the margin the same cut spent.

## Orders

Trim what shows no return, and read the result.
Find the cost that can come out, cut the slack that has produced no visible benefit, and judge the cut by the quarter that follows, since a leaner period with no incident is by every legible measure a saving cleanly made.
Bank the economy the numbers confirm, since the seat's evidence is the result it can read, and a cut that lowers cost and shows no harm is vindicated on the record, the blunt end pricing each decision by exactly the feedback that reaches it.

## Loses

The bill for the margin, which never reaches the seat that spent it.
The Blunt End can read the saving a cut returns and cannot read the risk it added, because the two are separated by distance and time: the economy lands this quarter and the accident it courts, if it comes, is far off, deniable, and registered somewhere the budget is not. What the position forfeits is the feedback that would price the cut correctly: the saving arrives with a clear signal and the margin leaves with none, so the seat receives applause for every economy and a bill for none, and the risk it took is entered against no account it reads. No operation available to the seat closes the gap, since the cost of a spent margin is an accident that has not happened and a distance to an edge no one has marked, so the blunt end books each cut as free precisely because the ledger that would charge it lies on the far side of a boundary the seat cannot see and a delay it will not wait out, the remoteness that lets it cut cleanly the same remoteness that hides what the cutting costs.

## Drives

Toward a leaner system and a lopsided ledger, saving on one side and silence on the other,
and a run of economies each applauded and none ever charged,
so the seat most answerable for the numbers is the seat least able to see what the numbers cost, pressing the point toward the edge from a distance that prices the saving and never the risk.
