# Place: The Line

## Taxonomy

[The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md). The operational front, where the work is actually done and the adaptations are made, the sharp end that meets the job under real time and effort and sees its own result and no further.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The work, the task in hand, and whether it got done.
Shown at the surface is everything the job needs and nothing beyond it: the task, the tools, the time available, the immediate result, a front arranged to finish the work and to show, at once, that it was finished.

## Holds

Holds the adaptation and its local reward, the moment a shortcut is proven.
The Line is where the method meets the moment and gives, where the step that asks more than there is gets dropped and the leaner way tried, so it holds the adaptation and the plain evidence for it: the job done, the effort saved, no incident, the shortcut confirmed by exactly the outcome the front can see.

## Offers

Offers a job finished and a margin spent, with only the first in view.
Here the work gets done under a load the method never budgeted for, so the Line offers competence and the satisfaction of a task closed, and offers, in the same act, a step toward the edge: the margin the adaptation spent is real and invisible, absent from everything the front can read, so the place hands over a success and pockets a cost it does not show.

## Withheld

Withholds the position of the point, and the distance still to the edge.
What the Line hides is where its accumulated adaptations have placed the system: the front sees each task and its result and never the sum, so the place that makes the moves withholds their total, present with every local outcome and absent the one reading that matters, how far the operating point has travelled and how little room is left, kept off the front because the boundary that would measure it is nowhere the front can see.
