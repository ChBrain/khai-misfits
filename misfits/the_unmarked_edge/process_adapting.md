# Process: Adapting

## Taxonomy

[The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md). The local change to the work that saves effort and finishes the job, the shortcut proven by its own success, the effort gradient made into a move of the operating point.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Hand](persona_the_hand.md), at [the Line](place_the_line.md), meeting a job with less time and effort than it asks.
Begins whenever the method demands more than the moment allows and a leaner way is found that gets the work done.

## Direction

Turns a shortfall of effort into a move toward the edge.
Because the job must be done with the time and hands there are, the Hand drops the step that has never mattered and keeps what works, so adapting converts a gap between method and reality into a change in how the work is done, each retained shortcut moving the operating point a little toward the boundary while returning a clean local success (Snook; Rasmussen).

## Lever

The success of the shortcut.
Because the leaner way finishes the task, saves the effort, and causes no incident on the day, adapting works through the plain reward of a move that pays: the evidence the sharp end can read all says the change was good, so the lever is the local outcome itself, which confirms each adaptation and recruits the next, the margin it spends nowhere in the result that vindicates it.

## Echo

A settled way of working, one step nearer the edge and proven safe.
The echo of adapting is a shortcut become the routine, retained because it worked and unquestioned because it always has, so the operating point sits a little closer to the boundary and the record of the move is a success, the drift advanced by a change that every visible measure calls an improvement.
