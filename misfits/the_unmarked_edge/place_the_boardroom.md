# Place: The Boardroom

## Taxonomy

[The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md). The blunt end, remote from the work, where the cost pressure is set and the quarter is read, the place a margin is cut as a number and a saving is booked before its bill comes due.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The budget, the results, and the competitor at the gate.
Shown at the surface is the case for efficiency: the cost that could come out, the leaner quarter that follows a cut, the pressure from the market and the board, everything that makes trimming the slack look like the plainest prudence.

## Holds

Holds the cut and its clean reward, the economy the numbers confirm.
The Boardroom is where cost is a line to be lowered and slack is money sitting idle, so it holds the decision to trim and the result that vindicates it: a quarter that came in cheaper with no incident to charge against the saving, the cut booked as free on the evidence the room can read.

## Offers

Offers a saving now and a risk deferred, with only the saving on the books.
Here a cut lands as a clear economy this period, so the Boardroom offers the reward of good management, and offers it shorn of its cost: the margin the cut removed sends no bill to this room and this quarter, so the place hands over a saving and defers, out of sight, the risk that might one day answer for it, the economy real and the account it will be charged to somewhere else entirely.

## Withheld

Withholds the bill, and the distance the cut moved the point.
What the Boardroom hides is the far side of its own economies: the accident a spent margin courts is remote in time and place, registered where the budget is not, so the room that sets the pressure withholds the consequence of it, present with every saving and absent every cost, kept from the one feedback that would price a cut correctly because that feedback lies across a delay the room will not wait out and a boundary it cannot see.
