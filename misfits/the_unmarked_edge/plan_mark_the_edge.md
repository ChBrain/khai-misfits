# Plan: Mark the Edge

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Envelope](position_the_envelope.md), inside [The Unmarked Edge](play_the_unmarked_edge.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Envelope](position_the_envelope.md)

## Direction

Reason to where the boundary must be, mark it as best it can be marked, and hold the operating point back from it, defending a margin the cost and effort gradients both want spent (Rasmussen). Make the boundary visible where it can be made visible, give the sharp end and the blunt end a sign of the edge they cannot otherwise see, and press a counter-gradient against the pull, arguing that a margin unspent is the price of an accident unhad. Accept that the case has no coordinate and must be won on inference, contradicted by every safe day and outweighed by every real saving, so the plan is a continuous cost paid for an accident that, paid well, never comes and so never proves the payment was due (Dekker).
