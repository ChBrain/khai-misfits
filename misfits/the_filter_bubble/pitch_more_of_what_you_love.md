# Pitch: More of What You Love

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Pleasant, personal, attentive.
A feed that seems to know you, full of what you like and agree with, your language and your people, each thing a little more to your taste than the last.
Nothing sinister in it: only a service giving you more of what you love.

## Undertow

Under the pleasure, the range of what you see narrowing with every choice.
Because the feed gives more of what you click and less of what you skip, the opposing view stops appearing, not banned but never offered, and sealed among the agreeable you meet only confirmation, hardening and drifting with a chorus that hears only itself.
The kindness to each reader is, in the sum, the loss of the common world.

## Nerve

The feeling of a feed that finally gets you, every post interesting, every voice agreeing,
sure it is simply a better medium, because for you it is,
while the other side quietly vanishes unbanned, and the ground where you might have argued with it goes with it.

## Echo

After the run: a thousand private feeds, each pleasant, each certain, no two seeing the same world,
no one lied to and nothing censored, only each reader clicking the agreeable and each feed serving more of it,
and a serendipity that would have held the world in common, refused as friction, because it was exactly the thing the reader clicks away.
