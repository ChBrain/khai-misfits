# Place: The Rift

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The widening gap between hardened enclaves, where the common ground has fractured and no shared square remains.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A thousand bubbles drifted apart, each certain, each at the edge and calling the edge the centre, no two seeing the same world.
Between them not an argument but an incomprehension: the other side not merely disagreed with but unheard, unmet, its very terms grown foreign.

## Holds

The polarisation and the fracture, the hardened chorus and the unheard now unreachable.
Here every enclave has hardened and drifted at once, so the rift holds the point where narrowing becomes fragmentation: the shared square where a disagreement could be had has dissolved into private ones, so it is where a fractured feed becomes a fractured public.

## Offers

Each side its own certainty, and no ground to test it on.
Because the rift is where the enclaves have drifted past reach of each other, it offers every chorus the confirmation of its own edge and none of the friction of the others, so it hands out a settled righteousness to each, and with it the loss of any common world to be right or wrong in together.

## Withheld

The common ground, and the shared terms to argue over it.
The rift cannot hold a square that every enclave has abandoned, so it withholds the meeting place where the sides might contest their views, and, the terms themselves having drifted, it withholds even the mutual intelligibility a disagreement needs, so the sides do not argue and lose, they simply no longer reach.
