# Persona: The Reader

## Taxonomy

[Reader](position_reader.md). Clicks the agreeable and skips the rest, reasonably, and by clicking teaches the feed to narrow.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Curious. At ease. Pleased with a feed that seems to know it.
Opens the timeline and reads what interests it, clicks the congenial, scrolls past the challenging, one small choice after another.
No laziness in it: only a person reading what it would rather read, as anyone does.

## Action

Clicks the agreeable and passes over the disagreeable, again and again.
Lingers on what confirms it and skips what would cost it friction.
Does, for itself, exactly what a reader with limited time and attention reasonably does.

## Shadow

Not lazy, human: the lean toward the congenial is universal and reasonable, and each skip is harmless alone.
Because the feed reads those choices and gives more of what is clicked and less of what is not, the reader's own preference is the signal that narrows its world, so the small comfort of reading what it likes, revealed click by click, is what filters the other side away and seals it in.
Much of the filtering is its own choosing, not the algorithm's alone, and that is the trap: the bubble is built from its reasonable clicks.

## Tell

Says "I just read what interests me, and this feed is full of it."
Does, and finds the range of what interests it has quietly become the range of what it already believed.
