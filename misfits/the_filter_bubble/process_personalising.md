# Process: Personalising

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The reading of the reader's clicks into a feed cut ever closer to its taste, sound as service and narrowing in sum.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A reader with a preference and a feed built to hold attention.
At [the timeline](place_the_timeline.md), the reader clicking the agreeable and skipping the rest, and the feed reading each choice as a signal to rank by.

## Direction

Toward a feed fitted to the revealed preference.
Each click for the congenial teaches the feed to serve more like it, and each skip teaches it to serve less, so personalising drives the timeline to match the reader's taste ever more closely, the fit tightening session by session.

## Lever

The reasonableness of the preference and the soundness of the service.
Because preferring the agreeable is human and giving people more of what they choose is what a good service does, each act that feeds the personalising is right for the one who makes it, so the process runs on individual good sense, the whole narrowing made of moves no one can fault.

## Echo

A feed the reader loves, cut close to its taste.
The reader has a timeline that seems to know it and the feed has its held attention, and the range of what is shown is narrower for it, so what reads as a service perfected is, unseen, the start of the narrowing that will seal the reader in.
