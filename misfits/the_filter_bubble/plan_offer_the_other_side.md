# Plan: Offer the Other Side

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the unheard's seat](position_unheard.md), inside [The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Unheard](position_unheard.md)

## Direction

Put the other side where the reader might meet it. Offer the challenging, the opposing, the disagreeable, the friction that could move a mind, and stay in view as the counterweight to the congenial. Be the half of the argument the reader would not otherwise choose.

## Orders

([The Unheard](persona_the_unheard.md) offers the opposing view and is skipped, turning up less each session as the feed reads the reader's passing-over.)

## Implementation

([The Timeline](place_the_timeline.md); the [narrowing](process_narrowing.md) process; the opposing view, never clicked, is dropped by [the filter](piece_the_filter.md), so [the serendipity](piece_the_serendipity.md) that might have moved the reader is filtered away.)

## Targets

- [ ] the opposing view is offered where the reader might meet it
- [ ] the reader passes it over as the disagreeable
- [ ] the feed reads the passing-over and offers it less
- [ ] the other side disappears unbanned, the range narrowed with nothing to mark it
