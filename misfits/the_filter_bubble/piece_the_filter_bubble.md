# Piece: The Filter Bubble

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The phenomenon whole, a feed that pleases each reader and fragments the common world, held open only against the pull of both.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Everywhere the production runs: from [the timeline](place_the_timeline.md) through [the bubble](place_the_bubble.md) to [the rift](place_the_rift.md), and against [the square](place_the_square.md) where it is resisted.
It is the whole arc, the personalised feed that narrows to an enclave that drifts to a fracture.

## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without a name: a preference, a filter, an echo, a drift, and no account of what they sum to.
With it the misfit is whole: the filter bubble is the trap entire, each reader served exactly what it wants and all readers left no world in common, the pleasant feed and the fractured public shown to be one thing. It is load bearing because it is the structure the whole production exists to stage (Pariser; Sunstein): the frame that binds the appetite, the personaliser, the confirmation, and the drift into a single bend, so the piece carries the phenomenon as a whole, a kindness to each that is, in the sum, the loss of the common world.

## Apparent

A better internet, a medium that finally gives everyone exactly what they want.
Seen at the surface, the filter bubble looks like pure progress, personalisation as service, each of us with a feed that knows us, and the truth that a medium giving each person their own edited world leaves no two of us a world in common, that the fragmentation is the personalisation succeeding, stays hidden behind how plainly good giving people what they want appears.

## Yearbook

The whole of it: the Filter Bubble is [the agreeable](piece_the_agreeable.md) read by [the filter](piece_the_filter.md), sealed by [the echo](piece_the_echo.md), hardened into [the polarisation](piece_the_polarisation.md), and answered only by [the serendipity](piece_the_serendipity.md) that costs the comfort the medium runs on.
It is the misfit named: a feed pleasant to each reader and fracturing to all, no censor in it and no hero, the common world quietly filtered away.
