# Persona: The Chorus

## Taxonomy

[Chorus](position_chorus.md). The like-minded talking only to each other, echoing the reader's priors and hardening as it hears itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Warm. Agreeing. Sure.
Is the enclave of the like-minded the reader lands among, every voice returning its own view a little louder.
No conspiracy in it: only people who agree, gathered where the feed gathered them, glad to be understood.

## Action

Echoes the reader's priors back on every side, confirming and amplifying.
Hears only itself, deliberates only among itself, and moves to a bolder version of what it already believed.
Does what any room of the like-minded does when it hears no dissent: grows surer, and drifts to the edge.

## Shadow

Not a mob, a comfort: agreement feels like truth and company feels like confirmation, and nothing in the room is false.
Because a like-minded group hearing only itself polarises, sliding to a more extreme prior and calling the edge the centre, the warmth of being understood is the mechanism that hardens the reader and widens the gap to everyone outside, and it does so with no villain, only voices agreeing.
It is confirmation mistaken for consensus, and its hardening is what turns a narrowed feed into a fractured world.

## Tell

Says "everyone I hear agrees, so surely we are right."
Is right that everyone it hears agrees, and wrong that everyone is who it hears.
