# Place: The Timeline

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). Where the reader reads and clicks, and where the feed reads the clicks and fits itself closer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A scrolling feed of posts ranked to the reader, the agreeable near the top and the challenging far down or gone.
The reader clicking and skipping, and the order quietly re-fitting to each choice, so the next session opens a shade more to its taste than the last.

## Holds

The reading and the personalising, the click and the ranking that reads it.
Here the reader's preference meets the feed's design, so the timeline holds the point where a reasonable choice becomes a narrowing signal: each click is registered and answered, and the sum of the answers is a feed cut ever closer to fit, so it is where taste turns into range lost.

## Offers

More of what the reader loves, endlessly.
Because the timeline is where the feed serves the revealed preference, it offers each reader a stream tuned to please, the congenial ranked up and the friction ranked away, so it hands out exactly the comfort the reader clicked for, and with it the narrowing that comfort drives.

## Withheld

The other side, and any sign of its going.
The timeline cannot show the reader what it filtered out, so it withholds the opposing view the reader did not click, and, showing only what remains, it withholds the absence itself, so the reader meets a narrowed world with nothing to mark that it narrowed.
