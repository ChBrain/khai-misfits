# Plan: Read What I Like

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the reader's seat](position_reader.md), inside [The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Reader](position_reader.md)

## Direction

Read what interests you. Click the agreeable and the congenial, scroll past the challenging and the dull, and enjoy a feed that fits your taste. You cannot read everything, so read what you like, one small reasonable choice at a time.

## Orders

([The Reader](persona_the_reader.md) clicks the agreeable and passes over the disagreeable, following its own taste as any reader with limited attention reasonably does.)

## Implementation

([The Timeline](place_the_timeline.md); the [personalising](process_personalising.md) process; each click for [the agreeable](piece_the_agreeable.md) is the signal [the filter](piece_the_filter.md) reads to serve more of the same.)

## Targets

- [ ] the agreeable is clicked and the disagreeable scrolled past
- [ ] the reader follows its own taste, one reasonable choice at a time
- [ ] the feed reads the clicks and fits the timeline closer
- [ ] the reader's own preference becomes the signal that narrows its world
