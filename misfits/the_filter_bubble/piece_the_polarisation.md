# Piece: The Polarisation

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The drift to the edge, the like-minded enclave hardening to a bolder prior as it hears only itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the rift](place_the_rift.md), where the drifted enclaves have fractured the common ground.
Set against [the square](place_the_square.md), the shared space its drift dissolves.

## Load Bearing

Without it the sealed reader is merely confirmed, and a hundred bubbles could sit side by side unchanged.
With it the enclaves move: a like-minded group deliberating only among itself shifts to a more extreme version of its view, so confirmation is not a resting state but a current, and every bubble drifts outward at once. The Polarisation is load bearing because it is what turns many narrow feeds into a fractured public (Isenberg; Sunstein): the group-polarisation finding is the step from sealed to split, so the piece carries the drift that hardens each enclave and pulls the common ground apart.

## Apparent

A community growing more committed, people finding their convictions among those who share them.
Seen as it happens, polarisation looks like conviction and solidarity, a group coming into its own, and the truth that deliberation among only the like-minded reliably pushes a group to its extreme, that the drift is a mechanism and not a choosing, stays hidden behind how much like principled commitment the slide to the edge appears.

## Yearbook

Driven by [the chorus](persona_the_chorus.md) through [polarising](process_polarising.md), the hardening to a bolder prior.
It is the fracture of the world: the Polarisation is what [the echo](piece_the_echo.md) hardens into, what drifts each bubble until the common ground is [the rift](place_the_rift.md), and the divide that [the serendipity](piece_the_serendipity.md), meeting the other side again, might have held off, or, forced too hard, only deepened.
