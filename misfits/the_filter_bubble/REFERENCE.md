# The Filter Bubble: Reference

The research warrant for [The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md).
The frame is Pariser's; the play runs on his account of the personalised feed, through Sunstein's work on
enclaves and group polarisation and the selective-exposure literature beneath both, all cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging the filter bubble as an **information system**: a personalised feed in which each reader's reasonable
preference for the agreeable, read and served by a system built to hold attention, narrows the range of what is
seen, seals the reader among the like-minded, and, multiplied across every enclave, fragments the common ground
on which a disagreement could be had. The play models the documented mechanics, the human lean toward the
congenial over the challenging, the personaliser that gives more of what is clicked and less of what is not, the
opposing view that disappears unbanned because it is never chosen, the confirmation returned among the
like-minded, the group that hardens and drifts to the edge when it hears only itself, and the exposure to the
unchosen that alone holds the range open, at a cost, as the load-bearing vectors of the structure, not as a tale
of a reader too lazy or a designer too cynical. The governing finding of the source literature is the play's
governing law: a medium that gives each person exactly the content they prefer serves each and fragments all,
because the preferred is the congenial, the congenial confirms, and confirmation among the like-minded polarises,
so the pleasanter the feed the narrower the world it leaves. It is distinct from the house's The Information
Cascade, where each actor rationally copies the visible action of those ahead on a shared public signal: there
the herd converges on one path from a common feed, here each reader diverges into a private one from a feed cut
to fit only them. And it is distinct from the house's Closing Ranks, where a cohesive group tightens against an
outside threat: there the sealing is a chosen loyalty under pressure, here it is an unchosen narrowing no one
intends and no threat drives.

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast the reader as lazy
  or the feed's designer as cynical, because the source finding is precisely that each move is reasonable:
  preferring the agreeable to the challenging is human, and building a service that gives people more of what
  they linger on is what good design does. The fragmentation is authored by no censor and emerges from the
  structure, a human appetite for the congenial met by a system built to satisfy it, so the misfit is that a
  medium giving each of us exactly what we want leaves no two of us a world in common.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into a costless correction. The one move that holds the
  range open is to seed the feed with the unchosen and keep public squares where strangers meet what they did
  not pick, and it costs the comfort and the attention the whole medium runs on, is resisted by the very readers
  it would serve, and forced too hard can harden the divide it meant to heal. The reckoning is a range held open
  against the preference of each reader and the design of the medium at once, not a free correction, so the cure
  for a fractured common world is the friction the reader is right to click away.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here, never claimed
  as the house's. The staging is the house's; the filter-bubble frame, the enclave and group-polarisation work,
  the selective-exposure finding, and the empirical nuance that user choice does much of the filtering are
  theirs, and the condition the trap actually requires, a personalised feed read from revealed preference plus a
  human lean toward the congenial, is carried as carefully as the finding itself, the empirical caveat included.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **[The Agreeable](piece_the_agreeable.md)**: carries the congenial content the reader prefers (Negroponte;
  Festinger; Frey), the confirming over the challenging that the feed reads and serves.
- **[The Filter](piece_the_filter.md)**: carries the personaliser (Pariser), the mechanism that gives more of
  what is clicked and quietly drops what is not.
- **[The Echo](piece_the_echo.md)**: carries the confirmation returned (Sunstein), the reader's priors met on
  every side among the like-minded.
- **[The Polarisation](piece_the_polarisation.md)**: carries the drift to the edge (Isenberg; Sunstein), the
  like-minded enclave hardening as it hears only itself.
- **[The Serendipity](piece_the_serendipity.md)**: carries the remedy (Sunstein), the exposure to the unchosen
  that holds the range open, and the friction that makes it costly.
- **[The Filter Bubble](piece_the_filter_bubble.md)**: carries the phenomenon whole (Pariser; Sunstein), a feed
  that pleases each reader and fragments the common world, held open only against the pull of both.
