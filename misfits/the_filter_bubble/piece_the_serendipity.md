# Piece: The Serendipity

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The exposure to the unchosen that holds the range open, and the friction that makes it costly.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the square](place_the_square.md), where the unchosen is seeded and strangers meet what they did not pick.
Set against [the timeline](place_the_timeline.md), the tailored feed whose smooth fit it is the deliberate break in.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is no remedy in the play, and the narrowing runs to the rift with nothing to answer it.
With it the misfit has its costly cure: exposure to the unchosen, the disagreeable turned up beside the congenial, is the one thing that widens the range and keeps a common ground. The Serendipity is load bearing because it is the remedy that carries its own defeat (Sunstein): the friction that would hold the world open is exactly what the reader clicks away and the feed is built to remove, so the piece carries both the cure and the reason it is refused, the unchosen encounter that costs the comfort and attention the whole medium runs on.

## Apparent

A nuisance, an interruption, the irrelevant thing cluttering a feed that was finally perfect.
Seen by the reader, serendipity looks like friction and noise, the post it did not want in the way of the posts it did, and the truth that this unchosen encounter is the one thing keeping its world wide and the common ground whole, that the nuisance is the remedy, stays hidden behind how much like a flaw the friction feels to the reader it would serve.

## Yearbook

Seeded by [the curator](persona_the_curator.md) through [mixing](process_mixing.md), the unchosen exposure that holds the range open.
It is the cure the play cannot make painless: the Serendipity is what [the filter](piece_the_filter.md) strips and [the narrowing](process_narrowing.md) removes, the friction that would break [the echo](piece_the_echo.md) and hold off [the polarisation](piece_the_polarisation.md), refused because it is exactly what [the agreeable](piece_the_agreeable.md) is preferred over.
