# Position: Unheard

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The seat of the opposing view, never clicked and so never offered, filtered out unbanned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A view to offer, and no way to be shown that does not run through the reader's click.
A standing in the feed that lasts only as long as it is chosen, and it is the disagreeable, so it is not.
No censor against it, and no defence either: it depends entirely on being picked, and it is passed over.

## Orders

Offer the other side.
Put the challenging and the opposing where the reader might meet it, the friction that could move a mind.
Stay in view, the counterweight to the congenial.

## Loses

Its place, exactly because it is disagreeable.
Because the feed shows more of the clicked and less of the skipped, and the reader skips the opposing, the seat is filtered down to nothing having lost no argument, so it cannot both be the challenging view and remain in the feed: the very disagreeableness that makes it worth showing is what gets it dropped.
It disappears unremarked, and its absence is the sign the bubble has sealed.

## Drives

Toward a view worth meeting and never met,
gone not by ban but by never being chosen,
so the friction that might have kept the world open is the first thing filtered away.
