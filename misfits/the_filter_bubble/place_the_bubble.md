# Place: The Bubble

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The sealed enclave the narrowed feed leaves the reader in, where everything agrees and nothing disconfirms.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A world that fits the reader perfectly, every post congenial, every voice agreeing, the reader's own priors returned on every side.
No walls visible: it looks like the whole world, only a world that happens to think exactly as the reader does, warm and complete and closed.

## Holds

The confirmation and the company, the sealed reader and the chorus around it.
Here the narrowed feed has finished its work, so the bubble holds the point where a personalised stream becomes an enclave: the reader meets only what confirms it, among the like-minded who confirm it too, so it is where a feed cut to taste becomes a room that hears only itself.

## Offers

Certainty, and the comfort of never being wrong.
Because the bubble is where nothing disconfirms, it offers the reader the ease of a settled belief and the warmth of unbroken agreement, so it hands out the pleasure of confirmation, and with it the hardening that a room hearing only itself undergoes.

## Withheld

The disconfirming, and the sense of a wall.
The bubble cannot show what it sealed out, so it withholds every view that would cost the reader friction, and, seeming to be the whole world, it withholds the knowledge that it is an enclave at all, so the reader is certain without ever seeing the edge of its certainty.
