# Piece: The Echo

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The confirmation returned, the reader's priors met on every side among the like-minded.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the bubble](place_the_bubble.md), where it returns the reader's priors on every side.
Set against [the square](place_the_square.md), where a stranger's disagreement would break the return.

## Load Bearing

Without it a narrowed feed is only a thinner reading list, and the reader keeps its own measure of its views.
With it the narrowing becomes a hall of mirrors: everything the reader meets confirms it, so its priors come back as consensus and its belief hardens from held to obvious. The Echo is load bearing because it is the step from a filtered feed to a sealed mind (Sunstein): confirmation among the like-minded is what turns a narrow range into certainty, so the piece carries the returned agreement that makes the reader sure, the confirmation that a room of the like-minded mistakes for the truth.

## Apparent

The comfort of being understood, a feed and a company that simply agree with you.
Seen from inside, the echo feels like being right and being in good company, warm and validating, and the truth that unbroken agreement is not consensus but a sealed sample, that the priors returned are the reader's own sent back, stays hidden behind how much like confirmation the warmth of agreement feels.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the chorus](persona_the_chorus.md) through [polarising](process_polarising.md), the confirmation returned on every side.
It is the seal of the bubble: the Echo is what [the filter](piece_the_filter.md) and the narrowing leave the reader in, the confirmation that hardens into [the polarisation](piece_the_polarisation.md), and the closed return that only [the serendipity](piece_the_serendipity.md), a disagreement let back in, can break.
