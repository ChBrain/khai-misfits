# Plot: The Mixing

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Square](place_the_square.md), the world fragmented and one actor moving to hold it open.
The range narrowed to enclaves, and the levers of serendipity and the common square at hand.

## Action

[Mixing](process_mixing.md): [The Curator](persona_the_curator.md), holding [the curator's seat](position_curator.md), seeds the unchosen and keeps a public square, against [The Reader](persona_the_reader.md), holding [the reader's seat](position_reader.md), whose pull is to the agreeable and whose feed is built to strip the friction.

## Stage

[The Curator](persona_the_curator.md), having tabled [Break the Bubble](plan_break_the_bubble.md), seeds the disagreeable and the unpicked into the feed and defends a square where strangers meet what they did not choose, spending comfort and attention to widen the range.
[The Reader](persona_the_reader.md), still on [Read What I Like](plan_read_what_i_like.md), clicks the friction away and drifts back toward the feed that fits, and pressed too hard only hardens against the other side.

## Tension

The one move that keeps a world in common is the one every reader is right to refuse.
There is no easy remedy: seeding the unchosen plainly holds the range open, yet it costs the comfort and attention the whole medium runs on, is resisted by the very readers it serves, and forced too hard can deepen the divide it meant to heal. So the play closes where it must, the range held open a while against the pull of each reader and the design of the feed ... or the friction is clicked away and the bubble re-forms.
