# Place: The Square

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The public common ground where strangers meet what they did not pick, held open only against the pull of every reader.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An open space no one tailored, where the unchosen turns up beside the chosen and strangers of every enclave cross.
The curator seeding the disagreeable into it, and the readers, free to leave, drifting back toward the feeds cut to fit them, so the square stands half-empty against the warm bubbles.

## Holds

The mixing and the resistance, the seeded serendipity and the reader's pull away from it.
Here the one move that keeps a world in common meets the preference that undoes it, so the square holds the point where the remedy meets its cost: exposure to the unpicked is offered, and the friction of it is exactly what the reader clicks away, so it is where holding the range open is bought against everyone it would serve.

## Offers

The unchosen, and a common ground to meet it on.
Because the square is where what no one picked still appears, it offers the reader the serendipity the feed filtered out and the strangers the shared space the rift dissolved, so it hands out the friction that keeps a world in common, and asks, in return, the comfort and attention the reader would rather spend elsewhere.

## Withheld

The comfort of the fit, and any way to make the reader stay.
The square cannot both hold the friction of the unchosen and match the ease of the tailored feed, so it withholds the smooth agreeable stream the reader prefers, and, unable to compel attention, it withholds any hold on the reader who is always free to click back to the bubble, so the common ground stays open only as long as someone spends to keep it so.
