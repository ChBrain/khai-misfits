# Persona: The Curator

## Taxonomy

[Curator](position_curator.md). Would seed the feed with the unchosen and keep a public square, against the pull of every reader and the design of the medium.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Public-minded. Uneasy. Set on holding the range open.
Sees the world fragmenting into pleasant enclaves and moves to keep the strangers meeting, seeding the unchosen and defending a common square.
No zealotry in it: only someone who would have the readers still share a world to argue over.

## Action

Seeds the feed with the disagreeable and the unpicked, and keeps public squares where strangers meet what they did not choose.
Breaks the smooth fit of the personaliser to let serendipity back in, the friction that widens the range.
Does the one thing that holds the common ground: gives the reader what it did not ask for.

## Shadow

Not a saviour: the remedy costs exactly what the medium runs on, and the readers it serves resist it.
Because serendipity is friction and friction is what the reader clicks away and the feed is built to remove, seeding the unchosen fights the comfort of each reader and the design of the whole system at once, and forced too hard it can harden the very divide it means to heal, exposure breeding backlash.
It cannot make the personalised feed widen the world; it can only spend comfort and attention to hold a range open against everyone's pull, and be resented for it.

## Tell

Says "you should see what you did not choose."
Offers it, and the reader, free to click away the friction, mostly does.
