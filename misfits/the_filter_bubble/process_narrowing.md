# Process: Narrowing

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The dropping of what the reader does not click, so the opposing view disappears unbanned and the range shrinks to the congenial.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A feed that serves more of the clicked and a reader that clicks the agreeable.
At [the timeline](place_the_timeline.md), the opposing view offered and passed over, and the feed reading the passing-over as a reason to offer it less.

## Direction

Toward a range shrunk to the agreeable.
What is not clicked is offered less and then not at all, so narrowing drives the challenging and the opposing out of the feed, until the reader sees only the congenial and the other side is gone, filtered down to nothing having lost no argument.

## Lever

The invisibility of the absence.
Because the feed drops the unclicked quietly and shows only what remains, the reader never meets the view that was removed and so never misses it, so the process runs unremarked, its work hidden by the very completeness of the feed it leaves, and no one has to ban a thing to make it vanish.

## Echo

A feed of the agreeable, the other side gone.
The reader meets only what confirms it and the opposing view has left no trace of its leaving, so what reads as a feed simply full of good things is, unseen, a range narrowed to a single side, the friction that might have moved the reader filtered away.
