# Plot: The Narrowing

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Timeline](place_the_timeline.md), what is not clicked no longer offered.
The opposing view turning up less each session, and the reader, meeting only the congenial, noticing nothing gone.

## Action

[Narrowing](process_narrowing.md): [The Feed](persona_the_feed.md), holding [the feed's seat](position_feed.md), stops showing the views the reader passes over, while [The Unheard](persona_the_unheard.md), holding [the unheard's seat](position_unheard.md), is offered less and then not at all.

## Stage

[The Feed](persona_the_feed.md), still on [Give Them More](plan_give_them_more.md), reads the reader's passing-over and demotes the opposing view until it drops from the feed entirely.
[The Unheard](persona_the_unheard.md), on [Offer the Other Side](plan_offer_the_other_side.md), offers the challenging and the opposing and is skipped, disappearing unbanned, having lost no argument.

## Tension

The other side does not lose a debate, it simply stops being shown, and no one marks its going.
There is no censor: the feed only serves less of what is skipped, and the reader only skips the disagreeable, each move as reasonable as before. But what is not clicked is filtered to nothing, so the plot closes with the range shrunk to the congenial and the friction that might have moved the reader gone, the absence invisible because the reader never sees what was removed.
