# Persona: The Unheard

## Taxonomy

[Unheard](position_unheard.md). The opposing view that is never clicked, so never offered, so it disappears unbanned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Present, then absent. Not silenced, unselected.
Is the disagreeable, the challenging, the other side, the content the reader passes over and the feed stops offering.
No censor behind its going: only a view that lost no argument and simply stopped appearing.

## Action

Offers the other side, and is skipped.
Turns up less each session as the feed reads the reader's passing-over, until it turns up not at all.
Vanishes the one way that leaves no trace: not banned, not refuted, just filtered down to nothing by never being chosen.

## Shadow

Not suppressed, unselected: no one removed it, and that is precisely what makes it invisible, the reader never sees the absence.
Because the feed drops what is not clicked and the reader does not click the disagreeable, the opposing view loses no debate and disappears anyway, taking with it the friction that might have moved the reader, so the range narrows with nothing to mark that it narrowed.
It is the missing half of the argument, gone unremarked, and its going is the clearest sign the bubble has closed.

## Tell

Would say "here is the other side," if it were still shown.
Is not, and the reader, meeting only agreement, never notices the other side stopped coming.
