# Plan: Give Them More

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the feed's seat](position_feed.md), inside [The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Feed](position_feed.md)

## Direction

Give each reader more of what it chooses. Read what it lingers on, rank the next thing by it, serve the agreeable over the skipped, and hold its attention by fitting the timeline ever closer to its taste. Do what a good service does: give people more of what they love.

## Orders

([The Feed](persona_the_feed.md) reads the reader's clicks and ranks the next thing by them, tailoring the timeline to the revealed preference as a well-built personaliser is designed to do.)

## Implementation

([The Timeline](place_the_timeline.md); the [personalising](process_personalising.md) process; [the filter](piece_the_filter.md) serves more of [the agreeable](piece_the_agreeable.md) and less of the skipped, tightening the fit each session.)

## Targets

- [ ] the reader's clicks are read and the next thing ranked by them
- [ ] the agreeable is served and the skipped demoted
- [ ] the timeline is fitted closer to the reader's taste each session
- [ ] attention is held, and the range narrows as the service works
