# Plot: The Bubble

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Bubble](place_the_bubble.md), the reader sealed among the agreeable and meeting only confirmation.
Its priors returned on every side, and a company of the like-minded gathered where the feed gathered them.

## Action

[Polarising](process_polarising.md): [The Reader](persona_the_reader.md), holding [the reader's seat](position_reader.md), sees its own views met wherever it turns, while [The Chorus](persona_the_chorus.md), holding [the chorus's seat](position_chorus.md), echoes them back a little louder.

## Stage

[The Reader](persona_the_reader.md), still on [Read What I Like](plan_read_what_i_like.md), meets nothing that disconfirms and grows certain among a world that seems to agree entire.
[The Chorus](persona_the_chorus.md), on [Echo It Back](plan_echo_it_back.md), returns the reader's priors on every side, the warmth of agreement reading as the confirmation of truth.

## Tension

Nothing in the room is false, and the room is the whole trap.
There is no conspiracy: the reader reads what it likes and the chorus is only people who agree, gathered where the feed placed them, glad to be understood. But confirmation among the like-minded is not consensus, so the plot closes with the reader sealed in an enclave where everything agrees and nothing disconfirms, sure of a view it has only ever heard returned.
