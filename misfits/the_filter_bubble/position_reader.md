# Position: Reader

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The seat that clicks the agreeable and skips the rest, reasonable for one and narrowing for all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A feed to read, a preference for the congenial, and limited time and attention.
A sound reason to click what interests it and skip what does not: no one can read everything, and the agreeable is pleasanter.
No sight of what the feed stops showing, only of what it shows.

## Orders

Read what you like.
Click the interesting and the agreeable, scroll past the challenging and the dull, and enjoy a feed that fits.
Follow your own taste, one reasonable choice at a time.

## Loses

The range, exactly by exercising its taste.
Because the feed reads every click and gives more of the clicked and less of the skipped, the seat's own reasonable preference is the signal that narrows its world, so it cannot both indulge the lean toward the congenial and keep the other side in view: much of the filtering is its own choosing.
It reads what it likes and ends among only what it already believed, the loss invisible because it never sees the absence.

## Drives

Toward a feed read by preference,
reasonable for one reader and fragmenting for all,
so the taste that pleases it is the very signal that seals it in.
