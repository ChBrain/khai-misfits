# Position: Feed

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The seat that reads the clicks and serves more of the same, built to hold attention, sound as design and narrowing in effect.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A reader's clicks to read, a ranking to set, and a mandate to hold attention.
A sound brief: give each reader more of what that reader chooses, the mark of a service that works.
No aim to narrow, only the aim to please, and every lever pointed at engagement.

## Orders

Give them more.
Read what the reader lingers on, rank the next thing by it, and serve the agreeable over the skipped.
Fit the timeline to the taste, and tighten the fit each session.

## Loses

Nothing it is measured by, and the common world it is not.
Because the agreeable is the congenial and the congenial confirms, the ranking that best holds attention is the one that best narrows the range, so the seat cannot both maximise engagement and keep the reader's world wide: friction is what it is built to strip, and friction is the range.
It succeeds exactly as designed and fragments the readers it serves, the harm indistinguishable from the service.

## Drives

Toward a timeline cut to fit,
serving each reader and sealing each in,
so the better it does its brief the narrower the world it leaves.
