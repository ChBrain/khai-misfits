# Process: Polarising

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The hardening and drift of a like-minded enclave that hears only itself, and the fracture of the common ground it drives.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A reader sealed among the agreeable and a chorus that echoes its priors.
At [the bubble](place_the_bubble.md), the like-minded deliberating only among themselves, each voice returned a little louder and no dissent within earshot.

## Direction

Toward the edge, and away from the common ground.
A like-minded group hearing only itself moves to a bolder version of its prior, so polarising drives each enclave outward to its extreme and, with every enclave drifting at once, drives the shared square apart, until the sides no longer disagree but no longer reach.

## Lever

The comfort of agreement mistaken for the confirmation of truth.
Because a room of the like-minded feels like consensus and being understood feels like being right, the drift to the edge is powered by nothing but agreement, so the process runs on warmth alone, the hardening made of voices each glad to confirm and none meaning to divide.

## Echo

Enclaves certain and drifted, the square between them gone.
Each chorus is surer and further out, calling its edge the centre, and the common ground where the sides might have argued has fractured, so what reads as a community finding its voice is, unseen, a public splitting into private certainties that can no longer hear each other.
