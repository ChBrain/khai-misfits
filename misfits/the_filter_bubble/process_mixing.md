# Process: Mixing

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The seeding of the unchosen back into the feed and the keeping of a common square, the one move that holds the range open, at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A curator who sees the world fragmenting and would hold it open.
At [the square](place_the_square.md), the disagreeable and the unpicked seeded in beside the chosen, and strangers of every enclave crossing where the feed would have kept them apart.

## Direction

Toward a range held open, against the pull that closes it.
The curator seeds the unchosen and defends the square, so mixing drives serendipity back into a feed built to remove it, widening the reader's world with the friction it filtered out, and holding the common ground the narrowing dissolved.

## Lever

Nothing the reader wants, and everything it clicks away.
Because serendipity is friction and friction is what the reader clicks off and the feed is built to strip, the process has no lever in the preference or the design, so it runs only on the curator's spending of comfort and attention, against the reader's pull and the medium's build, and pressed too hard it hardens the divide it meant to heal.

## Echo

A range held open, bought dear.
The reader has met what it did not pick and the square has held a while, and the comfort and attention it cost are gone and the reader is pulling back to the feed that fits, so what reads as the world reopened is, unseen, a friction sustained against everyone it serves, undone the moment the spending stops.
