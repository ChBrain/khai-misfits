# Position: Curator

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The seat that would seed the unchosen and keep a public square, against the reader's pull and the medium's design.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A lever over what the feed shows, and the will to widen it.
A public square to defend, where strangers might meet what they did not pick, and the knowledge of what the narrowing costs.
No power to make the reader want the friction, only the power to place it in the way.

## Orders

Break the bubble.
Seed the disagreeable and the unchosen, keep squares where the enclaves must meet, and let serendipity back into the feed.
Hold the range open, the one move that keeps a world in common.

## Loses

The comfort and the attention, exactly what the medium runs on.
Because serendipity is friction and the reader clicks the friction away and the feed is built to remove it, the seat cannot hold the range open without spending the engagement the whole system is measured by and defying the preference of every reader it serves, and pressed too hard the exposure hardens the divide.
It buys the common ground against the pull of each reader and the design of the medium at once, and is resented for the purchase.

## Drives

Toward a range held open,
bought against every reader's taste and the feed's own build,
so the one seat that would keep the world in common must fight the world to do it.
