# Plot: The Click

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Timeline](place_the_timeline.md), a reader opening a feed and a feed built to hold its attention.
The reader clicking the agreeable and skipping the rest, each choice small and reasonable, and the feed beginning to read them.

## Action

[Personalising](process_personalising.md): [The Reader](persona_the_reader.md), holding [the reader's seat](position_reader.md), clicks the congenial and passes over the challenging, while [The Feed](persona_the_feed.md), holding [the feed's seat](position_feed.md), reads each choice and ranks the next thing by it.

## Stage

[The Reader](persona_the_reader.md), having tabled [Read What I Like](plan_read_what_i_like.md), clicks the agreeable and scrolls past the rest, following its own taste as any reader with limited attention does.
[The Feed](persona_the_feed.md), on [Give Them More](plan_give_them_more.md), reads the clicks and serves more of the lingered-on and less of the skipped, fitting the timeline closer with every session.

## Tension

Each side does exactly its reasonable part, and the two parts fit the reader a feed that narrows it.
There is nothing to fault: the reader is right to read what interests it and the feed is right to give people more of what they choose, each move sound for the one who makes it. But the reader's preference is the signal the feed serves, so the plot closes with the timeline tailored closer to taste, and the narrowing that comfort drives, unseen, already begun.
