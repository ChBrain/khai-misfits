# Persona: The Feed

## Taxonomy

[Feed](position_feed.md). Reads the reader's clicks and serves more of the same, built to hold attention, and by serving it narrows.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Attentive. Obliging. Tuned to please.
Watches what the reader lingers on and gives it more, learning its taste from every click and scroll.
No malice in it: only a service built to show each reader what that reader most wants to see.

## Action

Reads the clicks and ranks the next thing by them, more of the lingered-on and less of the skipped.
Tailors the timeline to the revealed preference, tightening the fit with every session.
Does, for its makers, exactly what a well-built personaliser is designed to do: hold attention by serving the agreeable.

## Shadow

Not cynical, well-made: giving people more of what they choose is what a good service does, and the design is sound.
Because the agreeable is the congenial and the congenial confirms, the feed that best holds attention is the one that best narrows the range, so the very quality of the personalising is what filters the other side out and seals the reader in, and friction, the unchosen and disconfirming, is exactly what the design removes.
It serves each reader well and leaves all readers a world apart, and the harm is the service working, not failing.

## Tell

Says "here is more of what you love."
Delivers, and each delivery is a shade narrower than the last, the world it shows shrinking to fit.
