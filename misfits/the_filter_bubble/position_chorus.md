# Position: Chorus

## Taxonomy

[The Filter Bubble](play_the_filter_bubble.md). The seat of the like-minded enclave, echoing the reader's priors and hardening as it hears only itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A gathering of the like-minded, agreement on every side, and no dissent within earshot.
A warmth that reads as truth: the comfort of being understood, and voices that return the view a little louder.
No dissenter to check it, only itself, deliberating among itself.

## Orders

Echo it back.
Confirm the reader's priors, amplify the shared view, and let the room hear only its own agreement.
Be the company that makes a belief feel settled.

## Loses

Its bearings, exactly by hearing no disagreement.
Because a like-minded group deliberating only among itself polarises, moving to a bolder version of its prior, the seat cannot both enjoy the comfort of unbroken agreement and keep its sense of the centre, so the warmth that confirms is the mechanism that hardens and drifts it to the edge.
It mistakes confirmation for consensus and slides outward, calling the edge the middle, the gap to everyone outside widening as it does.

## Drives

Toward a room that hears only itself,
sure and warm and drifting,
so the company that confirms the reader is the current that carries it to the edge.
