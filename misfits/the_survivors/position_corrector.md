# Position: Corrector

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md). The reversal seat: reasoning from the plane that never came back.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The will to reason from the absent.
The nerve to trust the gap over the sample in hand.

## Orders

Count the failures.
Read the gaps.
Armour where there are no holes.

## Loses

The flattering lesson, the one everyone already believes.
The vivid evidence's easy authority, traded for a harder inference.

## Drives

Toward the true inference, drawn from what did not return.
Away from the clean story, which survives because it soothes.
The armour moved to where it saves a plane, not where the saved were struck.
