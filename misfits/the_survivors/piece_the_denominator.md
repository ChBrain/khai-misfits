# Piece: The Denominator

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md). The count of all who tried, hidden by the ones who lived.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the briefing](place_the_briefing.md), under the success rate no one writes down.
The whole field the winners came from: every plane that flew, every fund that launched, every founder who started with the same plan. The number below the line, of which the survivors are only the top.

## Load Bearing

Without it a survivor's recipe looks certain, a straight line from move to win.
With it the same recipe looks like a long shot that a few happened to clear, because the Denominator is the missing base rate: the winners are the numerator, the Drowned are the rest of the fraction, and a habit shared by every survivor can still be shared by nearly everyone who failed. Hiding the Denominator turns a lottery into a method, since a hundred who did the same and lost are not there to divide the one who did it and won.

## Apparent

A certainty.
It looks like proof of cause: they all did this, and they all won, so this wins. The missing Denominator is what makes it look airtight, because a ratio with its bottom hidden reads as a whole number, and a survivor's one-in-many looks like a one-to-one.

## Yearbook

Left blank by [the storyteller](persona_the_storyteller.md), restored by [the statistician](persona_the_statistician.md) who puts the failures back under the line.
It is the arithmetic of the absent: the Denominator is [the drowned](piece_the_drowned.md) counted, and without it [the lesson](piece_the_lesson.md) mistakes a rare escape for a repeatable rule.
