# Process: Prescription

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md). The finding turned to law.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A finding ready to become advice.
A pattern crisp enough to hand off.
A conclusion that wants an audience.

## Direction

The pattern turned into a teachable rule.
Toward the briefing, toward the next crews.
Toward practice, repeated and enforced.

## Lever

The authority of real evidence behind a backwards lesson.
Genuine data, pointed the wrong way.
The weight of proof, lent to the error.

## Echo

Armour drawn over the survivable holes, taught as law.
Reinforcement sent where it was never needed.
The lesson learned, precisely inverted.
