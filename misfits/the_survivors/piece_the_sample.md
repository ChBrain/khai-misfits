# Piece: The Sample

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md). The cases you actually see, and only those.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the hangar](place_the_hangar.md), the set of planes on the floor to be studied.
Everything that made it into the room: the bombers that returned, the funds still listed, the founders still interviewed, the products still shipping. The evidence you can lay hands on, all of it real.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to reason from and no study to do.
With it there is something to reason from that is quietly the wrong thing, because the Sample is not a fair draw from all the cases, it is exactly the cases that survived to be sampled. Every plane in the hangar is a plane that came back, so the Sample is complete, and truthful, and systematically missing the one group that would change the answer, which is the group that did not come back.

## Apparent

The evidence itself.
It looks like the data, the whole of what there is to go on, because it is the whole of what is present. Nothing about a full hangar announces the planes that are not in it, so the Sample reads as reality rather than as a slice of it cut by the outcome.

## Yearbook

Assembled by [the curator](persona_the_curator.md), read by [the reader](persona_the_reader.md), and mistaken by both for the population.
It is the ground the play stands on: the Sample is drawn by [the selection](piece_the_selection.md), so its pattern is the filter's shadow, and every honest reading of it points away from [the drowned](piece_the_drowned.md) who are not in it.
