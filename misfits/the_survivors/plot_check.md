# Plot: Check

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The briefing](place_the_briefing.md), the pattern written up and ready to teach.
The study is done and the finding is clear, so now it becomes advice: armour the holes, do what the winners did, and pass it to the crews who fly next.

## Action

[Prescription](process_prescription.md): [The Storyteller](persona_the_storyteller.md), holding [the advisor's seat](position_advisor.md), turns the survivors' pattern into a lesson and delivers it as settled fact.
Alongside, [The Mourner](persona_the_mourner.md) at [the witness's seat](position_witness.md) points at [the sea](place_the_sea.md) and asks where the lost were hit.

## Stage

[The Storyteller](persona_the_storyteller.md) tables [Sell the Lesson](plan_sell_the_lesson.md): teach the crews to reinforce where the survivors show damage, so [the lesson](piece_the_lesson.md) ships with [the denominator](piece_the_denominator.md) left blank and the recipe made to look certain.
[The Mourner](persona_the_mourner.md) tables [Count the Graveyard](plan_count_the_graveyard.md): ask after [the drowned](piece_the_drowned.md), the ones who did the same and sank, and is waved off as morbid, negative, no help to the crews who need an answer today.

## Tension

The lesson is confident and teachable, and it is pointed the wrong way.
The storyteller is sincere and the evidence is real, so the advice carries the full weight of good empirical work, and that weight is what makes it stick. And the mourner is right and unwelcome, because the one question that would overturn the lesson, who is not here and why, is slow, cheerless, and useless to a crew that wants steel bolted on by morning. So the doubt is filed, the recipe is taught, and the armour is drawn over the very holes that prove a plane can survive them.
