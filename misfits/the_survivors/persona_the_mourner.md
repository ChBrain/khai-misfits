# Persona: The Mourner

## Taxonomy

[Witness](position_witness.md). The one who points at the missing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Plain, sombre, quiet at the edge of the room.
He keeps thinking about the ones not here.
He counts the empty spaces on the line.

## Action

He points at the sea and asks where the lost were hit.
He raises the crews who taxied out and never came back.
He asks it once, and is waved off.

## Shadow

He is right and unwelcome.
The question that would fix it is slow and cheerless, so his doubt is filed.
The correct instinct arrives dressed as a mood nobody wants.

## Tell

"And the ones who did not come back?"
Nobody wants that at the briefing.
