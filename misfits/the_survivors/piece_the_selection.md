# Piece: The Selection

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md). The filter that lets the outcome choose the evidence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the sea](place_the_sea.md), at the line between what returns and what does not.
The sorting no one performs on purpose: the mission decides which planes come home, the market decides which funds stay listed, time decides which cases are still around to study. The outcome does the choosing, before any analyst arrives.

## Load Bearing

Without it the sample is a fair draw and its pattern means what it seems to mean.
With it the sample is drawn by the result under study, which is the whole disease, because the Selection conditions on survival: it admits a case only if it lived, so any trait shared by the survivors is confounded with surviving. Studying what the winners share is then studying the filter, not the cause, and the more cases the Selection removes the more confident and the more wrong the surviving pattern becomes.

## Apparent

No step at all.
It looks like nothing happened between the world and the data, because the removing was done off-stage by the outcome, not by any hand in the room. The Selection is invisible precisely because it acts before the study begins, so the analyst inherits a filtered sample as if it were the world.

## Yearbook

Worked silently by [attrition](process_attrition.md), inherited by [the curator](persona_the_curator.md) who takes the survivors for the field.
It is the engine of the misfit: the Selection makes [the sample](piece_the_sample.md) a slice cut by the outcome, so [the lesson](piece_the_lesson.md) read off it explains the filter and calls it the cause.
