# Persona: The Lost

## Taxonomy

[Casualty](position_casualty.md). The plane that went down and left nothing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Absent, silent, unphotographed.
A blank where a plane should be.
No scar to show, because there is no plane to show it.

## Action

It takes the fatal hit and never levels off.
It drops out of the sample before the sample is drawn.
It is never examined, never charted, never asked.

## Shadow

It holds the decisive evidence and cannot give it.
Its silence bends every conclusion the living reach.
The one fact that would fix the map is the one fact that sank.
