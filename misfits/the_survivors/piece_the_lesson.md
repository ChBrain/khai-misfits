# Piece: The Lesson

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md). The confident conclusion drawn from the survivors alone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the briefing](place_the_briefing.md), delivered to the next crews as settled fact.
The takeaway made portable: armour the bullet holes, do what the winners did, copy the founder's bold move, run the strategy that backtested clean. A rule, crisp and teachable, drawn straight off the ones who lived.

## Load Bearing

Without it the bias stays a curiosity and harms no one.
With it the bias becomes an instruction that spreads, because the Lesson turns a filtered pattern into advice and pushes it back into the world: it tells the living to reinforce where survivors were hit, which is where hits are survived, and to repeat what winners did, which most losers also did. The Lesson is where the error stops being a misreading and starts being a policy, plating the wrong parts and teaching the wrong habits with the full authority of evidence.

## Apparent

Hard-won wisdom.
It looks like exactly the kind of lesson worth trusting: specific, drawn from real cases, backed by a clear pattern in the data. That pedigree is what makes it dangerous, because it wears the marks of good empirical work while resting on a sample the outcome hand-picked.

## Yearbook

Taught by [the storyteller](persona_the_storyteller.md) under [sell the lesson](plan_sell_the_lesson.md), doubted only by [the mourner](persona_the_mourner.md).
It is where the misfit does its damage: built on [the sample](piece_the_sample.md) and blind to [the drowned](piece_the_drowned.md), the Lesson ships the survivors' recipe as law, until [the missing holes](piece_the_missing_holes.md) turn it over.
