# Piece: The Survivorship Bias

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md). The whole shape at once, and its costly cure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Over [the hangar](place_the_hangar.md) and [the sea](place_the_sea.md) together, the present studied and the absent ignored.
The phenomenon named: a lesson read off the cases that survived, sound on its sample and backwards on the world, because the outcome under study is the filter that assembled the evidence.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a heap of charts with no thesis.
With it the play has its law: the error needs no liar and no fool, only a sample cut by survival, a vivid pattern in it, and an honest analyst who reasons on what is present. It is also, and this is the part the shrug leaves out, curable: recover the missing, put the failures back under the line, reason from who is absent and why, and the inference turns over, so the Survivorship Bias carries its own remedy, which is not more data on the survivors but the deliberate hunt for the drowned that the survivors' presence makes so easy to skip.

## Apparent

A statistician's footnote.
It looks like a technicality, a caveat for careful people, easy to nod at and easy to commit, because naming the bias is simple and seeing it in your own full hangar is not. From inside it never looks like bias, only like learning from the best evidence available.

## Yearbook

Broken, when it is broken, by [the statistician](persona_the_statistician.md) through [recovery](process_recovery.md), at the price of the flattering lesson the survivors told.
It is the piece all the others resolve into: [the selection](piece_the_selection.md) cutting [the sample](piece_the_sample.md), [the holes](piece_the_holes.md) read without [the missing holes](piece_the_missing_holes.md), [the drowned](piece_the_drowned.md) left out of [the denominator](piece_the_denominator.md), make the Survivorship Bias, and only counting the absent unmakes it.
