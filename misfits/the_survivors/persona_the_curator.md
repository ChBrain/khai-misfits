# Persona: The Curator

## Taxonomy

[Selector](position_selector.md). The one who gathers the survivors as the field.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent, thorough, tireless in the hangar.
Proud of a full dataset, every row filled.
He counts what is in front of him and trusts the count.

## Action

He assembles the winners still around.
He walks the line and records each returned plane.
He treats them as the population, whole and complete.

## Shadow

Gathering only the survivors is invisible selection.
His thoroughness rigs the sample before anyone reads it.
The more completely he collects, the more confidently the field is wrong.

## Tell

"I collected every one I could find."
Every one he could find had survived to be found.
