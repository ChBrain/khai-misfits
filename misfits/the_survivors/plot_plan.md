# Plot: Plan

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The hangar](place_the_hangar.md) waiting, and a sortie coming home short.
The mission is flown, and the sorting is already done by the time anyone looks: some planes taxi in, some are on the seabed, and only the first kind will ever be examined.

## Action

[Return](process_return.md): [The Returner](persona_the_returner.md), holding [the survivor's seat](position_survivor.md), rolls into the hangar with its bullet holes on show, ready to be counted.
[Attrition](process_attrition.md): [The Lost](persona_the_lost.md) at [the casualty's seat](position_casualty.md) goes down over [the sea](place_the_sea.md) with nothing left to study.

## Stage

[The Returner](persona_the_returner.md) tables [Come Back](plan_come_back.md): make it home and be counted, so [the holes](piece_the_holes.md) it carries become part of the record.
[The Lost](persona_the_lost.md) tables [Fall Silent](plan_fall_silent.md): take the fatal hit and vanish from the sample, so [the drowned](piece_the_drowned.md) leave no plane, no chart, no word.

## Tension

Nobody chose the sample, and the sample is already rigged.
The survivor is honestly present and the casualty is honestly gone, and no hand sorted them, only the mission did. But that off-stage sorting is the whole trap: the hangar fills with planes that could take their hits and empties of the ones that could not, so before a single analyst arrives the evidence has been assembled by the outcome, and the holes about to be studied are only the holes a plane can wear and still come home.
