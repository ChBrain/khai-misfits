# Piece: The Drowned

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md). The uncounted failures, who did the same and left no trace.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the sea](place_the_sea.md), off every chart, out of every hangar.
The cases that did not come back: the bombers on the seabed, the funds delisted, the founders who followed the same recipe and are not interviewed, the worshippers who prayed and drowned all the same and hung up no votive tablet. Present once, absent now.

## Load Bearing

Without it the survivors look like the whole story and their pattern like the cause.
With it the story inverts, because the Drowned are the silent evidence the inference needs and cannot get: they are the ones who did what the winners did and lost anyway, so their number is the proof that the winners' recipe was neither necessary nor sufficient. They cannot testify, having drowned, and their silence is not neutral, it is the exact shape of the error, a missing counterweight that would have tipped every conclusion the other way.

## Apparent

Nothing, an empty sea.
It looks like there is nobody there, because that is what absence looks like: no plane, no fund, no name, no tablet. The Drowned are easy to leave out precisely because leaving them out costs nothing visible, and the study that omits them looks exactly as full as one that could not have.

## Yearbook

Pointed at by [the mourner](persona_the_mourner.md), and refused a hearing until [recovery](process_recovery.md) counts them in.
It is the missing half of the world: set against [the sample](piece_the_sample.md) that survived, the Drowned are [the denominator](piece_the_denominator.md) no one saw, and restoring them is the whole of the cure.
