# Persona: The Statistician

## Taxonomy

[Corrector](position_corrector.md). The reversal: the one who reasons from the absent.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Quiet, contrary, unbothered by the vivid.
He reasons about who is missing, not who is present.
He treats the blank spaces as data, too.

## Action

He puts the failures back under the line.
He reads the clean spots on the survivors as the fatal ones.
He armours the gaps, where no returning plane was ever struck.

## Shadow

The right answer costs the vivid story.
His correction spends the flattering lesson and makes him look like he is arguing with the evidence.
He wins the argument and loses the room.

## Tell

"Armour where there are no holes."
The planes that proved it are on the seabed.
