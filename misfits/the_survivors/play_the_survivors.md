# Play: The Survivors

## Estate

[khai-misfits](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-misfits): the house that holds this production and answers for the whole run.

## Name

The Survivors.
The bombers that made it home, wheeled into the hangar with their holes mapped and counted, studied for where a plane gets hit ... while the planes hit somewhere worse are on the seabed, uncounted, and the map of the survivors' holes is a map of the places a plane can be hit and live.

## Arc

The bend is this: they studied the ones that came back, and drew a lesson written by the ones that did not.
It runs on a sample and its shadow.
The sample is what returns: the survivors, the winners, the funds still listed, the founders still famous, every case that lived long enough to be examined, and it is real, and its pattern is real, and reading that pattern is sound arithmetic.
The shadow is what does not return: the lost, the drowned, the delisted, the founders who did the same and failed, none of them in the sample, because the very outcome under study is the filter that drew it.
So the analyst reads the survivors honestly and concludes exactly backwards, armouring the bullet holes because the holes are all he can see, when the holes are the marks of survivable hits and the fatal ones left no plane to study.
Recover the missing, reason from who is absent, and the true lesson turns over: but the survivors are in the hangar, holed and vivid and eager to teach, and the drowned say nothing at all.

## Triggers

**[Attrition](plot_attrition.md)**
The sortie flies, and some bombers come home and some do not. The Returner is wheeled into the hangar with its holes on show, and the Lost goes into the sea with nothing to show at all. Closes when the sample is all survivors and the fatal cases are gone from it.

**[Sample](plot_sample.md)**
Cue: the sample is assembled from the returned. The Curator gathers the winners, and the Reader reads the pattern their damage shares, honestly and soundly. Closes when a real pattern is read off a sample the outcome selected.

**[Lesson](plot_lesson.md)**
Cue: the pattern becomes advice. The Storyteller teaches the crews to armour the holes, and the Mourner points at the sea and asks where the lost were hit, and is waved off as morbid. Closes when the backwards lesson is taught and the doubt about the absent is filed.

**[Drawing Board](plot_drawing_board.md)**
Cue: someone reasons from who is not in the hangar. The Statistician recovers the missing, sees that the survivors' clean spots are where the lost were struck, and armours the gaps. Closes when the true lesson turns over at the cost of the flattering one ... or the survivors are too vivid to doubt, and the wrong armour ships.

## Stakes

Whether anyone reasons about the absent before the lesson is cast in steel.
Every plot moves the same way: toward the survivors, who are present, holed, and eager to teach, and away from the drowned, who are the whole of the evidence that would overturn them and can say nothing. The production cannot make the missing speak, because their silence is the very thing the outcome imposed. It can only show that no one lied, that the sample was real and the pattern was real and the arithmetic was clean, and that the one fact that would have saved the inference, who is not in the room and why, was the one fact the room was built to exclude.
