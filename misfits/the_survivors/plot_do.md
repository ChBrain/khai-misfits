# Plot: Do

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The winners' circle](place_the_winners_circle.md), the returned gathered, the study ready to begin.
The sample is in hand, all of it real, and the work now is to read what these cases share, which is careful, honest, and doomed by what is not in the room.

## Action

[Selection](process_selection.md): [The Curator](persona_the_curator.md), holding [the selector's seat](position_selector.md), assembles the sample from the survivors, the winners still around to be studied.
[Inference](process_inference.md): [The Reader](persona_the_reader.md) at [the analyst's seat](position_analyst.md) reads the pattern their damage shares at [the map room](place_the_map_room.md).

## Stage

[The Curator](persona_the_curator.md) tables [Study the Winners](plan_study_the_winners.md): gather the cases that lived and treat them as the field, so [the selection](piece_the_selection.md) is complete and invisible.
[The Reader](persona_the_reader.md) tables [Read the Pattern](plan_read_the_pattern.md): chart [the holes](piece_the_holes.md) across [the sample](piece_the_sample.md) and find, truly, where the survivors were struck, sound arithmetic on a rigged draw.

## Tension

Every step is correct, and the conclusion is already lost.
The curator gathers real cases and the reader reads them faithfully, and there is no error in the counting, the pattern in the survivors is exactly as reported. But the sample was cut by survival, so a trait shared by the living is tangled with living, and reading it cleanly only sharpens a confounded result. The better the analysis, the more certain the wrong answer, because rigour applied to a filtered sample refines the filter's shadow and calls it a finding.
