# Persona: The Reader

## Taxonomy

[Analyst](position_analyst.md). The one who reads the pattern honestly.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Rigorous, careful, unhurried.
He trusts the numbers and checks his work twice.
He wants nothing but what the data will tell him.

## Action

He charts the holes across every plane in the sample.
He finds what the survivors share, cleanly and soundly.
He shows the clusters and names them without flinching.

## Shadow

Rigour on a filtered sample sharpens a confounded answer.
His care makes the wrong result more certain, not less.
The tighter his method, the harder the filter is to see.

## Tell

"The pattern is unmistakable."
It is, and it is the filter's.
