# Persona: The Storyteller

## Taxonomy

[Advisor](position_advisor.md). The one who turns the pattern into a lesson.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fluent, persuasive, warm at the front of the room.
He loves a clean takeaway everyone can carry.
He makes the finding feel like common sense.

## Action

He teaches the crews to armour the holes.
He packages the recipe into a rule you can repeat.
He ships it as law, briefing after briefing.

## Shadow

He makes the backwards lesson portable and authoritative.
The error becomes policy the moment it becomes quotable.
Every crew now hardens exactly the wrong plate.

## Tell

"Do what the winners did."
Most of the losers did it too.
