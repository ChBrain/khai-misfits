# Process: Attrition

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md). The quiet removal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A plane that took a fatal hit.
Struck where the damage cannot be survived.
Falling before it can be logged.

## Direction

Down, into the sea, out of the sample.
Away from the floor, below the surface.
Toward the record no one keeps.

## Lever

The outcome removes the decisive cases before anyone looks.
Death does the sampling, silently.
The most informative planes are the first to go.

## Echo

A silence exactly shaped like the missing answer.
A gap that carries the whole finding.
The absence that no chart will ever show.
