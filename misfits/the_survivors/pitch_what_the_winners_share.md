# Pitch: What the Winners Share

## Taxonomy

[The Survivors](play_the_survivors.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Confident, data-driven, drawn from life.
Look at the ones who made it: see what they have in common, the habit they all kept, the move they all made, and there is your recipe.
The voice of hard-won lessons read off real cases, spoken by people who studied the winners closely and honestly and wrote down exactly what they found.

## Undertow

Under the evidence, a sample chosen by the outcome.
The winners share a pattern, and so did a thousand who did the same and drowned, and the drowned are not in the study, so the pattern is the mark of the filter, not the cause of the win.
No one fakes the data: they read the survivors faithfully, and reading only survivors faithfully is the whole error, because the sample was drawn by the very thing it claims to explain.

## Nerve

The feeling of learning from success,
the cases real, the pattern clear, the lesson earned from evidence,
never seeing that the evidence was assembled by the thing it was meant to explain.

## Echo

After the run: a confident recipe read off the living,
armour bolted over the bullet holes,
and a lesson taught by the survivors that the drowned, if they could speak, would flatly contradict.
