# Persona: The Returner

## Taxonomy

[Survivor](position_survivor.md). The plane that came home holed and got counted.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Lucky, vivid, wearing its damage plainly.
Glad to be studied, proud of every scar.
It flew when it had no business flying.

## Action

It taxis into the hangar under its own power.
It shows the crew exactly where it was hit.
It becomes the record everyone reads.

## Shadow

It is real evidence and the wrong evidence.
It is here only by having survived, so its holes teach where hits are survivable.
The map it draws is a map of forgiveness, not of danger.

## Tell

"Look where they got me, and I still flew."
Only the ones who could still fly are talking.
