# Process: Disturbing

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md). The Environment producing its full variety at the outcome, the indifferent throwing of disturbances that sets the measure the repertoire must meet.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A world with a range and no regard for the repertoire.
At [the field](place_the_field.md), the environment producing a disturbance from the full range it can produce and sending it to the outcome to be met or to pass.

## Direction

Toward the full variety thrown, indifferent to what can absorb it.
Because the world's variety is a property of the world, disturbing produces the whole range one disturbance at a time regardless of the responses on offer, so the measure the repertoire must meet stands unchanged by any trim, the field throwing after the cut exactly what it could throw before.

## Lever

The variety of the world, fixed outside the console.
Because the disturbance variety answers to the world and not to the controller, disturbing runs on no malice and no mercy, only on a range that keeps producing itself, so the disturbance that finds no matching response is not an attack but an ordinary member of the range, arriving because it is its turn.

## Echo

A disturbance produced, and the measure set.
The environment has thrown one more of its full range, so what reads as ordinary weather is, in truth, the standing measure the repertoire is required to match, the excess variety passing uncancelled the moment the responses fall below it.
