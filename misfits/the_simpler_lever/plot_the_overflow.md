# Plot: The Overflow

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Field](place_the_field.md), the world going on throwing the full variety it always could.
The Environment producing a disturbance from a range indifferent to how far the repertoire has been trimmed.

## Action

[Disturbing](process_disturbing.md): [The Environment](persona_the_environment.md), holding [the environment's seat](position_environment.md), throws its full range at the outcome, one disturbance after another without regard for the responses on offer.

## Stage

[The Environment](persona_the_environment.md), on [Throw Variety](plan_throw_variety.md), produces a [disturbance](piece_the_disturbance.md) for which the trimmed [repertoire](piece_the_repertoire.md) holds no matching state, the [variety](piece_the_variety.md) of the field now exceeding the variety of the responses.
The count has crossed: more distinct disturbances than distinct answers, [the shortfall](piece_the_shortfall.md) opened and the excess about to pass.

## Tension

The field throws no more than it always could, and the repertoire can no longer meet it.
There is no malice: the disturbance is an ordinary member of a range the world could always produce. But the responses have been cut below that range, so the plot closes with a disturbance arriving that has no matching state, the requisite variety unmet and the outcome held now only until this disturbance reaches it.
