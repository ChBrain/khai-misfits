# Piece: The Shortfall

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md). The gap when the repertoire falls below the disturbance variety, the disturbances that have no matching response and pass uncontrolled, the Law's inequality made flesh.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Opened at [the console](place_the_console.md), where the cut drops the count below the field.
Closed, if ever, at [the model](place_the_model.md), where the variety is matched back.

## Load Bearing

Without it a trimmed repertoire is just a smaller one, and no disturbance goes uncancelled.
With it the trap has its mechanism: the shortfall is the arithmetic difference when the count of responses falls below the count of disturbances, and by the requisite-variety inequality that difference is exactly the variety that passes through to the outcome uncontrolled, so regulation fails by a subtraction and not by a fault (Ashby, on the inequality that bounds the outcome's variety below by the disturbance variety less the regulator's, the excess that only a wider repertoire can absorb). The Shortfall is load bearing because it is the gap itself: not a broken part but a missing count, the disturbances with no state to meet them, so the piece carries the difference that a cut opens and a match closes, the exact measure of control given away.

## Apparent

Nothing at all, until it is everything.
Seen at the surface, the Shortfall shows as a blank, an absence with no reading on any gauge, because the disturbances it will fail to answer are not arriving while the gap is opening, and the truth that the control is already gone the moment the count crosses, that the gap is real from the cut and merely unexpressed, stays hidden behind how completely a missing response looks like nothing wrong until its disturbance comes.
