# Piece: The Disturbance

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md). The variety the environment throws, the range of distinct things the world can do to the outcome, the measure the repertoire must meet or fall short of.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Thrown from [the field](place_the_field.md), where the world produces its full range.
Counted at [the model](place_the_model.md), where its variety is laid against the repertoire's.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to regulate and no measure the repertoire must reach.
With it the trap has its measure: the disturbance variety is the count of distinct things the environment can do, fixed by the world and indifferent to the controller, so it is the floor the repertoire has to meet, and a repertoire below it must leave some disturbance uncancelled (Ashby, on the variety of disturbances that regulation must counter, the environment's variety as the demand on the regulator). The Disturbance is load bearing because it is the measure that does not move: the world throws its full range after the trim as before, so the piece carries the standing count against which every cut to the repertoire is silently scored, the disturbance with no matching response an ordinary member of a range that was always there.

## Apparent

Bad luck, an edge case, a freak event that happened to slip through.
Seen at the surface, the Disturbance that passes uncancelled looks like an unlucky one-off, a rare thing that arrived and was not caught, and the truth that it is an ordinary member of a range the world could always produce, that it passed because the repertoire was cut below the range and not because it was strange, stays hidden behind how much a gap in coverage looks, from the console, like a stroke of bad luck.
