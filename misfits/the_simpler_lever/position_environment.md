# Position: Environment

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md). The seat that throws the disturbances, where the variety the world can produce is a property of the world and not of the controller facing it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A range of disturbances and no regard for the repertoire: the full variety of things the world can do, indifferent to how much of it the regulator can answer.
A fixed measure: the disturbance variety sets the floor the repertoire must meet, whatever the controller wishes.
No malice and no mercy, and no way to be talked down to the size of the responses on offer.

## Orders

Throw variety.
Produce disturbances across the full range the world can produce, one after another, without regard for which the repertoire can still cancel.
Keep varying, because the world's variety answers to the world.

## Loses

Nothing of its own: the seat has no stake and no aim, it simply supplies the variety the other seats must meet.
Because it never agreed to throw only what the trimmed repertoire can answer, its full range stands after every cut, so the disturbance that finds no matching response is an ordinary member of a range the world could always produce, arriving now because it is its turn.
It gains no ground and yields none, and hands the regulator the one measure it must match or fall short of.

## Drives

Toward the full variety thrown,
indifferent to the repertoire that must absorb it,
so the seat that throws variety is the standing measure, and the outcome holds only while the responses meet the range the world keeps producing.
