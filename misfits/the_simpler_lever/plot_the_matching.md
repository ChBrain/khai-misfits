# Plot: The Matching

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Model](place_the_model.md), and someone unwilling to trust a lever too simple for its world.
The Engineer setting out to count the disturbances against the responses and close the gap before the outcome slips.

## Action

[Matching](process_matching.md): [The Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md), holding [the engineer's seat](position_engineer.md), amplifies the repertoire to answer each disturbance again, or attenuates the incoming variety so the field throws fewer.

## Stage

[The Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md), on [Match the Variety](plan_match_the_variety.md), raises the [variety](piece_the_variety.md) of [the repertoire](piece_the_repertoire.md) or lowers that of [the disturbance](piece_the_disturbance.md) until the pair meets the law, closing [the shortfall](piece_the_shortfall.md) against [the loss of control](piece_the_loss_of_control.md).
Against the match stand a lever that read as better-governed for being simple, and a cost that rebuilds the clutter the trimming removed.

## Tension

Matching the variety is the one move that holds the outcome, and it asks back the cost the simplification took away.
It rebuilds the responses that were cut or adds a whole apparatus upstream, and treats a legible lever as suspect for being small. So the plot closes one of two ways: the variety matched and the outcome held again ... or the remedy waived as needless complication, the requisite variety left unmet, and the shortfall kept for the world to find.
