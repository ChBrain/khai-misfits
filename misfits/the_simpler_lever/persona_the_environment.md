# Persona: The Environment

## Taxonomy

[Environment](position_environment.md). The source of disturbances, taken as a force and not a will: the world that goes on producing the full variety of things it can do, indifferent to how wide the regulator's repertoire has become.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Impersonal. Various. Unmalicious and unhelpful alike.
Is the field of disturbances, the weather, the load, the case mix, the demand, the fault modes, the world throwing at the outcome everything it is able to throw.
No intent in it: the Environment does not aim at the regulator's gaps, it simply keeps producing its own variety, and some of that variety happens to fall where no response remains.

## Action

Produces disturbances across the full range it always could, one after another, without regard for which of them the repertoire can still answer.
Throws the same variety after the trim as before it, because the world's variety is a property of the world and not of the controller watching it.
Does what an environment does: it varies, and its variety is the measure the regulator's repertoire must meet or fall short of.

## Shadow

Not a villain, a force with no malice and no mercy, and that is the whole difficulty: the Environment does not exploit the gap, it merely keeps varying until its variety crosses one, and the crossing is arithmetic, not attack.
Because the field never agreed to throw only what the trimmed repertoire can answer, its full variety stands whatever the regulator does, so the disturbance that has no matching response is not a special assault but an ordinary member of a range the world could always produce, arriving now only because it is its turn.
It bears the regulator no ill will, and bearing none is exactly why it cannot be relied on to spare the gap.

## Tell

Says, in effect, nothing: it does not speak, it varies.
Throws its full range as it always did, and cannot be talked down to the size of the repertoire, because its variety answers to the world and not to what the controller can currently absorb.
