# Pitch: The Simpler Lever

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Orderly, legible, well governed.
The plain good sense of trimming a controller down: a shorter menu, a coarser category, one rule for many cases, a lever that reads as better-governed the more it is cut, the outcome still held and the governance visibly tighter.
Nothing false in it on its face: each cut is a real economy, cheaper to run and easier to audit, and the outcome does stay held at the moment of every cut.

## Undertow

Under the tidiness, a count: a regulator holds an outcome no wider than its own variety, and only variety absorbs variety, so each simplification lowers the repertoire toward the disturbances the world can still throw.
Because a shortfall of responses is invisible exactly while the responses are not yet needed, the repertoire narrows past the disturbance variety with nothing breaking, and the first disturbance with no matching response passes through uncancelled, the outcome slipping though no component fails.
What makes the lever legible, that it is short enough to read and govern, is what leaves it too small to hold the world it faces.

## Nerve

The feeling of a console trimmed clean, the menu short and the governance tight,
sure the simpler lever is the better-governed one, because it reads so plainly and audits so easily,
while the count of responses has fallen below the count of disturbances, and the control is already gone, waiting only on the world to do the thing the lever no longer answers.

## Echo

After the run: an outcome that slips with no component broken, the regulator running exactly as designed and simply lacking the one state now needed,
the control lost silently at the cut and not at the slip, each simplification a real economy and the lever more legible at every step,
and the one move that would have held it, matching the variety by amplifying the repertoire or attenuating the disturbance, forgone because it costs back the clutter the trimming removed and reads, against a lever that looked well governed for being simple, as needless complication.
