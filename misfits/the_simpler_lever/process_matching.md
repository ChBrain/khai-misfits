# Process: Matching

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md). The Engineer restoring the requisite variety, the costly amplifying of the repertoire or attenuating of the disturbance that makes the pair meet the law and reads as needless complication.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A count of the disturbances against the responses and someone unwilling to trust a lever too simple for its world.
At [the model](place_the_model.md), the engineer laying the disturbance variety against the repertoire and closing the gap before the outcome slips.

## Direction

Toward the requisite variety restored and the outcome holdable again.
Because the law is met only when the responses reach the disturbances, matching either amplifies the repertoire so it answers each disturbance again or attenuates the incoming variety so the field throws fewer, so the pair together commands enough, the controller sized to the world and the outcome held once more.

## Lever

The willingness to pay back the cost the trimming removed.
Because amplifying rebuilds the clutter that was cut and attenuating adds a whole apparatus upstream, matching runs only on someone choosing to spend against a lever that looked better-governed for being simple, so the corrective is exacted continuously and thanked rarely, the averted slip leaving no trace and the complication it adds plain for all to feel.

## Echo

The variety matched, and the outcome held again.
The engineer has raised the responses or lowered the disturbances until the two meet, so what reads as needless complication is, in truth, the one act that restores the requisite variety, the outcome that now never slips leaving nothing to thank it for.
