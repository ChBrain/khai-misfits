# Place: The Field

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md). The disturbing environment, the world beyond the console that goes on producing the full variety of things it can do, indifferent to the repertoire that must absorb it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the disturbances come from, the weather, the load, the case mix, the demand, the fault modes, the world outside the controller that throws at the outcome everything it is able to throw.
The Environment producing its full range one disturbance at a time, and each disturbance travelling to the console to be met or to pass.

## Holds

The disturbance variety, and the measure it sets.
Here the world produces its full range regardless of the repertoire, so the Field holds the standing measure the responses must meet: a variety fixed by the world and not by the controller, so it is where the floor is set that the repertoire has to reach, and where the disturbance that finds no matching response waits, an ordinary member of the range, for its turn to arrive.

## Offers

The full variety of the world, laid against whatever repertoire faces it.
Because the Field is where the disturbances are produced, it offers the true measure of what the outcome must be held against, the whole range the world can throw, so it hands out the count that the repertoire is required to match, and hands it out unchanged by any trim, the field throwing after the cut exactly what it threw before.

## Withheld

Any promise to throw only what the repertoire can answer.
The Field produces its full range but cannot agree to spare the gap a trim has opened, because its variety answers to the world and not to the console, so it withholds the mercy of matching itself to the responses on offer, letting the trimmed repertoire face the untrimmed world, and, all measure and no regard, it withholds the disturbance that has no matching response until it is that disturbance's turn to come.
