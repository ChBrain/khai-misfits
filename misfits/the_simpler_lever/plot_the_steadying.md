## Tension

Holding the outcome is a real accomplishment, and it holds only so far as the repertoire covers the field.
There is nothing precarious on the surface: every disturbance is met and the variable is steady. But the control rests on a count, the responses reaching the disturbances, so the plot closes with the outcome held and the whole trap latent in the one fact that the holding lasts exactly as long as the repertoire stays as wide as the world.
