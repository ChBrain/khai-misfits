# Position: Regulator

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md). The seat that holds the outcome steady, where an outcome can be held no wider than the repertoire the seat is given.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A repertoire of responses and an outcome to hold: a set of distinct states and an essential variable to keep in bounds.
A real capacity: while the repertoire covers the field, the seat cancels each disturbance with its matching state and the control is genuine.
No power to answer beyond its variety, and no way to conjure a state the repertoire no longer holds.

## Orders

Hold steady.
Meet each disturbance with the response that cancels it, keep the essential variable within bounds, and run every state the repertoire keeps.
Match responses to disturbances, for as far as the repertoire reaches.

## Loses

The outcome it was made to hold, exactly when the repertoire is cut below the world.
Because the seat can only answer disturbances for which it still has a state, once the responses fall below the disturbances the seat meets one it cannot cancel, and it passes through uncontrolled, the outcome moving though the seat ran perfectly and every response it kept still works.
It gains a controller that runs as designed, and loses the outcome the moment the world does the thing no remaining state answers.

## Drives

Toward the disturbance cancelled and the outcome held,
for as far as the variety reaches and no further,
so the seat that holds steady holds only what its repertoire covers, and slips, silently, when the count of responses has fallen below the count of disturbances.
