# Piece: The Variety

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md). The count of distinct states, the measure in which the whole trap is denominated: how many different things a system can be, or do, or answer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Measured at [the console](place_the_console.md), where the repertoire's variety is run and cut.
Counted at [the model](place_the_model.md), where the field's variety is laid against the repertoire's.

## Load Bearing

Without it the trap has no unit, and cost and control cannot be weighed on the same scale.
With it the trap has its arithmetic: variety is the number of distinct states a thing can take, and regulation is a relation between two such counts, the disturbances a system faces and the responses it can make, so the whole law reduces to whether one count reaches another (Ashby, on variety as the measure of distinguishable states, the quantity that regulation is written in). The Variety is load bearing because it is the currency of the trap: not the size of any one thing but the count of distinct things, so the piece carries the measure by which a repertoire is either wide enough for its field or, cut, no longer is, the number that a simplification lowers and a disturbance sets.

## Apparent

A vague sense of complexity, of how complicated or how simple a thing is.
Seen at the surface, the Variety looks like a soft matter of taste, more complex or less, cleaner or busier, and the truth that it is an exact count with a hard threshold, that control turns on whether one variety reaches another and not on how tidy either looks, stays hidden behind how much complexity reads as a feeling rather than a number.

## Yearbook

Run and cut at [the console](place_the_console.md), counted at [the model](place_the_model.md), the unit the whole production is weighed in.
It is the currency of the trap: the Variety of [the repertoire](piece_the_repertoire.md) is set against the Variety of [the disturbance](piece_the_disturbance.md), and when the first falls below the second [the shortfall](piece_the_shortfall.md) opens and [the loss of control](piece_the_loss_of_control.md) follows, whatever [the legibility](piece_the_legibility.md) shows on the surface.
