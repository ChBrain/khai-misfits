# Position: Engineer

## Taxonomy

[The Simpler Lever](play_the_simpler_lever.md). The seat that would match variety before the outcome slips, where restoring the requisite variety costs back the very clutter the simplification removed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A count of the disturbances against the responses and the means to close the gap: the measure of the field and the tools to amplify or attenuate.
A real corrective: raising the repertoire's variety or lowering the disturbance's makes the pair meet the law and the outcome holdable again.
No cheap way to do it, and no welcome for the one who adds cost back to a lever that looked cleaner for being simple.

## Orders

Match the variety.
Amplify the repertoire so it answers each disturbance again, or attenuate the incoming variety with a filter so the field throws fewer, until the pair commands the requisite variety.
Size the controller to the world, treating the disturbance variety as the measure.

## Loses

The look of good governance, and the standing of one who keeps the lever simple.
Because amplifying rebuilds the responses that were cut and attenuating adds a whole apparatus upstream and screens off disturbances the field used to pass, the seat must spend that cost to hold the outcome, and the slip it prevents leaves no trace while the complication it adds is plain, so the averted disturbance looks, after, like one that was never going to come.
It gains the variety matched and the outcome held, and loses the legibility and the standing that keeping the lever simple would have kept.

## Drives

Toward the repertoire raised or the disturbance lowered until the two match,
at the standing cost of rebuilding the clutter the trimming removed,
so the seat that matches the variety holds the outcome again, and is read as adding needless complication to a lever everyone agreed was better for being simple.
