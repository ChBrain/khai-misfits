# Persona: The Tuner

## Taxonomy

[Tuner](position_tuner.md). Would raise the threshold and quiet the nuisance to restore the alarm's meaning, at the price of a danger sometimes unannounced.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Cool-headed. Unsentimental. Willing to say the hard thing about a life.
Reviews the flood of alarms and moves to retune, raising thresholds, prioritising, clustering, silencing the nuisance so the few real cries can stand out.
No callousness in it: only someone who sees that an alarm heard by no one saves no one, and would make it mean something again.

## Action

Raises the threshold and cuts the false alarms so the true ones carry weight, giving the channel its meaning back.
Prioritises the deadly over the trivial and quiets the shadows the low setting cried at.
Trades a flood no one heeds for a trickle that might be, knowing what the trade costs.

## Shadow

Not a saviour: every notch the threshold rises is a real event the alarm will now let pass, so the remedy buys attention with the risk of a miss.
Because the only way to make the alarm heard is to make it cry less, and crying less means catching less, the tuner cannot restore the signal without consenting, coldly, to sometimes not be warned, and the setter's instinct that no danger may be missed is exactly what will lower the threshold and flood the channel again.
It can make the alarm mean something, only by accepting the danger it will now occasionally miss.

## Tell

Says "an alarm no one answers is worse than one that sometimes stays silent."
Is right, and being right means signing, in cold blood, for the danger the higher threshold will let through.
