# Position: Responder

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The seat that must answer the alarm, and, met by a near-always-false signal, rationally learns to tune it out.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An alarm to answer, limited hands and attention, and a signal that is wrong far more often than right.
A sound reason to discount it: an alarm nearly always false is one that can nearly always wait.
No say over the threshold, only over how much of its attention each cry is given.

## Orders

Tune it out.
Silence, delay, and disable the alarm that cries wolf a hundred times, to stay able to act at all.
Read the base rate the threshold ignored, and treat each cry as near certainly nothing.

## Loses

The one true alarm, exactly by rightly discounting the false.
Because it cannot chase every false cry and remain any use, the seat must learn to ignore the channel, so it cannot both stay functional under the flood and stay alert to the rare true cry: the discounting that keeps it working is what has it silence the once it should not.
It doubts the alarm for the soundest of reasons and, for those reasons, misses the wolf.

## Drives

Toward an alarm rightly discounted,
sane against the flood and deaf to the one that is real,
so the reason it survives the false alarms is the reason it sleeps through the true.
