# Position: Tuner

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The seat that would raise the threshold to restore the alarm's meaning, against the setter's instinct and the cost of a danger let pass.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A lever over the threshold, the evidence of the flood, and the will to make the alarm mean something again.
A clear case: an alarm heard by no one saves no one, so the nuisance must be cut for the true cry to carry.
No way to cut the false without also cutting some true, only the choice of where to draw the line back.

## Orders

Tune the alarms.
Raise the threshold, prioritise the deadly, cluster and quiet the nuisance, so the few real cries stand out.
Trade a flood no one heeds for a trickle that might be, and own what the trade costs.

## Loses

The guarantee of catching every danger, exactly by making the alarm audible.
Because the only way to make the alarm heard is to make it cry less, and crying less means catching less, the seat cannot restore the signal without conceding some real event to the higher threshold: the meaning bought for the alarm is paid for in the danger it will now occasionally miss.
It ends the deafness only by consenting, in cold blood, to sometimes not be warned, and the setter's fear will lower the line and flood the channel again.

## Drives

Toward an alarm made audible,
its meaning bought back with a danger let pass,
so the one seat that would end the deafness must sign for the miss to do it.
