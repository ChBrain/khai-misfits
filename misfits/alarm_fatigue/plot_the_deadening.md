# Plot: The Deadening

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Din](place_the_din.md), an alarm wrong ninety-nine times in a hundred.
The responder stretched too thin to chase every shriek and reading the base rate the threshold ignored.

## Action

[Deadening](process_deadening.md): [The Responder](persona_the_responder.md), holding [the responder's seat](position_responder.md), discounts and silences the near-always-false alarm, while [The Alarm](persona_the_alarm.md), holding [the alarm's seat](position_alarm.md), keeps crying into ears that have stopped believing it.

## Stage

[The Responder](persona_the_responder.md), having tabled [Tune It Out](plan_tune_it_out.md), silences and disables and works through the flood, treating each cry as near certainly nothing so it can stay any use at all.
[The Alarm](persona_the_alarm.md), still on [Cry Out](plan_cry_out.md), sounds on faithfully, its every cry now landing on a responder taught by a hundred false ones to doubt them all.

## Tension

The responder's good sense is exactly what deafens it.
There is nothing to blame: an alarm wrong nearly every time is one a sane responder must learn to discount, and each silencing is coping, not carelessness. But discounting the channel to survive the flood is discounting it whole, so the plot closes with the response the alarm was meant to compel trained out of the room, the responder still at its work and no longer listening.
