# Place: The Review

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). Where the alarms would be retuned, the committee table the threshold is weighed at against the danger it lets pass.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The flood laid out in numbers, the count of alarms and the fraction false, and the thresholds up for reconsideration.
The tuner arguing to raise the line and quiet the nuisance, and the setter's fear in the room, the name of every danger a higher threshold would let through.

## Holds

The tuning and the resistance, the case for meaning restored and the cost of a miss conceded.
Here the move to make the alarm heard meets the fear that made it low, so the review holds the point where the remedy meets its price: raising the threshold plainly cuts the flood, and plainly concedes some real event to the higher line, so it is where the channel's meaning is bought against the danger the raise will let pass.

## Offers

The alarm's meaning back, at a stated price.
Because the review is where the threshold can be raised and the nuisance cut, it offers the tuner the one route to an audible alarm, so it hands out the remedy for the deafness, and hands it out with the bill attached, the real event the higher threshold will now occasionally miss.

## Withheld

A setting that catches everything and is still heard.
The review cannot offer a threshold that both misses no danger and cries seldom enough to be believed, so it withholds the free correction the setter would want, forcing the choice between coverage and credibility, and, the fear of the miss always present, it withholds any assurance the line will not simply be lowered again.
