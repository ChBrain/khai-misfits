# Piece: Alarm Fatigue

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The phenomenon whole, an alarm set to miss no danger that trains the responder to miss the true one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Everywhere the production runs: from [the panel](place_the_panel.md) through [the din](place_the_din.md) to [the floor](place_the_floor.md), and weighed at [the review](place_the_review.md).
It is the whole arc, the low threshold that floods the channel that deadens the responder that misses the wolf.

## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without a name: a threshold, a false cry, a numbness, a miss, and no account of how one becomes the next.
With it the misfit is whole: alarm fatigue is the trap entire, a setting made so no danger is missed becoming the reason the true danger is, the caution and the catastrophe shown to be one thing. It is load bearing because it is the structure the whole production exists to stage (Breznitz; the clinical literature): the frame that binds the low threshold, the flood, the rational deafness, and the missed cry into a single bend, so the piece carries the phenomenon as a whole, a vigilance that abolishes the very miss it was raised to prevent.

## Apparent

A discipline problem, a matter of responders who should simply pay more attention to their alarms.
Seen at the surface, alarm fatigue looks like carelessness to be trained away, a failure of individual vigilance, and the truth that it is the predictable sum of rational moves, a prudent setting and a benign world and a responder who correctly discounts a near-always-false cry, that no amount of exhortation removes the base rate driving it, stays hidden behind how much like a lapse of attention a missed alarm appears.

## Yearbook

The whole of it: Alarm Fatigue is [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) set low, raising [the false alarm](piece_the_false_alarm.md) in flood, breeding [the numbness](piece_the_numbness.md), delivering [the miss](piece_the_miss.md), and answered only by [the retune](piece_the_retune.md) that buys the alarm's meaning back with a danger let pass.
It is the misfit named: an alarm set to catch every danger that is heard as nothing, no villain in it and no free hero, the one true cry silenced with the false.
