# Place: The Panel

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). Where the threshold is set and the alarm fires, the console the cries are configured and raised at.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A board of alarms and their settings, thresholds dialled where the setter left them, every channel armed to cry on the merest sign.
The alarm lighting and sounding the instant a reading crosses its line, the harmless trip and the true one flashing the same on the same face.

## Holds

The setting and the firing, the low threshold and the cry that answers it.
Here the setter's caution becomes the alarm's obedience, so the panel holds the point where a prudent line drawn low becomes a cry raised often: the threshold is set to miss nothing, and the panel dutifully sounds on everything that crosses it, so it is where a careful setting turns into a constant cry.

## Offers

A warning for every trip, faithfully and without fail.
Because the panel fires the moment the threshold is crossed, it offers the setter exactly the vigilance asked for, an alarm that will miss no danger, so it hands out the assurance of total coverage, and with it the flood of false cries that total coverage of a benign world must raise.

## Withheld

Any mark of which cry is the one that matters.
The panel cannot tell the true trip from the false, so it withholds the distinction the responder most needs, lighting the wolf no differently from the shadow, and, sounding all its cries alike, it withholds the very signal within the signal that would make the real alarm worth answering.
