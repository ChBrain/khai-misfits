# Persona: The Setter

## Taxonomy

[Setter](position_setter.md). Sets the alarm low so no danger is missed, prudently, and by setting it low floods the channel.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Responsible. Haunted by the danger it must not miss.
Sets the threshold that decides when the alarm cries, and sets it low, because the danger is rare and deadly and a missed one is unforgivable.
No laxity in it: only someone guarding against a catastrophe by warning on anything that might be it.

## Action

Sets the alarm to trip on the merest sign, accepting false cries as the price of never missing a true one.
Weighs the cheap false alarm against the dreadful missed danger and errs, rightly for one alarm, toward the cry.
Arms the channel to warn on everything that could be the thing.

## Shadow

Not reckless, prudent: the low setting is right for a single deadly-if-missed event, and each false cry is cheap seen alone.
Because the world is mostly benign, a threshold low enough to catch the rare danger trips constantly on the harmless, so the caution that would miss no alarm fills the channel with false ones, and the flood is what deadens the responder it was meant to protect.
It sets the alarm so nothing is missed, and the setting is why the true one is.

## Tell

Says "set it low, because we cannot afford to miss even one."
Does, and afford is exactly the word, for the missing is bought with the very setting meant to prevent it.
