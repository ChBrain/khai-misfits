# Alarm Fatigue: Reference

The research warrant for [Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md).
The fable is Aesop's; the mechanism is Breznitz's psychology of false alarms and signal-detection theory,
carried through the clinical and industrial alarm-fatigue literature, all cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging alarm fatigue as a **signal system**: a monitored setting in which a rare, dangerous event is guarded
by an alarm set conservatively, so that against a mostly benign world the alarm fires overwhelmingly on the
harmless, and the flood of false positives desensitises the responder, who rationally discounts a near-always-
false signal, until the one true alarm arrives in the noise and is silenced with the rest. The play models the
documented mechanics, the low threshold that is right for a single deadly-if-missed event, the base rate that
turns a low threshold into a flood of false alarms, the responder's rational discounting of a signal wrong
ninety-nine times in a hundred, the silencing and disabling that follow, the missed true alarm that is the
false negative the low setting was meant to prevent, and the retune that alone restores the alarm's meaning, at
the cost of consenting to sometimes not be warned, as the load-bearing vectors of the structure, not as a tale
of a careless responder or a lazy nurse. The governing finding of the source literature is the play's governing
law: the detectability of a rare signal is set by the threshold, and a threshold low enough to miss almost no
true event will, against a low base rate, raise mostly false alarms, so the sensitivity that catches the danger
is the sensitivity that floods the channel and trains the responder to ignore it. It is distinct from the
house's The Streetlight Effect, where the search goes where the light is rather than where the answer is: there
the signal is looked for in the wrong place, here it sounds in the right place and is tuned out. And it is
distinct from the house's Watermelon Reporting, where bad news is dressed green to protect the reporter: there
the true signal is deliberately hidden, here it is broadcast plainly and drowned by the false ones beside it.

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast the responder as
  negligent or the setter as reckless, because the source finding is precisely that each move is rational:
  setting an alarm low to catch a rare deadly event is prudent, and discounting an alarm that is wrong nearly
  every time is the responder reading the same base rate the threshold ignored. The missed danger is authored by
  no carelessness and emerges from the structure, a cautious threshold and a benign world and a responder who
  learns, so the misfit is that an alarm set so no danger would be missed is what trains the one person who could
  act to miss it.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into a costless correction. The one move that restores the
  alarm's meaning is to raise the threshold and prioritise and quiet the nuisance, and every notch the threshold
  rises is a real event the alarm will now let pass, so cutting the false alarms buys back the responder's
  attention with the risk of the very miss the low setting was meant to prevent. The reckoning is a channel made
  audible again by consenting, coldly, to sometimes not be warned, not a free correction, so the cure for a
  deafening alarm is the deliberate acceptance of a danger occasionally unannounced.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here, never claimed
  as the house's. The staging is the house's; the cry-wolf fable, the psychology of false alarms, signal-
  detection theory, and the clinical and industrial alarm-management literature are theirs, and the condition
  the trap actually requires, a rare true signal and a low base rate so that a sensitive threshold fires mostly
  false, is carried as carefully as the finding itself.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **[The Threshold](piece_the_threshold.md)**: carries the detection criterion (Green and Swets), the setting
  that trades misses against false alarms and, set low against a low base rate, fires mostly false.
- **[The False Alarm](piece_the_false_alarm.md)**: carries the nuisance positive (Joint Commission; Cvach), the
  benign trip that, common because the world is benign, floods the channel.
- **[The Numbness](piece_the_numbness.md)**: carries the desensitisation (Breznitz), the responder's rational
  discounting of a signal that is near certainly nothing.
- **[The Miss](piece_the_miss.md)**: carries the false negative (Aesop; the Joint Commission toll), the one true
  alarm silenced with the false, the danger the low setting was meant to prevent.
- **[The Retune](piece_the_retune.md)**: carries the remedy (Cvach; Sendelbach; EEMUA 191), the raised threshold
  and prioritisation that restore the alarm's meaning, at the cost of a danger sometimes unannounced.
- **[Alarm Fatigue](piece_alarm_fatigue.md)**: carries the phenomenon whole (Breznitz; the clinical literature),
  an alarm set to miss no danger that trains the responder to miss the true one.
