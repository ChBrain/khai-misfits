# Position: Setter

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The seat that sets the threshold low so no danger is missed, right for one alarm and flooding for all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A threshold to set, a rare and deadly danger to guard against, and the knowledge that a missed one is unforgivable.
A sound reason to set it low: the false cry is cheap and the missed danger is catastrophe.
No control over the base rate, only over where the line is drawn.

## Orders

Set it low.
Warn on anything that might be the thing, and accept the false alarms as the price of never missing a true one.
Guard the deadly rare event the one way a single alarm can, by erring toward the cry.

## Loses

The responder's attention, exactly by setting the threshold low.
Because the world is mostly benign, a line drawn low enough to catch the rare danger trips on the common harmless, so the seat cannot both miss no alarm and keep the channel meaningful: the caution that catches everything floods the channel and deadens the one who must answer.
It sets the alarm so nothing is missed and, through the flood, ensures the true one will be.

## Drives

Toward a threshold set low,
right for one deadly alarm and deafening for all,
so the caution that would miss no danger is what loses the one that counts.
