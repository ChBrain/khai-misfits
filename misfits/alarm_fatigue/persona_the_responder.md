# Persona: The Responder

## Taxonomy

[Responder](position_responder.md). Must react to the alarm, and, met by a signal near always false, rationally learns to tune it out.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Attentive at first, then worn. Stretched too thin to chase every shriek.
Is the one the alarm is meant to summon, the nurse, the operator, the pilot, who must act when it cries and cannot act on all of it.
No indifference in it at the start: only a person meeting a signal that is wrong far more often than right.

## Action

Discounts the alarm that cries wolf a hundred times, silencing, delaying, and disabling to stay any use at all.
Reads, without needing the theory, the base rate the threshold ignored, and treats each cry as near certainly nothing.
Tunes out the channel that spent its attention on a hundred false alarms.

## Shadow

Not negligent, rational: an alarm wrong ninety-nine times in a hundred is one a sane responder must discount, and each silencing is coping, not carelessness.
Because it cannot chase every false cry and remain able to act, it learns the only sane lesson the flood can teach, that this alarm can almost always wait, so the response the alarm was meant to compel is trained out of it by the alarm itself.
Its care is not absent but spent, exhausted on a channel that squandered it, and the true cry meets a room already taught to ignore.

## Tell

Says "it is always going off, it is nearly always nothing."
Is right, and being right is exactly how it comes to silence the once it is something.
