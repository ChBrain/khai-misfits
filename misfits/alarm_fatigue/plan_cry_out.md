# Plan: Cry Out

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the alarm's seat](position_alarm.md), inside [Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Alarm](position_alarm.md)

## Direction

Cry the instant the threshold trips. Sound at the loose lead, the passing shadow, and the real emergency alike, faithfully and without judgement, because the one duty is to warn when the line is crossed, whatever the reading turns out to mean.

## Orders

([The Alarm](persona_the_alarm.md) sounds on every trip of the low threshold, crying a hundred times for the harmless and once for the wolf, in the identical voice.)

## Implementation

([The Din](place_the_din.md); the [flooding](process_flooding.md) process; each faithful cry adds another [false alarm](piece_the_false_alarm.md) to the channel, and the true cry, when it comes, is voiced the same as the false.)

## Targets

- [ ] the alarm sounds the moment the threshold trips
- [ ] the harmless and the dangerous are cried in the identical voice
- [ ] the faithful sounding fills the channel with false alarms
- [ ] the true cry, when it comes, cannot be told from the false ones beside it
