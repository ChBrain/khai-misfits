# Piece: The Numbness

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The responder's acquired discounting, the rational deafness the flood of false alarms teaches.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Set in at [the din](place_the_din.md), where the flood teaches the responder to doubt every cry.
Carried onto [the floor](place_the_floor.md), where it is waiting when the true alarm finally sounds.

## Load Bearing

Without it the flood is only noise, tiresome but harmless, and the true cry still lands on a listening ear.
With it the flood becomes a trained silence: the responder learns that a cry means almost certainly nothing and acts on the lesson, so the alarm loses not just its volume but its claim. The Numbness is load bearing because it is the step from a flooded channel to a missed danger (Breznitz): the false alarms do no harm until they have taught the responder to discount the channel, so the piece carries the acquired, rational deafness that turns a wall of noise into an unanswered wolf.

## Apparent

Experience, a seasoned hand no longer rattled by every beep.
Seen from outside, the numbness looks like composure, the calm of someone who knows the alarm is usually nothing, and the truth that this calm is a discounting that will meet the true cry exactly as it meets the false, that the unflappable ear is the deaf one, stays hidden behind how much like seasoned judgement a learned indifference appears.

## Yearbook

Grown in [the responder](persona_the_responder.md) through [deadening](process_deadening.md), the rational discount of a near-always-false alarm.
It is the hinge of the harm: the Numbness is what [the false alarm](piece_the_false_alarm.md) in its numbers breeds, what turns the true cry into [the miss](piece_the_miss.md), and what only [the retune](piece_the_retune.md), by making the alarm worth believing again, can lift.
