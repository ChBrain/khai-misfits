# Process: Warning

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The setting of a low threshold and the crying that follows, prudent for one alarm and, against a benign world, the source of the flood.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A rare and deadly danger, and the resolve to miss none of it.
At [the panel](place_the_panel.md), the setter drawing the threshold low and the alarm armed to trip on the merest sign.

## Direction

Toward an alarm that cries on anything that might be the danger.
The setter draws the line low to catch the rare event, and because the line is low it is crossed by the common harmless, so warning drives the alarm to sound not only for the wolf but for every shadow that resembles one, the coverage total and the cries mostly false.

## Lever

The soundness of erring toward the cry.
Because a false alarm is cheap and a missed danger is catastrophe, drawing the threshold low is right for any single alarm judged alone, so warning runs on impeccable caution, the flood assembled entirely from settings no one can fault.

## Echo

An alarm that will miss no danger, crying constantly.
The setter has the coverage it wanted and the channel has its first false cries, and the low threshold that guarantees no miss guarantees a flood, so what reads as vigilance perfected is, unseen, the start of the deluge that will teach the responder to ignore it.
