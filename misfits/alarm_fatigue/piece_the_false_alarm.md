# Piece: The False Alarm

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The nuisance positive, the benign trip that, common because the world is benign, floods the channel.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Raised at [the panel](place_the_panel.md), one more faithful cry on a harmless trip.
Massed at [the din](place_the_din.md), where the count of them turns the channel to noise.

## Load Bearing

Without it a low threshold is harmless, the alarm eager but seldom wrong, and the responder keeps its faith.
With it the low setting becomes a deluge: because the benign is common, the cheap false cry is not rare but constant, and its numbers are what drown the true. The False Alarm is load bearing because it is the unit the flood is built from (the Joint Commission; Cvach): one is trivial, a hundred a shift is a wall of noise, so the piece carries the benign cry whose individual cheapness and collective mass are the whole distance between a prudent setting and a deaf channel.

## Apparent

A small annoyance, a beep for nothing, cleared with a glance and forgotten.
Seen one at a time, the false alarm looks like a trivial cost, the cheap price of safety, and the truth that its sheer frequency is what teaches the responder to discount the channel, that a thousand trivial cries sum to a fatal deafness, stays hidden behind how minor a single beep for nothing appears.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the alarm](persona_the_alarm.md) through [flooding](process_flooding.md), the benign cry the low [threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) trips on.
It is the substance of the flood: the False Alarm in its numbers is what breeds [the numbness](piece_the_numbness.md), what buries the true cry, and what [the retune](piece_the_retune.md) exists to cut, though cutting it also cuts some of the true.
