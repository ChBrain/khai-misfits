# Process: Deadening

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The responder's rational discounting of a near-always-false alarm, sane against the flood and deaf to the one true cry.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A flood of false alarms met by a responder with finite attention.
At [the din](place_the_din.md) and out on [the floor](place_the_floor.md), the responder unable to chase every cry and reading the base rate the threshold ignored.

## Direction

Toward a channel discounted, silenced, and tuned out.
The responder meets an alarm wrong nearly every time and draws the only sane conclusion, that this cry is near certainly nothing, so deadening drives it to silence and disable and ignore, until the response the alarm was meant to compel is trained out and the true cry, when it lands, meets a room already taught to doubt.

## Lever

The rationality of discounting a signal that is almost always false.
Because an alarm wrong ninety-nine times in a hundred is one a sane responder must learn to wait on, each silencing is coping and not carelessness, so deadening runs on the responder's good sense, the deafness built entirely from reasonable reads of a flood that squandered its attention.

## Echo

A responder that survives the flood and sleeps through the wolf.
The responder has kept its feet under the deluge by learning to ignore it, and that same learning is waiting when the true cry comes, so what reads as an experienced hand unruffled by alarms is, unseen, the trained silence that will meet the one alarm that mattered.
