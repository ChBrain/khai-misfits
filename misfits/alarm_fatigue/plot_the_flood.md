# Plot: The Flood

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Din](place_the_din.md), the world mostly benign and the threshold low.
The alarm sounding on trip after trip, the harmless conditions common and each raising its faithful cry.

## Action

[Flooding](process_flooding.md): [The Alarm](persona_the_alarm.md), holding [the alarm's seat](position_alarm.md), cries on every trip of the low threshold, while [The Setter](persona_the_setter.md), holding [the setter's seat](position_setter.md), sees its total coverage vindicated in the ceaseless sounding.

## Stage

[The Alarm](persona_the_alarm.md), still on [Cry Out](plan_cry_out.md), sounds at the loose lead and the passing shadow, each cry faithful and the mass of them overwhelmingly false.
[The Setter](persona_the_setter.md), still on [Set It Low](plan_set_it_low.md), reads the constant crying as proof the danger cannot slip past, and holds the threshold where it is.

## Tension

Every cry is the alarm working, and the working is what empties it of meaning.
There is no malfunction: the alarm rightly sounds on every trip, and the trips are mostly harmless only because the world is. But the faithful cries come so thick and so nearly always false that no one of them can carry weight, so the plot closes with the channel saturated, the alarm's meaning draining cry by cry, and the true one, when it comes, already just another voice in the wall.
