# Position: Wolf

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The seat of the rare true event, arriving in the flood and sounding exactly like the false.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

One honest cry, and the terrible timing of arriving after a hundred false ones.
A real claim on the responder, and no way to press it but the same alarm that has cried for nothing all day.
No voice of its own, only the shared voice of the alarm, worn out before it speaks.

## Orders

Slip through.
Arrive rarely and truly, trip the alarm honestly, and sound in the one channel left to you.
Be the danger the whole system was set to catch, at the moment it is least able to hear.

## Loses

Its answer, exactly because it cannot be told from the false.
Because the alarm gives it no different voice and the responder has learned to doubt that voice, the seat cannot make its one true cry heard above the hundred that were not: the very rarity that makes it matter is what buries it in the constant false, and the honesty of its trigger counts for nothing when honesty is what the false alarms also had.
It is real, and sounds real, and is silenced for having sounded too often for nothing.

## Drives

Toward a true cry lost in the false,
real and plain and unanswered,
so the danger the alarm was built to catch is missed for arriving in the flood the alarm made.
