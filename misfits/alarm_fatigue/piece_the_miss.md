# Piece: The Miss

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The false negative, the one true alarm silenced with the false, the danger the low setting was meant to prevent.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Falls on [the floor](place_the_floor.md), where the real event sounds and meets the trained silence.
The shadow of [the review](place_the_review.md), the outcome each raised threshold is weighed against.

## Load Bearing

Without it the trap is only irritating, a channel of noise with no casualty, alarm fatigue as mere annoyance.
With it the misfit has its wound: the whole low setting was justified by the horror of a missed danger, and the flood it raised is precisely what causes one, so the trap closes on the exact outcome it was built to prevent. The Miss is load bearing because it is the cost that makes the structure a tragedy and not a nuisance (Aesop; the Joint Commission toll): it is the true cry unanswered, so the piece carries the false negative that the caution was meant to abolish and instead assures.

## Apparent

A freak lapse, a one-off failure to respond, the sort of thing a reprimand or a reminder will fix.
Seen after the fact, the miss looks like a single person's slip, a momentary inattention to blame and correct, and the truth that it was the predictable end of a rational chain, that the low setting and the flood and the deafness made it not an accident but an outcome, stays hidden behind how much like individual error a missed alarm appears.

## Yearbook

Suffered when [the wolf](persona_the_wolf.md) meets [deadening](process_deadening.md), the true cry lost in the flood of false.
It is the wound of the trap: the Miss is what [the numbness](piece_the_numbness.md) delivers, the very danger [the threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) was set low to prevent, and the outcome [the retune](piece_the_retune.md) trades against, since raising the line to be heard concedes a miss of its own.
