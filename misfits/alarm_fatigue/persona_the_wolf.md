# Persona: The Wolf

## Taxonomy

[Wolf](position_wolf.md). The rare true event, arriving in the flood of false alarms and sounding exactly like them.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Real. Seldom. Indistinguishable, at the moment it comes, from the shadows that came before it.
Is the danger the whole alarm was set to catch, the true reading at last, the emergency that is not a drill.
No disguise on it: it triggers the alarm honestly, and the alarm cries for it in the very voice it cried for nothing.

## Action

Comes rarely and truly, and trips the alarm exactly as the false alarms tripped it.
Arrives in a channel already flooded, its one honest cry stacked among a hundred that were not.
Sounds the same as all the rest, and so is answered the same as all the rest, which by now is not at all.

## Shadow

Not hidden, drowned: nothing conceals the wolf, it sets off the alarm as plainly as anything, and that is the trap.
Because it is rare and the false alarms are constant, the true cry is a needle in the noise, and because the alarm gives it no different voice and the responder has learned to doubt that voice, the real event is silenced not by any failure to sound but by having sounded too often for nothing.
It is the danger the low setting was meant to catch, missed because the low setting caught everything else first.

## Tell

Cries once, truly, in the same tone as the hundred false alarms before it.
Is silenced with them, the one that was real lost precisely because it could not be told from the ones that were not.
