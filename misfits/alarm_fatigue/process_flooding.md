# Process: Flooding

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The piling of false cries into a saturated channel, each cry faithful and the mass of them meaningless.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A low threshold and a world that is mostly benign.
At [the din](place_the_din.md), the alarm sounding on trip after trip, the harmless conditions common and each raising its faithful cry.

## Direction

Toward a channel so full no single cry can carry weight.
Each faithful sounding adds another false alarm, and because the benign is constant the cries come thick and are overwhelmingly false, so flooding drives the channel from a warning that means something to a noise that means nothing, the true cry, when it comes, one more voice in the wall.

## Lever

The faithfulness of the alarm and the base rate of the world.
Because the alarm rightly cries on every trip and the trips are mostly harmless, the flood needs no malfunction and no fault, only an obedient device and a benign world, so flooding runs on reliability itself, the deluge made of the alarm doing exactly its duty.

## Echo

A channel saturated, every cry worth the same nothing.
The alarm has sounded its hundred faithful cries and the channel is full, and no one of them can be told or weighed apart from the rest, so what reads as thorough monitoring is, unseen, the emptying of the alarm's meaning, the true cry now indistinguishable from the false.
