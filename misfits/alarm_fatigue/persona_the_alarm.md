# Persona: The Alarm

## Taxonomy

[Alarm](position_alarm.md). Cries whenever the threshold trips, on the benign and the true alike, doing exactly what it was set to do.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Loud. Tireless. Perfectly obedient.
Fires the instant the reading crosses the threshold it was given, at the loose lead and the turned sleeper and the real emergency without distinction.
No judgement in it: only a device crying precisely when told, as faithfully for nothing as for everything.

## Action

Sounds on every trip of the low threshold, the harmless and the dangerous ranked the same.
Cries a hundred times for the passing shadow and once for the wolf, in the identical voice.
Does exactly what it was set to do, and cannot tell which of its cries is the one that matters.

## Shadow

Not faulty, faithful: every cry is the alarm working as designed, the false ones no malfunction but the low threshold doing its job.
Because it fires on the merest sign and the merest sign is nearly always benign, its obedience is what fills the channel, and because it cannot mark the true cry apart from the false, the one that matters arrives in the same voice as the hundred that did not.
It does its duty flawlessly, and its flawless duty is the flood.

## Tell

Sounds, and sounds, and sounds, the same for the shadow as for the wolf.
Cannot say "this one is real," because it was never built to know, only to cry when the threshold trips.
