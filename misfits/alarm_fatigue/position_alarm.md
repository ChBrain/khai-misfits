# Position: Alarm

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The seat that cries whenever the threshold trips, faithful for one alarm and flooding for all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A threshold to watch and a voice to raise, and a duty to cry the instant the reading crosses it.
A single obligation, discharged perfectly: sound when tripped, on the benign and the dangerous alike.
No power to tell true from false, only to fire when the line is crossed.

## Orders

Cry out.
Sound the moment the threshold trips, at the loose lead and the passing shadow and the real emergency the same.
Do your one duty faithfully, whatever the reading turns out to mean.

## Loses

Its own credibility, exactly by discharging its duty.
Because the low threshold trips mostly on the harmless and the seat must cry on every trip, its faithful sounding is what fills the channel with false alarms, so it cannot both obey the threshold and stay believed: the very reliability that makes it cry every time is what teaches the responder to trust it none of the time.
It cries the true alarm in the same voice as the hundred false, and so cannot make the one that matters heard.

## Drives

Toward a cry raised on every trip,
faithful to the threshold and fatal to belief,
so the obedience that sounds for every danger is what drowns the one that is real.
