# Piece: The Retune

## Taxonomy

[Alarm Fatigue](play_alarm_fatigue.md). The remedy, the raised threshold and prioritisation that restore the alarm's meaning, at the price of a danger sometimes unannounced.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Decided at [the review](place_the_review.md), where the threshold is raised and the nuisance cut.
Aimed at [the din](place_the_din.md), the flood it exists to thin so a true cry can stand out.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play has no answer, and the flood runs to the miss with nothing to arrest it.
With it the misfit has its costly cure: raising the threshold and quieting the nuisance is the one thing that can make the alarm mean something again, and it works only by making the alarm cry less, which means catch less. The Retune is load bearing because it is the remedy that carries its own price (Cvach; Sendelbach; EEMUA 191): the same move that restores the signal concedes a share of the true events, so the piece carries both the cure for the deafness and the miss the cure must buy, a correction that is never free.

## Apparent

An obvious fix, just turn down the nuisance alarms and be done, common sense anyone would endorse.
Seen hopefully, the retune looks like a clean win, less noise and a heard alarm at no real cost, and the truth that every notch the threshold rises is a real event the alarm will now let pass, that quieting the false necessarily risks the true, stays hidden behind how much like a free improvement cutting the nuisance appears.

## Yearbook

Made by [the tuner](persona_the_tuner.md) through [tuning](process_tuning.md), the raised [threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) that thins the flood.
It is the cure the play cannot make painless: the Retune cuts [the false alarm](piece_the_false_alarm.md) to lift [the numbness](piece_the_numbness.md), and cannot do so without conceding a [miss](piece_the_miss.md) of its own, so the remedy for the deafness is bought by consenting to sometimes not be warned.
