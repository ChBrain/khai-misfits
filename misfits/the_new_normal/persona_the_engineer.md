# Persona: The Engineer

## Taxonomy

[Engineer](position_engineer.md). The one who sees the signal against the spec and asks to stop.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful, literal, uneasy about the trend.
Knows what the design limit is and can see the data has crossed it.
Wants to measure the anomaly against the spec, and says so.

## Action

Flags the signal and recommends holding until it is understood.
Points at the line the design drew and the point that sits past it.
Puts the concern on the record, against a room that wants to proceed.

## Shadow

The concern is real and the data keeps surviving, which makes it easy to overrule.
The Engineer can name the deviation and cannot prove the disaster, because the disaster has not happened yet, so caution reads as obstruction against a run of successes.

## Tell

"That's past the limit. We shouldn't fly until we know why."
The right question, asked into a room holding evidence that it flew.
