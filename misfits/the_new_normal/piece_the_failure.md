# Piece: The Failure

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The latent catastrophe released: the day the normalized deviation does what it always could.

## Owner

## Place

At [the pad](place_the_pad.md), where the system finally does the thing the spec drew the line to prevent.
The break, the burn-through, the loss: not a new kind of event but the same deviation, one increment past the last survival, meeting the day the margin was gone.

## Load Bearing

Without it the drift is invisible and the normalization looks like sound judgment forever.
With it the accumulated shift is made undeniable and paid for at once: the Failure is the moment the reference that moved so quietly is revealed to have moved toward a cliff. It is load bearing because it is the harm the play exists to show, the cost that was building through every success and came due in full.

## Apparent

A shocking, unforeseeable accident. A freak bad part.
It looks like a bolt from the blue, or one component that failed out of nowhere, so the instinct is to find the broken piece and replace it. That reading is the last defence of the baseline, because blaming a part protects the line that drifted, and lets the normalization survive its own catastrophe.
