# Plot: The Failure

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The pad](place_the_pad.md), then [the hearing](place_the_hearing.md).
The normalized deviation, one increment past the last survival, meets the day the margin is gone, and the system does the thing the spec drew the line to prevent.

## Action

[Reckoning](process_reckoning.md): [The Investigator](persona_the_investigator.md), holding [the investigator's seat](position_investigator.md), reads the record backward and reconstructs the drift.

## Stage

[The Investigator](persona_the_investigator.md) works [Reconstruct the Drift](plan_reconstruct_the_drift.md): lay [the failure](piece_the_failure.md) against the waivers that led to it and show it names [the normalization](piece_the_normalization.md), a chain of locally sound calls whose sum was fatal.
No one broke a rule; the rule moved.

## Tension

The truest finding is the hardest to act on.
The investigator can prove there was no villain, only a line that crept, and that is exactly what makes it unsatisfying: a system that failed with everyone doing their job offers no one to fire and no single part to swap. So the reckoning turns on a choice, name the drift and confront the process ... or blame one bad component, and let the baseline hold where it crept, primed to do this again.
