# Position: Independent Reviewer

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The reversal seat: outside the drift, it measures against the spec and resets the line.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

Independence from the successes, and the authority to halt.
The design limit as a live reference, uncarried by the room's history.

## Orders

Measure the anomaly against the spec, not the last success.
Refuse survival as proof of safety, and reset the baseline to the design limit.
Halt the schedule long enough to make the line real again.

## Drives

Toward the spec restored as the reference, and away from the baseline that only ever crept outward.
Toward the drift stopped and reversed, at the cost of the schedule the drifting line was buying.
