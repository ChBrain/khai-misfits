# Piece: The Signal

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The anomaly against the design limit: the warning that crosses the line and asks to be read.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the trend](place_the_trend.md), a point past the line the design drew, plotted among the ones that came before it.
The erosion deeper than the model allowed, the reading out of spec, the thing that was never supposed to appear and has: real data, honestly recorded, pointing at a limit.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to normalize and the standard cannot drift.
With it there is a warning that means one thing against the spec and another against the last success, and the whole misfit turns on which reference is used. The Signal is load bearing because it is the input the system must classify, and the classification, not the signal, is where the drift begins.

## Apparent

An off-nominal data point.
It looks like noise at the edge of tolerance, a one-off to be explained, the kind of thing every complex system produces and mostly survives. That reading is the opening, because a signal treated as noise is a signal measured against experience instead of the line.

## Yearbook

Raised by [the engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) through [flagging](process_flagging.md), against [the spec](piece_the_spec.md).
It is where the play begins: the Signal is what [the reclassification](piece_the_reclassification.md) rebooks as acceptable, and the first of the points whose sum is [the normalization](piece_the_normalization.md).
