# Plot: The Reset

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The redline](place_the_redline.md), and someone who has stopped asking whether it flew and started asking what the spec says.
The question has changed: not how many times has this survived, but where is the line the design drew, and how far has the anomaly crossed it.

## Action

[Resetting](process_resetting.md): [The Outsider](persona_the_outsider.md), holding [the independent reviewer's seat](position_independent_reviewer.md), measures the signal against the spec, refuses survival as proof, and halts to reset the line.

## Stage

[The Outsider](persona_the_outsider.md) tables [Reset the Baseline](plan_reset_the_baseline.md): drag the reference back to [the spec](piece_the_spec.md), so [the reset](piece_the_reset.md) restores the design limit and breaks the grip of the drifted baseline.
A signal is treated as a signal again, whatever its history of holding.

## Tension

The line is restored, and the schedule the drift was buying is gone.
Resetting works, and it costs: it halts a system that keeps working, sets aside real operational experience, and treats a survived risk as if it were new, which is why it takes an outsider to do and why it is resented. It trades a fast, confident, creeping line for a slow, conservative, fixed one ... or the reset is judged an overreaction, the experience is trusted again, and the baseline resumes its only direction.
