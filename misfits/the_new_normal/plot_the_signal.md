# Plot: The Signal

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The trend](place_the_trend.md), where a point sits past the line the design drew.
An anomaly that was never supposed to appear has: erosion deeper than the model allowed, a reading out of spec, plotted plainly among the runs before it.

## Action

[Flagging](process_flagging.md): [The Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md), holding [the engineer's seat](position_engineer.md), measures the point against the spec and recommends holding until it is understood.

## Stage

[The Engineer](persona_the_engineer.md) tables [Raise the Concern](plan_raise_the_concern.md): put [the signal](piece_the_signal.md) in front of the room and measure it against [the spec](piece_the_spec.md), not the last run.
The concern goes on the record, against a schedule that would rather proceed.

## Tension

Nothing here is alarmist, and it is the last moment the line is easy to hold.
The engineer is right that the data crossed the limit, and cannot prove the disaster, because the disaster has not happened and the system keeps surviving. So a true signal, correctly measured against the spec, arrives into a room already holding the one piece of evidence that will overrule it: it flew.
