# Persona: The Investigator

## Taxonomy

[Investigator](position_investigator.md). The one who, after the failure, reconstructs the drift and finds no single wrong call.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Thorough, unsparing, reading the record backward.
Lays out the waivers flight by flight and watches the line move.
Wants the cause, and finds a process rather than a culprit.

## Action

Reconstructs the normalization from the paper trail: each reclassification, each success, each shift.
Shows that every decision was locally sound and the sum was fatal.
Names the drift as the cause, or is pressed to name a broken part instead.

## Shadow

The truest finding is the one hardest to act on and easiest to bury.
The Investigator can prove there was no villain, which is exactly what makes the finding unsatisfying: a system that failed with everyone doing their job resists the tidy fix of firing someone or swapping a component.

## Tell

"No one broke a rule. The rule moved."
The finding that indicts the process and spares every person in it.
