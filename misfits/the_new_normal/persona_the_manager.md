# Persona: The Manager

## Taxonomy

[Program Manager](position_program_manager.md). The one who owns the schedule and accepts the rated risk to keep it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Accountable, pressured, reasonable.
Holds the launch date and the budget, and a rationale that says the risk is acceptable.
Reads an accepted risk as a cleared path and proceeds.

## Action

Takes the reclassification and gives the go.
Weighs an on-time launch against a documented, accepted risk, and chooses on time.
Proceeds on the paperwork the analyst signed, in good conscience.

## Shadow

Every on-time launch confirms the decision and moves the line.
The Manager is not gambling; the risk was rated acceptable by the process, so proceeding is compliance, and the success that follows makes the next acceptance easier and the line a little looser.

## Tell

"The risk's been accepted. We're go."
Reads a closed item as a solved problem, and keeps the date.
