# Place: The Review

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The readiness meeting where the anomaly is rated and the go is given.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A table of open items, a rationale on the screen, a poll around the room.
The formal gate before the run: concerns raised, risks accepted, sign-offs collected, the decision made in the open by people doing their jobs.

## Holds

The reclassification, and the authority to call an anomaly acceptable.
The place a signal is closed as within experience and the schedule is cleared to proceed.

## Offers

A defensible decision.
Here a risk can be accepted with documented rationale, sign-offs, and a clear record that the process was followed.

## Withheld

The spec, as a live reference.
The review can cite the last success and quote the record, but it lets the original limit slide out of the conversation, so the line the anomaly crossed is the one thing the meeting stops measuring against.
