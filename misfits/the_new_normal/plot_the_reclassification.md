# Plot: The Reclassification

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The review](place_the_review.md), where the flagged signal must be dispositioned before the next run.
The system flew with the anomaly aboard and nothing broke, and now the concern has to be closed one way or another.

## Action

[Rating](process_rating.md): [The Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md), holding [the risk analyst's seat](position_risk_analyst.md), rates the anomaly within experience.
Then [proceeding](process_proceeding.md): [The Manager](persona_the_manager.md) at [the program manager's seat](position_program_manager.md) takes the accepted risk and gives the go.

## Stage

[The Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md) works [Rate It Acceptable](plan_rate_it_acceptable.md) and [The Manager](persona_the_manager.md) works [Fly the Schedule](plan_fly_the_schedule.md): [the reclassification](piece_the_reclassification.md) rebooks the signal from open concern to accepted risk, on the strength of [the success](piece_the_success.md).
The item is closed, documented, defensible, and the date is kept.

## Tension

Everything is rigorous, and the rigor is the drift.
The rationale is real, the sign-offs are collected, the risk is accepted with eyes open, and the evidence genuinely says it survived; the paperwork of accepting a risk is identical to the paperwork of managing one. But the evidence is only the survivals, so a correct analysis promotes a warning to a precedent, and the line moves without anyone deciding to move it.
