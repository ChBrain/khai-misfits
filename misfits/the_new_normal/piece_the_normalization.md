# Piece: The Normalization

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The phenomenon whole: the standard eroded by its own survivals, and the reset that could hold it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the hearing](place_the_hearing.md), where the whole drift is finally laid out flight by flight and seen as one thing.
Not a single waiver but the pattern of all of them: the documented fact that a deviation was tolerated, reclassified, and normalized until it read as acceptable, and that no one decided to lower the standard, they only ever accepted the next survival.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a run of reasonable calls and one unlucky accident.
With it there is a named law it stands on: risk is redefined by its own survivals, so a standard drifts toward failure through normal people making locally sound trade-offs on the evidence they have, which is only ever the times it held. The Normalization is load bearing because it holds both the mechanism and its remedy, that the erosion is invisible from inside the drift and stopped only by measuring against the spec from outside it.

## Apparent

Hindsight. Obvious only after the fact.
It looks, in the wreckage, like a chain of clear mistakes anyone should have caught, and, before the fact, like ordinary careful operation. That gap is the disguise, because the same calls that read as damning in the hearing read as prudent in the review, and only the outcome tells them apart.

## Yearbook

Assembled from [the signal](piece_the_signal.md) through [the failure](piece_the_failure.md), and answered by [the reset](piece_the_reset.md).
It is the law the play runs on: the Normalization is why each [reclassification](piece_the_reclassification.md) looked sound and the sum was fatal, and why the only counter is to measure against [the spec](piece_the_spec.md) from outside the drift.
