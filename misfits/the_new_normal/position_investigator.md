# Position: Investigator

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The seat that reconstructs the drift after the failure.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The whole record, read after the fact, and the mandate to find cause.
The paper trail of every reclassification, laid end to end.

## Orders

Reconstruct the normalization: trace each waiver, each success, each shift of the line.
Show whether the failure was a broken part or a moved standard.
Name the cause the evidence supports.

## Loses

The comfort of a culprit.
A seat that finds every call was locally sound arrives at a process, not a person, and a process cause resists the tidy fix a hearing wants.

## Drives

Toward the drift named as the cause, and away from the single bad part that would spare the line.
Toward a finding that indicts the normalization, or is pressed into blaming a component.
