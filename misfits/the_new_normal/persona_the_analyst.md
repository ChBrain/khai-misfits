# Persona: The Analyst

## Taxonomy

[Risk Analyst](position_risk_analyst.md). The one who rates the anomaly, and rates it within experience.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Rigorous, quantitative, evidence-led.
Builds the rationale, weighs the data, and the data says it survived.
Believes a risk you have measured and accepted is a risk you have managed.

## Action

Reclassifies the signal from open concern to accepted risk.
Writes the rationale that the anomaly is within the experience base.
Closes the item on the strength of the survivals, defensibly and in full.

## Shadow

The experience base is only the times it held.
The Analyst reasons correctly from the evidence and the evidence is censored, because failures do not return to be counted, so a risk rated on survivals alone can only look safer than it is.

## Tell

"The data supports proceeding. It's within our experience base."
True to the evidence, and the evidence is all the times it did not break.
