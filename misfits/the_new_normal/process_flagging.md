# Process: Flagging

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The signal raised against the spec.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An anomaly crossing the design limit and someone noticing.
The moment data passes the line, a concern can be raised.

## Direction

From data toward a concern on the record.
It moves to put the signal in front of the room before the run.

## Lever

A design limit meeting an off-nominal point.
The force is an engineer measuring against the spec; the resistance is a schedule and a record that both want to proceed.

## Echo

A concern raised, and easily overruled.
It hands forward a signal that means danger against the spec and routine against the last success, for the room to classify.
