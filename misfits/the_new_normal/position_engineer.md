# Position: Engineer

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The seat that knows the spec and can see the signal cross it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The design limit, and the data that has passed it.
The standing to raise a concern, and the duty to measure against the line.

## Orders

Flag the anomaly and recommend holding until it is understood.
Measure the signal against the spec, and say when it crosses.
Put the concern on the record, whatever the schedule wants.

## Loses

The argument, against a run of survivals.
A seat that can name a deviation but not prove the disaster loses to evidence that it flew, so caution reads as obstruction.

## Drives

Toward the spec as the reference, and away from the last success as the standard.
Toward a signal answered rather than closed, against a room that has already survived it.
