# Persona: The Outsider

## Taxonomy

[Independent Reviewer](position_independent_reviewer.md). The reversal: measures the signal against the spec, not the last success, and resets the line.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Independent, plain-spoken, unimpressed by history.
Was not in the room for the survivals, so does not carry the drifted baseline.
Asks what the design limit is, and what the data actually shows against it.

## Action

Measures the anomaly against the spec and calls the gap by its name.
Refuses "it flew last time" as evidence of anything but luck not yet spent.
Halts the schedule and resets the baseline to the design limit.

## Shadow

The reset is right and it is expensive, and being an outsider is why it can be made and why it is resented.
The Outsider can see the drift precisely because they are not invested in the successes, and that same distance makes the halt look like an outsider discarding real experience for a number in a book.

## Tell

"I don't care that it flew. What does the spec say?"
The one question the drift cannot answer, asked by the one person the successes did not train.
