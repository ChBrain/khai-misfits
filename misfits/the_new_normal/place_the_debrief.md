# Place: The Debrief

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). Where the run is reviewed after the fact and the survival is banked as proof.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A room the morning after, a clean summary, a green board.
The post-run wrap-up: what happened, what held, the anomaly noted and marked survived, the outcome recorded as a win.

## Holds

The success, turned into evidence.
The place a survival is written up as proof the deviation was acceptable, and filed where the next review will find it.

## Offers

Confidence.
Here a good outcome can be logged, celebrated, and cited: the concern was raised, we flew, nothing broke, the record grows.

## Withheld

The margin that was actually left.
The debrief records that it survived and not by how much, so a run that nearly failed and a run that never could look identical on the board, and both read as vindication.
