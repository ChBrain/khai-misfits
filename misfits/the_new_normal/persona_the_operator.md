# Persona: The Operator

## Taxonomy

[Operator](position_operator.md). The one who runs it, and whose success banks the deviation.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Skilled, steady, focused on the run.
Executes the launch, the batch, the procedure, and brings it home.
Reads a good outcome as a job well done, because it is.

## Action

Runs the system with the accepted anomaly in place.
Delivers the success that the analyst and manager will cite next time.
Turns a rated risk into a survival, logged and filed as proof.

## Shadow

The clean run is exactly what makes the next deviation acceptable.
The Operator does everything right and the doing-right is the reinforcer: a survival that will be used as evidence the line can be crossed again, a little further.

## Tell

"Went perfectly. No issues."
A true report of one draw from a risk, read by everyone as a verdict on it.
