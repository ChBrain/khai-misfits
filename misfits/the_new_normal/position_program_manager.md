# Position: Program Manager

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The seat that owns the schedule and accepts the rated risk to keep it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The date, the budget, and the go decision.
A rationale that says the risk is acceptable, and the pressure to proceed.

## Orders

Take the accepted risk and give the go.
Weigh an on-time result against a documented, closed item, and keep the date.
Proceed on the process, not on a fresh reading of the line.

## Loses

The distinction between a risk managed and a risk merely accepted.
A seat that treats a closed item as a solved problem cannot feel the line move, because on paper nothing moved, an item was opened and closed by the rules.

## Drives

Toward the schedule kept and the risk accepted, and away from the hold that would cost the date.
Toward each on-time success confirming the last acceptance and easing the next.
