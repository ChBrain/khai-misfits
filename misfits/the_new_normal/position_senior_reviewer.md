# Position: Senior Reviewer

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The seat that carries the drifted baseline as authority.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The longest memory in the room, and the trust that comes with it.
A history of the anomaly, every case of which is a case it held.

## Orders

Bring experience to bear: reassure the room the deviation is fine.
Anchor decisions to what has been seen, and calm the newcomer's alarm.
Let hard-won history be the standard.

## Loses

The failures that would have corrected the memory, because there are none in it.
A seat whose authority is a record of survivals cannot tell wisdom from drift, because they look identical from inside a sample with no failures in it.

## Drives

Toward the last successes as the reference, and away from the spec no one has cited in years.
Toward a baseline defended as expertise, crept the whole time.
