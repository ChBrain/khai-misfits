# Process: Rating

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). Classifying the anomaly, and classifying it acceptable.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A flagged signal that must be dispositioned before the run.
Once a concern is raised, someone must rate it to close it.

## Direction

From open concern toward accepted risk.
It moves to a documented rationale that the anomaly is within experience.

## Lever

A record of survivals meeting a demand for a decision.
The force is honest analysis; the resistance is that the record holds only the times it held, so the analysis can only look safe.

## Echo

A closed item, rated acceptable.
It hands forward a signal promoted to precedent, and a line quietly moved to where the last point sits.
