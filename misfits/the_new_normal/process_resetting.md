# Process: Resetting

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The reversal: restoring the spec as the reference and dragging the baseline back.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An outside eye measuring the anomaly against the design limit.
When someone refuses survival as proof, the reset can begin.

## Direction

From the drifted baseline back toward the spec.
It moves the reference back to the design limit and treats a signal as a signal again.

## Lever

Independence from the successes meeting a room that trusts them.
The force is an outsider uncarried by the history; the resistance is the schedule and the experience the reset overrules.

## Echo

A line restored, and a schedule paid.
It hands forward a baseline dragged back to the spec, bought with the halt to do it and the pride the drift had banked.
