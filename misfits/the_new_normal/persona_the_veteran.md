# Persona: The Veteran

## Taxonomy

[Senior Reviewer](position_senior_reviewer.md). The one who carries the drifted baseline as hard-won wisdom.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Experienced, calm, trusted in the room.
Has seen this anomaly many times and watched it hold every time.
Reads the newcomer's alarm as inexperience, and reassures.

## Action

Vouches that the deviation is fine, because it always has been.
Anchors the room to the last successes rather than the original line.
Turns a personal history of survivals into the standard everyone defers to.

## Shadow

The wisdom is a memory of survivals, and it is the drift wearing a trusted face.
The Veteran genuinely knows more than anyone about how the anomaly has behaved, and every case they know is a case it did not fail, so their expertise is exactly the baseline that has crept.

## Tell

"I've seen this a hundred times. It always holds."
Real experience, and a sample with every failure missing.
