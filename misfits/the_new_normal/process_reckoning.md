# Process: Reckoning

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The failure, and the reconstruction that reads the drift backward.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

The normalized deviation finally doing what it always could.
Once the failure lands, the inquiry into how it was allowed begins.

## Direction

From catastrophe toward cause.
It moves to lay the waivers end to end and see whether a part broke or a line moved.

## Lever

The whole record read at once meeting the wish for a single culprit.
The force is a timeline that shows the drift; the resistance is the pull to blame a component and spare the process.

## Echo

The drift named, or a part blamed.
It hands forward either a finding that indicts the normalization, or a replaced part that lets the baseline hold where it crept.
