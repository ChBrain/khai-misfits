# Piece: The Reclassification

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The move into "within experience": the anomaly rebooked as acceptable on the evidence that it held.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the review](place_the_review.md), in the line item that changes a signal's status from open concern to accepted risk.
The rationale written after the flight, the waiver signed, the category shifted from out-of-family to within experience: a formal, documented, defensible act of reclassifying what the anomaly means.

## Load Bearing

Without it the signal stays a signal and must be answered each time it appears.
With it the signal is closed: rated acceptable, filed, and removed from the list of things that stop a launch, so the next occurrence starts from accepted rather than from open. The Reclassification is load bearing because it is the hinge of the drift, the exact act that converts a survival into a standard and a warning into a precedent.

## Apparent

Due diligence.
It looks like rigor: a documented rationale, a risk accepted with eyes open, a formal sign-off that the evidence supports proceeding. And it is rigorous, in form, which is what makes it so durable, because the paperwork of accepting a risk looks identical to the paperwork of managing one.

## Yearbook

Written by [the analyst](persona_the_analyst.md) through [rating](process_rating.md), on the strength of [the success](piece_the_success.md).
It is the act the misfit turns on: the Reclassification promotes [the signal](piece_the_signal.md) into [the baseline](piece_the_baseline.md), and repeated, it is how [the normalization](piece_the_normalization.md) is written one waiver at a time.
