# Position: Risk Analyst

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The seat that rates the anomaly and writes the rationale to accept it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The data, the models, and the authority to classify a risk.
The evidence of the survivals, and the tools to reason from it.

## Orders

Assess the anomaly and rate it against the record.
Write the rationale, accept the risk the evidence supports, and close the item.
Reason from what the system has done, which is survive.

## Loses

The failures, which never enter the record to be weighed.
A seat that reasons from an experience base of survivals cannot see the risk the survivals hid, so its honest analysis is systematically optimistic.

## Drives

Toward a documented, accepted, closed risk, and away from an open signal that would hold the line.
Toward a rationale sound in form and censored in evidence, one waiver at a time.
