# Pitch: It Flew Last Time

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Measured, confident, grounded in evidence.
The anomaly showed up, we assessed it, we flew, and it held: the data supports proceeding, the risk is within our experience, we have seen this before.
The voice of a careful organisation that trusts its record and reads a survival as a verdict.

## Undertow

Under the confidence, a line creeping outward one success at a time.
Each survival is banked as proof and becomes the reference the next signal is judged against, so the standard erodes not by a decision to lower it but by a run of good outcomes, and the evidence is only ever the times it did not break.
No one is reckless: every call is data-driven and defensible, and the sum of defensible calls is a baseline that has drifted to the edge.

## Nerve

The feeling of a room that has seen it a hundred times
and is right that it always held,
measuring each new anomaly against the last one that flew, and never against the line the design drew.

## Echo

After the run: a limit no one has cited in years,
a record full of survivals and empty of the near-misses,
and the one discipline that would have held, measuring the signal against the spec from outside the drift, dropped a little easier with every success, until the day it was needed and gone.
