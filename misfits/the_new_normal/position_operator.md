# Position: Operator

## Taxonomy

[The New Normal](play_the_new_normal.md). The seat that runs the system, and whose success banks the deviation.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The controls, the procedure, and the skill to bring it home.
The run itself, and the outcome it produces.

## Orders

Execute the run with the accepted anomaly in place.
Deliver the result, and log it.
Bring the system home, cleanly, as the job requires.

## Loses

Any say in what the clean run will be used to prove.
A seat measured on the outcome cannot control that a survival becomes evidence the line can be crossed again, further.

## Drives

Toward a good outcome and a banked success, and away from any signal that the good outcome was a draw from a risk.
Toward the survival that reinforces the next reclassification.
