# Place: The Hush

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The enforced silence around the contradiction, the space where the bind may not be named and the peace is kept by not speaking it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The unspoken air around the message, a quiet held over the contradiction where the rule against naming it lives.
The bystander smoothing the moment, the bound one's question dying before it is asked, the thing everyone senses and no one says.

## Holds

The sealing, and the silence it keeps.
Here the contradiction meets the rule against naming it, so the Hush holds the point where the bind is sealed: a thing sensed by all and spoken by none, so it is where metacommunication is forbidden and the peace is kept, the calm of the field always resting on the one thing that must not be said.

## Offers

A peace, bought by leaving the contradiction unspoken.
Because the Hush keeps the unspeakable unspoken, it offers the field a calm and the bystander a way to hold the household together, so it hands out the smooth surface of a bond not openly broken, and hands it out at the cost of the bound one, left alone with a contradiction that everyone feels and the rule forbids anyone to name.

## Withheld

The word that would free the bound one.
The Hush cannot both keep the peace and let the contradiction be named, so it withholds the one sentence that would dissolve the bind, sealing it in silence, and, all calm and no candour, it withholds any sign that the peace it keeps so smoothly is the very thing that holds the bound one inside the trap.
