# Piece: The Pattern

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The internalisation, the bound one learning to perceive the world in double-bind terms, so the trap runs on without the full apparatus.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Sets into the bound one across [the room](place_the_room.md), where the message comes again and again.
Would be unlearned only at [the threshold](place_the_threshold.md), where the bind can at last be seen from outside.

## Load Bearing

Without it the bind would need its full apparatus every time, and would loosen whenever the binder was absent.
With it the trap becomes self-sustaining: under the message often enough, the bound one stops expecting coherence, learns to hear the rejection folded inside every warmth, and comes to read the whole world in double-bind terms, so a single fragment of the message, or none, is enough to spring it. The Pattern is load bearing because it is the persistence the trap achieves (Bateson and colleagues): the internalised way of hearing, so the piece carries the bind grown into the person, the reason the trap outlasts the room it was learned in.

## Apparent

A personality, the plain and settled way this person simply is, wary and hard to reach.
Seen at the surface, the Pattern looks like character, a temperament that reads warmth as threat, and the truth that it is a learned way of hearing, laid down by a message that came too often, that the wariness was taught by a bind and is not the person's nature, that the trap has become the shape of their world, stays hidden behind how much a learned pattern comes to look like who someone is.

## Yearbook

Laid into [the bound](persona_the_bound.md) through [sealing](process_sealing.md), the bind become a way of hearing.
It is the persistence of the trap: the Pattern is [the no-win](piece_the_no_win.md) repeated until it is a habit of perception, needing [the double message](piece_the_double_message.md) no longer to run, the internalised twin of the field, the reason the [double bind](piece_the_double_bind.md) outlives its own occasion.
