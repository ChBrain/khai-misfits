# Plan: Name the Bind

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the namer's seat](position_namer.md), inside [The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md).

## Direction

Say aloud that the message asks two things at once and that no answer to it can be right. Point at the contradiction the rule forbids pointing at, talk about the message rather than inside it, or open a way out of the field that was held shut. Break the frame that seals the trap.

## Orders

([The Namer](persona_the_namer.md) names the bind from outside the rule that seals it, or opens a door in the field, at the cost of the accusation the bind is guarded by and the risk to the bond.)

## Targets

- [ ] the contradiction is named aloud, the message talked about rather than obeyed
- [ ] the rule against metacommunication is broken and the frame opened
- [ ] a way out of the field is offered, or the bind dissolved by the naming
- [ ] the accusation is met and the bond risked, or the naming is denied and the peace kept once more
