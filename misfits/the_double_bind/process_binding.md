# Process: Binding

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The sending of the two contradictory demands down one wire, the content calling one thing and the frame forbidding it, out of a need split against itself.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A need and its terror travelling together, and a bond to send them into.
At [the room](place_the_room.md), the binder calling for the closeness they want and flinching from it, the wish and the fear leaving on the same message.

## Direction

Toward a demand that cannot be met.
Because the content and the frame carry opposite injunctions, binding sends a message whose two levels cannot both be obeyed, so the call to come close arrives wrapped in the flinch that forbids it, and the ask is shaped, without anyone shaping it, so that no response can satisfy both the words and the way they are meant.

## Lever

A wish genuinely wanted and genuinely feared at once.
Because the binder truly wants the closeness and truly dreads it, and cannot hold the two apart, the double message is the honest expression of a divided need, not a trick, so binding runs on real longing, the contradiction assembled from no malice, only from a want at war with itself sent down a single wire.

## Echo

A call that punishes every answer to it.
The message has gone out carrying its own refusal, and the closeness it asks for cannot be given, so what reads as a simple request from someone who matters is, in truth, two opposite demands at once, a need sent in the one shape that makes it impossible to meet.
