# Process: Sealing

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The closing of the trap by the ban on naming and the ban on leaving, the silence that keeps the contradiction unspoken and the bound one held.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A contradiction sensed and a rule against speaking it.
At [the hush](place_the_hush.md), the bound one's question dying before it is asked and the bystander smoothing the moment, the field forbidding both the naming and the leaving.

## Direction

Toward the bind held shut and the way out closed.
Because the rule forbids metacommunication and the bond forbids exit, sealing keeps the contradiction unnamed and the field unleft, so the one move that would dissolve the trap is forbidden and the one road out is nailed shut, and the bound one is held inside with no answer, no naming, and no door.

## Lever

The wish to keep the peace and the preciousness of the bond.
Because naming the contradiction would break the calm and draw the accusation, and leaving would cost a bond of survival value, keeping silent and staying are the safe and loving moves, so sealing runs on peace-keeping and devotion, the trap closed by no jailer, only by a silence kept out of love and a bond too dear to leave.

## Echo

A contradiction everyone feels and no one names, and a door that will not open.
The bind has been sealed by silence and by the shut field, so what reads as a family keeping its peace is, in truth, a trap held closed around one of its own, the calm resting on the very thing that must not be said and the door held shut by the very love that makes it precious.
