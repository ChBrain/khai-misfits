# Persona: The Bound

## Taxonomy

[Bound](position_bound.md). The one who receives the double message and must answer it, held in the field and forbidden to name the contradiction, whose every response is the wrong one.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Willing. Trying. Anxious to get it right.
Is the one who wants to answer the call, to be close when closeness is asked, to do the thing the person they depend on is asking of them.
No defiance in it: only an earnest wish to satisfy someone who matters, and a growing bafflement that satisfying them is somehow never possible.

## Action

Answers the message and tries to obey both demands at once, coming near to the call and feeling the flinch, or holding back and refusing it.
Reaches for the response that will finally be right, and finds each response breaks one injunction or the other.
Does what anyone held and asked would do: tries, again, to give the person what they seem to want.

## Shadow

Not a failure at a simple task, the receiver of an impossible one, and that is the whole difficulty: there was no right answer to give, and the trying is what convinces the Bound the fault is theirs.
Because the two demands contradict across levels and the rule forbids naming the split, the Bound reads every wrong answer as their own inadequacy rather than the message's impossibility, so the earnest effort to comply becomes the slow lesson that they cannot love or be close correctly, and the harder they try the more certain the failure feels.
It only ever tried to answer, and the trying is what teaches it the failure is its own.

## Tell

Says "I do not understand what they want from me: whatever I do, it is wrong."
Says it, and cannot see that whatever they do is wrong by design, because the message asked two opposite things and forbade the noticing.
