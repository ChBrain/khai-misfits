# Piece: The Gag

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The ban on metacommunication, the injunction against naming the contradiction, the rule that forbids the one move that would dissolve the trap.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Enforced in [the hush](place_the_hush.md), the silence where the contradiction may not be spoken.
Broken only at [the threshold](place_the_threshold.md), where the forbidden sentence can be said.

## Load Bearing

Without it the bound one could simply say "you are asking two things at once," and the trap would come apart in a sentence.
With it the paradox is sealed: a rule, spoken or not, that forbids naming the contradiction, so that to point at the bind is to accuse the binder and draw denial or punishment. The Gag is load bearing because it is the seal the trap requires (Watzlawick, Beavin, and Jackson): the ban on metacommunication, so the piece carries the injunction that keeps the contradiction unspeakable, the reason the one move that frees the bound one is the one move they may not make.

## Apparent

Tact, the plain good sense of not making a scene or accusing someone who means well.
Seen at the surface, the Gag looks like courtesy, the decent restraint of leaving a hard thing unsaid, and the truth that it forbids the very naming that would dissolve the trap, that it turns the freeing word into an accusation, that its silence is what keeps the bound one held, stays hidden behind how much it feels like simple good manners not to say it.

## Yearbook

Kept by [the bystander](persona_the_bystander.md) through [sealing](process_sealing.md), the ban on naming the contradiction.
It is the seal of the trap: the Gag forbids the naming of [the double message](piece_the_double_message.md) and keeps [the bond](piece_the_bond.md)'s peace, the rule that [naming](process_naming.md) must break to free the Bound, the lock on the whole [double bind](piece_the_double_bind.md).
