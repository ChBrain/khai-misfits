# Persona: The Bystander

## Taxonomy

[Bystander](position_bystander.md). The third in the field who sees the bind and, under the same rule against naming it, keeps the peace and says nothing, sealing the trap by silence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Peaceable. Loyal. Keeping things together.
Is the other one in the room, the second parent, the sibling, the friend of the family, who senses the contradiction and lets it lie for the sake of the bond.
No malice in it: only a wish to keep the peace and not make things worse by stirring what is better left unsaid.

## Action

Sees the double message and says nothing, holding the same rule against naming it that binds everyone in the field.
Smooths the moment, keeps the household together, and does not point at the contradiction that would break the calm to name.
Does what a peacemaker in a strained field does: keeps things whole by leaving the unspeakable unspoken.

## Shadow

Not a coward, a keeper of the peace, and that is the whole difficulty: staying silent holds the family together, and the silence is exactly what seals the bind.
Because naming the contradiction would break the calm and turn the field against the namer, the Bystander who keeps the peace ratifies the rule that the bind may not be spoken, so the very loyalty that holds the bond together is what leaves the Bound alone inside it, and the more the peace is kept the more firmly the trap is sealed.
It keeps the peace out of love for the whole, and keeping it is what abandons the one caught.

## Tell

Says "best not to make a scene: you know how they are, let it go."
Lets it go, and by letting it go joins the silence that keeps the contradiction unnamed and the bound one held.
