# Position: Namer

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The seat that would name the contradiction from outside the rule that seals it, or open a door in the field, at the cost of the accusation and the bond.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A vantage a little outside the field and the words to name what it sees: the standing to talk about the message rather than inside it, and the case that no answer to the bind can be right.
A clear case: the way to free the bound one is to name the contradiction and to make the field one that can be left.
No way to name it without breaking the rule that forbids naming, and no cover from the accusation the bind is guarded by.

## Orders

Name the bind.
Say aloud that the message asks two things at once and that no answer to it can be right, point at the contradiction the rule forbids, or open a way out of the field.
Talk about the message, break the frame, and free the one held inside it.

## Loses

The peace and the safety of the bond, exactly by saying the thing that frees the bound one.
Because the rule forbids metacommunication and the field forbids leaving, the seat that names the bind draws the accusation the bind is guarded by, that it is cruel, or imagining things, or the one who broke the family, so it cannot dissolve the trap without breaking the injunction the trap is sealed with and risking the loss of the bond, the freeing move met as the betrayal that proves the bind.
It buys the bound one's freedom against the peace and the bond, and is taken, for a while, as the one who made the scene and broke the family.

## Drives

Toward the bind named and the trap dissolved,
bought against the peace kept and the bond risked,
so the one seat that would free the bound one must say the very thing the bind punishes most.
