# Process: Straining

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The bound one trying to answer the double message and failing at both, every response breaking one injunction, the no-win read as the Bound's own fault.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A double message received and a compulsion to answer it.
At [the room](place_the_room.md), the bound one reaching to give the one they depend on what they seem to want, coming near to the call or holding back from the flinch.

## Direction

Toward every answer wrong and the fault taken inward.
Because the two demands contradict across levels, straining meets one and breaks the other, so coming close answers the call and hits the flinch, holding back respects the flinch and refuses the call, and since the rule forbids naming the split, the bound one reads each failure as their own inadequacy, the impossibility of the message becoming the proof of their own.

## Lever

The earnest wish to satisfy someone who matters.
Because the bond is real and answering a loved one's call is the natural thing to do, each attempt to comply is a sound and loving move, so straining runs on genuine devotion, the self-blame assembled from no foolishness, only from trying faithfully to answer a message that no faithful answer could satisfy.

## Echo

A wrong answer, and a conviction that the wrongness is one's own.
The bound one has tried and failed and cannot see the failure as the message's, so what reads as someone who simply cannot get a simple thing right is, in truth, someone answering an unanswerable demand, learning from every wrong response that they are the thing that is wrong.
