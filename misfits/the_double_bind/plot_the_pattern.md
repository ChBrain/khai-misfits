# Plot: The Pattern

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Room](place_the_room.md), the message come again and again over long enough.
The bind sprung and sealed many times, the bound one worn by the repetition, the way of hearing beginning to set.

## Action

[Sealing](process_sealing.md): [The Bound](persona_the_bound.md), holding [the bound's seat](position_bound.md), stops expecting coherence and learns to hear the rejection folded inside every warmth, until the bind becomes a way of hearing.

## Stage

[The Bound](persona_the_bound.md), on [Please Them Both](plan_please_them_both.md), meets the double message so often that they cease to expect a message to mean one thing, and begin to read every warmth for the flinch inside it.
The bind, repeated, sets into perception, so that a fragment of the message, or none, is now enough to spring it, and the trap no longer needs its full apparatus to run.

## Tension

Learning to expect the flinch is a reasonable defence, and the defence is what carries the trap out of the room and into the person.
There is nothing to fault: after enough impossible messages, bracing for the rejection is only sense. But bracing becomes a way of hearing, so the plot closes with the bind grown into the Bound, self-sustaining, warmth heard as threat everywhere, and the room they could never leave now the only room they know how to be in.
