# Pitch: Come Closer

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Warm, inviting, a call to come near.
The plain appeal of a message that asks for closeness, for a response, for love, from someone who matters and means it.
Nothing false in the wish: the binder truly wants the closeness they call for, and the call sounds like the most natural thing in the world.

## Undertow

Under the invitation, a second demand that contradicts it, a ban on naming the contradiction, and no way to leave.
Because the same message that calls you close stiffens against the closeness, and the rule forbids you to say so, and the bond cannot be walked out of, every answer breaks one demand or another, so coming near meets the flinch and holding back refuses the call and naming it is met with denial.
The invitation that asks for closeness is built so that no closeness can satisfy it.

## Nerve

The feeling of being called warmly toward someone and flinched away from in the same breath,
sure the fault is yours for failing so simple a thing as coming when you are called,
while the rule against saying "you are asking two things at once" seals your mouth, and the door you would leave by will not open.

## Echo

After the run: a person who has stopped expecting messages to mean one thing, hearing the rejection folded inside every warmth,
no one cruel and no one scheming, only a need and its terror sent down one wire by someone caught in the same pattern themselves,
and the one word that would dissolve it, the naming of the bind, left unsaid because saying it breaks the rule the bind is sealed with and risks the loss of the bond it is made of.
