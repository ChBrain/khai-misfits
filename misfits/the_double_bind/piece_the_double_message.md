# Piece: The Double Message

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The two contradictory demands at different logical levels, the content calling one thing and the frame forbidding it, the paradox at the heart of the trap.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Sent in [the room](place_the_room.md), where the binder's divided need travels down one wire.
Could be spoken of only at [the threshold](place_the_threshold.md), where its two levels can be seen at once.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is only an ordinary demand, met or refused, and no trap at all.
With it the trap has its engine: a message whose content and frame command opposite things, come close and keep away, be spontaneous on demand, so that obeying either breaks the other. The Double Message is load bearing because it is the paradox the trap turns on (Bateson, Jackson, Haley, and Weakland): the two injunctions split across levels, so the piece carries the contradiction that makes every answer wrong, the impossible ask that cannot be met because meeting one half violates the other.

## Apparent

A simple request, a single clear thing being asked by someone who means it.
Seen at the surface, the Double Message looks like one demand, plainly stated, and the truth that it carries a second injunction at another level that forbids what the first commands, that its two halves cannot both be obeyed, that the failure to satisfy it is built into its shape, stays hidden behind how much it sounds like an ordinary thing to ask.

## Yearbook

Sent by [the binder](persona_the_binder.md) through [binding](process_binding.md), the two demands split across levels.
It is the engine of the trap: the Double Message springs [the no-win](piece_the_no_win.md) on whoever answers, guarded by [the gag](piece_the_gag.md) that forbids its naming, the paradox that [naming](process_naming.md) alone can speak, the heart of the whole [double bind](piece_the_double_bind.md).
