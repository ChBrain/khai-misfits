# Position: Bound

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The seat that must answer the double message, held in the field and forbidden to name the contradiction, where every response is wrong.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A need for the bond and an obligation to answer: the standing that comes of depending on the one who binds, and the compulsion to respond to their call.
A clear case: the person matters, the call is real, and answering it is the natural, loving thing to do.
No way to obey both demands, no way to name the split, and no way to leave the field.

## Orders

Please them both.
Answer the call, come close when closeness is asked, and try to give the one you depend on the thing they seem to want.
Reach, again, for the response that will finally be right.

## Loses

Any answer that could be right, and in time its own account of itself, exactly by trying to comply.
Because the two demands contradict across levels and the rule forbids naming the split, the seat cannot answer without breaking one injunction, and cannot see the impossibility as the message's rather than its own, so each failed attempt teaches it that the fault is its own, and the earnest effort to comply becomes the conviction that it cannot love or be close correctly.
It gains a bond it cannot bear to lose, and loses every right answer and, at last, the sense that a right answer was ever there to give.

## Drives

Toward the message answered and the answer wrong,
bought against its own account of the failure,
so the seat that only tried to comply learns the impossibility as its own fault.
