# Piece: The Double Bind

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The phenomenon whole, a contradictory message in a field that cannot be left and a mouth that cannot name it, so every answer is wrong.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Runs across all three: sent and answered in [the room](place_the_room.md), sealed in [the hush](place_the_hush.md), named only from [the threshold](place_the_threshold.md).
It is the whole apparatus, the message and the field and the silence that together leave no answer right.

## Load Bearing

Without it the pieces are separate: a bond, a contradictory message, a ban on speaking, a checkmate, a learned wariness, with nothing to bind them.
With it they are one mechanism: the bond holds the field shut, the double message contradicts across levels, the gag forbids the naming, the no-win springs, and the pattern sets it into the person, each part sealing the next. The Double Bind is load bearing because it is the structure entire (Bateson, Jackson, Haley, and Weakland): the contradictory injunction in an inescapable field with metacommunication forbidden, so the piece carries the trap whole, the reason no one need be cruel for a message to leave its receiver no answer that is not wrong.

## Apparent

A difficult relationship, a hard family, a person who cannot be pleased and one who cannot get it right.
Seen at the surface, the whole trap looks like ordinary trouble between people, and the truth that the message asks two opposite things at once, that the field cannot be left and the contradiction cannot be named, that no answer was ever available and no one need have meant harm, stays hidden behind how much a bind looks like a simple clash of two difficult people.
