# Process: Naming

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The speaking of the contradiction from outside the rule that seals it, the metacommunication or exit that dissolves the trap, at the cost of the one who makes it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A vantage outside the message and a willingness to say the forbidden thing.
At [the threshold](place_the_threshold.md), the namer standing a little outside the field and speaking of the message rather than inside it, or opening a door in the bond.

## Direction

Toward the trap dissolved, if the cost can be borne.
Because the bind is sealed by the ban on naming it, naming speaks the contradiction aloud and talks about the message instead of obeying it, so it drives toward the trap dissolved and the bound one freed: but the naming breaks the very injunction the bind is guarded by, so the process frees the bound one only by drawing the accusation and risking the bond the field is held together with.

## Lever

The truth that no answer to the bind can be right.
Because the contradiction is real and metacommunication is the one move that addresses it, naming the bind is the sound and freeing act, so naming runs on a true reading of the trap: yet the same word that dissolves the bind is the accusation the field forbids, so the process costs its maker exactly by being the thing that works.

## Echo

A bind dissolved, or a naming met with denial.
Either the contradiction is spoken and the trap comes apart, bought against the accusation and the risk to the bond, or the naming is met with "how dare you" and the peace is kept, the door shut again, and the bound one left to answer the next impossible message.
