# Place: The Room

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The intense relationship-field where the message is sent and answered, the bond of survival value that cannot be left.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place two people are bound together and depend on each other, a home or a closeness of survival value where the call and the answer pass between them.
The binder sending the double message, the bound one reaching to answer it, the door to the field standing shut.

## Holds

The binding and the straining, and the field they happen in.
Here the message meets the one who must answer it, so the Room holds the point where the impossible ask is made and attempted: a bond that cannot be left and a call that cannot be met, so it is where the double message arrives and the bound one strains at it, the survival of the bond making every answer feel like it has to be found.

## Offers

A closeness worth everything, and a call that asks for it.
Because the Room is where the bond of survival value lives, it offers the bound one the relationship they cannot do without and the binder the one they can send their need to, so it hands out the intimacy that makes the message matter, and hands it out inside a field whose very preciousness is what makes leaving it unthinkable.

## Withheld

The door out, and the room to answer freely.
The Room cannot both hold a bond of survival value and let it be walked out of lightly, so it withholds the easy exit, making the field one that cannot be left, and, all closeness and no distance, it withholds any answer to the message that is not shaped by the need to keep the bond, so the bound one strains at the impossible because the alternative is losing everything.
