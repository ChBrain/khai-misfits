# Place: The Threshold

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). Where the contradiction could be named and the field could be left, the edge of the bind at which metacommunication or exit becomes possible, or is refused.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The edge of the field, a little outside the message, where one can stand far enough back to see the two demands at once and speak of them.
The namer talking about the message rather than inside it, the door that was shut showing a way open, the forbidden sentence forming.

## Holds

The naming, and the exit it opens.
Here the bind meets the eye that can see it whole, so the Threshold holds the point where the trap could be dissolved: a vantage outside the rule where the contradiction can be spoken and the field can be left, so it is where metacommunication and exit become possible, the one edge from which the bind can be addressed rather than obeyed.

## Offers

A place to stand outside the message, and a door in the field.
Because the Threshold is the edge where one can see the bind whole, it offers the namer the vantage to speak of the contradiction and the bound one a way out of the room, so it hands out the possibility of naming and of leaving, and hands it out knowing that both break the rule the bind is sealed with and draw the accusation it is guarded by.

## Withheld

Any way to name the bind or leave the field without cost.
The Threshold can open the door and the sentence, but it cannot make the naming safe or the leaving free, so it withholds the costless exit, exacting the accusation and the loss of the bond as the price of both, and, all vantage and no shelter, it withholds any dissolving of the trap that does not spend the peace the bind was sealed with.
