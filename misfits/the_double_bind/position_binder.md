# Position: Binder

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The seat that sends the two contradictory demands down one wire, out of a need split against itself, not out of malice.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A need and its terror, held together: the standing of the one who is depended on, and a wish that arrives already at war with itself.
A clear case: the closeness it asks for is real and wanted, and asking for what you want is no crime.
No way to hold the wish and the fear apart, and no sight of the contradiction it sends down the wire.

## Orders

Send both at once.
Call for the closeness and flinch from it, ask for the response and forbid the way it is given, and let the need and its terror travel together.
Ask, and ask again when the asking is not answered.

## Loses

The closeness it actually wants, exactly by asking for it in two directions at once.
Because the wish and the fear cannot be held apart, the seat sends a message that cannot be met, so the closeness it craves never arrives, and it reads the failure as the other's refusal rather than its own impossible ask, asking again, harder, and binding tighter the one who could never have answered.
It gains the sending of its need and loses the meeting of it, wanting the bond and shaping the ask so the bond cannot form.

## Drives

Toward the need sent and the need unmet,
bought against the very closeness it calls for,
so the seat that only wanted to be close asks in the one way that closeness cannot answer.
