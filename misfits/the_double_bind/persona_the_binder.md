# Persona: The Binder

## Taxonomy

[Binder](position_binder.md). The one who sends the two contradictory demands down one wire, out of need and not malice, often caught in the same pattern themselves.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Needful. Sincere. Asking for something real.
Is the one who calls for closeness, or love, or a response, and means the call, a parent or partner reaching for what they genuinely want.
No scheme in it: the Binder truly wants the thing they ask for, and does not know they are asking it in a way that cannot be given.

## Action

Sends the double message, the content calling one thing and the frame forbidding it, come close and keep away, be spontaneous because I demand it.
Reaches for the closeness and flinches from it in the same breath, wanting the thing and frightened of it at once.
Does what someone caught between a need and its terror does: asks for both, and sends both down the same wire.

## Shadow

Not a manipulator, a person split by their own need, and that is the whole difficulty: the Binder does not design the bind, they leak it, so there is no villain to blame and no cruelty to point to.
Because the wish and the fear travel together and the Binder cannot hold them apart, the message carries both, and when the Bound cannot satisfy it the Binder feels only that their need is unmet again, so they ask again, harder, never seeing that the asking is shaped so it cannot be answered, often because they were bound the very same way once themselves.
It asks only for what it wants, and wanting it in two directions at once is what makes it impossible to give.

## Tell

Says "why can you never just do what I ask? it is not so much to want."
Wants it truly, and cannot see that what they ask contains its own refusal, so the failure looks like the Bound's and not the message's.
