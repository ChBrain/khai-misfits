# Plot: The Naming

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Threshold](place_the_threshold.md), someone who would say the forbidden thing.
A vantage a little outside the field, the two demands visible at once, the accusation and the bond both there to be risked.

## Action

[Naming](process_naming.md): [The Namer](persona_the_namer.md), holding [the namer's seat](position_namer.md), names the contradiction from outside the rule that seals it, against [The Bystander](persona_the_bystander.md) on [Keep the Peace](plan_keep_the_peace.md).

## Stage

[The Namer](persona_the_namer.md), on [Name the Bind](plan_name_the_bind.md), says aloud that the message asks two things at once and that no answer to it can be right, talking about the message rather than inside it, or opening a door in the field.
[The Bystander](persona_the_bystander.md), on [Keep the Peace](plan_keep_the_peace.md), holds to the silence, and the field turns the accusation the bind is guarded by against the one who spoke.

## Tension

Naming the bind is the one move that frees the bound one, and it breaks the very injunction the bind is sealed with.
There is nothing to fault: the contradiction is real and the namer only speaks it. But the rule forbids the speaking and the bond forbids the leaving, so the plot closes one of two ways: the bind is named and the trap comes apart, bought against the accusation and the risk to the bond ... or the naming is met with "how dare you," the peace is kept, and the bound one turns back to answer the next impossible message.
