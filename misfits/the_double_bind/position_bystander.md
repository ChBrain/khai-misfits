# Position: Bystander

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md). The seat of the third in the field who sees the bind and keeps the peace, sealing the trap by the silence the rule demands.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A place in the field and a sense of the strain: the standing of the one who is also in the room, and the wish to hold the bond together.
A clear case: naming the contradiction would break the calm and turn the field against the namer, and keeping the peace holds the family whole.
No cover for speaking the unspeakable, and no way to keep the peace and free the bound one at once.

## Orders

Keep the peace.
Sense the contradiction and let it lie, smooth the moment, and hold the household together by leaving the unspeakable unspoken.
Do not make a scene, and do not stir what is better left alone.

## Loses

The freeing of the bound one, exactly by keeping the field at peace.
Because naming the bind would break the calm and draw the accusation the field guards it with, the seat that keeps the peace ratifies the rule that the contradiction may not be spoken, so its loyalty to the whole is what leaves the Bound alone inside the trap, and the more faithfully the peace is kept the more firmly the bind is sealed.
It gains a household held together, and loses the one caught in it, keeping the bond whole at the cost of the person the bond is breaking.

## Drives

Toward the peace kept and the contradiction unnamed,
bought against the bound one left alone in it,
so the seat that would hold the family together seals the trap on the one it holds.
