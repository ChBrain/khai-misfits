# Plot: The Strain

## Taxonomy

[The Double Bind](play_the_double_bind.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Room](place_the_room.md), both demands in force and an answer compelled.
The call inviting and the frame forbidding, the bond making an answer feel necessary, no response available that meets both.

## Action

[Straining](process_straining.md): [The Bound](persona_the_bound.md), holding [the bound's seat](position_bound.md), tries to answer, coming near to the call and meeting the flinch, or holding back and refusing it.

## Stage

[The Bound](persona_the_bound.md), on [Please Them Both](plan_please_them_both.md), reaches for the response that will finally be right, and finds that coming close hits the flinch and holding back refuses the call.
[The Binder](persona_the_binder.md), on [Send Both at Once](plan_send_both_at_once.md), feels the need unmet again and asks again, harder, never seeing the ask contains its own refusal.

## Tension

Trying faithfully to answer a loved one is the natural thing, and the faithful trying is what teaches the Bound the failure is their own.
There is nothing to fault: the bond is real and answering it is loving. But no response satisfies both demands, and the rule forbids naming the split, so the plot closes with the answer given and wrong, the bound one damned for coming and damned for holding back, and reading the impossibility as their own inadequacy.
