# Persona: The Namer

## Taxonomy

[Namer](position_namer.md). Would say the forbidden thing, name the contradiction from outside the rule that seals it or open a door in the field, at the cost of the accusation and the bond.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Clear. Steady. Willing to say the unsayable.
Is the one who can stand a little outside the field, the therapist, the friend, the bound one in a stronger moment, and see the two demands for what they are.
No relish for the scene in it: only the refusal to leave the contradiction unnamed and the bound one alone with it.

## Action

Names the bind, says aloud that the message asks two things at once and that no answer to it can be right.
Points at the contradiction the rule forbids pointing at, or opens a way out of the field that was held shut.
Does what metacommunication does: talks about the message rather than inside it, and breaks the frame that sealed the trap.

## Shadow

Not a troublemaker breaking a calm, a clear eye breaking a spell, and that is the whole difficulty: naming the bind is the one move that frees the bound one, and it breaks the very injunction the bind is sealed with.
Because the rule forbids metacommunication and the field forbids leaving, the Namer who says the forbidden thing draws the accusation the bind is guarded by, that they are cruel, or imagining it, or the one who broke the family, so the move that dissolves the trap is met as the betrayal that proves the trap, and freeing the bound one costs the peace and risks the bond.
It would say the thing that ends the bind, and saying it is the thing the bind punishes most.

## Tell

Says "you are being asked for two things that cannot both be given: this is not yours to solve."
Says it, and is met with "how dare you," because the naming that frees the bound one is the accusation the field was built to forbid.
