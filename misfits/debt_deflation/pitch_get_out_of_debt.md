# Pitch: Get Out of Debt

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Responsible, sober, prudent.
The plain good sense of paying down your debts when times turn hard, selling what you can and getting your house in order.
Nothing rash in it: only the sound instinct to lighten the load and owe no one.

## Undertow

Under the prudence, a debt fixed in money growing heavier as the prices around it fall.
Because paying down means selling, and a whole economy selling at once drives prices down, the fixed debt rises in real weight even as the payments are made, so the harder everyone pays, the more, in real terms, they owe.
The responsible act, done by all together, is the thing that deepens the debt it means to clear.

## Nerve

The feeling of doing the right thing in a downturn, selling to pay down what you owe,
sure it is the way out, because for one household it is,
while every debtor sells too, prices sink, and the loan you are paying down quietly grows heavier than when you began.

## Echo

After the run: an economy that paid down its debts into a deeper slump, the more it paid the more it owed,
no one profligate and no one cruel, only each debtor prudently selling and each creditor sensibly calling,
and a reflation that would have halted it, refused as long as instinct held, because in a bust it looked like the one reckless thing.
