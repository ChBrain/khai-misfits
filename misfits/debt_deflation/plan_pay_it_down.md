# Plan: Pay It Down

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the debtor's seat](position_debtor.md), inside [Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md).

## Direction

Lighten the load by paying the debt down. Sell what you can to raise the cash, tighten the belt, hand the money back to the lender, and reduce the sum you owe. Get the house in order and owe no one, the sound thing to do in a downturn.

## Orders

([The Debtor](persona_the_debtor.md) liquidates its assets to raise cash and pays the debt down, doing for itself exactly what prudence in hard times demands.)
