# Persona: The Reflator

## Taxonomy

[Reflator](position_reflator.md). Lifts the price level or cuts the debt to halt the spiral, against every instinct of the moment.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Steady-nerved. Countercyclical. Willing to act against the grain.
Sees that the spiral runs on a falling price level and a fixed debt, and reaches to raise the one or cut the other.
No recklessness in it, though it looks like it: a considered move to stop a descent that prudence alone will only deepen.

## Action

Loosens money and runs deficits to lift the price level, or writes the nominal debt down and restructures it.
Halts the rise in the real burden by reflating the prices or forgiving the sum.
Acts, for the whole, against what looks prudent to each part in a bust.

## Shadow

It holds the one move that stops the spiral and the one move every sound instinct forbids.
Because reflation means creating money and spending deficits when caution screams the opposite, and forgiveness means a creditor's loss and a reward to the borrower who took the risk, its remedy runs against the deep urge to let the debt be paid and the bust clear itself, so it must act reckless-seeming and unfair-seeming to do the one thing that works.
It answers an economy paying itself deeper into debt with the act that prudence most condemns.

## Tell

Says "raise the price level, or cut the debt, or the spiral runs to the floor."
Acts, against the instinct of every debtor and creditor, because paying down alone will only sink them further.
