# Position: Reflator

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The seat that lifts the price level or cuts the debt to halt the spiral, against every instinct of the moment.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The means to reflate: to loosen money, run deficits, and lift the price level, or to write the nominal debt down.
The one move that stops the real burden climbing.
The appearance of recklessness and unfairness, for acting when caution says to wait.

## Orders

Lift the level.
Raise the price level by loose money and deficits, or cut the nominal debt by restructuring and relief, until the burden stops rising.
Act against the tide of paying-down, for the whole.

## Loses

Ease and its good name, exactly because the fix runs against every instinct.
Because reflating means creating money and running deficits when caution screams the opposite, and forgiveness means a creditor's loss and a borrower's reward, the seat cannot act without seeming reckless and unfair, and it stands alone against the deep urge to let the debt be paid and the bust clear.
It does the necessary thing at the moment it looks most unwise.

## Drives

Toward a spiral halted by lifting the level or cutting the debt,
the one move that works and the one prudence most condemns,
so the cure for an economy paying itself deeper into debt is the act every sound instinct forbids.
