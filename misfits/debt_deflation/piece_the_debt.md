# Piece: The Debt

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The fixed nominal obligation, owed in a set number of dollars whatever those dollars come to be worth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), where it is recorded as a figure that does not change.
Set against [the market](place_the_market.md), whose falling prices are what make the unchanged figure heavier.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing for the deflation to weigh upon, and the fall in prices is just cheaper goods.
With it the whole trap has its fulcrum: a claim fixed in money, indifferent to what money is worth, so that when prices fall the real cost of clearing it rises. The Debt is load bearing because it is the still point the mechanism turns on (Fisher; Minsky): everything else in the economy falls with prices, and the debt does not, so the piece carries the fixity that converts a general deflation into a rising burden on the borrower alone.

## Apparent

An ordinary obligation, a sum to be repaid like any other.
Seen in calm, the debt looks like a neutral figure, owed and to be settled, and the truth that its fixity in money is a live danger the moment prices can fall, that it is the one thing a deflation makes larger, stays hidden behind how unremarkable a fixed sum of money appears when prices are steady.

## Yearbook

Held by [the debt](persona_the_debt.md) through [deflating](process_deflating.md), the fixed sum that will not fall with prices.
It is the fulcrum of the ruin: the Debt is what [the distress sale](piece_the_distress_sale.md) is meant to reduce and cannot, what deflation turns into [the weight](piece_the_weight.md), and the fixed claim that only [the reflation](piece_the_reflation.md), lifting prices or cutting the sum, can lighten.
