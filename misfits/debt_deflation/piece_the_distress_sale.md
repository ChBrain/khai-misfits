# Piece: The Distress Sale

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The forced liquidation that is the only way to pay, and that, made by all at once, drives prices down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the market](place_the_market.md), where the assets are dumped for cash and each sale fetches less than the last.
Forced from [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), where the debt called and the debt feared demand the money the sale must raise.

## Load Bearing

Without it the debt could not be paid and the prices would not fall, and there would be no spiral.
With it the mechanism has its engine: to pay a debt in a downturn the borrower must sell, and a whole economy selling at once for cash drives the price level down. The Distress Sale is load bearing because it is the act that connects prudence to deflation (Koo): each sale is the sound way to pay, and the sum of the sales is the fall in prices, so the piece carries the forced liquidation by which paying down becomes the very thing that lowers prices and raises the debt.

## Apparent

Simple settling of accounts, a borrower selling to pay what it owes.
Seen alone, the distress sale looks like an ordinary transaction, an asset turned into cash to clear a debt, and the truth that a whole economy doing it at once drives the deflation that makes every remaining debt heavier, that the sale is both the escape and the trap, stays hidden behind how sensible one sale to pay one debt appears.

## Yearbook

Forced by [the debtor](persona_the_debtor.md) and [the creditor](persona_the_creditor.md) through [liquidating](process_liquidating.md), the selling that drives the fall.
It is prudence turned engine: the Distress Sale is what is meant to reduce [the debt](piece_the_debt.md) and instead drives the deflation behind [the weight](piece_the_weight.md), each sale a turn of [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md) that only [the reflation](piece_the_reflation.md) can arrest.
