# Process: Spiralling

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The self-reinforcing descent, heavier debt begetting more selling and lower prices begetting heavier debt.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A real debt burden risen by the first fall in prices.
At [the slump](place_the_slump.md), the heavier debt forcing more distress selling and the falling prices raising the debt again, each driving the other down.

## Direction

Toward a descent that feeds itself.
Heavier debt means more forced selling, more selling means lower prices, lower prices mean a heavier debt still, so spiralling drives the two downward together, and as firms fail below their fixed debts and the banks that lent them shrink and stop lending, the credit that might have broken the fall is gone with it.

## Lever

A loop in which each fall causes the next.
Because the rise in real debt drives the selling that drives the deflation that drives the real debt higher, the descent needs no fresh shock to continue, so spiralling works on the closed circle of the mechanism, amplified through failing banks and frozen credit, each turn making the next turn worse.

## Echo

An economy paying harder and owing more.
Net worth collapsed, firms bankrupt, credit frozen, and the real debt higher than when the paying began, so what each debtor did to escape the debt has, in the aggregate, driven the slump that deepens it, the more they pay the more they owe.
