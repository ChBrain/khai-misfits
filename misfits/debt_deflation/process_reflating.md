# Process: Reflating

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The price level lifted or the debt cut to halt the spiral, the remedy applied against every instinct.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A spiral running toward the floor and one actor able to act against it.
At [the mint](place_the_mint.md), the reflator loosening money and running deficits, or writing the nominal debt down, to stop the real burden climbing.

## Direction

Toward a real burden that stops rising.
By lifting the price level, reflating undoes the deflation that raises the debt, and by cutting the nominal sum it lowers the burden directly, so it drives the spiral to a halt against its own momentum, arresting the descent the paying-down alone could only deepen.

## Lever

An actor willing to do what the bust forbids.
Because only reflation or relief can stop the real debt climbing, and both run against the deep instinct to let the debt be paid and the bust clear, reflating works through the one hand that can create money and run deficits or force a write-down, so its lever is the nerve to act reckless-seeming and unfair-seeming for the one move that works.

## Echo

A spiral halted, at a cost borne against the grain.
The price level holds, the burden stops rising, the failures ease, and the price of it is loose money run against caution and debts forgiven against fairness, so what seemed the reckless thing is the recovery, bought by an actor willing to move against every instinct of the moment.
