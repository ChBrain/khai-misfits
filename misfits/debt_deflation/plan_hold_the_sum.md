# Plan: Hold the Sum

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the debt's seat](position_debt.md), inside [Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Debt](position_debt.md)

## Direction

Stay owed in dollars, unchanged in figure, whatever those dollars come to be worth. Demand the same number in good times and bad, indifferent to prices, neither rising nor falling with them. Hold the sum exactly.

## Orders

([The Debt](persona_the_debt.md) holds its fixed sum as prices fall, rising in real weight without a figure changing, and grows heavier the more the borrower pays.)

## Implementation

([The Ledger](place_the_ledger.md); the [deflating](process_deflating.md) process; [the debt](piece_the_debt.md) stays fixed in money while prices fall, so its real burden becomes [the weight](piece_the_weight.md) that rises.)

## Targets

- [ ] the sum stays owed in dollars, unchanged in figure
- [ ] the debt neither rises nor falls with the prices around it
- [ ] as prices fall, the real weight of the fixed sum rises
