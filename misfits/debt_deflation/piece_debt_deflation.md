# Piece: Debt-Deflation

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The phenomenon whole: an economy paying itself deeper into debt, and its remedy in a reflation the moment forbids.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Born at [the market](place_the_market.md) and [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), where selling drives prices down and the fixed debt grows heavier, and deepened at [the slump](place_the_slump.md).
Answered only at [the mint](place_the_mint.md), where the price level is lifted or the debt cut.

## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without the mechanism that binds them.
With it the whole trap stands in one object: a debt fixed in money, a distress sale that is the only way to pay, a deflation the selling drives, a real burden that rises as prices fall, a spiral that feeds itself, and a reflation that alone can halt it. Debt-Deflation is load bearing because it is the structure entire (Fisher): not a failure of discipline but its aggregation, each debtor right to pay down and the economy sunk by the paying, correctable only by a reflation that runs against every instinct of the bust.

## Apparent

A morality tale of profligacy, cured by everyone paying their debts and living within their means.
Seen from outside, debt-deflation looks like the wages of borrowing, to be answered by prudence and austerity, and the truth that the paying-down is what drives the spiral, that austerity in aggregate deepens the debt it means to clear, that the cure is the reflation prudence most condemns, stays hidden behind how plainly right paying your debts appears.

## Yearbook

The trap entire, staged across the run.
Debt-Deflation is [the distress sale](piece_the_distress_sale.md) that drives the deflation on [the debt](piece_the_debt.md), turning it into [the weight](piece_the_weight.md) and the weight into [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md), the descent that only [the reflation](piece_the_reflation.md), made against the instinct of the moment, can end.
