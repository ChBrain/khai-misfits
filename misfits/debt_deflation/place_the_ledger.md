# Place: The Ledger

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). Where the debt is recorded, fixed in money, and where its rising real weight is read.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The debt written in dollars, a fixed number that does not change as the world around it does.
Beside it the falling prices and shrinking asset values, so the same figure, unchanged, comes to represent an ever greater share of everything the borrower has and earns.

## Holds

The fixed nominal debt, and the paradox of its rising weight.
Here the sum stays constant while prices fall, so the ledger holds the still point around which the ruin turns: a figure that never moves yet grows heavier by the day, the exact place where the borrower's payments shrink the assets and leave the debt untouched.

## Offers

A clear account, and a cruel one.
Because the ledger records the debt in money and the money is worth more, it offers an honest reckoning that is also the trap laid bare: the debt undiminished though the borrower has sold and paid, the real burden risen though the figure held, so it shows plainly that the more was paid the more, in truth, is owed.

## Withheld

Any relief that prices alone could give.
The ledger cannot let a falling price level lighten the debt as it lightens all else, because the debt is fixed in money, so it withholds from the borrower the one mercy deflation grants everything else, holding the sum steady while the value of all that would pay it falls away.
