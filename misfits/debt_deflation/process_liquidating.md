# Process: Liquidating

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The selling of assets to raise cash to pay down debt, prudent for one and, done by all, the driver of the fall.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

Hard times over an indebted economy, and a turn to paying down.
At [the market](place_the_market.md), the debtor selling to raise cash and the creditor calling the shaky loan, both moving to reduce their exposure at once.

## Direction

Toward assets sold and prices driven down.
Each debtor liquidates to find the money to pay, and because paying means selling and all sell together, liquidating drives the assets onto a market with few buyers, so the sales that were meant to clear the debt push the price level down instead.

## Lever

The soundness of paying down and calling in.
Because paying a debt in a downturn is prudent and calling a shaky loan is a lender protecting itself, each act that feeds the selling is right for the one who makes it, so liquidating runs on individual good sense, the whole rush to safety made of moves no one can fault.

## Echo

Assets sold, cash raised, and prices falling.
The debtors have their money and the creditors their repayments, and the price of everything sold is lower for it, so what reads as a sound clearing of debt is, unseen, the start of the deflation that will make the remaining debt heavier.
