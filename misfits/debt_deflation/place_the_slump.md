# Place: The Slump

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). Where the spiral deepens, net worth and output and credit falling together as the real debt climbs.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The wider wreckage of the descent: firms failing as assets fall below their fixed debts, workers laid off, banks shrinking and breaking and lending no more.
Spending falling with confidence, prices falling with spending, and through all of it the real burden of the debt climbing, each turn feeding the next.

## Holds

The self-reinforcing loop, the whole economy caught in it.
Here the falling prices and the rising debt drive each other, so the slump holds the spiral entire: heavier debt forcing more distress and more selling, lower prices raising the debt again, the credit that might have broken the fall drying up as the banks that supplied it fail.

## Offers

A true measure of the descent, in failures and idle hands.
Because the slump is where the spiral shows in the real economy, it offers the plainest reckoning of the cost, the bankruptcies and the unemployment and the frozen credit, so it is where a paradox of prices and debt becomes ruined firms and workers out of work.

## Withheld

Any floor the paying-down can reach.
The slump cannot let the economy pay its way to the bottom and stop, because every payment drives the prices that raise the debt, so it withholds the resolution the debtors seek, deepening as they pay, and it withholds the credit that would arrest it, the lenders failing in the same descent.
