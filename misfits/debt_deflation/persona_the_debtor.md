# Persona: The Debtor

## Taxonomy

[Debtor](position_debtor.md). Pays down its debt by selling, prudently, and by selling drives the prices that raise the debt.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Responsible. Alarmed. Set on getting its house in order.
Carries debt into hard times and does the sound thing: sells what it can and pays the lender back.
No recklessness in it: only a household lightening its load the one way it knows.

## Action

Liquidates assets to raise the cash to pay down the debt.
Tightens the belt, hands the money back, and reduces the number it owes.
Does, for itself, exactly what prudence in a downturn demands.

## Shadow

Not profligate, prudent: the move is right for one and ruinous for all, because paying down means selling and a whole economy selling drives prices down.
Since the debt is fixed in money and the money grows dearer, the real weight of what remains rises even as it pays, so the harder it deleverages the heavier its burden, and its own sale is a fraction of the price fall that undoes it.
It is right to pay down, and the rightness, shared by all, is the trap.

## Tell

Says "times are hard, so I had better sell up and pay off my debt."
Does, and finds the debt heavier for everyone having said it.
