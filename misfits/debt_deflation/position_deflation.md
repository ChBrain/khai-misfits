# Position: Deflation

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The seat of the falling price level, the channel that turns paying-down into a heavier debt.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The general level of prices, pushed down as all sell at once and the money stock shrinks.
The power to make each dollar buy more, and so each dollar owed weigh more.
No will of its own, only the sum of a whole economy liquidating and hoarding.

## Orders

Drive prices down.
Fall across the board as the selling mounts and the loans are repaid and the money contracts.
Make cash dear and goods cheap, and the fixed debt heavy.

## Loses

Nothing itself, and it undoes everyone else.
Because a fall in prices is a rise in the real value of every fixed debt, and the paying-down drives the fall, the seat converts the economy's prudence into its ruin, and cannot help but feed the spiral, each fall prompting more distress and more selling and a further fall.
It is only a price level, and it does the whole of the damage.

## Drives

Toward prices falling and real debts rising together,
the channel between the debtor's effort and the debt's growth,
so the harder the economy pays, the lower the prices and the heavier the load.
