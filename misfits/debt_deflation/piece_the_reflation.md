# Piece: The Reflation

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The lift of the price level or the cut of the nominal debt that halts the spiral, the remedy at a cost the moment forbids.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the mint](place_the_mint.md), where money is loosened and deficits spent, or the debt is written down.
Set against [the slump](place_the_slump.md), whose every instinct argues to let the debt be paid and the bust clear.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play has no remedy, only a spiral that runs to the floor.
With it the misfit is answered, and answered at a price: raise the price level and the deflation that swells the debt is undone, or cut the nominal sum and the burden falls directly. The Reflation is load bearing because it is the one correction the structure allows and the hardest to make (Fisher): it means creating money and running deficits when caution screams the opposite, forgiving debts at the creditor's expense, and acting against the deep urge to let the bust clear itself, so the piece carries both the exit and the instinct that forbids it.

## Apparent

Recklessness, printing money and forgiving debts exactly when discipline is most called for.
Seen against the general paying-down, the reflation looks like the one irresponsible actor in a room of prudent ones, and the truth that it is the only move that stops the spiral, that its apparent recklessness is precisely the medicine the deflation needs, that letting the bust clear only sinks the economy further, stays hidden behind how right austerity feels in a downturn.

## Yearbook

Made by [the reflator](persona_the_reflator.md) through [reflating](process_reflating.md), the lift that halts the descent.
It is the exit and its cost: the Reflation is what raises the prices that had swelled [the debt](piece_the_debt.md), stops [the weight](piece_the_weight.md) climbing, and breaks [the spiral](piece_the_spiral.md), by the one act every instinct of the bust condemns.
