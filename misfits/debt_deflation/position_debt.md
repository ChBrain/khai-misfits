# Position: Debt

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The seat of the fixed nominal obligation, owed in a set sum whatever the money is worth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A number: a fixed sum of dollars, owed in full regardless of what those dollars buy.
Total indifference to prices: it neither rises nor falls with them in figure.
No stake in the world's fortunes, only the claim to be paid in money.

## Orders

Hold the sum.
Stay owed in dollars, unchanged in figure, whatever those dollars come to be worth.
Demand the same number in good times and bad.

## Loses

Nothing, and that is the trap: it cannot fall with the prices around it.
Because it is fixed in money while prices drop, the seat rises in real weight without a figure moving, gaining in goods and land exactly what deflation strips from them, so it grows heavier the more the borrower pays, the sale lowering all prices but its own.
It ends the bust larger in real terms than it began, having done nothing but stay still.

## Drives

Toward a burden that rises as prices fall,
fixed in money while the money grows dear,
so the one thing a deflation makes heavier is the debt everyone is straining to pay.
