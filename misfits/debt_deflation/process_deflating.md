# Process: Deflating

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The price level falling as all sell and money contracts, raising the real weight of every fixed debt.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A general liquidation and a shrinking money stock.
At [the market](place_the_market.md), the selling driving prices down, and at [the ledger](place_the_ledger.md), the fixed debt standing unchanged against them.

## Direction

Toward cheaper goods and a heavier debt.
As prices fall across the board, each dollar buys more, and because the debt is fixed in money, deflating drives its real weight up, so the same process that lowers the price of everything the borrower owns raises the true cost of everything the borrower owes.

## Lever

A debt fixed in money against a falling price level.
Because the debt does not fall with prices and everything else does, the gap between them opens as prices drop, so deflating works on the one asymmetry at the centre of the trap, converting a fall in prices directly into a rise in the real burden of the fixed sum.

## Echo

A borrower who has paid and yet owes more.
The prices are lower, the assets worth less, and the debt, unchanged in figure, heavier in truth, so what looks like the relief of cheaper everything is, for the debtor, the quiet growth of the one thing deflation does not cheapen.
