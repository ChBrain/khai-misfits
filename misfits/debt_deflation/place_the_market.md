# Place: The Market

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). Where the assets are sold to raise cash, and where the general selling drives the price level down.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Debtors bringing assets to sell, fields and stock and property offered to raise the cash to pay down debt.
Buyers scarce and cash dear, so each sale fetches less than the last, prices sliding as everyone sells at once and few will spend.

## Holds

The distress selling, and the deflation it drives.
Here the only way to pay meets the general rush to pay, so the market holds the point where prudence becomes deflation: each debtor's sale is sound alone, and the sum of the sales drives the price level down, so it is where paying-down turns into the falling prices that will raise the debt.

## Offers

Cash to whoever sells, at a falling price.
Because the market is where assets become the money to pay debts, it offers each debtor its escape route, the sale that raises the cash, so it hands out the means of paying down that, taken by all, lowers the price of everything sold and clears less debt with each transaction.

## Withheld

A price that holds.
The market cannot keep prices up while everyone sells and no one spends, so it withholds the value the debtors are counting on, letting each asset fetch less than it should, and being all sellers and few buyers it withholds the demand that would steady prices against the fixed debt.
