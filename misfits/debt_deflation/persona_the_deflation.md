# Persona: The Deflation

## Taxonomy

[Deflation](position_deflation.md). The falling price level a general liquidation drives, which raises the real weight of every fixed debt.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Pervasive. Quiet. Turning every price downward at once.
Is the general fall in prices as everyone sells to raise cash and the money stock shrinks with the loans repaid.
No agent behind it: only the sum of a whole economy selling, spending less, and hoarding what money it has.

## Action

Pushes prices down across the board as the selling mounts and the money contracts.
Makes each dollar buy more, so each dollar owed weighs more.
Turns the debtors' payments into their burden, lowering the price of everything they own and nothing they owe.

## Shadow

It is the mechanism that makes a virtue into a ruin, the channel through which prudence becomes catastrophe.
Because a fall in prices is a rise in the real value of fixed debt, and the paying-down is what drives the fall, deflation converts the economy's effort to lighten its load into the very thing that heavies it, and it feeds on itself, each fall prompting more distress and more selling and a further fall.
It is not the debt and not the debtor, but the price level between them, doing the whole of the damage.

## Tell

Says "everything is cheaper now," as if that were relief.
And makes the one thing that is not cheaper, the debt, heavier by exactly as much.
