# Position: Debtor

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The seat that pays down its debt by selling, prudent for one and ruinous for all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A debt fixed in money, and assets it can sell to raise the cash to pay it.
A sound reason to pay down: hard times, and the wish to owe no one.
No power over the price level, only over its own selling.

## Orders

Pay it down.
Sell what you can, hand the money to the lender, and reduce the sum you owe.
Get the house in order, the one way a household knows.

## Loses

The relief, exactly because all pay down at once.
Because paying means selling and a whole economy selling drives prices down, and the debt is fixed in money, the seat cannot lighten its load by a move that, shared by all, raises the real weight of the debt itself, so the harder it pays the more it owes.
It sells into a falling market and clears less of its debt than before it started.

## Drives

Toward a debt paid down by selling,
right for one household and crushing for all,
so the prudence that would free it alone sinks it among the prudent.
