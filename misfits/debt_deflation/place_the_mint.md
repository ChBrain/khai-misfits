# Place: The Mint

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). Where the price level can be lifted or the debt cut, the spiral halted and the cost of halting it met.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The levers of reflation: money loosened, deficits spent, the price level pushed back up, or the nominal debt written down and restructured.
The one actor willing to create money and run red in a bust, and around it the chorus of caution calling the move reckless while the real burden, at last, stops climbing.

## Holds

The remedy, and everything that makes it hard.
Here the spiral can be broken, so the mint holds the one move that answers the trap, the lift of prices or the cut of debt that stops the real weight rising, and holds with it all that resists the move: the dread of loose money, the creditor's loss, the deep instinct to let the debt be paid and the bust clear.

## Offers

A halted spiral, at the cost of caution and a creditor's loss.
Because the mint can act when debtors and creditors cannot, it offers the economy the reflation no private hand will supply and the relief no lender will grant itself, so it is where the descent is arrested, paid for in loose money run against instinct and debts forgiven against fairness.

## Withheld

A cure that looks prudent or costs no one.
The mint cannot make reflation feel safe or forgiveness feel fair, because both run against every instinct of the bust and land a loss on the lender, so it withholds the comfort of a costless fix, offering only a remedy that must be made to seem reckless to work, and paid for by someone.
