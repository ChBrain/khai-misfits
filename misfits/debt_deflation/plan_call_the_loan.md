# Plan: Call the Loan

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the creditor's seat](position_creditor.md), inside [Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Creditor](position_creditor.md)

## Direction

Guard the book as the market sours. Call in the loans you fear for, demand repayment or the collateral, and tighten what you lend. Protect what you are owed against borrowers that are straining and security that is falling.

## Orders

([The Creditor](persona_the_creditor.md) calls in the shaky loans and forces the borrower to sell into a falling market, tightening credit as its own capital thins.)

## Implementation

([The Ledger](place_the_ledger.md); the [liquidating](process_liquidating.md) process; calling [the debt](piece_the_debt.md) forces [the distress sale](piece_the_distress_sale.md), which lowers prices and sinks the creditor's own collateral.)

## Targets

- [ ] the loans feared for are called in, repayment or collateral demanded
- [ ] the borrower is forced to sell into a falling market
- [ ] credit is tightened as the creditor's capital thins
- [ ] the calling forces the selling that sours the market further
