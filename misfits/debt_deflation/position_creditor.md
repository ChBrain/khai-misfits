# Position: Creditor

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md). The seat that calls in the shaky loan to protect itself, and forces the selling that deepens the fall.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

Loans out against collateral that is falling, and borrowers that are straining.
The right to call the loan and demand repayment or the security behind it.
Capital of its own, thinning as the failures mount.

## Orders

Call the loan.
Demand repayment of the loans you fear for, take the collateral, and tighten what you lend.
Guard your book as the risk rises around you.

## Loses

Its security, exactly by the calling that was meant to protect it.
Because each loan it calls forces a sale that lowers prices, and lower prices sink the collateral behind its other loans and raise the real debt of the borrowers it still holds, the seat's prudence erodes the very safety it sought, one caller among many whose calling breaks them all.
It protects itself into a market too fallen to protect anyone.

## Drives

Toward a book guarded by calling the loans in,
sensible for one lender and collectively a vise,
so the self-protection of each creditor is the pressure that ruins them all.
