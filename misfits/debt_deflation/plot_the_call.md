# Plot: The Call

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ledger](place_the_ledger.md), hard times setting in over an economy carrying heavy debt.
The reckoning begins: creditors eyeing shaky loans, debtors resolving to pay down before it worsens.

## Action

[Liquidating](process_liquidating.md): [The Creditor](persona_the_creditor.md), holding [the creditor's seat](position_creditor.md), calls in the loans it fears for, while [The Debtor](persona_the_debtor.md), holding [the debtor's seat](position_debtor.md), moves to sell and settle.

## Stage

[The Creditor](persona_the_creditor.md), having tabled [Call the Loan](plan_call_the_loan.md), demands repayment of the loans it fears, guarding its book as the risk rises.
[The Debtor](persona_the_debtor.md), on [Pay It Down](plan_pay_it_down.md), resolves to sell what it can and clear its debt, and the whole economy turns at once to paying down.

## Tension

Everyone moves to safety, and the moving is what breaks them.
There is nothing to fault: the creditor is right to call a shaky loan and the debtor right to pay down in a downturn, each guarding itself as any prudent party would. But all turning to safety at once means all selling at once, so the plot closes with the economy resolved on the sound course of deleveraging, and the sale that will drive the prices down, unseen, about to begin.
