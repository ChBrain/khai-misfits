# Persona: The Creditor

## Taxonomy

[Creditor](position_creditor.md). Calls in the shaky loan to protect itself, and by calling forces the selling that deepens the fall.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Exposed. Watching its loans in a souring market.
Sees the collateral falling and the borrowers straining, and moves to protect what it is owed.
No cruelty in it: only a lender guarding its book as the risk rises around it.

## Action

Calls in the loans it fears for, and demands repayment or the collateral.
Forces the borrower to sell into a falling market to find the cash.
Tightens credit and lends less, as its own capital thins with the failures around it.

## Shadow

Its self-protection is the pressure that drives the distress selling and the deflation, and the deflation ruins its book anyway.
Because each loan it calls forces a sale that lowers prices, and lower prices raise the real debt and sink the collateral behind its other loans, the creditor's prudence undermines the very security it sought, so it protects itself into a worse position, one caller among many whose calling breaks them all.
It is right to guard its loan, and its guarding is the second half of the vise.

## Tell

Says "the market is turning, I must call this loan before it sours."
Calls, and the sale it forces sours the market further.
