# Persona: The Debt

## Taxonomy

[Debt](position_debt.md). The fixed nominal obligation, owed in a set number of dollars whatever those dollars come to be worth.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Fixed. Unmoved. Indifferent to the world's prices.
Is a set sum of money, owed in full whether that money buys much or little.
No malice in it: only a number on a ledger, holding exactly steady while everything around it moves.

## Action

Holds its sum as prices fall, so its real weight rises without a figure changing.
Stays owed in dollars while the dollars grow dear, gaining in goods and labour and land what deflation takes from them.
Grows heavier the more the borrower pays, because paying is selling and selling lowers the price of all but the debt.

## Shadow

It is the one thing in the economy that a falling price level makes larger.
Because it is fixed in money, every drop in prices is a rise in what it truly costs to clear, so the borrower's field halves in price and the loan against it does not shrink at all, the debt swelling in real terms precisely as the effort to pay it grows.
It is the still point around which the whole ruin turns.

## Tell

Says "I am owed in dollars, and dollars are what you must find."
And weighs more with every price that falls, though the number never moves.
