# Plot: The Fire Sale

## Taxonomy

[Debt-Deflation](play_debt_deflation.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Market](place_the_market.md), the only way to pay being to sell, and everyone selling at once.
Fields and stock and property brought to a market with few buyers and dear cash.

## Action

[Deflating](process_deflating.md): [The Debtor](persona_the_debtor.md), holding [the debtor's seat](position_debtor.md), liquidates its assets to raise cash, while [The Deflation](persona_the_deflation.md), holding [the deflation's seat](position_deflation.md), sets in as the general selling drives prices down.

## Stage

[The Debtor](persona_the_debtor.md), still on [Pay It Down](plan_pay_it_down.md), sells into the falling market to find the money, each sale fetching less than the last.
[The Deflation](persona_the_deflation.md), tabling [Drive Prices Down](plan_drive_prices_down.md), pushes prices lower across the board as all sell and the money contracts, making cash dear and goods cheap.

## Tension

The escape route is the trap: selling to pay is what lowers the price of everything sold.
There is no error here: selling to raise cash is the only way to pay, and each seller acts alone and reasonably. But a whole economy selling at once and few buying drives the price level down, so the plot closes with the debtors' cash raised, their assets sold for less than they hoped, and the deflation set in that will now turn on the one thing it cannot cheapen.
