# Position: Portfolio Director

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The top seat, accountable to the board.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The portfolio, the board's confidence, and the investment case.
Whoever holds this desk acts on the colour: funds, defends, and reports it upward as delivery.

## Orders

Protect the case. Report delivery. Keep the board confident.
The orders reward green, because red at this level reads as a threat to funding, and the seat is measured on the case staying fundable.

## Loses

Contact with the task, entirely.
By the time status reaches this desk it has been rounded green four times, so the seat cannot lose a red it was never shown.

## Drives

Toward a fundable, green portfolio.
The seat receives the green gratefully because green is what keeps the case alive, and acts on it as though it were the task, which it has never seen.
