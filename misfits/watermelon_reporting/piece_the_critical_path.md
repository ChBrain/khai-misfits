# Piece: The Critical Path

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The sideways axis: the red that travels task to task.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Along the floor of the play, desk to desk.
Not up the ladder: across it, from the seat that holds the red to the seat that takes the hand-off, and on.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is only a reporting chain.
With it there is a second axis, the one the work actually runs on, where a red task continues into the next because stopping costs whoever would have to stop.

## Apparent

Busy and on schedule.
It looks like momentum, a line that keeps moving, and the movement is exactly what hides that the float is gone and the path is already blown.

## Yearbook

It began when [The Doer](persona_the_doer.md) handed on rather than held.
It ran through [The Neighbour](persona_the_neighbour.md) and every desk after, each taking the input red and protecting its own dates.
It was red the whole way along, and only [the reckoning](process_reckoning.md) ever walked it end to end.
