# Place: The Stand-up

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). Where tasks roll up into a phase colour.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A short meeting, a board of tasks, a phase colour to be agreed.
The first room where the work becomes a status, and the last room where the Doer is in earshot.

## Holds

The rounding, in its earliest and most reasonable form.
Here a red task and a mitigation stand next to each other, and the phase is called amber before the Doer has finished the sentence.

## Offers

Control, demonstrated.
A place to show that the red is known, owned, and planned, which is what lets the phase report under control without reporting red.

## Withheld

A stop.
The room can round the colour but is not built to halt the phase, so the Doer's call for help is heard and then folded into a mitigation.
