# Position: Second Doer

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The next seat along the critical path, waiting on the hand-off.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A task of its own, and a dependency on the red one upstream.
Whoever holds this desk is accountable for their own colour, and their colour depends on the hand-off arriving.

## Orders

Take the input and keep going. Protect your own dates.
A stop upstream is a delay downstream, so the seat is ordered, in effect, to want the red to move rather than to hold.

## Loses

The standing it would need to second a stop.
The one desk positioned to back the Doer's call is the one desk whose own object argues against it.

## Drives

Toward its own task's progress.
The sideways momentum lives here: the red continues task to task not because anyone hides it, but because the next seat is paid to receive it and move.
