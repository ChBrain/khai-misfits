# Watermelon Reporting: Reference

The research warrant for [Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The
term is practitioner vocabulary from RAG and PMO practice; the mechanics the play
stages are grounded in the reporting-bias literature cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging watermelon reporting as a **systemic system**: an incentive gradient that
greens a status on the way up a hierarchy while the work stays red along the
critical path. The play models the documented mechanics, the mum effect, optimistic
biasing, strategic misrepresentation, and the deaf effect, as the load-bearing
vectors of the structure, not as a morality tale about dishonest people. The
governing finding of the source literature is the play's governing law: the bias is
overwhelmingly one-directional and driven by incentives, not by error.

## Origin

The phenomenon in practice, and the research spine beneath it.

| Source                      | Key Work / Event                                                                                                                                                                                                       | Scope                                                                                                                                                                    |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Rosen & Tesser**          | "On Reluctance to Communicate Undesirable Information: The MUM Effect", _Sociometry_ (1970); field study, _Psychological Reports_ (1971)                                                                               | Origin of the **mum effect**: senders are reluctant to transmit bad news to a recipient, even when not at fault. The pump beneath every upward rounding.                 |
| **Smith & Keil**            | "The Reluctance to Report Bad News on Troubled Software Projects", _Information Systems Journal_ (2003); Smith, Keil & Depledge, _JMIS_ (2001)                                                                         | The mum effect inside IS project status reporting: why a red status is withheld or softened as it climbs.                                                                |
| **Snow, Keil & Wallace**    | "The Effects of Optimistic and Pessimistic Biasing on Software Project Status Reporting", _Information & Management_ (2007)                                                                                            | Status reports are biased a majority of the time, and the bias is roughly twice as likely to be optimistic as pessimistic: the green rounding, measured.                 |
| **Cuellar, Keil & Johnson** | "The Deaf Effect Response to Bad News Reporting in Information Systems Projects", _e-Service Journal_ (2006)                                                                                                           | The **deaf effect**: receivers discount the red even when it is reported, so escalation runs on. The reason a raised red still does not stop the line.                   |
| **Flyvbjerg**               | "Delusion and Deception in Large Infrastructure Projects" (Flyvbjerg, Garbuio & Lovallo, _California Management Review_, 2009); "Top Ten Behavioral Biases in Project Management", _Project Management Journal_ (2021) | Separates **optimism bias** (cognitive) from **strategic misrepresentation** (incentive-driven understatement of cost and risk). The incentive account the play runs on. |
| **RAG / PMO practice**      | "Watermelon" status in Red-Amber-Green reporting (practitioner literature)                                                                                                                                             | The naming: a status green on the skin and red in the flesh, rounded green at each hand-off up the chain.                                                                |

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses
  to cast any seat as a liar, because the source finding is precisely that the
  rounding is individually defensible and nobody has to lie. The redness really is
  "because of X"; the misfit is that sorting X is always another seat's job.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into virtue. The skin splits
  only when a payoff flips (revenge, or an incoming regime with no sunk cost), so the
  reckoning is the next incentive winning, not honesty arriving.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and
  attributed here, never claimed as the house's. The staging is the house's; the
  findings are theirs.

## Encoding

Source to constraint, per element.

- **the Plans** (`plan_*.md`): carry the incentive gradient, one standing plan per
  seat, from the Doer who alone is served by stopping to the seats above and alongside
  who are each served by moving on (Flyvbjerg's incentive account; the mum effect).
- **[The External Problem](piece_the_external_problem.md)**: carries strategic
  misrepresentation and the mum effect together, the defensible "red because of X"
  that lets a seat green its own column truthfully.
- **[The Colour Travels](piece_the_colour_travels.md)**: carries the measured optimistic
  rounding (Snow, Keil & Wallace), one notch per rung, no rung lying outright.
- **[The Red Task](piece_the_red_task.md)**: carries the honest colour at the source, the
  ground truth every rung above holds a rounding of, red only at the workface.
- **[The Mitigated Amber](piece_the_mitigated_amber.md)**: carries the first notch (the
  mum effect, softened not withheld): red reframed as amber with a plan, control reported
  where the task has only a mitigation.
- **[The Rounded Green](piece_the_rounded_green.md)**: carries the second notch: amber
  turned green with the red filed in the register, the status and the truth kept in two
  documents that different people read.
- **[The Net Green](piece_the_net_green.md)**: carries the third notch: one red averaged
  into a healthy aggregate, counted honestly at a level where it cannot move the number.
- **[The Critical Path](piece_the_critical_path.md)**: carries the sideways axis, work
  continuing task to task past a blown path, that the reporting literature leaves
  implicit.
- **[The Cleaner](persona_the_cleaner.md)** and [Clean House](plot_clean_house.md):
  carry the reversed incentive and the deaf effect broken, exposure once it finally pays.
- **[The Watermelon](piece_the_watermelon.md)**: carries the phenomenon itself, green
  skin over red flesh, held whole by nobody.
