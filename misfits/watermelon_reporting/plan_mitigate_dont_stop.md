# Plan: Mitigate, Don't Stop

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the phase lead's seat](position_phase_lead.md), inside [Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Phase Lead](position_phase_lead.md)

## Direction

Keep the phase under control and on schedule.
Control is the object, and a mitigation demonstrates control where a stop would confess its loss.

## Orders

[The Mitigator](persona_the_mitigator.md) reports the phase amber, attaches a plan with an owner and a date, and rounds the red task into a managed risk.

## Implementation

[The stand-up](place_the_stand_up.md); the [roll-up](process_roll_up.md) process; [the mitigated amber](piece_the_mitigated_amber.md) as the phase's reported colour.

## Targets

- [ ] the red task is named as a risk, not a status
- [ ] a mitigation with owner and date is attached
- [ ] the phase reports amber, under control
- [ ] the external problem carries the colour: the phase is red because of the dependency, not the phase
