# Persona: The Cleaner

## Taxonomy

[Incoming Director](position_incoming_director.md). The one seat whose incentive points at the red.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Sharp and unsentimental.
Wears two faces of the same incentive: the insider passed over, holding a grievance, and the newcomer arriving with no sunk cost in the green.
For both, the red is not a threat. It is an asset.

## Action

Cleans the house.
Reads the fragments together in daylight, names the red the chain rounded away, and refuses to green it, because exposing the inherited red resets the baseline and damns whoever built it.

## Shadow

Not repair, the next incentive winning.
Runs the same pump as everyone else, pointed the other way: where every seat below said red because of X, the Cleaner says red because of them. The red finally travels because holding it now pays.

## Tell

Says "I have reviewed the position and it was never green."
Dates the discovery carefully, so the line between the old regime's red and the new regime's green falls exactly on the day they arrived.
