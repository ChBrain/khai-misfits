# Process: Reckoning

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The station after the split, where the fragments are read together.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A payoff that flips.
The moment a seat arrives, or is passed over, for whom exposing the red pays more than grinding it green, the review begins.

## Direction

Backward, and downward.
It runs the ladder in reverse: pulls the green tile, opens the register, reads the phase and the task, and traces the colour back to the workface it came from.

## Lever

A reversed incentive.
The force is the same pump every seat below used, pointed the other way: red because of them, so the more red surfaced, the cleaner the incoming slate.

## Echo

A whole, read in daylight, too late to un-spend the float.
It hands a reckoning back and resets the baseline, which is exposure and not repair, and then the meter is free to start again.
