# Process: Aggregation

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The station where projects aggregate into a programme.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A project tile joining a grid of others.
The moment a project is counted among many, its colour becomes a fraction of a larger number.

## Direction

Upward, and diluted.
It moves from project to programme by averaging, so a single red is netted against many greens until it no longer moves the whole.

## Lever

Scale.
The force is the true fact that the programme is bigger than any project; the resistance is that the same fact makes the red stop counting.

## Echo

A programme that is on track by the balance of its parts.
It hands the aggregate upward and recasts the red as one managed issue, honest at its level and blind to the level below.
