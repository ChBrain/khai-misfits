# Pitch: Reassuring Green

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Confident, competent, unbothered.
Every dashboard is green and every green is defended with a reason.
The voice of a programme that has no reason to sound worried, spoken by people who each have a reason not to.

## Undertow

Under the green, a meter running.
Each rounding is small and each rounding is fair, and they only ever round one way.
The colour never breaks; the gap between skin and flesh only widens.

## Nerve

The feeling of being shown a board of green
by people who are not lying,
while something on the critical path keeps not moving.

## Echo

After the run: a milestone that misses all at once,
a flesh that was red the whole time,
and a chain of reports in which no single line was false.
