# Position: Project Manager

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The rung that owns the project RAG tile.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The project, the status tile, and the risk register.
Whoever holds this desk decides what colour the project reports and where the red is filed if it is not on the tile.

## Orders

Deliver the project. Report status. Manage risk.
The orders let a risk live in the register while the tile reads green, so the seat can be compliant on all three at once.

## Loses

Nothing it is measured on, and the accuracy of the tile.
The seat keeps its autonomy by staying green, and what it gives up, the difference between a logged risk and a handled one, is not on its scorecard.

## Drives

Toward reporting on track.
A red tile invites intervention and the loss of control, so green is the seat's protection of its own hands on its own work.
