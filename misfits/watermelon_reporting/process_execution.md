# Process: Execution

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The sideways station: task to task along the critical path.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A task that goes red with its float already spent.
The moment the slack is gone and the work is not done, the choice arrives: stop the line, or hand on and continue.

## Direction

Sideways, and onward.
It moves task to task, not up: the red does not climb here, it travels along, from the seat that holds it to the seat that takes the hand-off.

## Lever

Momentum against correctness.
The force is everyone's need to keep their own dates; the resistance is the one Doer asking to stop, outweighed by every neighbour paid to keep going.

## Echo

A critical path that keeps moving with the red still inside it.
It hands the work forward and the colour upward, and continues despite the path, because stopping costs the ones who would have to stop.
