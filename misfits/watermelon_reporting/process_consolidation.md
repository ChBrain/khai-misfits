# Process: Consolidation

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The station where the phase becomes a project RAG tile.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An amber phase reaching the project status.
The moment the phase must be rendered as a single tile, the red needs somewhere to live that is not the tile.

## Direction

Upward, and apart.
It moves amber to green on the tile, and the red sideways into the risk register, splitting one status into a colour that is read and a record that is not.

## Lever

The gap between two documents.
The force is the seat's need to hold its autonomy; the resistance is only a reader who would open the tile and the register together, and that reader is not in the room.

## Echo

A green tile with a red register behind it.
It hands the green upward and keeps the red on file, true and unread, protecting the project from the intervention a red tile would invite.
