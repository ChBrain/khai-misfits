# Piece: The External Problem

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The pump: the move that turns owned red into someone else's.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Every rung, at the moment of rounding.
It has no fixed desk: it is the sentence each seat says to hand its colour on. It is not really red ... it is red because of X.

## Load Bearing

Without it no rounding would hold.
With it every seat can green its own status honestly, because the redness has been assigned to an X in another column, and the whole ladder is built from these assignments laid end to end.

## Apparent

True, which is the danger.
There really is a late dependency, a vendor, a missing input. The X is almost always real, so naming it is not a lie, and sorting it is always someone else's job.

## Yearbook

It was spoken first at [the stand-up](place_the_stand_up.md) and repeated at every rung above.
It sent the red circulating, task to X's owner to their X, instead of stopping.
At [the reckoning](process_reckoning.md) it was run in reverse, red because of them, and for once the pump served exposure instead of concealment.
