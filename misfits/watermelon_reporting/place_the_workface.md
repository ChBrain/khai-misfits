# Place: The Workface

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). Where the task is red and the critical path runs.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The task itself, open on the desk.
Not a tile, not a colour: the actual work, blocked or breaking, with the float already spent and the next task waiting.

## Holds

The true colour, and the float that is gone.
Every status the ladder will round begins here as a fact, before anyone rounds it.

## Offers

The one honest reading in the play.
Here, and only here, the red is red without a mitigation attached, because the work does not care what colour it was reported.

## Withheld

Any power to stop the chain.
The workface can see the red perfectly and cannot halt the line above it, so the truest place is also the most powerless.
