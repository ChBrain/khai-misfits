# Persona: The Doer

## Taxonomy

[Task Owner](position_task_owner.md). The one seat that actually touches the red.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Honest and a little tired.
Knows exactly how red the task is, because the task is on the desk and will not move.
The only person in the play who gains nothing from the colour looking better than it is.

## Action

Flags the red, asks for help, and keeps working the task anyway.
Says the true colour once, into a stand-up that has already decided the phase is amber, and then goes back to grinding because the work still has to happen.

## Shadow

Punished for candour.
To own the red is to be the red: the seat that reports it becomes the problem the chain names. So the safest honest move is to hand the colour a reason, an X, and let it travel as somebody else's.

## Tell

Says "it is red, and here is what I need" to a room that hears only the second half of a mitigation.
When nothing comes, stops saying red and starts saying blocked.
