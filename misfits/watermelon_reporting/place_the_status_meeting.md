# Place: The Status Meeting

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). Where the phase becomes a project RAG tile.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A slide with a coloured tile, a risk register open in another tab.
The room where amber is turned to green and the red is given somewhere else to live.

## Holds

The tile and the register, kept apart.
The tile is what is reported and the register is where the red is filed, and the whole art of the room is that the two are read by different people.

## Offers

A clean status and a place to put the mess.
The register lets the seat log the red honestly while the tile reports green honestly, so both can be true and the reader sees only one.

## Withheld

A reader who opens both.
The room offers everything except the one person who reads the tile and the register together, and without that person the green stands.
