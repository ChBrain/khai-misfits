# Plot: The Net Green

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The programme board](place_the_programme_board.md), then [the portfolio review](place_the_portfolio_review.md).
The green reaches the top. A grid of tiles, mostly green, one amber, none red, and a board waiting to be reassured.

## Action

[Aggregation](process_aggregation.md): [The Aggregator](persona_the_aggregator.md), holding [the programme manager's seat](position_programme_manager.md), nets the one red against many greens.
Then [portfolio reporting](process_portfolio_reporting.md): [The Reassured](persona_the_reassured.md) at [the portfolio director's seat](position_portfolio_director.md) presents delivery on track and signs it off.

## Stage

[The Aggregator](persona_the_aggregator.md) runs [Net It Out](plan_net_it_out.md) and hands up [the net green](piece_the_net_green.md): the red averaged until it disappears.
[The Reassured](persona_the_reassured.md) runs [Keep the Case Green](plan_keep_the_case_green.md): receives the green, protects the case, and makes it official.

## Tension

The aggregate is confirmed and keeping it green is rewarded.
Nobody in this room has seen [the red task](piece_the_red_task.md), and the colour they are signing is the sum of four roundings. The check checks the skin, and the skin is green.
