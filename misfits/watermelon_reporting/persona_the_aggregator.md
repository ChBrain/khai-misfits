# Persona: The Aggregator

## Taxonomy

[Programme Manager](position_programme_manager.md). The rung where one red is netted against many greens.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Strategic and unflappable.
Reads a portfolio of projects, not a task, and the portfolio is mostly green.
Sees the red, and sees that in aggregate it does not move the number.

## Action

Nets it out.
Reports the programme by the balance of its projects, so a single red project becomes a managed issue inside a healthy programme, true at the level the seat is accountable for and blind to the level below.

## Shadow

The programme's object is the aggregate, and the aggregate is green.
So the seat is not hiding the red: it is correctly reporting a whole in which the red has been averaged until it disappears.

## Tell

Says "one project needs watching, the programme is on track."
Speaks in portfolios when asked about a task, and in tasks when asked to spare a resource.
