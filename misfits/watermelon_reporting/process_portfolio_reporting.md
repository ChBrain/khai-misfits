# Process: Portfolio Reporting

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The top station: programme into portfolio, and out to the board.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A programme colour reaching the top of the ladder.
The moment the status must be reported to the board, it is already the sum of every rounding below.

## Direction

Upward, and out.
It moves the green from programme to portfolio and then to the board as delivery, the furthest point from the task and the point where the colour is acted on.

## Lever

The investment case.
The force is the need to keep the portfolio fundable; the resistance is that red at this level threatens the funding, so green is received gratefully and passed on.

## Echo

A board reassured by a green it has never traced to a task.
It hands delivery outward and makes the green official, so the split, when it comes, arrives here last and largest.
