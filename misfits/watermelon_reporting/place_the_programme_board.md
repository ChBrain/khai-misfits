# Place: The Programme Board

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). Where projects aggregate into a programme.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A grid of project tiles, mostly green, one amber, none red.
The room where a single project's colour becomes a fraction of a larger number.

## Holds

The aggregate.
Here the red is not hidden, it is diluted: counted honestly at a level where one project cannot move the whole, and so counted into invisibility.

## Offers

Perspective.
A place to say that the programme is bigger than any one project, which is true, and which is exactly what lets the red stop mattering.

## Withheld

The task.
The board sees projects, never tasks, so the red at the workface is two levels below anything this room is built to display.
