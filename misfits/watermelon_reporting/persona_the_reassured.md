# Persona: The Reassured

## Taxonomy

[Portfolio Director](position_portfolio_director.md). The top rung, where the colour is only ever green.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Confident and accountable upward.
Carries the portfolio to the board and the investment case with it.
Has never seen the red, because by the time the status reaches this desk it has been rounded green four times.

## Action

Presents green and is reassured by it.
Protects the case, because red at this level threatens funding, and threatened funding threatens the whole portfolio, so the seat has every reason to receive the green gratefully and pass it on.

## Shadow

The furthest seat from the task and the one that acts on the colour.
Signs off on a green that is the sum of four roundings, and the signature makes the green official the way the budget made the decision official in the chain next door.

## Tell

Says "delivery is on track" to the board, holding a tile, not a task.
When the milestone misses, is the most surprised person in the building, and the most honestly so.
