# Persona: The Neighbour

## Taxonomy

[Second Doer](position_second_doer.md). The next task along the critical path.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Busy and reasonable.
Has a task of their own and a hand-off due from the seat that is red.
Wants the line to keep moving, because a stop upstream lands as a delay downstream, on them.

## Action

Takes the hand-off the moment it comes, whatever colour it came in.
Would rather receive a red input and start than wait for a green one that never arrives, so the sideways momentum continues past the point where anyone should stop.

## Shadow

The one seat that could second the Doer's call for a stop, and the one seat paid not to.
Their own object is served by the red moving on, so they carry it forward instead of holding it still.

## Tell

Says "just send me what you have, I will work around it" and means it kindly.
Pre-loads the next report: if my part slips, it slipped because upstream came late.
