# Piece: The Watermelon

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The accumulated whole: green skin, red flesh.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

The gap between the two axes.
[The colour travels](piece_the_colour_travels.md) green up the ladder; [the critical path](piece_the_critical_path.md) runs red along the floor. The Watermelon is the standing distance between them.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play is a series of fair roundings and a busy schedule.
With it those are one object: a status green on the skin and red in the flesh, held whole by nobody, because the seat that reports the skin is never the seat that walks the flesh.

## Apparent

Green, until it is not.
From the top it is a healthy portfolio. From the workface it is a red task. It is both, and the two readings only ever meet when the skin splits.

## Yearbook

The ladder held the skin: [the mitigated amber](piece_the_mitigated_amber.md), [the rounded green](piece_the_rounded_green.md), [the net green](piece_the_net_green.md), each true at its rung.
The critical path held the flesh, red desk to desk the whole way.
[The Cleaner](persona_the_cleaner.md) was the first to cut it open, and read together what the chain had only ever read apart. The flesh had been red the whole time. Nobody had a word for the fruit until it split.
