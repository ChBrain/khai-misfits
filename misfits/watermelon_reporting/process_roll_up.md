# Process: Roll-up

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The first station up the ladder: task into phase.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A red task arriving at the stand-up as a status.
The moment the work becomes a colour to be agreed, the rounding begins.

## Direction

Upward, one notch.
It moves from task to phase, and in the move the red is turned to amber, because a phase with a mitigation reports under control.

## Lever

Reasonableness.
The force is the mitigation, an owner and a date attached, which makes the amber feel earned rather than rounded; the resistance is a Doer already out of earshot.

## Echo

A phase colour that is calmer than any task beneath it.
It hands the amber upward and files the red as a risk, and the first rounding is the one nobody notices.
