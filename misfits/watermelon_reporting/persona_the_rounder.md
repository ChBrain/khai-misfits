# Persona: The Rounder

## Taxonomy

[Project Manager](position_project_manager.md). The rung where amber becomes green.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Polished and in control.
Owns the RAG tile the project reports up, and knows what a red tile costs: attention, intervention, a loss of the autonomy that green buys.

## Action

Rounds amber to green with actions logged.
Moves the red off the status and into the risk register, where it becomes an assumption owned elsewhere, so the tile can read green and the register can hold the truth nobody reads.

## Shadow

A project that reports red invites a hand on the wheel.
So green is not optimism, it is self-defence: the colour protects the seat's control of its own work, and the control is worth more to the seat than the accuracy.

## Tell

Says "green, with risks being actively managed."
Points to the register when pressed, as if a logged risk were a handled one.
