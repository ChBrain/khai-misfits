# Position: Task Owner

## Taxonomy

[Watermelon Reporting](play_watermelon_reporting.md). The seat on the critical path, holding the red.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The task, the tools, and the true colour.
Whoever holds this desk is the only one who knows, first-hand and without rounding, how red the work is.

## Orders

Deliver the task. Flag the risk. Keep the critical path moving.
The orders point two ways at once: tell the truth about the colour, and do not be the reason the line stops.

## Loses

Leverage, and the safety of being honest.
The seat can report red but cannot compel a stop, and reporting red marks it as the owner of the problem, so candour costs it more than it buys.

## Drives

Toward the task actually working.
This is the one seat whose interest is genuinely served by stopping and fixing, and the one seat with no power to make the chain above it stop.
