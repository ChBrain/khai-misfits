# Persona: The Shopper

## Taxonomy

[Shopper](position_shopper.md). The end demand, buying near as ever, the small wobble that starts the swing.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ordinary. Steady. Barely changed at all.
Buys what it needs, a little more this week than last for reasons of its own, then settles back.
No drama in it: the true demand, small and calm, moving only a touch.

## Action

Buys at the counter, drawing the shelf down a shade further than usual.
Registers, without meaning to, as a single order slightly larger than the week before.
Returns, next week, to about what it always bought.

## Shadow

It is the one demand the whole chain is meant to serve and the one demand no link above the counter ever sees.
Because its small real wobble is visible only at the till, and passes as quickly as it came, everything the chain does above is a response to something other than it, so the calm true signal is drowned by the swing built to chase it.
It moved a little, once, and had already moved back before the factory heard.

## Tell

Says "I will take a couple more than usual this week."
And is read, four links up, as a market on the march.
