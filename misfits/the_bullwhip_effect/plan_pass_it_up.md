# Plan: Pass It Up

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the signal's seat](position_signal.md), inside [The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Signal](position_signal.md)

## Direction

Carry each link's order up to the next as the only evidence of demand there is. Deliver the number and not the reason, a step behind the demand that made it, so each stage reads its neighbour's cover as the market and adds its own on top. Grow at every hand-off.

## Orders

([The Signal](persona_the_signal.md) carries each order upward, drops its cause on the way, and swells at each stage as the cover of one link becomes the demand of the next.)

## Implementation

([The Line](place_the_line.md); the [amplifying](process_amplifying.md) process; [the lag](piece_the_lag.md) delivers each order late and stripped of context, so the distortion compounds into [the whip](piece_the_whip.md).)
