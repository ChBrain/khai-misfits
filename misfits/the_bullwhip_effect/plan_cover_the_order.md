# Plan: Cover the Order

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the retailer's seat](position_retailer.md), inside [The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Retailer](position_retailer.md)

## Direction

Keep the shelf stocked and guard against being caught short. When the shelf draws down, order enough to refill it and a cushion besides, safety stock against the next rise and the pipeline it takes to restock. Order the demand you saw plus a cover, so a rush never finds you empty.

## Orders

([The Retailer](persona_the_retailer.md) orders the wobble plus a cushion, unable to tell a blip from a trend, and sends up an order larger than the demand it saw.)

## Implementation

([The Line](place_the_line.md); the [ordering](process_ordering.md) process; [the cover](piece_the_cover.md) added on top of the demand makes the order larger than its cause, a distortion carried by [the lag](piece_the_lag.md).)

## Targets

- [ ] the shelf is refilled and a cushion ordered against the next rise
- [ ] the cover is added on top of the demand actually seen
- [ ] the order sent up is larger than the wobble that prompted it
- [ ] the sensible margin enters the chain as the next link's demand
