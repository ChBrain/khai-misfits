# Process: Whipping

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The top of the chain lurching glut to shortage, chasing on a lag a swing already reversed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An amplified order reaching the plant and a lag between acting and its effect.
At [the warehouse](place_the_warehouse.md), the factory ramping to the surge it was sent and then cutting when the surge turns out to be gone.

## Direction

Toward an overshoot each way in turn.
Because the order is the most amplified in the chain and arrives after the demand that made it has reversed, whipping drives the plant to run flat out into a surge already past and idle into a recovery already coming, so each correction lands late and swings the stock from glut to shortage.

## Lever

A correction applied on delayed information.
Because the factory acts on a signal a beat behind the world, like a hand reaching for a tap that answers late, its every adjustment is aimed at a state that has already changed, so whipping works on the lag itself, turning each sensible response into an overshoot in the opposite direction.

## Echo

A plant whipsawing on a wobble long since gone.
Overtime then idle, warehouse full then bare, the tip of the whip cracking hardest and last, so the stage furthest from the shopper pays the largest cost for the smallest real change, its swings all chasing a demand that settled weeks ago.
