# Place: The Clearinghouse

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). Where the true demand is pooled and shared, the loop shortened so every stage sees the shopper, and the cost of it met.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The shopper's real sales laid open to the whole chain: point-of-sale figures every link can read, orders smoothed, lags cut.
Firms that once saw only their own customer now looking at the same true demand, and each weighing whether to trust it over its own shelves.

## Holds

The remedy, and the whole price of it.
Here the shared signal is made, so the clearinghouse holds the one thing that stills the whip, every stage reacting to the wobble instead of the stage below, and holds with it what the fix costs: the open books, the surrendered cover, the trust in a partner's data over one's own stock.

## Offers

A chain that reacts to the shopper, at the cost of each link's private cover.
Because the clearinghouse gives every stage the true demand, it offers the amplification's cure, the distortion drained and the swing stilled, so it is where the wave made of local caution can be undone by a common view.

## Withheld

Any calm that keeps itself.
The clearinghouse can pool the signal and cannot make each firm keep sharing, because the next wobble tempts every link back to guarding its own shelf, so it withholds a self-sustaining peace, handing the chain a calm that holds only as long as the books stay open.
