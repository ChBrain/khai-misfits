# Process: Sharing

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The true demand opened to every stage, the loop shortened so each reacts to the wobble, the remedy at its cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A chain swinging on itself and one who moves to give it a common view.
At [the clearinghouse](place_the_clearinghouse.md), the sharer pooling the shopper's real sales and shortening the lags so every link can read them.

## Direction

Toward a chain that reacts to the shopper and not to itself.
Because the swing is fed by each stage seeing only the stage below, sharing drives the true demand to every link at once, so each can order to the wobble rather than to its neighbour's cover, draining the distortion and stilling the whip that the closed, lagged signal had built.

## Lever

A signal held in common against the pull of private cover.
Because the fix works only if every stage shares and keeps sharing, and each link is tempted by the next wobble to guard its own shelf again, sharing runs on trust maintained across firms, the open book chosen over the safe buffer, so its lever is a coordination held against every local reason to break it.

## Echo

A swing stilled, on a calm that must be kept.
The distortion drains, the orders smooth, the plant steadies to the shopper's real demand, and the whole of it rests on books that stay open, so what sharing achieves is real and provisional, the whip stilled for exactly as long as the chain keeps its common view.
