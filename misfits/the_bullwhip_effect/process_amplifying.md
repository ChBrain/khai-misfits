# Process: Amplifying

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The distortion compounding stage by stage, each link's cover becoming the demand the next link amplifies again.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A swollen order arriving as the only demand the next link sees.
At [the line](place_the_line.md), the signal climbing from stage to stage, each link reading its neighbour's order as the market.

## Direction

Toward a swing that grows at every stage.
Because the order each link receives already carries the cover of the one below, and each adds its own cover on top before passing it up, amplifying drives the distortion larger at every hand-off, a small swing at the shop becoming a bigger one at the distributor and a wild one at the plant.

## Lever

Each stage taking the stage below for the market.
Because no link sees the shopper, only its own customer's orders, each treats the swollen figure it receives as true demand and covers against it in turn, so amplifying works by the same prudence applied at every stage to a signal that is already the last stage's caution, compounding it upward.

## Echo

A wild order at the top from a wobble at the bottom.
Each stage has passed on something larger than it received, so by the top of the chain the demand has swelled out of all proportion to the shopper, and the signal that began as a small real change arrives at the factory as a wave.
