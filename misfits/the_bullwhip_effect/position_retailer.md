# Position: Retailer

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The seat that orders to cover its shelf, the same at every stage of the chain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A shelf to keep stocked and a customer whose orders are the only demand it sees.
A cover to add: safety stock against a stockout, and the pipeline it takes to refill.
No sight of the shopper if it is not the shop, only the stage below.

## Orders

Cover the order.
Refill the shelf, guard against the next rise, and order the demand you saw plus a cushion.
Never be the link caught short when the customer comes.

## Loses

The true size of the signal, exactly by covering itself.
Because it adds a buffer it cannot separate from the demand, and sends the sum up as the only news the next link gets, the seat cannot pass on the wobble alone, only the wobble swollen by its own caution, so its prudence enters the chain as demand.
It protects its shelf and enlarges the swing by the very same order.

## Drives

Toward an order larger than the demand that prompted it,
prudent in every link and amplifying at every link,
so the sensible cover of each stage is the market the stage above must chase.
