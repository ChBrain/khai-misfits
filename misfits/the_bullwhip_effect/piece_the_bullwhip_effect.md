# Piece: The Bullwhip Effect

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The phenomenon whole: a chain of prudent orders amplifying a wobble into a wave, and its remedy in a shared signal at the cost of local cover.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Born at [the counter](place_the_counter.md), grown up [the line](place_the_line.md), and landed at [the warehouse](place_the_warehouse.md).
Answered only at [the clearinghouse](place_the_clearinghouse.md), where the shared signal drains the swing.

## Load Bearing

Without it the other pieces are parts without the mechanism that binds them.
With it the whole trap stands in one object: a wobble at the till, a cover added at each stage, a lagged and filtered signal, an amplification compounding upward, and a whip at the top from a demand already gone. The Bullwhip Effect is load bearing because it is the structure entire (Forrester; Lee, Padmanabhan and Whang): not a failure of any link but the sum of every link's local good sense seen from no single vantage, correctable only by a shared view that costs each stage the cover it trusted.

## Apparent

A demand problem, a market too volatile for the chain to keep up with.
Seen from inside, the effect looks like the world heaving and the chain merely reacting, and the truth that the demand barely moved, that the chain amplified its own caution through lag and closed books, that the cure is a shared signal no link will freely surrender its buffer for, stays hidden behind how wild the orders look at the top and how prudent each one felt at its own stage.

## Yearbook

The trap entire, staged across the run.
The Bullwhip Effect is [the wobble](piece_the_wobble.md) swollen by [the cover](piece_the_cover.md) at every stage, carried up on [the lag](piece_the_lag.md) into [the whip](piece_the_whip.md), and stilled only by [the open book](piece_the_open_book.md) that gives every stage the shopper it never saw.
