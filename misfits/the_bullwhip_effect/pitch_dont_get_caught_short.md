# Pitch: Don't Get Caught Short

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Careful, well-run, covering its bases.
The steady good sense of keeping the shelves stocked and ordering a little extra so a rush never leaves you empty-handed.
Nothing rash in it: only a link doing its job, guarding against the shortfall that would cost it the sale.

## Undertow

Under the caution, an order larger than the demand that prompted it.
Because each link covers the rise it saw and a cushion besides, and sends that swollen order up as the only signal the next link gets, the cover of one becomes the demand of another, and the caution compounds stage by stage into a swing no one ordered.
The prudence that protects each link is the amplification that whipsaws them all.

## Nerve

The feeling of ordering a bit more than you strictly need, just to be safe,
sure it is only sensible, because a stockout would be worse,
while every link above you reads your safety margin as the market and adds a margin of its own.

## Echo

After the run: a factory swinging from overtime to idle on a wobble the till forgot weeks ago,
gluts then shortages, warehouses full then bare, and no one anywhere who panicked or gamed,
only each link covering itself, and a shared signal that would have stilled it all, held back behind every closed book.
