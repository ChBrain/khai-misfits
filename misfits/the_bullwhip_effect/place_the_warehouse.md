# Place: The Warehouse

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). Where the amplified swing lands as stock, glut then shortage, the whip read in full and empty shelves.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

Inventory lurching: aisles packed to the rafters when the surge is chased, then stripped bare when it reverses.
The plant running overtime to fill the warehouse, then standing idle as it drains, the swing of the whole chain made visible in pallets and gaps.

## Holds

The cost of the swing, counted in stock.
Here the amplification stops being a number in an order and becomes goods on a floor, so the warehouse holds the whipsaw made physical: the overstock bought chasing a surge already gone, and the stockout left when the cut lands after demand returns, the glut and the shortage the chain paid for its own caution.

## Offers

A true tally of the damage, after the fact.
Because the warehouse holds what was actually made against what was actually needed, it offers the plainest measure of the effect, the excess and the empties side by side, so it is where the swing that looked like prudence at each link is finally read as loss.

## Withheld

Any warning in time to prevent it.
The warehouse fills and empties on a lag, showing the glut only once it is bought and the shortage only once it bites, so it withholds the reading early enough to act on, recording the whip after it has cracked rather than before.
