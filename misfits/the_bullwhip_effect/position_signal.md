# Position: Signal

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The seat of the order in transit, the distorted demand each link reads from the one below.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An order to carry from each link to the next, and nothing else to carry with it.
A lag: it arrives after the demand that made it, and a filter: it drops the cause and keeps the number.
The whole authority of demand, in every link's eyes, and none of its truth.

## Orders

Pass it up.
Deliver each link's order to the stage above as the only evidence of demand there is.
Carry the number, not the reason, and carry it a step behind.

## Loses

The shopper, exactly by showing each link only the stage below.
Because it conveys orders and not consumption, late and stripped of context, the seat cannot let any link see past its own customer, so each reacts to the chain's own caution instead of the market, and the seat is taken for the world it is only ever a distorted echo of.
It is the news every link trusts, and it is always old and always the fear of the link beneath.

## Drives

Toward a demand read at each stage from the stage below,
lagged, filtered, and swelling as it climbs,
so the signal the chain steers by is the chain's own cover mistaken for the market.
