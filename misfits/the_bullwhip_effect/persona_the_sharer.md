# Persona: The Sharer

## Taxonomy

[Sharer](position_sharer.md). Opens the shopper's true demand to the whole chain, and answers for the cover each link must give up.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Coordinating. Plain-dealing. Looking at the whole line, not one link.
Sees that no stage can tell a wobble from a trend because none can see the till, and moves to let them all.
No naivety in it: knows that sharing asks rivals to open their books and surrender the buffers that felt like sense.

## Action

Puts the shopper's real demand in front of every stage, so each reacts to the wobble and not to the link below.
Shortens the lags and smooths the orders, draining the distortion the chain fed on itself.
Holds the shared view in place when the next wobble tempts each link back to covering only itself.

## Shadow

It offers the one thing that stills the whip and the one thing the chain is built to withhold.
Because sharing means trusting a partner's data over your own shelves and giving up the cover that guarded you, each link has a standing reason to keep its books closed and its buffer full, so the remedy runs against the local prudence that made the swing, and holds only as long as every stage keeps sharing.
It answers a wave made of caution with a trust each link must choose over its own safety margin.

## Tell

Says "let every stage see the shopper, and stop ordering against each other."
And opens the books, against every link's habit of guarding its own.
