# Piece: The Whip

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The amplified swing, the variance grown at each stage into the glut and shortage at the top.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the warehouse](place_the_warehouse.md), where the swing lands as overstock then empty shelves.
Traced from [the line](place_the_line.md), where it grew stage by stage from a wobble into a wave.

## Load Bearing

Without it the amplification would have no measure, only a vague sense the plant runs uneven.
With it the effect is exact: the order variance widening at each step upstream, small at the shop and violent at the factory, glut and shortage from a demand that barely moved. The Whip is load bearing because it is the effect itself, quantified (Forrester): the further from the till, the larger the swing, so the piece is both the damage read in overtime and idle stock and the precise shape of how a chain of prudent buffers turns a wobble into a wave, the tip cracking hardest from the gentlest flick of the handle.

## Apparent

A volatile market, a plant unlucky to face such swinging demand.
Seen from the factory, the whip looks like the market itself heaving, and the truth that the market barely moved, that the swing is the chain's own cover compounded and lagged back at it, that the violence grows with distance from the real demand, stays hidden behind how genuinely wild the orders at the top appear.

## Yearbook

Borne by [the factory](persona_the_factory.md) through [whipping](process_whipping.md), the swing read in full and empty shelves.
It is the wave the wobble became: the Whip is [the cover](piece_the_cover.md) compounded up the chain and cracked by [the lag](piece_the_lag.md), the cost tallied at [the warehouse](place_the_warehouse.md), and the swing [the open book](piece_the_open_book.md) stills by giving every stage [the wobble](piece_the_wobble.md) itself.
