# Persona: The Retailer

## Taxonomy

[Retailer](position_retailer.md). The link that sees the wobble and orders to cover it, standing for every stage of the chain.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent. Stock-minded. Wary of the empty shelf.
Watches its own sales and orders to keep the shelf full and a cushion behind it.
No greed in it: only a link determined not to be caught short when the next customer comes.

## Action

Reads the drawn-down shelf, and orders enough to refill it and to guard against the next rise.
Adds a cover, safety stock and the pipeline to restock, on top of the demand it actually saw.
Sends up an order larger than the wobble, in the ordinary course of covering itself.

## Shadow

Not panicked, prudent: each order it swells is the only demand the link above will ever see.
Because it must cover a stockout and cannot tell a wobble from a trend, it orders more than moved and passes that up as the market, so its sensible margin becomes the next stage's signal, and every link in the chain is a retailer doing the very same thing.
It does the responsible thing, and the responsible thing, repeated up the line, is the swing.

## Tell

Says "order a bit extra, so we are not caught short."
Does, and the bit extra is what the next link takes for demand.
