# Piece: The Wobble

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The small real change in end demand, the wobble at the till that starts the swing and is soon gone.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the counter](place_the_counter.md), where it registers as a slightly larger order and then passes.
Restored at [the clearinghouse](place_the_clearinghouse.md), where the shared signal lets every stage see it plain.

## Load Bearing

Without it there is nothing to amplify, and the chain sits still.
With it the whole effect has its seed: a demand that moved only a little, and only once. The Wobble is load bearing because it is the true signal against which every swing above is revealed as overreaction (Forrester; P&G): consumption barely changed, so the gluts and shortages at the plant cannot be blamed on the market, only on what the chain did with a small honest reading, so the piece carries the smallness of the cause that makes the size of the effect the misfit.

## Apparent

The start of a trend, a demand on the move that the chain must race to meet.
Seen from any link above the till, the wobble looks like the leading edge of something larger, and the truth that it was a one-week blip already reversed, that nearly all the swing is the chain answering itself rather than the market, stays hidden behind how much every order above magnifies it.

Made by [the shopper](persona_the_shopper.md) through [ordering](process_ordering.md), the small true change at the till.
It is the seed the swing forgets: the Wobble is what [the cover](piece_the_cover.md) is added to, what [the lag](piece_the_lag.md) carries up stripped of its size, and the real demand [the open book](piece_the_open_book.md) would put back before every stage to still [the whip](piece_the_whip.md).
