# Piece: The Lag

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The delayed, filtered signal, the stage-below orders each link acts on late and without their cause, so corrections overshoot.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the line](place_the_line.md), where each order arrives a step behind the demand that made it and stripped of its reason.
Shortened at [the clearinghouse](place_the_clearinghouse.md), where shared demand and cut delays let each stage act on the wobble in time.

## Load Bearing

Without it a link could correct cleanly and the swing would settle.
With it every correction lands late: the signal arrives after the demand has moved, and carries only a number, not the wobble-and-cover behind it. The Lag is load bearing because it is what turns amplification into oscillation (Sterman): a stage acting on delayed, context-free information overshoots one way and then the other, like a hand on a tap that answers a beat late, so the piece carries the delay and the filtering that make the chain chase its own tail, gluts and shortages alternating because each fix is aimed at a state already gone.

## Apparent

A minor inconvenience, orders that take a little while to arrive and act on.
Seen plainly, the lag looks like ordinary friction, the unavoidable delay of any real chain, and the truth that it is what converts a swelling signal into a whipsaw, that acting on old information guarantees the overshoot each way, stays hidden behind how normal a bit of delay seems.

## Yearbook

Carried by [the signal](persona_the_signal.md) through [amplifying](process_amplifying.md), the delay that makes the correction overshoot.
It is why the swing oscillates: the Lag is what delivers [the cover](piece_the_cover.md) late and stripped of [the wobble](piece_the_wobble.md), what drives [the whip](piece_the_whip.md) to crack each way in turn, and the delay [the open book](piece_the_open_book.md) shortens to let each stage act in time.
