# Position: Factory

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The seat at the top of the chain, making to the amplified order and bearing the whipsaw.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The plant that must produce whatever the top-of-chain order asks.
Capacity to ramp and to cut, at a cost each way.
The wildest order in the whole line, and the least sight of the shopper who set it moving.

## Orders

Chase the swing.
Ramp to the surge when the orders climb and idle the line when they fall, and meet the demand you are shown.
Serve the order at the top, whatever swing it carries.

## Loses

Its footing, exactly by reacting to the most amplified signal on a lag.
Because every link's cover has compounded into the order it sees, and that order arrives after the demand reversed, the seat cannot help but overshoot, running flat out into a surge already gone and standing idle into a recovery already coming.
It does just what its orders say and whipsaws hardest of all, the tip of a whip cracked by a hand that barely moved.

## Drives

Toward production swinging glut to shortage,
chasing on a lag the swing the chain built for it,
so the stage furthest from the wobble moves the most from it.
