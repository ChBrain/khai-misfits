# Position: Shopper

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The seat of end demand, the true signal the chain exists to serve.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A need, bought at the counter, steady but for a small wobble now and then.
The one demand the whole chain is meant to meet.
No reach beyond the till: it buys, and what happens upstream it never sees or intends.

## Orders

Buy as ever.
Take what is needed, a touch more this week for reasons of its own, then settle back.
Register, without meaning to, as an order slightly larger than before.

## Loses

Its voice, exactly because it speaks only at the till.
Because its real demand is visible at the counter and nowhere above, the seat cannot make the chain hear the wobble itself, only the orders that chase it, so the calm true signal is lost the moment it leaves the shelf.
It moves a little and is answered by a wave it never called for.

## Drives

Toward a small, near-steady demand,
seen only at the counter and gone as fast as it came,
so the one signal the chain should serve is the one no link above the till can read.
