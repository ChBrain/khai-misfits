# Plot: The Sharing

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Clearinghouse](place_the_clearinghouse.md), the warehouse still lurching and someone moving to give the chain a common view.
A push to let every stage see the shopper, not just the link below.

## Action

[Sharing](process_sharing.md): [The Sharer](persona_the_sharer.md), holding [the sharer's seat](position_sharer.md), opens the books and pools the true demand, while [The Factory](persona_the_factory.md), holding [the factory's seat](position_factory.md), begins to make to the wobble instead of the swing.

## Stage

[The Sharer](persona_the_sharer.md), having tabled [Open the Books](plan_open_the_books.md), puts the shopper's real sales before every stage and shortens the lags, draining the distortion the chain fed on itself.
[The Factory](persona_the_factory.md), leaving [Chase the Swing](plan_chase_the_swing.md) for the shared signal, steadies to the true demand, and [The Shopper](persona_the_shopper.md)'s calm wobble is at last the thing the whole line reads.

## Tension

The one signal that stills the swing is the one every link would rather keep to itself.
There is no easy calm here: sharing plainly works, yet it asks rivals to open their books, to trust a partner's figures over their own shelves, and to give up the cover that felt like prudence. So the play closes where it must, the true demand shared and the whip stilled ... or the next wobble comes, each link reaches again for its own buffer, the books fall shut, and the swing returns.
