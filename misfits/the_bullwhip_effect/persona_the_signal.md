# Persona: The Signal

## Taxonomy

[Signal](position_signal.md). The order travelling up the chain, lagged and stripped of its cause, read at each stage as demand.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

In motion. Partial. Always a step behind.
Is the order passed from link to link, arriving late and carrying only a number, never the reason behind it.
Says nothing of the shopper: only how much the stage below asked for, this time.

## Action

Carries each link's order upward as the sole evidence the next link has of demand.
Drops the context on the way, the wobble and the cover indistinguishable in the figure that arrives.
Grows at every hand-off, as each link adds its cover and passes the larger number on.

## Shadow

It is the market as far as any link can see, and it is never the market.
Because it shows a link only its own customer's orders, delayed and without their cause, each stage reacts to the stage below rather than to the shopper, so the signal is a mirror of the chain's own caution mistaken for the world, swelling as it climbs and lagging as it goes.
It is the only news any link gets, and it is always old and always someone else's fear.

## Tell

Says "this is what they ordered, so this is what they must want."
And carries, up the line, the cover of one link as the demand of the next.
