# Position: Sharer

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The seat that opens the true demand to the whole chain, and answers for the cover surrendered.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The one signal the chain lacks: the shopper's real demand, and the means to put it in front of every stage.
The levers of the remedy: shared data, shortened lags, smoothed and steadier orders.
The reluctance of firms to open their books, and the pull of the local cover, to work against.

## Orders

Open the books.
Let every stage see the shopper, not just the link below, and drain the distortion the chain fed on itself.
Hold the shared view when the next wobble tempts each link back to guarding its own shelf.

## Loses

Ease and the comfort of the buffer, exactly because the fix runs against local prudence.
Because sharing asks each firm to trust a partner's data over its own shelves and to give up the cover that guarded it, the seat cannot decree the calm, only build the shared sight and defend it, and it holds only as long as every link keeps its books open.
It answers a swing made of caution with a trust each link must choose over its own safety margin.

## Drives

Toward a chain that reacts to the wobble and not to itself,
bought with open books and surrendered buffers,
so the cure for a swing made of local prudence is the loss of that prudence to a common view.
