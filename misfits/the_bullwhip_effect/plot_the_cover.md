# Plot: The Cover

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Line](place_the_line.md), the retailer needing to refill its shelf and guard against being caught short.
The demand it saw, plus a cushion it cannot separate from it, going up as one order.

## Action

[Ordering](process_ordering.md): [The Retailer](persona_the_retailer.md), holding [the retailer's seat](position_retailer.md), orders the wobble plus a cover, while [The Signal](persona_the_signal.md), holding [the signal's seat](position_signal.md), takes up the swollen figure as demand.

## Stage

[The Retailer](persona_the_retailer.md), having tabled [Cover the Order](plan_cover_the_order.md), adds safety stock and pipeline on top of the rise it saw, so a rush will never leave it empty.
[The Signal](persona_the_signal.md), on [Pass It Up](plan_pass_it_up.md), carries the order upward as the only evidence of demand there is, the wobble and the cover indistinguishable in the number that leaves.

## Tension

The order that goes up is already bigger than the demand that came in.
There is no fault in the cover: not being caught short is the retailer's job, and a stockout would cost the sale. But the buffer cannot be told apart from the demand once it is in the order, and that swollen order is the only signal the next link will see, so the plot closes with the chain's first stage having quietly turned a small wobble into a larger one, in the ordinary course of covering itself.
