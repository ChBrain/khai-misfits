# Plot: The Whip

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Warehouse](place_the_warehouse.md), the amplified order reaching the factory at the top of the chain.
The wildest demand in the line, arriving after the wobble that set it moving has already reversed.

## Action

[Whipping](process_whipping.md): [The Factory](persona_the_factory.md), holding [the factory's seat](position_factory.md), ramps and cuts to the swollen order, while [The Signal](persona_the_signal.md), holding [the signal's seat](position_signal.md), delivers it late and larger than anything below.

## Stage

[The Factory](persona_the_factory.md), having tabled [Chase the Swing](plan_chase_the_swing.md), runs overtime into the surge and then idles the line when it collapses, glut then shortage in the warehouse.
[The Signal](persona_the_signal.md), on [Pass It Up](plan_pass_it_up.md), hands it the most amplified order on a lag, so each correction lands after the demand it aimed at is gone.

## Tension

The stage furthest from the wobble moves the most from it.
There is no fault at the plant: it makes to the orders it is given, which are the wildest in the chain. But those orders are the whole line's cover compounded and delivered late, so the factory overshoots each way in turn, flat out into a surge already past and idle into a recovery already coming, and the plot closes with the tip of the whip cracking on a wobble the till forgot weeks ago.
