# Persona: The Factory

## Taxonomy

[Factory](position_factory.md). The top of the chain, producing to the amplified order, whipsawed between overtime and idle.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Responsive. Hard-pressed. Furthest from the till.
Makes to the orders it receives, ramping up when they surge and cutting back when they fall.
No fault in it: a plant doing its best to meet the demand it is shown, which is the wildest demand of all.

## Action

Reads the swollen order at the top of the chain and swings production to match it.
Runs overtime to chase the surge, then idles the line when the surge reverses, glut then shortage.
Bears the full amplification, the wave that a wobble at the counter became by the time it climbed.

## Shadow

It is the tip of the whip, moving most from a hand that barely twitched.
Because every link's cover has compounded into the order it finally sees, the factory reacts hardest to the demand that moved least, and its own ramp-and-cut, chasing a signal already reversed, overshoots each way in turn.
It is doing exactly what its orders say, and its orders are the swing of the whole chain landing at once.

## Tell

Says "orders are through the roof, run the line flat out ... now they have collapsed, stand it down."
And whipsaws, on a wobble the shopper forgot weeks ago.
