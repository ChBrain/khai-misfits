# Place: The Counter

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The till where end demand is real, the one point in the chain the shopper's true signal can be seen.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The shopper buying, the shelf drawing down, a week's demand a shade above the last and then back to its usual.
The one honest reading of the market, small and calm, registered as it happens and gone as the shopper leaves.

## Holds

The true signal, in the only place it exists.
Here the demand is what it is, the wobble itself and not an order about it, so the counter holds the ground truth the whole chain is trying to serve, the reading against which every swing above will turn out to be an overreaction.

## Offers

An accurate demand, to whoever stands at the till.
Because the counter shows consumption and not orders, it offers the shopper's real signal plainly, so it is the one stage where a wobble looks like a wobble, and the one reading that, if it reached every link, would still the whole swing.

## Withheld

That signal, from everyone above it.
The counter cannot carry its truth up the chain, because only the shop stands at the till and every link above sees orders instead, so it withholds the real demand from the very stages that most need it, leaving them to read the market off each other.
