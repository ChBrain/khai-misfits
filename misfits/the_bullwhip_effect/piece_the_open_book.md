# Piece: The Open Book

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The shared true demand and shortened lag that let each stage react to the wobble and not to the stage below.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the clearinghouse](place_the_clearinghouse.md), where the shopper's real sales are pooled for every stage to read.
Set against [the line](place_the_line.md), whose closed, neighbour-to-neighbour orders it replaces with a common view.

## Load Bearing

Without it the play has no remedy, only a swing that renews with every wobble.
With it the misfit is answered, and answered at a price: the true demand put before every stage, the lags cut, the orders smoothed, so each link reacts to the shopper instead of its neighbour's cover. The Open Book is load bearing because it is the one correction the structure allows and the hardest to keep (Lee, Padmanabhan and Whang): it works only if every stage shares and keeps sharing, asks rivals to open their books and surrender the buffer that felt like prudence, so the piece carries both the exit and its fragility, a calm that holds only as long as the common view is kept.

## Apparent

An obvious fix, everyone just agreeing to pass the sales figures along.
Seen from outside, the open book looks like a simple matter of sharing data, and the truth that it asks independent firms to trust a partner's numbers over their own shelves, to give up the cover that guarded them, and to hold to it against every later wobble, stays hidden behind how easy sharing a spreadsheet sounds.

## Yearbook

Opened by [the sharer](persona_the_sharer.md) through [sharing](process_sharing.md), the common view that stills the swing.
It is the exit and its cost: the Open Book is what puts [the wobble](piece_the_wobble.md) before every stage, undoes [the lag](piece_the_lag.md) and the compounding of [the cover](piece_the_cover.md), and stills [the whip](piece_the_whip.md), for exactly as long as the books stay open.
