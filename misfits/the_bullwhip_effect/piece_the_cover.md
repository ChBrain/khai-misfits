# Piece: The Cover

## Taxonomy

[The Bullwhip Effect](play_the_bullwhip_effect.md). The buffer each link adds, safety stock and pipeline, that makes its order larger than the demand it saw.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Added along [the line](place_the_line.md), where each link orders the demand plus a cushion.
Surrendered at [the clearinghouse](place_the_clearinghouse.md), where a shared signal makes the private buffer needless.

## Load Bearing

Without it each order would match its demand and nothing would amplify.
With it every order leaves a stage larger than the demand that prompted it: safety stock against a stockout, and the pipeline it takes to refill. The Cover is load bearing because it is the local prudence that becomes the swing (Lee, Padmanabhan and Whang): each link is right to add it and cannot tell it apart from real demand once it is in the order, so the piece carries the exact way sensible protection at every stage compounds into the wave, the buffer indistinguishable, upstream, from the market.

## Apparent

Ordinary good management, a link keeping a sensible margin.
Seen at one stage, the cover looks like plain prudence no one could fault, and the truth that the same margin added at every stage is precisely what turns a wobble into a wave, that each buffer becomes the next link's demand, stays hidden behind how obviously right it is not to be caught short.
