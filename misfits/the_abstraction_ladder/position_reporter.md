# Position: Reporter

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md). The seat that climbs the ladder, abstracting the concrete for the level above, where an honest summary sheds the checkable particular with every rung.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A load of concrete particulars and a level above that cannot carry them: the specifics from below and the duty to compress them into something usable higher up.
A real necessity: no hierarchy carries every particular upward, so abstracting is the job and not a shortcut.
No way to climb the ladder and keep the checkable specific, because the specific is exactly what each rung must shed to be usable above.

## Orders

Climb the ladder.
Compress the concrete particulars into a general claim the level above can hold, give a fair account of what came from below, and make the report usable at the top.
Summarise honestly, because summarising honestly is the job.

## Loses

The checkable ground of the claim, exactly by summarising it fairly.
Because each rung up must turn the verifiable particular into the general abstraction and the general cannot be checked against any single fact, the seat's honest summary leaves the checkable specific on the rung below, so what climbs is a true account with nothing left in it to hold, the accountability leaking out with the particulars not by concealment but by the necessary climb.
It gains a report the level above can use, and loses the ground the report could be checked and held on.

## Drives

Toward the concrete abstracted and the particular shed,
a fair summary climbing and the check left below,
so the seat that summarises honestly sheds the ground, and what it sends up commands more and can be held less.
