# The Abstraction Ladder: Reference

The research warrant for [The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md).
The dynamic is the ascent of the abstraction ladder shedding the checkable particular: Hayakawa's abstraction
ladder and dead-level abstracting, Korzybski's levels of abstraction, Trope and Liberman's construal-level theory,
and Scott's legibility and the loss of metis, all cited below.

## Line of Work

Staging the abstraction ladder as a **climb-sheds-the-check structure**: a report must climb the ladder from the
concrete particular to the abstract to be usable at the top, and each rung up sheds the checkable specific, so the
higher a claim rises the more it commands and the less anyone can be held to it, the accountability leaking out
not by concealment but by the climb itself. The play models the documented mechanics, the necessary ascent from
the particular thing to the general class because no hierarchy carries every particular upward, the checkable
concrete shed at each rung and the summary that is fair yet unverifiable, the authority the abstract gains as
psychological and organisational height construe the world in vaguer terms, the accountability that finds no
purchase when a failed claim is traced down a chain of honest summaries, and the costly descent that alone puts
the check back under the abstraction, as the load-bearing vectors of the structure, not as a tale of vague talkers
or hidden bad news. The governing finding of the sources is the play's governing law: language ascends a ladder of
abstraction from the concrete referent to the high-level term, and a claim pitched too high to cash out in
particulars can be neither checked nor owned, so an organisation that must summarise upward to command builds an
authority at the top that answers to no fact and holds no author.

It is distinct from the house's Watermelon Reporting, and the two are close enough that the cut must be exact:
there the engine is the deliberate concealment of a known bad state, the reporter who filters the red out and
sends the green up, a chosen suppression of what is known; here nothing is hidden and every summary is a fair
account of the level below, the checkable particular shed not by choice but by the necessary climb, so Watermelon
Reporting conceals a known specific while the Abstraction Ladder sheds the specific sincerely. One filters bad
news; the other abstracts all news. It is distinct from The Decision Travels, where a correct decision crosses a
chain of stations and none validates it against the actual body: there the loss is of validation as a decision
moves forward through hand-offs, here the loss is of the checkable particular as a report climbs upward through
levels, a matter of vertical abstraction and not of a travelling decision. And it is distinct from The Frozen
Middle, where a layer of managers actively distorts or blocks what passes through it: the Abstraction Ladder needs
no distortion and no blocking, only the honest compression every rung must perform, the summary true and the
ground shed by the climb alone. (Lossy Compression, the non-deliberate discard of signal at each summary, is
carried here rather than staged apart: it is this same undeliberate shedding read as information, and the play
holds it as one facet of the climb.)

## Restrictions

What the house refuses to model, and how it credits what it stages.

- **No villains**: the elements are reduced to load-bearing vectors. The play refuses to cast the reporter as a
  vague talker or the principal as lazy, because the source finding is precisely that the climb is necessary and
  honest: no level above can carry every particular, the summary is a fair account of the ground below, and the
  top genuinely cannot run on the raw specifics. The shed accountability is authored by no one and emerges from
  the necessary ascent, so the misfit is that a claim high enough to command a hierarchy is abstract enough that
  nothing checkable survives in it, and the language that lets the top act is the same language that lets no one
  be held, the leak a property of the ladder and not a choice to hide.
- **No hero**: the play refuses to resolve the misfit into a costless correction. The one move that keeps the
  ground is to descend the ladder, demand the concrete particular under the abstract claim, sample the ground the
  summary stands on, and hold the report to a checkable fact and a name, and it means reading the particulars the
  abstraction was built to spare the top, distrusting a claim because it sounds strategic, and dragging the clean
  high language back to the weeds it rose above. The reckoning is a ground kept only by paying, continuously, the
  cost of reading the specifics the climb existed to shed, against being heard as lost in the detail, not a free
  correction.
- **Credit, not claim**: the research above is other people's work, packaged and attributed here, never claimed as
  the house's. The staging is the house's; the abstraction ladder and dead-level abstracting are Hayakawa's, the
  levels of abstraction and the map-territory distinction are Korzybski's, the construal of the distant in
  abstract terms is Trope and Liberman's, and the legible abstraction detached from local particular knowledge is
  Scott's. The honest boundary, that the summary is fair and the abstraction necessary so the shed check is
  undeliberate, is carried as carefully as the finding itself.
