# Plan: Hold to Account

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the auditor's seat](position_auditor.md), inside [The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Auditor](position_auditor.md)

## Direction

Trace the failed abstract claim back down the ladder, read each summary against the one below, and find the level where it can be pinned to a particular and a person. Seek the owner, because a claim that failed should have an author who can be held to it.

## Orders

([The Auditor](persona_the_auditor.md) climbs down through a chain of fair summaries and finds no rung where a checkable particular meets its owner, so the claim dissolves in its hands, the accountability shed on the way up.)

## Implementation

([The Summit](place_the_summit.md) down; the [slipping](process_slipping.md) process; [the grip](piece_the_grip.md) closes on air as each rung of [the ladder](piece_the_ladder.md) offers a fair [summary](piece_the_summary.md) and no [particular](piece_the_particulars.md) to pin.)

## Targets

- [ ] the failed claim is traced back down the ladder, rung by rung
- [ ] each summary is read against the one below and found fair, with no lie to expose
- [ ] the level where the claim meets a checkable particular and its owner is sought
- [ ] the grip finds no purchase, the accountability shed with the particulars on the way up
