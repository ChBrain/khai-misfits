# Persona: The Principal

## Taxonomy

[Principal](position_principal.md). The one at the top who acts on the abstraction, prizing the clean strategic summary and commanding on a claim that has nothing concrete left to be checked or held.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Strategic. Decisive. Above the detail.
Is the one who runs the enterprise from the summit, the executive, the board, the principal who sets direction on what climbs up to them.
No laziness in it: the Principal genuinely cannot read every particular and rightly wants the summary, so acting on the abstract is the only way to command at scale.

## Action

Takes the high-level summary, reads it as the language of strategy, and directs the enterprise on it.
Prizes the clean abstract claim and treats the concrete particular as detail beneath the level, the weeds a leader rises above.
Does what a principal at the top must: it acts on the abstraction, because the abstraction is all that can climb to where it sits.

## Shadow

Not idle, a commander who genuinely needs the summary, and that is the whole difficulty: the abstraction the Principal must act on is the abstraction that can be held to nothing.
Because the clean high claim sounds strategic and commands while the concrete sounds like weeds, the Principal rewards the abstract and dismisses the particular, so the climb is pulled upward and the claim that reaches the top directs the whole enterprise and cannot be checked against a single fact, the Principal ruling on language that has shed, on the way up, everything that could pin it or its authors to the ground.
It acts on the summary it rightly needs, and the summary it needs is the one nothing can be held to.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "I do not need the detail, give me the top line."
Says it rightly, unable to run on the raw particulars, and does not weigh that the top line it commands on is the claim from which every checkable particular has been shed.
