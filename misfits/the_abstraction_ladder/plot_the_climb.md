# Plot: The Climb

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Ground](place_the_ground.md), a load of concrete particulars and a level above that cannot carry them.
The Reporter taking the weld and the Tuesday and the ledger line, and summarising them for the top.

## Action

[Abstracting](process_abstracting.md): [The Reporter](persona_the_reporter.md), holding [the reporter's seat](position_reporter.md), compresses the particulars into a general claim the level above can hold.

## Stage

[The Reporter](persona_the_reporter.md), on [Climb the Ladder](plan_climb_the_ladder.md), turns [the particulars](piece_the_particulars.md) into [the summary](piece_the_summary.md), rung by rung up [the ladder](piece_the_ladder.md), a fair account of the ground below.
Each rung sheds the checkable specific, honestly and by necessity, because no level above can carry every particular, so what rises is true and no longer tied to any single fact.

## Tension

Summarising honestly for a level that cannot carry the particulars is necessary, and the honest summary sheds the checkable specific just the same.
There is nothing false: the account is fair and the climb is the only way to reach the top. But the concrete was the verifiable part, so the plot closes with a true summary risen and its ground left below, the accountability shed on the very rungs that carried the claim up, no one having hidden a thing.
