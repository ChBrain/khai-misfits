# Piece: The Cloud

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md). The phenomenon whole, a summit running on abstractions detached from the ground, commanding everything and answerable for nothing, the accountability leaked out by the climb.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Fills [the summit](place_the_summit.md), where the enterprise is directed on the abstract.
Dispersed, in part, at [the sounding](place_the_sounding.md), where a claim is dropped back to the ground.

## Load Bearing

Without it the shedding is a local loss, and a single claim goes uncheckable while the rest stay grounded.
With it the trap is complete and authorless: a whole top level running on abstractions cut loose from the checkable ground, directing the enterprise on language that answers to no fact and holds no owner, the accountability having leaked out with the particulars on every climb (Scott, on the high-modernist summary steering by legible abstraction detached from the local particular; Hayakawa, on a discourse stuck at the dead level of abstraction). The Cloud is load bearing because it is the phenomenon whole: not one shed claim but a summit that lives above the ground, commanding much and checkable on nothing, so the piece carries the reckoning at full strength, an enterprise steered from a height where authority is total and accountability has evaporated.

## Apparent

The strategic altitude, the high clear air of leadership, a top that sees the whole and sets the course.
Seen at the surface, the Cloud looks like the rightful view from the top, leadership operating at the level of the whole, and the truth that the height is detachment, that the summit commands on language nothing can check, that the accountability evaporated on the same climbs that lifted the authority, that a whole level can steer while answerable to no fact, stays hidden behind how much the strategic altitude looks like clear sight rather than lost ground.

## Yearbook

Filled by every [reporter](persona_the_reporter.md)'s [climb](process_abstracting.md) and [principal](persona_the_principal.md)'s [command](process_elevating.md), dispersed only by [the grounder](persona_the_grounder.md) at [the sounding](place_the_sounding.md).
It is the phenomenon whole: the Cloud is [the summary](piece_the_summary.md) become [the command](piece_the_command.md) with [the particulars](piece_the_particulars.md) shed and [the grip](piece_the_grip.md) closing on air, the summit steering from the height at the centre of the abstraction ladder.
