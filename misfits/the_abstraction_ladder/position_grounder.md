# Position: Grounder

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md). The seat that would descend the ladder and hold the abstract claim to a concrete particular, at the cost of reading the specifics the climb was built to shed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A claim that sounds strategic and a doubt about what is under it: the standing to demand the particular and the will to go down for it.
A real corrective: descending to the checkable fact is the one move that catches an abstract claim with nothing beneath it.
No cheapness to the descent, and no welcome for the one who drags the clean high language back to the weeds.

## Orders

Descend the ladder.
Ask, under the abstract claim, for the weld and the Tuesday and the ledger line, sample the ground the summary stands on, and hold the report to a checkable fact and a named owner.
Keep the claim on the ground, and pay the reading of the particulars the climb existed to shed.

## Loses

The standing of one who is strategic, and the ease the summary bought.
Because the top can act only on the abstract and the particulars are voluminous and slow, the seat must read the specifics the summary was built to spare, distrust a claim because it sounds strategic, and drag the high language back to the weeds, so the ground is held only by paying the cost the ladder was climbed to avoid, and the seat is heard as lost in the detail, unstrategic, blind to the big picture, against every reason the report rose.
It gains the claim kept checkable and the ground held, and loses the standing of rising above the detail.

## Drives

Toward the particular demanded and the ground kept,
the abstract dragged back down to the fact it rose from,
so the seat that descends the ladder holds the claim to the ground, and is read as failing to rise above it.
