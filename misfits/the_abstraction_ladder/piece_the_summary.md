# Piece: The Summary

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md). The abstracted report that climbs, the fair general claim the top can hold, true to the ground below and no longer tied to any checkable fact of it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Made along [the ladder](piece_the_ladder.md), each rung producing a more general one.
Received at [the summit](place_the_summit.md), where it is read as strategy and acted on.

## Load Bearing

Without it the top has nothing usable and the particulars never reach the level that must act.
With it the trap has its vehicle: a general claim that fairly represents the ground and can be carried where the ground cannot, so the summary is both the honest account the top needs and the abstraction that has shed the check (Hayakawa, on the higher-level term standing in for the many particulars below it). The Summary is load bearing because it is what climbs in the particulars' place: a true compression usable above and verifiable nowhere, so the piece carries the fair account that reaches the summit having left, on the rungs below, every specific that could hold it or its authors to the ground.

## Apparent

A clear, fair report, the honest top line of what is happening below.
Seen at the surface, the Summary looks like exactly what good reporting should produce, a faithful account the top can act on, and the truth that its fairness does not make it checkable, that a true summary has still shed the particulars that could verify it, that what climbed is honest and unholdable at once, stays hidden behind how faithful and useful a clean top line looks.

## Yearbook

It is the vehicle of the trap: the Summary carries [the particulars](piece_the_particulars.md) up as generality, becomes [the command](piece_the_command.md) at the top, and holds no ground for [the grip](piece_the_grip.md), a fair account risen into [the cloud](piece_the_cloud.md).
