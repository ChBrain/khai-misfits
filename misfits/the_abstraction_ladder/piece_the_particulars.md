## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md). The concrete checkable specifics, the weld and the Tuesday and the ledger line, shed on the way up and the only thing a claim could ever be verified or an owner held against.

## Place

## Load Bearing

Without them there is nothing that was ever checkable, and the abstraction sheds nothing of value.
With them the trap has its cost: the concrete specifics that alone can verify a claim and pin it to an owner, shed at each rung because they are too voluminous and too specific to climb (Scott, on the local particular knowledge, the metis, that the legible summary must leave behind; Korzybski, on the referents an abstraction omits). The Particulars are load bearing because they are the checkable ground the climb loses: the verifiable fact and the named owner that accountability needs, present at the bottom and absent at the top, so the piece carries the specific that could hold a claim and is exactly what cannot rise with it.

## Apparent

Detail, the weeds, the granular stuff beneath the level the top works at.
Seen at the surface, the Particulars look like mere granularity, the low detail a strategic view rightly rises above, and the truth that they are the only checkable part of any claim, that shedding them sheds the accountability, that the weeds are where verification and ownership actually live, stays hidden behind how minor and beneath-the-level concrete detail is made to look next to the strategic summary.
