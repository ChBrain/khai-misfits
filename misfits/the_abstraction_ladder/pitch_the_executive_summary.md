# Pitch: The Executive Summary

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Clean, strategic, high-level.
The plain good sense of summarising the particulars into a claim the top can act on, the abstract report that fits on a slide and commands the room.
Nothing false in it: the top cannot read every weld report, so the specifics must be climbed into a summary, and the summary is a fair account of the ground beneath it.

## Undertow

Under the clean summary, each rung up shedding the checkable particular, so the higher the claim rises the more it commands and the less anyone can be held to it.
Because the abstract sounds strategic and the concrete is what could be verified, the claim gains authority and loses accountability on the very same climb, and at the top it directs the whole and can be pinned to nothing.
What lets the top act, the abstraction, is exactly what lets no one be held.

## Nerve

The feeling of rising above the detail to the clean strategic claim,
sure it is only good summarising, because the top cannot run on the raw particulars,
while the specifics that would let the claim be checked are shed on every rung, and the language that commands the enterprise is the language nothing can hold to the ground.

## Echo

After the run: a summit running on abstractions that direct everything and answer for nothing, the particulars shed and the accountability leaked out with them,
no one having concealed a thing and every summary fair, only an honest report climbing the ladder it had to climb,
and the one move that would keep the ground, descending to the concrete particular and the name, forgone because it means reading the specifics the climb was built to shed and dragging the clean high language back to the weeds.
