# Plot: The Audit

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

The abstract command has failed, and someone climbs back down for who is accountable.
The Auditor tracing the claim rung by rung, looking for the level where it can be pinned to a particular and a person.

## Action

[Slipping](process_slipping.md): [The Auditor](persona_the_auditor.md), holding [the auditor's seat](position_auditor.md), traces the failed claim down and finds each rung a fair summary and no owner to hold.

## Stage

[The Auditor](persona_the_auditor.md), on [Hold to Account](plan_hold_to_account.md), descends [the ladder](piece_the_ladder.md) and reads each [summary](piece_the_summary.md) against the one below, and [the grip](piece_the_grip.md) closes on air, no rung holding a checkable particular met by its owner.
Every level reported fairly and nothing was hidden, so the claim dissolves in the tracing hand, the accountability shed on the way up long before the failure.

## Tension

An honest chain traced to its end is the fairest audit there is, and it is exactly what leaves the failure ownerless.
There is no cover-up: every rung passed up a fair account. But the checkable particulars were shed on the climb, so the plot closes with the blame finding no purchase, a real failure and no owner, the accountability not hidden but abstracted away, and no fault found taken, wrongly, for no fault.
