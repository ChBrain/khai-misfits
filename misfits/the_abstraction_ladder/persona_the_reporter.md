# Persona: The Reporter

## Taxonomy

[Reporter](position_reporter.md). The one who climbs the ladder, abstracting the concrete particulars into a summary the level above can hold, sincerely and by necessity, and shedding the checkable specific with every rung.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Diligent. Fair. Compressing for the level above.
Is the one who summarises upward, the analyst, the manager, the team lead, who takes what the ground reports and makes it usable for the top.
No deceit in it: the Reporter gives a fair account of the level below, hiding nothing, because summarising honestly is exactly the job.

## Action

Takes the concrete particulars and compresses them into a general claim the level above can act on, rung by rung.
Turns the weld and the Tuesday and the ledger line into slipping quality or a challenged unit, a fair summary of what came from below.
Does what a reporter in a hierarchy must: it abstracts the specific into the general, because no level above can carry every particular.

## Shadow

Not a liar, an honest summariser doing a necessary job, and that is the whole difficulty: the summary is fair, and a fair summary sheds the checkable particular just the same.
Because each rung up must compress the concrete into the general and the concrete was the verifiable part, the Reporter's honest abstraction leaves the checkable specific on the rung below, so what climbs is a true account that can no longer be checked against any single fact, the accountability leaking out with the particulars not because the Reporter concealed them but because carrying them up was never possible.
It summarises in good faith, and summarising in good faith is what sheds the ground the claim could be held to.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "in short, quality is slipping."
Says it as a fair account of a hundred particulars it did not carry up, and cannot, at that rung, point to the weld or the Tuesday that would let the claim be checked.
