# Place: The Summit

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md). The top of the ladder, where the abstraction is received and commands, the boardroom and the strategy deck, where the claim directs the enterprise and nothing concrete is left to check it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The place the enterprise is directed from, a summit, a board table, a slide of high-level claims, where the top acts on what has climbed to it.
The Principal reading the clean summary as strategy and setting the whole enterprise's course on it, the language all challenged and headwinds and delivery.

## Holds

The command, and the abstraction that has shed its check.
Here only the abstract can arrive, so the Summit holds the point where authority and unaccountability meet: a claim high enough to direct the whole and abstract enough that nothing verifiable survives in it, so it is where the report commands most and can be held least, the top ruling on language from which the checkable particular was shed on every rung it climbed to get here.

## Offers

A claim that can direct the whole enterprise.
Because the Summit receives only what the ladder could carry, it offers the Principal a summary usable at scale, a clean strategic language that commands the room and sets the course, so it hands out the abstraction the top must run on, and hands it out with every check shed, the claim that directs everything pinned to nothing and answerable to no fact.

## Withheld

Any particular the command could be checked or held on.
The Summit shows the abstract claim commanding but cannot show the ground beneath it, because the particulars were shed on the climb and only the summary arrived, so it withholds the checkable fact that would test the direction and the owner who could be held to it, letting the command run on language with nothing under it, and, all strategic authority and no concrete, it withholds from the auditor who later climbs down any rung where the claim was ever someone's, the top's command floating free of the ground it steers.
