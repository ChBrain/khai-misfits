# Plot: The Descent

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Sounding](place_the_sounding.md), and someone unwilling to take the strategic claim on trust.
The Grounder setting out to demand the concrete particular under the abstraction and to hold the report to a fact and a name.

## Action

[Grounding](process_grounding.md): [The Grounder](persona_the_grounder.md), holding [the grounder's seat](position_grounder.md), drops a line from the abstract claim to the checkable particular and samples the ground the summary stands on.

## Stage

[The Grounder](persona_the_grounder.md), on [Descend the Ladder](plan_descend_the_ladder.md), asks under [the summary](piece_the_summary.md) for [the particulars](piece_the_particulars.md), gives [the grip](piece_the_grip.md) something to hold, and drags the clean high language back down [the ladder](piece_the_ladder.md) to the ground.
Against the descent stand a summit that runs on the abstract, a ladder climbed to shed the specific, and an ear that hears the weeds as unstrategic.

## Tension

Descending to the particular is the one move that puts the check back under the claim, and it asks the reader to pay the cost the climb was built to avoid.
It reads the specifics the summary spared the top, distrusts a claim because it sounds strategic, and drags the high language back to the weeds. So the plot closes one of two ways: the claim held to the ground at real cost and the check restored ... or the descent waived as being in the detail, the clean summary taken on trust, and the ground let fall away.
