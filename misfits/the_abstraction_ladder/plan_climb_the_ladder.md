# Plan: Climb the Ladder

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the reporter's seat](position_reporter.md), inside [The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Reporter](position_reporter.md)

## Direction

Compress the concrete particulars into a general claim the level above can hold. Give a fair account of what came from below, rung by rung, and make the report usable at the top. Summarise honestly, because no hierarchy carries every particular upward and honest summary is the job.

## Orders

([The Reporter](persona_the_reporter.md) abstracts the concrete into the general for the level above, and by the fair climb sheds the checkable particular, so what rises is a true account with nothing left in it to hold.)

## Implementation

([The Ground](place_the_ground.md) up; the [abstracting](process_abstracting.md) process; [the particulars](piece_the_particulars.md) are compressed into [the summary](piece_the_summary.md) along [the ladder](piece_the_ladder.md), the checkable specific shed at every rung.)

## Targets

- [ ] the concrete particulars from below are compressed into a general claim the level above can hold
- [ ] a fair account of the level beneath is passed up, hiding nothing
- [ ] the checkable specific is shed at each rung, by the necessary climb and not by concealment
- [ ] what rises is a true summary with no particular left in it to check or hold
