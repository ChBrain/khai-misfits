# Process: Elevating

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md). The abstract claim prized as strategic and the concrete dismissed as weeds, the elevation that rewards the climb and gives the summary its command.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A summit that can act only on the abstract and a summary that has reached it.
At [the summit](place_the_summit.md), the principal reading the clean high claim as strategy and the particular as detail beneath the level.

## Direction

Toward the abstract rewarded and made command.
Because the high-level claim sounds strategic and commands while the concrete sounds like weeds, elevating prizes the summary and dismisses the particular, so the abstraction gains authority and directs the enterprise, the claim ruling more the higher it sits, and the climb pulled upward by a top that rewards the language furthest from the ground.

## Lever

The genuine need of the top for the abstract.
Because the summit truly cannot run on the raw particulars, elevating the summary is not vanity but the only way to command at scale, so the process runs on a real limit and not on a taste for the vague, the authority-without-check assembled from no laziness, only from a top that must act on the abstract and rightly prizes the claim it can actually use.

## Echo

A summary made command, and the concrete filed as staff.
The principal has elevated the abstract it needs, so what reads as strategic leadership is, in truth, the claim furthest from the ground given the most authority, the abstraction that answers to no particular made the language that directs the whole.
