# Position: Principal

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md). The seat at the top that acts on the abstraction, where commanding on the summary it needs means commanding on a claim nothing concrete can check or hold.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An enterprise to direct and no capacity for the particulars: the standing to command and the reach that can only take what climbs to the summit.
A real limit: the top genuinely cannot read every weld or ledger line, so the summary is a need and not a preference.
No concrete in the claim it acts on, and no way to check or hold a command built from what the climb has shed.

## Orders

Take the summary.
Read the high-level claim as the language of strategy, direct the enterprise on it, and prize the clean abstraction over the concrete detail beneath the level.
Command on the summary, because the summary is all that can reach the seat.

## Loses

The check on its own command, exactly by taking the summary it needs.
Because the abstract that reaches the top has shed every checkable particular and the seat can act only on that abstract, the Principal commands on a claim that directs the whole and can be pinned to nothing, so what the seat loses is the ground under its own direction: an enterprise steered by language that answers to no fact and holds no author, the summary it rightly needed being the one it can never check.
It gains a claim it can command at scale on, and loses the ground that command could be verified against.

## Drives

Toward the abstract commanded on and the particular dismissed,
the summit running on the summary and blind to the ground,
so the seat that takes the summary rules on the unchecked, and prizes the very abstraction that answers for nothing.
