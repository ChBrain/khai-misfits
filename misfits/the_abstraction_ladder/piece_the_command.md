# Piece: The Command

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md). The authority the abstract claim wields, the higher and vaguer it is the more it directs, the command the summary gains as it sheds the check.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Wielded at [the summit](place_the_summit.md), where the abstract claim directs the enterprise.
Prized through [elevating](process_elevating.md), where the strategic language is given its authority.

## Load Bearing

Without it the abstraction is merely uncheckable, not powerful, and shedding the ground would cost only knowledge, not accountability.
With it the trap closes: the abstract claim not only escapes the check but commands the enterprise, so the language furthest from the ground directs everything and answers to nothing, authority and unaccountability climbing together (Trope and Liberman, on construal-level theory, distance construing the world abstractly, the high view commanding in the abstract). The Command is load bearing because it is why the shed check matters: the abstraction that could be held to nothing is the very language that rules the whole, so the piece carries the authority the summary gains on the same rungs it loses its ground, the claim ruling most where it can be checked least.

## Apparent

Leadership, strategic direction, the rightful authority of the top setting the course.
Seen at the surface, the Command looks like leadership working, the high view directing the enterprise as it should, and the truth that the claim commands because it is abstract, that the authority rose on the same rungs the check fell off, that what directs the whole can be held to no fact, stays hidden behind how naturally the strategic language of the top reads as earned authority.
