# Persona: The Grounder

## Taxonomy

[Grounder](position_grounder.md). The one who would descend the ladder, demanding the concrete particular under the abstract claim and holding the report to a checkable fact and a name, at the cost of reading the specifics the climb was built to shed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Concrete. Sceptical of the summary. Willing to be in the weeds.
Is the one who insists on the particular, the leader who asks for the example, the reviewer who samples the ground the summary stands on.
No pedantry in it: the Grounder does not deny that abstraction is needed, only refuses to let the abstract command without a concrete particular under it that can be checked.

## Action

Asks, under the abstract claim, for the weld and the Tuesday and the ledger line, and samples the ground the summary stands on.
Holds the report to a checkable fact and a named owner, dragging the clean high language back down to the particular it rose from.
Does what keeps the ground: it descends the ladder, and descending is the one move that catches an abstract claim with nothing under it.

## Shadow

Not a free corrective, a costly and unwelcome descent, and that is the whole difficulty: the particular the Grounder demands is exactly what the climb existed to shed, so demanding it means undoing the compression the whole hierarchy runs on.
Because the top can only act on the abstract and the particulars are voluminous and slow, the Grounder must read the specifics the summary was built to spare the Principal, distrust a claim because it sounds strategic, and drag the clean high language back to the weeds, so the ground is held only by paying the cost the ladder was climbed to avoid, and the Grounder is heard as being in the weeds, unstrategic, unable to see the big picture, against every reason the report rose.
It descends to keep the claim checkable, and descending is read as failing to rise.

## Tell

Says, in effect, "give me one concrete example, with a name and a date."
Says it reaching for the particular the summary shed, and is heard, often, as lost in the detail and blind to the strategy everyone above is fluent in.
