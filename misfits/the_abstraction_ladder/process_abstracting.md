# Process: Abstracting

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md). The Reporter compressing the concrete particulars into a general claim for the level above, the honest climb that sheds the checkable specific with every rung.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A load of particulars and a level above that cannot carry them.
At [the ground](place_the_ground.md), the reporter taking the concrete facts and summarising them into something usable higher up.

## Direction

Toward the concrete turned general and sent up.
Because no level above can carry every particular, abstracting compresses the weld and the Tuesday and the ledger line into slipping quality or a challenged unit, a fair account of what came from below, so what climbs is true to the ground and, being general, no longer tied to any single fact of it, the specific shed on each rung by the summary that replaces it.

## Lever

The necessity of a usable summary.
Because the top cannot run on the raw particulars, abstracting is the only way a report reaches it, so the process runs on need and not on evasion, the shed check assembled from no concealment, only from an honest compression that must leave the verifiable specific behind to make the claim small enough to climb.

## Echo

A fair summary risen, and the checkable particular shed.
The reporter has abstracted honestly as the climb demands, so what reads as a clean report reaching the top is, in truth, a true account with its ground left below, the specific that could check it shed on the very rungs that carried it up.
