# Plot: The Free Hand

## Taxonomy

[The Abstraction Ladder](play_the_abstraction_ladder.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Summit](place_the_summit.md), the claim arrived with nothing concrete left in it.
The abstraction directing the enterprise and checkable against no single fact, the top commanding on language pinned to nothing.

## Action

[Elevating](process_elevating.md) come to its head: the abstract [command](piece_the_command.md) directs the whole and can be held to no particular, a free hand bought by the height that shed the check.

## Stage

The claim, risen through [the ladder](piece_the_ladder.md) and shed of [the particulars](piece_the_particulars.md), commands the enterprise from [the summit](place_the_summit.md) and answers to no fact, [the principal](persona_the_principal.md) directing on an abstraction nothing can check.
Authority and unaccountability have arrived together, the command total and the ground beneath it gone.

## Tension

The height that lets the claim direct everything is the height that leaves it holdable by nothing, so the free hand is the same thing as the shed check.
There is no villain: the top acts on the only language that could climb to it. But that language directs the whole while pinned to no particular, so the plot closes with the abstract command running the enterprise and answerable to no fact, the authority and the unaccountability the two faces of the one climb.
