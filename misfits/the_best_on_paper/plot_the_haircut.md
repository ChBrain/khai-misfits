# Plot: The Haircut

## Taxonomy

[The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Pick](place_the_pick.md), a sceptic weighing the top estimate against a prior before the choice is locked.
A dazzling number at the head of the list, and a doubt about how much of it is worth and how much is noise.

## Action

[Shrinking](process_shrinking.md): each estimate is pulled toward the prior by its noise, the boldest reading discounted hardest and the choice de-biased.

## Stage

[The Sceptic](persona_the_sceptic.md), on [Shrink to the Prior](plan_shrink_to_the_prior.md), pulls each [valuation](piece_the_valuation.md) toward the prior, removing the [overstatement](piece_the_overstatement.md) the maximum would create and, with it, often the top of the list.
The correction is right and the price is real, the flattered winner spared and the bold bet forgone in the same move.

## Tension

Distrusting the highest estimate is the sound Bayesian correction, and the sound correction costs the very bet it debunks.
There is no free fix: the Sceptic is right that the maximum is flattered, and shrinking toward the prior de-biases the choice, but the discount falls hardest on the boldest reading, so a genuinely-best option can be pulled below a safer one and passed. So the plot closes with the choice corrected and the letdown spared, bought with the dazzling prize the shrink declined, the reckoning a chooser who discounts their own best number and forgoes the top of the list, or one who takes it and is, on average, let down.
