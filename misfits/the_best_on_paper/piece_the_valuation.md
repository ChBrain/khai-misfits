# Piece: The Valuation

## Taxonomy

[The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md). The noisy but unbiased estimate of an option's worth, the honest reading that ranking will flatter and shrinkage would pull back.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Made at [the field](place_the_field.md), where each option is appraised, and ranked at [the pick](place_the_pick.md), where the highest is taken.
Flows from the appraisal of one option, an honest number as likely too low as too high, carried forward as the value the choice will be made on.

## Load Bearing

Without the Valuation there is nothing to choose among: it is the reading every option is reduced to and the choice is made on.
With it the slate is honest and the trap is set: the Valuation is an unbiased estimate of worth (Smith and Winkler; Stein), correct on average one option at a time, so it is beyond reproach until it is ranked. The Valuation is load bearing because it is both the honest input and the thing selection biases, the number that carries no slant alone and a systematic lift once the maximum is taken, so the piece holds the raw material of the curse, the fair reading whose fairness the choice undoes and whose overstatement only a shrink toward the prior removes.

## Apparent

Just an estimate, the honest best guess of what an option is worth.
Seen at the surface, the Valuation looks like solid ground, a number as ready to understate as to overstate and defensible on its own, and the truth that taking the highest of many such numbers conditions on the ones that ran high, that an unbiased estimate becomes a biased choice, that the reading is trustworthy singly and treacherous at the top of a list, stays hidden behind how honest each number plainly is.

## Yearbook

Made by [the Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md), ranked by [the Selector](persona_the_selector.md), and discounted by [the Sceptic](persona_the_sceptic.md) who trusts the largest least, the honest reading the whole choice turns on.
It is the raw material of the trap: the Valuation is what [valuing](process_valuing.md) produces and [maximising](process_maximising.md) selects, the estimate with [the error](piece_the_error.md) inside it that becomes [the overstatement](piece_the_overstatement.md) once chosen, the fair number [shrinking](process_shrinking.md) pulls back toward the prior.
