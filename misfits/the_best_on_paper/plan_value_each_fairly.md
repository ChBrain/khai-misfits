# Plan: Value Each Fairly

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Assay](position_the_assay.md), inside [The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Assay](position_the_assay.md)

## Direction

Appraise each option on its own merits and report the honest number. Leave every estimate as likely to fall short of the truth as to overshoot it, so no single valuation carries a slant, and treat the noise as the fact of a hard measurement, not a flaw to hide. The remit ends at the reading: value each option fairly, hand the slate on, and leave the choosing to the chooser.

## Orders

([The Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md) values each option independently and unbiasedly, its estimate as ready to understate as to overstate, and passes the slate up without slant.)

## Implementation

([The Field](place_the_field.md); the [valuing](process_valuing.md) process; the Analyst assigns each option [a valuation](piece_the_valuation.md) with a mean-zero [error](piece_the_error.md) inside it, honest one option at a time.)

## Targets

- [ ] each option is appraised on its own merits, independently of the rest
- [ ] every estimate is unbiased, as likely to fall short as to overshoot
- [ ] the noise is left as an honest fact of measurement, not slanted away
- [ ] the slate is handed on without a thumb on any scale
