# Plot: The Top Pick

## Taxonomy

[The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Pick](place_the_pick.md), the honest slate ranked and the highest number in reach.
An optimal rule about to be applied to unbiased estimates, the top of the list the obvious choice.

## Action

[Maximising](process_maximising.md): the highest estimate is taken, and with it the option most flattered by upward error.

## Stage

[The Selector](persona_the_selector.md), on [Take the Top](plan_take_the_top.md), ranks the estimates and chooses the highest [valuation](piece_the_valuation.md), booking the [overstatement](piece_the_overstatement.md) that selection creates for whoever tops the list.
The rule is optimal and the choice is sound, and the same act that takes the best on paper conditions on the errors that ran up.

## Tension

Taking the highest of unbiased estimates is the textbook-correct rule, and the correct rule is what injects the bias.
There is no recklessness: the Selector does the plainly right thing, choosing the option that scored highest given honest numbers, with no sign in any estimate that the maximum is not innocent. But the option that tops the list is disproportionately the one the noise lifted, so the plot closes with the winner chosen and its estimate standing above its truth by the very fact of being chosen, the sound decision to take the top of the list the same decision that overstates whatever sits there.
