# Process: Valuing

## Taxonomy

[The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md). The appraisal, each option assigned an honest estimate of its worth with a mean-zero error inside it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md), at [the Field](place_the_field.md), appraising each option on its own merits.
Begins whenever an option must be assessed and its worth reduced to a number, one option at a time and independent of the rest.

## Direction

Turns a thing of uncertain worth into a single unbiased estimate.
The option is measured as accurately as it can be and returned as a value that is as likely to fall short of the truth as to overshoot it, so valuing produces a reading that is honest on its own, the noise a fact of a hard appraisal rather than a slant in the method.

## Lever

The symmetry of an honest error.
Because a fair appraisal is as ready to understate as to overstate, valuing leaves an error centred on nothing, so each estimate is beyond reproach taken alone, its lever the very even-handedness that makes no single number wrong on average.

## Echo

A slate of honest estimates, each blameless and each carrying noise.
The echo of valuing is a field of unbiased readings, so every option can be stood behind one at a time, and the symmetric errors sit invisible in the numbers, waiting for a ranking to turn the ones that ran high into a bias no appraisal committed.
