# Place: The Field

## Taxonomy

[The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md). The slate of options, each with an honest estimate of its worth, the scene before the choice.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A row of options, each with a number beside it, the estimates laid out clean and even.
Shown as a fair starting point, a set of honest appraisals with no slant among them, so the field looks like solid ground from which any sensible choice can be made.

## Holds

Holds the unbiased valuations, and the mean-zero noise inside each.
The Field is where the honest estimates sit: every option carries a value as likely too low as too high, so the slate is beyond reproach one number at a time, and the errors, symmetric and centred on nothing, are invisible as long as no one ranks them.

## Offers

Offers a clean set of numbers to choose among, each defensible on its own.
Here every estimate can be stood behind, since no single one is biased, so the field offers the decider a fair slate and the appearance that choosing from it well is only a matter of taking the largest.

## Withheld

Withholds the fact that its errors are a trap for the maximum.
What the Field hides is that its symmetric, blameless noise becomes a bias the instant the top is taken, because the highest estimate is the one whose error most likely ran up, so the slate that looks like fair ground for any choice is ground that will flatter whichever option the ranking selects.
