# Position: The Review

## Taxonomy

[The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md). The adjusting seat, which holds the prior and re-weights each estimate by its reliability before the choice is made.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A prior, and a discount to apply.
A second look: the seat holds a belief about what options are worth before any estimate is seen, and weighs each reading against it, pulling the estimate toward the prior by how noisy it is.
One instrument only, since the seat's whole lever is the shrink, a discount on the reading proportioned to its unreliability, and it has no way to add worth, only to subtract overstatement.

## Orders

Shrink to the prior.
Pull each estimate toward the prior mean in proportion to its noise, discounting a dazzling reading built on thin evidence more than a modest one built on solid, then rank what remains.
Trust the corrected number, not the raw one, because the seat's function is to de-bias the choice and the shrunk estimate is the honest expectation the raw one was not.

## Loses

The raw top, which the shrink is bound to pull down.
The Review can correct the selection only by discounting, and the discount falls hardest on the boldest estimate, so the seat cannot de-bias the choice without lowering the very reading that looked best and, often, unseating it.
What the seat forfeits is the upside it declines: able to remove the overstatement only by shrinking, it must trade the flattered winners it would have regretted for the bold winners it will never take, so the seat that alone can correct the curse is the one whose sole instrument costs it the top of the list.

## Drives

Toward a choice de-biased by discount,
and a boldest bet pulled back and passed,
so the seat that can undo the overstatement is the one that pays for the correction in the dazzling options it shrinks away.
