# Plot: The Fair Read

## Taxonomy

[The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Field](place_the_field.md), a slate of options each to be valued on its merits.
A set of honest appraisals in the making, no slant among them and no thumb on any scale.

## Action

[Valuing](process_valuing.md): each option is estimated as fairly as it can be, the reading as likely to fall short as to overshoot.

## Stage

[The Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md), on [Value Each Fairly](plan_value_each_fairly.md), assigns each option [a valuation](piece_the_valuation.md) with a mean-zero [error](piece_the_error.md) inside it, honest one option at a time.
The reading is unbiased and beyond reproach, and nothing in it shows that the ranking to come will treat its honest errors as anything but honest.

## Tension

Valuing each option fairly is exactly the standard the work should meet, and the fair valuations are the raw material the choice will bias.
There is no fault: the Analyst measures straight, leaves errors as symmetric as they should be, and produces numbers no single one of which is wrong on average. But a symmetric error is what a maximum preys on, so the plot closes with a slate of honest estimates that are beyond reproach one at a time and a trap once ranked, the even-handedness that clears each reading laid out as precisely the noise the selection will turn against the winner.
