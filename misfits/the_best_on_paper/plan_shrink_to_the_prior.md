# Plan: Shrink to the Prior

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the Review](position_the_review.md), inside [The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [The Review](position_the_review.md)

## Direction

Pull each estimate toward the prior mean in proportion to its noise before ranking anything, discounting a dazzling reading built on thin evidence more than a modest one built on solid. Trust the corrected number rather than the raw one, because the shrunk estimate is the honest expectation the raw maximum was not. Accept that the option winning after the shrink will often not be the one that scored highest before, and that the boldest reading is pulled back hardest.

## Orders

([The Sceptic](persona_the_sceptic.md) shrinks each estimate toward the prior by how noisy it is, then chooses, de-biasing the pick at the cost of the boldest bet.)

## Implementation

([The Pick](place_the_pick.md); the [shrinking](process_shrinking.md) process; the Sceptic pulls each [valuation](piece_the_valuation.md) toward the prior, removing the [overstatement](piece_the_overstatement.md) selection would create and forgoing the top of the list.)

## Targets

- [ ] each estimate is pulled toward the prior in proportion to its noise
- [ ] the boldest reading is discounted hardest, the modest solid one least
- [ ] the corrected number, not the raw maximum, governs the choice
- [ ] the correction is accepted to cost the bold bet it shrinks away
