# Process: Shrinking

## Taxonomy

[The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md). The correction, pulling each estimate toward the prior by its noise so the choice is de-biased, at the cost of the boldest reading.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

[The Sceptic](persona_the_sceptic.md), at [the Pick](place_the_pick.md), discounting the estimates before the top is taken.
Begins when a decider declines to take the raw maximum and weighs each reading against a prior first, adjusting for how much of it is noise.

## Direction

Turns a raw estimate into the honest expectation behind it.
Each reading is pulled toward the prior mean in proportion to its unreliability, so a dazzling number built on thin evidence is discounted more than a modest one built on solid, and the shrunk estimate is the value one should actually expect, the overstatement the maximum would have booked removed before the choice is made.

## Lever

The prior, which the noisier a reading the more it should govern.
Because a less reliable estimate deserves less weight against prior belief, shrinking discounts each reading by its noise, so the flattered top is pulled back hardest, its lever the Bayesian fact that evidence is worth only as much as its precision.

## Echo

A de-biased choice, and the bold bet pulled back and passed.
The echo of shrinking is a pick that will not disappoint on average, bought at a price: the boldest reading is shrunk hardest, so the option that wins after the discount is rarely the one that scored highest before, and the correction that spares the letdown forgoes, with it, the dazzling bet it declined.
