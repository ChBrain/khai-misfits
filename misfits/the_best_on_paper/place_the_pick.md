# Place: The Pick

## Taxonomy

[The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md). The moment of choosing, where the estimates are ranked and the top is taken, or shrunk first and then taken.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A ranked list and a hand on the top of it, the highest number chosen as the obvious pick.
Shown as a decision well made, the optimal rule applied to honest numbers, so the choice looks like nothing but good sense taking the best on offer.

## Holds

Holds the selection, the act that turns an honest slate into a flattered winner.
The Pick is where the maximum is taken: ranking the estimates and choosing the highest conditions on the errors that ran up, so the winner is the option whose worth and whose luck summed largest, its estimate lifted above its truth by the very act of being chosen.

## Offers

Offers the decider the top of the list, and the sceptic the one chance to shrink it first.
Here the choice can go two ways: taken raw, the maximum flatters the winner and a letdown is the expected cost; shrunk toward the prior first, the boldest reading is pulled back and the pick de-biased at the cost of the top, so the scene is where the curse is either incurred or paid down.

## Withheld

Withholds the difference between the best option and the luckiest estimate.
What the Pick hides is that its winner is chosen on a number that mixes worth with error, so at the moment of choosing there is no telling how much of the top estimate is merit to keep and how much is noise that will not return, the flattered winner and the truly best looking exactly alike on the list.
