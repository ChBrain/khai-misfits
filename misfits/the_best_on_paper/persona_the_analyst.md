# Persona: The Analyst

## Taxonomy

[The Assay](position_the_assay.md). Values each option as fairly as it can be valued, the estimate unbiased and as likely to fall short as to overshoot, and does nothing wrong at all.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Careful. Even-handed. Honest to the number.
Is the one who appraises each option on its merits and reports a value, taking no side and putting no thumb on the scale, so each estimate is as likely to land above the truth as below it.
No bias wished on them: they measure straight, their errors are centred on nothing, so no single valuation they produce is wrong on average, which is exactly the standard the work is meant to meet.

## Action

Values each option in turn, honestly and independently, and hands the decider a slate of unbiased estimates.
Answers only to accuracy on each option taken alone, so the estimate for any one option is the best guess of its worth, wrong sometimes high and sometimes low but never slanted, the noise a fact of a hard measurement and not a flaw in the method.
Does what a careful analyst does: appraises each thing on its own, reports the number they honestly reach, and leaves the choosing to the chooser, no thought that the ranking to come will treat their honest errors as anything but honest.

## Shadow

Not a fudger, but the one whose flawless honesty is the raw material the selection will bias, and that is the quiet of it: every estimate is unbiased, and the maximum of them is not, so the analyst can do everything right and still hand up numbers that the pick will flatter.
Because the Analyst values each option straight, the errors they leave are symmetric and blameless, but a symmetric error is exactly what a maximum preys on, since the option that tops the list is the one whose error ran upward, so the honest spread of noise becomes, at the moment of choosing, a systematic lift on whoever wins.
The reading is unbiased and the pick is not, so the analyst who measured every option fairly is the one whose fair measurements the selection turns into an overstatement they never made.

## Tell

Says "each of these estimates is my honest best guess, as likely too low as too high."
Is telling the exact truth of every number one at a time, and cannot see that the truth of them singly is undone in the aggregate, that taking the top of their honest list conditions on the errors that ran high, so the even-handedness that clears each estimate is what the maximum turns into a bias.
