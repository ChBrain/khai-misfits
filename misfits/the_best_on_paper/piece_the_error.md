# Piece: The Error

## Taxonomy

[The Best on Paper](play_the_best_on_paper.md). The mean-zero noise inside each valuation, symmetric and blameless alone, that selection turns against the winner.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Sits inside every [valuation](piece_the_valuation.md) at [the field](place_the_field.md), and is preyed upon at [the pick](place_the_pick.md), where the maximum selects the ones that ran up.
Flows in every estimate, the gap between the reading and the truth, as ready to understate as to overstate and averaging to nothing across the slate.

## Load Bearing

Without the Error there is no curse: a slate of exact estimates would rank by worth alone and the top would be the best.
With it the ranking is not innocent: the Error is the symmetric noise in each reading (Galton), centred on nothing and no one's fault, but a maximum conditions on it, since the option that tops the list is the one whose error most likely ran up. The Error is load bearing because it is blameless taken alone and decisive taken at the top, the very thing that makes each estimate honest and the thing selection weaponises, so the piece carries the innocent noise the curse is built from, the mean-zero gap that averages away everywhere except where the choice lands.

## Apparent

Just noise, the ordinary imprecision of any hard estimate.
Seen at the surface, the Error looks like nothing to worry over, a symmetric wobble that cancels out across many options, and the truth that the maximum does not average it but selects it, that a mean-zero error becomes a one-sided lift for whoever wins, that the noise no appraisal can help is exactly what the ranking turns into a bias, stays hidden behind how plainly it all washes out on average.

## Yearbook

Left honestly by [the Analyst](persona_the_analyst.md), preyed on by [the Selector](persona_the_selector.md) who cannot see it, and reckoned with by [the Sceptic](persona_the_sceptic.md) who discounts for it, the blameless noise the trap is built from.
It is the innocent root of the trap: the Error is what [valuing](process_valuing.md) leaves and [maximising](process_maximising.md) conditions on, the symmetric gap inside [the valuation](piece_the_valuation.md) that the pick turns into [the overstatement](piece_the_overstatement.md) and [regressing](process_regressing.md) later undoes.
