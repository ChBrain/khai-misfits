# Plot: The Windfall

## Taxonomy

[Bootleggers and Baptists](play_bootleggers_and_baptists.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The Market](place_the_market.md), the rule's real effect landing as a protected gain.
A quiet interest the law enriches, with a stake sharp enough to be worth defending.

## Action

[Profiting](process_profiting.md): the interest banks the earning the rule creates and spends to keep the law, never arguing for it in its own name.

## Stage

[The Bootlegger](persona_the_bootlegger.md), on [Bank the Ban](plan_bank_the_ban.md), banks [the profit](piece_the_profit.md) the rule hands them and funds the defence of [the rule](piece_the_rule.md), organising where the diffuse public will not and letting the principle do the talking.
The gain is rational and the interest deceives no one, only backs a law that helps it and stays out of the light.

## Tension

Defending an advantage the law hands you is the rational act, and the rational act supplies the muscle the coalition needs.
There is no scheme: the interest backs a rule that pays it, as any interest would, and need neither manufacture the principle nor meet the Baptist. But a concentrated gain organises where a diffuse cost cannot, so the plot closes with the rule given a resourced, invisible defender, the profit banked and the muscle ready, and no deception required to keep it hidden.
