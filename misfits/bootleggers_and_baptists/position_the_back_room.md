# Position: The Back Room

## Taxonomy

[Bootleggers and Baptists](play_bootleggers_and_baptists.md). The profiting seat, where the rule is quietly banked and defended, out of the light and never argued for in its own name.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A protected gain, and the concentrated stake to defend it.
An interest the rule serves: the licence, the tariff, the ban that hands a monopoly, a benefit large and particular enough to be worth organising and paying for.
No case it can make in the open, since a profit has no argument fit for a podium, and none it needs while the principle speaks for the same law.

## Orders

Bank the ban.
Gain from the rule and spend to keep it, funding the defence of the law that protects the earning and letting the moral case do the public arguing.
Organise where the diffuse public will not, since the seat feels the stake sharply enough to show up when they do not.

## Loses

Its exposure, which the principle spares it.
Because the same rule the Pulpit preaches is the rule the Back Room banks, the seat need neither coordinate with the moralist nor be acknowledged by them, so the interest that would be seen and resisted on its own hides behind a conviction it never has to share, its money defending a law its name never touches.
It loses nothing and keeps everything, the one seat in the coalition that pays in cash and is never seen, safe behind a cover it did not have to buy.

## Drives

Toward a profit that need never speak,
and a principle that speaks for it,
so the seat with no case of its own is defended by the case of another, the interest riding hidden inside the rule.
