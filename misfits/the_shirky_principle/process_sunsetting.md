# Process: Sunsetting

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The incentive flipped: outcome funded, ending planned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An outside hand that sees the body cannot want its own success.
The decision to pay for the problem shrinking and to write an ending into the mandate.

## Direction

Money moving from the activity to the outcome, and a close-on-success clause into the charter.
Reward tied to the problem getting smaller, obsolescence planned rather than avoided.

## Lever

A measure taken from outside, where the interest in the problem's survival cannot reach.
Only an external eye can score the outcome honestly, because every inside seat is paid by the problem continuing.

## Echo

A body that is, for once, rewarded for making itself unnecessary.
An incentive pointed at the cure ... held only as long as the outside hand keeps it there, and undone the moment the sunset is repealed as reckless.
