# Persona: The Salaried

## Taxonomy

[Beneficiary](position_beneficiary.md). The one whose living depends on the problem lasting.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Committed, hard-working, glad of the work.
Draws a post the problem funds, and does it well and in good conscience.
Would be the last to say the problem should persist, and would mean that too.

## Action

Turns up, delivers, and depends, quietly, on the problem being there tomorrow.
Reasons its way, sincerely, to why the cure is not yet ready, the scope not yet complete, the need not yet met.
Never lies, and never quite reaches the conclusion its salary forbids.

## Shadow

It cannot understand the one thing its living depends on it not understanding.
The interest is invisible from the inside, so the blindness is honest, and honest blindness is the strongest kind, defended as integrity.
Not bought: simply unable to see past the paycheck it never thinks about.

## Tell

"I am just here to help."
Said truly, by someone who also needs the thing they are helping with to remain.
