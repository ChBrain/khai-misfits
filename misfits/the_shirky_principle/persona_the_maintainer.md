# Persona: The Maintainer

## Taxonomy

[Operator](position_operator.md). The one who solves the problem just enough, and no further.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Competent, steady, a safe pair of hands.
Runs the work day to day and keeps the problem from ever getting out of hand.
Prides itself on a situation always under control.

## Action

Relieves the symptom that would force a real fix, and files the cause as ongoing.
Solves to the level that keeps the pressure off and the franchise intact, never to the level that ends the need.
Calls a problem managed and means a problem kept.

## Shadow

Its competence is aimed at a simmer, not a cure.
A fully solved problem would be a job done and a job gone, so the skill is bent, without a decision, toward control rather than completion.
Mistakes managing the problem forever for solving it, because the two look identical from inside a good quarter.

## Tell

"We have it well in hand."
The steady-state report of a problem being held exactly where it pays to hold it.
