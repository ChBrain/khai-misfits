# Process: Displacing

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The body's survival quietly replacing its mission as the real goal.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An institution whose continuation depends on the problem continuing.
The slow drift that begins the moment the mission and the livelihood stop being the same thing.

## Direction

The stated goal, ending the problem, giving way to the unstated one, keeping the body.
Means becoming ends: procedures, budgets, and posts defended as though they were the cause.

## Lever

Interested blindness, which needs no dishonesty to work.
Those whose living depends on the problem simply never reach the conclusion their salary forbids, so the displacement happens sincerely and unseen.

## Echo

A mission still spoken and a survival actually served.
The cause honoured in every report and quietly subordinated to the institution that was built to advance it.
