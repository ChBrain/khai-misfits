# Persona: The Steward

## Taxonomy

[Custodian](position_custodian.md). The one who holds the mission, and the body the mission built.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Devoted, credible, mission-first.
Founded or inherited a body to solve a real problem, and believes in it wholly.
Speaks for the cause and means every word.

## Action

Holds the mandate and keeps the body funded and staffed.
Renews the appeal each cycle: the problem is real, the work is vital, the need goes on.
Guards the institution as though guarding the mission, because to it they are the same thing.

## Shadow

Cannot tell the mission from the body that carries it.
The end of the problem would be the end of the thing it has built and led, so it flinches from the cure without ever deciding to, and calls the flinch prudence.
Sincere all the way down, and structurally unable to want to win.

## Tell

"There is still so much work to do."
True, and the sentence that keeps the doors open whether or not it is.
