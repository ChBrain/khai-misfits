# Position: Beneficiary

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The seat whose livelihood the problem funds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A post, a salary, and a career the problem pays for.
Real skill and a genuine wish to help.
A living that quietly ends the day the problem does.

## Orders

Do the work well and in good conscience.
Serve the mission and trust that serving it is what is asked.
Keep the post, which means keep the body, which means keep the problem funded.

## Loses

The capacity to see its own interest.
The one conclusion its salary forbids is the one it never reaches, so its blindness is honest and therefore total, defended as integrity rather than felt as bias.
It cannot understand the thing its living depends on it not understanding.

## Drives

Toward reasons the cure is not yet ready,
reached sincerely and every time,
by someone who would be appalled to be told why they keep reaching them.
