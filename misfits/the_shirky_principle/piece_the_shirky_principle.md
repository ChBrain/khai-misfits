# Piece: The Shirky Principle

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The phenomenon whole: a solver that preserves its problem, held whole by nobody.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across the whole run, from [the charter](place_the_charter.md) to [the sunset review](place_the_sunset_review.md).
And wherever an institution's resources depend on the problem it exists to solve continuing to exist.

## Load Bearing

Without it the parts are a scatter: a mandate, a livelihood, a half-cure, an expansion.
With it they are one law: an institution funded by a problem comes to need the problem, so it preserves the thing it was built to remove. The Shirky Principle is load bearing because it is the whole read at once (Shirky; Kelly), and it names the only exit: not a more devoted body but a changed incentive, funding the outcome and sunsetting the mandate so that success, and not persistence, is the thing that pays. It is the provider's mirror of the perverse incentive, a cure that quietly keeps the disease.

## Apparent

A worthy body doing sincere, ongoing work.
It looks like an institution serving its mission faithfully, year after year, needed as ever. Nothing on the surface is a failure, and that is the whole of it, because the problem is preserved not by any cynic but by a structure of sincere people every one of whose incentives points away from the cure.

## Yearbook

Held whole by nobody: assembled from [the mandate](piece_the_mandate.md), [the livelihood](piece_the_livelihood.md), and [the perpetuation](piece_the_perpetuation.md), and answered only, and from outside, by [the sunsetter](persona_the_sunsetter.md).
It is the misfit named: the body built to end a problem becomes the reason it endures, and can be cured only by being paid to make itself unnecessary.
