# Position: Empire-Builder

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The seat that widens the problem so the work never ends.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The initiative to grow the mandate and the budget with it.
The framing that casts every expansion as more mission.
An eye for new instances of the problem, and a talent for finding them.

## Orders

Grow the remit and the resources it commands.
Redefine the threat as the old version fades, and launch the next programme before the last problem ends.
Keep the work inexhaustible by keeping the problem open.

## Loses

Any honest measure of success.
Because a shrinking problem is a shrinking budget, it enlarges the problem to fit the body rather than shrink the body to fit the problem, and calls the manufacture service.
The finish line moves out exactly as fast as it is approached.

## Drives

Toward a problem always bigger than last year,
grown to match the institution,
so the mandate renews on a need the mandate itself keeps making.
