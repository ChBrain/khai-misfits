# Position: Operator

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The seat that runs the work and holds the problem at a simmer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The day-to-day handling of the problem.
The skill to relieve the symptom and keep things under control.
The discretion over how far a fix is taken.

## Orders

Keep the problem from getting out of hand.
Relieve what presses, manage the rest, report it well in hand.
Take the fix to the level that keeps the pressure off, and file the cause as ongoing.

## Loses

The completion its competence could reach.
A solved problem is a job done and a job gone, so the skill bends toward control rather than cure, without a decision anyone makes.
Managing forever and solving become indistinguishable from inside a good quarter.

## Drives

Toward a steady simmer,
a problem held exactly where it pays to hold it,
called managed and meaning kept.
