# Position: Custodian

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The seat that holds the mission and the body built to serve it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

A founding mandate and the authority to renew it.
A budget, a staff, and a standing, all pinned to the problem.
A sincere devotion to the cause the body was made to serve.

## Orders

Keep the mission alive and the body funded to pursue it.
Make the case each cycle that the problem is real and the need goes on.
Guard the institution as the vessel of the mission.

## Loses

The ability to tell the mission from the vessel.
Ending the problem would end the body it has built, so it defends the body in the mission's name and cannot feel the difference.
The cure becomes the one outcome it is structurally unable to want.

## Drives

Toward a mission that never finishes,
because a finished mission is a closed institution,
and every renewal of the mandate feels like the cause being served.
