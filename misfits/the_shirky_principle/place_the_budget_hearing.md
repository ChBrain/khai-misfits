# Place: The Budget Hearing

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). Where funding renews on the problem's persistence.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A review of the year, a case for next year's money, a problem reported as real and ongoing.
The renewal where the body asks to continue and gives its reasons.

## Holds

The incentive laid bare.
Here a persisting problem is a renewed budget and a solved one is a closed line, so the case that pays is always the problem well in hand and never the problem ended.

## Offers

A fair account of good work.
Real progress, genuine effort, a situation managed and a need still present, all true, all fundable.

## Withheld

A reward for finishing.
Nothing in the hearing pays a body for making itself unnecessary, so the report that renews the mandate is managed, ongoing, still needed, never done.
