# Piece: The Perpetuation

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The problem kept alive: scope widened, new instances found, the threat kept warm.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the frontier](place_the_frontier.md), where the mandate grows into fresh ground.
Wherever an ending problem is met with expansion instead of celebration.

## Load Bearing

Without it a problem that genuinely faded would simply end, and the body with it.
With it fading is never allowed to finish: as the original cause recedes, the problem is enlarged, redefined, or renewed, so the work is inexhaustible by design. The Perpetuation is load bearing because it is the active preservation the principle predicts (Niskanen; Parkinson), the budget-maximising reflex that grows the problem to fit the institution, turning the body from a solver of a fixed problem into a manufacturer of an endless one.

## Apparent

Ambition, and a mission advancing.
It looks like growth to be proud of: new programmes, wider reach, more of the good work. Every expansion is framed as the cause going further, so the manufacture of more problem wears the face of progress.

## Yearbook

Driven by [the expander](persona_the_expander.md) through [expanding](process_expanding.md), and excused by [the displacement](piece_the_displacement.md).
It is the misfit reaching outward: the Perpetuation ensures the problem never runs out, so [the obsolescence](piece_the_obsolescence.md) that a finished problem would bring is pushed forever over the horizon.
