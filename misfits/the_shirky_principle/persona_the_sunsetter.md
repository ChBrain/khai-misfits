# Persona: The Sunsetter

## Taxonomy

[Reformer](position_reformer.md). The one who makes success the thing that pays, and endings the thing that is planned.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Outside, clear-eyed, a little ruthless.
Funds the outcome rather than the activity, and writes an ending into the mandate.
Willing to pay a body to make itself unnecessary.

## Action

Ties the money to the problem shrinking, not to the work continuing.
Sets a sunset on the charter, so success closes the body cleanly and on purpose.
Measures the outcome from outside, where the interest in the problem's survival cannot reach.

## Shadow

Its fix asks an institution to be organised to end itself.
Everything inside the body is arranged to renew the mandate, so the sunset reads as an attack on good people doing real work, and one repeal restores the whole incentive.
Right, resented, and always one budget cycle from being undone.

## Tell

"This body should be trying to put itself out of business."
The one sentence no institution funded by a problem can say about itself.
