# Place: The Frontier

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). Where the problem is widened and new instances are found.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A new initiative, an expanded remit, a threat redefined a size larger than before.
The edge where the mandate grows into fresh ground.

## Holds

The manufacture of more problem.
Here the original cause, as it fades, is not declared won but enlarged, so the work never runs out and the finish line moves out exactly as fast as it is approached.

## Offers

Growth, and the look of ambition.
More scope, more mission, more to do, all framed as the cause advancing rather than the body feeding.

## Withheld

The moment of enough.
The frontier has no far edge, because the point of it is that there is always more problem to find, so the body can grow forever and never arrive.
