# Piece: The Livelihood

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The budget, jobs, and standing that last only while the problem does.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the budget hearing](place_the_budget_hearing.md), where next year's money rests on this year's problem.
And in every pay packet the problem funds.

## Load Bearing

Without it the people inside could weigh the cure on its merits, with nothing of their own at stake.
With it every one of them has a living tied to the problem continuing, so the incentive is not abstract but personal, felt in the mortgage and the career. The Livelihood is load bearing because it is what makes the blindness sincere (Sinclair): it is difficult to get a person to understand a thing when their salary depends on not understanding it, so the interest in the problem's survival is defended not as greed but as conviction.

## Apparent

Just having a job.
It looks like nothing more than people earning a living doing work they believe in, which is what it is. The interest hides inside the ordinariness of a paycheck no one thinks about, which is exactly what lets it steer.

## Yearbook

Drawn by [the salaried](persona_the_salaried.md), and never quite seen by them.
It is the quiet engine: the Livelihood turns a room of honest people into a body that cannot afford the cure, feeding [the displacement](piece_the_displacement.md) one sincere, unreachable conclusion at a time.
