# Position: Reformer

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The seat that funds the outcome and sunsets the mandate.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The purse, and the power to tie it to results rather than activity.
The authority to write an ending into a charter.
A vantage outside the body, where the interest in the problem cannot reach.

## Orders

Pay for the problem shrinking, not for the work continuing.
Set a sunset so success closes the body cleanly and on purpose.
Measure the outcome from outside and flip the incentive.

## Loses

The goodwill of everyone the sunset threatens.
Asking a body to be organised to end itself reads as an attack on real people doing real work, so the reform is resented where it bites and undone by a single repeal.
Right, unpopular, and never more than one budget cycle from reversal.

## Drives

Toward an institution paid to make itself unnecessary,
which is the one thing a body funded by a problem is built never to do,
so the fix must be imposed and re-imposed from outside.
