# Pitch: Still Needed

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Earnest, devoted, mission-first.
A body that believes in the problem it fights and says so, sincerely, in every report and every appeal.
The voice of people who came to solve something real and are still solving it, still needed, still here.

## Undertow

Under the devotion, a body that cannot afford to win.
Everything it is, the budget, the posts, the standing, lasts only while the problem does, so a full cure is a resignation letter, and the mission quietly bends to keep the problem, and the body, alive.
Nobody schemes: the ones whose living depends on the problem simply, honestly, never come to see it that way.

## Nerve

The feeling of good work sincerely done,
the problem fought and never quite finished,
each renewal of the mandate a vindication and each near-solution a threat no one will name.

## Echo

After the run: a problem kept at a simmer,
a mandate that widened every time the old cause faded,
and an institution that outlived the thing it was built to end, by making sure it never did.
