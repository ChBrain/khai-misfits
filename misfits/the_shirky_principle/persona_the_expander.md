# Persona: The Expander

## Taxonomy

[Empire-Builder](position_empire_builder.md). The one who keeps the problem from ever running out.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Ambitious, forward-looking, full of new initiatives.
Sees the mandate as a thing to grow, and growth as obviously good.
Frames every widening as more of the mission, and believes it.

## Action

Widens the remit, finds new instances, redefines the threat as the old one fades.
Launches the next programme the moment the last problem shows signs of ending.
Keeps the work inexhaustible by keeping the definition of the problem open.

## Shadow

Its growth is the problem being manufactured to match the body.
When the original cause recedes, it does not celebrate but expands, because an ending problem is a shrinking budget, so it enlarges the problem to fit the institution rather than the reverse.
Calls empire-building service, and cannot see the difference.

## Tell

"The problem is bigger than we thought."
Announced, reliably, just as the problem was about to get smaller.
