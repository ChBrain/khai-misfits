# Place: The Charter

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). Where the body is defined by the problem it exists to solve.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A founding document, a mission statement, a reason for being.
The problem named as the thing this body was made to end.

## Holds

The bond between mission and existence.
Here the institution is tied to its problem so completely that the problem's end would be the institution's, and no one reading the charter can feel the difference between serving the cause and preserving the body.

## Offers

A clear and worthy purpose.
Something real to fight, a reason to fund and to staff, a mission sincere enough that everyone inside believes it.

## Withheld

Any clause for its own success.
The charter says how to pursue the problem and never what to do when it is solved, so the one event it does not plan for is the victory it was written to seek.
