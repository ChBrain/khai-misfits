# Plan: Solve Just Enough

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the operator's seat](position_operator.md), inside [The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Operator](position_operator.md)

## Direction

Keep the problem from getting out of hand. Relieve what presses, manage the rest, and report it well in hand. Take the fix to the level that keeps the pressure off and the franchise intact, and file the cause as ongoing.

## Orders

([The Maintainer](persona_the_maintainer.md) relieves the symptom that would force a real fix and calls the problem managed.)

## Implementation

([The Budget Hearing](place_the_budget_hearing.md); the [simmering](process_simmering.md) process; [the half-cure](piece_the_half_cure.md) holds the problem exactly where it pays to hold it.)

## Targets

- [ ] the pressing symptom is relieved
- [ ] the cause is filed as ongoing work
- [ ] the problem is held at a steady simmer
- [ ] managing forever and solving become indistinguishable
