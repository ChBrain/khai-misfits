# Piece: The Displacement

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The institution's survival quietly replacing its mission as the true objective.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

Across the whole body, in the slow drift from serving the cause to preserving the vessel.
Wherever a procedure, a budget, or a post is defended as though it were the mission.

## Load Bearing

Without it the body would notice the moment its survival and its mission parted, and correct.
With it the parting is never noticed, because survival dresses in the mission's clothes: the same words, the same reports, the same sincerity, now serving the institution rather than the cause. The Displacement is load bearing because it is the goal-substitution the principle runs on (Merton), means become ends, so a body can pursue its own continuation wholeheartedly while believing, honestly, that it is pursuing the problem.

## Apparent

Devotion to the mission.
It looks like a body more committed than ever: defending its work, protecting its people, fighting for its funding, all in the cause's name. The commitment is real, which is why no one inside can see that its object has changed.

## Yearbook

Enacted through [displacing](process_displacing.md), driven by [the livelihood](piece_the_livelihood.md), and unseen by [the steward](persona_the_steward.md) who leads it.
It is the misfit's sleight of hand: the Displacement lets [the perpetuation](piece_the_perpetuation.md) feel like service, so growing the problem and serving the mission become, from inside, the same act.
