# Process: Simmering

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). The problem solved just enough to stay managed and never ended.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A problem that could be closed, and a body that would close with it.
The pressure to relieve the symptom without removing the reason to be funded.

## Direction

The problem held at a steady, controllable level, eased when it flares and never cured.
Symptoms relieved, causes filed as ongoing, the whole thing kept just below the boil.

## Lever

The gap between managing a problem and solving it, which looks the same from inside a good quarter.
Competence aimed at control rather than completion, without anyone deciding it should be.

## Echo

A problem that never gets worse and never goes away.
A steady simmer reported as a situation well in hand, renewing the mandate on the very fact that it is not yet done.
