# Place: The Sunset Review

## Taxonomy

[The Shirky Principle](play_the_shirky_principle.md). Where the outcome is funded and an ending is written in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An outside panel, a measure of the problem shrinking, a clause that closes the body on success.
The one review that asks not how the work went but whether the problem got smaller.

## Holds

The flipped incentive.
Here success pays and obsolescence is planned, so for once the body is rewarded for shrinking the problem, and the interest in preserving it has no seat at the table.

## Offers

The only real cure for the misfit.
Money tied to outcome, a mandate that ends on victory, and a measure taken from outside where the problem's survival cannot reach.

## Withheld

The consent of the body it reviews.
The sunset asks an institution to be organised to end itself, which everyone inside experiences as an attack, so the review holds only as long as an outside hand keeps re-imposing it against the pull to repeal.
