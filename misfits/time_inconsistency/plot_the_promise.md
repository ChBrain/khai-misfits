# Plot: The Promise

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The podium](place_the_podium.md), an authority announcing a plan exactly right to announce.
Stable prices, an honoured patent, no ransom, no tax on capital that comes, and someone it is meant to move to act.

## Action

[Promising](process_promising.md): [The Authority](persona_the_authority.md), holding [the authority's seat](position_authority.md), declares the plan and means it, while [The Agent](persona_the_agent.md), holding [the agent's seat](position_agent.md), hears a promise that would, if kept, be worth acting on.

## Stage

[The Authority](persona_the_authority.md) tables [Do What's Best Now](plan_do_whats_best_now.md): make the plan best today, and keep the options open for tomorrow, so [the promise](piece_the_promise.md) is sincere and optimal to make.
[The Agent](persona_the_agent.md) weighs whether to act on it, the whole good outcome resting on whether the word will hold.

## Tension

The promise is perfect, and its perfection depends on one thing it cannot supply.
Nothing is wrong: the plan is genuinely good, plainly meant, and would move any listener if believed. That is the trap laid, not sprung: a sincere pledge whose entire worth is that it be relied upon, made by a hand that keeps, for tomorrow, the very freedom that will let it set the pledge down.
