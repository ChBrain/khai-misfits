# Place: The Podium

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). Where the promise is announced, and later revisited.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An authority at a lectern, a plan declared: stable prices, an honoured patent, no ransom, no tax on capital that comes.
And, the next day, the same lectern, the same authority, the plan now up for review.

## Holds

The two moments in one place.
Here the promise is made when making it is optimal, and here it is reconsidered when keeping it is not, so the podium holds both the sincere pledge and the reasonable second thought that empties it.

## Offers

A promise worth acting on, if it would hold.
The plan is genuinely good, plainly meant, and would move any listener to invest or invent or trust, so the podium offers exactly the inducement the outcome needs, on the one condition it cannot meet.

## Withheld

Any reason to keep the word tomorrow.
The podium grants the authority the same freedom next day that it had today, so nothing here binds the second moment to the first, and the promise carries no weight it cannot later set down.
