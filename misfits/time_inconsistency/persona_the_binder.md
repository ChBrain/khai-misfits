# Persona: The Binder

## Taxonomy

[Binder](position_binder.md). The one who ties the authority's hands, so the promise cannot be broken.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Deliberate, far-sighted, willing to give up power on purpose.
Understands that a promise is worth only as much as it cannot be broken, and moves to make it unbreakable.
Prefers a hand it cannot move to a word no one will trust.

## Action

Removes the discretion at the source.
Delegates the choice to a rule or an independent body that cannot renege, writes the promise into a constraint, or lashes the authority to the mast before the song can reach it.
Makes reneging impossible, so the agent, seeing it removed, acts at last and the good outcome forms.

## Shadow

The tie that saves the promise is the tie that cannot be undone when it should be.
It buys credibility by destroying flexibility, so the hand it binds against a foreseen temptation is the same hand bound against an unforeseen storm, and the very unbreakability that makes the promise good is what makes the rule unable to bend when bending is right.
Not a jailer by cruelty: one who trades the freedom to choose for the power to be believed, and pays for it later.

## Tell

"Take the choice away from me, so my promise will hold."
The deliberate surrender that makes the word credible and the hand immovable at once.
