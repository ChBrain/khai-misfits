# Plan: Do What's Best Now

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the authority's seat](position_authority.md), inside [Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Authority](position_authority.md)

## Direction

Make the plan that is best today, and mean it, to move someone to act. Then keep your options open, judge each later moment on its own merits, and do what is best when the time comes. Bind yourself to nothing the facts might outgrow.

## Orders

([The Authority](persona_the_authority.md) promises sincerely, and reneges correctly once the inducement's work is done.)

## Implementation

([The Podium](place_the_podium.md); the [promising](process_promising.md) process; [the promise](piece_the_promise.md) is optimal to make, and by the second moment [the temptation](piece_the_temptation.md) makes it optimal to break.)

## Targets

- [ ] the plan best today is announced, and sincerely meant
- [ ] the options are kept open and each later moment judged fresh
- [ ] once the acting is done, the promise costs and buys nothing
- [ ] the reasonable authority reneges, correctly, at the second moment
