# Persona: The Adviser

## Taxonomy

[Adviser](position_adviser.md). The one who names the trap: that discretion cannot hold a promise, and only a rule can.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Analytic, unsentimental, two moves ahead.
Sees both moments at once: the promise optimal now, the break optimal later, the agent's foresight between.
Holds the unwelcome truth that a free and reasonable authority is precisely the one that cannot be believed.

## Action

Names the inconsistency, and prescribes the cure.
Shows the authority that its own future judgement will break the word, and the agent that this is why the promise is empty, and says the thing neither wants to hear: rules, not discretion.
Recommends surrendering the very flexibility the authority values, because nothing less will make the promise hold.

## Shadow

The remedy it prescribes is the loss of the freedom that hired it.
Its diagnosis is correct and its cure is to tie the hands of the one who takes its advice, so the counsel that would save the outcome asks the authority to give up the discretion it was elected or appointed to exercise, and is resisted for exactly that reason.
The clearest reader of the trap prescribes the one medicine the patient least wants to swallow.

## Tell

"You cannot keep this promise. You can only make it unbreakable."
The finding that offers credibility solely at the price of freedom.
