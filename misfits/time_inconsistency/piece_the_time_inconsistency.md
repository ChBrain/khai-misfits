# Piece: The Time Inconsistency

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The phenomenon whole: the plan best made and worst kept, and its remedy in surrendered discretion at a cost.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the mast](place_the_mast.md), where the discretion is given up.
And across both moments, in every promise a free hand cannot hold.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a heap of moments with no shape.
With it the moments become one law: the promise optimal to make is not optimal to keep, so a free authority will reasonably break it, a reasoning agent will foresee the break and never act, and the good outcome forms only if the authority ties its own hands, which then cannot bend to a storm. The Time Inconsistency is load bearing because it is the whole finding (Kydland & Prescott) with its whole price attached: it holds the misfit together, and it holds that the remedy is rules over discretion, credibility bought by surrendering the flexibility that discretion prized, not willpower keeping a promise a free hand never could.

## Apparent

Either a call to always keep your word, or a case for never tying your hands.
Seen from one side it looks like a lesson in integrity, from the other like a warning against rigid rules, and both miss the structure: the honest promise cannot be kept by resolve, only made unbreakable, and the unbreakability that saves the outcome is the same that binds in the storm, so it is a trade, not a virtue and not a folly.
