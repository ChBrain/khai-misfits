# Position: Successor

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The seat that inherits the binding, and meets the storm it cannot answer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An office whose discretion was surrendered before its time.
A shock no one foresaw, and a clear sight of what it calls for.
A rule that forbids exactly the thing the moment needs.

## Orders

Hold the rule the binding set.
Do not break it for the storm, though the storm begs.
Keep the credibility every earlier promise was built on, whatever it costs now.

## Loses

The flexibility the moment demands, and bears the cost of a hand it did not tie.
The binding was aimed at a foreseen temptation and lands on an unforeseen emergency, so the price of the commitment falls on the seat that needed discretion, not the one that needed credit.
Trapped by a promise whose bill came due on its watch.

## Drives

Toward keeping the rule that traps it,
against a moment that would be better served by breaking it,
so the tie that secured the outcome is paid for by whoever holds the office when the storm arrives.
