# Position: Binder

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The seat that removes the discretion, so the promise cannot be broken.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The means to tie the authority's hands: a rule, a delegation, a mast.
The understanding that a promise is worth only what it cannot be broken.
The willingness to give up flexibility on purpose to gain credibility.

## Orders

Take the choice away at the source.
Delegate to a body that cannot renege, or write the promise into a binding constraint.
Make reneging impossible, so the promise is believed and the agent acts.

## Loses

The freedom to choose, deliberately, and the power to bend later.
It buys credibility by destroying discretion, so the hand it ties against a foreseen temptation is tied also against an unforeseen storm, and the unbreakability that makes the promise good is the rigidity that will cost.
Wins belief now by surrendering the judgement that a later moment might have needed.

## Drives

Toward a promise made unbreakable,
away from the discretion that emptied it,
so credibility is bought with the very flexibility whose loss will bite in the storm.
