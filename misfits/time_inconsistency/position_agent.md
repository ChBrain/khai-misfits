# Position: Agent

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The seat the promise is meant to move, that reasons forward and withholds.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

An action the promise is meant to induce: to invest, invent, trust, or restrain.
The wit to reason past the promise to the moment it will be tested.
No protection against a break except declining to rely on the word.

## Orders

Reason the promise forward.
Ask whether it will be kept when keeping it is costly.
Act only on what will hold, and withhold from what will not.

## Loses

The gain the promise offered, and rightly refuses it.
Foreseeing the reneging, it does not take the induced action, so it forgoes the good outcome it cannot safely reach, and its correct caution is what leaves the outcome unbuilt.
Punished for prudence only in that prudence, here, means not acting at all.

## Drives

Toward acting only on a promise that will hold,
away from a pledge the promiser will be right to break,
so the outcome dies of the agent's justified refusal, not of any broken word.
