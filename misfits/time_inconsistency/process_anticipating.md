# Process: Anticipating

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The agent reasoning the break forward, and withholding the induced action.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A promise heard, and an agent who can reason two moves ahead.
The plain logic that the authority will, at the second moment, be right to renege.

## Direction

Toward the induced action withheld.
The agent foresees the break before it comes, discounts the promise for it, and declines to invest, invent, or trust on a word it knows will not survive.

## Lever

Foresight of a break that has not yet happened.
The agent does not wait to be betrayed; it reasons the betrayal out in advance and acts on the forecast, so the promise is defeated by anticipation, not by any actual reneging.

## Echo

An outcome dead before it formed.
The induced action never taken, the good result never built, and a promise proved worthless without the promiser ever having to break it.
