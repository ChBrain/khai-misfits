# Plan: Tie the Hands

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the binder's seat](position_binder.md), inside [Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md).

## Owner

- Owner: [Binder](position_binder.md)

## Direction

Make the promise unbreakable at the source. Delegate the choice to a rule or an independent body that cannot renege, write the pledge into a binding constraint, lash the authority to the mast before the song can reach it. Give up the freedom to choose, so the promise can be believed.

## Orders

([The Binder](persona_the_binder.md) removes the discretion, so reneging becomes impossible and the agent acts at last.)

## Implementation

([The Mast](place_the_mast.md); the [committing](process_committing.md) process; [the commitment](piece_the_commitment.md) removes [the temptation](piece_the_temptation.md) by force, buying credibility at the price the storm will later charge.)

## Targets

- [ ] the discretion is surrendered and the choice handed to a rule or a body
- [ ] reneging is made impossible, not merely unwise
- [ ] the agent, seeing the break removed, takes the induced action
- [ ] the good outcome returns, and the hand is now immovable
