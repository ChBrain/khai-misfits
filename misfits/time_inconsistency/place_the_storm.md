# Place: The Storm

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). Where an unforeseen shock meets a rule that cannot bend.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

A crisis no one wrote the rule for, and a bound officer who can see exactly what it calls for.
The right move plain, and forbidden, the rope holding fast as the wind rises.

## Holds

The rigidity, come due.
Here the binding meets the shock it was never shaped for, so the rule that secured the promise now forbids the very thing the moment needs, and the storm holds the price of the commitment paid in full.

## Offers

A hard proof that the tie is real.
The rule holds when holding it hurts, so the storm offers the final evidence that the commitment was genuine, an unbreakability that does not blink even when breaking would be right.

## Withheld

The discretion the moment deserves.
The one time judgement should have been trusted, the rule allows none, so the storm withholds the flexibility the emergency calls for, and exacts, from whoever holds the office, the cost of a promise made unbreakable.
