# Position: Authority

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The seat that makes the promise and holds the discretion to break it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The power to announce a plan and to revise it.
A promise optimal to make now, and a free hand to judge each later moment fresh.
No way to bind its own future judgement, which is exactly the judgement that will renege.

## Orders

Make the plan that is best today.
Keep your options open.
Do what is best when each moment comes.

## Loses

Its credibility, by keeping its freedom.
Because it will reasonably renege when reneging is optimal, no promise it makes can be believed, so the flexibility it prizes empties its word and destroys the outcome the word was meant to buy.
Trusted with discretion, and untrustworthy because of it.

## Drives

Toward a free hand and a fresh judgement each moment,
away from any promise that could bind it,
so the reasonable authority is the one authority no promise can make believed.
