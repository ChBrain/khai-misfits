# Piece: The Rigidity

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The price of commitment: the bound hand that cannot move when a genuine shock says it should.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the storm](place_the_storm.md), the rule meeting a shock it was never shaped for.
Where the right move is plain and forbidden.

## Load Bearing

Without it commitment would be a free cure and the misfit would end happily.
With it the cure has a price: the same binding that removes the temptation removes every later choice, so when an unforeseen storm calls for exactly the move the rule forbids, the hand cannot make it, and holding the rule means doing the wrong thing for the moment to keep the credibility for the whole. The Rigidity is load bearing because it is the cost of delegation and rules that the literature names beside the cure (Rogoff), the flexibility surrendered to gain credibility, and the reason the remedy is a genuine trade and not a free lunch.

## Apparent

Needless stubbornness, or a rule outliving its sense.
Keeping a rule through a crisis it plainly does not fit looks like blind rigidity, so the cost reads as folly, when it is the honest price of the very unbreakability that made the promise work, paid exactly where the binding was never meant to reach.

## Yearbook

Borne by [the bound](persona_the_bound.md) through [committing](process_committing.md), the shadow of [the commitment](piece_the_commitment.md).
It is the bill the cure defers: the Rigidity is what makes [the time inconsistency](piece_the_time_inconsistency.md) a trade of freedom for credibility, not a costless escape.
