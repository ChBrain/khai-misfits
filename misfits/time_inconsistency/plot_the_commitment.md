# Plot: The Commitment

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The mast](place_the_mast.md), where someone moves to make the promise worth believing by making it impossible to break.
A rule to adopt, a choice to delegate, a rope to bind before the song reaches the ship.

## Action

[Committing](process_committing.md): [The Binder](persona_the_binder.md), holding [the binder's seat](position_binder.md), ties the authority's hands and hands the choice to a body that cannot renege, while [The Authority](persona_the_authority.md), holding [the authority's seat](position_authority.md), surrenders the discretion it prized.

## Stage

[The Binder](persona_the_binder.md) tables [Tie the Hands](plan_tie_the_hands.md): remove the choice at the source, so [the commitment](piece_the_commitment.md) makes reneging impossible and the agent, seeing the break gone, acts at last.
[The Authority](persona_the_authority.md) tables [Do What's Best Now](plan_do_whats_best_now.md) still, and finds it no longer can, the mast holding where its judgement would have wandered.

## Tension

The promise is saved by the authority giving up the freedom to keep it.
A hand that cannot break its word cannot be tempted to, so the very surrender that looks like weakness is the source of the strength the promise needed, and the good outcome returns. That is the paradox at rest: credibility bought not by resolve but by removing the choice, and a hand now immovable in the same stroke that made it trustworthy.
