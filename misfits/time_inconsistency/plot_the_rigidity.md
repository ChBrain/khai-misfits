# Plot: The Rigidity

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The storm](place_the_storm.md), a shock no one foresaw, and a rule that forbids exactly what the moment needs.
The right move is plain, and the rope holds fast as the wind rises.

## Action

[Committing](process_committing.md): [The Bound](persona_the_bound.md), holding [the successor's seat](position_successor.md), sees what the emergency calls for and cannot do it, while [The Binder](persona_the_binder.md), holding [the binder's seat](position_binder.md), watches the tie it set for a foreseen temptation bite an unforeseen crisis.

## Stage

[The Bound](persona_the_bound.md) tables [Keep the Rule](plan_keep_the_rule.md): hold the line though the storm begs, because breaking it now would unmake the credibility the binding bought, so [the rigidity](piece_the_rigidity.md) is borne and [the time inconsistency](piece_the_time_inconsistency.md) shows its whole price.
The credibility is kept intact, and the moment's true remedy is left undone.

## Tension

The tie that secured the promise is the tie that traps the promiser.
The rule holds when holding it hurts, which is the proof it is real and the reason it costs: the one time judgement should have been trusted, the commitment allows none, and whoever holds the office pays. The rule that bought the outcome binds in the storm ... or it is broken for the emergency, the right move made at last, and the credibility every promise rested on goes with it.
