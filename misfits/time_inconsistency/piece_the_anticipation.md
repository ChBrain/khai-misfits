# Piece: The Anticipation

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The rational foresight: the agent pricing the break in before it comes.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the market](place_the_market.md), in plans set on what will be done, not what was said.
Where the promise is discounted for the break everyone can see.

## Load Bearing

Without it the promise could work once before the break taught its lesson.
With it the promise never works at all: the agent reasons the second moment out in advance, sees that reneging will be optimal, and declines the induced action from the start, so the authority is defeated before it can even be tempted. The Anticipation is load bearing because it is the rational-expectations turn that makes the trap bite immediately (Barro & Gordon), the foresight that converts a future break into a present emptiness, and the reason discretion fails not eventually but at once.

## Apparent

Mere caution, or cynicism.
Declining to act on a promise looks like distrust, so the anticipation reads as the agent being difficult, when it is only correct: it has reasoned forward and found the promise void, and its refusal is the accurate pricing of a break that is genuinely coming.

## Yearbook

Performed by [the agent](persona_the_agent.md) through [anticipating](process_anticipating.md), foreseeing [the temptation](piece_the_temptation.md).
It is the foresight that dooms the outcome: the Anticipation yields [the unravelling](piece_the_unravelling.md), the good result withheld before it can form.
