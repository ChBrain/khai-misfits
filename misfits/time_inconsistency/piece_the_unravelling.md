# Piece: The Unravelling

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The outcome destroyed by foresight: the induced action withheld and the good result never formed.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the market](place_the_market.md), the good outcome priced out before it exists.
Where the invention is not made and the investment is not risked.

## Load Bearing

Without it the misfit would be a broken promise, a single loss with a culprit.
With it the loss has no culprit and no broken word: because the agent foresaw the break and never acted, there is nothing for the authority to renege on, and the good outcome simply never forms, killed in advance by the certainty of a reneging that never has to happen. The Unravelling is load bearing because it is the outcome the theory predicts under discretion (Barro & Gordon), the good equilibrium that cannot be reached because everyone can see why it will not hold, and the reason the cost is invisible: it is a thing that never happened.

## Apparent

Bad conditions, or a market that just would not move.
The missing outcome looks like weak demand or a failure of nerve, so the unravelling reads as circumstance rather than as the exact and predictable result of an unbelievable promise, and the cost, being an absence, leaves nothing to point at.

## Yearbook

Produced by [the agent](persona_the_agent.md) through [anticipating](process_anticipating.md), grown from [the anticipation](piece_the_anticipation.md).
It is the good outcome that never was: the Unravelling is what [the commitment](piece_the_commitment.md) exists to prevent, the result foresight forecloses.
