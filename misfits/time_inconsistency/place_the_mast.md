# Place: The Mast

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). Where the hands are tied and the promise made unbreakable.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

An authority binding itself on purpose: a rule adopted, a choice delegated to a body that cannot renege, a rope to the mast before the song.
The discretion surrendered, deliberately, in daylight.

## Holds

The commitment, and the credibility it buys.
Here reneging is made impossible rather than merely unwise, so the promise gains the one thing discretion could not give it, and the mast holds a word that will hold because the hand that made it no longer can move.

## Offers

A promise worth believing at last.
With the break removed, the agent can act, so the mast offers the good outcome back, bought by the authority's own surrender of the freedom to choose.

## Withheld

The power to change course.
The same binding that makes the promise credible makes the hand immovable, so the mast withholds every later choice as well, keeping the good ones and the necessary ones out of reach alike.
