# Place: The Market

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). Where the promise is judged and the future priced in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Shown

The agent weighing whether to act, reasoning the promise forward to the day it will be tested.
Prices and plans set not on what was said, but on what will be done.

## Holds

The anticipation, and the unravelling it brings.
Here the promise is discounted for the break everyone can see coming, so the market holds the good outcome dying in advance, priced out of existence before a single word is broken.

## Offers

A clear, forward-looking judgement.
The market reads intentions correctly and reasons two moves ahead, so it offers an honest reckoning of what the promise is worth, which is, under discretion, almost nothing.

## Withheld

The benefit of the doubt.
The market extends no trust a promise has not earned by being unbreakable, so it withholds the very reliance the authority needed, and acts only on what it knows will hold.
