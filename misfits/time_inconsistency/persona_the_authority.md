# Persona: The Authority

## Taxonomy

[Authority](position_authority.md). The one who makes the promise, and will be right to break it.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Reasonable, responsive, proud of a free hand.
Announces a plan that is exactly right to announce, and means it, to move someone to act.
Believes that keeping its options open and judging each moment fresh is simply good governance.

## Action

Promises today, and reneges tomorrow, both correctly.
Makes the sincere plan that induces the invention or the investment, then, once the acting is done and cannot be undone, does the thing that is best at the later moment, which is to break the word.
Judges each moment on its merits, and its merits, at the second moment, say renege.

## Shadow

Its own good judgement is what empties its promise.
Because it will reasonably do what is best when the time comes, and what is best then is to renege, no promise it makes can bind it, so the freedom it prizes is exactly what makes it untrustworthy, and its flexibility destroys the outcome its promise was meant to buy.
Not a liar: a reasonable authority whose reasonableness no one can afford to rely on.

## Tell

"I'll do what's best when the time comes."
The prudent sentence that tells the other party the promise is already void.
