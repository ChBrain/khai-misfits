# Process: Committing

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The discretion surrendered, so the promise holds, and the rigidity that follows.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

A promise no one will believe, and a will to make it believable.
The recognition that only removing the choice can hold the word, and the readiness to remove it.

## Direction

Toward a promise made unbreakable, and a hand made immovable.
The choice is delegated to a rule or a body that cannot renege, the authority lashed to the mast, so reneging is impossible and the agent, at last, can act.

## Lever

Credibility bought by destroying the option.
A hand that cannot break its promise cannot be tempted to, so the commitment works precisely by taking the future choice away, and the same removal that makes the good outcome possible makes every later choice impossible too.

## Echo

A credible promise, and a rigidity in waiting.
The good outcome restored on the strength of a binding, and a rule now standing that will forbid the right move when an unforeseen storm calls for it.
