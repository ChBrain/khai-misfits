# Persona: The Bound

## Taxonomy

[Successor](position_successor.md). The one who inherits the tied hands, and meets the storm the rule cannot answer.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Constrained, later, holding an office whose choices were made in advance.
Faces a shock no one foresaw, and sees plainly what the moment calls for.
Finds that the very thing the moment calls for is the thing the binding rule forbids.

## Action

Holds the line, and pays for it.
Keeps the rule when a genuine emergency begs for discretion, because breaking it now would unmake the credibility every earlier promise was built on.
Bears the cost of a hand that cannot move, doing the wrong thing for the moment to keep the right thing for the whole.

## Shadow

The rigidity that secured the promise falls on the one who did not choose it.
The binding was set against a foreseen temptation and lands on an unforeseen storm, so the cost of the commitment is borne not by the authority that needed credibility but by the successor who needed flexibility, and the tie is most painful exactly where it was never meant to reach.
Not a fool for keeping the rule: the holder of a promise whose price came due on their watch.

## Tell

"I can see what to do, and the rule won't let me do it."
The bound officer's true report, keeping the credibility that traps them.
