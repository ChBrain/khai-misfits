# Plan: Expect the Break

## Taxonomy

A **standing plan**: the intent of [the agent's seat](position_agent.md), inside [Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md).

## Owner

## Direction

Hear the promise, then reason past it to the moment it will be tested. Ask whether it will still be kept when keeping it is costly, and, seeing that it will rightly be broken, do not build on it. Act only on a word that will hold.

## Orders

([The Agent](persona_the_agent.md) foresees the reneging and withholds the induced action, so nothing forms to keep faith about.)

## Implementation

([The Market](place_the_market.md); the [anticipating](process_anticipating.md) process; [the anticipation](piece_the_anticipation.md) prices the break in, so the induced action is withheld and [the unravelling](piece_the_unravelling.md) follows.)

## Targets

- [ ] the promise is reasoned forward to the moment of the break
- [ ] the reneging is foreseen and priced in
- [ ] the induced action, invest, invent, trust, is withheld
- [ ] the good outcome dies of foresight, before any word is broken
