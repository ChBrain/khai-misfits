# Pitch: Do What's Best When the Time Comes

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The default key the whole production is read in.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Tenor

Reasonable, responsive, keeping its options open.
Judge each moment on its own merits, do what is best when the time comes, and never bind yourself to a plan the facts might outgrow.
The voice of sensible flexibility, spoken by people who are right that a free hand serves better in the moment and cannot see that a hand known to be free is a promise no one can trust.

## Undertow

Under the good sense, a promise everyone knows will be rightly broken.
The plan that is best to announce is not best to carry out, because by tomorrow the acting is done and keeping the word costs and buys nothing, so the reasonable authority will renege, and the one who was to act, seeing it coming, never acts at all.
No one lies: the promise was sincere, the reneging is right when it comes, and the good outcome dies of foresight, killed before it forms by a break no one has yet made.

## Nerve

The feeling of prudent, flexible judgement,
every option kept open, every moment weighed fresh,
the promise emptied the instant the other party can reason forward and see the sensible break waiting at the end of it.

## Echo

After the run: a promise worth making and worthless to make,
an invention not invented and an investment not made, for want of a word that would hold,
and a good outcome that returns only when the promiser gives up the freedom to choose.
