# Plot: The Unravelling

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Cue

[The market](place_the_market.md), the agent reasoning the promise forward to the day it will be tested.
The break is visible from here, before it comes, and plans are set on what will be done, not what was said.

## Action

[Anticipating](process_anticipating.md): [The Agent](persona_the_agent.md), holding [the agent's seat](position_agent.md), foresees the reneging and declines to act, while [The Adviser](persona_the_adviser.md), holding [the adviser's seat](position_adviser.md), shows the good outcome was gone before it formed.

## Stage

[The Agent](persona_the_agent.md) tables [Expect the Break](plan_expect_the_break.md): reason the promise forward, price the break in, and withhold the induced action, so [the anticipation](piece_the_anticipation.md) forecloses the outcome and [the unravelling](piece_the_unravelling.md) follows.
[The Adviser](persona_the_adviser.md) tables [Rules Not Discretion](plan_rules_not_discretion.md), naming the trap and the only cure.

## Tension

Nobody breaks a word, and the outcome dies anyway.
The authority never has to renege, because the agent, foreseeing the break, never acts, so the good result is destroyed by foresight alone, with no betrayal to point to and no one to blame. The cost is an absence: the invention not invented, the investment not made, killed in advance by a promise everyone could see would not hold.
