# Piece: The Temptation

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The ex-post optimal deviation: the break that becomes right once the inducement's work is done.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Place

At [the podium](place_the_podium.md), the plan reconsidered next day.
Where keeping the word has stopped being what is best.

## Load Bearing

Without it the promise would simply be kept, and there would be no misfit.
With it the plan that was best to make is no longer best to carry out: the invention is made, the capital is sunk, the hostage is taken, so the inducement cannot be undone and keeping the word now costs and buys nothing. The Temptation is load bearing because it is the inconsistency of the optimal plan itself (Strotz; Kydland & Prescott), the gap between the two moments that makes reneging the right call at the second, and the reason no sincere promise from a free hand can bind.

## Apparent

A sensible change of mind.
Because reneging really is optimal when the moment comes, the break looks like reasonable updating rather than betrayal, so the temptation wears the face of good judgement, which is exactly what makes it impossible to promise around.

## Yearbook

Reached by [the authority](persona_the_authority.md) through [reneging](process_reneging.md), waiting inside [the promise](piece_the_promise.md) from the start.
It is the right break that empties the word: the Temptation is what [the anticipation](piece_the_anticipation.md) foresees, and what commitment must make impossible.
