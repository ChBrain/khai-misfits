# Persona: The Agent

## Taxonomy

[Agent](position_agent.md). The one the promise is meant to move, who sees the break coming and does not act.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Projection

Shrewd, forward-looking, unwilling to be the fool.
Hears a promise that would, if kept, be worth acting on, and reasons past it to the moment it will be tested.
Sees that the authority will rightly renege, and declines to build its plans on a word it knows will break.

## Action

Reasons the promise forward, and withholds the act.
Foresees the second moment when reneging is optimal, prices the break in, and does not invest, invent, or trust on the strength of a pledge that will not survive.
Leaves the inducement unanswered, so there is nothing for the authority to keep faith about.

## Shadow

Its correct foresight is what kills the good outcome.
The authority never has to break the promise, because the agent, seeing the break coming, never acts, so the outcome dies before it forms, destroyed by a reneging that never happens and a suspicion that is entirely justified.
Not a cynic: a rational party right to distrust a promise the promiser will be right to break.

## Tell

"Why would I act on a promise they'll be right to break?"
The reasonable question whose answer, under discretion, is that there is no reason at all.
