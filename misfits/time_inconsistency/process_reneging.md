# Process: Reneging

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The break that becomes optimal at the second moment, once the inducement's work is done.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An inducement that has done its work and cannot be undone.
The invention made, the capital sunk, the hostage taken, and a promise that now costs and buys nothing.

## Direction

Toward the word set down, correctly.
At the second moment the best thing to do is no longer the thing promised, so the reasonable authority, judging fresh, breaks the pledge and keeps the gain.

## Lever

A promise whose purpose is spent.
Because the acting is already done, keeping the word can no longer induce it, so the promise has nothing left to buy and reneging is simply the better choice now, not a betrayal but an update.

## Echo

A right break, and a lesson taught.
The word set down when keeping it stopped paying, and every future promise from the same hand now read in the light of this one.
