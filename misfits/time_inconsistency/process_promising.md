# Process: Promising

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The plan announced at the first moment, optimal to make and meant to induce.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Initiated by

An outcome that needs someone to act, and an authority that can offer terms.
A world where investment, invention, restraint, or trust waits on a credible pledge.

## Direction

Toward a promise sincerely made and genuinely good.
The plan is announced, optimal at this moment, designed to move the agent to do the thing the outcome needs.

## Lever

A plan that is best precisely because it induces.
At the first moment the promise is the right thing to say, because saying it and being believed would produce the good outcome, so the authority means it, and means it well.

## Echo

A sincere pledge, and a second moment waiting.
The promise made, the inducement offered, and the future set in which keeping the word will no longer be what is best.
