# Position: Adviser

## Taxonomy

[Time Inconsistency](play_time_inconsistency.md). The seat that reads both moments and prescribes the rule.

## Owner

- Project: khai-misfits

## Has

The sight of the promise optimal now and the break optimal later.
The finding that discretion cannot hold a promise, and only commitment can.
No power to enact the cure, only to name it.

## Orders

Show the authority its future judgement will break the word.
Show the agent this is why the promise is empty.
Prescribe rules, not discretion.

## Loses

Its welcome, by naming the cure.
The remedy it must recommend is the surrender of the flexibility that the authority values and employs it to protect, so its correct counsel asks its patron to give up power, and is resisted for exactly that.
Right about the trap, and unwelcome for the only way out.

## Drives

Toward the rule that would make the promise hold,
against the discretion the authority is loath to lose,
so the true diagnosis prescribes the one medicine the patient least wants to take.
